====== SB 8.24.56 ======

===== Text =====

> aśrauṣīd ṛṣibhiḥ sākam
> ātma-tattvam asaṁśayam
> nāvy āsīno bhagavatā
> proktaṁ brahma sanātanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aśrauṣīt]]---he heard; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣibhiḥ]]---the great saintly persons; [[synonyms:s:sākam]]---with; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:t:tattvam]]---the science of self-realization; [[synonyms:a:asaṁśayam]]---without any doubt (because it was spoken by the Supreme Lord); [[synonyms:n:nāvi]] [[synonyms:ā:āsīnaḥ]]---sitting in the boat; [[synonyms:b:bhagavatā]]---by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:proktam]]---explained; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---all transcendental literatures; [[synonyms:s:sanātanam]]---eternally existing.

===== Translation =====

**While sitting in the boat, King Satyavrata, accompanied by the great saintly persons, listened to the instructions of the Supreme Personality of Godhead in regard to self-realization. These instructions were all from the eternal Vedic literature [brahma]. Thus the King and sages had no doubt about the Absolute Truth.**
