====== SB 8.24.43 ======

===== Text =====

> tam ūcur munayaḥ prītā
> rājan dhyāyasva keśavam
> sa vai naḥ saṅkaṭād asmād
> avitā śaṁ vidhāsyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:ū:ūcuḥ]]---said; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]]---all the saintly //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:p:prītāḥ]]---being pleased; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:d:dhyāyasva]]---meditate; [[synonyms:k:keśavam]]---upon the Supreme Lord, Keśava; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---His Lordship; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---us; [[synonyms:s:saṅkaṭāt]]---from the great danger; [[synonyms:a:asmāt]]---as now visible; [[synonyms:a:avitā]]---will save; [[synonyms:ś:śam]]---auspiciousness; [[synonyms:v:vidhāsyati]]---He will arrange.

===== Translation =====

**The saintly brāhmaṇas, being pleased with the King, said to him: O King, please meditate upon the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Keśava. He will save us from this impending danger and arrange for our well-being.**
