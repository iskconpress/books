====== SB 8.24.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ekadā kṛtamālāyāṁ
> kurvato jala-tarpaṇam
> tasyāñjaly-udake kācic
> chaphary ekābhyapadyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekadā]]---one day; [[synonyms:k:kṛtamālāyām]]---on the bank of the Kṛtamālā River; [[synonyms:k:kurvataḥ]]---executing; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:t:tarpaṇam]]---the offering of oblations of water; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:a:añjali]]---palmful; [[synonyms:u:udake]]---in the water; [[synonyms:k:kācit]]---some; [[synonyms:ś:śapharī]]---a small fish; [[synonyms:e:ekā]]---one; [[synonyms:a:abhyapadyata]]---was generated.

===== Translation =====

**One day while King Satyavrata was performing austerities by offering water on the bank of the River Kṛtamālā, a small fish appeared in the water in his palms.**
