====== SB 8.24.11 ======

===== Text =====

> yo 'sāv asmin mahā-kalpe
> tanayaḥ sa vivasvataḥ
> śrāddhadeva iti khyāto
> manutve hariṇārpitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---one who; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---He (the Supreme Person); [[synonyms:a:asmin]]---in this; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpe]]---great millennium; [[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:v:vivasvataḥ]]---of the sun-god; [[synonyms:ś:śrāddhadevaḥ]]---by the name Śrāddhadeva; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:khyātaḥ]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:m:manutve]]---in the position of Manu; [[synonyms:h:hariṇā]]---by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:arpitaḥ]]---was situated.

===== Translation =====

**In this [the present] millennium King Satyavrata later became the son of Vivasvān, the king of the sun planet, and was known as Śrāddhadeva. By the mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, he was given the post of Manu.**
