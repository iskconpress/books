====== SB 8.24.28 ======

===== Text =====

> namas te puruṣa-śreṣṭha
> sthity-utpatty-apyayeśvara
> bhaktānāṁ naḥ prapannānāṁ
> mukhyo hy ātma-gatir vibho

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto You; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---the best of all living entities, the best of all enjoyers; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---of maintenance; [[synonyms:u:utpatti]]---creation; [[synonyms:a:apyaya]]---and destruction; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhaktānām]]---of Your devotees; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---like us; [[synonyms:p:prapannānām]]---those who are surrendered; [[synonyms:m:mukhyaḥ]]---the supreme; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:g:gatiḥ]]---the supreme destination; [[synonyms:v:vibho]]---Lord Viṣṇu.

===== Translation =====

**O my Lord, master of creation, maintenance and annihilation, O best of enjoyers, Lord Viṣṇu, You are the leader and destination of surrendered devotees like us. Therefore let me offer my respectful obeisances unto You.**
