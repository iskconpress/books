====== SB 8.24.39 ======

===== Text =====

> ittham ādiśya rājānaṁ
> harir antaradhīyata
> so 'nvavaikṣata taṁ kālaṁ
> yaṁ hṛṣīkeśa ādiśat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ittham]]---as aforementioned; [[synonyms:ā:ādiśya]]---instructing; [[synonyms:r:rājānam]]---the King (Satyavrata); [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:antaradhīyata]]---disappeared from that place; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (the King); [[synonyms:a:anvavaikṣata]]---began to wait for; [[synonyms:t:tam]] [[synonyms:k:kālam]]---that time; [[synonyms:y:yam]]---which; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣīka]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśaḥ]]---Lord Hṛṣīkeśa, the master of all the senses; [[synonyms:ā:ādiśat]]---instructed.

===== Translation =====

**After thus instructing the King, the Supreme Personality of Godhead immediately disappeared. Then King Satyavrata began to wait for that time of which the Lord had instructed.**
