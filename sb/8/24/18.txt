====== SB 8.24.18 ======

===== Text =====

> nāhaṁ kamaṇḍalāv asmin
> kṛcchraṁ vastum ihotsahe
> kalpayaukaḥ suvipulaṁ
> yatrāhaṁ nivase sukham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kamaṇḍalau]]---in this waterpot; [[synonyms:a:asmin]]---in this; [[synonyms:k:kṛcchram]]---with great difficulty; [[synonyms:v:vastum]]---to live; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---here; [[synonyms:u:utsahe]]---like; [[synonyms:k:kalpaya]]---just consider; [[synonyms:o:okaḥ]]---residential place; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vipulam]]---more expanded; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherein; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nivase]]---can live; [[synonyms:s:sukham]]---in pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**O My dear King, I do not like living in this waterpot with such great difficulty. Therefore, please find some better reservoir of water where I can live comfortably.**
