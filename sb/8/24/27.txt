====== SB 8.24.27 ======

===== Text =====

> nūnaṁ tvaṁ bhagavān sākṣād
> dharir nārāyaṇo 'vyayaḥ
> anugrahāya bhūtānāṁ
> dhatse rūpaṁ jalaukasām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nūnam]]---certainly; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---You (are); [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇaḥ]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:avyayaḥ]]---inexhaustible; [[synonyms:a:anugrahāya]]---to show mercy; [[synonyms:b:bhūtānām]]---to all living entities; [[synonyms:d:dhatse]]---You have assumed; [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---a form; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:o:okasām]]---like an aquatic.

===== Translation =====

**My Lord, You are certainly the inexhaustible Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyaṇa, Śrī Hari. It is to show Your mercy to the living entities that You have now assumed the form of an aquatic.**
