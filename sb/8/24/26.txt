====== SB 8.24.26 ======

===== Text =====

> naivaṁ vīryo jalacaro
> dṛṣṭo 'smābhiḥ śruto 'pi vā
> yo bhavān yojana-śatam
> ahnābhivyānaśe saraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vīryaḥ]]---powerful; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:c:caraḥ]]---aquatic; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭaḥ]]---seen; [[synonyms:a:asmābhiḥ]]---by us; [[synonyms:ś:śrutaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---nor heard of; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---Your Lordship; [[synonyms:y:yojana]]-[[synonyms:ś:śatam]]---hundreds of miles; [[synonyms:a:ahnā]]---in one day; [[synonyms:a:abhivyānaśe]]---expanding; [[synonyms:s:saraḥ]]---water.

===== Translation =====

**My Lord, in one day You have expanded Yourself for hundreds of miles, covering the water of the river and the ocean. Before this I had never seen or heard of such an aquatic animal.**
