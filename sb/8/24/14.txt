====== SB 8.24.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tam āha sātikaruṇaṁ
> mahā-kāruṇikaṁ nṛpam
> yādobhyo jñāti-ghātibhyo
> dīnāṁ māṁ dīna-vatsala
> kathaṁ visṛjase rājan
> bhītām asmin sarij-jale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him (Satyavrata); [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---that small fish; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:k:karuṇam]]---extremely compassionate; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāruṇikam]]---extremely merciful; [[synonyms:n:nṛpam]]---unto King Satyavrata; [[synonyms:y:yādobhyaḥ]]---to the aquatics; [[synonyms:j:jñāti]]-[[synonyms:g:ghātibhyaḥ]]---who are always eager to kill the smaller fish; [[synonyms:d:dīnām]]---very poor; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---me; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsala]]---O protector of the poor; [[synonyms:k:katham]]---why; [[synonyms:v:visṛjase]]---you are throwing; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:b:bhītām]]---very much afraid; [[synonyms:a:asmin]]---within this; [[synonyms:s:sarit]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---in the water of the river.

===== Translation =====

**With an appealing voice, the poor small fish said to King Satyavrata, who was very merciful: My dear King, protector of the poor, why are you throwing Me in the water of the river, where there are other aquatics who can kill Me? I am very much afraid of them.**

===== Purport =====

In the //Matsya Purāṇa// it is said:

> ananta-śaktir bhagavān
> matsya-rūpī janārdanaḥ
> krīḍārthaṁ yācayām āsa
> svayaṁ satyavrataṁ nṛpam

"The Supreme Personality of Godhead possesses unlimited potency. Nonetheless, in His pastime in the form of a fish He begged protection from King Satyavrata."
