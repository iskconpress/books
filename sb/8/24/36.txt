====== SB 8.24.36 ======

===== Text =====

> dodhūyamānāṁ tāṁ nāvaṁ
> samīreṇa balīyasā
> upasthitasya me śṛṅge
> nibadhnīhi mahāhinā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dodhūyamānām]]---being tossed about; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nāvam]]---boat; [[synonyms:s:samīreṇa]]---by the wind; [[synonyms:b:balīyasā]]---very powerful; [[synonyms:u:upasthitasya]]---situated nearby; [[synonyms:m:me]]---of Me; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṅge]]---to the horn; [[synonyms:n:nibadhnīhi]]---bind; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:ahinā]]---by the large serpent (Vāsuki).

===== Translation =====

**Then, as the boat is tossed about by the powerful winds, attach the vessel to My horn by means of the great serpent Vāsuki, for I shall be present by your side.**
