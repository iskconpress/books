====== SB 8.24.32 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bhagavān uvāca
> saptame hy adyatanād ūrdhvam
> ahany etad arindama
> nimaṅkṣyaty apyayāmbhodhau
> trailokyaṁ bhūr-bhuvādikam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead said; [[synonyms:s:saptame]]---on the seventh; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:adyatanāt]]---from today; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhvam]]---forward; [[synonyms:a:ahani]]---on the day; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this creation; [[synonyms:a:arimdama]]---O King who can subdue your enemies; [[synonyms:n:nimaṅkṣyati]]---shall be inundated; [[synonyms:a:apyaya]]-[[synonyms:a:ambhodhau]]---in the ocean of destruction; [[synonyms:t:trailokyam]]---the three //lokas//; [[synonyms:b:bhūḥ]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādikam]]---namely Bhūrloka, Bhuvarloka and Svarloka.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead said: O King, who can subdue your enemies, on the seventh day from today the three worlds---Bhūḥ, Bhuvaḥ and Svaḥ---will all merge into the water of inundation.**
