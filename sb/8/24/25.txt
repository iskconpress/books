====== SB 8.24.25 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ vimohitas tena
> vadatā valgu-bhāratīm
> tam āha ko bhavān asmān
> matsya-rūpeṇa mohayan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vimohitaḥ]]---bewildered; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---by the fish; [[synonyms:v:vadatā]]---speaking; [[synonyms:v:valgu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāratīm]]---sweet words; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---You; [[synonyms:a:asmān]]---us; [[synonyms:m:matsya]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpeṇa]]---in the form of a fish; [[synonyms:m:mohayan]]---bewildering.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing these sweet words from the Supreme Personality of Godhead in the form of a fish, the King, being bewildered, asked Him: Who are You, sir? You simply bewilder us.**
