====== SB 8.24.13 ======

===== Text =====

> satyavrato 'ñjali-gatāṁ
> saha toyena bhārata
> utsasarja nadī-toye
> śapharīṁ draviḍeśvaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:satyavrataḥ]]---King Satyavrata; [[synonyms:a:añjali]]-[[synonyms:g:gatām]]---in the water held in the palms of the King; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:t:toyena]]---water; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:u:utsasarja]]---threw; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]-[[synonyms:t:toye]]---in the water of the river; [[synonyms:ś:śapharīm]]---that small fish; [[synonyms:d:draviḍa]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---Satyavrata, the King of Draviḍa.

===== Translation =====

**Satyavrata, the King of Draviḍadeśa, threw the fish into the water of the river along with the water in his palm, O King Parīkṣit, descendant of Bharata.**
