====== SB 8.24.21 ======

===== Text =====

> tata ādāya sā rājñā
> kṣiptā rājan sarovare
> tad āvṛtyātmanā so 'yaṁ
> mahā-mīno 'nvavardhata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from there; [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---taking away; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---the fish; [[synonyms:r:rājñā]]---by the King; [[synonyms:k:kṣiptā]]---being thrown; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King (Mahārāja Parīkṣit); [[synonyms:s:sarovare]]---in a lake; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:āvṛtya]]---covering; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanā]]---by the body; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---the fish; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mīnaḥ]]---gigantic fish; [[synonyms:a:anvavardhata]]---immediately developed.

===== Translation =====

**O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, the King took the fish from the well and threw Him in a lake, but the fish then assumed a gigantic form exceeding the extent of the water.**
