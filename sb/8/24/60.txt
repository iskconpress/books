====== SB 8.24.60 ======

===== Text =====

> avatāraṁ harer yo 'yaṁ
> kīrtayed anvahaṁ naraḥ
> saṅkalpās tasya sidhyanti
> sa yāti paramāṁ gatim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avatāram]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---whoever; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kīrtayet]]---narrates and chants; [[synonyms:a:anvaham]]---daily; [[synonyms:n:naraḥ]]---such a person; [[synonyms:s:saṅkalpāḥ]]---all ambitions; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him; [[synonyms:s:sidhyanti]]---become successful; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---such a person; [[synonyms:y:yāti]]---goes back; [[synonyms:p:paramām]] [[synonyms:g:gatim]]---back home to Godhead, the supreme place.

===== Translation =====

**One who narrates this description of the Matsya incarnation and King Satyavrata will certainly have all his ambitions fulfilled, and he will undoubtedly return home, back to Godhead.**
