====== SB 8.24.24 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣipyamāṇas tam āhedam
> iha māṁ makarādayaḥ
> adanty atibalā vīra
> māṁ nehotsraṣṭum arhasi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣipyamāṇaḥ]]---being thrown in the ocean; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---the fish said; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in this place; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---Me; [[synonyms:m:makara]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---dangerous aquatics like sharks; [[synonyms:a:adanti]]---will eat; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:b:balāḥ]]---because of being too powerful; [[synonyms:v:vīra]]---O heroic King; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---Me; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in this water; [[synonyms:u:utsraṣṭum]]---to throw; [[synonyms:a:arhasi]]---you deserve.

===== Translation =====

**While being thrown in the ocean, the fish said to King Satyavrata: O hero, in this water there are very powerful and dangerous sharks that will eat Me. Therefore you should not throw Me in this place.**
