====== SB 8.24.57 ======

===== Text =====

> atīta-pralayāpāya
> utthitāya sa vedhase
> hatvāsuraṁ hayagrīvaṁ
> vedān pratyāharad dhariḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atīta]]---passed; [[synonyms:p:pralaya]]-[[synonyms:a:apāye]]---at the end of the inundation; [[synonyms:u:utthitāya]]---to bring him to his senses after sleeping; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:v:vedhase]]---unto Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:h:hatvā]]---after killing; [[synonyms:a:asuram]]---the demon; [[synonyms:h:hayagrīvam]]---by the name Hayagrīva; [[synonyms:v:vedān]]---all the Vedic records; [[synonyms:p:pratyāharat]]---delivered; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**At the end of the last inundation [during the period of Svāyambhuva Manu] the Supreme Personality of Godhead killed the demon named Hayagrīva and delivered all the Vedic literatures to Lord Brahmā when Lord Brahmā awakened from sleeping.**
