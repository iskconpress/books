====== SB 8.24.44 ======

===== Text =====

> so 'nudhyātas tato rājñā
> prādurāsīn mahārṇave
> eka-śṛṅga-dharo matsyo
> haimo niyuta-yojanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:a:anudhyātaḥ]]---being meditated upon; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter (hearing the words of the saintly //brāhmaṇas//); [[synonyms:r:rājñā]]---by the King; [[synonyms:p:prādurāsīt]]---appeared (before him); [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:arṇave]]---in the great ocean of inundation; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śṛṅga]]-[[synonyms:d:dharaḥ]]---with one horn; [[synonyms:m:matsyaḥ]]---a big fish; [[synonyms:h:haimaḥ]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:n:niyuta]]-[[synonyms:y:yojanaḥ]]---eight million miles long.

===== Translation =====

**Then, while the King constantly meditated upon the Supreme Personality of Godhead, a large golden fish appeared in the ocean of inundation. The fish had one horn and was eight million miles long.**
