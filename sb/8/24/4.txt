====== SB 8.24.4 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-sūta uvāca
> ity ukto viṣṇu-rātena
> bhagavān bādarāyaṇiḥ
> uvāca caritaṁ viṣṇor
> matsya-rūpeṇa yat kṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]] [[synonyms:u:uktaḥ]]---thus being questioned; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:r:rātena]]---by Mahārāja Parīkṣit, known as Viṣṇurāta; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]]---the son of Vyāsadeva, Śukadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:c:caritam]]---the pastimes; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:m:matsya]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpeṇa]]---by Him in the form of a fish; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---was done.

===== Translation =====

**Sūta Gosvāmī said: When Parīkṣit Mahārāja thus inquired from Śukadeva Gosvāmī, that most powerful saintly person began describing the pastimes of the Lord's incarnation as a fish.**
