====== SB 8.24.19 ======

===== Text =====

> sa enāṁ tata ādāya
> nyadhād audañcanodake
> tatra kṣiptā muhūrtena
> hasta-trayam avardhata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---the King; [[synonyms:e:enām]]---unto the fish; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---taking out; [[synonyms:n:nyadhāt]]---placed; [[synonyms:a:audañcana]]-[[synonyms:u:udake]]---in a well of water; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---therein; [[synonyms:k:kṣiptā]]---being thrown; [[synonyms:m:muhūrtena]]---within a moment; [[synonyms:h:hasta]]-[[synonyms:t:trayam]]---three cubits; [[synonyms:a:avardhata]]---immediately developed.

===== Translation =====

**Then, taking the fish out of the waterpot, the King threw Him in a large well. But within a moment the fish developed to the length of three cubits.**
