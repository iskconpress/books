====== SB 8.24.42 ======

===== Text =====

> dhyāyan bhagavad-ādeśaṁ
> dadṛśe nāvam āgatām
> tām āruroha viprendrair
> ādāyauṣadhi-vīrudhaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhyāyan]]---remembering; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādeśam]]---the order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:dadṛśe]]---he saw; [[synonyms:n:nāvam]]---a boat; [[synonyms:ā:āgatām]]---coming near; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---aboard the boat; [[synonyms:ā:āruroha]]---got up; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:i:indraiḥ]]---with the saintly //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---taking; [[synonyms:a:auṣadhi]]---herbs; [[synonyms:v:vīrudhaḥ]]---and creepers.

===== Translation =====

**As Satyavrata remembered the order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, he saw a boat coming near him. Thus he collected herbs and creepers, and, accompanied by saintly brāhmaṇas, he got aboard the boat.**
