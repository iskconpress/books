====== SB 8.17.17 ======

===== Text =====

> athāpy upāyo mama devi cintyaḥ
> santoṣitasya vrata-caryayā te
> mamārcanaṁ nārhati gantum anyathā
> śraddhānurūpaṁ phala-hetukatvāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:api]]---in spite of this situation; [[synonyms:u:upāyaḥ]]---some means; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---by Me; [[synonyms:d:devi]]---O goddess; [[synonyms:c:cintyaḥ]]---must be considered; [[synonyms:s:santoṣitasya]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:v:vrata]]-[[synonyms:c:caryayā]]---observing the vow; [[synonyms:t:te]]---by you; [[synonyms:m:mama]] [[synonyms:a:arcanam]]---worshiping Me; [[synonyms:n:na]]---never; [[synonyms:a:arhati]]---deserves; [[synonyms:g:gantum]] [[synonyms:a:anyathā]]---to become otherwise; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]]-[[synonyms:a:anurūpam]]---according to one's faith and devotion; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---of the result; [[synonyms:h:hetukatvāt]]---from being the cause.

===== Translation =====

**Yet because I have been satisfied by the activities of your vow, O goddess Aditi, I must find some means to favor you, for worship of Me never goes in vain but certainly gives the desired result according to what one deserves.**
