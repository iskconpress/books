====== SB 8.17.21 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> etāvad uktvā bhagavāṁs
> tatraivāntaradhīyata
> aditir durlabhaṁ labdhvā
> harer janmātmani prabhoḥ
> upādhāvat patiṁ bhaktyā
> parayā kṛta-kṛtyavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:etāvat]]---in this way; [[synonyms:u:uktvā]]---saying (to her); [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tatra]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---in that very spot; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:a:adhīyata]]---disappeared; [[synonyms:a:aditiḥ]]---Aditi; [[synonyms:d:durlabham]]---a very rare achievement; [[synonyms:l:labdhvā]]---getting; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:j:janma]]---birth; [[synonyms:ā:ātmani]]---in herself; [[synonyms:p:prabhoḥ]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:u:upādhāvat]]---immediately went; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---unto her husband; [[synonyms:b:bhaktyā]]---with devotion; [[synonyms:p:parayā]]---great; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---thinking herself very successful.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After speaking in this way, the Supreme Personality of Godhead disappeared from that very spot. Aditi, having received the extremely valuable benediction that the Lord would appear as her son, considered herself very successful, and with great devotion she approached her husband.**
