====== SB 8.17.20 ======

===== Text =====

> naitat parasmā ākhyeyaṁ
> pṛṣṭayāpi kathañcana
> sarvaṁ sampadyate devi
> deva-guhyaṁ susaṁvṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:p:parasmai]]---to outsiders; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyeyam]]---is to be disclosed; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣṭayā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even though questioned; [[synonyms:k:kathañcana]]---by anyone; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---everything; [[synonyms:s:sampadyate]]---becomes successful; [[synonyms:d:devi]]---O lady; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:g:guhyam]]---very confidential even to the demigods; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvṛtam]]---very carefully kept confidential.

===== Translation =====

**O lady, even if someone inquires, you should not disclose this fact to anyone. That which is very confidential is successful if kept secret.**
