====== SB 8.17.18 ======

===== Text =====

> tvayārcitaś cāham apatya-guptaye
> payo-vratenānuguṇaṁ samīḍitaḥ
> svāṁśena putratvam upetya te sutān
> goptāsmi mārīca-tapasy adhiṣṭhitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by you; [[synonyms:a:arcitaḥ]]---being worshiped; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:a:apatya]]-[[synonyms:g:guptaye]]---giving protection to your sons; [[synonyms:p:payaḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vratena]]---by the //payo-vrata// vow; [[synonyms:a:anuguṇam]]---as far as possible; [[synonyms:s:samīḍitaḥ]]---properly worshiped; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---by My plenary portion; [[synonyms:p:putratvam]]---becoming your son; [[synonyms:u:upetya]]---taking this opportunity; [[synonyms:t:te]] [[synonyms:s:sutān]]---to your other sons; [[synonyms:g:goptā]] [[synonyms:a:asmi]]---I shall give protection; [[synonyms:m:mārīca]]---of Kaśyapa Muni; [[synonyms:t:tapasi]]---in the austerity; [[synonyms:a:adhiṣṭhitaḥ]]---situated.

===== Translation =====

**You have prayed to Me and properly worshiped Me by performing the great payo-vrata ceremony for the sake of protecting your sons. Because of Kaśyapa Muni's austerities, I shall agree to become your son and thus protect your other sons.**
