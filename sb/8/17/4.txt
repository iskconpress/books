====== SB 8.17.4 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyāḥ prādurabhūt tāta
> bhagavān ādi-puruṣaḥ
> pīta-vāsāś catur-bāhuḥ
> śaṅkha-cakra-gadā-dharaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]]---before her; [[synonyms:p:prādurabhūt]]---appeared; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---my dear King; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣaḥ]]---the original person; [[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsāḥ]]---dressed in yellow garments; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhuḥ]]---with four arms; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]-[[synonyms:c:cakra]]-[[synonyms:g:gadā]]-[[synonyms:d:dharaḥ]]---bearing the conchshell, disc, club and lotus flower.

===== Translation =====

**My dear King, the original Supreme Personality of Godhead, dressed in yellow garments and bearing a conchshell, disc, club and lotus in His four hands, then appeared before Aditi.**
