====== SB 8.17.19 ======

===== Text =====

> upadhāva patiṁ bhadre
> prajāpatim akalmaṣam
> māṁ ca bhāvayatī patyāv
> evaṁ rūpam avasthitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upadhāva]]---just go worship; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---your husband; [[synonyms:b:bhadre]]---O gentle woman; [[synonyms:p:prajāpatim]]---who is a Prajāpati; [[synonyms:a:akalmaṣam]]---very much purified because of his austerity; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---Me; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as well as; [[synonyms:b:bhāvayatī]]---thinking of; [[synonyms:p:patyau]]---within your husband; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---form; [[synonyms:a:avasthitam]]---situated there.

===== Translation =====

**Always thinking of Me as being situated within the body of your husband, Kaśyapa, go worship your husband, who has been purified by his austerity.**
