====== SB 8.17.25 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-brahmovāca
> jayorugāya bhagavann
> urukrama namo 'stu te
> namo brahmaṇya-devāya
> tri-guṇāya namo namaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:brahmā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Lord Brahmā offered prayers; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:u:urugāya]]---unto the Lord, who is constantly glorified; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O my Lord; [[synonyms:u:urukrama]]---whose activities are very glorious; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:astu]] [[synonyms:t:te]]---I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:b:brahmaṇya]]-[[synonyms:d:devāya]]---unto the Lord of the transcendentalists; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇāya]]---the controller of the three modes of nature; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances unto You again and again.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Brahmā said: O Supreme Personality of Godhead, all glories unto You, who are glorified by all and whose activities are all uncommon. I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, O Lord of the transcendentalists, controller of the three modes of nature. I offer my respectful obeisances unto You again and again.**
