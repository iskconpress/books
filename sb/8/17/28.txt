====== SB 8.17.28 ======

===== Text =====

> tvaṁ vai prajānāṁ sthira-jaṅgamānāṁ
> prajāpatīnām asi sambhaviṣṇuḥ
> divaukasāṁ deva divaś cyutānāṁ
> parāyaṇaṁ naur iva majjato 'psu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tvam]]---Your Lordship; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:prajānām]]---of all living entities; [[synonyms:s:sthira]]-[[synonyms:j:jaṅgamānām]]---either stationary or moving; [[synonyms:p:prajāpatīnām]]---of all the Prajāpatis; [[synonyms:a:asi]]---You are; [[synonyms:s:sambhaviṣṇuḥ]]---the generator of everyone; [[synonyms:d:diva]]-[[synonyms:o:okasām]]---of the inhabitants of the upper planetary system; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---O Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:d:divaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:cyutānām]]---of the demigods, who have now fallen from their residential quarters; [[synonyms:p:parāyaṇam]]---the supreme shelter; [[synonyms:n:nauḥ]]---boat; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:m:majjataḥ]]---of one drowning; [[synonyms:a:apsu]]---in the water.

===== Translation =====

**My Lord, You are the original generator of all living entities, stationary or moving, and You are also the generator of the Prajāpatis. O my Lord, as a boat is the only hope for a person drowning in the water, You are the only shelter for the demigods, who are now bereft of their heavenly position.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Eighth Canto, Seventeenth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "The Supreme Lord Agrees to Become Aditi's Son."
