====== SB 8.17.13 ======

===== Text =====

> tān vinirjitya samare
> durmadān asurarṣabhān
> pratilabdha-jaya-śrībhiḥ
> putrair icchasy upāsitum

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tān]]---them; [[synonyms:v:vinirjitya]]---defeating; [[synonyms:s:samare]]---in the fight; [[synonyms:d:durmadān]]---puffed up by strength; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabhān]]---the leaders of the demons; [[synonyms:p:pratilabdha]]---getting back; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---victory; [[synonyms:ś:śrībhiḥ]]---as well as the opulence; [[synonyms:p:putraiḥ]]---with your sons; [[synonyms:i:icchasi]]---you are desiring; [[synonyms:u:upāsitum]]---to come together and worship Me.

===== Translation =====

**O Devī, O goddess, I can understand that you want to regain your sons and be together with them to worship Me, after defeating the enemies in battle and retrieving your abode and opulences.**
