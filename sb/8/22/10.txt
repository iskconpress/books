====== SB 8.22.10 ======

===== Text =====

> itthaṁ sa niścitya pitāmaho mahān
> agādha-bodho bhavataḥ pāda-padmam
> dhruvaṁ prapede hy akutobhayaṁ janād
> bhītaḥ svapakṣa-kṣapaṇasya sattama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ittham]]---because of this (as stated above); [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:n:niścitya]]---definitely deciding on this point; [[synonyms:p:pitāmahaḥ]]---my grandfather; [[synonyms:m:mahān]]---the great devotee; [[synonyms:a:agādha]]-[[synonyms:b:bodhaḥ]]---my grandfather, who received unlimited knowledge because of his devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bhavataḥ]]---of Your Lordship; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padmam]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhruvam]]---the infallible, eternal shelter; [[synonyms:p:prapede]]---surrendered; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:akutaḥ]]-[[synonyms:b:bhayam]]---completely free of fear; [[synonyms:j:janāt]]---from ordinary common people; [[synonyms:b:bhītaḥ]]---being afraid; [[synonyms:s:svapakṣa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣapaṇasya]]---of Your Lordship, who kill the demons on our own side; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---O best of the best.

===== Translation =====

**My grandfather, the best of all men, who achieved unlimited knowledge and was worshipable for everyone, was afraid of the common men in this world. Being fully convinced of the substantiality afforded by shelter at Your lotus feet, He took shelter of Your lotus feet, against the will of his father and demoniac friends, who were killed by Your own self.**
