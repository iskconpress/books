====== SB 8.22.19 ======

===== Text =====

> baddhaṁ vīkṣya patiṁ sādhvī
> tat-patnī bhaya-vihvalā
> prāñjaliḥ praṇatopendraṁ
> babhāṣe 'vāṅ-mukhī nṛpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:baddham]]---arrested; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---her husband; [[synonyms:s:sādhvī]]---the chaste woman; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:patnī]]---Bali Mahārāja's wife; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]-[[synonyms:v:vihvalā]]---being very disturbed by fear; [[synonyms:p:prāñjaliḥ]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:p:praṇatā]]---having offered obeisances; [[synonyms:u:upendram]]---unto Vāmanadeva; [[synonyms:b:babhāṣe]]---addressed; [[synonyms:a:avāk]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhī]]---with face downward; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit.

===== Translation =====

**But Bali Mahārāja's chaste wife, afraid and aggrieved at seeing her husband arrested, immediately offered obeisances to Lord Vāmanadeva [Upendra]. She folded her hands and spoke as follows.**

===== Purport =====

Although Lord Brahmā was speaking, he had to stop for a while because Bali Mahārāja's wife, Vindhyāvali, who was very agitated and afraid, wanted to say something.
