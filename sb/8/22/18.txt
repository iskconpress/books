====== SB 8.22.18 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> tasyānuśṛṇvato rājan
> prahrādasya kṛtāñjaleḥ
> hiraṇyagarbho bhagavān
> uvāca madhusūdanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:a:anuśṛṇvataḥ]]---so that he could hear; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:p:prahrādasya]]---of Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:añjaleḥ]]---who was standing with folded hands; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyagarbhaḥ]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:m:madhusūdanam]]---unto Madhusūdana, the Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King Parīkṣit, Lord Brahmā then began to speak to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, within the hearing of Prahlāda Mahārāja, who stood nearby with folded hands.**
