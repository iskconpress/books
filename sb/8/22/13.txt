====== SB 8.22.13 ======

===== Text =====

> tam indra-senaḥ sva-pitāmahaṁ śriyā
> virājamānaṁ nalināyatekṣaṇam
> prāṁśuṁ piśaṅgāmbaram añjana-tviṣaṁ
> pralamba-bāhuṁ śubhagarṣabham aikṣata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---that Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:i:indra]]-[[synonyms:s:senaḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja, who now possessed all the military force of Indra; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:p:pitāmaham]]---unto his grandfather; [[synonyms:ś:śriyā]]---present with all beautiful features; [[synonyms:v:virājamānam]]---standing there; [[synonyms:n:nalina]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyata]]-[[synonyms:ī:īkṣaṇam]]---with eyes as broad as the petals of a lotus; [[synonyms:p:prāṁśum]]---a very beautiful body; [[synonyms:p:piśaṅga]]-[[synonyms:a:ambaram]]---dressed in yellow garments; [[synonyms:a:añjana]]-[[synonyms:t:tviṣam]]---with his body resembling black ointment for the eyes; [[synonyms:p:pralamba]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhum]]---very long arms; [[synonyms:ś:śubhaga]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabham]]---the best of all auspicious persons; [[synonyms:a:aikṣata]]---he saw.

===== Translation =====

**Then Bali Mahārāja saw his grandfather Prahlāda Mahārāja, the most fortunate personality, whose dark body resembled black ointment for the eyes. His tall, elegant figure was dressed in yellow garments, he had long arms, and his beautiful eyes were like the petals of a lotus. He was very dear and pleasing to everyone.**
