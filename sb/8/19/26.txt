====== SB 8.19.26 ======

===== Text =====

> yadṛcchā-lābha-tuṣṭasya
> tejo viprasya vardhate
> tat praśāmyaty asantoṣād
> ambhasevāśuśukṣaṇiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadṛcchā]]-[[synonyms:l:lābha]]-[[synonyms:t:tuṣṭasya]]---who is satisfied by things obtained by the grace of God; [[synonyms:t:tejaḥ]]---the brilliant effulgence; [[synonyms:v:viprasya]]---of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vardhate]]---increases; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that (effulgence); [[synonyms:p:praśāmyati]]---is diminished; [[synonyms:a:asantoṣāt]]---because of dissatisfaction; [[synonyms:a:ambhasā]]---by pouring of water; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---as; [[synonyms:ā:āśuśukṣaṇiḥ]]---a fire.

===== Translation =====

**A brāhmaṇa who is satisfied with whatever is providentially obtained is increasingly enlightened with spiritual power, but the spiritual potency of a dissatisfied brāhmaṇa decreases, as fire diminishes in potency when water is sprinkled upon it.**
