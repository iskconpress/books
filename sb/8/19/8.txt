====== SB 8.19.8 ======

===== Text =====

> tam āyāntaṁ samālokya
> śūla-pāṇiṁ kṛtāntavat
> cintayām āsa kāla-jño
> viṣṇur māyāvināṁ varaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Hiraṇyakaśipu); [[synonyms:ā:āyāntam]]---coming forward; [[synonyms:s:samālokya]]---observing minutely; [[synonyms:ś:śūla]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṇim]]---with a trident in his hand; [[synonyms:k:kṛtānta]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---just like death personified; [[synonyms:c:cintayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---thought; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:j:jñaḥ]]---who knows the progress of time; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇuḥ]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:m:māyāvinām]]---of all kinds of mystics; [[synonyms:v:varaḥ]]---the chief.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Hiraṇyakaśipu coming forward bearing a trident in his hand like personified death, Lord Viṣṇu, the best of all mystics and the knower of the progress of time, thought as follows.**
