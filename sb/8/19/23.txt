====== SB 8.19.23 ======

===== Text =====

> sapta-dvīpādhipatayo
> nṛpā vaiṇya-gayādayaḥ
> arthaiḥ kāmair gatā nāntaṁ
> tṛṣṇāyā iti naḥ śrutam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:d:dvīpa]]-[[synonyms:a:adhipatayaḥ]]---those who are proprietors of the seven islands; [[synonyms:n:nṛpāḥ]]---such kings; [[synonyms:v:vaiṇya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---Mahārāja Pṛthu, Mahārāja Gaya and others; [[synonyms:a:arthaiḥ]]---for fulfillment of ambition; [[synonyms:k:kāmaiḥ]]---for satisfying one's desires; [[synonyms:g:gatāḥ]] [[synonyms:n:na]]---could not reach; [[synonyms:a:antam]]---the end; [[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇāyāḥ]]---of their ambitions; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---by Us; [[synonyms:ś:śrutam]]---has been heard.

===== Translation =====

**We have heard that although powerful kings like Mahārāja Pṛthu and Mahārāja Gaya achieved proprietorship over the seven dvīpas, they could not achieve satisfaction or find the end of their ambitions.**
