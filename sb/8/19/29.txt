====== SB 8.19.29 ======

===== Text =====

> viṣṇave kṣmāṁ pradāsyantam
> uśanā asureśvaram
> jānaṁś cikīrṣitaṁ viṣṇoḥ
> śiṣyaṁ prāha vidāṁ varaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṣṇave]]---unto Lord Viṣṇu (Vāmanadeva); [[synonyms:k:kṣmām]]---the land; [[synonyms:p:pradāsyantam]]---who was ready to deliver; [[synonyms:u:uśanāḥ]]---Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaram]]---unto the King of the demons (Bali Mahārāja); [[synonyms:j:jānan]]---knowing well; [[synonyms:c:cikīrṣitam]]---what was the plan; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣyam]]---unto his disciple; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---said; [[synonyms:v:vidām]] [[synonyms:v:varaḥ]]---the best of the knowers of everything.

===== Translation =====

**Understanding Lord Viṣṇu's purpose, Śukrācārya, the best of the learned, immediately spoke as follows to his disciple, who was about to offer everything to Lord Vāmanadeva.**
