====== SB 8.19.31 ======

===== Text =====

> pratiśrutaṁ tvayaitasmai
> yad anartham ajānatā
> na sādhu manye daityānāṁ
> mahān upagato 'nayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratiśrutam]]---promised; [[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by you; [[synonyms:e:etasmai]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:y:yat]] [[synonyms:a:anartham]]---which is repugnant; [[synonyms:a:ajānatā]]---by you who have no knowledge; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]---very good; [[synonyms:m:manye]]---I think; [[synonyms:d:daityānām]]---of the demons; [[synonyms:m:mahān]]---great; [[synonyms:u:upagataḥ]]---has been achieved; [[synonyms:a:anayaḥ]]---inauspiciousness.

===== Translation =====

**You do not know what a dangerous position you have accepted by promising to give Him land. I do not think that this promise is good for you. It will bring great harm to the demons.**
