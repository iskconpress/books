====== SB 8.19.15 ======

===== Text =====

> bhavān ācaritān dharmān
> āsthito gṛhamedhibhiḥ
> brāhmaṇaiḥ pūrvajaiḥ śūrair
> anyaiś coddāma-kīrtibhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---your good self; [[synonyms:ā:ācaritān]]---executed; [[synonyms:d:dharmān]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:ā:āsthitaḥ]]---being situated; [[synonyms:g:gṛhamedhibhiḥ]]---by persons in household life; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇaiḥ]]---by the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:j:jaiḥ]]---by your forefathers; [[synonyms:ś:śūraiḥ]]---by great heroes; [[synonyms:a:anyaiḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and others also; [[synonyms:u:uddāma]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtibhiḥ]]---very highly elevated and famous.

===== Translation =====

**You also have observed the principles followed by great personalities who are householder brāhmaṇas, by your forefathers and by great heroes who are extremely famous for their exalted activities.**
