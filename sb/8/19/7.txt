====== SB 8.19.7 ======

===== Text =====

> niśamya tad-vadhaṁ bhrātā
> hiraṇyakaśipuḥ purā
> hantuṁ bhrātṛ-haṇaṁ kruddho
> jagāma nilayaṁ hareḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niśamya]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:v:vadham]]---the killing of Hiraṇyākṣa; [[synonyms:b:bhrātā]]---the brother; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyakaśipuḥ]]---Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:p:purā]]---formerly; [[synonyms:h:hantum]]---just to kill; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛ]]-[[synonyms:h:haṇam]]---the killer of his brother; [[synonyms:k:kruddhaḥ]]---very angry; [[synonyms:j:jagāma]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nilayam]]---to the residence; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**When Hiraṇyakaśipu heard the news of his brother's being killed, with great anger he went to the residence of Viṣṇu, the killer of his brother, wanting to kill Lord Viṣṇu.**
