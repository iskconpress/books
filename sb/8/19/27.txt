====== SB 8.19.27 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmāt trīṇi padāny eva
> vṛṇe tvad varadarṣabhāt
> etāvataiva siddho 'haṁ
> vittaṁ yāvat prayojanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---because of being satisfied by things easily obtained; [[synonyms:t:trīṇi]]---three; [[synonyms:p:padāni]]---steps; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vṛṇe]]---I ask; [[synonyms:t:tvat]]---from your good self; [[synonyms:v:varada]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabhāt]]---who are a munificent benedictor; [[synonyms:e:etāvatā]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---merely by such an endowment; [[synonyms:s:siddhaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I shall feel full satisfaction; [[synonyms:v:vittam]]---achievement; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as far as; [[synonyms:p:prayojanam]]---is needed.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore, O King, from you, the best of those who give charity, I ask only three paces of land. By such a gift I shall be very pleased, for the way of happiness is to be fully satisfied to receive that which is absolutely needed.**
