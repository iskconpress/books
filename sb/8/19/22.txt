====== SB 8.19.22 ======

===== Text =====

> tribhiḥ kramair asantuṣṭo
> dvīpenāpi na pūryate
> nava-varṣa-sametena
> sapta-dvīpa-varecchayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tribhiḥ]]---three; [[synonyms:k:kramaiḥ]]---by steps; [[synonyms:a:asantuṣṭaḥ]]---one who is dissatisfied; [[synonyms:d:dvīpena]]---by a complete island; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:p:pūryate]]---cannot be satisfied; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:sametena]]---even by possessing nine //varṣas//; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:d:dvīpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vara]]-[[synonyms:i:icchayā]]---by the desire to take possession of seven islands.

===== Translation =====

**If I were not satisfied with three paces of land, then surely I would not be satisfied even with possessing one of the seven islands, consisting of nine varṣas. Even if I possessed one island, I would hope to get others.**
