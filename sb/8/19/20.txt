====== SB 8.19.20 ======

===== Text =====

> na pumān mām upavrajya
> bhūyo yācitum arhati
> tasmād vṛttikarīṁ bhūmiṁ
> vaṭo kāmaṁ pratīccha me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pumān]]---any person; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---unto me; [[synonyms:u:upavrajya]]---after approaching; [[synonyms:b:bhūyaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:y:yācitum]]---to beg; [[synonyms:a:arhati]]---deserves; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:vṛtti]]-[[synonyms:k:karīm]]---suitable to maintain Yourself; [[synonyms:b:bhūmim]]---such land; [[synonyms:v:vaṭo]]---O small //brahmacārī//; [[synonyms:k:kāmam]]---according to the necessities of life; [[synonyms:p:pratīccha]]---take; [[synonyms:m:me]]---from me.

===== Translation =====

**O small boy, one who approaches me to beg something should not have to ask anything more, anywhere. Therefore, if You wish, You may ask from me as much land as will suffice to maintain You according to Your needs.**
