====== SB 8.19.36 ======

===== Text =====

> na tad dānaṁ praśaṁsanti
> yena vṛttir vipadyate
> dānaṁ yajñas tapaḥ karma
> loke vṛttimato yataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dānam]]---charity; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsanti]]---the saintly persons praise; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:v:vṛttiḥ]]---one's livelihood; [[synonyms:v:vipadyate]]---becomes endangered; [[synonyms:d:dānam]]---charity; [[synonyms:y:yajñaḥ]]---sacrifice; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerity; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---fruitive activities; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---in this world; [[synonyms:v:vṛttimataḥ]]---according to one's means of livelihood; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---as it is so.

===== Translation =====

**Learned scholars do not praise that charity which endangers one's own livelihood. Charity, sacrifice, austerity and fruitive activities are possible for one who is competent to earn his livelihood properly. [They are not possible for one who cannot maintain himself.]**
