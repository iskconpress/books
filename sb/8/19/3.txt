====== SB 8.19.3 ======

===== Text =====

> na hy etasmin kule kaścin
> niḥsattvaḥ kṛpaṇaḥ pumān
> pratyākhyātā pratiśrutya
> yo vādātā dvijātaye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:e:etasmin]]---in this; [[synonyms:k:kule]]---in the dynasty or family; [[synonyms:k:kaścit]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:niḥsattvaḥ]]---poor-minded; [[synonyms:k:kṛpaṇaḥ]]---miser; [[synonyms:p:pumān]]---any person; [[synonyms:p:pratyākhyātā]]---refuses; [[synonyms:p:pratiśrutya]]---after promising to give; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:a:adātā]]---not being charitable; [[synonyms:d:dvijātaye]]---unto the //brāhmaṇas.//

===== Translation =====

**I know that even until now, no one taking birth in your family has been poor-minded or miserly. No one has refused to give charity to brāhmaṇas, nor after promising to give charity has anyone failed to fulfill his promise.**
