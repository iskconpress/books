====== SB 8.11.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> atho surāḥ pratyupalabdha-cetasaḥ
> parasya puṁsaḥ parayānukampayā
> jaghnur bhṛśaṁ śakra-samīraṇādayas
> tāṁs tān raṇe yair abhisaṁhatāḥ purā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:a:atho]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:surāḥ]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:p:pratyupalabdha]]-[[synonyms:c:cetasaḥ]]---being enlivened again by revival of their consciousness; [[synonyms:p:parasya]]---of the Supreme; [[synonyms:p:puṁsaḥ]]---of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:parayā]]---supreme; [[synonyms:a:anukampayā]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:j:jaghnuḥ]]---began to beat; [[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---again and again; [[synonyms:ś:śakra]]---Indra; [[synonyms:s:samīraṇa]]---Vāyu; [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and others; [[synonyms:t:tān]] [[synonyms:t:tān]]---to those demons; [[synonyms:r:raṇe]]---in the fight; [[synonyms:y:yaiḥ]]---by whom; [[synonyms:a:abhisaṁhatāḥ]]---they were beaten; [[synonyms:p:purā]]---before.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Thereafter, by the supreme grace of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Hari, all the demigods, headed by Indra and Vāyu, were brought back to life. Being enlivened, the demigods began severely beating the very same demons who had defeated them before.**
