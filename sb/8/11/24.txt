====== SB 8.11.24 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvataḥ śara-kūṭena
> śakraṁ saratha-sārathim
> chādayām āsur asurāḥ
> prāvṛṭ-sūryam ivāmbudāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---all around; [[synonyms:ś:śara]]-[[synonyms:k:kūṭena]]---by a dense shower of arrows; [[synonyms:ś:śakram]]---Indra; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:r:ratha]]---with his chariot; [[synonyms:s:sārathim]]---and with his chariot driver; [[synonyms:c:chādayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsuḥ]]---covered; [[synonyms:a:asurāḥ]]---all the demons; [[synonyms:p:prāvṛṭ]]---in the rainy season; [[synonyms:s:sūryam]]---the sun; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:a:ambu]]-[[synonyms:d:dāḥ]]---clouds.

===== Translation =====

**Other demons covered Indra, along with his chariot and chariot driver, with incessant showers of arrows, just as clouds cover the sun in the rainy season.**
