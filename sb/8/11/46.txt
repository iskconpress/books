====== SB 8.11.46 ======

===== Text =====

> ye 'vaśiṣṭā raṇe tasmin
> nāradānumatena te
> baliṁ vipannam ādāya
> astaṁ girim upāgaman

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]---some of the demons who; [[synonyms:a:avaśiṣṭāḥ]]---remained; [[synonyms:r:raṇe]]---in the fight; [[synonyms:t:tasmin]]---in that; [[synonyms:n:nārada]]-[[synonyms:a:anumatena]]---by the order of Nārada; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:balim]]---Mahārāja Bali; [[synonyms:v:vipannam]]---in reverses; [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---taking; [[synonyms:a:astam]]---named Asta; [[synonyms:g:girim]]---to the mountain; [[synonyms:u:upāgaman]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**Following the order of Nārada Muni, whatever demons remained on the battlefield took Bali Mahārāja, who was in a precarious condition, to the hill known as Astagiri.**
