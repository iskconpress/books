====== SB 8.11.15 ======

===== Text =====

> gadā-prahāra-vyathito
> bhṛśaṁ vihvalito gajaḥ
> jānubhyāṁ dharaṇīṁ spṛṣṭvā
> kaśmalaṁ paramaṁ yayau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gadā]]-[[synonyms:p:prahāra]]-[[synonyms:v:vyathitaḥ]]---being aggrieved because of the blow from Jambhāsura's club; [[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---very much; [[synonyms:v:vihvalitaḥ]]---upset; [[synonyms:g:gajaḥ]]---the elephant; [[synonyms:j:jānubhyām]]---with its two knees; [[synonyms:d:dharaṇīm]]---the earth; [[synonyms:s:spṛṣṭvā]]---touching; [[synonyms:k:kaśmalam]]---unconsciousness; [[synonyms:p:paramam]]---ultimate; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---entered.

===== Translation =====

**Being beaten by Jambhāsura's club, Indra's elephant was confused and aggrieved. Thus it touched its knees to the ground and fell unconscious.**
