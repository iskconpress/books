====== SB 8.11.17 ======

===== Text =====

> tasya tat pūjayan karma
> yantur dānava-sattamaḥ
> śūlena jvalatā taṁ tu
> smayamāno 'hanan mṛdhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Mātali; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that service (bringing the chariot before Indra); [[synonyms:p:pūjayan]]---appreciating; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---such service to the master; [[synonyms:y:yantuḥ]]---of the chariot driver; [[synonyms:d:dānava]]-[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---the best of the demons, namely Jambhāsura; [[synonyms:ś:śūlena]]---by his trident; [[synonyms:j:jvalatā]]---which was blazing fire; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---Mātali; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:smayamānaḥ]]---smiling; [[synonyms:a:ahanat]]---struck; [[synonyms:m:mṛdhe]]---in the battle.

===== Translation =====

**Appreciating Mātali's service, Jambhāsura, the best of the demons, smiled. Nonetheless, he struck Mātali in the battle with a trident of blazing fire.**
