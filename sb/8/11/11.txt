====== SB 8.11.11 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ nirākṛto devo
> vairiṇā tathya-vādinā
> nāmṛṣyat tad-adhikṣepaṁ
> totrāhata iva dvipaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:n:nirākṛtaḥ]]---being defeated; [[synonyms:d:devaḥ]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:v:vairiṇā]]---by his enemy; [[synonyms:t:tathya]]-[[synonyms:v:vādinā]]---who was competent to speak the truth; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:amṛṣyat]]---lamented; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of him (Bali); [[synonyms:a:adhikṣepam]]---the chastisement; [[synonyms:t:totra]]---by the scepter or rod; [[synonyms:ā:āhataḥ]]---being beaten; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---just like; [[synonyms:d:dvipaḥ]]---an elephant.

===== Translation =====

**Since Mahārāja Bali's rebukes were truthful, King Indra did not at all become sorry, just as an elephant beaten by its driver's rod does not become agitated.**
