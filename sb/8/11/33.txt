====== SB 8.11.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmād indro 'bibhec chatror
> vajraḥ pratihato yataḥ
> kim idaṁ daiva-yogena
> bhūtaṁ loka-vimohanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---therefore; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the King of heaven; [[synonyms:a:abibhet]]---became very fearful; [[synonyms:ś:śatroḥ]]---from the enemy (Namuci); [[synonyms:v:vajraḥ]]---the thunderbolt; [[synonyms:p:pratihataḥ]]---was unable to hit and returned; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---because; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:i:idam]]---what is this; [[synonyms:d:daiva]]-[[synonyms:y:yogena]]---by some superior force; [[synonyms:b:bhūtam]]---it has happened; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:v:vimohanam]]---so wonderful to the people in general.

===== Translation =====

**When Indra saw the thunderbolt return from the enemy, he was very much afraid. He began to wonder whether this had happened because of some miraculous superior power.**

===== Purport =====

Indra's thunderbolt is invincible, and therefore when Indra saw that it had returned without doing any injury to Namuci, he was certainly very much afraid.
