====== SB 8.11.20 ======

===== Text =====

> vacobhiḥ paruṣair indram
> ardayanto 'sya marmasu
> śarair avākiran meghā
> dhārābhir iva parvatam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vacobhiḥ]]---with harsh words; [[synonyms:p:paruṣaiḥ]]---very rough and cruel; [[synonyms:i:indram]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:a:ardayantaḥ]]---chastising, piercing; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of Indra; [[synonyms:m:marmasu]]---in the heart, etc.; [[synonyms:ś:śaraiḥ]]---with arrows; [[synonyms:a:avākiran]]---covered all around; [[synonyms:m:meghāḥ]]---clouds; [[synonyms:d:dhārābhiḥ]]---with showers of rain; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---just as; [[synonyms:p:parvatam]]---a mountain.

===== Translation =====

**Rebuking Indra with harsh, cruel words that were piercing to the heart, these demons showered him with arrows, just as torrents of rain wash a great mountain.**
