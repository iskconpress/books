====== SB 8.11.21 ======

===== Text =====

> harīn daśa-śatāny ājau
> haryaśvasya balaḥ śaraiḥ
> tāvadbhir ardayām āsa
> yugapal laghu-hastavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:harīn]]---horses; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śatāni]]---ten times one hundred (one thousand); [[synonyms:ā:ājau]]---on the battlefield; [[synonyms:h:haryaśvasya]]---of King Indra; [[synonyms:b:balaḥ]]---the demon Bala; [[synonyms:ś:śaraiḥ]]---with arrows; [[synonyms:t:tāvadbhiḥ]]---with so many; [[synonyms:a:ardayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---put into tribulation; [[synonyms:y:yugapat]]---simultaneously; [[synonyms:l:laghu]]-[[synonyms:h:hastavān]]---with quick handling.

===== Translation =====

**Quickly handling the situation on the battlefield, the demon Bala put all of Indra's one thousand horses into tribulation by simultaneously piercing them all with an equal number of arrows.**
