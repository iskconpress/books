====== SB 8.11.32 ======

===== Text =====

> na tasya hi tvacam api vajra ūrjito
> bibheda yaḥ sura-patinaujaseritaḥ
> tad adbhutaṁ param ativīrya-vṛtra-bhit
> tiraskṛto namuci-śirodhara-tvacā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Namuci); [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:t:tvacam]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even the skin; [[synonyms:v:vajraḥ]]---the thunderbolt; [[synonyms:ū:ūrjitaḥ]]---very powerful; [[synonyms:b:bibheda]]---could pierce; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---the weapon which; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:p:patinā]]---by the king of the demigods; [[synonyms:o:ojasā]]---very forcefully; [[synonyms:ī:īritaḥ]]---had been released; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:adbhutam]] [[synonyms:p:param]]---it was extraordinarily wonderful; [[synonyms:a:ativīrya]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛtra]]-[[synonyms:b:bhit]]---so powerful that it could pierce the body of the very powerful Vṛtrāsura; [[synonyms:t:tiraskṛtaḥ]]---(now in the future) which had been repelled; [[synonyms:n:namuci]]-[[synonyms:ś:śirodhara]]-[[synonyms:t:tvacā]]---by the skin of Namuci's neck.

===== Translation =====

**Although King Indra hurled his thunderbolt at Namuci with great force, it could not even pierce his skin. It is very wonderful that the famed thunderbolt that had pierced the body of Vṛtrāsura could not even slightly injure the skin of Namuci's neck.**
