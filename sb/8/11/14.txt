====== SB 8.11.14 ======

===== Text =====

> sa siṁha-vāha āsādya
> gadām udyamya raṁhasā
> jatrāv atāḍayac chakraṁ
> gajaṁ ca sumahā-balaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Jambhāsura; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāhaḥ]]---being carried by a lion; [[synonyms:ā:āsādya]]---coming before King Indra; [[synonyms:g:gadām]]---his club; [[synonyms:u:udyamya]]---taking up; [[synonyms:r:raṁhasā]]---with great force; [[synonyms:j:jatrau]]---on the base of the neck; [[synonyms:a:atāḍayat]]---hit; [[synonyms:ś:śakram]]---Indra; [[synonyms:g:gajam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as well as his elephant; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:balaḥ]]---the greatly powerful Jambhāsura.

===== Translation =====

**The greatly powerful Jambhāsura, carried by a lion, approached Indra and forcefully struck him on the shoulder with his club. He also struck Indra's elephant.**
