====== SB 8.11.45 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> saṁyamya manyu-saṁrambhaṁ
> mānayanto muner vacaḥ
> upagīyamānānucarair
> yayuḥ sarve triviṣṭapam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:s:saṁyamya]]---controlling; [[synonyms:m:manyu]]---of anger; [[synonyms:s:saṁrambham]]---the aggravation; [[synonyms:m:mānayantaḥ]]---accepting; [[synonyms:m:muneḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vacaḥ]]---the words of Nārada Muni; [[synonyms:u:upagīyamāna]]---being praised; [[synonyms:a:anucaraiḥ]]---by their followers; [[synonyms:y:yayuḥ]]---returned; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of the demigods; [[synonyms:t:triviṣṭapam]]---to the heavenly planets.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Accepting the words of Nārada, the demigods gave up their anger and stopped fighting. Being praised by their followers, they returned to their heavenly planets.**
