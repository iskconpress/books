====== SB 8.11.23 ======

===== Text =====

> namuciḥ pañca-daśabhiḥ
> svarṇa-puṅkhair maheṣubhiḥ
> āhatya vyanadat saṅkhye
> satoya iva toyadaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namuciḥ]]---the demon named Namuci; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:d:daśabhiḥ]]---with fifteen; [[synonyms:s:svarṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:puṅkhaiḥ]]---with golden feathers attached; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:i:iṣubhiḥ]]---very powerful arrows; [[synonyms:ā:āhatya]]---piercing; [[synonyms:v:vyanadat]]---resounded; [[synonyms:s:saṅkhye]]---on the battlefield; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:t:toyaḥ]]---bearing water; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:t:toya]]-[[synonyms:d:daḥ]]---a cloud that delivers rain.

===== Translation =====

**Then Namuci, another demon, attacked Indra and injured him with fifteen very powerful golden-feathered arrows, which roared like a cloud full of water.**
