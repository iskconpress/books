====== SB 8.11.42 ======

===== Text =====

> anye 'py evaṁ pratidvandvān
> vāyv-agni-varuṇādayaḥ
> sūdayām āsur asurān
> mṛgān kesariṇo yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anye]]---others; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:pratidvandvān]]---the opposing party of belligerants; [[synonyms:v:vāyu]]---the demigod known as Vāyu; [[synonyms:a:agni]]---the demigod known as Agni; [[synonyms:v:varuṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the demigod known as Varuṇa and others; [[synonyms:s:sūdayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsuḥ]]---began to kill vigorously; [[synonyms:a:asurān]]---all the demons; [[synonyms:m:mṛgān]]---deer; [[synonyms:k:kesariṇaḥ]]---lions; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as.

===== Translation =====

**Vāyu, Agni, Varuṇa and other demigods began killing the demons who opposed them, just as lions kill deer in a forest.**
