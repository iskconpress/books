====== SB 8.11.29 ======

===== Text =====

> namucis tad-vadhaṁ dṛṣṭvā
> śokāmarṣa-ruṣānvitaḥ
> jighāṁsur indraṁ nṛpate
> cakāra paramodyamam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namuciḥ]]---the demon Namuci; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of those two demons; [[synonyms:v:vadham]]---the massacre; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:ś:śoka]]-[[synonyms:a:amarṣa]]---lamentation and grief; [[synonyms:r:ruṣā]]-[[synonyms:a:anvitaḥ]]---being very angry at this; [[synonyms:j:jighāṁsuḥ]]---wanted to kill; [[synonyms:i:indram]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:n:nṛ]]-[[synonyms:p:pate]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:c:cakāra]]---made; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---a great; [[synonyms:u:udyamam]]---endeavor.

===== Translation =====

**O King, when Namuci, another demon, saw the killing of both Bala and Pāka, he was full of grief and lamentation. Thus he angrily made a great attempt to kill Indra.**
