====== SB 8.11.36 ======

===== Text =====

> so 'yaṁ pratihato vajro
> mayā mukto 'sure 'lpake
> nāhaṁ tad ādade daṇḍaṁ
> brahma-tejo 'py akāraṇam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]] [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---therefore, this thunderbolt; [[synonyms:p:pratihataḥ]]---repelled; [[synonyms:v:vajraḥ]]---thunderbolt; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:m:muktaḥ]]---released; [[synonyms:a:asure]]---unto that demon; [[synonyms:a:alpake]]---less important; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:ādade]]---hold; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍam]]---it is now just like a rod; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:t:tejaḥ]]---as powerful as a //brahmāstra//; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:a:akāraṇam]]---now it is useless.

===== Translation =====

**But now, although the same thunderbolt has been released against a less important demon, it has been ineffectual. Therefore, although it was as good as a brahmāstra, it has now become useless like an ordinary rod. I shall therefore hold it no longer.**
