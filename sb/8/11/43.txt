====== SB 8.11.43 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmaṇā preṣito devān
> devarṣir nārado nṛpa
> vārayām āsa vibudhān
> dṛṣṭvā dānava-saṅkṣayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmaṇā]]---by Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:p:preṣitaḥ]]---sent; [[synonyms:d:devān]]---unto the demigods; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---the great sage of the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]]---Nārada Muni; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:v:vārayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---forbade; [[synonyms:v:vibudhān]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:d:dānava]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣayam]]---the total annihilation of the demons.

===== Translation =====

**O King, when Lord Brahmā saw the imminent total annihilation of the demons, he sent a message with Nārada, who went before the demigods to make them stop fighting.**
