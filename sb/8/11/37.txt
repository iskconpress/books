====== SB 8.11.37 ======

===== Text =====

> iti śakraṁ viṣīdantam
> āha vāg aśarīriṇī
> nāyaṁ śuṣkair atho nārdrair
> vadham arhati dānavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ś:śakram]]---unto Indra; [[synonyms:v:viṣīdantam]]---lamenting; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---spoke; [[synonyms:v:vāk]]---a voice; [[synonyms:a:aśarīriṇī]]---without any body, or from the sky; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣkaiḥ]]---by anything dry; [[synonyms:a:atho]]---also; [[synonyms:n:na]]---nor; [[synonyms:ā:ārdraiḥ]]---by anything moist; [[synonyms:v:vadham]]---annihilation; [[synonyms:a:arhati]]---is befitting; [[synonyms:d:dānavaḥ]]---this demon (Namuci).

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: While the morose Indra was lamenting in this way, an ominous, unembodied voice said from the sky, "This demon Namuci is not to be annihilated by anything dry or moist."**
