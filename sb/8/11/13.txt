====== SB 8.11.13 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhāyaṁ patitaṁ dṛṣṭvā
> jambho bali-sakhaḥ suhṛt
> abhyayāt sauhṛdaṁ sakhyur
> hatasyāpi samācaran

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhāyam]]---his intimate friend; [[synonyms:p:patitam]]---having fallen; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:j:jambhaḥ]]---the demon Jambha; [[synonyms:b:bali]]-[[synonyms:s:sakhaḥ]]---a very intimate friend of Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:s:suhṛt]]---and constant well-wisher; [[synonyms:a:abhyayāt]]---appeared on the scene; [[synonyms:s:sauhṛdam]]---very compassionate friendship; [[synonyms:s:sakhyuḥ]]---of his friend; [[synonyms:h:hatasya]]---who was injured and fallen; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:s:samācaran]]---just to perform friendly duties.

===== Translation =====

**When the demon Jambhāsura saw that his friend Bali had fallen, he appeared before Indra, the enemy, just to serve Bali Mahārāja with friendly behavior.**
