====== SB 8.11.38 ======

===== Text =====

> mayāsmai yad varo datto
> mṛtyur naivārdra-śuṣkayoḥ
> ato 'nyaś cintanīyas te
> upāyo maghavan ripoḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:a:asmai]]---unto him; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:v:varaḥ]]---a benediction; [[synonyms:d:dattaḥ]]---has been granted; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyuḥ]]---death; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ā:ārdra]]---by either a moist; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣkayoḥ]]---or by a dry medium; [[synonyms:a:ataḥ]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:anyaḥ]]---something else, another; [[synonyms:c:cintanīyaḥ]]---has to be thought of; [[synonyms:t:te]]---by you; [[synonyms:u:upāyaḥ]]---means; [[synonyms:m:maghavan]]---O Indra; [[synonyms:r:ripoḥ]]---of your enemy.

===== Translation =====

**The voice also said, "O Indra, because I have given this demon the benediction that he will never be killed by any weapon that is dry or moist, you have to think of another way to kill him."**
