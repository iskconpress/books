====== SB 8.11.19 ======

===== Text =====

> jambhaṁ śrutvā hataṁ tasya
> jñātayo nāradād ṛṣeḥ
> namuciś ca balaḥ pākas
> tatrāpetus tvarānvitāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jambham]]---Jambhāsura; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:h:hatam]]---had been killed; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:j:jñātayaḥ]]---friends and relatives; [[synonyms:n:nāradāt]]---from the source Nārada; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣeḥ]]---from the great saint; [[synonyms:n:namuciḥ]]---the demon Namuci; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:b:balaḥ]]---the demon Bala; [[synonyms:p:pākaḥ]]---the demon Pāka; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āpetuḥ]]---immediately arrived; [[synonyms:t:tvarā]]-[[synonyms:a:anvitāḥ]]---with great haste.

===== Translation =====

**When Nārada Ṛṣi informed Jambhāsura's friends and relatives that Jambhāsura had been killed, the three demons named Namuci, Bala and Pāka arrived on the battlefield in great haste.**
