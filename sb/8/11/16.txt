====== SB 8.11.16 ======

===== Text =====

> tato ratho mātalinā
> haribhir daśa-śatair vṛtaḥ
> ānīto dvipam utsṛjya
> ratham āruruhe vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:r:rathaḥ]]---chariot; [[synonyms:m:mātalinā]]---by his chariot driver named Mātali; [[synonyms:h:haribhiḥ]]---with horses; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śataiḥ]]---by ten times one hundred (one thousand); [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]]---yoked; [[synonyms:ā:ānītaḥ]]---being brought in; [[synonyms:d:dvipam]]---the elephant; [[synonyms:u:utsṛjya]]---keeping aside; [[synonyms:r:ratham]]---the chariot; [[synonyms:ā:āruruhe]]---got up; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the great Indra.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Mātali, Indra's chariot driver, brought Indra's chariot, which was drawn by one thousand horses. Indra then left his elephant and got onto the chariot.**
