====== SB 8.4.5 ======

===== Text =====

> so 'nukampita īśena
> parikramya praṇamya tam
> lokasya paśyato lokaṁ
> svam agān mukta-kilbiṣaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (King Hūhū); [[synonyms:a:anukampitaḥ]]---being favored; [[synonyms:ī:īśena]]---by the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:p:parikramya]]---circumambulating; [[synonyms:p:praṇamya]]---offering his obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:l:lokasya]]---all the demigods and men; [[synonyms:p:paśyataḥ]]---while seeing; [[synonyms:l:lokam]]---to the planet; [[synonyms:s:svam]]---his own; [[synonyms:a:agāt]]---went back; [[synonyms:m:mukta]]---being delivered; [[synonyms:k:kilbiṣaḥ]]---from the reactions of his sin.

===== Translation =====

**Having been favored by the causeless mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead and having regained his original form, King Hūhū circumambulated the Lord and offered his obeisances. Then, in the presence of all the demigods, headed by Brahmā, he returned to Gandharvaloka. He had been freed of all sinful reactions.**
