====== SB 8.4.2 ======

===== Text =====

> nedur dundubhayo divyā
> gandharvā nanṛtur jaguḥ
> ṛṣayaś cāraṇāḥ siddhās
> tuṣṭuvuḥ puruṣottamam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:neduḥ]]---vibrated; [[synonyms:d:dundubhayaḥ]]---kettledrums; [[synonyms:d:divyāḥ]]---in the sky of the higher planetary system; [[synonyms:g:gandharvāḥ]]---residents of Gandharvaloka; [[synonyms:n:nanṛtuḥ]]---danced; [[synonyms:j:jaguḥ]]---and sang; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---all the saintly sages; [[synonyms:c:cāraṇāḥ]]---the inhabitants of the Cāraṇa planet; [[synonyms:s:siddhāḥ]]---the inhabitants of the Siddha planet; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭuvuḥ]]---offered prayers; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:u:uttamam]]---to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Puruṣottama, the best of males.

===== Translation =====

**There was a beating of kettledrums in the heavenly planets, the inhabitants of Gandharvaloka began to dance and sing, while great sages and the inhabitants of Cāraṇaloka and Siddhaloka offered prayers to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Puruṣottama.**
