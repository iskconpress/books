====== SB 8.4.16 ======

===== Text =====

> idam āha hariḥ prīto
> gajendraṁ kuru-sattama
> śṛṇvatāṁ sarva-bhūtānāṁ
> sarva-bhūta-mayo vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prītaḥ]]---being pleased; [[synonyms:g:gajendram]]---unto Gajendra; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---O best of the Kuru dynasty; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇvatām]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtānām]]---in the presence of everyone; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:m:mayaḥ]]---all-pervading Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the great.

===== Translation =====

**O best of the Kuru dynasty, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the Supersoul of everyone, being thus pleased, addressed Gajendra in the presence of everyone there. He spoke the following blessings.**
