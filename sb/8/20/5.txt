====== SB 8.20.5 ======

===== Text =====

> nāhaṁ bibhemi nirayān
> nādhanyād asukhārṇavāt
> na sthāna-cyavanān mṛtyor
> yathā vipra-pralambhanāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bibhemi]]---am afraid of; [[synonyms:n:nirayāt]]---from a hellish condition of life; [[synonyms:n:na]]---nor; [[synonyms:a:adhanyāt]]---from a poverty-stricken condition; [[synonyms:a:asukha]]-[[synonyms:a:arṇavāt]]---nor from an ocean of distresses; [[synonyms:n:na]]---nor; [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]-[[synonyms:c:cyavanāt]]---from falling from a position; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyoḥ]]---nor from death; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:p:pralambhanāt]]---from the cheating of a //brāhmaṇa.//

===== Translation =====

**I do not fear hell, poverty, an ocean of distress, falldown from my position or even death itself as much as I fear cheating a brāhmaṇa.**
