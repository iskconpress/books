====== SB 8.20.16 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ śaptaḥ sva-guruṇā
> satyān na calito mahān
> vāmanāya dadāv enām
> arcitvodaka-pūrvakam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ś:śaptaḥ]]---being cursed; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:guruṇā]]---by his own spiritual master; [[synonyms:s:satyāt]]---from truthfulness; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:c:calitaḥ]]---who moved; [[synonyms:m:mahān]]---the great personality; [[synonyms:v:vāmanāya]]---unto Lord Vāmanadeva; [[synonyms:d:dadau]]---gave in charity; [[synonyms:e:enām]]---all the land; [[synonyms:a:arcitvā]]---after worshiping; [[synonyms:u:udaka]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrvakam]]---preceded by offering of water.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: Even after being cursed in this way by his own spiritual master, Bali Mahārāja, being a great personality, never deviated from his determination. Therefore, according to custom, he first offered water to Vāmanadeva and then offered Him the gift of land he had promised.**
