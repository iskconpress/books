====== SB 8.20.18 ======

===== Text =====

> yajamānaḥ svayaṁ tasya
> śrīmat pāda-yugaṁ mudā
> avanijyāvahan mūrdhni
> tad apo viśva-pāvanīḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yajamānaḥ]]---the worshiper (Bali Mahārāja); [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Lord Vāmanadeva; [[synonyms:ś:śrīmat]] [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:y:yugam]]---the most auspicious and beautiful pair of lotus feet; [[synonyms:m:mudā]]---with great jubilation; [[synonyms:a:avanijya]]---properly washing; [[synonyms:a:avahat]]---took; [[synonyms:m:mūrdhni]]---on his head; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:a:apaḥ]]---water; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:p:pāvanīḥ]]---which gives liberation to the whole universe.

===== Translation =====

**Bali Mahārāja, the worshiper of Lord Vāmanadeva, jubilantly washed the Lord's lotus feet and then took the water on his head, for that water delivers the entire universe.**
