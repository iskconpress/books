====== SB 8.20.17 ======

===== Text =====

> vindhyāvalis tadāgatya
> patnī jālaka-mālinī
> āninye kalaśaṁ haimam
> avanejany-apāṁ bhṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vindhyāvaliḥ]]---Vindhyāvali; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ā:āgatya]]---coming there; [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---the wife of Mahārāja Bali; [[synonyms:j:jālaka]]-[[synonyms:m:mālinī]]---decorated with a necklace of pearls; [[synonyms:ā:āninye]]---caused to be brought; [[synonyms:k:kalaśam]]---a waterpot; [[synonyms:h:haimam]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:a:avanejani]]-[[synonyms:a:apām]]---with water for the sake of washing the Lord's feet; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtam]]---filled.

===== Translation =====

**Bali Mahārāja's wife, known as Vindhyāvali, who was decorated with a necklace of pearls, immediately came and had a large golden waterpot brought there, full of water with which to worship the Lord by washing His feet.**
