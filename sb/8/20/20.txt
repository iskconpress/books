====== SB 8.20.20 ======

===== Text =====

> nedur muhur dundubhayaḥ sahasraśo
> gandharva-kimpūruṣa-kinnarā jaguḥ
> manasvinānena kṛtaṁ suduṣkaraṁ
> vidvān adād yad ripave jagat-trayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:neduḥ]]---began to beat; [[synonyms:m:muhuḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:d:dundubhayaḥ]]---trumpets and kettledrums; [[synonyms:s:sahasraśaḥ]]---by thousands and thousands; [[synonyms:g:gandharva]]---the residents of Gandharvaloka; [[synonyms:k:kimpūruṣa]]---the residents of Kimpuruṣaloka; [[synonyms:k:kinnarāḥ]]---and the residents of Kinnaraloka; [[synonyms:j:jaguḥ]]---began to sing and declare; [[synonyms:m:manasvinā]]---by the most exalted personality; [[synonyms:a:anena]]---by Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---was done; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:d:duṣkaram]]---an extremely difficult task; [[synonyms:v:vidvān]]---because of his being the most learned person; [[synonyms:a:adāt]]---gave Him a gift; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---that; [[synonyms:r:ripave]]---unto the enemy, Lord Viṣṇu, who was siding with Bali Mahārāja's enemies, the demigods; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:t:trayam]]---the three worlds.

===== Translation =====

**The Gandharvas, the Kimpuruṣas and the Kinnaras sounded thousands and thousands of kettledrums and trumpets again and again, and they sang in great jubilation, declaring, "How exalted a person is Bali Mahārāja, and what a difficult task he has performed! Even though he knew that Lord Viṣṇu was on the side of his enemies, he nonetheless gave the Lord the entire three worlds in charity."**
