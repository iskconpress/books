====== SB 8.20.31 ======

===== Text =====

> parjanya-ghoṣo jalajaḥ pāñcajanyaḥ
> kaumodakī viṣṇu-gadā tarasvinī
> vidyādharo 'siḥ śata-candra-yuktas
> tūṇottamāv akṣayasāyakau ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parjanya]]-[[synonyms:g:ghoṣaḥ]]---having a sound vibration like that of the clouds; [[synonyms:j:jalajaḥ]]---the Lord's conchshell; [[synonyms:p:pāñcajanyaḥ]]---which is known as Pāñcajanya; [[synonyms:k:kaumodakī]]---known by the name Kaumodakī; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:g:gadā]]---the club of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:t:tarasvinī]]---with great force; [[synonyms:v:vidyādharaḥ]]---named Vidyādhara; [[synonyms:a:asiḥ]]---the sword; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:y:yuktaḥ]]---with a shield decorated with hundreds of moons; [[synonyms:t:tūṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:uttamau]]---the best of quivers; [[synonyms:a:akṣayasāyakau]]---named Akṣayasāyaka; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord's conchshell, named Pāñcajanya, which made sounds like that of a cloud; the very forceful club named Kaumodakī; the sword named Vidyādhara, with a shield decorated with hundreds of moonlike spots; and also Akṣayasāyaka, the best of quivers---all of these appeared together to offer prayers to the Lord.**
