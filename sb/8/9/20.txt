====== SB 8.9.20 ======

===== Text =====

> kalpayitvā pṛthak paṅktīr
> ubhayeṣāṁ jagat-patiḥ
> tāṁś copaveśayām āsa
> sveṣu sveṣu ca paṅktiṣu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kalpayitvā]]---after arranging; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:paṅktīḥ]]---different seats; [[synonyms:u:ubhayeṣām]]---of both the demigods and the demons; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the master of the universe; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---all of them; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:u:upaveśayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---seated; [[synonyms:s:sveṣu]] [[synonyms:s:sveṣu]]---in their own places; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:p:paṅktiṣu]]---all in order.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead as Mohinī-mūrti, the master of the universe, arranged separate lines of sitting places and seated the demigods and demons according to their positions.**
