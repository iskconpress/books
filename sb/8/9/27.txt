====== SB 8.9.27 ======

===== Text =====

> pīta-prāye 'mṛte devair
> bhagavān loka-bhāvanaḥ
> paśyatām asurendrāṇāṁ
> svaṁ rūpaṁ jagṛhe hariḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:p:prāye]]---when almost finished being drunk; [[synonyms:a:amṛte]]---the nectar; [[synonyms:d:devaiḥ]]---by the demigods; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead as Mohinī-mūrti; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvanaḥ]]---the maintainer and well-wisher of the three worlds; [[synonyms:p:paśyatām]]---in the presence of; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:i:indrāṇām]]---all the demons, with their chiefs; [[synonyms:s:svam]]---own; [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---form; [[synonyms:j:jagṛhe]]---manifested; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead is the best friend and well-wisher of the three worlds. Thus when the demigods had almost finished drinking the nectar, the Lord, in the presence of all the demons, disclosed His original form.**
