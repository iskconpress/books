====== SB 8.9.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> te 'nyonyato 'surāḥ pātraṁ
> harantas tyakta-sauhṛdāḥ
> kṣipanto dasyu-dharmāṇa
> āyāntīṁ dadṛśuḥ striyam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:te]]---the demons; [[synonyms:a:anyonyataḥ]]---among themselves; [[synonyms:a:asurāḥ]]---the demons; [[synonyms:p:pātram]]---the container of nectar; [[synonyms:h:harantaḥ]]---snatching from one another; [[synonyms:t:tyakta]]-[[synonyms:s:sauhṛdāḥ]]---became inimical toward one another; [[synonyms:k:kṣipantaḥ]]---sometimes throwing; [[synonyms:d:dasyu]]-[[synonyms:d:dharmāṇaḥ]]---sometimes snatching like robbers; [[synonyms:ā:āyāntīm]]---coming forward; [[synonyms:d:dadṛśuḥ]]---saw; [[synonyms:s:striyam]]---a very beautiful and attractive woman.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Thereafter, the demons became inimical toward one another. Throwing and snatching the container of nectar, they gave up their friendly relationship. Meanwhile, they saw a very beautiful young woman coming forward toward them.**
