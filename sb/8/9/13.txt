====== SB 8.9.13 ======

===== Text =====

> ity abhivyāhṛtaṁ tasyā
> ākarṇyāsura-puṅgavāḥ
> apramāṇa-vidas tasyās
> tat tathety anvamaṁsata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:abhivyāhṛtam]]---the words that were spoken; [[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]]---Her; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṇya]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:p:puṅgavāḥ]]---the chiefs of the demons; [[synonyms:a:apramāṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vidaḥ]]---because they were all foolish; [[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]]---of Her; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---those words; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---let it be so; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:anvamaṁsata]]---agreed to accept.

===== Translation =====

**The chiefs of the demons were not very expert in deciding things. Upon hearing the sweet words of Mohinī-mūrti, they immediately assented. "Yes," they answered. "What You have said is all right." Thus the demons agreed to accept Her decision.**
