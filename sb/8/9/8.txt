====== SB 8.9.8 ======

===== Text =====

> ity upāmantrito daityair
> māyā-yoṣid-vapur hariḥ
> prahasya rucirāpāṅgair
> nirīkṣann idam abravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:u:upāmantritaḥ]]---being fervently requested; [[synonyms:d:daityaiḥ]]---by the demons; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:y:yoṣit]]---the illusory woman; [[synonyms:v:vapuḥ]] [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prahasya]]---smiling; [[synonyms:r:rucira]]---beautiful; [[synonyms:a:apāṅgaiḥ]]---by exhibiting attractive feminine features; [[synonyms:n:nirīkṣan]]---looking at them; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---these words; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---said.

===== Translation =====

**Having thus been requested by the demons, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who had assumed the form of a beautiful woman, began to smile. Looking at them with attractive feminine gestures, She spoke as follows.**
