====== SB 8.9.5 ======

===== Text =====

> nūnaṁ tvaṁ vidhinā subhrūḥ
> preṣitāsi śarīriṇām
> sarvendriya-manaḥ-prītiṁ
> vidhātuṁ saghṛṇena kim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nūnam]]---indeed; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---You; [[synonyms:v:vidhinā]]---by Providence; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrūḥ]]---O You with the beautiful eyebrows; [[synonyms:p:preṣitā]]---sent; [[synonyms:a:asi]]---certainly You are so; [[synonyms:ś:śarīriṇām]]---of all embodied living entities; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:i:indriya]]---of the senses; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]---and of the mind; [[synonyms:p:prītim]]---what is pleasing; [[synonyms:v:vidhātum]]---to administer; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:ghṛṇena]]---by Your causeless mercy; [[synonyms:k:kim]]---whether.

===== Translation =====

**O beautiful girl with beautiful eyebrows, certainly Providence, by His causeless mercy, has sent You to please the senses and minds of all of us. Is this not a fact?**
