====== SB 8.6.26 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> iti devān samādiśya
> bhagavān puruṣottamaḥ
> teṣām antardadhe rājan
> svacchanda-gatir īśvaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:devān]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:s:samādiśya]]---advising; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:u:uttamaḥ]]---the best of all persons; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---from them; [[synonyms:a:antardadhe]]---disappeared; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:svacchanda]]---free; [[synonyms:g:gatiḥ]]---whose movements; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King Parīkṣit, after advising the demigods in this way, the independent Supreme Personality of Godhead, the best of all living entities, disappeared from their presence.**
