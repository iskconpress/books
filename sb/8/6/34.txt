====== SB 8.6.34 ======

===== Text =====

> dūra-bhārodvaha-śrāntāḥ
> śakra-vairocanādayaḥ
> apārayantas taṁ voḍhuṁ
> vivaśā vijahuḥ pathi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dūra]]---for a great distance; [[synonyms:b:bhāra]]-[[synonyms:u:udvaha]]---by carrying the great load; [[synonyms:ś:śrāntāḥ]]---being fatigued; [[synonyms:ś:śakra]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:v:vairocana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and Mahārāja Bali (the son of Virocana) and others; [[synonyms:a:apārayantaḥ]]---being unable; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---the mountain; [[synonyms:v:voḍhum]]---to bear; [[synonyms:v:vivaśāḥ]]---being unable; [[synonyms:v:vijahuḥ]]---gave up; [[synonyms:p:pathi]]---on the way.

===== Translation =====

**Because of conveying the great mountain for a long distance, King Indra, Mahārāja Bali and the other demigods and demons became fatigued. Being unable to carry the mountain, they left it on the way.**
