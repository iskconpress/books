====== SB 8.6.21 ======

===== Text =====

> amṛtotpādane yatnaḥ
> kriyatām avilambitam
> yasya pītasya vai jantur
> mṛtyu-grasto 'maro bhavet

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:u:utpādane]]---in generating nectar; [[synonyms:y:yatnaḥ]]---endeavor; [[synonyms:k:kriyatām]]---do; [[synonyms:a:avilambitam]]---without delay; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of which nectar; [[synonyms:p:pītasya]]---anyone who drinks; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:j:jantuḥ]]---living entity; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyu]]-[[synonyms:g:grastaḥ]]---although in imminent danger of death; [[synonyms:a:amaraḥ]]---immortal; [[synonyms:b:bhavet]]---can become.

===== Translation =====

**Immediately endeavor to produce nectar, which a person who is about to die may drink to become immortal.**
