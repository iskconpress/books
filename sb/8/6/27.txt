====== SB 8.6.27 ======

===== Text =====

> atha tasmai bhagavate
> namaskṛtya pitāmahaḥ
> bhavaś ca jagmatuḥ svaṁ svaṁ
> dhāmopeyur baliṁ surāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---after this; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:b:bhagavate]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:namaskṛtya]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahaḥ]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:b:bhavaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as well as Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:j:jagmatuḥ]]---returned; [[synonyms:s:svam]] [[synonyms:s:svam]]---to their own; [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---abodes; [[synonyms:u:upeyuḥ]]---approached; [[synonyms:b:balim]]---King Bali; [[synonyms:s:surāḥ]]---all the other demigods.

===== Translation =====

**Then Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva, after offering their respectful obeisances to the Lord, returned to their abodes. All the demigods then approached Mahārāja Bali.**
