====== SB 8.6.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> evaṁ stutaḥ sura-gaṇair
> bhagavān harir īśvaraḥ
> teṣām āvirabhūd rājan
> sahasrārkodaya-dyutiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:stutaḥ]]---being worshiped by prayers; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇaiḥ]]---by the demigods; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the vanquisher of all inauspiciousness; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the supreme controller; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---in front of Lord Brahmā and all the demigods; [[synonyms:ā:āvirabhūt]]---appeared there; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King (Parīkṣit); [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---of thousands; [[synonyms:a:arka]]---of suns; [[synonyms:u:udaya]]---like the rising; [[synonyms:d:dyutiḥ]]---His effulgence.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King Parīkṣit, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari, being thus worshiped with prayers by the demigods and Lord Brahmā, appeared before them. His bodily effulgence resembled the simultaneous rising of thousands of suns.**
