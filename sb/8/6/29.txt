====== SB 8.6.29 ======

===== Text =====

> te vairocanim āsīnaṁ
> guptaṁ cāsura-yūtha-paiḥ
> śriyā paramayā juṣṭaṁ
> jitāśeṣam upāgaman

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:te]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:v:vairocanim]]---unto Balirāja, the son of Virocana; [[synonyms:ā:āsīnam]]---sitting down; [[synonyms:g:guptam]]---well protected; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:y:yūtha]]-[[synonyms:p:paiḥ]]---by the commanders of the //asuras//; [[synonyms:ś:śriyā]]---by opulence; [[synonyms:p:paramayā]]---supreme; [[synonyms:j:juṣṭam]]---blessed; [[synonyms:j:jita]]-[[synonyms:a:aśeṣam]]---who became the proprietor of all the worlds; [[synonyms:u:upāgaman]]---approached.

===== Translation =====

**The demigods approached Bali Mahārāja, the son of Virocana, and sat down near him. Bali Mahārāja was protected by the commanders of the demons and was most opulent, having conquered all the universes.**
