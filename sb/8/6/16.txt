====== SB 8.6.16 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> evaṁ viriñcādibhir īḍitas tad
> vijñāya teṣāṁ hṛdayaṁ yathaiva
> jagāda jīmūta-gabhīrayā girā
> baddhāñjalīn saṁvṛta-sarva-kārakān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:viriñca]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---by all the demigods, headed by Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ī:īḍitaḥ]]---being worshiped; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:v:vijñāya]]---understanding the expectation; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayam]]---the core of the heart; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:j:jagāda]]---replied; [[synonyms:j:jīmūta]]-[[synonyms:g:gabhīrayā]]---like the sound of clouds; [[synonyms:g:girā]]---by words; [[synonyms:b:baddha]]-[[synonyms:a:añjalīn]]---unto the demigods, who stood with folded hands; [[synonyms:s:saṁvṛta]]---restrained; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kārakān]]---senses.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: When the Lord was thus offered prayers by the demigods, headed by Lord Brahmā, He understood the purpose for which they had approached Him. Therefore, in a deep voice that resembled the rumbling of clouds, the Lord replied to the demigods, who all stood there attentively with folded hands.**
