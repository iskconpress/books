====== SB 8.6.36 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṁs tathā bhagna-manaso
> bhagna-bāhūru-kandharān
> vijñāya bhagavāṁs tatra
> babhūva garuḍa-dhvajaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tān]]---all the demigods and demons; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:b:bhagna]]-[[synonyms:m:manasaḥ]]---being brokenhearted; [[synonyms:b:bhagna]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhu]]---with broken arms; [[synonyms:ū:ūru]]---thighs; [[synonyms:k:kandharān]]---and shoulders; [[synonyms:v:vijñāya]]---knowing; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:b:babhūva]]---appeared; [[synonyms:g:garuḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvajaḥ]]---being carried on Garuḍa.

===== Translation =====

**The demigods and demons were frustrated and disheartened, and their arms, thighs and shoulders were broken. Therefore the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who knows everything, appeared there on the back of His carrier, Garuḍa.**
