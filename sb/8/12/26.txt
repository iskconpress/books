====== SB 8.12.26 ======

===== Text =====

> sā tam āyāntam ālokya
> vivastrā vrīḍitā bhṛśam
> nilīyamānā vṛkṣeṣu
> hasantī nānvatiṣṭhata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sā]]---that woman; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ā:āyāntam]]---who was coming near; [[synonyms:ā:ālokya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vivastrā]]---She was naked; [[synonyms:v:vrīḍitā]]---very bashful; [[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---so much; [[synonyms:n:nilīyamānā]]---was hiding; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣeṣu]]---among the trees; [[synonyms:h:hasantī]]---smiling; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:anvatiṣṭhata]]---stood in one place.

===== Translation =====

**The beautiful woman was already naked, and when She saw Lord Śiva coming toward Her, She became extremely bashful. Thus She kept smiling, but She hid Herself among the trees and did not stand in one place.**
