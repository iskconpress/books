====== SB 8.12.42 ======

===== Text =====

> ātmāṁśa-bhūtāṁ tāṁ māyāṁ
> bhavānīṁ bhagavān bhavaḥ
> sammatām ṛṣi-mukhyānāṁ
> prītyācaṣṭātha bhārata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtām]]---a potency of the Supreme Soul; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---unto her; [[synonyms:m:māyām]]---the illusory energy; [[synonyms:b:bhavānīm]]---who is the wife of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the powerful; [[synonyms:b:bhavaḥ]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:s:sammatām]]---accepted; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣi]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhyānām]]---by the great sages; [[synonyms:p:prītyā]]---in jubilation; [[synonyms:ā:ācaṣṭa]]---began to address; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, descendant of Bharata.

===== Translation =====

**O descendant of Bharata Mahārāja, Lord Śiva, in jubilation, then addressed his wife, Bhavānī, who is accepted by all authorities as the potency of Lord Viṣṇu.**
