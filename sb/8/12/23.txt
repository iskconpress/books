====== SB 8.12.23 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyāḥ karāgrāt sa tu kanduko yadā
> gato vidūraṁ tam anuvrajat-striyāḥ
> vāsaḥ sasūtraṁ laghu māruto 'harad
> bhavasya devasya kilānupaśyataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]]---of the beautiful woman; [[synonyms:k:kara]]-[[synonyms:a:agrāt]]---from the hand; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:k:kandukaḥ]]---the ball; [[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---had gone; [[synonyms:v:vidūram]]---far off; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---that ball; [[synonyms:a:anuvrajat]]---began to follow; [[synonyms:s:striyāḥ]]---of that woman; [[synonyms:v:vāsaḥ]]---the covering dress; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtram]]---with the belt; [[synonyms:l:laghu]]---because of being very fine; [[synonyms:m:mārutaḥ]]---the breeze; [[synonyms:a:aharat]]---blew away; [[synonyms:b:bhavasya]]---while Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:d:devasya]]---the chief demigod; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:anupaśyataḥ]]---was always looking.

===== Translation =====

**When the ball leaped from Her hand and fell at a distance, the woman began to follow it, but as Lord Śiva observed these activities, a breeze suddenly blew away the fine dress and belt that covered her.**
