====== SB 8.12.45 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> iti te 'bhihitas tāta
> vikramaḥ śārṅga-dhanvanaḥ
> sindhor nirmathane yena
> dhṛtaḥ pṛṣṭhe mahācalaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:a:abhihitaḥ]]---explained; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---my dear King; [[synonyms:v:vikramaḥ]]---prowess; [[synonyms:ś:śārṅga]]-[[synonyms:d:dhanvanaḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who carries the Śārṅga bow; [[synonyms:s:sindhoḥ]]---of the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:n:nirmathane]]---in the churning; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtaḥ]]---was held; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣṭhe]]---on the back; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:acalaḥ]]---the great mountain.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: My dear King, the person who bore the great mountain on His back for the churning of the ocean of milk is the same Supreme Personality of Godhead, known as Śārṅga-dhanvā. I have now described to you His prowess.**
