====== SB 8.12.46 ======

===== Text =====

> etan muhuḥ kīrtayato 'nuśṛṇvato
> na riṣyate jātu samudyamaḥ kvacit
> yad uttamaśloka-guṇānuvarṇanaṁ
> samasta-saṁsāra-pariśramāpaham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etat]]---this narration; [[synonyms:m:muhuḥ]]---constantly; [[synonyms:k:kīrtayataḥ]]---of one who chants; [[synonyms:a:anuśṛṇvataḥ]]---and also hears; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:r:riṣyate]]---annihilated; [[synonyms:j:jātu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:s:samudyamaḥ]]---the endeavor; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---at any time; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:u:uttamaśloka]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:anuvarṇanam]]---describing the transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:s:samasta]]---all; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---of material existence; [[synonyms:p:pariśrama]]---misery; [[synonyms:a:apaham]]---finishing.

===== Translation =====

**The endeavor of one who constantly hears or describes this narration of the churning of the ocean of milk will never be fruitless. Indeed, chanting the glories of the Supreme Personality of Godhead is the only means to annihilate all sufferings in this material world.**
