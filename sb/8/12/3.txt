====== SB 8.12.3 ======

===== Text =====

> sabhājito bhagavatā
> sādaraṁ somayā bhavaḥ
> sūpaviṣṭa uvācedaṁ
> pratipūjya smayan harim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabhājitaḥ]]---well received; [[synonyms:b:bhagavatā]]---by the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādaram]]---with great respect (as befitting Lord Śiva); [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:u:umayā]]---with Umā; [[synonyms:b:bhavaḥ]]---Lord Śambhu (Lord Śiva); [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:u:upaviṣṭaḥ]]---being comfortably situated; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pratipūjya]]---offering respect; [[synonyms:s:smayan]]---smiling; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---unto the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead welcomed Lord Śiva and Umā with great respect, and after being seated comfortably, Lord Śiva duly worshiped the Lord and smilingly spoke as follows.**
