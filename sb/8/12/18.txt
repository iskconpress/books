====== SB 8.12.18 ======

===== Text =====

> tato dadarśopavane vara-striyaṁ
> vicitra-puṣpāruṇa-pallava-drume
> vikrīḍatīṁ kanduka-līlayā lasad-
> dukūla-paryasta-nitamba-mekhalām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:d:dadarśa]]---Lord Śiva saw; [[synonyms:u:upavane]]---in a nice forest; [[synonyms:v:vara]]-[[synonyms:s:striyam]]---a very beautiful woman; [[synonyms:v:vicitra]]---of many varieties; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]---flowers; [[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]---pink; [[synonyms:p:pallava]]---leaves; [[synonyms:d:drume]]---in the midst of the trees; [[synonyms:v:vikrīḍatīm]]---engaged in playing; [[synonyms:k:kanduka]]---with a ball; [[synonyms:l:līlayā]]---by pastimes of playing; [[synonyms:l:lasat]]---shining; [[synonyms:d:dukūla]]---by a sari; [[synonyms:p:paryasta]]---covered; [[synonyms:n:nitamba]]---on her hips; [[synonyms:m:mekhalām]]---dressed with a belt.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, in a nice forest nearby, full of trees with reddish-pink leaves and varieties of flowers, Lord Śiva saw a beautiful woman playing with a ball. Her hips were covered with a shining sari and ornamented with a belt.**
