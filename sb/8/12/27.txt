====== SB 8.12.27 ======

===== Text =====

> tām anvagacchad bhagavān
> bhavaḥ pramuṣitendriyaḥ
> kāmasya ca vaśaṁ nītaḥ
> kareṇum iva yūthapaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tām]]---Her; [[synonyms:a:anvagacchat]]---followed; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:b:bhavaḥ]]---known as Bhava; [[synonyms:p:pramuṣita]]-[[synonyms:i:indriyaḥ]]---whose senses were agitated; [[synonyms:k:kāmasya]]---of lusty desires; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vaśam]]---victimized; [[synonyms:n:nītaḥ]]---having become; [[synonyms:k:kareṇum]]---a female elephant; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---just as; [[synonyms:y:yūthapaḥ]]---a male elephant.

===== Translation =====

**His senses being agitated, Lord Śiva, victimized by lusty desires, began to follow Her, just as a lusty elephant follows a she-elephant.**
