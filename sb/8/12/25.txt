====== SB 8.12.25 ======

===== Text =====

> tayāpahṛta-vijñānas
> tat-kṛta-smara-vihvalaḥ
> bhavānyā api paśyantyā
> gata-hrīs tat-padaṁ yayau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tayā]]---by Her; [[synonyms:a:apahṛta]]---taken away; [[synonyms:v:vijñānaḥ]]---good sense; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛta]]---done by Her; [[synonyms:s:smara]]---by the smiling; [[synonyms:v:vihvalaḥ]]---having become mad for Her; [[synonyms:b:bhavānyāḥ]]---while Bhavānī, the wife of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:p:paśyantyāḥ]]---was seeing all these incidents; [[synonyms:g:gata]]-[[synonyms:h:hrīḥ]]---bereft of all shame; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:padam]]---to the place where She was situated; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śiva, his good sense taken away by the woman because of lusty desires to enjoy with Her, became so mad for Her that even in the presence of Bhavānī he did not hesitate to approach Her.**
