====== SB 8.12.32 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyānudhāvato retaś
> caskandāmogha-retasaḥ
> śuṣmiṇo yūthapasyeva
> vāsitām anudhāvataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Lord Śiva); [[synonyms:a:anudhāvataḥ]]---who was following; [[synonyms:r:retaḥ]]---the semen; [[synonyms:c:caskanda]]---discharged; [[synonyms:a:amogha]]-[[synonyms:r:retasaḥ]]---of that person whose discharge of semen never goes in vain; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣmiṇaḥ]]---mad; [[synonyms:y:yūthapasya]]---of a male elephant; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---just like; [[synonyms:v:vāsitām]]---to a female elephant able to conceive pregnancy; [[synonyms:a:anudhāvataḥ]]---following.

===== Translation =====

**Just as a maddened bull elephant follows a female elephant who is able to conceive pregnancy, Lord Śiva followed the beautiful woman and discharged semen, even though his discharge of semen never goes in vain.**
