====== SB 8.12.17 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> iti bruvāṇo bhagavāṁs
> tatraivāntaradhīyata
> sarvataś cārayaṁś cakṣur
> bhava āste sahomayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bruvāṇaḥ]]---while speaking; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Lord Viṣṇu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---immediately; [[synonyms:a:antaradhīyata]]---disappeared from the vision of Lord Śiva and his associates; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:c:cārayan]]---moving; [[synonyms:c:cakṣuḥ]]---the eyes; [[synonyms:b:bhavaḥ]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]---remained; [[synonyms:s:saha]]-[[synonyms:u:umayā]]---with his wife, Umā.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: After speaking in this way, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu, immediately disappeared, and Lord Śiva remained there with Umā, looking for Him all around with moving eyes.**
