====== SB 8.1.22 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭāśīti-sahasrāṇi
> munayo ye dhṛta-vratāḥ
> anvaśikṣan vrataṁ tasya
> kaumāra-brahmacāriṇaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭāśīti]]---eighty-eight; [[synonyms:s:sahasrāṇi]]---thousand; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]]---great saintly persons; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---those who; [[synonyms:d:dhṛta]]-[[synonyms:v:vratāḥ]]---fixed in vows; [[synonyms:a:anvaśikṣan]]---took instructions; [[synonyms:v:vratam]]---vows; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---from him (Vibhu); [[synonyms:k:kaumāra]]---who was unmarried; [[synonyms:b:brahmacāriṇaḥ]]---and fixed in the //brahmacārī// stage of life.

===== Translation =====

**Vibhu remained a brahmacāri and never married throughout his life. From him, eighty-eight thousand other saintly persons took lessons on self-control, austerity and similar behavior.**
