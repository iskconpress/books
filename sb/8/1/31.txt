====== SB 8.1.31 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> bādarāyaṇa etat te
> śrotum icchāmahe vayam
> harir yathā gaja-patiṁ
> grāha-grastam amūmucat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---King Parīkṣit said; [[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇe]]---O son of Bādarāyaṇa (Vyāsadeva); [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:t:te]]---from you; [[synonyms:ś:śrotum]] [[synonyms:i:icchāmahe]]---desire to hear; [[synonyms:v:vayam]]---we; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Lord Hari; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---the manner in which; [[synonyms:g:gaja]]-[[synonyms:p:patim]]---the King of the elephants; [[synonyms:g:grāha]]-[[synonyms:g:grastam]]---when attacked by the crocodile; [[synonyms:a:amūmucat]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**King Parīkṣit said: My lord, Bādarāyaṇi, we wish to hear from you in detail how the King of the elephants, when attacked by a crocodile, was delivered by Hari.**
