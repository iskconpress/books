====== SB 8.1.6 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛtaṁ purā bhagavataḥ
> kapilasyānuvarṇitam
> ākhyāsye bhagavān yajño
> yac cakāra kurūdvaha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---already done; [[synonyms:p:purā]]---before; [[synonyms:b:bhagavataḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kapilasya]]---Kapila, the son of Devahūti; [[synonyms:a:anuvarṇitam]]---fully described; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāsye]]---I shall describe now; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yajñaḥ]]---of the name Yajñapati or Yajñamūrti; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever; [[synonyms:c:cakāra]]---executed; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:u:udvaha]]---O best of the Kurus.

===== Translation =====

**O best of the Kurus, I have already described [in the Third Canto] the activities of Kapila, the son of Devahūti. Now I shall describe the activities of Yajñapati, the son of Ākūti.**
