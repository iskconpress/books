====== SB 8.1.28 ======

===== Text =====

> satyakā harayo vīrā
> devās triśikha īśvaraḥ
> jyotirdhāmādayaḥ sapta
> ṛṣayas tāmase 'ntare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:satyakāḥ]]---the Satyakas; [[synonyms:h:harayaḥ]]---the Haris; [[synonyms:v:vīrāḥ]]---the Vīras; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:t:triśikhaḥ]]---Triśikha; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the King of heaven; [[synonyms:j:jyotirdhāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---headed by the celebrated Jyotirdhāma; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]---seven; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---sages; [[synonyms:t:tāmase]]---the reign of Tāmasa Manu; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within.

===== Translation =====

**During the reign of Tāmasa Manu, among the demigods were the Satyakas, Haris and Vīras. The heavenly King, Indra, was Triśikha. The sages in saptarṣi-dhāma were headed by Jyotirdhāma.**
