====== SB 8.1.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> svāyambhuvasyeha guro
> vaṁśo 'yaṁ vistarāc chrutaḥ
> yatra viśva-sṛjāṁ sargo
> manūn anyān vadasva naḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the King (Mahārāja Parīkṣit) said; [[synonyms:s:svāyambhuvasya]]---of the great personality Svāyambhuva Manu; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in this connection; [[synonyms:g:guro]]---O my spiritual master; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśaḥ]]---dynasty; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vistarāt]]---extensively; [[synonyms:ś:śrutaḥ]]---I have heard (from you); [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherein; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:s:sṛjām]]---of the great personalities known as the //prajāpatis,// such as Marīci; [[synonyms:s:sargaḥ]]---creation, involving the birth of many sons and grandsons from the daughters of Manu; [[synonyms:m:manūn]]---Manus; [[synonyms:a:anyān]]---other; [[synonyms:v:vadasva]]---kindly describe; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---to us.

===== Translation =====

**King Parīkṣit said: O my lord, my spiritual master, now I have fully heard from Your Grace about the dynasty of Svāyambhuva Manu. But there are also other Manus, and I want to hear about their dynasties. Kindly describe them to us.**
