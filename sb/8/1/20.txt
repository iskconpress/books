====== SB 8.1.20 ======

===== Text =====

> tatrendro rocanas tv āsīd
> devāś ca tuṣitādayaḥ
> ūrja-stambhādayaḥ sapta
> ṛṣayo brahma-vādinaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in this //manvantara//; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---Indra; [[synonyms:r:rocanaḥ]]---Rocana, the son of Yajña; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---became; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---demigods; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tuṣita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---Tuṣita and others; [[synonyms:ū:ūrja]]---Ūrja; [[synonyms:s:stambha]]---Stambha; [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and others; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]---seven; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---great saints; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:v:vādinaḥ]]---all faithful devotees.

===== Translation =====

**During the reign of Svārociṣa, the post of Indra was assumed by Rocana, the son of Yajña. Tuṣita and others became the principal demigods, and Ūrja, Stambha and others became the seven saints. All of them were faithful devotees of the Lord.**
