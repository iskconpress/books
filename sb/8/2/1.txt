====== SB 8.2.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> āsīd girivaro rājaṁs
> trikūṭa iti viśrutaḥ
> kṣīrodenāvṛtaḥ śrīmān
> yojanāyutam ucchritaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---there was; [[synonyms:g:girivaraḥ]]---a very big mountain; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:k:kūṭaḥ]]---Trikūṭa; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:viśrutaḥ]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:u:udena]]---by the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:ā:āvṛtaḥ]]---surrounded; [[synonyms:ś:śrīmān]]---very beautiful; [[synonyms:y:yojana]]---a measurement of eight miles; [[synonyms:a:ayutam]]---ten thousand; [[synonyms:u:ucchritaḥ]]---very high.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: My dear King, there is a very large mountain called Trikūṭa. It is ten thousand yojanas [eighty thousand miles] high. Being surrounded by the ocean of milk, it is very beautifully situated.**
