====== SB 8.2.7 ======

===== Text =====

> nānāraṇya-paśu-vrāta-
> saṅkula-droṇy-alaṅkṛtaḥ
> citra-druma-surodyāna-
> kalakaṇṭha-vihaṅgamaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]---with varieties of; [[synonyms:a:araṇya]]-[[synonyms:p:paśu]]---jungle animals; [[synonyms:v:vrāta]]---with a multitude; [[synonyms:s:saṅkula]]---filled; [[synonyms:d:droṇi]]---with valleys; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkṛtaḥ]]---very beautifully decorated; [[synonyms:c:citra]]---with varieties of; [[synonyms:d:druma]]---trees; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāna]]---in gardens maintained by the demigods; [[synonyms:k:kalakaṇṭha]]---sweetly chirping; [[synonyms:v:vihaṅgamaḥ]]---birds.

===== Translation =====

**The valleys beneath Trikūṭa Mountain are beautifully decorated by many varieties of jungle animals, and in the trees, which are maintained in gardens by the demigods, varieties of birds chirp with sweet voices.**
