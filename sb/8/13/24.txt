====== SB 8.13.24 ======

===== Text =====

> manur vai dharma-sāvarṇir
> ekādaśama ātmavān
> anāgatās tat-sutāś ca
> satyadharmādayo daśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manuḥ]]---the Manu; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:s:sāvarṇiḥ]]---Dharmasāvarṇi; [[synonyms:e:ekādaśamaḥ]]---eleventh; [[synonyms:ā:ātmavān]]---the controller of the senses; [[synonyms:a:anāgatāḥ]]---will come in the future; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:satyadharma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---Satyadharma and others; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten.

===== Translation =====

**In the eleventh manvantara, the Manu will be Dharma-sāvarṇi, who will be extremely learned in spiritual knowledge. From him there will come ten sons, headed by Satyadharma.**
