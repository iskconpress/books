====== SB 8.13.29 ======

===== Text =====

> svadhāmākhyo harer aṁśaḥ
> sādhayiṣyati tan-manoḥ
> antaraṁ satyasahasaḥ
> sunṛtāyāḥ suto vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svadhāmā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyaḥ]]---Svadhāmā; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśaḥ]]---a partial incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sādhayiṣyati]]---will rule; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:m:manoḥ]]---of that Manu; [[synonyms:a:antaram]]---the //manvantara//; [[synonyms:s:satyasahasaḥ]]---of Satyasahā; [[synonyms:s:sunṛtāyāḥ]]---of Sunṛtā; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---most powerful.

===== Translation =====

**From the mother named Sunṛtā and the father named Satyasahā will come Svadhāmā, a partial incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. He will rule that manvantara.**
