====== SB 8.13.32 ======

===== Text =====

> devahotrasya tanaya
> upahartā divaspateḥ
> yogeśvaro harer aṁśo
> bṛhatyāṁ sambhaviṣyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devahotrasya]]---of Devahotra; [[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:u:upahartā]]---the benefactor; [[synonyms:d:divaspateḥ]]---of Divaspati, the Indra at that time; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---Yogeśvara, the master of mystic powers; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśaḥ]]---a partial representation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bṛhatyām]]---in the womb of his mother, Bṛhatī; [[synonyms:s:sambhaviṣyati]]---will appear.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Devahotra known as Yogeśvara will appear as a partial incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. His mother's name will be Bṛhatī. He will perform activities for the welfare of Divaspati.**
