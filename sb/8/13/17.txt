====== SB 8.13.17 ======

===== Text =====

> devaguhyāt sarasvatyāṁ
> sārvabhauma iti prabhuḥ
> sthānaṁ purandarād dhṛtvā
> balaye dāsyatīśvaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devaguhyāt]]---from His father, Devaguhya; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatyām]]---in the womb of Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaumaḥ]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:p:prabhuḥ]]---the master; [[synonyms:s:sthānam]]---place; [[synonyms:p:purandarāt]]---from Lord Indra; [[synonyms:h:hṛtvā]]---taking away by force; [[synonyms:b:balaye]]---unto Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:d:dāsyati]]---will give; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the master.

===== Translation =====

**In the eighth manvantara, the greatly powerful Personality of Godhead Sārvabhauma will take birth. His father will be Devaguhya, and His mother will be Sarasvatī. He will take the kingdom away from Purandara [Lord Indra] and give it to Bali Mahārāja.**
