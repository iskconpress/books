====== SB 8.13.26 ======

===== Text =====

> āryakasya sutas tatra
> dharmasetur iti smṛtaḥ
> vaidhṛtāyāṁ harer aṁśas
> tri-lokīṁ dhārayiṣyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āryakasya]]---of Āryaka; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in that period (the eleventh //manvantara//); [[synonyms:d:dharmasetuḥ]]---Dharmasetu; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:v:vaidhṛtāyām]]---from the mother, Vaidhṛtā; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:aṁśaḥ]]---a partial incarnation; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:l:lokīm]]---the three worlds; [[synonyms:d:dhārayiṣyati]]---will rule.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Āryaka known as Dharmasetu, a partial incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, will appear from the womb of Vaidhṛtā, the wife of Āryaka, and will rule the three worlds.**
