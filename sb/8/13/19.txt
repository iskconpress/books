====== SB 8.13.19 ======

===== Text =====

> pārā-marīcigarbhādyā
> devā indro 'dbhutaḥ smṛtaḥ
> dyutimat-pramukhās tatra
> bhaviṣyanty ṛṣayas tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pārā]]---the Pāras; [[synonyms:m:marīcigarbha]]---the Marīcigarbhas; [[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---like that; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the king of heaven; [[synonyms:a:adbhutaḥ]]---Adbhuta; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---known; [[synonyms:d:dyutimat]]---Dyutimān; [[synonyms:p:pramukhāḥ]]---headed by; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in that ninth period of Manu; [[synonyms:b:bhaviṣyanti]]---will become; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---the seven //ṛṣis//; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---then.

===== Translation =====

**In this ninth manvantara, the Pāras and Marīcigarbhas will be among the demigods. The king of heaven, Indra, will be named Adbhuta, and Dyutimān will be among the seven sages.**
