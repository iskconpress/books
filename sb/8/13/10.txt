====== SB 8.13.10 ======

===== Text =====

> sāvarṇis tapatī kanyā
> bhāryā saṁvaraṇasya yā
> śanaiścaras tṛtīyo 'bhūd
> aśvinau vaḍavātmajau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāvarṇiḥ]]---Sāvarṇi; [[synonyms:t:tapatī]]---Tapatī; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---the daughter; [[synonyms:b:bhāryā]]---the wife; [[synonyms:s:saṁvaraṇasya]]---of King Saṁvaraṇa; [[synonyms:y:yā]]---she who; [[synonyms:ś:śanaiścaraḥ]]---Śanaiścara; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīyaḥ]]---the third issue; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---took birth; [[synonyms:a:aśvinau]]---the two Aśvinī-kumāras; [[synonyms:v:vaḍavā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:j:jau]]---the sons of the wife known as Vaḍavā.

===== Translation =====

**Chāyā had a son named Sāvarṇi and a daughter named Tapatī, who later became the wife of King Saṁvaraṇa. Chāyā's third child is known as Śanaiścara [Saturn]. Vaḍavā gave birth to two sons, namely the Aśvinī brothers.**
