====== SB 8.13.6 ======

===== Text =====

> atrāpi bhagavaj-janma
> kaśyapād aditer abhūt
> ādityānām avarajo
> viṣṇur vāmana-rūpa-dhṛk

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atra]]---in this Manu's reign; [[synonyms:a:api]]---certainly; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:j:janma]]---appearance of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kaśyapāt]]---by Kaśyapa Muni; [[synonyms:a:aditeḥ]]---of mother Aditi; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became possible; [[synonyms:ā:ādityānām]]---of the Ādityas; [[synonyms:a:avara]]-[[synonyms:j:jaḥ]]---the youngest; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇuḥ]]---Lord Viṣṇu Himself; [[synonyms:v:vāmana]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhṛk]]---appearing as Lord Vāmana.

===== Translation =====

**In this manvantara, the Supreme Personality of Godhead appeared as the youngest of all the Ādityas, known as Vāmana, the dwarf. His father was Kaśyapa and His mother Aditi.**
