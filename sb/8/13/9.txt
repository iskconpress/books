====== SB 8.13.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīyāṁ vaḍavām eke
> tāsāṁ saṁjñā-sutās trayaḥ
> yamo yamī śrāddhadevaś
> chāyāyāś ca sutāñ chṛṇu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīyām]]---the third wife; [[synonyms:v:vaḍavām]]---Vaḍavā; [[synonyms:e:eke]]---some people; [[synonyms:t:tāsām]]---of all three wives; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñā]]-[[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]] [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]]---three issues of Saṁjñā; [[synonyms:y:yamaḥ]]---one son named Yama; [[synonyms:y:yamī]]---Yamī, a daughter; [[synonyms:ś:śrāddhadevaḥ]]---Śrāddhadeva, another son; [[synonyms:c:chāyāyāḥ]]---of Chāyā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:sutān]]---the sons; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---just hear about.

===== Translation =====

**It is said that the sun-god had a third wife, named Vaḍavā. Of the three wives, the wife named Saṁjñā had three children-Yama, Yamī and Śrāddhadeva. Now let me describe the children of Chāyā.**
