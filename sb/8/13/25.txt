====== SB 8.13.25 ======

===== Text =====

> vihaṅgamāḥ kāmagamā
> nirvāṇarucayaḥ surāḥ
> indraś ca vaidhṛtas teṣām
> ṛṣayaś cāruṇādayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vihaṅgamāḥ]]---the Vihaṅgamas; [[synonyms:k:kāmagamāḥ]]---the Kāmagamas; [[synonyms:n:nirvāṇarucayaḥ]]---the Nirvāṇarucis; [[synonyms:s:surāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the king of heaven, Indra; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vaidhṛtaḥ]]---Vaidhṛta; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of them; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---the seven sages; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---headed by Aruṇa.

===== Translation =====

**The Vihaṅgamas, Kāmagamas, Nirvāṇarucis and others will be the demigods. The king of the demigods, Indra, will be Vaidhṛta, and the seven sages will be headed by Aruṇa.**
