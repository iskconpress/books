====== SB 8.13.22 ======

===== Text =====

> haviṣmān sukṛtaḥ satyo
> jayo mūrtis tadā dvijāḥ
> suvāsana-viruddhādyā
> devāḥ śambhuḥ sureśvaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haviṣmān]]---Haviṣmān; [[synonyms:s:sukṛtaḥ]]---Sukṛta; [[synonyms:s:satyaḥ]]---Satya; [[synonyms:j:jayaḥ]]---Jaya; [[synonyms:m:mūrtiḥ]]---Mūrti; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:d:dvijāḥ]]---the seven sages; [[synonyms:s:suvāsana]]---the Suvāsanas; [[synonyms:v:viruddha]]---the Viruddhas; [[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---and so on; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:ś:śambhuḥ]]---Śambhu; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---Indra, king of the demigods.

===== Translation =====

**Haviṣmān, Sukṛta, Satya, Jaya, Mūrti and others will be the seven sages, the Suvāsanas and Viruddhas will be among the demigods, and Śambhu will be their king, Indra.**
