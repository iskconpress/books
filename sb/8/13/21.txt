====== SB 8.13.21 ======

===== Text =====

> daśamo brahma-sāvarṇir
> upaśloka-suto manuḥ
> tat-sutā bhūriṣeṇādyā
> haviṣmat pramukhā dvijāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daśamaḥ]]---the tenth Manu; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:sāvarṇiḥ]]---Brahma-sāvarṇi; [[synonyms:u:upaśloka]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---born of Upaśloka; [[synonyms:m:manuḥ]]---will be Manu; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---his sons; [[synonyms:b:bhūriṣeṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---Bhūriṣeṇa and others; [[synonyms:h:haviṣmat]]---Haviṣmān; [[synonyms:p:pramukhāḥ]]---headed by; [[synonyms:d:dvijāḥ]]---the seven sages.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Upaśloka known as Brahma-sāvarṇi will be the tenth Manu. Bhūriṣeṇa will be among his sons, and the brāhmaṇas headed by Haviṣmān will be the seven sages.**
