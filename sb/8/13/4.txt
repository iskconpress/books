====== SB 8.13.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ādityā vasavo rudrā
> viśvedevā marud-gaṇāḥ
> aśvināv ṛbhavo rājann
> indras teṣāṁ purandaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ādityāḥ]]---the Ādityas; [[synonyms:v:vasavaḥ]]---the Vasus; [[synonyms:r:rudrāḥ]]---the Rudras; [[synonyms:v:viśvedevāḥ]]---the Viśvedevas; [[synonyms:m:marut]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇāḥ]]---and the Maruts; [[synonyms:a:aśvinau]]---the two Aśvinī brothers; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛbhavaḥ]]---the Ṛbhus; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the king of heaven; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of them; [[synonyms:p:purandaraḥ]]---Purandara.

===== Translation =====

**In this manvantara, O King, the Ādityas, the Vasus, the Rudras, the Viśvedevas, the Maruts, the two Aśvinī-kumāra brothers and the Ṛbhus are the demigods. Their head king [Indra] is Purandara.**
