====== SB 8.13.14 ======

===== Text =====

> yo 'sau bhagavatā baddhaḥ
> prītena sutale punaḥ
> niveśito 'dhike svargād
> adhunāste sva-rāḍ iva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bhagavatā]]---by the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:baddhaḥ]]---bound; [[synonyms:p:prītena]]---because of favor; [[synonyms:s:sutale]]---in the kingdom of Sutala; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:n:niveśitaḥ]]---situated; [[synonyms:a:adhike]]---more opulent; [[synonyms:s:svargāt]]---than the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:a:adhunā]]---at the present moment; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]---is situated; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rāṭ]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---equal to the position of Indra.

===== Translation =====

**With great affection, the Personality of Godhead bound Bali and then installed him in the kingdom of Sutala, which is more opulent than the heavenly planets. Mahārāja Bali now resides on that planet and is more comfortably situated than Indra.**
