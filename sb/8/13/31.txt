====== SB 8.13.31 ======

===== Text =====

> devāḥ sukarma-sutrāma-
> saṁjñā indro divaspatiḥ
> nirmoka-tattvadarśādyā
> bhaviṣyanty ṛṣayas tadā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:s:sukarma]]---the Sukarmās; [[synonyms:s:sutrāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁjñāḥ]]---and the Sutrāmas; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the king of heaven; [[synonyms:d:divaspatiḥ]]---Divaspati; [[synonyms:n:nirmoka]]---Nirmoka; [[synonyms:t:tattvadarśa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---and others, like Tattvadarśa; [[synonyms:b:bhaviṣyanti]]---will become; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---the seven sages; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time.

===== Translation =====

**In the thirteenth manvantara, the Sukarmās and Sutrāmas will be among the demigods, Divaspati will be the king of heaven, and Nirmoka and Tattvadarśa will be among the seven sages.**
