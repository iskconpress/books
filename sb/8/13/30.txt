====== SB 8.13.30 ======

===== Text =====

> manus trayodaśo bhāvyo
> deva-sāvarṇir ātmavān
> citrasena-vicitrādyā
> deva-sāvarṇi-dehajāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manuḥ]]---the Manu; [[synonyms:t:trayodaśaḥ]]---thirteenth; [[synonyms:b:bhāvyaḥ]]---will become; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:s:sāvarṇiḥ]]---Deva-sāvarṇi; [[synonyms:ā:ātmavān]]---well advanced in spiritual knowledge; [[synonyms:c:citrasena]]---Citrasena; [[synonyms:v:vicitra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---and others, like Vicitra; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:s:sāvarṇi]]---of Deva-sāvarṇi; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:j:jāḥ]]---sons.

===== Translation =====

**The thirteenth Manu will be named Deva-sāvarṇi, and he will be very advanced in spiritual knowledge. Among his sons will be Citrasena and Vicitra.**
