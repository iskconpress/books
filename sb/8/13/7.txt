====== SB 8.13.7 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepato mayoktāni
> sapta-manvantarāṇi te
> bhaviṣyāṇy atha vakṣyāmi
> viṣṇoḥ śaktyānvitāni ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepataḥ]]---in brief; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:u:uktāni]]---explained; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]---seven; [[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:a:antarāṇi]]---changes of Manu; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:b:bhaviṣyāṇi]]---the future Manus; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vakṣyāmi]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:ś:śaktyā]] [[synonyms:a:anvitāni]]---empowered by the energy; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**I have briefly explained to you the position of the seven Manus. Now I shall describe the future Manus, along with the incarnations of Lord Viṣṇu.**
