====== SB 8.13.2-3 ======

===== Text =====

> ikṣvākur nabhagaś caiva
> dhṛṣṭaḥ śaryātir eva ca
> nariṣyanto 'tha nābhāgaḥ
> saptamo diṣṭa ucyate
>
> tarūṣaś ca pṛṣadhraś ca
> daśamo vasumān smṛtaḥ
> manor vaivasvatasyaite
> daśa-putrāḥ parantapa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ikṣvākuḥ]]---Ikṣvāku; [[synonyms:n:nabhagaḥ]]---Nabhaga; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:d:dhṛṣṭaḥ]]---Dhṛṣṭa; [[synonyms:ś:śaryātiḥ]]---Śaryāti; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:n:nariṣyantaḥ]]---Nariṣyanta; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---as well as; [[synonyms:n:nābhāgaḥ]]---Nābhāga; [[synonyms:s:saptamaḥ]]---the seventh one; [[synonyms:d:diṣṭaḥ]]---Diṣṭa; [[synonyms:u:ucyate]]---is so celebrated; [[synonyms:t:tarūṣaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and Tarūṣa; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣadhraḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and Pṛṣadhra; [[synonyms:d:daśamaḥ]]---the tenth one; [[synonyms:v:vasumān]]---Vasumān; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---known; [[synonyms:m:manoḥ]]---of Manu; [[synonyms:v:vaivasvatasya]]---of Vaivasvata; [[synonyms:e:ete]]---all these; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:p:putrāḥ]]---ten sons; [[synonyms:p:parantapa]]---O King.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, among the ten sons of Manu are Ikṣvāku, Nabhaga, Dhṛṣṭa, Śaryāti, Nariṣyanta and Nābhāga. The seventh son is known as Diṣṭa. Then come Tarūṣa and Pṛṣadhra, and the tenth son is known as Vasumān.**
