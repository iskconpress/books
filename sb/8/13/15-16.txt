====== SB 8.13.15-16 ======

===== Text =====

> gālavo dīptimān rāmo
> droṇa-putraḥ kṛpas tathā
> ṛṣyaśṛṅgaḥ pitāsmākaṁ
> bhagavān bādarāyaṇaḥ
>
> ime saptarṣayas tatra
> bhaviṣyanti sva-yogataḥ
> idānīm āsate rājan
> sve sva āśrama-maṇḍale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gālavaḥ]]---Gālava; [[synonyms:d:dīptimān]]---Dīptimān; [[synonyms:r:rāmaḥ]]---Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:d:droṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son of Droṇācārya, namely Aśvatthāmā; [[synonyms:k:kṛpaḥ]]---Kṛpācārya; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣyaśṛṅgaḥ]]---Ṛṣyaśṛṅga; [[synonyms:p:pitā]] [[synonyms:a:asmākam]]---our father; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the incarnation of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇaḥ]]---Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:i:ime]]---all of them; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---the seven sages; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in the eighth //manvantara//; [[synonyms:b:bhaviṣyanti]]---will become; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:y:yogataḥ]]---as a result of their service to the Lord; [[synonyms:i:idānīm]]---at the present moment; [[synonyms:ā:āsate]]---they are all existing; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:sve]] [[synonyms:s:sve]]---in their own; [[synonyms:ā:āśrama]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍale]]---different hermitages.

===== Translation =====

**O King, during the eighth manvantara, the great personalities Gālava, Dīptimān, Paraśurāma, Aśvatthāmā, Kṛpācārya, Ṛṣyaśṛṅga and our father, Vyāsadeva, the incarnation of Nārāyaṇa, will be the seven sages. For the present, they are all residing in their respective āśramas.**
