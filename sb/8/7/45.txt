====== SB 8.7.45 ======

===== Text =====

> niśamya karma tac chambhor
> deva-devasya mīḍhuṣaḥ
> prajā dākṣāyaṇī brahmā
> vaikuṇṭhaś ca śaśaṁsire

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niśamya]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---the act; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śambhoḥ]]---of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:devasya]]---who is worshipable even for the demigods; [[synonyms:m:mīḍhuṣaḥ]]---he who bestows great benedictions upon people in general; [[synonyms:p:prajāḥ]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:d:dākṣāyaṇī]]---Bhavānī, the daughter of Dakṣa; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---Lord Viṣṇu also; [[synonyms:ś:śaśaṁsire]]---praised very much.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing of this act, everyone, including Bhavānī [the daughter of Mahārāja Dakṣa], Lord Brahmā, Lord Viṣṇu, and the people in general, very highly praised this deed performed by Lord Śiva, who is worshiped by the demigods and who bestows benedictions upon the people.**
