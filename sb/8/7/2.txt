====== SB 8.7.2 ======

===== Text =====

> hariḥ purastāj jagṛhe
> pūrvaṁ devās tato 'bhavan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Ajita; [[synonyms:p:purastāt]]---from the front; [[synonyms:j:jagṛhe]]---took; [[synonyms:p:pūrvam]]---at first; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:a:abhavan]]---took the front portion of Vāsuki.

===== Translation =====

**The Personality of Godhead, Ajita, grasped the front portion of the snake, and then the demigods followed.**
