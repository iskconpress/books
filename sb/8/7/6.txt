====== SB 8.7.6 ======

===== Text =====

> mathyamāne 'rṇave so 'drir
> anādhāro hy apo 'viśat
> dhriyamāṇo 'pi balibhir
> gauravāt pāṇḍu-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathyamāne]]---while the churning was going on; [[synonyms:a:arṇave]]---in the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:a:adriḥ]]---hill; [[synonyms:a:anādhāraḥ]]---without being supported by anything; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:apaḥ]]---in the water; [[synonyms:a:aviśat]]---drowned; [[synonyms:d:dhriyamāṇaḥ]]---captured; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:b:balibhiḥ]]---by the powerful demigods and demons; [[synonyms:g:gauravāt]]---from being very heavy; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍu]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---O son of Pāṇḍu (Mahārāja Parīkṣit).

===== Translation =====

**O son of the Pāṇḍu dynasty, when Mandara Mountain was thus being used as a churning rod in the ocean of milk, it had no support, and therefore although held by the strong hands of the demigods and demons, it sank into the water.**
