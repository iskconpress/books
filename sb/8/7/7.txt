====== SB 8.7.7 ======

===== Text =====

> te sunirviṇṇa-manasaḥ
> parimlāna-mukha-śriyaḥ
> āsan sva-pauruṣe naṣṭe
> daivenātibalīyasā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them (the demigods and demons); [[synonyms:s:sunirviṇṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:manasaḥ]]---their minds being very disappointed; [[synonyms:p:parimlāna]]---dried up; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śriyaḥ]]---the beauty of their faces; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:p:pauruṣe]]---with their own prowess; [[synonyms:n:naṣṭe]]---being lost; [[synonyms:d:daivena]]---by a providential arrangement; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:b:balīyasā]]---which is always stronger than anything else.

===== Translation =====

**Because the mountain had been sunk by the strength of providence, the demigods and demons were disappointed, and their faces seemed to shrivel.**
