====== SB 8.7.23 ======

===== Text =====

> guṇa-mayyā sva-śaktyāsya
> sarga-sthity-apyayān vibho
> dhatse yadā sva-dṛg bhūman
> brahma-viṣṇu-śivābhidhām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mayyā]]---acting in three modes of activity; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaktyā]]---by the external energy of Your Lordship; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of this material world; [[synonyms:s:sarga]]-[[synonyms:s:sthiti]]-[[synonyms:a:apyayān]]---creation, maintenance and annihilation; [[synonyms:v:vibho]]---O lord; [[synonyms:d:dhatse]]---you execute; [[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛk]]---you manifest yourself; [[synonyms:b:bhūman]]---O great one; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiva]]-[[synonyms:a:abhidhām]]---as Lord Brahmā, Lord Viṣṇu or Lord Śiva.

===== Translation =====

**O lord, you are self-effulgent and supreme. You create this material world by your personal energy, and you assume the names Brahmā, Viṣṇu and Maheśvara when you act in creation, maintenance and annihilation.**

===== Purport =====

This prayer is actually offered to Lord Viṣṇu, the //puruṣa,// who in His incarnations as the //guṇa-avatāras// assumes the names Brahmā, Viṣṇu and Maheśvara.
