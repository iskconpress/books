====== SB 8.7.40 ======

===== Text =====

> puṁsaḥ kṛpayato bhadre
> sarvātmā prīyate hariḥ
> prīte harau bhagavati
> prīye 'haṁ sacarācaraḥ
> tasmād idaṁ garaṁ bhuñje
> prajānāṁ svastir astu me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puṁsaḥ]]---with a person; [[synonyms:k:kṛpayataḥ]]---engaged in benevolent activities; [[synonyms:b:bhadre]]---O most gentle Bhavānī; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---the Supersoul; [[synonyms:p:prīyate]]---becomes pleased; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prīte]]---because of His pleasure; [[synonyms:h:harau]]---the Supreme Lord, Hari; [[synonyms:b:bhagavati]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prīye]]---also become pleased; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:c:cara]]-[[synonyms:a:acaraḥ]]---with all others, moving and nonmoving; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---therefore; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:g:garam]]---poison; [[synonyms:b:bhuñje]]---let me drink; [[synonyms:p:prajānām]]---of the living entities; [[synonyms:s:svastiḥ]]---welfare; [[synonyms:a:astu]]---let there be; [[synonyms:m:me]]---by me.

===== Translation =====

**My dear gentle wife Bhavānī, when one performs benevolent activities for others, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari, is very pleased. And when the Lord is pleased, I am also pleased, along with all other living creatures. Therefore, let me drink this poison, for all the living entities may thus become happy because of me.**
