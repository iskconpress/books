====== SB 8.7.37 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śiva uvāca
> aho bata bhavāny etat
> prajānāṁ paśya vaiśasam
> kṣīroda-mathanodbhūtāt
> kālakūṭād upasthitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śivaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śiva said; [[synonyms:a:aho]] [[synonyms:b:bata]]---how pitiable; [[synonyms:b:bhavāni]]---my dear wife, Bhavānī; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this situation; [[synonyms:p:prajānām]]---of all living entities; [[synonyms:p:paśya]]---just see; [[synonyms:v:vaiśasam]]---very dangerous; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:u:uda]]---of the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:m:mathana]]-[[synonyms:u:udbhūtāt]]---produced by the churning; [[synonyms:k:kālakūṭāt]]---because of the production of poison; [[synonyms:u:upasthitam]]---the present situation.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śiva said: My dear Bhavānī, just see how all these living entities have been placed in danger because of the poison produced from the churning of the ocean of milk.**
