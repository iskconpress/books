====== SB 8.7.16 ======

===== Text =====

> mathyamānāt tathā sindhor
> devāsura-varūtha-paiḥ
> yadā sudhā na jāyeta
> nirmamanthājitaḥ svayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathyamānāt]]---sufficiently being churned; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sindhoḥ]]---from the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---of the demigods; [[synonyms:a:asura]]---and the demons; [[synonyms:v:varūtha]]-[[synonyms:p:paiḥ]]---by the best; [[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:s:sudhā]]---nectar; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:j:jāyeta]]---did not come out; [[synonyms:n:nirmamantha]]---churned; [[synonyms:a:ajitaḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Ajita; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**When nectar did not come from the ocean of milk, despite so much endeavor by the best of the demigods and demons, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Ajita, personally began to churn the ocean.**
