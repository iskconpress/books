====== SB 8.7.34 ======

===== Text =====

> tat tasya te sad-asatoḥ parataḥ parasya
> nāñjaḥ svarūpa-gamane prabhavanti bhūmnaḥ
> brahmādayaḥ kim uta saṁstavane vayaṁ tu
> tat-sarga-sarga-viṣayā api śakti-mātram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]---therefore; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of that; [[synonyms:t:te]]---of Your Lordship; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:a:asatoḥ]]---of the living entities, moving and not moving; [[synonyms:p:parataḥ]]---transcendentally situated; [[synonyms:p:parasya]]---very difficult to understand; [[synonyms:n:na]]---nor; [[synonyms:a:añjaḥ]]---as it is; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gamane]]---to approach your reality; [[synonyms:p:prabhavanti]]---it is possible; [[synonyms:b:bhūmnaḥ]]---O great one; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---even such persons as Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:u:uta]]---what to speak of others; [[synonyms:s:saṁstavane]]---in offering prayers; [[synonyms:v:vayam]] [[synonyms:t:tu]]---as far as we are concerned; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of you; [[synonyms:s:sarga]]-[[synonyms:s:sarga]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣayāḥ]]---creations of the creation; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:m:mātram]]---to our ability.

===== Translation =====

**Even personalities like Lord Brahmā and other demigods cannot understand your position, for you are beyond the moving and nonmoving creation. Since no one can understand you in truth, how can one offer you prayers? It is impossible. As far as we are concerned, we are creatures of Lord Brahmā's creation. Under the circumstances, therefore, we cannot offer you adequate prayers, but as far as our ability allows, we have expressed our feelings.**
