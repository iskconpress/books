====== SB 8.7.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> te nāga-rājam āmantrya
> phala-bhāgena vāsukim
> parivīya girau tasmin
> netram abdhiṁ mudānvitāḥ
> ārebhire surā yattā
> amṛtārthe kurūdvaha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them (the demigods and the demons); [[synonyms:n:nāga]]-[[synonyms:r:rājam]]---the king of the Nāgas, snakes; [[synonyms:ā:āmantrya]]---inviting, or requesting; [[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgena]]---by promising a share of the nectar; [[synonyms:v:vāsukim]]---the snake Vāsuki; [[synonyms:p:parivīya]]---encircling; [[synonyms:g:girau]]---Mandara Mountain; [[synonyms:t:tasmin]]---unto it; [[synonyms:n:netram]]---the churning rope; [[synonyms:a:abdhim]]---the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:m:mudā]] [[synonyms:a:anvitāḥ]]---all surcharged with great pleasure; [[synonyms:ā:ārebhire]]---began to act; [[synonyms:s:surāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:y:yattāḥ]]---with great endeavor; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:arthe]]---for gaining nectar; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:u:udvaha]]---O King Parīkṣit, best of the Kurus.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O best of the Kurus, Mahārāja Parīkṣit, the demigods and demons summoned Vāsuki, king of the serpents, requesting him to come and promising to give him a share of the nectar. They coiled Vāsuki around Mandara Mountain as a churning rope, and with great pleasure they endeavored to produce nectar by churning the ocean of milk.**
