====== SB 8.7.27 ======

===== Text =====

> nābhir nabhas te śvasanaṁ nabhasvān
> sūryaś ca cakṣūṁṣi jalaṁ sma retaḥ
> parāvarātmāśrayaṇaṁ tavātmā
> somo mano dyaur bhagavan śiras te

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nābhiḥ]]---navel; [[synonyms:n:nabhaḥ]]---the sky; [[synonyms:t:te]]---of Your Lordship; [[synonyms:ś:śvasanam]]---breathing; [[synonyms:n:nabhasvān]]---the air; [[synonyms:s:sūryaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and the sun globe; [[synonyms:c:cakṣūṁṣi]]---your eyes; [[synonyms:j:jalam]]---the water; [[synonyms:s:sma]]---indeed; [[synonyms:r:retaḥ]]---semen; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:a:avara]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrayaṇam]]---the shelter of all living entities, low and high; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---your; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---self; [[synonyms:s:somaḥ]]---the moon; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]---mind; [[synonyms:d:dyauḥ]]---the higher planetary systems; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O Your Lordship; [[synonyms:ś:śiraḥ]]---head; [[synonyms:t:te]]---of you.

===== Translation =====

**O lord, the sky is your navel, the air is your breathing, the sun is your eyes, and the water is your semen. You are the shelter of all kinds of living entities, high and low. The god of the moon is your mind, and the upper planetary system is your head.**
