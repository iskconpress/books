====== SB 8.8.16 ======

===== Text =====

> bhūṣaṇāni vicitrāṇi
> viśvakarmā prajāpatiḥ
> hāraṁ sarasvatī padmam
> ajo nāgāś ca kuṇḍale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhūṣaṇāni]]---varieties of ornaments; [[synonyms:v:vicitrāṇi]]---all very nicely decorated; [[synonyms:v:viśvakarmā]] [[synonyms:p:prajāpatiḥ]]---Viśvakarmā, one of the //prajāpatis,// the sons of Lord Brahmā who generate progeny; [[synonyms:h:hāram]]---garland or necklace; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---the goddess of education; [[synonyms:p:padmam]]---a lotus flower; [[synonyms:a:ajaḥ]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:n:nāgāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---the inhabitants of Nāgaloka; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍale]]---two earrings.

===== Translation =====

**Viśvakarmā, one of the prajāpatis, supplied varieties of decorated ornaments. The goddess of learning, Sarasvatī, supplied a necklace, Lord Brahmā supplied a lotus flower, and the inhabitants of Nāgaloka supplied earrings.**
