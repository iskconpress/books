====== SB 8.8.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ṛṣayaḥ kalpayāṁ cakrur
> ābhiṣekaṁ yathā-vidhi
> jagur bhadrāṇi gandharvā
> naṭyaś ca nanṛtur jaguḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---the great sages; [[synonyms:k:kalpayām]] [[synonyms:c:cakruḥ]]---executed; [[synonyms:ā:ābhiṣekam]]---the //abhiṣeka// ceremony, which is required during the installation of the Deity; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---as directed in the authorized scriptures; [[synonyms:j:jaguḥ]]---chanted Vedic //mantras//; [[synonyms:b:bhadrāṇi]]---all good fortune; [[synonyms:g:gandharvāḥ]]---and the inhabitants of Gandharvaloka; [[synonyms:n:naṭyaḥ]]---the women who were professional dancers; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:n:nanṛtuḥ]]---very nicely danced on the occasion; [[synonyms:j:jaguḥ]]---and sang authorized songs prescribed in the //Vedas.//

===== Translation =====

**The great sages performed the bathing ceremony of the goddess of fortune as directed in the authorized scriptures, the Gandharvas chanted all-auspicious Vedic mantras, and the professional women dancers very nicely danced and sang authorized songs prescribed in the Vedas.**
