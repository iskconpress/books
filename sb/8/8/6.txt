====== SB 8.8.6 ======

===== Text =====

> kaustubhākhyam abhūd ratnaṁ
> padmarāgo mahodadheḥ
> tasmin maṇau spṛhāṁ cakre
> vakṣo-'laṅkaraṇe hariḥ
> tato 'bhavat pārijātaḥ
> sura-loka-vibhūṣaṇam
> pūrayaty arthino yo 'rthaiḥ
> śaśvad bhuvi yathā bhavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaustubha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyam]]---known as Kaustubha; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---was generated; [[synonyms:r:ratnam]]---a valuable gem; [[synonyms:p:padmarāgaḥ]]---another gem, named Padmarāga; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:udadheḥ]]---from that great ocean of milk; [[synonyms:t:tasmin]]---that; [[synonyms:m:maṇau]]---jewel; [[synonyms:s:spṛhām]] [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---desired to possess; [[synonyms:v:vakṣaḥ]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkaraṇe]]---to decorate His chest; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Lord, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was generated; [[synonyms:p:pārijātaḥ]]---the celestial flower named //pārijāta//; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhūṣaṇam]]---which decorates the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:p:pūrayati]]---fulfills; [[synonyms:a:arthinaḥ]]---giving persons desiring material wealth; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---that which; [[synonyms:a:arthaiḥ]]---by what is desired; [[synonyms:ś:śaśvat]]---always; [[synonyms:b:bhuvi]]---on this planet; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---Your Lordship (Mahārāja Parīkṣit).

===== Translation =====

**Generated thereafter from the great ocean were the celebrated gems Kaustubha-maṇi and Padmarāga-maṇi. Lord Viṣṇu, to decorate His chest, desired to possess them. Generated next was the pārijāta flower, which decorates the celestial planets. O King, as you fulfill the desires of everyone on this planet by fulfilling all ambitions, the pārijāta fulfills the desires of everyone.**
