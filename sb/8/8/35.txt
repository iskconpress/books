====== SB 8.8.35 ======

===== Text =====

> tam ālokyāsurāḥ sarve
> kalasaṁ cāmṛtābhṛtam
> lipsantaḥ sarva-vastūni
> kalasaṁ tarasāharan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:ālokya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:a:asurāḥ]]---the demons; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kalasam]]---the container of nectar; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhṛtam]]---filled with nectar; [[synonyms:l:lipsantaḥ]]---desiring strongly; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:v:vastūni]]---all objects; [[synonyms:k:kalasam]]---the jug; [[synonyms:t:tarasā]]---immediately; [[synonyms:a:aharan]]---snatched away.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing Dhanvantari carrying the jug of nectar, the demons, desiring the jug and its contents, immediately snatched it away by force.**
