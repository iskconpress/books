====== SB 8.8.29 ======

===== Text =====

> niḥsattvā lolupā rājan
> nirudyogā gata-trapāḥ
> yadā copekṣitā lakṣmyā
> babhūvur daitya-dānavāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niḥsattvāḥ]]---without strength; [[synonyms:l:lolupāḥ]]---very greedy; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:n:nirudyogāḥ]]---frustrated; [[synonyms:g:gata]]-[[synonyms:t:trapāḥ]]---shameless; [[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:u:upekṣitāḥ]]---neglected; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmyā]]---by the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:b:babhūvuḥ]]---they became; [[synonyms:d:daitya]]-[[synonyms:d:dānavāḥ]]---the demons and Rākṣasas.

===== Translation =====

**O King, because of being neglected by the goddess of fortune, the demons and Rākṣasas were depressed, bewildered and frustrated, and thus they became shameless.**
