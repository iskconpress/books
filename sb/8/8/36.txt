====== SB 8.8.36 ======

===== Text =====

> nīyamāne 'surais tasmin
> kalase 'mṛta-bhājane
> viṣaṇṇa-manaso devā
> hariṁ śaraṇam āyayuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīyamāne]]---being carried; [[synonyms:a:asuraiḥ]]---by the demons; [[synonyms:t:tasmin]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kalase]]---jug; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhājane]]---containing nectar; [[synonyms:v:viṣaṇṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:manasaḥ]]---aggrieved in mind; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---unto the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇam]]---to take shelter; [[synonyms:ā:āyayuḥ]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**When the jug of nectar was carried off by the demons, the demigods were morose. Thus they sought shelter at the lotus feet of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari.**
