====== SB 8.8.32 ======

===== Text =====

> dīrgha-pīvara-dor-daṇḍaḥ
> kambu-grīvo 'ruṇekṣaṇaḥ
> śyāmalas taruṇaḥ sragvī
> sarvābharaṇa-bhūṣitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dīrgha]]---long; [[synonyms:p:pīvara]]---stout and strong; [[synonyms:d:doḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍaḥ]]---the arms; [[synonyms:k:kambu]]---like a conchshell; [[synonyms:g:grīvaḥ]]---the neck; [[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]-[[synonyms:ī:īkṣaṇaḥ]]---reddish eyes; [[synonyms:ś:śyāmalaḥ]]---blackish complexion; [[synonyms:t:taruṇaḥ]]---very young; [[synonyms:s:sragvī]]---wearing a flower garland; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:ābharaṇa]]---with ornaments; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣitaḥ]]---decorated.

===== Translation =====

**He was strongly built; his arms were long, stout and strong; his neck, which was marked with three lines, resembled a conchshell; his eyes were reddish; and his complexion was blackish. He was very young, he was garlanded with flowers, and his entire body was fully decorated with various ornaments.**
