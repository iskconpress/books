====== SB 8.8.27 ======

===== Text =====

> brahma-rudrāṅgiro-mukhyāḥ
> sarve viśva-sṛjo vibhum
> īḍire 'vitathair mantrais
> tal-liṅgaiḥ puṣpa-varṣiṇaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahma]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:r:rudra]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:a:aṅgiraḥ]]---the great sage Aṅgirā Muni; [[synonyms:m:mukhyāḥ]]---headed by; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:s:sṛjaḥ]]---the directors of universal management; [[synonyms:v:vibhum]]---the very great personality; [[synonyms:ī:īḍire]]---worshiped; [[synonyms:a:avitathaiḥ]]---real; [[synonyms:m:mantraiḥ]]---by chanting; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:l:liṅgaiḥ]]---worshiping the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣiṇaḥ]]---throwing flowers like showers.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Brahmā, Lord Śiva, the great sage Aṅgirā, and similar directors of universal management showered flowers and chanted mantras indicating the transcendental glories of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
