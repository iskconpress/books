====== SB 8.8.3 ======

===== Text =====

> tata uccaiḥśravā nāma
> hayo 'bhūc candra-pāṇḍuraḥ
> tasmin baliḥ spṛhāṁ cakre
> nendra īśvara-śikṣayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:u:uccaiḥśravāḥ]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by the name Uccaiḥśravā; [[synonyms:h:hayaḥ]]---a horse; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---was generated; [[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṇḍuraḥ]]---being as white as the moon; [[synonyms:t:tasmin]]---unto it; [[synonyms:b:baliḥ]]---Mahārāja Bali; [[synonyms:s:spṛhām]] [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---desired to possess; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the King of the demigods; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:ś:śikṣayā]]---by the previous advice of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, a horse named Uccaiḥśravā, which was as white as the moon, was generated. Bali Mahārāja desired to possess this horse, and Indra, the King of heaven, did not protest, for he had previously been so advised by the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
