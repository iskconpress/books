====== SB 8.8.28 ======

===== Text =====

> śriyāvalokitā devāḥ
> saprajāpatayaḥ prajāḥ
> śīlādi-guṇa-sampannā
> lebhire nirvṛtiṁ parām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śriyā]]---by the goddess of fortune, Lakṣmī; [[synonyms:a:avalokitāḥ]]---being seen favorably with mercy; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:prajāpatayaḥ]]---with all the //prajāpatis//; [[synonyms:p:prajāḥ]]---and their generations; [[synonyms:ś:śīla]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sampannāḥ]]---all blessed with good behavior and good characteristics; [[synonyms:l:lebhire]]---achieved; [[synonyms:n:nirvṛtim]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:p:parām]]---the ultimate.

===== Translation =====

**All the demigods, along with the prajāpatis and their descendants, being blessed by Lakṣmījī's glance upon them, were immediately enriched with good behavior and transcendental qualities. Thus they were very much satisfied.**
