====== SB 8.8.31 ======

===== Text =====

> athodadher mathyamānāt
> kāśyapair amṛtārthibhiḥ
> udatiṣṭhan mahārāja
> puruṣaḥ paramādbhutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:u:udadheḥ]]---from the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:m:mathyamānāt]]---while being churned; [[synonyms:k:kāśyapaiḥ]]---by the sons of Kaśyapa, namely the demigods and the demons; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:arthibhiḥ]]---anxious to get nectar from the churning; [[synonyms:u:udatiṣṭhat]]---there appeared; [[synonyms:m:mahārāja]]---O King; [[synonyms:p:puruṣaḥ]]---a male person; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---highly; [[synonyms:a:adbhutaḥ]]---wonderful.

===== Translation =====

**O King, thereafter, while the sons of Kaśyapa, both demons and demigods, were engaged in churning the ocean of milk, a very wonderful male person appeared.**
