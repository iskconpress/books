====== SB 8.14.9 ======

===== Text =====

> sargaṁ prajeśa-rūpeṇa
> dasyūn hanyāt svarāḍ-vapuḥ
> kāla-rūpeṇa sarveṣām
> abhāvāya pṛthag guṇaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sargam]]---creation of progeny; [[synonyms:p:prajā]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpeṇa]]---in the form of the Prajāpati Marīci and others; [[synonyms:d:dasyūn]]---thieves and rogues; [[synonyms:h:hanyāt]]---kills; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rāṭ]]-[[synonyms:v:vapuḥ]]---in the form of the king; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpeṇa]]---in the form of time; [[synonyms:s:sarveṣām]]---of everything; [[synonyms:a:abhāvāya]]---for the annihilation; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---different; [[synonyms:g:guṇaḥ]]---possessing qualities.

===== Translation =====

**In the form of Prajāpati Marīci, the Supreme Personality of Godhead creates progeny; becoming the king, He kills the thieves and rogues; and in the form of time, He annihilates everything. All the different qualities of material existence should be understood to be qualities of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
