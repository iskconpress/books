====== SB 8.14.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> manvantareṣu bhagavan
> yathā manv-ādayas tv ime
> yasmin karmaṇi ye yena
> niyuktās tad vadasva me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---King Parīkṣit said; [[synonyms:m:manvantareṣu]]---in the reign of each Manu; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O great sage; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the Manus and others; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:i:ime]]---these; [[synonyms:y:yasmin]]---in which; [[synonyms:k:karmaṇi]]---activities; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which persons; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:n:niyuktāḥ]]---appointed; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vadasva]]---kindly describe; [[synonyms:m:me]]---to me.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Parīkṣit inquired: O most opulent Śukadeva Gosvāmī, please explain to me how Manu and the others in each manvantara are engaged in their respective duties, and by whose order they are so engaged.**
