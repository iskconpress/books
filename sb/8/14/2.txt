====== SB 8.14.2 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-ṛṣir uvāca
> manavo manu-putrāś ca
> munayaś ca mahī-pate
> indrāḥ sura-gaṇāś caiva
> sarve puruṣa-śāsanāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:m:manavaḥ]]---all the Manus; [[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:p:putrāḥ]]---all the sons of Manu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]]---all the great sages; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mahī]]-[[synonyms:p:pate]]---O King; [[synonyms:i:indrāḥ]]---all the Indras; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāsanāḥ]]---under the rule of the Supreme person.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: The Manus, the sons of Manu, the great sages, the Indras and all the demigods, O King, are appointed by the Supreme Personality of Godhead in His various incarnations such as Yajña.**
