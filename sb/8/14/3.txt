====== SB 8.14.3 ======

===== Text =====

> yajñādayo yāḥ kathitāḥ
> pauruṣyas tanavo nṛpa
> manv-ādayo jagad-yātrāṁ
> nayanty ābhiḥ pracoditāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the Lord's incarnation known as Yajña and others; [[synonyms:y:yāḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kathitāḥ]]---already spoken of; [[synonyms:p:pauruṣyaḥ]]---of the Supreme Person; [[synonyms:t:tanavaḥ]]---incarnations; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the Manus and others; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrām]]---universal affairs; [[synonyms:n:nayanti]]---conduct; [[synonyms:ā:ābhiḥ]]---by the incarnations; [[synonyms:p:pracoditāḥ]]---being inspired.

===== Translation =====

**O King, I have already described to you various incarnations of the Lord, such as Yajña. The Manus and others are chosen by these incarnations, under whose direction they conduct the universal affairs.**

===== Purport =====

The Manus execute the orders of the Supreme Personality of Godhead in His various incarnations.
