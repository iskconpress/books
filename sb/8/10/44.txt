====== SB 8.10.44 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ śūlaṁ tataḥ prāsaṁ
> tatas tomaram ṛṣṭayaḥ
> yad yac chastraṁ samādadyāt
> sarvaṁ tad acchinad vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ś:śūlam]]---lance; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prāsam]]---the //prāsa// weapon; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tomaram]]---the //tomara// weapon; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣṭayaḥ]]---the //ṛṣṭi// weapons; [[synonyms:y:yat]] [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever and whichever; [[synonyms:ś:śastram]]---weapon; [[synonyms:s:samādadyāt]]---Bali Mahārāja tried to use; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---all of them; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---those same weapons; [[synonyms:a:acchinat]]---cut to pieces; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the great Indra.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, one by one, Bali Mahārāja used a lance, prāsa, tomara, ṛṣṭis and other weapons, but whatever weapons he took up, Indra immediately cut them to pieces.**
