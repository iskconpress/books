====== SB 8.10.49 ======

===== Text =====

> tato mahā-ghanā vyomni
> gambhīra-paruṣa-svanāḥ
> aṅgārān mumucur vātair
> āhatāḥ stanayitnavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:g:ghanāḥ]]---big clouds; [[synonyms:v:vyomni]]---in the sky; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]-[[synonyms:p:paruṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:svanāḥ]]---making very deep rumbling sounds; [[synonyms:a:aṅgārān]]---embers; [[synonyms:m:mumucuḥ]]---released; [[synonyms:v:vātaiḥ]]---by the strong winds; [[synonyms:ā:āhatāḥ]]---harassed; [[synonyms:s:stanayitnavaḥ]]---with the sound of thunder.

===== Translation =====

**Fierce clouds, harassed by strong winds, then appeared in the sky. Rumbling very gravely with the sound of thunder, they began to shower live coals.**
