====== SB 8.10.47 ======

===== Text =====

> mahoragāḥ samutpetur
> dandaśūkāḥ savṛścikāḥ
> siṁha-vyāghra-varāhāś ca
> mardayanto mahā-gajāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:uragāḥ]]---big serpents; [[synonyms:s:samutpetuḥ]]---fell upon them; [[synonyms:d:dandaśūkāḥ]]---other poisonous animals and insects; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛścikāḥ]]---with scorpions; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]---lions; [[synonyms:v:vyāghra]]---tigers; [[synonyms:v:varāhāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and forest boars; [[synonyms:m:mardayantaḥ]]---smashing; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:g:gajāḥ]]---great elephants.

===== Translation =====

**Scorpions, large snakes and many other poisonous animals, as well as lions, tigers, boars and great elephants, all began falling upon the demigod soldiers, crushing everything.**
