====== SB 8.10.9 ======

===== Text =====

> uṣṭraiḥ kecid ibhaiḥ kecid
> apare yuyudhuḥ kharaiḥ
> kecid gaura-mukhair ṛkṣair
> dvīpibhir haribhir bhaṭāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uṣṭraiḥ]]---on the backs of camels; [[synonyms:k:kecit]]---some persons; [[synonyms:i:ibhaiḥ]]---on the backs of elephants; [[synonyms:k:kecit]]---some persons; [[synonyms:a:apare]]---others; [[synonyms:y:yuyudhuḥ]]---engaged in fighting; [[synonyms:k:kharaiḥ]]---on the backs of asses; [[synonyms:k:kecit]]---some persons; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhaiḥ]]---on white-faced monkeys; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛkṣaiḥ]]---on red-faced monkeys; [[synonyms:d:dvīpibhiḥ]]---on the backs of tigers; [[synonyms:h:haribhiḥ]]---on the backs of lions; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭāḥ]]---all the soldiers engaged in this way.

===== Translation =====

**Some soldiers fought on the backs of camels, some on the backs of elephants, some on asses, some on white-faced and red-faced monkeys, some on tigers and some on lions. In this way, they all engaged in fighting.**
