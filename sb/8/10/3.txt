====== SB 8.10.3 ======

===== Text =====

> sapatnānāṁ parām ṛddhiṁ
> dṛṣṭvā te diti-nandanāḥ
> amṛṣyamāṇā utpetur
> devān pratyudyatāyudhāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sapatnānām]]---of their rivals, the demigods; [[synonyms:p:parām]]---the best; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛddhim]]---opulence; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---observing; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:d:diti]]-[[synonyms:n:nandanāḥ]]---the sons of Diti, the Daityas; [[synonyms:a:amṛṣyamāṇāḥ]]---being intolerant; [[synonyms:u:utpetuḥ]]---ran toward (just to create a disturbance); [[synonyms:d:devān]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:p:pratyudyata]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyudhāḥ]]---their weapons raised.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the victory of the demigods, the demons became intolerant of their superior opulence. Thus they began to march toward the demigods with raised weapons.**
