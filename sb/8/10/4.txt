====== SB 8.10.4 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ sura-gaṇāḥ sarve
> sudhayā pītayaidhitāḥ
> pratisaṁyuyudhuḥ śastrair
> nārāyaṇa-padāśrayāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:s:sudhayā]]---by the nectar; [[synonyms:p:pītayā]]---which had been drunk; [[synonyms:e:edhitāḥ]]---being enlivened by such drinking; [[synonyms:p:pratisaṁyuyudhuḥ]]---they counterattacked the demons; [[synonyms:ś:śastraiḥ]]---by regular weapons; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrayāḥ]]---their real weapon being shelter at the lotus feet of Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, being enlivened because of drinking the nectar, the demigods, who are always at the shelter of the lotus feet of Nārāyaṇa, used their various weapons to counterattack the demons in a fighting spirit.**
