====== SB 8.10.51 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ samudra udvelaḥ
> sarvataḥ pratyadṛśyata
> pracaṇḍa-vātair uddhūta-
> taraṅgāvarta-bhīṣaṇaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:samudraḥ]]---the sea; [[synonyms:u:udvelaḥ]]---being agitated; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:p:pratyadṛśyata]]---appeared before everyone's vision; [[synonyms:p:pracaṇḍa]]---fierce; [[synonyms:v:vātaiḥ]]---by the winds; [[synonyms:u:uddhūta]]---agitated; [[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---of the waves; [[synonyms:ā:āvarta]]---whirling water; [[synonyms:b:bhīṣaṇaḥ]]---ferocious.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, whirlpools and sea waves, agitated by fierce blasts of wind, appeared everywhere, before everyone's vision, in a furious flood.**
