====== SB 8.10.46 ======

===== Text =====

> tato nipetus taravo
> dahyamānā davāgninā
> śilāḥ saṭaṅka-śikharāś
> cūrṇayantyo dviṣad-balam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from that great mountain; [[synonyms:n:nipetuḥ]]---began to fall; [[synonyms:t:taravaḥ]]---large trees; [[synonyms:d:dahyamānāḥ]]---blazing in fire; [[synonyms:d:dava]]-[[synonyms:a:agninā]]---by the forest fire; [[synonyms:ś:śilāḥ]]---and stones; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭaṅka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śikharāḥ]]---having edges with points as sharp as stone picks; [[synonyms:c:cūrṇayantyaḥ]]---smashing; [[synonyms:d:dviṣat]]-[[synonyms:b:balam]]---the strength of the enemies.

===== Translation =====

**From that mountain fell trees blazing in a forest fire. Chips of stone, with sharp edges like picks, also fell and smashed the heads of the demigod soldiers.**
