====== SB 8.10.54 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ suparṇāṁsa-kṛtāṅghri-pallavaḥ
> piśaṅga-vāsā nava-kañja-locanaḥ
> adṛśyatāṣṭāyudha-bāhur ullasac-
> chrī-kaustubhānarghya-kirīṭa-kuṇḍalaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:suparṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁsa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅghri]]-[[synonyms:p:pallavaḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, whose lotus feet spread over the two shoulders of Garuḍa; [[synonyms:p:piśaṅga]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsāḥ]]---whose dress is yellow; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:k:kañja]]-[[synonyms:l:locanaḥ]]---and whose eyes are just like the petals of a newly blossomed lotus; [[synonyms:a:adṛśyata]]---became visible (in the presence of the demigods); [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyudha]]---equipped with eight kinds of weapons; [[synonyms:b:bāhuḥ]]---arms; [[synonyms:u:ullasat]]---brilliantly exhibiting; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:k:kaustubha]]---the Kaustubha gem; [[synonyms:a:anarghya]]---of incalculable value; [[synonyms:k:kirīṭa]]---helmet; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍalaḥ]]---having earrings.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead, whose eyes resemble the petals of a newly blossomed lotus, sat on the back of Garuḍa, spreading His lotus feet over Garuḍa's shoulders. Dressed in yellow, decorated by the Kaustubha gem and the goddess of fortune, and wearing an invaluable helmet and earrings, the Supreme Lord, holding various weapons in His eight hands, became visible to the demigods.**
