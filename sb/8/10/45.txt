====== SB 8.10.45 ======

===== Text =====

> sasarjāthāsurīṁ māyām
> antardhāna-gato 'suraḥ
> tataḥ prādurabhūc chailaḥ
> surānīkopari prabho

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sasarja]]---released; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---now; [[synonyms:ā:āsurīm]]---demoniac; [[synonyms:m:māyām]]---illusion; [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]]---out of vision; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---having gone; [[synonyms:a:asuraḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prādurabhūt]]---there appeared; [[synonyms:ś:śailaḥ]]---a big mountain; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:a:anīka]]-[[synonyms:u:upari]]---above the heads of the soldiers of the demigods; [[synonyms:p:prabho]]---O my lord.

===== Translation =====

**My dear King, Bali Mahārāja then disappeared and resorted to demoniac illusions. A giant mountain, generated from illusion, then appeared above the heads of the demigod soldiers.**
