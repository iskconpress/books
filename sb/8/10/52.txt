====== SB 8.10.52 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ daityair mahā-māyair
> alakṣya-gatibhī raṇe
> sṛjyamānāsu māyāsu
> viṣeduḥ sura-sainikāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:daityaiḥ]]---by the demons; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:māyaiḥ]]---who were expert in creating illusions; [[synonyms:a:alakṣya]]-[[synonyms:g:gatibhiḥ]]---but invisible; [[synonyms:r:raṇe]]---in the fight; [[synonyms:s:sṛjyamānāsu]] [[synonyms:m:māyāsu]]---because of the creation of such an illusory atmosphere; [[synonyms:v:viṣeduḥ]]---became morose; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:s:sainikāḥ]]---the soldiers of the demigods.

===== Translation =====

**While this magical atmosphere in the fight was being created by the invisible demons, who were expert in such illusions, the soldiers of the demigods became morose.**
