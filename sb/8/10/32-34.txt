====== SB 8.10.32-34 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛṣākapis tu jambhena
> mahiṣeṇa vibhāvasuḥ
> ilvalaḥ saha vātāpir
> brahma-putrair arindama
>
> kāmadevena durmarṣa
> utkalo mātṛbhiḥ saha
> bṛhaspatiś cośanasā
> narakeṇa śanaiścaraḥ
>
> maruto nivātakavacaiḥ
> kāleyair vasavo 'marāḥ
> viśvedevās tu paulomai
> rudrāḥ krodhavaśaiḥ saha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛṣākapiḥ]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:j:jambhena]]---with Jambha; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣeṇa]]---with Mahiṣāsura; [[synonyms:v:vibhāvasuḥ]]---the fire-god; [[synonyms:i:ilvalaḥ]]---the demon Ilvala; [[synonyms:s:saha]] [[synonyms:v:vātāpiḥ]]---with his brother, Vātāpi; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:p:putraiḥ]]---with the sons of Brahmā, such as Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:a:arim]]-[[synonyms:d:dama]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, suppressor of enemies; [[synonyms:k:kāmadevena]]---with Kāmadeva; [[synonyms:d:durmarṣaḥ]]---Durmarṣa; [[synonyms:u:utkalaḥ]]---the demon Utkala; [[synonyms:m:mātṛbhiḥ]] [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with the demigoddesses known as the Mātṛkās; [[synonyms:b:bṛhaspatiḥ]]---the demigod Bṛhaspati; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:u:uśanasā]]---with Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:n:narakeṇa]]---with the demon known as Naraka; [[synonyms:ś:śanaiścaraḥ]]---the demigod Śani, or Saturn; [[synonyms:m:marutaḥ]]---the demigods of air; [[synonyms:n:nivātakavacaiḥ]]---with the demon Nivātakavaca; [[synonyms:k:kāleyaiḥ]]---with the Kālakeyas; [[synonyms:v:vasavaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:amarāḥ]]---the Vasus fought; [[synonyms:v:viśvedevāḥ]]---the Viśvedeva demigods; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:paulomaiḥ]]---with the Paulomas; [[synonyms:r:rudrāḥ]]---the eleven Rudras; [[synonyms:k:krodhavaśaiḥ]] [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with the Krodhavaśa demons.

===== Translation =====

**O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, suppressor of enemies [Arindama], Lord Śiva fought with Jambha, and Vibhāvasu fought with Mahiṣāsura. Ilvala, along with his brother Vātāpi, fought the sons of Lord Brahmā. Durmarṣa fought with Cupid, the demon Utkala with the Mātṛkā demigoddesses, Bṛhaspati with Śukrācārya, and Śanaiścara [Saturn] with Narakāsura. The Maruts fought Nivātakavaca, the Vasus fought the Kālakeya demons, the Viśvedeva demigods fought the Pauloma demons, and the Rudras fought the Krodhavaśa demons, who were victims of anger.**
