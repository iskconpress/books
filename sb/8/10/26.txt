====== SB 8.10.26 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyāsan sarvato devā
> nānā-vāha-dhvajāyudhāḥ
> lokapālāḥ saha-gaṇair
> vāyv-agni-varuṇādayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Lord Indra; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---situated; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---all around; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:v:vāha]]---with varieties of carriers; [[synonyms:d:dhvaja]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyudhāḥ]]---and with flags and weapons; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:p:pālāḥ]]---all the chiefs of various higher planetary systems; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:g:gaṇaiḥ]]---their associates; [[synonyms:v:vāyu]]---the demigod controlling air; [[synonyms:a:agni]]---the demigod controlling fire; [[synonyms:v:varuṇa]]---the demigod controlling water; [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---all of them surrounding Lord Indra.

===== Translation =====

**Surrounding Lord Indra, King of heaven, were the demigods, seated on various types of vehicles and decorated with flags and weapons. Present among them were Vāyu, Agni, Varuṇa and other rulers of various planets, along with their associates.**
