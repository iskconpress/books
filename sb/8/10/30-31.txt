====== SB 8.10.30-31 ======

===== Text =====

> aparājitena namucir
> aśvinau vṛṣaparvaṇā
> sūryo bali-sutair devo
> bāṇa-jyeṣṭhaiḥ śatena ca
>
> rāhuṇā ca tathā somaḥ
> pulomnā yuyudhe 'nilaḥ
> niśumbha-śumbhayor devī
> bhadrakālī tarasvinī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparājitena]]---with the demigod Aparājita; [[synonyms:n:namuciḥ]]---the demon Namuci; [[synonyms:a:aśvinau]]---the Aśvinī brothers; [[synonyms:v:vṛṣaparvaṇā]]---with the demon Vṛṣaparvā; [[synonyms:s:sūryaḥ]]---the sun-god; [[synonyms:b:bali]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaiḥ]]---with the sons of Bali; [[synonyms:d:devaḥ]]---the god; [[synonyms:b:bāṇa]]-[[synonyms:j:jyeṣṭhaiḥ]]---the chief of whom is Bāṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śatena]]---numbering one hundred; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rāhuṇā]]---by Rāhu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well as; [[synonyms:s:somaḥ]]---the moon-god; [[synonyms:p:pulomnā]]---Puloma; [[synonyms:y:yuyudhe]]---fought; [[synonyms:a:anilaḥ]]---the demigod Anila, who controls air; [[synonyms:n:niśumbha]]---the demon Niśumbha; [[synonyms:ś:śumbhayoḥ]]---with Śumbha; [[synonyms:d:devī]]---the goddess Durgā; [[synonyms:b:bhadrakālī]]---Bhadra Kālī; [[synonyms:t:tarasvinī]]---extremely powerful.

===== Translation =====

**The demigod Aparājita fought with Namuci, and the two Aśvinī-kumāra brothers fought with Vṛṣaparvā. The sun-god fought with the one hundred sons of Mahārāja Bali, headed by Bāṇa, and the moon-god fought with Rāhu. The demigod controlling air fought with Puloma, and Śumbha and Niśumbha fought the supremely powerful material energy, Durgādevī, who is called Bhadra Kālī.**
