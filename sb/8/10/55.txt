====== SB 8.10.55 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmin praviṣṭe 'sura-kūṭa-karmajā
> māyā vineśur mahinā mahīyasaḥ
> svapno yathā hi pratibodha āgate
> hari-smṛtiḥ sarva-vipad-vimokṣaṇam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmin]] [[synonyms:p:praviṣṭe]]---upon the entrance of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:asura]]---of the demons; [[synonyms:k:kūṭa]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]-[[synonyms:j:jā]]---because of the illusory, magical activities; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]---the false manifestations; [[synonyms:v:vineśuḥ]]---were immediately curbed; [[synonyms:m:mahinā]]---by the superior power; [[synonyms:m:mahīyasaḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is greater than the greatest; [[synonyms:s:svapnaḥ]]---dreams; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:pratibodhe]]---when awakening; [[synonyms:ā:āgate]]---has arrived; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:s:smṛtiḥ]]---remembrance of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:v:vipat]]---of all kinds of dangerous situations; [[synonyms:v:vimokṣaṇam]]---immediately vanquishes.

===== Translation =====

**As the dangers of a dream cease when the dreamer awakens, the illusions created by the jugglery of the demons were vanquished by the transcendental prowess of the Supreme Personality of Godhead as soon as He entered the battlefield. Indeed, simply by remembrance of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, one becomes free from all dangers.**
