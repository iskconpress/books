====== SB 8.10.37 ======

===== Text =====

> gajās turaṅgāḥ sarathāḥ padātayaḥ
> sāroha-vāhā vividhā vikhaṇḍitāḥ
> nikṛtta-bāhūru-śirodharāṅghrayaś
> chinna-dhvajeṣvāsa-tanutra-bhūṣaṇāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gajāḥ]]---elephants; [[synonyms:t:turaṅgāḥ]]---horses; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:r:rathāḥ]]---with chariots; [[synonyms:p:padātayaḥ]]---infantry soldiers; [[synonyms:s:sāroha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāhāḥ]]---carriers with the riders; [[synonyms:v:vividhāḥ]]---varieties; [[synonyms:v:vikhaṇḍitāḥ]]---cut to pieces; [[synonyms:n:nikṛtta]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhu]]---cut off arms; [[synonyms:ū:ūru]]---thighs; [[synonyms:ś:śirodhara]]---necks; [[synonyms:a:aṅghrayaḥ]]---legs; [[synonyms:c:chinna]]---cut up; [[synonyms:d:dhvaja]]---flags; [[synonyms:i:iṣvāsa]]---bows; [[synonyms:t:tanutra]]---armor; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣaṇāḥ]]---ornaments.

===== Translation =====

**The elephants, horses, chariots, charioteers, infantry soldiers and various kinds of carriers, along with their riders, were slashed to pieces. The arms, thighs, necks and legs of the soldiers were severed, and their flags, bows, armor and ornaments were torn apart.**
