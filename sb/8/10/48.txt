====== SB 8.10.48 ======

===== Text =====

> yātudhānyaś ca śataśaḥ
> śūla-hastā vivāsasaḥ
> chindhi bhindhīti vādinyas
> tathā rakṣo-gaṇāḥ prabho

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yātudhānyaḥ]]---carnivorous female demons; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śataśaḥ]]---hundreds upon hundreds; [[synonyms:ś:śūla]]-[[synonyms:h:hastāḥ]]---every one of them with a trident in hand; [[synonyms:v:vivāsasaḥ]]---completely naked; [[synonyms:c:chindhi]]---cut to pieces; [[synonyms:b:bhindhi]]---pierce; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vādinyaḥ]]---talking; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---in that way; [[synonyms:r:rakṣaḥ]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇāḥ]]---a band of Rākṣasas (a type of demon); [[synonyms:p:prabho]]---O my King.

===== Translation =====

**O my King, many hundreds of male and female carnivorous demons, completely naked and carrying tridents in their hands, then appeared, crying the slogans "Cut them to pieces! Pierce them!"**
