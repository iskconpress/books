====== SB 8.10.35 ======

===== Text =====

> ta evam ājāv asurāḥ surendrā
> dvandvena saṁhatya ca yudhyamānāḥ
> anyonyam āsādya nijaghnur ojasā
> jigīṣavas tīkṣṇa-śarāsi-tomaraiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ā:ājau]]---on the battlefield; [[synonyms:a:asurāḥ]]---the demons; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:i:indrāḥ]]---and the demigods; [[synonyms:d:dvandvena]]---two by two; [[synonyms:s:saṁhatya]]---mixing together; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yudhyamānāḥ]]---engaged in fighting; [[synonyms:a:anyonyam]]---with one another; [[synonyms:ā:āsādya]]---approaching; [[synonyms:n:nijaghnuḥ]]---slashed with weapons and killed; [[synonyms:o:ojasā]]---with great strength; [[synonyms:j:jigīṣavaḥ]]---everyone desiring victory; [[synonyms:t:tīkṣṇa]]---sharp; [[synonyms:ś:śara]]---with arrows; [[synonyms:a:asi]]---with swords; [[synonyms:t:tomaraiḥ]]---with lances.

===== Translation =====

**All of these demigods and demons assembled on the battlefield with a fighting spirit and attacked one another with great strength. All of them desiring victory, they fought in pairs, hitting one another severely with sharpened arrows, swords and lances.**
