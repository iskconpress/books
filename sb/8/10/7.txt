====== SB 8.10.7 ======

===== Text =====

> śaṅkha-tūrya-mṛdaṅgānāṁ
> bherī-ḍamariṇāṁ mahān
> hasty-aśva-ratha-pattīnāṁ
> nadatāṁ nisvano 'bhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---of conchshells; [[synonyms:t:tūrya]]---of big bugles; [[synonyms:m:mṛdaṅgānām]]---and of drums; [[synonyms:b:bherī]]---of bugles; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍamariṇām]]---of kettledrums; [[synonyms:m:mahān]]---great and tumultuous; [[synonyms:h:hasti]]---of elephants; [[synonyms:a:aśva]]---of horses; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:p:pattīnām]]---of fighters on chariots or on the ground; [[synonyms:n:nadatām]]---all of them making sounds together; [[synonyms:n:nisvanaḥ]]---a tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---so became.

===== Translation =====

**The sounds of the conchshells, bugles, drums, bherīs and ḍamarīs [kettledrums], as well as the sounds made by the elephants, horses and soldiers, who were both on chariots and on foot, were tumultuous.**
