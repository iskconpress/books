====== SB 8.18.17 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmā ity upanītāya
> yakṣa-rāṭ pātrikām adāt
> bhikṣāṁ bhagavatī sākṣād
> umādād ambikā satī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto Him (Lord Vāmanadeva); [[synonyms:i:iti]]---in this way; [[synonyms:u:upanītāya]]---who had undergone His sacred thread ceremony; [[synonyms:y:yakṣa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāṭ]]---Kuvera, the treasurer of heaven and King of the Yakṣas; [[synonyms:p:pātrikām]]---a pot for begging alms; [[synonyms:a:adāt]]---delivered; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣām]]---alms; [[synonyms:b:bhagavatī]]---mother Bhavānī, the wife of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:u:umā]]---Umā; [[synonyms:a:adāt]]---gave; [[synonyms:a:ambikā]]---the mother of the universe; [[synonyms:s:satī]]---the chaste.

===== Translation =====

**When Vāmanadeva had thus been given the sacred thread, Kuvera, King of the Yakṣas, gave Him a pot for begging alms, and mother Bhagavatī, the wife of Lord Śiva and most chaste mother of the entire universe, gave Him His first alms.**
