====== SB 8.18.18 ======

===== Text =====

> sa brahma-varcasenaivaṁ
> sabhāṁ sambhāvito vaṭuḥ
> brahmarṣi-gaṇa-sañjuṣṭām
> atyarocata māriṣaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He (Vāmanadeva); [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:v:varcasena]]---by His Brahman effulgence; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sabhām]]---the assembly; [[synonyms:s:sambhāvitaḥ]]---having been welcomed by everyone; [[synonyms:v:vaṭuḥ]]---the //brahmacārī//; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sañjuṣṭām]]---filled with great //brāhmaṇa// sages; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:a:arocata]]---surpassing, appeared beautiful; [[synonyms:m:māriṣaḥ]]---the best of the //brahmacārīs.//

===== Translation =====

**Having thus been welcomed by everyone, Lord Vāmanadeva, the best of the brahmacārīs, exhibited His Brahman effulgence. Thus He surpassed in beauty that entire assembly, which was filled with great saintly brāhmaṇas.**
