====== SB 8.18.19 ======

===== Text =====

> samiddham āhitaṁ vahniṁ
> kṛtvā parisamūhanam
> paristīrya samabhyarcya
> samidbhir ajuhod dvijaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:samiddham]]---blazing; [[synonyms:ā:āhitam]]---being situated; [[synonyms:v:vahnim]]---the fire; [[synonyms:k:kṛtvā]]---after making; [[synonyms:p:parisamūhanam]]---properly; [[synonyms:p:paristīrya]]---surpassing; [[synonyms:s:samabhyarcya]]---after offering worship; [[synonyms:s:samidbhiḥ]]---with sacrificial offerings; [[synonyms:a:ajuhot]]---completed the fire sacrifice; [[synonyms:d:dvijaḥ]]---the best of the //brāhmaṇas.//

===== Translation =====

**After Lord Śrī Vāmanadeva set a sacrificial fire, He offered worship and performed a fire sacrifice on the sacrificial field.**
