====== SB 8.18.23 ======

===== Text =====

> itthaṁ saśiṣyeṣu bhṛguṣv anekadhā
> vitarkyamāṇo bhagavān sa vāmanaḥ
> chatraṁ sadaṇḍaṁ sajalaṁ kamaṇḍaluṁ
> viveśa bibhrad dhayamedha-vāṭam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ittham]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiṣyeṣu]]---with their disciples; [[synonyms:b:bhṛguṣu]]---among the Bhṛgus; [[synonyms:a:anekadhā]]---in many ways; [[synonyms:v:vitarkyamāṇaḥ]]---being talked and argued about; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vāmanaḥ]]---Lord Vāmana; [[synonyms:c:chatram]]---umbrella; [[synonyms:s:sadaṇḍam]]---with the rod; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:j:jalam]]---filled with water; [[synonyms:k:kamaṇḍalum]]---waterpot; [[synonyms:v:viveśa]]---entered; [[synonyms:b:bibhrat]]---taking in hand; [[synonyms:h:hayamedha]]---of the //aśvamedha// sacrifice; [[synonyms:v:vāṭam]]---the arena.

===== Translation =====

**While the priests of the Bhṛgu dynasty and their disciples talked and argued in various ways, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Vāmanadeva, holding in His hands the rod, the umbrella and a waterpot full of water, entered the arena of the aśvamedha sacrifice.**
