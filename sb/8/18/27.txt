====== SB 8.18.27 ======

===== Text =====

> svāgatenābhinandyātha
> pādau bhagavato baliḥ
> avanijyārcayām āsa
> mukta-saṅga-manoramam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgatena]]---by words of welcome; [[synonyms:a:abhinandya]]---welcoming; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thus; [[synonyms:p:pādau]]---the two lotus feet; [[synonyms:b:bhagavataḥ]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:baliḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:a:avanijya]]---washing; [[synonyms:a:arcayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---worshiped; [[synonyms:m:mukta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]-[[synonyms:m:manoramam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is beautiful to the liberated souls.

===== Translation =====

**Thus offering a proper reception to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is always beautiful to the liberated souls, Bali Mahārāja worshiped Him by washing His lotus feet.**
