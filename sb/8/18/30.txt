====== SB 8.18.30 ======

===== Text =====

> adya naḥ pitaras tṛptā
> adya naḥ pāvitaṁ kulam
> adya sviṣṭaḥ kratur ayaṁ
> yad bhavān āgato gṛhān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adya]]---today; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:p:pitaraḥ]]---forefathers; [[synonyms:t:tṛptāḥ]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:a:adya]]---today; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:p:pāvitam]]---purified; [[synonyms:k:kulam]]---the whole family; [[synonyms:a:adya]]---today; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:i:iṣṭaḥ]]---properly executed; [[synonyms:k:kratuḥ]]---the sacrifice; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---Your Lordship; [[synonyms:ā:āgataḥ]]---arrived; [[synonyms:g:gṛhān]]---at our residence.

===== Translation =====

**O my Lord, because You have kindly arrived at our home, all my forefathers are satisfied, our family and entire dynasty have been sanctified, and the sacrifice we are performing is now complete because of Your presence.**
