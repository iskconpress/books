====== SB 8.18.16 ======

===== Text =====

> kamaṇḍaluṁ veda-garbhaḥ
> kuśān saptarṣayo daduḥ
> akṣa-mālāṁ mahārāja
> sarasvaty avyayātmanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kamaṇḍalum]]---a waterpot; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:g:garbhaḥ]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:k:kuśān]]---//kuśa// grass; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---the seven sages; [[synonyms:d:daduḥ]]---offered; [[synonyms:a:akṣa]]-[[synonyms:m:mālām]]---a string of Rudrākṣa beads; [[synonyms:m:mahārāja]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---the goddess Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:a:avyaya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---to the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**O King, Lord Brahmā offered a waterpot to the inexhaustible Supreme Personality of Godhead, the seven sages offered Him kuśa grass, and mother Sarasvatī gave Him a string of Rudrākṣa beads.**
