====== SB 8.18.32 ======

===== Text =====

> yad yad vaṭo vāñchasi tat pratīccha me
> tvām arthinaṁ vipra-sutānutarkaye
> gāṁ kāñcanaṁ guṇavad dhāma mṛṣṭaṁ
> tathānna-peyam uta vā vipra-kanyām
> grāmān samṛddhāṁs turagān gajān vā
> rathāṁs tathārhattama sampratīccha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yat]] [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever; [[synonyms:v:vaṭo]]---O //brahmacārī//; [[synonyms:v:vāñchasi]]---You desire; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:p:pratīccha]]---You may take; [[synonyms:m:me]]---from me; [[synonyms:t:tvām]]---You; [[synonyms:a:arthinam]]---desiring something; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:s:suta]]---O son of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:a:anutarkaye]]---I consider; [[synonyms:g:gām]]---a cow; [[synonyms:k:kāñcanam]]---gold; [[synonyms:g:guṇavat]] [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---a furnished residence; [[synonyms:m:mṛṣṭam]]---palatable; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well as; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food grains; [[synonyms:p:peyam]]---drink; [[synonyms:u:uta]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:k:kanyām]]---the daughter of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:g:grāmān]]---villages; [[synonyms:s:samṛddhān]]---prosperous; [[synonyms:t:turagān]]---horses; [[synonyms:g:gajān]]---elephants; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:r:rathān]]---chariots; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well as; [[synonyms:a:arhat]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---O best of those who are worshipable; [[synonyms:s:sampratīccha]]---You may take.

===== Translation =====

**O son of a brāhmaṇa, it appears that You have come here to ask me for something. Therefore, whatever You want You may take from me. O best of those who are worshipable. You may take from me a cow, gold, a furnished house, palatable food and drink, the daughter of a brāhmaṇa for Your wife, prosperous villages, horses, elephants, chariots or whatever You desire.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Eighth Canto, Eighteenth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "Lord Vāmanadeva, the Dwarf Incarnation."
