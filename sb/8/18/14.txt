====== SB 8.18.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyopanīyamānasya
> sāvitrīṁ savitābravīt
> bṛhaspatir brahma-sūtraṁ
> mekhalāṁ kaśyapo 'dadāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Lord Vāmanadeva; [[synonyms:u:upanīyamānasya]]---at the time of His being offered the sacred thread ceremony; [[synonyms:s:sāvitrīm]]---the Gāyatrī //mantra//; [[synonyms:s:savitā]]---the sun-god; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---chanted; [[synonyms:b:bṛhaspatiḥ]]---Bṛhaspati, the //guru// of the demigods; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtram]]---the sacred thread; [[synonyms:m:mekhalām]]---the belt of straw; [[synonyms:k:kaśyapaḥ]]---Kaśyapa Muni; [[synonyms:a:adadāt]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**At the sacred thread ceremony of Vāmanadeva, the sun-god personally uttered the Gāyatrī mantra, Bṛhaspati offered the sacred thread, and Kaśyapa Muni offered a straw belt.**
