====== SB 8.18.31 ======

===== Text =====

> adyāgnayo me suhutā yathā-vidhi
> dvijātmaja tvac-caraṇāvanejanaiḥ
> hatāṁhaso vārbhir iyaṁ ca bhūr aho
> tathā punītā tanubhiḥ padais tava

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adya]]---today; [[synonyms:a:agnayaḥ]]---the sacrificial fires; [[synonyms:m:me]]---executed by me; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:h:hutāḥ]]---properly offered oblations; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---in terms of the śāstric injunction; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmaja]]---O son of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:t:tvat]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avanejanaiḥ]]---which washed Your lotus feet; [[synonyms:h:hata]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁhasaḥ]]---who have become cleansed of all sinful reactions; [[synonyms:v:vārbhiḥ]]---by the water; [[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:b:bhūḥ]]---the surface of the globe; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---oh; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well; [[synonyms:p:punītā]]---sanctified; [[synonyms:t:tanubhiḥ]]---small; [[synonyms:p:padaiḥ]]---by the touch of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---Your.

===== Translation =====

**O son of a brāhmaṇa, today the fire of sacrifice is ablaze according to the injunction of the śāstra, and I have been freed from all the sinful reactions of my life by the water that has washed Your lotus feet. O my Lord, by the touch of Your small lotus feet the entire surface of the world has been sanctified.**
