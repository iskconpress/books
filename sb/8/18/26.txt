====== SB 8.18.26 ======

===== Text =====

> yajamānaḥ pramudito
> darśanīyaṁ manoramam
> rūpānurūpāvayavaṁ
> tasmā āsanam āharat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yajamānaḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja, who had engaged all the priests in performing the sacrifice; [[synonyms:p:pramuditaḥ]]---being very jubilant; [[synonyms:d:darśanīyam]]---pleasing to see; [[synonyms:m:manoramam]]---so beautiful; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---with beauty; [[synonyms:a:anurūpa]]---equal to His bodily beauty; [[synonyms:a:avayavam]]---all the different parts of the body; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:ā:āsanam]]---a sitting place; [[synonyms:ā:āharat]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**Bali Mahārāja, jubilant at seeing Lord Vāmanadeva, whose beautiful limbs contributed equally to the beauty of His entire body, offered Him a seat with great satisfaction.**
