====== SB 8.18.7 ======

===== Text =====

> śaṅkha-dundubhayo nedur
> mṛdaṅga-paṇavānakāḥ
> citra-vāditra-tūryāṇāṁ
> nirghoṣas tumulo 'bhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---conchshells; [[synonyms:d:dundubhayaḥ]]---kettledrums; [[synonyms:n:neduḥ]]---vibrated; [[synonyms:m:mṛdaṅga]]---drums; [[synonyms:p:paṇava]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānakāḥ]]---drums named //paṇavas// and //ānakas//; [[synonyms:c:citra]]---various; [[synonyms:v:vāditra]]---of these vibrations of musical sound; [[synonyms:t:tūryāṇām]]---and of other instruments; [[synonyms:n:nirghoṣaḥ]]---the loud sound; [[synonyms:t:tumulaḥ]]---tumultuous; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**Conchshells, kettledrums, drums, paṇavas and ānakas vibrated in concert. The sound of these and various other instruments was tumultuous.**
