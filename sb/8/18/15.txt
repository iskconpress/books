====== SB 8.18.15 ======

===== Text =====

> dadau kṛṣṇājinaṁ bhūmir
> daṇḍaṁ somo vanaspatiḥ
> kaupīnācchādanaṁ mātā
> dyauś chatraṁ jagataḥ pateḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dadau]]---gave, offered; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:ajinam]]---the skin of a deer; [[synonyms:b:bhūmiḥ]]---mother earth; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍam]]---a //brahmacārī's// rod; [[synonyms:s:somaḥ]]---the moon-god; [[synonyms:v:vanaḥ]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the king of the forest; [[synonyms:k:kaupīna]]---the underwear; [[synonyms:ā:ācchādanam]]---covering the body; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---His mother, Aditi; [[synonyms:d:dyauḥ]]---the heavenly kingdom; [[synonyms:c:chatram]]---an umbrella; [[synonyms:j:jagataḥ]]---of the whole universe; [[synonyms:p:pateḥ]]---of the master.

===== Translation =====

**Mother earth gave Him a deerskin, and the demigod of the moon, who is the king of the forest, gave Him a brahma-daṇḍa [the rod of a brahmacārī]. His mother, Aditi, gave Him cloth for underwear, and the deity presiding over the heavenly kingdom offered Him an umbrella.**
