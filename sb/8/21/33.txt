====== SB 8.21.33 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛthā manorathas tasya
> dūraḥ svargaḥ pataty adhaḥ
> pratiśrutasyādānena
> yo 'rthinaṁ vipralambhate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛthā]]---without any good result; [[synonyms:m:manorathaḥ]]---mental concoction; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him; [[synonyms:d:dūraḥ]]---far away; [[synonyms:s:svargaḥ]]---elevation to the higher planetary system; [[synonyms:p:patati]]---falls down; [[synonyms:a:adhaḥ]]---to a hellish condition of life; [[synonyms:p:pratiśrutasya]]---things promised; [[synonyms:a:adānena]]---being unable to give; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:a:arthinam]]---a beggar; [[synonyms:v:vipralambhate]]---cheats.

===== Translation =====

**Far from being elevated to the heavenly planets or fulfilling one's desire, one who does not properly give a beggar what he has promised falls down to a hellish condition of life.**
