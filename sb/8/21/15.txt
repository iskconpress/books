====== SB 8.21.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tān abhidravato dṛṣṭvā
> ditijānīkapān nṛpa
> prahasyānucarā viṣṇoḥ
> pratyaṣedhann udāyudhāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tān]]---them; [[synonyms:a:abhidravataḥ]]---thus going forward; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:d:ditija]]-[[synonyms:a:anīka]]-[[synonyms:p:pān]]---the soldiers of the demons; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:p:prahasya]]---smiling; [[synonyms:a:anucarāḥ]]---the associates; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:p:pratyaṣedhan]]---forbade; [[synonyms:u:udāyudhāḥ]]---taking up their weapons.

===== Translation =====

**O King, when the associates of Lord Viṣṇu saw the soldiers of the demons coming forward in violence, they smiled. Taking up their weapons, they forbade the demons to continue their attempt.**
