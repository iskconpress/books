====== SB 8.21.30 ======

===== Text =====

> yāvat tapaty asau gobhir
> yāvad induḥ sahoḍubhiḥ
> yāvad varṣati parjanyas
> tāvatī bhūr iyaṁ tava

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as far as; [[synonyms:t:tapati]]---is shining; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---the sun; [[synonyms:g:gobhiḥ]]---by the sunshine; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long or as far as; [[synonyms:i:induḥ]]---the moon; [[synonyms:s:saha]]-[[synonyms:u:uḍubhiḥ]]---with the luminaries or stars; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as far as; [[synonyms:v:varṣati]]---are pouring rain; [[synonyms:p:parjanyaḥ]]---the clouds; [[synonyms:t:tāvatī]]---to that much distance; [[synonyms:b:bhūḥ]]---land; [[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---in your possession.

===== Translation =====

**As far as the sun and moon shine with the stars and as far as the clouds pour rain, all the land throughout the universe is in your possession.**
