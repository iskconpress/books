====== SB 8.21.16-17 ======

===== Text =====

> nandaḥ sunando 'tha jayo
> vijayaḥ prabalo balaḥ
> kumudaḥ kumudākṣaś ca
> viṣvaksenaḥ patattrirāṭ
>
> jayantaḥ śrutadevaś ca
> puṣpadanto 'tha sātvataḥ
> sarve nāgāyuta-prāṇāś
> camūṁ te jaghnur āsurīm

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nandaḥ]] [[synonyms:s:sunandaḥ]]---the associates of Lord Viṣṇu such as Nanda and Sunanda; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---in this way; [[synonyms:j:jayaḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vijayaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:prabalaḥ]] [[synonyms:b:balaḥ]] [[synonyms:k:kumudaḥ]] [[synonyms:k:kumudākṣaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]] [[synonyms:v:viṣvaksenaḥ]]---as well as Jaya, Vijaya, Prabala, Bala, Kumada, Kumudākṣa and Viṣvaksena; [[synonyms:p:patattri]]-[[synonyms:r:rāṭ]]---Garuḍa, the king of the birds; [[synonyms:j:jayantaḥ]] [[synonyms:ś:śrutadevaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]] [[synonyms:p:puṣpadantaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:atha]] [[synonyms:s:sātvataḥ]]---Jayanta, Śrutadeva, Puṣpadanta and Sātvata; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:n:nāga]]-[[synonyms:a:ayuta]]-[[synonyms:p:prāṇāḥ]]---as powerful as ten thousand elephants; [[synonyms:c:camūm]]---the soldiers of the demons; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they; [[synonyms:j:jaghnuḥ]]---killed; [[synonyms:ā:āsurīm]]---demoniac.

===== Translation =====

**Nanda, Sunanda, Jaya, Vijaya, Prabala, Bala, Kumuda, Kumudākṣa, Viṣvaksena, Patattrirāṭ [Garuḍa], Jayanta, Śrutadeva, Puṣpadanta and Sātvata were all associates of Lord Viṣṇu. They were as powerful as ten thousand elephants, and now they began killing the soldiers of the demons.**
