====== SB 8.21.27 ======

===== Text =====

> hāhākāro mahān āsīd
> rodasyoḥ sarvato diśam
> nigṛhyamāṇe 'sura-patau
> viṣṇunā prabhaviṣṇunā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hāhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraḥ]]---a tumultuous roar of lamentation; [[synonyms:m:mahān]]---great; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---there was; [[synonyms:r:rodasyoḥ]]---in both the lower and upper planetary systems; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:d:diśam]]---all directions; [[synonyms:n:nigṛhyamāṇe]]---because of being suppressed; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:p:patau]]---when Bali Mahārāja, the King of the demons; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇunā]]---by Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:p:prabhaviṣṇunā]]---who is the most powerful everywhere.

===== Translation =====

**When Bali Mahārāja was thus arrested by Lord Viṣṇu, who is the most powerful, there was a great roar of lamentation in all directions throughout the upper and lower planetary systems of the universe.**
