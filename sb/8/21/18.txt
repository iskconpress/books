====== SB 8.21.18 ======

===== Text =====

> hanyamānān svakān dṛṣṭvā
> puruṣānucarair baliḥ
> vārayām āsa saṁrabdhān
> kāvya-śāpam anusmaran

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hanyamānān]]---being killed; [[synonyms:s:svakān]]---his own soldiers; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:anucaraiḥ]]---by the associates of the Supreme Person; [[synonyms:b:baliḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:v:vārayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---forbade; [[synonyms:s:saṁrabdhān]]---even though they were very angry; [[synonyms:k:kāvya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāpam]]---the curse given by Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:a:anusmaran]]---remembering.

===== Translation =====

**When Bali Mahārāja saw that his own soldiers were being killed by the associates of Lord Viṣṇu, he remembered the curse of Śukrācārya and forbade his soldiers to continue fighting.**
