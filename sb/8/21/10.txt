====== SB 8.21.10 ======

===== Text =====

> na vāyaṁ brahma-bandhur
> viṣṇur māyāvināṁ varaḥ
> dvija-rūpa-praticchanno
> deva-kāryaṁ cikīrṣati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhuḥ]]---Vāmanadeva, in the form of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇuḥ]]---He is Lord Viṣṇu Himself; [[synonyms:m:māyāvinām]]---of all cheaters; [[synonyms:v:varaḥ]]---the greatest; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---by assuming the form of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:praticchannaḥ]]---is disguised for the purpose of cheating; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāryam]]---the interest of the demigods; [[synonyms:c:cikīrṣati]]---He is trying for.

===== Translation =====

**"This Vāmana is certainly not a brāhmaṇa but the best of cheaters, Lord Viṣṇu. Assuming the form of a brāhmaṇa, He has covered His own form, and thus He is working for the interests of the demigods.**
