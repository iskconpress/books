====== SB 8.21.29 ======

===== Text =====

> padāni trīṇi dattāni
> bhūmer mahyaṁ tvayāsura
> dvābhyāṁ krāntā mahī sarvā
> tṛtīyam upakalpaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:padāni]]---footsteps; [[synonyms:t:trīṇi]]---three; [[synonyms:d:dattāni]]---have been given; [[synonyms:b:bhūmeḥ]]---of land; [[synonyms:m:mahyam]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by you; [[synonyms:a:asura]]---O King of the demons; [[synonyms:d:dvābhyām]]---by two steps; [[synonyms:k:krāntā]]---have been occupied; [[synonyms:m:mahī]]---all the land; [[synonyms:s:sarvā]]---completely; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīyam]]---for the third step; [[synonyms:u:upakalpaya]]---now find the means.

===== Translation =====

**O King of the demons, you have promised to give Me three steps of land, but I have occupied the entire universe with two steps. Now think about where I should put My third.**
