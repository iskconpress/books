====== SB 8.21.20 ======

===== Text =====

> yaḥ prabhuḥ sarva-bhūtānāṁ
> sukha-duḥkhopapattaye
> taṁ nātivartituṁ daityāḥ
> pauruṣair īśvaraḥ pumān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:prabhuḥ]]---that Supreme Person, the master; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtānām]]---of all living entities; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:u:upapattaye]]---for administering happiness and distress; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---Him; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:ativartitum]]---to overcome; [[synonyms:d:daityāḥ]]---O demons; [[synonyms:p:pauruṣaiḥ]]---by human endeavors; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the supreme controller; [[synonyms:p:pumān]]---a person.

===== Translation =====

**O Daityas, by human efforts no one can supersede the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who can bring happiness and distress to all living entities.**
