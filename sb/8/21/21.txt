====== SB 8.21.21 ======

===== Text =====

> yo no bhavāya prāg āsīd
> abhavāya divaukasām
> sa eva bhagavān adya
> vartate tad-viparyayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---the time factor, which represents the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---of us; [[synonyms:b:bhavāya]]---for the improvement; [[synonyms:p:prāk]]---formerly; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---was situated; [[synonyms:a:abhavāya]]---for the defeat; [[synonyms:d:diva]]-[[synonyms:o:okasām]]---of the demigods; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that time factor; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the representative of the Supreme Person; [[synonyms:a:adya]]---today; [[synonyms:v:vartate]]---is existing; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:v:viparyayam]]---just the opposite of our favor.

===== Translation =====

**The supreme time factor, which represents the Supreme Person, was previously in our favor and not in favor of the demigods, but now that same time factor is against us.**
