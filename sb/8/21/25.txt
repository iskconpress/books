====== SB 8.21.25 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> patyur nigaditaṁ śrutvā
> daitya-dānava-yūthapāḥ
> rasāṁ nirviviśū rājan
> viṣṇu-pārṣada tāḍitāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:p:patyuḥ]]---of their master (Bali Mahārāja); [[synonyms:n:nigaditam]]---what had been thus described; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:d:daitya]]-[[synonyms:d:dānava]]-[[synonyms:y:yūtha]]-[[synonyms:p:pāḥ]]---the leaders of the Daityas and demons; [[synonyms:r:rasām]]---the lower regions of the universe; [[synonyms:n:nirviviśūḥ]]---entered; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:p:pārṣada]]---by the associates of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:t:tāḍitāḥ]]---driven.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King, in accordance with the order of their master, Bali Mahārāja, all the chiefs of the demons and the Daityas entered the lower regions of the universe, to which they were driven by the soldiers of Viṣṇu.**
