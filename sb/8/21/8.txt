====== SB 8.21.8 ======

===== Text =====

> jāmbavān ṛkṣa-rājas tu
> bherī-śabdair mano-javaḥ
> vijayaṁ dikṣu sarvāsu
> mahotsavam aghoṣayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jāmbavān]]---who was named Jāmbavān; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:r:rājaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tu]]---the king in the form of a bear also; [[synonyms:b:bherī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabdaiḥ]]---by sounding the bugle; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:j:javaḥ]]---in mental ecstasy; [[synonyms:v:vijayam]]---victory; [[synonyms:d:dikṣu]]---in all directions; [[synonyms:s:sarvāsu]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:utsavam]]---festival; [[synonyms:a:aghoṣayat]]---declared.

===== Translation =====

**Jāmbavān, king of the bears, also joined in the ceremony. Sounding his bugle in all directions, he declared a great festival for Lord Vāmanadeva's victory.**
