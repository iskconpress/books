====== SB 8.21.14 ======

===== Text =====

> te sarve vāmanaṁ hantuṁ
> śūla-paṭṭiśa-pāṇayaḥ
> anicchanto bale rājan
> prādravañ jāta-manyavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:te]]---the demons; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:v:vāmanam]]---Lord Vāmanadeva; [[synonyms:h:hantum]]---to kill; [[synonyms:ś:śūla]]---tridents; [[synonyms:p:paṭṭiśa]]---lances; [[synonyms:p:pāṇayaḥ]]---all taking in hand; [[synonyms:a:anicchantaḥ]]---against the will; [[synonyms:b:baleḥ]]---of Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:p:prādravan]]---they pushed forward; [[synonyms:j:jāta]]-[[synonyms:m:manyavaḥ]]---aggravated by usual anger.

===== Translation =====

**O King, the demons, aggravated by their usual anger, took their lances and tridents in hand, and against the will of Bali Mahārāja they pushed forward to kill Lord Vāmanadeva.**
