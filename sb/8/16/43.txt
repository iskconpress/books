====== SB 8.16.43 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛtvā śirasi tac-cheṣāṁ
> devam udvāsayet tataḥ
> dvy-avarān bhojayed viprān
> pāyasena yathocitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛtvā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ś:śirasi]]---on the head; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣām]]---all the remnants (the water and flowers offered to the Deity); [[synonyms:d:devam]]---unto the Deity; [[synonyms:u:udvāsayet]]---should be thrown into a sacred place; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:a:avarān]]---a minimum of two; [[synonyms:b:bhojayet]]---should feed; [[synonyms:v:viprān]]---//brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:p:pāyasena]]---with sweet rice; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:u:ucitam]]---as each deserves.

===== Translation =====

**After touching to one's head all the flowers and water offered to the Deity, one should throw them into a sacred place. Then one should feed at least two brāhmaṇas with sweet rice.**
