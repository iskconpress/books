====== SB 8.16.35 ======

===== Text =====

> namo marakata-śyāma-
> vapuṣe 'dhigata-śriye
> keśavāya namas tubhyaṁ
> namas te pīta-vāsase

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; [[synonyms:m:marakata]]-[[synonyms:ś:śyāma]]-[[synonyms:v:vapuṣe]]---whose bodily hue is blackish like the //marakata// gem; [[synonyms:a:adhigata]]-[[synonyms:ś:śriye]]---under whose control is mother Lakṣmī, the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:k:keśavāya]]---Lord Keśava, who killed the Keśī demon; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tubhyam]]---I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:te]]---again I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; [[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsase]]---whose garment is yellow.

===== Translation =====

**My Lord, I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, who are dressed in yellow garments, whose bodily hue resembles the marakata gem, and who have full control over the goddess of fortune. O my Lord Keśava, I offer my respectful obeisances unto You.**
