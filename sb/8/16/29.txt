====== SB 8.16.29 ======

===== Text =====

> namas tubhyaṁ bhagavate
> puruṣāya mahīyase
> sarva-bhūta-nivāsāya
> vāsudevāya sākṣiṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tubhyam]]---I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; [[synonyms:b:bhagavate]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:puruṣāya]]---the Supreme Person; [[synonyms:m:mahīyase]]---the best of all personalities; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:n:nivāsāya]]---the person who lives in everyone's heart; [[synonyms:v:vāsudevāya]]---the Lord who lives everywhere; [[synonyms:s:sākṣiṇe]]---the witness of everything.

===== Translation =====

**O Supreme Personality of Godhead, greatest of all, who lives in everyone's heart and in whom everyone lives, O witness of everything, O Vāsudeva, supreme and all-pervading person, I offer my respectful obeisances unto You.**
