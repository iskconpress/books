====== SB 8.16.53 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryaṁ jñāna-sampannaṁ
> vastrābharaṇa-dhenubhiḥ
> toṣayed ṛtvijaś caiva
> tad viddhy ārādhanaṁ hareḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryam]]---the spiritual master; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:s:sampannam]]---very advanced in spiritual knowledge; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābharaṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhenubhiḥ]]---with clothing, ornaments and many cows; [[synonyms:t:toṣayet]]---should satisfy; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtvijaḥ]]---the priests recommended by the spiritual master; [[synonyms:c:ca]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---as well as; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:v:viddhi]]---try to understand that; [[synonyms:ā:ārādhanam]]---worship; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**One should satisfy the spiritual master [ācārya], who is very learned in Vedic literature, and should satisfy his assistant priests [known as hotā, udgātā, adhvaryu and brahma]. One should please them by offering them clothing, ornaments and cows. This is the ceremony called viṣṇu-ārādhana, or worship of Lord Viṣṇu.**
