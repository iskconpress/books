====== SB 8.16.47 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ tv ahar ahaḥ kuryād
> dvādaśāhaṁ payo-vratam
> harer ārādhanaṁ homam
> arhaṇaṁ dvija-tarpaṇam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:ahaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:ahaḥ]]---day after day; [[synonyms:k:kuryāt]]---should execute; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśa]]-[[synonyms:a:aham]]---until twelve days; [[synonyms:p:payaḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vratam]]---the observance of the //vrata// known as //payo-vrata//; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:ā:ārādhanam]]---worshiping the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:homam]]---by executing a fire sacrifice; [[synonyms:a:arhaṇam]]---worshiping the Deity; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:t:tarpaṇam]]---and satisfying the //brāhmaṇas// by feeding them.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, until twelve days have passed, one should observe this payo-vrata, worshiping the Lord every day, executing the routine duties, performing sacrifices and feeding the brāhmaṇas.**
