====== SB 8.16.37 ======

===== Text =====

> anvavartanta yaṁ devāḥ
> śrīś ca tat-pāda-padmayoḥ
> spṛhayanta ivāmodaṁ
> bhagavān me prasīdatām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anvavartanta]]---engaged in devotional service; [[synonyms:y:yam]]---unto whom; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:ś:śrīḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padmayoḥ]]---of the lotus feet of His Lordship; [[synonyms:s:spṛhayantaḥ]]---desiring; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---exactly; [[synonyms:ā:āmodam]]---celestial bliss; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:me]]---upon me; [[synonyms:p:prasīdatām]]---may be pleased.

===== Translation =====

**All the demigods, as well as the goddess of fortune, engage in the service of His lotus feet. Indeed, they respect the fragrance of those lotus feet. May the Supreme Personality of Godhead be pleased with me.**
