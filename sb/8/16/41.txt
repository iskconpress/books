====== SB 8.16.41 ======

===== Text =====

> niveditaṁ tad-bhaktāya
> dadyād bhuñjīta vā svayam
> dattvācamanam arcitvā
> tāmbūlaṁ ca nivedayet

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niveditam]]---this offering of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktāya]]---unto His devotee; [[synonyms:d:dadyāt]]---should be offered; [[synonyms:b:bhuñjīta]]---one should take; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:d:dattvā]] [[synonyms:ā:ācamanam]]---giving water to wash the hands and mouth; [[synonyms:a:arcitvā]]---in this way worshiping the Deity; [[synonyms:t:tāmbūlam]]---betel nuts with spices; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:n:nivedayet]]---one should offer.

===== Translation =====

**One should offer all the prasāda to a Vaiṣṇava or offer him some of the prasāda and then take some oneself. After this, one should offer the Deity ācamana and then betel nut and then again worship the Lord.**
