====== SB 8.16.56 ======

===== Text =====

> bhuktavatsu ca sarveṣu
> dīnāndha-kṛpaṇādiṣu
> viṣṇos tat prīṇanaṁ vidvān
> bhuñjīta saha bandhubhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhuktavatsu]]---after feeding; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:sarveṣu]]---everyone present there; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]---very poor; [[synonyms:a:andha]]---blind; [[synonyms:k:kṛpaṇa]]---those who are not //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---and so on; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu, who is situated in everyone's heart; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that (//prasāda//); [[synonyms:p:prīṇanam]]---pleasing; [[synonyms:v:vidvān]]---one who understands this philosophy; [[synonyms:b:bhuñjīta]]---should take //prasāda// himself; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:b:bandhubhiḥ]]---friends and relatives.

===== Translation =====

**One should distribute viṣṇu-prasāda to everyone, including the poor man, the blind man, the nondevotee and the non-brāhmaṇa. Knowing that Lord Viṣṇu is very pleased when everyone is sumptuously fed with viṣṇu-prasāda, the performer of yajña should then take prasāda with his friends and relatives.**
