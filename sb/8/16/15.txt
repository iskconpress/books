====== SB 8.16.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmād īśa bhajantyā me
> śreyaś cintaya suvrata
> hṛta-śriyo hṛta-sthānān
> sapatnaiḥ pāhi naḥ prabho

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ī:īśa]]---O powerful controller; [[synonyms:b:bhajantyāḥ]]---of your servitor; [[synonyms:m:me]]---me; [[synonyms:ś:śreyaḥ]]---auspiciousness; [[synonyms:c:cintaya]]---just consider; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vrata]]---O most gentle one; [[synonyms:h:hṛta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śriyaḥ]]---bereft of all opulence; [[synonyms:h:hṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:sthānān]]---bereft of a residence; [[synonyms:s:sapatnaiḥ]]---by the competitors; [[synonyms:p:pāhi]]---please protect; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---us; [[synonyms:p:prabho]]---O my lord.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore, most gentle lord, kindly favor your maidservant. We have now been deprived of our opulence and residence by our competitors, the demons. Kindly give us protection.**

===== Purport =====

Aditi, the mother of the demigods, appealed to Kaśyapa Muni to give the demigods protection. When we speak of the demigods, this also includes their mother.
