====== SB 8.16.58 ======

===== Text =====

> etat payo-vrataṁ nāma
> puruṣārādhanaṁ param
> pitāmahenābhihitaṁ
> mayā te samudāhṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:p:payaḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vratam]]---ceremony known as //payo-vrata//; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by that name; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārādhanam]]---the process of worshiping the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:param]]---the best; [[synonyms:p:pitāmahena]]---by my grandfather, Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:a:abhihitam]]---stated; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:s:samudāhṛtam]]---described in all details.

===== Translation =====

**This is the religious ritualistic ceremony known as payo-vrata, by which one may worship the Supreme Personality of Godhead. I received this information from Brahmā, my grandfather, and now I have described it to you in all details.**
