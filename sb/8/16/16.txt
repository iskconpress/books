====== SB 8.16.16 ======

===== Text =====

> parair vivāsitā sāhaṁ
> magnā vyasana-sāgare
> aiśvaryaṁ śrīr yaśaḥ sthānaṁ
> hṛtāni prabalair mama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paraiḥ]]---by our enemies; [[synonyms:v:vivāsitā]]---taken away from our residential quarters; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---the same; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:m:magnā]]---drowned; [[synonyms:v:vyasana]]-[[synonyms:s:sāgare]]---in an ocean of trouble; [[synonyms:a:aiśvaryam]]---opulence; [[synonyms:ś:śrīḥ]]---beauty; [[synonyms:y:yaśaḥ]]---reputation; [[synonyms:s:sthānam]]---place; [[synonyms:h:hṛtāni]]---all taken away; [[synonyms:p:prabalaiḥ]]---very powerful; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---my.

===== Translation =====

**The demons, our formidably powerful enemies, have taken away our opulence, our beauty, our fame and even our residence. Indeed, we have now been exiled, and we are drowning in an ocean of trouble.**
