====== SB 8.16.8 ======

===== Text =====

> apy agnayas tu velāyāṁ
> na hutā haviṣā sati
> tvayodvigna-dhiyā bhadre
> proṣite mayi karhicit

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:api]]---whether; [[synonyms:a:agnayaḥ]]---fires; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:velāyām]]---in the fire sacrifice; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:h:hutāḥ]]---offered; [[synonyms:h:haviṣā]]---by ghee; [[synonyms:s:sati]]---O chaste woman; [[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by you; [[synonyms:u:udvigna]]-[[synonyms:d:dhiyā]]---because of some anxiety; [[synonyms:b:bhadre]]---O auspicious woman; [[synonyms:p:proṣite]]---was away from home; [[synonyms:m:mayi]]---when I; [[synonyms:k:karhicit]]---sometimes.

===== Translation =====

**O chaste and auspicious woman, when I left home for other places, were you in so much anxiety that you did not offer oblations of ghee into the fire?**
