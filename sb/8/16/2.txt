====== SB 8.16.2 ======

===== Text =====

> ekadā kaśyapas tasyā
> āśramaṁ bhagavān agāt
> nirutsavaṁ nirānandaṁ
> samādher virataś cirāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekadā]]---one day; [[synonyms:k:kaśyapaḥ]]---the great sage Kaśyapa Muni; [[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]]---of Aditi; [[synonyms:ā:āśramam]]---to the shelter; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---greatly powerful; [[synonyms:a:agāt]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nirutsavam]]---without enthusiasm; [[synonyms:n:nirānandam]]---without jubilation; [[synonyms:s:samādheḥ]]---his trance; [[synonyms:v:virataḥ]]---stopping; [[synonyms:c:cirāt]]---after a long time.

===== Translation =====

**After many, many days, the great powerful sage Kaśyapa Muni arose from a trance of meditation and returned home to see the āśrama of Aditi neither jubilant nor festive.**
