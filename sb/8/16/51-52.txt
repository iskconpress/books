====== SB 8.16.51-52 ======

===== Text =====

> pūjāṁ ca mahatīṁ kuryād
> vitta-śāṭhya-vivarjitaḥ
> caruṁ nirūpya payasi
> śipiviṣṭāya viṣṇave
>
> sūktena tena puruṣaṁ
> yajeta susamāhitaḥ
> naivedyaṁ cātiguṇavad
> dadyāt puruṣa-tuṣṭidam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūjām]]---worship; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:m:mahatīm]]---very gorgeous; [[synonyms:k:kuryāt]]---should do; [[synonyms:v:vitta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāṭhya]]---miserly mentality (not spending sufficient money); [[synonyms:v:vivarjitaḥ]]---giving up; [[synonyms:c:carum]]---grains offered in the //yajña//; [[synonyms:n:nirūpya]]---seeing properly; [[synonyms:p:payasi]]---with milk; [[synonyms:ś:śipiviṣṭāya]]---unto the Supersoul, who is situated in the heart of every living entity; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇave]]---unto Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:s:sūktena]]---by chanting the Vedic //mantra// known as //puruṣa-sūkta//; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---by that; [[synonyms:p:puruṣam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yajeta]]---one should worship; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:samāhitaḥ]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:n:naivedyam]]---food offered to the Deity; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---prepared very gorgeously with all varieties of taste; [[synonyms:d:dadyāt]]---should offer; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:t:tuṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:d:dam]]---everything extremely pleasing to the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Giving up the miserly habit of not spending money, one should arrange for the gorgeous worship of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu, who is situated in the heart of every living entity. With great attention, one must prepare an oblation of grains boiled in ghee and milk and must chant the Puruṣa-sūkta mantra. The offerings of food should be of varieties of tastes. In this way, one should worship the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
