====== SB 8.16.3 ======

===== Text =====

> sa patnīṁ dīna-vadanāṁ
> kṛtāsana-parigrahaḥ
> sabhājito yathā-nyāyam
> idam āha kurūdvaha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Kaśyapa Muni; [[synonyms:p:patnīm]]---unto his wife; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]-[[synonyms:v:vadanām]]---having a dry face; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsana]]-[[synonyms:p:parigrahaḥ]]---after accepting a sitting place; [[synonyms:s:sabhājitaḥ]]---being honored by Aditi; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:n:nyāyam]]---according to time and place; [[synonyms:i:idam]] [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---spoke as follows; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:u:udvaha]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, the best of the Kurus.

===== Translation =====

**O best of the Kurus, when Kaśyapa Muni had been properly received and welcomed, he took his seat and then spoke as follows to his wife, Aditi, who was very morose.**
