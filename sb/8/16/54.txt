====== SB 8.16.54 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojayet tān guṇavatā
> sad-annena śuci-smite
> anyāṁś ca brāhmaṇāñ chaktyā
> ye ca tatra samāgatāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojayet]]---should distribute //prasāda//; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vatā]]---by rich foods; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:a:annena]]---with food prepared with ghee and milk, which is supposed to be very pure; [[synonyms:ś:śuci]]-[[synonyms:s:smite]]---O most pious lady; [[synonyms:a:anyān]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---others also; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇān]]---//brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:ś:śaktyā]]---as far as possible; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all of them who; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there (at the ceremonies); [[synonyms:s:samāgatāḥ]]---assembled.

===== Translation =====

**O most auspicious lady, one should perform all the ceremonies under the direction of learned ācāryas and should satisfy them and their priests. By distributing prasāda, one should also satisfy the brāhmaṇas and others who have assembled.**
