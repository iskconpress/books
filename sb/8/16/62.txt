====== SB 8.16.62 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmād etad vrataṁ bhadre
> prayatā śraddhayācara
> bhagavān parituṣṭas te
> varān āśu vidhāsyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---therefore; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vratam]]---observance of a //vrata// ceremony; [[synonyms:b:bhadre]]---my dear gentle lady; [[synonyms:p:prayatā]]---by observing rules and regulations; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---with faith; [[synonyms:ā:ācara]]---execute; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:parituṣṭaḥ]]---being very satisfied; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:v:varān]]---benedictions; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]---very soon; [[synonyms:v:vidhāsyati]]---will bestow.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore, my dear gentle lady, follow this ritualistic vow, strictly observing the regulative principles. By this process, the Supreme Person will very soon be pleased with you and will satisfy all your desires.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Eighth Canto, Sixteenth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship."
