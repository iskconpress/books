====== SB 8.16.44-45 ======

===== Text =====

> bhuñjīta tair anujñātaḥ
> seṣṭaḥ śeṣaṁ sabhājitaiḥ
> brahmacāry atha tad-rātryāṁ
> śvo bhūte prathame 'hani
>
> snātaḥ śucir yathoktena
> vidhinā susamāhitaḥ
> payasā snāpayitvārced
> yāvad vrata-samāpanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhuñjīta]]---should take the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:t:taiḥ]]---by the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:a:anujñātaḥ]]---being permitted; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:i:iṣṭaḥ]]---with friends and relatives; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣam]]---the remnants; [[synonyms:s:sabhājitaiḥ]]---properly honored; [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]]---observance of celibacy; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---of course; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:r:rātryām]]---at night; [[synonyms:ś:śvaḥ]] [[synonyms:b:bhūte]]---at the end of the night, when the morning comes; [[synonyms:p:prathame]] [[synonyms:a:ahani]]---on the first day; [[synonyms:s:snātaḥ]]---bathing; [[synonyms:ś:śuciḥ]]---becoming purified; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:u:uktena]]---as stated before; [[synonyms:v:vidhinā]]---by following the regulative principles; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:samāhitaḥ]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:p:payasā]]---with milk; [[synonyms:s:snāpayitvā]]---bathing the Deity; [[synonyms:a:arcet]]---should offer worship; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long as; [[synonyms:v:vrata]]-[[synonyms:s:samāpanam]]---the period of worship is not over.

===== Translation =====

**One should perfectly honor the respectable brāhmaṇas one has fed, and then, after taking their permission, one should take prasāda with his friends and relatives. For that night, one should observe strict celibacy, and the next morning, after bathing again, with purity and attention one should bathe the Deity of Viṣṇu with milk and worship Him according to the methods formerly stated in detail.**
