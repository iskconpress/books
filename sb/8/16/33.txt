====== SB 8.16.33 ======

===== Text =====

> namo hiraṇyagarbhāya
> prāṇāya jagad-ātmane
> yogaiśvarya-śarīrāya
> namas te yoga-hetave

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyagarbhāya]]---situated as the four-headed Hiraṇyagarbha, Brahmā; [[synonyms:p:prāṇāya]]---the source of everyone's life; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmane]]---the Supersoul of the entire universe; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śarīrāya]]---whose body is full of opulences and mystic power; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:te]]---I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:h:hetave]]---the original master of all mystic power.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, who are situated as Hiraṇyagarbha, the source of life, the Supersoul of every living entity. Your body is the source of the opulence of all mystic power. I offer my respectful obeisances unto You.**
