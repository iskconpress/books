====== SB 8.16.13 ======

===== Text =====

> ko nu me bhagavan kāmo
> na sampadyeta mānasaḥ
> yasyā bhavān prajādhyakṣa
> evaṁ dharmān prabhāṣate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---what; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O lord; [[synonyms:k:kāmaḥ]]---desire; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:s:sampadyeta]]---can be fulfilled; [[synonyms:m:mānasaḥ]]---within my mind; [[synonyms:y:yasyāḥ]]---of me; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---your good self; [[synonyms:p:prajā]]-[[synonyms:a:adhyakṣaḥ]]---Prajāpati; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:dharmān]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:p:prabhāṣate]]---talks.

===== Translation =====

**O my lord, since you are a Prajāpati and are personally my instructor in the principles of religion, where is the possibility that all my desires will not be fulfilled?**
