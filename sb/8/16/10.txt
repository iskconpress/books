====== SB 8.16.10 ======

===== Text =====

> api sarve kuśalinas
> tava putrā manasvini
> lakṣaye 'svastham ātmānaṁ
> bhavatyā lakṣaṇair aham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:api]]---whether; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kuśalinaḥ]]---in full auspiciousness; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---your; [[synonyms:p:putrāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:m:manasvini]]---O great-minded lady; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaye]]---I see; [[synonyms:a:asvastham]]---not in tranquillity; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---the mind; [[synonyms:b:bhavatyāḥ]]---of you; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇaiḥ]]---by symptoms; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I.

===== Translation =====

**O great-minded lady, are all your sons faring well? Seeing your withered face, I can perceive that your mind is not tranquil. How is this so?**
