====== SB 8.16.12 ======

===== Text =====

> agnayo 'tithayo bhṛtyā
> bhikṣavo ye ca lipsavaḥ
> sarvaṁ bhagavato brahmann
> anudhyānān na riṣyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:agnayaḥ]]---worshiping the fires; [[synonyms:a:atithayaḥ]]---receiving the guests; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtyāḥ]]---satisfying the servants; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣavaḥ]]---pleasing the beggars; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all of them who; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:l:lipsavaḥ]]---as they desire (are taken care of); [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:bhagavataḥ]]---of you, my lord; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:a:anudhyānāt]]---from always thinking; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:r:riṣyati]]---nothing is missed (everything is properly done).

===== Translation =====

**O beloved husband, the fires, guests, servants and beggars are all being properly cared for by me. Because I always think of you, there is no possibility that any of the religious principles will be neglected.**
