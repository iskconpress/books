====== SB 8.16.50 ======

===== Text =====

> trayodaśyām atho viṣṇoḥ
> snapanaṁ pañcakair vibhoḥ
> kārayec chāstra-dṛṣṭena
> vidhinā vidhi-kovidaiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:trayodaśyām]]---on the thirteenth day of the moon; [[synonyms:a:atho]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:s:snapanam]]---bathing; [[synonyms:p:pañcakaiḥ]]---by //pañcāmṛta,// five substances; [[synonyms:v:vibhoḥ]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kārayet]]---one should execute; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭena]]---enjoined in the scripture; [[synonyms:v:vidhinā]]---under regulative principles; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:k:kovidaiḥ]]---assisted by the priests who know the regulative principles.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, following the directions of the śāstra with help from brāhmaṇas who know the śāstra, on the thirteenth day of the moon one should bathe Lord Viṣṇu with five substances [milk, yogurt, ghee, sugar and honey].**
