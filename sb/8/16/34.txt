====== SB 8.16.34 ======

===== Text =====

> namas ta ādi-devāya
> sākṣi-bhūtāya te namaḥ
> nārāyaṇāya ṛṣaye
> narāya haraye namaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:te]]---I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:d:devāya]]---who are the original Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sākṣi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtāya]]---the witness of everything within the heart of everyone; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto You; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇāya]]---who take the incarnation of Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣaye]]---the sage; [[synonyms:n:narāya]]---the incarnation of a human being; [[synonyms:h:haraye]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, who are the original Personality of Godhead, the witness in everyone's heart, and the incarnation of Nara-Nārāyaṇa Ṛṣi in the form of a human being. O Personality of Godhead, I offer my respectful obeisances unto You.**
