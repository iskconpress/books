====== SB 8.16.38 ======

===== Text =====

> etair mantrair hṛṣīkeśam
> āvāhana-puraskṛtam
> arcayec chraddhayā yuktaḥ
> pādyopasparśanādibhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etaiḥ]] [[synonyms:m:mantraiḥ]]---by chanting all these //mantras//; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣīkeśam]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the master of all senses; [[synonyms:ā:āvāhana]]---calling; [[synonyms:p:puraskṛtam]]---honoring Him in all respects; [[synonyms:a:arcayet]]---one should worship; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---with faith and devotion; [[synonyms:y:yuktaḥ]]---engaged; [[synonyms:p:pādya]]-[[synonyms:u:upasparśana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---with the paraphernalia of worship (//pādya, arghya,// etc.).

===== Translation =====

**Kaśyapa Muni continued: By chanting all these mantras, welcoming the Supreme Personality of Godhead with faith and devotion, and offering Him items of worship [such as pādya and arghya], one should worship Keśava, Hṛṣīkeśa, Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
