====== SB 8.15.5 ======

===== Text =====

> tato rathaḥ kāñcana-paṭṭa-naddho
> hayāś ca haryaśva-turaṅga-varṇāḥ
> dhvajaś ca siṁhena virājamāno
> hutāśanād āsa havirbhir iṣṭāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:r:rathaḥ]]---a chariot; [[synonyms:k:kāñcana]]---with gold; [[synonyms:p:paṭṭa]]---and silk garments; [[synonyms:n:naddhaḥ]]---wrapped; [[synonyms:h:hayāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---horses also; [[synonyms:h:haryaśva]]-[[synonyms:t:turaṅga]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇāḥ]]---exactly of the same color as the horses of Indra (yellow); [[synonyms:d:dhvajaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---a flag also; [[synonyms:s:siṁhena]]---with the mark of a lion; [[synonyms:v:virājamānaḥ]]---existing; [[synonyms:h:huta]]-[[synonyms:a:aśanāt]]---from the blazing fire; [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---there was; [[synonyms:h:havirbhiḥ]]---by offerings of clarified butter; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭāt]]---worshiped.

===== Translation =====

**When ghee [clarified butter] was offered in the fire of sacrifice, there appeared from the fire a celestial chariot covered with gold and silk. There also appeared yellow horses like those of Indra, and a flag marked with a lion.**
