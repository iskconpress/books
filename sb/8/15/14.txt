====== SB 8.15.14 ======

===== Text =====

> ākāśa-gaṅgayā devyā
> vṛtāṁ parikha-bhūtayā
> prākāreṇāgni-varṇena
> sāṭṭālenonnatena ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākāśa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṅgayā]]---by Ganges water known as Ākāśa-gaṅgā; [[synonyms:d:devyā]]---the always-worshipable goddess; [[synonyms:v:vṛtām]]---surrounded; [[synonyms:p:parikha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtayā]]---as a trench; [[synonyms:p:prākāreṇa]]---by ramparts; [[synonyms:a:agni]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇena]]---resembling fire; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṭṭālena]]---with places for fighting; [[synonyms:u:unnatena]]---very high; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**The city was surrounded by trenches full of Ganges water, known as Ākāśa-gaṅgā, and by a high wall, which was the color of fire. Upon this wall were parapets for fighting.**
