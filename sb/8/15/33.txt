====== SB 8.15.33 ======

===== Text =====

> deveṣv atha nilīneṣu
> balir vairocanaḥ purīm
> deva-dhānīm adhiṣṭhāya
> vaśaṁ ninye jagat-trayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deveṣu]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nilīneṣu]]---when they disappeared; [[synonyms:b:baliḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:v:vairocanaḥ]]---the son of Virocana; [[synonyms:p:purīm]]---the heavenly kingdom; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:dhānīm]]---the residence of the demigods; [[synonyms:a:adhiṣṭhāya]]---taking possession of; [[synonyms:v:vaśam]]---under control; [[synonyms:n:ninye]]---brought; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:t:trayam]]---the three worlds.

===== Translation =====

**When the demigods had disappeared, Bali Mahārāja, the son of Virocana, entered the heavenly kingdom, and from there he brought the three worlds under his control.**
