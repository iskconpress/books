====== SB 8.15.18 ======

===== Text =====

> sura-strī-keśa-vibhraṣṭa-
> nava-saugandhika-srajām
> yatrāmodam upādāya
> mārga āvāti mārutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:s:strī]]---of the women of the demigods; [[synonyms:k:keśa]]---from the hair; [[synonyms:v:vibhraṣṭa]]---fallen; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:s:saugandhika]]---made of fresh, fragrant flowers; [[synonyms:s:srajām]]---of the flower garlands; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherein; [[synonyms:ā:āmodam]]---the fragrance; [[synonyms:u:upādāya]]---carrying; [[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the roads; [[synonyms:ā:āvāti]]---blows; [[synonyms:m:mārutaḥ]]---the breeze.

===== Translation =====

**The breezes blowing in the streets of the city bore the fragrance of the flowers falling from the hair of the women of the demigods.**
