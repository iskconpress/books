====== SB 8.15.24 ======

===== Text =====

> maghavāṁs tam abhipretya
> baleḥ paramam udyamam
> sarva-deva-gaṇopeto
> gurum etad uvāca ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maghavān]]---Indra; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---the situation; [[synonyms:a:abhipretya]]---understanding; [[synonyms:b:baleḥ]]---of Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:p:paramam]] [[synonyms:u:udyamam]]---great enthusiasm; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---by all the demigods; [[synonyms:u:upetaḥ]]---accompanied; [[synonyms:g:gurum]]---unto the spiritual master; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---the following words; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Bali Mahārāja's indefatigable endeavor and understanding his motive, King Indra, along with the other demigods, approached his spiritual master, Bṛhaspati, and spoke as follows.**
