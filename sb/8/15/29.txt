====== SB 8.15.29 ======

===== Text =====

> ojasvinaṁ baliṁ jetuṁ
> na samartho 'sti kaścana
> bhavad-vidho bhavān vāpi
> varjayitveśvaraṁ harim
> vijeṣyati na ko 'py enaṁ
> brahma-tejaḥ-samedhitam
> nāsya śaktaḥ puraḥ sthātuṁ
> kṛtāntasya yathā janāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:o:ojasvinam]]---so powerful; [[synonyms:b:balim]]---Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:j:jetum]]---to conquer; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:s:samarthaḥ]]---able; [[synonyms:a:asti]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kaścana]]---anyone; [[synonyms:b:bhavat]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhaḥ]]---like you; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---you yourself; [[synonyms:v:vā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---either; [[synonyms:v:varjayitvā]]---excepting; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaram]]---the supreme controller; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vijeṣyati]]---will conquer; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---anyone; [[synonyms:e:enam]]---him (Bali Mahārāja); [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:t:tejaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:samedhitam]]---now empowered with //brahma-tejas,// extraordinary spiritual power; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of him; [[synonyms:ś:śaktaḥ]]---is able; [[synonyms:p:puraḥ]]---in front; [[synonyms:s:sthātum]]---to stay; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:antasya]]---of Yamarāja; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:j:janāḥ]]---people.

===== Translation =====

**Neither you nor your men can conquer the most powerful Bali. Indeed, no one but the Supreme Personality of Godhead can conquer him, for he is now equipped with the supreme spiritual power [brahma-tejas]. As no one can stand before Yamarāja, no one can now stand before Bali Mahārāja.**
