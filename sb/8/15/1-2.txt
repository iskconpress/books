====== SB 8.15.1-2 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> baleḥ pada-trayaṁ bhūmeḥ
> kasmād dharir ayācata
> bhūteśvaraḥ kṛpaṇa-val
> labdhārtho 'pi babandha tam
>
> etad veditum icchāmo
> mahat kautūhalaṁ hi naḥ
> yācñeśvarasya pūrṇasya
> bandhanaṁ cāpy anāgasaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the King said; [[synonyms:b:baleḥ]]---of Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:t:trayam]]---three steps; [[synonyms:b:bhūmeḥ]]---of land; [[synonyms:k:kasmāt]]---why; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead (in the form of Vāmana); [[synonyms:a:ayācata]]---begged; [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the proprietor of all the universe; [[synonyms:k:kṛpaṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like a poor man; [[synonyms:l:labdha]]-[[synonyms:a:arthaḥ]]---He got the gift; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:b:babandha]]---arrested; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Bali); [[synonyms:e:etat]]---all this; [[synonyms:v:veditum]]---to understand; [[synonyms:i:icchāmaḥ]]---we desire; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]---very great; [[synonyms:k:kautūhalam]]---eagerness; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:y:yācñā]]---begging; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarasya]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇasya]]---who is full in everything; [[synonyms:b:bandhanam]]---arresting; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:a:anāgasaḥ]]---of he who was faultless.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Parīkṣit inquired: The Supreme Personality of Godhead is the proprietor of everything. Why did He beg three paces of land from Bali Mahārāja like a poor man, and when He got the gift for which He had begged, why did He nonetheless arrest Bali Mahārāja? I am very much anxious to know the mystery of these contradictions.**
