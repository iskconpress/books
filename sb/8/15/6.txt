====== SB 8.15.6 ======

===== Text =====

> dhanuś ca divyaṁ puraṭopanaddhaṁ
> tūṇāv ariktau kavacaṁ ca divyam
> pitāmahas tasya dadau ca mālām
> amlāna-puṣpāṁ jalajaṁ ca śukraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhanuḥ]]---a bow; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:divyam]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:p:puraṭa]]-[[synonyms:u:upanaddham]]---covered with gold; [[synonyms:t:tūṇau]]---two quivers; [[synonyms:a:ariktau]]---infallible; [[synonyms:k:kavacam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and armor; [[synonyms:d:divyam]]---celestial; [[synonyms:p:pitāmahaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his grandfather, namely Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:d:dadau]]---gave; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mālām]]---a garland; [[synonyms:a:amlāna]]-[[synonyms:p:puṣpām]]---made of flowers that do not fade away; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:j:jam]]---a conchshell (which is born in water); [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as well as; [[synonyms:ś:śukraḥ]]---Śukrācārya.

===== Translation =====

**A gilded bow, two quivers of infallible arrows, and celestial armor also appeared. Bali Mahārāja's grandfather Prahlāda Mahārāja offered Bali a garland of flowers that would never fade, and Śukrācārya gave him a conchshell.**
