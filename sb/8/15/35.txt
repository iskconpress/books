====== SB 8.15.35 ======

===== Text =====

> tatas tad-anubhāvena
> bhuvana-traya-viśrutām
> kīrtiṁ dikṣu-vitanvānaḥ
> sa reja uḍurāḍ iva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhāvena]]---because of performing such great sacrifices; [[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]-[[synonyms:t:traya]]---throughout the three worlds; [[synonyms:v:viśrutām]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:k:kīrtim]]---reputation; [[synonyms:d:dikṣu]]---in all directions; [[synonyms:v:vitanvānaḥ]]---spreading; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Bali Mahārāja); [[synonyms:r:reje]]---became effulgent; [[synonyms:u:uḍurāṭ]]---the moon; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like.

===== Translation =====

**When Bali Mahārāja performed these sacrifices, he gained a great reputation in all directions, throughout the three worlds. Thus he shone in his position, like the brilliant moon in the sky.**
