====== SB 8.15.13 ======

===== Text =====

> haṁsa-sārasa-cakrāhva-
> kāraṇḍava-kulākulāḥ
> nalinyo yatra krīḍanti
> pramadāḥ sura-sevitāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haṁsa]]---of swans; [[synonyms:s:sārasa]]---cranes; [[synonyms:c:cakrāhva]]---birds known as //cakravākas//; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇḍava]]---and water fowl; [[synonyms:k:kula]]---by groups; [[synonyms:ā:ākulāḥ]]---congested; [[synonyms:n:nalinyaḥ]]---lotus flowers; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---where; [[synonyms:k:krīḍanti]]---enjoyed sporting; [[synonyms:p:pramadāḥ]]---beautiful women; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:s:sevitāḥ]]---protected by the demigods.

===== Translation =====

**Beautiful women protected by the demigods sported in the gardens, which had lotus ponds full of swans, cranes, cakravākas and ducks.**
