====== SB 8.15.7 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ sa viprārjita-yodhanārthas
> taiḥ kalpita-svastyayano 'tha viprān
> pradakṣiṇī-kṛtya kṛta-praṇāmaḥ
> prahrādam āmantrya namaś-cakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Bali Mahārāja); [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:a:arjita]]---gained by the grace of the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:y:yodhana]]-[[synonyms:a:arthaḥ]]---possessing equipment for fighting; [[synonyms:t:taiḥ]]---by them (the //brāhmaṇas//); [[synonyms:k:kalpita]]---advice; [[synonyms:s:svastyayanaḥ]]---ritualistic performance; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---as; [[synonyms:v:viprān]]---all the //brāhmaṇas// (Śukrācārya and others); [[synonyms:p:pradakṣiṇī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---circumambulating; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:p:praṇāmaḥ]]---offered his respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prahrādam]]---unto Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:ā:āmantrya]]---addressing; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]-[[synonyms:c:cakāra]]---offered him obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**When Mahārāja Bali had thus performed the special ritualistic ceremony advised by the brāhmaṇas and had received, by their grace, the equipment for fighting, he circumambulated the brāhmaṇas and offered them obeisances. He also saluted Prahlāda Mahārāja and offered obeisances to him.**
