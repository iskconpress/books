====== SB 8.15.25 ======

===== Text =====

> bhagavann udyamo bhūyān
> baler naḥ pūrva-vairiṇaḥ
> aviṣahyam imaṁ manye
> kenāsīt tejasorjitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O my lord; [[synonyms:u:udyamaḥ]]---enthusiasm; [[synonyms:b:bhūyān]]---great; [[synonyms:b:baleḥ]]---of Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vairiṇaḥ]]---past enemy; [[synonyms:a:aviṣahyam]]---unbearable; [[synonyms:i:imam]]---this; [[synonyms:m:manye]]---I think; [[synonyms:k:kena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---got; [[synonyms:t:tejasā]]---prowess; [[synonyms:ū:ūrjitaḥ]]---achieved.

===== Translation =====

**My lord, our old enemy Bali Mahārāja now has new enthusiasm, and he has obtained such astonishing power that we think that perhaps we cannot resist his prowess.**
