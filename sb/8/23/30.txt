====== SB 8.23.30 ======

===== Text =====

> ya idaṁ deva-devasya
> harer adbhuta-karmaṇaḥ
> avatārānucaritaṁ
> śṛṇvan yāti parāṁ gatim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:devasya]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is worshiped by the demigods; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa, Hari; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]-[[synonyms:k:karmaṇaḥ]]---whose activities are all wonderful; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]-[[synonyms:a:anucaritam]]---activities performed in His different incarnations; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇvan]]---if one continues to hear; [[synonyms:y:yāti]]---he goes; [[synonyms:p:parām]] [[synonyms:g:gatim]]---to the supreme perfection, back home, back to Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**If one hears about the uncommon activities of the Supreme Personality of Godhead in His various incarnations, he is certainly elevated to the higher planetary system or even brought back home, back to Godhead.**
