====== SB 8.23.26-27 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmā śarvaḥ kumāraś ca
> bhṛgv-ādyā munayo nṛpa
> pitaraḥ sarva-bhūtāni
> siddhā vaimānikāś ca ye
>
> sumahat karma tad viṣṇor
> gāyantaḥ param adbhutam
> dhiṣṇyāni svāni te jagmur
> aditiṁ ca śaśaṁsire

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śarvaḥ]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:k:kumāraḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also Lord Kārttikeya; [[synonyms:b:bhṛgu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---headed by Bhṛgu Muni, one of the seven //ṛṣis//; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]]---the saintly persons; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:p:pitaraḥ]]---the inhabitants of Pitṛloka; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtāni]]---other living entities; [[synonyms:s:siddhāḥ]]---the residents of Siddhaloka; [[synonyms:v:vaimānikāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---human beings who can travel everywhere in outer space by airplane; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---such persons; [[synonyms:s:sumahat]]---highly praiseworthy; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activities; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---all those (activities); [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---done by Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:g:gāyantaḥ]]---glorifying; [[synonyms:p:param]] [[synonyms:a:adbhutam]]---uncommon and wonderful; [[synonyms:d:dhiṣṇyāni]]---to their respective planets; [[synonyms:s:svāni]]---own; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:j:jagmuḥ]]---departed; [[synonyms:a:aditim]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as well as Aditi; [[synonyms:ś:śaśaṁsire]]---praised all these activities of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Brahmā, Lord Śiva, Lord Kārttikeya, the great sage Bhṛgu, other saintly persons, the inhabitants of Pitṛloka and all other living entities present, including the inhabitants of Siddhaloka and living entities who travel in outer space by airplane, all glorified the uncommon activities of Lord Vāmanadeva. O King, while chanting about and glorifying the Lord, they returned to their respective heavenly planets. They also praised the position of Aditi.**
