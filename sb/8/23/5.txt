====== SB 8.23.5 ======

===== Text =====

> labdha-prasādaṁ nirmuktaṁ
> pautraṁ vaṁśa-dharaṁ balim
> niśāmya bhakti-pravaṇaḥ
> prahrāda idam abravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:labdha]]-[[synonyms:p:prasādam]]---who had achieved the blessings of the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nirmuktam]]---who was released from bondage; [[synonyms:p:pautram]]---his grandson; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśa]]-[[synonyms:d:dharam]]---the descendant; [[synonyms:b:balim]]---Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:n:niśāmya]]---after overhearing; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:p:pravaṇaḥ]]---in fully ecstatic devotion; [[synonyms:p:prahrādaḥ]]---Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---spoke.

===== Translation =====

**When Prahlāda Mahārāja heard how Bali Mahārāja, his grandson and descendant, had been released from bondage and had achieved the benediction of the Lord, he spoke as follows in a tone of greatly ecstatic devotion.**
