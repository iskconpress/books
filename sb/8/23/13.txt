====== SB 8.23.13 ======

===== Text =====

> athāhośanasaṁ rājan
> harir nārāyaṇo 'ntike
> āsīnam ṛtvijāṁ madhye
> sadasi brahma-vādinām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:u:uśanasam]]---unto Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇaḥ]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:a:antike]]---nearby; [[synonyms:ā:āsīnam]]---who was sitting; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtvijām]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the group of all the priests; [[synonyms:s:sadasi]]---in the assembly; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:v:vādinām]]---of the followers of Vedic principles.

===== Translation =====

**Hari, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyaṇa, thereafter addressed Śukrācārya, who was sitting nearby in the midst of the assembly with the priests [brahma, hotā, udgātā and adhvaryu]. O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, these priests were all brahma-vādīs, followers of the Vedic principles for performing sacrifices.**
