====== SB 8.23.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> ity uktavantaṁ puruṣaṁ purātanaṁ
> mahānubhāvo 'khila-sādhu-sammataḥ
> baddhāñjalir bāṣpa-kalākulekṣaṇo
> bhakty-utkalo gadgadayā girābravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:u:uktavantam]]---upon the order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:puruṣam]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:purātanam]]---the oldest of everyone; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhāvaḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja, who was a great and exalted soul; [[synonyms:a:akhila]]-[[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sammataḥ]]---as approved by all saintly persons; [[synonyms:b:baddha]]-[[synonyms:a:añjaliḥ]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:b:bāṣpa]]-[[synonyms:k:kala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākula]]-[[synonyms:ī:īkṣaṇaḥ]]---whose eyes were filled with tears; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:u:utkalaḥ]]---full of ecstatic devotion; [[synonyms:g:gadgadayā]]---which were faltering in devotional ecstasy; [[synonyms:g:girā]]---by such words; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---said.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: When the supreme, ancient, eternal Personality of Godhead had thus spoken to Bali Mahārāja, who is universally accepted as a pure devotee of the Lord and therefore a great soul, Bali Mahārāja, his eyes filled with tears, his hands folded and his voice faltering in devotional ecstasy, responded as follows.**
