====== SB 8.23.17 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi vadato bhūman
> kariṣyāmy anuśāsanam
> etac chreyaḥ paraṁ puṁsāṁ
> yat tavājñānupālanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---although there was no fault on the part of Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:v:vadataḥ]]---because of your order; [[synonyms:b:bhūman]]---O Supreme; [[synonyms:k:kariṣyāmi]]---I must execute; [[synonyms:a:anuśāsanam]]---because it is Your order; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this is; [[synonyms:ś:śreyaḥ]]---that which is the most auspicious; [[synonyms:p:param]]---supreme; [[synonyms:p:puṁsām]]---of all persons; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:t:tava]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]-[[synonyms:a:anupālanam]]---to obey Your order.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Viṣṇu, I must nonetheless act in obedience to Your order because obeying Your order is most auspicious and is the first duty of everyone.**
