====== SB 8.23.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> ity uktvā harim ānatya
> brahmāṇaṁ sabhavaṁ tataḥ
> viveśa sutalaṁ prīto
> balir muktaḥ sahāsuraiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]] [[synonyms:u:uktvā]]---saying this; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari; [[synonyms:ā:ānatya]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇam]]---unto Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavam]]---with Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:v:viveśa]]---he entered; [[synonyms:s:sutalam]]---the planet Sutala; [[synonyms:p:prītaḥ]]---being fully satisfied; [[synonyms:b:baliḥ]]---Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:m:muktaḥ]]---thus released; [[synonyms:s:saha]] [[synonyms:a:asuraiḥ]]---with his //asura// associates.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: After speaking in this way, Bali Mahārāja offered his obeisances first to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari, and then to Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva. Thus he was released from the bondage of the nāga-pāśa [the ropes of Varuṇa], and in full satisfaction he entered the planet known as Sutala.**
