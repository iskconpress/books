====== SB 8.23.19 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ baler mahīṁ rājan
> bhikṣitvā vāmano hariḥ
> dadau bhrātre mahendrāya
> tridivaṁ yat parair hṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:baleḥ]]---from Bali Mahārāja; [[synonyms:m:mahīm]]---the land; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣitvā]]---after begging; [[synonyms:v:vāmanaḥ]]---His Lordship Vāmana; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:dadau]]---delivered; [[synonyms:b:bhrātre]]---unto His brother; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:i:indrāya]]---Indra, the King of heaven; [[synonyms:t:tridivam]]---the planetary system of the demigods; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:p:paraiḥ]]---by others; [[synonyms:h:hṛtam]]---was taken.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, thus having taken all the land of Bali Mahārāja by begging, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Lord Vāmanadeva, delivered to His brother Indra all the land taken away by Indra's enemy.**
