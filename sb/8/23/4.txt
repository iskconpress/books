====== SB 8.23.4 ======

===== Text =====

> evam indrāya bhagavān
> pratyānīya triviṣṭapam
> pūrayitvāditeḥ kāmam
> aśāsat sakalaṁ jagat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:i:indrāya]]---unto King Indra; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:pratyānīya]]---giving back; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṭapam]]---his supremacy in the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:p:pūrayitvā]]---fulfilling; [[synonyms:a:aditeḥ]]---of Aditi; [[synonyms:k:kāmam]]---the desire; [[synonyms:a:aśāsat]]---ruled; [[synonyms:s:sakalam]]---complete; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---universe.

===== Translation =====

**Thus having delivered the proprietorship of the heavenly planets to Indra and having fulfilled the desire of Aditi, mother of the demigods, the Supreme Personality of Godhead ruled the affairs of the universe.**
