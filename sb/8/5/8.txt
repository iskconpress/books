====== SB 8.5.8 ======

===== Text =====

> indro mantradrumas tatra
> devā āpyādayo gaṇāḥ
> munayas tatra vai rājan
> haviṣmad-vīrakādayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the King of heaven; [[synonyms:m:mantradrumaḥ]]---known as Mantradruma; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in that sixth //manvantara//; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:ā:āpya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the Āpyas and others; [[synonyms:g:gaṇāḥ]]---that assembly; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]]---the seven sages; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:h:haviṣmat]]---of the name Haviṣmān; [[synonyms:v:vīraka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---Vīraka and others.

===== Translation =====

**During the reign of Cākṣuṣa Manu, the King of heaven was known as Mantradruma. Among the demigods were the Āpyas, and among the great sages were Haviṣmān and Vīraka.**
