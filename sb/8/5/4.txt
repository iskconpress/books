====== SB 8.5.4 ======

===== Text =====

> patnī vikuṇṭhā śubhrasya
> vaikuṇṭhaiḥ sura-sattamaiḥ
> tayoḥ sva-kalayā jajñe
> vaikuṇṭho bhagavān svayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patnī]]---the wife; [[synonyms:v:vikuṇṭhā]]---named Vikuṇṭhā; [[synonyms:ś:śubhrasya]]---of Śubhra; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhaiḥ]]---with the Vaikuṇṭhas; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:t:tamaiḥ]]---demigods; [[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---by Vikuṇṭhā and Śubhra; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:k:kalayā]]---with plenary expansions; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---appeared; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhaḥ]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**From the combination of Śubhra and his wife, Vikuṇṭhā, there appeared the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Vaikuṇṭha, along with demigods who were His personal plenary expansions.**
