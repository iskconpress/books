====== SB 8.5.10 ======

===== Text =====

> payodhiṁ yena nirmathya
> surāṇāṁ sādhitā sudhā
> bhramamāṇo 'mbhasi dhṛtaḥ
> kūrma-rūpeṇa mandaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:payodhim]]---the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:n:nirmathya]]---by churning; [[synonyms:s:surāṇām]]---of the demigods; [[synonyms:s:sādhitā]]---produced; [[synonyms:s:sudhā]]---nectar; [[synonyms:b:bhramamāṇaḥ]]---moving here and there; [[synonyms:a:ambhasi]]---within the water; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtaḥ]]---was staying; [[synonyms:k:kūrma]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpeṇa]]---in the form of a tortoise; [[synonyms:m:mandaraḥ]]---the mountain known as Mandara.

===== Translation =====

**By churning the ocean of milk, Ajita produced nectar for the demigods. In the form of a tortoise, He moved here and there, carrying on His back the great mountain known as Mandara.**
