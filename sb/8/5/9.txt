====== SB 8.5.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tatrāpi devasambhūtyāṁ
> vairājasyābhavat sutaḥ
> ajito nāma bhagavān
> aṁśena jagataḥ patiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tatra]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---again in that sixth //manvantara//; [[synonyms:d:devasambhūtyām]]---by Devasambhūti; [[synonyms:v:vairājasya]]---by her husband, Vairāja; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:a:ajitaḥ]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by the name Ajita; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---partially; [[synonyms:j:jagataḥ]] [[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the master of the universe.

===== Translation =====

**In this sixth manvantara millennium, Lord Viṣṇu, the master of the universe, appeared in His partial expansion. He was begotten by Vairāja in the womb of his wife, Devasambhūti, and His name was Ajita.**
