====== SB 8.5.13 ======

===== Text =====

> tvayā saṅkathyamānena
> mahimnā sātvatāṁ pateḥ
> nātitṛpyati me cittaṁ
> suciraṁ tāpa-tāpitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by Your Holiness; [[synonyms:s:saṅkathyamānena]]---being described; [[synonyms:m:mahimnā]]---by all the glories; [[synonyms:s:sātvatām]] [[synonyms:p:pateḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the master of the devotees; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:t:tṛpyati]]---is sufficiently satisfied; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:c:cittam]]---heart; [[synonyms:s:suciram]]---for such a long time; [[synonyms:t:tāpa]]---by miseries; [[synonyms:t:tāpitam]]---being distressed.

===== Translation =====

**My heart, which is disturbed by the three miserable conditions of material life, is not yet sated with hearing you describe the glorious activities of the Lord, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is the master of the devotees.**
