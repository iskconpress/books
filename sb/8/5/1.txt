====== SB 8.5.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> rājann uditam etat te
> hareḥ karmāgha-nāśanam
> gajendra-mokṣaṇaṁ puṇyaṁ
> raivataṁ tv antaraṁ śṛṇu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:u:uditam]]---already described; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activity; [[synonyms:a:agha]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśanam]]---by hearing which one can be freed from all misfortune; [[synonyms:g:gajendra]]-[[synonyms:m:mokṣaṇam]]---deliverance of Gajendra, the King of the elephants; [[synonyms:p:puṇyam]]---very pious to hear and describe; [[synonyms:r:raivatam]]---about Raivata Manu; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:a:antaram]]---in this millennium; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---kindly hear from me.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King, I have described to you the pastime of Gajendra-mokṣaṇa, which is most pious to hear. By hearing of such activities of the Lord, one can be freed from all sinful reactions. Now please listen as I describe Raivata Manu.**
