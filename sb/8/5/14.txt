====== SB 8.5.14 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-sūta uvāca
> sampṛṣṭo bhagavān evaṁ
> dvaipāyana-suto dvijāḥ
> abhinandya harer vīryam
> abhyācaṣṭuṁ pracakrame

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:s:sampṛṣṭaḥ]]---being questioned; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Śukadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:dvaipāyana]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:j:jāḥ]]---O //brāhmaṇas// assembled here; [[synonyms:a:abhinandya]]---congratulating Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vīryam]]---the glories of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:abhyācaṣṭum]]---to describe; [[synonyms:p:pracakrame]]---endeavored.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said: O learned brāhmaṇas assembled here at Naimiṣāraṇya, when Śukadeva Gosvāmī, the son of Dvaipāyana, was thus questioned by the King, he congratulated the King and then endeavored to describe further the glories of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
