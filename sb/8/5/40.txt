====== SB 8.5.40 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīr vakṣasaḥ pitaraś chāyayāsan
> dharmaḥ stanād itaraḥ pṛṣṭhato 'bhūt
> dyaur yasya śīrṣṇo 'psaraso vihārāt
> prasīdatāṁ naḥ sa mahā-vibhūtiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīḥ]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:v:vakṣasaḥ]]---from His chest; [[synonyms:p:pitaraḥ]]---the inhabitants of Pitṛloka; [[synonyms:c:chāyayā]]---from His shadow; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---became possible; [[synonyms:d:dharmaḥ]]---the principle of religion; [[synonyms:s:stanāt]]---from His bosom; [[synonyms:i:itaraḥ]]---irreligion (the opposite of //dharma//); [[synonyms:p:pṛṣṭhataḥ]]---from the back; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became possible; [[synonyms:d:dyauḥ]]---the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom; [[synonyms:ś:śīrṣṇaḥ]]---from the top of the head; [[synonyms:a:apsarasaḥ]]---the inhabitants of Apsaroloka; [[synonyms:v:vihārāt]]---by His sense enjoyment; [[synonyms:p:prasīdatām]]---kindly be pleased; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---upon us; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He (the Supreme Personality of Godhead); [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhūtiḥ]]---the greatest in all prowess.

===== Translation =====

**The goddess of fortune was generated from His chest, the inhabitants of Pitṛloka from His shadow, religion from His bosom, and irreligion [the opposite of religion] from His back. The heavenly planets were generated from the top of His head, and the Apsarās from His sense enjoyment. May that supremely powerful Personality of Godhead be pleased with us.**
