====== SB 2.10.15 ======

> अन्तः शरीर आकाशात्पुरुषस्य विचेष्टतः ।
> ओजः सहो बलं जज्ञे ततः प्राणो महानसुः ॥१५॥

===== Text =====

> antaḥ śarīra ākāśāt
> puruṣasya viceṣṭataḥ
> ojaḥ saho balaṁ jajñe
> tataḥ prāṇo mahān asuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:antaḥ]] [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---within the body; [[synonyms:ā:ākāśāt]]---from the sky; [[synonyms:p:puruṣasya]]---of Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:v:viceṣṭataḥ]]---while so trying, or willing; [[synonyms:o:ojaḥ]]---the energy of the senses; [[synonyms:s:sahaḥ]]---mental force; [[synonyms:b:balam]]---bodily strength; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---generated; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prāṇaḥ]]---the living force; [[synonyms:m:mahān]] [[synonyms:a:asuḥ]]---the fountainhead of everyone's life.

===== Translation =====

**From the sky situated within the transcendental body of the manifesting Mahā-Viṣṇu, sense energy, mental force and bodily strength are all generated, as well as the sum total of the fountainhead of the total living force.**
