====== SB 2.10.2 ======

> दशमस्य विशुद्ध्यर्थं नवानामिह लक्षणम् ।
> वर्णयन्ति महात्मानः श्रुतेनार्थेन चाञ्जसा ॥२॥

===== Text =====

> daśamasya viśuddhy-arthaṁ
> navānām iha lakṣaṇam
> varṇayanti mahātmānaḥ
> śrutenārthena cāñjasā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daśamasya]]---of the //summum bonum//; [[synonyms:v:viśuddhi]]---isolation; [[synonyms:a:artham]]---purpose; [[synonyms:n:navānām]]---of the other nine; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in this //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇam]]---symptoms; [[synonyms:v:varṇayanti]]---they describe; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmānaḥ]]---the great sages; [[synonyms:ś:śrutena]]---by Vedic evidences; [[synonyms:a:arthena]]---by direct explanation; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:añjasā]]---summarily.

===== Translation =====

**To isolate the transcendence of the summum bonum, the symptoms of the rest are described sometimes by Vedic inference, sometimes by direct explanation, and sometimes by summary explanations given by the great sages.**
