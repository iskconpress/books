====== SB 2.10.14 ======

> अधिदैवमथाध्यात्ममधिभूतमिति प्रभुः ।
> अथैकं पौरुषं वीर्यं त्रिधाभिद्यत तच्छृणु ॥१४॥

===== Text =====

> adhidaivam athādhyātmam
> adhibhūtam iti prabhuḥ
> athaikaṁ pauruṣaṁ vīryaṁ
> tridhābhidyata tac chṛṇu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adhidaivam]]---the controlling entities; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---now; [[synonyms:a:adhyātmam]]---the controlled entities; [[synonyms:a:adhibhūtam]]---the material bodies; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:p:prabhuḥ]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---in this way; [[synonyms:e:ekam]]---one only; [[synonyms:p:pauruṣam]]---of His Lordship; [[synonyms:v:vīryam]]---potency; [[synonyms:t:tridhā]]---in three; [[synonyms:a:abhidyata]]---divided; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---just hear from me.

===== Translation =====

**Just hear from me how the potency of His Lordship divides one into three, called the controlling entities, the controlled entities and the material bodies, in the manner mentioned above.**
