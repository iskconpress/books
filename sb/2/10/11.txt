====== SB 2.10.11 ======

> तास्ववात्सीत्स्वसृष्टासु सहस्रं परिवत्सरान् ।
> तेन नारायणो नाम यदापः पुरुषोद्भवाः ॥११॥

===== Text =====

> tāsv avātsīt sva-sṛṣṭāsu
> sahasraṁ parivatsarān
> tena nārāyaṇo nāma
> yad āpaḥ puruṣodbhavāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāsu]]---in that; [[synonyms:a:avātsīt]]---resided; [[synonyms:s:sva]]---own; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭāsu]]---in the matter of creation; [[synonyms:s:sahasram]]---one thousand; [[synonyms:p:parivatsarān]]---years of His measurement; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---for that reason; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇaḥ]]---the Personality of Godhead named Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:ā:āpaḥ]]---water; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:u:udbhavāḥ]]---emanated from the Supreme Person.

===== Translation =====

**That Supreme Person is not impersonal and therefore is distinctively a nara, or person. Therefore the transcendental water created from the Supreme Nara is known as nāra. And because He lies down on that water, He is known as Nārāyaṇa.**
