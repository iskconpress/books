====== SB 2.6.2 ======

> सर्वासूनां च वायोश्च तन्नासे परमायणे ।
> अश्विनोरोषधीनां च घ्राणो मोदप्रमोदयोः ॥२॥

===== Text =====

> sarvāsūnāṁ ca vāyoś ca
> tan-nāse paramāyaṇe
> aśvinor oṣadhīnāṁ ca
> ghrāṇo moda-pramodayoḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:a:asūnām]]---different kinds of life air; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vāyoḥ]]---of the air; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---His; [[synonyms:n:nāse]]---in the nose; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyaṇe]]---in the transcendental generating center; [[synonyms:a:aśvinoḥ]]---of the Aśvinī-kumāra demigods; [[synonyms:o:oṣadhīnām]]---of all medicinal herbs; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:g:ghrāṇaḥ]]---His smelling power; [[synonyms:m:moda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:p:pramodayoḥ]]---specific sport.

===== Translation =====

**His two nostrils are the generating centers of our breathing and of all other airs, His smelling powers generate the Aśvinī-kumāra demigods and all kinds of medicinal herbs, and His breathing energies produce different kinds of fragrance.**
