====== SB 2.6.9 ======

> पायुर्यमस्य मित्रस्य परिमोक्षस्य नारद ।
> हिंसाया निरृतेर्मृत्योर्निरयस्य गुदं स्मृतः ॥९॥

===== Text =====

> pāyur yamasya mitrasya
> parimokṣasya nārada
> hiṁsāyā nirṛter mṛtyor
> nirayasya gudaṁ smṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāyuḥ]]---the evacuating outlet; [[synonyms:y:yamasya]]---the controlling deity of death; [[synonyms:m:mitrasya]]---of Mitra; [[synonyms:p:parimokṣasya]]---of the evacuating hole; [[synonyms:n:nārada]]---O Nārada; [[synonyms:h:hiṁsāyāḥ]]---of envy; [[synonyms:n:nirṛteḥ]]---of misfortune; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyoḥ]]---of death; [[synonyms:n:nirayasya]]---of hell; [[synonyms:g:gudam]]---the rectum; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---is understood.

===== Translation =====

**O Nārada, the evacuating outlet of the universal form of the Lord is the abode of the controlling deity of death, Mitra, and the evacuating hole and the rectum of the Lord is the place of envy, misfortune, death, hell, etc.**
