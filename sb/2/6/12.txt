====== SB 2.6.12 ======

> धर्मस्य मम तुभ्यं च कुमाराणां भवस्य च ।
> विज्ञानस्य च सत्त्वस्य परस्यात्मा परायणम् ॥१२॥

===== Text =====

> dharmasya mama tubhyaṁ ca
> kumārāṇāṁ bhavasya ca
> vijñānasya ca sattvasya
> parasyātmā parāyaṇam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dharmasya]]---of religious principles, or of Yamarāja; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---mine; [[synonyms:t:tubhyam]]---of yours; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kumārāṇām]]---of the four Kumāras; [[synonyms:b:bhavasya]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and also; [[synonyms:v:vijñānasya]]---of transcendental knowledge; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:sattvasya]]---of truth; [[synonyms:p:parasya]]---of the great personality; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:p:parāyaṇam]]---dependent.

===== Translation =====

**Also, the consciousness of that great personality is the abode of religious principles---mine, yours, and those of the four bachelors Sanaka, Sanātana, Sanat-kumāra and Sanandana. That consciousness is also the abode of truth and transcendental knowledge.**
