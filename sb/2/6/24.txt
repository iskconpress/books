====== SB 2.6.24 ======

> तेषु यज्ञस्य पशवः सवनस्पतयः कुशाः ।
> इदं च देवयजनं कालश्चोरुगुणान्वितः ॥२४॥

===== Text =====

> teṣu yajñasya paśavaḥ
> savanaspatayaḥ kuśāḥ
> idaṁ ca deva-yajanaṁ
> kālaś coru-guṇānvitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṣu]]---in such sacrifices; [[synonyms:y:yajñasya]]---of the sacrificial performance; [[synonyms:p:paśavaḥ]]---the animals or the sacrificial ingredients; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vanaspatayaḥ]]---along with flowers and leaves; [[synonyms:k:kuśāḥ]]---the straw; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---all these; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as also; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:y:yajanam]]---the sacrificial altar; [[synonyms:k:kālaḥ]]---a suitable time; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as also; [[synonyms:u:uru]]---great; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:anvitaḥ]]---qualified.

===== Translation =====

**For performing sacrificial ceremonies, one requires sacrificial ingredients, such as flowers, leaves and straw, along with the sacrificial altar and a suitable time [spring].**
