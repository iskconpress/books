====== SB 2.9.26 ======

> तथापि नाथमानस्य नाथ नाथय नाथितम् ।
> परावरे यथा रूपेजानीयां ते त्वरूपिणः ॥२६॥

===== Text =====

> tathāpi nāthamānasya
> nātha nāthaya nāthitam
> parāvare yathā rūpe
> jānīyāṁ te tv arūpiṇaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---in spite of that; [[synonyms:n:nāthamānasya]]---of the one who is asking for; [[synonyms:n:nātha]]---O Lord; [[synonyms:n:nāthaya]]---please award; [[synonyms:n:nāthitam]]---as it is desired; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:a:avare]]---in the matter of mundane and transcendental; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as it is; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form; [[synonyms:j:jānīyām]]---may it be known; [[synonyms:t:te]]---Your; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:a:arūpiṇaḥ]]---one who is formless.

===== Translation =====

**In spite of that, my Lord, I am praying to You to kindly fulfill my desire. May I please be informed how, in spite of Your transcendental form, You assume the mundane form, although You have no such form at all.**
