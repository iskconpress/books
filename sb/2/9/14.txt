====== SB 2.9.14 ======

> श्रीर्यत्र रूपिण्युरुगायपादयोः करोति मानं बहुधा विभूतिभिः ।
> प्रेङ्खं श्रिता या कुसुमाकरानुगैर्विगीयमाना प्रियकर्म गायती ॥१४॥

===== Text =====

> śrīr yatra rūpiṇy urugāya-pādayoḥ
> karoti mānaṁ bahudhā vibhūtibhiḥ
> preṅkhaṁ śritā yā kusumākarānugair
> vigīyamānā priya-karma gāyatī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīḥ]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---in the Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:r:rūpiṇī]]---in her transcendental form; [[synonyms:u:urugāya]]---the Lord, who is sung of by the great devotees; [[synonyms:p:pādayoḥ]]---under the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karoti]]---does; [[synonyms:m:mānam]]---respectful services; [[synonyms:b:bahudhā]]---in diverse paraphernalia; [[synonyms:v:vibhūtibhiḥ]]---accompanied by her personal associates; [[synonyms:p:preṅkham]]---movement of enjoyment; [[synonyms:ś:śritā]]---taken shelter of; [[synonyms:y:yā]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kusumākara]]---spring; [[synonyms:a:anugaiḥ]]---by the black bees; [[synonyms:v:vigīyamānā]]---being followed by the songs; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]---activities of the dearmost; [[synonyms:g:gāyatī]]---singing.

===== Translation =====

**The goddess of fortune in her transcendental form is engaged in the loving service of the Lord's lotus feet, and being moved by the black bees, followers of spring, she is not only engaged in variegated pleasure---service to the Lord, along with her constant companions---but is also engaged in singing the glories of the Lord's activities.**
