====== SB 2.9.41 ======

> तं नारदः प्रियतमो रिक्थादानामनुव्रतः ।
> शुश्रूषमाणः शीलेन प्रश्रयेण दमेन च ॥४१॥

===== Text =====

> taṁ nāradaḥ priyatamo
> rikthādānām anuvrataḥ
> śuśrūṣamāṇaḥ śīlena
> praśrayeṇa damena ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him; [[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]]---the great sage Nārada; [[synonyms:p:priyatamaḥ]]---very dear; [[synonyms:r:riktha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādānām]]---of the inheritor sons; [[synonyms:a:anuvrataḥ]]---very obedient; [[synonyms:ś:śuśrūṣamāṇaḥ]]---always ready to serve; [[synonyms:ś:śīlena]]---by good behavior; [[synonyms:p:praśrayeṇa]]---by meekness; [[synonyms:d:damena]]---by sense control; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**Nārada, the most dear of the inheritor sons of Brahmā, always ready to serve his father, strictly follows the instructions of his father by his mannerly behavior, meekness and sense control.**
