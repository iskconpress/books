====== SB 2.8.19 ======

> तत्त्वानां परिसङ्ख्यानं लक्षणं हेतुलक्षणम् ।
> पुरुषाराधनविधिर्योगस्याध्यात्मिकस्य च ॥१९॥

===== Text =====

> tattvānāṁ parisaṅkhyānaṁ
> lakṣaṇaṁ hetu-lakṣaṇam
> puruṣārādhana-vidhir
> yogasyādhyātmikasya ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tattvānām]]---of the elements that constitute the creation; [[synonyms:p:parisaṅkhyānam]]---of the number of such elements; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇam]]---symptoms; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇam]]---the symptoms of the causes; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:ārādhana]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:v:vidhiḥ]]---rules and regulations; [[synonyms:y:yogasya]]---of cultivation of the //yoga// system; [[synonyms:a:adhyātmikasya]]---spiritual methods leading to devotional service; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**Kindly explain all about the elementary principles of creation, the number of such elementary principles, their causes, and their development, and also the process of devotional service and the method of mystic powers.**
