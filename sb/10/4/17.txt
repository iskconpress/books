====== SB 10.4.17 ======

===== Text =====

> daivam apy anṛtaṁ vakti
> na martyā eva kevalam
> yad-viśrambhād ahaṁ pāpaḥ
> svasur nihatavāñ chiśūn

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daivam]]---providence; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:a:anṛtam]]---lies; [[synonyms:v:vakti]]---say; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:m:martyāḥ]]---human beings; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kevalam]]---only; [[synonyms:y:yat]]-[[synonyms:v:viśrambhāt]]---because of believing that prophecy; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:p:pāpaḥ]]---the most sinful; [[synonyms:s:svasuḥ]]---of my sister; [[synonyms:n:nihatavān]]---killed; [[synonyms:ś:śiśūn]]---so many children.

===== Translation =====

**Alas, not only human beings but sometimes even providence lies. And I am so sinful that I believed the omen of providence and killed so many of my sister's children.**
