====== SB 10.4.31 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ cet tarhi bhojendra
> pura-grāma-vrajādiṣu
> anirdaśān nirdaśāṁś ca
> haniṣyāmo 'dya vai śiśūn

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:cet]]---if it is so; [[synonyms:t:tarhi]]---then; [[synonyms:b:bhoja]]-[[synonyms:i:indra]]---O King of Bhoja; [[synonyms:p:pura]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---in all the towns, villages and pasturing grounds; [[synonyms:a:anirdaśān]]---those who are less than ten days old; [[synonyms:n:nirdaśān]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and those who are just over ten days old; [[synonyms:h:haniṣyāmaḥ]]---we shall kill; [[synonyms:a:adya]]---beginning from today; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ś:śiśūn]]---all such children.

===== Translation =====

**If this is so, O King of the Bhoja dynasty, beginning today we shall kill all the children born in all the villages, towns and pasturing grounds within the past ten days or slightly more.**
