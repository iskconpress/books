~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.4.28~~

====== SB 10.4.28 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> kaṁsa evaṁ prasannābhyāṁ
> viśuddhaṁ pratibhāṣitaḥ
> devakī-vasudevābhyām
> anujñāto 'viśad gṛham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:k:kaṁsaḥ]]---King Kaṁsa; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:p:prasannābhyām]]---who were very much appeased; [[synonyms:v:viśuddham]]---in purity; [[synonyms:p:pratibhāṣitaḥ]]---being answered; [[synonyms:d:devakī]]-[[synonyms:v:vasudevābhyām]]---by Devakī and Vasudeva; [[synonyms:a:anujñātaḥ]]---taking permission; [[synonyms:a:aviśat]]---entered; [[synonyms:g:gṛham]]---his own palace.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: Thus having been addressed in purity by Devakī and Vasudeva, who were very much appeased, Kaṁsa felt pleased, and with their permission he entered his home.**
