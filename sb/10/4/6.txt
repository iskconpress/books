~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.4.6~~

====== SB 10.4.6 ======

===== Text =====

> nanv ahaṁ te hy avarajā
> dīnā hata-sutā prabho
> dātum arhasi mandāyā
> aṅgemāṁ caramāṁ prajām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nanu]]---however; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I am; [[synonyms:t:te]]---your; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:avarajā]]---younger sister; [[synonyms:d:dīnā]]---very poor; [[synonyms:h:hata]]-[[synonyms:s:sutā]]---deprived of all children; [[synonyms:p:prabho]]---O my lord; [[synonyms:d:dātum]] [[synonyms:a:arhasi]]---you deserve to give (some gift); [[synonyms:m:mandāyāḥ]]---to me, who am so poor; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---my dear brother; [[synonyms:i:imām]]---this; [[synonyms:c:caramām]]---last; [[synonyms:p:prajām]]---child.

===== Translation =====

**My lord, my brother, I am very poor, being bereft of all my children, but still I am your younger sister, and therefore it would be worthy of you to give me this last child as a gift.**
