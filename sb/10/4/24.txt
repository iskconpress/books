====== SB 10.4.24 ======

===== Text =====

> mocayām āsa nigaḍād
> viśrabdhaḥ kanyakā-girā
> devakīṁ vasudevaṁ ca
> darśayann ātma-sauhṛdam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mocayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---Kaṁsa released them; [[synonyms:n:nigaḍāt]]---from their iron shackles; [[synonyms:v:viśrabdhaḥ]]---with full confidence; [[synonyms:k:kanyakā]]-[[synonyms:g:girā]]---in the words of the goddess Durgā; [[synonyms:d:devakīm]]---toward his sister Devakī; [[synonyms:v:vasudevam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and his brother-in-law Vasudeva; [[synonyms:d:darśayan]]---fully exhibiting; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sauhṛdam]]---his family relationship.

===== Translation =====

**Fully believing in the words of the goddess Durgā, Kaṁsa exhibited his familial affection for Devakī and Vasudeva by immediately releasing them from their iron shackles.**
