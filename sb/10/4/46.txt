====== SB 10.4.46 ======

===== Text =====

> āyuḥ śriyaṁ yaśo dharmaṁ
> lokān āśiṣa eva ca
> hanti śreyāṁsi sarvāṇi
> puṁso mahad-atikramaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āyuḥ]]---the duration of life; [[synonyms:ś:śriyam]]---beauty; [[synonyms:y:yaśaḥ]]---fame; [[synonyms:d:dharmam]]---religion; [[synonyms:l:lokān]]---elevation to higher planets; [[synonyms:ā:āśiṣaḥ]]---blessings; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:h:hanti]]---destroys; [[synonyms:ś:śreyāṁsi]]---benedictions; [[synonyms:s:sarvāṇi]]---all; [[synonyms:p:puṁsaḥ]]---of a person; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]-[[synonyms:a:atikramaḥ]]---trespassing against great personalities.

===== Translation =====

**My dear King, when a man persecutes great souls, all his benedictions of longevity, beauty, fame, religion, blessings and promotion to higher planets will be destroyed.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Tenth Canto, Fourth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "The Atrocities of King Kaṁsa."
