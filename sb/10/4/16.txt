====== SB 10.4.16 ======

===== Text =====

> sa tv ahaṁ tyakta-kāruṇyas
> tyakta-jñāti-suhṛt khalaḥ
> kān lokān vai gamiṣyāmi
> brahma-heva mṛtaḥ śvasan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that person (Kaṁsa); [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tyakta]]-[[synonyms:k:kāruṇyaḥ]]---devoid of all mercy; [[synonyms:t:tyakta]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāti]]-[[synonyms:s:suhṛt]]---my relatives and friends have been rejected by me; [[synonyms:k:khalaḥ]]---cruel; [[synonyms:k:kān]] [[synonyms:l:lokān]]---which planets; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:g:gamiṣyāmi]]---shall go; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:h:hā]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like the killer of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:m:mṛtaḥ]] [[synonyms:ś:śvasan]]---either after death or while breathing.

===== Translation =====

**Being merciless and cruel, I have forsaken all my relatives and friends. Therefore, like a person who has killed a brāhmaṇa, I do not know to which planet I shall go, either after death or while breathing.**
