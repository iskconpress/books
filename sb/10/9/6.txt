====== SB 10.9.6 ======

===== Text =====

> sañjāta-kopaḥ sphuritāruṇādharaṁ
> sandaśya dadbhir dadhi-mantha-bhājanam
> bhittvā mṛṣāśrur dṛṣad-aśmanā raho
> jaghāsa haiyaṅgavam antaraṁ gataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sañjāta]]-[[synonyms:k:kopaḥ]]---in this way, Kṛṣṇa being very angry; [[synonyms:s:sphurita]]-[[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:adharam]]---swollen reddish lips; [[synonyms:s:sandaśya]]---capturing; [[synonyms:d:dadbhiḥ]]---by His teeth; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]-[[synonyms:m:mantha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhājanam]]---the pot in which yogurt was being churned; [[synonyms:b:bhittvā]]---breaking; [[synonyms:m:mṛṣā]]-[[synonyms:a:aśruḥ]]---with false tears in the eyes; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣat]]-[[synonyms:a:aśmanā]]---with a piece of stone; [[synonyms:r:rahaḥ]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:j:jaghāsa]]---began to eat; [[synonyms:h:haiyaṅgavam]]---the freshly churned butter; [[synonyms:a:antaram]]---within the room; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---having gone.

===== Translation =====

**Being very angry and biting His reddish lips with His teeth, Kṛṣṇa, with false tears in His eyes, broke the container of yogurt with a piece of stone. Then He entered a room and began to eat the freshly churned butter in a solitary place.**

===== Purport =====

It is natural that when a child becomes angry he can begin crying with false tears in his eyes. So Kṛṣṇa did this, and biting His reddish lips with His teeth, He broke the pot with a stone, entered a room and began to eat the freshly churned butter.
