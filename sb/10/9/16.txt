====== SB 10.9.16 ======

===== Text =====

> yadāsīt tad api nyūnaṁ
> tenānyad api sandadhe
> tad api dvy-aṅgulaṁ nyūnaṁ
> yad yad ādatta bandhanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---became; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even the new rope that had been joined; [[synonyms:n:nyūnam]]---still short; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---then, with the second rope; [[synonyms:a:anyat]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---another rope also; [[synonyms:s:sandadhe]]---she joined; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---that also; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅgulam]]---by a measurement of two fingers; [[synonyms:n:nyūnam]]---remained short; [[synonyms:y:yat]] [[synonyms:y:yat]] [[synonyms:ā:ādatta]]---in this way, one after another, whatever ropes she joined; [[synonyms:b:bandhanam]]---for binding Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**This new rope also was short by a measurement of two fingers, and when another rope was joined to it, it was still two fingers too short. As many ropes as she joined, all of them failed; their shortness could not be overcome.**
