====== SB 10.13.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śṛṇuṣvāvahito rājann
> api guhyaṁ vadāmi te
> brūyuḥ snigdhasya śiṣyasya
> guravo guhyam apy uta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śṛṇusva]]---please hear; [[synonyms:a:avahitaḥ]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King (Mahārāja Parīkṣit); [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:g:guhyam]]---very confidential (because ordinary men cannot understand the activities of Kṛṣṇa); [[synonyms:v:vadāmi]]---I shall explain; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:b:brūyuḥ]]---explain; [[synonyms:s:snigdhasya]]---submissive; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣyasya]]---of a disciple; [[synonyms:g:guravaḥ]]---spiritual masters; [[synonyms:g:guhyam]]---very confidential; [[synonyms:a:api]] [[synonyms:u:uta]]---even so.

===== Translation =====

**O King, kindly hear me with great attention. Although the activities of the Supreme Lord are very confidential, no ordinary man being able to understand them, I shall speak about them to you, for spiritual masters explain to a submissive disciple even subject matters that are very confidential and difficult to understand.**
