====== SB 10.13.6 ======

===== Text =====

> atra bhoktavyam asmābhir
> divārūḍhaṁ kṣudhārditāḥ
> vatsāḥ samīpe 'paḥ pītvā
> carantu śanakais tṛṇam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atra]]---here, on this spot; [[synonyms:b:bhoktavyam]]---our lunch should be eaten; [[synonyms:a:asmābhiḥ]]---by us; [[synonyms:d:diva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārūḍham]]---it is very late now; [[synonyms:k:kṣudhā]] [[synonyms:a:arditāḥ]]---we are fatigued with hunger; [[synonyms:v:vatsāḥ]]---the calves; [[synonyms:s:samīpe]]---nearby; [[synonyms:a:apaḥ]]---water; [[synonyms:p:pītvā]]---after drinking; [[synonyms:c:carantu]]---let them eat; [[synonyms:ś:śanakaiḥ]]---slowly; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇam]]---the grasses.

===== Translation =====

**I think we should take our lunch here, since we are already hungry because the time is very late. Here the calves may drink water and go slowly here and there and eat the grass.**
