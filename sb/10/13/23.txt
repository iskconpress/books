~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.13.23~~

====== SB 10.13.23 ======

===== Text =====

> tato nṛponmardana-majja-lepanā-
> laṅkāra-rakṣā-tilakāśanādibhiḥ
> saṁlālitaḥ svācaritaiḥ praharṣayan
> sāyaṁ gato yāma-yamena mādhavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King (Mahārāja Parīkṣit); [[synonyms:u:unmardana]]---by massaging them with oil; [[synonyms:m:majja]]---by bathing; [[synonyms:l:lepana]]---by smearing the body with oil and sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---by decorating with ornaments; [[synonyms:r:rakṣā]]---by chanting protective //mantras//; [[synonyms:t:tilaka]]---by decorating the body with //tilaka// marks in twelve places; [[synonyms:a:aśana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---and by feeding them sumptuously; [[synonyms:s:saṁlālitaḥ]]---in this way cared for by the mothers; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācaritaiḥ]]---by their characteristic behavior; [[synonyms:p:praharṣa]]-[[synonyms:y:yan]]---making the mothers very much pleased; [[synonyms:s:sāyam]]---evening; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---arrived; [[synonyms:y:yāma]]-[[synonyms:y:yamena]]---as the time of each activity passed; [[synonyms:m:mādhavaḥ]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, as required according to the scheduled round of His pastimes, Kṛṣṇa returned in the evening, entered the house of each of the cowherd boys, and engaged exactly like the former boys, thus enlivening their mothers with transcendental pleasure. The mothers took care of the boys by massaging them with oil, bathing them, smearing their bodies with sandalwood pulp, decorating them with ornaments, chanting protective mantras, decorating their bodies with tilaka and giving them food. In this way, the mothers served Kṛṣṇa personally.**
