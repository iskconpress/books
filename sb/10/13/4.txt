====== SB 10.13.4 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāgha-vadanān mṛtyo
> rakṣitvā vatsa-pālakān
> sarit-pulinam ānīya
> bhagavān idam abravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:a:agha]]-[[synonyms:v:vadanāt]]---from the mouth of Aghāsura; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyoḥ]]---death personified; [[synonyms:r:rakṣitvā]]---after saving; [[synonyms:v:vatsa]]-[[synonyms:p:pālakān]]---all the cowherd boys and calves; [[synonyms:s:sarit]]-[[synonyms:p:pulinam]]---to the bank of the river; [[synonyms:ā:ānīya]]---bringing them; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---these words; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---spoke.

===== Translation =====

**Then, after saving the boys and calves from the mouth of Aghāsura, who was death personified, Lord Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, brought them all to the bank of the river and spoke the following words.**
