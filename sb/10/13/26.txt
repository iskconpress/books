====== SB 10.13.26 ======

===== Text =====

> vrajaukasāṁ sva-tokeṣu
> sneha-vally ābdam anvaham
> śanair niḥsīma vavṛdhe
> yathā kṛṣṇe tv apūrvavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:o:okasām]]---of all the inhabitants of Vraja, Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:t:tokeṣu]]---for their own sons; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]-[[synonyms:v:vallī]]---the creeper of affection; [[synonyms:ā:ā]]-[[synonyms:a:abdam]]---for one year; [[synonyms:a:anu]]-[[synonyms:a:aham]]---every day; [[synonyms:ś:śanaiḥ]]---gradually; [[synonyms:n:niḥsīma]]---without limit; [[synonyms:v:vavṛdhe]]---increased; [[synonyms:y:yathā]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---exactly accepting Kṛṣṇa as their son; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as it had not been previously.

===== Translation =====

**Although the inhabitants of Vrajabhūmi, the cowherd men and cowherd women, previously had more affection for Kṛṣṇa than for their own children, now, for one year, their affection for their own sons continuously increased, for Kṛṣṇa had now become their sons. There was no limit to the increment of their affection for their sons, who were now Kṛṣṇa. Every day they found new inspiration for loving their children as much as they loved Kṛṣṇa.**
