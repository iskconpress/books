====== SB 10.13.32 ======

===== Text =====

> gopās tad-rodhanāyāsa-
> maughya-lajjoru-manyunā
> durgādhva-kṛcchrato 'bhyetya
> go-vatsair dadṛśuḥ sutān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāḥ]]---the cowherd men; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:r:rodhana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyāsa]]---of their attempt to stop the cows from going to their calves; [[synonyms:m:maughya]]---on account of the frustration; [[synonyms:l:lajjā]]---being ashamed; [[synonyms:u:uru]]-[[synonyms:m:manyunā]]---and at the same time becoming very angry; [[synonyms:d:durga]]-[[synonyms:a:adhva]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛcchrataḥ]]---although they passed the very rough way with great difficulty; [[synonyms:a:abhyetya]]---after reaching there; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsaiḥ]]---along with the calves; [[synonyms:d:dadṛśuḥ]]---saw; [[synonyms:s:sutān]]---their respective sons.

===== Translation =====

**The cowherd men, having been unable to check the cows from going to their calves, felt simultaneously ashamed and angry. They crossed the rough road with great difficulty, but when they came down and saw their own sons, they were overwhelmed by great affection.**

===== Purport =====

Everyone was increasing in affection for Kṛṣṇa. When the cowherd men coming down from the hill saw their own sons, who were no one else than Kṛṣṇa, their affection increased.
