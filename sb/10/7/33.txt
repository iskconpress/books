====== SB 10.7.33 ======

===== Text =====

> dṛṣṭvādbhutāni bahuśo
> nanda-gopo bṛhadvane
> vasudeva-vaco bhūyo
> mānayām āsa vismitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:a:adbhutāni]]---the very wonderful and astonishing incidents; [[synonyms:b:bahuśaḥ]]---many times; [[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gopaḥ]]---Nanda Mahārāja, the head of the cowherd men; [[synonyms:b:bṛhadvane]]---in Bṛhadvana; [[synonyms:v:vasudeva]]-[[synonyms:v:vacaḥ]]---the words spoken by Vasudeva when Nanda Mahārāja was in Mathurā; [[synonyms:b:bhūyaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:m:mānayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---accepted how true they were; [[synonyms:v:vismitaḥ]]---in great astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Having seen all these incidents in Bṛhadvana, Nanda Mahārāja became more and more astonished, and he remembered the words spoken to him by Vasudeva in Mathurā.**
