====== SB 10.7.10 ======

===== Text =====

> na te śraddadhire gopā
> bāla-bhāṣitam ity uta
> aprameyaṁ balaṁ tasya
> bālakasya na te viduḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:te]]---the cowherd men and ladies; [[synonyms:ś:śraddadhire]]---put their faith (in such statements); [[synonyms:g:gopāḥ]]---the cowherd men and women; [[synonyms:b:bāla]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāṣitam]]---childish talk from the assembled children; [[synonyms:i:iti]] [[synonyms:u:uta]]---thus spoken; [[synonyms:a:aprameyam]]---unlimited, inconceivable; [[synonyms:b:balam]]---the power; [[synonyms:t:tasya]] [[synonyms:b:bālakasya]]---of the small baby Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:te]]---the //gopīs// and //gopas//; [[synonyms:v:viduḥ]]---were aware of.

===== Translation =====

**The assembled gopīs and gopas, unaware that Kṛṣṇa is always unlimited, could not believe that baby Kṛṣṇa had such inconceivable power. They could not believe the statements of the children, and therefore they neglected these statements as being childish talk.**
