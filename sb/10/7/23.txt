====== SB 10.7.23 ======

===== Text =====

> nāpaśyat kaścanātmānaṁ
> paraṁ cāpi vimohitaḥ
> tṛṇāvarta-nisṛṣṭābhiḥ
> śarkarābhir upadrutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:apaśyat]]---saw; [[synonyms:k:kaścana]]---anyone; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---himself; [[synonyms:p:param]] [[synonyms:c:ca]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---or another; [[synonyms:v:vimohitaḥ]]---being illusioned; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇāvarta]]-[[synonyms:n:nisṛṣṭābhiḥ]]---thrown by Tṛṇāvartāsura; [[synonyms:ś:śarkarābhiḥ]]---by the sands; [[synonyms:u:upadrutaḥ]]---and thus being disturbed.

===== Translation =====

**Because of the bits of sand thrown about by Tṛṇāvarta, people could not see themselves or anyone else, and thus they were illusioned and disturbed.**
