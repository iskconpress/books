====== SB 10.2.3 ======

===== Text =====

> te pīḍitā niviviśuḥ
> kuru-pañcāla-kekayān
> śālvān vidarbhān niṣadhān
> videhān kośalān api

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:te]]---they (the kings of the Yadu dynasty); [[synonyms:p:pīḍitāḥ]]---being persecuted; [[synonyms:n:niviviśuḥ]]---took shelter or entered (the kingdoms); [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:p:pañcāla]]---the countries occupied by the Kurus and Pañcālas; [[synonyms:k:kekayān]]---the countries of the Kekayas; [[synonyms:ś:śālvān]]---the countries occupied by the Śālvas; [[synonyms:v:vidarbhān]]---the countries occupied by the Vidarbhas; [[synonyms:n:niṣadhān]]---the countries occupied by the Niṣadhas; [[synonyms:v:videhān]]---the country of Videha; [[synonyms:k:kośalān]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---as well as the countries occupied by the Kośalas.

===== Translation =====

**Persecuted by the demoniac kings, the Yādavas left their own kingdom and entered various others, like those of the Kurus, Pañcālas, Kekayas, Śālvas, Vidarbhas, Niṣadhas, Videhas and Kośalas.**
