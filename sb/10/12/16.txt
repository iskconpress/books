====== SB 10.12.16 ======

===== Text =====

> iti vyavasyājagaraṁ bṛhad vapuḥ
> sa yojanāyāma-mahādri-pīvaram
> dhṛtvādbhutaṁ vyātta-guhānanaṁ tadā
> pathi vyaśeta grasanāśayā khalaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vyavasya]]---deciding; [[synonyms:ā:ājagaram]]---python; [[synonyms:b:bṛhat]] [[synonyms:v:vapuḥ]]---a very, very large body; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Aghāsura; [[synonyms:y:yojana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyāma]]---occupying eight miles of land; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:adri]]-[[synonyms:p:pīvaram]]---as thick as a great mountain; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtvā]]---assuming this form; [[synonyms:a:adbhutam]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:v:vyātta]]---spread; [[synonyms:g:guhā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanam]]---having a mouth resembling a big cave in a mountain; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:pathi]]---on the road; [[synonyms:v:vyaśeta]]---occupied; [[synonyms:g:grasana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśayā]]---expecting to swallow all the cowherd boys; [[synonyms:k:khalaḥ]]---the most crooked.

===== Translation =====

**After thus deciding, that crooked Aghāsura assumed the form of a huge python, as thick as a big mountain and as long as eight miles. Having assumed this wonderful python's body, he spread his mouth like a big cave in the mountains and lay down on the road, expecting to swallow Kṛṣṇa and His associates the cowherd boys.**
