====== SB 10.12.21 ======

===== Text =====

> pratispardhete sṛkkabhyāṁ
> savyāsavye nagodare
> tuṅga-śṛṅgālayo 'py etās
> tad-daṁṣṭrābhiś ca paśyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratispardhete]]---just resembling; [[synonyms:s:sṛkkabhyām]]---with the corners of the mouth; [[synonyms:s:savya]]-[[synonyms:a:asavye]]---left and right; [[synonyms:n:naga]]-[[synonyms:u:udare]]---caves of a mountain; [[synonyms:t:tuṅga]]-[[synonyms:ś:śṛṅga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālayaḥ]]---the high mountain peaks; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although it is so; [[synonyms:e:etāḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:d:daṁṣṭrābhiḥ]]---they resemble the teeth of the animal; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paśyata]]---just see.

===== Translation =====

**On the left and right, the two depressions resembling mountain caves are the corners of its mouth, and the high mountain peaks are its teeth.**
