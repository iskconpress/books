====== SB 10.12.23 ======

===== Text =====

> dāvoṣṇa-khara-vāto 'yaṁ
> śvāsavad bhāti paśyata
> tad-dagdha-sattva-durgandho
> 'py antar-āmiṣa-gandhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāva]]-[[synonyms:u:uṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:khara]]-[[synonyms:v:vātaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---hot breath coming out exactly like fire; [[synonyms:ś:śvāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]] [[synonyms:b:bhāti]] [[synonyms:p:paśyata]]---just see how it resembles his breath; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:d:dagdha]]-[[synonyms:s:sattva]]---of burning corpses; [[synonyms:d:durgandhaḥ]]---the bad smell; [[synonyms:a:api]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:ā:āmiṣa]]-[[synonyms:g:gandha]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---is like the fleshy smell coming out from within.

===== Translation =====

**The hot fiery wind is the breath coming out of his mouth, which is giving off the bad smell of burning flesh because of all the dead bodies he has eaten.**
