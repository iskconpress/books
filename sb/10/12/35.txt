====== SB 10.12.35 ======

===== Text =====

> tad-adbhuta-stotra-suvādya-gītikā-
> jayādi-naikotsava-maṅgala-svanān
> śrutvā sva-dhāmno 'nty aja āgato 'cirād
> dṛṣṭvā mahīśasya jagāma vismayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]---that celebration performed by the demigods in the upper planetary system; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:s:stotra]]---prayers; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vādya]]---glorious musical sounds of drums and other instruments; [[synonyms:g:gītikā]]---celestial songs; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---sounds of //jaya,// etc.; [[synonyms:n:na]]-[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:u:utsava]]---celebrations simply for glorifying the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:s:svanān]]---transcendental sounds auspicious for everyone; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---hearing such sounds; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāmnaḥ]]---from his abode; [[synonyms:a:anti]]---nearby; [[synonyms:a:ajaḥ]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ā:āgataḥ]]---coming there; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mahi]]---the glorification; [[synonyms:ī:īśasya]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jagāma]] [[synonyms:v:vismayam]]---became astonished.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Brahmā heard the wonderful ceremony going on near his planet, accompanied by music and songs and sounds of "Jaya! Jaya!" he immediately came down to see the function. Upon seeing so much glorification of Lord Kṛṣṇa, he was completely astonished.**

===== Purport =====

Here the word //anti// means "near," indicating that even in the higher planetary systems near Brahmaloka, like Maharloka, Janaloka and Tapoloka, the festival of glorification of Lord Kṛṣṇa was going on.
