====== SB 10.12.19 ======

===== Text =====

> aho mitrāṇi gadata
> sattva-kūṭaṁ puraḥ sthitam
> asmat-saṅgrasana-vyātta-
> vyāla-tuṇḍāyate na vā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aho]]---oh; [[synonyms:m:mitrāṇi]]---friends; [[synonyms:g:gadata]]---just let us know; [[synonyms:s:sattva]]-[[synonyms:k:kūṭam]]---dead python; [[synonyms:p:puraḥ]] [[synonyms:s:sthitam]]---as it is just before us all; [[synonyms:a:asmat]]---all of us; [[synonyms:s:saṅgrasana]]---to devour us altogether; [[synonyms:v:vyātta]]-[[synonyms:v:vyāla]]-[[synonyms:t:tuṇḍā]]-[[synonyms:y:yate]]---the python has spread its mouth; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:v:vā]]---whether it is a fact or not.

===== Translation =====

**The boys said: Dear friends, is this creature dead, or is it actually a living python with its mouth spread wide just to swallow us all? Kindly clear up this doubt.**

===== Purport =====

The friends began to discuss among themselves the reality of the wonderful creature laying before them. Was it dead, or was it actually a living python trying to swallow them up?
