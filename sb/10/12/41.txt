~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.12.41~~

====== SB 10.12.41 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> brahman kālāntara-kṛtaṁ
> tat-kālīnaṁ kathaṁ bhavet
> yat kaumāre hari-kṛtaṁ
> jaguḥ paugaṇḍake 'rbhakāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Mahārāja Parīkṣit inquired; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O learned //brāhmaṇa// (Śukadeva Gosvāmī); [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---things done in the past, at a different time (in the //kaumāra// age); [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:k:kālīnam]]---described as happening now (in the //paugaṇḍa// age); [[synonyms:k:katham]] [[synonyms:b:bhavet]]---how could it be so; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which pastime; [[synonyms:k:kaumāre]]---in the //kaumāra// age; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---was done by Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jaguḥ]]---they described; [[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍake]]---in the //paugaṇḍa// age (after one year); [[synonyms:a:arbhakāḥ]]---all the boys.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Parīkṣit inquired: O great sage, how could things done in the past have been described as being done at the present? Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa performed this pastime of killing Aghāsura during His kaumāra age. How then, during His paugaṇḍa age, could the boys have described this incident as having happened recently?**
