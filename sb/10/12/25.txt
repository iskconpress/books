====== SB 10.12.25 ======

===== Text =====

> itthaṁ mitho 'tathyam ataj-jña-bhāṣitaṁ
> śrutvā vicintyety amṛṣā mṛṣāyate
> rakṣo viditvākhila-bhūta-hṛt-sthitaḥ
> svānāṁ niroddhuṁ bhagavān mano dadhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ittham]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mithaḥ]]---or another; [[synonyms:a:atathyam]]---a subject matter that is not a fact; [[synonyms:a:a]]-[[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---without knowledge; [[synonyms:b:bhāṣitam]]---while they were talking; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---Kṛṣṇa hearing them; [[synonyms:v:vicintya]]---thinking; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:amṛṣā]]---actually, truly; [[synonyms:m:mṛṣāyate]]---who is trying to appear as a false thing (actually the animal was Aghāsura, but because of poor knowledge they were thinking him to be a dead python); [[synonyms:r:rakṣaḥ]]---(Kṛṣṇa, however, could understand that) he was a demon; [[synonyms:v:viditvā]]---knowing it; [[synonyms:a:akhila]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:h:hṛt]]-[[synonyms:s:sthitaḥ]]---because He is //antaryāmī,// situated everywhere, in the core of everyone's heart; [[synonyms:s:svānām]]---of His own associates; [[synonyms:n:niroddhum]]---just to forbid them; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]] [[synonyms:d:dadhe]]---made up His mind.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Kṛṣṇa, who is situated as antaryāmī, the Supersoul, in the core of everyone's heart, heard the boys talking among themselves about the artificial python. Unknown to them, it was actually Aghāsura, a demon who had appeared as a python. Kṛṣṇa, knowing this, wanted to forbid His associates to enter the demon's mouth.**
