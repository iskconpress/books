~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.12.40~~

====== SB 10.12.40 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-sūta uvāca
> itthaṁ dvijā yādavadeva-dattaḥ
> śrutvā sva-rātuś caritaṁ vicitram
> papraccha bhūyo 'pi tad eva puṇyaṁ
> vaiyāsakiṁ yan nigṛhīta-cetāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī spoke to the assembled saints at Naimiṣāraṇya; [[synonyms:i:ittham]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:dvijāḥ]]---O learned //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:y:yādava]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:dattaḥ]]---Mahārāja Parīkṣit (or Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira), who was protected by Yādavadeva, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rātuḥ]]---of Kṛṣṇa, who was his savior within the womb of his mother, Uttarā; [[synonyms:c:caritam]]---the activities; [[synonyms:v:vicitram]]---all wonderful; [[synonyms:p:papraccha]]---inquired; [[synonyms:b:bhūyaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even again; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---such activities; [[synonyms:p:puṇyam]]---which are always full of pious activities (//śṛṇvatāṁ sva-kathāḥ kṛṣṇaḥ puṇya-śravaṇa-kīrtanaḥ:// [[books:sb:1:2:17|[SB 1.2.17]]] to hear about Kṛṣṇa is always pious); [[synonyms:v:vaiyāsakim]]---unto Śukadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:n:nigṛhīta]]-[[synonyms:c:cetāḥ]]---Parīkṣit Mahārāja had already become steady in hearing about Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said: O learned saints, the childhood pastimes of Śrī Kṛṣṇa are very wonderful. Mahārāja Parīkṣit, after hearing about those pastimes of Kṛṣṇa, who had saved him in the womb of his mother, became steady in his mind and again inquired from Śukadeva Gosvāmī to hear about those pious activities.**
