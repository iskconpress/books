~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.12.29~~

====== SB 10.12.29 ======

===== Text =====

> tadā ghana-cchadā devā
> bhayād dhā-heti cukruśuḥ
> jahṛṣur ye ca kaṁsādyāḥ
> kauṇapās tv agha-bāndhavāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:g:ghana]]-[[synonyms:c:chadāḥ]]---behind the clouds; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:b:bhayāt]]---on account of feeling danger because Kṛṣṇa had entered the mouth of the demon; [[synonyms:h:hā]]-[[synonyms:h:hā]]---alas, alas; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---in this way; [[synonyms:c:cukruśuḥ]]---they exclaimed; [[synonyms:j:jahṛṣuḥ]]---became jubilant; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---those; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kaṁsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---Kaṁsa and others; [[synonyms:k:kauṇapāḥ]]---the demons; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:agha]]-[[synonyms:b:bāndhavāḥ]]---the friends of Aghāsura.

===== Translation =====

**When Kṛṣṇa entered the mouth of Aghāsura, the demigods hidden behind the clouds exclaimed, "Alas! Alas!" But the friends of Aghāsura, like Kaṁsa and other demons, were jubilant.**
