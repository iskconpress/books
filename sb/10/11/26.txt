====== SB 10.11.26 ======

===== Text =====

> yan na mriyeta drumayor
> antaraṁ prāpya bālakaḥ
> asāv anyatamo vāpi
> tad apy acyuta-rakṣaṇam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yat]]---then again; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:m:mriyeta]]---did not die; [[synonyms:d:drumayoḥ]] [[synonyms:a:antaram]]---between the two trees; [[synonyms:p:prāpya]]---although He was between; [[synonyms:b:bālakaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:asau]]---that child, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:anyatamaḥ]]---another child; [[synonyms:v:vā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---or; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:a:api]] [[synonyms:a:acyuta]]-[[synonyms:r:rakṣaṇam]]---in that case also, He was saved by the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Even the other day, neither Kṛṣṇa nor any of His playmates died from the falling of the two trees, although the children were near the trees or even between them. This also is to be considered the mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
