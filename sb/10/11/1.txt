====== SB 10.11.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> gopā nandādayaḥ śrutvā
> drumayoḥ patato ravam
> tatrājagmuḥ kuru-śreṣṭha
> nirghāta-bhaya-śaṅkitāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:g:gopāḥ]]---all the cowherd men; [[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---headed by Nanda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:d:drumayoḥ]]---of the two trees; [[synonyms:p:patatoḥ]]---falling down; [[synonyms:r:ravam]]---the high sound, as terrible as a thunderbolt; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there, on the spot; [[synonyms:ā:ājagmuḥ]]---went; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:n:nirghāta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkitāḥ]]---who were afraid of falling thunderbolts.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, when the yamala-arjuna trees fell, all the cowherd men in the neighborhood, hearing the fierce sound and fearing thunderbolts, went to the spot.**
