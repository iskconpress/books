====== SB 10.11.24 ======

===== Text =====

> muktaḥ kathañcid rākṣasyā
> bāla-ghnyā bālako hy asau
> harer anugrahān nūnam
> anaś copari nāpatat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:muktaḥ]]---was delivered; [[synonyms:k:kathañcit]]---somehow or other; [[synonyms:r:rākṣasyāḥ]]---from the hands of the Rākṣasī Pūtanā; [[synonyms:b:bāla]]-[[synonyms:g:ghnyāḥ]]---who was determined to kill small children; [[synonyms:b:bālakaḥ]]---especially the child Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---because; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---He; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:a:anugrahāt]]---by the mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nūnam]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:anaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and the handcart; [[synonyms:u:upari]]---on top of the child; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:apatat]]---did fall down.

===== Translation =====

**The child Kṛṣṇa, simply by the mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, was somehow or other rescued from the hands of the Rākṣasī Pūtanā, who was determined to kill Him. Then, again by the mercy of the Supreme Godhead, the handcart missed falling upon the child.**
