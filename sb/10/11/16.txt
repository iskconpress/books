====== SB 10.11.16 ======

===== Text =====

> he rāmāgaccha tātāśu
> sānujaḥ kula-nandana
> prātar eva kṛtāhāras
> tad bhavān bhoktum arhati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:he]] [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---my dear son Balarāma; [[synonyms:ā:āgaccha]]---please come here; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---my dear darling; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]---immediately; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:a:anujaḥ]]---with Your younger brother; [[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the great hope of our family; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly in the morning; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:āhāraḥ]]---have taken Your breakfast; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---therefore; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---You; [[synonyms:b:bhoktum]]---to eat something more; [[synonyms:a:arhati]]---deserve.

===== Translation =====

**My dear Baladeva, best of our family, please come immediately with Your younger brother, Kṛṣṇa. You both ate in the morning, and now You ought to eat something more.**
