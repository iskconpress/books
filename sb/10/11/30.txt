====== SB 10.11.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tac chrutvaika-dhiyo gopāḥ
> sādhu sādhv iti vādinaḥ
> vrajān svān svān samāyujya
> yayū rūḍha-paricchadāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---hearing this advice of Upānanda's; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dhiyaḥ]]---voting unanimously; [[synonyms:g:gopāḥ]]---all the cowherd men; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]] [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]---very nice, very nice; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vādinaḥ]]---speaking, declaring; [[synonyms:v:vrajān]]---cows; [[synonyms:s:svān]] [[synonyms:s:svān]]---own respective; [[synonyms:s:samāyujya]]---assembling; [[synonyms:y:yayuḥ]]---started; [[synonyms:r:rūḍha]]-[[synonyms:p:paricchadāḥ]]---all the dresses and paraphernalia having been kept on the carts.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this advice from Upananda, the cowherd men unanimously agreed. "Very nice," they said. "Very nice." Thus they sorted out their household affairs, placed their clothing and other paraphernalia on the carts, and immediately started for Vṛndāvana.**
