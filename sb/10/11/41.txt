====== SB 10.11.41 ======

===== Text =====

> kadācid yamunā-tīre
> vatsāṁś cārayatoḥ svakaiḥ
> vayasyaiḥ kṛṣṇa-balayor
> jighāṁsur daitya āgamat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kadācit]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank of the Yamunā; [[synonyms:v:vatsān]]---the calves; [[synonyms:c:cārayatoḥ]]---when They were tending; [[synonyms:s:svakaiḥ]]---Their own; [[synonyms:v:vayasyaiḥ]]---with other playmates; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:balayoḥ]]---both Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma; [[synonyms:j:jighāṁsuḥ]]---desiring to kill Them; [[synonyms:d:daityaḥ]]---another demon; [[synonyms:ā:āgamat]]---reached there.

===== Translation =====

**One day while Rāma and Kṛṣṇa, along with Their playmates, were tending the calves on the bank of the River Yamunā, another demon arrived there, desiring to kill Them.**
