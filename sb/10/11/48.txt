====== SB 10.11.48 ======

===== Text =====

> sa vai bako nāma mahān
> asuro baka-rūpa-dhṛk
> āgatya sahasā kṛṣṇaṁ
> tīkṣṇa-tuṇḍo 'grasad balī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that creature; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:b:bakaḥ]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by the name Bakāsura; [[synonyms:m:mahān]] [[synonyms:a:asuraḥ]]---a great, gigantic demon; [[synonyms:b:baka]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhṛk]]---assumed the bodily shape of a big duck; [[synonyms:ā:āgatya]]---coming there; [[synonyms:s:sahasā]]---all of a sudden; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇam]]---Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tīkṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tuṇḍaḥ]]---sharp beak; [[synonyms:a:agrasat]]---swallowed; [[synonyms:b:balī]]---very powerful.

===== Translation =====

**That great-bodied demon was named Bakāsura. He had assumed the body of a duck with a very sharp beak. Having come there, he immediately swallowed Kṛṣṇa.**
