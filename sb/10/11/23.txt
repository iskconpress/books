====== SB 10.11.23 ======

===== Text =====

> utthātavyam ito 'smābhir
> gokulasya hitaiṣibhiḥ
> āyānty atra mahotpātā
> bālānāṁ nāśa-hetavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:utthātavyam]]---now this place should be left; [[synonyms:i:itaḥ]]---from here, from Gokula; [[synonyms:a:asmābhiḥ]]---by all of us; [[synonyms:g:gokulasya]]---of this place, Gokula; [[synonyms:h:hita]]-[[synonyms:e:eṣibhiḥ]]---by persons who desire good for this place; [[synonyms:ā:āyānti]]---are happening; [[synonyms:a:atra]]---here; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:utpātāḥ]]---many great disturbances; [[synonyms:b:bālānām]]---for the boys like Rāma and Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]-[[synonyms:h:hetavaḥ]]---having the definite purpose of killing Them.

===== Translation =====

**He said: My dear friends the cowherd men, in order to do good to this place, Gokula, we should leave it, because so many disturbances are always occurring here, just for the purpose of killing Rāma and Kṛṣṇa.**
