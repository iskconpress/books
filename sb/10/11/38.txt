====== SB 10.11.38 ======

===== Text =====

> avidūre vraja-bhuvaḥ
> saha gopāla-dārakaiḥ
> cārayām āsatur vatsān
> nānā-krīḍā-paricchadau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avidūre]]---not very far from the residential quarters of the Vrajavāsīs; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvaḥ]]---from the land known as Vraja; [[synonyms:s:saha]] [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:d:dārakaiḥ]]---with other boys of the same profession (cowherd boys); [[synonyms:c:cārayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsatuḥ]]---tended; [[synonyms:v:vatsān]]---the small calves; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---sporting; [[synonyms:p:paricchadau]]---dressed very nicely in different ways and equipped with implements.

===== Translation =====

**Not far away from Their residential quarters, both Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma, equipped with all kinds of playthings, played with other cowherd boys and began to tend the small calves.**
