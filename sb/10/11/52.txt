====== SB 10.11.52 ======

===== Text =====

> tadā bakāriṁ sura-loka-vāsinaḥ
> samākiran nandana-mallikādibhiḥ
> samīḍire cānaka-śaṅkha-saṁstavais
> tad vīkṣya gopāla-sutā visismire

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:b:baka]]-[[synonyms:a:arim]]---unto the enemy of Bakāsura; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsinaḥ]]---the celestial denizens of the higher planets; [[synonyms:s:samākiran]]---showered flowers; [[synonyms:n:nandana]]-[[synonyms:m:mallikā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---with such flowers as //mallikā,// which are grown in Nandana-kānana; [[synonyms:s:samīḍire]]---also congratulated Him; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:ā:ānaka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁstavaiḥ]]---by celestial kettledrums and conchshells, accompanied with prayers; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---by seeing this; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---the cowherd boys; [[synonyms:v:visismire]]---were struck with wonder.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, the celestial denizens of the higher planetary system showered mallikā-puṣpa, flowers grown in Nandana-kānana, upon Kṛṣṇa, the enemy of Bakāsura. They also congratulated Him by sounding celestial kettledrums and conchshells and by offering prayers. Seeing this, the cowherd boys were struck with wonder.**
