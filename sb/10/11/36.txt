====== SB 10.11.36 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvanaṁ govardhanaṁ
> yamunā-pulināni ca
> vīkṣyāsīd uttamā prītī
> rāma-mādhavayor nṛpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanam]]---the place known as Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:g:govardhanam]]---along with Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]-[[synonyms:p:pulināni]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and the banks of the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---seeing this situation; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---remained or was enjoyed; [[synonyms:u:uttamā]] [[synonyms:p:prītī]]---first-class pleasure; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhavayoḥ]]---of Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King Parīkṣit.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, when Rāma and Kṛṣṇa saw Vṛndāvana, Govardhana and the banks of the River Yamunā, They both enjoyed great pleasure.**
