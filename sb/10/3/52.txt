~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.3.52~~

====== SB 10.3.52 ======

===== Text =====

> devakyāḥ śayane nyasya
> vasudevo 'tha dārikām
> pratimucya pador loham
> āste pūrvavad āvṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devakyāḥ]]---of Devakī; [[synonyms:ś:śayane]]---on the bed; [[synonyms:n:nyasya]]---placing; [[synonyms:v:vasudevaḥ]]---Vasudeva; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:dārikām]]---the female child; [[synonyms:p:pratimucya]]---binding himself again; [[synonyms:p:padoḥ]] [[synonyms:l:loham]]---iron shackles on the two legs; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like before; [[synonyms:ā:āvṛtaḥ]]---bound.

===== Translation =====

**Vasudeva placed the female child on the bed of Devakī, bound his legs with the iron shackles, and thus remained there as before.**
