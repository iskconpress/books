====== SB 10.3.42 ======

===== Text =====

> tayor vāṁ punar evāham
> adityām āsa kaśyapāt
> upendra iti vikhyāto
> vāmanatvāc ca vāmanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---of you two, husband and wife; [[synonyms:v:vām]]---in both of you; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---even again; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I Myself; [[synonyms:a:adityām]]---in the womb of Aditi; [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---appeared; [[synonyms:k:kaśyapāt]]---by the semen of Kaśyapa Muni; [[synonyms:u:upendraḥ]]---by the name Upendra; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vikhyātaḥ]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:v:vāmanatvāt]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and because of being a dwarf; [[synonyms:v:vāmanaḥ]]---I was known as Vāmana.

===== Translation =====

**In the next millennium, I again appeared from the two of you, who appeared as My mother, Aditi, and My father, Kaśyapa. I was known as Upendra, and because of being a dwarf, I was also known as Vāmana.**
