====== SB 10.3.6 ======

===== Text =====

> jaguḥ kinnara-gandharvās
> tuṣṭuvuḥ siddha-cāraṇāḥ
> vidyādharyaś ca nanṛtur
> apsarobhiḥ samaṁ mudā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaguḥ]]---recited auspicious songs; [[synonyms:k:kinnara]]-[[synonyms:g:gandharvāḥ]]---the Kinnaras and Gandharvas, inhabitants of various planets in the heavenly planetary system; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭuvuḥ]]---offered their respective prayers; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:c:cāraṇāḥ]]---the Siddhas and Cāraṇas, other inhabitants of the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:v:vidyādharyaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and the Vidyādharīs, another group of inhabitants of the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:n:nanṛtuḥ]]---danced in transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:a:apsarobhiḥ]]---the Apsarās, beautiful dancers in the heavenly kingdom; [[synonyms:s:samam]]---along with; [[synonyms:m:mudā]]---in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**The Kinnaras and Gandharvas began to sing auspicious songs, the Siddhas and Cāraṇas offered auspicious prayers, and the Vidyādharīs, along with the Apsarās, began to dance in jubilation.**
