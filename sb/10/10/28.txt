====== SB 10.10.28 ======

===== Text =====

> tatra śriyā paramayā kakubhaḥ sphurantau
> siddhāv upetya kujayor iva jāta-vedāḥ
> kṛṣṇaṁ praṇamya śirasākhila-loka-nāthaṁ
> baddhāñjalī virajasāv idam ūcatuḥ sma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there, on the very spot where the two //arjunas// fell; [[synonyms:ś:śriyā]]---with beautification; [[synonyms:p:paramayā]]---superexcellent; [[synonyms:k:kakubhaḥ]]---all directions; [[synonyms:s:sphurantau]]---illuminating by effulgence; [[synonyms:s:siddhau]]---two perfect persons; [[synonyms:u:upetya]]---then coming out; [[synonyms:k:kujayoḥ]]---from between the two trees; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:j:jāta]]-[[synonyms:v:vedāḥ]]---fire personified; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇam]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:praṇamya]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:ś:śirasā]]---with the head; [[synonyms:a:akhila]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:n:nātham]]---to the Supreme Person, the controller of everything; [[synonyms:b:baddha]]-[[synonyms:a:añjalī]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:v:virajasau]]---fully cleansed of the mode of ignorance; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---the following words; [[synonyms:ū:ūcatuḥ]] [[synonyms:s:sma]]---uttered.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, in that very place where the two arjuna trees had fallen, two great, perfect personalities, who appeared like fire personified, came out of the two trees. The effulgence of their beauty illuminating all directions, with bowed heads they offered obeisances to Kṛṣṇa, and with hands folded they spoke the following words.**
