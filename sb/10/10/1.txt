~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.10.1~~

====== SB 10.10.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> kathyatāṁ bhagavann etat
> tayoḥ śāpasya kāraṇam
> yat tad vigarhitaṁ karma
> yena vā devarṣes tamaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the King further inquired; [[synonyms:k:kathyatām]]---please describe; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O supremely powerful one; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:ś:śāpasya]]---of cursing; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇam]]---the cause; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vigarhitam]]---abominable; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---act; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:d:devarṣeḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---the great sage Nārada became so angry.

===== Translation =====

**King Parīkṣit inquired from Śukadeva Gosvāmī: O great and powerful saint, what was the cause of Nalakūvara's and Maṇigrīva's having been cursed by Nārada Muni? What did they do that was so abominable that even Nārada, the great sage, became angry at them? Kindly describe this to me.**
