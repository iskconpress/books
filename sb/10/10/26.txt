====== SB 10.10.26 ======

===== Text =====

> ity antareṇārjunayoḥ
> kṛṣṇas tu yamayor yayau
> ātma-nirveśa-mātreṇa
> tiryag-gatam ulūkhalam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus deciding; [[synonyms:a:antareṇa]]---between; [[synonyms:a:arjunayoḥ]]---the two //arjuna// trees; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tu]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yamayoḥ]] [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---entered between the two trees; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:n:nirveśa]]-[[synonyms:m:mātreṇa]]---as soon as He entered (between the two trees); [[synonyms:t:tiryak]]---crossways; [[synonyms:g:gatam]]---so became; [[synonyms:u:ulūkhalam]]---the big mortar for grinding spices.

===== Translation =====

**Having thus spoken, Kṛṣṇa soon entered between the two arjuna trees, and thus the big mortar to which He was bound turned crosswise and stuck between them.**
