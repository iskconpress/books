====== SB 10.10.43 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> ity uktau tau parikramya
> praṇamya ca punaḥ punaḥ
> baddholūkhalam āmantrya
> jagmatur diśam uttarām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]] [[synonyms:u:uktau]]---having been ordered by the Supreme Personality of Godhead in this way; [[synonyms:t:tau]]---Nalakūvara and Maṇigrīva; [[synonyms:p:parikramya]]---circumambulating; [[synonyms:p:praṇamya]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:b:baddha]]-[[synonyms:u:ulūkhalam]] [[synonyms:ā:āmantrya]]---taking the permission of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who was bound to the wooden mortar; [[synonyms:j:jagmatuḥ]]---departed; [[synonyms:d:diśam]] [[synonyms:u:uttarām]]---to their respective destinations.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: The Supreme Personality of Godhead having spoken to the two demigods in this way, they circumambulated the Lord, who was bound to the wooden mortar, and offered obeisances to Him. After taking the permission of Lord Kṛṣṇa, they returned to their respective homes.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Tenth Canto, Tenth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "Deliverance of the //Yamala-arjuna// Trees."
