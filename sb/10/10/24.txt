====== SB 10.10.24 ======

===== Text =====

> ṛṣer bhāgavata-mukhyasya
> satyaṁ kartuṁ vaco hariḥ
> jagāma śanakais tatra
> yatrāstāṁ yamalārjunau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣeḥ]]---of the great sage and saintly person Nārada; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhyasya]]---of the topmost of all devotees; [[synonyms:s:satyam]]---truthful; [[synonyms:k:kartum]]---to prove; [[synonyms:v:vacaḥ]]---his words; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jagāma]]---went there; [[synonyms:ś:śanakaiḥ]]---very slowly; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---to the spot where; [[synonyms:ā:āstām]]---there were; [[synonyms:y:yamala]]-[[synonyms:a:arjunau]]---the twin //arjuna// trees.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Kṛṣṇa, to fulfill the truthfulness of the words of the greatest devotee, Nārada, slowly went to that spot where the twin arjuna trees were standing.**
