~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.5.32~~

====== SB 10.5.32 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> iti nandādayo gopāḥ
> proktās te śauriṇā yayuḥ
> anobhir anaḍud-yuktais
> tam anujñāpya gokulam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---Nanda Mahārāja and his companions; [[synonyms:g:gopāḥ]]---the cowherd men; [[synonyms:p:proktāḥ]]---being advised; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they; [[synonyms:ś:śauriṇā]]---by Vasudeva; [[synonyms:y:yayuḥ]]---started from that place; [[synonyms:a:anobhiḥ]]---by the bullock carts; [[synonyms:a:anaḍut]]-[[synonyms:y:yuktaiḥ]]---yoked with oxen; [[synonyms:t:tam]] [[synonyms:a:anujñāpya]]---taking permission from Vasudeva; [[synonyms:g:gokulam]]---for Gokula.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After Vasudeva advised Nanda Mahārāja in this way, Nanda Mahārāja and his associates, the cowherd men, took permission from Vasudeva, yoked their bulls to the bullock carts, and started riding for Gokula.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports to the Tenth Canto, Fifth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "The Meeting of Nanda Mahārāja and Vasudeva."
