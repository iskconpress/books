====== SB 10.5.3 ======

===== Text =====

> dhenūnāṁ niyute prādād
> viprebhyaḥ samalaṅkṛte
> tilādrīn sapta ratnaugha-
> śātakaumbhāmbarāvṛtān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhenūnām]]---of milk-giving cows; [[synonyms:n:niyute]]---two million; [[synonyms:p:prādāt]]---gave in charity; [[synonyms:v:viprebhyaḥ]]---unto the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:s:samalaṅkṛte]]---completely decorated; [[synonyms:t:tila]]-[[synonyms:a:adrīn]]---hills of grain; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]---seven; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:o:ogha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāta]]-[[synonyms:k:kaumbha]]-[[synonyms:a:ambara]]-[[synonyms:ā:āvṛtān]]---covered with jewels and cloth embroidered with gold.

===== Translation =====

**Nanda Mahārāja gave two million cows, completely decorated with cloth and jewels, in charity to the brāhmaṇas. He also gave them seven hills of grain, covered with jewels and with cloth decorated with golden embroidery.**
