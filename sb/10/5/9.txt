====== SB 10.5.9 ======

===== Text =====

> gopyaś cākarṇya muditā
> yaśodāyāḥ sutodbhavam
> ātmānaṁ bhūṣayāṁ cakrur
> vastrākalpāñjanādibhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopyaḥ]]---the feminine community, the wives of the cowherd men; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṇya]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:m:muditāḥ]]---became very glad; [[synonyms:y:yaśodāyāḥ]]---of mother Yaśodā; [[synonyms:s:suta]]-[[synonyms:u:udbhavam]]---the birth of a male child; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣayām]] [[synonyms:c:cakruḥ]]---dressed very nicely to attend the festival; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākalpa]]-[[synonyms:a:añjana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---with proper dress, ornaments, black ointment, and so on.

===== Translation =====

**The gopī wives of the cowherd men were very pleased to hear that mother Yaśodā had given birth to a son, and they began to decorate themselves very nicely with proper dresses, ornaments, black ointment for the eyes, and so on.**
