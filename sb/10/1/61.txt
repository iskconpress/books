====== SB 10.1.61 ======

===== Text =====

> tatheti sutam ādāya
> yayāv ānakadundubhiḥ
> nābhyanandata tad-vākyam
> asato 'vijitātmanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]]---very well; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sutam]] [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---taking back his child; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---left that place; [[synonyms:ā:ānakadundubhiḥ]]---Vasudeva; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:abhyanandata]]---did not very much value; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:v:vākyam]]---the words (of Kaṁsa); [[synonyms:a:asataḥ]]---who was without character; [[synonyms:a:avijita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---and without self-control.

===== Translation =====

**Vasudeva agreed and took his child back home, but because Kaṁsa had no character and no self-control, Vasudeva knew that he could not rely on Kaṁsa's word.**
