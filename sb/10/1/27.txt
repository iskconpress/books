====== SB 10.1.27 ======

===== Text =====

> śūraseno yadupatir
> mathurām āvasan purīm
> māthurāñ chūrasenāṁś ca
> viṣayān bubhuje purā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śūrasenaḥ]]---King Śūrasena; [[synonyms:y:yadu]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the chief of the Yadu dynasty; [[synonyms:m:mathurām]]---at the place known as Mathurā; [[synonyms:ā:āvasan]]---went to live; [[synonyms:p:purīm]]---in that city; [[synonyms:m:māthurān]]---at the place known as the Māthura district; [[synonyms:ś:śūrasenān]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and the place known as Śūrasena; [[synonyms:v:viṣayān]]---such kingdoms; [[synonyms:b:bubhuje]]---enjoyed; [[synonyms:p:purā]]---formerly.

===== Translation =====

**Formerly, Śūrasena, the chief of the Yadu dynasty, had gone to live in the city of Mathurā. There he enjoyed the places known as Māthura and Śūrasena.**
