~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.1.26~~

====== SB 10.1.26 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> ity ādiśyāmara-gaṇān
> prajāpati-patir vibhuḥ
> āśvāsya ca mahīṁ gīrbhiḥ
> sva-dhāma paramaṁ yayau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:ādiśya]]---after informing; [[synonyms:a:amara]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇān]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:p:prajāpati]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---Lord Brahmā, the master of the Prajāpatis; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---all-powerful; [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsya]]---after pacifying; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:m:mahīm]]---mother earth; [[synonyms:g:gīrbhiḥ]]---by sweet words; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---his own planet, known as Brahmaloka; [[synonyms:p:paramam]]---the best (within the universe); [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---returned.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: After thus advising the demigods and pacifying mother earth, the very powerful Lord Brahmā, who is the master of all other Prajāpatis and is therefore known as Prajāpati-pati, returned to his own abode, Brahmaloka.**
