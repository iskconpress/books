~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.1.29~~

====== SB 10.1.29 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyāṁ tu karhicic chaurir
> vasudevaḥ kṛtodvahaḥ
> devakyā sūryayā sārdhaṁ
> prayāṇe ratham āruhat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasyām]]---in that place known as Mathurā; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:k:karhicit]]---some time ago; [[synonyms:ś:śauriḥ]]---the demigod, descendant of Śūra; [[synonyms:v:vasudevaḥ]]---who appeared as Vasudeva; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:u:udvahaḥ]]---after being married; [[synonyms:d:devakyā]]---Devakī; [[synonyms:s:sūryayā]]---his newly married wife; [[synonyms:s:sārdham]]---along with; [[synonyms:p:prayāṇe]]---for returning home; [[synonyms:r:ratham]]---the chariot; [[synonyms:ā:āruhat]]---mounted.

===== Translation =====

**Some time ago, Vasudeva, who belonged to the demigod family [or to the Śūra dynasty], married Devakī. After the marriage, he mounted his chariot to return home with his newly married wife.**
