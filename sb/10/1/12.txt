====== SB 10.1.12 ======

===== Text =====

> etad anyac ca sarvaṁ me
> mune kṛṣṇa-viceṣṭitam
> vaktum arhasi sarvajña
> śraddadhānāya vistṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etat]]---all these details; [[synonyms:a:anyat]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and others also; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---everything; [[synonyms:m:me]]---unto me; [[synonyms:m:mune]]---O great sage; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viceṣṭitam]]---the activities of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vaktum]]---to describe; [[synonyms:a:arhasi]]---you are able; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---because you know everything; [[synonyms:ś:śraddadhānāya]]---because I am not envious but have all faith in Him; [[synonyms:v:vistṛtam]]---in full detail.

===== Translation =====

**O great sage, who know everything about Kṛṣṇa, please describe in detail all the activities of which I have inquired and also those of which I have not, for I have full faith and am very eager to hear of them.**
