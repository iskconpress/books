~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.6.24~~

====== SB 10.6.24 ======

===== Text =====

> indriyāṇi hṛṣīkeśaḥ
> prāṇān nārāyaṇo 'vatu
> śvetadvīpa-patiś cittaṁ
> mano yogeśvaro 'vatu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:indriyāṇi]]---all the senses; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣīkeśaḥ]]---Lord Hṛṣīkeśa, the proprietor of all the senses; [[synonyms:p:prāṇān]]---all kinds of life air; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇaḥ]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatu]]---may He give protection; [[synonyms:ś:śvetadvīpa]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the master of Śvetadvīpa, Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:c:cittam]]---the core of the heart; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]---the mind; [[synonyms:y:yogeśvaraḥ]]---Lord Yogeśvara; [[synonyms:a:avatu]]---may He give protection.

===== Translation =====

**May Hṛṣīkeśa protect Your senses, and Nārāyaṇa Your life air. May the master of Śvetadvīpa protect the core of Your heart, and may Lord Yogeśvara protect Your mind.**
