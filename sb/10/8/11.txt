====== SB 10.8.11 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> evaṁ samprārthito vipraḥ
> sva-cikīrṣitam eva tat
> cakāra nāma-karaṇaṁ
> gūḍho rahasi bālayoḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:samprārthitaḥ]]---being eagerly requested; [[synonyms:v:vipraḥ]]---the //brāhmaṇa// Gargamuni; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:c:cikīrṣitam]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---which he already desired to do and for which he had gone there; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:c:cakāra]]---performed; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:k:karaṇam]]---the name-giving ceremony; [[synonyms:g:gūḍhaḥ]]---confidentially; [[synonyms:r:rahasi]]---in a secluded place; [[synonyms:b:bālayoḥ]]---of the two boys (Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma).

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: Having thus been especially requested by Nanda Mahārāja to do that which he already desired to do, Gargamuni performed the name-giving ceremony for Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma in a solitary place.**
