~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.8.52~~

====== SB 10.8.52 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇo brahmaṇa ādeśaṁ
> satyaṁ kartuṁ vraje vibhuḥ
> saha-rāmo vasaṁś cakre
> teṣāṁ prītiṁ sva-līlayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇaḥ]]---the Supreme Personality, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:brahmaṇaḥ]]---of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ā:ādeśam]]---the order; [[synonyms:s:satyam]]---truthful; [[synonyms:k:kartum]]---to make; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vrajabhūmi, Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the supreme powerful; [[synonyms:s:saha]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmaḥ]]---along with Balarāma; [[synonyms:v:vasan]]---residing; [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---increased; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of all the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prītim]]---the pleasure; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:l:līlayā]]---by His transcendental pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the Supreme Personality, Kṛṣṇa, along with Balarāma, lived in Vrajabhūmi, Vṛndāvana, just to substantiate the benediction of Brahmā. By exhibiting different pastimes in His childhood, He increased the transcendental pleasure of Nanda and the other inhabitants of Vṛndāvana.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Tenth Canto, Eighth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled, "Lord Kṛṣṇa Shows the Universal Form Within His Mouth."
