====== SB 10.8.3 ======

===== Text =====

> sūpaviṣṭaṁ kṛtātithyaṁ
> girā sūnṛtayā munim
> nandayitvābravīd brahman
> pūrṇasya karavāma kim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:u:upaviṣṭam]]---when Gargamuni was seated very comfortably; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātithyam]]---and he had been properly received as a guest; [[synonyms:g:girā]]---by words; [[synonyms:s:sūnṛtayā]]---very sweet; [[synonyms:m:munim]]---Gargamuni; [[synonyms:n:nandayitvā]]---pleasing him in this way; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---said; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇasya]]---of one who is full in everything; [[synonyms:k:karavāma]] [[synonyms:k:kim]]---what can I do for you (kindly order me).

===== Translation =====

**When Gargamuni had been properly received as a guest and was very comfortably seated, Nanda Mahārāja submitted with gentle and submissive words: Dear sir, because you are a devotee, you are full in everything. Yet my duty is to serve you. Kindly order me. What can I do for you?**
