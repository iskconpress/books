====== SB 3.32.31 ======

> इत्येतत्कथितं गुर्वि ज्ञानं तद्ब्रह्मदर्शनम् ।
> येनानुबुद्ध्यते तत्त्वं प्रकृतेः पुरुषस्य च ॥३१॥

===== Text =====

> ity etat kathitaṁ gurvi
> jñānaṁ tad brahma-darśanam
> yenānubuddhyate tattvaṁ
> prakṛteḥ puruṣasya ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kathitam]]---described; [[synonyms:g:gurvi]]---O respectful mother; [[synonyms:j:jñānam]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:d:darśanam]]---revealing; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:a:anubuddhyate]]---is understood; [[synonyms:t:tattvam]]---the truth; [[synonyms:p:prakṛteḥ]]---of matter; [[synonyms:p:puruṣasya]]---of spirit; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**My dear respectful mother, I have already described the path of understanding the Absolute Truth, by which one can come to understand the real truth of matter and spirit and their relationship.**
