====== SB 3.32.17 ======

> रजसा कुण्ठमनसः कामात्मानोऽजितेन्द्रियाः ।
> पित्न्यजन्त्यनुदिनं गृहेष्वभिरताशयाः ॥१७॥

===== Text =====

> rajasā kuṇṭha-manasaḥ
> kāmātmāno 'jitendriyāḥ
> pitṝn yajanty anudinaṁ
> gṛheṣv abhiratāśayāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rajasā]]---by the mode of passion; [[synonyms:k:kuṇṭha]]---full of anxieties; [[synonyms:m:manasaḥ]]---their minds; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmānaḥ]]---aspiring for sense gratification; [[synonyms:a:ajita]]---uncontrolled; [[synonyms:i:indriyāḥ]]---their senses; [[synonyms:p:pitṝn]]---the forefathers; [[synonyms:y:yajanti]]---they worship; [[synonyms:a:anudinam]]---every day; [[synonyms:g:gṛheṣu]]---in home life; [[synonyms:a:abhirata]]---engaged; [[synonyms:ā:āśayāḥ]]---their minds.

===== Translation =====

**Such persons, impelled by the mode of passion, are full of anxieties and always aspire for sense gratification due to uncontrolled senses. They worship the forefathers and are busy day and night improving the economic condition of their family, social or national life.**
