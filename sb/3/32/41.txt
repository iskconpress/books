====== SB 3.32.41 ======

> श्रद्दधानाय भक्ताय विनीतायानसूयवे ।
> भूतेषु कृतमैत्राय शुश्रूषाभिरताय च ॥४१॥

===== Text =====

> śraddadhānāya bhaktāya
> vinītāyānasūyave
> bhūteṣu kṛta-maitrāya
> śuśrūṣābhiratāya ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddadhānāya]]---faithful; [[synonyms:b:bhaktāya]]---to the devotee; [[synonyms:v:vinītāya]]---respectful; [[synonyms:a:anasūyave]]---nonenvious; [[synonyms:b:bhūteṣu]]---to all living entities; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:m:maitrāya]]---friendly; [[synonyms:ś:śuśrūṣā]]---faithful service; [[synonyms:a:abhiratāya]]---eager to render; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**Instruction should be given to the faithful devotee who is respectful to the spiritual master, nonenvious, friendly to all kinds of living entities and eager to render service with faith and sincerity.**
