====== SB 3.17.29 ======

> स एवमुत्सिक्तमदेन विद्विषा दृढं प्रलब्धो भगवानपां पतिः ।
> रोषं समुत्थं शमयन्स्वया धिया व्यवोचदङ्गोपशमं गता वयम् ॥२९॥

===== Text =====

> sa evam utsikta-madena vidviṣā
> dṛḍhaṁ pralabdho bhagavān apāṁ patiḥ
> roṣaṁ samutthaṁ śamayan svayā dhiyā
> vyavocad aṅgopaśamaṁ gatā vayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Varuṇa; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:u:utsikta]]---puffed up; [[synonyms:m:madena]]---with vanity; [[synonyms:v:vidviṣā]]---by the enemy; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍham]]---deeply; [[synonyms:p:pralabdhaḥ]]---mocked; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---worshipful; [[synonyms:a:apām]]---of the waters; [[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the lord; [[synonyms:r:roṣam]]---anger; [[synonyms:s:samuttham]]---sprung up; [[synonyms:ś:śamayan]]---controlling; [[synonyms:s:svayā]] [[synonyms:d:dhiyā]]---by his reason; [[synonyms:v:vyavocat]]---he replied; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---O dear one; [[synonyms:u:upaśamam]]---desisting from warfare; [[synonyms:g:gatāḥ]]---gone; [[synonyms:v:vayam]]---we.

===== Translation =====

**Thus mocked by an enemy whose vanity knew no bounds, the worshipful lord of the waters waxed angry, but by dint of his reason he managed to curb the anger that had sprung up in him, and he replied: O dear one, we have now desisted from warfare, having grown too old for combat.**

===== Purport =====

As we see, warmongering materialists always create fighting without reason.
