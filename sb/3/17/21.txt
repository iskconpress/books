====== SB 3.17.21 ======

> तं वीक्ष्य दुःसहजवं रणत्काञ्चननूपुरम् ।
> वैजयन्त्या स्रजा जुष्टमंसन्यस्तमहागदम् ॥२१॥

===== Text =====

> taṁ vīkṣya duḥsaha-javaṁ
> raṇat-kāñcana-nūpuram
> vaijayantyā srajā juṣṭam
> aṁsa-nyasta-mahā-gadam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---having seen; [[synonyms:d:duḥsaha]]---difficult to control; [[synonyms:j:javam]]---temper; [[synonyms:r:raṇat]]---tinkling; [[synonyms:k:kāñcana]]---gold; [[synonyms:n:nūpuram]]---anklets; [[synonyms:v:vaijayantyā]] [[synonyms:s:srajā]]---with a //vaijayantī// garland; [[synonyms:j:juṣṭam]]---adorned; [[synonyms:a:aṁsa]]---on his shoulder; [[synonyms:n:nyasta]]---rested; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:g:gadam]]---a huge mace.

===== Translation =====

**Hiraṇyākṣa's temper was difficult to control. He had anklets of gold tinkling about his feet, he was adorned with a gigantic garland, and he rested his huge mace on one of his shoulders.**
