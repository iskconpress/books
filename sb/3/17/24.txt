====== SB 3.17.24 ======

> ततो निवृत्तः क्रीडिष्यन्गम्भीरं भीमनिस्वनम् ।
> विजगाहे महासत्त्वो वार्धिं मत्त इव द्विपः ॥२४॥

===== Text =====

> tato nivṛttaḥ krīḍiṣyan
> gambhīraṁ bhīma-nisvanam
> vijagāhe mahā-sattvo
> vārdhiṁ matta iva dvipaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---then; [[synonyms:n:nivṛttaḥ]]---returned; [[synonyms:k:krīḍiṣyan]]---for the sake of sport; [[synonyms:g:gambhīram]]---deep; [[synonyms:b:bhīma]]-[[synonyms:n:nisvanam]]---making a terrible sound; [[synonyms:v:vijagāhe]]---dived; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sattvaḥ]]---the mighty being; [[synonyms:v:vārdhim]]---in the ocean; [[synonyms:m:mattaḥ]]---in wrath; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:d:dvipaḥ]]---an elephant.

===== Translation =====

**After returning from the heavenly kingdom, the mighty demon, who was like an elephant in wrath, for the sake of sport dived into the deep ocean, which was roaring terribly.**
