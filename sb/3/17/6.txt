====== SB 3.17.6 ======

> उद्धसत्तडिदम्भोद घटया नष्टभागणे ।
> व्योम्नि प्रविष्टतमसा न स्म व्यादृश्यते पदम् ॥६॥

===== Text =====

> uddhasat-taḍid-ambhoda-
> ghaṭayā naṣṭa-bhāgaṇe
> vyomni praviṣṭa-tamasā
> na sma vyādṛśyate padam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uddhasat]]---laughing loudly; [[synonyms:t:taḍit]]---lightning; [[synonyms:a:ambhoda]]---of clouds; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭayā]]---by masses; [[synonyms:n:naṣṭa]]---lost; [[synonyms:b:bhā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the luminaries; [[synonyms:v:vyomni]]---in the sky; [[synonyms:p:praviṣṭa]]---enveloped; [[synonyms:t:tamasā]]---by darkness; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:s:sma]] [[synonyms:v:vyādṛśyate]]---could be seen; [[synonyms:p:padam]]---any place.

===== Translation =====

**The luminaries in the heavens were screened by masses of clouds, in which lightning sometimes flashed as though laughing. Darkness reigned everywhere, and nothing could be seen.**
