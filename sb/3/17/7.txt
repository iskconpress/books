====== SB 3.17.7 ======

> चुक्रोश विमना वार्धिरुदूर्मिः क्षुभितोदरः ।
> सोदपानाश्च सरितश्चुक्षुभुः शुष्कपङ्कजाः ॥७॥

===== Text =====

> cukrośa vimanā vārdhir
> udūrmiḥ kṣubhitodaraḥ
> sodapānāś ca saritaś
> cukṣubhuḥ śuṣka-paṅkajāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cukrośa]]---wailed aloud; [[synonyms:v:vimanāḥ]]---stricken with sorrow; [[synonyms:v:vārdhiḥ]]---the ocean; [[synonyms:u:udūrmiḥ]]---high waves; [[synonyms:k:kṣubhita]]---agitated; [[synonyms:u:udaraḥ]]---the creatures inside; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:u:udapānāḥ]]---with the drinking water of the lakes and the wells; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:saritaḥ]]---the rivers; [[synonyms:c:cukṣubhuḥ]]---were agitated; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]---withered; [[synonyms:p:paṅkajāḥ]]---lotus flowers.

===== Translation =====

**The ocean with its high waves wailed aloud as if stricken with sorrow, and there was a commotion among the creatures inhabiting the ocean. The rivers and lakes were also agitated, and lotuses withered.**
