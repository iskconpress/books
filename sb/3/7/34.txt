====== SB 3.7.34 ======

> दानस्य तपसो वापि यच्चेष्टापूर्तयोः फलम् ।
> प्रवासस्थस्य यो धर्मो यश्च पुंस उतापदि ॥३४॥

===== Text =====

> dānasya tapaso vāpi
> yac ceṣṭā-pūrtayoḥ phalam
> pravāsa-sthasya yo dharmo
> yaś ca puṁsa utāpadi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dānasya]]---of charity; [[synonyms:t:tapasaḥ]]---of penance; [[synonyms:v:vāpi]]---lake; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---that which; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭā]]---endeavor; [[synonyms:p:pūrtayoḥ]]---of reservoirs of water; [[synonyms:p:phalam]]---fruitive result; [[synonyms:p:pravāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthasya]]---one who is away from home; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---that which; [[synonyms:d:dharmaḥ]]---duty; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and which; [[synonyms:p:puṁsaḥ]]---of man; [[synonyms:u:uta]]---described; [[synonyms:ā:āpadi]]---in danger.

===== Translation =====

**Please also describe the fruitive results of charity and penance and of digging reservoirs of water. Please describe the situation of persons who are away from home and also the duty of a man in an awkward position.**

===== Purport =====

The digging of reservoirs of water for public use is a great work of charity, and retiring from family life after fifty years of age is a great act of penance performed by the sober human being.
