====== SB 3.7.32 ======

> धर्मार्थकाममोक्षाणां निमित्तान्यविरोधतः ।
> वार्ताया दण्डनीतेश्च श्रुतस्य च विधिं पृथक् ॥३२॥

===== Text =====

> dharmārtha-kāma-mokṣāṇāṁ
> nimittāny avirodhataḥ
> vārtāyā daṇḍa-nīteś ca
> śrutasya ca vidhiṁ pṛthak

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religiosity; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---economic development; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---sense gratification; [[synonyms:m:mokṣāṇām]]---salvation; [[synonyms:n:nimittāni]]---causes; [[synonyms:a:avirodhataḥ]]---without being contradictory; [[synonyms:v:vārtāyāḥ]]---on the principles of the means of livelihood; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:n:nīteḥ]]---of law and order; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ś:śrutasya]]---of the codes of scriptures; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vidhim]]---regulations; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---different.

===== Translation =====

**You may also describe the noncontradictory causes of religiosity, economic development, sense gratification and salvation and also the different means of livelihood and different processes of law and order as mentioned in the revealed scriptures.**
