====== SB 3.7.24 ======

> यत्र पुत्रैश्च पौत्रैश्च नप्तृभिः सह गोत्रजैः ।
> प्रजा विचित्राकृतय आसन्याभिरिदं ततम् ॥२४॥

===== Text =====

> yatra putraiś ca pautraiś ca
> naptṛbhiḥ saha gotrajaiḥ
> prajā vicitrākṛtaya
> āsan yābhir idaṁ tatam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherein; [[synonyms:p:putraiḥ]]---along with sons; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:pautraiḥ]]---along with grandsons; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:n:naptṛbhiḥ]]---with grandsons from daughters; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---along with; [[synonyms:g:gotra]]-[[synonyms:j:jaiḥ]]---of the same family; [[synonyms:p:prajāḥ]]---generations; [[synonyms:v:vicitra]]---of different kinds; [[synonyms:ā:ākṛtayaḥ]]---so done; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---exist; [[synonyms:y:yābhiḥ]]---by whom; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---all these planets; [[synonyms:t:tatam]]---spread.

===== Translation =====

**O my lord, I think that the process manifest in the forms of sons, grandsons and family members has spread all over the universe in different varieties and species.**
