====== SB 3.7.27 ======

> तिर्यङ्मानुषदेवानां सरीसृपपतत्त्रिणाम् ।
> वद नः सर्गसंव्यूहं गार्भस्वेदद्विजोद्भिदाम् ॥२७॥

===== Text =====

> tiryaṅ-mānuṣa-devānāṁ
> sarīsṛpa-patattriṇām
> vada naḥ sarga-saṁvyūhaṁ
> gārbha-sveda-dvijodbhidām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tiryak]]---subhuman; [[synonyms:m:mānuṣa]]---human beings; [[synonyms:d:devānām]]---of the superhuman beings, or demigods; [[synonyms:s:sarīsṛpa]]---reptiles; [[synonyms:p:patattriṇām]]---of the birds; [[synonyms:v:vada]]---kindly describe; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---unto me; [[synonyms:s:sarga]]---generation; [[synonyms:s:saṁvyūham]]---specific divisions; [[synonyms:g:gārbha]]---embryonic; [[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]---twice-born; [[synonyms:u:udbhidām]]---of the planets, etc.

===== Translation =====

**Also please describe the living beings under different classifications: subhumans, humans, those born of the embryo, those born of perspiration, those who are twice-born [birds], and the plants and vegetables. Kindly describe their generations and subdivisions also.**
