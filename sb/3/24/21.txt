====== SB 3.24.21 ======

> गते शतधृतौ क्षत्तः कर्दमस्तेन चोदितः ।
> यथोदितं स्वदुहित्ः प्रादाद्विश्वसृजां ततः ॥२१॥

===== Text =====

> gate śata-dhṛtau kṣattaḥ
> kardamas tena coditaḥ
> yathoditaṁ sva-duhitṝḥ
> prādād viśva-sṛjāṁ tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gate]]---after he departed; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:d:dhṛtau]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:k:kṣattaḥ]]---O Vidura; [[synonyms:k:kardamaḥ]]---Kardama Muni; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---by him; [[synonyms:c:coditaḥ]]---ordered; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:u:uditam]]---as told; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:d:duhitṝḥ]]---his own daughters; [[synonyms:p:prādāt]]---handed over; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:s:sṛjām]]---to the creators of the world's population; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter.

===== Translation =====

**O Vidura, after the departure of Brahmā, Kardama Muni, having been ordered by Brahmā, handed over his nine daughters, as instructed, to the nine great sages who created the population of the world.**
