====== SB 3.24.1 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> निर्वेदवादिनीमेवं मनोर्दुहितरं मुनिः ।
> दयालुः शालिनीमाह शुक्लाभिव्याहृतं स्मरन् ॥१॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> nirveda-vādinīm evaṁ
> manor duhitaraṁ muniḥ
> dayāluḥ śālinīm āha
> śuklābhivyāhṛtaṁ smaran

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]]---the great sage Maitreya; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:n:nirveda]]-[[synonyms:v:vādinīm]]---who was speaking words full of renunciation; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:m:manoḥ]]---of Svāyambhuva Manu; [[synonyms:d:duhitaram]]---to the daughter; [[synonyms:m:muniḥ]]---the sage Kardama; [[synonyms:d:dayāluḥ]]---merciful; [[synonyms:ś:śālinīm]]---who was worthy of praise; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---replied; [[synonyms:ś:śukla]]---by Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:a:abhivyāhṛtam]]---what was said; [[synonyms:s:smaran]]---recalling.

===== Translation =====

**Recalling the words of Lord Viṣṇu, the merciful sage Kardama replied as follows to Svāyambhuva Manu's praiseworthy daughter, Devahūti, who was speaking words full of renunciation.**
