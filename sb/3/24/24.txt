====== SB 3.24.24 ======

> अथर्वणेऽददाच्छान्तिं यया यज्ञो वितन्यते ।
> विप्रर्षभान्कृतोद्वाहान्सदारान्समलालयत् ॥२४॥

===== Text =====

> atharvaṇe 'dadāc chāntiṁ
> yayā yajño vitanyate
> viprarṣabhān kṛtodvāhān
> sadārān samalālayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atharvaṇe]]---to Atharvā; [[synonyms:a:adadāt]]---he gave away; [[synonyms:ś:śāntim]]---Śānti; [[synonyms:y:yayā]]---by whom; [[synonyms:y:yajñaḥ]]---sacrifice; [[synonyms:v:vitanyate]]---is performed; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabhān]]---the foremost //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:u:udvāhān]]---married; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:dārān]]---with their wives; [[synonyms:s:samalālayat]]---maintained them.

===== Translation =====

**He delivered Śānti to Atharvā. Because of Śānti, sacrificial ceremonies are well performed. Thus he got the foremost brāhmaṇas married, and he maintained them along with their wives.**
