====== SB 3.13.19 ======

> तस्याभिपश्यतः खस्थः क्षणेन किल भारत ।
> गजमात्रः प्रववृधे तदद्भुतमभून्महत् ॥१९॥

===== Text =====

> tasyābhipaśyataḥ kha-sthaḥ
> kṣaṇena kila bhārata
> gaja-mātraḥ pravavṛdhe
> tad adbhutam abhūn mahat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:a:abhipaśyataḥ]]---while thus observing; [[synonyms:k:kha]]-[[synonyms:s:sthaḥ]]---situated in the sky; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇena]]---suddenly; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---verily; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]---O descendant of Bharata; [[synonyms:g:gaja]]-[[synonyms:m:mātraḥ]]---just like an elephant; [[synonyms:p:pravavṛdhe]]---thoroughly expanded; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:a:adbhutam]]---extraordinary; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---transformed; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]---into a gigantic body.

===== Translation =====

**O descendant of Bharata, while Brahmā was observing Him, that boar became situated in the sky in a wonderful manifestation as gigantic as a great elephant.**
