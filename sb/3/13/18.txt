====== SB 3.13.18 ======

> इत्यभिध्यायतो नासा विवरात्सहसानघ ।
> वराहतोको निरगादङ्गुष्ठपरिमाणकः ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> ity abhidhyāyato nāsā-
> vivarāt sahasānagha
> varāha-toko niragād
> aṅguṣṭha-parimāṇakaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:abhidhyāyataḥ]]---while thinking; [[synonyms:n:nāsā]]-[[synonyms:v:vivarāt]]---from the nostrils; [[synonyms:s:sahasā]]---all of a sudden; [[synonyms:a:anagha]]---O sinless one; [[synonyms:v:varāha]]-[[synonyms:t:tokaḥ]]---a minute form of Varāha (a boar); [[synonyms:n:niragāt]]---came out; [[synonyms:a:aṅguṣṭha]]---the upper portion of the thumb; [[synonyms:p:parimāṇakaḥ]]---of the measurement.

===== Translation =====

**O sinless Vidura, all of a sudden, while Brahmā was engaged in thinking, a small form of a boar came out of his nostril. The measurement of the creature was not more than the upper portion of a thumb.**
