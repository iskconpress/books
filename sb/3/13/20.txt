====== SB 3.13.20 ======

> मरीचिप्रमुखैर्विप्रैः कुमारैर्मनुना सह ।
> दृष्ट्वा तत्सौकरं रूपं तर्कयामास चित्रधा ॥२०॥

===== Text =====

> marīci-pramukhair vipraiḥ
> kumārair manunā saha
> dṛṣṭvā tat saukaraṁ rūpaṁ
> tarkayām āsa citradhā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:marīci]]---the great sage Marīci; [[synonyms:p:pramukhaiḥ]]---headed by; [[synonyms:v:vipraiḥ]]---all //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:k:kumāraiḥ]]---with the four Kumāras; [[synonyms:m:manunā]]---and with Manu; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:s:saukaram]]---appearance like a boar; [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---form; [[synonyms:t:tarkayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---argued among themselves; [[synonyms:c:citradhā]]---in various ways.

===== Translation =====

**Struck with wonder at observing the wonderful boarlike form in the sky, Brahmā, with great brāhmaṇas like Marīci, as well as the Kumāras and Manu, began to argue in various ways.**
