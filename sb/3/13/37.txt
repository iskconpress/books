====== SB 3.13.37 ======

> दीक्षानुजन्मोपसदः शिरोधरं त्वं प्रायणीयोदयनीयदंष्ट्रः ।
> जिह्वा प्रवर्ग्यस्तव शीर्षकं क्रतोः सत्यावसथ्यं चितयोऽसवो हि ते ॥३७॥

===== Text =====

> dīkṣānujanmopasadaḥ śirodharaṁ
> tvaṁ prāyaṇīyodayanīya-daṁṣṭraḥ
> jihvā pravargyas tava śīrṣakaṁ kratoḥ
> satyāvasathyaṁ citayo 'savo hi te

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dīkṣā]]---initiation; [[synonyms:a:anujanma]]---spiritual birth, or repeated incarnations; [[synonyms:u:upasadaḥ]]---three kinds of desires (relationship, activities and ultimate goal); [[synonyms:ś:śiraḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dharam]]---the neck; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---You; [[synonyms:p:prāyaṇīya]]---after the result of initiation; [[synonyms:u:udayanīya]]---the last rites of desires; [[synonyms:d:daṁṣṭraḥ]]---the tusks; [[synonyms:j:jihvā]]---the tongue; [[synonyms:p:pravargyaḥ]]---prior activities; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---Your; [[synonyms:ś:śīrṣakam]]---head; [[synonyms:k:kratoḥ]]---of the sacrifice; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---fire without sacrifice; [[synonyms:ā:āvasathyam]]---fire of worship; [[synonyms:c:citayaḥ]]---aggregate of all desires; [[synonyms:a:asavaḥ]]---life breath; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:t:te]]---Your.

===== Translation =====

**Moreover, O Lord, the repetition of Your appearance is the desire for all kinds of initiation. Your neck is the place for three desires, and Your tusks are the result of initiation and the end of all desires. Your tongue is the prior activities of initiation, Your head is the fire without sacrifice as well as the fire of worship, and Your living forces are the aggregate of all desires.**
