====== SB 3.33.9 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> ईडितो भगवानेवं कपिलाख्यः परः पुमान् ।
> वाचाविक्लवयेत्याह मातरं मातृवत्सलः ॥९॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> īḍito bhagavān evaṁ
> kapilākhyaḥ paraḥ pumān
> vācāviklavayety āha
> mātaraṁ mātṛ-vatsalaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Maitreya said; [[synonyms:ī:īḍitaḥ]]---praised; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kapila]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyaḥ]]---named Kapila; [[synonyms:p:paraḥ]]---supreme; [[synonyms:p:pumān]]---person; [[synonyms:v:vācā]]---with words; [[synonyms:a:aviklavayā]]---grave; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---replied; [[synonyms:m:mātaram]]---to His mother; [[synonyms:m:mātṛ]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsalaḥ]]---very affectionate to His mother.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the Supreme Personality of Godhead Kapila, satisfied by the words of His mother, towards whom He was very affectionate, replied with gravity.**

===== Purport =====

Since the Lord is all-perfect, His exhibition of affection for His mother was also complete. After hearing the words of His mother, He most respectfully, with due gravity and good manners, replied.
