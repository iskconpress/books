====== SB 3.33.33 ======

> कपिलोऽपि महायोगी भगवान्पितुराश्रमात् ।
> मातरं समनुज्ञाप्य प्रागुदीचीं दिशं ययौ ॥३३॥

===== Text =====

> kapilo 'pi mahā-yogī
> bhagavān pitur āśramāt
> mātaraṁ samanujñāpya
> prāg-udīcīṁ diśaṁ yayau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kapilaḥ]]---Lord Kapila; [[synonyms:a:api]]---surely; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogī]]---the great sage; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:pituḥ]]---of His father; [[synonyms:ā:āśramāt]]---from the hermitage; [[synonyms:m:mātaram]]---from His mother; [[synonyms:s:samanujñāpya]]---having asked permission; [[synonyms:p:prāk]]-[[synonyms:u:udīcīm]]---northeast; [[synonyms:d:diśam]]---direction; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---He went.

===== Translation =====

**My dear Vidura, the great sage Kapila, the Personality of Godhead, left His father's hermitage with the permission of His mother and went towards the northeast.**
