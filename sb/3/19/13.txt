====== SB 3.19.13 ======

> जग्राह त्रिशिखं शूलं ज्वलज्ज्वलनलोलुपम् ।
> यज्ञाय धृतरूपाय विप्रायाभिचरन्यथा ॥१३॥

===== Text =====

> jagrāha tri-śikhaṁ śūlaṁ
> jvalaj-jvalana-lolupam
> yajñāya dhṛta-rūpāya
> viprāyābhicaran yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagrāha]]---took up; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:ś:śikham]]---three-pointed; [[synonyms:ś:śūlam]]---trident; [[synonyms:j:jvalat]]---flaming; [[synonyms:j:jvalana]]---fire; [[synonyms:l:lolupam]]---rapacious; [[synonyms:y:yajñāya]]---at the enjoyer of all sacrifices; [[synonyms:d:dhṛta]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpāya]]---in the form of Varāha; [[synonyms:v:viprāya]]---unto a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:a:abhicaran]]---acting malevolently; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as.

===== Translation =====

**He now took a trident which was as rapacious as a flaming fire and hurled it against the Lord, the enjoyer of all sacrifices, even as one would use penance for a malevolent purpose against a holy brāhmaṇa.**
