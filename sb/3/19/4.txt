====== SB 3.19.4 ======

> स तदा लब्धतीर्थोऽपि न बबाधे निरायुधम् ।
> मानयन्स मृधे धर्मं विष्वक्सेनं प्रकोपयन् ॥४॥

===== Text =====

> sa tadā labdha-tīrtho 'pi
> na babādhe nirāyudham
> mānayan sa mṛdhe dharmaṁ
> viṣvaksenaṁ prakopayan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that Hiraṇyākṣa; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:l:labdha]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrthaḥ]]---having gained an excellent opportunity; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:b:babādhe]]---attacked; [[synonyms:n:nirāyudham]]---having no weapon; [[synonyms:m:mānayan]]---respecting; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Hiraṇyākṣa; [[synonyms:m:mṛdhe]]---in battle; [[synonyms:d:dharmam]]---the code of combat; [[synonyms:v:viṣvaksenam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prakopayan]]---making angry.

===== Translation =====

**Even though the demon had an excellent opportunity to strike his unarmed foe without obstruction, he respected the law of single combat, thereby kindling the fury of the Supreme Lord.**
