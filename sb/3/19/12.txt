====== SB 3.19.12 ======

> स्वपौरुषे प्रतिहते हतमानो महासुरः ।
> नैच्छद्गदां दीयमानां हरिणा विगतप्रभः ॥१२॥

===== Text =====

> sva-pauruṣe pratihate
> hata-māno mahāsuraḥ
> naicchad gadāṁ dīyamānāṁ
> hariṇā vigata-prabhaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:p:pauruṣe]]---his valor; [[synonyms:p:pratihate]]---frustrated; [[synonyms:h:hata]]---destroyed; [[synonyms:m:mānaḥ]]---pride; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:asuraḥ]]---the great demon; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:aicchat]]---desired not (to take); [[synonyms:g:gadām]]---the mace; [[synonyms:d:dīyamānām]]---being offered; [[synonyms:h:hariṇā]]---by Hari; [[synonyms:v:vigata]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhaḥ]]---reduced in splendor.

===== Translation =====

**His valor thus frustrated, the great demon felt humiliated and was put out of countenance. He was reluctant to take back the mace when it was offered by the Personality of Godhead.**
