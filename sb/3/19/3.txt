====== SB 3.19.3 ======

> सा हता तेन गदया विहता भगवत्करात् ।
> विघूर्णितापतद्रेजे तदद्भुतमिवाभवत् ॥३॥

===== Text =====

> sā hatā tena gadayā
> vihatā bhagavat-karāt
> vighūrṇitāpatad reje
> tad adbhutam ivābhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sā]]---that mace; [[synonyms:h:hatā]]---struck; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---by Hiraṇyākṣa; [[synonyms:g:gadayā]]---with his mace; [[synonyms:v:vihatā]]---slipped; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:karāt]]---from the hand; [[synonyms:v:vighūrṇitā]]---whirling; [[synonyms:a:apatat]]---fell down; [[synonyms:r:reje]]---was shining; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:a:adbhutam]]---miraculous; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was.

===== Translation =====

**Struck by the demon's mace, however, the Lord's mace slipped from His hand and looked splendid as it fell down whirling. This was miraculous, for the mace was blazing wonderfully.**
