====== SB 3.19.11 ======

> तां स आपततीं वीक्ष्य भगवान्समवस्थितः ।
> जग्राह लीलया प्राप्तां गरुत्मानिव पन्नगीम् ॥११॥

===== Text =====

> tāṁ sa āpatatīṁ vīkṣya
> bhagavān samavasthitaḥ
> jagrāha līlayā prāptāṁ
> garutmān iva pannagīm

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tām]]---that mace; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:ā:āpatatīm]]---flying toward; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:samavasthitaḥ]]---stood firmly; [[synonyms:j:jagrāha]]---caught; [[synonyms:l:līlayā]]---easily; [[synonyms:p:prāptām]]---entered into His presence; [[synonyms:g:garutmān]]---Garuḍa; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---as; [[synonyms:p:pannagīm]]---a serpent.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord saw the mace flying toward Him, He stood firmly where He was and caught it with the same ease as Garuḍa, the king of birds, would seize a serpent.**
