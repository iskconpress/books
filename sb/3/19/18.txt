====== SB 3.19.18 ======

> प्रववुर्वायवश्चण्डास्तमः पांसवमैरयन् ।
> दिग्भ्यो निपेतुर्ग्रावाणः क्षेपणैः प्रहिता इव ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> pravavur vāyavaś caṇḍās
> tamaḥ pāṁsavam airayan
> digbhyo nipetur grāvāṇaḥ
> kṣepaṇaiḥ prahitā iva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pravavuḥ]]---were blowing; [[synonyms:v:vāyavaḥ]]---winds; [[synonyms:c:caṇḍāḥ]]---fierce; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---darkness; [[synonyms:p:pāṁsavam]]---caused by dust; [[synonyms:a:airayan]]---were spreading; [[synonyms:d:digbhyaḥ]]---from every direction; [[synonyms:n:nipetuḥ]]---came down; [[synonyms:g:grāvāṇaḥ]]---stones; [[synonyms:k:kṣepaṇaiḥ]]---by machine guns; [[synonyms:p:prahitāḥ]]---thrown; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---as if.

===== Translation =====

**Fierce winds began to blow from all directions, spreading darkness occasioned by dust and hail storms; stones came in volleys from every corner, as if thrown by machine guns.**
