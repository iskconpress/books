====== SB 3.11.39 ======

> कालोऽयं परमाण्वादिर्द्विपरार्धान्त ईश्वरः ।
> नैवेशितुं प्रभुर्भूम्न ईश्वरो धाममानिनाम् ॥३९॥

===== Text =====

> kālo 'yaṁ paramāṇv-ādir
> dvi-parārdhānta īśvaraḥ
> naiveśituṁ prabhur bhūmna
> īśvaro dhāma-māninām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kālaḥ]]---the eternal time; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:a:aṇu]]---atom; [[synonyms:ā:ādiḥ]]---beginning from; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:p:parārdha]]---two superdurations of time; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---to the end; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---controller; [[synonyms:n:na]]---never; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ī:īśitum]]---to control; [[synonyms:p:prabhuḥ]]---capable; [[synonyms:b:bhūmnaḥ]]---of the Supreme; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---controller; [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]-[[synonyms:m:māninām]]---of those who are body conscious.

===== Translation =====

**Eternal time is certainly the controller of different dimensions, from that of the atom up to the superdivisions of the duration of Brahmā's life; but, nevertheless, it is controlled by the Supreme. Time can control only those who are body conscious, even up to the Satyaloka or the other higher planets of the universe.**
