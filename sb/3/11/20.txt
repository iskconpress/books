====== SB 3.11.20 ======

> सन्ध्यासन्ध्यांशयोरन्तर्यः कालः शतसङ्ख्ययोः ।
> तमेवाहुर्युगं तज्ज्ञा यत्र धर्मो विधीयते ॥२०॥

===== Text =====

> sandhyā-sandhyāṁśayor antar
> yaḥ kālaḥ śata-saṅkhyayoḥ
> tam evāhur yugaṁ taj-jñā
> yatra dharmo vidhīyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]---transitional period before; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśayoḥ]]---and transitional period after; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---within; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---that which; [[synonyms:k:kālaḥ]]---duration of time; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkhyayoḥ]]---hundreds of years; [[synonyms:t:tam]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---that period; [[synonyms:ā:āhuḥ]]---they call; [[synonyms:y:yugam]]---millennium; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāḥ]]---the expert astronomers; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherein; [[synonyms:d:dharmaḥ]]---religion; [[synonyms:v:vidhīyate]]---is performed.

===== Translation =====

**The transitional periods before and after every millennium, which are a few hundred years as aforementioned, are known as yuga-sandhyās, or the conjunctions of two millenniums, according to the expert astronomers. In those periods all kinds of religious activities are performed.**
