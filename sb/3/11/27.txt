====== SB 3.11.27 ======

> मन्वन्तरेषु भगवान्बिभ्रत्सत्त्वं स्वमूर्तिभिः ।
> मन्वादिभिरिदं विश्वमवत्युदितपौरुषः ॥२७॥

===== Text =====

> manvantareṣu bhagavān
> bibhrat sattvaṁ sva-mūrtibhiḥ
> manv-ādibhir idaṁ viśvam
> avaty udita-pauruṣaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:a:antareṣu]]---in each change of Manu; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bibhrat]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:s:sattvam]]---His internal potency; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrtibhiḥ]]---by His different incarnations; [[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---as Manus; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:v:viśvam]]---the universe; [[synonyms:a:avati]]---maintains; [[synonyms:u:udita]]---discovering; [[synonyms:p:pauruṣaḥ]]---divine potencies.

===== Translation =====

**In each and every change of Manu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead appears by manifesting His internal potency in different incarnations, as Manu and others. Thus He maintains the universe by discovered power.**
