====== SB 3.16.1 ======

> ब्रह्मोवाच
> इति तद्गृणतां तेषां मुनीनां योगधर्मिणाम् ।
> प्रतिनन्द्य जगादेदं विकुण्ठनिलयो विभुः ॥१॥

===== Text =====

> brahmovāca
> iti tad gṛṇatāṁ teṣāṁ
> munīnāṁ yoga-dharmiṇām
> pratinandya jagādedaṁ
> vikuṇṭha-nilayo vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Lord Brahmā said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---speech; [[synonyms:g:gṛṇatām]]---praising; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of them; [[synonyms:m:munīnām]]---those four sages; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:d:dharmiṇām]]---engaged in linking with the Supreme; [[synonyms:p:pratinandya]]---after congratulating; [[synonyms:j:jagāda]]---said; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---these words; [[synonyms:v:vikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:n:nilayaḥ]]---whose abode is bereft of anxiety; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Brahmā said: After thus congratulating the sages for their nice words, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, whose abode is in the kingdom of God, spoke as follows.**
