====== SB 3.6.15 ======

> निर्भिन्ने अक्षिणी त्वष्टा लोकपालोऽविशद्विभोः ।
> चक्षुषांशेन रूपाणां प्रतिपत्तिर्यतो भवेत् ॥१५॥

===== Text =====

> nirbhinne akṣiṇī tvaṣṭā
> loka-pālo 'viśad vibhoḥ
> cakṣuṣāṁśena rūpāṇāṁ
> pratipattir yato bhavet

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirbhinne]]---thus being separated; [[synonyms:a:akṣiṇī]]---the eyes; [[synonyms:t:tvaṣṭā]]---the sun; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:p:pālaḥ]]---director of light; [[synonyms:a:aviśat]]---entered; [[synonyms:v:vibhoḥ]]---of the great; [[synonyms:c:cakṣuṣā]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---by the part of the eyesight; [[synonyms:r:rūpāṇām]]---of the forms; [[synonyms:p:pratipattiḥ]]---experience; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---by which; [[synonyms:b:bhavet]]---becomes.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, the two eyes of the gigantic form of the Lord were separately manifested. The sun, the director of light, entered them with the partial representation of eyesight, and thus the living entities can have vision of forms.**
