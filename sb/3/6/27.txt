====== SB 3.6.27 ======

> शीर्ष्णोऽस्य द्यौर्धरा पद्भ्यां खं नाभेरुदपद्यत ।
> गुणानां वृत्तयो येषु प्रतीयन्ते सुरादयः ॥२७॥

===== Text =====

> śīrṣṇo 'sya dyaur dharā padbhyāṁ
> khaṁ nābher udapadyata
> guṇānāṁ vṛttayo yeṣu
> pratīyante surādayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śīrṣṇaḥ]]---head; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of the gigantic form; [[synonyms:d:dyauḥ]]---the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:d:dharā]]---earthly planets; [[synonyms:p:padbhyām]]---on His legs; [[synonyms:k:kham]]---the sky; [[synonyms:n:nābheḥ]]---from the abdomen; [[synonyms:u:udapadyata]]---became manifested; [[synonyms:g:guṇānām]]---of the three modes of nature; [[synonyms:v:vṛttayaḥ]]---reactions; [[synonyms:y:yeṣu]]---in which; [[synonyms:p:pratīyante]]---manifest; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the demigods and others.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, from the head of the gigantic form, the heavenly planets were manifested, and from His legs the earthly planets and from His abdomen the sky separately manifested themselves. Within them the demigods and others also were manifested in terms of the modes of material nature.**
