====== SB 3.6.20 ======

> गुदं पुंसो विनिर्भिन्नं मित्रो लोकेश आविशत् ।
> पायुनांशेन येनासौ विसर्गं प्रतिपद्यते ॥२०॥

===== Text =====

> gudaṁ puṁso vinirbhinnaṁ
> mitro lokeśa āviśat
> pāyunāṁśena yenāsau
> visargaṁ pratipadyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gudam]]---evacuating outlet; [[synonyms:p:puṁsaḥ]]---of the gigantic form; [[synonyms:v:vinirbhinnam]]---being separately manifested; [[synonyms:m:mitraḥ]]---the sun-god; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśaḥ]]---the director named Mitra; [[synonyms:ā:āviśat]]---entered; [[synonyms:p:pāyunā]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---with the partial evacuation process; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---the living entity; [[synonyms:v:visargam]]---evacuation; [[synonyms:p:pratipadyate]]---performs.

===== Translation =====

**The evacuating channel separately became manifest, and the director named Mitra entered into it with partial organs of evacuation. Thus the living entities are able to pass stool and urine.**
