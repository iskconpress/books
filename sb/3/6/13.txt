====== SB 3.6.13 ======

> निर्भिन्नं तालु वरुणो लोकपालोऽविशद्धरेः ।
> जिह्वयांशेन च रसं ययासौ प्रतिपद्यते ॥१३॥

===== Text =====

> nirbhinnaṁ tālu varuṇo
> loka-pālo 'viśad dhareḥ
> jihvayāṁśena ca rasaṁ
> yayāsau pratipadyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirbhinnam]]---separated; [[synonyms:t:tālu]]---palate; [[synonyms:v:varuṇaḥ]]---the deity controlling water; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:p:pālaḥ]]---director of the planets; [[synonyms:a:aviśat]]---entered; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:j:jihvayā]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---with the part of the tongue; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:r:rasam]]---tastes; [[synonyms:y:yayā]]---by which; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---the living entity; [[synonyms:p:pratipadyate]]---expresses.

===== Translation =====

**When the palate of the gigantic form was separately manifested, Varuṇa, the director of water in the planetary systems, entered therein, and thus the living entity has the facility to taste everything with his tongue.**
