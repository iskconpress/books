====== SB 3.6.22 ======

> पादावस्य विनिर्भिन्नौ लोकेशो विष्णुराविशत् ।
> गत्या स्वांशेन पुरुषो यया प्राप्यं प्रपद्यते ॥२२॥

===== Text =====

> pādāv asya vinirbhinnau
> lokeśo viṣṇur āviśat
> gatyā svāṁśena puruṣo
> yayā prāpyaṁ prapadyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pādau]]---the legs; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of the gigantic form; [[synonyms:v:vinirbhinnau]]---being manifested separately; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśaḥ]] [[synonyms:v:viṣṇuḥ]]---the demigod Viṣṇu (not the Personality of Godhead); [[synonyms:ā:āviśat]]---entered; [[synonyms:g:gatyā]]---by the power of movement; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---with his own parts; [[synonyms:p:puruṣaḥ]]---living entity; [[synonyms:y:yayā]]---by which; [[synonyms:p:prāpyam]]---destination; [[synonyms:p:prapadyate]]---reaches.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter the legs of the gigantic form separately became manifest, and the demigod named Viṣṇu [not the Personality of Godhead] entered with partial movement. This helps the living entity move to his destination.**
