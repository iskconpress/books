====== SB 3.6.16 ======

> निर्भिन्नान्यस्य चर्माणि लोकपालोऽनिलोऽविशत् ।
> प्राणेनांशेन संस्पर्शं येनासौ प्रतिपद्यते ॥१६॥

===== Text =====

> nirbhinnāny asya carmāṇi
> loka-pālo 'nilo 'viśat
> prāṇenāṁśena saṁsparśaṁ
> yenāsau pratipadyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirbhinnāni]]---being separated; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of the gigantic form; [[synonyms:c:carmāṇi]]---skin; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:p:pālaḥ]]---the director; [[synonyms:a:anilaḥ]]---air; [[synonyms:a:aviśat]]---entered; [[synonyms:p:prāṇena]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---the part of the breathing; [[synonyms:s:saṁsparśam]]---touch; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---the living entity; [[synonyms:p:pratipadyate]]---can experience.

===== Translation =====

**When there was a manifestation of skin separated from the gigantic form, Anila, the deity directing the wind, entered with partial touch, and thus the living entities can realize tactile knowledge.**
