====== SB 3.6.26 ======

> सत्त्वं चास्य विनिर्भिन्नं महान्धिष्ण्यमुपाविशत् ।
> चित्तेनांशेन येनासौ विज्ञानं प्रतिपद्यते ॥२६॥

===== Text =====

> sattvaṁ cāsya vinirbhinnaṁ
> mahān dhiṣṇyam upāviśat
> cittenāṁśena yenāsau
> vijñānaṁ pratipadyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sattvam]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of the gigantic form; [[synonyms:v:vinirbhinnam]]---being separately manifested; [[synonyms:m:mahān]]---the total energy, //mahat-tattva//; [[synonyms:d:dhiṣṇyam]]---with control; [[synonyms:u:upāviśat]]---entered into; [[synonyms:c:cittena]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---along with His part of consciousness; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---the living entity; [[synonyms:v:vijñānam]]---specific knowledge; [[synonyms:p:pratipadyate]]---cultivates.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, when His consciousness separately manifested itself, the total energy, mahat-tattva, entered with His conscious part. Thus the living entity is able to conceive specific knowledge.**
