====== SB 3.5.35 ======

> अनिलेनान्वितं ज्योतिर्विकुर्वत्परवीक्षितम् ।
> आधत्ताम्भो रसमयं कालमायांशयोगतः ॥३५॥

===== Text =====

> anilenānvitaṁ jyotir
> vikurvat paravīkṣitam
> ādhattāmbho rasa-mayaṁ
> kāla-māyāṁśa-yogataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anilena]]---by the air; [[synonyms:a:anvitam]]---interacted; [[synonyms:j:jyotiḥ]]---electricity; [[synonyms:v:vikurvat]]---being transformed; [[synonyms:p:paravīkṣitam]]---being glanced over by the Supreme; [[synonyms:ā:ādhatta]]---created; [[synonyms:a:ambhaḥ]] [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:m:mayam]]---water with taste; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---of eternal time; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---and external energy; [[synonyms:y:yogataḥ]]---by a mixture.

===== Translation =====

**When electricity was surcharged in the air and was glanced over by the Supreme, at that time, by a mixture of eternal time and external energy, there occurred the creation of water and taste.**
