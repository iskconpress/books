====== SB 3.5.37 ======

> भूतानां नभआदीनां यद्यद्भव्यावरावरम् ।
> तेषां परानुसंसर्गाद्यथा सङ्ख्यं गुणान्विदुः ॥३७॥

===== Text =====

> bhūtānāṁ nabha-ādīnāṁ
> yad yad bhavyāvarāvaram
> teṣāṁ parānusaṁsargād
> yathā saṅkhyaṁ guṇān viduḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhūtānām]]---of all the physical elements; [[synonyms:n:nabhaḥ]]---the sky; [[synonyms:ā:ādīnām]]---beginning from; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---as; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---and as; [[synonyms:b:bhavya]]---O gentle one; [[synonyms:a:avara]]---inferior; [[synonyms:v:varam]]---superior; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:para]]---the Supreme; [[synonyms:a:anusaṁsargāt]]---last touch; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as many; [[synonyms:s:saṅkhyam]]---number; [[synonyms:g:guṇān]]---qualities; [[synonyms:v:viduḥ]]---you may understand.

===== Translation =====

**O gentle one, of all the physical elements, beginning from the sky down to the earth, all the inferior and superior qualities are due only to the final touch of the glance of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
