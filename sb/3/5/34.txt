====== SB 3.5.34 ======

> अनिलोऽपि विकुर्वाणो नभसोरुबलान्वितः ।
> ससर्ज रूपतन्मात्रं ज्योतिर्लोकस्य लोचनम् ॥३४॥

===== Text =====

> anilo 'pi vikurvāṇo
> nabhasoru-balānvitaḥ
> sasarja rūpa-tanmātraṁ
> jyotir lokasya locanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anilaḥ]]---air; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vikurvāṇaḥ]]---being transformed; [[synonyms:n:nabhasā]]---sky; [[synonyms:u:uru]]-[[synonyms:b:bala]]-[[synonyms:a:anvitaḥ]]---extremely powerful; [[synonyms:s:sasarja]]---created; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---form; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:m:mātram]]---sense perception; [[synonyms:j:jyotiḥ]]---electricity; [[synonyms:l:lokasya]]---of the world; [[synonyms:l:locanam]]---light to see.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter the extremely powerful air, interacting with the sky, generated the form of sense perception, and the perception of form transformed into electricity, the light to see the world.**
