====== SB 3.2.1 ======

> श्रीशुक उवाच
> इति भागवतः पृष्टः क्षत्त्रा वार्तां प्रियाश्रयाम् ।
> प्रतिवक्तुं न चोत्सेह औत्कण्ठ्यात्स्मारितेश्वरः ॥१॥

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> iti bhāgavataḥ pṛṣṭaḥ
> kṣattrā vārtāṁ priyāśrayām
> prativaktuṁ na cotseha
> autkaṇṭhyāt smāriteśvaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavataḥ]]---the great devotee; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣṭaḥ]]---being asked; [[synonyms:k:kṣattrā]]---by Vidura; [[synonyms:v:vārtām]]---message; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrayām]]---regarding the dearest; [[synonyms:p:prativaktum]]---to reply; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:u:utsehe]]---became eager; [[synonyms:a:autkaṇṭhyāt]]---by excessive anxiety; [[synonyms:s:smārita]]---remembrance; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: When the great devotee Uddhava was asked by Vidura to speak on the messages of the dearest [Lord Kṛṣṇa], Uddhava was unable to answer immediately due to excessive anxiety at the remembrance of the Lord.**
