====== SB 3.4.5 ======

> तथापि तदभिप्रेतं जानन्नहमरिन्दम ।
> पृष्ठतोऽन्वगमं भर्तुः पादविश्लेषणाक्षमः ॥५॥

===== Text =====

> tathāpi tad-abhipretaṁ
> jānann aham arindama
> pṛṣṭhato 'nvagamaṁ bhartuḥ
> pāda-viśleṣaṇākṣamaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---yet, in spite of; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:a:abhipretam]]---His desire; [[synonyms:j:jānan]]---knowing; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:a:arim]]-[[synonyms:d:dama]]---O subduer of the enemy (Vidura); [[synonyms:p:pṛṣṭhataḥ]]---behind; [[synonyms:a:anvagamam]]---followed; [[synonyms:b:bhartuḥ]]---of the master; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:v:viśleṣaṇa]]---separation from His lotus feet; [[synonyms:a:akṣamaḥ]]---not being able.

===== Translation =====

**Yet in spite of my knowing His desire [to destroy the dynasty], O Arindama [Vidura], I followed Him because it was impossible for me to bear separation from the lotus feet of the master.**
