====== SB 3.21.10 ======

> किरीटिनं कुण्डलिनं शङ्खचक्रगदाधरम् ।
> श्वेतोत्पलक्रीडनकं मनःस्पर्शस्मितेक्षणम् ॥१०॥

===== Text =====

> kirīṭinaṁ kuṇḍalinaṁ
> śaṅkha-cakra-gadā-dharam
> śvetotpala-krīḍanakaṁ
> manaḥ-sparśa-smitekṣaṇam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kirīṭinam]]---adorned with a crown; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍalinam]]---wearing earrings; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---conch; [[synonyms:c:cakra]]---disc; [[synonyms:g:gadā]]---mace; [[synonyms:d:dharam]]---holding; [[synonyms:ś:śveta]]---white; [[synonyms:u:utpala]]---lily; [[synonyms:k:krīḍanakam]]---plaything; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]---heart; [[synonyms:s:sparśa]]---touching; [[synonyms:s:smita]]---smiling; [[synonyms:ī:īkṣaṇam]]---and glancing.

===== Translation =====

**Adorned with a crown and earrings, He held His characteristic conch, disc and mace in three of His hands and a white lily in the fourth. He glanced about in a happy, smiling mood whose sight captivates the hearts of all devotees.**
