====== SB 3.8.28 ======

> कदम्बकिञ्जल्कपिशङ्गवाससा स्वलङ्कृतं मेखलया नितम्बे ।
> हारेण चानन्तधनेन वत्स श्रीवत्सवक्षःस्थलवल्लभेन ॥२८॥

===== Text =====

> kadamba-kiñjalka-piśaṅga-vāsasā
> svalaṅkṛtaṁ mekhalayā nitambe
> hāreṇa cānanta-dhanena vatsa
> śrīvatsa-vakṣaḥ-sthala-vallabhena

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kadamba]]-[[synonyms:k:kiñjalka]]---saffron dust of the //kadamba// flower; [[synonyms:p:piśaṅga]]---dress of the color; [[synonyms:v:vāsasā]]---by clothing; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkṛtam]]---well decorated; [[synonyms:m:mekhalayā]]---by the belt; [[synonyms:n:nitambe]]---on the waist; [[synonyms:h:hāreṇa]]---by the garland; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---highly; [[synonyms:d:dhanena]]---valuable; [[synonyms:v:vatsa]]---my dear Vidura; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvatsa]]---of the transcendental marking; [[synonyms:v:vakṣaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sthala]]---on the chest; [[synonyms:v:vallabhena]]---very pleasing.

===== Translation =====

**O my dear Vidura, the Lord's waist was covered with yellow cloth resembling the saffron dust of the kadamba flower, and it was encircled by a well-decorated belt. His chest was decorated with the śrīvatsa marking and a necklace of unlimited value.**
