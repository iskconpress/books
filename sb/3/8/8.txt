====== SB 3.8.8 ======

> साङ्ख्यायनः पारमहंस्यमुख्यो विवक्षमाणो भगवद्विभूतीः ।
> जगाद सोऽस्मद्गुरवेऽन्विताय पराशरायाथ बृहस्पतेश्च ॥८॥

===== Text =====

> sāṅkhyāyanaḥ pāramahaṁsya-mukhyo
> vivakṣamāṇo bhagavad-vibhūtīḥ
> jagāda so 'smad-gurave 'nvitāya
> parāśarāyātha bṛhaspateś ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāṅkhyāyanaḥ]]---the great sage Sāṅkhyāyana; [[synonyms:p:pāramahaṁsya]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhyaḥ]]---the chief of all transcendentalists; [[synonyms:v:vivakṣamāṇaḥ]]---while reciting; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhūtīḥ]]---the glories of the Lord; [[synonyms:j:jagāda]]---explained; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:a:asmat]]---of me; [[synonyms:g:gurave]]---unto the spiritual master; [[synonyms:a:anvitāya]]---followed; [[synonyms:p:parāśarāya]]---unto the sage Parāśara; [[synonyms:a:atha]] [[synonyms:b:bṛhaspateḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also to Bṛhaspati.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Sāṅkhyāyana was the chief amongst the transcendentalists, and when he was describing the glories of the Lord in terms of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, it so happened that my spiritual master, Parāśara, and Bṛhaspati both heard him.**
