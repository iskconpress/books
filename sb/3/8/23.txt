====== SB 3.8.23 ======

> मृणालगौरायतशेषभोग पर्यङ्क एकं पुरुषं शयानम् ।
> फणातपत्रायुतमूर्धरत्न द्युभिर्हतध्वान्तयुगान्ततोये ॥२३॥

===== Text =====

> mṛṇāla-gaurāyata-śeṣa-bhoga-
> paryaṅka ekaṁ puruṣaṁ śayānam
> phaṇātapatrāyuta-mūrdha-ratna-
> dyubhir hata-dhvānta-yugānta-toye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mṛṇāla]]---lotus flower; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---white all over; [[synonyms:ā:āyata]]---gigantic; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---body of Śeṣa-nāga; [[synonyms:p:paryaṅke]]---on the bed; [[synonyms:e:ekam]]---alone; [[synonyms:p:puruṣam]]---the Supreme Person; [[synonyms:ś:śayānam]]---was lying; [[synonyms:p:phaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātapatra]]---umbrella of a serpent hood; [[synonyms:ā:āyuta]]---bedecked with; [[synonyms:m:mūrdha]]---head; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]---jewels; [[synonyms:d:dyubhiḥ]]---by the rays; [[synonyms:h:hata]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvānta]]---darkness dissipated; [[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:a:anta]]---devastation; [[synonyms:t:toye]]---in the water.

===== Translation =====

**Brahmā could see that on the water there was a gigantic lotuslike white bedstead, the body of Śeṣa-nāga, on which the Personality of Godhead was lying alone. The whole atmosphere was illuminated by the rays of the jewels bedecking the hood of Śeṣa-nāga, and that illumination dissipated all the darkness of those regions.**
