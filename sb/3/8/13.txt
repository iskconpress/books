====== SB 3.8.13 ======

> तस्यार्थसूक्ष्माभिनिविष्टदृष्टेरन्तर्गतोऽर्थो रजसा तनीयान् ।
> गुणेन कालानुगतेन विद्धः सूष्यंस्तदाभिद्यत नाभिदेशात् ॥१३॥

===== Text =====

> tasyārtha-sūkṣmābhiniviṣṭa-dṛṣṭer
> antar-gato 'rtho rajasā tanīyān
> guṇena kālānugatena viddhaḥ
> sūṣyaṁs tadābhidyata nābhi-deśāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---His; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---subject; [[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---subtle; [[synonyms:a:abhiniviṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭeḥ]]---of one whose attention was fixed; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---internal; [[synonyms:a:arthaḥ]]---purpose; [[synonyms:r:rajasā]]---by the mode of passion of material nature; [[synonyms:t:tanīyān]]---very subtle; [[synonyms:g:guṇena]]---by the qualities; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:a:anugatena]]---in due course of time; [[synonyms:v:viddhaḥ]]---agitated; [[synonyms:s:sūṣyan]]---generating; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:a:abhidyata]]---pierced through; [[synonyms:n:nābhi]]-[[synonyms:d:deśāt]]---from the abdomen.

===== Translation =====

**The subtle subject matter of creation, on which the Lord's attention was fixed, was agitated by the material mode of passion, and thus the subtle form of creation pierced through His abdomen.**
