====== SB 3.8.14 ======

> स पद्मकोशः सहसोदतिष्ठत्कालेन कर्मप्रतिबोधनेन ।
> स्वरोचिषा तत्सलिलं विशालं विद्योतयन्नर्क इवात्मयोनिः ॥१४॥

===== Text =====

> sa padma-kośaḥ sahasodatiṣṭhat
> kālena karma-pratibodhanena
> sva-rociṣā tat salilaṁ viśālaṁ
> vidyotayann arka ivātma-yoniḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:k:kośaḥ]]---bud of a lotus flower; [[synonyms:s:sahasā]]---suddenly; [[synonyms:u:udatiṣṭhat]]---appeared; [[synonyms:k:kālena]]---by time; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---fruitive activities; [[synonyms:p:pratibodhanena]]---awakening; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rociṣā]]---by its own effulgence; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:s:salilam]]---water of devastation; [[synonyms:v:viśālam]]---vast; [[synonyms:v:vidyotayan]]---illuminating; [[synonyms:a:arkaḥ]]---the sun; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:y:yoniḥ]]---generating from the Personality of Viṣṇu.

===== Translation =====

**Piercing through, this sum total form of the fruitive activity of the living entities took the shape of the bud of a lotus flower generated from the Personality of Viṣṇu, and by His supreme will it illuminated everything, like the sun, and dried up the vast waters of devastation.**
