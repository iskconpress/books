====== SB 3.8.16 ======

> तस्यां स चाम्भोरुहकर्णिकायामवस्थितो लोकमपश्यमानः ।
> परिक्रमन्व्योम्नि विवृत्तनेत्रश्चत्वारि लेभेऽनुदिशं मुखानि ॥१६॥

===== Text =====

> tasyāṁ sa cāmbho-ruha-karṇikāyām
> avasthito lokam apaśyamānaḥ
> parikraman vyomni vivṛtta-netraś
> catvāri lebhe 'nudiśaṁ mukhāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasyām]]---in that; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Brahmā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:ambhaḥ]]---water; [[synonyms:r:ruha]]-[[synonyms:k:karṇikāyām]]---whorl of the lotus; [[synonyms:a:avasthitaḥ]]---being situated; [[synonyms:l:lokam]]---the world; [[synonyms:a:apaśyamānaḥ]]---without being able to see; [[synonyms:p:parikraman]]---circumambulating; [[synonyms:v:vyomni]]---in space; [[synonyms:v:vivṛtta]]-[[synonyms:n:netraḥ]]---while moving the eyes; [[synonyms:c:catvāri]]---four; [[synonyms:l:lebhe]]---achieved; [[synonyms:a:anudiśam]]---in terms of direction; [[synonyms:m:mukhāni]]---heads.

===== Translation =====

**Brahmā, born out of the lotus flower, could not see the world, although he was situated in the whorl. He therefore circumambulated all of space, and while moving his eyes in all directions he achieved four heads in terms of the four directions.**
