====== SB 3.1.7 ======

> यदा सभायां कुरुदेवदेव्याः केशाभिमर्शं सुतकर्म गर्ह्यम् ।
> न वारयामास नृपः स्नुषायाः स्वास्रैर्हरन्त्याः कुचकुङ्कुमानि ॥७॥

===== Text =====

> yadā sabhāyāṁ kuru-deva-devyāḥ
> keśābhimarśaṁ suta-karma garhyam
> na vārayām āsa nṛpaḥ snuṣāyāḥ
> svāsrair harantyāḥ kuca-kuṅkumāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:s:sabhāyām]]---the assembly; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:devyāḥ]]---of Draupadī, the wife of godly Yudhiṣṭhira; [[synonyms:k:keśa]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimarśam]]---insult by grabbing her hair; [[synonyms:s:suta]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]---action taken by his son; [[synonyms:g:garhyam]]---which was abominable; [[synonyms:n:na]]---did not; [[synonyms:v:vārayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---forbid; [[synonyms:n:nṛpaḥ]]---the King; [[synonyms:s:snuṣāyāḥ]]---of his nephew's wife; [[synonyms:s:svāsraiḥ]]---by her tears; [[synonyms:h:harantyāḥ]]---of she who was washing; [[synonyms:k:kuca]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṅkumāni]]---red dust on her breast.

===== Translation =====

**The King did not forbid his son Duḥśāsana's abominable action of grabbing the hair of Draupadī, the wife of the godly King Yudhiṣṭhira, even though her tears washed the red dust on her breast.**
