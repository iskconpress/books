====== SB 3.10.16 ======

> भूतसर्गस्तृतीयस्तु तन्मात्रो द्रव्यशक्तिमान् ।
> चतुर्थ ऐन्द्रियः सर्गो यस्तु ज्ञानक्रियात्मकः ॥१६॥

===== Text =====

> bhūta-sargas tṛtīyas tu
> tan-mātro dravya-śaktimān
> caturtha aindriyaḥ sargo
> yas tu jñāna-kriyātmakaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:s:sargaḥ]]---creation of matter; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīyaḥ]]---is the third; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:m:mātraḥ]]---sense perception; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---of the elements; [[synonyms:ś:śaktimān]]---generator; [[synonyms:c:caturthaḥ]]---the fourth; [[synonyms:a:aindriyaḥ]]---in the matter of the senses; [[synonyms:s:sargaḥ]]---creation; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---that which; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge-acquiring; [[synonyms:k:kriyā]]---working; [[synonyms:ā:ātmakaḥ]]---basically.

===== Translation =====

**The sense perceptions are created in the third creation, and from these the elements are generated. The fourth creation is the creation of knowledge and of working capacity.**
