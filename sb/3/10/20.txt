====== SB 3.10.20 ======

> उत्स्रोतसस्तमःप्राया अन्तःस्पर्शा विशेषिण ॥२०॥

===== Text =====

> utsrotasas tamaḥ-prāyā
> antaḥ-sparśā viśeṣiṇaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:utsrotasaḥ]]---they seek their subsistence upwards; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]-[[synonyms:p:prāyāḥ]]---almost unconscious; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśāḥ]]---slightly feeling within; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣiṇaḥ]]---with varieties of manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**All the immovable trees and plants seek their subsistence upwards. They are almost unconscious but have feelings of pain within. They are manifested in variegatedness.**
