====== SB 3.10.27 ======

> वैकृतास्त्रय एवैते देवसर्गश्च सत्तम ।
> वैकारिकस्तु यः प्रोक्तः कौमारस्तूभयात्मकः ॥२७॥

===== Text =====

> vaikṛtās traya evaite
> deva-sargaś ca sattama
> vaikārikas tu yaḥ proktaḥ
> kaumāras tūbhayātmakaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaikṛtāḥ]]---creations of Brahmā; [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]]---three kinds; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:e:ete]]---all these; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:s:sargaḥ]]---appearance of the demigods; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:sattama]]---O good Vidura; [[synonyms:v:vaikārikaḥ]]---creation of demigods by nature; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---which; [[synonyms:p:proktaḥ]]---described before; [[synonyms:k:kaumāraḥ]]---the four Kumāras; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:u:ubhaya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmakaḥ]]---both ways (namely //vaikṛta// and //prākṛta//).

===== Translation =====

**O good Vidura, these last three creations and the creation of demigods (the tenth creation) are vaikṛta creations, which are different from the previously described prākṛta (natural) creations. The appearance of the Kumāras is both.**
