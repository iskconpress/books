====== SB 3.18.21 ======

> आसन्नशौण्डीरमपेतसाध्वसं कृतप्रतीकारमहार्यविक्रमम् ।
> विलक्ष्य दैत्यं भगवान्सहस्रणीर्जगाद नारायणमादिसूकरम् ॥२१॥

===== Text =====

> āsanna-śauṇḍīram apeta-sādhvasaṁ
> kṛta-pratīkāram ahārya-vikramam
> vilakṣya daityaṁ bhagavān sahasra-ṇīr
> jagāda nārāyaṇam ādi-sūkaram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsanna]]---attained; [[synonyms:ś:śauṇḍīram]]---power; [[synonyms:a:apeta]]---devoid of; [[synonyms:s:sādhvasam]]---fear; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]---making; [[synonyms:p:pratīkāram]]---opposition; [[synonyms:a:ahārya]]---unopposable; [[synonyms:v:vikramam]]---having power; [[synonyms:v:vilakṣya]]---having seen; [[synonyms:d:daityam]]---the demon; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the worshipful Brahmā; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:n:nīḥ]]---the leader of thousands of sages; [[synonyms:j:jagāda]]---addressed; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇam]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the original; [[synonyms:s:sūkaram]]---having the form of a boar.

===== Translation =====

**After arriving at the place of combat, Brahmā, the leader of thousands of sages and transcendentalists, saw the demon, who had attained such unprecedented power that no one could fight with him. Brahmā then addressed Nārāyaṇa, who was assuming the form of a boar for the first time.**
