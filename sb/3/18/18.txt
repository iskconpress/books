====== SB 3.18.18 ======

> एवं गदाभ्यां गुर्वीभ्यां हर्यक्षो हरिरेव च ।
> जिगीषया सुसंरब्धावन्योन्यमभिजघ्नतुः ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> evaṁ gadābhyāṁ gurvībhyāṁ
> haryakṣo harir eva ca
> jigīṣayā susaṁrabdhāv
> anyonyam abhijaghnatuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gadābhyām]]---with their maces; [[synonyms:g:gurvībhyām]]---huge; [[synonyms:h:haryakṣaḥ]]---the demon Haryakṣa (Hiraṇyākṣa); [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---Lord Hari; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:j:jigīṣayā]]---with a desire for victory; [[synonyms:s:susaṁrabdhau]]---enraged; [[synonyms:a:anyonyam]]---each other; [[synonyms:a:abhijaghnatuḥ]]---they struck.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, the demon Haryakṣa and the Lord, the Personality of Godhead, struck each other with their huge maces, each enraged and seeking his own victory.**

===== Purport =====

Haryakṣa is another name for Hiraṇyākṣa, the demon.
