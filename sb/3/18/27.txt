====== SB 3.18.27 ======

> अधुनैषोऽभिजिन्नाम योगो मौहूर्तिको ह्यगात् ।
> शिवाय नस्त्वं सुहृदामाशु निस्तर दुस्तरम् ॥२७॥

===== Text =====

> adhunaiṣo 'bhijin nāma
> yogo mauhūrtiko hy agāt
> śivāya nas tvaṁ suhṛdām
> āśu nistara dustaram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adhunā]]---now; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---this; [[synonyms:a:abhijit]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---called //abhijit//; [[synonyms:y:yogaḥ]]---auspicious; [[synonyms:m:mauhūrtikaḥ]]---moment; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:agāt]]---has almost passed; [[synonyms:ś:śivāya]]---for the welfare; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---of us; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---You; [[synonyms:s:suhṛdām]]---of Your friends; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]---quickly; [[synonyms:n:nistara]]---dispose of; [[synonyms:d:dustaram]]---the formidable foe.

===== Translation =====

**The auspicious period known as abhijit, which is most opportune for victory, commenced at midday and has all but passed; therefore, in the interest of Your friends, please dispose of this formidable foe quickly.**
