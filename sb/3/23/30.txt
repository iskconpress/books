====== SB 3.23.30 ======

> अथादर्शे स्वमात्मानं स्रग्विणं विरजाम्बरम् ।
> विरजं कृतस्वस्त्ययनं कन्याभिर्बहुमानितम् ॥३०॥

===== Text =====

> athādarśe svam ātmānaṁ
> sragviṇaṁ virajāmbaram
> virajaṁ kṛta-svastyayanaṁ
> kanyābhir bahu-mānitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:ā:ādarśe]]---in a mirror; [[synonyms:s:svam]] [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---her own reflection; [[synonyms:s:srak]]-[[synonyms:v:viṇam]]---adorned with a garland; [[synonyms:v:viraja]]---unsullied; [[synonyms:a:ambaram]]---robes; [[synonyms:v:virajam]]---freed from all bodily dirt; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:svasti]]-[[synonyms:a:ayanam]]---decorated with auspicious marks; [[synonyms:k:kanyābhiḥ]]---by the maids; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mānitam]]---very respectfully served.

===== Translation =====

**Then in a mirror she beheld her own reflection. Her body was completely freed from all dirt, and she was adorned with a garland. Dressed in unsullied robes and decorated with auspicious marks of tilaka, she was served very respectfully by the maids.**
