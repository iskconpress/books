====== SB 3.23.40 ======

> वैश्रम्भके सुरसने नन्दने पुष्पभद्रके ।
> मानसे चैत्ररथ्ये च स रेमे रामया रतः ॥४०॥

===== Text =====

> vaiśrambhake surasane
> nandane puṣpabhadrake
> mānase caitrarathye ca
> sa reme rāmayā rataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaiśrambhake]]---in the Vaiśrambhaka garden; [[synonyms:s:surasane]]---in Surasana; [[synonyms:n:nandane]]---in Nandana; [[synonyms:p:puṣpabhadrake]]---in Puṣpabhadraka; [[synonyms:m:mānase]]---by the Mānasa-sarovara Lake; [[synonyms:c:caitrarathye]]---in Caitrarathya; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:r:reme]]---enjoyed; [[synonyms:r:rāmayā]]---by his wife; [[synonyms:r:rataḥ]]---satisfied.

===== Translation =====

**Satisfied by his wife, he enjoyed in that aerial mansion not only on Mount Meru but in different gardens known as Vaiśrambhaka, Surasana, Nandana, Puṣpabhadraka and Caitrarathya, and by the Mānasa-sarovara Lake.**
