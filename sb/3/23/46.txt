====== SB 3.23.46 ======

> एवं योगानुभावेन दम्पत्यो रममाणयोः ।
> शतं व्यतीयुः शरदः कामलालसयोर्मनाक् ॥४६॥

===== Text =====

> evaṁ yogānubhāvena
> dam-patyo ramamāṇayoḥ
> śataṁ vyatīyuḥ śaradaḥ
> kāma-lālasayor manāk

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhāvena]]---by yogic powers; [[synonyms:d:dam]]-[[synonyms:p:patyoḥ]]---the couple; [[synonyms:r:ramamāṇayoḥ]]---while enjoying themselves; [[synonyms:ś:śatam]]---a hundred; [[synonyms:v:vyatīyuḥ]]---passed; [[synonyms:ś:śaradaḥ]]---autumns; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---sexual pleasure; [[synonyms:l:lālasayoḥ]]---who were eagerly longing for; [[synonyms:m:manāk]]---like a short time.

===== Translation =====

**While the couple, who eagerly longed for sexual pleasure, were thus enjoying themselves by virtue of mystic powers, a hundred autumns passed like a brief span of time.**
