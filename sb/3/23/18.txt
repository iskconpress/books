====== SB 3.23.18 ======

> द्वाःसु विद्रुमदेहल्या भातं वज्रकपाटवत् ।
> शिखरेष्विन्द्रनीलेषु हेमकुम्भैरधिश्रितम् ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> dvāḥsu vidruma-dehalyā
> bhātaṁ vajra-kapāṭavat
> śikhareṣv indranīleṣu
> hema-kumbhair adhiśritam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvāḥsu]]---in the entrances; [[synonyms:v:vidruma]]---of coral; [[synonyms:d:dehalyā]]---with a threshold; [[synonyms:b:bhātam]]---beautiful; [[synonyms:v:vajra]]---bedecked with diamonds; [[synonyms:k:kapāṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---having doors; [[synonyms:ś:śikhareṣu]]---on the domes; [[synonyms:i:indra]]-[[synonyms:n:nīleṣu]]---of sapphires; [[synonyms:h:hema]]-[[synonyms:k:kumbhaiḥ]]---with gold pinnacles; [[synonyms:a:adhiśritam]]---crowned.

===== Translation =====

**The palace was very beautiful, with its coral thresholds at the entrances and its doors bedecked with diamonds. Gold pinnacles crowned its domes of sapphire.**
