====== SB 3.12.21 ======

> अथाभिध्यायतः सर्गं दश पुत्राः प्रजज्ञिरे ।
> भगवच्छक्तियुक्तस्य लोकसन्तानहेतवः ॥२१॥

===== Text =====

> athābhidhyāyataḥ sargaṁ
> daśa putrāḥ prajajñire
> bhagavac-chakti-yuktasya
> loka-santāna-hetavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:abhidhyāyataḥ]]---while thinking of; [[synonyms:s:sargam]]---creation; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten; [[synonyms:p:putrāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:p:prajajñire]]---were begotten; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]---regarding the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---potency; [[synonyms:y:yuktasya]]---empowered with; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the world; [[synonyms:s:santāna]]---generation; [[synonyms:h:hetavaḥ]]---the causes.

===== Translation =====

**Brahmā, who was empowered by the Supreme Personality of Godhead, thought of generating living entities and begot ten sons for the extension of the generations.**
