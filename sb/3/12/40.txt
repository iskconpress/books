====== SB 3.12.40 ======

> षोडश्युक्थौ पूर्ववक्त्रात्पुरीष्यग्निष्टुतावथ ।
> आप्तोर्यामातिरात्रौ च वाजपेयं सगोसवम् ॥४०॥

===== Text =====

> ṣoḍaśy-ukthau pūrva-vaktrāt
> purīṣy-agniṣṭutāv atha
> āptoryāmātirātrau ca
> vājapeyaṁ sagosavam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣoḍaśī]]-[[synonyms:u:ukthau]]---types of sacrifice; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vaktrāt]]---from the eastern mouth; [[synonyms:p:purīṣi]]-[[synonyms:a:agniṣṭutau]]---types of sacrifice; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:ā:āptoryāma]]-[[synonyms:a:atirātrau]]---types of sacrifice; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vājapeyam]]---type of sacrifice; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosavam]]---type of sacrifice.

===== Translation =====

**All the different varieties of fire sacrifices [ṣoḍaśī, uktha, purīṣi, agniṣṭoma, āptoryāma, atirātra, vājapeya and gosava] became manifested from the eastern mouth of Brahmā.**
