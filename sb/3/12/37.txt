====== SB 3.12.37 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> ऋग्यजुःसामाथर्वाख्यान्वेदान्पूर्वादिभिर्मुखैः ।
> शास्त्रमिज्यां स्तुतिस्तोमं प्रायश्चित्तं व्यधात्क्रमात् ॥३७॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> ṛg-yajuḥ-sāmātharvākhyān
> vedān pūrvādibhir mukhaiḥ
> śāstram ijyāṁ stuti-stomaṁ
> prāyaścittaṁ vyadhāt kramāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Maitreya said; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛk]]-[[synonyms:y:yajuḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sāma]]-[[synonyms:a:atharva]]---the four //Vedas//; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyān]]---of the name; [[synonyms:v:vedān]]---Vedic literatures; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---beginning with the front; [[synonyms:m:mukhaiḥ]]---by the mouths; [[synonyms:ś:śāstram]]---Vedic hymns not pronounced before; [[synonyms:i:ijyām]]---priestly rituals; [[synonyms:s:stuti]]-[[synonyms:s:stomam]]---the subject matter of the reciters; [[synonyms:p:prāyaścittam]]---transcendental activities; [[synonyms:v:vyadhāt]]---established; [[synonyms:k:kramāt]]---one after another.

===== Translation =====

**Maitreya said: Beginning from the front face of Brahmā, gradually the four Vedas---Ṛk, Yajur, Sāma and Atharva---became manifest. Thereafter, Vedic hymns which had not been pronounced before, priestly rituals, the subject matters of the recitation, and transcendental activities were all established, one after another.**
