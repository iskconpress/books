====== SB 3.12.15 ======

> इत्यादिष्टः स्वगुरुणा भगवान्नीललोहितः ।
> सत्त्वाकृतिस्वभावेन ससर्जात्मसमाः प्रजाः ॥१५॥

===== Text =====

> ity ādiṣṭaḥ sva-guruṇā
> bhagavān nīla-lohitaḥ
> sattvākṛti-svabhāvena
> sasarjātma-samāḥ prajāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:ādiṣṭaḥ]]---being ordered; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:guruṇā]]---by his own spiritual master; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:n:nīla]]-[[synonyms:l:lohitaḥ]]---Rudra, whose color is mixed blue and red; [[synonyms:s:sattva]]---power; [[synonyms:ā:ākṛti]]---bodily features; [[synonyms:s:svabhāvena]]---and with a very furious mode of nature; [[synonyms:s:sasarja]]---created; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:samāḥ]]---like his own prototype; [[synonyms:p:prajāḥ]]---generations.

===== Translation =====

**The most powerful Rudra, whose bodily color was blue mixed with red, created many offspring exactly resembling him in features, strength and furious nature.**
