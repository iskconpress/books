====== SB 3.14.7 ======

> अथात्रापीतिहासोऽयं श्रुतो मे वर्णितः पुरा ।
> ब्रह्मणा देवदेवेन देवानामनुपृच्छताम् ॥७॥

===== Text =====

> athātrāpītihāso 'yaṁ
> śruto me varṇitaḥ purā
> brahmaṇā deva-devena
> devānām anupṛcchatām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---now; [[synonyms:a:atra]]---in this matter; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:i:itihāsaḥ]]---history; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śrutaḥ]]---heard; [[synonyms:m:me]]---by me; [[synonyms:v:varṇitaḥ]]---described; [[synonyms:p:purā]]---years ago; [[synonyms:b:brahmaṇā]]---by Brahmā; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:devena]]---the foremost of the demigods; [[synonyms:d:devānām]]---by the demigods; [[synonyms:a:anupṛcchatām]]---asking.

===== Translation =====

**This history of the fight between the Lord as a boar and the demon Hiraṇyākṣa was heard by me in a year long ago as it was described by the foremost of the demigods, Brahmā, when he was questioned by the other demigods.**
