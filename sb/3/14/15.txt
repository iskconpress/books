====== SB 3.14.15 ======

> अथ मे कुरु कल्याणं कामं कमललोचन ।
> आर्तोपसर्पणं भूमन्नमोघं हि महीयसि ॥१५॥

===== Text =====

> atha me kuru kalyāṇaṁ
> kāmaṁ kamala-locana
> ārtopasarpaṇaṁ bhūmann
> amoghaṁ hi mahīyasi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---therefore; [[synonyms:m:me]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]---kindly do; [[synonyms:k:kalyāṇam]]---benediction; [[synonyms:k:kāmam]]---desire; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:l:locana]]---O lotus-eyed one; [[synonyms:ā:ārta]]---of the distressed; [[synonyms:u:upasarpaṇam]]---the approaching; [[synonyms:b:bhūman]]---O great one; [[synonyms:a:amogham]]---without failure; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:m:mahīyasi]]---to a great person.

===== Translation =====

**O lotus-eyed one, kindly bless me by fulfilling my desire. When someone in distress approaches a great person, his pleas should never go in vain.**

===== Purport =====

Diti knew well that her request might be rejected because of the untimely situation, but she pleaded that when there is an emergency or a distressful condition, there is no consideration of time or situation.
