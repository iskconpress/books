====== SB 3.14.8 ======

> दितिर्दाक्षायणी क्षत्तर्मारीचं कश्यपं पतिम् ।
> अपत्यकामा चकमे सन्ध्यायां हृच्छयार्दिता ॥८॥

===== Text =====

> ditir dākṣāyaṇī kṣattar
> mārīcaṁ kaśyapaṁ patim
> apatya-kāmā cakame
> sandhyāyāṁ hṛc-chayārditā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:ditiḥ]]---Diti; [[synonyms:d:dākṣāyaṇī]]---the daughter of Dakṣa; [[synonyms:k:kṣattaḥ]]---O Vidura; [[synonyms:m:mārīcam]]---the son of Marīci; [[synonyms:k:kaśyapam]]---Kaśyapa; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---her husband; [[synonyms:a:apatya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāmā]]---desirous of having a child; [[synonyms:c:cakame]]---longed for; [[synonyms:s:sandhyāyām]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:h:hṛt]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaya]]---by sex desires; [[synonyms:a:arditā]]---distressed.

===== Translation =====

**Diti, daughter of Dakṣa, being afflicted with sex desire, begged her husband, Kaśyapa, the son of Marīci, to have intercourse with her in the evening in order to beget a child.**
