====== SB 3.14.1 ======

> श्रीशुक उवाच
> निशम्य कौषारविणोपवर्णितां हरेः कथां कारणसूकरात्मनः ।
> पुनः स पप्रच्छ तमुद्यताञ्जलिर्न चातितृप्तो विदुरो धृतव्रतः ॥१॥

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> niśamya kauṣāraviṇopavarṇitāṁ
> hareḥ kathāṁ kāraṇa-sūkarātmanaḥ
> punaḥ sa papraccha tam udyatāñjalir
> na cātitṛpto viduro dhṛta-vrataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:n:niśamya]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:k:kauṣāraviṇā]]---by the sage Maitreya; [[synonyms:u:upavarṇitām]]---described; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kathām]]---narrations; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---for the reason of lifting the earth; [[synonyms:s:sūkara]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---of the boar incarnation; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:p:papraccha]]---inquired; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---from him (Maitreya); [[synonyms:u:udyata]]-[[synonyms:a:añjaliḥ]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:n:na]]---never; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:t:tṛptaḥ]]---very much satisfied; [[synonyms:v:viduraḥ]]---Vidura; [[synonyms:d:dhṛta]]-[[synonyms:v:vrataḥ]]---taken to a vow.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After hearing from the great sage Maitreya about the Lord's incarnation as Varāha, Vidura, who had taken a vow, begged him with folded hands to please narrate further transcendental activities of the Lord, since he [Vidura] did not yet feel satisfied.**
