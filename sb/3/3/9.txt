====== SB 3.3.9 ======

> तास्वपत्यान्यजनयदात्मतुल्यानि सर्वतः ।
> एकैकस्यां दश दश प्रकृतेर्विबुभूषया ॥९॥

===== Text =====

> tāsv apatyāny ajanayad
> ātma-tulyāni sarvataḥ
> ekaikasyāṁ daśa daśa
> prakṛter vibubhūṣayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāsu]]---unto them; [[synonyms:a:apatyāni]]---offspring; [[synonyms:a:ajanayat]]---begot; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:t:tulyāni]]---all like Himself; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---in all respects; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:e:ekasyām]]---in each and every one of them; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten; [[synonyms:p:prakṛteḥ]]---for expanding Himself; [[synonyms:v:vibubhūṣayā]]---so desiring.

===== Translation =====

**Just to expand Himself according to His transcendental features, the Lord begot in each and every one of them ten offspring with exactly His own qualities.**
