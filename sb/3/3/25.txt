====== SB 3.3.25 ======

> ततः कतिपयैर्मासैर्वृष्णिभोजान्धकादयः ।
> ययुः प्रभासं संहृष्टा रथैर्देवविमोहिताः ॥२५॥

===== Text =====

> tataḥ katipayair māsair
> vṛṣṇi-bhojāndhakādayaḥ
> yayuḥ prabhāsaṁ saṁhṛṣṭā
> rathair deva-vimohitāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:katipayaiḥ]]---a few; [[synonyms:m:māsaiḥ]]---months passing; [[synonyms:v:vṛṣṇi]]---the descendants of Vṛṣṇi; [[synonyms:b:bhoja]]---the descendants of Bhoja; [[synonyms:a:andhaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and others, like the sons of Andhaka; [[synonyms:y:yayuḥ]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhāsam]]---the place of pilgrimage named Prabhāsa; [[synonyms:s:saṁhṛṣṭāḥ]]---with great pleasure; [[synonyms:r:rathaiḥ]]---on their chariots; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---by Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vimohitāḥ]]---bewildered.

===== Translation =====

**A few months passed, and then, bewildered by Kṛṣṇa, all the descendants of Vṛṣṇi, Bhoja and Andhaka who were incarnations of demigods went to Prabhāsa, while those who were eternal devotees of the Lord did not leave but remained in Dvārakā.**
