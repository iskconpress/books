====== SB 3.3.16 ======

> एवं सञ्चिन्त्य भगवान्स्वराज्ये स्थाप्य धर्मजम् ।
> नन्दयामास सुहृदः साधूनां वर्त्म दर्शयन् ॥१६॥

===== Text =====

> evaṁ sañcintya bhagavān
> sva-rājye sthāpya dharmajam
> nandayām āsa suhṛdaḥ
> sādhūnāṁ vartma darśayan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sañcintya]]---thinking within Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rājye]]---in his own kingdom; [[synonyms:s:sthāpya]]---installing; [[synonyms:d:dharmajam]]---Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira; [[synonyms:n:nandayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---gladdened; [[synonyms:s:suhṛdaḥ]]---the friends; [[synonyms:s:sādhūnām]]---of the saints; [[synonyms:v:vartma]]---the path; [[synonyms:d:darśayan]]---by indicating.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa, thus thinking to Himself, established Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira in the position of supreme control of the world in order to show the ideal of administration on the path of piety.**
