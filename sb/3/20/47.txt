====== SB 3.20.47 ======

> देहेन वै भोगवता शयानो बहुचिन्तया ।
> सर्गेऽनुपचिते क्रोधादुत्ससर्ज ह तद्वपुः ॥४७॥

===== Text =====

> dehena vai bhogavatā
> śayāno bahu-cintayā
> sarge 'nupacite krodhād
> utsasarja ha tad vapuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dehena]]---with his body; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:b:bhogavatā]]---stretching out full length; [[synonyms:ś:śayānaḥ]]---lying fully stretched; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---great; [[synonyms:c:cintayā]]---with concern; [[synonyms:s:sarge]]---the creation; [[synonyms:a:anupacite]]---not proceeded; [[synonyms:k:krodhāt]]---out of anger; [[synonyms:u:utsasarja]]---gave up; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---in fact; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vapuḥ]]---body.

===== Translation =====

**Once Brahmā lay down with his body stretched at full length. He was very concerned that the work of creation had not proceeded apace, and in a sullen mood he gave up that body too.**
