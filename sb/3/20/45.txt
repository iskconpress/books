====== SB 3.20.45 ======

> स किन्नरान्किम्पुरुषान्प्रत्यात्म्येनासृजत्प्रभुः ।
> मानयन्नात्मनात्मानमात्माभासं विलोकयन् ॥४५॥

===== Text =====

> sa kinnarān kimpuruṣān
> pratyātmyenāsṛjat prabhuḥ
> mānayann ātmanātmānam
> ātmābhāsaṁ vilokayan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:k:kinnarān]]---the Kinnaras; [[synonyms:k:kimpuruṣān]]---the Kimpuruṣas; [[synonyms:p:pratyātmyena]]---from his reflection (in water); [[synonyms:a:asṛjat]]---created; [[synonyms:p:prabhuḥ]]---the lord of the living beings (Brahmā); [[synonyms:m:mānayan]]---admiring; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanā]] [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---himself by himself; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsam]]---his reflection; [[synonyms:v:vilokayan]]---seeing.

===== Translation =====

**One day, Brahmā, the creator of the living entities, beheld his own reflection in the water, and admiring himself, he evolved Kimpuruṣas as well as Kinnaras out of that reflection.**
