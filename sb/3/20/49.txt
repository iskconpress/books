====== SB 3.20.49 ======

> स आत्मानं मन्यमानः कृतकृत्यमिवात्मभूः ।
> तदा मनून्ससर्जान्ते मनसा लोकभावनान् ॥४९॥

===== Text =====

> sa ātmānaṁ manyamānaḥ
> kṛta-kṛtyam ivātmabhūḥ
> tadā manūn sasarjānte
> manasā loka-bhāvanān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---himself; [[synonyms:m:manyamānaḥ]]---considering; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtyam]]---had accomplished the object of life; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---as if; [[synonyms:ā:ātmabhūḥ]]---born from the Supreme; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:m:manūn]]---the Manus; [[synonyms:s:sasarja]]---created; [[synonyms:a:ante]]---at the end; [[synonyms:m:manasā]]---from his mind; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---of the world; [[synonyms:b:bhāvanān]]---promoting the welfare.

===== Translation =====

**One day Brahmā, the self-born, the first living creature, felt as if the object of his life had been accomplished. At that time he evolved from his mind the Manus, who promote the welfare activities or the universe.**
