====== SB 3.20.15 ======

> सोऽशयिष्टाब्धिसलिले आण्डकोशो निरात्मकः ।
> साग्रं वै वर्षसाहस्रमन्ववात्सीत्तमीश्वरः ॥१५॥

===== Text =====

> so 'śayiṣṭābdhi-salile
> āṇḍakośo nirātmakaḥ
> sāgraṁ vai varṣa-sāhasram
> anvavātsīt tam īśvaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---it; [[synonyms:a:aśayiṣṭa]]---lay; [[synonyms:a:abdhi]]-[[synonyms:s:salile]]---on the waters of the Causal Ocean; [[synonyms:ā:āṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:k:kośaḥ]]---egg; [[synonyms:n:nirātmakaḥ]]---in an unconscious state; [[synonyms:s:sāgram]]---a little more than; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---in fact; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:sāhasram]]---a thousand years; [[synonyms:a:anvavātsīt]]---became situated; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---in the egg; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**For over one thousand years the shiny egg lay on the waters of the Causal Ocean in the lifeless state. Then the Lord entered it as Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu.**

===== Purport =====

From this verse it appears that all the universes are floating in the Causal Ocean.
