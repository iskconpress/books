====== SB 3.20.32 ======

> अहो रूपमहो धैर्यमहो अस्या नवं वयः ।
> मध्ये कामयमानानामकामेव विसर्पति ॥३२॥

===== Text =====

> aho rūpam aho dhairyam
> aho asyā navaṁ vayaḥ
> madhye kāmayamānānām
> akāmeva visarpati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aho]]---oh; [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---what beauty; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---oh; [[synonyms:d:dhairyam]]---what self-control; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---oh; [[synonyms:a:asyāḥ]]---her; [[synonyms:n:navam]]---budding; [[synonyms:v:vayaḥ]]---youth; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the midst; [[synonyms:k:kāmayamānānām]]---of those passionately longing for; [[synonyms:a:akāmā]]---free from passion; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:v:visarpati]]---walking with us.

===== Translation =====

**The demons praised her: Oh, what a beauty! What rare self-control! What a budding youth! In the midst of us all, who are passionately longing for her, she is moving about like one absolutely free from passion.**
