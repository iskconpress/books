====== SB 3.20.14 ======

> तानि चैकैकशः स्रष्टुमसमर्थानि भौतिकम् ।
> संहत्य दैवयोगेन हैममण्डमवासृजन् ॥१४॥

===== Text =====

> tāni caikaikaśaḥ sraṣṭum
> asamarthāni bhautikam
> saṁhatya daiva-yogena
> haimam aṇḍam avāsṛjan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāni]]---those elements; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:e:ekaśaḥ]]---separately; [[synonyms:s:sraṣṭum]]---to produce; [[synonyms:a:asamarthāni]]---unable; [[synonyms:b:bhautikam]]---the material universe; [[synonyms:s:saṁhatya]]---having combined; [[synonyms:d:daiva]]-[[synonyms:y:yogena]]---with the energy of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:h:haimam]]---shining like gold; [[synonyms:a:aṇḍam]]---globe; [[synonyms:a:avāsṛjan]]---produced.

===== Translation =====

**Separately unable to produce the material universe, they combined with the help of the energy of the Supreme Lord and were able to produce a shining egg.**
