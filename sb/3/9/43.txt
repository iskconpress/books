====== SB 3.9.43 ======

> सर्ववेदमयेनेदमात्मनात्मात्मयोनिना ।
> प्रजाः सृज यथापूर्वं याश्च मय्यनुशेरते ॥४३॥

===== Text =====

> sarva-veda-mayenedam
> ātmanātmātma-yoninā
> prajāḥ sṛja yathā-pūrvaṁ
> yāś ca mayy anuśerate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:m:mayena]]---under complete Vedic wisdom; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanā]]---by the body; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---you; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:y:yoninā]]---directly born of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prajāḥ]]---living entities; [[synonyms:s:sṛja]]---generate; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrvam]]---as it was hereinbefore; [[synonyms:y:yāḥ]]---which; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:m:mayi]]---in Me; [[synonyms:a:anuśerate]]---lie.

===== Translation =====

**By following My instructions you can now generate the living entities as before, by dint of your complete Vedic wisdom and the body you have directly received from Me, the supreme cause of everything.**
