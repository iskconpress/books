====== SB 3.22.22 ======

> सोऽनु ज्ञात्वा व्यवसितं महिष्या दुहितुः स्फुटम् ।
> तस्मै गुणगणाढ्याय ददौ तुल्यां प्रहर्षितः ॥२२॥

===== Text =====

> so 'nu jñātvā vyavasitaṁ
> mahiṣyā duhituḥ sphuṭam
> tasmai guṇa-gaṇāḍhyāya
> dadau tulyāṁ praharṣitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Emperor Manu); [[synonyms:a:anu]]---afterward; [[synonyms:j:jñātvā]]---having known; [[synonyms:v:vyavasitam]]---the fixed decision; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣyāḥ]]---of the Queen; [[synonyms:d:duhituḥ]]---of his daughter; [[synonyms:s:sphuṭam]]---clearly; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---to him; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āḍhyāya]]---who was endowed with a host of virtues; [[synonyms:d:dadau]]---gave away; [[synonyms:t:tulyām]]---who was equal (in good qualities); [[synonyms:p:praharṣitaḥ]]---extremely pleased.

===== Translation =====

**After having unmistakably known the decision of the Queen, as well as that of Devahūti, the Emperor most gladly gave his daughter to the sage, whose host of virtues was equaled by hers.**
