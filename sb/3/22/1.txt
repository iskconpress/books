====== SB 3.22.1 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> एवमाविष्कृताशेष गुणकर्मोदयो मुनिम् ।
> सव्रीड इव तं सम्राडुपारतमुवाच ह ॥१॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> evam āviṣkṛtāśeṣa-
> guṇa-karmodayo munim
> savrīḍa iva taṁ samrāḍ
> upāratam uvāca ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]]---the great sage Maitreya; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣkṛta]]---having been described; [[synonyms:a:aśeṣa]]---all; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---of the virtues; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---of the activities; [[synonyms:u:udayaḥ]]---the greatness; [[synonyms:m:munim]]---the great sage; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vrīḍaḥ]]---feeling modest; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---as though; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Kardama); [[synonyms:s:samrāṭ]]---Emperor Manu; [[synonyms:u:upāratam]]---silent; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]---addressed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Maitreya said: After describing the greatness of the Emperor's manifold qualities and activities, the sage became silent, and the Emperor, feeling modesty, addressed him as follows.**
