====== SB 3.26.63 ======

> वह्निर्वाचा मुखं भेजे नोदतिष्ठत्तदा विराट् ।
> घ्राणेन नासिके वायुर्नोदतिष्ठत्तदा विराट् ॥६३॥

===== Text =====

> vahnir vācā mukhaṁ bheje
> nodatiṣṭhat tadā virāṭ
> ghrāṇena nāsike vāyur
> nodatiṣṭhat tadā virāṭ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vahniḥ]]---the god of fire; [[synonyms:v:vācā]]---with the organ of speech; [[synonyms:m:mukham]]---the mouth; [[synonyms:b:bheje]]---entered; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:u:udatiṣṭhat]]---did arise; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:v:virāṭ]]---the //virāṭ-puruṣa//; [[synonyms:g:ghrāṇena]]---with the olfactory sense; [[synonyms:n:nāsike]]---into His two nostrils; [[synonyms:v:vāyuḥ]]---the god of the winds; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:u:udatiṣṭhat]]---did arise; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:v:virāṭ]]---the //virāṭ-puruṣa.//

===== Translation =====

**The god of fire entered His mouth with the organ of speech, but the virāṭ-puruṣa could not be aroused. Then the god of wind entered His nostrils with the sense of smell, but still the virāṭ-puruṣa refused to be awakened.**
