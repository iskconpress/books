====== SB 3.26.64 ======

> अक्षिणी चक्षुषादित्यो नोदतिष्ठत्तदा विराट् ।
> श्रोत्रेण कर्णौ च दिशो नोदतिष्ठत्तदा विराट् ॥६४॥

===== Text =====

> akṣiṇī cakṣuṣādityo
> nodatiṣṭhat tadā virāṭ
> śrotreṇa karṇau ca diśo
> nodatiṣṭhat tadā virāṭ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:akṣiṇī]]---His two eyes; [[synonyms:c:cakṣuṣā]]---with the sense of sight; [[synonyms:ā:ādityaḥ]]---the sun-god; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:u:udatiṣṭhat]]---did arise; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:v:virāṭ]]---the //virāṭ-puruṣa//; [[synonyms:ś:śrotreṇa]]---with the sense of hearing; [[synonyms:k:karṇau]]---His two ears; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:d:diśaḥ]]---the deities presiding over the directions; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:u:udatiṣṭhat]]---did arise; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:v:virāṭ]]---the //virāṭ-puruṣa.//

===== Translation =====

**The sun-god entered the eyes of the virāṭ-puruṣa with the sense of sight, but still the virāṭ-puruṣa did not get up. Similarly, the predominating deities of the directions entered through His ears with the sense of hearing, but still He did not get up.**
