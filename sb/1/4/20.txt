====== SB 1.4.20 ======

> ऋग्यजुःसामाथर्वाख्या वेदाश्चत्वार उद्धृताः ।
> इतिहासपुराणं च पञ्चमो वेद उच्यते ॥२०॥

===== Text =====

> ṛg-yajuḥ-sāmātharvākhyā
> vedāś catvāra uddhṛtāḥ
> itihāsa-purāṇaṁ ca
> pañcamo veda ucyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛg]]-[[synonyms:y:yajuḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sāma]]-[[synonyms:a:atharva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyāḥ]]---the names of the four //Vedas//; [[synonyms:v:vedāḥ]]---the //Vedas//; [[synonyms:c:catvāraḥ]]---four; [[synonyms:u:uddhṛtāḥ]]---made into separate parts; [[synonyms:i:itihāsa]]---historical records (//Mahābhārata//); [[synonyms:p:purāṇam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and the //Purāṇas//; [[synonyms:p:pañcamaḥ]]---the fifth; [[synonyms:v:vedaḥ]]---the original source of knowledge; [[synonyms:u:ucyate]]---is said to be.

===== Translation =====

**The four divisions of the original sources of knowledge [the Vedas] were made separately. But the historical facts and authentic stories mentioned in the Purāṇas are called the fifth Veda.**
