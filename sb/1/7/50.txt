====== SB 1.7.50 ======

> नकुलः सहदेवश्च युयुधानो धनञ्जयः ।
> भगवान्देवकीपुत्रो ये चान्ये याश्च योषितः ॥५०॥

===== Text =====

> nakulaḥ sahadevaś ca
> yuyudhāno dhanañjayaḥ
> bhagavān devakī-putro
> ye cānye yāś ca yoṣitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nakulaḥ]]---Nakula; [[synonyms:s:sahadevaḥ]]---Sahadeva; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yuyudhānaḥ]]---Sātyaki; [[synonyms:d:dhanañjayaḥ]]---Arjuna; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:devakī]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son of Devakī, Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---those; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:anye]]---others; [[synonyms:y:yāḥ]]---those; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yoṣitaḥ]]---ladies.

===== Translation =====

**Nakula and Sahadeva [the younger brothers of the King] and also Sātyaki, Arjuna, the Personality of Godhead Lord Sri Kṛṣṇa, son of Devakī, and the ladies and others all unanimously agreed with the King.**
