====== SB 1.7.29 ======

> सूत उवाच
> श्रुत्वा भगवता प्रोक्तं फाल्गुनः परवीरहा ।
> स्पृष्ट्वापस्तं परिक्रम्य ब्राह्मं ब्राह्मास्त्रं सन्दधे ॥२९॥

===== Text =====

> sūta uvāca
> śrutvā bhagavatā proktaṁ
> phālgunaḥ para-vīra-hā
> spṛṣṭvāpas taṁ parikramya
> brāhmaṁ brāhmāstraṁ sandadhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūtaḥ]]---Sūta Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhagavatā]]---by the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:proktam]]---what was said; [[synonyms:p:phālgunaḥ]]---another name of Śrī Arjuna; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vīra]]-[[synonyms:h:hā]]---the killer of the opposing warrior; [[synonyms:s:spṛṣṭvā]]---after touching; [[synonyms:ā:āpaḥ]]---water; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---Him; [[synonyms:p:parikramya]]---circumambulating; [[synonyms:b:brāhmam]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:b:brāhma]]-[[synonyms:a:astram]]---the supreme weapon; [[synonyms:s:sandadhe]]---acted on.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said: Hearing this from the Personality of Godhead, Arjuna touched water for purification, and after circumambulating Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa, he cast his brahmāstra weapon to counteract the other one.**
