====== SB 1.7.3 ======

> तस्मिन्स्व आश्रमे व्यासो बदरीषण्डमण्डिते ।
> आसीनोऽप उपस्पृश्य प्रणिदध्यौ मनः स्वयम् ॥३॥

===== Text =====

> tasmin sva āśrame vyāso
> badarī-ṣaṇḍa-maṇḍite
> āsīno 'pa upaspṛśya
> praṇidadhyau manaḥ svayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmin]]---in that (//āśrama//); [[synonyms:s:sve]]---own; [[synonyms:ā:āśrame]]---in the cottage; [[synonyms:v:vyāsaḥ]]---Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:b:badarī]]---berry; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṇḍa]]---trees; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍite]]---surrounded by; [[synonyms:ā:āsīnaḥ]]---sitting; [[synonyms:a:apaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:upaspṛśya]]---touching water; [[synonyms:p:praṇidadhyau]]---concentrated; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]---the mind; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---himself.

===== Translation =====

**In that place, Śrīla Vyāsadeva, in his own āśrama, which was surrounded by berry trees, sat down to meditate after touching water for purification.**

===== Purport =====

Under instructions of his spiritual master Śrīla Nārada Muni, Vyāsadeva concentrated his mind in that transcendental place of meditation.
