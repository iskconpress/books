====== SB 1.7.57 ======

> वपनं द्रविणादानं स्थानान्निर्यापणं तथा ।
> एष हि ब्रह्मबन्धूनां वधो नान्योऽस्ति दैहिकः ॥५७॥

===== Text =====

> vapanaṁ draviṇādānaṁ
> sthānān niryāpaṇaṁ tathā
> eṣa hi brahma-bandhūnāṁ
> vadho nānyo 'sti daihikaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vapanam]]---cleaving the hairs from the head; [[synonyms:d:draviṇa]]---wealth; [[synonyms:a:adānam]]---forfeiting; [[synonyms:s:sthānāt]]---from the residence; [[synonyms:n:niryāpaṇam]]---driving away; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---also; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---all these; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhūnām]]---of the relatives of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vadhaḥ]]---killing; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:anyaḥ]]---any other method; [[synonyms:a:asti]]---there is; [[synonyms:d:daihikaḥ]]---in the matter of the body.

===== Translation =====

**Cutting the hair from his head, depriving him of his wealth and driving him from his residence are the prescribed punishments for the relative of a brāhmaṇa. There is no injunction for killing the body.**
