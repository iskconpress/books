====== SB 1.14.6 ======

> युधिष्ठिर उवाच
> सम्प्रेषितो द्वारकायां जिष्णुर्बन्धुदिदृक्षयाज् ।
> ञातुं च पुण्यश्लोकस्य कृष्णस्य च विचेष्टितम् ॥६॥

===== Text =====

> yudhiṣṭhira uvāca
> sampreṣito dvārakāyāṁ
> jiṣṇur bandhu-didṛkṣayā
> jñātuṁ ca puṇya-ślokasya
> kṛṣṇasya ca viceṣṭitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yudhiṣṭhiraḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira said; [[synonyms:s:sampreṣitaḥ]]---has gone to; [[synonyms:d:dvārakāyām]]---Dvārakā; [[synonyms:j:jiṣṇuḥ]]---Arjuna; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]---friends; [[synonyms:d:didṛkṣayā]]---for the sake of meeting; [[synonyms:j:jñātum]]---to know; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:p:puṇya]]-[[synonyms:ś:ślokasya]]---of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇasya]]---of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:viceṣṭitam]]---program of work.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira said to his younger brother Bhīmasena, I sent Arjuna to Dvārakā to meet his friends and to learn from the Personality of Godhead Kṛṣṇa of His program of work.**
