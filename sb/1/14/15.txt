====== SB 1.14.15 ======

> धूम्रा दिशः परिधयः कम्पते भूः सहाद्रिभिः ।
> निर्घातश्च महांस्तात साकं च स्तनयित्नुभिः ॥१५॥

===== Text =====

> dhūmrā diśaḥ paridhayaḥ
> kampate bhūḥ sahādribhiḥ
> nirghātaś ca mahāṁs tāta
> sākaṁ ca stanayitnubhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhūmrāḥ]]---smoky; [[synonyms:d:diśaḥ]]---all directions; [[synonyms:p:paridhayaḥ]]---encirclement; [[synonyms:k:kampate]]---throbbing; [[synonyms:b:bhūḥ]]---the earth; [[synonyms:s:saha]] [[synonyms:a:adribhiḥ]]---along with the hills and mountains; [[synonyms:n:nirghātaḥ]]---bolt from the blue; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:m:mahān]]---very great; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---O Bhīma; [[synonyms:s:sākam]]---with; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:stanayitnubhiḥ]]---thundering sound without any cloud.

===== Translation =====

**Just see how the smoke encircles the sky. It appears that the earth and mountains are throbbing. Just hear the cloudless thunder and see the bolts from the blue.**
