====== SB 1.6.6 ======

> एकात्मजा मे जननी योषिन्मूढा च किङ्करी ।
> मय्यात्मजेऽनन्यगतौ चक्रे स्नेहानुबन्धनम् ॥६॥

===== Text =====

> ekātmajā me jananī
> yoṣin mūḍhā ca kiṅkarī
> mayy ātmaje 'nanya-gatau
> cakre snehānubandhanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajā]]---having only one son; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:j:jananī]]---mother; [[synonyms:y:yoṣit]]---woman by class; [[synonyms:m:mūḍhā]]---foolish; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kiṅkarī]]---maidservant; [[synonyms:m:mayi]]---unto me; [[synonyms:ā:ātmaje]]---being her offspring; [[synonyms:a:ananya]]-[[synonyms:g:gatau]]---one who has no alternative for protection; [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---did it; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]-[[synonyms:a:anubandhanam]]---tied by affectionate bondage.

===== Translation =====

**I was the only son of my mother, who was not only a simple woman but a maidservant as well. Since I was her only offspring, she had no other alternative for protection: she bound me with the tie of affection.**
