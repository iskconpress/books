====== SB 1.13.12 ======

> इत्युक्तो धर्मराजेन सर्वं तत्समवर्णयत् ।
> यथानुभूतं क्रमशो विना यदुकुलक्षयम् ॥१२॥

===== Text =====

> ity ukto dharma-rājena
> sarvaṁ tat samavarṇayat
> yathānubhūtaṁ kramaśo
> vinā yadu-kula-kṣayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:u:uktaḥ]]---being asked; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:r:rājena]]---by King Yudhiṣṭhira; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:s:samavarṇayat]]---properly described; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhūtam]]---as he experienced; [[synonyms:k:kramaśaḥ]]---one after another; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:y:yadu]]-[[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣayam]]---annihilation of the Yadu dynasty.

===== Translation =====

**Thus being questioned by Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira, Mahātmā Vidura gradually described everything he had personally experienced, except news of the annihilation of the Yadu dynasty.**
