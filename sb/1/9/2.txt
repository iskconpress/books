====== SB 1.9.2 ======

> तदा ते भ्रातरः सर्वे सदश्वैः स्वर्णभूषितैः ।
> अन्वगच्छन्रथैर्विप्रा व्यासधौम्यादयस्तथा ॥२॥

===== Text =====

> tadā te bhrātaraḥ sarve
> sadaśvaiḥ svarṇa-bhūṣitaiḥ
> anvagacchan rathair viprā
> vyāsa-dhaumyādayas tathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:bhrātaraḥ]]---the brothers; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all together; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:a:aśvaiḥ]]---drawn by first-class horses; [[synonyms:s:svarṇa]]---gold; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣitaiḥ]]---being decorated with; [[synonyms:a:anvagacchan]]---followed one after another; [[synonyms:r:rathaiḥ]]---on the chariots; [[synonyms:v:viprāḥ]]---O //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---the sage Vyāsa; [[synonyms:d:dhaumya]]---Dhaumya; [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and others; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**At that time all his brothers followed him on beautiful chariots drawn by first-class horses decorated with gold ornaments. With them were Vyāsa and ṛṣis like Dhaumya [the learned priest of the Pāṇḍavas] and others.**
