====== SB 1.15.18 ======

> नर्माण्युदाररुचिरस्मितशोभितानि हे पार्थ हेऽर्जुन सखे कुरुनन्दनेति ।
> सञ्जल्पितानि नरदेव हृदिस्पृशानि स्मर्तुर्लुठन्ति हृदयं मम माधवस्य ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> narmāṇy udāra-rucira-smita-śobhitāni
> he pārtha he 'rjuna sakhe kuru-nandaneti
> sañjalpitāni nara-deva hṛdi-spṛśāni
> smartur luṭhanti hṛdayaṁ mama mādhavasya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:narmāṇi]]---conversation in jokes; [[synonyms:u:udāra]]---talked very frankly; [[synonyms:r:rucira]]---pleasing; [[synonyms:s:smita]]-[[synonyms:ś:śobhitāni]]---decorated with a smiling face; [[synonyms:h:he]]---note of address; [[synonyms:p:pārtha]]---O son of Pṛthā; [[synonyms:h:he]]---note of address; [[synonyms:a:arjuna]]---Arjuna; [[synonyms:s:sakhe]]---friend; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---son of the Kuru dynasty; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---and so on; [[synonyms:s:sañjalpitāni]]---such conversation; [[synonyms:n:nara]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---O King; [[synonyms:h:hṛdi]]---heart; [[synonyms:s:spṛśāni]]---touching; [[synonyms:s:smartuḥ]]---by remembering them; [[synonyms:l:luṭhanti]]---overwhelms; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayam]]---heart and soul; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---my; [[synonyms:m:mādhavasya]]---of Mādhava (Kṛṣṇa).

===== Translation =====

**O King! His jokings and frank talks were pleasing and beautifully decorated with smiles. His addresses unto me as "O son of Pṛthā, O friend, O son of the Kuru dynasty," and all such heartiness are now remembered by me, and thus I am overwhelmed.**
