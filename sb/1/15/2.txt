====== SB 1.15.2 ======

> शोकेन शुष्यद्वदन हृत्सरोजो हतप्रभः ।
> विभुं तमेवानुस्मरन्नाशक्नोत्प्रतिभाषितुम् ॥२॥

===== Text =====

> śokena śuṣyad-vadana-
> hṛt-sarojo hata-prabhaḥ
> vibhuṁ tam evānusmaran
> nāśaknot pratibhāṣitum

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śokena]]---due to bereavement; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣyat]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---drying up of the mouth; [[synonyms:h:hṛt]]-[[synonyms:s:sarojaḥ]]---lotuslike heart; [[synonyms:h:hata]]---lost; [[synonyms:p:prabhaḥ]]---bodily luster; [[synonyms:v:vibhum]]---the Supreme; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:anusmaran]]---thinking within; [[synonyms:n:na]]---could not; [[synonyms:a:aśaknot]]---be able; [[synonyms:p:pratibhāṣitum]]---properly replying.

===== Translation =====

**Due to grief, Arjuna's mouth and lotuslike heart had dried up. Therefore his body lost all luster. Now, remembering the Supreme Lord, he could hardly utter a word in reply.**
