====== SB 1.3.18 ======

> चतुर्दशं नारसिंहं बिभ्रद्दैत्येन्द्रमूर्जितम् ।
> ददार करजैरूरावेरकां कटकृद्यथा ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> caturdaśaṁ nārasiṁhaṁ
> bibhrad daityendram ūrjitam
> dadāra karajair ūrāv
> erakāṁ kaṭa-kṛd yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturdaśam]]---the fourteenth in the line; [[synonyms:n:nāra]]-[[synonyms:s:siṁham]]---the incarnation of the Lord as half-man and half-lion; [[synonyms:b:bibhrat]]---advented; [[synonyms:d:daitya]]-[[synonyms:i:indram]]---the king of the atheists; [[synonyms:ū:ūrjitam]]---strongly built; [[synonyms:d:dadāra]]---bifurcated; [[synonyms:k:karajaiḥ]]---by the nails; [[synonyms:ū:ūrau]]---on the lap; [[synonyms:e:erakām]]---canes; [[synonyms:k:kaṭa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛt]]---carpenter; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just like.

===== Translation =====

**In the fourteenth incarnation, the Lord appeared as Nṛsiṁha and bifurcated the strong body of the atheist Hiraṇyakaśipu with His nails, just as a carpenter pierces cane.**
