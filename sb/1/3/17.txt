====== SB 1.3.17 ======

> धान्वन्तरं द्वादशमं त्रयोदशममेव च ।
> अपाययत्सुरानन्यान्मोहिन्या मोहयन्स्त्रिया ॥१७॥

===== Text =====

> dhānvantaraṁ dvādaśamaṁ
> trayodaśamam eva ca
> apāyayat surān anyān
> mohinyā mohayan striyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhānvantaram]]---the incarnation of Godhead named Dhanvantari; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśamam]]---the twelfth in the line; [[synonyms:t:trayodaśamam]]---the thirteenth in the line; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:apāyayat]]---gave to drink; [[synonyms:s:surān]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:a:anyān]]---others; [[synonyms:m:mohinyā]]---by charming beauty; [[synonyms:m:mohayan]]---alluring; [[synonyms:s:striyā]]---in the form of a woman.

===== Translation =====

**In the twelfth incarnation, the Lord appeared as Dhanvantari, and in the thirteenth He allured the atheists by the charming beauty of a woman and gave nectar to the demigods to drink.**
