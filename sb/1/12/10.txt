====== SB 1.12.10 ======

> अस्त्रतेजः स्वगदया नीहारमिव गोपतिः ।
> विधमन्तं सन्निकर्षे पर्यैक्षत क इत्यसौ ॥१०॥

===== Text =====

> astra-tejaḥ sva-gadayā
> nīhāram iva gopatiḥ
> vidhamantaṁ sannikarṣe
> paryaikṣata ka ity asau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:astra]]-[[synonyms:t:tejaḥ]]---radiation of the //brahmāstra//; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:gadayā]]---by means of His own club; [[synonyms:n:nīhāram]]---drops of dew; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:g:gopatiḥ]]---the sun; [[synonyms:v:vidhamantam]]---the act of vanishing; [[synonyms:s:sannikarṣe]]---nearby; [[synonyms:p:paryaikṣata]]---observing; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:i:iti]] [[synonyms:a:asau]]---this body.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord was thus engaged in vanquishing the radiation of the brahmāstra, just as the sun evaporates a drop of dew. He was observed by the child, who thought about who He was.**
