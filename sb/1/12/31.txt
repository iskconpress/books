====== SB 1.12.31 ======

> स राजपुत्रो ववृधे आशु शुक्ल इवोडुपः ।
> आपूर्यमाणः पितृभिः काष्ठाभिरिव सोऽन्वहम् ॥३१॥

===== Text =====

> sa rāja-putro vavṛdhe
> āśu śukla ivoḍupaḥ
> āpūryamāṇaḥ pitṛbhiḥ
> kāṣṭhābhir iva so 'nvaham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the royal prince; [[synonyms:v:vavṛdhe]]---grew up; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]---very soon; [[synonyms:ś:śukle]]---waxing moon; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:u:uḍupaḥ]]---the moon; [[synonyms:ā:āpūryamāṇaḥ]]---luxuriantly; [[synonyms:p:pitṛbhiḥ]]---by the parental guardians; [[synonyms:k:kāṣṭhābhiḥ]]---plenary development; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:a:anvaham]]---day after day.

===== Translation =====

**As the moon, in its waxing fortnight, develops day after day, so the royal prince [Parīkṣit] very soon developed luxuriantly under the care and full facilities of his guardian grandfathers.**
