====== SB 1.10.31 ======

> एवंविधा गदन्तीनां स गिरः पुरयोषिताम् ।
> निरीक्षणेनाभिनन्दन्सस्मितेन ययौ हरिः ॥३१॥

===== Text =====

> evaṁvidhā gadantīnāṁ
> sa giraḥ pura-yoṣitām
> nirīkṣaṇenābhinandan
> sasmitena yayau hariḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evaṁvidhāḥ]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gadantīnām]]---thus praying and talking about Him; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He (the Lord); [[synonyms:g:giraḥ]]---of words; [[synonyms:p:pura]]-[[synonyms:y:yoṣitām]]---of the ladies of the capital; [[synonyms:n:nirīkṣaṇena]]---by His grace of glancing over them; [[synonyms:a:abhinandan]]---and greeting them; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:s:smitena]]---with a smiling face; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---departed; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**While the ladies of the capital of Hastināpura were greeting Him and talking in this way, the Lord, smiling, accepted their good greetings, and casting the grace of His glance over them, He departed from the city.**
