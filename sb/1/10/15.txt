====== SB 1.10.15 ======

> मृदङ्गशङ्खभेर्यश्च वीणापणवगोमुखाः ।
> धुन्धुर्यानकघण्टाद्या नेदुर्दुन्दुभयस्तथा ॥१५॥

===== Text =====

> mṛdaṅga-śaṅkha-bheryaś ca
> vīṇā-paṇava-gomukhāḥ
> dhundhury-ānaka-ghaṇṭādyā
> nedur dundubhayas tathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mṛdaṅga]]---sweet sounding drum; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---conchshell; [[synonyms:b:bheryaḥ]]---brass band; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vīṇā]]---string band; [[synonyms:p:paṇava]]---a kind of flute; [[synonyms:g:gomukhāḥ]]---another flute; [[synonyms:d:dhundhurī]]---another drum; [[synonyms:ā:ānaka]]---kettle; [[synonyms:g:ghaṇṭā]]---bell; [[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---others; [[synonyms:n:neduḥ]]---sounded; [[synonyms:d:dundubhayaḥ]]---other different types of drums; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---at that time.

===== Translation =====

**While the Lord was departing from the palace of Hastināpura, different types of drums -like the mṛdaṅga, dhola, nagra, dhundhurī and dundubhi-and flutes of different types, the vīṇā, gomukha and bherī, all sounded together to show Him honor.**
