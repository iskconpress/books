====== SB 1.10.18 ======

> उद्धवः सात्यकिश्चैव व्यजने परमाद्भुते ।
> विकीर्यमाणः कुसुमै रेजे मधुपतिः पथि ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> uddhavaḥ sātyakiś caiva
> vyajane paramādbhute
> vikīryamāṇaḥ kusumai
> reje madhu-patiḥ pathi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uddhavaḥ]]---a cousin-brother of Kṛṣṇa's; [[synonyms:s:sātyakiḥ]]---His driver; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:v:vyajane]]---engaged in fanning; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:a:adbhute]]---decorative; [[synonyms:v:vikīryamāṇaḥ]]---seated on scattered; [[synonyms:k:kusumaiḥ]]---flowers all around; [[synonyms:r:reje]]---commanded; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the master of Madhu (Kṛṣṇa); [[synonyms:p:pathi]]---on the road.

===== Translation =====

**Uddhava and Sātyaki began to fan the Lord with decorated fans, and the Lord, as the master of Madhu, seated on scattered flowers, commanded them along the road.**
