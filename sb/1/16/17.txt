====== SB 1.16.17 ======

> तस्यैवं वर्तमानस्य पूर्वेषां वृत्तिमन्वहम् ।
> नातिदूरे किलाश्चर्यं यदासीत्तन्निबोध मे ॥१७॥

===== Text =====

> tasyaivaṁ vartamānasya
> pūrveṣāṁ vṛttim anvaham
> nātidūre kilāścaryaṁ
> yad āsīt tan nibodha me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vartamānasya]]---remaining absorbed in such thought; [[synonyms:p:pūrveṣām]]---of his forefathers; [[synonyms:v:vṛttim]]---good engagement; [[synonyms:a:anvaham]]---day after day; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:d:dūre]]---far off; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---verily; [[synonyms:ā:āścaryam]]---astonishing; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---was; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---which; [[synonyms:n:nibodha]]---know it; [[synonyms:m:me]]---from me.

===== Translation =====

**Now you may hear from me of what happened while Mahārāja Parīkṣit was passing his days hearing of the good occupations of his forefathers and being absorbed in thought of them.**
