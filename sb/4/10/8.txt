====== SB 4.10.8 ======

> स तानापततो वीर उग्रधन्वा महारथः ।
> एकैकं युगपत्सर्वानहन्बाणैस्त्रिभिस्त्रिभिः ॥८॥

===== Text =====

> sa tān āpatato vīra
> ugra-dhanvā mahā-rathaḥ
> ekaikaṁ yugapat sarvān
> ahan bāṇais tribhis tribhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Dhruva Mahārāja; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āpatataḥ]]---falling upon him; [[synonyms:v:vīraḥ]]---hero; [[synonyms:u:ugra]]-[[synonyms:d:dhanvā]]---powerful bowman; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:r:rathaḥ]]---who could fight with many chariots; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:e:ekam]]---one after another; [[synonyms:y:yugapat]]---simultaneously; [[synonyms:s:sarvān]]---all of them; [[synonyms:a:ahan]]---killed; [[synonyms:b:bāṇaiḥ]]---by arrows; [[synonyms:t:tribhiḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tribhiḥ]]---by threes.

===== Translation =====

**Dhruva Mahārāja, who was a great charioteer and certainly a great bowman also, immediately began to kill them by simultaneously discharging arrows three at a time.**
