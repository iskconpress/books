====== SB 4.10.3 ======

> उत्तमस्त्वकृतोद्वाहो मृगयायां बलीयसा ।
> हतः पुण्यजनेनाद्रौ तन्मातास्य गतिं गता ॥३॥

===== Text =====

> uttamas tv akṛtodvāho
> mṛgayāyāṁ balīyasā
> hataḥ puṇya-janenādrau
> tan-mātāsya gatiṁ gatā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttamaḥ]]---Uttama; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:a:akṛta]]---without; [[synonyms:u:udvāhaḥ]]---marriage; [[synonyms:m:mṛgayāyām]]---on a hunting excursion; [[synonyms:b:balīyasā]]---very powerful; [[synonyms:h:hataḥ]]---was killed; [[synonyms:p:puṇya]]-[[synonyms:j:janena]]---by a Yakṣa; [[synonyms:a:adrau]]---on the Himalaya Mountains; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---mother (Suruci); [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of her son; [[synonyms:g:gatim]]---way; [[synonyms:g:gatā]]---followed.

===== Translation =====

**Dhruva Mahārāja's younger brother Uttama, who was still unmarried, once went on a hunting excursion and was killed by a powerful Yakṣa in the Himalaya Mountains. Along with him, his mother, Suruci, also followed the path of her son [she died].**
