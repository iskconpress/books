====== SB 4.10.22 ======

> इति ब्रुवंश्चित्ररथः स्वसारथिं यत्तः परेषां प्रतियोगशङ्कितः ।
> शुश्राव शब्दं जलधेरिवेरितं नभस्वतो दिक्षु रजोऽन्वदृश्यत ॥२२॥

===== Text =====

> iti bruvaṁś citra-rathaḥ sva-sārathiṁ
> yattaḥ pareṣāṁ pratiyoga-śaṅkitaḥ
> śuśrāva śabdaṁ jaladher iveritaṁ
> nabhasvato dikṣu rajo 'nvadṛśyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bruvan]]---talking; [[synonyms:c:citra]]-[[synonyms:r:rathaḥ]]---Dhruva Mahārāja, whose chariot was very beautiful; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:s:sārathim]]---to his charioteer; [[synonyms:y:yattaḥ]]---being on guard; [[synonyms:p:pareṣām]]---from his enemies; [[synonyms:p:pratiyoga]]---counterattack; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkitaḥ]]---being apprehensive; [[synonyms:ś:śuśrāva]]---heard; [[synonyms:ś:śabdam]]---sound; [[synonyms:j:jaladheḥ]]---from the ocean; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---as if; [[synonyms:ī:īritam]]---resounded; [[synonyms:n:nabhasvataḥ]]---because of wind; [[synonyms:d:dikṣu]]---in all directions; [[synonyms:r:rajaḥ]]---dust; [[synonyms:a:anu]]---then; [[synonyms:a:adṛśyata]]---was perceived.

===== Translation =====

**In the meantime, while Dhruva Mahārāja, doubtful of his mystic enemies, was talking with his charioteer, they heard a tremendous sound, as if the whole ocean were there, and they found that from the sky a great dust storm was coming over them from all directions.**
