====== SB 4.10.28 ======

> एवंविधान्यनेकानि त्रासनान्यमनस्विनाम् ।
> ससृजुस्तिग्मगतय आसुर्या माययासुराः ॥२८॥

===== Text =====

> evaṁ-vidhāny anekāni
> trāsanāny amanasvinām
> sasṛjus tigma-gataya
> āsuryā māyayāsurāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhāni]]---(phenomena) like this; [[synonyms:a:anekāni]]---many varieties of; [[synonyms:t:trāsanāni]]---fearful; [[synonyms:a:amanasvinām]]---to the less intelligent men; [[synonyms:s:sasṛjuḥ]]---they created; [[synonyms:t:tigma]]-[[synonyms:g:gatayaḥ]]---of heinous nature; [[synonyms:ā:āsuryā]]---demoniac; [[synonyms:m:māyayā]]---by illusion; [[synonyms:a:asurāḥ]]---the demons.

===== Translation =====

**The demon Yakṣas are by nature very heinous, and by their demoniac power of illusion they can create many strange phenomena to frighten one who is less intelligent.**
