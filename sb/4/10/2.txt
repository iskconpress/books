====== SB 4.10.2 ======

> इलायामपि भार्यायां वायोः पुत्र्यां महाबलः ।
> पुत्रमुत्कलनामानं योषिद्रत्नमजीजनत् ॥२॥

===== Text =====

> ilāyām api bhāryāyāṁ
> vāyoḥ putryāṁ mahā-balaḥ
> putram utkala-nāmānaṁ
> yoṣid-ratnam ajījanat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ilāyām]]---unto his wife named Ilā; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:b:bhāryāyām]]---unto his wife; [[synonyms:v:vāyoḥ]]---of the demigod Vāyu (controller of air); [[synonyms:p:putryām]]---unto the daughter; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:balaḥ]]---the greatly powerful Dhruva Mahārāja; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---son; [[synonyms:u:utkala]]---Utkala; [[synonyms:n:nāmānam]]---of the name; [[synonyms:y:yoṣit]]---female; [[synonyms:r:ratnam]]---jewel; [[synonyms:a:ajījanat]]---he begot.

===== Translation =====

**The greatly powerful Dhruva Mahārāja had another wife, named Ilā, who was the daughter of the demigod Vāyu. By her he begot a son named Utkala and a very beautiful daughter.**
