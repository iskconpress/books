====== SB 4.10.7 ======

> ततो निष्क्रम्य बलिन उपदेवमहाभटाः ।
> असहन्तस्तन्निनादमभिपेतुरुदायुधाः ॥७॥

===== Text =====

> tato niṣkramya balina
> upadeva-mahā-bhaṭāḥ
> asahantas tan-ninādam
> abhipetur udāyudhāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:n:niṣkramya]]---coming out; [[synonyms:b:balinaḥ]]---very powerful; [[synonyms:u:upadeva]]---of Kuvera; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭāḥ]]---great soldiers; [[synonyms:a:asahantaḥ]]---unable to tolerate; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of the conchshell; [[synonyms:n:ninādam]]---sound; [[synonyms:a:abhipetuḥ]]---attacked; [[synonyms:u:udāyudhāḥ]]---equipped with various weapons.

===== Translation =====

**O hero Vidura, the greatly powerful heroes of the Yakṣas, unable to tolerate the resounding vibration of the conchshell of Dhruva Mahārāja, came forth from their city with weapons and attacked Dhruva.**
