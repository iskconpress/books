====== SB 4.6.19-20 ======

> कुमुदोत्पलकह्लार शतपत्रवनर्द्धिभिः ।
> नलिनीषु कलं कूजत् खगवृन्दोपशोभितम् ॥१९॥
> मृगैः शाखामृगैः क्रोडैर्मृगेन्द्रैरृक्षशल्यकैः ।
> गवयैः शरभैर्व्याघ्रै रुरुभिर्महिषादिभिः ॥२०॥

===== Text =====

> kumudotpala-kahlāra-
> śatapatra-vanarddhibhiḥ
> nalinīṣu kalaṁ kūjat-
> khaga-vṛndopaśobhitam
>
> mṛgaiḥ śākhāmṛgaiḥ kroḍair
> mṛgendrair ṛkṣa-śalyakaiḥ
> gavayaiḥ śarabhair vyāghrai
> rurubhir mahiṣādibhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kumuda]]---//kumuda//; [[synonyms:u:utpala]]---//utpala//; [[synonyms:k:kahlāra]]---//kahlāra//; [[synonyms:ś:śatapatra]]---lotuses; [[synonyms:v:vana]]---forest; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛddhibhiḥ]]---being covered with; [[synonyms:n:nalinīṣu]]---in the lakes; [[synonyms:k:kalam]]---very sweetly; [[synonyms:k:kūjat]]---whispering; [[synonyms:k:khaga]]---of birds; [[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---groups; [[synonyms:u:upaśobhitam]]---decorated with; [[synonyms:m:mṛgaiḥ]]---with deer; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:m:mṛgaiḥ]]---with monkeys; [[synonyms:k:kroḍaiḥ]]---with boars; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:i:indraiḥ]]---with lions; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śalyakaiḥ]]---with //ṛkṣas// and //śalyakas//; [[synonyms:g:gavayaiḥ]]---with forest cows; [[synonyms:ś:śarabhaiḥ]]---with forest asses; [[synonyms:v:vyāghraiḥ]]---with tigers; [[synonyms:r:rurubhiḥ]]---with small deer; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---with buffalo, etc.

===== Translation =====

**There are different kinds of lotus flowers, such as kumuda, utpala and śatapatra. The forest appears to be a decorated garden, and the small lakes are full of various kinds of birds who whisper very sweetly. There are many kinds of other animals also, like deer, monkeys, boars, lions, ṛkṣas, śalyakas, forest cows, forest asses, tigers, small deer, buffalo and many other animals, who are fully enjoying their lives.**
