====== SB 4.6.18 ======

> खर्जूराम्रातकाम्राद्यैः प्रियालमधुकेङ्गुदैः ।
> द्रुमजातिभिरन्यैश्च राजितं वेणुकीचकैः ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> kharjūrāmrātakāmrādyaiḥ
> priyāla-madhukeṅgudaiḥ
> druma-jātibhir anyaiś ca
> rājitaṁ veṇu-kīcakaiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kharjūra]]-[[synonyms:ā:āmrātaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:āmra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyaiḥ]]---with //kharjūras, āmrātakas, āmras// and others; [[synonyms:p:priyāla]]-[[synonyms:m:madhuka]]-[[synonyms:i:iṅgudaiḥ]]---with //priyālas, madhukas// and //iṅgudas//; [[synonyms:d:druma]]-[[synonyms:j:jātibhiḥ]]---with varieties of trees; [[synonyms:a:anyaiḥ]]---other; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rājitam]]---decorated; [[synonyms:v:veṇu]]-[[synonyms:k:kīcakaiḥ]]---with //veṇus// (bamboos) and //kīcakas// (hollow bamboos).

===== Translation =====

**There are mango trees, priyāla, madhuka and iṅguda. Besides these there are other trees, like thin bamboos, kīcaka and varieties of other bamboo trees, all decorating the tract of Kailāsa Hill.**
