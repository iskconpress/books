====== SB 4.6.24 ======

> नन्दा चालकनन्दा च सरितौ बाह्यतः पुरः ।
> तीर्थपादपदाम्भोज रजसातीव पावने ॥२४॥

===== Text =====

> nandā cālakanandā ca
> saritau bāhyataḥ puraḥ
> tīrthapāda-padāmbhoja-
> rajasātīva pāvane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nandā]]---the Nandā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:alakanandā]]---the Alakanandā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:saritau]]---two rivers; [[synonyms:b:bāhyataḥ]]---outside; [[synonyms:p:puraḥ]]---from the city; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:a:ambhoja]]---of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:r:rajasā]]---by the dust; [[synonyms:a:atīva]]---exceedingly; [[synonyms:p:pāvane]]---sanctified.

===== Translation =====

**They also saw the two rivers named Nandā and Alakanandā. These two rivers are sanctified by the dust of the lotus feet of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Govinda.**
