====== SB 4.6.31 ======

> वैदूर्यकृतसोपाना वाप्य उत्पलमालिनीः ।
> प्राप्तं किम्पुरुषैर्दृष्ट्वा त आराद्ददृशुर्वटम् ॥३१॥

===== Text =====

> vaidūrya-kṛta-sopānā
> vāpya utpala-mālinīḥ
> prāptaṁ kimpuruṣair dṛṣṭvā
> ta ārād dadṛśur vaṭam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaidūrya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛta]]---made of //vaidūrya//; [[synonyms:s:sopānāḥ]]---staircases; [[synonyms:v:vāpyaḥ]]---lakes; [[synonyms:u:utpala]]---of lotus flowers; [[synonyms:m:mālinīḥ]]---containing rows; [[synonyms:p:prāptam]]---inhabited; [[synonyms:k:kimpuruṣaiḥ]]---by the Kimpuruṣas; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:t:te]]---those demigods; [[synonyms:ā:ārāt]]---not far away; [[synonyms:d:dadṛśuḥ]]---saw; [[synonyms:v:vaṭam]]---a banyan tree.

===== Translation =====

**They also saw that the bathing ghāṭas and their staircases were made of vaidūrya-maṇi. The water was full of lotus flowers. Passing by such lakes, the demigods reached a place where there was a great banyan tree.**
