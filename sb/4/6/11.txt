====== SB 4.6.11 ======

> नानामलप्रस्रवणैर्नानाकन्दरसानुभिः ।
> रमणं विहरन्तीनां रमणैः सिद्धयोषिताम् ॥११॥

===== Text =====

> nānāmala-prasravaṇair
> nānā-kandara-sānubhiḥ
> ramaṇaṁ viharantīnāṁ
> ramaṇaiḥ siddha-yoṣitām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:a:amala]]---transparent; [[synonyms:p:prasravaṇaiḥ]]---with waterfalls; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:k:kandara]]---caves; [[synonyms:s:sānubhiḥ]]---with summits; [[synonyms:r:ramaṇam]]---giving pleasure; [[synonyms:v:viharantīnām]]---sporting; [[synonyms:r:ramaṇaiḥ]]---with their lovers; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:y:yoṣitām]]---of the damsels of the mystics.

===== Translation =====

**There are many waterfalls, and in the mountains there are many beautiful caves in which the very beautiful wives of the mystics are found.**
