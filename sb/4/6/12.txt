====== SB 4.6.12 ======

> मयूरकेकाभिरुतं मदान्धालिविमूर्च्छितम् ।
> प्लावितै रक्तकण्ठानां कूजितैश्च पतत्त्रिणाम् ॥१२॥

===== Text =====

> mayūra-kekābhirutaṁ
> madāndhāli-vimūrcchitam
> plāvitai rakta-kaṇṭhānāṁ
> kūjitaiś ca patattriṇām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mayūra]]---peacocks; [[synonyms:k:kekā]]---with the cries; [[synonyms:a:abhirutam]]---resounding; [[synonyms:m:mada]]---by intoxication; [[synonyms:a:andha]]---blinded; [[synonyms:a:ali]]---by the bees; [[synonyms:v:vimūrcchitam]]---resounded; [[synonyms:p:plāvitaiḥ]]---with the singing; [[synonyms:r:rakta]]-[[synonyms:k:kaṇṭhānām]]---of the cuckoos; [[synonyms:k:kūjitaiḥ]]---with the whispering; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:patattriṇām]]---of other birds.

===== Translation =====

**On Kailāsa Hill there is always the rhythmical sound of the peacocks' sweet vibrations and the bees' humming. Cuckoos are always singing, and other birds whisper amongst themselves.**
