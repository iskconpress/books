====== SB 4.24.8 ======

> हविर्धानाद्धविर्धानी विदुरासूत षट्सुतान् ।
> बर्हिषदं गयं शुक्लं कृष्णं सत्यं जितव्रतम् ॥८॥

===== Text =====

> havirdhānād dhavirdhānī
> vidurāsūta ṣaṭ sutān
> barhiṣadaṁ gayaṁ śuklaṁ
> kṛṣṇaṁ satyaṁ jitavratam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:havirdhānāt]]---from Havirdhāna; [[synonyms:h:havirdhānī]]---the name of the wife of Havirdhāna; [[synonyms:v:vidura]]---O Vidura; [[synonyms:a:asūta]]---gave birth; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]---six; [[synonyms:s:sutān]]---sons; [[synonyms:b:barhiṣadam]]---of the name Barhiṣat; [[synonyms:g:gayam]]---of the name Gaya; [[synonyms:ś:śuklam]]---of the name Śukla; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇam]]---of the name Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:satyam]]---of the name Satya; [[synonyms:j:jitavratam]]---of the name Jitavrata.

===== Translation =====

**Havirdhāna, the son of Mahārāja Antardhāna, had a wife named Havirdhānī, who gave birth to six sons, named Barhiṣat, Gaya, Śukla, Kṛṣṇa, Satya and Jitavrata.**
