====== SB 4.24.2 ======

> हर्यक्षायादिशत्प्राचीं धूम्रकेशाय दक्षिणाम् ।
> प्रतीचीं वृकसंज्ञाय तुर्यां द्रविणसे विभुः ॥२॥

===== Text =====

> haryakṣāyādiśat prācīṁ
> dhūmrakeśāya dakṣiṇām
> pratīcīṁ vṛka-saṁjñāya
> turyāṁ draviṇase vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haryakṣāya]]---unto Haryakṣa; [[synonyms:a:adiśat]]---delivered; [[synonyms:p:prācīm]]---eastern; [[synonyms:d:dhūmrakeśāya]]---unto Dhūmrakeśa; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇām]]---the southern side; [[synonyms:p:pratīcīm]]---the western side; [[synonyms:v:vṛka]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁjñāya]]---unto his brother whose name was Vṛka; [[synonyms:t:turyām]]---the northern side; [[synonyms:d:draviṇase]]---unto another brother of his named Draviṇa; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the master.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Vijitāśva offered the eastern part of the world to his brother Haryakṣa, the southern part to Dhūmrakeśa, the western part to Vṛka and the northern part to Draviṇa.**
