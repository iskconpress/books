====== SB 4.1.32 ======

> एवं कामवरं दत्त्वा प्रतिजग्मुः सुरेश्वराः ।
> सभाजितास्तयोः सम्यग्दम्पत्योर्मिषतोस्ततः ॥३२॥

===== Text =====

> evaṁ kāma-varaṁ dattvā
> pratijagmuḥ sureśvarāḥ
> sabhājitās tayoḥ samyag
> dampatyor miṣatos tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:v:varam]]---desired benediction; [[synonyms:d:dattvā]]---offering; [[synonyms:p:pratijagmuḥ]]---returned; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvarāḥ]]---the chief demigods; [[synonyms:s:sabhājitāḥ]]---being worshiped; [[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---while they; [[synonyms:s:samyak]]---perfectly; [[synonyms:d:dampatyoḥ]]---the husband and wife; [[synonyms:m:miṣatoḥ]]---were looking on; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from there.

===== Translation =====

**Thus, while the couple looked on, the three deities Brahmā, Viṣṇu and Maheśvara disappeared from that place after bestowing upon Atri Muni the benediction.**
