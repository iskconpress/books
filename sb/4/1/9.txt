====== SB 4.1.9 ======

> प्रियव्रतोत्तानपादौ मनुपुत्रौ महौजसौ ।
> तत्पुत्रपौत्रनप्तॄणामनुवृत्तं तदन्तरम् ॥९॥

===== Text =====

> priyavratottānapādau
> manu-putrau mahaujasau
> tat-putra-pautra-naptṝṇām
> anuvṛttaṁ tad-antaram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:priyavrata]]---Priyavrata; [[synonyms:u:uttānapādau]]---Uttānapāda; [[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:p:putrau]]---sons of Manu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:o:ojasau]]---very great, powerful; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---their; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---sons; [[synonyms:p:pautra]]---grandsons; [[synonyms:n:naptṝṇām]]---grandsons from the daughter; [[synonyms:a:anuvṛttam]]---following; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:a:antaram]]---in that Manu's period.

===== Translation =====

**Svāyambhuva Manu's two sons, Priyavrata and Uttānapāda, became very powerful kings, and their sons and grandsons spread all over the three worlds during that period.**
