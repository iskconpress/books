====== SB 4.1.53 ======

> ययोर्जन्मन्यदो विश्वमभ्यनन्दत्सुनिर्वृतम् ।
> मनांसि ककुभो वाताः प्रसेदुः सरितोऽद्रयः ॥५३॥

===== Text =====

> yayor janmany ado viśvam
> abhyanandat sunirvṛtam
> manāṁsi kakubho vātāḥ
> praseduḥ sarito 'drayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yayoḥ]]---both of whom (Nara and Nārāyaṇa); [[synonyms:j:janmani]]---on the appearance; [[synonyms:a:adaḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:v:viśvam]]---universe; [[synonyms:a:abhyanandat]]---became glad; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:n:nirvṛtam]]---full of joy; [[synonyms:m:manāṁsi]]---everyone's mind; [[synonyms:k:kakubhaḥ]]---the directions; [[synonyms:v:vātāḥ]]---the air; [[synonyms:p:praseduḥ]]---became pleasant; [[synonyms:s:saritaḥ]]---the rivers; [[synonyms:a:adrayaḥ]]---the mountains.

===== Translation =====

**On the occasion of the appearance of Nara-Nārāyaṇa, the entire world was full of joy. Everyone's mind became tranquil, and thus in all directions the air, the rivers and the mountains became pleasant.**
