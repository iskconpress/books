====== SB 4.1.10 ======

> देवहूतिमदात्तात कर्दमायात्मजां मनुः ।
> तत्सम्बन्धि श्रुतप्रायं भवता गदतो मम ॥१०॥

===== Text =====

> devahūtim adāt tāta
> kardamāyātmajāṁ manuḥ
> tat-sambandhi śruta-prāyaṁ
> bhavatā gadato mama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devahūtim]]---Devahūti; [[synonyms:a:adāt]]---handed over; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---my dear son; [[synonyms:k:kardamāya]]---unto the great sage Kardama; [[synonyms:ā:ātmajām]]---daughter; [[synonyms:m:manuḥ]]---Lord Svāyambhuva Manu; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sambandhi]]---in that connection; [[synonyms:ś:śruta]]-[[synonyms:p:prāyam]]---heard almost in full; [[synonyms:b:bhavatā]]---by you; [[synonyms:g:gadataḥ]]---spoken; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---by me.

===== Translation =====

**My dear son, Svāyambhuva Manu handed over his very dear daughter Devahūti to Kardama Muni. I have already spoken to you about them, and you have heard about them almost in full.**
