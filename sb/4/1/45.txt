====== SB 4.1.45 ======

> मार्कण्डेयो मृकण्डस्य प्राणाद्वेदशिरा मुनिः ।
> कविश्च भार्गवो यस्य भगवानुशना सुतः ॥४५॥

===== Text =====

> mārkaṇḍeyo mṛkaṇḍasya
> prāṇād vedaśirā muniḥ
> kaviś ca bhārgavo yasya
> bhagavān uśanā sutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mārkaṇḍeyaḥ]]---Mārkaṇḍeya; [[synonyms:m:mṛkaṇḍasya]]---of Mṛkaṇḍa; [[synonyms:p:prāṇāt]]---from Prāṇa; [[synonyms:v:vedaśirāḥ]]---Vedaśirā; [[synonyms:m:muniḥ]]---great sage; [[synonyms:k:kaviḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---of the name Kavi; [[synonyms:b:bhārgavaḥ]]---of the name Bhārgava; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---whose; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---greatly powerful; [[synonyms:u:uśanā]]---Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---son.

===== Translation =====

**From Mṛkaṇḍa, Mārkaṇḍeya Muni was born, and from Prāṇa the sage Vedaśirā, whose son was Uśanā [Śukrācārya], also known as Kavi. Thus Kavi also belonged to the descendants of the Bhṛgu dynasty.**
