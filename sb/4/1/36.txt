====== SB 4.1.36 ======

> पुलस्त्योऽजनयत्पत्न्यामगस्त्यं च हविर्भुवि ।
> सोऽन्यजन्मनि दह्राग्निर्विश्रवाश्च महातपाः ॥३६॥

===== Text =====

> pulastyo 'janayat patnyām
> agastyaṁ ca havirbhuvi
> so 'nya-janmani dahrāgnir
> viśravāś ca mahā-tapāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pulastyaḥ]]---the sage Pulastya; [[synonyms:a:ajanayat]]---begot; [[synonyms:p:patnyām]]---in his wife; [[synonyms:a:agastyam]]---the great sage Agastya; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:h:havirbhuvi]]---in Havirbhū; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Agastya); [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:j:janmani]]---in the next birth; [[synonyms:d:dahra]]-[[synonyms:a:agniḥ]]---the digesting fire; [[synonyms:v:viśravāḥ]]---Viśravā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:t:tapāḥ]]---greatly powerful because of austerity.

===== Translation =====

**Pulastya begot in his wife, Havirbhū, one son of the name Agastya, who in his next birth became Dahrāgni. Besides him, Pulastya begot another very great and saintly son, whose name was Viśravā.**
