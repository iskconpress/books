====== SB 4.1.65 ======

> भवस्य पत्नी तु सती भवं देवमनुव्रता ।
> आत्मनः सदृशं पुत्रं न लेभे गुणशीलतः ॥६५॥

===== Text =====

> bhavasya patnī tu satī
> bhavaṁ devam anuvratā
> ātmanaḥ sadṛśaṁ putraṁ
> na lebhe guṇa-śīlataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhavasya]]---of Bhava (Lord Śiva); [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---the wife; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:s:satī]]---named Sati; [[synonyms:b:bhavam]]---to Bhava; [[synonyms:d:devam]]---a demigod; [[synonyms:a:anuvratā]]---faithfully engaged in service; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---of herself; [[synonyms:s:sadṛśam]]---similar; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---a son; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:l:lebhe]]---did not obtain; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śīlataḥ]]---by good qualities and by character.

===== Translation =====

**The sixteenth daughter, whose name was Satī, was the wife of Lord Śiva. She could not produce a child, although she always faithfully engaged in the service of her husband.**
