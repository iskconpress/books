====== SB 4.1.63 ======

> अग्निष्वात्ता बर्हिषदः सौम्याः पितर आज्यपाः ।
> साग्नयोऽनग्नयस्तेषां पत्नी दाक्षायणी स्वधा ॥६३॥

===== Text =====

> agniṣvāttā barhiṣadaḥ
> saumyāḥ pitara ājyapāḥ
> sāgnayo 'nagnayas teṣāṁ
> patnī dākṣāyaṇī svadhā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:agniṣvāttāḥ]]---the Agniṣvāttas; [[synonyms:b:barhiṣadaḥ]]---the Barhiṣadas; [[synonyms:s:saumyāḥ]]---the Saumyas; [[synonyms:p:pitaraḥ]]---the forefathers; [[synonyms:ā:ājyapāḥ]]---the Ājyapas; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:a:agnayaḥ]]---those whose means is by fire; [[synonyms:a:anagnayaḥ]]---those whose means is without fire; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of them; [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---the wife; [[synonyms:d:dākṣāyaṇī]]---the daughter of Dakṣa; [[synonyms:s:svadhā]]---Svadhā.

===== Translation =====

**The Agniṣvāttas, the Barhiṣadas, the Saumyas and the Ājyapas are the Pitās. They are either sāgnika or niragnika. The wife of all these Pitās is Svadhā, who is the daughter of King Dakṣa.**
