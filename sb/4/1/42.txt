====== SB 4.1.42 ======

> चित्तिस्त्वथर्वणः पत्नी लेभे पुत्रं धृतव्रतम् ।
> दध्यञ्चमश्वशिरसं भृगोर्वंशं निबोध मे ॥४२॥

===== Text =====

> cittis tv atharvaṇaḥ patnī
> lebhe putraṁ dhṛta-vratam
> dadhyañcam aśvaśirasaṁ
> bhṛgor vaṁśaṁ nibodha me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cittiḥ]]---Citti; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---also; [[synonyms:a:atharvaṇaḥ]]---of Atharvā; [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---wife; [[synonyms:l:lebhe]]---got; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---son; [[synonyms:d:dhṛta]]-[[synonyms:v:vratam]]---completely dedicated to a vow; [[synonyms:d:dadhyañcam]]---Dadhyañca; [[synonyms:a:aśvaśirasam]]---Aśvaśirā; [[synonyms:b:bhṛgoḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vaṁśam]]---generations of Bhṛgu; [[synonyms:n:nibodha]]---try to understand; [[synonyms:m:me]]---from me.

===== Translation =====

**Citti, wife of the sage Atharvā, gave birth to a son named Aśvaśirā by accepting a great vow called Dadhyañca. Now you may hear from me about the descendants of the sage Bhṛgu.**

===== Purport =====

The wife of Atharvā known as Citti is also known as Sānti. She was the eighth daughter of Kardama Muni.
