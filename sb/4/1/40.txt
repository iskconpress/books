====== SB 4.1.40 ======

> ऊर्जायां जज्ञिरे पुत्रा वसिष्ठस्य परन्तप ।
> चित्रकेतुप्रधानास्ते सप्त ब्रह्मर्षयोऽमलाः ॥४०॥

===== Text =====

> ūrjāyāṁ jajñire putrā
> vasiṣṭhasya parantapa
> citraketu-pradhānās te
> sapta brahmarṣayo 'malāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ū:ūrjāyām]]---in Ūrjā; [[synonyms:j:jajñire]]---took birth; [[synonyms:p:putrāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:v:vasiṣṭhasya]]---of the great sage Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:p:parantapa]]---O great one; [[synonyms:c:citraketu]]---Citraketu; [[synonyms:p:pradhānāḥ]]---headed by; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all the sons; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]---seven; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---great sages with spiritual knowledge; [[synonyms:a:amalāḥ]]---without contamination.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Vasiṣṭha begot in his wife, Ūrjā, sometimes called Arundhatī, seven spotlessly great sages, headed by the sage named Citraketu.**
