====== SB 4.1.13 ======

> पत्नी मरीचेस्तु कला सुषुवे कर्दमात्मजा ।
> कश्यपं पूर्णिमानं च ययोरापूरितं जगत् ॥१३॥

===== Text =====

> patnī marīces tu kalā
> suṣuve kardamātmajā
> kaśyapaṁ pūrṇimānaṁ ca
> yayor āpūritaṁ jagat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patnī]]---wife; [[synonyms:m:marīceḥ]]---of the sage named Marīci; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]---named Kalā; [[synonyms:s:suṣuve]]---gave birth; [[synonyms:k:kardama]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajā]]---daughter of Kardama Muni; [[synonyms:k:kaśyapam]]---of the name Kaśyapa; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇimānam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and of the name Pūrṇimā; [[synonyms:y:yayoḥ]]---by whom; [[synonyms:ā:āpūritam]]---spread all over; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the world.

===== Translation =====

**Kardama Muni's daughter Kalā, who was married to Marīci, gave birth to two children, whose names were Kaśyapa and Pūrṇimā. Their descendants are spread all over the world.**
