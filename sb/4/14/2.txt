====== SB 4.14.2 ======

> वीरमातरमाहूय सुनीथां ब्रह्मवादिनः ।
> प्रकृत्यसम्मतं वेनमभ्यषिञ्चन्पतिं भुवः ॥२॥

===== Text =====

> vīra-mātaram āhūya
> sunīthāṁ brahma-vādinaḥ
> prakṛty-asammataṁ venam
> abhyaṣiñcan patiṁ bhuvaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vīra]]---of Vena; [[synonyms:m:mātaram]]---mother; [[synonyms:ā:āhūya]]---calling; [[synonyms:s:sunīthām]]---of the name Sunīthā; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:v:vādinaḥ]]---the great sages learned in the //Vedas//; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---by the ministers; [[synonyms:a:asammatam]]---not approved of; [[synonyms:v:venam]]---Vena; [[synonyms:a:abhyaṣiñcan]]---enthroned; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---the master; [[synonyms:b:bhuvaḥ]]---of the world.

===== Translation =====

**The great sages then called for the Queen Mother, Sunīthā, and with her permission they installed Vena on the throne as master of the world. All the ministers, however, disagreed with this.**
