====== SB 4.14.13 ======

> एवमध्यवसायैनं मुनयो गूढमन्यवः ।
> उपव्रज्याब्रुवन्वेनं सान्त्वयित्वा च सामभिः ॥१३॥

===== Text =====

> evam adhyavasāyainaṁ
> munayo gūḍha-manyavaḥ
> upavrajyābruvan venaṁ
> sāntvayitvā ca sāmabhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:adhyavasāya]]---having decided; [[synonyms:e:enam]]---him; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]]---the great sages; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]-[[synonyms:m:manyavaḥ]]---concealing their anger; [[synonyms:u:upavrajya]]---having approached; [[synonyms:a:abruvan]]---spoke; [[synonyms:v:venam]]---to King Vena; [[synonyms:s:sāntvayitvā]]---after pacifying; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:sāmabhiḥ]]---with sweet words.

===== Translation =====

**The great sages, having thus decided, approached King Vena. Concealing their real anger, they pacified him with sweet words and then spoke as follows.**
