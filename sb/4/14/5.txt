====== SB 4.14.5 ======

> एवं मदान्ध उत्सिक्तो निरङ्कुश इव द्विपः ।
> पर्यटन्रथमास्थाय कम्पयन्निव रोदसी ॥५॥

===== Text =====

> evaṁ madāndha utsikto
> niraṅkuśa iva dvipaḥ
> paryaṭan ratham āsthāya
> kampayann iva rodasī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:m:mada]]-[[synonyms:a:andhaḥ]]---being blind with power; [[synonyms:u:utsiktaḥ]]---proud; [[synonyms:n:niraṅkuśaḥ]]---uncontrolled; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:d:dvipaḥ]]---an elephant; [[synonyms:p:paryaṭan]]---traveling; [[synonyms:r:ratham]]---a chariot; [[synonyms:ā:āsthāya]]---having mounted; [[synonyms:k:kampayan]]---causing to tremble; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:r:rodasī]]---the sky and earth.

===== Translation =====

**When he became overly blind due to his opulences, King Vena mounted a chariot and, like an uncontrolled elephant, began to travel through the kingdom, causing the sky and earth to tremble wherever he went.**
