====== SB 4.18.25 ======

> वटवत्सा वनस्पतयः पृथग्रसमयं पयः ।
> गिरयो हिमवद्वत्सा नानाधातून्स्वसानुषु ॥२५॥

===== Text =====

> vaṭa-vatsā vanaspatayaḥ
> pṛthag rasamayaṁ payaḥ
> girayo himavad-vatsā
> nānā-dhātūn sva-sānuṣu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsāḥ]]---making the banyan tree a calf; [[synonyms:v:vanaḥ]]-[[synonyms:p:patayaḥ]]---the trees; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---different; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:m:mayam]]---in the form of juices; [[synonyms:p:payaḥ]]---milk; [[synonyms:g:girayaḥ]]---the hills and mountains; [[synonyms:h:himavat]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsāḥ]]---making the Himalayas the calf; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:d:dhātūn]]---minerals; [[synonyms:s:sva]]---own; [[synonyms:s:sānuṣu]]---on their peaks.

===== Translation =====

**The trees made a calf out of the banyan tree, and thus they derived milk in the form of many delicious juices. The mountains transformed the Himalayas into a calf, and they milked a variety of minerals into a pot made of the peaks of hills.**
