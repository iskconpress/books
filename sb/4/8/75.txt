====== SB 4.8.75 ======

> चतुर्थमपि वै मासं द्वादशे द्वादशेऽहनि ।
> वायुभक्षो जितश्वासो ध्यायन्देवमधारयत् ॥७५॥

===== Text =====

> caturtham api vai māsaṁ
> dvādaśe dvādaśe 'hani
> vāyu-bhakṣo jita-śvāso
> dhyāyan devam adhārayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturtham]]---fourth; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---in that way; [[synonyms:m:māsam]]---the month; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśe]] [[synonyms:d:dvādaśe]]---on the twelfth; [[synonyms:a:ahani]]---day; [[synonyms:v:vāyu]]---air; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaḥ]]---eating; [[synonyms:j:jita]]-[[synonyms:ś:śvāsaḥ]]---controlling the breathing process; [[synonyms:d:dhyāyan]]---meditating; [[synonyms:d:devam]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:a:adhārayat]]---worshiped.

===== Translation =====

**In the fourth month Dhruva Mahārāja became a complete master of the breathing exercise, and thus he inhaled air only every twelfth day. In this way he became completely fixed in his position and worshiped the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
