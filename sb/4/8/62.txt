====== SB 4.8.62 ======

> इत्युक्तस्तं परिक्रम्य प्रणम्य च नृपार्भकः ।
> ययौ मधुवनं पुण्यं हरेश्चरणचर्चितम् ॥६२॥

===== Text =====

> ity uktas taṁ parikramya
> praṇamya ca nṛpārbhakaḥ
> yayau madhuvanaṁ puṇyaṁ
> hareś caraṇa-carcitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:u:uktaḥ]]---being spoken; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Nārada Muni); [[synonyms:p:parikramya]]---by circumambulating; [[synonyms:p:praṇamya]]---by offering obeisances; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]-[[synonyms:a:arbhakaḥ]]---the boy of the King; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---went to; [[synonyms:m:madhuvanam]]---a forest in Vṛndāvana known as Madhuvana; [[synonyms:p:puṇyam]]---which is auspicious and pious; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:carcitam]]---imprinted by the lotus feet of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**When Dhruva Mahārāja, the son of the King, was thus advised by the great sage Nārada, he circumambulated Nārada, his spiritual master, and offered him respectful obeisances. Then he started for Madhuvana, which is always imprinted with the lotus footprints of Lord Kṛṣṇa and which is therefore especially auspicious.**
