====== SB 4.8.50 ======

> पद्भ्यां नखमणिश्रेण्या विलसद्भ्यां समर्चताम् ।
> हृत्पद्मकर्णिकाधिष्ण्यमाक्रम्यात्मन्यवस्थितम् ॥५०॥

===== Text =====

> padbhyāṁ nakha-maṇi-śreṇyā
> vilasadbhyāṁ samarcatām
> hṛt-padma-karṇikā-dhiṣṇyam
> ākramyātmany avasthitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:padbhyām]]---by His lotus feet; [[synonyms:n:nakha]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śreṇyā]]---by the light of the jewellike nails on the toes; [[synonyms:v:vilasadbhyām]]---glittering lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:samarcatām]]---persons who are engaged in worshiping them; [[synonyms:h:hṛt]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:k:karṇikā]]---the whorl of the lotus flower of the heart; [[synonyms:d:dhiṣṇyam]]---situated; [[synonyms:ā:ākramya]]---seizing; [[synonyms:ā:ātmani]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:a:avasthitam]]---situated.

===== Translation =====

**Real yogīs meditate upon the transcendental form of the Lord as He stands on the whorl of the lotus of their hearts, the jewellike nails of His lotus feet glittering.**
