====== SB 4.8.76 ======

> पञ्चमे मास्यनुप्राप्ते जितश्वासो नृपात्मजः ।
> ध्यायन्ब्रह्म पदैकेन तस्थौ स्थाणुरिवाचलः ॥७६॥

===== Text =====

> pañcame māsy anuprāpte
> jita-śvāso nṛpātmajaḥ
> dhyāyan brahma padaikena
> tasthau sthāṇur ivācalaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañcame]]---in the fifth; [[synonyms:m:māsi]]---in the month; [[synonyms:a:anuprāpte]]---being situated; [[synonyms:j:jita]]-[[synonyms:ś:śvāsaḥ]]---and still controlling the breathing; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajaḥ]]---the son of the King; [[synonyms:d:dhyāyan]]---meditating; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:padā]] [[synonyms:e:ekena]]---with one leg; [[synonyms:t:tasthau]]---stood; [[synonyms:s:sthāṇuḥ]]---just like a column; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:a:acalaḥ]]---without movement.

===== Translation =====

**By the fifth month, Mahārāja Dhruva, the son of the King, had controlled his breathing so perfectly that he was able to stand on only one leg, just as a column stands, without motion, and concentrate his mind fully on the Parabrahman.**
