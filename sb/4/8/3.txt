====== SB 4.8.3 ======

> तयोः समभवल्लोभो निकृतिश्च महामते ।
> ताभ्यां क्रोधश्च हिंसा च यद्दुरुक्तिः स्वसा कलिः ॥३॥

===== Text =====

> tayoḥ samabhaval lobho
> nikṛtiś ca mahā-mate
> tābhyāṁ krodhaś ca hiṁsā ca
> yad duruktiḥ svasā kaliḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---those two; [[synonyms:s:samabhavat]]---were born; [[synonyms:l:lobhaḥ]]---Greed; [[synonyms:n:nikṛtiḥ]]---Cunning; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---O great soul; [[synonyms:t:tābhyām]]---from both of them; [[synonyms:k:krodhaḥ]]---Anger; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:h:hiṁsā]]---Envy; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---from both of whom; [[synonyms:d:duruktiḥ]]---Harsh Speech; [[synonyms:s:svasā]]---sister; [[synonyms:k:kaliḥ]]---Kali.

===== Translation =====

**Maitreya told Vidura: O great soul, from Dambha and Māyā were born Greed and Nikṛti, or Cunning. From their combination came children named Krodha (Anger) and Hiṁsā (Envy), and from their combination were born Kali and his sister Durukti (Harsh Speech).**
