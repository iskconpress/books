====== SB 4.8.74 ======

> तृतीयं चानयन्मासं नवमे नवमेऽहनि ।
> अब्भक्ष उत्तमश्लोकमुपाधावत्समाधिना ॥७४॥

===== Text =====

> tṛtīyaṁ cānayan māsaṁ
> navame navame 'hani
> ab-bhakṣa uttamaślokam
> upādhāvat samādhinā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīyam]]---the third month; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ā:ānayan]]---passing; [[synonyms:m:māsam]]---one month; [[synonyms:n:navame]] [[synonyms:n:navame]]---on each ninth; [[synonyms:a:ahani]]---on the day; [[synonyms:a:ap]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakṣaḥ]]---drinking water only; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]-[[synonyms:ś:ślokam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is worshiped by selected verses; [[synonyms:u:upādhāvat]]---worshiped; [[synonyms:s:samādhinā]]---in trance.

===== Translation =====

**In the third month he drank water only every nine days. Thus he remained completely in trance and worshiped the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is adored by selected verses.**
