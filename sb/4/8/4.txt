====== SB 4.8.4 ======

> दुरुक्तौ कलिराधत्त भयं मृत्युं च सत्तम ।
> तयोश्च मिथुनं जज्ञे यातना निरयस्तथा ॥४॥

===== Text =====

> duruktau kalir ādhatta
> bhayaṁ mṛtyuṁ ca sattama
> tayoś ca mithunaṁ jajñe
> yātanā nirayas tathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duruktau]]---in Durukti; [[synonyms:k:kaliḥ]]---Kali; [[synonyms:ā:ādhatta]]---produced; [[synonyms:b:bhayam]]---Fearfulness; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyum]]---Death; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---O greatest of all good men; [[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---of those two; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mithunam]]---by combination; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---were produced; [[synonyms:y:yātanā]]---Excessive Pain; [[synonyms:n:nirayaḥ]]---Hell; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well.

===== Translation =====

**O greatest of all good men, by the combination of Kali and Harsh Speech were born children named Mṛtyu (Death) and Bhīti (Fear). From the combination of Mṛtyu and Bhīti came children named Yātanā (Excessive Pain) and Niraya (Hell).**
