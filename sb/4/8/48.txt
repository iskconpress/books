====== SB 4.8.48 ======

> किरीटिनं कुण्डलिनं केयूरवलयान्वितम् ।
> कौस्तुभाभरणग्रीवं पीतकौशेयवाससम् ॥४८॥

===== Text =====

> kirīṭinaṁ kuṇḍalinaṁ
> keyūra-valayānvitam
> kaustubhābharaṇa-grīvaṁ
> pīta-kauśeya-vāsasam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kirīṭinam]]---the Lord is decorated with a jeweled helmet; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍalinam]]---with pearl earrings; [[synonyms:k:keyūra]]---jeweled necklace; [[synonyms:v:valaya]]-[[synonyms:a:anvitam]]---with jeweled bracelets; [[synonyms:k:kaustubha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābharaṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:grīvam]]---His neck is decorated by the Kaustubha jewel; [[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:k:kauśeya]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsasam]]---and He is dressed with yellow silk garments.

===== Translation =====

**The entire body of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Vāsudeva, is decorated. He wears a valuable jeweled helmet, necklaces and bracelets, His neck is adorned with the Kaustubha jewel, and He is dressed in yellow silk garments.**
