====== SB 4.17.1 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> एवं स भगवान्वैन्यः ख्यापितो गुणकर्मभिः ।
> छन्दयामास तान्कामैः प्रतिपूज्याभिनन्द्य च ॥१॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> evaṁ sa bhagavān vainyaḥ
> khyāpito guṇa-karmabhiḥ
> chandayām āsa tān kāmaiḥ
> pratipūjyābhinandya ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the great sage Maitreya continued to speak; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vainyaḥ]]---in the form of the son of King Vena; [[synonyms:k:khyāpitaḥ]]---being glorified; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:karmabhiḥ]]---by qualities and factual activities; [[synonyms:c:chandayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---pacified; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---those reciters; [[synonyms:k:kāmaiḥ]]---by various presentations; [[synonyms:p:pratipūjya]]---offering all respects; [[synonyms:a:abhinandya]]---offering prayers; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Maitreya continued: In this way the reciters who were glorifying Mahārāja Pṛthu readily described his qualities and chivalrous activities. At the end, Mahārāja Pṛthu offered them various presentations with all due respect and worshiped them adequately.**
