====== SB 4.17.17 ======

> लोके नाविन्दत त्राणं वैन्यान्मृत्योरिव प्रजाः ।
> त्रस्ता तदा निववृते हृदयेन विदूयता ॥१७॥

===== Text =====

> loke nāvindata trāṇaṁ
> vainyān mṛtyor iva prajāḥ
> trastā tadā nivavṛte
> hṛdayena vidūyatā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loke]]---within the three worlds; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:avindata]]---could obtain; [[synonyms:t:trāṇam]]---release; [[synonyms:v:vainyāt]]---from the hand of the son of King Vena; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyoḥ]]---from death; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:p:prajāḥ]]---men; [[synonyms:t:trastā]]---being very much afraid; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:n:nivavṛte]]---turned back; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayena]]---within her heart; [[synonyms:v:vidūyatā]]---very much aggrieved.

===== Translation =====

**Just as a man cannot escape the cruel hands of death, the cow-shaped earth could not escape the hands of the son of Vena. At length the earth, fearful, her heart aggrieved, turned back in helplessness.**
