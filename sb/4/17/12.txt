====== SB 4.17.12 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> पृथुः प्रजानां करुणं निशम्य परिदेवितम् ।
> दीर्घं दध्यौ कुरुश्रेष्ठ निमित्तं सोऽन्वपद्यत ॥१२॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> pṛthuḥ prajānāṁ karuṇaṁ
> niśamya paridevitam
> dīrghaṁ dadhyau kuruśreṣṭha
> nimittaṁ so 'nvapadyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the great saint Maitreya said; [[synonyms:p:pṛthuḥ]]---King Pṛthu; [[synonyms:p:prajānām]]---of the citizens; [[synonyms:k:karuṇam]]---pitiable condition; [[synonyms:n:niśamya]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:paridevitam]]---lamentation; [[synonyms:d:dīrgham]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:d:dadhyau]]---contemplated; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---O Vidura; [[synonyms:n:nimittam]]---the cause; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:a:anvapadyata]]---found out.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this lamentation and seeing the pitiable condition of the citizens, King Pṛthu contemplated this matter for a long time to see if he could find out the underlying causes.**
