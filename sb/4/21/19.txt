====== SB 4.21.19 ======

> शिशिरस्निग्धताराक्षः समैक्षत समन्ततः ।
> ऊचिवानिदमुर्वीशः सदः संहर्षयन्निव ॥१९॥

===== Text =====

> śiśira-snigdha-tārākṣaḥ
> samaikṣata samantataḥ
> ūcivān idam urvīśaḥ
> sadaḥ saṁharṣayann iva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śiśira]]---dew; [[synonyms:s:snigdha]]---wet; [[synonyms:t:tārā]]---stars; [[synonyms:a:akṣaḥ]]---eyes; [[synonyms:s:samaikṣata]]---glanced over; [[synonyms:s:samantataḥ]]---all around; [[synonyms:ū:ūcivān]]---began to speak; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:u:urvīśaḥ]]---highly elevated; [[synonyms:s:sadaḥ]]---amongst the members of the assembly; [[synonyms:s:saṁharṣayan]]---enhancing their pleasure; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like.

===== Translation =====

**Just to encourage the members of the assembly and to enhance their pleasure, King Pṛthu glanced over them with eyes that seemed like stars in a sky wet with dew. He then spoke to them in a great voice.**
