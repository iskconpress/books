====== SB 4.21.3 ======

> सवृन्दैः कदलीस्तम्भैः पूगपोतैः परिष्कृतम् ।
> तरुपल्लवमालाभिः सर्वतः समलङ्कृतम् ॥३॥

===== Text =====

> savṛndaiḥ kadalī-stambhaiḥ
> pūga-potaiḥ pariṣkṛtam
> taru-pallava-mālābhiḥ
> sarvataḥ samalaṅkṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndaiḥ]]---along with fruits and flowers; [[synonyms:k:kadalī]]-[[synonyms:s:stambhaiḥ]]---by the pillars of banana trees; [[synonyms:p:pūga]]-[[synonyms:p:potaiḥ]]---by collections of young animals and by processions of elephants; [[synonyms:p:pariṣkṛtam]]---very nicely cleansed; [[synonyms:t:taru]]---young plants; [[synonyms:p:pallava]]---new leaves of mango trees; [[synonyms:m:mālābhiḥ]]---by garlands; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:s:samalaṅkṛtam]]---nicely decorated.

===== Translation =====

**At the street crossings there were bunches of fruits and flowers, as well as pillars of banana trees and betel nut branches. All these combined decorations everywhere looked very attractive.**
