====== SB 4.21.17 ======

> सूक्ष्मवक्रासितस्निग्ध मूर्धजः कम्बुकन्धरः ।
> महाधने दुकूलाग्र्ये परिधायोपवीय च ॥१७॥

===== Text =====

> sūkṣma-vakrāsita-snigdha-
> mūrdhajaḥ kambu-kandharaḥ
> mahā-dhane dukūlāgrye
> paridhāyopavīya ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---very fine; [[synonyms:v:vakra]]---curly; [[synonyms:a:asita]]---black; [[synonyms:s:snigdha]]---slick; [[synonyms:m:mūrdhajaḥ]]---hair on the head; [[synonyms:k:kambu]]---like a conch; [[synonyms:k:kandharaḥ]]---neck; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:dhane]]---very valuable; [[synonyms:d:dukūla]]-[[synonyms:a:agrye]]---dressed with a //dhotī//; [[synonyms:p:paridhāya]]---on the upper portion of the body; [[synonyms:u:upavīya]]---placed like a sacred thread; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**The black, slick hair on his head was very fine and curly, and his neck, like a conchshell, was decorated with auspicious lines. He wore a very valuable dhotī, and there was a nice wrapper on the upper part of his body.**
