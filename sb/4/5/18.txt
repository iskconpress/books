====== SB 4.5.18 ======

> सर्व एवर्त्विजो दृष्ट्वा सदस्याः सदिवौकसः ।
> तैरर्द्यमानाः सुभृशं ग्रावभिर्नैकधाद्रवन् ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> sarva evartvijo dṛṣṭvā
> sadasyāḥ sa-divaukasaḥ
> tair ardyamānāḥ subhṛśaṁ
> grāvabhir naikadhādravan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtvijaḥ]]---the priests; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:s:sadasyāḥ]]---all the members assembled in the sacrifice; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:divaukasaḥ]]---along with the demigods; [[synonyms:t:taiḥ]]---by those (stones); [[synonyms:a:ardyamānāḥ]]---being disturbed; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---very greatly; [[synonyms:g:grāvabhiḥ]]---by stones; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:e:ekadhā]]---in different directions; [[synonyms:a:adravan]]---began to disperse.

===== Translation =====

**There was a continuous shower of stones, and all the priests and other members assembled at the sacrifice were put into immense misery. For fear of their lives, they dispersed in different directions.**
