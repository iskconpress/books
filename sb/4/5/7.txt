====== SB 4.5.7 ======

> अथर्त्विजो यजमानः सदस्याः ककुभ्युदीच्यां प्रसमीक्ष्य रेणुम् ।
> तमः किमेतत्कुत एतद्रजोऽभूदिति द्विजा द्विजपत्न्यश्च दध्युः ॥७॥

===== Text =====

> athartvijo yajamānaḥ sadasyāḥ
> kakubhy udīcyāṁ prasamīkṣya reṇum
> tamaḥ kim etat kuta etad rajo 'bhūd
> iti dvijā dvija-patnyaś ca dadhyuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtvijaḥ]]---the priests; [[synonyms:y:yajamānaḥ]]---the chief person performing the sacrifice (Dakṣa); [[synonyms:s:sadasyāḥ]]---all the persons assembled in the sacrificial arena; [[synonyms:k:kakubhi]] [[synonyms:u:udīcyām]]---in the northern direction; [[synonyms:p:prasamīkṣya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:r:reṇum]]---the dust storm; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---darkness; [[synonyms:k:kim]]---what; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kutaḥ]]---from where; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:r:rajaḥ]]---dust; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---has come; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:dvijāḥ]]---the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:p:patnyaḥ]]---the wives of the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:d:dadhyuḥ]]---began to speculate.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, all the persons assembled in the sacrificial arena---the priests, the chief of the sacrificial performance, and the brāhmaṇas and their wives---wondered where the darkness was coming from. Later they could understand that it was a dust storm, and all of them were full of anxiety.**
