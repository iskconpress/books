====== SB 4.5.15 ======

> रुरुजुर्यज्ञपात्राणि तथैकेऽग्नीननाशयन् ।
> कुण्डेष्वमूत्रयन्केचिद्बिभिदुर्वेदिमेखलाः ॥१५॥

===== Text =====

> rurujur yajña-pātrāṇi
> tathaike 'gnīn anāśayan
> kuṇḍeṣv amūtrayan kecid
> bibhidur vedi-mekhalāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rurujuḥ]]---broke; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:p:pātrāṇi]]---the pots used in the sacrifice; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---so; [[synonyms:e:eke]]---some; [[synonyms:a:agnīn]]---the sacrificial fires; [[synonyms:a:anāśayan]]---extinguished; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍeṣu]]---on the sacrificial arenas; [[synonyms:a:amūtrayan]]---passed urine; [[synonyms:k:kecit]]---some; [[synonyms:b:bibhiduḥ]]---tore down; [[synonyms:v:vedi]]-[[synonyms:m:mekhalāḥ]]---the boundary lines of the sacrificial arena.

===== Translation =====

**They broke all the pots made for use in the sacrifice, and some of them began to extinguish the sacrificial fire. Some tore down the boundary line of the sacrificial arena, and some passed urine on the arena.**
