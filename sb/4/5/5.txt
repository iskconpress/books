====== SB 4.5.5 ======

> आज्ञप्त एवं कुपितेन मन्युना स देवदेवं परिचक्रमे विभुम् ।
> मेनेतदात्मानमसङ्गरंहसा महीयसां तात सहः सहिष्णुम् ॥५॥

===== Text =====

> ājñapta evaṁ kupitena manyunā
> sa deva-devaṁ paricakrame vibhum
> mene-tadātmānam asaṅga-raṁhasā
> mahīyasāṁ tāta sahaḥ sahiṣṇum

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājñaptaḥ]]---being ordered; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this manner; [[synonyms:k:kupitena]]---angry; [[synonyms:m:manyunā]]---by Lord Śiva (who is anger personified); [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Vīrabhadra); [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:devam]]---he who is worshiped by the demigods; [[synonyms:p:paricakrame]]---circumambulated; [[synonyms:v:vibhum]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:m:mene]]---considered; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---himself; [[synonyms:a:asaṅga]]-[[synonyms:r:raṁhasā]]---with the power of Lord Śiva that cannot be opposed; [[synonyms:m:mahīyasām]]---of the most powerful; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---my dear Vidura; [[synonyms:s:sahaḥ]]---strength; [[synonyms:s:sahiṣṇum]]---capable of coping with.

===== Translation =====

**Maitreya continued: My dear Vidura, that black person was the personified anger of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, and he was prepared to execute the orders of Lord Śiva. Thus, considering himself capable of coping with any power offered against him, he circumambulated Lord Śiva.**
