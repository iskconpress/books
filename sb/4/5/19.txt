====== SB 4.5.19 ======

> जुह्वतः स्रुवहस्तस्य श्मश्रूणि भगवान्भवः ।
> भृगोर्लुलुञ्चे सदसि योऽहसच्छ्मश्रु दर्शयन् ॥१९॥

===== Text =====

> juhvataḥ sruva-hastasya
> śmaśrūṇi bhagavān bhavaḥ
> bhṛgor luluñce sadasi
> yo 'hasac chmaśru darśayan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:juhvataḥ]]---offering sacrificial oblations; [[synonyms:s:sruva]]-[[synonyms:h:hastasya]]---with the sacrificial ladle in his hand; [[synonyms:ś:śmaśrūṇi]]---the mustache; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the possessor of all opulences; [[synonyms:b:bhavaḥ]]---Vīrabhadra; [[synonyms:b:bhṛgoḥ]]---of Bhṛgu Muni; [[synonyms:l:luluñce]]---tore out; [[synonyms:s:sadasi]]---in the midst of the assembly; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who (Bhṛgu Muni); [[synonyms:a:ahasat]]---had smiled; [[synonyms:ś:śmaśru]]---his mustache; [[synonyms:d:darśayan]]---showing.

===== Translation =====

**Vīrabhadra tore off the mustache of Bhṛgu, who was offering the sacrificial oblations with his hands in the fire.**
