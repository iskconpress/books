====== SB 4.5.16 ======

> अबाधन्त मुनीनन्ये एके पत्नीरतर्जयन् ।
> अपरे जगृहुर्देवान्प्रत्यासन्नान्पलायितान् ॥१६॥

===== Text =====

> abādhanta munīn anye
> eke patnīr atarjayan
> apare jagṛhur devān
> pratyāsannān palāyitān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:abādhanta]]---blocked the way; [[synonyms:m:munīn]]---the sages; [[synonyms:a:anye]]---others; [[synonyms:e:eke]]---some; [[synonyms:p:patnīḥ]]---the women; [[synonyms:a:atarjayan]]---threatened; [[synonyms:a:apare]]---others; [[synonyms:j:jagṛhuḥ]]---arrested; [[synonyms:d:devān]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:p:pratyāsannān]]---near at hand; [[synonyms:p:palāyitān]]---who were fleeing.

===== Translation =====

**Some blocked the way of the fleeing sages, some threatened the women assembled there, and some arrested the demigods who were fleeing the pandal.**
