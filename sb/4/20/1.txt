====== SB 4.20.1 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> भगवानपि वैकुण्ठः साकं मघवता विभुः ।
> यज्ञैर्यज्ञपतिस्तुष्टो यज्ञभुक्तमभाषत ॥१॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> bhagavān api vaikuṇṭhaḥ
> sākaṁ maghavatā vibhuḥ
> yajñair yajña-patis tuṣṭo
> yajña-bhuk tam abhāṣata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the great sage Maitreya continued to speak; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhaḥ]]---the Lord of Vaikuṇṭha; [[synonyms:s:sākam]]---along with; [[synonyms:m:maghavatā]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yajñaiḥ]]---by the sacrifices; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the Lord of all //yajñas//; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭaḥ]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuk]]---the enjoyer of the //yajña//; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto King Pṛthu; [[synonyms:a:abhāṣata]]---said.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Maitreya continued: My dear Vidura, being very much satisfied by the performance of ninety-nine horse sacrifices, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Lord Viṣṇu, appeared on the scene. Accompanying Him was King Indra. Lord Viṣṇu then began to speak.**
