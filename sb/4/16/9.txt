====== SB 4.16.9 ======

> आप्याययत्यसौ लोकं वदनामृतमूर्तिना ।
> सानुरागावलोकेन विशदस्मितचारुणा ॥९॥

===== Text =====

> āpyāyayaty asau lokaṁ
> vadanāmṛta-mūrtinā
> sānurāgāvalokena
> viśada-smita-cāruṇā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpyāyayati]]---enhances; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---he; [[synonyms:l:lokam]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:v:vadana]]---by his face; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrtinā]]---moonlike; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:a:anurāga]]---affectionate; [[synonyms:a:avalokena]]---with glances; [[synonyms:v:viśada]]---bright; [[synonyms:s:smita]]---smiling; [[synonyms:c:cāruṇā]]---beautiful.

===== Translation =====

**This King, Pṛthu Mahārāja, by virtue of his affectionate glances and beautiful moonlike face, which is always smiling with great affection for the citizens, will enhance everyone's peaceful life.**
