====== SB 4.11.3 ======

> तस्यार्षास्त्रं धनुषि प्रयुञ्जतः सुवर्णपुङ्खाः कलहंसवाससः ।
> विनिःसृता आविविशुर्द्विषद्बलं यथा वनं भीमरवाः शिखण्डिनः ॥३॥

===== Text =====

> tasyārṣāstraṁ dhanuṣi prayuñjataḥ
> suvarṇa-puṅkhāḥ kalahaṁsa-vāsasaḥ
> viniḥsṛtā āviviśur dviṣad-balaṁ
> yathā vanaṁ bhīma-ravāḥ śikhaṇḍinaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---while Dhruva; [[synonyms:ā:ārṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:astram]]---the weapon given by Nārāyaṇa Ṛṣi; [[synonyms:d:dhanuṣi]]---on his bow; [[synonyms:p:prayuñjataḥ]]---fixed; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:puṅkhāḥ]]---(arrows) with golden shafts; [[synonyms:k:kalahaṁsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsasaḥ]]---with feathers like the wings of a swan; [[synonyms:v:viniḥsṛtāḥ]]---sprang out; [[synonyms:ā:āviviśuḥ]]---entered; [[synonyms:d:dviṣat]]-[[synonyms:b:balam]]---the soldiers of the enemy; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:v:vanam]]---into a forest; [[synonyms:b:bhīma]]-[[synonyms:r:ravāḥ]]---making a tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:ś:śikhaṇḍinaḥ]]---peacocks.

===== Translation =====

**Even as Dhruva Mahārāja fixed the weapon made by Nārāyaṇa Ṛṣi onto his bow, arrows with golden shafts and feathers like the wings of a swan flew out from it. They entered the enemy soldiers with a great hissing sound, just as peacocks enter a forest with tumultuous crowing.**
