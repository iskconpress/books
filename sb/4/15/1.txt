====== SB 4.15.1 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> अथ तस्य पुनर्विप्रैरपुत्रस्य महीपतेः ।
> बाहुभ्यां मथ्यमानाभ्यां मिथुनं समपद्यत ॥१॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> atha tasya punar viprair
> aputrasya mahīpateḥ
> bāhubhyāṁ mathyamānābhyāṁ
> mithunaṁ samapadyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Maitreya continued to speak; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thus; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:v:vipraiḥ]]---by the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:a:aputrasya]]---without a son; [[synonyms:m:mahīpateḥ]]---of the King; [[synonyms:b:bāhubhyām]]---from the arms; [[synonyms:m:mathyamānābhyām]]---being churned; [[synonyms:m:mithunam]]---a couple; [[synonyms:s:samapadyata]]---took birth.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Maitreya continued: My dear Vidura, thus the brāhmaṇas and the great sages again churned the two arms of King Vena's dead body. As a result a male and female couple came out of his arms.**
