====== SB 4.15.14 ======

> तस्मै जहार धनदो हैमं वीर वरासनम् ।
> वरुणः सलिलस्रावमातपत्रं शशिप्रभम् ॥१४॥

===== Text =====

> tasmai jahāra dhanado
> haimaṁ vīra varāsanam
> varuṇaḥ salila-srāvam
> ātapatraṁ śaśi-prabham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto him; [[synonyms:j:jahāra]]---presented; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]-[[synonyms:d:daḥ]]---the treasurer of the demigods (Kuvera); [[synonyms:h:haimam]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:v:vīra]]---O Vidura; [[synonyms:v:vara]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsanam]]---royal throne; [[synonyms:v:varuṇaḥ]]---the demigod Varuṇa; [[synonyms:s:salila]]-[[synonyms:s:srāvam]]---dropping particles of water; [[synonyms:ā:ātapatram]]---umbrella; [[synonyms:ś:śaśi]]-[[synonyms:p:prabham]]---as brilliant as the moon.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage continued: My dear Vidura, Kuvera presented the great King Pṛthu with a golden throne. The demigod Varuṇa presented him with an umbrella that constantly sprayed fine particles of water and was as brilliant as the moon.**
