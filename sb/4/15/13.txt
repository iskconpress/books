====== SB 4.15.13 ======

> सोऽभिषिक्तो महाराजः सुवासाः साध्वलङ्कृतः ।
> पत्न्यार्चिषालङ्कृतया विरेजेऽग्निरिवापरः ॥१३॥

===== Text =====

> so 'bhiṣikto mahārājaḥ
> suvāsāḥ sādhv-alaṅkṛtaḥ
> patnyārciṣālaṅkṛtayā
> vireje 'gnir ivāparaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---the King; [[synonyms:a:abhiṣiktaḥ]]---being coronated; [[synonyms:m:mahārājaḥ]]---Mahārāja Pṛthu; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsāḥ]]---exquisitely dressed; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkṛtaḥ]]---highly decorated with ornaments; [[synonyms:p:patnyā]]---along with his wife; [[synonyms:a:arciṣā]]---named Arci; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkṛtayā]]---nicely ornamented; [[synonyms:v:vireje]]---appeared; [[synonyms:a:agniḥ]]---fire; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:a:aparaḥ]]---another.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the great King Pṛthu, exquisitely dressed with garments and ornaments, was coronated and placed on the throne. The King and his wife, Arci, who was also exquisitely ornamented, appeared exactly like fire.**
