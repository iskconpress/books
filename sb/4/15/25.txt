====== SB 4.15.25 ======

> प्रभवो ह्यात्मनः स्तोत्रंजुगुप्सन्त्यपि विश्रुताः ।
> ह्रीमन्तः परमोदाराः पौरुषं वा विगर्हितम् ॥२५॥

===== Text =====

> prabhavo hy ātmanaḥ stotraṁ
> jugupsanty api viśrutāḥ
> hrīmantaḥ paramodārāḥ
> pauruṣaṁ vā vigarhitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhavaḥ]]---very powerful persons; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---of themselves; [[synonyms:s:stotram]]---praise; [[synonyms:j:jugupsanti]]---do not like; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:v:viśrutāḥ]]---very famous; [[synonyms:h:hrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mantaḥ]]---modest; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:u:udārāḥ]]---very magnanimous persons; [[synonyms:p:pauruṣam]]---powerful actions; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vigarhitam]]---abominable.

===== Translation =====

**As a person with a sense of honor and magnanimity does not like to hear about his abominable actions, a person who is very famous and powerful does not like to hear himself praised.**
