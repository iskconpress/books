====== SB 4.15.7 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> प्रशंसन्ति स्म तं विप्रा गन्धर्वप्रवरा जगुः ।
> मुमुचुः सुमनोधाराः सिद्धा नृत्यन्ति स्वःस्त्रियः ॥७॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> praśaṁsanti sma taṁ viprā
> gandharva-pravarā jaguḥ
> mumucuḥ sumano-dhārāḥ
> siddhā nṛtyanti svaḥ-striyaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the great saint Maitreya said; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsanti]] [[synonyms:s:sma]]---praised, glorified; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Pṛthu); [[synonyms:v:viprāḥ]]---all the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:g:gandharva]]-[[synonyms:p:pravarāḥ]]---the best of the Gandharvas; [[synonyms:j:jaguḥ]]---chanted; [[synonyms:m:mumucuḥ]]---released; [[synonyms:s:sumanaḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dhārāḥ]]---showers of flowers; [[synonyms:s:siddhāḥ]]---the personalities from Siddhaloka; [[synonyms:n:nṛtyanti]]---were dancing; [[synonyms:s:svaḥ]]---of the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:s:striyaḥ]]---women (the Apsarās).

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Maitreya continued: My dear Vidurajī, at that time all the brāhmaṇas highly praised and glorified King Pṛthu, and the best singers of Gandharvaloka chanted his glories. The inhabitants of Siddhaloka showered flowers, and the beautiful women in the heavenly planets danced in ecstasy.**
