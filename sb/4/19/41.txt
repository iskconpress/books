====== SB 4.19.41 ======

> विप्राः सत्याशिषस्तुष्टाः श्रद्धया लब्धदक्षिणाः ।
> आशिषो युयुजुः क्षत्तरादिराजाय सत्कृताः ॥४१॥

===== Text =====

> viprāḥ satyāśiṣas tuṣṭāḥ
> śraddhayā labdha-dakṣiṇāḥ
> āśiṣo yuyujuḥ kṣattar
> ādi-rājāya sat-kṛtāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viprāḥ]]---all the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---true; [[synonyms:ā:āśiṣaḥ]]---whose benedictions; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭāḥ]]---being very satisfied; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---with great respect; [[synonyms:l:labdha]]-[[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇāḥ]]---who obtained rewards; [[synonyms:ā:āśiṣaḥ]]---benedictions; [[synonyms:y:yuyujuḥ]]---offered; [[synonyms:k:kṣattaḥ]]---O Vidura; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:r:rājāya]]---upon the original king; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtāḥ]]---being honored.

===== Translation =====

**With great respect, the original king, Pṛthu, offered all kinds of rewards to the brāhmaṇas present at the sacrifice. Since all these brāhmaṇas were very much satisfied, they gave their heartfelt blessings to the King.**
