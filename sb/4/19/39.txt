====== SB 4.19.39 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> इत्थं स लोकगुरुणा समादिष्टो विशाम्पतिः ।
> तथा च कृत्वा वात्सल्यं मघोनापि च सन्दधे ॥३९॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> itthaṁ sa loka-guruṇā
> samādiṣṭo viśāmpatiḥ
> tathā ca kṛtvā vātsalyaṁ
> maghonāpi ca sandadhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the great sage Maitreya continued to speak; [[synonyms:i:ittham]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---King Pṛthu; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:g:guruṇā]]---by the original teacher of all people, Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:s:samādiṣṭaḥ]]---being advised; [[synonyms:v:viśām]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the king, master of the people; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---in that way; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kṛtvā]]---having done; [[synonyms:v:vātsalyam]]---affection; [[synonyms:m:maghonā]]---with Indra; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:sandadhe]]---concluded peace.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Maitreya continued: When King Pṛthu was thus advised by the supreme teacher, Lord Brahmā, he abandoned his eagerness to perform yajñas and with great affection concluded a peace with King Indra.**
