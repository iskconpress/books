====== SB 4.19.14 ======

> तं तादृशाकृतिं वीक्ष्य मेने धर्मं शरीरिणम् ।
> जटिलं भस्मनाच्छन्नं तस्मै बाणं न मुञ्चति ॥१४॥

===== Text =====

> taṁ tādṛśākṛtiṁ vīkṣya
> mene dharmaṁ śarīriṇam
> jaṭilaṁ bhasmanācchannaṁ
> tasmai bāṇaṁ na muñcati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:t:tādṛśa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛtim]]---in such dress; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:m:mene]]---considered; [[synonyms:d:dharmam]]---pious or religious; [[synonyms:ś:śarīriṇam]]---having a body; [[synonyms:j:jaṭilam]]---having knotted hair; [[synonyms:b:bhasmanā]]---by ashes; [[synonyms:ā:ācchannam]]---smeared all over the body; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto him; [[synonyms:b:bāṇam]]---arrow; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:m:muñcati]]---he did release.

===== Translation =====

**King Indra was fraudulently dressed as a sannyāsī, having knotted his hair on his head and smeared ashes all over his body. Upon seeing such dress, the son of King Pṛthu considered Indra a religious man and pious sannyāsī. Therefore he did not release his arrows.**
