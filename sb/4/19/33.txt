====== SB 4.19.33 ======

> नैवात्मने महेन्द्राय रोषमाहर्तुमर्हसि ।
> उभावपि हि भद्रं ते उत्तमश्लोकविग्रहौ ॥३३॥

===== Text =====

> naivātmane mahendrāya
> roṣam āhartum arhasi
> ubhāv api hi bhadraṁ te
> uttamaśloka-vigrahau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:ātmane]]---nondifferent from you; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:i:indrāya]]---upon the King of heaven, Indra; [[synonyms:r:roṣam]]---anger; [[synonyms:ā:āhartum]]---to apply; [[synonyms:a:arhasi]]---you ought; [[synonyms:u:ubhau]]---both of you; [[synonyms:a:api]]---certainly; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---also; [[synonyms:b:bhadram]]---good fortune; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:v:vigrahau]]---incarnations of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Brahmā continued: Let there be good fortune to both of you, for you and King Indra are both part and parcel of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Therefore you should not be angry with King Indra, who is nondifferent from you.**
