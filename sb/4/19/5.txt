====== SB 4.19.5 ======

> सिद्धा विद्याधरा दैत्या दानवा गुह्यकादयः ।
> सुनन्दनन्दप्रमुखाः पार्षदप्रवरा हरेः ॥५॥

===== Text =====

> siddhā vidyādharā daityā
> dānavā guhyakādayaḥ
> sunanda-nanda-pramukhāḥ
> pārṣada-pravarā hareḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siddhāḥ]]---the residents of Siddhaloka; [[synonyms:v:vidyādharāḥ]]---the residents of Vidyādhara-loka; [[synonyms:d:daityāḥ]]---the demoniac descendants of Diti; [[synonyms:d:dānavāḥ]]---the //asuras//; [[synonyms:g:guhyaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the Yakṣas, etc.; [[synonyms:s:sunanda]]-[[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:p:pramukhāḥ]]---headed by Sunanda and Nanda, the chief of Lord Viṣṇu's associates from Vaikuṇṭha; [[synonyms:p:pārṣada]]---associates; [[synonyms:p:pravarāḥ]]---most respectful; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord was accompanied by the residents of Siddhaloka and Vidyādhara-loka, all the descendants of Diti, and the demons and the Yakṣas. He was also accompanied by His chief associates, headed by Sunanda and Nanda.**
