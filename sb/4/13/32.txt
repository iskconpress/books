====== SB 4.13.32 ======

> तथा साधय भद्रं ते आत्मानं सुप्रजं नृप ।
> इष्टस्ते पुत्रकामस्य पुत्रं दास्यति यज्ञभुक् ॥३२॥

===== Text =====

> tathā sādhaya bhadraṁ te
> ātmānaṁ suprajaṁ nṛpa
> iṣṭas te putra-kāmasya
> putraṁ dāsyati yajña-bhuk

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:sādhaya]]---execute the sacrifice to get; [[synonyms:b:bhadram]]---good fortune; [[synonyms:t:te]]---to you; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---your own; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:p:prajam]]---good son; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭaḥ]]---being worshiped; [[synonyms:t:te]]---by you; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:k:kāmasya]]---desiring to have a son; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---a son; [[synonyms:d:dāsyati]]---He will deliver; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuk]]---the Lord, the enjoyer of the sacrifice.

===== Translation =====

**O King, we wish all good fortune for you. You have no son, but if you pray at once to the Supreme Lord and ask for a son, and if you execute the sacrifice for that purpose, the enjoyer of the sacrifice, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, will fulfill your desire.**
