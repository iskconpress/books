====== SB 4.13.6 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> ध्रुवस्य चोत्कलः पुत्रः पितरि प्रस्थिते वनम् ।
> सार्वभौमश्रियं नैच्छदधिराजासनं पितुः ॥६॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> dhruvasya cotkalaḥ putraḥ
> pitari prasthite vanam
> sārvabhauma-śriyaṁ naicchad
> adhirājāsanaṁ pituḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the great sage Maitreya said; [[synonyms:d:dhruvasya]]---of Dhruva Mahārāja; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:u:utkalaḥ]]---Utkala; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:p:pitari]]---after the father; [[synonyms:p:prasthite]]---departed; [[synonyms:v:vanam]]---for the forest; [[synonyms:s:sārva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhauma]]---including all lands; [[synonyms:ś:śriyam]]---opulence; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:aicchat]]---did not desire; [[synonyms:a:adhirāja]]---royal; [[synonyms:ā:āsanam]]---throne; [[synonyms:p:pituḥ]]---of the father.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Maitreya replied: My dear Vidura, when Mahārāja Dhruva departed for the forest, his son, Utkala, did not desire to accept the opulent throne of his father, which was meant for the ruler of all the lands of this planet.**
