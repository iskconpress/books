====== SB 4.13.41 ======

> आक्रीडे क्रीडतो बालान्वयस्यानतिदारुणः ।
> प्रसह्य निरनुक्रोशः पशुमारममारयत् ॥४१॥

===== Text =====

> ākrīḍe krīḍato bālān
> vayasyān atidāruṇaḥ
> prasahya niranukrośaḥ
> paśu-māram amārayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākrīḍe]]---in the playground; [[synonyms:k:krīḍataḥ]]---while playing; [[synonyms:b:bālān]]---boys; [[synonyms:v:vayasyān]]---of his age; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:d:dāruṇaḥ]]---very cruel; [[synonyms:p:prasahya]]---by force; [[synonyms:n:niranukrośaḥ]]---merciless; [[synonyms:p:paśu]]-[[synonyms:m:māram]]---as if slaughtering animals; [[synonyms:a:amārayat]]---killed.

===== Translation =====

**The boy was so cruel that while playing with young boys of his age he would kill them very mercilessly, as if they were animals meant for slaughter.**
