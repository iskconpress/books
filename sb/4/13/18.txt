====== SB 4.13.18 ======

> सुनीथाङ्गस्य या पत्नी सुषुवे वेनमुल्बणम् ।
> यद्दौःशील्यात्स राजर्षिर्निर्विण्णो निरगात्पुरात् ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> sunīthāṅgasya yā patnī
> suṣuve venam ulbaṇam
> yad-dauḥśīlyāt sa rājarṣir
> nirviṇṇo niragāt purāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sunīthā]]---Sunīthā; [[synonyms:a:aṅgasya]]---of Aṅga; [[synonyms:y:yā]]---she who; [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---the wife; [[synonyms:s:suṣuve]]---gave birth to; [[synonyms:v:venam]]---Vena; [[synonyms:u:ulbaṇam]]---very crooked; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whose; [[synonyms:d:dauḥśīlyāt]]---on account of bad character; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---the saintly King Aṅga; [[synonyms:n:nirviṇṇaḥ]]---very disappointed; [[synonyms:n:niragāt]]---went out; [[synonyms:p:purāt]]---from home.

===== Translation =====

**The wife of Aṅga, Sunīthā, gave birth to a son named Vena, who was very crooked. The saintly King Aṅga was very disappointed with Vena's bad character, and he left home and kingdom and went out to the forest.**
