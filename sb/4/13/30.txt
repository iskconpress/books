====== SB 4.13.30 ======

> नागच्छन्त्याहुता देवा न गृह्णन्ति ग्रहानिह ।
> सदसस्पतयो ब्रूत किमवद्यं मया कृतम् ॥३०॥

===== Text =====

> nāgacchanty āhutā devā
> na gṛhṇanti grahān iha
> sadasas-patayo brūta
> kim avadyaṁ mayā kṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:ā:āgacchanti]]---are coming; [[synonyms:ā:āhutāḥ]]---being invited; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:g:gṛhṇanti]]---are accepting; [[synonyms:g:grahān]]---shares; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in the sacrifice; [[synonyms:s:sadasaḥ]]-[[synonyms:p:patayaḥ]]---my dear priests; [[synonyms:b:brūta]]---kindly tell me; [[synonyms:k:kim]]---what; [[synonyms:a:avadyam]]---offense; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---was committed.

===== Translation =====

**King Aṅga addressed the priestly order: My dear priests, kindly tell me what offense I have committed. Although invited, the demigods are neither taking part in the sacrifice nor accepting their shares.**
