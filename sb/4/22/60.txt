====== SB 4.22.60 ======

> मातरिश्वेव सर्वात्मा बलेन महसौजसा ।
> अविषह्यतया देवो भगवान्भूतराडिव ॥६०॥

===== Text =====

> mātariśveva sarvātmā
> balena mahasaujasā
> aviṣahyatayā devo
> bhagavān bhūta-rāḍ iva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mātariśvā]]---the air; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---all-pervading; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---by bodily strength; [[synonyms:m:mahasā]] [[synonyms:o:ojasā]]---by courage and power; [[synonyms:a:aviṣahyatayā]]---by intolerance; [[synonyms:d:devaḥ]]---the demigod; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:r:rāṭ]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like Rudra, or Sadāśiva.

===== Translation =====

**In his bodily strength and in the strength of his senses, Mahārāja Pṛthu was as strong as the wind, which can go anywhere and everywhere. As far as his intolerance was concerned, he was just like the all-powerful Rudra expansion of Lord Śiva, or Sadāśiva.**
