====== SB 4.22.61 ======

> कन्दर्प इव सौन्दर्ये मनस्वी मृगराडिव ।
> वात्सल्ये मनुवन्नृणां प्रभुत्वे भगवानजः ॥६१॥

===== Text =====

> kandarpa iva saundarye
> manasvī mṛga-rāḍ iva
> vātsalye manuvan nṛṇāṁ
> prabhutve bhagavān ajaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kandarpaḥ]]---Cupid; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:s:saundarye]]---in beauty; [[synonyms:m:manasvī]]---in thoughtfulness; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:r:rāṭ]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like the king of the animals, the lion; [[synonyms:v:vātsalye]]---in affection; [[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like Svāyambhuva Manu; [[synonyms:n:nṛṇām]]---of human society; [[synonyms:p:prabhutve]]---in the matter of controlling; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the lord; [[synonyms:a:ajaḥ]]---Brahmā.

===== Translation =====

**In his bodily beauty he was just like Cupid, and in his thoughtfulness he was like a lion. In his affection he was just like Svāyambhuva Manu, and in his ability to control he was like Lord Brahmā.**
