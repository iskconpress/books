====== SB 4.12.2 ======

> धनद उवाच
> भो भोः क्षत्रियदायाद परितुष्टोऽस्मि तेऽनघ ।
> यत्त्वं पितामहादेशाद्वैरं दुस्त्यजमत्यजः ॥२॥

===== Text =====

> dhanada uvāca
> bho bhoḥ kṣatriya-dāyāda
> parituṣṭo 'smi te 'nagha
> yat tvaṁ pitāmahādeśād
> vairaṁ dustyajam atyajaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhana]]-[[synonyms:d:daḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the master of the treasury (Kuvera) said; [[synonyms:b:bhoḥ]] [[synonyms:b:bhoḥ]]---O; [[synonyms:k:kṣatriya]]-[[synonyms:d:dāyāda]]---O son of a //kṣatriya//; [[synonyms:p:parituṣṭaḥ]]---very glad; [[synonyms:a:asmi]]---I am; [[synonyms:t:te]]---with you; [[synonyms:a:anagha]]---O sinless one; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---you; [[synonyms:p:pitāmaha]]---of your grandfather; [[synonyms:ā:ādeśāt]]---under the instruction; [[synonyms:v:vairam]]---enmity; [[synonyms:d:dustyajam]]---difficult to avoid; [[synonyms:a:atyajaḥ]]---have given up.

===== Translation =====

**The master of the treasury, Kuvera, said: O sinless son of a kṣatriya, I am very glad to know that under the instruction of your grandfather you have given up your enmity, although it is very difficult to avoid. I am very pleased with you.**
