====== SB 4.12.31 ======

> तदा दुन्दुभयो नेदुर्मृदङ्गपणवादयः ।
> गन्धर्वमुख्याः प्रजगुः पेतुः कुसुमवृष्टयः ॥३१॥

===== Text =====

> tadā dundubhayo nedur
> mṛdaṅga-paṇavādayaḥ
> gandharva-mukhyāḥ prajaguḥ
> petuḥ kusuma-vṛṣṭayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:d:dundubhayaḥ]]---kettledrums; [[synonyms:n:neduḥ]]---resounded; [[synonyms:m:mṛdaṅga]]---drums; [[synonyms:p:paṇava]]---small drums; [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---etc.; [[synonyms:g:gandharva]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhyāḥ]]---the chief residents of Gandharvaloka; [[synonyms:p:prajaguḥ]]---sang; [[synonyms:p:petuḥ]]---showered; [[synonyms:k:kusuma]]---flowers; [[synonyms:v:vṛṣṭayaḥ]]---like rains.

===== Translation =====

**At that time drums and kettledrums resounded from the sky, the chief Gandharvas began to sing and other demigods showered flowers like torrents of rain upon Dhruva Mahārāja.**
