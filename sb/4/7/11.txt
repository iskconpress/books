====== SB 4.7.11 ======

> भवस्तवाय कृतधीर्नाशक्नोदनुरागतः ।
> औत्कण्ठ्याद्बाष्पकलया सम्परेतां सुतां स्मरन् ॥११॥

===== Text =====

> bhava-stavāya kṛta-dhīr
> nāśaknod anurāgataḥ
> autkaṇṭhyād bāṣpa-kalayā
> samparetāṁ sutāṁ smaran

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhava]]-[[synonyms:s:stavāya]]---for praying to Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:d:dhīḥ]]---although decided; [[synonyms:n:na]]---never; [[synonyms:a:aśaknot]]---was able; [[synonyms:a:anurāgataḥ]]---by feeling; [[synonyms:a:autkaṇṭhyāt]]---because of eagerness; [[synonyms:b:bāṣpa]]-[[synonyms:k:kalayā]]---with tears in the eyes; [[synonyms:s:samparetām]]---dead; [[synonyms:s:sutām]]---daughter; [[synonyms:s:smaran]]---remembering.

===== Translation =====

**King Dakṣa wanted to offer prayers to Lord Śiva, but as he remembered the ill-fated death of his daughter Satī, his eyes filled with tears, and in bereavement his voice choked up, and he could not say anything.**
