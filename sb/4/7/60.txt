====== SB 4.7.60 ======

> एतद्भगवतः शम्भोः कर्म दक्षाध्वरद्रुहः ।
> श्रुतं भागवताच्छिष्यादुद्धवान्मे बृहस्पतेः ॥६०॥

===== Text =====

> etad bhagavataḥ śambhoḥ
> karma dakṣādhvara-druhaḥ
> śrutaṁ bhāgavatāc chiṣyād
> uddhavān me bṛhaspateḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhagavataḥ]]---of the possessor of all opulences; [[synonyms:ś:śambhoḥ]]---of Śambhu (Lord Śiva); [[synonyms:k:karma]]---story; [[synonyms:d:dakṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:adhvara]]-[[synonyms:d:druhaḥ]]---who devastated the sacrifice of Dakṣa; [[synonyms:ś:śrutam]]---was heard; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavatāt]]---from a great devotee; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣyāt]]---from the disciple; [[synonyms:u:uddhavāt]]---from Uddhava; [[synonyms:m:me]]---by me; [[synonyms:b:bṛhaspateḥ]]---of Bṛhaspati.

===== Translation =====

**Maitreya said: My dear Vidura, I heard this story of the Dakṣa yajña, which was devastated by Lord Śiva, from Uddhava, a great devotee and a disciple of Bṛhaspati.**
