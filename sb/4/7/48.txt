====== SB 4.7.48 ======

> मैत्रेय उवाच
> इति दक्षः कविर्यज्ञं भद्र रुद्राभिमर्शितम् ।
> कीर्त्यमाने हृषीकेशे सन्निन्ये यज्ञभावने ॥४८॥

===== Text =====

> maitreya uvāca
> iti dakṣaḥ kavir yajñaṁ
> bhadra rudrābhimarśitam
> kīrtyamāne hṛṣīkeśe
> sanninye yajña-bhāvane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maitreyaḥ]]---Maitreya; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:dakṣaḥ]]---Dakṣa; [[synonyms:k:kaviḥ]]---being purified in consciousness; [[synonyms:y:yajñam]]---the sacrifice; [[synonyms:b:bhadra]]---O Vidura; [[synonyms:r:rudra]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimarśitam]]---devastated by Vīrabhadra; [[synonyms:k:kīrtya]]-[[synonyms:m:māne]]---being glorified; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣīkeśe]]---Hṛṣīkeśa (Lord Viṣṇu); [[synonyms:s:sanninye]]---arranged for restarting; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvane]]---the protector of sacrifice.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Maitreya said: After Lord Viṣṇu was glorified by all present, Dakṣa, his consciousness purified, arranged to begin again the yajña which had been devastated by the followers of Lord Śiva.**
