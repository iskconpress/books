====== SB 4.7.3 ======

> प्रजापतेर्दग्धशीर्ष्णो भवत्वजमुखं शिरः ।
> मित्रस्य चक्षुषेक्षेत भागं स्वं बर्हिषो भगः ॥३॥

===== Text =====

> prajāpater dagdha-śīrṣṇo
> bhavatv aja-mukhaṁ śiraḥ
> mitrasya cakṣuṣekṣeta
> bhāgaṁ svaṁ barhiṣo bhagaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prajāpateḥ]]---of Prajāpati Dakṣa; [[synonyms:d:dagdha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śīrṣṇaḥ]]---whose head has been burned to ashes; [[synonyms:b:bhavatu]]---let there be; [[synonyms:a:aja]]-[[synonyms:m:mukham]]---with the face of a goat; [[synonyms:ś:śiraḥ]]---a head; [[synonyms:m:mitrasya]]---of Mitra; [[synonyms:c:cakṣuṣā]]---through the eyes; [[synonyms:ī:īkṣeta]]---may see; [[synonyms:b:bhāgam]]---share; [[synonyms:s:svam]]---his own; [[synonyms:b:barhiṣaḥ]]---of the sacrifice; [[synonyms:b:bhagaḥ]]---Bhaga.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śiva continued: Since the head of Dakṣa has already been burned to ashes, he will have the head of a goat. The demigod known as Bhaga will be able to see his share of sacrifice through the eyes of Mitra.**
