====== SB 4.2.7 ======

> सदसस्पतिभिर्दक्षो भगवान्साधु सत्कृतः ।
> अजं लोकगुरुं नत्वा निषसाद तदाज्ञया ॥७॥

===== Text =====

> sadasas-patibhir dakṣo
> bhagavān sādhu sat-kṛtaḥ
> ajaṁ loka-guruṁ natvā
> niṣasāda tad-ājñayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sadasaḥ]]---of the assembly; [[synonyms:p:patibhiḥ]]---by the leaders; [[synonyms:d:dakṣaḥ]]---Dakṣa; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the possessor of all opulences; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]---properly; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtaḥ]]---was welcomed; [[synonyms:a:ajam]]---to the unborn (Brahmā); [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:g:gurum]]---to the teacher of the universe; [[synonyms:n:natvā]]---making obeisances; [[synonyms:n:niṣasāda]]---sat down; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñayā]]---by his (Brahmā's) order.

===== Translation =====

**Dakṣa was adequately welcomed by the president of the great assembly, Lord Brahmā. After offering Lord Brahmā respect, Dakṣa, by the order of Brahmā, properly took his seat.**
