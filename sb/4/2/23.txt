====== SB 4.2.23 ======

> बुद्ध्या पराभिध्यायिन्या विस्मृतात्मगतिः पशुः ।
> स्त्रीकामः सोऽस्त्वतितरां दक्षो बस्तमुखोऽचिरात् ॥२३॥

===== Text =====

> buddhyā parābhidhyāyinyā
> vismṛtātma-gatiḥ paśuḥ
> strī-kāmaḥ so 'stv atitarāṁ
> dakṣo basta-mukho 'cirāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:buddhyā]]---by intelligence; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:a:abhidhyāyinyā]]---by accepting the body as the self; [[synonyms:v:vismṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:g:gatiḥ]]---having forgotten the knowledge of Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:p:paśuḥ]]---an animal; [[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:k:kāmaḥ]]---attached to sex life; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Dakṣa); [[synonyms:a:astu]]---let; [[synonyms:a:atitarām]]---excessive; [[synonyms:d:dakṣaḥ]]---Dakṣa; [[synonyms:b:basta]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhaḥ]]---the face of a goat; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---in a very short time.

===== Translation =====

**Dakṣa has accepted the body as all in all. Therefore, since he has forgotten the viṣṇu-pāda, or viṣṇu-gati, and is attached to sex life only, within a short time he will have the face of a goat.**
