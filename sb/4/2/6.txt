====== SB 4.2.6 ======

> उदतिष्ठन्सदस्यास्ते स्वधिष्ण्येभ्यः सहाग्नयः ।
> ऋते विरिञ्चां शर्वं च तद्भासाक्षिप्तचेतसः ॥६॥

===== Text =====

> udatiṣṭhan sadasyās te
> sva-dhiṣṇyebhyaḥ sahāgnayaḥ
> ṛte viriñcāṁ śarvaṁ ca
> tad-bhāsākṣipta-cetasaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:udatiṣṭhan]]---stood up; [[synonyms:s:sadasyāḥ]]---the members of the assembly; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:d:dhiṣṇyebhyaḥ]]---from their own seats; [[synonyms:s:saha]]-[[synonyms:a:agnayaḥ]]---along with the fire-gods; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛte]]---except for; [[synonyms:v:viriñcām]]---Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śarvam]]---Śiva; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---his (Dakṣa's); [[synonyms:b:bhāsa]]---by the luster; [[synonyms:ā:ākṣipta]]---are influenced; [[synonyms:c:cetasaḥ]]---those whose minds.

===== Translation =====

**Influenced by his personal bodily luster, all the fire-gods and other participants in that great assembly, with the exceptions of Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva, gave up their own sitting places and stood in respect for Dakṣa.**
