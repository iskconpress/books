====== SB 4.2.12 ======

> गृहीत्वा मृगशावाक्ष्याः पाणिं मर्कटलोचनः ।
> प्रत्युत्थानाभिवादार्हे वाचाप्यकृत नोचितम् ॥१२॥

===== Text =====

> gṛhītvā mṛga-śāvākṣyāḥ
> pāṇiṁ markaṭa-locanaḥ
> pratyutthānābhivādārhe
> vācāpy akṛta nocitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gṛhītvā]]---taking; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāva]]---like a deer cub; [[synonyms:a:akṣyāḥ]]---of her who has eyes; [[synonyms:p:pāṇim]]---the hand; [[synonyms:m:markaṭa]]---of a monkey; [[synonyms:l:locanaḥ]]---he who has the eyes; [[synonyms:p:pratyutthāna]]---of rising from one's seat; [[synonyms:a:abhivāda]]---the honor; [[synonyms:a:arhe]]---to me, who deserves; [[synonyms:v:vācā]]---with sweet words; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:a:akṛta]] [[synonyms:n:na]]---he did not do; [[synonyms:u:ucitam]]---honor.

===== Translation =====

**He has eyes like a monkey's, yet he has married my daughter, whose eyes are just like those of a deer cub. Nevertheless he did not stand up to receive me, nor did he think it fit to welcome me with sweet words.**
