====== SB 4.9.57 ======

> मृष्टचत्वररथ्याट्ट मार्गं चन्दनचर्चितम् ।
> लाजाक्षतैः पुष्पफलैस्तण्डुलैर्बलिभिर्युतम् ॥५७॥

===== Text =====

> mṛṣṭa-catvara-rathyāṭṭa-
> mārgaṁ candana-carcitam
> lājākṣataiḥ puṣpa-phalais
> taṇḍulair balibhir yutam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mṛṣṭa]]---fully cleansed; [[synonyms:c:catvara]]---quadrangles; [[synonyms:r:rathyā]]---highways; [[synonyms:a:aṭṭa]]---raised sitting places; [[synonyms:m:mārgam]]---lanes; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---with sandalwood; [[synonyms:c:carcitam]]---sprinkled; [[synonyms:l:lāja]]---with fried rice; [[synonyms:a:akṣataiḥ]]---and barley; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]---with flowers; [[synonyms:p:phalaiḥ]]---and fruits; [[synonyms:t:taṇḍulaiḥ]]---with rice; [[synonyms:b:balibhiḥ]]---auspicious presentations; [[synonyms:y:yutam]]---provided with.

===== Translation =====

**All the quadrangles, lanes and streets in the city, and the raised sitting places at the crossings, were thoroughly cleansed and sprinkled with sandalwood water; and auspicious grains such as rice and barley, and flowers, fruits and many other auspicious presentations were scattered all over the city.**
