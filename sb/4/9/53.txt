====== SB 4.9.53 ======

> लाल्यमानं जनैरेवं ध्रुवं सभ्रातरं नृपः ।
> आरोप्य करिणीं हृष्टः स्तूयमानोऽविशत्पुरम् ॥५३॥

===== Text =====

> lālyamānaṁ janair evaṁ
> dhruvaṁ sabhrātaraṁ nṛpaḥ
> āropya kariṇīṁ hṛṣṭaḥ
> stūyamāno 'viśat puram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lālyamānam]]---being thus praised; [[synonyms:j:janaiḥ]]---by the people in general; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:dhruvam]]---Mahārāja Dhruva; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrātaram]]---with his brother; [[synonyms:n:nṛpaḥ]]---the King; [[synonyms:ā:āropya]]---placing; [[synonyms:k:kariṇīm]]---on the back of a she-elephant; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣṭaḥ]]---being so pleased; [[synonyms:s:stūyamānaḥ]]---and being so praised; [[synonyms:a:aviśat]]---returned; [[synonyms:p:puram]]---to his capital.

===== Translation =====

**The sage Maitreya continued: My dear Vidura, when everyone was thus praising Dhruva Mahārāja, the King was very happy, and he had Dhruva and his brother seated on the back of a she-elephant. Thus he returned to his capital, where he was praised by all classes of men.**
