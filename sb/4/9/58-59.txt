====== SB 4.9.58-59 ======

> ध्रुवाय पथि दृष्टाय तत्र तत्र पुरस्त्रियः ।
> सिद्धार्थाक्षतदध्यम्बु दूर्वापुष्पफलानि च ॥५८॥
> उपजह्रुः प्रयुञ्जाना वात्सल्यादाशिषः सतीः ।
> शृण्वंस्तद्वल्गुगीतानि प्राविशद्भवनं पितुः ॥५९॥

===== Text =====

> dhruvāya pathi dṛṣṭāya
> tatra tatra pura-striyaḥ
> siddhārthākṣata-dadhy-ambu-
> dūrvā-puṣpa-phalāni ca
>
> upajahruḥ prayuñjānā
> vātsalyād āśiṣaḥ satīḥ
> śṛṇvaṁs tad-valgu-gītāni
> prāviśad bhavanaṁ pituḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhruvāya]]---on Dhruva; [[synonyms:p:pathi]]---on the road; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭāya]]---seen; [[synonyms:t:tatra]] [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---here and there; [[synonyms:p:pura]]-[[synonyms:s:striyaḥ]]---household ladies; [[synonyms:s:siddhārtha]]---white mustard seed; [[synonyms:a:akṣata]]---barley; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---curd; [[synonyms:a:ambu]]---water; [[synonyms:d:dūrvā]]---newly grown grass; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]---flowers; [[synonyms:p:phalāni]]---fruits; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:u:upajahruḥ]]---they showered; [[synonyms:p:prayuñjānāḥ]]---uttering; [[synonyms:v:vātsalyāt]]---out of affection; [[synonyms:ā:āśiṣaḥ]]---blessings; [[synonyms:s:satīḥ]]---gentle ladies; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇvan]]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---their; [[synonyms:v:valgu]]---very pleasing; [[synonyms:g:gītāni]]---songs; [[synonyms:p:prāviśat]]---he entered; [[synonyms:b:bhavanam]]---the palace; [[synonyms:p:pituḥ]]---of his father.

===== Translation =====

**Thus as Dhruva Mahārāja passed on the road, from every place in the neighborhood all the gentle household ladies assembled to see him, and out of maternal affection they offered their blessings, showering him with white mustard seed, barley, curd, water, newly grown grass, fruits and flowers. In this way Dhruva Mahārāja, while hearing the pleasing songs sung by the ladies, entered the palace of his father.**
