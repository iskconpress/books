====== SB 4.9.51 ======

> तां शशंसुर्जना राज्ञीं दिष्ट्या ते पुत्र आर्तिहा ।
> प्रतिलब्धश्चिरं नष्टो रक्षिता मण्डलं भुवः ॥५१॥

===== Text =====

> tāṁ śaśaṁsur janā rājñīṁ
> diṣṭyā te putra ārti-hā
> pratilabdhaś ciraṁ naṣṭo
> rakṣitā maṇḍalaṁ bhuvaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tām]]---unto Queen Sunīti; [[synonyms:ś:śaśaṁsuḥ]]---offered praise; [[synonyms:j:janāḥ]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:r:rājñīm]]---unto the Queen; [[synonyms:d:diṣṭyā]]---by fortune; [[synonyms:t:te]]---your; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:ā:ārti]]-[[synonyms:h:hā]]---will vanquish all your pains; [[synonyms:p:pratilabdhaḥ]]---now returned; [[synonyms:c:ciram]]---since a long time; [[synonyms:n:naṣṭaḥ]]---lost; [[synonyms:r:rakṣitā]]---will protect; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍalam]]---the globe; [[synonyms:b:bhuvaḥ]]---earthly.

===== Translation =====

**The residents of the palace praised the Queen: Dear Queen, your beloved son was lost a long time ago, and it is your great fortune that he now has come back. It appears, therefore, that your son will be able to protect you for a very long time and will put an end to all your material pangs.**
