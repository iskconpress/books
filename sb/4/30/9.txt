====== SB 4.30.9 ======

> योऽनुस्मरति सन्ध्यायां युष्माननुदिनं नरः ।
> तस्य भ्रातृष्वात्मसाम्यं तथा भूतेषु सौहृदम् ॥९॥

===== Text =====

> yo 'nusmarati sandhyāyāṁ
> yuṣmān anudinaṁ naraḥ
> tasya bhrātṛṣv ātma-sāmyaṁ
> tathā bhūteṣu sauhṛdam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---one who; [[synonyms:a:anusmarati]]---always remembers; [[synonyms:s:sandhyāyām]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:y:yuṣmān]]---you; [[synonyms:a:anudinam]]---every day; [[synonyms:n:naraḥ]]---human being; [[synonyms:t:tasya]] [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛṣu]]---with his brothers; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sāmyam]]---personal equality; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as also; [[synonyms:b:bhūteṣu]]---with all living beings; [[synonyms:s:sauhṛdam]]---friendship.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord continued: Those who remember you every evening of every day will become friendly with their brothers and with all other living entities.**
