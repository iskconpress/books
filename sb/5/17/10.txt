====== SB 5.17.10 ======

===== Text =====


> anye ca nadā nadyaś ca varṣe varṣe santi bahuśo merv-ādi-giri-duhitaraḥ śataśaḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anye]]---many others; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:n:nadāḥ]]---rivers; [[synonyms:n:nadyaḥ]]---small rivers; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:varṣe]] [[synonyms:v:varṣe]]---in each tract of land; [[synonyms:s:santi]]---are; [[synonyms:b:bahuśaḥ]]---of many varieties; [[synonyms:m:meru]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:g:giri]]-[[synonyms:d:duhitaraḥ]]---daughters of the mountains beginning with Meru; [[synonyms:ś:śataśaḥ]]---in the hundreds.

===== Translation =====

**Many other rivers, both big and small, flow from the top of Mount Meru. These rivers are like daughters of the mountain, and they flow to the various tracts of land in hundreds of branches.**
