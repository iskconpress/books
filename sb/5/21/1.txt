====== SB 5.21.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> etāvān eva bhū-valayasya sanniveśaḥ pramāṇa-lakṣaṇato vyākhyātaḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:etāvān]]---so much; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:b:bhū]]-[[synonyms:v:valayasya]] [[synonyms:s:sanniveśaḥ]]---the arrangement of the whole universe; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇataḥ]]---according to measurement (fifty //crores// of //yojanas,// or four billion miles in width and length) and characteristics; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyātaḥ]]---estimated.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: My dear King, I have thus far described the diameter of the universe [fifty crores of yojanas, or four billion miles] and its general characteristics, according to the estimations of learned scholars.**
