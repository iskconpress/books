====== SB 5.21.6 ======

===== Text =====

> yāvad dakṣiṇāyanam ahāni vardhante yāvad udagayanaṁ rātrayaḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---until; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:ayanam]]---the sun passes to the southern side; [[synonyms:a:ahāni]]---the days; [[synonyms:v:vardhante]]---increase; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---until; [[synonyms:u:udagayanam]]---the sun passes to the northern side; [[synonyms:r:rātrayaḥ]]---the nights.

===== Translation =====

**Until the sun travels to the south the days grow longer, and until it travels to the north the nights grow longer.**
