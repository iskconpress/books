====== SB 5.21.5 ======

===== Text =====

> yadā vṛścikādiṣu pañcasu vartate tadāho-rātrāṇi viparyayāṇi bhavanti.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:v:vṛścika]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---headed by Vṛścika (Scorpio); [[synonyms:p:pañcasu]]---five; [[synonyms:v:vartate]]---remains; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:a:ahaḥ]]-[[synonyms:r:rātrāṇi]]---the days and nights; [[synonyms:v:viparyayāṇi]]---the opposite (the duration of the day decreases, and that of night increases); [[synonyms:b:bhavanti]]---are.

===== Translation =====

**When the sun passes through the five signs beginning with Vṛścika [Scorpio], the duration of the days decreases [until Capricorn], and then gradually it increases month after month, until day and night become equal [in Aries].**
