====== SB 5.19.1 ======

===== Text =====


> śrī-śuka uvāca
> kimpuruṣe varṣe bhagavantam ādi-puruṣaṁ lakṣmaṇāgrajaṁ sītābhirāmaṁ rāmaṁ tac-caraṇa-sannikarṣābhirataḥ parama-bhāgavato hanumān saha kimpuruṣair avirata-bhaktir upāste.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued to speak; [[synonyms:k:kimpuruṣe]] [[synonyms:v:varṣe]]---the tract of land known as Kimpuruṣa; [[synonyms:b:bhagavantam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣam]]---the original cause of all causes; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:agra]]-[[synonyms:j:jam]]---the elder brother of Lakṣmaṇa; [[synonyms:s:sītā]]-[[synonyms:a:abhirāmam]]---who is very pleasing to mother Sītā, or who is the husband of Sītādevī; [[synonyms:r:rāmam]]---Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sannikarṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:abhirataḥ]]---one always engaged in service at the lotus feet of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavataḥ]]---the great devotee celebrated throughout the universe; [[synonyms:h:hanumān]]---His Grace Hanumānjī; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:k:kimpuruṣaiḥ]]---the inhabitants of the tract of land known as Kimpuruṣa; [[synonyms:a:avirata]]---continuous; [[synonyms:b:bhaktiḥ]]---who possesses devotional service; [[synonyms:u:upāste]]---worships.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: My dear King, in Kimpuruṣa-varṣa the great devotee Hanumān is always engaged with the inhabitants of that land in devotional service to Lord Rāmacandra, the elder brother of Lakṣmaṇa and dear husband of Sītādevī.**
