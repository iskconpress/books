====== SB 5.24.12 ======

===== Text =====

> yatra hi mahāhi-pravara-śiro-maṇayaḥ sarvaṁ tamaḥ prabādhante.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yatra]]---where; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:ahi]]---of great serpents; [[synonyms:p:pravara]]---of the best; [[synonyms:ś:śiraḥ]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇayaḥ]]---the gems on the hoods; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---darkness; [[synonyms:p:prabādhante]]---drive away.

===== Translation =====

**Many great serpents reside there with gems on their hoods, and the effulgence of these gems dissipates the darkness in all directions.**
