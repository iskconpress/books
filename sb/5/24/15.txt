====== SB 5.24.15 ======

===== Text =====


> yasmin praviṣṭe 'sura-vadhūnāṁ prāyaḥ puṁsavanāni bhayād eva sravanti patanti ca.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yasmin]]---where; [[synonyms:p:praviṣṭe]]---when entered; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhūnām]]---of the wives of those demons; [[synonyms:p:prāyaḥ]]---almost always; [[synonyms:p:puṁsavanāni]]---fetuses; [[synonyms:b:bhayāt]]---because of fear; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:sravanti]]---slip out; [[synonyms:p:patanti]]---fall down; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**When the Sudarśana disc enters those provinces, the pregnant wives of the demons all have miscarriages due to fear of its effulgence.**
