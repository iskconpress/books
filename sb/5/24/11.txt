====== SB 5.24.11 ======

===== Text =====

> yatra ha vāva na bhayam aho-rātrādibhiḥ kāla-vibhāgair upalakṣyate.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yatra]]---where; [[synonyms:h:ha]] [[synonyms:v:vāva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:b:bhayam]]---fearfulness; [[synonyms:a:ahaḥ]]-[[synonyms:r:rātra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---because of days and nights; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhāgaiḥ]]---the divisions of time; [[synonyms:u:upalakṣyate]]---is experienced.

===== Translation =====

**Since there is no sunshine in those subterranean planets, time is not divided into days and nights, and consequently fear produced by time does not exist.**
