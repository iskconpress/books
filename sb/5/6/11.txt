====== SB 5.6.11 ======

===== Text =====

> te ca hy arvāktanayā nija-loka-yātrayāndha-paramparayāśvastās tamasy andhe svayam eva prapatiṣyanti.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:te]]---those people not following the Vedic principles; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:arvāktanayā]]---deviating from the eternal principles of Vedic religion; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrayā]]---by a practice arrived at by their own mental concoction; [[synonyms:a:andha]]-[[synonyms:p:paramparayā]]---by a disciplic succession of blind. ignorant people; [[synonyms:ā:āśvastāḥ]]---being encouraged; [[synonyms:t:tamasi]]---into the darkness of ignorance; [[synonyms:a:andhe]]---blindness; [[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---themselves; [[synonyms:p:prapatiṣyanti]]---will fall down.

===== Translation =====

**Low-class people, due to their gross ignorance, introduce a system of religion that deviates from the Vedic principles. Following their own mental concoctions, they automatically fall down into the darkest regions of existence.**

===== Purport =====

In this connection, one may see //Bhagavad-gītā,// Chapter Sixteen. where there is a description of the downfall of the //asuras// (16.16 and 16.23).
