====== SB 5.18.24 ======

===== Text =====


> ramyake ca bhagavataḥ priyatamaṁ mātsyam avatāra-rūpaṁ tad-varṣa-puruṣasya manoḥ prāk-pradarśitaṁ sa idānīm api mahatā bhakti-yogenārādhayatīdaṁ codāharati.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ramyake]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also in Ramyaka-varṣa; [[synonyms:b:bhagavataḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:t:tamam]]---the foremost; [[synonyms:m:mātsyam]]---fish; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---the form of the incarnation; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣasya]]---of the ruler of that land; [[synonyms:m:manoḥ]]---Manu; [[synonyms:p:prāk]]---previously (at the end of the Cākṣuṣa-manvantara); [[synonyms:p:pradarśitam]]---exhibited; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that Manu; [[synonyms:i:idānīm]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even until now; [[synonyms:m:mahatā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:y:yogena]]---by dint of advanced devotional service; [[synonyms:ā:ārādhayati]]---worships the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:u:udāharati]]---chants.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: In Ramyaka-varṣa, where Vaivasvata Manu rules, the Supreme Personality of Godhead appeared as Lord Matsya at the end of the last era [the Cākṣuṣa-manvantara]. Vaivasvata Manu now worships Lord Matsya in pure devotional service and chants the following mantra.**
