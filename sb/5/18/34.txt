====== SB 5.18.34 ======

===== Text =====


> uttareṣu ca kuruṣu bhagavān yajña-puruṣaḥ kṛta-varāha-rūpa āste taṁ tu devī haiṣā bhūḥ saha kurubhir askhalita-bhakti-yogenopadhāvati imāṁ ca paramām upaniṣadam āvartayati.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttareṣu]]---on the northern side; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kuruṣu]]---in the tract of land known as Kuru; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣaḥ]]---who accepts all results of sacrifices; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:v:varāha]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpaḥ]]---having accepted the form of a boar; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]---exists eternally; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---Him; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:devī]]---the goddess; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---certainly; [[synonyms:e:eṣā]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhūḥ]]---planet earth; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---along with; [[synonyms:k:kurubhiḥ]]---the inhabitants of the land known as Kuru; [[synonyms:a:askhalita]]---unfailing; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:y:yogena]]---by devotional service; [[synonyms:u:upadhāvati]]---worship; [[synonyms:i:imām]]---this; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:p:paramām]] [[synonyms:u:upaniṣadam]]---the supreme //Upaniṣad// (the process by which one can approach the Lord); [[synonyms:ā:āvartayati]]---chants again and again for the purpose of practice.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Dear King, the Supreme Lord in His boar incarnation, who accepts all sacrificial offerings, lives in the northern part of Jambūdvīpa. There, in the tract of land known as Uttarakuru-varṣa, mother earth and all the other inhabitants worship Him with unfailing devotional service by repeatedly chanting the following Upaniṣad mantra.**
