====== SB 5.18.39 ======

===== Text =====

> pramathya daityaṁ prativāraṇaṁ mṛdhe
> yo māṁ rasāyā jagad-ādi-sūkaraḥ
> kṛtvāgra-daṁṣṭre niragād udanvataḥ
> krīḍann ivebhaḥ praṇatāsmi taṁ vibhum iti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pramathya]]---after killing; [[synonyms:d:daityam]]---the demon; [[synonyms:p:prativāraṇam]]---most formidable opponent; [[synonyms:m:mṛdhe]]---in the fight; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---He who; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---me (the earth); [[synonyms:r:rasāyāḥ]]---fallen to the bottom of the universe; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---in this material world; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:s:sūkaraḥ]]---the original form of a boar; [[synonyms:k:kṛtvā]]---keeping it; [[synonyms:a:agra]]-[[synonyms:d:daṁṣṭre]]---on the end of the tusk; [[synonyms:n:niragāt]]---came out of the water; [[synonyms:u:udanvataḥ]]---from the Garbhodaka Ocean; [[synonyms:k:krīḍan]]---playing; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:i:ibhaḥ]]---elephant; [[synonyms:p:praṇatā]] [[synonyms:a:asmi]]---I bow down; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---to Him; [[synonyms:v:vibhum]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus.

===== Translation =====

**My Lord, as the original boar within this universe, You fought and killed the great demon Hiraṇyakṣa. Then You lifted me [the earth] from the Garbhodaka Ocean on the end of Your tusk, exactly as a sporting elephant plucks a lotus flower from the water. I bow down before You.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Fifth Canto, Eighteenth Chapter of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled, "The prayers Offered to the Lord by the Residents of Jambūdvīpa."
