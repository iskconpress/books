====== SB 5.26.19 ======

===== Text =====


> yas tv iha vai steyena balād vā hiraṇya-ratnādīni brāhmaṇasya vāpaharaty anyasya vānāpadi puruṣas tam amutra rājan yama-puruṣā ayasmayair agni-piṇḍaiḥ sandaṁśais tvaci niṣkuṣanti.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---any person who; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in this life; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:steyena]]---by thievery; [[synonyms:b:balāt]]---by force; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇya]]---gold; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]---gems; [[synonyms:ā:ādīni]]---and so on; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇasya]]---of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:a:apaharati]]---steals; [[synonyms:a:anyasya]]---of others; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:a:anāpadi]]---not in a calamity; [[synonyms:p:puruṣaḥ]]---a person; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:a:amutra]]---in the next life; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:y:yama]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣāḥ]]---the agents of Yamarāja; [[synonyms:a:ayaḥ]]-[[synonyms:m:mayaiḥ]]---made of iron; [[synonyms:a:agni]]-[[synonyms:p:piṇḍaiḥ]]---balls heated in fire; [[synonyms:s:sandaṁśaiḥ]]---with tongs; [[synonyms:t:tvaci]]---on the skin; [[synonyms:n:niṣkuṣanti]]---tear to pieces.

===== Translation =====

**My dear King, a person who in the absence of an emergency robs a brāhmaṇa---or, indeed, anyone else---of his gems and gold is put into a hell known as Sandaṁśa. There his skin is torn and separated by red-hot iron balls and tongs. In this way, his entire body is cut to pieces.**
