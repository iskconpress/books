====== SB 5.25.2 ======

===== Text =====


> yasyedaṁ kṣiti-maṇḍalaṁ bhagavato 'nanta-mūrteḥ sahasra-śirasa ekasminn eva śīrṣaṇi dhriyamāṇaṁ siddhārtha iva lakṣyate.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kṣiti]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍalam]]---universe; [[synonyms:b:bhagavataḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrteḥ]]---in the form of Anantadeva; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śirasaḥ]]---who has thousands of hoods; [[synonyms:e:ekasmin]]---on one; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:ś:śīrṣaṇi]]---hood; [[synonyms:d:dhriyamāṇam]]---is being sustained; [[synonyms:s:siddhārthaḥ]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---and like a white mustard seed; [[synonyms:l:lakṣyate]]---is seen.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: This great universe, situated on one of Lord Anantadeva's thousands of hoods, appears just like a white mustard seed. It is infinitesimal compared to the hood of Lord Ananta.**
