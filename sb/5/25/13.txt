====== SB 5.25.13 ======

===== Text =====

> evam-prabhāvo bhagavān ananto
> duranta-vīryoru-guṇānubhāvaḥ
> mūle rasāyāḥ sthita ātma-tantro
> yo līlayā kṣmāṁ sthitaye bibharti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhāvaḥ]]---who is so powerful; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:anantaḥ]]---Ananta; [[synonyms:d:duranta]]-[[synonyms:v:vīrya]]---insurmountable prowess; [[synonyms:u:uru]]---great; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhāvaḥ]]---possessing transcendental qualities and glories; [[synonyms:m:mūle]]---at the base; [[synonyms:r:rasāyāḥ]]---of the lower planetary systems; [[synonyms:s:sthitaḥ]]---existing; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:t:tantraḥ]]---completely self-sufficient; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:l:līlayā]]---easily; [[synonyms:k:kṣmām]]---the universe; [[synonyms:s:sthitaye]]---for its maintenance; [[synonyms:b:bibharti]]---sustains.

===== Translation =====

**There is no end to the great and glorious qualities of that powerful Lord Anantadeva. Indeed, His prowess is unlimited. Though self-sufficient, He Himself is the support of everything. He resides beneath the lower planetary systems and easily sustains the entire universe.**
