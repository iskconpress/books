====== SB 5.8.1 ======

===== Text =====


> śrī-śuka uvāca
> ekadā tu mahā-nadyāṁ kṛtābhiṣeka-naiyamikāvaśyako brahmākṣaram abhigṛṇāno muhūrta-trayam udakānta upaviveśa.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:ekadā]]---once upon a time; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:n:nadyām]]---in the great river known as Gaṇḍakī; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:abhiṣeka]]-[[synonyms:n:naiyamika]]-[[synonyms:a:avaśyakaḥ]]---having taken a bath after finishing the daily external duties such as passing stool and urine and brushing the teeth; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣaram]]---the //praṇava-mantra// (//oṁ//); [[synonyms:a:abhigṛṇānaḥ]]---chanting; [[synonyms:m:muhūrta]]-[[synonyms:t:trayam]]---for three minutes; [[synonyms:u:udaka]]-[[synonyms:a:ante]]---on the bank of the river; [[synonyms:u:upaviveśa]]---he sat down.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: My dear King, one day, after finishing his morning duties---evacuating, urinating and bathing---Mahārāja Bharata sat down on the bank of the River Gaṇḍakī for a few minutes and began chanting his mantra, beginning with oṁkāra.**
