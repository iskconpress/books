====== SB 5.8.3 ======

===== Text =====


> tayā pepīyamāna udake tāvad evāvidūreṇa nadato mṛga-pater unnādo loka-bhayaṅkara udapatat.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tayā]]---by the doe; [[synonyms:p:pepīyamāne]]---being drunk with great satisfaction; [[synonyms:u:udake]]---the water; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---exactly at that time; [[synonyms:a:avidūreṇa]]---very near; [[synonyms:n:nadataḥ]]---roaring; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:p:pateḥ]]---of one lion; [[synonyms:u:unnādaḥ]]---the tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhayam]]-[[synonyms:k:kara]]---very fearful to all living entities; [[synonyms:u:udapatat]]---arose.

===== Translation =====

**While the doe was drinking with great satisfaction, a lion, which was very close, roared very loudly. This was frightful to every living entity, and it was heard by the doe.**
