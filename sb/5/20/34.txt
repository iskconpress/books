====== SB 5.20.34 ======

===== Text =====


> tataḥ parastāl lokāloka-nāmācalo lokālokayor antarāle parita upakṣiptaḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from that ocean of sweet drinking water; [[synonyms:p:parastāt]]---beyond; [[synonyms:l:lokāloka]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named Lokāloka; [[synonyms:a:acalaḥ]]---a mountain; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:a:alokayoḥ]] [[synonyms:a:antarāle]]---between the countries full of sunlight and those without sunlight; [[synonyms:p:paritaḥ]]---all around; [[synonyms:u:upakṣiptaḥ]]---exists.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, beyond the ocean of sweet water and fully surrounding it, is a mountain named Lokāloka, which divides the countries that are full of sunlight from those not lit by the sun.**
