====== SB 5.20.11 ======

===== Text =====


> tad-varṣa-puruṣāḥ śrutadhara-vīryadhara-vasundhareṣandhara-saṁjñā bhagavantaṁ vedamayaṁ somam ātmānaṁ vedena yajante.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣāḥ]]---the residents of those tracts of land; [[synonyms:ś:śrutadhara]]---Śrutadhara; [[synonyms:v:vīryadhara]]---Vīryadhara; [[synonyms:v:vasundhara]]---Vasundhara; [[synonyms:i:iṣandhara]]---Iṣandhara; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñāḥ]]---known as; [[synonyms:b:bhagavantam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:m:mayam]]---fully conversant with the Vedic knowledge; [[synonyms:s:somam]] [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---represented by the living entity known as Soma; [[synonyms:v:vedena]]---by following the Vedic rules and regulations; [[synonyms:y:yajante]]---they worship.

===== Translation =====

**Strictly following the cult of varṇāśrama-dharma, the inhabitants of those islands, who are known as Śrutidharas, Vīryadharas, Vasundharas and Iṣandharas, all worship the expansion of the Supreme Personality of Godhead named Soma, the moon-god.**
