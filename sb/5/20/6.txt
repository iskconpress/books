====== SB 5.20.6 ======

===== Text =====


> plakṣādiṣu pañcasu puruṣāṇām āyur indriyam ojaḥ saho balaṁ buddhir vikrama iti ca sarveṣām autpattikī siddhir aviśeṣeṇa vartate.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:plakṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---in the islands headed by Plakṣa; [[synonyms:p:pañcasu]]---five; [[synonyms:p:puruṣāṇām]]---of the inhabitants; [[synonyms:ā:āyuḥ]]---long duration of life; [[synonyms:i:indriyam]]---soundness of the senses; [[synonyms:o:ojaḥ]]---bodily strength; [[synonyms:s:sahaḥ]]---mental strength; [[synonyms:b:balam]]---physical strength; [[synonyms:b:buddhiḥ]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:v:vikramaḥ]]---bravery; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:sarveṣām]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:a:autpattikī]]---inborn; [[synonyms:s:siddhiḥ]]---perfection; [[synonyms:a:aviśeṣeṇa]]---without distinction; [[synonyms:v:vartate]]---exists.

===== Translation =====

**O King, longevity, sensory prowess, physical and mental strength, intelligence and bravery are naturally and equally manifested in all the inhabitants of the five islands headed by Plakṣadvīpa.**
