====== SB 5.20.26 ======

===== Text =====


> eteṣāṁ varṣa-maryādā-girayo nadyaś ca sapta saptaiva īśāna uruśṛṅgo balabhadraḥ śatakesaraḥ sahasrasroto devapālo mahānasa iti anaghāyurdā ubhayaspṛṣṭir aparājitā pañcapadī sahasrasrutir nijadhṛtir iti.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eteṣām]]---of all these divisions; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:m:maryādā]]---acting as the boundary limits; [[synonyms:g:girayaḥ]]---the big hills; [[synonyms:n:nadyaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and the rivers also; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]---seven; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]---seven; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ī:īśānaḥ]]---Īśāna; [[synonyms:u:uruśṛṅgaḥ]]---Uruśṛṅga; [[synonyms:b:bala]]-[[synonyms:b:bhadraḥ]]---Balabhadra; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:k:kesaraḥ]]---Śatakesara; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:s:srotaḥ]]---Sahasrasrota; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:p:pālaḥ]]---Devapāla; [[synonyms:m:mahānasaḥ]]---Mahānasa; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:anaghā]]---Anaghā; [[synonyms:ā:āyurdā]]---Āyurdā; [[synonyms:u:ubhayaspṛṣṭiḥ]]---Ubhayaspṛṣṭi; [[synonyms:a:aparājitā]]---Aparājitā; [[synonyms:p:pañcapadī]]---Pañcapadī; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:s:srutiḥ]]---Sahasra-śruti; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:d:dhṛtiḥ]]---Nijadhṛti; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus.

===== Translation =====

**For these lands also, there are seven boundary mountains and seven rivers. The mountains are Īśāna, Uruśṛṅga, Balabhadra, Śatakesara, Sahasrasrota, Devapāla and Mahānasa. The rivers are Anaghā, Āyurdā, Ubhayaspṛṣṭi, Aparājitā, Pañcapadī, Sahasra-śruti and Nijadhṛti.**
