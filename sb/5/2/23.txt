====== SB 5.2.23 ======

===== Text =====


> samparete pitari nava bhrātaro meru-duhitṝr merudevīṁ pratirūpām ugradaṁṣṭrīṁ latāṁ ramyāṁ śyāmāṁ nārīṁ bhadrāṁ devavītim iti saṁjñā navodavahan.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:samparete]] [[synonyms:p:pitari]]---after the departure of their father; [[synonyms:n:nava]]---nine; [[synonyms:b:bhrātaraḥ]]---brothers; [[synonyms:m:meru]]-[[synonyms:d:duhitṝḥ]]---the daughters of Meru; [[synonyms:m:merudevīm]]---Merudevī; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpām]]---Pratirūpā; [[synonyms:u:ugra]]-[[synonyms:d:daṁṣṭrīm]]---Ugradaṁṣṭrī; [[synonyms:l:latām]]---Latā; [[synonyms:r:ramyām]]---Ramyā; [[synonyms:ś:śyāmām]]---Śyāmā; [[synonyms:n:nārīm]]---Nārī; [[synonyms:b:bhadrām]]---Bhadrā; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:v:vītim]]---Devavīti; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñāḥ]]---the names; [[synonyms:n:nava]]---nine; [[synonyms:u:udavahan]]---married.

===== Translation =====

**After the departure of their father, the nine brothers married the nine daughters of Meru named Merudevī, Pratirūpā, Ugradaṁṣṭrī, Latā, Ramyā, Śyāmā, Nārī, Bhadrā and Devavīti.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Fifth Canto, Second Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "The Activities of Mahārāja Āgnīdhra."
