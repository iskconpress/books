====== SB 5.16.26 ======

===== Text =====


>  kuraṅga-kurara-kusumbha-vaikaṅka-trikūṭa-śiśira-pataṅga-rucaka-niṣadha-śinīvāsa-kapila-śaṅkha-vaidūrya-jārudhi-haṁsa-ṛṣabha-nāga-kālañjara-nāradādayo viṁśati-girayo meroḥ karṇikāyā iva kesara-bhūtā mūla-deśe parita upakḷptāḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuraṅga]]---Kuraṅga; [[synonyms:k:kurara]]---Kurara; [[synonyms:k:kusumbha]]-[[synonyms:v:vaikaṅka]]-[[synonyms:t:trikūṭa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiśira]]-[[synonyms:p:pataṅga]]-[[synonyms:r:rucaka]]-[[synonyms:n:niṣadha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śinīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:k:kapila]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]-[[synonyms:v:vaidūrya]]-[[synonyms:j:jārudhi]]-[[synonyms:h:haṁsa]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabha]]-[[synonyms:n:nāga]]-[[synonyms:k:kālañjara]]-[[synonyms:n:nārada]]---the names of mountains; [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and so on; [[synonyms:v:viṁśati]]-[[synonyms:g:girayaḥ]]---twenty mountains; [[synonyms:m:meroḥ]]---of Sumeru Mountain; [[synonyms:k:karṇikāyāḥ]]---of the whorl of the lotus; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:k:kesara]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtāḥ]]---as filaments; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---at the base; [[synonyms:p:paritaḥ]]---all around; [[synonyms:u:upakḷptāḥ]]---arranged by the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**There are other mountains beautifully arranged around the foot of Mount Meru like the filaments around the whorl of a lotus flower. Their names are Kuraṅga, Kurara, Kusumbha, Vaikaṅka, Trikūṭa, Śiśira, Pataṅga, Rucaka, Niṣadha, Sinīvāsa, Kapila, Śaṅkha, Vaidūrya, Jārudhi, Haṁsa, Ṛṣabha, Nāga, Kālañjara and Nārada.**
