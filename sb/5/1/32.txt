====== SB 5.1.32 ======

===== Text =====


> jambū-plakṣa-śālmali-kuśa-krauñca-śāka-puṣkara-saṁjñās teṣāṁ parimāṇaṁ pūrvasmāt pūrvasmād uttara uttaro yathā-saṅkhyaṁ dvi-guṇa-mānena bahiḥ samantata upakḷptāḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jambū]]---Jambū; [[synonyms:p:plakṣa]]---Plakṣa; [[synonyms:ś:śālmali]]---Śālmali; [[synonyms:k:kuśa]]---Kuśa; [[synonyms:k:krauñca]]---Krauñca; [[synonyms:ś:śāka]]---Śāka; [[synonyms:p:puṣkara]]---Puṣkara; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñāḥ]]---known as; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of them; [[synonyms:p:parimāṇam]]---measurement; [[synonyms:p:pūrvasmāt]] [[synonyms:p:pūrvasmāt]]---from the former; [[synonyms:u:uttaraḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uttaraḥ]]---the following; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---according to; [[synonyms:s:saṅkhyam]]---number; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---twice as much; [[synonyms:m:mānena]]---with a measure; [[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]---outside; [[synonyms:s:samantataḥ]]---all around; [[synonyms:u:upakḷptāḥ]]---produced.

===== Translation =====

**The names of the islands are Jambū, Plakṣa, Śālmali, Kuśa, Krauñca, Śāka and Puṣkara. Each island is twice as large as the one preceding it, and each is surrounded by a liquid substance, beyond which is the next island.**

===== Purport =====

The ocean in each planetary system has a different type of liquid. How they are situated is explained in the next verse.
