====== SB 5.1.34 ======

===== Text =====


> duhitaraṁ corjasvatīṁ nāmośanase prāyacchad yasyām āsīd devayānī nāma kāvya-sutā.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duhitaram]]---the daughter; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ū:ūrjasvatīm]]---Ūrjasvatī; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:u:uśanase]]---unto the great sage Uśanā (Śukrācārya); [[synonyms:p:prāyacchat]]---he gave; [[synonyms:y:yasyām]]---unto whom; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---there was; [[synonyms:d:devayānī]]---Devayānī; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:k:kāvya]]-[[synonyms:s:sutā]]---the daughter of Śukrācārya.

===== Translation =====

**King Priyavrata then gave his daughter, Ūrjasvatī, in marriage to Śukrācārya, who begot in her a daughter named Devayānī.**
