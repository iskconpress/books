====== SB 5.1.25 ======

===== Text =====


>  āgnīdhredhmajihva-yajñabāhu-mahāvīra-hiraṇyareto-ghṛtapṛṣṭha-savana-medhātithi-vītihotra-kavaya iti sarva evāgni-nāmānaḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āgnīdhra]]---Āgnīdhra; [[synonyms:i:idhma]]-[[synonyms:j:jihva]]---Idhmajihva; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhu]]---Yajñabāhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:vīra]]---Mahāvīra; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇya]]-[[synonyms:r:retaḥ]]---Hiraṇyaretā; [[synonyms:g:ghṛtapṛṣṭha]]---Ghṛtapṛṣṭha; [[synonyms:s:savana]]---Savana; [[synonyms:m:medhā]]-[[synonyms:t:tithi]]---Medhātithi; [[synonyms:v:vītihotra]]---Vītihotra; [[synonyms:k:kavayaḥ]]---and Kavi; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all these; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:agni]]---of the demigod controlling fire; [[synonyms:n:nāmānaḥ]]---names.

===== Translation =====

**The ten sons of Mahārāja Priyavrata were named Āgnīdhra, Idhmajihva, Yajñabāhu, Mahāvīra, Hiraṇyaretā, Ghṛtapṛṣṭha, Savana, Medhātithi, Vītihotra and Kavi. These are also names of Agni, the fire-god.**
