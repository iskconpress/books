====== SB 5.15.3 ======

===== Text =====


> athāsuryāṁ tat-tanayo devadyumnas tato dhenumatyāṁ sutaḥ parameṣṭhī tasya suvarcalāyāṁ pratīha upajātaḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ā:āsuryām]]---in the womb of his wife, named Āsurī; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---one son of Devatājit; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:dyumnaḥ]]---named Devadyumna; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Devadyumna; [[synonyms:d:dhenu]]-[[synonyms:m:matyām]]---in the womb of Dhenumatī, the wife of Devadyumna; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---one son; [[synonyms:p:parameṣṭhī]]---named Parameṣṭhī; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Parameṣṭhī; [[synonyms:s:suvarcalāyām]]---in the womb of his wife, named Suvarcalā; [[synonyms:p:pratīhaḥ]]---the son named Pratīha; [[synonyms:u:upajātaḥ]]---appeared.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, in the womb of Āsurī, the wife of Devatājit, a son named Devadyumna was begotten. Devadyumna begot in the womb of his wife, Dhenumatī, a son named Parameṣṭhī. Parameṣṭhī begot a son named Pratīha in the womb of his wife, Suvarcalā.**
