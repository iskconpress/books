====== SB 5.9.7 ======

===== Text =====


> atha yavīyasī dvija-satī sva-garbha-jātaṁ mithunaṁ sapatnyā upanyasya svayam anusaṁsthayā patilokam agāt.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:y:yavīyasī]]---the youngest; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:s:satī]]---wife of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:garbha]]-[[synonyms:j:jātam]]---born of her womb; [[synonyms:m:mithunam]]---the twins; [[synonyms:s:sapatnyai]]---unto the co-wife; [[synonyms:u:upanyasya]]---entrusting; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:a:anusaṁsthayā]]---by following her husband; [[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:l:lokam]]---the planet named Patiloka; [[synonyms:a:agāt]]---went to.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, the brāhmaṇa's younger wife, after entrusting her twin children---the boy and girl---to the elder wife, departed for Patiloka, voluntarily dying with her husband.**
