====== SB 5.9.19 ======

===== Text =====


> evam eva khalu mahad-abhicārāti-kramaḥ kārtsnyenātmane phalati.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:khalu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]---to great personalities; [[synonyms:a:abhicāra]]---in the form of envy; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:k:kramaḥ]]---the limit of offense; [[synonyms:k:kārtsnyena]]---always; [[synonyms:ā:ātmane]]---unto oneself; [[synonyms:p:phalati]]---gives the result.

===== Translation =====

**When an envious person commits an offense before a great personality, he is always punished in the way mentioned above.**
