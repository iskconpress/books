====== SB 5.4.10 ======

===== Text =====


> tam anu kuśāvarta ilāvarto brahmāvarto malayaḥ ketur bhadrasena indraspṛg vidarbhaḥ kīkaṭa iti nava navati pradhānāḥ.

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:a:anu]]---following; [[synonyms:k:kuśāvarta]]---Kuśāvarta; [[synonyms:i:ilāvartaḥ]]---Ilāvarta; [[synonyms:b:brahmāvartaḥ]]---Brahmāvarta; [[synonyms:m:malayaḥ]]---Malaya; [[synonyms:k:ketuḥ]]---Ketu; [[synonyms:b:bhadra]]-[[synonyms:s:senaḥ]]---Bhadrasena; [[synonyms:i:indra]]-[[synonyms:s:spṛk]]---Indraspṛk; [[synonyms:v:vidarbhaḥ]]---Vidarbha; [[synonyms:k:kīkaṭaḥ]]---Kīkaṭa; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:n:nava]]---nine; [[synonyms:n:navati]]---ninety; [[synonyms:p:pradhānāḥ]]---older than.

===== Translation =====

**Following Bharata, there were ninety-nine other sons. Among these were nine elderly sons, named Kuśāvarta, Ilāvarta, Brahmāvarta, Malaya, Ketu, Bhadrasena, Indraspṛk, Vidarbha and Kīkaṭa.**
