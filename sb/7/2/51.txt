====== SB 7.2.51 ======

===== Text =====

> kuliṅga-mithunaṁ tatra
> vicarat samadṛśyata
> tayoḥ kuliṅgī sahasā
> lubdhakena pralobhitā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuliṅga]]-[[synonyms:m:mithunam]]---a pair of (male and female) birds known as //kuliṅga//; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there (where the hunter was hunting); [[synonyms:v:vicarat]]---wandering; [[synonyms:s:samadṛśyata]]---he saw; [[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---of the pair; [[synonyms:k:kuliṅgī]]---the female bird; [[synonyms:s:sahasā]]---suddenly; [[synonyms:l:lubdhakena]]---by the hunter; [[synonyms:p:pralobhitā]]---allured.

===== Translation =====

**While wandering in the forest, the hunter saw a pair of kuliṅga birds. Of the two, the female was captivated by the hunter's lure.**
