====== SB 7.2.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-nārada uvāca
> bhrātary evaṁ vinihate
> hariṇā kroḍa-mūrtinā
> hiraṇyakaśipū rājan
> paryatapyad ruṣā śucā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Nārada Muni said; [[synonyms:b:bhrātari]]---when the brother (Hiraṇyākṣa); [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vinihate]]---was killed; [[synonyms:h:hariṇā]]---by Hari; [[synonyms:k:kroḍa]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrtinā]]---in the form of the boar, Varāha; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyakaśipuḥ]]---Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:p:paryatapyat]]---was afflicted; [[synonyms:r:ruṣā]]---by anger; [[synonyms:ś:śucā]]---by grief.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nārada Muni said: My dear King Yudhiṣṭhira, when Lord Viṣṇu, in the form of Varāha, the boar, killed Hiraṇyākṣa, Hiraṇyākṣa's brother Hiraṇyakaśipu was extremely angry and began to lament.**

===== Purport =====

Yudhiṣṭhira had inquired from Nārada Muni why Hiraṇyakaśipu was so envious of his own son Prahlāda. Nārada Muni began narrating the story by explaining how Hiraṇyakaśipu had become a staunch enemy of Lord Viṣṇu.
