====== SB 7.2.17 ======

===== Text =====

> hiraṇyakaśipur bhrātuḥ
> samparetasya duḥkhitaḥ
> kṛtvā kaṭodakādīni
> bhrātṛ-putrān asāntvayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hiraṇyakaśipuḥ]]---Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:b:bhrātuḥ]]---of the brother; [[synonyms:s:samparetasya]]---deceased; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhitaḥ]]---being very much distressed; [[synonyms:k:kṛtvā]]---performing; [[synonyms:k:kaṭodaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādīni]]---ceremonies observed after a death; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛ]]-[[synonyms:p:putrān]]---the sons of his brother; [[synonyms:a:asāntvayat]]---pacified.

===== Translation =====

**After performing the ritualistic observances for the death of his brother, Hiraṇyakaśipu, being extremely unhappy, tried to pacify his nephews.**
