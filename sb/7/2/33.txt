====== SB 7.2.33 ======

===== Text =====

> aho vidhātrākaruṇena naḥ prabho
> bhavān praṇīto dṛg-agocarāṁ daśām
> uśīnarāṇām asi vṛttidaḥ purā
> kṛto 'dhunā yena śucāṁ vivardhanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aho]]---alas; [[synonyms:v:vidhātrā]]---by providence; [[synonyms:a:akaruṇena]]---who is merciless; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:p:prabho]]---O lord; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---Your Lordship; [[synonyms:p:praṇītaḥ]]---taken away; [[synonyms:d:dṛk]]---of sight; [[synonyms:a:agocarām]]---beyond the range; [[synonyms:d:daśām]]---to a state; [[synonyms:u:uśīnarāṇām]]---to the inhabitants of the state of Uśīnara; [[synonyms:a:asi]]---you were; [[synonyms:v:vṛtti]]-[[synonyms:d:daḥ]]---giving livelihood; [[synonyms:p:purā]]---formerly; [[synonyms:k:kṛtaḥ]]---finished; [[synonyms:a:adhunā]]---now; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:ś:śucām]]---of lamentation; [[synonyms:v:vivardhanaḥ]]---increasing.

===== Translation =====

**O lord, you have now been removed by cruel providence to a state beyond our sight. You had previously sustained the livelihood of the inhabitants of Uśīnara, and thus they were happy, but your condition now is the cause of their unhappiness.**
