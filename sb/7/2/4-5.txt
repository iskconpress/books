====== SB 7.2.4-5 ======

===== Text =====

> bho bho dānava-daiteyā
> dvimūrdhaṁs tryakṣa śambara
> śatabāho hayagrīva
> namuce pāka ilvala
>
> vipracitte mama vacaḥ
> puloman śakunādayaḥ
> śṛṇutānantaraṁ sarve
> kriyatām āśu mā ciram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhoḥ]]---O; [[synonyms:b:bhoḥ]]---O; [[synonyms:d:dānava]]-[[synonyms:d:daiteyāḥ]]---Dānavas and Daityas; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrdhan]]---Dvimūrdha (two-headed); [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣa]]---Tryakṣa (three-eyed); [[synonyms:ś:śambara]]---Śambara; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:b:bāho]]---Śatabāhu (hundred-armed); [[synonyms:h:hayagrīva]]---Hayagrīva (horse-headed); [[synonyms:n:namuce]]---Namuci; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]---Pāka; [[synonyms:i:ilvala]]---Ilvala; [[synonyms:v:vipracitte]]---Vipracitti; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---my; [[synonyms:v:vacaḥ]]---words; [[synonyms:p:puloman]]---Puloma; [[synonyms:ś:śakuna]]---Śakuna; [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and others; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇuta]]---just hear; [[synonyms:a:anantaram]]---after that; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kriyatām]]---let it be done; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]---quickly; [[synonyms:m:mā]]---do not; [[synonyms:c:ciram]]---delay.

===== Translation =====

**O Dānavas and Daityas! O Dvimūrdha, Tryakṣa, Śambara and Śatabāhu ! O Hayagrīva, Namuci, Pāka and Ilvala! O Vipracitti, Puloman, Śakuna and other demons! All of you, kindly hear me attentively and then act according to my words without delay.**
