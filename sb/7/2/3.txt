====== SB 7.2.3 ======

===== Text =====

> karāla-daṁṣṭrogra-dṛṣṭyā
> duṣprekṣya-bhrukuṭī-mukhaḥ
> śūlam udyamya sadasi
> dānavān idam abravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karāla]]-[[synonyms:d:daṁṣṭra]]---with terrible teeth; [[synonyms:u:ugra]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭyā]]---and fierce glance; [[synonyms:d:duṣprekṣya]]---horrible to see; [[synonyms:b:bhru]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṭī]]---with frowning eyebrows; [[synonyms:m:mukhaḥ]]---whose face; [[synonyms:ś:śūlam]]---trident; [[synonyms:u:udyamya]]---raising; [[synonyms:s:sadasi]]---in the assembly; [[synonyms:d:dānavān]]---to the demons; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---spoke.

===== Translation =====

**Exhibiting his terrible teeth, fierce glance and frowning eyebrows, terrible to see, he took up his weapon, a trident, and thus began speaking to his associates, the assembled demons.**
