====== SB 7.4.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-nārada uvāca
> evaṁ vṛtaḥ śata-dhṛtir
> hiraṇyakaśipor atha
> prādāt tat-tapasā prīto
> varāṁs tasya sudurlabhān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Nārada Muni said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]]---solicited; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:d:dhṛtiḥ]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyakaśipoḥ]]---of Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:p:prādāt]]---delivered; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---his; [[synonyms:t:tapasā]]---by the difficult austerities; [[synonyms:p:prītaḥ]]---being pleased; [[synonyms:v:varān]]---benedictions; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---unto Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:d:durlabhān]]---very rarely obtained.

===== Translation =====

**Nārada Muni continued: Lord Brahmā was very much satisfied by Hiraṇyakaśipu's austerities, which were difficult to perform. Therefore, when solicited for benedictions, he indeed granted them, although they were rarely to be achieved.**
