====== SB 7.4.15 ======

===== Text =====

> sa eva varṇāśramibhiḥ
> kratubhir bhūri-dakṣiṇaiḥ
> ijyamāno havir-bhāgān
> agrahīt svena tejasā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Hiraṇyakaśipu); [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:varṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśramibhiḥ]]---by persons who strictly followed the regulative principles of the four //varṇas// and four //āśramas//; [[synonyms:k:kratubhiḥ]]---by ritualistic ceremonies; [[synonyms:b:bhūri]]---abundant; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇaiḥ]]---offered with gifts; [[synonyms:i:ijyamānaḥ]]---being worshiped; [[synonyms:h:haviḥ]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgān]]---the portions of the oblations; [[synonyms:a:agrahīt]]---usurped; [[synonyms:s:svena]]---by his own; [[synonyms:t:tejasā]]---prowess.

===== Translation =====

**Being worshiped by sacrifices offered with great gifts by those who strictly followed the principles of varṇa and āśrama, Hiraṇyakaśipu, instead of offering shares of the oblations to the demigods, accepted them himself.**
