====== SB 7.4.35 ======

===== Text =====

> yaṁ sādhu-gāthā-sadasi
> ripavo 'pi surā nṛpa
> pratimānaṁ prakurvanti
> kim utānye bhavādṛśāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yam]]---whom; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:g:gāthā]]-[[synonyms:s:sadasi]]---in an assembly where saintly persons gather or exalted characteristics are discussed; [[synonyms:r:ripavaḥ]]---persons who were supposed to have been Prahlāda Mahārāja's enemies (even such a devotee as Prahlāda Mahārāja had enemies, including even his own father); [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:s:surāḥ]]---the demigods (the demigods are enemies of the demons, and since Prahlāda Mahārāja was born in a family of demons, the demigods should have been his enemies); [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King Yudhiṣṭhira; [[synonyms:p:pratimānam]]---a substantial example of the best among the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prakurvanti]]---they make; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:u:uta]]---what to speak of; [[synonyms:a:anye]]---others; [[synonyms:b:bhavādṛśāḥ]]---exalted personalities such as yourself.

===== Translation =====

**In any assembly where there are discourses about saints and devotees, O King Yudhiṣṭhira, even the enemies of the demons, namely the demigods, what to speak of you, would cite Prahlāda Mahārāja as an example of a great devotee.**
