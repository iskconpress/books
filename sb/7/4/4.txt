====== SB 7.4.4 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ labdha-varo daityo
> bibhrad dhemamayaṁ vapuḥ
> bhagavaty akarod dveṣaṁ
> bhrātur vadham anusmaran

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:l:labdha]]-[[synonyms:v:varaḥ]]---having obtained his desired boon; [[synonyms:d:daityaḥ]]---Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:b:bibhrat]]---acquiring; [[synonyms:h:hema]]-[[synonyms:m:mayam]]---possessing the luster of gold; [[synonyms:v:vapuḥ]]---a body; [[synonyms:b:bhagavati]]---unto Lord Viṣṇu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:akarot]]---maintained; [[synonyms:d:dveṣam]]---envy; [[synonyms:b:bhrātuḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vadham]]---the killing of his brother; [[synonyms:a:anusmaran]]---always thinking of.

===== Translation =====

**The demon Hiraṇyakaśipu, having thus been blessed by Lord Brahmā and having acquired a lustrous golden body, continued to remember the death of his brother and therefore be envious of Lord Viṣṇu.**

===== Purport =====

A demoniac person, in spite of acquiring all the opulences possible to obtain in this universe, continues to be envious of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
