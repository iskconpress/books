====== SB 7.7.6 ======

===== Text =====

> vyalumpan rāja-śibiram
> amarā jaya-kāṅkṣiṇaḥ
> indras tu rāja-mahiṣīṁ
> mātaraṁ mama cāgrahīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vyalumpan]]---plundered; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:ś:śibiram]]---the palace of my father. Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:a:amarāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāṅkṣiṇaḥ]]---eager to be victorious; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the head of the demigods, King Indra; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:m:mahiṣīm]]---the Queen; [[synonyms:m:mātaram]]---mother; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---my; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:agrahīt]]---captured.

===== Translation =====

**The victorious demigods plundered the palace of Hiraṇyakaśipu, the King of the demons, and destroyed everything within it. Then Indra, King of heaven, arrested my mother, the Queen.**
