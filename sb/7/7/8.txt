====== SB 7.7.8 ======

===== Text =====

> prāha naināṁ sura-pate
> netum arhasy anāgasam
> muñca muñca mahā-bhāga
> satīṁ para-parigraham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prāha]]---he said; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:e:enām]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:p:pate]]---O King of the demigods; [[synonyms:n:netum]]---to drag away; [[synonyms:a:arhasi]]---you deserve; [[synonyms:a:anāgasam]]---not at all sinful; [[synonyms:m:muñca]] [[synonyms:m:muñca]]---release, release; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāga]]---O greatly fortunate one; [[synonyms:s:satīm]]---chaste; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:p:parigraham]]---the wife of another person.

===== Translation =====

**Nārada Muni said: O Indra, King of the demigods, this woman is certainly sinless. You should not drag her off in this merciless way. O greatly fortunate one, this chaste woman is the wife of another. You must immediately release her.**
