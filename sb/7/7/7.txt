====== SB 7.7.7 ======

===== Text =====

> nīyamānāṁ bhayodvignāṁ
> rudatīṁ kurarīm iva
> yadṛcchayāgatas tatra
> devarṣir dadṛśe pathi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīyamānām]]---being taken away; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]-[[synonyms:u:udvignām]]---disturbed and full of fear; [[synonyms:r:rudatīm]]---crying; [[synonyms:k:kurarīm]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like a //kurarī// (osprey); [[synonyms:y:yadṛcchayā]]---by chance; [[synonyms:ā:āgataḥ]]---arrived; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---on the spot; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---the great saint Nārada; [[synonyms:d:dadṛśe]]---he saw; [[synonyms:p:pathi]]---on the road.

===== Translation =====

**As she was being led away, crying in fear like a kurarī captured by a vulture, the great sage Nārada, who at that time had no engagement, appeared on the scene and saw her in that condition.**
