====== SB 7.7.2 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-prahrāda uvāca
> pitari prasthite 'smākaṁ
> tapase mandarācalam
> yuddhodyamaṁ paraṁ cakrur
> vibudhā dānavān prati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:prahrādaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Prahlāda Mahārāja said; [[synonyms:p:pitari]]---when the demon father, Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:p:prasthite]]---left for; [[synonyms:a:asmākam]]---our; [[synonyms:t:tapase]]---to execute austerities; [[synonyms:m:mandara]]-[[synonyms:a:acalam]]---the hill known as Mandarācala; [[synonyms:y:yuddha]]-[[synonyms:u:udyamam]]---exertion of warfare; [[synonyms:p:param]]---very great; [[synonyms:c:cakruḥ]]---executed; [[synonyms:v:vibudhāḥ]]---the demigods, headed by King Indra; [[synonyms:d:dānavān]]---the demons; [[synonyms:p:prati]]---toward.

===== Translation =====

**Prahlāda Mahārāja said: When our father, Hiraṇyakaśipu, went to Mandarācala Mountain to execute severe austerities, in his absence the demigods, headed by King Indra, made a severe attempt to subdue all the demons in warfare.**
