====== SB 7.5.34 ======

===== Text =====

> āhāmarṣa-ruṣāviṣṭaḥ
> kaṣāyī-bhūta-locanaḥ
> vadhyatām āśv ayaṁ vadhyo
> niḥsārayata nairṛtāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āha]]---he said; [[synonyms:a:amarṣa]]---indignation; [[synonyms:r:ruṣā]]---and by severe anger; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭaḥ]]---overpowered; [[synonyms:k:kaṣāyī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]---becoming exactly like red-hot copper; [[synonyms:l:locanaḥ]]---whose eyes; [[synonyms:v:vadhyatām]]---let him be killed; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]---immediately; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vadhyaḥ]]---who is to be killed; [[synonyms:n:niḥsārayata]]---take away; [[synonyms:n:nairṛtāḥ]]---O demons.

===== Translation =====

**Indignant and angry, his reddish eyes like molten copper, Hiraṇyakaśipu said to his servants: O demons, take this boy away from me! He deserves to be killed. Kill him as soon as possible!**
