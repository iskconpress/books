====== SB 7.5.2 ======

===== Text =====

> tau rājñā prāpitaṁ bālaṁ
> prahlādaṁ naya-kovidam
> pāṭhayām āsatuḥ pāṭhyān
> anyāṁś cāsura-bālakān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tau]]---those two (Ṣaṇḍa and Amarka); [[synonyms:r:rājñā]]---by the King; [[synonyms:p:prāpitam]]---sent; [[synonyms:b:bālam]]---the boy; [[synonyms:p:prahlādam]]---named Prahlāda; [[synonyms:n:naya]]-[[synonyms:k:kovidam]]---who was aware of moral principles; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsatuḥ]]---instructed; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhyān]]---books of material knowledge; [[synonyms:a:anyān]]---other; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:b:bālakān]]---sons of the //asuras.//

===== Translation =====

**Prahlāda Mahārāja was already educated in devotional life, but when his father sent him to those two sons of Śukrācārya to be educated, they accepted him at their school along with the other sons of the asuras.**
