====== SB 7.5.33 ======

===== Text =====

> ity uktvoparataṁ putraṁ
> hiraṇyakaśipū ruṣā
> andhīkṛtātmā svotsaṅgān
> nirasyata mahī-tale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:u:uktvā]]---speaking; [[synonyms:u:uparatam]]---stopped; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---the son; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyakaśipuḥ]]---Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:r:ruṣā]]---with great anger; [[synonyms:a:andhīkṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---made blind to self-realization; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:u:utsaṅgāt]]---from his lap; [[synonyms:n:nirasyata]]---threw; [[synonyms:m:mahī]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---upon the ground.

===== Translation =====

**After Prahlāda Mahārāja had spoken in this way and become silent, Hiraṇyakaśipu, blinded by anger, threw him off his lap and onto the ground.**
