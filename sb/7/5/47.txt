====== SB 7.5.47 ======

===== Text =====

> aprameyānubhāvo 'yam
> akutaścid-bhayo 'maraḥ
> nūnam etad-virodhena
> mṛtyur me bhavitā na vā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aprameya]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:anubhāvaḥ]]---glory; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:akutaścit]]-[[synonyms:b:bhayaḥ]]---having no fear from any quarter; [[synonyms:a:amaraḥ]]---immortal; [[synonyms:n:nūnam]]---definitely; [[synonyms:e:etat]]-[[synonyms:v:virodhena]]---because of going against him; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyuḥ]]---death; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:b:bhavitā]]---may be; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or.

===== Translation =====

**I can see that this boy's strength is unlimited, for he has not feared any of my punishments. He appears immortal. Therefore, because of my enmity toward him, I shall die. Or maybe this will not take place.**
