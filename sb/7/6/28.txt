====== SB 7.6.28 ======

===== Text =====

> śrutam etan mayā pūrvaṁ
> jñānaṁ vijñāna-saṁyutam
> dharmaṁ bhāgavataṁ śuddhaṁ
> nāradād deva-darśanāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrutam]]---heard; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:p:pūrvam]]---formerly; [[synonyms:j:jñānam]]---confidential knowledge; [[synonyms:v:vijñāna]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁyutam]]---combined with its practical application; [[synonyms:d:dharmam]]---transcendental religion; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavatam]]---in relationship with the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ś:śuddham]]---having nothing to do with material activities; [[synonyms:n:nāradāt]]---from the great saint Nārada; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:d:darśanāt]]---who always sees.

===== Translation =====

**Prahlāda Mahārāja continued: I received this knowledge from the great saint Nārada Muni, who is always engaged in devotional service. This knowledge, which is called bhāgavata-dharma, is fully scientific. It is based on logic and philosophy and is free from all material contamination.**
