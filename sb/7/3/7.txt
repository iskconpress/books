====== SB 7.3.7 ======

===== Text =====

> tasya copaśamaṁ bhūman
> vidhehi yadi manyase
> lokā na yāvan naṅkṣyanti
> bali-hārās tavābhibhūḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of this; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---indeed; [[synonyms:u:upaśamam]]---the cessation; [[synonyms:b:bhūman]]---O great personality; [[synonyms:v:vidhehi]]---please execute; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:m:manyase]]---you think it right; [[synonyms:l:lokāḥ]]---all the inhabitants of the various planets; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long as; [[synonyms:n:naṅkṣyanti]]---will be lost; [[synonyms:b:bali]]-[[synonyms:h:hārāḥ]]---who are obedient to the worship; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---of you; [[synonyms:a:abhibhūḥ]]---O chief of all the universe.

===== Translation =====

**O great person, chief of the universe, if you think it proper, kindly stop these disturbances, meant to destroy everything, before all your obedient subjects are annihilated.**
