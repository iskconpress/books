====== SB 7.3.8 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyāyaṁ kila saṅkalpaś
> carato duścaraṁ tapaḥ
> śrūyatāṁ kiṁ na viditas
> tavāthāpi niveditam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:saṅkalpaḥ]]---determination; [[synonyms:c:carataḥ]]---who is executing; [[synonyms:d:duścaram]]---very difficult; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerity; [[synonyms:ś:śrūyatām]]---let it be heard; [[synonyms:k:kim]]---what; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:v:viditaḥ]]---known; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---of you; [[synonyms:a:athāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:n:niveditam]]---submitted.

===== Translation =====

**Hiraṇyakaśipu has undertaken a most severe type of austerity. Although his plan is not unknown to you, kindly listen as we submit his intentions.**
