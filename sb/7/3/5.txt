====== SB 7.3.5 ======

===== Text =====

> cukṣubhur nady-udanvantaḥ
> sadvīpādriś cacāla bhūḥ
> nipetuḥ sagrahās tārā
> jajvaluś ca diśo daśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cukṣubhuḥ]]---became agitated; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]-[[synonyms:u:udanvantaḥ]]---the rivers and oceans; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:dvīpa]]---with the islands; [[synonyms:a:adriḥ]]---and mountains; [[synonyms:c:cacāla]]---trembled; [[synonyms:b:bhūḥ]]---the surface of the globe; [[synonyms:n:nipetuḥ]]---fell; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:grahāḥ]]---with the planets; [[synonyms:t:tārāḥ]]---the stars; [[synonyms:j:jajvaluḥ]]---blazed; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:diśaḥ]] [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---the ten directions.

===== Translation =====

**Because of the power of his severe austerities, all the rivers and oceans were agitated, the surface of the globe, with its mountains and islands, began trembling, and the stars and planets fell. All directions were ablaze.**
