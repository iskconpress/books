====== SB 7.14.19 ======

===== Text =====

> kuryād apara-pakṣīyaṁ
> māsi prauṣṭha-pade dvijaḥ
> śrāddhaṁ pitror yathā-vittaṁ
> tad-bandhūnāṁ ca vittavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuryāt]]---one should perform; [[synonyms:a:apara]]-[[synonyms:p:pakṣīyam]]---during the fortnight of the dark moon; [[synonyms:m:māsi]]---in the month of Āśvina (October-November); [[synonyms:p:prauṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---in the month of Bhādra (August-September); [[synonyms:d:dvijaḥ]]---twice-born; [[synonyms:ś:śrāddham]]---oblations; [[synonyms:p:pitroḥ]]---unto the forefathers; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:v:vittam]]---according to one's means of income; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhūnām]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as well as relatives of forefathers; [[synonyms:v:vitta]]-[[synonyms:v:vān]]---one who is sufficiently rich.

===== Translation =====

**A brāhmaṇa who is sufficiently rich must offer oblations to the forefathers during the dark-moon fortnight in the latter part of the month of Bhādra. Similarly, he should offer oblations to the relatives of the forefathers during the mahālayā ceremonies in the month of Āśvina.***
