====== SB 7.12.19 ======

===== Text =====

> vanyaiś caru-puroḍāśān
> nirvapet kāla-coditān
> labdhe nave nave 'nnādye
> purāṇaṁ ca parityajet

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vanyaiḥ]]---by fruits and grains produced in the forest without cultivation; [[synonyms:c:caru]]---grains to be offered in a fire sacrifice; [[synonyms:p:puroḍāśān]]---the cakes prepared from //caru//; [[synonyms:n:nirvapet]]---one should execute; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:c:coditān]]---that which has grown naturally; [[synonyms:l:labdhe]]---on obtaining; [[synonyms:n:nave]]---new; [[synonyms:n:nave]] [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādye]]---newly produced food grains; [[synonyms:p:purāṇam]]---the stock of old grains; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:parityajet]]---one should give up.

===== Translation =====

**A vānaprastha should prepare cakes to be offered in sacrifice from fruits and grains grown naturally in the forest. When he obtains some new grains, he should give up his old stock of grains.**
