====== SB 7.12.3 ======

===== Text =====

> chandāṁsy adhīyīta guror
> āhūtaś cet suyantritaḥ
> upakrame 'vasāne ca
> caraṇau śirasā namet

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:chandāṁsi]]---//mantras// in the //Vedas,// like the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra// and the Gāyatrī //mantra//; [[synonyms:a:adhīyīta]]---one should chant or read regularly; [[synonyms:g:guroḥ]]---from the spiritual master; [[synonyms:ā:āhūtaḥ]]---being addressed or called (by him); [[synonyms:c:cet]]---if; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:y:yantritaḥ]]---faithful, well behaved; [[synonyms:u:upakrame]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:a:avasāne]]---at the end (of reading Vedic //mantras//); [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:c:caraṇau]]---at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:ś:śirasā]]---by the head; [[synonyms:n:namet]]---one should offer obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Being called by the spiritual master, the student should study the Vedic mantras regularly. Every day, before beginning his studies and at the end of his studies, the disciple should respectfully offer obeisances unto the spiritual master.**
