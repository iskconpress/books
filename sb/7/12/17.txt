====== SB 7.12.17 ======

===== Text =====

> vānaprasthasya vakṣyāmi
> niyamān muni-sammatān
> yān āsthāya munir gacched
> ṛṣi-lokam uhāñjasā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vānaprasthasya]]---of a person in the //vānaprastha-āśrama// (retired life); [[synonyms:v:vakṣyāmi]]---I shall now explain; [[synonyms:n:niyamān]]---the rules and regulations; [[synonyms:m:muni]]-[[synonyms:s:sammatān]]---which are recognized by great //munis,// philosophers and saintly persons; [[synonyms:y:yān]]---which; [[synonyms:ā:āsthāya]]---being situated in, or practicing; [[synonyms:m:muniḥ]]---a saintly person; [[synonyms:g:gacchet]]---is promoted; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣi]]-[[synonyms:l:lokam]]---to the planetary system where the seers and //munis// go (Maharloka); [[synonyms:u:uha]]---O King; [[synonyms:a:añjasā]]---without difficulty.

===== Translation =====

**O King, I shall now describe the qualifications for a vānaprastha, one who has retired from family life. By rigidly following the rules and regulations for the vānaprastha, one can easily be elevated to the upper planetary system known as Maharloka.**
