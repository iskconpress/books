====== SB 7.12.4 ======

===== Text =====

> mekhalājina-vāsāṁsi
> jaṭā-daṇḍa-kamaṇḍalūn
> bibhṛyād upavītaṁ ca
> darbha-pāṇir yathoditam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mekhalā]]---a belt made of straw; [[synonyms:a:ajina]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsāṁsi]]---garments made of deerskin; [[synonyms:j:jaṭā]]---matted hair; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---a rod; [[synonyms:k:kamaṇḍalūn]]---and a waterpot known as a //kamaṇḍalu//; [[synonyms:b:bibhṛyāt]]---he (the //brahmacārī//) should regularly carry or wear; [[synonyms:u:upavītam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and a sacred thread; [[synonyms:d:darbha]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṇiḥ]]---taking purified //kuśa// in his hand; [[synonyms:y:yathā]] [[synonyms:u:uditam]]---as recommended in the //śāstras.//

===== Translation =====

**Carrying pure kuśa grass in his hand, the brahmacārī should dress regularly with a belt of straw and with deerskin garments. He should wear matted hair, carry a rod and waterpot and be decorated with a sacred thread, as recommended in the śāstras.**
