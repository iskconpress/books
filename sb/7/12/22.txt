====== SB 7.12.22 ======

===== Text =====

> cared vane dvādaśābdān
> aṣṭau vā caturo muniḥ
> dvāv ekaṁ vā yathā buddhir
> na vipadyeta kṛcchrataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caret]]---should remain; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśa]]-[[synonyms:a:abdān]]---twelve years; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭau]]---for eight years; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:c:caturaḥ]]---four years; [[synonyms:m:muniḥ]]---a saintly, thoughtful man; [[synonyms:d:dvau]]---two; [[synonyms:e:ekam]]---one; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as well as; [[synonyms:b:buddhiḥ]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:v:vipadyeta]]---bewildered; [[synonyms:k:kṛcchrataḥ]]---because of hard austerities.

===== Translation =====

**Being very thoughtful, a vānaprastha should remain in the forest for twelve years, eight years, four years, two years or at least one year. He should behave in such a way that he will not be disturbed or troubled by too much austerity.**
