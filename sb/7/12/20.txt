====== SB 7.12.20 ======

===== Text =====

> agny-artham eva śaraṇam
> uṭajaṁ vādri-kandaram
> śrayeta hima-vāyv-agni-
> varṣārkātapa-ṣāṭ svayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:agni]]---the fire; [[synonyms:a:artham]]---to keep; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇam]]---a cottage; [[synonyms:u:uṭa]]-[[synonyms:j:jam]]---made of grass; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:a:adri]]-[[synonyms:k:kandaram]]---a cave in a mountain; [[synonyms:ś:śrayeta]]---the //vānaprastha// should take shelter of; [[synonyms:h:hima]]---snow; [[synonyms:v:vāyu]]---wind; [[synonyms:a:agni]]---fire; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]---rain; [[synonyms:a:arka]]---of the sun; [[synonyms:ā:ātapa]]---shining; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭ]]---enduring; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**A vānaprastha should prepare a thatched cottage or take shelter of a cave in a mountain only to keep the sacred fire, but he should personally practice enduring snowfall, wind, fire, rain and the shining of the sun.**
