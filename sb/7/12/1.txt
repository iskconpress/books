====== SB 7.12.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-nārada uvāca
> brahmacārī guru-kule
> vasan dānto guror hitam
> ācaran dāsavan nīco
> gurau sudṛḍha-sauhṛdaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Nārada Muni said; [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]]---a //brahmacārī,// a student living at the residence of the //guru//; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:k:kule]]---at the residence of the //guru//; [[synonyms:v:vasan]]---by living; [[synonyms:d:dāntaḥ]]---continuously practicing control of the senses; [[synonyms:g:guroḥ]] [[synonyms:h:hitam]]---only for the benefit of the //guru// (not for one's personal benefit); [[synonyms:ā:ācaran]]---practicing; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---very humbly, like a slave; [[synonyms:n:nīcaḥ]]---submissive, obedient; [[synonyms:g:gurau]]---unto the spiritual master; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---firmly; [[synonyms:s:sauhṛdaḥ]]---in friendship or good will.

===== Translation =====

**Nārada Muni said: A student should practice completely controlling his senses. He should be submissive and should have an attitude of firm friendship for the spiritual master. With a great vow, the brahmacārī should live at the guru-kula, only for the benefit of the guru.**
