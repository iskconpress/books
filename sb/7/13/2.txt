====== SB 7.13.2 ======

===== Text =====

> bibhṛyād yady asau vāsaḥ
> kaupīnācchādanaṁ param
> tyaktaṁ na liṅgād daṇḍāder
> anyat kiñcid anāpadi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bibhṛyāt]]---one should use; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---a person in the renounced order; [[synonyms:v:vāsaḥ]]---a garment or covering; [[synonyms:k:kaupīna]]---a loincloth (just to cover the private parts); [[synonyms:ā:ācchādanam]]---for covering; [[synonyms:p:param]]---that much only; [[synonyms:t:tyaktam]]---given up; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:l:liṅgāt]]---than the distinguishing marks of a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādeḥ]]---like the rod (//tridaṇḍa//); [[synonyms:a:anyat]]---other; [[synonyms:k:kiñcit]]---anything; [[synonyms:a:anāpadi]]---in ordinary undisturbed times.

===== Translation =====

**A person in the renounced order of life may try to avoid even a dress to cover himself. If he wears anything at all, it should be only a loincloth, and when there is no necessity, a sannyāsī should not even accept a daṇḍa. A sannyāsī should avoid carrying anything but a daṇḍa and kamaṇḍalu.**
