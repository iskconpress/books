====== SB 7.13.38 ======

===== Text =====

> kvacid alpaṁ kvacid bhūri
> bhuñje 'nnaṁ svādv asvādu vā
> kvacid bhūri guṇopetaṁ
> guṇa-hīnam uta kvacit
> śraddhayopahṛtaṁ kvāpi
> kadācin māna-varjitam
> bhuñje bhuktvātha kasmiṁś cid
> divā naktaṁ yadṛcchayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:a:alpam]]---very little; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bhūri]]---a great quantity; [[synonyms:b:bhuñje]]---I eat; [[synonyms:a:annam]]---food; [[synonyms:s:svādu]]---palatable; [[synonyms:a:asvādu]]---stale; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bhūri]]---great; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:upetam]]---a nice flavor; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:h:hīnam]]---without flavor; [[synonyms:u:uta]]---whether; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---respectfully; [[synonyms:u:upahṛtam]]---brought by someone; [[synonyms:k:kvāpi]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:kadācit]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:m:māna]]-[[synonyms:v:varjitam]]---offered without respect; [[synonyms:b:bhuñje]]---I eat; [[synonyms:b:bhuktvā]]---after eating; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---as such; [[synonyms:k:kasmin]] [[synonyms:c:cit]]---sometimes, in some place; [[synonyms:d:divā]]---during the daytime; [[synonyms:n:naktam]]---or at night; [[synonyms:y:yadṛcchayā]]---as it is available.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes I eat a very small quantity and sometimes a great quantity. Sometimes the food is very palatable, and sometimes it is stale. Sometimes prasāda is offered with great respect, and sometimes food is given neglectfully. Sometimes I eat during the day and sometimes at night. Thus I eat what is easily available.**
