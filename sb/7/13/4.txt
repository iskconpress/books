====== SB 7.13.4 ======

===== Text =====

> paśyed ātmany ado viśvaṁ
> pare sad-asato 'vyaye
> ātmānaṁ ca paraṁ brahma
> sarvatra sad-asan-maye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paśyet]]---one should see; [[synonyms:ā:ātmani]]---in the Supreme Soul; [[synonyms:a:adaḥ]]---this; [[synonyms:v:viśvam]]---universe; [[synonyms:p:pare]]---beyond; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:a:asataḥ]]---the creation or cause of creation; [[synonyms:a:avyaye]]---in the Absolute, which is free from deterioration; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---himself; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:p:param]]---the supreme; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---absolute; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:a:asat]]---in the cause and in the effect; [[synonyms:m:maye]]---all-pervading.

===== Translation =====

**The sannyāsī should always try to see the Supreme pervading everything and see everything, including this universe, resting on the Supreme.**
