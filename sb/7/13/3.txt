====== SB 7.13.3 ======

===== Text =====

> eka eva cared bhikṣur
> ātmārāmo 'napāśrayaḥ
> sarva-bhūta-suhṛc-chānto
> nārāyaṇa-parāyaṇaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekaḥ]]---alone; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:c:caret]]---can move; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣuḥ]]---a //sannyāsī// taking alms; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārāmaḥ]]---fully satisfied in the self; [[synonyms:a:anapāśrayaḥ]]---without depending on anything; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:s:suhṛt]]---becoming a well-wisher of all living entities; [[synonyms:ś:śāntaḥ]]---completely peaceful; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:parāyaṇaḥ]]---becoming absolutely dependent on Nārāyaṇa and becoming His devotee.

===== Translation =====

**The sannyāsī, completely satisfied in the self, should live on alms begged from door to door. Not being dependent on any person or any place, he should always be a friendly well-wisher to all living beings and be a peaceful, unalloyed devotee of Nārāyaṇa. In this way he should move from one place to another.**
