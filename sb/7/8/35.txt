====== SB 7.8.35 ======

===== Text =====

> niśāmya loka-traya-mastaka-jvaraṁ
> tam ādi-daityaṁ hariṇā hataṁ mṛdhe
> praharṣa-vegotkalitānanā muhuḥ
> prasūna-varṣair vavṛṣuḥ sura-striyaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niśāmya]]---hearing; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:t:traya]]---of the three worlds; [[synonyms:m:mastaka]]-[[synonyms:j:jvaram]]---the headache; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the original; [[synonyms:d:daityam]]---demon; [[synonyms:h:hariṇā]]---by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:hatam]]---killed; [[synonyms:m:mṛdhe]]---in battle; [[synonyms:p:praharṣa]]-[[synonyms:v:vega]]---by an outburst of ecstasy; [[synonyms:u:utkalita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanāḥ]]---whose faces blossomed; [[synonyms:m:muhuḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:p:prasūna]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣaiḥ]]---with showers of flowers; [[synonyms:v:vavṛṣuḥ]]---rained; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:s:striyaḥ]]---the wives of the demigods.

===== Translation =====

**Hiraṇyakaśipu had been exactly like a fever of meningitis in the head of the three worlds. Thus when the wives of the demigods in the heavenly planets saw that the great demon had been killed by the personal hands of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, their faces blossomed in great joy. The wives of the demigods again and again showered flowers from heaven upon Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva like rain.**
