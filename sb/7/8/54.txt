====== SB 7.8.54 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-vaitālikā ūcuḥ
> sabhāsu satreṣu tavāmalaṁ yaśo
> gītvā saparyāṁ mahatīṁ labhāmahe
> yas tām anaiṣīd vaśam eṣa durjano
> dviṣṭyā hatas te bhagavan yathāmayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vaitālikāḥ]] [[synonyms:ū:ūcuḥ]]---the inhabitants of Vaitālika-loka said; [[synonyms:s:sabhāsu]]---in great assemblies; [[synonyms:s:satreṣu]]---in the arenas of sacrifice; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---Your; [[synonyms:a:amalam]]---without any spot of material contamination; [[synonyms:y:yaśaḥ]]---reputation; [[synonyms:g:gītvā]]---singing; [[synonyms:s:saparyām]]---respectful position; [[synonyms:m:mahatīm]]---great; [[synonyms:l:labhāmahe]]---we achieved; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---he who; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---that (respectful position); [[synonyms:a:anaiṣīt]]---brought under; [[synonyms:v:vaśam]]---his control; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---this; [[synonyms:d:durjanaḥ]]---crooked person; [[synonyms:d:dviṣṭyā]]---by great fortune; [[synonyms:h:hataḥ]]---killed; [[synonyms:t:te]]---by You; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O Lord; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---exactly like; [[synonyms:ā:āmayaḥ]]---a disease.

===== Translation =====

**The inhabitants of Vaitālika-loka said: Dear Lord, because of chanting Your spotless glories in great assemblies and arenas of sacrifice, we were accustomed to great respect from everyone. This demon, however, usurped that position. Now, to our great fortune, You have killed this great demon, exactly as one cures a chronic disease.**
