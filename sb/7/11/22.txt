====== SB 7.11.22 ======

===== Text =====

> śauryaṁ vīryaṁ dhṛtis tejas
> tyāgaś cātmajayaḥ kṣamā
> brahmaṇyatā prasādaś ca
> satyaṁ ca kṣatra-lakṣaṇam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śauryam]]---power in battle; [[synonyms:v:vīryam]]---being unconquerable; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtiḥ]]---patience (even in reverses, a //kṣatriya// is very grave); [[synonyms:t:tejaḥ]]---ability to defeat others; [[synonyms:t:tyāgaḥ]]---giving charity; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:j:jayaḥ]]---not being overwhelmed by bodily necessities; [[synonyms:k:kṣamā]]---forgiveness; [[synonyms:b:brahmaṇyatā]]---faithfulness to the brahminical principles; [[synonyms:p:prasādaḥ]]---jolliness in any condition of life; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:satyam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and truthfulness; [[synonyms:k:kṣatra]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇam]]---these are the symptoms of a //kṣatriya.//

===== Translation =====

**To be influential in battle, unconquerable, patient, challenging and charitable, to control the bodily necessities, to be forgiving, to be attached to the brahminical nature and to be always jolly and truthful---these are the symptoms of the kṣatriya.**
