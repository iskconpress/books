====== SB 7.11.15 ======

===== Text =====

> vaiśyas tu vārtā-vṛttiḥ syān
> nityaṁ brahma-kulānugaḥ
> śūdrasya dvija-śuśrūṣā
> vṛttiś ca svāmino bhavet

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaiśyaḥ]]---the mercantile community; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛttiḥ]]---engaged in agriculture, cow protection, and trade; [[synonyms:s:syāt]]---must be; [[synonyms:n:nityam]]---always; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:a:anugaḥ]]---following the directions of the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:ś:śūdrasya]]---of the fourth-grade persons, the workers; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:ś:śuśrūṣā]]---the service of the three higher sections (the //brāhmaṇas, kṣatriyas// and //vaiśyas//); [[synonyms:v:vṛttiḥ]]---means of livelihood; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:svāminaḥ]]---of the master; [[synonyms:b:bhavet]]---he must be.

===== Translation =====

**The mercantile community should always follow the directions of the brāhmaṇas and engage in such occupational duties as agriculture, trade, and protection of cows. For the śūdras the only duty is to accept a master from a higher social order and engage in his service.**
