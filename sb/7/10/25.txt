====== SB 7.10.25 ======

===== Text =====

> prasāda-sumukhaṁ dṛṣṭvā
> brahmā narahariṁ harim
> stutvā vāgbhiḥ pavitrābhiḥ
> prāha devādibhir vṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:s:sumukham]]---whose face was bright because the Supreme Lord was pleased; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---seeing this situation; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:n:nara]]-[[synonyms:h:harim]]---unto Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:stutvā]]---offering prayers; [[synonyms:v:vāgbhiḥ]]---by transcendental words; [[synonyms:p:pavitrābhiḥ]]---without any material contamination; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---addressed (the Lord); [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---by other demigods; [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]]---surrounded.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Brahmā, surrounded by the other demigods, was bright-faced because the Lord was pleased. Thus he offered prayers to the Lord with transcendental words.**
