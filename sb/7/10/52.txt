====== SB 7.10.52 ======

===== Text =====

> rājovāca
> kasmin karmaṇi devasya
> mayo 'hañ jagad-īśituḥ
> yathā copacitā kīrtiḥ
> kṛṣṇenānena kathyatām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---King Yudhiṣṭhira inquired; [[synonyms:k:kasmin]]---for what reason; [[synonyms:k:karmaṇi]]---by which activities; [[synonyms:d:devasya]]---of Lord Mahādeva (Śiva); [[synonyms:m:mayaḥ]]---the great demon Maya Dānava; [[synonyms:a:ahan]]---vanquished; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśituḥ]]---of Lord Śiva, who controls the power of the material energy and is the husband of Durgādevī; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:u:upacitā]]---again expanded; [[synonyms:k:kīrtiḥ]]---reputation; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇena]]---by Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:anena]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kathyatām]]---please describe.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira said: For what reason did the demon Maya Dānava vanquish Lord Śiva's reputation? How did Lord Kṛṣṇa save Lord Śiva and expand his reputation again? Kindly describe these incidents.**
