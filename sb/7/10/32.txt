====== SB 7.10.32 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ sampūjya śirasā
> vavande parameṣṭhinam
> bhavaṁ prajāpatīn devān
> prahrādo bhagavat-kalāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sampūjya]]---worshiping; [[synonyms:ś:śirasā]]---by bowing the head; [[synonyms:v:vavande]]---offered prayers; [[synonyms:p:parameṣṭhinam]]---to Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:b:bhavam]]---to Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:p:prajāpatīn]]---to the great demigods entrusted with increasing the population; [[synonyms:d:devān]]---to all the great demigods; [[synonyms:p:prahrādaḥ]]---Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:k:kalāḥ]]---influential parts of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Prahlāda Mahārāja then worshiped and offered prayers to all the demigods, such as Brahmā, Śiva and the Prajāpatis, who are all parts of the Lord.**
