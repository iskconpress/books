====== SB 7.10.19 ======

===== Text =====

> yatra yatra ca mad-bhaktāḥ
> praśāntāḥ sama-darśinaḥ
> sādhavaḥ samudācārās
> te pūyante 'pi kīkaṭāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yatra]] [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherever and whenever; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktāḥ]]---My devotees; [[synonyms:p:praśāntāḥ]]---extremely peaceful; [[synonyms:s:sama]]-[[synonyms:d:darśinaḥ]]---equipoised; [[synonyms:s:sādhavaḥ]]---decorated with all good qualities; [[synonyms:s:samudācārāḥ]]---equally magnanimous; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:pūyante]]---are purified; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:k:kīkaṭāḥ]]---a degraded country or the inhabitants of such a place.

===== Translation =====

**Whenever and wherever there are peaceful, equipoised devotees who are well behaved and decorated with all good qualities, that place and the dynasties there, even if condemned, are purified.**

===== Purport =====

Wherever exalted devotees stay, not only they and their dynasties but the entire country is purified.
