====== SB 7.10.28 ======

===== Text =====

> diṣṭyā tat-tanayaḥ sādhur
> mahā-bhāgavato 'rbhakaḥ
> tvayā vimocito mṛtyor
> diṣṭyā tvāṁ samito 'dhunā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:diṣṭyā]]---by fortune; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:s:sādhuḥ]]---who is a great saintly person; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavataḥ]]---a great and exalted devotee; [[synonyms:a:arbhakaḥ]]---although a child; [[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by Your Lordship; [[synonyms:v:vimocitaḥ]]---released; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyoḥ]]---from the clutches of death; [[synonyms:d:diṣṭyā]]---also by great fortune; [[synonyms:t:tvām]] [[synonyms:s:samitaḥ]]---perfectly under Your shelter; [[synonyms:a:adhunā]]---now.

===== Translation =====

**By great fortune, Hiraṇyakaśipu's son Prahlāda Mahārāja has now been released from death, for although he is a child, he is an exalted devotee. Now he is fully under the protection of Your lotus feet.**
