====== SB 7.10.61 ======

===== Text =====

> vilokya bhagna-saṅkalpaṁ
> vimanaskaṁ vṛṣa-dhvajam
> tadāyaṁ bhagavān viṣṇus
> tatropāyam akalpayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vilokya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhagna]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkalpam]]---disappointed; [[synonyms:v:vimanaskam]]---extremely unhappy; [[synonyms:v:vṛṣa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvajam]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇuḥ]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---about the well of nectar; [[synonyms:u:upāyam]]---means (how to stop it); [[synonyms:a:akalpayat]]---considered.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Lord Śiva very much aggrieved and disappointed, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Lord Viṣṇu, considered how to stop this nuisance created by Maya Dānava.**
