====== SB 7.10.41 ======

===== Text =====

> ākhyātaṁ sarvam etat te
> yan māṁ tvaṁ paripṛṣṭavān
> damaghoṣa-sutādīnāṁ
> hareḥ sātmyam api dviṣām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākhyātam]]---described; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---everything; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---unto me; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---you; [[synonyms:p:paripṛṣṭavān]]---inquired; [[synonyms:d:damaghoṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:suta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādīnām]]---of the son of Damaghoṣa (Śiśupāla) and others; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sātmyam]]---equal bodily features; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:d:dviṣām]]---although they were inimical.

===== Translation =====

**Everything you asked me about how Śiśupāla and others attained salvation although they were inimical has now been explained to you by me.**
