====== SB 7.10.60 ======

===== Text =====

> siddhāmṛta-rasa-spṛṣṭā
> vajra-sārā mahaujasaḥ
> uttasthur megha-dalanā
> vaidyutā iva vahnayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:s:spṛṣṭāḥ]]---the demons, thus being touched by the powerful mystic nectarean liquid; [[synonyms:v:vajra]]-[[synonyms:s:sārāḥ]]---their bodies becoming invincible to thunderbolts; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:o:ojasaḥ]]---being extremely strong; [[synonyms:u:uttasthuḥ]]---again got up; [[synonyms:m:megha]]-[[synonyms:d:dalanāḥ]]---that which goes through the clouds; [[synonyms:v:vaidyutāḥ]]---lightning (which penetrates the clouds); [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:v:vahnayaḥ]]---fiery.

===== Translation =====

**When the dead bodies of the demons came in touch with the nectar, their bodies became invincible to thunderbolts. Endowed with great strength, they got up like lightning penetrating clouds.**
