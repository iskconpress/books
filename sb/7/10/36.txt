====== SB 7.10.36 ======

===== Text =====

> punaś ca vipra-śāpena
> rākṣasau tau babhūvatuḥ
> kumbhakarṇa-daśa-grīvau
> hatau tau rāma-vikramaiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāpena]]---being cursed by the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:r:rākṣasau]]---the two Rākṣasas; [[synonyms:t:tau]]---both of them; [[synonyms:b:babhūvatuḥ]]---incarnated as; [[synonyms:k:kumbhakarṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:g:grīvau]]---known as Kumbhakarṇa and the ten-headed Rāvaṇa (in their next birth); [[synonyms:h:hatau]]---they also were killed; [[synonyms:t:tau]]---both of them; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:v:vikramaiḥ]]---by the extraordinary strength of Lord Rāmacandra.

===== Translation =====

**Being cursed by the brāhmaṇas, the same two associates took birth again as Kumbhakarṇa and the ten-headed Rāvaṇa. These two Rākṣasas were killed by Lord Rāmacandra's extraordinary power.**
