====== SB 7.10.30 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bhagavān uvāca
> maivaṁ vibho 'surāṇāṁ te
> pradeyaḥ padma-sambhava
> varaḥ krūra-nisargāṇām
> ahīnām amṛtaṁ yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead replied (to Brahmā); [[synonyms:m:mā]]---do not; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vibho]]---O great person; [[synonyms:a:asurāṇām]]---unto the demons; [[synonyms:t:te]]---by you; [[synonyms:p:pradeyaḥ]]---bestow benedictions; [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:s:sambhava]]---O Lord Brahmā, born from the lotus flower; [[synonyms:v:varaḥ]]---benediction; [[synonyms:k:krūra]]-[[synonyms:n:nisargāṇām]]---persons who are by nature very cruel and jealous; [[synonyms:a:ahīnām]]---to snakes; [[synonyms:a:amṛtam]]---nectar or milk; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as.

===== Translation =====

**The Personality of Godhead replied: My dear Lord Brahmā, O great lord born from the lotus flower, just as it is dangerous to feed milk to a snake, so it is dangerous to give benedictions to demons, who are by nature ferocious and jealous. I warn you not to give such benedictions to any demon again.**
