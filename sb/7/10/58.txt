====== SB 7.10.58 ======

===== Text =====

> tato 'gni-varṇā iṣava
> utpetuḥ sūrya-maṇḍalāt
> yathā mayūkha-sandohā
> nādṛśyanta puro yataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:a:agni]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇāḥ]]---as brilliant as fire; [[synonyms:i:iṣavaḥ]]---arrows; [[synonyms:u:utpetuḥ]]---released; [[synonyms:s:sūrya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍalāt]]---from the sun globe; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:m:mayūkha]]-[[synonyms:s:sandohāḥ]]---beams of light; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:adṛśyanta]]---could not be seen; [[synonyms:p:puraḥ]]---the three residences; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---because of this (being covered by the arrows of Lord Śiva).

===== Translation =====

**The arrows released by Lord Śiva appeared like fiery beams emanating from the sun globe and covered the three residential airplanes, which could then no longer be seen.**
