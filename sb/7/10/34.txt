====== SB 7.10.34 ======

===== Text =====

> pratinandya tato devāḥ
> prayujya paramāśiṣaḥ
> sva-dhāmāni yayū rājan
> brahmādyāḥ pratipūjitāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratinandya]]---congratulating; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:p:prayujya]]---having offered; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśiṣaḥ]]---exalted benedictions; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāmāni]]---to their respective abodes; [[synonyms:y:yayuḥ]]---returned; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Yudhiṣṭhira; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---all the demigods, headed by Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:p:pratipūjitāḥ]]---being thoroughly worshiped (by Prahlāda Mahārāja).

===== Translation =====

**O King Yudhiṣṭhira, after all the demigods, headed by Lord Brahmā, were properly worshiped by Prahlāda Mahārāja, they offered Prahlāda their utmost benedictions and then returned to their respective abodes.**
