====== SB 7.10.46 ======

===== Text =====

> ya etat puṇyam ākhyānaṁ
> viṣṇor vīryopabṛṁhitam
> kīrtayec chraddhayā śrutvā
> karma-pāśair vimucyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:p:puṇyam]]---pious; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyānam]]---narration; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:v:vīrya]]---the supreme power; [[synonyms:u:upabṛṁhitam]]---in which is described; [[synonyms:k:kīrtayet]]---chants or repeats; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---with great faith; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---after properly hearing (from the right source); [[synonyms:k:karma]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśaiḥ]]---from the bondage of fruitive activities; [[synonyms:v:vimucyate]]---becomes liberated.

===== Translation =====

**One who hears and chants this narration about the omnipotence of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu, is certainly liberated from material bondage without fail.**
