====== SB 7.10.56 ======

===== Text =====

> tatas te seśvarā lokā
> upāsādyeśvaraṁ natāḥ
> trāhi nas tāvakān deva
> vinaṣṭāṁs tripurālayaiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they (the demigods); [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvarāḥ]]---with their rulers; [[synonyms:l:lokāḥ]]---the planets; [[synonyms:u:upāsādya]]---approaching; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaram]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:n:natāḥ]]---fell down in surrender; [[synonyms:t:trāhi]]---please save; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---us; [[synonyms:t:tāvakān]]---near and dear to you and very frightened; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---O Lord; [[synonyms:v:vinaṣṭān]]---almost finished; [[synonyms:t:tripura]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālayaiḥ]]---by the demons dwelling in those three planes.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, when the demons had begun to destroy the higher planetary systems, the rulers of those planets went to Lord Śiva, fully surrendered unto him and said: Dear Lord, we demigods living in the three worlds are about to be vanquished. We are your followers. Kindly save us.**
