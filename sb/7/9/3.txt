====== SB 7.9.3 ======

===== Text =====

> prahrādaṁ preṣayām āsa
> brahmāvasthitam antike
> tāta praśamayopehi
> sva-pitre kupitaṁ prabhum

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prahrādam]]---Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:p:preṣayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---requested; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:a:avasthitam]]---being situated; [[synonyms:a:antike]]---very near; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---my dear son; [[synonyms:p:praśamaya]]---just try to appease; [[synonyms:u:upehi]]---go near; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:p:pitre]]---because of your father's demoniac activities; [[synonyms:k:kupitam]]---greatly angered; [[synonyms:p:prabhum]]---the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter Lord Brahmā requested Prahlāda Mahārāja, who was standing very near him: My dear son, Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva is extremely angry at your demoniac father. Please go forward and appease the Lord.**
