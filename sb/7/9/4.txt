====== SB 7.9.4 ======

===== Text =====

> tatheti śanakai rājan
> mahā-bhāgavato 'rbhakaḥ
> upetya bhuvi kāyena
> nanāma vidhṛtāñjaliḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]]---so be it; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus accepting the words of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śanakaiḥ]]---very slowly; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King (Yudhiṣṭhira); [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavataḥ]]---the great, exalted devotee (Prahlāda Mahārāja); [[synonyms:a:arbhakaḥ]]---although only a small boy; [[synonyms:u:upetya]]---gradually going near; [[synonyms:b:bhuvi]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:k:kāyena]]---by his body; [[synonyms:n:nanāma]]---offered respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:v:vidhṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:añjaliḥ]]---folding his hands.

===== Translation =====

**Nārada Muni continued: O King, although the exalted devotee Prahlāda Mahārāja was only a little boy, he accepted Lord Brahmā's words. He gradually proceeded toward Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva and fell down to offer his respectful obeisances with folded hands.**
