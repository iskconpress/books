====== SB 7.1.39 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ śaptau sva-bhavanāt
> patantau tau kṛpālubhiḥ
> proktau punar janmabhir vāṁ
> tribhir lokāya kalpatām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:ś:śaptau]]---being cursed; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavanāt]]---from their abode, Vaikuṇṭha; [[synonyms:p:patantau]]---falling down; [[synonyms:t:tau]]---those two (Jaya and Vijaya); [[synonyms:k:kṛpālubhiḥ]]---by the merciful sages (Sanandana, etc.); [[synonyms:p:proktau]]---addressed; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:j:janmabhiḥ]]---with births; [[synonyms:v:vām]]---your; [[synonyms:t:tribhiḥ]]---three; [[synonyms:l:lokāya]]---for the position; [[synonyms:k:kalpatām]]---let it be possible.

===== Translation =====

**While Jaya and Vijaya, thus cursed by the sages, were falling to the material world, they were addressed as follows by the same sages, who were very kind to them. "O doorkeepers, after three births you will be able to return to your positions in Vaikuṇṭha, for then the duration of the curse will have ended."**
