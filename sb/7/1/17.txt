====== SB 7.1.17 ======

===== Text =====

> etad veditum icchāmaḥ
> sarva eva vayaṁ mune
> bhagavan-nindayā veno
> dvijais tamasi pātitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:v:veditum]]---to know; [[synonyms:i:icchāmaḥ]]---desire; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:v:vayam]]---we; [[synonyms:m:mune]]---O great sage; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:n:nindayā]]---because of blaspheming the Lord; [[synonyms:v:venaḥ]]---Vena, the father of Pṛthu Mahārāja; [[synonyms:d:dvijaiḥ]]---by the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:t:tamasi]]---into hell; [[synonyms:p:pātitaḥ]]---was thrown.

===== Translation =====

**O great sage, we are all eager to know the cause for this mercy of the Lord. I have heard that formerly a king named Vena blasphemed the Supreme Personality of Godhead and that all the brāhmaṇas consequently obliged him to go to hell. Śiśupāla should also have been sent to hell. How then did he merge into the Lord's existence?**
