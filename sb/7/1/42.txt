====== SB 7.1.42 ======

===== Text =====

> hiraṇyakaśipuḥ putraṁ
> prahlādaṁ keśava-priyam
> jighāṁsur akaron nānā
> yātanā mṛtyu-hetave

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hiraṇyakaśipuḥ]]---Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---son; [[synonyms:p:prahlādam]]---Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:k:keśava]]-[[synonyms:p:priyam]]---the beloved devotee of Keśava; [[synonyms:j:jighāṁsuḥ]]---desirous of killing; [[synonyms:a:akarot]]---enacted; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:y:yātanāḥ]]---tortures; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyu]]---death; [[synonyms:h:hetave]]---to cause.

===== Translation =====

**Desiring to kill his son Prahlāda, who was a great devotee of Lord Viṣṇu, Hiraṇyakaśipu tortured him in many ways.**
