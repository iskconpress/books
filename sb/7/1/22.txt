====== SB 7.1.22 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bādarāyaṇir uvāca
> rājñas tad vaca ākarṇya
> nārado bhagavān ṛṣiḥ
> tuṣṭaḥ prāha tam ābhāṣya
> śṛṇvatyās tat-sadaḥ kathāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:r:rājñaḥ]]---of the King (Yudhiṣṭhira); [[synonyms:t:tat]]---those; [[synonyms:v:vacaḥ]]---words; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṇya]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]]---Nārada Muni; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---powerful; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---sage; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭaḥ]]---being satisfied; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---spoke; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:ābhāṣya]]---after addressing; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇvatyāḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sadaḥ]]---in the presence of the assembly members; [[synonyms:k:kathāḥ]]---the topics.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After hearing the request of Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira, Nārada Muni, the most powerful spiritual master, who knew everything, was very pleased. Thus he replied in the presence of everyone taking part in the yajña.**
