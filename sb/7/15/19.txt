====== SB 7.15.19 ======

===== Text =====

> asantuṣṭasya viprasya
> tejo vidyā tapo yaśaḥ
> sravantīndriya-laulyena
> jñānaṁ caivāvakīryate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:asantuṣṭasya]]---of one who is not self-satisfied; [[synonyms:v:viprasya]]---of such a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:t:tejaḥ]]---strength; [[synonyms:v:vidyā]]---education; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerity; [[synonyms:y:yaśaḥ]]---fame; [[synonyms:s:sravanti]]---dwindle; [[synonyms:i:indriya]]---of the senses; [[synonyms:l:laulyena]]---because of greed; [[synonyms:j:jñānam]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:avakīryate]]---gradually vanishes.

===== Translation =====

**Because of greed for the sake of the senses, the spiritual strength, education, austerity and reputation of a devotee or brāhmaṇa who is not self-satisfied dwindle, and his knowledge gradually vanishes.**
