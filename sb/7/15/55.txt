====== SB 7.15.55 ======

===== Text =====

> deva-yānam idaṁ prāhur
> bhūtvā bhūtvānupūrvaśaḥ
> ātma-yājy upaśāntātmā
> hy ātma-stho na nivartate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:y:yānam]]---the process of elevation known as //deva-yāna//; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---on this (path); [[synonyms:p:prāhuḥ]]---it is said; [[synonyms:b:bhūtvā]] [[synonyms:b:bhūtvā]]---having repeated birth; [[synonyms:a:anupūrvaśaḥ]]---consecutively; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:y:yājī]]---one who is eager for self-realization; [[synonyms:u:upaśānta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---completely free from all material desires; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sthaḥ]]---situated in his own self; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:n:nivartate]]---does return.

===== Translation =====

**This gradual process of elevation for self-realization is meant for those who are truly aware of the Absolute Truth. After repeated birth on this path, which is known as deva-yāna, one attains these consecutive stages. One who is completely free from all material desires, being situated in the self, need not traverse the path of repeated birth and death.**
