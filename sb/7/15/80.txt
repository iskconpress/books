====== SB 7.15.80 ======

===== Text =====

> iti dākṣāyaṇīnāṁ te
> pṛthag vaṁśāḥ prakīrtitāḥ
> devāsura-manuṣyādyā
> lokā yatra carācarāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:dākṣāyaṇīnām]]---of the daughters of Mahārāja Dakṣa, like Aditi and Diti; [[synonyms:t:te]]---to you; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separately; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśāḥ]]---the dynasties; [[synonyms:p:prakīrtitāḥ]]---described (by me); [[synonyms:d:deva]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:a:asura]]---demons; [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---and human beings; [[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---and so on; [[synonyms:l:lokāḥ]]---all the planets within the universe; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherein; [[synonyms:c:cara]]-[[synonyms:a:acarāḥ]]---moving and nonmoving living entities.

===== Translation =====

**On all the planets within this universe, the varieties of living entities, moving and nonmoving, including the demigods, demons and human beings, were all generated from the daughters of Mahārāja Dakṣa. I have now described them and their different dynasties.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Seventh Canto, Fifteenth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "Instructions for Civilized Human Beings."
