====== SB 6.3.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> bhagavat-puruṣai rājan
> yāmyāḥ pratihatodyamāḥ
> patiṁ vijñāpayām āsur
> yamaṁ saṁyamanī-patim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣaiḥ]]---by the order carriers of the Lord, the Viṣṇudūtas; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:y:yāmyāḥ]]---the order carriers of Yamarāja; [[synonyms:p:pratihata]]-[[synonyms:u:udyamāḥ]]---whose efforts were defeated; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---their master; [[synonyms:v:vijñāpayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsuḥ]]---informed; [[synonyms:y:yamam]]---Yamarāja; [[synonyms:s:saṁyamanī]]-[[synonyms:p:patim]]---the master of the city Saṁyamanī.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī replied: My dear King, when the order carriers of Yamarāja were baffled and defeated by the order carriers of Viṣṇu, they approached their master, the controller of Saṁyamanī-purī and master of sinful persons, to tell him of this incident.**
