====== SB 6.3.34 ======

===== Text =====

> itthaṁ svabhartṛ-gaditaṁ bhagavan-mahitvaṁ
> saṁsmṛtya vismita-dhiyo yama-kiṅkarās te
> naivācyutāśraya-janaṁ pratiśaṅkamānā
> draṣṭuṁ ca bibhyati tataḥ prabhṛti sma rājan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ittham]]---of such power; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhartṛ]]-[[synonyms:g:gaditam]]---explained by their master (Yamarāja); [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:m:mahitvam]]---the extraordinary glory of the Supreme Personality of Godhead and His name, fame, form and attributes; [[synonyms:s:saṁsmṛtya]]---remembering; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]-[[synonyms:d:dhiyaḥ]]---whose minds were struck with wonder; [[synonyms:y:yama]]-[[synonyms:k:kiṅkarāḥ]]---all the servants of Yamarāja; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:acyuta]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]-[[synonyms:j:janam]]---a person sheltered by the lotus feet of Acyuta, Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pratiśaṅkamānāḥ]]---always fearing; [[synonyms:d:draṣṭum]]---to see; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bibhyati]]---they are afraid; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]] [[synonyms:p:prabhṛti]]---beginning from then; [[synonyms:s:sma]]---indeed; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing from the mouth of their master about the extraordinary glories of the Lord and His name, fame and attributes, the Yamadūtas were struck with wonder. Since then, as soon as they see a devotee, they fear him and dare not look at him again.**

===== Purport =====

Since this incident, the Yamadūtas have given up the dangerous behavior of approaching devotees. For the Yamadūtas, a devotee is dangerous.
