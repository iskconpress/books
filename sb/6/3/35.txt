====== SB 6.3.35 ======

===== Text =====

> itihāsam imaṁ guhyaṁ
> bhagavān kumbha-sambhavaḥ
> kathayām āsa malaya
> āsīno harim arcayan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:itihāsam]]---history; [[synonyms:i:imam]]---this; [[synonyms:g:guhyam]]---very confidential; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:k:kumbha]]-[[synonyms:s:sambhavaḥ]]---Agastya Muni, the son of Kumbha; [[synonyms:k:kathayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---explained; [[synonyms:m:malaye]]---in the Malaya Hills; [[synonyms:ā:āsīnaḥ]]---residing; [[synonyms:h:harim]] [[synonyms:a:arcayan]]---worshiping the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**When the great sage Agastya, the son of Kumbha, was residing in the Malaya Hills and worshiping the Supreme Personality of Godhead, I approached him, and he explained to me this confidential history.**
