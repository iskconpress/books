====== SB 6.16.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bādarāyaṇir uvāca
> atha deva-ṛṣī rājan
> samparetaṁ nṛpātmajam
> darśayitveti hovāca
> jñātīnām anuśocatām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---the great sage Nārada; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:samparetam]]---dead; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajam]]---the son of the King; [[synonyms:d:darśayitvā]]---making visible; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---explained; [[synonyms:j:jñātīnām]]---to all the relatives; [[synonyms:a:anuśocatām]]---who were lamenting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: My dear King Parīkṣit, by his mystic power the great sage Nārada brought the dead son into the vision of all the lamenting relatives and then spoke as follows.**
