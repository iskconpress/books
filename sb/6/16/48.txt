====== SB 6.16.48 ======

===== Text =====

> yaṁ vai śvasantam anu viśva-sṛjaḥ śvasanti
> yaṁ cekitānam anu cittaya uccakanti
> bhū-maṇḍalaṁ sarṣapāyati yasya mūrdhni
> tasmai namo bhagavate 'stu sahasra-mūrdhne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yam]]---whom; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ś:śvasantam]]---endeavoring; [[synonyms:a:anu]]---after; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:s:sṛjaḥ]]---the directors of the cosmic creation; [[synonyms:ś:śvasanti]]---also endeavor; [[synonyms:y:yam]]---whom; [[synonyms:c:cekitānam]]---perceiving; [[synonyms:a:anu]]---after; [[synonyms:c:cittayaḥ]]---all the knowledge-gathering senses; [[synonyms:u:uccakanti]]---perceive; [[synonyms:b:bhū]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍalam]]---the huge universe; [[synonyms:s:sarṣapāyati]]---become like seeds of mustard; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom; [[synonyms:m:mūrdhni]]---on the head; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:b:bhagavate]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, full with six opulences; [[synonyms:a:astu]]---may there be; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrdhne]]---who has thousands of hoods.

===== Translation =====

**My dear Lord, it is after You endeavor that Lord Brahmā, Indra and the other directors of the cosmic manifestation become occupied with their activities. It is after You perceive the material energy, My Lord, that the senses begin to perceive. The Supreme Personality of Godhead holds all the universes on His heads like seeds of mustard. I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, that Supreme Personality, who has thousands of hoods.**
