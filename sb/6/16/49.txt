====== SB 6.16.49 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> saṁstuto bhagavān evam
> anantas tam abhāṣata
> vidyādhara-patiṁ prītaś
> citraketuṁ kurūdvaha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:s:saṁstutaḥ]]---being worshiped; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:a:anantaḥ]]---Lord Ananta; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him; [[synonyms:a:abhāṣata]]---replied; [[synonyms:v:vidyādhara]]-[[synonyms:p:patim]]---the King of the Vidyādharas; [[synonyms:p:prītaḥ]]---being very pleased; [[synonyms:c:citraketum]]---King Citraketu; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:u:udvaha]]---O best of the Kuru dynasty, Mahārāja Parīkṣit.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: The Lord, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Anantadeva, being very much pleased with the prayers offered by Citraketu, the King of the Vidyādharas, replied to him as follows, O best of the Kuru dynasty, Mahārāja Parīkṣit.**
