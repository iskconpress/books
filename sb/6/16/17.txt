====== SB 6.16.17 ======

===== Text =====

> atha tasmai prapannāya
> bhaktāya prayatātmane
> bhagavān nāradaḥ prīto
> vidyām etām uvāca ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto him; [[synonyms:p:prapannāya]]---who was surrendered; [[synonyms:b:bhaktāya]]---being a devotee; [[synonyms:p:prayata]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmane]]---who was self-controlled; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]]---Nārada; [[synonyms:p:prītaḥ]]---being very pleased; [[synonyms:v:vidyām]]---transcendental knowledge; [[synonyms:e:etām]]---this; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---spoke; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, being very much pleased with Citraketu, who was a self-controlled devotee and surrendered soul, Nārada, the most powerful sage, spoke to him the following transcendental instructions.**
