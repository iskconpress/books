====== SB 6.16.23 ======

===== Text =====

> yan na spṛśanti na vidur
> mano-buddhīndriyāsavaḥ
> antar bahiś ca vitataṁ
> vyomavat tan nato 'smy aham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yat]]---whom; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:s:spṛśanti]]---can touch; [[synonyms:n:na]]---nor; [[synonyms:v:viduḥ]]---can know; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]---the mind; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---the intelligence; [[synonyms:i:indriya]]---the senses; [[synonyms:a:asavaḥ]]---the life airs; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---within; [[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]---outside; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vitatam]]---expanded; [[synonyms:v:vyoma]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like the sky; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:n:nataḥ]]---bowed; [[synonyms:a:asmi]]---am; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Brahman emanates from the Supreme Personality of Godhead and expands like the sky. Although untouched by anything material, it exists within and without. Nonetheless, the mind, intelligence, senses and living force can neither touch Him nor know Him. I offer unto Him my respectful obeisances.**
