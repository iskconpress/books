====== SB 6.16.27 ======

===== Text =====

> citraketus tu tāṁ vidyāṁ
> yathā nārada-bhāṣitām
> dhārayām āsa saptāham
> ab-bhakṣaḥ susamāhitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:citraketuḥ]]---King Citraketu; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vidyām]]---transcendental knowledge; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:n:nārada]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāṣitām]]---instructed by the great sage Nārada; [[synonyms:d:dhārayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---chanted; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:a:aham]]---continuously for one week; [[synonyms:a:ap]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakṣaḥ]]---only drinking water; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:samāhitaḥ]]---with great attention and care.

===== Translation =====

**Fasting and drinking only water, Citraketu for one week continuously chanted with great care and attention the mantra given by Nārada Muni.**
