====== SB 6.16.30 ======

===== Text =====

> mṛṇāla-gauraṁ śiti-vāsasaṁ sphurat-
> kirīṭa-keyūra-kaṭitra-kaṅkaṇam
> prasanna-vaktrāruṇa-locanaṁ vṛtaṁ
> dadarśa siddheśvara-maṇḍalaiḥ prabhum

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mṛṇāla]]-[[synonyms:g:gauram]]---white like the fibers of a lotus; [[synonyms:ś:śiti]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsasam]]---wearing garments of blue silk; [[synonyms:s:sphurat]]---glittering; [[synonyms:k:kirīṭa]]---helmet; [[synonyms:k:keyūra]]---armlets; [[synonyms:k:kaṭitra]]---belt; [[synonyms:k:kaṅkaṇam]]---whose bangles; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]]-[[synonyms:v:vaktra]]---smiling face; [[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:locanam]]---having reddish eyes; [[synonyms:v:vṛtam]]---surrounded; [[synonyms:d:dadarśa]]---he saw; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍalaiḥ]]---by the most perfect devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhum]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Upon reaching the shelter of Lord Śeṣa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Citraketu saw that He was as white as the white fibers of a lotus flower. He was dressed in bluish garments and adorned with a brilliantly glittering helmet, armlets, belt and bangles. His face was smiling, and His eyes were reddish. He was surrounded by such exalted liberated persons as Sanat-kumāra.**
