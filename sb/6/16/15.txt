====== SB 6.16.15 ======

===== Text =====

> sa itthaṁ pratibuddhātmā
> citraketur dvijoktibhiḥ
> gṛhāndha-kūpān niṣkrāntaḥ
> saraḥ-paṅkād iva dvipaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:i:ittham]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:pratibuddha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---being fully aware of spiritual knowledge; [[synonyms:c:citraketuḥ]]---King Citraketu; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:u:uktibhiḥ]]---by the instructions of the perfect //brāhmaṇas// (Aṅgirā and Nārada Muni); [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:a:andha]]-[[synonyms:k:kūpāt]]---from the dark well of family life; [[synonyms:n:niṣkrāntaḥ]]---came out; [[synonyms:s:saraḥ]]---of a lake or reservoir of water; [[synonyms:p:paṅkāt]]---from the mud; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:d:dvipaḥ]]---an elephant.

===== Translation =====

**Thus enlightened by the instructions of the brāhmaṇas Aṅgirā and Nārada, King Citraketu became fully aware of spiritual knowledge. As an elephant becomes free from a muddy reservoir of water, King Citraketu came out of the dark well of family life.**
