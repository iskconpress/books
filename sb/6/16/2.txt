====== SB 6.16.2 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-nārada uvāca
> jīvātman paśya bhadraṁ te
> mātaraṁ pitaraṁ ca te
> suhṛdo bāndhavās taptāḥ
> śucā tvat-kṛtayā bhṛśam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Nārada Muni said; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātman]]---O living entity; [[synonyms:p:paśya]]---just see; [[synonyms:b:bhadram]]---good fortune; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:m:mātaram]]---the mother; [[synonyms:p:pitaram]]---the father; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:t:te]]---of you; [[synonyms:s:suhṛdaḥ]]---friends; [[synonyms:b:bāndhavāḥ]]---relatives; [[synonyms:t:taptāḥ]]---aggrieved; [[synonyms:ś:śucā]]---by lamentation; [[synonyms:t:tvat]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtayā]]---because of you; [[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---very greatly.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nārada Muni said: O living entity, all good fortune unto you. Just see your father and mother. All your friends and relatives are overwhelmed with grief because of your passing away.**
