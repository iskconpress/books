====== SB 6.11.11 ======

===== Text =====

> airāvato vṛtra-gadābhimṛṣṭo
> vighūrṇito 'driḥ kuliśāhato yathā
> apāsarad bhinna-mukhaḥ sahendro
> muñcann asṛk sapta-dhanur bhṛśārtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:airāvataḥ]]---Airāvata, the elephant of King Indra; [[synonyms:v:vṛtra]]-[[synonyms:g:gadā]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimṛṣṭaḥ]]---struck by the club in Vṛtrāsura's hand; [[synonyms:v:vighūrṇitaḥ]]---shaken; [[synonyms:a:adriḥ]]---a mountain; [[synonyms:k:kuliśa]]---by a thunderbolt; [[synonyms:ā:āhataḥ]]---struck; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just like; [[synonyms:a:apāsarat]]---was pushed back; [[synonyms:b:bhinna]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhaḥ]]---having a broken mouth; [[synonyms:s:saha]]-[[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---with King Indra; [[synonyms:m:muñcan]]---spitting; [[synonyms:a:asṛk]]---blood; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:d:dhanuḥ]]---a distance measured by seven bows (approximately fourteen yards); [[synonyms:b:bhṛśa]]---very severely; [[synonyms:ā:ārtaḥ]]---aggrieved.

===== Translation =====

**Struck with the club by Vṛtrāsura like a mountain struck by a thunderbolt, the elephant Airāvata, feeling great pain and spitting blood from its broken mouth, was pushed back fourteen yards. In great distress, the elephant fell, with Indra on its back.**
