====== SB 6.11.17 ======

===== Text =====

> anye 'nu ye tveha nṛ-śaṁsam ajñā
> yad udyatāstrāḥ praharanti mahyam
> tair bhūta-nāthān sagaṇān niśāta-
> triśūla-nirbhinna-galair yajāmi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anye]]---others; [[synonyms:a:anu]]---follow; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---who; [[synonyms:t:tvā]]---you; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in this connection; [[synonyms:n:nṛ]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaṁsam]]---very cruel; [[synonyms:a:ajñāḥ]]---persons unaware of my prowess; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---if; [[synonyms:u:udyata]]-[[synonyms:a:astrāḥ]]---with their swords raised; [[synonyms:p:praharanti]]---attack; [[synonyms:m:mahyam]]---me; [[synonyms:t:taiḥ]]---with those; [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:n:nāthān]]---to such leaders of the ghosts as Bhairava; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇān]]---with their hordes; [[synonyms:n:niśāta]]---sharpened; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:ś:śūla]]---by the trident; [[synonyms:n:nirbhinna]]---separated or pierced; [[synonyms:g:galaiḥ]]---having their necks; [[synonyms:y:yajāmi]]---I shall offer sacrifices.

===== Translation =====

**You are naturally cruel. If the other demigods, unaware of my prowess, follow you by attacking me with raised weapons, I shall sever their heads with this sharp trident. With those heads I shall perform a sacrifice to Bhairava and the other leaders of the ghosts, along with their hordes.**
