====== SB 6.11.14 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-vṛtra uvāca
> diṣṭyā bhavān me samavasthito ripur
> yo brahma-hā guru-hā bhrātṛ-hā ca
> diṣṭyānṛṇo 'dyāham asattama tvayā
> mac-chūla-nirbhinna-dṛṣad-dhṛdācirāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛtraḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the great hero Vṛtrāsura said; [[synonyms:d:diṣṭyā]]---by good fortune; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---Your Lordship; [[synonyms:m:me]]---of me; [[synonyms:s:samavasthitaḥ]]---situated (in front); [[synonyms:r:ripuḥ]]---my enemy; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:h:hā]]---the killer of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:h:hā]]---the killer of your //guru//; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛ]]-[[synonyms:h:hā]]---the killer of my brother; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:diṣṭyā]]---by good fortune; [[synonyms:a:anṛṇaḥ]]---free from debt (to my brother); [[synonyms:a:adya]]---today; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:a:asat]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---O most abominable one; [[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---through you; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:ś:śūla]]---by my trident; [[synonyms:n:nirbhinna]]---being pierced; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣat]]---like stone; [[synonyms:h:hṛdā]]---whose heart; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Vṛtrāsura said: He who has killed a brāhmaṇa, he who has killed his spiritual master---indeed, he who has killed my brother---is now, by good fortune, standing before me face to face as my enemy. O most abominable one, when I pierce your stonelike heart with my trident, I shall be freed from my debt to my brother.**
