====== SB 6.5.9 ======

===== Text =====

> kathaṁ sva-pitur ādeśam
> avidvāṁso vipaścitaḥ
> anurūpam avijñāya
> aho sargaṁ kariṣyatha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:katham]]---how; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:p:pituḥ]]---of your own father; [[synonyms:ā:ādeśam]]---the order; [[synonyms:a:avidvāṁsaḥ]]---ignorant; [[synonyms:v:vipaścitaḥ]]---who knows everything; [[synonyms:a:anurūpam]]---suitable for you; [[synonyms:a:avijñāya]]---without knowing; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---alas; [[synonyms:s:sargam]]---the creation; [[synonyms:k:kariṣyatha]]---you will perform.

===== Translation =====

**Alas, your father is omniscient, but you do not know his actual order. Without knowing the actual purpose of your father, how will you create progeny?**
