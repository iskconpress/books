====== SB 6.5.10 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> tan niśamyātha haryaśvā
> autpattika-manīṣayā
> vācaḥ kūṭaṁ tu devarṣeḥ
> svayaṁ vimamṛśur dhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:n:niśamya]]---hearing; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:h:haryaśvāḥ]]---all the sons of Prajāpati Dakṣa; [[synonyms:a:autpattika]]---naturally awakened; [[synonyms:m:manīṣayā]]---by possessing the power to consider; [[synonyms:v:vācaḥ]]---of the speech; [[synonyms:k:kūṭam]]---the enigma; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:d:devarṣeḥ]]---of Nārada Muni; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---themselves; [[synonyms:v:vimamṛśuḥ]]---reflected upon; [[synonyms:d:dhiyā]]---with full intelligence.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Hearing these enigmatic words of Nārada Muni, the Haryaśvas considered them with their natural intelligence, without help from others.**
