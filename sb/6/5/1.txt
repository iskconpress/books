====== SB 6.5.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> tasyāṁ sa pāñcajanyāṁ vai
> viṣṇu-māyopabṛṁhitaḥ
> haryaśva-saṁjñān ayutaṁ
> putrān ajanayad vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:tasyām]]---in her; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Prajāpati Dakṣa; [[synonyms:p:pāñcajanyām]]---his wife named Pāñcajanī; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:u:upabṛṁhitaḥ]]---being made capable by the illusory energy of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:h:haryaśva]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁjñān]]---named the Haryaśvas; [[synonyms:a:ayutam]]---ten thousand; [[synonyms:p:putrān]]---sons; [[synonyms:a:ajanayat]]---begot; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---being powerful.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: Impelled by the illusory energy of Lord Viṣṇu, Prajāpati Dakṣa begot ten thousand sons in the womb of Pāñcajanī [Asiknī]. My dear King, these sons were called the Haryaśvas.**
