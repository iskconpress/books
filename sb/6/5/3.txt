====== SB 6.5.3 ======

===== Text =====

> tatra nārāyaṇa-saras
> tīrthaṁ sindhu-samudrayoḥ
> saṅgamo yatra sumahan
> muni-siddha-niṣevitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in that direction; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saraḥ]]---the lake named Nārāyaṇa-saras; [[synonyms:t:tīrtham]]---very holy place; [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:s:samudrayoḥ]]---of the River Sindhu and the sea; [[synonyms:s:saṅgamaḥ]]---confluence; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---where; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:m:mahat]]---very great; [[synonyms:m:muni]]---by sages; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]---and perfected human beings; [[synonyms:n:niṣevitam]]---frequented.

===== Translation =====

**In the west, where the River Sindhu meets the sea, there is a great place of pilgrimage known as Nārāyaṇa-saras. Many sages and others advanced in spiritual consciousness live there.**
