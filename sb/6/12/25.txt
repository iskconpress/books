====== SB 6.12.25 ======

===== Text =====

> sa tu vṛtrasya parighaṁ
> karaṁ ca karabhopamam
> ciccheda yugapad devo
> vajreṇa śata-parvaṇā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (King Indra); [[synonyms:t:tu]]---however; [[synonyms:v:vṛtrasya]]---of Vṛtrāsura; [[synonyms:p:parigham]]---the iron club; [[synonyms:k:karam]]---his hand; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:k:karabha]]-[[synonyms:u:upamam]]---as strong as the trunk of an elephant; [[synonyms:c:ciccheda]]---cut to pieces; [[synonyms:y:yugapat]]---simultaneously; [[synonyms:d:devaḥ]]---Lord Indra; [[synonyms:v:vajreṇa]]---with the thunderbolt; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:p:parvaṇā]]---having one hundred joints.

===== Translation =====

**With his thunderbolt named Śataparvan, Indra simultaneously cut to pieces Vṛtrāsura's club and his remaining hand.**
