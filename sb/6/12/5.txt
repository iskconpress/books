====== SB 6.12.5 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛtrasya karmāti-mahādbhutaṁ tat
> surāsurāś cāraṇa-siddha-saṅghāḥ
> apūjayaṁs tat puruhūta-saṅkaṭaṁ
> nirīkṣya hā heti vicukruśur bhṛśam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛtrasya]]---of Vṛtrāsura; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---the accomplishment; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---greatly; [[synonyms:a:adbhutam]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:s:sura]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:a:asurāḥ]]---and the demons; [[synonyms:c:cāraṇa]]---the Cāraṇas; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅghāḥ]]---and the society of Siddhas; [[synonyms:a:apūjayan]]---glorified; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:p:puruhūta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkaṭam]]---the dangerous position of Indra; [[synonyms:n:nirīkṣya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hā]] [[synonyms:h:hā]]---alas, alas; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vicukruśuḥ]]---lamented; [[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---very much.

===== Translation =====

**The denizens of various planets, like the demigods, demons, Cāraṇas and Siddhas, praised Vṛtrāsura's deed, but when they observed that Indra was in great danger, they lamented, "Alas! Alas!"**
