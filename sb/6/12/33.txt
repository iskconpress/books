====== SB 6.12.33 ======

===== Text =====

> vajras tu tat-kandharam āśu-vegaḥ
> kṛntan samantāt parivartamānaḥ
> nyapātayat tāvad ahar-gaṇena
> yo jyotiṣām ayane vārtra-hatye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vajraḥ]]---the thunderbolt; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:k:kandharam]]---his neck; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]-[[synonyms:v:vegaḥ]]---although very fast; [[synonyms:k:kṛntan]]---cutting; [[synonyms:s:samantāt]]---all around; [[synonyms:p:parivartamānaḥ]]---revolving; [[synonyms:n:nyapātayat]]---caused to fall; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]]---so many; [[synonyms:a:ahaḥ]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇena]]---by days; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---which; [[synonyms:j:jyotiṣām]]---of the luminaries like the sun and moon; [[synonyms:a:ayane]]---in moving to both sides of the equator; [[synonyms:v:vārtra]]-[[synonyms:h:hatye]]---at the time suitable for killing Vṛtrāsura.

===== Translation =====

**Although the thunderbolt revolved around Vṛtrāsura's neck with great speed, separating his head from his body took one complete year---360 days, the time in which the sun, moon and other luminaries complete a northern and southern journey. Then, at the suitable time for Vṛtrāsura to be killed, his head fell to the ground.**
