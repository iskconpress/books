====== SB 6.12.30 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛtra-grastaṁ tam ālokya
> saprajāpatayaḥ surāḥ
> hā kaṣṭam iti nirviṇṇāś
> cukruśuḥ samaharṣayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛtra]]-[[synonyms:g:grastam]]---swallowed by Vṛtrāsura; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Indra); [[synonyms:ā:ālokya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:prajāpatayaḥ]]---with Lord Brahmā and other //prajāpatis//; [[synonyms:s:surāḥ]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:h:hā]]---alas; [[synonyms:k:kaṣṭam]]---what a tribulation; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:n:nirviṇṇāḥ]]---being very morose; [[synonyms:c:cukruśuḥ]]---lamented; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---with the great sages.

===== Translation =====

**When the demigods, along with Brahmā, other prajāpatis and other great saintly persons, saw that Indra had been swallowed by the demon, they became very morose. "Alas," they lamented. "What a calamity ! What a calamity !"**
