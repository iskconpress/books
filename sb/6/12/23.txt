====== SB 6.12.23 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> iti bruvāṇāv anyonyaṁ
> dharma-jijñāsayā nṛpa
> yuyudhāte mahā-vīryāv
> indra-vṛtrau yudhām patī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bruvāṇau]]---speaking; [[synonyms:a:anyonyam]]---to one another; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:j:jijñāsayā]]---with a desire to know the supreme, ultimate religious principle (devotional service); [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:y:yuyudhāte]]---fought; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:vīryau]]---both very powerful; [[synonyms:i:indra]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:v:vṛtrau]]---and Vṛtrāsura; [[synonyms:y:yudhām]] [[synonyms:p:patī]]---both great military commanders.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Vṛtrāsura and King Indra spoke about devotional service even on the battlefield, and then as a matter of duty they again began fighting. My dear King, both of them were great fighters and were equally powerful.**
