====== SB 6.4.22 ======

===== Text =====

> astauṣīd dhaṁsa-guhyena
> bhagavantam adhokṣajam
> tubhyaṁ tad abhidhāsyāmi
> kasyātuṣyad yathā hariḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:astauṣīt]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:h:haṁsa]]-[[synonyms:g:guhyena]]---by the celebrated prayers known as //Haṁsa-guhya//; [[synonyms:b:bhagavantam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:adhokṣajam]]---who is beyond the reach of the senses; [[synonyms:t:tubhyam]]---unto you; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:a:abhidhāsyāmi]]---I shall explain; [[synonyms:k:kasya]]---with Dakṣa, the //prajāpati//; [[synonyms:a:atuṣyat]]---was satisfied; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---how; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**My dear King, I shall fully explain to you the Haṁsa-guhya prayers, which were offered to the Supreme Personality of Godhead by Dakṣa, and I shall explain how the Lord was pleased with him for those prayers.**

===== Purport =====

It is to be understood that the //Haṁsa-guhya// prayers were not composed by Dakṣa, but were existing in the Vedic literature.
