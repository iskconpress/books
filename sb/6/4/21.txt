====== SB 6.4.21 ======

===== Text =====

> tatrāghamarṣaṇaṁ nāma
> tīrthaṁ pāpa-haraṁ param
> upaspṛśyānusavanaṁ
> tapasātoṣayad dharim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:a:aghamarṣaṇam]]---Aghamarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:t:tīrtham]]---the holy place; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:h:haram]]---suitable for destroying all sinful reactions; [[synonyms:p:param]]---best; [[synonyms:u:upaspṛśya]]---performing //ācamana// and bathing; [[synonyms:a:anusavanam]]---regularly; [[synonyms:t:tapasā]]---by austerity; [[synonyms:a:atoṣayat]]---caused pleasure; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---to the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Near that mountain was a very holy place named Aghamarṣaṇa. There Prajāpati Dakṣa executed ritualistic ceremonies and satisfied the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari, by engaging in great austerities to please Him.**
