====== SB 6.4.51 ======

===== Text =====

> eṣā pañcajanasyāṅga
> duhitā vai prajāpateḥ
> asiknī nāma patnītve
> prajeśa pratigṛhyatām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eṣā]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pañcajanasya]]---of Pañcajana; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---O My dear son; [[synonyms:d:duhitā]]---the daughter; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:prajāpateḥ]]---another //prajāpati//; [[synonyms:a:asiknī]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the name Asiknī; [[synonyms:p:patnītve]]---as your wife; [[synonyms:p:prajeśa]]---O //prajāpati//; [[synonyms:p:pratigṛhyatām]]---let her be accepted.

===== Translation =====

**O My dear son Dakṣa, Prajāpati Pañcajana has a daughter named Asiknī, whom I offer to you so that you may accept her as your wife.**
