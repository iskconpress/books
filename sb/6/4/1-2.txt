====== SB 6.4.1-2 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> devāsura-nṛṇāṁ sargo
> nāgānāṁ mṛga-pakṣiṇām
> sāmāsikas tvayā prokto
> yas tu svāyambhuve 'ntare
>
> tasyaiva vyāsam icchāmi
> jñātuṁ te bhagavan yathā
> anusargaṁ yayā śaktyā
> sasarja bhagavān paraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the King said; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛṇām]]---of the demigods, the demons and the human beings; [[synonyms:s:sargaḥ]]---the creation; [[synonyms:n:nāgānām]]---of the Nāgas (serpentine living entities); [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:p:pakṣiṇām]]---of the beasts and birds; [[synonyms:s:sāmāsikaḥ]]---briefly; [[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by you; [[synonyms:p:proktaḥ]]---described; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---which; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---however; [[synonyms:s:svāyambhuve]]---of Svāyambhuva Manu; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the period; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of this; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vyāsam]]---the detailed account; [[synonyms:i:icchāmi]]---I wish; [[synonyms:j:jñātum]]---to know; [[synonyms:t:te]]---from you; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O my lord; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as well as; [[synonyms:a:anusargam]]---the subsequent creation; [[synonyms:y:yayā]]---by which; [[synonyms:ś:śaktyā]]---potency; [[synonyms:s:sasarja]]---created; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:paraḥ]]---transcendental.

===== Translation =====

**The blessed King said to Śukadeva Gosvāmī: My dear lord, the demigods, demons, human beings, Nāgas, beasts and birds were created during the reign of Svāyambhuva Manu. You have spoken about this creation briefly [in the Third Canto]. Now I wish to know about it elaborately. I also wish to know about the potency of the Supreme Personality of Godhead by which He brought about the secondary creation.**
