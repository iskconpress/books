====== SB 6.4.15 ======

===== Text =====

> alaṁ dagdhair drumair dīnaiḥ
> khilānāṁ śivam astu vaḥ
> vārkṣī hy eṣā varā kanyā
> patnītve pratigṛhyatām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alam]]---enough; [[synonyms:d:dagdhaiḥ]]---with burning; [[synonyms:d:drumaiḥ]]---the trees; [[synonyms:d:dīnaiḥ]]---poor; [[synonyms:k:khilānām]]---of the remainder of the trees; [[synonyms:ś:śivam]]---all good fortune; [[synonyms:a:astu]]---let there be; [[synonyms:v:vaḥ]]---of you; [[synonyms:v:vārkṣī]]---raised by the trees; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:e:eṣā]]---this; [[synonyms:v:varā]]---choice; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---daughter; [[synonyms:p:patnītve]]---into wifehood; [[synonyms:p:pratigṛhyatām]]---let her be accepted.

===== Translation =====

**There is no need to burn these poor trees any longer. Let whatever trees still remain be happy. Indeed, you should also be happy. Now, here is a beautiful, well-qualified girl named Māriṣā, who was raised by the trees as their daughter. You may accept this beautiful girl as your wife.**
