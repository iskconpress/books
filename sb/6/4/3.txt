====== SB 6.4.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-sūta uvāca
> iti sampraśnam ākarṇya
> rājarṣer bādarāyaṇiḥ
> pratinandya mahā-yogī
> jagāda muni-sattamāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Sūta Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sampraśnam]]---the inquiry; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṇya]]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rājarṣeḥ]]---of King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]]---Śukadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pratinandya]]---praising; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogī]]---the great //yogī//; [[synonyms:j:jagāda]]---replied; [[synonyms:m:muni]]-[[synonyms:s:sattamāḥ]]---O best of the sages.

===== Translation =====

**Sūta Gosvāmī said: O great sages [assembled at Naimiṣāraṇya], after the great yogi Śukadeva Gosvāmī heard King Parīkṣit's inquiry, he praised it and thus replied.**
