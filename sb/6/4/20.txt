====== SB 6.4.20 ======

===== Text =====

> tam abṛṁhitam ālokya
> prajā-sargaṁ prajāpatiḥ
> vindhya-pādān upavrajya
> so 'carad duṣkaraṁ tapaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---that; [[synonyms:a:abṛṁhitam]]---not increasing; [[synonyms:ā:ālokya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prajā]]-[[synonyms:s:sargam]]---the creation of the living entities; [[synonyms:p:prajāpatiḥ]]---Dakṣa, the generator of living entities; [[synonyms:v:vindhya]]-[[synonyms:p:pādān]]---the mountains near the Vindhya mountain range; [[synonyms:u:upavrajya]]---going to; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:a:acarat]]---executed; [[synonyms:d:duṣkaram]]---very difficult; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerities.

===== Translation =====

**But when Prajāpati Dakṣa saw that he was not properly generating all kinds of living entities, he approached a mountain near the Vindhya mountain range, and there he executed very difficult austerities.**
