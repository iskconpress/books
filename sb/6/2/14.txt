====== SB 6.2.14 ======

===== Text =====

> sāṅketyaṁ pārihāsyaṁ vā
> stobhaṁ helanam eva vā
> vaikuṇṭha-nāma-grahaṇam
> aśeṣāgha-haraṁ viduḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāṅketyam]]---as an assignation; [[synonyms:p:pārihāsyam]]---jokingly; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:s:stobham]]---as musical entertainment; [[synonyms:h:helanam]]---neglectfully; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:g:grahaṇam]]---chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:a:aśeṣa]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:agha]]-[[synonyms:h:haram]]---neutralizing the effect of sinful life; [[synonyms:v:viduḥ]]---advanced transcendentalists know.

===== Translation =====

**One who chants the holy name of the Lord is immediately freed from the reactions of unlimited sins, even if he chants indirectly [to indicate something else], jokingly, for musical entertainment, or even neglectfully. This is accepted by all the learned scholars of the scriptures.**
