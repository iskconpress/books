====== SB 6.2.20 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> ta evaṁ suvinirṇīya
> dharmaṁ bhāgavataṁ nṛpa
> taṁ yāmya-pāśān nirmucya
> vipraṁ mṛtyor amūmucan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they (the order carriers of Lord Viṣṇu); [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vinirṇīya]]---perfectly ascertaining; [[synonyms:d:dharmam]]---real religion; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavatam]]---in terms of devotional service; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Ajāmila); [[synonyms:y:yāmya]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśāt]]---from the bondage of the order carriers of Yamarāja; [[synonyms:n:nirmucya]]---releasing; [[synonyms:v:vipram]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyoḥ]]---from death; [[synonyms:a:amūmucan]]---rescued.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: My dear King, having thus perfectly judged the principles of devotional service with reasoning and arguments, the order carriers of Lord Viṣṇu released the brāhmaṇa Ajāmila from the bondage of the Yamadūtas and saved him from imminent death.**
