====== SB 6.2.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bādarāyaṇir uvāca
> evaṁ te bhagavad-dūtā
> yamadūtābhibhāṣitam
> upadhāryātha tān rājan
> pratyāhur naya-kovidāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śukadeva Gosvāmī, the son of Vyāsadeva, said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:d:dūtāḥ]]---the servants of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:y:yamadūta]]---by the servants of Yamarāja; [[synonyms:a:abhibhāṣitam]]---what was spoken; [[synonyms:u:upadhārya]]---hearing; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---unto them; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:p:pratyāhuḥ]]---replied properly; [[synonyms:n:naya]]-[[synonyms:k:kovidāḥ]]---being conversant in good arguments or good logic.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: My dear King, the servants of Lord Viṣṇu are always very expert in logic and arguments. After hearing the statements of the Yamadūtas, they replied as follows.**
