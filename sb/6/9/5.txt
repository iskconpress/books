====== SB 6.9.5 ======

===== Text =====

> soma-pīthaṁ tu yat tasya
> śira āsīt kapiñjalaḥ
> kalaviṅkaḥ surā-pītham
> annādaṁ yat sa tittiriḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:soma]]-[[synonyms:p:pītham]]---used for drinking //soma-rasa//; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---however; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Viśvarūpa); [[synonyms:ś:śiraḥ]]---the head; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---became; [[synonyms:k:kapiñjalaḥ]]---a francolin partridge; [[synonyms:k:kalaviṅkaḥ]]---a sparrow; [[synonyms:s:surā]]-[[synonyms:p:pītham]]---meant for drinking wine; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:a:adam]]---used for eating food; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:t:tittiriḥ]]---a common partridge.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, the head meant for drinking soma-rasa was transformed into a kapiñjala [francolin partridge]. Similarly, the head meant for drinking wine was transformed into a kalaviṅka [sparrow], and the head meant for eating food became a tittiri [common partridge].**
