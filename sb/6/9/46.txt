====== SB 6.9.46 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> athaivam īḍito rājan
> sādaraṁ tri-daśair hariḥ
> svam upasthānam ākarṇya
> prāha tān abhinanditaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ī:īḍitaḥ]]---being worshiped and offered obeisances; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādaram]]---with proper respect; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:d:daśaiḥ]]---by all the demigods from the higher planetary systems; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:svam]] [[synonyms:u:upasthānam]]---their prayer glorifying Him; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṇya]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---replied; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---unto them (the demigods); [[synonyms:a:abhinanditaḥ]]---being pleased.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King Parīkṣit, when the demigods offered the Lord their sincere prayers in this way, the Lord listened by His causeless mercy. Being pleased, He then replied to the demigods.**
