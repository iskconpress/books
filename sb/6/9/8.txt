====== SB 6.9.8 ======

===== Text =====

> turyaṁ cheda-viroheṇa
> vareṇa jagṛhur drumāḥ
> teṣāṁ niryāsa-rūpeṇa
> brahma-hatyā pradṛśyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:turyam]]---one fourth; [[synonyms:c:cheda]]---although being cut; [[synonyms:v:viroheṇa]]---of growing again; [[synonyms:v:vareṇa]]---because of the benediction; [[synonyms:j:jagṛhuḥ]]---accepted; [[synonyms:d:drumāḥ]]---the trees; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of them; [[synonyms:n:niryāsa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpeṇa]]---by the liquid oozing from the trees; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:h:hatyā]]---the reaction for killing a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:pradṛśyate]]---is visible.

===== Translation =====

**In return for Indra's benediction that their branches and twigs would grow back when trimmed, the trees accepted one fourth of the reactions for killing a brāhmaṇa. These reactions are visible in the flowing of sap from trees. [Therefore one is forbidden to drink this sap.]**
