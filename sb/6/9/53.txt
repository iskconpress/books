====== SB 6.9.53 ======

===== Text =====

> dadhyaṅṅ ātharvaṇas tvaṣṭre
> varmābhedyaṁ mad-ātmakam
> viśvarūpāya yat prādāt
> tvaṣṭā yat tvam adhās tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dadhyaṅ]]---Dadhyañca; [[synonyms:ā:ātharvaṇaḥ]]---the son of Atharvā; [[synonyms:t:tvaṣṭre]]---unto Tvaṣṭā; [[synonyms:v:varma]]---the protective covering known as Nārāyaṇa-kavaca; [[synonyms:a:abhedyam]]---invincible; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmakam]]---consisting of Myself; [[synonyms:v:viśvarūpāya]]---unto Viśvarūpa; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:p:prādāt]]---delivered; [[synonyms:t:tvaṣṭā]]---Tvaṣṭā; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---you; [[synonyms:a:adhāḥ]]---received; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him.

===== Translation =====

**Dadhyañca's invincible protective covering known as the Nārāyaṇa-kavaca was given to Tvaṣṭā, who delivered it to his son Viśvarūpa, from whom you have received it. Because of this Nārāyaṇa-kavaca, Dadhīci's body is now very strong. You should therefore beg him for his body.**
