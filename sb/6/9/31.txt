====== SB 6.9.31 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-devā ūcuḥ
> namas te yajña-vīryāya
> vayase uta te namaḥ
> namas te hy asta-cakrāya
> namaḥ supuru-hūtaye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:d:devāḥ]] [[synonyms:ū:ūcuḥ]]---the demigods said; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto You; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:v:vīryāya]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is able to give the results of sacrifice; [[synonyms:v:vayase]]---who is the time factor, which ends the results of //yajña//; [[synonyms:u:uta]]---although; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto You; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto You; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:asta]]-[[synonyms:c:cakrāya]]---who throws the disc; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:s:supuru]]-[[synonyms:h:hūtaye]]---having varieties of transcendental names.

===== Translation =====

**The demigods said: O Supreme Personality of Godhead, You are competent to give the results of sacrifice, and You are also the time factor that destroys all such results in due course. You are the one who releases the cakra to kill the demons. O Lord, who possess many varieties of names, we offer our respectful obeisances unto You.**
