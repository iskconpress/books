====== SB 6.9.12 ======

===== Text =====

> athānvāhārya-pacanād
> utthito ghora-darśanaḥ
> kṛtānta iva lokānāṁ
> yugānta-samaye yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:a:anvāhārya]]-[[synonyms:p:pacanāt]]---from the fire known as Anvāhārya; [[synonyms:u:utthitaḥ]]---arisen; [[synonyms:g:ghora]]-[[synonyms:d:darśanaḥ]]---appearing very fearful; [[synonyms:k:kṛtāntaḥ]]---personified annihilation; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:l:lokānām]]---of all the planets; [[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:a:anta]]---of the end of the millennium; [[synonyms:s:samaye]]---at the time; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, from the southern side of the sacrificial fire known as Anvāhārya came a fearful personality who looked like the destroyer of the entire creation at the end of the millennium.**
