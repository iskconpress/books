====== SB 6.17.16 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> evaṁ śaptaś citraketur
> vimānād avaruhya saḥ
> prasādayām āsa satīṁ
> mūrdhnā namreṇa bhārata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:ś:śaptaḥ]]---cursed; [[synonyms:c:citraketuḥ]]---King Citraketu; [[synonyms:v:vimānāt]]---from his airplane; [[synonyms:a:avaruhya]]---coming down; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:p:prasādayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---completely pleased; [[synonyms:s:satīm]]---Pārvatī; [[synonyms:m:mūrdhnā]]---by his head; [[synonyms:n:namreṇa]]---bent low; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]---O King Parīkṣit.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: My dear King Parīkṣit, when Citraketu was cursed by Pārvatī, he descended from his airplane, bowed before her with great humility and pleased her completely.**
