====== SB 6.17.11 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-pārvaty uvāca
> ayaṁ kim adhunā loke
> śāstā daṇḍa-dharaḥ prabhuḥ
> asmad-vidhānāṁ duṣṭānāṁ
> nirlajjānāṁ ca viprakṛt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:pārvatī]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the goddess Pārvatī said; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kim]]---whether; [[synonyms:a:adhunā]]---now; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---in the world; [[synonyms:ś:śāstā]]---the supreme controller; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:dharaḥ]]---the carrier of the rod of punishment; [[synonyms:p:prabhuḥ]]---the master; [[synonyms:a:asmat]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhānām]]---of persons like us; [[synonyms:d:duṣṭānām]]---criminals; [[synonyms:n:nirlajjānām]]---who have no shame; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:viprakṛt]]---the restrainer.

===== Translation =====

**The goddess Pārvatī said: Alas, has this upstart now received a post from which to punish shameless persons like us? Has he been appointed ruler, carrier of the rod of punishment? Is he now the only master of everything?**
