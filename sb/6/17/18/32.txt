====== SB 6.18.32 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kaśyapa uvāca
> varaṁ varaya vāmoru
> prītas te 'ham anindite
> striyā bhartari suprīte
> kaḥ kāma iha cāgamaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kaśyapaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Kaśyapa Muni said; [[synonyms:v:varam]]---benediction; [[synonyms:v:varaya]]---ask; [[synonyms:v:vāmoru]]---O beautiful woman; [[synonyms:p:prītaḥ]]---pleased; [[synonyms:t:te]]---with you; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:a:anindite]]---O irreproachable lady; [[synonyms:s:striyāḥ]]---for the woman; [[synonyms:b:bhartari]]---when the husband; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:p:prīte]]---pleased; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---what; [[synonyms:k:kāmaḥ]]---desire; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---here; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:agamaḥ]]---difficult to obtain.

===== Translation =====

**Kaśyapa Muni said: O beautiful woman, O irreproachable lady, since I am very much pleased by your behavior, you may ask me for any benediction you want. If a husband is pleased, what desires are difficult for his wife to obtain, either in this world or in the next?**
