====== SB 6.17.41 ======

===== Text =====

> ya etat prātar utthāya
> śraddhayā vāg-yataḥ paṭhet
> itihāsaṁ hariṁ smṛtvā
> sa yāti paramāṁ gatim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---any person who; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]---early in the morning; [[synonyms:u:utthāya]]---rising; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---with faith; [[synonyms:v:vāk]]-[[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---controlling the mind and words; [[synonyms:p:paṭhet]]---may read; [[synonyms:i:itihāsam]]---history; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:s:smṛtvā]]---remembering; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that person; [[synonyms:y:yāti]]---goes; [[synonyms:p:paramām]] [[synonyms:g:gatim]]---back home, back to Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**One who rises from bed early in the morning and recites this history of Citraketu, controlling his words and mind and remembering the Supreme Personality of Godhead, will return home, back to Godhead, without difficulty.**
