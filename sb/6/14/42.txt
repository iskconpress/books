====== SB 6.14.42 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ sandahyamānānāṁ
> sapatnyāḥ putra-sampadā
> rājño 'sammata-vṛttīnāṁ
> vidveṣo balavān abhūt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sandahyamānānām]]---of the queens, who were constantly burning in lamentation; [[synonyms:s:sapatnyāḥ]]---of the co-wife Kṛtadyuti; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:s:sampadā]]---due to the opulence of a son; [[synonyms:r:rājñaḥ]]---by the King; [[synonyms:a:asammata]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛttīnām]]---not being very much favored; [[synonyms:v:vidveṣaḥ]]---envy; [[synonyms:b:balavān]]---very strong; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: Being neglected by their husband and seeing Kṛtadyuti's opulence in possessing a son, Kṛtadyuti's co-wives always burned in envy, which became extremely strong.**
