====== SB 6.14.59 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> vilapantyā mṛtaṁ putram
> iti citra-vilāpanaiḥ
> citraketur bhṛśaṁ tapto
> mukta-kaṇṭho ruroda ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:v:vilapantyā]]---with the woman who was lamenting; [[synonyms:m:mṛtam]]---dead; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---for the son; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:citra]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāpanaiḥ]]---with various lamentations; [[synonyms:c:citraketuḥ]]---King Citraketu; [[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---very much; [[synonyms:t:taptaḥ]]---aggrieved; [[synonyms:m:mukta]]-[[synonyms:k:kaṇṭhaḥ]]---loudly; [[synonyms:r:ruroda]]---cried; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: Accompanied by his wife, who was thus lamenting for her dead son, King Citraketu began crying loudly with an open mouth, being greatly aggrieved.**
