====== SB 6.14.3 ======

===== Text =====

> rajobhiḥ sama-saṅkhyātāḥ
> pārthivair iha jantavaḥ
> teṣāṁ ye kecanehante
> śreyo vai manujādayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rajobhiḥ]]---with the atoms; [[synonyms:s:sama]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkhyātāḥ]]---having the same numerical strength; [[synonyms:p:pārthivaiḥ]]---of the earth; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in this world; [[synonyms:j:jantavaḥ]]---the living entities; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of them; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---those who; [[synonyms:k:kecana]]---some; [[synonyms:ī:īhante]]---act; [[synonyms:ś:śreyaḥ]]---for religious principles; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:m:manuja]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the human beings and so on.

===== Translation =====

**In this material world there are as many living entities as atoms. Among these living entities, a very few are human beings, and among them, few are interested in following religious principles.**
