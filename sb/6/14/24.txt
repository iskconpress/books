====== SB 6.14.24 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi pṛcchato brūyāṁ
> brahmann ātmani cintitam
> bhavato viduṣaś cāpi
> coditas tvad-anujñayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:p:pṛcchataḥ]]---asking; [[synonyms:b:brūyām]]---let me speak; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O great //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ā:ātmani]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:c:cintitam]]---anxiety; [[synonyms:b:bhavataḥ]]---to you; [[synonyms:v:viduṣaḥ]]---who know everything; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:c:coditaḥ]]---being inspired; [[synonyms:t:tvat]]---your; [[synonyms:a:anujñayā]]---by the order.

===== Translation =====

**O great soul, you are aware of everything, yet you are asking me why I am full of anxiety. Therefore, in response to your order, let me disclose the cause.**
