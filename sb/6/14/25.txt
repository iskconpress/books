====== SB 6.14.25 ======

===== Text =====

> loka-pālair api prārthyāḥ
> sāmrājyaiśvarya-sampadaḥ
> na nandayanty aprajaṁ māṁ
> kṣut-tṛṭ-kāmam ivāpare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:p:pālaiḥ]]---by great demigods; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:p:prārthyāḥ]]---desirable; [[synonyms:s:sāmrājya]]---a great empire; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---material opulence; [[synonyms:s:sampadaḥ]]---possessions; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:n:nandayanti]]---do not give pleasure; [[synonyms:a:aprajam]]---because of having no son; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kṣut]]---hunger; [[synonyms:t:tṛṭ]]---thirst; [[synonyms:k:kāmam]]---desiring to satisfy; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:a:apare]]---other enjoyable sense objects.

===== Translation =====

**As a person aggrieved by hunger and thirst is not pleased by the external gratification of flower garlands or sandalwood pulp, I am not pleased with my empire, opulence or possessions, which are desirable even for great demigods, because I have no son.**
