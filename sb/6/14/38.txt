====== SB 6.14.38 ======

===== Text =====

> citraketor atiprītir
> yathā dāre prajāvati
> na tathānyeṣu sañjajñe
> bālaṁ lālayato 'nvaham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:citraketoḥ]]---of King Citraketu; [[synonyms:a:atiprītiḥ]]---excessive attraction; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:d:dāre]]---unto the wife; [[synonyms:p:prajā]]-[[synonyms:v:vati]]---who begot a son; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---like that; [[synonyms:a:anyeṣu]]---unto the others; [[synonyms:s:sañjajñe]]---arose; [[synonyms:b:bālam]]---the son; [[synonyms:l:lālayataḥ]]---taking care of; [[synonyms:a:anvaham]]---constantly.

===== Translation =====

**As King Citraketu fostered his son very carefully, his affection for Queen Kṛtadyuti increased, but gradually he lost affection for the other wives, who had no sons.**
