====== SB 6.14.34 ======

===== Text =====

> tebhyo hiraṇyaṁ rajataṁ
> vāsāṁsy ābharaṇāni ca
> grāmān hayān gajān prādād
> dhenūnām arbudāni ṣaṭ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tebhyaḥ]]---unto them (the learned //brāhmaṇas//); [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyam]]---gold; [[synonyms:r:rajatam]]---silver; [[synonyms:v:vāsāṁsi]]---garments; [[synonyms:ā:ābharaṇāni]]---ornaments; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:g:grāmān]]---villages; [[synonyms:h:hayān]]---horses; [[synonyms:g:gajān]]---elephants; [[synonyms:p:prādāt]]---gave in charity; [[synonyms:d:dhenūnām]]---of cows; [[synonyms:a:arbudāni]]---groups of one hundred million; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]---six.

===== Translation =====

**Unto the brāhmaṇas who took part in the ritualistic ceremony the King gave charity of gold, silver, garments, ornaments, villages, horses and elephants, as well as sixty crores of cows [six hundred million cows].**
