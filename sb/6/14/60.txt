====== SB 6.14.60 ======

===== Text =====

> tayor vilapatoḥ sarve
> dampatyos tad-anuvratāḥ
> ruruduḥ sma narā nāryaḥ
> sarvam āsīd acetanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---while the two of them; [[synonyms:v:vilapatoḥ]]---were lamenting; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dam]]-[[synonyms:p:patyoḥ]]---the King, along with his wife; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:a:anuvratāḥ]]---their followers; [[synonyms:r:ruruduḥ]]---cried loudly; [[synonyms:s:sma]]---indeed; [[synonyms:n:narāḥ]]---the male members; [[synonyms:n:nāryaḥ]]---the female members; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---the whole kingdom; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---became; [[synonyms:a:acetanam]]---almost unconscious.

===== Translation =====

**As the King and Queen lamented, all their male and female followers joined them in crying. Because of the sudden accident, all the citizens of the kingdom were almost unconscious.**
