====== SB 6.14.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyaikadā tu bhavanam
> aṅgirā bhagavān ṛṣiḥ
> lokān anucarann etān
> upāgacchad yadṛcchayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him; [[synonyms:e:ekadā]]---once upon a time; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:b:bhavanam]]---to the palace; [[synonyms:a:aṅgirāḥ]]---Aṅgirā; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---very powerful; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---sage; [[synonyms:l:lokān]]---planets; [[synonyms:a:anucaran]]---traveling around; [[synonyms:e:etān]]---these; [[synonyms:u:upāgacchat]]---came; [[synonyms:y:yadṛcchayā]]---suddenly.

===== Translation =====

**Once upon a time, when the powerful sage named Aṅgirā was traveling all over the universe without engagement, by his sweet will he came to the palace of King Citraketu.**
