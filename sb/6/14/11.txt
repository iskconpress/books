====== SB 6.14.11 ======

===== Text =====

> tasya bhāryā-sahasrāṇāṁ
> sahasrāṇi daśābhavan
> sāntānikaś cāpi nṛpo
> na lebhe tāsu santatim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (King Citraketu); [[synonyms:b:bhāryā]]---of wives; [[synonyms:s:sahasrāṇām]]---of thousands; [[synonyms:s:sahasrāṇi]]---thousands; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten; [[synonyms:a:abhavan]]---there were; [[synonyms:s:sāntānikaḥ]]---quite capable of begetting sons; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:n:nṛpaḥ]]---the King; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:l:lebhe]]---obtained; [[synonyms:t:tāsu]]---in them; [[synonyms:s:santatim]]---a son.

===== Translation =====

**This Citraketu had ten million wives, but although he was capable of producing children, he did not receive a child from any of them. By chance, all the wives were barren.**
