====== SB 6.14.36 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛcchra-labdhe 'tha rājarṣes
> tanaye 'nudinaṁ pituḥ
> yathā niḥsvasya kṛcchrāpte
> dhane sneho 'nvavardhata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛcchra]]---with great difficulty; [[synonyms:l:labdhe]]---gained; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣeḥ]]---of the pious King Citraketu; [[synonyms:t:tanaye]]---for the son; [[synonyms:a:anudinam]]---day after day; [[synonyms:p:pituḥ]]---of the father; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---exactly as; [[synonyms:n:niḥsvasya]]---of a poor man; [[synonyms:k:kṛcchra]]-[[synonyms:ā:āpte]]---gained after great difficulty; [[synonyms:d:dhane]]---for riches; [[synonyms:s:snehaḥ]]---affection; [[synonyms:a:anvavardhata]]---increased.

===== Translation =====

**When a poor man gets some money after great difficulty, his affection for the money increases daily. Similarly, when King Citraketu, after great difficulty, received a son, his affection for the son increased day after day.**
