====== SB 6.14.61 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ kaśmalam āpannaṁ
> naṣṭa-saṁjñam anāyakam
> jñātvāṅgirā nāma ṛṣir
> ājagāma sanāradaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kaśmalam]]---misery; [[synonyms:ā:āpannam]]---having gotten; [[synonyms:n:naṣṭa]]---lost; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñam]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:a:anāyakam]]---without help; [[synonyms:j:jñātvā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:a:aṅgirāḥ]]---Aṅgirā; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---the saintly person; [[synonyms:ā:ājagāma]]---came; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]]---with Nārada Muni.

===== Translation =====

**When the great sage Aṅgirā understood that the King was almost dead in an ocean of lamentation, he went there with Nārada Ṛṣi.**
