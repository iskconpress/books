====== SB 6.14.8 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-sūta uvāca
> parīkṣito 'tha sampraśnaṁ
> bhagavān bādarāyaṇiḥ
> niśamya śraddadhānasya
> pratinandya vaco 'bravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣitaḥ]]---of Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sampraśnam]]---the perfect question; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]]---Śukadeva Gosvāmī, the son of Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:n:niśamya]]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śraddadhānasya]]---of his disciple, who was so faithful in understanding the truth; [[synonyms:p:pratinandya]]---congratulating; [[synonyms:v:vacaḥ]]---words; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---spoke.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said: After hearing Mahārāja Parīkṣit's very intelligent question, Śukadeva Gosvāmī, the most powerful sage, began answering his disciple with great affection.**
