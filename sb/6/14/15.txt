====== SB 6.14.15 ======

===== Text =====

> taṁ pūjayitvā vidhivat
> pratyutthānārhaṇādibhiḥ
> kṛtātithyam upāsīdat
> sukhāsīnaṁ samāhitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:p:pūjayitvā]]---after worshiping; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---according to the rules and regulations for receiving exalted guests; [[synonyms:p:pratyutthāna]]---by standing from the throne; [[synonyms:a:arhaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---offering worship and so on; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:atithyam]]---who was given hospitality; [[synonyms:u:upāsīdat]]---sat down near; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsīnam]]---who was seated very comfortably; [[synonyms:s:samāhitaḥ]]---controlling his mind and senses.

===== Translation =====

**Citraketu immediately stood up from his throne and offered him worship. He offered drinking water and eatables and in this way performed his duty as a host to a great guest. When the ṛṣi was seated very comfortably, the King, restraining his mind and senses, sat on the ground at the side of the ṛṣi's feet.**
