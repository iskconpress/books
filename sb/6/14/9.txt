====== SB 6.14.9 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> śṛṇuṣvāvahito rājann
> itihāsam imaṁ yathā
> śrutaṁ dvaipāyana-mukhān
> nāradād devalād api

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇuṣva]]---please hear; [[synonyms:a:avahitaḥ]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:i:itihāsam]]---history; [[synonyms:i:imam]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:ś:śrutam]]---heard; [[synonyms:d:dvaipāyana]]---of Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:m:mukhāt]]---from the mouth; [[synonyms:n:nāradāt]]---from Nārada; [[synonyms:d:devalāt]]---from Devala Ṛṣi; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King, I shall speak to you the same history I have heard from the mouths of Vyāsadeva, Nārada and Devala. Please listen with attention.**
