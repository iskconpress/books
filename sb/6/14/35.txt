====== SB 6.14.35 ======

===== Text =====

> vavarṣa kāmān anyeṣāṁ
> parjanya iva dehinām
> dhanyaṁ yaśasyam āyuṣyaṁ
> kumārasya mahā-manāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vavarṣa]]---showered, gave in charity; [[synonyms:k:kāmān]]---all desirable things; [[synonyms:a:anyeṣām]]---of others; [[synonyms:p:parjanyaḥ]]---a cloud; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:d:dehinām]]---of all living entities; [[synonyms:d:dhanyam]]---with the desire for an increase of opulence; [[synonyms:y:yaśasyam]]---an increase of reputation; [[synonyms:ā:āyuṣyam]]---and an increase of the duration of life; [[synonyms:k:kumārasya]]---of the newly born child; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:manāḥ]]---the beneficent King Citraketu.

===== Translation =====

**As a cloud indiscriminately pours water on the earth, the beneficent King Citraketu, to increase the reputation, opulence and longevity of his son, distributed like rainfall all desirable things to everyone.**
