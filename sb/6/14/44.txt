====== SB 6.14.44 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛtadyutir ajānantī
> sapatnīnām aghaṁ mahat
> supta eveti sañcintya
> nirīkṣya vyacarad gṛhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛtadyutiḥ]]---Queen Kṛtadyuti; [[synonyms:a:ajānantī]]---being unaware of; [[synonyms:s:sapatnīnām]]---of her co-wives; [[synonyms:a:agham]]---sinful act; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]---very great; [[synonyms:s:suptaḥ]]---sleeping; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sañcintya]]---thinking; [[synonyms:n:nirīkṣya]]---looking at; [[synonyms:v:vyacarat]]---was walking; [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at home.

===== Translation =====

**Unaware of the poison administered by her co-wives, Queen Kṛtadyuti walked within the house, thinking that her son was sleeping deeply. She did not understand that he was dead.**
