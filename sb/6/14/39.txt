====== SB 6.14.39 ======

===== Text =====

> tāḥ paryatapyann ātmānaṁ
> garhayantyo 'bhyasūyayā
> ānapatyena duḥkhena
> rājñaś cānādareṇa ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāḥ]]---they (the queens who did not have sons); [[synonyms:p:paryatapyan]]---lamented; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---themselves; [[synonyms:g:garhayantyaḥ]]---condemning; [[synonyms:a:abhyasūyayā]]---out of envy; [[synonyms:ā:ānapatyena]]---due to being without sons; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhena]]---by unhappiness; [[synonyms:r:rājñaḥ]]---of the King; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:anādareṇa]]---due to negligence; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**The other queens were extremely unhappy due to their being sonless. Because of the King's negligence toward them, they condemned themselves in envy and lamented.**
