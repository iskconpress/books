====== SB 6.14.57 ======

===== Text =====

> uttiṣṭha tāta ta ime śiśavo vayasyās
> tvām āhvayanti nṛpa-nandana saṁvihartum
> suptaś ciraṁ hy aśanayā ca bhavān parīto
> bhuṅkṣva stanaṁ piba śuco hara naḥ svakānām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttiṣṭha]]---kindly get up; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---my dear son; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they; [[synonyms:i:ime]]---all these; [[synonyms:ś:śiśavaḥ]]---children; [[synonyms:v:vayasyāḥ]]---playmates; [[synonyms:t:tvām]]---you; [[synonyms:ā:āhvayanti]]---are calling; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---O son of the King; [[synonyms:s:saṁvihartum]]---to play with; [[synonyms:s:suptaḥ]]---you have slept; [[synonyms:c:ciram]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:aśanayā]]---by hunger; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---you; [[synonyms:p:parītaḥ]]---overcome; [[synonyms:b:bhuṅkṣva]]---please eat; [[synonyms:s:stanam]]---at the breast (of your mother); [[synonyms:p:piba]]---drink; [[synonyms:ś:śucaḥ]]---lamentation; [[synonyms:h:hara]]---just dissipate; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---of us; [[synonyms:s:svakānām]]---your relatives.

===== Translation =====

**My dear son, you have slept a long time. Now please get up. Your playmates are calling you to play. Since you must be very hungry, please get up and suck my breast and dissipate our lamentation.**
