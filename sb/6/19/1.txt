====== SB 6.19.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> vrataṁ puṁsavanaṁ brahman
> bhavatā yad udīritam
> tasya veditum icchāmi
> yena viṣṇuḥ prasīdati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Mahārāja Parīkṣit said; [[synonyms:v:vratam]]---the vow; [[synonyms:p:puṁsavanam]]---called //puṁsavana//; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:b:bhavatā]]---by you; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:u:udīritam]]---was spoken of; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of that; [[synonyms:v:veditum]]---to know; [[synonyms:i:icchāmi]]---I want; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇuḥ]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:p:prasīdati]]---is pleased.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Parīkṣit said: My dear lord, you have already spoken about the puṁsavana vow. Now I want to hear about it in detail, for I understand that by observing this vow one can please the Supreme Lord, Viṣṇu.**
