====== SB 6.19.21 ======

===== Text =====

> etena pūjā-vidhinā
> māsān dvādaśa hāyanam
> nītvāthoparamet sādhvī
> kārtike carame 'hani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etena]]---with this; [[synonyms:p:pūjā]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhinā]]---regulated worship; [[synonyms:m:māsān]] [[synonyms:d:dvādaśa]]---twelve months; [[synonyms:h:hāyanam]]---a year; [[synonyms:n:nītvā]]---after passing; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:u:uparamet]]---should fast; [[synonyms:s:sādhvī]]---the chaste wife; [[synonyms:k:kārtike]]---in Kārttika; [[synonyms:c:carame]] [[synonyms:a:ahani]]---on the final day.

===== Translation =====

**The chaste wife must perform such devotional service continuously for one year. After one year passes, she should fast on the full-moon day in the month of Kārttika [October-November].**
