====== SB 6.19.23 ======

===== Text =====

> āśiṣaḥ śirasādāya
> dvijaiḥ prītaiḥ samīritāḥ
> praṇamya śirasā bhaktyā
> bhuñjīta tad-anujñayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āśiṣaḥ]]---blessings; [[synonyms:ś:śirasā]]---with the head; [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---accepting; [[synonyms:d:dvijaiḥ]]---by the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:p:prītaiḥ]]---who are pleased; [[synonyms:s:samīritāḥ]]---spoken; [[synonyms:p:praṇamya]]---after offering obeisances; [[synonyms:ś:śirasā]]---with the head; [[synonyms:b:bhaktyā]]---with devotion; [[synonyms:b:bhuñjīta]]---he should eat; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:a:anujñayā]]---with their permission.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, he should satisfy the brāhmaṇas. When the satisfied brāhmaṇas bestow their blessings, he should devotedly offer them respectful obeisances with his head, and with their permission he should take prasāda.**
