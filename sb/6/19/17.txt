====== SB 6.19.17 ======

===== Text =====

> patiṁ ca parayā bhaktyā
> mahāpuruṣa-cetasā
> priyais tais tair upanamet
> prema-śīlaḥ svayaṁ patiḥ
> bibhṛyāt sarva-karmāṇi
> patnyā uccāvacāni ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patim]]---the husband; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:parayā]]---supreme; [[synonyms:b:bhaktyā]]---with devotion; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:c:cetasā]]---accepting as the Supreme Person; [[synonyms:p:priyaiḥ]]---dear; [[synonyms:t:taiḥ]] [[synonyms:t:taiḥ]]---by those (offerings); [[synonyms:u:upanamet]]---should worship; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ś:śīlaḥ]]---being affectionate; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---himself; [[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the husband; [[synonyms:b:bibhṛyāt]]---should execute; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:karmāṇi]]---all activities; [[synonyms:p:patnyāḥ]]---of the wife; [[synonyms:u:ucca]]-[[synonyms:a:avacāni]]---high and low; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**Accepting her husband as the representative of the Supreme Person, a wife should worship him with unalloyed devotion by offering him prasāda. The husband, being very pleased with his wife, should engage himself in the affairs of his family.**

===== Purport =====

The family relationship of husband and wife should be established spiritually according to the process mentioned above.
