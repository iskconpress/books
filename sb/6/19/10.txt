====== SB 6.19.10 ======

===== Text =====

> praṇamed daṇḍavad bhūmau
> bhakti-prahveṇa cetasā
> daśa-vāraṁ japen mantraṁ
> tataḥ stotram udīrayet

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:praṇamet]]---should offer obeisances; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like a stick; [[synonyms:b:bhūmau]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---through devotion; [[synonyms:p:prahveṇa]]---humble; [[synonyms:c:cetasā]]---with a mind; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāram]]---ten times; [[synonyms:j:japet]]---should utter; [[synonyms:m:mantram]]---the //mantra//; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---then; [[synonyms:s:stotram]]---prayer; [[synonyms:u:udīrayet]]---should chant.

===== Translation =====

**One should offer obeisances unto the Lord with a mind humbled through devotion. While offering daṇḍavats by falling on the ground like a rod, one should chant the above mantra ten times. Then one should chant the following prayer.**
