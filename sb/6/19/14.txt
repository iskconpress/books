====== SB 6.19.14 ======

===== Text =====

> yathā yuvāṁ tri-lokasya
> varadau parameṣṭhinau
> tathā ma uttamaśloka
> santu satyā mahāśiṣaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]]---since; [[synonyms:y:yuvām]]---both of you; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:l:lokasya]]---of the three worlds; [[synonyms:v:vara]]-[[synonyms:d:dau]]---givers of benedictions; [[synonyms:p:parame]]-[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣṭhinau]]---the supreme rulers; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---therefore; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---O Lord, who are praised with excellent verses; [[synonyms:s:santu]]---may become; [[synonyms:s:satyāḥ]]---fulfilled; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśiṣaḥ]]---great ambitions.

===== Translation =====

**You are both the supreme rulers and benedictors of the three worlds. Therefore, my Lord, Uttamaśloka, may my ambitions be fulfilled by Your grace.**
