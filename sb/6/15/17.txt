====== SB 6.15.17 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-aṅgirā uvāca
> ahaṁ te putra-kāmasya
> putrado 'smy aṅgirā nṛpa
> eṣa brahma-sutaḥ sākṣān
> nārado bhagavān ṛṣiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅgirāḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the great sage Aṅgirā said; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:t:te]]---of you; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:k:kāmasya]]---desiring to have a son; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:d:daḥ]]---the giver of the son; [[synonyms:a:asmi]]---am; [[synonyms:a:aṅgirāḥ]]---Aṅgirā Ṛṣi; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---this; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]]---Nārada Muni; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---sage.

===== Translation =====

**Aṅgirā said: My dear King, when you desired to have a son, I approached you. Indeed, I am the same Aṅgirā Ṛṣi who gave you this son. As for this ṛṣi, he is the great sage Nārada, the direct son of Lord Brahmā**
