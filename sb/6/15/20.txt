====== SB 6.15.20 ======

===== Text =====

> tadaiva te paraṁ jñānaṁ
> dadāmi gṛham āgataḥ
> jñātvānyābhiniveśaṁ te
> putram eva dadāmy aham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:p:param]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:j:jñānam]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:d:dadāmi]]---I would have delivered; [[synonyms:g:gṛham]]---to your home; [[synonyms:ā:āgataḥ]]---came; [[synonyms:j:jñātvā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:a:abhiniveśam]]---absorption in something else (in material things); [[synonyms:t:te]]---your; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---a son; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:d:dadāmi]]---gave; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I.

===== Translation =====

**When I first came to your home, I could have given you the supreme transcendental knowledge, but when I saw that your mind was absorbed in material things, I gave you only a son, who caused you jubilation and lamentation.**
