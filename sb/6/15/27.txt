====== SB 6.15.27 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-nārada uvāca
> etāṁ mantropaniṣadaṁ
> pratīccha prayato mama
> yāṁ dhārayan sapta-rātrād
> draṣṭā saṅkarṣaṇaṁ vibhum

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāradaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Nārada Muni said; [[synonyms:e:etām]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mantra]]-[[synonyms:u:upaniṣadam]]---//Upaniṣad// in the form of a //mantra// by which one can achieve the highest goal of life; [[synonyms:p:pratīccha]]---accept; [[synonyms:p:prayataḥ]]---with great attention (after finishing the funeral ceremony of your dead son); [[synonyms:m:mama]]---from me; [[synonyms:y:yām]]---which; [[synonyms:d:dhārayan]]---accepting; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:r:rātrāt]]---after seven nights; [[synonyms:d:draṣṭā]]---you will see; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:v:vibhum]]---the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**The great sage Nārada continued: My dear King, attentively receive from me a mantra, which is most auspicious. After accepting it from me, in seven nights you will be able to see the Lord face to face.**
