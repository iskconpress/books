====== SB 6.10.30 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛtro 'surāṁs tān anugān manasvī
> pradhāvataḥ prekṣya babhāṣa etat
> palāyitaṁ prekṣya balaṁ ca bhagnaṁ
> bhayena tīvreṇa vihasya vīraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛtraḥ]]---Vṛtrāsura, the commander of the demons; [[synonyms:a:asurān]]---all the demons; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---them; [[synonyms:a:anugān]]---his followers; [[synonyms:m:manasvī]]---the great-minded; [[synonyms:p:pradhāvataḥ]]---fleeing; [[synonyms:p:prekṣya]]---observing; [[synonyms:b:babhāṣa]]---spoke; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:p:palāyitam]]---fleeing; [[synonyms:p:prekṣya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:balam]]---army; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bhagnam]]---broken; [[synonyms:b:bhayena]]---out of fear; [[synonyms:t:tīvreṇa]]---intense; [[synonyms:v:vihasya]]---smiling; [[synonyms:v:vīraḥ]]---the great hero.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing his army broken and all the asuras, even those known as great heroes, fleeing the battlefield out of intense fear, Vṛtrāsura, who was truly a great-minded hero, smiled and spoke the following words.**
