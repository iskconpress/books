====== SB 6.10.3 ======

===== Text =====

> api vṛndārakā yūyaṁ
> na jānītha śarīriṇām
> saṁsthāyāṁ yas tv abhidroho
> duḥsahaś cetanāpahaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:v:vṛndārakāḥ]]---O demigods; [[synonyms:y:yūyam]]---all of you; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:j:jānītha]]---do not know; [[synonyms:ś:śarīriṇām]]---of those who have material bodies; [[synonyms:s:saṁsthāyām]]---at the time of death, or while quitting this body; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---which; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---then; [[synonyms:a:abhidrohaḥ]]---severe pain; [[synonyms:d:duḥsahaḥ]]---unbearable; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---the consciousness; [[synonyms:a:apahaḥ]]---which takes away.

===== Translation =====

**O elevated demigods, at the time of death, severe, unbearable pain takes away the consciousness of all living entities who have accepted material bodies. Don't you know about this pain?**
