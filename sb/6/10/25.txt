====== SB 6.10.25 ======

===== Text =====

> na te śastrāstra-varṣaughā
> hy āseduḥ sura-sainikān
> chinnāḥ siddha-pathe devair
> laghu-hastaiḥ sahasradhā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:te]]---those; [[synonyms:ś:śastra]]-[[synonyms:a:astra]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:o:oghāḥ]]---showers of arrows and other weapons; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ā:āseduḥ]]---reached; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:s:sainikān]]---the armies of the demigods; [[synonyms:c:chinnāḥ]]---cut; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---in the sky; [[synonyms:d:devaiḥ]]---by the demigods; [[synonyms:l:laghu]]-[[synonyms:h:hastaiḥ]]---quick-handed; [[synonyms:s:sahasradhā]]---into thousands of pieces.

===== Translation =====

**The showers of various weapons and arrows released to kill the soldiers of the demigods did not reach them because the demigods, acting quickly, cut the weapons into thousands of pieces in the sky.**
