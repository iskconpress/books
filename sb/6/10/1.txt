====== SB 6.10.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bādarāyaṇir uvāca
> indram evaṁ samādiśya
> bhagavān viśva-bhāvanaḥ
> paśyatām animeṣāṇāṁ
> tatraivāntardadhe hariḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:indram]]---Indra, the heavenly King; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:samādiśya]]---after instructing; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvanaḥ]]---the original cause of all cosmic manifestations; [[synonyms:p:paśyatām]] [[synonyms:a:animeṣāṇām]]---while the demigods were looking on; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---then and there; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:antardadhe]]---disappeared; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After instructing Indra in this way, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari, the cause of the cosmic manifestation, then and there disappeared from the presence of the onlooking demigods.**
