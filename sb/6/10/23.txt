====== SB 6.10.23 ======

===== Text =====

> śūlaiḥ paraśvadhaiḥ khaḍgaiḥ
> śataghnībhir bhuśuṇḍibhiḥ
> sarvato 'vākiran śastrair
> astraiś ca vibudharṣabhān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śūlaiḥ]]---by spears; [[synonyms:p:paraśvadhaiḥ]]---by axes; [[synonyms:k:khaḍgaiḥ]]---by swords; [[synonyms:ś:śataghnībhiḥ]]---by //śataghnīs//; [[synonyms:b:bhuśuṇḍibhiḥ]]---by //bhuśuṇḍis//; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---all around; [[synonyms:a:avākiran]]---scattered; [[synonyms:ś:śastraiḥ]]---with weapons; [[synonyms:a:astraiḥ]]---with arrows; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vibudha]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabhān]]---the chiefs of the demigods.

===== Translation =====

**Armed with lances, tridents, axes, swords and other weapons like śataghnīs and bhuśuṇḍis, the demons attacked from different directions and scattered all the chiefs of the demigod armies.**
