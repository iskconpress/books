====== SB 6.10.17-18 ======

===== Text =====

> rudrair vasubhir ādityair
> aśvibhyāṁ pitṛ-vahnibhiḥ
> marudbhir ṛbhubhiḥ sādhyair
> viśvedevair marut-patim
>
> dṛṣṭvā vajra-dharaṁ śakraṁ
> rocamānaṁ svayā śriyā
> nāmṛṣyann asurā rājan
> mṛdhe vṛtra-puraḥsarāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rudraiḥ]]---by the Rudras; [[synonyms:v:vasubhiḥ]]---by the Vasus; [[synonyms:ā:ādityaiḥ]]---by the Ādityas; [[synonyms:a:aśvibhyām]]---by the Aśvinī-kumāras; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]---by the Pitās; [[synonyms:v:vahnibhiḥ]]---and the Vahnis; [[synonyms:m:marudbhiḥ]]---by the Maruts; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛbhubhiḥ]]---by the Ṛbhus; [[synonyms:s:sādhyaiḥ]]---by the Sādhyas; [[synonyms:v:viśve]]-[[synonyms:d:devaiḥ]]---by the Viśvadevas; [[synonyms:m:marut]]-[[synonyms:p:patim]]---Indra, the heavenly King; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vajra]]-[[synonyms:d:dharam]]---bearing the thunderbolt; [[synonyms:ś:śakram]]---another name of Indra; [[synonyms:r:rocamānam]]---shining; [[synonyms:s:svayā]]---by his own; [[synonyms:ś:śriyā]]---opulence; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:amṛṣyan]]---tolerated; [[synonyms:a:asurāḥ]]---all the demons; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:m:mṛdhe]]---in the fight; [[synonyms:v:vṛtra]]-[[synonyms:p:puraḥsarāḥ]]---headed by Vṛtrāsura.

===== Translation =====

**O King, when all the asuras came onto the battlefield, headed by Vṛtrāsura, they saw King Indra carrying the thunderbolt and surrounded by the Rudras, Vasus, Ādityas, Aśvinī-kumāras, Pitās, Vahnis, Maruts, Ṛbhus, Sādhyas and Viśvadevas. Surrounded by his company, Indra shone so brightly that his effulgence was intolerable to the demons.**
