====== SB 6.10.26 ======

===== Text =====

> atha kṣīṇāstra-śastraughā
> giri-śṛṅga-drumopalaiḥ
> abhyavarṣan sura-balaṁ
> cicchidus tāṁś ca pūrvavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:k:kṣīṇa]]---being reduced; [[synonyms:a:astra]]---of the arrows released by //mantras//; [[synonyms:ś:śastra]]---and weapons; [[synonyms:o:oghāḥ]]---the multitudes; [[synonyms:g:giri]]---of mountains; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṅga]]---with the peaks; [[synonyms:d:druma]]---with trees; [[synonyms:u:upalaiḥ]]---and with stones; [[synonyms:a:abhyavarṣan]]---showered; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:b:balam]]---the soldiers of the demigods; [[synonyms:c:cicchiduḥ]]---broke to pieces; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---them; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as before.

===== Translation =====

**As their weapons and mantras decreased, the demons began showering mountain peaks, trees and stones upon the demigod soldiers, but the demigods were so powerful and expert that they nullified all these weapons by breaking them to pieces in the sky as before.**
