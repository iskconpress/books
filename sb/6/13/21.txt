====== SB 6.13.21 ======

===== Text =====

> sa vājimedhena yathoditena
> vitāyamānena marīci-miśraiḥ
> iṣṭvādhiyajñaṁ puruṣaṁ purāṇam
> indro mahān āsa vidhūta-pāpaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Indra); [[synonyms:v:vājimedhena]]---by the //aśvamedha// sacrifice; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:u:uditena]]---described; [[synonyms:v:vitāyamānena]]---being performed; [[synonyms:m:marīci]]-[[synonyms:m:miśraiḥ]]---by the priests, headed by Marīci; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭvā]]---worshiping; [[synonyms:a:adhiyajñam]]---the Supreme Supersoul; [[synonyms:p:puruṣam]] [[synonyms:p:purāṇam]]---the original Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:m:mahān]]---worshipable; [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vidhūta]]-[[synonyms:p:pāpaḥ]]---being cleansed of all sinful reactions.

===== Translation =====

**King Indra was favored by Marīci and the other great sages. They performed the sacrifice just according to the rules and regulations, worshiping the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the Supersoul, the original person. Thus Indra regained his exalted position and was again honored by everyone.**
