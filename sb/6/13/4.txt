====== SB 6.13.4 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> vṛtra-vikrama-saṁvignāḥ
> sarve devāḥ saharṣibhiḥ
> tad-vadhāyārthayann indraṁ
> naicchad bhīto bṛhad-vadhāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:v:vṛtra]]---of Vṛtrāsura; [[synonyms:v:vikrama]]---by the powerful activities; [[synonyms:s:saṁvignāḥ]]---being full of anxieties; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:d:devāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:s:saha]] [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣibhiḥ]]---with the great sages; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhāya]]---for the killing of him; [[synonyms:ā:ārthayan]]---requested; [[synonyms:i:indram]]---Indra; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:aicchat]]---declined; [[synonyms:b:bhītaḥ]]---being afraid; [[synonyms:b:bṛhat]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhāt]]---due to killing a //brāhmaṇa.//

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī answered: When all the great sages and demigods were disturbed by the extraordinary power of Vṛtrāsura, they had assembled to ask Indra to kill him. Indra, however, being afraid of killing a brāhmaṇa, declined their request.**
