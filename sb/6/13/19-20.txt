====== SB 6.13.19-20 ======

===== Text =====

> athejyamāne puruṣe
> sarva-devamayātmani
> aśvamedhe mahendreṇa
> vitate brahma-vādibhiḥ
>
> sa vai tvāṣṭra-vadho bhūyān
> api pāpa-cayo nṛpa
> nītas tenaiva śūnyāya
> nīhāra iva bhānunā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---therefore; [[synonyms:i:ijyamāne]]---when worshiped; [[synonyms:p:puruṣe]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmani]]---the Supersoul and maintainer of the demigods; [[synonyms:a:aśvamedhe]]---through the //aśvamedha-yajña//; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:i:indreṇa]]---by King Indra; [[synonyms:v:vitate]]---being administered; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:v:vādibhiḥ]]---by the saints and //brāhmaṇas// expert in Vedic knowledge; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:t:tvāṣṭra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhaḥ]]---the killing of Vṛtrāsura, the son of Tvaṣṭā; [[synonyms:b:bhūyāt]]---may be; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:p:pāpacayaḥ]]---mass of sin; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:n:nītaḥ]]---was brought; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---by that (the horse sacrifice); [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ś:śūnyāya]]---to nothing; [[synonyms:n:nīhāraḥ]]---fog; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:b:bhānunā]]---by the brilliant sun.

===== Translation =====

**The horse sacrifice performed by the saintly brāhmaṇas relieved Indra of the reactions to all his sins because he worshiped the Supreme Personality of Godhead in that sacrifice. O King, although he had committed a gravely sinful act, it was nullified at once by that sacrifice, just as fog is vanquished by the brilliant sunrise.**
