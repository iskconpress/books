====== SB 6.13.18 ======

===== Text =====

> taṁ ca brahmarṣayo 'bhyetya
> hayamedhena bhārata
> yathāvad dīkṣayāṁ cakruḥ
> puruṣārādhanena ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Lord Indra); [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣayaḥ]]---the great saints and //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:a:abhyetya]]---approaching; [[synonyms:h:hayamedhena]]---with an //aśvamedha// sacrifice; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:y:yathāvat]]---according to the rules and regulations; [[synonyms:d:dīkṣayām]] [[synonyms:c:cakruḥ]]---initiated; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārādhanena]]---which consists of worship of the Supreme Person, Hari; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**O King, when Lord Indra reached the heavenly planets, the saintly brāhmaṇas approached him and properly initiated him into a horse sacrifice [aśvamedha-yajña] meant to please the Supreme Lord.**
