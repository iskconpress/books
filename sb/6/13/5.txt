====== SB 6.13.5 ======

===== Text =====

> indra uvāca
> strī-bhū-druma-jalair eno
> viśvarūpa-vadhodbhavam
> vibhaktam anugṛhṇadbhir
> vṛtra-hatyāṁ kva mārjmy aham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:indraḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---King Indra replied; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---by women; [[synonyms:b:bhū]]---the earth; [[synonyms:d:druma]]---the trees; [[synonyms:j:jalaiḥ]]---and water; [[synonyms:e:enaḥ]]---this (sin); [[synonyms:v:viśvarūpa]]---of Viśvarūpa; [[synonyms:v:vadha]]---from the killing; [[synonyms:u:udbhavam]]---produced; [[synonyms:v:vibhaktam]]---divided; [[synonyms:a:anugṛhṇadbhiḥ]]---showing their favor (to me); [[synonyms:v:vṛtra]]-[[synonyms:h:hatyām]]---the killing of Vṛtra; [[synonyms:k:kva]]---how; [[synonyms:m:mārjmi]]---shall become free from; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I.

===== Translation =====

**King Indra replied: When I killed Viśvarūpa, I received extensive sinful reactions, but I was favored by the women, land, trees and water, and therefore I was able to divide the sin among them. But now if I kill Vṛtrāsura, another brāhmaṇa, how shall I free myself from the sinful reactions?**
