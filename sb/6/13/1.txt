====== SB 6.13.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> vṛtre hate trayo lokā
> vinā śakreṇa bhūrida
> sapālā hy abhavan sadyo
> vijvarā nirvṛtendriyāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:v:vṛtre]] [[synonyms:h:hate]]---when Vṛtrāsura was killed; [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]] [[synonyms:l:lokāḥ]]---the three planetary systems (upper, middle and lower); [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except; [[synonyms:ś:śakreṇa]]---Indra, who is also called Śakra; [[synonyms:b:bhūri]]-[[synonyms:d:da]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, giver of great charity; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:pālāḥ]]---with the rulers of the various planets; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:abhavan]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sadyaḥ]]---immediately; [[synonyms:v:vijvarāḥ]]---without fear of death; [[synonyms:n:nirvṛta]]---very much pleased; [[synonyms:i:indriyāḥ]]---whose senses.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King Parīkṣit, who are so charitably disposed, when Vṛtrāsura was killed, all the presiding deities and everyone else in the three planetary systems was immediately pleased and free from trouble---everyone, that is, except Indra.**
