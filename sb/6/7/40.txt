====== SB 6.7.40 ======

===== Text =====

> yayā guptaḥ sahasrākṣo
> jigye 'sura-camūr vibhuḥ
> tāṁ prāha sa mahendrāya
> viśvarūpa udāra-dhīḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yayā]]---by which; [[synonyms:g:guptaḥ]]---protected; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣaḥ]]---the thousand-eyed demigod, Indra; [[synonyms:j:jigye]]---conquered; [[synonyms:a:asura]]---of the demons; [[synonyms:c:camūḥ]]---military power; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---becoming very powerful; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---spoke; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:m:mahendrāya]]---unto the King of heaven, Mahendra; [[synonyms:v:viśvarūpaḥ]]---Viśvarūpa; [[synonyms:u:udāra]]-[[synonyms:d:dhīḥ]]---very broad-minded.

===== Translation =====

**Viśvarūpa, who was most liberal, spoke to King Indra [Sahasrākṣa] the secret hymn that protected Indra and conquered the military power of the demons.**
