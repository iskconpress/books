====== SB 6.7.18 ======

===== Text =====

> tac chrutvaivāsurāḥ sarva
> āśrityauśanasaṁ matam
> devān pratyudyamaṁ cakrur
> durmadā ātatāyinaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---hearing that news; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:asurāḥ]]---the demons; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āśritya]]---taking shelter of; [[synonyms:a:auśanasam]]---of Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:m:matam]]---the instruction; [[synonyms:d:devān]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:p:pratyudyamam]]---action against; [[synonyms:c:cakruḥ]]---performed; [[synonyms:d:durmadāḥ]]---not very intelligent; [[synonyms:ā:ātatāyinaḥ]]---equipped with arms for fighting.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing of the pitiable condition of King Indra, the demons, following the instructions of their guru, Śukrācārya, equipped themselves with weapons and declared war against the demigods.**
