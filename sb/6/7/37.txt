====== SB 6.7.37 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi na pratibrūyāṁ
> gurubhiḥ prārthitaṁ kiyat
> bhavatāṁ prārthitaṁ sarvaṁ
> prāṇair arthaiś ca sādhaye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---still; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pratibrūyām]]---I may refuse; [[synonyms:g:gurubhiḥ]]---by persons on the level of my spiritual master; [[synonyms:p:prārthitam]]---request; [[synonyms:k:kiyat]]---of small value; [[synonyms:b:bhavatām]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:p:prārthitam]]---the desire; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---whole; [[synonyms:p:prāṇaiḥ]]---by my life; [[synonyms:a:arthaiḥ]]---by my possessions; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:sādhaye]]---I shall execute.

===== Translation =====

**All of you are my superiors. Therefore although accepting priesthood is sometimes reproachable, I cannot refuse even a small request from you. I agree to be your priest. I shall fulfill your request by dedicating my life and possessions.**
