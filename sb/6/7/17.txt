====== SB 6.7.17 ======

===== Text =====

> guror nādhigataḥ saṁjñāṁ
> parīkṣan bhagavān svarāṭ
> dhyāyan dhiyā surair yuktaḥ
> śarma nālabhatātmanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guroḥ]]---of his spiritual master; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:adhigataḥ]]---finding; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñām]]---trace; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣan]]---searching vigorously all around; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful Indra; [[synonyms:s:svarāṭ]]---independent; [[synonyms:d:dhyāyan]]---meditating; [[synonyms:d:dhiyā]]---by wisdom; [[synonyms:s:suraiḥ]]---by the demigods; [[synonyms:y:yuktaḥ]]---surrounded; [[synonyms:ś:śarma]]---peace; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:alabhata]]---obtained; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---of the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Although Indra searched vigorously with the assistance of the other demigods, he could not find Bṛhaspati. Then Indra thought, "Alas, my spiritual master has become dissatisfied with me, and now I have no means of achieving good fortune." Although Indra was surrounded by demigods, he could not find peace of mind.**
