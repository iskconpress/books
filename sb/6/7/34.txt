====== SB 6.7.34 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-ṛṣir uvāca
> abhyarthitaḥ sura-gaṇaiḥ
> paurahitye mahā-tapāḥ
> sa viśvarūpas tān āha
> prasannaḥ ślakṣṇayā girā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued to speak; [[synonyms:a:abhyarthitaḥ]]---being requested; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇaiḥ]]---by the demigods; [[synonyms:p:paurahitye]]---in accepting the priesthood; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:t:tapāḥ]]---highly advanced in austerity and penances; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:v:viśvarūpaḥ]]---Viśvarūpa; [[synonyms:t:tān]]---to the demigods; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---spoke; [[synonyms:p:prasannaḥ]]---being satisfied; [[synonyms:ś:ślakṣṇayā]]---sweet; [[synonyms:g:girā]]---with words.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: When all the demigods requested the great Viśvarūpa to be their priest, Viśvarūpa, who was advanced in austerities, was very pleased. He replied to them as follows.**
