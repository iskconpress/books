====== SB 6.7.11 ======

===== Text =====

> aho bata mayāsādhu
> kṛtaṁ vai dabhra-buddhinā
> yan mayaiśvarya-mattena
> guruḥ sadasi kātkṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aho]]---alas; [[synonyms:b:bata]]---indeed; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:a:asādhu]]---disrespectful; [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---the action done; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:dabhra]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhinā]]---being of less intelligence; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:m:mattena]]---being very proud of material opulence; [[synonyms:g:guruḥ]]---the spiritual master; [[synonyms:s:sadasi]]---in this assembly; [[synonyms:k:kāt]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtaḥ]]---mistreated.

===== Translation =====

**Alas, what a regrettable deed I have committed because of my lack of intelligence and my pride in my material opulences. I failed to show respect to my spiritual master when he entered this assembly, and thus I have insulted him.**
