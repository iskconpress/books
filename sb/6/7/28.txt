====== SB 6.7.28 ======

===== Text =====

> putrāṇāṁ hi paro dharmaḥ
> pitṛ-śuśrūṣaṇaṁ satām
> api putravatāṁ brahman
> kim uta brahmacāriṇām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:putrāṇām]]---of sons; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:paraḥ]]---superior; [[synonyms:d:dharmaḥ]]---religious principle; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]-[[synonyms:ś:śuśrūṣaṇam]]---the service of the parents; [[synonyms:s:satām]]---good; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:v:vatām]]---of those who have sons; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O dear //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:u:uta]]---what to speak; [[synonyms:b:brahmacāriṇām]]---of //brahmacārīs.//

===== Translation =====

**O brāhmaṇa, the highest duty of a son, even though he has sons of his own, is to serve his parents, and what to speak of a son who is a brahmacārī?**
