====== SB 6.7.10 ======

===== Text =====

> tarhy eva pratibudhyendro
> guru-helanam ātmanaḥ
> garhayām āsa sadasi
> svayam ātmānam ātmanā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tarhi]]---then, immediately; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:pratibudhya]]---realizing; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:h:helanam]]---disrespect to the spiritual master; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---his own; [[synonyms:g:garhayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---reproached; [[synonyms:s:sadasi]]---in that assembly; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---himself; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanā]]---by himself.

===== Translation =====

**Indra, the King of heaven, could immediately understand his mistake. Realizing he had disrespected his spiritual master, he condemned himself in the presence of all the members of the assembly.**
