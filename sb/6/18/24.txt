====== SB 6.18.24 ======

===== Text =====

> kadā nu bhrātṛ-hantāram
> indriyārāmam ulbaṇam
> aklinna-hṛdayaṁ pāpaṁ
> ghātayitvā śaye sukham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kadā]]---when; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛ]]-[[synonyms:h:hantāram]]---the killer of the brothers; [[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārāmam]]---very fond of sense gratification; [[synonyms:u:ulbaṇam]]---cruel; [[synonyms:a:aklinna]]-[[synonyms:h:hṛdayam]]---hard hearted; [[synonyms:p:pāpam]]---sinful; [[synonyms:g:ghātayitvā]]---having caused to be killed; [[synonyms:ś:śaye]]---shall I rest; [[synonyms:s:sukham]]---happily.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Indra, who is very much fond of sense gratification, has killed the two brothers Hiraṇyākṣa and Hiraṇyakaśipu by means of Lord Viṣṇu. Therefore Indra is cruel, hardhearted and sinful. When will I, having killed him, rest with a pacified mind?**
