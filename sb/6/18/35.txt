====== SB 6.18.35 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmāt pati-vratā nāryaḥ
> śreyas-kāmāḥ sumadhyame
> yajante 'nanya-bhāvena
> patim ātmānam īśvaram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---therefore; [[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:v:vratāḥ]]---devoted to the husband; [[synonyms:n:nāryaḥ]]---women; [[synonyms:ś:śreyaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāmāḥ]]---conscientious; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:m:madhyame]]---O thin-waisted woman; [[synonyms:y:yajante]]---worship; [[synonyms:a:ananya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvena]]---with devotion; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---the husband; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---the Supersoul; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaram]]---representative of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**My dear wife, whose body is so beautiful, your waist being thin, a conscientious wife should be chaste and should abide by the orders of her husband. She should very devoutly worship her husband as a representative of Vāsudeva.**
