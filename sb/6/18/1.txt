====== SB 6.18.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> pṛśnis tu patnī savituḥ
> sāvitrīṁ vyāhṛtiṁ trayīm
> agnihotraṁ paśuṁ somaṁ
> cāturmāsyaṁ mahā-makhān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:p:pṛśniḥ]]---Pṛśni; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---then; [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---wife; [[synonyms:s:savituḥ]]---of Savitā; [[synonyms:s:sāvitrīm]]---Sāvitrī; [[synonyms:v:vyāhṛtim]]---Vyāhṛti; [[synonyms:t:trayīm]]---Trayī; [[synonyms:a:agnihotram]]---Agnihotra; [[synonyms:p:paśum]]---Paśu; [[synonyms:s:somam]]---Soma; [[synonyms:c:cāturmāsyam]]---Cāturmāsya; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:makhān]]---the five Mahāyajñas.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Pṛśni, who was the wife of Savitā, the fifth of the twelve sons of Aditi, gave birth to three daughters---Sāvitrī, Vyāhṛti and Trayī---and the sons named Agnihotra, Paśu, Soma, Cāturmāsya and the five Mahāyajñas.**
