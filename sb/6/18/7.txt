====== SB 6.18.7 ======

===== Text =====

> paulomyām indra ādhatta
> trīn putrān iti naḥ śrutam
> jayantam ṛṣabhaṁ tāta
> tṛtīyaṁ mīḍhuṣaṁ prabhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paulomyām]]---in Paulomī (Śacīdevī); [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---Indra; [[synonyms:ā:ādhatta]]---begot; [[synonyms:t:trīn]]---three; [[synonyms:p:putrān]]---sons; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---by us; [[synonyms:ś:śrutam]]---heard; [[synonyms:j:jayantam]]---Jayanta; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabham]]---Ṛṣabha; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---my dear King; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīyam]]---third; [[synonyms:m:mīḍhuṣam]]---Mīḍhuṣa; [[synonyms:p:prabhuḥ]]---the lord.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, Indra, the King of the heavenly planets and eleventh son of Aditi, begot three sons, named Jayanta, Ṛṣabha and Mīḍhuṣa, in the womb of his wife, Paulomī. Thus we have heard.**
