====== SB 6.18.23 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> hata-putrā ditiḥ śakra-
> pārṣṇi-grāheṇa viṣṇunā
> manyunā śoka-dīptena
> jvalantī paryacintayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:h:hata]]-[[synonyms:p:putrā]]---whose sons were killed; [[synonyms:d:ditiḥ]]---Diti; [[synonyms:ś:śakra]]-[[synonyms:p:pārṣṇi]]-[[synonyms:g:grāheṇa]]---who was helping Lord Indra; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇunā]]---by Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:m:manyunā]]---with anger; [[synonyms:ś:śoka]]-[[synonyms:d:dīptena]]---kindled by lamentation; [[synonyms:j:jvalantī]]---burning; [[synonyms:p:paryacintayat]]---thought.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Just to help Indra, Lord Viṣṇu killed the two brothers Hiraṇyākṣa and Hiraṇyakaśipu. Because of their being killed, their mother, Diti, overwhelmed with lamentation and anger, contemplated as follows.**
