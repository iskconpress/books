====== SB 6.18.18 ======

===== Text =====

> bāṇa ārādhya giriśaṁ
> lebhe tad-gaṇa-mukhyatām
> yat-pārśve bhagavān āste
> hy adyāpi pura-pālakaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāṇaḥ]]---Bāṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ārādhya]]---having worshiped; [[synonyms:g:giriśam]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:l:lebhe]]---obtained; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of him (Lord Śiva); [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhyatām]]---the platform of being one of the chief associates; [[synonyms:y:yat]]-[[synonyms:p:pārśve]]---beside whom; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]---remains; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---because of which; [[synonyms:a:adya]]---now; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:p:pura]]-[[synonyms:p:pālakaḥ]]---the protector of the capital.

===== Translation =====

**Since King Bāṇa was a great worshiper of Lord Śiva, he became one of Lord Śiva's most celebrated associates. Even now, Lord Śiva protects King Bāṇa's capital and always stands beside him.**
