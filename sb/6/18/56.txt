====== SB 6.18.56 ======

===== Text =====

> mātṛ-ṣvasur abhiprāyam
> indra ājñāya mānada
> śuśrūṣaṇenāśrama-sthāṁ
> ditiṁ paryacarat kaviḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mātṛ]]-[[synonyms:s:svasuḥ]]---of his mother's sister; [[synonyms:a:abhiprāyam]]---the intention; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---Indra; [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---understanding; [[synonyms:m:māna]]-[[synonyms:d:da]]---O King Parīkṣit, who give respect to everyone; [[synonyms:ś:śuśrūṣaṇena]]---with service; [[synonyms:ā:āśrama]]-[[synonyms:s:sthām]]---residing in an //āśrama//; [[synonyms:d:ditim]]---Diti; [[synonyms:p:paryacarat]]---attended upon; [[synonyms:k:kaviḥ]]---seeing his own interest.

===== Translation =====

**O King, who are respectful to everyone, Indra understood Diti's purpose, and thus he contrived to fulfill his own interests. Following the logic that self-preservation is the first law of nature, he wanted to break Diti's promise. Thus he engaged himself in the service of Diti, his aunt, who was residing in an āśrama.**
