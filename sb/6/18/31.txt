====== SB 6.18.31 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ śuśrūṣitas tāta
> bhagavān kaśyapaḥ striyā
> prahasya parama-prīto
> ditim āhābhinandya ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:ś:śuśrūṣitaḥ]]---being served; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---O dear one; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the powerful; [[synonyms:k:kaśyapaḥ]]---Kaśyapa; [[synonyms:s:striyā]]---by the woman; [[synonyms:p:prahasya]]---smiling; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:p:prītaḥ]]---being very pleased; [[synonyms:d:ditim]]---to Diti; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:a:abhinandya]]---approving; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**O my dear one, the most powerful sage Kaśyapa, being extremely pleased by the mild behavior of his wife Diti, smiled and spoke to her as follows.**
