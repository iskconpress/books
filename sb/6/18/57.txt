====== SB 6.18.57 ======

===== Text =====

> nityaṁ vanāt sumanasaḥ
> phala-mūla-samit-kuśān
> patrāṅkura-mṛdo 'paś ca
> kāle kāla upāharat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityam]]---daily; [[synonyms:v:vanāt]]---from the forest; [[synonyms:s:sumanasaḥ]]---flowers; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---fruits; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---roots; [[synonyms:s:samit]]---wood for the sacrificial fire; [[synonyms:k:kuśān]]---and //kuśa// grass; [[synonyms:p:patra]]---leaves; [[synonyms:a:aṅkura]]---sprouts; [[synonyms:m:mṛdaḥ]]---and earth; [[synonyms:a:apaḥ]]---water; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kāle]] [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the proper time; [[synonyms:u:upāharat]]---brought.

===== Translation =====

**Indra served his aunt daily by bringing flowers, fruits, roots and wood for yajñas from the forest. He also brought kuśa grass, leaves, sprouts, earth and water exactly at the proper time.**
