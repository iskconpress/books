====== SB 6.18.16 ======

===== Text =====

> anuhrādasya sūryāyāṁ
> bāṣkalo mahiṣas tathā
> virocanas tu prāhrādir
> devyāṁ tasyābhavad baliḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anuhrādasya]]---of Anuhlāda; [[synonyms:s:sūryāyām]]---through Sūryā; [[synonyms:b:bāṣkalaḥ]]---Bāṣkala; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣaḥ]]---Mahiṣa; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---also; [[synonyms:v:virocanaḥ]]---Virocana; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:prāhrādiḥ]]---the son of Prahlāda; [[synonyms:d:devyām]]---through his wife; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was; [[synonyms:b:baliḥ]]---Bali.

===== Translation =====

**The wife of Anuhlāda was named Sūryā. She gave birth to two sons, named Bāṣkala and Mahiṣa. Prahlāda had one son, Virocana, whose wife gave birth to Bali Mahārāja.**
