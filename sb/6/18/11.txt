====== SB 6.18.11 ======

===== Text =====

> diter dvāv eva dāyādau
> daitya-dānava-vanditau
> hiraṇyakaśipur nāma
> hiraṇyākṣaś ca kīrtitau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:diteḥ]]---of Diti; [[synonyms:d:dvau]]---two; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:dāyādau]]---sons; [[synonyms:d:daitya]]-[[synonyms:d:dānava]]---by the Daityas and Dānavas; [[synonyms:v:vanditau]]---worshiped; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyakaśipuḥ]]---Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyākṣaḥ]]---Hiraṇyākṣa; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kīrtitau]]---known.

===== Translation =====

**First the two sons named Hiraṇyakaśipu and Hiraṇyākṣa took birth from Diti's womb. Both of them were very powerful and were worshiped by the Daityas and Dānavas.**
