====== SB 6.18.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tat-karma-guṇa-vīryāṇi
> kāśyapasya mahātmanaḥ
> paścād vakṣyāmahe 'dityāṁ
> yathaivāvatatāra ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]---His; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activities; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:v:vīryāṇi]]---and power; [[synonyms:k:kāśyapasya]]---of the son of Kaśyapa; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---the great soul; [[synonyms:p:paścāt]]---later; [[synonyms:v:vakṣyāmahe]]---I shall describe; [[synonyms:a:adityām]]---in Aditi; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---how; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:avatatāra]]---descended; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**Later [in the Eighth Canto of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam] I shall describe how Urukrama, Lord Vāmanadeva, appeared as the son of the great sage Kaśyapa and how He covered the three worlds with three steps. I shall describe the uncommon activities He performed, His qualities, His power and how He took birth from the womb of Aditi.**
