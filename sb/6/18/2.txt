====== SB 6.18.2 ======

===== Text =====

> siddhir bhagasya bhāryāṅga
> mahimānaṁ vibhuṁ prabhum
> āśiṣaṁ ca varārohāṁ
> kanyāṁ prāsūta suvratām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siddhiḥ]]---Siddhi; [[synonyms:b:bhagasya]]---of Bhaga; [[synonyms:b:bhāryā]]---the wife; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---my dear King; [[synonyms:m:mahimānam]]---Mahimā; [[synonyms:v:vibhum]]---Vibhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhum]]---Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āśiṣam]]---Āśī; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:varārohām]]---very beautiful; [[synonyms:k:kanyām]]---daughter; [[synonyms:p:prāsūta]]---bore; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vratām]]---virtuous.

===== Translation =====

**O King, Siddhi, who was the wife of Bhaga, the sixth son of Aditi, bore three sons, named Mahimā, Vibhu and Prabhu, and one extremely beautiful daughter, whose name was Āśī.**
