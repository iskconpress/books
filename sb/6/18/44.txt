====== SB 6.18.44 ======

===== Text =====

> iti sañcintya bhagavān
> mārīcaḥ kurunandana
> uvāca kiñcit kupita
> ātmānaṁ ca vigarhayan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sañcintya]]---thinking; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the powerful; [[synonyms:m:mārīcaḥ]]---Kaśyapa Muni; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---O descendant of Kuru; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---spoke; [[synonyms:k:kiñcit]]---somewhat; [[synonyms:k:kupitaḥ]]---angry; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---himself; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vigarhayan]]---condemning.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Kaśyapa Muni, thinking in this way, became somewhat angry. Condemning himself, O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, descendant of Kuru, he spoke to Diti as follows.**
