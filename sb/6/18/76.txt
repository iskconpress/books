====== SB 6.18.76 ======

===== Text =====

> tad idaṁ mama daurjanyaṁ
> bāliśasya mahīyasi
> kṣantum arhasi mātas tvaṁ
> diṣṭyā garbho mṛtotthitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---of me; [[synonyms:d:daurjanyam]]---evil deed; [[synonyms:b:bāliśasya]]---a fool; [[synonyms:m:mahīyasi]]---O best of women; [[synonyms:k:kṣantum]] [[synonyms:a:arhasi]]---please excuse; [[synonyms:m:mātaḥ]]---O mother; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---you; [[synonyms:d:diṣṭyā]]---by fortune; [[synonyms:g:garbhaḥ]]---the child within the womb; [[synonyms:m:mṛta]]---killed; [[synonyms:u:utthitaḥ]]---became alive.

===== Translation =====

**O my mother, O best of all women, I am a fool. Kindly excuse me for whatever offenses I have committed. Your forty-nine sons have been born unhurt because of your devotional service. As an enemy, I cut them to pieces, but because of your great devotional service they did not die.**
