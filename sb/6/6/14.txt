====== SB 6.6.14 ======

===== Text =====

> skandaś ca kṛttikā-putro
> ye viśākhādayas tataḥ
> doṣasya śarvarī-putraḥ
> śiśumāro hareḥ kalā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:skandaḥ]]---Skanda; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kṛttikā]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son of Kṛttikā; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all of whom; [[synonyms:v:viśākha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---headed by Viśākha; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Skanda); [[synonyms:d:doṣasya]]---of Doṣa; [[synonyms:ś:śarvarī]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son of his wife Śarvarī; [[synonyms:ś:śiśumāraḥ]]---Śiśumāra; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:k:kalā]]---an expansion of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**From Kṛttikā, another wife of Agni, came the son named Skanda, Kārttikeya, whose sons were headed by Viśākha. From the womb of Śarvarī, the wife of the Vasu named Doṣa, came the son named Śiśumāra, who was an expansion of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
