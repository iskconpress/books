====== SB 6.6.9 ======

===== Text =====

> mauhūrtikā deva-gaṇā
> muhūrtāyāś ca jajñire
> ye vai phalaṁ prayacchanti
> bhūtānāṁ sva-sva-kālajam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mauhūrtikāḥ]]---Mauhūrtikas; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇāḥ]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:m:muhūrtāyāḥ]]---from the womb of Muhūrtā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:j:jajñire]]---took birth; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all of whom; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:phalam]]---result; [[synonyms:p:prayacchanti]]---deliver; [[synonyms:b:bhūtānām]]---of the living entities; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:s:sva]]---their own; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:j:jam]]---born of time.

===== Translation =====

**The demigods named the Mauhūrtikas took birth from the womb of Muhūrtā. These demigods deliver the results of actions to the living entities of their respective times.**
