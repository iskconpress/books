====== SB 6.6.8 ======

===== Text =====

> marutvāṁś ca jayantaś ca
> marutvatyā babhūvatuḥ
> jayanto vāsudevāṁśa
> upendra iti yaṁ viduḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:marutvān]]---Marutvān; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:j:jayantaḥ]]---Jayanta; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:marutvatyāḥ]]---from Marutvatī; [[synonyms:b:babhūvatuḥ]]---took birth; [[synonyms:j:jayantaḥ]]---Jayanta; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśaḥ]]---an expansion of Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:u:upendraḥ]]---Upendra; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:y:yam]]---whom; [[synonyms:v:viduḥ]]---they know.

===== Translation =====

**The two sons who took birth from the womb of Marutvatī were Marutvān and Jayanta. Jayanta, who is an expansion of Lord Vāsudeva, is known as Upendra.**
