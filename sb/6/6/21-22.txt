====== SB 6.6.21-22 ======

===== Text =====

> tārkṣyasya vinatā kadrūḥ
> pataṅgī yāminīti ca
> pataṅgy asūta patagān
> yāminī śalabhān atha
>
> suparṇāsūta garuḍaṁ
> sākṣād yajñeśa-vāhanam
> sūrya-sūtam anūruṁ ca
> kadrūr nāgān anekaśaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tārkṣyasya]]---of Kaśyapa, whose other name is Tārkṣya; [[synonyms:v:vinatā]]---Vinatā; [[synonyms:k:kadrūḥ]]---Kadrū; [[synonyms:p:pataṅgī]]---Pataṅgī; [[synonyms:y:yāminī]]---Yāminī; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:pataṅgī]]---Pataṅgī; [[synonyms:a:asūta]]---gave birth; [[synonyms:p:patagān]]---to birds of different varieties; [[synonyms:y:yāminī]]---Yāminī; [[synonyms:ś:śalabhān]]---(gave birth to) locusts; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:suparṇā]]---the wife named Vinatā; [[synonyms:a:asūta]]---gave birth; [[synonyms:g:garuḍam]]---to the celebrated bird known as Garuḍa; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:y:yajñeśa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāhanam]]---the carrier of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:s:sūrya]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtam]]---the chariot driver of the sun-god; [[synonyms:a:anūrum]]---Anūru; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kadrūḥ]]---Kadrū; [[synonyms:n:nāgān]]---serpents; [[synonyms:a:anekaśaḥ]]---in varieties.

===== Translation =====

**Kaśyapa, who is also named Tārkṣya, had four wives---Vinatā [Suparṇā], Kadrū, Pataṅgī and Yāminī. Pataṅgī gave birth to many kinds of birds, and Yāminī gave birth to locusts. Vinatā [Suparṇā] gave birth to Garuḍa, the carrier of Lord Viṣṇu, and to Anūru, or Aruṇa, the chariot driver of the sun-god. Kadrū gave birth to different varieties of serpents.**
