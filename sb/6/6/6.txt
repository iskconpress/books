====== SB 6.6.6 ======

===== Text =====

> kakudaḥ saṅkaṭas tasya
> kīkaṭas tanayo yataḥ
> bhuvo durgāṇi yāmeyaḥ
> svargo nandis tato 'bhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kakudaḥ]]---from the womb of Kakud; [[synonyms:s:saṅkaṭaḥ]]---Saṅkaṭa; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---from him; [[synonyms:k:kīkaṭaḥ]]---Kīkaṭa; [[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---from whom; [[synonyms:b:bhuvaḥ]]---of the earth; [[synonyms:d:durgāṇi]]---many demigods, protectors of this universe (which is called Durgā); [[synonyms:y:yāmeyaḥ]]---of Yāmi; [[synonyms:s:svargaḥ]]---Svarga; [[synonyms:n:nandiḥ]]---Nandi; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Svarga); [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was born.

===== Translation =====

**From the womb of Kakud came the son named Saṅkaṭa, whose son was named Kīkaṭa. From Kīkaṭa came the demigods named Durgā. From Yāmi came the son named Svarga, whose son was named Nandi.**
