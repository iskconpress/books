====== SB 6.6.43 ======

===== Text =====

> pūṣānapatyaḥ piṣṭādo
> bhagna-danto 'bhavat purā
> yo 'sau dakṣāya kupitaṁ
> jahāsa vivṛta-dvijaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūṣā]]---Pūṣā; [[synonyms:a:anapatyaḥ]]---without children; [[synonyms:p:piṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:a:adaḥ]]---who lives by eating flour; [[synonyms:b:bhagna]]-[[synonyms:d:dantaḥ]]---with broken teeth; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---became; [[synonyms:p:purā]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dakṣāya]]---at Dakṣa; [[synonyms:k:kupitam]]---very angry; [[synonyms:j:jahāsa]]---laughed; [[synonyms:v:vivṛta]]-[[synonyms:d:dvijaḥ]]---uncovering his teeth.

===== Translation =====

**Pūṣā had no sons. When Lord Śiva was angry at Dakṣa, Pūṣā had laughed at Lord Śiva and shown his teeth. Therefore he lost his teeth and had to live by eating only ground flour.**
