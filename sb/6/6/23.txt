====== SB 6.6.23 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛttikādīni nakṣatrāṇ-
> īndoḥ patnyas tu bhārata
> dakṣa-śāpāt so 'napatyas
> tāsu yakṣma-grahārditaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛttikā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādīni]]---headed by Kṛttikā; [[synonyms:n:nakṣatrāṇi]]---the constellations; [[synonyms:i:indoḥ]]---of the moon-god; [[synonyms:p:patnyaḥ]]---the wives; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, descendant of the dynasty of Bharata; [[synonyms:d:dakṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāpāt]]---because of being cursed by Dakṣa; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---the moon-god; [[synonyms:a:anapatyaḥ]]---without children; [[synonyms:t:tāsu]]---in so many wives; [[synonyms:y:yakṣma]]-[[synonyms:g:graha]]-[[synonyms:a:arditaḥ]]---being oppressed by a disease that brings about gradual destruction.

===== Translation =====

**O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, best of the Bhāratas, the constellations named Kṛttikā were all wives of the moon-god. However, because Prajāpati Dakṣa had cursed him to suffer from a disease causing gradual destruction, the moon-god could not beget children in any of his wives.**
