====== SB 6.6.16 ======

===== Text =====

> vibhāvasor asūtoṣā
> vyuṣṭaṁ rociṣam ātapam
> pañcayāmo 'tha bhūtāni
> yena jāgrati karmasu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vibhāvasoḥ]]---of Vibhāvasu; [[synonyms:a:asūta]]---gave birth to; [[synonyms:ū:ūṣā]]---named Ūṣā; [[synonyms:v:vyuṣṭam]]---Vyuṣṭa; [[synonyms:r:rociṣam]]---Rociṣa; [[synonyms:ā:ātapam]]---Ātapa; [[synonyms:p:pañcayāmaḥ]]---Pañcayāma; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:b:bhūtāni]]---the living entities; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:j:jāgrati]]---are awakened; [[synonyms:k:karmasu]]---in material activities.

===== Translation =====

**Ūṣā, the wife of Vibhāvasu, gave birth to three sons---Vyuṣṭa, Rociṣa and Ātapa. From Ātapa came Pañcayāma, the span of day, who awakens all living entities to material activities.**
