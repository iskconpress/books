====== SB 6.6.5 ======

===== Text =====

> bhānos tu deva-ṛṣabha
> indrasenas tato nṛpa
> vidyota āsīl lambāyās
> tataś ca stanayitnavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhānoḥ]]---from the womb of Bhānu; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---of course; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabhaḥ]]---Deva-ṛṣabha; [[synonyms:i:indrasenaḥ]]---Indrasena; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Deva-ṛṣabha); [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:v:vidyotaḥ]]---Vidyota; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---appeared; [[synonyms:l:lambāyāḥ]]---from the womb of Lambā; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:stanayitnavaḥ]]---all the clouds.

===== Translation =====

**O King, a son named Deva-ṛṣabha was born from the womb of Bhānu, and from him came a son named Indrasena. From the womb of Lambā came a son named Vidyota, who generated all the clouds.**
