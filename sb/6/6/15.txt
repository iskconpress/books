====== SB 6.6.15 ======

===== Text =====

> vāstor āṅgirasī-putro
> viśvakarmākṛtī-patiḥ
> tato manuś cākṣuṣo 'bhūd
> viśve sādhyā manoḥ sutāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāstoḥ]]---of Vāstu; [[synonyms:ā:āṅgirasī]]---of his wife named Āṅgirasī; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:v:viśvakarmā]]---Viśvakarmā; [[synonyms:ā:ākṛtī]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the husband of Ākṛtī; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from them; [[synonyms:m:manuḥ]] [[synonyms:c:cākṣuṣaḥ]]---the Manu named Cākṣuṣa; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---was born; [[synonyms:v:viśve]]---the Viśvadevas; [[synonyms:s:sādhyāḥ]]---the Sādhyas; [[synonyms:m:manoḥ]]---of Manu; [[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---the sons.

===== Translation =====

**From Āṅgirasī, the wife of the Vasu named Vāstu, was born the great architect Viśvakarmā. Viśvakarmā became the husband of Ākṛtī, from whom the Manu named Cākṣuṣa was born. The sons of Manu were known as the Viśvadevas and Sādhyas.**
