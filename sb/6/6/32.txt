====== SB 6.6.32 ======

===== Text =====

> svarbhānoḥ suprabhāṁ kanyām
> uvāha namuciḥ kila
> vṛṣaparvaṇas tu śarmiṣṭhāṁ
> yayātir nāhuṣo balī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarbhānoḥ]]---of Svarbhānu; [[synonyms:s:suprabhām]]---Suprabhā; [[synonyms:k:kanyām]]---the daughter; [[synonyms:u:uvāha]]---married; [[synonyms:n:namuciḥ]]---Namuci; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vṛṣaparvaṇaḥ]]---of Vṛṣaparvā; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:ś:śarmiṣṭhām]]---Śarmiṣṭhā; [[synonyms:y:yayātiḥ]]---King Yayāti; [[synonyms:n:nāhuṣaḥ]]---the son of Nahuṣa; [[synonyms:b:balī]]---very powerful.

===== Translation =====

**The daughter of Svarbhānu named Suprabhā was married by Namuci. The daughter of Vṛṣaparvā named Śarmiṣṭhā was given to the powerful King Yayāti, the son of Nahuṣa.**
