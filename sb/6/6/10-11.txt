====== SB 6.6.10-11 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkalpāyās tu saṅkalpaḥ
> kāmaḥ saṅkalpajaḥ smṛtaḥ
> vasavo 'ṣṭau vasoḥ putrās
> teṣāṁ nāmāni me śṛṇu
>
> droṇaḥ prāṇo dhruvo 'rko 'gnir
> doṣo vāstur vibhāvasuḥ
> droṇasyābhimateḥ patnyā
> harṣa-śoka-bhayādayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkalpāyāḥ]]---from the womb of Saṅkalpā; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:s:saṅkalpaḥ]]---Saṅkalpa; [[synonyms:k:kāmaḥ]]---Kāma; [[synonyms:s:saṅkalpa]]-[[synonyms:j:jaḥ]]---the son of Saṅkalpā; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---known; [[synonyms:v:vasavaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:aṣṭau]]---the eight Vasus; [[synonyms:v:vasoḥ]]---of Vasu; [[synonyms:p:putrāḥ]]---the sons; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of them; [[synonyms:n:nāmāni]]---the names; [[synonyms:m:me]]---from me; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---just hear; [[synonyms:d:droṇaḥ]]---Droṇa; [[synonyms:p:prāṇaḥ]]---Prāṇa; [[synonyms:d:dhruvaḥ]]---Dhruva; [[synonyms:a:arkaḥ]]---Arka; [[synonyms:a:agniḥ]]---Agni; [[synonyms:d:doṣaḥ]]---Doṣa; [[synonyms:v:vāstuḥ]]---Vāstu; [[synonyms:v:vibhāvasuḥ]]---Vibhāvasu; [[synonyms:d:droṇasya]]---of Droṇa; [[synonyms:a:abhimateḥ]]---from Abhimati; [[synonyms:p:patnyāḥ]]---the wife; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śoka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---the sons named Harṣa, Śoka, Bhaya and so on.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Saṅkalpā was known as Saṅkalpa, and from him lust was born. The sons of Vasu were known as the eight Vasus. Just hear their names from me: Droṇa, Prāṇa, Dhruva, Arka, Agni, Doṣa, Vāstu and Vibhāvasu. From Abhimati, the wife of the Vasu named Droṇa, were generated the sons named Harṣa, Śoka, Bhaya and so on.**
