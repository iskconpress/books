====== SB 6.8.1-2 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> yayā guptaḥ sahasrākṣaḥ
> savāhān ripu-sainikān
> krīḍann iva vinirjitya
> tri-lokyā bubhuje śriyam
>
> bhagavaṁs tan mamākhyāhi
> varma nārāyaṇātmakam
> yathātatāyinaḥ śatrūn
> yena gupto 'jayan mṛdhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---King Parīkṣit said; [[synonyms:y:yayā]]---by which (the spiritual armor); [[synonyms:g:guptaḥ]]---protected; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣaḥ]]---the thousand-eyed King Indra; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāhān]]---with their carriers; [[synonyms:r:ripu]]-[[synonyms:s:sainikān]]---the soldiers and commanders of the enemies; [[synonyms:k:krīḍan]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---just like playing; [[synonyms:v:vinirjitya]]---conquering; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:l:lokyāḥ]]---of the three worlds (the higher, middle and lower planetary systems); [[synonyms:b:bubhuje]]---enjoyed; [[synonyms:ś:śriyam]]---the opulence; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O great sage; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---unto me; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāhi]]---please explain; [[synonyms:v:varma]]---defensive armor made of a //mantra//; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmakam]]---consisting of the mercy of Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---in which way; [[synonyms:ā:ātatāyinaḥ]]---who were endeavoring to kill him; [[synonyms:ś:śatrūn]]---enemies; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:g:guptaḥ]]---being protected; [[synonyms:a:ajayat]]---conquered; [[synonyms:m:mṛdhe]]---in the fight.

===== Translation =====

**King Parīkṣit inquired from Śukadeva Gosvāmī: My lord, kindly explain the Viṣṇu mantra armor that protected King Indra and enabled him to conquer his enemies, along with their carriers, and enjoy the opulence of the three worlds. Please explain to me that Nārāyaṇa armor, by which King Indra achieved success in battle, conquering the enemies who were endeavoring to kill him.**
