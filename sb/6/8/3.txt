====== SB 6.8.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bādarāyaṇir uvāca
> vṛtaḥ purohitas tvāṣṭro
> mahendrāyānupṛcchate
> nārāyaṇākhyaṁ varmāha
> tad ihaika-manāḥ śṛṇu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]]---the chosen; [[synonyms:p:purohitaḥ]]---priest; [[synonyms:t:tvāṣṭraḥ]]---the son of Tvaṣṭā; [[synonyms:m:mahendrāya]]---unto King Indra; [[synonyms:a:anupṛcchate]]---after he (Indra) inquired; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyam]]---named Nārāyaṇa-kavaca; [[synonyms:v:varma]]---defensive armor made of a //mantra//; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---he said; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---this; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:m:manāḥ]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---hear from me.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: King Indra, the leader of the demigods, inquired about the armor known as Nārāyaṇa-kavaca from Viśvarūpa, who was engaged by the demigods as their priest. Please hear Viśvarūpa's reply with great attention.**
