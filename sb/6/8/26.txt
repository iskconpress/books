====== SB 6.8.26 ======

===== Text =====

> tvaṁ tigma-dhārāsi-varāri-sainyam
> īśa-prayukto mama chindhi chindhi
> cakṣūṁṣi carmañ chata-candra chādaya
> dviṣām aghonāṁ hara pāpa-cakṣuṣām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tvam]]---you; [[synonyms:t:tigma]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāra]]-[[synonyms:a:asi]]-[[synonyms:v:vara]]---O best of swords possessing very sharp blades; [[synonyms:a:ari]]-[[synonyms:s:sainyam]]---the soldiers of the enemy; [[synonyms:ī:īśa]]-[[synonyms:p:prayuktaḥ]]---being engaged by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---my; [[synonyms:c:chindhi]] [[synonyms:c:chindhi]]---chop to pieces, chop to pieces; [[synonyms:c:cakṣūṁṣi]]---the eyes; [[synonyms:c:carman]]---O shield; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---possessing brilliant circles like a hundred moons; [[synonyms:c:chādaya]]---please cover; [[synonyms:d:dviṣām]]---of those who are envious of me; [[synonyms:a:aghonām]]---who are completely sinful; [[synonyms:h:hara]]---please take away; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:c:cakṣuṣām]]---of those whose eyes are very sinful.

===== Translation =====

**O king of sharp-edged swords, you are engaged by the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Please cut the soldiers of my enemies to pieces. Please cut them to pieces! O shield marked with a hundred brilliant moonlike circles, please cover the eyes of the sinful enemies. Pluck out their sinful eyes.**
