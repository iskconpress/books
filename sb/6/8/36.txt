====== SB 6.8.36 ======

===== Text =====

> etad dhārayamāṇas tu
> yaṁ yaṁ paśyati cakṣuṣā
> padā vā saṁspṛśet sadyaḥ
> sādhvasāt sa vimucyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:d:dhārayamāṇaḥ]]---a person employing; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:y:yam]] [[synonyms:y:yam]]---whomever; [[synonyms:p:paśyati]]---he sees; [[synonyms:c:cakṣuṣā]]---by his eyes; [[synonyms:p:padā]]---by his feet; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:s:saṁspṛśet]]---may touch; [[synonyms:s:sadyaḥ]]---immediately; [[synonyms:s:sādhvasāt]]---from all fear; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:v:vimucyate]]---is freed.

===== Translation =====

**If one employs this armor, whomever he sees with his eyes or touches with his feet is immediately freed from all the above-mentioned dangers.**
