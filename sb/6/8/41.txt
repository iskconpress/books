====== SB 6.8.41 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> ya idaṁ śṛṇuyāt kāle
> yo dhārayati cādṛtaḥ
> taṁ namasyanti bhūtāni
> mucyate sarvato bhayāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇuyāt]]---may hear; [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at a time of fear; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:d:dhārayati]]---employs this prayer; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ā:ādṛtaḥ]]---with faith and adoration; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him; [[synonyms:n:namasyanti]]---offer respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:b:bhūtāni]]---all living beings; [[synonyms:m:mucyate]]---is released; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---from all; [[synonyms:b:bhayāt]]---fearful conditions.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: My dear Mahārāja Parīkṣit, one who employs this armor or hears about it with faith and veneration when afraid because of any conditions in the material world is immediately freed from all dangers and is worshiped by all living entities.**
