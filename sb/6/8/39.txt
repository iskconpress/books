====== SB 6.8.39 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyopari vimānena
> gandharva-patir ekadā
> yayau citrarathaḥ strībhir
> vṛto yatra dvija-kṣayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his dead body; [[synonyms:u:upari]]---above; [[synonyms:v:vimānena]]---by airplane; [[synonyms:g:gandharva]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the King of Gandharvaloka, Citraratha; [[synonyms:e:ekadā]]---once upon a time; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---went; [[synonyms:c:citrarathaḥ]]---Citraratha; [[synonyms:s:strībhiḥ]]---by many beautiful women; [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]]---surrounded; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---where; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣayaḥ]]---the //brāhmaṇa// Kauśika had died.

===== Translation =====

**Surrounded by many beautiful women, Citraratha, the King of Gandharvaloka, was once passing in his airplane over the brāhmaṇa's body at the spot where the brāhmaṇa had died.**
