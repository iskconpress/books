====== SB 6.8.34 ======

===== Text =====

> vidikṣu dikṣūrdhvam adhaḥ samantād
> antar bahir bhagavān nārasiṁhaḥ
> prahāpayaḹ loka-bhayaṁ svanena
> sva-tejasā grasta-samasta-tejāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidikṣu]]---in all corners; [[synonyms:d:dikṣu]]---in all directions (east, west, north and south); [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhvam]]---above; [[synonyms:a:adhaḥ]]---below; [[synonyms:s:samantāt]]---on all sides; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---internally; [[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]---externally; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nārasiṁhaḥ]]---in the form of Nṛsiṁhadeva (half-lion and half-man); [[synonyms:p:prahāpayan]]---completely destroying; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhayam]]---fear created by animals, poison, weapons, water, air, fire and so on; [[synonyms:s:svanena]]---by His roar or the vibration of His name by His devotee Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:t:tejasā]]---by His personal effulgence; [[synonyms:g:grasta]]---covered; [[synonyms:s:samasta]]---all other; [[synonyms:t:tejāḥ]]---influences.

===== Translation =====

**Prahlāda Mahārāja loudly chanted the holy name of Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva. May Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva, roaring for His devotee Prahlāda Mahārāja, protect us from all fear of dangers created by stalwart leaders in all directions through poison, weapons, water, fire, air and so on. May the Lord cover their influence by His own transcendental influence. May Nṛsiṁhadeva protect us in all directions and in all corners, above, below, within and without.**
