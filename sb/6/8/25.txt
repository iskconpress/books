====== SB 6.8.25 ======

===== Text =====

> tvaṁ yātudhāna-pramatha-preta-mātṛ-
> piśāca-vipragraha-ghora-dṛṣṭīn
> darendra vidrāvaya kṛṣṇa-pūrito
> bhīma-svano 'rer hṛdayāni kampayan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tvam]]---you; [[synonyms:y:yātudhāna]]---Rākṣasas; [[synonyms:p:pramatha]]---Pramathas; [[synonyms:p:preta]]---Pretas; [[synonyms:m:mātṛ]]---Mātās; [[synonyms:p:piśāca]]---Piśācas; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:g:graha]]---//brāhmaṇa// ghosts; [[synonyms:g:ghora]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭīn]]---who have very fearful eyes; [[synonyms:d:darendra]]---O Pāñcajanya, the conchshell in the hands of the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vidrāvaya]]---drive away; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pūritaḥ]]---being filled with air from the mouth of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhīma]]-[[synonyms:s:svanaḥ]]---sounding extremely fearful; [[synonyms:a:areḥ]]---of the enemy; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayāni]]---the cores of the hearts; [[synonyms:k:kampayan]]---causing to tremble.

===== Translation =====

**O best of conchshells, O Pāñcajanya in the hands of the Lord, you are always filled with the breath of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Therefore you create a fearful sound vibration that causes trembling in the hearts of enemies like the Rākṣasas, pramatha ghosts, Pretas, Mātās, Piśācas and brāhmaṇa ghosts with fearful eyes.**
