====== SB 6.8.22 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvatsa-dhāmāpara-rātra īśaḥ
> pratyūṣa īśo 'si-dharo janārdanaḥ
> dāmodaro 'vyād anusandhyaṁ prabhāte
> viśveśvaro bhagavān kāla-mūrtiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvatsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāmā]]---the Lord, on whose chest the mark of Śrīvatsa is resting; [[synonyms:a:apara]]-[[synonyms:r:rātre]]---in the fourth part of the night; [[synonyms:ī:īśaḥ]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:p:pratyūṣe]]---in the end of the night; [[synonyms:ī:īśaḥ]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:a:asi]]-[[synonyms:d:dharaḥ]]---carrying a sword in the hand; [[synonyms:j:janārdanaḥ]]---Lord Janārdana; [[synonyms:d:dāmodaraḥ]]---Lord Dāmodara; [[synonyms:a:avyāt]]---may He protect; [[synonyms:a:anusandhyam]]---during each junction or twilight; [[synonyms:p:prabhāte]]---in the early morning (the sixth part of the night); [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the Lord of the whole universe; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrtiḥ]]---the personification of time.

===== Translation =====

**May the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who bears the Śrīvatsa on His chest, protect me after midnight until the sky becomes pinkish. May Lord Janārdana, who carries a sword in His hand, protect me at the end of night [during the last four ghaṭikās of night]. May Lord Dāmodara protect me in the early morning, and may Lord Viśveśvara protect me during the junctions of day and night.**
