====== SB 6.1.3 ======

===== Text =====

> adharma-lakṣaṇā nānā
> narakāś cānuvarṇitāḥ
> manvantaraś ca vyākhyāta
> ādyaḥ svāyambhuvo yataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adharma]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇāḥ]]---symptomized by impious activities; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:n:narakāḥ]]---hells; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:anuvarṇitāḥ]]---have been described; [[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:a:antaraḥ]]---the change of Manus [in one day of Brahmā there are fourteen Manus]; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyātaḥ]]---has been described; [[synonyms:ā:ādyaḥ]]---the original; [[synonyms:s:svāyambhuvaḥ]]---directly the son of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---wherein.

===== Translation =====

**You have also described [at the end of the Fifth Canto] the varieties of hellish life that result from impious activities, and you have described [in the Fourth Canto] the first manvantara, which was presided over by Svāyambhuva Manu, the son of Lord Brahmā.**
