====== SB 9.23.28 ======

===== Text =====

> jayadhvajāt tālajaṅghas
> tasya putra-śataṁ tv abhūt
> kṣatraṁ yat tālajaṅghākhyam
> aurva-tejopasaṁhṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayadhvajāt]]---of Jayadhvaja; [[synonyms:t:tālajaṅghaḥ]]---a son named Tālajaṅgha; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Tālajaṅgha); [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śatam]]---one hundred sons; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---were born; [[synonyms:k:kṣatram]]---a dynasty of //kṣatriyas//; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:t:tālajaṅgha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyam]]---were known as the Tālajaṅghas; [[synonyms:a:aurva]]-[[synonyms:t:tejaḥ]]---being very powerful; [[synonyms:u:upasaṁhṛtam]]---were killed by Mahārāja Sagara.

===== Translation =====

**Jayadhvaja had a son named Tālajaṅgha, who had one hundred sons. All the kṣatriyas in that dynasty, known as Tālajaṅgha, were annihilated by the great power received by Mahārāja Sagara from Aurva Ṛṣi.**
