====== SB 9.23.11 ======

===== Text =====

> bṛhadratho bṛhatkarmā
> bṛhadbhānuś ca tat-sutāḥ
> ādyād bṛhanmanās tasmāj
> jayadratha udāhṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bṛhadrathaḥ]]---Bṛhadratha; [[synonyms:b:bṛhatkarmā]]---Bṛhatkarmā; [[synonyms:b:bṛhadbhānuḥ]]---Bṛhadbhānu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---the sons of Pṛthulākṣa; [[synonyms:ā:ādyāt]]---from the eldest (Bṛhadratha); [[synonyms:b:bṛhanmanāḥ]]---Bṛhanmanā was born; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Bṛhanmanā); [[synonyms:j:jayadrathaḥ]]---a son named Jayadratha; [[synonyms:u:udāhṛtaḥ]]---celebrated as his son.

===== Translation =====

**The sons of Pṛthulākṣa were Bṛhadratha, Bṛhatkarmā and Bṛhadbhānu. From the eldest, Bṛhadratha, came a son named Bṛhanmanā, and from Bṛhanmanā came a son named Jayadratha.**
