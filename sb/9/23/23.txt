====== SB 9.23.23 ======

===== Text =====

> durmado bhadrasenasya
> dhanakaḥ kṛtavīryasūḥ
> kṛtāgniḥ kṛtavarmā ca
> kṛtaujā dhanakātmajāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:durmadaḥ]]---Durmada; [[synonyms:b:bhadrasenasya]]---of Bhadrasena; [[synonyms:d:dhanakaḥ]]---Dhanaka; [[synonyms:k:kṛtavīrya]]-[[synonyms:s:sūḥ]]---giving birth to Kṛtavīrya; [[synonyms:k:kṛtāgniḥ]]---by the name Kṛtāgni; [[synonyms:k:kṛtavarmā]]---Kṛtavarmā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kṛtaujāḥ]]---Kṛtaujā; [[synonyms:d:dhanaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajāḥ]]---sons of Dhanaka.

===== Translation =====

**The sons of Bhadrasena were known as Durmada and Dhanaka. Dhanaka was the father of Kṛtavīrya and also of Kṛtāgni, Kṛtavarmā and Kṛtaujā.**
