====== SB 9.23.14 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛṣasenaḥ sutas tasya
> karṇasya jagatīpate
> druhyoś ca tanayo babhruḥ
> setus tasyātmajas tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛṣasenaḥ]]---Vṛṣasena; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:t:tasya]] [[synonyms:k:karṇasya]]---of that same Karṇa; [[synonyms:j:jagatī]] [[synonyms:p:pate]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:d:druhyoḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---of Druhyu, the third son of Yayāti; [[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:b:babhruḥ]]---Babhru; [[synonyms:s:setuḥ]]---Setu; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Babhru); [[synonyms:ā:ātmajaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---a son thereafter.

===== Translation =====

**O King, the only son of Karṇa was Vṛṣasena. Druhyu, the third son of Yayāti, had a son named Babhru, and the son of Babhru was known as Setu.**
