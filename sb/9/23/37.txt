====== SB 9.23.37 ======

===== Text =====

> ahaṁ bandhyāsapatnī ca
> snuṣā me yujyate katham
> janayiṣyasi yaṁ rājñi
> tasyeyam upayujyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aham]]---I am; [[synonyms:b:bandhyā]]---sterile; [[synonyms:a:asa]]-[[synonyms:p:patnī]]---I have no co-wife; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:snuṣā]]---daughter-in-law; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:y:yujyate]]---could be; [[synonyms:k:katham]]---how; [[synonyms:j:janayiṣyasi]]---you will give birth to; [[synonyms:y:yam]]---which son; [[synonyms:r:rājñi]]---O my dear Queen; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---for him; [[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this girl; [[synonyms:u:upayujyate]]---will be very suitable.

===== Translation =====

**Śaibyā said, "I am sterile and have no co-wife. How can this girl be my daughter-in-law? Please tell me." Jyāmagha replied, "My dear Queen, I shall see that you indeed have a son and that this girl will be your daughter-in-law."**
