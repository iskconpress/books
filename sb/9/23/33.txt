====== SB 9.23.33 ======

===== Text =====

> teṣāṁ tu ṣaṭ pradhānānāṁ
> pṛthuśravasa ātmajaḥ
> dharmo nāmośanā tasya
> hayamedha-śatasya yāṭ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---out of so many sons; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]] [[synonyms:p:pradhānānām]]---of whom there were six foremost sons; [[synonyms:p:pṛthuśravasaḥ]]---of Pṛthuśravā; [[synonyms:ā:ātmajaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:d:dharmaḥ]]---Dharma; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by the name; [[synonyms:u:uśanā]]---Uśanā; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:h:hayamedha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śatasya]]---of one hundred //aśvamedha// sacrifices; [[synonyms:y:yāṭ]]---he was the performer.

===== Translation =====

**Among these many sons, six were the foremost, such as Pṛthuśravā and Pṛthukīrti. The son of Pṛthuśravā was known as Dharma, and his son was known as Uśanā. Uśanā was the performer of one hundred horse sacrifices.**
