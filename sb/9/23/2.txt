====== SB 9.23.2 ======

===== Text =====

> janamejayas tasya putro
> mahāśālo mahāmanāḥ
> uśīnaras titikṣuś ca
> mahāmanasa ātmajau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:janamejayaḥ]]---Janamejaya; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Janamejaya); [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:m:mahāśālaḥ]]---Mahāśāla; [[synonyms:m:mahāmanāḥ]]---(from Mahāśāla) a son named Mahāmanā; [[synonyms:u:uśīnaraḥ]]---Uśīnara; [[synonyms:t:titikṣuḥ]]---Titikṣu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mahāmanasaḥ]]---from Mahāmanā; [[synonyms:ā:ātmajau]]---two sons.

===== Translation =====

**From Sṛñjaya came a son named Janamejaya. From Janamejaya came Mahāśāla; from Mahāśāla, Mahāmanā; and from Mahāmanā two sons, named Uśīnara and Titikṣu.**
