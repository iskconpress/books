====== SB 9.23.25 ======

===== Text =====

> na nūnaṁ kārtavīryasya
> gatiṁ yāsyanti pārthivāḥ
> yajña-dāna-tapo-yogaiḥ
> śruta-vīrya-dayādibhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:n:nūnam]]---indeed; [[synonyms:k:kārtavīryasya]]---of Emperor Kārtavīrya; [[synonyms:g:gatim]]---the activities; [[synonyms:y:yāsyanti]]---could understand or achieve; [[synonyms:p:pārthivāḥ]]---everyone on the earth; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]---sacrifices; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---charity; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerities; [[synonyms:y:yogaiḥ]]---mystic powers; [[synonyms:ś:śruta]]---education; [[synonyms:v:vīrya]]---strength; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---by all these qualities.

===== Translation =====

**No other king in this world could equal Kārtavīryārjuna in sacrifices, charity, austerity, mystic power, education, strength or mercy.**
