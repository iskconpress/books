====== SB 9.23.12 ======

===== Text =====

> vijayas tasya sambhūtyāṁ
> tato dhṛtir ajāyata
> tato dhṛtavratas tasya
> satkarmādhirathas tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vijayaḥ]]---Vijaya; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Jayadratha); [[synonyms:s:sambhūtyām]]---in the womb of the wife; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter (from Vijaya); [[synonyms:d:dhṛtiḥ]]---Dhṛti; [[synonyms:a:ajāyata]]---took birth; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Dhṛti); [[synonyms:d:dhṛtavrataḥ]]---a son named Dhṛtavrata; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Dhṛtavrata); [[synonyms:s:satkarmā]]---Satkarmā; [[synonyms:a:adhirathaḥ]]---Adhiratha; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Satkarmā).

===== Translation =====

**The son of Jayadratha, by the womb of his wife Sambhūti, was Vijaya, and from Vijaya, Dhṛti was born. From Dhṛti came Dhṛtavrata; from Dhṛtavrata, Satkarmā; and from Satkarmā, Adhiratha.**
