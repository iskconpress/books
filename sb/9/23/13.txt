====== SB 9.23.13 ======

===== Text =====

> yo 'sau gaṅgā-taṭe krīḍan
> mañjūṣāntargataṁ śiśum
> kuntyāpaviddhaṁ kānīnam
> anapatyo 'karot sutam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:asau]]---one who (Adhiratha); [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:taṭe]]---on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:k:krīḍan]]---while playing; [[synonyms:m:mañjūṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:antaḥgatam]]---packed in a basket; [[synonyms:ś:śiśum]]---a baby was found; [[synonyms:k:kuntyā]] [[synonyms:a:apaviddham]]---this baby had been abandoned by Kuntī; [[synonyms:k:kānīnam]]---because the baby was born during her maiden state, before her marriage; [[synonyms:a:anapatyaḥ]]---this Adhiratha, being sonless; [[synonyms:a:akarot]]---accepted the baby; [[synonyms:s:sutam]]---as his son.

===== Translation =====

**While playing on the bank of the Ganges, Adhiratha found a baby wrapped up in a basket. The baby had been left by Kuntī because he was born before she was married. Because Adhiratha had no sons, he raised this baby as his own. [This son was later known as Karṇa.]**
