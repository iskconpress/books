====== SB 9.21.28-29 ======

===== Text =====

> supārśvāt sumatis tasya
> putraḥ sannatimāṁs tataḥ
> kṛtī hiraṇyanābhād yo
> yogaṁ prāpya jagau sma ṣaṭ
>
> saṁhitāḥ prācyasāmnāṁ vai
> nīpo hy udgrāyudhas tataḥ
> tasya kṣemyaḥ suvīro 'tha
> suvīrasya ripuñjayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:supārśvāt]]---from Supārśva; [[synonyms:s:sumatiḥ]]---a son named Sumati; [[synonyms:t:tasya]] [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---his son (Sumati's son); [[synonyms:s:sannatimān]]---Sannatimān; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:k:kṛtī]]---a son named Kṛtī; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyanābhāt]]---from Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---he who; [[synonyms:y:yogam]]---mystic power; [[synonyms:p:prāpya]]---getting; [[synonyms:j:jagau]]---taught; [[synonyms:s:sma]]---in the past; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]---six; [[synonyms:s:saṁhitāḥ]]---descriptions; [[synonyms:p:prācyasāmnām]]---of the Prācyasāma verses of the //Sāma Veda//; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:n:nīpaḥ]]---Nīpa; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:u:udgrāyudhaḥ]]---Udgrāyudha; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:k:kṣemyaḥ]]---Kṣemya; [[synonyms:s:suvīraḥ]]---Suvīra; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:suvīrasya]]---of Suvīra; [[synonyms:r:ripuñjayaḥ]]---a son named Ripuñjaya.

===== Translation =====

**From Supārśva came a son named Sumati, from Sumati came Sannatimān, and from Sannatimān came Kṛtī, who achieved mystic power from Brahmā and taught six saṁhitās of the Prācyasāma verses of the Sāma Veda. The son of Kṛtī was Nīpa; the son of Nīpa, Udgrāyudha; the son of Udgrāyudha, Kṣemya; the son of Kṣemya, Suvīra; and the son of Suvīra, Ripuñjaya.**
