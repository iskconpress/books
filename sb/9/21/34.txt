====== SB 9.21.34 ======

===== Text =====

> mithunaṁ mudgalād bhārmyād
> divodāsaḥ pumān abhūt
> ahalyā kanyakā yasyāṁ
> śatānandas tu gautamāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mithunam]]---twins, one male and one female; [[synonyms:m:mudgalāt]]---from Mudgala; [[synonyms:b:bhārmyāt]]---the son of Bharmyāśva; [[synonyms:d:divodāsaḥ]]---Divodāsa; [[synonyms:p:pumān]]---the male one; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---generated; [[synonyms:a:ahalyā]]---Ahalyā; [[synonyms:k:kanyakā]]---the female; [[synonyms:y:yasyām]]---through whom; [[synonyms:ś:śatānandaḥ]]---Śatānanda; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:g:gautamāt]]---generated by her husband, Gautama.

===== Translation =====

**Mudgala, the son of Bharmyāśva, had twin children, one male and the other female. The male child was named Divodāsa, and the female child was named Ahalyā. From the womb of Ahalyā by the semen of her husband, Gautama, came a son named Śatānanda.**
