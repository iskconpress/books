====== SB 9.21.10 ======

===== Text =====

> pānīya-mātram uccheṣaṁ
> tac caika-paritarpaṇam
> pāsyataḥ pulkaso 'bhyāgād
> apo dehy aśubhāya me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pānīya]]-[[synonyms:m:mātram]]---only the drinking water; [[synonyms:u:uccheṣam]]---what remained of the food; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---that also; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---for one; [[synonyms:p:paritarpaṇam]]---satisfying; [[synonyms:p:pāsyataḥ]]---when the King was about to drink; [[synonyms:p:pulkasaḥ]]---a //caṇḍāla//; [[synonyms:a:abhyāgāt]]---came there; [[synonyms:a:apaḥ]]---water; [[synonyms:d:dehi]]---please give; [[synonyms:a:aśubhāya]]---although I am a lowborn //caṇḍāla//; [[synonyms:m:me]]---to me.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, only the drinking water remained, and there was only enough to satisfy one person, but when the King was just about to drink it, a caṇḍāla appeared and said, "O King, although I am lowborn, kindly give me some drinking water."**
