====== SB 9.21.2 ======

===== Text =====

> guruś ca rantidevaś ca
> saṅkṛteḥ pāṇḍu-nandana
> rantidevasya mahimā
> ihāmutra ca gīyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guruḥ]]---a son named Guru; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rantidevaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and a son named Rantideva; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṛteḥ]]---from Saṅkṛti; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍu]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, descendant of Pāṇḍu; [[synonyms:r:rantidevasya]]---of Rantideva; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---in this world; [[synonyms:a:amutra]]---and in the next world; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:g:gīyate]]---are glorified.

===== Translation =====

**O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, descendant of Pāṇḍu, Saṅkṛti had two sons, named Guru and Rantideva. Rantideva is famous in both this world and the next, for he is glorified not only in human society but also in the society of the demigods.**
