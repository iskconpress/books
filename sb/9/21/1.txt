====== SB 9.21.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> vitathasya sutān manyor
> bṛhatkṣatro jayas tataḥ
> mahāvīryo naro gargaḥ
> saṅkṛtis tu narātmajaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:v:vitathasya]]---of Vitatha (Bharadvāja), who was accepted in the family of Mahārāja Bharata under special circumstances of disappointment; [[synonyms:s:sutāt]]---from the son; [[synonyms:m:manyoḥ]]---named Manyu; [[synonyms:b:bṛhatkṣatraḥ]]---Bṛhatkṣatra; [[synonyms:j:jayaḥ]]---Jaya; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:m:mahāvīryaḥ]]---Mahāvīrya; [[synonyms:n:naraḥ]]---Nara; [[synonyms:g:gargaḥ]]---Garga; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṛtiḥ]]---Saṅkṛti; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:nara]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajaḥ]]---the son of Nara.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Because Bharadvāja was delivered by the Marut demigods, he was known as Vitatha. The son of Vitatha was Manyu, and from Manyu came five sons-Bṛhatkṣatra, Jaya, Mahāvīrya, Nara and Garga. Of these five, the one known as Nara had a son named Saṅkṛti.**
