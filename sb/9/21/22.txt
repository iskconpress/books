====== SB 9.21.22 ======

===== Text =====

> ajamīḍhād bṛhadiṣus
> tasya putro bṛhaddhanuḥ
> bṛhatkāyas tatas tasya
> putra āsīj jayadrathaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ajamīḍhāt]]---from Ajamīḍha; [[synonyms:b:bṛhadiṣuḥ]]---a son named Bṛhadiṣu; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:b:bṛhaddhanuḥ]]---Bṛhaddhanu; [[synonyms:b:bṛhatkāyaḥ]]---Bṛhatkāya; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---was; [[synonyms:j:jayadrathaḥ]]---Jayadratha.

===== Translation =====

**From Ajamīḍha came a son named Bṛhadiṣu, from Bṛhadiṣu came a son named Bṛhaddhanu, from Bṛhaddhanu a son named Bṛhatkāya, and from Bṛhatkāya a son named Jayadratha.**
