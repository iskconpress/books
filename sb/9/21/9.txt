====== SB 9.21.9 ======

===== Text =====

> sa ādṛtyāvaśiṣṭaṁ yad
> bahu-māna-puraskṛtam
> tac ca dattvā namaścakre
> śvabhyaḥ śva-pataye vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (King Rantideva); [[synonyms:ā:ādṛtya]]---after honoring them; [[synonyms:a:avaśiṣṭam]]---the food that remained after the //brāhmaṇa// and //śūdra// were fed; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever there was; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:māna]]-[[synonyms:p:puraskṛtam]]---offering him much respect; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:dattvā]]---giving away; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]-[[synonyms:c:cakre]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:ś:śvabhyaḥ]]---unto the dogs; [[synonyms:ś:śva]]-[[synonyms:p:pataye]]---unto the master of the dogs; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the all-powerful King.

===== Translation =====

**With great respect, King Rantideva offered the balance of the food to the dogs and the master of the dogs, who had come as guests. The King offered them all respects and obeisances.**
