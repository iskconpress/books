====== SB 9.21.19-20 ======

===== Text =====

> gargāc chinis tato gārgyaḥ
> kṣatrād brahma hy avartata
> duritakṣayo mahāvīryāt
> tasya trayyāruṇiḥ kaviḥ
>
> puṣkarāruṇir ity atra
> ye brāhmaṇa-gatiṁ gatāḥ
> bṛhatkṣatrasya putro 'bhūd
> dhastī yad-dhastināpuram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gargāt]]---from Garga (another grandson of Bharadvāja); [[synonyms:ś:śiniḥ]]---a son named Śini; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Śini); [[synonyms:g:gārgyaḥ]]---a son named Gārgya; [[synonyms:k:kṣatrāt]]---although he was a //kṣatriya//; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---in deed; [[synonyms:a:avartata]]---became possible; [[synonyms:d:duritakṣayaḥ]]---a son named Duritakṣaya; [[synonyms:m:mahāvīryāt]]---from Mahāvīrya (another grandson of Bharadvāja); [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:t:trayyāruṇiḥ]]---the son named Trayyāruṇi; [[synonyms:k:kaviḥ]]---a son named Kavi; [[synonyms:p:puṣkarāruṇiḥ]]---a son named Puṣkarāruṇi; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:atra]]---therein; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gatim]]---the position of //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:g:gatāḥ]]---achieved; [[synonyms:b:bṛhatkṣatrasya]]---of the grandson of Bharadvāja named Bṛhatkṣatra; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became; [[synonyms:h:hastī]]---Hastī; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---from whom; [[synonyms:h:hastināpuram]]---the city of Hastināpura (New Delhi) was established.

===== Translation =====

**From Garga came a son named Śini, and his son was Gārgya. Although Gārgya was a kṣatriya, there came from him a generation of brahmaṇas. From Mahāvīrya came a son named Duritakṣaya, whose sons were Trayyāruṇi, Kavi and Puṣkarāruṇi. Although these sons of Duritakṣaya took birth in a dynasty of kṣatriyas, they too attained the position of brāhmaṇas. Bṛhatkṣatra had a son named Hastī, who established the city of Hastināpura [now New Delhi].**
