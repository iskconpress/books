====== SB 9.21.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tato bahuratho nāma
> purumīḍho 'prajo 'bhavat
> nalinyām ajamīḍhasya
> nīlaḥ śāntis tu tat-sutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Ripuñjaya); [[synonyms:b:bahurathaḥ]]---Bahuratha; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:p:purumīḍhaḥ]]---Purumīḍha, the younger brother of Dvimīḍha; [[synonyms:a:aprajaḥ]]---sonless; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---became; [[synonyms:n:nalinyām]]---through Nalinī; [[synonyms:a:ajamīḍhasya]]---of Ajamīḍha; [[synonyms:n:nīlaḥ]]---Nīla; [[synonyms:ś:śāntiḥ]]---Śānti; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---then; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Nīla.

===== Translation =====

**From Ripuñjaya came a son named Bahuratha. Purumīḍha was sonless. Ajamīḍha had a son named Nīla by his wife known as Nalinī, and the son of Nīla was Śānti.**
