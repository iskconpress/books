====== SB 9.21.8 ======

===== Text =====

> yāte śūdre tam anyo 'gād
> atithiḥ śvabhir āvṛtaḥ
> rājan me dīyatām annaṁ
> sagaṇāya bubhukṣate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāte]]---when he went away; [[synonyms:ś:śūdre]]---the //śūdra// guest; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:a:anyaḥ]]---another; [[synonyms:a:agāt]]---arrived there; [[synonyms:a:atithiḥ]]---guest; [[synonyms:ś:śvabhiḥ]] [[synonyms:ā:āvṛtaḥ]]---accompanied by dogs; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:m:me]]---unto me; [[synonyms:d:dīyatām]]---deliver; [[synonyms:a:annam]]---eatables; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇāya]]---with my company of dogs; [[synonyms:b:bubhukṣate]]---hankering for food.

===== Translation =====

**When the śūdra went away, another guest arrived, surrounded by dogs, and said, "O King, I and my company of dogs are very hungry. Please give us something to eat."**
