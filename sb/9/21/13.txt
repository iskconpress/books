====== SB 9.21.13 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣut-tṛṭ-śramo gātra-paribhramaś ca
> dainyaṁ klamaḥ śoka-viṣāda-mohāḥ
> sarve nivṛttāḥ kṛpaṇasya jantor
> jijīviṣor jīva-jalārpaṇān me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣut]]---from hunger; [[synonyms:t:tṛṭ]]---and thirst; [[synonyms:ś:śramaḥ]]---fatigue; [[synonyms:g:gātra]]-[[synonyms:p:paribhramaḥ]]---trembling of the body; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:dainyam]]---poverty; [[synonyms:k:klamaḥ]]---distress; [[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---lamentation; [[synonyms:v:viṣāda]]---moroseness; [[synonyms:m:mohāḥ]]---and bewilderment; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:n:nivṛttāḥ]]---finished; [[synonyms:k:kṛpaṇasya]]---of the poor; [[synonyms:j:jantoḥ]]---living entity (the //caṇḍāla//); [[synonyms:j:jijīviṣoḥ]]---desiring to live; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---maintaining life; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:a:arpaṇāt]]---by delivering; [[synonyms:m:me]]---mine.

===== Translation =====

**By offering my water to maintain the life of this poor caṇḍāla, who is struggling to live, I have been freed from all hunger, thirst, fatigue, trembling of the body, moroseness, distress, lamentation and illusion.**
