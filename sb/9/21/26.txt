====== SB 9.21.26 ======

===== Text =====

> jaigīṣavyopadeśena
> yoga-tantraṁ cakāra ha
> udaksenas tatas tasmād
> bhallāṭo bārhadīṣavāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaigīṣavya]]---of the great //ṛṣi// named Jaigīṣavya; [[synonyms:u:upadeśena]]---by the instruction; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:t:tantram]]---an elaborate description of the mystic //yoga// system; [[synonyms:c:cakāra]]---compiled; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---in the past; [[synonyms:u:udaksenaḥ]]---Udaksena; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Viṣvaksena); [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Udaksena); [[synonyms:b:bhallāṭaḥ]]---a son named Bhallāṭa; [[synonyms:b:bārhadīṣavāḥ]]---(all of these are known as) descendants of Bṛhadiṣu.

===== Translation =====

**Following the instructions of the great sage Jaigīṣavya, Viṣvaksena compiled an elaborate description of the mystic yoga system. From Viṣvaksena, Udaksena was born, and from Udaksena, Bhallāṭa. All these sons are known as descendants of Bṛhadiṣu.**
