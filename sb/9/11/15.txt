====== SB 9.11.15 ======

===== Text =====

> munau nikṣipya tanayau
> sītā bhartrā vivāsitā
> dhyāyantī rāma-caraṇau
> vivaraṁ praviveśa ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:munau]]---unto the great sage Vālmīki; [[synonyms:n:nikṣipya]]---giving in charge; [[synonyms:t:tanayau]]---the two sons Lava and Kuśa; [[synonyms:s:sītā]]---mother Sītādevī; [[synonyms:b:bhartrā]]---by her husband; [[synonyms:v:vivāsitā]]---banished; [[synonyms:d:dhyāyantī]]---meditating upon; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇau]]---the lotus feet of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:v:vivaram]]---within the earth; [[synonyms:p:praviveśa]]---she entered; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**Being forsaken by her husband, Sītādevī entrusted her two sons to the care of Vālmīki Muni. Then, meditating upon the lotus feet of Lord Rāmacandra, she entered into the earth.**

===== Purport =====

It was impossible for Sītādevī to live in separation from Lord Rāmacandra. Therefore, after entrusting her two sons to the care of Vālmīki Muni, she entered into the earth.
