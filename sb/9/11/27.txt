====== SB 9.11.27 ======

===== Text =====

> prāsāda-gopura-sabhā-
> caitya-deva-gṛhādiṣu
> vinyasta-hema-kalaśaiḥ
> patākābhiś ca maṇḍitām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prāsāda]]---in palaces; [[synonyms:g:gopura]]---palace gates; [[synonyms:s:sabhā]]---assembly houses; [[synonyms:c:caitya]]---raised platforms; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛha]]---temples wherein deities are worshiped; [[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---and so on; [[synonyms:v:vinyasta]]---placed; [[synonyms:h:hema]]-[[synonyms:k:kalaśaiḥ]]---with golden waterpots; [[synonyms:p:patākābhiḥ]]---by flags; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍitām]]---bedecked.

===== Translation =====

**The palaces, the palace gates, the assembly houses, the platforms for meeting places, the temples and all such places were decorated with golden waterpots and bedecked with various types of flags.**
