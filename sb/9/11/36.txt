====== SB 9.11.36 ======

===== Text =====

> bubhuje ca yathā-kālaṁ
> kāmān dharmam apīḍayan
> varṣa-pūgān bahūn nṝṇām
> abhidhyātāṅghri-pallavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bubhuje]]---He enjoyed; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:k:kālam]]---as long as required; [[synonyms:k:kāmān]]---all enjoyment; [[synonyms:d:dharmam]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:a:apīḍayan]]---without transgressing; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:pūgān]]---duration of years; [[synonyms:b:bahūn]]---many; [[synonyms:n:nṝṇām]]---of the people in general; [[synonyms:a:abhidhyāta]]---being meditated upon; [[synonyms:a:aṅghri]]-[[synonyms:p:pallavaḥ]]---His lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Without transgressing the religious principles, Lord Rāmacandra, whose lotus feet are worshiped by devotees in meditation, enjoyed with all the paraphernalia of transcendental pleasure for as long as needed.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Ninth Canto, Eleventh Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "Lord Rāmacandra Rules the World."
