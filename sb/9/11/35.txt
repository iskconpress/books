====== SB 9.11.35 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmin sa bhagavān rāmaḥ
> snigdhayā priyayeṣṭayā
> reme svārāma-dhīrāṇām
> ṛṣabhaḥ sītayā kila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmin]]---in that celestial palace; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:r:rāmaḥ]]---Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:s:snigdhayā]]---always pleased by her behavior; [[synonyms:p:priyayā]] [[synonyms:i:iṣṭayā]]---with His dearmost wife; [[synonyms:r:reme]]---enjoyed; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārāma]]---personal pleasure; [[synonyms:d:dhīrāṇām]]---of the greatest learned persons; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabhaḥ]]---the chief; [[synonyms:s:sītayā]]---with mother Sītā; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Rāmacandra, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, chief of the best learned scholars, resided in that palace with His pleasure potency, mother Sītā, and enjoyed complete peace.**
