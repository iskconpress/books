====== SB 9.11.12 ======

===== Text =====

> aṅgadaś citraketuś ca
> lakṣmaṇasyātmajau smṛtau
> takṣaḥ puṣkala ity āstāṁ
> bharatasya mahīpate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅgadaḥ]]---Aṅgada; [[synonyms:c:citraketuḥ]]---Citraketu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmaṇasya]]---of Lord Lakṣmaṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ātmajau]]---two sons; [[synonyms:s:smṛtau]]---were said to be; [[synonyms:t:takṣaḥ]]---Takṣa; [[synonyms:p:puṣkalaḥ]]---Puṣkala; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:āstām]]---were; [[synonyms:b:bharatasya]]---of Lord Bharata; [[synonyms:m:mahīpate]]---O King Parīkṣit.

===== Translation =====

**O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, Lord Lakṣmaṇa had two sons, named Aṅgada and Citraketu, and Lord Bharata also had two sons, named Takṣa and Puṣkala.**
