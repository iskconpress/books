====== SB 9.11.11 ======

===== Text =====

> antarvatny āgate kāle
> yamau sā suṣuve sutau
> kuśo lava iti khyātau
> tayoś cakre kriyā muniḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:antarvatnī]]---the pregnant wife; [[synonyms:ā:āgate]]---arrived; [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in due course of time; [[synonyms:y:yamau]]---twins; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---Sītādevī; [[synonyms:s:suṣuve]]---gave birth to; [[synonyms:s:sutau]]---two sons; [[synonyms:k:kuśaḥ]]---Kuśa; [[synonyms:l:lavaḥ]]---Lava; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:khyātau]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---of them; [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kriyāḥ]]---the ritualistic ceremonies of birth; [[synonyms:m:muniḥ]]---the great sage Vālmīki.

===== Translation =====

**When the time came, the pregnant mother Sītādevī gave birth to twin sons, later celebrated as Lava and Kuśa. The ritualistic ceremonies for their birth were performed by Vālmīki Muni.**
