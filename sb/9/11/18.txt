====== SB 9.11.18 ======

===== Text =====

> tata ūrdhvaṁ brahmacaryaṁ
> dhāryann ajuhot prabhuḥ
> trayodaśābda-sāhasram
> agnihotram akhaṇḍitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhvam]]---after mother Sītā's going into the earth; [[synonyms:b:brahmacaryam]]---complete celibacy; [[synonyms:d:dhārayan]]---observing; [[synonyms:a:ajuhot]]---performed a ritualistic ceremony and sacrifice; [[synonyms:p:prabhuḥ]]---Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:t:trayodaśa]]-[[synonyms:a:abda]]-[[synonyms:s:sāhasram]]---for thirteen thousand years; [[synonyms:a:agnihotram]]---the sacrifice known as Agnihotra-yajña; [[synonyms:a:akhaṇḍitam]]---without ceasing.

===== Translation =====

**After mother Sītā entered the earth, Lord Rāmacandra observed complete celibacy and performed an uninterrupted Agnihotra-yajña for thirteen thousand years.**
