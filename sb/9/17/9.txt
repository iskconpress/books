====== SB 9.17.9 ======

===== Text =====

> dhṛṣṭaketus tatas tasmāt
> sukumāraḥ kṣitīśvaraḥ
> vītihotro 'sya bhargo 'to
> bhārgabhūmir abhūn nṛpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhṛṣṭaketuḥ]]---Dhṛṣṭaketu; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Dhṛṣṭaketu; [[synonyms:s:sukumāraḥ]]---a son named Sukumāra; [[synonyms:k:kṣiti]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the emperor of the entire world; [[synonyms:v:vītihotraḥ]]---a son named Vītihotra; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---his son; [[synonyms:b:bhargaḥ]]---Bharga; [[synonyms:a:ataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:b:bhārgabhūmiḥ]]---a son named Bhārgabhūmi; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---generated; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, from Satyaketu came a son named Dhṛṣṭaketu, and from Dhṛṣṭaketu came Sukumāra, the emperor of the entire world. From Sukumāra came a son named Vītihotra; from Vītihotra, Bharga; and from Bharga, Bhārgabhūmi.**
