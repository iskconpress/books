====== SB 9.17.13 ======

===== Text =====

> devair abhyarthito daityān
> hatvendrāyādadād divam
> indras tasmai punar dattvā
> gṛhītvā caraṇau rajeḥ
> ātmānam arpayām āsa
> prahrādādy-ari-śaṅkitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devaiḥ]]---by the demigods; [[synonyms:a:abhyarthitaḥ]]---being requested; [[synonyms:d:daityān]]---the demons; [[synonyms:h:hatvā]]---killing; [[synonyms:i:indrāya]]---to Indra, the King of heaven; [[synonyms:a:adadāt]]---delivered; [[synonyms:d:divam]]---the kingdom of heaven; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the King of heaven; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto him, Rajī; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:d:dattvā]]---returning; [[synonyms:g:gṛhītvā]]---capturing; [[synonyms:c:caraṇau]]---the feet; [[synonyms:r:rajeḥ]]---of Rajī; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---the self; [[synonyms:a:arpayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---surrendered; [[synonyms:p:prahrāda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Prahlāda and others; [[synonyms:a:ari]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkitaḥ]]---being afraid of such enemies.

===== Translation =====

**On the request of the demigods, Rajī killed the demons and thus returned the kingdom of heaven to Lord Indra. But Indra, fearing such demons as Prahlāda, returned the kingdom of heaven to Rajī and surrendered himself at Rajī's lotus feet.**
