====== SB 9.17.7 ======

===== Text =====

> ṣaṣṭiṁ varṣa-sahasrāṇi
> ṣaṣṭiṁ varṣa-śatāni ca
> nālarkād aparo rājan
> bubhuje medinīṁ yuvā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṣṭim]]---sixty; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahasrāṇi]]---such thousands of years; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṣṭim]]---sixty; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śatāni]]---hundreds of years; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:alarkāt]]---except for Alarka; [[synonyms:a:aparaḥ]]---anyone else; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:b:bubhuje]]---enjoyed; [[synonyms:m:medinīm]]---the surface of the world; [[synonyms:y:yuvā]]---as a young man.

===== Translation =====

**Alarka, the son of Dyumān, reigned over the earth for sixty-six thousand years, my dear King Parīkṣit. No one other than him has reigned over the earth for so long as a young man.**
