====== SB 9.17.5 ======

===== Text =====

> tat-putraḥ ketumān asya
> jajñe bhīmarathas tataḥ
> divodāso dyumāṁs tasmāt
> pratardana iti smṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---his son (the son of Dhanvantari); [[synonyms:k:ketumān]]---Ketumān; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---his; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---took birth; [[synonyms:b:bhīmarathaḥ]]---a son named Bhīmaratha; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:d:divodāsaḥ]]---a son named Divodāsa; [[synonyms:d:dyumān]]---Dyumān; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:p:pratardanaḥ]]---Pratardana; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---known.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Dhanvantari was Ketumān, and his son was Bhīmaratha. The son of Bhīmaratha was Divodāsa, and the son of Divodāsa was Dyumān, also known as Pratardana.**
