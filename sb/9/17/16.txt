====== SB 9.17.16 ======

===== Text =====

> kuśāt pratiḥ kṣātravṛddhāt
> sañjayas tat-suto jayaḥ
> tataḥ kṛtaḥ kṛtasyāpi
> jajñe haryabalo nṛpaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuśāt]]---from Kuśa; [[synonyms:p:pratiḥ]]---a son named Prati; [[synonyms:k:kṣātravṛddhāt]]---the grandson of Kṣatravṛddha; [[synonyms:s:sañjayaḥ]]---a son named Sañjaya; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:j:jayaḥ]]---Jaya; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:k:kṛtaḥ]]---Kṛta; [[synonyms:k:kṛtasya]]---from Kṛta; [[synonyms:a:api]]---as well as; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---was born; [[synonyms:h:haryabalaḥ]]---Haryabala; [[synonyms:n:nṛpaḥ]]---the king.

===== Translation =====

**From Kuśa, the grandson of Kṣatravṛddha, was born a son named Prati. The son of Prati was Sañjaya, and the son of Sañjaya was Jaya. From Jaya, Kṛta was born, and from Kṛta, King Haryabala.**
