====== SB 9.17.12 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ śāntarajo jajñe
> kṛta-kṛtyaḥ sa ātmavān
> rajeḥ pañca-śatāny āsan
> putrāṇām amitaujasām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Citrakṛt; [[synonyms:ś:śāntarajaḥ]]---a son named Śāntaraja; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---was born; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtyaḥ]]---performed all kinds of ritualistic ceremonies; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:ā:ātmavān]]---a self-realized soul; [[synonyms:r:rajeḥ]]---of Rajī; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:ś:śatāni]]---five hundred; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---there were; [[synonyms:p:putrāṇām]]---sons; [[synonyms:a:amita]]-[[synonyms:o:ojasām]]---very, very powerful.

===== Translation =====

**From Citrakṛt was born a son named Śāntaraja, a self-realized soul who performed all kinds of Vedic ritualistic ceremonies and therefore did not beget any progeny. The sons of Rajī were five hundred, all very powerful.**
