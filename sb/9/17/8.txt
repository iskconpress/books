====== SB 9.17.8 ======

===== Text =====

> alarkāt santatis tasmāt
> sunītho 'tha niketanaḥ
> dharmaketuḥ sutas tasmāt
> satyaketur ajāyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alarkāt]]---from Alarka; [[synonyms:s:santatiḥ]]---a son known as Santati; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:s:sunīthaḥ]]---Sunītha; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---from him; [[synonyms:n:niketanaḥ]]---a son named Niketana; [[synonyms:d:dharmaketuḥ]]---Dharmaketu; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---and from Dharmaketu; [[synonyms:s:satyaketuḥ]]---Satyaketu; [[synonyms:a:ajāyata]]---was born.

===== Translation =====

**From Alarka came a son named Santati, and his son was Sunītha. The son of Sunītha was Niketana, the son of Niketana was Dharmaketu, and the son of Dharmaketu was Satyaketu.**
