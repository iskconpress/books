====== SB 9.17.11 ======

===== Text =====

> tad-gotraṁ brahmavij jajñe
> śṛṇu vaṁśam anenasaḥ
> śuddhas tataḥ śucis tasmāc
> citrakṛd dharmasārathiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:g:gotram]]---the descendant of Akriya; [[synonyms:b:brahmavit]]---Brahmavit; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---took birth; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---just hear from me; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśam]]---descendants; [[synonyms:a:anenasaḥ]]---of Anenā; [[synonyms:ś:śuddhaḥ]]---a son known as Śuddha; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:ś:śuciḥ]]---Śuci; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:c:citrakṛt]]---Citrakṛt; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:s:sārathiḥ]]---Dharmasārathi.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Akriya was known as Brahmavit, O King. Now hear about the descendants of Anenā. From Anenā came a son named Śuddha, and his son was Śuci. The son of Śuci was Dharmasārathi, also called Citrakṛt.**
