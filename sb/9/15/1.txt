====== SB 9.15.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bādarāyaṇir uvāca
> ailasya corvaśī-garbhāt
> ṣaḍ āsann ātmajā nṛpa
> āyuḥ śrutāyuḥ satyāyū
> rayo 'tha vijayo jayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:a:ailasya]]---of Purūravā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:u:urvaśī]]-[[synonyms:g:garbhāt]]---from the womb of Urvaśī; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]---six; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---there were; [[synonyms:ā:ātmajāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:ā:āyuḥ]]---Āyu; [[synonyms:ś:śrutāyuḥ]]---Śrutāyu; [[synonyms:s:satyāyuḥ]]---Satyāyu; [[synonyms:r:rayaḥ]]---Raya; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---as well as; [[synonyms:v:vijayaḥ]]---Vijaya; [[synonyms:j:jayaḥ]]---Jaya.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King Parīkṣit, from the womb of Urvaśī, six sons were generated by Purūravā. Their names were Āyu, Śrutāyu, Satyāyu, Raya, Vijaya and Jaya.**
