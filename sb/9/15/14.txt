====== SB 9.15.14 ======

===== Text =====

> yam āhur vāsudevāṁśaṁ
> haihayānāṁ kulāntakam
> triḥ-sapta-kṛtvo ya imāṁ
> cakre niḥkṣatriyāṁ mahīm

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yam]]---whom (Paraśurāma); [[synonyms:ā:āhuḥ]]---all the learned scholars say; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśam]]---an incarnation of Vāsudeva, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:haihayānām]]---of the Haihayas; [[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:a:antakam]]---the annihilator of the dynasty; [[synonyms:t:triḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtvaḥ]]---twenty-one times; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who (Paraśurāma); [[synonyms:i:imām]]---this; [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---made; [[synonyms:n:niḥkṣatriyām]]---devoid of //kṣatriyas//; [[synonyms:m:mahīm]]---the earth.

===== Translation =====

**Learned scholars accept this Paraśurāma as the celebrated incarnation of Vāsudeva who annihilated the dynasty of Kārtavīrya. Paraśurāma killed all the kṣatriyas on earth twenty-one times.**
