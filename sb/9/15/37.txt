====== SB 9.15.37 ======

===== Text =====

> sva-karma tat kṛtaṁ rāmaḥ
> pitre bhrātṛbhya eva ca
> varṇayām āsa tac chrutvā
> jamadagnir abhāṣata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]---his own activities; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---all those deeds; [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---which had been performed; [[synonyms:r:rāmaḥ]]---Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:p:pitre]]---unto his father; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛbhyaḥ]]---unto his brothers; [[synonyms:e:eva]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---as well as; [[synonyms:v:varṇayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---described; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:j:jamadagniḥ]]---the father of Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:a:abhāṣata]]---said as follows.

===== Translation =====

**Paraśurāma described to his father and brothers his activities in killing Kārtavīryārjuna. Upon hearing of these deeds, Jamadagni spoke to his son as follows.**
