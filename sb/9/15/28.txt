====== SB 9.15.28 ======

===== Text =====

> ghoram ādāya paraśuṁ
> satūṇaṁ varma kārmukam
> anvadhāvata durmarṣo
> mṛgendra iva yūthapam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghoram]]---extremely fierce; [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---taking in hand; [[synonyms:p:paraśum]]---a chopper; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:t:tūṇam]]---along with a quiver; [[synonyms:v:varma]]---a shield; [[synonyms:k:kārmukam]]---a bow; [[synonyms:a:anvadhāvata]]---followed; [[synonyms:d:durmarṣaḥ]]---Lord Paraśurāma, being exceedingly angry; [[synonyms:m:mṛgendraḥ]]---a lion; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:y:yūthapam]]---(goes to attack) an elephant.

===== Translation =====

**Taking up his fierce chopper, his shield, his bow and a quiver of arrows, Lord Paraśurāma, exceedingly angry, chased Kārtavīryārjuna just as a lion chases an elephant.**
