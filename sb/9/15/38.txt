====== SB 9.15.38 ======

===== Text =====

> rāma rāma mahābāho
> bhavān pāpam akāraṣīt
> avadhīn naradevaṁ yat
> sarva-devamayaṁ vṛthā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāma]] [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---my dear son Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:m:mahābāho]]---O great hero; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---you; [[synonyms:p:pāpam]]---sinful activities; [[synonyms:a:akāraṣīt]]---have executed; [[synonyms:a:avadhīt]]---have killed; [[synonyms:n:naradevam]]---the king; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---who is; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:m:mayam]]---the embodiment of all the demigods; [[synonyms:v:vṛthā]]---unnecessarily.

===== Translation =====

**O great hero, my dear son Paraśurāma, you have unnecessarily killed the king, who is supposed to be the embodiment of all the demigods. Thus you have committed a sin.**
