====== SB 9.15.34 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ sva-hastair acalān mṛdhe 'ṅghripān
> utkṣipya vegād abhidhāvato yudhi
> bhujān kuṭhāreṇa kaṭhora-neminā
> ciccheda rāmaḥ prasabhaṁ tv aher iva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:h:hastaiḥ]]---by his own hands; [[synonyms:a:acalān]]---hills; [[synonyms:m:mṛdhe]]---in the battlefield; [[synonyms:a:aṅghripān]]---trees; [[synonyms:u:utkṣipya]]---after uprooting; [[synonyms:v:vegāt]]---with great force; [[synonyms:a:abhidhāvataḥ]]---of he who was running very forcefully; [[synonyms:y:yudhi]]---in the battlefield; [[synonyms:b:bhujān]]---all the arms; [[synonyms:k:kuṭhāreṇa]]---by his axe; [[synonyms:k:kaṭhora]]-[[synonyms:n:neminā]]---which was very sharp; [[synonyms:c:ciccheda]]---cut to pieces; [[synonyms:r:rāmaḥ]]---Lord Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:p:prasabham]]---with great force; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:a:aheḥ]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---just like the hoods of a serpent.

===== Translation =====

**When his arrows were cut to pieces, Kārtavīryārjuna uprooted many trees and hills with his own hands and again rushed strongly toward Lord Paraśurāma to kill him. But Paraśurāma then used his axe with great force to cut off Kārtavīryārjuna's arms, just as one might lop off the hoods of a serpent.**
