====== SB 9.15.2-3 ======

===== Text =====

> śrutāyor vasumān putraḥ
> satyāyoś ca śrutañjayaḥ
> rayasya suta ekaś ca
> jayasya tanayo 'mitaḥ
>
> bhīmas tu vijayasyātha
> kāñcano hotrakas tataḥ
> tasya jahnuḥ suto gaṅgāṁ
> gaṇḍūṣī-kṛtya yo 'pibat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrutāyoḥ]]---of Śrutāyu; [[synonyms:v:vasumān]]---Vasumān; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:s:satyāyoḥ]]---of Satyāyu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ś:śrutañjayaḥ]]---a son named Śrutañjaya; [[synonyms:r:rayasya]]---of Raya; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:e:ekaḥ]]---by the name Eka; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:j:jayasya]]---of Jaya; [[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:a:amitaḥ]]---by the name Amita; [[synonyms:b:bhīmaḥ]]---by the name Bhīma; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vijayasya]]---of Vijaya; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:kāñcanaḥ]]---Kāñcana, the son of Bhīma; [[synonyms:h:hotrakaḥ]]---Hotraka, the son of Kāñcana; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---then; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Hotraka; [[synonyms:j:jahnuḥ]]---by the name Jahnu; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgām]]---all the water of the Ganges; [[synonyms:g:gaṇḍūṣī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---by one sip; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---he who (Jahnu); [[synonyms:a:apibat]]---drank.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Śrutāyu was Vasumān; the son of Satyāyu, Śrutañjaya; the son of Raya, Eka; the son of Jaya, Amita; and the son of Vijaya, Bhīma. The son of Bhīma was Kāñcana; the son of Kāñcana was Hotraka; and the son of Hotraka was Jahnu, who drank all the water of the Ganges in one sip.**
