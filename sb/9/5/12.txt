====== SB 9.5.12 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> iti saṁstuvato rājño
> viṣṇu-cakraṁ sudarśanam
> aśāmyat sarvato vipraṁ
> pradahad rāja-yācñayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saṁstuvataḥ]]---being prayed to; [[synonyms:r:rājñaḥ]]---by the King; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:c:cakram]]---the disc weapon of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:s:sudarśanam]]---of the name Sudarśana //cakra//; [[synonyms:a:aśāmyat]]---became no longer disturbing; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]]---in every respect; [[synonyms:v:vipram]]---unto the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:pradahat]]---causing to burn; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]---of the King; [[synonyms:y:yācñayā]]---by the begging.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: When the King offered prayers to the Sudarśana cakra and Lord Viṣṇu, because of his prayers the Sudarśana cakra became peaceful and stopped burning the brāhmaṇa known as Durvāsā Muni.**
