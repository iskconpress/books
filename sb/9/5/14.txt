====== SB 9.5.14 ======

===== Text =====

> durvāsā uvāca
> aho ananta-dāsānāṁ
> mahattvaṁ dṛṣṭam adya me
> kṛtāgaso 'pi yad rājan
> maṅgalāni samīhase

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:durvāsāḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Durvāsā Muni said; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---alas; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsānām]]---of the servants of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:mahattvam]]---greatness; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭam]]---seen; [[synonyms:a:adya]]---today; [[synonyms:m:me]]---by me; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgasaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---although I was an offender; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---still; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:m:maṅgalāni]]---good fortune; [[synonyms:s:samīhase]]---you are praying for.

===== Translation =====

**Durvāsā Muni said: My dear King, today I have experienced the greatness of devotees of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, for although I have committed an offense, you have prayed for my good fortune.**
