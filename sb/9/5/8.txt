====== SB 9.5.8 ======

===== Text =====

> yadā visṛṣṭas tvam anañjanena vai
> balaṁ praviṣṭo 'jita daitya-dānavam
> bāhūdarorv-aṅghri-śirodharāṇi
> vṛścann ajasraṁ pradhane virājase

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:v:visṛṣṭaḥ]]---sent; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---your good self; [[synonyms:a:anañjanena]]---by the transcendental Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:b:balam]]---the soldiers; [[synonyms:p:praviṣṭaḥ]]---entering among; [[synonyms:a:ajita]]---O indefatigable and unconquerable one; [[synonyms:d:daitya]]-[[synonyms:d:dānavam]]---of the Daityas and Dānavas, the demons; [[synonyms:b:bāhu]]---arms; [[synonyms:u:udara]]---bellies; [[synonyms:ū:ūru]]---thighs; [[synonyms:a:aṅghri]]---legs; [[synonyms:ś:śiraḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dharāṇi]]---necks; [[synonyms:v:vṛścan]]---separating; [[synonyms:a:ajasram]]---incessantly; [[synonyms:p:pradhane]]---in the battlefield; [[synonyms:v:virājase]]---you stay.

===== Translation =====

**O indefatigable one, when you are sent by the Supreme Personality of Godhead to enter among the soldiers of the Daityas and the Dānavas, you stay on the battlefield and unendingly separate their arms, bellies, thighs, legs and heads.**
