====== SB 9.5.21 ======

===== Text =====

> karmāvadātam etat te
> gāyanti svaḥ-striyo muhuḥ
> kīrtiṁ parama-puṇyāṁ ca
> kīrtayiṣyati bhūr iyam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karma]]---activity; [[synonyms:a:avadātam]]---without any tinge; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---all this; [[synonyms:t:te]]---your; [[synonyms:g:gāyanti]]---will sing; [[synonyms:s:svaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:striyaḥ]]---women from the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:m:muhuḥ]]---always; [[synonyms:k:kīrtim]]---glories; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:p:puṇyām]]---highly glorified and pious; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kīrtayiṣyati]]---will continuously chant; [[synonyms:b:bhūḥ]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this.

===== Translation =====

**All the blessed women in the heavenly planets will continuously chant about your spotless character at every moment, and the people of this world will also chant your glories continuously.**
