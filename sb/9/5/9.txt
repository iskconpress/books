====== SB 9.5.9 ======

===== Text =====

> sa tvaṁ jagat-trāṇa khala-prahāṇaye
> nirūpitaḥ sarva-saho gadā-bhṛtā
> viprasya cāsmat-kula-daiva-hetave
> vidhehi bhadraṁ tad anugraho hi naḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that person; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---your good self; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:t:trāṇa]]---O protector of the whole universe; [[synonyms:k:khala]]-[[synonyms:p:prahāṇaye]]---in killing the envious enemies; [[synonyms:n:nirūpitaḥ]]---are engaged; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:s:sahaḥ]]---all-powerful; [[synonyms:g:gadā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhṛtā]]---by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:viprasya]]---of this //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:asmat]]---our; [[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:d:daiva]]-[[synonyms:h:hetave]]---for the good fortune of the dynasty; [[synonyms:v:vidhehi]]---kindly do; [[synonyms:b:bhadram]]---all-good; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:a:anugrahaḥ]]---favor; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our.

===== Translation =====

**O protector of the universe, you are engaged by the Supreme Personality of Godhead as His all-powerful weapon in killing the envious enemies. For the benefit of our entire dynasty, kindly favor this poor brāhmaṇa. This will certainly be a favor for all of us.**
