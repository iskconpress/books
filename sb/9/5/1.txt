====== SB 9.5.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> evaṁ bhagavatādiṣṭo
> durvāsāś cakra-tāpitaḥ
> ambarīṣam upāvṛtya
> tat-pādau duḥkhito 'grahīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bhagavatā]] [[synonyms:ā:ādiṣṭaḥ]]---being ordered by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:durvāsāḥ]]---the great mystic //yogī// named Durvāsā; [[synonyms:c:cakra]]-[[synonyms:t:tāpitaḥ]]---being very much harassed by the Sudarśana //cakra//; [[synonyms:a:ambarīṣam]]---unto Mahārāja Ambarīṣa; [[synonyms:u:upāvṛtya]]---approaching; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:pādau]]---at his lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhitaḥ]]---being very much aggrieved; [[synonyms:a:agrahīt]]---he caught.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: When thus advised by Lord Viṣṇu, Durvāsā Muni, who was very much harassed by the Sudarśana cakra, immediately approached Mahārāja Ambarīṣa. Being very much aggrieved, the muni fell down and clasped the King's lotus feet.**
