====== SB 9.5.23 ======

===== Text =====

> saṁvatsaro 'tyagāt tāvad
> yāvatā nāgato gataḥ
> munis tad-darśanākāṅkṣo
> rājāb-bhakṣo babhūva ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṁvatsaraḥ]]---one complete year; [[synonyms:a:atyagāt]]---passed; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]]---as long as; [[synonyms:y:yāvatā]]---so long; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:ā:āgataḥ]]---returned; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---Durvāsā Muni, who had left that place; [[synonyms:m:muniḥ]]---the great sage; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:d:darśana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāṅkṣaḥ]]---desiring to see him again; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:a:ap]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakṣaḥ]]---taking only water; [[synonyms:b:babhūva]]---remained; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**Durvāsā Muni had left the place of Mahārāja Ambarīṣa, and as long as he had not returned---for one complete year---the King had fasted, maintaining himself simply by drinking water.**
