====== SB 9.5.18 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā tam akṛtāhāraḥ
> pratyāgamana-kāṅkṣayā
> caraṇāv upasaṅgṛhya
> prasādya samabhojayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him, Durvāsā Muni; [[synonyms:a:akṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:āhāraḥ]]---who refrained from taking food; [[synonyms:p:pratyāgamana]]---returning; [[synonyms:k:kāṅkṣayā]]---desiring; [[synonyms:c:caraṇau]]---the feet; [[synonyms:u:upasaṅgṛhya]]---approaching; [[synonyms:p:prasādya]]---pleasing in all respects; [[synonyms:s:samabhojayat]]---fed sumptuously.

===== Translation =====

**Expecting the return of Durvāsā Muni, the King had not taken his food. Therefore, when the sage returned, the King fell at his lotus feet, pleasing him in all respects, and fed him sumptuously.**
