====== SB 9.5.28 ======

===== Text =====

> ambarīṣasya caritaṁ
> ye śṛṇvanti mahātmanaḥ
> muktiṁ prayānti te sarve
> bhaktyā viṣṇoḥ prasādataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ambarīṣasya]]---of Mahārāja Ambarīṣa; [[synonyms:c:caritam]]---character; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---persons who; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇvanti]]---hear; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---of the great personality, the great devotee; [[synonyms:m:muktim]]---liberation; [[synonyms:p:prayānti]]---certainly they attain; [[synonyms:t:te]]---such persons; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:bhaktyā]]---simply by devotional service; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:p:prasādataḥ]]---by the mercy.

===== Translation =====

**By the grace of the Lord, those who hear about the activities of Mahārāja Ambarīṣa, the great devotee, certainly become liberated or become devotees without delay.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Ninth Canto, Fifth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "Durvāsā Muni's Life Spared."
