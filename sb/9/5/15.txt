====== SB 9.5.15 ======

===== Text =====

> duṣkaraḥ ko nu sādhūnāṁ
> dustyajo vā mahātmanām
> yaiḥ saṅgṛhīto bhagavān
> sātvatām ṛṣabho hariḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṣkaraḥ]]---difficult to do; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---what; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:sādhūnām]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:d:dustyajaḥ]]---impossible to give up; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanām]]---of the great persons; [[synonyms:y:yaiḥ]]---by which persons; [[synonyms:s:saṅgṛhītaḥ]]---achieved (by devotional service); [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sātvatām]]---of the pure devotees; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabhaḥ]]---the leader; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**For those who have achieved the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the master of the pure devotees, what is impossible to do, and what is impossible to give up?**
