====== SB 9.14.19 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> svāgataṁ te varārohe
> āsyatāṁ karavāma kim
> saṁramasva mayā sākaṁ
> ratir nau śāśvatīḥ samāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---the King (Purūravā) said; [[synonyms:s:svāgatam]]---welcome; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:v:varārohe]]---O best of beautiful women; [[synonyms:ā:āsyatām]]---kindly take your seat; [[synonyms:k:karavāma]] [[synonyms:k:kim]]---what can I do for you; [[synonyms:s:saṁramasva]]---just become my companion; [[synonyms:m:mayā]] [[synonyms:s:sākam]]---with me; [[synonyms:r:ratiḥ]]---a sexual relationship; [[synonyms:n:nau]]---between us; [[synonyms:ś:śāśvatīḥ]] [[synonyms:s:samāḥ]]---for many years.

===== Translation =====

**King Purūravā said: O most beautiful woman, you are welcome. Please sit here and tell me what I can do for you. You may enjoy with me as long as you desire. Let us pass our life happily in a sexual relationship.**
