====== SB 9.14.40 ======

===== Text =====

> antarvatnīm upālakṣya
> devīṁ sa prayayau purīm
> punas tatra gato 'bdānte
> urvaśīṁ vīra-mātaram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:antarvatnīm]]---pregnant; [[synonyms:u:upālakṣya]]---by observing; [[synonyms:d:devīm]]---Urvaśī; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, King Purūravā; [[synonyms:p:prayayau]]---returned; [[synonyms:p:purīm]]---to his palace; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---at that very spot; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---went; [[synonyms:a:abda]]-[[synonyms:a:ante]]---at the end of the year; [[synonyms:u:urvaśīm]]---Urvaśī; [[synonyms:v:vīra]]-[[synonyms:m:mātaram]]---the mother of one //kṣatriya// son.

===== Translation =====

**Understanding that Urvaśī was pregnant, Purūravā returned to his palace. At the end of the year, there at Kurukṣetra, he again obtained the association of Urvaśī, who was then the mother of a heroic son.**
