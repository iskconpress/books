====== SB 9.14.13 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmā tāṁ raha āhūya
> samaprākṣīc ca sāntvayan
> somasyety āha śanakaiḥ
> somas taṁ tāvad agrahīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---unto her, Tārā; [[synonyms:r:rahaḥ]]---in a secluded place; [[synonyms:ā:āhūya]]---putting her; [[synonyms:s:samaprākṣīt]]---inquired in detail; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:sāntvayan]]---pacifying; [[synonyms:s:somasya]]---this son belongs to Soma, the moon-god; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---she replied; [[synonyms:ś:śanakaiḥ]]---very slowly; [[synonyms:s:somaḥ]]---Soma; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---the child; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]]---immediately; [[synonyms:a:agrahīt]]---took charge of.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Brahmā then brought Tārā to a secluded place, and after pacifying her he asked to whom the child actually belonged. She replied very slowly, "This is the son of Soma, the moon-god." Then the moon-god immediately took charge of the child.**
