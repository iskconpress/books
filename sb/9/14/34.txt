====== SB 9.14.34 ======

===== Text =====

> aho jāye tiṣṭha tiṣṭha
> ghore na tyaktum arhasi
> māṁ tvam adyāpy anirvṛtya
> vacāṁsi kṛṇavāvahai

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aho]]---hello; [[synonyms:j:jāye]]---O my dear wife; [[synonyms:t:tiṣṭha]] [[synonyms:t:tiṣṭha]]---kindly stay, stay; [[synonyms:g:ghore]]---O most cruel one; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:tyaktum]]---to give up; [[synonyms:a:arhasi]]---you ought; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---me; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---you; [[synonyms:a:adya]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---until now; [[synonyms:a:anirvṛtya]]---having not gotten any happiness from me; [[synonyms:v:vacāṁsi]]---some words; [[synonyms:k:kṛṇavāvahai]]---let us talk for some time.

===== Translation =====

**O my dear wife, O most cruel one, kindly stay, kindly stay. I know that I have never made you happy until now, but you should not give me up for that reason. This is not proper for you. Even if you have decided to give up my company, let us nonetheless talk for some time.**
