====== SB 9.14.29 ======

===== Text =====

> yad-viśrambhād ahaṁ naṣṭā
> hṛtāpatyā ca dasyubhiḥ
> yaḥ śete niśi santrasto
> yathā nārī divā pumān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yat]]-[[synonyms:v:viśrambhāt]]---because of depending upon whom; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I (am); [[synonyms:n:naṣṭā]]---lost; [[synonyms:h:hṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:apatyā]]---bereft of my two sons, the lambs; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:dasyubhiḥ]]---by the plunderers; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---he who (my so-called husband); [[synonyms:ś:śete]]---lies down; [[synonyms:n:niśi]]---at night; [[synonyms:s:santrastaḥ]]---being afraid; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---a woman; [[synonyms:d:divā]]---during the daytime; [[synonyms:p:pumān]]---male.

===== Translation =====

**"Because I depended on him, the plunderers have deprived me of my two sons the lambs, and therefore I am now lost. My husband lies down at night in fear, exactly like a woman, although he appears to be a man during the day."**
