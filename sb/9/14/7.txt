====== SB 9.14.7 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-deva-gaṇopeto
> mahendro gurum anvayāt
> surāsura-vināśo 'bhūt
> samaras tārakāmayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---by all the different demigods; [[synonyms:u:upetaḥ]]---joined; [[synonyms:m:mahendraḥ]]---Mahendra, the King of heaven, Indra; [[synonyms:g:gurum]]---his spiritual master; [[synonyms:a:anvayāt]]---followed; [[synonyms:s:sura]]---of the demigods; [[synonyms:a:asura]]---and of the demons; [[synonyms:v:vināśaḥ]]---causing destruction; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:samaraḥ]]---a fight; [[synonyms:t:tārakā]]-[[synonyms:m:mayaḥ]]---simply because of Tārā, a woman, the wife of Bṛhaspati.

===== Translation =====

**King Indra, accompanied by all kinds of demigods, joined the side of Bṛhaspati. Thus there was a great fight, destroying both demons and demigods, only for the sake of Tārā, Bṛhaspati's wife.**
