====== SB 9.14.10 ======

===== Text =====

> tatyāja vrīḍitā tārā
> kumāraṁ kanaka-prabham
> spṛhām āṅgirasaś cakre
> kumāre soma eva ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tatyāja]]---gave delivery; [[synonyms:v:vrīḍitā]]---being very much ashamed; [[synonyms:t:tārā]]---Tārā, the wife of Bṛhaspati; [[synonyms:k:kumāram]]---to a child; [[synonyms:k:kanaka]]-[[synonyms:p:prabham]]---having a bodily effulgence like gold; [[synonyms:s:spṛhām]]---aspiration; [[synonyms:ā:āṅgirasaḥ]]---Bṛhaspati; [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---made; [[synonyms:k:kumāre]]---unto the child; [[synonyms:s:somaḥ]]---the moon-god; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: By Bṛhaspati's order, Tārā, who was very much ashamed, immediately gave birth to the child, who was very beautiful, with a golden bodily hue. Both Bṛhaspati and the moon-god, Soma, desired the beautiful child.**
