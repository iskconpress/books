====== SB 9.14.8 ======

===== Text =====

> nivedito 'thāṅgirasā
> somaṁ nirbhartsya viśva-kṛt
> tārāṁ sva-bhartre prāyacchad
> antarvatnīm avait patiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niveditaḥ]]---being fully informed; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thus; [[synonyms:a:aṅgirasā]]---by Aṅgirā Muni; [[synonyms:s:somam]]---the moon-god; [[synonyms:n:nirbhartsya]]---chastising severely; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛt]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:t:tārām]]---Tārā, the wife of Bṛhaspati; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhartre]]---unto her husband; [[synonyms:p:prāyacchat]]---delivered; [[synonyms:a:antarvatnīm]]---pregnant; [[synonyms:a:avait]]---could understand; [[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the husband (Bṛhaspati).

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Brahmā was fully informed by Aṅgirā about the entire incident, he severely chastised the moon-god, Soma. Thus Lord Brahmā delivered Tārā to her husband, who could then understand that she was pregnant.**
