====== SB 9.14.5 ======

===== Text =====

> yadā sa deva-guruṇā
> yācito 'bhīkṣṇaśo madāt
> nātyajat tat-kṛte jajñe
> sura-dānava-vigrahaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Soma, the moon-god); [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:g:guruṇā]]---by the spiritual master of the demigods, Bṛhaspati; [[synonyms:y:yācitaḥ]]---was begged; [[synonyms:a:abhīkṣṇaśaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:m:madāt]]---because of false pride; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:atyajat]]---did deliver; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛte]]---because of this; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:d:dānava]]---between the demigods and the demons; [[synonyms:v:vigrahaḥ]]---a fight.

===== Translation =====

**Although requested again and again by Bṛhaspati, the spiritual master of the demigods, Soma did not return Tārā. This was due to his false pride. Consequently, a fight ensued between the demigods and the demons.**
