====== SB 9.14.24 ======

===== Text =====

> tayā sa puruṣa-śreṣṭho
> ramayantyā yathārhataḥ
> reme sura-vihāreṣu
> kāmaṁ caitrarathādiṣu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tayā]]---with her; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭhaḥ]]---the best of human beings (Purūravā); [[synonyms:r:ramayantyā]]---enjoying; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:a:arhataḥ]]---as far as possible; [[synonyms:r:reme]]---enjoyed; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:v:vihāreṣu]]---in places resembling the heavenly parks; [[synonyms:k:kāmam]]---according to his desire; [[synonyms:c:caitraratha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---in the best gardens, like Caitraratha.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: The best of human beings, Purūravā, began freely enjoying the company of Urvaśī, who engaged in sexual activities with him in many celestial places, such as Caitraratha and Nandana-kānana, where the demigods enjoy.**
