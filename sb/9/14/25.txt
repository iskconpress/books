====== SB 9.14.25 ======

===== Text =====

> ramamāṇas tayā devyā
> padma-kiñjalka-gandhayā
> tan-mukhāmoda-muṣito
> mumude 'har-gaṇān bahūn

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ramamāṇaḥ]]---enjoying sex; [[synonyms:t:tayā]]---with her; [[synonyms:d:devyā]]---the heavenly goddess; [[synonyms:p:padma]]---of a lotus; [[synonyms:k:kiñjalka]]---like the saffron; [[synonyms:g:gandhayā]]---the fragrance of whom; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---her beautiful face; [[synonyms:ā:āmoda]]---by the fragrance; [[synonyms:m:muṣitaḥ]]---being enlivened more and more; [[synonyms:m:mumude]]---enjoyed life; [[synonyms:a:ahaḥ]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇān]]---days after days; [[synonyms:b:bahūn]]---many.

===== Translation =====

**Urvaśī's body was as fragrant as the saffron of a lotus. Being enlivened by the fragrance of her face and body, Purūravā enjoyed her company for many days with great jubilation.**
