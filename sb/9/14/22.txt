====== SB 9.14.22 ======

===== Text =====

> ghṛtaṁ me vīra bhakṣyaṁ syān
> nekṣe tvānyatra maithunāt
> vivāsasaṁ tat tatheti
> pratipede mahāmanāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghṛtam]]---clarified butter or nectar; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:v:vīra]]---O hero; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣyam]]---eatable; [[synonyms:s:syāt]]---shall be; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:ī:īkṣe]]---I shall see; [[synonyms:t:tvā]]---you; [[synonyms:a:anyatra]]---any other time; [[synonyms:m:maithunāt]]---except at the time of sexual intercourse; [[synonyms:v:vivāsasam]]---without any dress (naked); [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:i:iti]]---shall be like that; [[synonyms:p:pratipede]]---promised; [[synonyms:m:mahāmanāḥ]]---King Purūravā.

===== Translation =====

**Urvaśī said: "My dear hero, only preparations made in ghee [clarified butter] will be my eatables, and I shall not want to see you naked at any time, except at the time of sexual intercourse." The great-minded King Purūravā accepted these proposals.**
