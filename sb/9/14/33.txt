====== SB 9.14.33 ======

===== Text =====

> sa tāṁ vīkṣya kurukṣetre
> sarasvatyāṁ ca tat-sakhīḥ
> pañca prahṛṣṭa-vadanaḥ
> prāha sūktaṁ purūravāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Purūravā; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---Urvaśī; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---observing; [[synonyms:k:kurukṣetre]]---at the place known as Kurukṣetra; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatyām]]---on the bank of the Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sakhīḥ]]---her companions; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five; [[synonyms:p:prahṛṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vadanaḥ]]---being very happy and smiling; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---said; [[synonyms:s:sūktam]]---sweet words; [[synonyms:p:purūravāḥ]]---King Purūravā.

===== Translation =====

**Once during his travels all over the world, Purūravā saw Urvaśī, accompanied by five companions, on the bank of the Sarasvatī at Kurukṣetra. With jubilation in his face, he then spoke to her in sweet words as follows.**
