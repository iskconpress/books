====== SB 9.14.2 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra-śirasaḥ puṁso
> nābhi-hrada-saroruhāt
> jātasyāsīt suto dhātur
> atriḥ pitṛ-samo guṇaiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śirasaḥ]]---who has thousands of heads; [[synonyms:p:puṁsaḥ]]---of Lord Viṣṇu (Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu); [[synonyms:n:nābhi]]-[[synonyms:h:hrada]]-[[synonyms:s:saroruhāt]]---from the lotus produced from the lake of the navel; [[synonyms:j:jātasya]]---who appeared; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:d:dhātuḥ]]---of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:a:atriḥ]]---by the name Atri; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]-[[synonyms:s:samaḥ]]---like his father; [[synonyms:g:guṇaiḥ]]---qualified.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Viṣṇu [Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu] is also known as Sahasra-śīrṣā Puruṣa. From the lake of His navel sprang a lotus, on which Lord Brahmā was generated. Atri, the son of Lord Brahmā, was as qualified as his father.**
