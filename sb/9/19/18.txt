====== SB 9.19.18 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrṇaṁ varṣa-sahasraṁ me
> viṣayān sevato 'sakṛt
> tathāpi cānusavanaṁ
> tṛṣṇā teṣūpajāyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrṇam]]---completely; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahasram]]---one thousand years; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:v:viṣayān]]---sense gratification; [[synonyms:s:sevataḥ]]---enjoying; [[synonyms:a:asakṛt]]---without cessation, continuously; [[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---still; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:anusavanam]]---more and more; [[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇā]]---lusty desires; [[synonyms:t:teṣu]]---in sense gratification; [[synonyms:u:upajāyate]]---are increased.

===== Translation =====

**I have spent a full one thousand years enjoying sense gratification, yet my desire to enjoy such pleasure increases daily.**

===== Purport =====

Mahārāja Yayāti is explaining, in terms of his actual experience, how strong are sexual desires, even in old age.
