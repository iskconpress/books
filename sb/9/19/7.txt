====== SB 9.19.7 ======

===== Text =====

> tam eva preṣṭhatamayā
> ramamāṇam ajānyayā
> vilokya kūpa-saṁvignā
> nāmṛṣyad basta-karma tat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---the he-goat; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:preṣṭhatamayā]]---beloved; [[synonyms:r:ramamāṇam]]---engaged in sexual activities; [[synonyms:a:ajā]]---the she-goat; [[synonyms:a:anyayā]]---with another she-goat; [[synonyms:v:vilokya]]---by seeing; [[synonyms:k:kūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvignā]]---the she-goat who had fallen into the well; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:amṛṣyat]]---tolerated; [[synonyms:b:basta]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]---the business of the goat; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that (sex is accepted here as the business of the goat).

===== Translation =====

**When the she-goat who had fallen into the well saw her beloved goat engaged in sexual affairs with another she-goat, she could not tolerate the goat's activities.**
