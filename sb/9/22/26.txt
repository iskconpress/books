====== SB 9.22.26 ======

===== Text =====

> gāndhāryāṁ dhṛtarāṣṭrasya
> jajñe putra-śataṁ nṛpa
> tatra duryodhano jyeṣṭho
> duḥśalā cāpi kanyakā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gāndhāryām]]---in the womb of Gāndhārī; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtarāṣṭrasya]]---of Dhṛtarāṣṭra; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---were born; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śatam]]---one hundred sons; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---among the sons; [[synonyms:d:duryodhanaḥ]]---the son named Duryodhana; [[synonyms:j:jyeṣṭhaḥ]]---the eldest; [[synonyms:d:duḥśalā]]---Duḥśalā; [[synonyms:c:ca]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kanyakā]]---one daughter.

===== Translation =====

**Dhṛtarāṣṭra's wife, Gāndhārī, gave birth to one hundred sons and one daughter, O King. The oldest of the sons was Duryodhana, and the daughter's name was Duḥśalā.**
