====== SB 9.22.18-19 ======

===== Text =====

> soma-vaṁśe kalau naṣṭe
> kṛtādau sthāpayiṣyati
> bāhlīkāt somadatto 'bhūd
> bhūrir bhūriśravās tataḥ
>
> śalaś ca śāntanor āsīd
> gaṅgāyāṁ bhīṣma ātmavān
> sarva-dharma-vidāṁ śreṣṭho
> mahā-bhāgavataḥ kaviḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:soma]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---when the dynasty of the moon-god; [[synonyms:k:kalau]]---in this age of Kali; [[synonyms:n:naṣṭe]]---being lost; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādau]]---at the beginning of the next Satya-yuga; [[synonyms:s:sthāpayiṣyati]]---will reestablish; [[synonyms:b:bāhlīkāt]]---from Bāhlīka; [[synonyms:s:somadattaḥ]]---Somadatta; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---generated; [[synonyms:b:bhūriḥ]]---Bhūri; [[synonyms:b:bhūri]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravāḥ]]---Bhūriśravā; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ś:śalaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---a son named Śala; [[synonyms:ś:śāntanoḥ]]---from Śāntanu; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---generated; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāyām]]---in the womb of Gaṅgā, the wife of Śāntanu; [[synonyms:b:bhīṣmaḥ]]---a son named Bhīṣma; [[synonyms:ā:ātmavān]]---self-realized; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:v:vidām]]---of all religious persons; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭhaḥ]]---the best; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavataḥ]]---an exalted devotee; [[synonyms:k:kaviḥ]]---and a learned scholar.

===== Translation =====

**After the dynasty of the moon-god comes to an end in this age of Kali, Devāpi, in the beginning of the next Satya-yuga, will reestablish the Soma dynasty in this world. From Bāhlīka [the brother of Śāntanu] came a son named Somadatta, who had three sons, named Bhūri, Bhūriśravā and Śala. From Śāntanu, through the womb of his wife named Gaṅgā, came Bhīṣma, the exalted, self-realized devotee and learned scholar.**
