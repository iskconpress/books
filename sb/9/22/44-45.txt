====== SB 9.22.44-45 ======

===== Text =====

> daṇḍapāṇir nimis tasya
> kṣemako bhavitā yataḥ
> brahma-kṣatrasya vai yonir
> vaṁśo devarṣi-satkṛtaḥ
>
> kṣemakaṁ prāpya rājānaṁ
> saṁsthāṁ prāpsyati vai kalau
> atha māgadha-rājāno
> bhāvino ye vadāmi te

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daṇḍapāṇiḥ]]---Daṇḍapāṇi; [[synonyms:n:nimiḥ]]---Nimi; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---from him (Mahīnara); [[synonyms:k:kṣemakaḥ]]---a son named Kṣemaka; [[synonyms:b:bhavitā]]---will take birth; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---from whom (Nimi); [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣatrasya]]---of //brāhmaṇas// and //kṣatriyas//; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:y:yoniḥ]]---the source; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśaḥ]]---the dynasty; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣi]]-[[synonyms:s:satkṛtaḥ]]---respected by great saintly persons and demigods; [[synonyms:k:kṣemakam]]---King Kṣemaka; [[synonyms:p:prāpya]]---up to this point; [[synonyms:r:rājānam]]---the monarch; [[synonyms:s:saṁsthām]]---an end to them; [[synonyms:p:prāpsyati]]---there will be; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:k:kalau]]---in this Kali-yuga; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:māgadha]]-[[synonyms:r:rājānaḥ]]---the kings in the Māgadha dynasty; [[synonyms:b:bhāvinaḥ]]---the future; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all those who; [[synonyms:v:vadāmi]]---I shall explain; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Mahīnara will be Daṇḍapāṇi, and his son will be Nimi, from whom King Kṣemaka will be born. I have now described to you the moon-god's dynasty, which is the source of brāhmaṇas and kṣatriyas and is worshiped by demigods and great saints. In this Kali-yuga, Kṣemaka will be the last monarch. Now I shall describe to you the future of the Māgadha dynasty. Please listen.**
