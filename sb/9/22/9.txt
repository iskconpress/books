====== SB 9.22.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tataś ca sahadevo 'bhūt
> somāpir yac chrutaśravāḥ
> parīkṣir anapatyo 'bhūt
> suratho nāma jāhnavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and from him (Jarāsandha); [[synonyms:s:sahadevaḥ]]---Sahadeva; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---was born; [[synonyms:s:somāpiḥ]]---Somāpi; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---of him (Somāpi); [[synonyms:ś:śrutaśravāḥ]]---a son named Śrutaśravā; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣiḥ]]---the son of Kuru named Parīkṣi; [[synonyms:a:anapatyaḥ]]---without any son; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became; [[synonyms:s:surathaḥ]]---Suratha; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:j:jāhnavaḥ]]---was the son of Jahnu.

===== Translation =====

**From Jarāsandha came a son named Sahadeva; from Sahadeva, Somāpi; and from Somāpi, Śrutaśravā. The son of Kuru called Parīkṣi had no sons, but the son of Kuru called Jahnu had a son named Suratha.**
