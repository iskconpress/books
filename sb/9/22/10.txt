====== SB 9.22.10 ======

===== Text =====

> tato vidūrathas tasmāt
> sārvabhaumas tato 'bhavat
> jayasenas tat-tanayo
> rādhiko 'to 'yutāyv abhūt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Suratha); [[synonyms:v:vidūrathaḥ]]---a son named Vidūratha; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Vidūratha); [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaumaḥ]]---a son named Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Sārvabhauma); [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was born; [[synonyms:j:jayasenaḥ]]---Jayasena; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---the son of Jayasena; [[synonyms:r:rādhikaḥ]]---Rādhika; [[synonyms:a:ataḥ]]---and from him (Rādhika); [[synonyms:a:ayutāyuḥ]]---Ayutāyu; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---was born.

===== Translation =====

**From Suratha came a son named Vidūratha, from whom Sārvabhauma was born. From Sārvabhauma came Jayasena; from Jayasena, Rādhika; and from Rādhika, Ayutāyu.**
