====== SB 9.22.42 ======

===== Text =====

> pariplavaḥ sutas tasmān
> medhāvī sunayātmajaḥ
> nṛpañjayas tato dūrvas
> timis tasmāj janiṣyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pariplavaḥ]]---Pariplava; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Pariplava); [[synonyms:m:medhāvī]]---Medhāvī; [[synonyms:s:sunaya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajaḥ]]---the son of Sunaya; [[synonyms:n:nṛpañjayaḥ]]---Nṛpañjaya; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:d:dūrvaḥ]]---Dūrva; [[synonyms:t:timiḥ]]---Timi; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:j:janiṣyati]]---will take birth.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Sukhīnala will be Pariplava, and his son will be Sunaya. From Sunaya will come a son named Medhāvī; from Medhāvī, Nṛpañjaya; from Nṛpañjaya, Dūrva; and from Dūrva, Timi.**
