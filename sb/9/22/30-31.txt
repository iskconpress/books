~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.22.30-31~~

====== SB 9.22.30-31 ======

===== Text =====

> sahadeva-suto rājañ
> chrutakarmā tathāpare
> yudhiṣṭhirāt tu pauravyāṁ
> devako 'tha ghaṭotkacaḥ

> bhīmasenād dhiḍimbāyāṁ
> kālyāṁ sarvagatas tataḥ
> sahadevāt suhotraṁ tu
> vijayāsūta pārvatī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahadeva]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Sahadeva; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:ś:śrutakarmā]]---Śrutakarmā; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well as; [[synonyms:a:apare]]---others; [[synonyms:y:yudhiṣṭhirāt]]---from Yudhiṣṭhira; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:p:pauravyām]]---in the womb of Pauravī; [[synonyms:d:devakaḥ]]---a son named Devaka; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---as well as; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭotkacaḥ]]---Ghaṭotkaca; [[synonyms:b:bhīmasenāt]]---from Bhīmasena; [[synonyms:h:hiḍimbāyām]]---in the womb of Hiḍimbā; [[synonyms:k:kālyām]]---in the womb of Kālī; [[synonyms:s:sarvagataḥ]]---Sarvagata; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sahadevāt]]---from Sahadeva; [[synonyms:s:suhotram]]---Suhotra; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vijayā]]---Vijayā; [[synonyms:a:asūta]]---gave birth to; [[synonyms:p:pārvatī]]---the daughter of the Himalayan king.

===== Translation =====

**O King, the son of Sahadeva was Śrutakarmā. Furthermore, Yudhiṣṭhira and his brothers begot other sons in other wives. Yudhiṣṭhira begot a son named Devaka through the womb of Pauravī, and Bhīmasena begot a son named Ghaṭotkaca through his wife Hiḍimbā and a son named Sarvagata through his wife Kālī. Similarly, Sahadeva had a son named Suhotra through his wife named Vijayā, who was the daughter of the king of the mountains.**
