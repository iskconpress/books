====== SB 9.22.29 ======

===== Text =====

> yudhiṣṭhirāt prativindhyaḥ
> śrutaseno vṛkodarāt
> arjunāc chrutakīrtis tu
> śatānīkas tu nākuliḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yudhiṣṭhirāt]]---from Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira; [[synonyms:p:prativindhyaḥ]]---a son named Prativindhya; [[synonyms:ś:śrutasenaḥ]]---Śrutasena; [[synonyms:v:vṛkodarāt]]---begotten by Bhīma; [[synonyms:a:arjunāt]]---from Arjuna; [[synonyms:ś:śrutakīrtiḥ]]---a son named Śrutakīrti; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ś:śatānīkaḥ]]---a son named Śatānīka; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:n:nākuliḥ]]---of Nakula.

===== Translation =====

**From Yudhiṣṭhira came a son named Prativindhya, from Bhīma a son named Śrutasena, from Arjuna a son named Śrutakīrti, and from Nakula a son named Śatānīka.**
