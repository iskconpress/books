====== SB 9.22.39 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasrānīkas tat-putras
> tataś caivāśvamedhajaḥ
> asīmakṛṣṇas tasyāpi
> nemicakras tu tat-sutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasrānīkaḥ]]---Sahasrānīka; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son of Śatānīka; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Sahasrānīka); [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:aśvamedhajaḥ]]---Aśvamedhaja; [[synonyms:a:asīmakṛṣṇaḥ]]---Asīmakṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---from him (Aśvamedhaja); [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:n:nemicakraḥ]]---Nemicakra; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Śatānīka will be Sahasrānīka, and from him will come the son named Aśvamedhaja. From Aśvamedhaja will come Asīmakṛṣṇa, and his son will be Nemicakra.**
