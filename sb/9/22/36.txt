====== SB 9.22.36 ======

===== Text =====

> janamejayas tvāṁ viditvā
> takṣakān nidhanaṁ gatam
> sarpān vai sarpa-yāgāgnau
> sa hoṣyati ruṣānvitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:janamejayaḥ]]---the eldest son; [[synonyms:t:tvām]]---about you; [[synonyms:v:viditvā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:t:takṣakāt]]---by the Takṣaka serpent; [[synonyms:n:nidhanam]]---death; [[synonyms:g:gatam]]---undergone; [[synonyms:s:sarpān]]---the snakes; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:sarpa]]-[[synonyms:y:yāga]]-[[synonyms:a:agnau]]---in the fire of the sacrifice for killing all the snakes; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Janamejaya); [[synonyms:h:hoṣyati]]---will offer as a sacrifice; [[synonyms:r:ruṣā]]-[[synonyms:a:anvitaḥ]]---because of being very angry.

===== Translation =====

**Because of your death by the Takṣaka snake, your son Janamejaya will be very angry and will perform a sacrifice to kill all the snakes in the world.**
