====== SB 9.22.12-13 ======

===== Text =====

> devāpiḥ śāntanus tasya
> bāhlīka iti cātmajāḥ
> pitṛ-rājyaṁ parityajya
> devāpis tu vanaṁ gataḥ
>
> abhavac chāntanū rājā
> prāṅ mahābhiṣa-saṁjñitaḥ
> yaṁ yaṁ karābhyāṁ spṛśati
> jīrṇaṁ yauvanam eti saḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devāpiḥ]]---Devāpi; [[synonyms:ś:śāntanuḥ]]---Śāntanu; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Pratīpa); [[synonyms:b:bāhlīkaḥ]]---Bāhlīka; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:j:jāḥ]]---the sons; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]-[[synonyms:r:rājyam]]---the father's property, the kingdom; [[synonyms:p:parityajya]]---rejecting; [[synonyms:d:devāpiḥ]]---Devāpi, the eldest; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vanam]]---to the forest; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---left; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was; [[synonyms:ś:śāntanuḥ]]---Śāntanu; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the king; [[synonyms:p:prāk]]---before; [[synonyms:m:mahābhiṣa]]---Mahābhiṣa; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñitaḥ]]---most celebrated; [[synonyms:y:yam]] [[synonyms:y:yam]]---whomever; [[synonyms:k:karābhyām]]---with his hands; [[synonyms:s:spṛśati]]---touched; [[synonyms:j:jīrṇam]]---although very old; [[synonyms:y:yauvanam]]---youth; [[synonyms:e:eti]]---attained; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he.

===== Translation =====

**The sons of Pratīpa were Devāpi, Śāntanu and Bāhlīka. Devāpi left the kingdom of his father and went to the forest, and therefore Śāntanu became the king. Śāntanu, who in his previous birth was known as Mahābhiṣa, had the ability to transform anyone from old age to youth simply by touching that person with his hands.**
