====== SB 9.22.43 ======

===== Text =====

> timer bṛhadrathas tasmāc
> chatānīkaḥ sudāsajaḥ
> śatānīkād durdamanas
> tasyāpatyaṁ mahīnaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:timeḥ]]---of Timi; [[synonyms:b:bṛhadrathaḥ]]---Bṛhadratha; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Bṛhadratha); [[synonyms:ś:śatānīkaḥ]]---Śatānīka; [[synonyms:s:sudāsa]]-[[synonyms:j:jaḥ]]---the son of Sudāsa; [[synonyms:ś:śatānīkāt]]---from Śatānīka; [[synonyms:d:durdamanaḥ]]---a son named Durdamana; [[synonyms:t:tasya]] [[synonyms:a:apatyam]]---his son; [[synonyms:m:mahīnaraḥ]]---Mahīnara.

===== Translation =====

**From Timi will come Bṛhadratha; from Bṛhadratha, Sudāsa; and from Sudāsa, Śatānīka. From Śatānīka will come Durdamana, and from him will come a son named Mahīnara.**
