====== SB 9.22.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> mitrāyuś ca divodāsāc
> cyavanas tat-suto nṛpa
> sudāsaḥ sahadevo 'tha
> somako jantu-janmakṛt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:m:mitrāyuḥ]]---Mitrāyu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:d:divodāsāt]]---was born from Divodāsa; [[synonyms:c:cyavanaḥ]]---Cyavana; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Mitrāyu; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:sudāsaḥ]]---Sudāsa; [[synonyms:s:sahadevaḥ]]---Sahadeva; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:somakaḥ]]---Somaka; [[synonyms:j:jantu]]-[[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛt]]---the father of Jantu.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King, the son of Divodāsa was Mitrāyu, and from Mitrāyu came four sons, named Cyavana, Sudāsa, Sahadeva and Somaka. Somaka was the father of Jantu.**
