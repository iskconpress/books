====== SB 9.22.8 ======

===== Text =====

> anyasyām api bhāryāyāṁ
> śakale dve bṛhadrathāt
> ye mātrā bahir utsṛṣṭe
> jarayā cābhisandhite
> jīva jīveti krīḍantyā
> jarāsandho 'bhavat sutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anyasyām]]---in another; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:b:bhāryāyām]]---wife; [[synonyms:ś:śakale]]---parts; [[synonyms:d:dve]]---two; [[synonyms:b:bṛhadrathāt]]---from Bṛhadratha; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which two parts; [[synonyms:m:mātrā]]---by the mother; [[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]] [[synonyms:u:utsṛṣṭe]]---because of rejection; [[synonyms:j:jarayā]]---by the demoness named Jarā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:abhisandhite]]---when they were joined together; [[synonyms:j:jīva]] [[synonyms:j:jīva]] [[synonyms:i:iti]]---O living entity, be alive; [[synonyms:k:krīḍantyā]]---playing like that; [[synonyms:j:jarāsandhaḥ]]---Jarāsandha; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was generated; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son.

===== Translation =====

**Through the womb of another wife, Bṛhadratha begot two halves of a son. When the mother saw those two halves she rejected them, but later a she-demon named Jarā playfully joined them and said, "Come to life, come to life!" Thus the son named Jarāsandha was born.**
