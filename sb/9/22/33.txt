====== SB 9.22.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tava tātaḥ subhadrāyām
> abhimanyur ajāyata
> sarvātirathajid vīra
> uttarāyāṁ tato bhavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tava]]---your; [[synonyms:t:tātaḥ]]---father; [[synonyms:s:subhadrāyām]]---in the womb of Subhadrā; [[synonyms:a:abhimanyuḥ]]---Abhimanyu; [[synonyms:a:ajāyata]]---was born; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:atiratha]]-[[synonyms:j:jit]]---a great fighter who could defeat the //atirathas//; [[synonyms:v:vīraḥ]]---a great hero; [[synonyms:u:uttarāyām]]---in the womb of Uttarā; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Abhimanyu; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---your good self.

===== Translation =====

**My dear King Parīkṣit, your father, Abhimanyu, was born from the womb of Subhadrā as the son of Arjuna. He was the conqueror of all atirathas [those who could fight with one thousand charioteers]. From him, by the womb of Uttarā, the daughter of Virāḍrāja, you were born.**
