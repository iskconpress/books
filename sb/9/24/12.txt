====== SB 9.24.12 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛṣṇeḥ sumitraḥ putro 'bhūd
> yudhājic ca parantapa
> śinis tasyānamitraś ca
> nighno 'bhūd anamitrataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛṣṇeḥ]]---of Vṛṣṇi, the son of Sātvata; [[synonyms:s:sumitraḥ]]---Sumitra; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---appeared; [[synonyms:y:yudhājit]]---Yudhājit; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:p:param]]-[[synonyms:t:tapa]]---O king who can suppress enemies; [[synonyms:ś:śiniḥ]]---Śini; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:a:anamitraḥ]]---Anamitra; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:n:nighnaḥ]]---Nighna; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---appeared; [[synonyms:a:anamitrataḥ]]---from Anamitra.

===== Translation =====

**O King, Mahārāja Parīkṣit, who can suppress your enemies, the sons of Vṛṣṇi were Sumitra and Yudhājit. From Yudhājit came Śini and Anamitra, and from Anamitra came a son named Nighna.**
