====== SB 9.24.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tadaivopāgataṁ devaṁ
> vīkṣya vismita-mānasā
> pratyayārthaṁ prayuktā me
> yāhi deva kṣamasva me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tadā]]---at that time; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:u:upāgatam]]---appeared (before her); [[synonyms:d:devam]]---the sun-god; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]-[[synonyms:m:mānasā]]---very much surprised; [[synonyms:p:pratyaya]]-[[synonyms:a:artham]]---just to see the potency of the mystic power; [[synonyms:p:prayuktā]]---I have used it; [[synonyms:m:me]]---me; [[synonyms:y:yāhi]]---please return; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---O demigod; [[synonyms:k:kṣamasva]]---forgive; [[synonyms:m:me]]---me.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Kuntī called for the demigod of the sun, he immediately appeared before her, and she was very much surprised. She told the sun-god, "I was simply examining the effectiveness of this mystic power. I am sorry I have called you unnecessarily. Please return and excuse me."**
