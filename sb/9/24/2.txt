====== SB 9.24.2 ======

===== Text =====

> romapāda-suto babhrur
> babhroḥ kṛtir ajāyata
> uśikas tat-sutas tasmāc
> cediś caidyādayo nṛpāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:romapāda]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Romapāda; [[synonyms:b:babhruḥ]]---Babhru; [[synonyms:b:babhroḥ]]---from Babhru; [[synonyms:k:kṛtiḥ]]---Kṛti; [[synonyms:a:ajāyata]]---was born; [[synonyms:u:uśikaḥ]]---Uśika; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Kṛti; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Uśika); [[synonyms:c:cediḥ]]---Cedi; [[synonyms:c:caidya]]---Caidya (Damaghoṣa); [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and others; [[synonyms:n:nṛpāḥ]]---kings.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Romapāda was Babhru, from whom there came a son named Kṛti. The son of Kṛti was Uśika, and the son of Uśika was Cedi. From Cedi was born the king known as Caidya and others.**
