====== SB 9.24.47-48 ======

===== Text =====

> subhadro bhadrabāhuś ca
> durmado bhadra eva ca
> pauravyās tanayā hy ete
> bhūtādyā dvādaśābhavan
>
> nandopananda-kṛtaka-
> śūrādyā madirātmajāḥ
> kauśalyā keśinaṁ tv ekam
> asūta kula-nandanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:subhadraḥ]]---Subhadra; [[synonyms:b:bhadrabāhuḥ]]---Bhadrabāhu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:d:durmadaḥ]]---Durmada; [[synonyms:b:bhadraḥ]]---Bhadra; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:p:pauravyāḥ]]---of the wife named Pauravī; [[synonyms:t:tanayāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:e:ete]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---headed by Bhūta; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśa]]---twelve; [[synonyms:a:abhavan]]---were born; [[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:u:upananda]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtaka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śūra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---Nanda, Upananda, Kṛtaka, Śūra and others; [[synonyms:m:madirā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajāḥ]]---the sons of Madirā; [[synonyms:k:kauśalyā]]---Kauśalyā; [[synonyms:k:keśinam]]---a son named Keśī; [[synonyms:t:tu]] [[synonyms:e:ekam]]---only one; [[synonyms:a:asūta]]---gave birth to; [[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:n:nandanam]]---a son.

===== Translation =====

**From the womb of Pauravī came twelve sons, including Bhūta, Subhadra, Bhadrabāhu, Durmada and Bhadra. Nanda, Upananda, Kṛtaka, Śūra and others were born from the womb of Madirā. Bhadrā [Kauśalyā] gave birth to only one son, named Keśī.**
