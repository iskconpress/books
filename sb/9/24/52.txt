====== SB 9.24.52 ======

===== Text =====

> devarakṣitayā labdhā
> nava cātra gadādayaḥ
> vasudevaḥ sutān aṣṭāv
> ādadhe sahadevayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devarakṣitayā]]---by the wife named Devarakṣitā; [[synonyms:l:labdhāḥ]]---achieved; [[synonyms:n:nava]]---nine; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:atra]]---here; [[synonyms:g:gadā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---sons headed by Gadā; [[synonyms:v:vasudevaḥ]]---Śrīla Vasudeva; [[synonyms:s:sutān]]---sons; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭau]]---eight; [[synonyms:ā:ādadhe]]---begot; [[synonyms:s:sahadevayā]]---in the wife named Sahadevā.

===== Translation =====

**By the semen of Vasudeva in the womb of Devarakṣitā, nine sons were born, headed by Gadā. Vasudeva, who was religion personified, also had a wife named Sahadevā, by whose womb he begot eight sons, headed by Śruta and Pravara.**
