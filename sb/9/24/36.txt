====== SB 9.24.36 ======

===== Text =====

> taṁ sātyajan nadī-toye
> kṛcchrāl lokasya bibhyatī
> prapitāmahas tām uvāha
> pāṇḍur vai satya-vikramaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---that child; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---she (Kuntī); [[synonyms:a:atyajat]]---gave up; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]-[[synonyms:t:toye]]---in the water of the river; [[synonyms:k:kṛcchrāt]]---with great repentance; [[synonyms:l:lokasya]]---of the people in general; [[synonyms:b:bibhyatī]]---fearing; [[synonyms:p:prapitāmahaḥ]]---(your) great-grandfather; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---her (Kuntī); [[synonyms:u:uvāha]]---married; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍuḥ]]---the king known as Pāṇḍu; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:satya]]-[[synonyms:v:vikramaḥ]]---very pious and chivalrous.

===== Translation =====

**Because Kuntī feared people's criticisms, with great difficulty she had to give up her affection for her child. Unwillingly, she packed the child in a basket and let it float down the waters of the river. O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, your great-grandfather the pious and chivalrous King Pāṇḍu later married Kuntī.**
