====== SB 9.24.44 ======

===== Text =====

> sumitrārjunapālādīn
> samīkāt tu sudāmanī
> ānakaḥ karṇikāyāṁ vai
> ṛtadhāmā-jayāv api

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sumitra]]---Sumitra; [[synonyms:a:arjunapāla]]---Arjunapāla; [[synonyms:ā:ādīn]]---headed by; [[synonyms:s:samīkāt]]---from King Śamīka; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:sudāmanī]]---in the womb of Sudāmanī, his wife; [[synonyms:ā:ānakaḥ]]---King Ānaka; [[synonyms:k:karṇikāyām]]---in the womb of his wife Karṇikā; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtadhāmā]]---Ṛtadhāmā; [[synonyms:j:jayau]]---and Jaya; [[synonyms:a:api]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**From Śamīka, by the womb of his wife, Sudāmanī, came Sumitra, Arjunapāla and other sons. King Ānaka, by his wife, Karṇikā, begot two sons, namely Ṛtadhāmā and Jaya.**
