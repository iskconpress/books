====== SB 9.24.3-4 ======

===== Text =====

> krathasya kuntiḥ putro 'bhūd
> vṛṣṇis tasyātha nirvṛtiḥ
> tato daśārho nāmnābhūt
> tasya vyomaḥ sutas tataḥ
>
> jīmūto vikṛtis tasya
> yasya bhīmarathaḥ sutaḥ
> tato navarathaḥ putro
> jāto daśarathas tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:krathasya]]---of Kratha; [[synonyms:k:kuntiḥ]]---Kunti; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---was born; [[synonyms:v:vṛṣṇiḥ]]---Vṛṣṇi; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:n:nirvṛtiḥ]]---Nirvṛti; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:d:daśārhaḥ]]---Daśārha; [[synonyms:n:nāmnā]]---by name; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---was born; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him; [[synonyms:v:vyomaḥ]]---Vyoma; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:j:jīmūtaḥ]]---Jīmūta; [[synonyms:v:vikṛtiḥ]]---Vikṛti; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his (Jīmūta's son); [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom (Vikṛti); [[synonyms:b:bhīmarathaḥ]]---Bhīmaratha; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Bhīmaratha); [[synonyms:n:navarathaḥ]]---Navaratha; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:j:jātaḥ]]---was born; [[synonyms:d:daśarathaḥ]]---Daśaratha; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Kratha was Kunti; the son of Kunti, Vṛṣṇi; the son of Vṛṣṇi, Nirvṛti; and the son of Nirvṛti, Daśārha. From Daśārha came Vyoma; from Vyoma came Jīmūta; from Jīmūta, Vikṛti; from Vikṛti, Bhīmaratha; from Bhīmaratha, Navaratha; and from Navaratha, Daśaratha.**
