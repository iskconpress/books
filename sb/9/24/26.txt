====== SB 9.24.26 ======

===== Text =====

> śūro vidūrathād āsīd
> bhajamānas tu tat-sutaḥ
> śinis tasmāt svayaṁ bhojo
> hṛdikas tat-suto mataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śūraḥ]]---Śūra; [[synonyms:v:vidūrathāt]]---from Vidūratha, the son of Citraratha; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---was born; [[synonyms:b:bhajamānaḥ]]---Bhajamāna; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---and; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of him (Śūra); [[synonyms:ś:śiniḥ]]---Śini; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhojaḥ]]---the famous King Bhoja; [[synonyms:h:hṛdikaḥ]]---Hṛdika; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of him (Bhoja); [[synonyms:m:mataḥ]]---is celebrated.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Citraratha was Vidūratha, the son of Vidūratha was Śūra, and his son was Bhajamāna. The son of Bhajamāna was Śini, the son of Śini was Bhoja, and the son of Bhoja was Hṛdika.**
