====== SB 9.24.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> tasyāṁ vidarbho 'janayat
> putrau nāmnā kuśa-krathau
> tṛtīyaṁ romapādaṁ ca
> vidarbha-kula-nandanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:tasyām]]---in that girl; [[synonyms:v:vidarbhaḥ]]---the son born of Śaibyā named Vidarbha; [[synonyms:a:ajanayat]]---gave birth; [[synonyms:p:putrau]]---to two sons; [[synonyms:n:nāmnā]]---by the name; [[synonyms:k:kuśa]]-[[synonyms:k:krathau]]---Kuśa and Kratha; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīyam]]---and a third son; [[synonyms:r:romapādam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---Romapāda also; [[synonyms:v:vidarbha]]-[[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:n:nandanam]]---the favorite in the dynasty of Vidarbha.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: By the womb of the girl brought by his father, Vidarbha begot three sons, named Kuśa, Kratha and Romapāda. Romapāda was the favorite in the dynasty of Vidarbha.**
