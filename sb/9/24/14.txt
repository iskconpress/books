====== SB 9.24.14 ======

===== Text =====

> yuyudhānaḥ sātyakir vai
> jayas tasya kuṇis tataḥ
> yugandharo 'namitrasya
> vṛṣṇiḥ putro 'paras tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yuyudhānaḥ]]---Yuyudhāna; [[synonyms:s:sātyakiḥ]]---the son of Satyaka; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:j:jayaḥ]]---Jaya; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Yuyudhāna); [[synonyms:k:kuṇiḥ]]---Kuṇi; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Jaya); [[synonyms:y:yugandharaḥ]]---Yugandhara; [[synonyms:a:anamitrasya]]---a son of Anamitra; [[synonyms:v:vṛṣṇiḥ]]---Vṛṣṇi; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:a:aparaḥ]]---other; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Satyaka was Yuyudhāna, whose son was Jaya. From Jaya came a son named Kuṇi and from Kuṇi a son named Yugandhara. Another son of Anamitra was Vṛṣṇi.**
