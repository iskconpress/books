====== SB 9.24.25 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṁsā kaṁsavatī kaṅkā
> śūrabhū rāṣṭrapālikā
> ugrasena-duhitaro
> vasudevānuja-striyaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṁsā]]---Kaṁsā; [[synonyms:k:kaṁsavatī]]---Kaṁsavatī; [[synonyms:k:kaṅkā]]---Kaṅkā; [[synonyms:ś:śūrabhū]]---Śūrabhū; [[synonyms:r:rāṣṭrapālikā]]---Rāṣṭrapālikā; [[synonyms:u:ugrasena]]-[[synonyms:d:duhitaraḥ]]---the daughters of Ugrasena; [[synonyms:v:vasudeva]]-[[synonyms:a:anuja]]---of the younger brothers of Vasudeva; [[synonyms:s:striyaḥ]]---the wives.

===== Translation =====

**Kaṁsā, Kaṁsavatī, Kaṅkā, Śūrabhū and Rāṣṭrapālikā were the daughters of Ugrasena. They became the wives of Vasudeva's younger brothers.**
