====== SB 9.24.41 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṁsavatyāṁ devaśravasaḥ
> suvīra iṣumāṁs tathā
> bakaḥ kaṅkāt tu kaṅkāyāṁ
> satyajit purujit tathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṁsavatyām]]---in the womb of Kaṁsavatī; [[synonyms:d:devaśravasaḥ]]---from Devaśravā, a brother of Vasudeva's; [[synonyms:s:suvīraḥ]]---Suvīra; [[synonyms:i:iṣumān]]---Iṣumān; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well as; [[synonyms:b:bakaḥ]]---Baka; [[synonyms:k:kaṅkāt]]---from Kaṅka; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:k:kaṅkāyām]]---in his wife, named Kaṅkā; [[synonyms:s:satyajit]]---Satyajit; [[synonyms:p:purujit]]---Purujit; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well as.

===== Translation =====

**Vasudeva's brother named Devaśravā married Kaṁsavatī, by whom he begot two sons, named Suvīra and Iṣumān. Kaṅka, by his wife Kaṅkā, begot three sons, named Baka, Satyajit and Purujit.**
