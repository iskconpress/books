====== SB 9.24.42 ======

===== Text =====

> sṛñjayo rāṣṭrapālyāṁ ca
> vṛṣa-durmarṣaṇādikān
> harikeśa-hiraṇyākṣau
> śūrabhūmyāṁ ca śyāmakaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sṛñjayaḥ]]---Sṛñjaya; [[synonyms:r:rāṣṭrapālyām]]---through his wife, Rāṣṭrapālikā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vṛṣa]]-[[synonyms:d:durmarṣaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādikān]]---begot sons headed by Vṛṣa and Durmarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:h:harikeśa]]---Harikeśa; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇyākṣau]]---and Hiraṇyākṣa; [[synonyms:ś:śūrabhūmyām]]---in the womb of Śūrabhūmi; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śyāmakaḥ]]---King Śyāmaka.

===== Translation =====

**King Sṛñjaya, by his wife, Rāṣṭrapālikā, begot sons headed by Vṛṣa and Durmarṣaṇa. King Śyāmaka, by his wife, Śūrabhūmi, begot two sons, named Harikeśa and Hiraṇyākṣa.**
