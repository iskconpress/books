====== SB 9.24.50 ======

===== Text =====

> vipṛṣṭho dhṛtadevāyām
> eka ānakadundubheḥ
> śāntidevātmajā rājan
> praśama-prasitādayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vipṛṣṭhaḥ]]---Vipṛṣṭha; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtadevāyām]]---in the womb of the wife named Dhṛtadevā; [[synonyms:e:ekaḥ]]---one son; [[synonyms:ā:ānakadundubheḥ]]---of Ānakadundubhi, Vasudeva; [[synonyms:ś:śāntidevā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajāḥ]]---the sons of another wife, named Śāntidevā; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:p:praśama]]-[[synonyms:p:prasita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---Praśama, Prasita and other sons.

===== Translation =====

**From the womb of Dhṛtadevā, one of the wives of Ānakadundubhi [Vasudeva], came a son named Vipṛṣṭha. The sons of Śāntidevā, another wife of Vasudeva, were Praśama, Prasita and others.**
