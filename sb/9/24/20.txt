====== SB 9.24.20 ======

===== Text =====

> kapotaromā tasyānuḥ
> sakhā yasya ca tumburuḥ
> andhakād dundubhis tasmād
> avidyotaḥ punarvasuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kapotaromā]]---Kapotaromā; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his (son); [[synonyms:a:anuḥ]]---Anu; [[synonyms:s:sakhā]]---friend; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---whose; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tumburuḥ]]---Tumburu; [[synonyms:a:andhakāt]]---of Andhaka, the son of Anu; [[synonyms:d:dundubhiḥ]]---a son named Dundubhi; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Dundubhi); [[synonyms:a:avidyotaḥ]]---a son named Avidyota; [[synonyms:p:punarvasuḥ]]---a son named Punarvasu.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Vilomā was Kapotaromā, and his son was Anu, whose friend was Tumburu. From Anu came Andhaka; from Andhaka, Dundubhi; and from Dundubhi, Avidyota. From Avidyota came a son named Punarvasu.**
