====== SB 9.24.9 ======

===== Text =====

> babhrur devāvṛdha-sutas
> tayoḥ ślokau paṭhanty amū
> yathaiva śṛṇumo dūrāt
> sampaśyāmas tathāntikāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:babhruḥ]]---Babhru; [[synonyms:d:devāvṛdha]]---of Devāvṛdha; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:t:tayoḥ]]---of them; [[synonyms:ś:ślokau]]---two verses; [[synonyms:p:paṭhanti]]---all the members of the old generation recite; [[synonyms:a:amū]]---those; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇumaḥ]]---we have heard; [[synonyms:d:dūrāt]]---from a distance; [[synonyms:s:sampaśyāmaḥ]]---are actually seeing; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---similarly; [[synonyms:a:antikāt]]---presently also.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Devāvṛdha was Babhru. Concerning Devāvṛdha and Babhru there are two famous songs of prayer, which were sung by our predecessors and which we have heard from a distance. Even now I hear the same prayers about their qualities [because that which was heard before is still sung continuously].**
