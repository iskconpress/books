====== SB 9.24.60 ======

===== Text =====

> karmāṇy aparimeyāṇi
> manasāpi sureśvaraiḥ
> saha-saṅkarṣaṇaś cakre
> bhagavān madhusūdanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karmāṇi]]---activities; [[synonyms:a:aparimeyāṇi]]---immeasurable, unlimited; [[synonyms:m:manasā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even by such plans perceived within the mind; [[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaraiḥ]]---by the controllers of the universe like Brahmā and Śiva; [[synonyms:s:saha]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇaḥ]]---along with Saṅkarṣaṇa (Baladeva); [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---performed; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sūdanaḥ]]---the killer of the Madhu demon.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa, with the cooperation of Saṅkarṣaṇa, Balarāma, performed activities beyond the mental comprehension of even such personalities as Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva. [For instance, Kṛṣṇa arranged the Battle of Kurukṣetra to kill many demons for the relief of the entire world.]**
