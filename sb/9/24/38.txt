====== SB 9.24.38 ======

===== Text =====

> kaikeyo dhṛṣṭaketuś ca
> śrutakīrtim avindata
> santardanādayas tasyāṁ
> pañcāsan kaikayāḥ sutāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaikeyaḥ]]---the King of Kekaya; [[synonyms:d:dhṛṣṭaketuḥ]]---Dhṛṣṭaketu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ś:śrutakīrtim]]---a sister of Kuntī's named Śrutakīrti; [[synonyms:a:avindata]]---married; [[synonyms:s:santardana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---headed by Santardana; [[synonyms:t:tasyām]]---through her (Śrutakīrti); [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---there were; [[synonyms:k:kaikayāḥ]]---the sons of the King of Kekaya; [[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---sons.

===== Translation =====

**King Dhṛṣṭaketu, the King of Kekaya, married Śrutakīrti, another sister of Kuntī's. Śrutakīrti had five sons, headed by Santardana.**
