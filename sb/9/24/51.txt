====== SB 9.24.51 ======

===== Text =====

> rājanya-kalpa-varṣādyā
> upadevā-sutā daśa
> vasu-haṁsa-suvaṁśādyāḥ
> śrīdevāyās tu ṣaṭ sutāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājanya]]---Rājanya; [[synonyms:k:kalpa]]---Kalpa; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---Varṣa and others; [[synonyms:u:upadevā]]-[[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---sons of Upadevā, another wife of Vasudeva's; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten; [[synonyms:v:vasu]]---Vasu; [[synonyms:h:haṁsa]]---Haṁsa; [[synonyms:s:suvaṁśa]]---Suvaṁśa; [[synonyms:ā:ādyāḥ]]---and others; [[synonyms:ś:śrīdevāyāḥ]]---born of another wife, named Śrīdevā; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]---six; [[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---sons.

===== Translation =====

**Vasudeva also had a wife named Upadevā, from whom came ten sons, headed by Rājanya, Kalpa and Varṣa. From Śrīdevā, another wife, came six sons, such as Vasu, Haṁsa and Suvaṁśa.**
