====== SB 9.24.43 ======

===== Text =====

> miśrakeśyām apsarasi
> vṛkādīn vatsakas tathā
> takṣa-puṣkara-śālādīn
> durvākṣyāṁ vṛka ādadhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśrakeśyām]]---in the womb of Miśrakeśī; [[synonyms:a:apsarasi]]---who belonged to the Apsarā group; [[synonyms:v:vṛka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādīn]]---Vṛka and other sons; [[synonyms:v:vatsakaḥ]]---Vatsaka; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well; [[synonyms:t:takṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:puṣkara]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāla]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādīn]]---sons headed by Takṣa, Puṣkara and Śāla; [[synonyms:d:durvākṣyām]]---in the womb of his wife, Durvākṣī; [[synonyms:v:vṛkaḥ]]---Vṛka; [[synonyms:ā:ādadhe]]---begot.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, King Vatsaka, by the womb of his wife, Miśrakeśī, who was an Apsarā, begot sons headed by Vṛka. Vṛka, by his wife, Durvākṣī, begot Takṣa, Puṣkara, Śāla and so on.**
