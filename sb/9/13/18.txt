====== SB 9.13.18 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ śīradhvajo jajñe
> yajñārthaṁ karṣato mahīm
> sītā śīrāgrato jātā
> tasmāt śīradhvajaḥ smṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Hrasvaromā; [[synonyms:ś:śīradhvajaḥ]]---a son named Śīradhvaja; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---was born; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]-[[synonyms:a:artham]]---for performing sacrifices; [[synonyms:k:karṣataḥ]]---while plowing the field; [[synonyms:m:mahīm]]---the earth; [[synonyms:s:sītā]]---mother Sītā, the wife of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:ś:śīra]]-[[synonyms:a:agrataḥ]]---from the front portion of the plow; [[synonyms:j:jātā]]---was born; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ś:śīradhvajaḥ]]---was known as Śīradhvaja; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---celebrated.

===== Translation =====

**From Hrasvaromā came a son named Śīradhvaja [also called Janaka]. When Śīradhvaja was plowing a field, from the front of his plow [śīra] appeared a daughter named Sītādevī, who later became the wife of Lord Rāmacandra. Thus he was known as Śīradhvaja.**
