====== SB 9.13.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmād bṛhadrathas tasya
> mahāvīryaḥ sudhṛt-pitā
> sudhṛter dhṛṣṭaketur vai
> haryaśvo 'tha marus tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Devarāta; [[synonyms:b:bṛhadrathaḥ]]---a son named Bṛhadratha; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Bṛhadratha); [[synonyms:m:mahāvīryaḥ]]---a son named Mahāvīrya; [[synonyms:s:sudhṛt]]-[[synonyms:p:pitā]]---he became the father of King Sudhṛti; [[synonyms:s:sudhṛteḥ]]---from Sudhṛti; [[synonyms:d:dhṛṣṭaketuḥ]]---a son named Dhṛṣṭaketu; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:h:haryaśvaḥ]]---his son was Haryaśva; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:maruḥ]]---Maru; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter.

===== Translation =====

**From Devarāta came a son named Bṛhadratha and from Bṛhadratha a son named Mahāvīrya, who became the father of Sudhṛti. The son of Sudhṛti was known as Dhṛṣṭaketu, and from Dhṛṣṭaketu came Haryaśva. From Haryaśva came a son named Maru.**
