====== SB 9.13.16 ======

===== Text =====

> maroḥ pratīpakas tasmāj
> jātaḥ kṛtaratho yataḥ
> devamīḍhas tasya putro
> viśruto 'tha mahādhṛtiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maroḥ]]---of Maru; [[synonyms:p:pratīpakaḥ]]---a son named Pratīpaka; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Pratīpaka; [[synonyms:j:jātaḥ]]---was born; [[synonyms:k:kṛtarathaḥ]]---a son named Kṛtaratha; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---and from Kṛtaratha; [[synonyms:d:devamīḍhaḥ]]---Devamīḍha; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Devamīḍha; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:v:viśrutaḥ]]---Viśruta; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---from him; [[synonyms:m:mahādhṛtiḥ]]---a son named Mahādhṛti.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Maru was Pratīpaka, and the son of Pratīpaka was Kṛtaratha. From Kṛtaratha came Devamīḍha; from Devamīḍha, Viśruta; and from Viśruta, Mahādhṛti.**
