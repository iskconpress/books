====== SB 9.13.17 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛtirātas tatas tasmān
> mahāromā ca tat-sutaḥ
> svarṇaromā sutas tasya
> hrasvaromā vyajāyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛtirātaḥ]]---Kṛtirāta; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Mahādhṛti; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Kṛtirāta; [[synonyms:m:mahāromā]]---a son named Mahāromā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:s:svarṇaromā]]---Svarṇaromā; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his son; [[synonyms:h:hrasvaromā]]---Hrasvaromā; [[synonyms:v:vyajāyata]]---were all born.

===== Translation =====

**From Mahādhṛti was born a son named Kṛtirāta, from Kṛtirāta was born Mahāromā, from Mahāromā came a son named Svarṇaromā, and from Svarṇaromā came Hrasvaromā.**
