====== SB 9.13.19 ======

===== Text =====

> kuśadhvajas tasya putras
> tato dharmadhvajo nṛpaḥ
> dharmadhvajasya dvau putrau
> kṛtadhvaja-mitadhvajau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuśadhvajaḥ]]---Kuśadhvaja; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Śīradhvaja; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:d:dharmadhvajaḥ]]---Dharmadhvaja; [[synonyms:n:nṛpaḥ]]---the king; [[synonyms:d:dharmadhvajasya]]---from this Dharmadhvaja; [[synonyms:d:dvau]]---two; [[synonyms:p:putrau]]---sons; [[synonyms:k:kṛtadhvaja]]-[[synonyms:m:mitadhvajau]]---Kṛtadhvaja and Mitadhvaja.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Śīradhvaja was Kuśadhvaja, and the son of Kuśadhvaja was King Dharmadhvaja, who had two sons, namely Kṛtadhvaja and Mitadhvaja.**
