====== SB 9.13.7 ======

===== Text =====

> gandha-vastuṣu tad-dehaṁ
> nidhāya muni-sattamāḥ
> samāpte satra-yāge ca
> devān ūcuḥ samāgatān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gandha]]-[[synonyms:v:vastuṣu]]---in things very fragrant; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:d:deham]]---the body of Mahārāja Nimi; [[synonyms:n:nidhāya]]---having preserved; [[synonyms:m:muni]]-[[synonyms:s:sattamāḥ]]---all the great sages gathered there; [[synonyms:s:samāpte]] [[synonyms:s:satra]]-[[synonyms:y:yāge]]---at the end of the sacrifice known by the name Satra; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:devān]]---to all the demigods; [[synonyms:ū:ūcuḥ]]---requested or spoke; [[synonyms:s:samāgatān]]---who were assembled there.

===== Translation =====

**During the performance of the yajña, the body relinquished by Mahārāja Nimi was preserved in fragrant substances, and at the end of the Satra-yāga the great saints and brāhmaṇas made the following request to all the demigods assembled there.**
