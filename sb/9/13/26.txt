====== SB 9.13.26 ======

===== Text =====

> śunakas tat-suto jajñe
> vītahavyo dhṛtis tataḥ
> bahulāśvo dhṛtes tasya
> kṛtir asya mahāvaśī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śunakaḥ]]---Śunaka; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Ṛta; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---was born; [[synonyms:v:vītahavyaḥ]]---Vītahavya; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtiḥ]]---Dhṛti; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---the son of Vītahavya; [[synonyms:b:bahulāśvaḥ]]---Bahulāśva; [[synonyms:d:dhṛteḥ]]---from Dhṛti; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his son; [[synonyms:k:kṛtiḥ]]---Kṛti; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of Kṛti; [[synonyms:m:mahāvaśī]]---there was a son named Mahāvaśī.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Ṛta was Śunaka, the son of Śunaka was Vītahavya, the son of Vītahavya was Dhṛti, and the son of Dhṛti was Bahulāśva. The son of Bahulāśva was Kṛti, and his son was Mahāvaśī.**
