====== SB 9.13.22 ======

===== Text =====

> śucis tu tanayas tasmāt
> sanadvājaḥ suto 'bhavat
> ūrjaketuḥ sanadvājād
> ajo 'tha purujit sutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuciḥ]]---Śuci; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:s:sanadvājaḥ]]---Sanadvāja; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was born; [[synonyms:ū:ūrjaketuḥ]]---Ūrjaketu; [[synonyms:s:sanadvājāt]]---from Sanadvāja; [[synonyms:a:ajaḥ]]---Aja; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:purujit]]---Purujit; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Śatadyumna was named Śuci. From Śuci, Sanadvāja was born, and from Sanadvāja came a son named Ūrjaketu. The son of Ūrjaketu was Aja, and the son of Aja was Purujit.**
