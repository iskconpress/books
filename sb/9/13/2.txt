====== SB 9.13.2 ======

===== Text =====

> taṁ nirvartyāgamiṣyāmi
> tāvan māṁ pratipālaya
> tūṣṇīm āsīd gṛha-patiḥ
> so 'pīndrasyākaron makham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---that sacrifice; [[synonyms:n:nirvartya]]---after finishing; [[synonyms:ā:āgamiṣyāmi]]---I shall come back; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]]---until that time; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---me (Vasiṣṭha); [[synonyms:p:pratipālaya]]---wait for; [[synonyms:t:tūṣṇīm]]---silent; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---remained; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---Mahārāja Nimi; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:i:indrasya]]---of Lord Indra; [[synonyms:a:akarot]]---executed; [[synonyms:m:makham]]---the sacrifice.

===== Translation =====

**"I shall return here after finishing the yajña for Indra. Kindly wait for me until then." Mahārāja Nimi remained silent, and Vasiṣṭha began to perform the sacrifice for Lord Indra.**
