~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.1~~

====== SB 9.13.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> nimir ikṣvāku-tanayo
> vasiṣṭham avṛtartvijam
> ārabhya satraṁ so 'py āha
> śakreṇa prāg vṛto 'smi bhoḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:n:nimiḥ]]---King Nimi; [[synonyms:i:ikṣvāku]]-[[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---the son of Mahārāja Ikṣvāku; [[synonyms:v:vasiṣṭham]]---the great sage Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:a:avṛta]]---appointed; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtvijam]]---the chief priest of the sacrifice; [[synonyms:ā:ārabhya]]---beginning; [[synonyms:s:satram]]---the sacrifice; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:ś:śakreṇa]]---by Lord Indra; [[synonyms:p:prāk]]---before; [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:asmi]]---I was appointed; [[synonyms:b:bhoḥ]]---O Mahārāja Nimi.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After beginning sacrifices, Mahārāja Nimi, the son of Ikṣvāku, requested the great sage Vasiṣṭha to take the post of chief priest. At that time, Vasiṣṭha replied, "My dear Mahārāja Nimi, I have already accepted the same post in a sacrifice begun by Lord Indra.**
