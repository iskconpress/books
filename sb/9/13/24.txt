====== SB 9.13.24 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmāt samarathas tasya
> sutaḥ satyarathas tataḥ
> āsīd upagurus tasmād
> upagupto 'gni-sambhavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Kṣemādhi; [[synonyms:s:samarathaḥ]]---a son named Samaratha; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---from Samaratha; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:s:satyarathaḥ]]---Satyaratha; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Satyaratha); [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---was born; [[synonyms:u:upaguruḥ]]---Upaguru; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:u:upaguptaḥ]]---Upagupta; [[synonyms:a:agni]]-[[synonyms:s:sambhavaḥ]]---a partial expansion of the demigod Agni.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Kṣemādhi was Samaratha, and his son was Satyaratha. The son of Satyaratha was Upaguru, and the son of Upaguru was Upagupta, a partial expansion of the fire-god.**
