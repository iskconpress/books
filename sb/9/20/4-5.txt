~~Title: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.20.4-5~~

====== SB 9.20.4-5 ======

===== Text =====

> ṛteyus tasya kakṣeyuḥ
> sthaṇḍileyuḥ kṛteyukaḥ
> jaleyuḥ sannateyuś ca
> dharma-satya-vrateyavaḥ
>
> daśaite 'psarasaḥ putrā
> vaneyuś cāvamaḥ smṛtaḥ
> ghṛtācyām indriyāṇīva
> mukhyasya jagad-ātmanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛteyuḥ]]---Ṛteyu; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Raudrāśva); [[synonyms:k:kakṣeyuḥ]]---Kakṣeyu; [[synonyms:s:sthaṇḍileyuḥ]]---Sthaṇḍileyu; [[synonyms:k:kṛteyukaḥ]]---Kṛteyuka; [[synonyms:j:jaleyuḥ]]---Jaleyu; [[synonyms:s:sannateyuḥ]]---Sannateyu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---Dharmeyu; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---Satyeyu; [[synonyms:v:vrateyavaḥ]]---and Vrateyu; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten; [[synonyms:e:ete]]---all of them; [[synonyms:a:apsarasaḥ]]---born of an Apsarā; [[synonyms:p:putrāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:v:vaneyuḥ]]---the son named Vaneyu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:avamaḥ]]---the youngest; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---known; [[synonyms:g:ghṛtācyām]]---Ghṛtācī; [[synonyms:i:indriyāṇi]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---exactly like the ten senses; [[synonyms:m:mukhyasya]]---of the living force; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---the living force of the entire universe.

===== Translation =====

**Raudrāśva had ten sons, named Ṛteyu, Kakṣeyu, Sthaṇḍileyu, Kṛteyuka, Jaleyu, Sannateyu, Dharmeyu, Satyeyu, Vrateyu and Vaneyu. Of these ten sons, Vaneyu was the youngest. As the ten senses, which are products of the universal life, act under the control of life, these ten sons of Raudrāśva acted under Raudrāśva's full control. All of them were born of the Apsarā named Ghṛtācī.**
