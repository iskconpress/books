====== SB 9.20.3 ======

===== Text =====

> tasya sudyur abhūt putras
> tasmād bahugavas tataḥ
> saṁyātis tasyāhaṁyātī
> raudrāśvas tat-sutaḥ smṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Cārupada); [[synonyms:s:sudyuḥ]]---by the name Sudyu; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---appeared; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Sudyu); [[synonyms:b:bahugavaḥ]]---a son named Bahugava; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:s:saṁyātiḥ]]---a son named Saṁyāti; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---and from him; [[synonyms:a:ahaṁyātiḥ]]---a son named Ahaṁyāti; [[synonyms:r:raudrāśvaḥ]]---Raudrāśva; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---well known.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Cārupada was Sudyu, and the son of Sudyu was Bahugava. Bahugava's son was Saṁyāti. From Saṁyāti came a son named Ahaṁyāti, from whom Raudrāśva was born.**
