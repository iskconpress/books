====== SB 9.20.11 ======

===== Text =====

> kā tvaṁ kamala-patrākṣi
> kasyāsi hṛdayaṅ-game
> kiṁ svic cikīrṣitaṁ tatra
> bhavatyā nirjane vane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kā]]---who; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---are you; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:p:patra]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣi]]---O beautiful woman with eyes like the petals of a lotus; [[synonyms:k:kasya]] [[synonyms:a:asi]]---with whom are you related; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayam]]-[[synonyms:g:game]]---O most beautiful one, pleasing to the heart; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:s:svit]]---what kind of business; [[synonyms:c:cikīrṣitam]]---is being thought of; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:b:bhavatyāḥ]]---by you; [[synonyms:n:nirjane]]---solitary; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forest.

===== Translation =====

**O beautiful lotus-eyed woman, who are you? Whose daughter are you? What purpose do you have in this solitary forest? Why are you staying here?**
