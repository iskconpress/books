====== SB 9.20.12 ======

===== Text =====

> vyaktaṁ rājanya-tanayāṁ
> vedmy ahaṁ tvāṁ sumadhyame
> na hi cetaḥ pauravāṇām
> adharme ramate kvacit

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vyaktam]]---it appears; [[synonyms:r:rājanya]]-[[synonyms:t:tanayām]]---the daughter of a //kṣatriya//; [[synonyms:v:vedmi]]---can realize; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tvām]]---your good self; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:m:madhyame]]---O most beautiful; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:c:cetaḥ]]---the mind; [[synonyms:p:pauravāṇām]]---of persons who have taken birth in the Pūru dynasty; [[synonyms:a:adharme]]---in irreligion; [[synonyms:r:ramate]]---enjoys; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---at any time.

===== Translation =====

**O most beautiful one, it appears to my mind that you must be the daughter of a kṣatriya. Because I belong to the Pūru dynasty, my mind never endeavors to enjoy anything irreligiously.**

===== Purport =====

Mahārāja Duṣmanta indirectly expressed his desire to marry Śakuntalā, for she appeared to his mind to be the daughter of some //kṣatriya// king.
