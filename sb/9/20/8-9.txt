====== SB 9.20.8-9 ======

===== Text =====

> duṣmanto mṛgayāṁ yātaḥ
> kaṇvāśrama-padaṁ gataḥ
> tatrāsīnāṁ sva-prabhayā
> maṇḍayantīṁ ramām iva
>
> vilokya sadyo mumuhe
> deva-māyām iva striyam
> babhāṣe tāṁ varārohāṁ
> bhaṭaiḥ katipayair vṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṣmantaḥ]]---Mahārāja Duṣmanta; [[synonyms:m:mṛgayām]] [[synonyms:y:yātaḥ]]---when he went hunting; [[synonyms:k:kaṇva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrama]]-[[synonyms:p:padam]]---to the residence of Kaṇva; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---he came; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āsīnām]]---a woman sitting; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhayā]]---by her own beauty; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍayantīm]]---illuminating; [[synonyms:r:ramām]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---exactly like the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:v:vilokya]]---by observing; [[synonyms:s:sadyaḥ]]---immediately; [[synonyms:m:mumuhe]]---he became enchanted; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:m:māyām]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---exactly like the illusory energy of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:striyam]]---a beautiful woman; [[synonyms:b:babhāṣe]]---he addressed; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---her (the woman); [[synonyms:v:vara]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārohām]]---who was the best of beautiful women; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭaiḥ]]---by soldiers; [[synonyms:k:katipayaiḥ]]---a few; [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]]---surrounded.

===== Translation =====

**Once when King Duṣmanta went to the forest to hunt and was very much fatigued, he approached the residence of Kaṇva Muni. There he saw a most beautiful woman who looked exactly like the goddess of fortune and who sat there illuminating the entire āśrama by her effulgence. The King was naturally attracted by her beauty, and therefore he approached her, accompanied by some of his soldiers, and spoke to her.**
