====== SB 9.20.18 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṇvaḥ kumārasya vane
> cakre samucitāḥ kriyāḥ
> baddhvā mṛgendraṁ tarasā
> krīḍati sma sa bālakaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṇvaḥ]]---Kaṇva Muni; [[synonyms:k:kumārasya]]---of the son born of Śakuntalā; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---executed; [[synonyms:s:samucitāḥ]]---prescribed; [[synonyms:k:kriyāḥ]]---ritualistic ceremonies; [[synonyms:b:baddhvā]]---capturing; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:i:indram]]---a lion; [[synonyms:t:tarasā]]---by force; [[synonyms:k:krīḍati]]---playing; [[synonyms:s:sma]]---in the past; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bālakaḥ]]---the child.

===== Translation =====

**In the forest, Kaṇva Muni performed all the ritualistic ceremonies concerning the newborn child. Later, the boy became so powerful that he would capture a lion and play with it.**
