====== SB 9.20.29 ======

===== Text =====

> bharatasya mahat karma
> na pūrve nāpare nṛpāḥ
> naivāpur naiva prāpsyanti
> bāhubhyāṁ tridivaṁ yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bharatasya]]---of Mahārāja Bharata, the son of Mahārāja Duṣmanta; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]---very great, exalted; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activities; [[synonyms:n:na]]---neither; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:n:na]]---nor; [[synonyms:a:apare]]---after his time; [[synonyms:n:nṛpāḥ]]---kings as a class; [[synonyms:n:na]]---neither; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:āpuḥ]]---attained; [[synonyms:n:na]]---nor; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:prāpsyanti]]---will get; [[synonyms:b:bāhubhyām]]---by the strength of his arms; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:d:divam]]---the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as.

===== Translation =====

**As one cannot approach the heavenly planets simply by the strength of his arms (for who can touch the heavenly planets with his hands?), one cannot imitate the wonderful activities of Mahārāja Bharata. No one could perform such activities in the past, nor will anyone be able to do so in the future.**
