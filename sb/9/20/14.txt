====== SB 9.20.14 ======

===== Text =====

> āsyatāṁ hy aravindākṣa
> gṛhyatām arhaṇaṁ ca naḥ
> bhujyatāṁ santi nīvārā
> uṣyatāṁ yadi rocate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsyatām]]---please come sit here; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:aravinda]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣa]]---O great hero with eyes like the petals of a lotus; [[synonyms:g:gṛhyatām]]---please accept; [[synonyms:a:arhaṇam]]---humble reception; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:b:bhujyatām]]---please eat; [[synonyms:s:santi]]---what there is in stock; [[synonyms:n:nīvārāḥ]]---//nīvārā// rice; [[synonyms:u:uṣyatām]]---stay here; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:r:rocate]]---you so desire.

===== Translation =====

**O King with eyes like the petals of a lotus, kindly come sit down and accept whatever reception we can offer. We have a supply of nīvārā rice that you may kindly take. And if you so desire, stay here without hesitation.**
