====== SB 9.20.6 ======

===== Text =====

> ṛteyo rantināvo 'bhūt
> trayas tasyātmajā nṛpa
> sumatir dhruvo 'pratirathaḥ
> kaṇvo 'pratirathātmajaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛteyoḥ]]---from the son named Ṛteyu; [[synonyms:r:rantināvaḥ]]---the son named Rantināva; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---appeared; [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]]---three; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his (Rantināva's); [[synonyms:ā:ātmajāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:sumatiḥ]]---Sumati; [[synonyms:d:dhruvaḥ]]---Dhruva; [[synonyms:a:apratirathaḥ]]---Apratiratha; [[synonyms:k:kaṇvaḥ]]---Kaṇva; [[synonyms:a:apratiratha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmajaḥ]]---the son of Apratiratha.

===== Translation =====

**Ṛteyu had a son named Rantināva, who had three sons, named Sumati, Dhruva and Apratiratha. Apratiratha had only one son, whose name was Kaṇva.**
