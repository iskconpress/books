====== SB 9.20.19 ======

===== Text =====

> taṁ duratyaya-vikrāntam
> ādāya pramadottamā
> harer aṁśāṁśa-sambhūtaṁ
> bhartur antikam āgamat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:d:duratyaya]]-[[synonyms:v:vikrāntam]]---whose strength was insurmountable; [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---taking with her; [[synonyms:p:pramadā]]-[[synonyms:u:uttamā]]---the best of women, Śakuntalā; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of God; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]-[[synonyms:s:sambhūtam]]---a partial plenary incarnation; [[synonyms:b:bhartuḥ]] [[synonyms:a:antikam]]---unto her husband; [[synonyms:ā:āgamat]]---approached.

===== Translation =====

**Śakuntalā, the best of beautiful women, along with her son, whose strength was insurmountable and who was a partial expansion of the Supreme Godhead, approached her husband, Duṣmanta.**
