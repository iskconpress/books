====== SB 9.8.26 ======

===== Text =====

> adya naḥ sarva-bhūtātman
> kāma-karmendriyāśayaḥ
> moha-pāśo dṛḍhaś chinno
> bhagavaṁs tava darśanāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adya]]---today; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātman]]---O You, who are the Supersoul; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśayaḥ]]---being under the influence of lusty desires and fruitive activities; [[synonyms:m:moha]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśaḥ]]---this hard knot of illusion; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍhaḥ]]---very strong; [[synonyms:c:chinnaḥ]]---broken; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O my Lord; [[synonyms:t:tava]] [[synonyms:d:darśanāt]]---simply by seeing You.

===== Translation =====

**O Supersoul of all living entities, O Personality of Godhead, simply by seeing You I have now been freed from all lusty desires, which are the root cause of insurmountable illusion and bondage in the material world.**
