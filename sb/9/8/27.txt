====== SB 9.8.27 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> itthaṁ gītānubhāvas taṁ
> bhagavān kapilo muniḥ
> aṁśumantam uvācedam
> anugrāhya dhiyā nṛpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:i:ittham]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhāvaḥ]]---whose glories are described; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kapilaḥ]]---named Kapila Muni; [[synonyms:m:muniḥ]]---the great sage; [[synonyms:a:aṁśumantam]]---unto Aṁśumān; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:anugrāhya]]---being very merciful; [[synonyms:d:dhiyā]]---with the path of knowledge; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King Parīkṣit.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, when Aṁśumān had glorified the Lord in this way, the great sage Kapila, the powerful incarnation of Viṣṇu, being very merciful to him, explained to him the path of knowledge.**
