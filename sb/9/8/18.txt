====== SB 9.8.18 ======

===== Text =====

> ayodhyā-vāsinaḥ sarve
> bālakān punar āgatān
> dṛṣṭvā visismire rājan
> rājā cāpy anvatapyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ayodhyā]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsinaḥ]]---the inhabitants of Ayodhyā; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:bālakān]]---their sons; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ā:āgatān]]---having come back to life; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---after seeing this; [[synonyms:v:visismire]]---became astounded; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---King Sagara; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:api]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:anvatapyata]]---very much lamented (the absence of his son).

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, when all the inhabitants of Ayodhyā saw that their boys had come back to life, they were astounded, and King Sagara greatly lamented the absence of his son.**
