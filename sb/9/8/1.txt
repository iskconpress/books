====== SB 9.8.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> harito rohita-sutaś
> campas tasmād vinirmitā
> campāpurī sudevo 'to
> vijayo yasya cātmajaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:h:haritaḥ]]---the king named Harita; [[synonyms:r:rohita]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of King Rohita; [[synonyms:c:campaḥ]]---by the name Campa; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Harita; [[synonyms:v:vinirmitā]]---was constructed; [[synonyms:c:campā]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---the township known as Campāpurī; [[synonyms:s:sudevaḥ]]---by the name Sudeva; [[synonyms:a:ataḥ]]---thereafter (from Campa); [[synonyms:v:vijayaḥ]]---by the name Vijaya; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom (Sudeva); [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:j:jaḥ]]---the son.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: The son of Rohita was Harita, and Harita's son was Campa, who constructed the town of Campāpurī. The son of Campa was Sudeva, and his son was Vijaya.**
