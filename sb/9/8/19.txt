====== SB 9.8.19 ======

===== Text =====

> aṁśumāṁś codito rājñā
> turagānveṣaṇe yayau
> pitṛvya-khātānupathaṁ
> bhasmānti dadṛśe hayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṁśumān]]---the son of Asamañjasa; [[synonyms:c:coditaḥ]]---being ordered; [[synonyms:r:rājñā]]---by the King; [[synonyms:t:turaga]]---the horse; [[synonyms:a:anveṣaṇe]]---to search for; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---went out; [[synonyms:p:pitṛvya]]-[[synonyms:k:khāta]]---as described by his father's brothers; [[synonyms:a:anupatham]]---following that path; [[synonyms:b:bhasma]]-[[synonyms:a:anti]]---near the stack of ashes; [[synonyms:d:dadṛśe]]---he saw; [[synonyms:h:hayam]]---the horse.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Aṁśumān, the grandson of Mahārāja Sagara, was ordered by the King to search for the horse. Following the same path traversed by his uncles, Aṁśumān gradually reached the stack of ashes and found the horse nearby.**
