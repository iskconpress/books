====== SB 9.8.3 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛddhaṁ taṁ pañcatāṁ prāptaṁ
> mahiṣy anumariṣyatī
> aurveṇa jānatātmānaṁ
> prajāvantaṁ nivāritā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛddham]]---when he was old; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:p:pañcatām]]---death; [[synonyms:p:prāptam]]---who had obtained; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣī]]---the queen; [[synonyms:a:anumariṣyatī]]---who wanted to die with him and become //satī//; [[synonyms:a:aurveṇa]]---by the great sage Aurva; [[synonyms:j:jānatā]]---understanding that; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---the body of the queen; [[synonyms:p:prajā]]-[[synonyms:v:vantam]]---bore a son within the womb; [[synonyms:n:nivāritā]]---was forbidden.

===== Translation =====

**Bāhuka died when he was old, and one of his wives wanted to die with him, following the satī rite. At that time, however, Aurva Muni, knowing her to be pregnant, forbade her to die.**
