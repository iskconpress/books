====== SB 9.4.51 ======

===== Text =====

> diśo nabhaḥ kṣmāṁ vivarān samudrān
> lokān sapālāṁs tridivaṁ gataḥ saḥ
> yato yato dhāvati tatra tatra
> sudarśanaṁ duṣprasahaṁ dadarśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:diśaḥ]]---all directions; [[synonyms:n:nabhaḥ]]---in the sky; [[synonyms:k:kṣmām]]---on the surface of the earth; [[synonyms:v:vivarān]]---within the holes; [[synonyms:s:samudrān]]---within the seas; [[synonyms:l:lokān]]---all places; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:pālān]]---as well as their rulers; [[synonyms:t:tridivam]]---the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---gone; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Durvāsā Muni; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]] [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---wheresoever; [[synonyms:d:dhāvati]]---he went; [[synonyms:t:tatra]] [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there, everywhere; [[synonyms:s:sudarśanam]]---the disc of the Lord; [[synonyms:d:duṣprasaham]]---extremely fearful; [[synonyms:d:dadarśa]]---Durvāsā Muni saw.

===== Translation =====

**Just to protect himself, Durvāsā Muni fled everywhere, in all directions---in the sky, on the surface of the earth, in caves, in the ocean, on different planets of the rulers of the three worlds, and even on the heavenly planets---but wherever he went he immediately saw following him the unbearable fire of the Sudarśana cakra.**
