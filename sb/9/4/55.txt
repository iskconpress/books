====== SB 9.4.55 ======

===== Text =====

> pratyākhyāto viriñcena
> viṣṇu-cakropatāpitaḥ
> durvāsāḥ śaraṇaṁ yātaḥ
> śarvaṁ kailāsa-vāsinam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratyākhyātaḥ]]---being refused; [[synonyms:v:viriñcena]]---by Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:c:cakra]]-[[synonyms:u:upatāpitaḥ]]---being scorched by the blazing fire of Lord Viṣṇu's disc; [[synonyms:d:durvāsāḥ]]---the great mystic named Durvāsā; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇam]]---f or shelter; [[synonyms:y:yātaḥ]]---went; [[synonyms:ś:śarvam]]---unto Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:k:kailāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsinam]]---the resident of the place known as Kailāsa.

===== Translation =====

**When Durvāsā, who was greatly afflicted by the blazing fire of the Sudarśana cakra, was thus refused by Lord Brahmā, he tried to take shelter of Lord Śiva, who always resides on his planet, known as Kailāsa.**
