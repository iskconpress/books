====== SB 9.4.36 ======

===== Text =====

> tam ānarcātithiṁ bhūpaḥ
> pratyutthānāsanārhaṇaiḥ
> yayāce 'bhyavahārāya
> pāda-mūlam upāgataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him (Durvāsā); [[synonyms:ā:ānarca]]---worshiped; [[synonyms:a:atithim]]---although an uninvited guest; [[synonyms:b:bhūpaḥ]]---the King (Ambarīṣa); [[synonyms:p:pratyutthāna]]---by standing up; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---by offering a seat; [[synonyms:a:arhaṇaiḥ]]---and by paraphernalia for worship; [[synonyms:y:yayāce]]---requested; [[synonyms:a:abhyavahārāya]]---for eating; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlam]]---at the root of his feet; [[synonyms:u:upāgataḥ]]---fell down.

===== Translation =====

**After standing up to receive Durvāsā Muni, King Ambarīṣa offered him a seat and paraphernalia of worship. Then, sitting at his feet, the King requested the great sage to eat.**
