====== SB 9.4.11 ======

===== Text =====

> gṛhāṇa draviṇaṁ dattaṁ
> mat-satra-pariśeṣitam
> ity uktvāntarhito rudro
> bhagavān dharma-vatsalaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gṛhāṇa]]---please take now; [[synonyms:d:draviṇam]]---all the wealth; [[synonyms:d:dattam]]---is given (to you by me); [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:s:satra]]-[[synonyms:p:pariśeṣitam]]---the remnants of the sacrifice executed on my behalf; [[synonyms:i:iti]] [[synonyms:u:uktvā]]---after speaking like this; [[synonyms:a:antarhitaḥ]]---disappeared; [[synonyms:r:rudraḥ]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful demigod; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsalaḥ]]---adherent to the principles of religion.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śiva said, "Now you may take all the wealth remaining from the sacrifice, for I give it to you." After saying this, Lord Śiva, who is most adherent to the religious principles, disappeared from that place.**
