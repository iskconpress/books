====== SB 9.4.30 ======

===== Text =====

> vratānte kārtike māsi
> tri-rātraṁ samupoṣitaḥ
> snātaḥ kadācit kālindyāṁ
> hariṁ madhuvane 'rcayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vrata]]-[[synonyms:a:ante]]---at the end of observing the vow; [[synonyms:k:kārtike]]---in the month of Kārtika (October-November); [[synonyms:m:māsi]]---in that month; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:r:rātram]]---for three nights; [[synonyms:s:samupoṣitaḥ]]---after completely observing the fast; [[synonyms:s:snātaḥ]]---after bathing; [[synonyms:k:kadācit]]---once upon a time; [[synonyms:k:kālindyām]]---on the bank of the Yamunā; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:madhuvane]]---in that part of the Vṛndāvana area known as Madhuvana; [[synonyms:a:arcayat]]---worshiped the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**In the month of Kārtika, after observing that vow for one year, after observing a fast for three nights and after bathing in the Yamunā, Mahārāja Ambarīṣa worshiped the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari, in Madhuvana.**
