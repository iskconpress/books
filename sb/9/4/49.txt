====== SB 9.4.49 ======

===== Text =====

> tad-abhidravad udvīkṣya
> sva-prayāsaṁ ca niṣphalam
> durvāsā dudruve bhīto
> dikṣu prāṇa-parīpsayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]---of that disc; [[synonyms:a:abhidravat]]---moving toward him; [[synonyms:u:udvīkṣya]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:p:prayāsam]]---his own attempt; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:n:niṣphalam]]---having failed; [[synonyms:d:durvāsāḥ]]---Durvāsā Muni; [[synonyms:d:dudruve]]---began to run; [[synonyms:b:bhītaḥ]]---full of fear; [[synonyms:d:dikṣu]]---in every direction; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:parīpsayā]]---with a desire to save his life.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing that his own attempt had failed and that the Sudarśana cakra was moving toward him, Durvāsā Muni became very frightened and began to run in all directions to save his life.**
