====== SB 9.4.62 ======

===== Text =====

> ajānatā te paramānubhāvaṁ
> kṛtaṁ mayāghaṁ bhavataḥ priyāṇām
> vidhehi tasyāpacitiṁ vidhātar
> mucyeta yan-nāmny udite nārako 'pi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ajānatā]]---without knowledge; [[synonyms:t:te]]---of Your Lordship; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhāvam]]---the inconceivable prowess; [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---has been committed; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:a:agham]]---a great offense; [[synonyms:b:bhavataḥ]]---of Your Lordship; [[synonyms:p:priyāṇām]]---at the feet of the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vidhehi]]---now kindly do the needful; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of such an offense; [[synonyms:a:apacitim]]---counteraction; [[synonyms:v:vidhātaḥ]]---O supreme controller; [[synonyms:m:mucyeta]]---can be delivered; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---of whose; [[synonyms:n:nāmni]]---when the name; [[synonyms:u:udite]]---is awakened; [[synonyms:n:nārakaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even a person fit for going to hell.

===== Translation =====

**O my Lord, O supreme controller, without knowledge of Your unlimited prowess I have offended Your most dear devotee. Very kindly save me from the reaction of this offense. You can do everything, for even if a person is fit for going to hell, You can deliver him simply by awakening within his heart the holy name of Your Lordship.**
