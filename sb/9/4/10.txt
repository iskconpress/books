====== SB 9.4.10 ======

===== Text =====

> yat te pitāvadad dharmaṁ
> tvaṁ ca satyaṁ prabhāṣase
> dadāmi te mantra-dṛśo
> jñānaṁ brahma sanātanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever; [[synonyms:t:te]]---your; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---father; [[synonyms:a:avadat]]---explained; [[synonyms:d:dharmam]]---truth; [[synonyms:t:tvam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---you also; [[synonyms:s:satyam]]---truth; [[synonyms:p:prabhāṣase]]---are speaking; [[synonyms:d:dadāmi]]---I shall give; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto you; [[synonyms:m:mantra]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛśaḥ]]---who know the science of //mantra//; [[synonyms:j:jñānam]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:s:sanātanam]]---eternal.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śiva said: Whatever your father has said is the truth, and you also are speaking the same truth. Therefore, I, who know the Vedic mantras, shall explain transcendental knowledge to you.**
