====== SB 9.4.23 ======

===== Text =====

> yasya kratuṣu gīrvāṇaiḥ
> sadasyā ṛtvijo janāḥ
> tulya-rūpāś cānimiṣā
> vyadṛśyanta suvāsasaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom (Mahārāja Ambarīṣa); [[synonyms:k:kratuṣu]]---in sacrifices (performed by him); [[synonyms:g:gīrvāṇaiḥ]]---with the demigods; [[synonyms:s:sadasyāḥ]]---members for executing the sacrifice; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtvijaḥ]]---the priests; [[synonyms:j:janāḥ]]---and other expert men; [[synonyms:t:tulya]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpāḥ]]---appearing exactly like; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:animiṣāḥ]]---with unblinking eyes like those of the demigods; [[synonyms:v:vyadṛśyanta]]---being seen; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsasaḥ]]---well dressed with valuable garments.

===== Translation =====

**In the sacrifice arranged by Mahārāja Ambarīṣa, the members of the assembly and the priests [especially hotā, udgātā, brahmā and adhvaryu] were gorgeously dressed, and they all looked exactly like demigods. They eagerly saw to the proper performance of the yajña.**
