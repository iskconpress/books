====== SB 9.4.60 ======

===== Text =====

> tato nirāśo durvāsāḥ
> padaṁ bhagavato yayau
> vaikuṇṭhākhyaṁ yad adhyāste
> śrīnivāsaḥ śriyā saha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:n:nirāśaḥ]]---disappointed; [[synonyms:d:durvāsāḥ]]---the great mystic Durvāsā; [[synonyms:p:padam]]---to the place; [[synonyms:b:bhagavataḥ]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyam]]---the place known as Vaikuṇṭha; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---wherein; [[synonyms:a:adhyāste]]---lives perpetually; [[synonyms:ś:śrīnivāsaḥ]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:ś:śriyā]]---with the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, being disappointed even in taking shelter of Lord Śiva, Durvāsā Muni went to Vaikuṇṭha-dhāma, where the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyaṇa, resides with His consort, the goddess of fortune.**
