====== SB 9.4.9 ======

===== Text =====

> nābhāgas taṁ praṇamyāha
> taveśa kila vāstukam
> ity āha me pitā brahmañ
> chirasā tvāṁ prasādaye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nābhāgaḥ]]---Nābhāga; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him (Lord Śiva); [[synonyms:p:praṇamya]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---yours; [[synonyms:ī:īśa]]---O lord; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---certainly; [[synonyms:v:vāstukam]]---everything in the arena of sacrifice; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---father; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ś:śirasā]]---bowing my head; [[synonyms:t:tvām]]---unto you; [[synonyms:p:prasādaye]]---I am begging your mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Thereupon, after offering obeisances to Lord Śiva, Nābhāga said: O worshipable lord, everything in this arena of sacrifice is yours. This is the assertion of my father. Now, with great respect, I bow my head before you, begging your mercy.**
