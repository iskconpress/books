====== SB 9.4.46 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ bruvāṇa utkṛtya
> jaṭāṁ roṣa-pradīpitaḥ
> tayā sa nirmame tasmai
> kṛtyāṁ kālānalopamām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bruvāṇaḥ]]---speaking (Durvāsā Muni); [[synonyms:u:utkṛtya]]---uprooting; [[synonyms:j:jaṭām]]---a bunch of hair; [[synonyms:r:roṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:pradīpitaḥ]]---being reddish because he was very angry; [[synonyms:t:tayā]]---by that bunch of hair from his head; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Durvāsā Muni; [[synonyms:n:nirmame]]---created; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---to punish Mahārāja Ambarīṣa; [[synonyms:k:kṛtyām]]---a demon; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:a:anala]]-[[synonyms:u:upamām]]---appearing just like the blazing fire of devastation.

===== Translation =====

**As Durvāsā Muni said this, his face became red with anger. Uprooting a bunch of hair from his head, he created a demon resembling the blazing fire of devastation to punish Mahārāja Ambarīṣa.**
