====== SB 9.4.14 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> bhagavañ chrotum icchāmi
> rājarṣes tasya dhīmataḥ
> na prābhūd yatra nirmukto
> brahma-daṇḍo duratyayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---King Parīkṣit inquired; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O great //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ś:śrotum]] [[synonyms:i:icchāmi]]---I wish to hear (from you); [[synonyms:r:rājarṣeḥ]]---of the great King Ambarīṣa; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him; [[synonyms:d:dhīmataḥ]]---who was such a greatly sober personality; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:p:prābhūt]]---could act; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---upon whom (Mahārāja Ambarīṣa); [[synonyms:n:nirmuktaḥ]]---being released; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍaḥ]]---the curse of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:d:duratyayaḥ]]---which is insurmountable.

===== Translation =====

**King Parīkṣit inquired: O great personality, Mahārāja Ambarīṣa was certainly most exalted and meritorious in character. I wish to hear about him. How surprising it is that the curse of a brāhmaṇa, which is insurmountable, could not act upon him.**
