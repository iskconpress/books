====== SB 9.4.6 ======

===== Text =====

> taṁ kaścit svīkariṣyantaṁ
> puruṣaḥ kṛṣṇa-darśanaḥ
> uvācottarato 'bhyetya
> mamedaṁ vāstukaṁ vasu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Nābhāga; [[synonyms:k:kaścit]]---someone; [[synonyms:s:svīkariṣyantam]]---while accepting the riches given by the great sages; [[synonyms:p:puruṣaḥ]]---a person; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:darśanaḥ]]---black-looking; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---said; [[synonyms:u:uttarataḥ]]---from the north; [[synonyms:a:abhyetya]]---coming; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---my; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---these; [[synonyms:v:vāstukam]]---remnants of the sacrifice; [[synonyms:v:vasu]]---all the riches.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, while Nābhāga was accepting the riches, a black-looking person from the north came to him and said, "All the wealth from this sacrificial arena belongs to me."**
