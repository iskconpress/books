====== SB 9.4.7 ======

===== Text =====

> mamedam ṛṣibhir dattam
> iti tarhi sma mānavaḥ
> syān nau te pitari praśnaḥ
> pṛṣṭavān pitaraṁ yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mama]]---my; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---all these; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣibhiḥ]]---by the great saintly persons; [[synonyms:d:dattam]]---have been delivered; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:t:tarhi]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:sma]]---indeed; [[synonyms:m:mānavaḥ]]---Nābhāga; [[synonyms:s:syāt]]---let there be; [[synonyms:n:nau]]---of ourselves; [[synonyms:t:te]]---your; [[synonyms:p:pitari]]---unto the father; [[synonyms:p:praśnaḥ]]---an inquiry; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣṭavān]]---he also inquired; [[synonyms:p:pitaram]]---from his father; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as requested.

===== Translation =====

**Nābhāga then said, "These riches belong to me. The great saintly persons have delivered them to me." When Nābhāga said this, the black-looking person replied, "Let us go to your father and ask him to settle our disagreement." In accordance with this, Nābhāga inquired from his father.**
