====== SB 9.4.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ya etat saṁsmaret prātaḥ
> sāyaṁ ca susamāhitaḥ
> kavir bhavati mantra-jño
> gatiṁ caiva tathātmanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---about this incident; [[synonyms:s:saṁsmaret]]---may remember; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:s:sāyam]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and in the evening; [[synonyms:s:susamāhitaḥ]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:k:kaviḥ]]---learned; [[synonyms:b:bhavati]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:mantra]]-[[synonyms:j:jñaḥ]]---well aware of all Vedic //mantras//; [[synonyms:g:gatim]]---the destination; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---like that of the self-realized soul.

===== Translation =====

**If one hears and chants or remembers this narration in the morning and evening with great attention, he certainly becomes learned, experienced in understanding the Vedic hymns, and expert in self-realization.**
