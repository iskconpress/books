====== SB 9.10.33 ======

===== Text =====

> avakīryamāṇaḥ sukusumair
> lokapālārpitaiḥ pathi
> upagīyamāna-caritaḥ
> śatadhṛty-ādibhir mudā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avakīryamāṇaḥ]]---being overflooded; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:k:kusumaiḥ]]---by fragrant and beautiful flowers; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:p:pāla]]-[[synonyms:a:arpitaiḥ]]---offered by the princely order; [[synonyms:p:pathi]]---on the road; [[synonyms:u:upagīyamāna]]-[[synonyms:c:caritaḥ]]---being glorified for His uncommon activities; [[synonyms:ś:śatadhṛti]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---by personalities like Lord Brahmā and other demigods; [[synonyms:m:mudā]]---with great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Rāmacandra returned to His capital, Ayodhyā, He was greeted on the road by the princely order, who showered His body with beautiful, fragrant flowers, while great personalities like Lord Brahmā and other demigods glorified the activities of the Lord in great jubilation.**
