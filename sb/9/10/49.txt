====== SB 9.10.49 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ kṛta-śiraḥ-snānaḥ
> suvāsāḥ sragvy-alaṅkṛtaḥ
> svalaṅkṛtaiḥ suvāsobhir
> bhrātṛbhir bhāryayā babhau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiraḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:snānaḥ]]---having completely bathed, washing the head; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsāḥ]]---being nicely dressed; [[synonyms:s:sragvi]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkṛtaḥ]]---being decorated with a garland; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkṛtaiḥ]]---decorated nicely; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsobhiḥ]]---dressed nicely; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛbhiḥ]]---with His brothers; [[synonyms:b:bhāryayā]]---and with His wife, Sītā; [[synonyms:b:babhau]]---the Lord became very brilliant.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Rāmacandra, fully bathed and His head clean-shaven, dressed Himself very nicely and was decorated with a garland and ornaments. Thus He shone brightly, surrounded by His brothers and wife, who were similarly dressed and ornamented.**
