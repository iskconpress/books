====== SB 9.10.31 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmaḥ priyatamāṁ bhāryāṁ
> dīnāṁ vīkṣyānvakampata
> ātma-sandarśanāhlāda-
> vikasan-mukha-paṅkajām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmaḥ]]---Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:t:tamām]]---upon His dearmost; [[synonyms:b:bhāryām]]---wife; [[synonyms:d:dīnām]]---so poorly situated; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣya]]---looking; [[synonyms:a:anvakampata]]---became very compassionate; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sandarśana]]---when one sees his beloved; [[synonyms:ā:āhlāda]]---an ecstasy of joyful life; [[synonyms:v:vikasat]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---mouth; [[synonyms:p:paṅkajām]]---like a lotus.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing His wife in that condition, Lord Rāmacandra was very compassionate. When Rāmacandra came before her, she was exceedingly happy to see her beloved, and her lotuslike mouth showed her joy.**
