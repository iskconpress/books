====== SB 9.10.21 ======

===== Text =====

> rakṣaḥ-patiḥ sva-bala-naṣṭim avekṣya ruṣṭa
> āruhya yānakam athābhisasāra rāmam
> svaḥ-syandane dyumati mātalinopanīte
> vibhrājamānam ahanan niśitaiḥ kṣurapraiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rakṣaḥ]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---the leader of the Rākṣasas, Rāvaṇa; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bala]]-[[synonyms:n:naṣṭim]]---the destruction of his own soldiers; [[synonyms:a:avekṣya]]---after observing; [[synonyms:r:ruṣṭaḥ]]---became very angry; [[synonyms:ā:āruhya]]---riding on; [[synonyms:y:yānakam]]---his beautiful airplane decorated with flowers; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:a:abhisasāra]]---proceeded toward; [[synonyms:r:rāmam]]---Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:s:svaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:syandane]]---in the celestial chariot of Indra; [[synonyms:d:dyumati]]---glittering; [[synonyms:m:mātalinā]]---by Mātali, the chariot driver of Indra; [[synonyms:u:upanīte]]---having been brought; [[synonyms:v:vibhrājamānam]]---Lord Rāmacandra, as if brilliantly illuminating; [[synonyms:a:ahanat]]---Rāvaṇa struck him; [[synonyms:n:niśitaiḥ]]---very sharp; [[synonyms:k:kṣurapraiḥ]]---with arrows.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, when Rāvaṇa, the king of the Rākṣasas, observed that his soldiers had been lost, he was extremely angry. Thus he mounted his airplane, which was decorated with flowers, and proceeded toward Lord Rāmacandra, who sat on the effulgent chariot brought by Mātali, the chariot driver of Indra. Then Rāvaṇa struck Lord Rāmacandra with sharp arrows.**
