====== SB 9.10.52 ======

===== Text =====

> vanāni nadyo girayo
> varṣāṇi dvīpa-sindhavaḥ
> sarve kāma-dughā āsan
> prajānāṁ bharatarṣabha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vanāni]]---the forests; [[synonyms:n:nadyaḥ]]---the rivers; [[synonyms:g:girayaḥ]]---the hills and mountains; [[synonyms:v:varṣāṇi]]---various parts of the states or divisions on the surface of the earth; [[synonyms:d:dvīpa]]---islands; [[synonyms:s:sindhavaḥ]]---the oceans and seas; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:d:dughāḥ]]---full of their respective opulences; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---existed like that; [[synonyms:p:prajānām]]---of all the living beings; [[synonyms:b:bharata]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabha]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, best of the Bharata dynasty.

===== Translation =====

**O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, best of the Bharata dynasty, during the reign of Lord Rāmacandra the forests, the rivers, the hills and mountains, the states, the seven islands and the seven seas were all favorable in supplying the necessities of life for all living beings.**
