====== SB 9.10.25 ======

===== Text =====

> svān svān bandhūn pariṣvajya
> lakṣmaṇeṣubhir arditān
> ruruduḥ susvaraṁ dīnā
> ghnantya ātmānam ātmanā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svān]] [[synonyms:s:svān]]---their own respective husbands; [[synonyms:b:bandhūn]]---friends; [[synonyms:p:pariṣvajya]]---embracing; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:i:iṣubhiḥ]]---by the arrows of Lakṣmaṇa; [[synonyms:a:arditān]]---who were killed; [[synonyms:r:ruruduḥ]]---all the wives cried piteously; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:svaram]]---it was very sweet to hear; [[synonyms:d:dīnāḥ]]---very poor; [[synonyms:g:ghnantyaḥ]]---striking; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---their breasts; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanā]]---by themselves.

===== Translation =====

**Striking their breasts in affliction because their husbands had been killed by the arrows of Lakṣmaṇa, the women embraced their respective husbands and cried piteously in voices appealing to everyone.**
