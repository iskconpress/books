====== SB 9.10.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tato dadarśa bhagavān
> aśoka-vanikāśrame
> kṣāmāṁ sva-viraha-vyādhiṁ
> śiṁśapā-mūlam-āśritām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:d:dadarśa]]---saw; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:aśoka]]-[[synonyms:v:vanika]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrame]]---in a small cottage in the forest of Aśoka trees; [[synonyms:k:kṣāmām]]---very lean and thin; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:v:viraha]]-[[synonyms:v:vyādhim]]---suffering from the disease of separation from Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:ś:śiṁśapā]]---of the tree known as Siṁśapā; [[synonyms:m:mūlam]]---the root; [[synonyms:ā:āśritām]]---taking shelter of.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Lord Rāmacandra found Sītādevī sitting in a small cottage beneath the tree named Siṁśapā in a forest of Aśoka trees. She was lean and thin, being aggrieved because of separation from Him.**
