====== SB 9.10.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> khaṭvāṅgād dīrghabāhuś ca
> raghus tasmāt pṛthu-śravāḥ
> ajas tato mahā-rājas
> tasmād daśaratho 'bhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:k:khaṭvāṅgāt]]---from Mahārāja Khaṭvāṅga; [[synonyms:d:dīrghabāhuḥ]]---the son named Dīrghabāhu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:r:raghuḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him Raghu was born; [[synonyms:p:pṛthu]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravāḥ]]---saintly and celebrated; [[synonyms:a:ajaḥ]]---the son named Aja; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:r:rājaḥ]]---the great king called Mahārāja Daśaratha; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Aja; [[synonyms:d:daśarathaḥ]]---by the name Daśaratha; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was born.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: The son of Mahārāja Khaṭvāṅga was Dīrghabāhu, and his son was the celebrated Mahārāja Raghu. From Mahārāja Raghu came Aja, and from Aja was born the great personality Mahārāja Daśaratha.**
