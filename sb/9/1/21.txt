====== SB 9.1.21 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ vyavasito rājan
> bhagavān sa mahā-yaśāḥ
> astauṣīd ādi-puruṣam
> ilāyāḥ puṁstva-kāmyayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vyavasitaḥ]]---deciding; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:y:yaśāḥ]]---very famous; [[synonyms:a:astauṣīt]]---offered prayers; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣam]]---unto the Supreme Person, Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:i:ilāyāḥ]]---of Ilā; [[synonyms:p:puṁstva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāmyayā]]---for the transformation into a male.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King Parīkṣit, after the most famous and powerful Vasiṣṭha made this decision, he offered prayers to the Supreme Person, Viṣṇu, to transform Ilā into a male.**
