====== SB 9.1.27 ======

===== Text =====

> tathā tad-anugāḥ sarve
> ātma-liṅga-viparyayam
> dṛṣṭvā vimanaso 'bhūvan
> vīkṣamāṇāḥ parasparam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]]---similarly; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:a:anugāḥ]]---the companions of Sudyumna; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:l:liṅga]]-[[synonyms:v:viparyayam]]---the transformation of their sex into the opposite; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vimanasaḥ]]---morose; [[synonyms:a:abhūvan]]---they became; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣamāṇāḥ]]---looking over; [[synonyms:p:parasparam]]---one another.

===== Translation =====

**When his followers also saw their identities transformed and their sex reversed, they were all very morose and just looked at one another.**
