====== SB 9.1.2-3 ======

===== Text =====

> yo 'sau satyavrato nāma
> rājarṣir draviḍeśvaraḥ
> jñānaṁ yo 'tīta-kalpānte
> lebhe puruṣa-sevayā
>
> sa vai vivasvataḥ putro
> manur āsīd iti śrutam
> tvattas tasya sutāḥ proktā
> ikṣvāku-pramukhā nṛpāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:asau]]---he who was known; [[synonyms:s:satyavrataḥ]]---Satyavrata; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by the name; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---the saintly king; [[synonyms:d:draviḍa]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the ruler of the Draviḍa countries; [[synonyms:j:jñānam]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---one who; [[synonyms:a:atīta]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:a:ante]]---at the end of the period of the last Manu, or at the end of the last millennium; [[synonyms:l:lebhe]]---received; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevayā]]---by rendering service to the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vivasvataḥ]]---of Vivasvān; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:m:manuḥ]] [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---became the Vaivasvata Manu; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ś:śrutam]]---I have already heard; [[synonyms:t:tvattaḥ]]---from you; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---sons; [[synonyms:p:proktāḥ]]---have been explained; [[synonyms:i:ikṣvāku]]-[[synonyms:p:pramukhāḥ]]---headed by Ikṣvāku; [[synonyms:n:nṛpāḥ]]---many kings.

===== Translation =====

**Satyavrata, the saintly king of Draviḍadeśa who received spiritual knowledge at the end of the last millennium by the grace of the Supreme, later became Vaivasvata Manu, the son of Vivasvān, in the next manvantara [period of Manu]. I have received this knowledge from you. I also understand that such kings as Ikṣvāku were his sons, as you have already explained.**
