====== SB 9.1.15 ======

===== Text =====

> preṣito 'dhvaryuṇā hotā
> vyacarat tat samāhitaḥ
> gṛhīte haviṣi vācā
> vaṣaṭ-kāraṁ gṛṇan dvijaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:preṣitaḥ]]---being told to execute the sacrifice; [[synonyms:a:adhvaryuṇā]]---by the //ṛtvik// priest; [[synonyms:h:hotā]]---the priest in charge of offering oblations; [[synonyms:v:vyacarat]]---executed; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that (sacrifice); [[synonyms:s:samāhitaḥ]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:g:gṛhīte]] [[synonyms:h:haviṣi]]---upon taking the clarified butter for the first oblation; [[synonyms:v:vācā]]---by chanting the //mantra//; [[synonyms:v:vaṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāram]]---the //mantra// beginning with the word //vaṣaṭ//; [[synonyms:g:gṛṇan]]---reciting; [[synonyms:d:dvijaḥ]]---the //brāhmaṇa.//

===== Translation =====

**Told by the chief priest "Now offer oblations," the person in charge of oblations took clarified butter to offer. He then remembered the request of Manu's wife and performed the sacrifice while chanting the word "vaṣaṭ."**
