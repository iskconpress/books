====== SB 9.1.7 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> śrūyatāṁ mānavo vaṁśaḥ
> prācuryeṇa parantapa
> na śakyate vistarato
> vaktuṁ varṣa-śatair api

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:ś:śrūyatām]]---just hear from me; [[synonyms:m:mānavaḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vaṁśaḥ]]---the dynasty of Manu; [[synonyms:p:prācuryeṇa]]---as expansive as possible; [[synonyms:p:parantapa]]---O King, who can subdue your enemies; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:ś:śakyate]]---one is able; [[synonyms:v:vistarataḥ]]---very broadly; [[synonyms:v:vaktum]]---to speak; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śataiḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even if he does so for hundreds of years.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King, subduer of your enemies, now hear from me in great detail about the dynasty of Manu. I shall explain as much as possible, although one could not say everything about it, even in hundreds of years.**
