====== SB 9.1.37 ======

===== Text =====

> sa tasya tāṁ daśāṁ dṛṣṭvā
> kṛpayā bhṛśa-pīḍitaḥ
> sudyumnasyāśayan puṁstvam
> upādhāvata śaṅkaram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Sudyumna; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---that; [[synonyms:d:daśām]]---condition; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭvā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kṛpayā]]---out of mercy; [[synonyms:b:bhṛśa]]-[[synonyms:p:pīḍitaḥ]]---being very much aggrieved; [[synonyms:s:sudyumnasya]]---of Sudyumna; [[synonyms:ā:āśayan]]---desiring; [[synonyms:p:puṁstvam]]---the maleness; [[synonyms:u:upādhāvata]]---began to worship; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkaram]]---Lord Śiva.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing Sudyumna's deplorable condition, Vasiṣṭha was very much aggrieved. Desiring for Sudyumna to regain his maleness, Vasiṣṭha again began to worship Lord Śaṅkara [Śiva].**
