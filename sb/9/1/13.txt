====== SB 9.1.13 ======

===== Text =====

> aprajasya manoḥ pūrvaṁ
> vasiṣṭho bhagavān kila
> mitrā-varuṇayor iṣṭiṁ
> prajārtham akarod vibhuḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aprajasya]]---of he who had no son; [[synonyms:m:manoḥ]]---of Manu; [[synonyms:p:pūrvam]]---formerly; [[synonyms:v:vasiṣṭhaḥ]]---the great saint Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---powerful; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---indeed; [[synonyms:m:mitrā]]-[[synonyms:v:varuṇayoḥ]]---of the demigods named Mitra and Varuṇa; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭim]]---a sacrifice; [[synonyms:p:prajā]]-[[synonyms:a:artham]]---for the sake of getting sons; [[synonyms:a:akarot]]---executed; [[synonyms:v:vibhuḥ]]---the great person.

===== Translation =====

**Manu at first had no sons. Therefore, in order to get a son for him, the great saint Vasiṣṭha, who was very powerful in spiritual knowledge, performed a sacrifice to satisfy the demigods Mitra and Varuṇa.**
