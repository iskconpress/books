====== SB 9.1.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tān vilokyāmbikā devī
> vivāsā vrīḍitā bhṛśam
> bhartur aṅkāt samutthāya
> nīvīm āśv atha paryadhāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tān]]---all the saintly persons; [[synonyms:v:vilokya]]---seeing them; [[synonyms:a:ambikā]]---mother Durgā; [[synonyms:d:devī]]---the goddess; [[synonyms:v:vivāsā]]---because she was naked; [[synonyms:v:vrīḍitā]]---ashamed; [[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---highly; [[synonyms:b:bhartuḥ]]---of her husband; [[synonyms:a:aṅkāt]]---from the lap; [[synonyms:s:samutthāya]]---getting up; [[synonyms:n:nīvīm]]---breast; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]] [[synonyms:a:atha]]---very quickly; [[synonyms:p:paryadhāt]]---covered with cloth.

===== Translation =====

**When the goddess Ambikā saw the great saintly persons, she was very much ashamed because at that time she was naked. She immediately got up from the lap of her husband and tried to cover her breast.**
