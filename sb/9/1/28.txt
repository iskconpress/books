====== SB 9.1.28 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> katham evaṁ guṇo deśaḥ
> kena vā bhagavan kṛtaḥ
> praśnam enaṁ samācakṣva
> paraṁ kautūhalaṁ hi naḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Mahārāja Parīkṣit said; [[synonyms:k:katham]]---how; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---this; [[synonyms:g:guṇaḥ]]---quality; [[synonyms:d:deśaḥ]]---the country; [[synonyms:k:kena]]---why; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O most powerful; [[synonyms:k:kṛtaḥ]]---it was so done; [[synonyms:p:praśnam]]---question; [[synonyms:e:enam]]---this; [[synonyms:s:samācakṣva]]---just deliberate; [[synonyms:p:param]]---very much; [[synonyms:k:kautūhalam]]---eagerness; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Parīkṣit said: O most powerful brāhmaṇa, why was this place so empowered, and who made it so powerful? Kindly answer this question, for I am very eager to hear about this.**
