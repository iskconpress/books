====== SB 9.1.35 ======

===== Text =====

> sāpi taṁ cakame subhrūḥ
> somarāja-sutaṁ patim
> sa tasyāṁ janayām āsa
> purūravasam ātmajam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sā]]---Sudyumna, transformed into a woman; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto him (Budha); [[synonyms:c:cakame]]---desired sex; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrūḥ]]---very beautiful; [[synonyms:s:somarāja]]-[[synonyms:s:sutam]]---unto the son of the king of the moon; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---as her husband; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he (Budha); [[synonyms:t:tasyām]]---in her womb; [[synonyms:j:janayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---begot; [[synonyms:p:purūravasam]]---named Purūravā; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:j:jam]]---a son.

===== Translation =====

**The beautiful woman also desired to accept Budha, the son of the king of the moon, as her husband. Thus Budha begot in her womb a son named Purūravā.**
