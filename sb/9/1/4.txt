====== SB 9.1.4 ======

===== Text =====

> teṣāṁ vaṁśaṁ pṛthag brahman
> vaṁśānucaritāni ca
> kīrtayasva mahā-bhāga
> nityaṁ śuśrūṣatāṁ hi naḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of all those kings; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśam]]---the dynasties; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separately; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O great //brāhmaṇa// (Śukadeva Gosvāmī); [[synonyms:v:vaṁśa]]-[[synonyms:a:anucaritāni]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and their dynasties and characteristics; [[synonyms:k:kīrtayasva]]---kindly describe; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāga]]---O greatly fortunate one; [[synonyms:n:nityam]]---eternally; [[synonyms:ś:śuśrūṣatām]]---who are engaged in your service; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---of ourselves.

===== Translation =====

**O greatly fortunate Śukadeva Gosvāmī, O great brāhmaṇa, kindly describe to us separately the dynasties and characteristics of all those kings, for we are always eager to hear such topics from you.**
