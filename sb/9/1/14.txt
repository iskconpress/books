====== SB 9.1.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tatra śraddhā manoḥ patnī
> hotāraṁ samayācata
> duhitrartham upāgamya
> praṇipatya payovratā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in that sacrifice; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]]---Śraddhā; [[synonyms:m:manoḥ]]---of Manu; [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---the wife; [[synonyms:h:hotāram]]---to the priest performing the //yajña//; [[synonyms:s:samayācata]]---begged properly; [[synonyms:d:duhitṛ]]-[[synonyms:a:artham]]---for a daughter; [[synonyms:u:upāgamya]]---coming near; [[synonyms:p:praṇipatya]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:p:payaḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vratā]]---who was observing the vow of drinking only milk.

===== Translation =====

**During that sacrifice, Śraddhā, Manu's wife, who was observing the vow of subsisting only by drinking milk, approached the priest offering the sacrifice, offered obeisances to him and begged for a daughter.**
