====== SB 9.1.36 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ strītvam anuprāptaḥ
> sudyumno mānavo nṛpaḥ
> sasmāra sa kulācāryaṁ
> vasiṣṭham iti śuśruma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:strītvam]]---femininity; [[synonyms:a:anuprāptaḥ]]---having achieved in that way; [[synonyms:s:sudyumnaḥ]]---the male named Sudyumna; [[synonyms:m:mānavaḥ]]---the son of Manu; [[synonyms:n:nṛpaḥ]]---the king; [[synonyms:s:sasmāra]]---remembered; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāryam]]---the familial spiritual master; [[synonyms:v:vasiṣṭham]]---the most powerful Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:i:iti]] [[synonyms:ś:śuśruma]]---I have heard it (from reliable sources).

===== Translation =====

**I heard from reliable sources that King Sudyumna, the son of Manu, having thus achieved femininity, remembered his familial spiritual master, Vasiṣṭha.**
