====== SB 9.1.22 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmai kāma-varaṁ tuṣṭo
> bhagavān harir īśvaraḥ
> dadāv ilābhavat tena
> sudyumnaḥ puruṣarṣabhaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto him (Vasiṣṭha); [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:v:varam]]---the desired benediction; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭaḥ]]---being pleased; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the supreme controller, the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dadau]]---gave; [[synonyms:i:ilā]]---the girl, Ilā; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---became; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---because of this benediction; [[synonyms:s:sudyumnaḥ]]---by the name Sudyumna; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣabhaḥ]]---a nice male.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead, the supreme controller, being pleased with Vasiṣṭha, gave him the benediction he desired. Thus Ilā was transformed into a very fine male named Sudyumna.**
