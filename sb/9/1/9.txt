====== SB 9.1.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tasya nābheḥ samabhavat
> padma-koṣo hiraṇmayaḥ
> tasmiñ jajñe mahārāja
> svayambhūś catur-ānanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Him (the Supreme Personality of Godhead); [[synonyms:n:nābheḥ]]---from the navel; [[synonyms:s:samabhavat]]---generated; [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:k:koṣaḥ]]---a lotus; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇmayaḥ]]---known as Hiraṇmaya, or golden; [[synonyms:t:tasmin]]---on that golden lotus; [[synonyms:j:jajñe]]---appeared; [[synonyms:m:mahārāja]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:svayambhūḥ]]---one who is self-manifested, who takes birth without a mother; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanaḥ]]---with four heads.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, from the navel of the Supreme Personality of Godhead was generated a golden lotus, on which the four-faced Lord Brahmā took his birth.**
