====== SB 9.1.19 ======

===== Text =====

> niśamya tad vacas tasya
> bhagavān prapitāmahaḥ
> hotur vyatikramaṁ jñātvā
> babhāṣe ravi-nandanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niśamya]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:v:vacaḥ]]---those words; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Manu); [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:p:prapitāmahaḥ]]---the great-grandfather Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:h:hotuḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vyatikramam]]---discrepancy on the part of the //hotā// priest; [[synonyms:j:jñātvā]]---understanding; [[synonyms:b:babhāṣe]]---spoke; [[synonyms:r:ravi]]-[[synonyms:n:nandanam]]---unto Vaivasvata Manu, son of the sun-god.

===== Translation =====

**The most powerful great-grandfather Vasiṣṭha, after hearing these words of Manu, understood the discrepancy on the part of the priest. Thus he spoke as follows to the son of the sun-god.**
