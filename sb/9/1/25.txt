====== SB 9.1.25 ======

===== Text =====

> sukumāra-vanaṁ meror
> adhastāt praviveśa ha
> yatrāste bhagavāñ charvo
> ramamāṇaḥ sahomayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukumāra]]-[[synonyms:v:vanam]]---the forest known as Sukumāra; [[synonyms:m:meroḥ]] [[synonyms:a:adhastāt]]---at the foot of Mount Meru; [[synonyms:p:praviveśa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]---he entered; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherein; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]---was; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful (demigod); [[synonyms:ś:śarvaḥ]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:r:ramamāṇaḥ]]---engaged in enjoyment; [[synonyms:s:saha]] [[synonyms:u:umayā]]---with Umā, his wife.

===== Translation =====

**There in the north, at the bottom of Mount Meru, is a forest known as Sukumāra where Lord Śiva always enjoys with Umā. Sudyumna entered that forest.**
