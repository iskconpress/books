====== SB 9.1.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> manvantarāṇi sarvāṇi
> tvayoktāni śrutāni me
> vīryāṇy ananta-vīryasya
> hares tatra kṛtāni ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---King Parīkṣit said; [[synonyms:m:manvantarāṇi]]---all about the periods of the various Manus; [[synonyms:s:sarvāṇi]]---all of them; [[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by you; [[synonyms:u:uktāni]]---have been described; [[synonyms:ś:śrutāni]]---have been listened to; [[synonyms:m:me]]---by me; [[synonyms:v:vīryāṇi]]---wonderful activities; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:v:vīryasya]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who has unlimited potency; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of the Supreme Lord, Hari; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in those //manvantara// periods; [[synonyms:k:kṛtāni]]---which have been performed; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**King Parīkṣit said: My lord, Śukadeva Gosvāmī, you have elaborately described all the periods of the various Manus and, within those periods, the wonderful activities of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who has unlimited potency. I am fortunate to have heard all of this from you.**
