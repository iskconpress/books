====== SB 9.2.31 ======

===== Text =====

> taṁ bheje 'lambuṣā devī
> bhajanīya-guṇālayam
> varāpsarā yataḥ putrāḥ
> kanyā celavilābhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Tṛṇabindu); [[synonyms:b:bheje]]---accepted as husband; [[synonyms:a:alambuṣā]]---the girl Alambuṣā; [[synonyms:d:devī]]---goddess; [[synonyms:b:bhajanīya]]---worthy of accepting; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālayam]]---the reservoir of all good qualities; [[synonyms:v:vara]]-[[synonyms:a:apsarāḥ]]---the best of the Apsarās; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---from whom (Tṛṇabindu); [[synonyms:p:putrāḥ]]---some sons; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---a daughter; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:i:ilavilā]]---named Ilavilā; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was born.

===== Translation =====

**The best of the Apsarās, the highly qualified girl named Alambuṣā, accepted the similarly qualified Tṛṇabindu as her husband. She gave birth to a few sons and a daughter known as Ilavilā.**
