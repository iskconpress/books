====== SB 9.2.25 ======

===== Text =====

> viviṁśateḥ suto rambhaḥ
> khanīnetro 'sya dhārmikaḥ
> karandhamo mahārāja
> tasyāsīd ātmajo nṛpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viviṁśateḥ]]---from Viviṁśati; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:r:rambhaḥ]]---named Rambha; [[synonyms:k:khanīnetraḥ]]---named Khanīnetra; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of Rambha; [[synonyms:d:dhārmikaḥ]]---very religious; [[synonyms:k:karandhamaḥ]]---named Karandhama; [[synonyms:m:mahārāja]]---O King; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Khanīnetra); [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---was; [[synonyms:ā:ātmajaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Viviṁśati was Rambha, whose son was the great and religious King Khanīnetra. O King, the son of Khanīnetra was King Karandhama.**
