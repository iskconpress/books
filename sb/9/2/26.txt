====== SB 9.2.26 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyāvīkṣit suto yasya
> maruttaś cakravarty abhūt
> saṁvarto 'yājayad yaṁ vai
> mahā-yogy aṅgiraḥ-sutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Karandhama); [[synonyms:a:avīkṣit]]---named Avīkṣit; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom (Avīkṣit); [[synonyms:m:maruttaḥ]]---(the son) named Marutta; [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---the emperor; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became; [[synonyms:s:saṁvartaḥ]]---Saṁvarta; [[synonyms:a:ayājayat]]---engaged in performing sacrifice; [[synonyms:y:yam]]---unto whom (Marutta); [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogī]]---the great mystic; [[synonyms:a:aṅgiraḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Aṅgirā.

===== Translation =====

**From Karandhama came a son named Avīkṣit, and from Avīkṣit a son named Marutta, who was the emperor. The great mystic Saṁvarta, the son of Aṅgirā, engaged Marutta in performing a sacrifice [yajña].**
