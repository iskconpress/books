====== SB 9.2.19 ======

===== Text =====

> citraseno nariṣyantād
> ṛkṣas tasya suto 'bhavat
> tasya mīḍhvāṁs tataḥ pūrṇa
> indrasenas tu tat-sutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:citrasenaḥ]]---one named Citrasena; [[synonyms:n:nariṣyantāt]]---from Nariṣyanta, another son of Manu; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛkṣaḥ]]---Ṛkṣa; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Citrasena; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---became; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Ṛkṣa); [[synonyms:m:mīḍhvān]]---Mīḍhvān; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Mīḍhvān); [[synonyms:p:pūrṇaḥ]]---Pūrṇa; [[synonyms:i:indrasenaḥ]]---Indrasena; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of him (Pūrṇa).

===== Translation =====

**From Nariṣyanta came a son named Citrasena and from him a son named Ṛkṣa. From Ṛkṣa came Mīḍhvān, from Mīḍhvān came Pūrṇa, and from Pūrṇa came Indrasena.**
