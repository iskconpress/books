====== SB 9.2.33 ======

===== Text =====

> viśālaḥ śūnyabandhuś ca
> dhūmraketuś ca tat-sutāḥ
> viśālo vaṁśa-kṛd rājā
> vaiśālīṁ nirmame purīm

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśālaḥ]]---named Viśāla; [[synonyms:ś:śūnyabandhuḥ]]---named Śūnyabandhu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:dhūmraketuḥ]]---named Dhūmraketu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---the sons of Tṛṇabindu; [[synonyms:v:viśālaḥ]]---among the three, King Viśāla; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛt]]---made a dynasty; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the king; [[synonyms:v:vaiśālīm]]---by the name Vaiśālī; [[synonyms:n:nirmame]]---constructed; [[synonyms:p:purīm]]---a palace.

===== Translation =====

**Tṛṇabindu had three sons, named Viśāla, Śūnyabandhu and Dhūmraketu. Among these three, Viśāla created a dynasty and constructed a palace called Vaiśālī.**
