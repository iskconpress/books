====== SB 9.2.7 ======

===== Text =====

> vyāghro 'pi vṛkṇa-śravaṇo
> nistriṁśāgrāhatas tataḥ
> niścakrāma bhṛśaṁ bhīto
> raktaṁ pathi samutsṛjan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vyāghraḥ]]---the tiger; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇaḥ]]---its ear being cut off; [[synonyms:n:nistriṁśa]]-[[synonyms:a:agra]]-[[synonyms:ā:āhataḥ]]---because of being cut by the tip of the sword; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:n:niścakrāma]]---fled (from that place); [[synonyms:b:bhṛśam]]---very much; [[synonyms:b:bhītaḥ]]---being afraid; [[synonyms:r:raktam]]---blood; [[synonyms:p:pathi]]---on the road; [[synonyms:s:samutsṛjan]]---discharging.

===== Translation =====

**Because the tiger's ear had been cut by the edge of the sword, the tiger was very afraid, and it fled from that place, while bleeding on the street.**
