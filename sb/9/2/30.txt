====== SB 9.2.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tat-sutaḥ kevalas tasmād
> dhundhumān vegavāṁs tataḥ
> budhas tasyābhavad yasya
> tṛṇabindur mahīpatiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of him (Nara); [[synonyms:k:kevalaḥ]]---was named Kevala; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Kevala); [[synonyms:d:dhundhumān]]---a son was born named Dhundhumān; [[synonyms:v:vegavān]]---named Vegavān; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him (Dhundhumān); [[synonyms:b:budhaḥ]]---named Budha; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Vegavān); [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---there was; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom (Budha); [[synonyms:t:tṛṇabinduḥ]]---a son named Tṛṇabindu; [[synonyms:m:mahīpatiḥ]]---the king.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Nara was Kevala, and his son was Dhundhumān, whose son was Vegavān. Vegavān's son was Budha, and Budha's son was Tṛṇabindu, who became the king of this earth.**
