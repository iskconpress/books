====== SB 9.2.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> evaṁ gate 'tha sudyumne
> manur vaivasvataḥ sute
> putra-kāmas tapas tepe
> yamunāyāṁ śataṁ samāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:g:gate]]---had accepted the order of //vānaprastha//; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sudyumne]]---when Sudyumna; [[synonyms:m:manuḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vaivasvataḥ]]---Vaivasvata Manu, known as Śrāddhadeva; [[synonyms:s:sute]]---his son; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:k:kāmaḥ]]---desiring to get sons; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tepe]]---executed severe austerities; [[synonyms:y:yamunāyām]]---on the bank of the Yamunā; [[synonyms:ś:śatam]] [[synonyms:s:samāḥ]]---for one hundred years.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Thereafter, when his son Sudyumna had thus gone to the forest to accept the order of vānaprastha, Vaivasvata Manu [Śrāddhadeva], being desirous of getting more sons, performed severe austerities on the bank of the Yamunā for one hundred years.**
