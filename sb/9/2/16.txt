====== SB 9.2.16 ======

===== Text =====

> karūṣān mānavād āsan
> kārūṣāḥ kṣatra-jātayaḥ
> uttarā-patha-goptāro
> brahmaṇyā dharma-vatsalāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karūṣāt]]---from Karūṣa; [[synonyms:m:mānavāt]]---from the son of Manu; [[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:kārūṣāḥ]]---called the Kārūṣas; [[synonyms:k:kṣatra]]-[[synonyms:j:jātayaḥ]]---a group of //kṣatriyas//; [[synonyms:u:uttarā]]---northern; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---of the direction; [[synonyms:g:goptāraḥ]]---kings; [[synonyms:b:brahmaṇyāḥ]]---celebrated protectors of the brahminical culture; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsalāḥ]]---extremely religious.

===== Translation =====

**From Karūṣa, another son of Manu, came the Kārūṣa dynasty, a family of kṣatriyas. The Kārūṣa kṣatriyas were the kings of the northern direction. They were celebrated protectors of brahminical culture and were all firmly religious.**
