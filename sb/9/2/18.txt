====== SB 9.2.18 ======

===== Text =====

> vasoḥ pratīkas tat-putra
> oghavān oghavat-pitā
> kanyā caughavatī nāma
> sudarśana uvāha tām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vasoḥ]]---of Vasu; [[synonyms:p:pratīkaḥ]]---named Pratīka; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:o:oghavān]]---named Oghavān; [[synonyms:o:oghavat]]-[[synonyms:p:pitā]]---who was the father of Oghavān; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---his daughter; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:o:oghavatī]]---Oghavatī; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by the name; [[synonyms:s:sudarśanaḥ]]---Sudarśana; [[synonyms:u:uvāha]]---married; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---that daughter (Oghavatī).

===== Translation =====

**The son of Vasu was Pratīka, whose son was Oghavān. Oghavān's son was also known as Oghavān, and his daughter was Oghavatī. Sudarśana married that daughter.**
