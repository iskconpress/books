====== SB 9.2.22 ======

===== Text =====

> tato brahma-kulaṁ jātam
> āgniveśyāyanaṁ nṛpa
> nariṣyantānvayaḥ prokto
> diṣṭa-vaṁśam ataḥ śṛṇu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Agniveśya; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:k:kulam]]---a dynasty of //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:j:jātam]]---was generated; [[synonyms:ā:āgniveśyāyanam]]---known as Āgniveśyāyana; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:n:nariṣyanta]]---of Nariṣyanta; [[synonyms:a:anvayaḥ]]---descendants; [[synonyms:p:proktaḥ]]---have been explained; [[synonyms:d:diṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśam]]---the dynasty of Diṣṭa; [[synonyms:a:ataḥ]]---hereafter; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---hear.

===== Translation =====

**O King, from Agniveśya came a brahminical dynasty known as Āgniveśyāyana. Now that I have described the descendants of Nariṣyanta, let me describe the descendants of Diṣṭa. Please hear from me.**
