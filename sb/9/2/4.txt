====== SB 9.2.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ekadā prāviśad goṣṭhaṁ
> śārdūlo niśi varṣati
> śayānā gāva utthāya
> bhītās tā babhramur vraje

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekadā]]---once upon a time; [[synonyms:p:prāviśat]]---entered; [[synonyms:g:goṣṭham]]---the land of the cowshed; [[synonyms:ś:śārdūlaḥ]]---a tiger; [[synonyms:n:niśi]]---at night; [[synonyms:v:varṣati]]---while it was raining; [[synonyms:ś:śayānāḥ]]---lying down; [[synonyms:g:gāvaḥ]]---cows; [[synonyms:u:utthāya]]---getting up; [[synonyms:b:bhītāḥ]]---fearing; [[synonyms:t:tāḥ]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:babhramuḥ]]---scattered here and there; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in the land surrounding the cowshed.

===== Translation =====

**Once at night, while it was raining, a tiger entered the land of the cowshed. Upon seeing the tiger, all the cows, who were lying down, got up in fear and scattered here and there on the land.**
