====== SB 9.2.27 ======

===== Text =====

> maruttasya yathā yajño
> na tathānyo 'sti kaścana
> sarvaṁ hiraṇmayaṁ tv āsīd
> yat kiñcic cāsya śobhanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maruttasya]]---of Marutta; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:y:yajñaḥ]]---performance of sacrifice; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---like that; [[synonyms:a:anyaḥ]]---any other; [[synonyms:a:asti]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kaścana]]---anything; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---everything; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇ]]-[[synonyms:m:mayam]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---there was; [[synonyms:y:yat]] [[synonyms:k:kiñcit]]---whatever he had; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of Marutta; [[synonyms:ś:śobhanam]]---extremely beautiful.

===== Translation =====

**The sacrificial paraphernalia of King Marutta was extremely beautiful, for everything was made of gold. Indeed, no other sacrifice could compare to his.**
