====== SB 9.2.21 ======

===== Text =====

> tato 'gniveśyo bhagavān
> agniḥ svayam abhūt sutaḥ
> kānīna iti vikhyāto
> jātūkarṇyo mahān ṛṣiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Devadatta; [[synonyms:a:agniveśyaḥ]]---a son named Agniveśya; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:a:agniḥ]]---the fire-god; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:k:kānīnaḥ]]---Kānīna; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vikhyātaḥ]]---was celebrated; [[synonyms:j:jātūkarṇyaḥ]]---Jātūkarṇya; [[synonyms:m:mahān]] [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṣiḥ]]---the great saintly person.

===== Translation =====

**From Devadatta came a son known as Agniveśya, who was the fire-god Agni himself. This son, who was a celebrated saint, was well known as Kānīna and Jātūkarṇya.**

===== Purport =====

Agniveśya was also known as Kānīna and Jātūkarṇya.
