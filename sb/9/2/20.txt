====== SB 9.2.20 ======

===== Text =====

> vītihotras tv indrasenāt
> tasya satyaśravā abhūt
> uruśravāḥ sutas tasya
> devadattas tato 'bhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vītihotraḥ]]---Vītihotra; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:i:indrasenāt]]---from Indrasena; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Vītihotra; [[synonyms:s:satyaśravāḥ]]---known by the name Satyaśravā; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---there was; [[synonyms:u:uruśravāḥ]]---Uruśravā; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---was the son; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of him (Satyaśravā); [[synonyms:d:devadattaḥ]]---Devadatta; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Uruśravā; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**From Indrasena came Vītihotra, from Vītihotra came Satyaśravā, from Satyaśravā came the son named Uruśravā, and from Uruśravā came Devadatta.**
