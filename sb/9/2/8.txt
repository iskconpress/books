====== SB 9.2.8 ======

===== Text =====

> manyamāno hataṁ vyāghraṁ
> pṛṣadhraḥ para-vīra-hā
> adrākṣīt sva-hatāṁ babhruṁ
> vyuṣṭāyāṁ niśi duḥkhitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manyamānaḥ]]---thinking that; [[synonyms:h:hatam]]---has been killed; [[synonyms:v:vyāghram]]---the tiger; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣadhraḥ]]---Manu's son Pṛṣadhra; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vīra]]-[[synonyms:h:hā]]---although quite able to punish the enemy; [[synonyms:a:adrākṣīt]]---saw; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:h:hatām]]---had been killed by him; [[synonyms:b:babhrum]]---the cow; [[synonyms:v:vyuṣṭāyām]] [[synonyms:n:niśi]]---when the night had passed (in the morning); [[synonyms:d:duḥkhitaḥ]]---became very much unhappy.

===== Translation =====

**In the morning, when Pṛṣadhra, who was quite able to subdue his enemy, saw that he had killed the cow although at night he thought he had killed the tiger, he was very unhappy.**
