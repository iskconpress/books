====== SB 9.7.8 ======

===== Text =====

> so 'napatyo viṣaṇṇātmā
> nāradasyopadeśataḥ
> varuṇaṁ śaraṇaṁ yātaḥ
> putro me jāyatāṁ prabho

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that Hariścandra; [[synonyms:a:anapatyaḥ]]---being without a son; [[synonyms:v:viṣaṇṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---therefore very morose; [[synonyms:n:nāradasya]]---of Nārada; [[synonyms:u:upadeśataḥ]]---by the advice; [[synonyms:v:varuṇam]]---unto Varuṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇam]] [[synonyms:y:yātaḥ]]---took shelter; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:m:me]]---of me; [[synonyms:j:jāyatām]]---let there be born; [[synonyms:p:prabho]]---O my lord.

===== Translation =====

**Hariścandra had no son and was therefore extremely morose. Once, therefore, following the advice of Nārada, he took shelter of Varuṇa and said to him "My lord I have no son. Would you kindly give me one?"**
