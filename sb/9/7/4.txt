====== SB 9.7.4 ======

===== Text =====

> trasaddasyuḥ paurukutso
> yo 'naraṇyasya deha-kṛt
> haryaśvas tat-sutas tasmāt
> prāruṇo 'tha tribandhanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:trasaddasyuḥ]]---by the name Trasaddasyu; [[synonyms:p:paurukutsaḥ]]---the son of Purukutsa; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:a:anaraṇyasya]]---of Anaraṇya; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛt]]---the father; [[synonyms:h:haryaśvaḥ]]---by the name Haryaśva; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Anaraṇya; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him (Haryaśva); [[synonyms:p:prāruṇaḥ]]---by the name Prāruṇa; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then, from Prāruṇa; [[synonyms:t:tribandhanaḥ]]---his son, Tribandhana.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Purukutsa was Trasaddasyu, who was the father of Anaraṇya. Anaraṇya's son was Haryaśva, the father of Prāruṇa. Prāruṇa was the father of Tribandhana.**
