====== SB 9.7.14 ======

===== Text =====

> punar jātā yajasveti
> sa pratyāhātha so 'bravīt
> sānnāhiko yadā rājan
> rājanyo 'tha paśuḥ śuciḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:j:jātāḥ]]---have grown; [[synonyms:y:yajasva]]---now you offer the sacrifice; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Varuṇa; [[synonyms:p:pratyāha]]---replied; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Hariścandra; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---said; [[synonyms:s:sānnāhikaḥ]]---able to equip himself with a shield; [[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Varuṇa; [[synonyms:r:rājanyaḥ]]---the //kṣatriya//; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:p:paśuḥ]]---the sacrificial animal; [[synonyms:ś:śuciḥ]]---becomes purified.

===== Translation =====

**When the teeth grew in again, Varuṇa came and said to Hariścandra, "Now you can perform the sacrifice." But Hariścandra then said, "O King, when the sacrificial animal becomes a kṣatriya and is able to shield himself to fight with the enemy, then he will be purified."**
