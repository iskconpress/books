====== SB 9.7.11 ======

===== Text =====

> nirdaśe ca sa āgatya
> yajasvety āha so 'bravīt
> dantāḥ paśor yaj jāyerann
> atha medhyo bhaved iti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirdaśe]]---after ten days; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Varuṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āgatya]]---coming there; [[synonyms:y:yajasva]]---now sacrifice; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:āha]]---said; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Hariścandra; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---replied; [[synonyms:d:dantāḥ]]---the teeth; [[synonyms:p:paśoḥ]]---of the animal; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---when; [[synonyms:j:jāyeran]]---have appeared; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:m:medhyaḥ]]---fit for being sacrificed; [[synonyms:b:bhavet]]---will become; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus.

===== Translation =====

**After ten days, Varuṇa came again and said to Hariścandra, "Now you can perform the sacrifice." Hariścandra replied, "When an animal grows teeth, then it becomes pure enough to be sacrificed."**
