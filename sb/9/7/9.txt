====== SB 9.7.9 ======

===== Text =====

> yadi vīro mahārāja
> tenaiva tvāṁ yaje iti
> tatheti varuṇenāsya
> putro jātas tu rohitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:v:vīraḥ]]---there is a son; [[synonyms:m:mahārāja]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:t:tena]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---even by that son; [[synonyms:t:tvām]]---unto you; [[synonyms:y:yaje]]---I shall offer sacrifice; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as you desire; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus accepted; [[synonyms:v:varuṇena]]---by Varuṇa; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of Mahārāja Hariścandra; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:j:jātaḥ]]---was born; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:r:rohitaḥ]]---by the name Rohita.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, Hariścandra begged Varuṇa, "My lord, if a son is born to me, with that son I shall perform a sacrifice for your satisfaction." When Hariścandra said this, Varuṇa replied, "Let it be so." Because of Varuṇa's benediction, Hariścandra begot a son named Rohita.**
