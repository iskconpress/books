====== SB 9.7.23 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmai tuṣṭo dadāv indraḥ
> śātakaumbhamayaṁ ratham
> śunaḥśephasya māhātmyam
> upariṣṭāt pracakṣyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto him, King Hariścandra; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭaḥ]]---being very pleased; [[synonyms:d:dadau]]---delivered; [[synonyms:i:indraḥ]]---the King of heaven; [[synonyms:ś:śātakaumbha]]-[[synonyms:m:mayam]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:r:ratham]]---a chariot; [[synonyms:ś:śunaḥśephasya]]---about Śunaḥśepha; [[synonyms:m:māhātmyam]]---glories; [[synonyms:u:upariṣṭāt]]---in the course of describing the sons of Viśvāmitra; [[synonyms:p:pracakṣyate]]---will be narrated.

===== Translation =====

**King Indra, being very pleased with Hariścandra, offered him a gift of a golden chariot. Śunaḥśepha's glories will be presented along with the description of the son of Viśvāmitra.**
