====== SB 9.7.10 ======

===== Text =====

> jātaḥ suto hy anenāṅga
> māṁ yajasveti so 'bravīt
> yadā paśur nirdaśaḥ syād
> atha medhyo bhaved iti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jātaḥ]]---has been born; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:anena]]---by this son; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---O Hariścandra; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---unto me; [[synonyms:y:yajasva]]---offer sacrifice; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Varuṇa; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---said; [[synonyms:y:yadā]]---when; [[synonyms:p:paśuḥ]]---an animal; [[synonyms:n:nirdaśaḥ]]---has passed ten days; [[synonyms:s:syāt]]---should become; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---then; [[synonyms:m:medhyaḥ]]---suitable for offering in sacrifice; [[synonyms:b:bhavet]]---becomes; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus (Hariścandra said).

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, when the child was born, Varuṇa approached Hariścandra and said, "Now you have a son. With this son you can offer me a sacrifice." In answer to this, Hariścandra said, "After ten days have passed since an animal's birth, the animal becomes fit to be sacrificed."**
