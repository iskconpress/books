====== SB 9.18.28 ======

===== Text =====

> tathety avasthite prāha
> devayānī manogatam
> pitrā dattā yato yāsye
> sānugā yātu mām anu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:i:iti]]---when King Vṛṣaparvā agreed to Śukrācārya's proposal; [[synonyms:a:avasthite]]---the situation being settled in this way; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---said; [[synonyms:d:devayānī]]---the daughter of Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:m:manogatam]]---her desire; [[synonyms:p:pitrā]]---by the father; [[synonyms:d:dattā]]---given; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---to whomever; [[synonyms:y:yāsye]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:a:anugā]]---with her friends; [[synonyms:y:yātu]]---shall go; [[synonyms:m:mām]] [[synonyms:a:anu]]---as my follower or servant.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing Śukrācārya's request, Vṛṣaparvā agreed to fulfill Devayānī's desire, and he awaited her words. Devayānī then expressed her desire as follows: "Whenever I marry by the order of my father, my friend Śarmiṣṭhā must go with me as my maidservant, along with her friends."**
