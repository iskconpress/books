====== SB 9.18.8 ======

===== Text =====

> tā jalāśayam āsādya
> kanyāḥ kamala-locanāḥ
> tīre nyasya dukūlāni
> vijahruḥ siñcatīr mithaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāḥ]]---they; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśayam]]---to the lakeside; [[synonyms:ā:āsādya]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kanyāḥ]]---all the girls; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:l:locanāḥ]]---with eyes like lotus petals; [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank; [[synonyms:n:nyasya]]---giving up; [[synonyms:d:dukūlāni]]---their dresses; [[synonyms:v:vijahruḥ]]---began to sport; [[synonyms:s:siñcatīḥ]]---throwing water; [[synonyms:m:mithaḥ]]---on one another.

===== Translation =====

**When the young, lotus-eyed girls came to the bank of a reservoir of water, they wanted to enjoy by bathing. Thus they left their clothing on the bank and began sporting, throwing water on one another.**
