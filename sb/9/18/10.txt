====== SB 9.18.10 ======

===== Text =====

> śarmiṣṭhājānatī vāso
> guru-putryāḥ samavyayat
> svīyaṁ matvā prakupitā
> devayānīdam abravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śarmiṣṭhā]]---the daughter of Vṛṣaparvā; [[synonyms:a:ajānatī]]---without knowledge; [[synonyms:v:vāsaḥ]]---the dress; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:p:putryāḥ]]---of Devayānī, the daughter of the //guru//; [[synonyms:s:samavyayat]]---put on the body; [[synonyms:s:svīyam]]---her own; [[synonyms:m:matvā]]---thinking; [[synonyms:p:prakupitā]]---irritated and angry; [[synonyms:d:devayānī]]---the daughter of Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---said.

===== Translation =====

**Śarmiṣṭhā unknowingly put Devayānī's dress on her own body, thus angering Devayānī, who then spoke as follows.**
