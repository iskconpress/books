====== SB 9.18.6-7 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> ekadā dānavendrasya
> śarmiṣṭhā nāma kanyakā
> sakhī-sahasra-saṁyuktā
> guru-putryā ca bhāminī
>
> devayānyā purodyāne
> puṣpita-druma-saṅkule
> vyacarat kala-gītāli-
> nalinī-puline 'balā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:ekadā]]---once upon a time; [[synonyms:d:dānava]]-[[synonyms:i:indrasya]]---of Vṛṣaparvā; [[synonyms:ś:śarmiṣṭhā]]---Śarmiṣṭhā; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by name; [[synonyms:k:kanyakā]]---a daughter; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁyuktā]]---accompanied by thousands of friends; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:p:putryā]]---with the daughter of the //guru,// Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:b:bhāminī]]---very easily irritated; [[synonyms:d:devayānyā]]---with Devayānī; [[synonyms:p:pura]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---within the palace garden; [[synonyms:p:puṣpita]]---full of flowers; [[synonyms:d:druma]]---with nice trees; [[synonyms:s:saṅkule]]---congested; [[synonyms:v:vyacarat]]---was walking; [[synonyms:k:kala]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---with very sweet sounds; [[synonyms:a:ali]]---with bumblebees; [[synonyms:n:nalinī]]---with lotuses; [[synonyms:p:puline]]---in such a garden; [[synonyms:a:abalā]]---innocent.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: One day Vṛṣaparvā's daughter Śarmiṣṭhā, who was innocent but angry by nature, was walking with Devayānī, the daughter of Śukrācārya, and with thousands of friends, in the palace garden. The garden was full of lotuses and trees of flowers and fruits and was inhabited by sweetly singing birds and bumblebees.**
