====== SB 9.18.35 ======

===== Text =====

> priyām anugataḥ kāmī
> vacobhir upamantrayan
> na prasādayituṁ śeke
> pāda-saṁvāhanādibhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:priyām]]---his beloved wife; [[synonyms:a:anugataḥ]]---following; [[synonyms:k:kāmī]]---very, very lusty; [[synonyms:v:vacobhiḥ]]---by great words; [[synonyms:u:upamantrayan]]---appeasing; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:p:prasādayitum]]---to appease; [[synonyms:ś:śeke]]---was able; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvāhana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---even by massaging her feet.

===== Translation =====

**King Yayāti, who was very lusty, followed his wife, caught her and tried to appease her by speaking pleasing words and massaging her feet, but he could not satisfy her by any means.**
