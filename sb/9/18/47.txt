====== SB 9.18.47 ======

===== Text =====

> devayāny apy anudinaṁ
> mano-vāg-deha-vastubhiḥ
> preyasaḥ paramāṁ prītim
> uvāha preyasī rahaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:devayānī]]---Mahārāja Yayāti's wife, the daughter of Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:a:anudinam]]---twenty-four hours, day after day; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vāk]]---by her mind and words; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---body; [[synonyms:v:vastubhiḥ]]---with all requisite things; [[synonyms:p:preyasaḥ]]---of her beloved husband; [[synonyms:p:paramām]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:p:prītim]]---bliss; [[synonyms:u:uvāha]]---executed; [[synonyms:p:preyasī]]---very dear to her husband; [[synonyms:r:rahaḥ]]---in seclusion, without any disturbance.

===== Translation =====

**In secluded places, engaging her mind, words, body and various paraphernalia, Devayānī, the dear wife of Mahārāja Yayāti, always brought her husband the greatest possible transcendental bliss.**
