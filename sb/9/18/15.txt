====== SB 9.18.15 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ kṣipantīṁ śarmiṣṭhā
> guru-putrīm abhāṣata
> ruṣā śvasanty uraṅgīva
> dharṣitā daṣṭa-dacchadā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kṣipantīm]]---chastising; [[synonyms:ś:śarmiṣṭhā]]---the daughter of Vṛṣaparvā; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:p:putrīm]]---unto the daughter of the //guru,// Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:a:abhāṣata]]---said; [[synonyms:r:ruṣā]]---being very angry; [[synonyms:ś:śvasantī]]---breathing very heavily; [[synonyms:u:uraṅgī]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like a serpent; [[synonyms:d:dharṣitā]]---offended, trampled; [[synonyms:d:daṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:d:dat]]-[[synonyms:c:chadā]]---biting her lip with her teeth.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: When thus rebuked in cruel words, Śarmiṣṭhā was very angry. Breathing heavily like a serpent and biting her lower lip with her teeth, she spoke to the daughter of Śukrācārya as follows.**
