====== SB 9.18.50 ======

===== Text =====

> tam eva hṛdi vinyasya
> vāsudevaṁ guhāśayam
> nārāyaṇam aṇīyāṁsaṁ
> nirāśīr ayajat prabhum

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---Him only; [[synonyms:h:hṛdi]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:v:vinyasya]]---placing; [[synonyms:v:vāsudevam]]---Lord Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:g:guha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśayam]]---who exists in everyone's heart; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇam]]---who is Nārāyaṇa, or an expansion of Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:a:aṇīyāṁsam]]---invisible to material eyes, although existing everywhere; [[synonyms:n:nirāśīḥ]]---Yayāti, without any material desires; [[synonyms:a:ayajat]]---worshiped; [[synonyms:p:prabhum]]---the Supreme Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Without material desires, Mahārāja Yayāti worshiped the Supreme Lord, who is situated in everyone's heart as Nārāyaṇa and is invisible to material eyes, although existing everywhere.**

===== Purport =====

King Yayāti, although externally seeming very fond of material enjoyment, was internally thinking of becoming an eternal servant of the Lord.
