====== SB 9.18.19 ======

===== Text =====

> dattvā svam uttaraṁ vāsas
> tasyai rājā vivāsase
> gṛhītvā pāṇinā pāṇim
> ujjahāra dayā-paraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dattvā]]---giving; [[synonyms:s:svam]]---his own; [[synonyms:u:uttaram]]---upper; [[synonyms:v:vāsaḥ]]---cloth; [[synonyms:t:tasyai]]---unto her (Devayānī); [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:v:vivāsase]]---because she was naked; [[synonyms:g:gṛhītvā]]---catching; [[synonyms:p:pāṇinā]]---with his hand; [[synonyms:p:pāṇim]]---her hand; [[synonyms:u:ujjahāra]]---delivered; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:p:paraḥ]]---being very kind.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Devayānī naked in the well, King Yayāti immediately gave her his upper cloth. Being very kind to her, he caught her hand with his own and lifted her out.**
