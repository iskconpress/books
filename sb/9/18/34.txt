====== SB 9.18.34 ======

===== Text =====

> garbha-sambhavam āsuryā
> bhartur vijñāya māninī
> devayānī pitur gehaṁ
> yayau krodha-vimūrchitā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:garbha]]-[[synonyms:s:sambhavam]]---pregnancy; [[synonyms:ā:āsuryāḥ]]---of Śarmiṣṭhā; [[synonyms:b:bhartuḥ]]---made possible by her husband; [[synonyms:v:vijñāya]]---knowing (from the //brāhmaṇa// astrologers); [[synonyms:m:māninī]]---being very proud; [[synonyms:d:devayānī]]---the daughter of Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:p:pituḥ]]---of her father; [[synonyms:g:geham]]---to the house; [[synonyms:y:yayau]]---departed; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]-[[synonyms:v:vimūrchitā]]---frenzied because of anger.

===== Translation =====

**When the proud Devayānī understood from outside sources that Śarmiṣṭhā was pregnant by her husband, she was frenzied with anger. Thus she departed for her father's house.**
