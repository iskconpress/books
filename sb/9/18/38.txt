====== SB 9.18.38 ======

===== Text =====

> iti labdha-vyavasthānaḥ
> putraṁ jyeṣṭham avocata
> yado tāta pratīcchemāṁ
> jarāṁ dehi nijaṁ vayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:l:labdha]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavasthānaḥ]]---getting the opportunity to exchange his old age; [[synonyms:p:putram]]---unto his son; [[synonyms:j:jyeṣṭham]]---the eldest; [[synonyms:a:avocata]]---he requested; [[synonyms:y:yado]]---O Yadu; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---you are my beloved son; [[synonyms:p:pratīccha]]---kindly exchange; [[synonyms:i:imām]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jarām]]---invalidity; [[synonyms:d:dehi]]---and give; [[synonyms:n:nijam]]---your own; [[synonyms:v:vayaḥ]]---youth.

===== Translation =====

**When Yayāti received this benediction from Śukrācārya, he requested his eldest son: My dear son Yadu, please give me your youth in exchange for my old age and invalidity.**
