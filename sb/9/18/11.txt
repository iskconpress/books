====== SB 9.18.11 ======

===== Text =====

> aho nirīkṣyatām asyā
> dāsyāḥ karma hy asāmpratam
> asmad-dhāryaṁ dhṛtavatī
> śunīva havir adhvare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aho]]---alas; [[synonyms:n:nirīkṣyatām]]---just see; [[synonyms:a:asyāḥ]]---of her (Śarmiṣṭhā); [[synonyms:d:dāsyāḥ]]---just like our servant; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activities; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:asāmpratam]]---without any etiquette; [[synonyms:a:asmat]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāryam]]---the garment meant for me; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtavatī]]---she has put on; [[synonyms:ś:śunī]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like a dog; [[synonyms:h:haviḥ]]---clarified butter; [[synonyms:a:adhvare]]---meant for offering in the sacrifice.

===== Translation =====

**Oh, just see the activities of this servant-maid Śarmiṣṭhā! Disregarding all etiquette, she has put on my dress, just like a dog snatching clarified butter meant for use in a sacrifice.**
