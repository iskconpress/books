====== SB 9.18.31 ======

===== Text =====

> vilokyauśanasīṁ rājañ
> charmiṣṭhā suprajāṁ kvacit
> tam eva vavre rahasi
> sakhyāḥ patim ṛtau satī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vilokya]]---by seeing; [[synonyms:a:auśanasīm]]---Devayānī, the daughter of Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:ś:śarmiṣṭhā]]---the daughter of Vṛṣaparvā; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:p:prajām]]---possessing nice children; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---at some time; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (King Yayāti); [[synonyms:e:eva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:vavre]]---requested; [[synonyms:r:rahasi]]---in a secluded place; [[synonyms:s:sakhyāḥ]]---of her friend; [[synonyms:p:patim]]---the husband; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtau]]---at the appropriate time; [[synonyms:s:satī]]---being in that position.

===== Translation =====

**O King Parīkṣit, upon seeing Devayānī with a nice son, Śarmiṣṭhā once approached King Yayāti at the appropriate time for conception. In a secluded place, she requested the King, the husband of her friend Devayānī, to enable her to have a son also.**
