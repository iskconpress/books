====== SB 9.18.18 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyāṁ gatāyāṁ sva-gṛhaṁ
> yayātir mṛgayāṁ caran
> prāpto yadṛcchayā kūpe
> jalārthī tāṁ dadarśa ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasyām]]---when she; [[synonyms:g:gatāyām]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛham]]---to her home; [[synonyms:y:yayātiḥ]]---King Yayāti; [[synonyms:m:mṛgayām]]---hunting; [[synonyms:c:caran]]---wandering; [[synonyms:p:prāptaḥ]]---arrived; [[synonyms:y:yadṛcchayā]]---by chance; [[synonyms:k:kūpe]]---in the well; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:a:arthī]]---desiring to drink water; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---her (Devayānī); [[synonyms:d:dadarśa]]---saw; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**After throwing Devayānī into the well, Śarmiṣṭhā went home. Meanwhile, King Yayāti, while engaged in a hunting excursion, went to the well to drink water and by chance saw Devayānī.**
