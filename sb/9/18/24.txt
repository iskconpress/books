====== SB 9.18.24 ======

===== Text =====

> gate rājani sā dhīre
> tatra sma rudatī pituḥ
> nyavedayat tataḥ sarvam
> uktaṁ śarmiṣṭhayā kṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gate]] [[synonyms:r:rājani]]---after the departure of the King; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---she (Devayānī); [[synonyms:d:dhīre]]---learned; [[synonyms:t:tatra]] [[synonyms:s:sma]]---returning to her home; [[synonyms:r:rudatī]]---crying; [[synonyms:p:pituḥ]]---before her father; [[synonyms:n:nyavedayat]]---submitted; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---all; [[synonyms:u:uktam]]---mentioned; [[synonyms:ś:śarmiṣṭhayā]]---by Śarmiṣṭhā; [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---done.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, when the learned King returned to his palace, Devayānī returned home crying and told her father, Śukrācārya, about all that had happened because of Śarmiṣṭhā. She told how she had been thrown into the well but was saved by the King.**
