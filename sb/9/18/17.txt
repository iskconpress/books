====== SB 9.18.17 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ-vidhaiḥ suparuṣaiḥ
> kṣiptvācārya-sutāṁ satīm
> śarmiṣṭhā prākṣipat kūpe
> vāsaś cādāya manyunā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhaiḥ]]---such; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:p:paruṣaiḥ]]---by unkind words; [[synonyms:k:kṣiptvā]]---after chastising; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:sutām]]---the daughter of Śukrācārya; [[synonyms:s:satīm]]---Devayānī; [[synonyms:ś:śarmiṣṭhā]]---Śarmiṣṭhā; [[synonyms:p:prākṣipat]]---threw (her); [[synonyms:k:kūpe]]---into a well; [[synonyms:v:vāsaḥ]]---the garments; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:ā:ādāya]]---taking away; [[synonyms:m:manyunā]]---because of anger.

===== Translation =====

**Using such unkind words, Śarmiṣṭhā rebuked Devayānī, the daughter of Śukrācārya. In anger, she took away Devayānī's garments and threw Devayānī into a well.**
