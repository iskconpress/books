====== SB 9.16.13 ======

===== Text =====

> reṇukā duḥkha-śokārtā
> nighnanty ātmānam ātmanā
> rāma rāmeti tāteti
> vicukrośoccakaiḥ satī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:reṇukā]]---Reṇukā, the wife of Jamadagni; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śoka]]-[[synonyms:a:artā]]---being very much aggrieved in lamentation (over her husband's death); [[synonyms:n:nighnantī]]---striking; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---her own body; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanā]]---by herself; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---O Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---O Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:t:tāta]]---O my dear son; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vicukrośa]]---began to cry; [[synonyms:u:uccakaiḥ]]---very loudly; [[synonyms:s:satī]]---the most chaste woman.

===== Translation =====

**Lamenting in grief for the death of her husband, the most chaste Reṇukā struck her own body with her hands and cried very loudly, "O Rāma, my dear son Rāma!"**
