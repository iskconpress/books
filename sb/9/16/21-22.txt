====== SB 9.16.21-22 ======

===== Text =====

> dadau prācīṁ diśaṁ hotre
> brahmaṇe dakṣiṇāṁ diśam
> adhvaryave pratīcīṁ vai
> udgātre uttarāṁ diśam
>
> anyebhyo 'vāntara-diśaḥ
> kaśyapāya ca madhyataḥ
> āryāvartam upadraṣṭre
> sadasyebhyas tataḥ param

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dadau]]---gave as a gift; [[synonyms:p:prācīm]]---eastern; [[synonyms:d:diśam]]---direction; [[synonyms:h:hotre]]---unto the priest known as //hotā//; [[synonyms:b:brahmaṇe]]---unto the priest known as //brahmā//; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇām]]---southern; [[synonyms:d:diśam]]---direction; [[synonyms:a:adhvaryave]]---unto the priest known as //adhvaryu//; [[synonyms:p:pratīcīm]]---the western side; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:u:udgātre]]---unto the priest known as //udgātā//; [[synonyms:u:uttarām]]---northern; [[synonyms:d:diśam]]---side; [[synonyms:a:anyebhyaḥ]]---unto the others; [[synonyms:a:avāntara]]-[[synonyms:d:diśaḥ]]---the different corners (northeast, southeast, northwest and southwest); [[synonyms:k:kaśyapāya]]---unto Kaśyapa Muni; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:m:madhyataḥ]]---the middle portion; [[synonyms:ā:āryāvartam]]---the portion known as Āryāvarta; [[synonyms:u:upadraṣṭre]]---unto the //upadraṣṭā,// the priest acting as overseer to hear and check the //mantras//; [[synonyms:s:sadasyebhyaḥ]]---unto the //sadasyas,// the associate priests; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]] [[synonyms:p:param]]---whatever remained.

===== Translation =====

**After completing the sacrifice, Lord Paraśurāma gave the eastern direction to the hotā as a gift, the south to the brahmā, the west to the adhvaryu, the north to the udgātā, and the four corners---northeast, southeast, northwest and southwest---to the other priests. He gave the middle to Kaśyapa and the place known as Āryāvarta to the upadraṣṭā. Whatever remained he distributed among the sadasyas, the associate priests.**

===== Purport =====

The tract of land in India between the Himalaya Mountains and the Vindhya Hills is called Āryāvarta.
