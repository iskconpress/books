====== SB 9.16.2 ======

===== Text =====

> kadācid reṇukā yātā
> gaṅgāyāṁ padma-mālinam
> gandharva-rājaṁ krīḍantam
> apsarobhir apaśyata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kadācit]]---once upon a time; [[synonyms:r:reṇukā]]---Jamadagni's wife, the mother of Lord Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:y:yātā]]---went; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāyām]]---to the bank of the River Ganges; [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:m:mālinam]]---decorated with a garland of lotus flowers; [[synonyms:g:gandharva]]-[[synonyms:r:rājam]]---the King of the Gandharvas; [[synonyms:k:krīḍantam]]---sporting; [[synonyms:a:apsarobhiḥ]]---with the Apsarās (heavenly society girls); [[synonyms:a:apaśyata]]---she saw.

===== Translation =====

**Once when Reṇukā, the wife of Jamadagni, went to the bank of the Ganges to get water, she saw the King of the Gandharvas, decorated with a garland of lotuses and sporting in the Ganges with celestial women [Apsarās].**
