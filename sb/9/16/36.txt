====== SB 9.16.36 ======

===== Text =====

> eṣa vaḥ kuśikā vīro
> devarātas tam anvita
> anye cāṣṭaka-hārīta-
> jaya-kratumad-ādayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---this (Śunaḥśepha); [[synonyms:v:vaḥ]]---like you; [[synonyms:k:kuśikāḥ]]---O Kuśikas; [[synonyms:v:vīraḥ]]---my son; [[synonyms:d:devarātaḥ]]---he is known as Devarāta; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:a:anvita]]---just obey; [[synonyms:a:anye]]---others; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭaka]]---Aṣṭaka; [[synonyms:h:hārīta]]---Hārīta; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---Jaya; [[synonyms:k:kratumat]]---Kratumān; [[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---and others.

===== Translation =====

**Viśvāmitra said, "O Kuśikas [descendants of Kauśika], this Devarāta is my son and is one of you. Please obey his orders." O King Parīkṣit, Viśvāmitra had many other sons, such as Aṣṭaka, Hārīta, Jaya and Kratumān.**
