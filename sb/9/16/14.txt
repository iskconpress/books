====== SB 9.16.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tad upaśrutya dūrasthā
> hā rāmety ārtavat svanam
> tvarayāśramam āsādya
> dadṛśuḥ pitaraṁ hatam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]---that crying of Reṇukā; [[synonyms:u:upaśrutya]]---upon hearing; [[synonyms:d:dūra]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāḥ]]---although staying a long distance away; [[synonyms:h:hā]] [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---O Rāma, O Rāma; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:ārta]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---very aggrieved; [[synonyms:s:svanam]]---the sound; [[synonyms:t:tvarayā]]---very hastily; [[synonyms:ā:āśramam]]---to the residence of Jamadagni; [[synonyms:ā:āsādya]]---coming; [[synonyms:d:dadṛśuḥ]]---saw; [[synonyms:p:pitaram]]---the father; [[synonyms:h:hatam]]---killed.

===== Translation =====

**Although the sons of Jamadagni, including Lord Paraśurāma, were a long distance from home, as soon as they heard Reṇukā loudly calling "O Rāma, O my son," they hastily returned to the āśrama, where they saw their father already killed.**
