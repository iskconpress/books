====== SB 9.16.12 ======

===== Text =====

> yācyamānāḥ kṛpaṇayā
> rāma-mātrātidāruṇāḥ
> prasahya śira utkṛtya
> ninyus te kṣatra-bandhavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yācyamānāḥ]]---being begged for the life of her husband; [[synonyms:k:kṛpaṇayā]]---by the poor unprotected woman; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:m:mātrā]]---by the mother of Lord Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:d:dāruṇāḥ]]---very cruel; [[synonyms:p:prasahya]]---by force; [[synonyms:ś:śiraḥ]]---the head of Jamadagni; [[synonyms:u:utkṛtya]]---having separated; [[synonyms:n:ninyuḥ]]---took away; [[synonyms:t:te]]---the sons of Kārtavīryārjuna; [[synonyms:k:kṣatra]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhavaḥ]]---not //kṣatriyas,// but the most abominable sons of //kṣatriyas.//

===== Translation =====

**With pitiable prayers, Reṇukā, the mother of Paraśurāma and wife of Jamadagni, begged for the life of her husband. But the sons of Kārtavīryārjuna, being devoid of the qualities of kṣatriyas, were so cruel that despite her prayers they forcibly cut off his head and took it away.**
