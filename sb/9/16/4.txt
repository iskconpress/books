====== SB 9.16.4 ======

===== Text =====

> kālātyayaṁ taṁ vilokya
> muneḥ śāpa-viśaṅkitā
> āgatya kalaśaṁ tasthau
> purodhāya kṛtāñjaliḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:a:atyayam]]---passing the time; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vilokya]]---observing; [[synonyms:m:muneḥ]]---of the great sage Jamadagni; [[synonyms:ś:śāpa]]-[[synonyms:v:viśaṅkitā]]---being afraid of the curse; [[synonyms:ā:āgatya]]---returning; [[synonyms:k:kalaśam]]---the waterpot; [[synonyms:t:tasthau]]---stood; [[synonyms:p:purodhāya]]---putting in front of the sage; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:añjaliḥ]]---with folded hands.

===== Translation =====

**Later, understanding that the time for offering the sacrifice had passed, Reṇukā feared a curse from her husband. Therefore when she returned she simply put the waterpot before him and stood there with folded hands.**
