====== SB 9.16.25 ======

===== Text =====

> jāmadagnyo 'pi bhagavān
> rāmaḥ kamala-locanaḥ
> āgāminy antare rājan
> vartayiṣyati vai bṛhat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jāmadagnyaḥ]]---the son of Jamadagni; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:r:rāmaḥ]]---Lord Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:l:locanaḥ]]---whose eyes are like lotus petals; [[synonyms:ā:āgāmini]]---coming; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---in the //manvantara,// the time of one Manu; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:v:vartayiṣyati]]---will propound; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:b:bṛhat]]---Vedic knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**My dear King Parīkṣit, in the next manvantara the lotus-eyed Personality of Godhead Lord Paraśurāma, the son of Jamadagni, will be a great propounder of Vedic knowledge. In other words, he will be one of the seven sages.**
