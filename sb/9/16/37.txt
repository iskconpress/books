====== SB 9.16.37 ======

===== Text =====

> evaṁ kauśika-gotraṁ tu
> viśvāmitraiḥ pṛthag-vidham
> pravarāntaram āpannaṁ
> tad dhi caivaṁ prakalpitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way (some sons having been cursed and some blessed); [[synonyms:k:kauśika]]-[[synonyms:g:gotram]]---the dynasty of Kauśika; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:viśvāmitraiḥ]]---by the sons of Viśvāmitra; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]-[[synonyms:v:vidham]]---in different varieties; [[synonyms:p:pravara]]-[[synonyms:a:antaram]]---differences between one another; [[synonyms:ā:āpannam]]---obtained; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:p:prakalpitam]]---ascertained.

===== Translation =====

**Viśvāmitra cursed some of his sons and blessed the others, and he also adopted a son. Thus there were varieties in the Kauśika dynasty, but among all the sons, Devarāta was considered the eldest.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Ninth Canto, Sixteenth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "Lord Paraśurāma Destroys the World's Ruling Class."
