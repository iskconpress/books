====== SB 9.16.17 ======

===== Text =====

> gatvā māhiṣmatīṁ rāmo
> brahma-ghna-vihata-śriyam
> teṣāṁ sa śīrṣabhī rājan
> madhye cakre mahā-girim

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gatvā]]---going; [[synonyms:m:māhiṣmatīm]]---to the place known as Māhiṣmatī; [[synonyms:r:rāmaḥ]]---Lord Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:g:ghna]]---because of the killing of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vihata]]-[[synonyms:ś:śriyam]]---doomed, bereft of all opulences; [[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---of all of them (the sons of Kārtavīryārjuna and the other //kṣatriya// inhabitants); [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, Lord Paraśurāma; [[synonyms:ś:śīrṣabhiḥ]]---by the heads cut off from their bodies; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within the jurisdiction of Māhiṣmatī; [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---made; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:g:girim]]---a great mountain.

===== Translation =====

**O King, Lord Paraśurāma then went to Māhiṣmatī, which was already doomed by the sinful killing of a brāhmaṇa. In the midst of that city he made a mountain of heads, severed from the bodies of the sons of Kārtavīryārjuna.**
