====== SB 9.9.35 ======

===== Text =====

> yasmān me bhakṣitaḥ pāpa
> kāmārtāyāḥ patis tvayā
> tavāpi mṛtyur ādhānād
> akṛta-prajña darśitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yasmāt]]---because; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣitaḥ]]---was eaten up; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]---O sinful one; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārtāyāḥ]]---of a woman very much bereaved because of sexual desire; [[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---husband; [[synonyms:t:tvayā]]---by you; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---your; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:m:mṛtyuḥ]]---death; [[synonyms:ā:ādhānāt]]---when you try to discharge semen in your wife; [[synonyms:a:akṛta]]-[[synonyms:p:prajña]]---O foolish rascal; [[synonyms:d:darśitaḥ]]---this curse is placed upon you.

===== Translation =====

**O foolish, sinful person, because you have eaten my husband when I was sexually inclined and desiring to have the seed of a child, I shall also see you die when you attempt to discharge semen in your wife. In other words, whenever you attempt to sexually unite with your wife, you shall die.**
