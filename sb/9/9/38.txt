====== SB 9.9.38 ======

===== Text =====

> ata ūrdhvaṁ sa tatyāja
> strī-sukhaṁ karmaṇāprajāḥ
> vasiṣṭhas tad-anujñāto
> madayantyāṁ prajām adhāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataḥ]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhvam]]---in the near future; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he, the King; [[synonyms:t:tatyāja]]---gave up; [[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:s:sukham]]---the happiness of sexual intercourse; [[synonyms:k:karmaṇā]]---by destiny; [[synonyms:a:aprajāḥ]]---remained sonless; [[synonyms:v:vasiṣṭhaḥ]]---the great saint Vasiṣṭha; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:a:anujñātaḥ]]---being permitted by the King to beget a son; [[synonyms:m:madayantyām]]---in the womb of Madayantī, King Saudāsa's wife; [[synonyms:p:prajām]]---a child; [[synonyms:a:adhāt]]---begot.

===== Translation =====

**After being thus instructed, the King gave up the future happiness of sexual intercourse and by destiny remained sonless. Later, with the King's permission, the great saint Vasiṣṭha begot a child in the womb of Madayantī.**
