====== SB 9.9.25 ======

===== Text =====

> rākṣasaṁ bhāvam āpannaḥ
> pāde kalmāṣatāṁ gataḥ
> vyavāya-kāle dadṛśe
> vanauko-dampatī dvijau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rākṣasam]]---man-eating; [[synonyms:b:bhāvam]]---propensity; [[synonyms:ā:āpannaḥ]]---having gotten; [[synonyms:p:pāde]]---on the leg; [[synonyms:k:kalmāṣatām]]---a black spot; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---obtained; [[synonyms:v:vyavāya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time of sexual intercourse; [[synonyms:d:dadṛśe]]---he saw; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:o:okaḥ]]---living in the forest; [[synonyms:d:dam]]-[[synonyms:p:patī]]---a husband and wife; [[synonyms:d:dvijau]]---who were //brāhmaṇas.//

===== Translation =====

**Saudāsa thus acquired the propensity of a man-eater and received on his leg a black spot, for which he was known as Kalmāṣapāda. Once King Kalmāṣapāda saw a brāhmaṇa couple engaged in sexual intercourse in the forest.**
