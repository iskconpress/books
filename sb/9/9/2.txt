====== SB 9.9.2 ======

===== Text =====

> dilīpas tat-sutas tadvad
> aśaktaḥ kālam eyivān
> bhagīrathas tasya sutas
> tepe sa sumahat tapaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dilīpaḥ]]---named Dilīpa; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Aṁśumān; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like his father; [[synonyms:a:aśaktaḥ]]---being unable to bring the Ganges to the material world; [[synonyms:k:kālam]] [[synonyms:e:eyivān]]---became a victim of time and died; [[synonyms:b:bhagīrathaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tasya]] [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son Bhagīratha; [[synonyms:t:tepe]]---executed penance; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:m:mahat]]---very great; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerity.

===== Translation =====

**Like Aṁśumān himself, Dilīpa, his son, was unable to bring the Ganges to this material world, and he also became a victim of death in due course of time. Then Dilīpa's son, Bhagīratha, performed very severe austerities to bring the Ganges to this material world.**
