====== SB 9.9.22 ======

===== Text =====

> parivekṣyamāṇaṁ bhagavān
> vilokyābhakṣyam añjasā
> rājānam aśapat kruddho
> rakṣo hy evaṁ bhaviṣyasi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parivekṣyamāṇam]]---while examining the eatables; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:v:vilokya]]---when he saw; [[synonyms:a:abhakṣyam]]---unfit for consumption; [[synonyms:a:añjasā]]---very easily by his mystic power; [[synonyms:r:rājānam]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:a:aśapat]]---cursed; [[synonyms:k:kruddhaḥ]]---being very angry; [[synonyms:r:rakṣaḥ]]---a man-eater; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bhaviṣyasi]]---you shall become.

===== Translation =====

**While examining the food given to him, Vasiṣṭha Muni, by his mystic power, could understand that it was unfit to eat, being the flesh of a human being. He was very angry at this and immediately cursed Saudāsa to become a man-eater.**
