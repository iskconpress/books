====== SB 9.9.19 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rājovāca
> kiṁ nimitto guroḥ śāpaḥ
> saudāsasya mahātmanaḥ
> etad veditum icchāmaḥ
> kathyatāṁ na raho yadi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---King Parīkṣit said; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:n:nimittaḥ]]---for what reason; [[synonyms:g:guroḥ]]---of the spiritual master; [[synonyms:ś:śāpaḥ]]---curse; [[synonyms:s:saudāsasya]]---of Saudāsa; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---of the great soul; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:v:veditum]]---to know; [[synonyms:i:icchāmaḥ]]---I wish; [[synonyms:k:kathyatām]]---please tell me; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:r:rahaḥ]]---confidential; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if.

===== Translation =====

**King Parīkṣit said: O Śukadeva Gosvāmī, why did Vasiṣṭha, the spiritual master of Saudāsa, curse that great soul? I wish to know of this. If it is not a confidential matter, please describe it to me.**
