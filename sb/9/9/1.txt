====== SB 9.9.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> aṁśumāṁś ca tapas tepe
> gaṅgānayana-kāmyayā
> kālaṁ mahāntaṁ nāśaknot
> tataḥ kālena saṁsthitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:a:aṁśumān]]---the king named Aṁśumān; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]] [[synonyms:t:tepe]]---executed austerity; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]---the Ganges; [[synonyms:ā:ānayana]]-[[synonyms:k:kāmyayā]]---with a desire to bring the Ganges to this material world to deliver his forefathers; [[synonyms:k:kālam]]---time; [[synonyms:m:mahāntam]]---for a long duration; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:aśaknot]]---was successful; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:kālena]]---in due course of time; [[synonyms:s:saṁsthitaḥ]]---died.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: King Aṁśumān, like his grandfather, performed austerities for a very long time. Nonetheless, he could not bring the Ganges to this material world, and thereafter, in due course of time, he died.**
