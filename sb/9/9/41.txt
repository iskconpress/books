====== SB 9.9.41 ======

===== Text =====

> tato daśarathas tasmāt
> putra aiḍaviḍis tataḥ
> rājā viśvasaho yasya
> khaṭvāṅgaś cakravarty abhūt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Bālika; [[synonyms:d:daśarathaḥ]]---a son named Daśaratha; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:a:aiḍaviḍiḥ]]---named Aiḍaviḍi; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:v:viśvasahaḥ]]---the famous King Viśvasaha was born; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom; [[synonyms:k:khaṭvāṅgaḥ]]---the king named Khaṭvāṅga; [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---emperor; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**From Bālika came a son named Daśaratha, from Daśaratha came a son named Aiḍaviḍi, and from Aiḍaviḍi came King Viśvasaha. The son of King Viśvasaha was the famous Mahārāja Khaṭvāṅga.**
