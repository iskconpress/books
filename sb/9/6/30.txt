====== SB 9.6.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ kāla upāvṛtte
> kukṣiṁ nirbhidya dakṣiṇam
> yuvanāśvasya tanayaś
> cakravartī jajāna ha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---time; [[synonyms:u:upāvṛtte]]---being mature; [[synonyms:k:kukṣim]]---the lower part of the abdomen; [[synonyms:n:nirbhidya]]---piercing; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇam]]---the right side; [[synonyms:y:yuvanāśvasya]]---of King Yuvanāśva; [[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---a son; [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---with all the good symptoms of a king; [[synonyms:j:jajāna]]---generated; [[synonyms:h:ha]]---in the past.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, in due course of time, a son with all the good symptoms of a powerful king came forth from the lower right side of King Yuvanāśva's abdomen.**
