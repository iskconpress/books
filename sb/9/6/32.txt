====== SB 9.6.32 ======

===== Text =====

> na mamāra pitā tasya
> vipra-deva-prasādataḥ
> yuvanāśvo 'tha tatraiva
> tapasā siddhim anvagāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mamāra]]---died; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---the father; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of the baby; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:p:prasādataḥ]]---because of the mercy and blessings of the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:y:yuvanāśvaḥ]]---King Yuvanāśva; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tatra]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---in that very place; [[synonyms:t:tapasā]]---by executing austerity; [[synonyms:s:siddhim]]---perfection; [[synonyms:a:anvagāt]]---achieved.

===== Translation =====

**Because Yuvanāśva, the father of the baby, was blessed by the brāhmaṇas, he did not fall a victim to death. After this incident, he performed severe austerities and achieved perfection in that very spot.**
