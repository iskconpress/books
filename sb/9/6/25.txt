====== SB 9.6.25 ======

===== Text =====

> bahulāśvo nikumbhasya
> kṛśāśvo 'thāsya senajit
> yuvanāśvo 'bhavat tasya
> so 'napatyo vanaṁ gataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahulāśvaḥ]]---of the name Bahulāśva; [[synonyms:n:nikumbhasya]]---of Nikumbha; [[synonyms:k:kṛśāśvaḥ]]---of the name Kṛśāśva; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of Kṛśāśva; [[synonyms:s:senajit]]---Senajit; [[synonyms:y:yuvanāśvaḥ]]---of the name Yuvanāśva; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---was born; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Senajit; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:a:anapatyaḥ]]---without any sons; [[synonyms:v:vanam]] [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---retired to the forest as a //vānaprastha.//

===== Translation =====

**The son of Nikumbha was Bahulāśva, the son of Bahulāśva was Kṛśāśva, the son of Kṛśāśva was Senajit, and the son of Senajit was Yuvanāśva. Yuvanāśva had no sons, and thus he retired from family life and went to the forest.**
