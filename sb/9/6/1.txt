====== SB 9.6.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> virūpaḥ ketumāñ chambhur
> ambarīṣa-sutās trayaḥ
> virūpāt pṛṣadaśvo 'bhūt
> tat-putras tu rathītaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:v:virūpaḥ]]---by the name Virūpa; [[synonyms:k:ketumān]]---by the name Ketumān; [[synonyms:ś:śambhuḥ]]---by the name Śambhu; [[synonyms:a:ambarīṣa]]---of Ambarīṣa Mahārāja; [[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]] [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]]---the three sons; [[synonyms:v:virūpāt]]---from Virūpa; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣadaśvaḥ]]---of the name Pṛṣadaśva; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---there was; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rathītaraḥ]]---of the name Rathītara.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, Ambarīṣa had three sons, named Virūpa, Ketumān and Śambhu. From Virūpa came a son named Pṛṣadaśva, and from Pṛṣadaśva came a son named Rathītara.**
