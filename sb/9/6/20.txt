====== SB 9.6.20 ======

===== Text =====

> purañjayasya putro 'bhūd
> anenās tat-sutaḥ pṛthuḥ
> viśvagandhis tataś candro
> yuvanāśvas tu tat-sutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purañjayasya]]---of Purañjaya; [[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---was born; [[synonyms:a:anenāḥ]]---by the name Anenā; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:p:pṛthuḥ]]---of the name Pṛthu; [[synonyms:v:viśvagandhiḥ]]---of the name Viśvagandhi; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:c:candraḥ]]---by the name Candra; [[synonyms:y:yuvanāśvaḥ]]---of the name Yuvanāśva; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---indeed; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Purañjaya was known as Anenā, Anenā's son was Pṛthu, and Pṛthu's son was Viśvagandhi. Viśvagandhi's son was Candra, and Candra's son was Yuvanāśva.**
