====== SB 9.6.23-24 ======

===== Text =====

> dhundhumāra iti khyātas
> tat-sutās te ca jajvaluḥ
> dhundhor mukhāgninā sarve
> traya evāvaśeṣitāḥ
>
> dṛḍhāśvaḥ kapilāśvaś ca
> bhadrāśva iti bhārata
> dṛḍhāśva-putro haryaśvo
> nikumbhas tat-sutaḥ smṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhundhu]]-[[synonyms:m:māraḥ]]---the killer of Dhundhu; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:khyātaḥ]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutāḥ]]---his sons; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:j:jajvaluḥ]]---burned; [[synonyms:d:dhundhoḥ]]---of Dhundhu; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:a:agninā]]---by the fire emanating from the mouth; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all of them; [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]]---three; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣitāḥ]]---remained alive; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍhāśvaḥ]]---Dṛḍhāśva; [[synonyms:k:kapilāśvaḥ]]---Kapilāśva; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bhadrāśvaḥ]]---Bhadrāśva; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]---O Mahārāja Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍhāśva]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son of Dṛḍhāśva; [[synonyms:h:haryaśvaḥ]]---named Haryaśva; [[synonyms:n:nikumbhaḥ]]---Nikumbha; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---well known.

===== Translation =====

**O Mahārāja Parīkṣit, for this reason Kuvalayāśva is celebrated as Dhundhumāra ["the killer of Dhundhu"]. All but three of his sons, however, were burned to ashes by the fire emanating from Dhundhu's mouth. The remaining sons were Dṛḍhāśva, Kapilāśva and Bhadrāśva. From Dṛḍhāśva came a son named Haryaśva, whose son is celebrated as Nikumbha.**
