====== SB 9.6.28 ======

===== Text =====

> utthitās te niśamyātha
> vyudakaṁ kalaśaṁ prabho
> papracchuḥ kasya karmedaṁ
> pītaṁ puṁsavanaṁ jalam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:utthitāḥ]]---after awakening; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:n:niśamya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:v:vyudakam]]---empty; [[synonyms:k:kalaśam]]---the waterpot; [[synonyms:p:prabho]]---O King Parīkṣit; [[synonyms:p:papracchuḥ]]---inquired; [[synonyms:k:kasya]]---whose; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---act; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pītam]]---drunk; [[synonyms:p:puṁsavanam]]---which was to cause the birth of a child; [[synonyms:j:jalam]]---water.

===== Translation =====

**When the brāhmaṇas got up from bed and saw the waterpot empty, they inquired who had done this work of drinking the water meant for begetting a child.**
