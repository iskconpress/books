====== SB 9.6.43 ======

===== Text =====

> muniḥ praveśitaḥ kṣatrā
> kanyāntaḥpuram ṛddhimat
> vṛtaḥ sa rāja-kanyābhir
> ekaṁ pañcāśatā varaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:muniḥ]]---Saubhari Muni; [[synonyms:p:praveśitaḥ]]---admitted; [[synonyms:k:kṣatrā]]---by the palace messenger; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]-[[synonyms:a:antaḥpuram]]---into the residential quarters of the princesses; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛddhi]]-[[synonyms:m:mat]]---extremely opulent in all respects; [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]]---accepted; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:k:kanyābhiḥ]]---by all the princesses; [[synonyms:e:ekam]]---he alone; [[synonyms:p:pañcāśatā]]---by all fifty; [[synonyms:v:varaḥ]]---the husband.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, when Saubhari Muni became quite a young and beautiful person, the messenger of the palace took him inside the residential quarters of the princesses, which were extremely opulent. All fifty princesses then accepted him as their husband, although he was only one man.**
