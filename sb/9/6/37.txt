====== SB 9.6.37 ======

===== Text =====

> yāvat sūrya udeti sma
> yāvac ca pratitiṣṭhati
> tat sarvaṁ yauvanāśvasya
> māndhātuḥ kṣetram ucyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long as; [[synonyms:s:sūryaḥ]]---the sun; [[synonyms:u:udeti]]---has risen on the horizon; [[synonyms:s:sma]]---in the past; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long as; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:p:pratitiṣṭhati]]---continues to stay; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---all those things mentioned above; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---everything; [[synonyms:y:yauvanāśvasya]]---of the son of Yuvanāśva; [[synonyms:m:māndhātuḥ]]---called Māndhātā; [[synonyms:k:kṣetram]]---location; [[synonyms:u:ucyate]]---is said to be.

===== Translation =====

**All places, from where the sun rises on the horizon, shining brilliantly, to where the sun sets, are known as the possession of the celebrated Māndhātā, the son of Yuvanāśva.**
