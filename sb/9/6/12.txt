====== SB 9.6.12 ======

===== Text =====

> purañjayas tasya suta
> indravāha itīritaḥ
> kakutstha iti cāpy uktaḥ
> śṛṇu nāmāni karmabhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puram]]-[[synonyms:j:jayaḥ]]---Purañjaya ("the conqueror of the residence"); [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---his (Vikukṣi's); [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:i:indra]]-[[synonyms:v:vāhaḥ]]---Indravāha ("he whose carrier is Indra"); [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ī:īritaḥ]]---known as such; [[synonyms:k:kakutsthaḥ]]---Kakutstha ("situated on the hump of a bull"); [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:api]]---indeed; [[synonyms:u:uktaḥ]]---known as such; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---just hear; [[synonyms:n:nāmāni]]---all the names; [[synonyms:k:karmabhiḥ]]---according to one's work.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Saśāda was Purañjaya, who is also known as Indravāha and sometimes as Kakutstha. Please hear from me how he received different names for different activities.**
