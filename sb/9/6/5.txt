====== SB 9.6.5 ======

===== Text =====

> teṣāṁ purastād abhavann
> āryāvarte nṛpā nṛpa
> pañca-viṁśatiḥ paścāc ca
> trayo madhye 'pare 'nyataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṣām]]---among all of those sons; [[synonyms:p:purastāt]]---on the eastern side; [[synonyms:a:abhavan]]---they became; [[synonyms:ā:āryāvarte]]---in the place within the Himalaya and Vindhya mountains known as Āryāvarta; [[synonyms:n:nṛpāḥ]]---kings; [[synonyms:n:nṛpa]]---O King (Mahārāja Parīkṣit); [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:v:viṁśatiḥ]]---twenty-five; [[synonyms:p:paścāt]]---on the western side; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]]---three of them; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle (between east and west); [[synonyms:a:apare]]---others; [[synonyms:a:anyataḥ]]---in other places.

===== Translation =====

**Of the one hundred sons, twenty-five became kings in the western side of Āryāvarta, a place between the Himalaya and Vindhya mountains. Another twenty-five sons became kings in the east of Āryāvarta, and the three principal sons became kings in the middle. The other sons became kings in various other places.**
