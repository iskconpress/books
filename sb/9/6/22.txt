====== SB 9.6.22 ======

===== Text =====

> yaḥ priyārtham utaṅkasya
> dhundhu-nāmāsuraṁ balī
> sutānām eka-viṁśatyā
> sahasrair ahanad vṛtaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---he who; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:a:artham]]---for the satisfaction; [[synonyms:u:utaṅkasya]]---of the great sage Utaṅka; [[synonyms:d:dhundhu]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the name Dhundhu; [[synonyms:a:asuram]]---a demon; [[synonyms:b:balī]]---very powerful (Kuvalayāśva); [[synonyms:s:sutānām]]---of sons; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:v:viṁśatyā]]---by twenty-one; [[synonyms:s:sahasraiḥ]]---thousands; [[synonyms:a:ahanat]]---killed; [[synonyms:v:vṛtaḥ]]---surrounded.

===== Translation =====

**To satisfy the sage Utaṅka, the greatly powerful Kuvalayāśva killed a demon named Dhundhu. He did this with the assistance of his twenty-one thousand sons.**
