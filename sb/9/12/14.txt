====== SB 9.12.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tasmāc chākyo 'tha śuddhodo
> lāṅgalas tat-sutaḥ smṛtaḥ
> tataḥ prasenajit tasmāt
> kṣudrako bhavitā tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Sañjaya; [[synonyms:ś:śākyaḥ]]---Śākya; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ś:śuddhodaḥ]]---Śuddhoda; [[synonyms:l:lāṅgalaḥ]]---Lāṅgala; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---the son of Śuddhoda; [[synonyms:s:smṛtaḥ]]---is well known; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:p:prasenajit]]---Prasenajit; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Prasenajit; [[synonyms:k:kṣudrakaḥ]]---Kṣudraka; [[synonyms:b:bhavitā]]---will take birth; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter.

===== Translation =====

**From Sañjaya will come Śākya, from Śākya will come Śuddhoda, and from Śuddhoda will come Lāṅgala. From Lāṅgala will come Prasenajit, and from Prasenajit, Kṣudraka.**
