====== SB 9.12.12 ======

===== Text =====

> bhavitā marudevo 'tha
> sunakṣatro 'tha puṣkaraḥ
> tasyāntarikṣas tat-putraḥ
> sutapās tad amitrajit

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhavitā]]---will be born; [[synonyms:m:marudevaḥ]]---Marudeva; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sunakṣatraḥ]]---Sunakṣatra; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:puṣkaraḥ]]---Puṣkara, a son of Sunakṣatra; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Puṣkara; [[synonyms:a:antarikṣaḥ]]---Antarikṣa; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:s:sutapāḥ]]---Sutapā; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---from him; [[synonyms:a:amitrajit]]---a son named Amitrajit.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, from Supratīka will come Marudeva; from Marudeva, Sunakṣatra; from Sunakṣatra, Puṣkara; and from Puṣkara, Antarikṣa. The son of Antarikṣa will be Sutapā, and his son will be Amitrajit.**
