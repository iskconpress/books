====== SB 9.12.8 ======

===== Text =====

> tataḥ prasenajit tasmāt
> takṣako bhavitā punaḥ
> tato bṛhadbalo yas tu
> pitrā te samare hataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Viśvabāhu; [[synonyms:p:prasenajit]]---a son named Prasenajit was born; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:t:takṣakaḥ]]---Takṣaka; [[synonyms:b:bhavitā]]---would take birth; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from him; [[synonyms:b:bṛhadbalaḥ]]---a son named Bṛhadbala; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---he who; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:p:pitrā]]---by father; [[synonyms:t:te]]---your; [[synonyms:s:samare]]---in the fight; [[synonyms:h:hataḥ]]---killed.

===== Translation =====

**From Viśvabāhu came a son named Prasenajit, from Prasenajit came Takṣaka, and from Takṣaka came Bṛhadbala, who was killed in a fight by your father.**
