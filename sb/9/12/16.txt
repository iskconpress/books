====== SB 9.12.16 ======

===== Text =====

> ikṣvākūṇām ayaṁ vaṁśaḥ
> sumitrānto bhaviṣyati
> yatas taṁ prāpya rājānaṁ
> saṁsthāṁ prāpsyati vai kalau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ikṣvākūṇām]]---of the dynasty of King Ikṣvāku; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this (what has been described); [[synonyms:v:vaṁśaḥ]]---descendants; [[synonyms:s:sumitra]]-[[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---Sumitra being the last king of this dynasty; [[synonyms:b:bhaviṣyati]]---will appear in the future, while the Kali-yuga still continues; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---because; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him, Mahārāja Sumitra; [[synonyms:p:prāpya]]---getting; [[synonyms:r:rājānam]]---as a king in that dynasty; [[synonyms:s:saṁsthām]]---culmination; [[synonyms:p:prāpsyati]]---gets; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:k:kalau]]---at the end of Kali-yuga.

===== Translation =====

**The last king in the dynasty of Ikṣvāku will be Sumitra; after Sumitra there will be no more sons in the dynasty of the sun-god, and thus the dynasty will end.**


Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Ninth Canto, Twelfth Chapter, of the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// entitled "The Dynasty of Kuśa, the Son of Lord Rāmacandra."
