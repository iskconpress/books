====== SB 9.12.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> kuśasya cātithis tasmān
> niṣadhas tat-suto nabhaḥ
> puṇḍarīko 'tha tat-putraḥ
> kṣemadhanvābhavat tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:k:kuśasya]]---of Kuśa, the son of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:atithiḥ]]---Atithi; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from him; [[synonyms:n:niṣadhaḥ]]---Niṣadha; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:n:nabhaḥ]]---Nabha; [[synonyms:p:puṇḍarīkaḥ]]---Puṇḍarīka; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---his son; [[synonyms:k:kṣemadhanvā]]---Kṣemadhanvā; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---became; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter.

===== Translation =====

**Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: The son of Rāmacandra was Kuśa, the son of Kuśa was Atithi, the son of Atithi was Niṣadha, and the son of Niṣadha was Nabha. The son of Nabha was Puṇḍarīka, and from Puṇḍarīka came a son named Kṣemadhanvā.**
