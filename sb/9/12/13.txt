====== SB 9.12.13 ======

===== Text =====

> bṛhadrājas tu tasyāpi
> barhis tasmāt kṛtañjayaḥ
> raṇañjayas tasya sutaḥ
> sañjayo bhavitā tataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bṛhadrājaḥ]]---Bṛhadrāja; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:t:tasya]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---of Amitrajit; [[synonyms:b:barhiḥ]]---Barhi; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Barhi; [[synonyms:k:kṛtañjayaḥ]]---Kṛtañjaya; [[synonyms:r:raṇañjayaḥ]]---Raṇañjaya; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Kṛtañjaya; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:s:sañjayaḥ]]---Sañjaya; [[synonyms:b:bhavitā]]---will take birth; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Raṇañjaya.

===== Translation =====

**From Amitrajit will come a son named Bṛhadrāja, from Bṛhadrāja will come Barhi, and from Barhi will come Kṛtañjaya. The son of Kṛtañjaya will be known as Raṇañjaya, and from him will come a son named Sañjaya.**
