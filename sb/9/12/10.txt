====== SB 9.12.10 ======

===== Text =====

> ūrukriyaḥ sutas tasya
> vatsavṛddho bhaviṣyati
> prativyomas tato bhānur
> divāko vāhinī-patiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ū:ūrukriyaḥ]]---Ūrukriya; [[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---son; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Ūrukriya; [[synonyms:v:vatsavṛddhaḥ]]---Vatsavṛddha; [[synonyms:b:bhaviṣyati]]---will take birth; [[synonyms:p:prativyomaḥ]]---Prativyoma; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from Vatsavṛddha; [[synonyms:b:bhānuḥ]]---(from Prativyoma) a son named Bhānu; [[synonyms:d:divākaḥ]]---from Bhānu a son named Divāka; [[synonyms:v:vāhinī]]-[[synonyms:p:patiḥ]]---a great commander of soldiers.

===== Translation =====

**The son of Bṛhadraṇa will be Ūrukriya, who will have a son named Vatsavṛddha. Vatsavṛddha will have a son named Prativyoma, and Prativyoma will have a son named Bhānu, from whom Divāka, a great commander of soldiers, will take birth.**
