====== SB 9.12.15 ======

===== Text =====

> raṇako bhavitā tasmāt
> surathas tanayas tataḥ
> sumitro nāma niṣṭhānta
> ete bārhadbalānvayāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raṇakaḥ]]---Raṇaka; [[synonyms:b:bhavitā]]---will take birth; [[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---from Kṣudraka; [[synonyms:s:surathaḥ]]---Suratha; [[synonyms:t:tanayaḥ]]---the son; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sumitraḥ]]---Sumitra, the son of Suratha; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by the name; [[synonyms:n:niṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---the end of the dynasty; [[synonyms:e:ete]]---all the above-mentioned kings; [[synonyms:b:bārhadbala]]-[[synonyms:a:anvayāḥ]]---in the dynasty of King Bṛhadbala.

===== Translation =====

**From Kṣudraka will come Raṇaka, from Raṇaka will come Suratha, and from Suratha will come Sumitra, ending the dynasty. This is a description of the dynasty of Bṛhadbala.**
