====== SB 9.3.31 ======

===== Text =====

> tac chrutvā bhagavān brahmā
> prahasya tam uvāca ha
> aho rājan niruddhās te
> kālena hṛdi ye kṛtāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:p:prahasya]]---after laughing; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto King Kakudmī; [[synonyms:u:uvāca]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]---said; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---alas; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:n:niruddhāḥ]]---all gone; [[synonyms:t:te]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kālena]]---by the course of time; [[synonyms:h:hṛdi]]---within the core of the heart; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛtāḥ]]---who have been decided upon for acceptance as your son-in-law.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing his words, Lord Brahmā, who is most powerful, laughed loudly and said to Kakudmī: O King, all those whom you may have decided within the core of your heart to accept as your son-in-law have passed away in the course of time.**
