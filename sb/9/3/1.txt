====== SB 9.3.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śuka uvāca
> śaryātir mānavo rājā
> brahmiṣṭhaḥ sambabhūva ha
> yo vā aṅgirasāṁ satre
> dvitīyam ahar ūcivān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:ś:śaryātiḥ]]---the king named Śaryāti; [[synonyms:m:mānavaḥ]]---the son of Manu; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---ruler; [[synonyms:b:brahmiṣṭhaḥ]]---completely in awareness of Vedic knowledge; [[synonyms:s:sambabhūva]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]---so he became; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---one who; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---either; [[synonyms:a:aṅgirasām]]---of the descendants of Aṅgirā; [[synonyms:s:satre]]---in the arena of sacrifice; [[synonyms:d:dvitīyam]] [[synonyms:a:ahaḥ]]---the functions to be performed on the second day; [[synonyms:ū:ūcivān]]---narrated.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King, Śaryāti, another son of Manu, was a ruler completely aware of Vedic knowledge. He gave instructions about the functions for the second day of the yajña to be performed by the descendants of Aṅgirā.**
