====== SB 9.3.4 ======

===== Text =====

> te daiva-coditā bālā
> jyotiṣī kaṇṭakena vai
> avidhyan mugdha-bhāvena
> susrāvāsṛk tato bahiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:te]]---those two; [[synonyms:d:daiva]]-[[synonyms:c:coditā]]---as if impelled by providence; [[synonyms:b:bālā]]---that young daughter; [[synonyms:j:jyotiṣī]]---two glowworms within the hole of the earthworm; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭakena]]---with a thorn; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:avidhyat]]---pierced; [[synonyms:m:mugdha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvena]]---as if without knowledge; [[synonyms:s:susrāva]]---came out; [[synonyms:a:asṛk]]---blood; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---from there; [[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]---outside.

===== Translation =====

**As if induced by providence, the girl ignorantly pierced those two glowworms with a thorn, and when they were pierced, blood began to ooze out of them.**
