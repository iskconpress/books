====== SB 9.3.5 ======

===== Text =====

> śakṛn-mūtra-nirodho 'bhūt
> sainikānāṁ ca tat-kṣaṇāt
> rājarṣis tam upālakṣya
> puruṣān vismito 'bravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śakṛt]]---of stool; [[synonyms:m:mūtra]]---and of urine; [[synonyms:n:nirodhaḥ]]---stoppage; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---so became; [[synonyms:s:sainikānām]]---of all the soldiers; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇāt]]---immediately; [[synonyms:r:rājarṣiḥ]]---the King; [[synonyms:t:tam]] [[synonyms:u:upālakṣya]]---seeing the incident; [[synonyms:p:puruṣān]]---to his men; [[synonyms:v:vismitaḥ]]---being surprised; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Thereupon, all the soldiers of Śaryāti were immediately obstructed from passing urine and stool. Upon perceiving this, Śaryāti spoke to his associates in surprise.**
