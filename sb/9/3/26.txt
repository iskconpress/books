====== SB 9.3.26 ======

===== Text =====

> anvajānaṁs tataḥ sarve
> grahaṁ somasya cāśvinoḥ
> bhiṣajāv iti yat pūrvaṁ
> somāhutyā bahiṣ-kṛtau

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anvajānan]]---with their permission; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all the demigods; [[synonyms:g:graham]]---a full pot; [[synonyms:s:somasya]]---of //soma-rasa//; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:aśvinoḥ]]---of the Aśvinī-kumāras; [[synonyms:b:bhiṣajau]]---although only physicians; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---because; [[synonyms:p:pūrvam]]---before this; [[synonyms:s:soma]]-[[synonyms:ā:āhutyā]]---with a share in the //soma-yajña//; [[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtau]]---who had been disallowed or excluded.

===== Translation =====

**Although the Aśvinī-kumāras were only physicians and were therefore excluded from drinking soma-rasa in sacrifices, the demigods agreed to allow them henceforward to drink it.**
