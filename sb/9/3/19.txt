====== SB 9.3.19 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā duhitaraṁ prāha
> kṛta-pādābhivandanām
> āśiṣaś cāprayuñjāno
> nātiprīti-manā iva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King (Śaryāti); [[synonyms:d:duhitaram]]---unto the daughter; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---said; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:a:abhivandanām]]---who had already finished offering respectful obeisances to her father; [[synonyms:ā:āśiṣaḥ]]---blessings upon her; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:aprayuñjānaḥ]]---without offering to the daughter; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:atiprīti]]-[[synonyms:m:manāḥ]]---very much pleased; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like that.

===== Translation =====

**After receiving obeisances from his daughter, the King, instead of offering blessings to her, appeared very displeased and spoke as follows.**
