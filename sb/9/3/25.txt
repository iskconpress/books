====== SB 9.3.25 ======

===== Text =====

> hantuṁ tam ādade vajraṁ
> sadyo manyur amarṣitaḥ
> savajraṁ stambhayām āsa
> bhujam indrasya bhārgavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hantum]]---to kill; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him (Cyavana); [[synonyms:ā:ādade]]---Indra took up; [[synonyms:v:vajram]]---his thunderbolt; [[synonyms:s:sadyaḥ]]---immediately; [[synonyms:m:manyuḥ]]---because of great anger, without consideration; [[synonyms:a:amarṣitaḥ]]---being very much perturbed; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vajram]]---with the thunderbolt; [[synonyms:s:stambhayām]] [[synonyms:ā:āsa]]---paralyzed; [[synonyms:b:bhujam]]---the arm; [[synonyms:i:indrasya]]---of Indra; [[synonyms:b:bhārgavaḥ]]---Cyavana Muni, the descendant of Bhṛgu.

===== Translation =====

**King Indra, being perturbed and angry, wanted to kill Cyavana Muni, and therefore he impetuously took up his thunderbolt. But Cyavana Muni, by his powers, paralyzed Indra's arm that held the thunderbolt.**
