====== SB 9.3.13 ======

===== Text =====

> bāḍham ity ūcatur vipram
> abhinandya bhiṣaktamau
> nimajjatāṁ bhavān asmin
> hrade siddha-vinirmite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāḍham]]---yes, we shall act; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:ū:ūcatuḥ]]---they both replied, accepting the proposal of Cyavana; [[synonyms:v:vipram]]---unto the //brāhmaṇa// (Cyavana Muni); [[synonyms:a:abhinandya]]---congratulating him; [[synonyms:b:bhiṣak]]-[[synonyms:t:tamau]]---the two great physicians, the Aśvinī-kumāras; [[synonyms:n:nimajjatām]]---just dive; [[synonyms:b:bhavān]]---yourself; [[synonyms:a:asmin]]---in this; [[synonyms:h:hrade]]---lake; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:v:vinirmite]]---which is especially meant for all kinds of perfection.

===== Translation =====

**The great physicians, the Aśvinī-kumāras, very gladly accepted Cyavana Muni's proposal. Thus they told the brāhmaṇa, "Just dive into this lake of successful life." [One who bathes in this lake has his desires fulfilled.]**
