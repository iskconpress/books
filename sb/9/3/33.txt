====== SB 9.3.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tad gaccha deva-devāṁśo
> baladevo mahā-balaḥ
> kanyā-ratnam idaṁ rājan
> nara-ratnāya dehi bhoḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]---therefore; [[synonyms:g:gaccha]]---you go; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśaḥ]]---whose plenary portion is Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:b:baladevaḥ]]---known as Baladeva; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:balaḥ]]---the supreme powerful; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]-[[synonyms:r:ratnam]]---your beautiful daughter; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:n:nara]]-[[synonyms:r:ratnāya]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is always youthful; [[synonyms:d:dehi]]---just give to Him (in charity); [[synonyms:b:bhoḥ]]---O King.

===== Translation =====

**O King, leave here and offer your daughter to Lord Baladeva, who is still present. He is most powerful. Indeed, He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, whose plenary portion is Lord Viṣṇu. Your daughter is fit to be given to Him in charity.**
