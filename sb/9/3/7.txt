====== SB 9.3.7 ======

===== Text =====

> sukanyā prāha pitaraṁ
> bhītā kiñcit kṛtaṁ mayā
> dve jyotiṣī ajānantyā
> nirbhinne kaṇṭakena vai

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukanyā]]---the girl Sukanyā; [[synonyms:p:prāha]]---said; [[synonyms:p:pitaram]]---unto her father; [[synonyms:b:bhītā]]---being afraid; [[synonyms:k:kiñcit]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---has been done; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me; [[synonyms:d:dve]]---two; [[synonyms:j:jyotiṣī]]---luminous objects; [[synonyms:a:ajānantyā]]---because of ignorance; [[synonyms:n:nirbhinne]]---have been pierced; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭakena]]---with a thorn; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---indeed.

===== Translation =====

**Being very much afraid, the girl Sukanyā said to her father: I have done something wrong, for I have ignorantly pierced these two luminous substances with a thorn.**
