====== SB 9.3.34 ======

===== Text =====

> bhuvo bhārāvatārāya
> bhagavān bhūta-bhāvanaḥ
> avatīrṇo nijāṁśena
> puṇya-śravaṇa-kīrtanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhuvaḥ]]---of the world; [[synonyms:b:bhāra]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārāya]]---to lessen the burden; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvanaḥ]]---always the well-wisher of all the living entities; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇaḥ]]---now He has descended; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---with all the paraphernalia that is part of Him; [[synonyms:p:puṇya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtanaḥ]]---He is simply worshiped by hearing and chanting, by which one becomes purified.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Baladeva is the Supreme Personality of Godhead. One who hears and chants about Him is purified. Because He is always the well-wisher of all living entities, He has descended with all His paraphernalia to purify the entire world and lessen its burden.**
