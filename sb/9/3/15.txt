====== SB 9.3.15 ======

===== Text =====

> puruṣās traya uttasthur
> apīvyā vanitā-priyāḥ
> padma-srajaḥ kuṇḍalinas
> tulya-rūpāḥ suvāsasaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puruṣāḥ]]---men; [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]]---three; [[synonyms:u:uttasthuḥ]]---arose (from the lake); [[synonyms:a:apīvyāḥ]]---extremely beautiful; [[synonyms:v:vanitā]]-[[synonyms:p:priyāḥ]]---as a man becomes very attractive to women; [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:s:srajaḥ]]---decorated with garlands of lotuses; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍalinaḥ]]---with earrings; [[synonyms:t:tulya]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpāḥ]]---all of them had the same bodily features; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsasaḥ]]---very nicely dressed.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, three men with very beautiful bodily features emerged from the lake. They were nicely dressed and decorated with earrings and garlands of lotuses. All of them were of the same standard of beauty.**
