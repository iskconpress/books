====== Cc. Ādi 1.3 ======

===== Text =====

> yad advaitaṁ brahmopaniṣadi tad apy asya tanu-bhā
> ya ātmāntar-yāmī puruṣa iti so 'syāṁśa-vibhavaḥ
> ṣaḍ-aiśvaryaiḥ pūrṇo ya iha bhagavān sa svayam ayaṁ
> na caitanyāt krṣṇāj jagati para-tattvaṁ param iha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yat]]---that which; [[synonyms:a:advaitam]]---nondual; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the impersonal Brahman; [[synonyms:u:upaniṣadi]]---in the //Upaniṣads//; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:a:api]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---His; [[synonyms:t:tanu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhā]]---the effulgence of His transcendental body; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---the Supersoul; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:y:yāmī]]---indwelling Lord; [[synonyms:p:puruṣaḥ]]---supreme enjoyer; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---His; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhavaḥ]]---plenary expansion; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvaryaiḥ]]---with all six opulences; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇaḥ]]---full; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---here; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:c:caitanyāt]]---than Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇāt]]---than Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jagati]]---in the world; [[synonyms:p:para]]---higher; [[synonyms:t:tattvam]]---truth; [[synonyms:p:param]]---another; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---here.

===== Translation =====

**What the Upaniṣads describe as the impersonal Brahman is but the effulgence of His body, and the Lord known as the Supersoul is but His localized plenary portion. He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa Himself, full with six opulences. He is the Absolute Truth, and no other truth is greater than or equal to Him.**
