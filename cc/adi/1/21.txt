====== Cc. Ādi 1.21 ======

===== Text =====

> tinera smaraṇe haya vighna-vināśana
> anāyāse haya nija vāñchita-pūraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tinera]]---of these three; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇe]]---by remembrance; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:v:vighna]]-[[synonyms:v:vināśana]]---the destruction of all difficulties; [[synonyms:a:anāyāse]]---very easily; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---our own; [[synonyms:v:vāñchita]]---of the desired object; [[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---fulfillment.

===== Translation =====

**Such remembrance destroys all difficulties and very easily enables one to fulfill his own desires.**
