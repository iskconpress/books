====== CC Ādi 1.83 ======

===== Text =====

> prathama śloke kahi sāmānya maṅgalācaraṇa
> dvitīya ślokete kari viśeṣa vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathama]]---first; [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in the verse; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I express; [[synonyms:s:sāmānya]]---general; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇa]]---invocation of benediction; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]---second; [[synonyms:ś:ślokete]]---in the verse; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---particular; [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---offering of prayers.

===== Translation =====

**In the first verse I have invoked a general benediction, but in the second I have prayed to the Lord in a particular form.**
