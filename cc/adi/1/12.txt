====== CC Ādi 1.12 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-viṣṇur jagat-kartā
> māyayā yaḥ sṛjaty adaḥ
> tasyāvatāra evāyam
> advaitācārya īśvaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇuḥ]]---Mahā-Viṣṇu, the resting place of the efficient cause; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:k:kartā]]---the creator of the cosmic world; [[synonyms:m:māyayā]]---by the illusory energy; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:s:sṛjati]]---creates; [[synonyms:a:adaḥ]]---that universe; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---His; [[synonyms:a:avatāraḥ]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāryaḥ]]---of the name Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---the Supreme Lord, the resting place of the material cause.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Advaita Ācārya is the incarnation of Mahā-Viṣṇu, whose main function is to create the cosmic world through the actions of Māyā.**
