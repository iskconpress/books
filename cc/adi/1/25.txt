====== CC Ādi 1.25 ======

===== Text =====

> caturtha ślokete kari jagate āśīrvāda
> sarvatra māgiye kṛṣṇa-caitanya-prasāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturtha]]---fourth; [[synonyms:ś:ślokete]]---in the verse; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I make; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---for the world; [[synonyms:ā:āśīḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vāda]]---benediction; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:m:māgiye]]---I am begging; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the mercy.

===== Translation =====

**In the fourth verse I have invoked the benediction of the Lord upon all the world, praying to Lord Caitanya for His mercy upon all.**
