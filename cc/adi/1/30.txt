====== CC Ādi 1.30 ======

===== Text =====

> saba śrotā-vaiṣṇavere kari' namaskāra
> ei saba ślokera kari artha-vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śrotā]]---hearers or audience; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavere]]---unto the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of all these (fourteen) verses; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I make; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---of the meaning; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---analysis.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my obeisances unto all my Vaiṣṇava readers as I begin to explain the intricacies of all these verses.**
