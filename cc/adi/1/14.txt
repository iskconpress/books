====== Cc. Ādi 1.14 ======

===== Text =====

> pañca-tattvātmakaṁ kṛṣṇaṁ
> bhakta-rūpa-svarūpakam
> bhaktāvatāraṁ bhaktākhyaṁ
> namāmi bhakta-śaktikam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmakam]]---comprehending the five transcendental subject matters; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇam]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---in the form of a devotee; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpakam]]---in the expansion of a devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāram]]---in the incarnation of a devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyam]]---known as a devotee; [[synonyms:n:namāmi]]---I offer my obeisances; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaktikam]]---the energy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who supplies energy to the devotee.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my obeisances unto the Supreme Lord, Kṛṣṇa, who is nondifferent from His features as a devotee, devotional incarnation, devotional manifestation, pure devotee, and devotional energy.**
