====== Cc. Ādi 1.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vande gurūn īśa-bhaktān
> īśam īśāvatārakān
> tat-prakāśāṁś ca tac-chaktīḥ
> kṛṣṇa-caitanya-saṁjñakam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:g:gurūn]]---unto the spiritual masters; [[synonyms:ī:īśa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktān]]---unto the devotees of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:ī:īśam]]---unto the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:ī:īśa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārakān]]---unto the incarnations of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:p:prakāśān]]---unto the manifestations; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śaktīḥ]]---unto the potencies; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñakam]]---named.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances unto the spiritual masters, the devotees of the Lord, the Lord's incarnations, His plenary portions, His energies, and the primeval Lord Himself, Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya.**
