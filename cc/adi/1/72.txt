====== Cc. Ādi 1.72 ======

===== Text =====

> rāsotsavaḥ sampravṛtto
> gopī-maṇḍala-maṇḍitaḥ
> yogeśvareṇa kṛṣṇena
> tāsāṁ madhye dvayor dvayoḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:u:utsavaḥ]]---the festival of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:s:sampravṛttaḥ]]---was begun; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---by groups of //gopīs//; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍitaḥ]]---decorated; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvareṇa]]---by the master of all mystic powers; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇena]]---by Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāsām]]---of them; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:d:dvayoḥ]] [[synonyms:d:dvayoḥ]]---of each two.

===== Translation =====

**"When Lord Kṛṣṇa, surrounded by groups of cowherd girls, began the festivities of the rāsa dance, the Lord of all mystic powers placed Himself between each two girls."**

===== Purport =====

This verse is also quoted from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.33.3).
