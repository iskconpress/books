====== Cc. Ādi 1.98 ======

===== Text =====

> dui bhāi hṛdayera kṣāli' andhakāra
> dui bhāgavata-saṅge karāna sākṣātkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---brothers; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayera]]---of the heart; [[synonyms:k:kṣāli']]---purifying; [[synonyms:a:andhakāra]]---darkness; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---of the two //bhāgavatas//; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---by the association; [[synonyms:k:karāna]]---cause; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---a meeting.

===== Translation =====

**But these two brothers [Lord Caitanya and Lord Nityānanda] dissipate the darkness of the inner core of the heart, and thus They help one meet the two kinds of bhāgavatas [persons or things in relationship with the Personality of Godhead].**
