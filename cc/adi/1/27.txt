====== CC Ādi 1.27 ======

===== Text =====

> ei chaya śloke kahi caitanyera tattva
> āra pañca śloke nityānandera mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:c:chaya]]---six; [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in verses; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I describe; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---truth; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---further; [[synonyms:p:pañca]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in five verses; [[synonyms:n:nityānandera]]---of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---the glory.

===== Translation =====

**In these six verses I have described the truth about Lord Caitanya, whereas in the next five I have described the glory of Lord Nityānanda.**
