====== CC Ādi 1.36 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rūpa, sanātana, bhaṭṭa-raghunātha
> śrī-jīva, gopāla-bhaṭṭa, dāsa-raghunātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:j:jīva]]---Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Gopāla Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Śrīla Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**The instructing spiritual masters are Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī, Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Bhaṭṭa Raghunātha, Śrī Jīva Gosvāmī, Śrī Gopāla Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī and Śrīla Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī.**
