====== CC Ādi 1.99 ======

===== Text =====

> eka bhāgavata baḍa---bhāgavata-śāstra
> āra bhāgavata---bhakta bhakti-rasa-pātra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---in relation to the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---great; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---the other; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---in relation to the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---pure devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of the mellow of devotion; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the recipient.

===== Translation =====

**One of the bhāgavatas is the great scripture Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, and the other is the pure devotee absorbed in the mellows of loving devotion.**
