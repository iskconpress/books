====== CC Ādi 1.78 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche baladeva, paravyome nārāyaṇa
> yaiche vāsudeva pradyumnādi saṅkarṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:b:baladeva]]---Baladeva; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyome]]---in the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Pradyumna, etc.; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]---Saṅkarṣaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Examples of such vilāsa-vigrahas are Baladeva, Nārāyaṇa in Vaikuṇṭha-dhāma, and the catur-vyūha-Vāsudeva, Saṅkarṣaṇa, Pradyumna and Aniruddha.**
