====== Cc. Ādi 1.42 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya prabhu svayaṁ-bhagavān
> tāṅhāra padāravinde ananta praṇāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---is the original Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:a:aravinde]]---unto the lotus feet; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---innumerable; [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---respectful obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the Personality of Godhead Himself, and therefore I offer innumerable prostrations at His lotus feet.**
