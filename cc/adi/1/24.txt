====== CC Ādi 1.24 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīya ślokete kari vastura nirdeśa
> yāhā ha-ite jāni para-tattvera uddeśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:ś:ślokete]]---in the third verse; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I make; [[synonyms:v:vastura]]---of the object; [[synonyms:n:nirdeśa]]---indication; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from which; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I understand; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:t:tattvera]]---of the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:u:uddeśa]]---identification.

===== Translation =====

**In the third verse I indicate the Absolute Truth, who is the ultimate substance. With such a description, one can visualize the Supreme Truth.**
