====== CC Ādi 1.28 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dui śloke advaita-tattvākhyāna
> āra eka śloke pañca-tattvera vyākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---further; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in two verses; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---of Śrī Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---of the truth; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāna]]---description; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---further; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in one verse; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:t:tattvera]]---of the Pañca-tattva; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation.

===== Translation =====

**The next two verses describe the truth of Advaita Prabhu, and the following verse describes the Pañca-tattva [the Lord, His plenary portion, His incarnation, His energies and His devotees].**
