====== CC Ādi 1.67 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmā viṣṇu śiva---tina guṇāvatāre gaṇi
> śakty-āveśa---sanakādi, pṛthu, vyāsa-muni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---among the incarnations controlling the three modes of material nature; [[synonyms:g:gaṇi]]---I count; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---empowered incarnations; [[synonyms:s:sanaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the four Kumāras; [[synonyms:p:pṛthu]]---King Pṛthu; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]-[[synonyms:m:muni]]---Vyāsadeva.

===== Translation =====

**Brahmā, Viṣṇu and Śiva are qualitative incarnations. Empowered incarnations are those like the Kumāras, King Pṛthu and Mahā-muni Vyāsa [the compiler of the Vedas].**
