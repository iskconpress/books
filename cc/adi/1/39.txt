====== CC Ādi 1.39 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita ācārya---prabhura aṁśa-avatāra
> tāṅra pāda-padme koṭi praṇati āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---partial; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padme]]---unto the lotus feet; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---ten million; [[synonyms:p:praṇati]]---respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya is the Lord's partial incarnation, and therefore I offer my obeisances millions of times at His lotus feet.**
