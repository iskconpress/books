====== CC Ādi 1.87 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya āra prabhu nityānanda
> yāṅhāra prakāśe sarva jagat ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---on the appearance; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the world; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---full of happiness.

===== Translation =====

**The appearance of Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya and Prabhu Nityānanda has surcharged the world with happiness.**
