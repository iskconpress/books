====== Cc. Ādi 1.90 ======

===== Text =====

> ajñāna-tamera nāma kahiye 'kaitava'
> dharma-artha-kāma-mokṣa-vāñchā ādi saba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ajñāna]]-[[synonyms:t:tamera]]---of the darkness of ignorance; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I call; [[synonyms:k:kaitava]]---cheating process; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religiosity; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---economic development; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---sense gratification; [[synonyms:m:mokṣa]]---liberation; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---desire for; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and so on; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**The darkness of ignorance is called kaitava, the way of cheating, which begins with religiosity, economic development, sense gratification and liberation.**
