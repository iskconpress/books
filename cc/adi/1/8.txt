====== Cc. Ādi 1.8 ======

===== Text =====

> māyātīte vyāpi-vaikuṇṭha-loke
> pūrṇaiśvarye śrī-catur-vyūha-madhye
> rūpaṁ yasyodbhāti saṅkarṣaṇākhyaṁ
> taṁ śrī-nityānanda-rāmaṁ prapadye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:a:atīte]]---beyond the material creation; [[synonyms:v:vyāpi]]---all-expanding; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:l:loke]]---in Vaikuṇṭhaloka, the spiritual world; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarye]]---endowed with full opulence; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the quadruple expansions (Vāsudeva, Saṅkarṣaṇa, Pradyumna and Aniruddha); [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---form; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---whose; [[synonyms:u:udbhāti]]---appears; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyam]]---known as Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---to Him; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmam]]---to Lord Balarāma in the form of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:p:prapadye]]---I surrender.

===== Translation =====

**I surrender unto the lotus feet of Śrī Nityānanda Rāma, who is known as Saṅkarṣaṇa in the midst of the catur-vyūha [consisting of Vāsudeva, Saṅkarṣaṇa, Pradyumna and Aniruddha]. He possesses full opulences and resides in Vaikuṇṭhaloka, far beyond the material creation.**
