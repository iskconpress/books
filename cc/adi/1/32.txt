====== CC Ādi 1.32 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa, guru, bhakta, śakti, avatāra, prakāśa
> kṛṣṇa ei chaya-rūpe karena vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the Supreme Lord, Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---the spiritual masters; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---the potencies; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnations; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---plenary portions; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:c:chaya]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in these six features; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---enjoys.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa enjoys by manifesting Himself as the spiritual masters, the devotees, the diverse energies, the incarnations and the plenary portions. They are all six in one.**
