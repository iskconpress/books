====== CC Ādi 1.18 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaita-candra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glory; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Śrī Caitanya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glory; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---all glory to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glory; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---to the devotees of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**Glory to Śrī Caitanya and Nityānanda! Glory to Advaitacandra! And glory to all the devotees of Śrī Gaura [Lord Caitanya]!**
