====== CC Ādi 1.103 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dui prabhura kari caraṇa vandana
> yāṅhā ha-ite vighna-nāśa abhīṣṭa-pūraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---these; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lords; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---feet; [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---obeisance; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from which; [[synonyms:v:vighna]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destruction of obstacles; [[synonyms:a:abhīṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---fulfillment of desires.

===== Translation =====

**Let us therefore worship the holy feet of these two Lords. Thus one can be rid of all difficulties on the path of self-realization.**
