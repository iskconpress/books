====== CC Ādi 1.101 ======

===== Text =====

> eka adbhuta---sama-kāle doṅhāra prakāśa
> āra adbhuta---citta-guhāra tamaḥ kare nāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful thing; [[synonyms:s:sama]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the same time; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāra]]---of both; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---the manifestation; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---the other; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful thing; [[synonyms:c:citta]]-[[synonyms:g:guhāra]]---of the core of the heart; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---darkness; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destruction.

===== Translation =====

**The first wonder is that both brothers appear simultaneously, and the other is that They illuminate the innermost depths of the heart.**
