====== CC Ādi 6.52 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñi more kare guru jñāna
> tathāpiha mora haya dāsa-abhimāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---consideration as a spiritual master; [[synonyms:t:tathāpiha]]---still; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---the conception of being His servant.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya thinks, "Lord Caitanya considers Me His spiritual master, yet I feel Myself to be only His servant."**
