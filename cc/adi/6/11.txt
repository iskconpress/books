====== CC Ādi 6.11 ======

===== Text =====

> sahāya karena tāṅra la-iyā 'pradhāna'
> koṭi brahmāṇḍa karena icchāya nirmāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahāyakarena]]---He helps; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:l:lā]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---with; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the material energy; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---millions of universes; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---only by the will; [[synonyms:n:nirmāṇa]]---creation.

===== Translation =====

**He [Advaita Ācārya] helps in the pastimes of the puruṣa, with whose material energy and by whose will He creates innumerable universes.**
