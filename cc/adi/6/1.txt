====== CC Ādi 6.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vande taṁ śrīmad-advaitā-
> cāryam adbhuta-ceṣṭitam
> yasya prasādād ajño 'pi
> tat-svarūpaṁ nirūpayet

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:ś:śrīmat]]---with all opulences; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāryam]]---Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]-[[synonyms:c:ceṣṭitam]]---whose activities are wonderful; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom; [[synonyms:p:prasādāt]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:a:ajñaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even a foolish person; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpam]]---His characteristics; [[synonyms:n:nirūpayet]]---may describe.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances to Śrī Advaita Ācārya, whose activities are all wonderful. By His mercy, even a foolish person can describe His characteristics.**
