====== CC Ādi 6.54 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāra pramāṇa śuna---śāstrera vyākhyāna
> mahad-anubhava yāte sudṛḍha pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---evidence; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---the description in the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhava]]---the conception of great souls; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---strong; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---evidence.

===== Translation =====

**For evidence, please listen to the examples described in the revealed scriptures, which are also corroborated by the realization of great souls.**
