====== CC Ādi 6.57 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho rati-mati māge kṛṣṇera caraṇe
> tāhāra śrī-mukha-vāṇī tāhāte pramāṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he also; [[synonyms:r:rati]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]---affection and attraction; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---begs; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---unto the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---words from his mouth; [[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇe]]---evidence.

===== Translation =====

**He too prays for attachment and devotion to the lotus feet of Lord Kṛṣṇa, as the words from his own mouth give evidence.**
