====== CC Ādi 6.62 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīdāmādi vraje yata sakhāra nicaya
> aiśvarya-jñāna-hīna, kevala-sakhya-maya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīdāmā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Kṛṣṇa's friends, headed by Śrīdāmā; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sakhāra]]---of the friends; [[synonyms:n:nicaya]]---the group; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---of opulence; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:h:hīna]]---without; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---purely; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---fraternal affection.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa's friends in Vṛndāvana, headed by Śrīdāmā, have pure fraternal affection for Lord Kṛṣṇa and have no idea of His opulences.**
