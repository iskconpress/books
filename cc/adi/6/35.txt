====== CC Ādi 6.35 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅra dvārā kaila prabhu kīrtana pracāra
> yāṅra dvārā kaija prabhu jagat nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---by whom; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---spreading of the //saṅkīrtana// movement; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---by whom; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagat]] [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance of the entire world.

===== Translation =====

**It is through Him [Advaita Ācārya] that Lord Caitanya spread the saṅkīrtana movement and through Him that He delivered the world.**
