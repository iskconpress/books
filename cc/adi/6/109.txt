====== Cc. Ādi 6.109 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-bhāva aṅgīkari' hailā avatīrṇa
> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-rūpe sarva-bhāve pūrṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the ecstasy of being a devotee; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]]---incarnated; [[synonyms:ś:śri]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]] [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---complete in every respect.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore Lord Kṛṣṇa accepted the position of a devotee and descended in the form of Lord Caitanya, who is complete in every respect.**
