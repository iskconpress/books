====== Cc. Ādi 6.47 ======

===== Text =====

> dāsya-bhāve ānandita pāriṣada-gaṇa
> vidhi, bhava, nārada āra śuka, sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāsya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in the conception of being a servant; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the associates; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:b:bhava]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:n:nārada]]---the great sage Nārada; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śuka]]---Śukadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---and Sanātana.

===== Translation =====

**All the associates of Lord Kṛṣṇa, such as Brahmā, Śiva, Nārada, Śuka and Sanātana, are very much pleased in the sentiment of servitude.**
