====== CC Ādi 6.113 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-ācārya gosāñira mahimā apāra
> yāṅhāra huṅkāre kaila caitanyāvatāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited glories; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāre]]---by the vibration; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---brought; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnation of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**The glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya are boundless, for His sincere vibrations brought about Lord Caitanya's descent upon this earth.**
