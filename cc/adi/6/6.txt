====== CC Ādi 6.6 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-ācārya gosāñi sākṣāt īśvara
> yāṅhāra mahimā nahe jīvera gocara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of the name Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---whose glories; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jīveragocara]]---within the reach of the understanding of ordinary living beings.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya is indeed directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself. His glory is beyond the conception of ordinary living beings.**
