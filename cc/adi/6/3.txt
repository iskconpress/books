====== Cc. Ādi 6.3 ======

===== Text =====

> pañca śloke kahila śrī-nityānanda-tattva
> śloka-dvaye kahi advaitācāryera mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañca]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in five verses; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---described; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth of Śrī Nityānanda; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:d:dvaye]]---in two verses; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I describe; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---the glories.

===== Translation =====

**In five verses I have described the principle of Lord Nityānanda. Then in the following two verses I describe the glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya.**
