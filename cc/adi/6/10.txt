====== CC Ādi 6.10 ======

===== Text =====

> se puruṣera aṁśa---advaita, nāhi kichu bheda
> śarīra-viśeṣa tāṅra---nāhika viccheda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:p:puruṣera]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---part; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---difference; [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]-[[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---another specific transcendental body; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:v:viccheda]]---there is no separation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya is a plenary part of that puruṣa and so is not different from Him. Indeed, Śrī Advaita Ācārya is not separate but is another form of that puruṣa.**
