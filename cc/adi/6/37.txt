====== CC Ādi 6.37 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya gosāñi caitanyera mukhya aṅga
> āra eka aṅga tāṅra prabhu nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---primary; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---part; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---part; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya is a principal limb of Lord Caitanya. Another limb of the Lord is Nityānanda Prabhu.**
