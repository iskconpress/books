====== Cc. Ādi 6.26 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-viṣṇura aṁśa---advaita guṇa-dhāma
> īśvare abheda, teñi 'advaita' pūrṇa nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇura]]---of Lord Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---part; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---reservoir of all attributes; [[synonyms:ī:īśvare]]---from the Lord; [[synonyms:a:abheda]]---nondifferent; [[synonyms:t:teñi]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---nondifferent; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---full name.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita, who is a reservoir of virtues, is the main limb of Mahā-Viṣṇu. His full name is Advaita, for He is identical in all respects with that Lord.**
