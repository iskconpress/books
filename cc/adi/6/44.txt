====== CC Ādi 6.44 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-dāsa-abhimāne ye ānanda-sindhu
> koṭī-brahma-sukha nahe tāra eka bindu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāne]]---under this impression of being a servant of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---ocean of transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:k:koṭī]]-[[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---ten million times the transcendental bliss of becoming one with the Absolute; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of the ocean of transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:b:bindu]]---drop.

===== Translation =====

**The conception of servitude to Śrī Kṛṣṇa generates such an ocean of joy in the soul that even the joy of oneness with the Absolute, if multiplied ten million times, could not compare to a drop of it.**
