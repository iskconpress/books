====== Cc. Ādi 6.17 ======

===== Text =====

> 'nimittāṁśe' kare teṅho māyāte īkṣaṇa
> 'upādāna' advaita karena brahmāṇḍa-sṛjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nimitta]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśe]]---in the portion as the original cause; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:m:māyāte]]---in the external energy; [[synonyms:ī:īkṣaṇa]]---glancing; [[synonyms:u:upādāna]]---the material cause; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:s:sṛjana]]---creation of the material world.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Viṣṇu, in His efficient aspect, glances over the material energy, and Śrī Advaita, as the material cause, creates the material world.**
