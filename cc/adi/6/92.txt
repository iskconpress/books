====== CC Ādi 6.92 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra prakāśa-bheda, advaita-ācārya
> kāya-mano-vākye tāṅra bhakti sadā kārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]-[[synonyms:b:bheda]]---separate expansion; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]]---by His body, mind and words; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---occupational duty.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya is a separate expansion of Him. He always engages in devotional service with His thoughts, words and actions.**
