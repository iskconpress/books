====== CC Ādi 6.63 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-saṅge yuddha kare, skandhe ārohaṇa
> tārā dāsya-bhāve kare caraṇa-sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yuddha]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---fight; [[synonyms:s:skandhe]]---on His shoulders; [[synonyms:ā:ārohaṇa]]---getting up; [[synonyms:t:tārā]]---they; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in the conception of being Lord Kṛṣṇa's servants; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevana]]---worship the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Although they fight with Him and climb upon His shoulders, they worship His lotus feet in a spirit of servitude.**
