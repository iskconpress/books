====== CC Ādi 6.18 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi sāṅkhya māne, 'pradhāna'---kāraṇa
> jaḍa ha-ite kabhu nahe jagat-sṛjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:s:sāṅkhya]]---Sāṅkhya philosophy; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---accepts; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---ingredients; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---cause; [[synonyms:j:jaḍa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from matter; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there is not; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:s:sṛjana]]---the creation of the material world.

===== Translation =====

**Although the Sāṅkhya philosophy accepts that the material ingredients are the cause, the creation of the world never arises from dead matter.**
