====== CC Ādi 6.13 ======

===== Text =====

> koṭi aṁśa, koṭi śakti, koṭi avatāra
> eta lañā sṛje puruṣa sakala saṁsāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:koṭi]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---millions of parts and parcels; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]] [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---millions and millions of energies; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---millions upon millions of incarnations; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---all this; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:s:sṛje]]---creates; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the original person, Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---all the material world.

===== Translation =====

**Mahā-Viṣṇu creates the entire material world, with millions of His parts, energies and incarnations.**
