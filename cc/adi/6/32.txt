====== Cc. Ādi 6.32 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara-sārūpya pāya pāriṣada-gaṇa
> catur-bhuja, pīta-vāsa, yaiche nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:s:sārūpya]]---the same bodily features as the Lord; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the associates; [[synonyms:c:catur]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuja]]---four hands; [[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---yellow dress; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**His associates have the same bodily features as the Lord. They all have four arms and are dressed in yellow garments like Nārāyaṇa.**
