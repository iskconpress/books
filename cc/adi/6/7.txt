====== Cc. Ādi 6.7 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-viṣṇu sṛṣṭi karena jagad-ādi kārya
> tāṅra avatāra sākṣāt advaita ācārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---the original Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---creation; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the material world; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---the occupation; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:a:advaitaācārya]]---Prabhu Advaita Ācārya.

===== Translation =====

**Mahā-Viṣṇu performs all the functions for the creation of the universes. Śrī Advaita Ācārya is His direct incarnation.**
