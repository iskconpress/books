====== Cc. Ādi 6.48 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda avadhūta sabāte āgala
> caitanyera dāsya-preme ha-ilā pāgala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:a:avadhūta]]---the mendicant Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:s:sabāte]]---among all; [[synonyms:ā:āgala]]---foremost; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in the emotional ecstatic love of being a servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:p:pāgala]]---became mad.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda, the wandering mendicant, is the foremost of all the associates of Lord Caitanya. He became mad in the ecstasy of service to Lord Caitanya.**
