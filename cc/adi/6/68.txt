====== CC Ādi 6.68 ======

===== Text =====

> api bata madhu-puryām ārya-putro 'dhunāste
> smarati sa pitṛ-gehān saumya bandhūṁś ca gopān
> kvacid api sa kathāṁ naḥ kiṅkariṇāṁ gṛṇīte
> bhujam aguru-sugandhaṁ mūrdhny adhāsyat kadā nu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:api]]---certainly; [[synonyms:b:bata]]---regrettable; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:p:puryām]]---in the city of Mathurā; [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]-[[synonyms:p:putraḥ]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:a:adhunā]]---now; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]---resides; [[synonyms:s:smarati]]---remembers; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]-[[synonyms:g:gehān]]---the household affairs of His father; [[synonyms:s:saumya]]---O great soul (Uddhava); [[synonyms:b:bandhūn]]---His many friends; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:g:gopān]]---the cowherd boys; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:a:api]]---or; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:k:kathām]]---talks; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---of us; [[synonyms:k:kiṅkarīṇām]]---of the maidservants; [[synonyms:g:gṛṇīte]]---relates; [[synonyms:b:bhujam]]---hand; [[synonyms:a:aguru]]-[[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:g:gandham]]---having the fragrance of //aguru//; [[synonyms:m:mūrdhni]]---on the head; [[synonyms:a:adhāsyat]]---will keep; [[synonyms:k:kadā]]---when; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---may be.

===== Translation =====

**"O Uddhava! It is indeed regrettable that Kṛṣṇa resides in Mathurā. Does He remember His father's household affairs and His friends, the cowherd boys? O great soul! Does He ever talk about us, His maidservants? When will He lay on our heads His aguru-scented hand?"**

===== Purport =====

This verse appears in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.47.21) in the section known as the //Bhramara-gītā.// When Uddhava came to Vṛndāvana, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, in complete separation from Kṛṣṇa, sang like this.
