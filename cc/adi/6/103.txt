====== CC Ādi 6.103 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-sāmye nahe tāṅra mādhuryāsvādana
> bhakta-bhāve kare tāṅra mādhurya carvaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sāmye]]---on an equal level with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---relishing the sweetness; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---as a devotee; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]] [[synonyms:c:carvaṇa]]---chewing of the sweetness.

===== Translation =====

**The sweetness of Lord Kṛṣṇa is not to be tasted by those who consider themselves equal to Kṛṣṇa. It is to be tasted only through the sentiment of servitude.**
