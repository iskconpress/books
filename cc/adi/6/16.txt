====== Cc. Ādi 6.16 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane puruṣa---viśvera 'nimitta'-kāraṇa
> advaita-rūpe 'upādāna' hana nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:v:viśvera]]---of the entire material world; [[synonyms:n:nimitta]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the original cause; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of Advaita; [[synonyms:u:upādāna]]---the material cause; [[synonyms:h:hana]]---becomes; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Viṣṇu Himself is the efficient [nimitta] cause of the material world, and Nārāyaṇa in the form of Śrī Advaita is the material cause [upādāna].**
