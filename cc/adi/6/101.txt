====== CC Ādi 6.101 ======

===== Text =====

> ātmā haite kṛṣṇa bhakte baḍa kari' māne
> ihāte bahuta śāstra-vacana pramāṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than His own self; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---His devotee; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---accepts as greater; [[synonyms:i:ihāte]]---in this connection; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---many; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---quotations from revealed scripture; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇe]]---evidences.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa considers His devotees greater than Himself. In this connection the scriptures provide an abundance of evidence.**
