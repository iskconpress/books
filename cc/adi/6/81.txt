====== CC Ādi 6.81 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-preme unmatta, vihvala digambara
> kṛṣṇa-guṇa-līlā gāya, nāce nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:unmatta]]---almost mad; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:d:digambara]]---without any dress; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chants; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly.

===== Translation =====

**Intoxicated by ecstatic love for Lord Kṛṣṇa, he becomes overwhelmed and incessantly dances without clothing and sings about Lord Kṛṣṇa's qualities and pastimes.**
