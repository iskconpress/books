====== CC Ādi 6.19 ======

===== Text =====

> nija sṛṣṭi-śakti prabhu sañcāre pradhāne
> īśvarera śaktye tabe haye ta' nirmāṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]---own; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power for creation; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sañcāre]]---infuses; [[synonyms:p:pradhāne]]---in the ingredients; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]] [[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---by the power of the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:h:haye]]---there is; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:nirmāṇe]]---the beginning of creation.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord infuses the material ingredients with His own creative potency. Then, by the power of the Lord, creation takes place.**
