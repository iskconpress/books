====== CC Ādi 6.60 ======

===== Text =====

> manaso vṛttayo naḥ syuḥ
> kṛṣṇa-pādāmbujāśrayāḥ
> vāco 'bhidhāyinīr nāmnāṁ
> kāyas tat-prahvaṇādiṣu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manasaḥ]]---of the mind; [[synonyms:v:vṛttayaḥ]]---activities (thinking, feeling and willing); [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---of us; [[synonyms:s:syuḥ]]---let there be; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:a:ambuja]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:ā:āśrayāḥ]]---those sheltered by; [[synonyms:v:vācaḥ]]---the words; [[synonyms:a:abhidhāyinīḥ]]---speaking; [[synonyms:n:nāmnām]]---of His holy names; [[synonyms:k:kāyaḥ]]---the body; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---to Him; [[synonyms:p:prahvaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---bowing down to Him, etc.

===== Translation =====

**"May our minds be attached to the lotus feet of your Lord Kṛṣṇa, may our tongues chant His holy names, and may our bodies lie prostrate before Him.**
