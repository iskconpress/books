====== CC Ādi 6.77 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho āpanāke karena dāsa-bhāvanā
> kṛṣṇa-dāsa-bhāva vinu āche kona janā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He also; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāke]]---Himself; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvanā]]---considering a servant; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the conception of being a servant of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---without; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---what; [[synonyms:j:janā]]---person.

===== Translation =====

**He also considers Himself a servant of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Indeed, who is there who does not have this conception of being a servant of Lord Kṛṣṇa?**
