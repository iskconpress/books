====== CC Ādi 6.9 ======

===== Text =====

> icchāya ananta mūrti karena prakāśa
> eka eka mūrte karena brahmāṇḍe praveśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---by His will; [[synonyms:a:anantamūrti]]---unlimited forms; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation; [[synonyms:e:ekaeka]]---each and every; [[synonyms:m:mūrte]]---form; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍe]]---within the universe; [[synonyms:p:praveśa]]---entrance.

===== Translation =====

**By His will He manifests Himself in unlimited forms, in which He enters each and every universe.**
