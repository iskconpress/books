====== CC Ādi 6.84 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kṛṣṇa avatīrṇa---caitanya-īśvara
> ataeva āra saba,---tāṅhāra kiṅkara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]]---descended; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---Lord Caitanya, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---others; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---His servants.

===== Translation =====

**That same Lord Kṛṣṇa has descended as Lord Caitanya, the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Everyone, therefore, is His servant.**
