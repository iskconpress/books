====== CC Ādi 6.119 ======

===== Text =====

> dui śloke kahila advaita-tattva-nirūpaṇa
> pañca-tattvera vicāra kichu śuna, bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in two verses; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---described; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:n:nirūpaṇa]]---ascertaining the truth; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:t:tattvera]]---of the five truths; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Thus in two verses I have described the truth concerning Advaita Ācārya. Now, O devotees, please hear about the five truths [pañca-tattva].**
