====== CC Ādi 6.24 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvarera 'aṅga' aṁśa---cid-ānanda-maya
> māyāra sambandha nāhi' ei śloke kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iśvarera]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---limb; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---part; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---all-spiritual; [[synonyms:m:māyāra]]---of the material energy; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]---relationship; [[synonyms:n:nāhi']]---there is not; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---this verse; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---says.

===== Translation =====

**This verse describes that the limbs and plenary portions of the Lord are all spiritual; they have no relationship with the material energy.**
