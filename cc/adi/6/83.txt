====== Cc. Ādi 6.83 ======

===== Text =====

> eka kṛṣṇa---sarva-sevya, jagat-īśvara
> āra yata saba,---tāṅra sevakānucara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---one Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:s:sevya]]---worthy of being served by all; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Lord of the universe; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all others; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]-[[synonyms:a:anucara]]---servants of the servants.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa, the one master and the Lord of the universe, is worthy of being served by everyone. Indeed, everyone is but a servant of His servants.**
