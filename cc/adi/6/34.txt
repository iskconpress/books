====== Cc. Ādi 6.34 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅhāra tulasī-jale, yāṅhāra huṅkāre
> sva-gaṇa sahite caitanyera avatāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---by //tulasī// leaves and Ganges water; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāre]]---by the loud voice; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---His personal associates; [[synonyms:s:sahite]]---accompanied by; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---in the incarnation.

===== Translation =====

**He worshiped Kṛṣṇa with tulasī leaves and water of the Ganges and called for Him in a loud voice. Thus Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu appeared on earth, accompanied by His personal associates.**
