====== CC Ādi 6.8 ======

===== Text =====

> ye puruṣa sṛṣṭi-sthiti karena māyāya
> ananta brahmāṇḍa sṛṣṭi karena līlāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---that personality who; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---creation and maintenance; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:m:māyāya]]---through the external energy; [[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---unlimited universes; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---creation; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---by pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**That puruṣa creates and maintains with His external energy. He creates innumerable universes in His pastimes.**
