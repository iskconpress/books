====== CC Ādi 6.45 ======

===== Text =====

> muñi ye caitanya-dāsa āra nityānanda
> dāsa-bhāva-sama nahe anyatra ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servant of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the emotion of being a servant; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal to; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:a:anyatra]]---anywhere else; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**He says, "Nityānanda and I are servants of Lord Caitanya." Nowhere else is there such joy as that which is tasted in this emotion of servitude.**
