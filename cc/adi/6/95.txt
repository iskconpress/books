====== CC Ādi 6.95 ======

===== Text =====

> pṛthivī dharena yei śeṣa-saṅkarṣaṇa
> kāya-vyūha kari' karena kṛṣṇera sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pṛthivī]]---planets; [[synonyms:d:dharena]]---holds; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---that one who; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]---Lord Śeṣa Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---expanding Himself in different bodies; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service to Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Śeṣa Saṅkarṣaṇa, who holds all the planets on His head, expands Himself in different bodies to render service to Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
