====== Cc. Ādi 6.20 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-rūpe kare śakti-sañcāraṇa
> ataeva advaita hayena mukhya kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:s:sañcāraṇa]]---infusion of the energy; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---is; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the original cause.

===== Translation =====

**In the form of Advaita He infuses the material ingredients with creative energy. Therefore, Advaita is the original cause of creation.**
