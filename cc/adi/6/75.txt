====== CC Ādi 6.75 ======

===== Text =====

> ātmārāmasya tasyemā
> vayaṁ vai gṛha-dāsikāḥ
> sarva-saṅga-nivṛttyāddhā
> tapasā ca babhūvima

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmasya]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is satisfied in Himself; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---His; [[synonyms:i:imāḥ]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vayam]]---we; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---certainly; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsikāḥ]]---the maidservants of the home; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---association; [[synonyms:n:nivṛttyā]]---fully bereft of; [[synonyms:a:addhā]]---directly; [[synonyms:t:tapasā]]---on account of austerity; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:b:babhūvima]]---we have become.

===== Translation =====

**"Through austerity and through renunciation of all attachments, we have become maidservants in the home of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is satisfied in Himself."**

===== Purport =====

During the same incident, this verse, quoted from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.83.39), was spoken to Draupadī by a queen of Kṛṣṇa's named Lakṣmaṇā.
