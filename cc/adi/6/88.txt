====== Cc. Ādi 6.88 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-abhimāna mūla śrī-balarāme
> sei bhāve anugata tāṅra aṁśa-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---to think oneself a devotee; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---original; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:balarāme]]---in Lord Balarāma; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in that ecstasy; [[synonyms:a:anugata]]---followers; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all His parts and parcels.

===== Translation =====

**The source of the sentiment of servitude is indeed Lord Balarāma. The plenary expansions who follow Him are all influenced by that ecstasy.**
