====== Cc. Ādi 6.116 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya-caraṇe mora koṭi namaskāra
> ithe kichu aparādha nā labe āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---offering obeisances ten million times; [[synonyms:i:ithe]]---in this connection; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:labe]]---please do not take; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my obeisances ten million times to the lotus feet of Śrī Advaita Ācārya. Please do not take offense at this.**
