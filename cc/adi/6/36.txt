====== CC Ādi 6.36 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya gosāñira guṇa-mahimā apāra
> jīva-kīṭa kothāya pāibeka tāra pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glory of the attributes; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unfathomable; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:k:kīṭa]]---a living being who is just like a worm; [[synonyms:k:kothāya]]---where; [[synonyms:p:pāibeka]]---will get; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---the other side.

===== Translation =====

**The glory and attributes of Advaita Ācārya are unlimited. How can the insignificant living entities fathom them?**
