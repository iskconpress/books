====== CC Ādi 6.82 ======

===== Text =====

> pitā-mātā-guru-sakhā-bhāva kene naya
> kṛṣṇa-premera svabhāve dāsya-bhāva se karaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pitā]]---father; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---mother; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---superior teacher; [[synonyms:s:sakhā]]---friend; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the emotion; [[synonyms:k:kene]] [[synonyms:n:naya]]---let it be; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:premera]]---of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svabhāve]]---in a natural inclination; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]] [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the emotion of becoming a servant; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:k:karaya]]---does.

===== Translation =====

**All the emotions, whether those of father, mother, teacher or friend, are full of sentiments of servitude. That is the nature of love of Kṛṣṇa.**
