====== CC Ādi 6.98 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-mātra 'aṁśī'---kṛṣṇa, 'aṁśa'---avatāra
> aṁśī aṁśe dekhi jyeṣṭha-kaniṣṭha-ācāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only one; [[synonyms:a:aṁśī]]---source of all incarnations; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---of the part; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations; [[synonyms:a:aṁśī]]---is the source of all incarnations; [[synonyms:a:aṁśe]]---in the incarnation; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---we can see; [[synonyms:j:jyeṣṭha]]---as superior; [[synonyms:k:kaniṣṭha]]---and inferior; [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---behavior.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa is the source of all incarnations, and all others are His parts or partial incarnations. We find that the whole and the part behave as superior and inferior.**
