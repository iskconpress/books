====== CC Ādi 6.118 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya jaya śrī-advaita ācārya
> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya, nityānanda ārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:a:advaita]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---to Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---the superior.

===== Translation =====

**All glories, all glories to Śrī Advaita Ācārya! All glories to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and the superior Lord Nityānanda!**
