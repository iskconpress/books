====== CC Ādi 6.61 ======

===== Text =====

> karmabhir bhrāmyamāṇānāṁ
> yatra kvāpīśvarecchayā
> maṅgalācaritair dānai
> ratir naḥ kṛṣṇa īśvare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karmabhiḥ]]---by the activities; [[synonyms:b:bhrāmyamāṇānām]]---of those wandering within the material universe; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---wherever; [[synonyms:k:kva]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---anywhere; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:i:icchayā]]---by the supreme will of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācaritaiḥ]]---by auspicious activities; [[synonyms:d:dānaiḥ]]---like charity and philanthropy; [[synonyms:r:ratiḥ]]---the attraction; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---in Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ī:īśvare]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"Wherever we wander in the material universe under the influence of karma by the will of the Lord, may our auspicious activities cause our attraction to Lord Kṛṣṇa to increase."**

===== Purport =====

These verses from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.47.66-67) were spoken by the denizens of Vṛndāvana, headed by Mahārāja Nanda and his associates, to Uddhava, who had come from Mathurā.
