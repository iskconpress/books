====== CC Ādi 6.91 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkarṣaṇa-avatāra kāraṇābdhi-śāyī
> tāṅhāra hṛdaye bhakta-bhāva anuyāyī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---an incarnation of Lord Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:abdhi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---Lord Viṣṇu lying on the Causal Ocean; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the emotion of being a devotee; [[synonyms:a:anuyāyī]]---accordingly.

===== Translation =====

**The Viṣṇu who lies on the Causal Ocean is an incarnation of Lord Saṅkarṣaṇa, and, accordingly, the emotion of being a devotee is always present in His heart.**
