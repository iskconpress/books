====== CC Ādi 14.59 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā śuni' tā-sabāra mane ha-ila bhaya
> kona kichu jāne, kibā devāviṣṭa haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all the girls; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the minds; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something uncommon; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---He knows; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---what if; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---empowered by demigods; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---He is.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this supposed curse by Lord Caitanya, the girls, considering that He might know something uncommon or be empowered by demigods, were afraid that His curse might be effective.**
