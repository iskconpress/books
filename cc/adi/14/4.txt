====== Cc. Ādi 14.4 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahila janmalīlā-anukrama
> ebe kahi bālyalīlā-sūtrera gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---I have spoken; [[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of birth; [[synonyms:a:anukrama]]---chronological order; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:b:bālya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of the pastimes of childhood; [[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the //sūtras//; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---enumeration.

===== Translation =====

**I have already briefly spoken about the pastimes of His birth in chronological order. Now I shall give a synopsis of His childhood pastimes.**
