====== CC Ādi 14.16 ======

===== Text =====

> nārāyaṇera cihna-yukta śrī-hasta caraṇa
> ei śiśu sarva loke karibe tāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇera]]---of Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]-[[synonyms:y:yukta]]---with positive marks; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:hasta]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the palm and the sole; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śiśu]]---baby; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:l:loke]]---all the three worlds; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---will; [[synonyms:t:tāraṇa]]---deliver.

===== Translation =====

**"This baby has all the symptoms of Lord Nārāyaṇa on His palms and soles. He will be able to deliver all the three worlds.**
