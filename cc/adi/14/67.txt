====== CC Ādi 14.67 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣmī tāṅra aṅge dila puṣpa-candana
> mallikāra mālā diyā karila vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---Lakṣmīdevī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]---flowers; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:m:mallikāra]]---of the flower of the name //mallikā//; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garland; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---offered; [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---prayers.

===== Translation =====

**On hearing the order of the Supreme Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Lakṣmī immediately worshiped Him, offering sandalwood pulp and flowers for His body, garlanding Him with mallikā flowers, and offering prayers.**
