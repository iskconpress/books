====== Cc. Ādi 14.47 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhire yāñā ānilena dui nārikela
> dekhiyā apūrva haila vismita sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:ā:ānilena]]---He immediately brought; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:n:nārikela]]---coconuts; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]]---this wonder; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---astonished; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**He then immediately went outside the house and brought two coconuts. All the ladies were astonished to see such wonderful activities.**
