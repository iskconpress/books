====== CC Ādi 14.71 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-cāpalya dekhi' preme sarva jana
> śacī-jagannāthe dekhi' dena olāhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:c:cāpalya]]---naughtiness; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---out of love; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---all people; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---before Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]]---and Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing them; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gave; [[synonyms:o:olāhana]]---a little rebuke.

===== Translation =====

**When the neighboring people saw the naughty behavior of Lord Caitanya, out of love for Him they lodged complaints with Śacīmātā and Jagannātha Miśra.**
