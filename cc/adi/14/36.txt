====== CC Ādi 14.36 ======

===== Text =====

> eimate nānā-chale aiśvarya dekhāya
> bālya-bhāva prakaṭiyā paścāt lukāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eimate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:c:chale]]---under different excuses; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---opulence; [[synonyms:d:dekhāya]]---exhibits; [[synonyms:b:bālya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the status of a child; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭiyā]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:p:paścāt]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:l:lukāya]]---hides Himself.

===== Translation =====

**Thus under various excuses the Lord exhibited His opulences as much as possible in His childhood, and later, after exhibiting such opulences, He hid Himself.**
