====== CC Ādi 14.45 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu mṛdu-haste kaila mātāke tāḍana
> mātāke mūrcchitā dekhi' karaye krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:m:mṛdu]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---by His soft hand; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:m:mātāke]]---His mother; [[synonyms:t:tāḍana]]---chastise; [[synonyms:m:mātāke]]---His mother; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchitā]]---fainted; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---was; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**Once the child, Caitanya Mahāprabhu, chastised His mother with His soft hand, and His mother pretended to faint. Seeing this, the Lord began to cry.**
