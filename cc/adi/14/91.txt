====== Cc. Ādi 14.91 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' dvija gelā hañā ānandita
> miśra jāgiyā ha-ilā parama vismita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing so much; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very much pleased; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:j:jāgiyā]]---being awakened; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---highly; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---astonished.

===== Translation =====

**Being very much pleased, the brāhmaṇa left after talking with Jagannātha Miśra, and when Jagannātha Miśra awakened from his dream, he was very much astonished.**
