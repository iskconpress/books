====== CC Ādi 14.70 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata līlā kari' duṅhe gelā ghare
> gambhīra caitanya-līlā ke bujhite pāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---executing; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---home; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---very grave; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is able.

===== Translation =====

**After thus expressing their feelings to each other, Lord Caitanya and Lakṣmī returned home. Who can understand the grave pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu?**
