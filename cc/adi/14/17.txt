====== CC Ādi 14.17 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' karibe vaiṣṇava-dharmera pracāra
> ihā haite habe dui kulera nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---this child; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---will do; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---of Vaiṣṇavism, or devotional service; [[synonyms:d:dharmera]]---of the religion; [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---preaching; [[synonyms:i:ihā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from this; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:k:kulera]]---dynasties; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance.

===== Translation =====

**"This child will preach the Vaiṣṇava cult and deliver both His maternal and paternal families.**

===== Purport =====

Only Nārāyaṇa Himself or His bona fide representative can preach the cult of Vaiṣṇavism, or devotional service. When a Vaiṣṇava is born, he delivers both his maternal and paternal families simultaneously.
