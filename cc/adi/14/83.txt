====== CC Ādi 14.83 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina miśra putrera cāpalya dekhiyā
> dharma-śikṣā dila bahu bhartsanā kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:p:putrera]]---of his son; [[synonyms:c:cāpalya]]---the mischievous behavior; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]]---religious teaching; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:b:bhartsanā]]---rebuking; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing.

===== Translation =====

**On another occasion, Jagannātha Miśra, seeing the mischievous acts of his son, gave Him lessons in morality after rebuking Him greatly.**
