====== CC Ādi 14.95 ======

===== Text =====

> bālyalīlā-sūtra ei kaila anukrama
> ihā vistāriyāchena dāsa-vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bālya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---synopsis of the pastimes of childhood; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:a:anukrama]]---in chronological order; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vistāriyāchena]]---has elaborately explained; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**This is a synopsis of the childhood pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, placed herewith in chronological order. Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has already elaborately explained these pastimes in his book Caitanya-bhāgavata.**
