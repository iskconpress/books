====== CC Ādi 14.11 ======

===== Text =====

> stana piyāite putrera caraṇa dekhila
> sei cihna pāye dekhi' miśre bolāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:stana]]---her breast; [[synonyms:p:piyāite]]---while letting Him suck; [[synonyms:p:putrera]]---of her son; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---observed; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those very; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]---marks; [[synonyms:p:pāye]]---on the sole; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:miśre]]---Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---called for.

===== Translation =====

**While mother Śacī was feeding the child from her breast, she saw on His lotus feet all the marks that were visible on the floor of the room, and she called for Jagannātha Miśra.**
