====== CC Ādi 14.76 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu putra-saṅge śacī karilā śayana
> dekhe, divyaloka āsi' bharila bhavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---taking the boy; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:k:karilāśayana]]---took rest; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---celestial denizens; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:b:bharila]]---filled up; [[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---the whole house.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes, taking her son with her, mother Śacī would lie down on her bed, and she would see that denizens of the celestial world had come there, filling the entire house.**
