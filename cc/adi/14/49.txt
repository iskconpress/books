====== Cc. Ādi 14.49 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-snāna kari' pūjā karite lāgilā
> kanyāgaṇa-madhye prabhu āsiyā basilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bath in the Ganges; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---having taken; [[synonyms:p:pūjā]]---worship; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the girls; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the midst of; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming there; [[synonyms:b:basilā]]---sat down.

===== Translation =====

**When the girls engaged in worshiping the different demigods after bathing in the Ganges, the young Lord would come there and sit down among them.**
