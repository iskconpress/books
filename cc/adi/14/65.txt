====== CC Ādi 14.65 ======

===== Text =====

> duṅhā dekhi' duṅhāra citte ha-ila ullāsa
> deva-pūjā chale kaila duṅhe parakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṅhā]]---both of them; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in the minds; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:p:pūjā]]---worshiping the demigods; [[synonyms:c:chale]]---on the plea of; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---there was; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:p:parakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**They both enjoyed natural pleasure in seeing each nother, and under the pretext of demigod worship they manifested their feelings.**
