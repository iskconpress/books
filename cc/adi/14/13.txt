====== CC Ādi 14.13 ======

===== Text =====

> cihna dekhi' cakravartī balena hāsiyā
> lagna gaṇi' pūrve āmi rākhiyāchi likhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cihna]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing the marks; [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---says; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:l:lagna]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇi']]---by astrological calculation of the birth moment; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:r:rākhiyāchi]]---have kept; [[synonyms:l:likhiyā]]---after writing all these things.

===== Translation =====

**When Nīlāmbara Cakravartī saw those marks, he smilingly said, "Formerly I ascertained all this by astrological calculation and noted it in writing.**
