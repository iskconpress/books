====== Cc. Ādi 14.78 ======

===== Text =====

> calite caraṇe nūpura bāje jhanjhan
> śuni' camakita haila pitā-mātāra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:calite]]---while going; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---on the lotus feet; [[synonyms:n:nūpura]]---ankle bells; [[synonyms:b:bāje]]---sounded; [[synonyms:j:jhanjhan]]---tinkling; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:c:camakita]]---struck with wonder; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---of His father; [[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---and mother; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds.

===== Translation =====

**When the child was going out, there was a tinkling of ankle bells from His lotus feet. Hearing this, His father and mother were struck with wonder.**
