====== CC Ādi 14.61 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata cāpalya saba lokere dekhāya
> duḥkha kāro mane nahe, sabe sukha pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eimata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:c:cāpalya]]---cunning behavior; [[synonyms:s:sabalokere]]---unto the people in general; [[synonyms:d:dekhāya]]---exhibits; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:k:kāro]]---distress; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there is no such thing; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---enjoys.

===== Translation =====

**When this cunning behavior of the Lord with the girls became known to the people in general, it did not create misunderstandings among them. Rather, they enjoyed happiness in these dealings.**
