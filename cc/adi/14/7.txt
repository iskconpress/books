====== CC Ādi 14.7 ======

===== Text =====

> gṛhe dui jana dekhi laghupada-cihna
> tāhe śobhe dhvaja, vajra, śaṅkha, cakra, mīna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at home; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---the father and mother; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:laghu]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:c:cihna]]---the marks of the lotus feet, which were very small at that time; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---in those; [[synonyms:ś:śobhe]]---which were beautifully visible; [[synonyms:d:dhvaja]]---flag; [[synonyms:v:vajra]]---thunderbolts; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---conchshell; [[synonyms:c:cakra]]---disc; [[synonyms:m:mīna]]---fish.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord tried to walk, in His small footprints the specific marks of Lord Viṣṇu were visible, namely, the flag, thunderbolt, conchshell, disc and fish.**
