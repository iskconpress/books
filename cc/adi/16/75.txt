====== CC Ādi 16.75 ======

===== Text =====

> caturtha-caraṇe cāri 'bha'-kāra-prakāśa
> ataeva śabdālaṅkāra anuprāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturtha]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---in the fourth line; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four; [[synonyms:b:bha]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---of the letter //bha//; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestations; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ś:śabda]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---ornamental use of different sounds; [[synonyms:a:anuprāsa]]---alliteration.

===== Translation =====

**"In the fourth line the letter 'bha' occurs four times. This arrangement of alliteration is a pleasing ornamental use of sounds.**
