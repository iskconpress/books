====== Cc. Ādi 16.109 ======

===== Text =====

> e-saba līlā varṇiyāchena vṛndāvana-dāsa
> ye kichu viśeṣa ihāṅ karila prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:v:varṇiyāchena]]---has described; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---whatever; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---specifics; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---in this connection; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---I have made; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---presentation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has described all these incidents elaborately. I have only presented the specific incidents he has not described.**
