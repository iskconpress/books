====== Cc. Ādi 16.82 ======

===== Text =====

> ambujam ambuni jātaṁ kvacid api na jātam ambujād ambu
> mura-bhidi tad-viparītaṁ pādāmbhojān mahā-nadī jātā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ambujam]]---lotus flower; [[synonyms:a:ambuni]]---in the water; [[synonyms:j:jātam]]---is grown; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---at any time; [[synonyms:a:api]]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jātam]]---grown; [[synonyms:a:ambujāt]]---from a lotus flower; [[synonyms:a:ambu]]---water; [[synonyms:m:mura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhidi]]---in Kṛṣṇa, the killer of Murāsura; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:v:viparītam]]---just the opposite of that; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:a:ambhojāt]]---from the lotus flower of His feet; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:n:nadī]]---the great river; [[synonyms:j:jātā]]---has grown.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Everyone knows that lotus flowers grow in the water but water never grows from a lotus. All such contradictions, however, are wonderfully possible in Kṛṣṇa: the great river Ganges has grown from His lotus feet.'**
