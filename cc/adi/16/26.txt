====== Cc. Ādi 16.26 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-dāsa ihā kariyāchena vistāra
> sphuṭa nāhi kare doṣa-guṇera vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndavana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]]---has made; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---elaborate description; [[synonyms:s:sphuṭa]]---what was clear; [[synonyms:n:nahi]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇera]]---of both the faults and the virtues; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---analysis.

===== Translation =====

**Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has previously elaborately described this. That which is clear need not be scrutinized for good qualities and faults.**
