====== CC Ādi 16.30 ======

===== Text =====

> vasāilā tāre prabhu ādara kariyā
> digvijayī kahe mane avajñā kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made seated; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:ādara]]---adoration; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---offering him; [[synonyms:d:dig]]-[[synonyms:v:vijayī]]---Keśava Kāśmīrī; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within his mind; [[synonyms:a:avajñā]]---disregard; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord received him with adoration, but because Keśava Kāśmīrī was very proud, he talked to the Lord very inconsiderately.**
