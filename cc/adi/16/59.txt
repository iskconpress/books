====== Cc. Ādi 16.59 ======

===== Text =====

> 'dvitīya śrī-lakṣmī'---ihāṅ 'dvitīyatva' vidheya
> samāse gauṇa haila, śabdārtha gela kṣaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvitīyaśrī]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the word //dvitīya-śrī-lakṣmī// ("all-opulent goddess of fortune"); [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---this; [[synonyms:d:dvitīyatva]]---the quality of being a second; [[synonyms:v:vidheya]]---the unknown, which is to be explained; [[synonyms:s:samāse]]---in the compound word; [[synonyms:g:gauṇa]]---secondary; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ś:śabda]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the word's intended meaning; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---became; [[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---lost.

===== Translation =====

**"In the word 'dvitīya-śrīlakṣmī' ['the second all-opulent goddess of fortune'], the quality of being a second Lakṣmī is the unknown. In making this compound word, the meaning became secondary and the originally intended meaning was lost.**
