====== CC Ādi 16.96 ======

===== Text =====

> āji tāṅre nivediba, kari' japa-dhyāna
> śiśu-dvāre kaila more eta apamāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto her; [[synonyms:n:nivediba]]---I shall offer my prayers; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:j:japa]]---chanting; [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]]---meditation; [[synonyms:ś:śiśu]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---through a boy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---has done; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:a:apamāna]]---insult.

===== Translation =====

**"I shall offer prayers and meditation to the goddess of learning," the champion concluded, "and ask her why she has insulted me so greatly through this boy."**
