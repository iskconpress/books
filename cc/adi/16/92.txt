====== CC Ādi 16.92 ======

===== Text =====

> alaṅkāra nāhi paḍa, nāhi śāstrābhyāsa
> kemane e saba artha karile prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---the literary use of words; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pada]]---You never read; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---nor is there; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:a:abhyāsa]]---long practice in the discussion of the //śāstras//; [[synonyms:k:kemane]]---by which method; [[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---explanations; [[synonyms:k:karile]]---You have made; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**"I am surprised. You are not a literary student and do not have long experience in studying the śāstras. How have You been able to explain all these critical points?"**
