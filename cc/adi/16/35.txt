====== CC Ādi 16.35 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra kavitva kichu śunite haya mana
> kṛpā kari' kara yadi gaṅgāra varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kavitva]]---poetic integrity; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing Me; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you do; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāra]]---of mother Ganges; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**"Therefore I desire to hear your skill in composing poetry. We could hear this if you would mercifully describe the glory of mother Ganges."**
