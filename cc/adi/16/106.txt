====== CC Ādi 16.106 ======

===== Text =====

> sarasvatī svapne tāṅre upadeśa kaila
> sākṣāt īśvara kari' prabhuke jānila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---mother Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:s:svapne]]---in a dream; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]]---advice; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---gave; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Person; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---he understood.

===== Translation =====

**In a dream the goddess informed him of the Lord's position, and the poetic champion could understand that Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself.**
