====== CC Ādi 16.6 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-śāstre sarva paṇḍita pāya parājaya
> vinaya-bhaṅgīte kāro duḥkha nāhi haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in all scriptures; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned scholars; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---obtain; [[synonyms:p:parājaya]]---defeat; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---gentle; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgīte]]---by behavior; [[synonyms:k:kāro]]---anyone's; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord defeated all kinds of scholars in discourses about all the scriptures, yet because of His gentle behavior, none of them were unhappy.**
