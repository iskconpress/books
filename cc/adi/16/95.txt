====== Cc. Ādi 16.95 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā śuni' digvijayī karila niścaya
> śiśu-dvāre devī more kaila parājaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:d:dig]]-[[synonyms:v:vijayī]]---the champion; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---admitted; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---decision; [[synonyms:ś:śiśu]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---through this boy; [[synonyms:d:devī]]---mother Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---has done; [[synonyms:p:parājaya]]---defeat.

===== Translation =====

**When he heard this judgment from Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the paṇḍita sorrowfully wondered why mother Sarasvatī wanted to defeat him through a small boy.**
