====== CC Ādi 16.99 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi baḍa paṇḍita, mahākavi-śiromaṇi
> yāṅra mukhe bāhirāya aiche kavya-vāṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---greatly learned scholar; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kavi]]---of all great poets; [[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---the topmost; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth; [[synonyms:b:bāhirāya]]---emanates; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:k:kāvya]]-[[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---poetic language.

===== Translation =====

**"You are the most learned scholar and the topmost of all great poets, for otherwise how could such fine poetry come from your mouth?**
