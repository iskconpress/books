====== Cc. Ādi 16.101 ======

===== Text =====

> bhavabhūti, jayadeva, āra kālidāsa
> tāṅ-sabāra kavitve āche doṣera prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhavabhūti]]---of the name Bhavabhūti; [[synonyms:j:jayadeva]]---of the name Jayadeva; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kālidāsa]]---of the name Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:k:kavitve]]---in the poetic power; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:d:doṣera]]---of faults; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**"Even in the poetic compositions of such great poets as Bhavabhūti, Jayadeva and Kālidāsa there are many examples of faults.**
