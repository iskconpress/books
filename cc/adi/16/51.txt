====== CC Ādi 16.51 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena---ataeva puchiye tomāre
> vicāriyā guṇa-doṣa bujhāha āmāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:p:puchiye]]---I am asking; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---you; [[synonyms:v:vicāriyā]]---completely reviewing; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---faults; [[synonyms:b:bujhāha]]---teach; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]]---Me.

===== Translation =====

**Taking a humble position, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Because I am not of your level, I have asked you to teach Me by explaining the faults and good qualities in your poetry.**
