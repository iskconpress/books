====== CC Ādi 16.29 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle digvijayī tāhāṅi āilā
> gaṅgāre vandana kari' prabhure mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:d:dig]]-[[synonyms:v:vijayī]]---Keśava Kāśmīrī; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---reached; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāre]]---to mother Ganges; [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---prayers; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**Coincidentally Keśava Kāśmīrī Paṇḍita also came there. While offering his prayers to mother Ganges, he met Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
