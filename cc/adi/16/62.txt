====== CC Ādi 16.62 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bhavānī-bhartṛ'-śabda dile pāiyā santoṣa
> 'viruddha-mati-kṛt' nāma ei mahā doṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhavānī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhartṛśabda]]---the word //bhavānī-bhartṛ// ("the husband of Bhavānī"); [[synonyms:d:dile]]---you have placed; [[synonyms:p:pāiyā]]---getting; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---very much satisfaction; [[synonyms:v:viruddha]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛt]]---a statement of opposing elements; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---great; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault.

===== Translation =====

**"Here is another great fault. You have arranged the word 'bhavānī-bhartṛ' to your great satisfaction, but this betrays the fault of contradiction.**
