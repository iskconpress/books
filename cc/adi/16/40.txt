====== CC Ādi 16.40 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe digvijayī vyākhyāra śloka puchila
> śata ślokera eka śloka prabhu ta' paḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:d:dig]]-[[synonyms:v:vijayī]]---Keśava Kāśmīrī; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāra]]---for explanation; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---a verse; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired about; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---one hundred; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verses; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---then; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---recited.

===== Translation =====

**The Digvijayī, Keśava Kāśmīrī, inquired which verse He wanted explained. The Lord then recited one of the one hundred verses Keśava Kāśmīrī had composed.**
