====== Cc. Ādi 16.24 ======

===== Text =====

> śiṣya-gaṇa lañā punaḥ vidyāra vilāsa
> vidyā-bale sabā jini' auddhatya prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---disciples; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:v:vidyāra]]---of education; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastime; [[synonyms:v:vidyā]]-[[synonyms:b:bale]]---by the strength of education; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everyone; [[synonyms:j:jini']]---conquering; [[synonyms:a:auddhatya]]---of pride; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**After coming back from East Bengal, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again began educating others. By the strength of His education He conquered everyone, and thus He was greatly proud.**
