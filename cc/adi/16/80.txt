====== CC Ādi 16.80 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ihāṅ viṣṇu-pāda-padme gaṅgāra utpatti'
> virodhālaṅkāra ihā mahā-camatkṛti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---in this connection; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padme]]---in the lotus feet of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāra]]---of mother Ganges; [[synonyms:u:utpatti]]---beginning; [[synonyms:v:virodha]]---contradiction; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---literary decoration; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---it; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---very great; [[synonyms:c:camatkṛti]]---wonder.

===== Translation =====

**"The existence of mother Ganges begins from the lotus feet of the Lord. Although this statement that water comes from a lotus flower is a contradiction, in connection with Lord Viṣṇu it is a great wonder.**
