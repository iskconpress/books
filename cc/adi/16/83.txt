====== CC Ādi 16.83 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgāra mahattva---sādhya, sādhana tāhāra
> viṣṇu-pādotpatti---'anumāna' alaṅkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgāra]]---of the Ganges; [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---opulences; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]---subject matter; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---means; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:u:utpatti]]---her origin from the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:anumāna]]---called //anumāna// (hypothesis); [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---an ornament.

===== Translation =====

**"The real glory of mother Ganges is that she has grown from the lotus feet of Lord Viṣṇu. Such a hypothesis is another ornament, called anumāna.**
