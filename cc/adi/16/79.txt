====== CC Ādi 16.79 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gaṅgāte kamala janme'---sabāra subodha
> 'kamale gaṅgara janma'---atyanta virodha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgāte]]---in the river Ganges; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]---lotus flower; [[synonyms:j:janme]]---grows; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:s:subodha]]---understanding; [[synonyms:k:kamale]]---in the lotus flower; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāra]]---of the Ganges; [[synonyms:j:janma]]---birth; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---very much; [[synonyms:v:virodha]]---contradiction.

===== Translation =====

**"Everyone knows that lotus flowers grow in the water of the Ganges. But to say that the Ganges takes birth from a lotus flower seems extremely contradictory.**
