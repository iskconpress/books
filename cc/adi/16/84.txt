====== CC Ādi 16.84 ======

===== Text =====

> sthūla ei pañca doṣa, pañca alaṅkāra
> sūkṣma vicāriye yadi āchaye apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sthūla]]---gross; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---faults; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---literary ornaments; [[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---in detail; [[synonyms:v:vicāriye]]---we consider; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:ā:āchaye]]---there are; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited.

===== Translation =====

**"I have simply discussed the five gross faults and five literary embellishments of this verse, but if we consider it in fine detail we will find unlimited faults.**
