====== CC Ādi 16.56 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gaṅgāra mahattva'---śloke mūla 'vidheya'
> idaṁ śabde 'anuvāda'---pāche avidheya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgāra]] [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---glorification of mother Ganges; [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in the verse; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---chief; [[synonyms:v:vidheya]]---unknown; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word; [[synonyms:a:anuvāda]]---the known; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at the end; [[synonyms:a:avidheya]]---improper.

===== Translation =====

**"The glorification of the Ganges [mahattvaṁ gaṅgāyāḥ] is the principal unknown subject matter in this verse, and the known subject matter is indicated by the word 'idam,' which has been placed after the unknown.**
