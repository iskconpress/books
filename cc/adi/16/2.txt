====== CC Ādi 16.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaitacandra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---all glories to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:a:advaitacandra]]---all glories to Advaitacandra; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---to all the devotees of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Lord Nityānanda! All glories to Advaitacandra! And all glories to all the devotees of the Lord!**
