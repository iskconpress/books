====== CC Ādi 16.5 ======

===== Text =====

> śata śata śiṣya saṅge sadā adhyāpana
> vyākhyā śuni sarva-lokera camakita mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---very many; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---disciples; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with Him; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:a:adhyāpana]]---studying; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyā]]---explanation; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all people; [[synonyms:c:camakita]]---astonished; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---minds.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as the Lord became a teacher, many, many students came to Him, every one of them astonished to hear His mode of explanation.**
