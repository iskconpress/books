====== CC Ādi 16.110 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñira līlā---amṛtera dhāra
> sarvendriya tṛpta haya śravaṇe yāhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---drops of nectar; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]---all senses; [[synonyms:t:tṛpta]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of them all.

===== Translation =====

**The nectarean drops of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes can satisfy the senses of everyone who hears them.**
