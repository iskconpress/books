====== Cc. Ādi 16.68 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi ei śloke āche pañca alaṅkāra
> ei pañca-doṣe śloka kaila chārakhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in this verse; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---literary embellishments; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:d:doṣe]]---by the above-mentioned five faults; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---has been made; [[synonyms:c:chārakhāra]]---spoiled.

===== Translation =====

**"Although there are five literary ornaments decorating this verse, the entire verse has been spoiled by these five most faulty presentations.**
