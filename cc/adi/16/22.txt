====== CC Ādi 16.22 ======

===== Text =====

> antare jānilā prabhu, yāte antaryāmī
> deśere āilā prabhu śacī-duḥkha jāni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:antare]]---within Himself; [[synonyms:j:jānilā]]---knew; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---because; [[synonyms:a:antaryāmī]]---He is the Supersoul; [[synonyms:d:deśere]]---to the country; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---of mother Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---the unhappiness; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya knew about the disappearance of Lakṣmīdevī because He is the Supersoul Himself. Thus He returned home to solace His mother, Śacīdevī, who was greatly unhappy about the death of her daughter-in-law.**
