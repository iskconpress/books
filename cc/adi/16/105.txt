====== CC Ādi 16.105 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate nija ghare gelā dui jana
> kavi rātre kaila sarasvatī-ārādhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to their respective homes; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went back; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:kavi]]---poet; [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---of mother Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:ā:ārādhana]]---worship.

===== Translation =====

**In this way both the poet and Caitanya Mahāprabhu went back to their homes, and at night the poet worshiped mother Sarasvatī.**
