====== Cc. Ādi 16.48 ======

===== Text =====

> pratibhāra kāvya tomāra devatā santoṣe
> bhāla-mate vicārile jāni guṇa-doṣe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratibhāra]]---of ingenuity; [[synonyms:k:kāvya]]---poetry; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:d:devatā]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:santoṣe]]---satisfies; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---scrutinizingly; [[synonyms:v:vicārile]]---on analyzing; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I know; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:doṣe]]---there are faults and good qualities also.

===== Translation =====

**"There is no doubt that your poetry is full of ingenuity, and certainly it has satisfied the Supreme Lord. Yet if we scrutinizingly consider it we can find both good qualities and faults."**
