====== Cc. Ādi 16.67 ======

===== Text =====

> tina pāde anuprāsa dekhi anupama
> eka pāde nāhi, ei doṣa 'bhagna-krama'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:p:pāde]]---in three lines; [[synonyms:a:anuprāsa]]---alliteration; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:a:anupama]]---extraordinary; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:p:pāde]]---in one line; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not (alliteration); [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---this fault; [[synonyms:b:bhagna]]-[[synonyms:k:krama]]---deviation.

===== Translation =====

**"There is extraordinary alliteration in three lines of the verse, but in one line there is no such alliteration. This is the fault of deviation.**
