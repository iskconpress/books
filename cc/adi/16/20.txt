====== Cc. Ādi 16.20 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata baṅge prabhu kare nānā līlā
> ethā navadvīpe lakṣmī virahe duḥkhī hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:baṅge]]---in East Bengal; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---in Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the wife of Nimāi Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**Because the Lord was engaged in various ways in preaching work in East Bengal, His wife, Lakṣmīdevī, was very unhappy at home in separation from her husband.**
