====== CC Ādi 16.36 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā brāhmaṇa garve varṇite lāgilā
> ghaṭī eke śata śloka gaṅgāra varṇilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing this; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---the //paṇḍita,// Keśava Kāśmīrī; [[synonyms:g:garve]]---in pride; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---to describe; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭī]]---hour; [[synonyms:e:eke]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---hundred; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāra]]---of the Ganges; [[synonyms:v:varṇilā]]---described.

===== Translation =====

**When the brāhmaṇa, Keśava Kāśmīrī, heard this, he became still more puffed up, and within one hour he composed one hundred verses describing mother Ganges.**
