====== CC Ādi 16.78 ======

===== Text =====

> 'lakṣmīr iva' arthālaṅkāra---upamā-prakāśa
> āra arthālaṅkāra āche, nāma---'virodhābhāsa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmīr]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---the words //lakṣmīr iva// (like Lakṣmī); [[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---ornamental use of the meaning; [[synonyms:u:upamā]]---analogy; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---ornamental use of meaning; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---which is named; [[synonyms:v:virodha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---possibility of contradiction.

===== Translation =====

**"The use of the words 'lakṣmīr iva' ['like Lakṣmī'] manifests the ornament of meaning called upamā [analogy]. There is also the further ornament of meaning called virodhābhāsa, or a contradictory indication.**
