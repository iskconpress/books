====== CC Ādi 16.73 ======

===== Text =====

> śabdālaṅkāra---tina-pāde āche anuprāsa
> 'śrī-lakṣmī' śabde 'punar-uktavad-ābhāsa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śabda]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---ornamentation of sound; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:p:pāde]]---in three lines; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:a:anuprāsa]]---alliteration; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---in the words //śrī-lakṣmī//; [[synonyms:p:punar]]-[[synonyms:u:ukta]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---of repetition of the same word; [[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---there is a tinge.

===== Translation =====

**"There is a sound ornament of alliteration in three lines. And in the combination of the words 'śrī' and 'lakṣmī' there is the ornament of a tinge of redundancy.**
