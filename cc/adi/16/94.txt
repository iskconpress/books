====== Cc. Ādi 16.94 ======

===== Text =====

> śāstrera vicāra bhāla-manda nāhi jāni
> sarasvatī ye balāya, sei bali vāṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---discussion of //śāstra//; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:m:manda]]---good or bad; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---do not know; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---mother Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:b:balāya]]---whatever she speaks; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:b:bali]]---I say; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear sir, I do not know what is good composition and what is bad. But whatever I have spoken must be understood to have been spoken by mother Sarasvatī."**
