====== CC Ādi 16.87 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā prabhura vyākhyā digvijayī vismita
> mukhe nā niḥsare vākya, pratibhā stambhita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyā]]---explanation; [[synonyms:d:dig]]-[[synonyms:v:vijayī]]---the champion; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---struck with wonder; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---did not; [[synonyms:n:niḥsare]]---come out; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---words; [[synonyms:p:pratibhā]]---ingenuity; [[synonyms:s:stambhita]]---choked up.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing the explanation of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the champion poet was struck with wonder. His cleverness stunned, he could not say anything.**
