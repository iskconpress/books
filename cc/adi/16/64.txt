====== CC Ādi 16.64 ======

===== Text =====

> 'śiva-patnīra bhartā' ihā śunite viruddha
> 'viruddha-mati-kṛt' śabda śāstre nahe śuddha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śiva]]-[[synonyms:p:patnīra]]---of the wife of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:b:bhartā]]---husband; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:v:viruddha]]---contradiction; [[synonyms:v:viruddha]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛt]]---that which creates a contradiction; [[synonyms:ś:śabda]]---such a word; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in the scriptures; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---pure.

===== Translation =====

**"It is contradictory to hear that Lord Śiva's wife has another husband. The use of such words in literature creates the fault called viruddha-mati-kṛt.**
