====== Cc. Ādi 16.14 ======

===== Text =====

> svapna dekhi' miśra āsi' prabhura caraṇe
> svapnera vṛttānta saba kaila nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svapna]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing the dream; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the shelter of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:svapnera]]---of the dream; [[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]]---details; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---inform Him.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the dream, Tapana Miśra came to the shelter of Lord Caitanya's lotus feet, and he described all the details of the dream to the Lord.**
