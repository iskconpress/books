====== Cc. Ādi 16.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kaiśora-līlāra sūtra-anubandha
> śiṣya-gaṇa paḍāite karilā ārambha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kaiśora]]---the age of //kaiśora// (the age between the eleventh and fifteenth years); [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:a:anubandha]]---chronological synopsis; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---students; [[synonyms:p:paḍāite]]---to teach; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:ārambha]]---begin.

===== Translation =====

**At the age of eleven Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to teach students. This marks the beginning of His kaiśora age.**
