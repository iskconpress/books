====== Cc. Ādi 9.54 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ prema-phala-vitaraṇa
> ebe śuna, phala-dātā ye ye śākhā-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---however; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have explained; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---the fruit of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vitaraṇa]]---distribution; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:d:dātā]]---the giver of the fruit; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:y:ye]]---who and who; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---branches.

===== Translation =====

**After describing the Lord's distribution of the fruit of love of Godhead, I now wish to describe the different branches of the tree of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
