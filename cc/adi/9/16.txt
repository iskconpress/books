====== CC Ādi 9.16 ======

===== Text =====

> madhya-mūla paramānanda purī mahā-dhīra
> aṣṭa dike aṣṭa mūla vṛkṣa kaila sthira

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:m:mūla]]---the middle root; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]] [[synonyms:p:purī]]---of the name Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:dhīra]]---most sober; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]] [[synonyms:d:dike]]---in the eight directions; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]] [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---eight roots; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]---the tree; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:s:sthira]]---fixed.

===== Translation =====

**With the sober and grave Paramānanda Purī as the central root and the other eight roots in the eight directions, the tree of Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood firmly.**
