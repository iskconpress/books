====== CC Ādi 9.26 ======

===== Text =====

> mūla-skandhera śākhā āra upaśākhā-gaṇe
> lāgilā ye prema-phala,---amṛtake jine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mūla]]-[[synonyms:s:skandhera]]---of the chief trunk; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---branches; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:u:upaśākhā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---subbranches; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---as it grew; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---the fruit of love; [[synonyms:a:amṛtake]] [[synonyms:j:jine]]---such a fruit conquers nectar.

===== Translation =====

**Since Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu was the original trunk, the taste of the fruits that grew on the branches and subbranches surpassed the taste of nectar.**
