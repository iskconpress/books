====== Cc. Ādi 9.30 ======

===== Text =====

> añjali añjali bhari' phele caturdiśe
> daridra kuḍāñā khāya, mālākāra hāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:añjali]]---handful; [[synonyms:a:añjali]]---handful; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:p:phele]]---distributes; [[synonyms:c:catur]]-[[synonyms:d:diśe]]---in all directions; [[synonyms:d:daridra]]---poor; [[synonyms:k:kuḍāñā]]---picking up; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---eats; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---the gardener; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---smiles.

===== Translation =====

**The transcendental gardener, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, distributed handful after handful of fruit in all directions, and when the poor hungry people ate the fruit, the gardener smiled with great pleasure.**
