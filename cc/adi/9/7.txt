====== Cc. Ādi 9.7 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe, āmi 'viśvambhara' nāma dhari
> nāma sārthaka haya, yadi preme viśva bhari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:v:viśvambhara]]---Viśvambhara; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:d:dhari]]---accept; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:s:sārthaka]]---complete; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love of God; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]---the whole universe; [[synonyms:b:bhari]]---fulfilled.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya thought, "My name is Viśvambhara, 'one who maintains the entire universe.' Its meaning will be actualized if I can fill the whole universe with love of Godhead."**
