====== CC Ādi 9.27 ======

===== Text =====

> pākila ye prema-phala amṛta-madhura
> vilāya caitanya-mālī, nāhi laya mūla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pākila]]---ripened; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---the fruit of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---nectarean; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:v:vilāya]]---distributes; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:mālī]]---the gardener, Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---take; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---price.

===== Translation =====

**The fruits ripened and became sweet and nectarean. The gardener, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, distributed them without asking any price.**
