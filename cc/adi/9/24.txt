====== CC Ādi 9.24 ======

===== Text =====

> śiṣya, praśiṣya, āra upaśiṣya-gaṇa
> jagat vyāpila tāra nāhika gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---disciples; [[synonyms:p:praśiṣya]]---granddisciples; [[synonyms:a:ara]]---and; [[synonyms:u:upaśiṣya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---admirers; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:v:vyāpila]]---spread; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is none; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---enumeration.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the disciples and the granddisciples and their admirers spread throughout the entire world, and it is not possible to enumerate them all.**
