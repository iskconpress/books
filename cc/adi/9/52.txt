====== CC Ādi 9.52 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-loke matta kailā āpana-samāna
> preme matta loka vinā nāhi dekhi āna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loke]]---all people; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---He made; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]---Himself; [[synonyms:s:samāna]]---like; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love of God; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---do not; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---we see; [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---anything else.

===== Translation =====

**With His saṅkīrtana movement the Lord made everyone mad like Himself. We do not find anyone who was not intoxicated by His saṅkīrtana movement.**
