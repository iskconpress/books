====== Cc. Ādi 9.6 ======

===== Text =====

> mālā-kāraḥ svayaṁ kṛṣṇa-
> premāmara-taruḥ svayam
> dātā bhoktā tat-phalānāṁ
> yas taṁ caitanyam āśraye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraḥ]]---gardener; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love; [[synonyms:a:amara]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:t:taruḥ]]---tree; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:d:dātā]]---giver; [[synonyms:b:bhoktā]]---enjoyer; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:phalānām]]---of all the fruits of that tree; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---one who; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:c:caitanyam]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āśraye]]---I take shelter.

===== Translation =====

**I take shelter of the Supreme Personality of Godhead Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who Himself is the tree of transcendental love of Kṛṣṇa, its gardener and also the bestower and enjoyer of its fruits.**
