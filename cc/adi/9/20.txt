====== CC Ādi 9.20 ======

===== Text =====

> mukhya mukhya śākhā-gaṇera nāma agaṇana
> āge ta' kariba, śuna vṛkṣera varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---the foremost of them all; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the branches; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:a:agaṇana]]---uncountable; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---subsequently; [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall do; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣera]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---the description of the Caitanya tree.

===== Translation =====

**I shall try to name the foremost of the innumerable branches. Please hear the description of the Caitanya tree.**
