====== Cc. Ādi 9.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya gauracandra
> jaya jayādvaita jaya jaya nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---whose name is Gaurahari; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---to Advaita Gosāñi; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Nityānanda.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya, who is known as Gaurahari! All glories to Advaita and Nityānanda Prabhu!**
