====== Cc. Ādi 9.21 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛkṣera upare śākhā haila dui skandha
> eka 'advaita' nāma, āra 'nityānanda'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣera]]---of the tree; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the top; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---branch; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:s:skandha]]---trunks; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Śrī Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the name; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of the name Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**At the top of the tree the trunk branched into two. One trunk was named Śrī Advaita Prabhu and the other Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu.**
