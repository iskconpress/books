====== Cc. Ādi 9.31 ======

===== Text =====

> mālākāra kahe,---śuna, vṛkṣa-parivāra
> mūlaśākhā-upaśākhā yateka prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---the gardener; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:parivāra]]---the family of this transcendental tree of devotional service; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]-[[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---chief branches; [[synonyms:u:upaśākhā]]---subbranches; [[synonyms:y:yateka]]---as many; [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---varieties.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya thus addressed the multifarious varieties of branches and subbranches of the tree of devotional service:**
