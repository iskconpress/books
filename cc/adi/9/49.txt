====== Cc. Ādi 9.49 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-mādaka prema-phala peṭa bhari' khāya
> mātila sakala loka---hāse, nāce, gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mādaka]]---great intoxicant; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---this fruit of love of God; [[synonyms:p:peṭa]]---belly; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---let them eat; [[synonyms:m:mātila]]---became mad; [[synonyms:s:sakalaloka]]---all the people in general; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laugh; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant.

===== Translation =====

**The fruit of love of Godhead distributed by Caitanya Mahāprabhu is such a great intoxicant that anyone who eats it, filling his belly, immediately becomes maddened by it, and automatically he chants, dances, laughs and enjoys.**
