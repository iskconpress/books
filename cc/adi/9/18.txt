====== CC Ādi 9.18 ======

===== Text =====

> viśa viśa śākhā kari' eka eka maṇḍala
> mahā-mahā-śākhā chāila brahmāṇḍa sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśa]] [[synonyms:v:viśa]]---twenty, twenty; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---branches; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making a group; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---form a society; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---big branches; [[synonyms:c:chāila]]---covered; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---the whole universe; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the branches of the Caitanya tree formed a cluster or society, with great branches covering all the universe.**

===== Purport =====

Our International Society for Krishna Consciousness is one of the branches of the Caitanya tree.
