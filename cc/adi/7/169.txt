====== CC Ādi 7.169 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-caitanya, nityānanda, advaita,---tina jana
> śrīvāsa-gadādhara-ādi yata bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya,]] [[synonyms:n:nityānanda,]] [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Nityānanda Prabhu and Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---these three; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---personalities; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Śrīvāsa and Gadādhara; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---etc.; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**While chanting the Pañca-tattva mahā-mantra, one must chant the names of Śrī Caitanya, Nityānanda, Advaita, Gadādhara and Śrīvāsa with their many devotees. This is the process.**
