====== Cc. Ādi 7.129 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvāśraya īśvarera praṇava uddeśa
> 'tat tvam asi'---vākya haya vedera ekadeśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the reservoir of everything; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:praṇava]]---//oṁkāra//; [[synonyms:u:uddeśa]]---purpose; [[synonyms:t:tat]] [[synonyms:t:tvam]] [[synonyms:a:asi]]---the Vedic //mantra tat tvam asi// ("you are the same"); [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---statement; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:v:vedera]]---of the Vedic literature; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---partial understanding.

===== Translation =====

**"It is the purpose of the Supreme Personality of Godhead to present praṇava [oṁkāra] as the reservoir of all Vedic knowledge. The words tat tvam asi are only a partial explanation of the Vedic knowledge.**

===== Purport =====

//Tat tvam asi// means "you are the same spiritual identity."
