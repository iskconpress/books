====== CC Ādi 7.154 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhuke dekhite āise sakala sannyāsī
> prabhura praśaṁsā kare saba vārāṇasī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---they came; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsā]]---praise; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---they do; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]---the city of Vārāṇasī.

===== Translation =====

**Many Māyāvādī sannyāsīs of Vārāṇasī came to see the Lord after this incident, and the entire city praised Him.**
