====== Cc. Ādi 7.85 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcama puruṣārtha---premānandāmṛta-sindhu
> mokṣādi ānanda yāra nahe eka bindu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañcama]]---fifth; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---goal of life; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---the spiritual bliss of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---eternal; [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---ocean; [[synonyms:m:mokṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---liberation and other principles of religiosity; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasures derived from them; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---never comparable; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:b:bindu]]---drop.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'For a devotee who has actually developed bhāva, the pleasure derived from dharma, artha, kāma and mokṣa appears like a drop in the presence of the sea.**
