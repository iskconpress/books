====== CC Ādi 7.136 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya-kalpita artha,---ihā sabhe jāni
> sampradāya-anurodhe tabu tāhā māni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Śaṅkarācārya; [[synonyms:k:kalpita]]---imaginative; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sabhe]]---all of us; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---know; [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]-[[synonyms:a:anurodhe]]---but for the sake of our party; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---we accept.

===== Translation =====

**"We know that all this word jugglery springs from the imagination of Śaṅkarārārya, and yet because we belong to his sect, we accept it although it does not satisfy us.**
