====== Cc. Ādi 7.49 ======

===== Text =====

> itimadhye candraśekhara, miśra-tapana
> duḥkhī hañā prabhu-pāya kaila nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the meantime; [[synonyms:c:candraśekhara]]---the clerk of the name Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:t:tapana]]---as well as Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming very unhappy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---an appeal.

===== Translation =====

**While Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu was instructing Sanātana Gosvāmī, both Candraśekhara and Tapana Miśra became very unhappy. Therefore they submitted an appeal unto the lotus feet of the Lord.**
