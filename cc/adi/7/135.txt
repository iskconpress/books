====== CC Ādi 7.135 ======

===== Text =====

> sakala sannyāsī kahe,---'śunaha śrīpāda
> tumi ye khaṇḍile artha, e nahe vivāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:ś:śunaha]]---please hear; [[synonyms:ś:śrīpāda]]---Your Holiness; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍile]]---refuted; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:v:vivāda]]---quarrel.

===== Translation =====

**All the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs said, "Your Holiness, kindly know from us that we actually have no quarrel with Your refutation of these meanings, for You have given a clear understanding of the sūtras.**
