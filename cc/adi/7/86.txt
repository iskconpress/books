====== Cc. Ādi 7.86 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nāmera phala---'premā', sarva-śāstre kaya
> bhāgye sei premā tomāya karila udaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---result; [[synonyms:p:premā]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---in all; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---describe; [[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---fortunately; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:premā]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---Your; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---has done; [[synonyms:u:udaya]]---arisen.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The conclusion of all revealed scriptures is that one should awaken his dormant love of Godhead. You are greatly fortunate to have already done so.**
