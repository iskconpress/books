====== Cc. Ādi 7.126 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā ratna-rāśi haya cintāmaṇi haite
> tathāpiha maṇi rahe svarūpe avikṛte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]---varieties; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:r:rāśi]]---valuable jewels; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become possible; [[synonyms:c:cintāmaṇi]]---the touchstone; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:t:tathāpiha]]---still, certainly; [[synonyms:m:maṇi]]---the touchstone; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:s:svarūpe]]---in its original form; [[synonyms:a:avikṛte]]---without change.

===== Translation =====

**"Although a touchstone produces many varieties of valuable jewels, it nevertheless remains the same. It does not change its original form.**
