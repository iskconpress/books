====== Cc. Ādi 7.124 ======

===== Text =====

> avicintya-śakti-yukta śrī-bhagavān
> icchāya jagad-rūpe pāya pariṇāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avicintya]]---inconceivable; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---potency; [[synonyms:y:yūkta]]---possessed of; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]---the affluent; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---by His wish; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of the cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:p:pariṇāma]]---transformed by His energy.

===== Translation =====

**"The Supreme Personality of Godhead is opulent in all respects. Therefore by His inconceivable energies He has transformed the material cosmic manifestation.**
