====== CC Ādi 7.167 ======

===== Text =====

> setubandha paryanta kailā bhaktira pracāra
> kṛṣṇa-prema diyā kailā sabāra nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:setubandha]]---the place where Lord Rāmacandra constructed His bridge; [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---up to that place; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of the cult of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---broadcast; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---everyone; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the Lord went to the southernmost tip of the Indian peninsula, known as Setubandha [Cape Comorin]. Everywhere He distributed the bhakti cult and love of Kṛṣṇa, and in this way He delivered everyone.**
