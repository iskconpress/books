====== CC Ādi 7.9 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kṛṣṇa avatīrṇa śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya
> sei parikara-gaṇa saṅge saba dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---that very Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]]---has advented; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---in the form of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:p:parikara]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---associates; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---glorious.

===== Translation =====

**The selfsame Lord Kṛṣṇa advented Himself as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with all His eternal associates, who are also equally glorious.**
