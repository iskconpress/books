====== CC Ādi 7.152 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā kari' mahāprabhu āilā vāsāghara
> hena citra-līlā kare gaurāṅga-sundara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---accepting food from others; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:v:vāsāghara]]---to His residence; [[synonyms:h:hena]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:citra]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---wonderful pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sundara]]---very beautiful.

===== Translation =====

**After taking lunch among the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is known as Gaurasundara, returned to His residence. Thus the Lord performs His wonderful pastimes.**
