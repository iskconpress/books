====== Cc. Ādi 7.125 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi acintya-śaktye haya avikārī
> prākṛta cintāmaṇi tāhe dṛṣṭānta ye dhari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---yet; [[synonyms:a:acintya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---by inconceivable potency; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---remains; [[synonyms:a:avikārī]]---without change; [[synonyms:p:prākṛta]]---material; [[synonyms:c:cintāmaṇi]]---touchstone; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---in that respect; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭānta]]---example; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:d:dhari]]---we accept.

===== Translation =====

**"Using the example of a touchstone, which by its energy turns iron to gold and yet remains the same, we can understand that although the Supreme Personality of Godhead transforms His innumerable energies, He remains unchanged.**
