====== CC Ādi 7.166 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane dakṣiṇa deśa karilā gamana
> grāme grāme kailā kṛṣṇa-nāma pracāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:d:deśa]]---South India; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---went; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---traveling; [[synonyms:g:grāme]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in each and every village; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---He did; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pracāraṇa]]---broadcasting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally went to South India, and He broadcast the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa in every village and town.**
