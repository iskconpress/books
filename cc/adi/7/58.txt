====== Cc. Ādi 7.58 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dine gelā prabhu se vipra-bhavane
> dekhilena, vasiyāchena sannyāsīra gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---next; [[synonyms:d:dine]]---day; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:b:bhavane]]---in the house of; [[synonyms:d:dekhilena]]---He saw; [[synonyms:v:vasiyāchena]]---there were sitting; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]]---all the //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---in a group.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, when Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the house of that brāhmaṇa, He saw all the sannyāsīs of Benares sitting there.**
