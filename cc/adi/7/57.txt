====== Cc. Ādi 7.57 ======

===== Text =====

** TEXT 57**

> se vipra jānena prabhu nā yā'na kā'ra ghare
> tāṅhāra preraṇāya tāṅre atyāgraha kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---knew it; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---never; [[synonyms:y:yā'na]]---goes; [[synonyms:k:kā'ra]]---anyone's; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---house; [[synonyms:t:taṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:p:preraṇāya]]---by inspiration; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:a:atyāgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---strongly urging to accept the invitation.

===== Translation =====

** 57>The brāhmaṇa knew that Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu never went to anyone else's house, yet due to inspiration from the Lord he earnestly requested Him to accept this invitation.**
