====== CC Ādi 7.87 ======

===== Text =====

> premāra svabhāve kare citta-tanu kṣobha
> kṛṣṇera caraṇa-prāptye upajāya lobha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:premāra]]---out of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:svabhāve]]---by nature; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---it induces; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---the consciousness; [[synonyms:t:tanu]]---the body; [[synonyms:k:kṣobha]]---agitated; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:prāptye]]---to obtain; [[synonyms:u:upajāya]]---it so becomes; [[synonyms:l:lobha]]---aspiration.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'It is a characteristic of love of Godhead that by nature it induces transcendental symptoms in one's body and makes one more and more greedy to achieve the shelter of the lotus feet of the Lord.**
