====== CC Ādi 7.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ithe bhakta-bhāva dhare caitanya gosāñi
> 'bhakta-svarūpa' tāṅra nityānanda-bhāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ithe]]---for this reason; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the ecstasy of a devotee; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---accepts; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---the transcendental teacher; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---exactly like a pure devotee; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---brother.

===== Translation =====

**For this reason Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the supreme teacher, accepts the form of a devotee and accepts Lord Nityānanda as His elder brother.**
