====== Cc. Ādi 7.35 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsa kariyā prabhu kailā ākarṣaṇa
> yateka pālāñāchila tārkikādigaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the //sannyāsa// order; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---accepting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---attract; [[synonyms:y:yateka]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pālāñāchila]]---fled; [[synonyms:t:tārkika]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all persons, beginning with the logicians.

===== Translation =====

**After accepting the sannyāsa order, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu attracted the attention of all those who had evaded Him, beginning with the logicians.**
