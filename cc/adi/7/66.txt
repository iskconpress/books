====== Cc. Ādi 7.66 ======

===== Text =====

> puchila, tomāra nāma 'śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya'
> keśava-bhāratīra śiṣya, tāte tumi dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---the name Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:k:keśava]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāratīra]] [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---You are a disciple of Keśava Bhāratī; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that connection; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You are; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---glorious.

===== Translation =====

**Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī then said: "I understand that Your name is Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya. You are a disciple of Śrī Keśava Bhāratī, and therefore You are glorious.**
