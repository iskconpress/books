====== CC Ādi 7.139 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-aiśvarye tāṅra nāhi māyā-gandha
> sakala vedera haya bhagavān se 'sambandha'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---in His original form; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarye]]---opulence; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is none; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:g:gandha]]---contamination of the material world; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---in all; [[synonyms:v:vedera]]---//Vedas//; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it is so; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]---relationship.

===== Translation =====

**"In His original form the Supreme Personality of Godhead is full of transcendental opulences, which are free from the contamination of the material world. It is to be understood that in all Vedic literature the Supreme Personality of Godhead is the ultimate goal.**
