====== Cc. Ādi 7.161 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-divase lokera śuni' kolāhala
> vārāṇasī chāḍi' prabhu āilā nīlācala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]---night; [[synonyms:d:divase]]---day; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people in general; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---tumult; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]---the city of Benares; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---to Purī.

===== Translation =====

**Because the city of Vārāṇasī was always full of tumultuous crowds, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, after sending Sanātana to Vṛndāvana, returned to Jagannātha Purī.**
