====== CC Ādi 7.134 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mate pratisūtre karena dūṣaṇa
> śuni' camatkāra haila sannyāsīra gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtre]]---in each and every aphorism; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---shows; [[synonyms:d:dūṣaṇa]]---defects; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---struck with wonder; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---they became; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]]---of all the Māyāvādīs; [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the group.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus showed for each and every sūtra the defects in Śaṅkarācārya's explanations, all the assembled Māyāvādī sannyāsīs were struck with wonder.**
