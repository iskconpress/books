====== CC Ādi 7.162 ======

===== Text =====

> ei līlā kahiba āge vistāra kariyā
> saṅkṣepe kahilāṅ ihāṅ prasaṅga pāiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kahiba]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---later on; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---vivid description; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in short; [[synonyms:k:kahilāṅ]]---I have spoken; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---in this place; [[synonyms:p:prasaṅga]]---topics; [[synonyms:p:pāiyā]]---taking advantage of.

===== Translation =====

**I have here given a brief account of these pastimes of Lord Caitanya, but later I shall describe them in an extensive way.**
