====== Cc. Ādi 4.3 ======

===== Text =====

> caturtha ślokera artha kaila vivaraṇa
> pañcama ślokera artha śuna bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturtha]]---fourth; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:p:pañcama]]---fifth; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees.

===== Translation =====

**I have described the meaning of the fourth verse. Now, O devotees, kindly hear the explanation of the fifth verse.**
