====== CC Ādi 4.265 ======

===== Text =====

> rāga-mārge bhakta bhakti kare ye prakāre
> tāhā śikhāiba līlā-ācaraṇa-dvāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāga]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the path of spontaneous love; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---the devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:p:prakāre]]---in what way; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāiba]]---I shall teach; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---by means of practical demonstration.

===== Translation =====

**"I shall teach devotional service, which springs from the spontaneous love of the devotees, by demonstrating it Myself with My pastimes.**
