====== CC Ādi 4.127 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi yaiche paraspara viruddha-dharmāśraya
> rādhā-prema taiche sadā viruddha-dharma-maya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:p:paraspara]]---mutually; [[synonyms:v:viruddha]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---of conflicting characteristics; [[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the abode; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---the love of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---just so; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:v:viruddha]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---consists of conflicting characteristics.

===== Translation =====

**"Just as I am the abode of all mutually contradictory characteristics, so Rādhā's love is always full of similar contradictions.**
