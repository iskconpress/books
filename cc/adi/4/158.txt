====== Cc. Ādi 4.158 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera mādhurye kṛṣṇe upajaya lobha
> samyak āsvādite nāre, mane rahe kṣobha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mādhurye]]---in the sweetness; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---in Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:upajaya]]---arises; [[synonyms:l:lobha]]---eager desire; [[synonyms:s:samyak]]---fully; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādite]]---to taste; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---is not able; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:k:kṣobha]]---sorrow.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa's own beauty attracts Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself. But because He cannot fully enjoy it, His mind remains full of sorrow.**
