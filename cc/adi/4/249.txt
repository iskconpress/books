====== CC Ādi 4.249 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata anubhava āmāra pratīta
> vicāri' dekhiye yadi, saba viparīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:a:anubhava]]---affectionate feelings; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:p:pratīta]]---understood; [[synonyms:v:vicāri']]---by consideration; [[synonyms:d:dekhiye]]---I see; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everything; [[synonyms:v:viparīta]]---contrary.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way My affectionate feelings for Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī may be understood, but on analysis I find them contradictory.**
