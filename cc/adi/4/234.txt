====== CC Ādi 4.234 ======

===== Text =====

> e saba siddhānta haya āmrera pallava
> bhakta-gaṇa-kokilera sarvadā vallabha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]]---these; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---transcendental conclusions; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:ā:āmrera]]---of mango; [[synonyms:p:pallava]]---twigs; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kokilera]]---to those who are just like cuckoo birds; [[synonyms:s:sarvadā]]---always; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]---pleasing.

===== Translation =====

**All these conclusions are like the newly grown twigs of a mango tree; they are always pleasing to the devotees, who in this way resemble cuckoo birds.**
