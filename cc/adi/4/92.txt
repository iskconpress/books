====== CC Ādi 4.92 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-saundarya-kānti vaisaye yāṅhāte
> sarva-lakṣmī-gaṇera śobhā haya yāṅhā haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---of all beauty; [[synonyms:k:kānti]]---the splendor; [[synonyms:v:vaisaye]]---sits; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāte]]---in whom; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of all the goddesses of fortune; [[synonyms:ś:śobhā]]---the splendor; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from whom.

===== Translation =====

**The word "sarva-kānti" indicates that all beauty and luster rest in Her body. All the lakṣmīs derive their beauty from Her.**
