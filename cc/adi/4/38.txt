====== CC Ādi 4.38 ======

===== Text =====

> kona kāraṇe yabe haila avatāre mana
> yuga-dharma-kāla haila se kāle milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇe]]---by some reason; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---in incarnation; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---inclination; [[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---for the religion of the age; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---the time; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---conjunction.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord desired to appear for another reason, the time for promulgating the religion of the age also arose.**
