====== CC Ādi 4.78 ======

===== Text =====

> laksmī-gaṇa tāṅra vaibhava-vilāsāṁśa-rūpa
> mahiṣī-gaṇa vaibhava-prakāśa-svarūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the goddesses of fortune; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---Her; [[synonyms:v:vaibhava]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---as //vaibhava-vilāsa//; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---of plenary portions; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---having the form; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the queens; [[synonyms:v:vaibhava]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---of //vaibhava-prakāśa//; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---having the nature.

===== Translation =====

**The goddesses of fortune are Her plenary portions, and they display the forms of vaibhava-vilāsa. The queens are of the nature of Her vaibhava-prakāśa.**
