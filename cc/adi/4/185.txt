====== CC Ādi 4.185 ======

===== Text =====

> āra eka adbhuta gopī-bhāvera svabhāva
> buddhira gocara nahe yāhāra prabhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of the emotion of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---nature; [[synonyms:b:buddhira]]---of the intelligence; [[synonyms:g:gocara]]---an object of perception; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---the power.

===== Translation =====

**There is another wonderful feature of the emotion of the gopīs. Its power is beyond the comprehension of the intelligence.**
