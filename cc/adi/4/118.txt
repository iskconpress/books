====== CC Ādi 4.118 ======

===== Text =====

> harir eṣa na ced avātariṣyan
> mathurāyāṁ madhurākṣi rādhikā ca
> abhaviṣyad iyaṁ vṛthā visṛṣṭir
> makarāṅkas tu viśeṣatas tadātra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---this; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:c:cet]]---if; [[synonyms:a:avātariṣyat]]---would have descended; [[synonyms:m:mathurāyām]]---in Mathurā; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣi]]---O lovely-eyed one (Paurṇamāsī); [[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhikā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:a:abhaviṣyat]]---would have been; [[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vṛthā]]---useless; [[synonyms:v:visṛṣṭiḥ]]---the whole creation; [[synonyms:m:makara]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅkaḥ]]---the demigod of love, Cupid; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---then; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣataḥ]]---above all; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:a:atra]]---in this.

===== Translation =====

**"O Paurṇamāsī, if Lord Hari had not descended in Mathurā with Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, this entire creation-and especially Cupid, the demigod of love-would have been useless."**

===== Purport =====

This verse is spoken by Śrī Vṛndā-devī in the //Vidagdha-mādhava// (7.3) of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.
