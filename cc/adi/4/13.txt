====== CC Ādi 4.13 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva viṣṇu takhana kṛṣṇera śarīre
> viṣṇu-dvāre kare kṛṣṇa asura-saṁhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:t:takhana]]---at that time; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---in the body; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---by Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁhāre]]---killing the demons.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, therefore, Lord Viṣṇu is present in the body of Lord Kṛṣṇa, and Lord Kṛṣṇa kills the demons through Him.**
