====== Cc. Ādi 4.233 ======

===== Text =====

> hṛdaye dharaye ye caitanya-nityānanda
> e-saba siddhānte sei pāibe ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:d:dharaye]]---captures; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---and Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:s:siddhānte]]---by transcendental conclusions; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that man; [[synonyms:p:pāibe]]---will get; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---bliss.

===== Translation =====

**Anyone who has captured Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu in his heart will become blissful by hearing all these transcendental conclusions.**
