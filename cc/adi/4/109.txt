====== CC Ādi 4.109 ======

===== Text =====

> rātre pralāpa kare svarūpera kaṇṭha dhari'
> āveśe āpana bhāva kahaye ughāḍi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---delirium; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]]---of Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭha]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---embracing the neck; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]---His own; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---mood; [[synonyms:k:kahaye]]---speaks; [[synonyms:u:ughāḍi']]---exuberantly.

===== Translation =====

**At night He talked incoherently in grief with His arms around Svarūpa Dāmodara's neck. He spoke out His heart in ecstatic inspiration.**
