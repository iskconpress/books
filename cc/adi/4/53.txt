====== CC Ādi 4.53 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāva-grahaṇera hetu kaila dharma-sthāpana
> tāra mukhya hetu kahi, śuna sarva-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:g:grahaṇera]]---of accepting the mood; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---the reason; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religion; [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---establishing; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---principal; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---reason; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I say; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---everyone.

===== Translation =====

**To accept ecstatic love is the main reason He appeared and reestablished the religious system for this age. I shall now explain that reason. Everyone please listen.**
