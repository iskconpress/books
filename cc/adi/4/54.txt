====== CC Ādi 4.54 ======

===== Text =====

> mūla hetu āge ślokera kaila ābhāsa
> ebe kahi sei ślokera artha prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mūla]] [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---the root cause; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---gave; [[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---hint; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**Having first given hints about the verse describing the principal reason why the Lord appeared, now I shall manifest its full meaning.**
