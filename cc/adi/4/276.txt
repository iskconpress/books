====== Cc. Ādi 4.276 ======

===== Text =====

> maṅgalācaraṇaṁ kṛṣṇa-
> caitanya-tattva-lakṣaṇam
> prayojanaṁ cāvatāre
> śloka-ṣaṭkair nirūpitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇam]]---invoking auspiciousness; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇam]]---symptoms of the truth; [[synonyms:p:prayojanam]]---necessity; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---in the matter of His incarnation; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭkaiḥ]]---by six; [[synonyms:n:nirūpitam]]---ascertained.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the auspicious invocation, the essential nature of the truth of Lord Caitanya, and the need for His appearance have been set forth in six verses.**
