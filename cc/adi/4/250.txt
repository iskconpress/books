====== Cc. Ādi 4.250 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhāra darśane mora juḍāya nayana
> āmāra darśane rādhā sukhe ageyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in meeting; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:j:juḍāya]]---are satisfied; [[synonyms:n:nayana]]---eyes; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---of Me; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in meeting; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:a:ageyāna]]---more advanced.

===== Translation =====

**"My eyes are fully satisfied when I look upon Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, but by looking upon Me, She becomes even more advanced in satisfaction.**
