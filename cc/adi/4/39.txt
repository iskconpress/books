====== Cc. Ādi 4.39 ======

===== Text =====

> dui hetu avatari' lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> āpane āsvāde prema-nāma-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---reasons; [[synonyms:a:avatari']]---incarnating; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---Himself; [[synonyms:ā:āsvāde]]---tastes; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of God; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---and congregational chanting of the holy name.

===== Translation =====

**Thus with two intentions the Lord appeared with His devotees and tasted the nectar of prema with the congregational chanting of the holy name.**
