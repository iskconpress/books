====== Cc. Ādi 4.202 ======

===== Text =====

> aṅga-stambhārambham uttuṅgayantaṁ
> premānandaṁ dāruko nābhyanandat
> kaṁsārāter vījane yena sākṣād
> akṣodīyān antarāyo vyadhāyi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---of the limbs; [[synonyms:s:stambha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārambham]]---the beginning of stupefaction; [[synonyms:u:uttuṅgayantam]]---which was causing him to reach; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānandam]]---the joy of love; [[synonyms:d:dārukaḥ]]---Dāruka, the Lord's chariot driver; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:abhyanandat]]---welcomed; [[synonyms:k:kaṁsa]]-[[synonyms:a:arāteḥ]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa, the enemy of Kaṁsa; [[synonyms:v:vījane]]---in fanning with a //cāmara// fan; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---clearly; [[synonyms:a:akṣodīyān]]---greater; [[synonyms:a:antarāyaḥ]]---obstacle; [[synonyms:v:vyadhāyi]]---has been created.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Dāruka did not relish his ecstatic feelings of love, for they caused his limbs to become stunned and thus obstructed his service of fanning Lord Kṛṣṇa."**

===== Purport =====

This verse is from //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (3.2.62).
