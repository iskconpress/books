====== Cc. Ādi 4.153 ======

===== Text =====

> gopyaś ca kṛṣṇam upalabhya cirād abhīṣṭaṁ
> yat-prekṣaṇe dṛśiṣu pakṣma-kṛtaṁ śapanti
> dṛgbhir hṛdi kṛtam alaṁ parirabhya sarvās
> tad-bhāvam āpur api nitya-yujāṁ durāpam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopyaḥ]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇam]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:upalabhya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:c:cirāt]]---after a long time; [[synonyms:a:abhīṣṭam]]---desired object; [[synonyms:y:yat]]-[[synonyms:p:prekṣaṇe]]---in the seeing of whom; [[synonyms:d:dṛśiṣu]]---in the eyes; [[synonyms:p:pakṣma]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---the maker of eyelashes; [[synonyms:ś:śapanti]]---curse; [[synonyms:d:dṛgbhiḥ]]---with the eyes; [[synonyms:h:hṛdi]] [[synonyms:k:kṛtam]]---who entered the heart; [[synonyms:a:alam]]---enough; [[synonyms:p:parirabhya]]---embracing; [[synonyms:s:sarvāḥ]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvam]]---that highest stage of joy; [[synonyms:ā:āpuḥ]]---obtained; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:y:yujām]]---by perfected //yogīs//; [[synonyms:d:durāpam]]---difficult to obtain.

===== Translation =====

**"The gopīs saw their beloved Kṛṣṇa at Kurukṣetra after a long separation. They secured and embraced Him in their hearts through their eyes, and they attained a joy so intense that not even perfect yogīs can attain it. The gopīs cursed the creator for creating eyelids that interfered with their vision."**

===== Purport =====

This text is from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.82.39).
