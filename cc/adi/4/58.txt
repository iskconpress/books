====== Cc. Ādi 4.58 ======

===== Text =====

> ithi lāgi' āge kari tāra vivaraṇa
> yāhā haite haya gaurera mahimā-kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ithi]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for this; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---first; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I shall do; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from which; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:g:gaurera]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glory; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---relating.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore I shall first delineate the position of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa. From that the glory of Lord Caitanya will be known.**
