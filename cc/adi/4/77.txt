====== CC Ādi 4.77 ======

===== Text =====

> vaibhava-gaṇa yena tāṅra aṅga-vibhūti
> bimba-pratibimba-rūpa mahiṣīra tati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaibhava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the expansions; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as it were; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Her; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---of the body; [[synonyms:v:vibhūti]]---powerful expansions; [[synonyms:b:bimba]]---reflections; [[synonyms:p:pratibimba]]---counterreflections; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---having the form; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣīra]]---of the queens; [[synonyms:t:tati]]---the expansion.

===== Translation =====

**The goddesses of fortune are partial manifestations of Śrīmatī Rādhikā, and the queens are reflections of Her image.**
