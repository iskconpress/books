====== CC Ādi 4.43 ======

===== Text =====

> nija nija bhāva sabe śreṣṭha kari' māne
> nija-bhāve kare kṛṣṇa-sukha āsvādane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]---each his own; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---mood; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making the best; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---accepts; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in his own mood; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness with Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādane]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**Each kind of devotee feels that his sentiment is the most excellent, and thus in that mood he tastes great happiness with Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
