====== CC Ādi 4.115 ======

===== Text =====

> kaiśora-vayase kāma, jagat-sakala
> rāsādi-līlāya tina karila saphala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaiśora]]-[[synonyms:v:vayase]]---in the adolescent age; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---amorous love; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---the entire universe; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---such as the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---by pastimes; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---successful.

===== Translation =====

**In His youth Lord Kṛṣṇa made all three of His ages, and the entire universe, successful by His pastimes of amorous love like the rāsa dance.**
