====== Cc. Ādi 4.194 ======

===== Text =====

> kintu kṛṣṇera sukha haya gopī-rūpa-guṇe
> tāṅra sukhe sukha-vṛddhi haye gopī-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---the happiness; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---in the qualities and beauty of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in the happiness; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛddhi]]---increase of happiness; [[synonyms:h:haye]]---there is; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---in the //gopīs.//

===== Translation =====

**Kṛṣṇa, however, derives pleasure from the beauty and good qualities of the gopīs. And when the gopīs see His pleasure, the joy of the gopīs increases.**
