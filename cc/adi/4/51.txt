====== CC Ādi 4.51 ======

===== Text =====

> sureśānāṁ durgaṁ gatir atiśayenopaniṣadāṁ
> munīnāṁ sarva-svaṁ praṇata-paṭalīnāṁ madhurimā
> viniryāsaḥ premṇo nikhila-paśu-pālāmbuja-dṛśāṁ
> sa caitanyaḥ kiṁ me punar api dṛśor yāsyati padam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sura]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśānām]]---of the kings of the demigods; [[synonyms:d:durgam]]---fortress; [[synonyms:g:gatiḥ]]---the goal; [[synonyms:a:atiśayena]]---eminently; [[synonyms:u:upaniṣadām]]---of the //Upaniṣads//; [[synonyms:m:munīnām]]---of the sages; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:s:svam]]---the be-all and end-all; [[synonyms:p:praṇata]]-[[synonyms:p:paṭalīnām]]---of the groups of the devotees; [[synonyms:m:madhurimā]]---the sweetness; [[synonyms:v:viniryāsaḥ]]---the essence; [[synonyms:p:premṇaḥ]]---of love; [[synonyms:n:nikhila]]---all; [[synonyms:p:paśu]]-[[synonyms:p:pālā]]---of the cowherd women; [[synonyms:a:ambuja]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛśām]]---lotus-eyed; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:c:caitanyaḥ]]---Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:k:kim]]---what; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:a:api]]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:dṛśoḥ]]---of the two eyes; [[synonyms:y:yāsyati]]---will come; [[synonyms:p:padam]]---to the abode.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Caitanya is the shelter of the demigods, the goal of the Upaniṣads, the be-all and end-all of the great sages, the beautiful shelter of His devotees, and the essence of the love of the lotus-eyed gopīs. Will He again be the object of my vision?"**
