====== Cc. Ādi 4.8 ======

===== Text =====

> svayaṁ-bhagavānera karma nahe bhāra-haraṇa
> sthiti-kartā viṣṇu karena jagat-pālana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavānera]]---of the original Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---the business; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:b:bhāra]]-[[synonyms:h:haraṇa]]---taking away the burden; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]-[[synonyms:k:kartā]]---the maintainer; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:p:pālana]]---protection of the universe.

===== Translation =====

**To take away this burden, however, is not the work of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. The maintainer, Lord Viṣṇu, is the one who protects the universe.**
