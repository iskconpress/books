====== CC Ādi 4.17 ======

===== Text =====

> aiśvarya-jñānete saba jagat miśrita
> aiśvarya-śithila-preme nahi mora prīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]] [[synonyms:j:jñānete]]---with knowledge of majesty; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the universe; [[synonyms:m:miśrita]]---mixed; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śithila]]---weakened by majesty; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:p:prīta]]---pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**[Lord Kṛṣṇa thought:] "All the universe is filled with the conception of My majesty, but love weakened by that sense of majesty does not satisfy Me.**
