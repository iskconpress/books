====== CC Ādi 4.146 ======

===== Text =====

> aparikalita-pūrvaḥ kaś camatkāra-kārī
> sphurati mama garīyān eṣa mādhurya-pūraḥ
> ayam aham api hanta prekṣya yaṁ lubdha-cetāḥ
> sarabhasam upabhoktuṁ kāmaye rādhikeva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparikalita]]---not experienced; [[synonyms:p:pūrvaḥ]]---previously; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]-[[synonyms:k:kārī]]---causing wonder; [[synonyms:s:sphurati]]---manifests; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---My; [[synonyms:g:garīyān]]---more great; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]-[[synonyms:p:pūraḥ]]---abundance of sweetness; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:h:hanta]]---alas; [[synonyms:p:prekṣya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:y:yam]]---which; [[synonyms:l:lubdha]]-[[synonyms:c:cetāḥ]]---My mind being bewildered; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:r:rabhasam]]---impetuously; [[synonyms:u:upabhoktum]]---to enjoy; [[synonyms:k:kāmaye]]---desire; [[synonyms:r:rādhikā]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**"Who manifests an abundance of sweetness greater than Mine, which has never been experienced before and which causes wonder to all? Alas, I Myself, My mind bewildered upon seeing this beauty, impetuously desire to enjoy it like Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**

===== Purport =====

This text is from the //Lalita-mādhava// (8.34) of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī. It was spoken by Lord Kṛṣṇa when He saw the beauty of His own reflection in a jeweled fountain in Dvārakā.
