====== Cc. Ādi 4.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaita-candra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glory; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glory; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glory; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glory; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---to the devotees of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glory to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu. All glory to Lord Nityānanda. All glory to Śrī Advaita Ācārya. And all glory to all the devotees of Lord Caitanya.**
