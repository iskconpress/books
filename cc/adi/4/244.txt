====== CC Ādi 4.244 ======

===== Text =====

> mora vaṁśī-gīte ākarṣaye tri-bhuvana
> rādhāra vacane hare āmāra śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]-[[synonyms:g:gīte]]---by the vibration of the flute; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaye]]---I attract; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---the three worlds; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]] [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---the words of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---conquers; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hearing power.

===== Translation =====

**"The vibration of My transcendental flute attracts the three worlds, but My ears are enchanted by the sweet words of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
