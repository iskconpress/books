====== CC Ādi 4.76 ======

===== Text =====

> avatārī kṛṣṇa yaiche kare avatāra
> aṁśinī rādhā haite tina gaṇera vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avatārī]]---the source of all incarnations; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:a:aṁśinī]]---the source of all portions; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the groups; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expansion.

===== Translation =====

**Just as the fountainhead, Lord Kṛṣṇa, is the cause of all incarnations, so Śrī Rādhā is the cause of all these consorts.**
