====== Cc. Ādi 4.147 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-mādhuryera eka svābhāvika bala
> kṛṣṇa-ādi nara-nārī karaye cañcala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mādhuryera]]---of the sweetness; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:svābhāvika]]---natural; [[synonyms:b:bala]]---strength; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with; [[synonyms:n:nara]]-[[synonyms:n:nārī]]---men and women; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---makes; [[synonyms:c:cañcala]]---perturbed.

===== Translation =====

**The beauty of Kṛṣṇa has one natural strength: it thrills the hearts of all men and women, beginning with Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself.**
