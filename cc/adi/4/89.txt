====== Cc. Ādi 4.89 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva sarva-pūjyā, parama-devatā
> sarva-pālikā, sarva jagatera mātā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:p:pūjyā]]---worshipable by all; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---supreme; [[synonyms:d:devatā]]---goddess; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:p:pālikā]]---the protectress of all; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of all the universes; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore Rādhā is parama-devatā, the supreme goddess, and She is worshipable for everyone. She is the protectress of all, and She is the mother of the entire universe.**
