====== Cc. Ādi 4.63 ======

===== Text =====

> hlādinī sandhinī samvit
> tvayy ekā sarva-saṁsthitau
> hlāda-tāpa-karī miśrā
> tvayi no guṇa-varjite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hlādinī]]---pleasure potency; [[synonyms:s:sandhinī]]---existence potency; [[synonyms:s:samvit]]---knowledge potency; [[synonyms:t:tvayi]]---in You; [[synonyms:e:ekā]]---one; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁsthitau]]---who are the basis of all things; [[synonyms:h:hlāda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:t:tāpa]]---and misery; [[synonyms:k:karī]]---causing; [[synonyms:m:miśrā]]---a mixture of the two; [[synonyms:t:tvayi]]---in You; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:u:u]]---not; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:varjite]]---who are without the three modes of material nature.

===== Translation =====

**"O Lord, You are the support of everything. The three attributes hlādinī, sandhinī and samvit exist in You as one spiritual energy. But the material modes, which cause happiness, misery and mixtures of the two, do not exist in You, for You have no material qualities."**

===== Purport =====

This text is from the //Viṣṇu Purāṇa// (1.12.69).
