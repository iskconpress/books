====== Cc. Ādi 4.103 ======

===== Text =====

> avatārera āra eka āche mukhya-bīja
> rasika-śekhara kṛṣṇera sei kārya nija

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of the incarnation; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:b:bīja]]---principal seed; [[synonyms:r:rasika]]-[[synonyms:ś:śekhara]]---the foremost enjoyer of the mellows of love; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---business; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---own.

===== Translation =====

**There is a principal cause for Lord Kṛṣṇa's appearance. It grows from His own engagements as the foremost enjoyer of loving exchanges.**
