====== CC Ādi 4.167-169 ======

===== Text =====

> loka-dharma, veda-dharma, deha-dharma, karma
> lajjā, dhairya, deha-sukha, ātma-sukha-marma
>
> dustyaja ārya-patha, nija parijana
> sva-jane karaye yata tāḍana-bhartsana
>
> sarva-tyāga kari' kare kṛṣṇera bhajana
> kṛṣṇa-sukha-hetu kare prema-sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---customs of the people; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---Vedic injunctions; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---necessities of the body; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---fruitive work; [[synonyms:l:lajjā]]---bashfulness; [[synonyms:d:dhairya]]---patience; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---the happiness of the body; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---the happiness of the self; [[synonyms:m:marma]]---the essence; [[synonyms:d:dustyaja]]---difficult to give up; [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]-[[synonyms:p:patha]]---the path of //varṇāśrama//; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---own; [[synonyms:p:parijana]]---family members; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---one's own family; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---do; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tāḍana]]---punishment; [[synonyms:b:bhartsana]]---scolding; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāga]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---giving up everything; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---worship; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for the purpose of Lord Kṛṣṇa's happiness; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---out of love; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service.

===== Translation =====

**Social customs, scriptural injunctions, bodily demands, fruitive action, shyness, patience, bodily pleasures, self-gratification and the path of varṇāśrama-dharma, which is difficult to give up---the gopīs have forsaken all these, as well as their own relatives and their punishment and scolding, for the sake of serving Lord Kṛṣṇa. They render loving service to Him for the sake of His enjoyment.**
