====== Cc. Ādi 4.252 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-āliṅgana pāinu, janama saphale
> kṛṣṇa-sukhe magna rahe vṛkṣa kari' kole

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---the embrace; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I have gotten; [[synonyms:j:janama]] [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:phale]]---My birth is now fulfilled; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in the matter of pleasing Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:magna]]---immersed; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---She remains; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]---the tree; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kole]]---on the lap.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'I have gotten the embrace of Śrī Kṛṣṇa,' She thinks, 'so now My life is fulfilled.' Thus She remains immersed in pleasing Kṛṣṇa, taking the tree in Her arms.**
