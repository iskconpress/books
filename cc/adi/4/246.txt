====== CC Ādi 4.246 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi āmāra rase jagat sarasa
> rādhāra adhara-rasa āmā kare vaśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---of Me; [[synonyms:r:rase]]---by the taste; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---is palatable; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:a:adhara]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the taste of the lips; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---Me; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---submissive.

===== Translation =====

**"Although the entire creation is full of different tastes because of Me, I am charmed by the nectarean taste of the lips of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
