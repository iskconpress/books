====== CC Ādi 4.248 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata jagatera sukhe āmi hetu
> rādhikāra rūpa-guṇa āmāra jīvātu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the whole world; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in the matter of happiness; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I am; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---the cause; [[synonyms:r:rādhikāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---beauty and attributes; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:j:jīvātu]]---life and soul.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus although I am the source for the happiness of the entire world, the beauty and attributes of Śrī Rādhikā are My life and soul.**
