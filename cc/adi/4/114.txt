====== CC Ādi 4.114 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhikādi lañā kaila rāsādi-vilāsa
> vāñchā bhari' āsvādila rasera niryāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī and the other //gopīs//; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking along; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---fulfilling desires; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādila]]---He tasted; [[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of mellow; [[synonyms:n:niryāsa]]---the essence.

===== Translation =====

**In youth He tasted the essence of rasa, fulfilling His desires in pastimes like the rāsa dance with Śrīmatī Rādhikā and the other gopīs.**
