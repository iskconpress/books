====== CC Ādi 4.225 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya gosāñi rasera sadana
> aśeṣa-viśeṣe kaila rasa āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of mellow; [[synonyms:s:sadana]]---the residence; [[synonyms:a:aśeṣa]]-[[synonyms:v:viśeṣe]]---unlimited varieties of enjoyment; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---mellow; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya is the abode of rasa. He Himself tasted the sweetness of rasa in endless ways.**
