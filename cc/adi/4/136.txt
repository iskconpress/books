====== CC Ādi 4.136 ======

===== Text =====

> eta cinti' rahe kṛṣṇa parama-kautukī
> hṛdaye bāḍaye prema-lobha dhakdhaki

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:c:cinti']]---thinking this; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:k:kautukī]]---the supremely curious; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:b:bāḍaye]]---increases; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:l:lobha]]---eager desire for love; [[synonyms:d:dhakdhaki]]---blazing.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking in this way, Lord Kṛṣṇa was curious to taste that love. His eager desire for that love increasingly blazed in His heart.**
