====== CC Ādi 4.148 ======

===== Text =====

> śravaṇe, darśane ākarṣaye sarva-mana
> āpanā āsvādite kṛṣṇa karena yatana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---in hearing; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in seeing; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaye]]---attracts; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---all minds; [[synonyms:ā:āpanā]]---Himself; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādite]]---to taste; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---makes; [[synonyms:y:yatana]]---efforts.

===== Translation =====

**All minds are attracted by hearing His sweet voice and flute, or by seeing His beauty. Even Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself makes efforts to taste that sweetness.**
