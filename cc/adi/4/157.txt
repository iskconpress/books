====== Cc. Ādi 4.157 ======

===== Text =====

> apūrva mādhurī kṛṣṇera, apūrva tāra bala
> yāhāra śravaṇe mana haya ṭalamala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:apūrva]]---unprecedented; [[synonyms:m:mādhurī]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]]---unprecedented; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:b:bala]]---the strength; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---in hearing; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭalamala]]---unsteady.

===== Translation =====

**The sweetness of Lord Kṛṣṇa is unprecedented, and its strength is also unprecedented. Simply by one's hearing of such beauty, the mind becomes unsteady.**
