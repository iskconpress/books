====== CC Ādi 4.133 ======

===== Text =====

> viṣaya-jātīya sukha āmāra āsvāda
> āmā haite koṭi-guṇa āśrayera āhlāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṣaya]] [[synonyms:j:jātīya]]---relating to the object; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]]---tasting; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than Me; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---ten million times more; [[synonyms:ā:āśrayera]]---of the abode; [[synonyms:ā:āhlāda]]---pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**"I taste the bliss to which the object of love is entitled. But the pleasure of Rādhā, the abode of that love, is ten million times greater.**
