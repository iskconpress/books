====== Cc. Ādi 4.237 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva bhakta-gaṇe kari namaskāra
> nihśaṅke kahiye, tāra hauk camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I offer; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:n:niḥśaṅke]]---without any doubt; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I say; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:h:hauk]]---let there be; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore after offering obeisances to the devotees, for their satisfaction I shall speak without hesitating.**
