====== CC Ādi 4.36-37 ======

===== Text =====

> ei vāñchā yaiche kṛṣṇa-prākaṭya-kāraṇa
> asura-saṁhāra---ānuṣaṅga prayojana
>
> ei mata caitanya-kṛṣṇa pūrṇa bhagavān
> yuga-dharma-pravartana nahe tāṅra kāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prākaṭya]]---for the manifestation; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---reason; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁhāra]]---the killing of demons; [[synonyms:ā:ānuṣaṅga]]---secondary; [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---reason; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---like this; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---as Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---full; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religion of the age; [[synonyms:p:pravartana]]---initiating; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---the desire.

===== Translation =====

**Just as these desires are the fundamental reason for Kṛṣṇa's appearance whereas destroying the demons is only an incidental necessity, so for Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, promulgating the dharma of the age is incidental.**
