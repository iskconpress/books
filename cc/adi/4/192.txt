====== CC Ādi 4.192 ======

===== Text =====

> gopī-śobhā dekhi' kṛṣṇera śobhā bāḍhe yata
> kṛṣṇa-śobhā dekhi' gopīra śobhā bāḍhe tata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śobhā]]---the beauty of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śobhā]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:b:bāḍhe]]---increases; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as much as; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śobhā]]---the beauty of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:g:gopīra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:ś:śobhā]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:b:bāḍhe]]---increases; [[synonyms:t:tata]]---that much.

===== Translation =====

**The beauty of Lord Kṛṣṇa increases at the sight of the beauty of the gopīs. And the more the gopīs see Lord Kṛṣṇa's beauty, the more their beauty increases.**
