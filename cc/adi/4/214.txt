====== CC Ādi 4.214 ======

===== Text =====

> sei gopī-gaṇa-madhye uttamā rādhikā
> rūpe, guṇe, saubhāgye, preme sarvādhikā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among; [[synonyms:u:uttamā]]---the highest; [[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in beauty; [[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---in qualities; [[synonyms:s:saubhāgye]]---in good fortune; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:adhikā]]---above all.

===== Translation =====

**Among the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhikā is the foremost. She surpasses all in beauty, in good qualities, in good fortune and, above all, in love.**

===== Purport =====

Among all the //gopīs,// Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is the most exalted. She is the most beautiful, the most qualified, and above all the greatest lover of Kṛṣṇa.
