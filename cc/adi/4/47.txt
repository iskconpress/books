====== Cc. Ādi 4.47 ======

===== Text =====

> parakīyā-bhāve ati rasera ullāsa
> vraja vinā ihāra anyatra nāhi vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parakīyā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in the mood of //parakīyā,// or conjugal relations outside of marriage; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very great; [[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of mellow; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---increase; [[synonyms:v:vraja]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except for Vraja; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:a:anyatra]]---anywhere else; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence.

===== Translation =====

**There is a great increase of mellow in the unwedded conjugal mood. Such love is found nowhere but in Vraja.**
