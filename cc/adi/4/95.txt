====== CC Ādi 4.95 ======

===== Text =====

> jagat-mohana kṛṣṇa, tāṅhāra mohinī
> ataeva samastera parā ṭhākurāṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:m:mohana]]---enchanting the universe; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:m:mohinī]]---the enchantress; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:samastera]]---of all; [[synonyms:p:parā]]---foremost; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---goddess.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa enchants the world, but Śrī Rādhā enchants even Him. Therefore She is the supreme goddess of all.**
