====== CC Ādi 4.203 ======

===== Text =====

> govinda-prekṣaṇākṣepi-
> bāṣpa-pūrābhivarṣiṇam
> uccair anindad ānandam
> aravinda-vilocanā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinda]]---of Lord Govinda; [[synonyms:p:prekṣaṇa]]---the seeing; [[synonyms:ā:ākṣepi]]---hindering; [[synonyms:b:bāṣpa]]-[[synonyms:p:pūra]]---groups of tears; [[synonyms:a:abhivarṣiṇam]]---which cause to rain; [[synonyms:u:uccaiḥ]]---powerfully; [[synonyms:a:anindat]]---condemned; [[synonyms:ā:ānandam]]---the bliss; [[synonyms:a:aravinda]]-[[synonyms:v:vilocanā]]---the lotus-eyed Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**"The lotus-eyed Rādhārāṇī powerfully condemned the ecstatic love that caused a flow of tears that hindered Her sight of Govinda."**

===== Purport =====

This verse is also from //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (2.3.54).
