====== Cc. Ādi 4.212 ======

===== Text =====

> gopikā jānena kṛṣṇera manera vāñchita
> prema-sevā-paripāṭī, iṣṭa-samīhita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopikā]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---know; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:manera]]---of the mind; [[synonyms:v:vāñchita]]---the desired object; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---of service in love; [[synonyms:p:paripāṭī]]---perfection; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:s:samīhita]]---achievement of the desired goal of life.

===== Translation =====

**The gopīs know Kṛṣṇa's desires, and they know how to render perfect loving service for His enjoyment. They perform their service expertly for the satisfaction of their beloved.**
