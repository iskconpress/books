====== CC Ādi 4.150 ======

===== Text =====

> atṛpta ha-iyā kare vidhira nindana
> avidagdha vidhi bhāla nā jāne sṛjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atṛpta]]---unsatisfied; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---being; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:v:vidhira]]---of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:n:nindana]]---blaspheming; [[synonyms:a:avidagdha]]---inexperienced; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---well; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know; [[synonyms:s:sṛjana]]---creating.

===== Translation =====

**Such a person, being unsatisfied, begins to blaspheme Lord Brahmā, saying that he does not know the art of creating well and is simply inexperienced.**
