====== CC Ādi 4.215 ======

===== Text =====

> yathā rādhā priyā viṣṇos
> tasyāḥ kuṇḍaṁ priyaṁ tathā
> sarva-gopīṣu saivaikā
> viṣṇor atyanta-vallabhā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:p:priyā]]---very dear; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]]---Her; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍam]]---bathing place; [[synonyms:p:priyam]]---very dear; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---so also; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:g:gopīṣu]]---among all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---She; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:e:ekā]]---alone; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]-[[synonyms:v:vallabhā]]---most dear.

===== Translation =====

**"Just as Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is most dear to Śrī Kṛṣṇa, Her bathing place [Rādhā-kuṇḍa] is  also dear to Him. Among all the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is supermost and very dear to  Lord Kṛṣṇa."**

===== Purport =====

This verse is from the //Padma Purāṇa.//
