====== CC Ādi 4.110 ======

===== Text =====

> yabe yei bhāva uṭhe prabhura antara
> sei gīti-śloke sukha dena dāmodara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---mood; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---arises; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:g:gīti]]---by the song; [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---or verse; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara.

===== Translation =====

**Whenever a particular sentiment arose in His heart, Svarūpa Dāmodara satisfied Him by singing songs or reciting verses of the same nature.**
