====== CC Ādi 4.7 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yena pṛthivīra bhāra haribāre
> kṛṣṇa avatīrṇa hailā śāstrete pracāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīra]]---of the earth; [[synonyms:b:bhāra]]---burden; [[synonyms:h:haribāre]]---to take away; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]]---incarnated; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---was; [[synonyms:ś:śāstrete]]---the scriptures; [[synonyms:p:pracāre]]---proclaim.

===== Translation =====

**The scriptures proclaim that Lord Kṛṣṇa previously descended to take away the burden of the earth.**
