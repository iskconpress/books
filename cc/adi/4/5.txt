====== CC Ādi 4.5 ======

===== Text =====

> caturtha ślokera artha ei kaila sāra
> prema-nāma pracārite ei avatāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturtha]]---fourth; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---gave; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---essence; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name; [[synonyms:p:pracārite]]---to propagate; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation.

===== Translation =====

**I have given the essential meaning of the fourth verse: this incarnation descends to propagate the chanting of the holy name and spread love of God.**
