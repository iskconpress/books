====== CC Ādi 4.160 ======

===== Text =====

> atyanta-nigūḍha ei rasera siddhānta
> svarūpa-gosāñi mātra jānena ekānta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---extremely; [[synonyms:n:nigūḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of mellow; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---conclusion; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---knows; [[synonyms:e:ekānta]]---much.

===== Translation =====

**This conclusion of rasa is extremely deep. Only Svarūpa Dāmodara knows much about it.**
