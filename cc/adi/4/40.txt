====== CC Ādi 4.40 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dvāre ācaṇḍāle kīrtana sañcāre
> nāma-prema-mālā gāṅthi' parāila saṁsāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---by that; [[synonyms:ā:ā]]-[[synonyms:c:caṇḍāle]]---even among the //caṇḍālas//; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---the chanting of the holy names; [[synonyms:s:sañcāre]]---He infuses; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the holy names; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---and of love of God; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---a garland; [[synonyms:g:gāṅthi']]---stringing together; [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---He put it on; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāre]]---the whole material world.

===== Translation =====

**Thus He spread kīrtana even among the untouchables. He wove a wreath of the holy name and prema with which He garlanded the entire material world.**
