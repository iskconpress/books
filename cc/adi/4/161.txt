====== CC Ādi 4.161 ======

===== Text =====

> yebā keha anya jāne, seho tāṅhā haite
> caitanya-gosāñira teṅha atyanta marma yāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yebā]]---whoever; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:s:seho]]---he; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāhaite]]---from him (Svarūpa Dāmodara); [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---extremely; [[synonyms:m:marma]]---secret core; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---since.

===== Translation =====

**Anyone else who claims to know it must have heard it from him, for he was the most intimate companion of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
