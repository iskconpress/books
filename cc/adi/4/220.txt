====== Cc. Ādi 4.220 ======

===== Text =====

> sei rādhāra bhāva lañā caitanyāvatāra
> yuga-dharma nāma-prema kaila paracāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the emotion; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnation; [[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religion of the age; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---the holy name and love of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:paracāra]]---preaching.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya appeared with the sentiment of Rādhā. He preached the dharma of this age---the chanting of the holy name and pure love of God.**
