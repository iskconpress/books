====== CC Ādi 4.241 ======

===== Text =====

> āmā haite guṇī baḍa jagate asambhava
> ekali rādhāte tāhā kari anubhava

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than Me; [[synonyms:g:guṇī]]---qualified; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---greater; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in the world; [[synonyms:a:asambhava]]---there is no possibility; [[synonyms:e:ekali]]---only; [[synonyms:r:rādhāte]]---in Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:a:anubhava]]---I can understand.

===== Translation =====

**"One more qualified than Me is impossible to find in the world. But in Rādhā alone I feel the presence of one who can give Me pleasure.**
