====== Cc. Ādi 4.232 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva kahi kichu kariñā nigūḍha
> bujhibe rasika bhakta, nā bujhibe mūḍha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---squeezing; [[synonyms:n:nigūḍha]]---essence; [[synonyms:b:bujhibe]]---can understand; [[synonyms:r:rasika]]---humorous; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:b:bujhibe]]---will understand; [[synonyms:m:mūḍha]]---rascals.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore I shall mention them, revealing only their essence, so that loving devotees will understand them but fools will not.**
