====== CC Ādi 4.251 ======

===== Text =====

> paraspara veṇu-gīte haraye cetana
> mora bhrame tamālere kare āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paraspara]]---against each other; [[synonyms:v:veṇu]]-[[synonyms:g:gīte]]---the singing of the bamboo; [[synonyms:h:haraye]]---attracts; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---of Me; [[synonyms:b:bhrame]]---in mistake; [[synonyms:t:tamālere]]---a black tree known as //tamāla//; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---She does; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**"The flutelike murmur of the bamboos rubbing against one another steals Rādhārāṇī's consciousness, for She thinks it to be the sound of My flute. And She embraces a tamāla tree, mistaking it for Me.**
