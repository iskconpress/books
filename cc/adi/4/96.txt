====== CC Ādi 4.96 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā---pūrṇa-śakti, kṛṣṇa---pūrṇa-śaktimān
> dui vastu bheda nāi, śāstra-paramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---the complete energy; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaktimān]]---the complete possessor of energy; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---things; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---difference; [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:p:paramāṇa]]---the evidence of revealed scripture.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Rādhā is the full power, and Lord Kṛṣṇa is the possessor of full power. The two are not different, as evidenced by the revealed scriptures.**
