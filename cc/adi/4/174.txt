====== CC Ādi 4.174 ======

===== Text =====

> ātma-sukha-duḥkhe gopīra nāhika vicāra
> kṛṣṇa-sukha-hetu ceṣṭā mano-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in personal happiness or distress; [[synonyms:g:gopīra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---not; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for the purpose of Lord Kṛṣṇa's happiness; [[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---activity; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]---of the mind; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---the business.

===== Translation =====

**The gopīs do not care for their own pleasures or pains. All their physical and mental activities are directed toward offering enjoyment to Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
