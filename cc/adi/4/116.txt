====== Cc. Ādi 4.116 ======

===== Text =====

> so 'pi kaiśoraka-vayo
> mānayan madhusūdanaḥ
> reme strī-ratna-kūṭa-sthaḥ
> kṣapāsu kṣapitāhitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:a:api]]---especially; [[synonyms:k:kaiśoraka]]-[[synonyms:v:vayaḥ]]---the age of adolescence; [[synonyms:m:mānayan]]---honoring; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sūdanaḥ]]---the killer of the Madhu demon; [[synonyms:r:reme]]---enjoyed; [[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:r:ratna]]---of the gopīs; [[synonyms:k:kūṭa]]---in multitudes; [[synonyms:s:sthaḥ]]---situated; [[synonyms:k:kṣapāsu]]---in the autumn nights; [[synonyms:k:kṣapita]]-[[synonyms:a:ahitaḥ]]---who destroys misfortune.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Madhusūdana enjoyed His youth with pastimes on autumn nights in the midst of the jewellike milkmaids. Thus He dispelled all the misfortunes of the world."**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from the //Viṣṇu Purāṇa// (5.13.60).
