====== CC Ādi 4.199 ======

===== Text =====

> prīti-viṣayānande tad-āśrayānanda
> tāṅhā nāhi nija-sukha-vāñchāra sambandha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prīti]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in the joy of the object of love; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of that love; [[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---the joy of the abode; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāñchāra]]---of desire for one's own happiness; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]---relationship.

===== Translation =====

**The happiness of the abode of love is in the happiness of the object of that love. This is not a relationship of desire for personal gratification.**
