====== CC Ādi 12.11 ======

===== Text =====

> asārera nāme ihāṅ nāhi prayojana
> bheda jānibāre kari ekatra gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:asārera]]---of the useless persons; [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in their name; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---in this connection; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is no; [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---use; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---differences; [[synonyms:j:jānibāre]]---to know; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---in one list; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting.

===== Translation =====

**There is no need to name those who are useless. I have mentioned them only to distinguish them from the useful devotees.**
