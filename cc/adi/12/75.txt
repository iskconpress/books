====== CC Ādi 12.75 ======

===== Text =====

> acyutera yei mata, sei mata sāra
> āra yata mata saba haila chārakhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:acyutera]]---of Acyutānanda; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---which; [[synonyms:m:mata]]---direction; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mata]]---direction; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---essential; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mata]]---directions; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:c:chārakhāra]]---dismantled.

===== Translation =====

**It should be concluded, therefore, that the path of Acyutānanda is the essence of spiritual life. Those who did not follow this path simply scattered.**
