====== CC Ādi 12.91 ======

===== Text =====

> ei tina skandhera kailuṅ śākhāra gaṇana
> yāṅ-sabā-smaraṇe bhava-bandha-vimocana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]---of all these three; [[synonyms:s:skandhera]]---trunks; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---described; [[synonyms:ś:śākhāra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---enumeration of the branches; [[synonyms:y:yāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇe]]---by remembering; [[synonyms:b:bhava]]-[[synonyms:b:bandha]]---from entanglement in the material world; [[synonyms:v:vimocana]]---freedom.

===== Translation =====

**Simply by remembering the names of all these branches and subbranches of the three trunks I have described [Nityānanda, Advaita and Gadādhara], one attains freedom from the entanglement of material existence.**
