====== CC Ādi 12.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya mahāprabhu śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya
> jaya jaya nityānanda jayādvaita dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of the name Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jayaadvaita]]---all glories to Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---who are all very glorious.

===== Translation =====

** All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Lord Nityānanda! All glories to Śrī Advaita Prabhu! All of Them are glorious.**
