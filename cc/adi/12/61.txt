====== CC Ādi 12.61 ======

===== Text =====

> yādava-dāsa, vijaya-dāsa, dāsa janārdana
> ananta-dāsa, kānu-paṇḍita, dāsa nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yādava]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Yādava dāsa; [[synonyms:v:vijaya]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Vijaya dāsa; [[synonyms:d:dāsajanārdana]]---of the name Janārdana dāsa; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Ananta dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kānu]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---of the name Kānu Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:dāsanārāyaṇa]]---of the name Nārāyaṇa dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Yādava dāsa, Vijaya dāsa, Janārdana dāsa, Ananta dāsa, Kānu Paṇḍita and Nārāyaṇa dāsa were the nineteenth, twentieth, twenty-first, twenty-second, twentythird and twenty-fourth branches of Advaita Ācārya.**
