====== Cc. Ādi 12.33 ======

===== Text =====

> patra paḍiyā prabhura mane haila duḥkha
> bāhire hāsiyā kichu bale candra-mukha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patra]]---note; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---reading; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---the moon-faced.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu became unhappy upon reading the note, although His face still shone as brightly as the moon. Thus, smiling, He spoke as follows.**
