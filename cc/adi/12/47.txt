====== CC Ādi 12.47 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe, ihāke kene dile daraśana
> dui prakārete kare more viḍambana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Advaita Ācārya said; [[synonyms:i:ihāke]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:d:dile]]---You gave; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---audience; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:p:prakārete]]---in ways; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:v:viḍambana]]---cheating.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya then said to Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Why have You called back this man and allowed him to see You? He has cheated Me in two ways."**
