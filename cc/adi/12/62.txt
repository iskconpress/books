====== CC Ādi 12.62 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvatsa paṇḍita, brahmacārī haridāsa
> puruṣottama brahmacārī, āra kṛṣṇadāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvatsa]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---of the name Śrīvatsa Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]] [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---of the name Haridāsa Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottamabrahmacārī]]---of the name Puruṣottama Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---of the name Kṛṣṇadāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvatsa Paṇḍita, Haridāsa Brahmacārī, Puruṣottama Brahmacārī and Kṛṣṇadāsa were the twenty-fifth, twenty-sixth, twenty-seventh and twenty-eighth branches of Advaita Ācārya.**
