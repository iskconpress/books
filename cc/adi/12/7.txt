====== CC Ādi 12.7 ======

===== Text =====

> sei jala skandhe kare śākhāte sañcāra
> phale-phule bāḍe,---śākhā ha-ila vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:j:jala]]---that water; [[synonyms:s:skandhe]]---on the branches; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ś:śākhāte]]---on the subbranches; [[synonyms:s:sañcāra]]---growing; [[synonyms:p:phale]]-[[synonyms:p:phule]]---in fruits and flowers; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---the branches; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---widespread.

===== Translation =====

** As the trunk and branches were watered, the branches and subbranches spread lavishly, and the tree grew full with fruits and flowers.**
