====== Cc. Ādi 12.55 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' prastāve āche bahuta vicāra
> grantha-bāhulya-bhaye nāri likhibāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---in this; [[synonyms:p:prastāve]]---statement; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---many; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---considerations; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---of the book; [[synonyms:b:bāhulya]]---of the expansion; [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---out of fear; [[synonyms:n:nāri]]---I do not; [[synonyms:l:likhibāra]]---write.

===== Translation =====

**In this statement there are many confidential considerations. I do not write of them all, fearing an unnecessary increase in the volume of the book.**
