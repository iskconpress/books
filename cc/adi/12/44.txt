====== CC Ādi 12.44 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhuke kahena---tomāra nā bujhi e līlā
> āmā haite prasāda-pātra karilā kamalā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---do not; [[synonyms:b:bujhi]]---I understand; [[synonyms:e:e]]---these; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---Myself; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---more than; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---object of favor; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---You did; [[synonyms:k:kamalā]]---unto Kamalākānta Viśvāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya told Lord Caitanya, "I cannot understand Your transcendental pastimes. You have shown more favor to Kamalākānta than You generally show to Me.**
