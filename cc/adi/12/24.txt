====== CC Ādi 12.24 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā mantra paḍena ācārya, nā haya cetana
> ācāryera duḥkhe vaiṣṇava karena krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:m:mantra]]---hymns; [[synonyms:p:paḍena]]---chants; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---became; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---conscious; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in unhappiness; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---do; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---cry.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya chanted various mantras, but Gopāla did not come to consciousness. Thus all the Vaiṣṇavas present cried in sorrow at His plight.**
