====== CC Ādi 12.95 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāra mādhurya-gandhe lubdha haya mana
> ataeva taṭe rahi' cāki eka kaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---sweet and mellow; [[synonyms:g:gandhe]]---by the fragrance; [[synonyms:l:lubdha]]---attracted; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:t:taṭe]]---on the beach; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---standing; [[synonyms:c:cāki]]---I taste; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:kaṇa]]---particle.

===== Translation =====

**It is not possible to dip into that great ocean, but its sweet mellow fragrance attracts my mind. I therefore stand on the shore of that ocean to try to taste but a drop of it.**
