====== CC Ādi 12.39 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve mahāprabhu more karena sammāna
> duḥkha pāi' mane āmi kailuṅ anumāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---respect; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:p:pāi']]---becoming; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---made; [[synonyms:a:anumāna]]---a plan.

===== Translation =====

**"Formerly Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu always respected Me as His senior, but I did not like such respect. Therefore, My mind being afflicted by unhappiness, I made a plan.**
