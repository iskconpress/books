====== CC Ādi 12.63 ======

===== Text =====

> puruṣottama paṇḍita, āra raghunātha
> vanamālī kavicandra, āra vaidyanātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---of the name Puruṣottama Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---and Raghunātha; [[synonyms:v:vanamālī]] [[synonyms:k:kavicandra]]---of the name Vanamālī Kavicandra; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vaidyanātha]]---of the name Vaidyanātha.

===== Translation =====

**Puruṣottama Paṇḍita, Raghunātha, Vanamālī Kavicandra and Vaidyanātha were the twenty-ninth, thirtieth, thirty-first and thirty-second branches of Advaita Ācārya.**
