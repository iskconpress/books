====== CC Ādi 12.89 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' sañkṣepe kahilāṅ paṇḍitera gaṇa
> aiche āra śākhā-upaśākhāra gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahilāṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the branches of Śrī Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:u:upaśākhāra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---description of branches and subbranches.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have briefly described the branches and subbranches of Gadādhara Paṇḍita. There are still many more that I have not mentioned here.**
