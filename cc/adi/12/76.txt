====== CC Ādi 12.76 ======

===== Text =====

> sei ācārya-gaṇe mora koṭi namaskāra
> acyutānanda-prāya, caitanya---jīvana yāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the spiritual masters; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---millions; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:a:acyutānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---almost as good as Acyutānanda; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jīvana]]---life; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose.

===== Translation =====

**I therefore offer my respectful obeisances millions of times to Acyutānanda's actual followers, whose life and soul was Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
