====== CC Ādi 12.5 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-mālīra kṛpā-jalera secane
> sei jale puṣṭa skandha bāḍe dine dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:mālīra]]---of the gardener named Caitanya; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:j:jalera]]---of the water of His mercy; [[synonyms:s:secane]]---by sprinkling; [[synonyms:s:seijale]]---by that water; [[synonyms:p:puṣṭa]]---nourished; [[synonyms:s:skandha]]---branches; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increased; [[synonyms:d:dinedine]]---day after day.

===== Translation =====

** Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was also the gardener, and as He poured the water of His mercy on the tree, all the branches and subbranches grew, day after day.**
