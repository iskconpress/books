====== CC Ādi 12.32 ======

===== Text =====

> kintu tāṅra daive kichu ha-iyāche ṛṇa
> ṛṇa śodhibāre cāhi taṅkā śata-tina

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:d:daive]]---in due course of time; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iyāche]]---there was; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṇa]]---debt; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṇa]]---debt; [[synonyms:ś:śodhibāre]]---to liquidate; [[synonyms:c:cāhi]]---I want; [[synonyms:t:taṅkā]]---rupees; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:t:tina]]---about three hundred.

===== Translation =====

**But it also mentioned that Advaita Ācārya had recently incurred a debt of about three hundred rupees that Kamalākānta Viśvāsa wanted to liquidate.**
