====== CC Ādi 12.15 ======

===== Text =====

> jagad-gurute tumi kara aiche upadeśa
> tomāra ei upadeśe naṣṭa ha-ila deśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:g:gurute]]---on the spiritual master of the universe; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]]---instruction; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:u:upadeśe]]---by this instruction; [[synonyms:n:naṣṭa]]---spoiled; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---will become; [[synonyms:d:deśa]]---the country.

===== Translation =====

**He told his father, "Your instruction that Keśava Bhāratī is the spiritual master of Caitanya Mahāprabhu will spoil the entire country.**
