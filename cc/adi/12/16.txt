====== CC Ādi 12.16 ======

===== Text =====

> caudda bhuvanera guru---caitanya-gosāñi
> tāṅra guru---anya, ei kona śāstre nāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caudda]]---fourteen; [[synonyms:b:bhuvanera]]---planetary systems; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---master; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:guru]]---His spiritual master; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---someone else; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---any; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in scripture; [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---there is no mention.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the spiritual master of the fourteen worlds, but You say that someone else is His spiritual master. This is not supported by any revealed scripture."**
