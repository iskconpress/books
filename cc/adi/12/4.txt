====== CC Ādi 12.4 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛkṣera dvitīya skandha---ācārya-gosāñi
> tāṅra yata śākhā ha-ila, tāra lekhā nāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣera]]---of the tree; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]] [[synonyms:s:skandha]]---the second big branch; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Advaita Ācārya Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---branches; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:l:lekhā]]---description; [[synonyms:n:nāñi]]---there is not.

===== Translation =====

** Śrī Advaita Prabhu was the second big branch of the tree. There are many subbranches, but it is impossible to mention them all.**
