====== Cc. Ādi 12.53 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śikṣā sabākāre, sabe mane kaila
> ācārya-gosāñi mane ānanda pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]]---instruction; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---for all; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all present; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---took it; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---felt.

===== Translation =====

**When Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave this instruction to Kamalākānta, all present considered it to be meant for everyone. Thus Advaita Ācārya was greatly pleased.**
