====== Cc. Ādi 12.68 ======

===== Text =====

> sṛjāila, jīyāila, tāṅre nā mānila
> kṛtaghna ha-ilā, tāṅre skandha kruddha ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sṛjāila]]---fructified; [[synonyms:j:jīyāila]]---maintained; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mānila]]---accepted; [[synonyms:k:kṛtaghna]]---ungrateful; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---thus became; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to them; [[synonyms:s:skandha]]---trunk; [[synonyms:k:kruddha]]---angry; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**Some branches did not accept the original trunk that vitalized and maintained the entire tree. When they thus became ungrateful, the original trunk was angry at them.**
