====== CC Ādi 12.60 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha kara, āra kara bhavanātha
> hṛdayānanda sena, āra dāsa bholānātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---of the name Jagannātha Kara; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:b:bhavanātha]]---of the name Bhavanātha Kara; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayānanda]] [[synonyms:s:sena]]---of the name Hṛdayānanda Sena; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]] [[synonyms:b:bholānātha]]---of the name Bholānātha dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Jagannātha Kara, Bhavanātha Kara, Hṛdayānanda Sena and Bholānātha dāsa were the fifteenth, sixteenth, seventeenth and eighteenth branches of Advaita Ācārya.**
