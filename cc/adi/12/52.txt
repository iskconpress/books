====== Cc. Ādi 12.52 ======

===== Text =====

> loka-lajjā haya, dharma-kīrti haya hāni
> aiche karma nā kariha kabhu ihā jāni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:l:lajjā]]---unpopularity; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religion; [[synonyms:k:kīrti]]---reputation; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:h:hāni]]---damaged; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---work; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---do not; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---execute; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---ever; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus one becomes unpopular in the eyes of the people in general, for this damages his religiosity and fame. A Vaiṣṇava, especially one who acts as a spiritual master, must not act in such a way. One should always be conscious of this fact."**
