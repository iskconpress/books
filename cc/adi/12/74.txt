====== CC Ādi 12.74 ======

===== Text =====

> sei sei,---ācāryera kṛpāra bhājana
> anāyāse pāila sei caitanya-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sei]]---whoever; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāra]]---of the mercy; [[synonyms:b:bhājana]]---eligible candidate; [[synonyms:a:anāyāse]]---without difficulty; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**By the mercy of Advaita Ācārya, the devotees who strictly followed the path of Caitanya Mahāprabhu attained the shelter of Lord Caitanya's lotus feet without difficulty.**
