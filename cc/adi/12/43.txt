~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Ādi 12.43~~

====== CC Ādi 12.43 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' ācārya tāṅre kariyā āśvāsa
> ānandita ha-iyā āila mahāprabhu-pāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---speaking thus; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Śrī Advaita Ācārya Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Kamalākānta Viśvāsa; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsa]]---pacification; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---happy; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---went; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---to the place of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After pacifying Kamalākānta Viśvāsa in this way, Śrī Advaita Ācārya Prabhu went to see Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
