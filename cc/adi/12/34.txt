====== CC Ādi 12.34 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryere sthāpiyāche kariyā īśvara
> ithe doṣa nāhi, ācārya---daivata īśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryere]]---unto Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:sthāpiyāche]]---he established; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---mentioning; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---as the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:i:ithe]]---in this; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:d:daivataīśvara]]---He is actually the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"He has established Advaita Ācārya as an incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. There is nothing wrong in this, for He is indeed the Lord Himself.**
