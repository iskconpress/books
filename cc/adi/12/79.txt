====== CC Ādi 12.79 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-gadādhara paṇḍita śākhāte mahottama
> tāṅra upaśākhā kichu kari ye gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Śrī Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śākhāte]]---of the branch; [[synonyms:m:mahottama]]---very great; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:u:upaśākhā]]---branches and subbranches; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---let me do; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting.

===== Translation =====

**After describing the branches and subbranches of Advaita Ācārya, I shall now attempt to describe some of the descendants of Śrī Gadādhara Paṇḍita, the most important among the branches.**
