====== Cc. Ādi 12.78 ======

===== Text =====

> śākhā-upaśākhā, tāra nāhika gaṇana
> kichu-mātra kahi' kari dig-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:u:upaśākhā]]---branches and subbranches; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of them; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is no; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---something about them; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---describing; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I am simply giving; [[synonyms:d:dig]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---a glimpse of the direction.

===== Translation =====

**There are multifarious branches and subbranches of Advaita Ācārya. It is very difficult to enumerate them fully. I have simply given a glimpse of the whole trunk and its branches and subbranches.**
