====== Cc. Ādi 12.64 ======

===== Text =====

> lokanātha paṇḍita, āra murāri paṇḍita
> śrī-haricaraṇa, āra mādhava paṇḍita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lokanātha]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---of the name Lokanātha Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:m:murāri]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---of the name Murāri Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haricaraṇa]]---of the name Śrī Haricaraṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mādhavapaṇḍita]]---of the name Mādhava Paṇḍita.

===== Translation =====

**Lokanātha Paṇḍita, Murāri Paṇḍita, Śrī Haricaraṇa and Mādhava Paṇḍita were the thirty-third, thirty-fourth, thirty-fifth and thirty-sixth branches of Advaita Ācārya.**
