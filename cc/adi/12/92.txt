====== Cc. Ādi 12.92 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅ-sabā-smaraṇe pāi caitanya-caraṇa
> yāṅ-sabā-smaraṇe haya vāñchita pūraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇe]]---by remembering; [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---I get; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇe]]---by remembering; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:v:vāñchita]] [[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---fulfillment of all desires.

===== Translation =====

**Simply by remembering the names of all these Vaiṣṇavas, one can attain the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Indeed, simply by remembering their holy names, one achieves the fulfillment of all desires.**
