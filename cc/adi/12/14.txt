====== Cc. Ādi 12.14 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñira guru---keśava bhāratī
> ei pitāra vākya śuni' duḥkha pāila ati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---the spiritual master; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---His spiritual master; [[synonyms:k:keśava]] [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---of the name Keśava Bhāratī; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:p:pitāra]]---his father's; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---words; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very much.

===== Translation =====

**When Acyutānanda heard from his father that Keśava Bhāratī was the spiritual master of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he was very unhappy.**
