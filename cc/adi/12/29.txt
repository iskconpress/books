~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Ādi 12.29~~

====== CC Ādi 12.29 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlācale teṅho eka patrikā likhiyā
> pratāparudrera pāśa dila pāṭhāiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---Kamalākānta; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:p:patrikā]]---note; [[synonyms:l:likhiyā]]---writing; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudrera]]---Pratāparudra Mahārāja; [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---addressed to him; [[synonyms:d:dilapāṭhāiyā]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**When Kamalākānta Viśvāsa was in Jagannātha Purī, he sent a note through someone to Mahārāja Pratāparudra.**
