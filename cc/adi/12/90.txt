====== Cc. Ādi 12.90 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍitera gaṇa saba,---bhāgavata dhanya
> prāṇa-vallabha---sabāra śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---followers; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]] [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---glorious devotees; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vallabha]]---the heart and soul; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All the followers of Gadādhara Paṇḍita are considered great devotees because they have Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as their life and soul.**
