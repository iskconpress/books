====== Cc. Ādi 12.13 ======

===== Text =====

> acyutānanda---baḍa śākhā, ācārya-nandana
> ājanma sevilā teṅho caitanya-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:acyutānanda]]---of the name Acyutānanda; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---a big branch; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ājanma]]---from the very beginning of life; [[synonyms:s:sevilā]]---served; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**A big branch of Advaita Ācārya was Acyutānanda, His son. From the beginning of his life he engaged in the service of the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya.**
