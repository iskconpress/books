====== CC Ādi 12.46 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' mahāprabhu hāsite lāgilā
> bolāiyā kamalākānte prasanna ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---thus hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hāsite]]---to laugh; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:b:bolāiyā]]---calling; [[synonyms:k:kamalākānte]]---unto Kamalākānta; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu laughed with satisfaction and immediately called Kamalākānta Viśvāsa.**
