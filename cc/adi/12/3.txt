====== Cc. Ādi 12.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-caitanyāmara-taror
> dvitīya-skandha-rūpiṇaḥ
> śrīmad-advaita-candrasya
> śākhā-rūpān gaṇān numaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:amara]]---eternal; [[synonyms:t:taroḥ]]---of the tree; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]---second; [[synonyms:s:skandha]]---big branch; [[synonyms:r:rūpiṇaḥ]]---in the form of; [[synonyms:ś:śrīmat]]---the all-glorious; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candrasya]]---of Lord Advaitacandra; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpān]]---in the form of branches; [[synonyms:g:gaṇān]]---to all the followers; [[synonyms:n:numaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances.

===== Translation =====

** I offer my respectful obeisances to the all-glorious Advaita Prabhu, who forms the second branch of the eternal Caitanya tree, and to His followers, who form His subbranches.**
