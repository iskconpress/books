====== Cc. Ādi 13.52 ======

===== Text =====

> kona vāñchā pūraṇa lāgi' vrajendra-kumāra
> avatīrṇa haite mane karilā vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---fulfillment; [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the matter of; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:k:kumāra]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---to descend as an incarnation; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration.

===== Translation =====

**To fulfill a particular desire within His mind, Lord Kṛṣṇa, Vrajendra-kumāra, decided to descend on this planet after mature contemplation.**
