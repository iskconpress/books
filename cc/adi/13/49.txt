====== CC Ādi 13.49 ======

===== Text =====

> grantha-vistāra-bhaye chāḍilā ye ye sthāna
> sei sei sthāne kichu kariba vyākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:grantha]]---of the book; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---of expansion; [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---being afraid; [[synonyms:c:chāḍilā]]---gave up; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---which different places; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in those places; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall make; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**Being afraid of his book's becoming too voluminous, he left some places without vivid descriptions. I shall try to fill those places as far as possible.**
