====== CC Ādi 13.35 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye nīlācale chaya vatsara
> nṛtya, gīta, premabhakti-dāna nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---out of those twenty-four years; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---while He was staying at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:c:chaya]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---continuously for six years; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---distribution; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always.

===== Translation =====

**For six of these twenty-four years in Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī], He distributed love of Godhead by always chanting and dancing.**
