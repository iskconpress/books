====== Cc. Ādi 13.11 ======

===== Text =====

> cabbiśa vatsara-śeṣe kariyā sannyāsa
> āra cabbiśa vatsara kaila nīlācale vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]]---twenty-four; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---years; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end of; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---accepting; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---renounced order; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]]---twenty-four; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---years; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---reside.

===== Translation =====

**After twenty-four years He accepted the renounced order of life, sannyāsa, and He resided for twenty-four years more at Jagannātha Purī.**
