====== Cc. Ādi 13.97 ======

===== Text =====

> prasanna haila daśa dik, prasanna nadījala
> sthāvara-jaṅgama haila ānande vihvala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasanna]]---jubilant; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten; [[synonyms:d:dik]]---directions; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---the water of the rivers; [[synonyms:s:sthāvara]]---immovable; [[synonyms:j:jaṅgama]]---movable; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in joy; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**In this atmosphere, all the ten directions became jubilant, as did the waves of the rivers. Moreover, all beings, moving and nonmoving, were overwhelmed with transcendental bliss.**
