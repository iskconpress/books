====== CC Ādi 13.34 ======

===== Text =====

> cabbiśa vatsara chilā kariyā sannyāsa
> bhakta-gaṇa lañā kailā nīlācale vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]]---twenty-four; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---years; [[synonyms:c:chilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---accepting; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the renounced order; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with Him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---in Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---reside.

===== Translation =====

**For His remaining twenty-four years, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, after accepting the renounced order of life, stayed at Jagannātha Purī with His devotees.**
