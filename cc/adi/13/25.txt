====== Cc. Ādi 13.25 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gaurahari' bali' tāre hāse sarva nārī
> ataeva haila tāṅra nāma 'gaurahari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---of the name Gaurahari; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---addressing Him thus; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto the Lord; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laugh; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---all the ladies; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---of the name Gaurahari.

===== Translation =====

**When all the ladies saw this fun, they enjoyed laughing and called the Lord "Gaurahari." Since then, Gaurahari became another of His names.**
