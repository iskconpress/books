====== CC Ādi 13.62 ======

===== Text =====

> asaṅkhya bhaktera karāilā avatāra
> śeṣe avatīrṇa hailā vrajendra-kumāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of devotees; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---made into being; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at last; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]]---descended; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:k:kumāra]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa, Vrajendra-kumāra, first caused countless devotees to appear, and at last He appeared Himself.**
