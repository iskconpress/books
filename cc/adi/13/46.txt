====== CC Ādi 13.46 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara-svarūpa, āra gupta murāri
> mukhya-mukhya-līlā sūtre likhiyāche vicāri'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---of the name Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:g:gupta]] [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---of the name Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---most important; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:s:sūtre]]---in notes; [[synonyms:l:likhiyāche]]---have written; [[synonyms:v:vicāri']]---by mature deliberation.

===== Translation =====

**Devotees like Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara and Murāri Gupta have recorded all the principal pastimes of Lord Caitanya in the form of notes, after deliberate consideration.**
