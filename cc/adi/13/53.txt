====== CC Ādi 13.53 ======

===== Text =====

> āge avatārilā ye ye guru-parivāra
> saṅkṣepe kahiye, kahā nā yāya vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---first of all; [[synonyms:a:avatārilā]]---allowed to descend; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all those; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:p:parivāra]]---family of spiritual masters; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I describe; [[synonyms:k:kahā]]---to describe; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---possible; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expansively.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa therefore first allowed His family of superiors to descend on the earth. I shall try to describe them in brief because it is not possible to describe them fully.**
