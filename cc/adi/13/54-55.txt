====== CC Ādi 13.54-55 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-śacī-jagannātha, śrī-mādhava-purī
> keśava bhāratī, āra śrī-īśvara purī

> advaita ācārya, āra paṇḍita śrīvāsa
> ācāryaratna, vidyānidhi, ṭhākura haridāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Śrīmatī Śacidevī and Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhava]] [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Śrī Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:k:keśava]] [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---of the name Keśava Bhāratī; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:p:purī]]---of the name Śrī Īśvara Purī; [[synonyms:a:advaitaācārya]]---of the name Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---of the name Śrīvāsa Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:r:ratna]]---of the name Ācāryaratna; [[synonyms:v:vidyānidhi]]---of the name Vidyānidhi; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]] [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---of the name Ṭhākura Haridāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa, before appearing as Lord Caitanya, requested these devotees to precede Him: Śrī Śacīdevī, Jagannātha Miśra, Mādhavendra Purī, Keśava Bhāratī, Īśvara Purī, Advaita Ācārya, Śrīvāsa Paṇḍita, Ācāryaratna, Vidyānidhi and Ṭhākura Haridāsa.**
