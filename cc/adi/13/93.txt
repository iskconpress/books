====== CC Ādi 13.93 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya dhvani haila sakala bhuvana
> camatkāra haiyā loka bhāve mane mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---vibration; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---worlds; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:h:haiyā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---state; [[synonyms:m:mane]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---within their minds.

===== Translation =====

**All people thus chanted the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra during the lunar eclipse, and their minds were struck with wonder.**
