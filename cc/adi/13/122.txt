====== CC Ādi 13.122 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche prabhu śacī-ghare, kṛpāya kaila avatāre,
> yei ihā karaye śravaṇa
> gaura-prabhu dayāmaya, tāṅre hayena sadaya,
> sei pāya tāṅhāra caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the home of Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by His causeless mercy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---advent; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---does; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hear; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---being very merciful; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---upon him; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---becomes; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:daya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, out of His causeless mercy, made His advent in the house of Śacīdevī. Lord Caitanya is very merciful to anyone who hears this narration of His birth, and thus such a person attains the lotus feet of the Lord.**
