====== CC Ādi 13.51 ======

===== Text =====

> ādi-līlā-sūtra likhi, śuna, bhakta-gaṇa
> saṅkṣepe likhiye samyak nā yāya likhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the first part of His pastimes; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]] [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I write a synopsis; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all you devotees; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:l:likhiye]]---I write; [[synonyms:s:samyak]]---full; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---possible; [[synonyms:l:likhana]]---to write.

===== Translation =====

**My dear devotees of Lord Caitanya, let me now write a synopsis of the ādi-līlā; I write of these pastimes in brief because it is not possible to describe them in full.**
