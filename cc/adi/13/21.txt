====== CC Ādi 13.21 ======

===== Text =====

> 'hari' 'hari' bale loka haraṣita hañā
> janmilā caitanya-prabhu 'nāma' janmāiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy names of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---speak; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]---jubilant; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:j:janmilā]]---took birth; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name; [[synonyms:j:janmāiyā]]---after causing to appear.

===== Translation =====

**In jubilation everyone was chanting the holy name of the Lord---"Hari! Hari!"---and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then appeared, after first causing the appearance of the holy name.**
