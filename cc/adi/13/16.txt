====== CC Ādi 13.16 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ye śeṣa-līlā svarūpa-dāmodara
> sūtra kari' granthilena granthera bhitara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes at the end; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---of the name Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in the form of notes; [[synonyms:g:granthilena]]---recorded; [[synonyms:g:granthera]]---a book; [[synonyms:b:bhitara]]---within.

===== Translation =====

**His later pastimes [madhya-līlā and āntya-līlā] were recorded in the form of notes by His secretary, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, and thus kept within a book.**
