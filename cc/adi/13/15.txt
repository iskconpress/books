====== Cc. Ādi 13.15 ======

===== Text =====

> ādi-līlā-madhye prabhura yateka carita
> sūtra-rūpe murāri gupta karilā grathita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the original pastimes; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yateka]]---whatever; [[synonyms:c:carita]]---activities; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of notes; [[synonyms:m:murāri]] [[synonyms:g:gupta]]---of the name Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---has; [[synonyms:g:grathita]]---recorded.

===== Translation =====

**All the pastimes enacted by Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His ādi-lilā were recorded in summary form by Murāri Gupta.**
