====== CC Ādi 13.59 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha miśravara---padavī 'purandara'
> nanda-vasudeva-rūpa sadguṇa-sāgara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:v:vara]]---Jagannātha Miśra, who was the chief among the seven; [[synonyms:p:padavī]]---designation; [[synonyms:p:purandara]]---another name of Vasudeva; [[synonyms:n:nanda]]---Nanda, the father of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vasudeva]]---the father of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---like; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---good qualities; [[synonyms:s:sāgara]]---ocean.

===== Translation =====

**Jagannātha Miśra was designated as Purandara. Exactly like Nanda Mahārāja and Vasudeva, he was an ocean of all good qualities.**
