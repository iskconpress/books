====== CC Ādi 13.44 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta caitanya-līlā kṣudra jīva hañā
> ke varṇite pāre, tāhā vistāra kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:k:kṣudra]]---a small; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---living entity; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---describe; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expanding; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing so.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu are unlimited. How much can a small living entity elaborate about those transcendental pastimes?**
