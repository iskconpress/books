====== CC Ādi 13.36 ======

===== Text =====

> setubandha, āra gauḍa-vyāpi vṛndāvana
> prema-nāma pracāriyā karilā bhramaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:setubandha]]---Cape Comorin; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]---Bengal; [[synonyms:v:vyāpi]]---extending; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---love of Kṛṣṇa and the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pracāriyā]]---distributing; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---performed; [[synonyms:b:bhramaṇa]]---touring.

===== Translation =====

**Beginning from Cape Comorin and extending through Bengal to Vṛndāvana, during these six years He toured all of India, chanting, dancing and distributing love of Kṛṣṇa.**
