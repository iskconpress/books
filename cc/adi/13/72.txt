====== Cc. Ādi 13.72 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthamiśra-patnī śacīra udare
> aṣṭa kanyā krame haila, janmi' janmi' mare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---of the name Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---his wife; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]]---of Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:u:udare]]---within the womb; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]---eight; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---daughters; [[synonyms:k:krame]]---one after another; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---appeared; [[synonyms:j:janmi']]---after taking birth; [[synonyms:j:janmi']]---after taking birth; [[synonyms:m:mare]]---all died.

===== Translation =====

**Before the birth of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, eight daughters took birth one after another from the womb of Śacīmātā, the wife of Jagannātha Miśra. But just after their birth, they all died.**
