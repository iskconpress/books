====== CC Ādi 13.68 ======

===== Text =====

> lokera nistāra-hetu karena cintana
> kemate e saba lokera ha-ibe tāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all people; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for the matter of deliverance; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:c:cintana]]---contemplation; [[synonyms:k:kemate]]---how; [[synonyms:e:e]]---these; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of people in general; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---will become; [[synonyms:t:tāraṇa]]---liberation.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the condition of the world, He began to think seriously of how all these people could be delivered from the clutches of māyā.**
