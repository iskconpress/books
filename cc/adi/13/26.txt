====== CC Ādi 13.26 ======

===== Text =====

> bālya vayasa---yāvat hāte khaḍi dila
> paugaṇḍa vayasa---yāvat vivāha nā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bālya]] [[synonyms:v:vayasa]]---childhood age; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---until the time; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in His hand; [[synonyms:k:khaḍi]]---chalk; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---was given; [[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:v:vayasa]]---the part of boyhood known as //paugaṇḍa//; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---until; [[synonyms:v:vivāha]]---marriage; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did take place.

===== Translation =====

**His childhood lasted until the date of hāte khaḍi, the beginning of His education, and His age from the end of His childhood until He married is called paugaṇḍa.**
