====== CC Ādi 13.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya gauracandra
> jayādvaitacandra jaya jaya nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---Lord Gauracandra; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:ā:ādvaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---all glories to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories to; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Advaitacandra! All glories to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu!**
