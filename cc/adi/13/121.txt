====== CC Ādi 13.121 ======

===== Text =====

> lagna gaṇi' harṣamati, nīlāmbara cakravartī,
> gupte kichu kahila miśrere
> mahāpuruṣera cihna, lagne aṅge bhinna bhinna,
> dekhi,---ei tāribe saṁsāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lagna]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇi']]---by astrological calculation of the birth moment; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:n:nīlāmbaracakravartī]]---of the name Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; [[synonyms:g:gupte]]---in private; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:m:miśrere]]---unto Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣeracihna]]---all the symptoms of a great personality; [[synonyms:l:lagne]]---in the birth moment; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:b:bhinnabhinna]]---different; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this child; [[synonyms:t:tāribe]]---shall deliver; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāre]]---all the three worlds.

===== Translation =====

**After calculating the birth moment of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Nīlāmbara Cakravartī privately said to Jagannātha Miśra that he saw all the different symptoms of a great personality in both the body and birth moment of the child. Thus he understood that in the future this child would deliver all the three worlds.**
