====== CC Ādi 13.31 ======

===== Text =====

> kiśora vayase ārambhilā saṅkīrtana
> rātra-dine preme nṛtya, saṅge bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kiśora]] [[synonyms:v:vayase]]---just before the beginning of His youthful life; [[synonyms:ā:ārambhilā]]---began; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---the saṅkīrtana movement; [[synonyms:r:rātra]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---night and day; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Just prior to His youthful life, He began the saṅkīrtana movement. Day and night He used to dance in ecstasy with His devotees.**
