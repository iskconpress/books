====== CC Ādi 13.33 ======

===== Text =====

> cabbiśa vatsara aiche navadvīpa-grāme
> laoyāilā sarva-loke kṛṣṇa-prema-nāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]]---twenty-four; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---years; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpa]]---of the name Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the village; [[synonyms:l:laoyāilā]]---induced; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loke]]---every man; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in the holy name.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu lived in the Navadvīpa area for twenty-four years, and He induced every person to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra and thus merge in love of Kṛṣṇa.**
