====== CC Ādi 13.12 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye chaya vatsara---gamanāgamana
> kabhu dakṣiṇa, kabhu gauḍa, kabhu vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---out of that; [[synonyms:c:chaya]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---six years; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---touring; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---in South India; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---in Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**Of these last twenty-four years, He spent the first six years continuously touring India, sometimes in South India, sometimes in Bengal and sometimes in Vṛndāvana.**
