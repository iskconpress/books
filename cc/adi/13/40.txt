====== Cc. Ādi 13.40 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-divase kṛṣṇa-viraha-sphuraṇa
> unmādera ceṣṭā kare pralāpa-vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:divase]]---day and night; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viraha]]---feelings of separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sphuraṇa]]---awakening; [[synonyms:u:unmādera]]---of a madman; [[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---activities; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---talking inconsistently; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**Day and night Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt separation from Kṛṣṇa. Manifesting symptoms of this separation, He cried and talked very inconsistently, like a madman.**
