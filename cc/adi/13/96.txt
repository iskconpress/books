====== CC Ādi 13.96 ======

===== Text =====

> 'hari' bali' nārīgaṇa dei hulāhuli
> svarge vādya-nṛtya kare deva kutūhalī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---by saying the word Hari; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the ladies; [[synonyms:d:dei]]---chanting; [[synonyms:h:hulāhuli]]---the sound of //hulāhuli//; [[synonyms:s:svarge]]---in the heavenly planets; [[synonyms:v:vādya]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---music and dance; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---demigods; [[synonyms:k:kutūhalī]]---curious.

===== Translation =====

**While all the ladies vibrated the holy name of Hari on earth, in the heavenly planets dancing and music were going on, for the demigods were very curious.**
