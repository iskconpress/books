====== CC Ādi 13.73 ======

===== Text =====

> apatya-virahe miśrera duḥkhī haila mana
> putra lāgi' ārādhila viṣṇura caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:apatya]]---of children; [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation; [[synonyms:m:miśrera]]---of Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---son; [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the matter of; [[synonyms:ā:ārādhila]]---worshiped; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇura]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Jagannātha Miśra was very unhappy at the death of his children one after another. Therefore, desiring a son, he worshiped the lotus feet of Lord Viṣṇu.**
