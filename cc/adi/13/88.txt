====== Cc. Ādi 13.88 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlāmbara cakravartī kahila gaṇiyā
> ei māse putra habe śubha-kṣaṇa pāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlāmbara]] [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---of the name Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:g:gaṇiyā]]---by astrological calculation; [[synonyms:e:eimāse]]---in this month; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---son; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---will take birth; [[synonyms:ś:śubha]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---auspicious moment; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---taking advantage of.

===== Translation =====

**Nīlāmbara Cakravartī [the grandfather of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu] then did an astrological calculation and said that in that month, taking advantage of an auspicious moment, the child would take birth.**
