====== Cc. Ādi 13.85 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāra hṛdaya haite gelā tomāra hṛdaye
> hena bujhi, janmibena kona mahāśaye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from my heart; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---transferred; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---into your heart; [[synonyms:h:hena]]---like this; [[synonyms:b:bujhi]]---I understand; [[synonyms:j:janmibena]]---will take birth; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaye]]---very great personality.

===== Translation =====

**"From my heart it entered your heart. I therefore understand that a great personality will soon take birth."**
