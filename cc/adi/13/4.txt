====== Cc. Ādi 13.4 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya dāmodara-svarūpa jaya murāri gupta
> ei saba candrodaye tamaḥ kaila lupta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---of the name Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:m:murārigupta]]---of the name Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---of all these; [[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:u:udaye]]---such moons having arisen; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---darkness; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:l:lupta]]---dissipated.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Svarūpa Dāmodara and Murāri Gupta! All these brilliant moons have together dissipated the darkness of this material world.**
