====== CC Ādi 13.47 ======

===== Text =====

> sei, anusāre likhi līlā-sūtragaṇa
> vistāri' varṇiāchena tāhā dāsa-vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:a:anusāre]]---following; [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I write; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---notes; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---very explicitly; [[synonyms:v:varṇiyāchena]]---has described; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**The notes kept by Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara and Murāri Gupta are the basis of this book. Following those notes, I write of all the pastimes of the Lord. The notes have been described elaborately by Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura.**
