====== Cc. Ādi 13.45 ======

===== Text =====

> sūtra kari' gaṇe yadi āpane ananta
> sahasra-vadane teṅho nāhi pāya anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---aphorisms; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---counts; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---Śeṣa Nāga, the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadane]]---by thousands of mouths; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He also; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**If Śeṣaṇāga Ananta personally were to make the pastimes of Lord Caitanya into //sūtras,// even with His thousands of mouths there is no possibility that He could find their limit.**
