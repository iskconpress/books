====== CC Ādi 13.27 ======

===== Text =====

> vivāha karile haila navīna yauvana
> sarvatra laoyāila prabhu nāma-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vivāha]] [[synonyms:k:karile]]---after getting married; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---began; [[synonyms:n:navīna]]---new; [[synonyms:y:yauvana]]---youth; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:l:laoyāila]]---caused to take; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---the //saṅkīrtana// movement.

===== Translation =====

**After His marriage His youth began, and in His youth He induced everyone to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra anywhere and everywhere.**
