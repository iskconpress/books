====== Cc. Ādi 13.22 ======

===== Text =====

> janma-bālya-paugaṇḍa-kaiśora-yuvā-kāle
> hari-nāma laoyāilā prabhu nānā chale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:janma]]---time of birth; [[synonyms:b:bālya]]---childhood; [[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]---early boyhood; [[synonyms:k:kaiśora]]---end of boyhood; [[synonyms:y:yuvā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---youth; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:l:laoyāilā]]---caused to take; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:c:chale]]---under different pleas.

===== Translation =====

**At His birth, in His childhood, in His early and later boyhood as well as in His youth, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, under different pleas, induced people to chant the holy name of Hari [the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra].**
