====== CC Ādi 13.8 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya navadvīpe avatari
> āṭa-calliśa vatsara prakaṭa vihari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---at Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:a:avatari]]---adventing Himseif; [[synonyms:ā:āṭa]]-[[synonyms:c:calliśa]]---forty-eight; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---years; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---visible; [[synonyms:v:vihari]]---enjoying.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, adventing Himself in Navadvīpa, was visible for forty-eight years, enjoying His pastimes.**
