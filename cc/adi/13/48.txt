====== Cc. Ādi 13.48 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-līlāra vyāsa,---dāsa vṛndāvana
> madhura kariyā līlā karilā racana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---the authorized writer Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making it; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:r:racana]]---compilation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura, the authorized writer of the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, is as good as Śrīla Vyāsadeva. He has described the pastimes in such a way as to make them sweeter and sweeter.**
