====== CC Ādi 13.19 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-sad-guṇa-pūrṇāṁ tāṁ
> vande phālguna-pūrṇimām
> yasyāṁ śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanyo
> 'vatīrṇaḥ kṛṣṇa-nāmabhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sat]]---auspicious; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇām]]---filled with; [[synonyms:t:tām]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer obeisances; [[synonyms:p:phālguna]]---of the month of Phālguna; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇimām]]---the full-moon evening; [[synonyms:y:yasyām]]---in which; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanyaḥ]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇaḥ]]---advented; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa's; [[synonyms:n:nāmabhiḥ]]---with the chanting of the holy names.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances unto the full-moon evening in the month of Phālguna, an auspicious time full of auspicious symptoms, when Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu advented Himself with the chanting of the holy name, Hare Kṛṣṇa.**
