====== Cc. Ādi 13.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya gadādhara jaya śrīnivāsa
> jaya mukunda vāsudeva jaya haridāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajayagadādhara]]---all glories to Gadādhara Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:ś:śrīnivāsa]]---all glories to Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:j:jayamukunda]]---all glories to Mukunda; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---all glories to Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:j:jayaharidāsa]]---all glories to Haridāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Gadādhara Prabhu! All glories to Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura! All glories to Mukunda Prabhu and Vāsudeva Prabhu! All glories to Haridāsa Ṭhākura!**
