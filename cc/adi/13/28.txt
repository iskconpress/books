====== CC Ādi 13.28 ======

===== Text =====

> paugaṇḍa-vayase paḍena, paḍāna śiṣyagaṇe
> sarvatra karena kṛṣṇa-nāmera vyākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vayase]]---in the age of //paugaṇḍa//; [[synonyms:p:paḍena]]---studies; [[synonyms:p:paḍāna]]---teaches; [[synonyms:ś:śisya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---disciples; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**During His paugaṇḍa age He became a serious student and also taught disciples. In this way He used to explain the holy name of Kṛṣṇa everywhere.**
