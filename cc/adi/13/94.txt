====== Cc. Ādi 13.94 ======

===== Text =====

> jagat bhariyā loka bale---'hari' 'hari'
> sei-kṣaṇe gaurakṛṣṇa bhūme avatari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---fulfilling; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---said; [[synonyms:h:harihari]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:g:gaurakṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa in the form of Gaurahari; [[synonyms:b:bhūme]]---on the earth; [[synonyms:a:avatari]]---advented.

===== Translation =====

**When the whole world was thus chanting the holy name of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa in the form of Gaurahari advented Himself on the earth.**
