====== CC Ādi 13.50 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura līlāmṛta teṅho kaila āsvādana
> tāṅra bhukta-śeṣa kichu kariye carvaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:l:līlāmṛta]]---the nectar of the pastimes; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he (Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura); [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---taste; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bhukta]]---of food; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---remnants; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kariye]]---I do; [[synonyms:c:carvaṇa]]---chew.

===== Translation =====

**The transcendental pastimes of Lord Caitanya have actually been relished by Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura. I am simply trying to chew the remnants of food left by him.**
