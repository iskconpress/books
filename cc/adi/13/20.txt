====== Cc. Ādi 13.20 ======

===== Text =====

> phālguna-pūrṇimā-sandhyāya prabhura janmodaya
> sei-kāle daiva-yoge candra-grahaṇa haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:phālguna]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrṇimā]]---of the full moon of the month of Phālguna; [[synonyms:s:sandhyāya]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---at the time of His birth; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that moment; [[synonyms:d:daiva]]-[[synonyms:y:yoge]]---accidentally; [[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:g:grahaṇa]]---lunar eclipse; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---takes place.

===== Translation =====

**On the full-moon evening of the month of Phālguna when the Lord took birth, coincidentally there was also a lunar eclipse.**
