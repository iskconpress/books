====== CC Ādi 13.76 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhā ba-i viśve kichu nāhi dekhi āra
> ataeva 'viśvarūpa' nāma ye tāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]] [[synonyms:b:ba]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---except Him; [[synonyms:v:viśve]]---within this cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is none; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---further; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:viśvarūpa]]---universal form; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His.

===== Translation =====

**The gigantic universal form is called the Viśvarūpa incarnation of Mahā-saṅkarṣaṇa. Thus we do not find anything within this cosmic manifestation except the Lord Himself.**
