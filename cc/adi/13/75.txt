====== CC Ādi 13.75 ======

===== Text =====

> baladeva-prakāśa---parama-vyome 'saṅkarṣaṇa'
> teṅha---viśvera upādāna-nimitta-kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:baladeva]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation of Baladeva; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:v:vyome]]---in the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]---of the name Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---He; [[synonyms:v:viśvera]]---the cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:u:upādāna]]---ingredient; [[synonyms:n:nimitta]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---immediate cause.

===== Translation =====

**The expansion of Baladeva known as Saṅkarṣaṇa in the spiritual world is the ingredient and immediate cause of this material cosmic manifestation.**
