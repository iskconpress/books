====== CC Ādi 13.112 ======

===== Text =====

> suvarṇera kaḍi-ba-uli, rajatamudrā-pāśuli,
> suvarṇera aṅgada, kaṅkaṇa
> du-bāhute divya śaṅkha, rajatera malabaṅka,
> svarṇa-mudrāra nānā hāragaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:suvarṇera]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:k:kaḍi]]-[[synonyms:b:ba]]-[[synonyms:u:uli]]---bangles worn on the hand; [[synonyms:r:rajata]]-[[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---gold coins; [[synonyms:p:pāśuli]]---a kind of ornament covering the foot; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇera]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:a:aṅgada]]---a kind of ornament; [[synonyms:k:kaṅkaṇa]]---another kind of ornament for the hand; [[synonyms:d:du]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhute]]---in two arms; [[synonyms:d:divya]]---celestial; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---conchshell; [[synonyms:r:rajatera]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:m:malabaṅka]]---bangles for the foot; [[synonyms:s:svarṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mudrāra]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---varieties; [[synonyms:h:hāra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---necklaces.

===== Translation =====

**She brought different kinds of golden ornaments, including bangles for the hand, armlets, necklaces and anklets.**
