====== CC Ādi 13.64 ======

===== Text =====

> gītā-bhhāgavata kahe ācārya-gosāñi
> jñāna-karma nindi' kare bhaktira baḍāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gītā]]---//Bhagavad-gītā//; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---recites; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---the path of philosophical speculation; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---fruitive activity; [[synonyms:n:nindi']]---decrying; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---establishes; [[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:b:baḍāi]]---excellence.

===== Translation =====

**In these meetings of the Vaiṣṇavas, Advaita Ācārya used to recite Bhagavad-gītā and Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, decrying the paths of philosophical speculation and fruitive activity and establishing the superexcellence of devotional service.**
