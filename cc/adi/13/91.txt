====== CC Ādi 13.91 ======

===== Text =====

> a-kalaṅka gauracandra dilā daraśana
> sa-kalaṅka candre āra kon prayojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:a]]-[[synonyms:k:kalaṅka]]---without contamination; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---the moon of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---audience; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:k:kalaṅka]]---with contamination; [[synonyms:c:candre]]---for a moon; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---necessity.

===== Translation =====

**When the spotless moon of Caitanya Mahāprabhu became visible, what would be the need for a moon full of black marks on its body?**
