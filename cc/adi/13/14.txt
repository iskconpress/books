====== CC Ādi 13.14 ======

===== Text =====

> gārhasthye prabhura līlā---'ādi'-līlākhyāna
> 'madhya'- 'antya'-līlā---śeṣa-līlāra dui nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gārhasthye]]---in household life; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the original; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāna]]---has the name of; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]---middle; [[synonyms:a:antya]]---last; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---the last part of the pastimes; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---names.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of His household life are known as ādi-līlā, or the original pastimes. His later pastimes are known as madhya-līlā and antya-līlā, or the middle and final pastimes.**
