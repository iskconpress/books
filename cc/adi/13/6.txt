~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Ādi 13.6~~

====== CC Ādi 13.6 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahila granthārambhe mukha-bandha
> ebe kahi caitanya-līlā-krama-anubandha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---I have spoken; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārambhe]]---in the beginning of the book; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:b:bandha]]---preface; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:k:krama]]---the chronological order of His pastimes; [[synonyms:a:anubandha]]---as they are combined together.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have spoken the preface of Caitanya-caritāmṛta. Now I shall describe Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes in chronological order.**
