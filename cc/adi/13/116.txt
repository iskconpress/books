====== CC Ādi 13.116 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva aṅga---sunirmāṇa, suvarṇa-pratimā-bhāna,
> sarva aṅga---sulakṣaṇamaya
> bālakera divya jyoti, dekhi' pāila bahu prīti,
> vātsalyete dravila hṛdaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---all different parts of the body; [[synonyms:s:sunirmāṇa]]---well constructed; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]]---gold; [[synonyms:p:pratimā]]---form; [[synonyms:b:bhāna]]---like; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---parts of the body; [[synonyms:s:sulakṣaṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---full of auspicious signs; [[synonyms:b:bālakera]]---of the child; [[synonyms:d:divya]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:j:jyoti]]---effulgence; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:v:vātsalyete]]---by parental affection; [[synonyms:d:dravila]]---melted; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---her heart.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the transcendental bodily effulgence of the child, each of His nicely constructed limbs full of auspicious signs and resembling a form of gold, Sītā Ṭhākurāṇī was very much pleased, and because of her maternal affection, she felt as if her heart were melting.**
