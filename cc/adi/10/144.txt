====== CC Ādi 10.144 ======

===== Text =====

> bāiśa ghaḍā jala dine bharena rāmāi
> govinda-ājñāya sevā karena nandāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāiśa]]---twenty-two; [[synonyms:g:ghaḍā]]---big waterpots; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:d:dine]]---daily; [[synonyms:b:bharena]]---fills; [[synonyms:r:rāmāi]]---of the name Rāmāi; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---by the order of Govinda; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---renders; [[synonyms:n:nandāi]]---of the name Nandāi.

===== Translation =====

**Every day Rāmāi filled twenty-two big waterpots, whereas Nandāi personally assisted Govinda.**

===== Purport =====

In the //Gaura-gaṇoddeśa-dīpikā// (139) it is stated that two servants who formerly supplied milk and water to Lord Kṛṣṇa became Rāmāi and Nandāi in the pastimes of Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
