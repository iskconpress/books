====== CC Ādi 10.162 ======

===== Text =====

> eka eka śākhāra śakti ananta mahimā
> 'sahasra vadane' yāra dite nāre sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekaeka]]---of each and every; [[synonyms:ś:śākhāra]]---branch; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:s:sahasravadane]]---in thousands of mouths; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---becomes unable; [[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---limit.

===== Translation =====

**Each and every branch of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's devotees has unlimited spiritual power and glory. Even if one had thousands of mouths, it would be impossible to describe the limits of their activities.**
