====== CC Ādi 10.29 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura atyanta priya---paṇḍita gaṅgādāsa
> yāṅhāra smaraṇe haya sarva-bandha-nāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---very much; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---dear; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:g:gaṅgādāsa]]---of the name Paṇḍita Gaṅgādāsa; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---who; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇe]]---by remembering; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it becomes; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bandha]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---freedom from all kinds of bondage.

===== Translation =====

**Paṇḍita Gaṅgādāsa was the eighth dear branch of the tree of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. One who remembers his activities attains freedom from all bondage.**
