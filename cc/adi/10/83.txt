====== CC Ādi 10.83 ======

===== Text =====

> kulīnagrāmīra bhāgya kahane nā yāya
> śūkara carāya ḍoma, seha kṛṣṇa gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmīra]]---the residents of Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---fortune; [[synonyms:k:kahane]]---to speak; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is possible; [[synonyms:ś:śūkara]]---hogs; [[synonyms:c:carāya]]---tending; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍoma]]---sweeper; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---he also; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chants.

===== Translation =====

**"No one can describe the fortunate position of Kulīna-grāma. It is so sublime that even sweepers who tend their hogs there also chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra."**
