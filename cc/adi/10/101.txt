====== CC Ādi 10.101 ======

===== Text =====

> tina sandhyā rādhā-kuṇḍe apatita snāna
> vraja-vāsī vaiṣṇave kare āliṅgana māna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]---three times, namely morning, evening and noon; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍe]]---in the lake of Rādhā-kuṇḍa; [[synonyms:a:apatita]]---without failure; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---taking bath; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---inhabitants of Vrajabhūmi; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇave]]---all devotees; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:m:māna]]---and offering respect.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī took three baths daily in the Rādhā-kuṇḍa lake. As soon as he found a Vaiṣṇava residing in Vṛndāvana, he would embrace him and give him all respect.**
