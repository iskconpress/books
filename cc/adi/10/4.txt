====== CC Ādi 10.4 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñira yata pāriṣada-caya
> guru-laghu-bhāva tāṅra nā haya niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of the supreme spiritual master; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]-[[synonyms:c:caya]]---groups of associates; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:l:laghu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---conceptions of high and low; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of them; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---never; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---ascertained.

===== Translation =====

**The associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were many, but none of them should be considered lower or higher. This cannot be ascertained.**
