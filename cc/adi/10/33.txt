====== CC Ādi 10.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra anuja śākhā---śaṅkara-paṇḍita
> 'prabhu-pādopādhāna' yāṅra nāma vidita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his (Dāmodara Paṇḍita's); [[synonyms:a:anuja]]---younger brother; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---the eleventh branch; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---of the name Śaṅkara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord's; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:u:upadhāna]]---shoes; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:v:vidita]]---celebrated.

===== Translation =====

**The eleventh branch, the younger brother of Dāmodara Paṇḍita, was known as Śaṅkara Paṇḍita. He was celebrated as the shoes of the Lord.**
