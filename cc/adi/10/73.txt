====== CC Ādi 10.73 ======

===== Text =====

> vanamālī paṇḍita śākhā vikhyāta jagate
> soṇāra muṣala hala dekhila prabhura hāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vanamālī]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---of the name Vanamālī Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---the next branch; [[synonyms:v:vikhyāta]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in the world; [[synonyms:s:soṇāra]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:m:muṣala]]---club; [[synonyms:h:hala]]---plow; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand.

===== Translation =====

**Vanamālī Paṇḍita, the thirty-fifth branch of the tree, was very celebrated in this world. He saw a golden club and plow in the hands of the Lord.**

===== Purport =====

Vanamālī Paṇḍita saw Lord Caitanya in the ecstasy of Balarāma. This is described vividly in the //Caitanya-bhāgavata, Antya-līlā,// Chapter Nine.
