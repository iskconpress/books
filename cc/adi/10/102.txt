====== Cc. Ādi 10.102 ======

===== Text =====

> sārdha sapta-prahara kare bhaktira sādhane
> cāri daṇḍa nidrā, seha nahe kona-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārdha]]---one and a half hours; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:p:prahara]]---seven //praharas// (twenty-one hours); [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:s:sādhane]]---in execution; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---about two hours; [[synonyms:n:nidrā]]---sleeping; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that also; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kona]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---some days.

===== Translation =====

**He engaged himself in devotional service for more than twenty-two and a half hours a day, and for less than two hours he slept, although on some days that also was not possible.**
