====== CC Ādi 10.99 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra daṇḍavat kare, laya lakṣa nāma
> dui sahasra vaiṣṇavere nitya paraṇāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousand; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---takes; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]---one hundred thousand; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---holy names; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousand; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavere]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:p:paraṇāma]]---obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**As a daily duty, he regularly offered one thousand obeisances to the Lord, chanted at least one hundred thousand holy names and offered obeisances to two thousand Vaiṣṇavas.**
