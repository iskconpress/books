====== Cc. Ādi 10.68 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu yāṅra nitya laya thoḍa-mocā-phala
> yāṅra phuṭā-lauhapātre prabhu pilā jala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---takes; [[synonyms:t:thoḍa]]---the pulp of the banana tree; [[synonyms:m:mocā]]---the flowers of the banana tree; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---the fruits of the banana tree; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:p:phuṭā]]---broken; [[synonyms:l:lauha]]-[[synonyms:p:pātre]]---in the iron pot; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:p:pilā]]---drank; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water.

===== Translation =====

**Every day Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu jokingly snatched fruits, flowers and pulp from Śrīdhara and drank from his broken iron pot.**
