====== Cc. Ādi 10.143 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmāi-nandāi---doṅhe prabhura kiṅkara
> govindera saṅge sevā kare nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmāi]]-[[synonyms:n:nandāi]]---of the names Rāmāi and Nandāi; [[synonyms:d:doṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Lord Caitanya's; [[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---servants; [[synonyms:g:govindera]]---with Govinda; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---rendered; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---twenty-four hours a day.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmāi and Nandāi, the twentieth and twenty-first among the important devotees in Jagannātha Purī, always assisted Govinda twenty-four hours a day in rendering service to the Lord.**
