====== CC Ādi 10.108 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha ācārya prabhura priya dāsa
> prabhura ājñāte teṅho kaila gaṅgā-vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of the name Jagannātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:priya]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---very dear servant; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāte]]---by the order of the Lord; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---agreed; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---living on the bank of the Ganges.

===== Translation =====

**Jagannātha Ācārya, the fiftieth branch of the Caitanya tree, was an extremely dear servant of the Lord, by whose order he decided to live on the bank of the Ganges.**

===== Purport =====

Jagannātha Ācārya is stated in the //Gaura-gaṇoddeśa-dīpikā// (111) to have formerly been Durvāsā of Nidhuvana.
