====== CC Ādi 10.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-nityānanda
> jayādvaitacandra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jayaadvaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---all glories to Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---to the devotees of Lord Caitanya, headed by Śrīvāsa.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda! All glories to Advaita Prabhu, and all glories to the devotees of Lord Caitanya, headed by Śrīvāsa!**
