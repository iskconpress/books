====== CC Ādi 10.58 ======

===== Text =====

> 'pradyumna brahmacārī' tāṅra āge nāma chila
> 'nṛsiṁhānanda' nāma prabhu pāche ta' rākhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pradyumna]] [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]]---of the name Pradyumna Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---previously; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---was; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhānanda]]---of the name Nṛsiṁhānanda; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---afterward; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept it.

===== Translation =====

**The former Pradyumna Brahmacārī was given the name Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
