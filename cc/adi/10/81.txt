====== CC Ādi 10.81 ======

===== Text =====

> vāṇīnātha vasu ādi yata grāmī jana
> sabei caitanya-bhṛtya,---caitanya-prāṇadhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]] [[synonyms:v:vasu]]---of the name Vāṇīnātha Vasu; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---heading the list; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:g:grāmī]]---of the village; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---inhabitants; [[synonyms:s:sabei]]---all of them; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---servants of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---their life and soul was Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma village, headed by Vāṇīnātha Vasu, were servants of Lord Caitanya, who was their only life and wealth.**
