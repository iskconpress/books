====== CC Ādi 10.5 ======

===== Text =====

> yata yata mahānta kailā tāṅ-sabāra gaṇana
> keha karibāre nāre jyeṣṭha-laghu-krama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yatayata]]---as many as there are; [[synonyms:m:mahānta]]---great devotees; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:karibārenāre]]---can not do; [[synonyms:j:jyeṣṭha]]---elder; [[synonyms:l:laghu]]---younger; [[synonyms:k:krama]]---chronology.

===== Translation =====

**All the great personalities in the line of Lord Caitanya enumerated these devotees, but they could not distinguish between the greater and the lesser.**
