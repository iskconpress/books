====== CC Ādi 10.141 ======

===== Text =====

> aṅga-sevā govindere dilena īśvara
> jagannātha dekhite calena āge kāśīśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---taking care of the body; [[synonyms:g:govindere]]---unto Govinda; [[synonyms:d:dilena]]---He gave; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---the Jagannātha Deity; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---while going to visit; [[synonyms:c:calena]]---goes; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---of the name Kāśīśvara.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda cared for the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, whereas Kāśīśvara went in front of the Lord when He went to see Jagannātha in the temple.**
