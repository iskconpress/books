====== CC Ādi 10.163 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahila mahāprabhura bhakta-gaṇa
> samagra balite nāre 'sahasra-vadana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---described; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:s:samagra]]---all; [[synonyms:b:balite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---cannot; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---Lord Śeṣa, who has thousands of mouths.

===== Translation =====

**I have briefly described the devotees of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in different places. Even Lord Śeṣa, who has thousands of mouths, could not list them all.**
