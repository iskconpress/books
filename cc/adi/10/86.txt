====== CC Ādi 10.86 ======

===== Text =====

> mālīra icchāya śākhā bahuta bāḍila
> bāḍiyā paścima deśa saba ācchādila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālīra]] [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---on the desire of the gardener; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---branches; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---many; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---expanded; [[synonyms:b:bāḍiyā]]---so expanding; [[synonyms:p:paścima]]---western; [[synonyms:d:deśa]]---countries; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:ācchādila]]---covered.

===== Translation =====

**By the will of the supreme gardener, the branches of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī grew many times over, expanding throughout the western countries and covering the entire region.**
