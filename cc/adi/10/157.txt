====== CC Ādi 10.157 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ājñā pāñā vṛndāvanere āilā
> āsiyā śrī-rūpa-gosāñira nikaṭe rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---receiving; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanere]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---he came; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming there; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---at his shelter; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained.

===== Translation =====

**Later, by the order of Lord Caitanya, Raghunātha went to Vṛndāvana and remained there under the shelter of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.**
