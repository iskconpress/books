====== CC Ādi 10.76 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnātha siṁha---eka caitanyera dāsa
> akrūra bali' prabhu yāṅre kailā parihāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]] [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]---of the name of Gopīnātha Siṁha; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:c:caitanyeradāsa]]---servant of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:a:akrūrabali']]---famous as Akrūra; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---whom; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]]---joking.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Siṁha, the thirty-eighth branch of the tree, was a faithful servant of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The Lord jokingly addressed him as Akrūra.**

===== Purport =====

Actually he was Akrūra, as stated in the //Gaura-gaṇoddeśa-dīpikā,// verse 117.
