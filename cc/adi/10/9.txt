====== CC Ādi 10.9 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīpati, śrīnidhi---tāṅra dui sahodara
> cāri bhāira dāsa-dāsī, gṛha-parikara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīpati]]---of the name Śrīpati; [[synonyms:ś:śrīnidhi]]---of the name Śrīnidhi; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---their; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:s:sahodara]]---own brothers; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four; [[synonyms:b:bhāira]]---brothers; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsī]]---family members, manservants and maidservants; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:p:parikara]]---all counted in one family.

===== Translation =====

**Their two brothers were named Śrīpati and Śrīnidhi. These four brothers and their servants and maidservants are considered one big branch.**
