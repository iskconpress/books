====== Cc. Ādi 10.44 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra ananta guṇa---kahi diṅmātra
> ācārya gosāñi yāṅre bhuñjāya śrāddha-pātra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura's; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak; [[synonyms:d:diṅ]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only a small part; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Advaita Ācārya Prabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---to whom; [[synonyms:b:bhuñjāya]]---offered to eat; [[synonyms:ś:śrāddha]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---//prasāda// offered to Lord Viṣṇu.

===== Translation =====

**There was no end to the transcendental qualities of Haridāsa Ṭhākura. Here I mention but a fraction of his qualities. He was so exalted that Advaita Gosvāmī, when performing the śrāddha ceremony of his father, offered him the first plate.**
