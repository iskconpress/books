====== Cc. Ādi 10.149 ======

===== Text =====

> siṅgābhaṭṭa, kāmābhaṭṭa, dantura śivānanda
> gauḍe pūrva bhṛtya prabhura priya kamalānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṅgābhaṭṭa]]---of the name Siṅgābhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:kāmābhaṭṭa]]---of the name Kāmābhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:d:dantura]] [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---of the name Dantura Śivānanda; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]---formerly; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---servant; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---very dear; [[synonyms:k:kamalānanda]]---of the name Kamalānanda.

===== Translation =====

**Siṅgābhaṭṭa was the thirty-first, Kāmābhaṭṭa the thirty-second, Śivānanda the thirty-third and Kamalānanda the thirty-fourth. They all formerly served Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in Bengal, but later these servants left Bengal to live with the Lord in Jagannātha Purī.**
