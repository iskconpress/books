====== CC Ādi 10.95 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' niścaya kari' āila vṛndāvane
> āsi' rūpa-sanātanera vandila caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---having decided; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vandila]]---offered respects; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrīla Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī came to Vṛndāvana, visited Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī and offered them his obeisances.**
