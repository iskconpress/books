====== CC Ādi 10.46 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho siddhi pāile tāṅra deha lañā kole
> nācila caitanya-prabhu mahā-kutūhale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:s:siddhi]]---perfection; [[synonyms:p:pāile]]---after achieving; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---body; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kole]]---on the lap; [[synonyms:n:nācila]]---danced; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kutūhale]]---in great ecstasy.

===== Translation =====

**After the passing away of Haridāsa Ṭhākura, the Lord Himself took his body on His lap, and He danced with it in great ecstasy.**
