====== CC Ādi 10.22 ======

===== Text =====

> prītye karite cāhe prabhura lālana-pālana
> vairāgya-loka-bhaye prabhu nā māne kakhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prītye]]---in intimacy or affection; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wanted; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---the Lord's; [[synonyms:l:lālana]]-[[synonyms:p:pālana]]---maintenance; [[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---renouncement; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---fearing the public; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---did not; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---accept; [[synonyms:k:kakhana]]---any time.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita [as an incarnation of Satyabhāmā] always wanted to see to the comfort of Lord Caitanya, but since the Lord was a sannyāsī He did not accept the luxuries that Jagadānanda Paṇḍita offered.**
