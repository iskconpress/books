====== CC Ādi 10.121 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍa-deśa-bhaktera kaila saṅkṣepa kathana
> ananta caitanya-bhakta nā yāya gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---I have described; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepa]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---narration; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---can be; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counted.

===== Translation =====

**I have given a brief description of the devotees of Lord Caitanya in Bengal. Actually His devotees are innumerable.**
