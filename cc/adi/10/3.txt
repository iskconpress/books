====== Cc. Ādi 10.3 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mālīra---ei vṛkṣera akathya kathana
> ebe śuna mukhya-śākhāra nāma-vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mālīra]]---of this gardener; [[synonyms:e:eivṛkṣera]]---of this tree; [[synonyms:a:akathyakathana]]---inconceivable description; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---chief; [[synonyms:ś:śākhāra]]---branches; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the names; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**The description of Lord Caitanya as the gardener and the tree is inconceivable. Now hear with attention about the branches of this tree.**
