====== CC Ādi 10.147 ======

===== Text =====

> baḍa haridāsa, āra choṭa haridāsa
> dui kīrtanīyā rahe mahāprabhura pāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---of the name Baḍa Haridāsa; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:c:choṭaharidāsa]]---of the name Choṭa Haridāsa; [[synonyms:d:duikīrtanīyā]]---both of them were good singers; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---stay; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---with.

===== Translation =====

**Baḍa Haridāsa and Choṭa Haridāsa, the twenty-fourth and twenty-fifth devotees in Nīlācala, were good singers who always accompanied Lord Caitanya.**

===== Purport =====

Choṭa Haridāsa was later banished from the company of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, as stated in the //Antya-līlā,// Chapter Two.
