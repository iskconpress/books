====== CC Ādi 10.133 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda rāya, paṭṭanāyaka gopīnātha
> kalānidhi, sudhānidhi, nāyaka vāṇīnātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---of the name Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:paṭṭanāyaka]] [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---of the name Paṭṭanāyaka Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:k:kalānidhi]]---of the name Kalānidhi; [[synonyms:s:sudhānidhi]]---of the name Sudhānidhi; [[synonyms:n:nāyakavāṇīnātha]]---of the name Nāyaka Vāṇīnātha.

===== Translation =====

**The five sons of Bhavānanda Rāya were Rāmānanda Rāya, Paṭṭanāyaka Gopīnātha, Kalānidhi, Sudhānidhi and Nāyaka Vāṇīnātha.**
