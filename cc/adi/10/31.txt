====== CC Ādi 10.31 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara-paṇḍita śākhā premete pracaṇḍa
> prabhura upare yeṅho kaila vākya-daṇḍa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---of the name Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---another branch (the tenth branch); [[synonyms:p:premete]]---in affection; [[synonyms:p:pracaṇḍa]]---very much advanced; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon; [[synonyms:y:yeṅho]]---he who; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---chastisement by speaking.

===== Translation =====

**Dāmodara Paṇḍita, the tenth branch of the Caitanya tree, was so elevated in love of Lord Caitanya that he once unhesitatingly chastised the Lord with strong words.**
