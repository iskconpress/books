====== CC Ādi 10.87 ======

===== Text =====

> ā-sindhunadī-tīra āra himālaya
> vṛndāvana-mathurādi yata tīrtha haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ā]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:n:nadī]]---to the border of the river Sindhu; [[synonyms:t:tīra]]---border; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:h:himālaya]]---the Himalayan Mountains; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---of the name Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]---of the name Mathurā; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---heading the list; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are.

===== Translation =====

**Extending to the borders of the river Sindhu and the Himalayan Mountain valleys, these two branches expanded throughout India, including all the places of pilgrimage, such as Vṛndāvana, Mathurā and Haridvāra.**
