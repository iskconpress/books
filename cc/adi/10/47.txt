====== CC Ādi 10.47 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra līlā varṇiyāchena vṛndāvana-dāsa
> yebā avaśiṣṭa, āge kariba prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:v:varṇiyāchena]]---described; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:y:yebā]]---whatever; [[synonyms:a:avaśiṣṭa]]---remained undescribed; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---later in the book; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall make; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifest.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura vividly described the pastimes of Haridāsa Ṭhākura in his Caitanya-bhāgavata. Whatever has remained undescribed I shall try to explain later in this book.**
