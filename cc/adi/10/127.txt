====== Cc. Ādi 10.127 ======

===== Text =====

> ityādika pūrva-saṅgī baḍa bhakta-gaṇa
> nīlācale rahi' kare prabhura sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādika]]---all these and others; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅgī]]---former associates; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---great devotees; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service.

===== Translation =====

**All these devotees were associates of the Lord from the very beginning, and when the Lord took up residence in Jagannātha Purī they remained there to serve Him faithfully.**
