====== CC Ādi 10.161 ======

===== Text =====

> sakala bhariyā āche prema-phula-phale
> bhāsāila tri-jagat kṛṣṇa-prema-jale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---filled; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:phula]]---flowers; [[synonyms:p:phale]]---fruits; [[synonyms:b:bhāsāila]]---inundated; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jale]]---with water.

===== Translation =====

**Every branch and subbranch of the tree is full of innumerable fruits and flowers. They inundate the world with the waters of love of Kṛṣṇa.**
