====== CC Ādi 10.142 ======

===== Text =====

> aparaśa yāya gosāñi manuṣya-gahane
> manuṣya ṭheli' patha kare kāśī balavāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparaśa]]---untouched; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]-[[synonyms:g:gahane]]---in the crowd; [[synonyms:m:manuṣyaṭheli']]---pushing the crowd of men; [[synonyms:p:pathakare]]---clears the way; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:b:balavāne]]---very strong.

===== Translation =====

**When Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the temple of Jagannātha, Kāśīśvara, being very strong, cleared the crowds aside with his hands so that Caitanya Mahāprabhu could pass untouched.**
