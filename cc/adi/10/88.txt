====== CC Ādi 10.88 ======

===== Text =====

> dui śākhāra prema-phale sakala bhāsila
> prema-phalāsvāde loka unmatta ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:ś:śākhāra]]---of the two branches; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:phale]]---by the fruit of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhāsila]]---became overflooded; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---the fruit of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:ā:āsvāde]]---by tasting; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all people; [[synonyms:u:unmatta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**The fruits of love of Godhead which fructified on these two branches were distributed in abundance. Tasting these fruits, everyone became mad after them.**
