====== CC Ādi 10.27 ======

===== Text =====

> vāra-māsa tāhā prabhu karena aṅgīkāra
> 'rāghavera jhāli' bali' prasiddhi yāhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāra]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---the whole year; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---all those foods; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---did; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---accept; [[synonyms:r:rāghavera]] [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]---the bags of Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---so called; [[synonyms:p:prasiddhi]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of which.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord accepted these foods throughout the entire year. Those bags are still celebrated as rāghavera jhāli ["the bags of Rāghava Paṇḍita"].**
