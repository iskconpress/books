====== CC Ādi 10.10 ======

===== Text =====

> dui śākhāra upaśākhāya tāṅ-sabāra gaṇana
> yāṅra gṛhe mahāprabhura sadā saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:ś:śākhāra]]---of the two branches; [[synonyms:u:upaśākhāya]]---on the subbranches; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in whose house; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---congregational chanting.

===== Translation =====

**There is no counting the subbranches of these two branches. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu held congregational chanting daily at the house of Śrīvāsa Paṇḍita.**
