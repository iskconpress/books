====== CC Ādi 10.117 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ājñāya nityānanda gauḍe calilā
> tāṅra saṅge tina-jana prabhu-ājñāya āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---under the order of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went back; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in His company; [[synonyms:t:tinajana]]---three men; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---under the order of the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**By the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, three devotees accompanied Lord Nityānanda Prabhu when He returned to Bengal to preach.**
