====== Cc. Ādi 10.69 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura atipriya dāsa bhagavān paṇḍita
> yāṅra dehe kṛṣṇa pūrve hailā adhiṣṭhita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:atipriya]]---very dear; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servant; [[synonyms:b:bhagavānpaṇḍita]]---of the name Bhagavān Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the body; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:a:adhiṣṭhita]]---established.

===== Translation =====

**The thirtieth branch was Bhagavān Paṇḍita. He was an extremely dear servant of the Lord, but even previously he was a great devotee of Lord Kṛṣṇa who always kept the Lord within his heart.**
