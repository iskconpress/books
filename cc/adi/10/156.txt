====== CC Ādi 10.156 ======

===== Text =====

> baḍa haile nīlācale gelā prabhura sthāne
> aṣṭa-māsa rahila bhikṣā dena kona dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:h:haile]]---when he grew to be a young man; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---eight months; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---stayed; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gave; [[synonyms:k:konadine]]---some days.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghunātha grew to be a young man, he visited Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Jagannātha Purī and stayed there for eight months. Sometimes he offered prasāda to the Lord.**
