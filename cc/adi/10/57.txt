====== Cc. Ādi 10.57 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sākṣāte' sakala bhakta dekhe nirviśeṣa
> nakula brahmacāri-dehe prabhura 'āveśa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sākṣāte]]---directly; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---see; [[synonyms:n:nirviśeṣa]]---nothing peculiar but as He is; [[synonyms:n:nakulabrahmacārī]]---of the name Nakula Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the body; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---the Lord's; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---symptoms of power.

===== Translation =====

**The appearance of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in every devotee's presence is called sākṣāt. His appearance in Nakula Brahmacārī as a symptom of special prowess is an example of āveśa.**
