====== CC Ādi 10.139 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra siddhi-kāle doṅhe tāṅra ājñā pāñā
> nīlācale prabhu-sthāne milila āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:siddhi]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time of Īśvara Purī's passing away; [[synonyms:d:doṅhe]]---the two of them; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milila]]---met; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming there.

===== Translation =====

**In the list of prominent devotees at Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī], Kāśīśvara was the eighteenth and Govinda the nineteenth. They both came to see Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Jagannātha Purī, being thus ordered by Īśvara Purī at the time of his passing away.**
