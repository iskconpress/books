====== CC Ādi 10.118 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmadāsa, mādhava, āra vāsudeva ghoṣa
> prabhu-saṅge rahe govinda pāiyā santoṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmadāsa]]---of the name Rāmadāsa; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]---of the name Mādhava; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vāsudevaghoṣa]]---of the name Vāsudeva Ghoṣa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the company of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---of the name Govinda; [[synonyms:p:pāiyā]]---feeling; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---great satisfaction.

===== Translation =====

**These three were Rāmadāsa, Mādhava Ghoṣa and Vāsudeva Ghoṣa. Govinda Ghoṣa, however, remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Jagannātha Purī and thus felt great satisfaction.**
