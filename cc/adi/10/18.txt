~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Ādi 10.18~~

====== CC Ādi 10.18 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane mahāprabhu gāya yāṅra nṛtya-kāle
> prabhura caraṇa dhari' vakreśvara bale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sang; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time of dancing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---embracing; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---said.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally sang while Vakreśvara Paṇḍita danced, and thus Vakreśvara Paṇḍita fell at the lotus feet of the Lord and spoke as follows.**
