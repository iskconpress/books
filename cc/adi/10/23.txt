====== CC Ādi 10.23 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-jane khaṭmaṭi lāgāya kondala
> tāṅra prītyera kathā āge kahiba sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---two persons; [[synonyms:k:khaṭmaṭi]]---fighting over trifles; [[synonyms:l:lāgāya]]---continued; [[synonyms:k:kondala]]---quarrel; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:prītyera]]---affection; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---narration; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:k:kahiba]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**They sometimes appeared to fight over trifles, but these quarrels were based on their affection, of which I shall speak later.**
