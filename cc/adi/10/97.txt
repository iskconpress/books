====== Cc. Ādi 10.97 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura līlā yata bāhira-antara
> dui bhāi tāṅra mukhe śune nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bāhira]]---external; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---internal; [[synonyms:d:duibhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hear; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always.

===== Translation =====

**Because Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī was an assistant to Svarūpa Dāmodara, he knew much about the external and internal features of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya. Thus the two brothers Rūpa and Sanātana always used to hear of this from him.**
