====== CC Ādi 10.116 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmadāsa abhirāma---sakhya-premarāśi
> ṣolasāṅgera kāṣṭha tuli' ye karila vāṅśī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmadāsaabhirāma]]---of the name Rāmadāsa Abhirāma; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---friendship; [[synonyms:r:rāśi]]---great volume; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣolasa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅgera]]---of sixteen knots; [[synonyms:k:kaṣṭha]]---wood; [[synonyms:t:tuli']]---lifting; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---one who; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:v:vāṅśī]]---flute.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmadāsa Abhirāma was fully absorbed in the mellow of friendship. He made a flute of a bamboo stick with sixteen knots.**

===== Purport =====

Abhirāma was an inhabitant of Khānākula-kṛṣṇa-nagara.
