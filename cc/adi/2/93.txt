====== Cc. Ādi 2.93 ======

===== Text =====

> āśraya jānite kahi e nava padārtha
> e navera utpatti-hetu sei āśrayārtha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the ultimate shelter; [[synonyms:j:jānite]]---to know; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I discuss; [[synonyms:e:e]]---these; [[synonyms:n:nava]]---nine; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---categories; [[synonyms:e:e]]---these; [[synonyms:n:navera]]---of the nine; [[synonyms:u:utpatti]]---of the origin; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---cause; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---of the shelter; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning.

===== Translation =====

**"To know distinctly the ultimate shelter of everything that be, I have described the other nine categories. The cause for the appearance of these nine is rightly called their shelter.**
