====== CC Ādi 2.82 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva 'kṛṣṇa'-śabda āge anuvāda
> 'svayaṁ-bhagavattā' piche vidheya-saṁvāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabda]]---the word //kṛṣṇa//; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---first; [[synonyms:a:anuvāda]]---the subject; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavattā]]---being Himself the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:piche]]---afterwards; [[synonyms:v:vidheya]]---of the predicate; [[synonyms:s:saṁvāda]]---the message.

===== Translation =====

**"Therefore first the word 'kṛṣṇa' appears as the subject, followed by the predicate, describing Him as the original Personality of Godhead.**
