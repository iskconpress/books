====== CC Ādi 2.111 ======

===== Text =====

> sei ta' bhaktera vākya nahe vyabhicārī
> sakala sambhave tāṅte, yāte avatārī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of a devotee; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---speech; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:v:vyabhicārī]]---deviation; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sambhave]]---possibilities; [[synonyms:t:tāṅte]]---in Him; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---since; [[synonyms:a:avatārī]]---the source of all incarnations.

===== Translation =====

**But such words from the lips of a sincere devotee cannot be false. All possibilities abide in Him, for He is the primeval Lord.**
