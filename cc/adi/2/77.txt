====== Cc. Ādi 2.77 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche kahi,---ei vipra parama paṇḍita
> vipra---anuvāda, ihāra vidheya---pāṇḍitya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I say; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---great; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned man; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:a:anuvāda]]---subject; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:v:vidheya]]---predicate; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍitya]]---erudition.

===== Translation =====

**"For example, we may say: 'This vipra is a greatly learned man.' In this sentence, the vipra is the subject, and the predicate is his erudition.**
