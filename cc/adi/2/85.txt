====== CC Ādi 2.85 ======

===== Text =====

> nārāyaṇa aṁśī yei svayaṁ-bhagavān
> teṅha śrī-kṛṣṇa---aiche karita vyākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:a:aṁśī]]---the source of all incarnations; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---who; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Himself the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---He; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in such away; [[synonyms:k:karita]]---would have made; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus he would have said: 'Nārāyaṇa, the source of all incarnations, is the original Personality of Godhead. He has appeared as Śrī Kṛṣṇa.'**
