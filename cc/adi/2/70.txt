====== CC Ādi 2.70 ======

===== Text =====

> avatāra saba---puruṣera kalā, aṁśa
> svayaṁ-bhagavān kṛṣṇa sarva-avataṁsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnations; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:p:puruṣera]]---of the //puruṣa-avatāras//; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]---parts of plenary portions; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---plenary portions; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---of all; [[synonyms:a:avataṁsa]]---crest.

===== Translation =====

**All the incarnations of Godhead are plenary portions or parts of the plenary portions of the puruṣa-avatāras, but the primeval Lord is Śrī Kṛṣṇa. He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the fountainhead of all incarnations.**
