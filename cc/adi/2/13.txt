====== CC Ādi 2.13 ======

===== Text =====

> carma-cakṣe dekhe yaiche sūrya nirviśeṣa
> jñāna-mārge laite nāre kṛṣṇera viśeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:carma]]-[[synonyms:c:cakṣe]]---by the naked eye; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---one sees; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:s:sūrya]]---the sun; [[synonyms:n:nirviśeṣa]]---without variegatedness; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---by the path of philosophical speculation; [[synonyms:l:laite]]---to accept; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---not able; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---the variety.

===== Translation =====

**As with the naked eye one cannot know the sun except as a glowing substance, merely by philosophical speculation one cannot understand Lord Kṛṣṇa's transcendental varieties.**
