====== CC Ādi 2.73 ======

===== Text =====

> tāre kahe---kene kara kutarkānumāna
> śāstra-viruddhārtha kabhu nā haya pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāre]]---to him; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---one says; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you make; [[synonyms:k:ku]]-[[synonyms:t:tarka]]---of a fallacious argument; [[synonyms:a:anumāna]]---conjecture; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:v:viruddha]]---contrary to scripture; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---a meaning; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---evidence.

===== Translation =====

**To such a misguided interpreter we may reply, "Why should you suggest such fallacious logic? An interpretation is never accepted as evidence if it opposes the principles of scripture.**
