====== Cc. Ādi 2.81 ======

===== Text =====

> taiche kṛṣṇa avatāra-bhitare haila jñāta
> tāṅhāra viśeṣa-jñāna sei avijñāta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:taiche]]---in the same way; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---among the incarnations; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:j:jñāta]]---known; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---specific knowledge; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:a:avijñāta]]---unknown.

===== Translation =====

**"In the same way, when Kṛṣṇa was first counted among the incarnations, specific knowledge about Him was still unknown.**
