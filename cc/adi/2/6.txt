====== CC Ādi 2.6 ======

===== Text =====

> brahma, ātmā, bhagavān---anuvāda tina
> aṅga-prabhā, aṁśa, svarūpa---tina vidheya-cihna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the impersonal Brahman; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---the localized Paramātmā; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:anuvāda]]---subjects; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhā]]---bodily effulgence; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---partial manifestation; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---original form; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:v:vidheya]]-[[synonyms:c:cihna]]---predicates.

===== Translation =====

**Impersonal Brahman, localized Paramātmā and the Personality of Godhead are three subjects, and the glowing effulgence, the partial manifestation and the original form are their three respective predicates.**
