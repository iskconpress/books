====== CC Ādi 2.88 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅra bhagavattā haite anyera bhagavattā
> 'svayaṁ-bhagavān'-śabdera tāhātei sattā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:b:bhagavattā]]---the quality of being the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:a:anyera]]---of others; [[synonyms:b:bhagavattā]]---the quality of being the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabdera]]---of the word //svayaṁ-bhagavān//; [[synonyms:t:tāhātei]]---in that; [[synonyms:s:sattā]]---the presence.

===== Translation =====

**"Only the Personality of Godhead, the source of all other Divinities, is eligible to be designated svayaṁ bhagavān, or the primeval Lord.**
