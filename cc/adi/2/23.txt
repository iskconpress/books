====== Cc. Ādi 2.23 ======

===== Text =====

> para-vyomete vaise nārāyaṇa nāma
> ṣaḍ-aiśvarya-pūrṇa lakṣmī-kānta bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyomete]]---in the transcendental world; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---sits; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the name; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---of six kinds of opulences; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---full; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:k:kānta]]---the husband of the goddess of opulence; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nārāyaṇa, who dominates the transcendental world, is full in six opulences. He is the Personality of Godhead, the Lord of the goddess of fortune.**
