====== CC Ādi 2.83 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera svayaṁ-bhagavattā---ihā haila sādhya
> svayaṁ-bhagavānera kṛṣṇatva haila bādhya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavattā]]---the quality of being Himself the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]---to be established; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavānera]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇatva]]---the quality of being Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:b:bādhya]]---obligatory.

===== Translation =====

**"This establishes that Śrī Kṛṣṇa is the original Personality of Godhead. The original Personality of Godhead is therefore necessarily Kṛṣṇa.**
