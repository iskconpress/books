====== CC Ādi 2.68 ======

===== Text =====

> saba avatārera kari sāmānya-lakṣaṇa
> tāra madhye kṛṣṇa-candrera karila gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of the incarnations; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---making; [[synonyms:s:sāmānya]]---general; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---symptoms; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of them; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:candrera]]---of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhāgavatam describes the symptoms and deeds of the incarnations in general and counts Śrī Kṛṣṇa among them.**
