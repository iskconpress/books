{{description>"O my Lord, O Supreme Personality of Godhead! Kindly hear my third reason. There are countless universes and fathomless transcendental Vaikuṇṭhas.}}

====== Cc. Ādi 2.43 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīya kāraṇa śuna śrī-bhagavān
> ananta brahmāṇḍa bahu vaikuṇṭhādi dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]]---third; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---reason; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---O Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:a:aṇḍa]]---universes; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Vaikuṇṭha, etc.; [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---planets.

===== Translation =====

**"O my Lord, O Supreme Personality of Godhead! Kindly hear my third reason. There are countless universes and fathomless transcendental Vaikuṇṭhas.**
