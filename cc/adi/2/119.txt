====== Cc. Ādi 2.119 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-prabhura mahimā kahibāra tare
> kṛṣṇera mahimā kahi kariyā vistāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories; [[synonyms:k:kahibāra]] [[synonyms:t:tare]]---for the purpose of speaking; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:v:vistāre]]---in expansion.

===== Translation =====

**Just to enunciate the glories of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, I have tried to describe the glories of Śrī Kṛṣṇa in detail.**
