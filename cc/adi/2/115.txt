====== CC Ādi 2.115 ======

===== Text =====

> keho kahe, para-vyome nārāyaṇa hari
> sakala sambhave kṛṣṇe, yāte avatārī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keho]]---someone; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyome]]---in the transcendental world; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sakalasambhave]]---all possibilities; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---in Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---since; [[synonyms:a:avatārī]]---the source of all incarnations.

===== Translation =====

**Some call Him Hari, or the Nārāyaṇa of the transcendental world. Everything is possible in Kṛṣṇa, for He is the primeval Lord.**
