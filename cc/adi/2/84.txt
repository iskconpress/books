====== CC Ādi 2.84 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa yadi aṁśa haita, aṁśī nārāyaṇa
> tabe viparīta haita sūtera vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---plenary portion; [[synonyms:h:haita]]---were; [[synonyms:a:aṁśī]]---the source of all expansions; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:v:viparīta]]---the reverse; [[synonyms:h:haita]]---would have been; [[synonyms:s:sūtera]]---of Sūta Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the statement.

===== Translation =====

**"Had Kṛṣṇa been the plenary portion and Nārāyaṇa the primeval Lord, the statement of Sūta Gosvāmī would have been reversed.**
