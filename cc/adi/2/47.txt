====== CC Ādi 2.47 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa kahena---brahmā, tomāra nā bujhi vacana
> jīva-hṛdi, jale vaise sei nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---O Brahmā; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:b:bujhi]]---I understand; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---speech; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---of the living entity; [[synonyms:h:hṛdi]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:j:jale]]---in the water; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---sits; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Kṛṣṇa said, "Brahmā, I cannot understand what you are saying. Lord Nārāyaṇa is He who sits in the hearts of all living beings and lies down in the waters of the Kāraṇa Ocean."**
