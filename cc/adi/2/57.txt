====== Cc. Ādi 2.57 ======

===== Text =====

> sei tinera aṁśī paravyoma-nārāyaṇa
> teṅha tomāra vilāsa, tumi mūla-nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---these; [[synonyms:t:tinera]]---of the three; [[synonyms:a:aṁśī]]---source; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyoma]]---in the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---He; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastime expansion; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---original; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"The source of these three features is the Nārāyaṇa in the spiritual sky. He is Your vilāsa expansion. Therefore You are the ultimate Nārāyaṇa."**
