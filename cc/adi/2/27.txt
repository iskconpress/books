====== CC Ādi 2.27 ======

===== Text =====

> upāsanā-bhede jāni īśvara-mahimā
> ataeva sūrya tāṅra diyeta upamā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upāsanā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhede]]---by the different paths of worship; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I know; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---greatness; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:sūrya]]---the sun; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:d:diyeta]]---was given; [[synonyms:u:upamā]]---simile.

===== Translation =====

**Thus one may understand the glories of the Lord through different modes of worship, as the example of the sun illustrates.**
