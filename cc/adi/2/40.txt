====== CC Ādi 2.40 ======

===== Text =====

> jīvera īśvara---puruṣādi avatāra
> tāṅhā sabā haite tomāra aiśvarya apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of the living beings; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---//puruṣa// incarnations, etc.; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---Them; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---opulences; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---boundless.

===== Translation =====

**"The direct Lords of the living beings are the puruṣa incarnations. But Your opulence and power are more exalted than Theirs.**
