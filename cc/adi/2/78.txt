====== CC Ādi 2.78 ======

===== Text =====

> vipratva vikhyāta tāra pāṇḍitya ajñāta
> ataeva vipra āge, pāṇḍitya paścāta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vipratva]]---the quality of being a //vipra//; [[synonyms:v:vikhyāta]]---well known; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍitya]]---erudition; [[synonyms:a:ajñāta]]---unknown; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the word //vipra//; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---first; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍitya]]---erudition; [[synonyms:p:paścāta]]---afterwards.

===== Translation =====

**"The man's being a vipra is known, but his erudition is unknown. Therefore the person is identified first and his erudition later.**
