====== Cc. Ādi 2.8 ======

===== Text =====

> svayaṁ bhagavān kṛṣṇa, viṣṇu-paratattva
> pūrṇa-jñāna pūrṇānanda parama mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---of all-pervading Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the ultimate truth; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---full knowledge; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---full bliss; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---supreme; [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---greatness.

===== Translation =====

**Kṛṣṇa, the original form of the Personality of Godhead, is the summum bonum of the all-pervading Viṣṇu. He is all-perfect knowledge and all-perfect bliss. He is the Supreme Transcendence.**
