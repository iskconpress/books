====== CC Ādi 2.64 ======

===== Text =====

> śuna bhāi ei śloka karaha vicāra
> eka mukhya-tattva, tina tāhāra pracāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please listen; [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---brothers; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---please give; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---principal; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---truth; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---manifestations.

===== Translation =====

**My dear brothers, kindly listen to the explanation of this verse and consider its meaning: the one original entity is known in His three different features.**
