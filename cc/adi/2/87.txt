====== CC Ādi 2.87 ======

===== Text =====

> viruddhārtha kaha tumi, kahite kara roṣa
> tomāra arthe avimṛṣṭa-vidheyāṁśa-doṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viruddha]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---contrary meaning; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---say; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---pointing out; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you do; [[synonyms:r:roṣa]]---anger; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:a:arthe]]---in the meaning; [[synonyms:a:avimṛṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vidheya]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---of the unconsidered predicate portion; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---the fault.

===== Translation =====

**"You say something contradictory and become angry when this is pointed out. Your explanation has the defect of a misplaced object. This is an unconsidered adjustment.**
