====== CC Ādi 2.110 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva caitanya gosāñi paratattva-sīmā
> tāṅre kṣīroda-śāyī kahi, ki tāṅra mahimā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the highest limit of the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:k:kṣīroda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---Kṣīrodakaśāyī Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---if I say; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glory.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore Lord Caitanya is the Supreme Absolute Truth. To call Him Kṣīrodakaśāyī Viṣṇu does not add to His glory.**
