====== CC Ādi 2.109 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kṛṣṇa avatārī vrajendra-kumāra
> āpane caitanya-rūpe kaila avatāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatārī]]---the source of all incarnations; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:k:kumāra]]---the son of the King of Vraja; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation.

===== Translation =====

**That same Lord Kṛṣṇa, the fountainhead of all incarnations, is known as the son of the King of Vraja. He has descended personally as Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
