====== Cc. Ādi 2.15 ======

===== Text =====

> koṭī koṭī brahmāṇḍe ye brahmera vibhūti
> sei brahma govindera haya aṅga-kānti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:koṭī]]---tens of millions; [[synonyms:k:koṭī]]---tens of millions; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:a:aṇḍe]]---in universes; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:b:brahmera]]---of Brahman; [[synonyms:v:vibhūti]]---opulences; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---Brahman; [[synonyms:g:govindera]]---of Lord Govinda; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---bodily effulgence.

===== Translation =====

**"The opulences of the impersonal Brahman spread throughout the millions and millions of universes. That Brahman is but the bodily effulgence of Govinda.**
