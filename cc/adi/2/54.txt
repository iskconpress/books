====== CC Ādi 2.54 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi tinera māyā la-iyā vyavahāra
> tathāpi tat-sparśa nāhi, sabhe māyā-pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:t:tinera]]---of these three; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]---the material energy; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---taking; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---the dealings; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of that; [[synonyms:s:sparśa]]---the touch; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:s:sabhe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāra]]---beyond the material energy.

===== Translation =====

**"Although these three features of the Lord deal directly with the material energy, none of them are touched by it. They are all beyond illusion.**
