====== CC Ādi 2.7 ======

===== Text =====

> anuvāda āge, pāche vidheya sthāpana
> sei artha kahi, śuna śāstra-vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anuvāda]]---the subject; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---first; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---afterwards; [[synonyms:v:vidheya]]---the predicate; [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---placing; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please listen; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---to the description of the scriptures.

===== Translation =====

**A predicate always follows its subject. Now I shall explain the meaning of this verse according to the revealed scriptures.**
