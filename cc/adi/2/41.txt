====== CC Ādi 2.41 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva adhīśvara tumi sarva pitā
> tomāra śaktite tāṅrā jagat-rakṣitā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:adhīśvara]]---primeval Lord; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---of all; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---father; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:ś:śaktite]]---by the energy; [[synonyms:t:tāṅrā]]---They; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---of the cosmic creations; [[synonyms:r:rakṣitā]]---protectors.

===== Translation =====

**"Therefore You are the primeval Lord, the original father of everyone. They [the puruṣas] are protectors of the universes by Your power.**
