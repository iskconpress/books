====== CC Ādi 2.4 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīya ślokera artha kari vivaraṇa
> vastu-nirdeśa-rūpa maṅgalācaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]]---third; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---of the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:n:nirdeśa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---in the form of delineation; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]---auspicious; [[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇa]]---conduct.

===== Translation =====

**Let me describe the meaning of the third verse [of the first fourteen]. It is an auspicious vibration that describes the Absolute Truth.**
