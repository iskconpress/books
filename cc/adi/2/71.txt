====== CC Ādi 2.71 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva-pakṣa kahe---tomāra bhāla ta' vyākhyāna
> paravyoma-nārāyaṇa svayaṁ-bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:p:pakṣa]]---opposing side; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---nice; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---exposition; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyoma]]---situated in the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**An opponent may say, "This is your interpretation, but actually the Supreme Lord is Nārāyaṇa, who is in the transcendental realm.**
