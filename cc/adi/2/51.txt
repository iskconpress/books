====== Cc. Ādi 2.51 ======

===== Text =====

> hiraṇya-garbhera ātmā garbhodaka-śāyī
> vyaṣṭi-jīva-antaryāmī kṣīrodaka-śāyī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hiraṇya]]-[[synonyms:g:garbhera]]---of the total of the living entities; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---the Supersoul; [[synonyms:g:garbha]]-[[synonyms:u:udaka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:v:vyaṣṭi]]---the individual; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---of the living entity; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:y:yāmī]]---Supersoul; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:u:udaka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---Kṣīrodakaśāyī Viṣṇu.

===== Translation =====

**"Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu is the Supersoul of the aggregate of living entities, and Kṣīrodakaśāyī Viṣṇu is the Supersoul of each individual living being.**
