====== Cc. Ādi 2.101 ======

===== Text =====

> cic-chakti, svarūpa-śakti, antaraṅgā nāma
> tāhāra vaibhava ananta vaikuṇṭhādi dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---spiritual energy; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---personal energy; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅgā]]---internal; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:v:vaibhava]]---manifestations; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Vaikuṇṭha, etc.; [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---abodes.

===== Translation =====

**"The cit-śakti, which is also called svarūpa-śakti or antaraṅga-śakti, displays many varied manifestations. It sustains the kingdom of God and its paraphernalia.**
