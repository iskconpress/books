====== Cc. Ādi 2.120 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñira ei tattva-nirūpaṇa
> svayaṁ-bhagavān kṛṣṇa vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---of the truth; [[synonyms:n:nirūpaṇa]]---settling; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Himself the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of the King of Vraja.

===== Translation =====

**The conclusion is that Lord Caitanya is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa, the son of the King of Vraja.**
