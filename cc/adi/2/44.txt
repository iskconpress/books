====== CC Ādi 2.44 ======

===== Text =====

> ithe yata jīva, tāra trai-kālika karma
> tāhā dekha, sākṣī tumi, jāna saba marma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ithe]]---in these; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as many; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---living beings; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of them; [[synonyms:t:trai]]-[[synonyms:k:kālika]]---past, present and future; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---the activities; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---You see; [[synonyms:s:sākṣī]]---witness; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:j:jāna]]---You know; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---of everything; [[synonyms:m:marma]]---the essence.

===== Translation =====

**"Both in this material world and in the transcendental world, You see all the deeds of all living beings, in the past, present and future. Since You are the witness of all such deeds, You know the essence of everything.**
