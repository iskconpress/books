====== CC Ādi 2.106 ======

===== Text =====

> svayaṁ bhagavān kṛṣṇa, kṛṣṇa sarvāśraya
> parama īśvara kṛṣṇa sarva-śāstre kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the shelter of all; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---Supreme; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---all scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---say.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus the Personality of Godhead Śrī Kṛṣṇa is the original primeval Lord, the source of all other expansions. All the revealed scriptures accept Śrī Kṛṣṇa as the Supreme Lord.**
