====== Cc. Ādi 8.71 ======

===== Text =====

> āra yata vṛndāvane baise bhakta-gaṇa
> śeṣa-līlā śunite sabāra haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:y:yata]]---there are many others; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:b:baise]]---residents; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---great devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the last portions of Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**In Vṛndāvana there were also many other great devotees, all of whom desired to hear the last pastimes of Lord Caitanya.**
