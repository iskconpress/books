====== CC Ādi 8.80 ======

===== Text =====

> kulādhidevatā mora---madana-mohana
> yāṅra sevaka---raghunātha, rūpa, sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:a:adhidevatā]]---the family Deity; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---mine; [[synonyms:m:madana]]-[[synonyms:m:mohana]]---Lord Madana-mohana; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servitor; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**I accept as my family Deity Madana-mohana, whose worshipers are Raghunātha dāsa, Śrī Rūpa and Sanātana Gosvāmīs.**
