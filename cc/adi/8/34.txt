====== CC Ādi 8.34 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-līlā bhāgavate kahe veda-vyāsa
> caitanya-līlāra vyāsa---vṛndāvana-dāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in the book //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---tells; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---Vyāsadeva, the editor of the Vedic literature; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---compiler; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---is Vṛndāvana dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**As Vyāsadeva has compiled all the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, Ṭhākura Vṛndāvana dāsa has depicted the pastimes of Lord Caitanya.**
