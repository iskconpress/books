====== CC Ādi 8.46 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-candrera līlā ananta apāra
> varṇite varṇite grantha ha-ila vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:candrera]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unfathomable; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---describing; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---describing; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---the book; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expansive.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Lord Caitanya are unlimited and unfathomable. Therefore, in describing all those pastimes, the book became voluminous.**
