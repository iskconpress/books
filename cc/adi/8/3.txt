====== Cc. Ādi 8.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jayādvaita ācārya kṛpāmaya
> jaya jaya gadādhara paṇḍita mahāśaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---unto Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---teacher; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāmaya]]---very merciful; [[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories to; [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---of the name Gadādhara; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned scholar; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---great personality.

===== Translation =====

**Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Advaita Ācārya, who is very merciful, and also to that great personality Gadādhara Paṇḍita, the learned scholar.**
