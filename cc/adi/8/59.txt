====== CC Ādi 8.59 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita-gosāñira śiṣya---ananta ācārya
> kṛṣṇa-premamaya-tanu, udāra, sarva-ārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---disciple; [[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of the name Ananta Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:premamaya]]---always overwhelmed by love of God; [[synonyms:t:tanu]]---body; [[synonyms:u:udāra]]---magnanimous; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---in all respects; [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---advanced.

===== Translation =====

**Ananta Ācārya was a disciple of Gadādhara Paṇḍita. His body was always absorbed in love of Godhead. He was magnanimous and advanced in all respects.**
