====== CC Ādi 8.37 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgavate yata bhakti-siddhāntera sāra
> likhiyāchena iṅhā jāni' kariyā uddhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhāntera]]---in understanding devotional service; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---essence; [[synonyms:l:likhiyāchena]]---has written; [[synonyms:i:iṅhā]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---I know; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making; [[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---quotation.

===== Translation =====

**In Śrī Caitanya-maṅgala [later known as Śrī Caitanya-bhāgavata] Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has given the conclusion and essence of devotional service by quoting the authoritative statements of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.**
