====== CC Ādi 8.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya gauracandra
> jaya jaya paramānanda jaya nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---whose name is Lord Gaurāṅga; [[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]---most joyful; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---unto Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is known as Gaurasundara. I also offer my respectful obeisances unto Nityānanda Prabhu, who is always very joyful.**
