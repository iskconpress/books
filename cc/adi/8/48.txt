====== CC Ādi 8.48 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-līlā-varṇane ha-ila āveśa
> caitanyera śeṣa-līlā rahila avaśeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:v:varṇane]]---in the matter of description; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes in the last portion of His life; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]]---supplement.

===== Translation =====

**He ecstatically described the pastimes of Lord Nityānanda, but the later pastimes of Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained untold.**
