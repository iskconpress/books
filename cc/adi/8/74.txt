====== Cc. Ādi 8.74 ======

===== Text =====

> daraśana kari kailuṅ caraṇa vandana
> gosāñi-dāsa pūjārī kare caraṇa-sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---by visiting; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---doing; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---made; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worship; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Gosāñi dāsa; [[synonyms:p:pūjārī]]---priest; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service.

===== Translation =====

**When I visited the temple of Madana-mohana, the priest Gosāñi dāsa was serving the feet of the Lord, and I also prayed at the Lord's lotus feet.**
