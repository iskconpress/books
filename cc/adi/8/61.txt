====== CC Ādi 8.61 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-nityānande tāṅra parama viśvāsa
> caitanya-carite tāṅra parama ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānande]]---in Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---very great; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---faith; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:carite]]---in the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---great; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---satisfaction.

===== Translation =====

**Paṇḍita Haridāsa had great faith in Lord Caitanya and Nityānanda. Therefore he took great satisfaction in knowing about Their pastimes and qualities.**
