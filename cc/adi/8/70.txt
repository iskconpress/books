====== CC Ādi 8.70 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya-gosāñira śiṣya---cakravartī śivānanda
> niravadhi tāṅra citte caitanya-nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Ācārya Gosāñi; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---the disciple; [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]] [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---of the name Śivānanda Cakravartī; [[synonyms:n:niravadhi]]---always; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Caitanya and Nityānanda are situated.

===== Translation =====

**Among the disciples of Ananta Ācārya was Śivānanda Cakravartī, in whose heart Lord Caitanya and Nityānanda constantly dwelled.**
