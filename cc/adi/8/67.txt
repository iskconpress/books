====== Cc. Ādi 8.67 ======

===== Text =====

> yādavācārya gosāñi śrī-rūpera saṅgī
> caitanya-carite teṅho ati baḍa raṅgī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yādavācārya]]---of the name Yādavācārya; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---spiritual master; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:saṅgī]]---associate; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:carite]]---in the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very much; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---great; [[synonyms:r:raṅgī]]---enthusiastic.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Yādavācārya Gosāñi, a constant associate of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, was also very enthusiastic in hearing and chanting about Lord Caitanya's pastimes.**
