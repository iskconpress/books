====== Cc. Ādi 8.77 ======

===== Text =====

> ājñā-mālā pāñā āmāra ha-ila ānanda
> tāhāṅi karinu ei granthera ārambha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---the garland of order; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---receiving; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---great pleasure; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]]---then and there; [[synonyms:k:karinu]]---attempted; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:g:granthera]]---of //Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta//; [[synonyms:ā:ārambha]]---beginning.

===== Translation =====

**I was greatly pleased to have the garland signifying the order of the Lord, and then and there I commenced to write this book.**
