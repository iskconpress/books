====== Cc. Ādi 8.69 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra śiṣya---govinda pūjaka caitanya-dāsa
> mukundānanda cakravartī, premī kṛṣṇadāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---his disciple; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the Govinda Deity; [[synonyms:p:pūjaka]]---priest; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Caitanya dāsa; [[synonyms:m:mukundānanda]] [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---of the name Mukundānanda Cakravartī; [[synonyms:p:premī]]---a great lover; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---of the name Kṛṣṇadāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Among his disciples were Caitanya dāsa, who was a priest of the Govinda Deity, as well as Mukundānanda Cakravartī and the great devotee Kṛṣṇadāsa.**
