====== Cc. Ādi 8.83 ======

===== Text =====

> mūrkha, nīca, kṣudra muñi viṣaya-lālasa
> vaiṣṇavājñā-bale kari eteka sāhasa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mūrkha]]---foolish; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]---lowborn; [[synonyms:k:kṣudra]]---very insignificant; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---material; [[synonyms:l:lālasa]]---desires; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---of the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---on the strength of; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:e:eteka]]---so much; [[synonyms:s:sāhasa]]---energy.

===== Translation =====

**I am foolish, lowborn and insignificant, and I always desire material enjoyment; yet by the order of the Vaiṣṇavas I am greatly enthusiastic to write this transcendental literature.**
