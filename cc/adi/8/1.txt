====== CC Ādi 8.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vande caitanya-devaṁ taṁ
> bhagavantaṁ yad-icchayā
> prasabhaṁ nartyate citraṁ
> lekha-raṅge jaḍo 'py ayam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:devam]]---unto Lord Srī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---Him; [[synonyms:b:bhagavantam]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yat]]-[[synonyms:i:icchayā]]---by whose desires; [[synonyms:p:prasabham]]---all of a sudden; [[synonyms:n:nartyate]]---dancing; [[synonyms:c:citram]]---wonderfully; [[synonyms:l:lekha]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the matter of writing; [[synonyms:j:jaḍaḥ]]---dull fool; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respects to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, by whose desire I have become like a dancing dog and, although I am a fool, I have suddenly taken to the writing of Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta.**
