====== CC Ādi 8.40 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-dāsa-pade koṭi namaskāra
> aiche grantha kari' teṅho tārilā saṁsāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---unto the lotus feet of Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---millions; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---book; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---compiling; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:t:tarilā]]---delivered; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---all the world.

===== Translation =====

**I offer millions of obeisances unto the lotus feet of Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura. No one else could write such a wonderful book for the deliverance of all fallen souls.**
