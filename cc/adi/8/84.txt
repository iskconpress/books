====== Cc. Ādi 8.84 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rūpa-raghunātha-caraṇera ei bala
> yāṅra smṛte siddha haya vāñchita-sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:c:caraṇera]]---of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bala]]---strength; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:s:smṛte]]---by remembrance; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]---successful; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:v:vāñchita]]-[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all desires.

===== Translation =====

**The lotus feet of Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī and Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī are my source of strength. Remembering their lotus feet can fulfill all one's desires.**
