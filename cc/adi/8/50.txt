====== Cc. Ādi 8.50 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvane kalpa-drume suvarṇa-sadana
> mahā-yogapīṭha tāhāṅ, ratna-siṁhāsana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:d:drume]]---under the desire trees; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sadana]]---golden throne; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---great; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:p:pīṭha]]---pious temple; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]---bedecked with jewels; [[synonyms:s:siṁhāsana]]---throne.

===== Translation =====

**In Vṛndāvana, in a great place of pilgrimage underneath the desire trees, is a golden throne bedecked with jewels.**
