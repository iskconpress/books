====== CC Ādi 8.64 ======

===== Text =====

> kathāya sabhā ujjvala kare yena pūrṇa-candra
> nija-guṇāmṛte bāḍāya vaiṣṇava-ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kathāya]]---by words; [[synonyms:s:sabhā]]---assembly; [[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---illuminated; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---full moon; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---own; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛte]]---nectar of qualities; [[synonyms:b:bāḍāya]]---increases; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**Like the full moon, he illuminated the entire assembly of the Vaiṣṇavas by speaking Śrī Caitanya-maṅgala, and by the nectar of his qualities he increased their transcendental bliss.**
