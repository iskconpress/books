====== CC Ādi 8.53 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra sevaka sevā kare anukṣaṇa
> sahasra-vadane sevā nā yāya varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---many thousands; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servitors; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---render; [[synonyms:a:anukṣaṇa]]---always; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousands; [[synonyms:v:vadane]]---mouths; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---process of service; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not possible; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes on; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**In that temple of Govindajī, thousands of servitors always render service to the Lord in devotion. Even with thousands of mouths, one could not describe this service.**
