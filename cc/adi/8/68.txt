====== CC Ādi 8.68 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita-gosāñira śiṣya---bhugarbha gosāñi
> gaura-kathā vinā āra mukhe anya nāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Paṇḍita Gosāñi (Gadādhara Paṇḍita); [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---disciple; [[synonyms:b:bhugarbha]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---of the name Bhugarbha Gosāñi; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---else; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in his mouth; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---nothing else.

===== Translation =====

**Bhugarbha Gosāñi, a disciple of Gadādhara Paṇḍita, was always engaged in topics regarding Lord Caitanya, knowing nothing else.**
