====== CC Ādi 8.18 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa yadi chuṭe bhakte bhukti mukti diyā
> kabhu prema-bhakti nā dena rākhena lukāiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---of course; [[synonyms:c:chuṭe]]---goes away; [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---unto the devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhukti]]---material enjoyment; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---give; [[synonyms:r:rākhena]]---keeps; [[synonyms:l:lukāiyā]]---hiding.

===== Translation =====

**If a devotee wants material sense gratification or liberation from the Lord, Kṛṣṇa immediately delivers it, but pure devotional service He keeps hidden.**
