====== CC Ādi 8.35 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-dāsa kaila 'caitanya-maṅgala'
> yāṅhāra śravaṇe nāśe sarva amaṅgala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Vṛndāvana dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---compiled; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]---the book named //Caitanya-maṅgala//; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing; [[synonyms:n:nāśe]]---annihilated; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:a:amaṅgala]]---inauspiciousness.

===== Translation =====

**Ṭhākura Vṛndāvana dāsa has composed Śrī Caitanya-maṅgala. Hearing this book annihilates all misfortune.**
