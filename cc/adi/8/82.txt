====== Cc. Ādi 8.82 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-līlāte 'vyāsa'---vṛndāvana-dāsa
> tāṅra kṛpā vinā anye nā haya prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāte]]---in describing the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---is Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:a:anye]]---other; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---never; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifest.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura is the authorized writer on the pastimes of Lord Caitanya. Without his mercy, therefore, one cannot describe these pastimes.**
