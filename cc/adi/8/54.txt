====== CC Ādi 8.54 ======

===== Text =====

> sevāra adhyakṣa---śrī-paṇḍita haridāsa
> tāṅra yaśaḥ-guṇa sarva-jagate prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sevāra]]---of the service; [[synonyms:a:adhyakṣa]]---commander; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of his; [[synonyms:y:yaśaḥ]]---fame; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---quality; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jagate]]---all over the world; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---known.

===== Translation =====

**In that temple the chief servitor was Śrī Haridāsa Paṇḍita. His qualities and fame are known all over the world.**

===== Purport =====

Śrī Haridāsa Paṇḍita was a disciple of Śrī Ananta Ācārya, who was a disciple of Gadādhara Paṇḍita.
