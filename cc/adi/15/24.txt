====== Cc. Ādi 15.24 ======

===== Text =====

> bandhu-bāndhava asi' duṅhā prabodhila
> pitṛ-kriyā vidhi-mate īśvara karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bandhu]]---friends; [[synonyms:b:bāndhava]]---relatives; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:d:duṅhā]]---both of them; [[synonyms:p:prabodhila]]---pacified; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]-[[synonyms:k:kriyā]]---rituals performed after the death of the father; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---according to the Vedic system; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---executed.

===== Translation =====

**Friends and relatives came there to pacify both Lord Caitanya and His mother. Then Lord Caitanya, even though He was the Supreme Personality of Godhead, executed the rituals for His dead father according to the Vedic system.**
