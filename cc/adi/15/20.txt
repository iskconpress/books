====== CC Ādi 15.20 ======

===== Text =====

> gṛhastha ha-iyā kariba pitā-mātāra sevana
> ihāte-i tuṣṭa habena lakṣmī-nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gṛhastha]]---a householder; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall do; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]-[[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---of the parents; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:i:ihāte]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---in this; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:h:habena]]---will become; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---the goddess of fortune and Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Later I shall become a householder and thus serve My parents, for this action will very much satisfy Lord Nārāyaṇa and His wife, the goddess of fortune.'**
