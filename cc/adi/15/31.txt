====== Cc. Ādi 15.31 ======

===== Text =====

> vistāriyā varṇilā tāhā vṛndāvana-dāsa
> ei ta' paugaṇḍa-līlāra sūtra-prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vistāriyā]]---having elaborated; [[synonyms:v:varṇilā]]---has described; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Ṭhākura Vṛndāvana dāsa; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---this is; [[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes of His early age; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation of the synopsis.

===== Translation =====

**Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has elaborately described all these pastimes of the Lord's early age. What I have given is but a condensed presentation of the same pastimes.**
