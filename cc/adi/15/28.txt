====== CC Ādi 15.28 ======

===== Text =====

> daive eka dina prabhu paḍiyā āsite
> vallabhācāryera kanyā dekhe gaṅgā-pathe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daive]]---accidentally; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---after studying; [[synonyms:ā:āsite]]---while He was coming back; [[synonyms:v:vallabhācāryera]]---of Vallabhācārya; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---daughter; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way to the Ganges.

===== Translation =====

**One day when the Lord was coming back from school He accidentally saw the daughter of Vallabhācārya on the way to the Ganges.**
