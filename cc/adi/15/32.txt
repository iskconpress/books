~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Ādi 15.32~~

====== CC Ādi 15.32 ======

===== Text =====

> paugaṇḍa vayase līlā bahuta prakāra
> vṛndāvana-dāsa ihā kariyāchena vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:v:vayase]]---in His early age; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---of various kinds; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]]---has done; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---the elaborate explanation.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord performed many varieties of pastimes in His early age, and Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has described them elaborately.**
