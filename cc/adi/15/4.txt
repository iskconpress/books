====== CC Ādi 15.4 ======

===== Text =====

> paugaṇḍa-līlā caitanya-
> kṛṣṇasyāti-suvistṛtā
> vidyārambha-mukhā pāṇi-
> grahaṇāntā mano-harā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of the //paugaṇḍa// age; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇasya]]---of Lord Caitanya, who is Kṛṣṇa Himself; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:s:suvistṛtā]]---very much expanded; [[synonyms:v:vidyā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārambha]]---the beginning of education; [[synonyms:m:mukhā]]---chief business; [[synonyms:p:pāṇi]]-[[synonyms:g:grahaṇa]]---marriage; [[synonyms:a:antā]]---at the end; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:h:harā]]---very beautiful.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of the Lord during His paugaṇḍa age were very extensive. His education was His chief occupation, and after that His very beautiful marriage took place.**
