====== CC Ādi 15.11 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe miśra viśvarūpera dekhiyā yauvana
> kanyā cāhi' vivāha dite karilena mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:v:viśvarūpera]]---of Viśvarūpa, his elder son; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:y:yauvana]]---youthfulness; [[synonyms:k:kanyācāhi']]---wanting to find a girl; [[synonyms:v:vivāha]]---marriage; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:k:karilena]]---made up; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---his mind.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, seeing that Viśvarūpa was a grown-up youth, Jagannātha Miśra wanted to find a girl and arrange a marriage ceremony for Him.**
