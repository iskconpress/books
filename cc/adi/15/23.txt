====== CC Ādi 15.23 ======

===== Text =====

> kata dina rahi' miśra gelā para-loka
> mātā-putra duṅhāra bāḍila hṛdi śoka

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---some days; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---passed away; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---for the transcendental world; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---mother; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---son; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased; [[synonyms:h:hṛdi]]---in the hearts; [[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---lamentation.

===== Translation =====

**After some days, Jagannātha Miśra passed away from this world to the transcendental world, and both mother and son were very much aggrieved in their hearts.**
