====== Cc. Ādi 15.13 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni, śacī-miśrera duḥkhī haila mana
> tabe prabhu mātā-pitāra kaila āśvāsana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:m:miśrera]]---and of Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---very unhappy; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---minds; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]-[[synonyms:p:pitāra]]---of the parents; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsana]]---pacification.

===== Translation =====

**When Śacīmātā and Jagannātha Miśra heard of the departure of their elder son, Viśvarūpa, they were very unhappy, but Lord Caitanya tried to console them.**
