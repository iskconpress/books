====== CC Ādi 15.12 ======

===== Text =====

> viśvarūpa śuni' ghara chāḍi palāilā
> sannyāsa kariyā tīrtha karibāre gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśvarūpa]]---of the name Viśvarūpa; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---home; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi]]---giving up; [[synonyms:p:palāilā]]---went away; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the renounced order; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---accepting; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---the holy places; [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---for touring; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went away.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing of this, Viśvarūpa immediately left home and went away to accept sannyāsa and travel from one place of pilgrimage to another.**
