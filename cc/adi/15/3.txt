====== CC Ādi 15.3 ======

===== Text =====

> paugaṇḍa-līlāra sūtra kariye gaṇana
> paugaṇḍa-vayase prabhura mukhya adhyayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]---of the age from five years to ten years; [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---synopsis; [[synonyms:k:kariye]]---I do; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---enumerate; [[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vayase]]---in that age between five and ten years; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---chief; [[synonyms:a:adhyayana]]---studying.

===== Translation =====

**Let me now enumerate the activities of the Lord between the ages of five and ten. His chief occupation during this period was to engage Himself in study.**
