====== CC Ādi 15.25 ======

===== Text =====

> kata dine prabhu citte karilā cintana
> gṛhastha ha-ilāma, ebe cāhi gṛha-dharma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---after some days; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within His mind; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:c:cintana]]---consideration; [[synonyms:g:gṛhastha]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilāma]]---I remained in householder life; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:c:cāhi]]---I want; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---activities of family life.

===== Translation =====

**After some days the Lord thought, "I did not take sannyāsa, and since I am remaining at home it is My duty to act as a gṛhastha.**
