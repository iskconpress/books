====== CC Ādi 15.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaitacandra, jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]-[[synonyms:a:advaitacandra]]---all glories to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jayagaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---all glories to the devotees of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu! All glories to Advaita Ācārya! And all glories to the devotees of Lord Caitanya!**
