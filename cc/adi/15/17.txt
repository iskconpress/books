====== CC Ādi 15.17 ======

===== Text =====

> āste-vyaste pitā-mātā mukhe dila pāni
> sustha hañā kahe prabhu apūrva kāhinī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---with great haste; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]-[[synonyms:m:mātā]]---both the parents; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---on the mouth; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:p:pāni]]---water; [[synonyms:s:susthahañā]]---being revived; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]]---something astonishing; [[synonyms:k:kāhinī]]---narration.

===== Translation =====

**After His father and mother sprinkled water on His mouth with great haste, the Lord revived and said something wonderful they had never heard before.**
