====== CC Ādi 5.133 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya gosāñi
> sarva avatāra-līlā kari' sabāre dekhāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of different incarnations; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---exhibiting; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to everyone; [[synonyms:d:dekhāi]]---He showed.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu has exhibited to everyone all the pastimes of all the various incarnations.**
