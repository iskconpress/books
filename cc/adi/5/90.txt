====== CC Ādi 5.90 ======

===== Text =====

> acintya aiśvarya ei jāniha āmāra
> ei ta' gītāra artha kaila paracāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:acintya]]---inconceivable; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---opulence; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jāniha]]---you must know; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---of Me; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---this; [[synonyms:g:gītāra]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning of the //Bhagavad-gītā//; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:p:paracāra]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa propagated.

===== Translation =====

**"O Arjuna, you should know this as My inconceivable opulence." This is the meaning propagated by Lord Kṛṣṇa in the Bhagavad-gītā.**
