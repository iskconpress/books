====== CC Ādi 5.91 ======

===== Text =====

> sei ta' puruṣa yāṅra 'aṁśa' dhare nāma
> caitanyera saṅge sei nityānanda-rāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---that; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---Supreme Person; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---as part; [[synonyms:d:dhare]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---is known; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]]---Lord Nityānanda or Balarāma.

===== Translation =====

**That Mahā-puruṣa [Kāraṇodakaśāyī Viṣṇu] is known as a plenary part of Him who is Lord Nityānanda Balarāma, the favorite associate of Lord Caitanya.**
