====== CC Ādi 5.154 ======

===== Text =====

> sei amśa lañā jyeṣṭha-kaniṣṭhābhimāna
> aṁśāṁśi-rūpe śāstre karaye vyākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking that plenary portion; [[synonyms:j:jyeṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:k:kaniṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---considering Themselves the elder or younger; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśi]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---as the expansion and the original Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---does; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation.

===== Translation =====

**Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma present Themselves as elder or younger brother, but in the scriptures They are described as the original Supreme Personality of Godhead and His expansion.**
