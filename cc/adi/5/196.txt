====== CC Ādi 5.196 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prerilā more hātasāni diyā
> antardhāna kaila prabhu nija-gaṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prerilā]]---dispatched; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:h:hātasāni]]---indication of the hand; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:a:antardhānakaila]]---disappeared; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking His personal associates.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, He directed me toward Vṛndāvana by waving His hand. Then He disappeared with His associates.**
