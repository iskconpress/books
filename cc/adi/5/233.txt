====== Cc. Ādi 5.233 ======

===== Text =====

> āpanāra kathā likhi nirlajja ha-iyā
> nityānanda-guṇe lekhāya unmatta kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]]---personal; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---description; [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I write; [[synonyms:n:nirlajja]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---being shameless; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---the attributes of Nityānanda; [[synonyms:l:lekhāya]]---cause to write; [[synonyms:u:unmatta]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making like a madman.

===== Translation =====

**I have described my own story without reservations. The attributes of Lord Nityānanda, making me like a madman, force me to write these things.**
