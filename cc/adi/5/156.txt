====== Cc. Ādi 5.156 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-caitanya---sei kṛṣṇa, nityānanda---rāma
> nityānanda pūrṇa kare caitanyera kāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---that original Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---Balarāma; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---fulfills; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---all the desires of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya is the same Lord Kṛṣṇa, and Lord Nityānanda is Lord Balarāma. Lord Nityānanda fulfills all of Lord Caitanya's desires.**
