====== Cc. Ādi 5.229 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅra prāṇa-dhana---nityānanda-śrī-caitanya
> rādhā-kṛṣṇa-bhakti vine nāhi jāne anya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---life and soul; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Nityānanda and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---to Kṛṣṇa and Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:v:vine]]---except; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]] [[synonyms:a:anya]]---do not know anything else.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya and Lord Nityānanda are their life and soul. They do not know anything but devotional service to Śrī Śrī Rādhā-Kṛṣṇa.**
