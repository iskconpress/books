====== CC Ādi 5.42 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhā ye rāmera rūpa---mahā-saṅkarṣaṇa
> cic-chakti-āśraya tiṅho, kāraṇera kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:r:rāmera]] [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the personal feature of Balarāma; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]---Mahā-saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the shelter of the spiritual potency; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇera]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of all causes.

===== Translation =====

**There the personal feature of Balarāma called Mahā-saṅkarṣaṇa is the shelter of the spiritual energy. He is the primary cause, the cause of all causes.**
