====== CC Ādi 5.54 ======

===== Text =====

> cinmaya-jala sei parama kāraṇa
> yāra eka kaṇā gaṅgā patita-pāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---spiritual; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---original cause; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:kaṇā]]---drop; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]---the sacred Ganges; [[synonyms:p:patita]]-[[synonyms:p:pāvana]]---the deliverer of fallen souls.

===== Translation =====

**The water of the Kāraṇa Ocean, which is the original cause, is therefore spiritual. The sacred Ganges, which is but a drop of it, purifies the fallen souls.**
