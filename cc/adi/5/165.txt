====== Cc. Ādi 5.165 ======

===== Text =====

> ye nayana dekhite aśru haya mane yāra
> sei netre avicchinna vahe aśru-dhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]---his; [[synonyms:n:nayana]]---eyes; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---seeing; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---appear; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---from the mind; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of someone; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:netre]]---in his eyes; [[synonyms:a:avicchinna]]---continuously; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---flows; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---a shower of tears.

===== Translation =====

**When someone saw the eyes of Mīnaketana Rāmadāsa, tears would automatically flow from his own eyes, for a constant shower of tears flowed from the eyes of Mīnaketana Rāmadāsa.**
