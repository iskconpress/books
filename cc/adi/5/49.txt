====== CC Ādi 5.49 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭama ślokera kaila saṅkṣepe vivaraṇa
> navama ślokera artha śuna diyā mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭama]]---eighth; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---I have done; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:n:navama]]---the ninth; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:d:diyā]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---with mental attention.

===== Translation =====

**I have briefly explained the eighth verse. Now please listen with attention as I explain the ninth verse.**
