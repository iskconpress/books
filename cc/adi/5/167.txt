====== CC Ādi 5.167 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda bali' yabe karena huṅkāra
> tāhā dekhi' lokera haya mahā-camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---the name Nityānanda; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---whenever; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---makes a great sound; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing that; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---great wonder and astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Whenever he shouted aloud the name Nityānanda, the people around him were filled with great wonder and astonishment.**
