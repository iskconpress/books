====== CC Ādi 5.166 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu kona aṅge dekhi pulaka-kadamba
> eka aṅge jāḍya tāṅra, āra aṅge kampa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---in parts of the body; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]-[[synonyms:k:kadamba]]---eruptions of ecstasy like //kadamba// flowers; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---in one part of the body; [[synonyms:j:jāḍya]]---stunned; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---in another limb; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes there were eruptions of ecstasy like kadamba flowers on some parts of his body, and sometimes one limb would be stunned while another would be trembling.**
