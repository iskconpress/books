====== CC Ādi 5.3 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ṣaṭ-śloke kahila kṛṣṇa-caitanya-mahimā
> pañca-śloke kahi nityānanda-tattva-sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in six verses; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---described; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in five verses; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---let me explain; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---of the truth; [[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limitation.

===== Translation =====

**I have described the glory of Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya in six verses. Now, in five verses, I shall describe the glory of Lord Nityānanda.**
