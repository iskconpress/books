====== CC Ādi 5.158 ======

===== Text =====

> āra eka śuna tāṅra kṛpāra mahimā
> adhama jīvere caḍhāila ūrdhva-sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāra]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glory of His mercy; [[synonyms:a:adhama]] [[synonyms:j:jīvere]]---the downtrodden living being; [[synonyms:c:caḍhāila]]---He elevated; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhva]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---to the topmost limit.

===== Translation =====

**Please listen to another glory of His mercy. He made a fallen living entity climb to the highest limit.**
