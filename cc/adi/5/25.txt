====== CC Ādi 5.25 ======

===== Text =====

> ei tina loke kṛṣṇa kevala-līlā-maya
> nija-gaṇa lañā khele ananta samaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---in the locations; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---consisting of pastimes; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with His personal associates; [[synonyms:k:khele]]---He plays; [[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:s:samaya]]---unlimited time.

===== Translation =====

**Only in these three places [Dvārakā, Mathurā and Gokula] does the all-sporting Lord Kṛṣṇa perform His endless pastimes with His personal associates.**
