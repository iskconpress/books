====== Cc. Ādi 5.172 ======

===== Text =====

> utsavānte gelā tiṅho kariyā prasāda
> mora bhrātā-sane tāṅra kichu haila vāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:utsava]]-[[synonyms:a:ante]]---after the festival; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went away; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---showing mercy; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---of me; [[synonyms:b:bhrātā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with the brother; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vāda]]---controversy.

===== Translation =====

**At the end of the festival Mīnaketana Rāmadāsa went away, offering his blessings to everyone. At that time he had some controversy with my brother.**
