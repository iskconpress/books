====== Cc. Ādi 5.32 ======

===== Text =====

> vaikuṇṭha-bāhire eka jyotir-maya maṇḍala
> kṛṣṇera aṅgera prabhā, parama ujjvala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside the Vaikuṇṭhalokas; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:j:jyotiḥ]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]] [[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---the atmosphere of the glowing effulgence; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:aṅgera]]---of the body; [[synonyms:p:prabhā]]---rays; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---supremely; [[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---bright.

===== Translation =====

**Outside the Vaikuṇṭha planets is the atmosphere of the glowing effulgence, which consists of the supremely bright rays of the body of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
