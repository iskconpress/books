====== Cc. Ādi 5.146 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita ācārya, nityānanda,---dui aṅga
> dui-jana lañā prabhura yata kichu raṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---two limbs of the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:janalañā]]---taking the two of Them; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:r:raṅga]]---playful activities.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya and Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu, who are plenary parts of the Lord, are His principal associates. With these two the Lord performs His pastimes in various ways.**
