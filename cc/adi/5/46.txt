====== CC Ādi 5.46 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅhā haite viśvotpatti, yāṅhāte pralaya
> sei puruṣera saṅkarṣaṇa samāśraya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from whom; [[synonyms:v:viśva]]-[[synonyms:u:utpatti]]---the creation of the material cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāte]]---in whom; [[synonyms:p:pralaya]]---merging; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:puruṣera]]---of that Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]---of the name Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:s:samāśraya]]---the original shelter.

===== Translation =====

**Saṅkarṣaṇa is the original shelter of the puruṣa, from whom this world is created and in whom it is dissolved.**
