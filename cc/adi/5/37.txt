====== Cc. Ādi 5.37 ======

===== Text =====

> taiche para-vyome nānā cic-chakti-vilāsa
> nirviśeṣa jyotir-bimba bāhire prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:taiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyome]]---in the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---varieties; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes of spiritual energy; [[synonyms:n:nirviśeṣa]]---impersonal; [[synonyms:j:jyotiḥ]]---of the effulgence; [[synonyms:b:bimba]]---reflection; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifested.

===== Translation =====

**Thus in the spiritual sky there are varieties of pastimes within the spiritual energy. Outside the Vaikuṇṭha planets appears the impersonal reflection of light.**
