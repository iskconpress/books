====== CC Ādi 5.6 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kṛṣṇa---navadvīpe śrī-caitanya-candra
> sei balarāma---saṅge śrī-nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---that original Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---at Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:balarāma]]---that Lord Balarāma; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda.

===== Translation =====

**That original Lord Kṛṣṇa appeared in Navadvīpa as Lord Caitanya, and Balarāma appeared with Him as Lord Nityānanda.**
