====== Cc. Ādi 5.121 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra-vadane kare kṛṣṇa-guṇa gāna
> niravadhi guṇa gā'na, anta nāhi pā'na

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadane]]---in thousands of mouths; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]] [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---chanting of the holy attributes of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:niravadhi]]---continuously; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]] [[synonyms:g:gā'na]]---chanting of the transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:a:anta]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pā'na]]---does not reach the end.

===== Translation =====

**With His thousands of mouths He sings the glories of Lord Kṛṣṇa, but although He always sings in that way, He does not find an end to the qualities of the Lord.**
