====== CC Ādi 5.126 ======

===== Text =====

> e-saba pramāṇe jāni nityānanda-tattva-sīmā
> tāṅhāke 'ananta' kahi, ki tāṅra mahimā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇe]]---by the evidences; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I know; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limit of the truth of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāke]]---to Him (Lord Nityānanda, Balarāma); [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---Lord Ananta; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---if I say; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---what glory do I speak about Him.

===== Translation =====

**From these conclusions we can know the limit of the truth of Lord Nityānanda. But what glory is there in calling Him Ananta?**
