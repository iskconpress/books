====== Cc. Ādi 5.139 ======

===== Text =====

> kvacit krīḍā-pariśrāntaṁ
> gopotsaṅgopabarhaṇam
> svayaṁ viśrāmayaty āryaṁ
> pāda-saṁvāhanādibhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---playing; [[synonyms:p:pariśrāntam]]---very much fatigued; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:u:utsaṅga]]---the lap of a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:u:upabarhaṇam]]---whose pillow; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viśrāmayati]]---causing to rest; [[synonyms:ā:āryam]]---His elder brother; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvāhana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---by massaging His feet, etc.

===== Translation =====

**"Sometimes when Lord Kṛṣṇa's elder brother, Lord Balarāma, felt tired after playing and lay His head on the lap of a cowherd boy, Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself served Him by massaging His feet."**
