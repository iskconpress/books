====== Cc. Ādi 5.130 ======

===== Text =====

> keho kahe, kṛṣṇa kṣīroda-śāyī avatāra
> asambhava nahe, satya vacana sabāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keho]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---someone says; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṣīroda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---an incarnation of Lord Viṣṇu lying in the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:a:asambhava]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there is not impossibility; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---true; [[synonyms:v:vacana]] [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---everyone's statement.

===== Translation =====

**Some called Lord Kṛṣṇa an incarnation of Lord Kṣīrodakaśāyī. All these names are true; nothing is impossible.**
