====== CC Ādi 5.67 ======

===== Text =====

> agaṇya, ananta yata aṇḍa-sanniveśa
> tata-rūpe puruṣa kare sabāte prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:agaṇya]]---innumerable; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:a:aṇḍa]]---universes; [[synonyms:s:sanniveśa]]---groups; [[synonyms:t:tata]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in as many forms; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sabāte]]---in every one of them; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**The puruṣa enters each and every one of the countless universes. He manifests Himself in as many separate forms as there are universes.**
