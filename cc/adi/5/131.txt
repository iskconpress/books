====== CC Ādi 5.131 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa yabe avatare sarvāṁśa-āśraya
> sarvāṁśa āsi' tabe kṛṣṇete milaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:a:avatare]]---descends; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the shelter of all other //viṣṇu-tattvas//; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---all plenary portions; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇete]]---in Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:milaya]]---join.

===== Translation =====

**When the Supreme Personality of Godhead Kṛṣṇa appears, He is the shelter of all plenary parts. Thus at that time all His plenary portions join in Him.**
