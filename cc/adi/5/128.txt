====== Cc. Ādi 5.128 ======

===== Text =====

> avatāra-avatārī---abheda, ye jāne
> pūrve yaiche kṛṣṇake keho kāho kari' māne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārī]]---an incarnation and the source of all incarnations; [[synonyms:a:abheda]]---identical; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---anyone who knows; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇake]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:keho]]---somebody; [[synonyms:k:kāho]]---somewhere; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---accepts.

===== Translation =====

**They know that there is no difference between the incarnation and the source of all incarnations. Previously Lord Kṛṣṇa was regarded in the light of different principles by different people.**
