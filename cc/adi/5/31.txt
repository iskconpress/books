====== CC Ādi 5.31 ======

===== Text =====

> brahma-sāyujya-muktera tāhā nāhi gati
> vaikuṇṭha-bāhire haya tā'-sabāra sthiti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:sāyujya]]---of merging into the Supreme Brahman; [[synonyms:m:muktera]]---of the liberation; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---there (in Vaikuṇṭha); [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:g:gati]]---entrance; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside the Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:t:tā']]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---the residence of all of them.

===== Translation =====

**Those who attain brahma-sāyujya liberation cannot gain entrance into Vaikuṇṭha; their residence is outside the Vaikuṇṭha planets.**
