====== CC Ādi 5.150 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmera caritra saba,---duḥkhera kāraṇa
> svatantra līlāya duḥkha sahena lakṣmaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmera]] [[synonyms:c:caritra]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all the activities of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhera]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---causes of suffering; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:t:tantra]]---although independent; [[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---in the pastimes; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:s:sahena]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣmaṇa]]---Lakṣmaṇa tolerates.

===== Translation =====

**The activities of Lord Rāma were full of suffering, but Lakṣmaṇa, of His own accord, tolerated that suffering.**
