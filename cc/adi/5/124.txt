====== CC Ādi 5.124 ======

===== Text =====

> eta mūrti-bheda kari' kṛṣṇa-sevā kare
> kṛṣṇera śeṣatā pāñā 'śeṣa' nāma dhare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]]---so many; [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]-[[synonyms:b:bheda]]---different forms; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---serves Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣatā]]---ultimate end; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---having reached; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---assumes the name Śeṣa Nāga.

===== Translation =====

**He is thus called Lord Śeṣa, for He has attained the ultimate end of servitude to Kṛṣṇa. He takes many forms for the service of Kṛṣṇa, and thus He serves the Lord.**
