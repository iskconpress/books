====== CC Ādi 5.23 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurā-dvārakāya nija-rūpa prakāśiyā
> nānā-rūpe vilasaye catur-vyūha haiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]]---in Mathurā; [[synonyms:d:dvārakāya]]---in Dvārakā; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---personal body; [[synonyms:p:prakāśiyā]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in various ways; [[synonyms:v:vilasaye]]---enjoys pastimes; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]] [[synonyms:h:haiñā]]---expanding into four wonderful forms.

===== Translation =====

**He manifests His own, form in Mathurā and Dvārakā. He enjoys pastimes in various ways by expanding into the quadruple forms.**
