====== CC Ādi 5.188 ======

===== Text =====

> koṭi-candra jini' mukha ujjvala-varaṇa
> dāḍimba-bīja-sama danta tāmbūla-carvaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---millions upon millions of moons; [[synonyms:j:jini']]---surpassing; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---face; [[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]-[[synonyms:v:varaṇa]]---bright and brilliant; [[synonyms:d:dāḍimba]]-[[synonyms:b:bīja]]---pomegranate seeds; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like; [[synonyms:d:danta]]---teeth; [[synonyms:t:tāmbūla]]-[[synonyms:c:carvaṇa]]---chewing betel nut.

===== Translation =====

**His face was more beautiful than millions upon millions of moons, and His teeth were like pomegranate seeds because of His chewing betel.**
