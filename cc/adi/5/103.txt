====== CC Ādi 5.103 ======

===== Text =====

> sei padma-nāle haila caudda-bhuvana
> teṅho brahmā hañā sṛṣṭi karila sṛjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāle]]---within the stem of that lotus flower; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---were; [[synonyms:c:caudda]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---the fourteen worlds; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He Himself; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---appearing as Brahmā; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---the creation; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:s:sṛjana]]---created.

===== Translation =====

**Within the stem of that lotus were the fourteen worlds. Thus the Supreme Lord, as Brahmā, created the entire creation.**
