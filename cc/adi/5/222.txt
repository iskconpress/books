====== CC Ādi 5.222 ======

===== Text =====

> caudda-bhuvane yāṅra sabe kare dhyāna
> vaikuṇṭhādi-pure yāṅra līlā-guṇa gāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caudda]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---within the fourteen worlds; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]]---perform meditation; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:p:pure]]---in the abodes of the Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes and pastimes; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---chanting.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone in the fourteen worlds meditates upon Him, and all the denizens of Vaikuṇṭha sing of His qualities and pastimes.**
