====== CC Ādi 5.135 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu guru, kabhu sakhā, kabhu bhṛtya-līlā
> pūrve yena tina-bhāve vraje kaila khelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---spiritual master; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:s:sakhā]]---friend; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes as a servant; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in three different modes; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:k:khelā]]---played with Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes He serves Lord Caitanya as His guru, sometimes as His friend and sometimes as His servant, just as Lord Balarāma played with Lord Kṛṣṇa in these three different modes in Vraja.**
