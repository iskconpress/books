====== CC Ādi 5.134 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-rūpe nityānanda 'ananta'-prakāśa
> sei-bhāve---kahe muñi caitanyera dāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---unlimited manifestations; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in that transcendental emotion; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---He says; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---the servant of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Lord Nityānanda has unlimited incarnations. In transcendental emotion He calls Himself a servant of Lord Caitanya.**
