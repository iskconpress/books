====== Cc. Ādi 5.230 ======

===== Text =====

> se vaiṣṇavera pada-reṇu, tāra pada-chāyā
> adhamere dila prabhu-nityānanda-dayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of all those Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:r:reṇu]]---the dust of the feet; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---their; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:c:chāyā]]---the shade of the feet; [[synonyms:a:adhamere]]---unto this fallen soul; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:d:dayā]]---the mercy of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**The dust and shade of the lotus feet of the Vaiṣṇavas have been granted to this fallen soul by the mercy of Lord Nityānanda.**
