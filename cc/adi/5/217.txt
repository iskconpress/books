====== CC Ādi 5.217 ======

===== Text =====

> mo-adhame dila śrī-govinda daraśana
> kahibāra kathā nahe akathya-kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mo]]-[[synonyms:a:adhame]]---to one as abominable as me; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:g:govinda]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---the audience of Lord Śrī Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kahibāra]]---to speak this; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---words; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there are not; [[synonyms:a:akathya]]---unspeakable; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---narration.

===== Translation =====

**He granted to one as low as me the sight of Lord Govinda. Words cannot describe this, nor is it fit to be disclosed.**
