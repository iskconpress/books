====== CC Ādi 5.144-145 ======

===== Text =====

> guru-varga,---nityānanda, advaita ācārya
> śrīvāsādi, āra yata---laghu, sama, ārya
>
> sabe pāriṣada, sabe līlāra sahāya
> sabā lañā nija-kārya sādhe gaura-rāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:v:varga]]---elders; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:a:advaitaācārya]]---and Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura and others; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---others; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:l:laghu,]] [[synonyms:s:sama,]] [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---junior, equal or superior; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone; [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]---associates; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone; [[synonyms:l:līlārasahāya]]---helpers in the pastimes; [[synonyms:s:sabālañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kārya]]---His own aims; [[synonyms:s:sādhe]]---executes; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**His elders such as Lord Nityānanda, Advaita Ācārya and Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, as well as His other devotees-whether His juniors, equals or superiors-are all His associates who help Him in His pastimes. Lord Gaurāṅga fulfills His aims with their help.**
