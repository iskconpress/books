====== CC Ādi 5.80 ======

===== Text =====

> sei puruṣa sṛṣṭi-sthiti-pralayera kartā
> nānā avatāra kare, jagatera bhartā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:s:sthiti]]-[[synonyms:p:pralayera]]---of creation, maintenance and annihilation; [[synonyms:k:kartā]]---creator; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the material world; [[synonyms:b:bhartā]]---maintainer.

===== Translation =====

**That puruṣa [Kṣīrodakaśāyī Viṣṇu] is the performer of creation, maintenance and destruction. He manifests Himself in many incarnations, for He is the maintainer of the world.**
