====== CC Ādi 5.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vande 'nantādbhutaiśvaryaṁ
> śrī-nityānandam īśvaram
> yasyecchayā tat-svarūpam
> ajñenāpi nirūpyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vande]]---let me offer my obeisances; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---and wonderful; [[synonyms:a:aiśvaryam]]---whose opulence; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānandam]]---unto Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaram]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---whose; [[synonyms:i:icchayā]]---by the will; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpam]]---His identity; [[synonyms:a:ajñena]]---by the ignorant; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:n:nirūpyate]]---can be ascertained.

===== Translation =====

**Let me offer my obeisances to Lord Śrī Nityānanda, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, whose opulence is wonderful and unlimited. By His will, even a fool can understand His identity.**
