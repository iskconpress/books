====== Cc. Ādi 5.60 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-śaktye prakṛti haya gauṇa kāraṇa
> agni-śaktye lauha yaiche karaye jāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---by the energy of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---the material nature; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:g:gauṇa]]---indirect; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---cause; [[synonyms:a:agni]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---by the energy of fire; [[synonyms:l:lauha]]---iron; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---becomes; [[synonyms:j:jāraṇa]]---powerful or red-hot.

===== Translation =====

**Thus prakṛti, by the energy of Lord Kṛṣṇa, becomes the secondary cause, just as iron becomes red-hot by the energy of fire.**
