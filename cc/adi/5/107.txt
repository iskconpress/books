====== CC Ādi 5.107 ======

===== Text =====

> hena nārāyaṇa,---yāṅra aṁśera aṁśa
> sei prabhu nityānanda---sarva-avataṁsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]---such; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:a:aṁśera]]---of the plenary part; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---apart; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of the name Nityānanda; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:avataṁsa]]---the source of all incarnations.

===== Translation =====

**That Lord Nārāyaṇa is a part of a plenary part of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma, who is the source of all incarnations.**
