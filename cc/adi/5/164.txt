====== CC Ādi 5.164 ======

===== Text =====

> namaskāra karite, kā'ra uparete caḍe
> preme kā're vaṁśī māre, kāhāke cāpaḍe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---while offering obeisances, bowing down; [[synonyms:k:kā'ra]]---of someone; [[synonyms:u:uparete]]---on the body; [[synonyms:c:caḍe]]---gets up; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kā're]]---someone; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]---the flute; [[synonyms:m:māre]]---strikes; [[synonyms:k:kāhāke]]---someone; [[synonyms:c:cāpaḍe]]---slaps.

===== Translation =====

**In a joyful mood of love of God he sometimes climbed upon the shoulder of someone offering obeisances, and sometimes he struck others with his flute or mildly slapped them.**
