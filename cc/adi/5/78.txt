====== Cc. Ādi 5.78 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi kahiye tāṅre kṛṣṇera 'kalā' kari
> matsya-kūrmādy-avatārera tiṅho avatārī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I say; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to Him; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]---part of the part; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---making; [[synonyms:m:matsya]]---the fish incarnation; [[synonyms:k:kūrma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the tortoise incarnation and others; [[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of all these incarnations; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:a:avatārī]]---the original source.

===== Translation =====

**Although Kṣīrodaśāyī Viṣṇu is called a kalā of Lord Kṛṣṇa, He is the source of Matsya, Kūrma and the other incarnations.**
