====== CC Ādi 5.94 ======

===== Text =====

> sei ta' puruṣa ananta-brahmāṇḍa sṛjiyā
> saba aṇḍe praveśilā bahu-mūrti hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---innumerable universes; [[synonyms:s:sṛjiyā]]---creating; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:a:aṇḍe]]---in the egglike universes; [[synonyms:p:praveśilā]]---entered; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---taking multifarious forms.

===== Translation =====

**After creating millions of universes, the first puruṣa entered into each of them in a separate form, as Śrī Garbhodakaśāyī.**
