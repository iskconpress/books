====== CC Ādi 5.56 ======

===== Text =====

> mahat-sraṣṭā puruṣa, tiṅho jagat-kāraṇa
> ādya-avatāra kare māyāya īkṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahat]]-[[synonyms:s:sraṣṭā]]---the creator of the total material energy; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the person; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of the material cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:ā:ādya]]---original; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:māyāya]]---over the material energy; [[synonyms:ī:īkṣaṇa]]---glance.

===== Translation =====

**He is known as the first puruṣa, the creator of the total material energy. He, the cause of the universes, the first incarnation, casts His glance over māyā.**
