====== CC Ādi 5.29 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi kevala tāṅra krīḍā-mātra dharma
> tathāpi jīvere kṛpāya kare eka karma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---pastime only; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---characteristic function; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:j:jīvere]]---to the fallen souls; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the causeless mercy; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activity.

===== Translation =====

**Although His pastimes are His only characteristic functions, by His causeless mercy He performs one activity for the fallen souls.**
