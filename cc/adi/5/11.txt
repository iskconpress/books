====== CC Ādi 5.11 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-rūpe āsvādaye kṛṣṇa-sevānanda
> sei balarāma---gaura-saṅge nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in all these forms; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādaye]]---tastes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---the transcendental bliss of serving Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:balarāma]]---that Lord Balarāma; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Gaurasundara; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda.

===== Translation =====

**In all the forms He tastes the transcendental bliss of serving Kṛṣṇa. That same Balarāma is Lord Nityānanda, the companion of Lord Gaurasundara.**
