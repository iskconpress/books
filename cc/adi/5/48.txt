====== CC Ādi 5.48 ======

===== Text =====

> turīya, viśuddha-sattva, 'saṅkarṣaṇa' nāma
> tiṅho yāṅra aṁśa, sei nityānanda-rāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:turīya]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:v:viśuddha]]-[[synonyms:s:sattva]]---pure existence; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]] [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---of whom that Saṅkarṣaṇa is also a partial expansion; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]]---that person is known as Balarāma or Nityānanda.

===== Translation =====

**That Saṅkarṣaṇa, who is transcendental pure goodness, is a partial expansion of Nityānanda Balarāma.**
