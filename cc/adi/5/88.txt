====== CC Ādi 5.88 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata gītāteha punaḥ punaḥ kaya
> sarvadā īśvara-tattva acintya-śakti haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gītāteha]]---in the //Bhagavad-gītā//; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---it is said; [[synonyms:s:sarvadā]]---always; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth of the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:a:acintya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is inconceivable.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the Bhagavad-gītā also states again and again that the Absolute Truth always possesses inconceivable power.**
