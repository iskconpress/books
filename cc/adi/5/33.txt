====== CC Ādi 5.33 ======

===== Text =====

> 'siddha-loka' nāma tāra prakṛtira pāra
> cit-svarūpa, tāṅhā nāhi cic-chakti vikāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:'siddha]]-[[synonyms:l:loka']]---the region of the Siddhas; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of the effulgent atmosphere; [[synonyms:p:prakṛtira]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---beyond this material nature; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---full of knowledge; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---change of the spiritual energy.

===== Translation =====

**That region is called Siddhaloka, and it is beyond the material nature. Its essence is spiritual, but it does not have spiritual varieties.**
