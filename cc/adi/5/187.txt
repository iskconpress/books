====== CC Ādi 5.187 ======

===== Text =====

> candana-lepita-aṅga, tilaka suṭhāma
> matta-gaja jini' mada-manthara payāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:candana]]---with sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:l:lepita]]---smeared; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---body; [[synonyms:t:tilaka]] [[synonyms:s:suṭhāma]]---nicely decorated with //tilaka//; [[synonyms:m:matta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaja]]---a mad elephant; [[synonyms:j:jini']]---surpassing; [[synonyms:m:mada]]-[[synonyms:m:manthara]]---maddened by drinking; [[synonyms:p:payāna]]---movement.

===== Translation =====

**His body was anointed with sandalwood pulp, and He was nicely decorated with tilaka. His movements surpassed those of a maddened elephant.**
