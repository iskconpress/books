====== CC Ādi 5.199 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kṣaṇe vṛndāvane karinu gamana
> prabhura kṛpāte sukhe āinu vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---that very second; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---toward Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:karinu]]---I did; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---starting; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by the mercy of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:ā:āinu]]---arrived; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---at Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**That very second I started for Vṛndāvana, and by His mercy I reached there in great happiness.**
