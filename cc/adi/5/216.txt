====== CC Ādi 5.216 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-dayā more tāṅre dekhāila
> śrī-rādhā-madana-mohane prabhu kari' dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:d:dayā]]---the mercy of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Madanamohana; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:m:madana]]-[[synonyms:m:mohane]]---Rādhā-Madanamohana; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave as my Lord and master.

===== Translation =====

**The mercy of Lord Nityānanda showed me Śrī Madanamohana and gave me Śrī Madanamohana as my Lord and master.**
