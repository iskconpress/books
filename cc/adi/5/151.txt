====== Cc. Ādi 5.151 ======

===== Text =====

> niṣedha karite nāre, yāte choṭa bhāi
> mauna dhari' rahe lakṣmaṇa mane duḥkha pāi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niṣedha]] [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---unable to prohibit Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---because; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---younger brother; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---becoming silent; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmaṇa]]---Lakṣmaṇa; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:p:pāi']]---getting.

===== Translation =====

**As a younger brother He could not stop Lord Rāma from His resolution, and so He remained silent, although unhappy in His mind.**
