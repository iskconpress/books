====== CC Ādi 5.127 ======

===== Text =====

> athavā bhaktera vākya māni satya kari'
> sakala sambhave tāṅte, yāte avatārī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:athavā]]---otherwise; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]] [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---anything spoken by a pure devotee; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I accept; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---as truth; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---everything; [[synonyms:s:sambhave]]---possible; [[synonyms:t:tāṅte]]---in Him; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---since; [[synonyms:a:avatārī]]---the original source of all incarnations.

===== Translation =====

**But I accept it as the truth because it has been said by devotees. Since He is the source of all incarnations, everything is possible in Him.**
