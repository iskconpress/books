====== Cc. Ādi 5.137 ======

===== Text =====

> āpanāke bhṛtya kari' kṛṣṇe prabhu jāne
> kṛṣṇera kalāra kalā āpanāke māne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpanāke]]---Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---considering a servant; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---master; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---He knows; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kalāra]] [[synonyms:k:kalā]]---as a plenary portion of a plenary portion; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāke]]---Himself; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---He accepts.

===== Translation =====

**He considers Himself a servant and knows Kṛṣṇa to be His master. Thus He regards Himself as a fragment of His plenary portion.**
