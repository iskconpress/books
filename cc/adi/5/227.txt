{{description>Therefore who can describe the mercy of the lotus feet of Him [Lord Nityānanda] by whom I have attained the shelter of this Lord Govinda?}}

====== Cc. Ādi 5.227 ======

===== Text =====

> hena ye govinda prabhu, pāinu yāṅhā haite
> tāṅhāra caraṇa-kṛpā ke pāre varṇite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]---thus; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---this Lord Govinda; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---master; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I got; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from whom; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is able; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---to describe.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore who can describe the mercy of the lotus feet of Him [Lord Nityānanda] by whom I have attained the shelter of this Lord Govinda?**
