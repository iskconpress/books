====== CC Ādi 5.201 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya nityānanda, jaya kṛpā-maya
> yāṅhā haite pāinu rūpa-sanātanāśraya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---all glories to the most merciful Lord; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from whom; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I got; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---shelter at the lotus feet of Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**All glory, all glory to the merciful Lord Nityānanda, by whose mercy I have attained shelter at the lotus feet of Śrī Rūpa and Śrī Sanātana.**
