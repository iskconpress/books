====== Cc. Ādi 5.234 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-prabhura guṇa-mahimā apāra
> 'sahasra-vadane' śeṣa nāhi pāya yāṅra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories of transcendental attributes; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unfathomable; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadane]]---in thousands of mouths; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---ultimate end; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose.

===== Translation =====

**The glories of Lord Nityānanda's transcendental attributes are unfathomable. Even Lord Śeṣa with His thousands of mouths cannot find their limit.**
