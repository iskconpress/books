====== CC Ādi 5.138 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛṣāyamāṇau nardantau
> yuyudhāte parasparam
> anukṛtya rutair jantūṁś
> ceratuḥ prākṛtau yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛṣāyamāṇau]]---becoming like bulls; [[synonyms:n:nardantau]]---making roaring sounds; [[synonyms:y:yuyudhāte]]---both used to fight; [[synonyms:p:parasparam]]---each other; [[synonyms:a:anukṛtya]]---imitating; [[synonyms:r:rutaiḥ]]---with cries; [[synonyms:j:jantūn]]---the animals; [[synonyms:c:ceratuḥ]]---used to play; [[synonyms:p:prākṛtau]]---ordinary boys; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just like.

===== Translation =====

**"Acting just like ordinary boys, They played like roaring bulls as They fought each other, and They imitated the calls of various animals."**

===== Purport =====

This and the following quotation are from the //Bhāgavatam// (10.11.40 and 10.15.14).
