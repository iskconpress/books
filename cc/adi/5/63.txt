====== CC Ādi 5.63 ======

===== Text =====

> ghaṭera nimitta-hetu yaiche kumbhakāra
> taiche jagatera kartā---puruṣāvatāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghaṭera]]---of the earthen pot; [[synonyms:n:nimitta]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---original cause; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:kumbhakāra]]---the potter; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:j:jagatera]] [[synonyms:k:kartā]]---the creator of the material world; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the //puruṣa// incarnation, or Kāraṇārṇavaśāyī Viṣṇu.

===== Translation =====

**Just as the original cause of an earthen pot is the potter, so the creator of the material world is the first puruṣa incarnation [Kāraṇārṇavaśāyī Viṣṇu].**
