====== Cc. Ādi 5.47 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvāśraya, sarvādbhuta, aiśvarya apāra
> 'ananta' kahite nāre mahimā yāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the shelter of everything; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful in every respect; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---opulences; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unfathomed; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---Ananta Śeṣa; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---cannot speak; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]] [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---the glories of whom.

===== Translation =====

**He [Saṅkarṣaṇa] is the shelter of everything. He is wonderful in every respect, and His opulences are infinite. Even Ananta cannot describe His glory.**
