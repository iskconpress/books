{{description>That Mahā-puruṣa is identical with the Personality of Godhead. He is the original incarnation, the seed of all others, and the shelter of everything.}}

====== Cc. Ādi 5.82 ======

===== Text =====

> ādyāvatāra, mahā-puruṣa, bhagavān
> sarva-avatāra-bīja, sarvāśraya-dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ādya]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the original incarnation; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---Lord Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bīja]]---the seed of all different kinds of incarnations; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---the shelter of everything.

===== Translation =====

**That Mahā-puruṣa is identical with the Personality of Godhead. He is the original incarnation, the seed of all others, and the shelter of everything.**
