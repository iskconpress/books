====== CC Ādi 5.122 ======

===== Text =====

> sanakādi bhāgavata śune yāṅra mukhe
> bhagavānera guṇa kahe, bhāse prema-sukhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the great sages headed by Sanaka, Sananda, etc.; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hear; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from whose mouth; [[synonyms:b:bhagavānera]]---of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---float; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in the transcendental bliss of love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**The four Kumāras hear Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam from His lips, and they in turn repeat it in the transcendental bliss of love of Godhead.**
