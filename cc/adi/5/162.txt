====== CC Ādi 5.162 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāra ālaye aho-rātra-saṅkīrtana
> tāhāte āilā teṅho pāñā nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:ā:ālaye]]---at my house; [[synonyms:a:ahaḥ]]-[[synonyms:r:rātra]]---day and night; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---on account of this; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:p:pāñānimantraṇa]]---getting an invitation.

===== Translation =====

**At my house there was saṅkīrtana day and night, and therefore he visited there, having been invited.**
