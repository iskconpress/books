====== CC Ādi 5.81 ======

===== Text =====

> sṛṣṭy-ādi-nimitte yei aṁśera avadhāna
> sei ta' aṁśere kahi 'avatāra' nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:n:nimitte]]---for the cause of creation, maintenance and annihilation; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---which; [[synonyms:a:aṁśera]] [[synonyms:a:avadhāna]]---manifestation of the part; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---that certainly; [[synonyms:a:aṁśere]] [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak about that plenary expansion; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---by the name "incarnation."

===== Translation =====

**That fragment of the Mahā-puruṣa who appears for the purpose of creation, maintenance and annihilation is called an incarnation.**
