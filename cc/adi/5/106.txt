====== CC Ādi 5.106 ======

===== Text =====

> hiraṇya-garbha, antaryāmī, jagat-kāraṇa
> yāṅra aṁśa kari' kare virāṭa-kalpana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hiraṇya]]-[[synonyms:g:garbha]]---of the name Hiraṇyagarbha; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:y:yāmī]]---the Supersoul; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of the material world; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking as His expansion; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:v:virāṭa]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpana]]---conception of the universal form.

===== Translation =====

**He is the Supersoul, Hiraṇyagarbha, the cause of the material world. The universal form is conceived as His expansion.**
