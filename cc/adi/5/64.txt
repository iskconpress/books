====== CC Ādi 5.64 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa---kartā, māyā tāṅra karena sahāya
> ghaṭera kāraṇa---cakra-daṇḍādi upāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kartā]]---the creator; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]---material energy; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]---assistance; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭera]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of the earthen pot; [[synonyms:c:cakra]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the wheel, the rod, and so on; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---instruments.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa is the creator, and māyā only helps Him as an instrument, just like the potter's wheel and other instruments, which are the instrumental causes of a pot.**
