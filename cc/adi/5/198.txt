====== Cc. Ādi 5.198 ======

===== Text =====

> ki dekhinu ki śuninu, kariye vicāra
> prabhu-ājñā haila vṛndāvana yāibāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:d:dekhinu]]---what did I see; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:ś:śuninu]]---what did I hear; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---I began to consider; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order of my Lord; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yāibāra]]---to go.

===== Translation =====

**I thought about what I had seen and heard and concluded that the Lord had ordered me to proceed to Vṛndāvana at once.**
