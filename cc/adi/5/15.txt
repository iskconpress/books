====== CC Ādi 5.15 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvaga, ananta, vibhu---vaikuṇṭhādi dhāma
> kṛṣṇa, kṛṣṇa-avatārera tāhāñi viśrāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:g:ga]]---all-pervading; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:v:vibhu]]---greatest; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---all the places known as Vaikuṇṭhaloka; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of the incarnations of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāhāñi]]---there; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---the residence.

===== Translation =====

**That Vaikuṇṭha region is all-pervading, infinite and supreme. It is the residence of Lord Kṛṣṇa and His incarnations.**
