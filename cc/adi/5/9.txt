====== Cc. Ādi 5.9 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane karena kṛṣṇa-līlāra sahāya
> sṛṣṭi-līlā-kārya kare dhari' cāri kāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]] [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]---assistance in the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of the pastimes of creation; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---the work; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:c:cārikāya]]---four bodies.

===== Translation =====

**He Himself helps in the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa, and He does the work of creation in four other forms.**
