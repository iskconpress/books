====== CC Ādi 5.16 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāra upari-bhāge 'kṛṣṇa-loka'-khyāti
> dvārakā-mathurā-gokula---tri-vidhatve sthiti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:u:upari]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāge]]---on the top; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:k:khyāti]]---the planet known as Kṛṣṇaloka; [[synonyms:d:dvārakā]]-[[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:g:gokula]]---the three places known as Dvārakā, Mathurā and Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhatve]]---in three departments; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---situated.

===== Translation =====

**In the highest region of that spiritual sky is the spiritual planet called Kṛṣṇaloka. It has three divisions-Dvārakā, Mathurā and Gokula.**
