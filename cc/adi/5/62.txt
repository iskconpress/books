====== Cc. Ādi 5.62 ======

===== Text =====

> māyā-aṁśe kahi tāre nimitta-kāraṇa
> seha nahe, yāte kartā-hetu---nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśe]]---to the other portion of the material nature; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I say; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto her; [[synonyms:n:nimitta]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---immediate cause; [[synonyms:s:seha]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---that cannot be; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---because; [[synonyms:k:kartā]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---the original cause; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**The māyā aspect of material nature is the immediate cause of the cosmic manifestation. But it also cannot be the real cause, for the original cause is Lord Nārāyaṇa.**
