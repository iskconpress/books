====== CC Ādi 5.85 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi sarvāśraya tiṅho, tāṅhāte saṁsāra
> antarātmā-rūpe tiṅho jagat-ādhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the shelter of everything; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---He (the Lord); [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāte]]---in Him; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---the material creation; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of the Supersoul; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādhāra]]---the support of the whole creation.

===== Translation =====

**Although the Lord is the shelter of everything and although all the universes rest in Him, He, as the Supersoul, is also the support of everything.**
