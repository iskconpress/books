====== Cc. Ādi 5.65 ======

===== Text =====

> dūra haite puruṣa kare māyāte avadhāna
> jīva-rūpa vīrya tāte karena ādhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from a distance; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:māyāte]]---unto the material energy; [[synonyms:a:avadhāna]]---glancing over; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the living entities; [[synonyms:v:vīrya]]---seed; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in her; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:ādhāna]]---impregnation.

===== Translation =====

**The first puruṣa casts His glance at māyā from a distance, and thus He impregnates her with the seed of life in the form of the living entities.**
