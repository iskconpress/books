{{description>All glory, all glory to Lord Nityānanda Balarāma, by whose mercy I have attained shelter in the transcendental abode of Vṛndāvana.}}

====== Cc. Ādi 5.200 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya nityānanda, nityānanda-rāma
> yāṅhāra kṛpāte pāinu vṛndāvana-dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]]---to Lord Balarāma, who appeared as Nityānanda; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by whose mercy; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I got; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---shelter at Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**All glory, all glory to Lord Nityānanda Balarāma, by whose mercy I have attained shelter in the transcendental abode of Vṛndāvana.**
