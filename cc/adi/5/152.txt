====== CC Ādi 5.152 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-avatāre jyeṣṭha hailā sevāra kāraṇa
> kṛṣṇake karāila nānā sukha āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---in the incarnation of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jyeṣṭha]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---He became the elder brother; [[synonyms:s:sevāra]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---for the purpose of service; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇake]]---to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happinesses; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Kṛṣṇa appeared, He [Balarāma] became His elder brother to serve Him to His heart's content and make Him enjoy all sorts of happiness.**
