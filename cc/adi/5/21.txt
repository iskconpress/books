====== CC Ādi 5.21 ======

===== Text =====

> prema-netre dekhe tāra svarūpa-prakāśa
> gopa-gopī-saṅge yāṅhā kṛṣṇera vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:n:netre]]---with the eyes of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---one sees; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---its; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation of identity; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]---cowherd boys; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the cowherd damsels; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]]---where; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**But with the eyes of love of Godhead one can see its real identity as the place where Lord Kṛṣṇa performs His pastimes with the cowherd boys and cowherd girls.**
