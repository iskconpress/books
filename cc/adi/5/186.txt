====== CC Ādi 5.186 ======

===== Text =====

> suvarṇa-kuṇḍala karṇe, svarṇāṅgada-vālā
> pāyete nūpura bāje, kaṇṭhe puṣpa-mālā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍala]]---gold earrings; [[synonyms:k:karṇe]]---on the ears; [[synonyms:s:svarṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅgada]]---golden armlets; [[synonyms:v:vālā]]---and bangles; [[synonyms:p:pāyete]]---on the feet; [[synonyms:n:nūpura]]---ankle bells; [[synonyms:b:bāje]]---tinkle; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭhe]]---on the neck; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---flower garland.

===== Translation =====

**He wore golden earrings on His ears, and golden armlets and bangles. He wore tinkling anklets on His feet and a garland of flowers around His neck.**
