====== CC Ādi 5.159 ======

===== Text =====

> veda-guhya kathā ei ayogya kahite
> tathāpi kahiye tāṅra kṛpā prakāśite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:veda]]---like the //Vedas//; [[synonyms:g:guhya]]---very confidential; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---incident; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:ayogya]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---not fit to disclose; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I speak; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:p:prakāśite]]---to manifest.

===== Translation =====

**To disclose it is not proper, for it should be kept as confidential as the Vedas, yet I shall speak of it to make His mercy known to all.**
