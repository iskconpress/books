====== Cc. Ādi 5.38 ======

===== Text =====

> nirviśeṣa-brahma sei kevala jyotir-maya
> sāyujyera adhikārī tāṅhā pāya laya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirviśeṣa]]-[[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the impersonal Brahman effulgence; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:j:jyotiḥ]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---effulgent rays; [[synonyms:s:sāyujyera]]---the liberation called //sāyujya// (oneness with the Supreme); [[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---one who is fit for; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there (in the impersonal Brahman effulgence); [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---merging.

===== Translation =====

**That impersonal Brahman effulgence consists only of the effulgent rays of the Lord. Those fit for sāyujya liberation merge into that effulgence.**
