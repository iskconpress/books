====== CC Ādi 5.212 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-purandara śrī-madana-gopāla
> rāsa-vilāsī sākṣāt vrajendra-kumāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:p:purandara]]---the chief Deity of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:madana]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Madana Gopāla; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsī]]---the enjoyer of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:k:kumāra]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Madana Gopāla, the chief Deity of Vṛndāvana, is the enjoyer of the rāsa dance and is directly the son of the King of Vraja.**
