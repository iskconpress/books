====== CC Ādi 11.49 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīmanta, gokula-dāsa hariharānanda
> śivāi, nandāi, avadhūta paramānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:manta]]---of the name Śrīmanta; [[synonyms:g:gokula]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Gokula dāsa; [[synonyms:h:hariharānanda]]---of the name Hariharānanda; [[synonyms:ś:śivāi]]---of the name Śivāi; [[synonyms:n:nandāi]]---of the name Nandāi; [[synonyms:a:avadhūtaparamānanda]]---of the name Avadhūta Paramānanda.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīmanta was the forty-fifth, Gokula dāsa the forty-sixth, Hariharānanda the forty-seventh, Śivāi the forty-eighth, Nandāi the forty-ninth and Paramānanda the fiftieth.**
