====== Cc. Ādi 11.4 ======

===== Text =====

> tasya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-
> sat-premāmara-śākhinaḥ
> ūrdhva-skandhāvadhūtendoḥ
> śākhā-rūpān gaṇān numaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasya]]---His; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---of eternal love of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:amara]]---indestructible; [[synonyms:ś:śākhinaḥ]]---of the tree; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhva]]---very high; [[synonyms:s:skandha]]---branch; [[synonyms:a:avadhūta]]-[[synonyms:i:indoḥ]]---of Śrī Nityānanda; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpān]]---in the form of different branches; [[synonyms:g:gaṇān]]---to the devotees; [[synonyms:n:numaḥ]]---I offer my respects.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu is the topmost branch of the indestructible tree of eternal love of Godhead, Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu. I offer my respectful obeisances to all the subbranches of that topmost branch.**
