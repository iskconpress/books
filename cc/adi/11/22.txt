====== CC Ādi 11.22 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha vaidya upādhyāya mahāśaya
> yāṅhāra darśane kṛṣṇa-prema-bhakti haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]] [[synonyms:v:vaidya]]---the physician Raghunātha; [[synonyms:u:upādhyāya]] [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---a great personality with the title Upādhyāya; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by visiting; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---awakened.

===== Translation =====

**The physician Raghunātha, also known as Upādhyāya, was so great a devotee that simply by seeing him one would awaken his dormant love of Godhead.**
