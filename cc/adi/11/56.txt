====== CC Ādi 11.56 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvaśākhā-śreṣṭha vīrabhadra gosāñi
> tāṅra upaśākhā yata, tāra anta nāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---the best of all the branches; [[synonyms:v:vīrabhadra]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---of the name Vīrabhadra Gosāñi; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:u:upaśākhā]]---His subbranches; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of them; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---limit; [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---there is not.

===== Translation =====

**Among all the branches of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu, Vīrabhadra Gosāñi was the topmost. His subbranches were unlimited.**
