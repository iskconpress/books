====== Cc. Ādi 11.38 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-sadāśiva kavirāja---baḍa mahāśaya
> śrī-puruṣottama-dāsa---tāṅhāra tanaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:sadāśiva]] [[synonyms:k:kavirāja]]---of the name Śrī Sadāśiva Kavirāja; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---great; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---respectable gentleman; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Śrī Puruṣottama dāsa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:t:tanaya]]---his son.

===== Translation =====

**The twenty-third and twenty-fourth prominent devotees of Nityānanda Prabhu were Sadāśiva Kavirāja and his son Puruṣottama dāsa, who was the tenth gopāla.**
