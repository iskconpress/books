====== Cc. Ādi 11.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya mahāprabhu śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya
> tāṅhāra caraṇāśrita yei, sei dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---known as Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrita]]---all who have taken shelter at His lotus feet; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he is; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---glorious.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! Anyone who has taken shelter at His lotus feet is glorious.**
