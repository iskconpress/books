====== CC Ādi 11.6 ======

===== Text =====

> mālākarera icchā jale bāḍe śākhā-gaṇa
> prema-phula-phale bhari' chāila bhuvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:k:kārera]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---by the water of His wish; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increase; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the branches; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:phula]]-[[synonyms:p:phale]]---with flowers and fruits; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:c:chāila]]---covered; [[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---the whole world.

===== Translation =====

**Watered by the desire of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, these branches and subbranches have grown unlimitedly and covered the entire world with fruits and flowers.**
