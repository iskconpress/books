====== CC Ādi 11.53 ======

===== Text =====

> nartaka gopāla, rāmabhadra, gaurāṅga-dāsa
> nṛsiṁha-caitanya, mīnaketana rāmadāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nartaka]] [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the dancer Gopāla; [[synonyms:r:rāmabhadra]]---of the name Rāmabhadra; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Gaurāṅga dāsa; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of the name Nṛsiṁha-caitanya; [[synonyms:m:mīnaketana]] [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Mīnaketana Rāmadāsa.

===== Translation =====

**The dancer Gopāla was the seventy-first, Rāmabhadra the seventy-second, Gaurāṅga dāsa the seventy-third, Nṛsiṁha-caitanya the seventy-fourth and Mīnaketana Rāmadāsa the seventy-fifth.**

===== Purport =====

The //Gaura-gaṇoddeśa-dīpikā,// verse 68, describes Mīnaketana Rāmadāsa as an incarnation of Saṅkarṣaṇa.
