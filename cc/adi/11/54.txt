====== CC Ādi 11.54 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-dāsa---nārāyaṇīra nandana
> 'caitanya-maṅgala' yeṅho karila racana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---son of Nārāyaṇī; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]---the book of the name //Caitanya-maṅgala//; [[synonyms:y:yeṅho]]---who; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:r:racana]]---composition.

===== Translation =====

**Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura, the son of Śrīmatī Nārāyaṇī, composed Śrī Caitanya-maṅgala [later known as Śrī Caitanya-bhāgavata].**
