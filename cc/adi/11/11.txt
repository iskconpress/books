====== CC Ādi 11.11 ======

===== Text =====

> adyāpi yāṅhāra kṛpā-mahimā ha-ite
> caitanya-nityānanda gāya sakala jagate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adyāpi]]---until today; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glorious; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Śrī Caitanya-Nityānanda; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sing; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in the world.

===== Translation =====

**It is by the glorious mercy of Śrī Vīrabhadra Gosāñi that people all over the world now have the chance to chant the names of Caitanya and Nityānanda.**
