====== CC Ādi 11.60 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahilāṅ ei nityānanda-gaṇa
> yāṅhāra avadhi nā pāya 'sahasra-vadana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahilāṅ]]---described; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:a:avadhi]]---limitation; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:paya]]---get; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---the thousand-mouthed Śeṣa Nāga, on whom Lord Viṣṇu lies.

===== Translation =====

**I have briefly described only some of the followers and devotees of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu. Even the thousand-mouthed Śeṣa Nāga cannot describe all of these unlimited devotees.**
