====== Cc. Ādi 11.46 ======

===== Text =====

> nārāyaṇa, kṛṣṇadāsa āra manohara
> devānanda---cāri bhāi nitāi-kiṅkara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---of the name Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---of the name Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:m:manohara]]---of the name Manohara; [[synonyms:d:devānanda]]---of the name Devānanda; [[synonyms:c:cāribhāi]]---four brothers; [[synonyms:n:nitāi]]-[[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---servants of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**The thirty-second, thirty-third, thirty-fourth and thirty-fifth prominent devotees were Nārāyaṇa, Kṛṣṇadāsa, Manohara and Devānanda, who always engaged in the service of Lord Nityānanda.**
