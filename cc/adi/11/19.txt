====== Cc. Ādi 11.19 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsudeva gīte kare prabhura varṇane
> kāṣṭha-pāṣāṇa drave yāhāra śravaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---of the name Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:g:gīte]]---while singing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Nityānanda Prabhu and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:varṇane]]---in description; [[synonyms:k:kāṣṭha]]---wood; [[synonyms:p:pāṣāṇa]]---stone; [[synonyms:d:drave]]---melt; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing.

===== Translation =====

**When Vāsudeva Ghoṣa performed kīrtana, describing Lord Caitanya and Nityānanda, even wood and stone would melt upon hearing it.**
