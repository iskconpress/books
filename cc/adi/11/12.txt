====== CC Ādi 11.12 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vīrabhadra-gosāñira la-inu śaraṇa
> yāṅhāra prasāde haya abhīṣṭa-pūraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vīrabhadra]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Śrī Vīrabhadra Gosāñi; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:inu]]---I take; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]---shelter; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by mercy; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it becomes so; [[synonyms:a:abhīṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---fulfillment of desire.

===== Translation =====

**I therefore take shelter of the lotus feet of Vīrabhadra Gosāñi so that by His mercy my great desire to write Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta will be properly guided.**
