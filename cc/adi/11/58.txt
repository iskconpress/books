====== CC Ādi 11.58 ======

===== Text =====

> ei sarva-śākhā pūrṇa---pakva prema-phale
> yāre dekhe, tāre diyā bhāsāila sakale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---all branches; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---complete; [[synonyms:p:pakva]] [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:phale]]---with ripened fruits of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yāre]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---whomever they see; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---distributing to him; [[synonyms:b:bhāsāila]]---overflooded; [[synonyms:s:sakale]]---all of them.

===== Translation =====

**All these branches, the devotees of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, being full of ripened fruits of love of Kṛṣṇa, distributed these fruits to all they met, flooding them with love of Kṛṣṇa.**
