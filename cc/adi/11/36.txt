====== CC Ādi 11.36 ======

===== Text =====

> rāḍhe yāṅra janma kṛṣṇadāsa dvijavara
> śrī-nityānandera teṅho parama kiṅkara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāḍhe]]---in West Bengal; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:j:janma]]---birth; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---of the name Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:d:dvija]]-[[synonyms:v:vara]]---the best //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānandera]]---of Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---first-class; [[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---servant.

===== Translation =====

**The twenty-first devotee of Śrī Nityānanda in Bengal was Kṛṣṇadāsa Brāhmaṇa, who was a first-class servant of the Lord.**

===== Purport =====

Rāḍha-deśa refers to the part of Bengal where the Ganges does not flow.
