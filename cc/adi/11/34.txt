====== CC Ādi 11.34 ======

===== Text =====

> balarāma dāsa---kṛṣṇa-prema-rasāsvādī
> nityānanda-nāme haya parama unmādī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:balarāma]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Balarāma dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the nectar of always merging in love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādī]]---fully tasting; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in the name of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---greatly; [[synonyms:u:unmādī]]---maddened.

===== Translation =====

**Balarāma dāsa always fully tasted the nectar of love of Kṛṣṇa. Upon hearing the name of Nityānanda Prabhu, he would become greatly maddened.**
