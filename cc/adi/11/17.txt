====== CC Ādi 11.17 ======

===== Text =====

> gadādhara dāsa gopībhāve pūrṇānanda
> yāṅra ghare dānakeli kaila nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Gadādhara dāsa; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in the ecstasy of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---fully in transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in whose house; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---performance of //dānakeli-līlā//; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Gadādhara dāsa was always fully absorbed in ecstasy as a gopī. In his house Lord Nityānanda enacted the drama Dānakeli.**
