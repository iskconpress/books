====== Cc. Ādi 11.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-advaita, jaya nityānanda
> jaya jaya mahāprabhura sarva-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---unto Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---unto Lord Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---devotees.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Advaita Prabhu, Nityānanda Prabhu and all the devotees of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu!**
