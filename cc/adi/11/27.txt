====== Cc. Ādi 11.27 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānande samarpila jāti-kula-pāṅti
> śrī-caitanya-nityānande kari prāṇapati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānande]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:s:samarpila]]---he offered; [[synonyms:j:jāti]]---caste distinction; [[synonyms:k:kula]]---family; [[synonyms:p:pāṅti]]---fellowship; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:n:nityānande]]---in Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---making; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pati]]---the Lords of his life.

===== Translation =====

**Making Lord Caitanya and Lord Nityānanda the Lords of his life, Gaurīdāsa Paṇḍita sacrificed everything for the service of Lord Nityānanda, even the fellowship of his own family.**
