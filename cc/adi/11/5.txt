====== CC Ādi 11.5 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-nityānanda-vṛkṣera skandha gurutara
> tāhāte janmila śākhā-praśākhā vistara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣera]]---of the tree known as Śrī Nityānanda; [[synonyms:s:skandha]]---main branch; [[synonyms:g:gurutara]]---extremely heavy; [[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---from that branch; [[synonyms:j:janmila]]---grew; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---branches; [[synonyms:p:praśākhā]]---subbranches; [[synonyms:v:vistara]]---expansively.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu is an extremely heavy branch of the Śrī Caitanya tree. From that branch grow many branches and subbranches.**
