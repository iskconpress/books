====== CC Ādi 11.9 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara ha-iyā kahāya mahā-bhāgavata
> veda-dharmātīta hañā veda-dharme rata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---being; [[synonyms:k:kahāya]]---calls Himself; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---great devotee; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the principles of Vedic religion; [[synonyms:a:atīta]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:d:dharme]]---in the Vedic system; [[synonyms:r:rata]]---engaged.

===== Translation =====

**Although Vīrabhadra Gosāñi was the Supreme Personality of Godhead, He presented Himself as a great devotee. And although the Supreme Godhead is transcendental to all Vedic injunctions, He strictly followed the Vedic rituals.**
