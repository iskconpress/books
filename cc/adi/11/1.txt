====== CC Ādi 11.1 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-padāmbhoja-
> bhṛṅgān prema-madhūnmadān
> natvākhilān teṣu mukhyā
> likhyante katicin mayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of Lord Śrī Nityānanda; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:a:ambhoja]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:b:bhṛṅgān]]---the bumblebees; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]---by the honey; [[synonyms:u:unmadān]]---maddened; [[synonyms:n:natvā]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:a:akhilān]]---to all of them; [[synonyms:t:teṣu]]---out of them; [[synonyms:m:mukhyāḥ]]---the chief; [[synonyms:l:likhyante]]---being described; [[synonyms:k:katicit]]---a few of them; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by me.

===== Translation =====

**After offering my obeisances unto all of the devotees of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu, who are like bumblebees collecting honey from His lotus feet, I shall try to describe those who are the most prominent.**
