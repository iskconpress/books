====== Cc. Ādi 3.67 ======

===== Text =====

> āṅgopāṅga astra kare sva-kārya-sādhana
> 'aṅga'-śabdera artha āra śuna diyā mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āṅga]]-[[synonyms:u:upāṅga]]---plenary parts and associates; [[synonyms:a:astra]]---weapons; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:k:kārya]]---of their own business; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---as the accomplishment; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabdera]]---of the word //aṅga//; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**His plenary parts and associates perform the work of weapons as their own specific duties. Please hear from me another meaning of the word "aṅga."**
