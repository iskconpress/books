====== CC Ādi 3.17 ======

===== Text =====

> aiśvarya-jñāne vidhi-bhajana kariyā
> vaikuṇṭhake yāya catur-vidha mukti pāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---in knowledge of the opulences; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---according to rules and regulations; [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---worship; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhake]]---to Vaikuṇṭha; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---they go; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]]---four kinds; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---achieving.

===== Translation =====

**"By performing such regulated devotional service in awe and veneration, one may go to Vaikuṇṭha and attain the four kinds of liberation.**
