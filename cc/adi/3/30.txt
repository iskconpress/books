====== CC Ādi 3.30 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-siṁhera navadvīpe avatāra
> siṁha-grīva, siṁha-vīrya, siṁhera huṅkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:s:siṁhera]]---of the lionlike Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---at Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnation; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:g:grīva]]---having the neck of a lion; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:v:vīrya]]---the strength of a lion; [[synonyms:s:siṁherahuṅkāra]]---the roar of a lion.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the lionlike Lord Caitanya has appeared in Navadvīpa. He has the shoulders of a lion, the powers of a lion, and the loud voice of a lion.**
