====== CC Ādi 3.100 ======

===== Text =====

> nāma vinu kali-kāle dharma nāhi āra
> kali-kāle kaiche habe kṛṣṇa avatāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---except for the holy name; [[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religion; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:k:kaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation.

===== Translation =====

**"In this Age of Kali there is no religion other than the chanting of the holy name of the Lord, but how in this age will the Lord appear as an incarnation?**
