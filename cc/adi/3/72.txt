====== Cc. Ādi 3.72 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita, nityānanda---caitanyera dui aṅga
> aṅgera avayava-gaṇa kahiye upāṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---limbs; [[synonyms:a:aṅgera]]---of the limbs; [[synonyms:a:avayava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the constituent parts; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I say; [[synonyms:u:upāṅga]]---parts.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Prabhu and Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu are both plenary portions of Lord Caitanya. Thus They are the limbs [aṅgas] of His body. The parts of these two limbs are called the upāṅgas.**
