====== Cc. Ādi 3.13 ======

===== Text =====

> yatheṣṭa vihari' kṛṣṇa kare antardhāna
> antardhāna kari' mane kare anumāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]---as much as He wishes; [[synonyms:v:vihari']]---enjoying; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]]---disappearance; [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---disappearing; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---He makes; [[synonyms:a:anumāna]]---consideration.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa enjoys His transcendental pastimes as long as He wishes, and then He disappears. After disappearing, however, He thinks thus:**
