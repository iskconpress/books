====== CC Ādi 3.38 ======

===== Text =====

> idānīṁ dvāpare tiṅho hailā kṛṣṇa-varṇa
> ei saba śāstrāgama-purāṇera marma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:idānīm]]---now; [[synonyms:d:dvāpare]]---in the Dvāpara-yuga; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---was; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇa]]---blackish color; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgama]]---and Vedic literatures; [[synonyms:p:purāṇera]]---of the //Purāṇas//; [[synonyms:m:marma]]---the core.

===== Translation =====

**Now, in the Dvāpara-yuga, the Lord had descended in a blackish hue. This is the essence of the statements in the Purāṇas and other Vedic literatures with reference to the context.**
