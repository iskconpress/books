====== Cc. Ādi 3.15 ======

===== Text =====

> sakala jagate more kare vidhi-bhakti
> vidhi-bhaktye vraja-bhāva pāite nāhi śakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in the universe; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to Me; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---they do; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---regulative devotional service; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---by regulative devotional service; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the feelings of those in Vraja; [[synonyms:p:pāite]]---to obtain; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---the power.

===== Translation =====

**"Everywhere in the world people worship Me according to scriptural injunctions. But simply by following such regulative principles one cannot attain the loving sentiments of the devotees in Vrajabhūmi.**
