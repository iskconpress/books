====== Cc. Ādi 3.96 ======

===== Text =====

> prakaṭiyā dekhe ācārya sakala saṁsāra
> kṛṣṇa-bhakti gandha-hīna viṣaya-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prakaṭiyā]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---He saw; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---material existence; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---of devotion to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]-[[synonyms:h:hīna]]---without a trace; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---of the sense objects; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---affairs.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya having appeared, He found the world devoid of devotional service to Śrī Kṛṣṇa because people were engrossed in material affairs.**
