====== CC Ādi 3.35 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra yugāvatāra jāni' garga mahāśaya
> kṛṣṇera nāma-karaṇe kariyāche nirṇaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation for the age; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:g:garga]]---Garga Muni; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---the great personality; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:k:karaṇe]]---in the name-giving ceremony; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nirṇaya]]---ascertainment.

===== Translation =====

**Knowing Him [Lord Caitanya] to be the incarnation for Kali-yuga, Garga Muni, during the naming ceremony of Kṛṣṇa, predicted His appearance.**
