====== CC Ādi 3.31 ======

===== Text =====

> sei siṁha vasuk jīvera hṛdaya-kandare
> kalmaṣa-dvirada nāśe yāṅhāra huṅkāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]---lion; [[synonyms:v:vasuk]]---let Him sit; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of the living entities; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---of the heart; [[synonyms:k:kandare]]---in the cavern; [[synonyms:k:kalmaṣa]]---of sins; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:r:rada]]---the elephant; [[synonyms:n:nāśe]]---destroys; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāre]]---the roar.

===== Translation =====

**May that lion be seated in the core of the heart of every living being. Thus with His resounding roar may He drive away one's elephantine vices.**
