====== CC Ādi 3.62 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhu tuli' hari bali' prema-dṛṣṭye cāya
> kariyā kalmaṣa nāśa premete bhāsāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhutuli']]---raising the arms; [[synonyms:h:haribali']]---chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭye]]---with His glance of deep love; [[synonyms:c:cāya]]---He looks; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---causing; [[synonyms:k:kalmaṣa]]---to sins; [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destruction; [[synonyms:p:premete]]---in love of God; [[synonyms:b:bhāsāya]]---He floods.

===== Translation =====

**Raising His arms, chanting the holy name and looking upon all with deep love, He drives away all sins and floods everyone with love of Godhead.**
