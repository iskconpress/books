====== CC Ādi 3.26 ======

===== Text =====

> yuga-dharma-pravartana haya aṁśa haite
> āmā vinā anye nāre vraja-prema dite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---of the religion of the age; [[synonyms:p:pravartana]]---the inauguration; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---the plenary portion; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---for Me; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except; [[synonyms:a:anye]]---another; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---is not able; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love like that of the residents of Vraja; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to bestow.

===== Translation =====

**"My plenary portions can establish the principles of religion for each age. No one but Me, however, can bestow the kind of loving service performed by the residents of Vraja.**
