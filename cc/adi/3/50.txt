====== Cc. Ādi 3.50 ======

===== Text =====

> vyakta kari' bhāgavate kahe bāra bāra
> kali-yuge dharma---nāma-saṅkīrtana sāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vyakta]]---evident; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---they say; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---time and time again; [[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:y:yuge]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religion; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---congregational chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence.

===== Translation =====

**In Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam it is repeatedly and clearly said that the essence of religion in the Age of Kali is the chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.**
