====== CC Ādi 3.92 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya gosāñi prabhura bhakta-avatāra
> kṛṣṇa-avatāra-hetu yāṅhāra huṅkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Advaita Ācārya Gosāñi; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation of a devotee; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---of the incarnation; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---the cause; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---loud calls.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya Gosvāmī is an incarnation of the Lord as a devotee. His loud calling was the cause for Kṛṣṇa's incarnation.**
