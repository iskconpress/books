====== Cc. Ādi 3.85 ======

===== Text =====

> pratyakṣe dekhaha nānā prakaṭa prabhāva
> alaukika karma, alaukika anubhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratyakṣe]]---directly; [[synonyms:d:dekhaha]]---just see; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---manifested; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---influence; [[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activities; [[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:a:anubhāva]]---realizations in Kṛṣṇa consciousness.

===== Translation =====

**One can also directly see Lord Caitanya's manifest influence in His uncommon deeds and uncommon Kṛṣṇa conscious realization.**
