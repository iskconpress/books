====== CC Ādi 3.19 ======

===== Text =====

> yuga-dharma pravartāimu nāma-saṅkīrtana
> cāri bhāva-bhakti diyā nācāmu bhuvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religion of the age; [[synonyms:p:pravartāimu]]---I shall inaugurate; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---of the moods; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:n:nācāmu]]---I shall cause to dance; [[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---the world.

===== Translation =====

**"I shall personally inaugurate the religion of the age-nāma-saṅkīrtana, the congregational chanting of the holy name. I shall make the world dance in ecstasy, realizing the four mellows of loving devotional service.**
