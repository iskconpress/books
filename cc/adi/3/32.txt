====== CC Ādi 3.32 ======

===== Text =====

> prathama līlāya tāṅra 'viśvambhara' nāma
> bhakti-rase bharila, dharila bhūta-grāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathama]]---first; [[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---in the pastimes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:v:viśvambharanāma]]---the name Viśvambhara; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---with the mellow of devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bharila]]---He filled; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---saved; [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]---all the living entities.

===== Translation =====

**In His early pastimes He is known as Viśvambhara because He floods the world with the nectar of devotion and thus saves the living beings.**
