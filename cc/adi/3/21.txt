====== Cc. Ādi 3.21 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane nā kaile dharma śikhāna nā yāya
> ei ta' siddhānta gītā-bhāgavate gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---if not practiced; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religion; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāna]]---the teaching; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---does not advance; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---conclusion; [[synonyms:g:gītā]]---in the //Bhagavad-gītā//; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---they sing.

===== Translation =====

**"Unless one practices devotional service himself, he cannot teach it to others. This conclusion is indeed confirmed throughout the Gītā and Bhāgavatam.**
