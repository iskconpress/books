====== CC Ādi 3.60 ======

===== Text =====

> jīvera kalmaṣa-tamo nāśa karibāre
> aṅga-upāṅga-nāma nānā astra dhare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of the living entity; [[synonyms:k:kalmaṣa]]---of sinful activities; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---the darkness; [[synonyms:n:nāśa]] [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---for destroying; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---associates; [[synonyms:u:upāṅga]]---devotees; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---holy names; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:a:astra]]---weapons; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---He holds.

===== Translation =====

**The sinful life of the living beings results from ignorance. To destroy that ignorance, He has brought various weapons, such as His plenary associates, His devotees and the holy name.**
