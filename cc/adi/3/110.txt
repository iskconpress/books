====== CC Ādi 3.110 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyera avatāre ei mukhya hetu
> bhaktera icchāya avatare dharma-setu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---in the incarnation; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---principal; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---cause; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the devotee; [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---by the desire; [[synonyms:a:avatare]]---He descends; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:s:setu]]---protector of religion.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore the principal reason for Śrī Caitanya's descent is this appeal by Advaita Ācārya. The Lord, the protector of religion, appears by the desire of His devotee.**
