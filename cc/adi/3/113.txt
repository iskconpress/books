====== CC Ādi 3.113 ======

===== Text =====

> caturtha ślokera artha haila suniścite
> avatīrṇa hailā gaura prema prakāśite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturtha]]---fourth; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:n:niścite]]---very surely; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---incarnated; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of God; [[synonyms:p:prakāśite]]---to manifest.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have surely determined the meaning of the fourth verse. Lord Gaurāṅga [Lord Caitanya] appeared as an incarnation to preach unalloyed love of God.**
