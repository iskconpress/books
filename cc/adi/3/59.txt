====== CC Ādi 3.59 ======

===== Text =====

> pratyakṣa tāṅhāra tapta-kāñcanera dyuti
> yāṅhāra chaṭāya nāśe ajñāna-tamastati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratyakṣa]]---vivid; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:t:tapta]]---molten; [[synonyms:k:kāñcanera]]---of gold; [[synonyms:d:dyuti]]---effulgence; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:c:chaṭāya]]---by the luster; [[synonyms:n:nāśe]]---destroys; [[synonyms:a:ajñāna]]---of ignorance; [[synonyms:t:tamastati]]---the extent of the darkness.

===== Translation =====

**One can vividly see His glowing complexion of molten gold, which dispels the darkness of ignorance.**
