====== CC Ādi 3.74 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda gosāñi sākṣāt haladhara
> advaita ācārya gosāñi sākṣāt īśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Nityānanda Gosāñi; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:h:hala]]-[[synonyms:d:dhara]]---Lord Balarāma, the holder of the plow; [[synonyms:a:advaitaācāryagosāñi]]---Śrī Advaita Ācārya Gosāñi; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda Gosāñi is directly Haladhara [Lord Balarāma], and Advaita Ācārya is the Personality of Godhead Himself.**
