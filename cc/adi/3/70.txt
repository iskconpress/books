====== CC Ādi 3.70 ======

===== Text =====

> jala-śāyī antar-yāmī yei nārāyaṇa
> seho tomāra aṁśa, tumi mūla nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---lying in the water; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:y:yāmī]]---indwelling Supersoul; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---He who; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:s:seho]]---He; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---plenary portion; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---original; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**The manifestation of the Nārāyaṇa who predominates in everyone's heart, as well as the Nārāyaṇa who lives in the waters [Kāraṇa, Garbha and Kṣīra], is Your plenary portion. You are therefore the original Nārāyaṇa.**
