====== Cc. Ādi 3.84 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgavata, bhārata-śāstra, āgama, purāṇa
> caitanya-kṛṣṇa-avatāre prakaṭa pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---//Mahābhārata//; [[synonyms:ā:āgama]]---works of Vedic literature; [[synonyms:p:purāṇa]]---the //Purāṇas//; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---as Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---in the incarnation; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---displayed; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---evidence.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, the Mahābhārata, the Purāṇas and other works of Vedic literature all give evidence to prove that Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the incarnation of Kṛṣṇa.**
