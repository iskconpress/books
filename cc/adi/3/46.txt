====== CC Ādi 3.46 ======

===== Text =====

> candanera aṅgada-bālā, candana-bhūṣaṇa
> nṛtya-kāle pari' karena kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:candanera]]---of sandalwood; [[synonyms:a:aṅgada]]---and armlets; [[synonyms:b:bālā]]---bangles; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---of sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣaṇa]]---decorations; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time of dancing; [[synonyms:p:pari']]---putting on; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---congregational chanting of the name of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**He is decorated with sandalwood bangles and armlets and anointed with the pulp of sandalwood. He especially wears these decorations to dance in śrī-kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana.**
