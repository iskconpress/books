====== CC Ādi 3.24 ======

===== Text =====

> utsīdeyur ime lokā
> na kuryāṁ karma ced aham
> saṅkarasya ca kartā syām
> upahanyām imāḥ prajāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:udsīdeyuḥ]]---would fall into ruin; [[synonyms:i:ime]]---these; [[synonyms:l:lokāḥ]]---worlds; [[synonyms:n:nakuryām]]---did not perform; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---action; [[synonyms:c:cet]]---if; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarasya]]---of unwanted population; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kartā]]---a creator; [[synonyms:s:syām]]---would become; [[synonyms:u:upahanyām]]---would spoil; [[synonyms:i:imāḥ]]---these; [[synonyms:p:prajāḥ]]---living entities.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'If I did not show the proper principles of religion, all these worlds would fall into ruin. I would be a cause of unwanted population and would spoil all these living beings.'**
