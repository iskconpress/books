====== CC Ādi 3.104 ======

===== Text =====

> tulasī-dala-mātreṇa
> jalasya culukena vā
> vikrīṇīte svam ātmānaṁ
> bhaktebhyo bhakta-vatsalaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tulasī]]---of //tulasī//; [[synonyms:d:dala]]---a leaf; [[synonyms:m:mātreṇa]]---by only; [[synonyms:j:jalasya]]---of water; [[synonyms:c:culukena]]---by a palmful; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vikrīṇīte]]---sells; [[synonyms:s:svam]]---His own; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---self; [[synonyms:b:bhaktebhyaḥ]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsalaḥ]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa, who is affectionate to His devotees.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Kṛṣṇa, who is very affectionate toward His devotees, sells Himself to a devotee who offers Him merely a tulasī leaf and a palmful of water."**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from the //Gautamīya-tantra.//
