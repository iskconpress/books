====== CC Ādi 3.37 ======

===== Text =====

> śukla, rakta, pīta-varṇa---ei tina dyuti
> satya-tretā-kali-kāle dharena śrī-pati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śukla]]---white; [[synonyms:r:rakta]]---red; [[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇa]]---the color yellow; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:d:dyuti]]---lusters; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---in Satya-yuga; [[synonyms:t:tretā]]---in Tretā-yuga; [[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:d:dharena]]---manifests; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:pati]]---the husband of the goddess of fortune.

===== Translation =====

**White, red and yellow---these are the three bodily lusters that the Lord, the husband of the goddess of fortune, assumes in the ages of Satya, Tretā and Kali respectively.**
