====== Cc. Ādi 3.61 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaktira virodhī karma-dharma vā adharma
> tāhāra 'kalmaṣa' nāma, sei mahā-tamaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---to devotional service; [[synonyms:v:virodhī]]---averse; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activity; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:a:adharma]]---irreligious; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:k:kalmaṣa]]---sin; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---great darkness.

===== Translation =====

**The greatest ignorance consists of activities, whether religious or irreligious, that are opposed to devotional service. They are to be known as sins [kalmaṣa].**
