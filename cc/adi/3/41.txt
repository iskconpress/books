====== CC Ādi 3.41 ======

===== Text =====

> tapta-hema-sama-kānti, prakāṇḍa śarīra
> nava-megha jini kaṇṭha-dhvani ye gambhīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tapta]]-[[synonyms:h:hema]]---as molten gold; [[synonyms:s:sama]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---same luster; [[synonyms:p:prakāṇḍa]]---enormous; [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]---body; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:m:megha]]---new clouds; [[synonyms:j:jini]]---conquering; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the sound of the voice; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---deep.

===== Translation =====

**The luster of His expansive body resembles molten gold. The deep sound of His voice conquers the thundering of newly assembled clouds.**
