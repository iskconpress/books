====== CC Ādi 3.65 ======

===== Text =====

> anya avatāre saba sainya-śastra saṅge
> caitanya-kṛṣṇera sainya aṅga-upāṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---in incarnations; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sainya]]---soldiers; [[synonyms:ś:śastra]]---and weapons; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa as Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:s:sainya]]---soldiers; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---plenary parts; [[synonyms:u:upāṅge]]---and associates.

===== Translation =====

**In other incarnations the Lord descended with armies and weapons, but in this incarnation His soldiers are His plenary parts and associates.**
