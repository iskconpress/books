====== Cc. Ādi 3.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-caitanya-prabhuṁ vande
> yat-pādāśraya-vīryataḥ
> saṅgṛhṇāty ākara-vrātād
> ajñaḥ siddhānta-san-maṇīn

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhum]]---to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---of whom; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---of the shelter of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:v:vīryataḥ]]---from the power; [[synonyms:s:saṅgṛhṇāti]]---collects; [[synonyms:ā:ākara]]-[[synonyms:v:vrātāt]]---from the multitude of mines in the form of scriptures; [[synonyms:a:ajñaḥ]]---a fool; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---of conclusion; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇīn]]---the best jewels.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. By the potency of the shelter of His lotus feet, even a fool can collect the valuable jewels of conclusive truth from the mines of the revealed scriptures.**
