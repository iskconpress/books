====== CC Ādi 3.80 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bhāgavata-sandarbha'-granthera maṅgalācaraṇe
> e-śloka jīva-gosāñi kariyāchena vyākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:s:sandarbha]]-[[synonyms:g:granthera]]---of the book called //Bhāgavata-sandarbha//; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇe]]---in the auspicious introduction; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Jīva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]]---has made; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---in explaining.

===== Translation =====

**In the auspicious introduction of Bhāgavata-sandarbha, Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī has given the following verse as an explanation.**
