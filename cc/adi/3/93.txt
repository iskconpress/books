====== CC Ādi 3.93 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa yadi pṛthivīte karena avatāra
> prathame karena guru-vargera sañcāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīte]]---on the earth; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---makes; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:p:prathame]]---first; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---makes; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:v:vargera]]---of the group of respectable predecessors; [[synonyms:s:sañcāra]]---the advent.

===== Translation =====

**Whenever Śrī Kṛṣṇa desires to manifest His incarnation on earth, first He creates the incarnations of His respectable predecessors.**
