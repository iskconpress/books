====== CC Ādi 3.23 ======

===== Text =====

> paritrāṇāya sādhūnāṁ
> vināśāya ca duṣkṛtām
> dharma-saṁsthāpanārthāya
> sambhavāmi yuge yuge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paritrāṇāya]]---for the deliverance; [[synonyms:s:sādhūnām]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vināśāya]]---for the destruction; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:d:duṣkṛtām]]---of the miscreants; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:s:saṁsthāpana]]-[[synonyms:a:arthāya]]---for the purpose of establishing; [[synonyms:s:sambhavāmi]]---I appear; [[synonyms:y:yugeyuge]]---in every age.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'To deliver the pious and to annihilate the miscreants, as well as to reestablish the principles of religion, I Myself appear, millennium after millennium.'**

===== Purport =====

Texts 22 and 23 were spoken by Lord Kṛṣṇa in the //Bhagavad-gītā// (4.7-8). Texts 24 and 25, which follow, are also from the //Bhagavad-gītā// (3.24, 21).
