====== Cc. Ādi 3.75 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsādi pāriṣada sainya saṅge lañā
> dui senā-pati bule kīrtana kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Śrīvāsa and others; [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]---associates; [[synonyms:s:sainya]]---soldiers; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:s:senā]]-[[synonyms:p:pati]]---captains; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---travel; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanakariyā]]---chanting the holy name.

===== Translation =====

**These two captains, with Their soldiers such as Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, travel everywhere, chanting the holy name of the Lord.**
