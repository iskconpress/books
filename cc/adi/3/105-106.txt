====== CC Ādi 3.105-106 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ślokārtha ācārya karena vicāraṇa
> kṛṣṇake tulasī-jala deya yei jana

> tāra ṛṇa śodhite kṛṣṇa karena cintana---
> 'jala-tulasīra sama kichu ghare nāhi dhana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:v:vicāraṇa]]---considering; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇake]]---to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---//tulasī// and water; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---gives; [[synonyms:y:yeijana]]---that person who; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---to Him; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṇa]]---the debt; [[synonyms:ś:śodhite]]---to pay; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:c:cintana]]---thinking; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:t:tulasīra]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal to water and //tulasī//; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---wealth.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya considered the meaning of the verse in this way: Not finding any way to repay the debt He owes to one who offers Him a tulasī leaf and water, Lord Kṛṣṇa thinks, "There is no wealth in My possession that is equal to a tulasī leaf and water."**
