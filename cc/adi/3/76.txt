====== CC Ādi 3.76 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṣaṇḍa-dalana-vānā nityānanda rāya
> ācārya-huṅkāre pāpa-pāṣaṇḍī palāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṣaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:dalana]]---of trampling the atheists; [[synonyms:v:vānā]]---having the feature; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---the honorable; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāre]]---by the war cry; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]---sins; [[synonyms:p:pāṣaṇḍī]]---and atheists; [[synonyms:p:palāya]]---run away.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda's very features indicate that He is the subduer of the unbelievers. All sins and unbelievers flee from the loud shouts of Advaita Ācārya.**
