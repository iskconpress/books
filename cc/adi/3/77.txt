====== CC Ādi 3.77 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkīrtana-pravartaka śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya
> saṅkīrtana-yajñe tāṅre bhaje, sei dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]-[[synonyms:p:pravartaka]]---the initiator of congregational chanting; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---of congregational chanting; [[synonyms:y:yajñe]]---by the sacrifice; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---worships; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---fortunate.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya is the initiator of saṅkīrtana [congregational chanting of the holy name of the Lord]. One who worships Him through saṅkīrtana is fortunate indeed.**
