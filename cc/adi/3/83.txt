====== CC Ādi 3.83 ======

===== Text =====

> aham eva kvacid brahman
> sannyāsāśramam āśritaḥ
> hari-bhaktiṁ grāhayāmi
> kalau pāpa-hatān narān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---somewhere; [[synonyms:b:brahman]]---O //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśramam]]---the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:ā:āśritaḥ]]---taking recourse to; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktim]]---devotional service to the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:g:grāhayāmi]]---I shall give; [[synonyms:k:kalau]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:h:hatān]]---sinful; [[synonyms:n:narān]]---to men.

===== Translation =====

**"O learned brāhmaṇa, sometimes I accept the renounced order of life to induce the fallen people of the Age of Kali to accept devotional service to the Lord."**
