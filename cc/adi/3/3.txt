====== CC Ādi 3.3 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīya ślokera artha kaila vivaraṇa
> caturtha ślokera artha śuna bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]]---third; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:c:caturtha]]---fourth; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees.

===== Translation =====

**I have given the purport of the third verse. Now, O devotees, please listen to the meaning of the fourth with full attention.**
