====== CC Ādi 3.33 ======

===== Text =====

> ḍubhṛñ dhātura artha---poṣaṇa, dhāraṇa
> puṣila, dharila prema diyā tri-bhuvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubhṛñ]]---known as //ḍubhṛñ// (//bhṛ//); [[synonyms:d:dhātura]]---of the verbal root; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:p:poṣaṇa]]---nourishing; [[synonyms:d:dhāraṇa]]---maintaining; [[synonyms:p:puṣila]]---nourished; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---maintained; [[synonyms:p:premadiyā]]---distributing love of God; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---in the three worlds.

===== Translation =====

**The verbal root "ḍubhṛñ" [which is the root of the word "viśvambhara"] indicates nourishing and maintaining. He [Lord Caitanya] nourishes and maintains the three worlds by distributing love of God.**
