====== CC Ādi 3.68 ======

===== Text =====

> 'aṅga'-śabde aṁśa kahe śāstra-paramāṇa
> aṅgera avayava 'upāṅga'-vyākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word //aṅga,// or limb; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---part; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---of the scriptures; [[synonyms:p:paramāṇa]]---the evidence; [[synonyms:a:aṅgera]]---of the limb; [[synonyms:a:avayava]]---the constituent part; [[synonyms:u:upāṅga]]-[[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---the exposition of the word //upāṅga.//

===== Translation =====

**According to the evidence of the revealed scriptures, a bodily limb [aṅga] is also called a part [aṁśa], and a part of a limb is called a partial part [upāṅga].**
