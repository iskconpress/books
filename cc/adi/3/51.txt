====== Cc. Ādi 3.51 ======

===== Text =====

> iti dvāpara urv-īśa
> stuvanti jagad-īśvaram
> nānā-tantra-vidhānena
> kalāv api yathā śṛṇu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:d:dvāpare]]---in the Dvāpara Age; [[synonyms:u:uru]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśa]]---O King; [[synonyms:s:stuvanti]]---they praise; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvaram]]---the Lord of the universe; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:t:tantra]]---of scriptures; [[synonyms:v:vidhānena]]---by the regulations; [[synonyms:k:kalau]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---in which manner; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---please hear.

===== Translation =====

**"O King, in this way people in Dvāpara-yuga worshiped the Lord of the universe. In Kali-yuga they also worship the Supreme Personality of Godhead by the regulations of the revealed scriptures. Kindly now hear of that from me.**

===== Purport =====

This verse is spoken by Saint Karabhājana in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (11.5.31).
