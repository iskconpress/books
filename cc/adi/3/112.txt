====== CC Ādi 3.112 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ślokera artha kahi saṅkṣepera sāra
> bhaktera icchāya kṛṣṇera sarva avatāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I relate; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepera]]---of conciseness; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the pith; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the devotee; [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---by the desire; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations.

===== Translation =====

**The essence of the meaning of this verse is that Lord Kṛṣṇa appears in all His innumerable eternal forms because of the desires of His pure devotees.**
