====== CC Ādi 17.322 ======

===== Text =====

> navamete 'bhakti-kalpa-vṛkṣera varṇana'
> śrī-caitanya-mālī kailā vṛkṣa āropaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:navamete]]---in the Ninth Chapter; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣera]]---of the desire tree of devotional service; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---the description; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:mālī]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as the gardener; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]---the tree; [[synonyms:ā:āropaṇa]]---implantation.

===== Translation =====

**The Ninth Chapter describes the desire tree of devotional service. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Himself is the gardener who planted it.**
