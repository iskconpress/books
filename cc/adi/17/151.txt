====== CC Ādi 17.151 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata duṅhāra kathā haya ṭhāre-ṭhore
> bhitarera artha keha bujhite nā pāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---conversation; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---took place; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāre]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhore]]---with different indications; [[synonyms:b:bhitarera]]---inner; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is not able.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the Kazi and the Lord talked with each other with various indications, but no outsider could understand the inner meaning of their conversation.**
