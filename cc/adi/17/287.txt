====== CC Ādi 17.287 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ihoṅ kṛṣṇa nahe, ihoṅ nārāyaṇa mūrti'
> eta bali' tāṅre sabhe kare nati-stuti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihoṅ]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:i:ihoṅ]]---this is; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---the form; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:s:sabhe]]---all the gopīs; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:n:nati]]-[[synonyms:s:stuti]]---obeisances and prayers.

===== Translation =====

**"He is not Kṛṣṇa! He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyaṇa." After saying this, they offered obeisances and the following respectful prayers.**
