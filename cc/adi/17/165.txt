====== CC Ādi 17.165 ======

===== Text =====

> tomarā jīyāite nāra,---vadha-mātra sāra
> naraka ha-ite tomāra nāhika nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomarā]]---you Muslims; [[synonyms:j:jīyāite]]---bring to life; [[synonyms:n:nāra]]---cannot; [[synonyms:v:vadha]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---killing only; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence; [[synonyms:n:naraka]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from hell; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is not; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance.

===== Translation =====

**"Since you Muslims cannot bring killed animals back to life, you are responsible for killing them. Therefore you are going to hell; there is no way for your deliverance.**
