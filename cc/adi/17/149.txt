====== CC Ādi 17.149 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlāmbara cakravartī haya tomāra nānā
> se-sambandhe hao tumi āmāra bhāginā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlāmbara]] [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---of the name Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---maternal grandfather; [[synonyms:s:se]]-[[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---by such a relationship; [[synonyms:h:hao]]---become; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:b:bhāginā]]---nephew (the son of my sister).

===== Translation =====

**"Nīlāmbara Cakravartī is Your maternal grandfather, and by this relationship You are thus my nephew.**
