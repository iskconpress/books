====== CC Ādi 17.274 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ādi-līlāra kaila sūtra gaṇana
> vistāri varṇilā ihā dāsa vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:l:līlara]]---of the //ādi-līlā// (the first portion of Lord Caitanya's pastimes); [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---synopsis; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---enumeration; [[synonyms:v:vistāri]]---elaborately; [[synonyms:v:varṇilā]]---described; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have summarized the incidents of the ādi-līlā. Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has described them elaborately [in his Caitanya-bhāgavata].**
