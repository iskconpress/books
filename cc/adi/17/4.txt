====== CC Ādi 17.4 ======

===== Text =====

> vidyā-saundarya-sad-veśa-
> sambhoga-nṛtya-kīrtanaiḥ
> prema-nāma-pradānaiś ca
> gauro dīvyati yauvane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidyā]]---education; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---beauty; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---nice dress; [[synonyms:s:sambhoga]]---enjoyment; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanaiḥ]]---by chanting; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of the Lord, which induces one to become a devotee; [[synonyms:p:pradānaiḥ]]---by distributing; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:g:gauraḥ]]---Lord Śrī Gaurasundara; [[synonyms:d:dīvyati]]---illuminated; [[synonyms:y:yauvane]]---in His youth.

===== Translation =====

**Exhibiting His scholarship, beauty and fine dress, Lord Caitanya danced, as He chanted and distributed the holy name of the Lord to awaken dormant love of Kṛṣṇa. Thus Lord Śrī Gaurasundara shone in His youthful pastimes.**
