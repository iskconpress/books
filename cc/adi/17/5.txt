====== CC Ādi 17.5 ======

===== Text =====

> yauvana-praveśe aṅgera aṅga vibhūṣaṇa
> divya vastra, divya veśa, mālya-candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yauvana]]-[[synonyms:p:praveśe]]---on the entrance of His youth; [[synonyms:a:aṅgera]]---of the body; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---limbs; [[synonyms:v:vibhūṣaṇa]]---ornaments; [[synonyms:d:divya]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---garments; [[synonyms:d:divya]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:v:veśa]]---dress; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]---garland; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---(smeared with) sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**As He entered His youth, the Lord decorated Himself with ornaments, dressed Himself in fine cloth, garlanded Himself with flowers and smeared Himself with sandalwood.**
