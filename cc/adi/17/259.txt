====== CC Ādi 17.259 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-jña gosāñi jāni' sabāra durgati
> ghare vasi' cinte tā'-sabāra avyāhati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---all-knowing; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:d:durgati]]---degradation; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:c:cinte]]---contemplates; [[synonyms:t:tā']]---of them; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:a:avyāhati]]---the rescue.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, being omniscient, could understand the degradation of these students. Thus He sat at home, contemplating how to rescue them.**
