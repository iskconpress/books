====== CC Ādi 17.72 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇe prabhu nāma-mahimā kahila
> śuniyā paḍuyā tāhāṅ artha-vāda kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories of the holy name; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---explained; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:paḍuyā]]---the students; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāda]]---interpretation; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did.

===== Translation =====

**Once the Lord explained the glories of the holy name to the devotees, but some ordinary students who heard Him fashioned their own interpretation.**
