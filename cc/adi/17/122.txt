====== Cc. Ādi 17.122 ======

===== Text =====

> 'haraye namaḥ, kṛṣṇa yādavāya namaḥ
> gopāla govinda rāma śrī-madhusūdana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haraye]] [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances to Lord Hari; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---O Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yādavāya]]---unto the descendant of the Yadu dynasty; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---all obeisances; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---of the name Gopāla; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---of the name Govinda; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---of the name Rāma; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:madhusūdana]]---of the name Śrī Madhusūdana.

===== Translation =====

**[All the devotees sang this popular song along with the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra.] "Haraye namaḥ, kṛṣṇa yādavāya namaḥ gopāla govinda rāma śrī-madhusūdana."**
