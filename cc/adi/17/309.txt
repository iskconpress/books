====== CC Ādi 17.309 ======

===== Text =====

> adbhuta caitanya-līlāya yāhāra viśvāsa
> sei jana yāya caitanyera pada pāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---in the pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---anyone whose; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---faith; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---person; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---makes progress; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---near.

===== Translation =====

**Only a person who has firm faith in the wonderful pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu can approach His lotus feet.**
