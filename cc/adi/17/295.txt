====== Cc. Ādi 17.295 ======

===== Text =====

> sei nanda-suta---ihaṅ caitanya-gosāñi
> sei baladeva---ihaṅ nityānanda bhāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:s:suta]]---the same son of Nanda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:i:ihaṅ]]---now; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:baladeva]]---the selfsame Baladeva; [[synonyms:i:ihaṅ]]---now; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---Nityānanda Prabhu, the brother of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**The former son of Nanda Mahārāja is now Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the former Baladeva, Kṛṣṇa's brother, is now Nityānanda Prabhu, the brother of Lord Caitanya.**
