====== CC Ādi 17.27 ======

===== Text =====

> taru-sama sahiṣṇutā vaiṣṇava karibe
> bhartsana-tāḍane kāke kichu nā balibe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:taru]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like a tree; [[synonyms:s:sahiṣṇutā]]---forbearance; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotee; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---should practice; [[synonyms:b:bhartsana]]---rebuking; [[synonyms:t:tāḍane]]---chastising; [[synonyms:k:kāke]]---unto anyone; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:b:balibe]]---will utter.

===== Translation =====

**"A devotee engaged in chanting the holy name of the Lord should practice forbearance like that of a tree. Even if rebuked or chastised, he should not say anything to others to retaliate.**
