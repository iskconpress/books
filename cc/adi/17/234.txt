====== CC Ādi 17.234 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' prabhu 'bala' 'bala' balena āveśe
> śrīvāsa varṇena vṛndāvana-līlā-rase

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bala]] [[synonyms:b:bala]]---go on speaking, go on speaking; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---He says; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:v:varṇena]]---describes; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---the transcendental mellow of the pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this reply, the Lord said in ecstasy, "Go on talking! Go on talking!" Thus Śrīvāsa described the transcendental mellow pastimes of Śrī Vṛndāvana.**
