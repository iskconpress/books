====== CC Ādi 17.297 ======

===== Text =====

> prema-bhakti diyā teṅho bhāsā'la jagate
> tāṅra caritra loke nā pāre bujhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāsā'la]]---overflooded; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in the world; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:c:caritra]]---character; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---able; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu overflooded the entire world by distributing transcendental loving service. No one can understand His character and activities.**
