====== CC Ādi 17.273 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅge nityānanda, candraśekhara ācārya
> mukunda-datta,---ei tina kaila sarva kārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in His company; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:c:candraśekhara]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Candraśekhara Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]-[[synonyms:d:datta]]---Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]---these three; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---necessary activities.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted sannyāsa, three personalities were with Him to perform all the necessary activities. They were Nityānanda Prabhu, Candraśekhara Ācārya and Mukunda Datta.**
