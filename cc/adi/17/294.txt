====== CC Ādi 17.294 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vrajeśvara---ihaṅ jagannātha pitā
> sei vrajeśvarī---ihaṅ śacīdevī mātā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vrajeśvara]]---the King of Vraja; [[synonyms:i:ihaṅ]]---now; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---the father of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vrajeśvarī]]---Queen of Vraja; [[synonyms:i:ihaṅ]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śacīdevī]]---Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Father Nanda, the King of Vrajabhūmi, is now Jagannātha Miśra, the father of Caitanya Mahāprabhu. And mother Yaśodā, the Queen of Vrajabhūmi, is now Śacīdevī, Lord Caitanya's mother.**
