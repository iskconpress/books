====== CC Ādi 17.47 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-ghāṭe vṛkṣa-tale rahe ta' vasiyā
> eka dina bale kichu prabhuke dekhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:g:ghāṭe]]---on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---underneath a tree; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing.

===== Translation =====

**Since leprosy is an infectious disease, Gopāla Cāpāla left the village to sit down on the bank of the Ganges underneath a tree. One day, however, he saw Caitanya Mahāprabhu passing by and spoke to Him as follows.**
