====== Cc. Ādi 17.279 ======

===== Text =====

> śyāmasundara, śikhipiccha-guñjā-vibhūṣaṇa
> gopa-veśa, tri-bhaṅgima, muralī-vadana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śyāma]]-[[synonyms:s:sundara]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa, who has a bluish color; [[synonyms:ś:śikhi]]-[[synonyms:p:piccha]]---with a peacock feather on the head; [[synonyms:g:guñjā]]---a garland of //guñjā// (small berries or conchshells); [[synonyms:v:vibhūṣaṇa]]---decorations; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---with the dress of a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅgima]]---curved in three places; [[synonyms:m:muralī]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---holding a flute to His mouth.

===== Translation =====

**He has a bluish complexion, a peacock feather on His head, a guñjā garland and the decorations of a cowherd boy. His body is curved in three places, and He holds a flute to His mouth.**
