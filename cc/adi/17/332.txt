====== CC Ādi 17.332 ======

===== Text =====

> ye yei aṁśa kahe, śune sei dhanya
> acire milibe tāre śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---any part of this; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---anyone who describes; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---anyone who hears; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---glorious; [[synonyms:a:acire]]---very soon; [[synonyms:m:milibe]]---will meet; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---Him; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Anyone who describes or hears any part of this elaborate subject will very soon receive the causeless mercy of Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
