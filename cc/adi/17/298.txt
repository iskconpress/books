====== Cc. Ādi 17.298 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-ācārya-gosāñi bhakta-avatāra
> kṛṣṇa avatāriyā kailā bhaktira pracāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Śrī Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---spiritual master; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnation of a devotee; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:avatāriyā]]---descending; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---propagation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Advaita Ācārya Prabhu appeared as an incarnation of a devotee. He is in the category of Kṛṣṇa, but He descended to this earth to propagate devotional service.**
