====== CC Ādi 17.300 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsādi yata mahāprabhura bhakta-gaṇa
> nija nija bhāve karena caitanya-sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]---in their own respective; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---emotions; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---do; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, have their own emotional humors in which they render service unto Him.**
