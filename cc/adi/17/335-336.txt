====== CC Ādi 17.335-336 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-svarūpa-śrī-rūpa-śrī-sanātana
> śrī-raghunātha-dāsa, āra śrī-jīva-caraṇa
>
> śire dhari vandoṅ, nitya karoṅ tāṅra āśa
> caitanya-caritāmṛta kahe kṛṣṇadāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Śrī Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Jīva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on the head; [[synonyms:d:dhari]]---placing; [[synonyms:v:vandoṅ]]---I worship; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---always; [[synonyms:k:karoṅ]]---I do; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---their; [[synonyms:ā:āśa]]---hope to serve; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritāmṛta]]---the book named //Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta//; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---describes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---Śrīla Kṛṣṇadāsa Kavirāja Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**I wish to place the lotus feet of the Gosvāmīs on my head. Their names are Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara, Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī, Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī and Śrī Jīva Gosvāmī. Placing their lotus feet on my head, always hoping to serve them, I, Kṛṣṇadāsa, narrate Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta, following in their footsteps.**
