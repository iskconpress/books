====== CC Ādi 17.299 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhya, dāsya,---dui bhāva sahaja tāṅhāra
> kabhu prabhu karena tāṅre guru-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhya]]---fraternity; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]---servitude; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---ecstasies; [[synonyms:s:sahaja]]---natural; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to Him; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---of spiritual master; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---treatment.

===== Translation =====

**His natural emotions were always on the platform of fraternity and servitude, but the Lord sometimes treated Him as His spiritual master.**
