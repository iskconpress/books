====== CC Ādi 17.163 ======

===== Text =====

> kali-kāle taiche śakti nāhika brāhmaṇe
> ataeva go-vadha keha nā kare ekhane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---such; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is none; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇe]]---in the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:v:vadha]]---killing of cows; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---execute; [[synonyms:e:ekhane]]---at the present.

===== Translation =====

**"Formerly there were powerful brāhmaṇas who could make such experiments using Vedic hymns, but now, because of the Kali-yuga, brāhmaṇas are not so powerful. Therefore the killing of cows and bulls for rejuvenation is forbidden.**
