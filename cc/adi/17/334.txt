====== Cc. Ādi 17.334 ======

===== Text =====

> yata yata bhakta-gaṇa vaise vṛndāvane
> namra hañā śire dharoṅ sabāra caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:y:yata]]---each and every one; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---reside; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:n:namra]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming humble; [[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on my head; [[synonyms:d:dharoṅ]]---I place; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances to all the residents of Vṛndāvana. I wish to place their lotus feet on my head in great humbleness.**
