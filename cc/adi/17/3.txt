====== CC Ādi 17.3 ======

===== Text =====

> kaiśora-līlāra sūtra karila gaṇana
> yauvana-līlāra sūtra kari anukrama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaiśora]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the activities before His youth; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---synopsis; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---I have done; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---an enumeration; [[synonyms:y:yauvana]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes of youth; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---synopsis; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I enumerate; [[synonyms:a:anukrama]]---in chronological order.

===== Translation =====

**I have already given a synopsis of the kaiśora-līlā of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Now let me enumerate His youthful pastimes in chronological order.**
