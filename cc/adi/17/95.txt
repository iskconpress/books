====== CC Ādi 17.95 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāse kahena prabhu kariyā viṣāda
> loka bhaya pāya,---mora haya aparādha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāse]]---unto Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:v:viṣāda]]---morose; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---become afraid; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord became morose and said to Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, "When I adopted the mood of Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva, people were greatly afraid. Therefore I stopped, since causing fear among people is an offense."**
