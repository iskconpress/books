====== CC Ādi 17.108 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva janme chilā tumi jagat-āśraya
> paripūrṇa bhagavān---sarvaiśvaryamaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]] [[synonyms:j:janme]]---in the previous birth; [[synonyms:c:chilā]]---were; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---universe; [[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---shelter; [[synonyms:p:paripūrṇa]]---with full potencies; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---full of all opulences.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear sir, in Your previous birth You were the shelter of all creation, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, full of all opulences.**
