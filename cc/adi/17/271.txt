====== CC Ādi 17.271 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāratī kahena,---tumi īśvara, antaryāmī
> ye karāha, se kariba,---svatantra nahi āmi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāratī]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Keśava Bhāratī replied; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:antaryāmī]]---You know everything from within; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:karāha]]---You cause to do; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I must do; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:n:nahi]]---not; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I.

===== Translation =====

**Keśava Bhāratī replied to the Lord, "You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the Supersoul. I must do whatever You cause me to do. I am not independent of You."**
