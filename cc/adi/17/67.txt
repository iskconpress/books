====== CC Ādi 17.67 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṅgī kari' jñāna-mārga karila vyākhyāna
> krodhāveśe prabhu tāre kaila avajñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṅgī]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing it in fun; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:m:mārga]]---the path of philosophical speculation; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the mood of anger; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---to Him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:a:avajñāna]]---disrespect.

===== Translation =====

**Thus He whimsically began to explain the path of philosophical speculation, and the Lord, in His anger, seemingly disrespected Him.**
