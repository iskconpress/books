====== Cc. Ādi 17.48 ======

===== Text =====

> grāma-sambandhe āmi tomāra mātula
> bhāginā, mui kuṣṭha-vyādhite hañāchi vyākula

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---in a village relationship; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I (am); [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:m:mātula]]---maternal uncle; [[synonyms:b:bhāgina]]---nephew; [[synonyms:m:mui]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kuṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:v:vyādhite]]---by the disease of leprosy; [[synonyms:h:hañāchi]]---have become; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]]---too much afflicted.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear nephew, I am Your maternal uncle in our village relationship. Please see how greatly this attack of leprosy has afflicted me.**
