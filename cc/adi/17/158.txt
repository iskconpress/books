====== CC Ādi 17.158 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra vedete āche go-vadhera vāṇī
> ataeva go-vadha kare baḍa baḍa muni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:v:vedete]]---in Your Vedic literature; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhera]]---for cow-killing; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---injunction; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:v:vadha]]---cow-killing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very, very great; [[synonyms:m:muni]]---sages.

===== Translation =====

**As a learned scholar, the Kazi challenged Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "In Your Vedic scriptures there is an injunction for killing a cow. On the strength of this injunction, great sages peformed sacrifices involving cow-killing."**
