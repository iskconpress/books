====== Cc. Ādi 17.29 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata vaiṣṇava kāre kichu nā māgiba
> ayācita-vṛtti, kiṁvā śāka-phala khāiba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---a devotee; [[synonyms:k:kāre]]---from anyone; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:m:māgiba]]---shall ask for; [[synonyms:a:ayācita]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛtti]]---the profession of not asking for anything; [[synonyms:k:kiṁvā]]---or; [[synonyms:ś:śāka]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---fruits; [[synonyms:k:khāiba]]---shall eat.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus a Vaiṣṇava should not ask anything from anyone else. If someone gives him something without being asked, he should accept it, but if nothing comes, a Vaiṣṇava should be satisfied to eat whatever vegetables and fruits are easily available.**
