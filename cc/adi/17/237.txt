====== CC Ādi 17.237 ======

===== Text =====

> vaṁśī-vādye gopī-gaṇera vane ākarṣaṇa
> tāṅ-sabāra saṅge yaiche vana-viharaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]-[[synonyms:v:vādye]]---on hearing the sound of the flute; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of all the gopīs; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---the attraction; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the company; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---in what way; [[synonyms:v:vana]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:v:viharaṇa]]---wandering.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura extensively explained how the gopīs were attracted to the forests of Vṛndāvana by the vibration of Kṛṣṇa's flute and how they wandered together in the forest.**
