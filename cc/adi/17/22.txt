====== Cc. Ādi 17.22 ======

===== Text =====

> kali-kāle nāma-rūpe kṛṣṇa-avatāra
> nāma haite haya sarva-jagat-nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in this Age of Kali; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of the holy name; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---holy name; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---of the world; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance.

===== Translation =====

**"In this Age of Kali, the holy name of the Lord, the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, is the incarnation of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Simply by chanting the holy name, one associates with the Lord directly. Anyone who does this is certainly delivered.**
