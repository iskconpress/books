====== CC Ādi 17.317 ======

===== Text =====

> caturthe kahiluṅ janmera 'mūla' prayojana
> sva-mādhurya-premānanda-rasa-āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturthe]]---in the Fourth Chapter; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:j:janmera]]---of His birth; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---the real; [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---necessity; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---His own transcendental sweetness; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---of ecstatic joy from love; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the mellows; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**The Fourth Chapter describes the main reason for His appearance, which is to taste the mellows of His own transcendental loving service and His own sweetness.**
