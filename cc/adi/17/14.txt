====== CC Ādi 17.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe catur-bhuja hailā, tina aṅga vakra
> dui haste veṇu bājāya, duye śaṅkha-cakra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuja]]---four-armed; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---body; [[synonyms:v:vakra]]---curved; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:h:haste]]---in two hands; [[synonyms:v:veṇu]] [[synonyms:b:bājāya]]---blowing the flute; [[synonyms:d:duye]]---in two (hands); [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]-[[synonyms:c:cakra]]---conchshell and disc.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter the Lord showed Him His four-armed form, standing in a three-curved posture. With two hands He played upon a flute, and in the other two He carried a conchshell and disc.**
