====== Cc. Ādi 17.239 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bala' 'bala' bale prabhu śunite ullāsa
> śrīvāsa kahena tabe rāsa rasera vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bala]] [[synonyms:b:bala]]---go on speaking, go on speaking; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---hearing; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---very jubilantly; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrivāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]---//rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:r:rasera]]---filled with transcendental humors; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord, hearing with great pleasure, said, "Go on speaking! Go on speaking!" Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura described the rāsa-līlā dance, which is filled with transcendental mellows.**
