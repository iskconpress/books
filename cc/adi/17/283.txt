====== Cc. Ādi 17.283 ======

===== Text =====

> nibhṛta-nikuñje vasi' dekhe rādhāra bāṭa
> anveṣite āilā tāhāṅ gopikāra ṭhāṭa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nibhṛta]]---solitary; [[synonyms:n:nikuñje]]---in a bush; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---waiting to see; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:b:bāṭa]]---the passing; [[synonyms:a:anveṣite]]---while searching; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:g:gopikāra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāṭa]]---the phalanx.

===== Translation =====

**Kṛṣṇa was sitting in a solitary bush, waiting for Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī to pass by. But while He was searching, the gopīs arrived there, like a phalanx of soldiers.**
