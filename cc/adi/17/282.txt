====== CC Ādi 17.282 ======

===== Text =====

> vasanta-kāle rāsa-līlā kare govardhane
> antardhāna kailā saṅketa kari' rādhā-sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vasanta]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---during the season of spring; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:g:govardhane]]---near the Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]]---disappearance; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:s:saṅketa]]---indication; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**During the season of springtime, when the rāsa dance was going on, suddenly Kṛṣṇa disappeared from the scene, indicating that He wanted to be alone with Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
