====== CC Ādi 17.238 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhi madhye chaya-ṛtu līlāra varṇana
> madhu-pāna, rāsotsava, jala-keli kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhimadhye]]---during that; [[synonyms:c:chaya]]-[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtu]]---the six seasons; [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drinking of the honey; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:u:utsava]]---dancing the //rāsa-līlā//; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---swimming in the Yamunā; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---narrations.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa Paṇḍita narrated all the pastimes enacted during the six changing seasons. He described the drinking of honey, the celebration of the rāsa dance, the swimming in the Yamunā, and other such incidents.**
