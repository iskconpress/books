====== CC Ādi 17.325 ======

===== Text =====

> trayodaśe mahāprabhura 'janma-vivaraṇa'
> kṛṣṇa-nāma-saha yaiche prabhura janama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:trayodaśe]]---in the Thirteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:janma]]---of the birth; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---the description; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---along with the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---in what way; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:j:janama]]---the birth.

===== Translation =====

**The Thirteenth Chapter describes the birth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, which took place with the chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.**
