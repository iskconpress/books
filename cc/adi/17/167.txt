====== Cc. Ādi 17.167 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā-sabāra śāstra-kartā---seha bhrānta haila
> nā jāni' śāstrera marma aiche ājñā dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:k:kartā]]---compilers of scripture; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---they also; [[synonyms:b:bhrānta]]---mistaken; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---without knowing; [[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]] [[synonyms:m:marma]]---the essence of scriptures; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave.

===== Translation =====

**"There are many mistakes and illusions in your scriptures. Their compilers, not knowing the essence of knowledge, gave orders that were against reason and argument."**
