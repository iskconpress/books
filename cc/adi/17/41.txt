====== CC Ādi 17.41 ======

===== Text =====

> baḍa baḍa loka saba ānila bolāiyā
> sabāre kahe śrīvāsa hāsiyā hāsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---respectable; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---persons; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought them; [[synonyms:b:bolāiyā]]---causing to be called; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to everyone; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---addresses; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]] [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---while smiling.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura called for all the respectable gentlemen of the neighborhood and smilingly addressed them as follows.**
