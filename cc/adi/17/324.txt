====== CC Ādi 17.324 ======

===== Text =====

> ekādaśe 'nityānanda-śākhā-vivaraṇa'
> dvādaśe 'advaita-skandha śākhāra varṇana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekādaśe]]---in the Eleventh Chapter; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]---of the branches of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśe]]---in the Twelfth Chapter; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:s:skandha]]---the trunk known as Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śākhāra]]---of the branch; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**The Eleventh Chapter describes the branch called Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu. The Twelfth Chapter describes the branch called Śrī Advaita Prabhu.**
