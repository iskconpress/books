====== CC Ādi 17.236 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe 'bala' 'bala' prabhu bale vāra-vāra
> punaḥ punaḥ kahe śrīvāsa kariyā vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:b:balabala]]---go on speaking, go on speaking; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:v:vāra]]-[[synonyms:v:vāra]]---again, again; [[synonyms:p:punaḥpunaḥ]]---again, again; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expansion.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter the Lord again and again asked him, "Speak on! Speak on!" Thus Śrīvāsa again and again described the pastimes of Vṛndāvana, vividly expanding them.**
