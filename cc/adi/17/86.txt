====== CC Ādi 17.86 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata pratidina phale bāra māsa
> vaiṣṇava khāyena phala,---prabhura ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---every day; [[synonyms:p:phale]]---fruit grew; [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---twelve; [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---months; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:khāyena]]---eat; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---the fruits; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---the Lord's; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---satisfaction.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, fruits grew on the tree every day throughout the twelve months of the year, and the Vaiṣṇavas used to eat them, to the Lord's great satisfaction.**
