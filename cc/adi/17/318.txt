====== Cc. Ādi 17.318 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcame 'śrī-nityānanda'-tattva nirūpaṇa
> nityānanda hailā rāma rohiṇī-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañcame]]---in the Fifth Chapter; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth; [[synonyms:n:nirūpaṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---was; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---Balarāma; [[synonyms:r:rohiṇī]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Rohiṇī.

===== Translation =====

**The Fifth Chapter describes the truth of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, who is none other than Balarāma, the son of Rohiṇī.**
