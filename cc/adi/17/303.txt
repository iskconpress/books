====== Cc. Ādi 17.303 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva āpane prabhu gopī-bhāva dhari'
> vrajendra-nandane kahe 'prāṇa-nātha' kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the ecstasy of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandane]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---addresses; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]---O Lord of My life (husband); [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore the Lord Himself, accepting the emotional ecstasy of the gopīs, now addresses the son of Nanda Mahārāja, "O master of My life! O My dear husband!"**
