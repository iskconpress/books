====== CC Ādi 17.262 ======

===== Text =====

> nistārite āilāma āmi, haila viparīta
> e-saba durjanera kaiche ha-ibeka hita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nistārite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:ā:āilāma]]---have come; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it has become; [[synonyms:v:viparīta]]---just the opposite; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:d:durjanera]]---of the rogues; [[synonyms:k:kaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibeka]]---it will be; [[synonyms:h:hita]]---the benefit.

===== Translation =====

**"I have come to deliver all the fallen souls, but now just the opposite has happened. How can these rogues be delivered? How may they be benefited?**
