====== CC Ādi 17.211 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera kīrtana kare nīca bāḍa bāḍa
> ei pāpe navadvīpa ha-ibe ujāḍa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]---lower class; [[synonyms:b:bāḍa]] [[synonyms:b:bāḍa]]---again and again; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:p:pāpe]]---by this sin; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpa]]---the whole city of Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---will become; [[synonyms:u:ujāḍa]]---deserted.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Now the lower classes are chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra again and again. For this sinful activity, the entire city of Navadvīpa will become deserted.**
