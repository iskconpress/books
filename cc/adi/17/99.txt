====== Cc. Ādi 17.99 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina śiva-bhakta śiva-guṇa gāya
> prabhura aṅgane nāce, ḍamaru bājāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---another day; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---a devotee of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the qualities of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chants; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:a:aṅgane]]---in the courtyard; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍamaru]]---a kind of musical instrument; [[synonyms:b:bājāyā]]---plays on it.

===== Translation =====

**On another day a great devotee of Lord Śiva, chanting of Lord Śiva's qualities, came to Lord Caitanya's house, where he began dancing in the courtyard and playing his ḍamaru [a musical instrument].**
