====== CC Ādi 17.36 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtana śuni' bāhire tārā jvali' puḍi' mare
> śrīvāsere duḥkha dite nānā yukti kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing the chanting; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:t:tārā]]---the nonbelievers; [[synonyms:j:jvali']]---burned; [[synonyms:p:puḍi']]---to ashes; [[synonyms:m:mare]]---die; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsere]]---unto Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---troubles; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:y:yukti]]---plans; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the nonbelievers almost burned to ashes and died in envy. To retaliate, they planned various ways to give trouble to Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura.**
