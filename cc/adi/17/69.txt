====== CC Ādi 17.69 ======

===== Text =====

> murāri-gupta-mukhe śuni' rāma-guṇa-grāma
> lalāṭe likhila tāṅra 'rāmadāsa' nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:murāri]]-[[synonyms:g:gupta]]---of Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]---glories; [[synonyms:l:lalāṭe]]---on the forehead; [[synonyms:l:likhila]]---wrote; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---the eternal servant of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name.

===== Translation =====

**Murāri Gupta was a great devotee of Lord Rāmacandra. When Lord Caitanya heard Lord Rāmacandra's glories from his mouth, He immediately wrote on his forehead "rāmadāsa" [the eternal servant of Lord Rāmacandra].**
