====== CC Ādi 17.116 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-gosāñi prabhura āveśa jānila
> gaṅgā-jala-pātra āni' sammukhe dharila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---could understand; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---Ganges water; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---pot; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:s:sammukhe]]---in front; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---placed it.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu Gosāñi, understanding the ecstatic mood of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, brought a pot of Ganges water as a token and put it before Him.**
