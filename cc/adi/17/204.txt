====== CC Ādi 17.204 ======

===== Text =====

> āsi' kahe,---hindura dharma bhāṅgila nimāi
> ye kīrtana pravartāila, kabhu śuni nāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---they said; [[synonyms:h:hindura]]---of the Hindus; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgila]]---has broken; [[synonyms:n:nimāi]]---Nimāi Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:p:pravartāila]]---has introduced; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---we heard; [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---never.

===== Translation =====

**"Coming to me, the Hindus complained, 'Nimāi Paṇḍita has broken the Hindu religious principles. He has introduced the saṅkīrtana system, which we never heard from any scripture.**
