====== CC Ādi 17.66 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya-gosāñire prabhu kare guru-bhakti
> tāhāte ācārya baḍa haya duḥkha-mati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---unto Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---offering respects like a spiritual master; [[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---in that way; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]---aggrieved.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya respected Advaita Ācārya as His spiritual master, but Advaita Ācārya Prabhu was greatly aggrieved by such behavior.**
