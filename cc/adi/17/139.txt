====== CC Ādi 17.139 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata kīrtana kari' nagare bhramilā
> bhramite bhramite sabhe kājī-dvāre gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---executing; [[synonyms:n:nagare]]---in the city; [[synonyms:b:bhramilā]]---circumambulated; [[synonyms:b:bhramite]] [[synonyms:b:bhramite]]---while thus moving; [[synonyms:s:sabhe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kājī]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door of the Kazi; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---reached.

===== Translation =====

**Peforming kīrtana in this way, circumambulating through every nook and corner of the city, they finilly reached the door of the Kazi.**
