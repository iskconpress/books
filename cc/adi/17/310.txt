====== Cc. Ādi 17.310 ======

===== Text =====

> prasaṅge kahila ei siddhāntera sāra
> ihā yei śune, śuddha-bhakti haya tāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasaṅge]]---in the course of discussion; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---it was said; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:siddhāntera]]---of the conclusion; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---unalloyed devotional service; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his.

===== Translation =====

**In this discourse I have explained the essence of the devotional conclusion. Anyone who hears this develops unalloyed devotional service to the Lord.**
