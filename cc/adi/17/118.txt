====== Cc. Ādi 17.118 ======

===== Text =====

> mada-matta-gati baladeva-anukāra
> ācārya śekhara tāṅre dekhe rāmākāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mada]]-[[synonyms:m:matta]]---being intoxicated by drinking Vāruṇī; [[synonyms:g:gati]]---movement; [[synonyms:b:baladeva]]---Lord Baladeva; [[synonyms:a:anukāra]]---imitating; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ś:śekhara]]---at the head; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---in the form of Balarāma.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord, in His ecstasy of Baladeva, was moving as if intoxicated by the beverage, Advaita Ācārya, the chief of the ācāryas [ācārya śekhara], saw Him in the form of Balarāma.**
