====== CC Ādi 17.119 ======

===== Text =====

> vanamālī ācārya dekhe soṇāra lāṅgala
> sabe mili' nṛtya kare āveśe vihvala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vanamālī]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of the name Vanamālī Ācārya; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:s:soṇāra]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:l:lāṅgala]]---plow; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting together; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dance; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---perform; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**Vanamālī Ācārya saw a golden plow in the hand of Balarāma, and the devotees all assembled together and danced, overwhelmed by ecstasy.**
