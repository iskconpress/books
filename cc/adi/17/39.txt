====== CC Ādi 17.39 ======

===== Text =====

> kalāra pāta upare thuila oḍa-phula
> haridrā, sindūra āra rakta-candana, taṇḍula

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kalāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāta]]---a banana leaf; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon it; [[synonyms:t:thuila]]---placed; [[synonyms:o:oḍa]]-[[synonyms:p:phula]]---a particular type of flower; [[synonyms:h:haridrā]]---turmeric; [[synonyms:s:sindūra]]---vermilion; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rakta]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---red sandalwood; [[synonyms:t:taṇḍula]]---rice.

===== Translation =====

**On the upper portion of a plantain leaf he placed such paraphernalia for worship as oḍa-phula, turmeric, vermilion, red sandalwood and rice.**
