====== CC Ādi 17.214 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe āmi prīti-vākya kahila sabāre
> sabe ghare yāha, āmi niṣedhiba tāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]-[[synonyms:v:vākya]]---sweet words; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---back home; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---go; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhiba]]---shall prohibit; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---Him (Nimāi Paṇḍita).

===== Translation =====

**"After hearing their complaints, in sweet words I told them, 'Please go back home. I shall certainly prohibit Nimāi Paṇḍita from continuing His Hare Kṛṣṇa movement.'**
