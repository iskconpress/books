====== CC Ādi 17.178-179 ======

===== Text =====

> kājī kahe,---yabe āmi hindura ghare giyā
> kīrtana kariluṅ mānā mṛdaṅga bhāṅgiyā
>
> sei rātre eka siṁha mahā-bhayaṅkara
> nara-deha, siṁha-mukha, garjaye vistara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kājī]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Kazi replied; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:h:hindura]]---of a Hindu; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going there; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]]---made; [[synonyms:m:mānā]]---prohibition; [[synonyms:m:mṛdaṅga]]---the drum; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgiyā]]---breaking; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---on that night; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]---lion; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhayaṅ]]-[[synonyms:k:kara]]---very fearful; [[synonyms:n:nara]]-[[synonyms:d:deha]]---having a body like a human being's; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---having a face like a lion's; [[synonyms:g:garjaye]]---was roaring; [[synonyms:v:vistara]]---very loudly.

===== Translation =====

**The Kazi said, "When I went to the Hindu's house, broke the drum and forbade the performance of congregational chanting, in my dreams that very night I saw a greatly fearful lion, roaring very loudly, His body like a human being's and His face like a lion's.**
