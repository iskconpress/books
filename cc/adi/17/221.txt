====== CC Ādi 17.221 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---eka dāna māgiye tomāya
> saṅkīrtana vāda yaiche nahe nadīyāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---charity; [[synonyms:m:māgiye]]---I beg; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---from you; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:v:vāda]]---opposition; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as it may be; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not be; [[synonyms:n:nadīyāya]]---in the district of Nadia.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord said, "I wish to beg you for one favor in charity. You must pledge that this saṅkīrtana movement will not be checked, at least in the district of Nadia."**
