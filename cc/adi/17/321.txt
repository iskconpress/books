====== CC Ādi 17.321 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭame 'caitanya-līlā-varṇana'-kāraṇa
> eka kṛṣṇa-nāmera mahā-mahimā-kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭame]]---in the Eighth Chapter; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:lilā]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇana]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the reason for describing Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahimā]]-[[synonyms:k:kathana]]---description of great glories.

===== Translation =====

**The Eighth Chapter gives the reason for describing Lord Caitanya's pastimes. It also describes the greatness of Lord Kṛṣṇa's holy name.**
