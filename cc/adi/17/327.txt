====== CC Ādi 17.327 ======

===== Text =====

> ṣoḍaśa paricchede 'kaiśora-līlā' ra uddeśa
> saptadaśe 'yauvana-līlā' kahiluṅ viśeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣoḍaśa]]---sixteenth; [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the chapter; [[synonyms:k:kaiśora]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of pastimes prior to youth; [[synonyms:u:uddeśa]]---indication; [[synonyms:s:saptadaśe]]---in the Seventeenth Chapter; [[synonyms:y:yauvana]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of youth; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have stated; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---specifically.

===== Translation =====

**In the Sixteenth Chapter I have indicated the pastimes of the kaiśora age [the age prior to youth]. In the Seventeenth Chapter I have specifically described His youthful pastimes.**
