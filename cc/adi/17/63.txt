====== Cc. Ādi 17.63 ======

===== Text =====

> saṁsāra-sukha tomāra ha-uka vināśa
> śāpa śuni' prabhura citte ha-ila ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---material happiness; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:u:uka]]---may it become; [[synonyms:v:vināśa]]---all vanquished; [[synonyms:ś:śāpa]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this curse; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within His mind; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa cursed the Lord, "You shall be bereft of all material happiness!" When the Lord heard this, He felt great jubilation within Himself.**
