====== CC Ādi 17.21 ======

===== Text =====

> harer nāma harer nāma
> harer nāmaiva kevalam
> kalau nāsty eva nāsty eva
> nāsty eva gatir anyathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kevalam]]---only; [[synonyms:k:kalau]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:asti]]---there is none; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:asti]]---there is none; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:asti]]---there is none; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:g:gatiḥ]]---destination; [[synonyms:a:anyathā]]---otherwise.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'In this Age of Kali there is no other means, no other means, no other means for self-realization than chanting the holy name, chanting the holy name, chanting the holy name of Lord Hari.'**
