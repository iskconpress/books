====== CC Ādi 17.120 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata nṛtya ha-ila cāri prahara
> sandhyāya gaṅgā-snāna kari' sabe gelā ghara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was performed; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four; [[synonyms:p:prahara]]---a period of time lasting three hours; [[synonyms:s:sandhyāya]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---taking bath in the Ganges; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---home.

===== Translation =====

**In this way they danced continuously for twelve hours, and in the evening they all took bath in the Ganges and then returned to their homes.**
