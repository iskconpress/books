====== Cc. Ādi 17.329 ======

===== Text =====

> pañca-prabandhe pañca-rasera carita
> saṅkṣepe kahiluṅ ati,---nā kailuṅ vistṛta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:p:prabandhe]]---in five chapters; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of five transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:c:carita]]---the character; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I stated; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---greatly; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I did not make; [[synonyms:v:vistṛta]]---expanded.

===== Translation =====

**After the chapters of the preface, I have described five transcendental mellows in five chapters. I have described them very briefly rather than expansively.**
