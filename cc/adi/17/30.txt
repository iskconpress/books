====== CC Ādi 17.30 ======

===== Text =====

> sadā nāma la-iba, yathā-lābhete santoṣa
> eita ācāra kare bhakti-dharma-poṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iba]]---one should chant; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---inasmuch as; [[synonyms:l:lābhete]]---gains; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:e:eita]]---this; [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---behavior; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:poṣa]]---maintenance.

===== Translation =====

**"One should strictly follow the principle of always chanting the holy name, and one should be satisfied with whatever he gets easily. Such devotional behavior solidly maintains one's devotional service.**
