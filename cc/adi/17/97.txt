====== CC Ādi 17.97 ======

===== Text =====

> aparādha nāhi, kaile lokera nistāra
> ye tomā' dekhila, tāra chuṭila saṁsāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---did not; [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---committed; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---liberation; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:t:tomā']]---You; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:c:chuṭila]]---became free; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---material bondage.

===== Translation =====

**"There was no offense in Your appearing as Nṛsiṁhadeva. Rather, any man who saw You in that mood was immediately liberated from the bondage of material existence."**
