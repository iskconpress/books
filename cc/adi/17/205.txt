====== Cc. Ādi 17.205 ======

===== Text =====

> maṅgalacaṇḍī viṣahari kari' jāgaraṇa
> tā'te vādya, nṛtya, gīta,---yogya ācaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:c:caṇḍī]]---of the religious performance for worship of Maṅgalacaṇḍī; [[synonyms:v:viṣahari]]---of the religious performance for worship of Viṣahari; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---observing; [[synonyms:j:jāgaraṇa]]---night vigil; [[synonyms:t:tā'te]]---in that ceremony; [[synonyms:v:vādya]]---musical performance; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---suitable; [[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇa]]---custom.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'When we keep a nightlong vigil to observe religious performances for the worship of Maṅgalacaṇḍī and Viṣahari, playing on musical instruments, dancing and chanting are certainly fitting customs.**
