====== CC Ādi 17.142 ======

===== Text =====

> uddhata loka bhāṅge kājīra ghara-puṣpavana
> vistāri' varṇilā ihā dāsa-vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uddhata]]---agitated; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---persons; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅge]]---break; [[synonyms:k:kājīra]]---of the Kazi; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---house; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vana]]---flower garden; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---elaborately; [[synonyms:v:varṇilā]]---described; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**Naturally some of the people who were very much agitated began to retaliate the Kazi's actions by wrecking his house and flower garden. Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has elaborately described this incident.**
