~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Ādi 17.330~~

====== CC Ādi 17.330 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-dāsa ihā 'caitanya-maṅgale'
> vistāri' varṇilā nityānanda-ājñā-bale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Ṭhākura Vṛndāvana dāsa; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgale]]---in his book //Caitanya-maṅgala//; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---expanding; [[synonyms:v:varṇilā]]---described; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---of the order; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---on the strength.

===== Translation =====

**By the order and strength of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu, Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has elaborately described in his Caitanya-maṅgala all that I have not.**
