~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Ādi 17.34~~

====== CC Ādi 17.34 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu śrīvāsera gṛhe nirantara
> rātre saṅkīrtana kaila eka saṁvatsara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsera]]---of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in the home; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always; [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---congregational chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:s:saṁvatsara]]---one full year.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu regularly led congregational chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra in the house of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura every night for one full year.**
