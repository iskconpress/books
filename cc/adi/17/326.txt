====== Cc. Ādi 17.326 ======

===== Text =====

> caturdaśe 'bālya-līlāra' kichu vivaraṇa
> pañcadaśe 'paugaṇḍa-līlāra' saṅkṣepe kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturdaśe]]---in the Fourteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:b:bālya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the Lord's childhood pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:p:pañcadaśe]]---in the Fifteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes of the //paugaṇḍa// (boyhood) age; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---the telling.

===== Translation =====

**The Fourteenth Chapter gives some description of the Lord's childhood pastimes. The Fifteenth briefly describes the Lord's boyhood pastimes.**
