====== CC Ādi 17.59 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe vipra la-ila āsi śrīvāsa śaraṇa
> tāṅhāra kṛpāya haila pāpa-vimocana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after that; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa// (Gopāla Cāpāla); [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---took shelter; [[synonyms:ā:āsi]]---coming; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]---shelter of his lotus feet; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by his mercy; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vimocana]]---free from all sinful reaction.

===== Translation =====

**Then the brāhmaṇa, Gopāla Cāpāla, went to Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura and took shelter of his lotus feet, and by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura's mercy he was freed from all sinful reactions.**
