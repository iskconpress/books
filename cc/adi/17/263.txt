====== Cc. Ādi 17.263 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāke praṇati kare, haya pāpa-kṣaya
> tabe se ihāre bhakti laoyāile laya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāke]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:p:praṇati]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---they offer; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---destruction of sinful reactions; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:se]]---they; [[synonyms:i:ihāre]]---unto them; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:l:laoyāile]]---if causing to take; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---will take.

===== Translation =====

**"If these rogues offer Me obeisances, the reactions of their sinful activities will be nullified. Then, if I induce them, they will take to devotional service.**
