====== CC Ādi 17.209 ======

===== Text =====

> nagariyāke pāgala kaila sadā saṅkīrtana
> rātre nidrā nāhi yāi, kari jāgaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nagariyāke]]---all the citizens; [[synonyms:p:pāgala]]---mad; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---He has made; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:n:nidrā]]---sleep; [[synonyms:n:nāhiyāi]]---we do not get; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---observe; [[synonyms:j:jāgaraṇa]]---wakefulness.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'He has made all the people practically mad by always performing congregational chanting. At night we cannot get any sleep; we are always kept awake.**
