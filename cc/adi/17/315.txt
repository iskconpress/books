====== CC Ādi 17.315 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho ta' caitanya-kṛṣṇa---śacīra nandana
> tṛtīya paricchede janmera 'sāmānya' kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa with the name of Śrī Caitanya; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Third Chapter; [[synonyms:j:janmera]]---of His birth; [[synonyms:s:sāmānya]]---general; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---reason.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is Kṛṣṇa Himself, has now appeared as the son of mother Śacī. The Third Chapter describes the general cause of His appearance.**
