====== CC Ādi 17.210 ======

===== Text =====

> 'nimāñi' nāma chāḍi' ebe bolāya 'gaurahari'
> hindura dharma naṣṭa kaila pāṣaṇḍa sañcāri'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nimāñi]]---Nimāi; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:b:bolāya]]---calls; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Gaurahari; [[synonyms:h:hindura]]---of the Hindus; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religious principles; [[synonyms:n:naṣṭakaila]]---spoiled; [[synonyms:p:pāṣaṇḍa]]---irreligion; [[synonyms:s:sañcāri']]---introducing.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Now He has given up His own name Nimāi and introduced Himself by the name Gaurahari. He has spoiled the Hindu religious principles and introduced the irreligion of nonbelievers.**
