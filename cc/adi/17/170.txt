====== CC Ādi 17.170 ======

===== Text =====

> kalpita āmāra śāstra,---āmi saba jāni
> jāti-anurodhe tabu sei śāstra māni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kalpita]]---imagined; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---our; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---scripture; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everything; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---know; [[synonyms:j:jāti]]---by community; [[synonyms:a:anurodhe]]---being obliged; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---scripture; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I accept.

===== Translation =====

**"I know that our scriptures are full of imagination and mistaken ideas, yet because I am a Muslim I accept them for the sake of my community, despite their insufficient support.**
