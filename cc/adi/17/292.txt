====== CC Ādi 17.292 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhāra viśuddha-bhāvera acintya prabhāva
> ye kṛṣṇere karāilā dvi-bhuja-svabhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:v:viśuddha]]---purified; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of the ecstasy; [[synonyms:a:acintya]]---inconceivable; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---influence; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇere]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---forced; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuja]]---two-armed; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---original form.

===== Translation =====

**The influence of Rādhārāṇī's pure ecstasy is so inconceivably great that it forced Kṛṣṇa to come to His original two-armed form.**
