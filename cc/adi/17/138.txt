====== CC Ādi 17.138 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-dāsa ihā 'caitanya-maṅgale'
> vistāri' varṇiyāchena, prabhu-kṛpā-bale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgale]]---in his book named //Caitanya-maṅgala//; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---elaborately; [[synonyms:v:varṇiyāchena]]---has described; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:b:bale]]---by the strength of mercy.

===== Translation =====

**By the grace of the Lord, Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has elaborately described this incident in his Caitanya-maṅgala.**
