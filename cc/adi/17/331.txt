====== Cc. Ādi 17.331 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-līlā---adbhuta, ananta
> brahmā-śiva-śeṣa yāṅra nāhi pāya anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---Lord Śeṣa Nāga; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of which; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---end.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are wonderful and unlimited. Even personalities like Lord Brahmā, Lord Śiva and Śeṣa Nāga cannot find their end.**
