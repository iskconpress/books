====== CC Ādi 17.296 ======

===== Text =====

> vātsalya, dāsya, sakhya---tina bhāvamaya
> sei nityānanda---kṛṣṇa-caitanya-sahāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]---paternity; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]---servitude; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]---fraternity; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---emotional ecstasies; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]---the assistant.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu always feels the ecstatic emotions of paternity, servitude and friendship. He always assists Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in that way.**
