====== Cc. Ādi 17.81 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhite dekhite vṛkṣa ha-ila phalita
> pākila aneka phala, sabei vismita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---as people were seeing; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]---the tree; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:phalita]]---fully grown with fruits; [[synonyms:p:pākila]]---ripened; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---many; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---fruits; [[synonyms:s:sabei]]---every one of them; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---struck with wonder.

===== Translation =====

**As people looked on, the tree became fully grown, with fruits that fully ripened. Thus everyone was struck with wonder.**
