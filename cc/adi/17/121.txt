====== CC Ādi 17.121 ======

===== Text =====

> nagariyā loke prabhu yabe ājñā dilā
> ghare ghare saṅkīrtana karite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nagariyā]]---citizens; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---all the people; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in each and every home; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to perform; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord ordered all the citizens of Navadvīpa to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra, and in each and every home they began performing saṅkīrtana regularly.**
