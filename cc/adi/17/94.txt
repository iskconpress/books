====== CC Ādi 17.94 ======

===== Text =====

> loka-bhaya dekhi' prabhura bāhya ha-ila
> śrīvāsa-gṛhete giyā gadā phelāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---the fearful people; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing this; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external sense; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---appeared; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhete]]---in the house of Śrīvāsa Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going there; [[synonyms:g:gadā]]---the club; [[synonyms:p:phelāila]]---threw away.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the people so afraid, the Lord came to His external senses and thus returned to the house of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura and threw away the club.**
