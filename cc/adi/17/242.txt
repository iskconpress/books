====== CC Ādi 17.242 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu durgā, lakṣmī haya, kabhu vā cic-chakti
> khāṭe vasi' bhakta-gaṇe dilā prema-bhakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:d:durgā]]---the part of Goddess Durgā; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---the spiritual potency; [[synonyms:k:khāṭe]]---on a cot; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord sometimes took the part of Goddess Durgā, Lakṣmī [the goddess of fortune] or the chief potency, Yogamāyā. Sitting on a cot, He delivered love of Godhead to all the devotees present.**
