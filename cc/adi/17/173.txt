====== Cc. Ādi 17.173 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra nagare haya sadā saṅkīrtana
> vādya-gīta-kolāhala, saṅgīta, nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:n:nagare]]---in your city; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vādya]]---musical sounds; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---song; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---tumultuous roaring; [[synonyms:s:saṅgīta]]---singing; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**"In your city there is always congregational chanting of the holy name. A tumultuous uproar of music, singing and dancing is always going on.**
