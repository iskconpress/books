====== CC Ādi 17.156 ======

===== Text =====

> sei śāstre kahe,---pravṛtti-nivṛtti-mārga-bheda
> nivṛtti-mārge jīva-mātra-vadhera niṣedha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in the scripture (the Koran); [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---it is ordered; [[synonyms:p:pravṛtti]]---of attachment; [[synonyms:n:nivṛtti]]---of detachment; [[synonyms:m:mārga]]---ways; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---difference; [[synonyms:n:nivṛtti]]---of detachment; [[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the path; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---of any living entity; [[synonyms:v:vadhera]]---of killing; [[synonyms:n:niṣedha]]---prohibition.

===== Translation =====

**"According to the Koran, there are two ways of advancement---through increasing the propensity to enjoy and decreasing the propensity to enjoy. On the path of decreasing attachment [nivṛtti-mārga], the killing of animals is prohibited.**
