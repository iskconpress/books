====== CC Ādi 17.174 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi kājī---hindu-dharma-virodhe adhikārī
> ebe ye nā kara mānā bujhite nā pāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kājī]]---the magistrate; [[synonyms:h:hindu]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religious principles of the Hindus; [[synonyms:v:virodhe]]---in opposing; [[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---have the right; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:m:mānā]]---you do not forbid; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am not able.

===== Translation =====

**"As a Muslim magistrate, you have the right to oppose the performance of Hindu ceremonies, but now you do not forbid them. I cannot understand the reason why."**
