====== CC Ādi 17.320 ======

===== Text =====

> saptama paricchede 'pañca-tattve'ra ākhyāna
> pañca-tattva mili' yaiche kailā prema-dāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saptama]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Seventh Chapter; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:t:tattvera]]---of the five //tattvas// (truths); [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāna]]---the elaboration; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the five //tattvas//; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---combining together; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---in what way; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]---distribution of love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**The Seventh Chapter describes the Pañca-tattva-Śrī Caitanya, Prabhu Nityānanda, Śrī Advaita, Gadādhara and Śrīvāsa. They all combined together to distribute love of Godhead everywhere.**
