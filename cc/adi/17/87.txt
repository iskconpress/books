====== CC Ādi 17.87 ======

===== Text =====

> ei saba līlā kare śacīra nandana
> anya loka nāhi jāne vinā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]]---of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---son; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---other people; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---do not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---know; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**These are confidential pastimes of the son of Śacī. Other than devotees, no one knows of this incident.**

===== Purport =====

Nondevotees cannot believe this incident, yet the place where the tree grew still exists in Māyāpur. It is called Āmra-ghaṭṭa or Āma-ghāṭā.
