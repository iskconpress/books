====== CC Ādi 17.25 ======

===== Text =====

> anyathā ye māne, tāra nāhika nistāra
> nāhi, nāhi, nāhi---e tina 'eva'-kāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anyathā]]---otherwise; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---accepts; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is no; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is nothing else, nothing else, nothing else; [[synonyms:e:e]]---in this; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:e:eva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---bearing the meaning of emphasis.

===== Translation =====

**"This verse clearly states that anyone who accepts any other path cannot be delivered. This is the reason for the triple repetition 'nothing else, nothing else, nothing else,' which emphasizes the real process of self-realization.**
