====== CC Ādi 17.314 ======

===== Text =====

> dvitīya paricchede 'caitanya-tattva-nirūpaṇa'
> svayaṁ bhagavān yei vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvitīya]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Second Chapter; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:n:nirūpaṇa]]---description of the truth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---who; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**The Second Chapter explains the truth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Lord Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda.**
