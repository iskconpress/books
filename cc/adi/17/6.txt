====== CC Ādi 17.6 ======

===== Text =====

> vidyāra auddhatye kāhoṅ nā kare gaṇana
> sakala paṇḍita jini' kare adhyāpana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidyāra]] [[synonyms:a:auddhatye]]---because of pride in education; [[synonyms:k:kāhoṅ]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---care; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned scholars; [[synonyms:j:jini']]---conquering; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:adhyāpana]]---studies.

===== Translation =====

**By dint of pride in His education, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, not caring for anyone else, defeated all kinds of learned scholars while executing His studies.**
