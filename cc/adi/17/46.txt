====== Cc. Ādi 17.46 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvāṅga beḍila kīṭe, kāṭe nirantara
> asahya vedanā, duḥkhe jvalaye antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---all over the body; [[synonyms:b:beḍila]]---became covered; [[synonyms:k:kīṭe]]---by insects; [[synonyms:k:kāṭe]]---biting; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always; [[synonyms:a:asahya]]---unbearable; [[synonyms:v:vedanā]]---pain; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in unhappiness; [[synonyms:j:jvalaye]]---burns; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---without cessation.

===== Translation =====

**Incessantly covered with germs and insects biting him all over his body, Gopāla Cāpāla felt unbearable pain. His entire body burned in distress.**
