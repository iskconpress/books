====== CC Ādi 17.240 ======

===== Text =====

> kahite, śunite aiche prātaḥ-kāla haila
> prabhu śrīvāsere toṣi' āliṅgana kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kahite]]---speaking; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---hearing; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]]---morning; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---appeared; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsere]]---unto Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:toṣi']]---satisfying; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did.

===== Translation =====

**As the Lord thus requested and Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura spoke, the morning appeared, and the Lord embraced Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura and satisfied him.**
