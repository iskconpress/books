====== CC Ādi 17.74 ======

===== Text =====

> sagaṇe sacele giyā kaila gaṅgā-snāna
> bhaktira mahimā tāhāṅ karila vyākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---with His followers; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:c:cele]]---without leaving the clothes; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing in the Ganges; [[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation.

===== Translation =====

**Without even changing His garments, Lord Caitanya took a bath in the Ganges with His companions. There He explained the glories of devotional service.**
