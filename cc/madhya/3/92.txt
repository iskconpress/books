====== Cc. Madhya 3.92 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā yatna-dainye prabhure karāila bhojana
> ācāryera icchā prabhu karila pūraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:y:yatna]]-[[synonyms:d:dainye]]---in this way, by various efforts and by humility; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---caused; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---the wish of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---fulfillment.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, by submitting various humble requests, Advaita Ācārya made Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda eat. Thus Caitanya Mahāprabhu fulfilled all the desires of Advaita Ācārya.**
