====== Cc. Madhya 3.11 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda, ācāryaratna, mukunda, tina jana
> prabhu-pāche-pāche tine karena gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryaratna]]---Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---and Mukunda; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---three persons; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāche]]-[[synonyms:p:pāche]]---following the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tine]]---all three of them; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---go.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went toward Vṛndāvana, Nityānanda Prabhu, Candraśekhara and Prabhu Mukunda followed Him.**
