====== CC Madhya 3.119 ======

===== Text =====

> premera utkaṇṭhā,---prabhura nāhi kṛṣṇa-saṅga
> virahe bāḍila prema-jvālāra taraṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:premerautkaṇṭhā]]---the ecstasy of the love; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---meeting with Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:j:jvālāra]]---of flames of love; [[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---waves.

===== Translation =====

**When Advaita Ācārya danced in that way, Lord Caitanya felt ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa, and because of His separation, the waves and flames of love increased.**
