====== CC Madhya 3.173 ======

===== Text =====

> mātāra vyagratā dekhi' prabhura vyagra mana
> bhakta-gaṇa ekatra kari' balilā vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---of the mother; [[synonyms:v:vyagratā]]---eagerness; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vyagra]]---agitated; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---assembling together; [[synonyms:b:balilā]]---said; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw His mother's great eagerness, He became a little agitated. He therefore assembled all the devotees present and spoke to them.**
