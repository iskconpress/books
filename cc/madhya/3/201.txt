====== CC Madhya 3.201 ======

===== Text =====

> dine kṛṣṇa-kathā-rasa bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> rātre mahā-mahotsava saṅkīrtana-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dine]]---during daytime; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---discussion on Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:utsava]]---a great festival; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the matter of congregational chanting.

===== Translation =====

**During the day the devotees discussed subject matters concerning Kṛṣṇa, and at night there was a great festival of congregational chanting at the house of Advaita Ācārya.**
