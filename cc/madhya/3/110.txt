====== CC Madhya 3.110 ======

===== Text =====

> gaura-deha-kānti sūrya jiniyā ujjvala
> aruṇa-vastra-kānti tāhe kare jhala-mala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaura]]---fair-complexioned; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---of the body; [[synonyms:k:kānti]]---the luster; [[synonyms:s:sūrya]]---the sun; [[synonyms:j:jiniyā]]---conquering; [[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---bright; [[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]---reddish; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---the beauty of the garments; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---in that; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:j:jhala]]-[[synonyms:m:mala]]---glittering.

===== Translation =====

**They saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's very fair-complexioned body and its bright luster, which conquered the brilliance of the sun. Over and above this was the beauty of the saffron garments that glittered upon His body.**
