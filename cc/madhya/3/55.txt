====== Cc. Madhya 3.55 ======

===== Text =====

> dui pāśe dharila saba mṛt-kuṇḍikā bhari'
> cāṅpākalā-dadhi-sandeśa kahite nā pāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---on two sides; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---kept; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mṛt]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍikā]]---earthen pots; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:c:cāṅpā]]-[[synonyms:k:kalā]]---a kind of banana known as //cāṅpā-kalā//; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]-[[synonyms:s:sandeśa]]---mixed with yogurt and //sandeśa//; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to say; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am able.

===== Translation =====

**In two places there were earthen pots filled with another preparation made with yogurt, sandeśa [a sweetmeat made with curd] and banana. I am unable to describe it all.**
