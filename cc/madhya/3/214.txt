====== CC Madhya 3.214 ======

===== Text =====

> jananī prabodhi' kara bhakta samādhāna
> tumi vyagra haile kāro nā rahibe prāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jananī]] [[synonyms:p:prabodhi']]---pacifying the mother; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---make; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---adjustments; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:v:vyagra]] [[synonyms:h:haile]]---if becoming agitated; [[synonyms:k:kāro]]---anyone's; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:r:rahibe]]---will not remain; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---the life.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Please pacify all the devotees and My mother. If You become agitated, no one will be able to continue to exist."**
