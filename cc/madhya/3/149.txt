====== CC Madhya 3.149 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' punaḥ punaḥ kare namaskāra
> tuṣṭa hañā āi kole kare bāra bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offers; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being pleased; [[synonyms:ā:āi]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:k:kole]]---on the lap; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---takes; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, the Lord offered obeisances to His mother again and again, and mother Śacī, being pleased, took Him again and again on her lap.**
