====== CC Madhya 3.91 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe---ye diyāchi, tāhā nā chāḍibā
> ekhana ye diye, tāra ardheka khāibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Advaita Ācārya said; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:d:diyāchi]]---whatever I have given; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍibā]]---please do not give it up; [[synonyms:e:ekhana]]---now; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:d:diye]]---I am giving; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:a:ardheka]]---half of it; [[synonyms:k:khāibā]]---You will eat.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya said, "Please do not give up whatever I have already given You. Now, whatever I am giving, You may eat half and leave half."**
