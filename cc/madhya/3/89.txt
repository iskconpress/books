====== CC Madhya 3.89 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vyañjana ācārya punaḥ karena pūraṇa
> ei mata punaḥ punaḥ pariveśe vyañjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seivyañjana]]---that half-finished vegetable; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---filling; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:p:pariveśe]]---distributes; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as half of the vegetable in the pot was finished, Advaita Ācārya filled it up again. In this way, as the Lord finished half of a preparation, Advaita Ācārya again and again filled it up.**
