====== CC Madhya 3.137 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhāte ācāryaratna dolāya caḍāñā
> bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge āilā śacīmātā lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryaratna]]---Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:d:dolāya]]---in a palanquin; [[synonyms:c:caḍāñā]]---seating; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:m:mātā]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---bringing.

===== Translation =====

**In the morning Candraśekhara brought Śacīmātā from her house with many devotees, and he seated her in a palanquin.**
