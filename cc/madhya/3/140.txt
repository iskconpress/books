====== CC Madhya 3.140 ======

===== Text =====

> śacī-āge paḍilā prabhu daṇḍavat hañā
> kāndite lāgilā śacī kole uṭhāiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like a stick; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:k:kāndite]]---to cry; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:k:kole]]---on the lap; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāiñā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as mother Śacī appeared on the scene, Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell before her like a stick. Mother Śacī began to cry, taking the Lord on her lap.**
