====== CC Madhya 3.16 ======

===== Text =====

> gupte tā-sabāke āni' ṭhākura nityānanda
> śikhāilā sabākāre kariyā prabandha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gupte]]---in confidence; [[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāke]]---unto all the cowherd boys; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---taking them; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]] [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---instructed; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:p:prabandha]]---by making a reasonable story.

===== Translation =====

**Calling all the boys in confidence and telling a reasonable story, Nityānanda Prabhu instructed them as follows.**
