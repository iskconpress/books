====== CC Madhya 3.58 ======

===== Text =====

> āratira kāle dui prabhu bolāila
> prabhu-saṅge sabe āsi' ārati dekhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āratirakāle]]---during the time of //ārati//; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---He called; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the Lords; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all other people; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:ā:ārati]]---the //ārati// ceremony; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---observed.

===== Translation =====

**It is the system, after offering food, to perform bhoga-ārati. Advaita Prabhu asked the two brothers, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu, to come see the ārati. The two Lords and all others present went to see the ārati ceremony.**
