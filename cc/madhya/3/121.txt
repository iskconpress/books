====== CC Madhya 3.121 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura antara mukunda jāne bhāla-mate
> bhāvera sadṛśa pada lāgilā gāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---the heart; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---very well; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---to the ecstatic mood; [[synonyms:s:sadṛśa]]---suitable; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---verses; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]] [[synonyms:g:gāite]]---began to sing.

===== Translation =====

**When Mukunda saw the ecstasy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he understood the feelings of the Lord and began to sing many stanzas augmenting the force of the Lord's ecstasy.**
