====== CC Madhya 3.142 ======

===== Text =====

> aṅga muche, mukha cumbe, kare nirīkṣaṇa
> dekhite nā pāya,---aśru bharila nayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the body; [[synonyms:m:muche]]---caresses; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---face; [[synonyms:c:cumbe]]---kisses; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nirīkṣaṇa]]---observing; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---not able; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:b:bharila]]---filled; [[synonyms:n:nayana]]---the eyes.

===== Translation =====

**Out of love she began to caress the body of the Lord. Sometimes she kissed His face and tried to observe Him carefully, but because her eyes were filled with tears, she could not see.**
