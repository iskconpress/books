====== Cc. Madhya 3.204 ======

===== Text =====

> śacīra ānanda bāḍe dekhi' putra-mukha
> bhojana karāñā pūrṇa kaila nija-sukha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śacīra]]---of Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---pleasure increases; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---the face of her son; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---feeding; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---full; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---her own happiness.

===== Translation =====

**As mother Śacī constantly saw the face of her son and fed Him, her own happiness increased and was indeed complete.**
