====== CC Madhya 3.56 ======

===== Text =====

> anna-vyañjana-upari dila tulasī-mañjarī
> tina jala-pātre suvāsita jala bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]-[[synonyms:u:upari]]---on top of the boiled rice and vegetables; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---placed; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]-[[synonyms:m:mañjarī]]---flowers of //tulasī//; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:p:pātre]]---waterpots; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsita]]---scented; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling.

===== Translation =====

**Upon the stack of boiled rice and all the vegetables were flowers of the tulasī trees. There were also pots filled with scented rosewater.**
