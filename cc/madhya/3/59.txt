====== Cc. Madhya 3.59 ======

===== Text =====

> ārati kariyā kṛṣṇe karā'la śayana
> ācārya āsi' prabhure tabe kailā nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āratikariyā]]---after finishing the //ārati//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karā'la]]---made to do; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down to rest; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**After ārati was performed for the Deities in the temple, Lord Kṛṣṇa was made to lie down to rest. Advaita Ācārya then came out to submit something to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
