====== CC Madhya 3.57 ======

===== Text =====

> tina śubhra-pīṭha, tāra upari vasana
> ei-rūpe sākṣāt kṛṣṇe karāila bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:ś:śubhra]]-[[synonyms:p:pīṭha]]---white sitting places; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of them; [[synonyms:u:upari]]---on top; [[synonyms:v:vasana]]---soft cloth; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made to do; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**There were three sitting places where soft cloths were placed. Thus Lord Kṛṣṇa was offered all the food, and the Lord took it very pleasantly.**
