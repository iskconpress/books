====== CC Madhya 3.192 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' sabākāre īṣat hāsiñā
> vidāya karila prabhu sammāna kariñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:ī:īṣat]] [[synonyms:h:hāsiñā]]---smiling very mildly; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---bid them farewell; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]] [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---showing all respect.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, offering due respects to all the devotees and smiling very mildly, bid them farewell.**
