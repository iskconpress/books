====== CC Madhya 3.172 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' bhakta-gaṇa kahe kari' namaskāra
> mātāra ye icchā sei sammata sabāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---of mother Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---whatever desire; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:sammata]]---agreeable; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---to all the devotees

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this appeal from mother Śacī, all the devotees offered obeisances and said, "We all agree to whatever mother Śacī desires."**
