====== CC Madhya 3.76 ======

===== Text =====

> tina janāra bhakṣya-piṇḍa---tomāra eka grāsa
> tāra lekhāya ei anna nahe pañca-grāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tinajanāra]]---of three persons; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]]-[[synonyms:p:piṇḍa]]---the stack of eatables; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:g:grāsa]]---one morsel; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---to that; [[synonyms:l:lekhāya]]---in proportion; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:a:anna]]---this food; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:g:grāsa]]---five morsels.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya said, "The amount of food that three people can eat does not constitute even a morsel for You. In proportion to that, these edibles are not even five morsels of food for You."**
