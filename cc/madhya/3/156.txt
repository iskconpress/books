====== CC Madhya 3.156 ======

===== Text =====

> ānande nācaye sabe bali' 'hari' 'hari'
> ācārya-mandira haila śrī-vaikuṇṭha-purī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in pleasure; [[synonyms:n:nācaye]]---dance; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:m:mandira]]---the house of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---a spiritual Vaikuṇṭha planet.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was chanting the holy names of Hari and dancing. In this way the domicile of Advaita Ācārya was converted into Śrī Vaikuṇṭha Purī.**
