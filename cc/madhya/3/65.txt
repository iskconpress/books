====== Cc. Madhya 3.65 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche anna ye kṛṣṇake karāya bhojana
> janme janme śire dharoṅ tāṅhāra caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in this way; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---the eatables; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇake]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---made to do; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:j:janme]] [[synonyms:j:janme]]---birth after birth; [[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on My head; [[synonyms:d:dharoṅ]]---I keep; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu approved of all the methods employed in cooking and offering food to Kṛṣṇa. Indeed, He was so pleased that He said, "Frankly, I will personally take the lotus feet of anyone who can offer Kṛṣṇa such nice food and place those lotus feet on My head birth after birth."**
