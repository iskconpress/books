====== CC Madhya 3.73 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya bale---akapaṭe karaha āhāra
> yadi khāite nā pāra pāte rahibeka āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryabale]]---Advaita Ācārya says; [[synonyms:a:akapaṭe]]---without pretense; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---kindly do; [[synonyms:ā:āhāra]]---eating; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:khāite]]---to eat; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---You are not able; [[synonyms:p:pāte]]---on the leaf; [[synonyms:r:rahibeka]] [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---let the balance remain.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya then requested the Lord to simply accept the prasāda without pretense. If He could not eat it all, the balance could be left on the plate.**
