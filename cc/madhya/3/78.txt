====== Cc. Madhya 3.78 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' jala dila dui gosāñira hāte
> hāsiyā lāgilā duṅhe bhojana karite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etabali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---supplied water; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---on the hands; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of Them; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to eat.

===== Translation =====

**Upon saying this, Advaita Ācārya supplied water to the two Lords so that They could wash Their hands. The two Lords then sat down and, smiling, began to eat the prasāda.**
