====== Cc. Madhya 3.54 ======

===== Text =====

> dugdha-ciḍā-kalā āra dugdha-laklakī
> yateka karila' tāhā kahite nā śaki

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice made with milk; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]---mixed with bananas; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:l:laklakī]]---a kind of squash known as //lāu,// boiled with milk; [[synonyms:y:yateka]]---all that; [[synonyms:k:karila']]---was prepared; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to describe; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:ś:śaki]]---I am able.

===== Translation =====

**Besides the other preparations, there were chipped rice made with milk and mixed with bananas, and also white squash boiled in milk. Indeed, it is not possible to describe all the preparations that were made.**
