====== Cc. Madhya 3.170 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā-sabā-sane habe anyatra milana
> muñi abhāginīra mātra ei daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with all of you; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:a:anyatra]]---in another place; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:a:abhāginīra]]---of one who is unfortunate; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Śacīmātā submitted, "As far as you are concerned, you can meet Nimāi, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, many times somewhere else, but what is the possibility of my ever meeting Him again? I shall have to remain at home. A sannyāsī never returns to his home."**
