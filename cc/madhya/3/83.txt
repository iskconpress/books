====== CC Madhya 3.83 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda bale---yabe kaile nimantraṇa
> tata dite cāha, yata kariye bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānandabale]]---Lord Nityānanda said; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---You have done; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:t:tata]]---so much; [[synonyms:d:dite]] [[synonyms:c:cāha]]---You must supply; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as much as; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---I can eat.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda Prabhu replied, "Whatever I may be, You have invited Me. Therefore You must supply as much as I want to eat."**
