====== Cc. Madhya 3.152 ======

===== Text =====

> keśa nā dekhiyā bhakta yadyapi pāya duḥkha
> saundarya dekhite tabu pāya mahā-sukha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keśa]]---hair; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---not seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---the beautiful posture; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---great happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Although the devotees were unhappy at not seeing the Lord's hair, they nonetheless derived great happiness from seeing His beauty.**
