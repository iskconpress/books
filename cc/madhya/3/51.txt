====== CC Madhya 3.51 ======

===== Text =====

> battiśā-āṭhiyā kalāra ḍoṅgā baḍa baḍa
> cale hāle nāhi,---ḍoṅgā ati baḍa daḍa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:battiśā]]-[[synonyms:ā:āṭhiyā]]---producing thirty-two bunches of bananas; [[synonyms:k:kalāra]]---of the banana tree; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍoṅgā]]---pots made of leaves; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---big; [[synonyms:c:cale]] [[synonyms:h:hāle]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---they did not tilt or totter; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍoṅgā]]---pots; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---big; [[synonyms:d:daḍa]]---strong.

===== Translation =====

**All the vegetables were served in pots made of banana leaves taken from trees producing at least thirty-two bunches of bananas. These pots were very strong and big and did not tilt or totter.**
