====== Cc. Madhya 3.108 ======

===== Text =====

> śāntipurera loka śuni' prabhura āgamana
> dekhite āilā loka prabhura caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāntipurera]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people of Śāntipura; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---the arrival of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to see; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**When the people of Śāntipura heard that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was staying there, they all immediately came to see His lotus feet.**
