====== CC Madhya 3.62 ======

===== Text =====

> mukunda kahe---mora kichu kṛtya nāhi sare
> pāche muñi prasāda pāmu, tumi yāha ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukundakahe]]---Mukunda said; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---of me; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---to perform; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:s:sare]]---not yet finished; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:pāmu]]---shall accept; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:y:yāha]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---You both kindly enter the room.

===== Translation =====

**When Mukunda was called for, he submitted, "My dear sir, I have something to do that is not yet finished. Later I shall accept the prasāda, so You two Prabhus should now please enter the room."**
