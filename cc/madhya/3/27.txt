====== CC Madhya 3.27 ======

===== Text =====

> aho bhāgya, yamunāre pāiluṅ daraśana
> eta bali' yamunāra karena stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ahobhāgya]]---oh, My great fortune; [[synonyms:y:yamunāre]]---of the river Yamunā; [[synonyms:p:pāiluṅ]]---I have gotten; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---vision; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---after saying this; [[synonyms:y:yamunāra]]---of the river Yamunā; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---praising.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord said, "Oh, what good fortune! Now I have seen the river Yamunā." Thus thinking the Ganges to be the river Yamunā, Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to offer prayers to it.**
