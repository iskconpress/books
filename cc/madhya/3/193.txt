====== Cc. Madhya 3.193 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā vidāya diyā prabhu calite kaila mana
> haridāsa kāndi' kahe karuṇa vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---asking everyone to return home; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:c:calite]]---to go; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---decided; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kāndi']]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura began to cry; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:k:karuṇa]]---pathetic; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**After requesting all the devotees to return home, the Lord decided to go to Jagannātha Purī. At that time Haridāsa Ṭhākura began to cry and speak some pathetic words.**
