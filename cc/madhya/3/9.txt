====== Cc. Madhya 3.9 ======

===== Text =====

> sei veṣa kaila, ebe vṛndāvana giyā
> kṛṣṇa-niṣevaṇa kari nibhṛte vasiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:veṣa]]---dress; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---accepted; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana-dhāma; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:niṣevaṇa]]---service to the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I shall execute; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting.

===== Translation =====

**After accepting the sannyāsa order, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu decided to go to Vṛndāvana and engage Himself wholly and solely in the service of Mukunda in a solitary place.**
