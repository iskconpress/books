====== CC Madhya 3.101 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe---nā kariba sannyāsi-nimantraṇa
> sannyāsī nāśila mora saba smṛti-dharma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryakahe]]---Advaita Ācārya said; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall never do; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsi]]-[[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation to the //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:n:nāśila]]---has spoiled; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:smṛti]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---regulative principles of the //smṛti-śāstra.//

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya replied, "I shall never again invite another sannyāsī, for it is a sannyāsī who has spoiled all My brahminical smṛti regulations."**
