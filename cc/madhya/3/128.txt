====== CC Madhya 3.128 ======

===== Text =====

> jara-jara haila prabhu bhāvera prahāre
> bhūmite paḍila, śvāsa nāhika śarīre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jara]]-[[synonyms:j:jara]]---tottering; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of the ecstatic moods; [[synonyms:p:prahāre]]---in the onslaught; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell; [[synonyms:ś:śvāsa]]---breathing; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there was not; [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---in the body.

===== Translation =====

**The entire body of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to totter due to the onslaught of various ecstatic symptoms. As a result, He immediately fell on the ground, and His breathing almost stopped.**
