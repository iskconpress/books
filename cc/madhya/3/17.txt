====== CC Madhya 3.17 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-patha prabhu puchena tomāre
> gaṅgā-tīra-patha tabe dekhāiha tāṅre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:p:patha]]---the path to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:p:puchena]]---inquires; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---from you; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]]-[[synonyms:p:patha]]---the path on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:d:dekhāiha]]---please show; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him.

===== Translation =====

**"If Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asks you about the path to Vṛndāvana, please show Him the path on the bank of the Ganges instead."**
