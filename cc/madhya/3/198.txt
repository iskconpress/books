====== CC Madhya 3.198 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe ta' ācārya kahe vinaya kariñā
> dina dui-cāri raha kṛpā ta' kariñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Advaita Ācārya says; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---offering all respect; [[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:c:cāri]]---another two or four days; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---kindly remain; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---showing.

===== Translation =====

**After that, Advaita Ācārya respectfully requested Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu to show Him mercy by remaining another two or four days.**
