====== CC Madhya 3.218 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-gṛhe prabhura vilāsa śune yei jana
> acire milaye tāṅre kṛṣṇa-prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the house of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the pastimes; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---one who; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---person; [[synonyms:a:acire]]---very soon; [[synonyms:m:milaye]]---meets; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the riches of love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**If one hears the activities of the Lord at the house of Advaita Ācārya, he will certainly very soon attain the riches of love of Kṛṣṇa.**
