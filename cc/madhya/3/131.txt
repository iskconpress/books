====== Cc. Madhya 3.131 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda saṅge bule prabhuke dhariñā
> ācārya, haridāsa bule pāche ta', nāciñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---walks; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dhariñā]]---catching; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---walk; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind; [[synonyms:t:ta]]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:nāciñā]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda began to walk with Caitanya Mahāprabhu to see that He would not fall, and Advaita Ācārya and Haridāsa Ṭhākura followed Them, dancing.**
