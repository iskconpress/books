====== Cc. Madhya 3.77 ======

===== Text =====

> mora bhāgye, mora ghare, tomāra āgamana
> chāḍaha cāturī, prabhu, karaha bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:morabhāgye]]---by My fortune; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at My home; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---appearance; [[synonyms:c:chāḍaha]]---please give up; [[synonyms:c:cāturī]]---all this jugglery; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---My Lord; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---just do; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya continued, "By My great fortune You have just come to My home. Please do not juggle words. Just begin eating and do not talk."**
