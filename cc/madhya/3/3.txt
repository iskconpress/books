====== CC Madhya 3.3 ======

===== Text =====

> cabbiśa vatsara-śeṣa yei māgha-māsa
> tāra śukla-pakṣe prabhu karilā sannyāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]]---twenty-fourth; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---of the year; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---at the end; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---that; [[synonyms:m:māgha]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---the month of Māgha (January and February); [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:ś:śukla]]-[[synonyms:p:pakṣe]]---in the waxing period of the moon; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---accepted; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the //sannyāsa// order of life.

===== Translation =====

**At the end of His twenty-fourth year, in the month of Māgha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the sannyāsa order during the waxing period of the moon.**
