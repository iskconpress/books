====== CC Madhya 3.5 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloka paḍi' prabhu bhāvera āveśe
> bhramite pavitra kaila saba rāḍha-deśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eiśloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of ecstasy; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in a condition; [[synonyms:b:bhramite]]---wandering; [[synonyms:p:pavitra]]---purified; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:r:rāḍha]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---all of the tract of land known as Rāḍha-deśa.

===== Translation =====

**Passing through the tract of land known as Rāḍha-deśa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited the following verse in ecstasy.**
