====== Cc. Madhya 3.14 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' tā-sabāra nikaṭa gelā gaurahari
> 'bala' 'bala' bale sabāra śire hasta dhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bala]] [[synonyms:b:bala]]---go on speaking, go on speaking; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---He said; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:ś:śire]] [[synonyms:h:hasta]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---keeping His hand on their heads.

===== Translation =====

**When He heard all the cowherd boys also chanting "Hari! Hari!" Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very pleased. He approached them, put His hand on their heads and said, "Go on chanting like that."**
