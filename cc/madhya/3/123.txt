====== CC Madhya 3.123 ======

===== Text =====

> aśru, kampa, pulaka, sveda, gadgada vacana
> kṣaṇe uṭhe, kṣaṇe paḍe, kṣaṇeka rodana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]---standing of bodily hairs in ecstasy; [[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:g:gadgada]]---faltering; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---stands; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeka]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**Tears fell from His eyes, and His whole body trembled. His hair stood on end, He perspired heavily, and His words faltered. Sometimes He stood, and sometimes He fell. And sometimes He cried.**
