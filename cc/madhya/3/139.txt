====== CC Madhya 3.139 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kṛtya kari' kare nāma-saṅkīrtana
> śacīmātā lañā āilā advaita-bhavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---the morning duties; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:m:mātā]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---at the house of Advaita Ācārya.

===== Translation =====

**In the morning, after regular duties were completed and the Lord was chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, the people accompanied Śacīmātā to the house of Advaita Ācārya.**
