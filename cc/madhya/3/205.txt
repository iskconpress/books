====== CC Madhya 3.205 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata advaita-gṛhe bhakta-gaṇa mile
> vañcilā kataka-dina mahā-kutūhale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the home of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mile]]---meet together; [[synonyms:v:vañcilā]]---passed; [[synonyms:k:kataka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---some days; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kutūhale]]---in a greatly festive mood.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, at Advaita Ācārya's house all the devotees met and passed some days together in a greatly festive mood.**
