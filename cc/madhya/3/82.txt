====== CC Madhya 3.82 ======

===== Text =====

> daridra-brāhmaṇa-ghare ye pāilā muṣṭy-eka anna
> ihāte santuṣṭa hao, chāḍa lobha-mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daridra]]-[[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---of a poor //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the home; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---You have gotten; [[synonyms:m:muṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one handful; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:i:ihāte]]---in this; [[synonyms:s:santuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hao]]---please be satisfied; [[synonyms:c:chāḍa]]---give up; [[synonyms:l:lobha]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---Your greedy mentality.

===== Translation =====

**"I am a poor brāhmaṇa, and You have come to My home. Please be satisfied with whatever little food You have received and give up Your greedy mentality."**
