====== Cc. Madhya 3.143 ======

===== Text =====

> kāndiyā kahena śacī, bāchāre nimāñi
> viśvarūpa-sama nā kariha niṭhurāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāndiyā]]---crying; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:b:bāchāre]]---my darling; [[synonyms:n:nimāñi]]---O Nimāi; [[synonyms:v:viśvarūpa]]---Viśvarūpa; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do not do; [[synonyms:n:niṭhurāi]]---cruelty.

===== Translation =====

**Understanding that Lord Caitanya had accepted the renounced order of life, Śacīmātā, crying, said to the Lord, "My darling Nimāi, do not be cruel like Viśvarūpa, Your elder brother."**
