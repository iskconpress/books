====== CC Madhya 3.112 ======

===== Text =====

> sandhyāte ācārya ārambhila saṅkīrtana
> ācārya nācena, prabhu karena darśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sandhyāte]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ārambhila]]---began; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nācena]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---seeing.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as it was evening, Advaita Ācārya began the congregational chanting. He even began to dance Himself, and the Lord saw the performance.**
