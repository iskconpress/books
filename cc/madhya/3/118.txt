====== CC Madhya 3.118 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' ācārya ānande karena nartana
> prahareka-rātri ācārya kaila saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in pleasure; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing; [[synonyms:p:prahareka]]---about three hours; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---at night; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---performed //saṅkīrtana,// or congregational chanting.

===== Translation =====

**So speaking, Advaita Ācārya performed congregational chanting with great pleasure for three hours that night and danced all the time.**
