====== Cc. Madhya 3.111 ======

===== Text =====

> āise yāya loka harṣe, nāhi samādhāna
> lokera saṅghaṭṭe dina haila avasāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āise]]---come; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---go; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all people; [[synonyms:h:harṣe]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---calculation; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people; [[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭe]]---in crowds; [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the day; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:a:avasāna]]---the end.

===== Translation =====

**People came and went with great pleasure. There was no calculating how many people assembled there before the day was over.**
