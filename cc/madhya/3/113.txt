====== CC Madhya 3.113 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda gosāñi bule ācārya dhariñā
> haridāsa pāche nāce haraṣita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānandagosāñi]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---began to move, dancing; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:d:dhariñā]]---following Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being pleased.

===== Translation =====

**When Advaita Ācārya began to dance, Nityānanda Prabhu began dancing behind Him. Haridāsa Ṭhākura, being very pleased, also began dancing behind Him.**
