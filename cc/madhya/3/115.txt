====== CC Madhya 3.115 ======

===== Text =====

> ei pada gāoyāiyā harṣe karena nartana
> sveda-kampa-pulakāśru-huṅkāra-garjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:p:pada]]---this verse; [[synonyms:g:gāoyāiyā]]---causing to be sung; [[synonyms:h:harṣe]]---in pleasure; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing; [[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---shivering; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]---standing of hairs; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears on account of pleasure; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---thundering; [[synonyms:g:garjana]]---bellowing.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya led the saṅkīrtana party, and with great pleasure He sang this verse. There was a manifestation of ecstatic perspiration, shivering, raised hairs, tears in the eyes and sometimes thundering and bellowing.**
