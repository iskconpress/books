====== CC Madhya 3.182 ======

===== Text =====

> tāte ei yukti bhāla, mora mane laya
> nīlācale rahe yadi, dui kārya haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yukti]]---consideration; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---as good; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---mind; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---takes; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---in Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---He stays; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---purposes; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are achieved.

===== Translation =====

**Mother Śacī said, "This consideration is good. In my opinion, if Nimāi remains at Jagannātha Purī, He may not leave any one of us and at the same time can remain aloof as a sannyāsī. Thus both purposes are fulfilled.**
