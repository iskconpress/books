====== CC Madhya 3.35 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe, mithyā nahe śrīpāda-vacana
> yamunāte snāna tumi karilā ekhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryakahe]]---Advaita Ācārya replied; [[synonyms:m:mithyā]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---this is not untrue; [[synonyms:ś:śrīpāda]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the words of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:y:yamunāte]]---in the river Yamunā; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:e:ekhana]]---just now.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accused Nityānanda of cheating Him, Śrīla Advaita Ācārya said, "Whatever Nityānanda Prabhu has told You is not false. You have indeed just now taken Your bath in the river Yamunā."**
