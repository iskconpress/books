====== CC Madhya 3.159 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya-gosāñira bhāṇḍāra---akṣaya, avyaya
> yata dravya vyaya kare tata dravya haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍāra]]---storehouse; [[synonyms:a:akṣaya]]---inexhaustible; [[synonyms:a:avyaya]]---indestructible; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---commodities; [[synonyms:v:vyaya]]---expenditure; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tata]]---so much; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---commodity; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes filled.

===== Translation =====

**The supplies of Advaita Ācārya were inexhaustible and indestructible. As many goods and commodities as He used, just as many again appeared.**
