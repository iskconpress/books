====== CC Madhya 3.71 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe---chāḍa tumi āpanāra curi
> āmi saba jāni tomāra sannyāsera bhāri-bhuri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryakahe]]---Advaita Ācārya replied; [[synonyms:c:chāḍa]]---give up; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]]---of Yourself; [[synonyms:c:curi]]---the concealment; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---know; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsera]]---of the acceptance of the renounced order; [[synonyms:b:bhāri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuri]]---the confidential meaning.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not accept the food that had already been served, Advaita Ācārya said, "Please give up Your concealment. I know what You are, and I know the confidential meaning of Your accepting the sannyāsa order."**
