====== CC Madhya 3.200 ======

===== Text =====

> ānandita haila ācārya, śacī, bhakta, saba
> prati-dina kare ācārya mahā-mahotsava

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānanditahaila]]---became pleased; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---mother Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---every day; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:utsava]]---great festival.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya's decision was received very happily by Advaita Ācārya, mother Śacī and all the devotees. Advaita Ācārya celebrated every day with a great festival.**
