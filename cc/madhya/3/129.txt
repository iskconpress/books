====== CC Madhya 3.129 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhiyā cintita hailā yata bhakta-gaṇa
> ācambite uṭhe prabhu kariyā garjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:c:cintita]]---anxious; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---all of a sudden; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---rises; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making; [[synonyms:g:garjana]]---thundering.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing the condition of the Lord, all the devotees became very anxious. Then, suddenly, the Lord got up and began to make thundering sounds.**
