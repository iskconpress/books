====== CC Madhya 3.39 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-muṣṭi anna muñi kariyāchoṅ pāka
> śukhārukhā vyañjana kailuṅ, sūpa āra śāka

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:m:muṣṭi]]---one palmful; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---rice; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchoṅ]]---have done; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]---cooking; [[synonyms:ś:śukhā]]-[[synonyms:r:rukhā]]---not very luxurious; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:s:sūpa]]---liquid vegetables; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śāka]]---spinach.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Prabhu continued, "At My home I have just cooked one palmful of rice. The vegetables are always very simple. There is no luxurious cooking-simply a little liquid vegetable and spinach."**
