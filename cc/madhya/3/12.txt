====== CC Madhya 3.12 ======

===== Text =====

> yei yei prabhu dekhe, sei sei loka
> premāveśe 'hari' bale, khaṇḍe duḥkha-śoka

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yeiyei]]---whoever; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---those persons; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---exclaim "Hari"; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍe]]---pass over; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---all kinds of material unhappiness and lamentation.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed through Rāḍha-deśa, whoever saw Him in ecstasy exclaimed, "Hari! Hari!" As they chanted this with the Lord, all the unhappiness of material existence diminished.**
