====== CC Madhya 3.169 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' śacī sabākāre karila minati
> nimāñira daraśana āra muñi pāba kati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing of this; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:m:minati]]---submission; [[synonyms:n:nimāñira]]---of Nimāi, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visit; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---any more; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:pāba]]---shall get; [[synonyms:k:kati]]---where or how many times.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing these proposals made by other devotees of the Lord, mother Śacī said to the devotees, "How many times shall I get the chance to see Nimāi again?"**
