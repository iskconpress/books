====== CC Madhya 3.160 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dina haite śacī karena randhana
> bhakta-gaṇa lañā prabhu karena bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seidinahaite]]---from that date; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:r:randhana]]---cooking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---dining.

===== Translation =====

**From the day Śacīmātā arrived at the house of Advaita Ācārya, she took charge of the cooking, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dined in the company of all the devotees.**
