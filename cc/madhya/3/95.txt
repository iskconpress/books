====== CC Madhya 3.95 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāta dui-cāri lāge ācāryera aṅge
> bhāta aṅge lañā ācārya nāce bahu-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāta]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:c:cāri]]---two or four pieces of the thrown rice; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---touch; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---the body of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhāta]]---the rice; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on His body; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---the Ācārya began to dance; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in many ways.

===== Translation =====

**When two or four pieces of the thrown rice touched His body, Advaita Ācārya began to dance in various ways with the rice still stuck to His body.**
