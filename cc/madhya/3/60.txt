====== CC Madhya 3.60 ======

===== Text =====

> gṛhera bhitare prabhu karuna gamana
> dui bhāi āilā tabe karite bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gṛherabhitare]]---within the room; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karuna]]---kindly do; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---entering; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers, Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---to partake of the //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Prabhu said, "My dear Lords, kindly enter this room." The two brothers, Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu, then came forward to take the prasāda.**
