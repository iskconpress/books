====== Cc. Madhya 3.104 ======

===== Text =====

> sugandhi candane lipta kaila kalevara
> sugandhi puṣpa-mālā āni' dila hṛdaya-upara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:g:gandhi]]---fragrant; [[synonyms:c:candane]]---in sandalwood; [[synonyms:l:lipta]]---smeared; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:k:kalevara]]---the bodies; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:g:gandhi]]---very fragrant; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---flower garlands; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]-[[synonyms:u:upara]]---on the chests.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya then smeared the bodies of the Lords with sandalwood pulp and then placed very fragrant flower garlands on Their chests.**
