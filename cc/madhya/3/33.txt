====== Cc. Madhya 3.33 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe---tumi yāhāṅ, sei vṛndāvana
> mora bhāgye gaṅgā-tīre tomāra āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryakahe]]---Ācārya replied; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherever You are; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---by My great fortune; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---Your appearance.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya disclosed the whole situation, telling Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Wherever You are, that is Vṛndāvana. Now it is My great fortune that You have come to the bank of the Ganges."**
