~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 3.18-19~~

====== CC Madhya 3.18-19 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu puchilena,---'śuna, śiśu-gaṇa
> kaha dekhi, kon pathe yāba vṛndāvana'

> śiśu saba gaṅgā-tīra-patha dekhāila
> sei pathe āveśe prabhu gamana karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:p:puchilena]]---inquired; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:ś:śiśu]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O boys; [[synonyms:k:kahadekhi]]---please tell Me; [[synonyms:k:konpathe]]---in which way; [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ś:śiśu]]---the boys; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]]-[[synonyms:p:patha]]---the path on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the path; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:g:gamanakarila]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**When the cowherd boys were questioned by Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu about the path to Vṛndāvana, the boys showed Him the path on the bank of the Ganges, and the Lord went that way in ecstasy.**
