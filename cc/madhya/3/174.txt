====== CC Madhya 3.174 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā-sabāra ājñā vinā calilāma vṛndāvana
> yāite nārila, vighna kaila nivartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:c:calilāma]]---I started; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---for Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yāite]] [[synonyms:n:nārila]]---not able to go; [[synonyms:v:vighna]]---some obstruction; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivartana]]---made to return.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu informed them all, "Without your order, I tried to go to Vṛndāvana. There was some obstacle, however, and I had to return.**
