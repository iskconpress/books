====== CC Madhya 3.34 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---nityānanda āmāre vañcilā
> gaṅgāke āniyā more yamunā kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]]---Me; [[synonyms:v:vañcilā]]---has cheated; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāke]]---to the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:ā:āniyā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:m:more]]---Me; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]---the river Yamunā; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---informed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "Nityānanda has cheated Me. He has brought Me to the bank of the Ganges and told Me that it was the Yamunā."**
