====== Cc. Madhya 3.116 ======

===== Text =====

> phiri' phiri' kabhu prabhura dharena caraṇa
> caraṇe dhariyā prabhure balena vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:phiri']] [[synonyms:p:phiri']]---turning and turning; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dharena]]---catches; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto the Lord; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---says; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**While dancing, Advaita Ācārya would sometimes turn around and around and catch the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Advaita Ācārya would then speak to Him as follows.**
