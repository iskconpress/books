====== CC Madhya 3.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tā'-sabāra stuti kare,---tomarā bhāgyavān
> kṛtārtha karile more śunāñā hari-nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tā']]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:s:stuti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu praised the behavior; [[synonyms:t:tomarā]]---you; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---fortunate; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---successful; [[synonyms:k:karile]]---you have made; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to Me; [[synonyms:ś:śunāñā]]---by chanting; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Hari.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus blessed them all, saying that they were all fortunate. In this way He praised them, and He felt very successful because they chanted the holy name of Lord Hari.**
