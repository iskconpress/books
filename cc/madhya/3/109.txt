====== Cc. Madhya 3.109 ======

===== Text =====

> 'hari' 'hari' bale loka ānandita hañā
> camatkāra pāila prabhura saundarya dekhiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:harihari]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---say; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonder; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:d:dekhiñā]]---by seeing.

===== Translation =====

**Being very pleased, all the people loudly began to shout the holy name of the Lord, "Hari! Hari!" Indeed, they became struck with wonder upon seeing the beauty of the Lord.**
