====== Cc. Madhya 3.215 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu tāṅre kari' āliṅgana
> nivṛtti kariyā kaila svacchanda gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:n:nivṛtti]]---stop; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:svacchanda]]---without anxiety; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going toward Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Advaita Ācārya and stopped Him from following any further. Then, without anxiety, He proceeded to Jagannātha Purī.**
