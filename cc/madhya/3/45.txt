====== CC Madhya 3.45 ======

===== Text =====

> sārdraka, vāstuka-śāka vividha prakāra
> paṭola, kuṣmāṇḍa-baḍi, mānakacu āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārdraka]]---pots with ginger dishes; [[synonyms:v:vāstuka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāka]]---spinach; [[synonyms:v:vividha]]---various; [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---kinds; [[synonyms:p:paṭola]]---a kind of vegetable; [[synonyms:k:kuṣmāṇḍa]]---squash; [[synonyms:b:baḍi]]---with split //dāl//; [[synonyms:m:mānakacu]]---the root of a vegetable tree called //kacu//; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**Among the cooked vegetables were paṭolas, squash, mānakacu and a salad made with pieces of ginger and various types of spinach.**
