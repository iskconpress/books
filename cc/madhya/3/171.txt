====== CC Madhya 3.171 ======

===== Text =====

> yāvat ācārya-gṛhe nimāñira avasthāna
> muñi bhikṣā dimu, sabākāre māgoṅ dāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long as; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in the house of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nimāñira]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avasthāna]]---the stay; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dimu]]---shall supply the food; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---everyone; [[synonyms:m:māgoṅ]]---I beg; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---this charity.

===== Translation =====

**Mother Śacī appealed to all the devotees to give her this charity: As long as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at the house of Advaita Ācārya, only she would supply Him food.**
