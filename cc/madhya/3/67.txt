====== CC Madhya 3.67 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu bale---vaisa tine kariye bhojana
> ācārya kahe---āmi kariba pariveśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhubale]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:v:vaisa]]---sit down; [[synonyms:t:tine]]---in the three places; [[synonyms:k:kariye]]---do; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Advaita Ācārya replied; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:k:kariba]] [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]]---I shall distribute.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Let Us sit down in these three places, and We shall take prasāda." However, Advaita Ācārya said, "I shall distribute the prasāda."**
