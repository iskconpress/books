====== CC Madhya 3.13 ======

===== Text =====

> gopa-bālaka saba prabhuke dekhiyā
> 'hari' 'hari' bali' ḍāke ucca kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:b:bālaka]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all the cowherd boys; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---vibrating the sounds "Hari Hari"; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāke]]---shout; [[synonyms:u:ucca]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---loudly.

===== Translation =====

**All the cowherd boys who saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passing joined with Him and began to shout loudly, "Hari! Hari!"**
