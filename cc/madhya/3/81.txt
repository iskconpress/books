====== CC Madhya 3.81 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe---tumi hao tairthika sannyāsī
> kabhu phala-mūla khāo, kabhu upavāsī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryakahe]]---Advaita Ācārya replied to Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:h:hao]]---are; [[synonyms:t:tairthika]] [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---a mendicant wandering on pilgrimages; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:m:mūla]]---fruits and roots; [[synonyms:k:khāo]]---You eat; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:u:upavāsī]]---sometimes fasting.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya replied, "Sir, You are a mendicant traveling on pilgrimage. Sometimes You eat fruits and roots, and sometimes You simply go on fasting.**
