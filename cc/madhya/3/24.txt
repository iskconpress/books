====== CC Madhya 3.24 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---śrīpāda, tomāra kothāke gamana
> śrīpāda kahe, tomāra saṅge yāba vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---the Lord inquired; [[synonyms:ś:śrīpāda]]---sir; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:k:kothāke]]---where; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going; [[synonyms:ś:śrīpāda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Nityānanda Prabhu replied; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---You; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---toward Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was in ecstasy, and He asked where Nityānanda Prabhu was going. Nityānanda replied that He was going with Him toward Vṛndāvana.**
