====== CC Madhya 3.26 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' ānila tāṅre gaṅgā-sannidhāne
> āveśe prabhura haila gaṅgāre yamunā-jñāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etabali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---He brought; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:s:sannidhāne]]---near the Ganges; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāre]]---of the river Ganges; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---acceptance as the river Yamunā.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Nityānanda Prabhu took Caitanya Mahāprabhu near the Ganges, and the Lord, in His ecstasy, accepted the river Ganges as the river Yamunā.**
