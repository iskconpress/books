====== Cc. Madhya 3.122 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya uṭhāila prabhuke karite nartana
> pada śuni' prabhura aṅga nā yāya dhāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāila]]---raised; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing; [[synonyms:p:pada]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---by hearing the stanzas; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the body; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---possible; [[synonyms:d:dhāraṇa]]---to hold.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya raised the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to help Him dance, but the Lord, after hearing the stanzas sung by Mukunda, could not be held due to His bodily symptoms.**
