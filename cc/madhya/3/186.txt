====== Cc. Madhya 3.186 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' bhakta-gaṇa tāṅre karila stavana
> veda-ājñā yaiche, mātā, tomāra vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to her; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---praying; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---an injunction of the //Vedas//; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---like; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---my dear mother; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---your word.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing Śacīmātā, all the devotees offered her prayers and assured her that her order, like a Vedic injunction, could not be violated.**
