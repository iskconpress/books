====== Cc. Madhya 3.72 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana karaha, chāḍa vacana-cāturī
> prabhu kahe---eta anna khāite nā pāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojanakaraha]]---kindly accept this //prasāda//; [[synonyms:c:chāḍa]]---give up; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]-[[synonyms:c:cāturī]]---jugglery of words; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:k:khāite]]---to eat; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am not able.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya thus requested Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to eat and give up juggling words. The Lord replied, "I certainly cannot eat so much food."**
