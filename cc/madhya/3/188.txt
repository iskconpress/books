====== CC Madhya 3.188 ======

===== Text =====

> navadvīpa-vāsī ādi yata bhakta-gaṇa
> sabāre sammāna kari' balilā vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:navadvīpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---all the inhabitants of Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---primarily; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to all of them; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---respect; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing; [[synonyms:b:balilā]]---said; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---these words.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered respects to all the devotees present from Navadvīpa and other towns, speaking to them as follows.**
