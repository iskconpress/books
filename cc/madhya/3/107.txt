====== CC Madhya 3.107 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe ta' ācārya saṅge lañā dui jane
> karila icchāya bhojana, ye āchila mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabeta']]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---the two persons, namely Mukunda and Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---according to desire; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:ā:āchila]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---whatever there was in mind.

===== Translation =====

**Thereupon Advaita Ācārya took prasāda with Mukunda and Haridāsa, and they all wholeheartedly ate as much as they desired.**
