====== CC Madhya 3.153-155 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsa, rāmāi, vidyānidhi, gadādhara
> gaṅgādāsa, vakreśvara, murāri, śuklāmbara
>
> buddhimanta khāṅ, nandana, śrīdhara, vijaya
> vāsudeva, dāmodara, mukunda, sañjaya
>
> kata nāma la-iba yata navadvīpa-vāsī
> sabāre mililā prabhu kṛpā-dṛṣṭye hāsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:r:rāmāi]]---Rāmāi; [[synonyms:v:vidyānidhi]]---Vidyānidhi; [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgādāsa]]---Gaṅgādāsa; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri; [[synonyms:ś:śuklāmbara]]---Śuklāmbara; [[synonyms:b:buddhimanta]] [[synonyms:k:khāṅ]]---Buddhimanta Khāṅ; [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Nandana; [[synonyms:ś:śrīdhara]]---Śrīdhara; [[synonyms:v:vijaya]]---Vijaya; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:s:sañjaya]]---Sañjaya; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---how many names; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iba]]---I shall mention; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---the inhabitants of Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭye]]---with merciful glances; [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa, Rāmāi, Vidyānidhi, Gadādhara, Gaṅgādāsa, Vakreśvara, Murāri, Śuklāmbara, Buddhimanta Khāṅ, Nandana, Śrīdhara, Vijaya, Vāsudeva, Dāmodara, Mukunda, Sañjaya and all the others, however many I can mention-indeed, all the inhabitants of Navadvīpa-arrived there, and the Lord met them with smiles and glances of mercy.**
