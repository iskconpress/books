====== CC Madhya 3.53 ======

===== Text =====

> saghṛta-pāyasa nava-mṛt-kuṇḍikā bhariñā
> tina pātre ghanāvarta-dugdha rākheta dhariñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:ghṛta]]-[[synonyms:p:pāyasa]]---sweet rice mixed with ghee; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:m:mṛt]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍikā]]---new earthen pots; [[synonyms:b:bhariñā]]---filling; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:p:pātre]]---in three pots; [[synonyms:g:ghana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āvarta]]-[[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---finely condensed milk; [[synonyms:r:rākheta]]---were kept; [[synonyms:d:dhariñā]]---fixing.

===== Translation =====

**Along with the various vegetables was sweet rice mixed with ghee. This was kept in new earthen pots. Earthen pots filled with highly condensed milk were placed in three places.**
