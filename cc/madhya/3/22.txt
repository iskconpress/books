====== CC Madhya 3.22 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe navadvīpe tumi kariha gamana
> śacī-saha lañā āisa saba bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---to Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---should do; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking along; [[synonyms:ā:āisa]]---come back; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**"After that," Nityānanda Prabhu continued, "I shall go to Advaita Ācārya's house, and you should go to Navadvīpa and return with mother Śacī and all the other devotees."**
