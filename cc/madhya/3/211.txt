====== CC Madhya 3.211 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅre pradakṣiṇa kari' karila gamana
> ethā ācāryera ghare uṭhila krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:p:pradakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---circumambulating; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---there arose; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**When everything was arranged, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu circumambulated His mother and then started for Jagannātha Purī. In the house of Advaita Ācārya there arose tumultuous crying.**
