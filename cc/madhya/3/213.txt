====== CC Madhya 3.213 ======

===== Text =====

> kata dūra giyā prabhu kari' yoḍa hāta
> ācārye prabodhi' kahe kichu miṣṭa bāta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---after going some distance; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]] [[synonyms:h:hāta]]---folded hands; [[synonyms:ā:ācārye]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabodhi']]---pacifying; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:m:miṣṭa]] [[synonyms:b:bāta]]---sweet words.

===== Translation =====

**After He had followed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for some distance, Advaita Ācārya was petitioned by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with folded hands. The Lord spoke the following sweet words.**
