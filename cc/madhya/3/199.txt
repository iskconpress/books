====== Cc. Madhya 3.199 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryera vākya prabhu nā kare laṅghana
> rahilā advaita-gṛhe, nā kaila gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]] [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---the words of Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:l:laṅghana]]---does not deny; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the house of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---did not go immediately.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya Mahāprabhu never violated the request of Advaita Ācārya; therefore He remained at His home and did not leave immediately for Jagannātha Purī.**
