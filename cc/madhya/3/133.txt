====== Cc. Madhya 3.133 ======

===== Text =====

> tina dina upavāse kariyā bhojana
> uddaṇḍa-nṛtyete prabhura haila pariśrama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tinadina]]---three days; [[synonyms:u:upavāse]]---in fasting; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:u:uddaṇḍa]]---jumping high; [[synonyms:n:nṛtyete]]---in dancing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:pariśrama]]---fatigue.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord had been fasting for three days, and after that period He took eatables sumptuously. Thus when He danced and jumped high, He became a little fatigued.**
