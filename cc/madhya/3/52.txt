====== Cc. Madhya 3.52 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcāśa pañcāśa ḍoṅgā vyañjane pūriñā
> tina bhogera āśe pāśe rākhila dhariñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañcāśa]] [[synonyms:p:pañcāśa]]---fifty and fifty; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍoṅgā]]---pots; [[synonyms:v:vyañjane]]---with vegetables; [[synonyms:p:pūriñā]]---filling; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:b:bhogera]]---of eating places; [[synonyms:ā:āśe]] [[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---all around; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept; [[synonyms:d:dhariñā]]---fixing.

===== Translation =====

**All around the three eating places were a hundred pots filled with various kinds of vegetables.**
