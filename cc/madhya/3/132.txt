====== CC Madhya 3.132 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata prahareka nāce prabhu raṅge
> kabhu harṣa, kabhu viṣāda, bhāvera taraṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prahareka]]---for about three hours; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:v:viṣāda]]---moroseness; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of ecstasy; [[synonyms:t:taraṅge]]---in the waves.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the Lord danced for at least three hours. Sometimes the symptoms of ecstasy were visible, including pleasure, moroseness and many other waves of ecstatic emotional love.**
