====== CC Madhya 3.175 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi sahasā āmi kariyāchi sannyāsa
> tathāpi tomā-sabā haite nahiba udāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:s:sahasā]]---all of a sudden; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchi]] [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---accepted //sannyāsa//; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of you; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:n:nahiba]]---I shall never be; [[synonyms:u:udāsa]]---indifferent.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear friends, although I have suddenly accepted this renounced order, I still know that I shall never be indifferent to you.**
