====== CC Madhya 3.38 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe tina dina ācha upavāsa
> āji mora ghare bhikṣā, cala mora vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---three days; [[synonyms:ā:ācha]]---You are; [[synonyms:u:upavāsa]]---fasting; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the house; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---alms; [[synonyms:c:cala]]---kindly come; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---to My residence.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya said, "You have been fasting continuously for three days in Your ecstasy of love for Kṛṣṇa. I therefore invite You to My home, where You may kindly take Your alms. Come with Me to My residence."**
