====== CC Madhya 3.157 ======

===== Text =====

> yata loka āila mahāprabhuke dekhite
> nānā-grāma haite, āra navadvīpa haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yataloka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from various villages; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Navadvīpa.

===== Translation =====

**People came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu from various other villages nearby, as well as Navadvīpa.**
