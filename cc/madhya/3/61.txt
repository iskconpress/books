====== CC Madhya 3.61 ======

===== Text =====

> mukunda, haridāsa,---dui prabhu bolāila
> yoḍa-hāte dui-jana kahite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the two Lords; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---called for; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu went to accept the prasāda, They both called Mukunda and Haridāsa to come with Them. However, Mukunda and Haridāsa, both with folded hands, spoke as follows.**
