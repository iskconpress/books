====== CC Madhya 3.102 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' dui jane karāila ācamana
> uttama śayyāte la-iyā karāila śayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etabali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---unto the two personalities; [[synonyms:k:karāila]] [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---washed Their hands and mouth; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---very nice; [[synonyms:ś:śayyāte]]---on a bed; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made Them do; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Advaita Ācārya made the Lords wash Their hands and mouths. He then took Them to a nice bed and made Them lie down to take rest.**
