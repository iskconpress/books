====== CC Madhya 3.105 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya karite cāhe pāda-saṁvāhana
> saṅkucita hañā prabhu balena vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvāhana]]---massaging the feet; [[synonyms:s:saṅkucita]]---hesitant; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---says; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the words.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord lay down on the bed, Advaita Ācārya waited to massage His legs, but the Lord was very hesitant and spoke as follows to Advaita Ācārya.**
