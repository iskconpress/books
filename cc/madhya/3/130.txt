====== Cc. Madhya 3.130 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bal' 'bal' bale, nāce, ānande vihvala
> bujhana nā yāya bhāva-taraṅga prabala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bal]] [[synonyms:b:bal]]---speak, speak; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---the Lord says; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in pleasure; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:b:bujhana]]---understanding; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---not possible; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---the waves of ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:prabala]]---powerful.

===== Translation =====

**Upon standing up, the Lord said, "Go on speaking! Go on speaking!" Thus He began to dance, overwhelmed with pleasure. No one could understand the strong waves of this ecstasy.**
