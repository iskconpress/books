====== CC Madhya 3.21 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu laye yāba āmi tāṅhāra mandire
> sāvadhāne rahena yena naukā lañā tīre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhulaye]]---taking the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---shall go; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to the house; [[synonyms:s:sāvadhāne]]---very carefully; [[synonyms:r:rahena]]---let Him stay; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---there; [[synonyms:n:naukā]]---boat; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda Gosvāmī told him, "I shall take Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to the bank of the Ganges at Śāntipura, and Advaita Ācārya should carefully stay there on shore with a boat.**
