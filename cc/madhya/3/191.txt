====== CC Madhya 3.191 ======

===== Text =====

> ājñā deha nīlācale kariye gamana
> madhye madhye āsi' tomāya diba daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha]]---give permission; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:k:kariye]]---I do; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going; [[synonyms:m:madhye]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming here; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---to all of you; [[synonyms:d:diba]]---I shall give; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---audience.

===== Translation =====

**After thus instructing the devotees, the Lord asked their permission to go to Jagannātha Purī. He assured them that at intervals He would come there and meet them again and again.**
