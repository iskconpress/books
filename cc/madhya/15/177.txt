====== CC Madhya 15.177 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra eka rāi-nāśe hāni nāhi māni
> aiche eka aṇḍa-nāśe kṛṣṇera nāhi hāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of it; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:r:rāi]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśe]]---loss of a mustard seed; [[synonyms:h:hāni]]---loss; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---notice; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:a:aṇḍa]]---universe; [[synonyms:n:nāśe]]---being lost; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:h:hāni]]---there is no loss.

===== Translation =====

**"Of the millions of mustard seeds floating in that pot, if one seed is lost, the loss is not at all significant. Similarly, if one universe is lost, it is not significant to Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
