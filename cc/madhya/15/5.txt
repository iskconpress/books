====== CC Madhya 15.5 ======

===== Text =====

> prathamāvasare jagannātha-daraśana
> nṛtya-gīta kare daṇḍa-paraṇāma, stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathama]]-[[synonyms:a:avasare]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing the Deity of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:p:paraṇāma]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---offering prayers.

===== Translation =====

**In the beginning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the Deity of Lord Jagannātha in the temple. He offered Him obeisances and prayers and danced and sang before Him.**
