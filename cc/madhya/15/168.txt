====== CC Madhya 15.168 ======

===== Text =====

> asamartha nahe kṛṣṇa, dhare sarva bala
> tomāke vā kene bhuñjāibe pāpa-phala?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:asamarthanahe]]---is not unable; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---possesses; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:b:bala]]---all potencies; [[synonyms:t:tomāke]]---//you//; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---then; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:b:bhuñjāibe]]---would cause to suffer; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---results of sinful reactions.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa is not incapable, for He has all potencies. Why would He induce you to suffer the sinful reactions of other living entities?**
