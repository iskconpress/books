====== Cc. Madhya 15.70 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dravya rahu---śuna nārikelera kathā
> pāṅca gaṇḍā kari' nārikela vikāya tathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:r:rahu]]---apart from the other commodities; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---just hear; [[synonyms:n:nārikelera]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the incident of offering coconuts; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇḍā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---at the price of five //gaṇḍās//; [[synonyms:n:nārikela]]---coconut; [[synonyms:v:vikāya]]---is sold; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there.

===== Translation =====

**"Apart from other commodities, just hear about his coconut offering. A coconut is sold at the rate of five gaṇḍās each.**
