====== Cc. Madhya 15.39 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu saba bhakte bolāila
> gauḍa-deśe yāha sabe vidāya karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabemahāprabhu]]---thereafter Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---called; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---return; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---bade farewell.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu called for all the devotees and asked them to return to Bengal. In this way, He bade farewell to them.**
