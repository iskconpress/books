====== CC Madhya 15.233 ======

===== Text =====

> udyoga nā chila mora gṛhiṇīra randhane
> yāṅra śaktye bhoga siddha, sei tāhā jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:udyoga]]---exertion; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:chila]]---there was not; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---of me; [[synonyms:g:gṛhiṇīra]]---of my wife; [[synonyms:r:randhane]]---in cooking; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---by whose potency; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:s:siddha]]---the food has been prepared; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---He; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows that.

===== Translation =====

**"My wife and I did not especially exert ourselves in the cooking. He by whose power the food has been prepared knows everything.**
