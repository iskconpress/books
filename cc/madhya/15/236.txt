====== CC Madhya 15.236 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---bhāla kaile, śāstra-ājñā haya
> kṛṣṇera sakala śeṣa bhṛtya āsvādaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---you have spoken correctly; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is such an order in the revealed scripture; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---everything left by Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---the servant; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādaya]]---partakes of.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "Yes, you have spoken correctly. The śāstras enjoin that the devotee can partake of everything left by Kṛṣṇa.**
