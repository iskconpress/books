====== Cc. Madhya 15.89 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata piṭhā-pānā, kṣīra-odana
> parama pavitra, āra kare sarvottama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:piṭhā]]-[[synonyms:p:pānā]]---cakes and sweet rice; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:o:odana]]---concentrated milk; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:p:pavitra]]---highly purified; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---he makes; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---first class, tasteful.

===== Translation =====

**"He prepared cakes, sweet rice, concentrated milk and everything else with great attention, and the cooking conditions were purified so that the food was first class and tasteful.**
