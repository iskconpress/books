====== Cc. Madhya 15.284 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma-gṛhe dāsa-dāsī, ye kukkura
> seha mora priya, anya jana rahu dūra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsī]]---servants and maidservants; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kukkura]]---even a dog; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---all of them; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---to Me; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---very dear; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---others; [[synonyms:r:rahu]] [[synonyms:d:dūra]]---what to speak of.

===== Translation =====

**"Everyone in Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya's house is very dear to Me, including his maids and servants and even his dog. And what to speak of his relatives?**
