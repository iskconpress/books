====== Cc. Madhya 15.175 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta aiśvarya kṛṣṇera vaikuṇṭhādi-dhāma
> tāra gaḍa-khāi---kāraṇābdhi yāra nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---opulence; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---innumerable Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of Vaikuṇṭhaloka; [[synonyms:g:gaḍa]]-[[synonyms:k:khāi]]---surrounding water; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:abdhi]]---Causal Ocean; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name.

===== Translation =====

**"The entire spiritual world constitutes the unlimited opulence of Kṛṣṇa, and there are innumerable Vaikuṇṭha planets there. The Causal Ocean is considered the surrounding waters of Vaikuṇṭhaloka.**
