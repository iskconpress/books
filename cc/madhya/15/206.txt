====== CC Madhya 15.206 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhye eka dvāra tāra, prabhu praveśite
> pāka-śālāra eka dvāra anna pariveśite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhye]]---outside; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---one door; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of this room; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:p:praveśite]]---for the entrance of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śālāra]]---of the kitchen; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---another door; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:p:pariveśite]]---to serve.

===== Translation =====

**The room was so constructed that there was only one door as an entrance from the outside for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. There was another door attached to the kitchen, and it was through this door that the food was brought.**
