====== CC Madhya 15.76 ======

===== Text =====

> jala-śūnya phala dekhi' paṇḍita---haraṣita
> phala bhāṅgi' śasye kare sat-pātra pūrita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:ś:śūnya]]---without water; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---fruit; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:p:phala]] [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgi']]---breaking the fruit; [[synonyms:ś:śasye]]---with the pulp; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---another plate; [[synonyms:p:pūrita]]---filled.

===== Translation =====

**"When Rāghava Paṇḍita saw that the juice had been drunk from the coconuts, he was very pleased. He would then break the coconut, take out the pulp and put it on another plate.**
