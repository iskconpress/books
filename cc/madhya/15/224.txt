====== Cc. Madhya 15.224 ======

===== Text =====

> annādi dekhiyā prabhu vismita hañā
> bhaṭṭācārye kahe kichu bhaṅgi kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing the arrangement of food; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vismita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being astonished; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārye]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said to Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgi]]---gesture; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was a little astonished to see the gorgeous arrangement, and, gesturing, He spoke to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
