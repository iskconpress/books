====== CC Madhya 15.60 ======

===== Text =====

> kibā mora kathāya mane bhrama hañā gela!
> kibā kona jantu āsi' sakala khāila?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kibā]]---or; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:k:kathāya]]---when I was thinking like that; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:b:bhrama]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---I was mistaken; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---or; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:j:jantu]]---some animal; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate everything.

===== Translation =====

**"She began to wonder whether there was actually anything on the plate in the first place. Then again she thought that some animal might have come and eaten everything.**
