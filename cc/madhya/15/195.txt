====== CC Madhya 15.195 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara-svarūpa,---ei bāndhava āmāra
> kabhu tomāra saṅge yābe, kabhu ekeśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bāndhava]] [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my very intimate friend; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with You; [[synonyms:y:yābe]]---will come; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:e:ekeśvara]]---alone.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Dāmodara Svarūpa is my intimate friend. He will come sometimes with You and sometimes alone.**
