====== CC Madhya 15.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata anyonye karena namaskāra
> prabhure nimantraṇa kare ācārya bāra bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:a:anyonye]]---to one another; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---offer; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**In this way both Advaita Ācārya and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would offer Their respectful obeisances unto one another. Then Advaita Ācārya would extend invitations to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again and again.**
