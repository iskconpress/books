====== CC Madhya 15.199 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura iṅgita pāñā ānandita mana
> sei dina mahāprabhura kaila nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:i:iṅgita]]---acceptance; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---receiving; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very happy; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---on that day; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**Having this arrangement confirmed by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Bhaṭṭācārya became very glad and immediately invited the Lord to his house on that very day.**
