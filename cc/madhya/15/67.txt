====== CC Madhya 15.67 ======

===== Text =====

> eteka kahite prabhu vihvala ha-ilā
> loka vidāya karite prabhu dhairya dharilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eteka]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became overwhelmed; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to bid farewell to the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhairya]] [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---maintained patience.

===== Translation =====

**While describing all this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was a little overwhelmed, but just to finish bidding farewell to the devotees, He remained patient.**
