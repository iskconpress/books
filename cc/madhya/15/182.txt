====== Cc. Madhya 15.182 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura vicchede bhakta karena rodana
> bhaktera vicchede prabhura viṣaṇṇa haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---from Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vicchede]]---by separation; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---do; [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---crying; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vicchede]]---by the separation; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:viṣaṇṇa]]---morose; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Due to the impending separation from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the devotees began to cry. The Lord was also morose due to separation from the devotees.**
