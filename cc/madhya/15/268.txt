====== CC Madhya 15.268 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvare ta' aparādha phale tata-kṣaṇa
> eta bali' paḍe dui śāstrera vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvare]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense; [[synonyms:p:phale]]---brings results; [[synonyms:t:tata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---immediately; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---quotations from revealed scriptures.

===== Translation =====

**"When one offends the Supreme Personality of Godhead, karma immediately takes effect." After saying this, he recited two verses from revealed scripture.**
