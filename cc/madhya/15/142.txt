====== CC Madhya 15.142 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kṛṣṇa bhaja tumi, hao kṛṣṇāśraya
> kṛṣṇa vinā anya-upāsanā mane nāhi laya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---that Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhaja]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---engage yourself in His service; [[synonyms:h:hao]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---take shelter of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:u:upāsanā]]---any other worship; [[synonyms:m:mane]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---does not appeal to the mind.

===== Translation =====

**"I then requested Murāri Gupta, 'Worship Kṛṣṇa and take shelter of Him. But for His service, nothing appeals to the mind.'**
