====== Cc. Madhya 15.203 ======

===== Text =====

> āpani bhaṭṭācārya kare pākera saba karma
> ṣāṭhīra mātā---vicakṣaṇā, jāne pāka-marma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---arranges; [[synonyms:p:pākera]]---of cooking; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:k:karma]]---all activities; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭhīra]] [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of Ṣāṭhī; [[synonyms:v:vicakṣaṇā]]---very experienced; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]-[[synonyms:m:marma]]---how to cook.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya personally began to help his wife cook. His wife, the mother of Ṣāṭhī, was very experienced, and she knew how to cook nicely.**
