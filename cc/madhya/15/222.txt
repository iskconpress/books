====== CC Madhya 15.222 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle mahāprabhu madhyāhna kariyā
> ekale āila tāṅra hṛdaya jāniyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---finishing His midday duties; [[synonyms:e:ekale]]---alone; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---the heart; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---knowing.

===== Translation =====

**When everything was ready, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came there alone after finishing His midday duties. He knew the heart of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
