====== Cc. Madhya 15.290 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-pada dhari' bhaṭṭa kahite lāgilā
> marita' amogha, tāre kene jīyāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching hold of; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak; [[synonyms:m:marita']] [[synonyms:a:amogha]]---Amogha would have died; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---//him//; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:j:jīyāilā]]---have You brought to life.

===== Translation =====

**Catching hold of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's lotus feet, Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Why did You bring Amogha back to life? It would have been better had he died."**
