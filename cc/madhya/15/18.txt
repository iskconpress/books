====== CC Madhya 15.18 ======

===== Text =====

> dadhi-dugdha-bhāra sabe nija-skandhe kari'
> mahotsava-sthāne āilā bali 'hari' 'hari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dadhi]]-[[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---of milk and yogurt; [[synonyms:b:bhāra]]---carriers; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:skandhe]]---on their shoulders; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---keeping; [[synonyms:m:mahotsava]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of the festival; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:b:bali]] [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---chanting Hari, Hari.

===== Translation =====

**Having dressed up like cowherd boys, all the devotees carried pots of milk and yogurt balanced on rods over their shoulders. Thus they all arrived at the festival grounds chanting the holy name of Hari.**
