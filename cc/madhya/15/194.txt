====== CC Madhya 15.194 ======

===== Text =====

> purī-gosāñira bhikṣā pāṅca-dina mora ghare
> pūrve āmi kahiyāchoṅ tomāra gocare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---invitation for lunch; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---five days; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at my home; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kahiyāchoṅ]]---mentioned; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:g:gocare]]---it is known to you.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then submitted that Paramānanda Purī Gosvāmī should accept a five-day invitation at his place. This was settled before the Lord.**
