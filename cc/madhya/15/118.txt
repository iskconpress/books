====== CC Madhya 15.118 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaktera mahimā prabhu kahite pāya sukha
> bhaktera mahimā kahite haya pañca-mukha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakteramahimā]]---the glories of a devotee; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:p:pāya]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---gets happiness; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of a devotee; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---five faced.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very happy just to speak of the glories of His devotees. Indeed, when He spoke of their glories, it was as if He had five faces.**
