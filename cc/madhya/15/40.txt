====== CC Madhya 15.40 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāre kahila prabhu---pratyabda āsiyā
> guṇḍicā dekhiyā yābe āmāre miliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to all of them; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:a:abda]]---every year; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]---the function at the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:y:yābe]]---you should go; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]] [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---after meeting Me.

===== Translation =====

**Bidding farewell to all the devotees, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested them to return to Jagannātha Purī every year to see Him and then see the cleansing of the Guṇḍicā temple.**
