~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 15.283~~

====== CC Madhya 15.283 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu āśvāsana kare sparśi' tāra gātra
> sārvabhauma-sambandhe tumi mora sneha-pātra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---pacifies; [[synonyms:s:sparśi']]---touching; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:g:gātra]]---body; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---because of a relation to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---object of affection.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu pacified Amogha by touching his body and saying, "You are the object of My affection because you are the son-in-law of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
