====== CC Madhya 15.258 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' mahāprabhu calilā bhavane
> bhaṭṭācārya tāṅra ghare gelā tāṅra sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]] [[synonyms:b:bhavane]]---returned to His residence; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to His place; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Him.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left and returned to His residence. Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya also followed Him.**
