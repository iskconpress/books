====== Cc. Madhya 15.52 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlācale āchoṅ muñi tāṅhāra ājñāte
> madhye madhye āsimu tāṅra caraṇa dekhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlācale]] [[synonyms:ā:āchoṅ]]---stay at Jagannātha Purī, Nīlācala; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāte]]---under her order; [[synonyms:m:madhye]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---at intervals; [[synonyms:ā:āsimu]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---her; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"I am staying here at Jagannātha Purī, Nīlācala, to comply with her orders. However, at intervals I shall go see her lotus feet.**
