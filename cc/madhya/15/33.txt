====== Cc. Madhya 15.33 ======

===== Text =====

> hanumān-āveśe prabhu vṛkṣa-śākhā lañā
> laṅkā-gaḍe caḍi' phele gaḍa bhāṅgiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hanumān]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the emotion of being Hanumān; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śākhā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking a large branch of a tree; [[synonyms:l:laṅkā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaḍe]]---on the Laṅkā fort; [[synonyms:c:caḍi']]---ascending; [[synonyms:p:phele]]---breaks down; [[synonyms:g:gaḍa]]---the fort; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgiyā]]---dismantling.

===== Translation =====

**Displaying the emotions of Hanumān, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took up a large tree branch, and, mounting the walls of the Laṅkā fort, began to dismantle it.**
