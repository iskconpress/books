====== CC Madhya 15.158 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe vāsudeve prabhu kari' āliṅgana
> tāṅra guṇa kahe hañā sahasra-vadana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeve]]---Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---his good qualities; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---began to explain; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---possessing thousands of mouths.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Vāsudeva Datta and began to speak of his glories as if He had a thousand mouths.**
