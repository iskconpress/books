====== CC Madhya 15.35 ======

===== Text =====

> gosāñira āveśa dekhi' loke camatkāra
> sarva-loka jaya' jaya' bale bāra bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---all people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---all people; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---speak; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone became very astonished to see the emotional ecstasy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and everyone began to chant, "All glories! All glories!" again and again.**
