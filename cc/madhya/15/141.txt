====== Cc. Madhya 15.141 ======

===== Text =====

> madhura-caritra kṛṣṇera madhura-vilāsa
> cāturya-vaidagdhya kare yāṅra līlā-rasa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---pleasing character; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---melodious pastimes; [[synonyms:c:cāturya]]---expertise; [[synonyms:v:vaidagdhya]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---manifests; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of pastimes; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---mellows.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'His character is very sweet, and His pastimes are melodious. He is expert in intelligence, and thus He enjoys all His pastimes and mellows.'**
