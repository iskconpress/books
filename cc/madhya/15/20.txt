====== Cc. Madhya 15.20 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane pratāparudra, āra miśra-kāśī
> sārvabhauma, āra paḍichā-pātra tulasī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]] [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---personally King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:k:kāśī]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]] [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]---Paḍichāpātra Tulasī, the temple superintendent.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, King Pratāparudra was also personally present with Kāśī Miśra, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Tulasī Paḍichāpātra.**
