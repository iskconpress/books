====== Cc. Madhya 15.288 ======

===== Text =====

> uṭha, snāna kara, dekha jagannātha-mukha
> śīghra āsi, bhojana kara, tabe mora sukha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uṭha]]---get up; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---take your bath; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---see; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---Lord Jagannātha's face; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi]]---coming back very soon; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---take your lunch; [[synonyms:t:tabe]] [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---then I shall be very happy.

===== Translation =====

**"Just get up and take your bath and go see the face of Lord Jagannātha. Then return here to eat your lunch. In this way I shall be happy.**
