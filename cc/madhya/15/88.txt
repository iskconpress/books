====== Cc. Madhya 15.88 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata vyañjanera śāka, mūla, phala
> ei mata ciḍā, huḍuma, sandeśa sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vyañjanera]]---of vegetables; [[synonyms:ś:śāka]]---spinach; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---radishes; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---fruits; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice; [[synonyms:h:huḍuma]]---powdered rice; [[synonyms:s:sandeśa]]---sweetmeats; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus with great care and attention Rāghava Paṇḍita would prepare spinach, other vegetables, radishes, fruits, chipped rice, powdered rice and sweetmeats.**
