====== Cc. Madhya 15.102 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe rāmānanda, āra satyarāja khāṅna
> prabhura caraṇe kichu kaila nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after this; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Vasu; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:s:satyarāja]] [[synonyms:k:khāṅna]]---Satyarāja Khān; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Rāmānanda Vasu and Satyarāja Khān both submitted questions at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
