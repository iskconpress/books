====== CC Madhya 15.202 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācāryera gṛhe saba dravya āche bhari'
> yebā śāka-phalādika, ānāila āhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]] [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---all kinds of ingredients; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:y:yebā]]---whatever; [[synonyms:ś:śāka]]---spinach; [[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādika]]---fruits and so on; [[synonyms:ā:ānāila]]---he brought; [[synonyms:ā:āhari']]---collecting.

===== Translation =====

**At Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya's house, there was always a full stock of food. Whatever spinach, vegetables, fruit and so on were required, he collected and brought back home.**
