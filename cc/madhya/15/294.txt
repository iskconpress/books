====== Cc. Madhya 15.294 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---gopīnātha, ihāñi rahibā
> iṅho prasāda pāile, vārtā āmāke kahibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:i:ihāñi]] [[synonyms:r:rahibā]]---please stay here; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:p:pāile]]---when he takes his lunch; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---the news; [[synonyms:ā:āmāke]] [[synonyms:k:kahibā]]---inform Me.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then told Gopīnātha, "Stay here and inform Me when Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya has taken his prasāda."**
