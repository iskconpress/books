====== CC Madhya 15.134 ======

===== Text =====

> 'dāru'-jala'-rūpe kṛṣṇa prakaṭa samprati
> 'daraśana'-'snāne' kare jīvera mukati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāru]]---wood; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the forms of; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---manifested; [[synonyms:s:samprati]]---at the present moment; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---by seeing; [[synonyms:s:snāne]]---by bathing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]] [[synonyms:m:mukati]]---the deliverance of the conditioned souls.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "In this Age of Kali, Kṛṣṇa is manifest in two forms-wood and water. Thus He helps conditioned souls to become liberated by seeing the wood and bathing in the water.**
