====== CC Madhya 15.188 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe mora ghare bhikṣā karaha 'māsa' bhari'
> prabhu kahe,---dharma nahe, karite nā pāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at my place; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---lunch; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---accept; [[synonyms:m:māsa]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---for one month; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principle; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---it is not; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am unable.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Please accept my invitation for lunch for one month." The Lord replied, "That is not possible because it is against the religious principles of a sannyāsī."**
