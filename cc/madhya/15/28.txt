====== Cc. Madhya 15.28 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu-mūlya vastra prabhu-mastake bāndhila
> ācāryādi prabhura gaṇere parāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---very valuable; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---cloth; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:m:mastake]]---on the head of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāndhila]]---wrapped; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaṇere]]---on the associates; [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---put.

===== Translation =====

**This valuable cloth was wrapped around the head of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The other devotees, headed by Advaita Ācārya, also had cloths wrapped about their heads.**
