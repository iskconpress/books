====== Cc. Madhya 15.130 ======

===== Text =====

> mukundere kahe punaḥ madhura vacana
> 'tomāra kārya---dharme dhana-upārjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukundere]]---to Mukunda; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:m:madhura]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---sweet words; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---your duty; [[synonyms:d:dharme]] [[synonyms:d:dhana]]-[[synonyms:u:upārjana]]---to earn both material and spiritual wealth.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again spoke to Mukunda with sweet words: "Your duty is to earn both material and spiritual wealth.**
