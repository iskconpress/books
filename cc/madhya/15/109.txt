====== CC Madhya 15.109 ======

===== Text =====

> anuṣaṅga-phale kare saṁsārera kṣaya
> citta ākarṣiyā karāya kṛṣṇe premodaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anuṣaṅga]]-[[synonyms:p:phale]]---as a simultaneous result; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:saṁsārera]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---annihilation of entanglement in the material world; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---thought; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣiyā]]---attracting; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---causes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---arousal of transcendental love.

===== Translation =====

**"By chanting the holy name of the Lord, one dissolves his entanglement in material activities. After this, one becomes very attracted to Kṛṣṇa, and thus dormant love for Kṛṣṇa is awakened.**
