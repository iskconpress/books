====== CC Madhya 15.119 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇe kahe,---śuna mukundera prema
> nigūḍha nirmala prema, yena dagdha hema

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu informed His devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:m:mukunderaprema]]---Mukunda's love of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nigūḍha]]---very deep; [[synonyms:n:nirmala]]---pure; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:d:dagdha]]---clarified; [[synonyms:h:hema]]---gold.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then informed all His devotees, "Please hear about Mukunda's love of Godhead. It is a very deep and pure love and can only be compared with purified gold.**
