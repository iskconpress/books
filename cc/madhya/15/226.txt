====== CC Madhya 15.226 ======

===== Text =====

> śata culāya śata jana pāka yadi kare
> tabu śīghra eta dravya rāndhite nā pāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:c:culāya]]---in one hundred ovens; [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---one hundred men; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:y:yadi]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---if engaged in cooking; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---so soon; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---so many preparations; [[synonyms:r:rāndhite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---could not cook.

===== Translation =====

**"Even a hundred men cooking on a hundred ovens could not possibly finish all these preparations within so short a time.**
