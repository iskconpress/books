====== CC Madhya 15.211 ======

===== Text =====

> dugdha-tumbī, dugdha-kuṣmāṇḍa, vesara, lāphrā
> mocā-ghaṇṭa, mocā-bhājā, vividha śākrā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:t:tumbī]]---squash cooked with milk; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṣmāṇḍa]]---pumpkin cooked with milk; [[synonyms:v:vesara]]---a preparation made from chick-pea flour; [[synonyms:l:lāphrā]]---a combination of several vegetables; [[synonyms:m:mocā]]-[[synonyms:g:ghaṇṭa]]---boiled banana flowers; [[synonyms:m:mocā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhājā]]---fried banana flowers; [[synonyms:v:vividha]]---various; [[synonyms:ś:śākrā]]---vegetables.

===== Translation =====

**There were preparations of dugdha-tumbī, dugdha-kuṣmāṇḍa, vesara, lāphrā, mocā-ghaṇṭa, mocā-bhājā and other vegetables.**
