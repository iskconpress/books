====== Cc. Madhya 15.43 ======

===== Text =====

> rāma-dāsa, gadādhara ādi kata jane
> tomāra sahāya lāgi' dilu tomāra sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Rāmadāsa; [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara dāsa; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---some people; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]---assistants; [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---as; [[synonyms:d:dilu]]---I give; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with You.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu was given assistants like Rāmadāsa, Gadādhara dāsa and several others. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I give them to You to assist You.**
