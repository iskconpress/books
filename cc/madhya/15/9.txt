====== Cc. Madhya 15.9 ======

===== Text =====

> gale mālā dena, māthāya tulasī-mañjarī
> yoḍa-hāte stuti kare pade namaskari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gale]]---on the neck; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garland; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---offers; [[synonyms:m:māthāya]]---on the head; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]-[[synonyms:m:mañjarī]]---flowers of //tulasī//; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:s:stuti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offers prayers; [[synonyms:p:pade]]---unto the lotus feet; [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Prabhu would also place a flower garland around the Lord's neck and tulasī flowers [mañjarīs] on His head. Then, with folded hands, Advaita Ācārya would offer obeisances and prayers unto the Lord.**
