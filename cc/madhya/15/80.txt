====== CC Madhya 15.80 ======

===== Text =====

> avasara nāhi haya, vilamba ha-ila
> phala-pātra-hāte sevaka dvāre ta' rahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avasara]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was little time; [[synonyms:v:vilamba]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---it was late; [[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the pot of fruits; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hands; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servant; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained.

===== Translation =====

**"When the coconuts were brought, there was little time to offer them because it was already late. The servant, holding the container of coconuts, remained standing at the door.**
