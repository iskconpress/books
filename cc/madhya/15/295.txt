====== CC Madhya 15.295 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu gelā īśvara-daraśane
> bhaṭṭa snāna darśana kari' karilā bhojane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing his bathing and seeing of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---accepted food.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to see Lord Jagannātha. Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya completed his bath, went to see Lord Jagannātha and then returned to his house to accept food.**
