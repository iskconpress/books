====== Cc. Madhya 15.71 ======

===== Text =====

> vāṭite kata śata vṛkṣe lakṣa lakṣa phala
> tathāpi śunena yathā miṣṭa nārikela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāṭite]]---in his garden; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---how many hundreds of; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]]---trees; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]] [[synonyms:p:phala]]---millions of fruits; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:ś:śunena]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---where; [[synonyms:m:miṣṭa]] [[synonyms:n:nārikela]]---sweet coconut.

===== Translation =====

**"Although he already has hundreds of trees and millions of fruits, he is still very eager to hear about the place where sweet coconut is available.**
