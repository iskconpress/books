====== Cc. Madhya 15.296 ======

===== Text =====

> sei amogha haila prabhura bhakta 'ekānta'
> preme nāce, kṛṣṇa-nāma laya mahā-śānta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:a:amogha]]---that same Amogha; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:e:ekānta]]---unflinching; [[synonyms:p:preme]] [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances in ecstasy; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---chants the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śānta]]---very peaceful.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Amogha became an unalloyed devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He danced in ecstasy and peacefully chanted the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
