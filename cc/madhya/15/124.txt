====== CC Madhya 15.124 ======

===== Text =====

> rājāra jñāna,---rāja-vaidyera ha-ila maraṇa
> āpane nāmiyā tabe karāila cetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājāra]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---the King thought; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:v:vaidyera]]---of the royal physician; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:m:maraṇa]]---there was death; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:n:nāmiyā]]---getting down; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:k:karāila]] [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---brought him to consciousness.

===== Translation =====

**"The King, fearing that the royal physician was killed, personally descended and brought him to his consciousness.**
