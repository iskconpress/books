====== CC Madhya 15.57 ======

===== Text =====

> nimāñi nāhika ethā, ke kare bhojana
> mora dhyāne aśru-jale bharila nayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nimāñi]]---Nimāi; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---is not present here; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---who will eat them; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:d:dhyāne]]---on meditation upon Me; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---with tears; [[synonyms:b:bharila]] [[synonyms:n:nayana]]---eyes become filled.

===== Translation =====

**"My mother was thinking, 'Nimāi is not here. Who will accept all this food?' As she meditated upon Me in this way, her eyes filled with tears.**
