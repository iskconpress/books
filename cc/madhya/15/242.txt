====== Cc. Madhya 15.242 ======

===== Text =====

> govardhana-yajñe anna khāilā rāśi rāśi
> tāra lekhāya ei anna nahe eka grāsī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:y:yajñe]]---in the Govardhana-pūjā sacrifice; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:k:khāilā]]---You ate; [[synonyms:r:rāśi]] [[synonyms:r:rāśi]]---stacks; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---to that; [[synonyms:l:lekhāya]]---in comparison; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:g:grāsī]]---one morsel.

===== Translation =====

**"Indeed," Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya continued, "at the Govardhana-pūjā ceremony You ate stacks of rice. In comparison to that, this small quantity is not even a morsel for You.**
