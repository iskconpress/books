====== CC Madhya 15.253 ======

===== Text =====

> duṅhāra duḥkha dekhi' prabhu duṅhā prabodhiyā
> duṅhāra icchāte bhojana kaila tuṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]] [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the lamentation of both; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duṅhā]] [[synonyms:p:prabodhiyā]]---pacifying them; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]] [[synonyms:i:icchāte]]---by the will of both of them; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---took His lunch; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---with great satisfaction.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the lamentation of both husband and wife, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tried to pacify them. According to their desire, He ate the prasāda and was very satisfied.**
