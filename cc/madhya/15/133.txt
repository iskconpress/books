====== CC Madhya 15.133 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma, vidyā-vācaspati,---dui bhāi
> dui-jane kṛpā kari' kahena gosāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:v:vidyā]]-[[synonyms:v:vācaspati]]---Vidyā-vācaspati; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---two brothers; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---unto the two; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---out of His causeless mercy; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Out of His causeless mercy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave the following directions to the brothers Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Vidyā-vācaspati.**
