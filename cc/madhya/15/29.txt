====== CC Madhya 15.29 ======

===== Text =====

> kānāñi-khuṭiyā, jagannātha,---dui-jana
> āveśe bilāila ghare chila yata dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kānāñi]]-[[synonyms:k:khuṭiyā]]---Kānāñi Khuṭiyā; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Jagannātha Māhāti; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bilāila]]---distributed; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---was; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---riches.

===== Translation =====

**In ecstasy, Kānāñi Khuṭiyā, who was dressed as Nanda Mahārāja, and Jagannātha Māhāti, who was dressed as mother Yaśodā, distributed all the riches they had stocked at home.**
