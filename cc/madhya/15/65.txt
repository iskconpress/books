====== Cc. Madhya 15.65 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra preme āni' āmāya karāya bhojane
> antare mānaye sukha, bāhye nāhi māne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:preme]]---by her love; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:ā:āmāya]]---Me; [[synonyms:k:karāya]] [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---causes to eat; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within herself; [[synonyms:m:mānaye]]---she feels; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:b:bāhye]]---externally; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---does not accept.

===== Translation =====

**"Being obliged by her love, I am brought there to eat. Mother knows all these things internally and feels happiness, but externally she does not accept them.**
