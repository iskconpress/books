====== CC Madhya 15.166 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa sei satya kare, yei māge bhṛtya
> bhṛtya-vāñchā-pūrti vinu nāhi anya kṛtya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---fulfills as true; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---wants; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---servant; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]-[[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---the desire of His servant; [[synonyms:p:pūrti]]---fulfilling; [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---without; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---duty.

===== Translation =====

**"Whatever a pure devotee wants from his master, Lord Kṛṣṇa doubtlessly grants because He has no duty other than to fulfill the desire of His devotee.**
