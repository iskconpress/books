====== Cc. Madhya 15.205 ======

===== Text =====

> āra ghara mahāprabhura bhikṣāra lāgiyā
> nibhṛte kariyāche bhaṭṭa nūtana kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---the other room; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣāra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---for taking lunch; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]] [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]]---constructed in a solitary place; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:n:nūtana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---newly done.

===== Translation =====

**The other room was for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's lunch. The Lord's lunch room was very secluded, and it was newly constructed by Bhaṭṭācārya.**
