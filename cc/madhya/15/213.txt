====== CC Madhya 15.213 ======

===== Text =====

> nava-nimbapatra-saha bhṛṣṭa-vārtākī
> phula-baḍi paṭola-bhājā, kuṣmāṇḍa-māna-cākī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nava]]---newly grown; [[synonyms:n:nimba]]-[[synonyms:p:patra]]---nimba leaves; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---along with; [[synonyms:b:bhṛṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vārtākī]]---fried eggplant; [[synonyms:p:phula]]-[[synonyms:b:baḍī]]---light baḍī; [[synonyms:p:paṭola]]-[[synonyms:b:bhājā]]---fried //paṭola// vegetable; [[synonyms:k:kuṣmāṇḍa]]---of pumpkin; [[synonyms:m:māna]]---of squash; [[synonyms:c:cākī]]---rounds.

===== Translation =====

**Other preparations included eggplant mixed with newly grown nimba leaves fried together, light baḍī, fried paṭola, and fried rounds of squash and pumpkin.**
