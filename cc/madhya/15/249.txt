====== Cc. Madhya 15.249 ======

===== Text =====

> śunitei bhaṭṭācārya ulaṭi' cāhila
> tāṅra avadhāna dekhi' amogha palāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śunitei]]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:u:ulaṭi']] [[synonyms:c:cāhila]]---turned his eyes upon him; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:a:avadhāna]]---attention; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:a:amogha]]---Amogha; [[synonyms:p:palāila]]---left.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Amogha said this, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya turned his eyes upon him. Seeing Bhaṭṭācārya's attitude, Amogha immediately left.**
