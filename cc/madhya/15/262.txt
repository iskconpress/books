====== CC Madhya 15.262 ======

===== Text =====

> kimvā nija-prāṇa yadi kari vimocana
> dui yogya nahe, dui śarīra brāhmaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kimvā]]---or; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---my own life; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:v:vimocana]]---I give up; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---both such actions; [[synonyms:y:yogya]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---are not befitting; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]---both the bodies; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---//brāhmaṇas.//

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya continued, "Or, if I give up my own life, this sinful action may be atoned. However, neither of these ideas are befitting because both bodies belong to brāhmaṇas.**
