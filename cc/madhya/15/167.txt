====== CC Madhya 15.167 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmāṇḍa jīvera tumi vāñchile nistāra
> vinā pāpa-bhoge habe sabāra uddhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---of the universe; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of all living entities; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:v:vāñchile]]---if you desire; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhoge]]---undergoing tribulations of sinful activities; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---everyone's; [[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---liberation.

===== Translation =====

**"If you desire the deliverance of all living entities within the universe, then all of them can be delivered even without your undergoing the tribulations of sinful activity.**
