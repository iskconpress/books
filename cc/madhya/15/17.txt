====== Cc. Madhya 15.17 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-janma-yātrā-dine nanda-mahotsava
> gopa-veśa hailā prabhu lañā bhakta saba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---observance of the birth of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the day of; [[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:m:mahotsava]]---the festival observed by Nanda Mahārāja, the father of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---dressed Himself as a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees also celebrated the festival of Janmāṣṭamī, Kṛṣṇa's birthday, which is also called Nanda-mahotsava, the festival of Nanda Mahārāja. At that time Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees dressed themselves as cowherd boys.**
