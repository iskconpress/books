====== CC Madhya 15.25 ======

===== Text =====

> alāta-cakrera prāya laguḍa phirāya
> dekhi' sarva-loka-citte camatkāra pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alāta]]-[[synonyms:c:cakrera]]---the circle of a firebrand; [[synonyms:p:prāya]]---like; [[synonyms:l:laguḍa]] [[synonyms:p:phirāya]]---wheels the rod; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---became very astonished.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu whirled the rod in a circle like a firebrand, the heart of everyone who saw it was astonished.**
