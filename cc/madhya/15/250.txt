====== CC Madhya 15.250 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya lāṭhi lañā mārite dhāila
> palāila amogha, tāra lāga nā pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:l:lāṭhi]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking a stick; [[synonyms:m:mārite]]---to strike; [[synonyms:d:dhāila]]---ran; [[synonyms:p:palāila]]---fled; [[synonyms:a:amogha]]---Amogha; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---him; [[synonyms:l:lāga]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---could not catch.

===== Translation =====

**Bhaṭṭācārya ran after him to strike him with a stick, but Amogha fled so fast that Bhaṭṭācārya could not catch him.**
