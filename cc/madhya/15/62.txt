====== Cc. Madhya 15.62 ======

===== Text =====

> anna-vyañjana-pūrṇa dekhi' sakala bhājane
> dekhiyā saṁśaya haila kichu camatkāra mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---filled with rice and vegetables; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:b:bhājane]]---all the cooking pots; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was doubt; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonder; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**"When she saw that all the pots were still filled with rice and vegetables, there was some doubt in her mind, and she was astonished.**
