====== Cc. Madhya 15.82 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita kahe,---dvāre loka kare gatāyāte
> tāra pada-dhūli uḍi' lāge upara bhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita said; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---through the door; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:g:gatāyāte]]---coming and going; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---their; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:d:dhūli]]---dust of the feet; [[synonyms:u:uḍi']]---being blown; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---touches; [[synonyms:u:upara]]---upward; [[synonyms:b:bhite]]---the ceiling.

===== Translation =====

**"Rāghava Paṇḍita then said, 'People are always coming and going through that door. The dust from their feet blows up and touches the ceiling.**
