====== CC Madhya 15.171 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra icchā-mātre habe brahmāṇḍa-mocana
> sarva mukta karite kṛṣṇera nāhi kichu śrama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]-[[synonyms:m:mātre]]---simply by your desire; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:m:mocana]]---deliverance of the universe; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---everyone; [[synonyms:m:mukta]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to liberate; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---even a little; [[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---labor.

===== Translation =====

**"Because of your honest desire, all living entities within the universe will be delivered, for Kṛṣṇa does not have to do anything to deliver all the living entities of the universe.**
