====== CC Madhya 15.77 ======

===== Text =====

> śasya samarpaṇa kari' bāhire dheyāna
> śasya khāñā kṛṣṇa kare śūnya bhājana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śasya]]---the pulp; [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside the temple room; [[synonyms:d:dheyāna]]---meditates; [[synonyms:ś:śasya]] [[synonyms:k:khāñā]]---eating the pulp; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:ś:śūnya]]---vacant; [[synonyms:b:bhājana]]---the plate.

===== Translation =====

**"After offering the pulp, he would meditate outside the temple door. In the meantime, Lord Kṛṣṇa, having eaten the pulp, would leave the plate empty.**
