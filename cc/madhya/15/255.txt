====== CC Madhya 15.255 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvāṅge parāila prabhura mālya-candana
> daṇḍavat hañā bale sadainya vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---all over the body; [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---put; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---a flower garland and sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:dainya]]---humble; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---statement.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya then placed a flower garland over Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and smeared His body with sandalwood pulp. After offering obeisances, the Bhaṭṭācārya submitted the following humble statement.**
