====== CC Madhya 15.32 ======

===== Text =====

> vijayā-daśamī---laṅkā-vijayera dine
> vānara-sainya kailā prabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vijayā]]---victory; [[synonyms:d:daśamī]]---tenth day; [[synonyms:l:laṅkā]]-[[synonyms:v:vijayera]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the day celebrating the conquering of Laṅkā; [[synonyms:v:vānara]]-[[synonyms:s:sainya]]---monkey soldiers; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---arranged; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---taking all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**On the victory day celebrating the conquest of Laṅkā-a day known as Vijayā-daśamī-Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dressed up all His devotees like monkey soldiers.**
