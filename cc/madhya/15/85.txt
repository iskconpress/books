====== CC Madhya 15.85 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe āra nārikela saṁskāra karāila
> parama pavitra kari' bhoga lāgāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:n:nārikela]]---coconuts; [[synonyms:s:saṁskāra]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made clipped and cleaned; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:p:pavitra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great attention to keep them pure; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:l:lāgāila]]---offered for eating.

===== Translation =====

**"Thereafter, Rāghava Paṇḍita had other coconuts gathered, cleansed and clipped, and with great attention he offered them to the Deity to eat.**
