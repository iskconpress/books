====== CC Madhya 15.192 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu krame krame pāṅca-dina ghāṭāila
> pāṅca-dina tāṅra bhikṣā niyama karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:krame]] [[synonyms:k:krame]]---gradually; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---to five days; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭāila]]---reduced; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---for five days; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---invitation for lunch; [[synonyms:n:niyama]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---accepted regularly.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, by and by, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu reduced the duration to five days. Thus for five days He regularly accepted the invitation to lunch.**
