====== Cc. Madhya 15.19 ======

===== Text =====

> kānāñi-khuṭiyā āchena 'nanda'-veśa dhari'
> jagannātha-māhāti hañāchena 'vrajeśvarī'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kānāñi]]-[[synonyms:k:khuṭiyā]]---Kānāñi Khuṭiyā; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---is; [[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---in the dress of Nanda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:m:māhāti]]---Jagannātha Māhāti; [[synonyms:h:hañāchena]]---was; [[synonyms:v:vrajeśvarī]]---mother Yaśodā.

===== Translation =====

**Kānāñi Khuṭiyā dressed himself like Nanda Mahārāja, and Jagannātha Māhāti dressed himself as mother Yaśodā.**
