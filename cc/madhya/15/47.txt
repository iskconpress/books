====== CC Madhya 15.47 ======

===== Text =====

> ei vastra mātāke diha', ei saba prasāda
> daṇḍavat kari' āmāra kṣamāiha aparādha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eivastra]]---this cloth; [[synonyms:m:mātāke]] [[synonyms:d:diha']]---deliver to My mother, Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---all these remnants of the food of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:k:kṣamāiha]]---cause to be excused; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offenses.

===== Translation =====

**"Take this prasāda of Lord Jagannātha's and this cloth and deliver them to My mother, Śacīdevī. After offering her obeisances, please request her to excuse My offenses.**
