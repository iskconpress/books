====== CC Madhya 15.74 ======

===== Text =====

> bhogera samaya punaḥ chuli' saṁskari'
> kṛṣṇe samarpaṇa kare mukha chidra kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhogera]] [[synonyms:s:samaya]]---at the time of offering //bhoga//; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:c:chuli']]---clipping; [[synonyms:s:saṁskari']]---cleansing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---offering; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---at the top; [[synonyms:c:chidra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making a hole.

===== Translation =====

**"At the time of offering bhoga, the coconuts are again clipped and cleansed. After holes are made in them, they are offered to Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
