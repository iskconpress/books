====== CC Madhya 15.81 ======

===== Text =====

> dvārera upara bhite teṅho hāta dila
> sei hāte phala chuṅila, paṇḍita dekhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvārera]] [[synonyms:u:upara]]---above the door; [[synonyms:b:bhite]]---on the ceiling; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:h:hāta]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---brushed his hand; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---with that hand; [[synonyms:p:phala]] [[synonyms:c:chuṅila]]---touched the fruit; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw.

===== Translation =====

**"Rāghava Paṇḍita then saw that the servant touched the ceiling above the door and then touched the coconuts with the same hand.**
