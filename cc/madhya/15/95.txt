====== Cc. Madhya 15.95 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gṛhastha' hayena iṅho, cāhiye sañcaya
> sañcaya nā kaile kuṭumba-bharaṇa nāhi haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gṛhastha]]---householder; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---is; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---he (Vāsudeva Datta); [[synonyms:c:cāhiye]] [[synonyms:s:sañcaya]]---requires to save some money; [[synonyms:s:sañcaya]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---without saving money; [[synonyms:k:kuṭumba]]-[[synonyms:b:bharaṇa]]---maintenance of a family; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is not possible.

===== Translation =====

**"Being a householder, Vāsudeva Datta needs to save some money. Because he is not doing so, it is very difficult for him to maintain his family.**
