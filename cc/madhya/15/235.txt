====== Cc. Madhya 15.235 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭa kahe,---anna, pīṭha,---samāna prasāda
> anna khābe, pīṭhe vasite kāhāṅ aparādha?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:p:pīṭha]]---sitting place; [[synonyms:s:samāna]]---equal; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---mercy remnants of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:k:khābe]]---You will eat the food; [[synonyms:p:pīṭhe]] [[synonyms:v:vasite]]---to sit on the place; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---where is the offense.

===== Translation =====

**Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Both the food and the sitting place are the Lord's mercy. If You can eat the remnants of the food, what is the offense in Your sitting in this place?"**
