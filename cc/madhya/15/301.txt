====== CC Madhya 15.301 ======

===== Text =====

> śraddhā kari' ei līlā śune yei jana
> acirāt pāya sei caitanya-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with faith and love; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---this pastime; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---whoever; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---attains; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever hears these pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with faith and love will attain the shelter of the Lord's lotus feet very soon.**
