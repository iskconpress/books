====== Cc. Madhya 15.176 ======

===== Text =====

> tāte bhāse māyā lañā ananta brahmāṇḍa
> gaḍa-khāite bhāse yena rāi-pūrṇa bhāṇḍa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that water; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---floats; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]---the material energy; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---universes; [[synonyms:g:gaḍa]]-[[synonyms:k:khāite]]---in the surrounding water; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---floats; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:r:rāi]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍa]]---a pot filled with mustard seeds.

===== Translation =====

**"Māyā and her unlimited material universes are situated in that Causal Ocean. Indeed, māyā appears to be floating like a pot filled with mustard seeds.**
