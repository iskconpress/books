====== Cc. Madhya 15.38 ======

===== Text =====

> kibā yukti kaila duṅhe, keha nāhi jāne
> phale anumāna pāche kaila bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kibāyuktikaila]]---what consultation They had; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of Them; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---no one knows; [[synonyms:p:phale]]---by the result; [[synonyms:a:anumāna]]---guess; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**No one could understand what the brothers discussed between Themselves, but later all the devotees could guess what the subject matter was.**
