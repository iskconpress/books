====== CC Madhya 15.104 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena,---'kṛṣṇa-sevā', 'vaiṣṇava-sevana'
> 'nirantara kara kṛṣṇa-nāma-saṅkīrtana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahena]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---serving Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:s:sevana]]---abiding by the orders of Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---without cessation; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Without cessation continue chanting the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Whenever possible, serve Him and His devotees, the Vaiṣṇavas."**
