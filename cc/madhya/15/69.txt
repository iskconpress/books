====== Cc. Madhya 15.69 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāra kṛṣṇa-sevāra kathā śuna, sarva-jana
> parama-pavitra sevā ati sarvottama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevāra]]---of service to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---stories; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---all people; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:p:pavitra]]---supremely pure; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very much; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---well accomplished.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then informed everyone, "Just hear about the pure devotional service rendered to Kṛṣṇa by Rāghava Paṇḍita. Indeed, Rāghava Paṇḍita's service is supremely pure and highly accomplished.**
