~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 15.54-55~~

====== CC Madhya 15.54-55 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina śālyanna, vyañjana pāṅca-sāta
> śāka, mocā-ghaṇṭa, bhṛṣṭa-paṭola-nimba-pāta
>
> lembu-ādā-khaṇḍa, dadhi, dugdha, khaṇḍa-sāra
> śālagrāme samarpilena bahu upahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:ś:śāli]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---cooked rice made of //śāli// paddy; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five to seven different kinds; [[synonyms:ś:śāka]]---spinach; [[synonyms:m:mocā]]-[[synonyms:g:ghaṇṭa]]---curry made from banana flowers; [[synonyms:b:bhṛṣṭa]]---fried; [[synonyms:p:paṭola]]---//paṭola// vegetables; [[synonyms:n:nimba]]-[[synonyms:p:pāta]]---with leaves of the //nimba// tree; [[synonyms:l:lembu]]---lemon; [[synonyms:ā:ādā]]-[[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---pieces of ginger; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---milk; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---sugar candy; [[synonyms:ś:śālagrāme]]---unto Lord Viṣṇu in the form of the //śālagrāma//; [[synonyms:s:samarpilena]]---offered; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:u:upahāra]]---many other varieties of food.

===== Translation =====

**"One day My mother, Śacī, offered food to Śālagrāma Viṣṇu. She offered rice cooked from śāli paddies, various kinds of vegetables, spinach, curry made of banana flowers, fried paṭola with nimba leaves, pieces of ginger with lemon, and also yogurt, milk, sugar candy and many other foods.**
