====== Cc. Madhya 15.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaita-candra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---unto Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---all glories to Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---to the devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu! All glories to Advaitacandra! And all glories to all the devotees of Lord Caitanya!**
