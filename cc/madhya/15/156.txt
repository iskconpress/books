====== Cc. Madhya 15.156 ======

===== Text =====

> sākṣāt hanumān tumi śrī-rāma-kiṅkara
> tumi kene chāḍibe tāṅra caraṇa-kamala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:h:hanumān]]---Hanumān; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---the servant of Śrī Rāma; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:c:chāḍibe]]---should give up; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kamala]]---lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way, I congratulated Murāri Gupta, saying, 'Indeed, you are the incarnation of Hanumān. Consequently you are the eternal servant of Lord Rāmacandra. Why should you give up the worship of Lord Rāmacandra and His lotus feet?' "**
