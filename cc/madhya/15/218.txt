====== CC Madhya 15.218 ======

===== Text =====

> rasālā-mathita dadhi, sandeśa apāra
> gauḍe utkale yata bhakṣyera prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rasālā]]---delicious; [[synonyms:m:mathita]]---churned; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---curd; [[synonyms:s:sandeśa]]---a sweetmeat; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:u:utkale]]---in Orissa; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣyera]]---of eatables; [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---kinds.

===== Translation =====

**Other preparations included a very delicious churned curd and a variety of sandeśa sweetmeats. Indeed, all the various eatables available in Bengal and Orissa were prepared.**
