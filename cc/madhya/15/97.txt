====== CC Madhya 15.97 ======

===== Text =====

> prati-varṣe āmāra saba bhakta-gaṇa lañā
> guṇḍicāya āsibe sabāya pālana kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣe]]---each and every year; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by the devotees; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicāya]]---to perform the Guṇḍicā cleansing festival; [[synonyms:ā:āsibe]]---you will come; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---to everyone; [[synonyms:p:pālana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---providing maintenance.

===== Translation =====

**"Come every year and bring all My devotees with you to the Guṇḍicā festival. I also request you to maintain all of them."**
