====== CC Madhya 15.161 ======

===== Text =====

> karite samartha tumi hao, dayāmaya
> tumi mana kara, tabe anāyāse haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karite]]---to execute; [[synonyms:s:samartha]]---capable; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:h:hao]]---are; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---O merciful one; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---if You so desire; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:a:anāyāse]]---without difficulty; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it becomes possible.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord, You are certainly capable of doing whatever You like, and You are indeed merciful. If You so desire, You can very easily do whatever You want.**
