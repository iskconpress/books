====== CC Madhya 15.51 ======

===== Text =====

> ki kāya sannyāse mora, prema nija-dhana
> ye-kāle sannyāsa kailuṅ, channa haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kāya]]---what business; [[synonyms:s:sannyāse]]---in the renounced order; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---of Me; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---My real wealth; [[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at which time; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I accepted the renounced order; [[synonyms:c:channa]]---deranged; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"I had no business in accepting this renounced order and sacrificing My love for My mother, which is My real property. Actually I was in a crazy state of mind when I accepted sannyāsa.**
