====== CC Madhya 15.286 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu dekhi' sārvabhauma dharilā caraṇe
> prabhu tāṅre āliṅgiyā vasilā āsane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---caught hold of His feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsane]]---sat down on the seat.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing the Lord, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya immediately caught hold of His lotus feet. The Lord also embraced him and sat down.**
