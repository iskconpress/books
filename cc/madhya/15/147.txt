====== Cc. Madhya 15.147 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata sarva-rātri karena krandana
> mane soyāsti nāhi, rātri kaila jāgaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:r:rātri]]---the whole night; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---cried; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:soyāsti]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is no rest; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---the whole night; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---kept; [[synonyms:j:jāgaraṇa]]---awake.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus Murāri Gupta cried the entire night. There was no rest for his mind; therefore he could not sleep but stayed awake the entire night.**
