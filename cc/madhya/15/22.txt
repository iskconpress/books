====== CC Madhya 15.22 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita kahe,---satya kahi, nā kariha kopa
> laguḍa phirāite pāra, tabe jāni gopa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Advaita Ācārya says; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak the truth; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:k:kopa]]---please do not be angry; [[synonyms:l:laguḍa]]---stick, staff; [[synonyms:p:phirāite]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---if You can wheel around; [[synonyms:t:tabe]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---then I shall understand; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]---cowherd boy.

===== Translation =====

**It was at this time that Śrīla Advaita Ācārya said, "Please do not be angry. I speak the truth. I shall know whether You are a cowherd boy only if You can wheel this rod about."**
