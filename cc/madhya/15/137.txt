====== CC Madhya 15.137 ======

===== Text =====

> murāri-guptere prabhu kari' āliṅgana
> tāṅra bhakti-niṣṭhā kahena, śune bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:murāri]]-[[synonyms:g:guptere]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:n:niṣṭhā]]---faith in devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śune]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees hear.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Murāri Gupta and began to speak about his firm faith in devotional service. This was heard by all the devotees.**
