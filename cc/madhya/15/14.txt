====== Cc. Madhya 15.14 ======

===== Text =====

> punarukti haya, tāhā nā kailuṅ varṇana
> āra bhakta-gaṇa kare prabhure nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]-[[synonyms:u:ukti]]---reputation; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---other devotees; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**Since Advaita Ācārya's invitation has been described by Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura, I shall not repeat the story. However, I shall say that other devotees also extended invitations to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
