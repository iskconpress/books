====== CC Madhya 15.26 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata nityānanda phirāya laguḍa
> ke bujhibe tāṅhā duṅhāra gopa-bhāva gūḍha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:phirāya]] [[synonyms:l:laguḍa]]---wheels a rod; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:b:bujhibe]]---will understand; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of Them; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the ecstasy of the cowherd boys; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]---very deep.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu also played at whirling the rod. Who can understand how They were ecstatically immersed in the deep emotions of the cowherd boys?**
