====== CC Madhya 15.105 ======

===== Text =====

> satyarāja bale,---vaiṣṇava ciniba kemane?
> ke vaiṣṇava, kaha tāṅra sāmānya lakṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:satyarāja]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---Satyarāja Khān said; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---a Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:c:ciniba]] [[synonyms:k:kemane]]---how shall I recognize; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---who is a Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please say; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him; [[synonyms:s:sāmānya]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇe]]---common symptoms.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this, Satyarāja said, "How can I recognize a Vaiṣṇava? Please let me know what a Vaiṣṇava is. What are his common symptoms?"**
