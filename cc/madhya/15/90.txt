====== CC Madhya 15.90 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśamdi, ācāra ādi aneka prakāra
> gandha, vastra, alaṅkāra, sarva dravya-sāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśamdi]]---a kind of pickle; [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---other pickles; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and so on; [[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:p:pra]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---of many varieties; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---scents; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---clothing; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---ornaments; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---of things; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---best.

===== Translation =====

**"Rāghava Paṇḍita would also offer all kinds of pickles, such as kāśamdi. He offered various scents, garments, ornaments and the best of everything.**
