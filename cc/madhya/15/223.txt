====== CC Madhya 15.223 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya kaila tabe pāda prakṣālana
> gharera bhitare gelā karite bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:pāda]] [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing the feet; [[synonyms:g:gharera]] [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within the room; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---entered; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---to take lunch.

===== Translation =====

**After Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya washed the Lord's feet, the Lord entered the room to take His lunch.**
