====== CC Madhya 15.98 ======

===== Text =====

> kulīna-grāmīre kahe sammāna kariyā
> pratyabda āsibe yātrāya paṭṭa-ḍorī lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmīre]]---unto the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great respect; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:a:abda]]---each year; [[synonyms:ā:āsibe]]---please come; [[synonyms:y:yātrāya]]---during the Ratha-yātrā festival; [[synonyms:p:paṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍorī]]---silken rope; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then with great respect extended an invitation to all the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma, asking them to come every year and bring silken rope to carry Lord Jagannātha during the Ratha-yātrā festival.**
