====== CC Madhya 15.200 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ṣāṭhīra mātā' nāma, bhaṭṭācāryera gṛhiṇī
> prabhura mahā-bhakta teṅho, snehete jananī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭhīra]] [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of Ṣāṭhī; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]] [[synonyms:g:gṛhiṇī]]---the wife of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---a great devotee; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---she; [[synonyms:s:snehete]]---in affection; [[synonyms:j:jananī]]---just like a mother.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya's wife was known as the mother of Ṣāṭhī. She was a great devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and she was affectionate like a mother.**
