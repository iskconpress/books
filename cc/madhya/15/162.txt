====== CC Madhya 15.162 ======

===== Text =====

> jīvera duḥkha dekhi' mora hṛdaya bidare
> sarva-jīvera pāpa prabhu deha' mora śire

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of all conditioned souls; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing the sufferings; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---heart; [[synonyms:b:bidare]]---breaks; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of all living entities; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]---the sinful reactions; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---My dear Lord; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---just put; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:ś:śire]]---upon my head.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord, my heart breaks to see the sufferings of all conditioned souls; therefore I request You to transfer the karma of their sinful lives upon my head.**
