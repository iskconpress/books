====== CC Madhya 15.297 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche citra-līlā kare śacīra nandana
> yei dekhe, śune, tāṅra vismaya haya mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in this way; [[synonyms:c:citra]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---varieties of pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---anyone who sees; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---astonished; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His various pastimes. Whoever sees them or hears of them becomes truly astonished.**
