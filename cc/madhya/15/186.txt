====== CC Madhya 15.186 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-pāśa āsi' sārvabhauma eka dina
> yoḍa-hāta kari' kichu kaila nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]]-[[synonyms:h:hāta]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**One day Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya came before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with folded hands and submitted a request.**
