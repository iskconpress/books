====== CC Madhya 15.240 ======

===== Text =====

> dvārakāte ṣola-sahasra mahiṣī-mandire
> aṣṭādaśa mātā, āra yādavera ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvārakāte]]---at Dvārakā-dhāma; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣola]]-[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---sixteen thousand; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣī]]---queens; [[synonyms:m:mandire]]---palaces; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭādaśa]] [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---eighteen mothers; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yādavera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house of the Yadu dynasty.

===== Translation =====

**"At Dvārakā, You keep sixteen thousand queens in sixteen thousand palaces. Also, there are eighteen mothers and numerous friends and relatives of the Yadu dynasty.**
