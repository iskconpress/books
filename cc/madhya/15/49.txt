====== Cc. Madhya 15.49 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra prema-vaśa āmi, tāṅra sevā---dharma
> tāhā chāḍi' kariyāchi vātulera karma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---subordinate to her love; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---her service; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---My religion; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up that; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchi]]---I performed; [[synonyms:v:vātulera]] [[synonyms:k:karma]]---the acts of a madman.

===== Translation =====

**"I am subordinate to the love of My mother, and it is My duty to serve her in return. Instead of doing so, I have accepted this renounced order. Certainly this is the act of a madman.**
