====== CC Madhya 15.164 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' mahāprabhura citta dravilā
> aśru-kampa-svarabhaṅge kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---heart; [[synonyms:d:dravilā]]---became softened; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:s:svara]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅge]]---with faltering of the voice; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard Vāsudeva Datta's statement, His heart became very soft. Tears flowed from His eyes, and He began to tremble. In a faltering voice He spoke as follows.**
