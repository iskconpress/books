====== Cc. Madhya 15.285 ======

===== Text =====

> aparādha' nāhi, sadā lao kṛṣṇa-nāma
> eta bali' prabhu āilā sārvabhauma-sthāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparādha']] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---do not commit offenses; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:l:lao]]---chant; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---to the place of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.

===== Translation =====

**"Amogha, always chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra and do not commit any further offenses." After giving Amogha this instruction, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to Sārvabhauma's house."**
