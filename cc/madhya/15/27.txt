====== CC Madhya 15.27 ======

===== Text =====

> pratāparudrera ājñāya paḍichā-tulasī
> jagannāthera prasāda-vastra eka lañā āsi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratāparudrera]]---of King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---on the order; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]-[[synonyms:t:tulasī]]---the temple superintendent named Tulasī; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:v:vastra]]---used cloth; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āsi]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**Following the orders of Mahārāja Pratāparudra, the temple superintendent named Tulasī brought one of Lord Jagannātha's used cloths.**
