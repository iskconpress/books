====== CC Madhya 15.145 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' ghare gela, cinti' rātri-kāle
> raghunātha-tyāga-cintāya ha-ila vikale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went to his house; [[synonyms:c:cinti']]---thinking; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at night; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:t:tyāga]]---giving up; [[synonyms:c:cintāya]]---by thoughts of; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:v:vikale]]---became overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**"After this, Murāri Gupta went home and spent the whole night thinking how he would be able to give up the association of Raghunātha, Lord Rāmacandra. Thus he was overwhelmed.**
