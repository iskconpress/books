====== CC Madhya 15.87 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu-mūlya diyā āni' kariyā yatana
> pavitra saṁskāra kari' kare nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---high price; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:y:yatana]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:p:pavitra]]---purified; [[synonyms:s:saṁskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---trimming; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---offers to the Deity.

===== Translation =====

**"All these fruits were collected from distant places and were bought at a high price. After trimming them with great care and purity, Rāghava Paṇḍita offered them to the Deity.**
