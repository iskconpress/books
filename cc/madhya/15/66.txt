====== CC Madhya 15.66 ======

===== Text =====

> ei vijayā-daśamīte haila ei rīti
> tāṅhāke puchiyā tāṅra karāiha pratīti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:v:vijayā]]-[[synonyms:d:daśamīte]]---on the previous Vijayā-daśamī day; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:r:rīti]]---such an incident; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāke]]---unto her; [[synonyms:p:puchiyā]]---asking; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---her; [[synonyms:k:karāiha]]---make; [[synonyms:p:pratīti]]---belief.

===== Translation =====

**"Such an incident took place on the last Vijayā-daśamī day. You can ask her about this incident and thus make her believe that I actually go there."**
