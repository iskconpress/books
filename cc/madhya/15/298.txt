====== CC Madhya 15.298 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche bhaṭṭa-gṛhe kare bhojana-vilāsa
> tāra madhye nānā citra-caritra-prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastime of eating; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that pastime; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:c:citra]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---of varieties of activities; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu enjoyed eating in Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya's house. Within that one pastime, many wonderful pastimes were manifest.**
