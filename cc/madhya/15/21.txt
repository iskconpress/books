====== Cc. Madhya 15.21 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhā-sabā lañā prabhu kare nṛtya-raṅga
> dadhi-dugdha haridrā-jale bhare sabāra aṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]]---performed dancing in jubilation; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---milk; [[synonyms:h:haridrā]]---turmeric; [[synonyms:j:jale]]---with water; [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---covered; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---bodies.

===== Translation =====

**As usual, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced jubilantly. Everyone was covered with milk, yogurt and yellow turmeric water.**
