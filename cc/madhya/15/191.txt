====== Cc. Madhya 15.191 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sārvabhauma prabhura caraṇe dhariyā
> 'daśa-dina bhikṣā kara' kahe vinati kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching the lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---for ten days; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---accept lunch; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:v:vinati]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great submission.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then caught hold of the Lord's lotus feet and submissively begged, "Please accept lunch for at least ten days."**
