====== CC Madhya 15.139 ======

===== Text =====

> svayaṁ bhagavān kṛṣṇa---sarvāṁśī, sarvāśraya
> viśuddha-nirmala-prema, sarva-rasamaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśī]]---the source of all others; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the reservoir of all energies; [[synonyms:v:viśuddha]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:n:nirmala]]---free from all material contamination; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---the reservoir of all pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the origin of all incarnations and the source of everything. He is pure transcendental love itself, and He is the reservoir of all pleasure.**
