====== CC Madhya 15.276 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma-saṅge tomāra 'kaluṣa' haila kṣaya
> 'kalmaṣa' ghucile jīva 'kṛṣṇa-nāma' laya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---by the association of Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kaluṣa]]---contamination; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---is now vanquished; [[synonyms:k:kalmaṣa]]---contamination; [[synonyms:g:ghucile]]---when dispelled; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---the living entity; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---can chant.

===== Translation =====

**"However, due to the association of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, all your contamination is now vanquished. When a person's heart is cleansed of all contamination, he is able to chant the mahā-mantra, Hare Kṛṣṇa.**
