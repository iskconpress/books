====== CC Madhya 15.37 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina mahāprabhu nityānande lañā
> dui bhāi yukti kaila nibhṛte vasiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānandelañā]]---taking Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---two brothers; [[synonyms:y:yukti]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---consulted; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]] [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting in a solitary place.

===== Translation =====

**One day the two brothers, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu, consulted one another, sitting together in a solitary place.**
