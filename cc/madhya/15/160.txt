====== CC Madhya 15.160 ======

===== Text =====

> jagat tārite prabhu tomāra avatāra
> mora nivedana eka karaha aṅgīkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagat]] [[synonyms:t:tārite]]---to deliver the whole world; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---petition; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---please accept.

===== Translation =====

**Vāsudeva Datta told Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "My dear Lord, You incarnate just to deliver all conditioned souls. I have now one petition, which I wish You would accept.**
