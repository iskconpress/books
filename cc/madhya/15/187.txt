====== CC Madhya 15.187 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe saba vaiṣṇava gauḍa-deśe cali' gela
> ebe prabhura nimantraṇe avasara haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:c:cali,]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---have returned; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇe]]---for invitations; [[synonyms:a:avasara]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there is a chance.

===== Translation =====

**Since all the Vaiṣṇavas had returned to Bengal, there was a good chance that the Lord would accept an invitation.**
