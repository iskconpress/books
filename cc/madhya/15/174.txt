====== Cc. Madhya 15.174 ======

===== Text =====

> taiche eka brahmāṇḍa yadi mukta haya
> tabu alpa-hāni kṛṣṇera mane nāhi laya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---one universe; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:m:mukta]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes liberated; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]-[[synonyms:h:hāni]]---very little loss; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---the mind; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---does not take it very seriously.

===== Translation =====

**"In the same way, if one universe is vacated due to the living entities' having been liberated, that is a very little thing for Kṛṣṇa. He does not take it very seriously.**
