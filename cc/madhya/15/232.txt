====== Cc. Madhya 15.232 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya bale---prabhu nā karaha vismaya
> yei khābe, tāṅhāra śaktye bhoga siddha haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---do not become astonished; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:k:khābe]]---whoever shall eat; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---by His grace; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---the food; [[synonyms:s:siddha]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---has been prepared.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "It is not so wonderful, my Lord. Everything has been made possible by the energy and mercy of He who will eat the food.**
