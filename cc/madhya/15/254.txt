====== CC Madhya 15.254 ======

===== Text =====

> ācamana karāñā bhaṭṭa dila mukha-vāsa
> tulasī-mañjarī, lavaṅga, elāci rasa-vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācamana]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---washing the mouth, hands and legs of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:dila]] [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---gave some flavored spices; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]-[[synonyms:m:mañjarī]]---the flowers of //tulasī//; [[synonyms:l:lavaṅga]]---cloves; [[synonyms:e:elāci]]---cardamom; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---that which brings saliva.

===== Translation =====

**After Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu finished eating, Bhaṭṭācārya washed His mouth, hands and legs and offered Him flavored spices, tulasī-mañjarī, cloves, and cardamom.**
