====== CC Madhya 15.282 ======

===== Text =====

> caḍāite caḍāite gāla phulāila
> hāte dhari' gopīnāthācārya niṣedhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caḍāite]] [[synonyms:c:caḍāite]]---slapping over and over again; [[synonyms:g:gāla]]---the cheeks; [[synonyms:p:phulāila]]---he made them swollen; [[synonyms:h:hāte]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching his hands; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhila]]---forbade.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, Amogha continued slapping his face over and over until his cheeks were swollen. Finally Gopīnātha Ācārya stopped him by catching hold of his hands.**
