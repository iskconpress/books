====== CC Madhya 15.7 ======

===== Text =====

> ghare vasi' kare prabhu nāma saṅkīrtana
> advaita āsiyā kare prabhura pūjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting in His room; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting on beads; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:pūjana]]---worship of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Sitting in His room, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would chant on His beads, and Advaita Prabhu would come there to worship the Lord.**
