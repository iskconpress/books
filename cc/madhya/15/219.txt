====== CC Madhya 15.219 ======

===== Text =====

> śraddhā kari' bhaṭṭācārya saba karāila
> śubhra-pīṭhopari sūkṣma vasana pātila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great respect; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---had them all prepared; [[synonyms:ś:śubhra]]---white; [[synonyms:p:pīṭha]]---a wooden platform; [[synonyms:u:upari]]---over; [[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---fine; [[synonyms:v:vasana]]---cloth; [[synonyms:p:pātila]]---spread.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Bhaṭṭācārya prepared a great variety of food and spread a fine cloth over a white wooden platform.**
