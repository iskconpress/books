====== CC Madhya 15.201 ======

===== Text =====

> ghare āsi' bhaṭṭācārya tāṅre ājñā dila
> ānande ṣāṭhīra mātā pāka caḍāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming home; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---her; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---ordered; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---with great satisfaction; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭhīra]] [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of Ṣāṭhī; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:c:caḍāila]]---began cooking.

===== Translation =====

**After returning to his home, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya gave orders to his wife, and his wife, known as Ṣāṭhīra Mātā, the mother of Ṣāṭhī, began cooking with great pleasure.**
