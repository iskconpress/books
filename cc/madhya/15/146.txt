====== CC Madhya 15.146 ======

===== Text =====

> kemane chāḍiba raghunāthera caraṇa
> āji rātrye prabhu mora karāha maraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kemanechāḍiba]]---how shall I give up; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Lord Raghunātha; [[synonyms:ā:āji]] [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---this night; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---O Lord Raghunātha; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:k:karāha]] [[synonyms:m:maraṇa]]---please cause death.

===== Translation =====

**"Murāri Gupta then began to pray at the lotus feet of Lord Rāmacandra. He prayed that death would come that night because it was not possible for him to give up the service of the lotus feet of Raghunātha.**
