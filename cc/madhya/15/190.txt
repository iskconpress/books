====== Cc. Madhya 15.190 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe punaḥ,---dina 'pañca-daśa'
> prabhu kahe,---tomāra bhikṣā 'eka' divasa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:d:daśa]]---fifteen days; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---lunch at your place; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:divasa]]---only one day.

===== Translation =====

**When Sārvabhauma requested Caitanya Mahāprabhu to accept lunch for fifteen days, the Lord said, "I shall accept lunch at your place for one day only."**
