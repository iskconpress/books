====== Cc. Madhya 15.260 ======

===== Text =====

> ghare āsi' bhaṭṭācārya ṣāṭhīra mātā-sane
> āpanā nindiyā kichu balena vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---returning home; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭhīra]] [[synonyms:m:mātā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with the mother of Ṣāṭhī; [[synonyms:ā:āpanā]] [[synonyms:n:nindiyā]]---condemning himself; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:b:balena]] [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---speaks words.

===== Translation =====

**After returning to his home, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya consulted with his wife, the mother of Ṣāṭhī. After personally condemning himself, he began to speak as follows.**
