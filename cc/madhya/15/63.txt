====== CC Madhya 15.63 ======

===== Text =====

> īśāne bolāñā punaḥ sthāna lepāila
> punarapi gopālake anna samarpila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśāne]]---to Īśāna, the servant; [[synonyms:b:bolāñā]]---calling; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---the place; [[synonyms:l:lepāila]]---cleaned; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:g:gopālake]]---unto Gopāla; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---cooked rice and vegetables; [[synonyms:s:samarpila]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus wondering, she called Īśāna, the servant, and had the place cleaned again. She then offered another plate to Gopāla.**
