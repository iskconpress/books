====== CC Madhya 15.8 ======

===== Text =====

> sugandhi-salile dena pādya, ācamana
> sarvāṅge lepaye prabhura sugandhi candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:g:gandhi]]-[[synonyms:s:salile]]---with scented water; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---offers; [[synonyms:p:pādya]]---water for washing the feet; [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---washing the mouth; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---all over the body; [[synonyms:l:lepaye]]---smears; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:g:gandhi]] [[synonyms:c:candana]]---fragrant sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**While worshiping Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Advaita Ācārya would offer Him scented water to wash His mouth and feet. Then Advaita Ācārya would smear very fragrant sandalwood pulp all over His body.**
