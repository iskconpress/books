====== CC Madhya 15.92 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' rāghavere kaila āliṅgane
> ei-mata sammānila sarva bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:r:rāghavere]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgane]]---He embraced; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sammānila]]---showed respect; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---to the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then mercifully embraced Rāghava Paṇḍita. The Lord also offered all the other devotees a reception with similar respect.**
