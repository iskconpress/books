====== CC Madhya 15.229 ======

===== Text =====

> annera saurabhya, varṇa---ati manorama
> rādhā-kṛṣṇa sākṣāt ihāṅ kariyāchena bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:annera]] [[synonyms:s:saurabhya]]---the flavor of the cooked rice; [[synonyms:v:varṇa]]---color; [[synonyms:a:ati]] [[synonyms:m:manorama]]---very attractive; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa and Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---all this; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---have eaten.

===== Translation =====

**"The color of the rice is so attractive and its aroma so good that it appears Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa have directly taken it.**
