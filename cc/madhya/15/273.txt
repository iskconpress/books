====== CC Madhya 15.273 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' kṛpāmaya prabhu āilā dhāñā
> amoghere kahe tāra buke hasta diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running; [[synonyms:a:amoghere]]---unto Amogha; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---He says; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---His; [[synonyms:b:buke]]---on the chest; [[synonyms:h:hasta]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---keeping His hand.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard that Amogha was going to die, He immediately ran to him in great haste. Placing His hand on Amogha's chest, He spoke as follows.**
