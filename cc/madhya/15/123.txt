====== Cc. Madhya 15.123 ======

===== Text =====

> śikhi-piccha dekhi' mukunda premāviṣṭa hailā
> ati-ucca ṭuṅgi haite bhūmite paḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śikhi]]-[[synonyms:p:piccha]]---peacock feathers; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda dāsa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became ecstatic in love of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:u:ucca]]---very high; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭuṅgi]]---platform; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down.

===== Translation =====

**"Just by seeing the peacock-feathered fan, Mukunda dāsa became absorbed in ecstatic love of Godhead and fell from the high platform onto the ground.**
