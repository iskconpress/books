====== CC Madhya 15.170 ======

===== Text =====

> yas tv indra-gopam athavendram aho sva-karma-
> bandhānurūpa-phala-bhājanam ātanoti
> karmāṇi nirdahati kintu ca bhakti-bhājāṁ
> govindam ādi-puruṣaṁ tam ahaṁ bhajāmi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---He who (Govinda); [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:i:indra]]-[[synonyms:g:gopam]]---to the small red insect called //indra-gopa//; [[synonyms:a:athavā]]---or even; [[synonyms:i:indram]]---to Indra, King of heaven; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---oh; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]---of one's own fruitive activities; [[synonyms:b:bandha]]---bondage; [[synonyms:a:anurūpa]]---according to; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---of reactions; [[synonyms:b:bhājanam]]---enjoying or suffering; [[synonyms:ā:ātanoti]]---bestows; [[synonyms:k:karmāṇi]]---all fruitive activities and their reactions; [[synonyms:n:nirdahati]]---destroys; [[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---certainly; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:b:bhājām]]---of persons engaged in devotional service; [[synonyms:g:govindam]]---unto Lord Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣam]]---the original person; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhajāmi]]---offer my obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto the original Personality of Godhead, Govinda, who regulates the sufferings and enjoyments of fruitive activity for everyone-from the heavenly King Indra down to the smallest insect [indra-gopa]. That very Personality of Godhead destroys the fruitive karma of one engaged in devotional service.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Brahma-saṁhitā// (5.54).
