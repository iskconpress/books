====== CC Madhya 15.117 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' harṣe kahe prabhu---"kahile niścaya
> yāṅhā haite kṛṣṇa-bhakti sei guru haya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:h:harṣe]]---in great jubilation; [[synonyms:k:kahe]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:kahile]] [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---you have spoken correctly; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from whom; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:g:guru]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is the spiritual master.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing Mukunda dāsa give this proper decision, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu confirmed it, saying, "Yes, it is correct. One who awakens devotion to Kṛṣṇa is certainly a spiritual master."**
