====== CC Madhya 15.230 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra bahuta bhāgya kata praśaṁsiba
> āmi---bhāgyavān, ihāra avaśeṣa pāba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---great; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---fortune; [[synonyms:k:kata]]---how much; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsiba]]---shall I praise; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---fortunate; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]]---remnants; [[synonyms:p:pāba]]---shall get.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Bhaṭṭācārya, your fortune is very great. How much shall I praise you? I also am very fortunate to be able to take the remnants of this food.**
