====== CC Madhya 15.84 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' phala phele prācīra laṅghiyā
> aiche pavitra prema-sevā jagat jiniyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:phala]] [[synonyms:p:phele]]---throws away the fruits; [[synonyms:p:prācīra]] [[synonyms:l:laṅghiyā]]---across the boundary wall; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:p:pavitra]]---pure; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service in love; [[synonyms:j:jagat]] [[synonyms:j:jiniyā]]---conquering all the world.

===== Translation =====

**"Such was the service of Rāghava Paṇḍita. He did not accept the coconuts but threw them over the wall. His service is purely based on unalloyed love, and it conquers the whole world.**
