====== CC Madhya 15.112 ======

===== Text =====

> khaṇḍera mukunda-dāsa, śrī-raghunandana
> śrī-narahari,---ei mukhya tina jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:khaṇḍera]]---of the place known as Khaṇḍa; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Mukunda dāsa; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raghunandana]]---Śrī Raghunandana; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:narahari]]---Śrī Narahari; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---chief; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---persons.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then turned His attention to three persons-Mukunda dāsa, Raghunandana and Śrī Narahari-inhabitants of the place called Khaṇḍa.**
