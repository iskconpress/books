====== Cc. Madhya 15.103 ======

===== Text =====

> gṛhastha viṣayī āmi, ki mora sādhane
> śrī-mukhe ājñā kara prabhu---nivedi caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gṛhastha]]---householder; [[synonyms:v:viṣayī]]---materialistic man; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:sādhane]]---the process of my advancement in spiritual life; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from Your own mouth; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---please order; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:n:nivedi]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---I submit unto Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Satyarāja Khān said, "My dear Lord, being a householder and a materialistic man, I do not know the process of advancing in spiritual life. I therefore submit myself unto Your lotus feet and request You to give me orders."**
