====== Cc. Madhya 15.183 ======

===== Text =====

> gadādhara-paṇḍita rahilā prabhura pāśe
> yameśvare prabhu yāṅre karāilā āvāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---along with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yameśvare]]---at Yameśvara; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---unto whom; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---made to take; [[synonyms:ā:āvāse]]---residence.

===== Translation =====

**Gadādhara Paṇḍita remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and he was given a place to live at Yameśvara.**

===== Purport =====

Yameśvara is on the southwest side of the Jagannātha temple. Gadādhara Paṇḍita resided there, and there was a small garden and a sandy beach known as Yameśvara-ṭoṭā.
