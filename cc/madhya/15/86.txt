====== CC Madhya 15.86 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata kalā, āmra, nāraṅga, kāṅṭhāla
> yāhā yāhā dūra-grāme śuniyāche bhāla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]---bananas; [[synonyms:ā:āmra]]---mangoes; [[synonyms:n:nāraṅga]]---oranges; [[synonyms:k:kāṅṭhāla]]---jackfruit; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---whatever; [[synonyms:d:dūra]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in distant villages; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyāche]]---he heard; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---good.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way he collected excellent bananas, mangoes, oranges, jackfruits and whatever first-class fruits from distant villages he had heard about.**
