====== CC Madhya 15.121 ======

===== Text =====

> eka dina mleccha-rājāra ucca-ṭuṅgite
> cikitsāra vāt kahe tāṅhāra agrete

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekadina]]---one day; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:r:rājāra]]---of the Mohammedan King; [[synonyms:u:ucca]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭuṅgite]]---on a high platform; [[synonyms:c:cikitsāra]] [[synonyms:v:vāt]]---talk of medical treatment; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---was speaking; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:a:agrete]]---before him.

===== Translation =====

**"One day Mukunda dāsa, the royal physician, was seated with the Mohammedan King on a high platform and was telling the King about medical treatment.**
