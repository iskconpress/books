====== CC Madhya 15.16 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-māsa rahilā sabe mahāprabhu-saṅge
> jagannāthera nānā yātrā dekhe mahā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---four months; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remain; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---many festivals; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---they see; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---with great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees remained at Jagannātha Purī for four continuous months, and they observed all Lord Jagannātha's festivals with great pleasure.**
