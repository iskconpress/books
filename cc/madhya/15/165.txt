====== Cc. Madhya 15.165 ======

===== Text =====

> "tomāra vicitra nahe, tumi---sākṣāt prahlāda
> tomāra upare kṛṣṇera sampūrṇa prasāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---in you; [[synonyms:v:vicitra]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---this is not extraordinary; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]] [[synonyms:p:prahlāda]]---incarnation of Prahlāda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon you; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sampūrṇa]]---complete; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Accepting Vāsudeva Datta as a great devotee, the Lord said, "Such a statement is not at all astonishing because you are the incarnation of Prahlāda Mahārāja. It appears that Lord Kṛṣṇa has bestowed complete mercy upon you. There is no doubt about it.**
