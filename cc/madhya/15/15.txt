====== Cc. Madhya 15.15 ======

===== Text =====

> eka eka dina eka eka bhakta-gṛhe mahotsava
> prabhu-saṅge tāhāṅ bhojana kare bhakta saba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---each and every day; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in the house of one devotee after another; [[synonyms:m:mahotsava]]---festival; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---lunch; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---accept; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**Every day one devotee after another would invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and the other devotees to lunch and would also hold a festival.**
