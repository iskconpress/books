====== Cc. Madhya 15.78 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu śasya khāñā punaḥ pātra bhare śāṁse
> śraddhā bāḍe paṇḍitera, prema-sindhu bhāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:ś:śasya]] [[synonyms:k:khāñā]]---eating the pulp; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the plate; [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---fills; [[synonyms:ś:śāṁse]]---with pulp; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]]---faith; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---in the ocean of love; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---floats.

===== Translation =====

**"Sometimes, after eating the pulp, Kṛṣṇa would fill the plate again with new pulp. In this way, Rāghava Paṇḍita's faith increases, and he floats in an ocean of love.**
