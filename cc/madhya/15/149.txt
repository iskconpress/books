====== Cc. Madhya 15.149 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunāthera pāya muñi veciyāchoṅ māthā
> kāḍhite nā pāri māthā, mane pāi vyathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---unto the lotus feet of Lord Raghunātha; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:v:veciyāchoṅ]]---sold; [[synonyms:m:māthā]]---head; [[synonyms:k:kāḍhite]]---to cut off; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am unable; [[synonyms:m:māthā]]---my head; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in my mind; [[synonyms:p:pāi]] [[synonyms:v:vyathā]]---I get too much pain.

===== Translation =====

**"Murāri Gupta said, 'I have sold my head to the lotus feet of Raghunātha. I cannot withdraw my head, for that would give me too much pain.**
