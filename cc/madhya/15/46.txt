====== CC Madhya 15.46 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra ghare kīrtane āmi nitya nāciba
> tumi dekhā pābe, āra keha nā dekhiba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāraghare]]---in your house; [[synonyms:k:kīrtane]]---in the performance of congregational chanting; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:n:nāciba]]---shall dance; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:d:dekhā]] [[synonyms:p:pābe]]---will be able to see; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---else; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiba]]---will not see.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, "Perform congregational chanting daily, and be assured that I shall also dance in your presence. You will be able to see this dancing, but not others.**
