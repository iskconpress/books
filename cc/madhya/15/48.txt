====== CC Madhya 15.48 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra sevā chāḍi' āmi kariyāchi sannyāsa
> dharma nahe, kari āmi nija dharma-nāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up her service; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchi]]---accepted; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:d:dharma]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---it is not My religion; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---perform; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destruction of My religious principles.

===== Translation =====

**"I have given up the service of My mother and have accepted the sannyāsa order. Actually I should not have done this, for by so doing, I have destroyed My religious principles.**
