====== CC Madhya 15.30 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' mahāprabhu baḍa santoṣa pāilā
> mātā-pitā-jñāne duṅhe namaskāra kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very much; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---felt; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]-[[synonyms:p:pitā]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---accepting as father and mother; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---unto both of them; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---offered obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was greatly satisfied to see this. Accepting them both as His father and mother, He offered them obeisances.**
