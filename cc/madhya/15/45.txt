====== Cc. Madhya 15.45 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsa-paṇḍite prabhu kari' āliṅgana
> kaṇṭhe dhari' kahe tāṅre madhura vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍite]]---unto Śrīvāsa Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭhe]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching his neck; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:m:madhura]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---sweet words.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Śrīvāsa Paṇḍita and, with His arm about his neck, began to speak to him in sweet words.**
