====== CC Madhya 15.131 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunandanera kārya---kṛṣṇera sevana
> kṛṣṇa-sevā vinā iṅhāra anya nāhi mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunandanerakārya]]---the duty of Raghunandana; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---worshiping Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except for worshiping Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---intention.

===== Translation =====

**"Furthermore it is the duty of Raghunandana to always engage in Lord Kṛṣṇa's service. He has no other intention but the service of Lord Kṛṣṇa."**
