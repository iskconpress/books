====== CC Madhya 15.159 ======

===== Text =====

> nija-guṇa śuni' datta mane lajjā pāñā
> nivedana kare prabhura caraṇe dhariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---his personal qualities; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:d:datta]]---Vāsudeva Datta; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:l:lajjā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---being ashamed; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---submits; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**When Caitanya Mahāprabhu glorified him, Vāsudeva Datta immediately became very embarrassed and shy. He then submitted himself, touching the Lord's lotus feet.**
