====== CC Madhya 15.251 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe gāli, śāpa dite bhaṭṭācārya āilā
> nindā śuni' mahāprabhu hāsite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:g:gāli]]---calling by ill names; [[synonyms:ś:śāpa]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---cursing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:n:nindā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing the criticism; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hāsite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to laugh.

===== Translation =====

**Bhaṭṭācārya then began to curse and call his son-in-law ill names. When Bhaṭṭācārya returned, he saw that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was laughing to hear him criticize Amogha.**
