====== CC Madhya 15.209 ======

===== Text =====

> keyāpatra-kalākholā-ḍoṅgā sāri sāri
> cāri-dike dhariyāche nānā vyañjana bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keyā]]-[[synonyms:p:patra]]---the leaf of the //keyā// plant; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]-[[synonyms:k:kholā]]---the skin of the banana tree; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍoṅgā]]---pots; [[synonyms:s:sāri]] [[synonyms:s:sāri]]---one after another; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---on all sides; [[synonyms:d:dhariyāche]]---were holding; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---cooked vegetables; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filled.

===== Translation =====

**There were a number of pots made of the bark of banana trees and the leaves of the keyā plant. These pots were filled with various cooked vegetables and placed on all sides of the leaf.**
