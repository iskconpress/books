====== Cc. Madhya 15.189 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,---bhikṣā karaha viśa dina
> prabhu kahe,---eha nahe yati-dharma-cihna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---accept lunch; [[synonyms:v:viśa]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---for twenty days; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:e:eha]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---this is not; [[synonyms:y:yati]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:c:cihna]]---the symptom of a person in the renounced order of life.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma then said, "Please accept the invitation for twenty days," but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "It is not a religious principle of the renounced order."**
