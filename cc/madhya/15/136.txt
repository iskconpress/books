====== Cc. Madhya 15.136 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma, kara 'dāru-brahma'-ārādhana
> vācaspati, kara jala-brahmera sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---O Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---be engaged in; [[synonyms:d:dāru]]-[[synonyms:b:brahma]]---of wooden Brahman; [[synonyms:ā:ārādhana]]---worship; [[synonyms:v:vācaspati]]---and you, Vācaspati; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:b:brahmera]]---of Paraṁ Brahman manifested in water; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---worship.

===== Translation =====

**"Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, you should engage in the worship of Lord Jagannātha Puruṣottama, and Vācaspati should worship mother Ganges."**
