====== CC Madhya 15.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> nīlācale rahi' kare nṛtya-gīta-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with His devotees; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying at Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---chanting and dancing with great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed at Jagannātha Purī, He constantly enjoyed chanting and dancing with His devotees.**
