====== CC Madhya 15.153 ======

===== Text =====

> sādhu sādhu, gupta, tomāra sudṛḍha bhajana
> āmāra vacaneha tomāra nā ṭalila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sādhu]] [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]---all glories unto you; [[synonyms:g:gupta]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---firmly fixed; [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---method of worship; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:v:vacaneha]]---even on the request; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭalila]]---did not budge; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"I said to him, 'All glories to you, Murāri Gupta! Your method of worship is very firmly fixed-so much so that even upon My request your mind did not turn.**
