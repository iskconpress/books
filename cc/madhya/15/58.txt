====== CC Madhya 15.58 ======

===== Text =====

> śīghra yāi' muñi saba karinu bhakṣaṇa
> śūnya-pātra dekhi' aśru kariyā mārjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very soon; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:karinu]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---ate; [[synonyms:ś:śūnya]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the dish empty; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---smearing with her hands.

===== Translation =====

**"While she was thus thinking and crying, I immediately went there with great haste and ate everything. Seeing the dish empty, she wiped her tears away.**
