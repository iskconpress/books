====== Cc. Madhya 15.34 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kāhāṅre rāvṇā' prabhu kahe krodhāveśe
> jagan-mātā hare pāpī, mārimu savaṁśe'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāhāṅre]] [[synonyms:r:rāvṇā]]---where is the rascal Rāvaṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great anger; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of the universe; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---kidnaps; [[synonyms:p:pāpī]]---sinful; [[synonyms:m:mārimu]]---I shall kill; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---with all his family.

===== Translation =====

**In the ecstasy of Hanumān, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu angrily said, "Where is the rascal Rāvaṇa? He has kidnapped the universal mother, Sītā. Now I shall kill him and all his family."**
