====== CC Madhya 11.197 ======

===== Text =====

> samudra-snāna kari' prabhu āilā nija sthāne
> advaitādi gelā sindhu karibāre snāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:samudra]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after bathing in the sea; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to His own place; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---devotees, headed by Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---to the ocean; [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---just to take; [[synonyms:s:snāne]]---bath.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His residence after taking a bath in the sea, all the devotees, headed by Advaita Prabhu, went to bathe in the sea.**
