====== Cc. Madhya 11.228 ======

===== Text =====

> āra sampradāye nāce paṇḍita-vakreśvara
> śrīvāsa nāce āra sampradāya-bhitara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāye]]---in another //sampradāya,// or group; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhitara]]---in the middle of a group.

===== Translation =====

**Vakreśvara Paṇḍita began to dance in another group, and in yet another group Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura began to dance.**
