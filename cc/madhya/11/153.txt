====== Cc. Madhya 11.153 ======

===== Text =====

> murāri nā dekhiyā prabhu kare anveṣaṇa
> murāri la-ite dhāñā āilā bahu-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---without; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:anveṣaṇa]]---inquiry; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---to take; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---many persons.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu could not see Murāri amongst the devotees, He inquired about him. Thereupon many people immediately went to Murāri, running to take him to the Lord.**
