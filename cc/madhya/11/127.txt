====== CC Madhya 11.127 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita karila prabhura caraṇa vandana
> ācāryere kaila prabhu prema-āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worship; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryere]]---unto Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**First Advaita Ācārya offered prayers to the lotus feet of the Lord, and the Lord immediately embraced Him in ecstatic love.**
