====== CC Madhya 11.76 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya kahe,-ei svarūpa-dāmodara
> mahāprabhura haya iṅha dvitīya kalevara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this gentleman; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---his name is Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:i:iṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]---the second; [[synonyms:k:kalevara]]---expansion of the body.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "Here is Svarūpa Dāmodara, who is practically the second expansion of the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
