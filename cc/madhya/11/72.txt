====== CC Madhya 11.72 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi kāho nāhi cini, cinite mana haya
> gopīnāthācārya sabāre karā'be paricaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kāho]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---do not; [[synonyms:c:cini]]---know; [[synonyms:c:cinite]] [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---I desire to know; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:karā'be]] [[synonyms:p:paricaya]]---will identify.

===== Translation =====

**"Actually I do not know any of them, although I have a desire to know them. Since Gopīnātha Ācārya knows them all, he will give you their names."**
