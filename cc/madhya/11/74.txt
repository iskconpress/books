====== CC Madhya 11.74 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara-svarūpa, govinda,-dui jana
> mālā-prasāda lañā yāya, yāhāṅ vaiṣṇava-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---flower garlands and remnants of Lord Jagannātha's food; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---went; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the Vaiṣṇavas.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara and Govinda, taking the flower garlands and prasāda of Lord Jagannātha, proceeded to where all the Vaiṣṇavas were standing.**
