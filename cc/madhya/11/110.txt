====== CC Madhya 11.110 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭa kahe,-bhakta-gaṇa āila jāniñā
> prabhura iṅgite prasāda yāya tāṅrā lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---have come; [[synonyms:j:jāniñā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:i:iṅgite]]---by the indication; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of the food of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---go; [[synonyms:t:tāṅrā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Understanding that all the devotees have come, Lord Caitanya gave the sign, and therefore Vāṇīnātha and the others have brought such great quantities of mahā-prasāda."**
