====== CC Madhya 11.41 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣetre āsi' rājā sārvabhaume bolāilā
> sārvabhaume namaskari' tāṅhāre puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---for Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:b:bolāilā]]---called; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---unto Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---he asked him.

===== Translation =====

**When King Pratāparudra returned to Jagannātha Purī, he called for Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya. When the Bhaṭṭācārya went to see the King, the King offered him respects and made the following inquiries.**
