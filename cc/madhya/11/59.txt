====== CC Madhya 11.59 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' gajapatira mane sukha upajila
> prabhure milite ei mantraṇā dṛḍha kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:g:gajapatira]]---of King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---awakened; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mantraṇā]]---instruction; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---decided to accept rigidly.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Pratāparudra took the Bhaṭṭācārya's advice and firmly decided to follow his instructions. Thus he felt transcendental happiness.**
