====== Cc. Madhya 11.75 ======

===== Text =====

> prathamete mahāprabhu pāṭhāilā duṅhāre
> rājā kahe, ei dui kon cināha āmāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathamete]]---at first; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāre]]---two persons; [[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King said; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]---these two; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---who are they; [[synonyms:c:cināha]]---kindly identify; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]]---to me.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu first sent them both in advance. The King inquired, "Who are these two? Please let me know their identities."**
