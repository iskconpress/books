====== Cc. Madhya 11.132 ======

===== Text =====

> āpana-nikaṭe prabhu sabā vasāilā
> āpani śrī-haste sabāre mālya-gandha dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---by His own side; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made sit; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally Himself; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with His hand; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]---garland; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made all the devotees sit at His side, and with His own hand He offered them garlands and sandalwood pulp.**
