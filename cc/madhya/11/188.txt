====== Cc. Madhya 11.188 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahe,-prabhu, nā chuṅio more
> muñi-nīca, aspṛśya, parama pāmare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my dear Lord; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:chuṅio]] [[synonyms:m:more]]---please do not touch me; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]---most fallen; [[synonyms:a:aspṛśya]]---untouchable; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:p:pāmare]]---the lowest of mankind.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "My dear Lord, please do not touch me, for I am most fallen and untouchable and am the lowest among men."**
