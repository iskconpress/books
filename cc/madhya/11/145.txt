====== CC Madhya 11.145 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsa kahena,-kene kaha viparīta
> kṛpā-mūlye cāri bhāi ha-i tomāra krīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura replied; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:k:kaha]] [[synonyms:v:viparīta]]---do You speak just the opposite; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlye]]---by the price of Your mercy; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---we four brothers; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---become; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:k:krīta]]---purchased.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa then replied to the Lord, "Why are You speaking in a contradictory way? Rather, we four brothers have been purchased by Your mercy."**
