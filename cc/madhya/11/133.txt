====== CC Madhya 11.133 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya, ācārya tabe mahāprabhura sthāne
> yathā-yogya mililā sabākāra sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---as it is befitting; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:s:sabākāra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with all the Vaiṣṇavas assembled there.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Gopīnātha Ācārya and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya met all the Vaiṣṇavas at the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in a befitting manner.**
