====== CC Madhya 11.180 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnāthe dekhāila saba vāsā-ghara
> vāṇīnātha-ṭhāñi dila prasāda vistara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnāthe]]---unto Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:g:ghara]]---residential places; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---unto Vāṇīnātha Rāya; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:v:vistara]]---remnants of food in large quantities.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha was then shown all the residential places, and Vāṇīnātha was given large quantities of food [mahā-prasāda] left by Lord Jagannātha.**
