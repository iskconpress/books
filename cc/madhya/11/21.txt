====== Cc. Madhya 11.21 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra nāma śuni' haila mahā-premāveśa
> mora hāte dhari' kare pirīti viśeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---great; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---my hand; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:pirīti]]---loving symptoms; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---specific.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, as soon as the King heard Your holy name, he was immediately overwhelmed by great ecstatic love. Catching my hand, he displayed all the symptoms of love.**
