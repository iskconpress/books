====== Cc. Madhya 11.61 ======

===== Text =====

> rājāre prabodhiyā bhaṭṭa gelā nijālaya
> snāna-yātrā-dine prabhura ānanda hṛdaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājāre]]---the King; [[synonyms:p:prabodhiyā]]---encouraging; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---departed; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaya]]---to his own home; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the day of the bathing ceremony of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---full of happiness; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---heart.

===== Translation =====

**After thus encouraging the King, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya returned home. On the day of Lord Jagannātha's bathing ceremony, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very happy at heart.**
