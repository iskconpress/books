====== Cc. Madhya 11.9 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,-satya tomāra vacana
> jagannātha-sevaka rājā kintu bhaktottama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---true; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---statement; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servant of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---a great devotee.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "My dear Lord, what You have said is correct, but this King is not an ordinary king. He is a great devotee and servant of Lord Jagannātha."**
