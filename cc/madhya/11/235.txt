====== CC Madhya 11.235 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-nṛtya, mahā-prema, mahā-saṅkīrtana
> dekhi' premāveśe bhāse nīlācala-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---great dancing; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---great love; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---great congregational chanting; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---float; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---all the residents of Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing the great dancing, great love and great saṅkīrtana, all the people of Jagannātha Purī floated in an ecstatic ocean of love.**
