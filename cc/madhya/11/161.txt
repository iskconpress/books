====== CC Madhya 11.161 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāre sammāni' prabhura ha-ila ullāsa
> haridāse nā dekhiyā kahe,-kāhāṅ haridāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāre]] [[synonyms:s:sammāni']]---respecting everyone; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---without seeing; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---where is Haridāsa.

===== Translation =====

**After thus offering respect to each and every devotee, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very jubilant. However, not seeing Haridāsa Ṭhākura, He inquired, "Where is Haridāsa?"**
