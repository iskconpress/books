====== CC Madhya 11.212 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle rāmānanda āilā prabhu-sthāne
> prabhu milāila tāṅre saba vaiṣṇava-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milāila]]---caused to meet; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him (Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya); [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the devotees of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**At this time Rāmānanda Rāya also came to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord took the opportunity to introduce all the Vaiṣṇavas to him.**
