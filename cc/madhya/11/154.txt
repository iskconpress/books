====== Cc. Madhya 11.154 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛṇa dui-guccha murāri daśane dhariyā
> mahāprabhu āge gelā dainyādhīna hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]---of straw; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:g:guccha]]---bunches; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri; [[synonyms:d:daśane]]---in his teeth; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]-[[synonyms:a:adhīna]]---under obligation of meekness; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Murāri Gupta, catching two bunches of straw in his teeth, went before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with humility and meekness.**
