====== CC Madhya 11.39 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-śīghra giyā kara daraśana
> aiche ghara yāi' kara kuṭumba milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]] [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going hastily; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---see Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:g:ghara]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going home; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---just do; [[synonyms:k:kuṭumba]]---family; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu advised, "Immediately go to Lord Jagannātha's temple to see the Lord. Then go home and meet your family members."**
