====== CC Madhya 11.125 ======

===== Text =====

> siṁha-dvāra ḍāhine chāḍi' saba vaiṣṇava-gaṇa
> kāśī-miśra-gṛha-pathe karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]] [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāhine]]---on the right side of the lion gate; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving aside; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛha]]---to the house of Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---began to proceed.

===== Translation =====

**Beginning from the right side of the lion gate, or the main gate of the temple, all the Vaiṣṇavas began to proceed toward the house of Kāśī Miśra.**
