====== CC Madhya 11.20 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra nāma śuni' rājā ānandita haila
> āsana haite uṭhi' more āliṅgana kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very much pleased; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from his throne; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---standing; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**"When I submitted this proposal, the King immediately became very much pleased upon hearing Your name. Indeed, he instantly arose from his throne and embraced me.**
