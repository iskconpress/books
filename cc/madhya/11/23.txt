====== CC Madhya 11.23 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi-chāra, yogya nahi tāṅra daraśane
> tāṅre yei bhaje tāṅra saphala jīvane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:c:chāra]]---very much fallen; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---fit; [[synonyms:n:nahi]]---not; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---for interviewing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---worships; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:saphala]]---successful; [[synonyms:j:jīvane]]---life.

===== Translation =====

**"Then Mahārāja Pratāparudra very humbly said, 'I am most fallen and abominable, and I am unfit to receive an interview with the Lord. One's life is successful if one engages in His service.'**
