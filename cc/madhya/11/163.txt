====== CC Madhya 11.163 ======

===== Text =====

> milana-sthāne āsi' prabhure nā mililā
> rājapatha-prānte dūre paḍiyā rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:milana]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in the meeting place; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---did meet; [[synonyms:r:rājapatha]]-[[synonyms:p:prānte]]---on the side of the common road; [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---at a distant place; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---falling flat; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura did not come to the Lord's meeting place but remained fallen flat on the common road at a distance.**
