====== CC Madhya 11.220 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu jagannāthera mandira beḍiyā
> pradakṣiṇa kari' bulena nartana kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:m:mandira]]---temple; [[synonyms:b:beḍiyā]]---walking all around; [[synonyms:p:pradakṣiṇa]]---circumambulation; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:b:bulena]]---walks; [[synonyms:n:nartana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**At this time Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu circumambulated the temple of Jagannātha and continuously danced about the whole area.**
