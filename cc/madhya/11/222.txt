====== CC Madhya 11.222 ======

===== Text =====

> aśru, pulaka, kampa, sveda, gambhīra huṅkāra
> premera vikāra dekhi' loke camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]] [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---deep resounding; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformation; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---all the people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---were astonished.

===== Translation =====

**While kīrtana was going on, there was a transformation of ecstatic love and much tears, jubilation, trembling, perspiration and deep resounding in the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Upon seeing this transformation, all the people present became very much astonished.**
