====== Cc. Madhya 11.54 ======

===== Text =====

> ratha-yātrā-dine prabhu saba bhakta lañā
> ratha-āge nṛtya karibena premāviṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the day of the car festival ceremony; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with Him; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the chariot; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:karibena]]---will dance; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---in great ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**"On the day of the car festival, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu will dance before the Deity in great ecstatic love.**
