====== Cc. Madhya 11.123 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' vidāya dila sei dui-jane
> sārvabhauma dekhite āila vaiṣṇava-milane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---granted permission to go; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---to those two persons; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:m:milane]]---in the meeting of all the Vaiṣṇavas.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, the King gave them permission to leave. Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya also went to see the assembly of all the Vaiṣṇavas.**
