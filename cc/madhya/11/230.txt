====== CC Madhya 11.230 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-dike nṛtya-gīta kare yata jana
> sabe dekhe,-prabhu kare āmāre daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---on four sides; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---all people; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---everyone sees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---looking at me.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood in the middle of the dancers, and all the dancers in all directions perceived that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was looking at them.**
