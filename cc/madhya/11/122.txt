====== CC Madhya 11.122 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ājñā pāliha duṅhe sāvadhāna hañā
> ājñā nahe, tabu kariha, iṅgita bujhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order; [[synonyms:p:pāliha]]---carry out; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of you; [[synonyms:s:sāvadhāna]]---careful; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---although there is no direct order; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do; [[synonyms:i:iṅgita]]---indication; [[synonyms:b:bujhiyā]]---understanding.

===== Translation =====

**"The orders of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu must be carefully carried out. Although the Lord may not give direct orders, you are still to carry out His desires simply by understanding His indications."**
