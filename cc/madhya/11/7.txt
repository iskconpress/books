====== CC Madhya 11.7 ======

===== Text =====

> virakta sannyāsī āmāra rāja-daraśana
> strī-daraśana-sama viṣera bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:virakta]]---unattached; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---person in the renounced order; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---meeting a king; [[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---meeting a woman; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like; [[synonyms:v:viṣera]]---of poison; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---drinking.

===== Translation =====

**"Since I am in the renounced order, it is as dangerous for Me to meet a king as to meet a woman. To meet either would be just like drinking poison."**
