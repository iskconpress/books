====== CC Madhya 11.17 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya-saṅge prabhura dekhi' sneha-vyavahāra
> sarva bhakta-gaṇera mane haila camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---very intimate behavior; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's intimate dealings with Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya, all the devotees there were astonished.**
