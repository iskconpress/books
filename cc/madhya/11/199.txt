====== Cc. Madhya 11.199 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāre vasāilā prabhu yogya krama kari'
> śrī-haste pariveśana kaila gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made to sit; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---befitting; [[synonyms:k:krama]]---in order, one after another; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---setting; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---by His own transcendental hand; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]]---distribution; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**One after the other, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made all the devotees sit in their proper places. He then began to distribute prasāda with His own transcendental hand.**
