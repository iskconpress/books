====== CC Madhya 11.205 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda lañā bhikṣā karite vaisa tumi
> vaiṣṇavera pariveśana karitechi āmi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking along Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---luncheon; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to take; [[synonyms:v:vaisa]]---sit down; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]]---distribution of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:k:karitechi]]---am doing; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I.

===== Translation =====

**"You may sit down and accept the luncheon with Nityānanda Prabhu, and I shall distribute the prasāda to all the Vaiṣṇavas."**
