====== CC Madhya 11.131 ======

===== Text =====

> miśrera āvāsa sei haya alpa sthāna
> asaṅkhya vaiṣṇava tāhāṅ haila parimāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśrera]] [[synonyms:ā:āvāsa]]---the residence of Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:a:alpa]] [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---insufficient place; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---were; [[synonyms:p:parimāṇa]]---overcrowded.

===== Translation =====

**Since the residence of Kāśī Miśra was insufficient, all the assembled devotees were very much overcrowded.**
