====== Cc. Madhya 11.157 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-murāri, kara dainya saṁvaraṇa
> tomāra dainya dekhi' mora vidīrṇa haya mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---My dear Murāri; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:d:dainya]] [[synonyms:s:saṁvaraṇa]]---please restrain your great humility; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]---humility; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:v:vidīrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind becomes disturbed.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord said, "My dear Murāri, please restrain your unnecessary humility. My mind is disturbed to see your meekness."**
