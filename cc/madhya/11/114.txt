====== CC Madhya 11.114 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ upavāsa, yāhāṅ nāhi mahā-prasāda
> prabhu-ājñā-prasāda-tyāge haya aparādha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:u:upavāsa]]---fasting; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---direct order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:t:tyāge]]---giving up; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense.

===== Translation =====

**"When mahā-prasāda is not available, there must be fasting, but when the Supreme Personality of Godhead directly orders one to take prasāda, neglecting such an opportunity is offensive.**
