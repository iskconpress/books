====== CC Madhya 11.241 ======

===== Text =====

> yāvat āchilā sabe mahāprabhu-saṅge
> prati-dina ei-mata kare kīrtana-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---so long; [[synonyms:ā:āchilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---every day; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---//saṅkīrtana// in great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**As long as the devotees remained at Jagannātha Purī with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the pastime of saṅkīrtana was performed with great jubilation every day.**
