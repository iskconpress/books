====== CC Madhya 11.97 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya kahe ei madhura vacana
> caitanyera sṛṣṭi-ei prema-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---replied; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:madhura]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---transcendental sweetness of the voice; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---the creation of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting in the ecstasy of love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "This sweet transcendental sound is a special creation of the Lord known as prema-saṅkīrtana, congregational chanting in love of Godhead.**
