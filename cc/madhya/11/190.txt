====== Cc. Madhya 11.190 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣaṇe kṣaṇe kara tumi sarva-tīrthe snāna
> kṣaṇe kṣaṇe kara tumi yajña-tapo-dāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---at every moment; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrthe]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing in all the holy places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---at every moment; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---perform; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]---sacrifices; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerities; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---charity.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu exalted Haridāsa Ṭhākura, stating, "At every moment you take your bath in all the holy places of pilgrimage, and at every moment you perform great sacrifices, austerity and charity.**
