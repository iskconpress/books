====== CC Madhya 11.223 ======

===== Text =====

> pickāri-dhārā jini' aśru nayane
> cāri-dikera loka saba karaye siṅcāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pickāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dhārā]]---like water coming in force from a syringe; [[synonyms:j:jini']]---conquering; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:n:nayane]]---in the eyes; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dikera]]---in all four directions; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:s:siṅcāne]]---moistened.

===== Translation =====

**The tears from the eyes of the Lord came out with great force, like water from a syringe. Indeed, all the people who surrounded Him were moistened by His tears.**
