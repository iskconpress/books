====== Cc. Madhya 11.55 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe puṣpodyāne karibena praveśa
> sei-kāle ekale tumi chāḍi' rāja-veśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---into the garden at Guṇḍicā where the Lord stays; [[synonyms:k:karibena]] [[synonyms:p:praveśa]]---will enter; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:e:ekale]]---alone; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---the royal dress.

===== Translation =====

**"On that Ratha-yātrā festival day, after dancing before the Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu will enter the Guṇḍicā garden. At that time you should go there alone, stripped of your royal dress.**
