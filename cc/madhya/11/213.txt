====== CC Madhya 11.213 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā lañā gelā prabhu jagannāthālaya
> kīrtana ārambha tathā kaila mahāśaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaya]]---to the temple of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:ā:ārambha]]---beginning; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---the great personality.

===== Translation =====

**The great Personality of Godhead, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, then took all of them to the temple of Jagannātha and began the congregational chanting of the holy name there.**
