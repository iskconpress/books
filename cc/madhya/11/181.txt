====== CC Madhya 11.181 ======

===== Text =====

> vāṇīnātha āilā bahu prasāda piṭhā lañā
> gopīnātha āilā vāsā saṁskāra kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---a very large quantity of; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:p:piṭhā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---also taking cakes with them; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---residential places; [[synonyms:s:saṁskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---cleansing.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Vāṇīnātha Rāya returned with large quantities of Lord Jagannātha's food remnants, including cakes and other good eatables. Gopīnātha Ācārya also returned after cleansing all the residential quarters.**
