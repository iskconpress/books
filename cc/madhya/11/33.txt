====== Cc. Madhya 11.33 ======

===== Text =====

> purī, bhāratī-gosāñi, svarūpa, nityānanda
> jagadānanda, mukundādi yata bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---on the level of the spiritual master; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī, Brahmānanda Bhāratī Gosāñi, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi, Lord Nityānanda, Jagadānanda, Mukunda and others were present before the Lord at that time.**
