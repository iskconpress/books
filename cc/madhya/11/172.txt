====== CC Madhya 11.172 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāra kariyāchi vāsā-gṛha-sthāna
> mahā-prasāda sabākāre kari samādhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---for all of them; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchi]]---we have arranged; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---residential places for staying; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---to all of them; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---let us do; [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---distribution.

===== Translation =====

**"Accommodations have been arranged for all the Vaiṣṇavas. Now let us distribute mahā-prasāda to all of them."**
