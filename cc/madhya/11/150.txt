====== CC Madhya 11.150 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' śivānanda-sena premāviṣṭa hañā
> daṇḍavat hañā paḍe śloka paḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming absorbed in pure love; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---a verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---reciting.

===== Translation =====

**Immediately upon hearing this, Śivānanda Sena became absorbed in ecstatic love and fell down on the ground, offering obeisances to the Lord. He then began to recite the following verse.**
