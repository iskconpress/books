====== CC Madhya 11.47 ======

===== Text =====

> adarśanīyān api nīca-jātīn
> saṁvīkṣate hanta tathāpi no mām
> mad-eka-varjaṁ kṛpayiṣyatīti
> nirṇīya kiṁ so 'vatatāra devaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adarśanīyān]]---upon those who are unfit to be seen; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:j:jātīn]]---the lower class of men; [[synonyms:s:saṁvīkṣate]]---puts His merciful glance; [[synonyms:h:hanta]]---alas; [[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---still; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:u:u]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---upon me; [[synonyms:m:mat]]---myself; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---alone; [[synonyms:v:varjam]]---rejecting; [[synonyms:k:kṛpayiṣyati]]---He will bestow His mercy; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:n:nirṇīya]]---deciding; [[synonyms:k:kim]]---whether; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avatatāra]]---has descended; [[synonyms:d:devaḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Alas, has Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made His advent deciding that He will deliver all others with the exception of me? He bestows His merciful glance upon many lower-class men who are usually not even to be seen.' "**

===== Purport =====

This verse is found in the //Śrī Caitanya-candrodaya-nāṭaka// (8.28).
