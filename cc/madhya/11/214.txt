====== CC Madhya 11.214 ======

===== Text =====

> sandhyā-dhūpa dekhi' ārambhilā saṅkīrtana
> paḍichā āsi' sabāre dila mālya-candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]-[[synonyms:d:dhūpa]]---//dhūpa-ārati// just in the beginning of the evening; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---they all saw; [[synonyms:ā:ārambhilā]]---began; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---the inspector of the temple; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---offered; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---flower garlands and sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the dhūpa-ārati of the Lord, they all began congregational chanting. Then the paḍichā, the superintendent of the temple, came and offered flower garlands and sandalwood pulp to everyone.**
