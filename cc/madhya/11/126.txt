====== CC Madhya 11.126 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle mahāprabhu nija-gaṇa-saṅge
> vaiṣṇave mililā āsi' pathe bahu-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nila]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of His personal assistants; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇave]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**In the meantime, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, accompanied by His personal associates, met all the Vaiṣṇavas on the road with great jubilation.**
