====== CC Madhya 11.242 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ prabhura kīrtana-vilāsa
> yebā ihā śune, haya caitanyera dāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---thus I have explained; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes in; [[synonyms:y:yebā]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---listens to; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---a servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**In this way I have explained the Lord's pastime of saṅkīrtana, and I bless everyone with this benediction: By listening to this description, one will surely become a servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
