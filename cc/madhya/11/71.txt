====== CC Madhya 11.71 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭa kahe,-aṭṭālikāya kara ārohaṇa
> gopīnātha cine sabāre, karābe daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:a:aṭṭālikāya]]---on the roof of the palace; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:ā:ārohaṇa]]---just go up; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:c:cine]]---knows; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---everyone; [[synonyms:k:karābe]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---he will show.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya requested the King, "Go up on the roof of the palace. Gopīnātha Ācārya knows every one of the devotees. He will identify them for you.**
