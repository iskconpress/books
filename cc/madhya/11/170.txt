====== CC Madhya 11.170 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva vaiṣṇava dekhi' sukha baḍa pāilā
> yathā-yogya sabā-sane ānande mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very much; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---got; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---as is befitting; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---along with everyone; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing all the Vaiṣṇavas together, Kāśī Miśra and the superintendent became very happy. With great happiness they met with the devotees in a befitting manner.**
