====== CC Madhya 11.83 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe,-iṅhāra nāma advaita ācārya
> mahāprabhura mānya-pātra, sarva-śirodhārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya said; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---His name; [[synonyms:a:advaita]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mānya]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---honorable; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śirodhārya]]---the topmost devotee.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya replied, "His name is Advaita Ācārya. He is honored even by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and He is therefore the topmost devotee.**
