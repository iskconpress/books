====== CC Madhya 11.201 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu nā khāile keha nā kare bhojana
> ūrdhva-haste vasi' rahe sarva bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:khāile]]---without eating; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhva]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---raising the hand; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remain; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees kept their hands raised over the prasāda distributed to them, for they did not want to eat without seeing the Lord eat first.**
