====== CC Madhya 11.148 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara kahe,-śaṅkara choṭa āmā haite
> ebe āmāra baḍa bhāi tomāra kṛpāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita replied; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]]---younger; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than me; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---elder brother; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by the mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Dāmodara Paṇḍita replied, "Śaṅkara is my younger brother, but from today he becomes my elder brother because of Your special mercy upon him."**
