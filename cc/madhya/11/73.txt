====== CC Madhya 11.73 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' tina jana aṭṭālikāya caḍila
> hena-kāle vaiṣṇava saba nikaṭe āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---the three persons (namely, the King, Gopīnātha Ācārya and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya); [[synonyms:a:aṭṭālikāya]]---on the roof of the palace; [[synonyms:c:caḍila]]---went up; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---the Vaiṣṇava devotees; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---nearby; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**After Sārvabhauma said this, he went up to the top of the palace with the King and Gopīnātha Ācārya. At this time all the Vaiṣṇava devotees from Bengal drew closer to the palace.**
