====== Cc. Madhya 11.206 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu prasādānna govinda-hāte dilā
> yatna kari' haridāsa-ṭhākure pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---remnants of Jagannātha's food; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand of Govinda; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered; [[synonyms:y:yatna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great attention; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākure]]---unto Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu carefully delivered some prasāda into the hands of Govinda to be given to Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
