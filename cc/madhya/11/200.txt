====== CC Madhya 11.200 ======

===== Text =====

> alpa anna nāhi āise dite prabhura hāte
> dui-tinera anna dena eka eka pāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alpa]] [[synonyms:a:anna]]---a small quantity of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---come; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:t:tinera]]---or of three; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---He delivers; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:p:pāte]]---on each and every plantain leaf.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees were served prasāda on plantain leaves, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu distributed on each leaf a quantity suitable for two or three men to eat, for His hand could not distribute less than that.**
