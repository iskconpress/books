====== CC Madhya 11.79 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe govinda daṇḍavat kaila ācāryere
> tāṅre nāhi cine ācārya, puchila dāmodare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---falling flat to offer obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryere]]---unto Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:c:cine]]---recognized; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:d:dāmodare]]---to Svarūpa Dāmodara.

===== Translation =====

**When Govinda offered his obeisances by falling down flat before Advaita Ācārya, Advaita Ācārya asked Svarūpa Dāmodara about his identity, for He did not know Govinda at that time.**
