====== CC Madhya 11.236 ======

===== Text =====

> gajapati rājā śuni' kīrtana-mahattva
> aṭṭālikā caḍi' dekhe svagaṇa-sahita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gajapati]] [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King of Orissa; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---the greatness of //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:a:aṭṭālikā]] [[synonyms:c:caḍi']]---ascending to the top of the palace; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:s:svagaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---along with his personal associates.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing the greatness of the saṅkīrtana, King Pratāparudra went up to the top of his palace and watched the performance with his personal associates.**
