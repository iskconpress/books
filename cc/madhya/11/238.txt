====== CC Madhya 11.238 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtana-samāptye prabhu dekhi' puṣpāñjali
> sarva vaiṣṇava lañā prabhu āilā vāsā cali'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:s:samāptye]]---at the end of the performance of //kīrtana//; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:p:puṣpāñjali]]---offering flowers to the Lord Jagannātha Deity; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanying; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---to His residence; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---going.

===== Translation =====

**After the saṅkīrtana ended, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu watched the offering of flowers to the Lord Jagannātha Deity. Then He and all the Vaiṣṇavas returned to His residence.**
