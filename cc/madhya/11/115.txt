====== Cc. Madhya 11.115 ======

===== Text =====

> viśeṣe śrī-haste prabhu kare pariveśana
> eta lābha chāḍi' kon kare upoṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśeṣe]]---especially; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with His transcendental hands; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]]---distribution; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:l:lābha]]---profit; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:u:upoṣaṇa]]---fasting.

===== Translation =====

**"When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is distributing prasāda with His transcendental hand, who will neglect such an opportunity and accept the regulative principle of fasting?**
