====== CC Madhya 11.152 ======

===== Text =====

> prathame murāri-gupta prabhure nā miliyā
> bāhirete paḍi' āche daṇḍavat hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathame]]---at first; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]-[[synonyms:g:gupta]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---without; [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---meeting; [[synonyms:b:bāhirete]]---outside; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---falling down; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---was there; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---falling flat like a stick; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming so.

===== Translation =====

**Murāri Gupta at first did not meet the Lord but rather remained outside the door, falling down like a stick to offer obeisances.**
