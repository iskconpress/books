====== CC Madhya 11.63 ======

===== Text =====

> gopī-bhāve virahe prabhu vyākula hañā
> ālālanāthe gelā prabhu sabāre chāḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in the mood of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]]---agitated; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:ā:ālālanāthe]]---to Ālālanātha; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---having given up.

===== Translation =====

**Due to separation from Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt the same great anxiety the gopīs feel in separation from Kṛṣṇa. In this condition He gave up all association and went to Ālālanātha.**
