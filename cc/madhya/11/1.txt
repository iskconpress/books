====== Cc. Madhya 11.1 ======

===== Text =====

> aty-uddaṇḍaṁ tāṇḍavaṁ gauracandraḥ
> kurvan bhaktaiḥ śrī-jagannātha-gehe
> nānā-bhāvālaṅkṛtāṅgaḥ sva-dhāmnā
> cakre viśvaṁ prema-vanyā-nimagnam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ati]]---very much; [[synonyms:u:uddaṇḍam]]---high jumping; [[synonyms:t:tāṇḍavam]]---very graceful dancing; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:c:candraḥ]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kurvan]]---performing; [[synonyms:b:bhaktaiḥ]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:g:gehe]]---in the temple of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅgaḥ]]---having many ecstatic symptoms manifested in His transcendental body; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāmnā]]---by the influence of His ecstatic love; [[synonyms:c:cakre]]---made; [[synonyms:v:viśvam]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vanyā]]-[[synonyms:n:nimagnam]]---merged into the inundation of ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu merged the entire world into the ocean of ecstatic love by performing His beautiful dances within the temple of Jagannātha. He danced exquisitely and jumped high.**
