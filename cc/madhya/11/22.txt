====== CC Madhya 11.22 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra ye vartana, tumi khāo sei vartana
> niścinta hañā bhaja caitanyera caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:v:vartana]]---remuneration; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:khāo]]---take; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vartana]]---pension; [[synonyms:n:niścinta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---without anxiety; [[synonyms:b:bhaja]]---just worship; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"As soon as he heard my petition, he immediately granted me a pension without reductions. Thus the King granted me a full salary as a pension and requested me to engage without anxiety in the service of Your lotus feet.**
