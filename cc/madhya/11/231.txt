====== CC Madhya 11.231 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri janera nṛtya dekhite prabhura abhilāṣa
> sei abhilāṣe kare aiśvarya prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:j:janera]]---of the four persons; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:abhilāṣa]]---desire; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:a:abhilāṣe]]---for that purpose; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---exhibition of a miracle.

===== Translation =====

**Wanting to see the dancing of the four great personalities, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu exhibited Himself in such a way to appear as if He were seeing everyone.**
