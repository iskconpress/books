====== Cc. Madhya 11.225 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-dike cāri sampradāya uccaiḥsvare gāya
> madhye tāṇḍava-nṛtya kare gaurarāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---on four sides; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---the four groups; [[synonyms:u:uccaiḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:svare]]---very loudly; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:t:tāṇḍava]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---jumping and dancing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:g:gaurarāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**On all four sides the four saṅkīrtana groups chanted very loudly, and in the middle Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced, jumping high.**
