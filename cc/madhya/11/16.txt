====== Cc. Madhya 11.16 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya praṇati kaila, prabhu kaila āliṅgana
> dui jane premāveśe karena krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:p:praṇati]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---Rāmānanda Rāya offered his obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---both of them; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---did; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**Upon meeting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Rāmānanda Rāya offered his obeisances. The Lord embraced him, and both of them began to cry in the great ecstasy of love.**
