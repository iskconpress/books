====== Cc. Madhya 11.210 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana samāpta haila, kaila ācamana
> sabāre parāila prabhu mālya-candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---lunch; [[synonyms:s:samāpta]]---ending; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---washing the mouth; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---on everyone; [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---put; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---a garland and sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**After everyone had finished his lunch and washed his mouth and hands, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally decorated everyone with flower garlands and sandalwood pulp.**
