====== Cc. Madhya 11.105 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,-sabe jagannātha nā dekhiyā
> caitanyera vāsā-gṛhe calilā dhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King said; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---without visiting; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to the residential place; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---they went; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running.

===== Translation =====

**The King said, "Instead of visiting the temple of Lord Jagannātha, all the devotees are running toward the residence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu."**
