====== Cc. Madhya 11.219 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtana dekhi' sabāra mane haila camatkāra
> kabhu nāhi dekhi aiche premera vikāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the performance of //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---never; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---see; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformation.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was astonished to see such a performance of saṅkīrtana, and they all agreed that never before had kīrtana been so performed and ecstatic love of God so exhibited.**
