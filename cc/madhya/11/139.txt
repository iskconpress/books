====== CC Madhya 11.139 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsu kahe,-mukunda ādau pāila tomāra saṅga
> tomāra caraṇa pāila sei punar-janma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsukahe]]---Vāsudeva Datta said; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:ā:ādau]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---Your association; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---Your lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]-[[synonyms:j:janma]]---transcendental rebirth.

===== Translation =====

**Vāsudeva replied, "Mukunda got Your association in the beginning. As such, he has taken shelter at Your lotus feet. That is his transcendental rebirth."**
