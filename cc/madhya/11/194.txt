====== CC Madhya 11.194 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-sthāne rahi' kara nāma saṅkīrtana
> prati-dina āsi' āmi kariba milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in this place; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---perform; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---every day; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---shall do; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested Haridāsa Ṭhākura, "Remain here and chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra. I shall personally come here to meet you daily.**
