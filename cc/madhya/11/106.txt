====== Cc. Madhya 11.106 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭa kahe,-ei ta' svābhāvika prema-rīta
> mahāprabhu milibāre utkaṇṭhita cita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Bhaṭṭācārya replied; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---this is; [[synonyms:s:svābhāvika]]---spontaneous; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rīta]]---attraction of love; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---for meeting; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]]---anxious; [[synonyms:c:cita]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "This is spontaneous love. All the devotees are very anxious to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
