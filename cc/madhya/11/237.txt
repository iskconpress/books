====== Cc. Madhya 11.237 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtana dekhiyā rājāra haila camatkāra
> prabhuke milite utkaṇṭhā bāḍila apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing the performance of //kīrtana//; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]]---of the King; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---anxiety; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimitedly.

===== Translation =====

**The King was very much astonished to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's kīrtana, and the King's anxiety to meet Him increased unlimitedly.**
