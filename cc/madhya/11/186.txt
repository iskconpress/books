====== CC Madhya 11.186 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu dekhi' paḍe āge daṇḍavat hañā
> prabhu āliṅgana kaila tāṅre uṭhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell down; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Him; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---flat like a stick; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---embraced; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---raising him up.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Haridāsa Ṭhākura saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he immediately fell down like a stick to offer Him obeisances, and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu raised him up and embraced him.**
