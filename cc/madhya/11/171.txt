====== CC Madhya 11.171 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-pade dui jane kaila nivedane
> ājñā deha',-vaiṣṇavera kari samādhāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---unto the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---submission; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---please order; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---let us do; [[synonyms:s:samādhāne]]---accommodation.

===== Translation =====

**Both submitted to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Please give us orders so that we may make proper arrangements to accommodate all the Vaiṣṇavas.**
