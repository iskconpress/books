====== CC Madhya 11.130 ======

===== Text =====

> eke eke sarva-bhakte kaila sambhāṣaṇa
> sabā lañā abhyantare karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekeeke]]---one after another; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---to every devotee; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sambhāṣaṇa]]---address; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:a:abhyantare]]---inside; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---entered.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord addressed all the devotees one after another and took all of them with Him into the house.**
