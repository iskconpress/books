====== Cc. Madhya 11.5 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe-ei pratāparudra rāya
> utkaṇṭhā hañāche, tomā milibāre cāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---King Pratāparudra of Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]] [[synonyms:h:hañāche]]---has been very anxious; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---You; [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---to meet; [[synonyms:c:cāya]]---he wants.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "There is a king named Pratāparudra Rāya. He is very anxious to meet You, and he wants Your permission."**
