====== Cc. Madhya 11.36 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-rāya, tumi ki kārya karile?
> īśvare nā dekhi' kene āge ethā āile?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---My dear Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---what; [[synonyms:k:karile]]---have done; [[synonyms:ī:īśvare]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---without seeing; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---first; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:ā:āile]]---you came.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "What have you done, My dear Rāya? Why did you not first see Lord Jagannātha and then come here? Why have you come here first?"**
