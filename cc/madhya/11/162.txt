====== CC Madhya 11.162 ======

===== Text =====

> dūra haite haridāsa gosāñe dekhiyā
> rājapatha-prānte paḍi' āche daṇḍavat hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from a distance; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñe]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:r:rājapatha]]-[[synonyms:p:prānte]]---at the side of the common road; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---falling down; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---he was; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---offering obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then saw in the distance that Haridāsa Ṭhākura was lying down flat on the road offering obeisances.**
