====== CC Madhya 11.169 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle kāśī-miśra, paḍichā,-dui jana
> āsiyā karila prabhura caraṇa vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---the superintendent; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**At this time, Kāśī Miśra, along with the superintendent of the temple, came and offered his respects unto the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
