====== CC Madhya 11.221 ======

===== Text =====

> āge-pāche gāna kare cāri sampradāya
> āchāḍera kāle dhare nityānanda rāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]]-[[synonyms:p:pāche]]---in front and in the rear; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---singing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four; [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---groups; [[synonyms:ā:āchāḍera]]---of falling down; [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---captures; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Lord Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**As the circumambulation was performed, the four kīrtana parties sang in front and in the rear. When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell down to the ground, Śrī Nityānanda Rāya Prabhu lifted Him up.**
