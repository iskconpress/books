====== CC Madhya 11.217 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtanera dhvani mahā-maṅgala uṭhila
> caturdaśa loka bhari' brahmāṇḍa bhedila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtanera]] [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the vibration of the //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgala]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---all good fortune awakened; [[synonyms:c:caturdaśa]]---fourteen; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---planetary systems; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling up; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---the whole universe; [[synonyms:b:bhedila]]---penetrated.

===== Translation =====

**When the tumultuous vibration of saṅkīrtana resounded, all good fortune immediately awakened, and the sound penetrated the whole universe through the fourteen planetary systems.**
