====== CC Madhya 11.108 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,-bhavānandera putra vāṇīnātha
> prasāda lañā saṅge cale pāṅca-sāta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King said; [[synonyms:b:bhavānandera]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---the son of Bhavānanda; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking //mahā-prasāda//; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five or seven men.

===== Translation =====

**The King said, "The son of Bhavānanda Rāya named Vāṇīnātha, along with five or seven other men, went there to obtain the remnants of Lord Jagannātha's food.**
