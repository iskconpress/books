====== Cc. Madhya 11.208 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa gosāñi, dāmodara, jagadānanda
> vaiṣṇavere pariveśe tina jane-ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Gosāñi; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavere]] [[synonyms:p:pariveśe]]---distributed to the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---the three persons; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---very jubilant.

===== Translation =====

**Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, Dāmodara Paṇḍita and Jagadānanda all began to distribute prasāda to the devotees with great pleasure.**
