====== CC Madhya 11.224 ======

===== Text =====

> 'beḍā-nṛtya' mahāprabhu kari' kata-kṣaṇa
> mandirera pāche rahi' karaye kīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:beḍā]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---the dancing surrounding the temple; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:m:mandirera]] [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at the rear of the temple; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---congregational chanting.

===== Translation =====

**After circumambulating the temple, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for some time remained at the rear of the temple and continued His saṅkīrtana.**
