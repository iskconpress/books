====== CC Madhya 11.185 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu āilā tabe haridāsa-milane
> haridāsa kare preme nāma-saṅkīrtane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:m:milane]]---to meet Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---chanting of the holy name.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to meet Haridāsa Ṭhākura, and He saw him engaged in chanting the mahā-mantra with ecstatic love. Haridāsa chanted, "Hare Kṛṣṇa, Hare Kṛṣṇa, Kṛṣṇa Kṛṣṇa, Hare Hare Hare Rāma, Hare Rāma, Rāma Rāma, Hare Hare."**
