====== CC Madhya 11.198 ======

===== Text =====

> āsi' jagannāthera kaila cūḍā daraśana
> prabhura āvāse āilā karite bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming back; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:c:cūḍā]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---looking at the top of the temple; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āvāse]]---at the residence; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---to take their luncheon.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing in the sea, all the devotees, headed by Advaita Prabhu, returned, and on their return they saw the top of the Jagannātha temple. They then went to the residence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to take their luncheon.**
