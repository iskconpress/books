====== CC Madhya 11.182 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu kahe,-śuna, sarva vaiṣṇava-gaṇa
> nija-nija-vāsā sabe karaha gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---kindly listen; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---to the respective residential quarters; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---make; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then addressed all the Vaiṣṇavas and requested that they listen to Him. He said, "Now you can go to your respective residential quarters.**
