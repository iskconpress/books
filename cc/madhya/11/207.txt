====== Cc. Madhya 11.207 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane vasilā saba sannyāsīre lañā
> pariveśana kare ācārya haraṣita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīre]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with Him the //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---distributes; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---with great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally sat down to accept lunch with the other sannyāsīs, and Gopīnātha Ācārya began to distribute the prasāda with great pleasure.**
