====== CC Madhya 11.193 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali tāṅre lañā gelā puṣpodyāne
> ati nibhṛte tāṅre dilā vāsā-sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali]]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking him; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---in the flower garden; [[synonyms:a:ati]] [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a very much secluded place; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---a place to remain.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took Haridāsa Ṭhākura within the flower garden, and there, in a very much secluded place, He showed him his residence.**
