====== CC Madhya 11.40 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu ājñā pāñā rāya calilā daraśane
> rāyera prema-bhakti-rīti bujhe kon jane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhuājñā]]---the Lord's permission; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:r:rāyera]]---of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rīti]]---process; [[synonyms:b:bujhe]]---understands; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---what person.

===== Translation =====

**Having received Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's permission, Rāmānanda Rāya hastily went to the temple of Lord Jagannātha. Who can understand the devotional service of Rāya Rāmānanda?**
