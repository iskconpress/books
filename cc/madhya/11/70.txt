====== CC Madhya 11.70 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura gaṇa yata āila gauḍa haite
> bhaṭṭācārya, eke eke dekhāha āmāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---associates; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---who have come; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:e:eke]] [[synonyms:e:eke]]---one after another; [[synonyms:d:dekhāha]]---please show; [[synonyms:ā:āmāte]]---to me.

===== Translation =====

**"Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, please show me, one after another, all of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's devotees who are coming from Bengal."**
