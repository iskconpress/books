====== CC Madhya 11.11 ======

===== Text =====

> ākārād api bhetavyaṁ
> strīṇāṁ viṣayiṇām api
> yathāher manasaḥ kṣobhas
> tathā tasyākṛter api

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākārāt]]---from bodily features; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:b:bhetavyam]]---to be feared; [[synonyms:s:strīṇām]]---of women; [[synonyms:v:viṣayiṇām]]---of materialistic persons; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:a:aheḥ]]---from a serpent; [[synonyms:m:manasaḥ]]---of the mind; [[synonyms:k:kṣobhaḥ]]---agitation; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---so; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of it; [[synonyms:ā:ākṛteḥ]]---from the appearance; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Just as one is immediately frightened upon seeing a live serpent or even the form of a serpent, one endeavoring for self-realization should similarly fear a materialistic person and a woman. Indeed, he should not even glance at their bodily features.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Śrī Caitanya-candrodaya-nāṭaka// (8.24).
