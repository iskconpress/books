====== Cc. Madhya 11.239 ======

===== Text =====

> paḍichā āniyā dila prasāda vistara
> sabāre bāṅṭiyā tāhā dilena īśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---the superintendent of the temple; [[synonyms:ā:āniyā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---of remnants of Jagannātha's food; [[synonyms:v:vistara]]---a large quantity; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭiyā]]---distributing; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dilena]]---gave; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**The superintendent of the temple then brought large quantities of prasāda, which Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally distributed to all the devotees.**
