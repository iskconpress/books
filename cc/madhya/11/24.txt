====== Cc. Madhya 11.24 ======

===== Text =====

> parama kṛpālu teṅha vrajendra-nandana
> kona-janme more avaśya dibena daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]]---very much; [[synonyms:k:kṛpālu]]---merciful; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Mahārāja Nanda; [[synonyms:k:kona]]-[[synonyms:j:janme]]---in some future birth; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:dibena]]---will give; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---interview.

===== Translation =====

**"The King then said, 'Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda. He is very merciful, and I hope that in a future birth He will allow me an interview.'**
