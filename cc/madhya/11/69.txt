====== CC Madhya 11.69 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,-paḍichāke āmi ājñā diba
> vāsā ādi ye cāhiye,-paḍichā saba diba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King said; [[synonyms:p:paḍichāke]]---unto the attendant; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:diba]]---shall give orders; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---residential quarters; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and other arrangements; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:c:cāhiye]]---whatever you want; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---the attendant; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everything; [[synonyms:d:diba]]---will supply.

===== Translation =====

**The King replied, "I shall give orders to the attendant in the temple. He will arrange for everyone's residential quarters and prasāda, as you desire.**
