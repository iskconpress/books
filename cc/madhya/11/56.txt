====== CC Madhya 11.56 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa-rāsa-pañcādhyāya' karite paṭhana
> ekale yāi' mahāprabhura dharibe caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:a:adhyāya]]---the five chapters in the Tenth Canto of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// in which Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes of the //rāsa// dance are described; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:p:paṭhana]]---to recite; [[synonyms:e:ekale]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going alone; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dharibe]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---catch hold of the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu enters the Guṇḍicā, you should also go there and read the five chapters about Lord Kṛṣṇa's dancing with the gopīs. In this way you can catch hold of the Lord's lotus feet.**
