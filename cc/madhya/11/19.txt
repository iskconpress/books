====== CC Madhya 11.19 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi kahi,-āmā haite nā haya 'viṣaya'
> caitanya-caraṇe rahoṅ, yadi ājñā haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I said; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---by me; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is possible; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---government service; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahoṅ]]---I may stay; [[synonyms:y:yadi]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---if you kindly give me permission.

===== Translation =====

**"I said, 'Your Majesty, I am now not willing to engage in political activities. I desire only to stay at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Kindly give me permission.'**
