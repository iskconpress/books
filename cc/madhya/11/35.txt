====== CC Madhya 11.35 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-rāya, dekhile kamala-nayana?
> rāya kahe-ebe yāi pāba daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---My dear Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:d:dekhile]]---have you seen; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:n:nayana]]---the lotus-eyed Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya replied; [[synonyms:e:ebe]] [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---now I shall go; [[synonyms:p:pāba]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---I shall visit the temple.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu next asked Rāmānanda Rāya, "Have you already visited the temple of the lotus-eyed Lord Jagannātha?"**


**Rāmānanda Rāya replied, "I shall now go visit the temple."**
