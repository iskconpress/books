====== Cc. Madhya 11.124 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnāthācārya bhaṭṭācārya sārvabhauma
> dūre rahi' dekhe prabhura vaiṣṇava-milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:dūre]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---standing a little off; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---see; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting with the Vaiṣṇavas.

===== Translation =====

**From a distant place both Gopīnātha Ācārya and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya watched the meeting of all the Vaiṣṇavas with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
