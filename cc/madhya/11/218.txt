====== CC Madhya 11.218 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtana-ārambhe prema uthali' calila
> nīlācala-vāsī loka dhāñā āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārambhe]]---in the beginning of the //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:u:uthali']]---overpowering; [[synonyms:c:calila]]---began to proceed; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---all the residents of Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**When the congregational chanting began, ecstatic love immediately overflooded everything, and all the residents of Jagannātha Purī came running.**
