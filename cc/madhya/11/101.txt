====== CC Madhya 11.101 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,-śāstra-pramāṇe caitanya hana kṛṣṇa
> tabe kene paṇḍita saba tāṅhāte vitṛṣṇa?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King said; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:p:pramāṇe]]---by the evidence of revealed scripture; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hana]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---therefore; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---so-called learned scholars; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāte]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:v:vitṛṣṇa]]---indifferent.

===== Translation =====

**The King said, "According to evidence given in the revealed scriptures, it is concluded that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself. Why, then, are learned scholars sometimes indifferent to Him?"**
