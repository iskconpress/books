====== CC Madhya 11.204 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya āsiyāchena bhikṣāra prasādānna lañā
> purī, bhāratī āchena tomāra apekṣā kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyāchena]]---has come; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣāra]]---for eating; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the remnants of all kinds of food; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---are; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---for You; [[synonyms:a:apekṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---waiting.

===== Translation =====

**"Gopīnātha Ācārya has already come bringing sufficient remnants of food to distribute to all the sannyāsīs, and sannyāsīs like Paramānanda Purī and Brahmānanda Bhāratī are waiting for You.**
