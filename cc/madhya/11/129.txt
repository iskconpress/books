====== CC Madhya 11.129 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsādi karila prabhura caraṇa vandana
> pratyeke karila prabhu prema-āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---devotees headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:pratyeke]]---to everyone; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing in love.

===== Translation =====

**After this, all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, offered prayers to the lotus feet of the Lord, and the Lord embraced each and every one of them in great love and ecstasy.**
