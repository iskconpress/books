====== CC Madhya 11.143 ======

===== Text =====

> pratyeka vaiṣṇava sabe likhiyā la-ila
> krame krame dui grantha sarvatra vyāpila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratyeka]]---each and every; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotee; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:l:likhiyā]]---copying; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---took; [[synonyms:k:krame]] [[synonyms:k:krame]]---by and by; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---the two books; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:v:vyāpila]]---become broadcast.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, each and every Vaiṣṇava copied the two books. By and by, the two books [the Brahma-saṁhitā and Śrī Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta] were broadcast all over India.**
