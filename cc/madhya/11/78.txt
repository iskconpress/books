====== CC Madhya 11.78 ======

===== Text =====

> ādau mālā advaitere svarūpa parāila
> pāche govinda dvitīya mālā āni' tāṅre dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ādau]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---a garland; [[synonyms:a:advaitere]]---unto Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---offered; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---after that; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda, the Lord's personal servant; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]---a second; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garland; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered to Him.

===== Translation =====

**At the beginning, Svarūpa Dāmodara came forward and garlanded Advaita Ācārya. Govinda next came and offered a second garland to Advaita Ācārya.**
