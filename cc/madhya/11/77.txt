====== CC Madhya 11.77 ======

===== Text =====

> dvitīya, govinda-bhṛtya, ihāṅ doṅhā diyā
> mālā pāṭhāñāchena prabhu gaurava kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]---the second; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---personal servant; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:d:doṅhā]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---through these two persons; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---flower garlands; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāñāchena]]---has sent; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaurava]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---giving much honor.

===== Translation =====

**"The second person is Govinda, Lord Caitanya's personal servant. The Lord has sent garlands and remnants of Lord Jagannātha's food with these two persons simply to honor the devotees from Bengal."**
