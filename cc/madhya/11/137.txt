====== CC Madhya 11.137 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsudeva dekhi' prabhu ānandita hañā
> tāṅre kichu kahe tāṅra aṅge hasta diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming very much pleased; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says something; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on his body; [[synonyms:h:hasta]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---placing His hand.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw Vāsudeva Datta, the father of Mukunda Datta, He immediately became very happy and, placing His hand on his body, began to speak.**
