====== CC Madhya 11.226 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu-kṣaṇa nṛtya kari' prabhu sthira hailā
> cāri mahāntere tabe nācite ājñā dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for a long period; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---dancing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sthira]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became silent; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:mahāntere]]---to four great personalities; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:n:nācite]]---to dance; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---ordered.

===== Translation =====

**After dancing for a long time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became still and ordered four great personalities to begin to dance.**
