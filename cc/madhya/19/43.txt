====== Cc. Madhya 19.43 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura mahimā dekhi' loke camatkāra
> prayāge prabhura līlā nāri varṇibāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the greatness; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---in all people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]]---at Prayāga; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes; [[synonyms:n:nāri]]---I am not able; [[synonyms:v:varṇibāra]]---to describe.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was astounded to see the greatness of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Indeed, I cannot properly describe the pastimes of the Lord at Prayāga.**
