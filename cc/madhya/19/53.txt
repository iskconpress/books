~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 19.53~~

====== CC Madhya 19.53 ======

===== Text =====

> namo mahā-vadānyāya
> kṛṣṇa-prema-pradāya te
> kṛṣṇāya kṛṣṇa-caitanya-
> nāmne gaura-tviṣe namaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:vadānyāya]]---who is most munificent and charitably disposed; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pradāya]]---who can give; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto You; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇāya]]---the original Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmne]]---under the name Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:t:tviṣe]]---whose complexion is the golden complexion of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**"O most munificent incarnation! You are Kṛṣṇa Himself appearing as Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu. You have assumed the golden color of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, and You are widely distributing pure love of Kṛṣṇa. We offer our respectful obeisances unto You.**
