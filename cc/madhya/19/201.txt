====== CC Madhya 19.201 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa yadi rukmiṇīre kailā parihāsa
> 'kṛṣṇa chāḍibena'---jāni' rukmiṇīra haila trāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---although; [[synonyms:r:rukmiṇīre]]---unto Rukmiṇī, the first queen; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]]---joking; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:chāḍibena]]---will give me up; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---thinking; [[synonyms:r:rukmiṇīra]]---of Rukmiṇī; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:t:trāsa]]---shock.

===== Translation =====

**"Although Kṛṣṇa was joking with Queen Rukmiṇī, she was thinking that He was going to give up her company, and she was therefore shocked.**
