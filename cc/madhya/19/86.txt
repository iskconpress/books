====== CC Madhya 19.86 ======

===== Text =====

> savaṁśe sei jala mastake dharila
> nūtana kaupīna-bahirvāsa parāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---with all the family members; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:m:mastake]]---on the head; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---sprinkled; [[synonyms:n:nūtana]]---fresh; [[synonyms:k:kaupīna]]---underwear; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---external covering; [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---put on.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya and his whole family then sprinkled that water over their heads. They then offered the Lord new underwear and outer garments.**
