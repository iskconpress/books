====== Cc. Madhya 19.188 ======

===== Text =====

> pañca-rasa 'sthāyī' vyāpī rahe bhakta-mane
> sapta gauṇa 'āgantuka' pāiye kāraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---five direct transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:s:sthāyī]]---permanently existing; [[synonyms:v:vyāpī]]---expanded; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remain situated; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the heart of a devotee; [[synonyms:s:sapta]] [[synonyms:g:gauṇa]]---seven indirect mellows; [[synonyms:ā:āgantuka]]---accidental; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---appearing; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇe]]---under certain conditions.

===== Translation =====

**"The five direct transcendental mellows of devotional service are permanently situated in the heart of the devotee, whereas the seven indirect emotions appear suddenly under certain conditions and appear more powerful.**
