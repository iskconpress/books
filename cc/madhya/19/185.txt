====== CC Madhya 19.185 ======

===== Text =====

> śānta, dāsya, sakhya, vātsalya, madhura-rasa nāma
> kṛṣṇa-bhakti-rasa-madhye e pañca pradhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śānta]]---neutrality; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]---servitude; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]---friendship; [[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]---paternal affection; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---conjugal love; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---different names; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---of devotional service to the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the mellows; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among; [[synonyms:e:e]]---these; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---chief.

===== Translation =====

**"The chief transcendental mellows experienced with the Supreme Personality of Godhead are five-śānta, dāsya, sakhya, vātsalya and madhura.**
