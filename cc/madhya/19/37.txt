====== CC Madhya 19.37 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhā lañā rūpa-gosāñi prayāge āilā
> mahāprabhu tāhāṅ śuni' ānandita hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking him along; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pra]] [[synonyms:y:yāge]]---to Prayāga; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---were very much pleased.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī and Anupama Mallika went to Prayāga, and they were very pleased to hear news that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was there.**
