====== Cc. Madhya 19.254 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu āilā śuni' śiṣṭa śiṣṭa jana
> brāhmaṇa, kṣatriya āsi' karena daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has arrived; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣṭa]] [[synonyms:ś:śiṣṭa]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---all respectable persons; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---belonging to the brāhmaṇa community; [[synonyms:k:kṣatriya]]---belonging to the kṣatriya community; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---see.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had come, all the respectable members of the brāhmaṇa and kṣatriya communities came to see Him.**
