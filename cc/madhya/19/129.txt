====== CC Madhya 19.129 ======

===== Text =====

> karoṅyā-mātra hāte, kāṅthā chiṅḍā, bahirvāsa
> kṛṣṇa-kathā, kṛṣṇa-nāma, nartana-ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karoṅyā]]---the waterpot of a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand; [[synonyms:k:kāṅthā]] [[synonyms:c:chiṅḍā]]---torn quilt; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---outer garments; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---discussion of Kṛṣṇa's pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---chanting the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]-[[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---dancing in jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**"They carry only waterpots, and they wear torn quilts. They always chant the holy names of Kṛṣṇa and discuss His pastimes. In great jubilation, they also dance.**
