====== Cc. Madhya 19.32 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā śrī-rūpa likhila sanātana-ṭhāñi
> 'vṛndāvana calilā śrī-caitanya-gosāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:l:likhila]]---wrote; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---has gone; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Upon receiving this message from his two messengers, Rūpa Gosvāmī immediately wrote a letter to Sanātana Gosvāmī saying that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had departed for Vṛndāvana.**
