====== CC Madhya 19.4 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-bhāi viṣaya-tyāgera upāya sṛjila
> bhau-dhana diyā dui brāhmaṇe varila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāgera]]---of giving up material activities; [[synonyms:u:upāya]] [[synonyms:s:sṛjila]]---discovered a means; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---much money; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---paying; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇe]]---two //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:v:varila]]---appointed.

===== Translation =====

**The two brothers devised a means whereby they could give up their material activities. For this purpose, they appointed two brāhmaṇas and paid them a large amount of money.**
