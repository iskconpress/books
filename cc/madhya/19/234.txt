====== Cc. Madhya 19.234 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata madhure saba bhāva-samāhāra
> ataeva āsvādādhikye kare camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:madhure]]---on the platform of conjugal love; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:s:samāhāra]]---amalgamation of the feelings; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]]-[[synonyms:a:adhikye]]---from the increase of tasting by the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---is certainly wonderful.

===== Translation =====

**"Similarly, on the platform of conjugal love, all the feelings of the devotees are amalgamated. The intensified taste is certainly wonderful."**
