====== CC Madhya 19.219 ======

===== Text =====

> kevala 'svarūpa-jñāna' haya śānta-rase
> 'pūrṇaiśvarya-prabhu-jñāna' adhika haya dāsye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge of the constitutional position of one's self; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:ś:śānta]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---in the mellow of neutrality; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge of the full opulences of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:adhika]]---greater; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:d:dāsye]]---in the transcendental mellow of servitude.

===== Translation =====

**"On the platform of śānta-rasa, one only realizes his constitutional position. However, when one is raised to the platform of dāsya-rasa, he better understands the full opulence of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
