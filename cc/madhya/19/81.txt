====== CC Madhya 19.81 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura bhare naukā kare ṭalamala
> ḍubite lāgila naukā, jhalake bhare jala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---because of the weight; [[synonyms:n:naukā]]---the boat; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭalamala]]---tilting; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubite]]---to sink; [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began; [[synonyms:n:naukā]]---the boat; [[synonyms:j:jhalake]]---in gushes; [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---fills; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water.

===== Translation =====

**Due to the Lord's heavy weight, the boat began to tilt. It began filling up with water and was on the verge of sinking.**
