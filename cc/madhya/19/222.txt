====== CC Madhya 19.222 ======

===== Text =====

> śāntera guṇa, dāsyera sevana---sakhye dui haya
> dāsyera 'sambhrama-gaurava'-sevā, sakhye 'viśvāsa'-maya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāntera]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities of //śānta-rasa//; [[synonyms:d:dāsyera]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---the service of the //dāsya-rasa//; [[synonyms:s:sakhye]]---on the platform of fraternity; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two qualities; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:d:dāsyera]]---of the //dāsya// platform; [[synonyms:s:sambhrama]]-[[synonyms:g:gaurava]]---with awe and veneration; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:s:sakhye]]---on the platform of fraternity; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---spread with confidence.

===== Translation =====

**"The qualities of śānta-rasa and the service of dāsya-rasa are both present on the platform of sakhya-rasa. On the platform of fraternity, the qualities of dāsya-rasa are mixed with the confidence of fraternity instead of awe and veneration.**
