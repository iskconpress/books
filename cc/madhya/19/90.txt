~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 19.90~~

====== CC Madhya 19.90 ======

===== Text =====

> mukha-vāsa diyā prabhure karāila śayana
> āpane bhaṭṭa karena prabhura pāda-samvāhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---spices; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made to do; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---resting; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Śrīla Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:samvāhana]]---massaging the leg.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord was then given spices to purify His mouth. Afterwards He was made to rest, and Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya personally massaged His legs.**
