====== CC Madhya 19.116 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-pāśe yata siddhānta śunilā
> rūpe kṛpā kari' tāhā saba sañcārilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---from Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---the ultimate conclusions; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---he heard; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---unto Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing His causeless mercy; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all those; [[synonyms:s:sañcārilā]]---infused.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu taught Rūpa Gosvāmī all the conclusions He had heard from Rāmānanda Rāya and duly empowered him so that he could understand them.**
