====== Cc. Madhya 19.196 ======

===== Text =====

> vasudeva-devakīra kṛṣṇa caraṇa vandila
> aiśvarya-jñāne duṅhāra mane bhaya haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vasudeva]]-[[synonyms:d:devakīra]]---of Vasudeva and Devakī; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---to the lotus feet; [[synonyms:v:vandila]]---offered prayers; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---because of knowledge of the opulence; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the minds; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was fear.

===== Translation =====

**"When Kṛṣṇa offered prayers at the lotus feet of His mother and father, Vasudeva and Devakī, they both felt awe, reverence and fear due to knowledge of His opulences.**
