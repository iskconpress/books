====== CC Madhya 19.172 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣaṇaṁ bhakti-yogasya
> nirguṇasya hy udāhṛtam
> ahaituky avyavahitā
> yā bhaktiḥ puruṣottame

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇam]]---the symptom; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:y:yogasya]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:n:nirguṇasya]]---beyond the three modes of nature; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:u:udāhṛtam]]---is cited; [[synonyms:a:ahaitukī]]---causeless; [[synonyms:a:avyavahitā]]---uninterrupted; [[synonyms:y:yā]]---which; [[synonyms:b:bhaktiḥ]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottame]]---to the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'These are the characteristics of transcendental loving service to Puruṣottama, the Supreme Personality of Godhead: it is causeless, and it cannot be obstructed in any way.**
