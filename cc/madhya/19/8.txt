====== CC Madhya 19.8 ======

===== Text =====

> daṇḍa-bandha lāgi' cauṭhi sañcaya karilā
> bhāla-bhāla vipra-sthāne sthāpya rākhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:b:bandha]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---in case of legal implications; [[synonyms:c:cauṭhi]]---one-fourth; [[synonyms:s:sañcaya]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---he collected; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---very respectable; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in the custody of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:sthāpya]] [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept deposited.

===== Translation =====

**He kept one-fourth of his wealth with a respectable brāhmaṇa. He kept this for his personal safety because he was expecting some legal complications.**
