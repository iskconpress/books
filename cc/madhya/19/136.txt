====== CC Madhya 19.136 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---śuna, rūpa, bhakti-rasera lakṣaṇa
> sūtra-rūpe kahi, vistāra nā yāya varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please listen; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---My dear Rūpa; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of the transcendental mellows in devotional service; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of a synopsis; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I shall explain; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---the whole breadth; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is possible; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Rūpa, please listen to Me. It is not possible to describe devotional service completely; therefore I am just trying to give you a synopsis of the symptoms of devotional service.**
