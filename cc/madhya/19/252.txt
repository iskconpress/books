====== CC Madhya 19.252 ======

===== Text =====

> eta jāni' tāṅra bhikṣā kailā aṅgīkāra
> vāsā-niṣṭhā kailā candraśekharera ghara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---on this understanding; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---lunch; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---He accepted; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:n:niṣṭhā]]---residence; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:c:candraśekharera]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---the house of Candraśekhara.

===== Translation =====

**With this understanding, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu agreed to accept lunch at the place of Tapana Miśra. The Lord made His residence at the home of Candraśekhara.**
