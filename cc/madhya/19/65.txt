====== CC Madhya 19.65 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe bhaṭṭa mahāprabhure nimantraṇa kaila
> mahāprabhu dui-bhāi tāṅhāre milāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---invited; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa invited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for lunch, and the Lord introduced the brothers Rūpa and Vallabha to him.**
