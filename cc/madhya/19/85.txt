====== CC Madhya 19.85 ======

===== Text =====

> ānandita hañā bhaṭṭa dila divyāsana
> āpane karila prabhura pāda-prakṣālana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming pleased; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---a nice sitting place; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing of the feet.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived at his home, Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya, being greatly pleased, offered the Lord a nice sitting place and personally washed His feet.**
