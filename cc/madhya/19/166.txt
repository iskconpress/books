====== CC Madhya 19.166 ======

===== Text =====

> 'śuddha-bhakti' haite haya 'premā' utpanna
> ataeva śuddha-bhaktira kahiye 'lakṣaṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---pure devotional service without material contaminations; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:premā]]---love of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:u:utpanna]]---produced; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of pure devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---let me explain; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms.

===== Translation =====

**"When one is situated in pure devotional service, he develops love of Godhead; therefore let me describe some of the symptoms of pure devotional service.**

===== Purport =====

In //Bhagavad-gītā// [[books:bg:18:55|(Bg. 18.55)]] it is said: //bhaktyā mām abhijānāti yāvān yaś cāsmi tattvataḥ.// One cannot understand the Supreme Personality of Godhead in truth unless he takes to devotional service.
