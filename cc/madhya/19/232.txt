====== Cc. Madhya 19.232 ======

===== Text =====

> kānta-bhāve nijāṅga diyā karena sevana
> ataeva madhura-rasera haya 'pañca' guṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kānta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---on the platform of conjugal love; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---own body; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---executes; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of the mellow of conjugal love; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:p:pañca]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---five kinds of transcendental qualities.

===== Translation =====

**"On the platform of conjugal love, the devotee offers his body in the service of the Lord. Thus on this platform all five transcendental qualities are present.**
