====== CC Madhya 19.107 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe prabhu tāṅre kailā āliṅgana
> prema matta hañā teṅho karena nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:m:matta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being overwhelmed by ecstatic love; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---began to dance.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Raghupati Upādhyāya in ecstatic love. Raghupati Upādhyāya also was overwhelmed by love, and he began to dance.**
