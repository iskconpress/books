====== Cc. Madhya 19.82 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi bhaṭṭera āge prabhura dhairya haila mana
> durvāra udbhaṭa prema nahe samvaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabhācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhairya]]---patient; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:d:durvāra]]---difficult to stop; [[synonyms:u:udbhaṭa]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there is not; [[synonyms:s:samvaraṇa]]---checking.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tried to restrain Himself as far as possible before Vallabhācārya, but although He tried to keep calm, His ecstatic love could not be checked.**
