====== CC Madhya 19.105 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---bhāla tattva śikhāilā more
> eta bali' śloka paḍe gadgada-svare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---good; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---conclusions; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]] [[synonyms:m:more]]---you have taught Me; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited the full verse; [[synonyms:g:gadgada]]-[[synonyms:s:svare]]---in a faltering voice.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "You have certainly given first-class conclusions." After saying this, He began to recite the full verse with a faltering voice.**
