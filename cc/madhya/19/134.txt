====== CC Madhya 19.134 ======

===== Text =====

> hṛdi yasya preraṇayā
> pravartito 'haṁ varāka-rūpo 'pi
> tasya hareḥ pada-kamalaṁ
> vande caitanya-devasya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hṛdi]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom (the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who gives His pure devotees intelligence with which to spread the Kṛṣṇa consciousness movement); [[synonyms:p:preraṇayā]]---by the inspiration; [[synonyms:p:pravartitaḥ]]---engaged; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:v:varāka]]---insignificant and low; [[synonyms:r:rūpaḥ]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of Him; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---who is Lord Hari, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:k:kamalam]]---to the lotus feet; [[synonyms:v:vande]]---let me offer my prayers; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:devasya]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**"Although I am the lowest of men and have no knowledge, the inspiration to write transcendental literatures about devotional service has been mercifully bestowed upon me. Therefore I am offering my obeisances at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who has given me the chance to write these books."**
