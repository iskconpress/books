====== Cc. Madhya 19.231 ======

===== Text =====

> madhura-rase---kṛṣṇa-niṣṭhā, sevā atiśaya
> sakhyera asaṅkoca, lālana-mamatādhikya haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---on the platform of conjugal love; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:niṣṭhā]]---attachment for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:a:atiśaya]]---an improved rendering service; [[synonyms:s:sakhyera]]---of the platform of fraternity; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkoca]]---relaxation; [[synonyms:l:lālana]]---maintenance; [[synonyms:m:mamatā]]-[[synonyms:a:adhikya]]---increase of intimacy; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**"On the platform of conjugal love, attachment for Kṛṣṇa, rendering service unto Him, the relaxed feelings of fraternity and the feelings of maintenance all increase in intimacy.**
