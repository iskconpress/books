====== Cc. Madhya 19.137 ======

===== Text =====

> pārāpāra-śūnya gabhīra bhakti-rasa-sindhu
> tomāya cākhāite tāra kahi eka 'bindu'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāra]]-[[synonyms:a:apāra]]---the length and breadth; [[synonyms:ś:śūnya]]---without; [[synonyms:g:gabhīra]]---deep; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of the mellows in devotional service; [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---to you; [[synonyms:c:cākhāite]]---to give a taste; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of this ocean; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:b:bindu]]---drop.

===== Translation =====

**"The ocean of the transcendental mellow of devotional service is so big that no one can estimate its length and breadth. However, just to help you taste it, I am describing but one drop.**
