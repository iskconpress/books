====== CC Madhya 19.100 ======

===== Text =====

> prema dekhi' upādhyāyera haila camatkāra
> 'manuṣya nahe, iṅho---kṛṣṇa'---karila nirdhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing His ecstatic love; [[synonyms:u:upādhyāyera]]---of Raghupati Upādhyāya; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonder; [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not a human being; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:n:nirdhāra]]---made assessment.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghupati Upādhyāya saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic symptoms, he decided that the Lord was not a human being but Kṛṣṇa Himself.**
