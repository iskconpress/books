====== CC Madhya 19.238 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhāte uṭhiyā yabe karilā gamana
> tabe tāṅra pade rūpa kare nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]]---getting up; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:p:pade]]---at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arose and prepared to leave for Vārāṇasī [Benares], Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī made the following statement at the Lord's lotus feet.**
