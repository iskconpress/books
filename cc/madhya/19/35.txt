====== CC Madhya 19.35 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche taiche chuṭi' tumi āisa vṛndāvana'
> eta likhi' dui-bhāi karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]] [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---somehow or other; [[synonyms:c:chuṭi']]---getting released; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:ā:āisa]]---come; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:l:likhi']]---writing this; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers, namely Rūpa Gosvāmī and his younger brother Anupama; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departed.

===== Translation =====

**"Somehow or other get yourself released and come to Vṛndāvana." After writing this, the two brothers [Rūpa Gosvāmī and Anupama] went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
