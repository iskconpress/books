====== Cc. Madhya 19.131 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu bhakti-rasa-śāstra karaye likhana
> caitanya-kathā śune, kare caitanya-cintana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---transcendental literature about the mellows of devotional service; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:l:likhana]]---write; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---talks about the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---they hear; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:cintana]]---thinking of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**"Sometimes they write transcendental literatures about devotional service, and sometimes they hear about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and spend their time thinking about the Lord."**
