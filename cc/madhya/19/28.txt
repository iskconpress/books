====== Cc. Madhya 19.28 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle gela rājā uḍiyā mārite
> sanātane kahe,---tumi cala mora sāthe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]] [[synonyms:m:mārite]]---to attack the Orissa province; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he said to Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:c:cala]]---you come; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:sāthe]]---along with me.

===== Translation =====

**At this time, the Nawab was going to attack the province of Orissa, and he told Sanātana Gosvāmī, "Come along with me."**

===== Purport =====

Hussain Shah attacked the province of Orissa in 1424 Śakābda Era. At that time he conquered the feudal princes of neighboring Orissa.
