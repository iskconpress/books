====== CC Madhya 19.128 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vipra-gṛhe' sthūla-bhikṣā, kāhāṅ mādhu-karī
> śuṣka ruṭī-cānā civāya bhoga parihari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in the house of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:sthūla]]-[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---full meals; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:m:mādhu]]-[[synonyms:k:karī]]---begging little by little like honeybees; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]---dry; [[synonyms:r:ruṭī]]---bread; [[synonyms:c:cānā]]---chick-peas; [[synonyms:c:civāya]]---chew; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:p:parihari']]---giving up all kinds of material enjoyment.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīla Rūpa and Sanātana Gosvāmī beg a little food from the houses of brāhmaṇas. Giving up all kinds of material enjoyment, they only take some dry bread and fried chick-peas.**
