====== Cc. Madhya 19.94 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' ānandita haila upādhyāyera mana
> prabhu tāṅre kahila,---'kaha kṛṣṇera varṇana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:u:upādhyāyera]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Upādhyāya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---spoke; [[synonyms:k:kaha]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---just try to describe Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Raghupati Upādhyāya was very pleased to hear the Lord's blessings. The Lord then asked him to describe Kṛṣṇa.**
