====== CC Madhya 19.245 ======

===== Text =====

> rātre teṅho svapna dekhe,---prabhu āilā ghare
> prātaḥ-kāle āsi' rahe grāmera bāhire

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he (Candraśekhara); [[synonyms:s:svapna]]---a dream; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---saw; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---has come; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his home; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---he remained; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]] [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside the city.

===== Translation =====

**In a dream Candraśekhara had seen that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had come to his home; therefore in the morning Candraśekhara went outside the city to receive the Lord.**
