====== CC Madhya 19.52 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-kṛpā pāñā duṅhe dui hāta yuḍi'
> dīna hañā stuti kare vinaya ācari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the Lord's mercy; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:h:hāta]]---hands; [[synonyms:y:yuḍi']]---folding; [[synonyms:d:dīna]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---most humbly; [[synonyms:s:stuti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offer prayers; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:ā:ācari']]---with submission.

===== Translation =====

**After receiving the Lord's causeless mercy, the brothers folded their hands and in great humility offered the following prayers unto the Lord.**
