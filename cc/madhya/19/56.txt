====== CC Madhya 19.56 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpa kahena,---teṅho bandī haya rāja-ghare
> tumi yadi uddhāra', tabe ha-ibe uddhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bandī]]---arrested; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the court of the government; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:u:uddhāra']]---kindly rescue; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---he will be; [[synonyms:u:uddhāre]]---relieved from that entanglement.

===== Translation =====

**Rūpa Gosvāmī replied, "Sanātana has now been arrested by the government of Hussain Shah. If You kindly save him, he can be liberated from that entanglement."**
