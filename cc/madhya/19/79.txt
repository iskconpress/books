====== Cc. Madhya 19.79 ======

===== Text =====

> huṅkāra kari' yamunāra jale dilā jhāṅpa
> prabhu dekhi' sabāra mane haila bhaya-kāṅpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making a loud sound; [[synonyms:y:yamunāra]] [[synonyms:j:jale]]---in the water of the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:j:jhāṅpa]]---a plunge; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāṅpa]]---fear and trembling.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, as soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the River Yamunā, He immediately made a great sound and jumped into the water. Everyone was filled with fear and trembling to see this.**
