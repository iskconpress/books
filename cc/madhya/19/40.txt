====== CC Madhya 19.40 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-yamunā prayāga nārila ḍubāite
> prabhu ḍubāila kṛṣṇa-premera vanyāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:y:yamunā]]---the River Ganges and River Yamunā; [[synonyms:p:prayāga]]---Prayāga; [[synonyms:n:nārila]]---were not able; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubāite]]---to flood; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubāila]]---flooded; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:premera]]---of ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vanyāte]]---in an inundation.

===== Translation =====

**Prayāga is located at the confluence of two rivers---the Ganges and the Yamunā. Although these rivers were not able to flood Prayāga with water, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inundated the whole area with waves of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
