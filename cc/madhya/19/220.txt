====== CC Madhya 19.220 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara-jñāna, sambhrama-gaurava pracura
> 'sevā' kari' kṛṣṇe sukha dena nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge of the supreme controller; [[synonyms:s:sambhrama]]-[[synonyms:g:gaurava]]---awe and veneration; [[synonyms:p:pracura]]---abundant; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly.

===== Translation =====

**"On the dāsya-rasa platform, knowledge of the Supreme Personality of Godhead is revealed with awe and veneration. By rendering service unto Lord Kṛṣṇa, the devotee in dāsya-rasa gives constant happiness to the Lord.**
