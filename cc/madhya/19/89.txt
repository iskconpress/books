====== CC Madhya 19.89 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya śrī-rūpe deoyāila 'avaśeṣa'
> tabe sei prasāda kṛṣṇadāsa pāila śeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---to Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:deoyāila]]---offered; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]]---the remnants; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---the balance.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya first offered the remnants of the Lord's food to Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and then to Kṛṣṇadāsa.**
