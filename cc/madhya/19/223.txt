====== Cc. Madhya 19.223 ======

===== Text =====

> kāndhe caḍe, kāndhe caḍāya, kare krīḍā-raṇa
> kṛṣṇe seve, kṛṣṇe karāya āpana-sevana!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāndhe]]---on the shoulders; [[synonyms:c:caḍe]]---gets up; [[synonyms:k:kāndhe]] [[synonyms:c:caḍāya]]---sometimes takes on his own shoulders; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṇa]]---mock fighting; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]] [[synonyms:s:seve]]---serves Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---causes; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:s:sevana]]---his own service.

===== Translation =====

**"On the sakhya-rasa platform, the devotee sometimes offers the Lord service and sometimes makes Kṛṣṇa serve him in exchange. In their mock fighting, the cowherd boys would sometimes climb on Kṛṣṇa's shoulders, and sometimes they would make Kṛṣṇa climb on their shoulders.**
