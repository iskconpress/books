====== CC Madhya 19.99 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena,---kaha, teṅho paḍe kṛṣṇa-līlā
> premāveśe prabhura deha-mana āyuyāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please go on speaking; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---body and mind; [[synonyms:ā:āyuyāilā]]---became slackened.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested Raghupati Upādhyāya to continue speaking about the pastimes of Śrī Kṛṣṇa. Thus the Lord was absorbed in ecstatic love, and His mind and body slackened.**

===== Purport =====

Our minds and bodies are always engaged in material activities. When they are activated on the spiritual platform, they slacken on the material platform.
