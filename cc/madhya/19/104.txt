====== Cc. Madhya 19.104 ======

===== Text =====

> rasa-gaṇa-madhye tumi śreṣṭha māna' kāya?
> 'ādya eva paro rasaḥ'---kahe upādhyāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among all the mellows; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---as supreme; [[synonyms:m:māna']]---accept; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]---which one; [[synonyms:ā:ādyaḥ]]---conjugal love; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:paraḥ]] [[synonyms:r:rasaḥ]]---the best of all mellows; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---replied; [[synonyms:u:upādhyāya]]---Raghupati Upādhyāya.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked, "Among all the mellows, which do you consider best?" Raghupati Upādhyāya replied, "The mellow of conjugal love is supermost."**
