====== CC Madhya 19.123 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura yata baḍa baḍa bhakta mātra
> rūpa-sanātana---sabāra kṛpā-gaurava-pātra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---great, great; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---up to; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrīla Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---of the mercy; [[synonyms:g:gaurava]]---and honor; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---objects.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī were the objects of love and honor for all the great stalwart devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
