====== CC Madhya 19.248 ======

===== Text =====

> nija ghare lañā prabhure bhikṣā karāila
> bhaṭṭācārye candraśekhara nimantraṇa kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his own place; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhiksā]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---offered lunch; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārye]]---unto Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:c:candraśekhara]]---Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**Tapana Miśra took Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his own house and gave Him lunch. Candraśekhara invited Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya to take lunch at his home.**
