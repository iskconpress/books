====== Cc. Madhya 19.173 ======

===== Text =====

> sālokya-sārṣṭi-sāmīpya-
> sārūpyaikatvam apy uta
> dīyamānaṁ na gṛhṇanti
> vinā mat-sevanaṁ janāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sālokya]]---being on the same planet as Me; [[synonyms:s:sārṣṭi]]---having opulence equal to Mine; [[synonyms:s:sāmīpya]]---having direct association with Me; [[synonyms:s:sārūpya]]---having the same form as Me; [[synonyms:e:ekatvam]]---oneness with Me; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:u:uta]]---or; [[synonyms:d:dīyamānam]]---being given; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:g:gṛhṇanti]]---accept; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:s:sevanam]]---My service; [[synonyms:j:janāḥ]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'My devotees do not accept sālokya, sārṣṭi, sārūpya, sāmīpya or oneness with Me-even if I offer these liberations-in preference to serving Me.**
