====== CC Madhya 19.63 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-kathāya prabhura mahā-prema uthalila
> bhaṭṭera saṅkoce prabhu samvaraṇa kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathāya]]---in the discussion on Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---great love; [[synonyms:u:uthalila]]---arose; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:saṅkoce]]---due to shyness; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:samvaraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---restrained Himself.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt great ecstatic love when they began discussing Kṛṣṇa, but the Lord checked His feelings because He felt shy before Vallabha Bhaṭṭa.**
