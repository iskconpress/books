====== CC Madhya 19.73 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' mahāprabhu tāṅre bahu praśaṁśilā
> premāviṣṭa hañā śloka paḍite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---very much; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁśilā]]---praised; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming ecstatic in love of Godhead; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to recite.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very pleased to hear Vallabha Bhaṭṭa quoting from śāstra about the position of a devotee. The Lord praised him personally, and, feeling ecstatic love of Godhead, began to quote many verses from śāstra.**
