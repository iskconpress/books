====== Cc. Madhya 19.62 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho daṇḍavat kaila, prabhu kailā āliṅgana
> dui jane kṛṣṇa-kathā haila kata-kṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---between the two of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics about Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there were; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu his obeisances, and the Lord embraced him. After that, they discussed topics about Kṛṣṇa for some time.**
