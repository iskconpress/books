====== Cc. Madhya 19.39 ======

===== Text =====

> keha kānde, keha hāse, keha nāce, gāya
> 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa' bali' keha gaḍāgaḍi yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---some cried; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---some laughed; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---some danced; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chanted; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying Kṛṣṇa, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---some; [[synonyms:g:gaḍāgaḍi]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---rolled on the ground.

===== Translation =====

**Some of the people following the Lord were crying. Some were laughing, some dancing and some chanting. Indeed, some of them were rolling on the ground, exclaiming, "Kṛṣṇa! Kṛṣṇa!"**
