====== Cc. Madhya 19.97 ======

===== Text =====

> 'āge kaha'---prabhu-vākye upādhyāya kahila
> raghupati upādhyāya namaskāra kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please speak further; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]]---on the request of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:upādhyāya]]---Raghupati Upādhyāya; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:r:raghupati]] [[synonyms:u:upādhyāya]]---Raghupati Upādhyāya; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghupati Upādhyāya was requested by the Lord to recite more, he immediately offered his respects to the Lord and granted His request.**
