====== Cc. Madhya 19.31 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei dui cara rūpa-ṭhāñi āila
> 'vṛndāvana calilā prabhu'---āsiyā kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:c:cara]]---messengers; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to the presence of Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came back; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has departed for Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---they informed.

===== Translation =====

**The two persons who went to Jagannātha Purī to inquire about the Lord's departure returned and informed Rūpa Gosvāmī that the Lord had already departed for Vṛndāvana.**
