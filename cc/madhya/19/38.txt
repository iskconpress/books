====== CC Madhya 19.38 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu caliyāchena bindu-mādhava-daraśane
> lakṣa lakṣa loka āise prabhura milane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caliyāchena]]---was going; [[synonyms:b:bindu]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see Lord Bindu Mādhava; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---many hundreds of thousands of people; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milane]]---for meeting.

===== Translation =====

**At Prayāga, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to see the temple of Bindu Mādhava, and many hundreds of thousands of people followed Him just to meet Him.**
