====== CC Madhya 19.45 ======

===== Text =====

> vipra-gṛhe āsi' prabhu nibhṛte vasilā
> śrī-rūpa-vallabha duṅhe āsiyā mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to the house of that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vallabha]]---the two brothers Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Vallabha; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met Him.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was sitting in a solitary place in the home of that Deccan brāhmaṇa, Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Vallabha [Anupama Mallika] came to meet Him.**
