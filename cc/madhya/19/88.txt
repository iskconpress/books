====== CC Madhya 19.88 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā karāila prabhure sasneha yatane
> rūpa-gosāñi dui-bhāiye karāila bhojane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made take His lunch; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sasneha]]---with affection; [[synonyms:y:yatane]]---with great care; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāiye]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:k:karāila]] [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---made eat.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was offered lunch with great care and affection. The brothers Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Vallabha were also offered food.**
