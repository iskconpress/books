====== CC Madhya 19.192 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ kṛṣṇa-rati haya duita prakāra
> aiśvarya-jñāna-miśrā, kevalā-bheda āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rati]]---attachment for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:d:duita]]---twofold; [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---varieties; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:m:miśrā]]---knowledge of Kṛṣṇa mixed with a reverential attitude; [[synonyms:k:kevalā]]---pure attachment; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---division; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other.

===== Translation =====

**"Attachment for Kṛṣṇa is divided into two categories. One is attachment with awe and reverence, and the other is pure attachment without reverence.**
