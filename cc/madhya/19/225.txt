====== CC Madhya 19.225 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mamatā' adhika, kṛṣṇe ātma-sama jñāna
> ataeva sakhya-rasera vaśa bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mamatā]]---intimacy; [[synonyms:a:adhika]]---increase; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---the notion of equality; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---by the mellow of fraternity; [[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---subjected; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"On the platform of sakhya-rasa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead is obliged to the devotees who are intimate with Kṛṣṇa and think themselves equal to Him.**
