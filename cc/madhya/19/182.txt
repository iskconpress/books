====== CC Madhya 19.182 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche dadhi, sitā, ghṛta, marīca, karpūra
> milane,rasālā' haya amṛta madhura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:s:sitā]]---sugar candy; [[synonyms:g:ghṛta]]---clarified butter; [[synonyms:m:marīca]]---black pepper; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---camphor; [[synonyms:m:milane]]---in mixing together; [[synonyms:r:rasālā]]---very tasteful; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---nectarean; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---and sweet.

===== Translation =====

**"These tastes are like a combination of yogurt, sugar candy, ghee [clarified butter], black pepper and camphor and are as palatable as sweet nectar.**
