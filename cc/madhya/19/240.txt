====== CC Madhya 19.240 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---tomāra kartavya, āmāra vacana
> nikaṭe āsiyācha tumi, yāha vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:k:kartavya]]---your duty; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---My order; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyācha]]---have come near; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---go; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Your duty is to carry out My order. You have come near Vṛndāvana. Now you should go there.**
