====== Cc. Madhya 19.34 ======

===== Text =====

> daśa-sahasra mudrā tathā āche mudi-sthāne
> tāhā diyā kara śīghra ātma-vimocane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahasra]] [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---ten thousand coins; [[synonyms:t:tāthā]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:m:mudi]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in the grocer's place; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---with this amount; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---get; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---as soon as possible; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:v:vimocane]]---release from the internment.

===== Translation =====

**Rūpa Gosvāmī further informed Śrīla Sanātana Gosvāmī: "I have left a deposit of ten thousand coins with the grocer. Use that money to get out of prison.**
