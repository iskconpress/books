====== CC Madhya 19.67 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭa milibāre yāya, duṅhe palāya dūre
> 'aspṛśya pāmara muñi, nā chuṅiha more'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---to meet; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:p:palāya]]---ran away; [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---to a distant place; [[synonyms:a:aspṛśya]]---untouchable; [[synonyms:p:pāmara]]---most fallen; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:chuṅiha]]---do not touch; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me.

===== Translation =====

**When Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya walked toward them, they ran away to a more distant place. Rūpa Gosvāmī said, "I am untouchable and most sinful. Please do not touch me."**
