====== CC Madhya 19.58 ======

===== Text =====

> madhyāhna karite vipra prabhure kahilā
> rūpa-gosāñi se-divasa tathāñi rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to accept lunch; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa// of Deccan; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---requested; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:se]]-[[synonyms:d:divasa]]---that day; [[synonyms:t:tathāñi]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was then requested by the brāhmaṇa to accept His lunch. Rūpa Gosvāmī also remained there that day.**
