====== CC Madhya 19.36 ======

===== Text =====

> anupama mallika, tāṅra nāma---'śrī-vallabha'
> rūpa-gosāñira choṭa-bhāi---parama-vaiṣṇava

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anupama]] [[synonyms:m:mallika]]---Anupama Mallika; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---his name; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vallabha]]---Śrī Vallabha; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---younger brother; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---great devotee.

===== Translation =====

**Rūpa Gosvāmī's younger brother was a great devotee whose actual name was Śrī Vallabha, but he was given the name Anupama Mallika.**
