====== CC Madhya 19.112 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅra icchā, prayāge yāñā karibe nimantraṇa'
> eta bali' prabhu lañā karila gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---there is a desire; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]] [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going to Prayāga; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---may do; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitations; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---he departed for Prayāga.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭa said, "If anyone likes, he can go to Prayāga and extend invitations to the Lord." In this way he took the Lord with him and departed for Prayāga.**
