====== CC Madhya 19.236 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāvite bhāvite kṛṣṇa sphuraye antare
> kṛṣṇa-kṛpāya ajña pāya rasa-sindhu-pāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāvite]] [[synonyms:b:bhāvite]]---in this way when one is strictly in thought; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sphuraye]] [[synonyms:a:antare]]---manifests within; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:ajña]]---one not expert in knowledge; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---reaches; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāre]]---the shore of the ocean of transcendental mellows.

===== Translation =====

**"When one thinks of Kṛṣṇa constantly, love for Him is manifest within the heart. Even though one may be ignorant, one can reach the shore of the ocean of transcendental love by Lord Kṛṣṇa's mercy."**
