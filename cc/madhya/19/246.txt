====== CC Madhya 19.246 ======

===== Text =====

> ācambite prabhu dekhi' caraṇe paḍilā
> ānandita hañā nija-gṛhe lañā gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at His feet; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---he fell; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming very glad; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to his own place; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**While Candraśekhara was waiting outside the city, he suddenly saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrive, and he fell down at the Lord's feet. Being very happy, he took the Lord to his home.**
