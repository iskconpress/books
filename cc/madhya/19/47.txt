====== Cc. Madhya 19.47 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā śloka paḍi' uṭhe, paḍe bāra bāra
> prabhu dekhi' premāveśa ha-ila duṅhara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---stood up; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell down; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them.

===== Translation =====

**Both brothers were overwhelmed with ecstatic emotion, and reciting various Sanskrit verses, they stood up and fell down again and again.**
