====== CC Madhya 19.77 ======

===== Text =====

> sagaṇe prabhure bhaṭṭa naukāte caḍāñā
> bhikṣā dite nija-ghare calilā lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---with His associates; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:n:naukāte]]---a boat; [[synonyms:c:caḍāñā]]---putting aboard; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to offer lunch; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his own place; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya then put Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His associates aboard a boat and took them to his own place to offer them lunch.**
