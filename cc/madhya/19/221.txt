====== CC Madhya 19.221 ======

===== Text =====

> śāntera guṇa dāsye āche, adhika---'sevana'
> ataeva dāsya-rasera ei 'dui' guṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāntera]]---of the platform of //śānta-rasa//; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the qualities; [[synonyms:d:dāsye]]---on the platform of servitude; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---are; [[synonyms:a:adhika]]---additional; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---serving; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of the platform of //dāsya-rasa//; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---these two qualities (namely //śānta// and //dāsya//).

===== Translation =====

**"The qualities of śānta-rasa are also present in dāsya-rasa, but service is added. Thus the dāsya-rasa platform contains the qualities of both śānta-rasa and dāsya-rasa.**
