====== Cc. Madhya 19.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe tāṅre bāndhi' rākhi' karilā gamana
> ethā nīlācala haite prabhu calilā vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:b:bāndhi']]---arresting; [[synonyms:r:rākhi']]---keeping; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---he went away; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---at this time; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---departed for Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**The Nawab again arrested Sanātana Gosvāmī and kept him in prison. At this time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed for Vṛndāvana from Jagannātha Purī.**
