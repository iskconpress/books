====== Cc. Madhya 19.26 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana kahe,---tumi svatantra gauḍeśvara
> ye yei doṣa kare, deha' tāra phala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the ruler of Bengal; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---faults; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---one commits; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---you award; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:p:phala]]---the results of that.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "You are the supreme ruler of Bengal and are completely independent. Whenever someone commits a fault, you punish him accordingly."**
