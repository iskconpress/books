====== CC Madhya 19.41 ======

===== Text =====

> bhiḍa dekhi' dui bhāi rahilā nirjane
> prabhura āveśa haila mādhava-daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhiḍa]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the crowd; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:n:nirjane]]---in a secluded place; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---by seeing the Deity, Bindu Mādhava.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the great crowd, the two brothers remained standing in a secluded place. They could see that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was ecstatic to see Lord Bindu Mādhava.**
