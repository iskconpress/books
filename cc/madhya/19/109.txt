====== CC Madhya 19.109 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu dekhibāre grāmera saba-loka āila
> prabhu-daraśane sabe 'kṛṣṇa-bhakta' ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---of the village; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---simply by seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became devotees of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had arrived, all the villagers went to see Him. Simply by seeing Him, they all became devotees of Kṛṣṇa.**
