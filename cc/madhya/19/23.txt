====== Cc. Madhya 19.23 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana kahe,---nahe āmā haite kāma
> āra eka-jana diyā kara samādhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī replied; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---me; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---execution of the duty; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---someone else; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---by means of; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---execute the management.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī replied, "You can no longer expect any service from me. Please arrange for someone else to tend to the management."**
