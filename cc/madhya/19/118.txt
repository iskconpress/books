====== Cc. Madhya 19.118 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda-senera putra 'kavi-karṇapūra'
> 'rūpera milana' sva-granthe likhiyāchena pracura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:senera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---the son; [[synonyms:k:kavi]]-[[synonyms:k:karṇapūra]]---Kavi-karṇapūra; [[synonyms:r:rūpera]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:granthe]]---in his own book; [[synonyms:l:likhiyāchena]] [[synonyms:p:pracura]]---has written profusely.

===== Translation =====

**In his book Caitanya-candrodaya, Kavi-karṇapūra, the son of Śivānanda Sena, has elaborately described the meeting between Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
