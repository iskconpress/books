====== Cc. Madhya 19.46 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-guccha tṛṇa duṅhe daśane dhariyā
> prabhu dekhi' dūre paḍe daṇḍavat hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:g:guccha]]---two bunches; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]---straw; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:d:daśane]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---holding in the teeth; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---in a distant place; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell down; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like rods; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the Lord from a distance, the two brothers put two clumps of straw between their teeth and immediately fell down on the ground like rods, offering Him obeisances.**
