====== CC Madhya 19.181 ======

===== Text =====

> sāttvika-vyabhicāri-bhāvera milane
> kṛṣṇa-bhakti-rasa haya amṛta āsvādane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāttvika]]-[[synonyms:v:vyabhicāri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of //sāttvika// and //vyabhicārī// with //sthāyibhāva//; [[synonyms:m:milane]]---by mixing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the transcendental mellows of devotional service to the Lord; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---nectarean; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādane]]---in tasting.

===== Translation =====

**"When the higher standard of ecstatic love is mixed with the symptoms of sāttvika and vyabhicārī, the devotee relishes the transcendental bliss of loving Kṛṣṇa in a variety of nectarean tastes.**
