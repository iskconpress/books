====== CC Madhya 19.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rūpa-sanātana rahe rāmakeli-grāme
> prabhure miliyā gelā āpana-bhavane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---the brothers named Rūpa and Sanātana; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---stayed; [[synonyms:r:rāmakeli]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in Rāmakeli; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---meeting; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went back; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavane]]---to their own homes.

===== Translation =====

**After meeting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in the village of Rāmakeli, the brothers Rūpa and Sanātana returned to their homes.**
