====== CC Madhya 19.256 ======

===== Text =====

> śraddhā kari' ei kathā śune ye jane
> prema-bhakti pāya sei caitanya-caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with faith; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---this description; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---any person who; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---achieves; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever hears this narration with faith and love certainly develops love of God at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
