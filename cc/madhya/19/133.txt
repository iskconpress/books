====== CC Madhya 19.133 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyera kṛpā rūpa likhiyāchena āpane
> rasāmṛta-sindhu-granthera maṅgalācaraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:l:likhiyāchena]]---has written; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:r:rasāmṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:g:granthera]]---of the book known as //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu//; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇe]]---in the auspicious introduction.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī has personally spoken about the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in his auspicious introduction to his book Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu [1.1.2].**
