====== Cc. Madhya 19.70 ======

===== Text =====

> duṅhāra mukhe nirantara kṛṣṇa-nāma śuni'
> bhaṭṭa kahe, prabhura kichu iṅgita-bhaṅgī jāni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouths of both Rūpa Gosvāmī and his brother Vallabha; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---continuously; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing the chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:i:iṅgita]]---indications; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgī]]---hints; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---understanding.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing the holy name constantly vibrated by the two brothers, Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya could understand the hints of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
