====== CC Madhya 19.11 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpa-gosāñi nīlācale pāṭhāila dui-jana
> prabhu yabe vṛndāvana karena gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---makes; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī sent two people to Jagannātha Purī to find out when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would depart for Vṛndāvana.**
