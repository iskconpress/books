====== Cc. Madhya 19.124 ======

===== Text =====

> keha yadi deśe yāya dekhi' vṛndāvana
> tāṅre praśna karena prabhura pāriṣada-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to his country; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto that person; [[synonyms:p:praśna]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---put questions; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---personal associates.

===== Translation =====

**If someone returned to his country after seeing Vṛndāvana, the associates of the Lord would ask him questions.**
