====== CC Madhya 19.59 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya dui bhāiye nimantraṇa kaila
> prabhura śeṣa prasāda-pātra dui-bhāi pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāiye]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---invited to take lunch; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the remnants of the plate of food offered to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāi]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---the two brothers obtained.

===== Translation =====

**Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya invited the two brothers to take lunch also. The remnants of food from the plate of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were offered to them.**
