====== Cc. Madhya 19.83 ======

===== Text =====

> deśa-pātra dekhi' mahāprabhu dhairya ha-ila
> āḍāilera ghāṭe naukā āsi' uttarila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deśa]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the circumstances; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhairya]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became calm; [[synonyms:ā:āḍāilera]] [[synonyms:g:ghāṭe]]---at the shore of the village Āḍāila; [[synonyms:n:naukā]]---the boat; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:u:uttarila]]---landed.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the circumstances, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu finally became calm so that the boat was able to reach the shore of Āḍāila and land there.**
