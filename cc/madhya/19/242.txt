====== Cc. Madhya 19.242 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅre āliṅgiyā prabhu naukāte caḍilā
> mūrcchita hañā teṅho tāhāñi paḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:naukāte]]---in a boat; [[synonyms:c:caḍilā]]---got aboard; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---fainting; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he (Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī); [[synonyms:t:tāhāñi]]---on the spot; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell.

===== Translation =====

**After embracing Rūpa Gosvāmī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got into a boat. Rūpa Gosvāmī fainted and fell down on the spot.**
