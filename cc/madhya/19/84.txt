====== CC Madhya 19.84 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaye bhaṭṭa saṅge rahe, madhyāhna karāñā
> nija-gṛhe ānilā prabhure saṅgete lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---with fear; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's association; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---after arranging for His bath; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to his own home; [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saṅgete]]---in company; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Fearing for the Lord's welfare, Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya stayed in His association. After arranging for the Lord's bath, he took Him to his own house.**
