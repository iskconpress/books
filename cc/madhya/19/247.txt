====== CC Madhya 19.247 ======

===== Text =====

> tapana-miśra śuni' āsi' prabhure mililā
> iṣṭa-goṣṭhī kari' prabhura nimantraṇa kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tapana]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met the Lord; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---conversing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**Tapana Miśra also heard news of the Lord's arrival in Vārāṇasī, and he went to Candraśekhara's house to meet Him. After talking, he invited the Lord to take lunch at his place.**
