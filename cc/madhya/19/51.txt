====== CC Madhya 19.51 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloka paḍi' duṅhāre kailā āliṅgana
> kṛpāte duṅhāra māthāya dharilā caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāre]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embraced; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---out of causeless mercy; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:m:māthāya]]---on the heads; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---placed; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---His feet.

===== Translation =====

**After reciting this verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced both brothers, and out of His causeless mercy He placed His feet on their heads.**
