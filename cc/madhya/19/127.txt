====== CC Madhya 19.127 ======

===== Text =====

> "aniketa duṅhe, vane yata vṛkṣa-gaṇa
> eka eka vṛkṣera tale eka eka rātri śayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aniketa]]---without a residence; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---as many trees as there are; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣera]]---of one tree after another; [[synonyms:t:tale]]---at the base; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---one night after another; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down to sleep.

===== Translation =====

**"The brothers actually have no fixed residence. They reside beneath trees-one night under one tree and the next night under another.**
