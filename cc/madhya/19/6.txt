====== Cc. Madhya 19.6 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rūpa-gosāñi tabe naukāte bhariyā
> śrī-rūpa-gosāñi āpanāra ghare āilā bahu-dhana lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:n:naukāte]] [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---filling boats; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his own house; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking large amounts of riches.

===== Translation =====

**At this time, Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī returned home, taking with him large quantities of riches loaded in boats.**
