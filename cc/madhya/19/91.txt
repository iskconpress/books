====== Cc. Madhya 19.91 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu pāṭhāila tāṅre karite bhojane
> bhojana kari' āilā teṅho prabhura caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him (Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya); [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---to take his lunch; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after taking lunch; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---to the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**While Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya was massaging Him, the Lord asked him to go take prasāda. After taking prasāda, he returned to the lotus feet of the Lord.**
