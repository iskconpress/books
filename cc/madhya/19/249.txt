====== CC Madhya 19.249 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā karāñā miśra kahe prabhu-pāya dhari'
> eka bhikṣā māgi, more deha' kṛpā kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---after offering the lunch; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---touching; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---one favor; [[synonyms:m:māgi]]---I beg; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---kindly deliver; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by Your causeless mercy.

===== Translation =====

**After offering lunch to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Tapana Miśra begged a favor from the Lord and requested Him to reward him mercy.**
