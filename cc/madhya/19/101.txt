====== CC Madhya 19.101 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---upādhyāya, śreṣṭha māna' kāya?
> 'śyāmam eva paraṁ rūpaṁ'---kahe upādhyāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired; [[synonyms:u:upādhyāya]]---My dear Upādhyāya; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---the supermost; [[synonyms:m:māna']]---you consider; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]---what; [[synonyms:ś:śyāmam]]---Śyāmasundara, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:param]] [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---the supreme form; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---replied; [[synonyms:u:upādhyāya]]---Raghupati Upādhyāya.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked Raghupati Upādhyāya, "According to your decision, who is the foremost being?" Raghupati Upādhyāya replied, "Lord Śyāmasundara is the supreme form."**
