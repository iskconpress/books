====== CC Madhya 19.44 ======

===== Text =====

> dākṣiṇātya-vipra-sane āche paricaya
> sei vipra nimantriyā nila nijālaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dākṣiṇātya]]---Deccan; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:paricaya]]---acquaintance; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:n:nimantriyā]]---inviting; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---brought; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaya]]---to his own place.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had made an acquaintance with a brāhmaṇa from Deccan [in South India], and that brāhmaṇa invited Him for meals and took Him to his place.**
