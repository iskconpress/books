====== CC Madhya 19.237 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu tāṅre kailā āliṅgana
> vārāṇasī calibāre prabhura haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]---toward Benares; [[synonyms:c:calibāre]]---to go; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī. The Lord then decided to go to the city of Benares.**
