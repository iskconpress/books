====== CC Madhya 19.251 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu jānena---dina pāṅca-sāta se rahiba
> sannyāsīra saṅge bhikṣā kāhāṅ nā kariba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---knows; [[synonyms:d:dina]]---days; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five days or at the most a week; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:r:rahiba]]---I shall stay; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with other Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---lunch; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall not take.

===== Translation =====

**It was known to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that He would remain there only five or seven days. He would not accept any invitation that involved Māyāvādī sannyāsīs.**
