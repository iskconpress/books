====== CC Madhya 19.113 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-pathe mahāprabhure naukāte vasāñā
> prayāge āilā bhaṭṭa gosāñire lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the Ganges; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:naukāte]] [[synonyms:v:vasāñā]]---making to sit down on the boat; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went to Prayāga; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:g:gosāñire]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya avoided the River Yamunā. Putting the Lord on a boat in the River Ganges, he went with Him to Prayāga.**
