====== Cc. Madhya 19.98 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṁ prati kathayitum īśe
> samprati ko vā pratītim āyātu
> go-pati-tanayā-kuñje
> gopa-vadhūṭī-viṭaṁ brahma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kam]] [[synonyms:p:prati]]---unto whom; [[synonyms:k:kathayitum]]---to speak; [[synonyms:ī:īśe]]---am I able; [[synonyms:s:samprati]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:p:pratītim]]---belief; [[synonyms:ā:āyātu]]---would do; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:p:pati]]---of the sun-god; [[synonyms:t:tanayā]]---of the daughter (the Yamunā); [[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in the bushes on the bank; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhūṭī]]---of the cowherd girls; [[synonyms:v:viṭam]]---the hunter; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"To whom can I speak who will believe me when I say that Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, is hunting the gopīs in the bushes by the banks of the River Yamunā? In this way the Lord demonstrates His pastimes."**

===== Purport =====

This verse was also later included in //padyāvalī// (98).
