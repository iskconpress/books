====== Cc. Madhya 19.66 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-bhāi dūra haite bhūmite paḍiyā
> bhaṭṭe daṇḍavat kailā ati dīna hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from a distance; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---falling flat; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭe]]---to Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:a:ati]] [[synonyms:d:dīna]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very humble.

===== Translation =====

**From a distance, the brothers Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Vallabha fell on the ground and offered obeisances to Vallabha Bhaṭṭa with great humility.**
