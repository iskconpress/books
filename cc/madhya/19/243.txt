====== Cc. Madhya 19.243 ======

===== Text =====

> dākṣiṇātya-vipra tāṅre ghare lañā gelā
> tabe dui bhāi vṛndāvanere calilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dākṣiṇātya]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa// from Deccan; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him (Rūpa Gosvāmī); [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking to his home; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanere]]---toward Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa from Deccan took Rūpa Gosvāmī to his home, and thereafter the two brothers departed for Vṛndāvana.**
