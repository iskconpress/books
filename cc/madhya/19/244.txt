====== CC Madhya 19.244 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu cali' cali' āilā vārāṇasī
> candraśekhara mililā grāmera bāhire āsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cali']] [[synonyms:c:cali']]---walking and walking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]---at Vārāṇasī; [[synonyms:c:candraśekhara]]---Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---He met; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---of the village; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**After walking and walking, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu finally arrived at Vārāṇasī, where He met Candraśekhara, who was coming out of the city.**
