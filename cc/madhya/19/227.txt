====== Cc. Madhya 19.227 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhyera guṇa---'asaṅkoca', 'agaurava' sāra
> mamatādhikye tāḍana-bhartsana-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhyera]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the quality of fraternity; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkoca]]---without any formality; [[synonyms:a:agaurava]]---without any veneration; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence; [[synonyms:m:mamatā]]-[[synonyms:a:adhikye]]---on account of greater intimacy; [[synonyms:t:tāḍana]]---of chastisement; [[synonyms:b:bhartsana]]---of rebuking; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---behavior.

===== Translation =====

**"The essence of fraternal love is intimacy devoid of the formality and veneration found in the dāsya-rasa. Due to a greater sense of intimacy, the devotee functioning in paternal love chastises and rebukes the Lord in an ordinary way.**
