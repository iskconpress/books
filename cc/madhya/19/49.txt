====== CC Madhya 19.49 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera karuṇā kichu nā yāya varṇane
> viṣaya-kūpa haite kāḍila tomā dui-jane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karuṇā]]---the mercy; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is possible; [[synonyms:v:varṇane]]---to describe; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]-[[synonyms:k:kūpa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the well of material enjoyment; [[synonyms:k:kāḍila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---you; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---both.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "It is not possible to describe Kṛṣṇa's mercy, for He has delivered you both from the well of material enjoyment.**
