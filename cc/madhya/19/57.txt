====== CC Madhya 19.57 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---sanātanera hañāche mocana
> acirāt āmā-saha ha-ibe milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:h:hañāche]]---there has been; [[synonyms:m:mocana]]---release; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:ā:āmāsaha]]---with Me; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---there will be meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately replied, "Sanātana has already been released from his confinement, and he will very soon meet with Me."**
