====== CC Madhya 19.15 ======

===== Text =====

> asvāsthyera chadma kari' rahe nija-ghare
> rāja-kārya chāḍilā, nā yāya rāja-dvāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:asvāsthyera]]---of not being well; [[synonyms:c:chadma]]---pretext; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:k:kārya]]---government service; [[synonyms:c:chāḍilā]]---relinquished; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---did not go; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---to the court of the Nawab.

===== Translation =====

**On the pretext of bad health, Sanātana Gosvāmī remained home. Thus he gave up government service and did not go to the royal court.**
