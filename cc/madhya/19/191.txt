====== CC Madhya 19.191 ======

===== Text =====

> madhura-rase bhakta-mukhya---vraje gopī-gaṇa
> mahiṣī-gaṇa, lakṣmī-gaṇa, asaṅkhya gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---in the mellow of conjugal love; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---the chief devotees; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the queens in Dvārakā; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the goddesses of fortune in Vaikuṇṭha; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---of innumerable reckoning.

===== Translation =====

**"The chief devotees in conjugal love are the gopīs in Vṛndāvana, the queens in Dvārakā and the goddesses of fortune in Vaikuṇṭha. These devotees are innumerable.**
