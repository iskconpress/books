====== CC Madhya 19.64 ======

===== Text =====

> antare gara-gara prema, nahe samvaraṇa
> dekhi' camatkāra haila vallabha-bhaṭṭera mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:antare]]---inside; [[synonyms:g:gara]]-[[synonyms:g:gara]]---raged; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there was not; [[synonyms:s:samvaraṇa]]---checking; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---detecting; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---on the mind of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa.

===== Translation =====

**Although the Lord restrained Himself externally, ecstatic love raged within. There was no checking that. Vallabha Bhaṭṭa was astonished to detect this.**
