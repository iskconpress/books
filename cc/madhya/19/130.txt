====== Cc. Madhya 19.130 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭa-prahara kṛṣṇa-bhajana, cāri daṇḍa śayane
> nāma-saṅkīrtane seha nahe kona dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:prahara]]---twenty-four hours; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---worshiping Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---four //daṇḍas// (one //daṇḍa// equals twenty-four minutes); [[synonyms:ś:śayane]]---for sleeping; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---because of chanting the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that much time; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---some days.

===== Translation =====

**"They engage almost twenty-four hours daily in rendering service to the Lord. They usually sleep only an hour and a half, and some days, when they continuously chant the Lord's holy name, they do not sleep at all.**
