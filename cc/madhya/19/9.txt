====== CC Madhya 19.9 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍe rākhila mudrā daśa-hājāre
> sanātana vyaya kare, rākhe mudi-ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept; [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---coins; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:h:hājāre]]---ten thousand; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---his elder brother; [[synonyms:v:vyaya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---spent; [[synonyms:r:rākhe]]---deposited; [[synonyms:m:mudi]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the place of a local grocer.

===== Translation =====

**He deposited ten thousand coins, which were later spent by Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī, in the custody of a local Bengali grocer.**
