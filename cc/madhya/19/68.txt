====== CC Madhya 19.68 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭera vismaya haila, prabhura harṣa mana
> bhaṭṭere kahilā prabhu tāṅra vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was surprise; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]---very happy; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭere]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---said to Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description of Rūpa Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭācārya was very surprised at this. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, however, was very pleased, and He therefore spoke to him this description of Rūpa Gosvāmī.**
