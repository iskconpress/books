====== Cc. Madhya 19.87 ======

===== Text =====

> gandha-puṣpa-dhūpa-dīpe mahā-pūjā kaila
> bhaṭṭācārye mānya kari' pāka karāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gandha]]---scents; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]---flowers; [[synonyms:d:dhūpa]]---incense; [[synonyms:d:dīpe]]---by lamps; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pūjā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---he worshiped the Lord with great pomp; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārye]]---to Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mānya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering respect; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---engaging in cooking.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabhācārya worshiped the Lord with great pomp, offering scents, incense, flowers and lamps, and with great respect he induced Balabhadra Bhaṭṭa [the Lord's cook] to cook.**
