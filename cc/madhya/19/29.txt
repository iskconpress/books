====== CC Madhya 19.29 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho kahe,---yābe tumi devatāya duḥkha dite
> mora śakti nāhi, tomāra saṅge yāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī replied; [[synonyms:y:yābe]]---will go; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:d:devatāya]]---to the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give unhappiness; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---my power; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in company with you; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī replied, "You are going to Orissa to give pain to the Supreme Personality of Godhead. For this reason I am powerless to go with you."**
