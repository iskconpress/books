====== CC Madhya 19.139 ======

===== Text =====

> keśāgra-śateka-bhāga punaḥ śatāṁśa kari
> tāra sama sūkṣma jīvera 'svarūpa' vicāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keśa]]-[[synonyms:a:agra]]---from the tip of a hair; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one hundred; [[synonyms:b:bhāga]]---divisions; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---one hundred divisions; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---making; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal to that; [[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---very fine; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of the living entity; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the actual form; [[synonyms:v:vicāri]]---I consider.

===== Translation =====

**"The length and breadth of the living entity is described as one tenthousandth part of the tip of a hair. This is the original subtle nature of the living entity.**
