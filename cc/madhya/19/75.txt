====== Cc. Madhya 19.75 ======

===== Text =====

> bhagavad-bhakti-hīnasya
> jātiḥ śāstraṁ japas tapaḥ
> aprāṇasyeva dehasya
> maṇḍanaṁ loka-rañjanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:h:hīnasya]]---of a person devoid of devotional service to the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:j:jātiḥ]]---birth in a high caste; [[synonyms:ś:śāstram]]---knowledge in revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:j:japaḥ]]---pronunciation of //mantras//; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerities and penances; [[synonyms:a:aprāṇasya]]---which is dead; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like; [[synonyms:d:dehasya]]---of a body; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍanam]]---decoration; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---to the whims of people in general; [[synonyms:r:rañjanam]]---simply pleasing.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'For a person devoid of devotional service, birth in a great family or nation, knowledge of revealed scripture, performance of austerities and penance, and chanting of Vedic mantras are all like ornaments on a dead body. Such ornaments simply serve the concocted pleasures of the general populace.' "**
