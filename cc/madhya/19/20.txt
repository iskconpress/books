====== CC Madhya 19.20 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,---tomāra sthāne vaidya pāṭhāiluṅ
> vaidya kahe,---vyādhi nāhi, sustha ye dekhiluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Nawab said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to your place; [[synonyms:v:vaidya]]---a physician; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāiluṅ]]---I sent; [[synonyms:v:vaidya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the physician said; [[synonyms:v:vyādhi]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is no disease; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:stha]]---completely healthy; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dekhiluṅ]]---I have seen.

===== Translation =====

**The Nawab said, "I sent my physician to you, and he has reported that you are not diseased. As far as he could see, you are completely healthy.**
