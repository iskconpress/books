====== Cc. Madhya 9.43 ======

===== Text =====

> nija-nija-śāstrodgrāhe sabāi pracaṇḍa
> sarva mata duṣi' prabhu kare khaṇḍa khaṇḍa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:n:nija]]---their own; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---of the scripture; [[synonyms:u:udgrāhe]]---to establish the conclusion; [[synonyms:s:sabāi]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:pracaṇḍa]]---very powerful; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mata]]---opinions; [[synonyms:d:duṣi']]---condemning; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---breaking to pieces.

===== Translation =====

**All of these adherents of various scriptures were ready to present the conclusions of their respective scriptures, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu broke all their opinions to pieces and established His own cult of bhakti based on the Vedas, Vedānta, the Brahma-sūtra and the philosophy of acintya-bhedābheda-tattva.**
