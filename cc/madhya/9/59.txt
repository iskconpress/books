====== CC Madhya 9.59 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---sabe kaha 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa' 'hari'
> guru-karṇe kaha kṛṣṇa-nāma ucca kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---chant; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy names of Lord Kṛṣṇa and Hari; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:k:karṇe]]---near the ear of your spiritual master; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---chant; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:ucca]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---very loudly.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then replied to the Buddhist disciples, "You should all chant the names of Kṛṣṇa and Hari very loudly near the ear of your spiritual master.**
