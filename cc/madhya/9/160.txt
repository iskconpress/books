====== CC Madhya 9.160 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛpā kari' kahile more kṛṣṇera mahimā
> yāṅra rūpa-guṇaiśvaryera keha nā pāya sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing causeless mercy; [[synonyms:k:kahile]]---You have spoken; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvaryera]]---of forms, qualities and opulence; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**"Out of Your causeless mercy You have told me of the glories of Lord Kṛṣṇa. No one can reach the end of the opulence, qualities and forms of the Lord.**
