====== CC Madhya 9.107 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vipra mahāprabhura baḍa bhakta haila
> cāri māsa prabhu-saṅga kabhu nā chāḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seivipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---big; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---for four months; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---association of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---did not; [[synonyms:c:chāḍila]]---give up.

===== Translation =====

**That brāhmaṇa became a great devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and for four continuous months he did not give up the Lord's company.**
