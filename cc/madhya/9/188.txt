====== CC Madhya 9.188 ======

===== Text =====

> jagan-mātā mahā-lakṣmī sītā-ṭhākurāṇī
> rākṣase sparśila tāṅre,---ihā kāne śuni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of the universe; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the supreme goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:s:sītā]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---mother Sītā; [[synonyms:r:rākṣase]]---the demon Rāvaṇa; [[synonyms:s:sparśila]]---touched; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---her; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kāne]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---I have heard.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Sir, mother Sītā is the mother of the universe and the supreme goddess of fortune. She has been touched by the demon Rāvaṇa, and I am troubled upon hearing this news.**
