====== Cc. Madhya 9.90 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nāma vinā keha nāhi kahe āra
> sabe kṛṣṇa-bhakta haila,---loke camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speak; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---anything else; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa's devotees; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---the people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, they did not chant anything but the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, and all of them became Lord Kṛṣṇa's devotees. Thus the general populace was astonished.**
