====== CC Madhya 9.122 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣmī kene nā pāila, ihāra ki kāraṇa
> tapa kari' kaiche kṛṣṇa pāila śruti-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---did not; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---get; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---cause; [[synonyms:t:tapa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---undergoing severe austerities; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---how; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---attained; [[synonyms:ś:śruti]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---Vedic authorities.

===== Translation =====

**"But can you tell Me why the goddess of fortune, Lakṣmī, could not enter the rāsa dance? The authorities of Vedic knowledge could enter the dance and associate with Kṛṣṇa.**
