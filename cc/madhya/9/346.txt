====== CC Madhya 9.346 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha-daraśana premāveśe kaila
> kampa-sveda-pulakāśrute śarīra bhāsila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:a:aśrute]]---with tears; [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]---the whole body; [[synonyms:b:bhāsila]]---was inundated.

===== Translation =====

**Due to ecstatic love experienced upon visiting Lord Jagannātha, inundations of trembling, perspiration, tears and jubilation swept the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
