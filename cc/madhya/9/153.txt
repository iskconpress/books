====== Cc. Madhya 9.153 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nārāyaṇa, yaiche eka-i svarūpa
> gopī-lakṣmī-bheda nāhi haya eka-rūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa and Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---one; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---form; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---difference; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---one form.

===== Translation =====

**"There is no difference between Lord Kṛṣṇa and Lord Nārāyaṇa, for They are of the same form. Similarly, there is no difference between the gopīs and the goddess of fortune, for they also are of the same form.**
