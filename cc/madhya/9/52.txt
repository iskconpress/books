====== Cc. Madhya 9.52 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhuke vaiṣṇava jāni' bauddha ghare gela
> sakala bauddha mili' tabe kumantraṇā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing to be a Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:b:bauddha]]---the Buddhists; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---returned home; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:b:bauddha]]---all the Buddhists; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---coming together; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:ku]]-[[synonyms:m:mantraṇā]]---plot; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**The Buddhists could understand that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was a Vaiṣṇava, and they returned home very unhappy. Later, however, they began to plot against the Lord.**
