====== CC Madhya 9.128 ======

===== Text =====

> vraja-lokera bhāve pāiye tāṅhāra caraṇa
> tāṅre īśvara kari' nāhi jāne vraja-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the inhabitants of Goloka Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in the ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---one gets; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa's; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Person; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---do not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---know; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---the inhabitants of Vrajabhūmi.

===== Translation =====

**"By following in the footsteps of the inhabitants of the planet known as Vrajaloka or Goloka Vṛndāvana one can attain the shelter of the lotus feet of Śrī Kṛṣṇa. However, in that planet the inhabitants do not know that Lord Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
