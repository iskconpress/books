====== CC Madhya 9.177 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra saṅge mahāprabhu kari iṣṭagoṣṭhī
> tāṅra ājñā lañā āilā purī kāmakoṣṭhī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---discussing spiritual subject matter; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:purī]] [[synonyms:k:kāmakoṣṭhī]]---to Kāmakoṣṭhī-purī.

===== Translation =====

**After talking with Lord Śiva, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took his permission to leave and went to Kāmakoṣṭhī-purī.**
