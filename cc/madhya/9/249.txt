====== Cc. Madhya 9.249 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-mūrti dekhi' prabhu mahā-sukha pāila
> premāveśe bahu-kṣaṇa nṛtya-gīta kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the Deity of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---great happiness; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---dancing and singing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu received great pleasure in seeing this beautiful form of Gopāla. For a long time He danced and chanted in ecstatic love.**
