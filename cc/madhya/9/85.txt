====== Cc. Madhya 9.85 ======

===== Text =====

> cāturmāsye kṛpā kari' raha mora ghare
> kṛṣṇa-kathā kahi' kṛpāya uddhāra' āmāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāturmāsye]]---during this period of Cāturmāsya; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---please stay; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at my place; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---speaking; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by Your mercy; [[synonyms:u:uddhāra']] [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]]---kindly deliver me.

===== Translation =====

**Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa said, "Please be merciful to me and stay at my house during Cāturmāsya. Speak about Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes and kindly deliver me by Your mercy."**
