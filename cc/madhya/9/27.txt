====== Cc. Madhya 9.27 ======

===== Text =====

> sei haite kṛṣṇa-nāma jihvāte vasilā
> kṛṣṇa-nāma sphure, rāma-nāma dūre gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seihaite]]---since that time; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jihvāte]]---on the tongue; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---was seated tightly; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sphure]]---automatically comes; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---far away; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**"Since then, the holy name of Kṛṣṇa has been tightly fixed upon my tongue. Indeed, since I have been chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa, the holy name of Lord Rāmacandra has gone far away.**
