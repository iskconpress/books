====== Cc. Madhya 9.18 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha dekhi' kaila praṇati stavana
> tāhāṅ eka vipra prabhura kaila nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghu]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing Lord Rāmacandra, the descendant of Mahārāja Raghu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered; [[synonyms:p:praṇati]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---prayers; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the Deity of Lord Rāmacandra, the descendant of King Raghu, the Lord offered His prayers and obeisances. Then a brāhmaṇa invited the Lord to take lunch.**
