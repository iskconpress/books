====== CC Madhya 9.165 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura viyoge bhaṭṭa haila acetana
> ei raṅga-līlā kare śacīra nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:v:viyoge]]---on account of separation from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:a:acetana]]---unconscious; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:r:raṅga]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastime at Śrī Raṅga-kṣetra; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī.

===== Translation =====

**When He did so, Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa fell down unconscious. Such are the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the son of mother Śacī, at Śrī Raṅga-kṣetra.**
