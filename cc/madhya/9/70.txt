====== Cc. Madhya 9.70 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe nṛtya-gīta bahuta karila
> dina-dui rahi' loke 'kṛṣṇa-bhakta' kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---much; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---performed; [[synonyms:d:dina]]-[[synonyms:d:dui]]---for two days; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed at Viṣṇu-kāñcī for two days, He danced and performed kīrtana in ecstasy. When all the people saw Him, they were converted into devotees of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
