====== CC Madhya 9.47 ======

===== Text =====

> bauddhācārya mahā-paṇḍita nija nava-mate
> prabhura āge udgrāha kari' lāgilā balite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bauddha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---the leader in Buddhist philosophy; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---greatly learned scholar; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---own; [[synonyms:n:nava]]---nine; [[synonyms:m:mate]]---philosophical conclusions; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:udgrāha]]---argument; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:b:balite]]---to speak.

===== Translation =====

**One of them was a leader of the Buddhist cult and was a very learned scholar. To establish the nine philosophical conclusions of Buddhism, he came before the Lord and began to speak.**
