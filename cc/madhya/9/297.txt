====== CC Madhya 9.297 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera brāhmaṇī, teṅha---mahā-pativratā
> vātsalye hayena teṅha yena jagan-mātā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇī]]---wife; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---she; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---great; [[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:v:vratā]]---devoted to her husband; [[synonyms:v:vātsalye]]---in affection; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---was; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---she; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of the whole universe.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Raṅga Purī also remembered the wife of Jagannātha Miśra. She was very much devoted and chaste. As for her affection, she was exactly like the mother of the universe.**
