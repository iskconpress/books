====== CC Madhya 9.278 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata tāṅra ghare garva cūrṇa kari'
> phalgu-tīrthe tabe cali āilā gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at his place; [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:c:cūrṇa]]---broken; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:p:phalgu]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrthe]]---to the holy place named Phalgu-tīrtha; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:c:cali]]---walking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu broke the pride of the Tattvavādīs to pieces. He then went to the holy place known as Phalgu-tīrtha.**
