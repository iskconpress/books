====== Cc. Madhya 9.272 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsī dekhiyā more karaha vañcana
> nā kahilā teñi sādhya-sādhana-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---a person in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---you do; [[synonyms:v:vañcana]]---duplicity; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---did not describe; [[synonyms:t:teñi]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]---objective; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---process of achievement; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---symptoms.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued speaking to the Tattvavādī ācārya: "Seeing that I am a mendicant in the renounced order of life, you have been playing with Me in a duplicitous way. You have not actually described the process and ultimate objective."**
