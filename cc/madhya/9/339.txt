====== CC Madhya 9.339 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura āgamana śuni' nityānanda rāya
> uṭhiyā calilā, preme theha nāhi pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---arrival; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---got up and started; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:t:theha]]---patience; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---does not get.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Nityānanda received news of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's arrival, He immediately got up and started out to see Him. Indeed, He was very impatient in His great ecstasy.**
