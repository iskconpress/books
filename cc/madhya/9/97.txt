====== CC Madhya 9.97 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu puchila tāṅre, śuna, mahāśaya
> kon artha jāni' tomāra eta sukha haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---from him; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśaya]]---My dear sir; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so great; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked the brāhmaṇa, "My dear sir, why are you in such ecstatic love? Which portion of the Bhagavad-gītā gives you such transcendental pleasure?"**
