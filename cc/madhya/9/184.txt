====== CC Madhya 9.184 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra upāsanā śuni' prabhu tuṣṭa hailā
> āste-vyaste sei vipra randhana karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:u:upāsanā]]---method of worship; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---was very much pleased; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---with great haste; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:r:randhana]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---began to cook.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very much satisfied to hear about the brāhmaṇa's method of worship. Finally the brāhmaṇa hastily made arrangements for cooking.**
