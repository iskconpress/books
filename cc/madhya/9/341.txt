====== CC Madhya 9.341 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnāthācārya calilā ānandita hañā
> prabhure mililā sabe pathe lāg pāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---along the way; [[synonyms:l:lāg]]---contact; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya also went in a very happy mood. They all went to meet the Lord, and they finally contacted Him on the way.**
