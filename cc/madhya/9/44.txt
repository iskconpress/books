====== Cc. Madhya 9.44 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvatra sthāpaya prabhu vaiṣṇava-siddhānte
> prabhura siddhānta keha nā pāre khaṇḍite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:s:sthāpaya]]---establishes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhānte]]---the conclusion of the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---conclusion; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is not able; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍite]]---to defy.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu established the devotional cult everywhere. No one could defeat Him.**
