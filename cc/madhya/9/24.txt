====== CC Madhya 9.24 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve tumi nirantara laite rāma-nāma
> ebe kene nirantara lao kṛṣṇa-nāma''

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly; [[synonyms:l:laite]]---used to chant; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly; [[synonyms:l:lao]]---you chant; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Formerly you were constantly chanting the holy name of Lord Rāma. Why are you now constantly chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa?"**
