====== CC Madhya 9.56 ======

===== Text =====

> terache paḍila thāli,---māthā kāṭi' gela
> mūrcchita hañā ācārya bhūmite paḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:terache]]---at an angle; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell down; [[synonyms:t:thāli]]---the plate; [[synonyms:m:māthā]]---the head; [[synonyms:k:kāṭi']]---cutting; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]]---unconscious; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---the teacher; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell down.

===== Translation =====

**The plate was made of metal, and when its edge hit the head of the teacher, it cut him, and the teacher immediately fell to the ground unconscious.**
