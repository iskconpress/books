====== CC Madhya 9.201 ======

===== Text =====

> pativratā-śiromaṇi janaka-nandinī
> jagatera mātā sītā---rāmera gṛhiṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:v:vratā]]---chaste woman; [[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---the topmost; [[synonyms:j:janaka]]-[[synonyms:n:nandinī]]---is the daughter of King Janaka; [[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of all the three worlds; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother; [[synonyms:s:sītā]]---Sītā; [[synonyms:r:rāmera]]---of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:g:gṛhiṇī]]---wife.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīmatī Sītādevī is the mother of the three worlds and the wife of Lord Rāmacandra. Among chaste women she is supreme, and she is the daughter of King Janaka.**
