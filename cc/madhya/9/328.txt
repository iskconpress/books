====== CC Madhya 9.328 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-kāle rāya punaḥ kaila āgamana
> dui jane kṛṣṇa-kathāya kaila jāgaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at night; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---coming; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---the two of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathāya]]---in discourses on topics of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:j:jāgaraṇa]]---keeping awake through the night.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya returned at night, and he and the Lord discussed topics concerning Kṛṣṇa. Thus they passed the night.**
