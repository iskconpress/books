====== CC Madhya 9.191 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara-preyasī sītā---cid-ānanda-mūrti
> prākṛta-indriyera tāṅre dekhite nāhi śakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:p:preyasī]]---the dearmost wife of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sītā]]---mother Sītā; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---spiritual blissful form; [[synonyms:p:prākṛta]]---material; [[synonyms:i:indriyera]]---of the senses; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---her; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "Sītādevī, the dearmost wife of the Supreme Lord Rāmacandra, certainly has a spiritual form full of bliss. No one can see her with material eyes, for no materialist has such power.**
