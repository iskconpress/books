====== Cc. Madhya 9.243 ======

===== Text =====

> dina-dui tāhāṅ kari' kīrtana-nartana
> payasvinī āsiyā dekhe śaṅkara nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dina]]-[[synonyms:d:dui]]---two days; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:n:nartana]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:p:payasvinī]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming to the bank of the Payasvinī River; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]] [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---the temple of Śaṅkara-nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted and danced at Śrī Janārdana for two days. He then went to the bank of the Payasvinī River and visited the temple of Śaṅkara-nārāyaṇa.**
