====== CC Madhya 9.292 ======

===== Text =====

> adbhuta premera vanyā duṅhāra uthalila
> duṅhe mānya kari' duṅhe ānande vasila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vanyā]]---inundation; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:u:uthalila]]---arose; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:m:mānya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering respect; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---with great happiness; [[synonyms:v:vasila]]---sat down.

===== Translation =====

**They were both inundated by the wonderful ecstasy of love that was aroused in them. Finally they sat down and respectfully began to converse.**
