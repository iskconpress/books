====== CC Madhya 9.226 ======

===== Text =====

> gosāñira saṅge rahe kṛṣṇadāsa brāhmaṇa
> bhaṭṭathāri-saha tāhāṅ haila daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---a //brāhmaṇa// servant named Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭathāri]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with the Bhaṭṭathāris; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---a meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was accompanied by His servant, Kṛṣṇadāsa. He was a brāhmaṇa, but he met with the Bhaṭṭathāris there.**
