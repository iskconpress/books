====== Cc. Madhya 9.307 ======

===== Text =====

> 'karṇāmṛta'-sama vastu nāhi tribhuvane
> yāhā haite haya kṛṣṇe śuddha-prema-jñāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karṇāmṛta]]---the //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta//; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like; [[synonyms:v:vastu]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is nothing; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---in the three worlds; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from which; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---knowledge of pure devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**There is no comparison to the Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta within the three worlds. By studying this book, one is elevated to the knowledge of pure devotional service to Kṛṣṇa.**
