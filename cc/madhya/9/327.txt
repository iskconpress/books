====== Cc. Madhya 9.327 ======

===== Text =====

> loka dehki' rāmānanda gelā nija-ghare
> madhyāhne uṭhilā prabhu bhikṣā karibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the people; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāya Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---departed; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his own home; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhne]]---at noon; [[synonyms:u:uṭhilā]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to take His lunch.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the people who gathered there, Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya returned to his own home. At noon, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up to take His lunch.**
