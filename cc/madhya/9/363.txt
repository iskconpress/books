====== CC Madhya 9.363 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-candrera līlā---agādha, gambhīra
> praveśa karite nāri,---sparśi rahi' tīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:candrera]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:agādha]]---unfathomable; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---deep; [[synonyms:p:praveśa]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to enter into; [[synonyms:n:nāri]]---I am unable; [[synonyms:s:sparśi]]---I touch; [[synonyms:r:rahi']] [[synonyms:t:tīra]]---standing on the bank.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are just like an unfathomable ocean. It is not possible for me to enter into them. Simply standing on the shore, I am but touching the water.**
