====== CC Madhya 9.273 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' tattvācārya hailā antare lajjita
> prabhura vaiṣṇavatā dekhi, ha-ilā vismita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---the //ācārya// of the Tattvavāda-sampradāya; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:l:lajjita]]---ashamed; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavatā]]---devotion in Vaiṣṇavism; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---struck with wonder.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the ācārya of the Tattvavāda-sampradāya became very much ashamed. Upon observing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's rigid faith in Vaiṣṇavism, he was struck with wonder.**
