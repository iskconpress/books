====== Cc. Madhya 9.28 ======

===== Text =====

> bālya-kāla haite mora svabhāva eka haya
> nāmera mahimā-śāstra kariye sañcaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bālya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from my childhood; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---practice; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---concerning the glories; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:s:sañcaya]]---I collect.

===== Translation =====

**"From my childhood I have been practicing this chanting and have been collecting the glories of the holy name from revealed scriptures.**
