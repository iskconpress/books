====== CC Madhya 9.294 ======

===== Text =====

> kautuke purī tāṅre puchila janma-sthāna
> gosāñi kautuke kahena 'navadvīpa' nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kautuke]]---out of curiosity; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Śrī Raṅga Purī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---asked; [[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---the place of birth; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kautuke]]---as a matter of course; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---said; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpa]]---Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name.

===== Translation =====

**Out of curiosity, Śrī Raṅga Purī asked Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu about His birthplace, and the Lord informed him that it was Navadvīpa-dhāma.**
