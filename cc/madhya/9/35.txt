====== Cc. Madhya 9.35 ======

===== Text =====

> iṣṭa-deva rāma, tāṅra nāme sukha pāi
> sukha pāñā rāma-nāma rātri-dina gāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---my worshipable Lord; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---Lord Śrī Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in His holy name; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---I get happiness; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting such transcendental happiness; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Rāma; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---day and night; [[synonyms:g:gāi]]---I chant.

===== Translation =====

**"My worshipable Lord has been Lord Rāmacandra, and by chanting His holy name I received happiness. Because I received such happiness, I chanted the holy name of Lord Rāma day and night.**
