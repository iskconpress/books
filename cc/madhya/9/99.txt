====== CC Madhya 9.99 ======

===== Text =====

> arjunera rathe kṛṣṇa haya rajju-dhara
> vasiyāche hāte totra śyāmala sundara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:arjunera]]---of Arjuna; [[synonyms:r:rathe]]---in the chariot; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:r:rajju]]-[[synonyms:d:dhara]]---holding the reins; [[synonyms:v:vasiyāche]]---He was sitting there; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand; [[synonyms:t:totra]]---a bridle; [[synonyms:ś:śyāmala]]---blackish; [[synonyms:s:sundara]]---very beautiful.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa continued, "Actually I only see Lord Kṛṣṇa sitting on a chariot as Arjuna's charioteer. Taking the reins in His hands, He appears very beautiful and blackish.**
