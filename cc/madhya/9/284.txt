====== CC Madhya 9.284 ======

===== Text =====

> bahuta ādare prabhuke bhikṣā karāila
> bhikṣā kari' tathā eka śubha-vārtā pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:ā:ādare]]---with great love; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---offered lunch; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after finishing His lunch; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:śubha]]-[[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---auspicious news; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got.

===== Translation =====

**This brāhmaṇa offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu food with great respect and love. After finishing His lunch, the Lord received auspicious news.**
