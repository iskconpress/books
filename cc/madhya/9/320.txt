====== CC Madhya 9.320 ======

===== Text =====

> daṇḍavat hañā paḍe caraṇe dhariyā
> āliṅgana kaila prabhu tāṅre uṭhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---like a stick; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---getting up.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāmānanda Rāya fell flat, touching the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord immediately raised him to his feet and embraced him.**
