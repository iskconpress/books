====== Cc. Madhya 9.204 ======

===== Text =====

> sītā lañā rākhilena pārvatīra sthāne
> 'māyā-sītā' diyā agni vañcilā rāvaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sītā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking away mother Sītā; [[synonyms:r:rākhilena]]---kept; [[synonyms:p:pārvatīra]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---with mother Pārvatī, or goddess Durgā; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:s:sītā]]---the false, illusory form of Sītā; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:a:agni]]---fire-god; [[synonyms:v:vañcilā]]---cheated; [[synonyms:r:rāvaṇe]]---the demon Rāvaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**The fire-god, Agni, took away the real Sītā and brought her to the place of Pārvatī, goddess Durgā. An illusory form of mother Sītā was then delivered to Rāvaṇa, and in this way Rāvaṇa was cheated.**
