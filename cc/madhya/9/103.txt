====== CC Madhya 9.103 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' sei vipre kaila āliṅgana
> prabhu-pada dhari' vipra karena rodana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---He embraced; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]---the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced the brāhmaṇa, and the brāhmaṇa, catching the lotus feet of the Lord, began to cry.**
