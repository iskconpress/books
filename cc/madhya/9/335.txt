====== CC Madhya 9.335 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu tāṅre āsite ājñā diyā
> nīlācale calilā prabhu ānandita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:ā:āsite]]---to come; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving an order; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---with great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**Giving orders to Rāmānanda Rāya to come to Nīlācala, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed for Jagannātha Purī with great pleasure.**
