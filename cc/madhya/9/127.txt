====== CC Madhya 9.127 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---kṛṣṇera eka svabhāva vilakṣaṇa
> sva-mādhurye sarva citta kare ākarṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---characteristic; [[synonyms:v:vilakṣaṇa]]---special; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhurye]]---His conjugal love; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---hearts; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---attraction.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord replied, "Lord Kṛṣṇa has a special characteristic: He attracts everyone's heart by the mellow of His personal conjugal love.**
