====== Cc. Madhya 9.216 ======

===== Text =====

> mano-duḥkhe bhāla bhikṣā nā dila sei dine
> mora bhāgye punarapi pāiluṅ daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mano]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---out of great mental distress; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---good lunch; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---could not give You; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---that day; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---because of my good fortune; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:p:pāiluṅ]]---I have gotten; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---visit.

===== Translation =====

**"Due to my mental distress I could not give You a very nice lunch the other day. Now, by good fortune, You have come again to my home."**
