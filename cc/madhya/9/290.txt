====== CC Madhya 9.290 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhuke uthāñā kaila āliṅgana
> galāgali kari' duṅhe karena krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uthāñā]]---lifting up; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:g:galāgali]]---shoulder to shoulder; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---do; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Raṅga Purī lifted Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu up and embraced Him. As the two of them embraced shoulder to shoulder, they began to cry in ecstasy.**
