====== Cc. Madhya 9.151 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' prabhu tāṅra garva cūrṇa kariyā
> tāṅre sukha dite kahe siddhānta phirāiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his (of Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa); [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:c:cūrṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---smashing into pieces; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give happiness; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]] [[synonyms:p:phirāiyā]]---turning the whole conversation.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu deflated the pride of Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa, but just to make him happy again, He spoke as follows.**
