====== CC Madhya 9.330 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda kahe,---prabhu, tomāra ājñā pāñā
> rājāke likhiluṅ āmi vinaya kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my dear Lord; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---Your permission; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:r:rājāke]] [[synonyms:l:likhiluṅ]]---have written a letter to the King; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great humility.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya said, "My dear Lord, with Your permission I have already written a letter to the King with great humility.**
