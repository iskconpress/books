====== CC Madhya 9.50 ======

===== Text =====

> bauddhācārya 'nava praśna' saba uṭhāila
> dṛḍha yukti-tarke prabhu khaṇḍa khaṇḍa kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bauddha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---the teacher of the Buddhist cult; [[synonyms:n:nava]] [[synonyms:p:praśna]]---nine different types of questions; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāila]]---raised; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---strong; [[synonyms:y:yukti]]---argument; [[synonyms:t:tarke]]---with logic; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---broke into pieces.

===== Translation =====

**The teacher of the Buddhist cult set forth the nine principles, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu broke them to pieces with His strong logic.**
