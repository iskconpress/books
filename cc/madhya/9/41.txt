====== Cc. Madhya 9.41 ======

===== Text =====

> gosāñira saundarya dekhi' tāte premāveśa
> sabe 'kṛṣṇa' kahe, 'vaiṣṇava' haila sarva-deśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---uttered the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---Vaiṣṇava devotees; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---everyone.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord's bodily features were very beautiful, and in addition He was always in the ecstasy of love of Godhead. Simply by seeing Him, everyone began chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa, and thus everyone became a Vaiṣṇava devotee.**
