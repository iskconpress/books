====== CC Madhya 9.83 ======

===== Text =====

> nija-ghare lañā kaila pāda-prakṣālana
> sei jala lañā kaila sa-vaṁśe bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his own home; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing of the feet; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:j:jala]]---that water; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---with all the family members; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---drinking.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa took Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home. After he washed the Lord's feet, all the members of his family drank the water.**
