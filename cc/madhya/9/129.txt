====== CC Madhya 9.129 ======

===== Text =====

> keha tāṅre putra-jñāne udukhale bāndhe
> keha sakhā-jñāne jini' caḍe tāṅra kāndhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---by accepting as a son; [[synonyms:u:udukhale]]---to a big mortar; [[synonyms:b:bāndhe]]---ties; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:s:sakhā]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---by accepting as a friend; [[synonyms:j:jini']]---conquering; [[synonyms:c:caḍe]]---gets up; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:k:kāndhe]]---on the shoulder.

===== Translation =====

**"There someone may accept Him as a son and sometimes bind Him to a grinding mortar. Someone else may accept Him as an intimate friend and, attaining victory over Him, playfully mount His shoulders.**
