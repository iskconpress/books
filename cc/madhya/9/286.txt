====== CC Madhya 9.286 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā calilā prabhu tāṅre dekhibāre
> vipra-gṛhe vasi' āchena, dekhilā tāṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the house of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---was; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---saw; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---him.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this news, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately went to see Śrī Raṅga Purī at the brāhmaṇa's home. Upon entering, the Lord saw him sitting there.**
