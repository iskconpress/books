====== Cc. Madhya 9.57 ======

===== Text =====

> hāhākāra kari' kānde saba śiṣya-gaṇa
> sabe āsi' prabhu-pade la-ila śaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hāhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---a roaring sound; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---cry; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---disciples; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---to the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---took; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]---shelter.

===== Translation =====

**When the teacher fell unconscious, his Buddhist disciples cried aloud and ran to the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for shelter.**
