====== Cc. Madhya 9.364 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-carita śraddhāya śune yei jana
> yateka vicāre, tata pāya prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:carita]]---the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhāya]]---with faith; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---which person; [[synonyms:y:yateka]] [[synonyms:v:vicāre]]---as far as he analytically studies; [[synonyms:t:tata]]---so far; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---he gets; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the riches of ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**The more one hears the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with faith, analytically studying them, the more one attains the ecstatic riches of love of Godhead.**
