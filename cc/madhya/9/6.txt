====== CC Madhya 9.6 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva nāma-mātra kariye gaṇana
> kahite nā pāri tāra yathā anukrama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only as a token record; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---I count; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to tell; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am unable; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:a:anukrama]]---chronological order.

===== Translation =====

**Because it is impossible for me to record all these places in chronological order, I simply make a token gesture of recording them.**
