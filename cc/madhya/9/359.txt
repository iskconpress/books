====== CC Madhya 9.359 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta caitanya-līlā kahite nā jāni
> lobhe lajjā khāñā tāra kari ṭānāṭāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I do not know; [[synonyms:l:lobhe]]---out of greed; [[synonyms:l:lajjā]] [[synonyms:k:khāñā]]---becoming shameless; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of them; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭānāṭāni]]---some attempt only.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Lord Caitanya are unlimited. No one can properly describe His activities, yet I make the attempt out of greed. This but reveals my shamelessness.**
