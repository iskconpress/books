====== CC Madhya 9.141 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---bhaṭṭa, tumi nā kariha saṁśaya
> 'svayaṁ-bhagavān' kṛṣṇa ei ta' niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---My dear Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do not do; [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]]---doubt; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---is Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---this is the conclusion.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then continued, "My dear Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa, please do not continue doubting. Lord Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, and this is the conclusion of the Vedic literature.**
