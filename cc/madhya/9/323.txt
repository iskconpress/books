====== Cc. Madhya 9.323 ======

===== Text =====

> tīrtha-yātrā-kathā prabhu sakala kahilā
> karṇāmṛta, brahma-saṁhitā,---dui puṅthi dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of His pilgrimage; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---described everything; [[synonyms:k:karṇāmṛta]]---the book named //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta//; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁhitā]]---the book named //Brahma-saṁhitā//; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:p:puṅthi]]---scriptures; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave Rāmānanda Rāya a vivid description of His travels to the holy places and told him how He had acquired the two books named Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta and Brahma-saṁhitā. The Lord delivered the books to Rāmānanda Rāya.**
