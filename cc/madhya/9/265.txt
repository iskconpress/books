====== Cc. Madhya 9.265 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-dharmān parityajya
> mām ekaṁ śaraṇaṁ vraja
> ahaṁ tvāṁ sarva-pāpebhyo
> mokṣayiṣyāmi mā śucaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:d:dharmān]]---all kinds of occupational duties; [[synonyms:p:parityajya]]---giving up; [[synonyms:m:mām]] [[synonyms:e:ekam]]---unto Me only; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇam]]---as shelter; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]---go; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tvām]]---unto you; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:p:pāpebhyaḥ]]---from all the reactions of sinful life; [[synonyms:m:mokṣayiṣyāmi]]---will give liberation; [[synonyms:m:mā]]---do not; [[synonyms:ś:śucaḥ]]---worry.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Abandon all varieties of religion and just surrender unto Me. I shall deliver you from all sinful reactions. Do not fear.'**
