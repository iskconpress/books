====== CC Madhya 9.23 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā kari' mahāprabhu tāṅre praśna kaila
> ''kaha vipra, ei tomāra kon daśā haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after accepting lunch; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:p:praśna]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---asked a question; [[synonyms:k:kaha]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---My dear //brāhmaṇa// friend, please say; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---situation; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing His lunch there, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked the brāhmaṇa, "My dear friend, kindly tell Me what your position is now.**
