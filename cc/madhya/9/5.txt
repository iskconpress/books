====== Cc. Madhya 9.5 ======

===== Text =====

> sei saba tīrthera krama kahite nā pāri
> dakṣiṇa-vāme tīrtha-gamana haya pherāpheri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all those; [[synonyms:t:tīrthera]]---of holy places; [[synonyms:k:krama]]---the chronological order; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to tell of; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am unable; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāme]]---left and right; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---visiting the holy places; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:pherāpheri]]---going and coming back

===== Translation =====

**I cannot chronologically record all the places of pilgrimage visited by Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. I can only summarize everything by saying that the Lord visited all holy places right and left, coming and going.**
