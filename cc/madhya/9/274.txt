====== CC Madhya 9.274 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe,---tumi yei kaha, sei satya haya
> sarva-śāstre vaiṣṇavera ei suniścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Tattvavādī //ācārya// said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---say; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---truth; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in all revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of the devotees of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---conclusion.

===== Translation =====

**The Tattvavādī ācārya replied, "What You have said is certainly factual. It is the conclusion of all the revealed scriptures of the Vaiṣṇavas.**
