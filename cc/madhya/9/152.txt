====== CC Madhya 9.152 ======

===== Text =====

> duḥkha nā bhāviha, bhaṭṭa, kailuṅ parihāsa
> śāstra-siddhānta śuna, yāte vaiṣṇava-viśvāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---do not; [[synonyms:b:bhāviha]]---bear; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---My dear Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]] [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]]---I was simply making a joke; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---the conclusion of the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---in which; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---the faith of the Vaiṣṇavas.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord pacified Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa by saying, "Actually whatever I have said is by way of jest. Now you can hear from Me the conclusion of the śāstras, in which every Vaiṣṇava devotee has firm faith.**
