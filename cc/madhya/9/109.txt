~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 9.109~~

====== CC Madhya 9.109 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-vaiṣṇava' bhaṭṭa seve lakṣmī-nārāyaṇa
> tāṅra bhakti dekhi' prabhura tuṣṭa haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---a devotee of the Rāmānuja-sampradāya; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:s:seve]]---used to worship; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---the Deities of Lord Nārāyaṇa and the goddess of fortune, Lakṣmī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]]---happy; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Being a Vaiṣṇava in the Rāmānuja-sampradāya, Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa worshiped the Deities of Lakṣmī and Nārāyaṇa. Seeing his pure devotion, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very much satisfied.**
