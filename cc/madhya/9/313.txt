====== CC Madhya 9.313 ======

===== Text =====

> saptatāla dekhi' prabhu āliṅgana kaila
> saśarīre saptatāla vaikuṇṭhe calila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:t:tāla]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing the seven palm trees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---embraced; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---with their bodies; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:t:tāla]]---the seven palm trees; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhe]] [[synonyms:c:calila]]---returned to Vaikuṇṭhaloka.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the seven palm trees, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced them. As a result, they all returned to Vaikuṇṭhaloka, the spiritual world.**
