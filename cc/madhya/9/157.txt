====== CC Madhya 9.157 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭa kahe,---kāhāṅ āmi jīva pāmara
> kāhāṅ tumi sei kṛṣṇa,---sākṣāt īśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa said; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---whereas; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---an ordinary living being; [[synonyms:p:pāmara]]---fallen; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---whereas; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the same Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---directly the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa then said, "I am an ordinary fallen living entity, but You are Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself.**
