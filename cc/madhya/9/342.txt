====== CC Madhya 9.342 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu premāveśe sabāya kaila āliṅgana
> premāveśe sabe kare ānanda-krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kailaāliṅgana]]---embraced; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---all of them did; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying in pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord was also filled with ecstatic love, and He embraced them all. Out of their love, they began to cry with pleasure.**
