====== CC Madhya 9.40 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura prabhāve loka āila daraśane
> lakṣārbuda loka āise nā yāya gaṇane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhāve]]---by the influence; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see Him; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:arbuda]]---many millions; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---persons; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇane]]---can be counted.

===== Translation =====

**Due to the influence of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, many millions of men came just to see Him. Indeed, the assembly being unlimited, its members could not be counted.**
