====== CC Madhya 9.26 ======

===== Text =====

> bālyāvadhi rāma-nāma-grahaṇa āmāra
> tomā dekhi' kṛṣṇa-nāma āila eka-bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bālya]]-[[synonyms:a:avadhi]]---since the days of my childhood; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:g:grahaṇa]]---chanting the holy name of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---upon seeing You; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---once only.

===== Translation =====

**"From my childhood I have been chanting the holy name of Lord Rāmacandra, but upon seeing You I chanted the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa just once.**
