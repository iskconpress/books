====== Cc. Madhya 9.207 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniñā prabhura ānandita haila mana
> rāmadāsa-viprera kathā ha-ila smaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniñā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very much pleased; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:r:rāmadāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of the //brāhmaṇa// known as Rāmadāsa; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---words; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]---He remembered.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this story, He was very much pleased, and He remembered the words of Rāmadāsa Vipra.**
