====== CC Madhya 9.110 ======

===== Text =====

> nirantara tāṅra saṅge haila sakhya-bhāva
> hāsya-parihāse duṅhe sakhyera svabhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---being associated with him; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---a friendly relationship; [[synonyms:h:hāsya]]---laughing; [[synonyms:p:parihāse]]---joking; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:s:sakhyera]]---of fraternity; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---nature.

===== Translation =====

**Constantly associating with each other, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa gradually developed a friendly relationship. Indeed, sometimes they laughed and joked together.**
