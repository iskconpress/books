====== CC Madhya 9.298 ======

===== Text =====

> randhane nipuṇā tāṅ-sama nāhi tribhuvane
> putra-sama sneha karena sannyāsi-bhojane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:randhane]]---in cooking; [[synonyms:n:nipuṇā]]---very expert; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like her; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is none; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---in the three worlds; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like to her own sons; [[synonyms:s:sneha]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---she was affectionate; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---in feeding the //sannyāsīs.//

===== Translation =====

**He also remembered how Śrī Jagannātha Miśra's wife, Śacīmātā, was expert in cooking. He recalled that she was very affectionate toward the sannyāsīs and fed them exactly like her own sons.**
