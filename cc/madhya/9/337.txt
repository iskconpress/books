====== CC Madhya 9.337 ======

===== Text =====

> yāhāṅ yāya, loka uṭhe hari-dhvani kari'
> dekhi' ānandita-mana hailā gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---wherever He goes; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---people stand up; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---vibrating the holy name of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---happy; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---in mind; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Wherever Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went, the holy name of Śrī Hari was vibrated. Seeing this, the Lord became very happy.**
