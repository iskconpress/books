====== CC Madhya 9.196 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura vacane viprera ha-ila viśvāsa
> bhojana karila, haila jīvanera āśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---in the words of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---faith; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---he took his lunch; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:j:jīvanera]]---for living; [[synonyms:ā:āśa]]---hope.

===== Translation =====

**Although the brāhmaṇa was fasting, he had faith in the words of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and accepted food. In this way his life was saved.**
