====== CC Madhya 9.345 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe sārvabhauma karilā rodane
> sabā-saṅge āilā prabhu īśvara-daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:r:rodane]]---cried; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see the Jagannātha temple.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya cried in great ecstatic love. Then the Lord, accompanied by them all, went to the temple of Jagannātha.**
