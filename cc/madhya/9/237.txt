====== Cc. Madhya 9.237 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-bhakta-gaṇa-saha tāhāṅ goṣṭhī kaila
> 'brahma-saṁhitādhyāya'-puṅthi tāhāṅ pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---among highly advanced devotees; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---discussed; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁhitā]]-[[synonyms:a:adhyāya]]---one chapter of the //Brahma-saṁhitā//; [[synonyms:p:puṅthi]]---scripture; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---found.

===== Translation =====

**In the temple of Ādi-keśava, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu discussed spiritual matters among highly advanced devotees. While there, He found a chapter of the Brahma-saṁhitā.**
