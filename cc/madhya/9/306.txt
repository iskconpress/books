====== CC Madhya 9.306 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta śuni' prabhura ānanda haila
> āgraha kariyā puṅthi lekhāñā laila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:karṇāmṛta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing the //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta//; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was great happiness; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kariya]]---with great eagerness; [[synonyms:p:puṅthi]]---the book; [[synonyms:l:lekhāñā]]---getting copied; [[synonyms:l:laila]]---took.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very much pleased to hear the book Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta, and with great eagerness He had it copied and took it with Him.**
