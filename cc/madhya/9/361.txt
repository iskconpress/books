====== CC Madhya 9.361 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-carita śuna śraddhā-bhakti kari'
> mātsarya chāḍiyā mukhe bala 'hari' 'hari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:carita]]---the activities of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]]---faith; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:m:mātsarya]]---envy; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---by the mouth; [[synonyms:b:bala]]---say; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of the Lord (Hari, Hari).

===== Translation =====

**Please hear the transcendental pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with faith and devotion. Giving up envy of the Lord, everyone chant the Lord's holy name, Hari.**
