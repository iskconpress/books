====== CC Madhya 9.161 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe se jāninu kṛṣṇa-bhakti sarvopari
> kṛtārtha karile, more kahile kṛpā kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:j:jāninu]]---I understand; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:u:upari]]---above all; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---successful; [[synonyms:k:karile]]---You have made; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kahile]]---You have spoken; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by Your causeless mercy.

===== Translation =====

**"I can now understand that devotional service unto Lord Kṛṣṇa is the supreme form of worship. Out of Your causeless mercy You have made my life successful simply by explaining the facts."**
