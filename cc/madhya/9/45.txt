====== CC Madhya 9.45 ======

===== Text =====

> hāri' hāri' prabhu-mate karena praveśa
> ei-mate 'vaiṣṇava' prabhu kaila dakṣiṇa deśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hāri']] [[synonyms:h:hāri']]---being defeated; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---into the cult of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:p:praveśa]]---enter; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---Vaiṣṇava devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---South India; [[synonyms:d:deśa]]---country.

===== Translation =====

**Being thus defeated by Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all these philosophers and their followers entered into His cult. In this way Lord Caitanya made South India into a country of Vaiṣṇavas.**
