====== CC Madhya 9.12 ======

===== Text =====

> sei saba vaiṣṇava mahāprabhura darśane
> kṛṣṇa-upāsaka haila, laya kṛṣṇa-nāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all those; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by seeing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:upāsaka]]---devotees of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---took; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāme]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**After meeting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all those different Vaiṣṇavas became devotees of Kṛṣṇa and began chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra.**
