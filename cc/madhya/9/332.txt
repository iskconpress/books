====== CC Madhya 9.332 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---ethā mora e-nimitte āgamana
> tomā lañā nīlācale kariba gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:n:nimitte]]---for this reason; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---coming back; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking you; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall do; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "It is for this purpose alone that I have returned. I want to take you with Me to Jagannātha Purī."**
