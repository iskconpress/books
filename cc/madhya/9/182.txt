====== Cc. Madhya 9.182 ======

===== Text =====

> vipra kahe,---prabhu, mora araṇye vasati
> pākera sāmagrī vane nā mile samprati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vipra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the //brāhmaṇa// replied; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---O Lord; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:a:araṇye]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:v:vasati]]---residence; [[synonyms:p:pākera]] [[synonyms:s:sāmagrī]]---the ingredients for cooking; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:m:mile]]---are not available; [[synonyms:s:samprati]]---at this time.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa replied, "My dear Lord, we are living in the forest. For the time being we cannot get all the ingredients for cooking.**
