====== CC Madhya 9.89 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣa lakṣa loka āila nānā-deśa haite
> sabe kṛṣṇa-nāma kahe prabhuke dekhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣa]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]---many hundreds of thousands; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---of people; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came there; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---different countries; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---seeing.

===== Translation =====

**Many hundreds of thousands of people from various countries came to see the Lord, and after seeing Him they all chanted the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra.**
