====== Cc. Madhya 9.112 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāra ṭhākura kṛṣṇa---gopa, go-cāraka
> sādhvī hañā kene cāhe tāṅhāra saṅgama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---My worshipable Deity; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]---cowherd; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:c:cāraka]]---a tender of cows; [[synonyms:s:sādhvī]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being so chaste; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:s:saṅgama]]---association.

===== Translation =====

**"However, my Lord is Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa, a cowherd boy who is engaged in tending cows. Why is it that Lakṣmī, being such a chaste wife, wants to associate with My Lord?**
