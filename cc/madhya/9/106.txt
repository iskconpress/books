====== Cc. Madhya 9.106 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu tāṅre karāila śikṣaṇa
> ei bāt kāhāṅ nā kariha prakāśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣaṇa]]---instruction; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:b:bāt]]---this version; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---anywhere; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---do not; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prakāśana]]---revelation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then taught the brāhmaṇa very thoroughly and requested him not to disclose the fact that He was Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself.**
