====== CC Madhya 9.60 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā-sabāra 'guru' tabe pāibe cetana
> saba bauddha mili' kare kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---all of you; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---the spiritual master; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:pāibe]]---will get; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bauddha]]---all the Buddhist disciples; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---coming together; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra.//

===== Translation =====

**"By this method your spiritual master will regain his consciousness." Following Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's advice, all the Buddhist disciples began to chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa congregationally.**
