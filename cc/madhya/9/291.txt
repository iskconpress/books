====== CC Madhya 9.291 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣaṇeke āveśa chāḍi' duṅhāra dhairya haila
> īśvara-purīra sambandha gosāñi jānāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeke]]---after just a few moments; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:d:dhairya]]---patience; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Īśvara Purī; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]---relationship; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jānāila]]---disclosed.

===== Translation =====

**After some moments, they came to their senses and became patient. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then informed Śrī Raṅga Purī about His relationship with Īśvara Purī.**
