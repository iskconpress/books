====== Cc. Madhya 9.154 ======

===== Text =====

> gopī-dvāre lakṣmī kare kṛṣṇa-saṅgāsvāda
> īśvaratve bheda mānile haya aparādha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---through the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]]---tasting the sweetness of the association of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaratve]]---in the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---difference; [[synonyms:m:mānile]]---if one considers; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense.

===== Translation =====

**"The goddess of fortune enjoys the association of Kṛṣṇa through the gopīs. One should not see a difference between the forms of the Lord, for such a conception is offensive.**
