====== CC Madhya 9.254 ======

===== Text =====

> tattvavādī ācārya---saba śāstrete pravīṇa
> tāṅre praśna kaila prabhu hañā yena dīna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tattvavādī]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---the chief preacher of the Tattvavāda community; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śāstrete]]---in revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:p:pravīṇa]]---experienced; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:p:praśna]]---question; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]---very humble.

===== Translation =====

**The chief ācārya of the Tattvavāda community was very learned in the revealed scriptures. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu humbly questioned him.**
