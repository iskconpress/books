====== CC Madhya 9.175 ======

===== Text =====

> śiva-durgā rahe tāhāṅ brāhmaṇera veśe
> mahāprabhu dekhi' doṅhāra ha-ila ullāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śiva]]-[[synonyms:d:durgā]]---Lord Śiva and his wife, Durgā; [[synonyms:r:rahe]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---stayed there; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇera]] [[synonyms:v:veśe]]---in the dress of //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:u:ullāse]]---great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**It was in Śrī Śaila that Lord Śiva and his wife Durgā lived in the dress of brāhmaṇas, and when they saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they became very much pleased.**
