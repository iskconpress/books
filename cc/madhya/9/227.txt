====== Cc. Madhya 9.227 ======

===== Text =====

> strī-dhana dekhāñā tāṅra lobha janmāila
> ārya sarala viprera buddhi-nāśa kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---women; [[synonyms:d:dekhāñā]]---showing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:l:lobha]]---attraction; [[synonyms:j:janmāila]]---they created; [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---gentleman; [[synonyms:s:sarala]]---simple; [[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---loss of intelligence; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---they made.

===== Translation =====

**With women the Bhaṭṭathāris allured the brāhmaṇa Kṛṣṇadāsa, who was simple and gentle. By virtue of their bad association, they polluted his intelligence.**
