====== CC Madhya 9.276 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---karmī, jñānī,---dui bhakti-hīna
> tomāra sampradāye dekhi sei dui cihna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:karmī]]---fruitive worker; [[synonyms:j:jñānī]]---mental speculator; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---both of them; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:h:hīna]]---nondevotees; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:s:sampradāye]]---in the community; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---both; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]---symptoms.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Both the fruitive worker and the speculative philosopher are considered nondevotees. We see both elements present in your sampradāya.**
