====== CC Madhya 9.343 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma bhaṭṭācārya ānande calilā
> samudrera tīre āsi' prabhure mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in pleasure; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:samudrera]] [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the beach by the ocean; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya also went to see the Lord with great pleasure, and he met Him on the beach by the sea.**
