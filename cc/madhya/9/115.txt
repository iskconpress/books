====== CC Madhya 9.115 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭa kahe, kṛṣṇa-nārāyaṇa---eka-i svarūpa
> kṛṣṇete adhika līlā-vaidagdhyādi-rūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa said; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa and Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:i:i]] [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---one and the same; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇete]]---in Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:adhika]]---more; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:v:vaidagdhya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---sportive nature.

===== Translation =====

**Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa then said, "Lord Kṛṣṇa and Lord Nārāyaṇa are one and the same, but the pastimes of Kṛṣṇa are more relishable due to their sportive nature.**
