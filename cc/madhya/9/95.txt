====== CC Madhya 9.95 ======

===== Text =====

> keha hāse, keha ninde, tāhā nāhi māne
> āviṣṭa hañā gītā paḍe ānandita-mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---someone laughs; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:ninde]]---someone criticizes; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---he does not care for; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:g:gītā]] [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---reads the //Bhagavad-gītā//; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---his mind.

===== Translation =====

**Due to his incorrect pronunciation, people sometimes criticized him and laughed at him, but he did not care. He was full of ecstasy due to reading the Bhagavad-gītā and was personally very happy.**
