====== Cc. Madhya 9.134 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhyāntare gopī-deha vraje yabe pāila
> sei dehe kṛṣṇa-saṅge rāsa-krīḍā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---externally and internally; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:d:deha]]---the body of a //gopī//; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vrajabhūmi; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---they got; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in that body; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---pastimes of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed.

===== Translation =====

**"The personified authorities on the Vedic hymns acquired bodies like those of the gopīs and took birth in Vrajabhūmi. In those bodies they were allowed to enter into the Lord's rāsa-līlā dance.**
