====== Cc. Madhya 9.87 ======

===== Text =====

> kāverīte snāna kari' śrī-raṅga darśana
> pratidina premāveśe karena nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāverīte]]---in the river known as Kāverī; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking a bath; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---visiting the temple of Śrī Raṅga; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---every day; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does perform; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**While there, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His bath in the river Kāverī and visited the temple of Śrī Raṅga. Every day the Lord also danced in ecstasy.**
