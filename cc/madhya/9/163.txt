====== CC Madhya 9.163 ======

===== Text =====

> cāturmāsya pūrṇa haila, bhaṭṭa-ājñā lañā
> dakṣiṇa calilā prabhu śrī-raṅga dekhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]]---the period of Cāturmāsya; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became completed; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking permission from Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---south; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---proceeded; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---visiting Śrī Raṅga.

===== Translation =====

**When the period of Cāturmāsya was completed, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took permission to leave Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa, and after visiting Śrī Raṅga He proceeded further toward southern India.**
