====== Cc. Madhya 9.63 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-rūpe kautuka kari' śacīra nandana
> antardhāna kaila, keha nā pāya darśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kautuka]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making fun; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---disappeared; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---audience.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the son of Śacīdevī, then suddenly and humorously disappeared from everyone's sight, and it was impossible for anyone to find Him.**
