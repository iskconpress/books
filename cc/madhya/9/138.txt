====== CC Madhya 9.138 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve bhaṭṭera mane eka chila abhimāna
> 'śrī-nārāyaṇa' hayena svayaṁ-bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---before this; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---there was; [[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---an impression; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---the form of the Lord as Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---is; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Before this explanation was given by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa thought that Śrī Nārāyaṇa was the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
