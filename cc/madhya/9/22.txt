====== Cc. Madhya 9.22 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ siddhavaṭa āilā sei vipra-ghare
> sei vipra kṛṣṇa-nāma laya nirantare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṭa]]---to the place known as Siddhavaṭa; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---chants; [[synonyms:n:nirantare]]---constantly.

===== Translation =====

**After visiting the temple of Trivikrama, the Lord returned to Siddhavaṭa, where He again visited the house of the brāhmaṇa, who was now constantly chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra.**
