====== CC Madhya 9.214 ======

===== Text =====

> vipra kahe,---tumi sākṣāt śrī-raghunandana
> sannyāsīra veṣe more dilā daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vipra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the //brāhmaṇa// said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raghunandana]]---Lord Śrī Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:v:veṣe]]---in the dress of a mendicant; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---You gave; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---audience.

===== Translation =====

**After receiving the manuscript, the brāhmaṇa, being very much pleased, said, "Sir, You are Lord Rāmacandra Himself and have come in the dress of a sannyāsī to give me audience.**
