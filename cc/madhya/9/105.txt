====== CC Madhya 9.105 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-sphūrtye tāṅra mana hañāche nirmala
> ataeva prabhura tattva jānila sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrtye]]---by revelation of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:h:hañāche]]---did become; [[synonyms:n:nirmala]]---purified; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---truth; [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---could understand; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**The mind of the brāhmaṇa was purified by the revelation of Lord Kṛṣṇa, and therefore he could understand the truth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in all details.**
