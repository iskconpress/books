====== Cc. Madhya 9.228 ======

===== Text =====

> prāte uṭhi' āilā vipra bhaṭṭathāri-ghare
> tāhāra uddeśe prabhu āilā satvare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---rising from bed; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa// Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭathāri]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the place of the Bhaṭṭathāris; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]] [[synonyms:u:uddeśe]]---for him; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:satvare]]---very soon.

===== Translation =====

**Allured by the Bhaṭṭathāris, Kṛṣṇadāsa went to their place early in the morning. The Lord also went there very quickly just to find him out.**
