====== CC Madhya 9.340 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda, dāmodara-paṇḍita, mukunda
> nāciyā calilā, dehe nā dhare ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:n:nāciyā]]---dancing; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---the body; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---does not hold; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda Rāya, Jagadānanda, Dāmodara Paṇḍita and Mukunda all became ecstatic in their happiness, and dancing along the way, they went to meet the Lord.**
