====== CC Madhya 9.77 ======

===== Text =====

> deva-sthāne āsi' kaila viṣṇu daraśana
> śrī-vaiṣṇavera saṅge tāhāṅ goṣṭhī anukṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place known as Devasthāna; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting the temple of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the Vaiṣṇavas in the disciplic succession of Rāmānuja; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---discussion; [[synonyms:a:anukṣaṇa]]---always.

===== Translation =====

**At Devasthāna, Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited the temple of Lord Viṣṇu, and there He talked with the Vaiṣṇavas in the disciplic succession of Rāmānujācārya. These Vaiṣṇavas are known as Śrī Vaiṣṇavas.**
