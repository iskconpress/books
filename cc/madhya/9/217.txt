====== CC Madhya 9.217 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' sei vipra sukhe pāka kaila
> uttama prakāre prabhuke bhikṣā karāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---cooked; [[synonyms:u:uttama]] [[synonyms:p:prakāre]]---very nicely; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---lunch; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---gave.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, the brāhmaṇa very happily cooked food, and a first-class dinner was offered to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
