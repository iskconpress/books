====== CC Madhya 9.20 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dina tāṅra ghare rahi' bhikṣā kari'
> tāṅre kṛpā kari' āge calilā gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seidina]]---on that day; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---the house of that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting //prasāda//; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing mercy; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:h:hari]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**That day, Lord Caitanya remained there and accepted prasāda at his house. After bestowing mercy upon him in this way, the Lord proceeded ahead.**
