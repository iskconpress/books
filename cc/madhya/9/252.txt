====== Cc. Madhya 9.252 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vaiṣṇavatā' sabāra antare garva jāni'
> īṣat hāsiyā kichu kahe gauramaṇi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavatā]]---Vaiṣṇavism; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:ī:īṣat]]---mildly; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu could understand that the Tattvavādīs were very proud of their Vaiṣṇavism. He therefore smiled and began to speak to them.**
