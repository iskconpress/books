====== Cc. Madhya 9.81 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe kaila bahuta gāna nartana
> dekhi' camatkāra haila saba lokera mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---various; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---songs; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing which; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---were; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of persons; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---minds.

===== Translation =====

**In the temple of Raṅganātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted and danced in ecstatic love of Godhead. Seeing His performance, everyone was struck with wonder.**
