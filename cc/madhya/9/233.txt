====== CC Madhya 9.233 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭathāri-ghare mahā uṭhila krandana
> keśe dhari' vipre lañā karila gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭathāri]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the home of the Bhaṭṭathāris; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---great; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---there arose; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying; [[synonyms:k:keśe]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching by the hair; [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---the //brāhmaṇa// Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure.

===== Translation =====

**While there was much roaring and crying at the Bhaṭṭathāri community, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu grabbed Kṛṣṇadāsa by the hair and took him away.**
