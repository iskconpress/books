====== CC Madhya 9.21 ======

===== Text =====

> skanda-kṣetra-tīrthe kaila skanda daraśana
> trimaṭha āilā, tāhāṅ dekhi' trivikrama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:skanda]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetra]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrthe]]---in the holy place known as Skanda-kṣetra; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:skanda]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting Lord Skanda (Kārttikeya, son of Lord Śiva); [[synonyms:t:trimaṭha]]---at Trimaṭha; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:trivikrama]]---a form of Lord Viṣṇu, Trivikrama.

===== Translation =====

**At the holy place known as Skanda-kṣetra, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited the temple of Skanda. From there He went to Trimaṭha, where He saw the Viṣṇu Deity Trivikrama.**
