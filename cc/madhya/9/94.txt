====== CC Madhya 9.94 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭādaśādhyāya paḍe ānanda-āveśe
> aśuddha paḍena, loka kare upahāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭādaśa]]-[[synonyms:a:adhyāya]]---eighteen chapters; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---reads; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:a:aśuddha]] [[synonyms:p:paḍena]]---could not pronounce the text correctly; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:u:upahāse]]---joking.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa regularly read the eighteen chapters of the Bhagavad-gītā in great transcendental ecstasy, but because he could not pronounce the words correctly, people used to joke about him.**
