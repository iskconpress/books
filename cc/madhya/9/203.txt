====== CC Madhya 9.203 ======

===== Text =====

> 'māyā-sītā' rāvaṇa nila, śunilā ākhyāne
> śuni' mahāprabhu haila ānandita mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:s:sītā]]---false, illusory Sītā; [[synonyms:r:rāvaṇa]]---the demon Rāvaṇa; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---heard; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāne]]---in the narration of the //Kūrma Purāṇa//; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very happy; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing from the Kūrma Purāṇa how Rāvaṇa had kidnapped a false form of mother Sītā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very much satisfied.**
