====== CC Madhya 9.353 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā karāñā tāṅre karāila śayana
> āpane sārvabhauma kare pāda-saṁvāhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---after giving lunch; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lie down to rest; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvāhana]]---massaging the legs.

===== Translation =====

**After offering food to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya made Him lie down to rest, and he personally began to massage the legs of the Lord.**
