====== CC Madhya 9.92 ======

===== Text =====

> eka eka dine cāturmāsya pūrṇa haila
> kataka brāhmaṇa bhikṣā dite nā pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---day by day; [[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]]---the period of Cāturmāsya; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became filled; [[synonyms:k:kataka]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---some of the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to offer Him lunch; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---did not; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---get the opportunity.

===== Translation =====

**Each day the Lord was invited by a different brāhmaṇa, but some of the brāhmaṇas did not get the opportunity to offer Him lunch because the period of Cāturmāsya came to an end.**
