====== Cc. Madhya 9.91 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-raṅga-kṣetre vaise yata vaiṣṇava-brāhmaṇa
> eka eka dina sabe kaila nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---in Śrī Raṅga-kṣetra; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---residing; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---Vaiṣṇava //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---every day; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invited the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**All the Vaiṣṇava brāhmaṇas residing in Śrī Raṅga-kṣetra invited the Lord to their homes. Indeed, He had an invitation every day.**
