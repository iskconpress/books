====== CC Madhya 9.285 ======

===== Text =====

> mādhava-purīra śiṣya 'śrī-raṅga-purī' nāma
> sei grāme vipra-gṛhe karena viśrāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---a disciple of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Śrī Raṅga Purī; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in that village; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in the house of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---rests.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu received word that Śrī Raṅga Purī, one of the disciples of Śrī Mādhavendra Purī, was present in that village at the home of a brāhmaṇa.**
