====== CC Madhya 9.159 ======

===== Text =====

> more pūrṇa kṛpā kaila lakṣmī-nārāyaṇa
> tāṅra kṛpāya pāinu tomāra caraṇa-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---complete; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---the Deity of mother goddess of fortune and Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by Their mercy; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I have gotten; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---vision of the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"I have been engaged in the service of Lakṣmī-Nārāyaṇa, and it is due to Their mercy that I have been able to see Your lotus feet.**
