====== CC Madhya 9.84 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā karāñā kichu kaila nivedana
> cāturmāsya āsi' prabhu, haila upasanna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---after offering lunch; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission; [[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]]---the period of Cāturmāsya; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:u:upasanna]]---has already arrived.

===== Translation =====

**After offering lunch to the Lord, Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa submitted that the period of Cāturmāsya had already arrived.**
