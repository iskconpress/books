====== CC Madhya 9.32 ======

===== Text =====

> rāma rāmeti rāmeti
> rame rāme manorame
> sahasra-nāmabhis tulyaṁ
> rāma-nāma varānane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāma]]---Rāma; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---Rāma; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---Rāma; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:r:rame]]---I enjoy; [[synonyms:r:rāme]]---in the holy name of Rāma; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:r:rame]]---most beautiful; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmabhiḥ]]---with the one thousand names; [[synonyms:t:tulyam]]---equal; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Rāma; [[synonyms:v:vara]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānane]]---O lovely-faced woman.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Lord Śiva addressed his wife Durgā as Varānanā and explained, "I chant the holy name of Rāma, Rāma, Rāma and thus enjoy this beautiful sound. This holy name of Rāmacandra is equal to one thousand holy names of Lord Viṣṇu."'**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from the //Bṛhad-viṣṇu-sahasranāma-stotra// in the //Uttara-khaṇḍa// of the //Padma Purāṇa// (72.335).
