====== Cc. Madhya 9.329 ======

===== Text =====

> dui jane kṛṣṇa-kathā kahe rātri-dine
> parama-ānande gela pāṅca-sāta dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speak; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---passed; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---five to seven days.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu discussed Kṛṣṇa day and night, and thus they passed from five to seven days in great happiness.**
