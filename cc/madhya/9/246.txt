====== CC Madhya 9.246 ======

===== Text =====

> nartaka gopāla dekhe parama-mohane
> madhvācārye svapna diyā āilā tāṅra sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nartaka]] [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---dancing Gopāla; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---saw; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:m:mohane]]---most beautiful; [[synonyms:m:madhva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārye]]---unto Madhvācārya; [[synonyms:s:svapna]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---appearing in a dream; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place.

===== Translation =====

**While at the Uḍupī monastery, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw "dancing Gopāla," a most beautiful Deity. This Deity appeared to Madhvācārya in a dream.**
