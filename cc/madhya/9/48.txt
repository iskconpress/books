====== CC Madhya 9.48 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi asambhāṣya bauddha ayukta dekhite
> tathāpi balilā prabhu garva khaṇḍāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:a:asambhāṣya]]---not fit for discussion; [[synonyms:b:bauddha]]---followers of Buddha's philosophy; [[synonyms:a:ayukta]]---not fit; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:b:balilā]]---spoke; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāite]]---to diminish.

===== Translation =====

**Although the Buddhists are unfit for discussion and should not be seen by Vaiṣṇavas, Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke to them just to decrease their false pride.**
