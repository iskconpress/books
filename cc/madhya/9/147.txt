====== Cc. Madhya 9.147 ======

===== Text =====

> svayaṁ bhagavān 'kṛṣṇa' hare lakṣmīra mana
> gopikāra mana harite nāre 'nārāyaṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---is Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---attracts; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmīra]]---of the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:g:gopikāra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds; [[synonyms:h:harite]]---to attract; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---is not able; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Lord Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa, attracts the mind of the goddess of fortune, but Lord Nārāyaṇa cannot attract the minds of the gopīs. This proves the superexcellence of Kṛṣṇa.**
