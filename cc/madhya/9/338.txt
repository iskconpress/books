====== CC Madhya 9.338 ======

===== Text =====

> ālālanāthe āsi' kṛṣṇadāse pāṭhāila
> nityānanda-ādi nija-gaṇe bolāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ālālanāthe]]---to the place known as Ālālanātha; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāse]]---Kṛṣṇadāsa, His assistant; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent ahead; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---personal associates; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---called for.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord reached Ālālanātha, He sent His assistant Kṛṣṇadāsa ahead to call for Nityānanda and other personal associates.**
