====== CC Madhya 9.319 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda rāya śuni' prabhura āgamana
> ānande āsiyā kaila prabhu-saha milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---return; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāmānanda Rāya heard of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's arrival, he was very much pleased, and he immediately went to see Him.**
