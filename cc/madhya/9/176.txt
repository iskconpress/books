====== CC Madhya 9.176 ======

===== Text =====

> tina dina bhikṣā dila kari' nimantraṇa
> nibhṛte vasi' gupta-vārtā kahe dui jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---for three days; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---offered alms; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---inviting Him; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting together; [[synonyms:g:gupta]]-[[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---confidential talks; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speak; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---both of them.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śiva, dressed like a brāhmaṇa, gave alms to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and invited Him to spend three days in a solitary place. Sitting there together, they talked very confidentially.**
