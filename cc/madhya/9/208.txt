====== CC Madhya 9.208 ======

===== Text =====

> e-saba siddhānta śuni' prabhura ānanda haila
> brāhmaṇera sthāne māgi' sei patra nila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---all these conclusive statements; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---happiness; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---from the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---asking; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:p:patra]]---leaves; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard these conclusive statements from the Kūrma Purāṇa, He felt great happiness. After asking the brāhmaṇas' permission, He took possession of those manuscript leaves.**
