====== Cc. Madhya 9.39 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ haite cali' āge gelā eka grāme
> brāhmaṇa-samāja tāhāṅ, karila viśrāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from there; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---going; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---forward; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---to a village; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:samāja]]---assembly of //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:v:viśrāme]]---He rested.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then left Vṛddhakāśī and proceeded further. In one village He saw that most of the residents were brāhmaṇas, and He took His rest there.**
