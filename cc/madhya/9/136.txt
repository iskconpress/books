====== CC Madhya 9.136 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣmī cāhe sei dehe kṛṣṇera saṅgama
> gopikā-anugā hañā nā kaila bhajana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the body; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅgama]]---the association of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gopikā]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:a:anugā]]---follower; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---did not; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---perform; [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---worship.

===== Translation =====

**"The goddess of fortune, Lakṣmī, wanted to enjoy Kṛṣṇa and at the same time retain her spiritual body in the form of Lakṣmī. However, she did not follow in the footsteps of the gopīs in her worship of Kṛṣṇa.**
