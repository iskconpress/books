====== Cc. Madhya 9.62 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa bali' ācārya prabhure karena vinaya
> dekhiyā sakala loka ha-ila vismaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---the so-called spiritual master of the Buddhists; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---submission; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing this; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---astonished.

===== Translation =====

**When the spiritual master of the Buddhists began to chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa and submitted to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the people who were gathered there were astonished.**
