====== CC Madhya 9.37 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kṛṣṇa tumi sākṣāt---ihā nirdhārila
> eta kahi' vipra prabhura caraṇe paḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:n:nirdhārila]]---concluded; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:padila]]---fell down.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa concluded, "Sir, You are that Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself. This is my conclusion." Saying this, the brāhmaṇa fell down at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
