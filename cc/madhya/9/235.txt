====== Cc. Madhya 9.235 ======

===== Text =====

> keśava dekhiyā preme āviṣṭa hailā
> nati, stuti, nṛtya, gīta, bahuta karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keśava]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---after seeing the Deity of Lord Keśava; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became overwhelmed; [[synonyms:n:nati]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---prayer; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---performed in various ways.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord saw the Ādi-keśava temple, He was immediately overwhelmed with ecstasy. Offering various obeisances and prayers, He chanted and danced.**
