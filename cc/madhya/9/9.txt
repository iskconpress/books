====== CC Madhya 9.9 ======

===== Text =====

> dakṣiṇa deśera loka aneka prakāra
> keha jñānī, keha karmī, pāṣaṇḍī apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:d:deśera]]---of South India; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---many; [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---varieties; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:j:jñānī]]---philosophical speculator; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:k:karmī]]---fruitive worker; [[synonyms:p:pāṣaṇḍī]]---nondevotees; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---innumerable.

===== Translation =====

**In South India there were many types of people. Some were philosophical speculators, and some were fruitive workers, but in any case there were innumerable nondevotees.**
