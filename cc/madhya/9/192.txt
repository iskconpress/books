====== CC Madhya 9.192 ======

===== Text =====

> sparśibāra kārya āchuka, nā pāya darśana
> sītāra ākṛti-māyā harila rāvaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sparśibāra]]---to touch; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---business; [[synonyms:ā:āchuka]]---let it be; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---sight; [[synonyms:s:sītāra]]---of mother Sītā; [[synonyms:ā:ākṛti]]-[[synonyms:m:māyā]]---the form made of //māyā//; [[synonyms:h:harila]]---took away; [[synonyms:r:rāvaṇa]]---the demon Rāvaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"To say nothing of touching mother Sītā, a person with material senses cannot even see her. When Rāvaṇa kidnapped her, he kidnapped only her material, illusory form.**
