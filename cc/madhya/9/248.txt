====== CC Madhya 9.248 ======

===== Text =====

> madhvācārya āni' tāṅre karilā sthāpana
> adyāvadhi sevā kare tattvavādi-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Madhvācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---installed; [[synonyms:a:adya]]-[[synonyms:a:avadhi]]---to date; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---worship; [[synonyms:t:tattvavādi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the Tattvavādīs.

===== Translation =====

**Madhvācārya brought this dancing Gopāla Deity to Uḍupī and installed Him in the temple. To date, the followers of Madhvācārya, known as Tattvavādīs, worship this Deity.**
