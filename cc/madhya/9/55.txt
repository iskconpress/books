====== Cc. Madhya 9.55 ======

===== Text =====

> bauddha-gaṇera upare anna paḍe amedhya haiyā
> bauddhācāryera māthāya thāli paḍila bājiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bauddha]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---all the Buddhists; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---the food; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---began to fall down; [[synonyms:a:amedhya]]---untouchable; [[synonyms:h:haiyā]]---being; [[synonyms:b:bauddha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of the teacher of the Buddhists; [[synonyms:m:māthāya]]---on the head; [[synonyms:t:thāli]]---the plate; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell down; [[synonyms:b:bājiyā]]---making a great sound.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, the untouchable food fell upon the Buddhists, and the large bird dropped the plate on the head of the chief Buddhist teacher. When it fell on his head, it made a big sound.**
