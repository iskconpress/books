====== CC Madhya 9.250 ======

===== Text =====

> tattvavādi-gaṇa prabhuke 'māyāvādī' jñāne
> prathama darśane prabhuke nā kaila sambhāṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tattvavādi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the Tattvavādīs; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:māyāvādī]] [[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---considering as a Māyāvādī //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:p:prathama]] [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in the first meeting; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---did not; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---do; [[synonyms:s:sambhāṣaṇe]]---addressing.

===== Translation =====

**When they first saw Him, the Tattvavādī Vaiṣṇavas considered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu a Māyāvādī sannyāsī. Therefore they did not talk to Him.**
