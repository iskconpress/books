====== CC Madhya 9.34 ======

===== Text =====

> ei vākye kṛṣṇa-nāmera mahimā apāra
> tathāpi la-ite nāri, śuna hetu tāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eivākye]]---in this statement; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---to chant; [[synonyms:n:nāri]]---I am unable; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---just hear; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---the reason; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that.

===== Translation =====

**"According to this statement of the śāstras, the glories of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa are unlimited. Still I could not chant His holy name. Please hear the reason for this.**
