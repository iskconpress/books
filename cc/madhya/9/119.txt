====== CC Madhya 9.119 ======

===== Text =====

> vinodinī lakṣmīra haya kṛṣṇe abhilāṣa
> ihāte ki doṣa, kene kara parihāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vinodinī]]---the enjoyer; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmīra]]---of the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---for Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:abhilāṣa]]---desire; [[synonyms:i:ihāte]]---in this; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You do; [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]]---joking.

===== Translation =====

**Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa further explained, "Mother Lakṣmī, the goddess of fortune, is also an enjoyer of transcendental bliss; therefore if she wanted to enjoy herself with Kṛṣṇa, what fault is there? Why are You joking so about this?"**
