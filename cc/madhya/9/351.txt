====== Cc. Madhya 9.351 ======

===== Text =====

> divya mahā-prasāda aneka ānāila
> pīṭhā-pānā ādi jagannātha ye khāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:divya]]---very nice; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food from Jagannātha; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---various; [[synonyms:ā:ānāila]]---brought; [[synonyms:p:pīṭhā]]-[[synonyms:p:pānā]] [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---such as cakes and condensed milk; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya brought various types of food remnants that had been left by Lord Jagannātha. He brought all kinds of cakes and condensed-milk preparations.**
