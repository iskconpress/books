====== CC Madhya 9.180 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛtamālāya snāna kari' āilā tāṅra ghare
> bhikṣā ki dibena vipra,---pāka nāhi kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛtamālāya]]---in the Kṛtamālā River; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---bathing; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the home; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---offering of alms; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:d:dibena]]---what shall give; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]---cooking; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---did not do.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing in the river Kṛtamālā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the brāhmaṇa's house to take lunch, but He saw that the food was unprepared because the brāhmaṇa had not cooked it.**
