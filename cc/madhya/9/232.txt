====== CC Madhya 9.232 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra astra tāra aṅge paḍe hāta haite
> khaṇḍa khaṇḍa haila bhaṭṭathāri palāya cāri bhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:a:astra]]---their weapons; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on their bodies; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fall; [[synonyms:h:hāta]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from their hands; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---cut into pieces; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭathāri]]---the nomads; [[synonyms:p:palāya]]---run away; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:b:bhite]]---in the four directions.

===== Translation =====

**However, their weapons fell from their hands and struck their own bodies. When some of the Bhaṭṭathāris were thus cut to pieces, the others ran away in the four directions.**
