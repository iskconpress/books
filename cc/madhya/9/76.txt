====== CC Madhya 9.76 ======

===== Text =====

> amṛtaliṅga-śiva dekhi' vandana karila
> saba śivālaye śaiva 'vaiṣṇava' ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:l:liṅga]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiva]]---the Lord Śiva deity named Amṛta-liṅga; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vandana]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaye]]---in all the temples of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ś:śaiva]]---devotees of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became devotees of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the Śiva deity named Amṛta-liṅga, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His obeisances. Thus He visited all the temples of Lord Śiva and converted the devotees of Lord Śiva into Vaiṣṇavas.**
