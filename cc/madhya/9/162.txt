====== CC Madhya 9.162 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' bhaṭṭa paḍilā prabhura caraṇe
> kṛpā kari' prabhu tāṅre kailā āliṅgane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing him mercy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgane]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa fell down before the lotus feet of the Lord, and the Lord, out of His causeless mercy, embraced him.**
