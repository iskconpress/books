====== Cc. Madhya 9.296 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha-miśra-ghare bhikṣā ye karila
> apūrva mocāra ghaṇṭa tāhāṅ ye khāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house of Śrī Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---lunch; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---took; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]]---unprecedented; [[synonyms:m:mocāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghaṇṭa]]---curry made of plantain flowers; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Śrī Raṅga Purī recalled Navadvīpa, he also recalled accompanying Śrī Mādhavendra Purī to the house of Jagannātha Miśra, where he had taken lunch. He even remembered the taste of an unprecedented curry made of banana flowers.**
