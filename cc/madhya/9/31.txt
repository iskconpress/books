====== CC Madhya 9.31 ======

===== Text =====

> paraṁ brahma dui-nāma samāna ha-ila
> punaḥ āra śāstre kichu viśeṣa pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:param]] [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---two names (Rāma and Kṛṣṇa); [[synonyms:s:samāna]]---on an equal level; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---were; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---further; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---specification; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---is found.

===== Translation =====

**"As far as the holy names of Rāma and Kṛṣṇa are concerned, they are on an equal level, but for further advancement we receive some specific information from revealed scriptures.**
