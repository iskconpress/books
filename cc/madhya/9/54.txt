====== CC Madhya 9.54 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle mahā-kāya eka pakṣī āila
> ṭhoṅṭe kari' anna-saha thāli lañā gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāya]]---having a large body; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:p:pakṣī]]---bird; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---appeared there; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhoṅṭe]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by the beak; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with food; [[synonyms:t:thāli]]---the plate; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went away.

===== Translation =====

**When the contaminated food was offered to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, a very large bird appeared on the spot, picked up the plate in its beak and flew away.**
