====== Cc. Madhya 9.169 ======

===== Text =====

> tina-dina preme doṅhe kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge
> sei vipra-ghare doṅhe rahe eka-saṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---three days; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:d:doṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---discussing topics of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in jubilation; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the home of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:d:doṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---stayed; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---together.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed with Paramānanda Purī in the brāhmaṇa's house where he was residing. The two of them passed three days there discussing topics of Kṛṣṇa.**
