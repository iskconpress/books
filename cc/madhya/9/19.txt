====== CC Madhya 9.19 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vipra rāma-nāma nirantara laya
> 'rāma''rāma' vinā anya vāṇī nā kahaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seivipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---chants; [[synonyms:r:rāma]] [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---the holy names Rāma Rāma; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---vibration; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:k:kahaya]]---speak.

===== Translation =====

**That brāhmaṇa constantly chanted the holy name of Rāmacandra. Indeed, but for chanting Lord Rāmacandra's holy name, that brāhmaṇa did not speak a word.**
