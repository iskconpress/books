====== Cc. Madhya 9.348 ======

===== Text =====

> mālā-prasāda pāñā prabhu susthira ha-ilā
> jagannāthera sevaka saba ānande mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the garland and //prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:sthira]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became patient; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servants; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:ānandemililā]]---met Him in great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became patient after receiving the garland and prasāda of Lord Jagannātha. All the servants of Lord Jagannātha met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with great pleasure.**
