====== CC Madhya 9.113 ======

===== Text =====

> ei lāgi' sukha-bhoga chāḍi' cira-kāla
> vrata-niyama kari' tapa karila apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for this reason; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---the enjoyment of Vaikuṇṭha; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:c:cira]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:v:vrata]]-[[synonyms:n:niyama]]---vows and regulative principles; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:t:tapa]]---austerity; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---performed unlimitedly.

===== Translation =====

**"Just to associate with Kṛṣṇa, Lakṣmī abandoned all transcendental happiness in Vaikuṇṭha and for a long time accepted vows and regulative principles and performed unlimited austerities."**
