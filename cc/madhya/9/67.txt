====== CC Madhya 9.67 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛsiṁhe praṇati-stuti premāveśe kaila
> prabhura prabhāve loka camatkāra haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhe]]---unto Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:p:praṇati]]-[[synonyms:s:stuti]]---obeisances and prayers; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhāve]]---by the influence; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---were astonished.

===== Translation =====

**In great ecstatic love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered obeisances and prayers unto Lord Nṛsiṁha. The people were astonished to see Lord Caitanya's influence.**
