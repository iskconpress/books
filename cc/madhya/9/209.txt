====== CC Madhya 9.209 ======

===== Text =====

> nūtana patra lekhāñā pustake deoyāila
> pratīti lāgi' purātana patra māgi' nila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nūtana]]---new; [[synonyms:p:patra]]---leaves; [[synonyms:l:lekhāñā]]---getting written; [[synonyms:p:pustake]]---the book; [[synonyms:d:deoyāila]]---He gave; [[synonyms:p:pratīti]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for direct evidence; [[synonyms:p:purātana]]---the old; [[synonyms:p:patra]]---leaves; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---requesting; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---He took.

===== Translation =====

**Since the Kūrma Purāṇa was very old, the manuscript was also very old. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took possession of the original leaves in order to have direct evidence. The text was copied onto new leaves in order that the Purāṇa be replaced.**
