====== CC Madhya 9.65 ======

===== Text =====

> tripati āsiyā kaila śrī-rāma daraśana
> raghunātha-āge kaila praṇāma stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tripati]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming to Tirupati; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visited the temple of Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---offering prayers.

===== Translation =====

**After arriving at Tirupati, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited the temple of Lord Rāmacandra. He offered His prayers and obeisances before Rāmacandra, the descendant of King Raghu.**
