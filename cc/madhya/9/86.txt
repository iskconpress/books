====== CC Madhya 9.86 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra ghare rahilā prabhu kṛṣṇa-kathā-rase
> bhaṭṭa-saṅge goṅāila sukhe cāri māse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in his home; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---stayed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---enjoying the transcendental mellow of discussing Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:g:goṅāila]]---passed; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māse]]---four months.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at the house of Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa for four continuous months. The Lord passed His days in great happiness, enjoying the transcendental mellow of discussing Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes.**
