====== CC Madhya 9.308 ======

===== Text =====

> saundarya-mādhurya-kṛṣṇa-līlāra avadhi
> sei jāne, ye 'karṇāmṛta' paḍe niravadhi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---beauty; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avadhi]]---limit; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---he knows; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---one who; [[synonyms:k:karṇāmṛta]]---the book //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta//; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---studies; [[synonyms:n:niravadhi]]---constantly.

===== Translation =====

**One who constantly reads the Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta can fully understand the beauty and melodious taste of the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
