====== Cc. Madhya 9.283 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe kaila bahuta kīrtana-nartana
> tāhāṅ eka vipra tāṅre kaila nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the great ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---much; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:n:nartana]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted and danced in various ways as usual. A brāhmaṇa, seeing Him in ecstatic love, was very much pleased and invited the Lord to his home for lunch.**
