====== CC Madhya 9.111 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---bhaṭṭa, tomāra lakṣmī-ṭhākurāṇī
> kānta-vakṣaḥ-sthitā, pativratā-śiromaṇi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---My dear Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:k:kānta]]---of her husband, Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:v:vakṣaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sthitā]]---situated on the chest; [[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:v:vratā]]---chaste woman; [[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---the topmost.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told the Bhaṭṭācārya, "Your worshipable goddess of fortune, Lakṣmī, always remains on the chest of Nārāyaṇa, and she is certainly the most chaste woman in the creation.**
