====== CC Madhya 9.167 ======

===== Text =====

> paramānanda-purī tāhāṅ rahe catur-māsa
> śuni' mahāprabhu gelā purī-gosāñira pāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:c:catur]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---four months; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---the spiritual master; [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---near.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī was staying at Ṛṣabha Hill during the four months of the rainy season, and when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this, He immediately went to see him.**
