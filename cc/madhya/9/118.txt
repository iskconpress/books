====== CC Madhya 9.118 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-saṅge pativratā-dharma nahe nāśa
> adhika lābha pāiye, āra rāsa-vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:v:vratā]]---of chastity; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---vow; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---lost; [[synonyms:a:adhika]]---more; [[synonyms:l:lābha]]---profit; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---I get; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the enjoyment in the //rāsa// dance.

===== Translation =====

**"The goddess of fortune considered that her vow of chastity would not be damaged by her relationship with Kṛṣṇa. Rather, by associating with Kṛṣṇa she could enjoy the benefit of the rāsa dance."**
