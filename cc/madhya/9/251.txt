====== CC Madhya 9.251 ======

===== Text =====

> pāche premāveśa dekhi' haila camatkāra
> vaiṣṇava-jñāne bahuta karila satkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---became struck with wonder; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---understanding as a Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---much; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:satkāra]]---reception.

===== Translation =====

**Later, after seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in ecstatic love, they were struck with wonder. Then, considering Him a Vaiṣṇava, they gave Him a nice reception.**
