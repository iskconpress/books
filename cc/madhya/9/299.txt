====== CC Madhya 9.299 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra eka yogya putra kariyāche sannyāsa
> 'śaṅkarāraṇya' nāma tāṅra alpa vayasa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---her; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---deserving; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---son; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]]---has accepted; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkarāraṇya]]---Śaṅkarāraṇya; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]---little; [[synonyms:v:vayasa]]---age.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Raṅga Purī also remembered that one of her deserving sons had accepted the renounced order at a very young age. His name was Śaṅkarāraṇya.**
