====== Cc. Madhya 9.75 ======

===== Text =====

> go-samāje śiva dekhi' āilā vedāvana
> mahādeva dekhi' tāṅre karilā vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:s:samāje]]---at the place named Go-samāja; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the deity of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]] [[synonyms:v:vedāvana]]---He arrived at Vedāvana; [[synonyms:m:mahādeva]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---offered prayers.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then visited a place known as Go-samāja, where He saw Lord Śiva's temple. He then arrived at Vedāvana, where He saw another deity of Lord Śiva and offered him prayers.**

===== Purport =====

Go-samāja is a place of pilgrimage for the devotees of Lord Śiva. It is very important and is located next to Vedāvana.
