====== CC Madhya 9.173 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' tāṅra ṭhāñi ei ājñā lañā
> dakṣiṇe calilā prabhu haraṣita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from him; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking permission; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇe]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed for southern India; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very much pleased.

===== Translation =====

**After talking in this way with Paramānanda Purī, the Lord took his permission to leave and, very much pleased, departed for southern India.**
