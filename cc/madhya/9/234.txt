====== CC Madhya 9.234 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dina cali' āilā payasvinī-tīre
> snāna kari' gelā ādi-keśava-mandire

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---on that very day; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---walking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:payasvinī]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---to the bank of the Payasvinī River; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---bathing; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:k:keśava]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to the temple of Ādi-keśava.

===== Translation =====

**That very night, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His assistant Kṛṣṇadāsa arrived at the bank of the Payasvinī River. They took their bath and then went to see the temple of Ādi-keśava.**
