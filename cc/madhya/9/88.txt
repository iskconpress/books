====== CC Madhya 9.88 ======

===== Text =====

> saundaryādi premāveśa dekhi, sarva-loka
> dekhibāre āise, dekhe, khaṇḍe duḥkha-śoka

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saundarya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the beauty of the body, etc.; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---His ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---all men; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---come there; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---and see; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍe]] [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---are relieved from all unhappiness and distress.

===== Translation =====

**The beauty of Lord Caitanya's body and His ecstatic love of God were witnessed by everyone. Many people used to come see Him, and as soon as they saw Him, all their unhappiness and distress vanished.**
