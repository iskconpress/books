====== CC Madhya 9.140 ======

===== Text =====

> ei tāṅra garva prabhu karite khaṇḍana
> parihāsa-dvāre uṭhāya eteka vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his (Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa's); [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍana]]---to curb; [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---by joking; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāya]]---raises; [[synonyms:e:eteka]]---so many; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had understood this misconception of Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa's, and to correct it the Lord talked so much in a joking way.**
