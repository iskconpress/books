====== CC Madhya 9.80 ======

===== Text =====

> kāverīte snāna kari' dekhi' raṅganātha
> stuti-praṇati kari' mānilā kṛtārtha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāverīte]]---in the river known as Kāverī; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after bathing; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---visiting; [[synonyms:r:raṅga]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]---the Raṅganātha temple; [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---prayers; [[synonyms:p:praṇati]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering; [[synonyms:m:mānilā]]---thought Himself; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---very successful.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing in the river Kāverī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the temple of Raṅganātha and offered His ardent prayers and obeisances. Thus He felt Himself successful.**
