====== CC Madhya 9.46 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṣaṇḍī āila yata pāṇḍitya śuniyā
> garva kari' āila saṅge śiṣya-gaṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṣaṇḍī]]---nonbelievers; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came there; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍitya]]---erudition; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:g:garva]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great pride; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came there; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---disciples; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**When the nonbelievers heard of the erudition of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they came to Him with great pride, bringing their disciples with them.**
