====== Cc. Madhya 9.350 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu lañā sārvabhauma nija-ghare gelā
> mora ghare bhikṣā bali' nimantraṇa kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his own home; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---luncheon; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---invited.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then took the Lord with him to his home, saying, "Today's luncheon will be at my home." In this way he invited the Lord.**
