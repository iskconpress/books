====== Cc. Madhya 9.125 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi jīva,---kṣudra-buddhi, sahaje asthira
> īśvarera līlā---koṭi-samudra-gambhīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---I am an ordinary living being; [[synonyms:k:kṣudra]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---possessing limited intelligence; [[synonyms:s:sahaje]] [[synonyms:a:asthira]]---very easily agitated; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:s:samudra]]---as millions of oceans; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---as deep.

===== Translation =====

**Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa then admitted, "I am an ordinary human being. Since my intelligence is very much limited and I am easily agitated, my mind cannot enter within the deep ocean of the pastimes of the Lord.**
