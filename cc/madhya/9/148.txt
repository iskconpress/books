====== CC Madhya 9.148 ======

===== Text =====

> nārāyaṇera kā kathā, śrī-kṛṣṇa āpane
> gopikāre hāsya karāite haya 'nārāyaṇe'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇera]]---of Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:k:kā]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---what to speak; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---Himself; [[synonyms:g:gopikāre]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:h:hāsya]] [[synonyms:k:karāite]]---to make them jubilant; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇe]]---in the form of Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"To say nothing of Lord Nārāyaṇa personally, Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself appeared as Nārāyaṇa just to play a joke on the gopīs.**
