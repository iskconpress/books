====== Cc. Madhya 9.202 ======

===== Text =====

> rāvaṇa dekhiyā sītā laila agnira śaraṇa
> rāvaṇa haite agni kaila sītāke āvaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāvaṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---after seeing Rāvaṇa; [[synonyms:s:sītā]]---mother Sītā; [[synonyms:l:laila]]---took; [[synonyms:a:agnira]]---of fire; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]---shelter; [[synonyms:r:rāvaṇa]]---Rāvaṇa; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:a:agni]]---fire; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sītāke]]---unto mother Sītā; [[synonyms:ā:āvaraṇa]]---covering.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāvaṇa came to kidnap mother Sītā and she saw him, she took shelter of the fire-god, Agni. The fire-god covered the body of mother Sītā, and in this way she was protected from the hands of Rāvaṇa.**
