====== CC Madhya 9.210 ======

===== Text =====

> patra lañā punaḥ dakṣiṇa-mathurā āilā
> rāmadāsa vipre sei patra āni dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patra]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking those leaves; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mathurā]]---to southern Mathurā; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:r:rāmadāsa]] [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---unto the //brāhmaṇa// known as Rāmadāsa; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:patra]]---those leaves; [[synonyms:ā:āni]]---bringing back; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to southern Mathurā [Madurai] and delivered the original manuscript of the Kūrma Purāṇa to Rāmadāsa Vipra.**
