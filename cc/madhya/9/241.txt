====== CC Madhya 9.241 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu yatne sei puṅthi nila lekhāiyā
> 'ananta padmanābha' āilā haraṣita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahuyatne]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:puṅthi]]---that scripture; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:l:lekhāiyā]]---having it copied; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:p:padmanābha]]---to Ananta Padmanābha; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu copied the Brahma-saṁhitā and afterwards, with great pleasure, went to a place known as Ananta Padmanābha.**

===== Purport =====

Concerning Ananta Padmanābha, one should refer to //Madhya-līlā,// Chapter One, text 115.
