====== Cc. Madhya 9.336 ======

===== Text =====

> yei pathe pūrve prabhu kailā āgamana
> sei pathe calilā dekhi, sarva vaiṣṇava-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---the path by which; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---came; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---by that way; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---Vaiṣṇavas.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned by the same road He formerly took to Vidyānagara, and all the Vaiṣṇavas along the way saw Him again.**
