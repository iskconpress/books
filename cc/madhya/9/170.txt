====== CC Madhya 9.170 ======

===== Text =====

> purī-gosāñi bale,---āmi yāba puruṣottame
> puruṣottama dekhi' gauḍe yāba gaṅgā-snāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---said; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---shall go; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottame]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after visiting Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]] [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---I shall go to Bengal; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:s:snāne]]---for bathing in the Ganges.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that he was going to see Puruṣottama at Jagannātha Purī. After seeing Lord Jagannātha there, he would go to Bengal to bathe in the Ganges.**
