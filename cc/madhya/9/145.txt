====== CC Madhya 9.145 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi ye paḍilā śloka, se haya pramāṇa
> sei śloke āise 'kṛṣṇa---svayaṁ bhagavān'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---have recited; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---evidence; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in that verse; [[synonyms:ā:āise]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa is; [[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"You have recited the śloka beginning with 'siddhāntatas tv abhede 'pi.' That very verse is evidence that Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
