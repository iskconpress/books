====== CC Madhya 9.344 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma mahāprabhura paḍilā caraṇe
> prabhu tāṅre uṭhāñā kaila āliṅgane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---making get up; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgane]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya fell down at the lotus feet of the Lord, and the Lord pulled him up and embraced him.**
