====== Cc. Madhya 4.165 ======

===== Text =====

> grīṣma-kāle gopīnātha paribe candana
> śuni' ānandita haila sevakera mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:grīṣma]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the summer; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Lord Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:p:paribe]]---will put on; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sevakera]]---of the servants; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds.

===== Translation =====

**The servants of Gopīnātha became very pleased to hear that in the summer all the sandalwood pulp would be used to anoint the body of Gopīnātha.**
