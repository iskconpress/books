====== CC Madhya 4.130 ======

===== Text =====

> svapna dekhi' pūjārī uṭhi' karilā vicāra
> snāna kari' kapāṭa khuli, mukta kaila dvāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svapna]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing the dream; [[synonyms:p:pūjārī]]---the priest; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---considerations; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking his bath before entering the Deity room; [[synonyms:k:kapāṭa]]---the door; [[synonyms:k:khuli]]---opening; [[synonyms:m:mukta]]---opened; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---the doors.

===== Translation =====

**Awaking from the dream, the priest immediately rose from bed and thought it wise to take a bath before entering the Deity's room. He then opened the temple door.**
