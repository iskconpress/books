====== CC Madhya 4.78 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā anubhava kaila mādhava gosāñi
> tāṅra ṭhāñi gopālera lukāna kichu nāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:a:anubhava]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---perceived; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Mādhavendra Purī Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---before him; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:l:lukāna]]---secret; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---there is not.

===== Translation =====

**How Gopāla ate everything while the food remained the same was transcendentally perceived by Mādhavendra Purī Gosvāmī; nothing remains a secret to the devotees of the Lord.**
