====== Cc. Madhya 4.187 ======

===== Text =====

> ei tāra gāḍha premā loke dekhāite
> gopāla tāṅre ājñā dila candana ānite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]---intense; [[synonyms:p:premā]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---unto the people; [[synonyms:d:dekhāite]]---to show; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood; [[synonyms:ā:ānite]]---to bring.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Gopāla wanted to show how intensely Mādhavendra Purī loved Kṛṣṇa; therefore He asked him to go to Nīlācala to fetch sandalwood and camphor.**
