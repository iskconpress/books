====== CC Madhya 4.146 ======

===== Text =====

> pratiṣṭhāra svabhāva ei jagate vidita
> ye nā vāñche, tāra haya vidhātā-nirmita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratiṣṭhāra]]---of reputation; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---the nature; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in the world; [[synonyms:v:vidita]]---known; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---the person who; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:v:vāñche]]---does not desire it; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it is; [[synonyms:v:vidhātā]]-[[synonyms:n:nirmita]]---created by providence.

===== Translation =====

**Even though one may not like it, reputation, as ordained by providence, comes to him. Indeed, one's transcendental reputation is known throughout the entire world.**
