====== CC Madhya 4.109 ======

===== Text =====

> sevāra nirbandha---loka karila sthāpana
> ājñā māgi' gauḍa-deśe karila gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sevāranirbandha]]---the arrangements for executing the daily routine of the worship of the Lord; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---establish; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---taking the order; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---toward Bengal; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**Before leaving, Mādhavendra Purī made all arrangements for regular Deity worship, and he engaged different people in various duties. Then, taking up the order of Gopāla, he started for Bengal.**
