====== Cc. Madhya 4.9 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva tāṅra pāye kari namaskāra
> tāṅra pāya aparādha nā ha-uk āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:pāye]]---at his lotus feet; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---to the lotus feet of Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:u:uk]]---let it happen; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my.

===== Translation =====

**I thus offer my respectful obeisances unto the lotus feet of Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura. I hope that I will not offend his lotus feet by this action.**
