====== CC Madhya 4.170 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-mukhe mādhava-purīra amṛta-carita
> bhakta-gaṇe śunāñā prabhu kare āsvādita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:c:carita]]---nectarean characteristics; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śunāñā]]---making hear; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādita]]---relished.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally praised the nectarean characteristics of Mādhavendra Purī, and while He related all this to the devotees, He personally relished it.**
