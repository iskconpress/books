====== CC Madhya 4.100 ======

===== Text =====

> svarṇa, raupya, vastra, gandha, bhakṣya-upahāra
> asaṅkhya āise, nitya bāḍila bhāṇḍāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarṇa]]---gold; [[synonyms:r:raupya]]---silver; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---garments; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---scents; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]]-[[synonyms:u:upahāra]]---presentations for eating; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]]---countless; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daiiy; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased; [[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍāra]]---the store.

===== Translation =====

**Thus countless presentations of gold, silver, garments, scented articles and eatables arrived. The store of Gopāla increased daily.**
