====== CC Madhya 4.74 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra pāśe dadhi, dugdha, māṭhā, śikhariṇī
> pāyasa, mathanī, sara pāśe dhari āni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---by the side of the vegetables; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---milk; [[synonyms:m:māṭhā]]---buttermilk; [[synonyms:ś:śikhariṇī]]---a sweet preparation made with yogurt; [[synonyms:p:pāyasa]]---sweet rice; [[synonyms:m:mathanī]]---cream; [[synonyms:s:sara]]---solid cream collected over yogurt; [[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---by the side; [[synonyms:d:dhari]]---keeping; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing.

===== Translation =====

**Pots of yogurt, milk, buttermilk and śikhariṇī, sweet rice, cream and solid cream were placed alongside the vegetables.**

===== Purport =====

In this kind of ceremony, which is called Annakūṭa, cooked rice is stacked like a small mountain for //prasāda// distribution.
