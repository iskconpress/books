====== CC Madhya 4.139 ======

===== Text =====

> pātra prakṣālana kari' khaṇḍa khaṇḍa kaila
> bahir-vāse bāndhi' sei ṭhikāri rākhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the pot; [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---washing; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---breaking into pieces; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bahir]]-[[synonyms:v:vāse]]---in his outer wrapper; [[synonyms:b:bāndhi']]---binding; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhikāri]]---pieces of pot; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Mādhavendra Purī washed the pot and broke it into pieces. He then bound all the pieces in his outer cloth and kept them nicely.**
