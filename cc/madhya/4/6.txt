====== Cc. Madhya 4.6 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva tāhā varṇile haya punarukti
> dambha kari' varṇi yadi taiche nāhi śakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---such activities; [[synonyms:v:varṇile]]---if describing; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:punarukti]]---repetition; [[synonyms:d:dambha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being proud; [[synonyms:v:varṇi]]---I describe; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---such; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore I very humbly submit that since these incidents have already been nicely described by Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura, I would be very proud to repeat the same thing, and this would not be very good. I do not have such powers.**
