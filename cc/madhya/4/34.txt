====== CC Madhya 4.34 ======

===== Text =====

> vasi' nāma laya purī, nidrā nāhi haya
> śeṣa-rātre tandrā haila,---bāhya-vṛtti-laya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting there; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---chants the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:n:nidrā]]---sleep; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was not; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at the end of the night; [[synonyms:t:tandrā]]---dozing; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛtti]]---of external activities; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---stop.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī could not sleep. He sat and chanted the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, and at the end of the night he dozed a little, and his external activities stopped.**
