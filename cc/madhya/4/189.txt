====== Cc. Madhya 4.189 ======

===== Text =====

> parīkṣā karite gopāla kaila ājñā dāna
> parīkṣā kariyā śeṣe haila dayāvān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parīkṣā]]---test; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to make; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---giving; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣā]]---the test; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:v:vān]]---merciful.

===== Translation =====

**"To test the intense love of Mādhavendra Purī, Gopāla, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, ordered him to bring sandalwood from Nīlācala, and when Mādhavendra Purī passed this examination, the Lord became very merciful to him.**
