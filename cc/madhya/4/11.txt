====== CC Madhya 4.11 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā lāgi' eka-dina eka grāma giyā
> āpane bahuta anna ānila māgiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for //prasāda//; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---in one day; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:g:grāma]]---to one village; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---a great quantity; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---of rice and other eatables; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought; [[synonyms:m:māgiyā]]---begging.

===== Translation =====

**Each day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally went to a village and collected a great quantity of rice and other grains for the preparation of prasāda.**
