====== CC Madhya 4.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya gauracandra jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaitacandra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---all glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---all glories to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---all glories to Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---all glories to the devotees of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Nityānanda Prabhu! All glories to Advaita Prabhu! And all glories to all the devotees of Lord Caitanya!**
