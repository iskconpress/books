====== CC Madhya 4.182 ======

===== Text =====

> 'maṇeka candana, tolā-viśeka karpūra
> gopāle parāiba'---ei ānanda pracura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maṇeka]] [[synonyms:c:candana]]---one //māna// of sandalwood; [[synonyms:t:tolā]]---a measurement of weight; [[synonyms:v:viśeka]]---twenty; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---camphor; [[synonyms:g:gopāle]]---on Gopāla; [[synonyms:p:parāiba]]---I shall smear; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:p:pracura]]---sufficient.

===== Translation =====

**"Without considering his personal comforts, Mādhavendra Purī carried one māna [about eighty-two pounds] of sandalwood and twenty tolās [about eight ounces] of camphor to smear over the body of Gopāla. This transcendental pleasure was sufficient for him.**
