====== Cc. Madhya 4.196 ======

===== Text =====

> śeṣa-kāle ei śloka paṭhite paṭhite
> siddhi-prāpti haila purīra ślokera sahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the end; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:p:paṭhite]] [[synonyms:p:paṭhite]]---by reciting repeatedly; [[synonyms:s:siddhi]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---attainment of perfection; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---this verse; [[synonyms:s:sahite]]---with.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī recited this verse again and again at the end of his material existence. Thus uttering this verse, he attained the ultimate goal of life.**
