====== Cc. Madhya 4.154 ======

===== Text =====

> calila mādhava-purī candana lañā
> kata-dine remuṇāte uttarila giyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:calila]]---proceeded; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:c:candana]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the sandalwood; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---after some days; [[synonyms:r:remuṇāte]]---at the same temple of Remuṇā; [[synonyms:u:uttarila]]---reached; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Mādhavendra Purī started for Vṛndāvana with the burden of sandalwood, and after some days he again reached the village of Remuṇā and the Gopīnātha temple there.**
