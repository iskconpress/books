====== CC Madhya 4.150 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla candana māge,---śuni' bhakta-gaṇa
> ānande candana lāgi' karila yatana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---wants; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:c:candana]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for sandalwood; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:y:yatana]]---endeavor.

===== Translation =====

**When all the devotees at Jagannātha Purī heard that the Gopāla Deity wanted sandalwood, in great pleasure they all endeavored to collect it.**
