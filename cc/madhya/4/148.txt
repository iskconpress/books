====== Cc. Madhya 4.148 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi udvega haila palāite mana
> ṭhākurera candana-sādhana ha-ila bandhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:u:udvega]]---anxiety; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:palāite]]---to go away; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurera]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---of the sandalwood; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---the collecting; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:bandhana]]---bondage.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī wanted to leave Jagannātha Purī because the people were honoring him as a great devotee; however, this threatened to hinder his collecting sandalwood for the Gopāla Deity.**
