====== CC Madhya 4.209 ======

===== Text =====

> nāma-saṅkīrtane sei rātri goṅāilā
> maṅgala-ārati dekhi' prabhāte calilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---in congregational chanting; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---night; [[synonyms:g:goṅāilā]]---passed; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārati]]---the early //ārati//; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed that night at the temple engaged in congregational chanting. In the morning, after seeing the maṅgala-ārati performance, He departed.**
