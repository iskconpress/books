====== CC Madhya 4.188 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu pariśrame candana remuṇā ānila
> ānanda bāḍila mane, duḥkha nā gaṇila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:p:pariśrame]]---with labor; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood; [[synonyms:r:remuṇā]]---to Remuṇā (the village of Gopīnātha); [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---difficulties; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:g:gaṇila]]---counted.

===== Translation =====

**"With great trouble and after much labor, Mādhavendra Purī brought the load of sandalwood to Remuṇā. However, he was still very pleased; he discounted all the difficulties.**
