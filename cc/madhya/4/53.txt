====== CC Madhya 4.53 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-mahā-baliṣṭha loka ekatra kariñā
> parvata-upari gela purī ṭhākura lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:baliṣṭha]]---who are very strong; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---persons; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]] [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---assembling; [[synonyms:p:parvata]]-[[synonyms:u:upari]]---to the top of the hill; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the Deity.

===== Translation =====

**Since the Deity was very heavy, some of the stronger men assembled to carry Him to the top of the hill. Mādhavendra Purī also went there.**
