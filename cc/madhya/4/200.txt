====== Cc. Madhya 4.200 ======

===== Text =====

> premonmāda haila, uṭhi' iti-uti dhāya
> huṅkāra karaye, hāse, kānde, nāce, gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāda]]---the madness of love; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:u:uti]] [[synonyms:d:dhāya]]---runs here and there; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---resounding; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---does; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laughs; [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---cries; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---and sings.

===== Translation =====

**Exhibiting ecstatic emotions, the Lord began to run here and there, making resounding noises. Sometimes He laughed, and sometimes He cried, and sometimes He danced and sang.**
