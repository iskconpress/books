====== CC Madhya 4.40 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra prema-vaśe kari' sevā aṅgīkāra
> darśana diyā nistāriba sakala saṁsāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśe]]---by being subjugated by the love; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---of service; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---acceptance; [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving audience; [[synonyms:n:nistāriba]]---I shall deliver; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---the material world.

===== Translation =====

**"I have accepted your service due to your ecstatic love for Me. Thus I shall appear, and by My audience all fallen souls will be delivered.**
