====== CC Madhya 4.54 ======

===== Text =====

> pātharera siṁhāsane ṭhākura vasāila
> baḍa eka pāthara pṛṣṭhe avalamba dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pātharera]]---of stone; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsane]]---on a throne; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---the Deity; [[synonyms:v:vasāila]]---installed; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---big; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:p:pāthara]]---stone; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣṭhe]]---at the back; [[synonyms:a:avalamba]]---support; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave.

===== Translation =====

**A big stone was made into a throne, and the Deity was installed upon it. Another big stone was placed behind the Deity for support.**
