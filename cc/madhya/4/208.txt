====== Cc. Madhya 4.208 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnātha-rūpe yadi kariyāchena bhojana
> bhakti dekhāite kaila prasāda bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in His //arcā// incarnation as Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---although; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]]---has done; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:d:dekhāite]]---to exhibit; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**Being identical with the Gopīnātha Deity, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had already tasted and eaten the pots of sweet rice. Yet just to manifest devotional service, He again ate the pots of sweet rice as a devotee.**
