====== CC Madhya 4.212 ======

===== Text =====

> śraddhā-yukta hañā ihā śune yei jana
> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caraṇe sei pāya prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]]-[[synonyms:y:yukta]]---possessed of faith; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---that; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---person; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the treasure of love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**One who hears this narration with faith and devotion attains the treasure of love of Godhead at the lotus feet of Śrī Kṛṣṇa.**
