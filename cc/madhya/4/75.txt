====== Cc. Madhya 4.75 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-mate anna-kūṭa karila sājana
> purī-gosāñi gopālere kaila samarpaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:k:kūṭa]]---of the Annakūṭa ceremony; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sājana]]---performance; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:g:gopālere]]---unto the Gopāla Deity; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---offering.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the Annakūṭa ceremony was performed, and Mādhavendra Purī Gosvāmī personally offered everything to Gopāla.**
