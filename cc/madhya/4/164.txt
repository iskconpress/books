====== Cc. Madhya 4.164 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāke candana dile, gopāla ha-ibe śītala
> svatantra īśvara---tāṅra ājñā se prabala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāke]]---unto Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:c:candana]] [[synonyms:d:dile]]---when sandalwood will be given; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---will become; [[synonyms:ś:śītala]]---cool; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the most independent Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prabala]]---powerful.

===== Translation =====

**"If the sandalwood pulp is smeared over the body of Gopīnātha, then Gopāla will be cooled. After all, the Supreme Personality of Godhead is completely independent; His order is all-powerful."**
