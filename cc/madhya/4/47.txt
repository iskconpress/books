====== Cc. Madhya 4.47 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-snāna kari' purī grāma-madhye gelā
> saba loka ekatra kari' kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---morning bath; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within the village; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---entered; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---assembling; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**After taking his morning bath, Mādhavendra Purī entered the village and assembled all the people. Then he spoke as follows.**
