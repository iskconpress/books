====== Cc. Madhya 4.159 ======

===== Text =====

> karpūra-sahita ghaṣi' e-saba candana
> gopīnāthera aṅge nitya karaha lepana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karpūra]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with the camphor; [[synonyms:g:ghaṣi']]---grinding; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all this; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthera]]---of Śrī Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---do; [[synonyms:l:lepana]]---smearing.

===== Translation =====

**"Now just grind all the sandalwood together with the camphor and then smear the pulp on the body of Gopīnātha daily until it is finished.**
