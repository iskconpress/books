====== Cc. Madhya 4.21 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve śrī-mādhava-purī āilā vṛndāvana
> bhramite, bhramite gelā giri govardhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Śrīla Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:b:bhramite]] [[synonyms:b:bhramite]]---while traveling; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:g:giri]] [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]---to the hill known as Govardhana.

===== Translation =====

**Once, Śrī Mādhavendra Purī traveled to Vṛndāvana, where he came upon the hill known as Govardhana.**
