====== CC Madhya 4.126 ======

===== Text =====

> nija kṛtya kari' pūjārī karila śayana
> svapane ṭhākura āsi' balilā vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---his own duty; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:p:pūjārī]]---the //brāhmaṇa// priest in the temple; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---took; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---rest; [[synonyms:s:svapane]]---in a dream; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---the Deity; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:b:balilā]]---said; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the words.

===== Translation =====

**Finishing his daily duties, the priest went to take rest. In a dream he saw the Gopīnātha Deity come to talk to him, and He spoke as follows.**
