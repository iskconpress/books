====== CC Madhya 4.138 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' namaskari' karilā gamana
> āveśe karilā purī se kṣīra bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---returned; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Purī Gosāñi; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---sweet rice; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**The priest offered his obeisances to Mādhavendra Purī and returned to the temple. Then, in ecstasy, Mādhavendra Purī ate the sweet rice offered to him by Kṛṣṇa.**
