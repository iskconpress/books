====== Cc. Madhya 4.82 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛṇa-ṭāṭi diyā cāri-dik āvarila
> uparete eka ṭāṭi diyā ācchādila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭāṭi]]---straw mattress; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---with; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dik]]---all around; [[synonyms:ā:āvarila]]---covered; [[synonyms:u:uparete]]---on top; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭāṭi]]---similar straw mattress; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---with; [[synonyms:ā:ācchādila]]---covered.

===== Translation =====

**A temporary temple was constructed by covering the bed all around with a straw mattress. Thus there was a bed and a straw mattress to cover it.**
