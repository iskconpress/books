====== CC Madhya 4.112 ======

===== Text =====

> remuṇāte kaila gopīnātha daraśana
> tāṅra rūpa dekhiñā haila vihvala-mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:remuṇāte]]---in the village of Remuṇā; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---the Deity of Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---beauty; [[synonyms:d:dekhiñā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---bewildered; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**Going into South India, Śrī Mādhavendra Purī visited Remuṇā, where Gopīnātha is situated. Upon seeing the beauty of the Deity, Mādhavendra Purī was overwhelmed.**
