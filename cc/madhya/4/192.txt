====== Cc. Madhya 4.192 ======

===== Text =====

> ghaṣite ghaṣite yaiche malayaja-sāra
> gandha bāḍe, taiche ei ślokera vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghaṣite]] [[synonyms:g:ghaṣite]]---rubbing and rubbing; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:m:malayaja]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---sandalwood; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---the fragrance; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---the consideration.

===== Translation =====

**Continuous rubbing increases the aroma of Malaya sandalwood. Similarly, consideration of this verse increases one's understanding of its importance.**
