====== CC Madhya 4.203 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloke ughāḍilā premera kapāṭa
> gopīnātha-sevaka dekhe prabhura prema-nāṭa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---this verse; [[synonyms:u:ughāḍilā]]---uncovered; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of conjugal love; [[synonyms:k:kapāṭa]]---the door; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servants of the Gopīnātha Deity; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---see; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:n:nāṭa]]---the dance in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**This verse uncovered the door of ecstatic love, and when it was exhibited, all the servants of Gopīnātha saw Caitanya Mahāprabhu dance in ecstasy.**
