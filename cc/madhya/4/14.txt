====== Cc. Madhya 4.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra pāda-padma nikaṭa praṇāma karite
> tāṅra puṣpa-cūḍā paḍila prabhura māthāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]]---the lotus feet of Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near; [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---while offering; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:c:cūḍā]]---helmet of flowers; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell down; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:m:māthāte]]---on the head.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His obeisances at the lotus feet of the Gopīnātha Deity, the helmet of flowers on the head of Gopīnātha fell down and landed on the head of Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
