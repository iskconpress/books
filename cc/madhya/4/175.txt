====== Cc. Madhya 4.175 ======

===== Text =====

> karpūra-candana yāṅra aṅge caḍāila
> ānande purī-gosāñira prema uthalila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karpūra]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---camphor and sandalwood; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on whose body; [[synonyms:c:caḍāila]]---put; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pieasure; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:u:uthalila]]---welled up.

===== Translation =====

**"Mādhavendra Purī smeared the sandalwood pulp over the body of Gopīnātha, and in this way he was overpowered with love of Godhead.**
