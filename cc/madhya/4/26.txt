====== CC Madhya 4.26 ======

===== Text =====

> bālakera saundarye purīra ha-ila santoṣa
> tāhāra madhura-vākye gela bhoka-śoṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bālakera]]---of the boy; [[synonyms:s:saundarye]]---in the beauty; [[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---very much satisfaction; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]]---by the sweet words; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---forgot; [[synonyms:b:bhoka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śoṣa]]---all hunger and thirst.

===== Translation =====

**When he saw the beauty of that boy, Mādhavendra Purī became very satisfied. Hearing His sweet words, he forgot all hunger and thirst.**
