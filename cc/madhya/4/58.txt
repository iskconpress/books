====== CC Madhya 4.58 ======

===== Text =====

> bhoga-sāmagrī āila sandeśādi yata
> nānā upahāra, tāhā kahite pāri kata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]-[[synonyms:s:sāmagrī]]---ingredients for eatables to be offered; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---brought in; [[synonyms:s:sandeśa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---sweetmeats; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all kinds of; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:u:upahāra]]---presentations; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to say; [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am able; [[synonyms:k:kata]]---how much.

===== Translation =====

**Various foods and sweetmeats, as well as other kinds of presentations, were brought there. I am unable to describe all these.**
