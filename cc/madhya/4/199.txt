====== Cc. Madhya 4.199 ======

===== Text =====

> āste-vyaste kole kari' nila nityānanda
> krandana kariyā tabe uṭhe gauracandra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---in great haste; [[synonyms:k:kole]]---on the lap; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---got up; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell to the ground in ecstatic love, Lord Nityānanda quickly took Him on His lap. Crying, Caitanya Mahāprabhu then got up again.**
