====== CC Madhya 4.204 ======

===== Text =====

> lokera saṅghaṭṭa dekhi' prabhura bāhya haila
> ṭhākurera bhoga sari' ārati bājila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of people; [[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭa]]---a crowd; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---appeared; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurera]]---of the Deity; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---offering; [[synonyms:s:sari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:ā:ārati]]---performance of //ārati//; [[synonyms:b:bājila]]---resounded.

===== Translation =====

**When many people crowded around Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, He regained His external senses. In the meantime, the offering to the Deity had been finished, and there was a resounding ārati performance.**
