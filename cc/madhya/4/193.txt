====== Cc. Madhya 4.193 ======

===== Text =====

> ratna-gaṇa-madhye yaiche kaustubha-maṇi
> rasa-kāvya-madhye taiche ei śloka gaṇi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the valuable jewels; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:kaustubha]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇi]]---the jewel known as Kaustubha-maṇi; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:k:kāvya]]---poetry dealing with the mellows of devotional service; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:g:gaṇi]]---I count.

===== Translation =====

**As the Kaustubha-maṇi is considered the most precious of valuable stones, this verse is similarly considered the best of poems dealing with the mellows of devotional service.**
