====== Cc. Madhya 4.83 ======

===== Text =====

> purī-gosāñi ājñā dila sakala brāhmaṇe
> ā-bāla-vṛddha grāmera loka karāha bhojane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇe]]---to all the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:ā:ā]]-[[synonyms:b:bāla]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛddha]]---beginning from the children up to the old persons; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---of the village; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:k:karāha]]---make; [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---take //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**After the Lord was laid down to rest on the bed, Mādhavendra Purī gathered all the brāhmaṇas who had prepared the prasāda and said to them, "Now feed everyone sumptuously, from the children on up to the aged!"**
