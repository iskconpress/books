====== CC Madhya 4.180 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-jana gopālera ājñāmṛta pāñā
> sahasra krośa āsi' bule candana māgiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---such a personality; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of the Gopāla Deity; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the nectarean order; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---a thousand; [[synonyms:k:krośa]]---a distance of two miles; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---walks; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood; [[synonyms:m:māgiñā]]---begging.

===== Translation =====

**"After receiving the transcendental orders of Gopāla, this great personality traveled thousands of miles just to collect sandalwood by begging.**
