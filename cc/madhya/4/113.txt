====== Cc. Madhya 4.113 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛtya-gīta kari' jaga-mohane vasilā
> 'kyā kyā bhoga lāge?' brāhmaṇe puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after performing dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:j:jaga]]-[[synonyms:m:mohane]]---in the corridor of the temple; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---he sat down; [[synonyms:k:kyā]] [[synonyms:k:kyā]]---what; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---foods; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---they offer; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇe]]---from the //brāhmaṇa// priest; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired.

===== Translation =====

**In the corridor of the temple, from which people generally viewed the Deity, Mādhavendra Purī chanted and danced. Then he sat down there and asked a brāhmaṇa what kinds of foods they offered to the Deity.**
