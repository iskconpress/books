====== CC Madhya 4.194 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloka kahiyāchena rādhā-ṭhākurāṇī
> tāṅra kṛpāya sphuriyāche mādhavendra-vāṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:k:kahiyāchena]]---has spoken; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---Her; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:s:sphuriyāche]]---has manifested; [[synonyms:m:mādhavendra]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---the words.

===== Translation =====

**Actually this verse was spoken by Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī Herself, and by Her mercy only was it manifest in the words of Mādhavendra Purī.**
