====== CC Madhya 4.153 ======

===== Text =====

> ghāṭī-dānī chāḍāite rāja-pātra dvāre
> rāja-lekhā kari' dila purī-gosāñira kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghāṭī]]-[[synonyms:d:dānī]]---from the toll collectors; [[synonyms:c:chāḍāite]]---to get release; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---papers showing governmental sanction; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the gates; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:l:lekhā]]---governmental permission; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Purī Gosāñi, Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---into the hand.

===== Translation =====

**To get past the toll collectors along the way, Mādhavendra Purī was supplied with the necessary release papers from government officers. The papers were placed in his hand.**
