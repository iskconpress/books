====== CC Madhya 4.205 ======

===== Text =====

> ṭhākure śayana karāñā pūjārī haila bāhira
> prabhura āge āni' dila prasāda bāra kṣīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākure]]---the Deity; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down; [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---causing to do; [[synonyms:p:pūjārī]]---the priest; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:b:bāhira]]---out of the temple; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---offered; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of food; [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---twelve; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---pots of sweet rice.

===== Translation =====

**When the Deities were laid down to rest, the priest came out of the temple and offered all twelve pots of sweet rice to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
