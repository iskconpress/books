====== CC Madhya 4.211 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' ākhyāne kahilā doṅhāra mahimā
> prabhura bhakta-vātsalya, āra bhakta-prema-sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāne]]---in the narration; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---described; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāra]]---of the two; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]---attachment for His devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the highest limit of ecstatic love by the devotee.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described both the transcendental glories of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's affection for His devotees and the highest limit of ecstatic love of God.**
