====== Cc. Madhya 4.66 ======

===== Text =====

> ārātrika kari' kaila bahuta stavana
> daṇḍavat kari' kaila ātma-samarpaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ārātrika]]---the performance of //ārātrika//; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---chanted; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---varieties; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---of prayers; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---self-surrender.

===== Translation =====

**After the last offering of tāmbūla and pan, bhoga-ārātrika was performed. Finally everyone offered various prayers and then obeisances, falling flat before the Deity in full surrender.**
