====== CC Madhya 4.101 ======

===== Text =====

> eka mahā-dhanī kṣatriya karāila mandira
> keha pāka-bhāṇḍāra kaila, keha ta' prācīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:dhanī]]---very rich man; [[synonyms:k:kṣatriya]]---of the royal order; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---constructed; [[synonyms:m:mandira]]---a temple; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍāra]]---clay pots for cooking; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:prācīra]]---the boundary walls.

===== Translation =====

**One very rich kṣatriya of the royal order constructed a temple, someone made cooking utensils, and someone constructed boundary walls.**
