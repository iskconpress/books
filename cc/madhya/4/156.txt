====== Cc. Madhya 4.156 ======

===== Text =====

> purī dekhi' sevaka saba sammāna karila
> kṣīra-prasāda diyā tāṅre bhikṣā karāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the priest or servant; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---all respects; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---offered; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---sweet rice //prasāda//; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made to eat.

===== Translation =====

**When the priest of Gopīnātha saw Mādhavendra Purī again, he offered all respects to him and, giving him the sweet rice prasāda, made him eat.**
