====== CC Madhya 4.143 ======

===== Text =====

> cali' cali' āilā purī śrī-nīlācala
> jagannātha dekhi' hailā premete vihvala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cali']] [[synonyms:c:cali']]---walking and walking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---he reached; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---known as Nīlācala; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:premete]]---in love; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**Walking and walking, Mādhavendra Purī finally reached Jagannātha Purī, which is also known as Nīlācala. There he saw Lord Jagannātha and was overwhelmed with loving ecstasy.**
