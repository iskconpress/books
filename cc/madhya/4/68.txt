====== CC Madhya 4.68 ======

===== Text =====

> kumbhakāra ghare chila ye mṛd-bhājana
> saba ānāila prāte, caḍila randhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kumbhakāra]]---of the potters of the village; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the houses; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---there was; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:m:mṛd]]-[[synonyms:b:bhājana]]---clay pots; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:ānāila]]---brought; [[synonyms:p:prāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:c:caḍila]]---started; [[synonyms:r:randhana]]---cooking.

===== Translation =====

**When the villagers brought their stock of rice, dāl and flour, the potters of the village brought all kinds of cooking pots, and in the morning the cooking began.**
