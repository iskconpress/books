====== CC Madhya 4.129 ======

===== Text =====

> mādhava-purī sannyāsī āche hāṭete vasiñā
> tāhāke ta' ei kṣīra śīghra deha lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---of the name Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---a mendicant; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:h:hāṭete]]---in the marketplace; [[synonyms:v:vasiñā]]---sitting; [[synonyms:t:tāhāke]]---to him; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---pot of sweet rice; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very quickly; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---give; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**"A sannyāsī named Mādhavendra Purī is sitting in the vacant marketplace. Please take this pot of sweet rice from behind Me and deliver it to him."**
