====== CC Madhya 4.48 ======

===== Text =====

> grāmera īśvara tomāra---govardhana-dhārī
> kuñje āche, cala, tāṅre bāhira ye kari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---of the village; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the proprietor; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:d:dhārī]]---the lifter of Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:k:kuñje]] [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---in the bushes in the jungle; [[synonyms:c:cala]]---let us go; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:b:bāhira]] [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---take out.

===== Translation =====

**"The proprietor of this village, Govardhana-dhārī, is lying in the bushes. Let us go there and rescue Him from that place.**
