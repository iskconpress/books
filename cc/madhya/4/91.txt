====== Cc. Madhya 4.91 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-kāle ṭhākurere karāiyā śayana
> purī-gosāñi kaila kichu gavya bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at night; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurere]]---the Deity; [[synonyms:k:karāiyā]]---causing to do; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down for rest; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:g:gavya]]---milk preparation; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Mādhavendra Purī did not eat anything throughout the day, but at night, after laying the Deity down to rest, he took a milk preparation.**
