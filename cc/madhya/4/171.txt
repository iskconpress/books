====== CC Madhya 4.171 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---nityānanda, karaha vicāra
> purī-sama bhāgyavān jagate nāhi āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---just consider; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---fortunate; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in the world; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---anyone else.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked Nityānanda Prabhu to judge whether there was anyone within the world as fortunate as Mādhavendra Purī.**
