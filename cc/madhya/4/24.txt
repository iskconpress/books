====== Cc. Madhya 4.24 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla-bālaka eka dugdha-bhāṇḍa lañā
> āsi' āge dhari' kichu balila hāsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:b:bālaka]]---cowherd boy; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking a pot of milk; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:ā:āge]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---holding it in front; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:b:balila]]---said; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling.

===== Translation =====

**While he was sitting beneath a tree, an unknown cowherd boy came with a pot of milk, placed it before Mādhavendra Purī and, smiling, addressed him as follows.**
