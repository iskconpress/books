====== CC Madhya 4.149 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera sevaka yata, yateka mahānta
> sabāke kahila purī gopāla-vṛttānta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]] [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servants of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:y:yateka]] [[synonyms:m:mahānta]]---all respectable devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabāke]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---told; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]]---the narration of Gopāla.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Mādhavendra Purī told all the servants of Lord Jagannātha and all the great devotees there the story of the appearance of Śrī Gopāla.**
