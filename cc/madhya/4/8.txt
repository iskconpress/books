====== Cc. Madhya 4.8 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra sūtre āche, teṅha nā kaila varṇana
> yathā-kathañcit kari' se līlā kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sūtre]]---in the synopsis; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---did not describe; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:k:kathañcit]]---something of them; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:s:se]]---these; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---narration.

===== Translation =====

**Some of the incidents he did not describe elaborately but only summarized, and these I shall try to describe in this book.**
