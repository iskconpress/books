====== CC Madhya 4.23 ======

===== Text =====

> śaila parikramā kari' govinda-kuṇḍe āsi'
> snāna kari, vṛkṣa-tale āche sandhyāya vasi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śaila]]---the hill; [[synonyms:p:parikramā]]---circumambulation; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍe]]---to the bank of the Govinda-kuṇḍa; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---taking a bath; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---under the shade of a tree; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---is; [[synonyms:s:sandhyāya]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---resting.

===== Translation =====

**After circumambulating the hill, Mādhavendra Purī went to Govinda-kuṇḍa and took his bath. He then sat beneath a tree to take his evening rest.**
