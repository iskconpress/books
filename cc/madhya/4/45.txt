====== Cc. Madhya 4.45 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇake dekhinu muñi nārinu cinite
> eta bali' premāveśe paḍilā bhūmite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇake]] [[synonyms:d:dekhinu]]---saw Lord Kṛṣṇa personally; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nārinu]]---was unable; [[synonyms:c:cinite]]---to identify; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī began to lament, "I saw Lord Kṛṣṇa directly, but I could not recognize Him!" Thus he fell down on the ground in ecstatic love.**
