====== CC Madhya 4.207 ======

===== Text =====

> sāta kṣīra pūjārīke bāhuḍiyā dila
> pañca-kṣīra pañca-jane vāṅṭiyā khāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---seven pots of sweet rice; [[synonyms:p:pūjārīke]]---toward the //pūjārī//; [[synonyms:b:bāhuḍiyā]]---pushing forward; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---five pots of sweet rice; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---to five men; [[synonyms:v:vāṅṭiyā]]---distributing; [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate.

===== Translation =====

**The seven remaining pots were pushed forward and delivered to the priest. Then the five pots of sweet rice the Lord had accepted were distributed among the five devotees, and they ate the prasāda.**
