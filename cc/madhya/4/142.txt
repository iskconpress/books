====== CC Madhya 4.142 ======

===== Text =====

> sei bhaye rātri-śeṣe calilā śrī-purī
> sei-khāne gopīnāthe daṇḍavat kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---fearing that; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end of the night; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---left; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Śrī Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:khāne]]---on that spot; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthe]]---to Lord Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking this, Śrī Mādhavendra Purī offered his obeisances to Gopīnātha on the spot and left Remuṇā before morning.**
