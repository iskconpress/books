====== CC Madhya 4.177 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-dayā-maya prabhu---bhakata-vatsala
> candana pari' bhakta-śrama karila saphala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]---very; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhakata]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsala]]---very attached to His devotees; [[synonyms:c:candana]] [[synonyms:p:pari']]---putting on the sandalwood; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---the trouble of the devotee; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:s:saphala]]---successful.

===== Translation =====

**"The Lord is very merciful and attached to His devotees, so when Gopīnātha was covered with sandalwood pulp, Mādhavendra Purī's labor became successful."**
