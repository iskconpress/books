====== CC Madhya 4.127 ======

===== Text =====

> uṭhaha, pūjārī, kara dvāra vimocana
> kṣīra eka rākhiyāchi sannyāsi-kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uṭhaha]]---please get up; [[synonyms:p:pūjārī]]---O priest; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---just do; [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---the door; [[synonyms:v:vimocana]]---opening; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---sweet rice; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one pot; [[synonyms:r:rākhiyāchi]]---I have kept; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsi]]---of the mendicant, Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---for the reason.

===== Translation =====

**"O priest, please get up and open the door of the temple. I have kept one pot of sweet rice for the sannyāsī Mādhavendra Purī.**
