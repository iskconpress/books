====== Cc. Madhya 4.103 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍa ha-ite āilā dui vairāgī brāhmaṇa
> purī-gosāñi rākhila tāre kariyā yatana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from Bengal; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:v:vairāgī]]---of the renounced order; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---persons born in //brāhmaṇa// families; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---them; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making; [[synonyms:y:yatana]]---all efforts.

===== Translation =====

**Eventually two brāhmaṇas in the renounced order arrived from Bengal, and Mādhavendra Purī, who liked them very much, kept them in Vṛndāvana and gave them all kinds of comforts.**
