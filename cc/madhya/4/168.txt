====== CC Madhya 4.168 ======

===== Text =====

> pratyaha candana parāya, yāvat haila anta
> tathāya rahila purī tāvat paryanta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratyaha]]---daily; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:p:parāya]]---smears over the body; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---until; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---an end; [[synonyms:t:tathāya]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]]---that time; [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---until.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the sandalwood pulp was smeared over the body of Gopīnātha until the whole stock was finished. Mādhavendra Purī stayed there until that time.**
