====== CC Madhya 4.15 ======

===== Text =====

> cūḍā pāñā mahāprabhura ānandita mana
> bahu nṛtya-gīta kaila lañā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cūḍāpāñā]]---getting the helmet; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---various kinds; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**When the Deity's helmet fell upon His head, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very pleased, and thus He chanted and danced in various ways with His devotees.**
