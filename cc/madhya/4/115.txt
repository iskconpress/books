====== Cc. Madhya 4.115 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche ihā bhoga lāge, sakala-i puchiba
> taiche bhiyāne bhoga gopāle lāgāiba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just like; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---here; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---foods; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---they offer; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---all; [[synonyms:p:puchiba]]---I shall inquire; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:b:bhiyāne]]---in the kitchen; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---foods; [[synonyms:g:gopāle]]---to Śrī Gopāla; [[synonyms:l:lāgāiba]]---I shall arrange.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī thought, "I shall inquire from the priest what foods are offered to Gopīnātha so that by making arrangements in our kitchen, we can offer similar foods to Śrī Gopāla."**
