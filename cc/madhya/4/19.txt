====== CC Madhya 4.19 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṣīra-corā gopīnātha' prasiddha tāṅra nāma
> bhakta-gaṇe kahe prabhu sei ta' ākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:c:corā]] [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---the Gopīnātha who stole the pot of sweet rice; [[synonyms:p:prasiddha]]---very famous; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---His name; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---tells; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāna]]---that narration.

===== Translation =====

**That Deity was known widely as Kṣīra-corā-gopīnātha, and Caitanya Mahāprabhu told His devotees the story of how the Deity became so famous.**
