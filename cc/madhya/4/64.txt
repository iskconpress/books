====== CC Madhya 4.64 ======

===== Text =====

> dhūpa, dīpa, kari' nānā bhoga lāgāila
> dadhi-dugdha-sandeśādi yata kichu āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhūpa]]---incense; [[synonyms:d:dīpa]]---lamp; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---burning; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---foods; [[synonyms:l:lāgāila]]---were offered; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---milk; [[synonyms:s:sandeśa]]---sweetmeats; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as much as; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---received.

===== Translation =====

**After the bathing ceremony was finished, incense and lamps were burned and all kinds of food offered before the Deity. These foods included yogurt, milk and as many sweetmeats as were received.**
