====== CC Madhya 4.98 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla-prakaṭa śuni' nānā deśa haite
> nānā dravya lañā loka lāgila āsite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---of the Deity of Gopāla; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---appearance; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:d:deśa]]---countries; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---things; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began; [[synonyms:ā:āsite]]---to come.

===== Translation =====

**In this way not only the neighboring villages but all the other provinces came to know of Gopāla's appearance. Thus people came from all over, bringing a variety of presentations.**
