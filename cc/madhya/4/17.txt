====== CC Madhya 4.17 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā-rūpe prītye kaila prabhura sevana
> sei rātri tāhāṅ prabhu karilā vañcana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in various ways; [[synonyms:p:prītye]]---on account of love; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---that night; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vañcana]]---passing.

===== Translation =====

**Because of their love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they served Him in many ways, and that night the Lord stayed at the temple of Gopīnātha.**
