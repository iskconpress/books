====== CC Madhya 4.114 ======

===== Text =====

> sevāra sauṣṭhava dekhi' ānandita mane
> uttama bhoga lāge---ethā bujhi anumāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sevāra]]---of the worship; [[synonyms:s:sauṣṭhava]]---excellence; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:u:uttama]] [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---first-class foods; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---they offer; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bujhi]]---I understand; [[synonyms:a:anumāne]]---by deduction.

===== Translation =====

**From the excellence of the arrangements, Mādhavendra Purī understood by deduction that only the best food was offered.**
