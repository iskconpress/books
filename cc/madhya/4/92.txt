====== CC Madhya 4.92 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle punaḥ taiche karila sevana
> anna lañā eka-grāmera āila loka-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---just as before; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---rendered; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with food grains; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---of one village; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the people.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning, the rendering of service to the Deity began again, and people from one village arrived with all kinds of food grains.**
