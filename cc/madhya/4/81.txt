====== Cc. Madhya 4.81 ======

===== Text =====

> śayyā karāila, nūtana khāṭa ānāñā
> nava vastra āni' tāra upare pātiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śayyā]]---a bedstead; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nūtana]]---new; [[synonyms:k:khāṭa]]---bedstead; [[synonyms:ā:ānāñā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:n:nava]] [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---new cloth; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of the cot; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the top; [[synonyms:p:pātiyā]]---spreading.

===== Translation =====

**Arranging for the Lord's rest, Śrī Mādhavendra Purī brought a new cot, and over this he spread a new bedspread and thus made the bed ready.**
