====== CC Madhya 4.97 ======

===== Text =====

> āśa-pāśa vraja-bhūmera yata grāma saba
> eka eka dina sabe kare mahotsava

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āśa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---neighboring; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūmera]]---of Vrajabhūmi; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]---villages; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one after another; [[synonyms:d:dina]]---days; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---perform; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:utsava]]---festivals.

===== Translation =====

**All the villages in neighboring Vrajabhūmi [Vṛndāvana] became aware of the appearance of Gopāla, and all the people from these villages came to see Him. Day after day they all performed the Annakūṭa ceremony.**
