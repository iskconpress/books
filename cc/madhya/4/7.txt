====== CC Madhya 4.7 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-maṅgale yāhā karila varṇana
> sūtra-rūpe sei līlā kariye sūcana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgale]]---in the book named //Caitanya-maṅgala//; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---has described; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of a synopsis; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---those pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:s:sūcana]]---I shall present.

===== Translation =====

**I am therefore presenting only a synopsis of those events already described elaborately by Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura in his Caitanya-maṅgala [now known as Caitanya-bhāgavata].**
