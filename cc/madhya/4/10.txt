====== CC Madhya 4.10 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu calilā nīlācale
> cāri bhakta saṅge kṛṣṇa-kīrtana-kutūhale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---proceeded; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---toward Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---four devotees; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---for chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kutūhale]]---in great eagerness.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu proceeded toward Jagannātha Purī with four of His devotees, and He chanted the holy name of the Lord, the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra, with great eagerness.**
