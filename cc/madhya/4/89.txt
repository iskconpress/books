====== Cc. Madhya 4.89 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla prakaṭa haila,---deśe śabda haila
> āśa-pāśa grāmera loka dekhite āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---has appeared; [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---throughout the country; [[synonyms:ś:śabda]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---the news spread; [[synonyms:ā:āśa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---neighboring; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---of the villages; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came to see.

===== Translation =====

**When it was advertised throughout the country that Lord Gopāla had appeared atop Govardhana Hill, all the people from neighboring villages came to see the Deity.**
