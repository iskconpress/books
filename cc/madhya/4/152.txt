====== CC Madhya 4.152 ======

===== Text =====

> eka vipra, eka sevaka, candana vahite
> purī-gosāñira saṅge dila sambala-sahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekavipra]]---one //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---one servant; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---the sandalwood; [[synonyms:v:vahite]]---to carry; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:s:sambala]]-[[synonyms:s:sahite]]---with the necessary expenditure.

===== Translation =====

**One brāhmaṇa and one servant were given to Mādhavendra Purī just to carry the sandalwood. He was also given the necessary traveling expenses.**
