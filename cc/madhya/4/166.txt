====== CC Madhya 4.166 ======

===== Text =====

> purī kahe,---ei dui ghaṣibe candana
> āra janā-dui deha, diba ye vetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Mādhavendra Purī said; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]---these two assistants; [[synonyms:g:ghaṣibe]]---will grind; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---the sandalwood; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:j:janā]]-[[synonyms:d:dui]]---two men; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---apply; [[synonyms:d:diba]]---I shall pay; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vetana]]---salary.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī said, "These two assistants will regularly grind the sandalwood, and you should also get two other people to help. I shall pay their salary."**
