====== CC Madhya 4.16 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura prabhāva dekhi' prema-rūpa-guṇa
> vismita ha-ilā gopīnāthera dāsa-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---the influence; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---His beauty; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---and His qualities; [[synonyms:v:vismita]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became struck with wonder; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthera]]---of the Gopīnātha Deity; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the servants.

===== Translation =====

**All the servants of the Deity were struck with wonder due to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's intense love, His exquisite beauty and His transcendental qualities.**
