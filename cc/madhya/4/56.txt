====== CC Madhya 4.56 ======

===== Text =====

> nava śata-ghaṭa jala kaila upanīta
> nānā vādya-bherī bāje, strī-gaṇa gāya gīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nava]]---nine; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:g:ghaṭa]]---hundreds of waterpots; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:u:upanīta]]---brought; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:v:vādya]]---musical sounds; [[synonyms:b:bherī]]---bugles; [[synonyms:b:bāje]]---vibrate; [[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the women; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---various songs.

===== Translation =====

**When the Deity was being installed, nine hundred pots of water were brought from Govinda-kuṇḍa. There were musical sounds of bugles and drums and the singing of women.**
