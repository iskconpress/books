====== CC Madhya 4.132 ======

===== Text =====

> dvāra diyā grāme gelā sei kṣīra lañā
> hāṭe hāṭe bule mādhava-purīke cāhiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvāradiyā]]---closing the door; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---to the village; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---pot of sweet rice; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:h:hāṭe]] [[synonyms:h:hāṭe]]---in every stall; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---walks; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purīke]]---to Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:c:cāhiñā]]---calling.

===== Translation =====

**Closing the door of the temple, he went to the village with the pot of sweet rice. He called out in every stall in search of Mādhavendra Purī.**
