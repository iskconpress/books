====== CC Madhya 4.90 ======

===== Text =====

> ekeka dina ekeka grāme la-ila māgiñā
> anna-kūṭa kare sabe haraṣita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekekadina]]---one day after another; [[synonyms:e:ekeka]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---one village after another; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---took permission; [[synonyms:m:māgiñā]]---begging; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:k:kūṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---perform the Annakūṭa ceremony; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**One village after another was pleased to beg Mādhavendra Purī to allot them one day to perform the Annakūṭa ceremony. Thus, day after day, the Annakūṭa ceremony was performed for some time.**
