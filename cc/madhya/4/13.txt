====== CC Madhya 4.13 ======

===== Text =====

> remuṇāte gopīnātha parama-mohana
> bhakti kari' kaila prabhu tāṅra daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:remuṇāte]]---in that village of Remuṇā; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---the Deity of Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:m:mohana]]---very attractive; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great devotion; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visit.

===== Translation =====

**The Deity of Gopīnātha in the temple at Remuṇā was very attractive. Lord Caitanya visited the temple and offered His obeisances with great devotion.**
