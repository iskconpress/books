====== Cc. Madhya 4.136 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣīrera vṛttānta tāṅre kahila pūjārī
> śuni' premāviṣṭa haila śrī-mādhava-purī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣīrera]] [[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]]---all the incidents that took place on account of the pot of sweet rice; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---explained; [[synonyms:p:pūjārī]]---the priest; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---saturated with love of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Śrīla Mādhavendra Purī.

===== Translation =====

**When the story about the pot of sweet rice was explained to him in detail, Śrī Mādhavendra Purī at once became absorbed in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa.**
