====== CC Madhya 4.71 ======

===== Text =====

> janā pāṅca-sāta ruṭi kare rāśi-rāśi
> anna-vyañjana saba rahe ghṛte bhāsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:janā]] [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five to seven men; [[synonyms:r:ruṭi]]---chapatis; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:r:rāśi]]-[[synonyms:r:rāśi]]---in great quantity; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---food grains and vegetables; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:g:ghṛte]]---in ghee; [[synonyms:b:bhāsi']]---overflooding.

===== Translation =====

**Five to seven men had prepared a huge quantity of chapatis, which were sufficiently covered with ghee [clarified butter], as were all the vegetables, rice and dāl.**
