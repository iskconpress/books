====== CC Madhya 4.167 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata candana deya pratyaha ghaṣiyā
> parāya sevaka saba ānanda kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---gives; [[synonyms:p:pratyaha]]---daily; [[synonyms:g:ghaṣiyā]]---grinding; [[synonyms:p:parāya]]---caused to put on; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servants; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of it; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---feeling.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Gopīnāthajī was supplied ground sandalwood pulp daily. The servants of Gopīnātha were very pleased with this.**
