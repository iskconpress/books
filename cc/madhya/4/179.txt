====== CC Madhya 4.179 ======

===== Text =====

> parama virakta, maunī, sarvatra udāsīna
> grāmya-vārtā-bhaye dvitīya-saṅga-hīna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paramavirakta]]---totally renounced or unattached; [[synonyms:m:maunī]]---silent; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:u:udāsīna]]---uninterested; [[synonyms:g:grāmya]]-[[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---of mundane topics; [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---in fear; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]---second; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---associate; [[synonyms:h:hīna]]---without.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "Śrī Mādhavendra Purī used to remain alone. He was completely renounced and always very silent. He was uninterested in everything material, and for fear of talking about mundane things, he always lived without a companion.**
