====== Cc. Madhya 4.88 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ dina-śeṣe prabhura karāila utthāna
> kichu bhoga lāgāila karāila jala-pāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:d:dina]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end of the day; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---caused to do; [[synonyms:u:utthāna]]---rising; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---food; [[synonyms:l:lāgāila]]---offered; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---caused to do; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drinking.

===== Translation =====

**After taking rest, the Deity must be awakened at the end of the day, and immediately some food and some water must be offered to Him.**

===== Purport =====

This offering is called //vaikāli-bhoga,// food offered at the end of the day.
