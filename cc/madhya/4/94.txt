====== Cc. Madhya 4.94 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva-dina-prāya vipra karila randhana
> taiche anna-kūṭa gopāla karila bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---almost like the previous day; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---all the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:r:randhana]]---cooking; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:k:kūṭa]]---heaps of food; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Deity of Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, almost like before, there was an Annakūṭa ceremony. All the brāhmaṇas prepared foods and Gopāla accepted them.**
