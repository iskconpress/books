====== Cc. Madhya 4.158 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla āsiyā kahe,---śuna he mādhava
> karpūra-candana āmi pāilāma saba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Deity of Gopāla; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:h:he]]---O; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---the camphor and sandalwood; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:pāilāma]]---have received; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī dreamed that Gopāla came before him and said, "O Mādhavendra Purī, I have already received all the sandalwood and camphor.**
