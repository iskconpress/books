====== CC Madhya 4.50 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' loka tāṅra saṅge calilā hariṣe
> kuñja kāṭi' dvāra kari' karilā praveśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---him; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:h:hariṣe]]---with great pleasure; [[synonyms:k:kuñja]] [[synonyms:k:kāṭi']]---cutting the bushes; [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---a way; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:p:praveśe]]---entered.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this, all the people accompanied Mādhavendra Purī with great pleasure. According to his directions, they cut down bushes, cleared a path and entered the jungle.**
