====== Cc. Madhya 4.198 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloka paḍite prabhu ha-ilā mūrcchite
> premete vivaśa hañā paḍila bhūmite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]]---reciting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchite]]---unconscious; [[synonyms:p:premete]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:v:vivaśa]]---uncontrolled; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell down; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited this verse, He immediately fell to the ground unconscious. He was overwhelmed and had no control over Himself.**
