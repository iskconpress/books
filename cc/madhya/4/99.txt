====== CC Madhya 4.99 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurāra loka saba baḍa baḍa dhanī
> bhakti kari' nānā dravya bheṭa deya āni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurāra]]---of the city of Mathurā; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very big; [[synonyms:d:dhanī]]---capitalists; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---out of devotion; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---various kinds of things; [[synonyms:b:bheṭa]]---presents; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---gave; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing.

===== Translation =====

**The people of Mathurā, who are very big capitalists, also brought various presentations and offered them before the Deity in devotional service.**
