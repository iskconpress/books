====== CC Madhya 4.35 ======

===== Text =====

> svapne dekhe, sei bālaka sammukhe āsiñā
> eka kuñje lañā gela hātete dhariñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svapne]]---in a dream; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---he saw; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bālaka]]---the very same boy; [[synonyms:s:sammukhe]]---in front; [[synonyms:ā:āsiñā]]---coming; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in one of the bushes; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking him; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:h:hātete]] [[synonyms:d:dhariñā]]---holding him by the hand.

===== Translation =====

**In a dream Mādhavendra Purī saw the very same boy. The boy came before him and, holding his hand, took him to a bush in the jungle.**
