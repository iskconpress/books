====== CC Madhya 4.5 ======

===== Text =====

> sahaje vicitra madhura caitanya-vihāra
> vṛndāvana-dāsa-mukhe amṛtera dhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahaje]]---naturally; [[synonyms:v:vicitra]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vihāra]]---the activities; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]]---of nectar; [[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---shower.

===== Translation =====

**By nature all the activities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are very wonderful and sweet, and when they are described by Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura, they become like a shower of nectar.**
