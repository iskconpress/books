====== Cc. Madhya 4.38 ======

===== Text =====

> eka maṭha kari' tāhāṅ karaha sthāpana
> bahu śītala jale kara śrī-aṅga mārjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:m:maṭha]]---temple; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---constructing; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---do; [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---installment; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:ś:śītala]]---cold; [[synonyms:j:jale]]---in water; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---My transcendental body; [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---washing.

===== Translation =====

**"Please construct a temple on top of that hill," the boy continued, "and install Me in that temple. After this, wash Me with large quantities of cold water so that My body may be cleansed.**
