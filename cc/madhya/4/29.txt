====== CC Madhya 4.29 ======

===== Text =====

> keha anna māgi' khāya, keha dugdhāhāra
> ayācaka-jane āmi diye ta' āhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---begging; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---eats; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āhāra]]---drinks milk; [[synonyms:a:ayācaka]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---a person who does not beg; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:d:diye]]---supply; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:āhāra]]---eatables.

===== Translation =====

**"In this village a person can beg food from others and thus eat. Some people drink only milk, but if a person does not ask anyone for food, I supply him all his eatables.**
