====== CC Madhya 4.145 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mādhava-purī śrīpāda āila',---loke haila khyāti
> saba loka āsi' tāṅre kare bahu bhakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Śrī Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:ś:śrīpāda]]---//sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---has come; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---among the people; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:khyāti]]---reputation; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all people; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion.

===== Translation =====

**When Mādhavendra Purī came to Jagannātha Purī, people were aware of his transcendental reputation. Therefore crowds of people came and offered him all sorts of respect in devotion.**
