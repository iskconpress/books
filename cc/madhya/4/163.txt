====== CC Madhya 4.163 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ājñā haila,---ei karpūra-candana
> gopīnāthera aṅge nitya karaha lepana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was an order of the Lord; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---camphor; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---and sandalwood; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthera]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body of Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---do; [[synonyms:l:lepana]]---smearing.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī said, "Smear the body of Gopīnātha with this camphor and sandalwood I have brought for Gopāla in Vṛndāvana. Do this regularly every day.**
