====== Cc. Madhya 4.36 ======

===== Text =====

> kuñja dekhāñā kahe,---āmi ei kuñje ra-i
> śīta-vṛṣṭi-vātāgnite mahā-duḥkha pāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuñja]] [[synonyms:d:dekhāñā]]---while showing him the bush; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---He says; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in the bush; [[synonyms:r:ra]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---reside; [[synonyms:ś:śīta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛṣṭi]]---in chilly cold and in showering rain; [[synonyms:v:vāta]]---in severe wind; [[synonyms:a:agnite]]---and in scorching heat; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---I am experiencing great pain.

===== Translation =====

**The boy showed Mādhavendra Purī the bush and said, "I reside in this bush, and because of this I suffer very much from severe cold, rain showers, winds and scorching heat.**
