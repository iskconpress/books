====== Cc. Madhya 4.184 ======

===== Text =====

> mleccha-deśa dūra patha, jagāti apāra
> ke-mate candana niba---nāhi e vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---the countries governed by Muslims; [[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:p:patha]]---long journey; [[synonyms:j:jagāti]]---watchmen; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:k:ke]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---how; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---the sandalwood; [[synonyms:n:niba]]---I shall take; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was not; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration.

===== Translation =====

**"Mādhavendra Purī was not at all anxious during the long journey to Vṛndāvana through the provinces governed by the Muslims and filled with unlimited numbers of watchmen.**
