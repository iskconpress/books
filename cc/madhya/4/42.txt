====== CC Madhya 4.42 ======

===== Text =====

> śaila-upari haite āmā kuñje lukāñā
> mleccha-bhaye sevaka mora gela palāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śaila]]-[[synonyms:u:upari]]---the top of the hill; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---Me; [[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in the bushes; [[synonyms:l:lukāñā]]---concealing; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---from fear of the Muslims; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servant; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:p:palāñā]]---running away.

===== Translation =====

**"When the Muslims attacked, the priest who was serving Me hid Me in this bush in the jungle. Then he ran away out of fear of the attack.**
