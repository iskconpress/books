====== CC Madhya 4.210 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla-gopīnātha-purī-gosāñira guṇa
> bhakta-saṅge śrī-mukhe prabhu kailā āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---of the Gopāla Deity; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---of the Gopīnātha Deity; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the qualities; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in His own mouth; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally tasted with His own mouth the transcendental qualities of Gopālajī, Gopīnātha and Śrī Mādhavendra Purī.**
