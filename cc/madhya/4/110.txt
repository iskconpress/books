====== CC Madhya 4.110 ======

===== Text =====

> śāntipura āilā advaitācāryera ghare
> purīra prema dekhi' ācārya ānanda antare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāntipura]]---to the place known as Śāntipura; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the home; [[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:p:prema]]---the ecstatic love of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleased; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within Himself.

===== Translation =====

**When Mādhavendra Purī arrived at the house of Advaita Ācārya in Śāntipura, the Ācārya became very pleased upon seeing the ecstatic love of Godhead manifest in Mādhavendra Purī.**
