====== Cc. Madhya 14.232 ======

===== Text =====

> vraja-rasa-gīta śuni' prema uthalila
> puruṣottama-grāma prabhu preme bhāsāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---songs about the mellows of Vṛndāvana-dhāma; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:u:uthalila]]---awakened; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]---the place known as Puruṣottama, Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---with ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bhāsāila]]---inundated.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic love was awakened by hearing the songs of Vṛndāvana. In this way He inundated Puruṣottama, Jagannātha Purī, with love of Godhead.**
