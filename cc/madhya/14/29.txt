====== Cc. Madhya 14.29 ======

===== Text =====

> amṛta-maṇḍā, saravatī, āra kumḍā-kurī
> sarāmṛta, sarabhājā, āra sarapurī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍā]]---a variety of papaya; [[synonyms:s:saravatī]]---a kind of orange; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kumḍā]]-[[synonyms:k:kurī]]---crushed squash; [[synonyms:s:sarāmṛta]]---cream; [[synonyms:s:sara]]-[[synonyms:b:bhājā]]---fried cream; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:s:sara]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---a kind of //purī// made with cream.

===== Translation =====

**There were also papayas and saravatī, a type of orange, and also crushed squash. There was also regular cream, fried cream and a type of purī made with cream.**
