====== CC Madhya 14.182 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' prabhu hailā ānandita mana
> sukhāviṣṭa hañā svarūpe kailā āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---very happy in His mind; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being absorbed in happiness; [[synonyms:s:svarūpe]]---unto Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very happy, and, being absorbed in this happiness, He embraced Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.**
