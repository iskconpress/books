====== CC Madhya 14.31 ======

===== Text =====

> padmacini, candrakānti, khājā, khaṇḍasāra
> viyari, kadmā, tilākhājāra prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:c:cini]]---sugar obtained from lotus flowers; [[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---a kind of bread made from urad dhal; [[synonyms:k:khājā]]---a crispy sweetmeat; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---sugar candy; [[synonyms:v:viyari]]---a sweetmeat made from fried rice; [[synonyms:k:kadmā]]---a sweetmeat made from sesame seeds; [[synonyms:t:tilākhājāra]]---cookies made from sesame seeds; [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---all varieties.

===== Translation =====

**There was lotus flower sugar, a kind of bread made from urad dhal, crispy sweetmeats, sugar candy, fried rice sweets, sesame seed sweets and cookies made from sesame seeds.**
