====== Cc. Madhya 14.141 ======

===== Text =====

> nāyikāra svabhāva, prema-vṛtte bahu bheda
> sei bhede nānā-prakāra mānera udbheda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāyikāra]]---of the heroine; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---nature; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛtte]]---in the matter of loving affairs; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---varieties; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bhede]]---in each variety; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---various types; [[synonyms:m:mānera]]---of the jealous anger of a woman; [[synonyms:u:udbheda]]---subdivisions.

===== Translation =====

**"The characteristics and modes of love are different in different women. Their jealous anger also takes on different varieties and qualities.**
