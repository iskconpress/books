====== Cc. Madhya 14.125 ======

===== Text =====

> gopī-saṅge yata līlā haya upavane
> nigūḍha kṛṣṇera bhāva keha nāhi jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---all pastimes; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:u:upavane]]---that are in those gardens; [[synonyms:n:nigūḍha]]---very confidential; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---ecstasies; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---know.

===== Translation =====

**"All the pastimes with the gopīs that take place in those gardens are very confidential ecstasies of Lord Kṛṣṇa. No one knows them.**
