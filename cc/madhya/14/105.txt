====== CC Madhya 14.105 ======

===== Text =====

> 'jagannātha-vallabha' nāma baḍā puṣpārāma
> nava dina karena prabhu tathāi viśrāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:v:vallabha]]---Jagannātha-vallabha; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very big; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārāma]]---garden; [[synonyms:n:nava]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---nine days; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tathāi]]---there; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---resting.

===== Translation =====

**The garden of His pastimes was very large and was named Jagannātha vallabha. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took his rest there for nine days.**
