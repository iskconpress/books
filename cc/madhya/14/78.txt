====== CC Madhya 14.78 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-dui jane meli' kare jala-raṇa
> keha hāre, keha jine-prabhu kare daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---forming a party of two men; [[synonyms:m:meli']]---joining; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:r:raṇa]]---fighting in the water; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:h:hāre]]---someone is defeated; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:j:jine]]---someone is victorious; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---sees.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes two would pair off to fight in the water. One would emerge victorious and the other defeated, and the Lord would watch all this fun.**
