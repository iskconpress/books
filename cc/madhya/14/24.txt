====== CC Madhya 14.24 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma-rāmānanda-vāṇīnāthe diyā
> prasāda pāṭhā'la rājā bahuta kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnāthe]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---through Vāṇīnātha Rāya; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhā'la]]---had sent; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---in a large quantity.

===== Translation =====

**The King also sent a large quantity of prasāda through Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Rāmānanda Rāya and Vāṇīnātha Rāya.**
