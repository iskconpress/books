====== CC Madhya 14.61 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṇḍu-vijaya tabe kare sevaka-gaṇe
> jagannātha vasilā giyā nija-siṁhāsane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṇḍu]]-[[synonyms:v:vijaya]]---the getting down from the car; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the servants; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat; [[synonyms:g:giya]]---going; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:siṁhāsane]]---on His own throne.

===== Translation =====

**All the servants of Lord Jagannātha then took Him down from the car, and the Lord went to sit on His throne.**
