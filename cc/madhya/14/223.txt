====== CC Madhya 14.223 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta kāma-dhenu tāhāṅ phire vane vane
> dugdha-mātra dena, keha nā māge anya dhane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:d:dhenu]]---cows that can fulfill all desires; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:phire]]---graze; [[synonyms:v:vane]] [[synonyms:v:vane]]---from forest to forest; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]] [[synonyms:d:dena]]---deliver milk only; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---wants; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:d:dhane]]---any other riches.

===== Translation =====

**"In Vṛndāvana there are cows that fulfill all desires [kāma-dhenus], and their number is unlimited. They graze from forest to forest and deliver only milk. The people want nothing else.**
