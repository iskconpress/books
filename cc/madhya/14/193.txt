====== CC Madhya 14.193 ======

===== Text =====

> lalita-bhūṣita rādhā dekhe yadi kṛṣṇa
> duṅhe duṅhā milibāre hayena satṛṣṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lalita]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūṣita]]---decorated with //lalita-alaṅkāra//; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of Them; [[synonyms:d:duṅhā]]---the two of Them; [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---to meet; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---become; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇa]]---very anxious.

===== Translation =====

**"When Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa happens to see Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī decorated with these lalita ornaments, They both anxiously want to meet one another.**
