====== CC Madhya 14.134 ======

===== Text =====

> acetanavat tāre karena tāḍane
> nānā-mata gāli dena bhaṇḍa-vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:acetana]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---almost unconscious; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto the servants; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tāḍane]]---chastisement; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---various kinds of; [[synonyms:g:gāli]]---amusement; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---speaks; [[synonyms:b:bhaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vacane]]---various loose language.

===== Translation =====

**When the servants fall down before the lotus feet of the goddess of fortune, they almost fall unconscious. They are chastised and made the butt of jokes and loose language.**
