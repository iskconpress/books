====== CC Madhya 14.238 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṅgi kari' svarūpa sabāra śrama jānāila
> bhakta-gaṇera śrama dekhi' prabhura bāhya haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṅgi]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing an indication; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---the fatigue; [[synonyms:j:jānāila]]---made known; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---the fatigue; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---become externally conscious.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara then informed the Lord that all the devotees were fatigued. Seeing this situation, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came to His external senses.**
