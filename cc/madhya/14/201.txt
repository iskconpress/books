====== CC Madhya 14.201 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata āra saba bhāva-vibhūṣaṇa
> yāhāte bhūṣita rādhā hare kṛṣṇa mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhūṣaṇa]]---ecstatic ornaments; [[synonyms:y:yāhāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣita]]---being decorated; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---attracts; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is ornamented and decorated with various ecstatic symptoms, which attract the mind of Śrī Kṛṣṇa.**
