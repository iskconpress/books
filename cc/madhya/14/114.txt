====== CC Madhya 14.114 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlācale āilā punaḥ bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> dekhite utkaṇṭhā herā-pañcamīra raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with His devotees; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---very eager; [[synonyms:h:herā]]-[[synonyms:p:pañcamīra]] [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---performance of the festival known as Herā-pañcamī.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His personal devotees returned to Nīlācala with great eagerness to see the Herā-pañcamī festival.**
