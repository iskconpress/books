====== Cc. Madhya 14.6 ======

===== Text =====

> saba-bhaktera ājñā nila yoḍa-hāta hañā
> prabhu-pada dhari' paḍe sāhasa kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took permission; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]]-[[synonyms:h:hāta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching the feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls; [[synonyms:s:sāhasa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great courage.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Pratāparudra was so humble that with folded hands he first took permission from all the devotees. Then, with great courage, he fell down and touched the lotus feet of the Lord.**
