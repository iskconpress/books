~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 14.255~~

====== CC Madhya 14.255 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata bhakta-gaṇe yātrā dekhāila
> bhakta-gaṇa lañā vṛndāvana-keli kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the Ratha-yātrā festival; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---pastimes of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed the Ratha-yātrā ceremony to His devotees and performed the Vṛndāvana pastimes with them.**
