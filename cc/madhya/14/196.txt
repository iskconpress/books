====== CC Madhya 14.196 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhire vāmatā-krodha, bhitare sukha mane
> 'kuṭṭamita'-nāma ei bhāva-vibhūṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:v:vāmatā]]---opposition; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]---anger; [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:k:kuṭṭamita]]---//kuṭṭamita//; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhūṣaṇe]]---ornament of an ecstatic attitude.

===== Translation =====

**"This ecstatic dress of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's is called kuṭṭamita. When it is manifest, She externally tries to avoid Kṛṣṇa, and She apparently becomes angry, although She is very happy within.**
