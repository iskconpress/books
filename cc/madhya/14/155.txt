====== CC Madhya 14.155 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara kahe,-kṛṣṇa rasika-śekhara
> rasa-āsvādaka, rasamaya-kalevara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Dāmodara said; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rasika]]-[[synonyms:ś:śekhara]]---the master of transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvādaka]]---the taster of transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]-[[synonyms:k:kalevara]]---whose body is made of all transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**Dāmodara Gosvāmī said, "Kṛṣṇa is the master of all transcendental mellows. He is the taster of transcendental mellows, and His body is composed of transcendental bliss.**
