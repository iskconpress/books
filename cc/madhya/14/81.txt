====== CC Madhya 14.81 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsa-sahita jala khele gadādhara
> rāghava-paṇḍita sane khele vakreśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:k:khele]]---performs this water sport; [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:khele]]---sports; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara Paṇḍita.

===== Translation =====

**Another duel took place between Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura and Gadādhara Paṇḍita, and yet another between Rāghava Paṇḍita and Vakreśvara Paṇḍita. Thus they all engaged in throwing water.**
