====== Cc. Madhya 14.112 ======

===== Text =====

> seita' kariha,-prabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> svacchande āsiyā yaiche karena daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seita']] [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do that; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---taking with Him all the devotees; [[synonyms:s:svacchande]]---freely; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---pays a visit to the temple.

===== Translation =====

**"Arrange the festival in such a way that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu may freely go with His devotees to visit the Deity without difficulty."**
