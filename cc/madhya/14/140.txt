====== CC Madhya 14.140 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-kaha vrajera mānera prakāra
> svarūpa kahe,-gopī-māna-nadī śata-dhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please tell; [[synonyms:v:vrajera]]---of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:m:mānera]]---of the egoistic pride; [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---the varieties; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara replied; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:m:māna]]---the pride of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]---like a river; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---with hundreds of branches.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Please tell me of the varieties of egoistic pride manifest in Vṛndāvana." Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "The pride of the gopīs is like a river flowing with hundreds of tributaries.**
