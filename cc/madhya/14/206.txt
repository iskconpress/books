====== Cc. Madhya 14.206 ======

===== Text =====

> eta sampatti chāḍi' kene gelā vṛndāvana
> tāṅre hāsya karite lakṣmī karilā sājana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:s:sampatti]]---so much opulence; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---He went; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:h:hāsya]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to make Him a laughingstock; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:s:sājana]]---so much decoration.

===== Translation =====

**"She wondered, 'Why did Lord Jagannātha give up so much opulence and go to Vṛndāvana?' To make Him a laughingstock, the goddess of fortune made arrangements for much decoration.**
