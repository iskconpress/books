====== CC Madhya 14.30 ======

===== Text =====

> hari-vallabha, seṅoti, karpūra, mālatī
> ḍālimā marica-lāḍu, navāta, amṛti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:v:vallabha]]---a sweetmeat like bread fried in ghee (like a doughnut); [[synonyms:s:seṅoti]]---a sweetmeat made of a kind of fragrant flower; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---a flower; [[synonyms:m:mālatī]]---another flower; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍālimā]]---pomegranate; [[synonyms:m:marica]]-[[synonyms:l:lāḍu]]---a sweetmeat made with black pepper; [[synonyms:n:navāta]]---another kind of sweetmeat, made with fused sugar; [[synonyms:a:amṛti]]---a preparation generally called //amṛti-jilipi,// made with rice powder and chick-pea flour, mixed with yogurt, fried in ghee and immersed in sugar water.

===== Translation =====

**There were also sweets like hari-vallabha and sweets made of seṅoti flowers, karpūra flowers and mālatī flowers. There were pomegranates, sweets made with black pepper, sweets made with fused sugar, and amṛti-jilipi.**
