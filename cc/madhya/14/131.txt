====== CC Madhya 14.131 ======

===== Text =====

> alaukika aiśvarya saṅge bahu-parivāra
> kruddha hañā lakṣmīdevī āilā siṁha-dvāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---opulence; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---accompanied by; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:p:parivāra]]---many members of the family; [[synonyms:k:kruddha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being angry; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:d:devī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---at the main gate of the temple.

===== Translation =====

**In an angry mood, the goddess of fortune arrived at the main gate of the temple accompanied by many members of her family, all of whom exhibited uncommon opulence.**
