====== CC Madhya 14.122 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,-śuna, prabhu, kāraṇa ihāra
> vṛndāvana-krīḍāte lakṣmīra nāhi adhikāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa replied; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---O my Lord; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]] [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---the reason for this; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍāte]]---in the pastimes of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmīra]]---of the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:a:adhikāra]]---admission.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "My dear Lord, please hear the reason for this. Lakṣmīdevī, the goddess of fortune, cannot be admitted to the pastimes of Vṛndāvana.**
