====== CC Madhya 14.47 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāṅ jagannāthera ratha-calana-samaya
> gauḍa saba ratha ṭāne, āge nāhi yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---outside the garden; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:c:calana]]-[[synonyms:s:samaya]]---at the time of drawing the car; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]---the workers named //gauḍas// who draw the car; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:r:ratha]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭāne]]---pull the car; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---forward; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---it does not go.

===== Translation =====

**Outside the garden, when it was time to pull Jagannātha's car, all the workers called gauḍas tried to pull it, but it would not move forward.**
