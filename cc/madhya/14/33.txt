====== CC Madhya 14.33 ======

===== Text =====

> dadhi, dugdha, nanī, takra, rasālā, śikhariṇī
> sa-lavaṇa mudgāṅkura, ādā khāni khāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---milk; [[synonyms:n:nanī]]---butter; [[synonyms:t:takra]]---buttermilk; [[synonyms:r:rasālā]]---fruit juice; [[synonyms:ś:śikhariṇī]]---a preparation made of fried yogurt and sugar candy; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:l:lavaṇa]]---salty; [[synonyms:m:mudga]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅkura]]---mung dhal sprouts; [[synonyms:ā:ādā]]---ginger; [[synonyms:k:khāni]] [[synonyms:k:khāni]]---cut into pieces.

===== Translation =====

**There was yogurt, milk, butter, buttermilk, fruit juice, a preparation made of fried yogurt and sugar candy, and salty mung dhal sprouts with shredded ginger.**
