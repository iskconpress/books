====== CC Madhya 14.233 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣmī-devī yathā-kāle gelā nija-ghara
> prabhu nṛtya kare, haila tṛtīya prahara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:d:devī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in due course of time; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghara]]---to her apartment; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---dances; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there arrived; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:p:prahara]]---the third period of the day, the afternoon.

===== Translation =====

**Finally the goddess of fortune returned to her apartment. In due course of time, as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was dancing, afternoon arrived.**
