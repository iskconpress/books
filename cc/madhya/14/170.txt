====== CC Madhya 14.170 ======

===== Text =====

> kila-kiñcitādi-bhāvera śuna vivaraṇa
> ye bhāva-bhūṣāya rādhā hare kṛṣṇa-mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kila]]-[[synonyms:k:kiñcita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with the ecstasy named //kila-kiñcita//; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of ecstasies; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---the description; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūṣāya]]---with these ecstatic ornaments; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---enchants; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Now hear a description of different ecstasies, beginning with kila-kiñcita. With these ecstatic ornaments, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī enchants the mind of Kṛṣṇa.**
