====== CC Madhya 14.128 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle, khacita yāhe vividha ratana
> suvarṇera caudolā kari' ārohaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---while Svarūpa Dāmodara and Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu were talking; [[synonyms:k:khacita]]---were bedecked; [[synonyms:y:yāhe]]---on which; [[synonyms:v:vividha]]---varieties; [[synonyms:r:ratana]]---gems; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇera]]---made of gold; [[synonyms:c:caudolā]]---a palanquin carried by four men; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:ā:ārohaṇa]]---riding upon.

===== Translation =====

**While Svarūpa Dāmodara and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were talking, the procession of the goddess of fortune came by. She was riding upon a golden palanquin carried by four men and bedecked with a variety of jewels.**
