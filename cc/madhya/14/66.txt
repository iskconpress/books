====== Cc. Madhya 14.66 ======

===== Text =====

> advaitādi bhakta-gaṇa nimantraṇa kaila
> mukhya mukhya nava jana nava dina pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Advaita ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---invited Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---chief and important; [[synonyms:n:nava]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---nine persons; [[synonyms:n:nava]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---nine days; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got.

===== Translation =====

**For nine days, nine chief devotees, headed by Advaita Ācārya, got an opportunity to invite the Lord to their homes.**
