====== CC Madhya 14.44 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ājñāya govinda dīna-hīna jane
> duḥkhī kāṅgāla āni' karāya bhojane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---on the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---His personal servant; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]-[[synonyms:h:hīna]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---unto all poor men; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:k:kāṅgāla]]---beggars; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---inviting; [[synonyms:k:karāya]] [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---fed sumptuously.

===== Translation =====

**Following the orders of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Govinda, His personal servant, called for all the poor beggars, who were unhappy due to their poverty, and fed them sumptuously.**
