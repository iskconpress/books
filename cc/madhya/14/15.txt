====== CC Madhya 14.15 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva-sevā dekhi' tāṅre kṛpā upajila
> anusandhāna vinā kṛpā-prasāda karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---previous service; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---awakened; [[synonyms:a:anusandhāna]]---inquiry; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---of mercy; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---grace; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---bestowed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mercy was aroused because of the King's previous service. Therefore without even asking who he was, the Lord immediately bestowed His mercy upon him.**
