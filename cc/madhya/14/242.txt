====== CC Madhya 14.242 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha dekhi' karena nartana-kīrtana
> narendre jala-krīḍā kare lañā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:n:narendre]]---in the lake known as Narendrasarovara; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---sporting in the water; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---with the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as He saw Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to chant and dance. Afterward, accompanied by His devotees, the Lord enjoyed sporting in the lake called Narendra-sarovara.**
