====== Cc. Madhya 14.9 ======

===== Text =====

> śunite śunite prabhura santoṣa apāra
> 'bala, bala' bali' prabhu bale bāra bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śunite]] [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---by hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---great satisfaction; [[synonyms:b:bala]] [[synonyms:b:bala]]---go on reciting; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard these verses, He was pleased beyond limits, and He said again and again, "Go on reciting, go on reciting."**
