====== CC Madhya 14.204 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvanera sampad dekha,-puṣpa-kisalaya
> giridhātu-śikhipiccha-guñjāphala-maya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanera]]---of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:sampad]]---the opulence; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---see; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:k:kisalaya]]---a few flowers and twigs; [[synonyms:g:giri]]-[[synonyms:d:dhātu]]---some minerals from the hills; [[synonyms:ś:śikhi]]-[[synonyms:p:piccha]]---some peacock feathers; [[synonyms:g:guñjā]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---some //guñjā-phala.//

===== Translation =====

**"As far as Vṛndāvana's opulence is concerned, it consists of a few flowers and twigs, some minerals from the hills, a few peacock feathers and the plant known as guñjā.**
