====== CC Madhya 14.205 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana dekhibāre gelā jagannātha
> śuni' lakṣmī-devīra mane haila āsoyātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana-dhāma; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:d:devīra]]---of the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:ā:āsoyātha]]---envy.

===== Translation =====

**"When Jagannātha decided to see Vṛndāvana, He went there, and upon hearing this, the goddess of fortune experienced restlessness and jealousy.**
