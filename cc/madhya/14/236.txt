====== CC Madhya 14.236 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda dekhiyā prabhura bhāvāveśa
> nikaṭe nā āise, rahe kichu dūra-deśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---the ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---nearby; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---does not come; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---//keeps//; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---a little; [[synonyms:d:dūra]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---far away.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the ecstatic love of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Nityānanda Prabhu did not approach but remained a little distance away.**
