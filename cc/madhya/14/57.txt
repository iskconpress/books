====== CC Madhya 14.57 ======

===== Text =====

> ānande karaye loka 'jaya' 'jaya'-dhvani
> 'jaya jagannātha' ba-i āra nāhi śuni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---do; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the sound of "all glories, all glories"; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---all glories to Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:b:ba]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---except for this; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---no one could hear anything else.

===== Translation =====

**When the car moved forward, everyone began to chant with great pleasure, "All glories! All glories!" and "All glories to Lord Jagannātha!" No one could hear anything else.**
