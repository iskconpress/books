====== CC Madhya 14.115 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśī-miśra prabhure bahu ādara kariyā
> svagaṇa-saha bhāla-sthāne vasāila lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---very much; [[synonyms:ā:ādara]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---offering respect; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with His associates; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in a nice place; [[synonyms:v:vasāila]]---made seated; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Kāśī Miśra received Caitanya Mahāprabhu with great respect, and taking the Lord and His associates to a very nice place, he had them seated.**
