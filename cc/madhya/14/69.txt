====== CC Madhya 14.69 ======

===== Text =====

> eka dina nimantraṇa kare dui-tine mili'
> ei-mata mahāprabhura nimantraṇa-keli

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:t:tine]]---two or three persons; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---combining; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:k:keli]]---pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**Since they could not get one day each, two or three devotees combined to extend an invitation. These are the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's acceptance of invitations.**
