====== CC Madhya 14.71 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu advaite nācāya, kabhu nityānande
> kabhu haridāse nācāya, kabhu acyutānande

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:a:advaite]]---Advaita //ācārya//; [[synonyms:n:nācāya]]---made dance; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nityānande]]---sometimes Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:h:haridāse]] [[synonyms:n:nācāya]]---sometimes made Haridāsa Ṭhākura dance; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:a:acyutānande]]---Acyutānanda.

===== Translation =====

**By chanting and dancing, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu induced Advaita Ācārya to dance. Sometimes He induced Nityānanda, Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Acyutānanda to dance.**
