====== Cc. Madhya 14.247 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṇḍu-vijayera tuli phāṭi-phuṭi yāya
> jagannāthera bhare tulā uḍiyā palāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṇḍu]]-[[synonyms:v:vijayera]]---of the ceremony of Pāṇḍu-vijaya; [[synonyms:t:tuli]]---batches of cotton; [[synonyms:p:phāṭi]]-[[synonyms:p:phuṭi]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---become broken; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]] [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---by the weight of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:t:tulā]]---the cotton; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]] [[synonyms:p:palāya]]---floats in the air.

===== Translation =====

**When the Jagannātha Deity is carried, at intervals He is placed on cotton pads. When the ropes broke, the cotton pads also broke due to the weight of Lord Jagannātha, and the cotton floated in the air.**
