====== Cc. Madhya 14.23 ======

===== Text =====

> madhyāhna karilā prabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> vāṇīnātha prasāda lañā kaila āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---accepted lunch; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all kinds of remnants of Jagannātha's food; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---arrival.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Vāṇīnātha Rāya brought all kinds of prasāda, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted lunch with the devotees.**
