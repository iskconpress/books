====== Cc. Madhya 14.35 ======

===== Text =====

> prasāde pūrita ha-ila ardha upavana
> dekhiyā santoṣa haila mahāprabhura mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---with all the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:pūrita]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became filled; [[synonyms:a:ardha]] [[synonyms:u:upavana]]---half of the garden; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---in the mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw half the garden filled with a variety of prasāda, He was very satisfied.**
