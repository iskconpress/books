====== CC Madhya 14.146 ======

===== Text =====

> sarala vyavahāra, kare mānera poṣaṇa
> kimvā solluṇṭha-vākye kare priya-nirasana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarala]] [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---plain dealings; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:mānera]]---of jealous anger; [[synonyms:p:poṣaṇa]]---maintenance; [[synonyms:k:kimvā]]---or; [[synonyms:s:solluṇṭha]]---smiling a little; [[synonyms:v:vākye]]---by words; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---of the lover; [[synonyms:n:nirasana]]---refutation.

===== Translation =====

**"The sober heroine is very simple in her behavior. She keeps her jealous anger within her heart, but with mild words and smiles she refutes the advances of her lover.**
