====== CC Madhya 14.40 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu nā khāile, keha nā kare bhojana
> svarupa-gosāñi tabe kaila nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:khāile]]---without eating; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---accepts the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted.

===== Translation =====

**However, the devotees could not accept the prasāda until Caitanya Mahāprabhu took it. It was Svarūpa Gosvāmī who informed the Lord of this.**
