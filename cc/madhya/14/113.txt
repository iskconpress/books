====== CC Madhya 14.113 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle mahāprabhu nija-gaṇa lañā
> jagannātha darśana kaila sundarācale yāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking His associates; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---visiting Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:s:sundarācale]]---to the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**In the morning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His personal associates with Him to see Lord Jagannātha at Sundarācala.**

===== Purport =====

Sundarācala is the Guṇḍicā temple. The temple of Jagannātha at Jagannātha Purī is called Nīlācala, and the temple at Guṇḍicā is called Sundarācala.
