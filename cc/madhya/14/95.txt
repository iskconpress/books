====== CC Madhya 14.95 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina āsi' kaila īśvara daraśana
> prāṅgaṇe nṛtya-gīta kaila kata-kṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prāṅgaṇe]]---in the yard; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu also went to the temple of Guṇḍicā and saw the Lord. He then chanted and danced in the yard for some time.**
