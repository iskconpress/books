====== Cc. Madhya 14.37 ======

===== Text =====

> keyā-patra-droṇī āila bojhā pāṅca-sāta
> eka eka jane daśa donā dila,-eta pāta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keyā]]-[[synonyms:p:patra]]-[[synonyms:d:droṇī]]---plates made of leaves of the //ketakī// tree; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---arrived; [[synonyms:b:bojhā]]---in loads; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five or seven; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---to each and every man; [[synonyms:d:daśa]] [[synonyms:d:donā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---ten such plates were given; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:p:pāta]]---so many dishes.

===== Translation =====

**There then arrived five or seven loads of plates made of the leaves of the ketakī tree. Each man was supplied ten of these plates, and in this way the dishes were served.**
