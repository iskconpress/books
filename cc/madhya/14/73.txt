====== CC Madhya 14.73 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvane āilā kṛṣṇa-ei prabhura jñāna
> kṛṣṇera viraha-sphūrti haila avasāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa arrived; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---this is consciousness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---from Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viraha]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---feelings of separation; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:a:avasāna]]---ended.

===== Translation =====

**At this time Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt that Lord Kṛṣṇa had returned to Vṛndāvana. Thinking this, His feelings of separation from Kṛṣṇa subsided.**
