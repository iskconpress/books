====== Cc. Madhya 14.171 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā dekhi' kṛṣṇa yadi chuṅite kare mana
> dāna-ghāṭi-pathe yabe varjena gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:c:chuṅite]]---to touch; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---desires; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]-[[synonyms:g:ghāṭi]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way leading toward the spot from where one crosses the river to the other side; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:v:varjena]]---prohibits; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**"When Śrī Kṛṣṇa sees Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī and wants to touch Her body, He prohibits Her from going to the spot where one can cross the River Yamunā.**
