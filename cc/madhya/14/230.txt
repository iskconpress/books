====== Cc. Madhya 14.230 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhāra śuddha-rasa prabhu āveśe śunila
> sei rasāveśe prabhu nṛtya ārambhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---pure transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]] [[synonyms:ś:śunila]]---heard with great ecstasy; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in absorption in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:ā:ārambhila]]---began dancing.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard these discussions about the pure transcendental mellow of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī. Absorbed in transcendental ecstasy, the Lord began to dance.**
