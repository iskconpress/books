====== CC Madhya 14.111 ======

===== Text =====

> dviguṇa kariyā kara saba upahāra
> ratha-yātrā haite yaiche haya camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making a double portion; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---make; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all kinds of; [[synonyms:u:upahāra]]---presentations; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than the car festival; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---so that; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it becomes; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---more wonderful.

===== Translation =====

**"You should also double the quantity of prasāda. Make so much that it will even surpass the Ratha-yātrā festival.**
