====== CC Madhya 14.218 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,-śrīvāsa, śuna sāvadhāne
> vṛndāvana-sampad tomāra nāhi paḍe mane?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara said; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---my dear Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]] [[synonyms:s:sāvadhāne]]---carefully please hear; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:s:sampad]]---the opulence of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara then retorted, "My dear Śrīvāsa, please hear me with attention. You have forgotten the transcendental opulence of Vṛndāvana.**
