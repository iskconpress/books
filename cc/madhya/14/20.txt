====== CC Madhya 14.20 ======

===== Text =====

> 'rājā'-hena jñāna kabhu nā kaila prakāśa
> antare sakala jānena, bāhire udāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:h:hena]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---such knowledge; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifested; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---everything; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---knows; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:u:udāsa]]---indifferent.

===== Translation =====

**Although within His heart Caitanya Mahāprabhu knew everything that was happening, externally He did not disclose it. Nor did He disclose that He knew He was talking with King Pratāparudra.**
