====== CC Madhya 14.7 ======

===== Text =====

> āṅkhi mudi' prabhu preme bhūmite śayāna
> nṛpati naipuṇye kare pāda-saṁvāhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āṅkhi]] [[synonyms:m:mudi']]---with closed eyes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:ś:śayāna]]---lying down; [[synonyms:n:nṛpati]]---the King; [[synonyms:n:naipuṇye]]---very expertly; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvāhana]]---massaging the legs.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was lying on the ground with His eyes closed in ecstatic love and emotion, and the King very expertly began to massage His legs.**
