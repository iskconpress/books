====== Cc. Madhya 14.130 ======

===== Text =====

> tāmbūla-sampuṭa, jhāri, vyajana, cāmara
> sāthe dāsī śata, hāra divya bhūṣāmbara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāmbūla]]-[[synonyms:s:sampuṭa]]---boxes containing the ingredients for betel nut preparation; [[synonyms:j:jhāri]]---water pitchers; [[synonyms:v:vyajana]]---fans; [[synonyms:c:cāmara]]---whisks; [[synonyms:s:sāthe]]---along with; [[synonyms:d:dāsī]]---maidservants; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---hundreds; [[synonyms:h:hāra]]---necklaces; [[synonyms:d:divya]]---valuable; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣāmbara]]---dresses.

===== Translation =====

**The maidservants were carrying water pitchers, cāmara whisks and boxes for betel nuts. There were hundreds of maidservants, all attractively dressed with valuable necklaces.**
