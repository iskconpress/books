====== CC Madhya 14.120 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhira ha-ite kare ratha-yātrā-chala
> sundarācale yāya prabhu chāḍi' nīlācala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhira]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---externally; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]-[[synonyms:c:chala]]---an excuse to enjoy the car festival; [[synonyms:s:sundarācale]]---to Sundarācala, the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**"Externally He gives the excuse that He wants to participate in the Ratha-yātrā festival, but actually He wants to leave Jagannātha Purī to go to Sundarācala, Guṇḍicā temple, the replica of Vṛndāvana.**
