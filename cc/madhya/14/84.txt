====== CC Madhya 14.84 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita, gambhīra, duṅhe-prāmāṇika jana
> bāla-cāñcalya kare, karāha varjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned scholars; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---very grave; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:p:prāmāṇika]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---authoritative persons; [[synonyms:b:bāla]]-[[synonyms:c:cāñcalya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---sport like children; [[synonyms:k:karāha]] [[synonyms:v:varjana]]---ask them to stop.

===== Translation =====

**"Tell Bhaṭṭācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya to stop their childish play because they are both learned scholars and very grave and great personalities."**
