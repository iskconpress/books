====== CC Madhya 14.151 ======

===== Text =====

> 'madhyā' 'pragalbhā' dhare dhīrādi-vibheda
> tāra madhye sabāra svabhāve tina bheda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyā]]---the intermediate; [[synonyms:p:pragalbhā]]---the impudent; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---contain; [[synonyms:d:dhīra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:v:vibheda]]---the three divisions of //dhīra, adhīra// and //dhīrādhīrā//; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among them; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in nature; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---three divisions.

===== Translation =====

**"Both the intermediate and impudent heroines can be classified as sober, restless and both sober and restless. All their characteristics can be further classified in three divisions.**
