====== CC Madhya 14.219 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvane sāhajika ye sampat-sindhu
> dvārakā-vaikuṇṭha-sampat-tāra eka bindu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:sāhajika]]---natural; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:s:sampat]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---ocean of opulence; [[synonyms:d:dvārakā]]---of Dvārakā; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:s:sampat]]---all the opulence of the spiritual world; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:bindu]]---one drop.

===== Translation =====

**"The natural opulence of Vṛndāvana is just like an ocean. The opulence of Dvārakā and Vaikuṇṭha is not even to be compared to a drop.**
