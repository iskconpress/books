====== CC Madhya 14.142 ======

===== Text =====

> samyak gopikāra māna nā yāya kathana
> eka-dui-bhede kari dig-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:samyak]]---fully; [[synonyms:g:gopikāra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:m:māna]]---jealous anger; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is possible; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---to speak; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dui]]---one, two; [[synonyms:b:bhede]]---in different types; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I make; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---indication.

===== Translation =====

**"It is not possible to give a complete statement about the different types of jealous anger manifest by the gopīs, but a few principles may serve as an indication.**
