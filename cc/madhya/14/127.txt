====== Cc. Madhya 14.127 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,-premavatīra ei ta' svabhāva
> kāntera audāsya-leśe haya krodha-bhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī replied; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vatīra]]---of the girl who is too afflicted by love; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the nature; [[synonyms:k:kāntera]]---of the beloved; [[synonyms:a:audāsya]]---of negligence; [[synonyms:l:leśe]]---even by a fractional part; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---anger.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "It is the nature of a girl afflicted by love to become immediately angry upon finding neglect on the part of her lover."**
