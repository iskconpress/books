====== Cc. Madhya 14.116 ======

===== Text =====

> rasa-viśeṣa prabhura śunite mana haila
> īṣat hāsiyā prabhu svarūpe puchila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---a particular mellow; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was a desire; [[synonyms:ī:īṣat]] [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling mildly; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svarūpe]] [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara.

===== Translation =====

**After taking His seat, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wanted to hear about a particular mellow of devotional service; therefore, mildly smiling, He began to question Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
