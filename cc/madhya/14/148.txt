====== CC Madhya 14.148 ======

===== Text =====

> 'dhīrādhīrā' vakra-vākye kare upahāsa
> kabhu stuti, kabhu nindā, kabhu vā udāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhīra]]-[[synonyms:a:adhīra]]---the heroine who is a combination of sobriety and restlessness; [[synonyms:v:vakra]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:u:upahāsa]]---jokes with equivocal words; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---sometimes praise; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nindā]]---sometimes blasphemy; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:v:vā]] [[synonyms:u:udāsa]]---sometimes indifference.

===== Translation =====

**"The heroine who is a combination of sobriety and restlessness always jokes with equivocal words. She sometimes praises her lover, sometimes blasphemes him and sometimes remains indifferent.**
