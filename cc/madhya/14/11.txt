====== CC Madhya 14.11 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi more dile bahu amūlya ratana
> mora kichu dite nāhi, diluṅ āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:d:dile]]---delivered; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---various; [[synonyms:a:amūlya]]---incalculable; [[synonyms:r:ratana]]---gems; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---of Me; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:d:diluṅ]]---I give; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing the verses recited by the King, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You have given Me invaluable gems, but I have nothing to give you in return. Therefore I am simply embracing you."**
