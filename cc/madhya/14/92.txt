====== CC Madhya 14.92 ======

===== Text =====

> purī, bhāratī ādi yata mukhya bhakta-gaṇa
> ācāryera nimantraṇe karilā bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---chief; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇe]]---by the invitation; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---accepted their lunch.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī, Brahmānanda Bhāratī and all the other chief devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took lunch at the invitation of Advaita Ācārya.**
