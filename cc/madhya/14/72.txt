====== CC Madhya 14.72 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu vakreśvare, kabhu āra bhakta-gaṇe
> trisandhyā kīrtana kare guṇḍicā-prāṅgaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:v:vakreśvare]]---sometimes Vakreśvara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---other devotees; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]---three times (morning, evening and noon); [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs //kīrtana//; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]-[[synonyms:p:prāṅgaṇe]]---in the yard of the Guṇḍicā temple.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu engaged Vakreśvara and other devotees in chanting and dancing. Three times daily-morning, noon and evening-He would perform saṅkīrtana in the yard of the Guṇḍicā temple.**
