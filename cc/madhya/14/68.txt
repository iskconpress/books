====== Cc. Madhya 14.68 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri māsera dina mukhya-bhakta bāṅṭi' nila
> āra bhakta-gaṇa avasara nā pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māsera]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the days of four months; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---the chief devotees; [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭi']] [[synonyms:n:nila]]---shared among themselves; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---other devotees; [[synonyms:a:avasara]]---opportunity; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---did not get.

===== Translation =====

**For the four-month period, all the daily invitations were shared among the important devotees. The rest of the devotees did not get an opportunity to extend an invitation to the Lord.**
