====== Cc. Madhya 14.243 ======

===== Text =====

> udyāne āsiyā kaila vana-bhojana
> ei-mata krīḍā kaila prabhu aṣṭa-dina

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---to the garden; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---picnic in the forest; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---constantly for eight days.

===== Translation =====

**Then, entering the flower garden, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His meal. In this way He continuously performed all kinds of pastimes for eight days.**
