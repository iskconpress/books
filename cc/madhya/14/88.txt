====== Cc. Madhya 14.88 ======

===== Text =====

> hāsi' mahāprabhu tabe advaite ānila
> jalera upare tāṅre śeṣa-śayyā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:a:advaite]] [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---called for Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jalera]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the surface of the water; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śayyā]]---the Śeṣa Nāga bed; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**After Gopīnātha Ācārya finished talking, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled and, calling for Advaita Ācārya, made Him act like the Śeṣa Nāga bed.**
