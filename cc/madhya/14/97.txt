====== CC Madhya 14.97 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛkṣa-vallī praphullita prabhura daraśane
> bhṛṅga-pika gāya, vahe śītala pavane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:v:vallī]]---trees and creepers; [[synonyms:p:praphullita]]---joyful; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---by the sight; [[synonyms:b:bhṛṅga]]---bumblebees; [[synonyms:p:pika]]---birds; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---were blowing; [[synonyms:ś:śītala]]---cool; [[synonyms:p:pavane]]---breezes.

===== Translation =====

**There were multifarious trees and creepers in the garden, and they were all jubilant to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Indeed, the birds were chirping, the bees were buzzing, and a cool breeze was blowing.**
