====== CC Madhya 14.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrīvāsādi gaura-bhakta-gaṇa
> jaya śrotā-gaṇa,-yāṅra gaura prāṇa-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the devotees of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrotā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the hearers; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the life and soul.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura! All glories to the readers who have taken Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as their life and soul.**
