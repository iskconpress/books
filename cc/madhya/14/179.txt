====== CC Madhya 14.179 ======

===== Text =====

> ei bhāva-yukta dekhi' rādhāsya-nayana
> saṅgama ha-ite sukha pāya koṭi-guṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eibhāva]]---with these ecstatic symptoms; [[synonyms:y:yukta]]---combined together; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:a:asya]]-[[synonyms:n:nayana]]---the face and eyes of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:saṅgama]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---than direct embracing; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---enjoys happiness; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---millions of times more.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa is thousands upon thousands of times more satisfied when He sees Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's face light up from this combination of ecstatic love than He is by direct union with Her.**


This is further explained in the following verse from the //Ujjvala-nīlamaṇi// (//Anubhāva-prakaraṇa// 46) of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.
