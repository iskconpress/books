====== Cc. Madhya 14.77 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu eka maṇḍala, kabhu aneka maṇḍala
> jala-maṇḍūka-vādye sabe bājāya karatāla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---sometimes one circle; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---various circles; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍūka]]-[[synonyms:v:vādye]]---like the croaking sound of frogs in the water; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:bājāya]]---play; [[synonyms:k:karatāla]]---cymbals.

===== Translation =====

**While in the water they sometimes formed a circle and sometimes many circles, and while in the water they used to play cymbals and imitate the croaking of frogs.**
