====== CC Madhya 14.83 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu tāṅ doṅhāra cāñcalya dekhiyā
> gopīnāthācārye kichu kahena hāsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]] [[synonyms:d:doṅhāra]]---of these two persons; [[synonyms:c:cāñcalya]]---restlessness; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārye]]---unto Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the exuberance of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya, He smiled and spoke to Gopīnātha Ācārya.**
