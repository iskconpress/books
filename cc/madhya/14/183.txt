====== Cc. Madhya 14.183 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vilāsādi'-bhāva-bhūṣāra kaha ta' lakṣaṇa
> yei bhāve rādhā hare govindera mana?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with transcendental enjoyment; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---of ecstasy; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣāra]]---of the ornaments; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please speak; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---by which symptoms; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---enchants; [[synonyms:g:govindera]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Śrī Govinda.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then asked Svarūpa Dāmodara, "Please speak of the ecstatic ornaments decorating the body of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, by which She enchants the mind of Śrī Govinda."**
