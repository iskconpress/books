====== CC Madhya 14.14 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bhūridā' 'bhūridā' bali' kare āliṅgana
> iṅho nāhi jāne,-ihoṅ haya kon jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhūri]]-[[synonyms:d:dā]]---the most munificent; [[synonyms:b:bhūri]]-[[synonyms:d:dā]]---the most munificent; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---crying; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know; [[synonyms:i:ihoṅ]]---Pratāparudra Mahārāja; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---who.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing the recitation of this verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately embraced the reciter, King Pratāparudra, and cried, "You are the most munificent! You are the most munificent!" At this point Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not even know who the King was.**
