====== CC Madhya 14.235 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā-premāveśe prabhu hailā sei mūrti
> nityānanda dūre dekhi' karilena stuti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---exactly that same form; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:d:dūre]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing from a distant place; [[synonyms:k:karilena]] [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---offered prayers.

===== Translation =====

**While dancing absorbed in Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's ecstatic love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu appeared in Her very form. Seeing this from a distant place, Nityānanda Prabhu offered prayers.**
