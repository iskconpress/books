====== CC Madhya 14.48 ======

===== Text =====

> ṭānite nā pāre gauḍa, ratha chāḍi' dila
> pātra-mitra lañā rājā vyagra hañā āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭānite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---they could not pull; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]---the //gauḍas//; [[synonyms:r:ratha]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave up the attempt; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]-[[synonyms:m:mitra]]---all the officers and friends; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with him; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:v:vyagra]]---in great anxiety; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---arrived.

===== Translation =====

**When the gauḍas saw that they could not budge the car, they abandoned the attempt. Then the King arrived in great anxiety, and he was accompanied by his officers and friends.**
