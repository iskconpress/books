====== CC Madhya 14.10 ======

===== Text =====

> "tava kathāmṛtaṁ" śloka rājā ye paḍila
> uṭhi' premāveśe prabhu āliṅgana kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tava]] [[synonyms:k:kathāmṛtam]]---beginning with the words //tava kathāmṛtam//; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---as he recited; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as the King recited the verse beginning with the words "tava kathāmṛtam," the Lord immediately arose in ecstatic love and embraced him.**
