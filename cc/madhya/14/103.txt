====== Cc. Madhya 14.103 ======

===== Text =====

> jala-krīḍā kari' punaḥ āilā udyāne
> bhojana-līlā kailā prabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---sporting in the water; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---in the garden; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes of accepting //prasāda//; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---with all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**After sporting in the water, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to the garden and accepted prasāda with the devotees.**
