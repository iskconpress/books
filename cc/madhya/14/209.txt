====== CC Madhya 14.209 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' mahā-lakṣmīra saba dāsī-gaṇe
> kaṭi-vastre bāndhi' āne prabhura nija-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣmīra]]---of the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dāsī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---maidservants; [[synonyms:k:kaṭi]]-[[synonyms:v:vastre]]---by their waist clothes; [[synonyms:b:bāndhi']]---binding; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---bring; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---personal servants.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way all the maidservants of the goddess of fortune arrested the servants of Jagannātha, bound them around the waist and brought them before the goddess of fortune.**
