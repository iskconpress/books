====== CC Madhya 14.244 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dine jagannāthera bhitara-vijaya
> rathe caḍi' jagannātha cale nijālaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the next day; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:b:bhitara]]-[[synonyms:v:vijaya]]---coming out from inside the temple; [[synonyms:r:rathe]] [[synonyms:c:caḍi']]---riding on the car; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---returns; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaya]]---to His own home.

===== Translation =====

**The next day Lord Jagannātha came out from the temple and, riding on the car, returned to His own abode.**
