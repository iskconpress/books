====== CC Madhya 14.220 ======

===== Text =====

> parama puruṣottama svayaṁ bhagavān
> kṛṣṇa yāhāṅ dhanī tāhāṅ vṛndāvana-dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---personally the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:d:dhanī]]---actually opulent; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---Vṛndāvana-dhāma.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead full of all opulences, and His complete opulences are exhibited only in Vṛndāvana-dhāma.**
