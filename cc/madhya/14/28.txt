====== CC Madhya 14.28 ======

===== Text =====

> manoharā-lāḍu ādi śateka prakāra
> amṛta-guṭikā-ādi, kṣīrasā apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manoharā]]-[[synonyms:l:lāḍu]]---a kind of //sandeśa//; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:ś:śateka]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---hundreds of varieties; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:g:guṭikā]]---round sweetmeats; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:k:kṣīrasā]]---condensed milk; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---of various qualities.

===== Translation =====

**There were hundreds of different types of sweetmeats like manoharā-lāḍu, sweets like amṛta-guṭikā and various types of condensed milk.**
