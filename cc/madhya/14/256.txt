====== CC Madhya 14.256 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñira līlā-ananta, apāra
> 'sahasra-vadana' yāra nāhi pāya pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---without end; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---Lord Śeṣa, who has thousands of hoods; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Lord Caitanya are unlimited and endless. Even Sahasravadana, Lord Śeṣa, cannot reach the limits of His pastimes.**
