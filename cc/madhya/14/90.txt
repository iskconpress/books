====== CC Madhya 14.90 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita nija-śakti prakaṭa kariyā
> mahāprabhu lañā bule jalete bhāsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---His personal potency; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---after manifesting; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---carrying Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---moves; [[synonyms:j:jalete]]---on the water; [[synonyms:b:bhāsiyā]]---floating.

===== Translation =====

**Manifesting His personal potency, Advaita Ācārya floated about on the water, carrying Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
