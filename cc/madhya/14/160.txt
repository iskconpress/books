====== CC Madhya 14.160 ======

===== Text =====

> gopī-gaṇa-madhye śreṣṭhā rādhā-ṭhākurāṇī
> nirmala-ujjvala-rasa-prema-ratna-khani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---of all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭhā]]---the chief; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:n:nirmala]]---purified; [[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---brilliant; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---in mellows; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:k:khani]]---the jewel mine.

===== Translation =====

**"Of all the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is the chief. She is a jewel mine of ecstatic love and the source of all purified transcendental conjugal mellows.**
