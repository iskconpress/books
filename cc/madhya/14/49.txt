====== Cc. Madhya 14.49 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-malla-gaṇe dila ratha cālāite
> āpane lāgilā ratha, nā pāre ṭānite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:malla]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the big wrestlers; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:c:cālāite]]---to pull out; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---engaged; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭānite]]---could not move.

===== Translation =====

**The King then arranged for big wrestlers to try to pull the car, and even the King himself joined in, but the car could not be moved.**
