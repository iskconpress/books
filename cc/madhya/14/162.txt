====== CC Madhya 14.162 ======

===== Text =====

> vāmya-svabhāve māna uṭhe nirantara
> tāra madhye uṭhe kṛṣṇera ānanda-sāgara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāmya]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāve]]---because of left-wing character; [[synonyms:m:māna]]---womanly anger; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---awakens; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in that dealing; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---is awakening; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sāgara]]---an ocean of transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**"Because She is a left-wing gopī, Her womanly anger is always awakening, but Kṛṣṇa derives transcendental bliss from Her activities.**
