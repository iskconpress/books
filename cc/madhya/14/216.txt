====== CC Madhya 14.216 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-śrīvāsa, tomāte nārada-svabhāva
> aiśvarya-bhāve tomāte, īśvara-prabhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---My dear Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:t:tomāte]]---in you; [[synonyms:n:nārada]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---the nature of Nārada; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---the mood of full opulence; [[synonyms:t:tomāte]]---in you; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---the power of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then told Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, "My dear Śrīvāsa, your nature is exactly like that of Nārada Muni. The Supreme Personality of Godhead's opulence is having a direct influence upon you.**
