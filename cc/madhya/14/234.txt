====== CC Madhya 14.234 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri sampradāya gāna kari' bahu śrānta haila
> mahāprabhura premāveśa dviguṇa bāḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---four groups of //saṅkīrtana// parties; [[synonyms:g:gāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after singing; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:ś:śrānta]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---were fatigued; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---the ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---twofold; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased.

===== Translation =====

**After much singing, all four saṅkīrtana parties grew fatigued, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic love increased twofold.**
