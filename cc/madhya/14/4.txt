====== CC Madhya 14.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata prabhu āchena premera āveśe
> hena-kāle pratāparudra karila praveśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---was; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstatic emotion of love; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:p:praveśe]]---entered.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was resting in ecstatic love, Mahārāja Pratāparudra entered the garden.**
