====== Cc. Madhya 14.224 ======

===== Text =====

> sahaja lokera kathā-yāhāṅ divya-gīta
> sahaja gamana kare,-yaiche nṛtya-pratīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahaja]] [[synonyms:l:lokera]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the talks of all the plain people; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---transcendental music; [[synonyms:s:sahaja]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---natural walking; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---they do; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---like; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:p:pratīta]]---appearing like dancing.

===== Translation =====

**"In Vṛndāvana, the natural speech of the people sounds like music, and their natural motion resembles a dance.**
