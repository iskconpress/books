====== CC Madhya 14.149 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mugdhā', 'madhyā', 'pragalbhā',-tina nāyikāra bheda
> 'mugdhā' nāhi jāne mānera vaidagdhya-vibheda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mugdhā]]---captivated; [[synonyms:m:madhyā]]---intermediate; [[synonyms:p:pragalbhā]]---impudent; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:n:nāyikāra]]---of heroines; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---divisions; [[synonyms:m:mugdhā]]---the captivated; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know; [[synonyms:m:mānera]]---of jealous anger; [[synonyms:v:vaidagdhya]]-[[synonyms:v:vibheda]]---the intricacies of cunning behavior.

===== Translation =====

**"Heroines may also be classified as captivated, intermediate and impudent. The captivated heroine does not know very much about the cunning intricacies of jealous anger.**
