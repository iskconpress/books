====== CC Madhya 14.74 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā-saṅge kṛṣṇa-līlā-ei haila jñāne
> ei rase magna prabhu ha-ilā āpane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---this was His consciousness; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:r:rase]] [[synonyms:m:magna]]---merged in this mellow; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---remained personally.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was always thinking of the pastimes of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa, and He remained personally merged in this consciousness.**
