====== CC Madhya 14.169 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bhāva-bhūṣāya bhūṣita śrī-rādhāra aṅga
> dekhite uthale kṛṣṇa-sukhābdhi-taraṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]]---so many; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūṣāya]]---with the ornaments of ecstasy; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣita]]---decorated; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the body; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:u:uthale]]---awakens; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:a:abdhi]]---of the ocean of Kṛṣṇa's happiness; [[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---waves.

===== Translation =====

**"When Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's body manifests the ornaments of many ecstatic symptoms, the ocean of Kṛṣṇa's happiness immediately displays transcendental waves.**
