====== CC Madhya 14.214 ======

===== Text =====

> dugdha āuṭi' dadhi mathe tomāra gopī-gaṇe
> āmāra ṭhākurāṇī vaise ratna-siṁhāsane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dugdha]] [[synonyms:ā:āuṭi']]---boiling milk; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---into yogurt; [[synonyms:m:mathe]]---churn; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---//gopīs//; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---mistress; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---sits down; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:s:siṁhāsane]]---on a throne of gems.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura continued to address Svarūpa Dāmodara: "Your gopīs are engaged in boiling milk and churning it to turn it into yogurt, but my mistress, the goddess of fortune, sits on a throne made of jewels and gems."**
