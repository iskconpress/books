====== Cc. Madhya 14.64 ======

===== Text =====

> ānande mahāprabhura prema uthalila
> dekhi' saba loka prema-sāgare bhāsila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love; [[synonyms:u:uthalila]]---flooded; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all people; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sāgare]]---in the ocean of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhāsila]]---were flooded.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was chanting and dancing, He was overwhelmed with ecstatic love, and all the people who saw Him were also flooded in the ocean of love of Godhead.**
