====== CC Madhya 14.17 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu bale,-ke tumi, karilā mora hita?
> ācambite āsi' piyāo kṛṣṇa-līlāmṛta?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---who are you; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---you have done; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:h:hita]]---welfare; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---all of a sudden; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:piyāo]]---you make Me drink; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the nectar of the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Finally Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Who are you? You have done so much for Me. All of a sudden you have come here and made Me drink the nectar of the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa."**
