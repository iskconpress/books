====== Cc. Madhya 14.191 ======

===== Text =====

> mukhe-netre haya nānā-bhāvera udgāra
> ei kāntā-bhāvera nāma 'lalita'-alaṅkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---on the mouth; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---on the eyes; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of various ecstasies; [[synonyms:u:udgāra]]---the awakening; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kāntā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of the condition of the female; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:l:lalita]]---of lalita; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---the ornament.

===== Translation =====

**"When there is an awakening of various ecstatic features on Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's face and in Her eyes, the lalita ornaments are manifest.**
