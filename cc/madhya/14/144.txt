====== Cc. Madhya 14.144 ======

===== Text =====

> 'dhīrā' kānte dūre dekhi' kare pratyutthāna
> nikaṭe āsile, kare āsana pradāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhīra]]---the sober; [[synonyms:k:kānte]]---the hero; [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---from a distance; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:pratyutthāna]]---stands up; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]] [[synonyms:ā:āsile]]---when the hero comes nearby; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---of a seat; [[synonyms:p:pradāna]]---offering.

===== Translation =====

**"When a sober heroine sees her hero approaching from a distance, she immediately stands up to receive him. When he comes near, she immediately offers him a place to sit.**
