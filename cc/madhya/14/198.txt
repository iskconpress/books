====== Cc. Madhya 14.198 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-vāñchā pūrṇa haya, kare pāṇi-rodha
> antare ānanda rādhā, bāhire vāmya-krodha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---the desire of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---fulfilled; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---let it be; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:pāṇi]]-[[synonyms:r:rodha]]---checking with Her hand; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:v:vāmya]]---opposition; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]---and anger.

===== Translation =====

**"Although Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī was checking Her sari with Her hand, internally She was thinking, 'Let Kṛṣṇa satisfy His desires.' In this way She was very pleased within, although She externally displayed opposition and anger.**
