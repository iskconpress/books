====== CC Madhya 14.101 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-saṅge svarūpādi kīrtanīyā gāya
> dik-vidik nāhi jñāna premera vanyāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyā]]---chanters; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sing; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:v:vidik]]---of time and circumstances; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:v:vanyāya]]---by inundation.

===== Translation =====

**Then devotees like Svarūpa Dāmodara and other kīrtana performers began to sing along with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Being inundated with ecstatic love, they lost all consideration of time and circumstance.**
