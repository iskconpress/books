====== CC Madhya 14.253 ======

===== Text =====

> prati vatsara guṇḍicāte bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> paṭṭa-ḍorī lañā āise ati baḍa raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prativatsara]]---every year; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicāte]]---to the Guṇḍicā temple cleansing ceremony; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with other devotees; [[synonyms:p:paṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍorī]]---silken rope; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---extremely; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---great; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---with pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**Every year thereafter, when the Guṇḍicā temple was being cleansed, Satyarāja and Rāmānanda Vasu would come with other devotees and with great pleasure bring silken rope.**
