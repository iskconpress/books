====== Cc. Madhya 14.129 ======

===== Text =====

> chatra-cāmara-dhvajā patākāra gaṇa
> nānā-vādya-āge nāce deva-dāsī-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:chatra]]---of umbrellas; [[synonyms:c:cāmara]]---and whisks; [[synonyms:d:dhvajā]]---and flags; [[synonyms:p:patākāra]]---and big flags; [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---congregation; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:v:vādya]]---varieties of musical parties; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---dancing girls.

===== Translation =====

**The palanquin was also surrounded by people carrying umbrellas, cāmara whisks and flags, and it was preceded by musicians and dancing girls.**
