====== Cc. Madhya 14.58 ======

===== Text =====

> nimeṣe ta' gela ratha guṇḍicāra dvāra
> caitanya-pratāpa dekhi' loke camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nimeṣe]]---in a moment; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---arrived; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicāra]] [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---at the door of the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:pratāpa]]---the strength of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---all the people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished.

===== Translation =====

**In a moment the car reached the door of the Guṇḍicā temple. Upon seeing the uncommon strength of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the people were struck with wonder.**
