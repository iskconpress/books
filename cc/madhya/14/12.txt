====== Cc. Madhya 14.12 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' sei śloka paḍe bāra bāra
> dui-janāra aṅge kampa, netre jala-dhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---that verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:janāra]]---of both of them (Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and the King Pratāparudra); [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---in the bodies; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---in the eyes; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---flow of water.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to recite the same verse again and again. Both the King and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were trembling, and tears were flowing from their eyes.**
