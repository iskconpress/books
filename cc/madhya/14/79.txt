====== CC Madhya 14.79 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-nityānande jala-phelāpheli
> ācārya hāriyā pāche kare gālāgāli

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānande]]---both Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:p:phelāpheli]]---throwing water on each other; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:h:hāriyā]]---Advaita Ācārya, after being defeated; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at the end; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:g:gālāgāli]]---accusing.

===== Translation =====

**The first sporting took place between Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu, who threw water upon one another. Advaita Ācārya was defeated, and He later began to rebuke Nityānanda Prabhu, calling Him bad names.**
