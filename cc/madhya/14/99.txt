====== CC Madhya 14.99 ======

===== Text =====

> eka eka vṛkṣa-tale eka eka gāna gāya
> parama-āveśe ekā nāce gaurarāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---under each and every tree; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]---a different; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---song; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sings; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:e:ekā]]---alone; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gaurarāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**As Vāsudeva Datta sang a different song beneath each and every tree, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced there alone in great ecstasy.**
