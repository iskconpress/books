====== CC Madhya 14.42 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu vaise nija-gaṇa lañā
> bhojana karāila sabāke ākaṇṭha pūriyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---sits; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with His personal associates; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---fed; [[synonyms:s:sabāke]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:ākaṇṭha]] [[synonyms:p:pūriyā]]---filling to the neck.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down with His personal associates and fed everyone of them very sumptuously until they were filled to the necks.**
