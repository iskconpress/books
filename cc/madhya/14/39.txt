====== CC Madhya 14.39 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṅti pāṅti kari' bhakta-gaṇe vasāilā
> pariveśana karibāre āpane lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṅti]] [[synonyms:p:pāṅti]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in different lines; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made seated; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]]---distribution; [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to do; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees sat down in lines, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally began to distribute the prasāda.**
