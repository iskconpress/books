====== CC Madhya 14.225 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvatra jala-yāhāṅ amṛta-samāna
> cid-ānanda jyotiḥ svādya-yāhāṅ mūrtimān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---the water; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:samāna]]---equal to nectar; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:j:jyotiḥ]]---effulgence; [[synonyms:s:svādya]]---perceived; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:m:mūrtimān]]---assuming a form.

===== Translation =====

**"The water in Vṛndāvana is nectar, and the brahmajyoti effulgence, which is full of transcendental bliss, is directly perceived there in its form.**
