====== CC Madhya 14.119 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-sama ei upavana-gaṇa
> tāhā dekhibāre utkaṇṭhita haya mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---exactly resembling Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---all these; [[synonyms:u:upavana]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---neighboring gardens; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---those gardens; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---for seeing; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]]---very eager; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---His mind becomes.

===== Translation =====

**Pointing out the neighboring gardens, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "All these gardens exactly resemble Vṛndāvana; therefore Lord Jagannātha is very eager to see them again.**
