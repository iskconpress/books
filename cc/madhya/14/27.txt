====== CC Madhya 14.27 ======

===== Text =====

> nāraṅga, cholaṅga, ṭābā, kamalā, bīja-pūra
> bādāma, chohārā, drākṣā, piṇḍa-kharjura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāraṅga]]---oranges; [[synonyms:c:cholaṅga]]---grapefruits; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭābā]]---another type of orange; [[synonyms:k:kamalā]]---tangerines; [[synonyms:b:bīja]]-[[synonyms:p:pūra]]---another type of tangerine; [[synonyms:b:bādāma]]---almonds; [[synonyms:c:chohārā]]---dried fruit; [[synonyms:d:drākṣā]]---raisins; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:k:kharjura]]---dates.

===== Translation =====

**There were also oranges, grapefruit, tangerines, almonds, dried fruit, raisins and dates.**
