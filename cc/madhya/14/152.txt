====== Cc. Madhya 14.152 ======

===== Text =====

> keha 'prakharā', keha 'mṛdu', keha haya 'samā'
> sva-svabhāve kṛṣṇera bāḍāya prema-sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---some; [[synonyms:p:prakharā]]---very talkative; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---some; [[synonyms:m:mṛdu]]---very mild; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---some of them are; [[synonyms:s:samā]]---equipoised; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāve]]---by their own characteristics; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bāḍāya]]---increases; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---limit of loving ecstasy.

===== Translation =====

**"Some of them are very talkative, some are mild, and some are equipoised. Each heroine, according to her own character, increases Śrī Kṛṣṇa's loving ecstasy.**
