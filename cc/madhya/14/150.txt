====== CC Madhya 14.150 ======

===== Text =====

> mukha ācchādiyā kare kevala rodana
> kāntera priya-vākya śuni' haya parasanna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukha]] [[synonyms:ā:ācchādiyā]]---covering the face; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---crying; [[synonyms:k:kāntera]]---of the lover; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:v:vākya]]---sweet words; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:p:parasanna]]---satisfied.

===== Translation =====

**"The captivated heroine simply covers her face and goes on crying. When she hears sweet words from her lover, she is very satisfied.**
