====== CC Madhya 14.211 ======

===== Text =====

> rathera upare kare daṇḍera tāḍana
> cora-prāya kare jagannāthera sevaka-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rathera]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the car; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍera]] [[synonyms:t:tāḍana]]---chastisement by sticks; [[synonyms:c:cora]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---almost like thieves; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---they treated; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the personal servants.

===== Translation =====

**"All the maidservants began to beat the Ratha car with sticks, and they treated the servants of Lord Jagannātha almost like thieves.**
