~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 14.108~~

====== CC Madhya 14.108 ======

===== Text =====

> mahotsava kara taiche viśeṣa sambhāra
> dekhi' mahāprabhura yaiche haya camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahotsava]]---the festival; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---perform; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---in such a way; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]] [[synonyms:s:sambhāra]]---with great gorgeousness; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---so that; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**King Pratāparudra said, "Hold this festival in such a gorgeous way that upon seeing it, Caitanya Mahāprabhu will be completely pleased and astonished.**
