====== Cc. Madhya 14.135 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣmī-saṅge dāsī-gaṇera prāgalbhya dekhiyā
> hāse mahāprabhura gaṇa mukhe hasta diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the company of the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:d:dāsī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the maidservants; [[synonyms:p:prāgalbhya]]---impudence; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---smile; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the companions; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---on their faces; [[synonyms:h:hasta]]---hands; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---covering.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's associates saw such impudence exhibited by the maidservants of the goddess of fortune, they covered their faces with their hands and began to smile.**
