====== Cc. Madhya 14.38 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtanīyāra pariśrama jāni' gaurarāya
> tāṅ-sabāre khāoyāite prabhura mana dhāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyāra]]---of all the singers; [[synonyms:p:pariśrama]]---labor; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:g:gaurarāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāite]]---to fill; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:d:dhāya]]---the mind was very eager.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu understood the labor of all the kīrtana chanters; therefore He was very eager to feed them sumptuously.**
