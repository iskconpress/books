====== Cc. Madhya 14.241 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā lañā nānā-raṅge karilā bhojana
> sandhyā snāna kari' kaila jagannātha daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with all the devotees; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in great jubilation; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---took the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after taking an evening bath; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visit to Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu finished His afternoon lunch, and after His evening bath, He went to see Lord Jagannātha.**
