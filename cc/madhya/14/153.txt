====== CC Madhya 14.153 ======

===== Text =====

> prākharya, mārdava, sāmya svabhāva nirdoṣa
> sei sei svabhāve kṛṣṇe karāya santoṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prākharya]]---talkativeness; [[synonyms:m:mārdava]]---mildness; [[synonyms:s:sāmya]]---being equipoised; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---nature; [[synonyms:n:nirdoṣa]]---faultless; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in those transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---they make; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---happy.

===== Translation =====

**"Although some of the gopīs are talkative, some mild and some equipoised, all of them are transcendental and faultless. They please Kṛṣṇa by their unique characteristics."**
