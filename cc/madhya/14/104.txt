====== Cc. Madhya 14.104 ======

===== Text =====

> nava dina guṇḍicāte rahe jagannātha
> mahāprabhu aiche līlā kare bhakta-sātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nava]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---nine days; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicāte]]---in the temple of Guṇḍicā; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---stays; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in the above-mentioned way; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:sātha]]---with His devotees.

===== Translation =====

**For nine continuous days His Lordship Śrī Jagannātha-deva stayed at the Guṇḍicā temple. During this time Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu also stayed there and performed the pastimes with His devotees that have already been described.**
