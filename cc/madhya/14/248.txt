====== CC Madhya 14.248 ======

===== Text =====

> kulīna-grāmī rāmānanda, satyarāja khāṅna
> tāṅre ājñā dila prabhu kariyā sammāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmī]]---the inhabitants of the village known as Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:s:satyarāja]] [[synonyms:k:khāṅna]]---Satyarāja Khān; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to them; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave an order; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---showing great respect.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda and Satyarāja Khān were present from Kulīna-grāma, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, with great respect, gave them the following orders.**
