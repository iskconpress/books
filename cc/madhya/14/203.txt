====== Cc. Madhya 14.203 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsa hāsiyā kahe,-śuna, dāmodara
> āmāra lakṣmīra dekha sampatti vistara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---O Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣmīra]]---of my goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---just see; [[synonyms:s:sampatti]] [[synonyms:v:vistara]]---the great opulence.

===== Translation =====

**This time, Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura smiled and told Dāmodara Paṇḍita, "My dear sir, please hear! Just see how opulent my goddess of fortune is!**
