====== Cc. Madhya 14.156 ======

===== Text =====

> premamaya-vapu kṛṣṇa bhakta-premādhīna
> śuddha-preme, rasa-guṇe, gopikā-pravīṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]-[[synonyms:v:vapu]]---body of love and ecstasy; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:a:adhīna]]---always subordinate to the loving feelings of His devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in pure uncontaminated love; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---and in the qualities of transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:g:gopikā]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:p:pravīṇa]]---very experienced.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa is full of ecstatic love and always subordinate to the love of his devotees. The gopīs are very experienced in pure love and in the dealings of transcendental mellows.**
