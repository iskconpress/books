====== CC Madhya 14.102 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata kata-kṣaṇa kari' vana-līlā
> narendra-sarovare gelā karite jala-khelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes in the garden; [[synonyms:n:narendra]]-[[synonyms:s:sarovare]]---in the lake known as Narendrasarovara; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---they went; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:khelā]]---sporting in the water.

===== Translation =====

**After thus performing pastimes in the garden for some time, they all went to a lake called Narendra-sarovara and there enjoyed sporting in the water.**
