====== Cc. Madhya 14.54 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu saba hastī ghucāila
> nija-gaṇe ratha-kāchi ṭānibāre dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:h:hastī]]---the elephants; [[synonyms:g:ghucāila]]---let free; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---to His own men; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:k:kāchi]]---the rope of the car; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭānibāre]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave to pull.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu let all the elephants go free and placed the car's ropes in the hands of His own men.**
