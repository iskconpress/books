====== CC Madhya 14.67 ======

===== Text =====

> āra bhakta-gaṇa cāturmāsye yata dina
> eka eka dina kari' karila baṇṭana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the remaining devotees; [[synonyms:c:cāturmāsye]]---in the four months of the rainy season; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---all the days; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---one day each; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:b:baṇṭana]]---shared.

===== Translation =====

**During the four months of the rainy season, the remaining devotees extended invitations to the Lord for one day each. In this way they shared invitations.**
