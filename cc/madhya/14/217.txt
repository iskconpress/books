====== CC Madhya 14.217 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅho dāmodara-svarūpa-śuddha-vrajavāsī
> aiśvarya nā jāne iṅho śuddha-preme bhāsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---here; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---a pure inhabitant of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---he does not know opulence; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in pure devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bhāsi']]---floating.

===== Translation =====

**"Svarūpa Dāmodara is a pure devotee of Vṛndāvana. He does not even know what opulence is, for he is simply absorbed in pure devotional service."**
