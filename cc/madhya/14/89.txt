====== Cc. Madhya 14.89 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane tāṅhāra upara karila śayana
> 'śeṣa-śāyī-līlā' prabhu kaila prakaṭana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:u:upara]]---upon Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lay down; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Śeṣaśāyī Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:p:prakaṭana]]---demonstrated.

===== Translation =====

**Lying down on Advaita Prabhu, who was floating on the water, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu demonstrated the pastime of Śeṣaśāyī Viṣṇu.**
