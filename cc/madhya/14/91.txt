====== CC Madhya 14.91 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata jala-krīḍā kari' kata-kṣaṇa
> āiṭoṭā āilā prabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---sporting in the water; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after performing; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:ā:āiṭoṭā]]---to the place named Āiṭoṭā; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---accompanied by the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**After sporting in the water for some time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His place at Āiṭoṭā, accompanied by His devotees.**
