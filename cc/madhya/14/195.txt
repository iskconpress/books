====== CC Madhya 14.195 ======

===== Text =====

> lobhe āsi' kṛṣṇa kare kañcukākarṣaṇa
> antare ullāsa, rādhā kare nivāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lobhe]]---in greed; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kañcuka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---snatching the border of Her sari; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---very much pleased; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nivāraṇa]]---stopping.

===== Translation =====

**"When Kṛṣṇa comes forward and greedily snatches at the border of Rādhārāṇī's sari, She is actually very pleased within, but overtly She tries to stop Him.**
