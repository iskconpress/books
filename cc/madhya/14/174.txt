====== CC Madhya 14.174 ======

===== Text =====

> garvābhilāṣa-rudita-
> smitāsūyā-bhaya-krudhām
> saṅkarī-karaṇaṁ harṣād
> ucyate kila-kiñcitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:a:abhilāṣa]]---ambition; [[synonyms:r:rudita]]---crying; [[synonyms:s:smita]]---smiling; [[synonyms:a:asūyā]]---envying; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fearing; [[synonyms:k:krudhām]]---anger; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarī]]-[[synonyms:k:karaṇam]]---the act of shrinking away; [[synonyms:h:harṣāt]]---because of jubilation; [[synonyms:u:ucyate]]---is called; [[synonyms:k:kila]]-[[synonyms:k:kiñcitam]]---ecstatic symptoms known as //kila-kiñcita.//

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Pride, ambition, weeping, smiling, envy, fear and anger are the seven ecstatic loving symptoms manifest by a jubilant shrinking away, and these symptoms are called kila-kiñcita-bhāva.'**
