====== CC Madhya 14.231 ======

===== Text =====

> rasāveśe prabhura nṛtya, svarūpera gāna
> 'bala' 'bala' bali' prabhu pāte nija-kāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic mellows; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---the dancing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]] [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---and singing by Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:b:bala]] [[synonyms:b:bala]]---go on speaking, go on speaking; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāte]]---extends; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kāṇa]]---own ear.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was dancing in ecstatic love and Svarūpa Dāmodara was singing, the Lord said, "Go on singing! Go on singing!" The Lord then extended His own ears.**
