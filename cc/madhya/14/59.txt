====== CC Madhya 14.59 ======

===== Text =====

> 'jaya gauracandra', jaya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya'
> ei-mata kolāhala loke dhanya dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---all glories to Gaurahari; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---all glories to Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---a tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people in general; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]] [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---began to chant, "Wonderful, wonderful!"

===== Translation =====

**The crowd made a tumultuous vibration, chanting, "Jaya Gauracandra! Jaya Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya!" Then the people began to chant, "Wonderful! Wonderful!"**
