====== CC Madhya 14.60 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhiyā pratāparudra pātra-mitra-saṅge
> prabhura mahimā dekhi' preme phule aṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]-[[synonyms:m:mitra]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with his ministers and friends; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the greatness; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love; [[synonyms:p:phule]]---eruptions; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the greatness of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Pratāparudra Mahārāja and his ministers and friends were so moved by ecstatic love that their hair stood on end.**
