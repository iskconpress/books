====== CC Madhya 14.43 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana kari' vasilā prabhu kari' ācamana
> prasāda ubarila, khāya sahasreka jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after eating; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord sat down; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---washing the mouth; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:u:ubarila]]---there was so much excess; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---ate; [[synonyms:s:sahasreka]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---thousands of men.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing, the Lord washed His mouth and sat down. There was so much extra prasāda that it was distributed to thousands.**
