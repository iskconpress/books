====== CC Madhya 14.237 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda vinā prabhuke dhare kon jana
> prabhura āveśa nā yāya, nā rahe kīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except for Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---can catch; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---what person; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---the ecstasy; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---does not go away; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---could not be continued; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---kīrtana.

===== Translation =====

**Only Nityānanda Prabhu could catch Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, but the ecstatic mood of the Lord would not stop. At the same time, kīrtana could not be continued.**
