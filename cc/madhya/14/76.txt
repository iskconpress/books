====== Cc. Madhya 14.76 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane sakala bhakte siñce jala diyā
> saba bhakta-gaṇa siñce caudike beḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:s:siñce]]---sprinkles; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---with water; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:s:siñce]]---sprinkle; [[synonyms:c:cau]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]] [[synonyms:b:beḍiyā]]---surrounding the Lord on all sides.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord personally splashed all the devotees with water, and the devotees, surrounding Him on all sides, also splashed the Lord.**
