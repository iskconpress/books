====== Cc. Madhya 14.50 ======

===== Text =====

> vyagra hañā āne rājā matta-hātī-gaṇa
> ratha cālāite rathe karila yojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vyagra]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---with eagerness; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---brings; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:m:matta]]-[[synonyms:h:hātī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---very strong elephants; [[synonyms:r:ratha]] [[synonyms:c:cālāite]]---to make the car move; [[synonyms:r:rathe]]---to the car; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:y:yojana]]---harnessed.

===== Translation =====

**Becoming even more eager to move the car, the King had very strong elephants brought forth and harnessed to it.**
