====== CC Madhya 14.70 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle snāna kari' dekhi' jagannātha
> saṅkīrtane nṛtya kare bhakta-gaṇa sātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking a bath; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---in the performance of //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---dances; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:s:sātha]]---with the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**After taking His bath early in the morning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go see Lord Jagannātha in the temple. Then He would perform saṅkīrtana with His devotees.**
