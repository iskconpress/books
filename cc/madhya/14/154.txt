====== CC Madhya 14.154 ======

===== Text =====

> e-kathā śuniyā prabhura ānanda apāra
> 'kaha, kaha, dāmodara',-bale bāra bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing this description; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited happiness; [[synonyms:k:kaha]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please go on speaking; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---My dear Dāmodara; [[synonyms:b:bale]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---He said again and again.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt unlimited happiness upon hearing these descriptions, and He again and again requested Svarūpa Dāmodara to continue speaking.**
