====== Cc. Madhya 14.184 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe ta' svarūpa-gosāñi kahite lāgilā
> śuni' prabhura bhakta-gaṇa mahā-sukha pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---achieved great happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Being thus requested, Svarūpa Dāmodara began to speak. All the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were very happy to hear him.**
