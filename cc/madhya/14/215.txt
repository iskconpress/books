====== CC Madhya 14.215 ======

===== Text =====

> nārada-prakṛti śrīvāsa kare parihāsa
> śuni' hāse mahāprabhura yata nija-dāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārada]]-[[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---with the nature of Nārada Muni; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]]---joking; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---smile; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---personal servants.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, who was enjoying the mood of Nārada Muni, thus made jokes. Hearing him, all the personal servants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to smile.**
