====== Cc. Madhya 14.121 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā-puṣpodyāne tathā khele rātri-dine
> lakṣmīdevīre saṅge nāhi laya ki kāraṇe?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---in the various flower gardens; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:k:khele]]---He plays; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---both day and night; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:d:devīre]]---Lakṣmīdevī, the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---take; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇe]]---what is the reason.

===== Translation =====

**"The Lord enjoys His pastimes day and night in various flower gardens there. But why did He not take Lakṣmīdevī, the goddess of fortune, with Him?"**
