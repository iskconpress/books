====== Cc. Madhya 14.252 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgyavān satyarāja vasu rāmānanda
> sevā-ājñā pāñā haila parama-ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---very fortunate; [[synonyms:s:satyarāja]]---Satyarāja; [[synonyms:v:vasu]] [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Vasu; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order for service; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---supremely; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---happy.

===== Translation =====

**After receiving orders from the Lord for the rendering of service, the fortunate Satyarāja and Rāmānanda Vasu were highly pleased.**
