====== Cc. Madhya 14.185 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā vasi' āche, kibā vṛndāvane yāya
> tāhāṅ yadi ācambite kṛṣṇa-daraśana pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]] [[synonyms:v:vasi']] [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is sitting; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---or; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is going to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---all of a sudden; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets the opportunity to see Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Sometimes when Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is sitting or when She is going to Vṛndāvana, She sometimes sees Kṛṣṇa.**
