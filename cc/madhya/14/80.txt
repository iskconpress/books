====== CC Madhya 14.80 ======

===== Text =====

> vidyānidhira jala-keli svarūpera sane
> gupta-datte jala-keli kare dui jane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidyānidhira]]---of Vidyānidhi; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---water sports; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:g:gupta]]-[[synonyms:d:datte]]---both Murāri Gupta and Vāsudeva Datta; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---water sports; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---two persons.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara and Vidyānidhi also threw water upon one another, and Murāri Gupta and Vāsudeva Datta also sported in that way.**
