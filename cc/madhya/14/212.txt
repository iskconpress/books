====== CC Madhya 14.212 ======

===== Text =====

> saba bhṛtya-gaṇa kahe,-yoḍa kari' hāta
> 'kāli āni diba tomāra āge jagannātha,

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---all the servants said; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:h:hāta]]---folding the hands; [[synonyms:k:kāli]]---tomorrow; [[synonyms:ā:āni]]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:diba]]---we shall give; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of you; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**"Finally all of Lord Jagannātha's servants submitted to the goddess of fortune with folded hands, assuring her that they would bring Lord Jagannātha before her the very next day.**
