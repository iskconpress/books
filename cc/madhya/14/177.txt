====== Cc. Madhya 14.177 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā-svādu aṣṭa-bhāva ekatra milana
> yāhāra āsvāde tṛpta haya kṛṣṇa-mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:s:svādu]]---tasteful; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---eight kinds of ecstatic symptoms; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---at one place; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:ā:āsvāde]]---by the tasting; [[synonyms:t:tṛpta]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"There are eight symptoms of ecstatic love on the platform of transcendental jubilation, and when they are combined and tasted by Kṛṣṇa, the Lord's mind is completely satisfied.**
