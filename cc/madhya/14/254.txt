====== CC Madhya 14.254 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe jagannātha yāi' vasilā siṁhāsane
> mahāprabhu ghare āilā lañā bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---//going//; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat; [[synonyms:s:siṁhāsane]]---on His throne; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to His residence; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went back; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Lord Jagannātha returned to His temple and sat on His throne while Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His residence with His devotees.**
