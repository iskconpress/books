====== CC Madhya 14.240 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera prasāda āila bahu upahāra
> lakṣmīra prasāda āila vividha prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the //prasāda// offered to Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---arrived; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:u:upahāra]]---offerings; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmīra]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---food offered to Lakṣmīdevī; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---arrived; [[synonyms:v:vividha]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---all varieties.

===== Translation =====

**Then there arrived in large quantities a variety of food that had been offered to Śrī Jagannātha and a variety that had been offered to the goddess of fortune.**
