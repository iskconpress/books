====== Cc. Madhya 14.62 ======

===== Text =====

> subhadrā-balarāma nija-siṁhāsane āilā
> jagannāthera snāna-bhoga ha-ite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:subhadrā]]-[[synonyms:b:balarāma]]---Subhadrā and Balarāma; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---own; [[synonyms:s:siṁhāsane]]---on thrones; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]-[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---bathing and offering food; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to take place.

===== Translation =====

**Subhadrā and Balarāma also sat on their respective thrones. There followed the bathing of Lord Jagannātha and finally the offering of food.**
