====== CC Madhya 14.197 ======

===== Text =====

> stanādharādi-grahaṇe
> hṛt-prītāv api sambhramāt
> bahiḥ krodho vyathitavat
> proktaṁ kuṭṭamitaṁ budhaiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:stana]]---breasts; [[synonyms:a:adhara]]---lips; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and so on; [[synonyms:g:grahaṇe]]---when capturing; [[synonyms:h:hṛt]]-[[synonyms:p:prītau]]---satisfaction of the heart; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even though; [[synonyms:s:sambhramāt]]---because of respectfulness; [[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]---externally; [[synonyms:k:krodhaḥ]]---anger; [[synonyms:v:vyathita]]---aggrieved; [[synonyms:v:vat]]---as if; [[synonyms:p:proktam]]---called; [[synonyms:k:kuṭṭamitam]]---the technical term //kuṭṭamita//; [[synonyms:b:budhaiḥ]]---by learned scholars.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'When the border of Her sari and the cloth veiling Her face are caught, She externally appears offended and angry, but within Her heart She is very happy. Learned scholars call this attitude kuṭṭamita.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Ujjvala-nīlamaṇi// (//Anubhāva-prakaraṇa// 49).
