====== CC Madhya 14.175 ======

===== Text =====

> āra sāta bhāva āsi' sahaje milaya
> asta-bhāva-sammilane 'mahābhāva' haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:s:sāta]]---seven; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---ecstatic symptoms; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming together; [[synonyms:s:sahaje]]---naturally; [[synonyms:m:milaya]]---become mixed; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---of eight kinds of ecstatic emotional symptoms; [[synonyms:s:sammilane]]---by the combining; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is //mahābhāva.//

===== Translation =====

**"There are seven other transcendental ecstatic symptoms, and when they combine on the platform of jubilation, the combination is called mahābhāva.**
