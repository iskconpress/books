====== Cc. Madhya 14.110 ======

===== Text =====

> dhvajāvṛnda-patākā-ghaṇṭāya karaha maṇḍana
> nānā-vādya-nṛtya-dolāya karaha sājana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhvajā]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---all kinds of flags; [[synonyms:p:patākā]]---big flags; [[synonyms:g:ghaṇṭāya]]---with ringing bells; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---make; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍana]]---decoration; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:v:vādya]]---all kinds of musical parties; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:d:dolāya]]---on the carrier; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:s:sājana]]---decorate attractively.

===== Translation =====

**"Collect all kinds of small and large flags and ringing bells. Then decorate the carrier and have various musical and dancing parties accompany it. In this way decorate the carrier attractively.**
