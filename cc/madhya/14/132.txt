====== CC Madhya 14.132 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera mukhya mukhya yata bhṛtya-gaṇe
> lakṣmīdevīra dāsī-gaṇa karena bandhane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---the principal; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---servants; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:d:devīra]]---of the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:d:dāsī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---maidservants; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:b:bandhane]]---arrested.

===== Translation =====

**When the procession arrived, the maidservants of the goddess of fortune began to arrest all the principal servants of Lord Jagannātha.**
