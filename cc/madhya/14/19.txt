====== CC Madhya 14.19 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu tāṅre aiśvarya dekhāila
> 'kāreha nā kahibe' ei niṣedha karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to the King; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---divine power; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed; [[synonyms:k:kāreha]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahibe]]---do not speak to anyone; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:n:niṣedha]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---forbade.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu displayed some of His divine opulences to the King, and He forbade him to disclose this to anyone.**
