====== Cc. Madhya 14.245 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat kaila prabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> parama ānande karena nartana-kīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting and dancing.

===== Translation =====

**As previously, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees again chanted and danced with great pleasure.**
