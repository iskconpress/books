====== CC Madhya 14.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya gauracandra śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya
> jaya jaya nityānanda jayādvaita dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---to Gauracandra; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---exalted.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, known as Gauracandra! All glories to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu! All glories to Advaita Ācārya, who is so exalted!**
