====== CC Madhya 14.22 ======

===== Text =====

> daṇḍavat kari' rājā bāhire calilā
> yoḍa hasta kari' saba bhaktere vandilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]]---folded; [[synonyms:h:hasta]]---hands; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhaktere]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vandilā]]---offered prayers.

===== Translation =====

**Submissively offering prayers to the devotees with folded hands and offering obeisances to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the King went outside.**
