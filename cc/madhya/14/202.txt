====== CC Madhya 14.202 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta kṛṣṇera līlā nā yāya varṇana
> āpane varṇena yadi 'sahasra-vadana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is possible; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:v:varṇena]]---describes; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---the thousand-mouthed Śeṣa.

===== Translation =====

**"It is not at all possible to describe the unlimited pastimes of Śrī Kṛṣṇa, even though He Himself describes them in His incarnation of Sahasra-vadana, the thousand-mouthed Śeṣa Nāga."**
