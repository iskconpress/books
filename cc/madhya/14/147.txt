====== CC Madhya 14.147 ======

===== Text =====

> 'adhīrā' niṣṭhura-vākye karaye bhartsana
> karṇotpale tāḍe, kare mālāya bandhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adhīrā]]---the restless heroine; [[synonyms:n:niṣṭhura]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]]---by cruel words; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhartsana]]---chastisement; [[synonyms:k:karṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:utpale]] [[synonyms:t:tāḍe]]---pulls the ear; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:mālāya]]---with a garland; [[synonyms:b:bandhana]]---binding.

===== Translation =====

**"The restless heroine, however, sometimes chastises her lover with cruel words, sometimes pulls his ear and sometimes binds him with a flower garland.**
