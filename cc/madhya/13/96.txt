====== CC Madhya 13.96 ======

===== Text =====

> kruddha hañā tāṅre kichu cāhe balibāre
> āpani pratāparudra nivārila tāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kruddha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming angry; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:b:balibāre]]---to speak; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:n:nivārila]]---stopped; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him.

===== Translation =====

**As the angered Haricandana was about to speak to Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, Pratāparudra Mahārāja personally stopped him.**
