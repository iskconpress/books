====== CC Madhya 13.163 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñira bhāgya nā yāya varṇana
> prabhute āviṣṭa yāṅra kāya, vākya, mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---the fortune; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---can be described; [[synonyms:p:prabhute]]---in the service of the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---fully absorbed; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]---body; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---words; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**No one can describe the good fortune of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, for he is always absorbed in the service of the Lord with his body, mind and words.**
