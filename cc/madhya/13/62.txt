====== Cc. Madhya 13.62 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma, kāśī-miśra,-dui mahāśaya
> rājāre prasāda dekhi' ha-ilā vismaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---two great personalities; [[synonyms:r:rājāre]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---mercy; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---astonished.

===== Translation =====

**When the two great personalities Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Kāśī Miśra saw Caitanya Mahāprabhu's causeless mercy upon the King, they were astonished.**
