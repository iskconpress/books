====== CC Madhya 13.208 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā yei śune sei śrī-caitanya pāya
> sudṛḍha viśvāsa-saha prema-bhakti haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---will achieve Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---firm; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---conviction; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service in great love; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**Anyone who hears this description of the car festival will attain Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He will also attain the elevated state by which he will have firm conviction in devotional service and love of Godhead.**
