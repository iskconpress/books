====== CC Madhya 13.151 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā-sabāra prema-rase, āmāke karila vaśe,
> āmi tomāra adhīna kevala
> tomā-sabā chāḍāñā, āmā dūra-deśe lañā,
> rākhiyāche durdaiva prabala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---by the ecstasy and mellows of loving affairs; [[synonyms:ā:āmāke]]---Me; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---you have made; [[synonyms:v:vaśe]]---subservient; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of you; [[synonyms:a:adhīna]]---subservient; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---from all of you; [[synonyms:c:chāḍāñā]]---separating; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---Me; [[synonyms:d:dūra]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to distant countries; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:r:rākhiyāche]]---has kept; [[synonyms:d:durdaiva]]---misfortune; [[synonyms:p:prabala]]---very powerful.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'My dear Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, I am always subservient to the loving affairs of all of you. I am under your control only. My separation from you and residence in distant places have occurred due to My strong misfortune.**
