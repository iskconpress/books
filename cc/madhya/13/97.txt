====== Cc. Madhya 13.97 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgyavān tumi-iṅhāra hasta-sparśa pāilā
> āmāra bhāgye nāhi, tumi kṛtārtha hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you are very fortunate; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:hasta]]---of the hand; [[synonyms:s:sparśa]]---touch; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---have received; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---in my fortune; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is no such thing; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---have become graced.

===== Translation =====

**King Pratāparudra said, "You are very fortunate, for you have been graced by the touch of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura. I am not so fortunate. You should feel obliged to him."**
