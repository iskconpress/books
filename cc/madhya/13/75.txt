====== CC Madhya 13.75 ======

===== Text =====

> ei daśa jana prabhura saṅge gāya, dhāya
> āra saba sampradāya cāri dike gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:daśa]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---these ten persons; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:d:dhāya]]---run; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---others; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---groups of men; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:d:dike]]---all around; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant.

===== Translation =====

**These devotees [Svarūpa Dāmodara and the devotees in his charge] sang along with the Lord, and they also ran beside Him. All the other groups of men also sang.**
