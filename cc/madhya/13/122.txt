====== Cc. Madhya 13.122 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloka mahāprabhu paḍe bāra bāra
> svarūpa vinā artha keha nā jāne ihāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except for Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this.

===== Translation =====

**This verse was recited by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again and again. But for Svarūpa Dāmodara, no one could understand its meaning.**
