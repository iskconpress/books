====== Cc. Madhya 13.55 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtana dekhiyā jagannātha haraṣita
> saṅkīrtana dekhe ratha kariyā sthagita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtanadekhiyā]]---by seeing the performance of //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---performance of //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sthagita]]---stopping.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Jagannātha was very pleased by the saṅkīrtana, and He brought His car to a standstill just to see the performance.**
