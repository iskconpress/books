====== CC Madhya 13.205 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahila prabhura mahā-saṅkīrtana
> jagannāthera āge yaiche karila nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---the great congregational chanting; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---He did; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described the great performance of congregational chanting by Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as He danced in front of Lord Jagannātha.**
