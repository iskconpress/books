====== CC Madhya 13.101 ======

===== Text =====

> uddaṇḍa nṛtye prabhura adbhuta vikāra
> aṣṭa sāttvika bhāva udaya haya sama-kāla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uddaṇḍa]]---jumping; [[synonyms:n:nṛtye]]---by dancing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformations; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]] [[synonyms:s:sāttvika]]---eight transcendental kinds; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:u:udaya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---awaken; [[synonyms:s:sama]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]]---simultaneously.

===== Translation =====

**When Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced and jumped high, eight wonderful transformations indicative of divine ecstasy were seen in His body. All these symptoms were visible simultaneously.**
