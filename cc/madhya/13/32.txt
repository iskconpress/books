====== Cc. Madhya 13.32 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtanīyā-gaṇe dila mālya-candana
> svarūpa, śrīvāsa,-yāhāṅ mukhya dui-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the performers of //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---garlands and sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---principal; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord also gave garlands and sandalwood pulp to the performers of saṅkīrtana. The chief two performers were Svarūpa Dāmodara and Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura.**
