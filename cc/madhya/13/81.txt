====== CC Madhya 13.81 ======

===== Text =====

> eta paḍi' punarapi karila praṇāma
> yoḍa-hāte bhakta-gaṇa vande bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting these; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---the Lord offered; [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vande]]---offer prayer; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Having recited all these verses from scripture, the Lord again offered His obeisances, and all the devotees, with folded hands, also offered prayers to the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
