====== CC Madhya 13.7 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita, nitāi ādi saṅge bhakta-gaṇa
> sukhe mahāprabhu dekhe īśvara-gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nitāi]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---how the Lord is starting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His prominent devotees-Advaita Ācārya, Nityānanda Prabhu and others-were very happy to observe how Lord Jagannātha began the Ratha-yātrā.**
