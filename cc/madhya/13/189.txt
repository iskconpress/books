====== Cc. Madhya 13.189 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu ratha pradakṣiṇa kariyā
> ratha-pāche yāi' ṭhele rathe māthā diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:p:pradakṣiṇa]]---circumambulation; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:p:pāche]]---to the rear of the car; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhele]]---pushes; [[synonyms:r:rathe]]---on the car; [[synonyms:m:māthā]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---by placing the head.

===== Translation =====

**After circumambulating Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went behind the car and began pushing it with His head.**
