====== Cc. Madhya 13.33 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri sampradāye haila cabbiśa gāyana
> dui dui mārdaṅgika haila aṣṭa jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāye]]---in the four parties; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there were; [[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]]---twenty-four; [[synonyms:g:gāyana]]---performers of //kīrtana//; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two in each party; [[synonyms:m:mārdaṅgika]]---players of //mṛdaṅga// drums; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there were; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---eight persons.

===== Translation =====

**There were altogether four parties of kīrtana performers, comprising twenty-four chanters. In each party there were also two mṛdaṅga players, making an additional eight persons.**
