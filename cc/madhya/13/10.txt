====== Cc. Madhya 13.10 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṭi-taṭe baddha, dṛḍha sthūla paṭṭa-ḍorī
> dui dike dayitā-gaṇa uṭhāya tāhā dhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṭi]]-[[synonyms:t:taṭe]]---on the waist; [[synonyms:b:baddha]]---bound; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---strong; [[synonyms:s:sthūla]]---thick; [[synonyms:p:paṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍorī]]---rope made of silk; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:d:dike]]---from two sides; [[synonyms:d:dayitā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the //dayitās//; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāya]]---raise; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that rope; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord Jagannātha Deity was bound at the waist by a strong, thick rope made of silk. From two sides the dayitās caught hold of this rope and raised the Deity.**
