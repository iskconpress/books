====== CC Madhya 13.77 ======

===== Text =====

> namo brahmaṇya-devāya
> go-brāhmaṇa-hitāya ca
> jagad-dhitāya kṛṣṇāya
> govindāya namo namaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---all obeisances; [[synonyms:b:brahmaṇya]]-[[synonyms:d:devāya]]---to the Lord worshipable by persons in brahminical culture; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---for cows and //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:h:hitāya]]---beneficial; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:h:hitāya]]---to one who always is benefiting the whole world; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇāya]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:govindāya]]---unto Govinda; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---repeated obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Lord Kṛṣṇa, who is the worshipable Deity for all brahminical men, who is the well-wisher of cows and brāhmaṇas, and who is always benefiting the whole world. I offer my repeated obeisances to the Personality of Godhead, known as Kṛṣṇa and Govinda.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from the //Viṣṇu Purāṇa// (1.19.65).
