====== CC Madhya 13.46 ======

===== Text =====

> khaṇḍera sampradāya kare anyatra kīrtana
> narahari nāce tāhāṅ śrī-raghunandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:khaṇḍera]]---of the place named Khaṇḍa; [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---party; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:a:anyatra]]---in a different place; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting; [[synonyms:n:narahari]]---Narahari; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raghunandana]]---Raghunandana.

===== Translation =====

**Another party was formed by the people of Khaṇḍa. These people were singing in a different place. In that group, Narahari Prabhu and Raghunandana were dancing.**
