====== Cc. Madhya 13.107 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu stambha, kabhu prabhu bhūmite loṭāya
> śuṣka-kāṣṭha-sama pada-hasta nā calaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:s:stambha]]---stunned; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:l:loṭāya]]---rolls; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]---dry; [[synonyms:k:kāṣṭha]]---wood; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:h:hasta]]---legs and hands; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---do not; [[synonyms:c:calaya]]---move.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes He appeared stunned, and sometimes He rolled on the ground. Indeed, sometimes His legs and hands became as hard as dry wood, and He did not move.**
