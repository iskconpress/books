====== CC Madhya 13.135 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa saṅge yāra artha kare āsvādana
> nṛtya-madhye sei śloka karena paṭhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---taste; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the midst of dancing; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---that verse; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:p:paṭhana]]---recites.

===== Translation =====

**While dancing, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again began to recite that verse, which He tasted in the association of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.**
