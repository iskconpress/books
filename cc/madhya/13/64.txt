====== CC Madhya 13.64 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu eka mūrti, kabhu hana bahu-mūrti
> kārya-anurūpa prabhu prakāśaye śakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---one form; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:h:hana]]---becomes; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---many forms; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]-[[synonyms:a:anurūpa]]---according to the program of activities; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prakāśaye]]---exhibits; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---His internal potency.

===== Translation =====

**According to His need, the Lord sometimes exhibited one form and sometimes many. This was being executed by His internal potency.**
