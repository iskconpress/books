====== CC Madhya 13.11 ======

===== Text =====

> ucca dṛḍha tulī saba pāti' sthāne sthāne
> eka tulī haite tvarāya āra tulīte āne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ucca]]---puffed up; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---strong; [[synonyms:t:tulī]]---pads made of cotton; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pāti']]---spreading; [[synonyms:s:sthānesthāne]]---from one place to another; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:t:tulī]]---one pad; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:t:tvarāya]]---very soon; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---next; [[synonyms:t:tulīte]]---on the pad; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---bring.

===== Translation =====

**Strong, puffed-up cotton pads called tulīs were spread out from the throne to the car, and the heavy Deity of Lord Jagannātha was carried from one pillow-like pad to the next by the dayitās.**
