====== Cc. Madhya 13.25 ======

===== Text =====

> sūkṣma śveta-bālu pathe pulinera sama
> dui dike ṭoṭā, saba-yena vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---fine; [[synonyms:ś:śveta]]-[[synonyms:b:bālu]]---white sand; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the path; [[synonyms:p:pulinerasama]]---just like the bank of the Yamunā; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:d:dike]]---on two sides; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭoṭā]]---gardens; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---like; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---the holy place Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**The fine, white sand spread all over the path resembled the bank of the Yamunā, and the small gardens on both sides looked just like those in Vṛndāvana.**
