====== CC Madhya 13.31 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-ācārya, āra prabhu-nityānanda
> śrī-hasta-sparśe duṅhāra ha-ila ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:hasta]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśe]]---by the touch of the transcendental hand of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of Them; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**Similarly, when Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu felt the touch of the transcendental hand of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, They were both very pleased.**
