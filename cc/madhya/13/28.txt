====== CC Madhya 13.28 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣaṇe sthira hañā rahe, ṭānileha nā cale
> īśvara-icchāya cale, nā cale kāro bale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:s:sthira]]---still; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---stays; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭāṅileha]]---in spite of being pulled; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:cale]]---does not go; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---by the will of the Lord; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:cale]]---does not go; [[synonyms:k:kāro]]---of anyone; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---by the strength.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes the car would stand still and not move, even though it was pulled very vigorously. The chariot therefore moved by the will of the Lord, not by the strength of any ordinary person.**
