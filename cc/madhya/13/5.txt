====== CC Madhya 13.5 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṇḍu-vijaya dekhibāre karila gamana
> jagannātha yātrā kaila chāḍi' siṁhāsana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṇḍu]]-[[synonyms:v:vijaya]]---the ceremony named Pāṇḍu-vijaya; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---for seeing; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---go; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---departure; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving; [[synonyms:s:siṁhāsana]]---the throne.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His personal associates then went to see the ceremony of Pāṇḍu-vijaya. During this ceremony, Lord Jagannātha leaves His throne and gets up onto the car.**
