====== CC Madhya 13.180 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata prabhu nṛtya karite bhramite
> pratāparudrera āge lāgilā paḍite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---dancing; [[synonyms:b:bhramite]]---wandering; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudrera]]---of King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]]---to fall down.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was dancing and wandering in this way, He fell down in front of Mahārāja Pratāparudra.**
