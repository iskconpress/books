====== Cc. Madhya 13.125 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha dekhi' prabhura se bhāva uṭhila
> sei bhāvāviṣṭa hañā dhuyā gāoyāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---that ecstasy; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---awakened; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---absorbed in that ecstasy; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:d:dhuyā]]---refrain; [[synonyms:g:gāoyāila]]---caused to sing.

===== Translation =====

**Similarly, after seeing Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu awoke with the ecstasy of the gopīs. Being absorbed in this ecstasy, He asked Svarūpa Dāmodara to sing the refrain.**
