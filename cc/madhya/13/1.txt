~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 13.1~~

====== CC Madhya 13.1 ======

===== Text =====

> sa jīyāt kṛṣṇa-caitanyaḥ
> śrī-rathāgre nanarta yaḥ
> yenāsīj jagatāṁ citraṁ
> jagannātho 'pi vismitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:j:jīyāt]]---may live long; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanyaḥ]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:a:agre]]---in the front of the car; [[synonyms:n:nanarta]]---danced; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---there was; [[synonyms:j:jagatām]]---of the whole universe; [[synonyms:c:citram]]---wonder; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthaḥ]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:v:vismitaḥ]]---was astonished.

===== Translation =====

**May the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya, who danced in front of the car of Śrī Jagannātha, be all glorified! By seeing His dancing, not only was the whole universe held in wonder, but Lord Jagannātha Himself became very much astonished.**
