====== CC Madhya 13.202 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe mahāprabhu upavana pāñā
> puṣpodyāne gṛha-piṇḍāya rahilā paḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:upavana]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---having come to a nice nearby garden; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---in that flower garden; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:p:piṇḍāya]]---on the raised platform; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---falling flat.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the garden. Immersed in a great ecstatic emotion, He fell flat on a raised platform there.**
