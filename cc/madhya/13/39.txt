====== CC Madhya 13.39 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgādāsa, haridāsa, śrīmān, śubhānanda
> śrī-rāma paṇḍita, tāhāṅ nāce nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgādāsa]]---Gaṅgādāsa; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa; [[synonyms:ś:śrīmān]]---Śrīmān; [[synonyms:ś:śubhānanda]]---Śubhānanda; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Śrī Rāma Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda.

===== Translation =====

**The five singers who responded to the singing of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura were Gaṅgādāsa, Haridāsa, Śrīmān, Śubhānanda and Śrī Rāma Paṇḍita. Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu was appointed as a dancer.**
