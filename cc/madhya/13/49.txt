====== Cc. Madhya 13.49 ======

===== Text =====

> vaiṣṇavera megha-ghaṭāya ha-ila bādala
> kīrtanānande saba varṣe netra-jala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:m:megha]]-[[synonyms:g:ghaṭāya]]---by the assembly of clouds; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:bādala]]---rainfall; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in the blissful situation of chanting; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of them; [[synonyms:v:varṣe]]---rain; [[synonyms:n:netra]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---tears from the eyes.

===== Translation =====

**All the Vaiṣṇavas came together like an assembly of clouds. As the devotees chanted the holy names, tears fell in great ecstasy like rainfall from their eyes.**
