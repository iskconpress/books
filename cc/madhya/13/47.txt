====== CC Madhya 13.47 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera āge cāri sampradāya gāya
> dui pāśe dui, pāche eka sampradāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of the Deity Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---four groups were chanting; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---on two sides; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---another two groups; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at the rear; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---another group.

===== Translation =====

**Four parties chanted and danced in front of Lord Jagannātha, and on both sides were two other parties. Another was at the rear.**
