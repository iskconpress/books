====== CC Madhya 13.4 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina mahāprabhu hañā sāvadhāna
> rātre uṭhi' gaṇa-saṅge kaila prātaḥ-snāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:s:sāvadhāna]]---very careful; [[synonyms:r:rātre]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up at night; [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with His personal devotees; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---took; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing early in the morning.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His personal associates got up in the dark and attentively took their early morning baths.**
