====== CC Madhya 13.164 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpera indriye prabhura nijendriya-gaṇa
> āviṣṭa hañā kare gāna-āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpera]]---of Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:i:indriye]]---in the senses; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---own senses; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being fully absorbed; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---the singing; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**The senses of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were identical with the senses of Svarūpa. Therefore Caitanya Mahāprabhu used to become fully absorbed in tasting the singing of Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
