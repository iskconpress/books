====== CC Madhya 13.86 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-prabhu dui hāta prasāriyā
> prabhure dharite cāhe āśa-pāśa dhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:h:hāta]]---hands; [[synonyms:p:prasāriyā]]---stretching; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dharite]]---to catch; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:ā:āśa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---here and there; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu would stretch out His two hands and try to catch the Lord when He was running here and there.**
