====== Cc. Madhya 13.170 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura hṛdaye ānanda-sindhu uthalila
> unmāda, jhañjhā-vāta tat-kṣaṇe uṭhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the heart of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:u:uthalila]]---arose; [[synonyms:u:unmāda]]---madness; [[synonyms:j:jhañjhā]]-[[synonyms:v:vāta]]---hurricane; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---immediately; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---intensified.

===== Translation =====

**An ocean of transcendental bliss expanded in the heart of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and symptoms of madness immediately intensified like a hurricane.**
