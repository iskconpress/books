====== CC Madhya 13.68 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu kare nṛtya-raṅge
> bhāsāila saba loka premera taraṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---dancing in great pleasure; [[synonyms:b:bhāsāila]]---inundated; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:t:taraṅge]]---in waves of ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced in great jubilation and inundated all the people with waves of ecstatic love.**
