====== CC Madhya 13.52 ======

===== Text =====

> āra eka śakti prabhu karila prakāśa
> eka-kāle sāta ṭhāñi karila vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---mystic power; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifest; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---simultaneously; [[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in seven places; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---performed; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu then exhibited another mystic power by performing pastimes simultaneously in all seven groups.**
