====== CC Madhya 13.192 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ nṛtya kari' jagannātha āge āilā
> jagannātha dekhi' nṛtya karite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after performing the dance; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---appeared; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to perform; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing the dance before Lord Baladeva and Subhadrā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came before Lord Jagannātha's car. Upon seeing Lord Jagannātha, He began to dance again.**
