====== CC Madhya 13.89 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśīśvara govindādi yata bhakta-gaṇa
> hātāhāti kari' haila dvitīya āvaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Govinda; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:h:hātāhāti]]---linked hand to hand; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]---a second; [[synonyms:ā:āvaraṇa]]---covering circle.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees headed by Kāśīśvara and Govinda linked hands and formed a second circle around the Lord.**
