====== CC Madhya 13.179 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu sukhe nṛtya-raṅga dekhe ratha rākhi'
> se kautuka ye dekhila, sei tāra sākṣī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]]---amusement in dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:r:rākhi']]---stopping; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:k:kautuka]]---that amusement; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---//he//; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:s:sākṣī]]---witness.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Jagannātha and Lord Balarāma sometimes stopped the car and happily observed Lord Caitanya's dancing. Anyone who was able to see Them stop and watch the dancing bore witness to Their pastimes.**
