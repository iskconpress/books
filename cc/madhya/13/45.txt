====== CC Madhya 13.45 ======

===== Text =====

> śāntipurera ācāryera eka sampradāya
> acyutānanda nāce tathā, āra saba gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāntipurera]]---of Śāntipura; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---group; [[synonyms:a:acyutānanda]]---the son of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---the rest; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---were singing.

===== Translation =====

**There was another party that came from Śāntipura and was formed by Advaita Ācārya. Acyutānanda was the dancer, and the rest of the men were singers.**
