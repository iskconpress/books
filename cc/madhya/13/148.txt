====== CC Madhya 13.148 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā rādhikā-vāṇī, vraja-prema mane āni,
> bhāve vyākulita deha-mana
> vraja-lokera prema śuni', āpanāke 'ṛṇī' māni',
> kare kṛṣṇa tāṅre āśvāsana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]-[[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---the statement of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---the love of Vraja; [[synonyms:m:mane]] [[synonyms:ā:āni]]---remembering; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in that ecstasy; [[synonyms:v:vyākulita]]---very much perturbed; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---the body and mind; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing of the loving affairs; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāke]]---Himself; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṇī]] [[synonyms:m:māni']]---considering very indebted; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Her; [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsana]]---pacification.

===== Translation =====

**"After hearing Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's statements, Lord Kṛṣṇa's love for the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana was evoked, and His body and mind became very perturbed. After hearing of their love for Him, He immediately thought Himself to be always indebted to the residents of Vṛndāvana. Then Kṛṣṇa began to pacify Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī as follows.**
