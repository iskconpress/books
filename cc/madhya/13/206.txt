====== Cc. Madhya 13.206 ======

===== Text =====

> rathāgrete prabhu yaiche karilā nartana
> caitanyāṣṭake rūpa-gosāñi karyāche varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:a:agrete]]---in front of the car; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---performed; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:a:aṣṭake]]---in the prayer named //Caitanyāṣṭaka//; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:karyāche]]---has done; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---a vivid description.

===== Translation =====

**In his prayer known as the Caitanyāṣṭaka, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī has given a vivid description of the Lord's dancing before the car of Jagannātha.**

===== Purport =====

Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī composed three prayers, each with the title //Caitanyāṣṭaka.// The verse next quoted is from the first of the //Caitanyāṣṭaka// prayers included in the book //Stava-mālā.//
