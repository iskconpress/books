====== Cc. Madhya 13.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe pratāparudra kare āpane sevana
> suvarṇa-mārjanī lañā kare patha sammārjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at this time; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]]---golden; [[synonyms:m:mārjanī]]---broom; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---road; [[synonyms:s:sammārjana]]---cleansing.

===== Translation =====

**While the Lord was being carried from the throne to the car, King Pratāparudra personally engaged in the Lord's service by cleansing the road with a broom that had a golden handle.**
