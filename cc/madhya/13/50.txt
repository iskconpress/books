====== Cc. Madhya 13.50 ======

===== Text =====

> tri-bhuvana bhari' uṭhe kīrtanera dhvani
> anya vādyādira dhvani kichui nā śuni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvana]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling the three worlds; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---arose; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanera]] [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---vibration of saṅkīrtana; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:v:vādya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādira]]---of musical instruments; [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the sound; [[synonyms:k:kichui]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hears.

===== Translation =====

**When the saṅkīrtana resounded, it filled the three worlds. Indeed, no one could hear mundane sounds or musical instruments other than the saṅkīrtana.**
