====== CC Madhya 13.177 ======

===== Text =====

> preme nāce, gāya, loka, kare kolāhala
> prabhura nṛtya dekhi' sabe ānande vihvala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---a great noise; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone danced and chanted in ecstatic love, and a great noise resounded. Everyone was overwhelmed with transcendental bliss just to see the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
