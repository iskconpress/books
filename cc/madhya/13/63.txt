====== Cc. Madhya 13.63 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata līlā prabhu kaila kata-kṣaṇa
> āpane gāyena, nācā'na nija-bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]] [[synonyms:g:gāyena]]---personally sings; [[synonyms:n:nācā'na]]---made to dance; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---His own personal devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His pastimes for some time in this way. He personally sang and induced His personal associates to dance.**
