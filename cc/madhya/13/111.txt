====== Cc. Madhya 13.111 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata tāṇḍava-nṛtya kaila kata-kṣaṇa
> bhāva-viśeṣe prabhura praveśila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:t:tāṇḍava]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---devastating dancing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:v:viśeṣe]]---in a particular ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:praveśila]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind entered.

===== Translation =====

**After performing His devastating dance for some time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind entered into a mood of ecstatic love.**
