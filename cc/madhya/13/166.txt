====== Cc. Madhya 13.166 ======

===== Text =====

> aṅgulite kṣata habe jāni' dāmodara
> bhaye nija-kare nivāraye prabhu-kara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅgulite]]---on the finger; [[synonyms:k:kṣata]]---injury; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---will take place; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---out of fear; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kare]]---by his own hand; [[synonyms:n:nivāraye]]---checks; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kara]]---the hand of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Feeling that the Lord would injure His finger by writing in this way, Svarūpa Dāmodara checked Him with his own hand.**
