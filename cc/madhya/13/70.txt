====== CC Madhya 13.70 ======

===== Text =====

> āge śuna jagannāthera guṇḍicā-gamana
> tāra āge prabhu yaiche karilā nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going to the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before that; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**Now please hear about Lord Jagannātha's going to the Guṇḍicā temple while Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced before the Ratha car.**
