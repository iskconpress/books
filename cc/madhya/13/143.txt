====== CC Madhya 13.143 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana, govardhana, yamunā-pulina, vana,
> sei kuñje rāsādika līlā
> sei vrajera vraja-jana, mātā, pitā, bandhu-gaṇa,
> baḍa citra, kemane pāsarilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---the transcendental land known as Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]---Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]-[[synonyms:p:pulina]]---the bank of the Yamunā; [[synonyms:v:vana]]---all the forests where the pastimes of the Lord took place; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in the bushes in that forest; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādika]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vrajera]]---of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---inhabitants; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---mother; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---father; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---friends; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:c:citra]]---most wonderful; [[synonyms:k:kemane]] [[synonyms:p:pāsarilā]]---how have You forgotten.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'It is amazing that You have forgotten the land of Vṛndāvana. And how is it that You have forgotten Your father, mother and friends? How have You forgotten Govardhana Hill, the bank of the Yamunā, and the forest where You enjoyed the rāsa-līlā dance?**
