====== CC Madhya 13.93 ======

===== Text =====

> rājāra āge haricandana dekhe śrīnivāsa
> haste tāṅre sparśi' kahe,-hao eka-pāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of the King; [[synonyms:h:haricandana]]---Haricandana; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:ś:śrīnivāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:haste]]---with his hand; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:s:sparśi']]---touching; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:h:hao]]---please come; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---to one side.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura standing before the King, Haricandana touched Śrīvāsa with his hand and requested him to step aside.**
