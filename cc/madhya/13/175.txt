====== CC Madhya 13.175 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha-sevaka yata rāja-pātra-gaṇa
> yātrika loka, nīlācala-vāsī yata jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servants of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---and the government officers; [[synonyms:y:yātrika]]---pilgrim visitors; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---the residents of Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---as many people as there were.

===== Translation =====

**He sprinkled the minds of the servants of Lord Jagannātha, the government officers, the pilgrim visitors, the general populace and all the residents of Jagannātha Purī.**
