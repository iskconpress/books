====== Cc. Madhya 13.193 ======

===== Text =====

> caliyā āila ratha 'balagaṇḍi'-sthāne
> jagannātha ratha rākhi' dekhe ḍāhine vāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caliyā]]---moving; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:b:balagaṇḍi]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place known as Balagaṇḍi; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---car; [[synonyms:r:rākhi']]---after stopping; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāhine]] [[synonyms:v:vāme]]---left and right.

===== Translation =====

**When they reached the place called Balagaṇḍi, Lord Jagannātha stopped His car and began to look left and right.**
