====== Cc. Madhya 13.118 ======

===== Text =====

> gaura yadi pāche cale, śyāma haya sthire
> gaura āge cale, śyāma cale dhīre-dhīre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:p:pāche]] [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes behind; [[synonyms:ś:śyāma]]---Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:s:sthire]]---still; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āge]] [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes forward; [[synonyms:ś:śyāma]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:d:dhīre]]-[[synonyms:d:dhīre]]---slowly.

===== Translation =====

**When Caitanya Mahāprabhu was dramatically enacting the song, He would sometimes fall behind in the procession. At such times, Lord Jagannātha would come to a standstill. When Caitanya Mahāprabhu again went forward, Lord Jagannātha's car would slowly start again.**
