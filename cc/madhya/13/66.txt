====== CC Madhya 13.66 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yaiche rāsādi līlā kaila vṛndāvane
> alaukika līlā gaura kaila kṣaṇe kṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the //rāsa-līlā// and other pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---moment after moment.

===== Translation =====

**Just as Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa formerly performed the rāsa-līlā dance and other pastimes at Vṛndāvana, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed uncommon pastimes moment after moment.**
