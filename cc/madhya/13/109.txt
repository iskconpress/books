====== CC Madhya 13.109 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu netre nāsāya jala, mukhe paḍe phena
> amṛtera dhārā candra-bimbe vahe yena

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---from the eyes; [[synonyms:n:nāsāya]]---from the nostrils; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell; [[synonyms:p:phena]]---foam; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]]---of nectar; [[synonyms:d:dhārā]]---torrents; [[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:b:bimbe]]---from the moon; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---flow; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if.

===== Translation =====

**Water flowed from His eyes and sometimes through His nostrils, and foam fell from His mouth. These flowings appeared to be torrents of nectar descending from the moon.**
