====== Cc. Madhya 13.69 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata haila kṛṣṇera rathe ārohaṇa
> tāra āge prabhu nācāila bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rathe]]---on the car; [[synonyms:ā:ārohaṇa]]---getting up; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before it; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nācāila]]---caused to dance; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Lord Jagannātha mounted His car, and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inspired all His devotees to dance in front of it.**
