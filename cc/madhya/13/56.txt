====== CC Madhya 13.56 ======

===== Text =====

> pratāparudrera haila parama vismaya
> dekhite vivaśa rājā haila premamaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratāparudrera]]---of King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---very much; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:v:vivaśa]]---inactive; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**King Pratāparudra also was astonished to see the saṅkīrtana. He became inactive and was converted to ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa.**
