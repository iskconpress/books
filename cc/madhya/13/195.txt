====== Cc. Madhya 13.195 ======

===== Text =====

> āge nṛtya kare gaura lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> ratha rākhi' jagannātha karena daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:r:ratha]] [[synonyms:r:rākhi']]---after stopping the car; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---sees.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees were dancing in front of the car, and, having stopped the car, Lord Jagannātha watched the dancing.**
