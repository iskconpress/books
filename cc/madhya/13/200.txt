====== CC Madhya 13.200 ======

===== Text =====

> āge pāche, dui pārśve puṣpodyāna-vane
> yei yāhā pāya, lāgāya,-nāhika niyame

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]] [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---in front or at the end; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:p:pārśve]]---on two sides; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāna]]-[[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the flower gardens; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---one who; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets the opportunity; [[synonyms:l:lāgāya]]---offers; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:n:niyame]]---there are no hard-and-fast rules.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees offered their foods everywhere, in front and behind the car, on the two sides and within the flower garden. Wherever possible, they made their offering to the Lord, for there were no hard-and-fast rules.**
