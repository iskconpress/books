====== Cc. Madhya 13.165 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāvera āveśe kabhu bhūmite vasiyā
> tarjanīte bhūme likhe adhomukha hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāvera]] [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---because of ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting; [[synonyms:t:tarjanīte]]---with the ring finger; [[synonyms:b:bhūme]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:l:likhe]]---writes; [[synonyms:a:adhomukha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---looking down.

===== Translation =====

**In emotional ecstasy, Caitanya Mahāprabhu would sometimes sit on the ground and, looking down, would write on the ground with His finger.**
