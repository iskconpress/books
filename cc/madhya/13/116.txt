====== CC Madhya 13.116 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthe netra diyā sabe nāce, gāya
> kīrtanīyā saha prabhu pāche pāche yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]]---on Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:n:netra]]---the eyes; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---keeping; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:n:nāce]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---dance and sing; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyā]]---the performers of //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāche]] [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at the rear; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes forward.

===== Translation =====

**While dancing and singing, all the devotees in front of Lord Jagannātha kept their eyes on Him. Caitanya Mahāprabhu then went to the end of the procession with the saṅkīrtana performers.**
