====== CC Madhya 13.162 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛtya-kāle sei bhāve āviṣṭa hañā
> śloka paḍi' nāce jagannātha-mukha cāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---while dancing; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in such ecstasy; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---absorbed; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting these verses; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---the face of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:c:cāñā]]---looking upon.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced completely absorbed in ecstatic emotion. While looking at the face of Lord Jagannātha, He danced and recited these verses.**
