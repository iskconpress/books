====== Cc. Madhya 13.35 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda, advaita, haridāsa, vakreśvare
> cāri jane ājñā dila nṛtya karibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvare]]---Vakreśvara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---to these four persons; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---the Lord gave an order; [[synonyms:n:nṛtyakaribāre]]---to dance.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered Nityānanda Prabhu, Advaita Ācārya, Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Vakreśvara Paṇḍita to dance in each of the four respective parties.**
