====== CC Madhya 13.152 ======

===== Text =====

> priyā priya-saṅga-hīnā, priya priyā-saṅga vinā,
> nāhi jīye,-e satya pramāṇa
> mora daśā śone yabe, tāṅra ei daśā habe,
> ei bhaye duṅhe rākhe prāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:priyā]]---a woman beloved; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]-[[synonyms:h:hīnā]]---being separated from the man beloved; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---the man beloved; [[synonyms:p:priyā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---being separated from the woman beloved; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jīye]]---cannot live; [[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---this is factual evidence; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---situation; [[synonyms:ś:śone]] [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when one hears; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---situation; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---out of this fear; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:r:rākhe]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---keep their life.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'When a woman is separated from the man she loves or a man is separated from his beloved woman, neither of them can live. It is a fact that they live only for one another, for if one dies and the other hears of it, he or she will die also.**
