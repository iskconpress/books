====== CC Madhya 13.106 ======

===== Text =====

> deha-kānti gaura-varṇa dekhiye aruṇa
> kabhu kānti dekhi yena mallikā-puṣpa-sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---of the luster of the body; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇa]]---white complexion; [[synonyms:d:dekhiye]]---everyone saw; [[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]---pink; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:kānti]]---the luster; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:m:mallikā]]-[[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---resembling the //mallikā// flower.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone saw the complexion of His body change from white to pink, so that His luster resembled that of the mallikā flower.**
