====== CC Madhya 13.98 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura nṛtya dekhi' loke haila camatkāra
> anya āchuk, jagannāthera ānanda apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---everyone; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:ā:āchuk]]---let alone others; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---there was extreme happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was astonished by the dancing of Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and even Lord Jagannātha became extremely happy to see Him.**
