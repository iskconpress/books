====== CC Madhya 13.95 ======

===== Text =====

> cāpaḍa māriyā tāre kaila nivāraṇa
> cāpaḍa khāñā kruddha hailā haricandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāpaḍa]] [[synonyms:m:māriyā]]---slapping; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nivāraṇa]]---stopped; [[synonyms:c:cāpaḍa]] [[synonyms:k:khāñā]]---getting the slap; [[synonyms:k:kruddha]]---angry; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:h:haricandana]]---Haricandana.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura slapped Haricandana to stop him from pushing him. In turn, this made Haricandana angry.**
