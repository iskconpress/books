====== CC Madhya 13.158 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra ye prema-guṇa, kare āmā ākarṣaṇa,
> ānibe āmā dina daśa biśe
> punaḥ āsi' vṛndāvane, vraja-vadhū tomā-sane,
> vilasiba rajanī-divase

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---//do//; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---Me; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---attracting; [[synonyms:ā:ānibe]]---will bring; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---Me; [[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:d:daśa]] [[synonyms:b:biśe]]---within ten or twenty days; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhū]]---all the damsels of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with You; [[synonyms:v:vilasiba]]---I shall enjoy; [[synonyms:r:rajanī]]-[[synonyms:d:divase]]---both day and night.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Your loving qualities always attract Me to Vṛndāvana. Indeed, they will bring Me back within ten or twenty days, and when I return I shall enjoy both day and night with You and all the damsels of Vrajabhūmi.'**
