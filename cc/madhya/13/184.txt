====== CC Madhya 13.184 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi rājāra dekhi' hāḍira sevana
> prasanna hañāche tāṅre milibāre mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]]---of the King; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hāḍira]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---the service of a sweeper; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]] [[synonyms:h:hañāche]]---was satisfied; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---to see him; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---His mind.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had already been satisfied by the King's behavior, for the King had accepted the service of a sweeper for Lord Jagannātha. Therefore Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu actually desired to see the King.**
