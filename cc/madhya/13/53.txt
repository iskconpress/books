~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 13.53~~

====== CC Madhya 13.53 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe kahe,-prabhu āchena mora sampradāya
> anya ṭhāñi nāhi yā'na āmāre dayāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabekahe]]---everyone said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---is present; [[synonyms:m:morasampradāya]]---in my group; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in other places; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:y:yā'na]]---go; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]]---unto me; [[synonyms:d:dayāya]]---bestows His mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone said, "Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu is present in my group. Indeed, He does not go anywhere else. He is bestowing His mercy upon us."**
