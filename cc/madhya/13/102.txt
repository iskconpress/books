====== CC Madhya 13.102 ======

===== Text =====

> māṁsa-vraṇa sama roma-vṛnda pulakita
> śimulīra vṛkṣa yena kaṇṭaka-veṣṭita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māṁsa]]---skin; [[synonyms:v:vraṇa]]---pimples; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like; [[synonyms:r:roma]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---the hairs of the body; [[synonyms:p:pulakita]]---erupted; [[synonyms:ś:śimulīra]] [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]---cotton tree; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭaka]]---by thorns; [[synonyms:v:veṣṭita]]---surrounded.

===== Translation =====

**His skin erupted with goose pimples, and the hairs of His body stood on end. His body resembled the śimulī [silk cotton tree], all covered with thorns.**
