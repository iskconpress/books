====== CC Madhya 13.58 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma-saṅge rājā kare ṭhārāṭhāri
> āra keha nāhi jāne caitanyera curi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhārāṭhāri]]---indication; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---further; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:curi]]---tricks.

===== Translation =====

**The King and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya were both aware of the Lord's activities, but no one else could see the tricks of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
