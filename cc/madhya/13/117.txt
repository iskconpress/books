====== CC Madhya 13.117 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthe magna prabhura nayana-hṛdaya
> śrī-hasta-yuge kare gītera abhinaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]]---into Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:m:magna]]---absorbed; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nayana]]-[[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---the eyes and mind; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:hasta]]-[[synonyms:y:yuge]]---with His two arms; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:g:gītera]]---of the song; [[synonyms:a:abhinaya]]---dramatic movement.

===== Translation =====

**His eyes and mind fully absorbed in Lord Jagannātha, Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to play the drama of the song with His two arms.**
