====== CC Madhya 13.37 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara, nārāyaṇa, datta govinda
> rāghava paṇḍita, āra śrī-govindānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:d:dattagovinda]]---Govinda Datta; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:g:govindānanda]]---Śrī Govindānanda.

===== Translation =====

**The five who responded to the singing of Svarūpa Dāmodara were Dāmodara Paṇḍita, Nārāyaṇa, Govinda Datta, Rāghava Paṇḍita and Śrī Govindānanda.**
