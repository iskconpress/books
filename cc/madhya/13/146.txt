====== Cc. Madhya 13.146 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra ye anya veśa, anya saṅga, anya deśa,
> vraja-jane kabhu nāhi bhāya
> vraja-bhūmi chāḍite nāre, tomā nā dekhile mare,
> vraja-janera ki habe upāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:v:veśa]]---different dress; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---other associates; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:d:deśa]]---other countries; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---to the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---appeal; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūmi]]---the land of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:c:chāḍite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---they do not like to leave; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---You; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:d:dekhile]]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mare]]---they die; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:j:janera]]---of the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---will be; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---means.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The inhabitants of Vṛndāvana do not want You dressed like a prince, nor do they want You to associate with great warriors in a different country. They cannot leave the land of Vṛndāvana, and without Your presence, they are all dying. What is their condition to be?**
