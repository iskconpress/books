====== CC Madhya 13.51 ======

===== Text =====

> sāta ṭhāñi bule prabhu 'hari' 'hari' bali'
> 'jaya jagannātha', balena hasta-yuga tuli'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in the seven places; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---wanders; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---chanting the holy names Hari, Hari; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---all glories to Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---says; [[synonyms:h:hasta]]-[[synonyms:y:yuga]]---His two arms; [[synonyms:t:tuli']]---raising.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu wandered through all seven groups chanting the holy name, "Hari, Hari!" Raising His arms, He shouted, "All glories to Lord Jagannātha!"**
