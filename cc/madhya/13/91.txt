====== CC Madhya 13.91 ======

===== Text =====

> haricandanera skandhe hasta ālambiyā
> prabhura nṛtya dekhe rājā āviṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haricandanera]]---of Haricandana; [[synonyms:s:skandhe]]---on the shoulder; [[synonyms:h:hasta]]---hand; [[synonyms:ā:ālambiyā]]---putting; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees the dancing; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---Mahārāja Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---in great ecstasy.

===== Translation =====

**With his hands on the shoulders of Haricandana, King Pratāparudra could see Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu dancing, and the King felt great ecstasy.**
