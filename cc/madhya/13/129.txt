====== CC Madhya 13.129 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāṅ rāja-veśa, saṅge saba kṣatriya-gaṇa
> tāhāṅ gopa-veśa, saṅge muralī-vādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here, at Kurukṣetra; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---dressed like a royal prince; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with You; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kṣatriya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---great warriors; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there, in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---dressed like a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with You; [[synonyms:m:muralī]]-[[synonyms:v:vādana]]---the blowing of Your transcendental flute.

===== Translation =====

**"Here at Kurukṣetra You are dressed like a royal prince, accompanied by great warriors, but in Vṛndāvana You appeared just like an ordinary cowherd boy, accompanied only by Your beautiful flute.**
