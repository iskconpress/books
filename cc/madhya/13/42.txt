====== CC Madhya 13.42 ======

===== Text =====

> govinda-ghoṣa-pradhāna kaila āra sampradāya
> haridāsa, viṣṇudāsa, rāghava, yāhāṅ gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:g:ghoṣa]]---Govinda Ghosh; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the chief; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---formed; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---group; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---the younger Haridāsa; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇudāsa]]---Viṣṇudāsa; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sing.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord formed another group, appointing Govinda Ghosh as leader. In this group the younger Haridāsa, Viṣṇudāsa and Rāghava were the responding singers.**
