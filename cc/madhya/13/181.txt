====== Cc. Madhya 13.181 ======

===== Text =====

> sambhrame pratāparudra prabhuke dharila
> tāṅhāke dekhite prabhura bāhya-jñāna ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sambhrame]]---with great respect; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---picked up; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāke]]---Him; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Pratāparudra picked the Lord up with great respect, but upon seeing the King, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu came to His external senses.**
