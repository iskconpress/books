====== Cc. Madhya 13.124 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yaiche kurukṣetre saba gopī-gaṇa
> kṛṣṇera darśana pāñā ānandita mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]] [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as previously; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---in the holy place known as Kurukṣetra; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the //gopīs// of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dar]]-[[synonyms:ś:śana]]---interview; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---very much pleased within the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Formerly, all the gopīs of Vṛndāvana were very pleased when they met with Kṛṣṇa in the holy place Kurukṣetra.**
