====== Cc. Madhya 13.90 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhire pratāparudra lañā pātra-gaṇa
> maṇḍala hañā kare loka nivāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---his own associates; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---circle; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---of the crowd; [[synonyms:n:nivāraṇa]]---checking.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Pratāparudra and his personal assistants formed a third circle around the two inner circles just to check the crowd from coming too near.**
