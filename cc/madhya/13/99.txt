====== CC Madhya 13.99 ======

===== Text =====

> ratha sthira kaila, āge nā kare gamana
> animiṣa-netre kare nṛtya daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:s:sthira]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---stopped; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---forward; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---moving; [[synonyms:a:animiṣa]]---unblinking; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---with eyes; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---of the dancing; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing.

===== Translation =====

**The car came to a complete standstill and remained immobile while Lord Jagannātha, with unblinking eyes, watched the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
