====== CC Madhya 13.112 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṇḍava-nṛtya chāḍi' svarūpere ājñā dila
> hṛdaya jāniyā svarūpa gāite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṇḍava]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up such devastating dancing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpere]]---unto Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave an order; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---mind; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:g:gāite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to sing.

===== Translation =====

**After abandoning the dancing, the Lord ordered Svarūpa Dāmodara to sing. Understanding His mind, Svarūpa Dāmodara began to sing as follows.**
