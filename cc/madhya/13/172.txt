====== Cc. Madhya 13.172 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāvodaya, bhāva-śānti, sandhi, śābalya
> sañcārī, sāttvika, sthāyī svabhāva-prābalya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---awakening of emotion; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śānti]]---emotions of peace; [[synonyms:s:sandhi]]---the junction of different emotions; [[synonyms:ś:śābalya]]---mixing of all emotions; [[synonyms:s:sañcārī]]---impetuses for all kinds of emotion; [[synonyms:s:sāttvika]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:s:sthāyī]]---prevalent; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---natural emotion; [[synonyms:p:prābalya]]---increase.

===== Translation =====

**There was an increase in all the natural emotional symptoms. Thus there were awakening emotions, peacefulness, joined, mixed, transcendental and prevalent emotions, and impetuses for emotion.**
