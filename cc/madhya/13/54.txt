====== CC Madhya 13.54 ======

===== Text =====

> keha lakhite nāre prabhura acintya-śakti
> antaraṅga-bhakta jāne, yāṅra śuddha-bhakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:l:lakhite]]---see; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---cannot; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:acintya]]---inconceivable; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power; [[synonyms:a:antaraṅga]]---intimate; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---pure devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**Actually, no one could see the inconceivable potency of the Lord. Only the most confidential devotees, those in pure, unalloyed devotional service, could understand.**
