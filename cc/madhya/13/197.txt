====== CC Madhya 13.197 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera choṭa-baḍa yata bhakta-gaṇa
> nija nija uttama-bhoga kare samarpaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]]---neophyte; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---advanced; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]---personally cooked; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]-[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---firstclass food; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---offering.

===== Translation =====

**All kinds of devotees of Lord Jagannātha-from neophytes to the most advanced-offered their best cooked food to the Lord.**
