====== Cc. Madhya 13.201 ======

===== Text =====

> bhogera samaya lokera mahā bhiḍa haila
> nṛtya chāḍi' mahāprabhu upavane gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhogera]] [[synonyms:s:samaya]]---at the time the food was offered; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all the people; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---great; [[synonyms:b:bhiḍa]]---crowd; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up His dancing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:upavane]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went to a nearby garden.

===== Translation =====

**While the food was being offered, a large crowd of people gathered. At that time Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stopped His dancing and went to a nearby garden.**
