====== CC Madhya 13.22 ======

===== Text =====

> līlāya caḍila īśvara rathera upara
> āra dui rathe caḍe subhadrā, haladhara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---for the matter of pastimes; [[synonyms:c:caḍila]]---got up; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:r:rathera]]---a car; [[synonyms:u:upara]]---aboard; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]---another two; [[synonyms:r:rathe]]---in the cars; [[synonyms:c:caḍe]]---got up; [[synonyms:s:subhadrā]]---the sister of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:haladhara]]---Balarāma.

===== Translation =====

**For the pastimes of the Ratha-yātrā ceremony, Lord Jagannātha got aboard one car, and His sister, Subhadrā, and elder brother, Balarāma, got aboard two other cars.**
