====== CC Madhya 13.94 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛtyāveśe śrīnivāsa kichui nā jāne
> bāra bāra ṭhele, teṅho krodha haila mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---fully absorbed in seeing the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrīnivāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kichui]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---know; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhele]]---when he pushes; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]---angry; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Absorbed in watching Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dance, Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura could not understand why he was being touched and pushed. After he was pushed again and again, he became angry.**
