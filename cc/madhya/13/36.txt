====== Cc. Madhya 13.36 ======

===== Text =====

> prathama sampradāye kaila svarūpa-pradhāna
> āra pañca-jana dila tāṅra pāligāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathamasampradāye]]---in the first party; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---fixed; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---as the chief; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---five persons; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:pāligāna]]---responders.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara was chosen as the leader of the first party and was given five assistants to respond to his chanting.**
