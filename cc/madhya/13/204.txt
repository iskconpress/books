====== CC Madhya 13.204 ======

===== Text =====

> yata bhakta kīrtanīyā āsiyā ārāme
> prati-vṛkṣa-tale sabe karena viśrāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyā]]---who were performing //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:ā:ārāme]]---in the resting place; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---under each and every tree; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---take; [[synonyms:v:viśrāme]]---rest.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees who were performing saṅkīrtana came there and took rest under each and every tree.**
