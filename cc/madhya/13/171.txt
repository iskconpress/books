====== CC Madhya 13.171 ======

===== Text =====

> ānandonmāde uṭhāya bhāvera taraṅga
> nānā-bhāva-sainye upajila yuddha-raṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāde]]---the madness of transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāya]]---causes to arise; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of emotion; [[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---waves; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---emotions; [[synonyms:s:sainye]]---among soldiers; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---there appeared; [[synonyms:y:yuddha]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]]---fighting.

===== Translation =====

**The madness of transcendental bliss created waves of various emotions. The emotions appeared like opposing soldiers staging a fight.**
