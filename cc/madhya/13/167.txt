====== CC Madhya 13.167 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura bhāvānurūpa svarūpera gāna
> yabe yei rasa tāhā kare mūrtimān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:a:anurūpa]]---following the ecstatic emotions; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]]---of Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---the singing; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---mellow; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:m:mūrtimān]]---personified.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara used to sing exactly according to the ecstatic emotion of the Lord. Whenever a particular mellow was being tasted by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Svarūpa Dāmodara would personify it by singing.**
