====== CC Madhya 13.178 ======

===== Text =====

> anyera ki kāya, jagannātha-haladhara
> prabhura nṛtya dekhi' sukhe calilā manthara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anyera]] [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kāya]]---apart from the actions of others; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:haladhara]]---Balarāma; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---the dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---moved; [[synonyms:m:manthara]]---slowly.

===== Translation =====

**Apart from the others, even Lord Jagannātha and Lord Balarāma, with great happiness, began to move very slowly upon seeing the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
