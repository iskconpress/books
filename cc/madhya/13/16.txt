====== CC Madhya 13.16 ======

===== Text =====

> candana-jalete kare patha niṣecane
> tuccha sevā kare vasi' rāja-siṁhāsane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:candana]]-[[synonyms:j:jalete]]---with sandalwood water; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---road; [[synonyms:n:niṣecane]]---sprinkling; [[synonyms:t:tuccha]]---insignificant, menial; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---although in possession of; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:s:siṁhāsane]]---the royal throne.

===== Translation =====

**The King sprinkled the road with sandalwood-scented water. Although he was the owner of the royal throne, he engaged in menial service for the sake of Lord Jagannātha.**
