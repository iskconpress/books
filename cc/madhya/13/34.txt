====== CC Madhya 13.34 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu mane vicāra kariyā
> cāri sampradāya dila gāyana bāṅṭiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---considering; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---four parties; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:g:gāyana]] [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭiyā]]---dividing the singers.

===== Translation =====

**When the four parties were formed, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, after some consideration, divided the chanters.**
