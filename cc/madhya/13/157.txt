====== Cc. Madhya 13.157 ======

===== Text =====

> sei śatru-gaṇa haite, vraja-jana rākhite,
> rahi rājye udāsīna hañā
> yebā strī-putra-dhane, kari rājya āvaraṇe,
> yadu-gaṇera santoṣa lāgiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:ś:śatru]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from enemies; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---to the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:r:rākhite]]---to give protection; [[synonyms:r:rahi]]---I remain; [[synonyms:r:rājye]]---in My kingdom; [[synonyms:u:udāsīna]]---indifferent; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:y:yebā]]---whatever; [[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:d:dhane]]---with wives, sons and wealth; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:r:rājya]] [[synonyms:ā:āvaraṇe]]---I decorate My kingdom; [[synonyms:y:yadu]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the Yadu dynasty; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---for the matter of.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'I wish to protect the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana from the attacks of My enemies. That is why I remain in My kingdom; otherwise I am indifferent to My royal position. Whatever wives, sons and wealth I maintain in the kingdom are only for the satisfaction of the Yadus.**
