====== CC Madhya 13.115 ======

===== Text =====

> dhīre dhīre jagannātha karena gamana
> āge nṛtya kari' calena śacīra nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhīredhīre]]---slowly, slowly; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---movement; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:c:calena]]---goes forward; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī.

===== Translation =====

**The car of Lord Jagannātha began to move slowly while the son of mother Śacī went ahead and danced in front.**
