====== Cc. Madhya 13.17 ======

===== Text =====

> uttama hañā rājā kare tuccha sevana
> ataeva jagannāthera kṛpāra bhājana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttama]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---although very respectable; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---accepts; [[synonyms:t:tuccha]]---menial; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāra]]---in the matter of mercy; [[synonyms:b:bhājana]]---suitable candidate.

===== Translation =====

**Although the King was the most exalted respectable person, still he accepted menial service for the Lord; he, therefore, became a suitable candidate for receiving the Lord's mercy.**
