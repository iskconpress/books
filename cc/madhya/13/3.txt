====== CC Madhya 13.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya śrotā-gaṇa, śuna, kari' eka mana
> ratha-yātrāya nṛtya prabhura parama mohana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrotā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the listeners; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---keeping yourself; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---in one attention; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrāya]]---in the car festival; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---extremely; [[synonyms:m:mohana]]---enchanting.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to the listeners of Caitanya-caritāmṛta. Please hear the description of the dancing of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu at the Ratha-yātrā festival. His dancing is very enchanting. Please hear of it with great attention.**
