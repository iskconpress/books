====== CC Madhya 13.176 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura nṛtya prema dekhi' haya camatkāra
> kṛṣṇa-prema uchalila hṛdaye sabāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:uchalila]]---infatuated; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the hearts; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the dancing and ecstatic love of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, everyone became astonished. In their hearts they became infatuated with love of Kṛṣṇa.**
