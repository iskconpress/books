====== Cc. Madhya 13.44 ======

===== Text =====

> kulīna-grāmera eka kīrtanīyā-samāja
> tāhāṅ nṛtya karena rāmānanda, satyarāja

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---of the village known as Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyā]]-[[synonyms:s:samāja]]---//saṅkīrtana// party; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---dances; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:s:satyarāja]]---Satyarāja Khān.

===== Translation =====

**There was a saṅkīrtana party from the village known as Kulīna-grāma, and Rāmānanda and Satyarāja were appointed the dancers in this group.**
