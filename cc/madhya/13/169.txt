====== CC Madhya 13.169 ======

===== Text =====

> sūryera kiraṇe mukha kare jhalamala
> mālya, vastra, divya alaṅkāra, parimala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūryera]]---of the sun; [[synonyms:k:kiraṇe]]---by the rays of sunshine; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---the face; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:j:jhalamala]]---glittering; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]---garland; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---garments; [[synonyms:d:divya]] [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---beautiful ornaments; [[synonyms:p:parimala]]---surrounded by a fragrance.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Jagannātha was garlanded, dressed with nice garments and adorned with beautiful ornaments. His face was glittering from the rays of sunshine, and the entire atmosphere was fragrant.**
