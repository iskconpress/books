====== CC Madhya 13.40 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsudeva, gopīnātha, murāri yāhāṅ gāya
> mukunda-pradhāna kaila āra sampradāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sing; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---chief; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---formed; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---group.

===== Translation =====

**Another group was formed consisting of Vāsudeva, Gopīnātha, and Murāri. All these were responsive singers, and Mukunda was the chief singer.**
