====== CC Madhya 13.43 ======

===== Text =====

> mādhava, vāsudeva-ghoṣa,-dui sahodara
> nṛtya karena tāhāṅ paṇḍita-vakreśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]---Mādhava; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:g:ghoṣa]]---Vāsudeva Ghosh; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:s:sahodara]]---two brothers; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---dances; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara Paṇḍita.

===== Translation =====

**Two brothers named Mādhava Ghosh and Vāsudeva Ghosh also joined this group as responsive singers. Vakreśvara Paṇḍita was the dancer.**
