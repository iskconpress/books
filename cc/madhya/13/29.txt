====== CC Madhya 13.29 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu saba lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> svahaste parāila sabe mālya-candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---by His own hand; [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---decorated; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---with flower garlands and pulp of sandalwood.

===== Translation =====

**As the car stood still, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gathered all His devotees and, with His own hand, decorated them with flower garlands and sandalwood pulp.**
