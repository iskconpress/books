====== CC Madhya 13.190 ======

===== Text =====

> ṭhelitei calila ratha 'haḍa' 'haḍa' kari'
> catur-dike loka saba bale 'hari' 'hari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhelitei]]---as soon as He pushed; [[synonyms:c:calila]]---departed; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:h:haḍa]] [[synonyms:h:haḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making a rattling noise; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---all around; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---chant; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of the Lord, Hari Hari.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as He pushed, the car immediately started to move, making a rattling noise. The people all around began to chant the holy name of the Lord, "Hari! Hari!"**
