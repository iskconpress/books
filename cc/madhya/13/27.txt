====== CC Madhya 13.27 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gauḍa' saba ratha ṭāne kariyā ānanda
> kṣaṇe śīghra cale ratha, kṣaṇe cale manda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]---the pullers of the car; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭāne]]---pull; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---feeling; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---happiness; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]] [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes very fast; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]---the car; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:m:manda]]---very slow.

===== Translation =====

**The pullers of the car were known as gauḍas, and they pulled with great pleasure. However, the car sometimes went very fast and sometimes very slow.**
