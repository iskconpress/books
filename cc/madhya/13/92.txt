====== CC Madhya 13.92 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle śrīnivāsa premāviṣṭa-mana
> rājāra āge rahi' dekhe prabhura nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:ś:śrīnivāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---with a greatly ecstatic mind; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of the King; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---keeping himself; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---the dancing.

===== Translation =====

**While the King beheld the dancing, Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, standing in front of him, became ecstatic as he saw the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
