====== CC Madhya 13.6 ======

===== Text =====

> āpani pratāparudra lañā pātra-gaṇa
> mahāprabhura gaṇe karāya vijaya-darśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with him; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---his associates; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---associates; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---causes; [[synonyms:v:vijaya]]-[[synonyms:d:darśana]]---seeing the Pāṇḍu-vijaya ceremony.

===== Translation =====

**King Pratāparudra in person, as well as his entourage, allowed the Pāṇḍu-vijaya ceremony to be seen by all the associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
