====== CC Madhya 13.9 ======

===== Text =====

> kataka dayitā kare skandha ālambana
> kataka dayitā dhare śrī-padma-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kataka]] [[synonyms:d:dayitā]]---some of the //dayitās//; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:s:skandha]]---of the shoulders; [[synonyms:ā:ālambana]]---capturing; [[synonyms:k:kataka]]---some; [[synonyms:d:dayitā]]---servants called //dayitās//; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---catch; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**While carrying the Deity of Lord Jagannātha, some of the dayitās took hold of the shoulders of the Lord, and some caught His lotus feet.**
