====== CC Madhya 13.188 ======

===== Text =====

> avasara jāni' āmi kariba nivedana
> sei-kāle yāi' kariha prabhura milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avasara]] [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---understanding an opportune moment; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---shall do; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---you do; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya continued, "I shall submit your petition when there is an opportune moment. It will then be easy for you to come and meet the Lord."**
