====== Cc. Madhya 13.85 ======

===== Text =====

> āchāḍa khāñā paḍe bhūme gaḍi' yāya
> suvarṇa-parvata yaiche bhūmete loṭāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āchāḍa]] [[synonyms:k:khāñā]]---crashing; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls; [[synonyms:b:bhūme]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:g:gaḍi']]---rolling; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:parvata]]---a golden mountain; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as if; [[synonyms:b:bhūmete]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:l:loṭāya]]---rolls.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell down with a crash while dancing, He would roll on the ground. At such times it appeared that a golden mountain was rolling on the ground.**
