====== CC Madhya 10.26 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' ānandita haila sabākāra mana
> sabe āsi' sārvabhaume kaila nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---happy; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---were; [[synonyms:s:sabākāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---everyone coming; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---unto Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing of the Lord's return, everyone became very happy, and they all went to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and spoke to him as follows.**
