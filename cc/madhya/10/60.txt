====== Cc. Madhya 10.60 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu tāṅre kaila āliṅgana
> tāṅra putra saba śire dharila caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---his sons; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on the head; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---kept; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---His feet.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Bhavānanda Rāya. The Lord then touched the heads of his sons with His lotus feet.**
