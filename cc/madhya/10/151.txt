====== CC Madhya 10.151 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dine mukunda-datta kahe prabhura sthāne
> brahmānanda-bhāratī āilā tomāra daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---the next day; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]-[[synonyms:d:datta]]---Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place; [[synonyms:b:brahmānanda]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---has come; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see You.

===== Translation =====

**The next day Mukunda Datta informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Brahmānanda Bhāratī has come to see You."**
