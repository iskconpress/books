====== CC Madhya 10.93 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura āgamana teṅha tāhāṅi śunila
> śīghra nīlācala yāite tāṅra icchā haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhuraāgamana]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's return; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]]---there; [[synonyms:ś:śunila]]---heard; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very soon; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**While residing at the house of Śacīmātā, Paramānanda Purī heard the news of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's return to Jagannātha Purī. He therefore decided to go there as soon as possible.**
