====== CC Madhya 10.12 ======

===== Text =====

> bhavad-vidhā bhāgavatās
> tīrthī-bhūtāḥ svayaṁ vibho
> tīrthī-kurvanti tīrthāni
> svāntaḥ-sthena gadā-bhṛtā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhavat]]---your good self; [[synonyms:v:vidhāḥ]]---like; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavatāḥ]]---devotees; [[synonyms:t:tīrthī]]---as holy places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:b:bhūtāḥ]]---existing; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---themselves; [[synonyms:v:vibho]]---O almighty one; [[synonyms:t:tīrthī]]-[[synonyms:k:kurvanti]]---make into holy places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:t:tīrthāni]]---the holy places; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sthena]]---being situated in their hearts; [[synonyms:g:gadā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhṛtā]]---by the Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Saints of your caliber are themselves places of pilgrimage. Because of their purity, they are constant companions of the Lord, and therefore they can purify even the places of pilgrimage.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse, spoken by Mahārāja Yudhiṣṭhira to Vidura in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// [[books:sb:1:13:10|(1.13.10)]], is also quoted in the //Ādi-līlā// (1.63).
