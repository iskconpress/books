====== CC Madhya 10.59 ======

===== Text =====

> dina-pāṅca-sāta bhitare āsibe rāmānanda
> tāṅra saṅge pūrṇa habe āmāra ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dina]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five or seven days; [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within; [[synonyms:ā:āsibe]]---will come; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:habe]]---will be full; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya is coming within five to seven days. As soon as he arrives, My desires will be fulfilled. I take great pleasure in his company."**
