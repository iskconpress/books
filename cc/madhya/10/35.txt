====== CC Madhya 10.35 ======

===== Text =====

> sukhī hailā dekhi' prabhu vāsāra saṁsthāna
> yei vāsāya haya prabhura sarva-samādhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukhī]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very happy; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāsāra]]---of the residential quarters; [[synonyms:s:saṁsthāna]]---situation; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:v:vāsāya]]---at which place; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---fulfillment of all necessities.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very happy to see His residential quarters, in which all His necessities were taken care of.**
