====== CC Madhya 10.103 ======

===== Text =====

> 'puruṣottama ācārya' tāṅra nāma pūrvāśrame
> navadvīpe chilā teṅha prabhura caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Puruṣottama Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrame]]---in the previous //āśrama//; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---at Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:c:chilā]]---was; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the feet.

===== Translation =====

**When Svarūpa Dāmodara was residing at Navadvīpa under the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, his name was Puruṣottama Ācārya.**
