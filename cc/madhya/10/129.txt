====== Cc. Madhya 10.129 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu dila tāṅre nibhṛte vāsā-ghara
> jalādi-paricaryā lāgi' dila eka kiṅkara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:g:ghara]]---residential quarters; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---supplying water, etc.; [[synonyms:p:paricaryā]]---service; [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the purpose of; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---servant.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then gave Svarūpa Dāmodara residence in a solitary place and ordered a servant to serve him with a supply of water and other necessities.**
