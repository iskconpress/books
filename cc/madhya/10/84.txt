====== CC Madhya 10.84 ======

===== Text =====

> rāghava-paṇḍita, āra ācārya nandana
> kateka kahiba āra yata prabhura gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kateka]]---how many; [[synonyms:k:kahiba]]---shall I describe; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Rāghava Paṇḍita, the son of Advaita Ācārya and all the devotees became very much satisfied. How many can I describe?**
