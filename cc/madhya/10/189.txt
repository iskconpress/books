====== Cc. Madhya 10.189 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahila prabhura vaiṣṇava-milana
> ihā yei śune, pāya caitanya-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting with all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this narration; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described the meeting of all the Vaiṣṇavas with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Whoever hears this description ultimately attains His shelter.**
