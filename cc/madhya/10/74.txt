====== CC Madhya 10.74 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei kṛṣṇadāse gauḍe pāṭhāila
> vaiṣṇava-sabāke dite mahā-prasāda dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāse]]---Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāke]]---to all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of Jagannātha's food; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---they gave.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Kālā Kṛṣṇadāsa was sent to Bengal, and he was given sufficient quantities of Lord Jagannātha's food remnants to distribute there.**
