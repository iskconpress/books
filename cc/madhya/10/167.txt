====== CC Madhya 10.167 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāratī kahe,-sārvabhauma, madhyastha hañā
> iṅhāra sane āmāra 'nyāya' bujha' mana diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāratī]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī said; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---O Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:s:stha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming a mediator; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:n:nyāya]]---logic; [[synonyms:b:bujha']]---try to understand; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---with attention.

===== Translation =====

**Brahmānanda Bhāratī said, "My dear Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, please become the mediator in this logical argument between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and me."**
