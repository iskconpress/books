====== Cc. Madhya 10.30 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-prasāda diyā tāhāṅ mililā sevaka-gaṇa
> mahāprabhu sabākāre kaila āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of the food of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the servants of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**All the servants of Lord Jagannātha delivered remnants of the Lord's food to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. In return, Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced them all.**
