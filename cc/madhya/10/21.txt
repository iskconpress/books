====== CC Madhya 10.21 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,-aiche kāśī-miśrera bhavana
> ṭhākurera nikaṭa, haya parama nirjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King replied; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---exactly like that; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśrera]] [[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---the house of Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurera]] [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---very much; [[synonyms:n:nirjana]]---secluded.

===== Translation =====

**The King replied, "Kāśī Miśra's house is exactly what you require. It is near the temple and is very much secluded, calm and quiet."**
