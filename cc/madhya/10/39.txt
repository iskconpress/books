====== CC Madhya 10.39 ======

===== Text =====

> ei saba loka, prabhu, vaise nīlācale
> utkaṇṭhita hañāche sabe tomā milibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all these people; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---reside; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]] [[synonyms:h:hañāche]]---they have become very anxious; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---You; [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---to meet.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya said, "My dear Lord, all these people who are residents of Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī, have been very anxious to meet You.**
