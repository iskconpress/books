====== Cc. Madhya 10.24 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata puruṣottama-vāsī yata jana
> prabhuke milite sabāra utkaṇṭhita mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---the residents of Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---persons; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]]---anxious; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**Thus all the residents of Jagannātha Purī, which is also known as Puruṣottama, became anxious to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again.**
