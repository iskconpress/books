====== CC Madhya 10.146 ======

===== Text =====

> nirvicāraṁ guror ājñā
> mayā kāryā mahātmanaḥ
> śreyo hy evaṁ bhavatyāś ca
> mama caiva viśeṣataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirvicāram]]---to be obeyed without consideration; [[synonyms:g:guroḥ]]---of the spiritual master; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by Me; [[synonyms:k:kāryā]]---must be done; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---of the great soul; [[synonyms:ś:śreyaḥ]]---good fortune; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bhavatyāḥ]]---for you; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---for Me; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣataḥ]]---specifically.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The order of a great personality like a father must be executed without consideration because there is good fortune in such an order for both of us. In particular, there is good fortune for Me.' "**
