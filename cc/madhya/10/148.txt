====== CC Madhya 10.148 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura priya bhṛtya kari' sabe kare māna
> sakala vaiṣṇavera govinda kare samādhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---dear; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---servant; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---understanding; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:m:māna]]---respect; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of devotees; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---service.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone respected Govinda as the dearest servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and Govinda served all the Vaiṣṇavas and saw to their needs.**
