====== Cc. Madhya 10.92 ======

===== Text =====

> āira mandire sukhe karilā viśrāma
> āi tāṅre bhikṣā dilā kariyā sammāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āira]] [[synonyms:m:mandire]]---at the house of Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---took; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---lodging; [[synonyms:ā:āi]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave boarding; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---with great respect.

===== Translation =====

**At Navadvīpa, Paramānanda Purī took his board and lodging at the house of Śacīmātā. She provided him with everything very respectfully.**
