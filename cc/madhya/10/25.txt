====== CC Madhya 10.25 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-lokera utkaṇṭhā yabe atyanta bāḍila
> mahāprabhu dakṣiṇa haite tabahi āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all people; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---anxieties; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---very much; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from South India; [[synonyms:t:tabahi]]---at that very time; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---returned.

===== Translation =====

**When all the residents of Jagannātha Purī became extremely anxious to meet the Lord again, He returned from South India.**
