====== CC Madhya 10.16 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi rākhite tāṅre bahu yatna kailuṅ
> īśvarera svatantra icchā, rākhite nāriluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:r:rākhite]]---to keep; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---various; [[synonyms:y:yatna]]---endeavors; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I made; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:r:rākhite]]---to keep; [[synonyms:n:nāriluṅ]]---I was unable.

===== Translation =====

**"Still, I endeavored very hard to keep Him here, but because He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead and completely independent, I was not successful."**
