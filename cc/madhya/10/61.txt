====== CC Madhya 10.61 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu tāṅre ghare pāṭhāila
> vāṇīnātha-paṭṭanāyake nikaṭe rākhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him (Bhavānanda Rāya); [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his home; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent back; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]-[[synonyms:p:paṭṭanāyake]]---Vāṇīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then sent Bhavānanda Rāya back to his home, and He kept only Vāṇīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka in His personal service.**
