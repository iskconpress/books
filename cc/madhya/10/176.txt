====== CC Madhya 10.176 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nāma sphure mukhe, mane netre kṛṣṇa
> tomāke tad-rūpa dekhi' hṛdaya-satṛṣṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sphure]]---is manifest; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---before the eyes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the presence of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tomāke]]---You; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---His form; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---I see; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---my heart; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇa]]---very eager.

===== Translation =====

**Brahmānanda Bhāratī continued, "Since I have seen You, I have been feeling Lord Kṛṣṇa's presence in my mind and have been seeing Him before my eyes. I now want to chant the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Over and above this, within my heart I consider You to be Kṛṣṇa, and I am therefore very eager to serve You.**
