====== CC Madhya 10.38 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sārvabhauma prabhura dakṣiṇa-pārśve vasi'
> milāite lāgilā saba puruṣottama-vāsī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pārśve]]---by the right side; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:m:milāite]]---to introduce; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---residents of Puruṣottama (Jagannātha Purī).

===== Translation =====

**After this, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, sitting at the right hand of the Lord, began to introduce all the inhabitants of Puruṣottama, Jagannātha Purī.**
