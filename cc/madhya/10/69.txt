====== CC Madhya 10.69 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-śrīvāsādi yata bhakta-gaṇa
> sabei āsibe śuni' prabhura āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and all the devotees like Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabei]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āsibe]]---will come; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---arrival.

===== Translation =====

**"After hearing news of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's arrival, devotees like Advaita and Śrīvāsa will certainly come to see Him.**
