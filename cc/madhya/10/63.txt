====== CC Madhya 10.63 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-bhaṭṭācārya, śunaha iṅhāra carita
> dakṣiṇa giyāchila iṅha āmāra sahita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---My dear Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ś:śunaha]]---just hear; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:c:carita]]---his character; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:g:giyāchila]]---went to South India; [[synonyms:i:iṅha]]---this man; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with Me.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Bhaṭṭācārya, just consider this man's character. He went with Me to South India.**
