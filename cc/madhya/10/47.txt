====== CC Madhya 10.47 ======

===== Text =====

> e-saba vaiṣṇava-ei kṣetrera bhūṣaṇa
> ekānta-bhāve cinte sabe tomāra caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---all these pure devotees; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:k:kṣetrera]]---of this holy place; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣaṇa]]---ornaments; [[synonyms:e:ekānta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---without deviation; [[synonyms:c:cinte]]---meditate; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---on Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"All these pure devotees serve as ornaments to Jagannātha Purī. They are always undeviatingly meditating upon Your lotus feet."**
