====== Cc. Madhya 10.90 ======

===== Text =====

> mukunda, narahari, raghunandana khaṇḍa haite
> ācāryera ṭhāñi āilā nīlācala yāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:n:narahari]]---Narahari; [[synonyms:r:raghunandana]]---Raghunandana; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the place known as Khaṇḍa; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Nīlācala (Jagannātha Purī).

===== Translation =====

**Mukunda, Narahari, Raghunandana and all the others came from Khaṇḍa to Advaita Ācārya's home to accompany Him to Jagannātha Purī.**
