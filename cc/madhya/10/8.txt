====== CC Madhya 10.8 ======

===== Text =====

> virakta sannyāsī teṅho rahena nirjane
> svapneha nā karena teṅho rāja-daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:virakta]]---detached; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---in the renounced order; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:r:rahena]]---keeps Himself; [[synonyms:n:nirjane]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:s:svapneha]]---even in dreams; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---do; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---interview with a king.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is in the renounced order and is very much detached from worldly affairs. He stays in solitary places, and even in dreams He does not grant interviews to a king.**
