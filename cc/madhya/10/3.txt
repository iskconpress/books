====== CC Madhya 10.3 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yabe mahāprabhu calilā dakṣiṇe
> pratāparudra rājā tabe bolāila sārvabhaume

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇe]]---for His South Indian tour; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---called for; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed for South India, King Pratāparudra called Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya to his palace.**
