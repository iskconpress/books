====== CC Madhya 10.183 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' bhāratīre lañā nija-vāsā āilā
> bhāratī-gosāñi prabhura nikaṭe rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:b:bhāratīre]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with Him; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsā]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned to His own residence; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---in the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took Brahmānanda Bhāratī with Him to His residence. From that time on, Brahmānanda Bhāratī remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
