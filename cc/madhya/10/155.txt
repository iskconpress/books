====== CC Madhya 10.155 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhiyā ta' chadma kaila yena dekhe nāñi
> mukundere puche,-kāhāṅ bhāratī-gosāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:c:chadma]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---pretended; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:n:nāñi]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mukundere]] [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquired from Mukunda; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī, my spiritual master.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Brahmānanda Bhāratī wearing the deerskin, Caitanya Mahāprabhu pretended not to see him. Instead, He asked Mukunda Datta, "Where is Brahmānanda Bhāratī, My spiritual master?"**
