====== CC Madhya 10.89 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura samācāra śuni' kulīna-grāma-vāsī
> satyarāja-rāmānanda mililā sabe āsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---news; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:s:satyarāja]]---Satyarāja; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**The inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma-Satyarāja, Rāmānanda and all the other devotees there-came and joined Advaita Ācārya.**
