====== Cc. Madhya 10.126 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe svarūpa kaila nitāira caraṇa vandana
> nityānanda-prabhu kaila prema-āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nitāira]]---of Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worship; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing in love.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara then worshiped the lotus feet of Nityānanda Prabhu, and Nityānanda in turn embraced him in the ecstasy of love.**
