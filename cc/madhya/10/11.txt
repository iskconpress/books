====== CC Madhya 10.11 ======

===== Text =====

> tīrtha pavitra karite kare tīrtha-bhramaṇa
> sei chale nistāraye sāṁsārika jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---holy places; [[synonyms:p:pavitra]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to purify; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhramaṇa]]---touring in places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:c:chale]]---on that plea; [[synonyms:n:nistāraye]]---delivers; [[synonyms:s:sāṁsārika]]---conditioned; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---souls.

===== Translation =====

**"Great saints go to holy places of pilgrimage in order to purify them. For that reason Caitanya Mahāprabhu is visiting many tīrthas and delivering many, many conditioned souls.**
