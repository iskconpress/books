====== CC Madhya 10.109 ======

===== Text =====

> guru-ṭhāñi ājñā māgi' āilā nīlācale
> rātri-dine kṛṣṇa-prema-ānanda-vihvale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from his spiritual master; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---asking permission; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---by ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vihvale]]---overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**After taking permission from his sannyāsa-guru, Svarūpa Dāmodara went to Nīlācala and accepted the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Then all day and night, in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa, he enjoyed transcendental mellows in the loving service of the Lord.**
