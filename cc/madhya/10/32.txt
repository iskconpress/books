====== Cc. Madhya 10.32 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśī-miśra āsi' paḍila prabhura caraṇe
> gṛha-sahita ātmā tāṅre kaila nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell down; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with his house; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---his personal self; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived at his house, Kāśī Miśra immediately fell down at His lotus feet and surrendered himself and all his possessions.**
