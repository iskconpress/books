====== CC Madhya 10.20 ======

===== Text =====

> ṭhākurera nikaṭa, āra ha-ibe nirjane
> e-mata nirṇaya kari' deha' eka sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurera]] [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near the place of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---must be; [[synonyms:n:nirjane]]---secluded; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nirṇaya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---considering carefully; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---please give; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---one place.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Caitanya's residence should be very much secluded and also near the temple of Jagannātha. Please consider this proposal and give me a nice place for Him."**
