====== CC Madhya 10.5 ======

===== Text =====

> śunilāṅa tomāra ghare eka mahāśaya
> gauḍa ha-ite āilā, teṅho mahā-kṛpāmaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śunilāṅa]]---I have heard; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---great personality; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from Bengal; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---has come; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---very merciful.

===== Translation =====

**The King said to the Bhaṭṭācārya, "I have heard that a great personality has come from Bengal and is staying at your home. I have also heard that He is very, very merciful.**
