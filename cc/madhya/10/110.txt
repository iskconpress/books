====== CC Madhya 10.110 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṇḍityera avadhi, vākya nāhi kāro sane
> nirjane rahaye, loka saba nāhi jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṇḍityera]] [[synonyms:a:avadhi]]---the limit of learned scholarship; [[synonyms:v:vākya]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---no word; [[synonyms:k:kāro]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with anyone; [[synonyms:n:nirjane]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:r:rahaye]]---stays; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---do not know.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara was the limit of all learned scholarship, but he did not exchange words with anyone. He simply remained in a solitary place, and no one could understand where he was.**
