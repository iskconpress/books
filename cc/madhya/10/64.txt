====== CC Madhya 10.64 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭathāri-kāche gelā āmāre chāḍiyā
> bhaṭṭathāri haite iṅhāre āniluṅ uddhāriyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭathāri]]-[[synonyms:k:kāche]]---in the association of the Bhaṭṭathāris; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---he went; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up My company; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭathāri]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the Bhaṭṭathāris; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āniluṅ]]---I brought; [[synonyms:u:uddhāriyā]]---after rescuing.

===== Translation =====

**"He left My company to associate with the Bhaṭṭathāris, but I rescued him from their company and brought him here.**
