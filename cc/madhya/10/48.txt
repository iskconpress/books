====== Cc. Madhya 10.48 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sabe bhūme paḍe daṇḍavat hañā
> sabā āliṅgilā prabhu prasāda kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:bhūme]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell down; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---flat like rods; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgilā]]---embraced; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---being very merciful.

===== Translation =====

**After this introduction, everyone fell to the ground like rods. Being very merciful upon them all, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced each one of them.**
