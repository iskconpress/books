====== CC Madhya 10.76 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-prasāda diyā tāṅre kaila namaskāra
> dakṣiṇa haite āilā prabhu,-kahe samācāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering the //mahā-prasāda//; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---he offered respects by bowing down; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the South India tour; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]] [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---he delivered this news.

===== Translation =====

**Upon reaching mother Śacī, Kālā Kṛṣṇadāsa first offered his obeisances and delivered the food remnants [mahā-prasāda]. He then informed her of the good news that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had returned from His South Indian tour.**
