====== Cc. Madhya 10.161 ======

===== Text =====

> carmāmbara chāḍi' brahmānanda parila vasana
> prabhu āsi' kaila tāṅra caraṇa vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:carma]]-[[synonyms:a:ambara]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up the deerskin garment; [[synonyms:b:brahmānanda]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:p:parila]]---put on; [[synonyms:v:vasana]]---cloth garment; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the feet.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Brahmānanda Bhāratī gave up his deerskin and covered himself with sannyāsī robes, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came and offered His respects at his lotus feet.**
