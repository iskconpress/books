====== Cc. Madhya 10.33 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu catur-bhuja-mūrti tāṅre dekhāila
> ātmasāt kari' tāre āliṅgana kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:catur]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuja]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---four-armed form; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed; [[synonyms:ā:ātmasāt]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then showed Kāśī Miśra His four-armed form. Then, accepting him for His service, the Lord embraced him.**
