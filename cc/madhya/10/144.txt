====== CC Madhya 10.144 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭa kahe,-gurura ājñā haya balavān
> guru-ājñā nā laṅghiye, śāstra-pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:g:gurura]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order of the spiritual master; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:b:balavān]]---strong; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order of the spiritual master; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:l:laṅghiye]]---we can disobey; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---scriptural; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---injunction.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "The order of the spiritual master is very strong and cannot be disobeyed. That is the injunction of the śāstras, the revealed scriptures.**
