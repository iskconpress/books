====== CC Madhya 10.106 ======

===== Text =====

> parama virakta teṅha parama paṇḍita
> kāya-mane āśriyāche śrī-kṛṣṇa-carita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]]---very; [[synonyms:v:virakta]]---renounced; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---great; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned scholar; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---with body and mind; [[synonyms:ā:āśriyāche]]---took shelter of; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:carita]]---the Personality of Godhead Śrī Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara was a great renunciant as well as a great learned scholar. With heart and soul he took shelter of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Kṛṣṇa.**
