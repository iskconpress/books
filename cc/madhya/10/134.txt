====== Cc. Madhya 10.134 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśīśvara āsibena saba tīrtha dekhiyā
> prabhu-ājñāya muñi āinu tomā-pade dhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:ā:āsibena]]---will come; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---holy places; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---visiting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---under the order of my spiritual master; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:ā:āinu]]---have come; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---to Your; [[synonyms:p:pade]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running.

===== Translation =====

**"Kāśīśvara will also come here after visiting all the holy places. However, following the orders of my spiritual master, I have hastily come to be present at Your lotus feet."**
