====== CC Madhya 10.94 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura eka bhakta-'dvija kamalākānta' nāma
> tāṅre lañā nīlācale karilā prayāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---one devotee; [[synonyms:d:dvija]] [[synonyms:k:kamalākānta]]---Dvija Kamalākānta; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accepting as his companion; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prayāṇa]]---departure.

===== Translation =====

**There was a devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's named Dvija Kamalākānta, whom Paramānanda Purī took with him to Jagannātha Purī.**
