====== CC Madhya 10.141 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' govindere kaila āliṅgana
> govinda karila prabhura caraṇa vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:g:govindere]]---unto Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Govinda, and Govinda in turn offered his respectful obeisances unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's lotus feet.**
