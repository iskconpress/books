====== CC Madhya 10.179 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-kṛṣṇe tomāra gāḍha premā haya
> yāhāṅ netra paḍe, tāhāṅ śrī-kṛṣṇa sphuraya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:p:premā]]---love; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:n:netra]]---eyes; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fall; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sphuraya]]---becomes manifest.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "You have a deep ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; therefore wherever you turn your eyes, you simply heighten your Kṛṣṇa consciousness."**
