====== Cc. Madhya 10.22 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' rājā rahe utkaṇṭhita hañā
> bhaṭṭācārya kāśī-miśre kahila āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]]---very anxious; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśre]]---unto Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, the King became very anxious for the Lord to return. Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then went to Kāśī Miśra to convey the King's desire.**
