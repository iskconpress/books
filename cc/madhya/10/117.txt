====== CC Madhya 10.117 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-nityānandera parama priyatama
> śrīvāsādi bhakta-gaṇera haya prāṇa-sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nityānandera]]---of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---very; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---dear; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---exactly like the life and soul.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara was very dear to Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu, and he was the life and soul of all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura.**
