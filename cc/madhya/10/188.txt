====== CC Madhya 10.188 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe āsi' mililā prabhura śrī-caraṇe
> prabhu kṛpā kari' sabāya rākhila nija sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---under the shelter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing mercy; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---every one of them; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---under His protection.

===== Translation =====

**Since all the devotees came to Him for shelter, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed them all mercy and kept them under His protection.**
