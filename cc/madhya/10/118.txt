====== CC Madhya 10.118 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dāmodara āsi' daṇḍavat hailā
> caraṇe paḍiyā śloka paḍite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---that Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---fell flat to offer obeisances; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---falling down at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---a verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to recite.

===== Translation =====

**When Svarūpa Dāmodara came to Jagannātha Purī, he fell flat before the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, offering Him obeisances and reciting a verse.**
