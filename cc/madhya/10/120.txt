====== CC Madhya 10.120 ======

===== Text =====

> uṭhāñā mahāprabhu kaila āliṅgana
> dui-jane premāveśe haila acetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---after raising him; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---two persons; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstasy of love; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:a:acetana]]---unconscious.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu raised Svarūpa Dāmodara to his feet and embraced him. They both became ecstatic in love and fell unconscious.**
