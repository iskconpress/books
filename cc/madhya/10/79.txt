====== Cc. Madhya 10.79 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryere prasāda diyā kari' namaskāra
> samyak kahila mahāprabhura samācāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryere]]---unto Śrī Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of Jagannātha's food; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:s:samyak]]---completely; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---informed; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---news.

===== Translation =====

**After paying Him respectful obeisances, Kṛṣṇadāsa offered mahā-prasāda to Advaita Ācārya. He then informed Him of the news of Lord Caitanya in complete detail.**
