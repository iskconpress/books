====== CC Madhya 10.107 ======

===== Text =====

> 'niścinte kṛṣṇa bhajiba' ei ta' kāraṇe
> unmāde karila teṅha sannyāsa grahaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niścinte]]---without disturbance; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhajiba]]---I shall worship; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---for this; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇe]]---reason; [[synonyms:u:unmāde]]---ecstatic; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:g:grahaṇe]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**He was very enthusiastic to worship Śrī Kṛṣṇa without disturbance, and therefore, almost in madness, he accepted the sannyāsa order.**
