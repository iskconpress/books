====== CC Madhya 10.52 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-hena ratna yāṅhāra tanaya
> tāṅhāra mahimā loke kahana nā yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:h:hena]]---like Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]---jewel; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:t:tanaya]]---son; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glorification; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---within this world; [[synonyms:k:kahana]]---to describe; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is possible.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu honored Bhavānanda Rāya by saying, "The glories of a person who has a jewel of a son like Rāmānanda Rāya cannot be described within this mortal world.**
