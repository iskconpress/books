====== CC Madhya 10.111 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-rasa-tattva-vettā, deha-prema-rūpa
> sākṣāt mahāprabhura dvitīya svarūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of transcendental mellows in relationship with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---of the truth; [[synonyms:v:vettā]]---cognizant; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---body; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---personified //prema//; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]---second; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---representation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara was the personification of ecstatic love, fully cognizant of the transcendental mellows in relationship with Kṛṣṇa. He directly represented Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as His second expansion.**
