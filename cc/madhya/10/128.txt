====== CC Madhya 10.128 ======

===== Text =====

> paramānanda purīra kaila caraṇa vandana
> purī-gosāñi tāṅre kaila prema-āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paramānanda]] [[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---he did; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing in love.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara also offered his worshipful prayers at the lotus feet of Paramānanda Purī, who, in return, embraced him in ecstatic love.**
