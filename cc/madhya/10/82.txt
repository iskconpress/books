====== CC Madhya 10.82 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryaratna, āra paṇḍita vakreśvara
> ācāryanidhi, āra paṇḍita gadādhara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryaratna]]---Ācāryaratna; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryanidhi]]---Ācāryanidhi; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita.

===== Translation =====

**Ācāryaratna, Vakreśvara Paṇḍita, Ācāryanidhi and Gadādhara Paṇḍita were all very much pleased to hear this news.**
