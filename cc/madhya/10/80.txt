====== CC Madhya 10.80 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' ācārya-gosāñira ānanda ha-ila
> premāveśe huṅkāra bahu nṛtya-gīta kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of the spiritual master; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was much jubilation; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---rumbling sound; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---various; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed.

===== Translation =====

**When Advaita Ācārya Gosvāmī heard of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's return, He became very much pleased. In His great ecstasy of love, He made a rumbling sound and danced and chanted for a long time.**
