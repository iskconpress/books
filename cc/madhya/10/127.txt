====== CC Madhya 10.127 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda, mukunda, śaṅkara, sārvabhauma
> sabā-saṅge yathā-yogya karila milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with all; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---as is befitting; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**After worshiping Nityānanda Prabhu, Svarūpa Dāmodara met Jagadānanda, Mukunda, Śaṅkara and Sārvabhauma, as was befitting.**
