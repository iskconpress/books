====== CC Madhya 10.91 ======

===== Text =====

> se-kāle dakṣiṇa haite paramānanda-purī
> gaṅgā-tīre-tīre āilā nadīyā nagarī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:se]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the South; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---along the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nadīyā]] [[synonyms:n:nagarī]]---to the town of Nadia.

===== Translation =====

**At that time Paramānanda Purī came from South India. Traveling along the banks of the Ganges, he ultimately reached the town of Nadia.**
