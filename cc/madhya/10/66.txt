====== CC Madhya 10.66 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' kṛṣṇadāsa kāndite lāgila
> madhyāhna karite mahāprabhu cali' gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---Kālā Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:k:kāndite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to cry; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]]---noon lunch; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to execute; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cali']] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---left.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing the Lord reject him, Kālā Kṛṣṇadāsa began to cry. However, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, not caring for him, immediately left to take His noon lunch.**
