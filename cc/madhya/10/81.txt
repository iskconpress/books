====== CC Madhya 10.81 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa ṭhākurera haila parama ānanda
> vāsudeva datta, gupta murāri, sena śivānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---topmost; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]] [[synonyms:d:datta]]---Vāsudeva Datta; [[synonyms:g:gupta]] [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:s:sena]] [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena.

===== Translation =====

**Also hearing this auspicious news, Haridāsa Ṭhākura became very much pleased. So also did Vāsudeva Datta, Murāri Gupta and Śivānanda Sena.**
