====== CC Madhya 10.95 ======

===== Text =====

> satvare āsiyā teṅha mililā prabhure
> prabhura ānanda haila pāñā tāṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:satvare]]---very soon; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---happiness; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---him.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī very soon arrived at Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's place. The Lord was very happy to see him.**
