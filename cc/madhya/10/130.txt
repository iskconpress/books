====== CC Madhya 10.130 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina sārvabhauma-ādi bhakta-saṅge
> vasiyā āchena mahāprabhu kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]] [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---was sitting; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---engaged in discussions of topics concerning Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**The next day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat with all the devotees, headed by Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, and they discussed the pastimes of Kṛṣṇa.**
