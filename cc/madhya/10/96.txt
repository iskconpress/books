====== CC Madhya 10.96 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe kaila tāṅra caraṇa vandana
> teṅha premāveśe kaila prabhure āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the feet; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**In the great ecstasy of love, the Lord worshiped the lotus feet of Paramānanda Purī, and in turn Paramānanda Purī embraced the Lord in great ecstasy.**
