====== CC Madhya 10.45 ======

===== Text =====

> candaneśvara, siṁheśvara, murāri brāhmaṇa
> viṣṇudāsa,-iṅha dhyāye tomāra caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:candaneśvara]]---Candaneśvara; [[synonyms:s:siṁheśvara]]---Siṁheśvara; [[synonyms:m:murāri]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---the //brāhmaṇa// named Murāri; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇudāsa]]---Viṣṇudāsa; [[synonyms:i:iṅha]]---all of them; [[synonyms:d:dhyāye]]---meditate; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---on the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"Here are Candaneśvara, Siṁheśvara, Murāri Brāhmaṇa and Viṣṇudāsa. They are all constantly engaged in meditating on Your lotus feet.**
