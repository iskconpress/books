====== Cc. Madhya 10.72 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra dakṣina-gamana śuni' śacī 'āi'
> advaitādi bhakta saba āche duḥkha pāi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---South Indian tour; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]] [[synonyms:ā:āi]]---mother Śacī; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Śrī Advaita Prabhu and others; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---remain; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāi']]---in great unhappiness.

===== Translation =====

**"Mother Śacī and all the devotees headed by Advaita Prabhu are all very unhappy due to not receiving news about Your return from Your South Indian tour.**
