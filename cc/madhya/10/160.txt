====== Cc. Madhya 10.160 ======

===== Text =====

> āji haite nā pariba ei carmāmbara
> prabhu bahirvāsa ānāilā jāniyā antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from today; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pariba]]---I shall not put on; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:c:carma]]-[[synonyms:a:ambara]]---deerskin garment; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bahirḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---the cloth of a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:ā:ānāilā]]---had someone bring; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---his contemplation.

===== Translation =====

**"From today on I shall not wear this deerskin." As soon as Brahmānanda Bhāratī decided this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, understanding his mind, immediately sent for the robes of a sannyāsī.**
