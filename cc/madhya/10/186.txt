====== Cc. Madhya 10.186 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhuke lañā karā'na īśvara daraśana
> āge loka-bhiḍa saba kari' nivāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:karā'na]]---helps in; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhiḍa]]---crowds of people; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:n:nivāraṇa]]---restraining.

===== Translation =====

**Kāśīśvara used to usher Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu into the Jagannātha temple. He would precede the Lord into the crowd and keep the people from touching Him.**
