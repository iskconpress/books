====== Cc. Madhya 10.62 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya saba loke vidāya karāila
> tabe prabhu kālā-kṛṣṇadāse bolāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loke]]---all persons; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---asked to leave; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kālā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāse]]---Kālā Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---called for.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then asked all the people to leave. Afterward, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu called for Kālā Kṛṣṇadāsa, who had accompanied the Lord during His South Indian tour.**
