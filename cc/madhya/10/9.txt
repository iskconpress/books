====== Cc. Madhya 10.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi prakāre tomā karāitāma daraśana
> samprati karilā teṅho dakṣiṇa gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---yet; [[synonyms:p:prakāre]]---somehow or other; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---you; [[synonyms:k:karāitāma]]---I would have arranged; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---interview; [[synonyms:s:samprati]]---recently; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---has done; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---to the southern part of India; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure.

===== Translation =====

**"Still, I would have tried to arrange your interview, but He has recently left to tour South India."**
