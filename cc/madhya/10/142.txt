====== CC Madhya 10.142 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-bhaṭṭācārya, karaha vicāra
> gurura kiṅkara haya mānya se āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---My dear Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---just consider; [[synonyms:g:gurura]] [[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---the servant of the spiritual master; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:m:mānya]]---respectable; [[synonyms:s:se]]---he; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---to Me.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then continued speaking to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya: "Consider this point. The servant of the spiritual master is always respectable for Me.**
