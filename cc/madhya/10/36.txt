====== CC Madhya 10.36 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,-prabhu, yogya tomāra vāsā
> tumi aṅgīkāra kara,-kāśī-miśrera āśā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my dear Lord; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---just befitting; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---residential quarters; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---accept; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśrera]] [[synonyms:ā:āśā]]---the hope of Kāśī Miśra.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "This place is just befitting You. Please accept it. It is the hope of Kāśī Miśra that You do."**
