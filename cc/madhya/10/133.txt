====== CC Madhya 10.133 ======

===== Text =====

> siddha-prāpti-kāle gosāñi ājñā kaila more
> kṛṣṇa-caitanya-nikaṭe rahi seviha tāṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siddhi]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time of his departure from this mortal world to achieve the highest perfection of life; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---my spiritual master; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---at the place of Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:r:rahi]]---remaining; [[synonyms:s:seviha]]---render service; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---unto Him.

===== Translation =====

**"Just before his departure from this mortal world to attain the highest perfection, Īśvara Purī told me that I should go to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and render service unto Him.**
