====== CC Madhya 10.174 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta ṭhāñi hāra' tumi,-e tomāra svabhāva
> āra eka śuna tumi āpana prabhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in the presence of a devotee; [[synonyms:h:hāra']]---become defeated; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---nature; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]] [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---Your own influence.

===== Translation =====

**"It is Your natural characteristic to accept defeat at the hands of Your devotee. There is also another glory of Yours, which I ask You to hear attentively.**
