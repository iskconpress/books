====== Cc. Madhya 10.115 ======

===== Text =====

> vidyāpati, caṇḍīdāsa, śrī-gīta-govinda
> ei tina gīte karā'na prabhura ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidyāpati]]---an old Vaiṣṇava poet from the province of Mithilā; [[synonyms:c:caṇḍīdāsa]]---a Bengali Vaiṣṇava poet born in the village of Nānnura, in the Birbhum district; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:g:govinda]]---a celebrated poem by Jayadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:g:gīte]]---songs; [[synonyms:k:karā'na]]---cause; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara used to read the poems of Vidyāpati and Caṇḍīdāsa and Jayadeva Gosvāmī's Śrī Gīta-govinda. He used to make Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu very happy by singing these songs.**
