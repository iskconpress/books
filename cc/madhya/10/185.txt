====== CC Madhya 10.185 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśīśvara gosāñi āilā āra dine
> sammāna kariyā prabhu rākhilā nija sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Kāśīśvara Gosāñi, another devotee; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---the next day; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---giving all respect; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at His own place.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, Kāśīśvara Gosāñi also came and remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who received him with great respect.**
