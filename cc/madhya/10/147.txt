====== CC Madhya 10.147 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu tāṅre kaila aṅgīkāra
> āpana-śrī-aṅga-sevāya dila adhikāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after that; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---acceptance; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]---personal; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---of the transcendental body; [[synonyms:s:sevāya]]---in the service; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:a:adhikāra]]---responsibility.

===== Translation =====

**After Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Govinda and engaged him in the service of His personal body.**
