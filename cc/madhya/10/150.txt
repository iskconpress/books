====== Cc. Madhya 10.150 ======

===== Text =====

> govindera saṅge kare prabhura sevana
> govindera bhāgya-sīmā nā yāya varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govindera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:g:govindera]]---of Govinda; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limit of good fortune; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---can be described.

===== Translation =====

**They all remained with Govinda to serve Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; therefore no one could estimate the good fortune of Govinda.**
