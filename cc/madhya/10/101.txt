====== Cc. Madhya 10.101 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśī-miśrera āvāse nibhṛte eka ghara
> prabhu tāṅre dila, āra sevāra kiṅkara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśrera]]---of Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āvāse]]---at the house; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---solitary; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---room; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:s:sevāra]]---to serve him; [[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---one servant.

===== Translation =====

**There was a solitary room at Kāśī Miśra's house, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave it to Paramānanda Purī. He also gave him one servant.**
