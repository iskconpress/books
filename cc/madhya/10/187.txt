====== CC Madhya 10.187 ======

===== Text =====

> yata nada nadī yaiche samudre milaya
> aiche mahāprabhura bhakta yāhāṅ tāhāṅ haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nada]] [[synonyms:n:nadī]]---rivers; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:s:samudre]]---in the sea; [[synonyms:m:milaya]]---meet; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---they were.

===== Translation =====

**As all the rivers flow into the sea, all the devotees throughout the country finally came to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's shelter.**
