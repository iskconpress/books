====== CC Madhya 10.28 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya kahe,-kāli kāśī-miśrera ghare
> prabhu yāibena, tāhāṅ milāba sabāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Bhaṭṭācārya replied; [[synonyms:k:kāli]]---tomorrow; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśrera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house of Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yāibena]]---will go; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:m:milāba]] [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---I shall arrange for a meeting with all of you.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya replied to the people, "Tomorrow the Lord will be at the house of Kāśī Miśra. I shall arrange for you all to meet Him."**
