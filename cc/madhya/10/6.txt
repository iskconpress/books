====== Cc. Madhya 10.6 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāre bahu kṛpā kailā, kahe sarva-jana
> kṛpā kari' karāha more tāṅhāra darśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---unto you; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---great mercy; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---showed; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---everyone; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:k:karāha]]---arrange; [[synonyms:m:more]]---for me; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---interview.

===== Translation =====

**"I have also heard that this great personality has shown you great favor. At any rate, this is what I hear from many different people. Now, being merciful upon me, you should do me the favor of arranging an interview."**
