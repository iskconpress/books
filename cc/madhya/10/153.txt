====== CC Madhya 10.153 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' mahāprabhu bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> cali' āilā brahmānanda-bhāratīra āge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---walking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:b:brahmānanda]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāratīra]]---of Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in the presence.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees came into the presence of Brahmānanda Bhāratī.**
