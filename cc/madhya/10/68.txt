====== CC Madhya 10.68 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍa-deśe pāṭhāite cāhi eka-jana
> 'āi'ke kahibe yāi, prabhura āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāite]]---to send; [[synonyms:c:cāhi]]---we want; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---one person; [[synonyms:ā:āike]]---mother Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:k:kahibe]]---will inform; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---going; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---arrival.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord's four devotees considered, "We want a person to go to Bengal just to inform Śacīmātā about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's arrival at Jagannātha Purī.**
