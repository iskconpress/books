====== CC Madhya 10.78 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā sabāra haila parama ullāsa
> advaita-ācārya-gṛhe gelā kṛṣṇadāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---supreme; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---happiness; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of Advaita Ācārya Prabhu; [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to the home; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---Kṛṣṇadāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing of Lord Caitanya's return to Purī, everyone became very glad. Kṛṣṇadāsa next went to the house of Advaita Ācārya.**
