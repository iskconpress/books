====== CC Madhya 10.34 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu tāhāṅ vasilā āsane
> caudike vasilā nityānandādi bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:ā:āsane]]---on His seat; [[synonyms:c:cau]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---on four sides; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu next sat down at the place prepared for Him, and all the devotees, headed by Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, surrounded Him.**
