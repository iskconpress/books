====== CC Madhya 10.40 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛṣita cātaka yaiche kare hāhākāra
> taiche ei saba,-sabe kara aṅgīkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛṣita]]---thirsty; [[synonyms:c:cātaka]]---the //cātaka// bird; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:h:hāhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---vibration of disappointment; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of these; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---kindly accept.

===== Translation =====

**"In Your absence all these people have been exactly like thirsty cātaka birds crying in disappointment. Kindly accept them."**
