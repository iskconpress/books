====== CC Madhya 10.50 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,-ei rāya bhavānanda
> iṅhāra prathama putra-rāya rāmānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya continued to speak; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this person; [[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:b:bhavānanda]]---Bhavānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:prathama]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---first son; [[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya continued, "This is Bhavānanda Rāya, the father of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya, who is his first son."**
