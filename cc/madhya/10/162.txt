====== CC Madhya 10.162 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāratī kahe,-tomāra ācāra loka śikhāite
> punaḥ nā karibe nati, bhaya pāṅa citte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāratī]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---behavior; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāite]]---to teach; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---will do; [[synonyms:n:nati]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear; [[synonyms:p:pāṅa]]---I get; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Brahmānanda Bhāratī said, "You instruct the general populace by Your behavior. I will not do anything against Your wishes; otherwise You will not offer me respects but will neglect me. I am afraid of this.**
