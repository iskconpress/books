====== CC Madhya 10.105 ======

===== Text =====

> 'caitanyānanda' guru tāṅra ājñā dilena tāṅre
> vedānta paḍiyā paḍāo samasta lokere

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---of the name Caitanyānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---spiritual master; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:d:dilena]]---gave; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:v:vedānta]] [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---reading the //Vedānta-sūtra//; [[synonyms:p:paḍāo]]---teach; [[synonyms:s:samasta]]---all; [[synonyms:l:lokere]]---people.

===== Translation =====

**At the conclusion of his sannyāsa, his spiritual master, Caitanyānanda Bhāratī, ordered him, "Read Vedānta-sūtra and teach it to all others."**
