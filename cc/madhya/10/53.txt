====== Cc. Madhya 10.53 ======

===== Text =====

> sākṣāt pāṇḍu tumi, tomāra patnī kuntī
> pañca-pāṇḍava tomāra pañca-putra mahā-mati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]] [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍu]]---directly Mahārāja Pāṇḍu; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:p:patnī]]---wife; [[synonyms:k:kuntī]]---like Kuntīdevī; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṇḍava]]---five Pāṇḍavas; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---five sons; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]---all highly intellectual.

===== Translation =====

**"You are Mahārāja Pāṇḍu himself, and your wife is Kuntīdevī herself. All your highly intellectual sons are representatives of the five Pāṇḍavas."**
