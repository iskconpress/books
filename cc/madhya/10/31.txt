====== CC Madhya 10.31 ======

===== Text =====

> darśana kari' mahāprabhu calilā bāhire
> bhaṭṭācārya ānila tāṅre kāśī-miśra-ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the house of Kāśī Miśra.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left the temple. The Bhaṭṭācārya then took Him to the house of Kāśī Miśra.**
