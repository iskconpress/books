====== CC Madhya 10.86 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryera sabe kaila caraṇa vandana
> ācārya-gosāñi sabāre kaila āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---offering obeisances at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to all; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees offered respectful obeisances at the lotus feet of Advaita Ācārya, and in return Advaita Ācārya embraced them all.**
