====== Cc. Madhya 10.184 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmabhadrācārya, āra bhagavān ācārya
> prabhu-pade rahilā duṅhe chāḍi' sarva kārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmabhadra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Rāmabhadra Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---under the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---all other responsibilities.

===== Translation =====

**Later, Rāmabhadra Ācārya and Bhagavān Ācārya also joined them and, giving up all other responsibilities, remained under Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's shelter.**
