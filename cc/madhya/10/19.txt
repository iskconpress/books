====== CC Madhya 10.19 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya kahe,-teṅho āsibe alpa-kāle
> rahite tāṅre eka sthāna cāhiye virale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:ā:āsibe]]---will come; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---very soon; [[synonyms:r:rahite]]---to keep; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---place; [[synonyms:c:cāhiye]]---I want; [[synonyms:v:virale]]---secluded.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "His Holiness Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu will return very soon. I wish to have a nice place ready for Him, a place solitary and peaceful.**
