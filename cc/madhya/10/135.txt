====== CC Madhya 10.135 ======

===== Text =====

> gosāñi kahila, 'purīśvara' vātsalya kare more
> kṛpā kari' mora ṭhāñi pāṭhāilā tomāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:p:purīśvara]]---Īśvara Purī; [[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]---paternal affection; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to My place; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---you.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "My spiritual master, Īśvara Purī, always favors Me with paternal affection. Therefore, out of his causeless mercy, he has sent you here."**
