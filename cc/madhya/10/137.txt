====== CC Madhya 10.137 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-īśvara haya parama svatantra
> īśvarera kṛpā nahe veda-paratantra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead or Īśvara Purī; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---supremely; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead or of Īśvara Purī; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:p:paratantra]]---subject to the Vedic rules.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Both the Supreme Personality of Godhead and My spiritual master, Īśvara Purī, are completely independent. Therefore neither the mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead nor that of Īśvara Purī is subject to any Vedic rules or regulations.**
