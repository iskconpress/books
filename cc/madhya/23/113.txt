====== CC Madhya 23.113 ======

===== Text =====

> ye tu dharmāmṛtam idaṁ
> yathoktaṁ paryupāsate
> śraddadhānā mat-paramā
> bhaktās te 'tīva me priyāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]---the devotees who; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛtam]]---eternal religious principle of Kṛṣṇa consciousness; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:u:uktam]]---as mentioned above; [[synonyms:p:paryupāsate]]---worship; [[synonyms:ś:śraddadhānāḥ]]---having faith and devotion; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:p:paramāḥ]]---accepting Me as the Supreme or the ultimate goal of life; [[synonyms:b:bhaktāḥ]]---such devotees; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they; [[synonyms:a:atīva]]---very much; [[synonyms:m:me]]---My; [[synonyms:p:priyāḥ]]---dear.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'He who thus follows this imperishable religious principle of Kṛṣṇa consciousness with great faith and devotion, fully accepting Me as the supreme goal, is very, very dear to Me.'**
