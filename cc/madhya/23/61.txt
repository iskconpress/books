====== Cc. Madhya 23.61 ======

===== Text =====

> udghūrṇā, vivaśa-ceṣṭā---divyonmāda-nāma
> virahe kṛṣṇa-sphūrti, āpanāke 'kṛṣṇa'-jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:udghūrṇā]]---unsteadiness; [[synonyms:v:vivaśa]]-[[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---boastful activities; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named transcendental madness; [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---manifestation of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāke]]---oneself; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---thinking as Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Udghūrṇā [unsteadiness] and vivaśa-ceṣṭā [boastful activities] are aspects of transcendental madness. In separation from Kṛṣṇa, one experiences the manifestation of Kṛṣṇa, and one thinks oneself to be Kṛṣṇa.**
