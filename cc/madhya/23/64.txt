====== CC Madhya 23.64 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhikādye 'pūrva-rāga' prasiddha 'pravāsa', 'māne'
> 'prema-vaicittya' śrī-daśame mahiṣī-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhikādye]]---in Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī and the other //gopīs//; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:r:rāga]]---feelings before union; [[synonyms:p:prasiddha]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:p:pravāsa]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---also //pravāsa// and //māna//; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vaicittya]]---feelings of fear of separation; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:d:daśame]]---in the Tenth Canto; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---among the queens.

===== Translation =====

**"Of the four kinds of separation, three [pūrva-rāga, pravāsa and māna] are celebrated in Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī and the gopīs. In Dvārakā, among the queens, feelings of prema-vaicittya are very prominent.**
