====== CC Madhya 23.119 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sanātana prabhura caraṇe dhariyā
> nivedana kare dante tṛṇa-guccha lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching the lotus feet; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---submits a petition; [[synonyms:d:dante]]---in the teeth; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:guccha]]---a bunch of straw; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī then humbly accepted his position as lower than a piece of straw, and, symbolically holding some straw in his mouth, he fell down, clasped the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and submitted the following petition.**
