====== Cc. Madhya 23.110 ======

===== Text =====

> yo na hṛsyati na dveṣṭi
> na śocati na kāṅkṣati
> śubhāśubha-parityāgī
> bhaktimān yaḥ sa me priyaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---he who; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:h:hṛṣyati]]---is not jubilant (upon getting something favorable); [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:d:dveṣṭi]]---does not hate (being artificially influenced by something unfavorable); [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:ś:śocati]]---laments; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kāṅkṣati]]---desires; [[synonyms:ś:śubha]]-[[synonyms:a:aśubha]]---the materially auspicious and inauspicious; [[synonyms:p:parityāgī]]---completely rejecting; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:m:mān]]---possessing devotion; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that person; [[synonyms:m:me]] [[synonyms:p:priyaḥ]]---very dear to Me.

===== Translation =====

**"One who neither rejoices nor hates, who neither laments nor desires, who renounces both auspicious and inauspicious things and who is devoted to Me is very dear to Me.**
