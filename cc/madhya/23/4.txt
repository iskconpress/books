====== CC Madhya 23.4 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇe rati gāḍha haile 'prema'-abhidhāna
> kṛṣṇa-bhakti-rasera ei 'sthāyi-bhāva'-nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rati]]---affection; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:h:haile]]---when it becomes; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:a:abhidhāna]]---called love of God; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of the mellows of devotional service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sthāyi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---called //sthāyi-bhāva.//

===== Translation =====

**"When affection for Kṛṣṇa becomes deeper, one attains love of Godhead in devotional service. Such a position is called sthāyi-bhāva, permanent enjoyment of the mellows of devotional service to Kṛṣṇa.**
