====== CC Madhya 23.59 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mādane' cumbanādi haya ananta vibheda
> 'udghūrṇā', 'citra-jalpa' 'mohane' dui bheda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mādane]]---in the stage of //mādana//; [[synonyms:c:cumbana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---kissing and similar activities; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:v:vibheda]]---unlimited divisions; [[synonyms:u:udghūrṇā]]---unsteadiness; [[synonyms:c:citra]]-[[synonyms:j:jalpa]]---various mad talks; [[synonyms:m:mohane]]---the stage of //mohana//; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---two divisions.

===== Translation =====

**"On the mādana platform there are kissing and many other symptoms, which are unlimited. In the mohana stage, there are two divisions-udghūrṇā [unsteadiness] and citra-jalpa [varieties of mad emotional talks].**

===== Purport =====

For further information, see //Madhya-līlā// (1.87).
