====== Cc. Madhya 23.49 ======

===== Text =====

> dadhi yena khaṇḍa-marica-karpūra-milane
> 'rasālākhya' rasa haya apūrvāsvādane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---sugar candy; [[synonyms:m:marica]]---black pepper; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---camphor; [[synonyms:m:milane]]---being mixed; [[synonyms:r:rasāla]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhya]]---known os delicious; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---mellow; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvādane]]---by an unprecedented taste.

===== Translation =====

**"Yogurt mixed with sugar candy, black pepper and camphor is very palatable and tasty. Similarly, when permanent ecstasy mixes with other ecstatic symptoms, it becomes unprecedentedly tasty.**
