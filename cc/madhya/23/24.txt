====== CC Madhya 23.24 ======

===== Text =====

> bhukti, siddhi, indriyārtha tāre nāhi bhāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhukti]]---material enjoyment; [[synonyms:s:siddhi]]---mystic power; [[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the objects of the senses; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---do not appeal.

===== Translation =====

**"In the material field, people are interested in material enjoyment, mystic power and sense gratification. However, these things do not appeal to the devotee at all.**
