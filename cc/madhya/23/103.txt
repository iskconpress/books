====== CC Madhya 23.103 ======

===== Text =====

> tumiha kariha bhakti-śāstrera pracāra
> mathurāya lupta-tīrthera kariha uddhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumiha]]---you also; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---should perform; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]] [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---propagation of the revealed scriptures of devotional service; [[synonyms:m:mathurāya]]---in Mathurā; [[synonyms:l:lupta]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrthera]]---of lost places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---should make; [[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---recovery.

===== Translation =====

**"O Sanātana, you should broadcast the revealed scriptures on devotional service and excavate the lost places of pilgrimage in the district of Mathurā.**
