====== CC Madhya 23.115 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sanātana saba siddhānta puchilā
> bhāgavata-siddhānta gūḍha sakali kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---conclusive statements; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired about; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---the conclusive statements about devotional service mentioned in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]---very confidential; [[synonyms:s:sakali]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu described.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Sanātana Gosvāmī inquired from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu about all the conclusive statements concerning devotional service, and the Lord very vividly explained all the confidential meanings of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.**
