====== CC Madhya 23.123 ======

===== Text =====

> 'muñi ye śikhāluṅ tore sphuruka sakala'
> ei tomāra vara haite habe mora bala"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāluṅ]]---have instructed; [[synonyms:t:tore]]---unto you; [[synonyms:s:sphuruka]] [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---let it be manifested; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:v:vara]]---Your benediction; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:b:bala]]---my strength.

===== Translation =====

**"Now, will You please tell me, 'Let whatever I have instructed all be fully manifest unto you.' By benedicting me in this way, You will give me strength to describe all this."**
