====== CC Madhya 23.54 ======

===== Text =====

> śānta-rase śānti-rati 'prema' paryanta haya
> dāsya-rati 'rāga' paryanta krameta bāḍaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śānta]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---in the mellow of neutrality; [[synonyms:ś:śānti]]-[[synonyms:r:rati]]---spiritual attachment in peacefulness; [[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---up to love of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]-[[synonyms:r:rati]]---attachment in servitude; [[synonyms:r:rāga]]---spontaneous love; [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---up to; [[synonyms:k:krameta]]---gradually; [[synonyms:b:bāḍaya]]---increases.

===== Translation =====

**"The position of neutrality increases up to the point where one can appreciate love of Godhead. The mellow of servitorship gradually increases to the point of spontaneous love of Godhead.**
