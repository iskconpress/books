====== CC Madhya 23.71 ======

===== Text =====

> vividhādbhuta-bhāṣā-vit
> satya-vākyaḥ priyaṁ vadaḥ
> vāvadūkaḥ supāṇḍityo
> buddhimān pratibhānvitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vividha]]---various; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:b:bhāṣā]]-[[synonyms:v:vit]]---knower of languages; [[synonyms:s:satya]]-[[synonyms:v:vākyaḥ]]---whose words are truthful; [[synonyms:p:priyam]] [[synonyms:v:vadaḥ]]---who speaks very pleasingly; [[synonyms:v:vāvadūkaḥ]]---expert in speaking; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṇḍityaḥ]]---very learned; [[synonyms:b:buddhimān]]---very wise; [[synonyms:p:pratibhā]]-[[synonyms:a:anvitaḥ]]---genius.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Kṛṣṇa is the linguist of all wonderful languages. He is a truthful and very pleasing speaker. He is expert in speaking, and He is a very wise, learned scholar and a genius.**
