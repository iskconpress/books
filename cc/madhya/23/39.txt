====== Cc. Madhya 23.39 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅra citte kṛṣṇa-premā karaye udaya
> tāṅra vākya, kriyā, mudrā vijñeha nā bujhaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in whose heart; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:premā]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:u:udaya]]---awakens; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---words; [[synonyms:k:kriyā]]---activities; [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---symptoms; [[synonyms:v:vijñeha]]---even a learned scholar; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:bujhaya]]---does not understand.

===== Translation =====

**"Even the most learned man cannot understand the words, activities and symptoms of a person situated in love of Godhead.**
