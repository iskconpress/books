====== CC Madhya 23.5 ======

===== Text =====

> śuddha-sattva-viśeṣātmā
> prema-sūryāṁśu-sāmya-bhāk
> rucibhiś citta-masṛṇya-
> kṛd asau bhāva ucyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:s:sattva]]---by unadultered goodness; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---distinguished; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---whose nature; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---of love of God; [[synonyms:s:sūrya]]---like the sun; [[synonyms:a:aṁśu]]---a ray; [[synonyms:s:sāmya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāk]]---which is similar to; [[synonyms:r:rucibhiḥ]]---by different tastes; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---of the heart; [[synonyms:m:masṛṇya]]---softness; [[synonyms:k:kṛt]]---which causes; [[synonyms:a:asau]]---that softness; [[synonyms:b:bhāvaḥ]]---emotion; [[synonyms:u:ucyate]]---is called.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'When devotional service is situated on the transcendental platform of pure goodness, it is like a ray of the sunlight of love for Kṛṣṇa. At such a time, devotional service causes the heart to be softened by various tastes, and it is called bhāva [emotion].'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is found in the //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (1.3.1).
