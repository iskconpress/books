====== CC Madhya 23.86 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta guṇa śrī-rādhikāra, pañciśa---pradhāna
> yei guṇera 'vaśa' haya kṛṣṇa bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---unlimited qualities; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rādhikāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:p:pañciśa]]---twenty-five; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---chief; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:g:guṇera]]---of those qualities; [[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---under the control; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"Similarly, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī has unlimited transcendental qualities, of which twenty-five qualities are principal. Śrī Kṛṣṇa is controlled by these transcendental qualities of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
