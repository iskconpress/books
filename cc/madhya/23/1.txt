====== Cc. Madhya 23.1 ======

===== Text =====

> cirād adattaṁ nija-gupta-vittaṁ
> svaprema-nāmāmṛtam atyudāraḥ
> āpāmaraṁ yo vitatāra gauraḥ
> kṛṣṇo janebhyas tam ahaṁ prapadye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cirāt]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:a:adattam]]---not given; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gupta]]-[[synonyms:v:vittam]]---His own personal confidential property; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---of love for Him; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the holy name; [[synonyms:a:amṛtam]]---the ambrosia; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:u:udāraḥ]]---most munificent; [[synonyms:ā:ā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāmaram]]---even down to the lowest of men; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---one who; [[synonyms:v:vitatāra]]---distributed; [[synonyms:g:gauraḥ]]---Śrī Gaurasundara; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇaḥ]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself; [[synonyms:j:janebhyaḥ]]---to the people in general; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---to Him; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:p:prapadye]]---offer obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**The most munificent Supreme Personality of Godhead, known as Gaurakṛṣṇa, distributed to everyone-even the lowest of men-His own confidential treasury in the form of the nectar of love of Himself and the holy name. This was never given to the people at any time before. I therefore offer my respectful obeisances unto Him.**
