====== Cc. Madhya 23.17 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅhāra hṛdaye ei bhāvāṅkura haya
> tāṅhāte eteka cihna sarva-śāstre kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅkura]]---seed of emotion; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāte]]---in him; [[synonyms:e:eteka]]---these; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]---symptoms; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---all revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---say.

===== Translation =====

**"If one actually has the seed of transcendental emotion in his heart, the symptoms will be visible in his activities. That is the verdict of all revealed scriptures.**
