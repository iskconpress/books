====== Cc. Madhya 23.120 ======

===== Text =====

> "nīca-jāti, nīca-sevī, muñi---supāmara
> siddhānta śikhāilā,---yei brahmāra agocara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---lower class; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:s:sevī]]---servant of lowborn people; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:supāmara]]---very,very fallen; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]] [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---You have taught the topmost conclusions in detail; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---which; [[synonyms:b:brahmāra]]---of Brahmā; [[synonyms:a:agocara]]---beyond the reach.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "My dear Lord, I am a very lowborn person. Indeed, I am a servant to lowborn people; therefore I am very, very downtrodden. Nonetheless, You have taught me conclusions unknown even to Lord Brahmā.**
