====== CC Madhya 23.11 ======

===== Text =====

> anartha-nivṛtti haile bhaktye 'niṣṭhā' haya
> niṣṭhā haite śravaṇādye 'ruci' upajaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anartha]]-[[synonyms:n:nivṛtti]]---disappearance of all unwanted contamination; [[synonyms:h:haile]]---when there is; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---in devotional service; [[synonyms:n:niṣṭhā]]---firm faith; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:n:niṣṭhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from such firm faith; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādye]]---in hearing, chanting and so on; [[synonyms:r:ruci]]---taste; [[synonyms:u:upajaya]]---awakens.

===== Translation =====

**"When one is freed from all unwanted contamination, he advances with firm faith. When firm faith in devotional service awakens, a taste for hearing and chanting also awakens.**
