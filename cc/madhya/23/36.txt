====== CC Madhya 23.36 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-līlā-sthāne kare sarvadā vasati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in the place where Kṛṣṇa has His pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:s:sarvadā]]---always; [[synonyms:v:vasati]]---abode.

===== Translation =====

**"A devotee absorbed in ecstatic emotion for Kṛṣṇa always resides in a place where Kṛṣṇa's pastimes were performed.**
