====== CC Madhya 23.33 ======

===== Text =====

> rodana-bindu-maranda-syandi-
> dṛg-indīvarādya govinda
> tava madhura-svara-kaṇṭhī
> gāyati nāmāvalīṁ bālā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rodana]]-[[synonyms:b:bindu]]---with teardrops; [[synonyms:m:maranda]]---like the nectar or juice of flowers; [[synonyms:s:syandi]]---pouring; [[synonyms:d:dṛk]]-[[synonyms:i:indīvarā]]---whose lotus eyes; [[synonyms:ā:ādya]]---today; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---O my Lord Govinda; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---Your; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:s:svara]]-[[synonyms:k:kaṇṭhī]]---who has a very sweet voice; [[synonyms:g:gāyati]]---sings; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:āvalīm]]---holy names; [[synonyms:b:bālā]]---this young girl (Rādhikā).

===== Translation =====

**“ 'O Govinda, this youthful girl named Rādhikā is today constantly pouring forth tears like nectar falling from flowers. She is also singing Your holy name in a sweet voice.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is found in the //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (1.3.38).
