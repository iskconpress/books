====== Cc. Madhya 23.38 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇe 'ratira' cihna ei kailuṅ vivaraṇa
> 'kṛṣṇa-premera' cihna ebe śuna sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:ratira]]---of attraction; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]---the symptoms; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---all these; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]] [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---I have described; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:premera]]---of love for Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]---the symptoms; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]] [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---please hear, Sanātana.

===== Translation =====

**"These are the symptoms of a person who has developed attraction [bhāva] for Kṛṣṇa. Now let me describe the symptoms of a person who is actually elevated to love of Kṛṣṇa. O Sanātana, please hear this from Me.**
