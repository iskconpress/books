====== Cc. Madhya 23.125 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahiluṅ---'prema'-prayojana-saṁvāda
> vistāri' kahana nā yāya prabhura prasāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---briefly; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:prayojana]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvāda]]---the discussion of the ultimate goal of life, love of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---expansively; [[synonyms:k:kahana]]---describing; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---not possible; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the benediction of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have briefly described a discussion of the ultimate goal of life, love of Godhead. The mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu cannot be described expansively.**
