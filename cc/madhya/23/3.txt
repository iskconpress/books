====== Cc. Madhya 23.3 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe śuna bhakti-phala 'prema'-prayojana
> yāhāra śravaṇe haya bhakti-rasa-jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]] [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---now hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---the result of practice of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---the ultimate goal of life; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing of which; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---transcendental knowledge of the mellows of devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "Now hear, O Sanātana, about the result of devotional service, which is love of Godhead, life's ultimate goal. If one hears this description, he will be enlightened in the transcendental mellows of devotional service.**
