====== CC Madhya 23.78 ======

===== Text =====

> atha pañca-guṇā ye syur
> aṁśena giriśādiṣu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atha]]---now(over and above these); [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇāḥ]]---five qualities; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:s:syuḥ]]---may exist; [[synonyms:a:aṁśena]]---by part; [[synonyms:g:giriśa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---in demigods like Lord Śiva.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Apart from these fifty qualities, there are five other qualities found in the Supreme Personality of Godhead that are partially present in demigods like Śiva.**

===== Purport =====

This verse and the following seven verses are also found in the //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (2.1.37-44).
