====== Cc. Madhya 23.48 ======

===== Text =====

> vibhāva, anubhāva, sāttvika, vyabhicārī
> sthāyi-bhāva 'rasa' haya ei cāri mili'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vibhāva]]---special ecstasy; [[synonyms:a:anubhāva]]---subordinate ecstasy; [[synonyms:s:sāttvika]]---natural ecstasy; [[synonyms:v:vyabhicārī]]---transitory ecstasy; [[synonyms:s:sthāyi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---permanent ecstasy; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---mellow; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---these four; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**"The permanent ecstasy becomes a more and more tasteful transcendental mellow through the mixture of special ecstasy, subordinate ecstasy, natural ecstasy and transitory ecstasy.**
