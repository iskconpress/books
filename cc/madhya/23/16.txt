====== CC Madhya 23.16 ======

===== Text =====

> satāṁ prasaṅgān mama vīrya-saṁvido
> bhavanti hṛt-karṇa-rasāyanāḥ kathāḥ
> taj-joṣaṇād āśv apavarga-vartmani
> śraddhā ratir bhaktir anukramiṣyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:satām]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prasaṅgāt]]---bu the intimate association; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---of Me; [[synonyms:v:vīrya]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁvidaḥ]]---talks full of spiritual potency; [[synonyms:b:bhavanti]]---appear; [[synonyms:h:hṛt]]---to the heart; [[synonyms:k:karṇa]]---and to the ears; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyanāḥ]]---a source of sweetness; [[synonyms:k:kathāḥ]]---talks; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of them; [[synonyms:j:joṣaṇāt]]---from proper cultivation; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]---quickly; [[synonyms:a:apavarga]]---of liberation; [[synonyms:v:vartmani]]---on the path; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]]---faith; [[synonyms:r:ratiḥ]]---attraction; [[synonyms:b:bhaktiḥ]]---love; [[synonyms:a:anukramiṣyati]]---will follow one after another.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The spiritually powerful message of Godhead can be properly discussed only in a society of devotees, and it is greatly pleasing to hear in that association. If one hears from devotees, the way of transcendental experience quickly opens to him, and gradually he attains firm faith that in due course develops into attraction and devotion.'**
