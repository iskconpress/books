====== Cc. Madhya 23.58 ======

===== Text =====

> adhirūḍha-mahābhāva---dui ta' prakāra
> sambhoge 'mādana', virahe 'mohana' nāma tāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adhirūḍha]]-[[synonyms:m:mahābhāva]]---highly advanced ecstasy; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---two varieties; [[synonyms:s:sambhoge]]---in actually meeting; [[synonyms:m:mādana]]---//mādana//; [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation; [[synonyms:m:mohana]]---//mohana//; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the names; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of them.

===== Translation =====

**"Highly advanced ecstasy is divided into two categories-mādana and mohana. Meeting together is called mādana, and separation is called mohana.**
