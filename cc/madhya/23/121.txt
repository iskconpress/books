====== CC Madhya 23.121 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi ye kahilā, ei siddhāntāmṛta-sindhu
> mora mana chuṅite nāre ihāra eka-bindu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:y:ye]]---You; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---have spoken; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of the ambrosia of conclusive truth; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---my mind; [[synonyms:c:chuṅite]]---to touch; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---is not able; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of it; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bindu]]---even a drop.

===== Translation =====

**"The conclusions that You have told me are the ocean of the ambrosia of truth. My mind is unable to approach even a drop of that ocean.**
