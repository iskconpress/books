====== Cc. Madhya 23.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ruci haite bhaktye haya 'āsakti' pracura
> āsakti haite citte janme kṛṣṇe prīty-aṅkura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ruci]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from such a taste; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---in devotional service; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:ā:āsakti]]---attachment; [[synonyms:p:pracura]]---deep; [[synonyms:ā:āsakti]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from attachment; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:j:janme]]---appears; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅkura]]---the seed of affection.

===== Translation =====

**"After taste is awakened, a deep attachment arises, and from that attachment the seed of love for Kṛṣṇa grows in the heart.**
