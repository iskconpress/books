====== Cc. Madhya 23.124 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu tāṅra śire dhari' kare
> vara dilā'---ei saba sphuruka tomāre'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after that; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on the head; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---holding; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---by the hand; [[synonyms:v:vara]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave the benediction; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all this; [[synonyms:s:sphuruka]] [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---let it be manifested to you properly.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then placed His hand on Sanātana Gosvāmī's head and benedicted him, saying, "Let all these instructions be manifest to you."**
