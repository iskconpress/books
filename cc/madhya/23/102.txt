====== Cc. Madhya 23.102 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve prayāge āmi rasera vicāre
> tomāra bhāi rūpe kailuṅ śakti-sañcāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]]---in Prayāga; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:r:rasera]] [[synonyms:v:vicāre]]---in consideration of different mellows; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---your brother; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:s:sañcāre]]---endowment of all power.

===== Translation =====

**"Previously I empowered your brother Rūpa Gosvāmī to understand these mellows. I did this while instructing him at the Daśāśvamedha-ghāṭa in Prayāga.**
