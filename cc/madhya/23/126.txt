====== CC Madhya 23.126 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura upadeśāmṛta śune yei jana
> acirāt milaye tāṅre kṛṣṇa-prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the nectar of the instructions; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---without delay; [[synonyms:m:milaye]]---meets; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the treasure of love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever hears these instructions given to Sanātana Gosvāmī by the Lord comes very soon to realize love of God, Kṛṣṇa.**
