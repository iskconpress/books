====== Cc. Madhya 23.74 ======

===== Text =====

> dakṣiṇo vinayī hrīmān
> śaraṇāgata-pālakaḥ
> sukhī bhakta-suhṛt prema-
> vaśyaḥ sarva-śubhaṅkaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇaḥ]]---simple and liberal; [[synonyms:v:vinayī]]---humble; [[synonyms:h:hrīmān]]---bashful when glorified; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇāgata]]-[[synonyms:p:pālakaḥ]]---protector of the surrendered soul; [[synonyms:s:sukhī]]---//always// happy; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:suhṛt]]---well-wisher of the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśyaḥ]]---submissive to love; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śubhaṅkaraḥ]]---all-auspicious.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Kṛṣṇa is very simple and liberal, He is humble and bashful, and He is the protector of the surrendered soul. He is very happy, and He is always the well-wisher of His devotee. He is all-auspicious, and He is submissive to love.**
