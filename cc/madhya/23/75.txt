====== CC Madhya 23.75 ======

===== Text =====

> pratāpī kīrtimān rakta-
> lokaḥ sādhu-samāśrayaḥ
> nārīgaṇa-manohārī
> sarvārādhyaḥ samṛddhimān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratāpī]]---very influential; [[synonyms:k:kīrtimān]]---famous for good works; [[synonyms:r:rakta]]-[[synonyms:l:lokaḥ]]---who is the object of the attachment of all people; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrayaḥ]]---the shelter of the good and virtuous; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to women; [[synonyms:m:manohārī]]---attractive; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārādhyaḥ]]---worshipable by everyone; [[synonyms:s:samṛddhimān]]---very rich.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Kṛṣṇa is very influential and famous, and He is the object of attachment for everyone. He is the shelter of the good and the virtuous. He is attractive to the minds of women, and He is worshiped by everyone. He is very, very rich.**
