====== CC Madhya 23.92 ======

===== Text =====

> nāyaka, nāyikā,---dui rasera 'ālambana'
> sei dui śreṣṭha,---rādhā, vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāyaka]]---hero; [[synonyms:n:nāyikā]]---heroine; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of mellows; [[synonyms:ā:ālambana]]---the basis; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---chief; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---and Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda.

===== Translation =====

**"The basis of all transcendental mellows is the hero and the heroine, and Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī and Lord Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda, are the best.**
