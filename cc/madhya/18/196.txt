====== Cc. Madhya 18.196 ======

===== Text =====

> 'karma', jñāna', 'yoga' āge kariyā sthāpana
> saba khaṇḍi' sthāpe 'īśvara', 'tāṅhāra sevana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karma]]---fruitive activities; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---speculative knowledge; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]---mystic power; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---establishing; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍi']]---refuting everything; [[synonyms:s:sthāpe]]---establishes; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---His service.

===== Translation =====

**"In the Koran there are descriptions of fruitive activity, speculative knowledge, mystic power and union with the Supreme, but ultimately everything is refuted as the Lord's personal feature and His devotional service is established.**
