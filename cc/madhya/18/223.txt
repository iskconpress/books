====== Cc. Madhya 18.223 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-gamana, prabhu-caritra ananta
> 'sahasra-vadana' yāṅra nāhi pā'na anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---Lord Śeṣa, who has thousands of hoods; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:pā'na]]---get; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---limit.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's visit to Vṛndāvana and His activities there are unlimited. Even Lord Śeṣa, who has thousands of hoods, cannot reach the end of His activities.**
