====== CC Madhya 18.73 ======

===== Text =====

> cetana pāñā punaḥ gaḍāgaḍi yāya
> hāse, kānde, nāce, paḍe, uccaiḥ-svare gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cetana]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting His senses back; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:g:gaḍāgaḍi]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---rolls on the ground; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laughs; [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---cries; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:u:uccaiḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:svare]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sings very loudly.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord regained His senses, He began to roll on the ground. He would sometimes laugh, cry, dance and fall down. He would also chant very loudly.**
