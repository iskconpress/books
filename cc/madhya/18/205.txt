====== Cc. Madhya 18.205 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---uṭha, kṛṣṇa-nāma tumi la-ilā
> koṭi-janmera pāpa gela, 'pavitra' ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:u:uṭha]]---please get up; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---have taken; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:j:janmera]]---of many millions of births; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---your sinful reactions have gone; [[synonyms:p:pavitra]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---you have become pure.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Please get up. You have chanted the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; therefore the sinful reactions you have accrued for many millions of lives are now gone. You are now pure."**
