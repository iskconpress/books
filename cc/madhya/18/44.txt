====== CC Madhya 18.44 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu kuñje rahe, kabhu rahe grāmāntare
> sei bhakta, tāhāṅ āsi' dekhaye tāṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in the bushes; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---He stays; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---in a different village; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---that devotee; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:d:dekhaye]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---sees Him.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, giving some excuse, Gopāla sometimes remains in the bushes of the forest, and sometimes He stays in a village. One who is a devotee comes to see the Deity.**
