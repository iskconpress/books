====== CC Madhya 18.123 ======

===== Text =====

> darśanera kārya āchuka, ye tomāra 'nāma' śune
> sei kṛṣṇa-preme matta, tāre tribhuvane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:darśanera]] [[synonyms:k:kārya]] [[synonyms:ā:āchuka]]---aside from seeing You; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---holy name; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that man; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---delivers; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---the three worlds.

===== Translation =====

**"Apart from seeing You, whoever listens to Your holy name is made mad with ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa and is able to deliver the three worlds.**
