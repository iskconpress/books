====== CC Madhya 18.89 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-saṅge madhyāhne akrūra tīrthe āilā
> prabhura avaśiṣṭa-pātra-prasāda pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the Lord; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhne]]---in the afternoon; [[synonyms:a:akrūra]] [[synonyms:t:tīrthe]]---to Akrūra-tīrtha; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avaśiṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---got.

===== Translation =====

**Kṛṣṇadāsa returned to Akrūra-tīrtha with the Lord, and remnants of the Lord's food were given to him.**
