====== Cc. Madhya 18.144 ======

===== Text =====

> 'soro-kṣetre, āge yāñā kari' gaṅgā-snāna
> sei pathe prabhu lañā kariye payāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:soro]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---to the holy place named Soro-kṣetra; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---first, beyond; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---having taken bath in the Ganges; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---that way; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:p:payāna]]---let us go.

===== Translation =====

**"After going to the holy place named Soro-kṣetra, and taking bath in the Ganges, let us take Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that way and go.**
