====== CC Madhya 18.116 ======

===== Text =====

> yas tu nārāyaṇaṁ devaṁ
> brahma-rudrādi-daivataiḥ
> samatvenaiva vīkṣeta
> sa pāṣaṇḍī bhaved dhruvam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---any person who; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---however; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the master of such demigods as Brahmā and Śiva; [[synonyms:d:devam]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:r:rudra]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:d:daivataiḥ]]---with such demigods; [[synonyms:s:samatvena]]---on an equal level; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:v:vīkṣeta]]---observes; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---such a person; [[synonyms:p:pāṣaṇḍī]]---pāṣaṇḍī; [[synonyms:b:bhavet]]---must be; [[synonyms:d:dhruvam]]---certainly.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'A person who considers demigods like Brahmā and Śiva to be on an equal level with Nārāyaṇa is to be considered an offender and a pāṣaṇḍī.' "**
