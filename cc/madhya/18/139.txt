====== CC Madhya 18.139 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe bhaṭṭācārya sei brāhmaṇe lañā
> yukti karilā kichu nibhṛte vasiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇe]]---the Sanoḍiyā //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:y:yukti]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---consulted; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]] [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting in a solitary place.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya took the Sanoḍiyā brāhmaṇa to a secluded place and consulted with him.**
