====== Cc. Madhya 18.215 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vipre, kṛṣṇadāse, prabhu vidāya dilā
> yoḍa-hāte dui-jana kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---to the Sanoḍiyā //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāse]]---and the Rājaputa Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---asked to go back; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**At Soro-kṣetra, the Lord requested the Sanoḍiyā brāhmaṇa and Rājaputa Kṛṣṇadāsa to return home, but with folded hands they began to speak as follows.**
