====== CC Madhya 18.157 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' mahāprabhure naukāya vasāñā
> pāra kari' bhaṭṭācārya calilā lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:naukāya]]---on a boat; [[synonyms:v:vasāñā]]---making sit down; [[synonyms:p:pāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---crossing the river; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya made Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sit aboard a boat. After they crossed the river, he took the Lord with him.**
