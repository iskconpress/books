====== CC Madhya 18.94 ======

===== Text =====

> loke kahe,---kṛṣṇa prakaṭa kālīya-dahera jale!
> kālīya-śire nṛtya kare, phaṇā-ratna jvale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loke]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---all the people replied; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---Kṛṣṇa is again manifest; [[synonyms:k:kālīya]]-[[synonyms:d:dahera]] [[synonyms:j:jale]]---in the water of Lake Kālīya; [[synonyms:k:kālīya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on the head of the serpent Kālīya; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:phaṇā]]-[[synonyms:r:ratna]] [[synonyms:j:jvale]]---the jewels on the hoods blaze.

===== Translation =====

**The people replied, "Kṛṣṇa has again manifest Himself on the waters of the Kālīya Lake. He dances on the hoods of the serpent Kālīya, and the jewels on those hoods are blazing.**
