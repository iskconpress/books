====== CC Madhya 18.29 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā grāmera loka cintita ha-ila
> prathame gopāla lañā gāṅṭhuli-grāme khuila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people in the village; [[synonyms:c:cintita]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became very anxious; [[synonyms:p:prathame]]---first; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking Gopāla; [[synonyms:g:gāṅṭhuli]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the village known as Gāṅṭhuli; [[synonyms:k:khuila]]---kept Him hidden.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, all the villagers became very anxious. They first took Gopāla and moved Him to a village known as Gāṅṭhuli.**
