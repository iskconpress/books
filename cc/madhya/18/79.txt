====== Cc. Madhya 18.79 ======

===== Text =====

> akrūrera loka āise prabhure dekhite
> loka-bhiḍe svacchande nāre 'kīrtana' karite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:akrūrera]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people at Akrūra-tīrtha; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhiḍe]]---because of such a crowd of people; [[synonyms:s:svacchande]]---without disturbance; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---was not able; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to perform //kīrtana.//

===== Translation =====

**All the people who lived near Akrūra-tīrtha came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and due to the large crowds, the Lord could not peacefully chant the holy name.**
