====== Cc. Madhya 18.28 ======

===== Text =====

> āji rātrye palāha, nā rahiha eka-jana
> ṭhākura lañā bhāga', āsibe kāli yavana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]] [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---this night; [[synonyms:p:palāha]]---go away; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:r:rahiha]]---do not remain; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---one person; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---the Deity; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhāga']]---go away; [[synonyms:ā:āsibe]]---will come; [[synonyms:k:kāli]]---tomorrow; [[synonyms:y:yavana]]---the Mohammedan soldiers.

===== Translation =====

**"Flee this village tonight, and do not allow one person to remain. Take the Deity with you and leave, for the Mohammedan soldiers will come tomorrow."**
