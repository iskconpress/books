====== CC Madhya 18.13 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata stuti kare premāviṣṭa hañā
> tīre nṛtya kare kuṇḍa-līlā saṅariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:stuti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offers prayers; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---overwhelmed by ecstatic love; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---dances; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes of Rādhā-kuṇḍa; [[synonyms:s:saṅariyā]]---remembering.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus offered prayers to Rādhā-kuṇḍa. Overwhelmed by ecstatic love, He danced on the bank, remembering the pastimes Lord Kṛṣṇa performed on the bank of Rādhā-kuṇḍa.**
