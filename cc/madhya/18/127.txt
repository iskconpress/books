====== CC Madhya 18.127 ======

===== Text =====

> sei saba loke prabhu prasāda karila
> kṛṣṇa-preme matta loka nija-ghare gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loke]]---unto all those persons; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---bestowed His causeless mercy; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---persons; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---returned to their own homes.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then bestowed His causeless mercy upon all the people there, and everyone became ecstatic with love of God. Finally they all returned to their homes.**
