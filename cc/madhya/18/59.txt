====== CC Madhya 18.59 ======

===== Text =====

> kichu deva-mūrti haya parvata-upare?
> loka kahe,---mūrti haya gophāra bhitare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---deities; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are there; [[synonyms:p:parvata]]-[[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the top of the hill; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---people said; [[synonyms:m:mūrti]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are deities; [[synonyms:g:gophāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within a cave.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked, "Are there any deities on top of this hill?" The local people replied, "There are deities on this hill, but they are located within a cave.**
