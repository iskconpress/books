====== CC Madhya 18.53 ======

===== Text =====

> ei saba mukhya-bhakta lañā nija-saṅge
> śrī-gopāla daraśana kailā bahu-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---chief devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---taking with him personally; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---performed in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**It was with great jubilation that Rūpa Gosvāmī visited Lord Gopāla accompanied by all these devotees.**
