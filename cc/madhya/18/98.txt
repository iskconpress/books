====== CC Madhya 18.98 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu dekhi' 'satya' kṛṣṇa-daraśana
> nijājñāne satya chāḍi' 'asatye satya-bhrama'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---truly; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:a:ajñāne]]---by their personal lack of knowledge; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up the real truth; [[synonyms:a:asatye]]---untruth; [[synonyms:s:satya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrama]]---mistaking for the truth.

===== Translation =====

**When the people saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they actually saw Kṛṣṇa, but because they were following their own imperfect knowledge, they accepted the wrong thing as Kṛṣṇa.**
