====== CC Madhya 18.224 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhā ke kahite pāre kṣudra jīva hañā
> dig-daraśana kailuṅ muñi sūtra kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---who can describe; [[synonyms:k:kṣudra]]---very little; [[synonyms:j:jīva]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a conditioned soul; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]] [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---have simply made an indication; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---in codes.

===== Translation =====

**What ordinary living being can describe the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu? I have only indicated the general direction in the form of codes.**
