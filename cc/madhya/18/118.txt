====== Cc. Madhya 18.118 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ākṛtye' tomāre dekhi 'vrajendra-nandana'
> deha-kānti pītāmbara kaila ācchādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākṛtye]]---by bodily features; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---You; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---we see; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---directly the son of Mahārāja Nanda; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---the luster of the body; [[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:a:ambara]]---golden covering; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:ā:ācchādana]]---covered.

===== Translation =====

**"By Your bodily features we can see that You are none other than the son of Nanda Mahārāja, although the golden luster of Your body has covered Your original complexion.**
