====== Cc. Madhya 18.25 ======

===== Text =====

> anārurukṣave śailaṁ
> svasmai bhaktābhimānine
> avaruhya gireḥ kṛṣṇo
> gaurāya svam adarśayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anārurukṣave]]---who was unwilling to climb up; [[synonyms:ś:śailam]]---the mountain; [[synonyms:s:svasmai]]---unto Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimānine]]---considering Himself a devotee of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avaruhya]]---getting down; [[synonyms:g:gireḥ]]---from the hill; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇaḥ]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gaurāya]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:a:adarśayat]]---showed.

===== Translation =====

**Coming down from Govardhana Hill, Lord Gopāla granted an interview to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who was unwilling to climb the hill, thinking Himself a devotee of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
