====== CC Madhya 18.76 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-līlā-kālera sei vṛkṣa purātana
> tāra tale piṅḍi-bāndhā parama-cikkaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:k:kālera]]---of the time of Lord Kṛṣṇa's presence; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]---that tamarind tree; [[synonyms:p:purātana]]---very old; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:t:tale]]---underneath that tree; [[synonyms:p:piṅḍi]]-[[synonyms:b:bāndhā]]---a platform; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:c:cikkaṇa]]---very shiny.

===== Translation =====

**The tamarind tree named Teṅtulī-talā was very old, having been there since the time of Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes. Beneath the tree was a very shiny platform.**
