====== CC Madhya 18.27 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-jana āsi' rātre grāmīke balila
> 'tomāra grāma mārite turuka-dhārī sājila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---one person; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:g:grāmīke]]---to the inhabitants of the village; [[synonyms:b:balila]]---said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]---village; [[synonyms:m:mārite]]---to attack; [[synonyms:t:turuka]]-[[synonyms:d:dhārī]]---Turkish Mohammedan soldiers; [[synonyms:s:sājila]]---are prepared.

===== Translation =====

**One person who came to the village informed the inhabitants, "The Turkish soldiers are now preparing to attack your village.**
