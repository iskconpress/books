====== Cc. Madhya 18.133 ======

===== Text =====

> kānyakubja-dākṣiṇātyera vaidika brāhmaṇa
> dainya kari, kare mahāprabhura nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kānyakubja]]---brāhmaṇas from Kānyakubja; [[synonyms:d:dākṣiṇātyera]]---certain //brāhmaṇas// from South India; [[synonyms:v:vaidika]]---followers of the Vedic religion; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---//brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:d:dainya]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---with great humility; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇas from different places, such as Kānyakubja and South India, who were all strict followers of the Vedic religion, offered invitations to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with great humility.**
