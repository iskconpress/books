====== CC Madhya 18.189 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---tomāra śāstra sthāpe 'nirviśeṣe'
> tāhā khaṇḍi' 'saviśeṣa' sthāpiyāche śeṣe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued to speak; [[synonyms:t:tomāraśāstra]]---your scripture (the Koran); [[synonyms:s:sthāpe]]---establishes; [[synonyms:n:nirviśeṣe]]---impersonalism; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍi']]---refuting that; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---personal God; [[synonyms:s:sthāpiyāche]]---established; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "The Koran has certainly established impersonalism, but at the end it refutes that impersonalism and establishes the personal God.**
