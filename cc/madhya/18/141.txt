====== CC Madhya 18.141 ======

===== Text =====

> lokera saṅghaṭṭa, āra nimantraṇera jañjāla
> nirantara āveśa prabhura nā dekhiye bhāla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lokera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭa]]---crowds of people; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇera]] [[synonyms:j:jañjāla]]---the disturbance of invitations; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiye]] [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---I do not see any good in this.

===== Translation =====

**"Now there is a crowd of people here, and these invitations are causing much disturbance. In addition, the Lord is always ecstatic and emotional. I do not find the situation here very good.**
