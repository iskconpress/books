====== Cc. Madhya 18.210 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅ-sabāre kṛpā kari' prabhu ta' calilā
> seita pāṭhāna saba 'vairāgī' ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to all of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---bestowing mercy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:s:seita]]---they; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāna]]---the Mohammedans of the Pāṭhāna community; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vairāgī]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became mendicants.

===== Translation =====

**Bestowing His mercy upon them in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left. All the Pāṭhāna Mohammedans then became mendicants.**
