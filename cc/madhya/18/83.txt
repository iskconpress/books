====== Cc. Madhya 18.83 ======

===== Text =====

> 'keśī' snāna kari' sei 'kālīya-daha' yāite
> āmli-talāya gosāñire dekhe ācambite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keśī]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after taking his bath at the place known as Keśī-tīrtha; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:k:kālīya]]-[[synonyms:d:daha]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---going to the Kālīya-daha; [[synonyms:ā:āmli]]-[[synonyms:t:talāya]]---at the place known as Āmli-talā; [[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing at Keśī-tīrtha, Kṛṣṇadāsa went toward Kālīya-daha and suddenly saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sitting at Āmli-talā [Teṅtulī-talā].**
