====== CC Madhya 18.5 ======

===== Text =====

> tīrtha 'lupta' jāni' prabhu sarvajña bhagavān
> dui dhānya-kṣetre alpa-jale kailā snāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---holy place; [[synonyms:l:lupta]]---lost; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---omniscient; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:d:dhānya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---in paddy fields; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---in not very deep water; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---took a bath.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then understood that the holy place called Rādhā-kuṇḍa was no longer visible. However, being the omniscient Supreme Personality of Godhead, He discovered Rādhā-kuṇḍa and Śyāma-kuṇḍa in two paddy fields. There was only a little water, but He took His bath there.**
