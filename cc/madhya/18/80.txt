====== Cc. Madhya 18.80 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvane āsi' prabhu vasiyā ekānta
> nāma-saṅkīrtana kare madhyāhna-paryanta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting; [[synonyms:e:ekānta]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]]-[[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---until noon.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go to Vṛndāvana and sit in a solitary place. It was there that He chanted the holy name until noon.**
