====== CC Madhya 18.137 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' jhāṅpa dilā jalera upare
> ḍubiyā rahilā prabhu jalera bhitare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:j:jhāṅpa]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---jumped; [[synonyms:j:jalera]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---above the water; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubiyā]]---sinking; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jalera]] [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within the water.

===== Translation =====

**While considering how Akrūra remained within the water, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately jumped in and stayed under water for some time.**
