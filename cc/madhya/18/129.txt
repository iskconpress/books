====== CC Madhya 18.129 ======

===== Text =====

> mādhava-purīra śiṣya seita brāhmaṇa
> mathurāra ghare-ghare karā'na nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---disciple; [[synonyms:s:seita]]---that; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:m:mathurāra]]---of Mathurā City; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---home to home; [[synonyms:k:karā'na]]---causes to make; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa disciple of Mādhavendra Purī went from house to house in Mathurā and inspired other brāhmaṇas to invite Caitanya Mahāprabhu to their homes.**
