====== Cc. Madhya 18.151 ======

===== Text =====

> udvigna ha-ila prāṇa, sahite nā pāri
> prabhura ye ājñā haya, sei śire dhari"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:udvigna]]---agitated; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---has become; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---my mind; [[synonyms:s:sahite]]---to bear; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am unable; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---what; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śire]] [[synonyms:d:dhari]]---I accept that.

===== Translation =====

**"My mind has become very agitated, and I cannot bear this anxiety. May everything rest on the permission of Your Lordship. I will accept whatever You want to do."**
