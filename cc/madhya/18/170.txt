====== CC Madhya 18.170 ======

===== Text =====

> ei yati vyādhite kabhu hayena mūrcchita
> abaṅhi cetana pāibe, ha-ibe samvita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:y:yati]]---this //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:v:vyādhite]]---under the influence of disease; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:h:hayena]] [[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]]---becomes unconscious; [[synonyms:a:abaṅhi]]---very soon; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:p:pāibe]]---will get back; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]] [[synonyms:s:samvita]]---will come to His proper senses.

===== Translation =====

**"This sannyāsī sometimes falls unconscious due to the influence of a disease. Please sit down here, and you will see that He will very soon regain consciousness and His normal condition.**
