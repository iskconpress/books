====== CC Madhya 18.105 ======

===== Text =====

> dūra haite tāhā dekhi' lokera haya 'bhrama'
> 'kālīyera śarīre kṛṣṇa kariche nartana'!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from a distant place; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing that; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of people in general; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:b:bhrama]]---mistake; [[synonyms:k:kālīyera]]---of the snake Kālīya; [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---on the body; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kariche]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---is dancing.

===== Translation =====

**"From a distance, people mistakenly think that they are seeing Kṛṣṇa dancing on the body of the Kālīya serpent.**
