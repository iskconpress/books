====== CC Madhya 18.164 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure dekhiyā mleccha karaye vicāra
> ei yati-pāśa chila suvarṇa apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]---the Mohammedans; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---considered; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:y:yati]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---within the possession of this //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---a large quantity of gold.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the Lord unconscious, the soldiers thought, "This sannyāsī must have possessed a large quantity of gold.**
