====== CC Madhya 18.136 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ghāṭe akrūra vaikuṇṭha dekhila
> vrajavāsī loka 'goloka' darśana kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:g:ghāṭe]]---in this bathing place; [[synonyms:a:akrūra]]---Akrūra; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw the spiritual world; [[synonyms:v:vrajavāsī]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:g:goloka]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---saw Goloka.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thought, "At this bathing place, Akrūra saw Vaikuṇṭha, the spiritual world, and all the inhabitants of Vraja saw Goloka Vṛndāvana."**
