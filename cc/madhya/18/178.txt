====== Cc. Madhya 18.178 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe prabhu yabe karena citkāra
> mlecchera hṛdaye yena lāge śeladhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:c:citkāra]]---loudly shouts; [[synonyms:m:mlecchera]] [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the hearts of the Mohammedan soldiers; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---strikes; [[synonyms:ś:śela]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---a thunderbolt.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord shouted very loudly in ecstatic love, it appeared to the Mohammedan soldiers that their hearts were struck by thunderbolts.**
