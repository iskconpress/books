====== CC Madhya 18.84 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura rūpa-prema dekhi' ha-ila camatkāra
> premāveśe prabhure karena namaskāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---personal beauty and ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---became astonished; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---offers obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing the Lord's personal beauty and ecstatic love, Kṛṣṇadāsa was very astonished. Out of ecstatic love, he offered his respectful obeisances unto the Lord.**
