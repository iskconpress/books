====== Cc. Madhya 18.146 ======

===== Text =====

> āpanāra duḥkha kichu kari' nivedana
> 'makara-paṅcasi prayāge' kariha sūcana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]]---personal; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission; [[synonyms:m:makara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṅcasi]]---the full-moon day in the month of Māgha; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]]---to Prayāga; [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:s:sūcana]]---kindly inform.

===== Translation =====

**The Sanoḍiyā brāhmaṇa continued, "Kindly submit to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu the unhappiness you are feeling within yourself. Then propose that we all go to Prayāga on the full-moon day of the month of Māgha.**
