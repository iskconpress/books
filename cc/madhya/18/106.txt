====== CC Madhya 18.106 ======

===== Text =====

> naukāte kālīya-jñāna, dīpe ratna-jñāne!
> jāliyāre mūḍha-loka 'kṛṣṇa' kari' māne!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:naukāte]]---on the boat; [[synonyms:k:kālīya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge as the Kālīya snake; [[synonyms:d:dīpe]]---on the torch; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---consideration as jewels; [[synonyms:j:jāliyāre]]---the fisherman; [[synonyms:m:mūḍha]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---foolish men; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---accept as Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"These fools think that the boat is the Kālīya serpent and the torchlight the jewels on his hoods. People also mistake the fisherman to be Kṛṣṇa.**
