====== CC Madhya 18.14 ======

===== Text =====

> kuṇḍera mṛttikā lañā tilaka karila
> bhaṭṭācārya-dvārā mṛttikā saṅge kari' laila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍera]]---of the lake; [[synonyms:m:mṛttikā]]---earth; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tilaka]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---formed //tilaka//; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---with the help of Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mṛttikā]]---earth; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:l:laila]]---took.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then marked His body with tilaka made from the mud of Rādhā-kuṇḍa, and with the help of Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya, He collected some of the mud and took it with Him.**
