====== CC Madhya 18.143 ======

===== Text =====

> vipra kahe,---prayāge prabhu lañā yāi
> gaṅgā-tīra-pathe yāi, tabe sukha pāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vipra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the brāhmaṇa said; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]]---to Prayāga; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---let us go; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---let us go; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---we shall get pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**The Sanoḍiyā brāhmaṇa said, "Let us take Him to Prayāga and go along the banks of the Ganges. It will be very pleasurable to go that way.**
