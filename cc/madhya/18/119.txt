====== CC Madhya 18.119 ======

===== Text =====

> mṛga-mada vastre bāndhe, tabu nā lukāya
> 'īśvara-svabhāva' tomāra ṭākā nāhi yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:m:mada]]---deer musk; [[synonyms:v:vastre]]---in cloth; [[synonyms:b:bāndhe]]---wraps; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:l:lukāya]]---is concealed; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---characteristics as the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭākā]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---are not concealed.

===== Translation =====

**"As the aroma of deer musk cannot be concealed by wrapping it in a cloth, Your characteristics as the Supreme Personality of Godhead cannot be concealed by any means.**
