====== CC Madhya 18.62 ======

===== Text =====

> vrajendra-vrajeśvarīra kaila caraṇa vandana
> premāveśe kṛṣṇera kaila sarvāṅga-sparśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:i:indra]]---of the King of Vraja, Nanda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvarīra]]---and of the Queen of Vraja, mother Yaśodā; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśana]]---touching the whole body.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His respects to Nanda Mahārāja and mother Yaśodā, and with great ecstatic love He touched the body of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
