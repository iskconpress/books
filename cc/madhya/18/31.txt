====== CC Madhya 18.31 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche mleccha-bhaye gopāla bhāge bāre-bāre
> mandira chāḍi' kuñje rahe, kibā grāmāntare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---because of fear of the Mohammedans; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Deity of Gopāla Rāya; [[synonyms:b:bhāge]]---runs away; [[synonyms:b:bāre]]-[[synonyms:b:bāre]]---again and again; [[synonyms:m:mandira]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up the temple; [[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in the bush; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---or; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---in a different village.

===== Translation =====

**Due to fear of the Mohammedans, the Gopāla Deity was moved from one place to another again and again. Thus giving up His temple, Lord Gopāla would sometimes live in a bush and sometimes in one village after another.**
