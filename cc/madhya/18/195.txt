====== CC Madhya 18.195 ======

===== Text =====

> mokṣādi ānanda yāra nahe eka 'kaṇa'
> pūrṇānanda-prāpti tāṅra caraṇa-sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mokṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---liberation and so on; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---even; [[synonyms:k:kaṇa]]---a fragment; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---attainment of completely blissful life; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service to His lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"The happiness of liberation, whereby one merges into the Lord's existence, cannot even be compared to a fragment of the transcendental bliss obtained by service unto the Lord's lotus feet.**
