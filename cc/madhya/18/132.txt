====== CC Madhya 18.132 ======

===== Text =====

> avasara nā pāya loka nimantraṇa dite
> sei vipre sādhe loka nimantraṇa nite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avasara]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---do not get the opportunity; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to offer invitations; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---unto that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:sādhe]]---request; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:n:nite]]---to accept the invitation.

===== Translation =====

**Since everyone did not get an opportunity to offer invitations to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally, they requested the Sanoḍiyā brāhmaṇa to ask the Lord to accept their invitations.**
