====== CC Madhya 18.159 ======

===== Text =====

> yāite eka vṛkṣa-tale prabhu sabā lañā
> vasilā, sabāra patha-śrānti dekhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāite]]---while passing; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---underneath a tree; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:p:patha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śrānti]]---fatigue because of walking; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---understanding.

===== Translation =====

**While walking, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, understanding that the others were fatigued, took them all beneath a tree and sat down.**
