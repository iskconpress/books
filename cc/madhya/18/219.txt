====== Cc. Madhya 18.219 ======

===== Text =====

> yei yei jana prabhura pāila daraśana
> sei preme matta haya, kare kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---person; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāila]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---got the sight; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---with ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:matta]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes overwhelmed; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever got to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would feel himself overwhelmed with ecstatic love and would begin to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra.**
