====== CC Madhya 18.90 ======

===== Text =====

> prāte prabhu-saṅge āilā jala-pātra lañā
> prabhu-saṅge rahe gṛha-strī-putra chāḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---carrying a waterpot; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]] [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]---home; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---wife; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---children; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---leaving aside.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning, Kṛṣṇadāsa went with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to Vṛndāvana and carried His waterpot. Kṛṣṇadāsa thus left his wife, home and children in order to remain with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
