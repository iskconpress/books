====== CC Madhya 18.180 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya āsi' prabhure dhari' vasāila
> mleccha-gaṇa dekhi' mahāprabhura 'bāhya' haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---immediately coming near; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---taking; [[synonyms:v:vasāila]]---made to sit; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the Mohammedan soldiers; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and made Him sit down. Seeing the Mohammedan soldiers, the Lord regained His normal senses.**
