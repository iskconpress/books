====== Cc. Madhya 18.148 ======

===== Text =====

> "sahite nā pāri āmi lokera gaḍabaḍi
> nimantraṇa lāgi' loka kare huḍāhuḍi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---cannot tolerate; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of people; [[synonyms:g:gaḍabaḍi]]---disturbance; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitations; [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:h:huḍāhuḍi]]---hurrying.

===== Translation =====

**Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya told the Lord, "I can no longer tolerate the disturbance of the crowd. People are coming one after another to offer invitations.**
