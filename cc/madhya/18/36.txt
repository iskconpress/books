====== CC Madhya 18.36 ======

===== Text =====

> sei grāme giyā kaila gopāla-daraśana
> premāveśe prabhu kare kīrtana-nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---to that village; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:n:nartana]]---chanting and dancing.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then went to the village of Gāṅṭhuli-grāma and saw the Lord Gopāla Deity. Overwhelmed by ecstatic love, He began to chant and dance.**
