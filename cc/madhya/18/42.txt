====== CC Madhya 18.42 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata gopālera karuṇa svabhāva
> yei bhakta janera dekhite haya 'bhāva'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of the Deity Gopāla; [[synonyms:k:karuṇa]] [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---kind behavior; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---which; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:j:janera]]---of persons who are devotees; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**This is the way of Lord Gopāla's kind behavior to His devotees. Seeing this, the devotees were overwhelmed by ecstatic love.**
