====== CC Madhya 18.169 ======

===== Text =====

> ei yati---āmāra guru, āmi---māthura brāhmaṇa
> pātsāra āge āche mora 'śata jana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:y:yati]]---this //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:g:guru]]---my spiritual master; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:m:māthura]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---a //brāhmaṇa// from Mathurā; [[synonyms:p:pātsāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in the service of the Mohammedan king; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---one hundred persons.

===== Translation =====

**"This sannyāsī is my spiritual master, and I am from Mathurā. I am a brāhmaṇa, and I know many people who are in the service of the Mohammedan king.**
