====== CC Madhya 18.20 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-prema-saundarya dekhi' loke camatkāra
> haridevera bhṛtya prabhura karila satkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---ecstatic love and beauty; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:devera]]---of Lord Harideva; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---servants; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:s:satkāra]]---offered a good reception.

===== Translation =====

**The people were astonished when they saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic love and personal beauty. The priests who served the Harideva Deity offered the Lord a good reception.**
