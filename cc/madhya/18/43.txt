====== Cc. Madhya 18.43 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhite utkaṇṭhā haya, nā caḍe govardhane
> kona chale gopāla āsi' utare āpane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was great anxiety; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:caḍe]]---does not go up; [[synonyms:g:govardhane]]---on the hill known as Govardhana; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:c:chale]]---by some trick; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Deity Gopāla; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:u:utare]]---descends; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very anxious to see Gopāla, but He did not want to climb Govardhana Hill. Therefore by some trick the Deity Gopāla personally descended.**
