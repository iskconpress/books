====== Cc. Madhya 18.177 ======

===== Text =====

> huṅkāra kariyā uṭhe, bale 'hari' 'hari'
> premāveśe nṛtya kare ūrdhva-bāhu kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:huṅ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---resounded very loudly; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---stands up; [[synonyms:b:bale]] [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---chants Hari, Hari; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---dances; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhva]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhu]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---raising his arms upward.

===== Translation =====

**Coming to His senses, the Lord very loudly began chanting the holy name, "Hari! Hari!" The Lord raised His arms upward and began to dance in ecstatic love.**
