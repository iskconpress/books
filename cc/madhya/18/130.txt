====== CC Madhya 18.130 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurāra yata loka brāhmaṇa sajjana
> bhaṭṭācārya-sthāne āsi' kare nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurāra]]---of Mathurā; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]] [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---gentlemen and //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---unto Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---offer invitations.

===== Translation =====

**Thus all the respectable people of Mathurā, headed by the brāhmaṇas, came to Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya and extended invitations to the Lord.**
