====== CC Madhya 18.181 ======

===== Text =====

> mleccha-gaṇa āsi' prabhura vandila caraṇa
> prabhu-āge kahe,---ei ṭhak cāri-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the Mohammedan soldiers; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---after coming there; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vandila]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---worshiped the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said before the Lord; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhak]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---these four persons are rogues.

===== Translation =====

**All the Mohammedan soldiers then came before the Lord, worshiped His lotus feet and said, "Here are four rogues.**
