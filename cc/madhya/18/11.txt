====== CC Madhya 18.11 ======

===== Text =====

> kuṇḍera 'mādhurī'---yena rādhāra 'madhurimā'
> kuṇḍera 'mahimā'---yena rādhāra 'mahimā'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍera]]---of the lake; [[synonyms:m:mādhurī]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:m:madhurimā]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍera]]---of the lake; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories.

===== Translation =====

**"The attraction of Rādhā-kuṇḍa is as sweet as that of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī. Similarly, the glories of the kuṇḍa [lake] are as glorious as Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
