====== CC Madhya 18.18 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mathurā'-padmera paścima-dale yāṅra vāsa
> 'harideva' nārāyaṇa---ādi parakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:p:padmera]]---of the lotus flower of Mathurā; [[synonyms:p:paścima]]-[[synonyms:d:dale]]---on the western petal; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---Lord Harideva; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---incarnation of Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---original; [[synonyms:p:parakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**Harideva is an incarnation of Nārāyaṇa, and His residence is on the western petal of the lotus of Mathurā.**
