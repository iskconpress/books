====== Cc. Madhya 18.204 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛpā kari' bala more 'sādhya-sādhane'
> eta bali' paḍe mahāprabhura caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by Your causeless mercy; [[synonyms:b:bala]]---speak; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to me; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]-[[synonyms:s:sādhane]]---the ultimate object of life and the process to achieve it; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, the saintly Mohammedan fell at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and requested Him to speak of life's ultimate goal and the process by which it could be obtained.**
