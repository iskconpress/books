====== CC Madhya 18.117 ======

===== Text =====

> loka kahe,---tomāte kabhu nahe jīva'-mati
> kṛṣṇera sadṛśa tomāra ākṛti-prakṛti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the people said; [[synonyms:t:tomāte]]---unto You; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there is not; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]---considering an ordinary living being; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sadṛśa]]---like Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:ā:ākṛti]]---bodily features; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---characteristics.

===== Translation =====

**After Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu explained the difference between an ordinary living being and the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the people said, "No one considers You an ordinary human being. You are like Kṛṣṇa in every respect, in both bodily features and characteristics.**
