====== Cc. Madhya 18.57 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ līlā-sthalī dekhi' gelā 'nandīśvara'
> 'nandīśvara' dekhi' preme ha-ilā vihvala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---at Kāmyavana; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthalī]]---all the places of pastimes; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---visiting; [[synonyms:g:gelā]] [[synonyms:n:nandīśvara]]---went to Nandīśvara; [[synonyms:n:nandīśvara]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---while seeing Nandīśvara; [[synonyms:p:preme]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---became overwhelmed by ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**After visiting the places of Kṛṣṇa's pastimes at Kāmyavana, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to Nandīśvara. While there, He was overwhelmed with ecstatic love.**

===== Purport =====

Nandīśvara is the house of Mahārāja Nanda.
