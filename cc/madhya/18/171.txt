====== Cc. Madhya 18.171 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣaṇeka ihāṅ vaisa, bāndhi' rākhaha sabāre
> iṅhāke puchiyā, tabe māriha sabāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeka]]---for some time; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]] [[synonyms:v:vaisa]]---sit down here; [[synonyms:b:bāndhi']]---arresting; [[synonyms:r:rākhaha]]---keep; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of us; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāke]] [[synonyms:p:puchiyā]]---after questioning Him; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:m:māriha]] [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---you can kill all of us.

===== Translation =====

**"Sit down here for a while and keep us all under arrest. When the sannyāsī regains his senses, you can question Him. Then, if you like, you can kill us all."**
