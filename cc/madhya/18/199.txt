====== CC Madhya 18.199 ======

===== Text =====

> mleccha kahe,---yei kaha, sei 'satya' haya
> śāstre likhiyāche, keha la-ite nā pāraya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mleccha]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Mohammedan replied; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---what You say; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is true; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in the Koran; [[synonyms:l:likhiyāche]]---it has been written; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---to take; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāraya]]---is not able.

===== Translation =====

**The saintly Mohammedan replied, "All that You have said is true. This has certainly been written in the Koran, but our scholars can neither understand nor accept it.**
