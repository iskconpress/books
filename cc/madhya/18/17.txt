====== CC Madhya 18.17 ======

===== Text =====

> preme matta cali' āilā govardhana-grāma
> 'harideva' dekhi' tāhāṅ ha-ilā praṇāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---proceeding; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]---to the village known as Govardhana; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---the Deity named Harideva installed there; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---offered obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Mad with ecstatic love, the Lord came to the village known as Govardhana. It was there that He saw the Deity Harideva and offered His obeisances unto Him.**
