====== CC Madhya 18.155 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle mahāprabhu prātaḥ-snāna kaila
> 'vṛndāvana chāḍiba' jāni' premāveśa haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---took His morning bath; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍiba]]---I shall have to leave Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became ecstatic in love.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up early. After taking His bath, He became ecstatic with love, knowing that He now had to leave Vṛndāvana.**
