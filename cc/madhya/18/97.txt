====== Cc. Madhya 18.97 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-āge kahe loka,---śrī-kṛṣṇa dekhila
> 'sarasvatī' ei vākye 'satya' kahāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people began to say; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---that they have seen Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---the goddess of learning; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:v:vākye]]---this statement; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---true; [[synonyms:k:kahāila]]---caused the people to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone came before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and said, "Now we have directly seen Lord Kṛṣṇa." Thus by the mercy of the goddess of learning they were made to speak the truth.**
