====== CC Madhya 18.93 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu dekhi' karila loka caraṇa vandana
> prabhu kahe,---kāhāṅ haite karilā āgamana?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---offered; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---respect unto His lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from where; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---have you come.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the people offered respects at His lotus feet. The Lord then asked them, "Where are you all coming from?"**
