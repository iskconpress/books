====== CC Madhya 18.192 ======

===== Text =====

> sṛṣṭi, sthiti, pralaya tāṅhā haite haya
> sthūla-sūkṣma-jagatera teṅho samāśraya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---creation; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---maintenance; [[synonyms:p:pralaya]]---dissolution; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---Him; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes possible; [[synonyms:s:sthūla]]---gross; [[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---subtle; [[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:s:samāśraya]]---the only shelter.

===== Translation =====

**"Creation, maintenance and dissolution come from Him. He is the original shelter of all gross and subtle cosmic manifestations.**
