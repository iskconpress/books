====== Cc. Madhya 18.74 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-raṅge sei-dina tathā goṅāilā
> sandhyā-kāle akrūre āsi' bhikṣā nirvāhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in this amusement; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---that day; [[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:g:goṅāilā]]---passed the day there; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:a:akrūre]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---returning to Akrūra-tīrtha; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:n:nirvāhilā]]---took His meal.

===== Translation =====

**Being thus transcendentally amused, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed that day happily at Keśī-tīrtha. In the evening He returned to Akrūra-tīrtha, where He took His meal.**
