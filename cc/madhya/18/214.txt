====== CC Madhya 18.214 ======

===== Text =====

> soro-kṣetre āsi' prabhu kailā gaṅgā-snāna
> gaṅgā-tīra-pathe kailā prayāge prayāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:soro]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---to Soro-kṣetra; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing in the Ganges; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the path on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]] [[synonyms:p:prayāṇa]]---departure for Prayāga.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu next went to a holy place of pilgrimage called Soro-kṣetra. He took His bath in the Ganges there and started for Prayāga on the path along the banks of the Ganges.**
