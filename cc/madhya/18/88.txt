====== CC Madhya 18.88 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu tāṅre kṛpā kailā āliṅgana kari
> preme matta haila sei nāce, bale 'hari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---bestowed His mercy; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:matta]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became mad; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---chants; [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then bestowed upon Kṛṣṇadāsa His causeless mercy by embracing him. Kṛṣṇadāsa became mad with ecstatic love and began to dance and chant the holy name of Hari.**
