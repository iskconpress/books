====== CC Madhya 18.163 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle tāhāṅ āśoyāra daśa āilā
> mleccha-pāṭhāna ghoḍā haite uttarilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---just at this time; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āśoyāra]]---soldiers; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---ten; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]---Mohammedans; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāna]]---the race of Pāṭhānas; [[synonyms:g:ghoḍā]]---horses; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:u:uttarilā]]---got down.

===== Translation =====

**While the Lord was unconscious, ten cavalry soldiers belonging to the Mohammedan Pāṭhāna military order rode up and dismounted.**
