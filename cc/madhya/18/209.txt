====== CC Madhya 18.209 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa' bali' paḍe sei mahāprabhura pāya
> prabhu śrī-caraṇa dila tāṅhāra māthāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that Vijulī Khān; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---placed His foot; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:m:māthāya]]---on his head.

===== Translation =====

**Vijulī Khān also fell down at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord placed His foot on his head.**
