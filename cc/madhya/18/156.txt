====== CC Madhya 18.156 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhya vikāra nāhi, premāviṣṭa mana
> bhaṭṭācārya kahe,---cala, yāi mahāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---symptoms; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there were not; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind was full of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:c:cala]]---let us go; [[synonyms:y:yāi]] [[synonyms:m:mahāvana]]---let us go to Mahāvana.

===== Translation =====

**Although the Lord did not exhibit any external symptoms, His mind was filled with ecstatic love. At that time, Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Let us go to Mahāvana [Gokula]."**
