~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 18.41~~

====== CC Madhya 18.41 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla mandire gelā, prabhu rahilā tale
> prabhura vāñchā pūrṇa saba karila gopāle

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Deity Gopāla; [[synonyms:m:mandire]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned to His temple; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]] [[synonyms:t:tale]]---remain at the bottom; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---desires; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:g:gopāle]]---the Deity Gopāla.

===== Translation =====

**The Deity Gopāla then returned to His own temple, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at the bottom of the hill. Thus all the desires of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were satisfied by the Deity Gopāla.**
