====== CC Madhya 18.33 ======

===== Text =====

> govardhana dekhi' prabhu premāviṣṭa hañā
> nācite nācite calilā śloka paḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govardhana]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming ecstatic in love; [[synonyms:n:nācite]] [[synonyms:n:nācite]]---dancing and dancing; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---reciting the following verse.

===== Translation =====

**Just by seeing Govardhana Hill, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became ecstatic with love of Kṛṣṇa. While dancing and dancing, He recited the following verse.**
