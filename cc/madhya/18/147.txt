====== Cc. Madhya 18.147 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-tīra-pathe sukha jānāiha tāṅre
> bhaṭṭācārya āsi' tabe kahila prabhure

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]]---on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the path; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:j:jānāiha]]---kindly let know; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:kahila]] [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**"Tell the Lord of the happiness you will feel in traveling via the banks of the Ganges." Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya therefore submitted this prayer to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
