====== Cc. Madhya 18.6 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' saba grāmya-lokera vismaya haila mana
> preme prabhu kare rādhā-kuṇḍera stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:g:grāmya]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all the people of the village; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became astonished; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍera]]---of Rādhā-kuṇḍa; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---prayers.

===== Translation =====

**When the people of the village saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu taking His bath in those two ponds in the middle of the paddy fields, they were very astonished. The Lord then offered His prayers to Śrī Rādhā-kuṇḍa.**
