====== CC Madhya 18.35 ======

===== Text =====

> 'govinda-kuṇḍādi' tīrthe prabhu kailā snāna
> tāhāṅ śunilā---gopāla gela gāṅṭhuli grāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Govinda-kuṇḍa and others; [[synonyms:t:tīrthe]]---in the holy places; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---performed bathing; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---heard; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Gopāla Deity; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---has gone; [[synonyms:g:gāṅṭhuli]]---Gāṅṭhuli; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]---to the village.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then took His bath in a lake called Govinda-kuṇḍa, and while He was there, He heard that the Deity Gopāla had already gone to Gāṅṭhuli-grāma.**
