====== CC Madhya 18.120 ======

===== Text =====

> alaukika 'prakṛti' tomāra---buddhi-agocara
> tomā dekhi' kṛṣṇa-preme jagat pāgala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---characteristics; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]-[[synonyms:a:agocara]]---beyond our imagination; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing You; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:p:pāgala]]---mad.

===== Translation =====

**"Indeed, Your characteristics are uncommon and beyond the imagination of an ordinary living being. Simply by seeing You, the entire universe becomes mad with ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
