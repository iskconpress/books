====== CC Madhya 18.217 ======

===== Text =====

> mleccha-deśa, keha kāhāṅ karaye utpāta
> bhaṭṭācārya---paṇḍita, kahite nā jānena vāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---this is a country occupied by the Mohammedans; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---anywhere; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:u:utpāta]]---can create a disturbance; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned scholar; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---does not know; [[synonyms:v:vāt]]---language.

===== Translation =====

**"This country is mainly occupied by Mohammedans. At any place someone can create a disturbance, and although Your companion Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya is a learned scholar, he does not know how to speak the local language."**
