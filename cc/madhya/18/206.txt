====== CC Madhya 18.206 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa' kaha, 'kṛṣṇa' kaha,---kailā upadeśa
> sabe'kṛṣṇa' kahe, sabāra haila premāveśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---just chant "Kṛṣṇa"; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---just chant "Kṛṣṇa"; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu instructed; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then told all the Mohammedans there, "Chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa! Chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa!" As they all began to chant, they were overwhelmed by ecstatic love.**
