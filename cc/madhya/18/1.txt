====== CC Madhya 18.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvane sthira-carān
> nandayan svāvalokanaiḥ
> ātmānaṁ ca tad-ālokād
> gaurāṅgaḥ parito 'bhramat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:sthira]]-[[synonyms:c:carān]]---to the living entities, both moving and not moving; [[synonyms:n:nandayan]]---giving pleasure; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:a:avalokanaiḥ]]---by His personal glances; [[synonyms:ā:ātmānam]]---to Himself; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālokāt]]---by seeing them; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅgaḥ]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:paritaḥ]]---all around; [[synonyms:a:abhramat]]---traveled.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu traveled all over Vṛndāvana and pleased all living entities, moving and nonmoving, with His glances. The Lord took much personal pleasure in seeing everyone. In this way Lord Gaurāṅga traveled in Vṛndāvana.**
