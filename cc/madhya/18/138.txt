====== Cc. Madhya 18.138 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' kṛṣṇadāsa kāndi' phukāra karila
> bhaṭṭācārya śīghra āsi' prabhure uṭhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:k:kāndi']]---crying; [[synonyms:p:phu]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---called loudly; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---hastily; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhāila]]---raised Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**When Kṛṣṇadāsa saw that Caitanya Mahāprabhu was drowning, he cried and shouted very loudly. Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya immediately came and pulled the Lord out.**
