====== Cc. Madhya 18.4 ======

===== Text =====

> āriṭe rādhā-kuṇḍa-vārtā puche loka-sthāne
> keha nāhi kahe, saṅgera brāhmaṇa nā jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āriṭe]]---in the village known as Āriṭ-grāma; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news of Rādhākuṇḍa; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquires; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---from the local people; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---could say; [[synonyms:s:saṅgera]]---the companion; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked the local people, "Where is Rādhā-kunda?" No one could inform Him, and the brāhmaṇa accompanying Him did not know either.**
