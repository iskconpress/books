====== CC Madhya 18.103 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle bhavya-loka prabhu-sthāne āilā
> 'kṛṣṇa dekhi' āilā?'---prabhu tāṅhāre puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---the next morning; [[synonyms:b:bhavya]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---respectable gentlemen; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---have you come; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired from them.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning some respectable gentlemen came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord asked them, "Have you seen Kṛṣṇa?"**
