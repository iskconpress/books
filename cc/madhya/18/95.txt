====== CC Madhya 18.95 ======

===== Text =====

> sākṣāt dekhila loka---nāhika saṁśaya
> śuni' hāsi' kahe prabhu,---saba 'satya' haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people saw; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]]---there is no doubt; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---laughing; [[synonyms:k:kahe]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---all that you have said is correct.

===== Translation =====

**"Everyone has seen Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself. There is no doubt about it." Hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to laugh. He then said, "Everything is correct."**
