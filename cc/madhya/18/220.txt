====== CC Madhya 18.220 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra saṅge anyonye, tāṅra saṅge āna
> ei-mata 'vaiṣṇava' kailā saba deśa-grāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:a:anyonye]]---other; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---and with him; [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---another; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:deśa]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]---villages and towns.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became a Vaiṣṇava, and whoever met that Vaiṣṇava also became a Vaiṣṇava. In this way, all the towns and villages one after the other became Vaiṣṇava.**
