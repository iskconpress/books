====== CC Madhya 18.21 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya 'brahma-kuṇḍe' pāka yāñā kaila
> brahma-kuṇḍe snāna kari' prabhu bhikṣā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍe]]---at the lake called Brahma-kuṇḍa; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]---cooking; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going there; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---peformed; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍe]]---at Brahma-kuṇḍa; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking a bath; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---accepted lunch.

===== Translation =====

**At Brahma-kuṇḍa, Bhaṭṭācārya cooked food, and the Lord, after taking His bath at Brahma-kuṇḍa, accepted His lunch.**
