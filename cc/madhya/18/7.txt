====== CC Madhya 18.7 ======

===== Text =====

> saba gopī haite rādhā kṛṣṇera preyasī
> taiche rādhā-kuṇḍa priya 'priyāra sarasi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:preyasī]]---most beloved; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍa]]---Rādhā-kuṇḍa; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---very dear; [[synonyms:p:priyāra]] [[synonyms:s:sarasī]]---the lake of the most beloved Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**"Of all the gopīs, Rādhārāṇī is the dearmost. Similarly, the lake known as Rādhā-kuṇḍa is very dear to the Lord because it is very dear to Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
