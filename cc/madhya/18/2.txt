====== Cc. Madhya 18.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya gauracandra jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaita-candra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---to Lord Gauracandra (Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu); [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to Śrī Advaita Gosāñi; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---to the devotees of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Gauracandra! All glories to Nityānanda Prabhu! All glories to Advaita Prabhu! And all glories to the devotees of Lord Caitanya headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura!**
