====== CC Madhya 18.86 ======

===== Text =====

> rājaputa-jāti muñi, o-pāre mora ghara
> mora icchā haya---'haṅa vaiṣṇava-kiṅkara'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājaputa]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---belong to the Rājaputa caste; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:o:o]]-[[synonyms:p:pāre]]---on the other side of the Yamunā; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---my residence; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---I wish; [[synonyms:h:haṅa]]---to become; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:k:kiṅkara]]---the servant of a Vaiṣṇava.

===== Translation =====

**"I belong to the Rājaputa caste, and my home is just on the other side of the River Yamunā. However, I wish to be the servant of a Vaiṣṇava.**
