====== Cc. Madhya 18.77 ======

===== Text =====

> nikaṭe yamunā vahe śītala samīra
> vṛndāvana-śobhā dekhe yamunāra nīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near the Teṅtulī-talā, or Āmli-talā; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]---the Yamunā; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---flows; [[synonyms:ś:śītala]] [[synonyms:s:samīra]]---very cool breeze; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:ś:śobhā]]---the beauty of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:y:yamunāra]]---of the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:n:nīra]]---water.

===== Translation =====

**Since the River Yamunā flowed near Teṅtulī-talā, a very cool breeze blew there. While there, the Lord saw the beauty of Vṛndāvana and the water of the River Yamunā.**
