====== Cc. Madhya 18.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe cali' āilā prabhu 'sumanaḥ-sarovara'
> tāhāṅ 'govardhana' dekhi' ha-ilā vihvala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---traveling; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sumanaḥ]]-[[synonyms:s:sarovara]]---to the lake known as Sumanaḥ; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]---Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---became overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**From Rādhā-kuṇḍa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to Sumanaḥ Lake. When He saw Govardhana Hill from there, He was overwhelmed by joy.**
