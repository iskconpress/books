====== CC Madhya 18.16 ======

===== Text =====

> govardhana dekhi' prabhu ha-ilā daṇḍavat
> 'eka śilā' āliṅgiyā ha-ilā unmatta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govardhana]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---offered obeisances like a straight rod; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:ś:śilā]]---one piece of stone; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:u:unmatta]]---maddened.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord saw Govardhana Hill, He immediately offered obeisances, falling down on the ground like a rod. He embraced one piece of rock from Govardhana Hill and became mad.**
