====== CC Madhya 18.202 ======

===== Text =====

> aneka dekhinu muñi mleccha-śāstra haite
> 'sādhya-sādhana-vastu' nāri nirdhārite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aneka]]---many; [[synonyms:d:dekhinu]]---have studied; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---Mohammedan scripture; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]---the ultimate goal of life; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---how to approach it; [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---matter; [[synonyms:n:nāri]] [[synonyms:n:nirdhārite]]---I cannot decide conclusively.

===== Translation =====

**"I have studied the Mohammedan scripture very extensively, but from it I cannot conclusively decide what the ultimate goal of life is or how I can approach it.**
