====== Cc. Madhya 18.104 ======

===== Text =====

> loka kahe,---rātrye kaivartya naukāte caḍiyā
> kālīya-dahe matsya māre, deuṭī jvāliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the sensible respectable persons said; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:k:kaivartya]]---a fisherman; [[synonyms:n:naukāte]]---on a boat; [[synonyms:c:caḍiyā]]---getting up; [[synonyms:k:kālīya]]-[[synonyms:d:dahe]]---in the lake of Kālīya; [[synonyms:m:matsya]] [[synonyms:m:māre]]---catches fish; [[synonyms:d:deuṭī]] [[synonyms:j:jvāliyā]]---lighting a torch.

===== Translation =====

**These respectable gentlemen replied, "At night in the Kālīya Lake a fisherman lighting a torch in his boat catches many fish.**
