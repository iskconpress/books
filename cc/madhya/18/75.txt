====== Cc. Madhya 18.75 ======

===== Text =====

> prāte vṛndāvane kailā 'cīra-ghāṭe' snāna
> teṅtulī-talāte āsi' karilā viśrāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:c:cīra]]-[[synonyms:g:ghāṭe]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing at Cīra-ghāṭa; [[synonyms:t:teṅtulī]]-[[synonyms:t:talāte]]---underneath the Teṅtulī tree; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---took rest.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Vṛndāvana and took His bath at Cīra-ghāṭa. He then went to Teṅtulī-talā, where He took rest.**
