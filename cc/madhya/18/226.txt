====== CC Madhya 18.226 ======

===== Text =====

> ādyopānta caitanya-līlā---'alaukika' jāna'
> śraddhā kari' śuna ihā, 'satya' kari' māna'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ādya]]-[[synonyms:u:upānta]]---from beginning to end; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:alaukika]] [[synonyms:j:jāna']]---everyone should know as uncommon; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with faith; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]] [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---hear this; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:m:māna']]---accepting it as true and correct.

===== Translation =====

**From beginning to end the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are uncommon. Just hear them with faith and accept them as true and correct.**
