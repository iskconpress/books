====== CC Madhya 18.64 ======

===== Text =====

> līlā-sthala dekhi' tāhāṅ gelā 'śeṣaśāyī'
> 'lakṣmī' dekhi' ei śloka paḍena gosāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthala]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the places of pastimes; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---departed; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---for seeing Śeṣaśāyī; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍena]]---recites; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the places of Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes, Śrī Caitanya went to Śeṣaśāyī, where He saw Lakṣmī and recited the following verse.**
