====== CC Madhya 18.188 ======

===== Text =====

> yei yei kahila, prabhu sakali khaṇḍila
> uttara nā āise mukhe, mahā-stabdha haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---whatever he spoke; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sakali]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍila]]---refuted everything; [[synonyms:u:uttara]]---answer; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---could not come; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in his mouth; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:stabdha]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---he became greatly stunned.

===== Translation =====

**Whatever arguments he put forward, the Lord refuted them all. Finally the person became stunned and could not speak.**
