====== CC Madhya 18.176 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā pāṭhāna mane saṅkoca ha-ila
> hena-kāle mahāprabhu 'caitanya' pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāna]]---the Mohammedan soldiers; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:saṅkoca]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was a little hesitation; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---came to his senses.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this challenge, the Pāṭhāna soldiers became hesitant. Then suddenly Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu regained consciousness.**
