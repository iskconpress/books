====== Cc. Madhya 18.193 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-śreṣṭha, sarvārādhya, kāraṇera kāraṇa
> tāṅra bhaktye haya jīvera saṁsāra-tāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---the Supreme Truth; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārādhya]]---worshipable by everyone; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇera]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of all causes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---by devotional service; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of the living entity; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]-[[synonyms:t:tāraṇa]]---deliverance from material existence.

===== Translation =====

**"The Lord is the Supreme Truth worshipable by everyone. He is the cause of all causes. By engaging in His devotional service, the living entity is relieved from material existence.**
