====== Cc. Madhya 18.173 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇadāsa kahe,---āmāra ghara ei grāme
> dui-śata turkī āche, śateka kāmāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rājaputa Kṛṣṇadāsa said; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---my home; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in this village; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:t:turkī]]---two hundred Turks; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---I have; [[synonyms:ś:śateka]] [[synonyms:k:kāmāne]]---one hundred cannons.

===== Translation =====

**Rājaputa Kṛṣṇadāsa said, "I have my home here, and I also have about two hundred Turkish soldiers and about one hundred cannons.**
