====== CC Madhya 18.140 ======

===== Text =====

> āji āmi āchilāṅa uṭhāiluṅ prabhure
> vṛndāvane ḍubena yadi, ke uṭhābe tāṅre?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:ā:āchilāṅa]]---was present; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāiluṅ]]---raised; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubena]] [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if He drowns; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhābe]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---who will raise Him.

===== Translation =====

**Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Since I was present today, it was possible for me to pull the Lord up. However, if He starts to drown at Vṛndāvana, who will help Him?**
