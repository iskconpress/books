====== CC Madhya 18.208 ======

===== Text =====

> alpa vayasa tāṅra, rājāra kumāra
> 'rāmadāsa' ādi pāṭhāna---cākara tāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alpa]] [[synonyms:v:vayasa]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his age is very young; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]] [[synonyms:k:kumāra]]---son of the king; [[synonyms:r:rāmadāsa]]---Rāmadāsa; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---heading the list; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāna]]---the Mohammedans; [[synonyms:c:cākara]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---servants of him.

===== Translation =====

**Vijulī Khān was very young, and he was the son of the king. All the other Mohammedans, Pāṭhānas, headed by Rāmadāsa, were his servants.**
