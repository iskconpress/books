====== CC Madhya 18.69 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gokula' dekhiyā āilā 'mathurā'-nagare
> janma-sthāna' dekhi' rahe sei vipra-ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gokula]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing Gokula; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:n:nagare]]---in the city of Mathurā; [[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---the birthplace of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---stays; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house of the Sanoḍiyā //brāhmaṇa.//

===== Translation =====

**After seeing Gokula, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Mathurā, where He saw the birthplace of the Lord. While there, He stayed at the house of the Sanoḍiyā brāhmaṇa.**
