====== Cc. Madhya 18.128 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata kata-dina 'akrūre' rahilā
> kṛṣṇa-nāma-prema diyā loka nistārilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---for some days; [[synonyms:a:akrūre]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---stayed at Akrūra-tīrtha; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---distributing; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---everyone; [[synonyms:n:nistārilā]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained for some days in Akrūra-tīrtha. He delivered everyone there simply by distributing the holy name of Kṛṣṇa and ecstatic love for the Lord.**
