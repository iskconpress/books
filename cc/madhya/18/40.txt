====== CC Madhya 18.40 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla saṅge cali' āilā nṛtya-gīta kari
> ānanda-kolāhale loka bale 'hari' 'hari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Gopāla; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---walking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:k:kolāhale]]---in great jubilation; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---say; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---Hari, Hari.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya Mahāprabhu walked with the Deity of Gopāla, and He chanted and danced. A large and jubilant crowd of people also chanted the transcendental name of Kṛṣṇa, "Hari! Hari!"**
