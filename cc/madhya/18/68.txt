====== CC Madhya 18.68 ======

===== Text =====

> yamalārjuna-bhaṅgādi dekhila sei sthala
> premāveśe prabhura mana haila ṭalamala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yamala]]-[[synonyms:a:arjuna]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅga]]---the place where the twin //arjuna// trees were broken; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sthala]]---that place; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭalamala]]---agitated.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing the place where the twin arjuna trees were broken by Śrī Kṛṣṇa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was moved to great ecstatic love.**
