====== Cc. Madhya 18.54 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-māsa rahi' gopāla gelā nija-sthāne
> śrī-rūpa-gosāñi āilā śrī-vṛndāvane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying for one month; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Deity Gopāla; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to His own place; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**After staying at Mathurā for one month, the Deity Gopāla returned to His own place, and Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī returned to Vṛndāvana.**
