====== CC Madhya 18.19 ======

===== Text =====

> harideva-āge nāce preme matta hañā
> saba loka dekhite āila āścarya śuniyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Harideva; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:matta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming maddened; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing.

===== Translation =====

**Mad with ecstatic love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to dance before the Harideva Deity. Hearing of the Lord's wonderful activities, all the people came to see Him.**
