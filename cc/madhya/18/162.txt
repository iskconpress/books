====== Cc. Madhya 18.162 ======

===== Text =====

> acetana hañā prabhu bhūmite paḍilā
> mukhe phenā paḍe, nāsāya śvāsa ruddha hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:acetana]]---unconscious; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]] [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell on the ground; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---at the mouth; [[synonyms:p:phenā]] [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---there was foam; [[synonyms:n:nāsāya]]---in the nostrils; [[synonyms:ś:śvāsa]]---breath; [[synonyms:r:ruddha]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---stopped.

===== Translation =====

**Filled with ecstatic love, the Lord fell unconscious to the ground. He foamed about the mouth, and His breathing stopped.**
