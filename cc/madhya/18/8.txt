====== Cc. Madhya 18.8 ======

===== Text =====

> yathā rādhā priyā viṣṇos
> tasyāḥ kuṇḍaṁ priyaṁ tathā
> sarva-gopīṣu saivaikā
> viṣṇor atyanta-vallabhā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:p:priyā]]---beloved; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]]---Her; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍam]]---lake; [[synonyms:p:priyam]]---very dear; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---similarly; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:g:gopīṣu]]---among all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---She; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:e:ekā]]---alone; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---very much; [[synonyms:v:vallabhā]]---dear.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is most dear to Lord Kṛṣṇa, and Her lake known as Rādhā-kuṇḍa is also very dear to Him. Of all the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is certainly the most beloved.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from the //Padma Purāṇa.//
