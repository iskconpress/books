====== CC Madhya 18.153 ======

===== Text =====

> "tumi āmāya āni' dekhāilā vṛndāvana
> ei 'ṛṇa' āmi nāriba karite śodhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:ā:āmāya]]---Me; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dekhāilā]]---showed; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---the holy place named Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṇa]]---this debt; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:n:nāriba]]---I shall not be able; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:ś:śodhana]]---to repay.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You have brought Me here to show Me Vṛndāvana. I am very much indebted to you, and I shall not be able to repay this debt.**
