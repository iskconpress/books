====== Cc. Madhya 25.153 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva bhāgavata karaha vicāra
> ihā haite pābe sūtra-śrutira artha-sāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---try to understand scrutinizingly; [[synonyms:i:ihā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from this; [[synonyms:p:pābe]]---you will get; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śrutira]]---of the Vedic philosophy, the //Brahma-sūtra//; [[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the actual meaning.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu advised Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī, "Study Śrīmad Bhāgavatam very scrutinizingly. Then you will understand the actual meaning of Brahma-sūtra"**
