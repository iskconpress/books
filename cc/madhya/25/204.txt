====== Cc. Madhya 25.204 ======

===== Text =====

> śuṣka-kāṣṭha āni' rāya vece mathurāte
> pāṅca chaya paisā haya eka eka bojhāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]-[[synonyms:k:kāṣṭha]] [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---collecting dry wood from the forest; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vece]]---sells; [[synonyms:m:mathurāte]]---at Mathurā; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]] [[synonyms:c:chaya]]---five or six; [[synonyms:p:paisā]]---paise; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:bojhāte]]---in exchange for each load of dry wood.

===== Translation =====

**Subuddhi Rāya would collect dry wood in the forest and take it to the city of Mathurā to sell. For each load he would receive five or six paise.**
