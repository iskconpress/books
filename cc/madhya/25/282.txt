====== Cc. Madhya 25.282 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīman-madana-gopāla-
> govindadeva-tuṣṭaye
> caitanyārpitam astv etac
> caitanya-caritāmṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīman]]-[[synonyms:m:madana]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---of the Deity named Śrīman Madana-gopāla; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---of the Deity named Govindadeva; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭaye]]---for the satisfaction; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:a:arpitam]]---offered unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:astu]]---let it be; [[synonyms:e:etat]]---this; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritāmṛtam]]---the book known as //Caitanya-caritāmṛta.//

===== Translation =====

**For the satisfaction of Śrī Madana-gopāla and Govindadeva, we pray that this book, Caitanya-caritāmṛta, may be offered to Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
