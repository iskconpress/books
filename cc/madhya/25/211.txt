====== CC Madhya 25.211 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurāte subuddhi-rāya tāhāre mililā
> rūpa-anupama-kathā sakali kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurāte]]---at Mathurā; [[synonyms:s:subuddhi]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met him; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:a:anupama]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---news about his younger brothers, Rūpa Gosvāmī and Anupama; [[synonyms:s:sakali]]---everything; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---described.

===== Translation =====

**When Sanātana Gosvāmī met Subuddhi Rāya at Mathurā, Subuddhi Rāya explained everything about his younger brothers Rūpa Gosvāmī and Anupama.**
