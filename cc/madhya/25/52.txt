====== CC Madhya 25.52 ======

===== Text =====

> 'pātañjala' kahe,-'īśvara haya svarūpa-jñāna'
> veda-mate kahe tāṅre 'svayaṁ-bhagavān'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pātañjala]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Pātañjala philosophers say; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---the Supreme Lord is; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---self-realization; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in the Vedic version; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---they say; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to Him; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"The Pātañjala philosophers say that when one is self-realized, he understands the Lord. Similarly, according to the Vedas and Vedic principles, the original cause is the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
