====== Cc. Madhya 25.46 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' sei kare kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana
> śuni' prakāśānanda kichu kahena vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---speaking so much; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---the disciple of Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---the chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prakāśānanda]]---the //guru,// Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, the disciple of Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī began to chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa. Hearing this, Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī made the following statement.**
