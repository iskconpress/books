====== Cc. Madhya 25.110 ======

===== Text =====

> sṛṣṭira pūrve ṣaḍ-aiśvarya-pūrṇa āmi ta' ha-iye
> 'prapañca', 'prakṛti', 'puruṣa' āmātei laye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭira]] [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---before the creation of this cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---full of six opulences; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iye]]---indeed existed; [[synonyms:p:prapañca]]---the total material energy; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---material nature; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the living entities; [[synonyms:ā:āmātei]] [[synonyms:l:laye]]---were all existing in Me.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Before the creation of the cosmic manifestation,' the Lord said, 'I existed, and the total material energy, material nature and the living entities all existed in Me.**
