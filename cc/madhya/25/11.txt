====== Cc. Madhya 25.11 ======

===== Text =====

> eta cinti' nimantrila sannyāsīra gaṇe
> tabe sei vipra āila mahāprabhura sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:c:cinti']]---thinking this; [[synonyms:n:nimantrila]]---he invited; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---approached; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking like this, the Mahārāṣṭrian brāhmaṇa extended an invitation to all the sannyāsīs of Vārāṇasī. After doing this, he finally approached Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to extend Him an invitation.**
