====== CC Madhya 25.13 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-duḥkha dekhi' prabhu manete cintila
> sannyāsīra mana phirāite mana ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the unhappiness of the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:manete]] [[synonyms:c:cintila]]---considered within His mind; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds of the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:p:phirāite]]---to turn; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu decided.

===== Translation =====

**They submitted their request, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, seeing His devotees' unhappiness, decided to turn the minds of the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs.**
