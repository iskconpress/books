====== CC Madhya 25.74 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi tomāre saba brahma-sama bhāse
> loka-śikṣā lāgi' aiche karite nā āise'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---for you; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everyone; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal to the impersonal Brahman; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---appears; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the enlightenment of people in general; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in such a way; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---you should not present yourself.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear sir, for you everyone is on the level of impersonal Brahman, but for the enlightenment of people in general you should not behave in that way."**
