====== CC Madhya 25.62 ======

===== Text =====

> mādhava-saundarya dekhi' āviṣṭa ha-ilā
> aṅganete āsi' preme nācite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:s:saundarya]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing the beauty of Lord Bindu Mādhava; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became ecstatic in love; [[synonyms:a:aṅganete]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming to the courtyard; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in great love; [[synonyms:n:nācite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to dance.

===== Translation =====

**Upon reaching the temple of Bindu Mādhava, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, seeing the beauty of Lord Bindu Mādhava, became overwhelmed in ecstatic love. He then began to dance in the courtyard of the temple.**
