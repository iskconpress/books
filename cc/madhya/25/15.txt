====== CC Madhya 25.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu tāṅra nimantraṇa mānilā
> āra dina madhyāhna kari' tāṅra ghare gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:m:mānilā]]---accepted; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after finishing His //madhyāhna// activities (taking bath and murmuring //mantras//); [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---He went to the house of the Mahārāṣṭrian //brāhmaṇa.//

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted his invitation, and the next day, after finishing His noontime activities, He went to the brāhmaṇa's house.**
