====== CC Madhya 25.44 ======

===== Text =====

> vyāsa-sūtrera artha ācārya kariyāche ācchādana
> ei haya satya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the codes of the Vedānta known as //Vyāsa-sūtra//; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meanings; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Śaṅkarācārya; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]] [[synonyms:ā:ācchādana]]---has purposely covered; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---the truth; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the words and explanation given by Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**"The conclusion is that the import of the Vedānta-sūtra is covered by the imaginary explanation of Śaṅkarācārya. Whatever Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu has said is perfectly true.**
