~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 25.234~~

====== CC Madhya 25.234 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mahāprabhu āilā'-grāme kolāhala haila
> sārvabhauma, rāmānanda, vāṇīnātha milila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has arrived; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the town; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was spreading of the news; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:m:milila]]---came and met Him.

===== Translation =====

**When the news spread that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had arrived at Jagannātha Purī, devotees like Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Rāmānanda Rāya and Vāṇīnātha Rāya all came to meet Him.**
