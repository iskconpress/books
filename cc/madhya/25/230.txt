====== Cc. Madhya 25.230 ======

===== Text =====

> āra saba bhakta prabhura caraṇe paḍilā
> sabā āliṅgiyā prabhu premāviṣṭa hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the other devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing all of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---overwhelmed in ecstatic love and emotion; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**All the other devotees also came and fell down at the Lord's lotus feet. In return, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced them all with great ecstatic love.**
