====== Cc. Madhya 25.30 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakti vinā mukti nahe, bhāgavate kaya
> kali-kāle nāmābhāse sukhe mukti haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without devotional service; [[synonyms:m:mukti]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there is no question of liberation; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]] [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---it is said in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in this Age of Kali; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāse]]---even by a slight appreciation of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---without difficulty; [[synonyms:m:mukti]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---one can get liberation.

===== Translation =====

**"In this Age of Kali, one cannot attain liberation without taking to the devotional service of the Lord. In this age, even if one does not chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa perfectly, he still attains liberation very easily.**
