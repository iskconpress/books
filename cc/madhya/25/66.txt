====== Cc. Madhya 25.66 ======

===== Text =====

> nikaṭe hari-dhvani śuni' parakāśānanda
> dekhite kautuke āilā lañā śiṣya-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---nearby; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing the chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra; [[synonyms:p:parakāśānanda]]---Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:k:kautuke]]---in great eagerness; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---all the disciples.

===== Translation =====

**When Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī, who was staying nearby, heard this tumultuous chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, he and his disciples immediately came to see the Lord.**
