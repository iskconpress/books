====== Cc. Madhya 25.255 ======

===== Text =====

> ṣoḍaśe-vṛndāvana-yātrā gauḍa-deśa-pathe
> punaḥ nīlācale āilā, nāṭaśālā haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣoḍaśe]]---in the Sixteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---departure for visiting Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way through the province of Bengal; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaśālā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Kānāi Nāṭaśālā.

===== Translation =====

**In the Sixteenth Chapter Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed for Vṛndāvana and journeyed through Bengal. He later returned to Jagannātha Purī from Kānāi Nāṭaśālā.**
