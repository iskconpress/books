====== CC Madhya 25.47 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryera āgraha-'advaita-vāda' sthāpite
> tāte sūtrārtha vyākhyā kare anya rīte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Śaṅkarācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]]---the eagerness; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:v:vāda]]---monism; [[synonyms:s:sthāpite]]---to establish; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---for that reason; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning of the //Brahma-sūtra,// or Vedānta philosophy; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyā]]---explanation; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:r:rīte]]---in a different way.

===== Translation =====

**Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī said, "Śaṅkarācārya was very eager to establish the philosophy of monism. Therefore he explained Vedānta-sūtra, or Vedānta philosophy, in a different way to support monistic philosophy.**
