====== CC Madhya 25.53 ======

===== Text =====

> chayera chaya mata vyāsa kailā āvartana
> sei saba sūtra lañā 'vedānta'-varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:chayera]]---of the six philosophers; [[synonyms:c:chaya]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---six different theses; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āvartana]]---analyzed fully; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---the codes; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:v:vedānta]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---explaining the Vedānta philosophy.

===== Translation =====

**"After studying the six philosophical theses, Vyāsadeva completely summarized them all in the codes of Vedānta philosophy.**
