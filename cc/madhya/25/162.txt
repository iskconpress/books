====== CC Madhya 25.162 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe saba loka śunite āgraha karila
> 'ekaṣaṣṭi' artha prabhu vivari' kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people gathered there; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---expressed their eagerness; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṣṭi]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---sixty-one different meanings of the verse; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vivari']]---elaborately; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---explained.

===== Translation =====

**When all the people gathered there expressed the desire to hear again the sixty-one different meanings of the ātmārāma-śloka, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again explained them.**
