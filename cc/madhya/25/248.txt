====== CC Madhya 25.248 ======

===== Text =====

> ṣaṣṭhe-sārvabhaumera karilā uddhāra
> saptame-tīrtha-yātrā, vāsudeva nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṣṭhe]]---in the Sixth Chapter; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaumera]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---the Lord delivered; [[synonyms:s:saptame]]---in the Seventh Chapter; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---going to different holy places; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]] [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---delivering Vāsudeva.

===== Translation =====

**In the Sixth Chapter I have told how Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya was delivered, and in the Seventh Chapter I have described the Lord's tour of different holy places and His deliverance of Vāsudeva.**
