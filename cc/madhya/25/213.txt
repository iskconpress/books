====== CC Madhya 25.213 ======

===== Text =====

> subuddhi-rāya bahu sneha kare sanātane
> vyavahāra-sneha sanātana nāhi māne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:subuddhi]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]---affection; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]-[[synonyms:s:sneha]]---love and affection because of a previous relationship; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---was hesitant to accept.

===== Translation =====

**Subuddhi Rāya and Sanātana Gosvāmī knew one another before accepting the renounced order. Therefore Subuddhi Rāya showed much affection to Sanātana Gosvāmī, but Sanātana Gosvāmī hesitated to accept his sentiments and affections.**
