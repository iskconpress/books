====== CC Madhya 25.179 ======

===== Text =====

> tapana miśra, raghunātha, mahārāṣṭrīya brāhmaṇa
> candraśekhara, kīrtanīyā-paramānanda,-pañca jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tapana]] [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha; [[synonyms:m:mahārāṣṭrīya]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---the Mahārāṣṭrian //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:c:candraśekhara]]---Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyā]]-[[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]---Paramānanda, who used to perform //kīrtana//; [[synonyms:p:pañca]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---these five persons.

===== Translation =====

**These five devotees were Tapana Miśra, Raghunātha, the Mahārāṣṭrian brāhmaṇa, Candraśekhara and Paramānanda Kīrtanīyā.**
