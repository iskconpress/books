====== CC Madhya 25.94 ======

===== Text =====

> praṇavera yei artha, gāyatrīte sei haya
> sei artha catuḥ-ślokīte vivariyā kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:praṇavera]]---of the sound vibration, //oṁkāra//; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:g:gāyatrīte]]---in the Gāyatrī //mantra//; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---that same meaning; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:ś:ślokīte]]---in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// summarized in four //ślokas//; [[synonyms:v:vivariyā]]---describing elaborately; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---has said.

===== Translation =====

**"The meaning of the sound vibration oṁkāra is present in the Gāyatrī mantra. The same is elaborately explained in the four ślokas of Śrīmad Bhāgavatam known as catuḥ-ślokī.**
