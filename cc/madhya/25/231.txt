====== CC Madhya 25.231 ======

===== Text =====

> ānanda-samudre bhāse saba bhakta-gaṇe
> sabā lañā cale prabhu jagannātha-daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:samudre]]---in the ocean of transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---float; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see the Jagannātha Deity in the temple.

===== Translation =====

**Thus they all merged in the ocean of transcendental bliss. Then the Lord and all His devotees proceeded toward the temple of Jagannātha to see the Deity.**
