====== CC Madhya 25.45 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñi yei kahe, sei mata sāra
> āra yata mata, sei saba chārakhāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---whatever He has said; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---that explanation is actually the essence of Vedic knowledge; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---any other opinion not in collaboration with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's statement; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---these; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:c:chārakhāra]]---all distortions.

===== Translation =====

**"Whatever meaning Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gives is perfect. Any other interpretation is only a distortion."**
