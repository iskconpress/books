====== Cc. Madhya 25.187 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yabe subuddhi-rāya chilā gauḍe 'adhikāri'
> husena-khāṅ 'saiyada' kare tāhāra cākarī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:s:subuddhi]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:c:chilā]]---resided; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---a very respectable man; [[synonyms:h:husena]]-[[synonyms:k:khāṅ]]---Nawab Hussain Khān; [[synonyms:s:saiyada]]---named Saiyada; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]] [[synonyms:c:cākarī]]---service of Subuddhi Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Formerly Subuddhi Rāya had been a big landholder in Gauḍa-deśa [Bengal]. Saiyada Hussain Khān was then a servant of Subuddhi Rāya.**
