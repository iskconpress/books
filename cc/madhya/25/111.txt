====== Cc. Madhya 25.111 ======

===== Text =====

> sṛṣṭi kari' tāra madhye āmi praveśiye
> prapañca ye dekha saba, seha āmi ha-iye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after creating; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within the creation; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:p:praveśiye]]---I enter as Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:p:prapañca]]---the cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---you see; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iye]]---I am.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'After creating the cosmic manifestation, I entered into it. Whatever you see in the cosmic manifestation is but an expansion of My energy.**
