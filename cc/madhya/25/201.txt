~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 25.201~~

====== CC Madhya 25.201 ======

===== Text =====

> pāñā ājñā rāya vṛndāvanere calilā
> prayāga, ayodhyā diyā naimiṣāraṇye āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāñā]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---getting this order; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanere]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went toward Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:pra]] [[synonyms:y:yāga]]---Allahabad; [[synonyms:a:ayodhyā]]---Ayodhyā (the kingdom of Lord Rāmacandra); [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---through; [[synonyms:n:naimiṣāraṇye]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Naimiṣāraṇya, (a place near Lucknow).

===== Translation =====

**Thus receiving the order from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to go to Vṛndāvana, Subuddhi Rāya left Vārāṇasī and went through Prayāga, Ayodhyā and Naimiṣāraṇya toward Vṛndāvana.**
