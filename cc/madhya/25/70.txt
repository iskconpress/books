====== CC Madhya 25.70 ======

===== Text =====

> loka-saṅghaṭṭa dekhi' prabhura 'bāhya' yabe haila
> sannyāsīra gaṇa dekhi' nṛtya saṁvarila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing the great crowd of people; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:y:yabe]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---when there was; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the groups of Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs,// headed by Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:s:saṁvarila]]---suspended His dancing.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu regained His external consciousness, He saw that many Māyāvādī sannyāsīs and other people were gathered there. He therefore suspended His dancing for the time being.**
