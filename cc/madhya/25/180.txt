====== CC Madhya 25.180 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe cāhe prabhu-saṅge nīlācala yāite
> sabāre vidāya dilā prabhu yatna-sahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---every one of them wanted; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to all of them; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yatna]]-[[synonyms:s:sahite]]---with great attention.

===== Translation =====

**These five wanted to accompany Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to Jagannātha Purī, but the Lord attentively bade them farewell.**
