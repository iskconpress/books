====== Cc. Madhya 25.50 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mīmāṁsaka' kahe,-'īśvara haya karmera aṅga'
> 'sāṅkhya' kahe,-jagatera prakṛti kāraṇa-prasaṅga'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mīmāṁsaka]]---the Mīmāṁsaka philosophers; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:karmera]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---subject to fruitive activities; [[synonyms:s:sāṅkhya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the atheistic Sāṅkhya philosophers say; [[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the cosmic manifestation; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---nature; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause; [[synonyms:p:prasaṅga]]---thesis.

===== Translation =====

**"The Mīmāṁsaka philosophers conclude that if there is a God, He is subjected to our fruitive activities. Similarly, the Sāṅkhya philosophers who analyze the cosmic manifestation say that the cause of the cosmos is material nature.**
