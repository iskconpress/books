====== Cc. Madhya 25.163 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā lokera baḍa camatkāra haila
> caitanya-gosāñi-'śrī-kṛṣṇa', nirdhārila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all the people; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very great; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonder; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---personally Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nirdhārila]]---they concluded.

===== Translation =====

**When everyone heard Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's explanation of the ātmārāma-śloka, everyone was astonished and struck with wonder. They concluded that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was none other than Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself.**
