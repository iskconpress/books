====== CC Madhya 25.250 ======

===== Text =====

> navame-kahiluṅ dakṣiṇa-tīrtha-bhramaṇa
> daśame-kahiluṅ sarva-vaiṣṇava-milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:navame]]---in the Ninth Chapter; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhramaṇa]]---going on pilgrimage in South India; [[synonyms:d:daśame]]---in the Tenth Chapter; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting of all kinds of devotees.

===== Translation =====

**In the Ninth Chapter I have described the Lord's tour of South India and the different places of pilgrimage. In the Tenth Chapter I have described the meeting of all the devotees of the Lord.**
