====== CC Madhya 25.240 ======

===== Text =====

> madhya-līlāra kariluṅ ei dig-daraśana
> chaya vatsara kailā yaiche gamanāgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of this division, known as //Madhya-līlā//; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---this summary inspection; [[synonyms:c:chaya]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---continuously for six years; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---going and coming back.

===== Translation =====

**I have thus given a summary of the Madhya-līlā, which is a special description of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's travels to and from Jagannātha Purī. Indeed, the Lord traveled to and fro continuously for six years.**
