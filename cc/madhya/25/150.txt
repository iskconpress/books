====== CC Madhya 25.150 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa-bhakti-rasa-svarūpa' śrī-bhāgavata
> tāte veda-śāstra haite parama mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---of devotional service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of the transcendental mellow; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---the very form; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---the Vedic literature; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---has greater utility and value.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam gives direct information of the mellow derived from service to Kṛṣṇa. Therefore Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam is above all other Vedic literatures.**
