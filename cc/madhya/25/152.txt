====== CC Madhya 25.152 ======

===== Text =====

> vayaṁ tu na vitṛpyāma
> uttamaḥśloka-vikrame
> yac chṛṇvatāṁ rasa-jñānāṁ
> svādu svādu pade pade

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vayam]] [[synonyms:t:tu]]---we of course; [[synonyms:n:na]]---never; [[synonyms:v:vitṛpyāmaḥ]]---are satisfied; [[synonyms:u:uttamaḥ]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:v:vikrame]]---in the activities and pastimes of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---which; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇvatām]]---of those hearing; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:j:jñānām]]---who know the taste of mellows; [[synonyms:s:svādu]] [[synonyms:s:svādu]]---more palatable; [[synonyms:p:pade]] [[synonyms:p:pade]]---in every step.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'We never tire of hearing the transcendental pastimes of the Personality of Godhead, who is glorified by hymns and prayers. Those who enjoy association with Him relish hearing His pastimes at every moment.' "**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// [[books:sb:1:1:19|(1.1.19)]].
