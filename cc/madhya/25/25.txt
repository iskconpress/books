====== Cc. Madhya 25.25 ======

===== Text =====

> upaniṣadera karena mukhyārtha vyākhyāna
> śuniyā paṇḍita-lokera juḍāya mana-kāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upaniṣadera]]---of the Vedic version known as the //Upaniṣads//; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---He does; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the original meaning; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the learned scholars; [[synonyms:j:juḍāya]]---satisfies; [[synonyms:m:mana]]-[[synonyms:k:kāṇa]]---the minds and ears.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu explains the direct meaning of the Upaniṣads. When all learned scholars hear this, their minds and ears are satisfied.**
