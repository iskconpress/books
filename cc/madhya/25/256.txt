====== CC Madhya 25.256 ======

===== Text =====

> saptadaśe-vanapathe mathurā-gamana
> aṣṭādaśe-vṛndāvana-vihāra-varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saptadaśe]]---in the Seventeenth Chapter; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---through the forest path; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's going to Mathurā; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭādaśe]]---in the Eighteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:v:vihāra]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description of His touring the forest of Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**In the Seventeenth Chapter I have described the Lord's journey through the great forest of Jhārikhaṇḍa and His arrival at Mathurā. In the Eighteenth Chapter there is a description of His tour of the forest of Vṛndāvana.**
