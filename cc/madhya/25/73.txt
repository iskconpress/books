====== CC Madhya 25.73 ======

===== Text =====

> śreṣṭha hañā kene kara hīnera vandana
> āmāra sarva-nāśa haya, tumi brahma-sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a more honorable person; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you do; [[synonyms:h:hīnera]]---of an inferior person; [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worship; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---I become minimized in My strength; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---you are equal with the impersonal Brahman.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "You are a great, spiritually advanced personality, and therefore you cannot worship a person like Me. I am far inferior. If you do so, My spiritual power will be diminished, for you are as good as the impersonal Brahman.**
