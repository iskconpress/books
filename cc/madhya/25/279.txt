====== CC Madhya 25.279 ======

===== Text =====

> e amṛta kara pāna, yāra sama nāhi āna,
> citte kari' sudṛḍha viśvāsa
> nā paḍa' kutarka-garte, amedhya karkaśa āvarte,
> yāte paḍile haya sarva-nāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:a:amṛta]] [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---all of you must drink this nectar; [[synonyms:y:yāra]] [[synonyms:s:sama]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---there is no comparison to this nectar; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:s:sudṛḍha]] [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---having firm faith in this conclusion; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:paḍa']] [[synonyms:k:kutarka]] [[synonyms:g:garte]]---do not fall down into the pit of false arguments; [[synonyms:a:amedhya]] [[synonyms:k:karkaśa]] [[synonyms:ā:āvarte]]---in the untouchable, harsh whirlpool; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---in which; [[synonyms:p:paḍile]]---if one falls down; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---the purpose of life will be spoiled.

===== Translation =====

**The readers should relish this wonderful nectar because nothing compares to it. Keeping their faith firmly fixed within their minds, they should be careful not to fall into the pit of false arguments or the whirlpools of unfortunate situations. If one falls into such positions, he is finished.**
