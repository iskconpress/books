====== CC Madhya 25.69 ======

===== Text =====

> harṣa, dainya, cāpalyādi 'sañcārī' vikāra
> dekhi' kāśī-vāsī lokera haila camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:harṣa]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]---humility; [[synonyms:c:cāpalya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---talking in ecstasy and so on; [[synonyms:s:sañcārī]] [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---the transient transformations; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---the inhabitants of Benares; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all the people; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---there was astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**All the people were astonished to see the Lord's jubilation and humility and to hear Him talk in ecstasy. Indeed, all the residents of Benares [Kāśī] saw the bodily transformations and were astonished.**
