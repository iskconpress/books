====== CC Madhya 25.65 ======

===== Text =====

> caudikete lakṣa loka bale 'hari' 'hari'
> uṭhila maṅgala-dhvani svarga-martya bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cau]]-[[synonyms:d:dikete]]---all around; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]---hundreds of thousands; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---chant; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---O Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---there arose; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---an auspicious sound; [[synonyms:s:svarga]]-[[synonyms:m:martya]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---overwhelming all the universe.

===== Translation =====

**In all directions, hundreds and thousands of people began to chant "Hari! Hari!" Thus there arose a tumultuous and auspicious sound filling the entire universe.**
