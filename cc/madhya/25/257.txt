====== CC Madhya 25.257 ======

===== Text =====

> ūnaviṁśe-mathurā haite prayāga-gamana
> tāra madhye śrī-rūpere śakti-sañcāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ū:ūnaviṁśe]]---in the Nineteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Mathurā; [[synonyms:p:prayāga]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going to Prayāga; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpere]]---Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:s:sañcāraṇa]]---empowering to spread devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**In the Nineteenth Chapter, the Lord returned to Prayāga from Mathurā and empowered Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī to spread devotional service.**
