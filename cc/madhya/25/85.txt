====== Cc. Madhya 25.85 ======

===== Text =====

> naiṣāṁ matis tāvad urukramāṅghriṁ
> spṛśaty anarthāpagamo yad-arthaḥ
> mahīyasāṁ pāda-rajo-'bhiṣekaṁ
> niṣkiñcanānāṁ na vṛṇīta yāvat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:e:eṣām]]---of those who are attached to household life; [[synonyms:m:matiḥ]]---the interest; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]]---that long; [[synonyms:u:urukrama]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅghrim]]---the lotus feet of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is credited with uncommon activities; [[synonyms:s:spṛśati]]---touches; [[synonyms:a:anartha]]---of unwanted things; [[synonyms:a:apagamaḥ]]---vanquishing; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---of which; [[synonyms:a:arthaḥ]]---results; [[synonyms:m:mahīyasām]]---of the great personalities, devotees; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:r:rajaḥ]]---of the dust of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:a:abhiṣekam]]---sprinkling on the head; [[synonyms:n:niṣkiñcanānām]]---who are completely detached from material possessions; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:v:vṛṇīta]]---does not do; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long as.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Unless human society accepts the dust of the lotus feet of great mahātmās-devotees who have nothing to do with material possessions-mankind cannot turn its attention to the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa. Those lotus feet vanquish all the unwanted miserable conditions of material life.'**
