====== CC Madhya 25.254 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcadaśe-bhaktera guṇa śrī-mukhe kahila
> sārvabhauma-ghare bhikṣā, amogha tārila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañcadaśe]]---in the Fifteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally described; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the house of Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---accepting lunch; [[synonyms:a:amogha]] [[synonyms:t:tārila]]---He delivered Amogha.

===== Translation =====

**In the Fifteenth Chapter Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu highly praised the qualities of His devotees and accepted lunch at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya. At that time, He delivered Amogha.**
