====== Cc. Madhya 25.261 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcaviṁśe-kāśī-vāsīre vaiṣṇava-karaṇa
> kāśī haite punaḥ nīlācale āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:v:viṁśe]]---in the Twenty-fifth Chapter; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsīre]]---the residents of Vārāṇasī (Kāśī); [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:k:karaṇa]]---making Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Kāśī; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---coming back to Jagannātha Purī (Nīlācala).

===== Translation =====

**In the Twenty-fifth Chapter there is a description of how the residents of Vārāṇasī were converted to Vaiṣṇavism. The Lord also returned to Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī] from Vārāṇasī.**
