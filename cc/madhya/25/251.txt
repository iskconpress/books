====== CC Madhya 25.251 ======

===== Text =====

> ekādaśe-śrī-mandire 'beḍā-saṅkīrtana'
> dvādaśe-guṇḍicā-mandira-mārjana-kṣālana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekādaśe]]---in the Eleventh Chapter; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---in the Jagannātha temple; [[synonyms:b:beḍā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra// all around; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśe]]---in the Twelfth Chapter; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]-[[synonyms:m:mandira]]---of the temple known as Guṇḍicā; [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣālana]]---cleansing and washing.

===== Translation =====

**In the Eleventh Chapter I have described the great chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra that surrounded the Lord. In the Twelfth Chapter I have given a narration of the cleansing and washing of the Guṇḍicā temple.**
