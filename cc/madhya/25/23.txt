====== Cc. Madhya 25.23 ======

===== Text =====

> prakāśānandera śiṣya eka tāṅhāra samāna
> sabhā-madhye kahe prabhura kariyā sammāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prakāśānandera]] [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one of the disciples of Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:s:samāna]]---equal in learning with Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:s:sabhā]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the assembly of the //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---explains; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---respecting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu seriously.

===== Translation =====

**One of the disciples of Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī, who was as learned as his guru, began to speak in that assembly, offering all respects to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
