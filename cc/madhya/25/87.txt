====== CC Madhya 25.87 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhure lañā tathāya vasila
> prabhure prakāśānanda puchite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tathāya]] [[synonyms:v:vasila]]---sat down there; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prakāśānanda]]---Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:p:puchite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to inquire.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī sat down with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and began to question the Lord as follows.**
