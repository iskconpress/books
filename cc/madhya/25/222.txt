====== CC Madhya 25.222 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā mahāprabhu yadi nīlādri calilā
> nirjana vana-pathe yāite mahā sukha pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---on the other side; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:n:nīlādri]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went back to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:n:nirjana]] [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on a solitary forest path; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---traveling; [[synonyms:m:mahā]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---got very great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Jagannātha Purī, He passed through the solitary forest, and He received great pleasure in doing so.**
