====== CC Madhya 25.119 ======

===== Text =====

> ṛte 'rthaṁ yat pratīyeta
> na pratīyeta cātmani
> tad vidyād ātmano māyāṁ
> yathābhāso yathā tamaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṛ:ṛte]]---without; [[synonyms:a:artham]]---value; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---that which; [[synonyms:p:pratīyeta]]---appears to be; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pratīyeta]]---appears to be; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:ātmani]]---in relation to Me; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vidyāt]]---you must know; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---My; [[synonyms:m:māyām]]---illusory energy; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:ā:ābhāsaḥ]]---the reflection; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]---the darkness.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'What appears to be truth without Me, is certainly My illusory energy, for nothing can exist without Me. It is like a reflection of a real light in the shadows, for in the light there are neither shadows nor reflections.**
