====== Cc. Madhya 25.12 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle nindā śuni' śekhara, tapana
> duḥkha pāñā prabhu-pade kailā nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---exactly at this time; [[synonyms:n:nindā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---by hearing the criticism (of Lord Caitanya by the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//); [[synonyms:ś:śekhara]] [[synonyms:t:tapana]]---Candraśekhara and Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---feeling very unhappy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---unto the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted a request.

===== Translation =====

**At this time, Candraśekhara and Tapana Miśra both heard blasphemous criticism against Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and felt very unhappy. They came to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's lotus feet to submit a request.**
