====== CC Madhya 25.61 ======

===== Text =====

> pathe sei vipra saba vṛttānta kahila
> śuni' mahāprabhu sukhe īṣat hāsila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---explained the whole incident; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:ī:īṣat]]---mildly; [[synonyms:h:hāsila]]---smiled.

===== Translation =====

**While the Lord was on His way, the Mahārāṣṭrian brāhmaṇa informed Him about the incident that took place in the camp of Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī. Hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled happily.**
