====== CC Madhya 25.280 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-caitanya, nityānanda, advaitādi bhakta-vṛnda,
> āra yata śrotā bhakta-gaṇa
> tomā-sabāra śrī-caraṇa, kari śire vibhūṣaṇa,
> yāhā haite abhīṣṭa-pūraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---as well as the devotees like Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:ś:śrotā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees who listen; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of all of you; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:ś:śire]] [[synonyms:v:vibhūṣaṇa]]---I keep on my head as a helmet; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from which; [[synonyms:a:abhīṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---all my purposes will be served.

===== Translation =====

**In conclusion, I submit to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Nityānanda Prabhu, Advaita Prabhu, and all the other devotees and readers that I accept your lotus feet as the helmet on my head. In this way, all my purposes will be served.**
