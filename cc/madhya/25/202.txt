====== CC Madhya 25.202 ======

===== Text =====

> kataka divasa rāya naimiṣāraṇye rahilā
> prabhu vṛndāvana haite prayāga yāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kataka]] [[synonyms:d:divasa]]---a few days; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:n:naimiṣāraṇye]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---stayed at Naimiṣāraṇya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prayāga]]---to Allahabad; [[synonyms:y:yāilā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**Subuddhi Rāya stayed for some time at Naimiṣāraṇya. During that time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to Prayāga after visiting Vṛndāvana.**
