====== CC Madhya 25.142 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva bhāgavata-sūtrera 'artha'-rūpa
> nija-kṛta sūtrera nija-'bhāṣya'-svarūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the //Brahma-sūtra//; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---of the meaning; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the form; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛta]]---made by himself; [[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the //Vedānta-sūtra//; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāṣya]]---of his own commentary; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---the original form.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam gives the actual meaning of the Vedānta-sūtra. The author of the Vedānta-sūtra is Vyāsadeva, and he himself has explained those codes in the form of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.**
