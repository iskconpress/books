====== CC Madhya 25.221 ======

===== Text =====

> dina daśa rahi' rūpa gauḍe yātrā kaila
> sanātana-rūpera ei caritra kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:d:daśa]]---about ten days; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]] [[synonyms:y:yātrā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---went back to Bengal; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---of Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī and Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:caritra]]---character; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---I have described.

===== Translation =====

**After staying in Vārāṇasī for about ten days, Rūpa Gosvāmī returned to Bengal. In this way I have described the activities of Rūpa and Sanātana.**
