====== CC Madhya 25.43 ======

===== Text =====

> paramārtha-vicāra gela, kari mātra 'vāda'
> kāhāṅ mukti pāba, kāhāṅ kṛṣṇera prasāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---discussion on spiritual matters; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---has gone; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---we do; [[synonyms:m:mātra]] [[synonyms:v:vāda]]---only argument and word jugglery; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:p:pāba]]---we shall get; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"The atheists, headed by the Māyāvādī philosophers, do not care for liberation or Kṛṣṇa's mercy. They simply continue to put forward false arguments and counter---theories to atheistic philosophy, not considering or engaging in spiritual matters.**
