====== Cc. Madhya 25.252 ======

===== Text =====

> trayodaśe-ratha-āge prabhura nartana
> caturdaśe-'herā-pañcamī'-yātrā-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:trayodaśe]]---in the Thirteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of the Jagannātha Ratha; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dancing; [[synonyms:c:caturdaśe]]---in the Fourteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:h:herā]]-[[synonyms:p:pañcamī]]---Herā-pañcamī, which takes place on the fifth day of Ratha-yātrā; [[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---festival; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting.

===== Translation =====

**In the Thirteenth Chapter I have described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dancing before the chariot of Jagannātha. In the Fourteenth Chapter, there is an account of the Herā-pañcamī function.**
