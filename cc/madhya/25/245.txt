====== Cc. Madhya 25.245 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīya paricchede-prabhura kahiluṅ sannyāsa
> ācāryera ghare yaiche karilā vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Third Chapter; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---acceptance of the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the house of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---enjoyed His pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**In the Third Chapter I have described the Lord's acceptance of the renounced order and how He enjoyed His pastimes in the house of Advaita Ācārya.**
