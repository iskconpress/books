====== Cc. Madhya 25.165 ======

===== Text =====

> saba kāśī-vāsī kare nāma-saṅkīrtana
> preme hāse, kāṅde, gāya, karaye nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---all the inhabitants of Kāśī (Vārāṇasī); [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---they laughed; [[synonyms:k:kāṅde]]---they cried; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chanted; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---and danced.

===== Translation =====

**All the inhabitants of Kāśī [Vārāṇasī] began chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra in ecstatic love. Sometimes they laughed, sometimes they cried, sometimes they chanted, and sometimes they danced.**
