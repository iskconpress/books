====== CC Madhya 25.228 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara-svarūpa, paṇḍita-gadādhara
> jagadānanda, kāśīśvara, govinda, vakreśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara, the learned scholar; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---//Jagadānanda//; [[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara.

===== Translation =====

**Devotees like Svarūpa Dāmodara, Gadādhara Paṇḍita, Jagadānanda, Kāśīśvara, Govinda and Vakreśvara all came to meet the Lord.**
