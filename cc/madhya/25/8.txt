====== Cc. Madhya 25.8 ======

===== Text =====

> "prabhura svabhāva,-yebā dekhe sannidhāne
> 'svarūpa' anubhavi' tāṅre 'īśvara' kari' māne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yebā]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:s:sannidhāne]]---nearby; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---His personality; [[synonyms:a:anubhavi']]---realizing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---as the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---accepts.

===== Translation =====

**The Mahārāṣṭrian brāhmaṇa thought, "Whoever closely sees the characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately realizes His personality and accepts Him as the Supreme Lord.**
