====== Cc. Madhya 25.237 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe duṅhe jagannātha-prasāda ānila
> sabā-saṅge mahāprabhu bhojana karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both Sārvabhauma and Paṇḍita Gosāñi; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought the //mahā-prasāda// of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---dined.

===== Translation =====

**Upon receiving Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's order, both Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Paṇḍita Gosāñi brought sufficient prasāda from the temple of Jagannātha. The Lord then dined with everyone at His own place.**
