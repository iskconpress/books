====== Cc. Madhya 25.175 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu yabe snāne yāna viśveśvara-daraśane
> dui-dike loka kare prabhu-vilokane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:s:snāne]] [[synonyms:y:yāna]]---goes to bathe; [[synonyms:v:viśveśvara]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---or to see the Deity of Lord Viśveśvara; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---on two sides; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:v:vilokane]]---seeing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to take His bath in the Ganges and to see the temple of Viśveśvara, people would line up on both sides to see the Lord.**
