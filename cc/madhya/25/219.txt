====== Cc. Madhya 25.219 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśīte prabhura caritra śuni' tinera mukhe
> sannyāsīre kṛpā śuni' pāilā baḍa sukhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśīte]]---at Vārāṇasī (Kāśī); [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caritra]]---the activity; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:tinera]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouths of the three persons; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīre]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy shown to the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing about; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---he got; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---very great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**While staying at Vārāṇasī, Rūpa Gosvāmī heard of all Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's activities. When he heard of His deliverance of the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs, he became very happy.**
