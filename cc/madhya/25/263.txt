====== CC Madhya 25.263 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahiluṅ ei madhya-līlāra sāra
> koṭi-granthe varṇana nā yāya ihāra vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---essence of //Madhya-līlā//; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:g:granthe]]---in millions of books; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is not possible; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]] [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---elaborately.

===== Translation =====

**I have now summarized the entire subject matter of the Madhya-līlā. These pastimes cannot be described elaborately even in millions of books.**
