====== CC Madhya 25.29 ======

===== Text =====

> harer nāma-ślokera yei karilā vyākhyāna
> sei satya sukhadārtha parama pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:harer]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse beginning //harer nāma harer nāma//; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---the explanation; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---true; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:d:da]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---a meaning that is pleasing to accept; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---the supreme evidence.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's explanation of the verse beginning 'harer nāma harer nāma' is not only pleasing to the ear but is strong, factual evidence.**
