====== Cc. Madhya 25.214 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-virakta sanātana bhramena vane vane
> prati-vṛkṣe, prati-kuñje rahe rātri-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:virakta]]---highly elevated in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhramena]]---wanders; [[synonyms:v:vane]] [[synonyms:v:vane]]---from forest to forest; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]]---under every tree; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in every bush; [[synonyms:r:rahe]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---remains day and night.

===== Translation =====

**Being very advanced in the renounced order, Sanātana Gosvāmī used to wander from forest to forest, never taking shelter of any habitation built of stone. He used to live under trees or beneath bushes both day and night.**
