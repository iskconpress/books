====== CC Madhya 25.247 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcame-sākṣi-gopāla-caritra-varṇana
> nityānanda kahe, prabhu karena āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañcame]]---in the Fifth Chapter; [[synonyms:s:sākṣi]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Sākṣi-gopāla; [[synonyms:c:caritra]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---a description of the characteristics; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Nityānanda described this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasted it.

===== Translation =====

**In the Fifth Chapter I have narrated the story of Sākṣi-gopāla. Lord Nityānanda Prabhu narrated this while Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu listened.**
