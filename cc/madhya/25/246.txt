====== CC Madhya 25.246 ======

===== Text =====

> caturthe-mādhava purīra caritra-āsvādana
> gopāla sthāpana, kṣīra-curira varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturthe]]---in the Fourth Chapter; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]] [[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:c:caritra]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---relishing the characteristics; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]] [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---the installation of Gopāla; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:c:curira]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---a description of Gopīnātha's stealing condensed milk at Remuṇā.

===== Translation =====

**In the Fourth Chapter I have described Mādhavendra Purī's installation of the Gopāla Deity as well as Gopīnātha's stealing a pot of condensed milk at Remuṇā.**
