====== CC Madhya 25.226 ======

===== Text =====

> ānande vihvala bhakta-gaṇa dhāñā āilā
> narendre āsiyā sabe prabhure mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---very hastily came; [[synonyms:n:narendre]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming to the shore of Narendra Lake; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**Being overwhelmed with great pleasure, all the devotees hastily went to see the Lord. They met Him on the banks of Narendra-sarovara, the celebrated lake.**
