====== CC Madhya 25.243 ======

===== Text =====

> prathama paricchede-śeṣa-līlāra sūtra-gaṇa
> tathi-madhye kona bhāgera vistāra varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathama]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the First Chapter; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]] [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the codes of the //Antya-līlā,// Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes at the end; [[synonyms:t:tathi]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgera]]---of some portion of the book; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---a vivid description.

===== Translation =====

**In the First Chapter I have given a synopsis of the last pastimes [Antya-līlā]. Within this chapter is a vivid description of some of the pastimes of the Lord that took place toward the end of His life.**
