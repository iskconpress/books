====== CC Madhya 25.266 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-bhāgavata-tattva-rasa karilā pracāre
> kṛṣṇa-tulya bhāgavata, jānāilā saṁsāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the truth and transcendental taste of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:p:pracāre]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu preached elaborately; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tulya]]---identical with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:j:jānāilā]] [[synonyms:s:saṁsāre]]---has preached within this world.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has personally preached the transcendental truths and mellows of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam. Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam and the Supreme Personality of Godhead are identical, for Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam is the sound incarnation of Śrī Kṛṣṇa.**
