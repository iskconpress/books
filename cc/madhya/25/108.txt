====== Cc. Madhya 25.108 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāra kṛpāya ei saba sphuruka tomāre"
> eta bali' tina tattva kahilā tāṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by mercy; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:s:sphuruka]] [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---let them be awakened in you; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the three truths; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---explained to him.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa assured Lord Brahmā, 'By My mercy all these things will be awakened in you.' Saying this, the Lord began to explain the three truths [tattvas] to Lord Brahmā.**
