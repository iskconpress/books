====== Cc. Madhya 25.160 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle sei mahārāṣṭrīya brāhmaṇa
> sabhāte kahila sei śloka-vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mahārāṣṭrīya]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---the //brāhmaṇa// of Mahārāṣṭra province; [[synonyms:s:sabhāte]]---in the meeting; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---declared; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---the description of the //ātmārāma-śloka// explained by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**At this time the brāhmaṇa from the province of Mahārāṣṭra mentioned Lord Caitanya's explanation of the ātmārāma verse.**
