====== Cc. Madhya 25.21 ======

===== Text =====

> upadeśa lañā kare kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana
> sarva-loka hāse, gāya, karaye nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upadeśa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---getting instructions from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---join; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---the //saṅkīrtana// movement; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---everyone began to laugh in pleasure; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---and dance.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as people received instructions from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they began to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra. Thus everyone laughed, chanted and danced with the Lord.**
