====== CC Madhya 25.209 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-tīra-pathe prabhu prayāgere āilā
> tāhā śuni' dui-bhāi se pathe calilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prayāgere]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Prayāga; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this news; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers named Rūpa and Anupama; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:p:pathe]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---traveled on that path.

===== Translation =====

**When Rūpa Gosvāmī heard that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had gone to Prayāga on the road along the banks of the Ganges, both Rūpa and his brother Anupama went that way to meet the Lord.**
