====== CC Madhya 25.82 ======

===== Text =====

> tabu pūjya hao, tumi baḍa āmā haite
> sarva-nāśa haya mora tomāra nindāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:p:pūjya]] [[synonyms:h:hao]]---You are worshipable; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---You are much greater; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than me; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---everything becomes lost; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:n:nindāte]]---by blaspheming You.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You are the Supreme Lord, and although You consider Yourself the Lord's servant, You are nonetheless worshipable. You are much greater than I am; therefore all my spiritual achievements have been lost because I have blasphemed You.**
