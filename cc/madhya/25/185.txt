====== CC Madhya 25.185 ======

===== Text =====

> kata-kṣaṇe uṭhi' sabe duḥkhe ghare āilā
> sanātana-gosāñi vṛndāvanere calilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---after some time; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---rising; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in great unhappiness; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned to their homes; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanere]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---proceeded toward Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**After some time, all the devotees got up and returned to their homes very much grief-stricken. Sanātana Gosvāmī proceeded toward Vṛndāvana alone.**
