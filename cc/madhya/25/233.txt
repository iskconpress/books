====== Cc. Madhya 25.233 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha-sevaka āni' mālā-prasāda dilā
> tulasī paḍichā āsi' caraṇa vandilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the priests who were servitors of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---distributed flower garlands and //prasāda//; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]] [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---the temple servant known as Tulasī; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandilā]]---worshiped the lotus feet of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**The priests immediately brought them flower garlands and prasāda. The temple's watchman, who was named Tulasī, also came and offered his obeisances to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
