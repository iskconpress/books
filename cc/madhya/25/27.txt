====== Cc. Madhya 25.27 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya-kalpita artha ye paṇḍita śune
> mukhe 'haya' 'haya' kare, hṛdaya nā māne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpita]]---imagined by Śaṅkarācārya; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---any learned person who hears; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---only in the mouth; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---yes it is, yes it is; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---his heart; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---does not accept.

===== Translation =====

**"All the interpretations of Śaṅkarācārya are imaginary. Such imaginary interpretations are verbally accepted by learned scholars, but they do not appeal to the heart.**
