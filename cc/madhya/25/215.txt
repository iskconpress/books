====== CC Madhya 25.215 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurā-māhātmya-śāstra saṅgraha kariyā
> lupta-tīrtha prakaṭa kailā vanete bhramiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:m:māhātmya]]---giving a description of the greatness of Mathurā; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---books; [[synonyms:s:saṅgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---collecting; [[synonyms:l:lupta]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---lost holy places; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---discovering; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---he did; [[synonyms:v:vanete]] [[synonyms:b:bhramiyā]]---traveling within the forest.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Sanātana Gosvāmī collected some books about archaeological excavations in Mathurā, and, wandering in the forest, he sought to renovate all those holy places.**
