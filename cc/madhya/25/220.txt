====== CC Madhya 25.220 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura upara lokera praṇati dekhiyā
> sukhī hailā loka-mukhe kīrtana śuniyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:upara]]---upon; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people in general; [[synonyms:p:praṇati]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing the surrender; [[synonyms:s:sukhī]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very happy; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the general public; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing the description.

===== Translation =====

**When Rūpa Gosvāmī saw that all the people of Vārāṇasī respected Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he became very happy. He even heard stories from the general populace.**
