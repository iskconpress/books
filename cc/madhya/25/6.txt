====== CC Madhya 25.6 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsīre kṛpā pūrve likhiyāchoṅ vistāriyā
> uddeśe kahiye ihāṅ saṅkṣepa kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsīre]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy upon the //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---before this; [[synonyms:l:likhiyāchoṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:v:vistāriyā]]---elaborately; [[synonyms:u:uddeśe]]---in reference to that; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---let me speak; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---in brief.

===== Translation =====

**In the Seventh Chapter of Ādi-līlā I have already elaborately described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's deliverance of the sannyāsīs at Vārāṇasī, but I shall briefly repeat it in this chapter.**
