====== CC Madhya 25.169 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśīte grāhaka nāhi, vastu nā vikāya
> punarapi deśe vahi' laoyā nāhi yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśīte]]---in Kāśī (Benares); [[synonyms:g:grāhaka]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was no customer; [[synonyms:v:vastu]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:v:vikāya]]---it was not selling; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to My own country; [[synonyms:v:vahi']]---carrying (it); [[synonyms:l:laoyā]]---to take; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---was not possible.

===== Translation =====

**"Although I came to Vārāṇasī to sell My goods, there were no customers, and it appeared necessary for Me to carry them back to My own country.**
