====== CC Madhya 25.139 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe śuna, prema, yei-mūla 'prayojana'
> pulakāśru-nṛtya-gīta-yāhāra lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]] [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---now hear; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---which; [[synonyms:m:mūla]] [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---the chief objective; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]-[[synonyms:a:aśru]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---trembling of the body, tears in the eyes, dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms of which.

===== Translation =====

**"Now hear from Me what actual love of Godhead is. It is the prime object of life and is symptomized by bodily trembling, tears in the eyes, chanting and dancing.**
