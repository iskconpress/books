====== CC Madhya 25.19 ======

===== Text =====

> lokera saṅghaṭṭa āise prabhure dekhite
> nānā śāstre paṇḍita āise śāstra vicārite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lokera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭa]]---crowds of men; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---come; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---scholars learned in various scriptures; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---used to come; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]] [[synonyms:v:vicārite]]---to talk on various scriptures.

===== Translation =====

**Crowds of people came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu from that day on, and scholars of various scriptures discussed different subject matters with the Lord.**
