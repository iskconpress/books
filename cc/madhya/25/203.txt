====== CC Madhya 25.203 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurā āsiyā rāya prabhu-vārtā pāila
> prabhura lāga nā pāñā mane baḍa duḥkha haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---when he came to Mathurā; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:v:vārtā]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got information of the Lord's itinerary; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lāga]]---contact; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---not getting; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very great; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**After reaching Mathurā, Subuddhi Rāya received information of the Lord's itinerary. He became very unhappy because he was not able to contact the Lord.**
