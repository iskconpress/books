====== CC Madhya 25.92 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra sūtrera artha kona jīva nāhi jāne
> ataeva āpane sūtrārtha kariyāche vyākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sūtrera]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning of Vyāsadeva's //Vedānta-sūtra//; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---any; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---living being; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meanings of the //sūtras//; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---has described.

===== Translation =====

**"The purpose of the Vedānta-sūtra is very difficult for an ordinary person to understand, but Vyāsadeva, out of his causeless mercy, has personally explained the meaning.**
