====== Cc. Madhya 25.239 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā yei śraddhā kari' karaye śravaṇa
> acirāt pāya sei caitanya-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with faith and love; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hears; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever hears Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes with faith and love very soon attains shelter at the Lord's lotus feet.**
