====== CC Madhya 25.177 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata dina pañca loka nistāriyā
> āra dina calilā prabhu udvigna hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five days; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:n:nistāriyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---on the next day; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:udvigna]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very eager.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, for five days, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu delivered the people of Vārāṇasī. Finally, on the next day, He became very eager to leave.**
