====== CC Madhya 25.242 ======

===== Text =====

> madhya-līlāra krama ebe kari anuvāda
> anuvāda kaile haya kathāra āsvāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]] [[synonyms:k:krama]]---a chronological list of the pastimes described in the //Madhya-līlā// of //Caitanya-caritāmṛta//; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I may do; [[synonyms:a:anuvāda]]---assessment; [[synonyms:a:anuvāda]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---by assessing in that way; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kathāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]]---tasting of all the topics.

===== Translation =====

**I shall now chronologically reassess the chapters of Madhya-līlā so that one can relish the transcendental features of these topics.**
