====== CC Madhya 25.186 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā rūpa-gosāñi yabe mathurā āilā
> dhruva-ghāṭe tāṅre subuddhi-rāya mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Rūpa Gosāñi; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Mathurā; [[synonyms:d:dhruva]]-[[synonyms:g:ghāṭe]]---at the bank of the Yamunā known as Dhruva-ghāṭa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:s:subuddhi]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---a devotee of Lord Caitanya named Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**When Rūpa Gosvāmī reached Mathurā, he met Subuddhi Rāya on the banks of the Yamunā at a place called Dhruva-ghāṭa.**
