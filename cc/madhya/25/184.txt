====== CC Madhya 25.184 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' calilā prabhu sabā āliṅgiyā
> sabei paḍilā tathā mūrcchita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---began to proceed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:s:sabei]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---fainting.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced them all and began to proceed on His way, and they all fainted and fell down.**
