====== Cc. Madhya 25.171 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe kahe,-loka tārite tomāra avatāra
> 'pūrva' 'dakṣiṇa' 'paścima' karilā nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---everyone says; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:t:tārite]]---to deliver the fallen souls; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---Your incarnation; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]---east; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---south; [[synonyms:p:paścima]]---west; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---You have delivered.

===== Translation =====

**All the Lord's devotees then said, "You have incarnated to deliver fallen souls. You have delivered them in the east and in the south, and now you are delivering them in the west.**
