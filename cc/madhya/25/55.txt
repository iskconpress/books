====== CC Madhya 25.55 ======

===== Text =====

> parama kāraṇa īśvara keha nāhi māne
> sva-sva-mata sthāpe para-matera khaṇḍane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the supreme cause, the cause of all causes; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---none of the above-mentioned philosophers accept; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---their own personal opinions; [[synonyms:s:sthāpe]]---they establish; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:m:matera]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍane]]---busy in refuting the opinions of others.

===== Translation =====

**"Of the philosophers mentioned, none really cares for the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the cause of all causes. They are always busy refuting the philosophical theories of others and establishing their own.**
