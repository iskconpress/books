====== CC Madhya 25.98 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-veda-upaniṣade yata kichu haya
> tāra artha lañā vyāsa karilā sañcaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:v:veda]]---the four divisions of the //Vedas// (//Sāma, Yajur, Ṛg// and //Atharva//); [[synonyms:u:upaniṣade]]---and in 108 //Upaniṣads//; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is there; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meanings of those Vedic literatures; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking together; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:s:sañcaya]]---collected.

===== Translation =====

**"Vyāsadeva collected whatever Vedic conclusions were in the four Vedas and 108 Upaniṣads and placed them in the codes of the Vedānta-sūtra.**
