====== CC Madhya 25.262 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcaviṁśati paricchede ei kailuṅ anuvāda
> yāhāra śravaṇe haya granthārtha-āsvāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:v:viṁśati]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Twenty-fifth Chapter; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]] [[synonyms:a:anuvāda]]---I have made repetition; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---hearing which; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]]---understanding of the whole contents of the book.

===== Translation =====

**I have thus summarized these pastimes in the Twenty-fifth Chapter. Hearing this, one can understand the whole purport of this scripture.**
