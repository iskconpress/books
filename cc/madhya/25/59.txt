====== CC Madhya 25.59 ======

===== Text =====

> e saba vṛttānta śuni' mahārāṣṭrīya brāhmaṇa
> prabhure kahite sukhe karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]]---all these descriptions; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahārāṣṭrīya]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---the Mahārāṣṭrian //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to inform; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---very happily; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing all these statements, the Mahārāṣṭrian brāhmaṇa very jubilantly went to inform Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
