====== Cc. Madhya 25.200 ======

===== Text =====

> āra kṛṣṇa-nāma laite kṛṣṇa-sthāne sthiti-
> mahā-pātakera haya ei prāyaścitti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---further; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra; [[synonyms:l:laite]]---continuously chanting; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]] [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---being situated in company with Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātakera]]---of all kinds of sinful activity; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:prāyaścitti]]---atonement.

===== Translation =====

**"When you are situated at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa, no sinful reaction can touch you. This is the best solution to all sinful activity."**
