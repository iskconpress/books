====== CC Madhya 25.190 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra strī tāra aṅge dekhe māraṇera cihne
> subuddhi-rāyere mārite kahe rājā-sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:s:strī]]---his wife; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on his body; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:m:māraṇera]] [[synonyms:c:cihne]]---the mark of the whip; [[synonyms:s:subuddhi]]-[[synonyms:r:rāyere]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:m:mārite]]---to kill; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in the presence of the King.

===== Translation =====

**Later, when the wife of Nawab Saiyada Hussain Khān saw the whip marks on his body, she requested him to kill Subuddhi Rāya.**
