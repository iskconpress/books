====== CC Madhya 25.7 ======

===== Text =====

> yāhāṅ tāhāṅ prabhura nindā kare sannyāsīra gaṇa
> śuni' duḥkhe mahārāṣṭrīya vipra karaye cintana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---anywhere and everywhere; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nindā]]---criticism of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in great unhappiness; [[synonyms:m:mahārāṣṭrīya]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa// of Mahārāṣṭra province; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:c:cintana]]---was contemplating.

===== Translation =====

**When the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs were criticizing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu anywhere and everywhere in Vārāṇasī, the Mahārāṣṭrian brāhmaṇa, hearing this blasphemy, began to think about this unhappily.**
