====== Cc. Madhya 25.5 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsīra gaṇa prabhure yadi upekṣila
> bhakta-duḥkha khaṇḍāite tāre kṛpā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:u:upekṣila]]---criticized; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---the unhappiness of the devotees; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāite]]---to drive away; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---to them; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---showed His mercy.

===== Translation =====

**When the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs at Vārāṇasī criticized Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord's devotees became very depressed. To satisfy them, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed His mercy to the sannyāsīs.**
