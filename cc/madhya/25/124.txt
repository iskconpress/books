====== CC Madhya 25.124 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāte ye 'prīti', sei 'prema'-'prayojana'
> kārya-dvāre kahi tāra 'svarūpa'-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāte]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---affection; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---the ultimate goal of life; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---by practical example; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---let Me inform; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---its; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---natural characteristics.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Supreme affection for Me is called love of Godhead, and that is the ultimate goal of life. Let Me explain by practical example the natural characteristics of such love.**
