====== CC Madhya 25.86 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe tomāra pādābje upajibe bhakti
> tathi lāgi' kari tomāra caraṇe praṇati"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:a:abje]]---to the lotus feet; [[synonyms:u:upajibe]]---will grow; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:t:tathi]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for that reason; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:p:praṇati]]---humble obeisances at Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"Henceforward I shall certainly develop devotional service unto Your lotus feet. For this reason I have come to You and have fallen down at Your lotus feet."**
