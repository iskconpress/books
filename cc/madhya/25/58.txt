====== Cc. Madhya 25.58 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-vāṇī-amṛtera dhāra
> tiṅho ye kahaye vastu, sei 'tattva'-sāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---the message of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---a continuous shower of nectar; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kahaye]] [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---whatever He says to be the ultimate truth, the //summum bonum//; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:tattva]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---that is the essence of all spiritual knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**"The words of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are a shower of nectar. Whatever He concludes to be the ultimate truth is indeed the summum bonum of all spiritual knowledge."**
