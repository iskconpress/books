====== CC Madhya 25.106 ======

===== Text =====

> ei 'tina' tattva āmi kahinu tomāre
> 'jīva' tumi ei tina nāribe jānibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---all three of these features of the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kahinu]]---shall speak; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---unto you; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---a living being; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]---these three; [[synonyms:n:nāribe]]---will not be able; [[synonyms:j:jānibāre]]---to understand.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'O Brahmā, I shall explain all these truths to you. You are a living being, and without My explanation you will not be able to understand your relationship with Me, devotional activity and life's ultimate goal.**
