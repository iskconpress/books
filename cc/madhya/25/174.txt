====== CC Madhya 25.174 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣa koṭi loka āise, nāhika gaṇana
> saṅkīrṇa-sthāne prabhura nā pāya daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣa]] [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---hundreds and thousands; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---come; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---there was no counting; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in a small place; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---could not get an audience.

===== Translation =====

**Hundreds and thousands of people came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. There was no counting the number. Because the Lord's residence was very small, not everyone could see Him.**
