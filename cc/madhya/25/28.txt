====== CC Madhya 25.28 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-vākya dṛḍha satya māni
> kali-kāle sannyāse 'saṁsāra' nāhi jini

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:v:vākya]]---the words of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---very firm and convincing; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I admit as truth; [[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in this Age of Kali; [[synonyms:s:sannyāse]]---simply by accepting the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jini]]---one cannot escape the material clutches.

===== Translation =====

**"The words of Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu are firm and convincing, and I accept them as true. In this Age of Kali, one cannot be delivered from material clutches simply by formally accepting the renounced order.**
