====== Cc. Madhya 25.18 ======

===== Text =====

> ye divasa prabhu sannyāsīre kṛpā kaila
> se divasa haite grāme kolāhala haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:d:divasa]]---the day when; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīre]]---to the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---showed His mercy; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:d:divasa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---beginning from that day; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the locality; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there were many talks about this incident.

===== Translation =====

**Beginning from the day on which Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed His mercy to the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs, there were vivid discussions about this conversion among the inhabitants of Vārāṇasī.**
