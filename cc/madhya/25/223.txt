====== Cc. Madhya 25.223 ======

===== Text =====

> sukhe cali' āise prabhu balabhadra-saṅge
> pūrvavat mṛgādi-saṅge kailā nānā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in a very pleasing atmosphere; [[synonyms:c:cali']] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---comes back; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:balabhadra]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the servant of Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the forest animals; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---various pleasing activities.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu happily returned to Jagannātha Purī in the company of His servant, Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya. As previously, the Lord performed many pleasing pastimes with the forest animals.**
