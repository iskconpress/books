====== Cc. Madhya 25.269 ======

===== Text =====

> śraddhā kari' ei līlā śuna, bhakta-gaṇa
> ihāra prasāde pāibā caitanya-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---having faith and love; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---these pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]] [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by the grace of this transcendental hearing; [[synonyms:p:pāibā]]---you will get; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All devotees should hear about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes with faith and love. By the grace of the Lord, one can thus attain shelter at His lotus feet.**
