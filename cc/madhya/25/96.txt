====== Cc. Madhya 25.96 ======

===== Text =====

> nārada sei artha vyāsere kahilā
> śuni' veda-vyāsa mane vicāra karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārada]]---the great sage Nārada; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the same purpose; [[synonyms:v:vyāsere]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---explained to Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---considered very carefully.

===== Translation =====

**"Whatever Lord Brahmā told Nārada Muni was again explained by Nārada Muni to Vyāsadeva. Vyāsadeva later considered these instructions in his mind.**
