====== CC Madhya 25.93 ======

===== Text =====

> yei sūtra-kartā, se yadi karaye vyākhyāna
> tabe sūtrera mūla artha lokera haya jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:k:kartā]]---the person who has made the //Vedānta-sūtra//; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that person; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explains the meaning; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the codes of //Vedānta-sūtra//; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]---the original; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people in general; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---comes within knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**"If the Vedānta-sūtra is explained by Vyāsadeva himself, who has written it, its original meaning can be understood by the people in general.**
