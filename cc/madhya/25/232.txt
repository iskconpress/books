====== CC Madhya 25.232 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha dekhi' prabhu premāviṣṭa hailā
> bhakta-saṅge bahu-kṣaṇa nṛtya-gīta kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became overwhelmed with love and affection; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the society of the devotees; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---chanted and danced.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw Lord Jagannātha in the temple, He was immediately overwhelmed with love and affection. He chanted and danced with His devotees for a long time.**
