====== CC Madhya 25.10 ======

===== Text =====

> vārāṇasī-vāsa āmāra haya sarva-kāle
> sarva-kāla duḥkha pāba, ihā nā karile"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence at Vārāṇasī; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---always; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]]---always; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāba]]---I will suffer unhappiness; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karile]]---if I do not execute.

===== Translation =====

**"I shall have to reside at Vārāṇasī the rest of my life. If I do not try to carry out this plan, I shall certainly continue to suffer mental depression."**
