====== CC Madhya 25.173 ======

===== Text =====

> vārāṇasī-grāme yadi kolāhala haila
> śuni' grāmī deśī loka āsite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the city of Vārāṇasī; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was broadcasting of this news; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:g:grāmī]]---from the villages; [[synonyms:d:deśī]]---from the towns; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:ā:āsite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---people began to pour in.

===== Translation =====

**After the news of these events was broadcast, everyone from the surrounding neighborhoods began to pour in to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
