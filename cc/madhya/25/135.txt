====== CC Madhya 25.135 ======

===== Text =====

> eita' 'sambandha', śuna 'abhidheya' bhakti
> bhāgavate prati-śloke vyāpe yāra sthiti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eita']]---this; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]---relationship; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:a:abhidheya]]---the function; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---known as devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in each and every verse; [[synonyms:v:vyāpe]]---pervades; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---the situation.

===== Translation =====

**"This is one's eternal relationship with the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Now please hear about the execution of devotional service. This principle pervades practically all the verses of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.**
