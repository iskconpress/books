====== Cc. Madhya 25.41 ======

===== Text =====

> sūtrera pariṇāma-vāda, tāhā nā māniyā
> 'vivarta-vāda' sthāpe, 'vyāsa bhrānta' baliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the codes of the //Vedānta-sūtra//; [[synonyms:p:pariṇāma]]-[[synonyms:v:vāda]]---the transformation of energy; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:m:māniyā]]---not accepting this fact; [[synonyms:v:vivarta]]-[[synonyms:v:vāda]]---the theory of illusion; [[synonyms:s:sthāpe]]---establishes; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]] [[synonyms:b:bhrānta]] [[synonyms:b:baliyā]]---accusing Vyāsadeva to be mistaken.

===== Translation =====

**"Not accepting the transformation of energy, Śrīpād Śaṅkarācārya has tried to establish the theory of illusion under the plea that Vyāsadeva has made a mistake.**

===== Purport =====

For a further explanation of this verse, one may refer to Chapter Seven (verses 121-126).
