====== Cc. Madhya 25.168 ======

===== Text =====

> nija-gaṇa lañā prabhu kahe hāsya kari'
> kāśīte āmi āilāṅa vecite bhāvakāli

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with His personal associates; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:h:hāsya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---laughingly; [[synonyms:k:kāśīte]]---in Kāśī; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:ā:āilāṅa]]---I came; [[synonyms:v:vecite]]---to sell; [[synonyms:b:bhāvakāli]]---emotional ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Among His own associates, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu laughingly said, "I came here to sell My emotional ecstatic love.**
