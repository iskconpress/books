====== CC Madhya 25.99 ======

===== Text =====

> yei sūtre yei ṛk-viṣaya-vacana
> bhāgavate sei ṛk śloke nibandhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:s:sūtre]]---in the codes of //Vedānta-sūtra//; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛk]]---Vedic //mantra//; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---subject matter to be explained; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛk]]---that same Vedic //mantra//; [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in eighteen thousand verses; [[synonyms:n:nibandhana]]---compiling.

===== Translation =====

**"In Vedānta-sūtra, the purpose of all Vedic knowledge is explained, and in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam the same purpose has been explained in eighteen thousand verses.**
