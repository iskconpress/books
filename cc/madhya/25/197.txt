====== CC Madhya 25.197 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe yadi mahāprabhu vārāṇasī āilā
> tāṅre mili' rāya āpana-vṛttānta kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at this moment; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Vārāṇasī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting Him; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---explained his personal situation.

===== Translation =====

**In his state of perplexity, Subuddhi Rāya met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu when the Lord was at Vārāṇasī. Subuddhi Rāya explained his position and asked Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu what he should do.**
