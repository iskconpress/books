====== CC Madhya 25.244 ======

===== Text =====

> dvitīya paricchede-prabhura pralāpa-varṇana
> tathi-madhye nānā-bhāvera dig-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvitīya]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Second Chapter; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---a description of His behaving like a crazy man; [[synonyms:t:tathi]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of different emotional ecstasies; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---indication.

===== Translation =====

**In the Second Chapter I have described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's talking like a crazy man. Within this chapter it is indicated how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu manifested His different emotional moods.**
