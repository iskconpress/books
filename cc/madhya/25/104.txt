====== Cc. Madhya 25.104 ======

===== Text =====

> sādhanera phala-'prema' mūla-prayojana
> sei preme pāya jīva āmāra 'sevana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sādhanera]] [[synonyms:p:phala]]---the result of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]-[[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---the chief goal; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:preme]]---by that love of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---//gets//; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---the living entity; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'By rendering devotional service, one gradually rises to the platform of love of Godhead. That is the chief goal of life. On the platform of love of Godhead, one is eternally engaged in the service of the Lord.**
