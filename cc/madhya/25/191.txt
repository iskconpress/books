====== Cc. Madhya 25.191 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,-āmāra poṣṭā rāya haya 'pitā'
> tāhāre mārimu āmi,-bhāla nahe kathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King said; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:p:poṣṭā]]---maintainer; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---just like my father; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]] [[synonyms:m:mārimu]]---shall kill him; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---this is not a good proposal.

===== Translation =====

**Hussain Khān replied, "Subuddhi Rāya has maintained me very carefully. He was just like a father to me," he said. "Now you are asking me to kill him. This is not a very good proposal."**
