====== Cc. Madhya 25.49 ======

===== Text =====

> yei grantha-kartā cāhe sva-mata sthāpite
> śāstrera sahaja artha nahe tāṅhā haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]-[[synonyms:k:kartā]]---author; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]] [[synonyms:s:sthāpite]]---to establish his own opinion; [[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]]---of the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:s:sahaja]]---the direct; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from such an author.

===== Translation =====

**"Anyone who wants to establish his own opinion or philosophy certainly cannot explain any scripture according to the principle of direct interpretation.**
