====== Cc. Madhya 25.24 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya haya 'sākṣāt nārāyaṇa'
> 'vyāsa-sūtrera' artha karena ati-manorama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]] [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---the codes of Vyāsadeva (//Vedānta-sūtra//); [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---He explains; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:m:manorama]]---very nicely.

===== Translation =====

**He said, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyaṇa Himself. When He explains the Vedānta-sūtra, He does so very nicely.**
