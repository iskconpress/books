====== Cc. Madhya 25.253 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye vraja-devīra bhāvera śravaṇa
> svarūpa kahilā, prabhu kailā āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in that; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:d:devīra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī described; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---personally tasted.

===== Translation =====

**Also in the Fourteenth Chapter the emotional ecstasy of the gopīs was described by Svarūpa Dāmodara and tasted by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
