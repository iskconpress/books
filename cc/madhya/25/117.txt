====== CC Madhya 25.117 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche sūryera sthāne bhāsaye 'ābhāsa'
> sūrya vinā svatantra tāra nā haya prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:s:sūryera]]---of the sun; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in place; [[synonyms:b:bhāsaye]]---appears; [[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---the illumination; [[synonyms:s:sūrya]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without the sun; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independently; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is not; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Sometimes a reflection of the sun is experienced in place of the sun, but its illumination is never possible independent of the sun.**
