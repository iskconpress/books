====== CC Madhya 25.189 ======

===== Text =====

> pāche yabe husena-khāṅ gauḍe 'rājā' ha-ila
> subuddhi-rāyere tiṅho bahu bāḍāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:h:husena]]-[[synonyms:k:khāṅ]]---Hussain Khān; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was appointed Nawab, or governor, by the central Mohammedan government; [[synonyms:s:subuddhi]]-[[synonyms:r:rāyere]]---unto Subuddhi Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:b:bāḍāila]]---increased the opulences.

===== Translation =====

**Later Hussain Khān somehow or other was appointed Nawab by the central Mohammedan government. As a matter of obligation, he increased the opulences of Subuddhi Rāya.**
