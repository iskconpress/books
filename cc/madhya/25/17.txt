====== CC Madhya 25.17 ======

===== Text =====

> grantha bāḍe, punarukti haya ta' kathana
> tāhāṅ ye nā likhiluṅ, tāhā kariye likhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:grantha]]---the size of the book; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]-[[synonyms:u:ukti]]---repetition; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---would be; [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---of subject matters once described; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there (in the Seventh Chapter); [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:likhiluṅ]]---I have not described; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:l:likhana]]---I am writing.

===== Translation =====

**Since I have already described this incident very elaborately in the Seventh Chapter of Ādi-līlā, I do not wish to increase the size of this book by giving another description. However, I shall try to include in this chapter whatever was not described there.**
