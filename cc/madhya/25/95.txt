====== CC Madhya 25.95 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmāre īśvara catuḥ-ślokī ye kahilā
> brahmā nārade sei upadeśa kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmāre]]---to Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:ś:ślokī]]---the four famous verses known as //catuḥ-ślokī//; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---whatever was explained; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:n:nārade]]---to Nārada Muni; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---instructed.

===== Translation =====

**"Whatever was spoken by the Supreme Personality of Godhead to Lord Brahmā in the four verses of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, was also explained to Nārada by Lord Brahmā.**
