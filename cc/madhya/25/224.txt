====== Cc. Madhya 25.224 ======

===== Text =====

> āṭhāranālāte āsi' bhaṭṭācārya brāhmaṇe
> pāṭhāñā bolāilā nija-bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āṭhāranālāte]]---to a place near Jagannātha Purī named Āṭhāranālā; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇe]]---the //brāhmaṇa// known as Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāñā]]---sending; [[synonyms:b:bolāilā]]---called for; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---His own personal associates.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived at a place known as Āṭhāranālā near Jagannātha Purī, He sent Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya to call for His devotees.**
