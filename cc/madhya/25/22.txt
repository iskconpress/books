====== CC Madhya 25.22 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure praṇata haila sannyāsīra gaṇa
> ātma-madhye goṣṭhī kare chāḍi' adhyayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:praṇata]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among themselves; [[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---discussed; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']] [[synonyms:a:adhyayana]]---giving up so-called studies of Vedānta.

===== Translation =====

**All the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs offered their obeisances unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and then began to discuss His movement, giving up their studies of Vedānta and Māyāvāda philosophy.**
