====== CC Madhya 25.89 ======

===== Text =====

> sūtrera karilā tumi mukhyārtha-vivaraṇa
> tāhā śuni' sabāra haila camatkāra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the //Brahma-sūtra//; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---have done; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description of the direct meaning; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing that; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, whatever direct meaning You have given when explaining the Brahma-sūtra is certainly very wonderful to all of us.**
