====== CC Madhya 20.166 ======

===== Text =====

> 'svayaṁ-rūpa' 's va yaṁ-prakāśa'---dui rūpe sphūrti
> svayaṁ-rūpe---eka 'kṛṣṇa' vraje gopa-mūrti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sva]] [[synonyms:y:yam]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the original form of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sva]] [[synonyms:y:yam]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---the personal manifestation; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in two forms; [[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---exhibition; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the original form; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---the cowherd boy.

===== Translation =====

**"The original form of the Lord [svayaṁ-rūpa] is exhibited in two forms-svayaṁ-rūpa and svayaṁ-prakāśa. In His original form as svayaṁ-rūpa, Kṛṣṇa is observed as a cowherd boy in Vṛndāvana.**
