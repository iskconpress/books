====== Cc. Madhya 20.47 ======

===== Text =====

> 'dvāre eka 'vaiṣṇava haya, bolāha tāṅhāre'
> candraśekhara dekhe---'vaiṣṇava' nāhika dvāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at your door; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---one Vaiṣṇava devotee; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:b:bolāha]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāi]]-[[synonyms:e:e]]---please call him; [[synonyms:c:candraśekhara]]---Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---a devotee; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is not; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "There is a devotee at your door. Please call him in." Going outside, Candraśekhara could not see a Vaiṣṇava at his door.**
