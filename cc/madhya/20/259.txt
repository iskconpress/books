====== CC Madhya 20.259 ======

===== Text =====

> māyā-dvāre sṛje teṅho brahmāṇḍera gaṇa
> jaḍa-rūpā prakṛti nahe brahmāṇḍa-kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---by the agency of the external energy; [[synonyms:s:sṛje]]---creates; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---Lord Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍera]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the groups of universes; [[synonyms:j:jaḍa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpā]]---appearing dull; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---the material energy; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of the cosmic manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**"By the agency of the material energy, this same Lord Saṅkarṣaṇa creates all the universes. The dull material energy-known in modern language as nature-is not the cause of the material universe.**
