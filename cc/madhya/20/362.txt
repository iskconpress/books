====== Cc. Madhya 20.362 ======

===== Text =====

> ei saba kārya---tāṅra taṭastha-lakṣaṇa
> anya avatāra aiche jāne muni-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---all these activities; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:t:taṭastha]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---marginal characteristics; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---another incarnation; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that same way; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---know; [[synonyms:m:muni]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the great saintly persons like Vyāsadeva.

===== Translation =====

**"All these activities are His marginal characteristics. Great saintly persons understand the incarnations of the Supreme Personality of Godhead by the indications of the two characteristics known as svarūpa and taṭastha. All the incarnations of Kṛṣṇa should be understood in this way.**
