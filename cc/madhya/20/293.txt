====== Cc. Madhya 20.293 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' dvitīya-puruṣa---brahmāṇḍera īśvara
> māyāra 'āśraya' haya, tabu māyā-pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the second Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍera]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the master of the universe; [[synonyms:m:māyāra]]---of the external, material energy; [[synonyms:ā:āśraya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes the shelter; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāra]]---is beyond the touch of the material energy.

===== Translation =====

**"This second Personality of Godhead, known as Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu, is the master of each and every universe and the shelter of the external energy. Nonetheless, He remains beyond the touch of the external energy.**
