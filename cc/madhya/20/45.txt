====== CC Madhya 20.45 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe vārāṇasī gosāñi āilā kata-dine
> śuni ānandita ha-ilā prabhura āgamane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]---to Vārāṇasī; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---after a few days; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---he became; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āgamane]]---about the arrival.

===== Translation =====

**After a few days, Sanātana Gosvāmī arrived at Vārāṇasī. He was very pleased to hear about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's arrival there.**
