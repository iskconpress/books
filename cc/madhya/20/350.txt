====== CC Madhya 20.350 ======

===== Text =====

> rāja-mantrī sanātana---buddhye bṛhaspati
> prabhura kṛpāte puche asaṅkoca-mati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:m:mantrī]] [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī was formerly an intelligent minister for Nawab Hussain Shah; [[synonyms:b:buddhye]]---in intelligence; [[synonyms:b:bṛhaspati]]---exactly like Bṛhaspati, the priest in the heavenly kingdom; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---because of the unlimited mercy of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquires; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkoca]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]---without hesitation.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī had been a minister under Nawab Hussain Shah, and he was undoubtedly as intelligent as Bṛhaspati, the chief priest of the heavenly kingdom. Due to the Lord's unlimited mercy, Sanātana Gosvāmī questioned Him without hesitation.**
