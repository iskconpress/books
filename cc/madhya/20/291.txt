====== CC Madhya 20.291 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmā, viṣṇu, śiva---tāṅra guṇa-avatāra
> sṛṣṭi-sthiti-pralayera tinera adhikāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations of the material qualities; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:s:sthiti]]-[[synonyms:p:pralayera]]---of the three functions, namely creation, maintenance and dissolution; [[synonyms:t:tinera]] [[synonyms:a:adhikāra]]---there is control by the three deities (Lord Brahmā, Lord Viṣṇu and Lord Śiva).

===== Translation =====

**"Brahmā, Viṣṇu and Śiva are His three incarnations of the material qualities. Creation, maintenance and destruction respectively are under the charge of these three personalities.**
