====== CC Madhya 20.401 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇasya pūrṇatamatā
> vyaktābhūd gokulāntare
> pūrṇatā pūrṇataratā
> dvārakā-mathurādiṣu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇasya]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tamatā]]---being most perfect; [[synonyms:v:vyaktā]]---manifested; [[synonyms:a:abhūt]]---became; [[synonyms:g:gokula]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---in the domain of Gokula Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇatā]]---completeness; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:taratā]]---more completeness; [[synonyms:d:dvārakā]]---in Dvārakā; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādiṣu]]---and Mathurā, and so on.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The most complete qualities of Kṛṣṇa are manifest within Vṛndāvana, and His complete and more complete qualities are manifest in Dvārakā and Mathurā.'**
