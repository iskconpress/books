====== Cc. Madhya 20.26 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei sāta mohara hastete kariyā
> bhūñāra kāche yāñā kahe mohara dhariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:m:mohara]]---these seven golden coins; [[synonyms:h:hastete]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---taking in the hands; [[synonyms:b:bhūñāra]] [[synonyms:k:kāche]]---to the landlord; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:m:mohara]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---holding the golden coins.

===== Translation =====

**Thereupon, Sanātana Gosvāmī took the seven gold coins in his hands and went to the landlord. Holding the gold coins before him, he spoke as follows.**
