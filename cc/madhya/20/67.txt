====== Cc. Madhya 20.67 ======

===== Text =====

> tapana-miśrere āra candraśekharere
> prabhu-ājñāya sanātana mililā doṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tapana]]-[[synonyms:m:miśrere]]---unto Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:c:candraśekharere]]---unto Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---by the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāre]]---both of them.

===== Translation =====

**By the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Sanātana Gosvāmī met both Tapana Miśra and Candraśekhara.**
