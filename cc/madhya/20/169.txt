====== CC Madhya 20.169 ======

===== Text =====

> saubhary-ādi-prāya sei kāya-vyūha naya
> kāya-vyūha haile nāradera vismaya nā haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saubhari]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with the sage named Saubhari; [[synonyms:p:prāya]]---like; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]]---the expansion of one's body; [[synonyms:n:naya]]---is not; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]]---expansions of the body; [[synonyms:h:haile]]---if there are; [[synonyms:n:nāradera]]---of Nārada Muni; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---the astonishment; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there cannot be.

===== Translation =====

**"The prābhava-prakāśa expansions of Lord Kṛṣṇa are not like the expansions of the sage Saubhari. Had they been so, Nārada would not have been astonished to see them.**
