====== Cc. Madhya 20.346 ======

===== Text =====

> dhyāyan kṛte yajan yajñais
> tretāyāṁ dvāpare 'rcayan
> yad āpnoti tad āpnoti
> kalau saṅkīrtya keśavam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhyāyan]]---meditating; [[synonyms:k:kṛte]]---in the Satya-yuga; [[synonyms:y:yajan]]---worshiping; [[synonyms:y:yajñaiḥ]]---by the performance of great sacrifices; [[synonyms:t:tretāyām]]---in the Tretā-yuga; [[synonyms:d:dvāpare]]---in the Dvāpara-yuga; [[synonyms:a:arcayan]]---worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever; [[synonyms:ā:āpnoti]]---is achieved; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:āpnoti]]---is obtained; [[synonyms:k:kalau]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtya]]---simply by chanting; [[synonyms:k:keśavam]]---the pastimes and qualities of Lord Keśava.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Whatever is achieved by meditation in Satya-yuga, by the performance of yajña in Tretā-yuga or by the worship of Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet in Dvāpara-yuga is also obtained in the Age of Kali simply by chanting and glorifying Lord Keśava.'**
