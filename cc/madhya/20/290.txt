====== CC Madhya 20.290 ======

===== Text =====

> 'rudra'-rūpa dhari kare jagat saṁhāra
> sṛṣṭi, sthiti, pralaya haya icchāya yāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rudra]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]] [[synonyms:d:dhari]]---accepting the form of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:j:jagat]] [[synonyms:s:saṁhāra]]---dissolution of the universal creation; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---creation; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---maintenance; [[synonyms:p:pralaya]]---and dissolution; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---take place; [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---by the will; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of whom.

===== Translation =====

**"The Supreme Lord, and His form of Rudra [Lord Śiva], brings about the dissolution of this material creation. In other words, by His will only, there is creation, maintenance and dissolution of the whole cosmic manifestation.**
