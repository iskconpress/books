====== CC Madhya 20.78 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe miśra purātana eka dhuti dila
> teṅho dui bahirvāsa-kaupīna karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:p:purātana]]---old; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:d:dhuti]]---dhoti; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he (Sanātana Gosvāmī); [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---outer coverings; [[synonyms:k:kaupīna]]---underwear; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**When Tapana Miśra gave Sanātana Gosvāmī a used dhoti, Sanātana immediately tore it in pieces to make two sets of outer cloth and underwear.**
