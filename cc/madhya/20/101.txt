====== CC Madhya 20.101 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛpā kari' yadi more kariyācha uddhāra
> āpana-kṛpāte kaha 'kartavya' āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by Your causeless mercy; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]]---You have done; [[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---deliverance; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by Your own mercy; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please speak; [[synonyms:k:kartavya]] [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my duty.

===== Translation =====

**"Out of Your causeless mercy, You have delivered me from the materialistic path. Now, by the same causeless mercy, please tell me what my duty is.**
