====== CC Madhya 20.197 ======

===== Text =====

> pradyumnera mūrti---trivikrama, vāmana, śrīdhara
> aniruddhera mūrti---hṛṣīkeśa, padmanābha, dāmodara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pradyumnera]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---expansions of the form of Pradyumna; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:v:vikrama]]---Trivikrama; [[synonyms:v:vāmana]]---Vāmana; [[synonyms:ś:śrīdhara]]---Śrīdhara; [[synonyms:a:aniruddhera]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---expansions of Aniruddha; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣīkeśa]]---Hṛṣīkeśa; [[synonyms:p:padmanābha]]---Padmanābha; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara.

===== Translation =====

**"The expansions of Pradyumna are Trivikrama, Vāmana and Śrīdhara. The expansions of Aniruddha are Hṛṣīkeśa, Padmanābha and Dāmodara.**
