====== CC Madhya 20.56 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"tomā sparśi ātma pavitrite
> bhakti-bale pāra tumi brahmāṇḍa śodhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:s:sparśi]]---I touch you; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]] [[synonyms:p:pavitrite]]---to purify Myself; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:b:bale]]---the strength of your devotional service; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---are able; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---the whole universe; [[synonyms:ś:śodhite]]---to purify.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord replied, "I am touching you just to purify Myself because by the force of your devotional service you can purify the whole universe.**
