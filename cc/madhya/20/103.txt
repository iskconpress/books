====== Cc. Madhya 20.103 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sādhya'-'sādhana'-tattva puchite nā jāni
> kṛpā kari' saba tattva kaha ta' āpani"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sādhya]]---of the goal of spiritual life; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---of the process of obtaining that goal; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---truth; [[synonyms:p:puchite]]---to inquire; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I do not know; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by Your causeless mercy; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---all such truths; [[synonyms:k:kaha]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---please personally explain to me.

===== Translation =====

**"Actually I do not know how to inquire about the goal of life and the process for obtaining it. Being merciful upon me, please explain all these truths."**
