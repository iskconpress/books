====== Cc. Madhya 20.83 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana jānila ei prabhure nā bhāya
> bhoṭa tyāga karibāre cintilā upāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]] [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī could understand; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---is not approved; [[synonyms:b:bhoṭa]]---the woolen blanket; [[synonyms:t:tyāga]]---giving up; [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to do; [[synonyms:c:cintilā]]---considered; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---a means.

===== Translation =====

**Because Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was repeatedly glancing at this valuable woolen blanket, Sanātana Gosvāmī could understand that the Lord did not approve of it. He then began to consider a way to give it up.**
