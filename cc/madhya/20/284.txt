====== Cc. Madhya 20.284 ======

===== Text =====

> sei puruṣa ananta-koṭi brahmāṇḍa sṛjiyā
> ekaika-mūrtye praveśilā bahu mūrti hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---that Personality of Godhead, Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:k:koṭi]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---millions and trillions of brahmāṇḍas, or universes; [[synonyms:s:sṛjiyā]]---after creating; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:e:eka]]---in each one of them; [[synonyms:m:mūrtye]]---in a form; [[synonyms:p:praveśilā]]---entered; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming many forms.

===== Translation =====

**"After creating the total number of universes, which are unlimited, the Mahā-Viṣṇu expanded Himself into unlimited forms and entered into each of them.**
