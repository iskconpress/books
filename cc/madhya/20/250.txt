====== CC Madhya 20.250 ======

===== Text =====

> prathamei kare kṛṣṇa 'puruṣāvatāra'
> seita puruṣa haya trividha prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathamei]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnation of the three Viṣṇus (Mahā-Viṣṇu, Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu and Kṣīrodakaśāyī Viṣṇu); [[synonyms:s:seita]]---that; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]] [[synonyms:p:pra]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---three different manifestations.

===== Translation =====

**"In the beginning, Kṛṣṇa incarnates Himself as puruṣa-avatāras, or Viṣṇu incarnations. These are of three types.**

===== Purport =====

Up to this verse, the many types of expansions have been described. Now the manifestations of the Lord's different potencies will be described.
