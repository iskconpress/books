====== CC Madhya 20.364 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana kahe,---"yāte īśvara-lakṣaṇa
> pīta-varṇa, kārya---prema-dāna-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sanātana said; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---in whom; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the characteristics of the Lord are found; [[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇa]]---yellowish color; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---activities; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]---distributing love of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---and chanting congregationally the holy name of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī, "The color of the personality in whom the characteristics of the Lord are found is yellowish. His activities include the distribution of love of Godhead and the chanting of the holy names of the Lord.**
