====== CC Madhya 20.188 ======

===== Text =====

> vaibhava-prakāśe āra prābhava-vilāse
> eka-i mūrtye baladeva bhāva-bhede bhāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaibhava]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---in vaibhava manifestation; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:prābhava]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāse]]---in the prābhava pastime form; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:i:i]] [[synonyms:m:mūrtye]]---in one form; [[synonyms:b:baladeva]]---Lord Baladeva; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhede]]---according to different emotions; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---exists.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Balarāma is a vaibhava-prakāśa manifestation of Kṛṣṇa. He is also manifest in the original quadruple expansion of Vāsudeva, Saṅkarṣaṇa, Pradyumna and Aniruddha. These are prābhava-vilāsa expansions with different emotions.**
