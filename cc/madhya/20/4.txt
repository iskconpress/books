====== CC Madhya 20.4 ======

===== Text =====

> patrī pāñā sanātana ānandita hailā
> yavana-rakṣaka-pāśa kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patrī]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---receiving the note; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very pleased; [[synonyms:y:yavana]]---meat-eater; [[synonyms:r:rakṣaka]]---the superintendent of the jail; [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---before; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**When Sanātana Gosvāmī received this note from Rūpa Gosvāmī, he became very pleased. He immediately went to the jail superintendent, who was a meat-eater, and spoke as follows.**
