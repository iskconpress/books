====== CC Madhya 20.136 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche śāstra kahe,---karma, jñāna, yoga tyaji'
> 'bhaktye' kṛṣṇa vaśa haya, bhaktye tāṅre bhaji

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Vedic literatures confirm; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---fruitive activities; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---speculative knowledge; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]---the mystic yoga system; [[synonyms:t:tyaji']]---giving up; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---by devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the Supreme Absolute Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vaśa]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes satisfied; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---by devotional service; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:b:bhaji]]---we worship.

===== Translation =====

**"Revealed scriptures conclude that one should give up fruitive activity, speculative knowledge and the mystic yoga system and instead take to devotional service, by which Kṛṣṇa can be fully satisfied.**
