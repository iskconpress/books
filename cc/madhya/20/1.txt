====== CC Madhya 20.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vande 'nantādbhutaiśvaryaṁ
> śrī-caitanya-mahāprabhum
> nīco 'pi yat-prasādāt syād
> bhakti-śāstra-pravartakaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:a:aiśvaryam]]---possessing opulences; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhum]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nīcaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even a person in the lowest status of life; [[synonyms:y:yat]]-[[synonyms:p:prasādāt]]---by whose mercy; [[synonyms:s:syāt]]---may become; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---of the science of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:pravartakaḥ]]---an inaugurator.

===== Translation =====

**Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who has unlimited, wonderful opulences. By His mercy, even a person born as the lowest of men can spread the science of devotional service.**
