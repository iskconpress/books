====== CC Madhya 20.22 ======

===== Text =====

> dui upavāse kailā randhana-bhojane
> rāja-mantrī sanātana vicārilā mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:u:upa]] [[synonyms:v:vāse]]---fasting for two days; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:r:randhana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---cooking and eating; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:m:mantrī]]---the former minister of the Nawab; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana; [[synonyms:v:vicārilā]]---considered; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Because Sanātana had been fasting for two days, he cooked the food and ate it. However, having formerly been a minister of the Nawab, he began to contemplate the situation.**
