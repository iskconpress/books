====== CC Madhya 20.314 ======

===== Text =====

> pālanārtha svāṁśa viṣṇu-rūpe avatāra
> sattva-guṇa draṣṭā, tāte guṇa-māyā-pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pālana]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---for maintenance; [[synonyms:s:svāṁśa]]---personal plenary expansion; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:s:sattva]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---of the mode of goodness; [[synonyms:d:draṣṭā]]---director; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāra]]---transcendental to the material modes of nature.

===== Translation =====

**"For the maintenance of the universe, Lord Kṛṣṇa descends as His personal plenary expansion in the form of Viṣṇu. He is the director of the mode of goodness; therefore He is transcendental to the material energy.**
