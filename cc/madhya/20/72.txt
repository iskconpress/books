====== CC Madhya 20.72 ======

===== Text =====

> madhyāhna kariyā prabhu gelā bhikṣā karibāre
> sanātane lañā gelā tapana-miśrera ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---finishing bathing at noon; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to accept lunch; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:t:tapana]]-[[synonyms:m:miśrera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the house of Tapana Miśra.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing at noon, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the house of Tapana Miśra for lunch. He took Sanātana Gosvāmī with Him.**
