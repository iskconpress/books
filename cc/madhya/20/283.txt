====== CC Madhya 20.283 ======

===== Text =====

> eita kahiluṅ prathama puruṣera tattva
> dvitīya puruṣera ebe śunaha mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eita]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have explained; [[synonyms:p:prathama]] [[synonyms:p:puruṣera]]---of the first incarnation of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]] [[synonyms:p:puruṣera]]---of the second incarnation of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śunaha]]---please hear; [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---glories.

===== Translation =====

**"I have thus explained the truth of the first Personality of Godhead, Mahā Viṣṇu. I shall now explain the glories of the second Personality of Godhead.**
