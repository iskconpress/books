====== Cc. Madhya 20.75 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā kari' mahāprabhu viśrāma karila
> miśra prabhura śeṣa-pātra sanātane dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after taking His lunch; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---took rest; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the plate of remnants; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered to Sanātana.

===== Translation =====

**After eating, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took rest for a while. Tapana Miśra then gave Sanātana Gosvāmī the remnants of food left by Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
