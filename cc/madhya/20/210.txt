====== CC Madhya 20.210 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera prābhava-vilāsa---vāsudevādi cāri jana
> sei cāri-janāra vilāsa---viṁśati gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prābhava]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---prābhava pastime forms; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Vāsudeva and others; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---quadruple expansions; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:j:janāra]]---of the four-personalities; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastime forms; [[synonyms:v:viṁśati]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counted as twenty.

===== Translation =====

**"Vāsudeva and the three others are direct prābhava pastime forms of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Of these quadruple forms, the pastime expansions are twenty in number.**
