====== Cc. Madhya 20.193 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhā haite punaḥ catur-vyūha-parakāśa
> āvaraṇa-rūpe cāri-dike yāṅra vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from that original //catur-vyūha//; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]]-[[synonyms:p:parakāśa]]---manifestation of quadruple expansions; [[synonyms:ā:āvaraṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in the form of a covering; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---in four directions; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus the original quadruple forms again manifest Themselves in a second quadruple expansion. The residences of these second quadruple expansions cover the four directions.**
