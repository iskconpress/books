====== CC Madhya 20.300 ======

===== Text =====

> līlāvatārera kailuṅ dig-daraśana
> guṇāvatārera ebe śuna vivarana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of the incarnations of pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---indicating the direction only; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of incarnations of the material qualities; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]] [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---hear the description.

===== Translation =====

**"I have given a few examples of pastime incarnations. Now I will describe the guṇa-avatāras, the incarnations of the material qualities. Please listen.**
