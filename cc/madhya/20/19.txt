====== CC Madhya 20.19 ======

===== Text =====

> 'iṅhāra ṭhāñi suvarṇera aṣṭa mohara haya'
> śuni' ānandita bhūñā sanātane kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in the possession of this man; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇera]]---of gold; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]---eight; [[synonyms:m:mohara]]---coins; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:b:bhūñā]]---the landlord; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---to Sanātana; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---says.

===== Translation =====

**The palmist said, "This man Sanātana possesses eight gold coins." Hearing this, the landlord was very pleased and spoke the following to Sanātana Gosvāmī.**
