====== CC Madhya 20.129 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvajñera vākye kare dhanera uddeśe
> aiche veda-purāṇa jīve 'kṛṣṇa' upadeśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvajñera]]---of the astrologer; [[synonyms:v:vāk]] [[synonyms:y:ye]]---the words; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:d:dhanera]]---of the riches; [[synonyms:u:uddeśe]]---news; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:p:purāṇa]]---Vedic literatures; [[synonyms:j:jīve]]---unto the living entity, the conditioned soul; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:upadeśe]]---instructs.

===== Translation =====

**"Just as the words of the astrologer Sarvajña gave news of the poor man's treasure, Vedic literatures advise one about Kṛṣṇa consciousness when one is inquisitive to know why he is in a distressed material condition.**
