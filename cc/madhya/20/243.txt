====== CC Madhya 20.243 ======

===== Text =====

> prakāśa-vilāsera ei kailuṅ vivaraṇa
> svāṁśera bheda ebe śuna, sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsera]]---of pastime forms and manifestations; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have made; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:s:svāṁśera]]---of personal expansions; [[synonyms:b:bhede]]---the differences; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---O Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**"I have already described the pastime and prakāśa forms. Now please hear about the different personal expansions.**
