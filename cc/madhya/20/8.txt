====== CC Madhya 20.8 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṅca sahasra mudrā tumi kara aṅgīkāra
> puṇya, artha,---dui lābha ha-ibe tomāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṅca]] [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---five thousand; [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---golden coins; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---please accept; [[synonyms:p:puṇya]]---pious activity; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---material gain; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:l:lābha]]---two kinds of achievement; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---will be; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---yours.

===== Translation =====

**"Here are five thousand gold coins. Please accept them. By releasing me, you will receive the results of pious activities and gain material profit as well. Thus you will profit in two ways simultaneously."**
