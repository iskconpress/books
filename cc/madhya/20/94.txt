====== CC Madhya 20.94 ======

===== Text =====

> prasanna hañā prabhu tāṅre kṛpā kaila
> tāṅra kṛpāya praśna karite tāṅra śakti haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasanna]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very pleased; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered His causeless mercy; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by His mercy; [[synonyms:p:praśna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to inquire; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was strength.

===== Translation =====

**Being pleased with Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed His causeless mercy upon him. By the Lord's mercy, Sanātana Gosvāmī received the spiritual strength to inquire from Him.**
