====== Cc. Madhya 20.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei yavana kahe,---"śuna, mahāśaya
> tomāre chāḍiba, kintu kari rāja-bhaya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:y:yavana]]---meat-eater; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---just hear; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---my dear sir; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---you; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiba]]---I would release; [[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---I am afraid of the government.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Sanātana Gosvāmī convinced the jailkeeper, who replied, "Please hear me, my dear sir. I am willing to release you, but I am afraid of the government."**
