====== Cc. Madhya 20.12 ======

===== Text =====

> aneka dekhila, tāra lāg nā pāila
> dāḍukā-sahita ḍubi kāhāṅ vahi' gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aneka]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---I looked; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:l:lāg]]---contact; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---could not obtain; [[synonyms:d:dāḍukā]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with the shackles; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubi]]---drowning; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---somewhere; [[synonyms:v:vahi']] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---washed away.

===== Translation =====

**"Tell him, 'I looked for him a long time, but I could not find any trace of him. He jumped in with his shackles, and therefore he was drowned and washed away by the waves.'**
