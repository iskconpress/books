====== CC Madhya 20.191 ======

===== Text =====

> ei cāri haite cabbiśa mūrti parakāśa
> astra-bhede nāma-bheda---vaibhava-vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from these four; [[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]]---twenty-four; [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---forms; [[synonyms:p:parakāśa]]---manifestation; [[synonyms:a:astra]]-[[synonyms:b:bhede]]---according to the different weapons; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:b:bheda]]---the difference of names; [[synonyms:v:vaibhava]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the //vaibhava// pastime expansions.

===== Translation =====

**"From the original quadruple expansion, twenty-four forms are manifest. They differ according to the placement of weapons in Their four hands. They are called vaibhava-vilāsa.**
