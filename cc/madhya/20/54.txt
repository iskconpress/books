====== CC Madhya 20.54 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu tāṅra hāta dhari' lañā gelā
> piṇḍāra upare āpana-pāśe vasāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:h:hāta]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching the hand; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---took him inside; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍāra]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---in an elevated place; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---near Him; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made Sanātana Gosvāmī sit down.

===== Translation =====

**Catching his hand, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took Sanātana Gosvāmī inside and made him sit in an elevated place next to Him.**
