====== CC Madhya 20.388 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-dine haya ṣaṣṭi-daṇḍa-parimāṇa
> tina-sahasra chaya-śata 'pala' tāra māna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---during the whole day and night; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---of sixty daṇḍas (a measure of time); [[synonyms:p:parimāṇa]]---duration; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---three thousand; [[synonyms:c:chaya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]]---six hundred; [[synonyms:p:pala]]---palas; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:m:māna]]---measurement.

===== Translation =====

**"According to Vedic astronomical calculations, the rotation of the sun consists of sixty daṇḍas, and it is divided into thirty-six hundred palas.**
