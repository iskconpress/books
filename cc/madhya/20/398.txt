====== Cc. Madhya 20.398 ======

===== Text =====

> vraje kṛṣṇa---sarvaiśvarya-prakāśe 'pūrṇatama'
> purī-dvaye, paravyome---'pūrṇatara', 'pūrṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---manifestation of His full opulence; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---most complete; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:d:dvaye]]---in Dvārakā and Mathurā; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyome]]---and in the spiritual world; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tara]]---more complete; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---complete.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa is complete in the spiritual sky [Vaikuṇṭha]. He is more complete in Mathurā and Dvārakā, and He is most complete in Vṛndāvana, Vraja, due to His manifesting all His opulences.**

===== Purport =====

This is confirmed in the following three verses from the //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (2.1.221-223).
