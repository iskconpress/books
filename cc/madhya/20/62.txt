====== Cc. Madhya 20.62 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi kahe prabhu,---"śuna, sanātana
> kṛṣṇa---baḍa dayāmaya, patita-pāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---saying this; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---continued to speak; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---My dear Sanātana; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very much; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:p:patita]]-[[synonyms:p:pāvana]]---deliverer of the fallen souls.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "My dear Sanātana, please hear from Me. Kṛṣṇa is very merciful, and He is the deliverer of all fallen souls.**
