====== Cc. Madhya 20.402 ======

===== Text =====

> ei kṛṣṇa---vraje 'pūrṇatama' bhagavān
> āra saba svarūpa---'pūrṇatara' 'pūrṇa' nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the same Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most complete manifestation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---forms; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tara]]---more complete; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---complete; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa is the most complete Supreme Personality of Godhead in Vṛndāvana. Elsewhere all His expansions are either complete or more complete.**
