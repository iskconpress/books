====== CC Madhya 20.396 ======

===== Text =====

> goloka, gokula-dhāma---'vibhu' kṛṣṇa-sama
> kṛṣṇecchāya brahmāṇḍa-gaṇe tāhāra saṅkrama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:goloka]]---the planet known as Goloka; [[synonyms:g:gokula]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---the spiritual land, the pasturing fields for the surabhi cows; [[synonyms:v:vibhu]]---opulent and powerful; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---as much as Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---by the supreme will of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---in each of the universes; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of the Goloka and Gokula dhāmas; [[synonyms:s:saṅkrama]]---appearance.

===== Translation =====

**"The spiritual abode known as Goloka, which is a pasturing land for surabhi cows, is as powerful and opulent as Kṛṣṇa. By the will of Kṛṣṇa, the original Goloka and Gokula dhāmas are manifest with Him in all the universes.**
