====== CC Madhya 20.5 ======

===== Text =====

> "tumi eka jindā-pīra mahā-bhāgyavān
> ketāba-korāṇa-śāstre āche tomāra jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:j:jindā]]-[[synonyms:p:pīra]]---a living saint; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---very fortunate; [[synonyms:k:ketāba]]---books; [[synonyms:k:korāṇa]]---the Koran; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in the scripture; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī told the Mohammedan jailkeeper, "Dear sir, you are a saintly person and are very fortunate. You have full knowledge of the revealed scriptures such as the Koran and similar books.**
