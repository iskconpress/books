====== CC Madhya 20.239 ======

===== Text =====

> nārāyaṇa-bhede nānā astra-bheda-dhara
> ityādika bheda ei saba astra-kara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhede]]---according to the different opinion about the bodily features of Lord Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:a:astra]]---of weapons; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]-[[synonyms:d:dhara]]---differences in holding; [[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādika]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---differentiated; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:a:astra]]-[[synonyms:k:kara]]---weapons in the hands.

===== Translation =====

**"According to the Hayaśīrṣa Pañcarātra, Nārāyaṇa and others are also presented differently as holding the weapons in different hands.**
