====== CC Madhya 20.89 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---'tomāra bhoṭa-kambala kothā gela?'
> prabhu-pade saba kathā gosāñi kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:b:bhoṭa]]-[[synonyms:k:kambala]]---woolen blanket; [[synonyms:k:kothā]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---where did it go; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---unto the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---narration; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said.

===== Translation =====

**When Sanātana Gosvāmī returned, the Lord asked, "Where is your woolen blanket?" Sanātana Gosvāmī then narrated the whole story to the Lord.**
