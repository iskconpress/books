====== Cc. Madhya 20.317 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmā, śiva---ājñā-kārī bhakta-avatāra
> pālanārthe viṣṇu---kṛṣṇera svarūpa-ākāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]-[[synonyms:k:kārī]]---order carriers; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations of devotees; [[synonyms:p:pālana]]-[[synonyms:a:arthe]]---for maintenance; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---in the form of a personal feature.

===== Translation =====

**"The conclusion is that Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva are simply devotee incarnations who carry out orders. However, Lord Viṣṇu, the maintainer, is the personal feature of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
