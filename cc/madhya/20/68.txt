====== CC Madhya 20.68 ======

===== Text =====

> tapana-miśra tabe tāṅre kailā nimantraṇa
> prabhu kahe,---'kṣaura karāha, yāha, sanātana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tapana]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him (Sanātana Gosvāmī); [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:kṣaura]] [[synonyms:k:karāha]]---get shaved; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---go; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---My dear Sanātana.

===== Translation =====

**Tapana Miśra then extended an invitation to Sanātana, and Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked Sanātana to go get a shave.**
