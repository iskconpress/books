====== CC Madhya 20.144 ======

===== Text =====

> vedādi sakala śāstre kṛṣṇa---mukhya sambandha
> tāṅra jñāne ānuṣaṅge yāya māyā-bandha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with the Vedas; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---chief; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]---central point or central attraction; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---by knowledge of Him; [[synonyms:ā:ānuṣaṅge]]---simultaneously; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes away; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:b:bandha]]---the bondage of material existence.

===== Translation =====

**"In all revealed scriptures, beginning with the Vedas, the central point of attraction is Kṛṣṇa. When complete knowledge of Him is realized, the bondage of māyā, the illusory energy, is automatically broken.**
