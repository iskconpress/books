====== CC Madhya 20.150 ======

===== Text =====

> vaikuṇṭha, brahmāṇḍa-gaṇa---śakti-kārya ha ya
> svarūpa-śakti śakti-kāryera---kṛṣṇa samāśraya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---the spiritual world; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---universes of the material world; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:k:kārya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---they are all activities of Kṛṣṇa's potencies; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---of the internal potency; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:k:kāryera]]---of the activities of the external potency; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:samāśraya]]---the original source.

===== Translation =====

**"Both the material and spiritual world are transformations of Kṛṣṇa's internal and external potencies. Therefore Kṛṣṇa is the original source of both material and spiritual manifestations.**
