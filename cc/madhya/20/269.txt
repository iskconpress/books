====== CC Madhya 20.269 ======

===== Text =====

> kāraṇābdhi-pāre māyāra nitya avasthiti
> virajāra pāre paravyome nāhi gati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:abdhi]]-[[synonyms:p:pāre]]---on one bank of the Causal Ocean; [[synonyms:m:māyāra]]---of the material energy; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---eternal; [[synonyms:a:avasthiti]]---position; [[synonyms:v:virajāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---on the other bank of the Virajā, or the Causal Ocean; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyome]]---in the spiritual world or sky; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:g:gati]]---admission.

===== Translation =====

**"The Virajā, or Causal Ocean, is the border between the spiritual and material worlds. The material energy is situated on one shore of that ocean, and it cannot enter onto the other shore, which is the spiritual sky.**
