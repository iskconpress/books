====== CC Madhya 20.393 ======

===== Text =====

> alāta-cakra-prāya sei līlā-cakra phire
> saba līlā saba brahmāṇḍe krame udaya kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alāta]]-[[synonyms:c:cakra]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---exactly like a wheel of fire; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:c:cakra]]---the cycle of Kṛṣṇa's pastimes; [[synonyms:p:phire]]---turns; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---all these pastimes; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍe]]---in all the universes; [[synonyms:k:krame]]---one after another; [[synonyms:u:udaya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---become manifest.

===== Translation =====

**"The cycle of His pastimes turns like a wheel of fire. Thus Kṛṣṇa exhibits His pastimes one after the other in every universe.**
