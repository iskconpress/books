====== Cc. Madhya 20.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi yavana-mana prasanna nā dekhilā
> sāta-hājāra mudrā tāra āge rāśi kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:y:yavana]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of the meat-eater; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---he saw; [[synonyms:s:sāta]]-[[synonyms:h:hājāra]]---seven thousand; [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---golden coins; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:r:rāśi]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made a stack.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī could see that the mind of the meat-eater was still not satisfied. He then stacked Seven thousand gold coins before him.**
