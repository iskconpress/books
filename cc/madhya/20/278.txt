====== Cc. Madhya 20.278 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅho mahat-sraṣṭā puruṣa---'mahā-viṣṇu' nāma
> ananta brahmāṇḍa tāṅra loma-kūpe dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]-[[synonyms:s:sraṣṭā]]---the creator of the mahat-tattva, or total material energy; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the person; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---called Lord Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---universes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of His body; [[synonyms:l:loma]]-[[synonyms:k:kūpe]]---within the hair holes; [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---are situated.

===== Translation =====

**"The first form of Lord Viṣṇu is called Mahā-Viṣṇu. He is the original creator of the total material energy. The innumerable universes emanate from the pores of His body.**
