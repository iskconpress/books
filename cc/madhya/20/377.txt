====== CC Madhya 20.377 ======

===== Text =====

> eita kahiluṅ śakty-āveśa-avatāra
> bālya-paugaṇḍa-dharmera śunaha vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eita]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have explained; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnations specifically empowered; [[synonyms:b:bālya]]---in childhood; [[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]---in boyhood; [[synonyms:d:dharmera]]---of the characteristics; [[synonyms:ś:śunaha]]---now hear; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---the consideration.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus I have explained specifically empowered incarnations. Now please hear about the characteristics of Lord Kṛṣṇa's childhood, boyhood and youth.**
