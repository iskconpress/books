====== CC Madhya 20.36 ======

===== Text =====

> tāre vidāya diyā gosāñi calilā ekalā
> hāte karoṅyā, chiṅḍā kānthā, nirbhaya ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---bidding him farewell; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:c:calilā]] [[synonyms:e:ekalā]]---began to travel alone; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand; [[synonyms:k:karoṅyā]]---a beggar's pot; [[synonyms:c:chiṅḍā]] [[synonyms:k:kānthā]]---a torn quilt; [[synonyms:n:nirbhaya]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---he became free from all anxiety.

===== Translation =====

**After departing from Īśāna, Sanātana Gosvāmī began traveling alone with a waterpot in his hand. Simply covered with a torn quilt, he thus lost all his anxiety.**
