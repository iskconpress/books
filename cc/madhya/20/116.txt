====== CC Madhya 20.116 ======

===== Text =====

> apareyam itas tv anyam
> prakṛtiṁ viddhi me parām
> jīva-bhūtāṁ mahā-bāho
> yayedaṁ dhāryate jagat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparā]]---inferior energy; [[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this material world; [[synonyms:i:itaḥ]]---beyond this; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:a:anyām]]---another; [[synonyms:p:prakṛtim]]---energy; [[synonyms:v:viddhi]]---you must know; [[synonyms:m:me]]---of Me; [[synonyms:p:parām]]---which is superior energy; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtām]]---they are the living entities; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bāho]]---O mighty-armed; [[synonyms:y:yayā]]---by which; [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this material world; [[synonyms:d:dhāryate]]---is being conducted; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the cosmic manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Besides this inferior nature, O mighty-armed Arjuna, there is a superior energy of Mine, which consists of all living entities who are struggling with material nature and are sustaining the universe.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from //Bhagavad-gītā// [[books:bg:7:5|(Bg. 7.5)]]. It is also quoted in the //Ādi-līlā// (Chapter Seven, verse 118).
