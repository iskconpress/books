====== CC Madhya 20.374 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vibhūti' kahiye yaiche gītā-ekādaśe
> jagat vyāpila kṛṣṇa-śakty-ābhāsāveśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vibhūti]]---specific power; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---we say; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just like; [[synonyms:g:gītā]]---of Bhagavad-gītā; [[synonyms:e:ekādaśe]]---in the Eleventh Chapter; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---throughout the whole universe; [[synonyms:v:vyāpila]]---He expanded; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---by the reflection of His power.

===== Translation =====

**"As explained in the Eleventh Chapter of Bhagavad-gītā, Kṛṣṇa has spread Himself all over the universe in many personalities through specific powers, known as vibhūti.**

===== Purport =====

The expansion of specific //māyā// powers is explained in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// [[books:sb:2:7:39|(2.7.39)]].
