====== Cc. Madhya 20.200 ======

===== Text =====

> jyaiṣṭhe---trivikrama, āṣāḍhe---vāmana deveśa
> śrāvaṇe---śrīdhara, bhādre---deva hṛṣīkeśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jyaiṣṭhe]]---in the month of Jyaiṣṭha (May-June); [[synonyms:t:trivikrama]]---Trivikrama; [[synonyms:ā:āṣāḍhe]]---in the month of Āṣāḍha (June-July); [[synonyms:v:vāmana]] [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśa]]---Lord Vāmana; [[synonyms:ś:śrāvaṇe]]---in the month of Śrāvaṇa (July-August); [[synonyms:ś:śrīdhara]]---Śrīdhara; [[synonyms:b:bhādre]]---in the month of Bhādra (August-September); [[synonyms:d:deva]] [[synonyms:h:hṛṣīkeśa]]---Lord Hṛṣīkeśa.

===== Translation =====

**"In the month of Jyaiṣṭha, the predominating Deity is Trivikrama. In Āṣāḍha the Deity is Vāmana, in Śrāvaṇa the Deity is Śrīdhara, and in Bhādra the Deity is Hṛṣīkeśa.**
