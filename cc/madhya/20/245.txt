====== Cc. Madhya 20.245 ======

===== Text =====

> avatāra haya kṛṣṇera ṣaḍ-vidha prakāra
> puruṣāvatāra eka, līlāvatāra āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---six kinds; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations of Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations for the execution of pastimes; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also.

===== Translation =====

**"There are six types of incarnations [avatāras] of Kṛṣṇa. One is the incarnations of Viṣṇu [puruṣa-avatāras], and another is the incarnations meant for the performance of pastimes [līlā-avatāras].**
