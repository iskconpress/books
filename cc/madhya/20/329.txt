====== CC Madhya 20.329 ======

===== Text =====

> yugāvatāra ebe śuna, sanātana
> satya-tretā-dvāpara-kali-yugera gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation of millenniums; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---O Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:satya]]-[[synonyms:t:tretā]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāpara]]-[[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:y:yugera]]---of the Satya-yuga, Tretā-yuga, Dvāpara-yuga and Kali-yuga; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---the chronological order.

===== Translation =====

**"O Sanātana, now hear from Me about the yuga-avatāras, the incarnations for the millenniums. First of all, there are four yugas-Satya-yuga, Tretā-yuga, Dvāpara-yuga and Kali-yuga.**
