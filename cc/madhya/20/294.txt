====== CC Madhya 20.294 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīya-puruṣa viṣṇu---'guṇa-avatāra'
> dui avatāra-bhitara gaṇanā tāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the third Personality; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnation of the material quality of goodness; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bhitara]]---within the two incarnations; [[synonyms:g:gaṇanā]]-[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---He is designated.

===== Translation =====

**"The third expansion of Viṣṇu is the Kṣīrodakaśāyī Viṣṇu, who is the incarnation of the quality of goodness. He is to be counted within the two types of incarnations [puruṣa-avatāras and guṇa-avatāras].**
