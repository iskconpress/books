====== CC Madhya 20.254 ======

===== Text =====

> icchā-jñāna-kriyā vinā nā haya sṛjana
> tinera tina-śakti meli' prapañca-racana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:icchā]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:k:kriyā]]---thinking, feeling, willing, knowledge and activity; [[synonyms:v:vina]]---without; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sṛjana]]---creation; [[synonyms:t:tinera]]---of the three; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---three potencies; [[synonyms:m:meli']]---being amalgamated; [[synonyms:p:prapañca]]-[[synonyms:r:racana]]---there is the cosmic manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**"There is no possibility of creation without thinking, feeling, willing, knowledge and activity. The combination of the supreme will, knowledge and action brings about the cosmic manifestation.**
