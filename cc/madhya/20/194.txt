====== Cc. Madhya 20.194 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-janera punaḥ pṛthak tina tina mūrti
> keśavādi yāhā haite vilāsera pūrti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:j:janera]]---of the original of the four expansions; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separate; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three each; [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---forms; [[synonyms:k:keśava]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with Lord Keśava; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from which; [[synonyms:v:vilāsera]] [[synonyms:p:pūrti]]---the vilāsa expansions are fulfilled.

===== Translation =====

**"Again these quadruple forms expand three times, beginning with Keśava. That is the fulfillment of the pastime forms.**
