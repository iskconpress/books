====== CC Madhya 20.98 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sanātana prabhura caraṇe dhariyā
> dainya vinati kare dante tṛṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]---humility; [[synonyms:v:vinati]]---bowing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:d:dante]]---in the teeth; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]---a straw; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Putting a straw in his mouth and bowing down, Sanātana Gosvāmī clasped the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and humbly spoke as follows.**
