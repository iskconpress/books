====== CC Madhya 20.82 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātanera vairāgye prabhura ānanda apāra
> bhoṭa-kambala pāne prabhu cāhe bāre bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vairāgye]]---by the renunciation; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---happiness; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:b:bhoṭa]]-[[synonyms:k:kambala]]---the woolen blanket; [[synonyms:p:pāne]]---towards; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---looks; [[synonyms:b:bāre]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---repeatedly.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt unlimited happiness to observe Sanātana Gosvāmī's strict following of the principles of sannyāsa. However, He repeatedly glanced at the woolen blanket Sanātana Gosvāmī was wearing.**
