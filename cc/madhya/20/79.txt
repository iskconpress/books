====== CC Madhya 20.79 ======

===== Text =====

> mahārāṣṭrīya dvije prabhu milāilā sanātane
> sei vipra tāṅre kaila mahā-nimantraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:r:rāṣṭrīya]]---from Mahārāṣṭra; [[synonyms:d:dvije]]---the brāhmaṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---brāhmaṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---full; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇe]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**When Caitanya Mahāprabhu introduced the Mahārāṣṭrīya brāhmaṇa to Sanātana, the brāhmaṇa immediately invited Sanātana Gosvāmī for full meals.**
