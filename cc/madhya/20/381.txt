====== CC Madhya 20.381 ======

===== Text =====

> pūtanā-vadhādi yata līlā kṣaṇe kṣaṇe
> saba līlā nitya prakaṭa kare anukrame

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūtanā]]-[[synonyms:v:vadha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---killing of the demons like Pūtanā; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---one moment after another; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---all these pastimes; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---eternally; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:anukrame]]---one after another.

===== Translation =====

**"When Lord Kṛṣṇa appears, from moment to moment He exhibits His different pastimes, beginning with the killing of Pūtanā. All these pastimes are eternally being demonstrated one after another.**
