====== Cc. Madhya 20.323 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta brahmāṇḍe aiche karaha gaṇana
> mahā-viṣṇu eka-śvāse brahmāra jīvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍe]]---in innumerable universes; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---just try to count; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Lord Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śvāse]]---by one exhalation; [[synonyms:b:brahmāra]] [[synonyms:j:jīvana]]---the duration of life of one Brahmā.

===== Translation =====

**"The number of manvantara-avatāras for only one universe has been given. One can only imagine how many manvantara-avatāras exist in the innumerable universes. And all these universes and Brahmās exist only during one exhalation of Mahā-Viṣṇu.**
