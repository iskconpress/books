====== CC Madhya 20.285 ======

===== Text =====

> praveśa kariyā dekhe, saba---andhakāra
> rahite nāhika sthāna, karilā vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:praveśa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---after entering; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---He sees; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:a:andhakāra]]---complete darkness; [[synonyms:r:rahite]]---to remain there; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---there was no place; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---then He considered.

===== Translation =====

**"When Mahā-Viṣṇu entered each of the limitless universes, He saw that there was darkness all around and that there was no place to stay. He therefore began to consider the situation.**
