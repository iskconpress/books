====== Cc. Madhya 20.282 ======

===== Text =====

> samasta brahmāṇḍa-gaṇera iṅho antaryāmī
> kāraṇābdhiśāyī---saba jagatera svāmī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:samasta]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the aggregate of the brahmāṇḍas, or universes; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---that Lord Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:a:antaryāmī]]---the Supersoul; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:abdhi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---Lord Mahā-Viṣṇu, lying on the Causal Ocean; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of all the universes; [[synonyms:s:svāmī]]---the Supreme Lord.

===== Translation =====

**"Mahā-Viṣṇu is the Supersoul of all the universes. Lying on the Causal Ocean, He is the master of all material worlds.**
