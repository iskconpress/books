====== Cc. Madhya 20.405 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā yei śune, paḍe, sei bhāgyavān
> kṛṣṇera svarūpa-tattvera haya kichu jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this narration; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---anyone who hears; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---or reads; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---such a person; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---is most fortunate; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:t:tattvera]]---of personal bodily features; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever hears or recites these descriptions of the expansions of Kṛṣṇa's body is certainly a very fortunate man. Although this is very difficult to understand, one can nonetheless acquire some knowledge about the different features of Kṛṣṇa's body.**
