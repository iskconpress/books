====== CC Madhya 20.287 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra nābhi-padma haite uṭhila eka padma
> sei padme ha-ila brahmāra janma-sadma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nābhi]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from His lotus navel; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---grew; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:p:padma]]---lotus flower; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:padme]]---on that lotus flower; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:brahmāra]]---of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:s:sadma]]---the place of generation.

===== Translation =====

**"A lotus flower then sprouted from the lotus navel of that Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu. That lotus flower became Lord Brahmā's birthplace.**
