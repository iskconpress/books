====== CC Madhya 20.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe bhūñā gosāñira saṅge cāri pāika dila
> rātrye rātrye vana-pathe parvata pāra kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:b:bhūñā]]---the landlord; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:p:pāika]]---four watchmen; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]] [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---during the whole night; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the jungle path; [[synonyms:p:parvata]]---the hilly tract of land; [[synonyms:p:pāra]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---took him across.

===== Translation =====

**After this settlement was made, the landlord gave Sanātana Gosvāmī four watchmen to accompany him. They went through the forest path for the whole night and thus brought him over the hilly tract of land.**
