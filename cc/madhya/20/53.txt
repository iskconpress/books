====== CC Madhya 20.53 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-jane galāgali rodana apāra
> dekhi' candraśekharera ha-ila camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---the two persons; [[synonyms:g:galāgali]]---shoulder to shoulder; [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---crying; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:c:candraśekharera]]---of Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Shoulder to shoulder, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Sanātana Gosvāmī began to cry unlimitedly. Candraśekhara was very astonished to see this.**
