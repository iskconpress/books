====== CC Madhya 20.92 ======

===== Text =====

> tina mudrāra bhoṭa gāya, mādhukarī grāsa
> dharma-hāni haya, loka kare upahāsa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:m:mudrāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhoṭa]]---a woolen blanket costing three gold coins; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---on the body; [[synonyms:m:mādhukarī]] [[synonyms:g:grāsa]]---and practicing the mādhukarī system; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:h:hāni]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---that is a religious discrepancy; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:u:upahāsa]]---people will joke.

===== Translation =====

**"It is contradictory to practice mādhukarī and at the same time wear a valuable blanket. One loses his spiritual strength by doing this, and one will also become an object for jokes."**
