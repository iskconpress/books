~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 20.216~~

====== CC Madhya 20.216 ======

===== Text =====

> prayāge mādhava, mandāre śrī-madhusūdana
> ānandāraṇye vāsudeva, padmanābha janārdana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prayāge]]---at Prayāga; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]---Bindu Mādhava; [[synonyms:m:mandāre]]---at Mandāra-parvata; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:madhusūdana]]---Śrī Madhusūdana; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:a:araṇye]]---at the place known as Ānandāraṇya; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Lord Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:p:padmanābha]]---Lord Padmanābha; [[synonyms:j:janārdana]]---Lord Janārdana.

===== Translation =====

**"At Prayāga, the Lord is situated as Bindu Mādhava, and at Mandāra-parvata, the Lord is known as Madhusūdana. Vāsudeva, Padmanābha and Janārdana reside at Ānandāraṇya.**
