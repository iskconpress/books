====== CC Madhya 20.167 ======

===== Text =====

> 'prābhava-vaibhava'-rūpe dvividha prakāśe
> eka-vapu bahu rūpa yaiche haila rāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prābhava]]---prābhava; [[synonyms:v:vaibhava]]---vaibhava; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in forms; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]] [[synonyms:p:pra]]-[[synonyms:k:kāśe]]---twofold manifestations; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:v:vapu]]---the same original form; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---expanded into unlimited numbers; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---like; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it was; [[synonyms:r:rāse]]---while dancing in the rāsa dance with the gopīs.

===== Translation =====

**"In His original form, Kṛṣṇa manifests Himself in two features-prābhava and vaibhava. He expands His one original form into many, as He did during the rāsa-līlā dance.**
