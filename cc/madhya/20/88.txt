====== Cc. Madhya 20.88 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' kāṅthā la-ila, bhoṭa tāṅre diyā
> gosāñira ṭhāṅi āilā kāṅthā gale diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:k:kāṅthā]] [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---he took the quilt; [[synonyms:b:bhoṭa]]---the blanket; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāṅi]]---to Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:k:kāṅthā]]---quilt; [[synonyms:g:gale]]---onto the shoulder; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---keeping.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Sanātana Gosvāmī exchanged the blanket for the quilt. He then returned to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with the quilt on his shoulder.**
