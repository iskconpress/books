====== CC Madhya 20.211 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhā-sabāra pṛthak vaikuṇṭha---paravyoma-dhāme
> pūrvādi aṣṭa-dike tina tina krame

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhā]]---of them; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separate; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---a Vaikuṇṭha planet; [[synonyms:p:paravyoma]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāme]]---in the spiritual world; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning from the east; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---in the eight directions; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three in each; [[synonyms:k:krame]]---in consecutive order.

===== Translation =====

**"All these forms preside over different Vaikuṇṭha planets in the spiritual world, beginning from the east in consecutive order. In each of eight directions, there are three different forms.**
