====== CC Madhya 20.74 ======

===== Text =====

> miśra kahe,---'sanātanera kichu kṛtya āche
> tumi bhikṣā kara, prasāda tāṅre diba pāche'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Tapana Miśra said; [[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---duty; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You take Your lunch; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of Your food; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:diba]]---I shall deliver; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at the end.

===== Translation =====

**Tapana Miśra then said, "Sanātana has some duty to perform; therefore he cannot accept lunch now. At the conclusion of the meal, I shall supply Sanātana with some remnants."**
