====== Cc. Madhya 20.179 ======

===== Text =====

> govindera mādhurī dekhi' vāsudevera kṣobha
> se mādhurī āsvādite upajaya lobha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govindera]]---of Lord Govinda; [[synonyms:m:mādhurī]]---the sweetness; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vāsudevera]]---of Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:k:kṣobha]]---agitation; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mādhurī]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādite]]---to taste; [[synonyms:u:upajaya]]---awakens; [[synonyms:l:lobha]]---greed.

===== Translation =====

**"Indeed, Vāsudeva is agitated just to see the sweetness of Govinda, and a transcendental greed awakens in Him to enjoy that sweetness.**
