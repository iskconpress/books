====== CC Madhya 20.32 ======

===== Text =====

> gosāñi kahe,---"keha dravya la-ibe āmā māri'
> āmāra prāṇa rakṣā kara dravya aṅgīkari' "

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone else; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---the valuable coins; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---will take; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:m:māri']]---killing me; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---life; [[synonyms:r:rakṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---save; [[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkari']]---by accepting these coins.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī replied, "If you do not accept these coins, someone else will kill me for them. It is better that you save me from the danger by accepting the coins."**
