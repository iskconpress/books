====== CC Madhya 20.331 ======

===== Text =====

> āsan varṇās trayo hy asya
> gṛhṇato 'nu-yugaṁ tanūḥ
> śuklo raktas tathā pīta
> idānīṁ kṛṣṇatāṁ gataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsan]]---there were; [[synonyms:v:varṇāḥ]]---colors; [[synonyms:t:trayaḥ]]---three; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of your son; [[synonyms:g:gṛhṇataḥ]]---accepting; [[synonyms:a:anu]]-[[synonyms:y:yugam]]---according to the millennium; [[synonyms:t:tanūḥ]]---body; [[synonyms:ś:śuklaḥ]]---white; [[synonyms:r:raktaḥ]]---red; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---as well as; [[synonyms:p:pītaḥ]]---yellow; [[synonyms:i:idānīm]]---just now; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇatām]] [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---He has assumed a blackish hue.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'This child formerly had three colors according to the prescribed color for different millenniums. Formerly He was white, red and yellow, and now He has assumed a blackish color.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.8.13) was spoken by Gargamuni when performing the name-giving ceremony for Kṛṣṇa at the house of Nanda Mahārāja. The following two verses are also from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (11.5.21,24).
