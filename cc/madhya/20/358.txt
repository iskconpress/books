====== CC Madhya 20.358 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgavatārambhe vyāsa maṅgalācaraṇe
> 'parameśvara' nirūpila ei dui lakṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārambhe]]---in the beginning of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---the great author Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇe]]---in the auspicious invocation; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nirūpila]]---has described; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇe]]---by these two characteristics, namely svarūpa (personal) and taṭastha (marginal) symptoms.

===== Translation =====

**"In the auspicious invocation in the beginning of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, Śrīla Vyāsadeva has described the Supreme Personality of Godhead by these symptoms.**
