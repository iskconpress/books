====== CC Madhya 20.389 ======

===== Text =====

> sūryodaya haite ṣaṣṭi-pala-kramodaya
> sei eka daṇḍa, aṣṭa daṇḍe 'prahara' haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūrya]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---beginning from the sunrise; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:p:pala]]---sixty palas; [[synonyms:k:krama]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---gradually rising higher and higher; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---one daṇḍa; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍe]]---in eight daṇḍas; [[synonyms:p:prahara]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is a prahara.

===== Translation =====

**"The sun rises in steps consisting of sixty palas. Sixty palas equal one daṇḍa, and eight daṇḍas comprise one prahara.**
