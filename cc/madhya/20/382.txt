====== Cc. Madhya 20.382 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta brahmāṇḍa, tāra nāhika gaṇana
> kona līlā kona brahmāṇḍe haya prakaṭana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---innumerable universes; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---there is no counting; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---some pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍe]]---in some universe; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭana]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**"The consecutive pastimes of Kṛṣṇa are manifest in one of the innumerable universes moment after moment. There is no possibility of counting the universes, but in any case some pastime of the Lord is being manifest at every moment in one universe or another.**
