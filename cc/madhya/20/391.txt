====== CC Madhya 20.391 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche kṛṣṇera līlā-maṇḍala caudda-manvantare
> brahmāṇḍa-maṇḍala vyāpi' krame krame phire

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in the same way; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---groups of different pastimes; [[synonyms:c:caudda]]-[[synonyms:m:manvantare]]---in the duration of fourteen Manus; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---all of the universes; [[synonyms:v:vyāpi']]---spreading through; [[synonyms:k:krame]] [[synonyms:k:krame]]---gradually; [[synonyms:p:phire]]---return.

===== Translation =====

**"Just like the sun, there is an orbit to Kṛṣṇa's pastimes, which are manifest one after the other. During the lifetime of fourteen Manus, this orbit expands through all the universes, and gradually it returns. Thus Kṛṣṇa moves with His pastimes through all the universes, one after another.**
