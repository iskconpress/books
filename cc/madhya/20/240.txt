====== CC Madhya 20.240 ======

===== Text =====

> 'svayaṁ bhagavān' āra 'līlā-puruṣottama'
> ei dui nāma dhare vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]]---the Lord Puruṣottama of pastimes; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]---these two; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---names; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---takes; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa, the original Supreme Personality of Godhead, indicated as the son of Mahārāja Nanda, has two names. One is svayaṁ bhagavān, and the other is līlā-puruṣottama.**
