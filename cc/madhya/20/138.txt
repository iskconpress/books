====== Cc. Madhya 20.138 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaktyāham ekayā grāhyaḥ
> śraddhayātmā priyaḥ satām
> bhaktiḥ punāti man-niṣṭhā
> śva-pākān api sambhavāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaktyā]]---by devotional service; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:e:ekayā]]---unflinching; [[synonyms:g:grāhyaḥ]]---obtainable; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---by faith; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---the most dear; [[synonyms:p:priyaḥ]]---to be served; [[synonyms:s:satām]]---by the devotees; [[synonyms:b:bhaktiḥ]]---the devotional service; [[synonyms:p:punāti]]---purifies; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:n:niṣṭhā]]---fixed only on Me; [[synonyms:ś:śva]]-[[synonyms:p:pākān]]---the lowest of human beings, who are accustomed to eat dogs; [[synonyms:a:api]]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:sambhavāt]]---from all faults due to birth and other circumstances.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Being very dear to the devotees and sādhus, I am attained through unflinching faith and devotional service. This bhakti-yoga system, which gradually increases attachment for Me, purifies even a human being born among dog-eaters. That is to say, everyone can be elevated to the spiritual platform by the process of bhakti-yoga.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (11.14.21).
