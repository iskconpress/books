====== CC Madhya 20.31 ======

===== Text =====

> santuṣṭa ha-ilāṅa āmi, mohara nā la-iba
> puṇya lāgi' parvata tomā' pāra kari' diba"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:santuṣṭa]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilāṅa]]---have become; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:m:mohara]]---the golden coins; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iba]]---I shall not take; [[synonyms:p:puṇya]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---simply for pious activity; [[synonyms:p:parvata]]---the hilly tract of land; [[synonyms:t:tomā']]---you; [[synonyms:p:pāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:d:diba]]---I shall get across.

===== Translation =====

**"I am very satisfied with your behavior. I shall not accept these gold coins, but I shall get you across that hilly tract of land simply to perform a pious activity."**
