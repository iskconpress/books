====== Cc. Madhya 20.297 ======

===== Text =====

> līlāvatāra kṛṣṇera nā yāya gaṇana
> pradhāna kariyā kahi dig-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations for pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---are not countable; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---chiefly; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---let me describe; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---by a sample direction.

===== Translation =====

**"No one can count the innumerable pastime incarnations of Lord Kṛṣṇa, but I shall describe the principal ones.**
