====== Cc. Madhya 20.226 ======

===== Text =====

> paravyome vāsudevādi---nija nija astra-dhara
> tāṅra mata kahi, ye-saba astra-kara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyome]]---in the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with Lord Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]---Their own respective; [[synonyms:a:astra]]-[[synonyms:d:dhara]]---holding of different weapons; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mata]] [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I am speaking the opinion of Siddhārtha-saṁhitā; [[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:a:astra]]-[[synonyms:k:kara]]---weapons in the different hands.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus in the spiritual sky the expansions, headed by Vāsudeva, hold weapons in Their own respective order. I am repeating the opinion of Siddhārtha-saṁhitā in describing Them.**
