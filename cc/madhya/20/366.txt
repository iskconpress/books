====== CC Madhya 20.366 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---caturāli chāḍa, sanātana
> śaktyāveśāvatārera śuna vivarana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:c:caturāli]]---very intelligent argument; [[synonyms:c:chāḍa]]---give up; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---O Sanātana; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of the especially empowered incarnations; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---the description.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "O Sanātana, you must give up your intelligent tricks. Just try to understand the meaning of the śaktyāveśa-avatāra.**
