====== CC Madhya 20.404 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta svarūpa kṛṣṇera nāhika gaṇana
> śākhā-candra-nyāye kari dig-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---forms; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---there is no counting; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:n:nyāye]]---by the logic of showing the moon through the branches of a tree; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---only partial showing.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way Kṛṣṇa's transcendental forms are expanded unlimitedly. No one can count them. Whatever I have explained is simply a little glimpse. It is like showing the moon through the branches of a tree."**
