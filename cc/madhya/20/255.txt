====== Cc. Madhya 20.255 ======

===== Text =====

> kriyā-śakti-pradhāna saṅkarṣaṇa balarāma
> prākṛtāprākṛta-sṛṣṭi karena nirmāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kriyā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the predominator of the creative energy; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]---Lord Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:b:balarāma]]---Lord Balarāma; [[synonyms:p:prākṛta]]---material; [[synonyms:a:aprākṛta]]---spiritual; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---worlds; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nirmāṇa]]---creation.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Saṅkarṣaṇa is Lord Balarāma. Being the predominator of the creative energy, He creates both the material and spiritual worlds.**
