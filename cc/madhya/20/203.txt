====== Cc. Madhya 20.203 ======

===== Text =====

> ei cāri-janera vilāsa-mūrti āra aṣṭa jana
> tāṅ sabāra nāma kahi, śuna sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:j:janera]]---of the four personalities; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---pastime forms; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---more; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---eight personalities; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]] [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy names; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I shall mention; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---O Sanātana.

===== Translation =====

**"From Vāsudeva, Saṅkarṣaṇa, Pradyumna and Aniruddha, there are eight additional pastime expansions. O Sanātana, please hear Me as I mention Their names.**
