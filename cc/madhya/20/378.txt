====== CC Madhya 20.378 ======

===== Text =====

> kiśora-śekhara-dharmī vrajendra-nandana
> prakaṭa-līlā karibāre yabe kare mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kiśora]]-[[synonyms:ś:śekhara]]---topmost of youth; [[synonyms:d:dharmī]]---whose natural position; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Mahārāja Nanda; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---manifested pastimes; [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to perform; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"As the son of Mahārāja Nanda, Lord Kṛṣṇa is by nature the paragon of kiśora [youth]. He chooses to exhibit His pastimes at that age.**
