====== CC Madhya 20.21 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' anna dila kariyā sammāna
> sanātana āsi' tabe kaila nadī-snāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---supplied food grains; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---showing great respect; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing in the river.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, the landlord offered Sanātana grains to cook. Sanātana then went to the riverside and took his bath.**
