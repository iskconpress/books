====== Cc. Madhya 20.55 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-haste karena tāṅra aṅga sammārjana
> teṅho kahe,---'more, prabhu, nā kara sparśana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---by the spiritual hand; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---of his body; [[synonyms:s:sammārjana]]---cleansing; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he said; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:s:sparśana]]---do not touch.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began cleansing Sanātana Gosvāmī's body with His own transcendental had, Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "O my Lord, please do not touch me."**
