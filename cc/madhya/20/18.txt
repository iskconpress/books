====== CC Madhya 20.18 ======

===== Text =====

> sei bhūñāra saṅge haya hāta-gaṇitā
> bhūñāra kāṇe kahe sei jāni' ei kathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhūñāra]]---the landlord; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:h:hāta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇita]]---an expert in palmistry; [[synonyms:b:bhūñāra]]---of the landlord; [[synonyms:k:kāṇe]]---in the ear; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that man; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---this statement.

===== Translation =====

**A man who was expert in palmistry was at that time staying with the landlord. Knowing about Sanātana, he whispered the following in the landlord's ear.**
