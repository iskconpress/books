====== CC Madhya 20.319 ======

===== Text =====

> manvantarāvatāra ebe śuna, sanātana
> asaṅkhya gaṇana tāṅra, śunaha kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manu]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the Manu incarnations; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---O Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of them; [[synonyms:ś:śunaha]]---just hear; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause.

===== Translation =====

**"O Sanātana, just hear about the Manu incarnations [manvantara-avatāras]. They are unlimited, and no one can count them. Just hear of their source.**
