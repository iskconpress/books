====== CC Madhya 20.105 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-śakti dhara tumi, jāna tattva-bhāva
> jāni' dārḍhya lāgi' puche,---sādhura svabhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---the energy of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dhara]]---process; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:j:jāna]]---know; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the factual position; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---although knowing all these things; [[synonyms:d:dārḍhya]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the sake of strictness; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---he inquires; [[synonyms:s:sādhura]]---of the saintly persons; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the nature.

===== Translation =====

**"Since you possess Lord Kṛṣṇa's potency, you certainly know these things. However, it is the nature of a sādhu to inquire. Although he knows these things, the sādhu inquires for the sake of strictness.**
