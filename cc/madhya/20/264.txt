====== Cc. Madhya 20.264 ======

===== Text =====

> māyātīta paravyome sabāra avasthāna
> viśve avatari' dhare 'avatāra' nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:a:atīta]]---beyond the material nature; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyome]]---in the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---all of them; [[synonyms:a:avasthāna]]---residence; [[synonyms:v:viśve]]---within the material universe; [[synonyms:a:avatari']]---coming down; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---take; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name avatāra.

===== Translation =====

**"All the expansions of Lord Kṛṣṇa are actually residents of the spiritual world. However, when they descend into the material world, they are called incarnations [avatāras].**
