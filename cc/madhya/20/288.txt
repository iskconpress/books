====== CC Madhya 20.288 ======

===== Text =====

> sei padma-nāle ha-ila caudda bhuvana
> teṅho 'brahmā' hañā sṛṣṭi karila sṛjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāle]]---within the stem of that lotus; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became manifested; [[synonyms:c:caudda]]---fourteen; [[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---planetary systems; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---having become; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---the material creation; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:s:sṛjana]]---created.

===== Translation =====

**"In the stem of that lotus flower the fourteen worlds were generated. Then He became Lord Brahmā and created the entire universe.**
