====== CC Madhya 20.367 ======

===== Text =====

> śaktyāveśāvatāra kṛṣṇera asaṅkhya gaṇana
> dig-daraśana kari mukhya mukhya jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnations especially empowered by the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---unlimited and innumerable; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---let Me describe some of them; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---who are counted as the chief.

===== Translation =====

**"There are unlimited śaktyāveśa-avatāras of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Let Me describe the chief among them.**
