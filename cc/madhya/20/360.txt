====== CC Madhya 20.360 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloke 'paraṁ'-śabde 'kṛṣṇa'-nirūpaṇa
> 'satyaṁ' śabde kahe tāṅra svarūpa-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in this verse; [[synonyms:p:param]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word param, or supreme; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nirūpaṇa]]---there is an indication; [[synonyms:s:satyam]] [[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word satyam, or Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---indicates; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---personal characteristics.

===== Translation =====

**"In this invocation from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, the word param indicates Lord Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, and the word satyam indicates His personal characteristics.**
