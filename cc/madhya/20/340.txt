====== CC Madhya 20.340 ======

===== Text =====

> pita'-varṇa dhari' tabe kailā pravartana
> prema-bhakti dilā loke lañā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---assuming the color yellow; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:p:pravartana]]---introduced the saṅkīrtana movement; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---He distributed love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---to the people in general; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---accompanied by His devotees.

===== Translation =====

**"Accompanied by His personal devotees, Lord Kṛṣṇa, assuming a golden color, introduces the hari-nāma-saṅkīrtana, the chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra, in the Age of Kali. By this process, He delivers love for Kṛṣṇa to the general populace.**
