====== Cc. Madhya 20.324 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-viṣṇura niśvāsera nāhika paryanta
> eka manvantarāvatārera dekha lekhāra anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇura]]---of Lord Mahā-Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:n:niśvāsera]]---of the exhalations; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---there is no limit; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:m:manvantara]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of only one feature of the Lord, namely the //manvantara-avatāra//; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---just see; [[synonyms:l:lekhāra]] [[synonyms:a:anta]]---it is beyond the power of writing.

===== Translation =====

**"There is no limit to the exhalations of Mahā-Viṣṇu. Just see how impossible it is to speak or write of even only the manvantara-avatāra.**
