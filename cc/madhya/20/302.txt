====== Cc. Madhya 20.302 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakti-miśra-kṛta-puṇye kona jīvottama
> rajo-guṇe vibhāvita kari' tāṅra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:p:puṇye]]---because of pious activities mixed with devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---someone; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---the best of the living entities; [[synonyms:r:rajaḥ]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---by the mode of passion; [[synonyms:v:vibhāvita]]---influenced; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"Because of his past pious activities mixed with devotional service, the first-class living entity is influenced by the mode of passion within his mind.**
