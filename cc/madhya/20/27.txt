====== Cc. Madhya 20.27 ======

===== Text =====

> "ei sāta suvarṇa mohara āchila āmāra
> ihā lañā dharma dekhi' parvata kara pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:sāta]]---these seven; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]] [[synonyms:m:mohara]]---golden coins; [[synonyms:ā:āchila]]---were; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---mine; [[synonyms:i:ihā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accepting them; [[synonyms:d:dharma]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---observing religious principles; [[synonyms:p:parvata]]---the hilly tract of land; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---kindly get me across.

===== Translation =====

**"I have these seven gold coins with me. Please accept them, and from a religious point of view please get me across that hilly tract of land.**
