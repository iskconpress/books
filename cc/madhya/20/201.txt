====== CC Madhya 20.201 ======

===== Text =====

> āśvine---padmanābha, kārtike dāmodara
> 'rādhā-dāmodara' anya vrajendra-koṅara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āśvine]]---in the month of Āśvina (September-October); [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:n:nābha]]---Padmanābha; [[synonyms:k:kārtike]]---in the month of Kārttika (October-November); [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---the Dāmodara of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---another; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:k:koṅara]]---the son of Mahārāja Nanda.

===== Translation =====

**"In the month of Āśvina, the predominating Deity is Padmanābha, and in Kārttika it is Dāmodara. This Dāmodara is different from Rādhā-Dāmodara, the son of Nanda Mahārāja in Vṛndāvana.**
