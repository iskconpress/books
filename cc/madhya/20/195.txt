====== Cc. Madhya 20.195 ======

===== Text =====

> cakrādi-dhāraṇa-bhede nāma-bheda saba
> vāsudevera mūrti---keśava, nārāyaṇa, mādhava

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cakra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---of the disc and other weapons; [[synonyms:d:dhāraṇa]]---of holding; [[synonyms:b:bhede]]---by differences; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of names; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---differences; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vāsudevera]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---the expansions of Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:k:keśava]]---Keśava; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]---Mādhava.

===== Translation =====

**"Out of the catur-vyūha, there are three expansions of each and every form, and they are named differently according to the position of the weapons. The Vāsudeva expansions are Keśava, Nārāyaṇa and Mādhava.**
