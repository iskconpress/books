====== CC Madhya 20.403 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahiluṅ kṛṣṇera svarūpa-vicāra
> 'ananta' kahite nāre ihāra vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration of His different forms and features; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---Lord Ananta; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---not able to describe; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---the expanse.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus I have briefly described Kṛṣṇa's manifestation of transcendental forms. This subject matter is so large that even Lord Ananta cannot describe it fully.**
