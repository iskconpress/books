====== CC Madhya 20.238 ======

===== Text =====

> keśava-bhede padma-śaṅkha-gadā-cakra-dhara
> mādhava-bhede cakra-gadā-śaṅkha-padma-kara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keśava]]-[[synonyms:b:bhede]]---according to the different opinion about Lord Keśava; [[synonyms:p:padma]]---lotus; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---conch; [[synonyms:g:gadā]]---club; [[synonyms:c:cakra]]---and disc; [[synonyms:d:dhara]]---holding; [[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:b:bhede]]---according to the different opinion about the bodily features of Lord Mādhava; [[synonyms:c:cakra]]---disc; [[synonyms:g:gadā]]---club; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---conch; [[synonyms:p:padma]]---lotus; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---in the hands.

===== Translation =====

**"Keśava is described differently as holding the lotus, conch, club and disc, and Mādhava is described as holding disc, club, conch and lotus in His hands.**
