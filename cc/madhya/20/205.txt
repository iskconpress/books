====== CC Madhya 20.205 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsudevera vilāsa dui---adhokṣaja, puruṣottama
> saṅkarṣaṇera vilāsa---upendra, acyuta dui-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudevera]] [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the pastime expansions of Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:a:adhokṣaja]]---Adhokṣaja; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]]---Puruṣottama; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇera]] [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the pastime expansions of Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:u:upendra]]---Upendra; [[synonyms:a:acyuta]]---Acyuta; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---the two persons. persons.

===== Translation =====

**"Of these eight expansions, two are pastime forms of Vāsudeva. Their names are Adhokṣaja and Puruṣottama. The two pastime forms of Saṅkarṣaṇa are Upendra and Acyuta.**
