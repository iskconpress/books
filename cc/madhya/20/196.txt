====== CC Madhya 20.196 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkarṣaṇera mūrti---govinda, viṣṇu, madhusūdana
> e anya govinda---nahe vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇera]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---the expansions of Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sūdana]]---Madhusūdana; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---another; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:n:nahe]] [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---not the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**"The expansions of Saṅkarṣaṇa are Govinda, Viṣṇu and Madhusūdana. This Govinda is different from the original Govinda, for He is not the son of Mahārāja Nanda.**
