====== CC Madhya 20.263 ======

===== Text =====

> sṛṣṭi-hetu yei mūrti prapañce avatare
> sei īśvara-mūrti 'avatāra' nāma dhare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for the purpose of creation; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---which form of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prapañce]]---in the material world; [[synonyms:a:avatare]]---descends; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---form of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---takes the name.

===== Translation =====

**"The form of the Lord that descends into the material world to create is called an avatāra, or incarnation.**
