====== CC Madhya 20.207 ======

===== Text =====

> ei cabbiśa mūrti---prābhava-vilāsa pradhāna
> astra-dhāraṇa-bhede dhare bhinna bhinna nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---all of these twenty-four forms; [[synonyms:p:prābhava]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastime forms of the prābhava expansions; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---chief; [[synonyms:a:astra]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāraṇa]]---of holding the weapons; [[synonyms:b:bhede]]---in terms of differences; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---accept; [[synonyms:b:bhinna]] [[synonyms:b:bhinna]]---separate from one another; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---names.

===== Translation =====

**"All these twenty-four forms constitute the chief prābhava-vilāsa pastime forms of the Lord. They are named differently according to the position of weapons in Their hands.**
