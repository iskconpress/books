====== CC Madhya 20.172 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta prakāśe kṛṣṇera nāhi mūrti-bheda
> ākāra-varṇa-astra-bhede nāma-vibheda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---in innumerable manifestations; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]-[[synonyms:b:bheda]]---difference of form; [[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---of features; [[synonyms:v:varṇa]]---of color; [[synonyms:a:astra]]---of weapons; [[synonyms:b:bhede]]---according to differentiation; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:v:vibheda]]---difference of names.

===== Translation =====

**"When the Lord expands Himself in innumerable forms, there is no difference in the forms, but due to different features, bodily colors and weapons, the names are different.**
