====== CC Madhya 20.177 ======

===== Text =====

> sva yaṁ-rūpera gopa-veśa, gopa-abhimāna
> vāsudevera kṣatriya-veśa, 'āmi---kṣatriya'-jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---of the original form; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---the dress of a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---thinking Himself a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:v:vāsudevera]]---of Vāsudeva, the son of Vasudeva and Devakī; [[synonyms:k:kṣatriya]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---the dress is like that of a kṣatriya; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kṣatriya]]---a kṣatriya; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**"In His original form, the Lord dresses like a cowherd boy and thinks Himself one of them. When He appears as Vāsudeva, the son of Vasudeva and Devakī, His dress and consciousness are those of a kṣatriya, a warrior.**
