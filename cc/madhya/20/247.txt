====== CC Madhya 20.247 ======

===== Text =====

> bālya, paugaṇḍa haya vigrahera dharma
> eta-rūpe līlā karena vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bālya]]---childhood; [[synonyms:p:paugaṇḍa]]---boyhood; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:v:vigrahera]]---of the Deity; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---characteristics; [[synonyms:e:eta]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in so many forms; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---executes; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**"Childhood and boyhood are the typical ages of the Deity. Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda, performed His pastimes as a child and as a boy.**
