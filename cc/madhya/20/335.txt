====== CC Madhya 20.335 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-'dhyāna' kare loka jñāna-adhikārī
> tretāra dharma 'yajña' karāya 'rakta'-varṇa dhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhyāna]]---meditation upon Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---perform; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---who are advanced in spiritual knowledge; [[synonyms:t:tretāra]]---of the Tretā-yuga; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the occupational duty; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]---performance of sacrifices; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---induces; [[synonyms:r:rakta]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---assuming a reddish color.

===== Translation =====

**"In the Satya-yuga the people were generally advanced in spiritual knowledge and could meditate upon Kṛṣṇa very easily. The people's occupational duty in Tretā-yuga was to perform great sacrifices. This was induced by the Personality of Godhead in His reddish incarnation.**
