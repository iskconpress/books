====== CC Madhya 20.38 ======

===== Text =====

> sei hājipure rahe---śrīkānta tāra nāma
> gosāñira bhaginī-pati, kare rāja-kāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:h:hājipure]]---in Hājipura; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---there is; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kānta]]---Śrīkānta; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhaginī]]-[[synonyms:p:pati]]---sister's husband; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---executes; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:k:kāma]]---government service.

===== Translation =====

**In Hājipura there was a gentlemen named Śrīkānta, who happened to be the husband of Sanātana Gosvāmī's sister. He was engaged there in government service.**
