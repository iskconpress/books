====== CC Madhya 20.189 ======

===== Text =====

> ādi-catur-vyūha---iṅhāra keha nāhi sama
> ananta caturvyūha-gaṇera prākaṭya-kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]]---the original quadruple group; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---no one; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the quadruple expansions; [[synonyms:p:prākaṭya]]---of manifestation; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause.

===== Translation =====

**"The first expansion of the caturvyūha, quadruple forms, is unique. There is nothing to compare with Them. These quadruple forms are the source of unlimited quadruple forms.**
