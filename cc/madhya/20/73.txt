====== Cc. Madhya 20.73 ======

===== Text =====

> pāda-prakṣālana kari' bhikṣāte vasilā
> 'sanātane bhikṣā deha'---miśrere kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing the feet; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣāte]]---to lunch; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]] [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:deha]]---give Sanātana also lunch; [[synonyms:m:miśrere]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---He asked Tapana Miśra.

===== Translation =====

**After washing His feet, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down for lunch. He asked Tapana Miśra to supply Sanātana Gosvāmī lunch also.**
