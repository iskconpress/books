====== Cc. Madhya 20.48 ======

===== Text =====

> 'dvārete vaiṣṇava nāhi'---prabhure kahila
> 'keha haya' kari' prabhu tāhāre puchila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvārete]]---at my door; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is no Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---he informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is there anyone; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]] [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired from him.

===== Translation =====

**When Candraśekhara informed the Lord that no Vaiṣṇava was at his door, the Lord asked him, "Is there anyone at your door at all?"**
