====== CC Madhya 20.152 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera svarūpa-vicāra śuna, sanātana
> advaya-jñāna-tattva, vraje vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration of the eternal form; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---My dear Sanātana; [[synonyms:a:advaya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the Absolute Truth without duality; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**"O Sanātana, please hear about the eternal form of Lord Kṛṣṇa. He is the Absolute Truth, devoid of duality but present in Vṛndāvana as the son of Nanda Mahārāja.**
