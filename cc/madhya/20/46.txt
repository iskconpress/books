====== CC Madhya 20.46 ======

===== Text =====

> candraśekharera ghare āsi' dvārete vasilā
> mahāprabhu jāni' candraśekhare kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:candraśekharera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the house of Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---going; [[synonyms:d:dvārete]]---at the door; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:c:candraśekhare]]---to Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---said.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī then went to the house of Candraśekhara and sat down by the door. Understanding what was happening, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke to Candraśekhara.**
