====== Cc. Madhya 20.186 ======

===== Text =====

> prābhava-vilāsa---vāsudeva, saṅkarṣaṇa
> pradyumna, aniruddha,---mukhya cāri-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prābhava]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the //prābhava-vilāsa// forms; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:s:saṅkarṣaṇa]]---Saṅkarṣaṇa; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]]---Pradyumna; [[synonyms:a:aniruddha]]---Aniruddha; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---the four chief expansions.

===== Translation =====

**"The chief quadruple expansions are named Vāsudeva, Saṅkarṣaṇa, Pradyumna and Aniruddha. These are called prābhava-vilāsa.**
