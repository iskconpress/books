====== Cc. Madhya 20.132 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ei sthāne āche dhana'---yadi dakṣiṇe khudibe
> 'bhīmarula-barulī' uṭhibe, dhana nā pāibe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at this place; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---is; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---treasure; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇe]]---on the southern side; [[synonyms:k:khudibe]]---you will dig; [[synonyms:b:bhīmarula]]-[[synonyms:b:barulī]]---wasps and drones; [[synonyms:u:uṭhibe]]---will rise; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the riches; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāibe]]---you will not get.

===== Translation =====

**"The astrologer said, 'The treasure is in this place, but if you dig toward the southern side, the wasps and drones will rise, and you will not get your treasure.**
