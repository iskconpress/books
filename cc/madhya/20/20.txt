====== CC Madhya 20.20 ======

===== Text =====

> "rātrye parvata pāra kariba nija-loka diyā
> bhojana karaha tumi randhana kariyā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:p:parvata]]---the hilly tract; [[synonyms:p:pāra]] [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall cross; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---with my own men; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---just take your meal; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:r:randhana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---cooking.

===== Translation =====

**The landlord said, "I shall get you across that hilly tract at night with my own men. Now just take your lunch and cook for yourself."**
