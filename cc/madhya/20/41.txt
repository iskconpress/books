====== CC Madhya 20.41 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-jana mili' tathā iṣṭa-goṣṭhī kaila
> bandhana-mokṣaṇa-kathā gosāñi sakali kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]] [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting together; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---various types of conversation; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bandhana]]-[[synonyms:m:mokṣaṇa]]---of the arrest and release; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the story; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sakali]]---everything; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---narrated.

===== Translation =====

**When they met, they had many conversations. Sanātana Gosvāmī told him in detail about his arrest and release.**
