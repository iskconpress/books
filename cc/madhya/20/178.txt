====== Cc. Madhya 20.178 ======

===== Text =====

> saundarya, aiśvarya, mādhurya, vaidagdhya-vilāsa
> vrajendra-nandane ihā adhika ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---the opulence; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---the sweetness; [[synonyms:v:vaidagdhya]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the intellectual pastimes; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandane]]---of the son of Nanda Mahārāja and Yaśodā; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---all these; [[synonyms:a:adhika]] [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---more jubilant.

===== Translation =====

**"When one compares the beauty, opulence, sweetness and intellectual pastimes of Vāsudeva, the warrior, to Kṛṣṇa, the cowherd boy, son of Nanda Mahārāja, one sees that Kṛṣṇa's attributes are more pleasant.**
