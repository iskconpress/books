====== Cc. Madhya 20.65 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kemane chuṭilā' bali prabhu praśna kailā
> ādyopānta saba kathā teṅho śunāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kemane]] [[synonyms:c:chuṭilā]]---how were you released; [[synonyms:b:bali]]---saying; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:praśna]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---inquired; [[synonyms:ā:ādya]]-[[synonyms:u:upānta]]---from beginning to the end; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the narration; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:ś:śunāilā]]---described.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then asked Sanātana Gosvāmī, "How were you released from prison?" Sanātana then described the story from beginning to end.**
