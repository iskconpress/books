====== CC Madhya 20.86 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kahe,---"rahasya kara prāmāṇika hañā?
> bahu-mūlya bhoṭa dibā kena kāṅthā lañā?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he said; [[synonyms:r:rahasya]]---joking; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you do; [[synonyms:p:prāmāṇika]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---although being a man of authority; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---very valuable; [[synonyms:b:bhoṭa]]---woolen blanket; [[synonyms:d:dibā]]---you would give; [[synonyms:k:kena]]---why; [[synonyms:k:kāṅthā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking this quilt.

===== Translation =====

**The mendicant replied, "Sir, you are a respectable gentleman. Why are you joking with me? Why would you trade your valuable blanket for my torn quilt?"**
