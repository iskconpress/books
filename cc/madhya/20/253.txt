====== Cc. Madhya 20.253 ======

===== Text =====

> icchā-śakti-pradhāna kṛṣṇa---icchāya sarva-kartā
> jñāna-śakti-pradhāna vāsudeva adhiṣṭhātā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:icchā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---of willpower; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---predominator; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---simply by willing; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kartā]]---the creator of everything; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]-[[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the predominator of the power of knowledge; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Lord Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:a:adhiṣṭhātā]]---reservoir.

===== Translation =====

**"The predominator of the willing potency is Lord Kṛṣṇa, for by His supreme will everything comes into existence. In willing, there is a need for knowledge, and that knowledge is expressed through Vāsudeva.**
