====== CC Madhya 20.115 ======

===== Text =====

> tayā tirohitatvāc ca
> śaktiḥ kṣetra-jña-saṁjñitā
> sarva-bhūteṣu bhū-pāla
> tāratamyena vartate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tayā]]---by her; [[synonyms:t:tiraḥ]]-[[synonyms:h:hitatvāt]]---from being freed from the influence; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:ś:śaktiḥ]]---the potency; [[synonyms:k:kṣetra]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---kṣetra-jña; [[synonyms:s:saṁjñitā]]---known by the name; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūteṣu]]---in different types of bodies; [[synonyms:b:bhū]]-[[synonyms:p:pāla]]---O King; [[synonyms:t:tāratamyena]]---in different degrees; [[synonyms:v:vartate]]---exists.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'This living entity, covered by the influence of nescience, exists in different forms in the material condition. O King, he is thus proportionately freed from the influence of material energy, to greater or lesser degrees.'**
