====== Cc. Madhya 20.268 ======

===== Text =====

> sei puruṣa virajāte karena śayana
> 'kāraṇābdhiśāyī' nāma jagat-kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:virajāte]]---on the border known as Virajā; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lies down; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:abdhi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---Kāraṇābdhiśāyī; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---is the original cause of material creation.

===== Translation =====

**"That original Personality of Godhead, named Saṅkarṣaṇa, first lies down in the river Virajā, which serves as a border between the material and spiritual worlds. As Kāraṇābdhiśāyī Viṣṇu, He is the original cause of the material creation.**
