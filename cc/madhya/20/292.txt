====== CC Madhya 20.292 ======

===== Text =====

> hiraṇyagarbha-antaryāmī---garbhodakaśāyī
> 'sahasra-śīrṣādi' kari' vede yāṅre gāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hiraṇyagarbha]]---named Hiraṇyagarbha; [[synonyms:a:antaryāmī]]---the Supersoul; [[synonyms:g:garbha]]-[[synonyms:u:udaka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāyī]]---Lord Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śīrṣā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by the Vedic hymns beginning with sahasra-śīrṣā (//Ṛg Veda-saṁhitā// 10.90); [[synonyms:v:vede]] [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]] [[synonyms:g:ga]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---unto whom the Vedas pray.

===== Translation =====

**"Garbhodakaśāyī Viṣṇu, known within the universe as Hiraṇyagarbha and the antaryāmī, or Supersoul, is glorified in the Vedic hymns, beginning with the hymn that starts with the word sahasra-śīrṣā.**
