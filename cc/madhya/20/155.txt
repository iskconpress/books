====== CC Madhya 20.155 ======

===== Text =====

> svayaṁ bhagavān kṛṣṇa, 'govinda' para nāma
> sarvaiśvarya-pūrṇa yāṅra goloka---nitya-dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sva]] [[synonyms:y:yam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:p:para]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---another name; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---full of all opulences; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:g:goloka]]---Goloka Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---eternal abode.

===== Translation =====

**"The original Supreme Personality of Godhead is Kṛṣṇa. His original name is Govinda. He is full of all opulences, and His eternal abode is known as Goloka Vṛndāvana.**
