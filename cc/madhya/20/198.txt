====== CC Madhya 20.198 ======

===== Text =====

> dvādaśa-māsera devatā---ei-bāra jana
> mārga-śīrṣe---keśava, pauṣe---nārāyaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvādaśa]]-[[synonyms:m:māsera]]---of the twelve months; [[synonyms:d:devatā]]---predominating Deities; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---twelve Personalities of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:mārga]]-[[synonyms:ś:śīrṣe]]---the month of Agrahāyana (November-December); [[synonyms:k:keśava]]---Keśava; [[synonyms:p:pauṣe]]---the month of Pauṣa (December-January); [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---Nārāyaṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"These twelve are the predominating Deities of the twelve months. Keśava is the predominating Deity of Agrahāyana, and Nārāyaṇa is the predominating Deity of Pauṣa.**
