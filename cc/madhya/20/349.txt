====== Cc. Madhya 20.349 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-yugāvatāre ei ta' gaṇana
> śuni' bhaṅgi kari' tāṅre puche sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---of the incarnations in the four different yugas; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---such enumeration; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgi]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---giving a hint; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquired; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus I have given a description of the incarnations of the four different yugas." After hearing all this, Sanātana Gosvāmī gave an indirect hint to the Lord.**
