====== Cc. Madhya 20.40 ======

===== Text =====

> ṭuṅgi upara vasi' sei gosāñire dekhila
> rātrye eka-jana-saṅge gosāñi-pāśa āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭuṅgi]] [[synonyms:u:upara]] [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting in an elevated place; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that Śrīkānta; [[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with a servant; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---near Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---he came.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrīkānta was sitting in an elevated place, he could see Sanātana Gosvāmī. That night he took a servant and went to see Sanātana Gosvāmī.**
