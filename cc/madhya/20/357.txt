====== Cc. Madhya 20.357 ======

===== Text =====

> ākṛti, prakṛti, svarūpa,---svarūpa-lakṣaṇa
> kārya-dvārā jñāna,---ei taṭastha-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākṛti]]---bodily features; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]---nature; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---form; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---personal symptoms; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---by activities; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:taṭastha]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the marginal symptom.

===== Translation =====

**"Bodily features, nature and form are the personal characteristics. Knowledge of His activities provides the marginal characteristic.**
