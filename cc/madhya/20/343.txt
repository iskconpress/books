====== CC Madhya 20.343 ======

===== Text =====

> āra tina-yuge dhyānādite yei phala haya
> kali-yuge kṛṣṇa-nāme sei phala pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:y:yuge]]---in the three other yugas; [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādite]]---by processes beginning with meditation; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---result; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:y:yuge]]---in this Age of Kali; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāme]]---by chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:phala]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---one gets the same achievement.

===== Translation =====

**"In the other three yugas-Satya, Tretā and Dvāpara-people perform different types of spiritual activities. Whatever results they achieve in that way, they can achieve in Kali-yuga simply by chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahāmantra.**
