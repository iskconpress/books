====== CC Madhya 20.348 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat likhi yabe guṇāvatāra-gaṇa
> asaṅkhya saṅkhyā tāṅra, nā haya gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I write; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---incarnations of the material modes of nature; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]]---innumerable; [[synonyms:s:saṅkhya]]---counting; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of them; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---not actually countable.

===== Translation =====

**"As stated before when I described the incarnations of the material modes [guṇa-avatāras], one should consider that these incarnations also are unlimited and that no one can count them.**
