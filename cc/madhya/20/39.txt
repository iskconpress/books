====== CC Madhya 20.39 ======

===== Text =====

> tina lakṣa mudrā rājā diyāche tāra sthāne
> ghoḍā mūlya lañā pāṭhāya pātsāra sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]---300,000; [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---golden coins; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the king or nawab; [[synonyms:d:diyāche]]---has given; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in his custody; [[synonyms:g:ghoḍā]]---of horses; [[synonyms:m:mūlya]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the price; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāya]]---sends; [[synonyms:p:pātsāra]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the care of the emperor.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīkānta had 300,000 gold coins with him, which had been given to him by the emperor for the purchase of horses. Thus Śrīkānta was buying horses and dispatching them to the emperor.**
