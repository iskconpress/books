====== CC Madhya 20.97 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-svarūpa-mādhuryaiś-
> varya-bhakti-rasāśrayam
> tattvaṁ sanātanāyeśaḥ
> kṛpayopadideśa saḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---of the real identity of Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---of conjugal love; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---of opulence; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:ā:āśrayam]]---the shelter; [[synonyms:t:tattvam]]---the truth; [[synonyms:s:sanātanāya]]---unto Śrī Sanātana; [[synonyms:ī:īśaḥ]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛpayā]]---by His causeless mercy; [[synonyms:u:upadideśa]]---instructed; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, personally told Sanātana Gosvāmī about Lord Kṛṣṇa's real identity. He also told him about the Lord's conjugal love, His personal opulence and the mellows of devotional service. All these truths were explained to Sanātana Gosvāmī by the Lord Himself out of His causeless mercy.**
