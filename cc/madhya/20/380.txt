====== CC Madhya 20.380 ======

===== Text =====

> vayaso vividhatve 'pi
> sarva-bhakti-rasāśrayaḥ
> dharmī kiśora evātra
> nitya-līlā-vilāsa vān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vayasaḥ]]---of age; [[synonyms:v:vividhatve]]---in varieties; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---of all kinds; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrayaḥ]]---the shelter of devotional service; [[synonyms:d:dharmī]]---whose constitutional nature; [[synonyms:k:kiśoraḥ]]---in the age before youth; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:atra]]---in this; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of eternal pastimes; [[synonyms:v:vilāsavān]]---the supreme enjoyer.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The Supreme Personality of Godhead is eternally enjoying Himself, and He is the shelter of all kinds of devotional service. Although His ages are various, His age known as kiśora [pre-youth] is best of all.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is found in the //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (2.1.63).
