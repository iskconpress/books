====== CC Madhya 20.241 ======

===== Text =====

> purīra āvaraṇa-rūpe purīra nava-deśe
> nava-vyūha-rūpe nava-mūrti parakāśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Dvārakā Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āvaraṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---as a covering for the four sides; [[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---in nine different parts of the city; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in nine Deities; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---nine forms; [[synonyms:p:parakāśe]]---manifests.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa personally surrounds Dvārakā Purī as its protector. In different parts of the Purī, in nine places, He expands in nine different forms.**
