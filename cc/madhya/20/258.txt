====== CC Madhya 20.258 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra-patraṁ kamalaṁ
> gokulākhyaṁ mahat-padam
> tat-karṇikāraṁ tad-dhāma
> tad anantāṁśa-sambhavam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:p:patram]]---with thousands of petals; [[synonyms:k:kamalam]]---resembling a lotus flower; [[synonyms:g:gokula]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyam]]---named Gokula; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]-[[synonyms:p:padam]]---the supreme abode; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:k:karṇikāram]]---the whorl of that lotus flower; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---the abode of the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---from the expansion of energy of Ananta; [[synonyms:s:sambhavam]]---creation.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Gokula, the supreme abode and planet, appears like a lotus flower that has a thousand petals. The whorl of that lotus is the abode of the Supreme Lord, Kṛṣṇa. This lotus-shaped supreme abode is created by the will of Lord Ananta.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is quoted from //Brahma-saṁhitā// (5.2).
