====== CC Madhya 20.49 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho kahe,---eka 'daraveśa' āche dvāre
> 'tāṅre āna' prabhura vākye kahila tāṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he replied; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:daraveśa]]---one Muslim mendicant; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---bring him; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vākye]]---the order; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---unto him.

===== Translation =====

**Candraśekhara replied, "There is a Muslim mendicant." Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately said, "Please bring him here." Candraśekhara then spoke to Sanātana Gosvāmī, who was still sitting beside the door.**
