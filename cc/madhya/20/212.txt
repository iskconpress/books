====== Cc. Madhya 20.212 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi paravyoma sabākāra nitya-dhāma
> tathāpi brahmāṇḍe kāro kāṅho sannidhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:p:paravyoma]]---the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:s:sabākāra]]---of all of Them; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---the eternal abode; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍe]]---in the material universes; [[synonyms:k:kāro]]---of some of Them; [[synonyms:k:kāṅho]]---somewhere; [[synonyms:s:sannidhāna]]---the residential places.

===== Translation =====

**"Although They all have Their residences eternally in the spiritual sky, some of Them are situated within the material universes.**
