====== CC Madhya 20.50 ======

===== Text =====

> 'prabhu tomāya bolāya, āisa, daraveśa!'
> śuni' ānande sanātana karilā praveśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---unto you; [[synonyms:b:bolāya]]---calls; [[synonyms:ā:āisa]]---come here; [[synonyms:d:daraveśa]]---O Muslim mendicant; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:p:praveśa]]---entered.

===== Translation =====

**"O Muslim mendicant, please come in. The Lord is calling you." Sanātana Gosvāmī was very pleased to hear this order, and he entered Candraśekhara's house.**
