====== CC Madhya 24.105 ======

===== Text =====

> āge yata yata artha vyākhyāna kariba
> kṛṣṇa-guṇāsvādera ei hetu jāniba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as many as; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meanings; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]] [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall explain; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvādera]]---of tasting the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---reason; [[synonyms:j:jāniba]]---we shall understand.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way I shall progressively explain all the words in the verse. It should be understood that all these words are meant to enable one to taste the transcendental quality of Kṛṣṇa.**
