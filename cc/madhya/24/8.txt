====== CC Madhya 24.8 ======

===== Text =====

> kibā pralāpilāṅa, kichu nāhika smaraṇe
> tomāra saṅga-bale yadi kichu haya mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kibā]]---what; [[synonyms:p:pralāpilāṅa]]---I have said; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is not; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇe]]---in memory; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of you; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]-[[synonyms:b:bale]]---by the strength of association; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in My mind.

===== Translation =====

**"I do not recall what I spoke in that connection, but if something comes to My mind due to association with you, I shall explain it.**
