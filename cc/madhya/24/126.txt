====== CC Madhya 24.126 ======

===== Text =====

> nāradera saṅge śaunakādi muni-gaṇa
> mumukṣā chāḍiyā kailā kṛṣṇera bhajana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāradera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---by the association of the great saintly person Nārada; [[synonyms:ś:śaunaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:m:muni]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the great sages headed by Śaunaka Muni; [[synonyms:m:mumukṣā]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up the desire for liberation; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---devotional service to Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"By associating with the great saint Nārada, the great sages like Śaunaka and others gave up the desire for liberation and engaged in Kṛṣṇa's devotional service.**
