====== CC Madhya 24.107 ======

===== Text =====

> jñāna-mārge upāsaka---duita' prakāra
> kevala brahmopāsaka, mokṣākāṅkṣī āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the path of philosophical speculation; [[synonyms:u:upāsaka]]---worshipers; [[synonyms:d:duita']] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---two varieties; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:u:upāsaka]]---the worshiper of impersonal Brahman; [[synonyms:m:mokṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāṅkṣī]]---desiring liberation; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**"There are two kinds of worshipers on the path of philosophical speculation-one is brahma-upāsaka, a worshiper of the impersonal Brahman, and the other is called mokṣākāṅkṣī, one who desires liberation.**
