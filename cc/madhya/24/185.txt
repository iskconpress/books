====== CC Madhya 24.185 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ca'---avadhāraṇe, ihā 'api'---samuccaye
> dhṛtimanta hañā bhaje pakṣi-mūrkha-caye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:ca]]---the word //ca//; [[synonyms:a:avadhāraṇe]]---in emphasis; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---here; [[synonyms:a:api]]---the word //api//; [[synonyms:s:samuccaye]]---in the sense of an aggregate; [[synonyms:d:dhṛtimanta]]---fully saturated; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---worship; [[synonyms:p:pakṣi]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrkha]]-[[synonyms:c:caye]]---dull creatures like the birds, the most foolish.

===== Translation =====

**"The word ca is for emphasis, and the word api is used as an aggregate. It is to be understood that even dull creatures [birds and illiterates] can also be situated in endurance and engage in Kṛṣṇa's devotional service.**
