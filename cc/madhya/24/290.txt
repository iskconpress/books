====== Cc. Madhya 24.290 ======

===== Text =====

> sādhana-siddha---dāsa, sakhā, guru, kānta-gaṇa
> jāta-rati sādhaka-bhakta---cāri-vidha jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sādhana]]-[[synonyms:s:siddha]]---those who have perfected themselves by devotional service; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servants; [[synonyms:s:sakhā]]---friends; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---superiors; [[synonyms:k:kāntā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---beloved damsels; [[synonyms:j:jāta]]-[[synonyms:r:rati]] [[synonyms:s:sādhaka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees who have become mature by devotional service; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---they are also of four varieties.

===== Translation =====

**"Among those who have perfected themselves by devotional service, there are servants, friends, superiors and beloved damsels. Similarly, there are four types of mature devotees.**
