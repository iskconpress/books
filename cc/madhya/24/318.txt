====== CC Madhya 24.318 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-tulya bhāgavata---vibhu, sarvāśraya
> prati-śloke prati-akṣare nānā artha kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tulya]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// is identical with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vibhu]]---the supreme; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---the origin of everything, or that which controls everything; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in every verse; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣare]]---in every syllable; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---there are varieties of imports.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam is as great as Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Lord and shelter of everything. In each and every verse of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam and in each and every syllable, there are various meanings.**
