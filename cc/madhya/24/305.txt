====== CC Madhya 24.305 ======

===== Text =====

> "urukrame eva bhaktim eva ahaitukīm eva kurvanty eva"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:urukrame]]---unto the most powerful; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:b:bhaktim]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:a:ahaitukīm]]---without motives; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:k:kurvanti]]---they perform; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only.

===== Translation =====

**"The words urukrama, bhakti, ahaitukī and kurvanti are added to the word eva again and again. Thus another meaning is explained.**
