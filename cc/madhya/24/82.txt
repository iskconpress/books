====== Cc. Madhya 24.82 ======

===== Text =====

> 'brahma-ātmā'-śabde yadi kṛṣṇere kahaya
> 'rūḍhi-vṛttye' nirviśeṣa antaryāmī kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---brahma and //ātmā//; [[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by these words; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇere]] [[synonyms:k:kahaya]]---Kṛṣṇa is indicated; [[synonyms:r:rūḍhi]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛttye]]---by the direct meaning; [[synonyms:n:nirviśeṣa]]---impersonal; [[synonyms:a:antaryāmī]]---the Supersoul; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---is said.

===== Translation =====

**"Although the words brahma and ātmā indicate Kṛṣṇa, their direct meaning refers only to the impersonal Brahman and the Supersoul.**
