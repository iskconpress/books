====== CC Madhya 24.53 ======

===== Text =====

> vaṁśī-gīte hare kṛṣṇa lakṣmy-ādira mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]-[[synonyms:g:gīte]]---by the vibration of His flute; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---attracts; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:laksmī]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādira]]---of the goddess of fortune and others; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa even attracts the mind of the goddess of fortune simply by vibrating His transcendental flute.**
