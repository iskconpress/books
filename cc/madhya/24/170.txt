====== CC Madhya 24.170 ======

===== Text =====

> sad-dharmasyāvabodhāya
> yeṣāṁ nirbandhinī matiḥ
> acirād eva sarvārthaḥ
> sidhyaty eṣām abhīpsitaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:d:dharmasya]]---of the path of progressive devotional service; [[synonyms:a:avabodhāya]]---for understanding; [[synonyms:y:yeṣām]]---those whose; [[synonyms:n:nirbandhinī]]---unflinching; [[synonyms:m:matiḥ]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:arthaḥ]]---the goal of life; [[synonyms:s:sidhyati]]---becomes fulfilled; [[synonyms:e:eṣām]]---of these persons; [[synonyms:a:abhīpsitaḥ]]---desired.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Those who are anxious to awaken their spiritual consciousness, who have unflinching intelligence and who are not deviated, certainly attain the desired goal of life.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from the //Nāradīya Purāṇa.//
