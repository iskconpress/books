====== Cc. Madhya 24.72 ======

===== Text =====

> bṛhattvād bṛṁhaṇatvāc ca
> tad brahma paramaṁ viduḥ
> tasmai namas te sarvātman
> yogi-cintyāvikāravat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bṛhattvāt]]---because of being all-pervasive; [[synonyms:b:bṛṁhaṇatvāt]]---because of increasing unlimitedly; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:p:paramam]]---the ultimate; [[synonyms:v:viduḥ]]---they know; [[synonyms:t:tasmai]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto You; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātman]]---the Supreme Soul; [[synonyms:y:yogi]]-[[synonyms:c:cintya]]---appreciable by great //yogīs//; [[synonyms:a:avikāra]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---without change.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'I offer my respectful obeisances to the Absolute Truth, the summum bonum. He is the all-pervasive, all-increasing subject matter for the great yogīs. He is changeless, and He is the soul of all.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from the //Viṣṇu Purāṇa// (1.12.57).
