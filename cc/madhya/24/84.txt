====== CC Madhya 24.84 ======

===== Text =====

> rāga-bhakti-vidhi-bhakti haya dui-rūpa
> 'svayaṁ-bhagavattve', bhagavattve---prakāśa dvi-rūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāga]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---spontaneous devotional service; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---regulative devotional service; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the two kinds of devotional service; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavattve]]---in the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhagavattve]]---and in His personal expansion; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]] [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the two kinds of manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**"There are two kinds of devotional activity-spontaneous and regulative. By spontaneous devotional service, one attains the original Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa, and by the regulative process one attains the expansion of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
