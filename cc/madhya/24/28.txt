====== Cc. Madhya 24.28 ======

===== Text =====

> eka bhukti kahe, bhoga---ananta-prakāra
> siddhi---aṣṭādaśa, mukti---pañca-vidhākāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---first; [[synonyms:b:bhukti]]---material enjoyment by doing something; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---is known; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---enjoyment; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---unlimited varieties; [[synonyms:s:siddhi]]---the yogic perfections; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭādaśa]]---eighteen in number; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---five varieties.

===== Translation =====

**"First we take the word bhukti [material enjoyment], which is of unlimited variety. We may also take the word siddhi [perfection], which has eighteen varieties. Similarly, the word mukti has five varieties.**
