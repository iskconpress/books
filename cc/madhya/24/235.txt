====== Cc. Madhya 24.235 ======

===== Text =====

> śyāma-varṇa rakta-netra mahā-bhayaṅkara
> dhanur-bāṇa haste,---yena yama daṇḍa-dhara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śyāma]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇa]]---blackish color; [[synonyms:r:rakta]]-[[synonyms:n:netra]]---reddish eyes; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhayaṅkara]]---very fearful bodily features; [[synonyms:d:dhanuḥ]]-[[synonyms:b:bāṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haste]]---with arrows and bow in hand; [[synonyms:y:yena]] [[synonyms:y:yama]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhara]]---exactly like the superintendent of death, Yamarāja.

===== Translation =====

**"The hunter's body was blackish. He had reddish eyes, and he appeared fierce. It was as if the superintendent of death, Yamarāja, was standing there with bows and arrows in his hands.**
