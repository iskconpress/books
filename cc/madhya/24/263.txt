====== CC Madhya 24.263 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei mṛgādi tine nārada sustha kaila
> sustha hañā mṛgādi tine dhāñā palāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---animals, beginning with the deer; [[synonyms:t:tine]]---three; [[synonyms:n:nārada]]---the sage Nārada; [[synonyms:s:sustha]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---brought to their senses; [[synonyms:s:sustha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---coming to consciousness; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the pierced animals; [[synonyms:t:tine]]---three; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]] [[synonyms:p:palāila]]---very swiftly fled away from that place.

===== Translation =====

**"The three animals that were half-killed were then brought to their consciousness by the sage Nārada. Indeed, the animals got up and swiftly fled.**
