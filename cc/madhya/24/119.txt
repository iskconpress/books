====== Cc. Madhya 24.119 ======

===== Text =====

> guṇākṛṣṭa hañā kare kṛṣṇera bhajana
> ekādaśa-skandhe tāṅra bhakti-vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being attracted by the transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---engaged in; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---the devotional service of the Lord; [[synonyms:e:ekādaśa]]-[[synonyms:s:skandhe]]---in the Eleventh Canto of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of them; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description of the devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**"In the Eleventh Canto of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam there is a full description of the devotional service of the nine Yogendras, who rendered devotional service because they were attracted by the Lord's transcendental qualities.**
