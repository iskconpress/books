====== CC Madhya 24.307 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ātmā'-śabde kahe 'kṣetrajña jīva'-lakṣaṇa
> brahmādi kīṭa-paryanta---tāṅra śaktite gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word //ātmā//; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---it is said; [[synonyms:k:kṣetra]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]] [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---the living entity who knows about his body; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---symptom; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning from Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:k:kīṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---down to the insignificant ant; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:ś:śaktite]]---as the marginal potency; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting.

===== Translation =====

**"The word ātmā also refers to the living entity who knows about his body. That is another symptom. From Lord Brahmā down to the insignificant ant, everyone is counted as the marginal potency of the Lord.**
