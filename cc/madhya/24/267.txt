====== CC Madhya 24.267 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina anna āne daśa-biśa jane
> dine tata laya, yata khāya dui jane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---in one day; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---brought; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:b:biśa]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---enough for ten to twenty men; [[synonyms:d:dine]]---in a day; [[synonyms:t:tata]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---would accept only as much; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as; [[synonyms:k:khāya]] [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---the two of them required to eat.

===== Translation =====

**"In one day enough food was brought for ten or twenty people, but the hunter and his wife would accept only as much as they could eat.**
