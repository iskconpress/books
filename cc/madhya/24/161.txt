====== CC Madhya 24.161 ======

===== Text =====

> ei chaya yogī sādhu-saṅgādi-hetu pāñā
> kṛṣṇa bhaje kṛṣṇa-guṇe ākṛṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:c:chaya]]---six; [[synonyms:y:yogī]]---mystics; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]---of devotees; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the association; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---because of; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---render service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ākṛṣṭa]]---attracted; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**"When a purified yogī associates with devotees, he engages in Lord Kṛṣṇa's devotional service, being attracted by the Lord's transcendental qualities.**
