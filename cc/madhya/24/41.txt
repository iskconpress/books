====== Cc. Madhya 24.41 ======

===== Text =====

> 'guṇa' śabdera artha---kṛṣṇera guṇa ananta
> sac-cid-rūpa-guṇa sarva pūrṇānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guṇa]] [[synonyms:ś:śabdera]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning of the word //guṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]] [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---Kṛṣṇa has unlimited qualities; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---such qualities are spiritual and eternal; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---full of all transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**"The word guṇa means 'quality.' The qualities of Kṛṣṇa are transcendentally situated and are unlimited in quantity. All of the spiritual qualities are full of transcendental bliss.**
