====== CC Madhya 24.167 ======

===== Text =====

> eho kṛṣṇa-guṇākṛṣṭa mahā-muni hañā
> ahaitukī bhakti kare nirgrantha hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eho]]---such //yogīs//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛṣṭa]]---attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:muni]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming great saintly persons; [[synonyms:a:ahaitukī]] [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---they perform causeless devotional service; [[synonyms:n:nirgrantha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming indifferent to the mystic //yoga// process.

===== Translation =====

**"Being attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa, yogīs become great saints. At that time, not being hampered by the yogic process, they engage in unalloyed devotional service.**
