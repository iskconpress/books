====== CC Madhya 24.212 ======

===== Text =====

> dehārāmī dehe bhaje 'dehopādhi brahma'
> sat-saṅge seha kare kṛṣṇera bhajana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dehārāmī]]---persons who have accepted this body as the self and are interested only in sense gratification; [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the body; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---worships; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:u:upādhi]] [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---Brahman having the body as a designation; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of devotees; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---such a person; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---service to Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"One in the bodily conception worships his own body as Brahman, but when he comes in contact with the devotee, he gives up this mistaken idea and engages himself in the devotional service of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
