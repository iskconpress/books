====== CC Madhya 24.264 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhiyā vyādhera mane haila camatkāra
> ghare gela vyādha, guruke kari' namaskāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vyādhera]]---of the hunter; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonder; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---home; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vyādha]]---the hunter; [[synonyms:g:guruke]]---to the spiritual master; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---offering obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**"When the hunter saw the half-killed animals flee, he was certainly struck with wonder. He then offered his respectful obeisances to the sage Nārada and returned home.**
