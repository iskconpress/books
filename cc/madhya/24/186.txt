====== CC Madhya 24.186 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ātmā'-śabde 'buddhi' kahe buddhi-viśeṣa
> sāmānya-buddhi-yukta yata jīva avaśeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word //ātmā//; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---it is said; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]-[[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---a particular type of intelligence; [[synonyms:s:sāmānya]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhi]]-[[synonyms:y:yukta]]---endowed with common intelligence; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---living entities; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]]---the rest.

===== Translation =====

**"The word ātmā is also used for a particular type of intelligence. Since all living entities generally have some intelligence, more or less, they are included.**
