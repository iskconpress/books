====== CC Madhya 24.135 ======

===== Text =====

> nirodho 'syānu śayanam
> ātmanaḥ saha śaktibhiḥ
> muktir hitvānyathā-rūpaṁ
> svarūpeṇa vyavasthitiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirodhaḥ]]---winding up; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of this; [[synonyms:a:anu]]---after; [[synonyms:ś:śayanam]]---lying down; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:ś:śaktibhiḥ]]---the energies (marginal and external); [[synonyms:m:muktiḥ]]---liberation; [[synonyms:h:hitvā]]---giving up; [[synonyms:a:anyathā]]---other; [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---form; [[synonyms:s:svarūpeṇa]]---with one's own eternal form; [[synonyms:v:vyavasthitiḥ]]---staying.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The living entities and other potencies merge in the Mahā-Viṣṇu as the Lord lies down and winds up [destroys] the cosmic manifestation. Liberation means being situated in one's eternal original form, which he attains after giving up the changeable gross and subtle bodies.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// [[books:sb:2:10:6|(2.10.6)]].
