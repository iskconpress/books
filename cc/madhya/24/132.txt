====== CC Madhya 24.132 ======

===== Text =====

> brahma-bhūtaḥ prasannātmā
> na śocati na kāṅkṣati
> samaḥ sarveṣu bhūteṣu
> mad-bhaktiṁ labhate parām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtaḥ]]---being one with the Absolute; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---fully joyful; [[synonyms:n:na]]---never; [[synonyms:ś:śocati]]---laments; [[synonyms:n:na]]---never; [[synonyms:k:kāṅkṣati]]---desires; [[synonyms:s:samaḥ]]---equally disposed; [[synonyms:s:sarveṣu]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhūteṣu]]---to living entities; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktim]]---My devotional service; [[synonyms:l:labhate]]---gains; [[synonyms:p:parām]]---transcendental.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'One who is thus transcendentally situated at once realizes the Supreme Brahman and is fully joyful. Be never laments nor desires to have anything; he is equally disposed to every living entity. In that state he attains pure devotional service unto Me.'**
