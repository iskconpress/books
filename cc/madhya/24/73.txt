====== Cc. Madhya 24.73 ======

===== Text =====

> sei brahma-śabde kahe svayaṁ-bhagavān
> advitīya-jñāna, yāṅhā vinā nāhi āna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---//brahma//; [[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---it is said; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:advitīya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---the supreme one, without duality; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]]---which; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---there is nothing else.

===== Translation =====

**"The proper meaning of the word brahma is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is one without a second and without whom nothing exists.**
