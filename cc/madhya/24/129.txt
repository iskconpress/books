====== Cc. Madhya 24.129 ======

===== Text =====

> jīvan-mukta' aneka, sei dui bheda jāni
> 'bhaktye jīvan-mukta', jñāne jīvan-mukta' māni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīvat]]-[[synonyms:m:mukta]]---liberated in this life; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---there are many; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---all of them; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---two divisions; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---we consider; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]] [[synonyms:j:jīvat]]-[[synonyms:m:mukta]]---one liberated in this life by pursuing the process of devotional service; [[synonyms:j:jñāne]] [[synonyms:j:jīvat]]-[[synonyms:m:mukta]]---a person liberated in this life by following the process of philosophical speculation; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---we can understand.

===== Translation =====

**"There are many people who are liberated even in this lifetime. Some are liberated by discharging devotional service, and others are liberated through the philosophical speculative process.**
