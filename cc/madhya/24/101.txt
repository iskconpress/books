====== CC Madhya 24.101 ======

===== Text =====

> 'pra'-śabde---mokṣa-vāñchā kaitava-pradhāna
> ei śloke śrīdhara-svāmī kariyāchena vyākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the affix //pra//; [[synonyms:m:mokṣa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---the desire for being liberated; [[synonyms:k:kaitava]]-[[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---first-class cheating; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in this verse; [[synonyms:ś:śrīdhara]]-[[synonyms:s:svāmī]]---the great commentator Śrīdhara Svāmī; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]]---has made; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation.

===== Translation =====

**"The prefix pra in the word projjhita specifically refers to those desiring liberation or oneness with the Supreme. Such a desire should be understood to result from a cheating propensity. The great commentator Śrīdhara Svāmī has explained this verse in that way.**
