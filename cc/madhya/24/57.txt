====== Cc. Madhya 24.57 ======

===== Text =====

> guru-tulya strī-gaṇera vātsalye ākarṣaṇa
> dāsya-sakhyādi-bhāve puruṣādi gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:t:tulya]]---on the level of a superior guardian; [[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the ladies of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vātsalye]]---in parental affection; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---attracting; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]-[[synonyms:s:sakhya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---servants, friends, and others; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in the mode of; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the males of Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**"The women of Vṛndāvana, who are on the level of superior guardians, are attracted maternally. The men of Vṛndāvana are attracted as servants, friends and fathers to Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
