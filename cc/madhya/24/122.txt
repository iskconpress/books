====== CC Madhya 24.122 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mumukṣu' jagate aneka saṁsārī jana
> 'mukti' lāgi' bhaktye kare kṛṣṇera bhajana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mumukṣu]]---desiring to be liberated; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in this world; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---many; [[synonyms:s:saṁsārī]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---engaged in material activities; [[synonyms:m:mukti]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the sake of liberation; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---in devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---perform; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---the worship of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"There are many people within this material world who desire liberation, and for this purpose they render devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
