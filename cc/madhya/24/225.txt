====== Cc. Madhya 24.225 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ca' evārthe---'munayaḥ eva' kṛṣṇere bhajaya
> "ātmārāmā api"---'api' 'garhā'-artha kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:ca]]---the word //ca//; [[synonyms:e:eva]]-[[synonyms:a:arthe]]---in the sense of //eva//; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---just the saintly persons; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇere]] [[synonyms:b:bhajaya]]---engage themselves in the devotional service of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmāḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---also in this combination, //ātmārāmā api//; [[synonyms:a:api]]---the word //api//; [[synonyms:g:garhā]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---in the sense of censure.

===== Translation =====

**"The word ca is also used to indicate the certainty that only saintly persons are engaged in rendering devotional service to Kṛṣṇa. In the combination ātmārāmā api, api is used in the sense of censure.**
