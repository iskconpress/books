====== CC Madhya 24.136 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-bahirmukha-doṣe māyā haite bhaya
> kṛṣṇonmukha bhakti haite māyā-mukta haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---of going against Kṛṣṇa consciousness; [[synonyms:d:doṣe]]---by the fault; [[synonyms:m:māyā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the illusory energy; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:unmukha]]---in favor of Kṛṣṇa consciousness; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:m:mukta]]---liberated from //māyā//; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---one becomes.

===== Translation =====

**"By opposing Kṛṣṇa consciousness, one again becomes conditioned and fearful due to the influence of māyā. By executing devotional service faithfully, one is liberated from māyā.**
