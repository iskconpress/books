====== Cc. Madhya 24.4 ======

===== Text =====

> 'pūrve śuniyāchoṅ, tumi sārvabhauma-sthāne
> eka śloke āṭhāra artha kairācha vyākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyāchoṅ]]---I heard; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in one verse; [[synonyms:ā:āṭhāra]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---eighteen meanings; [[synonyms:k:kairācha]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---have explained.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "My Lord, I have heard that previously, at the home of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, You explained the ātmārāma verse in eighteen different ways.**
