====== CC Madhya 24.260 ======

===== Text =====

> nadī-tīre eka-khāni kuṭīra kariyā
> tāra āge eka-piṇḍi tulasī ropiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nadī]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank of the river; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:k:khāni]]---just one; [[synonyms:k:kuṭīra]]---a cottage; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---constructing; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---just in front of the cottage; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:p:piṇḍi]]---one raised platform; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]---a tulasī plant; [[synonyms:r:ropiyā]]---growing.

===== Translation =====

**"Nārada Muni continued, 'Leave your home and go to the river. There you should construct a small cottage, and in front of the cottage you should grow a tulasī plant on a raised platform.**
