====== CC Madhya 24.130 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bhaktye jīvan-mukta' guṇākṛṣṭa hañā kṛṣṇa bhaje
> śuṣka-jñāne jīvan-mukta aparādhe adho maje

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaktye]] [[synonyms:j:jīvat]]-[[synonyms:m:mukta]]---persons liberated in this life by discharging devotional service; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---engage in the devotional service of the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]] [[synonyms:j:jīvat]]-[[synonyms:m:mukta]]---so-called liberated in this life by dry, speculative knowledge; [[synonyms:a:aparādhe]]---by offenses; [[synonyms:a:adho]] [[synonyms:m:maje]]---fall down.

===== Translation =====

**"Those who are liberated by devotional service become more and more attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa. Thus they engage in His service. Those who are liberated by the speculative process eventually fall down again due to offensive activity.**
