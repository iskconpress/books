====== CC Madhya 24.216 ======

===== Text =====

> 'tapasvī' prabhṛti yata dehārāmī haya
> sādhu-saṅge tapa chāḍi' śrī-kṛṣṇa bhajaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tapasvī]]---persons who undergo severe penances; [[synonyms:p:prabhṛti]]---and so on; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dehārāmī]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are within the category of the bodily concept of life; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of devotees; [[synonyms:t:tapa]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up all such processes of penance and austerity; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bhajaya]]---engage themselves in the service of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"The tapasvīs, those who undergo severe austerities and penances to elevate themselves to the higher planetary systems, are also in the same category. When such persons come in contact with a devotee, they give up all those practices and engage in Lord Kṛṣṇa's service.**
