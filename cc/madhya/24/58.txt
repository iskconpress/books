====== Cc. Madhya 24.58 ======

===== Text =====

> pakṣī, mṛga, vṛkṣa, latā, cetanācetana
> preme matta kari' ākarṣaye kṛṣṇa-guṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pakṣī]]---birds; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]---animals; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]---trees; [[synonyms:l:latā]]---creepers; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]-[[synonyms:a:acetana]]---living entities and even the stones and wood; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---captivated; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaye]]---attract; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the qualities of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"The qualities of Kṛṣṇa captivate and attract everything, living and dead. Even birds, animals and trees are attracted to Kṛṣṇa's qualities.**
