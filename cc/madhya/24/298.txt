====== CC Madhya 24.298 ======

===== Text =====

> āṭānna ca-kārera saba lopa haya
> eka ātmārāma-śabde āṭānna artha kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āṭānna]]---fifty-eight; [[synonyms:c:ca]]-[[synonyms:k:kārera]]---of the additions of the word //ca//; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:lopa]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---all of them are rejected; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāma]]---//ātmārāma//; [[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word; [[synonyms:ā:āṭānna]] [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---fifty-eight imports are automatically derived.

===== Translation =====

**"When all the ca-kāra, or additions to the word ca, are taken away, fifty-eight different meanings can still be understood by the one word ātmārāma.**
