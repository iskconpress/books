====== Cc. Madhya 24.14 ======

===== Text =====

> 'muni'-ādi śabdera artha śuna, sanātana
> pṛthak pṛthak artha pāche kariba milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:muni]]---the word //muni//; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and the other; [[synonyms:ś:śabdera]]---of the words; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---My dear Sanātana; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separately; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---after; [[synonyms:k:kariba]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---I shall combine.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Sanātana, first hear the meanings of the other words, beginning with the word muni. I shall first explain their separate meanings, then combine them.**
