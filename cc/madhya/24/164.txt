====== CC Madhya 24.164 ======

===== Text =====

> ei saba śānta yabe bhaje bhagavān
> 'śānta' bhakta kari' tabe kahi tāṅra nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:ś:śānta]]---neutral; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---worship; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ś:śānta]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees in the neutral stage of devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---describing as; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---that time; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---their; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name.

===== Translation =====

**"These thirteen types of yogīs and munis are called śānta-bhaktas, for they render transcendental loving service to the Supreme Personality of Godhead in the neutral stage.**
