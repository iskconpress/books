====== CC Madhya 24.335 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-mūrti -lakṣaṇa, āra śālagrāma-lakṣaṇa
> kṛṣṇa-kṣetra-yātrā, kṛṣṇa-mūrti-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---characteristics of the Deity; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śālagrāma]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---characteristics of the //śālagrāma-śilā//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetra]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---visiting places like Vṛndāvana, Dvārakā and Mathurā; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting the Deity in the temple.

===== Translation =====

**"The characteristics of the Deities should be discussed as well as the characteristics of the śālagrāma-śilā. One should also discuss visiting the Deities in the temple and touring holy places like Vṛndāvana, Mathurā and Dvārakā.**
