====== CC Madhya 24.273 ======

===== Text =====

> ete na hy adbhutā vyādha
> tavāhiṁsādayo guṇāḥ
> hari-bhaktau pravṛttā ye
> na te syuḥ paratāpinaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ete]]---all these; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:adbhutāḥ]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:v:vyādha]]---O hunter; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---your; [[synonyms:a:ahiṁsā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādayaḥ]]---nonviolence and others; [[synonyms:g:guṇāḥ]]---qualities; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktau]]---in devotional service; [[synonyms:p:pravṛttāḥ]]---engaged; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---those who; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:t:te]]---they; [[synonyms:s:syuḥ]]---are; [[synonyms:p:paratāpinaḥ]]---envious of other living entities.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'O hunter, good qualities like nonviolence, which you have developed, are not very astonishing, for those engaged in the Lord's devotional service are never inclined to give pain to others because of envy.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from the //Skanda Purāṇa.//
