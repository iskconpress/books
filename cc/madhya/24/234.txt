====== Cc. Madhya 24.234 ======

===== Text =====

> kata-dūre dekhe vyādha vṛkṣe oṅta hañā
> mṛga māribāre āche bāṇa yuḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:d:dūre]]---somewhat farther; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---Nārada Muni saw; [[synonyms:v:vyādha]]---the hunter; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]] [[synonyms:o:oṅta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---hiding behind a tree; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]] [[synonyms:m:māribāre]]---to kill the animals; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---was there; [[synonyms:b:bāṇa]] [[synonyms:y:yuḍiyā]]---with arrows in the hand.

===== Translation =====

**"When Nārada Muni advanced farther, he saw a hunter behind a tree. This hunter was holding arrows, and he was ready to kill more animals.**
