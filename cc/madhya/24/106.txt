====== CC Madhya 24.106 ======

===== Text =====

> śloka-vyākhyā lāgi' ei kariluṅ ābhāsa
> ebe kari ślokera mūlārtha prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:v:vyākhyā]]---of the explanation of the verse; [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the matter; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]]---I did; [[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---indication; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---let Me do; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the real meaning; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---the manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**"I have given all these explanations just to indicate the purpose of the verse. Allow Me to explain the real purpose of the verse.**
