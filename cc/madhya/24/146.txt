====== CC Madhya 24.146 ======

===== Text =====

> "ātmārāmāś ca api" kare kṛṣṇe ahaitukī bhakti
> "munayaḥ santaḥ" iti kṛṣṇa-manane āsakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---self-realized persons also; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:ahaitukī]] [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---unmotivated devotional service; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]] [[synonyms:s:santaḥ]]---great saintly persons and transcendentalists; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:manane]]---in meditation on Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āsakti]]---attraction.

===== Translation =====

**"The six kinds of ātmārāmas render devotional service to Kṛṣṇa without ulterior motives. The words munayaḥ and santaḥ indicate those who are very attached to meditating upon Kṛṣṇa.**
