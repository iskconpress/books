====== Cc. Madhya 24.311 ======

===== Text =====

> ṣāṭi artha kahiluṅ, saba---kṛṣṇera bhajane
> sei artha haya ei saba udāharaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭi]]---sixty; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---imports; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I explained; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:b:bhajane]]---aiming at rendering transcendental loving service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---that is the only meaning; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:u:udāharaṇe]]---examples.

===== Translation =====

**"I have thus explained sixty different meanings, and all of them aim at the service of Lord Kṛṣṇa. After giving so many examples, that is the only meaning.**
