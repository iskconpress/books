====== Cc. Madhya 24.343 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvatra pramāṇa dibe purāṇa-vacana
> śrī-mūrti-viṣṇu-mandira karaṇa-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---evidence; [[synonyms:d:dibe]]---you should give; [[synonyms:p:purāṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---quoting from the //Purāṇas//; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---the Deity; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:m:mandira]]---of the Viṣṇu temple; [[synonyms:k:karaṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the characteristics of constructing.

===== Translation =====

**"Whatever you say about Vaiṣṇava behavior, the establishment of Vaiṣṇava temples and Deities and everything else should be supported by evidence from the Purāṇas.**
