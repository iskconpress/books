====== CC Madhya 24.6 ======

===== Text =====

> āścarya śuniyā mora utkaṇṭhita mana
> kṛpā kari' kaha yadi, juḍāya śravaṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]]---desirous; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing Your causeless mercy; [[synonyms:k:kaha]] [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if You speak; [[synonyms:j:juḍāya]]---pleases; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---the ear.

===== Translation =====

**"I have heard this wonderful story and am therefore very inquisitive to hear it again. If You would kindly repeat it, I would be very pleased to hear."**
