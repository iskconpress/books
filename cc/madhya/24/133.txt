====== CC Madhya 24.133 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-vīthī-pathikair upāsyāḥ
> svānanda-siṁhāsana-labdha-dīkṣāḥ
> śaṭhena kenāpi vayaṁ haṭhena
> dāsī-kṛtā gopa-vadhū-viṭena

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:v:vīthī]]---of the path of monism; [[synonyms:p:pathikaiḥ]]---by the wanderers; [[synonyms:u:upāsyāḥ]]---worshipable; [[synonyms:s:svānanda]]---of self-realization; [[synonyms:s:siṁhāsana]]---on the throne; [[synonyms:l:labdha]]-[[synonyms:d:dīkṣāḥ]]---being initiated; [[synonyms:ś:śaṭhena]]---by a cheater; [[synonyms:k:kenāpi]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vayam]]---I; [[synonyms:h:haṭhena]]---by force; [[synonyms:d:dāsī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtā]]---made into a maidservant; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhū]]-[[synonyms:v:viṭena]]---engaged in joking with the //gopīs.//

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Although I was worshiped by those on the path of monism and initiated into self-realization through the yoga system, I am nonetheless forcibly turned into a maidservant by some cunning boy who is always joking with the gopīs.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse written by Bilvamaṅgala Ṭhākura.
