====== CC Madhya 24.291 ======

===== Text =====

> ajāta-rati sādhaka-bhakta,---e cāri prakāra
> vidhi-mārge bhakte ṣoḍaśa bheda pracāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ajāta]]-[[synonyms:r:rati]] [[synonyms:s:sādhaka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---immature devotees engaged in devotional service; [[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---there are also the same four varieties; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the path of regulative devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣoḍaśa]] [[synonyms:b:bheda]] [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---there are sixteen varieties.

===== Translation =====

**"Within the category of regulative devotional service, there are also immature devotees. These are also of four varieties. Thus in regulative devotional service there are altogether sixteen varieties.**
