====== CC Madhya 24.125 ======

===== Text =====

> aho mahātman bahu-doṣa-duṣṭo
> 'py ekena bhāty eṣa bhavo guṇena
> sat-saṅgamākhyena sukhāvahena
> kṛtādya no yena kṛśā mumukṣā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aho]] [[synonyms:m:mahātman]]---O great devotee; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:d:doṣa]]-[[synonyms:d:duṣṭaḥ]]---infected with varieties of material disease or attachment; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:e:ekena]]---with one; [[synonyms:b:bhāti]]---shines; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhavaḥ]]---birth in this material world; [[synonyms:g:guṇena]]---with a good quality; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅgama]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyena]]---known as association with devotees; [[synonyms:s:sukhā]]-[[synonyms:ā:āvahena]]---which brings about happiness; [[synonyms:k:kṛtā]]---made; [[synonyms:a:adya]]---now; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---our; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by which; [[synonyms:k:kṛśā]]---insignificant; [[synonyms:m:mumukṣā]]---the desire for liberation.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'O great learned devotee, although there are many faults in this material world, there is one good opportunity---the association with devotees. Such association brings about great happiness. Due to this good quality, our strong desire to achieve liberation by merging into the Brahman effulgence has become weakened.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from the //Hari-bhakti-sudhodaya.//
