====== CC Madhya 24.34 ======

===== Text =====

> kāntā-gaṇera rati pāya 'mahābhāva'-sīmā
> 'bhakti'-śabdera ei saba arthera mahimā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāntā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the devotees in conjugal love; [[synonyms:r:rati]]---the attraction; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---attain; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limit of //mahābhāva//; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:ś:śabdera]]---of the word; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:a:arthera]]---of the meanings; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---of the glories.

===== Translation =====

**"The gopīs of Vṛndāvana who are attached to Kṛṣṇa in conjugal love can increase their ecstatic love up to the point of mahābhāva [the greatest ecstatic love]. These are some of the glorious meanings of the word bhakti, devotional service.**
