====== Cc. Madhya 24.109 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakti vinā kevala jñāne 'mukti' nāhi haya
> bhakti sādhana kare yei 'prāpta-brahma-laya'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---by philosophical speculation; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is not; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---practice; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:p:prāpta]]-[[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:l:laya]]---as good as merging into the impersonal Brahman.

===== Translation =====

**"One cannot attain liberation simply through philosophical speculation devoid of devotional service. However, if one renders devotional service, he is automatically on the Brahman platform.**
