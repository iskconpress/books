====== Cc. Madhya 24.302 ======

===== Text =====

> 'nirgranthā eva' hañā, 'api'---nirdhāraṇe
> ei 'ūnaṣaṣṭi' prakāra artha kariluṅ vyākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirgranthā]]---the word //nirgranthā//; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---also the word //eva//; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:a:api]]---the word //api//; [[synonyms:n:nirdhāraṇe]]---in fixation; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ū:ūnaṣaṣṭi]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---in this way fifty-nine imports; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---I have described.

===== Translation =====

**"Then taking the word nirgranthā and considering api in the sense of sustenance, I have tried to explain fifty-nine meanings of the word.**
