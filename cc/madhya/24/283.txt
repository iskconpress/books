====== Cc. Madhya 24.283 ======

===== Text =====

> ei āra tina artha gaṇanāte pāila
> ei dui artha mili' 'chābbiśa' artha haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this example; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---three imports; [[synonyms:g:gaṇanāte]]---in calculating; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---we have obtained; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:m:mili']]---by adding these two imports; [[synonyms:c:chābbiśa]]---twenty-six; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---imports; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there have been.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way we have found three more meanings. Combine these with the other meanings, and the total number of meanings add up to twenty-six in all.**
