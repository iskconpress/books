====== Cc. Madhya 24.327 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe tāra diśā sphure mo-nīcera hṛdaya
> īśvara tumi,---ye karāha, sei siddha haya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---if you do so; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:d:diśā]]---the process of writing; [[synonyms:s:sphure]]---manifests; [[synonyms:m:mo]]-[[synonyms:n:nīcera]]---of someone lowborn like me; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:karāha]]---whatever You cause to do; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:siddha]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---that is perfectly done.

===== Translation =====

**"If You would please manifest Yourself within my heart and personally direct me in writing this book, then, although I am lowborn, I may hope to be able to write it. You can do this because You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead Yourself, and whatever You direct is perfect."**
