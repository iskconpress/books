====== CC Madhya 24.322 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata kahiluṅ eka ślokera vyākhyāna
> vātulera pralāpa kari' ke kare pramāṇa?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have spoken; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of a verse; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---the explanation; [[synonyms:v:vātulera]] [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---the talking of a madman; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---who will accept this as evidential proof.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way, like a madman, I have explained the meaning of just one verse. I do not know who will take this as evidence.**
