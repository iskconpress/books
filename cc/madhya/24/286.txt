====== CC Madhya 24.286 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅte rame yei, sei saba---'ātmārāma'
> 'vidhi-bhakta', 'rāga-bhakta',---dui-vidha nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅte]]---in all those Supreme Personalities of Godhead; [[synonyms:r:rame]]---takes pleasure in devotional service; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---those persons who; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāma]]---are also known as //ātmārāma//; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees following the regulative principles; [[synonyms:r:rāga]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees following spontaneous love; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---two different varieties.

===== Translation =====

**"One who always engages in the service of the Supreme Personality of Godhead is called ātmārāma. There are two types of ātmārāma. One is an ātmārāma engaged in regulative devotional service, and the other is an ātmārāma engaged in spontaneous devotional service.**
