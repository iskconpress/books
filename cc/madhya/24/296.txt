====== CC Madhya 24.296 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ātmārāmāś ca ātmārāmāś ca' āṭānna-bāra
> śeṣe saba lopa kari' rākhi eka-bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]] [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---repeating //ātmārāmāḥ//; [[synonyms:ā:āṭānna]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---fifty-eight times; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:lopa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---rejecting all; [[synonyms:r:rākhi]]---we keep; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---only one.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way, one can repeat the word ātmārāmāḥ with ca for each of the fifty-eight meanings. By following the rule previously stated and rejecting all but the last, we retain that which represents all the meanings.**
