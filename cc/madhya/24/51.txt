====== Cc. Madhya 24.51 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpa-guṇa-śravaṇe rukmiṇy-ādira ākarṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---beauty; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing; [[synonyms:r:rukmiṇī]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādira]]---of the queens, headed by Rukmiṇī; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---attracting.

===== Translation =====

**"The queens in Dvārakā, who are headed by Rukmiṇī, are also attracted to Kṛṣṇa simply by hearing about His transcendental beauty and qualities.**
