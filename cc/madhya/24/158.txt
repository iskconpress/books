====== CC Madhya 24.158 ======

===== Text =====

> 'yogārurukṣu', 'yogārūḍha' 'prāpta-siddhi' āra
> ei tina bhede haya chaya prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārurukṣu]]---persons desiring elevation to the platform of yogic perfection; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārūḍha]]---persons already elevated to that position; [[synonyms:p:prāpta]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhi]]---persons who have achieved the success; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]---these three; [[synonyms:b:bhede]]---by varieties; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:c:chaya]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---six kinds.

===== Translation =====

**"By these three divisions of advancement in yoga-yogārurukṣu, yogārūḍha and prāpta-siddhi---there are six kinds of mystic yogīs.**
