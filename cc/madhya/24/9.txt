====== Cc. Madhya 24.9 ======

===== Text =====

> sahaje āmāra kichu artha nāhi bhāse
> tomā-sabāra saṅga-bale ye kichu prakāśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahaje]]---generally; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---does not manifest; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]-[[synonyms:b:bale]]---by the strength of your association; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---manifests.

===== Translation =====

**"Generally by Myself I cannot give an explanation, but by the strength of your association something may manifest itself.**
