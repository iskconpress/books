====== Cc. Madhya 24.274 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei vyādha doṅhāre aṅgane ānila
> kuśāsana āni' doṅhāre bhaktye vasāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vyādha]]---hunter; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāre]]---both Nārada Muni and Parvata; [[synonyms:a:aṅgane]] [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought in the courtyard of his house; [[synonyms:k:kuśa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsana]] [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing straw mats for sitting; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāre]]---both of them; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---with great devotion; [[synonyms:v:vasāila]]---made to sit down.

===== Translation =====

**"The hunter then received both the great sages in the courtyard of his house. He spread out a straw mat for them to sit upon, and with great devotion he begged them to sit down.**
