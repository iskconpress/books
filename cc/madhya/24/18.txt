====== CC Madhya 24.18 ======

===== Text =====

> nir niścaye niṣ kramārthe
> nir nirmāṇa-niṣedhayoḥ
> grantho dhane 'tha sandarbhe
> varṇa-saṅgrathane 'pi ca

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niḥ]]---the prefix //niḥ//; [[synonyms:n:niścaye]]---in the sense of ascertainment; [[synonyms:n:niḥ]]---the prefix //niḥ//; [[synonyms:k:krama]]-[[synonyms:a:arthe]]---in the meaning of succession; [[synonyms:n:niḥ]]---the prefix //niḥ//; [[synonyms:n:nirmāṇa]]---in the sense of forming; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhayoḥ]]---in the sense of forbidding; [[synonyms:g:granthaḥ]]---the word //grantha//; [[synonyms:d:dhane]]---in the sense of wealth; [[synonyms:a:atha]]---also; [[synonyms:s:sandarbhe]]---thesis; [[synonyms:v:varṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅgrathane]]---in the sense of tying together words; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The prefix niḥ may be used for a sense of ascertainment, gradation, construction or forbidding. The word grantha means riches, thesis and composition.'**
