====== CC Madhya 24.314 ======

===== Text =====

> artha śuni' sanātana vismita hañā
> stuti kare mahāprabhura caraṇe dhariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---by hearing the meaning (of the //ātmārāma// verse); [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vismita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming struck with wonder; [[synonyms:s:stuti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offers prayers; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---touching the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing all the explanations of all the different meanings of the ātmārāma verse, Sanātana Gosvāmī was struck with wonder. He fell down at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and began to offer prayers.**
