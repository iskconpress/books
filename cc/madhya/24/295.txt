====== CC Madhya 24.295 ======

===== Text =====

> itaretara 'ca' diyā samāsa kariye
> 'āṭānna' bāra ātmārāma nāma la-iye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:itaretara]]---in different varieties; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---the word //ca//; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---adding; [[synonyms:s:samāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kariye]]---make a compound word; [[synonyms:ā:āṭānna]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---fifty-eight times; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāma]]---the //ātmārāma// devotees; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iye]]---I take their different names.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way, as I add the word ca to one word after another, I make a compound. Thus the different names of ātmārāmas can be taken fifty-eight times.**
