====== CC Madhya 24.154 ======

===== Text =====

> antaryāmi-upāsaka 'ātmārāma' kaya
> sei ātmārāma yogīra dui bheda haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:antaryāmi]]---of the Supersoul; [[synonyms:u:upāsaka]]---worshiper; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāma]] [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---is also said to be an //ātmārāma//; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāma]]---that //ātmārāma//; [[synonyms:y:yogīra]]---of the mystic //yogī//; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bheda]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are two kinds.

===== Translation =====

**"The yogī who worships the Supersoul within himself is also called ātmārāma. There are two types of ātmārāma-yogīs.**
