====== CC Madhya 24.79 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kṛṣṇa-prāpti-hetu trividha 'sādhana'
> jñāna, yoga, bhakti,---tinera pṛthak lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---of achieving the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---causes; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]] [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---the three kinds of execution; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]---mystic yoga practice; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---and devotional service; [[synonyms:t:tinera]]---of these three; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms are different.

===== Translation =====

**"There are three ways to attain the lotus feet of the Absolute Truth, Kṛṣṇa. There is the process of philosophical speculation, the practice of mystic yoga and the execution of devotional service. Each of these has its different characteristics.**
