====== CC Madhya 24.121 ======

===== Text =====

> mokṣākāṅkṣī jñānī haya tina-prakāra
> mumukṣu, jīvan-mukta, prāpta-svarūpa āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mokṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāṅkṣī]]---those who desire to merge into the impersonal Brahman; [[synonyms:j:jñānī]]---advanced in knowledge; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---three varieties; [[synonyms:m:mumukṣu]]---desiring to be liberated; [[synonyms:j:jīvat]]-[[synonyms:m:mukta]]---already liberated, even in this life; [[synonyms:p:prāpta]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---self-realized; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**"Those who wish to merge into the impersonal Brahman are also divided into three categories---those desiring to be liberated, those already liberated and those who have realized Brahman.**
