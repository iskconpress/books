====== CC Madhya 24.85 ======

===== Text =====

> rāga-bhaktye vraje svayaṁ-bhagavāne pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāga]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---by the discharge of spontaneous devotional service; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhagavāne]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---one gets.

===== Translation =====

**"By executing spontaneous devotional service in Vṛndāvana, one attains the original Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa.**
