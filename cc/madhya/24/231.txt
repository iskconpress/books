====== Cc. Madhya 24.231 ======

===== Text =====

> vana-pathe dekhe mṛga āche bhūme paḍi'
> bāṇa-viddha bhagna-pāda kare dhaḍ-phaḍi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the forest path; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---saw; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]---deer; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:bhūme]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---lying on the ground; [[synonyms:b:bāṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viddha]]---pierced by an arrow; [[synonyms:b:bhagna]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]---broken legs; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:d:dhaḍ]]-[[synonyms:p:phaḍi]]---twisting with pain.

===== Translation =====

**"Nārada Muni saw that a deer was lying on the path through the forest and that it was pierced by an arrow. It had broken legs and was twisting due to much pain.**
