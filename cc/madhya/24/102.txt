====== CC Madhya 24.102 ======

===== Text =====

> sakāma-bhakte 'ajña' jāni' dayālu bhagavān
> sva-caraṇa diyā kare icchāra pidhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakāma]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---to devotees who still have material desires to fulfill; [[synonyms:a:ajña]]---foolish; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:d:dayālu]]---merciful; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---His own lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:i:icchāra]] [[synonyms:p:pidhāna]]---the covering of other desires.

===== Translation =====

**"When the merciful Lord Kṛṣṇa understands a devotee's foolish desire for material prosperity, He gratefully gives him the shelter of His lotus feet. In this way, the Lord covers his undesirable ambitions.**
