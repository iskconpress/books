====== CC Madhya 24.80 ======

===== Text =====

> tina sādhane bhagavān tina svarūpe bhāse
> brahma, paramātmā, bhagavattā,---trividha prakāśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:s:sādhane]]---by these three different processes; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in identities; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---appears; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the impersonal feature; [[synonyms:p:paramātmā]]---the localized feature; [[synonyms:b:bhagavattā]]---and the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:trividha]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---three manifestations.

===== Translation =====

**"The Absolute Truth is the same, but according to the process by which one understands Him, He appears in three forms-as Brahman, Paramātmā, and Bhagavān, the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
