====== CC Madhya 24.193 ======

===== Text =====

> sat-saṅga, kṛṣṇa-sevā, bhāgavata, nāma
> vraje vāsa,---ei pañca sādhana pradhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---association with devotees; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---engagement in the service of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---devotees and the book known as //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:v:vraje]] [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence in Vṛndāvana or Mathurā; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]] [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the chief processes of devotion.

===== Translation =====

**"To be elevated to the platform of devotional service, the following five items should be observed: association with devotees, engagement in the service of Lord Kṛṣṇa, the reading of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, the chanting of the holy names and residence at Vṛndāvana or Mathurā.**
