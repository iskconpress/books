====== CC Madhya 24.300 ======

===== Text =====

> "asmin vane vṛkṣāḥ phalanti" yaiche haya
> taiche saba ātmārāma kṛṣṇe bhakti karaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:asmin]] [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in this forest; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣāḥ]] [[synonyms:p:phalanti]]---different varieties of trees bear fruit; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---just as in this sentence; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāma]]---self-realized souls; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]] [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:k:karaya]]---engage themselves in loving devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"This ātmārāma verse is like the sentence, 'In this forest many different trees bear fruit.' All ātmārāmas render devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
