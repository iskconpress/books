====== Cc. Madhya 24.55 ======

===== Text =====

> yogya-bhāve jagate yata yuvatīra gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yogya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---by proper behavior; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---within the three worlds; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:y:yuvatīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the groups of young girls.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa attracts not only the minds of the gopīs and the goddesses of fortune but the minds of all the young girls in the three worlds as well.**
