====== Cc. Madhya 24.301 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ātmārāmāś ca' samuccaye kahiye ca-kāra
> 'munayaś ca' bhakti kare,---ei artha tāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---similarly the word //ātmārāmāḥ// with the word //ca// added; [[synonyms:s:samuccaye]]---in aggregation; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---means; [[synonyms:c:ca]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---the word //ca//; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---the word //munayaḥ// with the word //ca// added; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---all of them engage in devotional service; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---this is the perfect meaning of the verse.

===== Translation =====

**"After fifty-eight times uttering the word ātmārāmāḥ and taking ca in a sense of aggregation, one may add the word munayaḥ. That will mean that they also render devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa. In this way there are fifty-nine meanings.**
