====== CC Madhya 24.168 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ātmā'-śabde 'yatna' kahe---yatna kariyā
> "munayo 'pi" kṛṣṇa bhaje guṇākṛṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word //ātmā//; [[synonyms:y:yatna]]---endeavor; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---one means; [[synonyms:y:yatna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---by great endeavor; [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even great saintly persons; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---take to the devotional service of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being attracted by His transcendental qualities.

===== Translation =====

**"Ātmā also means 'endeavor.' Being attracted by Kṛṣṇa's transcendental qualities, some saints make a great endeavor to come to the point of rendering service to Him.**
