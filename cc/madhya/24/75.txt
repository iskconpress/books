====== CC Madhya 24.75 ======

===== Text =====

> sei advaya-tattva kṛṣṇa---svayaṁ-bhagavān
> tina-kāle satya tiṅho---śāstra-pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:a:advaya]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---Absolute Truth without a second; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in three phases of time (past, present and future); [[synonyms:s:satya]]---truth; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---the verdict of all Vedic literature.

===== Translation =====

**"That Absolute Truth without a second is Lord Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead. He is the supreme truth in the past, present and future. That is the evidence of all revealed scriptures.**
