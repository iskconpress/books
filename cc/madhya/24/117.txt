====== Cc. Madhya 24.117 ======

===== Text =====

> harer guṇākṣipta-matir
> bhagavān bādarāyaṇiḥ
> adhyagān mahad-ākhyānaṁ
> nityaṁ viṣṇu-jana-priyaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hareḥ]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṣipta]]-[[synonyms:m:matiḥ]]---whose mind was agitated by the qualities; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the most powerful transcendentalist; [[synonyms:b:bādarāyaṇiḥ]]---Śukadeva, son of Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:a:adhyagāt]]---studied; [[synonyms:m:mahat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyānam]]---the great epic description; [[synonyms:n:nityam]]---eternally; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]-[[synonyms:p:priyaḥ]]---who is very dear to the Vaiṣṇavas, devotees of Lord Viṣṇu.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Being very much attracted by the transcendental pastimes of the Lord, the mind of Śrīla Śukadeva Gosvāmī was agitated by Kṛṣṇa consciousness. He therefore began to study Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam by the grace of his father.'**

This is a quotation from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// [[books:sb:1:7:11|(1.7.11)]].
