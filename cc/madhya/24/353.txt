====== CC Madhya 24.353 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa-prema', 'bhakti-rasa', 'bhaktira siddhānta'
> ihāra śravaṇe bhakta jānena saba anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the mellows of devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bhaktira]] [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---the conclusions of devotional service; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing this chapter; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---a devotee; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---knows; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---limits.

===== Translation =====

**By reading this chapter, a pure devotee can understand love of Kṛṣṇa, the mellows of devotional service and the conclusion of devotional service. Everyone can understand all these things to their ultimate end by studying this chapter.**
