====== CC Madhya 24.96 ======

===== Text =====

> ei cāri sukṛti haya mahā-bhāgyavān
> tat-tat-kāmādi chāḍi' haya śuddha-bhaktimān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---these four persons; [[synonyms:s:sukṛti]]---pious men; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---highly fortunate; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:t:tat]]---those respective; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---aspirations; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktimān]]---pure devotees.

===== Translation =====

**"Because they have a pious background, all four types of people are to be considered greatly fortunate. Such people gradually give up material desires and become pure devotees.**
