====== Cc. Madhya 24.114 ======

===== Text =====

> sanakādyera kṛṣṇa-kṛpāya saurabhe hare mana
> guṇākṛṣṭa hañā kare nirmala bhajana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādyera]]---of the four Kumāras, headed by Sanaka; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:saurabhe]]---the fragrance; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---took away; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---thus being attracted by the qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---perform; [[synonyms:n:nirmala]] [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---pure devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**"The minds of the four Kumāras were attracted by the aroma of the flowers offered to Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet. Being thus attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa, they engaged in pure devotional service.**
