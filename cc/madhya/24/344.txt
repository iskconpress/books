====== CC Madhya 24.344 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sāmānya' sad-ācāra, āra 'vaiṣṇava'-ācāra
> kartavyākartavya saba 'smārta' vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāmānya]]---general; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---good behavior; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---of devotees of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---etiquette; [[synonyms:k:kartavya]]-[[synonyms:a:akartavya]]---things which are to be done and which are not to be done; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:smārta]]---connected with regulative principles; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---business.

===== Translation =====

**"You should give general and specific descriptions of the behavior and activities of a Vaiṣṇava. You should outline things that are to be done and things that are not to be done. All this should be described as regulations and etiquette.**
