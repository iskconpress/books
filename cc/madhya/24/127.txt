====== CC Madhya 24.127 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera darśane, kāro kṛṣṇera kṛpāya
> mumukṣā chāḍiyā guṇe bhaje tāṅra pā'ya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---simply by meeting Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kāro]]---someone; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the favor of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mumukṣā]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up the desire for liberation; [[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---being attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---engages in service; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:pā'ya]]---at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Simply by meeting Kṛṣṇa or receiving Kṛṣṇa's special favor, one can give up the desire for liberation. Being attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa, one can engage in His service.**
