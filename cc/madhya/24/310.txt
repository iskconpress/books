====== CC Madhya 24.310 ======

===== Text =====

> bhramite bhramite yadi sādhu-saṅga pāya
> saba tyaji' tabe tiṅho kṛṣṇere bhajaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhramite]] [[synonyms:b:bhramite]]---wandering in different forms in different manners; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---one obtains the association of devotees; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:t:tyaji']]---giving up everything; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:t:tiṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇere]] [[synonyms:b:bhajaya]]---engages himself in the service of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"The living entities are wandering in different species of life on different planets, but if by chance they get the association of a pure devotee [sādhu] they give up all other engagements and engage in the service of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
