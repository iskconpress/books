====== CC Madhya 24.143 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaktye mukti pāileha avaśya kṛṣṇere bhajaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---by devotional service; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:p:pāileha]]---if one gets; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇere]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhajaya]]---renders service.

===== Translation =====

**"When one is actually liberated by executing devotional service, he always engages in the transcendental loving service of the Lord.**
