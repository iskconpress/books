====== CC Madhya 24.351 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ sanātane prabhura prasāda
> yāhāra śravaṇe cittera khaṇḍe avasāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have explained; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the mercy of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---hearing which; [[synonyms:c:cittera]]---of the heart; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍe]]---disappears; [[synonyms:a:avasāda]]---moroseness.

===== Translation =====

**I have thus explained the mercy bestowed on Sanātana Gosvāmī by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. If one hears this, all moroseness in the heart will diminish.**
