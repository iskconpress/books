====== CC Madhya 24.354 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-caitanya-nityānanda-advaita-caraṇa
> yāṅra prāṇa-dhana, sei pāya ei dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Lord Nityānanda and Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---whose life and soul; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---such a person; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---this treasure-house of devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**The conclusion of this chapter can be known to one whose life and soul are the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Nityānanda Prabhu and Advaita Prabhu.**
