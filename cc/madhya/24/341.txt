====== CC Madhya 24.341 ======

===== Text =====

> ekādaśī, janmāṣṭamī, vāmana-dvādaśī
> śrī-rāma-navamī, āra nṛsiṁha-caturdaśī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekādaśī]]---the eleventh day of the fortnight; [[synonyms:j:janmāṣṭamī]]---the birthday ceremony of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vāmana]]-[[synonyms:d:dvādaśī]]---the birthday or appearance day of Lord Vāmana; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:navamī]]---the birthday ceremony of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]-[[synonyms:c:caturdaśī]]---the appearance day of Lord Nṛsiṁha.

===== Translation =====

**"Ekādaśī, Janmāṣṭamī, Vāmana-dvādaśī, Rāma-navamī and Nṛsiṁha-caturdaśī should all be observed.**
