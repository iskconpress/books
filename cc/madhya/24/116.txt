====== CC Madhya 24.116 ======

===== Text =====

> vyāsa-kṛpāya śukadevera līlādi-smaraṇa
> kṛṣṇa-guṇākṛṣṭa hañā karena bhajana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy of Śrīla Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:ś:śukadevera]]---of Śukadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]---remembrance of the transcendental pastimes of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛṣṭa]]---attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performed; [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---loving service.

===== Translation =====

**"By the mercy of Śrīla Vyāsadeva, Śukadeva Gosvāmī was attracted by the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Being thus attracted by Kṛṣṇa's transcendental qualities, he also became a devotee and engaged in His service.**
