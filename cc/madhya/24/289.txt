====== CC Madhya 24.289 ======

===== Text =====

> vidhi-bhaktye nitya-siddha pāriṣada---'dāsa'
> 'sakhā' 'guru' 'kāntā-gaṇa',---cāri-vidha prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---by regulative devotional service; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:s:siddha]] [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]---eternally perfect associate; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---the servant; [[synonyms:s:sakhā]] [[synonyms:g:guru]] [[synonyms:k:kāntā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---friends, superiors and beloved damsels; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---they are manifested in four varieties.

===== Translation =====

**"By executing regulative devotional service, one is elevated to the platform of an eternally perfect associate-such as a servant, friend, superior or beloved woman. These are of four varieties.**
