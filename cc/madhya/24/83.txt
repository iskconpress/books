====== CC Madhya 24.83 ======

===== Text =====

> jñāna-mārge---nirviśeṣa-brahma prakāśe
> yoga-mārge---antaryāmi-svarūpete bhāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---the process of philosophical speculation; [[synonyms:n:nirviśeṣa]]-[[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the impersonal Brahman effulgence; [[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---becomes manifest; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---by practicing mystic //yoga//; [[synonyms:a:antaryāmi]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpete]]---in the localized aspect, Supersoul; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---appears.

===== Translation =====

**"If one follows the path of philosophical speculation, the Absolute Truth manifests Himself as impersonal Brahman, and if one follows the path of mystic yoga, He manifests Himself as the Supersoul.**
