====== CC Madhya 24.218 ======

===== Text =====

> dehārāmī, sarva-kāma---saba ātmārāma
> kṛṣṇa-kṛpāya kṛṣṇa bhaje chāḍi' saba kāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dehārāmī]]---persons who are in the bodily concept of life; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāma]]---full of all material desires; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāma]]---enjoying self-satisfaction; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---become engaged in the devotional service of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---giving up all sorts of material desire.

===== Translation =====

**"As long as one labors under the bodily conception, he must fulfill volumes and volumes of material desires. Thus a person is called ātmārāma. When such an ātmārāma is favored by the mercy of Kṛṣṇa, he gives up his so-called self-satisfaction and engages in the transcendental loving service of the Lord.**
