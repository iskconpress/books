====== Cc. Madhya 24.70 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' ekādaśa padera artha-nirṇaya
> ebe ślokārtha kari, yathā ye lāgaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---this; [[synonyms:e:ekādaśa]]---eleven; [[synonyms:p:padera]]---of the words; [[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:n:nirṇaya]]---demonstration of import; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the total meaning of the verse; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---let Me do; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as much as; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:l:lāgaya]]---applicable.

===== Translation =====

**"I have now described the different meanings of the eleven separate words. Now let Me give the complete meaning of the śloka, as it is applied in different places.**
