====== CC Madhya 24.37 ======

===== Text =====

> tvat-sākṣāt-karaṇāhlāda-
> viśuddhābdhi-sthitasya me
> sukhāni goṣpadāyante
> brāhmāṇy api jagad-guro

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tvat]]---Your; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---meeting; [[synonyms:k:karaṇa]]---such action; [[synonyms:ā:āhlāda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:v:vi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---spiritually purified; [[synonyms:a:abdhi]]---ocean; [[synonyms:s:sthitasya]]---being situated; [[synonyms:m:me]]---by me; [[synonyms:s:sukhāni]]---happiness; [[synonyms:g:goṣpadāyante]]---a small hole created by the hoof of a calf; [[synonyms:b:brāhmāṇi]]---the pleasure derived from impersonal Brahman understanding; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:g:guro]]---O master of the universe.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'My dear Lord, O master of the universe, since I have directly seen You, my transcendental bliss has taken the shape of a great ocean. Being situated in that ocean, I now realize all other so-called happiness to be like the water contained in the hoofprint of a calf.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from the //Hari-bhakti-sudhodaya// (14.36).
