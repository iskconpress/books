====== CC Madhya 24.270 ======

===== Text =====

> āste-vyaste dhāñā āse, patha nāhi pāya
> pathera pipīlikā iti-uti dhare pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---with great alacrity; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running; [[synonyms:ā:āse]]---came; [[synonyms:p:patha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---does not get the path; [[synonyms:p:pathera]]---on the path; [[synonyms:p:pipīlikā]]---ants; [[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:u:uti]]---here and there; [[synonyms:d:dhare]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---touch the foot.

===== Translation =====

**"With great alacrity the hunter began to run toward his spiritual master, but he could not fall down and offer obeisances because ants were running hither and thither around his feet.**
