====== CC Madhya 24.191 ======

===== Text =====

> vicāra kariyā yabe bhaje kṛṣṇa-pāya
> sei buddhi dena tāṅre, yāte kṛṣṇa pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---one worships; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---that intelligence; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---one gets the shelter of the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Considering all these points, when one engages in the service of Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet, Kṛṣṇa gives one the intelligence by which he can gradually progress toward perfection in service to the Lord.**
