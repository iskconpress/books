====== CC Madhya 24.165 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ātmā' śabde 'mana' kaha---mane yei rame
> sādhu-saṅge seha bhaje śrī-kṛṣṇa-caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---ātmā; [[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---if you say; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:r:rame]]---one who is satisfied by speculation; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---by the association of devotees; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---he also; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---takes to devotional service; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"The word ātmā sometimes means 'the mind.' In this case, the word ātmārāma means 'a person who is satisfied by mental speculation.' When such a person associates with a pure devotee, he takes to devotional service at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa.**
