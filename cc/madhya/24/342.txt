====== CC Madhya 24.342 ======

===== Text =====

> ei sabe viddhā-tyāga, aviddhā-karaṇa
> akaraṇe doṣa, kaile bhaktira lambhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all these things; [[synonyms:v:viddhā]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāga]]---to avoid //viddha-ekādaśī// or mixed Ekādaśī; [[synonyms:a:aviddhā]]-[[synonyms:k:karaṇa]]---performing the pure Ekādaśī; [[synonyms:a:akaraṇe]] [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---the fault of not performing them; [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---if done so; [[synonyms:b:bhaktira]] [[synonyms:l:lambhana]]---there will be discrepancies in devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**"You should recommend the avoidance of mixed Ekādaśī and the performance of pure Ekādaśī. You should also describe the fault in not observing this. One should be very careful as far as these items are concerned. If one is not careful, one will be negligent in executing devotional service.**
