====== Cc. Madhya 24.292 ======

===== Text =====

> rāga-mārge aiche bhakte ṣoḍaśa vibheda
> dui mārge ātmārāmera batriśa vibheda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāga]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the path of spontaneous loving service; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in the same way; [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣoḍaśa]] [[synonyms:v:vibheda]]---divided into sixteen varieties; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the two paths, namely regulative devotional service and spontaneous devotional service; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmera]]---of persons enjoying with the Supreme Self; [[synonyms:b:batriśa]] [[synonyms:v:vibheda]]---there are thirty-two varieties of devotees.

===== Translation =====

**"On the path of spontaneous devotional service, there are also sixteen categories of devotees. Thus there are thirty-two types of ātmārāmas enjoying the Supreme Lord on these two paths.**
