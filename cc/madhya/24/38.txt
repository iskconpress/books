====== CC Madhya 24.38 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvākarṣaka, sarvāhlādaka, mahā-rasāyana
> āpanāra bale kare sarva-vismāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaka]]---all-attractive; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āhlādaka]]---all-pleasing; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:a:ayana]]---the complete abode of transcendental mellow; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---by His own strength; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---causes; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:v:vismāraṇa]]---forgetfulness of all other bliss.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa is so exalted that He is more attractive than anything else and more pleasing than anything else. He is the most sublime abode of bliss. By His own strength, He causes one to forget all other ecstasies.**
