====== Cc. Madhya 24.174 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ātmā'-śabde 'dhṛti' kahe,---dhairye yei rame
> dhairyavanta eva hañā karaya bhajane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word //ātmā//; [[synonyms:d:dhṛti]]---perseverance; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---it is said; [[synonyms:d:dhairye]]---with perseverance; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:r:rame]]---anyone who endeavors; [[synonyms:d:dhairyavanta]]---such persons with endurance; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:k:karaya]]---perform; [[synonyms:b:bhajane]]---devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**"Another meaning of ātmā is dhṛti, or endurance. A person who endeavors with endurance is ātmārāma. With endurance, such a person engages in devotional service.**
