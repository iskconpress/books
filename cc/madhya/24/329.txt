====== CC Madhya 24.329 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi ei sūtrera śuna dig-daraśana
> sakāraṇa likhi ādau guru-āśrayaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the synopsis of this book; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---an indication; [[synonyms:s:sakāraṇa]]---the cause; [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---we should write; [[synonyms:ā:ādau]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśrayaṇa]]---accepting a bona fide spiritual master.

===== Translation =====

**"Because you asked me for a synopsis, please hear these few indications. In the beginning one must take shelter of a bona fide spiritual master.**
