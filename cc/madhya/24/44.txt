====== CC Madhya 24.44 ======

===== Text =====

> sanakādira mana harila saurabhādi guṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādira]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds of saintly sages like Sanaka and Sanātana; [[synonyms:h:harila]]---attracted; [[synonyms:s:saurabha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---such as the transcendental aroma of His lotus feet; [[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---by the quality.

===== Translation =====

**"The minds of the four boy sages [Sanaka, Sanātana, Sanandana and Sanat-kumāra] were attracted to the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa by the aroma of the tulasī that had been offered to the Lord.**
