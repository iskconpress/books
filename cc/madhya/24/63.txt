====== CC Madhya 24.63 ======

===== Text =====

> nija-guṇe tabe hare dehendriya-mana
> aiche kṛpālu kṛṣṇa, aiche tāṅra guṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---by transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---attracts; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---the body, senses and mind; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:k:kṛpālu]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---merciful Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---transcendental qualities.

===== Translation =====

**"When the devotee is freed from all sinful material activities, Kṛṣṇa attracts his body, mind and senses to His service. Thus Kṛṣṇa is very merciful, and His transcendental qualities are very attractive.**
