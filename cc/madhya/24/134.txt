====== CC Madhya 24.134 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakti-bale 'prāpta-svarūpa' divya-deha pāya
> kṛṣṇa-guṇākṛṣṭa hañā bhaje kṛṣṇa-pā'ya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:b:bale]]---by the strength of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:prāpta]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---attaining his original status; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:d:deha]]---a transcendental body; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---one gets; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛṣṭa]]---attracted by the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---takes to devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pā'ya]]---at Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"One who has attained his constitutional position by the strength of devotional service attains a transcendental body even in this lifetime. Being attracted by Lord Kṛṣṇa's transcendental qualities, one fully engages in service at His lotus feet.**
