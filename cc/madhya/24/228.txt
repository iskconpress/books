====== CC Madhya 24.228 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇārāmāś ca' eva---haya kṛṣṇa-manana
> vyādha hañā haya pūjya bhāgavatottama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārāmāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---one who takes pleasure in Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:manana]]---meditation on Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vyādha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a hunter; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:pūjya]]---worshipable; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---the best of the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**"The words kṛṣṇārāmaś ca refer to one who takes pleasure in thinking of Kṛṣṇa. Even though such a person may be a hunter, he is still worshipable and is the best of devotees.**
