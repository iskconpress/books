====== CC Madhya 24.315 ======

===== Text =====

> "sākṣāt īśvara tumi vrajendra-nandana
> tomāra niśvāse sarva-veda-pravartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Mahārāja Nanda; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:n:niśvāse]]---by Your breathing; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:v:veda]]---all Vedic literatures; [[synonyms:p:pravartana]]---are vibrated.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "My dear Lord, You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda. All the Vedic literatures are vibrated through Your breathing.**
