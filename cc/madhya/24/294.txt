====== Cc. Madhya 24.294 ======

===== Text =====

> batriśe chābbiśe mili, aṣṭa-pañcāśa
> āra eka bheda śuna arthera prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:batriśe]]---these thirty-two varieties; [[synonyms:c:chābbiśe]]---the formerly described twenty-six varieties; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---added together; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:pañcāśa]]---they become fifty-eight; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---different; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:a:arthera]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation of import.

===== Translation =====

**"When we add the twenty-six types of devotees to these thirty-two, they altogether number fifty-eight. You may now hear from Me of manifestations with different meanings.**
