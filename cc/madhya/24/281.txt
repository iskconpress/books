====== CC Madhya 24.281 ======

===== Text =====

> nārada kahe,---'aiche raha, tumi bhāgyavān'
> eta bali' dui-jana ha-ilā antardhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārada]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Nārada Muni said; [[synonyms:a:aiche]] [[synonyms:r:raha]]---live like that; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---certainly you are very fortunate; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---both Nārada Muni and Parvata Muni; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]]---disappeared.

===== Translation =====

**"Nārada Muni approved his not wanting more than a daily supply of food, and he blessed him, saying, 'You are fortunate.' Nārada Muni and Parvata Muni then disappeared from that place.**
