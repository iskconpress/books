====== CC Madhya 24.244 ======

===== Text =====

> nārada kahe,---'eka-vastu māgi tomāra sthāne'
> vyādha kahe,---"mṛgādi laha, yei tomāra mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārada]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Nārada Muni said; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:v:vastu]] [[synonyms:m:māgi]]---I wish to beg one thing; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---from you; [[synonyms:v:vyādha]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the hunter replied; [[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:l:laha]]---take some of the hunted animals; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---whatever you like.

===== Translation =====

**"Nārada Muni then told the hunter, 'I have one thing to beg of you.' The hunter replied, 'You may take whatever animals or anything else you would like.**
