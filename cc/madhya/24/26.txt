====== CC Madhya 24.26 ======

===== Text =====

> "svaritañitaḥ kartrabhiprāye kriyā-phale"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarita]]-[[synonyms:ñ:ñitaḥ]]---of verbs having an indicatory //ñ// or a //svarita// accent; [[synonyms:k:kartṛ]]-[[synonyms:a:abhiprāye]]---is meant for the agent; [[synonyms:k:kriyā]]-[[synonyms:p:phale]]---when the fruit of the action.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The terminations of the ātmanepada are employed when the fruit of the action accrues to the agent of verbs having an indicatory ñ or a svarita accent.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from Pāṇini's //sūtras// (1.3.72).
