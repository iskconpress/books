====== CC Madhya 24.144 ======

===== Text =====

> "muktā api līlayā vigrahaṁ
> kṛtvā bhagavantaṁ bhajante"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:muktāḥ]]---liberated; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:l:līlayā]]---by the pastimes; [[synonyms:v:vigraham]]---the form of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛtvā]]---having installed; [[synonyms:b:bhagavantam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhajante]]---worship.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Even a liberated soul merged in the impersonal Brahman effulgence is attracted to the pastimes of Kṛṣṇa. Be thus installs a Deity and renders the Lord service.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from Śaṅkarācārya's commentary on the //Nṛsiṁha-tāpanī Upaniṣad.//
