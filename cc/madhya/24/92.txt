====== CC Madhya 24.92 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakti vinu kona sādhana dite nāre phala
> saba phala deya bhakti svatantra prabala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---without devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---practice for perfection; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---not able; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---any result; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:p:phala]]---all the results of different processes; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---give; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:p:prabala]]---and powerful.

===== Translation =====

**"The other processes cannot yield results unless they are associated with devotional service. Devotional service, however, is so strong and independent that it can give one all the desired results.**
