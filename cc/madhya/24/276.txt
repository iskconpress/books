====== CC Madhya 24.276 ======

===== Text =====

> kampa-pulakāśru haila kṛṣṇa-nāma gāñā
> ūrdhva bāhu nṛtya kare vastra uḍāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]-[[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears and jubilation; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there were; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:g:gāñā]]---chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhva]] [[synonyms:b:bāhu]]---raising the arms; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---began to dance; [[synonyms:v:vastra]] [[synonyms:u:uḍāñā]]---waving his garments up and down.

===== Translation =====

**"When the hunter chanted the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra before his spiritual master, his body trembled, and tears welled in his eyes. Filled with ecstatic love, he raised his hands and began to dance, waving his garments up and down.**
