====== CC Madhya 24.139 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakti vinu mukti nāhi, bhaktye mukti haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---without; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---actually by devotional service; [[synonyms:m:mukti]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---liberation is attained.

===== Translation =====

**"One does not attain liberation without rendering devotional service. Liberation is only attained by devotional service.**
