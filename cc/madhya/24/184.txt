====== CC Madhya 24.184 ======

===== Text =====

> hṛṣīkeśe hṛṣīkāṇi
> yasya sthairya-gatāni hi
> sa eva dhairyam āpnoti
> saṁsāre jīva-cañcale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hṛṣīkeśe]]---to the master of the senses; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣīkāṇi]]---all the senses; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---whose; [[synonyms:s:sthairya]]-[[synonyms:g:gatāni]]---fixed; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that person; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---of course; [[synonyms:d:dhairyam]] [[synonyms:ā:āpnoti]]---attains the position of //dhairya,// endurance; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāre]]---in the material world; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:c:cañcale]]---where everyone is disturbed.

===== Translation =====

**"In this material world, all living entities are disturbed due to their flickering position. A devotee, however, is fixed in the service of the lotus feet of the Lord, the master of the senses. Such a person is to be considered situated in endurance and patience.**
