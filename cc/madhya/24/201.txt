====== Cc. Madhya 24.201 ======

===== Text =====

> jīvera svabhāva---kṛṣṇa-'dāsa'-abhimāna
> dehe ātma-jñāne ācchādita sei 'jñāna'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīvera]] [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---the original characteristic of all living entities; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servant of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---the conception; [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the material body; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---by the conception of the self; [[synonyms:ā:ācchādita]]---covered; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---that original consciousness.

===== Translation =====

**"The original nature of every living entity is to consider himself the eternal servant of Kṛṣṇa. However, under the influence of māyā, he thinks himself to be the body, and thus his original consciousness is covered.**
