====== CC Madhya 24.333 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīcandana-mālya-dhṛti, tulasī-āharaṇa
> vastra-pīṭha-gṛha-saṁskāra, kṛṣṇa-prabodhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---gopī-candana (available in Vṛndāvana and Dvārakā); [[synonyms:m:mālya]]---beads on the neck; [[synonyms:d:dhṛti]]---wearing regularly; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]-[[synonyms:ā:āharaṇa]]---collecting //tulasī// leaves; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---cloth; [[synonyms:p:pīṭha]]---temple; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]---the house; [[synonyms:s:saṁskāra]]---cleansing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prabodhana]]---awakening the Deity of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"After this, you should describe how one should decorate his body with gopīcandana, wear neck beads, collect tulasī leaves from the tulasī tree, cleanse his cloth and the altar, cleanse one's own house or apartment and go to the temple and ring the bell just to draw the attention of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
