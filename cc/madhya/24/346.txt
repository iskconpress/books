====== CC Madhya 24.346 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahilu prabhura sanātane prasāda
> yāhāra śravaṇe cittera khaṇḍe avasāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kahilu]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---mercy; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---hearing which; [[synonyms:c:cittera]]---of the mind; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍe]]---disappears; [[synonyms:a:avasāda]]---all moroseness.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have narrated Lord Caitanya's mercy upon Sanātana Gosvāmī. When one hears these topics, one's heart will be cleansed of all contamination.**
