====== Cc. Madhya 24.340 ======

===== Text =====

> dina-kṛtya, pakṣa-kṛtya, ekādaśy-ādi-vivaraṇa
> māsa-kṛtya, janmāṣṭamyādi-vidhi-vicāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dina]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---daily duties; [[synonyms:p:pakṣa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---duties on the fortnights; [[synonyms:e:ekādaśī]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description of Ekādaśī and so on; [[synonyms:m:māsa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---duties every month; [[synonyms:j:janmāṣṭamī]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---of performing Janmāṣṭamī and other ceremonies; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---of the process; [[synonyms:v:vicāraṇa]]---consideration.

===== Translation =====

**"You should describe the ritualistic duties of every day, and you should describe the fortnightly duties-especially observing Ekādaśī fast, which comes every fortnight. You should also describe the duties of every month, especially the observance of ceremonies like Janmāṣṭamī, Rāma-navamī and Nṛsiṁha-caturdaśī.**
