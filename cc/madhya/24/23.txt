====== CC Madhya 24.23 ======

===== Text =====

> māyā-śaktye brahmāṇḍādi-paripāṭī-sṛjana
> 'urukrama'-śabdera ei artha nirūpaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---by His external potency; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---of material universes and so on; [[synonyms:p:paripāṭī]]---an orderly arrangement; [[synonyms:s:sṛjana]]---creating; [[synonyms:u:urukrama]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabdera]]---of the word //urukrama//; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---of the meaning; [[synonyms:n:nirūpaṇa]]---ascertainment.

===== Translation =====

**"The word urukrama indicates the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who, by His external potency, has perfectly created innumerable universes.**
