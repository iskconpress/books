====== CC Madhya 24.3 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sanātana prabhura caraṇe dhariyā
> punarapi kahe kichu vinaya kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great humility.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Sanātana Gosvāmī clasped the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and humbly submitted the following petition.**
