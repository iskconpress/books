====== Cc. Madhya 24.188 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-kṛpāya sādhu-saṅge rati-buddhi pāya
> saba chāḍi' śuddha-bhakti kare kṛṣṇa-pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of devotees; [[synonyms:r:rati]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---devotional attraction and intelligence; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---one obtains; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up everything; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---pure devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"By the mercy of Kṛṣṇa and by the association of devotees, one increases his attraction to and intelligence for pure devotional service; therefore one gives up everything and engages himself at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa and His pure devotees.**
