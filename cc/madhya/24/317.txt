====== CC Madhya 24.317 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"kene kara āmāra stavana
> bhāgavatera svarūpa kene nā kara vicāraṇa?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:kene]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---why do you do; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---My personal glorification; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavatera]] [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the real form of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you do not do; [[synonyms:v:vicāraṇa]]---consideration.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Why are you glorifying Me personally? You should understand the transcendental position of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam. Why don't you consider this important point?**
