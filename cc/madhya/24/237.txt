====== CC Madhya 24.237 ======

===== Text =====

> kruddha hañā vyādha tāṅre gāli dite cāya
> nārada-prabhāve mukhe gāli nāhi āya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kruddha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very angry; [[synonyms:v:vyādha]]---the hunter; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:g:gāli]] [[synonyms:d:dite]] [[synonyms:c:cāya]]---wanted to abuse; [[synonyms:n:nārada]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhāve]]---by the influence of Nārada; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth; [[synonyms:g:gāli]]---abusive language; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:ā:āya]]---did not come.

===== Translation =====

**"When all the animals fled, the hunter wanted to chastise Nārada with abusive language, but due to Nārada's presence, he could not utter anything abusive.**
