====== CC Madhya 24.221 ======

===== Text =====

> ca-śabde 'samuccaye', āra artha kaya
> 'ātmārāmāś ca munayaś ca' kṛṣṇere bhajaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:ca]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by the word //ca//; [[synonyms:s:samuccaye]]---in aggregation; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---import; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---is meant; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmāḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]] [[synonyms:m:munayaḥ]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---all the //ātmārāmas// and //munis//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇere]] [[synonyms:b:bhajaya]]---worship Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"As mentioned above, the word ca has been used to mean 'aggregate.' According to this meaning, all the ātmārāmas and the munis engage in Kṛṣṇa's service. Besides 'aggregate,' there is another meaning of the word ca.**
