====== Cc. Madhya 24.78 ======

===== Text =====

> ātatatvāc ca mātṛtvād
> ātmā hi paramo hariḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātatatvāt]]---due to being all-pervading; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mātṛtvāt]]---due to being the progenitor; [[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]---the soul; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:paramaḥ]]---supreme; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Hari, the Personality of Godhead, is the all-pervasive original source of everything; He is therefore the Supersoul of everything.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from the //Bhāvārtha-dīpikā,// Śrīdhara Svāmī's commentary on //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.//
