====== CC Madhya 24.153 ======

===== Text =====

> "nirgranthā api"ra ei 'api'---sambhāvane
> ei sāta artha prathame kariluṅ vyākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirgranthāḥ]] [[synonyms:a:apira]]---of the words //nirgranthāḥ// api; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:api]]---//api//; [[synonyms:s:sambhāvane]]---in the matter of exposition; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---these seven different meanings; [[synonyms:p:prathame]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---in explanation.

===== Translation =====

**"Api added to the word nirgranthāḥ is used for exposition. Thus I have tried to clarify the seven types of meaning.**
