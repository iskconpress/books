====== CC Madhya 16.158 ======

===== Text =====

> madyapa yavana-rājāra āge adhikāra
> tāṅra bhaye pathe keha nāre calibāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madyapa]]---drunkard; [[synonyms:y:yavana]]---Mohammedan; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]]---of a king; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:a:adhikāra]]---the government; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---being afraid of such a king; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---not able; [[synonyms:c:calibāra]]---to travel.

===== Translation =====

**He informed the Lord that the territory ahead was ruled by a Moslem governor, who was a drunkard. Out of fear for this king, no one could walk the road freely.**
