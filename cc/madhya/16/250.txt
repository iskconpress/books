====== CC Madhya 16.250 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe navadvīpe tāṅre dila pāṭhāñā
> nīlādri calilā saṅge bhakta-gaṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---to Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---her; [[synonyms:d:dila]] [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāñā]]---sent back; [[synonyms:n:nīlādri]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīmatī Śacīdevī was sent back to Navadvīpa, and the Lord and His devotees started for Jagannātha Purī, Nīlādri.**
