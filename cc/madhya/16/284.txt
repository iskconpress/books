====== Cc. Madhya 16.284 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' saba bhakta kahe prabhura caraṇe
> sabākāra icchā paṇḍita kaila nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---unto the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sabākāra]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---everyone's desire; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---has made; [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this statement, the devotees present at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stated that Gadādhara Paṇḍita had properly presented their desire.**
