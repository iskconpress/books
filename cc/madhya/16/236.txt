====== CC Madhya 16.236 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvajña gaurāṅga-prabhu jāni' tāṅra mana
> śikṣā-rūpe kahe tāṅre āśvāsa-vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---omniscient; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---as an instruction; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words of assurance.

===== Translation =====

**Since Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was omniscient, He could understand Raghunātha dāsa's mind. The Lord therefore instructed him with the following reassuring words.**
