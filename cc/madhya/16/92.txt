====== Cc. Madhya 16.92 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā prabhura vāṇī mane vicāraya
> prabhu-sane ati haṭha kabhu bhāla naya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---the words; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in their minds; [[synonyms:v:vicāraya]]---considered; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very much; [[synonyms:h:haṭha]]---tricks; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:n:naya]]---s not very good.

===== Translation =====

**When Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya heard these words, they began to consider that it was not at all good that they played so many tricks on the Lord.**
