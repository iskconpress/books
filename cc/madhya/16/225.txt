====== CC Madhya 16.225 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra pitā sadā kare ācārya-sevana
> ataeva ācārya tāṅre hailā parasanna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---his father; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:sevana]]---worship of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---therefore Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---upon him; [[synonyms:h:hailā]] [[synonyms:p:parasanna]]---became pleased.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa's father, Govardhana, always rendered much service to Advaita Ācārya. Consequently Advaita Ācārya was very pleased with the family.**
