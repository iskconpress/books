====== CC Madhya 16.255 ======

===== Text =====

> gadādhara-paṇḍita āsi' prabhure mililā
> sabāra agrete prabhu kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:a:agrete]]---in front of all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**Gadādhara Paṇḍita also came and met the Lord. Then, before all the devotees, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to speak as follows.**
