====== CC Madhya 16.120 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura daraśane sabe haila premamaya
> 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa' kahe, netra aśru variṣaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---by seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---overwhelmed with love; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---chanted the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:netra]]---the eyes; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:v:variṣaya]]---poured.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing the Lord, they all felt themselves overwhelmed with love of Godhead, and, tears pouring from their eyes, they began to chant the holy name, "Kṛṣṇa! Kṛṣṇa!"**
