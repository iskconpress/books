====== CC Madhya 16.242 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' mahāprabhu tāṅre vidāya dila
> ghare āsi' mahāprabhura śikṣā ācarila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---returning home; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]]---the instruction; [[synonyms:ā:ācarila]]---practiced.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade farewell to Raghunātha dāsa, who returned home and did exactly what the Lord told him.**
