====== CC Madhya 16.26 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda-sena kare saba samādhāna
> ghāṭiyāla prabodhi' dena sabāre vāsā-sthāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---all arrangements; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭiyāla]]---the men in charge of levying taxes; [[synonyms:p:prabodhi']]---satisfying; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to everyone; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---resting places.

===== Translation =====

**As stated, Śivānanda Sena used to make all arrangements for the party's necessities. In particular, he used to pacify the men in charge of levying taxes, and he found resting places for everyone.**
