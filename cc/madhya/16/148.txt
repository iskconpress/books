====== CC Madhya 16.148 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu lāgi' dharma-karma chāḍe bhakta-gaṇa
> bhakta-dharma-hāni prabhura nā haya sahana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the sake of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]---all prescribed duties; [[synonyms:c:chāḍe]]---give up; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---of the duty of a devotee; [[synonyms:h:hāni]]---the abandonment; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is not; [[synonyms:s:sahana]]---tolerable.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees would abandon all kinds of duties for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's sake, yet the Lord did not like the devotees' giving up their promised duties.**
