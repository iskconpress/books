====== CC Madhya 16.32 ======

===== Text =====

> mādhava-purīra kathā, gopāla-sthāpana
> tāṅhāre gopāla yaiche māgila candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]-[[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the narration of Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---installation of the Deity of Gopāla; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:m:māgila]]---He begged; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood.

===== Translation =====

**They then all discussed the story of Śrī Mādhavendra Purī's installation of the Gopāla Deity, and they discussed how Gopāla begged sandalwood from him.**
