====== CC Madhya 16.71 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho kahe,---"ke vaiṣṇava, ki tāṅra lakṣaṇa?"
> tabe hāsi' kahe prabhu jāni' tāṅra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he said; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---a Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---his mind.

===== Translation =====

**The inhabitant of Kulīna-grāma said, "Please let me know who is actually a Vaiṣṇava and what his symptoms are." Understanding his mind, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled and gave the following reply.**
