~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 16.240~~

====== CC Madhya 16.240 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana dekhi' yabe āsiba nīlācale
> tabe tumi āmā-pāśa āsiha kona chale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after visiting Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ā:āsiba]]---I shall come back; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---to Me; [[synonyms:ā:āsiha]]---please come; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:c:chale]]---by some pretext.

===== Translation =====

**"You may see me at Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī, when I return after visiting Vṛndāvana. By that time you can think of some trick to escape.**
