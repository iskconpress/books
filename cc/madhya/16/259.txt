====== Cc. Madhya 16.259 ======

===== Text =====

> yathā rahi, tathā ghara-prācīra haya cūrṇa
> yathā netra paḍe tathā loka dekhi pūrṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]] [[synonyms:r:rahi]]---wherever I stayed; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]-[[synonyms:p:prācīra]]---the building and the boundary walls; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---became; [[synonyms:c:cūrṇa]]---broken; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---wherever; [[synonyms:n:netra]]---the eyes; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---filled.

===== Translation =====

**"Indeed, the crowd was so large that the house and the boundary walls of the house where I stayed were destroyed, and wherever I looked I could see only large crowds.**
