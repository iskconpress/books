====== CC Madhya 16.111 ======

===== Text =====

> 'grāme-grāme' nūtana āvāsa karibā
> pāṅca-sāta navya-gṛhe sāmagrye bharibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:grāme]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in every village; [[synonyms:n:nūtana]]---new; [[synonyms:ā:āvāsa]]---residential places; [[synonyms:k:karibā]]---you should construct; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five to seven; [[synonyms:n:navya]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in new houses; [[synonyms:s:sāmagrye]]---with food; [[synonyms:b:bharibā]]---you should fill.

===== Translation =====

**His orders read: "In every village you should construct new residences, and in five or seven new houses, you should store all kinds of food.**
