====== CC Madhya 16.169 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' yavanera mana phiri' gela
> āpana-'viśvāsa' uḍiyā sthāne pāṭhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing this; [[synonyms:y:yavanera]]---of the Mohammedan governor; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:p:phiri']] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---became changed; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]---own; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---secretary; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]---of the representative of the Orissan government; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this, the mind of the Mohammedan governor was changed. He then sent his own secretary to the representative of the Orissan government.**
