====== Cc. Madhya 16.61 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra mukha dekhi' hāse śacīra nandana
> aṅgīkāra jāni' ācārya karena nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---His face; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---smiles; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---understanding the acceptance; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performed; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the face of Advaita Ācārya, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled. Understanding that the Lord had accepted the proposal, Advaita Ācārya started to dance.**
