====== Cc. Madhya 16.116 ======

===== Text =====

> caturdvāre karaha uttama navya vāsa
> rāmānanda, yāha tumi mahāprabhu-pāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturdvāre]]---at the place named Caturdvāra; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---make; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---very nice; [[synonyms:n:navya]] [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---new residential quarters; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:y:yāha]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you please go; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---near Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**The King continued, "At Caturdvāra, please construct new residential quarters. Now, Rāmānanda, you can return to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu."**
