====== CC Madhya 16.1 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍodyānaṁ gaura-meghaḥ
> siñcan svālokanāmṛtaiḥ
> bhavāgni-dagdha-janatā-
> vīrudhaḥ samajīvayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:u:udyānam]]---upon the garden known as Gauḍa-deśa; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:m:meghaḥ]]---the cloud known as Gaura; [[synonyms:s:siñcan]]---pouring water; [[synonyms:s:sva]]---His own; [[synonyms:ā:ālokana]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛtaiḥ]]---with the nectar of the glance; [[synonyms:b:bhava]]-[[synonyms:a:agni]]---by the blazing fire of material existence; [[synonyms:d:dagdha]]---having been burnt; [[synonyms:j:janatā]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:v:vīrudhaḥ]]---who are like creepers and plants; [[synonyms:s:samajīvayat]]---revived.

===== Translation =====

**By the nectar of His personal glance, the cloud known as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu poured water upon the garden of Gauḍa-deśa and revived the people, who were like creepers and plants burning in the forest fire of material existence.**
