====== Cc. Madhya 16.251 ======

===== Text =====

> sei saba loka pathe karena sevana
> sukhe nīlācala āilā śacīra nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all those persons; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---rendered all service; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees who accompanied Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu rendered all kinds of service on the way to Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī. Thus in great happiness the Lord returned.**
