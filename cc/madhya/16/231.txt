====== CC Madhya 16.231 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe yadi mahāprabhu 'śāntipura' āilā
> śuniyā pitāre raghunātha nivedilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śāntipura]]---to Śāntipura; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:pitāre]]---unto his father; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:n:nivedilā]]---submitted.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghunātha dāsa learned that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had arrived at Śāntipura, he submitted a request to his father.**
