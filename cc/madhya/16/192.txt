====== Cc. Madhya 16.192 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei mahāprabhura caraṇa vandiyā
> sabāra caraṇa vandi' cale hṛṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---the governor; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandiyā]]---after worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandi']]---offering respect to the feet of all the other devotees; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---departed; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very pleased.

===== Translation =====

**After this, the Mohammedan governor offered prayers to the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as well as to the lotus feet of all His devotees. After that, the governor departed. Indeed, he was very pleased.**
