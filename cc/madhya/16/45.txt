====== CC Madhya 16.45 ======

===== Text =====

> vāṇīnātha, kāśī-miśra prasāda ānila
> svahaste sabāre prabhu prasāda khāoyāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought all kinds of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with His own hand; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of the food of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāila]]---fed.

===== Translation =====

**Vāṇīnātha Rāya and Kāśī Miśra then brought a large quantity of prasāda, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu distributed it with His own hand and fed them all.**
