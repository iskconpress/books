====== CC Madhya 16.180 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-pātra ānila tāṅre kariyā sammāna
> yoḍa-hāte prabhu-āge laya kṛṣṇa-nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the Orissan representative; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---showing great respect; [[synonyms:y:yoḍa]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---with folded hands; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:laya]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---chanted the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Arriving in that way, the Mohammedan governor was respectfully brought before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu by the mahā-pātra. The governor then stood before the Lord with folded hands, and he chanted the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.**
