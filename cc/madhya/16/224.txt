====== CC Madhya 16.224 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura caraṇe paḍe premāviṣṭa hañā
> prabhu pāda-sparśa kaila karuṇā kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell down; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---absorbed in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---touched with His feet; [[synonyms:k:karuṇā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---showing.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghunātha dāsa went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he fell at the Lord's lotus feet in ecstatic love. Showing him mercy, the Lord touched him with His feet.**
