====== CC Madhya 16.151 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu vidāya dila, rāya yāya tāṅra sane
> kṛṣṇa-kathā rāmānanda-sane rātri-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---the Lord bade them farewell; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Him; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---discussion of topics of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade farewell to the officers, and Rāya Rāmānanda continued on with the Lord. The Lord talked to Rāmānanda Rāya about Śrī Kṛṣṇa day and night.**
