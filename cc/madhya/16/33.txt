====== CC Madhya 16.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra lāgi' gopīnātha kṣīra curi kaila
> mahāprabhura mukhe āge e kathā śunila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for him (Mādhavendra Purī); [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---the Deity named Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---condensed milk; [[synonyms:c:curi]]---stealing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---previously; [[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---this incident; [[synonyms:ś:śunila]]---heard.

===== Translation =====

**It was Gopīnātha who stole condensed milk for the sake of Mādhavendra Purī. This incident had been previously related by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Himself.**
