====== CC Madhya 16.253 ======

===== Text =====

> ānandita bhakta-gaṇa āsiyā mililā
> prema-āliṅgana prabhu sabāre karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing in love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to all devotees; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees then came and met the Lord with great happiness. The Lord also embraced each of them in great ecstatic love.**
