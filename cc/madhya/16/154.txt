====== CC Madhya 16.154 ======

===== Text =====

> bhūmete paḍilā rāya nāhika cetana
> rāye kole kari' prabhu karaye krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhūmete]] [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down on the ground; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---there was no consciousness; [[synonyms:r:rāye]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kole]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking on the lap; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---began crying.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāmānanda Rāya fell to the ground and lost consciousness, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took him upon His lap and began to cry.**
