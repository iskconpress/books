====== CC Madhya 16.143 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍite lañā yāite sārvabhaume ājñā dilā
> bhaṭṭācārya kahe,---"uṭha, aiche prabhura līlā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍite]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---unto Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave an order; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:u:uṭha]]---please get up; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the way of the Lord's pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya to take Gadādhara Paṇḍita with him. The Bhaṭṭācārya told Gadādhara Paṇḍita, "Get up! Such are the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
