====== CC Madhya 16.7 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda, sārvabhauma, dui-janā-sthāne
> tabe yukti kare prabhu---'yāba vṛndāvane'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:janā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---before the two persons; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:y:yukti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---consulted; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāba]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---I shall go to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Himself consulted Rāmānanda Rāya and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, saying, "I shall go to Vṛndāvana."**
