====== CC Madhya 16.206 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ haite āge gelā śivānanda-ghara
> vāsudeva-gṛhe pāche āilā īśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from there; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu proceeded; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:ghara]]---to the house of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to the house of Vāsudeva Datta; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---after this; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**From the house of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, the Lord went to the house of Śivānanda Sena and then to the house of Vāsudeva Datta.**
