====== Cc. Madhya 16.155 ======

===== Text =====

> rāyera vidāya-bhāva nā yāya sahana
> kahite nā pāri ei tāhāra varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāyera]] [[synonyms:v:vidāya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---feelings of separation from Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---not possible; [[synonyms:s:sahana]]---to tolerate; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am not able; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---a description.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya's separation from Caitanya Mahāprabhu is very difficult to describe. It is almost intolerable, and therefore I cannot describe it further.**
