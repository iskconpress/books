====== CC Madhya 16.42 ======

===== Text =====

> narendra āsiyā tāhāṅ sabāre mililā
> mahāprabhura datta mālā sabāre parāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:narendra]]---to the lake named Narendra; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---everyone; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:datta]]---given; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garlands; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]] [[synonyms:p:parāilā]]---offered to everyone.

===== Translation =====

**When the devotees from Bengal reached Lake Narendra, Svarūpa Dāmodara and the others met them and offered them the garlands given by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
