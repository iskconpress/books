====== CC Madhya 16.70 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"vaiṣṇava-sevā, nāma-saṅkīrtana
> dui kara, śīghra pābe śrī-kṛṣṇa-caraṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service to the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you perform these two things; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very soon; [[synonyms:p:pābe]]---you will get; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---shelter at the lotus feet of the Lord, Śrī Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord replied, "You should engage yourself in the service of the servants of Kṛṣṇa and always chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa. If you do these two things, you will very soon attain shelter at Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet."**
