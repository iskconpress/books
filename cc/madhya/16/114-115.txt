====== Cc. Madhya 16.114-115 ======

===== Text =====

> eka navya-naukā āni' rākhaha nadī-tīre
> yāhāṅ snāna kari' prabhu yā'na nadī-pāre
>
> tāhāṅ stambha ropaṇa kara 'mahā-tīrtha' kari'
> nitya snāna kariba tāhāṅ, tāhāṅ yena mari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:n:navya]]---new; [[synonyms:n:naukā]]---boat; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:r:rākhaha]]---keep; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank of the river; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking bath; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yā'na]]---goes; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]-[[synonyms:p:pāre]]---on the other bank of the river; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:s:stambha]]---a memorial column; [[synonyms:r:ropaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---establish; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making that place a great place of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall bathe; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:yena]] [[synonyms:m:mari]]---let me die.

===== Translation =====

**The King also ordered them to maintain a new boat on the banks of the river, and wherever Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His bath or crossed to the other side of the river, they should establish a memorial column and make that place a great place of pilgrimage. "Indeed," said the King, "I will take my bath there. And let me also die there."**
