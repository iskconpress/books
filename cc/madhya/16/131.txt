====== Cc. Madhya 16.131 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita kahe,---"yāhāṅ tumi, sei nīlācala
> kṣetra-sannyāsa mora yāuka rasātala"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita said; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You are situated; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:k:kṣetra]]-[[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---vow to remain in a holy place of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:y:yāuka]]---let it go; [[synonyms:r:rasātala]]---to hell.

===== Translation =====

**When he was requested to return to Jagannātha Purī, Gadādhara Paṇḍita told the Lord, "Wherever You are staying is Jagannātha Purī. Let my so-called kṣetra-sannyāsa go to hell."**
