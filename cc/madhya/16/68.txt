====== Cc. Madhya 16.68 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅre vidāya dila prabhu kari' āliṅgana
> ei-mata vidāya dila saba bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him (Nityānanda Prabhu); [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---to all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Nityānanda Prabhu and bade Him farewell. He then bade farewell to all the other devotees.**
