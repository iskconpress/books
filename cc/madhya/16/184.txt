====== Cc. Madhya 16.184 ======

===== Text =====

> 'caṇḍāla---pavitra yāṅra śrī-nāma-śravaṇe
> hena-tomāra ei jīva pāila daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caṇḍāla]]---the dog-eater, the lowest of mankind; [[synonyms:p:pavitra]]---purified; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing the holy name; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of such a one as You; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---this conditioned living entity; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---has gotten; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---the personal visit.

===== Translation =====

**"Simply by hearing Your holy name, a caṇḍāla, lowest of men, can be purified. Now this conditioned soul has received Your personal interview.**
