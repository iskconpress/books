====== CC Madhya 16.58 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryaratna-ādi yata mukhya bhakta-gaṇa
> madhye madhye prabhure karena nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryaratna]]---Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---chief devotees; [[synonyms:m:madhye]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---at intervals; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---gave invitations.

===== Translation =====

**All the chief devotees, headed by Candraśekhara [Ācāryaratna] used to extend invitations to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu periodically.**
