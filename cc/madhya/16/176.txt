====== CC Madhya 16.176 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' viśvāsere kahila vacana
> "bhāgya tāṅra---āsi' karuka prabhu daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsere]]---unto the secretary of the Mohammedan governor; [[synonyms:k:kahila]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---spoke the following words; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---great fortune; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:karuka]]---let him do; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After thinking this, the mahā-pātra immediately informed the Mohammedan secretary, "It is a great fortune for your governor. Let him come visit Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
