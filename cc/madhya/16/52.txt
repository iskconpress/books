====== CC Madhya 16.52 ======

===== Text =====

> ghaṭa bhari' prabhura teṅho abhiṣeka kaila
> tāṅra abhiṣeke prabhu mahā-tṛpta haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghaṭa]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling one waterpot; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:a:abhiṣeka]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed the bathing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:a:abhiṣeke]]---by the act of bathing the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:t:tṛpta]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became very satisfied.

===== Translation =====

**It was Kṛṣṇadāsa who filled a great waterpot and poured it over the Lord while He was taking His bath. The Lord was greatly satisfied by this.**
