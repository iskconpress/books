====== Cc. Madhya 16.227 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu tāṅre vidāya diyā gelā nīlācala
> teṅho ghare āsi' hailā premete pāgala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---bidding farewell; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went back; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---returning home; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:premete]] [[synonyms:p:pāgala]]---mad in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**After bidding farewell to Raghunātha dāsa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Jagannātha Purī. After returning home, Raghunātha dāsa became mad with ecstatic love.**
