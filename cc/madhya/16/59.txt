====== CC Madhya 16.59 ======

===== Text =====

> cāturmāsya-ante punaḥ nityānande lañā
> kibā yukti kare nitya nibhṛte vasiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]]-[[synonyms:a:ante]]---at the end of Cāturmāsya; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nityānande]]---Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking into confidence; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---what; [[synonyms:y:yukti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---They consult; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting.

===== Translation =====

**At the end of the four-month Cāturmāsya period, Caitanya Mahāprabhu again consulted with Nityānanda Prabhu daily in a solitary place. No one could understand what Their consultation was about.**
