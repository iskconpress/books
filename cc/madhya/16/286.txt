====== CC Madhya 16.286 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dina gadādhara kaila nimantraṇa
> tāhāṅ bhikṣā kaila prabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---that day; [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---gave an invitation; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---at his place; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---took lunch; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---His devotees.

===== Translation =====

**That day Gadādhara Paṇḍita extended an invitation to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord took His lunch at his place with the other devotees.**
