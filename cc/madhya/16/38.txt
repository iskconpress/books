====== CC Madhya 16.38 ======

===== Text =====

> āṭhāranālāke āilā gosāñi śuniyā
> dui-mālā pāṭhāilā govinda-hāte diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āṭhāranālāke]]---Āṭhāranālā; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---they have reached; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---two garlands; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---through the hands of Govinda.

===== Translation =====

**When they all arrived at a bridge called Āṭhāranālā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, hearing the news of their arrival, sent two garlands with Govinda.**
