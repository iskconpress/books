====== Cc. Madhya 16.229 ======

===== Text =====

> pañca pāika tāṅre rākhe rātri-dine
> cāri sevaka, dui brāhmaṇa rahe tāṅra sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañca]]---five; [[synonyms:p:pāika]]---watchmen; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him (Raghunātha dāsa); [[synonyms:r:rākhe]]---keep; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---four personal servants; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---two //brāhmaṇas// to cook; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remain; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with him.

===== Translation =====

**His father even had five watchmen guard him day and night. Four personal servants were employed to look after his comfort, and two brāhmaṇas were employed to cook for him.**
