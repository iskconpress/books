====== CC Madhya 16.285 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāra icchāya prabhu cāri māsa rahilā
> śuniyā pratāparudra ānandita hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---because of everyone's desire; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---for four months; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very, very happy.

===== Translation =====

**Being requested by all the devotees, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu agreed to remain at Jagannātha Purī for four months. Hearing this, King Pratāparudra became very happy.**
