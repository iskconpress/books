====== CC Madhya 16.243 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhya vairāgya, vātulatā sakala chāḍiyā
> yathā-yogya kārya kare anāsakta hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhya]] [[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---external renunciation; [[synonyms:v:vātulatā]]---craziness; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---as it is befitting; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---duties; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:a:anāsakta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being without attachment.

===== Translation =====

**After returning home, Raghunātha dāsa gave up all craziness and external pseudo renunciation and engaged in his household duties without attachment.**
