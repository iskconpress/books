====== CC Madhya 16.29 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānandera paricaya saba sevaka sane
> bahuta sammāna āsi' kaila sevaka-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānandera]]---of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:paricaya]]---there was acquaintance; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with the priests of the temple; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---great respect; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the priests.

===== Translation =====

**All the priests of the temple had been previously acquainted with Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; therefore they all came to offer great respects to the Lord.**
