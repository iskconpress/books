====== CC Madhya 16.262 ======

===== Text =====

> vidyā-bhakti-buddhi-bale parama pravīṇa
> tabu āpanāke māne tṛṇa haite hīna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidyā]]---education; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---and intelligence; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---in strength; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---very; [[synonyms:p:pravīṇa]]---experienced; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāke]]---themselves; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---they think; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]---a straw; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than; [[synonyms:h:hīna]]---lower.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīla Rūpa and Sanātana are very experienced in education, devotional service, intelligence and strength, yet they think themselves inferior to straw in the street.**
