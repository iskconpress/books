====== Cc. Madhya 16.49 ======

===== Text =====

> kulīna-grāmī paṭṭa-ḍorī jagannāthe dila
> pūrvavat ratha-agre nartana karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmī]]---the residents of Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:p:paṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍorī]]---silken ropes; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---offered to Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like the previous year; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:a:agre]]---in front of the car; [[synonyms:n:nartana]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---danced.

===== Translation =====

**The inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma delivered silken ropes to Lord Jagannātha, and as previously they all danced before the Lord's car.**
