====== Cc. Madhya 16.93 ======

===== Text =====

> duṅhe kahe,---ebe varṣā, calite nāribā
> vijayā-daśamī āile avaśya calibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṅhe]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---both of them said; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:v:varṣā]]---rainy season; [[synonyms:c:calite]] [[synonyms:n:nāribā]]---You will not be able to go; [[synonyms:v:vijayā]]-[[synonyms:d:daśamī]]---the Vijayā-daśamī day; [[synonyms:ā:āile]]---when it arrives; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:c:calibā]]---You will go.

===== Translation =====

**They both said, "Now that the rainy season is here, it will be difficult for You to travel. It is better to wait for Vijayā-daśamī before departing for Vṛndāvana."**
