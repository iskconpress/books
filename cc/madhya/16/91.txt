====== CC Madhya 16.91 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍa-deśa diyā yāba tāṅ-sabā dekhiyā
> tumi duṅhe ājñā deha' parasanna hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---the country known as Bengal; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---through; [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---both of them; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of you; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---give Me permission; [[synonyms:p:parasanna]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very pleased.

===== Translation =====

**"I shall go to Vṛndāvana through Bengal and see both My mother and the River Ganges. Now would you two be pleased to give Me permission?"**
