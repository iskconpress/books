====== CC Madhya 16.11 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi svatantra prabhu nahe nivāraṇa
> bhakta-icchā vinā prabhu nā kare gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---fully independent; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nahe]] [[synonyms:n:nivāraṇa]]---there is no checking Him; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:i:icchā]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without the permission of devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---does not go.

===== Translation =====

**Although the Lord is completely independent and incapable of being checked by anyone, He still did not go without the permission of His devotees.**
