====== CC Madhya 16.214-215 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye mililā yaiche rūpa-sanātana
> nṛsiṁhānanda kaila yaiche pathera sājana
>
> sūtra-madhye sei līlā āmi ta' varṇiluṅ
> ataeva punaḥ tāhā ihāṅ nā likhiluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---He met; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---the two brothers Rūpa and Sanātana; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhānanda]]---Nṛsiṁhānanda; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:p:pathera]] [[synonyms:s:sājana]]---decoration of the road; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the synopsis; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---those pastimes; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:v:varṇiluṅ]]---have described; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:likhiluṅ]]---I have not written.

===== Translation =====

**Those narrations tell how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu met the brothers Rūpa and Sanātana and how Nṛsiṁhānanda decorated the road. I have already described these in an earlier synopsis of this book; therefore I will not repeat the narrations here.**

===== Purport =====

This information is given in //Ādi-līlā// (Chapter Ten, verse 35) and //Madhya-līlā// (Chapter One, verses 155-162 and 175-226).
