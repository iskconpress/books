====== CC Madhya 16.179 ======

===== Text =====

> dūra haite prabhu dekhi' bhūmete paḍiyā
> daṇḍavat kare aśru-pulakita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from a distance; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhūmete]] [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---falling down on the ground; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:p:pulakita]]---jubilant; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu from a distant place, the Mohammedan governor fell to the ground and offered obeisances. Tears came to his eyes, and he was jubilant with ecstatic emotions.**
