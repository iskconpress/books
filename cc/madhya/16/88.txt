====== CC Madhya 16.88 ======

===== Text =====

> bahuta utkaṇṭhā mora yāite vṛndāvana
> tomāra haṭhe dui vatsara nā kailuṅ gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---great anxiety; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:y:yāite]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:h:haṭhe]]---by your tricks; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---for two years; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I did not do; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My desire to go to Vṛndāvana has very much increased. Because of your tricks, I have not been able to go there for the past two years.**
