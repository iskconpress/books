====== CC Madhya 16.40 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāñi ārambha kaila kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana
> nācite nācite cali' āilā dui-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāñi]]---on that very spot; [[synonyms:ā:ārambha]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---began; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nācite]] [[synonyms:n:nācite]]---dancing and dancing; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---going; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---reached; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---both of Them.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, They began chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa on that very spot, and, dancing and dancing, both Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu reached Jagannātha Purī.**
