====== CC Madhya 16.30 ======

===== Text =====

> sei rātri saba mahānta tāhāñi rahilā
> bāra kṣīra āni' āge sevaka dharilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---that night; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:m:mahānta]]---all the great devotees; [[synonyms:t:tāhāñi]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained there; [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---twelve pots; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---condensed milk; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the priests; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---placed.

===== Translation =====

**That night, all the great devotees remained in the temple, and the priests brought twelve pots of condensed milk, which they placed before Lord Nityānanda Prabhu.**
