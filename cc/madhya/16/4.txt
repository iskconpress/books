====== Cc. Madhya 16.4 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma, rāmānanda, āni' dui jana
> duṅhāke kahena rājā vinaya-vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---calling; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāke]]---unto both of them; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---said; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---submissive words.

===== Translation =====

**The King therefore called for Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya, and he spoke the following submissive words to them.**
