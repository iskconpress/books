====== Cc. Madhya 16.107 ======

===== Text =====

> sustha kari, rāmānanda rājāre vasāilā
> kāya-mano-vākye prabhu tāṅre kṛpā kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sustha]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---comforting him; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāya Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:r:rājāre]] [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made the King sit down; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:m:mano]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]]---with body, mind and words; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---showed His mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Finally Rāmānanda Rāya pacified the King and made him sit down. The Lord also bestowed mercy upon him through His body, mind and words.**
