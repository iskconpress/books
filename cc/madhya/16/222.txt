====== CC Madhya 16.222 ======

===== Text =====

> sei govardhanera putra---raghunātha dāsa
> bālya-kāla haite teṅho viṣaye udāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:g:govardhanera]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---son of Govardhana Majumadāra; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:b:bālya]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from his very childhood; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:v:viṣaye]] [[synonyms:u:udāsa]]---indifferent to material happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa was the son of Govardhana Majumadāra. From childhood, he was uninterested in material enjoyment.**
