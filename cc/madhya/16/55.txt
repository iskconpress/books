====== CC Madhya 16.55 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya-gosāñi prabhura kaila nimantraṇa
> tāra madhye kaila yaiche jhaḍa-variṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that episode; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---occurred; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:j:jhaḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:variṣaṇa]]---rainstorm.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya then extended an invitation to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and there was a great rainstorm connected with that incident.**
