====== CC Madhya 16.144 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi jāna, kṛṣṇa nija-pratijñā chāḍilā
> bhakta kṛpā-vaśe bhīṣmera pratijñā rākhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:j:jāna]]---you know; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:p:pratijñā]]---His own promise; [[synonyms:c:chāḍilā]]---gave up; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśe]]---being obliged by the devotional service of a devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhīṣmera]]---of Grandfather Bhīṣma; [[synonyms:p:pratijñā]] [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept the promise.

===== Translation =====

**"You should know that Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself violated His own promise just to keep the promise of Grandfather Bhīṣma.**
