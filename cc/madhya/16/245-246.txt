====== CC Madhya 16.245-246 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāṅ prabhu ekatra kari' saba bhakta-gaṇa
> advaita-nityānandādi yata bhakta-jana

> sabā āliṅgana kari' kahena gosāñi
> sabe ājñā deha'---āmi nīlācale yāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here (at Śāntipura); [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---assembling in one place; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---embracing every one of them; [[synonyms:k:kahena]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---just give Me permission; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---may go.

===== Translation =====

**Meanwhile, at Śāntipura, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu assembled all His devotees-headed by Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu-embraced them all and asked their permission to return to Jagannātha Purī.**
