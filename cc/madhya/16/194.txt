====== CC Madhya 16.194 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle sei bahu naukā sājāñā
> prabhuke ānite dila viśvāsa pāṭhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---the governor; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:n:naukā]]---boats; [[synonyms:s:sājāñā]]---decorating; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānite]]---to bring; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---the secretary; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāñā]]---sending.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning the governor sent his secretary with many nicely decorated boats to bring Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to the other side of the river.**
