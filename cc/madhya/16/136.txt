====== Cc. Madhya 16.136 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' paṇḍita-gosāñi pṛthak calilā
> kaṭaka āsi' prabhu tāṅre saṅge ānāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---proceeded separately; [[synonyms:k:kaṭaka]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---when He came to Kaṭaka; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:ā:ānāilā]]---brought.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Gadādhara Paṇḍita Gosvāmī traveled alone, but when they all arrived at Kaṭaka, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu called him, and he went in the Lord's company.**
