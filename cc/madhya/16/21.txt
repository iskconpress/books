====== CC Madhya 16.21 ======

===== Text =====

> se vatsara prabhu dekhite saba ṭhākurāṇī
> calilā ācārya-saṅge acyuta-jananī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---that year; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---all the wives of the devotees; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:a:acyuta]]-[[synonyms:j:jananī]]---the mother of Acyutānanda.

===== Translation =====

**That year also all the wives of the devotees [ṭhākurāṇīs] also went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Sītādevī, the mother of Acyutānanda, went with Advaita Ācārya.**
