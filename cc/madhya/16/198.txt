====== Cc. Madhya 16.198 ======

===== Text =====

> jala-dasyu-bhaye sei yavana calila
> daśa naukā bhari' bahu sainya saṅge nila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:d:dasyu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---because of fearing pirates; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:y:yavana]]---Mohammedan governor; [[synonyms:c:calila]]---went along; [[synonyms:d:daśa]] [[synonyms:n:naukā]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling ten boats; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:s:sainya]]---soldiers; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took.

===== Translation =====

**The Mohammedan governor then personally accompanied Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Because of pirates, the governor took ten boats, which were full with many soldiers.**
