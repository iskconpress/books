====== CC Madhya 16.113 ======

===== Text =====

> dui mahā-pātra,---'haricandana', 'mardarāja'
> tāṅre ājñā dila rājā---'kariha sarva kāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---two respectable officers; [[synonyms:h:haricandana]]---Haricandana; [[synonyms:m:mardarāja]]---Mardarāja; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to them; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave orders; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:k:kāya]]---everything needed.

===== Translation =====

**The King ordered two respectable officers named Haricandana and Mardarāja to do whatever was necessary to carry out these orders.**
