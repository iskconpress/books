====== CC Madhya 16.95 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera prasāda prabhu yata pāñāchila
> kaḍāra, candana, ḍora, saba saṅge laila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of food; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pāñāchila]]---had obtained; [[synonyms:k:kaḍāra]]---a kind of //tilaka//; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍora]]---ropes; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]] [[synonyms:l:laila]]---He took with Him.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord collected whatever remnants of food were left by Lord Jagannātha. He also took remnants of the Lord's kaḍāra ointment, sandalwood and ropes with Him.**
