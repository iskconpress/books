====== Cc. Madhya 16.288 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata gaura-līlā---ananta, apāra
> saṅkṣepe kahiye, kahā nā yāya vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unfathomed; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I describe; [[synonyms:k:kahā]]---describing; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---no one can do elaborately and completely.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performs His pastimes, which are unlimited and unfathomable. Somehow or other, these have briefly been described. It is not possible to describe them elaborately.**
