====== CC Madhya 16.127-129 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-saṅge purī-gosāñi, svarūpa-dāmodara
> jagadānanda, mukunda, govinda, kāśīśvara
>
> haridāsa-ṭhākura, āra paṇḍita-vakreśvara
> gopīnāthācārya, āra paṇḍita-dāmodara
>
> rāmāi, nandāi, āra bahu bhakta-gaṇa
> pradhāna kahiluṅ, sabāra ke kare gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Paṇḍita Vakreśvara; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Paṇḍita Dāmodara; [[synonyms:r:rāmāi]]---Rāmāi; [[synonyms:n:nandāi]]---Nandāi; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---many devotees; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the chief; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have mentioned; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---can make an account.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī Gosvāmī, Svarūpa Dāmodara, Jagadānanda, Mukunda, Govinda, Kāśīśvara, Haridāsa Ṭhākura, Vakreśvara Paṇḍita, Gopīnātha Ācārya, Dāmodara Paṇḍita, Rāmāi, Nandāi and many other devotees accompanied the Lord. I have mentioned only the chief devotees. No one can describe the total number.**
