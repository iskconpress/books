====== CC Madhya 16.118 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura calibāra pathe rahe sāri hañā
> sandhyāte calilā prabhu nija-gaṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:c:calibāra]] [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the route of walking; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:s:sāri]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being in a line; [[synonyms:s:sandhyāte]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:c:calilā]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord departed; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking His own men.

===== Translation =====

**All these ladies went to the road the Lord was taking and remained there in a line. That evening, the Lord departed with His devotees.**
