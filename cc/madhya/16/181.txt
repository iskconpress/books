====== CC Madhya 16.181 ======

===== Text =====

> "adhama yavana-kule kena janma haila
> vidhi more hindu-kule kena nā janmāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adhama]]---low; [[synonyms:y:yavana]]-[[synonyms:k:kule]]---in the family of a Mohammedan; [[synonyms:k:kena]]---why; [[synonyms:j:janma]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was birth; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---providence; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:h:hindu]]-[[synonyms:k:kule]]---in the family of a Hindu; [[synonyms:k:kena]]---why; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:j:janmāila]]---caused to be born.

===== Translation =====

**The governor then submissively asked, "Why was I born in a Mohammedan family? This is considered a low birth. Why didn't supreme Providence grant me a birth in a Hindu family?**
