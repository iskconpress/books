====== Cc. Madhya 16.220 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlāmbara cakravartī---ārādhya duṅhāra
> cakravartī kare duṅhāya 'bhrātṛ'-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlāmbara]] [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---the grandfather of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ārādhya]] [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---very worshipable for these two; [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāya]]---to the two of them; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛ]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---treating as brothers.

===== Translation =====

**Nīlāmbara Cakravartī, the grandfather of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, was much worshiped by the two brothers, but Nīlāmbara Cakravartī used to treat them as his own brothers.**
