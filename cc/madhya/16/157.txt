====== Cc. Madhya 16.157 ======

===== Text =====

> dina dui-cāri teṅho karila sevana
> āge calibāre sei kahe vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:c:cāri]]---two or four days; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---served the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---forward; [[synonyms:c:calibāre]]---for going; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that officer; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---spoke; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---detailed information.

===== Translation =====

**For two or four days, the government officer served the Lord. He also gave the Lord detailed information of what was ahead.**
