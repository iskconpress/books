====== CC Madhya 16.232 ======

===== Text =====

> "ājñā deha', yāñā dekhi prabhura caraṇa
> anyathā, nā rahe mora śarīre jīvana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---kindly give me permission; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I may see; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:anyathā]]---otherwise; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---will not remain; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---within the body; [[synonyms:j:jīvana]]---life.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa asked his father, "Please give me permission to go see the lotus feet of the Lord. If you do not, my life will not remain within this body."**
