====== Cc. Madhya 16.98 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda āilā pāche dolāya caḍiyā
> vāṇīnātha bahu prasāda dila pāṭhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind; [[synonyms:d:dolāya]] [[synonyms:c:caḍiyā]]---riding on a palanquin; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha Rāya; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---a large quantity of; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of the food of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāñā]]---sending.

===== Translation =====

**After Lord Caitanya reached Bhavānīpura, Rāmānanda Rāya arrived on his palanquin, and Vāṇīnātha Rāya had a large quantity of prasāda sent to the Lord.**
