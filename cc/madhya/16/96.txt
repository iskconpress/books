====== CC Madhya 16.96 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthe ājñā māgi' prabhāte calilā
> uḍiyā-bhakta-gaṇa saṅge pāche cali' āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]]---from Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---taking permission; [[synonyms:p:prabhāte]]---early in the morning; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees of Orissa; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---following; [[synonyms:c:cali']] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**After taking Lord Jagannātha's permission early in the morning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed, and all the devotees of Orissa began following Him.**
