====== CC Madhya 16.112 ======

===== Text =====

> āpani prabhuke lañā tāhāṅ uttaribā
> rātri-dibā vetra-haste sevāya rahibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally, yourself; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:u:uttaribā]]---you should go there; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dibā]]---night and day; [[synonyms:v:vetra]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with a cane in the hands; [[synonyms:s:sevāya]] [[synonyms:r:rahibā]]---should remain engaged in His service.

===== Translation =====

**"You should personally take the Lord to these newly constructed houses. Day and night you should engage in His service with a stick in your hands."**
