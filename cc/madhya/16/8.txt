====== Cc. Madhya 16.8 ======

===== Text =====

> duṅhe kahe,---ratha-yātrā kara daraśana
> kārtika āile, tabe kariha gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṅhe]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---both of them said; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the Ratha-yātrā festival; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---please see; [[synonyms:k:kārtika]] [[synonyms:ā:āile]]---when the month of Kārttika arrives; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---You can go.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya requested the Lord to observe first the Ratha-yātrā festival. Then when the month of Kārttika arrived, He could go to Vṛndāvana.**
