====== Cc. Madhya 16.270 ======

===== Text =====

> 'durlabha' 'durgama' sei 'nirjana' vṛndāvana
> ekākī yāiba, kibā saṅge eka-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:durlabha]]---very rare; [[synonyms:d:durgama]]---invincible; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nirjana]]---solitary; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---the land of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:e:ekākī]]---alone; [[synonyms:y:yāiba]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---or; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Me; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---only one person.

===== Translation =====

**"I then began to consider that Vṛndāvana is a very solitary place. It is invincible and very difficult to attain. I therefore decided to go there alone or, at the most, take only one person with Me.**
