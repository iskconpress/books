====== Cc. Madhya 16.204 ======

===== Text =====

> rāghava-paṇḍita āsi' prabhu lañā gelā
> pathe yāite loka-bhiḍe kaṣṭe-sṛṣṭye āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went to his place; [[synonyms:p:pathe]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---passing on the road; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:b:bhiḍe]]---in the crowd of men; [[synonyms:k:kaṣṭe]]-[[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭye]]---with great difficulty; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---reached.

===== Translation =====

**At length Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was taken away by Rāghava Paṇḍita. There was a great crowd assembled along the way, and the Lord reached Rāghava Paṇḍita's residence with great difficulty.**
