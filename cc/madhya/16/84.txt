====== CC Madhya 16.84 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhura cāri vatsara gela
> dakṣiṇa yāñā āsite dui vatsara lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---years; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---passed; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---after touring southern India; [[synonyms:ā:āsite]]---to come back; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---He took two years.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed four years. He spent the first two years on His tour in South India.**
