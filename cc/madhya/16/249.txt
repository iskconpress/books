====== Cc. Madhya 16.249 ======

===== Text =====

> mātāra caraṇe dhari' bahu vinaya karila
> vṛndāvana yāite tāṅra ājñā la-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---of Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---the feet; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---submitted most humbly; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---her; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---permission; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---took.

===== Translation =====

**Clasping the feet of His mother, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu very humbly requested her permission. Thus she gave Him leave to go to Vṛndāvana.**
