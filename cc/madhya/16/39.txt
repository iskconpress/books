====== Cc. Madhya 16.39 ======

===== Text =====

> dui mālā govinda dui-jane parāila
> advaita, avadhūta-gosāñi baḍa sukha pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---the two garlands; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]] [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---placed on the necks of two personalities; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:a:avadhūta]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---became very happy.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda offered the two garlands to Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu, and They both became very happy.**
