====== CC Madhya 16.126 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda, mardarāja, śrī-haricandana
> saṅge sevā kari' cale ei tina jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:m:mardarāja]]---Mardarāja; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haricandana]]---Śrī Haricandana; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in company; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---rendering service; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---went; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---these three gentlemen.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya, Mardarāja and Śrī Haricandana always went with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and rendered various services.**
