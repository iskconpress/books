====== CC Madhya 16.191 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ yāite kara tumi sahāya-prakāra
> ei baḍa ājñā, ei baḍa upakāra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go there; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---all kinds of assistance; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---this is a great order; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:u:upakāra]]---this is a great favor.

===== Translation =====

**"Please give Him all assistance so that He can go there. This is your first great order, and if you can comply, you will render a great service."**
