====== Cc. Madhya 16.183 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' mahā-pātra āviṣṭa hañā
> prabhuke karena stuti caraṇe dhariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the representative of the Orissan government; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being overwhelmed; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---makes; [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---prayers; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching His feet.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing the governor's submissive statement, the mahā-pātra was overwhelmed with joy. He clasped the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and began to offer the following prayers.**
