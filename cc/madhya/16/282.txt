====== CC Madhya 16.282 ======

===== Text =====

> ei āge āilā, prabhu, varṣāra cāri māsa
> ei cāri māsa kara nīlācale vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---just; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---have come; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:v:varṣāra]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---the four months of the rainy season; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---these four months; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---just do; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---living.

===== Translation =====

**Taking this opportunity, Gadādhara Paṇḍita said, "Just now the four months of the rainy season have begun. You should therefore spend the next four months in Jagannātha Purī.**
