====== CC Madhya 16.254 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśī-miśra, rāmānanda, pradyumna, sārvabhauma
> vāṇīnātha, śikhi-ādi yata bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]]---Pradyumna; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:ś:śikhi]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Śikhi Māhiti and others; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Kāśī Miśra, Rāmānanda Rāya, Pradyumna, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Vāṇīnātha Rāya, Śikhi Māhiti and all the other devotees met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
