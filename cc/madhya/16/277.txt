====== Cc. Madhya 16.277 ======

===== Text =====

> nirvighne ebe kaiche yāiba vṛndāvane
> sabe meli' yukti deha' hañā parasanne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirvighne]]---without obstacles; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:y:yāiba]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:m:meli']]---altogether; [[synonyms:y:yukti]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---give Me consultation; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:p:parasanne]]---being very pleased with Me.

===== Translation =====

**"Now I wish that you all will be pleased with Me and give Me good consultation. Tell Me how I shall be able to go to Vṛndāvana without impediments.**
