====== CC Madhya 16.43 ======

===== Text =====

> siṁha-dvāra-nikaṭe āilā śuni' gaurarāya
> āpane āsiyā prabhu mililā sabāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---the lion gate; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:g:gaurarāya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mililā]] [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---met them all.

===== Translation =====

**When the devotees finally reached the lion gate, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard the news and personally went to meet them.**
