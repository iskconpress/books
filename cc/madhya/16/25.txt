====== CC Madhya 16.25 ======

===== Text =====

> saba ṭhākurāṇī mahāprabhuke bhikṣā dite
> prabhura nānā priya dravya nila ghara haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---all the wives of the great devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhuke]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to offer food; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:p:priya]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---pleasing foods; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:g:ghara]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from home.

===== Translation =====

**To offer Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu various types of food, all the wives of the great devotees brought from home various things that pleased Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
