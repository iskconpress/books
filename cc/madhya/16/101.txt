====== CC Madhya 16.101 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-rāya saba-gaṇe nimantrila
> bāhira udyāne āsi' prabhu vāsā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the followers of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantrila]]---invited; [[synonyms:b:bāhira]] [[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---in an outside garden; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made His resting place.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya invited all the others for their meals, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made His resting place in a garden outside the temple.**
