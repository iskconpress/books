~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 16.14-15~~

====== CC Madhya 16.14-15 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi prabhura ājñā gauḍete rahite
> nityānanda-prabhuke prema-bhakti prakāśite
>
> tathāpi calilā mahāprabhure dekhite
> nityānandera prema-ceṣṭā ke pāre bujhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order; [[synonyms:g:gauḍete]] [[synonyms:r:rahite]]---to stay in Bengal; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---ecstatic love of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prakāśite]]---to preach; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:n:nityānandera]]---of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---the activities of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is able; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand.

===== Translation =====

**Although the Lord told Nityānanda Prabhu to stay in Bengal and spread ecstatic love of God, Nityānanda left to go see Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Who can understand Nityānanda Prabhu's ecstatic love?**
