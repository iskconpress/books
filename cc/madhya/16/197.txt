====== Cc. Madhya 16.197 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-pātre mahāprabhu karilā vidāya
> kāndite kāndite sei tīre rahi' cāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātre]]---unto the //mahā-pātra//; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:vidāya]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:k:kāndite]] [[synonyms:k:kāndite]]---crying and crying; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that //mahā-pātra//; [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank; [[synonyms:r:rahi']] [[synonyms:c:cāya]]---stood and watched.

===== Translation =====

**Finally Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade farewell to the mahā-pātra. Standing on the river bank and looking at the boat, the mahā-pātra began to cry.**
