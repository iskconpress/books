====== CC Madhya 16.289 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra-vadane kahe āpane 'ananta'
> tabu eka līlāra teṅho nāhi pāya anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadane]]---in thousands of mouths; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---Anantadeva; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of one pastime only; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He (Anantadeva); [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---the end.

===== Translation =====

**Although Lord Anantadeva is always describing the pastimes of the Lord with His thousands of mouths, He cannot reach the end of even one of the Lord's pastimes.**
