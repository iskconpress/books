====== CC Madhya 16.10 ======

===== Text =====

> āji-kāli kari' uṭhāya vividha upāya
> yāite sammati nā deya vicchedera bhaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]]-[[synonyms:k:kāli]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---delaying today and tomorrow; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāya]]---they put forward; [[synonyms:v:vividha]] [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---many devices; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go; [[synonyms:s:sammati]]---permission; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:deya]]---did not give; [[synonyms:v:vicchedera]] [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---because of fearing separation.

===== Translation =====

**In this way they both presented many impediments, indirectly not granting the Lord permission to go to Vṛndāvana. They did this because they were afraid of separation from Him.**
