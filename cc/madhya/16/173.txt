====== Cc. Madhya 16.173 ======

===== Text =====

> bahuta utkaṇṭhā tāṅra, karyāche vinaya
> tomā-sane ei sandhi, nāhi yuddha-bhaya'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---very much; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---anxiety; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:k:karyāche]]---has made; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---submissive petition; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with you; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sandhi]]---a peace proposal; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:y:yuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear of fighting.

===== Translation =====

**"The Mohammedan governor is very eager, and he has submitted this petition with great respect. It is a proposal for peace. You need not fear that we will fight."**
