====== CC Madhya 16.77 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-sahita tāṅra haya sakhya-prīti
> dui-janāya kṛṣṇa-kathāya ekatra-i sthiti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]-[[synonyms:p:prīti]]---very intimate friendship; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:janāya]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathāya]]---in topics of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---on the same level; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---position.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Puṇḍarīka Vidyānidhi had a friendly, intimate relationship, and as far as discussing topics about Kṛṣṇa, they were situated on the same platform.**
