====== CC Madhya 16.196 ======

===== Text =====

> eka navīna naukā, tāra madhye ghara
> svagaṇe caḍāilā prabhu tāhāra upara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:n:navīna]]---new; [[synonyms:n:naukā]]---boat; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---a room; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---with His associates; [[synonyms:c:caḍāilā]]---put on board; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]] [[synonyms:u:upara]]---on it.

===== Translation =====

**One of the boats had been newly constructed, and it had a room in the middle. It was on this boat that they put Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His associates.**
