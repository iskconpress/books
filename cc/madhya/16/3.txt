====== CC Madhya 16.3 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ha-ila icchā yāite vṛndāvana
> śuniyā pratāparudra ha-ilā vimana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---the desire; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---Mahārāja Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:v:vimana]]---became morose.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu decided to go to Vṛndāvana, and Mahārāja Pratāparudra became very morose upon hearing this news.**
