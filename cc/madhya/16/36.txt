====== CC Madhya 16.36 ======

===== Text =====

> sākṣi-gopālera kathā kahe nityānanda
> śuniyā vaiṣṇava-mane bāḍila ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sākṣi]]-[[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of the Deity known as Sākṣi-gopāla; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the narration; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---describes; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the minds of all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---the transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**When Nityānanda Prabhu described all the activities of Sākṣi-gopāla, transcendental bliss increased in the minds of all the Vaiṣṇavas.**


For these activities refer to //Madhya-līlā,// Chapter Five, verses 8-138.
