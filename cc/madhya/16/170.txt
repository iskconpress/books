====== CC Madhya 16.170 ======

===== Text =====

> 'viśvāsa' āsiyā prabhura caraṇa vandila
> 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa' kahi' preme vihvala ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---the secretary; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:v:vandila]]---worshiped; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the holy name of the Lord, Kṛṣṇa, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---uttering; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**The Mohammedan secretary came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. When he offered his respects to the Lord's lotus feet and uttered the holy name of the Lord, "Kṛṣṇa, Kṛṣṇa," he also was overwhelmed with ecstatic love.**
