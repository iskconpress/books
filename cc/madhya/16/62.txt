====== CC Madhya 16.62 ======

===== Text =====

> kibā prārthanā, kibā ājñā---keha nā bujhila
> āliṅgana kari' prabhu tāṅre vidāya dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kibā]]---what; [[synonyms:p:prārthanā]]---the request; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---what; [[synonyms:ā:ājnā]]---the order; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:bujhila]]---did not know; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to Him; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---bade farewell.

===== Translation =====

**No one knew what Advaita Ācārya requested or what the Lord ordered. After embracing the Ācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade Him farewell.**
