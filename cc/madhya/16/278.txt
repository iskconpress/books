====== CC Madhya 16.278 ======

===== Text =====

> gadādhare chāḍi' genu, iṅho duḥkha pāila
> sei hetu vṛndāvana yāite nārila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gadādhare]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving aside Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:g:genu]]---I went; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---became unhappy; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for that reason; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana-dhāma; [[synonyms:y:yāite]] [[synonyms:n:nārila]]---I was unable to go.

===== Translation =====

**"I left Gadādhara Paṇḍita here, and he became very unhappy. For this reason I could not go to Vṛndāvana."**
