====== Cc. Madhya 16.66 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda kahe,---āmi 'deha' tumi 'prāṇa'
> 'deha' 'prāṇa' bhinna nahe,---ei ta pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu said; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---the body; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---the life; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---the body; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---the life; [[synonyms:b:bhinna]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not separate; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta]] [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---this is the evidence.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu replied, "O Lord, You are the life, and I am the body. There is no difference between the body and life itself, but life is more important than the body.**
