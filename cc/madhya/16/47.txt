====== CC Madhya 16.47 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata bhakta-gaṇa rahilā cāri māsa
> prabhura sahita kare kīrtana-vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---for four months; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---the pastimes of //saṅkīrtana.//

===== Translation =====

**For four continuous months all the devotees remained there and enjoyed chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
