====== CC Madhya 16.178 ======

===== Text =====

> 'viśvāsa' yāñā tāṅhāre sakala kahila
> hindu-veśa dhari' sei yavana āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---the secretary; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---returning; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---unto the Mohammedan governor; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---told everything; [[synonyms:h:hindu]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---accepting the dress of a Hindu; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:y:yavana]]---that Mohammedan governor; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**The secretary returned to the Mohammedan governor and informed him of this news. Dressing himself like a Hindu, the Mohammedan governor then came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
