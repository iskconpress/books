====== CC Madhya 16.54 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat ratha-yātrā kaila daraśana
> herā-pañcamī-yātrā dekhe lañā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as in the previous year; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the car festival; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---saw; [[synonyms:h:herā]]-[[synonyms:p:pañcamī]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the festival of Herā-pañcamī; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---He sees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---with all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**As in the previous year, the Lord, with all the devotees, saw the Ratha-yātrā festival and the Herā-pañcamī festival as well.**
