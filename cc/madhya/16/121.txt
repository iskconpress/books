====== Cc. Madhya 16.121 ======

===== Text =====

> emana kṛpālu nāhi śuni tribhuvane
> kṛṣṇa-premā haya yāṅra dūra daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:emana]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpālu]]---such a merciful person; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---we hear; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---within the three worlds; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:premā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---one gets love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---by seeing from a distance.

===== Translation =====

**There is no one as merciful as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu within all three worlds. Simply by seeing Him from a distance, one is overwhelmed with love of Godhead.**
