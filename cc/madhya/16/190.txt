====== CC Madhya 16.190 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mukunda datta kahe,---'śuna, mahāśaya
> gaṅgā-tīra yāite mahāprabhura mana haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]] [[synonyms:d:datta]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Mukunda Datta, a devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, said; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]] [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---my dear sir, kindly hear me; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind or desire; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is.

===== Translation =====

**Mukunda Datta then told the Mohammedan governor, "My dear sir, please hear. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wishes to go to the bank of the Ganges.**
