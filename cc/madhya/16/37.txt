====== CC Madhya 16.37 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhuke milite sabāra utkaṇṭhā antare
> śīghra kari' āilā sabe śrī-nīlācale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhuke]] [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---anxiety; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making great haste; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---reached; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone in the party was very anxious at heart to see Caitanya Mahāprabhu; therefore they hastily went on to Jagannātha Purī.**
