====== Cc. Madhya 16.27 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakṣya diyā karena sabāra sarvatra pālane
> parama ānande yāna prabhura daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---supplying food; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---he does; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:p:pālane]]---maintenance; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:y:yāna]]---he goes; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena also supplied food to all the devotees and took care of them along the way. In this way, feeling great happiness he went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Jagannātha Purī.**
