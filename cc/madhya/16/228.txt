====== CC Madhya 16.228 ======

===== Text =====

> bāra bāra palāya teṅho nīlādri yāite
> pitā tāṅre bāndhi' rākhe āni' patha haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:p:palāya]]---leaves home; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:n:nīlādri]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---his father; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:b:bāndhi']]---binding; [[synonyms:r:rākhe]]---keeps; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing back; [[synonyms:p:patha]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the road.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa used to run away from home again and again to go to Jagannātha Purī, but his father kept binding him and bringing him back.**
