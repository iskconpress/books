====== Cc. Madhya 16.18 ======

===== Text =====

> khaṇḍa-vāsī narahari, śrī-raghunandana
> sarva-bhakta cale, tāra ke kare gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]] [[synonyms:n:narahari]]---Narahari, a resident of the village Khaṇḍa; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raghunandana]]---Śrī Raghunandana; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---went; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---can count.

===== Translation =====

**Narahari and Śrī Raghunandana, who were from the village of Khaṇḍa, and many other devotees also departed. Who can count them?**
