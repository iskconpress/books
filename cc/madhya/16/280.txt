====== CC Madhya 16.280 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi yāhāṅ-yāhāṅ raha, tāhāṅ 'vṛndāvana'
> tāhāṅ yamunā, gaṅgā, sarva-tīrtha-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]-[[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---stay; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---that place is Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]---the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]---the River Gaṅgā; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all other holy places of pilgrimage.

===== Translation =====

**Gadādhara Paṇḍita said, "Wherever You stay is Vṛndāvana, as well as the River Yamunā, the River Ganges and all other places of pilgrimage.**
