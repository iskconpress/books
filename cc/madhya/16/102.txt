====== Cc. Madhya 16.102 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā kari' bakula-tale karilā viśrāma
> pratāparudra-ṭhāñi rāya karila payāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after taking lunch; [[synonyms:b:bakula]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---underneath a //bakula// flower tree; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---took rest; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to the presence of Mahārāja Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:p:payāna]]---departed.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was taking rest beneath a bakula tree, Rāmānanda Rāya immediately went to Mahārāja Pratāparudra.**
