====== CC Madhya 16.223 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsa kari' prabhu yabe śāntipura āilā
> tabe āsi' raghunātha prabhure mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after accepting the //sannyāsa// order; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ś:śāntipura]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went to Śāntipura; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Śāntipura after accepting the renounced order, Raghunātha dāsa met Him.**
