~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 16.252~~

====== CC Madhya 16.252 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu āsi' jagannātha daraśana kaila
> 'mahāprabhu āilā'---grāme kolāhala haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---returning; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---to Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visit; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has come back; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the town; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was great agitation.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived in Jagannātha Purī, He visited the temple of the Lord. News then spread all over the city that He had returned.**
