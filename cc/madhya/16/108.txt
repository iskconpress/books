====== CC Madhya 16.108 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche tāṅhāre kṛpā kaila gaurarāya
> "pratāparudra-santrātā" nāma haila yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---showed; [[synonyms:g:gaurarāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]-[[synonyms:s:santrātā]]---the deliverer of Mahārāja Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---by which.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed such mercy to the King that from that day on the Lord became known as Pratāparudra-santrātā, the deliverer of Mahārāja Pratāparudra.**
