====== Cc. Madhya 16.156 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe 'oḍhra-deśa-sīmā' prabhu cali' āilā
> tathā rāja-adhikārī prabhure mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:o:oḍhra]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the boundary of Orissa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---traveling; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---reached; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---a government officer; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu finally arrived at the border of the state of Orissa, a government officer came there to meet Him.**
