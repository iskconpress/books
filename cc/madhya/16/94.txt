====== Cc. Madhya 16.94 ======

===== Text =====

> ānande mahāprabhu varṣā kaila samādhāna
> vijayā-daśamī-dine karila payāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:varṣā]]---the rainy season; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---passed; [[synonyms:v:vijayā]]-[[synonyms:d:daśamī]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---on Vijayā-daśamī, the day when the victory was won by Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:p:payāna]]---He departed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very pleased to thus receive their permission. He waited until the rainy season passed, and when the day of Vijayā-daśamī arrived, He departed for Vṛndāvana.**
