====== Cc. Madhya 16.24 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryaratna-saṅge cale tāṅhāra gṛhiṇī
> tāṅhāra premera kathā kahite nā jāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryaratna]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:g:gṛhiṇī]]---wife; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the description of the ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I do not know how.

===== Translation =====

**The wife of Candraśekhara [Ācāryaratna] also went. I cannot speak of the greatness of Candraśekhara's love for the Lord.**
