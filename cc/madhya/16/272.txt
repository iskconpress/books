====== CC Madhya 16.272 ======

===== Text =====

> bādiyāra bāji pāti' calilāṅa tathāre
> bahu-saṅge vṛndāvana gamana nā kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bādiyāra]]---of a gypsy; [[synonyms:b:bāji]]---the magic; [[synonyms:p:pāti']]---demonstrating; [[synonyms:c:calilāṅa]]---I went; [[synonyms:t:tathāre]]---there; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with many men; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana dhāma; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---no one does.

===== Translation =====

**"I then understood that I was going to Vṛndāvana like a magician with his show, and this is certainly not good. No one should go to Vṛndāvana with so many men.**
