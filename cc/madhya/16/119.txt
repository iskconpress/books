====== Cc. Madhya 16.119 ======

===== Text =====

> 'citrotpalā-nadī' āsi' ghāṭe kaila snāna
> mahiṣī-sakala dekhi' karaye praṇāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:citrotpalā]]-[[synonyms:n:nadī]]---to the river named Citrotpalā; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭe]]---on the bank; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---took a bath; [[synonyms:m:mahiṣī]]-[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all the queens and ladies of the palace; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---offered their obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the bank of the River Citrotpalā to take His bath, all the queens and ladies of the palace offered their obeisances to Him.**
