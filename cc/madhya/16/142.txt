====== Cc. Madhya 16.142 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' mahāprabhu naukāte caḍilā
> mūrcchita hañā paṇḍita tathāi paḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:naukāte]] [[synonyms:c:caḍilā]]---got on a boat; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---fainting; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tathāi]]---there; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got into a boat, and Gadādhara Paṇḍita immediately fell down in an unconscious state.**
