====== CC Madhya 16.146 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata prabhu tomāra viccheda sahiyā
> tomāra pratijñā rakṣā kaila yatna kariyā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of you; [[synonyms:v:viccheda]] [[synonyms:s:sahiyā]]---tolerating the separation; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:pratijñā]]---your vow; [[synonyms:r:rakṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---protected; [[synonyms:y:yatna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great endeavor.

===== Translation =====

**"Similarly, tolerating your separation, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has protected your vow with great endeavor."**
