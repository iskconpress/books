====== CC Madhya 16.195 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-pātra cali' āilā mahāprabhura sane
> mleccha āsi' kaila prabhura caraṇa vandane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the Orissan government representative; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---moving; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]---the governor of the other side; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandane]]---worshiping the lotus feet of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**The mahā-pātra crossed the river with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and when they reached the other shore, the Mohammedan governor personally received the Lord and worshiped His lotus feet.**
