====== CC Madhya 16.23 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānandera bālaka, nāma---caitanya-dāsa
> teṅho caliyāche prabhure dekhite ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]] [[synonyms:b:bālaka]]---the son of Śivānanda; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Caitanya dāsa; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:c:caliyāche]]---was going; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---jubilantly.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya dāsa, the son of Śivānanda Sena, also jubilantly accompanied them as they went to see the Lord.**
