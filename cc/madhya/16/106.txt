====== CC Madhya 16.106 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ stuti kari' rājā karaye praṇāma
> prabhu-kṛpā-aśrute tāṅra deha haila snāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:s:stuti]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering prayers; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---of the mercy of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:aśrute]]---by the tears; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---the body; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathed.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord embraced the King, the King again and again offered prayers and obeisances. In this way, the Lord's mercy brought tears from the King, and the Lord's body was bathed with these tears.**
