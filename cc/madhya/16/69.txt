====== CC Madhya 16.69 ======

===== Text =====

> kulīna-grāmī pūrvavat kaila nivedana
> "prabhu, ājñā kara,---āmāra kartavya sādhana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmī]]---one of the residents of Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like last year; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---submitted; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---petition; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---order; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:k:kartavya]]---necessary; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---performance.

===== Translation =====

**As in the previous year, one of the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma submitted a petition to the Lord, saying, "My Lord, kindly tell me what my duty is and how I should execute it."**
