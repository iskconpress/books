====== Cc. Madhya 16.202 ======

===== Text =====

> sei naukā caḍi' prabhu āilā 'pānihāṭi'
> nāvikere parāila nija-kṛpā-sāṭī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:naukā]] [[synonyms:c:caḍi']]---boarding the same boat; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---reached; [[synonyms:p:pānihāṭi]]---the place named Pānihāṭi; [[synonyms:n:nāvikere]]---the captain of the boat; [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---He put on; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:s:sāṭī]]---His own used cloth as special mercy.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord finally reached Pānihāṭi, and, as an act of mercy, He gave the captain of the boat one of His personal garments.**
