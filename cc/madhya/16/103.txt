====== Cc. Madhya 16.103 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' ānandita rājā ati-śīghra āilā
> prabhu dekhi' daṇḍavat bhūmete padilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---hastily; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---obeisances offered falling flat; [[synonyms:b:bhūmete]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down. obeisances offered falling flat; [[synonyms:b:bhūmete]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down.

===== Translation =====

**The King was very happy to hear the news, and he hastily went there. Upon seeing the Lord, he fell flat to offer Him obeisances.**
