====== CC Madhya 16.133 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"sevā chāḍibe, āmāya lāge doṣa
> iṅhā rahi' sevā kara,---āmāra santoṣa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍibe]]---you will give up the service; [[synonyms:ā:āmāya]]---to Me; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---will attach; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault; [[synonyms:i:iṅhā]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying here; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---just be engaged in service; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "If you abandon His service, it will be My fault. It is better that you remain here and render service. That will be My satisfaction."**
