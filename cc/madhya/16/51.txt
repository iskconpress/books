====== Cc. Madhya 16.51 ======

===== Text =====

> rāḍhī eka vipra, teṅho---nityānanda dāsa
> mahā-bhāgyavān teṅho, nāma---kṛṣṇadāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāḍhī]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---one //brāhmaṇa// resident of Rāḍha-deśa (where the Ganges does not flow); [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servant of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---greatly fortunate; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---Kṛṣṇadāsa.

===== Translation =====

**One brāhmaṇa named Kṛṣṇadāsa, who was a resident of Rāḍha-deśa and a servant of Lord Nityānanda's, was a very fortunate person.**
