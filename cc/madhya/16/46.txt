====== Cc. Madhya 16.46 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva vatsare yāṅra yei vāsā-sthāna
> tāhāṅ sabā pāṭhāñā karāila viśrāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]] [[synonyms:v:vatsare]]---in the previous year; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---wherever; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---place to stay; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāñā]]---sending; [[synonyms:k:karāila]] [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---made them rest.

===== Translation =====

**In the previous year, everyone had his own particular residence, and the same residences were again offered. Thus they all went to take rest.**
