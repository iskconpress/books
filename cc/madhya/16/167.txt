====== CC Madhya 16.167 ======

===== Text =====

> kahibāra kathā nahe---dekhile se jāni
> tāṅhāra prabhāve tāṅre 'īśvara' kari' māni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kahibāra]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---describable topic; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---this is not; [[synonyms:d:dekhile]]---if one sees; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---he can understand; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:p:prabhāve]]---by His influence; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---as the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I accept.

===== Translation =====

**"Actually these things cannot even be described. One can understand them only by seeing. By His influence, I accept Him as the Supreme Personality of Godhead."**
