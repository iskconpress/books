====== CC Madhya 16.104 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ uṭhe, punaḥ paḍe praṇaya-vihvala
> stuti kare, pulakāṅga, paḍe aśru-jala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---he arose; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---he fell down; [[synonyms:p:praṇaya]]-[[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed with love; [[synonyms:s:stuti]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offers prayers; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the entire body quivering in joy; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell down; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---tears.

===== Translation =====

**Being overwhelmed with love, the King again and again got up and fell down. When he offered prayers, his whole body shivered, and tears fell from his eyes.**
