====== Cc. Madhya 16.174 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' mahā-pātra kahe hañā vismaya
> 'madyapa yavanera citta aiche ke karaya!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the representative of the Orissan government; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---becoming astonished; [[synonyms:m:madyapa]]---drunkard; [[synonyms:y:yavanera]]---of the Mohammedan; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---the heart; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:k:karaya]]---who has made.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this proposal, the representative of the Orissan government, the mahā-pātra, was very astonished. He thought, "The Mohammedan governor is a drunkard. Who has changed his mind?**
