====== Cc. Madhya 16.44 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā lañā kaila jagannātha-daraśana
> sabā lañā āilā punaḥ āpana-bhavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking them all; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went back; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---to His own residence.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all His devotees visited Lord Jagannātha. Finally, accompanied by them all, He returned to His own residence.**
