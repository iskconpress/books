====== CC Madhya 16.248 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ haite avaśya āmi 'vṛndāvana' yāba
> sabe ājñā deha', tabe nirvighne āsiba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from there; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---shall go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---give Me permission; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:n:nirvighne]]---without disturbance; [[synonyms:ā:āsiba]]---I shall come back.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I shall certainly go to Vṛndāvana from Jagannātha Purī. If all of you give Me permission, I shall return here again without difficulty."**
