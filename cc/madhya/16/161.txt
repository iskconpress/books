====== CC Madhya 16.161 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kāle se yavanera eka anucara
> 'uḍiyā-kaṭake' āila kari' veśāntara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:y:yavanera]]---of the Mohammedan governor; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:a:anucara]]---one follower; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]-[[synonyms:k:kaṭake]]---to the camp of Orissan soldiers; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:v:veśa]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]]---changing the dress.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, a follower of the Mohammedan governor arrived at the Orissa encampment dressed in disguise.**
