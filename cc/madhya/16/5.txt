====== Cc. Madhya 16.5 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlādri chāḍi' prabhura mana anyatra yāite
> tomarā karaha yatna tāṅhāre rākhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlādri]]---Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:a:anyatra]]---elsewhere; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go; [[synonyms:t:tomarā]]---both of you; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---make; [[synonyms:y:yatna]]---endeavor; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---Him; [[synonyms:r:rākhite]]---to keep.

===== Translation =====

**Pratāparudra Mahārāja said, "Please endeavor to keep Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu here at Jagannātha purī, for now He is thinking of going elsewhere.**
