====== Cc. Madhya 16.85 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dui vatsara cāhe vṛndāvana yāite
> rāmānanda-haṭhe prabhu nā pāre calite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---another two years; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---He wanted; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:h:haṭhe]]---by the tricks of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---was not able; [[synonyms:c:calite]]---to go.

===== Translation =====

**The other two years, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wanted to go to Vṛndāvana, but He could not leave Jagannātha Purī because of Rāmānanda Rāya's tricks.**
