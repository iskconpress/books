====== CC Madhya 16.171 ======

===== Text =====

> dhairya hañā uḍiyāke kahe namaskari'
> 'tomā-sthāne pāṭhāilā mleccha adhikārī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhairya]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming calm; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyāke]]---to the representative of the Orissan government; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering respect; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to your place; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---has sent; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]---the Mohammedan; [[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---governor.

===== Translation =====

**After calming down, the Mohammedan secretary offered his respects and informed the representative of the Orissan government, "The Mohammedan governor has sent me here.**
