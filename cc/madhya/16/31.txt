====== CC Madhya 16.31 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣīra bāṅṭi' sabāre dila prabhu-nityānanda
> kṣīra-prasāda pāñā sabāra bāḍila ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---the condensed milk; [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭi']]---dividing; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of condensed milk from the Deity; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---the transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**When the condensed milk was placed before Nityānanda Prabhu, He distributed the prasāda to everyone, and thus everyone's transcendental bliss increased.**
