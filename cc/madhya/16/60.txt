====== CC Madhya 16.60 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya-gosāñi prabhuke kahe ṭhāre-ṭhore
> ācārya tarjā paḍe, keha bujhite nā pāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāre]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhore]]---by indications; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tarjā]] [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---reads some poetic passages; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---was not able.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrīla Advaita Ācārya said something to Caitanya Mahāprabhu through gestures and read some poetic passages, which no one understood.**
