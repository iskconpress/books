====== CC Madhya 16.100 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kaṭake' āsiyā kaila 'gopāla' daraśana
> svapneśvara-vipra kaila prabhura nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṭake]]---to the city of Kaṭaka; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:s:svapneśvara]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the brāhmaṇa named Svapneśvara; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**After reaching the city of Kaṭaka, He saw the temple of Gopāla, and a brāhmaṇa there named Svapneśvara invited the Lord to eat.**
