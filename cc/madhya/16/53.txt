====== CC Madhya 16.53 ======

===== Text =====

> balagaṇḍi-bhogera bahu prasāda āila
> sabā saṅge mahāprabhu prasāda khāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:balagaṇḍi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhogera]]---of the food offered at Balagaṇḍi; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---a great quantity of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---arrived; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of the food of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate.

===== Translation =====

**The remnants of food offered to the Lord at Balagaṇḍi then arrived in great quantity, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all His devotees ate it.**

===== Purport =====

In reference to Balagaṇḍi, see //Madhya-līlā// (13.193).
