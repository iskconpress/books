====== CC Madhya 16.87 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu sārvabhauma-rāmānanda-sthāne
> āliṅgana kari' kahe madhura vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---before Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:m:madhura]] [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---sweet words.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu placed a proposal before Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya. He embraced them and spoke sweet words.**
