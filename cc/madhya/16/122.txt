====== Cc. Madhya 16.122 ======

===== Text =====

> naukāte caḍiyā prabhu haila nadī pāra
> jyotsnāvatī rātrye cali' āilā caturdvāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:naukāte]] [[synonyms:c:caḍiyā]]---getting on the boat; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:n:nadī]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---across the river; [[synonyms:j:jyotsnāvatī]]---lighted by the full moon; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---in the night; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---walking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:c:caturdvāra]]---to Caturdvāra.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then got into a new boat and crossed the river. Walking in the full moonlight, He finally reached the town known as Caturdvāra.**
