====== Cc. Madhya 16.20 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāra sarva-kārya karena, dena vāsā-sthāna
> śivānanda jāne uḍiyā-pathera sandhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kārya]]---everything necessary to be done; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---he does; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---residential places; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]-[[synonyms:p:pathera]]---of the roads in Orissa; [[synonyms:s:sandhāna]]---the junctions.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena took care of all the necessities the devotees required. In particular, he made arrangements for residential quarters, and he knew the roads of Orissa.**
