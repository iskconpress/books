====== CC Madhya 16.182 ======

===== Text =====

> 'hindu' haile pāitāma tomāra caraṇa-sannidhāna
> vyartha mora ei deha, yāuka parāṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hinduhaile]]---if I was born in a Hindu family; [[synonyms:p:pāitāma]]---I would have gotten; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:sannidhāna]]---proximity; [[synonyms:v:vyartha]]---useless; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---body; [[synonyms:y:yāuka]] [[synonyms:p:parāṇa]]---let me die immediately.

===== Translation =====

**"If I had taken birth in a Hindu family, it would have been easy for me to remain near Your lotus feet. Since my body is now useless, let me die immediately."**
