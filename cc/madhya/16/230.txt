====== Cc. Madhya 16.230 ======

===== Text =====

> ekādaśa jana tāṅre rākhe nirantara
> nīlācale yāite nā pāya, duḥkhita antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekādaśa]]---eleven; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---persons; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:r:rākhe]]---keep; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---day and night; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---was not able; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhita]] [[synonyms:a:antara]]---very unhappy within the mind.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, eleven people were incessantly keeping Raghunātha dāsa under control. Thus he could not go to Jagannātha Purī, and because of this he was very unhappy.**
