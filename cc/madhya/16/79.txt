====== Cc. Madhya 16.79 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha parena tathā 'māḍuyā' vasana
> dekhiyā saghṛṇa haila vidyānidhira mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:parena]]---puts on; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:m:māḍuyā]] [[synonyms:v:vasana]]---cloth with starch; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:ghṛṇa]]---with hatred; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:v:vidyānidhira]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Vidyānidhi.

===== Translation =====

**When Puṇḍarīka Vidyānidhi saw that Lord Jagannātha was given a starched garment, he became a little hateful. In this way his mind was polluted.**
