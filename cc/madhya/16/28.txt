====== CC Madhya 16.28 ======

===== Text =====

> remuṇāya āsiyā kaila gopīnātha daraśana
> ācārya karila tāhāṅ kīrtana, nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:remuṇāya]]---to Remuṇā; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---after coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting the temple of Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---performed; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**When they all arrived at Remuṇā, they went to see Lord Gopīnātha. In the temple there, Advaita Ācārya danced and chanted.**
