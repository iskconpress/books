====== CC Madhya 16.13 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe meli' gelā advaita ācāryera pāśe
> prabhu dekhite ācārya calilā ullāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone; [[synonyms:m:meli']]---after assembling together; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of the leader of Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---in the presence; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:u:ullāse]]---in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**All the Bengali devotees gathered around Advaita Ācārya, and in great jubilation Advaita Ācārya departed to Jagannātha Purī to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
