====== CC Madhya 16.19 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda-sena kare ghāṭi samādhāna
> sabāre pālana kari' sukhe lañā yāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---made; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭi]] [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---arrangements for expenditures to clear the tax collecting centers; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---everyone; [[synonyms:p:pālana]]---maintaining; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:y:yāna]]---goes.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena, who was in charge of the party, made arrangements to clear the tax collecting centers. He took care of all the devotees and happily traveled with them.**
