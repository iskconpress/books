====== CC Madhya 16.193 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-pātra tāṅra sane kaila kolākuli
> aneka sāmagrī diyā karila mitāli

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the Orissan representative; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kolākuli]]---embracing; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---various; [[synonyms:s:sāmagrī]]---materials; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving as gift; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:m:mitāli]]---established friendship.

===== Translation =====

**Before the governor left, the mahā-pātra embraced him and offered him many material gifts. He thus established a friendship with him.**
