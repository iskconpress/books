====== CC Madhya 16.287 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣāte paṇḍitera sneha, prabhura āsvādana
> manuṣyera śaktye dui nā yāya varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣāte]]---in feeding; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]---the affection; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting; [[synonyms:m:manuṣyera]]---of an ordinary human being; [[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---in the power; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---these two; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---not possible; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---the description.

===== Translation =====

**No ordinary human being can possibly describe Gadādhara Paṇḍita's affectionate presentation of food and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's tasting this food.**
