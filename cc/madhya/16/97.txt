====== CC Madhya 16.97 ======

===== Text =====

> uḍiyā-bhakta-gaṇe prabhu yatne nivārilā
> nija-gaṇa-saṅge prabhu 'bhavānīpura, āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the devotees of Orissa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yatne]]---with great care; [[synonyms:n:nivārilā]]---stopped; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with His personal associates; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhavānīpura]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Bhavānīpura.

===== Translation =====

**It was with great care that Caitanya Mahāprabhu forbade the Orissa devotees to follow Him. Accompanied by personal associates, He first went to Bhavānīpura.**

===== Purport =====

One goes through Bhavānīpura before reaching a well-known place named Jānkādei-pura, or Jānakīdevī-pura.
