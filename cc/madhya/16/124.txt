====== CC Madhya 16.124 ======

===== Text =====

> rājāra ājñāya paḍichā pāṭhāya dine-dine
> bahuta prasāda pāṭhāya diyā bahu-jane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājāra]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---by the order of the King; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---the superintendent of the temple; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāya]]---sent; [[synonyms:d:dine]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day after day; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---a large quantity of food; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāya]]---he sent; [[synonyms:d:diyā]] [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---carried by many persons.

===== Translation =====

**Following the King's orders, the superintendent of the temple sent large quantities of prasāda every day, and it was carried by many persons.**
