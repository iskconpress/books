====== CC Madhya 16.41 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ mālā diyā svarūpādi nija-gaṇa
> āgu bāḍi' pāṭhāila śacīra nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garlands; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and others; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---personal associates; [[synonyms:ā:āgu]] [[synonyms:b:bāḍi']]---going forward; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī.

===== Translation =====

**Then, for the second time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sent garlands through Svarūpa Dāmodara and other personal associates. Thus they went forward, sent by the son of mother Śacī.**
