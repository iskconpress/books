====== CC Madhya 16.235 ======

===== Text =====

> 'rakṣakera hāte muñi kemane chuṭiba!
> kemane prabhura saṅge nīlācale yāba?'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rakṣakera]] [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---from the clutches of the watchmen; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kemane]]---how; [[synonyms:c:chuṭiba]]---shall get release; [[synonyms:k:kemane]]---how; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---I shall go.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa thought, "How shall I be able to get free from the hands of the watchmen? How shall I be able to go with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to Nīlācala?"**
