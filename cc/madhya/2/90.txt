====== Cc. Madhya 2.90 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi vṛddha jarātura, likhite kāṅpaye kara,
> mane kichu smaraṇa nā haya
> nā dekhiye nayane, nā śuniye śravaṇe,
> tabu likhi'---e baḍa vismaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:v:vṛddha]]---old man; [[synonyms:j:jarā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātura]]---disturbed by invalidity; [[synonyms:l:likhite]]---to write; [[synonyms:k:kāṅpaye]]---trembles; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---the hand; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]---remembrance; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is not; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiye]]---I cannot see; [[synonyms:n:nayane]]---by the eyes; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuniye]]---I cannot hear; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---with the ears; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:l:likhi']]---writing; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---a great wonder.

===== Translation =====

**I have now become too old and disturbed by invalidity. While I write, my hands tremble. I cannot remember anything, nor can I see or hear properly. Still I write, and this is a great wonder.**
