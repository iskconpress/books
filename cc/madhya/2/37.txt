====== CC Madhya 2.37 ======

===== Text =====

> ye kāle vā svapane, dekhinu vaṁśī-vadane,
> sei kāle āilā dui vairi
> 'ānanda' āra 'madana', hari' nila mora mana,
> dekhite nā pāinu netra bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time; [[synonyms:v:vā]] [[synonyms:s:svapane]]---or in dreams; [[synonyms:d:dekhinu]]---I saw; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]-[[synonyms:v:vadane]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa's face with His flute; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---appeared; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:v:vairi]]---enemies; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:m:madana]]---Cupid; [[synonyms:h:hari']]---stealing; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I was able; [[synonyms:n:netra]]---eyes; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---fulfilling.

===== Translation =====

**"Whenever I had the chance to see Lord Kṛṣṇa's face and His flute, even in a dream, two enemies would appear before Me. They were pleasure and Cupid, and since they took away My mind, I was not able to see the face of Kṛṣṇa to the full satisfaction of My eyes.**
