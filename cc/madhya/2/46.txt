====== CC Madhya 2.46 ======

===== Text =====

> dūre śuddha-prema-gandha, kapaṭa premera bandha,
> seha mora nāhi kṛṣṇa-pāya
> tabe ye kari krandana, sva-saubhāgya prakhyāpana,
> kari, ihā jāniha niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dūre]]---far away; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:g:gandha]]---a scent of pure devotional love; [[synonyms:k:kapaṭa]]---false; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bandha]]---binding; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---but; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:s:saubhāgya]]---My own fortune; [[synonyms:p:prakhyāpana]]---demonstration; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jāniha]]---know; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---certainly.

===== Translation =====

**"Actually, My love for Kṛṣṇa is far, far away. Whatever I do is actually false. When you see Me cry, I am simply exhibiting My great fortune. Please try to understand this beyond a doubt.**
