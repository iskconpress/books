====== CC Madhya 2.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śeṣa ye rahila prabhura dvādaśa vatsara
> kṛṣṇera viyoga-sphūrti haya nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---at the end; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---those; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśa]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---twelve years; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viyoga]]---of separation; [[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---manifestation; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always.

===== Translation =====

**During His last twelve years, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu always manifested all the symptoms of ecstasy in separation from Kṛṣṇa.**
