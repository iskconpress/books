====== Cc. Madhya 2.40 ======

===== Text =====

> śuna mora prāṇera bāndhava
> nāhi kṛṣṇa-prema-dhana, daridra mora jīvana,
> dehendriya vṛthā mora saba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---kindly hear; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:p:prāṇera]]---of life; [[synonyms:b:bāndhava]]---friends; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is none; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---wealth of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:daridra]]---poverty-stricken; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:j:jīvana]]---life; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]---all the limbs and senses of My body; [[synonyms:v:vṛthā]]---fruitless; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "My dear friends, you are all My life and soul; therefore I tell you that I possess no wealth of love for Kṛṣṇa. Consequently My life is poverty-stricken. My limbs and senses are useless."**
