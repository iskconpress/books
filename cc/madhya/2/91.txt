====== Cc. Madhya 2.91 ======

===== Text =====

> ei antya-līlā-sāra, sūtra-madhye vistāra,
> kari' kichu kariluṅ varṇana
> ihā-madhye mari yabe, varṇite nā pāri tabe,
> ei līlā bhakta-gaṇa-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:a:antya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of the //antya-līlā// (Lord Caitanya's pastimes at the end); [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the form of a synopsis; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expansion; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---have described; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the meantime; [[synonyms:m:mari]]---I die; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---to describe; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---not able; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---these pastimes; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the treasure of the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**In this chapter I have to some extent described the essence of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya at the end. If I die in the meantime and cannot describe them in detail, at least the devotees will have this transcendental treasure.**
