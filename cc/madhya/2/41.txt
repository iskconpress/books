====== CC Madhya 2.41 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ kahe,---hāya hāya, śuna, svarūpa-rāmarāya,
> ei mora hṛdaya-niścaya
> śuni karaha vicāra, haya, naya---kaha sāra,
> eta bali' śloka uccāraya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:h:hāya]] [[synonyms:h:hāya]]---alas; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---kindly hear; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---My dear Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]-[[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---the certainty in My heart; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---just make; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---judgment; [[synonyms:h:haya,]] [[synonyms:n:naya]]---correct or not; [[synonyms:k:kaha]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---tell Me the essence; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---another verse; [[synonyms:u:uccāraya]]---recites.

===== Translation =====

**Again He addressed both Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāya Rāmānanda, speaking despondently, "Alas! My friends, you can now know the certainty within My heart, and after knowing My heart you should judge whether I am correct or not. You can speak of this properly." Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then began to chant another verse.**
