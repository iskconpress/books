====== CC Madhya 2.59 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra darśana-vine, adhanya e rātri-dine,
> ei kāla nā yāya kāṭana
> tumi anāthera bandhu, apāra karuṇā-sindhu,
> kṛpā kari' deha daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---audience; [[synonyms:v:vine]]---without; [[synonyms:a:adhanya]]---inauspicious; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---night and day; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---this time; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---does not go; [[synonyms:k:kāṭana]]---passing; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:a:anāthera]] [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]---friend of the helpless; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:k:karuṇā]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---ocean of mercy; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing mercy; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---kindly give; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---audience.

===== Translation =====

**"All these inauspicious days and nights are not passing, for I have not met You. It is difficult to know how to pass all this time. But You are the friend of the helpless and an ocean of mercy. Kindly give Me Your audience, for I am in a precarious position."**
