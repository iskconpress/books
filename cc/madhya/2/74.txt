====== CC Madhya 2.74 ======

===== Text =====

> māraḥ svayaṁ nu madhura-dyuti-maṇḍalaṁ nu
> mādhuryam eva nu mano-nayanāmṛtaṁ nu
> veṇī-mṛjo nu mama jīvita-vallabho nu
> kṛṣṇo 'yam abhyudayate mama locanāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māraḥ]]---Cupid; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---whether; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:d:dyuti]]---of effulgence; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍalam]]---encirclement; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---whether; [[synonyms:m:mādhuryam]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---even; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---certainly; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:n:nayana]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛtam]]---nectar for the mind and eyes; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---whether; [[synonyms:v:veṇī]]-[[synonyms:m:mṛjaḥ]]---loosening of the hair; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---whether; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---My; [[synonyms:j:jīvita]]-[[synonyms:v:vallabhaḥ]]---the pleasure of the life and soul; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---whether; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇaḥ]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:abhyudayate]]---manifests; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---My; [[synonyms:l:locanāya]]---for the eyes.

===== Translation =====

**In the attitude of Rādhārāṇī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu addressed the gopīs, " 'My dear friends, where is that Kṛṣṇa, Cupid personified, who has the effulgence of a kadamba flower, who is sweetness itself, the nectar of My eyes and mind, He who loosens the hair of the gopīs, who is the supreme source of transcendental bliss and My life and soul? Has He come before My eyes again?' "**

===== Purport =====

This is another verse from the //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta// (68).
