====== Cc. Madhya 2.12 ======

===== Text =====

> hasta-padera sandhi saba vitasti-pramāṇe
> sandhi chāḍi' bhinna haye, carma rahe sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hasta]]-[[synonyms:p:padera]]---of the hands and legs; [[synonyms:s:sandhi]]---joints; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vitasti]]---about eight inches; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇe]]---in length; [[synonyms:s:sandhi]]---joints; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---dislocated; [[synonyms:b:bhinna]]---separated; [[synonyms:h:haye]]---become; [[synonyms:c:carma]]---skin; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in the place.

===== Translation =====

**The joints of His hands and legs would sometimes become separated by eight inches, and they remained connected only by the skin.**
