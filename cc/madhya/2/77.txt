====== Cc. Madhya 2.77 ======

===== Text =====

> caṇḍīdāsa, vidyāpati, rāyera nāṭaka-gīti,
> karṇāmṛta, śrī-gīta-govinda
> svarūpa-rāmānanda-sane, mahāprabhu rātri-dine,
> gāya, śune---parama ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caṇḍīdāsa]]---the poet Caṇḍīdāsa; [[synonyms:v:vidyāpati]]---the poet Vidyāpati; [[synonyms:r:rāyera]]---of the poet Rāya Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---the //Jagannātha-vallabha-nāṭaka//; [[synonyms:g:gīti]]---songs; [[synonyms:k:karṇāmṛta]]---the //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta// of Bilvamaṅgala Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the //Gīta-govinda,// by Jayadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Rāya Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sings; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---with great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**He also passed His time reading the books and singing the songs of Caṇḍīdāsa and Vidyāpati, and listening to quotations from the Jagannātha-vallabha-nāṭaka, Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta and Gīta-govinda. Thus in the association of Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāya Rāmānanda, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed His days and nights chanting and hearing with great pleasure.**
