====== CC Madhya 2.10 ======

===== Text =====

> upavanodyāna dekhi' vṛndāvana-jñāna
> tāhāṅ yāi' nāce, gāya, kṣaṇe mūrcchā yā'na

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upavana]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāna]]---small parks; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---took them to be the forests of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sings; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---in a moment; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchā]]---unconsciousness; [[synonyms:y:yā'na]]---goes.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes Caitanya Mahāprabhu mistook the small parks of the city for Vṛndāvana. Sometimes He would go there, dance and chant and sometimes fall unconscious in spiritual ecstasy.**
