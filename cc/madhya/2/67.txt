====== CC Madhya 2.67 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi deva---krīḍā-rata, bhuvanera nārī yata,
> tāhe kara abhīṣṭa krīḍana
> tumi mora dayita, mote vaise tomāra cita,
> mora bhāgye kaile āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]-[[synonyms:r:rata]]---engaged in Your pastimes; [[synonyms:b:bhuvanera]]---of all the universes; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---women; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---in those pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You do; [[synonyms:a:abhīṣṭa]]---desired; [[synonyms:k:krīḍana]]---acting; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:d:dayita]]---merciful; [[synonyms:m:mote]]---to Me; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---rest; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:c:cita]]---mind; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---by fortune; [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---You have made; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---appearance.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You are engaged in Your pastimes, and You utilize all the women in the universe according to Your desire. You are so kind to Me. Please divert Your attention to Me, for by fortune You have appeared before Me.**
