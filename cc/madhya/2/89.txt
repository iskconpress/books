====== CC Madhya 2.89 ======

===== Text =====

> śeṣa-līlāra sūtra-gaṇa, kailuṅ kichu vivaraṇa,
> ihāṅ vistārite citta haya
> thāke yadi āyuḥ-śeṣa, vistāriba līlā-śeṣa,
> yadi mahāprabhura kṛpā haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes at the end; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the synopsis; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:v:vistārite]]---to expand more and more; [[synonyms:c:citta]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is a desire; [[synonyms:t:thāke]]---remains; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:ā:āyuḥ]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---the end of life; [[synonyms:v:vistāriba]]---I shall describe; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---at the end; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**I have already given a synopsis of all the facts and figures of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's last pastimes, and I have a desire to describe them elaborately. If I remain longer and am fortunate enough to receive the mercy of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, I shall try to describe them again more elaborately.**
