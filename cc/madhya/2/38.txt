====== Cc. Madhya 2.38 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ yadi kona kṣaṇa, kayāya kṛṣṇa daraśana
> tabe sei ghaṭī-kṣaṇa-pala
> diyā mālya-candana, nānā ratna-ābharaṇa,
> alaṅkṛta karimu sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---moment; [[synonyms:k:kayāya]]---helps; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pala]]---seconds, moments and hours; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---garlands and sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]---jewels; [[synonyms:ā:ābharaṇa]]---ornaments; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkṛta]]---decorated; [[synonyms:k:karimu]]---I shall make; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**"If by chance such a moment comes when I can once again see Kṛṣṇa, then I shall worship those seconds, moments and hours with flower garlands and pulp of sandalwood and decorate them with all kinds of jewels and ornaments."**
