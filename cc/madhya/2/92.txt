====== CC Madhya 2.92 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe ei sūtra kaila, yei ihāṅ nā likhila,
> āge tāhā kariba vistāra
> yadi tata dina jiye, mahāprabhura kṛpā haye,
> icchā bhari' kariba vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---these notes; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---I have made; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---in this; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:likhila]]---I could not write; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in the future; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall make; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expansion; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:t:tata]]---so many; [[synonyms:d:dina]]---days; [[synonyms:j:jiye]]---I live; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy; [[synonyms:h:haye]]---there is; [[synonyms:i:icchā]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---satisfying the desire; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall do; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration.

===== Translation =====

**In this chapter I have briefly described the antya-līlā. Whatever I have not described I shall describe extensively in the future. If by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mercy I live for so many days that I can fulfill my desires, I will give full consideration to these pastimes.**
