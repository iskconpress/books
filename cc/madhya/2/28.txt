====== CC Madhya 2.28 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-rūpādi-niṣevaṇaṁ vinā
> vyarthāni me 'hāny akhilendriyāṇy alam
> pāṣāṇa-śuṣkendhana-bhārakāṇy aho
> bibharmi vā tāni kathaṁ hata-trapaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---of the transcendental form and pastimes of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:niṣevaṇam]]---the service; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:v:vyarthāni]]---meaningless; [[synonyms:m:me]]---My; [[synonyms:a:ahāni]]---days; [[synonyms:a:akhila]]---all; [[synonyms:i:indriyāṇi]]---senses; [[synonyms:a:alam]]---entirely; [[synonyms:p:pāṣāṇa]]---dead stones; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]---dry; [[synonyms:i:indhana]]---wood; [[synonyms:b:bhārakāṇi]]---burdens; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---alas; [[synonyms:b:bibharmi]]---I bear; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:t:tāni]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:katham]]---how; [[synonyms:h:hata]]-[[synonyms:t:trapaḥ]]---without shame.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'My dear friends, unless I serve the transcendental form, qualities and pastimes of Śrī Kṛṣṇa, all My days and all My senses will become entirely useless. Now I am uselessly bearing the burden of My senses, which are like stone blocks and dried wood. I do not know how long I will be able to continue without shame.'**
