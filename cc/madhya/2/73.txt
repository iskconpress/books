====== CC Madhya 2.73 ======

===== Text =====

> mūrcchāya haila sākṣātkāra, uṭhi' kare huhuṅkāra,
> kahe---ei āilā mahāśaya
> kṛṣṇera mādhurī-guṇe, nānā bhrama haya mane,
> śloka paḍi' karaye niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mūrcchāya]]---in the swoon; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:sākṣātkāra]]---direct meeting; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:h:hu]]-[[synonyms:h:huṅ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---thus; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---He has come; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśaya]]---the great personality; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mādhurī]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---by qualities; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:b:bhrama]]---mistakes; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---does; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---ascertainment.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was thus unconscious, He happened to meet the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Consequently He got up and immediately made a tumultuous sound, very loudly declaring, "Now Kṛṣṇa, the great personality, is present." In this way, because of Kṛṣṇa's sweet qualities, Caitanya Mahāprabhu made different types of mistakes in His mind. Thus by reciting the following verse, He ascertained the presence of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
