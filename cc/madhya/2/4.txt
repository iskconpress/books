====== CC Madhya 2.4 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rādhikāra ceṣṭā yena uddhava-darśane
> ei-mata daśā prabhura haya rātri-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rādhikāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---the activities; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---just like; [[synonyms:u:uddhava]]-[[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in seeing Uddhava at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---the condition; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's state of mind, day and night, was practically identical to Rādhārāṇī's state of mind when Uddhava came to Vṛndāvana to see the gopīs.**
