~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 2.87~~

====== CC Madhya 2.87 ======

===== Text =====

> yebā nāhi bujhe keha, śunite śunite seha,
> ki adbhuta caitanya-carita
> kṛṣṇe upajibe prīti, jānibe rasera rīti,
> śunilei baḍa haya hita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yebā]]---whoever; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:b:bujhe]]---understand; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]] [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---hearing and hearing; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---he; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:carita]]---pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:upajibe]]---will develop; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---love; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---he will understand; [[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:r:rīti]]---the ways; [[synonyms:ś:śunilei]]---simply by hearing; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---great; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:h:hita]]---benefit.

===== Translation =====

**If one does not understand in the beginning but continues to hear again and again, the wonderful effects of Lord Caitanya's pastimes will bring love for Kṛṣṇa. Gradually one will come to understand the loving affairs between Kṛṣṇa and the gopīs and other associates of Vṛndāvana. Everyone is advised to continue to hear over and over again in order to greatly benefit.**
