====== CC Madhya 2.15 ======

===== Text =====

> kāhāṅ mora prāṇa-nātha muralī-vadana
> kāhāṅ karoṅ kāhāṅ pāṅ vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]---Lord of the life; [[synonyms:m:muralī]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---playing the flute; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---what; [[synonyms:k:karoṅ]]---shall I do; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:p:pāṅ]]---I shall get; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Mahārāja Nanda.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu used to express His mind in this way, "Where is the Lord of My life, who is playing His flute? What shall I do now? Where should I go to find the son of Mahārāja Nanda?**
