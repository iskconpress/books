====== CC Madhya 17.227 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra-guṇa prema bāḍe mathurā daraśane
> lakṣa-guṇa prema bāḍe, bhramena yabe vane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---one thousand times; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increased; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]---Mathurā; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---upon seeing; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---a hundred thousand times; [[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---love increases; [[synonyms:b:bhramena]]---wanders; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forests of Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord's ecstatic love increased a thousand times when He visited Mathurā, but it increased a hundred thousand times when He wandered in the forests of Vṛndāvana.**
