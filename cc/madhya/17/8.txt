====== CC Madhya 17.8 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-jana kahe,---'tumi īśvara 'svatantra'
> yei icchā, sei karibā, naha 'paratantra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the two persons replied; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---completely independent; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---whatever You desire; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:karibā]]---You will do; [[synonyms:n:naha]]---You are not; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:t:tantra]]---dependent on anyone.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this, Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī replied, "Dear Lord, You are completely independent. Since You are not dependent on anyone, You do whatever You desire.**
