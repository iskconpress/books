====== CC Madhya 17.20 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra vacana prabhu aṅgīkāra kaila
> balabhadra-bhaṭṭācārye saṅge kari' nila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---his words; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---accepted; [[synonyms:b:balabhadra]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārye]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took with Him.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the request of Svarūpa Dāmodara Paṇḍita and agreed to take Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya with Him.**
