====== CC Madhya 17.163 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvathā-niścita---iṅho kṛṣṇa-avatāra
> mathurā āilā lokera karite nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvathā]]---in every respect; [[synonyms:n:niścita]]---ascertained; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---has come to Mathurā; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to perform; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance.

===== Translation =====

**"Certainly Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is in all respects the incarnation of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Now He has come to Mathurā to deliver everyone."**
