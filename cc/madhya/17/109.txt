====== CC Madhya 17.109 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhā dekhi' jñāna haya---'ei nārāyaṇa
> yei tāṅre dekhe, kare kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing that; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---one comes to the conclusion; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyaṇa Himself; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"When one sees all these features, one takes Him to be Nārāyaṇa Himself. Whoever sees Him immediately begins to chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.**
