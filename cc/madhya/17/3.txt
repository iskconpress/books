====== CC Madhya 17.3 ======

===== Text =====

> śarat-kāla haila, prabhura calite haila mati
> rāmānanda-svarūpa-saṅge nibhṛte yukati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śarat]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---autumn arrived; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calite]]---to travel; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:m:mati]]---desire; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---solitary; [[synonyms:y:yukati]]---consultation.

===== Translation =====

**When autumn arrived, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu decided to go to Vṛndāvana. In a solitary place, He consulted with Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.**
