====== Cc. Madhya 17.52 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍa, baṅga, utkala, dakṣiṇa-deśe giyā
> lokera nistāra kaila āpane bhramiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]---Bengal; [[synonyms:b:baṅga]]---East Bengal; [[synonyms:u:utkala]]---Orissa; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---southern India; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all the people; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---liberation; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhramiyā]]---touring.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally toured Bengal, East Bengal, Orissa and the southern countries, and He delivered all kinds of people by spreading Kṛṣṇa consciousness.**
