====== Cc. Madhya 17.123 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura daraśane śuddha hañāche tāṅra mana
> prabhu-āge duḥkhī hañā kahe vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---by seeing personally the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---purified; [[synonyms:h:hañāche]]---was; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---his mind; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before the Lord; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very much unhappy; [[synonyms:k:kahe]] [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---described the incidents.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa's mind was already purified by seeing the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He therefore went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and described what took place before the Māyāvādī sannyāsī Prakāśānanda.**
