====== CC Madhya 17.198 ======

===== Text =====

> mṛga-mṛgī mukha dekhi' prabhu-aṅga cāṭe
> bhaya nāhi kare, saṅge yāya vāṭe-vāṭe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mṛga]]-[[synonyms:m:mṛgī]]---the deer, both male and female; [[synonyms:m:mukha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing His face; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]] [[synonyms:c:cāṭe]]---began to lick the body of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---they were not at all afraid; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---go with Him; [[synonyms:v:vāṭe]]-[[synonyms:v:vāṭe]]---all along the road.

===== Translation =====

**When the does and bucks came and saw the Lord's face, they began to lick His body. Not being at all afraid of Him, they accompanied Him along the path.**
