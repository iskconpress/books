====== CC Madhya 17.209 ======

===== Text =====

> śuka-śārikā prabhura hāte uḍi' paḍe
> prabhuke śunāñā kṛṣṇera guṇa-śloka paḍe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śārikā]]---the parrots, male and female; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---on the hand; [[synonyms:u:uḍi']]---flying; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fall; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śunāñā]]---causing to hear; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---chanted verses about the transcendental qualities.

===== Translation =====

**Both parrots flew onto the hand of the Lord and began to chant the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa, and the Lord listened to them.**
