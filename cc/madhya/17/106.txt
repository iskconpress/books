====== CC Madhya 17.106 ======

===== Text =====

> "eka sannyāsī āilā jagannātha haite
> tāṅhāra mahimā-pratāpa nā pāri varṇite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---person in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---has come; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:p:pratāpa]]---influence; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]] [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---I cannot describe.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa told Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī, "There is a sannyāsī who has come from Jagannātha Purī, and I cannot describe His wonderful influence and glories.**
