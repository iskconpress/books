====== Cc. Madhya 17.204 ======

===== Text =====

> prati vṛkṣa-latā prabhu karena āliṅgana
> puṣpādi dhyāne karena kṛṣṇe samarpaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]---each and every; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:latā]]---tree and creeper; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embraced; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---all the flowers and fruits; [[synonyms:d:dhyāne]]---in meditation; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---do; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---offering.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to embrace each and every tree and creeper, and they began to offer their fruits and flowers as if in meditation.**
