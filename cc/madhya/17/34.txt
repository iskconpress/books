====== CC Madhya 17.34 ======

===== Text =====

> pathe yāite kare prabhu ucca saṅkīrtana
> madhura kaṇṭha-dhvani śuni' āise mṛgī-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pathe]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---while passing on the path; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:ucca]]---loud; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of Hare Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the voice from His throat; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mṛgī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---she-deer.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted very loudly while passing through the jungle. Hearing His sweet voice, all the does came near Him.**
