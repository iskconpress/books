====== CC Madhya 17.130 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva tāra mukhe nā āise kṛṣṇa-nāma
> 'kṛṣṇa-nāma', 'kṛṣṇa-svarūpa'---duita 'samāna'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in their mouths; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---manifests; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---the personality of the Lord; [[synonyms:d:duita]] [[synonyms:s:samāna]]---both identical.

===== Translation =====

**"The holy name of Kṛṣṇa is not manifest in their mouths because they are offenders unto Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is identical with His holy name.**
