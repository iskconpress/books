====== CC Madhya 17.169 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' prabhu kaila tāṅra caraṇa vandana
> bhaya pāñā prabhu-pāya padilā brāhmaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the feet; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---being afraid; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:paya]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---the //brāhmaṇa.//

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard about Mādhavendra Purī's relationship with the brāhmaṇa, He immediately offered obeisances at his feet. Becoming fearful, the brāhmaṇa also immediately fell at the Lord's feet.**
