====== Cc. Madhya 17.13 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---nija-saṅgī kāṅho nā la-iba
> eka-jane nile, ānera mane duḥkha ha-iba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅgī]]---of My associates; [[synonyms:k:kāṅho]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iba]]---I shall take; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]] [[synonyms:n:nile]]---if I take someone; [[synonyms:ā:ānera]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind of others; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iba]]---there will be unhappiness.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I shall not take any of My associates with Me because if I choose someone, all the others will be unhappy.**
