====== CC Madhya 17.91 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura 'śeṣānna' miśra savaṁśe khāila
> 'prabhu āilā' śuni' candraśekhara āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---along with his family; [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---the Lord has arrived; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:c:candraśekhara]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---Candraśekhara came.

===== Translation =====

**The remnants of food left by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were taken by the whole family of Tapana Miśra. When news spread that the Lord had come, Candraśekhara also came to see Him.**
