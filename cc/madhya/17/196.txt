====== CC Madhya 17.196 ======

===== Text =====

> sustha hañā prabhu kare aṅga-kaṇḍūyana
> prabhu-saṅge cale, nāhi chāḍe dhenu-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sustha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming patient; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---of the body; [[synonyms:k:kaṇḍūyana]]---scratching; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---go; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍe]]---do not give up; [[synonyms:d:dhenu]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the cows.

===== Translation =====

**Becoming pacified, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to caress the cows, and the cows, being unable to give up His company, went with Him.**
