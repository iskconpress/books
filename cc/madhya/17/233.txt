====== CC Madhya 17.233 ======

===== Text =====

> jagat bhāsila caitanya-līlāra pāthāre
> yāṅra yata śakti tata pāthāre sāṅtāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:b:bhāsila]]---floated; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāthāre]]---in the inundation; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as much; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power; [[synonyms:t:tata]]---that much; [[synonyms:p:pāthāre]]---in the inundation; [[synonyms:s:sāṅtāre]]---swims.

===== Translation =====

**The whole world became merged in the inundation of the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. One can swim in that water to the extent that he has the strength.**
