====== CC Madhya 17.37 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle vyāghra tathā āila pāṅca-sāta
> vyāghra-mṛgī mili' cale mahāprabhura sātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:v:vyāghra]]---tigers; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five to seven; [[synonyms:v:vyāghra]]-[[synonyms:m:mṛgī]]---the tigers and deer; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---coming together; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---go; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sātha]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was passing through the jungle, five or seven tigers came. Joining the deer, the tigers began to follow the Lord.**
