====== CC Madhya 17.84 ======

===== Text =====

> 'pūrve śuniyāchi prabhu karyāchena sannyāsa'
> niścaya kariyā haila hṛdaye ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyāchi]]---I have heard; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karyāchena]] [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---has accepted the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---ascertaining that; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**Tapana Miśra then began to think, "I have heard that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has accepted the renounced order." Thinking this, Tapana Miśra became very jubilant within his heart.**
