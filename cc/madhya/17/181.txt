====== Cc. Madhya 17.181 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu tāṅre yadi 'bhikṣā' māgila
> dainya kari' sei vipra kahite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---from him; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:m:māgila]]---requested lunch; [[synonyms:d:dainya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---out of humility; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu willingly requested food from the brāhmaṇa, and the brāhmaṇa, feeling a natural humility, began to speak as follows.**
