====== CC Madhya 17.173 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-premā tāṅhā, yāṅhā tāṅhāra'sambandha'
> tāhāṅ vinā ei premāra kāhāṅ nāhi gandha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:premā]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]]---where; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]---relationship; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without him; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:p:premāra]]---of this ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---there is no possibility of even a scent.

===== Translation =====

**"This kind of ecstatic love can be experienced only when one has a relationship with Mādhavendra Purī. Without him, even a scent of such transcendental ecstatic love is impossible."**
