====== CC Madhya 17.188 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣa-saṅkhya loka āise, nāhika gaṇana
> bāhira hañā prabhu dila daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkhya]]---numbering hundreds of thousands; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---people came; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---there is no counting; [[synonyms:b:bāhira]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---coming out; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dila]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---gave audience.

===== Translation =====

**People came by hundreds of thousands, and no one could count them. Therefore Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came out of the house to give audience to the people.**
