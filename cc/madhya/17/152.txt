====== CC Madhya 17.152 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mathurā' calite pathe yathā rahi' yāya
> kṛṣṇa-nāma-prema diyā lokere nācāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]]---to Mathurā; [[synonyms:c:calite]]---going; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---wherever; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa and His ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:l:lokere]] [[synonyms:n:nācāya]]---made the people dance.

===== Translation =====

**Wherever the Lord stopped to rest on the way to Mathurā, He delivered the holy name of Kṛṣṇa and ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa. Thus He made the people dance.**
