====== Cc. Madhya 17.174 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe bhaṭṭācārya tāre 'sambandha' kahila
> śuni' ānandita vipra nācite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---explained the relationship; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---being pleased; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:n:nācite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to dance.

===== Translation =====

**Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya then explained the relationship between Mādhavendra Purī and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. After hearing this, the brāhmaṇa became very pleased and began to dance.**
