====== CC Madhya 17.162 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅhāra darśane loke preme matta hañā
> hāse, kānde, nāce, gāya, kṛṣṇa-nāma lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by seeing whom; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love; [[synonyms:m:matta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming mad; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laugh; [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---cry; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**The people said, "Simply by seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, everyone is maddened with love of Kṛṣṇa. Indeed, everyone is laughing, crying, dancing, chanting and taking the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.**
