====== CC Madhya 17.206 ======

===== Text =====

> sthāvara-jaṅgama mili' kare kṛṣṇa-dhvani
> prabhura gambhīra-svare yena prati-dhvani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sthāvara]]-[[synonyms:j:jaṅgama]]---all living entities, nonmoving and moving; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting together; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---perform; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---vibration of the sound Hare Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]-[[synonyms:s:svare]]---deep voice; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---responsive vibration.

===== Translation =====

**All moving and nonmoving creatures then began to vibrate the transcendental sound of Hare Kṛṣṇa, as if they were echoing the deep sound of Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
