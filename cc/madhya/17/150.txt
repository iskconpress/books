====== CC Madhya 17.150 ======

===== Text =====

> yamunā dekhiyā preme paḍe jhāṅpa diyā
> āste-vyaste bhaṭṭācārya uṭhāya dhariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yamunā]]---the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:j:jhāṅpa]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---jumping; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---in great haste; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāya]]---raises; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the River Yamunā, He threw Himself in it. Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya hastily caught the Lord and very carefully raised Him up again.**
