====== CC Madhya 17.148 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura virahe tine ekatra miliyā
> prabhu-guṇa gāna kare preme matta hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---because of separation from the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tine]]---all three; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---together; [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---meeting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the transcendental qualities of the Lord; [[synonyms:g:gāna]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---chant; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---with love; [[synonyms:m:matta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being mad.

===== Translation =====

**Feeling separation from the Lord, the three used to meet and glorify the holy qualities of the Lord. Thus they were absorbed in ecstatic love.**
