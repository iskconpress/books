====== Cc. Madhya 17.97 ======

===== Text =====

> nirantara duṅhe cinti tomāra caraṇa
> 'sarvajña īśvara' tumi dilā daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---incessantly; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---we two; [[synonyms:c:cinti]]---think of; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---Your lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---omniscient; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:d:dilā]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---gave Your audience.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, we two think of Your lotus feet incessantly. Although You are the omniscient Supreme Personality of Godhead, You have granted us Your audience.**
