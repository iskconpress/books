====== CC Madhya 17.141 ======

===== Text =====

> ei saba rahu---kṛṣṇa-caraṇa-sambandhe
> ātmārāmera mana hare tulasīra gandhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:r:rahu]]---apart from the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---in relation to the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārāmera]]---of self-realized persons; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---attracts; [[synonyms:t:tulasīra]] [[synonyms:g:gandhe]]---the aroma of //tulasī// leaves.

===== Translation =====

**"Apart from the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa, when tulasī leaves are offered at the lotus feet of Śrī Kṛṣṇa, even the aroma of the leaves attracts the minds of self-realized persons.**
