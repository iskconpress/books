====== CC Madhya 17.19 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vipra vahi' nibe vastrāmbu-bhājana
> bhaṭṭācārya bhikṣā dibe kari' bhikṣāṭana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the other //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vahi']] [[synonyms:n:nibe]]---will carry; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]-[[synonyms:a:ambu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhājana]]---the cloth and waterpot; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dibe]]---will arrange for cooking; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]-[[synonyms:a:aṭana]]---collecting alms.

===== Translation =====

**"The other brāhmaṇa can carry Your cloth and waterpot, and Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya will collect alms and cook for You."**
