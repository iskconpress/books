====== CC Madhya 17.50 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi prabhu loka-saṅghaṭṭera trāse
> prema 'gupta' karena, bāhire nā prakāśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭera]]---of crowds of people; [[synonyms:t:trāse]]---being afraid; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:g:gupta]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---keeps hidden; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---manifest.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord did not always manifest His ecstasy. Being afraid of a great assembly of people, the Lord kept His ecstasy concealed.**
