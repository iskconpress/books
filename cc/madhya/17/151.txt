====== Cc. Madhya 17.151 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata tina-dina prayāge rahilā
> kṛṣṇa-nāma-prema diyā loka nistārilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---for three days; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]]---at Prayāga; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---and ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:n:nistārilā]]---delivered the people.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord stayed at Prayāga for three days. He delivered the holy name of Kṛṣṇa and ecstatic love. Thus He delivered many people.**
