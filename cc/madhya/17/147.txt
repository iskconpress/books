====== Cc. Madhya 17.147 ======

===== Text =====

> sei tina saṅge cale, prabhu niṣedhila
> dūra haite tina-jane ghare pāṭhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]---those three; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---go; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhila]]---forbade; [[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from a distance; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---the three persons; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---home; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent back.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu started for Mathurā, all three devotees started to go with Him. However, the Lord forbade them to accompany Him, and from a distance He asked them to return home.**
