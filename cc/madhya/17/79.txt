====== CC Madhya 17.79 ======

===== Text =====

> adhama-kākere kailā garuḍa-samāna
> 'svatantra īśvara' tumi---svayaṁ bhagavān"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adhama]]-[[synonyms:k:kākere]]---the most condemned crow; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---You have made; [[synonyms:g:garuḍa]]-[[synonyms:s:samāna]]---like Garuḍa; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the original Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"You have made me Your carrier Garuḍa, although I am no better than a condemned crow. Thus You are the independent Personality of Godhead, the original Lord.**
