====== Cc. Madhya 17.226 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlācale chilā yaiche premāveśa mana
> vṛndāvana yāite pathe haila śata-guṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:c:chilā]]---was; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---always in a mentality of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---going; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---one hundred times.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind was absorbed in ecstatic love at Jagannātha Purī, but when He passed along the road on the way to Vṛndāvana, that love increased a hundred times.**
