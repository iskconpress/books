====== CC Madhya 17.100 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu dui bhṛtyera vaśe
> icchā nāhi, tabu tathā rahilā dina-daśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtyera]]---by servants; [[synonyms:v:vaśe]]---being obliged; [[synonyms:i:icchā]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was no such desire; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:d:dina]]-[[synonyms:d:daśe]]---for ten days.

===== Translation =====

**Even though He had not made such a plan, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained for ten days at Vārāṇasī, being obligated by the requests of His two servants.**
