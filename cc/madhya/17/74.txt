====== Cc. Madhya 17.74 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana-mukhe kṛṣṇa āmā śikhāilā
> tāhā vighna kari' vana-pathe lañā āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth of Sanātana; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---gave instructions; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vighna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making a hindrance; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the path through the forest; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus I was going to Vṛndāvana with a big crowd, but through the mouth of Sanātana, Kṛṣṇa taught Me a lesson. Thus by making some impediment, He has brought Me on a path through the forest to Vṛndāvana.**
