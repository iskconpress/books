====== Cc. Madhya 17.154 ======

===== Text =====

> pathe yāhāṅ yāhāṅ haya yamunā-darśana
> tāhāṅ jhāṅpa diyā paḍe preme acetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]-[[synonyms:d:darśana]]---meeting with the Yamunā River; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:j:jhāṅpa]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]] [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---jumps over and falls down; [[synonyms:p:preme]] [[synonyms:a:acetana]]---unconscious in the ecstasy of love.

===== Translation =====

**While the Lord was going to Mathurā, He came across the River Yamunā several times, and as soon as He saw the River Yamunā, He would immediately jump in, falling unconscious in the water in the ecstasy of love of Kṛṣṇa.**
