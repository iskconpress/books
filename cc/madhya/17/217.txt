====== Cc. Madhya 17.217 ======

===== Text =====

> śuka-śārī uḍi' punaḥ gela vṛkṣa-ḍāle
> mayūrera nṛtya prabhu dekhe kutūhale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śārī]]---the male and female parrots; [[synonyms:u:uḍi']]---flying; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāle]]---to the branch of a tree; [[synonyms:m:mayūrera]]---of the peacocks; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:k:kutūhale]]---with curiosity.

===== Translation =====

**Both parrots then flew onto a tree branch, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to watch the dancing of peacocks with curiosity.**
