====== CC Madhya 17.33 ======

===== Text =====

> keha bhūme paḍe, keha karaye citkāra
> dekhi' bhaṭṭācāryera mane haya camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---some of them; [[synonyms:b:bhūme]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fall down; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---some of them; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---perform; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---screaming; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Some of the elephants fell to the ground, and some screamed in ecstasy. Seeing this, Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya was completely astonished.**
