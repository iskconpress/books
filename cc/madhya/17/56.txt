====== Cc. Madhya 17.56 ======

===== Text =====

> yāhāṅ nadī dekhe tāhāṅ mānaye---'kālindī'
> mahā-premāveśe nāce prabhu paḍe kāndi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]---river; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:m:mānaye]]---considers; [[synonyms:k:kālindī]]---the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:k:kāndi']]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**Similarly, whenever Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw a river, He immediately accepted it as the River Yamunā. Thus while in the forest He was filled with great ecstatic love, and He danced and fell down crying.**
