====== CC Madhya 17.108 ======

===== Text =====

> ājānu-lambita bhuja, kamala-nayana
> yata kichu īśvarera sarva sal-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājānu]]-[[synonyms:l:lambita]]---reaching down to the knees; [[synonyms:b:bhuja]]---arms; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:n:nayana]]---eyes like the petals of a lotus flower; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as many as; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---transcendental symptoms.

===== Translation =====

**"He has arms that extend to His knees, and His eyes are like the petals of a lotus. In His person are all the transcendental symptoms of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
