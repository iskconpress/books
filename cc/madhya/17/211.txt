====== CC Madhya 17.211 ======

===== Text =====

> śuka-mukhe śuni' tabe kṛṣṇera varṇana
> śārikā paḍaye tabe rādhikā-varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuka]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth of the male parrot; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---a description of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śārikā]]---female parrot; [[synonyms:p:paḍaye]]---recites; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---a description of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this description of Lord Kṛṣṇa from the male parrot, the female parrot began to recite a description of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
