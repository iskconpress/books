====== CC Madhya 17.122 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' sei vipra mahā-duḥkha pāilā
> 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa' kahi' tathā haite uṭhi' gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---became very much aggrieved; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---uttering the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tathā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from there; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---got up and went away.

===== Translation =====

**When the brāhmaṇa heard Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī speak like this about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he became very grief-stricken. Chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa, he immediately left.**
