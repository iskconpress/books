====== CC Madhya 17.21 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva-rātrye jagannātha dekhi' 'ājñā' lañā
> śeṣa-rātre uṭhi' prabhu calilā lukāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---on the previous night; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking permission; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:r:rātre]]---near the end of night; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---rising; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---started; [[synonyms:l:lukāñā]]---without being seen.

===== Translation =====

**On the previous night, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had visited Lord Jagannātha and taken His permission. Now, near the end of night, the Lord got up and started immediately. He was not seen by others.**
