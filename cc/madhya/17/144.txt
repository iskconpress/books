====== CC Madhya 17.144 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāvakāli vecite āmi āilāṅa kāśīpure
> grāhaka nāhi, nā vikāya, lañā yāba ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāvakāli]]---devotional sentiments; [[synonyms:v:vecite]]---to sell; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:ā:āilāṅa]]---came; [[synonyms:k:kāśīpure]]---to the city of Kāśī; [[synonyms:g:grāhaka]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is no customer; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:v:vikāya]]---do not sell; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:y:yāba]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---then I must take my commodity back home.

===== Translation =====

**"I have come here to sell My emotional ecstatic sentiments in this city of Kāśī, but I cannot find any customers. If they are not sold, I must take them back home.**
