====== CC Madhya 17.220 ======

===== Text =====

> āste-vyaste mahāprabhura lañā bahirvāsa
> jala-seka kare aṅge, vastrera vātāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---with great haste; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---covering cloth; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:s:seka]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---sprinkle water; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:v:vastrera]] [[synonyms:v:vātāsa]]---fanning with the cloth.

===== Translation =====

**They hastily sprinkled water over the Lord's body. Then they took up His cloth and began to fan Him with it.**
