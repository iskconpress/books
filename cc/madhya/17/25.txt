====== Cc. Madhya 17.25 ======

===== Text =====

> nirjana-vane cale prabhu kṛṣṇa-nāma lañā
> hasti-vyāghra patha chāḍe prabhure dekhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirjana]]-[[synonyms:v:vane]]---in a solitary forest; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---walks; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hasti]]---elephants; [[synonyms:v:vyāghra]]---tigers; [[synonyms:p:patha]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍe]]---leave the path; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord passed through the solitary forest chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa, the tigers and elephants, seeing Him, gave way.**
