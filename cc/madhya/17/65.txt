====== CC Madhya 17.65 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya sevā kare, snehe yaiche 'dāsa'
> tāṅra vipra vahe jala-pātra-bahirvāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---renders service; [[synonyms:s:snehe]]---in great affection; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---exactly like; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---a servant; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---his //brāhmaṇa// assistant; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---carries; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the waterpot; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---and garments.

===== Translation =====

**Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya was so affectionate to the Lord that he was rendering service just like a menial servant. His assistant brāhmaṇa carried the waterpot and garments.**
