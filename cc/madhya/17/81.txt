====== Cc. Madhya 17.81 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata balabhadra karena stavana
> prema-sevā kari' tuṣṭa kaila prabhura mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:balabhadra]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---offers; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---prayers; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---rendering service in love; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]]---pacified; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya offered his prayers to the Lord. By rendering service unto Him in ecstatic love, He pacified the Lord's mind.**
