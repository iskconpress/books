====== CC Madhya 17.175 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe vipra prabhure lañā āilā nija-ghare
> āpana-icchāya prabhura nānā sevā kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his home; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:i:icchāya]]---by his own will; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---services; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---rendered.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa then took Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home and, out of his own free will, began to serve the Lord in various ways.**
