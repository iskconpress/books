====== CC Madhya 17.172 ======

===== Text =====

> kintu tomāra prema dekhi' mane anumāni
> mādhavendra-purīra 'sambandha' dhara---jāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kintu]]---still; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:prema]]---Your ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in my mind; [[synonyms:a:anumāni]]---I imagine; [[synonyms:m:mādhavendra]]-[[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Śrī Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]---relationship; [[synonyms:d:dhara]]---You have; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I can understand.

===== Translation =====

**"Upon seeing Your ecstatic love, I can just imagine that You must have some relationship with Mādhavendra Purī. This is my understanding.**
