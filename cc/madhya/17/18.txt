====== CC Madhya 17.18 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāre saṅge laha yadi, sabāra haya 'sukha'
> vana-pathe yāite tomāra nahibe kona 'duḥkha'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāre]]---him; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along; [[synonyms:l:laha]]---You accept; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---everyone will be happy; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the path through the jungle; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---going; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nahibe]]---there will not be; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---any; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---difficulty.

===== Translation =====

**"If You can also take him with You, we will be very happy. If two people go with You through the jungle, there will certainly be no difficulty or inconvenience.**
