====== CC Madhya 17.22 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle bhakta-gaṇa prabhu nā dekhiyā
> anveṣaṇa kari' phire vyākula hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---early in the morning; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---not seeing; [[synonyms:a:anveṣaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---searching; [[synonyms:p:phire]]---wander; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming very anxious.

===== Translation =====

**Because the Lord had departed, the devotees, unable to see Him early in the morning, began to search for Him with great anxiety.**
