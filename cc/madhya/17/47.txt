====== CC Madhya 17.47 ======

===== Text =====

> yei grāma diyā yāna, yāhāṅ karena sthiti
> se-saba grāmera lokera haya 'prema-bhakti'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:g:grāma]]---which villages; [[synonyms:d:diyā]] [[synonyms:y:yāna]]---the Lord goes through; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---takes; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---rest; [[synonyms:s:se]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all those; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---of the villages; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is awakening of; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---ecstatic love of God.

===== Translation =====

**In all the villages through which the Lord passed and in all the places He rested on His journey, everyone was purified and awakened to ecstatic love of God.**
