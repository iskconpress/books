====== CC Madhya 17.225 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura premāveśa dekhi' brāhmaṇa---vismita
> prabhura rakṣā lāgi' vipra ha-ilā cintita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---astonished; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rakṣā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the protection; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:c:cintita]]---very anxious.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa was astounded to see the symptoms of ecstatic love exhibited by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He then became anxious to give the Lord protection.**
