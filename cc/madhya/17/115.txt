====== Cc. Madhya 17.115 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā prakāśānanda bahuta hāsilā
> vipre upahāsa kari' kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prakāśānanda]]---Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:h:hāsilā]]---laughed very much; [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---at the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:u:upahāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---jokingly laughing; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī laughed very much to hear this description. Joking and laughing at the brāhmaṇa, he began to speak as follows.**
