====== CC Madhya 17.88 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura caraṇodaka savaṁśe kaila pāna
> bhaṭṭācāryera pūjā kaila kariyā sammāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:udaka]]---the water used to wash the lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---with his whole family; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drank; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:pūjā]]---worship; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---showing; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---respect.

===== Translation =====

**He washed the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and afterwards he and his whole family drank the wash water. He also worshiped Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya and showed him respect.**
