====== CC Madhya 17.76 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārye āliṅgiyā tāṅhāre kahila
> 'tomāra prasāde āmi eta sukha pāila'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārye]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by your kindness; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya and told him, "It is only by your kindness that I am now so happy."**
