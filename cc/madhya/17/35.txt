====== Cc. Madhya 17.35 ======

===== Text =====

> ḍāhine-vāme dhvani śuni' yāya prabhu-saṅge
> prabhu tāra aṅga muche, śloka paḍe raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāhine]]-[[synonyms:v:vāme]]---right and left; [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---vibration; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---they follow; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---their; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---bodies; [[synonyms:m:muche]]---pats; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in great curiosity.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing the Lord's great vibration, all the does followed Him left and right. While reciting a verse with great curiosity, the Lord patted them.**
