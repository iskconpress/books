====== CC Madhya 17.207 ======

===== Text =====

> mṛgera galā dhari' prabhu karena rodane
> mṛgera pulaka aṅge, aśru nayane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mṛgera]]---of the deer; [[synonyms:g:galā]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching the necks; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:r:rodane]]---crying; [[synonyms:m:mṛgera]]---of the deer; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:n:nayane]]---in the eyes.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then clasped the necks of the deer and began to cry. There was jubilation manifest in the bodies of the deer, and tears were in their eyes.**
