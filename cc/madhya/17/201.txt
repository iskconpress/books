====== Cc. Madhya 17.201 ======

===== Text =====

> phula-phala bhari' ḍāla paḍe prabhu-pāya
> bandhu dekhi' bandhu yena'bheṭa' lañā yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:phula]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---loaded with fruits and flowers; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāla]]---the branches; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fall down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing one friend; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]---another friend; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:b:bheṭa]]---a presentation; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes.

===== Translation =====

**The trees and creepers, overloaded with fruits and flowers, fell down at the lotus feet of the Lord and greeted Him with various presentations as if they were friends.**
