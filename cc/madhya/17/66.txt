====== CC Madhya 17.66 ======

===== Text =====

> nirjharete uṣṇodake snāna tina-bāra
> dui-sandhyā agni-tāpa kāṣṭhera apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirjharete]]---in the waterfalls; [[synonyms:u:uṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:udake]]---in warm water; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bath; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---thrice; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]---morning and evening; [[synonyms:a:agni]]-[[synonyms:t:tāpa]]---heating by a fire; [[synonyms:k:kāṣṭhera]]---of wood; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---without limit.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord used to bathe three times a day in the warm water of the waterfalls. He also used to heat Himself morning and evening with a fire made of the limitless wood.**
