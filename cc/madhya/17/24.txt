====== CC Madhya 17.24 ======

===== Text =====

> prasiddha patha chāḍi' prabhu upapathe calilā
> 'kaṭaka' ḍāhine kari' vane praveśilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasiddha]]---well-known; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---public way; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:upapathe]]---through a bypass; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---began to walk; [[synonyms:k:kaṭaka]]---the city of Kaṭaka; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāhine]]---on the right side; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---keeping; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---within the forest; [[synonyms:p:praveśilā]]---entered.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord abandoned walking on the well-known public road and went instead along a bypass. He thus kept the city of Kaṭaka on His right as He entered the forest.**
