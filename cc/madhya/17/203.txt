====== CC Madhya 17.203 ======

===== Text =====

> tā-sabāra prīti dekhi' prabhu bhāvāveśe
> sabā-sane krīḍā kare hañā tāra vaśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---affection; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---sporting; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---their; [[synonyms:v:vaśe]]---under control.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing their affection, the Lord was moved by ecstatic love. He began to sport with them exactly as a friend sports with another friend. Thus He voluntarily came under the control of His friends.**
