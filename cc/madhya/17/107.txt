====== CC Madhya 17.107 ======

===== Text =====

> sakala dekhiye tāṅte adbhuta-kathana
> prakāṇḍa-śarīra, śuddha-kāñcana-varaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiye]]---I see everything; [[synonyms:t:tāṅte]]---in Him; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]-[[synonyms:k:kathana]]---wonderful description; [[synonyms:p:prakāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]---very large body; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---pure; [[synonyms:k:kāñcana]]---gold; [[synonyms:v:varaṇa]]---complexion.

===== Translation =====

**"Everything is wonderful about that sannyāsī. He has a very well built and luxurious body, and His complexion is like purified gold.**
