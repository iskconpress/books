====== CC Madhya 17.117 ======

===== Text =====

> 'caitanya'-nāma tāṅra, bhāvuka-gaṇa lañā
> deśe deśe grāme grāme bule nācāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Caitanya; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His name; [[synonyms:b:bhāvuka]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by some sentimentalists; [[synonyms:d:deśe]] [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---from country to country; [[synonyms:g:grāme]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---from village to village; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---travels; [[synonyms:n:nācāñā]]---causing to dance.

===== Translation =====

**Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī continued, "I know that His name is Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya and that He is accompanied by many sentimentalists. His followers dance with Him, and He tours from country to country and village to village.**
