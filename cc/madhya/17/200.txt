====== CC Madhya 17.200 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu dekhi' vṛndāvanera vṛkṣa-latā-gaṇe
> aṅkura pulaka, madhu-aśru variṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:latā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the trees and creepers; [[synonyms:a:aṅkura]]---twigs; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]---jubilant; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears in the form of honey; [[synonyms:v:variṣaṇe]]---pour.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the trees and creepers of Vṛndāvana became jubilant. Their twigs stood up, and they began to shed tears of ecstasy in the form of honey.**
