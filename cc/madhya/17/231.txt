====== CC Madhya 17.231 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvane haila prabhura yateka premera vikāra
> koṭi-granthe 'ananta' likhena tāhāra vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there were; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yateka]]---as many; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformations of ecstasy; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:g:granthe]]---in millions of books; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---Lord Ananta; [[synonyms:l:likhena]]---writes; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of them; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---elaboration.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Ananta writes millions of books elaborately describing the transformations of ecstatic love experienced by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in Vṛndāvana.**
