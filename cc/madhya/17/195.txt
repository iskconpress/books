====== CC Madhya 17.195 ======

===== Text =====

> gābhī dekhi' stabdha prabhu premera taraṅge
> vātsalye gābhī prabhura cāṭe saba-aṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gābhī]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the cows; [[synonyms:s:stabdha]]---stunned; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:t:taraṅge]]---in the waves of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:v:vātsalye]]---in great affection; [[synonyms:g:gābhī]]---all the cows; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cāṭe]]---licked; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---all over the body.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the herds approach Him, the Lord was stunned with ecstatic love. The cows then began to lick His body in great affection.**
