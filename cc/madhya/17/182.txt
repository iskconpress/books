====== CC Madhya 17.182 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāre 'bhikṣā' diba---baḍa bhāgya se āmāra
> tumi---īśvara, nāhi tomāra vidhi-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---unto You; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:diba]]---I shall offer food; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---great fortune; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---regulative behavior.

===== Translation =====

**"It is a great fortune for me to offer You food. You are the Supreme Lord, and, being in the transcendental position, You are not restricted in any way.**
