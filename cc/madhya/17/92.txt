====== CC Madhya 17.92 ======

===== Text =====

> miśrera sakhā teṅho prabhura pūrva dāsa
> vaidya-jāti, likhana-vṛtti, vārāṇasī-vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśrera]] [[synonyms:s:sakhā]]---friend of Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---former servant; [[synonyms:v:vaidya]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---by caste a physician; [[synonyms:l:likhana]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛtti]]---by profession a clerk; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---resident of Vārāṇasī.

===== Translation =====

**Candraśekhara happened to be a friend of Tapana Miśra's, and he was long known to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as His servant. He was a physician by caste, and by profession he was a clerk. At the time he was living in Vārāṇasī.**
