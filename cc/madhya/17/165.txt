====== CC Madhya 17.165 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ārya, sarala, tumi---vṛddha brāhmaṇa
> kāhāṅ haite pāile tumi ei prema-dhana?'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---advanced in devotional service; [[synonyms:s:sarala]]---simple; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:v:vṛddha]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---elderly //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from where; [[synonyms:p:pāile]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---did you obtain; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---transcendental opulence of ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You are an elderly brāhmaṇa, you are sincere, and you are advanced in spiritual life. Wherefrom have you gotten this transcendental opulence of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa?"**
