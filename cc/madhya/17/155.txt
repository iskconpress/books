====== CC Madhya 17.155 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurā-nikaṭe āilā---mathurā dekhiyā
> daṇḍavat hañā paḍe premāviṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near Mathurā; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing the city of Mathurā; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---in the great ecstasy of love.

===== Translation =====

**When He approached Mathurā and saw the city, He immediately fell to the ground and offered obeisances with great ecstatic love.**
