====== Cc. Madhya 17.26 ======

===== Text =====

> pāle-pāle vyāghra, hastī, gaṇḍāra, śūkara-gaṇa
> tāra madhye āveśe prabhu karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāle]]-[[synonyms:p:pāle]]---in flocks; [[synonyms:v:vyāghra]]---tigers; [[synonyms:h:hastī]]---elephants; [[synonyms:g:gaṇḍāra]]---rhinoceros; [[synonyms:ś:śūkara]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---boars; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---through them; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---passed.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord passed through the jungle in great ecstasy, packs of tigers, elephants, rhinoceros and boars came, and the Lord passed right through them.**
