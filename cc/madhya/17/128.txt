====== CC Madhya 17.128 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāra kāraṇa more kaha kṛpā kari'
> tomā dekhi' mukha mora bale 'kṛṣṇa' 'hari"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---cause; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please speak; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by Your causeless mercy; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing You; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---mouth; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy names of Kṛṣṇa and Hari.

===== Translation =====

**"Why could Prakāśānanda not utter the names of Kṛṣṇa and Hari? He chanted the name Caitanya thrice. As far as I am concerned, simply by seeing You I am moved to chant the holy names of Kṛṣṇa and Hari."**
