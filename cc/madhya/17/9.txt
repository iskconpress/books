====== CC Madhya 17.9 ======

===== Text =====

> kintu āmā-duṅhāra śuna eka nivedane
> 'tomāra sukhe āmāra sukha'---kahilā āpane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]-[[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of us; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---one submission; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---by your happiness; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---our happiness; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---You have already stated; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**"Dear Lord, kindly hear our one petition. You have already said that You will derive happiness from our happiness. This is Your own statement.**
