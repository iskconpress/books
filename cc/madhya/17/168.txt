====== CC Madhya 17.168 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla prakaṭa kari' sevā kaila 'mahāśaya'
> adyāpiha tāṅhāra sevā 'govardhane' haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Deity Gopāla; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---installing; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---that great personality; [[synonyms:a:adyāpiha]]---still now; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---of that Deity Gopāla; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---the service; [[synonyms:g:govardhane]]---on the Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is conducted.

===== Translation =====

**"After installing the Deity Gopāla, Śrīla Mādhavendra Purī rendered Him service. That very Deity is still being worshiped at Govardhana Hill."**
