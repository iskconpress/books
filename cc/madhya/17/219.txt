====== CC Madhya 17.219 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure mūrcchita dekhi' sei ta brāhmaṇa
> bhaṭṭācārya-saṅge kare prabhura santarpaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]]---unconscious; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:ta]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---indeed that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:santarpaṇa]]---taking care.

===== Translation =====

**When the brāhmaṇa saw that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was unconscious, he and Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya took care of Him.**
