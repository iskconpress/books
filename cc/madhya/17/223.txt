====== CC Madhya 17.223 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇāveśe prabhura preme garagara mana
> 'bol' 'bol' kari' uṭhi' karena nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---by love; [[synonyms:g:garagara]]---disturbed; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:b:bol]] [[synonyms:b:bol]]---chant, chant; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---saying; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---standing up; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---began to dance.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind wandered in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa. He immediately stood up and said, "Chant! Chant!" Then He Himself began to dance.**
