====== Cc. Madhya 17.189 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhu tuli' bale prabhu 'hari-bola'-dhvani
> preme matta nāce loka kari' hari-dhvani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhu]] [[synonyms:t:tuli']]---raising the arms; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:b:bola]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the transcendental sound vibration Hari bol; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---making the transcendental vibration Hari.

===== Translation =====

**When the people assembled, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu raised His arms and said very loudly, "Hari bol!" The people responded to the Lord and became ecstatic. As if mad, they began to dance and vibrate the transcendental sound, "Hari!"**
