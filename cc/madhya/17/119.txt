====== Cc. Madhya 17.119 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma bhaṭṭācārya---paṇḍita prabala
> śuni' caitanyera saṅge ha-ila pāgala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:p:prabala]]---a learned scholar; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---I have heard; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of Caitanya; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:p:pāgala]]---has become a madman.

===== Translation =====

**"Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya was a very learned scholar, but I have heard that he also has become a madman due to his association with this Caitanya.**
