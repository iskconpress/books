====== CC Madhya 17.101 ======

===== Text =====

> mahārāṣṭrīya vipra āise prabhu dekhibāre
> prabhura rūpa-prema dekhi' haya camatkāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahārāṣṭrīya]]---belonging to the Mahārāṣṭra state; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---one //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---comes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---beauty and ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:c:camatkāre]]---becomes astonished.

===== Translation =====

**At Vārāṇasī there was a Mahārāṣṭrīyan brāhmaṇa who used to come daily to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. This brāhmaṇa was simply astonished to see the Lord's personal beauty and ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
