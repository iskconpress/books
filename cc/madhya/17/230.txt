====== CC Madhya 17.230 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata prema---yāvat bhramila 'bāra' vana
> ekatra likhiluṅ, sarvatra nā yāya varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---so long; [[synonyms:b:bhramila]]---He traveled; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:v:vana]]---through the twelve forests of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---in one place; [[synonyms:l:likhiluṅ]]---I have written; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---cannot be described.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have written a description of the ecstatic love Lord Caitanya manifested while He walked through the twelve forests of Vṛndāvana. To describe it all would be impossible.**
