====== CC Madhya 17.157 ======

===== Text =====

> premānande nāce, gāya, saghana huṅkāra
> prabhura premāveśa dekhi' loke camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chants; [[synonyms:s:saghana]]---repeatedly; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---tumultuous sound vibrations; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---all people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted, danced and made loud vibrations, all the people were astonished to see His ecstatic love.**
