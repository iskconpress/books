====== CC Madhya 17.197 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṣṭe-sṛṣṭye dhenu saba rākhila goyāla
> prabhu-kaṇṭha-dhvani śuni' āise mṛgī-pāla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṣṭe]]-[[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭye]]---with great difficulty; [[synonyms:d:dhenu]]---the cows; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept back; [[synonyms:g:goyāla]]---the cowherd men; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kaṇṭha]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the musical voice of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mṛgī]]-[[synonyms:p:pāla]]---flocks of deer.

===== Translation =====

**It was with great difficulty that the cowherd men were able to keep the cows back. Then when the Lord chanted, all the deer heard His sweet voice and approached Him.**
