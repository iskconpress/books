====== CC Madhya 17.77 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho kahena,---"tumi 'kṛṣṇa', tumi 'dayāmaya'
> adhama jīva muñi, more ha-ilā sadaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---You are Kṛṣṇa Himself; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:a:adhama]]---the lowest of the low; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---living entity; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---You have been; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:daya]]---favorable.

===== Translation =====

**Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "My dear Lord, You are Kṛṣṇa Himself, and therefore You are merciful. I am a fallen living entity, but You have bestowed a great favor upon me.**
