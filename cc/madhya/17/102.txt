====== CC Madhya 17.102 ======

===== Text =====

> vipra saba nimantraya, prabhu nāhi māne
> prabhu kahe,---'āji mora hañāche nimantraṇe'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the brāhmaṇas; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nimantraya]]---invite; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---does not accept; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord replies; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:h:hañāche]]---has been; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇe]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**When the brāhmaṇas of Vārāṇasī would invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to lunch, the Lord would not accept their invitations. He would reply, "I have already been invited somewhere else."**
