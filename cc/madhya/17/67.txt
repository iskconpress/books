====== CC Madhya 17.67 ======

===== Text =====

> nirantara premāveśe nirjane gamana
> sukha anubhavi' prabhu kahena vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nirjane]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:a:anubhavi']]---feeling happiness; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---statement.

===== Translation =====

**While traveling in this secluded forest and feeling very happy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made the following statement.**
