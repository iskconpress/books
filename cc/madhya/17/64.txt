====== Cc. Madhya 17.64 ======

===== Text =====

> parama santoṣa prabhura vanya-bhojane
> mahā-sukha pāna, ye dina rahena nirjane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]]---very much; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vanya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---in eating vegetables collected from the forest; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---gets great happiness; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---on which day; [[synonyms:r:rahena]]---stays; [[synonyms:n:nirjane]]---in a solitary place.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord was always very happy to eat these forest vegetables, and He was even happier when He had an opportunity to stay in a solitary place.**
