====== CC Madhya 17.43 ======

===== Text =====

> kautuka dekhiyā prabhu hāsite lāgilā
> tā-sabāke tāhāṅ chāḍi' āge cali' gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kautuka]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing this fun; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hāsite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to smile; [[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāke]]---all of them; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving there; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---forward; [[synonyms:c:cali']] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---advanced.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw all this fun, He began to smile. Finally He left the animals and continued on His way.**
