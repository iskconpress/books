====== Cc. Madhya 17.215 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ śārī kahe śuke kari' parihāsa
> tāhā śuni' prabhura haila vismaya-premollāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ś:śārī]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the female parrot said; [[synonyms:ś:śuke]]---unto the male parrot; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]]---jokingly; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing that; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---awakening of ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Then the female parrot began to speak jokingly to śuka, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was struck with wonderful ecstatic love to hear her speak.**
