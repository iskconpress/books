====== CC Madhya 17.180 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi purī dekhi' tāṅra 'vaiṣṇava,-ācāra
> 'śiṣya' kari' tāṅra bhikṣā kaila aṅgīkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---behavior like a Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting him as his disciple; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---food offered by him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---accepted.

===== Translation =====

**Although the brāhmaṇa belonged to the Sanoḍiyā community, Śrīla Mādhavendra Purī saw that he behaved like a Vaiṣṇava and therefore accepted him as his disciple. The food he cooked was also accepted by Mādhavendra Purī.**
