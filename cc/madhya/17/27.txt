====== Cc. Madhya 17.27 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' bhaṭṭācāryera mane haya mahā-bhaya
> prabhura pratāpe tārā eka pāśa haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---great fear; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:pratāpe]]---by the influence of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tārā]]---they; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:p:pāśa]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---stand to one side.

===== Translation =====

**Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya was very much afraid to see them, but by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's influence, all the animals stood to one side.**
