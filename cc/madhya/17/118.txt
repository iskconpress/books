====== CC Madhya 17.118 ======

===== Text =====

> yei tāṅre dekhe, sei īśvara kari' kahe
> aiche mohana-vidyā---ye dekhe se mohe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---as the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:m:mohana]]-[[synonyms:v:vidyā]]---hypnotism; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---anyone who sees; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:m:mohe]]---he becomes illusioned.

===== Translation =====

**"Whoever sees Him accepts Him as the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Since He has some mystic power by which He hypnotizes people, everyone who sees Him is illusioned.**
