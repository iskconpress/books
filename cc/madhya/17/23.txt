====== Cc. Madhya 17.23 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi sabāya kaila nivāraṇa
> nivṛtta hañā rahe sabe jāni' prabhura mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivāraṇa]]---forbidding; [[synonyms:n:nivṛtta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being restrained; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remain; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**While all the devotees were searching for the Lord, Svarūpa Dāmodara restrained them. Then everyone fell silent, knowing the mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
