====== CC Madhya 17.146 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' sei vipre ātmasātha kari'
> prāte uṭhi mathurā calilā gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ā:ātmasātha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting as His devotee; [[synonyms:p:prāte]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhi]]---rising early in the morning; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---started for Mathurā; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted that brāhmaṇa as His devotee. The next morning, rising very early, the Lord started for Mathurā.**
