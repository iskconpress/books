====== CC Madhya 17.5 ======

===== Text =====

> rātrye uṭhi' vana-pathe palāñā yāba
> ekākī yāiba, kāhoṅ saṅge nā la-iba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātrye]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---rising at night; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road to the forest; [[synonyms:p:palāñā]] [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---I shall go away secretly; [[synonyms:e:ekākī]] [[synonyms:y:yāiba]]---I shall go alone; [[synonyms:k:kāhoṅ]]---anyone; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Me; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iba]]---I shall not take.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I shall leave early in the morning and go incognito, taking the road to the forest. I shall go alone and not take anyone with Me.**
