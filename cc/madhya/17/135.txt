====== CC Madhya 17.135 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nāma, kṛṣṇa-guṇa, kṛṣṇa-līlā-vṛnda
> kṛṣṇera svarūpa-sama---saba cid-ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the transcendental qualities of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---the transcendental pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Kṛṣṇa's personality; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---spiritual and full of bliss.

===== Translation =====

**"The holy name of Kṛṣṇa, His transcendental qualities and pastimes as well as Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself are all equal. They are all spiritual and full of bliss.**
