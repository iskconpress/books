====== Cc. Madhya 17.160 ======

===== Text =====

> loka 'hari' 'hari' bale, kolāhala haila
> 'keśava'-sevaka prabhuke mālā parāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---began to chant the holy names Hari, Hari; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was a great uproar; [[synonyms:k:keśava]]-[[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the priest in the service of Lord Keśava; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mālā]] [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---offered a garland.

===== Translation =====

**All the people then began to chant "Hari! Hari!" and there was a great uproar. The priest in Lord Keśava's service offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu a garland.**
