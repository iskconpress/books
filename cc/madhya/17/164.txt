====== CC Madhya 17.164 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu sei brāhmaṇe lañā
> tāṅhāre puchilā kichu nibhṛte vasiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after that; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇe]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]] [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting in a solitary place.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took aside the brāhmaṇa. Sitting in a solitary place, the Lord began to question him.**
