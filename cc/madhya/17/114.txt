====== CC Madhya 17.114 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhile se jāni tāṅra 'īśvarera rīti'
> alaukika kathā śuni' ke kare pratīti?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhile]]---simply by seeing; [[synonyms:s:se]]---Him; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I understand; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]] [[synonyms:r:rīti]]---characteristics of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---story; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:pratīti]]---will believe.

===== Translation =====

**"Simply by seeing Him, one understands that He possesses all the characteristics of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Such characteristics are certainly uncommon. Who will believe it?"**
