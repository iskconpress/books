====== CC Madhya 17.30 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dine mahāprabhu kare nadī snāna
> matta-hasti-yūtha āila karite jala-pāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---another day; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nadī]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing in the river; [[synonyms:m:matta]]-[[synonyms:h:hasti]]-[[synonyms:y:yūtha]]---a herd of maddened elephants; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drinking water.

===== Translation =====

**Another day, while Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was bathing in a river, a herd of maddened elephants came there to drink water.**
