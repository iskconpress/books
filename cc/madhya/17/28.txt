====== CC Madhya 17.28 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina pathe vyāghra kariyāche śayana
> āveśe tāra gāye prabhura lāgila caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the path; [[synonyms:v:vyāghra]]---a tiger; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---was lying down; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:g:gāye]]---on his body; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---touched; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**One day a tiger was lying on the path, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, walking along the path in ecstatic love, touched the tiger with His feet.**
