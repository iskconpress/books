====== CC Madhya 17.194 ======

===== Text =====

> pathe gābhī-ghaṭā care prabhure dekhiyā
> prabhuke beḍaya āsi' huṅkāra kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:g:gābhī]]-[[synonyms:g:ghaṭā]]---groups of cows; [[synonyms:c:care]]---graze; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---after seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]] [[synonyms:b:beḍaya]]---they surrounded the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:h:huṅ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making a loud vibration.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed through Vṛndāvana, herds of grazing cows saw Him pass and, immediately surrounding Him, began to moo very loudly.**
