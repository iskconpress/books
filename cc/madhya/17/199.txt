====== CC Madhya 17.199 ======

===== Text =====

> śuka, pika, bhṛṅga prabhure dekhi' 'pañcama' gāya
> śikhi-gaṇa nṛtya kari' prabhu-āge yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuka]]---parrots; [[synonyms:p:pika]]---cuckoos; [[synonyms:b:bhṛṅga]]---bumblebees; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:pañcama]]---the fifth musical note; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sing; [[synonyms:ś:śikhi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---peacocks; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---go.

===== Translation =====

**Bumblebees and birds like the parrot and cuckoo all began to sing loudly on the fifth note, and the peacocks began to dance in front of the Lord.**
