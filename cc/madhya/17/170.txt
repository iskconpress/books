====== Cc. Madhya 17.170 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"tumi 'guru', āmi 'śiṣya'-prāya
> 'guru' hañā 'śiṣye' namaskāra nā yuyāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---My spiritual master; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---like your disciple; [[synonyms:g:guru]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being the spiritual master; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣye]]---unto the disciple; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---is not befitting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You are on the platform of My spiritual master, and I am your disciple. Since you are My spiritual master, it is not befitting that you offer Me obeisances."**
