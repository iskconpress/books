====== CC Madhya 17.94 ======

===== Text =====

> candraśekhara kahe,---"prabhu, baḍa kṛpā kailā
> āpane āsiyā bhṛtye daraśana dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:candraśekhara]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Candraśekhara said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my dear Lord; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---You have shown Your causeless mercy; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtye]]---unto Your servant; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave Your audience.

===== Translation =====

**Candraśekhara said, "My dear Lord, You bestowed Your causeless mercy upon me because I am Your old servant. Indeed, You have come here personally to give me Your audience.**
