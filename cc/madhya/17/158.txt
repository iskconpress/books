====== CC Madhya 17.158 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-vipra paḍe prabhura caraṇa dhariyā
> prabhu-saṅge nṛtya kare premāviṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---one //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---falls down; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---he dances; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being absorbed in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**One brāhmaṇa fell at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and then began to dance with Him in ecstatic love.**
