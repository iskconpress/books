====== CC Madhya 17.55 ======

===== Text =====

> vana dekhi' bhrama haya---ei 'vṛndāvana'
> śaila dekhi' mane haya---ei 'govardhana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vana]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the forest; [[synonyms:b:bhrama]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is illusion; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana forest; [[synonyms:ś:śaila]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing a hill; [[synonyms:m:mane]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---considers; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]---this is Govardhana Hill.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed through the Jhārikhaṇḍa forest, He took it for granted that it was Vṛndāvana. When He passed over the hills, He took it for granted that they were Govardhana.**
