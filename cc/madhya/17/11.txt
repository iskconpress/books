====== CC Madhya 17.11 ======

===== Text =====

> 'uttama brāhmaṇa' eka saṅge avaśya cāhi
> bhikṣā kari' bhikṣā dibe, yābe pātra vahi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttama]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---a high-class //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:c:cāhi]]---we want; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---collecting alms; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dibe]]---will give You food; [[synonyms:y:yābe]]---will go; [[synonyms:p:pātra]] [[synonyms:v:vahi']]---bearing Your waterpot.

===== Translation =====

**"Our Lord, please take one very nice brāhmaṇa with You. He will collect alms for You, cook for You, give You prasāda, and carry Your waterpot while traveling.**
