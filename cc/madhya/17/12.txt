====== Cc. Madhya 17.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ana-pathe yāite nāhi 'bhojyānna'-brāhmaṇa
> ājñā kara,---saṅge caluka vipra eka-jana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the forest path; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---going; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:b:bhojya]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---a //brāhmaṇa// whose food can be accepted; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---please give permission; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along; [[synonyms:c:caluka]]---may go; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---one person.

===== Translation =====

**"When You go through the jungle, there will be no brāhmaṇa available from whom You can accept lunch. Therefore please give permission for at least one pure brāhmaṇa to accompany You."**
