====== CC Madhya 17.191 ======

===== Text =====

> svayambhu, viśrāma, dīrgha-viṣṇu, bhūteśvara
> mahāvidyā, gokarṇādi dekhilā vistara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayambhu]]---Svayambhu; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---Viśrāma; [[synonyms:d:dīrgha]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]---Dīrgha Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:b:bhūteśvara]]---Bhūteśvara; [[synonyms:m:mahāvidyā]]---Mahāvidyā; [[synonyms:g:gokarṇa]]---Gokarṇa; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and so on; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---saw; [[synonyms:v:vistara]]---many.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited all the holy places on the banks of the Yamunā, including Svayambhu, Viśrāma-ghāṭa, Dīrgha Viṣṇu, Bhūteśvara, Mahāvidyā and Gokarṇa.**
