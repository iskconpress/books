====== CC Madhya 17.58 ======

===== Text =====

> ye-grāme rahena prabhu, tathāya brāhmaṇa
> pāṅca-sāta jana āsi' kare nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in whatever village; [[synonyms:r:rahena]]---stays; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tathāya]]---there; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---//brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---five or seven persons; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**Whenever Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited a village, a few brāhmaṇas-five or seven-would come and extend invitations to the Lord.**
