====== Cc. Madhya 17.90 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā kari' mahāprabhu karilā śayana
> miśra-putra raghu kare pāda-samvāhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after finishing His lunch; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---took rest; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---the son of Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:r:raghu]]---Raghu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:samvāhana]]---massaging the legs.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His rest after lunch, the son of Tapana Miśra, named Raghu, used to massage His legs.**
