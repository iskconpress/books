====== CC Madhya 17.218 ======

===== Text =====

> mayūrera kaṇṭha dekhi' prabhura kṛṣṇa-smṛti haila
> premāveśe mahāprabhu bhūmite paḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mayūrera]]---of the peacocks; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭha]]---necks; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:smṛti]]---remembrance of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell down.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord saw the bluish necks of the peacocks, His remembrance of Kṛṣṇa immediately awakened, and He fell to the ground in ecstatic love.**
