====== Cc. Madhya 17.202 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu dekhi' vṛndāvanera sthāvara-jaṅgama
> ānandita---bandhu yena dekhe bandhu-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanera]]---of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:sthāvara]]-[[synonyms:j:jaṅgama]]---all living entities, moving and not moving; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very jubilant; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]---friend; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---see; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---friends.

===== Translation =====

**Thus all the moving and nonmoving living entities of Vṛndāvana became very jubilant to see the Lord. It was as if friends were made happy by seeing another friend.**
