====== Cc. Madhya 17.222 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṇṭaka-durgama vane aṅga kṣata haila
> bhaṭṭācārya kole kari' prabhure sustha kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṇṭaka]]-[[synonyms:d:durgama]]---difficult to traverse because of thorns; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the body; [[synonyms:k:kṣata]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became injured; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kole]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking Him on his lap; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sustha]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---pacified.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord rolled on the ground, sharp thorns injured His body. Taking Him on his lap, Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya pacified Him.**
