====== Cc. Madhya 5.156 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' prabhu āge calilā śīghra-gati
> bujhite nā pāre keha dui prabhura mati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of the other devotees; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---began to go; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]-[[synonyms:g:gati]]---very swiftly; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---able; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lords; [[synonyms:m:mati]]---intentions.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then began to walk very swiftly before all the other devotees. No one could understand the real purpose of the two Lords, Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu.**
