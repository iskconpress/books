====== CC Madhya 5.134 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-mukhe śuni' gopāla-carita
> tuṣṭa hailā mahāprabhu svabhakta-sahita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:c:carita]]---the narration of Gopāla; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very pleased; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with His devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard the narration of Gopāla's activities. Both He and His personal devotees became very pleased.**
