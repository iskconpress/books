====== Cc. Madhya 5.106 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bhāvi' sei vipra phiriyā cāhila
> hāsiñā gopāla-deva tathāya rahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bhāvi']]---thinking like this; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:phiriyā]]---turning; [[synonyms:c:cāhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:h:hāsiñā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---Lord Gopāladeva, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tathāya]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---stayed.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking this, the brāhmaṇa turned to look back, and He saw that Gopāla, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, was standing there smiling.**
