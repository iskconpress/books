====== Cc. Madhya 5.118 ======

===== Text =====

> mandira kariyā rājā sevā cālāila
> 'sākṣi-gopāla' bali' tāṅra nāma khyāti haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mandira]]---a temple; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---constructing; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:c:cālāila]]---regularly carried on; [[synonyms:s:sākṣi]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---by the name Sākṣi-gopāla; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---known as; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:k:khyāti]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was.

===== Translation =====

**The King constructed a nice temple, and regular service was executed. Gopāla became very famous under the name of Sākṣi-gopāla [the witness Gopāla].**
