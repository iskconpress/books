====== Cc. Madhya 5.87 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei choṭa-vipra gelā vṛndāvana
> daṇḍavat kari' kahe saba vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after this; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---young //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after offering respects; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---tells; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---the description.

===== Translation =====

**After the meeting, the young brāhmaṇa started for Vṛndāvana. Upon arriving there, he first offered his respectful obeisances to the Deity and then narrated everything in full detail.**
