====== CC Madhya 5.18 ======

===== Text =====

> vipra bale---tumi mora bahu sevā kailā
> sahāya hañā more tīrtha karāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viprabale]]---the elderly //brāhmaṇa// says; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---various; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---have rendered; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]---assistant; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to me; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---pilgrimage; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---helped to do.

===== Translation =====

**The older man told the younger, "You have rendered various types of service to me. You have assisted me in traveling to all these places of pilgrimage.**
