====== CC Madhya 5.148 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānande kahe prabhu,---deha mora daṇḍa
> nityānanda bale,---daṇḍa haila tina khaṇḍa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānande]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---asks; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---give; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---//sannyāsa// staff; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---Śrī Nityānanda replies; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---Your //sannyāsa// staff; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---divided into three parts.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu had thus regained external consciousness, He asked Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, "Please return My staff." Nityānanda Prabhu then replied, "It has been broken into three parts."**
