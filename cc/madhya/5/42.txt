====== Cc. Madhya 5.42 ======

===== Text =====

> vipra bale,---"sākṣī bolāñā karibeka nyāya
> jiti' kanyā labe, mora vyartha dharma haya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viprabale]]---the //brāhmaṇa// says; [[synonyms:s:sākṣī]]---a witness; [[synonyms:b:bolāñā]]---calling for; [[synonyms:k:karibeka]]---there will be; [[synonyms:n:nyāya]]---justice; [[synonyms:j:jiti']]---winning; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---the daughter; [[synonyms:l:labe]]---he will take; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:v:vyartha]]---meaningless; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---will be.

===== Translation =====

**The elderly brāhmaṇa said, "If I do not give my daughter to the young brāhmaṇa, he will call Śrī Gopālajī as a witness. Thus he will take my daughter by force, and in that case my religious principles will become meaningless."**
