====== CC Madhya 5.109 ======

===== Text =====

> āila sakala loka sākṣī dekhibāre
> gopāla dekhiñā loka daṇḍavat kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people; [[synonyms:s:sākṣī]]---the witness; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:d:dekhiñā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do.

===== Translation =====

**All the townspeople went to see the witness Gopāla, and when they saw the Lord actually standing there, they all offered their respectful obeisances.**
