====== Cc. Madhya 5.53 ======

===== Text =====

> ṭheñā dekhi' sei vipra palāñā gela
> āra dina grāmera loka ekatra karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭheñā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the stick in his hand; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the young //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:palāñā]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---fled from that place; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the inhabitants of the village; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---gathered in one place.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing a stick in the hand of the son, the younger brāhmaṇa fled. The next day, however, he gathered together all the people of the village.**
