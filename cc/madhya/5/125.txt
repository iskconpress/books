====== Cc. Madhya 5.125 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra mahiṣī āilā gopāla-darśane
> bhakti kari' bahu alaṅkāra kaila samarpaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāramahiṣī]]---his Queen; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:d:darśane]]---to see the Gopāla Deity; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in great devotion; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---various; [[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---of ornaments; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇe]]---presentation.

===== Translation =====

**When the Gopāla Deity was installed at Kaṭaka, the Queen of Puruṣottama-deva went to see Him and, with great devotion, presented various kinds of ornaments.**
