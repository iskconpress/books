====== Cc. Madhya 5.7 ======

===== Text =====

> sei rātri tāhāṅ rahi' bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> gopālera pūrva-kathā śune bahu raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---that night; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the other devotees; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---previous narration; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**That night Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed in the temple of Gopāla, and along with all the devotees, He heard the narration of the witness Gopāla with great pleasure.**
