====== CC Madhya 5.4 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛtya-gīta kaila preme bahuta stavana
> yājapure se rātri karilā yāpana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---executed; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---various; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---prayers; [[synonyms:y:yājapure]]---in the village of Yājapura; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---that night; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:y:yāpana]]---passing.

===== Translation =====

**In the temple of Varāhadeva, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu engaged in chanting and dancing and offered prayers. He passed that night in the temple.**
