====== CC Madhya 5.145 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa āviṣṭa hañā, sabe nāce gāya
> premāveśe prabhu-saṅge rāja-mārge yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---ecstatic; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---absorbed in love of God; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the pathway; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees became ecstatic in the association of Lord Caitanya, and thus absorbed in love of God, they were dancing and singing while going along the main road.**
