====== CC Madhya 5.121 ======

===== Text =====

> sei rājā jini' nila tāṅra siṁhāsana
> 'māṇikya-siṁhāsana' nāma aneka ratana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seirājā]]---that King (Mahārāja Puruṣottama-deva); [[synonyms:j:jini']]---conquering; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---the throne; [[synonyms:m:māṇikya]]-[[synonyms:s:siṁhāsana]]---the throne known as Māṇikya-siṁhāsana; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---various; [[synonyms:r:ratana]]---bedecked with jewels.

===== Translation =====

**That King was victorious over the King of Vidyānagara, and he took possession of his throne, the Māṇikya-siṁhāsana, which was bedecked with many jewels.**
