====== CC Madhya 5.103 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate cali' vipra nija-deśe āilā
> grāmera nikaṭa āsi' manete cintilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---walking; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---own; [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to the country; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---to the village; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:m:manete]]---within his mind; [[synonyms:c:cintilā]]---thought.

===== Translation =====

**The young brāhmaṇa walked and walked in this way until he eventually arrived in his own country. When he neared his own village, he began to think as follows.**
