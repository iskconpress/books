====== Cc. Madhya 5.8 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-gosāñi yabe tīrtha bhramilā
> sākṣi-gopāla dekhibāre kaṭaka āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]] [[synonyms:b:bhramilā]]---traveled to the places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:s:sākṣi]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the witness Gopāla; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:k:kaṭaka]]---to the town of Kaṭaka; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**Previously, when Nityānanda Prabhu had toured all over India to see different places of pilgrimage, He also had come to see Sākṣi-gopāla at Kaṭaka.**
