====== CC Madhya 5.15 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla-saundarya duṅhāra mana nila hari'
> sukha pāñā rahe tāhāṅ dina dui-cāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---the beauty of the Gopāla Deity; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took away; [[synonyms:h:hari']]---carrying; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---feeling this transcendental happiness; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---in that temple; [[synonyms:d:dina]]---days; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:c:cāri]]---two or four.

===== Translation =====

**The beauty of the Gopāla Deity stole away their minds, and feeling great happiness, they remained there for two or four days.**
