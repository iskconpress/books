====== CC Madhya 5.49 ======

===== Text =====

> āsiñā parama-bhaktye namaskāra kari'
> vinaya kariñā kahe kara dui yuḍi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsiñā]]---coming; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---in great devotion; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---with great humility; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---hands; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:y:yuḍi']]---folding.

===== Translation =====

**The young brāhmaṇa came to him and offered respectful obeisances. Then, very humbly folding his hands, he spoke as follows.**
