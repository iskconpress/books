====== CC Madhya 5.3 ======

===== Text =====

> calite calite āilā yājapura-grāma
> varāha-ṭhākura dekhi' karilā praṇāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:calitecalite]]---walking on and on; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---reached; [[synonyms:y:yājapura]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]---the village of Yājapura-grāma; [[synonyms:v:varāha]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---the temple of Varāhadeva; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---offered; [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Walking and walking, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His party finally arrived at Yājapura on the river Vaitaraṇī. There He saw the temple of Varāhadeva and offered His obeisances unto Him.**
