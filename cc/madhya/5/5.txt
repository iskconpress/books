====== CC Madhya 5.5 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṭake āilā sākṣi-gopāla dekhite
> gopāla-saundarya dekhi' hailā ānandite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṭake]]---in the town of Kaṭaka (Cuttak); [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:s:sākṣi]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the witness Gopāla; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---of the Deity of Gopāla; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:ā:ānandite]]---very pleased.

===== Translation =====

**Afterwards, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the town of Kaṭaka to see the temple of the witness Gopāla. When He saw the Deity of Gopāla, He was very much pleased with His beauty.**
