====== Cc. Madhya 5.13 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvane govinda-sthāne mahā-devālaya
> se mandire gopālera mahā-sevā haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---within Pañcakrośī Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place where the present Govinda temple is situated; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaya]]---a great temple; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:m:mandire]]---in that temple; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of the Deity of Gopāla; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---gorgeous worship; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**In the village of Pañcakrośī Vṛndāvana, at the site where the Govinda temple is now situated, there was a great temple where gorgeous worship of Gopāla was performed.**
