====== CC Madhya 5.131 ======

===== Text =====

> svapne dekhi' sei rāṇī rājāke kahila
> rājā-saha muktā lañā mandire āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svapne]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the dream; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rāṇī]]---the Queen; [[synonyms:r:rājāke]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---spoke; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with the King; [[synonyms:m:muktā]]---the pearl; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to the temple; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---they went.

===== Translation =====

**After dreaming this, the Queen explained it to her husband, the King. Both the King and the Queen then went to the temple with the pearl.**
