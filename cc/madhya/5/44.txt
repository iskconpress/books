====== CC Madhya 5.44 ======

===== Text =====

> nāhi kahi---nā kahio e mithyā-vacana
> sabe kahibe---'mora kichu nāhika smaraṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāhikahi]]---I did not say; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahio]]---do not say; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mithyā]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---false statement; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---only; [[synonyms:k:kahibe]]---you shall say; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---not; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]---remembrance.

===== Translation =====

**"You do not have to flatly deny that you spoke such a thing. There is no need to make a false statement. Simply say that you do not remember what you said.**
