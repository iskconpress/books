====== Cc. Madhya 5.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaitacandra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---to the devotees of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu! All glories to Śrī Advaita Prabhu! And all glories to the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu!**
