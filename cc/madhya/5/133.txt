====== CC Madhya 5.133 ======

===== Text =====

> sei haite gopālera kaṭakete sthiti
> ei lāgi 'sākṣi-gopāla' nāma haila khyāti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seihaite]]---since that time; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of Gopāla; [[synonyms:k:kaṭakete]]---in the town of Kaṭaka; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---the establishment; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi]]---for this reason; [[synonyms:s:sākṣi]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the witness Gopāla; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:k:khyāti]]---celebrated.

===== Translation =====

**Since then, Gopāla has been situated in the city of Kaṭaka [Cuttak], and He has been known ever since as Sākṣi-gopāla.**
