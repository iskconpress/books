====== Cc. Madhya 5.41 ======

===== Text =====

> jñāti loka kahe,---'morā tomāke chāḍiba'
> strī-putra kahe,---'viṣa khāiyā mariba'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jñāti]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the relatives; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---answer; [[synonyms:m:morā]]---we all; [[synonyms:t:tomāke]]---you; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiba]]---will give up; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---wife; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---sons; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:v:viṣa]]---poison; [[synonyms:k:khāiyā]]---drinking; [[synonyms:m:mariba]]---we shall die.

===== Translation =====

**The relatives unanimously said, "If you give your daughter to that boy, we shall give up all connection with you." Indeed, his wife and sons declared, "If such a thing happens, we shall take poison and die."**
