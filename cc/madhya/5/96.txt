====== CC Madhya 5.96 ======

===== Text =====

> pratimā naha tumi---sākṣāt vrajendra-nandana
> vipra lāgi' kara tumi akārya-karaṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratimā]]---a statue; [[synonyms:n:naha]]---are not; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:v:vipra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the sake of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You can do; [[synonyms:a:akārya]]-[[synonyms:k:karaṇa]]---an action You have never done before.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You are not a statue; You are directly the son of Mahārāja Nanda. Now, for the sake of the old brāhmaṇa, You can do something You have never done before."**
