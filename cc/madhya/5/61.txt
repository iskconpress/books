====== Cc. Madhya 5.61 ======

===== Text =====

> saba dhana lañā kahe---'core la-ila dhana'
> 'kanyā dite cāhiyāche'---uṭhāila vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---money; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:c:core]]---a thief; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---took; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---all the money; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---the daughter; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give in charity; [[synonyms:c:cāhiyāche]]---has promised; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāila]]---has raised; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---a slogan.

===== Translation =====

**"Having taken all my father's money, this rogue claimed that it was taken by some thief. Now he is claiming that my father has promised to give him his daughter in charity.**
