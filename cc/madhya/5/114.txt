====== CC Madhya 5.114 ======

===== Text =====

> duṅhāra satye tuṣṭa ha-ilāṅ, duṅhe māga' vara"
> dui-vipra vara māge ānanda-antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]] [[synonyms:s:satye]]---in the truthfulness of both of you; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilāṅ]]---I have be come satisfied; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of you; [[synonyms:m:māga']]---ask; [[synonyms:v:vara]]---some benediction; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---both the //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:v:vara]]---a benediction; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---beg; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleased; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---within.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord continued, "I have become very pleased by the truthfulness of you both. Now you can ask for a benediction." Thus with great pleasure the two brāhmaṇas begged for a benediction.**
