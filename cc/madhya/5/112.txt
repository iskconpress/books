====== CC Madhya 5.112 ======

===== Text =====

> sakala lokera āge gopāla sākṣī dila
> baḍa-vipra choṭa-vipre kanyā-dāna kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in the presence; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:s:sākṣī]]---witness; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the elderly //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vipre]]---unto the young //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]---giving the daughter in charity; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did.

===== Translation =====

**Thus in the presence of all the townspeople, Lord Gopāla bore witness that the elderly brāhmaṇa had offered his daughter in charity to the young brāhmaṇa.**
