====== CC Madhya 5.110 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla-saundarya dekhi' loke ānandita
> pratimā caliñā āilā,---śuniñā vismita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---of Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---everyone; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:p:pratimā]]---the Deity; [[synonyms:c:caliñā]]---walking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:ś:śuniñā]]---hearing this; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---surprised.

===== Translation =====

**When the people arrived, they were very pleased to see the beauty of Gopāla, and when they heard that He had actually walked there, they were all surprised.**
