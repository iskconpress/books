====== CC Madhya 5.123 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra bhakti-vaśe gopāla tāṅre ājñā dila
> gopāla la-iyā sei kaṭake āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśe]]---under the obligation of the devotional service; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave the order; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Gopāla Deity; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---taking; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that King; [[synonyms:k:kaṭake]]---to the city of Kaṭaka; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---returned.

===== Translation =====

**When the King begged Him to come to his kingdom, Gopāla, who was already obliged for his devotional service, accepted his prayer. Thus the King took the Gopāla Deity and went back to Kaṭaka.**
