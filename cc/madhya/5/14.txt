====== CC Madhya 5.14 ======

===== Text =====

> keśī-tīrtha, kālīya-hradādike kaila snāna
> śrī-gopāla dekhi' tāhāṅ karilā viśrāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keśī]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---the bathing place on the bank of the Yamunā known as Keśī-ghāṭa; [[synonyms:k:kālīya]]-[[synonyms:h:hrada]]---the bathing place on the bank of the Yamunā known as Kāliya-ghāṭa; [[synonyms:ā:ādike]]---in such different bathing places; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by visiting the temple of Gopāla; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---took; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]]---rest.

===== Translation =====

**After taking baths at different bathing places along the river Yamunā, such as Keśī-ghāṭa and Kāliya-ghāṭa, the pilgrims visited the temple of Gopāla. Afterwards, they took rest in that temple.**
