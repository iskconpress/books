====== CC Madhya 5.10 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve vidyānagarera dui ta' brāhmaṇa
> tīrtha karibāre duṅhe karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:v:vidyānagarera]]---of the town known as Vidyānagara; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---//brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]] [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to tour places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---began; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---journey.

===== Translation =====

**Formerly at Vidyānagara in South India there were two brāhmaṇas who made a long tour to see different places of pilgrimage.**
