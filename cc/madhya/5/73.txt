====== CC Madhya 5.73 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe iṅho gopālera āgete kahila
> tumi jāna, ei vipre kanyā āmi dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---this gentleman; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of the Gopāla Deity; [[synonyms:ā:āgete]]---in front; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---he said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:j:jāna]]---my Lord, please know; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---unto this young //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---my daughter; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---have offered.

===== Translation =====

**"Then this gentleman said in front of the Gopāla Deity, 'My dear Lord, please bear witness. I have offered my daughter to this brāhmaṇa in charity.'**
