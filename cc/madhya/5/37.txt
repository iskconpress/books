====== CC Madhya 5.37 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina nija-loka ekatra karila
> tā-sabāra āge saba vṛttānta kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---all his relatives; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---in one place; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---assembled; [[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]]---narration; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---spoke.

===== Translation =====

**Thus one day the elderly brāhmaṇa called for a meeting of all his relatives and friends, and before them all he narrated what had taken place in front of Gopāla.**
