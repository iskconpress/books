====== CC Madhya 5.36 ======

===== Text =====

> tīrthe vipre vākya diluṅ,---kemate satya haya
> strī, putra, jñāti, bandhu jānibe niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tīrthe]]---on pilgrimage; [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---to a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---word of honor; [[synonyms:d:diluṅ]]---I have given; [[synonyms:k:kemate]]---how; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---true; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it is; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---wife; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---sons; [[synonyms:j:jñāti]]---relatives; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]---friends; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---will know; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---certainly.

===== Translation =====

**He began to think, "I have given my word to a brāhmaṇa in a holy place, and what I promised will certainly come to pass. I must now disclose this to my wife, sons, other relatives and friends."**
