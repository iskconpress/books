====== CC Madhya 5.88 ======

===== Text =====

> "brahmaṇya-deva tumi baḍa dayā-maya
> dui viprera dharma rākha hañā sadaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmaṇya]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---O Lord of brahminical culture; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religious principles; [[synonyms:r:rākha]]---protect; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:daya]]---merciful.

===== Translation =====

**He said, "My Lord, You are the protector of brahminical culture, and You are also very merciful. Therefore, kindly show Your great mercy by protecting the religious principles of us two brāhmaṇas.**
