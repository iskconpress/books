====== Cc. Madhya 5.70 ======

===== Text =====

> kanyā dite nāribe, habe asatya-vacana
> punarapi kahe vipra kariyā yatana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---daughter; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:n:nāribe]]---you will not be able; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---it will become; [[synonyms:a:asatya]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---a false statement; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he says; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:y:yatana]]---with great attention.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'My dear sir, you will not be able to fulfill your promise. Your promise will be broken.' Yet, again and again the brāhmaṇa emphasized his promise.**
