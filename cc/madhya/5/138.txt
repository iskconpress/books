====== Cc. Madhya 5.138 ======

===== Text =====

> duṅhā dekhi' nityānanda-prabhu mahā-raṅge
> ṭhārāṭhāri kari' hāse bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṅhā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing both of Them; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in great jubilation; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhārāṭhāri]]---indication; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laughs; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with the other devotees.

===== Translation =====

**When Nityānanda saw both the Gopāla Deity and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in that way, He began to exchange remarks with the devotees, all of whom were smiling.**
