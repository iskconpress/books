====== CC Madhya 5.39 ======

===== Text =====

> nīce kanyā dile kula yāibeka nāśa
> śuniñā sakala loka karibe upahāsa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīce]]---to a lower family; [[synonyms:k:kanyā]]---daughter; [[synonyms:d:dile]]---if offering; [[synonyms:k:kula]]---family tradition; [[synonyms:y:yāibeka]]---will go to; [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destruction; [[synonyms:ś:śuniñā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---friends; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---will do; [[synonyms:u:upahāsa]]---joking.

===== Translation =====

**They unanimously agreed, "If you offer your daughter to a degraded family, your aristocracy will be lost. When people hear of this, they will make jokes and laugh at you."**
