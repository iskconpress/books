====== Cc. Madhya 5.58 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' tāṅra putra vākya-cchala pāñā
> pragalbha ha-iyā kahe sammukhe āsiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---his son; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]-[[synonyms:c:cchala]]---for jugglery of words; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting a chance; [[synonyms:p:pragalbha]]---impudent; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:s:sammukhe]]---in the front; [[synonyms:ā:āsiñā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**When the elderly brāhmaṇa's son heard this, he took the opportunity to juggle some words. Becoming very impudent, he stood before the assembly and spoke as follows.**
