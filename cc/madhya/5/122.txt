====== CC Madhya 5.122 ======

===== Text =====

> puruṣottama-deva sei baḍa bhakta ārya
> gopāla-caraṇe māge,---'cala mora rājya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---King Puruṣottama-deva; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very great; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---Āryan; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Gopāla; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---begs; [[synonyms:c:cala]]---please come; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:r:rājya]]---to the kingdom.

===== Translation =====

**King Puruṣottama-deva was a great devotee and was advanced in the civilization of the Āryans. He begged at the lotus feet of Gopāla, "Please come to my kingdom."**
