====== CC Madhya 5.6 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe nṛtya-gīta kaila kata-kṣaṇa
> āviṣṭa hañā kaila gopāla stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstasy of love of God; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being overwhelmed; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]] [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---prayers to Gopāla.

===== Translation =====

**While there, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu engaged in chanting and dancing for some time, and being overwhelmed, He offered many prayers to Gopāla.**
