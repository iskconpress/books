====== Cc. Madhya 5.35 ======

===== Text =====

> deśe āsi' dui-jane gelā nija-ghare
> kata dine baḍa-vipra cintita antare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deśe]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---after returning to their own country; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---both of them; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to their respective homes; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---after some time; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the elderly //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:c:cintita]]---very anxious; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within.

===== Translation =====

**After returning to Vidyānagara, each brāhmaṇa went to his respective home. After some time, the elderly brāhmaṇa became very anxious.**
