====== Cc. Madhya 5.116 ======

===== Text =====

> gopāla rahilā, duṅhe karena sevana
> dekhite āilā saba deśera loka-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---stayed; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---do; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:deśera]]---of the countries; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---the people.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Gopāla stayed, and the two brāhmaṇas engaged in His service. After hearing of the incident, many people from different countries began to come to see Gopāla.**
