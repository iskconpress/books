====== CC Madhya 5.126 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra nāsāte bahu-mūlya muktā haya
> tāhā dite icchā haila, manete cintaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāranāsāte]]---on the nostril of the Queen; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---very valuable; [[synonyms:m:muktā]]---pearl; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---the desire; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:manete]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:c:cintaya]]---thinks.

===== Translation =====

**The Queen had a very valuable pearl, which she wore on her nose, and she wished to give it to Gopāla. She then began to think as follows.**
