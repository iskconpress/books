====== Cc. Madhya 5.102 ======

===== Text =====

> nūpurera dhvani śuni' ānandita mana
> uttamānna pāka kari' karāya bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nūpurera]]---of the ankle bells; [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the sounds; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very much pleased; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---first-class rice; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]---cooking; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---causes; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**While Gopāla followed the young brāhmaṇa, the tinkling sound of His ankle bells could be heard. The brāhmaṇa became very pleased, and he cooked first-class rice for Gopāla to eat.**
