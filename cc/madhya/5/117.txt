====== CC Madhya 5.117 ======

===== Text =====

> se deśera rājā āila āścarya śuniñā
> parama santoṣa pāila gopāle dekhiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:d:deśera]]---of that country; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]---about the wonder; [[synonyms:ś:śuniñā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---great; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---achieved; [[synonyms:g:gopāle]]---Gopāla; [[synonyms:d:dekhiñā]]---by seeing.

===== Translation =====

**Eventually the King of that country heard of this wonderful story, and he also came to see Gopāla and thus became very satisfied.**
