====== CC Madhya 5.46 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' viprera cintita haila mana
> ekānta-bhāve cinte vipra gopāla-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of the old //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:c:cintita]]---agitated; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:e:ekānta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---with single-minded attention; [[synonyms:c:cinte]]---thinks; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---of the lotus feet of Śrī Gopālajī.

===== Translation =====

**When the elderly brāhmaṇa heard this, his mind became very agitated. Feeling helpless, he simply turned his attention to the lotus feet of Gopāla.**
