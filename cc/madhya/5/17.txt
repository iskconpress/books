====== CC Madhya 5.17 ======

===== Text =====

> choṭa-vipra kare sadā tāṅhāra sevana
> tāṅhāra sevāya viprera tuṣṭa haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the younger //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his (the old //brāhmaṇa's//); [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sevāya]]---by the service; [[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of the old //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]]---pacified; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, the young brāhmaṇa always rendered service to the older one, and the old man, being very satisfied with his service, was pleased with him.**
