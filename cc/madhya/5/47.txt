====== CC Madhya 5.47 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mora dharma rakṣā pāya, nā mare nija-jana
> dui rakṣā kara, gopāla, lainu śaraṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:r:rakṣā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---spared; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mare]]---die; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---own kinsmen; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:r:rakṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You kindly protect; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---my Lord Gopāla; [[synonyms:l:lainu]]---I have taken; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]---shelter under Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**The elderly brāhmaṇa prayed, "My dear Lord Gopāla, I have taken shelter of Your lotus feet, and therefore I request You to please protect my religious principles from disturbance and at the same time save my kinsmen from dying."**
