====== Cc. Madhya 12.88 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛṇa, dhūli, jhiṅkura, saba ekatra kariyā
> bahirvāse lañā phelāya bāhira kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]---straws; [[synonyms:d:dhūli]]---dust; [[synonyms:j:jhiṅkura]]---grains of sand; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---in one place; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---combining; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāse]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking on His personal cloth; [[synonyms:p:phelāya]]---throws; [[synonyms:b:bāhira]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---outside.

===== Translation =====

**After Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu collected all the straw, dust and grains of sand in one place, He gathered it all in His cloth and threw it outside.**
