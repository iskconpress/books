====== CC Madhya 12.64 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa' kahe, nāce, karaye rodana
> tāṅra bhāgya dekhi' ślāghā kare bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---O Kṛṣṇa, O Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---chants; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---does; [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---crying; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---fortune; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:ś:ślāghā]]---praise; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**The boy began to cry and dance, and he chanted, "Kṛṣṇa! Kṛṣṇa!" Upon seeing his bodily symptoms and his chanting and dancing, all the devotees praised him for his great spiritual fortune.**
