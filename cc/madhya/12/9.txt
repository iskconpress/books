====== Cc. Madhya 12.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅ-sabāra prasāde mile śrī-prabhura pāya
> prabhu-kṛpā vinā mora rājya nāhi bhāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by the mercy of all of them; [[synonyms:m:mile]]---one gets; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy of the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:r:rājya]]---kingdom; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---appeal to me.

===== Translation =====

**"By the mercy of all the devotees, one can attain the shelter of the lotus feet of the Lord. Without His mercy, my kingdom does not appeal to me.**
