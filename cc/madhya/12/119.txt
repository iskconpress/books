====== CC Madhya 12.119 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prakṣālana kaila śrī-jagamohana
> bhoga-mandira-ādi tabe kaila prakṣālana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:j:jagamohana]]---in front of the temple; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]-[[synonyms:m:mandira]]---the place where food is offered; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---all such places; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washed.

===== Translation =====

**They washed the Jagamohana area and then the place where food was kept. All other places were also washed.**
