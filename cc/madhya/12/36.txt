====== CC Madhya 12.36 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe nityānanda-gosāñi govindera pāśa
> māgiyā la-ila prabhura eka bahirvāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:g:govindera]] [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---from Govinda, the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:māgiyā]]---requesting; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---took; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---outer garment.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda Prabhu then obtained an external garment used by the Lord by requesting it from Govinda.**
