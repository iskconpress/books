====== Cc. Madhya 12.8 ======

===== Text =====

> sei saba dayālu more hañā sadaya
> mora lāgi' prabhu-pade karibe vinaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of them; [[synonyms:d:dayālu]]---merciful; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:daya]]---favorably disposed; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for me; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---at the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---will do; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---humble submission.

===== Translation =====

**"If all the devotees associated with the Lord are favorably disposed toward me, they can submit my petition at the lotus feet of the Lord.**
