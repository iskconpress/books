====== CC Madhya 12.18 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda kahe,-tomāya cāhi nivedite
> nā kahile rahite nāri, kahite bhaya citte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Nityānanda said; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---unto You; [[synonyms:c:cāhi]]---we want; [[synonyms:n:nivedite]]---to submit; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahile]]---if we do not speak; [[synonyms:r:rahite]] [[synonyms:n:nāri]]---we cannot stay; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---but to speak; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]] [[synonyms:c:citte]]---we are very fearful.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu then said, "We want to tell You something. Although we cannot stay without speaking, we are still very much afraid to speak.**
