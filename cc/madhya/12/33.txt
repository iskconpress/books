====== Cc. Madhya 12.33 ======

===== Text =====

> eka yukti āche, yadi kara avadhāna
> tumi nā milileha tāṅre, rahe tāṅra prāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:y:yukti]]---one plan; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:a:avadhāna]]---You consider it; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:m:milileha]]---may not meet; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---with him; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---life.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu then submitted one suggestion for the Lord's consideration. "There is a way," He suggested, "by which You need not meet the King but which would enable the King to continue living.**
