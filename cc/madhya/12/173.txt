====== CC Madhya 12.173 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi bhāla miṣṭa-prasāda lañā
> prabhuke nivedana kare āge dāṇḍāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---first-class; [[synonyms:m:miṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---sweetmeats; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offered; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Him; [[synonyms:d:dāṇḍāñā]]---standing.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī then brought some excellent sweetmeats and, standing before the Lord, offered them to Him.**
