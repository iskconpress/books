====== CC Madhya 12.149 ======

===== Text =====

> śunitei gopālera ha-ila cetana
> 'hari' bali' nṛtya kare sarva-bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śunitei]]---upon hearing; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]]---of Śrī Gopāla; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---chanting the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---danced; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Gopāla heard the voice of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he immediately came to his senses. All the devotees then began to dance, chanting the holy name of Hari.**
