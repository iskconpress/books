====== CC Madhya 12.27 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi kon kṣudra-jīva, tomāke vidhi diba?
> āpani milibe tāṅre, tāhāo dekhiba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:k:kon]]---I am just some; [[synonyms:k:kṣudra]]-[[synonyms:j:jīva]]---insignificant living entity; [[synonyms:t:tomāke]]---unto You; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---injunction; [[synonyms:d:diba]]---I shall give; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---You; [[synonyms:m:milibe]]---will meet; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---the King; [[synonyms:t:tāhāo]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiba]]---I shall see it.

===== Translation =====

**"I am merely an insignificant jīva, so what power do I have to give directions to You? By Your own personal choice You will meet with the King. I shall see it.**
