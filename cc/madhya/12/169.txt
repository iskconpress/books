====== CC Madhya 12.169 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda beḍāya pariveśana karite
> prabhura pāte bhāla-dravya dena ācambite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:b:beḍāya]]---walks; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]]---distribution of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:pāte]]---on the plate of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]]---the first-class preparations; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---puts; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda went to distribute prasāda, and suddenly he placed all the first-class preparations on the plate of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
