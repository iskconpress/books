====== CC Madhya 12.95 ======

===== Text =====

> āra śata jana śata ghaṭe jala bhari'
> prathamei lañā āche kāla apekṣā kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---about one hundred men; [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:g:ghaṭe]]---in a hundred waterpots; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:p:prathamei]]---in the first instance; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---were; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---the time; [[synonyms:a:apekṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---awaiting.

===== Translation =====

**While the temple was being swept, about a hundred men stood ready with filled waterpots, and they simply awaited the Lord's order to throw the water from them.**
