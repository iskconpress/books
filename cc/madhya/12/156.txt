====== Cc. Madhya 12.156 ======

===== Text =====

> purī-gosāñi, mahāprabhu, bhāratī brahmānanda
> advaita-ācārya, āra prabhu-nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]] [[synonyms:b:brahmānanda]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Among the devotees present with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were Paramānanda Purī, Brahmānanda Bhāratī, Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu.**
