====== CC Madhya 12.176 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata dui-jana kare bāra-bāra
> vicitra ei dui bhaktera sneha-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---both persons (Svarūpa Dāmodara and Jagadānanda); [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:b:bāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:v:vicitra]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of devotees; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---affectionate behavior.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara and Jagadānanda again and again offered the Lord some food. Thus they behaved affectionately with the Lord. This was very, very uncommon.**
