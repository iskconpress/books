====== Cc. Madhya 12.115 ======

===== Text =====

> śata-haste karena yena kṣālana-mārjana
> pratijana-pāśe yāi' karāna śikṣana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with one hundred hands; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---He does; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:k:kṣālana]]-[[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---rinsing and washing; [[synonyms:p:pratijana]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going to the side of everyone; [[synonyms:k:karāna]] [[synonyms:ś:śikṣaṇa]]---He was teaching them.

===== Translation =====

**It appeared as though Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were cleansing and washing with a hundred hands. He approached every devotee just to teach him how to work.**
