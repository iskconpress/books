====== Cc. Madhya 12.206 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu sukhe lañā saba bhakta-gaṇa
> jagannātha-daraśane karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---for visiting Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**On this occasion, greatly happy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took all the devotees with Him and visited the Lord in the temple.**
