====== Cc. Madhya 12.196 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata dui-jane kare balābali
> vyāja-stuti kare duṅhe, yena gālāgāli

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---two persons; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:b:balābali]]---accusing and counteraccusing; [[synonyms:v:vyāja]]-[[synonyms:s:stuti]]---praise in the form of accusations; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of Them; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:g:gālāgāli]]---exchanges of ill names.

===== Translation =====

**Thus They both went on talking and praising one another, although Their praise appeared negative, for it appeared as if They exchanged ill names.**
