====== CC Madhya 12.48 ======

===== Text =====

> rājāra milane bhikṣukera dui loka nāśa
> paraloka rahu, loke kare upahāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājāramilane]]---by meeting with a king; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣukera]]---of the mendicant; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---in two worlds; [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destruction; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---spiritual world; [[synonyms:r:rahu]]---let alone; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---in this material world; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:u:upahāsa]]---joking.

===== Translation =====

**"If a mendicant meets a king, this world and the next world are both destroyed for the mendicant. Indeed, what is there to say of the next world? In this world, people will joke if a sannyāsī meets a king."**
