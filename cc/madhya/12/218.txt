====== CC Madhya 12.218 ======

===== Text =====

> madhye madhye bhoga lāge, madhye daraśana
> bhogera samaye prabhu karena kīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhye]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---at intervals; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---there were offerings of food; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhogera]] [[synonyms:s:samaye]]---at the time of offering //prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---performed congregational chanting.

===== Translation =====

**Their looking at the face of Lord Jagannātha was interrupted only when He was offered food. Afterwards they would again look upon His face. When the food was being offered to the Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His kīrtana.**
