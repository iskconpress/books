====== CC Madhya 12.151 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu kṣaṇeka viśrāma kariyā
> snāna karibāre gelā bhakta-gaṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeka]]---for some time; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---taking rest; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---for bathing; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**After taking rest, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all the devotees departed to take their baths.**
