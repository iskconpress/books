====== Cc. Madhya 12.171 ======

===== Text =====

> punarapi sei dravya kare nirīkṣaṇa
> tāṅra bhaye prabhu kichu karena bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---that very thing; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nirīkṣaṇa]]---sees carefully; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---out of fear of Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**When the food was thus delivered, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu looked at it for some time. Being afraid of Jagadānanda, He finally ate something.**
