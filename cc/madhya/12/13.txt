====== CC Madhya 12.13 ======

===== Text =====

> patrī dekhi' sabāra mane ha-ila vismaya
> prabhu-pade gajapatira eta bhakti haya!!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patrī]]---the letter; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---unto the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gajapatira]]---of the King of Orissa; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**Upon reading the letter, everyone was astonished to see that King Pratāparudra had so much devotion for the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
