====== CC Madhya 12.211 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛṣārta prabhura netra-bhramara-yugala
> gāḍha tṛṣṇāya piye kṛṣṇera vadana-kamala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛṣā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārta]]---thirsty; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:netra]]---eyes; [[synonyms:b:bhramara]]-[[synonyms:y:yugala]]---like two bumblebees; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇāya]]---in thirst; [[synonyms:p:piye]]---drinks; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vadana]]-[[synonyms:k:kamala]]---the lotuslike face.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very thirsty to see the Lord, and His eyes became like two bumblebees drinking the honey from the lotuslike eyes of Lord Jagannātha, who is Kṛṣṇa Himself.**
