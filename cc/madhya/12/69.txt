====== CC Madhya 12.69 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> nirantara krīḍā kare saṅkīrtana-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the society of His pure devotees; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs pastimes; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the course of His //saṅkīrtana// movement.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu acts in the society of His pure devotees, performing His pastimes and spreading the saṅkīrtana movement.**
