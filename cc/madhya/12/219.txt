====== CC Madhya 12.219 ======

===== Text =====

> darśana-ānande prabhu saba pāsarilā
> bhakta-gaṇa madhyāhna karite prabhure lañā gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:darśana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---because of pleasure due to seeing the face of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everything; //pāsarilā//--forgot; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]]---noontime lunch; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to accept; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---took.

===== Translation =====

**Feeling such great pleasure upon seeing the face of Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu forgot everything. The devotees, however, took Him to His lunch at noontime.**
