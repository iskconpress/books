====== Cc. Madhya 12.145 ======

===== Text =====

> āste-vyaste ācārya tāṅre kaila kole
> śvāsa-rahita dekhi' ācārya hailā vikale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---with great haste; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---took; [[synonyms:k:kole]]---on His lap; [[synonyms:ś:śvāsa]]-[[synonyms:r:rahita]]---without breathing; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:v:vikale]]---agitated.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Gopāla fainted, Advaita Ācārya hastily took him upon His lap. Seeing that he was not breathing, He became very much agitated.**
