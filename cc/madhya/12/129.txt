====== CC Madhya 12.129 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ āsi' prabhu pāya karila vinaya
> 'ajña-aparādha' kṣamā karite yuyāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---again coming back; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---made a submission; [[synonyms:a:ajña]]-[[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense by innocent person; [[synonyms:k:kṣamā]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to be excused; [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---deserves.

===== Translation =====

**After Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī returned within the temple, he requested Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to excuse that innocent person.**
