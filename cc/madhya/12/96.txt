====== CC Madhya 12.96 ======

===== Text =====

> 'jala āna' bali' yabe mahāprabhu kahila
> tabe śata ghaṭa āni' prabhu-āge dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---bring water; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---ordered; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:g:ghaṭa]]---one hundred pots; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu called for water, all the men immediately brought the hundred waterpots, which were completely filled, and delivered them before the Lord.**
