====== CC Madhya 12.37 ======

===== Text =====

> sei bahirvāsa sārvabhauma-pāśa dila
> sārvabhauma sei vastra rājāre pāṭhā'la

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---garment; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---in the care of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---cloth; [[synonyms:r:rājāre]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhā'la]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Nityānanda Prabhu delivered the old cloth to the care of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya sent it to the King.**
