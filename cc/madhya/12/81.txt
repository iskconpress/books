====== CC Madhya 12.81 ======

===== Text =====

> guṇḍicā-mandire gelā karite mārjana
> prathame mārjanī lañā karila śodhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to the temple known as Guṇḍicā; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---washing; [[synonyms:p:prathame]]---in the first instance; [[synonyms:m:mārjanī]]---the brooms; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:ś:śodhana]]---cleansing.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the Lord and His associates went to cleanse the Guṇḍicā temple. At first they cleansed the temple with the brooms.**
