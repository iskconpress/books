====== Cc. Madhya 12.214 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-mukha-sundara-kānti bāḍhe kṣaṇe kṣaṇe
> koṭi-bhakta-netra-bhṛṅga kare madhu-pāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---of His beautiful face; [[synonyms:s:sundara]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---attractive luster; [[synonyms:b:bāḍhe]]---increases; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---at every moment; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---of millions of devotees; [[synonyms:n:netra]]-[[synonyms:b:bhṛṅga]]---eyes like bumblebees; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---engaged; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāne]]---in drinking the honey.

===== Translation =====

**The luster of His beautiful face increased at every moment, and the eyes of hundreds and thousands of devotees drank its honey like bumblebees.**
