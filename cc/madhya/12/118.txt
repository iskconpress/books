====== CC Madhya 12.118 ======

===== Text =====

> e-kathā śuniyā sabe saṅkucita hañā
> bhāla-mate karma kare sabe mana diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing these words; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:s:saṅkucita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being ashamed; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---very well; [[synonyms:k:karma]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do work; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---with attention.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as they heard Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu say this, everyone became ashamed. Thus the devotees began to work with great attention.**
