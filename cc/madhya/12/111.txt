====== CC Madhya 12.111 ======

===== Text =====

> jala bhare, ghara dhoya, kare hari-dhvani
> 'kṛṣṇa' 'hari' dhvani vinā āra nāhi śuni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---they were drawing water; [[synonyms:g:ghara]] [[synonyms:d:dhoya]]---washing the rooms; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---chanting the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---vibration; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---anything else; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was not; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing.

===== Translation =====

**Some people were filling the pots, and others were washing the rooms, but everyone was engaged in chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa and Hari.**
