====== CC Madhya 12.183 ======

===== Text =====

> tārkika-śṛgāla-saṅge bheu-bheu kari
> sei mukhe ebe sadā kahi 'kṛṣṇa' 'hari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tārkika]]---logician; [[synonyms:ś:śṛgāla]]---jackals; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of; [[synonyms:b:bheu]]-[[synonyms:b:bheu]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---barking; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in that very mouth; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---speak; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hari]]---Hari.

===== Translation =====

**"In the association of the jackals known as logicians, I simply continued to bark a resounding 'bheu bheu.' Now, from the same mouth I am chanting the holy names Kṛṣṇa and Hari.**
