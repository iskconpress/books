====== Cc. Madhya 12.134 ======

===== Text =====

> praṇālikā chāḍi' yadi pāni vahāila
> nūtana nadī yena samudre milila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:praṇālikā]]---water from the outlets; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---releasing; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:p:pāni]]---water; [[synonyms:v:vahāila]]---flowed; [[synonyms:n:nūtana]]---new; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]---river; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:s:samudre]]---in the ocean; [[synonyms:m:milila]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**When the water from the different rooms was finally let out through the halls, it appeared as if new rivers were rushing out to meet the waters of the ocean.**
