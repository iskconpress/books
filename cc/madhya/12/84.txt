====== Cc. Madhya 12.84 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-dike śata bhakta sammārjanī-kare
> āpani śodhena prabhu, śikhā'na sabāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---all around; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---hundreds of; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:s:sammārjanī]]-[[synonyms:k:kare]]---do the cleansing work; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:ś:śodhena]]---cleanses; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śikhā'na]] [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---teaching all others.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, hundreds of devotees were engaged in cleansing all around the temple, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was personally carrying out the operation just to instruct others.**
