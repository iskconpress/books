====== CC Madhya 12.83 ======

===== Text =====

> choṭa-baḍa-mandira kaila mārjana-śodhana
> pāche taiche śodhila śrī-jagamohana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:b:baḍa]]-[[synonyms:m:mandira]]---all the small and big temples; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]-[[synonyms:ś:śodhana]]---proper cleansing; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---in the similar way; [[synonyms:ś:śodhila]]---cleansed; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:j:jagamohana]]---the place between the original temple and the //kīrtana// hall.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the Lord and His companions cleansed and swept all the temple's buildings, big and small, and finally cleansed the area between the temple and the kīrtana hall.**
