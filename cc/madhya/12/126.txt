====== Cc. Madhya 12.126 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara-mandire mora pada dhoyāila
> sei jala āpani lañā pāna kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---in the temple of the Lord; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---feet; [[synonyms:d:dhoyāila]]---washed; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:j:jala]]---that water; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:pāna]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---drank.

===== Translation =====

**"This person from Bengal has washed My feet within the temple of the Personality of Godhead. Not only that, but he has drunk the water himself.**
