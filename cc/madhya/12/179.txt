====== CC Madhya 12.179 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnāthācārya uttama mahā-prasāda āni'
> sārvabhaume diyā kahe sumadhura vāṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---first-class; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:m:madhura]]---very sweet; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya also brought first-class food and offered it to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya while speaking sweet words.**
