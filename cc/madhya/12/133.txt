====== CC Madhya 12.133 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata saba purī karila śodhana
> śītala, nirmala kaila-yena nija-mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:p:purī]]---all of Guṇḍicā Purī; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:ś:śodhana]]---they cleansed; [[synonyms:ś:śītala]]---cool; [[synonyms:n:nirmala]]---clean; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---his own mind.

===== Translation =====

**In this way all the quarters of the Guṇḍicā temple were completely cleansed and cleared. All quarters were cool and spotless, like one's cleansed and pacified mind.**
