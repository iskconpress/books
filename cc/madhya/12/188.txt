====== CC Madhya 12.188 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita-nityānanda vasiyāchena eka ṭhāñi
> dui-jane krīḍā-kalaha lāgila tathāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasiyāchena]]---sat; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in one place; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---those two persons; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]-[[synonyms:k:kalaha]]---mock fighting; [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began; [[synonyms:t:tathāi]]---there.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu sat side by side, and when prasāda was being distributed They engaged in a type of mock fighting.**
