====== CC Madhya 12.28 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā tomāre sneha kare, tumi-sneha-vaśa
> tāṅra snehe karābe tāṅre tomāra paraśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sneha]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---loves; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---controlled by love and affection; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:snehe]]---by love; [[synonyms:k:karābe]]---will do; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:p:paraśa]]---touching.

===== Translation =====

**"The King is very much attached to You, and You are feeling affection and love toward him. Thus I can understand that by virtue of the King's affection for You, You will touch him.**
