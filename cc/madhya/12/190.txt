====== Cc. Madhya 12.190 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu ta' sannyāsī, uṅhāra nāhi apacaya
> anna-doṣe sannyāsīra doṣa nāhi haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:u:uṅhāra]]---for Him; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:a:apacaya]]---any discrepancy; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:d:doṣe]]---by contamination of food; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]]---of a person in the renounced order; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is in the renounced order of life. Consequently He does not recognize discrepancies. As a matter of fact, a sannyāsī is not affected by eating food from anywhere and everywhere.**
