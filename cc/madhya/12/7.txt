====== CC Madhya 12.7 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura nikaṭe āche yata bhakta-gaṇa
> mora lāgi' tāṅ-sabāre kariha nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---in the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for me; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---please submit; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---petition.

===== Translation =====

**In this letter the King requested Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, "Please appeal to all the devotees associated with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and submit this petition to them on my behalf.**
