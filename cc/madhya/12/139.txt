====== Cc. Madhya 12.139 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-dike bhakta-aṅga kaila prakṣālana
> śrāvaṇera megha yena kare variṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---all around; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the bodies of the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing; [[synonyms:ś:śrāvaṇera]] [[synonyms:m:megha]]---exactly like a cloud in the month of Śrāvaṇa (July-August); [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:v:variṣaṇa]]---pour.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu washed the bodies of all the devotees with the tears from His eyes. The tears poured like rains in the month of Śrāvaṇa.**
