====== CC Madhya 12.189 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita kahe,-avadhūtera saṅge eka paṅkti
> bhojana kariluṅ, nā jāni habe kon gati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Advaita Ācārya said; [[synonyms:a:avadhūtera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with a mendicant; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:p:paṅkti]]---in one line; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]]---I am taking My food; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I do not know; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---will be; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:g:gati]]---destination.

===== Translation =====

**First Advaita Ācārya said, "I am sitting in line with an unknown mendicant, and because I am eating with Him, I do not know what kind of destination is awaiting Me.**
