====== CC Madhya 12.216 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> madhyāhna paryanta kaila śrī-mukha daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---His associates; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---up to midday; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performs; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing the face of Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees enjoyed transcendental bliss upon seeing the face of Jagannātha. This continued to midday.**
