====== CC Madhya 12.128 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe svarūpa gosāñi tāra ghāḍe hāta diyā
> ḍhekā māri' purīra bāhira rākhilena lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:g:ghāḍe]]---on the neck; [[synonyms:h:hāta]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---touching with the hand; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍhekā]] [[synonyms:m:māri']]---pushing a little; [[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:b:bāhira]]---out of the temple of Guṇḍicā Purī; [[synonyms:r:rākhilena]]---kept; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**At this point Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī caught the Gauḍīya Vaiṣṇava by the neck and, giving him a little push, ejected him from the Guṇḍicā Purī temple and made him stay outside.**
