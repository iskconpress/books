====== CC Madhya 12.79 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dine prabhāte lañā nija-gaṇa
> śrī-haste sabāra aṅge lepilā candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the next day; [[synonyms:p:prabhāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---His personal devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---by His own hand; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on everyone's body; [[synonyms:l:lepilā]] [[synonyms:c:candana]]---smeared pulp of sandalwood.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, early in the morning, the Lord took His personal associates with Him and, with His own hand, smeared sandalwood pulp on their bodies.**
