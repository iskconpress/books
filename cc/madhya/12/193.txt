====== Cc. Madhya 12.193 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda kahe-tumi advaita-ācārya
> 'advaita-siddhānte' bādhe śuddha-bhakti-kārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya, or a teacher of impersonal monism; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhānte]]---in that monistic conclusion; [[synonyms:b:bādhe]]---is greatly hindered; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:k:kārya]]---the matter of pure devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu immediately refuted Śrīla Advaita Ācārya, saying, "You are a teacher of impersonal monism, and the monistic conclusion is a great hindrance to progressive, pure devotional service.**
