====== Cc. Madhya 12.60 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅre dekhi, mahāprabhura kṛṣṇa-smṛti haila
> premāveśe tāṅre mili' kahite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing him; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:smṛti]]---remembrance of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the boy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately remembered Kṛṣṇa. Meeting the boy in ecstatic love, the Lord began to speak.**
