====== CC Madhya 12.200 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe pariveśaka svarūpādi sāta jana
> gṛhera bhitare kaila prasāda bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:pariveśaka]]---the distributors of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---seven men; [[synonyms:g:gṛhera]] [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within the room; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating of //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**The seven persons headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara who were engaged in distributing prasāda to others then took their meals within the room.**
