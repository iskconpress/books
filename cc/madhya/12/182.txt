====== Cc. Madhya 12.182 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu vinā keha nāhi dayāmaya
> kākere garuḍa kare,-aiche kon haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anybody; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---so merciful; [[synonyms:k:kākere]]---unto a crow; [[synonyms:g:garuḍa]]---the biggest eagle; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---transformed; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---who is another.

===== Translation =====

**"But for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu," Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya continued, "who is so merciful? He has converted a crow into a Garuḍa. Who could be so merciful?**
