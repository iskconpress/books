~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 12.1~~

====== CC Madhya 12.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-guṇḍicā-mandiram ātma-vṛndaiḥ
> sammārjayan kṣālanataḥ sa gauraḥ
> sva-citta-vac chītalam ujjvalaṁ ca
> kṛṣṇopaveśaupayikaṁ cakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]---known as Guṇḍicā; [[synonyms:m:mandiram]]---the temple; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndaiḥ]]---with His associates; [[synonyms:s:sammārjayan]]---washing; [[synonyms:k:kṣālanataḥ]]---by cleansing; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:g:gauraḥ]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:c:citta]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like His own heart; [[synonyms:ś:śītalam]]---cool and calm; [[synonyms:u:ujjvalam]]---bright and clean; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:upaveśa]]---for the sitting; [[synonyms:a:aupayikam]]---befitting; [[synonyms:c:cakāra]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu washed and cleansed the Guṇḍicā temple with His devotees and associates. In this way He made the temple as cool and bright as His own heart, and thus He made the place befitting for Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa to sit.**
