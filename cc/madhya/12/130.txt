====== CC Madhya 12.130 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhura mane santoṣa ha-ilā
> sāri kari' dui pāśe sabāre vasāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---there was satisfaction; [[synonyms:s:sāri]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making a line; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---on two sides; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made to sit.

===== Translation =====

**After this incident, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very much satisfied. He then asked all of the devotees to sit down in two lines on both sides.**
