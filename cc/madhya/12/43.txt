====== CC Madhya 12.43 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-pade prema-bhakti jānāila rājāra
> prasaṅga pāñā aiche kahe bāra-bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---unto the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:j:jānāila]]---informed; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]]---of the King; [[synonyms:p:prasaṅga]]---discussion; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:b:bāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Rāmānanda Rāya informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu about the ecstatic love of the King. Indeed, as soon as there was some opportunity, he repeatedly informed the Lord about the King.**
