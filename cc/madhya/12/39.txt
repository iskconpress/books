====== CC Madhya 12.39 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda rāya yabe 'dakṣiṇa' haite āilā
> prabhu-saṅge rahite rājāke nivedilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---South India; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahite]]---to stay; [[synonyms:r:rājāke]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:n:nivedilā]]---requested.

===== Translation =====

**After returning from his service in South India, Rāmānanda Rāya requested the King to allow him to remain with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
