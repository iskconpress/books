====== CC Madhya 12.177 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhaume prabhu vasāñāchena vāma-pāśe
> dui bhaktera sneha dekhi' sārvabhauma hāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vasāñāchena]]---made to sit; [[synonyms:v:vāma]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---on His left side; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the two devotees; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]---the affection; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---smiles.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord made Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya sit on His left side, and when Sārvabhauma saw the behavior of Svarūpa Dāmodara and Jagadānanda, he smiled.**
