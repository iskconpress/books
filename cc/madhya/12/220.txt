====== CC Madhya 12.220 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle ratha-yātrā habeka jāniyā
> sevaka lāgāya bhoga dviguṇa kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the car festival; [[synonyms:h:habeka]]---would take place; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the priestly servants of the Lord; [[synonyms:l:lāgāya]]---offer; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---food; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---increasing to double.

===== Translation =====

**Knowing that the car festival would take place in the morning, all the servants of Lord Jagannātha were doubling their offerings of food.**
