====== Cc. Madhya 12.197 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu sarva-vaiṣṇavera nāma lañā
> mahā-prasāda dena mahā-amṛta siñciyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---names; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---calling; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of the food of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---delivers; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---transcendental nectar; [[synonyms:s:siñciyā]]---sprinkling.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, calling all the Vaiṣṇavas, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu distributed mahā-prasāda as if sprinkling nectar. At that time the mock fight between Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu became more and more delicious.**
