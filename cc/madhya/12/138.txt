====== Cc. Madhya 12.138 ======

===== Text =====

> sveda, kampa, vaivarṇyāśru pulaka, huṅkāra
> nija-aṅga dhui' āge cale aśru-dhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:v:vaivarṇya]]---fading; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---roaring; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---personal body; [[synonyms:d:dhui']]---washing; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---forward; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---a flow of tears.

===== Translation =====

**As usual, when Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced, there were perspiration, trembling, fading, tears, jubilation and roaring. Indeed, the tears from His eyes washed His body and those before Him.**
