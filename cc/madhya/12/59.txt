====== CC Madhya 12.59 ======

===== Text =====

> pītāmbara, dhare aṅge ratna-ābharaṇa
> śrī-kṛṣṇa-smaraṇe teṅha hailā 'uddīpana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pīta]]-[[synonyms:a:ambara]]---dressed in yellow cloth; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---carries; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābharaṇa]]---ornaments of jewels; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:smaraṇe]]---for remembering Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---was; [[synonyms:u:uddīpana]]---stimulation.

===== Translation =====

**The prince was dressed in yellow cloth, and there were jeweled ornaments decorating his body. Therefore anyone who saw him would remember Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
