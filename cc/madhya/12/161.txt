====== Cc. Madhya 12.161 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-saṅge prabhu karuna prasāda aṅgīkāra
> e-saṅge vasite yogya nahi muñi chāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---let Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sit down with the devotees; [[synonyms:k:karuna]]---let Him do; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---of remnants of food; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---accepting; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with this batch; [[synonyms:v:vasite]]---to sit down; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---befitting; [[synonyms:n:nahi]]---am not; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:c:chāra]]---most abominable.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "Let Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu take His lunch with the devotees. Since I am abominable, I cannot sit down among you.**
