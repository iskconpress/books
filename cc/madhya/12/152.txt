====== CC Madhya 12.152 ======

===== Text =====

> tīre uṭhi' parena prabhu śuṣka vasana
> nṛsiṁha-deve namaskari' gelā upavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tīre]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting on the bank; [[synonyms:p:parena]]---puts on; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]] [[synonyms:v:vasana]]---dry garments; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:deve]]---unto Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:g:gelā]] [[synonyms:u:upavana]]---entered a garden.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing, the Lord stood on the bank of the lake and put on dry garments. After offering obeisances to Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva, whose temple was nearby, the Lord entered a garden.**
