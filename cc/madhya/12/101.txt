====== CC Madhya 12.101 ======

===== Text =====

> keha jala āni' deya mahāprabhura kare
> keha jala deya tāṅra caraṇa-upare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing water; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---delivers; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---to the hand of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:d:deya]]---pours water; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Someone brought water to pour into the hands of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and someone poured water on His lotus feet.**
