====== Cc. Madhya 12.202 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa govinda-pāśa kichu māgi' nila
> sei prasādānna govinda āpani pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the other devotees; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---from Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---a little; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---begging; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---partook.

===== Translation =====

**The remnants of food left by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were later distributed among devotees who begged for them, and finally Govinda personally took the last remnants.**
