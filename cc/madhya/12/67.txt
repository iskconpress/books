====== CC Madhya 12.67 ======

===== Text =====

> putre āliṅgana kari' premāviṣṭa hailā
> sākṣāt paraśa yena mahāprabhura pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:putre]]---his son; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---he became ecstatic; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:p:paraśa]]---touch; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---he got.

===== Translation =====

**Just by embracing his son, the King was filled with ecstatic love, just as if he had touched Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu directly.**
