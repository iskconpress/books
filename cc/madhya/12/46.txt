====== CC Madhya 12.46 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda prabhu-pāya kaila nivedana
> eka-bāra pratāparudre dekhāha caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---once only; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudre]]---unto Mahārāja Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:d:dekhāha]]---show; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya frankly requested Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Please show Your lotus feet to the King at least once."**
