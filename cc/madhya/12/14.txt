====== CC Madhya 12.14 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe kahe,-prabhu tāṅre kabhu nā milibe
> āmi-saba kahi yadi, duḥkha se mānibe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabekahe]]---everyone said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:m:milibe]]---would see; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of us; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---say; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:s:se]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mānibe]]---will feel.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees gave their opinion and said, "The Lord will never meet the King, and if we requested Him to do so, the Lord will surely feel very unhappy."**
