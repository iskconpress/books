====== Cc. Madhya 12.62 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛtārtha ha-ilāṅa āmi iṅhāra daraśane
> eta bali' punaḥ tāre kaila āliṅgane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilāṅa]]---have become very much obligated; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---of this boy; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---by seeing; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgane]]---embrace.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "I have become very much obligated just by seeing this boy." After saying this, the Lord again embraced the prince.**
