====== Cc. Madhya 12.168 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvajña prabhu jānena yāṅre yei bhāya
> tāṅre tāṅre sei deoyāya svarūpa-dvārāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the omniscient Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---knows; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---to whom; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---appeals; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto each person; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:d:deoyāya]]---orders to administer; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārāya]]---by Svarūpa Dāmodara.

===== Translation =====

**Since Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is omniscient, He knew what type of preparation each person liked. He therefore had Svarūpa Dāmodara deliver these preparations to their full satisfaction.**
