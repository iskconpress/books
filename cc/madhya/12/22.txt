====== CC Madhya 12.22 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi śuniyā prabhura komala haya mana
> tathāpi bāhire kahe niṣṭhura vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:komala]]---softened; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---He says; [[synonyms:n:niṣṭhura]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---hard words.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing all these statements, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind was certainly softened, but externally He wished to speak some harsh words.**
