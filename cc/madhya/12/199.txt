====== CC Madhya 12.199 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu saba nija-bhakta-gaṇe
> sabākāre śrī-haste dilā mālya-candane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---personal devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with His own hand; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candane]]---flower garlands and sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered flower garlands and sandalwood pulp to all His devoted personal associates.**
