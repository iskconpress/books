====== CC Madhya 12.122 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle gauḍīyā eka subuddhi sarala
> prabhura caraṇa-yuge dila ghaṭa-jala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:g:gauḍīyā]]---Vaiṣṇava from Bengal; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---very intelligent; [[synonyms:s:sarala]]---simple; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:y:yuge]]---on the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---poured; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭa]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---one potful of water.

===== Translation =====

**After everything was thoroughly washed, a Vaiṣṇava from Bengal, who was very intelligent and simple, came and poured water on the lotus feet of the Lord.**
