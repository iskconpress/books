====== CC Madhya 12.203 ======

===== Text =====

> svatantra īśvara prabhu kare nānā khelā
> 'dhoyā-pākhalā' nāma kaila ei eka līlā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svatantra]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the independent Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:k:khelā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:d:dhoyā]]-[[synonyms:p:pākhalā]]---washing and cleansing; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastime.

===== Translation =====

**The fully independent Supreme Personality of Godhead performs various types of pastimes. The pastime of washing and cleansing the Guṇḍicā temple is but one of them.**
