====== CC Madhya 12.113 ======

===== Text =====

> yei yei kahe, sei kahe kṛṣṇa-nāme
> kṛṣṇa-nāma ha-ila saṅketa saba-kāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---anyone who was speaking; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāme]]---by uttering the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa became; [[synonyms:s:saṅketa]]---indication; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:k:kāme]]---for everyone who wanted something.

===== Translation =====

**Whenever anyone had to speak, he did so by uttering the holy name of Kṛṣṇa. Consequently, the holy name of Kṛṣṇa became an indication for everyone who wanted something.**
