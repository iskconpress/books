====== Cc. Madhya 12.47 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-rāmānanda, kaha vicāriyā
> rājāke milite yuyāya sannyāsī hañā?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---My dear Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please ask Me; [[synonyms:v:vicāriyā]]---after due consideration; [[synonyms:r:rājāke]]---the King; [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---is it befitting; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "My dear Rāmānanda, you should make this request after duly considering whether it is befitting for a sannyāsī to meet a king.**
