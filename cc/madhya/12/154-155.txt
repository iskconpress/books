~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 12.154-155~~

====== CC Madhya 12.154-155 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśī-miśra, tulasī-paḍichā-dui jana
> pañca-śata loka yata karaye bhojana

> tata anna-piṭhā-pānā saba pāṭhāila
> dekhi' mahāprabhura mane santoṣa ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]-[[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---Tulasī, the superintendent of the temple; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---five hundred men; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as much; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eat; [[synonyms:t:tata]]---so; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:p:piṭhā]]-[[synonyms:p:pānā]]---rice, cakes and sweet rice; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**Kāśī Miśra and Tulasī, the superintendent of the temple, brought as much prasāda as five hundred men could eat. Seeing the large quantity of prasāda, which consisted of rice, cakes, sweet rice and a variety of vegetables, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very much satisfied.**
