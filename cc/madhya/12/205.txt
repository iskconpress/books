====== CC Madhya 12.205 ======

===== Text =====

> pakṣa-dina duḥkhī loka prabhura adarśane
> darśana kariyā loka sukha pāila mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pakṣa]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---for a fortnight; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:a:adarśane]]---without the sight; [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---by seeing; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was unhappy for a fortnight because they could not see the Deity of Lord Jagannātha. Upon seeing the Lord at the festival, the devotees were very happy.**
