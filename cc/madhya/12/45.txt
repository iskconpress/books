====== Cc. Madhya 12.45 ======

===== Text =====

> utkaṇṭhāte pratāparudra nāre rahibāre
> rāmānanda sādhilena prabhure milibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhāte]]---in great anxiety; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:n:nāre]] [[synonyms:r:rahibāre]]---could not stay; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:sādhilena]]---solicited; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---to meet.

===== Translation =====

**Mahārāja Pratāparudra, in great anxiety, could not endure not seeing the Lord; therefore Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya, by his diplomacy, arranged a meeting with the Lord for the King.**
