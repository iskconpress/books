====== CC Madhya 12.153 ======

===== Text =====

> udyāne vasilā prabhu bhakta-gaṇa lañā
> tabe vāṇīnātha āilā mahā-prasāda lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---in the garden; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha Rāya; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---bringing all kinds of //mahā-prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**In the garden, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down with the other devotees. Vāṇīnātha Rāya then came and brought all kinds of mahā-prasāda.**
