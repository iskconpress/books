====== CC Madhya 12.11 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya patrī dekhi' cintita hañā
> bhakta-gaṇa-pāśa gelā sei patrī lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:patrī]]---the letter; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:c:cintita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming very anxious; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---near; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:patrī]]---letter; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**When the Bhaṭṭācārya received this letter, he became very anxious. He then took the letter and went to the devotees of the Lord.**
