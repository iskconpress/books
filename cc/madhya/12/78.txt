====== CC Madhya 12.78 ======

===== Text =====

> nūtana eka-śata ghaṭa, śata sammārjanī
> paḍichā āniyā dila prabhura icchā jāni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nūtana]]---new; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]]---one hundred; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭa]]---waterpots; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---hundred; [[synonyms:s:sammārjanī]]---brooms; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---the superintendent; [[synonyms:ā:āniyā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---the desire; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as the superintendent understood the desire of the Lord, he immediately delivered a hundred new waterpots and a hundred brooms for sweeping the temple.**
