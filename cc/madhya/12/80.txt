====== Cc. Madhya 12.80 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-haste dila sabāre eka eka mārjanī
> saba-gaṇa lañā prabhu calilā āpani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---by His own hand; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto every one of them; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one by one; [[synonyms:m:mārjanī]]---a broom; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the associates; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**He then gave each devotee a broom with His own hand, and taking all of them personally with Him, the Lord went to Guṇḍicā.**
