====== Cc. Madhya 12.91 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāra jhyāṅṭāna bojhā ekatra karila
> sabā haite prabhura bojhā adhika ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:j:jhyāṅṭāna]]---the dirt collected; [[synonyms:b:bojhā]]---load; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---combined in one place; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than all of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:b:bojhā]]---the pile of dirt collected by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:adhika]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was greater.

===== Translation =====

**Even though all the devotees collected dirt in one pile, the dirt collected by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was much greater.**
