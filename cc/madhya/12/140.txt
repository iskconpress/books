====== Cc. Madhya 12.140 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-ucca-saṅkīrtane ākāśa bharila
> prabhura uddaṇḍa-nṛtye bhūmi-kampa haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:ucca]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---by a great and loud performance of chanting; [[synonyms:ā:ākāśa]]---the sky; [[synonyms:b:bharila]]---became filled; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uddaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtye]]---by dancing and jumping high; [[synonyms:b:bhūmi]]-[[synonyms:k:kampa]]---earthquake; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**The sky was filled with the great and loud chanting of saṅkīrtana, and the earth shook from the jumping and dancing of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
