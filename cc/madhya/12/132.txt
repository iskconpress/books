====== Cc. Madhya 12.132 ======

===== Text =====

> ke kata kuḍāya, saba ekatra kariba
> yāra alpa, tāra ṭhāñi piṭhā-pānā la-iba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:k:kuḍāya]]---how much one has collected; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---in one place; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---I shall gather; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]---small; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from him; [[synonyms:p:piṭhā]]-[[synonyms:p:pānā]] [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iba]]---I will ask for cakes and sweet rice as a fine.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was picking up the straws and grains of sand, He said, "I shall gather everyone's collection, and I shall ask whoever has collected less than all the others to pay a fine of sweet cakes and sweet rice."**
