====== CC Madhya 12.17 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-ki kahite sabāra āgamana
> dekhiye kahite cāha,-nā kaha, ki kāraṇa?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---there is the presence here; [[synonyms:d:dekhiye]]---I see; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:c:cāha]]---you want to speak; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---but do not speak; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---what is the reason.

===== Translation =====

**After they arrived at Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's place, the Lord, seeing them, said, "What have you all come here to say? I see that you want to say something, but you do not speak. What is the reason?"**
