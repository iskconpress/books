====== Cc. Madhya 12.208 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura āge purī, bhāratī,-duṅhāra gamana
> svarūpa, advaita,-duṅhera pārśve dui-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---first they went; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:d:duṅhera]]---of both; [[synonyms:p:pārśve]]---on the two sides; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went toward the temple, Paramānanda Purī and Brahmānanda Bhāratī walked in front of Him, and at His two sides walked Svarūpa Dāmodara and Advaita Ācārya.**
