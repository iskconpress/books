====== CC Madhya 12.110 ======

===== Text =====

> ghaṭe ghaṭe ṭheki' kata ghaṭa bhāṅgi' gela
> śata śata ghaṭa loka tāhāṅ lañā āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghaṭe]] [[synonyms:g:ghaṭe]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭheki']]---when there was a collision between one pot and another; [[synonyms:k:kata]]---so many; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭa]]---pots; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgi']] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---became broken; [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---hundreds of; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭa]]---pots; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**Many of the waterpots were broken when people collided with one another, and hundreds of men had to bring new waterpots to fill.**
