====== Cc. Madhya 12.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrīvāsādi gaura-bhakta-gaṇa
> śakti deha,-kari yena caitanya varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]] [[synonyms:d:deha]]---please give me power; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:y:yena]]---so that I may do; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to the devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura! I beg their power so that I can properly describe Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
