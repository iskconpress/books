====== CC Madhya 12.105 ======

===== Text =====

> śata ghaṭa jale haila mandira mārjana
> mandira śodhiyā kaila-yena nija mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]]---one hundred; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭa]]---of waterpots; [[synonyms:j:jale]]---by the water; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mandira]]---the temple; [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---cleansed; [[synonyms:m:mandira]]---the temple; [[synonyms:ś:śodhiyā]]---cleansing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---his own mind.

===== Translation =====

**In this way all the rooms were cleansed with a hundred waterpots. After the rooms had been cleansed, the minds of the devotees were as clean as the rooms.**
