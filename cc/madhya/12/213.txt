====== CC Madhya 12.213 ======

===== Text =====

> bāndhulīra phula jini' adhara suraṅga
> īṣat hasita kānti-amṛta-taraṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāndhulīra]] [[synonyms:p:phula]]---a kind of red flower named //bāndhulī//; [[synonyms:j:jini']]---conquering; [[synonyms:a:adhara]]---chin; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]]---buff color; [[synonyms:ī:īṣat]]---mild; [[synonyms:h:hasita]]---smiling; [[synonyms:k:kānti]]---luster; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---nectar; [[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---waves.

===== Translation =====

**The chin of the Lord, tinged with buff color, conquered the beauty of the bāndhulī flower. This increased the beauty of His mild smiling, which was like lustrous waves of nectar.**
