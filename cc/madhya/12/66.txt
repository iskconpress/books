====== CC Madhya 12.66 ======

===== Text =====

> vidāya hañā rāya āila rāja-putre lañā
> rājā sukha pāila putrera ceṣṭā dekhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---taking leave; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came back; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:putre]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the King's son; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---felt great happiness; [[synonyms:p:putrera]]---of his son; [[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---activities; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya and the boy then departed from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and Rāmānanda took him back to the King's palace. The King was very happy when he heard of his son's activities.**
