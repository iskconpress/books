====== CC Madhya 12.98 ======

===== Text =====

> khāparā bhariyā jala ūrdhve cālāila
> sei jale ūrdhva śodhi bhitti prakṣālila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:khāparā]]---basin; [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---filling; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhve]]---on the ceiling; [[synonyms:c:cālāila]]---began to throw; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:j:jale]]---with that water; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhva]] [[synonyms:ś:śodhi]]---washing the ceiling; [[synonyms:b:bhitti]]---walls and floor; [[synonyms:p:prakṣālila]]---washed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Himself and His devotees began to throw water onto the ceiling. When this water fell, it washed the walls and floor.**
