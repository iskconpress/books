====== CC Madhya 12.53 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-pūrṇa yaiche dugdhera kalasa
> surā-bindu-pāte keha nā kare paraśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---completely filled; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:d:dugdhera]]---of milk; [[synonyms:k:kalasa]]---container; [[synonyms:s:surā]]-[[synonyms:b:bindu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāte]]---with simply a drop of liquor; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:paraśa]]---touch.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "There may be much milk in a big pot, but if it is contaminated by a drop of liquor, it is untouchable.**
