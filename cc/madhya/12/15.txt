====== CC Madhya 12.15 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,-sabe cala' eka-bāra
> milite nā kahiba, kahiba rāja-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhaumakahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:c:cala']]---let all of us go; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---once; [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahiba]]---we shall not request; [[synonyms:k:kahiba]]---we shall simply describe; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---the behavior of the King.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then said, "We shall go once again to the Lord, but we shall not request Him to meet the King. Rather, we shall simply describe the good behavior of the King."**
