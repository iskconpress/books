====== CC Madhya 12.160 ======

===== Text =====

> 'haridāsa' bali' prabhu ḍāke ghane ghana
> dūre rahi' haridāsa kare nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---calling Haridāsa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāke]]---calls; [[synonyms:g:ghane]] [[synonyms:g:ghana]]---repeatedly; [[synonyms:d:dūre]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---standing at a distance; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was repeatedly calling, "Haridāsa, Haridāsa," and at that time Haridāsa, standing at a distance, spoke as follows.**
