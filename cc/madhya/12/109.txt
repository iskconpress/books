====== Cc. Madhya 12.109 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda, advaita, svarūpa, bhāratī, purī
> iṅhā vinu āra saba āne jala bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:i:iṅhā]]---these; [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---except; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---others; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---bring; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling up.

===== Translation =====

**With the exception of Nityānanda Prabhu, Advaita Ācārya, Svarūpa Dāmodara, Brahmānanda Bhāratī and Paramānanda Purī, everyone was engaged in filling the waterpots and bringing them there.**
