====== CC Madhya 12.49 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda kahe,-tumi īśvara svatantra
> kāre tomāra bhaya, tumi naha paratantra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:k:kāre]] [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---why should You be afraid of anyone; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:n:naha]]---You are not; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:t:tantra]]---dependent.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya replied, "My Lord, You are the supreme independent personality. You have nothing to fear from anyone because You are not dependent on anyone."**
