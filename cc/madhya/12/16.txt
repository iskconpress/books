====== CC Madhya 12.16 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' sabe gelā mahāprabhura sthāne
> kahite unmukha sabe, nā kahe vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---deciding like this; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:u:unmukha]]---ready; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---do not; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---any word.

===== Translation =====

**Having thus reached a decision, they all went to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. There, although ready to speak, they could not even utter a word.**
