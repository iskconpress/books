====== CC Madhya 12.42 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-saṅge dui jana kṣetre yabe āilā
> rāmānanda rāya tabe prabhure mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---together; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---these two persons; [[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---at Jagannātha-kṣetra (Jagannātha Purī); [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**The King and Rāmānanda Rāya returned together to Jagannātha-kṣetra [Purī], and Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
