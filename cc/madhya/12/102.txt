====== CC Madhya 12.102 ======

===== Text =====

> keha lukāñā kare sei jala pāna
> keha māgi' laya, keha anye kare dāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:l:lukāñā]]---concealing himself; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:j:jala]]---of that water; [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drinking; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:m:māgi']] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---begging, takes; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---another; [[synonyms:a:anye]]---to another; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---gives; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---in charity.

===== Translation =====

**The water that fell from the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was drunk by someone who hid himself. Someone else begged for that water, and another person was giving that water in charity.**
