====== CC Madhya 12.19 ======

===== Text =====

> yogyāyogya tomāya saba cāhi nivedite
> tomā nā milile rājā cāhe yogī haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---befitting; [[synonyms:a:ayogya]]---not befitting; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---unto You; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---we all; [[synonyms:c:cāhi]]---want; [[synonyms:n:nivedite]]---to submit; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---You; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:m:milile]]---if he does not meet; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:y:yogī]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---to become a mendicant.

===== Translation =====

**"We want to submit before You something that may or may not be befitting. The matter is this: unless he sees You, the King of Orissa will become a mendicant."**
