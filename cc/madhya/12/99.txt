====== CC Madhya 12.99 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-haste karena siṁhāsanera mārjana
> prabhu āge jala āni' deya bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with His own hand; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:s:siṁhāsanera]] [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---washing of the sitting place of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before the Lord; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---deliver; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to wash the sitting place of Lord Jagannātha with His own hands, and all the devotees began to bring water to the Lord.**
