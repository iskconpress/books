====== CC Madhya 12.70 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryādi bhakta kare prabhure nimantraṇa
> tāhāṅ tāhāṅ bhikṣā kare lañā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---here and there; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---takes His lunch; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Some of the prominent devotees like Advaita Ācārya used to invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to take His meals at their homes. The Lord accepted such invitations accompanied by His devotees.**
