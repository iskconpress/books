====== CC Madhya 12.85 ======

===== Text =====

> premollāse śodhena, layena kṛṣṇa-nāma
> bhakta-gaṇa 'kṛṣṇa' kahe, kare nija-kāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:ullāse]]---in great jubilation; [[synonyms:ś:śodhena]]---cleanses; [[synonyms:l:layena]]---chants; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Hare Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---chant Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kāma]]---their own duty.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu washed and cleansed the temple in great jubilation, chanting the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa all the time. Similarly, all the devotees were also chanting and at the same time performing their respective duties.**
