~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 12.163-164~~

====== CC Madhya 12.163-164 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi, jagadānanda, dāmodara
> kāśīśvara, gopīnātha, vāṇīnātha, śaṅkara
>
> pariveśana kare tāhāṅ ei sāta-jana
> madhye madhye hari-dhvani kare bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha,]] [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha,]] [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Gopīnātha, Vāṇīnātha and Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---distribute; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:s:sāta]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---seven persons; [[synonyms:m:madhye]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---at intervals; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---resounding of the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, Jagadānanda, Dāmodara Paṇḍita, Kāśīśvara, Gopīnātha, Vāṇīnātha and Śaṅkara distributed prasāda, and the devotees chanted the holy names at intervals.**
