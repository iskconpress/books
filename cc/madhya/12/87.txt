====== Cc. Madhya 12.87 ======

===== Text =====

> bhoga-mandira śodhana kari' śodhila prāṅgaṇa
> sakala āvāsa krame karila śodhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]-[[synonyms:m:mandira]]---the place where food is placed; [[synonyms:ś:śodhana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---cleansing; [[synonyms:ś:śodhila]] [[synonyms:p:prāṅgaṇa]]---cleansed the yard; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āvāsa]]---residential places; [[synonyms:k:krame]]---one after another; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:ś:śodhana]]---cleansed.

===== Translation =====

**After this, the place where the Deity's food was kept [bhoga-mandira] was cleansed. Then the yard was cleansed, and then all the residential quarters, one after the other.**
