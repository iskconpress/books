====== Cc. Madhya 12.52 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,-kata pāpīra kariyācha avyāhati
> īśvara-sevaka tomāra bhakta gajapati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya replied; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:p:pāpīra]]---of numberless sinful persons; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]]---You have done; [[synonyms:a:avyāhati]]---deliverance; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---a servitor of the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:g:gajapati]]---the King.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya replied, "My dear Lord, You have delivered so many sinful people. This King Pratāparudra, the King of Orissa, is actually a servitor of the Lord and Your devotee."**
