====== Cc. Madhya 12.107 ======

===== Text =====

> śata śata jana jala bhare sarovare
> ghāṭe sthāna nāhi, keha kūpe jala bhare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---hundreds of men; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---draw water; [[synonyms:s:sarovare]]---from the lake; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭe]]---on the bank; [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---place; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:k:kūpe]]---from the well; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---draws the water.

===== Translation =====

**Since hundreds of men were engaged in bringing water from the lake, there was no place to stand on the banks. Consequently someone began to draw water from a well.**
