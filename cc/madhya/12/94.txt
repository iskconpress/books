====== Cc. Madhya 12.94 ======

===== Text =====

> saba vaiṣṇava lañā yabe dui-bāra śodhila
> dekhi' mahāprabhura mane santoṣa ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---for the second time; [[synonyms:ś:śodhila]]---cleansed; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**After Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all the Vaiṣṇavas cleansed the temple for the second time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very happy to see the cleansing work.**
