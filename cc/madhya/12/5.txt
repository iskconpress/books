====== CC Madhya 12.5 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṭaka haite patrī dila sārvabhauma-ṭhāñi
> prabhura ājñā haya yadi, dekhibāre yāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṭakahaite]]---from Kaṭaka, the capital of Orissa; [[synonyms:p:patrī]]---a letter; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---sent; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to the place; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]] [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---I can go and see.

===== Translation =====

**The King sent a letter from his capital, Kaṭaka, to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, entreating him to obtain the Lord's permission so that he could go and see Him.**
