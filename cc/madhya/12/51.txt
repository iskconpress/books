====== Cc. Madhya 12.51 ======

===== Text =====

> śukla-vastre masi-bindu yaiche nā lukāya
> sannyāsīra alpa chidra sarva-loke gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śukla]]-[[synonyms:v:vastre]]---on white cloth; [[synonyms:m:masi]]-[[synonyms:b:bindu]]---a spot of ink; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as much as; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:l:lukāya]]---become hidden; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]]---of a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]---a very little; [[synonyms:c:chidra]]---fault; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loke]]---the general public; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---advertise.

===== Translation =====

**"As soon as the general public finds a little fault in the behavior of a sannyāsī, they advertise it like wildfire. A black spot of ink cannot be hidden on a white cloth. It is always very prominent."**
