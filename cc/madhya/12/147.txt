====== Cc. Madhya 12.147 ======

===== Text =====

> aneka karila, tabu nā haya cetana
> ācārya kāndena, kānde saba bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---much endeavor was done; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was not; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kāndena]]---Advaita Ācārya began to cry; [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---cried; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the other devotees.

===== Translation =====

**When the boy did not regain consciousness after some time, Advaita Ācārya and the other devotees began to cry.**
