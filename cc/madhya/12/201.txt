====== CC Madhya 12.201 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura avaśeṣa govinda rākhila dhariyā
> sei anna haridāse kichu dila lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]]---remnants; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---saved; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---keeping; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:a:anna]]---that //prasāda//; [[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---unto Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda saved some remnants of food left by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and kept them carefully. Later, one portion of these remnants was delivered to Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
