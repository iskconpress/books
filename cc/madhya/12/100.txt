====== CC Madhya 12.100 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa kare gṛha-madhya prakṣālana
> nija nija haste kare mandira mārjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:m:madhya]]---within the room; [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]---each one of them; [[synonyms:h:haste]]---in the hand; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:mandira]] [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---cleansing of the temple.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees within the temple began to wash. Each one had a broom in his hand, and in this way they cleansed the temple of the Lord.**
