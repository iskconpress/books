====== Cc. Madhya 12.166 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi premāveśe prabhu hailā asthira
> samaya bujhiyā prabhu hailā kichu dhīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:a:asthira]]---agitated; [[synonyms:s:samaya]] [[synonyms:b:bujhiyā]]---understanding the time and circumstances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---somewhat; [[synonyms:d:dhīra]]---patient.

===== Translation =====

**Just by remembering the pastimes of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was agitated by ecstatic love. But considering the time and circumstance, He remained somewhat patient.**
