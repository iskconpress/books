====== CC Madhya 12.137 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-dike bhakta-gaṇa karena kīrtana
> madhye nṛtya karena prabhu matta-siṁha-sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---all around; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:matta]]-[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---just like a maddened lion.

===== Translation =====

**All around Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu all the devotees performed congregational chanting. The Lord, just like a maddened lion, danced in the middle.**
