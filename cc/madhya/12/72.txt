====== CC Madhya 12.72 ======

===== Text =====

> prathamei kāśī-miśre prabhu bolāila
> paḍichā-pātra, sārvabhaume bolāñā ānila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathamei]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśre]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---called for; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the superintendent of the temple; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:b:bolāñā]]---calling; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu first of all called for Kāśī Miśra, then for the superintendent of the temple, then for Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
