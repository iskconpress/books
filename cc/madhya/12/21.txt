====== CC Madhya 12.21 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhiba se mukha-candra nayana bhariyā
> dhariba se pāda-padma hṛdaye tuliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhiba]]---I shall see; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---moonlike face; [[synonyms:n:nayana]] [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---to the fulfillment of the eyes; [[synonyms:d:dhariba]]---I shall catch; [[synonyms:s:se]]---those; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---on my heart; [[synonyms:t:tuliyā]]---raising.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu continued, "The King also expressed his desire to see the moonlike face of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his eye's full satisfaction. He would like to raise the lotus feet of the Lord to his heart."**
