====== CC Madhya 12.26 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara kahe,-tumi svatantra īśvara
> kartavyākartavya saba tomāra gocara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Paṇḍita Dāmodara said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---fully independent; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kartavya]]---duty which is permissible; [[synonyms:a:akartavya]]---duty which is not permissible; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:g:gocara]]---within knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**Dāmodara immediately replied, "My Lord, You are the fully independent Supreme Personality of Godhead. Since everything is known to You, You know what is permissible and what is not permissible.**
