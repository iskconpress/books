====== Cc. Madhya 12.141 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpera ucca-gāna prabhure sadā bhāya
> ānande uddaṇḍa nṛtya kare gaurarāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpera]]---of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:u:ucca]]-[[synonyms:g:gāna]]---loud singing; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sadā]] [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---always very pleasing; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in jubilation; [[synonyms:u:uddaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---jumping high and dancing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:g:gaurarāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu always liked the loud chanting of Svarūpa Dāmodara. Therefore when Svarūpa Dāmodara sang, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced and jumped high in jubilation.**
