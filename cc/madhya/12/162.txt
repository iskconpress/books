====== Cc. Madhya 12.162 ======

===== Text =====

> pāche more prasāda govinda dibe bahirdvāre
> mana jāni' prabhu punaḥ nā balila tāṅre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at last; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dibe]]---will deliver; [[synonyms:b:bahir]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---outside the door; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---understanding the mind; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:b:balila]]---called; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him.

===== Translation =====

**"Govinda will give me prasāda later, outside the door." Understanding his mind, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not call him again.**
