====== CC Madhya 12.58 ======

===== Text =====

> sundara, rājāra putra-śyāmala-varaṇa
> kiśora vayasa, dīrgha kamala-nayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sundara]]---beautiful; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---the son of the King; [[synonyms:ś:śyāmala]]-[[synonyms:v:varaṇa]]---blackish complexion; [[synonyms:k:kiśora]] [[synonyms:v:vayasa]]---the age just before youth; [[synonyms:d:dīrgha]]---long; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:n:nayana]]---lotus eyes.

===== Translation =====

**The prince, just entering upon his youth, was very beautiful. He was blackish in complexion and had large lotus eyes.**
