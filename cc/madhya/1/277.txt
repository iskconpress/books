====== CC Madhya 1.277 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu dekhi' preme loka ānandita mana
> prabhuke īśvara bali' karaye stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all people; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---joyous; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---as the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---accepting; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---did; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---prayer.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the Lord, everyone became joyful out of love. Everyone accepted the Lord as the Supreme, and thus they offered their prayers.**
