====== CC Madhya 1.103 ======

===== Text =====

> jiyaḍa-nṛsiṁhe kaila nṛsiṁha-stavana
> pathe-pathe grāme-grāme nāma-pravartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jiyaḍa]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhe]]---the place of pilgrimage known as Jiyaḍa-nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]---to Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---praying; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---every village; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:p:pravartana]]---introduction of the holy name of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**After visiting Kūrma-kṣetra, the Lord visited the South Indian temple of Jiyaḍa-nṛsiṁha and offered His prayers to Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva. On His way, He introduced the chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra in every village.**
