====== Cc. Madhya 1.123 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-sane dina kata tāhāñi rahilā
> gauḍera bhakta āise, samācāra pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:k:kata]]---some days; [[synonyms:t:tāhāñi]]---there at Ālālanātha; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]]---of Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---come; [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---news; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---He got.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained for some days at Ālālanātha. In the meantime, Caitanya Mahāprabhu received news that all the devotees from Bengal were coming to Jagannātha Purī.**
