====== CC Madhya 1.276 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhu tuli' bale prabhu bala' 'hari' 'hari'
> uṭhila---śrī-hari-dhvani catur-dik bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhu]] [[synonyms:t:tuli']]---raising the arms; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bala']]---speak; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of the Lord, Hari; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---arose; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---vibration of the sound Hari; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dik]]---the four directions; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling.

===== Translation =====

**Raising His arms, the Lord asked everyone to chant loudly the vibration of the holy name of Lord Hari. There immediately arose a great stir, and the vibration of "Hari!" filled all directions.**
