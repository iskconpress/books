====== CC Madhya 1.110 ======

===== Text =====

> cāturmāsya tāṅhā prabhu śrī-vaiṣṇavera sane
> goṅāila nṛtya-gīta-kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]]---observance of the four months ot the rainy season; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with the Śrī Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:g:goṅāila]]---passed; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---singing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---in chanting the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed the Cāturmāsya months with the Śrī Vaiṣṇavas, dancing and singing, and also chanting the holy name of the Lord.**
