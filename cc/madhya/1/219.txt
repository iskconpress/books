====== CC Madhya 1.219 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda, haridāsa, śrīvāsa, gadādhara
> mukunda, jagadānanda, murāri, vakreśvara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara.

===== Translation =====

**All the Vaiṣṇava associates of the Lord were present, including Nityānanda, Haridāsa Ṭhākura, Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, Gadādhara Paṇḍita, Mukunda, Jagadānanda, Murāri and Vakreśvara.**
