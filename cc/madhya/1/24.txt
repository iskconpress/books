====== CC Madhya 1.24 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-gosāñire pāṭhāila gauḍa-deśe
> teṅho gauḍa-deśa bhāsāila prema-rase

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---Nityānanda Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---the tract of land known as Gauḍa-deśa, or Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhāsāila]]---overflooded; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---with ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sent Nityānanda Prabhu from Jagannātha Purī to Bengal, which was known as Gauḍa-deśa, and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu overflooded this country with the transcendental loving service of the Lord.**
