====== Cc. Madhya 1.86 ======

===== Text =====

> tri-bhaṅga-sundara vraje vrajendra-nandana
> kāhāṅ pāba, ei vāñchā bāḍe anukṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅga]]---bent in three places; [[synonyms:s:sundara]]---beautiful; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:p:pāba]]---shall I get; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:a:anukṣaṇa]]---incessantly.

===== Translation =====

**Absorbed in the ecstasy of the gopīs, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu wished to see Lord Jagannātha in His original form as Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja, standing in Vṛndāvana and appearing very beautiful, His body curved in three places. His desire to see that form was always increasing.**
