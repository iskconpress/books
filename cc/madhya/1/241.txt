====== CC Madhya 1.241 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-tīra-pathe lañā prayāge āilā
> śrī-rūpa āsi' prabhuke tathāi mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---the path on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prayāge]]---in Allahabad; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---of the name Śrī Rūpa; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tathāi]]---there; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**After leaving Mathurā, the Lord began to walk along a path on the bank of the Ganges, and finally He reached the holy place named Prayāga [Allahabad]. It was there that Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī came and met the Lord.**
