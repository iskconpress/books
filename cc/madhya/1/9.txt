====== CC Madhya 1.9 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva tāra āmi sūtra-mātra kailuṅ
> ye kichu viśeṣa, sūtra-madhyei kahiluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only the synopsis; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---did; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---whatever; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---specifics; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:m:madhyei]] [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have already stated within the synopsis.

===== Translation =====

**I have therefore given only a synopsis of those incidents, and whatever specifics were to be related have already been given in that synopsis.**
