====== CC Madhya 1.268 ======

===== Text =====

> manuṣyera veśa dhari' yātrikera chale
> prabhura darśana kare āsi' nīlācale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manuṣyera]]---of human beings; [[synonyms:v:veśa]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---dressing themselves; [[synonyms:y:yātrikerachale]]---as if pilgrims; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---visit; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**Dressing like human beings on pilgrimage, they all used to come to Jagannātha Purī to visit Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
