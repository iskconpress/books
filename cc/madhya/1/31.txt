====== CC Madhya 1.31 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu vraje pāṭhāila rūpa-sanātana
> prabhu-ājñāya dui bhāi āilā vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---to Vṛndāvana-dhāma; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---the two brothers Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---upon the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duibhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana-dhāma.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then sent the two brothers Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrīla Sanātana Gosvāmī to Vraja. By His order, they went to Śrī Vṛndāvana-dhāma.**
