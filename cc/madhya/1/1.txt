====== Cc. Madhya 1.1 ======

===== Text =====

> yasya prasādād ajño 'pi
> sadyaḥ sarva-jñatāṁ vrajet
> sa śrī-caitanya-devo me
> bhagavān samprasīdatu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom; [[synonyms:p:prasādāt]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:a:ajñaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---even a person who has no knowledge; [[synonyms:s:sadyaḥ]]---immediately; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jñatām]]---all knowledge; [[synonyms:v:vrajet]]---can achieve; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:devaḥ]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:me]]---on me; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:samprasīdatu]]---may He bestow His causeless mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Even a person with no knowledge can immediately acquire all knowledge simply by the benediction of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Therefore I am praying to the Lord for His causeless mercy upon me.**
