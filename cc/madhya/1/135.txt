====== Cc. Madhya 1.135 ======

===== Text =====

> pratāparudrere kṛpā kaila sei sthāne
> gauḍīyā-bhakte ājñā dila vidāyera dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratāparudrere]]---unto King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:seisthāne]]---in that garden; [[synonyms:g:gauḍīyā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---to all the devotees of Bengal; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:v:vidāyera]]---of departure; [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the day.

===== Translation =====

**In that garden, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed His mercy upon King Pratāparudra. Afterwards, when the Bengali devotees were about to return home, the Lord gave separate orders to almost every one of them.**
