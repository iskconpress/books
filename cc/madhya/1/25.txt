====== CC Madhya 1.25 ======

===== Text =====

> sahajei nityānanda---kṛṣṇa-premoddāma
> prabhu-ājñāya kaila yāhāṅ tāhāṅ prema-dāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahajei]]---by nature; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:uddāma]]---very much inspired in transcendental loving service to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---by the order of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---anywhere and everywhere; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]---distribution of that love.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu is by nature very much inspired in rendering transcendental loving service to Lord Kṛṣṇa. Now, being ordered by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, He distributed this loving service anywhere and everywhere.**
