====== CC Madhya 1.18 ======

===== Text =====

> śeṣa-līlāra 'madhya' 'antya',---dui nāma haya
> līlā-bhede vaiṣṇava saba nāma-bheda kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the //śeṣa-līlā,// or pastimes at the end; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]---the middle; [[synonyms:a:antya]]---the final; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---names; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhede]]---by the difference of pastimes; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---the devotees of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:b:bheda]]---different names; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---say.

===== Translation =====

**The final pastimes of the Lord, occurring in His last twenty-four years, are called madhya [middle] and antya [final]. All the devotees of the Lord refer to His pastimes according to these divisions.**
