====== CC Madhya 1.36 ======

===== Text =====

> ei saba grantha kaila gosāñi sanātana
> rūpa-gosāñi kaila yata, ke karu gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---compiled; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]] [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:karu]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---can count.

===== Translation =====

**We have already given the names of four books compiled by Sanātana Gosvāmī. Similarly, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī has also compiled many books, which no one can even count.**
