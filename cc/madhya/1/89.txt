====== Cc. Madhya 1.89 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsa kari' cabbiśa vatsara kailā ye ye karma
> ananta, apāra---tāra ke jānibe marma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after accepting the renounced order; [[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---twenty-four years; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:y:yeye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activities; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---insurmountable; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---will know; [[synonyms:m:marma]]---the purport.

===== Translation =====

**For the twenty-four years after Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the renounced order, whatever pastimes He executed were unlimited and unfathomable. Who can understand the purport of such pastimes ?**
