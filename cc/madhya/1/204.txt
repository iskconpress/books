====== Cc. Madhya 1.204 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane ayogya dekhi' mane pāṅ kṣobha
> tathāpi tomāra guṇe upajaya lobha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---ourselves; [[synonyms:a:ayogya]]---most unfit; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:p:pāṅ]]---get; [[synonyms:k:kṣobha]]---lamentation; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---in transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:u:upajaya]]---there is; [[synonyms:l:lobha]]---attraction.

===== Translation =====

**"We are very depressed at being unfit candidates for Your mercy. Yet since we have heard of Your transcendental qualities, we are very much attracted to You.**
