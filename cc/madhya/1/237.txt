====== CC Madhya 1.237 ======

===== Text =====

> dina kata tāhāṅ rahi' calilā vṛndāvana
> lukāñā calilā rātre, nā jāne kona jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:k:kata]]---a few days; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---started; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---for Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:l:lukāñā]]---keeping secret; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---started; [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---did not know; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---person.

===== Translation =====

**After remaining at Jagannātha Purī for a few days, the Lord secretly started for Vṛndāvana at night. He did this without anyone's knowledge.**
