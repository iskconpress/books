====== CC Madhya 1.207 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' mahāprabhu kahe,---śuna, dabira-khāsa
> tumi dui bhāi---mora purātana dāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:d:dabirakhāsa]]---Dabira Khāsa; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---two brothers; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:p:purātana]]---old; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servants.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing the prayer of Dabira Khāsa and Sākara Mallika, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Dabira Khāsa, you two brothers are My old servants.**
