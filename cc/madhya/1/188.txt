====== CC Madhya 1.188 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya dayā-maya
> patita-pāvana jaya, jaya mahāśaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---the most merciful; [[synonyms:p:patita]]-[[synonyms:p:pāvana]]---the savior of the fallen souls; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---glories; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---glories; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---to the great personality.

===== Translation =====

**"All glories to Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the most merciful savior of the fallen souls! All glories to the Supreme Personality!**
