====== CC Madhya 1.145 ======

===== Text =====

> guṇḍicāte nṛtya-ante kaila jala-keli
> herā-pañcamīte dekhila lakṣmī-devīra kelī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guṇḍicāte]]---in the neighborhood of the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:a:ante]]---after dancing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---sporting in the water; [[synonyms:h:herā]]-[[synonyms:p:pañcamīte]]---on the day of Herā-pañcamī; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:d:devīra]]---of the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:k:kelī]]---activities.

===== Translation =====

**After dancing in the Guṇḍicā temple, the Lord sported in the water with His devotees, and on Herā-pañcamī day they all saw the activities of the goddess of fortune, Lakṣmīdevī.**
