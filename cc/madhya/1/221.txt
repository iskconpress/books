====== CC Madhya 1.221 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā-pāśa ājñā māgi' calana-samaya
> prabhu-pade kahe kichu kariyā vinaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---from; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---taking; [[synonyms:c:calana]]-[[synonyms:s:samaya]]---at the time of departure; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---at the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**After begging the permission of all the Vaiṣṇavas present, the two brothers, at the time of their departure, humbly submitted something at the lotus feet of the Lord.**
