====== CC Madhya 1.217 ======

===== Text =====

> doṅhā āliṅgiyā prabhu balila bhakta-gaṇe
> sabe kṛpā kari' uddhāraha dui jane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:doṅhā]]---both of them; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:balila]]---said; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing; [[synonyms:u:uddhāraha]]---deliver; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---the two; [[synonyms:j:jane]]---persons.

===== Translation =====

**After this, the Lord embraced both of them and requested all of the devotees present to be merciful upon them and deliver them.**
