====== Cc. Madhya 1.73 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---tāre āmi santuṣṭa hañā
> āliṅgana kailu sarva-śakti sañcāriyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:santuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very much satisfied; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kailu]]---embraced; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---all potencies; [[synonyms:s:sañcāriyā]]---bestowing.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord said, "I was so much pleased with Rūpa Gosvāmī that I embraced him and bestowed upon him all necessary potencies for preaching the bhakti cult.**
