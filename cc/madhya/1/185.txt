====== CC Madhya 1.185 ======

===== Text =====

> dui guccha tṛṇa duṅhe daśane dhariñā
> gale vastra bāndhi' paḍe daṇḍavat hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:g:guccha]]---bunches; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]---of straw; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:d:daśane]]---in the teeth; [[synonyms:d:dhariñā]]---catching; [[synonyms:g:gale]]---on the neck; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---cloth; [[synonyms:b:bāndhi']]---binding; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fall; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---like rods; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**In great humility, both brothers took bunches of straw between their teeth and, each binding a cloth around his neck, fell down like rods before the Lord.**
