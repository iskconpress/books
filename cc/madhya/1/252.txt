~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 1.252~~

====== CC Madhya 1.252 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita-gosāñi kaila nīlācale vāsa
> vakreśvara, dāmodara, śaṅkara, haridāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---living; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---of the name Vakreśvara; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---of the name Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**Residing with the Lord at Jagannātha Purī were Paṇḍita Gosāñi and other devotees, such as Vakreśvara, Dāmodara, Śaṅkara and Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
