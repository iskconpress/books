====== Cc. Madhya 1.66 ======

===== Text =====

> daive āsi' prabhu yabe ūrdhvete cāhilā
> cāle goṅjā tāla-patre sei śloka pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daive]]---accidentally; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhvete]]---on the roof; [[synonyms:c:cāhilā]]---He looked; [[synonyms:c:cāle]]---in the roof; [[synonyms:g:goṅjā]]---pushed; [[synonyms:t:tāla]]-[[synonyms:p:patre]]---the palm leaf; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---got.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the residence of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, He accidentally saw the palm leaf on the roof, and thus He read the verse composed by him.**
