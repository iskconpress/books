====== CC Madhya 1.107 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-raṅga-kṣetra āilā kāverīra tīra
> śrī-raṅga dekhiyā preme ha-ilā asthira

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetra]]---to the place where the temple of Raṅganātha is situated; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:k:kāverīra]]---of the river Kāverī; [[synonyms:t:tīra]]---the bank; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---after seeing this temple; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:a:asthira]]---agitated.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came to the land of Śrī Raṅga-kṣetra, on the bank of the Kāverī, He visited the temple of Śrī Raṅganātha and was there overwhelmed in the ecstasy of love of Godhead.**
