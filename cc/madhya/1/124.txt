====== CC Madhya 1.124 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-sārvabhauma āgraha kariñā
> nīlācale āilā mahāprabhuke la-iñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---showing great eagerness; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iñā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**When the devotees from Bengal arrived at Jagannātha Purī, both Nityānanda Prabhu and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya greatly endeavored to take Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu back to Jagannātha Purī.**
