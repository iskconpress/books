====== CC Madhya 1.100 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda, jagadānanda, dāmodara, mukunda
> pāche āsi' mili' sabe pāila ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of the name Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---of the name Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---of the name Dāmodara; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---of the name Mukunda; [[synonyms:p:pācheāsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure

===== Translation =====

**The Lord had left Nityānanda's company and had gone alone to the Jagannātha temple, but later Nityānanda, Jagadānanda, Dāmodara and Mukunda came to see Him, and after seeing Him they were very pleased.**
