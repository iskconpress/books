====== Cc. Madhya 1.45 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata nānā grantha kariyā prakāśa
> goṣṭhī sahite kailā vṛndāvane vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---books; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---publication; [[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---family members; [[synonyms:s:sahite]]---with; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, Sanātana Gosvāmī and their nephew Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī, as well as practically all of their family members, lived in Vṛndāvana and published important books on devotional service.**
