====== Cc. Madhya 1.251 ======

===== Text =====

> nirantara nṛtya-gīta kīrtana-vilāsa
> ācaṇḍāle prema-bhakti karilā prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---without stopping; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---of //saṅkīrtana//; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---enjoyment; [[synonyms:ā:ā]]-[[synonyms:c:caṇḍāle]]---to everyone, even to the lowest person; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**At Jagannātha Purī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed chanting and dancing unceasingly. Thus He enjoyed the pastime of saṅkīrtana. He manifested His causeless mercy, pure love of God, to everyone, including the lowest man.**
