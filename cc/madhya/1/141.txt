====== Cc. Madhya 1.141 ======

===== Text =====

> pathe sārvabhauma saha sabāra milana
> sārvabhauma bhaṭṭācāryera kāśīte gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaumabhaṭṭācāryera]]---of the devotee named Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kāśīte]]---to Vārāṇasī; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone met Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya on his way to Vārāṇasī.**
