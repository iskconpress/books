====== Cc. Madhya 1.93 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda prabhu mahāprabhu bhulāiyā
> gaṅgā-tīre lañā āilā 'yamunā' baliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhulāiyā]]---bewildering; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]---the river Yamunā; [[synonyms:b:baliyā]]---informing.

===== Translation =====

**First of all, Lord Nityānanda bewildered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu by bringing Him along the banks of the Ganges, saying that it was the river Yamunā.**
