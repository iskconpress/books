====== CC Madhya 1.267 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmāṇḍa-bhitare haya caudda bhuvana
> caudda-bhuvane vaise yata jīva-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within the universe; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:c:cauddabhuvana]]---fourteen planetary systems; [[synonyms:c:caudda]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---in those fourteen planetary systems; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---reside; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as many as there are; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---living entities.

===== Translation =====

**There are fourteen planetary systems within the universe, and all living entities reside in those planetary systems.**
