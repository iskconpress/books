====== Cc. Madhya 1.157 ======

===== Text =====

> pathe dui dike puṣpa-bakulera śreṇī
> madhye madhye dui-pāśe divya puṣkariṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:d:duidike]]---on both sides; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:b:bakulera]]---of //bakula// flower trees; [[synonyms:ś:śreṇī]]---rows; [[synonyms:m:madhyemadhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---on both sides; [[synonyms:d:divya]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:p:puṣkariṇī]]---lakes.

===== Translation =====

**He mentally decorated both sides of the road with bakula flower trees, and at intervals on both sides he placed lakes of a transcendental nature.**
