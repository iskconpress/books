====== CC Madhya 1.102 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe ta' karilā prabhu dakṣiṇa gamana
> kūrma-kṣetre kaila vāsudeva vimocana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---to southern India; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---traveling; [[synonyms:k:kūrma]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---at the pilgrimage site known as Kūrma-kṣetra; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---of the name Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:v:vimocana]]---deliverance.

===== Translation =====

**After bestowing mercy upon Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, the Lord started for southern India. When He came to Kūrma-kṣetra, He delivered a person named Vāsudeva.**
