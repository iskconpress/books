====== CC Madhya 1.99 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma lañā gelā āpana-bhavana
> tṛtīya prahare prabhura ha-ila cetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---to his own house; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:p:prahare]]---in the afternoon; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness.

===== Translation =====

**After Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw Lord Jagannātha in the temple and fell down unconscious, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya took Him to his home. The Lord remained unconscious until the afternoon, when He finally regained His consciousness.**
