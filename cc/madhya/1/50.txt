====== Cc. Madhya 1.50 ======

===== Text =====

> viṁśati vatsara aiche kailā gatāgati
> anyonye duṅhāra duṅhā vinā nāhi sthiti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṁśati]]---twenty; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---years; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gata]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgati]]---going and coming; [[synonyms:a:anyonye]]---mutually; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of Lord Caitanya and the devotees; [[synonyms:d:duṅhā]]---the two; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---peace.

===== Translation =====

**For twenty consecutive years this meeting took place, and the situation became so intense that the Lord and the devotees could not be happy without meeting one another.**
