====== Cc. Madhya 1.272 ======

===== Text =====

> daśa-dike koṭī koṭī loka hena kāle
> jaya kṛṣṇa-caitanya' bali' kare kolāhale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---in the ten directions; [[synonyms:k:koṭī]] [[synonyms:k:koṭī]]---many thousands of men; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:h:henakāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:j:jayakṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---all glories to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---loudly crying; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:k:kolāhale]]---a tumultuous sound.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was apparently in a angry mood and chastising His devotees, many thousands of people outside loudly cried in a tumultuous voice, "All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu!"**
