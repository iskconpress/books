====== Cc. Madhya 1.245 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurā pāṭhāilā tāṅre diyā bhakti-bala
> sannyāsīre kṛpā kari' gelā nīlācala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]]---to Mathurā; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:b:bala]]---the strength of devotion; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīre]]---unto the Māyāvādī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---giving; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went back; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---to Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**After fully instructing Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sent him to Mathurā with empowered devotional service. In Benares He also bestowed His mercy upon the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs. He then returned to Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī].**
