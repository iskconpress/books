====== CC Madhya 1.182 ======

===== Text =====

> ghare āsi' dui bhāi yukati kariñā
> prabhu dekhibāre cale veśa lukāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghareāsi']]---after returning home; [[synonyms:d:duibhāi]]---two brothers; [[synonyms:y:yukati]]---arguments; [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---making; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---go; [[synonyms:v:veśa]]---dress; [[synonyms:l:lukāñā]]---hiding.

===== Translation =====

**After returning to his residence, Dabira Khāsa and his brother decided after much consideration to go see the Lord incognito.**
