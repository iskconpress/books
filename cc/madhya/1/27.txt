====== CC Madhya 1.27 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñi yāṅre bale 'baḍa bhāi'
> teṅho kahe, mora prabhu---caitanya-gosāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---unto whom; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---elder brother; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:m:moraprabhu]]---My Lord; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---the supreme master, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya Mahāprabhu used to address Nityānanda Prabhu as His elder brother, whereas Nityānanda Prabhu addressed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as His Lord.**
