====== CC Madhya 1.136 ======

===== Text =====

> pratyabda āsibe ratha-yātrā-daraśane
> ei chale cāhe bhakta-gaṇera milane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:a:abda]]---every year; [[synonyms:ā:āsibe]]---you should all come; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the car festival; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see; [[synonyms:e:eichale]]---under this plea; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---desires; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:milane]]---the meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu desired to meet all the devotees of Bengal every year. Therefore He ordered them to come to see the Ratha-yātrā festival every year.**
