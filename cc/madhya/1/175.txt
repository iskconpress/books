====== Cc. Madhya 1.175 ======

===== Text =====

> dabira khāsere rājā puchila nibhṛte
> gosāñira mahimā teṅho lāgila kahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dabira]] [[synonyms:k:khāsere]]---of the name Dabira Khāsa (then the name of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī); [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in privacy; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak.

===== Translation =====

**In private, the King inquired from Dabira Khāsa [Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī], who began to speak about the glories of the Lord.**
