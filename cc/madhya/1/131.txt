====== CC Madhya 1.131 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍa ha-ite sarva vaiṣṇavera āgamana
> kulīna-grāma-vāsi-saṅge prathama milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from Bengal; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---appearance; [[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsi]]---the residents of Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with them; [[synonyms:p:prathama]]---first; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees from Bengal gradually began arriving at Jagannātha Purī. At this time, the residents of Kulīna-grāma also came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for the first time.**
