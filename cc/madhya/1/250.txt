====== Cc. Madhya 1.250 ======

===== Text =====

> prativarṣa āisena tāhāṅ gauḍera bhakta-gaṇa
> cāri māsa rahe prabhura saṅge sammilana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prativarṣa]]---each year; [[synonyms:ā:āisena]]---visit; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]]---of Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four; [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---months; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remain; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:s:sammilana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**During those eighteen years, all the devotees of Bengal used to visit Him at Jagannātha Purī every year. They would remain there for four continuous months and enjoy the company of the Lord.**
