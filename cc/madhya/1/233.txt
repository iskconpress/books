====== Cc. Madhya 1.233 ======

===== Text =====

> śacī-devī āni' tāṅre kaila namaskāra
> sāta dina tāṅra ṭhāñi bhikṣā-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:d:devī]]---mother Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---calling her; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:s:sātadina]]---seven days; [[synonyms:t:tāṅraṭhāñi]]---from Śacīdevī; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---accepting meals.

===== Translation =====

**Taking this opportunity, Śrī Advaita Ācārya Prabhu sent for mother Śacīdevī, and she remained at His house for seven days to prepare the meals for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
