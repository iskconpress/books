====== Cc. Madhya 1.205 ======

===== Text =====

> vāmana yaiche cāṅda dharite cāhe kare
> taiche ei vāñchā mora uṭhaye antare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāmana]]---a dwarf; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:c:cāṅda]]---the moon; [[synonyms:d:dharite]]---to capture; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---our; [[synonyms:u:uṭhaye]]---awakens; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the mind.

===== Translation =====

**"Indeed, we are like a dwarf who wants to capture the moon. Although we are completely unfit, a desire to receive Your mercy is awakening within our minds.**
