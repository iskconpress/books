====== Cc. Madhya 1.168 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍeśvara yavana-rājā prabhāva śuniñā
> kahite lāgila kichu vismita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---King of Bengal; [[synonyms:y:yavana]]-[[synonyms:r:rājā]]---Muslim king; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---influence; [[synonyms:ś:śuniñā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to say; [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---astonished; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**When the Muslim King of Bengal heard of Caitanya Mahāprabhu's influence in attracting innumerable people, he became very astonished and began to speak as follows.**

===== Purport =====

At that time the Muslim king of Bengal was Nawab Hussain Shah Bādasāha.
