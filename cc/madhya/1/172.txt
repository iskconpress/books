====== CC Madhya 1.172 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikhārī sannyāsī kare tīrtha paryaṭana
> tāṅre dekhibāre āise dui cāri jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikhāri]]---beggar; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---mendicant; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---of holy places; [[synonyms:p:paryaṭana]]---touring; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:d:dekhibṁre]]---to see; [[synonyms:ṁ:ṁise]]---come; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:c:cṁri]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---only a few people.

===== Translation =====

**Keśava Chatrī informed the Muslim King that Caitanya Mahāprabhu was a mendicant touring different places of pilgrimage and that, as such, only a few people came to see Him.**
