====== CC Madhya 1.79 ======

===== Text =====

> rāja-veśa, hātī, ghoḍā, manuṣya gahana
> kāhāṅ gopa-veśa, kāhāṅ nirjana vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---royal dress; [[synonyms:h:hātī]]---elephants; [[synonyms:g:ghoḍā]]---horses; [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---men; [[synonyms:g:gahana]]---crowds; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---the dress of a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:n:nirjana]]---solitary; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---of the name Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**She thought of Him in the calm and quiet atmosphere of Vṛndāvana, dressed as a cowherd boy. But at Kurukṣetra He was in a royal dress and was accompanied by elephants, horses and crowds of men. Thus the atmosphere was not congenial for Their meeting.**
