====== CC Madhya 1.158 ======

===== Text =====

> ratna-bāṅdhā ghāṭa, tāhe praphulla kamala
> nānā pakṣi-kolāhala, sudhā-sama jala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:b:bāṅdhā]]---constructed with jewels; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭa]]---bathing places; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---there; [[synonyms:p:praphulla]]---fully blossoming; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]---lotus flowers; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:p:pakṣi]]---of birds; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---vibrations; [[synonyms:s:sudhā]]---nectar; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water.

===== Translation =====

**These lakes had bathing places constructed with jewels, and they were filled with blossoming lotus flowers. There were various birds chirping, and the water was exactly like nectar.**
