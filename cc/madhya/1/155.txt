====== CC Madhya 1.155 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana yābena prabhu śuni' nṛsiṁhānanda
> patha sājāila mane pāiyā ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yābena]]---will go; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhānanda]]---of the name Nṛsiṁhānanda; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---the way; [[synonyms:s:sājāila]]---decorated; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:p:pāiyā]]---getting; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī heard that Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go to Vṛndāvana, he became very pleased and mentally began decorating the way there.**
