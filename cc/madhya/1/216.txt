====== Cc. Madhya 1.216 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali duṅhāra śire dharila dui hāte
> dui bhāi prabhu-pada nila nija māthe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etabali]]---saying this; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on the heads of both of them; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---placed; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---hands; [[synonyms:d:duibhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]---the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:n:nijamāthe]]---on their own heads.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then placed His two hands on the heads of both of them, and in return they immediately placed the lotus feet of the Lord on their heads.**
