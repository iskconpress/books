====== CC Madhya 1.248 ======

===== Text =====

> madhya-līlāra kailuṅ ei sūtra-vivaraṇa
> antya-līlāra sūtra ebe śuna, bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the //madhya-līlā,// the middle portion of His pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have made; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---synopsis; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:a:antya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes at the end, known as //antya-līlā//; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---synopsis; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have given a synopsis of the madhya-līlā, the middle pastimes of the Lord. Now, O devotees, kindly hear the synopsis of the final pastimes of the Lord, known as antya-līlā.**
