{{description>" 'If a woman is attached to a man other than her husband, she will appear very busy in carrying out her household affairs, but within her heart she is always relishing feelings of association with her paramour.'}}

====== Cc. Madhya 1.211 ======

===== Text =====

> para-vyasaninī nārī
> vyagrāpi gṛha-karmasu
> tad evāsvādayaty antar
> nava-saṅga-rasāyanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyasaninī]]---attached to another man; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---a woman; [[synonyms:v:vyagrāapi]]---although zealous; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:k:karmasu]]---in household affairs; [[synonyms:t:tateva]]---that only; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādayati]]---tastes; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---within herself; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---of new association; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:a:ayanam]]---mellow.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'If a woman is attached to a man other than her husband, she will appear very busy in carrying out her household affairs, but within her heart she is always relishing feelings of association with her paramour.'**
