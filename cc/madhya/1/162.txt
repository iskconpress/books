====== Cc. Madhya 1.162 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kānāñira nāṭaśālā' haite āsiba phiriñā
> jānibe paścāt, kahilu niścaya kariñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kānāñira]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śālā]]---the place of the name Kānāi Nāṭaśālā; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:ā:āsiba]]---will come; [[synonyms:p:phiriñā]]---returning; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---you will know; [[synonyms:p:paścāt]]---later; [[synonyms:k:kahilu]]---I say; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---assurance; [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---making.

===== Translation =====

**Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī said, "The Lord will go to Kānāi Nāṭaśālā and then will return. All of you will come to know of this later, but I now say this with great assurance."**
