====== CC Madhya 1.20 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ yei līlā, tāra 'madhya-līlā' nāma
> tāra pāche līlā---'antya-līlā' abhidhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---in those places; [[synonyms:y:yeilīlā]]---all the pastimes; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of those; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the middle pastimes; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāche]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---all the pastimes after that period; [[synonyms:a:antya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---last pastimes; [[synonyms:a:abhidhāna]]---the nomenclature.

===== Translation =====

**All the pastimes performed by the Lord in those places are known as the madhya-līlā, and whatever pastimes were performed after that are called the antya-līlā.**
