====== CC Madhya 1.111 ======

===== Text =====

> cāturmāsya-ante punaḥ dakṣiṇa gamana
> paramānanda-purī saha tāhāñi milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]]-[[synonyms:a:ante]]---at the end of Cāturmāsya; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---traveling in South India; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---of the name Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:t:tāhāñi]]---there; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**After the end of Cāturmāsya, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued traveling throughout South India. At that time He met Paramānanda Purī.**
