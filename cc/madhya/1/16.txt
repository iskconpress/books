====== CC Madhya 1.16 ======

===== Text =====

> cabbiśa vatsara śeṣe yei māgha-māsa
> tāra śukla-pakṣe prabhu karilā sannyāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---of those twenty-four years; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---which; [[synonyms:m:māgha]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---the month of Māgha (January-February); [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that month; [[synonyms:ś:śukla]]-[[synonyms:p:pakṣe]]---during the fortnight of the waxing moon; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---accepted; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the renounced order of life.

===== Translation =====

**At the end of His twenty-fourth year, in the month of Māgha, during the fortnight of the waxing moon, the Lord accepted the renounced order of life, sannyāsa.**
