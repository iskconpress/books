====== Cc. Madhya 1.19 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye chaya vatsara---gamanāgamana
> nīlācala-gauḍa-setubandha-vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that period; [[synonyms:c:chayavatsara]]---for six years; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---going and coming; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---from Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:s:setubandha]]---and from Cape Comorin; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana-dhāma.

===== Translation =====

**For six years of the last twenty-four, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu traveled all over India from Jagannātha Purī to Bengal and from Cape Comorin to Vṛndāvana.**
