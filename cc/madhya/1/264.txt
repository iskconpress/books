====== CC Madhya 1.264 ======

===== Text =====

> pradyumna miśrere prabhu rāmānanda-sthāne
> kṛṣṇa-kathā śunāila kahi' tāṅra guṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pradyumna]] [[synonyms:m:miśrere]]---of the name Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śunāila]]---caused to hear; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---explaining; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---the transcendental qualities.

===== Translation =====

**After explaining the transcendental qualities of Rāmānanda Rāya, the Lord sent Pradyumna Miśra to the residence of Rāmānanda Rāya, and Pradyumna Miśra learned kṛṣṇa-kathā from him.**
