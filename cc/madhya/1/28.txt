====== CC Madhya 1.28 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi āpani haye prabhu balarāma
> tathāpi caitanyera kare dāsa-abhimāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:h:haye]]---is; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord; [[synonyms:b:balarāma]]---Balarāma; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---accepts; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---conception as the eternal servant.

===== Translation =====

**Although Nityānanda Prabhu is none other than Balarāma Himself, He nonetheless always thinks of Himself as the eternal servant of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
