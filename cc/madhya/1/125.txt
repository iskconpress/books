====== Cc. Madhya 1.125 ======

===== Text =====

> virahe vihvala prabhu nā jāne rātri-dine
> hena-kāle āilā gauḍera bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]]---of Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu finally left Ālālanātha to return to Jagannātha Purī, He was overwhelmed both day and night due to separation from Jagannātha. His lamentation knew no bounds. During this time, all the devotees from different parts of Bengal, and especially from Navadvīpa, arrived in Jagannātha Purī.**
