====== CC Madhya 1.133 ======

===== Text =====

> snāna-yātrā dekhi' prabhu saṅge bhakta-gaṇa
> sabā lañā kailā prabhu guṇḍicā mārjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:snāna]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the bathing ceremony; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]] [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---washing and cleaning the Guṇḍicā temple.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the bathing ceremony of Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu washed and cleaned Śrī Guṇḍicā temple with the assistance of many devotees.**
