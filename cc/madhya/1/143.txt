====== CC Madhya 1.143 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā lañā kaila guṇḍicā-gṛha-sammārjana
> ratha-yātrā-daraśane prabhura nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabālañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:s:sammārjana]]---washing of the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the car festival; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---in seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**First the Lord washed the temple of Guṇḍicā very thoroughly. Then everyone saw the Ratha-yātrā festival and the Lord's dancing before the car.**
