====== CC Madhya 1.247 ======

===== Text =====

> ānande bhakta-saṅge sadā kīrtana-vilāsa
> jagannātha-daraśana, premera vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great delight; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with devotees; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---of chanting; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---enjoyment; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of ecstasy; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**While at Jagannātha Purī, the Lord passed His time in great joy by performing saṅkīrtana and visiting the temple of Jagannātha in great ecstasy.**
