====== CC Madhya 1.119 ======

===== Text =====

> sei purātana patra āgraha kari' nila
> rāmadāse dekhāiyā duḥkha khaṇḍāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:purātana]]---old; [[synonyms:p:patra]]---page; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]]---with great enthusiasm; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:d:dāse]]---to the //brāhmaṇa// Rāmadāsa; [[synonyms:d:dekhāiyā]]---showing; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāila]]---mitigated.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu eagerly tore this page from the Kūrma Purāṇa, although the book was very old, and He later showed it to Rāmadāsa Vipra, whose unhappiness was mitigated.**
