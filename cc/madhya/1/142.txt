====== CC Madhya 1.142 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure mililā sarva vaiṣṇava āsiyā
> jala-krīḍā kaila prabhu sabāre la-iyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---arriving at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---sporting in the water; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**After arriving at Jagannātha Purī, all the Vaiṣṇavas met with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Later, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sported in the water, taking all the devotees with Him.**
