====== CC Madhya 1.5 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīmān rāsa-rasārambhī
> vaṁśī-vaṭa-taṭa-sthitaḥ
> karṣan veṇu-svanair gopīr
> gopīnāthaḥ śriye 'stu naḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīmān]]---the most beautiful form; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]---of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārambhī]]---the initiator of the mellow; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṭa]]---the celebrated place named Vaṁśīvaṭa; [[synonyms:t:taṭa]]---on the bank of Yamunā; [[synonyms:s:sthitaḥ]]---being situated; [[synonyms:k:karṣan]]---attracting; [[synonyms:v:veṇu]]-[[synonyms:s:svanaiḥ]]---by the sounds of the flute; [[synonyms:g:gopīḥ]]---all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāthaḥ]]---the master of all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:ś:śriye]]---the opulence of love and affection; [[synonyms:a:astu]]---let there be; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---upon us.

===== Translation =====

**May Gopīnāthajī, who attracts all the gopīs with the song of His flute and who has begun the most melodious rāsa dance on the bank of the Yamunā in Vaṁśīvaṭa, be merciful upon us.**
