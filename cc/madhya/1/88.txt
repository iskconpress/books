====== CC Madhya 1.88 ======

===== Text =====

> dvādaśa vatsara śeṣa aiche goṅāila
> ei mata śeṣa-līlā tri-vidhāne kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvādaśa]]---twelve; [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---years; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---final; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:g:goṅāila]]---passed away; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the last pastimes; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhāne]]---in three ways; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---executed.

===== Translation =====

**The last twelve years of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were passed in this transcendental craziness. Thus He executed His last pastimes in three ways.**
