====== CC Madhya 1.236 ======

===== Text =====

> balabhadra bhaṭṭācārya, āra paṇḍita dāmodara
> dui-jana-saṅge prabhu āilā nīlācala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:balabhadra]] [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---of the name Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went back; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---to Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**The two persons who accompanied Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to Jagannātha Purī [Nīlācala] were Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya and Dāmodara Paṇḍita.**
