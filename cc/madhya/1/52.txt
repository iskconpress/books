====== Cc. Madhya 1.52 ======

===== Text =====

> nirantara rātri-dina viraha unmāde
> hāse, kānde, nāce, gāya parama viṣāde

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---without cessation; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---night and day; [[synonyms:v:viraha]]---of separation; [[synonyms:u:unmāde]]---in madness; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laughs; [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---cries; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chants; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---great; [[synonyms:v:viṣāde]]---in moroseness.

===== Translation =====

**In the attitude of separation, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu appeared mad both day and night. Sometimes He laughed, and sometimes He cried; sometimes He danced, and sometimes He chanted in great sorrow.**
