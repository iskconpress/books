====== Cc. Madhya 1.11-12 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye yei bhāga dāsa-vṛndāvana
> 'caitanya-maṅgale' vistāri' karilā varṇana
>
> sei bhāgera ihāṅ sūtra-mātra likhiba
> tāhāṅ ye viśeṣa kichu, ihāṅ vistāriba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---amongst them; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---which; [[synonyms:b:bhāga]]---portion; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgale]]---in his book //Caitanya-maṅgala//; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---elaborating; [[synonyms:k:karilāvarṇana]]---has described; [[synonyms:s:seibhāgera]]---of that portion; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here in this book; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---the synopsis only; [[synonyms:l:likhiba]]---I shall write; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---special details; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅvistāriba]]---I shall describe elaborately.

===== Translation =====

**I shall describe only in synopsis that portion which Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has described very elaborately in his book Caitanya-maṅgala. Whatever incidents are outstanding, however, I shall later elaborate.**
