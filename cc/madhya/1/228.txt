====== Cc. Madhya 1.228 ======

===== Text =====

> sei rātre prabhu tāhāṅ cinte mane mana
> saṅge saṅghaṭṭa bhāla nahe, kaila sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---that night; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:c:cinte]]---thinks; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within His mind; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭa]]---crowds of men; [[synonyms:b:bhālanahe]]---is not good; [[synonyms:k:kailasanātana]]---Sanātana has so spoken.

===== Translation =====

**That night the Lord considered Sanātana Gosvāmī's proposal that He should not go to Vṛndāvana followed by so many people.**
