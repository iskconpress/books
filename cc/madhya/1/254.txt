====== Cc. Madhya 1.254 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣetra-vāsī rāmānanda rāya prabhṛti
> prabhu-saṅge ei saba kaila nitya-sthiti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣetra]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---residents of Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---of the name Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:prabhṛti]]---and others; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the Lord; [[synonyms:e:eisaba]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---permanently living.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya and other devotees who were residents of Jagannātha Purī also remained permanently with the Lord.**
