====== Cc. Madhya 1.179 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra citte caitanyere kaiche haya jñāna
> tomāra citte yei laya, sei ta' pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in your mind; [[synonyms:c:caitanyere]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---mind; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---takes; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---that is evidence.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī informed the King about his mind as a way of knowing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He assured the King that whatever occurred in his mind could be considered evidence.**
