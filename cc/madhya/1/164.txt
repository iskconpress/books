====== CC Madhya 1.164 ======

===== Text =====

> yāhāṅ yāya prabhu, tāhāṅ koṭi-saṅkhya loka
> dekhite āise, dekhi' khaṇḍe duḥkha-śoka

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkhya]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---an unlimited number of people; [[synonyms:d:dekhiteāise]]---come to see Him; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍe]]---removes; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---lamentation.

===== Translation =====

**Wherever the Lord visited, crowds of innumerable people came to see Him. When they saw Him, all their unhappiness and lamentation disappeared.**
