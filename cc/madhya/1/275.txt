====== CC Madhya 1.275 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā lokera dainya dravilā hṛdaya
> bāhire āsi' daraśana dilā dayā-maya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]---humility; [[synonyms:d:dravilā]]---became softened; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---the heart; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---audience; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---the merciful.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord heard the humble petition made by the people, his heart softened. Being very merciful, He immediately came out and gave audience to all of them.**
