====== CC Madhya 1.193 ======

===== Text =====

> brāhmaṇa-jāti tārā, navadvīpe ghara
> nīca-sevā nāhi kare, nahe nīcera kūrpara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---born in a //brāhmaṇa// family; [[synonyms:t:tārā]]---they; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---the holy place of Navadvīpa-dhāma; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---their house; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service to degraded persons; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:n:nīcera]]---of low persons; [[synonyms:k:kūrpara]]---an instrument.

===== Translation =====

**"The brothers Jagāi and Mādhāi belonged to the brāhmaṇa caste, and their residence was in the holy place of Navadvīpa. They never served low-class persons, nor were they instruments to abominable activities.**
