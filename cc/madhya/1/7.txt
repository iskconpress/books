====== Cc. Madhya 1.7 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya nityānanda jayādvaita-candra
> jaya śrīvāsādi jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---all glories to Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---to all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---all glories unto the devotees of Lord Gaurasundara.

===== Translation =====

**All glories unto Lord Nityānanda and Advaita Prabhu, and all glories unto all the devotees of Lord Caitanya, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura!**
