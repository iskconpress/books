====== Cc. Madhya 1.261 ======

===== Text =====

> tuṣṭa hañā prabhu tāṅre pāṭhāilā vṛndāvana
> advaitera haste prabhura adbhuta bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very much pleased; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent back; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:a:advaitera]]---of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:haste]]---in the hands; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---feasting.

===== Translation =====

**Being pleased, the Lord sent Sanātana Gosvāmī back to Vṛndāvana. After that, He was fed wonderfully by the hands of Śrī Advaita Ācārya.**
