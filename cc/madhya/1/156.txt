====== CC Madhya 1.156 ======

===== Text =====

> kuliyā nagara haite patha ratne bāndhāila
> nivṛnta puṣpa-śayyā upare pātila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuliyā]] [[synonyms:n:nagara]]---the city of Kuliyā; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---way; [[synonyms:r:ratne]]---with jewels; [[synonyms:b:bāndhāila]]---constructed; [[synonyms:n:nivṛnta]]---stemless; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śayyā]]---flower bed; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---on top; [[synonyms:p:pātila]]---laid down.

===== Translation =====

**First Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī contemplated a broad road starting from the city of Kuliyā. He bedecked the road with jewels, upon which he then laid a bed of stemless flowers.**
