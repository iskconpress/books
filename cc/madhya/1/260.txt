====== CC Madhya 1.260 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sanātana-gosāñira punar-āgamana
> jyaiṣṭha-māse prabhu tāṅre kaila parīkṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---again coming; [[synonyms:j:jyaiṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:m:māse]]---in the month of Jyaiṣṭha (May-June); [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣaṇa]]---examination.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter Sanātana Gosvāmī met the Lord again, and the Lord tested him in scorching heat during the month of Jyaiṣṭha.**
