====== CC Madhya 1.177 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra maṅgala vāñche, kārya-siddhi haya
> ihāra āśīrvāde tomāra sarvatra-i jaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]---good fortune; [[synonyms:v:vāñche]]---He desires; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---of business; [[synonyms:s:siddhi]]---the perfection; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:ā:āśīrvāde]]---by the blessings; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---victory.

===== Translation =====

**"This prophet always desires your good fortune. By His grace, all your business is successful. By His blessings, you will attain victory everywhere.**
