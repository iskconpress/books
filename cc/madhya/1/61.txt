====== Cc. Madhya 1.61 ======

===== Text =====

> śloka kari' eka tāla-patrete likhiyā
> āpana vāsāra cāle rākhila guñjiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---composing the verse; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:t:tāla]]-[[synonyms:p:patrete]]---on a palm leaf; [[synonyms:l:likhiyā]]---writing; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]---his own; [[synonyms:v:vāsāra]]---of the residence; [[synonyms:c:cāle]]---on the roof; [[synonyms:r:rākhila]]---kept; [[synonyms:g:guñjiyā]]---pushing.

===== Translation =====

**After composing this verse, Rūpa Gosvāmī wrote it on a palm leaf and put it on the roof of the thatched house in which he was living.**
