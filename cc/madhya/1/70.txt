====== Cc. Madhya 1.70 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' tāṇre bahu prasāda kariyā
> svarūpa-gosāñire śloka dekhāila lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---showing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---unto Svarūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed various benedictions upon Rūpa Gosvāmī, and taking the verse, He later showed it to Svarūpa Gosvāmī.**
