====== CC Madhya 1.121 ======

===== Text =====

> punarapi nīlācale gamana karila
> bhakta-gaṇe meliyā snāna-yātrā dekhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going back; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:meliyā]]---meeting; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the bathing ceremony of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw.

===== Translation =====

**After collecting these books, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Jagannātha Purī. At that time, the bathing ceremony of Jagannātha was taking place, and He saw it.**
