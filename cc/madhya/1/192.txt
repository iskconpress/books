====== CC Madhya 1.192 ======

===== Text =====

> jagāi-mādhāi dui karile uddhāra
> tāhāṅ uddhārite śrama nahila tomāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagāi]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhāi]]---the two brothers Jagāi and Mādhāi; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:k:karile]]---You did; [[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---deliverance; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:u:uddhārite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---exertion; [[synonyms:n:nahila]]---there was not; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You.

===== Translation =====

**"You have delivered the two brothers Jagāi and Mādhāi, but to deliver them You did not have to exert Yourself very much.**
