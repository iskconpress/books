====== CC Madhya 1.243 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rūpe śikṣā karāi' pāṭhāilā vṛndāvana
> āpane karilā vārāṇasī āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]] [[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāi']]---teaching Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---toward Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---Himself; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]]---to Benares; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**After instructing Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī at Prayāga at the Daśāśvamedha-ghāṭa, Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered him to go to Vṛndāvana. The Lord then returned to Vārāṇasī.**
