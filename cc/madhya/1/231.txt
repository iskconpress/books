====== CC Madhya 1.231 ======

===== Text =====

> eta cinti prātaḥ-kāle gaṅgā-snāna kari'
> 'nīlācale yāba' bali' calilā gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etacinti]]---thus thinking; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing in the Ganges; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:n:nīlācaleyāba]]---I shall go to Nīlācala (Jagannātha Purī); [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---started; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking like this, the Lord took His morning bath in the Ganges and started for Nīlācala, saying "I shall go there."**
