====== Cc. Madhya 1.265 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnātha paṭṭanāyaka---rāmānanda-bhrātā
> rājā māritechila, prabhu haila trātā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnāthapaṭṭanāyaka]]---of the name Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrātā]]---the brother of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:m:māritechila]]---condemned to death; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:t:trātā]]---the deliverer.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu saved Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka, the younger brother of Rāmānanda Rāya, from being condemned to death by the King.**
