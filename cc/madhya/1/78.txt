====== Cc. Madhya 1.78 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rādhikā kurukṣetre kṛṣṇera daraśana
> yadyapi pāyena, tabu bhāvena aichana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kuru]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣetre]]---on the field of Kurukṣetra; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:p:pāyena]]---She gets; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:b:bhāvena]]---thinks; [[synonyms:a:aichana]]---in this way.

===== Translation =====

**The subject of His thoughts was Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, who met Kṛṣṇa on the field of Kurukṣetra. Although She met Kṛṣṇa there, She was nonetheless thinking of Him in the following way.**
