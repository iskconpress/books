====== CC Madhya 1.282 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' loke kari' śubha-dṛṣṭi dāna
> abhyantare gelā, lokera pūrṇa haila kāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---thus saying; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---unto the people; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:ś:śubha]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭi]]---auspicious glance; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---charity; [[synonyms:a:abhyantare]]---within the room; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all the people; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---fulfilled; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---the desire.

===== Translation =====

**Thus speaking, the Lord entered His room after glancing auspiciously upon the people out of charity. In this way the desires of the people were completely fulfilled.**
