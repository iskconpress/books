====== Cc. Madhya 1.235 ======

===== Text =====

> janā dui saṅge āmi yāba nīlācale
> āmāre milibā āsi' ratha-yātrā-kāle

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:janā]]---persons; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:y:yāba]]---shall go; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]]---Me; [[synonyms:m:milibā]]---will meet; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---during the time of the car festival.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, although requesting all the devotees to go back, allowed two people to follow Him. He requested all the devotees to come to Jagannātha Purī and meet Him during the car festival.**
