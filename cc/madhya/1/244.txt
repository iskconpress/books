====== CC Madhya 1.244 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśīte prabhuke āsi' mililā sanātana
> dui māsa rahi' tāṅre karāilā śikṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśīte]]---at Vārāṇasī; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---arriving; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---of the name Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---months; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---did; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣaṇa]]---instruction.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived at Vārāṇasī, Sanātana Gosvāmī met Him there. The Lord remained there for two months and instructed Sanātana Gosvāmī perfectly.**
