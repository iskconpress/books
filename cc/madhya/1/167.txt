====== Cc. Madhya 1.167 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ nṛtya kare prabhu preme acetana
> koṭi koṭi loka āise dekhite caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:acetana]]---unconscious; [[synonyms:k:koṭikoṭi]]---innumerable; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---His lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**While performing saṅkīrtana in Rāmakeli-grāma, the Lord danced and sometimes lost consciousness due to love of God. While at Rāmakeli-grāma, an unlimited number of people came to see His lotus feet.**
