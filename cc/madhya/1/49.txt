====== CC Madhya 1.49 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-ajñāya bhakta-gaṇa pratyabda āsiyā
> guṇḍicā dekhiyā yā'na prabhure miliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:a:ajñāya]]---upon the order of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:pratyabda]]---every year; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming there; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]---the festival of Guṇḍicā-yātrā; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:y:yā'na]]---return; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Following the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the devotees used to visit Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu every year. They would see the Guṇḍicā festival at Jagannātha Purī and then return home after four months.**
