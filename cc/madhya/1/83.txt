====== CC Madhya 1.83 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgavatera śloka-gūḍhārtha viśada kariñā
> rūpa-gosāñi śloka kaila loka bujhāiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavatera]]---of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---confidential meaning; [[synonyms:v:viśada]]---elaborate description; [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---doing; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---compiled; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:b:bujhāiñā]]---making understand.

===== Translation =====

**In one verse, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī has explained the confidential meaning of the verse of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam for the understanding of the general populace.**
