====== CC Madhya 1.273 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya mahāprabhu---vrajendra-kumāra
> jagat tārite prabhu, tomāra avatāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajayamahāprabhu]]---all glories to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:k:kumāra]]---originally Lord Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:t:tārite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation.

===== Translation =====

**All the people began to call very loudly, "All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is the son of Mahārāja Nanda! Now You have appeared in order to deliver the whole world!**
