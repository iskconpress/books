====== CC Madhya 1.146 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-janma-yātrāte prabhu gopa-veśa hailā
> dadhi-bhāra vahi' tabe laguḍa phirāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrāte]]---on the birthday ceremony of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]]---dressed like a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---was; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāra]]---a balance for pots of yogurt; [[synonyms:v:vahi']]---carrying; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:l:laguḍa]]---a rod; [[synonyms:p:phirāilā]]---wheeled about.

===== Translation =====

**On Janmāṣṭamī, Lord Kṛṣṇa's birthday, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dressed Himself as a cowherd boy. At that time He carried a balance with pots of yogurt and wheeled a rod about.**
