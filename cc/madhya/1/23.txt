====== CC Madhya 1.23 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye chaya vatsara bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> prema-bhakti pravartāilā nṛtya-gīta-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that period; [[synonyms:c:chayavatsara]]---for six years; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---the loving service of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:pravartāilā]]---introduced; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the matter of chanting and dancing.

===== Translation =====

**Of these eighteen years at Jagannātha Purī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spent six years with His many devotees. By chanting and dancing, He introduced the loving service of the Lord.**
