====== Cc. Madhya 1.15 ======

===== Text =====

> cabbiśa vatsara prabhura gṛhe avasthāna
> tāhāṅ ye karilā līlā---'ādi-līlā' nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cabbiśa]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---for twenty-four years; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at home; [[synonyms:a:avasthāna]]---residing; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---He performed; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---are called //ādi-līlā.//

===== Translation =====

**For twenty-four years, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at home, and whatever pastimes He performed during that time are called the ādi-līlā.**
