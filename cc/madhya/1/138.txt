====== Cc. Madhya 1.138 ======

===== Text =====

> varṣāntare advaitādi bhaktera āgamana
> prabhure dekhite sabe karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---at the end of the year; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---coming to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going to Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**At the end of the year, all the devotees from Bengal, headed by Advaita Ācārya, again came to see the Lord. Indeed, there was a great rush of devotees to Jagannātha Purī.**
