====== CC Madhya 1.176 ======

===== Text =====

> ye tomāre rājya dila, ye tomāra gosāñā
> tomāra deśe tomāra bhāgye janmilā āsiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]---that one who; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---unto you; [[synonyms:r:rājya]]---kingdom; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---the one who; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:gosāñā]]---prophet; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---in your country; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---on account of your good fortune; [[synonyms:j:janmilā]]---took birth; [[synonyms:ā:āsiñā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī said, "The Supreme Personality of Godhead, who gave you this kingdom and whom you accept as a prophet, has taken birth in your country due to your good fortune.**
