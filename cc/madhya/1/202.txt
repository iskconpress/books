====== CC Madhya 1.202 ======

===== Text =====

> more dayā kari' kara sva-dayā saphala
> akhila brahmāṇḍa dekhuka tomāra dayā-bala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:more]]---to us; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---make; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:d:dayā]]---Your own mercy; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---successful; [[synonyms:a:akhila]]---throughout; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---the universe; [[synonyms:d:dekhuka]]---let it be seen; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:b:bala]]---power of mercy.

===== Translation =====

**"We are the most fallen; therefore by showing us Your mercy, Your mercy is most successful. Let the power of Your mercy be exhibited throughout the entire universe!**
