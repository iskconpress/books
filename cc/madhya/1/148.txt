====== CC Madhya 1.148 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana yāite kaila gauḍere gamana
> pratāparudra kaila pathe vividha sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gauḍere]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:v:vividha]]---various; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---services.

===== Translation =====

**To visit Vṛndāvana, the Lord went to Gauḍa-deśa [Bengal]. On the way, King Pratāparudra performed a variety of service to please the Lord.**
