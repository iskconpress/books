====== CC Madhya 1.22 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭādaśa-varṣa kevala nīlācale sthiti
> āpani ācari' jīve śikhāilā bhakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭādaśa]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]---for eighteen years; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---in Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---staying; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:ā:ācari']]---behaving; [[synonyms:j:jīve]]---unto the living entities; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---instructed; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**For eighteen continuous years, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at Jagannātha Purī and, through His personal behavior, instructed all living entities in the mode of devotional service.**
