====== Cc. Madhya 1.225 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi vastutaḥ prabhura kichu nāhi bhaya
> tathāpi laukika-līlā, loka-ceṣṭā-maya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:v:vastutaḥ]]---in fact; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:l:laukika]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---general pastimes; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---consisting of popular behavior.

===== Translation =====

**Although Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was Śrī Kṛṣṇa Himself, the Supreme Lord, and was therefore not at all fearful, He still acted like a human being to teach neophytes how to act.**
