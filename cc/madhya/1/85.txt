====== CC Madhya 1.85 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu dekhi' jagannāthe
> subhadrā-sahita dekhe, vaṁśī nāhi hāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:subhadrā]]---Subhadrā; [[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---He sees; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]---the flute; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw Jagannātha, He saw that the Lord was with His sister Subhadrā and was not holding a flute in His hand.**
