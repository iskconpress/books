====== Cc. Madhya 1.173 ======

===== Text =====

> yavane tomāra ṭhāñi karaye lāgāni
> tāṅra hiṁsāya lābha nāhi, haya āra hāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yavane]]---your Muslim servant; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---place; [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---does; [[synonyms:l:lāgāni]]---instigation; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:h:hiṁsāya]]---to become jealous; [[synonyms:l:lābha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is no profit; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---rather; [[synonyms:h:hāni]]---loss.

===== Translation =====

**Keśava Chatrī said, "Out of jealousy your Muslim servant plots against Him. I think that you should not be very interested in Him, for there is no profit in it. Rather, there is simply loss."**
