====== CC Madhya 1.246 ======

===== Text =====

> chaya vatsara aiche prabhu karilā vilāsa
> kabhu iti-uti, kabhu kṣetra-vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:chayavatsara]]---six years; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:u:uti]]---here and there; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:kṣetra]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residing at Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord traveled all over India for six years. He was sometimes here and sometimes there performing His transcendental pastimes, and sometimes He remained at Jagannātha Purī.**
