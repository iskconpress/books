====== CC Madhya 1.183 ======

===== Text =====

> ardha-rātre dui bhāi āilā prabhu-sthāne
> prathame mililā nityānanda-haridāsa sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:r:rātre]]---in the dead of night; [[synonyms:d:duibhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:p:prathame]]---first; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Lord Nityānanda and Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with.

===== Translation =====

**Thus in the dead of night the two brothers, Dabira Khāsa and Sākara Mallika, went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu incognito. First they met Nityānanda Prabhu and Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
