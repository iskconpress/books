====== CC Madhya 1.191 ======

===== Text =====

> patita-pāvana-hetu tomāra avatāra
> āmā-ba-i jagate, patita nāhi āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patita]]-[[synonyms:p:pāvana]]---deliverance of the fallen; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for the matter of; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]-[[synonyms:b:ba]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---than us; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---in this world; [[synonyms:p:patita]]---fallen; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---more.

===== Translation =====

**Both brothers submitted, "Dear Lord, You have incarnated to deliver the fallen souls. You should consider that in this world there is none so fallen as us.**
