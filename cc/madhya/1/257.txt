====== CC Madhya 1.257 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsera siddhi-prāpti,---adbhuta se saba
> āpani mahāprabhu yāṅra kaila mahotsava

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:s:siddhi]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---passing away; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:s:se]]---those; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all incidents; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:u:utsava]]---festival.

===== Translation =====

**At Jagannātha Purī, Haridāsa Ṭhākura passed away. The incident was very wonderful because the Lord Himself performed the festival of Ṭhākura Haridāsa's departure.**
