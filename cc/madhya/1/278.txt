====== CC Madhya 1.278 ======

===== Text =====

> stava śuni' prabhuke kahena śrīnivāsa
> ghare gupta hao, kene bāhire prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:stava]]---prayers; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---unto the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śrīnivāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:g:gupta]]---covered; [[synonyms:h:hao]]---You are; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifested.

===== Translation =====

**While the people were offering their prayers unto the Lord, Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura sarcastically said to the Lord, "At home, You wanted to be covered. Why have You exposed Yourself outside?"**
