====== CC Madhya 1.8 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve kahiluṅ ādi-līlāra sūtra-gaṇa
> yāhā vistāriyāchena dāsa-vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the //ādi-līlā//; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the synopsis; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---which; [[synonyms:v:vistāriyāchena]]---has elaborately explained; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**I have previously described in synopsis the ādi-līlā [initial pastimes], which have already been fully described by Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura.**
