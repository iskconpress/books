====== CC Madhya 1.174 ======

===== Text =====

> rājāre prabodhi' keśava brāhmaṇa pāṭhāñā
> calibāra tare prabhure pāṭhāila kahiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājāre]]---unto the King; [[synonyms:p:prabodhi']]---pacifying; [[synonyms:k:keśava]]---of the name Keśava Chatrī; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---one //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāñā]]---sending there; [[synonyms:c:calibāra]] [[synonyms:t:tare]]---for the sake of leaving; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---unto the Lord; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:k:kahiñā]]---telling.

===== Translation =====

**After pacifying the King in this way, Keśava Chatrī sent a brāhmaṇa messenger to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, requesting Him to leave without delay.**
