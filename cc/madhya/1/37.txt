====== CC Madhya 1.37 ======

===== Text =====

> pradhāna pradhāna kichu kariye gaṇana
> lakṣa granthe kaila vraja-vilāsa varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pradhānapradhāna]]---the most important ones; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kariye]]---I do; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---enumeration; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]---hundreds and thousands; [[synonyms:g:granthe]]---in verses; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---of the pastimes of the Lord in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**I shall therefore enumerate the chief books compiled by Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī. He has described the pastimes of Vṛndāvana in thousands of verses.**
