====== CC Madhya 1.226 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' caraṇa vandi' gelā dui-jana
> prabhura sei grāma haite calite haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandi']]---offering prayers to the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went back; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]---village; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:c:calite]]---to go; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Having spoken thus, the two brothers offered prayers to the lotus feet of the Lord and returned to their homes. Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu then desired to leave that village.**
