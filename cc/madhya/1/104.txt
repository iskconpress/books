====== CC Madhya 1.104 ======

===== Text =====

> godāvarī-tīra-vane vṛndāvana-bhrama
> rāmānanda rāya saha tāhāñi milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:godāvarī]]-[[synonyms:t:tīra]]---on the bank of the river Godāvarī; [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in the forest; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrama]]---mistook as Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:r:rāmānandarāya]]---of the name Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:t:tāhāñi]]---there; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Once the Lord mistook the forest on the bank of the river Godāvarī to be Vṛndāvana. In that place He happened to meet Rāmānanda Rāya.**
