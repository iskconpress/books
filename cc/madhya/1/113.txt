====== CC Madhya 1.113 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-raṅga-purī saha tāhāñi milana
> rāma-dāsa viprera kaila duḥkha-vimocana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅga]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---of the name Śrī Raṅga-purī; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:t:tāhāñi]]---there; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Rāmadāsa; [[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:v:vimocana]]---deliverance from all sufferings.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then met Śrī Raṅgapurī and mitigated all the sufferings of a brāhmaṇa named Rāmadāsa.**
