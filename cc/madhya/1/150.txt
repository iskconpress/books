====== CC Madhya 1.150 ======

===== Text =====

> āsi' vidyā-vācaspatira gṛhete rahilā
> prabhure dekhite loka-saṅghaṭṭa ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming to Bengal; [[synonyms:v:vidyā]]-[[synonyms:v:vācaspatira]]---of Vidyā-vācaspati; [[synonyms:g:gṛhete]]---at the home; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭa]]---crowds of men; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---there were.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu reached Vidyānagara, Bengal, on the way to Vṛndāvana, He stopped at the house of Vidyā-vācaspati, who was the brother of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya. When Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu suddenly arrived at his house, great crowds of people gathered.**
