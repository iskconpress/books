====== CC Madhya 1.269 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina śrīvāsādi yata bhakta-gaṇa
> mahāprabhura guṇa gāñā karena kīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura and others; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:g:gāñā]]---describing; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---perform; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting.

===== Translation =====

**One day all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura, were chanting the transcendental qualities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
