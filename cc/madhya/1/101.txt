====== CC Madhya 1.101 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sārvabhaume prabhu prasāda karila
> āpana-īśvara-mūrti tāṅre dekhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---unto Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasādakarila]]---bestowed mercy; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]---His own; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---original form as the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed.

===== Translation =====

**After this incident, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed His mercy upon Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya by showing him His original form as the Lord.**
