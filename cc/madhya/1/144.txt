====== CC Madhya 1.144 ======

===== Text =====

> upavane kaila prabhu vividha vilāsa
> prabhura abhiṣeka kaila vipra kṛṣṇadāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upavane]]---in the garden by the road; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vividha]]---varieties of; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:abhiṣeka]]---bathing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---of the name Kṛṣṇadāsa.

===== Translation =====

**In the garden along the road from the Jagannātha temple to Guṇḍicā, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed various pastimes. A brāhmaṇa named Kṛṣṇadāsa performed the bathing ceremony of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
