====== CC Madhya 1.74 ======

===== Text =====

> yogya pātra haya gūḍha-rasa-vivecane
> tumio kahio tāre gūḍha-rasākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---suitable; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---recipient; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]---confidential; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the mellows; [[synonyms:v:vivecane]]---in analyzing; [[synonyms:t:tumio]]---you also; [[synonyms:k:kahio]]---instruct; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]---confidential; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the mellows; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāne]]---in describing.

===== Translation =====

**"I accept Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī as quite fit to understand the confidential mellows of devotional service, and I recommend that you explain devotional service to him further."**
