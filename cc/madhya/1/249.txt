====== CC Madhya 1.249 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana haite yadi nīlācale āilā
> āṭhāra varṣa tāhāṅ vāsa, kāhāṅ nāhi gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---though; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:ā:āṭhāra]]---eighteen; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]]---years; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---anywhere; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord returned to Jagannātha Purī from Vṛndāvana, He remained there and did not go anywhere else for eighteen years.**
