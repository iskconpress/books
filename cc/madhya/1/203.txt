====== CC Madhya 1.203 ======

===== Text =====

> na mṛṣā paramārtham eva me
> śṛṇu vijñāpanam ekam agrataḥ
> yadi me na dayiṣyase tadā
> dayanīyas tava nātha durlabhaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mṛṣā]]---untruth; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:a:artham]]---full of meaning; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:m:me]]---my; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---kindly hear; [[synonyms:v:vijñāpanam]]---submission; [[synonyms:e:ekam]]---one; [[synonyms:a:agrataḥ]]---first; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:m:me]]---unto me; [[synonyms:n:nadayiṣyase]]---You will not show mercy; [[synonyms:t:tadā]]---then; [[synonyms:d:dayanīyaḥ]]---candidate for mercy; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nātha]]---O Lord; [[synonyms:d:durlabhaḥ]]---difficult to find.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Let us submit one piece of information before You, dear Lord. It is not at all false but is full of meaning. It is this: If You are not merciful upon us, then it will be very, very difficult to find more suitable candidates for Your mercy.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is from the //Stotra-ratna// (47), by Śrī Yāmunācārya.
