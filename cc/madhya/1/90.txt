====== CC Madhya 1.90 ======

===== Text =====

> uddeśa karite kari dig-daraśana
> mukhya mukhya līlāra kari sūtra gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uddeśa]]---indication; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to make; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:d:dig]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---a general survey; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]---the chief; [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---synopsis; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---enumeration.

===== Translation =====

**Just to indicate those pastimes, I am presenting a general survey of the chief pastimes in the form of a synopsis.**
