====== CC Madhya 1.137 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma-ghare prabhura bhikṣā-paripāṭī
> ṣāṭhīra mātā kahe, yāte rāṇḍī ha-uk ṣāṭhī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---eating; [[synonyms:p:paripāṭī]]---sumptuously; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭhīra]] [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of Ṣāṭhī, who was the daughter of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:r:rāṇḍī]]---widow; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:u:uk]]---let her become; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭhī]]---Ṣāṭhī, the daughter.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was invited to dine at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya. While He was eating sumptuously, the son-in-law of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya [the husband of his daughter Ṣāṭhī] criticized Him. Because of this, Ṣāṭhī's mother cursed him by praying that Ṣāṭhī would become a widow. In other words, she cursed her son-in-law to die.**
