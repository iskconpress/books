====== CC Madhya 1.199 ======

===== Text =====

> āmā uddhārite balī nāhi tri-bhuvane
> patita-pāvana tumi---sabe tomā vine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---us; [[synonyms:u:uddhārite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:b:balī]]---powerful; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---within the three worlds; [[synonyms:p:patita]]-[[synonyms:p:pāvana]]---deliverer of the fallen; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---only; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---You; [[synonyms:v:vine]]---except.

===== Translation =====

**"No one within the three worlds is sufficiently powerful to deliver us. You are the only savior of the fallen souls; therefore there is no one but You.**
