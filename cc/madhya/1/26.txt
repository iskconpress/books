====== CC Madhya 1.26 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra caraṇe mora koṭi namaskāra
> caitanyera bhakti yeṅho laoyāila saṁsāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---unto His lotus feet; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---the devotional service; [[synonyms:y:yeṅho]]---one who; [[synonyms:l:laoyāila]]---caused to take; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---the whole world.

===== Translation =====

**I offer innumerable obeisances unto the lotus feet of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu, who is so kind that He spread the service of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu all over the world.**
