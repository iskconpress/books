====== CC Madhya 1.6 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya gauracandra jaya kṛpā-sindhu
> jaya jaya śacī-suta jaya dīna-bandhu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---to the ocean of mercy; [[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories unto You; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:s:suta]]---the son of Śacī; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories unto You; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhu]]---the friend of the fallen.

===== Translation =====

**All glories unto Śrī Gaurahari, who is an ocean of mercy! All glories unto You, the son of Śacīdevī, for You are the only friend of all fallen souls!**
