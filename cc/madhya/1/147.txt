====== CC Madhya 1.147 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍera bhakta-gaṇe tabe karila vidāya
> saṅgera bhakta lañā kare kīrtana sadāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍera]]---of Gauḍa-deśa (Bengal); [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---to the devotees; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---gave; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]]---farewell; [[synonyms:s:saṅgera]]---of constant companionship; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:s:sadāya]]---always.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade farewell to all the devotees from Gauḍa-deśa [Bengal] and continued chanting with His intimate devotees who constantly remained with Him.**
