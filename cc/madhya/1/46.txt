====== Cc. Madhya 1.46 ======

===== Text =====

> prathama vatsare advaitādi bhakta-gaṇa
> prabhure dekhite kaila, nīlādri gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathama]]---the first; [[synonyms:v:vatsare]]---in the year; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nīlādri]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**The first year after Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the renounced order of life, all the devotees, headed by Śrī Advaita Prabhu, went to see the Lord at Jagannātha Purī.**
