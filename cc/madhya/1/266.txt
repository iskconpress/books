====== Cc. Madhya 1.266 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmacandra-purī-bhaye bhikṣā ghāṭāilā
> vaiṣṇavera duḥkha dekhi' ardheka rākhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---due to fear of Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---the proportion of eating; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭāilā]]---decreased; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---understanding; [[synonyms:a:ardheka]]---half of the portion; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmacandra Purī criticized Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's eating; therefore the Lord reduced His eating to a minimum. However, when all the Vaiṣṇavas became very sorry, the Lord increased His portion to half as much as usual.**
