====== CC Madhya 1.132 ======

===== Text =====

> narahari dāsa ādi yata khaṇḍa-vāsī
> śivānanda-sena-saṅge mililā sabe āsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:narahari]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Narahari dāsa; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---heading the list; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---devotees of the place known as Khaṇḍa; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]]---of the name Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---He met; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there.

===== Translation =====

**Eventually Narahari dāsa and other inhabitants of Khaṇḍa, along with Śivānanda Sena, all arrived, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu met them.**
