====== CC Madhya 1.130 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara-svarūpa-milane parama ānanda
> śikhi-māhiti-milana, rāya bhavānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:m:milane]]---in meeting; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---great; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:ś:śikhi]]-[[synonyms:m:māhiti]]---of the name Śikhi Māhiti; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:b:bhavānanda]]---Bhavānanda, the father of Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Eventually there was a meeting with Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, and the Lord became very greatly pleased. Then there was a meeting with Śikhi Māhiti and with Bhavānanda Rāya, the father of Rāmānanda Rāya.**
