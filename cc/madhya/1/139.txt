====== Cc. Madhya 1.139 ======

===== Text =====

> ānande sabāre niyā dena vāsa-sthāna
> śivānanda sena kare sabāra pālana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:n:niyā]]---taking; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---residential quarters; [[synonyms:ś:śivānandasena]]---of the name Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:p:pālana]]---maintenance.

===== Translation =====

**When all the devotees from Bengal arrived, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu allotted them residential quarters, and Śivānanda Sena was put in charge of their maintenance.**
