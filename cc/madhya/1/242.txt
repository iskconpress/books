====== CC Madhya 1.242 ======

===== Text =====

> daṇḍavat kari' rūpa bhūmite paḍilā
> parama ānande prabhu āliṅgana dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---great; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in delight; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave.

===== Translation =====

**At Prayāga, Rūpa Gosvāmī fell down on the ground to offer obeisances to the Lord, and the Lord embraced him with great delight.**
