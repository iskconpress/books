====== CC Madhya 7.94 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa upavāsī tāhāṅi rahilā
> āra dine duḥkhī hañā nīlācale āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:u:upavāsī]]---fasting; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the next day; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees remained there and fasted, but the next day they all unhappily returned to Jagannātha Purī.**
