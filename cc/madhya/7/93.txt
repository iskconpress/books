====== Cc. Madhya 7.93 ======

===== Text =====

> vicchede vyākula prabhu calilā duḥkhī hañā
> pāche kṛṣṇadāsa yāya jala-pātra lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vicchede]]---in separation; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]]---perturbed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went on; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---just behind; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---His servant Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---went; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the waterpot; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**In separation, the Lord became very perturbed and walked on unhappily. His servant, Kṛṣṇadāsa, who was carrying His waterpot, followed behind.**
