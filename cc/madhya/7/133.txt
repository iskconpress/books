====== Cc. Madhya 7.133 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva ihāṅ kahilāṅ kariyā vistāra
> ei-mata jānibe prabhura sarvatra vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:k:kahilāṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---elaborately; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---you will know; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---the behavior.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described the Lord's behavior elaborately in the case of Kūrma. In this way, you will know Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dealings throughout South India.**
