====== CC Madhya 7.122 ======

===== Text =====

> ghare āni' prabhura kaila pāda prakṣālana
> sei jala vaṁśa-sahita karila bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---after bringing Him home; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:pāda]] [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:j:jala]]---that water; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with all the family members; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---drank.

===== Translation =====

**This brāhmaṇa brought Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home, washed His lotus feet and, with his family members, drank that water.**
