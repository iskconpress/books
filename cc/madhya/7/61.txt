====== Cc. Madhya 7.61 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sārvabhauma kahe prabhura caraṇe
> avaśya pālibe, prabhu, mora nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---unto the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:pālibe]]---You will keep; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---request.

===== Translation =====

**While Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was departing, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya submitted the following at His lotus feet, "My Lord, I have one final request that I hope You will kindly fulfill.**
