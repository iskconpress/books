====== CC Madhya 7.73 ======

===== Text =====

> vajrād api kaṭhorāṇi
> mṛdūni kusumād api
> lokottarāṇāṁ cetāṁsi
> ko nu vijñātum īśvaraḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vajrāt]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---than a thunderbolt; [[synonyms:k:kaṭhorāṇi]]---harder; [[synonyms:m:mṛdūni]]---softer; [[synonyms:k:kusumāt]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---than a flower; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:u:uttarāṇām]]---persons above the human platform of behavior; [[synonyms:c:cetāṁsi]]---the hearts; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:n:nu]]---but; [[synonyms:v:vijñātum]]---to understand; [[synonyms:ī:īśvaraḥ]]---able.

===== Translation =====

**"The hearts of those above common behavior are sometimes harder than a thunderbolt and sometimes softer than a flower. How can one accommodate such contradictions in great personalities?"**
