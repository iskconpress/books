====== Cc. Madhya 7.65 ======

===== Text =====

> pāṇḍitya āra bhakti-rasa,---duṅhera teṅho sīmā
> sambhāṣile jānibe tumi tāṅhāra mahimā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāṇḍitya]]---learning; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the mellows of devotional service; [[synonyms:d:duṅhera]]---of these two; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limit; [[synonyms:s:sambhāṣile]]---when You talk with him; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---will know; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories.

===== Translation =====

**"He is a most learned scholar as well as an expert in devotional mellows. Actually he is most exalted, and if You talk with him, You will see how glorious he is.**
