====== CC Madhya 7.50 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāra vinaye prabhura śithila haila mana
> rahila divasa katho, nā kaila gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:v:vinaye]]---on the request; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śithila]]---slackened; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---stayed; [[synonyms:d:divasa]]---days; [[synonyms:k:katho]]---a few; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya's request, Caitanya Mahāprabhu relented. He stayed a few days longer and did not depart.**
