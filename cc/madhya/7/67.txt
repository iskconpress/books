====== CC Madhya 7.67 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra prasāde ebe jāninu tāṅra tattva
> sambhāṣile jānibe tāṅra yemana mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by Your mercy; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:j:jāninu]]---I have understood; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him (Rāmānanda Rāya); [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth; [[synonyms:s:sambhāṣile]]---in talking together; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---You will know; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:y:yemana]]---such; [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---greatness.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya said, "By Your mercy I can now understand the truth about Rāmānanda Rāya. In talking with him, You also will acknowledge his greatness."**
