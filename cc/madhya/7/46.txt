====== CC Madhya 7.46 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' sārvabhauma hailā atyanta kātara
> caraṇe dhariyā kahe viṣāda-uttara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---greatly; [[synonyms:k:kātara]]---agitated; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:v:viṣāda]]---of lamentation; [[synonyms:u:uttara]]---a reply.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya became very agitated. Catching hold of the lotus feet of Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he gave this sorrowful reply.**
