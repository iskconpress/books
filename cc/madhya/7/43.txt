====== CC Madhya 7.43 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā kṛṣṇa-vārtā kahi' kahila tāṅhāre
> 'tomāra ṭhāñi āilāṅ ājñā māgibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---topics on Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---discussing; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---He informed; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to your place; [[synonyms:ā:āilāṅ]]---I have come; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:m:māgibāre]]---to beg.

===== Translation =====

**After they had discussed various topics about Lord Kṛṣṇa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu informed Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, "I have come to your place just to receive your order.**
