====== Cc. Madhya 7.47 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bahu-janmera puṇya-phale pāinu tomāra saṅga
> hena-saṅga vidhi mora karileka bhaṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:j:janmera]]---of many births; [[synonyms:p:puṇya]]-[[synonyms:p:phale]]---as the fruit of pious activities; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I got; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---association; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---such association; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---providence; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:k:karileka]]---has done; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅga]]---breaking.

===== Translation =====

**"After many births, due to some pious activity I got Your association. Now providence is breaking this invaluable association.**
