====== Cc. Madhya 7.41 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe tāṅra vākya prabhu kari' aṅgīkāre
> tāhā-sabā lañā gelā sārvabhauma-ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---the words; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāre]]---acceptance; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.

===== Translation =====

**Accepting the request of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, Lord Caitanya took all His devotees and went to the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
