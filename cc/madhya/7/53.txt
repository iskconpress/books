====== CC Madhya 7.53 ======

===== Text =====

> āge ta' kahiba tāhā kariyā vistāra
> ebe kahi prabhura dakṣiṇa-yātrā-samācāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---later; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---indeed; [[synonyms:k:kahiba]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---all those incidents; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---elaboration; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---//now//; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---let me describe; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---in South India; [[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---of the touring; [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---the narration.

===== Translation =====

**Later I shall tell about this in elaborate detail, but at present I wish to describe Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's South Indian tour.**
