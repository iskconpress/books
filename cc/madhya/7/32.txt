====== CC Madhya 7.32 ======

===== Text =====

> guṇe doṣodgāra-cchale sabā niṣedhiyā
> ekākī bhramibena tīrtha vairāgya kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---in the good qualities; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]-[[synonyms:u:udgāra]]-[[synonyms:c:chale]]---on the plea of attributing faults; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhiyā]]---forbidding; [[synonyms:e:ekākī]]---alone; [[synonyms:b:bhramibena]]---will tour; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---the sacred places of pilgrimage; [[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---regulative principles of the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---observing.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore, to prevent them from accompanying Him and becoming unhappy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu declared their good qualities to be faults.**

===== Purport =====

The Lord wanted to tour all the places of pilgrimage alone and strictly observe the duties of the renounced order.
