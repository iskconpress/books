====== Cc. Madhya 7.140 ======

===== Text =====

> aneka prakāra vilāpa karite lāgilā
> sei-kṣaṇe āsi' prabhu tāṅre āliṅgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---various kinds; [[synonyms:v:vilāpa]]---lamentation; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---immediately; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming back; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgilā]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**When Vāsudeva, the leper brāhmaṇa, was lamenting due to not being able to see Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord immediately returned to that spot and embraced him.**
