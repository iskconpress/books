====== CC Madhya 7.1 ======

===== Text =====

> dhanyaṁ taṁ naumi caitanyaṁ
> vāsudevaṁ dayārdraḥ-dhī
> naṣṭa-kuṣṭhaṁ rūpa-puṣṭaṁ
> bhakti-tuṣṭaṁ cakāra yaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhanyam]]---auspicious; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:n:naumi]]---I offer obeisances; [[synonyms:c:caitanyam]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāsudevam]]---unto the //brāhmaṇa// Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārdraḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dhī]]---being compassionate; [[synonyms:n:naṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṣṭham]]---cured the leprosy; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:p:puṣṭam]]---beautiful; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:t:tuṣṭam]]---satisfied in devotional service; [[synonyms:c:cakāra]]---made; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, being very compassionate toward a brāhmaṇa named Vāsudeva, cured him of leprosy. He transformed him into a beautiful man satisfied with devotional service. I offer my respectful obeisances unto the glorious Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
