====== Cc. Madhya 7.151 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahila prabhura prathama gamana
> kūrma-daraśana, vāsudeva-vimocana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---thus I have described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prathama]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---the first tour; [[synonyms:k:kūrma]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting the temple of Kūrma; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:v:vimocana]]---and liberating the leper //brāhmaṇa// of the name Vāsudeva.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I end my description of the first tour of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, His visiting the temple of Kūrma and His liberating the leper brāhmaṇa Vāsudeva.**
