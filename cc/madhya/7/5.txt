====== Cc. Madhya 7.5 ======

===== Text =====

> phālgunera śeṣe dola-yātrā se dekhila
> premāveśe tāṅhā bahu nṛtya-gīta kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:phālgunera]]---of the month of Phālguna; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end; [[synonyms:d:dola]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the Dola-yātrā festival; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstasy of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed.

===== Translation =====

**At the end of the month of Phālguna, He witnessed the Dola-yātrā ceremony, and in His usual ecstatic love of God, He chanted and danced in various ways on the occasion.**
