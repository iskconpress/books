====== CC Madhya 7.4 ======

===== Text =====

> māgha-śukla-pakṣe prabhu karila sannyāsa
> phālgune āsiyā kaila nīlācale vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māgha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śukla]]-[[synonyms:p:pakṣe]]---in the waxing fortnight of the month of Māgha; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---accepted; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:p:phālgune]]---in the next month, Phālguna; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the renounced order during the waxing fortnight of the month of Māgha. During the following month, Phālguna, He went to Jagannātha Purī and resided there.**
