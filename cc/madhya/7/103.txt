====== CC Madhya 7.103 ======

===== Text =====

> sei yāi' grāmera loka vaiṣṇava karaya
> anya-grāmī āsi' tāṅre dekhi' vaiṣṇava haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going to his own village; [[synonyms:g:grāmera]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people of the village; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:k:karaya]]---makes; [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmī]]---inhabitants from different villages; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing them; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become devotees.

===== Translation =====

**When these individuals returned to their villages, they also converted others into devotees. When others came to see them, they also were converted.**
