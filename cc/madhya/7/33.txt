====== CC Madhya 7.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe cāri-jana bahu minati karila
> svatantra īśvara prabhu kabhu nā mānila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---four men; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:m:minati]]---petitions; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---submitted; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mānila]]---accepted.

===== Translation =====

**Four devotees then humbly insisted that they go with the Lord, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, being the independent Supreme Personality of Godhead, did not accept their request.**
