====== CC Madhya 7.152 ======

===== Text =====

> śraddhā kari' ei līlā ye kare śravaṇa
> acirāte milaye tāre caitanya-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great faith; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---this pastime; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hearing; [[synonyms:a:acirāte]]---very soon; [[synonyms:m:milaye]]---meets; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**One who hears these pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with great faith will surely very soon attain the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
