====== CC Madhya 7.44 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsa kari' viśvarūpa giyāche dakṣiṇe
> avaśya kariba āmi tāṅra anveṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after accepting the //sannyāsa// order; [[synonyms:v:viśvarūpa]]---Viśvarūpa (the elder brother of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu); [[synonyms:g:giyāche]]---has gone; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇe]]---to South India; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---shall do; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:a:anveṣaṇe]]---searching for.

===== Translation =====

**"My elder brother, Viśvarūpa, has taken sannyāsa and gone to South India. Now I must go search for Him.**
