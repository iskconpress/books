====== Cc. Madhya 7.57 ======

===== Text =====

> ājñā-mālā pāñā harṣe namaskāra kari'
> ānande dakṣiṇa-deśe cale gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---the garland of permission; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:h:harṣe]]---in great jubilation; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---with great pleasure; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to South India; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thus receiving Lord Jagannātha's permission in the form of a garland, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered obeisances, and then in great jubilation He prepared to depart for South India.**
