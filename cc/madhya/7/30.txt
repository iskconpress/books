====== Cc. Madhya 7.30 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyera bhakta-vātsalya---akathya-kathana
> āpane vairāgya-duḥkha karena sahana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]---the love for His devotees; [[synonyms:a:akathya]]-[[synonyms:k:kathana]]---indescribable by words; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---of the renounced order; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sahana]]---toleration.

===== Translation =====

**No one can properly describe Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's affection for His devotees. He always tolerated all kinds of personal unhappiness resulting from His acceptance of the renounced order of life.**
