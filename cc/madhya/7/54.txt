====== Cc. Madhya 7.54 ======

===== Text =====

> dina pāṅca rahi' prabhu bhaṭṭācārya-sthāne
> calibāra lāgi' ājñā māgilā āpane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]---five days; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya's place; [[synonyms:c:calibāra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for starting; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---begged; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**After staying five days at the home of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally asked his permission to depart for South India.**
