====== CC Madhya 7.70 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' mahāprabhu karilā gamana
> mūrcchita hañā tāhāṅ paḍilā sārvabhauma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]]---fainted; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed on His tour, and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya immediately fainted and fell to the ground.**
