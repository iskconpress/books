====== CC Madhya 7.102 ======

===== Text =====

> grāmāntara haite dekhite āila yata jana
> tāṅra darśana-kṛpāya haya tāṅra sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from different villages; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---all the persons; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy of seeing him; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---similar Vaiṣṇavas.

===== Translation =====

**Simply by seeing such empowered individuals, people from different villages would become like them by the mercy of their glance.**
