====== CC Madhya 7.139 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura gamana kūrma-mukhete śuniñā
> bhūmite paḍilā duḥkhe mūrcchita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---the going; [[synonyms:k:kūrma]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhete]]---from the mouth of the //brāhmaṇa// Kūrma; [[synonyms:ś:śuniñā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in great distress; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]]---unconsciousness; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**When the leper Vāsudeva came to Kūrma's house to see Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he was informed that the Lord had already left. The leper then fell to the ground unconscious.**
