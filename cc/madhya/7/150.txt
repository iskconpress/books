====== CC Madhya 7.150 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vāsudevoddhāra' ei kahila ākhyāna
> 'vāsudevāmṛta-prada' haila prabhura nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---giving liberation to Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---is described; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāna]]---narration; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:p:prada]]---the giver of nectar to Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's holy name.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu reclaimed the leper Vāsudeva and so received the name Vāsudevāmṛta-prada.**
