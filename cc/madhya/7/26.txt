====== Cc. Madhya 7.26 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāra āge āmi nā jāni vyavahāra
> iṅhāre nā bhāya svatantra caritra āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of him; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---know; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---social etiquette; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāre]]---for him; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---exists; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:c:caritra]]---character; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My.

===== Translation =====

**"According to Dāmodara, I am still a neophyte as far as social etiquette is concerned; therefore he does not like My independent nature.**
