====== CC Madhya 7.146 ======

===== Text =====

> kintu āchilāṅ bhāla adhama hañā
> ebe ahaṅkāra mora janmibe āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:ā:āchilāṅ]]---I was; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---all right; [[synonyms:a:adhama]]---the lowest of mankind; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:a:ahaṅkāra]]---pride; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:j:janmibe]]---will appear; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**Being meek and humble, the brāhmaṇa Vāsudeva worried that he would become proud after being cured by the grace of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
