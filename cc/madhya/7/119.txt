====== CC Madhya 7.119 ======

===== Text =====

> kata-kṣaṇe prabhu yadi bāhya prakāśilā
> kūrmera sevaka bahu sammāna karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---after some time; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:p:prakāśilā]]---manifested; [[synonyms:k:kūrmera]]---of the Lord Kūrma Deity; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---a servant; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---respect; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---showed.

===== Translation =====

**After some time, when Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu manifested His external consciousness, a priest of the Lord Kūrma Deity gave Him various offerings.**
