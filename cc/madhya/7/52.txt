====== CC Madhya 7.52 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra brāhmaṇī, tāṅra nāma---'ṣāṭhīra mātā'
> rāndhi' bhikṣā dena teṅho, āścarya tāṅra kathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇī]]---his wife; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---her name; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣāṭhīra]] [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of Ṣāṭhī; [[synonyms:r:rāndhi']]---cooking; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dena]]---offers food; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---she; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---her; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---narration.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya's wife, whose name was Ṣāṭhīmātā (the mother of Ṣāṭhī), did the cooking. The narrations of these pastimes are very wonderful.**
