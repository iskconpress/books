====== CC Madhya 7.19 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsa kariyā āmi calilāṅ vṛndāvana
> tumi āmā lañā āile advaita-bhavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---after accepting the renounced order; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:c:calilāṅ]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---toward Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---Me; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āile]]---went; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---to the house of Advaita Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**"After accepting the sannyāsa order, I decided to go to Vṛndāvana, but You took Me instead to the house of Advaita Prabhu.**
