====== Cc. Madhya 7.85 ======

===== Text =====

> madhyāhna kariyā āilā devatā-mandire
> nija-gaṇa praveśi' kapāṭa dila bahir-dvāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---performing bathing, etc; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:d:devatā]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to the temple of the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:p:praveśi']]---allowing His own men; [[synonyms:k:kapāṭa]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---shut; [[synonyms:b:bahir]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---the outside door.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing Their baths, They returned at noon to the temple. Admitting His own men, Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu closed the outside door.**
