====== CC Madhya 7.42 ======

===== Text =====

> namaskari' sārvabhauma āsana nivedila
> sabākāre mili' tabe āsane vasila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---sitting places; [[synonyms:n:nivedila]]---offered; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after that; [[synonyms:ā:āsane]] [[synonyms:v:vasila]]---he took his seat.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as they entered his house, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya offered the Lord obeisances and a place to sit. After seating all the others, the Bhaṭṭācārya took his seat.**
