====== Cc. Madhya 7.75 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa śīghra āsi' laila prabhura sātha
> vastra-prasāda lañā tabe āilā gopīnātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very swiftly; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:l:laila]]---took; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sātha]]---the company; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---the garments; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---and Lord Jagannātha's //prasāda//; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya.

===== Translation =====

**Immediately all the devotees came and partook of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's company. Afterwards, Gopīnātha Ācārya came with the garments and prasāda.**
