====== CC Madhya 7.108 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata kailā yāvat gelā setubandhe
> sarva-deśa 'vaiṣṇava' haila prabhura sambandhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---until; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:setubandhe]]---to the southernmost part of India; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---all the countries; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---in connection with.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the Lord went to the extreme southern part of India, and He converted all the provinces to Vaiṣṇavism.**
