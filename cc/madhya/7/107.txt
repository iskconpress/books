====== Cc. Madhya 7.107 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura kṛpāya haya mahābhāgavata
> sei saba ācārya hañā tārila jagat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---become; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---first-class devotees; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all such persons; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---teachers; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:t:tārila]]---liberated; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world.

===== Translation =====

**By the mercy of the Supreme Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, everyone became a first-class devotee. Later they became teachers or spiritual masters and liberated the entire world.**
