====== CC Madhya 7.76 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā-saṅge prabhu tabe ālālanātha āilā
> namaskāra kari' tāre bahu-stuti kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:ā:ālālanātha]]---the place named Ālālanātha; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---reached; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:s:stuti]]---many prayers; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees followed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to a place known as Ālālanātha. There they all offered respects and various prayers.**
