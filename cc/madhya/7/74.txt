====== Cc. Madhya 7.74 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda prabhu bhaṭṭācārye uṭhāila
> tāṅra loka-saṅge tāṅre ghare pāṭhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānandaprabhu]]---Lord Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārye]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāila]]---raised; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with associates; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him (the Bhaṭṭācārya); [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his home; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda Prabhu raised Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and with the help of His men saw him to his home.**
