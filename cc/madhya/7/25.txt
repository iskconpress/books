====== CC Madhya 7.25 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi ta'---sannyāsī, dāmodara---brahmacārī
> sadā rahe āmāra upara śikṣā-daṇḍa dhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---I indeed; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---of the name Dāmodara; [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]]---in a stage of complete celibacy; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:u:upara]]---on Me; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---a stick for My education; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---keeping.

===== Translation =====

**"Although I am in the renounced order of life and Dāmodara is a brahmacārī, he still keeps a stick in his hand just to educate Me.**
