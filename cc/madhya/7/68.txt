====== Cc. Madhya 7.68 ======

===== Text =====

> aṅgīkāra kari' prabhu tāṅhāra vacana
> tāṅre vidāya dite tāṅre kaila āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting this proposal; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---of him (Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya); [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the request; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to offer farewell; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya's request that He meet Rāmānanda Rāya. Bidding Sārvabhauma farewell, the Lord embraced him.**
