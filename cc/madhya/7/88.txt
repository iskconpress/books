====== CC Madhya 7.88 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu dvāra karāila mocana
> ānande āsiyā loka pāila daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---the door; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made; [[synonyms:m:mocana]]---opening; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---sight.

===== Translation =====

**After lunch, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made them open the door. In this way everyone received His audience with great pleasure.**
