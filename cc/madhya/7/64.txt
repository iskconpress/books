====== CC Madhya 7.64 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra saṅgera yogya teṅho eka jana
> pṛthivīte rasika bhakta nāhi tāṅra sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:s:saṅgera]]---of association; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---fit; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he (Rāmānanda Rāya); [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---person; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīte]]---in the world; [[synonyms:r:rasika]]---expert in transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is none; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like him.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya continued, "Rāmānanda Rāya is a fit person to associate with You; no other devotee can compare with him in knowledge of the transcendental mellows.**
