====== CC Madhya 7.123 ======

===== Text =====

> aneka-prakāra snehe bhikṣā karāila
> gosāñira śeṣānna sa-vaṁśe khāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aneka]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---various kinds; [[synonyms:s:snehe]]---in affection; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---food; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---made Him eat; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---with all the members of the family; [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate.

===== Translation =====

**With great affection and respect, that Kūrma brāhmaṇa made Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu eat all kinds of food. After that, the remnants were shared by all the members of the family.**
