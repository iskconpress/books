====== CC Madhya 7.100 ======

===== Text =====

> sei-jana nija-grāme kariyā gamana
> 'kṛṣṇa' bali' hāse, kānde, nāce anukṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---that person; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---to his own village; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---returning there; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laughs; [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---cries; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:a:anukṣaṇa]]---always.

===== Translation =====

**Being thus empowered, they would return to their own villages, always chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa and sometimes laughing, crying and dancing.**
