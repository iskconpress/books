====== CC Madhya 7.138 ======

===== Text =====

> rātrite śunilā teṅho gosāñira āgamana
> dekhibāre āilā prabhāte kūrmera bhavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātrite]]---at night; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---heard; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---the arrival; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see Him; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---he came; [[synonyms:p:prabhāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:k:kūrmera]]---of the //brāhmaṇa// named Kūrma; [[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---to the house.

===== Translation =====

**Then one night Vāsudeva heard of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's arrival, and in the morning he came to see the Lord at the house of Kūrma.**
