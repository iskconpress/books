====== CC Madhya 7.116 ======

===== Text =====

> darśane 'vaiṣṇava' haila, bale 'kṛṣṇa' 'hari'
> premāveśe nāce loka ūrdhva bāhu kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in seeing; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---they became devotees; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---started to say; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hari]]---Lord Hari; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the great ecstasy of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhva]] [[synonyms:b:bāhu]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---raising the arms.

===== Translation =====

**Just by seeing Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, everyone became a devotee. They began to chant "Kṛṣṇa" and "Hari" and all the holy names. They all were merged in a great ecstasy of love, and they began to dance, raising their arms.**
