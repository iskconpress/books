====== Cc. Madhya 7.80 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhiyā lokera mane haila camatkāra
> yata loka āise, keha nāhi yāya ghara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing all this; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the minds; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came there; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---home.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone present was astonished to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dancing and His bodily transformations. Whoever came did not want to return home.**
