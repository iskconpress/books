====== CC Madhya 7.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi-saba bandhu mora bandhu-kṛtya kaile
> ihāṅ āni' more jagannātha dekhāile

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of you; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]---friends; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:b:bandhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---duties of a friend; [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---you have executed; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to Me; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dekhāile]]---you have shown.

===== Translation =====

**"You are all My friends, and you have properly executed the duties of friends by bringing Me here to Jagannātha Purī and giving Me the chance to see Lord Jagannātha in the temple.**
