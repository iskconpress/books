====== CC Madhya 7.115 ======

===== Text =====

> āścarya śuniyā loka āila dekhibāre
> prabhura rūpa-prema dekhi' hailā camatkāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]---wonderful occurrence; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---beauty; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---and love of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāre]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing of these wonderful occurrences, everyone came to see Him there. When they saw the beauty of the Lord and His ecstatic condition, they were all struck with wonder.**
