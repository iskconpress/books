====== CC Madhya 7.110 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhuke ye bhaje, tāre tāṅra kṛpā haya
> sei se e-saba līlā satya kari' laya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---worships; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:se]]---such person; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---truth; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting as; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---takes.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's empowering of others can be understood by one who is actually a devotee of the Lord and who has received His mercy.**
