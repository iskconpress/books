====== Cc. Madhya 7.83 ======

===== Text =====

> atikāla haila, loka chāḍiyā nā yāya
> tabe nityānanda-gosāñi sṛjilā upāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atikāla]]---very late; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it was; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---do not go; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---the spiritual master; [[synonyms:s:sṛjilā]]---invented; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---a means.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing that it was already getting late, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, the spiritual master, invented a means to disperse the crowd.**
