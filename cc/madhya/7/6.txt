====== CC Madhya 7.6 ======

===== Text =====

> caitre rahi' kaila sārvabhauma-vimocana
> vaiśākhera prathame dakṣiṇa yāite haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitre]]---in the month of Caitra (March-April); [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---residing there; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:v:vimocana]]---liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:v:vaiśākhera]]---of the month of Vaiśākha; [[synonyms:p:prathame]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]---to South India; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it was; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**During the month of Caitra, while living at Jagannātha Purī, the Lord delivered Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, and in the beginning of the next month (Vaiśākha), He decided to go to South India.**
