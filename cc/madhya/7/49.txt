====== CC Madhya 7.49 ======

===== Text =====

> svatantra-īśvara tumi karibe gamana
> dina katho raha, dekhi tomāra caraṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svatantra]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the independent Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---will make; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure; [[synonyms:d:dina]]---days; [[synonyms:k:katho]]---some; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---please stay; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I may see; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You are the independent Supreme Personality of Godhead. Certainly You will depart. I know that. Still, I ask You to stay here a few days more so that I can see Your lotus feet."**
