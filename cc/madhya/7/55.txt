====== CC Madhya 7.55 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura āgrahe bhaṭṭācārya sammata ha-ilā
> prabhu tāṅre lañā jagannātha-mandire gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āgrahe]]---by the eagerness of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:sammata]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became agreeable; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him (Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya); [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to the temple of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**After receiving the Bhaṭṭācārya's permission, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to see Lord Jagannātha in the temple. He took the Bhaṭṭācārya with Him.**
