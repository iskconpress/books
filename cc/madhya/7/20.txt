====== Cc. Madhya 7.20 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlācala āsite pathe bhāṅgilā mora daṇḍa
> tomā-sabāra gāḍha-snehe āmāra kārya-bhaṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlācala]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āsite]]---going there; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the road; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgilā]]---You broke; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---//sannyāsa// staff; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]-[[synonyms:s:snehe]]---on account of the deep affection; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅga]]---disturbance of activities.

===== Translation =====

**"While on the way to Jagannātha Purī, You broke My sannyāsa staff. I know that all of you have great affection for Me, but such things disturb My activities.**
