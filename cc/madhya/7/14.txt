====== Cc. Madhya 7.14 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā sabāra mane haila mahā-duḥkha
> niḥśabda ha-ilā, sabāra śukāila mukha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the minds; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---great unhappiness; [[synonyms:n:niḥśabda]]---silent; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:ś:śukāila]]---dried up; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---the faces.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this message from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the devotees became very unhappy and remained silent with sullen faces.**
