====== CC Madhya 7.56 ======

===== Text =====

> darśana kari' ṭhākura-pāśa ājñā māgilā
> pūjārī prabhure mālā-prasāda āni' dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---visiting the Lord; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---from the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---begged permission; [[synonyms:p:pūjārī]]---the priest; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garland; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu also begged His permission. The priest then immediately delivered prasāda and a garland to Lord Caitanya.**
