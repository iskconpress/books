====== Cc. Madhya 7.142 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura kṛpā dekhi' tāṅra vismaya haila mana
> śloka paḍi' pāye dhari, karaye stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of the //brāhmaṇa// Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]] [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---there was astonishment in his mind; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting a verse; [[synonyms:p:pāye]] [[synonyms:d:dhari]]---touching His lotus feet; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---offers prayers.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa Vāsudeva was astonished to behold the wonderful mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and he began to recite a verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, touching the Lord's lotus feet.**
