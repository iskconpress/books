====== CC Madhya 7.23 ======

===== Text =====

> mukunda hayena duḥkhī dekhi' sannyāsa-dharma
> tinabāre śīte snāna, bhūmite śayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---becomes; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---My regulative principles in the renounced order; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:b:bāre]]---three times; [[synonyms:ś:śīte]]---in the winter; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bath; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down.

===== Translation =====

**"Being a sannyāsī, I have a duty to lie down on the ground and take a bath three times a day, even during the winter. But Mukunda becomes very unhappy when he sees My severe austerities.**
