====== Cc. Madhya 7.3 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate sārvabhaumera nistāra karila
> dakṣiṇa-gamane prabhura icchā upajila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaumera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---the liberation; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---was executed; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gamane]]---in going to South India; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---a desire; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---arose.

===== Translation =====

**After delivering Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, the Lord desired to go to South India to preach.**
