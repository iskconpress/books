====== CC Madhya 7.13 ======

===== Text =====

> viśvarūpa-siddhi-prāpti jānena sakala
> dakṣiṇa-deśa uddhārite karena ei chala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśvarūpa]]---of Viśvarūpa; [[synonyms:s:siddhi]]---of perfection; [[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---achievement; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---the Lord knows; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---everything; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---South India; [[synonyms:u:uddhārite]]---just to liberate; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---makes; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:c:chala]]---pretense.

===== Translation =====

**Knowing everything, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was aware that Viśvarūpa had already passed away. A pretense of ignorance was necessary, however, so that He could go to South India and liberate the people there.**
