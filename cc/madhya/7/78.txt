====== CC Madhya 7.78 ======

===== Text =====

> caudikete saba loka bale 'hari' 'hari'
> premāveśe madhye nṛtya kare gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caudikete]]---all around; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all persons; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---shout; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All around Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is also known as Gaurahari, people began to shout the holy name of Hari. Lord Caitanya, immersed in His usual ecstasy of love, danced in the midst of them.**
