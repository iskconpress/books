====== CC Madhya 7.60 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri kopīna-bahirvāsa rākhiyāchi ghare
> tāhā, prasādānna, lañā āisa vipra-dvāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:k:kopīna]]-[[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---four sets of loincloths and external clothing; [[synonyms:r:rākhiyāchi]]---I have kept; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---remnants of food of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āisa]]---come here; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---by means of some //brāhmaṇa.//

===== Translation =====

**"Bring the four sets of loincloths and outer garments I keep at home, and also some prasāda of Lord Jagannātha's. You may carry these things with the help of some brāhmaṇa."**
