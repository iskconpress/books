====== Cc. Madhya 7.16 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-eka saṅge caluka, nā paḍa haṭha-raṅge
> yāre kaha sei dui caluk tomāra saṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---or one; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with You; [[synonyms:c:caluka]]---let go; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---do not; [[synonyms:p:paḍa]]---fall; [[synonyms:h:haṭha]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the clutches of thieves and rogues; [[synonyms:y:yāre]]---whoever; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---You say; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:c:caluk]]---let go; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---You; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with.

===== Translation =====

**"Let one or two of us go with You; otherwise You may fall into the clutches of thieves and rogues along the way. They may be whomever You like, but two persons should go with You.**
