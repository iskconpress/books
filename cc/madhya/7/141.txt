====== CC Madhya 7.141 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-sparśe duḥkha-saṅge kuṣṭha dūre gela
> ānanda sahite aṅga sundara ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśe]]---by the touch of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with his unhappiness; [[synonyms:k:kuṣṭha]]---the infection of leprosy; [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---to a distant place; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:s:sahite]]---with great pleasure; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---whole body; [[synonyms:s:sundara]]---beautiful; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu touched him, both the leprosy and his distress went to a distant place. Indeed, Vāsudeva's body became very beautiful, to his great happiness.**
