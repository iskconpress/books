====== CC Madhya 7.84 ======

===== Text =====

> madhyāhna karite gelā prabhuke lañā
> tāhā dekhi' loka āise caudike dhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to take lunch at noon; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing that; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:c:caudike]]---all around; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Nityānanda Prabhu took Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for lunch at noon, everyone came running around Them.**
