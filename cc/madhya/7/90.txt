====== Cc. Madhya 7.90 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-rūpe sei ṭhāñi bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> sei rātri goṅāilā kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in that place; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---that night; [[synonyms:g:goṅāilā]]---passed; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in great pleasure discussing Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then passed the night there and discussed the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa with His devotees with great pleasure.**
