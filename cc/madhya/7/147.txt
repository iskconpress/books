====== CC Madhya 7.147 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"kabhu tomāra nā habe abhimāna
> nirantara kaha tumi 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa' nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---at any time; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---pride; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---incessantly; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---chant; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**To protect the brāhmaṇa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu advised him to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra incessantly. By doing so, he would never become unnecessarily proud.**
