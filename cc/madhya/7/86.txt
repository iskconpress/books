====== Cc. Madhya 7.86 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe gopīnātha dui-prabhure bhikṣā karāila
> prabhura śeṣa prasādānna sabe bāṅṭi' khāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto the two Lords Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---gave prasāda to eat; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---the remnants; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭi']]---sharing; [[synonyms:k:kbāila]]---ate.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya then brought prasāda for the two Lords to eat, and after They had eaten, the remnants of the food were distributed to all the devotees.**
