====== Cc. Madhya 7.31 ======

===== Text =====

> sei duḥkha dekhi' yei bhakta duḥkha pāya
> sei duḥkha tāṅra śaktye sahana nā yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---that unhappiness; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---that unhappiness; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:ś:śaktye]]---by the power; [[synonyms:s:sahana]]---toleration; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---possible.

===== Translation =====

**The regulative principles observed by Caitanya Mahāprabhu were sometimes intolerable, and all the devotees became greatly affected by them. Although strictly observing the regulative principles, Caitanya Mahāprabhu could not tolerate the unhappiness felt by His devotees.**
