====== CC Madhya 7.135 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura anuvraji' kūrma bahu dūra āilā
> prabhu tāṅre yatna kari' ghare pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:anuvraji']]---following behind; [[synonyms:k:kūrma]]---the brāhmaṇa named Kūrma; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:d:dūra]]---distance; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:y:yatna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking much care; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his home; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left, the brāhmaṇa Kūrma followed Him a great distance, but eventually Lord Caitanya took care to send him back home.**
