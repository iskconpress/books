====== CC Madhya 7.97 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloka paḍi' pathe calilā gaurahari
> loka dekhi' pathe kahe,---bala 'hari' 'hari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting this verse //kṛṣṇa! kṛṣṇa!//; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing other people; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---He says; [[synonyms:b:bala]]---say; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of Lord Hari.

===== Translation =====

**Chanting this verse, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, known as Gaurahari, went on His way. As soon as He saw someone, He would request him to chant "Hari! Hari!"**
