====== Cc. Madhya 7.77 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe nṛtya-gīta kaila kata-kṣaṇa
> dekhite āilā tāhāṅ vaise yata jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the great ecstasy of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---who live; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---all the men.

===== Translation =====

**In great ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced and chanted for some time. Indeed, all the neighbors came to see Him.**
