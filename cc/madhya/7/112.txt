====== CC Madhya 7.112 ======

===== Text =====

> prathamei kahila prabhura ye-rūpe gamana
> ei-mata jāniha yāvat dakṣiṇa-bhramaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathamei]]---at the beginning; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---I have explained; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---as; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---the touring; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:j:jāniha]]---you should know; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long as; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhramaṇa]]---touring in South India.

===== Translation =====

**Whatever I have stated about the beginning of the Lord's movement should also be understood to hold for as long as the Lord toured South India.**
