====== CC Madhya 7.98 ======

===== Text =====

> sei loka prema-matta hañā bale 'hari' 'kṛṣṇa'
> prabhura pāche saṅge yāya darśana-satṛṣṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---that person; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened in love of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---says; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the holy name of Lord Hari and Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind the Lord; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]-[[synonyms:s:satṛṣṇa]]---being very eager to see Him.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever heard Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu chant "Hari, Hari," also chanted the holy name of Lord Hari and Kṛṣṇa. In this way they all followed the Lord, very eager to see Him.**
