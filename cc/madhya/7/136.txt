====== Cc. Madhya 7.136 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vāsudeva'-nāma eka dvija mahāśaya
> sarvāṅge galita kuṣṭha, tāte kīḍā-maya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the name Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dvija]]---one //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---a great person; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---all over his body; [[synonyms:g:galita]]---acute; [[synonyms:k:kuṣṭha]]---leprosy; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:k:kīḍā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---full of living worms.

===== Translation =====

**There was also a brāhmaṇa named Vāsudeva, who was a great person but was suffering from leprosy. Indeed, his body was filled with living worms.**
