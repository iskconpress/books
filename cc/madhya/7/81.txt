====== CC Madhya 7.81 ======

===== Text =====

> keha nāce, keha gāya, 'śrī-kṛṣṇa' 'gopāla'
> premete bhāsila loka,---strī-vṛddha-ābāla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---someone dances; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---someone chants; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa's name; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Gopāla's name; [[synonyms:p:premete]]---in love of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhāsila]]---floated; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all the people; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---women; [[synonyms:v:vṛddha]]---old men; [[synonyms:ā:ā]]-[[synonyms:b:bāla]]---from the children.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone-including children, old men and women-began to dance and chant the holy names of Śrī Kṛṣṇa and Gopāla. In this way they all floated in the ocean of love of Godhead.**
