====== CC Madhya 7.58 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya-saṅge āra yata nija-gaṇa
> jagannātha pradakṣiṇa kari' karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---personal devotees; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:pradakṣiṇa]]---circumambulation; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure.

===== Translation =====

**Accompanied by His personal associates and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu circumambulated the altar of Jagannātha. The Lord then departed on His South Indian tour.**
