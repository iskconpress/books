====== CC Madhya 7.91 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle snāna kari' karilā gamana
> bhakta-gaṇe vidāya dilā kari' āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bath; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after taking; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---started; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---tour; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]]---farewell; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning, after taking His bath, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu started on His South Indian tour. He bade farewell to the devotees by embracing them.**
