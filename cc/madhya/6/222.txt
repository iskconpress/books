====== Cc. Madhya 6.222 ======

===== Text =====

> vasite āsana diyā duṅheta vasilā
> prasādānna khuli' prabhu tāṅra hāte dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vasite]]---to sit; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---carpet; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:d:duṅheta]]---both of them; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:k:khuli']]---opening; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya offered a carpet for the Lord to sit upon, and both of them sat there. Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu opened the prasāda and placed it in the hands of the Bhaṭṭācārya.**
