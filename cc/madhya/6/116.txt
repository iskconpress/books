====== CC Madhya 6.116 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni mahāprabhu kahe aiche mat kaha
> āmā prati bhaṭṭācāryera haya anugraha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing them; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:m:mat]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---do not speak; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:p:prati]]---toward Me; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:a:anugraha]]---mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Do not speak like that. Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya has shown great affection and mercy toward Me.**
