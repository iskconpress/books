====== Cc. Madhya 6.33 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma pāṭhāila sabā darśana karite
> 'candaneśvara' nija-putra dila sabāra sāthe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent them; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all; [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to see Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:c:candana]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---of the name Candaneśvara; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---his son; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:s:sāthe]]---with all of them.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya then sent them all back to see Lord Jagannātha, and he asked his own son Candaneśvara to accompany them as a guide.**
