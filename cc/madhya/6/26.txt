====== CC Madhya 6.26 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara-darśane prabhu preme acetana
> sārvabhauma lañā gelā āpana-bhavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in the ecstasy of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:acetana]]---unconscious; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---has taken; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---to his own home.

===== Translation =====

**"Upon seeing Lord Jagannātha, Caitanya Mahāprabhu became ecstatic and fell unconscious, and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya has taken Him to his home in this condition.**
