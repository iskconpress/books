====== Cc. Madhya 6.34 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha dekhi' sabāra ha-ila ānanda
> bhāvete āviṣṭa hailā prabhu nityānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:b:bhāvete]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was then very pleased to see the Deity of Lord Jagannātha. Lord Nityānanda in particular was overwhelmed with ecstasy.**
