====== CC Madhya 6.66 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnātha prabhu lañā tāhāṅ vāsā dila
> jala, jala-pātrādika sarva sāmādhāna kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---apartment; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādika]]---waterpots and other vessels; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sāmādhāna]]---arrangements; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Gopīnātha Ācārya took Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu to the residential quarters and showed Him where to find water, tubs and waterpots. Indeed, he arranged everything.**
