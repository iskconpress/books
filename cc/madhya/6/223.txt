====== Cc. Madhya 6.223 ======

===== Text =====

> prasādānna pāñā bhaṭṭācāryera ānanda haila
> snāna, sandhyā, danta-dhāvana yadyapi nā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---the remnants of food; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]---morning duties; [[synonyms:d:danta]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāvana]]---washing the teeth; [[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---finished.

===== Translation =====

**At that time the Bhaṭṭācārya had not even washed his mouth, nor had he taken his bath or finished his morning duties. Nonetheless, he was very pleased to receive the prasāda of Lord Jagannātha.**
