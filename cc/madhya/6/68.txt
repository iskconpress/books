====== CC Madhya 6.68 ======

===== Text =====

> mukunda-datta lañā āilā sārvabhauma sthāne
> sārvabhauma kichu tāṅre balilā vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukunda]]-[[synonyms:d:datta]]---of the name Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:b:balilā]]---said; [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---in words.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya then took Mukunda Datta with him and went to Sārvabhauma's house. When they arrived, Sārvabhauma addressed Mukunda Datta as follows.**
