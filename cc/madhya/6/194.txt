====== Cc. Madhya 6.194 ======

===== Text =====

> ātmārāmāś ca-śloke 'ekādaśa' pada haya
> pṛthak pṛthak kaila padera artha niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmārāmāś]] [[synonyms:c:ca]]---known as such; [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in the verse; [[synonyms:e:ekādaśa]]---eleven; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---words; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separately one after another; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:p:padera]]---of the words; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---certainty.

===== Translation =====

**There are eleven words in the ātmārāma verse, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu explained each word, one after the other.**

===== Purport =====

The words in the //ātmārāma// verse are //ātmārāmāḥ, ca, munayaḥ, nirgranthāḥ, api, urukrame, kurvanti, ahaitukīm, bhaktim, ittham-bhūta-guṇaḥ// and //hariḥ.//
