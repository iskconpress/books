====== CC Madhya 6.3 ======

===== Text =====

> āveśe calilā prabhu jagannātha-mandire
> jagannātha dekhi' preme ha-ilā asthire

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to the temple of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the Jagannātha Deity; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:a:asthire]]---restless.

===== Translation =====

**In ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went from Āṭhāranālā to the temple of Jagannātha. After seeing Lord Jagannātha, He became very restless due to love of Godhead.**
