====== CC Madhya 6.205 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura kṛpāya tāṅra sphurila saba tattva
> nāma-prema-dāna-ādi varṇena mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---to him; [[synonyms:s:sphurila]]---manifested; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---truths; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]---distribution of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and so on; [[synonyms:v:varṇena]]---describes; [[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---the importance.

===== Translation =====

**By the mercy of the Lord, all truths were revealed to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, and he could understand the importance of chanting the holy name and distributing love of Godhead everywhere.**
