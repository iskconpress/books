====== CC Madhya 6.46 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' pīṭhā-pānā saba khāoyāilā
> bhikṣā karāñā ācamana karāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:pīṭhā]]-[[synonyms:p:pānā]]---many kinds of cakes and condensed-milk preparations; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāilā]]---made to eat; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---after offering //prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]] [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---made them wash their hands, feet and mouths.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, he made them all eat the various cakes and condensed-milk preparations. After feeding them, he offered them water to wash their hands, feet and mouths.**
