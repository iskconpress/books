====== CC Madhya 6.113 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryera siddhānte mukundera haila santoṣa
> bhaṭṭācāryera vākya mane haila duḥkha-roṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:siddhānte]]---with the conclusions; [[synonyms:m:mukundera]]---of Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:v:vākye]]---by the words; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:r:roṣa]]---and anger.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Mukunda Datta felt very satisfied to hear the conclusive statements of Gopīnātha Ācārya, but he became very unhappy and angry to hear the statements put forward by Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
