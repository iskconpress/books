====== Cc. Madhya 6.189 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' bhaṭṭācārya śloka karila vyākhyāna
> tarka-śāstra-mata uṭhāya vividha vidhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation; [[synonyms:t:tarka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---scriptures dealing with logic; [[synonyms:m:mata]]---according to; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāya]]---raises; [[synonyms:v:vividha]]---various; [[synonyms:v:vidhāna]]---propositions.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then began to explain the ātmārāma verse, and according to the principles of logic, he put forward various propositions.**
