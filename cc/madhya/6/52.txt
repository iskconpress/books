====== CC Madhya 6.52 ======

===== Text =====

> 'viśvambhara'---nāma iṅhāra, tāṅra iṅho putra
> nīlāmbara cakravartīra hayena dauhitra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśvambhara]]---of the name Viśvambhara; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Jagannātha Miśra; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---son; [[synonyms:n:nīlāmbara]] [[synonyms:c:cakravartīra]]---of Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---is; [[synonyms:d:dauhitra]]---grandson (daughter's son).

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the son of that Jagannātha Miśra, and His former name was Viśvambhara Miśra. He also happens to be the grandson of Nīlāmbara Cakravartī."**
