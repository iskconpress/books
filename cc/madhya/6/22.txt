====== CC Madhya 6.22 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-gosāñike ācārya kaila namaskāra
> sabe meli' puche prabhura vārtā bāra bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñike]]---unto Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:m:meli']]---meeting them all; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquires; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Gopīnātha Ācārya saw Nityānanda Prabhu, he offered his obeisances unto Him. In this way, meeting all the devotees, he asked about news of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu again and again.**
