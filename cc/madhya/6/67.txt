====== CC Madhya 6.67 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina gopīnātha prabhu sthāne giyā
> śayyotthāna daraśana karāila lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:ś:śayyā]]-[[synonyms:u:utthāna]]---the rising from bed of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---caused; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking Him.

===== Translation =====

**The next day Gopīnātha Ācārya took Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu to see the early rising of Lord Jagannātha.**
