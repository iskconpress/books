====== CC Madhya 6.283 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche paripāṭī kare bhikṣā-nirvāhana
> vistāriyā āge tāhā kariba varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:p:paripāṭī]]---perfectly; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---of offering alms; [[synonyms:n:nirvāhana]]---the execution; [[synonyms:v:vistāriyā]]---in full detail; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---later; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:karibavarṇana]]---I shall describe.

===== Translation =====

**I shall also describe in full detail how Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya perfectly rendered service to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu by offering Him alms.**
