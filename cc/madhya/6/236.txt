====== CC Madhya 6.236 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' mahāprabhu āilā nija-sthāne
> sei haite bhaṭṭācāryera khaṇḍila abhimāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---speaking in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to His own residence; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from that time; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍila]]---was dismantled; [[synonyms:a:abhimāne]]---false pride.

===== Translation =====

**After speaking to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His residence. From that day on, the Bhaṭṭācārya was free because his false pride had been dismantled.**
