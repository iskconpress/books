====== CC Madhya 6.64 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnāthācāryake kahe sārvabhauma
> 'tumi gosāñire lañā karāiha daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācāryake]]---to Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:karāiha]]---make Him do; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing of Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then told Gopīnātha Ācārya, "Take Gosvāmījī and show Him Lord Jagannātha.**
