====== CC Madhya 6.16 ======

===== Text =====

> mūrcchita haila, cetana nā haya śarīre
> sārvabhauma lañā gelā āpanāra ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mūrcchita]]---unconscious; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---in His body; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking Him; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]]---his own; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the home.

===== Translation =====

**The people said that the sannyāsī had fallen unconscious upon seeing the Deity of Lord Jagannātha. Because His consciousness did not return, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya had taken Him to his home.**
