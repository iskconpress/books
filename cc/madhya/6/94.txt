====== Cc. Madhya 6.94 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-bhāgavata haya caitanya-gosāñi
> ei kali-kāle viṣṇura avatāra nāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---a great devotee; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the Age of Kali; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇura]]---of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---there is not.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is certainly a great, uncommon devotee, but we cannot accept Him as an incarnation of Lord Viṣṇu because, according to śāstra, there is no incarnation in this Age of Kali.**
