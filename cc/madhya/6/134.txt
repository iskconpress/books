====== CC Madhya 6.134 ======

===== Text =====

> mukhyārtha chāḍiyā kara gauṇārtha kalpanā
> 'abhidhā'-vṛtti chāḍi' kara śabdera lakṣaṇā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---direct meaning; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you do; [[synonyms:g:gauṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---indirect meaning; [[synonyms:k:kalpanā]]---imagining; [[synonyms:a:abhidhā]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛtti]]---the meaning that is understood immediately; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you do; [[synonyms:ś:śabdera]]---of the words; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇā]]---interpretation.

===== Translation =====

**"For each sūtra the direct meaning must be accepted without interpretation. However, you simply abandon the direct meaning and proceed with your imaginative interpretation.**
