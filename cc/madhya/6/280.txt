====== CC Madhya 6.280 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācāryera vaiṣṇavatā dekhi' sarva-jana
> prabhuke jānila---'sākṣāt vrajendra-nandana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavatā]]---clear understanding of Vaiṣṇava philosophy; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---all persons; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---knew; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing transcendental Vaiṣṇavism in Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, everyone could understand that Lord Caitanya was none other than Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.**
