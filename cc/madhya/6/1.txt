====== CC Madhya 6.1 ======

===== Text =====

> naumi taṁ gauracandraṁ yaḥ
> kutarka-karkaśāśayam
> sārvabhaumaṁ sarva-bhūmā
> bhakti-bhūmānam ācarat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:naumi]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:g:gauracandram]]---who is known as Lord Gauracandra; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:k:ku]]-[[synonyms:t:tarka]]---by bad arguments; [[synonyms:k:karkaśa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśayam]]---whose heart was hard; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaumam]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūmā]]---the Lord of everything; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūmānam]]---into a great personality of devotion; [[synonyms:ā:ācarat]]---converted.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances unto Lord Gauracandra, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who converted the hardhearted Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, the reservoir of all bad logic, into a great devotee.**
