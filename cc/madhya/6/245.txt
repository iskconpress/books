====== CC Madhya 6.245 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya kahe tāṅre kari' namaskāre
> tomāra sambandhe prabhu kṛpā kaila more

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:n:namaskāre]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---on account of your relationship; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---showed; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me.

===== Translation =====

**Offering his obeisances to Gopīnātha Ācārya, the Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Because I am related to you and you are a devotee, by your mercy the Lord has shown mercy to me.**
