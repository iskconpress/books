====== Cc. Madhya 6.187 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' bhaṭṭācārya kahe,---'śuna, mahāśaya
> ei ślokera artha śunite vāñchā haya'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśaya]]---my dear Sir; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of this verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---a desire; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing the ātmārāma verse, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya addressed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "My dear Sir, please explain this verse. I have a great desire to hear Your explanation of it."**
