====== Cc. Madhya 6.54 ======

===== Text =====

> 'miśra purandara' tāṅra mānya, hena jāni
> pitāra sambandhe doṅhāke pūjya kari' māni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśra]] [[synonyms:p:purandara]]---Jagannātha Miśra Purandara; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mānya]]---respectable; [[synonyms:h:hena]]---thus; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I know; [[synonyms:p:pitāra]] [[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---in relationship to my father; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāke]]---both of them (Nīlāmbara Cakravartī and Jagannātha Miśra); [[synonyms:p:pūjya]]---respectable; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---thinking; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I accept.

===== Translation =====

**"Jagannātha Miśra Purandara was respected by my father. Thus because of their relationship with my father, I respect both Jagannātha Miśra and Nīlāmbara Cakravartī."**
