====== CC Madhya 6.57 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' mahāprabhu kaila śrī-viṣṇu smaraṇa
> bhaṭṭācārye kahe kichu vinaya vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]] [[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]---remembering Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārye]]---to Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---very humble statements.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this from the Bhaṭṭācārya, He immediately remembered Lord Viṣṇu and began to speak humbly to him as follows.**
