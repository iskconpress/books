====== CC Madhya 6.232 ======

===== Text =====

> āji tumi niṣkapaṭe hailā kṛṣṇāśraya
> kṛṣṇa āji niṣkapaṭe tomā haila sadaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:n:niṣkapaṭe]]---without a doubt; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---have become; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---under the shelter of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:n:niṣkapaṭe]]---without reservation; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---unto you; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---has become; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:daya]]---very merciful.

===== Translation =====

**"Indeed, today you have undoubtedly taken shelter of the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa, and Kṛṣṇa, without reservation, has become very merciful toward you.**
