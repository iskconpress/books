====== Cc. Madhya 6.207 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' sukhe prabhu tāṅre kaila āliṅgana
> bhaṭṭācārya premāveśe haila acetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstasy of love of God; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:a:acetana]]---unconscious.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing the one hundred verses, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu happily embraced Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, who was immediately overwhelmed in ecstatic love of Godhead and fell unconscious.**
