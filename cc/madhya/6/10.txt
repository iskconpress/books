====== Cc. Madhya 6.10 ======

===== Text =====

> sūkṣma tulā āni' nāsā-agrete dharila
> īṣat calaye tulā dekhi' dhairya haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---fine; [[synonyms:t:tulā]]---cotton; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:n:nāsā]]---of the nostril; [[synonyms:a:agrete]]---in front; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---held; [[synonyms:ī:īṣat]]---slightly; [[synonyms:c:calaye]]---moves; [[synonyms:t:tulā]]---the cotton; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:d:dhairya]]---patience; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya then took a fine cotton swab and put it before the Lord's nostrils. When he saw the cotton move very slightly, he became hopeful.**
