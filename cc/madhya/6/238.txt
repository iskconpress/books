====== CC Madhya 6.238 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnāthācārya tāṅra vaiṣṇavatā dekhiyā
> 'hari' 'hari' bali' nāce hāte tāli diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya, the brother-in-law of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavatā]]---firm faith in Vaiṣṇavism; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:h:hāte]] [[synonyms:t:tāli]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---clapping his two hands.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing that Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya was firmly fixed in the cult of Vaiṣṇavism, Gopīnātha Ācārya, his brother-in-law, began to dance, clap his hands and chant "Hari! Hari!"**
