====== CC Madhya 6.35 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe meli' dhari tāṅre susthira karila
> īśvara-sevaka mālā-prasāda āni' dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:m:meli']]---meeting all together; [[synonyms:d:dhari]]---caught; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:sthira]]---steady; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the priest of the Deity; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garland; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---offering; [[synonyms:ā:āni]]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Nityānanda Prabhu nearly fainted, all the devotees caught Him and steadied Him. At that time, the priest of Lord Jagannātha brought a garland that had been offered to the Deity and offered it to Nityānanda Prabhu.**
