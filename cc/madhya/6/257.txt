====== CC Madhya 6.257 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma hailā prabhura bhakta ekatāna
> mahāprabhura sevā-vinā nāhi jāne āna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---a devotee; [[synonyms:e:ekatāna]]---without deviation; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---except; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---anything else.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya became an unalloyed devotee of Caitanya Mahāprabhu; he did not know anything but the service of the Lord.**
