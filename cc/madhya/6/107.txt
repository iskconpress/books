====== CC Madhya 6.107 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra ye śiṣya kahe kutarka, nānā-vāda
> ihāra ki doṣa---ei māyāra prasāda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]]---disciples; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:k:ku]]-[[synonyms:t:tarka]]---false arguments; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:v:vāda]]---jugglery of philosophy; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---their; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:māyāra]]---of illusion; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---benediction.

===== Translation =====

**"The false arguments and philosophical word jugglery of your disciples are not faults of theirs. They have simply received the benediction of Māyāvāda philosophy.**
