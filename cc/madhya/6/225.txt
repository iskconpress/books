====== Cc. Madhya 6.225 ======

===== Text =====

> śuṣkaṁ paryuṣitaṁ vāpi
> nītaṁ vā dūra-deśataḥ
> prāpti-mātreṇa bhoktavyaṁ
> nātra kāla-vicāraṇā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuṣkam]]---dry; [[synonyms:p:paryuṣitam]]---stale; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:n:nītam]]---brought; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:d:dūra]]-[[synonyms:d:deśataḥ]]---from a distant country; [[synonyms:p:prāpti]]-[[synonyms:m:mātreṇa]]---only with the receiving; [[synonyms:b:bhoktavyam]]---to be eaten; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:a:atra]]---in this; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:v:vicāraṇā]]---consideration of time or place.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya said, " 'One should eat the mahā-prasāda of the Lord immediately upon receiving it, even though it is dried up, stale or brought from a distant country. One should consider neither time nor place.**
