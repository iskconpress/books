====== CC Madhya 6.267 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sālokyādi' cāri yadi haya sevā-dvāra
> tabu kadācit bhakta kare aṅgīkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sālokya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with //sālokya//; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four kinds of liberation; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---a means of rendering service to the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:k:kadācit]]---occasionally; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---a pure devotee; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---acceptance.

===== Translation =====

**"If there is a chance to serve the Supreme Personality of Godhead, a pure devotee sometimes accepts the sālokya, sārūpya, sāmīpya or sārṣṭi forms of liberation, but never sāyujya.**
