====== CC Madhya 6.212 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe bhaṭṭācārye prabhu susthira karila
> sthira hañā bhaṭṭācārya bahu stuti kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārye]]---unto Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:sthira]]---pacification; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sthira]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being pacified; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---prayers; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu pacified the Bhaṭṭācārya, and when he was quieted, he offered many prayers to the Lord.**
