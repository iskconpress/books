====== Cc. Madhya 6.9 ======

===== Text =====

> śvāsa-praśvāsa nāhi udara-spandana
> dekhiyā cintita haila bhaṭṭācāryera mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śvāsa]]-[[synonyms:p:praśvāsa]]---breathing; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was not; [[synonyms:u:udara]]---of the abdomen; [[synonyms:s:spandana]]---movement; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:c:cintita]]---full of anxiety; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Examining the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Sārvabhauma saw that His abdomen was not moving and that He was not breathing. Seeing His condition, the Bhaṭṭācārya became very anxious.**
