====== CC Madhya 6.210 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnāthācārya kahe mahāprabhura prati
> 'sei bhaṭṭācāryera prabhu kaile ei gati'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of the name Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prati]]---to; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of that Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---You have made; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:g:gati]]---such a situation.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya told Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Sir, You have brought all this upon Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya."**
