====== CC Madhya 6.29 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' gopīnātha sabāre lañā
> sārvabhauma-ghare gelā haraṣita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with him; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming very pleased.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this and feeling very pleased, Gopīnātha Ācārya immediately took all the devotees with him and approached the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
