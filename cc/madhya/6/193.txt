====== Cc. Madhya 6.193 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācāryera prārthanāte prabhu vyākhyā kaila
> tāṅra nava artha-madhye eka nā chuṅila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prārthanāte]]---on the request; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyā]]---explanation; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:n:nava]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---of the nine different types of explanations; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the midst; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:c:chuṅila]]---touched.

===== Translation =====

**Upon the request of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to explain the verse, without touching upon the nine explanations given by the Bhaṭṭācārya.**
