====== Cc. Madhya 6.71 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnātha kahe,---nāma śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya
> guru iṅhāra keśava-bhāratī mahā-dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya replied; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---His name; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of the name Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---//sannyāsa-guru//; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:k:keśava]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---of the name Keśava Bhāratī; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---the greatly fortunate personality.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya replied, "The Lord's name is Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya, and His sannyāsa preceptor is the greatly fortunate Keśava Bhāratī."**
