====== CC Madhya 6.4 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha āliṅgite calilā dhāñā
> mandire paḍilā preme āviṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgite]]---to embrace; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---very swiftly; [[synonyms:m:mandire]]---in the temple; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went swiftly to embrace Lord Jagannātha, but when He entered the temple, He was so overwhelmed with love of Godhead that He fainted on the floor.**
