====== CC Madhya 6.42 ======

===== Text =====

> suvarṇa-thālīra anna uttama vyañjana
> bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge prabhu karena bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:suvarṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:thālīra]]---on golden plates; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---rice; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---first-class; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---accepts; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---lunch.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya Mahāprabhu was offered special rice and first-class vegetables on golden plates. He thus took lunch in the company of His devotees.**
