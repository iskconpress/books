====== CC Madhya 6.23 ======

===== Text =====

> mukunda kahe,---'mahāprabhu sannyāsa kariyā
> nīlācale āilā saṅge āmā-sabā lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukunda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Mukunda Datta replies; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---after accepting the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---has come; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Him; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of us; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Mukunda Datta continued, "After accepting the sannyāsa order, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu has come to Jagannātha Purī and has brought all of us with Him.**
