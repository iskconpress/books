====== CC Madhya 6.47 ======

===== Text =====

> ājñā māgi' gelā gopīnātha ācāryake lañā
> prabhura nikaṭa āilā bhojana kariñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---taking permission; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]] [[synonyms:ā:ācāryake]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kariñā]]---after taking lunch.

===== Translation =====

**Begging permission from Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then went with Gopīnātha Ācārya to take lunch. After finishing their lunch, they returned to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
