====== CC Madhya 6.88 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,---ācārya, kaha sāvadhāne
> tomāte īśvara-kṛpā ithe ki pramāṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya says; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---my dear Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---kindly speak; [[synonyms:s:sāvadhāne]]---very carefully; [[synonyms:t:tomāte]]---unto you; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy of the Lord; [[synonyms:i:ithe]]---in this matter; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:p:pramāṇe]]---by what evidence.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "My dear Gopīnātha Ācārya, please speak with great care. What is the proof that you have received the mercy of the Lord?"**
