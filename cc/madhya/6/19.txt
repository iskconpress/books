====== CC Madhya 6.19 ======

===== Text =====

> mukunda-sahita pūrve āche paricaya
> mukunda dekhiyā tāṅra ha-ila vismaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukunda]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:paricaya]]---acquaintance; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him (Gopīnātha Ācārya); [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya had previously been acquainted with Mukunda Datta, and when the Ācārya saw him at Jagannātha Purī, he was very much astonished.**
