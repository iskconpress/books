====== Cc. Madhya 6.250 ======

===== Text =====

> nija kṛta dui śloka likhiyā tāla-pāte
> 'prabhuke diha' bali' dila jagadānanda-hāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]---by him; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]---composed; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:l:likhiyā]]---writing; [[synonyms:t:tāla]]-[[synonyms:p:pāte]]---on a leaf of a palm tree; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]] [[synonyms:d:diha]]---give to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave it; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hands of Jagadānanda.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya then composed two verses on the leaf of a palm tree. Giving the palm leaf to Jagadānanda Prabhu, the Bhaṭṭācārya requested him to deliver it to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
