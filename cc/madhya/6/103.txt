====== Cc. Madhya 6.103 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-varṇaṁ tviṣākṛṣṇaṁ
> sāṅgopāṅgāstra-pārṣadam
> yajñaiḥ saṅkīrtana-prāyair
> yajanti hi su-medhasaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇam]]---chanting the two syllables //kṛṣ// and //ṇa//; [[synonyms:t:tviṣā]]---by complexion; [[synonyms:a:akṛṣṇam]]---not blackish; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---accompanied by personal expansions; [[synonyms:u:upa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---devotees; [[synonyms:a:astra]]---the weapon of chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra; [[synonyms:p:pārṣadam]]---and associates like Gadādhara, Svarūpa Dāmodara, etc.; [[synonyms:y:yajñaiḥ]]---by sacrifice; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---congregational chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:p:prāyaiḥ]]---chiefly consisting of; [[synonyms:y:yajanti]]---worship; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:m:medhasaḥ]]---those who are intelligent.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'In this Age of Kali, those who are intelligent perform the congregational chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, worshiping the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who appears in this age always describing the glories of Kṛṣṇa. That incarnation is yellowish in hue and is always associated with His plenary expansions [such as Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu] and personal expansions [such as Gadādhara], as well as His devotees and associates [such as Svarūpa Dāmodara].'**

===== Purport =====

This verse from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (11.5.32) is explained by Śrī Jīva Gosvāmī in his //Krama-sandarbha,// as quoted by Śrīla Bhaktivinoda Ṭhākura in regard to the explanation of //Ādi-līlā,// Third Chapter, verse 52.
