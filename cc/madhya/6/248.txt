====== CC Madhya 6.248 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda dāmodara,---dui saṅge lañā
> ghare āila bhaṭṭācārya jagannātha dekhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---of the name Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---of the name Dāmodara; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two persons; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his home; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---returned; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing in the temple.

===== Translation =====

**After visiting the temple of Lord Jagannātha, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya returned home with Jagadānanda and Dāmodara.**
