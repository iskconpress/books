====== CC Madhya 6.17 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' sabe jānilā ei mahāprabhura kārya
> hena-kāle āilā tāhāṅ gopīnāthācārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:j:jānilā]]---could understand; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---the activities; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of the name Gopīnātha Ācārya.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, the devotees could understand that they were speaking of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Just then, Śrī Gopīnātha Ācārya arrived.**
