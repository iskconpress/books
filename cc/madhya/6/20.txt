====== Cc. Madhya 6.20 ======

===== Text =====

> mukunda tāṅhāre dekhi' kaila namaskāra
> teṅho āliṅgiyā puche prabhura samācāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquires; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---news.

===== Translation =====

**Mukunda Datta offered obeisances unto Gopīnātha Ācārya upon meeting him. Then the Ācārya embraced Mukunda Datta and inquired about news of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
