====== CC Madhya 6.100 ======

===== Text =====

> pratiyuge karena kṛṣṇa yuga-avatāra
> tarka-niṣṭha hṛdaya tomāra nāhika vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:y:yuge]]---in every age or millennium; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---makes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yuga]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation for the age; [[synonyms:t:tarka]]-[[synonyms:n:niṣṭha]]---hardened by argument; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---heart; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is not; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya continued, "There is certainly an incarnation in every age, and such an incarnation is called the yuga-avatāra. But your heart has become so hardened by logic and argument that you cannot consider all these facts.**
