====== CC Madhya 6.249 ======

===== Text =====

> uttama uttama prasāda bahuta ānilā
> nija-vipra-hāte dui janā saṅge dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttama]] [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---very first-class; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food offered to Jagannātha; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---in great quantity; [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---of his own //brāhmaṇa// servant; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:j:janā]]---persons; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya brought large quantities of excellent food remnants blessed by Lord Jagannātha. All this prasāda was given to his own brāhmaṇa servant, along with Jagadānanda and Dāmodara.**
