====== Cc. Madhya 6.217 ======

===== Text =====

> pūjārī āniyā mālā-prasādānna dilā
> prasādānna-mālā pāñā prabhu harṣa hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūjārī]]---the priest; [[synonyms:ā:āniyā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garlands; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---offered; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---and garlands; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]---pleased; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**The priest there presented Him with garlands and prasāda that had been offered to Lord Jagannātha. This pleased Caitanya Mahāprabhu very much.**
