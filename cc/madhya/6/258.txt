====== CC Madhya 6.258 ======

===== Text =====

> 'śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya śacī-sūta guṇa-dhāma'
> ei dhyāna, ei japa, laya ei nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:s:sūta]]---the son of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---the reservoir of all good qualities; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]]---meditation; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:j:japa]]---chanting; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---he takes; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---holy name.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya always chanted the holy name of Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya, son of mother Śacī and reservoir of all good qualities. Indeed, chanting the holy names became his meditation.**
