====== Cc. Madhya 6.233 ======

===== Text =====

> āji se khaṇḍila tomāra dehādi-bandhana
> āji tumi chinna kaile māyāra bandhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍila]]---dismantled; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhana]]---material bondage due to the bodily concept of life; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:c:chinna]]---cut to pieces; [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---did; [[synonyms:m:māyāra]]---of illusory energy; [[synonyms:b:bandhana]]---the shackles.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Bhaṭṭācārya, today you have been released from material bondage in the bodily conception of life; you have cut to pieces the shackles of the illusory energy.**
