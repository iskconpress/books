~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 6.176~~

====== CC Madhya 6.176 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate kalpita bhāṣye śata doṣa dila
> bhaṭṭācārya pūrva-pakṣa apāra karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kalpita]]---imagined; [[synonyms:b:bhāṣye]]---in the commentary; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---hundreds; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---of faults; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:p:pakṣa]]---opposing elements; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimitedly; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---manifested.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu criticized Śaṅkarācārya's Śārīraka-bhāṣya as imaginary, and He pointed out hundreds of faults in it. To defend Śaṅkarācārya, however, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya presented unlimited opposition.**
