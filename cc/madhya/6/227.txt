====== CC Madhya 6.227 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' ānandita haila mahāprabhura mana
> premāviṣṭa hañā prabhu kailā āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing this; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very much pleased; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---absorbed in the ecstasy of love of God; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very pleased to see this. He became ecstatic in love of Godhead and embraced Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
