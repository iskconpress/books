====== CC Madhya 6.77 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' gopīnātha-mukunda duṅhe duḥkhā hailā
> gopīnāthācārya kichu kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:m:mukunda]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya and Mukunda Datta; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhā]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---of the name Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya and Mukunda Datta became very unhappy when they heard this. Gopīnātha Ācārya therefore addressed Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya as follows.**
