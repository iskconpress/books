====== CC Madhya 6.185 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ātmārāma' paryanta kare īśvara bhajana
> aiche acintya bhagavānera guṇa-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ātmā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārāma]]---self-satisfied; [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---up to; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---devotional service to the Lord; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:a:acintya]]---inconceivable; [[synonyms:b:bhagavānera]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---transcendental qualities.

===== Translation =====

**"Even the self-satisfied sages perform devotional service to the Supreme Lord. Such are the transcendental qualities of the Lord. They are full of inconceivable spiritual potency.**
