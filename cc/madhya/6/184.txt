====== CC Madhya 6.184 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---bhaṭṭācārya, nā kara vismaya
> bhagavāne bhakti---parama-puruṣārtha haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---My dear Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:b:bhagavāne]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---the Supreme; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---human interest; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then told him, "Do not be astonished. Actually, devotional service unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead is the highest perfection of human activity.**
