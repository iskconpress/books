====== CC Madhya 6.28 ======

===== Text =====

> cala, sabe yāi sārvabhaumera bhavana
> prabhu dekhi' pāche kariba īśvara darśana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cala]]---let us go; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---we shall go; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhaumera]] [[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---to the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---we shall do; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---seeing of Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**"First let us all go to the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and see Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Later we shall come to see Lord Jagannātha."**
