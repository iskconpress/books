====== CC Madhya 6.53 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,---nīlāmbara cakravartī
> viśāradera samādhyāyī,---ei tāṅra khyāti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma said; [[synonyms:n:nīlāmbara]] [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---the gentleman named Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; [[synonyms:v:viśāradera]]---of Maheśvara Viśārada (Sārvabhauma's father); [[synonyms:s:samādhyāyī]]---class friend; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him; [[synonyms:k:khyāti]]---acquaintance.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya said, "Nīlāmbara Cakravartī was a classmate of my father, Maheśvara Viśārada. I knew him as such.**
