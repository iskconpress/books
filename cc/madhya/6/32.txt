====== CC Madhya 6.32 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā sahita yathā-yogya karila milana
> prabhu dekhi' sabāra haila haraṣita mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---as it was befitting; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma met with all the devotees and offered them a proper welcome. They were all pleased to see Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
