~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 6.243~~

====== CC Madhya 6.243 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ślokera artha śunāila kariyā vistāra
> śuni' bhaṭṭācārya-mane haila camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of this verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ś:śunāila]]---made hear; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---extensive description; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonder.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu very elaborately explained the harer nāma verse of the Bṛhan-nāradīya Purāṇa, and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya was struck with wonder to hear His explanation.**
