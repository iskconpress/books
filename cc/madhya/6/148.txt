====== CC Madhya 6.148 ======

===== Text =====

> vedera nigūḍha artha bujhana nā haya
> purāṇa-vākye sei artha karaya niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vedera]]---of the Vedic literature; [[synonyms:n:nigūḍha]]---confidential; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:b:bujhana]]---understanding; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:purāṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]]---by the words of the //Purāṇas//; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:k:karaya]]---makes; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---certain.

===== Translation =====

**"The confidential meaning of the Vedas is not easily understood by common men; therefore that meaning is supplemented by the words of the Purāṇas.**
