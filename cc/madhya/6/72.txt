====== CC Madhya 6.72 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,---'iṅhāra nāma sarvottama
> bhāratī-sampradāya iṅho---hayena madhyama'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---first-class; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]-[[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---the community of the Bhāratī //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:h:hayena]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:madhyama]]---middle-class.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, " 'Śrī Kṛṣṇa' is a very good name, but He belongs to the Bhāratī community. Therefore He is a second-class sannyāsī."**
