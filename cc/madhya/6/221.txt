====== Cc. Madhya 6.221 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhire prabhura teṅho pāila daraśana
> āste-vyaste āsi' kaila caraṇa vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside the house; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---sight; [[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---with great haste; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya noticed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu outside, and with great haste he went to Him and offered prayers unto His lotus feet.**
