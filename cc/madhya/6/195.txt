====== CC Madhya 6.195 ======

===== Text =====

> tat-tat-pada-prādhānye 'ātmārāma' milāñā
> aṣṭādaśa artha kaila abhiprāya lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]---all those items; [[synonyms:p:prādhānye]]---principally; [[synonyms:ā:ātmārāma]]---the word //ātmārāma//; [[synonyms:m:milāñā]]---causing to meet; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭādaśa]]---eighteen; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meanings; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:a:abhiprāya]]---purpose; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accepting.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu took each word specifically and combined it with the word "ātmārāma." He thus explained the word "ātmārāma" in eighteen different ways.**
