====== CC Madhya 6.128 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya kahe,---nā bujhi', hena jñāna yāra
> bujhibāra lāgi' seha puche punarbāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:bujhi']]---not understanding; [[synonyms:h:hena]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---the knowledge; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of someone; [[synonyms:b:bujhibāra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---just to understand; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---he also; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquires; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "I accept that You do not understand, yet even one who does not understand inquires about the subject matter.**
