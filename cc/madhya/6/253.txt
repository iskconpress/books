~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 6.253~~

====== CC Madhya 6.253 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu śloka paḍi' patra chiṇḍiyā phelila
> bhittye dekhi' bhakta saba śloka kaṇṭhe kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reading; [[synonyms:p:patra]]---the palm leaf; [[synonyms:c:chiṇḍiyā]]---tearing to pieces; [[synonyms:p:phelila]]---threw; [[synonyms:b:bhittye]]---on the outside wall; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭhe]]---within the throat; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---kept.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu read the two verses, He immediately tore up the palm leaf. However, all the devotees read these verses on the outside wall, and they all kept them within their hearts. The verses read as follows.**
