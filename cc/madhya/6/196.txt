====== CC Madhya 6.196 ======

===== Text =====

> bhagavān, tāṅra śakti, tāṅra guṇa-gaṇa
> acintya prabhāva tinera nā yāya kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---His potencies; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---His transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:a:acintya]]---inconceivable; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---the influence; [[synonyms:t:tinera]]---of the three; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---possible; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "The Supreme Personality of Godhead, His different potencies and His transcendental qualities all have inconceivable prowess. It is not possible to explain them fully.**
