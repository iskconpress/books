====== CC Madhya 6.93 ======

===== Text =====

> iṣṭa-goṣṭhī vicāra kari, nā kariha roṣa
> śāstra-dṛṣṭye kahi, kichu nā la-iha doṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---discussion among friends; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---we do; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---make; [[synonyms:r:roṣa]]---anger; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭye]]---according to the conclusion of scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---we speak; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iha]]---take; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya said, "We are just having a discussion among friends and considering the points described in the scriptures. Do not become angry. I am simply speaking on the strength of the śāstras. Please don't take any offense.**
