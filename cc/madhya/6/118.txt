====== CC Madhya 6.118 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina mahāprabhu bhaṭṭācārya-sane
> ānande karilā jagannātha daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āradina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---along with Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great pleasure; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---to Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---visit.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya together visited the temple of Lord Jagannātha. Both of them were in a very pleasant mood.**
