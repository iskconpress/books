====== CC Madhya 6.11 ======

===== Text =====

> vasi' bhaṭṭācārya mane karena vicāra
> ei kṛṣṇa-mahāpremera sāttvika vikāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting down; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in his mind; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:premera]]---of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sāttvika]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformation.

===== Translation =====

**Sitting beside Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he thought, "This is a transcendental ecstatic transformation brought about by love of Kṛṣṇa."**
