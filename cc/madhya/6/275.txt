====== CC Madhya 6.275 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi 'mukti'-śabdera haya pañca vṛtti
> rūḍhi-vṛttye kahe tabu 'sāyujye' pratīti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:ś:śabdera]]---of the word; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:pañca]] [[synonyms:v:vṛtti]]---five meanings; [[synonyms:r:rūḍhi]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛttye]]---by the chief or direct meaning; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---it says; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:s:sāyujye]]---to become one with the Supreme; [[synonyms:p:pratīti]]---the conception.

===== Translation =====

**"The word 'mukti' refers to five kinds of liberation. Usually its direct meaning conveys the idea of becoming one with the Lord.**
