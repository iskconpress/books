====== CC Madhya 6.260 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgavatera 'brahma-stave'ra śloka paḍilā
> śloka-śeṣe dui akṣara-pāṭha phirāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavatera]]---from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:stavera]]---of the prayers of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---a verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---recited; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at the end of the verse; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:a:akṣara]]---of two syllables; [[synonyms:p:pāṭha]]---the reading; [[synonyms:p:phirāilā]]---changed.

===== Translation =====

**He began to quote one of Lord Brahmā's prayers from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, but he changed two syllables at the end of the verse.**
