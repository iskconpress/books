====== CC Madhya 6.38 ======

===== Text =====

> huṅkāra kariyā uṭhe 'hari' 'hari' bali'
> ānande sārvabhauma tāṅra laila pada-dhūli

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making a loud sound; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---got up; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---chanting Hari, Hari; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in pleasure; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:l:laila]]---took; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:d:dhūli]]---the dust of the feet.

===== Translation =====

**Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up and very loudly chanted, "Hari! Hari!" Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya was very pleased to see the Lord regain consciousness, and he took the dust of the Lord's lotus feet.**
