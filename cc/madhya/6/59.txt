====== CC Madhya 6.59 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi bālaka-sannyāsī---bhānda-manda nāhi jāni
> tomāra āśraya niluṅ, guru kari' māni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bālaka]]-[[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---a young //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:b:bhānda]]-[[synonyms:m:manda]]---good and bad; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---know; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---shelter; [[synonyms:n:niluṅ]]---have taken; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---spiritual master; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking as; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I accept.

===== Translation =====

**"I am a young sannyāsī, and I actually have no knowledge of what is good and what is bad. Therefore I am taking shelter of you and accepting you as My spiritual master.**
