====== CC Madhya 6.220 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa' sphuṭa kahi' bhaṭṭācārya jāgilā
> kṛṣṇa-nāma śuni' prabhura ānanda bāḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---chanting the name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sphuṭa]]---distinctly; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:j:jāgilā]]---got up from the bed; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:b:bāḍilā]]---increased.

===== Translation =====

**As Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya arose from bed, he distinctly chanted, "Kṛṣṇa, Kṛṣṇa." Lord Caitanya was very pleased to hear him chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.**
