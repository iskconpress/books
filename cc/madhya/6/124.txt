====== CC Madhya 6.124 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭama-divase tāṅre puche sārvabhauma
> sāta dina kara tumi vedānta śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭama]]-[[synonyms:d:divase]]---on the eighth day; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquires; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---seven days; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:v:vedānta]]---Vedānta philosophy; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hearing.

===== Translation =====

**On the eighth day, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said to Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "You have been listening to Vedānta philosophy from me continually for seven days.**
