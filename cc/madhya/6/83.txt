====== CC Madhya 6.83 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvarera kṛpā-leśa haya ta' yāhāre
> sei ta' īśvara-tattva jānibāre pāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]]---of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:l:leśa]]---a little mercy; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:y:yāhāre]]---upon whom; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---he certainly; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:j:jānibāre]]---to know; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is able.

===== Translation =====

**The Ācārya continued, "If one receives but a tiny bit of the Lord's favor by dint of devotional service, he can understand the nature of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
