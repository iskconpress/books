====== CC Madhya 6.131 ======

===== Text =====

> sūtrera artha bhāṣya kahe prakāśiyā
> tumi, bhāṣya kaha---sūtrera artha ācchādiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūtrera]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meanings of the //sūtras//; [[synonyms:b:bhāṣya]]---the purport; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---one speaks; [[synonyms:p:prakāśiyā]]---clearly manifesting; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:b:bhāṣya]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---make a comment; [[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the //sūtras//; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meanings; [[synonyms:ā:ācchādiyā]]---covering.

===== Translation =====

**"The meaning of the aphorisms in the Vedānta-sūtra contain clear purports in themselves, but other purports you presented simply covered the meaning of the sūtras like a cloud.**

===== Purport =====

Please refer to //Ādi-līlā,// Seventh Chapter, verses 106-146, for an explanation of this verse.
