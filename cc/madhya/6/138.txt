====== Cc. Madhya 6.138 ======

===== Text =====

> vyāsa-sūtrera artha---yaiche sūryera kiraṇa
> sva-kalpita bhāṣya-meghe kare ācchādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtrera]]---of the //Vedānta-sūtra,// by Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meanings; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:s:sūryera]]---of the sun; [[synonyms:k:kiraṇa]]---shining rays; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpita]]---imaginative; [[synonyms:b:bhāṣya]]---of the commentary; [[synonyms:m:meghe]]---by the cloud; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:ācchādana]]---covering.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "The Brahma-sūtra, compiled by Śrīla Vyāsadeva, is as radiant as the sun. One who tries to interpret its meaning simply covers that sunshine with a cloud.**
