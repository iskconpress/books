====== Cc. Madhya 6.219 ======

===== Text =====

> aruṇodaya-kāle haila prabhura āgamana
> sei-kāle bhaṭṭācāryera haila jāgaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---before sunrise; [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---the coming; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:j:jāgaraṇa]]---arising from bed.

===== Translation =====

**He arrived at the Bhaṭṭācārya's house a little before sunrise, just when the Bhaṭṭācārya was arising from bed.**
