====== Cc. Madhya 6.277 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā hāsena prabhu ānandita-mane
> bhaṭṭācārya kaila prabhu dṛḍha āliṅgane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing this explanation; [[synonyms:h:hāsena]]---laughs; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---with great pleasure in His mind; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---unto Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---firm; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgane]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this explanation, the Lord began to laugh and, with great pleasure, immediately embraced Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya very firmly.**
