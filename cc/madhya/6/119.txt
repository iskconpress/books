====== Cc. Madhya 6.119 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya-saṅge tāṅra mandire āilā
> prabhure āsana diyā āpane vasilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His (Lord Jagannātha's); [[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to the temple; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---sitting place; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down.

===== Translation =====

**When they entered the temple, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya offered Caitanya Mahāprabhu a seat, while he himself sat down on the floor out of due respect for a sannyāsī.**
