====== CC Madhya 6.74 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭācārya kahe,---'iṅhāra prauḍha yauvana
> kemate sannyāsa-dharma ha-ibe rakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---His; [[synonyms:p:prauḍha]]---full; [[synonyms:y:yauvana]]---youth; [[synonyms:k:kemate]]---how; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---principles of a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:r:rakṣaṇa]]---protection.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya inquired, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is in His full-fledged youthful life. How can He keep the principles of sannyāsa?**
