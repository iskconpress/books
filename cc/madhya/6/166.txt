====== CC Madhya 6.166 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvarera śrī-vigraha sac-cid-ānandākāra
> se-vigrahe kaha sattva-guṇera vikāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vigraha]]---the form; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---complete in eternity, cognizance and bliss; [[synonyms:s:se]]-[[synonyms:v:vigrahe]]---about that form of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---you say; [[synonyms:s:sattva]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇera]]---of the quality of material goodness; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformation.

===== Translation =====

**"The transcendental form of the Supreme Personality of Godhead is complete in eternity, cognizance and bliss. However, you describe this transcendental form as a product of material goodness.**
