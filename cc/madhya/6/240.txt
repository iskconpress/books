====== CC Madhya 6.240 ======

===== Text =====

> daṇḍavat kari' kaila bahu-vidha stuti
> dainya kari' kahe nija pūrva-durmati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after offering obeisances by falling flat on the ground; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---he did; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]]---various types of; [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---prayers; [[synonyms:d:dainya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in great humbleness; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---describes; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---his personal; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:d:durmati]]---previous bad disposition.

===== Translation =====

**When he met Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Bhaṭṭācārya fell down flat to offer Him respects. After offering various prayers to Him, he spoke of his previous bad disposition with great humility.**
