~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 6.209~~

====== CC Madhya 6.209 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' gopīnāthācārya haraṣita-mana
> bhaṭṭācāryera nṛtya dekhi' hāse prabhura gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing this; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---a pleased mind; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laughs; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the associates of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**While Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya was in this ecstasy. Gopīnātha Ācārya was very pleased, The associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu all laughed to see the Bhaṭṭācārya dance so.**
