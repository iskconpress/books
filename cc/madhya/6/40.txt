====== CC Madhya 6.40 ======

===== Text =====

> samudra-snāna kari' mahāprabhu śīghra āilā
> caraṇa pākhāli' prabhu āsane vasilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:samudra]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---a bath in the sea; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very soon; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---feet; [[synonyms:p:pākhāli']]---washing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsane]]---on a seat; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing in the sea, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees returned very soon. The Lord then washed His feet and sat down on a carpet to take lunch.**
