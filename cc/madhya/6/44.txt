====== Cc. Madhya 6.44 ======

===== Text =====

> pīṭhā-pānā deha tumi iṅhā-sabākāre
> tabe bhaṭṭācārya kahe yuḍi' dui kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pīṭhā]]-[[synonyms:p:pānā]]---cakes and condensed milk; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---give; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:i:iṅhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---to all these devotees; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:y:yuḍi']]---folding; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---two hands.

===== Translation =====

**"You can offer the cakes and other preparations made with condensed milk to all the devotees." Hearing this, the Bhaṭṭācārya folded his hands and spoke as follows.**
