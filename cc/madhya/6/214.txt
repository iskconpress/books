====== CC Madhya 6.214 ======

===== Text =====

> tarka-śāstre jaḍa āmi, yaiche lauha-piṇḍa
> āmā dravāile tumi, pratāpa pracaṇḍa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tarka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---due to logical scriptures; [[synonyms:j:jaḍa]]---dull; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just like; [[synonyms:l:lauha]]-[[synonyms:p:piṇḍa]]---an iron bar; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---me; [[synonyms:d:dravāile]]---melted; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:p:pratāpa]]---power; [[synonyms:p:pracaṇḍa]]---very great.

===== Translation =====

**"I had become dull-headed due to reading too many books on logic. Consequently I had become like an iron bar. Nonetheless, You have melted me, and therefore Your influence is very great."**
