====== CC Madhya 6.79 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāte vikhyāta iṅho parama-īśvara
> ajña-sthāne kichu nahe vijñera gocara'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:vikhyāta]]---celebrated; [[synonyms:i:iṅho]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:ajña]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---before an ignorant person; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:v:vijñera]]---of the person who knows; [[synonyms:g:gocara]]---information.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya continued, "Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu is celebrated as the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Those who are ignorant in this connection find the conclusion of knowledgeable men very difficult to understand."**
