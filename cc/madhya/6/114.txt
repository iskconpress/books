====== Cc. Madhya 6.114 ======

===== Text =====

> gosāñira sthāne ācārya kaila āgamana
> bhaṭṭācāryera nāme tāṅre kaila nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place where Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was staying; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Gopīnātha Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---coming; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]] [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---on behalf of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**According to the instructions of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Gopīnātha Ācārya went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and invited Him on the Bhaṭṭācārya's behalf.**
