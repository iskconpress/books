====== CC Madhya 6.229 ======

===== Text =====

> sveda-kampa-aśru duṅhe ānande bhāsilā
> premāviṣṭa hañā prabhu kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:b:bhāsilā]]---floated; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---absorbed in ecstatic love of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**As they danced and embraced, spiritual symptoms manifested in their bodies. They perspired, trembled and shed tears, and the Lord began to speak in His ecstasy.**
