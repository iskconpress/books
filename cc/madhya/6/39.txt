====== CC Madhya 6.39 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma kahe,---śīghra karaha madhyāhna
> muñi bhikṣā dimu āji mahā-prasādānna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---//says//; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very soon; [[synonyms:k:karaha]]---do; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:a:ahna]]---midday duties; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---alms; [[synonyms:d:dimu]]---shall offer; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---remnants of food offered to Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**The Bhaṭṭācārya informed all of them, "Please take your midday baths immediately. Today I shall offer you mahā-prasāda, the remnants of food offered to Lord Jagannātha."**
