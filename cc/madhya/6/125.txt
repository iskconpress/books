====== CC Madhya 6.125 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāla-manda nāhi kaha, raha mauna dhari'
> bujha, ki nā bujha,---ihā bujhite nā pāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:m:manda]]---right or wrong; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---not speak; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---keep; [[synonyms:m:mauna]]---silence; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---holding; [[synonyms:b:bujha]]---understand; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---or; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:b:bujha]]---understand; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am able.

===== Translation =====

**"You have simply been listening, fixed in Your silence. Since You do not say whether You think it is right or wrong, I cannot know whether You are actually understanding Vedānta philosophy or not."**
