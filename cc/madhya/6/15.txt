====== Cc. Madhya 6.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhā śune loke kahe anyonye bāt
> eka sannyāsī āsi' dekhi' jagannātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---at that place; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---they hear; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---talk; [[synonyms:a:anyonye]]---among themselves; [[synonyms:b:bāt]]---topics; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---mendicant; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming there; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---the Deity of Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**There the devotees heard the people talking about a mendicant who had come to Jagannātha Purī and seen the Deity of Jagannātha.**
