====== Cc. Madhya 6.36 ======

===== Text =====

> prasāda pāñā sabe hailā ānandita mane
> punarapi āilā sabe mahāprabhura sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting this honor of the garland; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---pleased in the mind; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place where Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was staying.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was pleased to receive this garland worn by Lord Jagannātha. Afterwards they all returned to the place where Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was staying.**
