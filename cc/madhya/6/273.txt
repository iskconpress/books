====== Cc. Madhya 6.273 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-arthe 'kṛṣṇa' kahi, kene pāṭha phiri
> sārvabhauma kahe,---o-pāṭha kahite nā pāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:a:arthe]]---by two interpretations; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I accept; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:p:pāṭha]]---reading; [[synonyms:p:phiri]]---changing; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sārvabhauma replied; [[synonyms:o:o]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṭha]]---such a reading; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to say; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---am able.

===== Translation =====

**"Since I can understand Kṛṣṇa according to these two meanings," Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "what point is there in changing the verse?" Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya replied, "I was not able to give that reading to the verse.**
