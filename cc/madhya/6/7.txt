====== Cc. Madhya 6.7 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu-kṣaṇe caitanya nahe, bhogera kāla haila
> sārvabhauma mane tabe upāya cintila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there was not; [[synonyms:b:bhogera]]---of offering food; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---the time; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it became; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---remedy; [[synonyms:c:cintila]]---thought.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained unconscious for a long time. Meanwhile, the time for offering prasāda to Lord Jagannātha came, and the Bhaṭṭācārya tried to think of a remedy.**
