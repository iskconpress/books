~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Madhya 6.224~~

====== CC Madhya 6.224 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-prasāde manera saba jāḍya gela
> ei śloka paḍi' anna bhakṣaṇa karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by the mercy of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:manera]]---of the mind; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jāḍya]]---dullness; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went away; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---these verses; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eating; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did.

===== Translation =====

**By the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the dullness in the mind of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya was eradicated. After reciting the following two verses, he ate the prasāda offered to him.**
