====== Cc. Madhya 6.218 ======

===== Text =====

> sei prasādānna-mālā añcale bāndhiyā
> bhaṭṭācāryera ghare āilā tvarāyukta hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---those remnants of food; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---and garlands; [[synonyms:a:añcale]]---in the end of His cloth; [[synonyms:b:bāndhiyā]]---binding; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the house; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went; [[synonyms:t:tvarā]]-[[synonyms:y:yukta]]---hasty; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being.

===== Translation =====

**Carefully tying the prasāda and garlands in a cloth, Caitanya Mahāprabhu hastened to the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
