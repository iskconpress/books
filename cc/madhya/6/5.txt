====== CC Madhya 6.5 ======

===== Text =====

> daive sārvabhauma tāṅhāke kare daraśana
> paḍichā mārite teṅho kaila nivāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daive]]---by chance; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāke]]---Him; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:paḍichā]]---the watchman in the temple; [[synonyms:m:mārite]]---to beat; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivāraṇa]]---forbidding.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell down, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya happened to see Him. When the watchman threatened to beat the Lord, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya immediately forbade him.**
