====== Cc. Madhya 6.213 ======

===== Text =====

> 'jagat nistārile tumi,---seha alpa-kārya
> āmā uddhārile tumi,---e śakti āścarya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:n:nistārile]]---have delivered; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]-[[synonyms:k:kārya]]---minor activity; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---me; [[synonyms:u:uddhārile]]---have delivered; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]---wonderful.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "My dear Sir, You have delivered the entire world, but that is not a very great task. However, You have also delivered me, and that is certainly the work of very wonderful powers.**
