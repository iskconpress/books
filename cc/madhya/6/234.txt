====== CC Madhya 6.234 ======

===== Text =====

> āji kṛṣṇa-prāpti-yogya haila tomāra mana
> veda-dharma laṅghi' kaile prasāda bhakṣaṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---for attainment of the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---fit; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---has become; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:v:veda]]---of the four //Vedas//; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the principles; [[synonyms:l:laṅghi']]---surpassing; [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---you have done; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of food offered to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**"Today your mind has become fit to take shelter of the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa because, surpassing the Vedic regulative principles, you have eaten the remnants of food offered to the Lord.**
