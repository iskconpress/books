====== CC Madhya 6.105 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra āge eta kathāra nāhi prayojana
> ūṣara-bhūmite yena bījera ropaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāraāge]]---before you; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so many; [[synonyms:k:kathāra]]---of words; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---necessity; [[synonyms:ū:ūṣara]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---in barren land; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---like; [[synonyms:b:bījera]]---of the seed; [[synonyms:r:ropaṇa]]---sowing.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Ācārya then said, "There is no need to quote so much evidence from the śāstras, for you are a very dry speculator. There is no need to sow seeds in barren land.**
