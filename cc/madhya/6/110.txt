====== CC Madhya 6.110 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe bhaṭṭācārya kahe, yāha gosāñira sthane
> āmāra nāme gaṇa-sahita kara nimantraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---please go; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]] [[synonyms:s:sthane]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in my name; [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with His associates; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---make; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇe]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this from Gopīnātha Ācārya, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said, "First go to the place where Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is staying and invite Him here with His associates. Ask Him on my account.**
