====== Cc. Madhya 6.117 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāra sannyāsa-dharma cāhena rākhite
> vātsalye karuṇā karena, ki doṣa ihāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---regulative principles of //sannyāsa//; [[synonyms:c:cāhena]]---he wants; [[synonyms:r:rākhite]]---to keep; [[synonyms:v:vātsalye]]---out of paternal affection; [[synonyms:k:karuṇā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault; [[synonyms:i:ihāte]]---in this connection.

===== Translation =====

**"Out of paternal affection for Me, he wants to protect Me and see that I follow the regulative principles of a sannyāsī. What fault is there in this?"**
