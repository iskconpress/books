====== CC Madhya 6.97 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgavata-bhārata dui śāstrera pradhāna
> sei dui-grantha-vākye nāhi avadhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:b:bhārata]]---//Mahābhārata//; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]]---of all Vedic scriptures; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the most prominent; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:g:grantha]]---of the two scriptures; [[synonyms:v:vākye]]---in the statements; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:a:avadhāna]]---attention.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam and the Mahābhārata are the two most important Vedic scriptures, but you have paid no attention to their statements.**
