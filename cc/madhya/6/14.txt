====== CC Madhya 6.14 ======

===== Text =====

> eta cinti' bhaṭṭācārya āchena vasiyā
> nityānandādi siṁha-dvāre milila āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:c:cinti']]---thinking like this; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---was; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---all the devotees, headed by Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the entrance door of the Jagannātha temple; [[synonyms:m:milila]]---met; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**While the Bhaṭṭācārya was thinking in this way at his home, all the devotees of Caitanya Mahāprabhu, headed by Nityānanda Prabhu, approached the Siṁha-dvāra [the entrance door of the temple].**
