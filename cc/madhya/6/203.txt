====== CC Madhya 6.203 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhāila tāṅre āge catur-bhuja-rūpa
> pāche śyāma-vaṁśī-mukha svakīya svarūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---showed; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---at first; [[synonyms:c:catur]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuja]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the form with four hands; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---afterwards; [[synonyms:ś:śyāma]]---blackish; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---with a flute to the mouth; [[synonyms:s:svakīya]]---personal; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---form.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu first showed him the four-handed form and then appeared before him in His original form of Kṛṣṇa, with a blackish complexion and a flute to His lips.**
