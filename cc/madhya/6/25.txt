====== Cc. Madhya 6.25 ======

===== Text =====

> anyonye lokera mukhe ye kathā śunila
> sārvabhauma-gṛhe prabhu,---anumāna kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anyonye]]---among themselves; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people in general; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouths; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that which; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---talk; [[synonyms:ś:śunila]]---was heard; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the home of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:a:anumāna]]---a guess; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made.

===== Translation =====

**"From the talk of the people in general, we have guessed that the Lord is now at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya.**
