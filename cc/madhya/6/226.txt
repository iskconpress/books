====== Cc. Madhya 6.226 ======

===== Text =====

> na deśa-niyamas tatra
> na kāla-niyamas tathā
> prāptam annaṁ drutaṁ śiṣṭair
> bhoktavyaṁ harir abravīt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:d:deśa]]---of the country; [[synonyms:n:niyamaḥ]]---regulation; [[synonyms:t:tatra]]---in that; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---of time; [[synonyms:n:niyamaḥ]]---regulation; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---so also; [[synonyms:p:prāptam]]---received; [[synonyms:a:annam]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:d:drutam]]---hastily; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣṭaiḥ]]---by gentlemen; [[synonyms:b:bhoktavyam]]---to be eaten; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:a:abravīt]]---has said.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'The prasāda of Lord Kṛṣṇa is to be eaten by gentlemen as soon as it is received; there should be no hesitation. There are no regulative principles concerning time and place. This is the order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.' "**

===== Purport =====

These verses are quoted from the //Padma Purāṇa.//
