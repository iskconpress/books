====== CC Madhya 6.31 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhaume jānāñā sabā nila abhyantare
> nityānanda-gosāñire teṅho kaila namaskāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:j:jānāñā]]---informing and taking permission; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---took; [[synonyms:a:abhyantare]]---within the house; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---unto Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered; [[synonyms:n:namaskāre]]---obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya permitted all the devotees to enter his house, and upon seeing Nityānanda Prabhu, the Bhaṭṭācārya offered Him obeisances.**
