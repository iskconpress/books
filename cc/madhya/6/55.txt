====== CC Madhya 6.55 ======

===== Text =====

> nadīyā-sambandhe sārvabhauma hṛṣṭa hailā
> prīta hañā gosāñire kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nadīyā]]-[[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---in connection with Nadīyā; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣṭa]]---pleased; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prīta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---thus being pleased; [[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu belonged to the Nadīyā district, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya became very pleased and addressed the Lord as follows.**
