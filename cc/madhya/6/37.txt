====== Cc. Madhya 6.37 ======

===== Text =====

> ucca kari' kare sabe nāma-saṅkīrtana
> tṛtīya prahare haila prabhura cetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ucca]]---very loudly; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---began; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:p:prahare]]---in the forenoon; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness.

===== Translation =====

**All of the devotees then began to loudly chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra. Just before noon the Lord regained His consciousness.**
