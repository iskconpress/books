====== CC Madhya 8.242 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' duṅhe nija-nija kārye gelā
> sandhyā-kāle rāya punaḥ āsiyā mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:n:nija]]---their own respective; [[synonyms:k:kārye]]---in the duties; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---departed; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming there; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**In this way they both departed to perform their respective duties. Then, in the evening, Rāmānanda Rāya returned to see Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
