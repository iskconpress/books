====== CC Madhya 8.290 ======

===== Text =====

> gupte rākhiha, kāhāṅ nā kario prakāśa
> āmāra bātula-ceṣṭā loke upahāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gupte]]---in secret; [[synonyms:r:rākhiha]]---keep; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---anywhere; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kario]]---make; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---exposure; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:b:bātula]]-[[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---activities like a madman; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---among the general people; [[synonyms:u:upahāsa]]---laughter.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord then requested Rāmānanda Rāya, "Keep all these talks a secret. Please do not expose them anywhere and everywhere. Since My activities appear to be like those of a madman, people may take them lightly and laugh."**
