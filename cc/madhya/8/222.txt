====== Cc. Madhya 8.222 ======

===== Text =====

> vraja-lokera kona bhāva lañā yei bhaje
> bhāva-yogya deha pāñā kṛṣṇa pāya vraje

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the planet known as Goloka Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---mood; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accepting; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---executes devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---suitable for that spiritual attraction; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---a body; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**"In his liberated stage the devotee is attracted by one of the five humors in the transcendental loving service of the Lord. As he continues to serve the Lord in that transcendental mood, he attains a spiritual body to serve Kṛṣṇa in Goloka Vṛndāvana.**
