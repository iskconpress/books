====== CC Madhya 8.149 ======

===== Text =====

> aparikalita-pūrvaḥ kaś camatkāra-kārī
> sphurati mama garīyān eṣa mādhurya-pūraḥ
> ayam aham api hanta prekṣya yaṁ lubdha-cetāḥ
> sa-rabhasam upabhoktuṁ kāmaye rādhikeva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparikalita]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrvaḥ]]---not previously experienced; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]-[[synonyms:k:kārī]]---causing wonder; [[synonyms:s:sphurati]]---manifests; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---My; [[synonyms:g:garīyān]]---more great; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]-[[synonyms:p:pūraḥ]]---abundance of sweetness; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:h:hanta]]---alas; [[synonyms:p:prekṣya]]---seeing; [[synonyms:y:yam]]---which; [[synonyms:l:lubdha]]-[[synonyms:c:cetāḥ]]---My mind being bewildered; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:r:rabhasam]]---impetuously; [[synonyms:u:upabhoktum]]---to enjoy; [[synonyms:k:kāmaye]]---desire; [[synonyms:r:rādhikā]] [[synonyms:i:iva]]---like Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Upon seeing His own reflection in a bejeweled pillar of His Dvārakā palace, Kṛṣṇa desired to embrace it, saying, "Alas, I have never seen such a person before. Who is He? Just by seeing Him I have become eager to embrace Him, exactly like Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī." ' "**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's //Lalita-mādhava// (8.34).
