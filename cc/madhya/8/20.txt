====== CC Madhya 8.20 ======

===== Text =====

> uṭhi' prabhu kahe,---ūṭha, kaha 'kṛṣṇa' 'kṛṣṇa'
> tāre āliṅgite prabhura hṛdaya satṛṣṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---rising; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:u:uṭha]]---get up; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---chant; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgite]]---to embrace; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---the heart; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇa]]---very eager.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord stood up and asked Rāmānanda Rāya to arise and chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa. Indeed, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very eager to embrace him.**
