====== Cc. Madhya 8.129 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sannyāsī' baliyā more nā kariha vañcana
> kṛṣṇa-rādhā-tattva kahi' pūrṇa kara mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---a person in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:b:baliyā]]---taking as; [[synonyms:m:more]]---Me; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do not do; [[synonyms:v:vañcana]]---cheating; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth about Rādhā-Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---describing; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---complete; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---make; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---my mind.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "Please do not try to cheat me, thinking of Me as a learned sannyāsī. Please satisfy My mind by just describing the truth of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa."**
