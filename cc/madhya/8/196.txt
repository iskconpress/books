====== CC Madhya 8.196 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---'sādhya-vastura avadhi' ei haya
> tomāra prasāde ihā jāniluṅ niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu confirmed; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]-[[synonyms:v:vastura]]---of the object of life; [[synonyms:a:avadhi']]---the limit; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of you; [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jāniluṅ]]---I have understood; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---conclusively.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu confirmed these verses recited by Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya, saying, "This is the limit of the goal of human life. Only by your mercy have I come to understand it conclusively.**
