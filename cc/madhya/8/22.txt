====== Cc. Madhya 8.22 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe tāre kaila prabhu dṛḍha āliṅgana
> premāveśe prabhu-bhṛtya doṅhe acetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---firm; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---the servant and the master; [[synonyms:d:doṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:a:acetana]]---unconscious.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya very firmly. Indeed, both the master and the servant almost lost consciousness due to ecstatic love.**
