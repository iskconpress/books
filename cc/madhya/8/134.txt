====== Cc. Madhya 8.134 ======

===== Text =====

> parama īśvara kṛṣṇa---svayaṁ bhagavān
> sarva-avatārī, sarva-kāraṇa-pradhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]]---supreme; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---controller; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:avatārī]]---the source of all incarnations; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the supreme cause of all causes.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya then began to speak on kṛṣṇa-tattva. "Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead," he said. "He is personally the original Godhead, the source of all incarnations and the cause of all causes.**
