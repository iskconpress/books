====== Cc. Madhya 8.228 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva gopī-bhāva kari aṅgīkāra
> rātri-dina cinte rādhā-kṛṣṇera vihāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the loving mood of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---making; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---acceptance; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---day and night; [[synonyms:c:cinte]]---one thinks; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vihāra]]---the pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**"Therefore one should accept the mood of the gopīs in their service. In such a transcendental mood, one should always think of the pastimes of Śrī Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa.**
