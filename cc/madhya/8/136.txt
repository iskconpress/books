====== CC Madhya 8.136 ======

===== Text =====

> sac-cid-ānanda-tanu, vrajendra-nandana
> sarvaiśvarya-sarvaśakti-sarvarasa-pūrṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:t:tanu]]---Kṛṣṇa's body is transcendental, full of knowledge, bliss and eternity; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Mahārāja Nanda; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---all opulences; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---all potencies; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---the reservoir of all transcendental mellows.

===== Translation =====

**"The transcendental body of Śrī Kṛṣṇa is eternal and full of bliss and knowledge. He is the son of Nanda Mahārāja. He is full of all opulences and potencies, as well as all spiritual mellows.**
