====== Cc. Madhya 8.173 ======

===== Text =====

> rāga-tāmbūla-rāge adhara ujjvala
> prema-kauṭilya---netra-yugale kajjala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāga]]---of love; [[synonyms:t:tāmbūla]]---of the betel nut; [[synonyms:r:rāge]]---by the reddish color; [[synonyms:a:adhara]]---lips; [[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---brilliant; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:k:kauṭilya]]---the double dealings in loving affairs; [[synonyms:n:netra]]-[[synonyms:y:yugale]]---on the two eyes; [[synonyms:k:kajjala]]---the ointment.

===== Translation =====

**"Her attachment for Kṛṣṇa is the reddish color of betel nuts on Her brilliant lips. Her double-dealings in loving affairs are just like the black ointment around Her eyes.**
