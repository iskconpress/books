====== CC Madhya 8.180 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇake karāya śyāma-rasa-madhu pāna
> nirantara pūrṇa kare kṛṣṇera sarva-kāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇake]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---She induces; [[synonyms:ś:śyāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of the mellow of conjugal love; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]---the honey; [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drinking; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---complete; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāma]]---all kinds of lusty desires.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī induces Kṛṣṇa to drink the honey of the conjugal relationship. She is therefore engaged in satisfying all the lusty desires of Kṛṣṇa.**
