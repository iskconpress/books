====== CC Madhya 8.119 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇera svarūpa' kaha 'rādhāra svarūpa'
> 'rasa' kon tattva, 'prema'---kon tattva-rūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---the transcendental features; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---speak; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---the transcendental features; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---mellows; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---that truth; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---actual form.

===== Translation =====

**"Kindly explain the transcendental features of Kṛṣṇa and Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī. Also explain the truth of transcendental mellows and the transcendental form of love of Godhead.**
