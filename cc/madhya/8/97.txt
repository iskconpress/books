====== Cc. Madhya 8.97 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---īhāra āge puche hena jane
> eta-dina nāhi jāni, āchaye bhuvane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya replied; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---beyond this point; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquires; [[synonyms:h:hena]]---such; [[synonyms:j:jane]]---a person; [[synonyms:e:eta]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---until this day; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I did not know; [[synonyms:ā:āchaye]]---there is; [[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---within this material world.

===== Translation =====

**Rāya Rāmānanda replied, "Until this day I did not know anyone within this material world who could inquire beyond this perfectional stage of devotional service.**
