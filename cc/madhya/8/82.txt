====== CC Madhya 8.82 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-prāptira upāya bahu-vidha haya
> kṛṣṇa-prāpti-tāratamya bahuta āchaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāptira]]---of achieving the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---means; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]]---various; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---of achieving the favor of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāratamya]]---comparisons; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---various; [[synonyms:ā:āchaya]]---there are.

===== Translation =====

**"There are various means and processes by which one may attain the favor of Lord Kṛṣṇa. All those transcendental processes will be studied from the viewpoint of comparative importance.**
