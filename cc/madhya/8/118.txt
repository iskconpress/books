====== CC Madhya 8.118 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe se jāniluṅ sādhya-sādhana-nirṇaya
> āge āra āche kichu, śunite mana haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:j:jāniluṅ]]---I have understood; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]---of the ultimate goal; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---and of the process; [[synonyms:n:nirṇaya]]---the ascertainment; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---more; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it is.

===== Translation =====

**"Now I have come to understand the sublime goal of life and the process of achieving it. Nevertheless, I think that there is something more ahead, and My mind is desiring to have it.**
