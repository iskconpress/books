====== CC Madhya 8.238 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---āilāṅa śuni' tomāra guṇa
> kṛṣṇa-kathā śuni, śuddha karāite mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:ā:āilāṅa]]---I have come; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---these topics about Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---I hear; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]] [[synonyms:k:karāite]]---just to make pure; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord replied, "Having heard about your good qualities, I have come here. I have come to hear about Kṛṣṇa from you and thus purify My mind.**
