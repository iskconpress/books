====== CC Madhya 8.41 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāra saṅge brāhmaṇādi sahasreka jana
> tomāra darśane sabāra dravī-bhūta mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with me; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---//brāhmaṇas// and others; [[synonyms:s:sahasreka]]---more than one thousand; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---persons; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in seeing; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:d:dravī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]---became melted; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the hearts.

===== Translation =====

**"Along with me there are about a thousand men-including the brāhmaṇas-and all of them appear to have had their hearts melted simply by seeing You.**
