====== CC Madhya 8.262 ======

===== Text =====

> iṣṭa-goṣṭhī kṛṣṇa-kathā kahi' kata-kṣaṇa
> prabhu-pada dhari' rāya kare nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---spiritual discussion; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---talking; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]---the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**That next evening, after discussing the topic of Kṛṣṇa for some time, Rāmānanda Rāya caught hold of the lotus feet of the Lord and spoke as follows.**
