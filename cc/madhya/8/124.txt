====== CC Madhya 8.124 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---māyāvādī āmi ta' sannyāsī
> bhakti-tattva nāhi jāni, māyāvāde bhāsi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:m:māyāvādī]]---a follower of the Māyāvāda philosophy; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---one in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truths of transcendental loving service; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I know; [[synonyms:m:māyāvāde]]---in the philosophy of impersonalism; [[synonyms:b:bhāsi]]---I float.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I am a Māyāvādī in the renounced order of life, and I do not even know what transcendental loving service to the Lord is. I simply float in the ocean of Māyāvāda philosophy.**
