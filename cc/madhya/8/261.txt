====== CC Madhya 8.261 ======

===== Text =====

> doṅhe nija-nija-kārye calilā vihāne
> sandhyā-kāle rāya āsi' mililā āra dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:doṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kārye]]---in their respective duties; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:v:vihāne]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming again; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---next; [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the day.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning they both departed to perform their respective duties, but in the evening Rāmānanda Rāya returned to meet the Lord again.**
