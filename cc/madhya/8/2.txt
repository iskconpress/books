====== Cc. Madhya 8.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaita-candra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jayajaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---all glories to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---all glories to the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Lord Nityānanda! All glories to Advaita Ācārya! And all glories to all the devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu!**
