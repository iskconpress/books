====== CC Madhya 8.86 ======

===== Text =====

> guṇādhikye svādādhikya bāḍe prati-rase
> śānta-dāsya-sakhya-vātsalyera guṇa madhurete vaise

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādhikye]]---by the increase of transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:s:svāda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādhikya]]---increase of taste; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---in each mellow; [[synonyms:ś:śānta]]---of neutrality; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]---of servitude; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]---of fraternity; [[synonyms:v:vātsalyera]]---and of paternal affection; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the qualities; [[synonyms:m:madhurete]]---in the conjugal mellow; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---appear.

===== Translation =====

**"As the qualities increase, so the taste also increases in each and every mellow. Therefore the qualities found in śānta-rasa, dāsya-rasa, sakhya-rasa and vātsalya-rasa are all manifest in conjugal love [mādhurya-rasa].**
