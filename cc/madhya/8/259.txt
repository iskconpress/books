====== CC Madhya 8.259 ======

===== Text =====

> abhāgiyā jñānī āsvādaye śuṣka jñāna
> kṛṣṇa-premāmṛta pāna kare bhāgyavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:abhāgiyā]]---unfortunate; [[synonyms:j:jñānī]]---the philosophical speculators; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādaye]]---taste; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]---dry; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---empiric knowledge; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the nectar of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drinking; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---the fortunate.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya concluded, "The unfortunate empiric philosophers taste the dry process of philosophical knowledge, whereas the devotees regularly drink the nectar of love of Kṛṣṇa. Therefore they are most fortunate of all."**
