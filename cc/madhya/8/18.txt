====== CC Madhya 8.18 ======

===== Text =====

> sūrya-śata-sama kānti, aruṇa vasana
> subalita prakāṇḍa deha, kamala-locana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sūrya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]]---hundreds of suns; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like; [[synonyms:k:kānti]]---luster; [[synonyms:a:aruṇa]]---saffron; [[synonyms:v:vasana]]---garments; [[synonyms:s:subalita]]---very strongly built; [[synonyms:p:prakāṇḍa]]---big; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---body; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:l:locana]]---eyes like lotus petals.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya then saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to be as brilliant as a hundred suns. The Lord was covered by a saffron garment. He was large in body and very strongly built, and His eyes were like lotus petals.**
