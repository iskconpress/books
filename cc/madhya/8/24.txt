====== CC Madhya 8.24 ======

===== Text =====

> stambha, sveda, aśru, kampa, pulaka, vaivarṇya
> duṅhāra mukhete śuni' gadgada 'kṛṣṇa' varṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:stambha]]---paralysis; [[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---shivering; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]---horripilation; [[synonyms:v:vaivarṇya]]---paleness; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:m:mukhete]]---in the mouth; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:g:gadgada]]---faltering; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:v:varṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa's name.

===== Translation =====

**When they embraced each other, ecstatic symptoms-paralysis, perspiration, tears, shivering, paleness and standing up of the bodily hairs-appeared. The word "Kṛṣṇa" came from their mouths falteringly.**
