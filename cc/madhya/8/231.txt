====== CC Madhya 8.231 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāte dṛṣṭānta---lakṣmī karila bhajana
> tathāpi nā pāila vraje vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭānta]]---the evidence; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---worship; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Mahārāja Nanda, Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"The unspoken example in this connection is the goddess of fortune, who worshiped Lord Kṛṣṇa in order to attain His pastimes in Vṛndāvana. But due to her opulent life-style, she could not attain the service of Kṛṣṇa in Vṛndāvana.**
