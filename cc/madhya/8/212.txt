====== Cc. Madhya 8.212 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi sakhīra kṛṣṇa-saṅgame nāhi mana
> tathāpi rādhikā yatne karāna saṅgama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:s:sakhīra]]---of the gopīs; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅgame]]---directly enjoying with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:y:yatne]]---with great endeavor; [[synonyms:k:karāna]]---causes; [[synonyms:s:saṅgama]]---association with Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Although the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's friends, do not desire to enjoy themselves directly with Kṛṣṇa, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī makes a great endeavor to induce Kṛṣṇa to enjoy Himself with the gopīs.**
