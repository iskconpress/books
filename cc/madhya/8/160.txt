====== CC Madhya 8.160 ======

===== Text =====

> premera parama-sāra 'mahābhāva' jāni
> sei mahābhāva-rūpā rādhā-ṭhākurāṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:premera]]---of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essential part; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the transcendental ecstasy of the name //mahābhāva//; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---we know; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpā]]---the personification of the //mahābhāva// transcendental ecstasy; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**"The essential part of love of Godhead is called mahābhāva, transcendental ecstasy, and that ecstasy is represented by Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
