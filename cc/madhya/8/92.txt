====== CC Madhya 8.92 ======

===== Text =====

> ei 'preme'ra anurūpa nā pāre bhajite
> ataeva 'ṛṇī' haya---kahe bhāgavate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of love of God; [[synonyms:a:anurūpa]]---exactly to the proportion; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is able; [[synonyms:b:bhajite]]---to reciprocate; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṇī]]---debtor; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---is stated; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.//

===== Translation =====

**"In Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam [10.32.22] it is said that Lord Kṛṣṇa cannot proportionately reciprocate devotional service in the mādhurya-rasa; therefore He always remains a debtor to such devotees.**
