====== CC Madhya 8.141 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā-bhaktera rasāmṛta nānā-vidha haya
> sei saba rasāmṛtera 'viṣaya' 'āśraya'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of various types of devotees; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the nectar of devotion or transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]]---different varieties; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛtera]]---of the nectar of devotion; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---subject; [[synonyms:ā:āśraya]]---object.

===== Translation =====

**"Each and every devotee has a certain type of transcendental mellow in relation to Kṛṣṇa. But in all transcendental relationships the devotee is the worshiper [āśraya], and Kṛṣṇa is the object of worship [viṣaya].**
