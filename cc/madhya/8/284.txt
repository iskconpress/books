====== CC Madhya 8.284 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu tāṅre hasta sparśi' karāilā cetana
> sannyāsīra veṣa dekhi' vismita haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:h:hasta]]---the hand; [[synonyms:s:sparśi']]---touching; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---made; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---conscious; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]]---of the //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:v:veṣa]]---the dress; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---struck with wonder; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāmānanda Rāya fell to the ground unconscious, Caitanya Mahāprabhu touched his hand, and he immediately regained consciousness. But when he saw Lord Caitanya in the dress of a sannyāsī, he was struck with wonder.**
