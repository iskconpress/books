====== CC Madhya 8.98 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāra madhye rādhāra prema---'sādhya-śiromaṇi'
> yāṅhāra mahimā sarva-śāstrete vākhāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among the loving affairs of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]] [[synonyms:p:prema]]---the love of Godhead of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---the topmost perfection; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glorification; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstrete]]---in every scripture; [[synonyms:v:vākhāni]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**"Among the loving affairs of the gopīs," Rāmānanda Rāya continued, "Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's love for Śrī Kṛṣṇa is topmost. Indeed, the glories of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī are highly esteemed in all revealed scriptures.**
