====== CC Madhya 8.133 ======

===== Text =====

> mora jihvā---vīṇā-yantra, tumi---vīṇā-dhārī
> tomāra mane yei uṭhe, tāhāi uccāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:j:jihvā]]---my tongue; [[synonyms:v:vīṇā]]-[[synonyms:y:yantra]]---a stringed instrument; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:v:vīnā]]-[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍhārī]]---the player of the stringed instrument; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in Your mind; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---whatever arises; [[synonyms:t:tāhāi]]---that; [[synonyms:u:uccāri]]---I vibrate.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, my tongue is just like a stringed instrument, and You are its player. Therefore I simply vibrate whatever arises in Your mind."**
