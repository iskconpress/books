====== CC Madhya 8.108 ======

===== Text =====

> ei dui-ślokera artha vicārile jāni
> vicārite uṭhe yena amṛtera khani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verses; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meanings; [[synonyms:v:vicārile]]---if considering; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I can understand; [[synonyms:v:vicārite]]---while considering; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---arises; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---like; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]]---of nectar; [[synonyms:k:khani]]---a mine.

===== Translation =====

**"Simply by considering these two verses one can understand what nectar there is in such dealings. It is exactly like freeing a mine of nectar.**
