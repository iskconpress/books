====== CC Madhya 8.10 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat 'vaiṣṇava' kari' sarva loka-gaṇe
> godāvarī-tīre prabhu āilā kata-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the people; [[synonyms:g:godāvarī]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank of the river Godāvarī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---after some days.

===== Translation =====

**As previously, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu converted to Vaiṣṇavism many people He met on the road. After some days, the Lord reached the banks of the River Godāvarī.**
