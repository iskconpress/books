====== CC Madhya 8.179 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nāma-guṇa-yaśa---avataṁsa kāṇe
> kṛṣṇa-nāma-guṇa-yaśa-pravāha-vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the qualities; [[synonyms:y:yaśa]]---the fame; [[synonyms:a:avataṁsa]]---ornaments; [[synonyms:k:kāṇe]]---on the ear; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the holy name; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---of the qualities; [[synonyms:y:yaśa]]---of the fame; [[synonyms:p:pravāha]]---waves; [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---in Her talking.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's earrings represent the name, fame and qualities of Lord Kṛṣṇa. The glories of Lord Kṛṣṇa's name, fame and qualities are always inundating Her speech.**
