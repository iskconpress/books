====== Cc. Madhya 8.122 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra śikṣāya paḍi yena śuka-pāṭha
> sākṣāt īśvara tumi, ke bujhe tomāra nāṭa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śikṣāya]]---by Your instruction; [[synonyms:p:paḍi]]---I recite; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---like; [[synonyms:ś:śuka]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṭha]]---the reciting of a parrot; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:b:bujhe]]---can understand; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nāṭa]]---dramatic performance.

===== Translation =====

**"I simply repeat like a parrot whatever instructions You have given me. You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself. Who can understand Your dramatic performances?**
