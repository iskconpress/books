====== Cc. Madhya 8.11 ======

===== Text =====

> godāvarī dekhi' ha-ila 'yamunā'-smaraṇa
> tīre vana dekhi' smṛti haila vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:godāvarī]]---the River Godāvarī; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]] [[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]---remembrance of the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the banks; [[synonyms:v:vana]]---the forests; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:smṛti]]---remembrance; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Śrī Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**When He saw the River Godāvarī, the Lord remembered the River Yamunā, and when He saw the forest on the banks of the river, He remembered Śrī Vṛndāvana-dhāma.**
