====== Cc. Madhya 8.309 ======

===== Text =====

> alaukika līlā ei parama nigūḍha
> viśvāse pāiye, tarke haya bahu-dūra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---most; [[synonyms:n:nigūḍha]]---confidential; [[synonyms:v:viśvāse]]---by faith; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---we can get; [[synonyms:t:tarke]]---by argument; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:d:dūra]]---far away.

===== Translation =====

**This part of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes is most confidential. One can derive benefit quickly only by faith; otherwise by arguing one will always remain far away.**
