====== Cc. Madhya 8.277 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā-kṛṣṇe tomāra mahā-prema haya
> yāhāṅ tāhāṅ rādhā-kṛṣṇa tomāre sphuraya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---great love; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---anywhere and everywhere; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa and Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---unto you; [[synonyms:s:sphuraya]]---appear.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "My dear Rāya, you are an advanced devotee and are always filled with ecstatic love for Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa. Therefore whatever you see-anywhere and everywhere-simply awakens your Kṛṣṇa consciousness."**
