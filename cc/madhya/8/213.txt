====== CC Madhya 8.213 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā-cchale kṛṣṇe preri' saṅgama karāya
> ātma-kṛṣṇa-saṅga haite koṭi-sukha pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:c:chale]]---under different pleas; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:preri']]---sending; [[synonyms:s:saṅgama]]---direct association; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---induces; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---personal association with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---ten million times more happiness; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---She gets.

===== Translation =====

**"Presenting various pleas for the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī sometimes sends the gopīs to Kṛṣṇa just to enable them to associate with Him directly. At such times, She enjoys a happiness ten million times greater than that enjoyed through direct association.**
