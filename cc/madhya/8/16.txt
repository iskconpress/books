====== CC Madhya 8.16 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu tāṅre dekhi' jānila---ei rāma-rāya
> tāṅhāre milite prabhura mana uṭhi' dhāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---could understand; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---him; [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---rising; [[synonyms:d:dhāya]]---runs after.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu could understand that the person who had come to bathe in the river was Rāmānanda Rāya. The Lord wanted so much to meet him that His mind immediately began running after him.**
