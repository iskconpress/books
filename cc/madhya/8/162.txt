====== CC Madhya 8.162 ======

===== Text =====

> premera 'svarūpa-deha'---prema-vibhāvita
> kṛṣṇera preyasī-śreṣṭhā jagate vidita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:premera]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:d:deha]]---actual body; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---by love of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vibhāvita]]---influence; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:preyasī]]---of the dear friends; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭhā]]---topmost; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---throughout the whole world; [[synonyms:v:vidita]]---known.

===== Translation =====

**"The body of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is a veritable transformation of love of Godhead; She is the dearmost friend of Kṛṣṇa, and this is known throughout the world.**
