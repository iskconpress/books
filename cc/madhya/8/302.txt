====== CC Madhya 8.302 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda hailā prabhura virahe vihvala
> prabhura dhyāne rahe viṣaya chāḍiyā sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:dhyāne]]---in meditation on Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---worldly business; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāmānanda Rāya began to feel separation from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he was overwhelmed. Meditating on the Lord, he gave up all his material business.**
