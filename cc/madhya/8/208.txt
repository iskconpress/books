====== CC Madhya 8.208 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa saha rādhikāra līlā ye karāya
> nija-sukha haite tāte koṭi sukha pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rādhikāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---they bring about; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---personal happiness; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---ten million times; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---the happiness; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---they derive.

===== Translation =====

**"The happiness of the gopīs increases ten million times when they serve to engage Śrī Śrī Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa in Their transcendental pastimes.**
