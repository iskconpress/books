====== CC Madhya 8.311 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda rāye mora koṭī namaskāra
> yāṅra mukhe kaila prabhu rasera vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāye]]---unto Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:k:koṭī]]---ten million; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in whose mouth; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rasera]] [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---the expansion of transcendental mellows.

===== Translation =====

**I offer ten million obeisances unto the lotus feet of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya because from his mouth much spiritual information has been expanded by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
