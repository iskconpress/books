====== CC Madhya 8.26 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' sannyāsīra teja dekhi brahma-sama
> śūdre āliṅgiyā kene karena krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---this indeed; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]]---of the //sannyāsī,// Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:teja]]---bodily effulgence; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---we see; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---exactly like Brahman; [[synonyms:ś:śūdre]]---a //śūdra,// or worker; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**These brāhmaṇas began to think, "We can see that this sannyāsī has a luster like the effulgence of Brahman, but how is it He is crying upon embracing a śūdra, a member of the fourth caste in the social order?"**
