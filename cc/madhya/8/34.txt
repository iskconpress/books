====== CC Madhya 8.34 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhaume tomāra kṛpā,---tāra ei cihna
> aspṛśya sparśile hañā tāṅra premādhīna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhaume]]---unto Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of such mercy; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]---the symptom; [[synonyms:a:aspṛśya]]---untouchable; [[synonyms:s:sparśile]]---You have touched; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:a:adhīna]]---influenced by love.

===== Translation =====

**"I can see that You have bestowed special mercy upon Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya. Therefore You have touched me, although I am untouchable. This is due only to his love for You.**
