====== Cc. Madhya 8.273 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-bhāgavata dekhe sthāvara-jaṅgama
> tāhāṅ tāhāṅ haya tāṅra śrī-kṛṣṇa-sphuraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---a first-class advanced devotee; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:s:sthāvara]]-[[synonyms:j:jaṅgama]]---the movable and inert; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---here and there; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sphuraṇa]]---manifestation of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"A devotee advanced on the spiritual platform sees everything movable and inert as the Supreme Lord. For him, everything he sees here and there is but a manifestation of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
