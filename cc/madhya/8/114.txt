====== CC Madhya 8.114 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhā vinu rāsa-līlā nāhi bhāya citte
> maṇḍalī chāḍiyā gelā rādhā anveṣite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]] [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---without Her; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---illuminates; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍalī]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---leaving the circle of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:a:anveṣite]]---to search for.

===== Translation =====

**"The rāsa dance does not shine in the heart of Kṛṣṇa without Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī. Therefore, He also gave up the circle of the rāsa dance and went out to search for Her.**
