====== CC Madhya 8.13 ======

===== Text =====

> ghāṭa chāḍi' kata-dūre jala-sannidhāne
> vasi' prabhu kare kṛṣṇa-nāma-saṅkīrtane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghāṭa]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving the bathing place; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:d:dūre]]---a short distance away; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:s:sannidhāne]]---near the water; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---chanting of the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing in the river, the Lord walked a little distance from the bathing place and engaged in chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.**
