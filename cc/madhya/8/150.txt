====== CC Madhya 8.150 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' saṅkṣepe kahila kṛṣṇera svarūpa
> ebe saṅkṣepe kahi śuna rādhā-tattva-rūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---I have said; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---the original form; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in summary; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the actual position.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya then said, "I have thus briefly explained the original form of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Now let me describe the position of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
