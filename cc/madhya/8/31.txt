====== Cc. Madhya 8.31 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā milibāre mora ethā āgamana
> bhāla haila, anāyāse pāiluṅ daraśana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]---you; [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---to meet; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---coming; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it was very good; [[synonyms:a:anāyāse]]---without difficulty; [[synonyms:p:pāiluṅ]]---I have gotten; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---interview.

===== Translation =====

**"Indeed, I have come here just to meet you. It is very good that even without making an effort I have gotten your interview here."**
