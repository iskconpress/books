====== CC Madhya 8.21 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi puchila,---tumi rāya rāmānanda?
> teṅho kahe,---sei haṅa dāsa śūdra manda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---He inquired; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he replied; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:h:haṅa]]---I am that; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servant; [[synonyms:ś:śūdra]]---belonging to the //śūdra// community; [[synonyms:m:manda]]---very low.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then inquired whether he was Rāmānanda Rāya, and he replied, "Yes, I am Your very low servant, and I belong to the śūdra community."**
