====== CC Madhya 8.165 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mahābhāva-cintāmaṇi' rādhāra svarūpa
> lalitādi sakhī---tāṅra kāya-vyūha-rūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---of the topmost spiritual ecstasy; [[synonyms:c:cintā]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇi]]---the touchstone; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]] [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---the transcendental form of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:l:lalitā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]---the //gopī// associates of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:v:vyūha]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---expansions of Her spiritual body.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is the topmost spiritual gem, and the other gopīs-Lalitā, Viśākhā and so on-are expansions of Her spiritual body.**
