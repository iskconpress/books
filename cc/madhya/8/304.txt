====== CC Madhya 8.304 ======

===== Text =====

> sahaje caitanya-caritra---ghana-dugdha-pūra
> rāmānanda-caritra tāhe khaṇḍa pracura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahaje]]---generally; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---the activities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:ghana]]-[[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:p:pūra]]---like condensed milk; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---the story of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---in that; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---sugar candy; [[synonyms:p:pracura]]---a large quantity.

===== Translation =====

**The activities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are like condensed milk, and the activities of Rāmānanda Rāya are like large quantities of sugar candy.**
