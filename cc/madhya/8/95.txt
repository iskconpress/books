====== Cc. Madhya 8.95 ======

===== Text =====

> tatrātiśuśubhe tābhir
> bhagavān devakī-sutaḥ
> madhye maṇīnāṁ haimānāṁ
> mahā-marakato yathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tatra]]---there; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:ś:śuśubhe]]---was very beautiful; [[synonyms:t:tābhiḥ]]---by them; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:devakī]]-[[synonyms:s:sutaḥ]]---son of Devakī; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the midst; [[synonyms:m:maṇīnām]]---of valuable jewels; [[synonyms:h:haimānām]]---lined with gold; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:marakataḥ]]---the jewel of the name //marakata//; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Although the son of Devakī, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, is also the reservoir of all kinds of beauty, when He is among the gopīs He nonetheless becomes more beautiful, for He resembles a mārakata jewel surrounded by gold and other jewels.' "**
