====== CC Madhya 8.154 ======

===== Text =====

> sac-cid-ānanda-maya kṛṣṇera svarūpa
> ataeva svarūpa-śakti haya tina rūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---eternal bliss and knowledge; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---the real transcendental form; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---His spiritual personal potency; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---three forms.

===== Translation =====

**"Originally Lord Kṛṣṇa is sac-cid-ānanda-vigraha, the transcendental form of eternity, bliss and knowledge; therefore His personal potency, the internal potency, has three different forms.**
