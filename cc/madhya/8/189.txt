====== CC Madhya 8.189 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-dina kuñje krīḍā kare rādhā-saṅge
> kaiśora vayasa saphala kaila krīḍā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---day and night; [[synonyms:k:kuñje]]---in the gardens or bushes of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kaiśora]]---the pre-youthful; [[synonyms:v:vayasa]]---age; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---fruitful; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---taking pleasure in different pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**"Day and night Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa enjoys the company of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī in the bushes of Vṛndāvana. Thus His pre-youthful age is fulfilled through His affairs with Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
