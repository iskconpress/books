====== CC Madhya 8.296 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina rāya-pāśe vidāya māgilā
> vidāyera kāle tāṅre ei ājñā dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---before Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---begged farewell; [[synonyms:v:vidāyera]] [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time of departure; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave.

===== Translation =====

**The next day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu begged Rāmānanda Rāya to give Him permission to leave, and at the time of farewell the Lord gave him the following orders.**
