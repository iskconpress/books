====== CC Madhya 8.53 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi viccheda doṅhāra sahana nā yāya
> tathāpi daṇḍavat kari' calilā rāma-rāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:v:viccheda]]---separation; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāra]]---of both of them; [[synonyms:s:sahana]]---toleration; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---possible; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---departed; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Although neither could tolerate the other's separation, Rāmānanda Rāya nonetheless offered his obeisances to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and departed.**
