====== CC Madhya 8.104 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---tabe śuna premera mahimā
> tri-jagate rādhā-premera nāhika upamā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya replied; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of that love; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:j:jagate]]---within the three worlds; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:p:premera]]---of the loving affairs of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is not; [[synonyms:u:upamā]]---comparison.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya continued, "Please therefore hear from me about the glories of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's loving affairs. They are beyond compare within these three worlds.**
