====== CC Madhya 8.198 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---yei kahāo, sei kahi vāṇī
> ki kahiye bhāla-manda, kichui nā jāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāyakahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya replied; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:kahāo]]---You make me speak; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---message; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I am speaking; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:m:manda]]---good or bad; [[synonyms:k:kichui]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I do not know anything.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya replied, "I do not know what I am saying, but You have made me speak what I have spoken, be it good or bad. I am simply repeating that message.**
