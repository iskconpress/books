====== CC Madhya 8.110 ======

===== Text =====

> sādhāraṇa-preme dekhi sarvatra 'samatā'
> rādhāra kuṭila-preme ha-ila 'vāmatā'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sādhāraṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in general love of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---we see; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:s:samatā]]---equality; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kuṭila]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in the crooked love of Godhead; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vāmatā]]---opposition.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa is equal to everyone in His general dealings, but due to the conflicting ecstatic love of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, there were opposing elements.**
