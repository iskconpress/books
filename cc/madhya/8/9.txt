====== Cc. Madhya 8.9 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhāte uṭhiyā prabhu calilā premāveśe
> dig-vidik nāhi jñāna rātri-divase

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhāte]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]]---rising; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstatic love; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:v:vidik]]---the right or wrong direction; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was not; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:divase]]---day and night.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning, in the great ecstasy of love, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu started on His tour with no knowledge of the proper direction, and He continued the whole day and night.**
