====== CC Madhya 8.14 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle dolāya caḍi' rāmānanda rāya
> snāna karibāre āilā, bājanā bājāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:d:dolāya]] [[synonyms:c:caḍi']]---riding on a palanquin; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bath; [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to take; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came there; [[synonyms:b:bājanā]] [[synonyms:b:bājāya]]---accompanied by a musical band.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, accompanied by the sounds of music, Rāmānanda Rāya came there on a palanquin to take his bath.**
