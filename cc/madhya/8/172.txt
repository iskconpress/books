====== Cc. Madhya 8.172 ======

===== Text =====

> pracchanna-māna vāmya---dhammilla-vinyāsa
> 'dhīrādhīrātmaka' guṇa---aṅge paṭa-vāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pracchanna]]---covered; [[synonyms:m:māna]]---anger; [[synonyms:v:vāmya]]---craftiness; [[synonyms:d:dhammilla]]---of the bunches of hair; [[synonyms:v:vinyāsa]]---arrangement; [[synonyms:d:dhīra]]-[[synonyms:a:adhīra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmaka]]---consisting of anger due to jealousy, which anger is sometimes expressed and sometimes suppressed; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the quality; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:p:paṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---silk covering.

===== Translation =====

**"Craftiness and covered anger constitute the arrangement of Her hair. The quality of anger due to jealousy is just like the silk garment covering Her body.**
