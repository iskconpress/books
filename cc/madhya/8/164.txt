====== CC Madhya 8.164 ======

===== Text =====

> sei mahābhāva haya 'cintāmaṇi-sāra'
> kṛṣṇa-vāñchā pūrṇa kare ei kārya tāṅra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---supreme ecstasy; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:c:cintāmaṇi]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of spiritual life; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---all the desires of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---fulfills; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---business; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---Her.

===== Translation =====

**"That supreme ecstasy of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is the essence of spiritual life. Her only business is to fulfill all the desires of Kṛṣṇa.**
