====== CC Madhya 8.35 ======

===== Text =====

> kāhāṅ tumi---sākṣāt īśvara nārāyaṇa
> kāhāṅ muñi---rāja-sevī viṣayī śūdrādhama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---whereas; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---whereas; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:s:sevī]]---government servant; [[synonyms:v:viṣayī]]---materialist; [[synonyms:ś:śūdra]]-[[synonyms:a:adhama]]---worse than a //śūdra,// or fourth-class man.

===== Translation =====

**"You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyaṇa Himself, and I am only a government servant interested in materialistic activities. Indeed, I am the lowest amongst men of the fourth caste.**
