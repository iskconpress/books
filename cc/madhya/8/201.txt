====== Cc. Madhya 8.201 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā-kṛṣṇera līlā ei ati gūḍhatara
> dāsya-vātsalyādi-bhāve nā haya gocara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this is; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very much; [[synonyms:g:gūḍhatara]]---more confidential; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]---of servitude; [[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and of paternal love, etc.; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in the moods; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is not; [[synonyms:g:gocara]]---appreciated.

===== Translation =====

**"The pastimes of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa are very confidential. They cannot be understood through the mellows of servitude, fraternity or paternal affection.**
