====== Cc. Madhya 8.283 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' rāmānanda hailā ānande mūrcchite
> dharite nā pāre deha, paḍilā bhūmite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing this form; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---there was; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchite]]---fainting; [[synonyms:d:dharite]]---to hold him; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---able; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---the body; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground.

===== Translation =====

**Upon seeing this form, Rāmānanda Rāya lost consciousness in transcendental bliss. Unable to remain standing, he fell to the ground.**
