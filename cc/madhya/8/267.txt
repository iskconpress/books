====== Cc. Madhya 8.267 ======

===== Text =====

> eka saṁśaya mora āchaye hṛdaye
> kṛpā kari' kaha more tāhāra niścaye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]]---one doubt; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:ā:āchaye]]---there is; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please say; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:n:niścaye]]---the ascertainment.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya then said that he had but one doubt within his heart, and he petitioned the Lord, "Please be merciful upon me and just remove my doubt."**
