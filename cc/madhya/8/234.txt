====== CC Madhya 8.234 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata premāveśe rātri goṅāilā
> prātaḥ-kāle nija-nija-kārye duṅhe gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love of Godhead; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---the night; [[synonyms:g:goṅāilā]]---passed; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kārye]]---to their own respective duties; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---departed.

===== Translation =====

**The entire night was passed in this way, in ecstatic love of Godhead. In the morning they both departed to tend to their respective duties.**
