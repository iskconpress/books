====== Cc. Madhya 8.33 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra kṛpāya pāinu tomāra daraśana
> āji saphala haila mora manuṣya-janama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by his mercy; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I have gotten; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---interview; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---successful; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---has become; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]-[[synonyms:j:janama]]---birth as a human being.

===== Translation =====

**"By his mercy I have received Your interview here. Consequently I consider that today I have become a successful human being.**
