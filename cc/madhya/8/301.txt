====== CC Madhya 8.301 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vidyāpūre' nānā-mata loka vaise yata
> prabhu-darśane 'vaiṣṇava' haila chāḍi' nija-mata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidyāpūre]]---in the town of Vidyānagara; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---various opinions; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---reside; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees of Lord Viṣṇu; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---own opinions.

===== Translation =====

**All the residents of Vidyānagara were of different faiths, but after seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they abandoned their own faiths and became Vaiṣṇavas.**
