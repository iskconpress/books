====== CC Madhya 8.281 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane āile more karite uddhāra
> ebe kapaṭa kara,---tomāra kona vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:ā:āile]]---You have come; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to make; [[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---deliverance; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---//now//; [[synonyms:k:kapaṭa]]---duplicity; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You do; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---what; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---behavior.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, by Your causeless mercy You have appeared before me to grant me liberation. Now You are playing in a duplicitous way. What is the reason for this behavior?"**
