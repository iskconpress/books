====== CC Madhya 8.272 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---kṛṣṇe tomāra gāḍha-prema haya
> premāra svabhāva ei jāniha niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---deep love; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:premāra]]---of such transcendental love; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---the nature; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jāniha]]---please know; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---certainly.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "You have a deep love for Kṛṣṇa, and one who has such deep ecstatic love for the Lord naturally sees things in such a way. Please take this from Me to be certain.**
