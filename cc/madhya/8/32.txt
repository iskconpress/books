====== Cc. Madhya 8.32 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---sārvabhauma kare bhṛtya-jñāna
> parokṣeha mora hite haya sāvadhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya replied; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---thinks of me as his servant; [[synonyms:p:parokṣeha]]---in my absence; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---of me; [[synonyms:h:hite]]---for the benefit; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:s:sāvadhāna]]---always careful.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya replied, "Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya thinks of me as his servant. Even in my absence he is very careful to do me good.**
