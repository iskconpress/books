====== Cc. Madhya 8.186 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---jāniluṅ kṛṣṇa-rādhā-prema-tattva
> śunite cāhiye duṅhāra vilāsa-mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya replied; [[synonyms:j:jāniluṅ]]---now I have understood; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---of the loving affairs; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:c:cāhiye]]---I desire; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both of Them; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]-[[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---the greatness of the enjoyment.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Now I have come to understand the truth of the loving affairs between Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa. Nonetheless, I still want to hear how both of Them gloriously enjoy such love."**
