====== CC Madhya 8.170 ======

===== Text =====

> saundarya---kuṅkuma, sakhī-praṇaya---candana
> smita-kānti---karpūra, tine---aṅge vilepana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---Her personal beauty; [[synonyms:k:kuṅkuma]]---a red powder known as //kuṅkuma//; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:p:praṇaya]]---Her love for Her associates; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---the sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:s:smita]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---the sweetness of Her smile; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---camphor; [[synonyms:t:tine]]---by these three things; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:v:vilepana]]---smearing.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's personal beauty is compared to the reddish powder known as kuṅkuma. Her affection for Her associates is compared to sandalwood pulp, and the sweetness of Her smile is compared to camphor. All these, combined together, are smeared over Her body.**
