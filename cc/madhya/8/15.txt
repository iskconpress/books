====== Cc. Madhya 8.15 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra saṅge bahu āilā vaidika brāhmaṇa
> vidhi-mate kaila teṅho snānādi-tarpaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:v:vaidika]]---following the Vedic principles; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---//brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---according to ritualistic ceremonies; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he, Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:t:tarpaṇa]]---bathing and offering oblations, etc.

===== Translation =====

**Many brāhmaṇas, following the Vedic principles, accompanied Rāmānanda Rāya. According to the Vedic rituals, Rāmānanda Rāya took his bath and offered oblations to his forefathers.**
