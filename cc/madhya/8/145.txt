====== CC Madhya 8.145 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣmī-kāntādi avatārera hare mana
> lakṣmī-ādi nārī-gaṇera kare ākarṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:k:kānta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---goddess of fortune's husband (Nārāyaṇa); [[synonyms:a:avatārera]]---of the incarnation; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---He enchants; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---the goddess of fortune; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of all women; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---attraction.

===== Translation =====

**"He also attracts Nārāyaṇa, who is the incarnation of Saṅkarṣaṇa and the husband of the goddess of fortune. He attracts not only Nārāyaṇa but also all women, headed by the goddess of fortune, the consort of Nārāyaṇa.**
