====== CC Madhya 8.109 ======

===== Text =====

> śata-koṭi gopī-saṅge rāsa-vilāsa
> tāra madhye eka-mūrtye rahe rādhā-pāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---hundreds of thousands; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---dancing in the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among them; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrtye]]---by one of His transcendental forms; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---by the side of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**"Although Kṛṣṇa was in the midst of hundreds of thousands of gopīs during the rāsa dance, He still kept Himself in one of His transcendental forms by the side of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
