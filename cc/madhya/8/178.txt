====== CC Madhya 8.178 ======

===== Text =====

> nijāṅga-saurabhālaye garva-paryaṅka
> tā'te vasi' āche, sadā cinte kṛṣṇa-saṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---Her personal body; [[synonyms:s:saurabha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaye]]---in the abode of aroma; [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:p:paryaṅka]]---bedstead; [[synonyms:t:tā'te]]---on that; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---lying; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:c:cinte]]---thinks; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---the association of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's bedstead is pride itself, and it is situated in the abode of Her bodily aroma. She is always seated there thinking of Kṛṣṇa's association.**
