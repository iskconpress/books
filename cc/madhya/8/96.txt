====== CC Madhya 8.96 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---ei 'sādhyāvadhi' suniścaya
> kṛpā kari' kaha, yadi āge kichu haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sādhya]]-[[synonyms:a:avadhi]]---the highest limit of perfection; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful to Me; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please speak; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---further; [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is something.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "This is certainly the limit of perfection, but please be merciful to Me and speak more if there is more."**
