====== Cc. Madhya 8.12 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vane kata-kṣaṇa kari' nṛtya-gāna
> godāvarī pāra hañā tāhāṅ kaila snāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vane]]---in that forest; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---performing; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:g:gāna]]---dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:g:godāvarī]]---the river; [[synonyms:p:pāra]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---crossing; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---took; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bath.

===== Translation =====

**After performing His usual chanting and dancing for some time in this forest, the Lord crossed the river and took His bath on the other bank.**
