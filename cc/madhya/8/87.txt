====== Cc. Madhya 8.87 ======

===== Text =====

> ākāśādira guṇa yena para-para bhūte
> dui-tina krame bāḍe pañca pṛthivīte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākāśa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādira]]---of the sky, air and so on; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the qualities; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---just as; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:p:para]]---one after another; [[synonyms:b:bhūte]]---in the material elements; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:t:tina]]---two and then three; [[synonyms:k:krame]]---by gradations; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increase; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]---all five; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīte]]---in earth.

===== Translation =====

**"The qualities in the material elements-sky, air, fire, water and earth-increase one after another by a gradual process of one, two and three, and at the last stage, in the element earth, all five qualities are completely visible.**
