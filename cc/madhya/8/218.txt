====== Cc. Madhya 8.218 ======

===== Text =====

> nijendriya-sukha-vāñchā nāhi gopikāra
> kṛṣṇe sukha dite kare saṅgama-vihāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---for personal sense gratification; [[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---the desire; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:g:gopikāra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:s:saṅgama]]-[[synonyms:v:vihāra]]---mingling and enjoying with Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Among the gopīs, there is not a pinch of desire for sense gratification. Their only desire is to give pleasure to Kṛṣṇa, and this is why they mingle with Him and enjoy with Him.**
