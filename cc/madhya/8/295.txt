====== Cc. Madhya 8.295 ======

===== Text =====

> krame uṭhāite seha uttama vastu pāya
> aiche praśnottara kaila prabhu-rāmarāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:krame]]---gradually; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāite]]---to raise; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that person; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---best; [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---metal; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---//gets//; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---so also; [[synonyms:p:praśna]]-[[synonyms:u:uttara]]---the questions and answers; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---have done; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---and Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya worked like miners, excavating all kinds of valuable metals, each one better than the other. Their questions and answers are exactly like that.**
