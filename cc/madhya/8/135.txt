====== Cc. Madhya 8.135 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta vaikuṇṭha, āra ananta avatāra
> ananta brahmāṇḍa ihāṅ,---sabāra ādhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---innumerable Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---innumerable incarnations; [[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---innumerable universes; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---in this material world; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:ā:ādhāra]]---the resting place.

===== Translation =====

**"There are innumerable Vaikuṇṭha planets as well as innumerable incarnations. In the material world also there are innumerable universes, and Kṛṣṇa is the supreme resting place for all of them.**
