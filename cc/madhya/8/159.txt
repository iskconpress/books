====== Cc. Madhya 8.159 ======

===== Text =====

> hlādinīra sāra aṁśa, tāra 'prema' nāma
> ānanda-cinmaya-rasa premera ākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hlādinīra]]---of this pleasure potency; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essential; [[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---part; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---its; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of God; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---full of pleasure; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the platform of spiritual mellows; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāna]]---the explanation.

===== Translation =====

**"The most essential part of this pleasure potency is love of Godhead [prema]. Consequently, the explanation of love of Godhead is also a transcendental mellow full of pleasure.**
