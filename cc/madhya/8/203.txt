====== CC Madhya 8.203 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhī vinā ei līlā puṣṭa nāhi haya
> sakhī līlā vistāriyā, sakhī āsvādaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhī]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---these pastimes; [[synonyms:p:puṣṭa]]---nourished; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are never; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes; [[synonyms:v:vistāriyā]]---expanding; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādaya]]---taste this mellow.

===== Translation =====

**"Without the gopīs, these pastimes between Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa cannot be nourished. Only by their cooperation are such pastimes broadcast. It is their business to taste the mellows.**
