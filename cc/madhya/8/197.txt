====== CC Madhya 8.197 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sādhya-vastu' 'sādhana' vinu keha nāhi pāya
> kṛpā kari' kaha, rāya, pābāra upāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sādhya]]-[[synonyms:v:vastu]]---the goal of life; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]] [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---without practicing the process; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---no one achieves; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---very mercifully; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please explain; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---My dear Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:pābāra]] [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---the means of achieving.

===== Translation =====

**"The goal of life cannot be achieved unless one practices the process. Now, being merciful upon Me, please explain that means by which this goal can be attained."**
