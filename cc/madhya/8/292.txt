====== CC Madhya 8.292 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-rūpa daśa-rātri rāmānanda-saṅge
> sukhe goṅāilā prabhu kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:r:rātri]]---ten nights; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:g:goṅāilā]]---passed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in transcendental pleasure by discussing talks of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**For ten nights Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya spent a happy time discussing the pastimes of Kṛṣṇa.**
