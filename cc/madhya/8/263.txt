====== CC Madhya 8.263 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa-tattva', 'rādhā-tattva', 'prema-tattva-sāra'
> 'rasa-tattva' 'līlā-tattva' vividha prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth about Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth about Rādha; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of Their loving affairs; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth about transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth about the pastimes of the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vividha]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---of different varieties.

===== Translation =====

**"There is transcendental variety in talks about Kṛṣṇa and Rādhārāṇī and Their transcendental loving affairs, humors and pastimes."**
