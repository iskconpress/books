====== CC Madhya 8.305 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā-kṛṣṇa-līlā---tāte karpūra-milana
> bhāgyavān yei, sei kare āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Śrī Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that composition; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---the camphor; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---mixture; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---fortunate; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---one who; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**Their meeting is exactly like a mixture of condensed milk and sugar candy. When they talk of the pastimes of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa, camphor is added. One who tastes this combined preparation is most fortunate.**
