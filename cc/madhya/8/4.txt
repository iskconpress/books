====== CC Madhya 8.4 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛsiṁha dekhiyā kaila daṇḍavat-praṇati
> premāveśe kaila bahu nṛtya-gīta-stuti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---by seeing Lord Nṛsiṁha in the temple; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]-[[synonyms:p:praṇati]]---offering of obeisances, falling flat before the Deity; [[synonyms:p:premāveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---all kinds of; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---chanting; [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---and offering of prayers.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the Deity of Lord Nṛsiṁha in the temple, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His respectful obeisances by falling flat. Then, in ecstatic love, He performed various dances, chanted, and offered prayers.**
