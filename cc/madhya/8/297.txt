====== CC Madhya 8.297 ======

===== Text =====

> viṣaya chāḍiyā tumi yāha nīlācale
> āmi tīrtha kari' tāṅhā āsiba alpa-kāle

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---material engagement; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---giving up; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---go; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---finishing My touring and pilgrimage; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āsiba]]---shall return; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---very soon.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told him, "Give up all material engagements and come to Jagannātha Purī. I will return there very soon after finishing My tour and pilgrimage.**
