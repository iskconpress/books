====== CC Madhya 8.235 ======

===== Text =====

> vidāya-samaye prabhura caraṇe dhariyā
> rāmānanda rāya kahe vinati kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidāya]]-[[synonyms:s:samaye]]---at the point of departure; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---capturing; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:v:vinati]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great humility.

===== Translation =====

**Before departing from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Rāmānanda Rāya fell to the ground and caught hold of the Lord's lotus feet. He then spoke submissively as follows.**
