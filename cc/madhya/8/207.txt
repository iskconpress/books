====== Cc. Madhya 8.207 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhīra svabhāva eka akathya-kathana
> kṛṣṇa-saha nija-līlāya nāhi sakhīra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhīra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---natural inclination; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:a:akathya]]---inexplicable; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---narration; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---in His personal pastimes; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:s:sakhīra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**"There is an inexplicable fact about the natural inclinations of the gopīs. The gopīs never want to enjoy themselves with Kṛṣṇa personally.**
