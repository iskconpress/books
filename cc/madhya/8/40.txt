====== Cc. Madhya 8.40 ======

===== Text =====

> mahad-vicalanaṁ nṛṇāṁ
> gṛhiṇāṁ dīna-cetasām
> niḥśreyasāya bhagavan
> nānyathā kalpate kvacit

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahat]]-[[synonyms:v:vicalanam]]---the wandering of saintly persons; [[synonyms:n:nṛṇām]]---of human beings; [[synonyms:g:gṛhiṇām]]---who are householders; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]-[[synonyms:c:cetasām]]---low-minded; [[synonyms:n:niḥśreyasāya]]---for the ultimate benefit; [[synonyms:b:bhagavan]]---O my Lord; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:anyathā]]---not any other purpose; [[synonyms:k:kalpate]]---one imagines; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]]---at any time.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'My dear Lord, sometimes great saintly persons go to the homes of householders, although these householders are generally low-minded. When a saintly person visits their homes, one can understand that it is for no other purpose than to benefit the householders.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.8.4).
