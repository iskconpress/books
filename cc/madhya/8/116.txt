====== CC Madhya 8.116 ======

===== Text =====

> śata-koṭi-gopīte nahe kāma-nirvāpaṇa
> tāhātei anumāni śrī-rādhikāra guṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---hundreds and thousands; [[synonyms:g:gopīte]]---in the midst of //gopīs//; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there is not; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nirvāpaṇa]]---satisfaction of lust; [[synonyms:t:tāhātei]]---by that way; [[synonyms:a:anumāni]]---we can imagine; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rādhikāra]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the transcendental quality of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**"Since Kṛṣṇa's lusty desires were not satisfied even in the midst of hundreds of thousands of gopīs and He was thus searching after Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, we can easily imagine how transcendentally qualified She is."**
