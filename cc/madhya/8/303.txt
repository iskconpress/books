====== Cc. Madhya 8.303 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahiluṅ rāmānandera milana
> vistāri' varṇite nāre sahasra-vadana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting with Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---expanding; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---to describe; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---not able; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---Lord Śeṣa Nāga, who has thousands of hoods.

===== Translation =====

**I have briefly described the meeting between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya. No one can actually describe this meeting exhaustively. It is even impossible for Lord Śeṣa Nāga, who has thousands of hoods.**
