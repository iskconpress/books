====== Cc. Madhya 8.19 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhiyā tāṅhāra mane haila camatkāra
> āsiyā karila daṇḍavat namaskāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonder; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming there; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---like a rod; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāmānanda Rāya saw the wonderful sannyāsī, he was struck with wonder. He went to Him and immediately offered his respectful obeisances, falling down flat like a rod.**
