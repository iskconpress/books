====== Cc. Madhya 8.29 ======

===== Text =====

> sustha hañā duṅhe sei sthānete vasilā
> tabe hāsi' mahāprabhu kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:stha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming steady; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:sthānete]]---in the place; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**When they regained their sanity, they both sat down, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled and began to speak as follows.**
