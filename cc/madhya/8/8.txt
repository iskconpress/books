====== CC Madhya 8.8 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat kona vipre kaila nimantraṇa
> sei rātri tāhāṅ rahi' karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---//brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---that night; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---touring.

===== Translation =====

**As usual, a brāhmaṇa offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu an invitation. The Lord passed the night in the temple and then commenced His tour again.**
