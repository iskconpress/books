====== CC Madhya 8.233 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' prabhu tāṅre kaila āliṅgana
> dui jane galāgali karena krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing so much; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---both of them; [[synonyms:g:galāgali]]---embracing shoulder to shoulder; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---did; [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Rāmānanda Rāya, and both of them, embracing shoulder to shoulder, began to cry.**
