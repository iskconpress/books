====== Cc. Madhya 8.101 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe---āge kaha, śunite pāi sukhe
> apūrvāmṛta-nadī vahe tomāra mukhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---ahead; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please speak; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---I get; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---happiness; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---of unprecedented nectar; [[synonyms:n:nadī]]---a river; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---flows; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from your mouth.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Please speak on. I am very happy to hear you because a river of unprecedented nectar is flowing from your mouth.**
