====== CC Madhya 8.176 ======

===== Text =====

> saubhāgya-tilaka cāru-lalāṭe ujjvala
> prema-vaicittya---ratna, hṛdaya---tarala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saubhāgya]]-[[synonyms:t:tilaka]]---the //tilaka// of good fortune; [[synonyms:c:cāru]]---beautiful; [[synonyms:l:lalāṭe]]---on the forehead; [[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---brilliant; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vaicittya]]---diversity; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]---the jewel; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---the heart; [[synonyms:t:tarala]]---the locket.

===== Translation =====

**"The tilaka of good fortune is on Her beautiful broad forehead. Her various loving affairs are a gem, and Her heart is the locket.**
