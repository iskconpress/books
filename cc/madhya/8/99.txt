====== Cc. Madhya 8.99 ======

===== Text =====

> yathā rādhā priyā viṣṇos
> tasyāḥ kuṇḍaṁ priyaṁ tathā
> sarva-gopīṣu saivaikā
> viṣṇor atyanta-vallabhā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]]---just as; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:p:priyā]]---very dear; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]]---Her; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍam]]---bathing place; [[synonyms:p:priyam]]---very dear; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---so also; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:g:gopīṣu]]---among all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:s:sā]]---She; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:e:ekā]]---alone; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇoḥ]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]-[[synonyms:v:vallabhā]]---very dear.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Just as Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is most dear to Śrī Kṛṣṇa, Her bathing place known [Rādhā-kuṇḍa] is also dear to Him. Among all the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is supermost and very dear to Lord Kṛṣṇa.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is from the //Padma Purāṇa// and is included in the //Laghu-bhāgavatāmṛta// (2.1.45), by Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī. It also appears in //Ādi-līlā,// Chapter Four, verse 215, and again in //Madhya-līlā,// Chapter Eighteen, verse 8.
