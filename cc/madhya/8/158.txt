====== Cc. Madhya 8.158 ======

===== Text =====

> sukha-rūpa kṛṣṇa kare sukha āsvādana
> bhakta-gaṇe sukha dite 'hlādinī'---kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---embodiment of pleasure; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:h:hlādinī]]---the pleasure potency; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa tastes all kinds of transcendental happiness, although He Himself is happiness personified. The pleasure relished by His pure devotees is also manifest by His pleasure potency.**
