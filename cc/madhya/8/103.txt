====== CC Madhya 8.103 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā lāgi' gopīre yadi sākṣāt kare tyāga
> tabe jāni,---rādhāya kṛṣṇera gāḍha-anurāga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the sake of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:g:gopīre]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tyāga]]---rejection; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---we can understand; [[synonyms:r:rādhāya]]---in Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]---intense; [[synonyms:a:anurāga]]---affection.

===== Translation =====

**"If Lord Kṛṣṇa rejected the company of the other gopīs for Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, we can understand that Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa has intense affection for Her."**
