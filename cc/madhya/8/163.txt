====== CC Madhya 8.163 ======

===== Text =====

> ānanda-cinmaya-rasa-pratibhāvitābhis
> tābhir ya eva nija-rūpatayā kalābhiḥ
> goloka eva nivasty akhilātma-bhūto
> govindam ādi-puruṣaṁ tam ahaṁ bhajāmi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---bliss; [[synonyms:c:cit]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:m:maya]]---consisting of; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---mellows; [[synonyms:p:prati]]---every second; [[synonyms:b:bhāvitābhiḥ]]---who are engrossed with; [[synonyms:t:tābhiḥ]]---with those; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpatayā]]---with His own form; [[synonyms:k:kalābhiḥ]]---who are parts of portions of His pleasure potency; [[synonyms:g:goloke]]---in Goloka Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:nivasti]]---resides; [[synonyms:a:akhila]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātma]]---as the soul of all; [[synonyms:b:bhūtaḥ]]---who exists; [[synonyms:g:govindam]]---Lord Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣam]]---the original personality; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---Him; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhajāmi]]---worship.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'I worship Govinda, the primeval Lord, who resides in His own realm, Goloka, with Rādhā, who resembles His own spiritual figure and who embodies the ecstatic potency [hlādinī]. Their companions are Her confidantes, who embody extensions of Her bodily form and who are imbued and permeated with ever-blissful spiritual rasa.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Brahma-saṁhitā// (5.37).
