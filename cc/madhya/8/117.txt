====== Cc. Madhya 8.117 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe---ye lāgi' āilāma tomā-sthāne
> sei saba tattva-vastu haila mora jñāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the matter of which; [[synonyms:ā:āilāma]]---I have come; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to your place; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all those; [[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:v:vastu]]---objects of truth; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---were; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---in knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to Rāmānanda Rāya, "That for which I have come to your residence has now become an object of truth in My knowledge.**
