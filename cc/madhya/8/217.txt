====== CC Madhya 8.217 ======

===== Text =====

> nijendriya-sukha-hetu kāmera tātparya
> kṛṣṇa-sukha-tātparya gopī-bhāva-varya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]---of one's own senses; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---of the happiness; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for the reason; [[synonyms:k:kāmera]]---of lusty desire; [[synonyms:t:tātparya]]---intention; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---the happiness; [[synonyms:t:tātparya]]---intention; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:v:varya]]---the foremost mood of the //gopīs.//

===== Translation =====

**"Lusty desires are experienced when one is concerned with his own personal sense gratification. The mood of the gopīs is not like that. Their only desire is to satisfy the senses of Kṛṣṇa.**
