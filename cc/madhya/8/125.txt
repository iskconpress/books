====== Cc. Madhya 8.125 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma-saṅge mora mana nirmala ha-ila
> 'kṛṣṇa-bhakti-tattva kaha,' tāṅhāre puchila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the company of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:n:nirmala]]---clarified; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truths of transcendental loving service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please explain; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---to him; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---I inquired.

===== Translation =====

**"Due to the association of Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, My mind has been enlightened. Therefore I have asked Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya about the truths of transcendental loving service to Kṛṣṇa.**
