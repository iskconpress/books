====== CC Madhya 8.151 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera ananta-śakti, tāte tina---pradhāna
> 'cic-chakti', 'māyā-śakti', 'jīva-śakti'-nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---unlimited potencies; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---chief; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---spiritual potency; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---material potency; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---marginal potency, or living entities; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa has unlimited potencies, which can be divided into three main parts. These are the spiritual potency, the material potency and the marginal potency, which is known as the living entities.**
