====== Cc. Madhya 8.270 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāte prakaṭa dekhoṅ sa-vaṁśī vadana
> nānā bhāve cañcala tāhe kamala-nayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---manifested; [[synonyms:d:dekhoṅ]]---I see; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśī]]---with the flute; [[synonyms:v:vadana]]---the face; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in various modes; [[synonyms:c:cañcala]]---restless; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---in that; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:n:nayana]]---the lotus eyes.

===== Translation =====

**"I see that You are holding a flute to Your mouth, and Your lotus eyes are moving very restlessly due to various ecstasies.**
