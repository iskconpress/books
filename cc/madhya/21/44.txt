====== CC Madhya 21.44 ======

===== Text =====

> madhuraiśvarya-mādhurya-kṛpādi-bhāṇḍāra
> yogamāyā dāsī yāhāṅ rāsādi līlā-sāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---of sweetness and opulence; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---of conjugal love; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and of mercy and so on; [[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍāra]]---storehouse; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:m:māyā]]---the spiritual energy; [[synonyms:d:dāsī]]---maidservant; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the //rāsa// dance and other pastimes; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the quintessence of all pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**"Vṛndāvana is the storehouse of Kṛṣṇa's mercy and the sweet opulences of conjugal love. That is where the spiritual energy, working as a maidservant, exhibits the rāsa dance, the quintessence of all pastimes.**
