====== CC Madhya 21.10 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata kṛṣṇera divya sad-guṇa ananta
> brahmā-śiva-sanakādi nā pāya yāṅra anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:divya]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---spiritual qualities; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:s:sanaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the four Kumāras and so on; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---obtain; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of which; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**"The spiritual qualities of Kṛṣṇa are also unlimited. Great personalities like Lord Brahmā, Lord Śiva and the four Kumāras cannot estimate the spiritual qualities of the Lord.**
