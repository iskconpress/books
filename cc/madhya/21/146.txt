====== CC Madhya 21.146 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi ta' bāula, āna kahite āna kahi
> kṛṣṇera mādhuryāmṛta-srote yāi vahi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:b:bāula]]---I am a madman; [[synonyms:ā:āna]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to speak something; [[synonyms:ā:āna]] [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak on something else; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---of the nectar of the sweetness; [[synonyms:s:srote]]---in the waves of; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---I go; [[synonyms:v:vahi']]---being carried away.

===== Translation =====

**"Since I have become a madman, I am saying one thing instead of another. This is because I am being carried away by the waves of the nectarean ocean of Lord Kṛṣṇa's transcendental sweetness."**
