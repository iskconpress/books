====== Cc. Madhya 21.61 ======

===== Text =====

> vismita hañā brahmā dvārīke kahilā
> 'kaha giyā sanaka-pitā caturmukha āilā'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vismita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming surprised; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:d:dvārīke]]---unto the doorman; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---replied; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---inform; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:s:sanaka]]-[[synonyms:p:pitā]]---the father of the four Kumāras; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---four-headed; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---has come.

===== Translation =====

**"When the doorman asked, 'Which Brahmā?' Lord Brahmā was surprised. He told the doorman, 'Please go inform Lord Kṛṣṇa that I am the four-headed Brahmā who is the father of the four Kumāras.'**
