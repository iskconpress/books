====== CC Madhya 21.147 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu kṣaṇeka mauna kari' rahe
> mane eka kari' punaḥ sanātane kahe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeka]]---for a moment; [[synonyms:m:mauna]]---silence; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within His mind; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---adjusting things; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---instructs.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then remained silent for a moment. Finally, adjusting things within His mind, He again spoke to Sanātana Gosvāmī.**
