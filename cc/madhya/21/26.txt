====== CC Madhya 21.26 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ye tomāra ananta vaibhavāmṛta-sindhu
> mora vāṅ-mano-gamya nahe eka bindu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all this; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:v:vaibhava]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---ocean of the nectar of Your opulence; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:v:vāk]]-[[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:g:gamya]]---within the reach of words and mind; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:bindu]]---even a drop.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'My Lord, Your opulence is like an unlimited ocean of nectar, and it is verbally and mentally impossible for me to realize even a drop of that ocean.**
