====== CC Madhya 21.88 ======

===== Text =====

> tasyāḥ pāre paravyoma
> tripād-bhūtaṁ sanātanam
> amṛtaṁ śāśvataṁ nityam
> anantaṁ paramaṁ padam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasyāḥ]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---on the other bank of the Virajā River; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyoma]]---the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:p:pād]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūtam]]---existing as three fourths of the opulence of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:s:sanātanam]]---eternal; [[synonyms:a:amṛtam]]---without deterioration; [[synonyms:ś:śāśvatam]]---without being subjected to the control of time; [[synonyms:n:nityam]]---constantly existing; [[synonyms:a:anantam]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:p:paramam]]---supreme; [[synonyms:p:padam]]---abode.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Beyond the river Virajā is a spiritual nature, which is indestructible, eternal, inexhaustible and unlimited. It is the supreme abode consisting of three fourths of the Lord's opulences. It is known as paravyoma, the spiritual sky.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from the //Padma Purāṇa,// recited here by Lord Kṛṣṇa.
