====== CC Madhya 21.42 ======

===== Text =====

> ei artha---madhyama, śuna 'gūḍha' artha āra
> tina āvāsa-sthāna kṛṣṇera śāstre khyāti yāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---this explanation; [[synonyms:m:madhyama]]---middle; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]---confidential; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:ā:āvāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---residential places; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:k:khyāti]]---fame; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which.

===== Translation =====

**"This is the middle meaning. Now please hear the confidential meaning. Lord Kṛṣṇa has three places of residence, which are well known from revealed scriptures.**

===== Purport =====

Kṛṣṇa has three abodes-His internal abode (Goloka Vṛndāvana), His intermediate abode (the spiritual sky), and His external abode (this material world).
