====== Cc. Madhya 21.19 ======

===== Text =====

> "kṛṣṇa-vatsair asaṅkhyātaiḥ"---śukadeva-vāṇī
> kṛṣṇa-saṅge kata gopa---saṅkhyā nāhi jāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsaiḥ]] [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhyātaiḥ]]---Kṛṣṇa was accompanied by an unlimited number of calves and cowherd boys; [[synonyms:ś:śukadeva]]-[[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---the words of Śukadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:g:gopa]]---how many cowherd boys; [[synonyms:s:saṅkhyā]]---the count; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---we do not know.

===== Translation =====

**"According to Śukadeva Gosvāmī, Kṛṣṇa had unlimited cows and cowherd boys with Him. No one could count their actual number.**
