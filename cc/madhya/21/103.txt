====== CC Madhya 21.103 ======

===== Text =====

> yogamāyā cic-chakti, viśuddha-sattva-pariṇati,
> tāra śakti loke dekhāite
> ei rūpa-ratana, bhakta-gaṇera gūḍha-dhana,
> prakaṭa kailā nitya-līlā haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:m:māyā]]---internal energy; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---spiritual potency; [[synonyms:v:viśuddha]]-[[synonyms:s:sattva]]---of transcendental pure goodness; [[synonyms:p:pariṇati]]---a transformation; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---the potency of such energy; [[synonyms:l:loke]] [[synonyms:d:dekhāite]]---to exhibit within the material world; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:ratana]]---this beautiful, transcendental, jewellike form; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]] [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the most confidential treasure of the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---exhibition; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the eternal pastimes of the Lord; [[synonyms:r:ratana]]---this beautiful, transcendental, jewellike form; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]] [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the most confidential treasure of the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]]---exhibition; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the eternal pastimes of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**"The transcendental form of Kṛṣṇa is shown to the world by Lord Kṛṣṇa's internal spiritual energy, which is a transformation of pure goodness. This jewellike form is the most confidential treasure of the devotees. This form is manifest from Kṛṣṇa's eternal pastimes.**
