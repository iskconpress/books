====== Cc. Madhya 21.71 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera acintya-śakti lakhite keha nāre
> yata brahmā, tata mūrti eka-i śarīre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:acintya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---inconceivable potencies; [[synonyms:l:lakhite]]---to observe; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---not able; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---all Brahmās; [[synonyms:t:tata]] [[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---so many forms; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:i:i]] [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---in the same body.

===== Translation =====

**"No one can estimate the inconceivable potency of Kṛṣṇa. All the Brahmās who were there were resting in the one body of Kṛṣṇa.**
