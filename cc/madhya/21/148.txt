====== CC Madhya 21.148 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera mādhurī āra mahāprabhura mukhe
> ihā yei śune, sei bhāse prema-sukhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mādhurī]]---the sweetness; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this statement; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---floats; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in the transcendental bliss of love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**I now summarize these teachings of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. If anyone gets an opportunity to hear about the sweetness of Kṛṣṇa in this chapter of Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta, he will certainly be eligible to float in the transcendentally blissful ocean of love of God.**
