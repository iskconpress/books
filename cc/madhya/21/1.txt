====== CC Madhya 21.1 ======

===== Text =====

> agaty-eka-gatiṁ natvā
> hīnārthādhika-sādhakam
> śrī-caitanyaṁ likhāmy asya
> mādhuryaiśvarya-śīkaram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:agati]]-[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:g:gatim]]---to the only shelter for the conditioned souls who do not know the goal of life; [[synonyms:n:natvā]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:h:hīna]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---of the necessities of the conditioned souls, who are poor in spiritual knowledge; [[synonyms:a:adhika]]---increase; [[synonyms:s:sādhakam]]---bringing about; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanyam]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:likhāmi]]---I am writing; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of Him; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---of the sweetness and opulence; [[synonyms:ś:śīkaram]]---a small portion.

===== Translation =====

**Offering my obeisances unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, let me describe a particle of His opulence and sweetness. He is most valuable for a fallen conditioned soul bereft of spiritual knowledge, and He is the only shelter for those who do not know the real goal of life.**
