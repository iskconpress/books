====== Cc. Madhya 21.3 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva svarūpera dhāma---paravyoma-dhāme
> pṛthak pṛthak vaikuṇṭha saba, nāhika gaṇane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]]---of the personal forms; [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---abode; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyoma]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāme]]---in the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separate; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇane]]---there is no counting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "All the transcendental forms of the Lord are situated in the spiritual sky. They preside over spiritual planets in that abode, but there is no counting those Vaikuṇṭha planets.**
