====== Cc. Madhya 21.52 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra tale 'bāhyāvāsa' virajāra pāra
> ananta brahmāṇḍa yāhāṅ koṭhari apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:t:tale]]---below the spiritual world; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:ā:āvāsa]]---external abode; [[synonyms:v:virajāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---on the other side of the river Virajā; [[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---unlimited number of universes; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:k:koṭhari]]---apartments; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited.

===== Translation =====

**"On the other side of the river Virajā is the external abode, which is full of unlimited universes, each containing unlimited atmospheres.**
