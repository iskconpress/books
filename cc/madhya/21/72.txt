====== CC Madhya 21.72 ======

===== Text =====

> pāda-pīṭa-mukuṭāgra-saṅghaṭṭe uṭhe dhvani
> pāda-pīṭhe stuti kare mukuṭa hena jāni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:pīṭa]]---at Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet; [[synonyms:m:mukuṭa]]-[[synonyms:a:agra]]---of the tops of the helmets; [[synonyms:s:saṅghaṭṭe]]---in the crowding together; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]] [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---there arose a sound; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:pīṭhe]] [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---offering prayers unto the lotus feet; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:m:mukuṭa]]---the helmets; [[synonyms:h:hena]] [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---appearing as such.

===== Translation =====

**"When all the helmets struck together at Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet, there was a tumultuous sound. It appeared that the helmets themselves were offering prayers unto Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet.**
