====== CC Madhya 21.86 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmāṇḍānurūpa brahmāra śarīra-vadana
> ei-rūpe pāli āmi brahmāṇḍera gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:a:anurūpa]]---according to the size of a universe; [[synonyms:b:brahmāra]]---of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---heads on the body; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:pāli]] [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I maintain; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍera]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the innumerable groups of universes.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'According to the size of the universe, there are so many heads on the body of Brahmā. In this way I maintain innumerable universes [brahmāṇḍas].**
