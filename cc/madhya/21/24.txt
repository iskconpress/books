====== Cc. Madhya 21.24 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā dekhi' brahmā hailā mohita, vismita
> stuti kari' ei pāche karilā niścita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing this; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mohita]]---astonished; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---struck with wonder; [[synonyms:s:stuti]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering prayers; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at the end; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:n:niścita]]---conclusion.

===== Translation =====

**"When the Lord Brahmā from this universe saw this pastime, he was astonished and struck with wonder. After offering his prayers, he gave the following conclusion.**
