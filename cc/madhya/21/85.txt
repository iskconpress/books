====== CC Madhya 21.85 ======

===== Text =====

> kona brahmāṇḍa śata-koṭi, kona lakṣa-koṭi
> kona niyuta-koṭi, kona koṭi-koṭi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---some universes; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---one billion //yojanas//; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]-[[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---one trillion //yojanas//; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:n:niyuta]]-[[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---ten trillion; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:k:koṭi]]---one hundred trillion.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Some of the universes are one billion yojanas, some one trillion, some ten trillion and some one hundred trillion yojanas. Thus they are almost unlimited in area.**

===== Purport =====

A //yojana// equals eight miles.
