====== Cc. Madhya 21.48 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta vaikuṇṭha yāhāṅ bhāṇḍāra-koṭhari
> pāriṣada-gaṇe ṣaḍ-aiśvarye āche bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍāra]]-[[synonyms:k:koṭhari]]---like rooms of a treasure-house; [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---eternal associates; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarye]]---with the six opulences; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---are; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling.

===== Translation =====

**"Innumerable Vaikuṇṭha planets, which are just like different rooms of a treasure-house, are all there, filled with all opulences. Those unlimited planets house the Lord's eternal associates, who are also enriched with the six opulences."**
