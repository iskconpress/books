====== CC Madhya 21.23 ======

===== Text =====

> eka kṛṣṇa-deha haite sabāra prakāśe
> kṣaṇeke sabāi sei śarīre praveśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:deha]]---transcendental body of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---the manifestation; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeke]]---in a second; [[synonyms:s:sabāi]]---every one of Them; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---in that body of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:praveśe]]---enter.

===== Translation =====

**"All these transcendental bodies emanated from the body of Kṛṣṇa, and within a second They all entered again into His body.**
