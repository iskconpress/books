====== Cc. Madhya 21.89 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe kṛṣṇa brahmāre dilena vidāya
> kṛṣṇera vibhūti-svarūpa jānāna nā yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:brahmāre]]---unto the Lord Brahmā of this universe; [[synonyms:d:dilena]] [[synonyms:v:vidāya]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vibhūti]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---spiritual verification of opulence; [[synonyms:j:jānāna]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is not possible to understand.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way Lord Kṛṣṇa bade farewell to the four-headed Brahmā of this universe. We may thus understand that no one can calculate the extent of Kṛṣṇa's energies.**
