====== CC Madhya 21.47 ======

===== Text =====

> 'madhyama-āvāsa' kṛṣṇera---ṣaḍ-aiśvarya-bhāṇḍāra
> ananta svarūpe yāhāṅ karena vihāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyama]]-[[synonyms:ā:āvāsa]]---the middle residence; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍāra]]---the storehouse of six opulences; [[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in unlimited forms; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:v:vihāra]]---enjoys His pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**"The spiritual sky, which is full in all six opulences, is the interim residence of Lord Kṛṣṇa. It is there that an unlimited number of forms of Kṛṣṇa enjoy Their pastimes.**
