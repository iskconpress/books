====== CC Madhya 21.5 ======

===== Text =====

> saba vaikuṇṭha---vyāpaka, ānanda-cinmaya
> pāriṣada-ṣaḍaiśvarya-pūrṇa saba haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---the spiritual planets; [[synonyms:v:vyāpaka]]---vast; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---made of spiritual bliss; [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]---associates; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---six kinds of opulence; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---in full; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are.

===== Translation =====

**"Each Vaikuṇṭha planet is very large, and each is made of spiritual bliss. The inhabitants are all associates of the Supreme Lord, and they have full opulence like the Lord Himself. Thus they are all situated.**
