====== CC Madhya 21.65 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kon brahmā?' puchile tumi kon abhiprāye?
> āmā ba-i jagate āra kon brahmā haye?'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---which Brahmā; [[synonyms:p:puchile]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You inquired; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:a:abhiprāye]]---by what intention; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:b:ba]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---except me; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---within this universe; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---which; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:h:haye]]---is there.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Why did you inquire which Brahmā had come to see You? What is the purpose of such an inquiry? Is there any other Brahmā besides me within this universe?'**
