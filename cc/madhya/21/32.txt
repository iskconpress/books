====== Cc. Madhya 21.32 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgavatera ei śloka paḍilā āpane
> artha āsvādite sukhe karena vyākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavatera]]---of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---recited; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādite]]---to taste; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---describes the meaning.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally recited the following verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, and to relish the meaning, He began to explain it Himself.**
