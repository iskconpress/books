====== CC Madhya 21.87 ======

===== Text =====

> 'eka-pāda vibhūti' ihāra nāhi parimāṇa
> 'tripāda vibhūti'ra kebā kare parimāṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]] [[synonyms:v:vibhūti]]---a one-fourth manifestation of My opulence; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:p:parimāṇa]]---measurement; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]] [[synonyms:v:vibhūtira]]---of the spiritual world, having three fourths of My energy; [[synonyms:k:kebā]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---can do; [[synonyms:p:parimāṇa]]---measurement.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'No one can measure the length and breadth of one fourth of My energy. Who can measure the three fourths that is manifested in the spiritual world?'**
