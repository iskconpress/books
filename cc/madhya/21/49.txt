====== CC Madhya 21.49 ======

===== Text =====

> goloka-nāmni nija-dhāmni tale ca tasya
> devī-maheśa-hari-dhāmasu teṣu teṣu
> te te prabhāva-nicayā vihitāś ca yena
> govindam ādi-puruṣaṁ tam ahaṁ bhajāmi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:goloka]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmni]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāmni]]---in the planet known as Goloka Vṛndāvana, the personal abode of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tale]]---in the part underneath; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tasya]]---of that; [[synonyms:d:devī]]---of the goddess Durgā; [[synonyms:m:maheśa]]---of Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:h:hari]]---of Nārāyaṇa; [[synonyms:d:dhāmasu]]---in the planets; [[synonyms:t:teṣu]] [[synonyms:t:teṣu]]---in each of them; [[synonyms:t:te]] [[synonyms:t:te]]---those respective; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]-[[synonyms:n:nicayāḥ]]---opulences; [[synonyms:v:vihitāḥ]]---established; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:g:govindam]]---unto that Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣam]]---the original Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhajāmi]]---offer my obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Below the planet named Goloka Vṛndāvana are the planets known as Devī-dhāma, Maheśa-dhāma and Hari-dhāma. These are opulent in different ways. They are managed by the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Govinda, the original Lord. I offer my obeisances unto Him.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Brahma-saṁhitā// (5.43).
