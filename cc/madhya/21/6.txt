====== CC Madhya 21.6 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta vaikuṇṭha eka eka deśe yāra
> sei paravyoma-dhāmera ke karu vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]] [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---unlimited Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]---certain; [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---in a place; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyoma]]---of the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:d:dhāmera]]---of the abode; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:k:karu]] [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---who can understand the breadth.

===== Translation =====

**"Since all the Vaikuṇṭha planets are located in a certain corner of the spiritual sky, who can measure the spiritual sky?**
