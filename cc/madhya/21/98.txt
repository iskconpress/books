====== Cc. Madhya 21.98 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera aiśvarya---apāra amṛtera sindhu
> avagāhite nāri, tāra chuilaṅ eka bindu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---the opulence of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---an ocean of nectar; [[synonyms:a:avagāhite]]---to bathe; [[synonyms:n:nāri]]---I am unable; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:c:chuilaṅ]]---I touched; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:bindu]]---only one drop.

===== Translation =====

**"The unlimited potencies of Kṛṣṇa are just like an ocean of nectar. Since one cannot bathe within that ocean, I have only touched a drop of it."**
