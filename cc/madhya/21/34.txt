====== CC Madhya 21.34 ======

===== Text =====

> parama īśvara kṛṣṇa svayaṁ bhagavān
> tāte baḍa, tāṅra sama keha nāhi āna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]]---supreme; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---controller; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the original Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---most exalted; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---else.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa is the original Supreme Personality of Godhead; therefore He is the greatest of all. No one is equal to Him, nor is anyone greater than Him.**
