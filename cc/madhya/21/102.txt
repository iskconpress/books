====== CC Madhya 21.102 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera madhura rūpa, śuna, sanātana
> ye rūpera eka kaṇa, ḍubāya saba tribhuvana,
> sarva prāṇī kare ākarṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---form; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---O My dear Sanātana; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---of which form; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:kaṇa]]---even a fraction; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubāya]]---floods; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---the three worlds; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇī]]---all living entities; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---attracting.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Sanātana, the sweet, attractive transcendental form of Kṛṣṇa is so nice. Just try to understand it. Even a fractional understanding of Kṛṣṇa's beauty can merge all three worlds in the ocean of love. He attracts all the living entities within the three worlds.**
