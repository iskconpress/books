====== CC Madhya 21.57 ======

===== Text =====

> tripāda-vibhūti kṛṣṇera---vākya-agocara
> eka-pāda vibhūtira śunaha vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhūti]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---three fourths of the energy of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]-[[synonyms:a:agocara]]---beyond words; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]] [[synonyms:v:vibhūtira]]---of one fourth of the energy; [[synonyms:ś:śunaha]]---please hear; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---breadth.

===== Translation =====

**"The three-fourths part of Lord Kṛṣṇa's energy is beyond our speaking power. Let us therefore hear elaborately about the remaining one fourth of His energy.**
