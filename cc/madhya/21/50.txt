====== CC Madhya 21.50 ======

===== Text =====

> pradhāna-parama-vyomnor
> antare virajā nadī
> vedāṅga-sveda-janitais
> toyaiḥ prasrāvitā śubhā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]-[[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:v:vyomnoḥ]] [[synonyms:a:antare]]---between the material world and spiritual world; [[synonyms:v:virajā]] [[synonyms:n:nadī]]---is a river known as Virajā; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---of the transcendental body of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sveda]]-[[synonyms:j:janitaiḥ]]---produced from the perspiration; [[synonyms:t:toyaiḥ]]---with water; [[synonyms:p:prasrāvitā]]---flowing; [[synonyms:ś:śubhā]]---all-auspicious.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Between the spiritual and material worlds is a body of water known as the river Virajā. This water is generated from the bodily perspiration of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is known as Vedāṅga. Thus the river flows.'**
