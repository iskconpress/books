====== Cc. Madhya 21.97 ======

===== Text =====

> sei svārājya-lakṣmī kare nitya pūrṇa kāma
> ataeva vede kahe 'svayaṁ bhagavān'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:svārājya]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]---that personal opulence; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---//does//; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---eternally; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---fulfilling; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---all desires; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:vede]]---in the Vedas; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---it is said; [[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"Because Be possesses the spiritual potencies which fulfill all His desires, Kṛṣṇa is accepted as the Supreme Personality of Godhead. This is the Vedic version.**
