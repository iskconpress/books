====== CC Madhya 21.17 ======

===== Text =====

> prākṛtāprākṛta sṛṣṭi kailā eka-kṣaṇe
> aśeṣa-vaikuṇṭhājāṇḍa svasvanātha-sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prākṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:aprākṛta]]---material and spiritual; [[synonyms:s:sṛṣṭi]]---creation; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---in one moment; [[synonyms:a:aśeṣa]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭha]]---Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:a:aja]]-[[synonyms:a:aṇḍa]]---material planets; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with their own predominating deities.

===== Translation =====

**"At Vṛndāvana, the Lord immediately created all material and spiritual planets in one moment. Indeed, all of them were created with their predominating deities.**
