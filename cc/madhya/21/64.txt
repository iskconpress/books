====== Cc. Madhya 21.64 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmā kahe,---'tāhā pāche kariba nivedana
> eka saṁśaya mane haya, karaha chedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Brahmā said; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:k:kariba]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---I shall submit unto You; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]]---doubt; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:c:chedana]]---kindly dissipate it.

===== Translation =====

**"Being questioned, Lord Brahmā immediately replied, 'I shall later tell You why I have come. First of all there is a doubt in my mind which I wish You would kindly dissipate.**
