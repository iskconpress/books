====== CC Madhya 21.80 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe kṛṣṇa sarva-brahmā-gaṇe vidāya dilā
> daṇḍavat hañā sabe nija ghare gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:brahmā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto all the Brahmās; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned to their respective homes.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa then bade farewell to all the Brahmās there, and after offering their obeisances, they all returned to their respective homes.**
