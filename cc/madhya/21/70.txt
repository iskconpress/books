====== Cc. Madhya 21.70 ======

===== Text =====

> āsi' saba brahmā kṛṣṇa-pāda-pīṭha-āge
> daṇḍavat karite mukuṭa pāda-pīṭhe lāge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---all the Brahmās; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:pīṭha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---offering their obeisances; [[synonyms:m:mukuṭa]]---helmets; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:pīṭhe]]---at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---touched.

===== Translation =====

**"All the Brahmās who came to see Kṛṣṇa offered their respects at His lotus feet, and when they did this, their helmets touched His lotus feet.**
