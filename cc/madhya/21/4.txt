====== CC Madhya 21.4 ======

===== Text =====

> śata, sahasra, ayuta, lakṣa, koṭī-yojana
> eka eka vaikuṇṭhera vistāra varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]]---a hundred; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---a thousand; [[synonyms:a:ayuta]]---ten thousand; [[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]---a hundred thousand; [[synonyms:k:koṭī]]---ten million; [[synonyms:y:yojana]]---a distance of eight miles; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]---each and every one; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhera]]---of the spiritual planets; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---the breadth; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**"The breadth of each Vaikuṇṭha planet is described as eight miles multiplied by one hundred, by one thousand, by ten thousand, by one hundred thousand, and by ten million. In other words, each Vaikuṇṭha planet is expanded beyond our ability to measure.**
