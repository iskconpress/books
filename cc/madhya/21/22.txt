====== Cc. Madhya 21.22 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe hailā caturbhuja vaikuṇṭhera pati
> pṛthak pṛthak brahmāṇḍera brahmā kare stuti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuja]]---four-handed; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhera]] [[synonyms:p:pati]]---predominating Deities of the Vaikuṇṭha planets; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separately; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍera]]---of the universes; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---the predominating deities known as Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---offer prayers.

===== Translation =====

**"The cowherd boys then became four-handed Nārāyaṇas, predominating Deities of Vaikuṇṭha planets. All the separate Brahmās from different universes began to offer their prayers unto the Lords.**
