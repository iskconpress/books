====== CC Madhya 21.28 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera mahimā rahu---kebā tāra jñātā
> vṛndāvana-sthānera dekha āścarya vibhutā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:r:rahu]]---let be; [[synonyms:k:kebā]]---//who//; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of those; [[synonyms:j:jñātā]]---a knower; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:s:sthānera]]---of the abode of Kṛṣṇa, Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---just see; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:v:vibhutā]]---opulences.

===== Translation =====

**"Let the glories of Lord Kṛṣṇa be! Who could be aware of all of them? His abode, Vṛndāvana, has many wonderful opulences. Just try to see them all.**
