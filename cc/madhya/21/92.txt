====== Cc. Madhya 21.92 ======

===== Text =====

> antaraṅga-pūrṇaiśvarya-pūrṇa tina dhāma
> tinera adhīśvara---kṛṣṇa svayaṁ bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:antaraṅga]]---internal; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---full of all opulences; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---three abodes; [[synonyms:t:tinera]] [[synonyms:a:adhīśvara]]---the Lord of all three; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"These three places are full of internal potencies, and Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, is their sole proprietor.**
