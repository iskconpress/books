====== Cc. Madhya 21.46 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra tale paravyoma---'viṣṇuloka'-nāma
> nārāyaṇa-ādi ananta svarūpera dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:t:tale]]---below Vṛndāvana-dhāma; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:v:vyoma]]---the spiritual sky; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇu]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---known as Viṣṇuloka; [[synonyms:n:nārāyaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Nārāyaṇa and others; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---of personal expansions; [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---the place.

===== Translation =====

**"Below the Vṛndāvana planet is the spiritual sky, which is known as Viṣṇuloka. In Viṣṇuloka there are innumerable Vaikuṇṭha planets controlled by Nārāyaṇa and other innumerable expansions of Kṛṣṇa.**
