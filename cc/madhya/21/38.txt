====== CC Madhya 21.38 ======

===== Text =====

> e sāmānya, tryadhīśvarera śuna artha āra
> jagat-kāraṇa tina puruṣāvatāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:s:sāmānya]]---this is a general description; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:a:adhīśvarera]]---of the master of the three worlds; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of the material creation; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---//puruṣa// incarnations of Viṣṇu.

===== Translation =====

**"This is only a general description. Please try to understand another meaning of tryadhīśa. The three puruṣa incarnations of Viṣṇu are the original cause of the material creation.**
