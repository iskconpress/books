====== CC Madhya 22.128 ======

===== Text =====

> sādhu-saṅga, nāma-kīrtana, bhāgavata-śravaṇa
> mathurā-vāsa, śrī-mūrtira śraddhāya sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---association with devotees; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hearing //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---living at Mathurā; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrtira]] [[synonyms:ś:śraddhāya]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---worshiping the Deity with faith and veneration.

===== Translation =====

**"One should associate with devotees, chant the holy name of the Lord, hear Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, reside at Mathurā and worship the Deity with faith and veneration.**
