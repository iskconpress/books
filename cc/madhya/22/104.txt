====== CC Madhya 22.104 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe sādhana-bhakti-lakṣaṇa śuna, sanātana
> yāhā haite pāi kṛṣṇa-prema-mahā-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---regulative principles for executing devotional service; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---My dear Sanātana; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from which; [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---one can get; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the most valuable treasure of love for Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Sanātana, please now hear about the regulative principles for the execution of devotional service. By this process, one can attain the highest perfection of love of Godhead, which is the most desirable treasure.**
