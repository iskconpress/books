====== Cc. Madhya 22.116 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-prītye bhoga-tyāga, kṛṣṇa-tīrthe vāsa
> yāvan-nirvāha-pratigraha, ekādaśy-upavāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prītye]]---for satisfaction of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāga]]---acceptance and rejection of something; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrthe]] [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence in a place where Kṛṣṇa is situated; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]-[[synonyms:n:nirvāha]]---as much as required to keep the body and soul together; [[synonyms:p:pratigraha]]---acceptance of gifts; [[synonyms:e:ekādaśī]]-[[synonyms:u:upavāsa]]---observance of fasting on the Ekādaśī day.

===== Translation =====

**"The next steps are as follows: (6) One should be prepared to give up everything for Kṛṣṇa's satisfaction, and one should also accept everything for Kṛṣṇa's satisfaction. (7) One must live in a place where Kṛṣṇa is present-a city like Vṛndāvana or Mathurā or a Kṛṣṇa temple. (8) One should acquire a livelihood that is just sufficient to keep body and soul together. (9) One must fast on Ekādaśī day.**
