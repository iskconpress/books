====== CC Madhya 22.166 ======

===== Text =====

> yāhā haite pāi kṛṣṇera prema-sevana
> eita' kahiluṅ 'abhidheya'-vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from which; [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---I can get; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sevana]]---affectionate service; [[synonyms:e:eita']]---this; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:a:abhidheya]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description of the means (devotional service) in detail.

===== Translation =====

**"That by which one can attain loving service to the Lord I have described in detail as the execution of devotional service called abhidheya.**
