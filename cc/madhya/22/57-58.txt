====== CC Madhya 22.57-58 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-guhyatamaṁ bhūyaḥ
> śṛṇu me paramaṁ vacaḥ
> iṣṭo 'si me dṛḍham iti
> tato vakṣyāmi te hitam
>
> man-manā bhava mad-bhakto
> mad-yājī māṁ namaskuru
> mām evaiṣyasi satyaṁ te
> pratijāne priyo 'si me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:g:guhya]]-[[synonyms:t:tamam]]---most confidential of all; [[synonyms:b:bhūyaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ś:śṛṇu]]---hear; [[synonyms:m:me]]---My; [[synonyms:p:paramam]] [[synonyms:v:vacaḥ]]---supreme instruction; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭaḥ]]---beloved; [[synonyms:a:asi]]---you are; [[synonyms:m:me]]---My; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍham]] [[synonyms:i:iti]]---very firmly; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:vakṣyāmi]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:t:te]]---to you; [[synonyms:h:hitam]]---words of benediction; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:m:manāḥ]]---whose mind is always on Me; [[synonyms:b:bhava]]---become; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktaḥ]]---My devotee; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:y:yājī]]---My worshiper; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:n:namaskuru]]---offer obeisances; [[synonyms:m:mām]] [[synonyms:e:eva]]---to Me only; [[synonyms:e:eṣyasi]]---you will come; [[synonyms:s:satyam]]---truly; [[synonyms:t:te]]---to you; [[synonyms:p:pratijāne]]---I promise; [[synonyms:p:priyaḥ]] [[synonyms:a:asi]]---you are dear; [[synonyms:m:me]]---My.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Because you are My very dear friend, I am speaking to you the most confidential part of knowledge. Hear this from Me, for it is for your benefit. Always think of Me and become My devotee, worship Me and offer obeisances unto Me. Thus you will come to Me without fail. I promise you this because you are My very dear friend.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Bhagavad-gītā// (18.64-65).
