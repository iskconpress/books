====== CC Madhya 22.92 ======

===== Text =====

> mā drākṣīḥ kṣīṇa-puṇyān kvacid api
> bhagavad-bhakti-hīnān manuṣyān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mā]]---do not; [[synonyms:d:drākṣīḥ]]---see; [[synonyms:k:kṣīṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:puṇyān]]---who are bereft of all piety; [[synonyms:k:kvacit]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---at any time; [[synonyms:b:bhagavat]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:h:hīnān]]---who are bereft of Kṛṣṇa consciousness and devotional service; [[synonyms:m:manuṣyān]]---persons.

===== Translation =====

**" One should not even see those who are bereft of devotional service in Kṛṣṇa consciousness and who are therefore devoid of pious activities.**
