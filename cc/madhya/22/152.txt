====== CC Madhya 22.152 ======

===== Text =====

> rāgamayī-bhaktira haya 'rāgātmikā' nāma
> ta-hā śuni' lubdha haya kona bhāgyavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāga]]-[[synonyms:m:mayī]]---consisting of attachment; [[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:r:rāgātmikā]]---spontaneous love; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:l:lubdha]]---covetous; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---some fortunate person.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus devotional service which consists of rāga [deep attachment] is called rāgātmikā, spontaneous loving service. If a devotee covets such a position, he is considered to be most fortunate.**
