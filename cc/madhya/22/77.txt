====== Cc. Madhya 22.77 ======

===== Text =====

> sei saba guṇa haya vaiṣṇava-lakṣaṇa
> saba kahā nā yāya, kari dig-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---all those transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms of a Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kahā]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---cannot be explained; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I shall do; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---a general review.

===== Translation =====

**"All these transcendental qualities are the characteristics of pure Vaiṣṇavas, and they cannot be fully explained, but I shall try to point out some of the important qualities.**
