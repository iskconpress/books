====== CC Madhya 22.106 ======

===== Text =====

> śravaṇādi-kriyā---tāra 'svarūpa'-lakṣaṇa
> 'taṭastha'-lakṣaṇe upajāya prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:k:kriyā]]---the process of hearing, chanting and so forth; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---symptoms of the nature; [[synonyms:t:taṭastha]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇe]]---marginal symptoms; [[synonyms:u:upajāya]]---awakens; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"The spiritual activities of hearing, chanting, remembering and so forth are the natural characteristics of devotional service. The marginal characteristic is that it awakens pure love for Kṛṣṇa.**
