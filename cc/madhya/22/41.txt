====== CC Madhya 22.41 ======

===== Text =====

> kāma lāgi' kṛṣṇe bhaje, pāya kṛṣṇa-rase
> kāma chāḍi' 'dāsa' haite haya abhilāṣe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāma]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for fulfillment of one's material desires; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]] [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---one engages in the transcendental service of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---he gets; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---a taste of the lotus feet of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kāma]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up all desires for material enjoyment; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---to be an eternal servant of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:a:abhilāṣe]]---aspiration.

===== Translation =====

**"When someone engages in Lord Kṛṣṇa's devotional service for the satisfaction of the senses and instead acquires a taste to serve Kṛṣṇa, he gives up his material desires and willingly offers himself as an eternal servant of Kṛṣṇa.**
