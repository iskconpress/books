====== CC Madhya 22.17 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-bhakti haya abhidheya-pradhāna
> bhakti-mukha-nirīkṣaka karma-yoga-jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:a:abhidheya]]-[[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the chief function of the living entity; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---of the face of devotional service; [[synonyms:n:nirīkṣaka]]---observers; [[synonyms:k:karma]]-[[synonyms:y:yoga]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---fruitive activities, mystic yoga and speculative knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**"Devotional service to Kṛṣṇa is the chief function of the living entity. There are different methods for the liberation of the conditioned soul-karma, jñāna, yoga and bhakti---but all are dependent on bhakti.**
