====== CC Madhya 22.69 ======

===== Text =====

> yāhāra komala śraddhā, se 'kaniṣṭha' jana
> krame krame teṅho bhakta ha-ibe 'uttama'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:k:komala]] [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]]---soft faith; [[synonyms:s:se]]---such a person; [[synonyms:k:kaniṣṭha]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---a neophyte devotee; [[synonyms:k:krame]] [[synonyms:k:krame]]---by a gradual progression; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---will become; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---first class.

===== Translation =====

**"One whose faith is soft and pliable is called a neophyte, but by gradually following the process, he will rise to the platform of a first-class devotee.**
