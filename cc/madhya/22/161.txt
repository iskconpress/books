====== Cc. Madhya 22.161 ======

===== Text =====

> dāsa-sakhā-pitrādi-preyasīra gaṇa
> rāga-mārge nija-nija-bhāvera gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servants; [[synonyms:s:sakhā]]---friends; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---parents; [[synonyms:p:preyasīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---conjugal lovers; [[synonyms:r:rāga]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the path of spontaneous loving service; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:n:nija]]---of one's own choice; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of the ecstasy; [[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---counting.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa has many types of devotees-some are servants, some are friends, some are parents, and some are conjugal lovers. Those who are situated in one of these attitudes of spontaneous love according to their choice are considered to be on the path of spontaneous loving service.**
