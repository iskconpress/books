====== CC Madhya 22.151 ======

===== Text =====

> iṣṭe 'gāḍha-tṛṣṇā'---rāgera svarūpa-lakṣaṇa
> iṣṭe 'āviṣṭatā'---ei taṭastha-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṣṭe]]---in the desired object, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]-[[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇā]]---deep attachment; [[synonyms:r:rāgera]]---of spontaneous love; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the primary symptom; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭe]]---unto the Supreme; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭatā]]---absorption; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:taṭastha]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the marginal symptom.

===== Translation =====

**"The primary characteristic of spontaneous love is deep attachment for the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Absorption in Him is a marginal characteristic.**
