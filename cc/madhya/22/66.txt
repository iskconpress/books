====== CC Madhya 22.66 ======

===== Text =====

> śāstre yuktau ca nipuṇaḥ
> sarvathā dṛḍha-niścayaḥ
> prauḍha-śraddho 'dhikārī yaḥ
> sa bhaktāv uttamo mataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:y:yuktau]]---in logic; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:n:nipuṇaḥ]]---expert; [[synonyms:s:sarvathā]]---in all respects; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]-[[synonyms:n:niścayaḥ]]---who is firmly convinced; [[synonyms:p:prauḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhaḥ]]---who has faith; [[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---eligible; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bhaktau]]---in devotional service; [[synonyms:u:uttamaḥ]]---highest; [[synonyms:m:mataḥ]]---is considered.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'One who is expert in logic and understanding of revealed scriptures, and who always has firm conviction and deep faith that is not blind, is to be considered a topmost devotee in devotional service.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse appears in the //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (1.2.17) by Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.
