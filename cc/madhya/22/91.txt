{{description>" 'It is better to accept the miseries of being encaged within bars and surrounded by burning flames than to associate with those bereft of Kṛṣṇa consciousness. Such association is a very great hardship.'}}

====== Cc. Madhya 22.91 ======

===== Text =====

> varaṁ huta-vaha-jvālā-
> pañjarāntar-vyavasthitiḥ
> na śauri-cintā-vimukha-
> jana-saṁvāsa-vaiśasam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varam]]---better; [[synonyms:h:huta]]-[[synonyms:v:vaha]]---of fire; [[synonyms:j:jvālā]]---in the flames; [[synonyms:p:pañjara]]-[[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---inside a cage; [[synonyms:v:vyavasthitiḥ]]---abiding; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:ś:śauri]]-[[synonyms:c:cintā]]---of Kṛṣṇa consciousness, or thought of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vimukha]]---bereft; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---of persons; [[synonyms:s:saṁvāsa]]---of the association; [[synonyms:v:vaiśasam]]---the calamity.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'It is better to accept the miseries of being encaged within bars and surrounded by burning flames than to associate with those bereft of Kṛṣṇa consciousness. Such association is a very great hardship.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from the //Kātyāyana-saṁhitā.//
