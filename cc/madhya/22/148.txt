====== Cc. Madhya 22.148 ======

===== Text =====

> vaidhī-bhakti-sādhanera kahiluṅ vivaraṇa
> rāgānugā-bhaktira lakṣaṇa śuna, sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaidhī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---of devotional service according to the regulative principles; [[synonyms:s:sādhanera]]---of the execution; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have made; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description; [[synonyms:r:rāgānugā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of spontaneous devotional service; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptoms; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---O Sanātana.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Sanātana, I have now in detail described devotional service according to the regulative principles. Now hear from Me about spontaneous devotional service and its characteristics.**
