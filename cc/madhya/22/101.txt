====== CC Madhya 22.101 ======

===== Text =====

> tavāsmīti vadan vācā
> tathaiva manasā vidan
> tat-sthānam āśritas tanvā
> modate śaraṇāgataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tava]]---His; [[synonyms:a:asmi]]---I am; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vadan]]---saying; [[synonyms:v:vācā]]---by words; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---so; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:m:manasā]]---with the mind; [[synonyms:v:vidan]]---knowing; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:s:sthānam]]---His place; [[synonyms:ā:āśritaḥ]]---taken shelter of; [[synonyms:t:tanvā]]---by the body; [[synonyms:m:modate]]---he enjoys; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āgataḥ]]---fully surrendered.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'One whose body is fully surrendered takes shelter at the holy place where Kṛṣṇa had His pastimes, and he prays to the Lord, "My Lord, I am Yours." Understanding this with his mind, he enjoys spiritual bliss.**

===== Purport =====

These last two verses appear in the //Hari-bhakti-vilāsa// (11.417,418).
