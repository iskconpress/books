====== CC Madhya 22.33 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa, tomāra haṅa' yadi bale eka-bāra
> māyā-bandha haite kṛṣṇa tāre kare pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---O my Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:h:haṅa]]---I am Yours; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---someone says; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---once; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:b:bandha]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the bondage of conditional life; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---releases.

===== Translation =====

**"One is immediately freed from the clutches of māyā if he seriously and sincerely says, 'My dear Lord Kṛṣṇa, although I have forgotten You for so many long years in the material world, today I am surrendering unto You. I am Your sincere and serious servant. Please engage me in Your service.'**
