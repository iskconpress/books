====== CC Madhya 22.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vande śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-
> devaṁ taṁ karuṇārṇavam
> kalāv apy ati-gūḍheyaṁ
> bhaktir yena prakāśitā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:devam]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:k:karuṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:arṇavam]]---who is an ocean of mercy; [[synonyms:k:kalau]]---in this Age of Kali; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very; [[synonyms:g:gūḍhā]]---confidential; [[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhaktiḥ]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---by whom; [[synonyms:p:prakāśitā]]---manifested.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He is an ocean of transcendental mercy, and although the subject matter of bhakti-yoga is very confidential, He has nonetheless manifested it so niceIy, even in this Age of Kali, the age of quarrel.**
