====== CC Madhya 22.5 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-bhakti---abhidheya, sarva-śāstre kaya
> ataeva muni-gaṇa kariyāche niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:abhidheya]]---the real activity of life; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---all Vedic literatures; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---say; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:m:muni]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all saintly persons; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]]---have made; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---ascertainment.

===== Translation =====

**"A human being's activities should be centered only about devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa. That is the verdict of all Vedic literatures, and all saintly people have ascertained this.**
