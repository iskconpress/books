====== CC Madhya 22.108 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta sādhana-bhakti---dui ta' prakāra
> eka 'vaidhī bhakti', 'rāgānugā-bhakti' āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---process of devotional service; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---two kinds; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:v:vaidhī]] [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---the regulative devotional service; [[synonyms:r:rāgānugā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---spontaneous devotional service; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and.

===== Translation =====

**"There are two processes of practical devotional service. One is regulative devotional service, and the other is spontaneous devotional service.**
