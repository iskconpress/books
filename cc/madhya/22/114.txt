====== CC Madhya 22.114 ======

===== Text =====

> vividhāṅga sādhana-bhaktira bahuta vistāra
> saṅkṣepe kahiye kichu sādhanāṅga-sāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vividha]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---varieties of limbs (regulative principles); [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktira]]---of regulative devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---many; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expansions; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I shall speak; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essential parts of the practice of devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**"I shall say something about the various practices of devotional service, which is expanded in so many ways. I wish to speak briefly of the essential practices.**
