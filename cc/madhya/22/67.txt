====== Cc. Madhya 22.67 ======

===== Text =====

> śāstra-yukti nāhi jāne dṛḍha, śraddhāvān
> 'madhyama-adhikārī' sei mahā-bhāgyavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:y:yukti]]---logical arguments on the basis of revealed scripture; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---firmly; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhāvān]]---faithful; [[synonyms:m:madhyama]]-[[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---second-class devotee; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---very fortunate.

===== Translation =====

**"One who is not very expert in argument and logic based on revealed scriptures but who has firm faith is considered a second-class devotee. He also must be considered most fortunate.**
