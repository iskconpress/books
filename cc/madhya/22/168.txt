====== CC Madhya 22.168 ======

===== Text =====

> abhidheya sādhana-bhakti śune yei jana
> acirāt pāya sei kṛṣṇa-prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:abhidheya]]---necessary duty; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service in practice; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the treasure of love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Whoever hears the process of practical devotional service very soon attains shelter at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa in love and affection.**
