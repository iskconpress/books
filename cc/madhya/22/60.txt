====== Cc. Madhya 22.60 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ājñā-bale bhaktera 'śraddhā' yadi haya
> sarva-karma tyāga kari' se kṛṣṇa bhajaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]-[[synonyms:b:bale]]---on the strength of this supreme order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]]---faith; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]---all other activities, material and spiritual; [[synonyms:t:tyāga]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---leaving aside; [[synonyms:s:se]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bhajaya]]---serves Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"If the devotee has faith in the strength of this order, he worships Lord Kṛṣṇa and gives up all other activities.**
