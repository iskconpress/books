====== CC Madhya 22.159 ======

===== Text =====

> nijābhīṣṭa kṛṣṇa-preṣṭha pācheta' lāgiyā
> nirantara sevā kare antarmanā hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:a:abhīṣṭa]]---one's own choice; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preṣṭha]]---the servitor of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pācheta']] [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---following; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---twenty-four hours a day; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---executes; [[synonyms:a:antarmanā]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being.

===== Translation =====

**"Actually the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana are very dear to Kṛṣṇa. If one wants to engage in spontaneous loving service, he must follow the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana and constantly engage in devotional service within his mind.**
