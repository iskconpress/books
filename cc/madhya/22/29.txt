====== CC Madhya 22.29 ======

===== Text =====

> jñānī jīvan-mukta-daśā pāinu kari, māne
> vastutaḥ buddhi 'śuddha' nahe kṛṣṇa-bhakti vine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jñānī]]---the speculative philosophers; [[synonyms:j:jīvan]]-[[synonyms:m:mukta]]-[[synonyms:d:daśā]]---the stage of liberation even while in this body; [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I have gotten; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---considers; [[synonyms:v:vastutaḥ]]---factually; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---purified; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:v:vine]]---without devotional service to Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"There are many philosophical speculators [jñānīs] belonging to the Māyāvāda school who consider themselves liberated and call themselves Nārāyaṇa. However, their intelligence is not purified unless they engage in Kṛṣṇa's devotional service.**
