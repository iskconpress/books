{{description>" 'Let me offer my respectful obeisances again and again to those who always eagerly meditate upon the Supreme Personality of Godhead as a husband, son, friend, brother, father or intimate friend.'}}

====== Cc. Madhya 22.163 ======

===== Text =====

> pati-putra-suhṛd-bhrātṛ-
> pitṛvan mitravad dharim
> ye dhyāyanti sadodyuktās
> tebhyo 'pīha namo namaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pati]]---a husband; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---a son; [[synonyms:s:suhṛt]]---a friend; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛ]]---a brother; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]---a father; [[synonyms:v:vat]]---like; [[synonyms:m:mitra]]---an intimate friend; [[synonyms:v:vat]]---like; [[synonyms:h:harim]]---on the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---all those who; [[synonyms:d:dhyāyanti]]---meditate; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:u:udyuktāḥ]]---full of eagerness; [[synonyms:t:tebhyaḥ]]---unto them; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---here; [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]] [[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---repeated respectful obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Let me offer my respectful obeisances again and again to those who always eagerly meditate upon the Supreme Personality of Godhead as a husband, son, friend, brother, father or intimate friend.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse appears in the //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (1.2.308).
