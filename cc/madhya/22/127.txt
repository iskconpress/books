====== CC Madhya 22.127 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvathā śaraṇāpatti, kārtikādi-vrata
> 'catuḥ-ṣaṣṭi aṅga' ei parama-mahattva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvathā]]---in all respects; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āpatti]]---surrender; [[synonyms:k:kārtika]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:v:vrata]]---to observe special vows in the month of Kārttika; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṣṭi]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---sixty-four parts; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:m:mahattva]]---very important items.

===== Translation =====

**"Thirty-four: One should surrender to Kṛṣṇa in all respects. (35) One should observe particular vows like kārtika-vrata. These are some of the sixty-four important items of devotional service.**
