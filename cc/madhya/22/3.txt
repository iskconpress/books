====== CC Madhya 22.3 ======

===== Text =====

> eita kahiluṅ sambandha-tattvera vicāra
> veda-śāstre upadeśe, kṛṣṇa---eka sāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eita]]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:s:sambandha]]-[[synonyms:t:tattvera]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---consideration of one's relationship with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---all Vedic literature; [[synonyms:u:upadeśe]]---instructs; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the only essential point.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I have described one's relation with Kṛṣṇa in various ways. This is the subject matter of all the Vedas. Kṛṣṇa is the center of all activities.**
