====== CC Madhya 22.13 ======

===== Text =====

> sei doṣe māyā-piśācī daṇḍa kare tāre
> ādhyātmikādi tāpa-traya tāre jāri, māre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:doṣe]]---because of this fault; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:p:piśācī]]---the witch known as the external energy; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---gives punishment; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:ā:ādhyātmika]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with those pertaining to the body and mind; [[synonyms:t:tāpa]]-[[synonyms:t:traya]]---the threefold miseries; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:j:jāri']]---burning; [[synonyms:m:māre]]---gives pain.

===== Translation =====

**"Due to his being opposed to Kṛṣṇa consciousness, the conditioned soul is punished by the witch of the external energy, māyā. He is thus ready to suffer the threefold miseries-miseries brought about by the body and mind, the inimical behavior of other living entities and natural disturbances caused by the demigods.**
