====== CC Madhya 22.164 ======

===== Text =====

> ei mata kare yebā rāgānugā-bhakti
> kṛṣṇera caraṇe tāṅra upajaya 'prīti'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---executes; [[synonyms:y:yebā]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:r:rāgānugā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---spontaneous devotional service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---to the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:u:upajaya]]---awakens; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---affection.

===== Translation =====

**"If one engages in spontaneous loving service to the Lord, his affection at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa gradually increases.**
