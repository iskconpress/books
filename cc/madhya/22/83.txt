====== Cc. Madhya 22.83 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-bhakti-janma-mūla haya 'sādhu-saṅga'
> kṛṣṇa-prema janme, teṅho punaḥ mukhya aṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---of devotional service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:m:mūla]]---the root cause; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---association with advanced devotees; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:janme]]---awakens; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---that same association with devotees; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the chief principle.

===== Translation =====

**"The root cause of devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa is association with advanced devotees. Even when one's dormant love for Kṛṣṇa awakens, association with devotees is still most essential.**
