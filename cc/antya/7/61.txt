====== Cc. Antya 7.61 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina saba vaiṣṇava prabhu-sthāne āilā
> sabā-sane mahāprabhu bhaṭṭe milāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭe]] [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced Vallabha Bhaṭṭa.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, when all the Vaiṣṇavas came to the abode of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord introduced Vallabha Bhaṭṭa to them all.**
