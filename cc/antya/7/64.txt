====== CC Antya 7.64 ======

===== Text =====

> paramānanda purī-saṅge sannyāsīra gaṇa
> eka-dike vaise saba karite bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paramānanda]] [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the //sannyāsī// associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---on one side; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---sat down; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---to accept the //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**All the sannyāsī associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu headed by Paramānanda Purī, sat on one side and thus partook of the prasāda.**
