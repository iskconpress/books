====== CC Antya 7.160 ======

===== Text =====

> īṣat hāsiyā prabhu kailā āliṅgana
> sabāre śunāñā kahena madhura vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īṣat]] [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling slightly; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embraced; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all others; [[synonyms:ś:śunāñā]]---causing to hear; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---began to say; [[synonyms:m:madhura]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---sweet words.

===== Translation =====

**Smiling slightly, the Lord embraced him and spoke sweet words so that others would also hear.**
