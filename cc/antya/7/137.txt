====== CC Antya 7.137 ======

===== Text =====

> aparādha chāḍi' kara kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtana
> acirāt pābe tabe kṛṣṇera caraṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparādha]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up offenses; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chant the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:p:pābe]]---you will get; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---shelter at the lotus feet of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Abandoning your offenses, chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, the holy names of the Lord. Then very soon you will achieve shelter at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa."**
