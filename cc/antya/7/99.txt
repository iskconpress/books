====== CC Antya 7.99 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi vicāre paṇḍitera nāhi kichu doṣa
> tathāpi prabhura gaṇa tāṅre kare praṇaya-roṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:v:vicāre]]---conclusively; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---there was no fault; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:praṇaya]]-[[synonyms:r:roṣa]]---showed affectionate anger.

===== Translation =====

**Although Gadādhara Paṇḍita Gosāñi was not in the least at fault, some of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's devotees showed affectionate anger toward him.**
