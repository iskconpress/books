====== CC Antya 7.139 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu avatīrṇa hailā jagat tārite
> mānilena nimantraṇa, tāre sukha dite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---made His advent; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the universe; [[synonyms:t:tārite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:m:mānilena]]---He accepted; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---the invitation; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---to him; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who had descended to deliver the entire universe, accepted the invitation of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa just to give him happiness.**
