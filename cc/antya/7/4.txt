====== CC Antya 7.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata vilāsa prabhura bhakta-gaṇa lañā
> hena-kāle vallabha-bhaṭṭa milila āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with His devotees; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---the greatly learned scholar named Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:m:milila]]---met; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His pastimes with His devotees. Then a learned scholar named Vallabha Bhaṭṭa went to Jagannātha Purī to meet the Lord.**

===== Purport =====

For a description of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa, one may refer to the //Madhya-līlā,// Chapter Nineteen, text 61.
