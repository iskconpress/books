====== Cc. Antya 7.59 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāṅi rahena sabe, vāsā---nānā-sthāne
> ihāṅi pāibā tumi sabāra darśane"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāṅi]]---here; [[synonyms:r:rahena]] [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them are living; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---their residential places; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in various quarters; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅi]]---here; [[synonyms:p:pāibā]]---will get; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---everyone's audience.

===== Translation =====

**"At present they are all living here. Their residences are in various quarters. Here you will get the audience of them all."**
