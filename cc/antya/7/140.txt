====== Cc. Antya 7.140 ======

===== Text =====

> jagatera 'hita' ha-uka---ei prabhura mana
> daṇḍa kari' kare tāra hṛdaya śodhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the entire world; [[synonyms:h:hita]]---welfare; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:u:uka]]---let there be; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---punishing; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---heart; [[synonyms:ś:śodhana]]---purifying.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is always eager to see everyone in the material world happy. Therefore sometimes He chastises someone just to purify his heart.**
