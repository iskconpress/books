====== Cc. Antya 7.128 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi---ajña, 'hita'-sthāne māni 'apamāne'
> indra yena kṛṣṇera nindā karila ajñāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:a:ajña]]---ignorant fool; [[synonyms:h:hita]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---what is for my benefit; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I consider; [[synonyms:a:apamāne]]---as an insult; [[synonyms:i:indra]]---King Indra; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nindā]]---offense; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did; [[synonyms:a:ajñāne]]---out of ignorance.

===== Translation =====

**"I am an ignorant fool, for I interpret as an insult what is meant for my benefit, just like King Indra, who out of ignorance tried to surpass Kṛṣṇa, the Supreme Lord.**
