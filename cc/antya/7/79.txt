====== CC Antya 7.79 ======

===== Text =====

> eta mata ratha-yātrā sakale dekhila
> prabhura caritre bhaṭṭera camatkāra haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the car festival; [[synonyms:s:sakale]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caritre]]---by the character of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Vallabha Bhaṭṭa witnessed the car festival. He was simply astonished by the characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
