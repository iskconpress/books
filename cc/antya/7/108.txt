====== Cc. Antya 7.108 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva nāma laya, nāmera 'phala' pāya
> nāmera phale kṛṣṇa-pade 'prema' upajāya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---chants the holy name; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the name; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---result; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]] [[synonyms:p:phale]]---as a result of chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:u:upajāya]]---develops.

===== Translation =====

**"Following this religious principle, a pure devotee of Lord Kṛṣṇa always chants the holy name. As a result of this, he gets the fruit of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa."**
