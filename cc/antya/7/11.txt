====== CC Antya 7.11 ======

===== Text =====

> kali-kālera dharma---kṛṣṇa-nāma-saṅkīrtana
> kṛṣṇa-śakti vinā nahe tāra pravartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kali]]-[[synonyms:k:kālera]]---of this age of Kali; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the duty; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without being empowered by Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:p:pravartana]]---propagation.

===== Translation =====

**"The fundamental religious system in the age of Kali is the chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa. Unless empowered by Kṛṣṇa, one cannot propagate the saṅkīrtana movement.**
