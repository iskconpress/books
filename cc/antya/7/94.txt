====== CC Antya 7.94 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nāma-vyākhyā yadi karaha śravaṇa
> tabe mora lajjā-paṅka haya prakṣālana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyā]]---explanation; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---you hear; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:l:lajjā]]-[[synonyms:p:paṅka]]---the mud of shame; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]]---washing.

===== Translation =====

**"Please hear my explanation of the meaning of Lord Kṛṣṇa's name. In that way the mud of the shame that has come upon me will be washed off."**
