====== CC Antya 7.8 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra darśana ye pāya sei bhāgyavān
> tomāke dekhiye,---yena sākṣāt bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---Your audience; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---anyone who gets; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---very fortunate; [[synonyms:t:tomāke]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiye]]---I see You; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"One who receives Your audience is fortunate indeed, for You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself.**
