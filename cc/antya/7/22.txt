====== CC Antya 7.22 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅha dekhāilā more bhakti-yoga-pāra
> tāṅra prasāde jāniluṅ 'kṛṣṇa-bhakti-yoga' sāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:d:dekhāilā]]---has shown; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to Me; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:y:yoga]]---of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---the limit; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by his mercy; [[synonyms:j:jāniluṅ]]---I have understood; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---of devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yoga]]---of the //yoga// system; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence.

===== Translation =====

**"Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya has shown Me the limit of devotional service. Only by his mercy have I understood that devotional service to Kṛṣṇa is the essence of all mystic yoga.**
