====== CC Antya 7.155 ======

===== Text =====

> pathe paṇḍitere svarūpa kahena vacana
> "parīkṣite prabhu tomāre kailā upekṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitere]]---unto Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:k:kahena]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---said some words; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣite]]---to test; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:u:upekṣaṇa]]---neglected.

===== Translation =====

**On the way, Svarūpa Dāmodara said to Gadādhara Paṇḍita, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wanted to test you. Therefore He neglected you.**
