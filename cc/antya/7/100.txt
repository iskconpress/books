====== CC Antya 7.100 ======

===== Text =====

> pratyaha vallabha-bhaṭṭa āise prabhu-sthāne
> 'udgrāhādi' prāya kare ācāryādi-sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:a:aha]]---daily; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---comes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:udgrāha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:p:prāya]]---unnecessary argument; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Advaita Ācārya and others.

===== Translation =====

**Every day, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa would come to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to engage in unnecessary arguments with Advaita Ācārya and other great personalities, such as Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
