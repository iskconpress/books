====== CC Antya 7.88 ======

===== Text =====

> phalgu-prāya bhaṭṭera nāmādi saba-vyākhyā
> sarvajña prabhu jāni' tāre karena upekṣā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:phalgu]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---generally useless; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the holy name and so on; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyā]]---explanations; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---omniscient; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:u:upekṣā]]---neglects.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is omniscient. Therefore He could understand that Vallabha Bhaṭṭa's explanations of Kṛṣṇa's name and Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam were useless. Therefore He did not care about them.**
