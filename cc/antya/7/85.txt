====== Cc. Antya 7.85 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"kṛṣṇa-nāmera bahu artha nā māni
> "śyāma-sundara' 'yaśodā-nandana,'---ei-mātra jāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---many meanings; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I do not accept; [[synonyms:ś:śyāma]]-[[synonyms:s:sundara]]---//Śyāmasundara//; [[synonyms:y:yaśodā]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Yaśodānandana; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only this; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I know.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "I do not accept many different meanings for the holy name of Kṛṣṇa. I know only that Lord Kṛṣṇa is Śyāmasundara and Yaśodānandana. That's all I know.**
