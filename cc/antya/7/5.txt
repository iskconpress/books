====== CC Antya 7.5 ======

===== Text =====

> āsiyā vandila bhaṭṭa prabhura caraṇe
> prabhu 'bhāgavata-buddhye' kailā āliṅgane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:v:vandila]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhye]]---accepting him as a great devotee; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgane]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**When Vallabha Bhaṭṭa arrived, he offered his obeisances at the lotus feet of the Lord. Accepting him as a great devotee, the Lord embraced him.**
