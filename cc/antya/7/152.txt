====== CC Antya 7.152 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi ye āmāra ṭhāñi kara āgamana
> tāhātei prabhu more dena olāhana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to me; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---come; [[synonyms:t:tāhātei]]---due to that; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:o:olāhana]]---punishment by words.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Vallabha Bhaṭṭa, your coming to me is not appreciated by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Therefore He sometimes speaks to chastise me."**
