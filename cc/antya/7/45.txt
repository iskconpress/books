====== CC Antya 7.45 ======

===== Text =====

> aiśvarya-jñāna haite kevalā-bhāva---pradhāna
> pṛthivīte bhakta nāhi uddhava-samāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than transcendental love in opulence; [[synonyms:k:kevalā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---pure love; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---more prominent; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīte]]---on the surface of the world; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is no devotee; [[synonyms:u:uddhava]]-[[synonyms:s:samāna]]---like Uddhava.

===== Translation =====

**"Completely distinct from love of Kṛṣṇa in opulence, pure love of Kṛṣṇa is on the highest level. On the surface of the world there is no devotee greater than Uddhava.**
