====== Cc. Antya 7.142 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda-paṇḍitera śuddha gāḍha bhāva
> satyabhāmā-prāya prema 'vāmya-svabhāva'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---pure; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:satyabhāmā]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---like Satyabhāmā; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---his love for the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vāmya]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---quarrelsome nature.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita's pure ecstatic love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very deep. It can be compared to the love of Satyabhāmā, who always quarreled with Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
