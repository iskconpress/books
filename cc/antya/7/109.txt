====== CC Antya 7.109 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā vallabha-bhaṭṭa haila nirvacana
> ghare yāi' mane duḥkhe karena cintana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:n:nirvacana]]---speechless; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---returning home; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---unhappy; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:c:cintana]]---began to consider.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa was speechless. He returned home greatly unhappy and began to consider thus.**
