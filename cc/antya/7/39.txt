====== CC Antya 7.39 ======

===== Text =====

> 'śuddha-prema' vraja-devīra---kāma-gandha-hīna
> 'kṛṣṇa-sukha-tātparya',---ei tāra cihna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---unalloyed love; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:d:devīra]]---of the //gopīs// or Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]-[[synonyms:g:gandha]]-[[synonyms:h:hīna]]---without a scent of material lust; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:t:tātparya]]---the purpose; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]---the symptom.

===== Translation =====

**"The unalloyed love of the gopīs and Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is without any trace of material lust. The criterion of such transcendental love is that its only purpose is to satisfy Kṛṣṇa.**
