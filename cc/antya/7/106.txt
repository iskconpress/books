====== CC Antya 7.106 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' prabhu kahena,---"tumi nā jāna dharma-marma
> svāmi-ājñā pāle,---ei pati-vratā-dharma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāna]]---do not know; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:m:marma]]---real religious principles; [[synonyms:s:svāmi]]---of the husband; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:p:pāle]]---obeys; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:v:vratā]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religious principle of a chaste woman.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Vallabha Bhaṭṭa, you do not know religious principles. Actually, the first duty of a chaste woman is to carry out the order of her husband.**
