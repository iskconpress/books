====== CC Antya 7.133 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīdhara-svāmī-prasāde 'bhāgavata' jāni
> jagad-guru śrīdhara-svāmī 'guru' kari' māni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīdhara]]-[[synonyms:s:svāmī]]---of Śrīdhara Svāmī; [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---we can understand //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:g:guru]]---the spiritual master of the entire world; [[synonyms:ś:śrīdhara]]-[[synonyms:s:svāmī]]---Śrīdhara Svāmī; [[synonyms:g:guru]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---as a spiritual master; [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I accept.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīdhara Svāmī is the spiritual master of the entire world because by his mercy we can understand Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam. I therefore accept him as a spiritual master.**
