====== CC Antya 7.157 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita kahena,---prabhu svatantra sarvajña-śiromaṇi
> tāṅra sane 'haṭha' kari,---bhāla nāhi māni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:s:sarvajña]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---the best of the omniscient; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Him; [[synonyms:h:haṭha]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---if I talk on an equal level; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---good; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I do not think it is.

===== Translation =====

**Gadādhara Paṇḍita said, "Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is completely independent. He is the topmost omniscient personality. It would not look well for me to talk to Him as if His equal.**
