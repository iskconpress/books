====== CC Antya 7.43 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvottama bhajana ei sarva-bhakti jini'
> ataeva kṛṣṇa kahe,---'āmi tomāra ṛṇī'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---above all; [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---all types of bhakti; [[synonyms:j:jini']]---conquering; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa says; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛṇī]]---debtor.

===== Translation =====

**"The conjugal love of the gopīs is the most exalted devotional service, surpassing all other methods of bhakti. Therefore Lord Kṛṣṇa is obliged to say, 'My dear gopīs, I cannot repay you. Indeed, I am always indebted to you.'**
