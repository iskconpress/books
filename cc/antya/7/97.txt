====== CC Antya 7.97 ======

===== Text =====

> ābhijātye paṇḍita karite nāre niṣedhana
> "e saṅkaṭe rākha, kṛṣṇa la-ilāṅa śaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ābhijātye]]---because of his aristocracy; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]] [[synonyms:n:niṣedhana]]---could not forbid; [[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkaṭe]]---in this danger; [[synonyms:r:rākha]]---please protect; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---O Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ilāṅa]]---I have taken; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]---shelter.

===== Translation =====

**Because Vallabha Bhaṭṭa was a learned brāhmaṇa, Gadādhara Paṇḍita could not forbid him. Thus he began to think of Lord Kṛṣṇa. "My dear Lord Kṛṣṇa," he requested, "please protect me in this danger. I have taken shelter of You.**
