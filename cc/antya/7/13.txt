====== CC Antya 7.13 ======

===== Text =====

> jagate karilā tumi kṛṣṇa-nāma prakāśe
> yei tomā dekhe, sei kṛṣṇa-preme bhāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagate]]---throughout the entire world; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---have done; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---manifestation of the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees You; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---floats.

===== Translation =====

**"You have manifested the holy name of Kṛṣṇa throughout the entire world. Anyone who sees You is immediately absorbed in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa.**
