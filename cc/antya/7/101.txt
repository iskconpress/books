====== CC Antya 7.101 ======

===== Text =====

> yei kichu kare bhaṭṭa 'siddhānta' sthāpana
> śunitei ācārya tāhā karena khaṇḍana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---conclusion; [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---establishing; [[synonyms:ś:śunitei]]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍana]]---refuted.

===== Translation =====

**Whatever conclusions Vallabha Bhaṭṭa eagerly presented were refuted by personalities like Advaita Ācārya.**
