====== CC Antya 7.91 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura upekṣāya saba nīlācalera jana
> bhaṭṭera vyākhyāna kichu nā kare śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:upekṣāya]]---because of neglect; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nīlācalera]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---people in Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explanation of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---do not hear.

===== Translation =====

**Because Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not take Vallabha Bhaṭṭa very seriously, none of the people in Jagannātha Purī would hear any of his explanations.**
