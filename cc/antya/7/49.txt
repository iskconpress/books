====== CC Antya 7.49 ======

===== Text =====

> nāmera mahimā āmi tāṅra ṭhāñi śikhiluṅ
> tāṅra prasāde nāmera mahimā jāniluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāmera]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of the holy name; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from him; [[synonyms:ś:śikhiluṅ]]---have learned; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by his mercy; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories; [[synonyms:j:jāniluṅ]]---I could understand.

===== Translation =====

**"I have learned about the glories of the Lord's holy name from Haridāsa Ṭhākura, and by his mercy I have understood these glories.**
