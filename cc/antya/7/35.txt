====== CC Antya 7.35 ======

===== Text =====

> aiśvarya dekhileha 'śuddhera' nahe aiśvarya-jñāna
> ataeva aiśvarya ha-ite 'kevala'-bhāva pradhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---opulence; [[synonyms:d:dekhileha]]---even after seeing; [[synonyms:ś:śuddhera]]---of a pure devotee; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge of opulence; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---than the understanding of opulence; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---pure emotion; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---more eminent.

===== Translation =====

**"Even if a pure devotee sees the opulence of Kṛṣṇa, he does not accept it. Therefore pure consciousness is more exalted than consciousness of the Lord's opulence.**
