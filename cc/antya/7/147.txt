====== Cc. Antya 7.147 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yena kṛṣṇa yadi parihāsa kaila
> śuni' rukmiṇīra mane trāsa upajila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---play a joke; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rukmiṇīra]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind of Rukmiṇīdevī; [[synonyms:t:trāsa]]---fear; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---arose.

===== Translation =====

**Previously, in kṛṣṇa-līlā, when Lord Kṛṣṇa wanted to joke with Rukmiṇīdevī, she took His words seriously, and fear awoke within her mind.**
