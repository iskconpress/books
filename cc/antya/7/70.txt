====== CC Antya 7.70 ======

===== Text =====

> prasāda pāya vaiṣṇava-gaṇa bale, 'hari' 'hari'
> hari hari dhvani uṭhe saba brahmāṇḍa bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---accept; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---chant; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---Hari, Hari; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---the vibration of Hari, Hari; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---rises; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---the entire universe; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling.

===== Translation =====

**Accepting the prasāda, all the Vaiṣṇavas chanted the holy names Hari, Hari. The rising vibration of the holy name of Hari filled the entire universe.**
