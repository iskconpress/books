====== Cc. Antya 7.112 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina āsi' vasilā prabhure namaskari'
> sabhāte kahena kichu mane garva kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabhāte]]---in the assembly; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---he said; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:g:garva]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being proud.

===== Translation =====

**The next day when he came to the assembly of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he sat down after offering obeisances to the Lord and said something with great pride.**
