====== CC Antya 7.159 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' paṇḍita prabhura sthāne āilā
> rodana kariyā prabhura caraṇe paḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:r:rodana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---crying; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Gadādhara Paṇḍita went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and fell down crying at the lotus feet of the Lord.**
