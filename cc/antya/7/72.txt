====== CC Antya 7.72 ======

===== Text =====

> ratha-yātrā-dine prabhu kīrtana ārambhilā
> pūrvavat sāta sampradāya pṛthak karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the day of the car festival; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:ā:ārambhilā]]---began the congregational chanting; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---in seven groups; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---He divided.

===== Translation =====

**On the day of the car festival, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began the congregational chanting. As He had done previously, He divided all the devotees into seven groups.**
