====== CC Antya 7.172 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ vallabha-bhaṭṭera milana
> yāhāra śravaṇe pāya gaura-prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---thus I have explained; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---the meeting of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing which; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---one can get; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the treasure of love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**I have thus explained the Lord's meeting with Vallabha Bhaṭṭa. By hearing of this incident, one can achieve the treasure of love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
