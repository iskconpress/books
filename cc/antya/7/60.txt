====== CC Antya 7.60 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe bhaṭṭa kahe bahu vinaya vacana
> bahu dainya kari' prabhure kaila nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa said; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---very; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---humble; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:d:dainya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in all humility; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invited to dine.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, with great submission and humility, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa invited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to dine at his home.**
