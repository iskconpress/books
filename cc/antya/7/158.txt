====== Cc. Antya 7.158 ======

===== Text =====

> yei kahe, sei sahi nija-śire dhari'
> āpane karibena kṛpā guṇa-doṣa vicāri' "

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---whatever He says; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sahi]]---I tolerate that; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on my head; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---bearing; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---automatically; [[synonyms:k:karibena]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---He will be merciful; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---attributes and faults; [[synonyms:v:vicāri']]---after considering.

===== Translation =====

**"I can tolerate whatever He says, bearing it upon my head. He will automatically be merciful to me after considering my faults and attributes."**
