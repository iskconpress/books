====== CC Antya 7.126 ======

===== Text =====

> "āmi ajña jīva,---ajñocita karma kailuṅ
> tomāra āge mūrkha āmi pāṇḍitya prakāśiluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:a:ajña]] [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---a foolish living being; [[synonyms:a:ajña]]-[[synonyms:u:ucita]]---fit for a fool; [[synonyms:k:karma]]---activity; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before You; [[synonyms:m:mūrkha]]---a fool; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍitya]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśiluṅ]]---have tried to demonstrate learning.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭa admitted, "I am a great fool, and indeed I have acted like a fool by trying to demonstrate to You my learning.**
