====== Cc. Antya 7.86 ======

===== Text =====

> tamāla-śyāmala-tviṣi
> śrī-yaśodā-stanandhaye
> kṛṣṇa-nāmno rūḍhir iti
> sarva-śāstra-vinirṇayaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tamāla]]-[[synonyms:ś:śyāmala]]-[[synonyms:t:tviṣi]]---whose complexion is dark blue, resembling a //tamāla// tree; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:y:yaśodā]]-[[synonyms:s:stanam]]-[[synonyms:d:dhaye]]---sucking the breast of mother Yaśodā; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāmnaḥ]]---of the name Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rūḍhiḥ]]---the chief meaning; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---of all revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:v:vinirṇayaḥ]]---the conclusion.

===== Translation =====

**"The only purport of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa is that He is dark blue like a tamāla tree and is the son of mother Yaśodā. This is the conclusion of all the revealed scriptures.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse from the //Nāma-kaumuḍī//
