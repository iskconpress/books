====== CC Antya 7.116 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' mahāprabhu mauna dharilā
> śuniyā sabāra mane santoṣa ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---became very grave; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---there was great satisfaction.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very grave. All the devotees present derived great satisfaction from hearing this statement.**
