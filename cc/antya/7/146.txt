====== CC Antya 7.146 ======

===== Text =====

> ei lakṣya pāñā prabhu kailā roṣābhāsa
> śuni' paṇḍitera citte upajila trāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:l:lakṣya]]---aim; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:r:roṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---made a semblance of anger; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---arose; [[synonyms:t:trāsa]]---fear.

===== Translation =====

**For this purpose Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sometimes showed His apparent anger. Hearing of this anger inspired great fear in the heart of Gadādhara Paṇḍita.**
