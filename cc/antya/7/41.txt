====== CC Antya 7.41 ======

===== Text =====

> gopī-gaṇera śuddha-prema aiśvarya-jñāna-hīna
> premete bhartsanā kare ei tāra cihna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---unalloyed love; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]-[[synonyms:h:hīna]]---devoid of knowledge of opulences; [[synonyms:p:premete]]---of pure love; [[synonyms:b:bhartsanā]]---chastisement; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:c:cihna]]---the symptom.

===== Translation =====

**"Obsessed with pure love, without knowledge of opulences, the gopīs sometimes chastised Kṛṣṇa. That is a symptom of pure ecstatic love.**
