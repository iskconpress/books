====== Cc. Antya 7.135 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīdharera anugata ye kare likhana
> saba loka mānya kari' karibe grahaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīdharera]]---of Śrīdhara Svāmī; [[synonyms:a:anugata]]---following in the footsteps; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:l:likhana]]---writes; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---everyone; [[synonyms:m:mānya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great honor; [[synonyms:k:karibe]] [[synonyms:g:grahaṇa]]---will accept.

===== Translation =====

**"One who comments on Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam following in the footsteps of Śrīdhara Svāmī will be honored and accepted by everyone.**
