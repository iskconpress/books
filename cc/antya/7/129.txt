====== Cc. Antya 7.129 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra kṛpā-añjane ebe garva-āndhya gela
> tumi eta kṛpā kailā,---ebe 'jñāna' haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:a:añjane]]---by the eye-ointment of Your mercy; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:g:garva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āndhya]]---the blindness of false pride; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---has gone; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---such; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---have shown; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---has become.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You have cured the blindness of my false pride by smearing my eyes with the ointment of Your mercy. You have bestowed so much mercy upon me that my ignorance is now gone.**
