====== CC Antya 7.6 ======

===== Text =====

> mānya kari' prabhu tāre nikaṭe vasāilā
> vinaya kariyā bhaṭṭa kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mānya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great respect; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---seated; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great humility; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**With great respect, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu seated Vallabha Bhaṭṭa near Him. Then Vallabha Bhaṭṭa very humbly began to speak.**
