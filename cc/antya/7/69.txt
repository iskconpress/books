====== Cc. Antya 7.69 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-prasāda vallabha-bhaṭṭa bahu ānāila
> prabhu-saha sannyāsi-gaṇa bhojane vasila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---food offered to Śrī Jagannātha; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---a large quantity; [[synonyms:ā:ānāila]]---brought; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:b:bhojane]] [[synonyms:v:vasila]]---sat down to accept the //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭa had brought a large quantity of mahā-prasāda offered to Lord Jagannātha. Thus all the sannyāsīs sat down to eat with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
