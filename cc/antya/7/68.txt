====== CC Antya 7.68 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa, jagadānanda, kāśīśvara, śaṅkara
> pariveśana kare, āra rāghava, dāmodara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---distribute; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]] [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Rāghava and Dāmodara.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara, Jagadānanda, Kāśīśvara and Śaṅkara, along with Rāghava and Dāmodara Paṇḍita, took charge of distributing the prasāda.**
