====== CC Antya 7.149 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍitera sane tāra mana phiri' gela
> kiśora-gopāla-upāsanāya mana dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---in the association of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:p:phiri']] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---became converted; [[synonyms:k:kiśora]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---of Kṛṣṇa as a young boy; [[synonyms:u:upāsanāya]]---to the worship; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---he gave his mind.

===== Translation =====

**In the association of Gadādhara Paṇḍita, his mind was converted, and he dedicated his mind to worshiping Kiśora-gopāla, Kṛṣṇa as a young boy.**
