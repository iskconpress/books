====== CC Antya 7.141 ======

===== Text =====

> svagaṇa-sahita prabhura nimantraṇa kailā
> mahāprabhu tāre tabe prasanna ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with His associates; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---upon him; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became very pleased.

===== Translation =====

**When Vallabha Bhaṭṭa invited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His associates, the Lord was very pleased with him.**
