====== Cc. Antya 7.76 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' vallabha-bhaṭṭera haila camatkāra
> ānande vihvala nāhi āpana-sāmbhāla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---was astonishment; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]] [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed by transcendental happiness; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was not; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:s:sāmbhāla]]---keeping his normal position.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing all this, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa was completely astonished. He was overwhelmed by transcendental bliss and lost himself.**
