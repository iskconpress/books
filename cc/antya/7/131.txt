====== CC Antya 7.131 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe---"tumi 'paṇḍita' 'mahā-bhāgavata'
> dui-guṇa yāhāṅ, tāhāṅ nāhi garva-parvata

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---very learned scholar; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---great devotee; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---two qualities; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there cannot be; [[synonyms:g:garva]]-[[synonyms:p:parvata]]---the mountain of pride.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You are both a greatly learned scholar and a great devotee. Wherever there are two such attributes, there cannot be a mountain of false pride.**
