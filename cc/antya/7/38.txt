====== CC Antya 7.38 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara-svarūpa---'prema-rasa' mūrtimān
> yāṅra saṅge haila vraja-madhura-rasa-jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the transcendental mellows of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:mūrtimān]]---personified; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---by whose association; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]---of Vraja; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of the mellow of conjugal love; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge.

===== Translation =====

**"The transcendental mellow of ecstatic love is personified by Svarūpa Dāmodara. By his association I have understood Vṛndāvana's transcendental mellow of conjugal love.**
