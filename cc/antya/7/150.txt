====== CC Antya 7.150 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍitera ṭhāñi cāhe mantrādi śikhite
> paṇḍita kahe,---"ei karma nahe āmā haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wanted; [[synonyms:m:mantra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:ś:śikhite]]---to be initiated; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita said; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:k:karma]]---this work; [[synonyms:n:nahe]] [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---is not possible for me.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭa wanted to be initiated by Gadādhara Paṇḍita, but Gadādhara Paṇḍita refused, saying, "The work of acting as a spiritual master is not possible for me.**
