====== CC Antya 7.165 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-prabhura līlā ke bujhite pāre?
> eka-līlāya vahe gaṅgāra śata śata dhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can understand; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---in one activity; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---flow; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgāra]]---of the Ganges; [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:d:dhāre]]---hundreds and hundreds of branches.

===== Translation =====

**No one can understand the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. They are like the Ganges, for hundreds and thousands of branches flow from even one of His activities.**
