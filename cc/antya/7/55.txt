====== CC Antya 7.55 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaṭṭera manete ei chila dīrgha garva
> prabhura vacana śuni' se ha-ila kharva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]] [[synonyms:m:manete]]---in the mind of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---was existing; [[synonyms:d:dīrgha]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the words; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---by hearing; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was; [[synonyms:k:kharva]]---cut down.

===== Translation =====

**Such pride had existed for a long time within Vallabha Bhaṭṭa's mind, but as he heard the preaching of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, his pride was cut down.**
