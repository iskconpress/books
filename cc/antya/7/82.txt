====== CC Antya 7.82 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"bhāgavatārtha bujhite nā pāri
> bhāgavatārtha śunite āmi nahi adhikārī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I cannot understand; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the purport of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:n:nahi]] [[synonyms:a:adhikārī]]---I am not the proper person.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord replied, "I do not understand the meaning of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam. Indeed, I am not a suitable person to hear its meaning.**
