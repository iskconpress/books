====== CC Antya 7.80 ======

===== Text =====

> yātrānantare bhaṭṭa yāi mahāprabhu-sthāne
> prabhu-caraṇe kichu kaila nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]-[[synonyms:a:anantare]]---after the Ratha-yātrā; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---going; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**One day, after the festival was over, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa went to the abode of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and submitted a request at the lotus feet of the Lord.**
