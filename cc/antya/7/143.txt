====== CC Antya 7.143 ======

===== Text =====

> bāra-bāra praṇaya kalaha kare prabhu-sane
> anyonye khaṭmaṭi cale dui-jane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:p:praṇaya]]---loving; [[synonyms:k:kalaha]]---quarrel; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:anyonye]]---mutual; [[synonyms:k:khaṭmaṭi]]---picking a quarrel; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes on; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---between the two.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita was accustomed to provoking loving quarrels with the Lord. There was always some disagreement between them.**
