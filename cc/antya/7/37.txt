====== Cc. Antya 7.37 ======

===== Text =====

> kahana nā yāya rāmānandera prabhāva
> rāya-prasāde jāniluṅ vrajera 'śuddha' bhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kahana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---cannot be described; [[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]] [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---the influence of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:j:jāniluṅ]]---I have understood; [[synonyms:v:vrajera]]---of the inhabitants of Vraja; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]] [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---unalloyed love.

===== Translation =====

**"It is impossible to describe the influence and knowledge of Rāmānanda Rāya, for only by his mercy have I understood the unalloyed love of the residents of Vṛndāvana.**
