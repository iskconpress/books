====== Cc. Antya 7.107 ======

===== Text =====

> patira ājñā,---nirantara tāṅra nāma la-ite
> patira ājñā pati-vratā nā pāre laṅghite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patira]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the husband's order; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---to chant; [[synonyms:p:patira]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order of the husband; [[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:v:vratā]]---a chaste, devoted wife; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]] [[synonyms:l:laṅghite]]---cannot deny.

===== Translation =====

**"The order of Kṛṣṇa is to chant His name incessantly. Therefore one who is chaste and adherent to the husband Kṛṣṇa must chant the Lord's name, for she cannot deny the husband's order.**
