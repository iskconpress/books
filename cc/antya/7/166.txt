====== Cc. Antya 7.166 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍitera saujanya, brahmaṇyatā-guṇa
> dṛḍha prema-mudrā loke karilā khyāpana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:s:saujanya]]---the gentle behavior of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:brahmaṇyatā]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the attributes of a perfect //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---firm; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---characteristic of love; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:k:khyāpana]]---proclaimed.

===== Translation =====

**Gadādhara Paṇḍita is celebrated all over the world for his gentle behavior, his brahminical attributes and his steady love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
