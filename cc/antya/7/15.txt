====== CC Antya 7.15 ======

===== Text =====

> santv avatārā bahavaḥ
> puṣkara-nābhasya sarvato-bhadrāḥ
> kṛṣṇād anyaḥ ko vā latāsv
> api premado bhavati"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:santu]]---let there be; [[synonyms:a:avatārāḥ]]---incarnations; [[synonyms:b:bahavaḥ]]---many; [[synonyms:p:puṣkara]]-[[synonyms:n:nābhasya]]---of the Lord, from whose navel grows a lotus flower; [[synonyms:s:sarvataḥ]] [[synonyms:b:bhadrāḥ]]---completely auspicious; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇāt]]---than Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:anyaḥ]]---other; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]] [[synonyms:v:vā]]---who possibly; [[synonyms:l:latāsu]]---on the surrendered souls; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:daḥ]]---the bestower of love; [[synonyms:b:bhavati]]---is.

===== Translation =====

**"There may be many all-auspicious incarnations of the Personality of Godhead, but who other than Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa can bestow love of God upon the surrendered souls?' "**

===== Purport =====

This is a verse written by Bilvamaṅgala Ṭhākura. it is found in the //Laghu-bhāgavatāmṛta// (1.5.37).
