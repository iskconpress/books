====== CC Antya 7.23 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-rāya kṛṣṇa-rasera 'nidhāna'
> teṅha jānāilā---kṛṣṇa---svayaṁ bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of the transcendental mellow of Kṛṣṇa's devotional service; [[synonyms:n:nidhāna]]---the mine; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:j:jānāilā]]---has given instruction; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---Himself; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya is the ultimate knower of the transcendental mellows of Lord Kṛṣṇa's devotional service. He has instructed Me that Lord Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
