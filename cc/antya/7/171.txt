====== CC Antya 7.171 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅi vallabha-bhaṭṭa prabhura ājñā laila
> paṇḍita-ṭhāñi pūrva-prārthita saba siddhi haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the permission of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:laila]]---took; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:p:prārthita]]---as previously petitioned; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:s:siddhi]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---everything was perfectly executed.

===== Translation =====

**There Vallabha Bhaṭṭa took permission from Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and his desire to be initiated by Gadādhara Paṇḍita was thus fulfilled.**
