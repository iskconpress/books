====== CC Antya 7.148 ======

===== Text =====

> vallabha-bhaṭṭera haya vātsalya-upāsana
> bāla-gopāla-mantre teṅho karena sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vallabha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]-[[synonyms:u:upāsana]]---worship as a parent; [[synonyms:b:bāla]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:m:mantre]]---with the //mantra// of Bāla-gopāla, child Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---practices; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---worship.

===== Translation =====

**Vallabha Bhaṭṭa was accustomed to worshiping the Lord as child Kṛṣṇa. Therefore he had been initiated into the Bāla-gopāla mantra and was thus worshiping the Lord.**
