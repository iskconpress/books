====== Cc. Antya 7.21 ======

===== Text =====

> ṣaḍ-darśana-vettā bhaṭṭācārya-sārvabhauma
> ṣaḍ-darśane jagad-guru bhāgavatottama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:d:darśana]]---of the six philosophical theses; [[synonyms:v:vettā]]---the knower; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]-[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]]-[[synonyms:d:darśane]]---in six philosophical theses; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:g:guru]]---the spiritual master of the entire world; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---the best of the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**"Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya perfectly knows the six philosophical theses. He is therefore the spiritual master of the entire world in the six paths of philosophy. He is the best of devotees.**
