====== CC Antya 7.63 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe bhaṭṭa bahu mahā-prasāda ānāila
> gaṇa-saha mahāprabhure bhojana karāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---Lord Jagannātha's remnants; [[synonyms:ā:ānāila]]---brought in; [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]] [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with His associates; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---he fed.

===== Translation =====

**Then Vallabha Bhaṭṭa brought in a great quantity of Lord Jagannātha's mahā-prasāda and sumptuously fed Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His associates.**
