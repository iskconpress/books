====== CC Antya 7.130 ======

===== Text =====

> aparādha kainu, kṣama, la-inu śaraṇa
> kṛpā kari' mora māthe dharaha caraṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparādha]] [[synonyms:k:kainu]]---I have committed offenses; [[synonyms:k:kṣama]]---please excuse; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:inu]] [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]---I have taken shelter; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:m:māthe]]---on my head; [[synonyms:d:dharaha]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---please keep Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, I have committed offenses. Please excuse me. I seek shelter of You. Please be merciful unto me by placing Your lotus feet on my head."**
