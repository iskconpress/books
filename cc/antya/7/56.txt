====== CC Antya 7.56 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura mukhe vaiṣṇavatā śuniyā sabāra
> bhaṭṭera icchā haila tāṅ-sabāre dekhibāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavatā]]---the standard of Vaiṣṇavism; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]] [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---hearing of all the devotees; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāra]]---to see.

===== Translation =====

**When Vallabha Bhaṭṭa heard from the mouth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu about the pure Vaiṣṇavism of all these devotees, he immediately desired to see them.**
