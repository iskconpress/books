====== CC Antya 7.96 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi paṇḍita āra nā kailā aṅgīkāra
> bhaṭṭa yāi' tabu paḍe kari' balātkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---did not accept; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---reads; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:b:balātkāra]]---forcibly.

===== Translation =====

**Although Gadādhara Paṇḍita Gosāñi did not want to hear it, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa began to read his explanation with great force.**
