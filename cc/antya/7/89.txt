====== CC Antya 7.89 ======

===== Text =====

> vimanā hañā bhaṭṭa gelā nija-ghara
> prabhu-viṣaye bhakti kichu ha-ila antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vimanā]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---feeling morose; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghara]]---to his home; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣaye]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---somewhat; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---different.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu rigidly declined to hear his explanations, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa went home feeling morose. His faith and devotion to the Lord changed.**
