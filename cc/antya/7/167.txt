====== CC Antya 7.167 ======

===== Text =====

> abhimāna-paṅka dhuñā bhaṭṭere śodhilā
> sei-dvārā āra saba loke śikhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]-[[synonyms:p:paṅka]]---the mud of false pride; [[synonyms:d:dhuñā]]---washing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭere]] [[synonyms:ś:śodhilā]]---purified Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---by that; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all other; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---persons; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---instructed.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord purified Vallabha Bhaṭṭa by cleansing him of the mud of false pride. By such activities the Lord also instructed others.**
