====== Cc. Antya 7.164 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍite prabhura prasāda kahana nā yāya
> 'gadāira gaurāṅga' bali' yāṅre loke gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍite]]---upon Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kahana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---no one can explain; [[synonyms:g:gadāira]] [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]---the Gaurāṅga of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---as; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---whom; [[synonyms:l:loke]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---people say.

===== Translation =====

**No one can say how merciful the Lord is to Gadādhara Paṇḍita, but people know the Lord as Gadāira Gaura, "the Lord Gaurāṅga of Gadādhara Paṇḍita."**
