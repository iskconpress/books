====== CC Antya 7.104 ======

===== Text =====

> pati-vratā hañā patira nāma nāhi laya
> tomarā kṛṣṇa-nāma-laha,---kon dharma haya?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pati]]-[[synonyms:v:vratā]]---devoted to the husband; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:p:patira]]---of the husband; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---does not utter; [[synonyms:t:tomarā]]---all of you; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:l:laha]]---chant the name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principle; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is it.

===== Translation =====

**"It is the duty of a chaste wife, devoted to her husband, not to utter her husband's name, but all of you chant the name of Kṛṣṇa. How can this be called a religious principle?"**
