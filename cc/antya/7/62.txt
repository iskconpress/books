====== CC Antya 7.62 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vaiṣṇave'ra teja dekhi' bhaṭṭera camatkāra
> tāṅ-sabāra āge bhaṭṭa---khadyota-ākāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:t:teja]]---the brilliance; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---surprised; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:khadyota]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---like a glowworm.

===== Translation =====

**He was surprised to see the brilliance of their faces. Indeed, among them Vallabha Bhaṭṭa seemed just like a glowworm.**
