====== CC Antya 7.123 ======

===== Text =====

> āpanā jānāite āmi kari abhimāna
> se garva khaṇḍāite mora karena apamāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpanā]] [[synonyms:j:jānāite]]---advertising myself; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---am falsely proud; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:g:garva]]---that pride; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāite]]---to cut down; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:a:apamāna]]---He insults me.

===== Translation =====

**"I am falsely proud, advertising myself as a learned scholar. Therefore Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu insults me just to favor me by cutting down this false pride.**
