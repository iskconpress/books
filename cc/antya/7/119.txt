====== Cc. Antya 7.119 ======

===== Text =====

> ajña jīva nija-'hite' 'ahita' kari' māne
> garva cūrṇa haile, pāche ughāḍe nayane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ajña]] [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---the ignorant living entity; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:h:hite]]---his personal benefit; [[synonyms:a:ahita]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---considers a loss; [[synonyms:g:garva]] [[synonyms:c:cūrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haile]]---when pride is cut down; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---afterward; [[synonyms:u:ughāḍe]] [[synonyms:n:nayane]]---the eyes open.

===== Translation =====

**An ignorant living being does not recognize his actual profit. Because of ignorance and material pride, he sometimes considers profit a loss, but when his pride is cut down he can actually see his true benefit.**
