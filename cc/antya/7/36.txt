====== CC Antya 7.36 ======

===== Text =====

> e saba śikhāilā more rāya-rāmānanda
> anargala rasa-vettā prema-sukhānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all this; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---instructed; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:a:anargala]]---incessantly; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:v:vettā]]---one who understands transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---absorbed in the happiness of ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Rāmānanda Rāya is extremely aware of transcendental mellows. He is incessantly absorbed in the happiness of ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa. It is he who has instructed Me all this.**
