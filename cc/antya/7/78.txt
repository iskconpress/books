====== Cc. Antya 7.78 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura saundarya dekhi āra premodaya
> 'ei ta' sākṣāt kṛṣṇa' bhaṭṭera ha-ila niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---arousing of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---certainty.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the beauty of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and the awakening of His ecstatic love, Vallabha Bhaṭṭa concluded, "Here is Lord Kṛṣṇa, without a doubt."**
