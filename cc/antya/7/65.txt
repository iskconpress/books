====== CC Antya 7.65 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita, nityānanda-rāya---pārśve dui-jana
> madhye mahāprabhu vasilā, āge-pāche bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:p:pārśve]]---on the sides; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two personalities; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat in the midst of the devotees. Advaita Ācārya and Lord Nityānanda each sat on one side of the Lord. The other devotees sat in front of the Lord and behind Him.**
