====== CC Antya 7.46 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅha yāṅra pada-dhūli karena prārthana
> svarūpera saṅge pāiluṅ e saba śikṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:d:dhūli]]---dust of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:p:prārthana]]---desires; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---from Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:p:pāiluṅ]]---I have gotten; [[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣaṇa]]---instructions.

===== Translation =====

**"Uddhava desires to take on his head the dust of the gopīs' lotus feet. I have learned about all these transcendental loving affairs of Lord Kṛṣṇa from Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
