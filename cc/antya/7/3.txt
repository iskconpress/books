====== CC Antya 7.3 ======

===== Text =====

> varṣāntare yata gauḍera bhakta-gaṇa āilā
> pūrvavat mahāprabhu sabāre mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---the next year; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]]---of Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met every one of them.

===== Translation =====

**The next year, all the devotees of Bengal went to visit Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and as previously, the Lord met each and every one of them.**
