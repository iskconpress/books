====== CC Antya 7.67 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura bhakta-gaṇa dekhi' bhaṭṭera camatkāra
> pratyeke sabāra pade kaila namaskāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---surprise; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:e:eke]]---unto each and every one; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:p:pade]]---at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---he offered obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**When Vallabha Bhaṭṭa saw all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he was greatly surprised, but in devotion he offered his obeisances at the lotus feet of each and every one of them.**
