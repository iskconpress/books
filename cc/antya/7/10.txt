====== CC Antya 7.10 ======

===== Text =====

> yeṣāṁ saṁsmaraṇāt puṁsāṁ
> sadyaḥ śuddhyanti vai gṛhāḥ
> kiṁ punar darśana-sparśa-
> pāda-śaucāsanādibhiḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yeṣām]]---of whom; [[synonyms:s:saṁsmaraṇāt]]---by remembrance; [[synonyms:p:puṁsām]]---of persons; [[synonyms:s:sadyaḥ]]---immediately; [[synonyms:ś:śuddhyanti]]---become purified; [[synonyms:v:vai]]---certainly; [[synonyms:g:gṛhāḥ]]---the houses; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---what to speak of; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---by seeing; [[synonyms:s:sparśa]]---touching; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śauca]]---washing the feet; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādibhiḥ]]---by offering a seat and so on.

===== Translation =====

**"One can immediately purify his entire house simply by remembering exalted personalities, to say nothing of directly seeing them, touching their lotus feet, washing their feet or offering them places to sit.'**

===== Purport =====

This is a quotation from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// [[books:sb:1:19:33|(1.19.33)]].
