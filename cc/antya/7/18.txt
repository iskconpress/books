====== CC Antya 7.18 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-śāstre kṛṣṇa-bhaktye nāhi yāṅra sama
> ataeva 'advaita-ācārya' tāṅra nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in all revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktye]]---in the devotional service of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---is not; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---without a competitor; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---//ācārya//; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---His name.

===== Translation =====

**"He is unparalleled in His understanding of all the revealed scriptures and the devotional service of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Therefore He is called Advaita Ācārya.**
