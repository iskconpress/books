====== CC Antya 7.121 ======

===== Text =====

> svagaṇa-sahite mora mānilā nimantraṇa
> ebe kene prabhura mote phiri' gela mana?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sahite]]---accompanied by His personal associates; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:m:mānilā]]---accepted; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mote]]---unto me; [[synonyms:p:phiri']] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---has changed; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"He accepted my invitation with His other devotees, and He was kind to me. Why has He now changed so much here at Jagannātha Purī?**
