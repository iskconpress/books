====== Cc. Antya 7.144 ======

===== Text =====

> gadādhara-paṇḍitera śuddha gāḍha bhāva
> rukmiṇī-devīra yaiche 'dakṣiṇa-svabhāva'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---pure; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:r:rukmiṇī]]-[[synonyms:d:devīra]]---of Rukmiṇīdevī; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---submissive nature.

===== Translation =====

**Gadādhara Paṇḍita's pure ecstatic love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was also very deep. It was like that of Rukmiṇīdevī, who was always especially submissive to Kṛṣṇa.**
