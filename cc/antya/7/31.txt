====== CC Antya 7.31 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mora sakhā,' 'mora putra,'---ei 'śuddha' mana
> ataeva śuka-vyāsa kare praśaṁsana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:sakhā]]---my friend; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---my son; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---pure; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:ś:śuka]]-[[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---Śukadeva Gosvāmī and Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:k:karepraśaṁsana]]---praise.

===== Translation =====

**"In pure Kṛṣṇa consciousness, without knowledge of the Lord's opulences, a devotee considers Kṛṣṇa his friend or son. Therefore this devotional attitude is praised even by Śukadeva Gosvāmī and Vyāsadeva, the supreme authority.**
