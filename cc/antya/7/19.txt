====== Cc. Antya 7.19 ======

===== Text =====

> yāṅhāra kṛpāte mlecchera haya kṛṣṇa-bhakti
> ke kahite pāre tāṅra vaiṣṇavatā-śakti?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by mercy; [[synonyms:m:mlecchera]]---of mlecchas; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can describe; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavatā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power of Vaiṣṇavism.

===== Translation =====

**"He is such a great personality that by His mercy He can convert even the meateaters [mlecchas] to the devotional service of Kṛṣṇa. Who, therefore, can estimate the power of His Vaiṣṇavism?**

===== Purport =====

It is extremely difficult to convert a //mleccha,// or meat-eater, into a devotee of Lord Kṛṣṇa. Therefore anyone who can do so is situated on the highest level of Vaiṣṇavism.
