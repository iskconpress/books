====== CC Antya 7.163 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍitera bhāva-mudrā kahana nā yāya
> 'gadādhara-prāṇa-nātha' nāma haila yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---characteristics and ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kahana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---can not be described; [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]-[[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]---the Lord of the life of Gadādhara; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes.

===== Translation =====

**No one can describe the characteristics and ecstatic love of Gadādhara Paṇḍita. Therefore another name for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is Gadādhara-prāṇanātha, "the life and soul of Gadādhara Paṇḍita."**
