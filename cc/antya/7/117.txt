====== CC Antya 7.117 ======

===== Text =====

> jagatera hita lāgi' gaura-avatāra
> antarera abhimāna jānena tāhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the entire world; [[synonyms:h:hita]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the benefit; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnation of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:antarera]] [[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---internal pride; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---understands; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---his.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu descended as an incarnation for the benefit of the entire world. Thus He knew the mind of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa very well.**
