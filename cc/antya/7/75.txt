====== CC Antya 7.75 ======

===== Text =====

> caudda mādala bāje ucca saṅkīrtana
> eka eka nartakera preme bhāsila bhuvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caudda]] [[synonyms:m:mādala]]---fourteen //mṛdaṅgas//; [[synonyms:b:bāje]]---were being played; [[synonyms:u:ucca]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---loud congregational chanting; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]---of each group; [[synonyms:n:nartakera]]---of the dancer; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bhāsila]] [[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---inundated the entire world.

===== Translation =====

**Fourteen mṛdaṅgas resounded with the loud congregational chanting, and in each group was a dancer whose dance of ecstatic love inundated the entire world.**
