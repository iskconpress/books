~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya 7.153-154~~

====== CC Antya 7.153-154 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata bhaṭṭera katheka dina gela
> śeṣe yadi prabhu tāre suprasanna haila
>
> nimantraṇera dine paṇḍite bolāilā
> svarūpa, jagadānanda, govinde pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:katheka]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---some days; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---passed; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣe]]---at last; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---upon him; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:p:prasanna]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became very pleased; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇera]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the day of invitation; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍite]] [[synonyms:b:bolāilā]]---He called for Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:g:govinde]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---He sent.

===== Translation =====

**Some days passed, and when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, finally pleased with Vallabha Bhaṭṭa, accepted his invitation, the Lord sent Svarūpa Dāmodara, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita and Govinda to call for Gadādhara Paṇḍita.**
