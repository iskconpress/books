====== Cc. Antya 7.132 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīdhara-svāmī nindi' nija-ṭīkā kara!
> śrīdhara-svāmī nāhi māna',---eta 'garva' dhara!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīdhara]]-[[synonyms:s:svāmī]]---a great commentator on //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:n:nindi']]---blaspheming; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭīkā]]---your own commentary; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you make; [[synonyms:ś:śrīdhara]]-[[synonyms:s:svāmī]]---Śrīdhara Svāmī; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māna']]---you do not accept; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---this; [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:d:dhara]]---you bear.

===== Translation =====

**"You have dared criticize Śrīdhara Svāmī, and you have begun your own commentary on Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, not accepting his authority. That is your false pride.**
