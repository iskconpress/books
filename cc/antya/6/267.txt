====== CC Antya 6.267 ======

===== Text =====

> sei vipra bhṛtya, cāri-śata mudrā lañā
> nīlācale raghunāthe mililā āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---that //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---the servants; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---four hundred coins; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---with Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**The servants and brāhmaṇa brought four hundred coins to Jagannātha Purī, and there they met Raghunātha dāsa.**
