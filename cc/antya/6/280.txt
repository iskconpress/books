====== CC Antya 6.280 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāra saṅkoce āmi eta dina nila
> bhāla haila---jāniyā āpani chāḍi dila"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkoce]]---because of his eagerness; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---for so many days; [[synonyms:n:nila]]---I accepted; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it is very good; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---automatically; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---he has given up.

===== Translation =====

**"Because of Raghunātha dāsa's eagerness, I accepted his invitation for many days. It is very good that Raghunātha dāsa, knowing this, has now automatically given up this practice."**
