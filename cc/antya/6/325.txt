====== CC Antya 6.325 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu nānā līlā kare
> raghunāthera vairāgya dekhi' santoṣa antare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---many pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]]---of Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---renunciation; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]] [[synonyms:a:antare]]---satisfied within.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed many pastimes at Jagannātha Purī. Seeing the severe penances performed by Raghunātha dāsa in the renounced order, the Lord was greatly satisfied.**
