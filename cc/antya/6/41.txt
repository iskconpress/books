====== Cc. Antya 6.41 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-candrera kṛpā hañāche iṅhāre
> caitanya-candrera 'bātula' ke rākhite pāre?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:candrera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:h:hañāche]] [[synonyms:i:iṅhāre]]---has been bestowed upon him; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:candrera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bātula]]---madman; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:r:rākhite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can keep.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has fully bestowed His mercy on him. Who can keep home such a madman of Caitanyacandra?"**
