====== Cc. Antya 6.67 ======

===== Text =====

> ekeka janāre dui dui holnā dila
> dadhi-ciḍā dugdha-ciḍā, duite bhijāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekeka]] [[synonyms:j:janāre]]---to each and every one of them; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two and two; [[synonyms:h:holnā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---earthen pots were supplied; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]-[[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice with yogurt; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice with condensed milk; [[synonyms:d:duite]]---in the two pots; [[synonyms:b:bhijāila]]---were soaked.

===== Translation =====

**Each and every one of them was supplied two earthen pots-one of chipped rice soaked in yogurt and the other of chipped rice soaked in condensed milk.**
