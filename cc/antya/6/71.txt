====== Cc. Antya 6.71 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle āilā tathā rāghava paṇḍita
> hāsite lāgilā dekhi' hañā vismita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---the great scholar named Rāghava paṇḍita; [[synonyms:h:hāsite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to laugh; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---being astonished.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Rāghava Paṇḍita arrived there. Seeing the situation, he began to laugh in great surprise.**
