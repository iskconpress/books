====== CC Antya 6.272 ======

===== Text =====

> māsa-dui yabe raghunātha nā kare nimantraṇa
> svarūpe puchilā tabe śacīra nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dui]]---for two months; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---does not invite; [[synonyms:s:svarūpe]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghunātha dāsa neglected to invite Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for two consecutive months, the Lord, the son of Śacī, questioned Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
