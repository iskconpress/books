====== Cc. Antya 6.282 ======

===== Text =====

> govinda-pāśa śuni' prabhu puchena svarūpere
> 'raghu bhikṣā lāgi' ṭhāḍa kene nahe siṁha-dvāre'?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---from Govinda; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:puchena]] [[synonyms:s:svarūpere]]---inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:raghu]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for begging; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kene]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---why does he not stand; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the Siṁha-dvāra gate.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this news from Govinda, He inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara, "Why does Raghunātha dāsa no longer stand at the Siṁha-dvāra gate to beg alms?"**
