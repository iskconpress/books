====== CC Antya 6.206 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyera bhakta-vātsalya kahite nā pāri
> govindere kahe raghunāthe dayā kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]---affection for devotees; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I cannot express properly; [[synonyms:g:govindere]]---to Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---He said; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---upon Raghunātha; [[synonyms:d:dayā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being very merciful.

===== Translation =====

**I cannot properly express the affection of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for His devotees. Being merciful toward Raghunātha dāsa, the Lord spoke as follows to Govinda.**
