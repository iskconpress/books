====== CC Antya 6.40 ======

===== Text =====

> daḍira bandhane tāṅre rākhibā ke-mate?
> janma-dātā-pitā nāre 'prārabdha' khaṇḍāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daḍira]] [[synonyms:b:bandhane]]---by bonds of ropes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:r:rākhibā]]---will you keep; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:m:mate]]---how; [[synonyms:j:janma]]-[[synonyms:d:dātā]] [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---the father who begets the child; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---is not able; [[synonyms:p:prārabdha]]---the reaction of previous activities; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāite]]---to nullify.

===== Translation =====

**"How then could we keep this boy home by binding him with ropes? It is not possible even for one's father to nullify the reactions of one's past activities.**
