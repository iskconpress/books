====== CC Antya 6.299 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-vitasti dui-vastra, piṅḍā eka-khāni
> svarūpa dilena kuṅjā ānibāre pāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:v:vitasti]]---about six inches long; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:v:vastra]]---two cloths; [[synonyms:p:piṅḍā]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:k:khāni]]---one wooden platform; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:d:dilena]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī delivered; [[synonyms:k:kuṅjā]]---a jug; [[synonyms:ā:ānibāre]] [[synonyms:p:pāni]]---for bringing water.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara gave Raghunātha dāsa two cloths, each about six inches long, a wooden platform and a jug in which to keep water.**
