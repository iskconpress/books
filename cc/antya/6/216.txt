====== CC Antya 6.216 ======

===== Text =====

> siṁha-dvāre annārthī vaiṣṇave dekhiyā
> pasārira ṭhāñi anna dena kṛpā ta' kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the Siṁha gate; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:a:arthī]]---in need of some eatables; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇave]]---Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:pasārira]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from the shopkeepers; [[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:d:dena]]---deliver some eatables; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---out of mercy.

===== Translation =====

**If they see a Vaiṣṇava standing at the Siṁha-dvāra begging alms, out of mercy they arrange with the shopkeepers to give him something to eat.**
