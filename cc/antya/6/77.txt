====== Cc. Antya 6.77 ======

===== Text =====

> sakala-lokera ciḍā pūrṇa yabe ha-ila
> dhyāne tabe prabhu mahāprabhure ānila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakala]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---full; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was; [[synonyms:d:dhyāne]]---in meditation; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]] [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**When chipped rice had been served to everyone, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, in meditation, brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
