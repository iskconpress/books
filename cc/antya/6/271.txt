====== CC Antya 6.271 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata nimantraṇa varṣa dui kailā
> pāche raghunātha nimantraṇa chāḍi' dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:v:varṣa]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]---for two years; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---continued; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---at the end; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave up.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa continued to invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in this way for two years, but at the end of the second year he stopped.**
