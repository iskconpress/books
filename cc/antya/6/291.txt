====== CC Antya 6.291 ======

===== Text =====

> govardhana-śilā prabhu hṛdaye-netre dhare
> kabhu nāsāya ghrāṇa laya, kabhu śire kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:ś:śilā]]---the stone from Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---on the heart; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---on the eyes; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---puts; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:n:nāsāya]]---with the nose; [[synonyms:g:ghrāṇa]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---smells; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:ś:śire]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---He puts on His head.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord would put the stone to His heart or sometimes to His eyes. Sometimes He would smell it with His nose and sometimes place it on His head.**
