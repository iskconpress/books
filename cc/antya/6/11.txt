====== CC Antya 6.11 ======

===== Text =====

> ei dui janāra saubhāgya kahana nā yāya
> prabhura 'antaraṅga' bali' yāṅre loke gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:janāra]]---of these two personalities; [[synonyms:s:saubhāgya]]---fortune; [[synonyms:k:kahana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---cannot be described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:antaraṅga]]---very intimate and confidential associates; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---as; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---whom; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---say.

===== Translation =====

**To describe the fortunate position of Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī is extremely difficult. They were renowned as intimately confidential friends of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
