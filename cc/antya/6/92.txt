====== CC Antya 6.92 ======

===== Text =====

> yata dravya lañā āise, saba mūlya kari' laya
> tāra dravya mūlya diyā tāhāre khāoyāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---all materials; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---come; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mūlya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---Raghunātha purchased; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---of their goods; [[synonyms:m:mūlya]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving the price; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]] [[synonyms:k:khāoyāya]]---fed them.

===== Translation =====

**As they came, bringing all kinds of food, Raghunātha dāsa purchased it all. He gave them the price for their goods and later fed them the very same food.**
