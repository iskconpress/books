====== Cc. Antya 6.190 ======

===== Text =====

> aṅganete dūre rahi' karena praṇipāta
> mukunda-datta kahe,---'ei āila raghunātha'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅganete]]---in the courtyard; [[synonyms:d:dūre]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---keeping himself at a distant place; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:p:praṇipāta]]---offered his obeisances; [[synonyms:m:mukunda]]-[[synonyms:d:datta]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Mukunda Datta said; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---has come; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Staying at a distant place in the courtyard, he fell down to offer obeisances. Then Mukunda Datta said, "Here is Raghunātha."**
