====== Cc. Antya 6.201 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunāthera kṣīṇatā-mālinya dekhiyā
> svarūpere kahena prabhu kṛpārdra-citta hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]]---of Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kṣīṇatā]]---thinness; [[synonyms:m:mālinya]]---dirty condition of the body; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpere]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---said to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---out of mercy; [[synonyms:ā:ārdra]]---melted; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---heart; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Raghunātha dāsa skinny and dirty because of having traveled for twelve days and fasted, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, His heart melting due to causeless mercy, spoke to Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
