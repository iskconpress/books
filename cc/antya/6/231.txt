====== Cc. Antya 6.231 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura āge svarūpa nivedilā āra dine
> raghunātha nivedaya prabhura caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:n:nivedilā]]---submitted; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the next day; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]] [[synonyms:n:nivedaya]]---Raghunātha dāsa inquires; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī submitted to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Raghunātha dāsa has this to say at Your lotus feet.**
