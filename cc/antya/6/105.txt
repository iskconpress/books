====== CC Antya 6.105 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛtyera mādhurī kebā varṇibāre pāre
> mahāprabhu āise yei nṛtya dekhibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛtyera]] [[synonyms:m:mādhurī]]---the sweetness of the dancing; [[synonyms:k:kebā]]---who; [[synonyms:v:varṇibāre]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can describe; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu comes; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see.

===== Translation =====

**No one can properly describe the sweetness of Lord Nityānanda's dancing. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally comes to see it.**
