====== CC Antya 6.289 ======

===== Text =====

> pārśve gāṅthā guñjā-mālā, govardhana-śilā
> dui vastu mahāprabhura āge āni' dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pārśve]]---on one side; [[synonyms:g:gāṅthā]]---strung together; [[synonyms:g:guñjā]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---the garland of small conchshells; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:ś:śilā]]---the stone from Govardhana; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---two things; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āni']] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---presented.

===== Translation =====

**He presented Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu these two items---the garland of conchshells and the stone from Govardhana Hill.**
