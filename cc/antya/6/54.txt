====== Cc. Antya 6.54 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mahotsava'-nāma śuni' brāhmaṇa-sajjana
> āsite lāgila loka asaṅkhya-gaṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahotsava]]---festival; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---brāhmaṇas and other gentlemen; [[synonyms:ā:āsite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to pour in; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇana]]---in numerable.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as they heard that a festival was going to be held, all kinds of brāhmaṇas and other gentlemen began to arrive. Thus there were innumerable people.**
