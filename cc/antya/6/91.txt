====== CC Antya 6.91 ======

===== Text =====

> mahotsava śuni' pasāri nānā-grāma haite
> ciḍā, dadhi, sandeśa, kalā ānila vecite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahotsava]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing about this festival; [[synonyms:p:pasāri]]---the shopkeepers; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]---various villages; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:s:sandeśa]]---sweetmeats; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]---bananas; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought; [[synonyms:v:vecite]]---to sell.

===== Translation =====

**When the shopkeepers of many other villages heard about the festival, they arrived there to sell chipped rice, yogurt, sweetmeats and bananas.**
