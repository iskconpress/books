====== CC Antya 6.304 ======

===== Text =====

> "aṣṭa-kauḍira khājā-sandeśa kara samarpaṇa
> śraddhā kari' dile, sei amṛtera sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:k:kauḍira]]---costing eight //kauḍis//; [[synonyms:k:khājā]]-[[synonyms:s:sandeśa]]---//khājā// and //sandeśa// sweetmeats; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---offer; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with love and faith; [[synonyms:d:dile]]---if you offer; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---just like nectar.

===== Translation =====

**"Offer the Govardhana stone eight kauḍis worth of the first-class sweetmeats known as khājā and sandeśa. If you offer them with faith and love, they will be just like nectar."**
