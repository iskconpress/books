====== CC Antya 6.70 ======

===== Text =====

> keha upare, keha tale, keha gaṅgā-tīre
> biśa-jana tina-ṭhāñi pariveśana kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---some on the platform; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:t:tale]]---some at the base of the platform; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---some on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:b:biśa]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---twenty men; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in three places; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---distributed.

===== Translation =====

**Thus some sat on the platform, some at the base of the platform, and some on the bank of the Ganges, and they were all supplied two pots each by the twenty men who distributed the food.**
