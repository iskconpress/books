====== Cc. Antya 6.268 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha-dāsa aṅgīkāra nā karila
> dravya lañā dui-jana tāhāṅi rahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---did not accept; [[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the wealth; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]] [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained there.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa did not accept the money and men sent by his father. Therefore a servant and the brāhmaṇa stayed there with the money.**
