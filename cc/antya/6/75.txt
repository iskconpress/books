====== CC Antya 6.75 ======

===== Text =====

> gopa-jāti āmi bahu gopa-gaṇa saṅge
> āmi sukha pāi ei pulina-bhojana-raṅge"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---belonging to the community of cowherd boys; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---cowherd boys; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---become very happy; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pulina]]---by the riverside; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the enjoyment of eating.

===== Translation =====

**"I belong to a community of cowherd boys, and therefore I generally have many cowherd associates with Me. I am happy when we eat together in a picnic like this by the sandy bank of the river."**
