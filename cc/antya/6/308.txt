====== CC Antya 6.308 ======

===== Text =====

> ānande raghunāthera bāhya vismaraṇa
> kāya-mane sevilena gaurāṅga-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]]---of Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]] [[synonyms:v:vismaraṇa]]---forgetting everything external; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---by mind and body; [[synonyms:s:sevilena]]---served; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa's transcendental bliss was boundless. Forgetting everything external, he served the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with his body and mind.**
