====== CC Antya 6.176 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā tāṅra sevaka rakṣaka tāṅre nā dekhiyā
> tāṅra guru-pāśe vārtā puchilena giyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here, at his home; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servant; [[synonyms:r:rakṣaka]]---watchman; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---not seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---from his spiritual master; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news; [[synonyms:p:puchilena]]---inquired; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**At the house of Raghunātha dāsa, the servant and watchman, not seeing him there, immediately went to inquire about him from his spiritual master, Yadunandana Ācārya.**
