====== CC Antya 6.50 ======

===== Text =====

> "nikaṭe nā āisa, corā, bhāga' dūre dūre
> āji lāg pāñāchi, daṇḍimu tomāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---nearby; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ā:āisa]]---you do not come; [[synonyms:c:corā]]---thief; [[synonyms:b:bhāga']]---you go away; [[synonyms:d:dūre]] [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---a long distance; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:l:lāg]] [[synonyms:p:pāñāchi]]---I have caught; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍimu]] [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---I shall punish you.

===== Translation =====

**"You are just like a thief, for instead of coming near, you stay away at a distant place. Now that I have captured you, I shall punish you.**
