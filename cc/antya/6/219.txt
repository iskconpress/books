====== CC Antya 6.219 ======

===== Text =====

> keha chatre māgi' khāya, yebā kichu pāya
> keha rātre bhikṣā lāgi' siṁha-dvāre raya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---some; [[synonyms:c:chatre]]---at the almshouse; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---begging; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---eat; [[synonyms:y:yeba]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---little; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---they receive; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---some; [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for begging alms; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]] [[synonyms:r:raya]]---stand at the gate known as Siṁha-dvāra.

===== Translation =====

**It is a custom for some Vaiṣṇavas to beg from the charity booths and eat whatever they obtain, whereas others stand at night at the Siṁha-dvāra gate, begging alms from the servants.**
