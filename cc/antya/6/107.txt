====== CC Antya 6.107 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojane vasilā prabhu nija-gaṇa lañā
> mahāprabhura āsana ḍāhine pātiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---to eat; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with His own personal associates; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---sitting place; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāhine]] [[synonyms:p:pātiyā]]---setting on the right side.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda Prabhu sat down for supper with His personal associates and made a sitting place on His right side for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
