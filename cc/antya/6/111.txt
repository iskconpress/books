====== CC Antya 6.111 ======

===== Text =====

> rāghava-ṭhākurera prasāda amṛtera sāra
> mahāprabhu yāhā khāite āise bāra bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurera]]---of Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---food offered to the Deity; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of nectar; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---which; [[synonyms:k:khāite]]---to eat; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---came; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**The food prepared and offered to the Deity by Rāghava Paṇḍita was like the essence of nectar. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came there again and again to eat such prasāda.**
