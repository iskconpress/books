====== CC Antya 6.207 ======

===== Text =====

> "pathe iṅha kariyāche bahuta laṅghana
> kata-dina kara ihāra bhāla santarpaṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:i:iṅha]]---this Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]]---has done; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---much; [[synonyms:l:laṅghana]]---fasting and difficult endeavor; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---for some days; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---good; [[synonyms:s:santarpaṇa]]---attention.

===== Translation =====

**"On the way, Raghunātha dāsa has fasted and undergone hardships for many days. Therefore, take good care of him for some days so that he may eat to his satisfaction."**
