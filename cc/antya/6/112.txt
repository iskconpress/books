====== CC Antya 6.112 ======

===== Text =====

> pāka kari' rāghava yabe bhoga lāgāya
> mahāprabhura lāgi' bhoga pṛthak bāḍaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after cooking; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava paṇḍita; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:l:lāgāya]]---offers food to the Deity; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---offering; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separate; [[synonyms:b:bāḍaya]]---arranges.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāghava Paṇḍita offered the food to the Deity after cooking, he would make a separate offering for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
