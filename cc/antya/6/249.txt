====== CC Antya 6.249 ======

===== Text =====

> se manuṣya śivānanda-senere puchila
> "mahāprabhura sthāne eka 'vairāgī' dekhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---that messenger; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:senere]]---from Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vairāgī]]---a person in the renounced order; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---did you see.

===== Translation =====

**That man inquired from Śivānanda Sena, "Did you see anyone in the renounced order at the residence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu?**
