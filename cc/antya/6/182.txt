====== CC Antya 6.182 ======

===== Text =====

> patrī diyā śivānande vārtā puchila
> śivānanda kahe,---'teṅha ethā nā āila'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patrī]]---letter; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering; [[synonyms:ś:śivānande]]---unto Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śivānanda said; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---did not come.

===== Translation =====

**After delivering the letter, the men inquired from Śivānanda Sena about Raghunātha dāsa, but Śivānanda Sena replied, "He did not come here."**
