====== CC Antya 6.269 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe raghunātha kari' aneka yatana
> māse dui-dina kailā prabhura nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:y:yatana]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:m:māse]]---every month; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---two days; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---he made; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Raghunātha dāsa invited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his house with great attention for two days every month.**
