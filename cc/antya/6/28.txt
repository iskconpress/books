====== Cc. Antya 6.28 ======

===== Text =====

> pālaka hañā pālyere tāḍite nā yuyāya
> tumi sarva-śāstra jāna jindā-pīra'-prāya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pālaka]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a maintainer; [[synonyms:p:pālyere]]---the person who is maintained; [[synonyms:t:tāḍite]]---to punish; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---is not good; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---all scriptures; [[synonyms:j:jāna]]---know; [[synonyms:j:jindā]]-[[synonyms:p:pīra]]---a living saintly person; [[synonyms:p:prāya]]---just like.

===== Translation =====

**"For a maintainer to punish the person he maintains is not good. You are expert in all the scriptures. Indeed, you are like a living saint."**
