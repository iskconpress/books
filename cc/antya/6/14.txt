====== Cc. Antya 6.14 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura śikṣāte teṅho nija-ghare yāya
> markaṭa-vairāgya chāḍi' hailā 'viṣayi-prāya'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ś:śikṣāte]]---by the instruction of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:y:yā]] [[synonyms:y:ya]]---returned to his home; [[synonyms:m:markaṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---monkey renunciation; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:v:viṣayi]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---like a pounds-and-shillings man.

===== Translation =====

**Instead of becoming a so-called renunciate, Raghunātha dāsa, foIlowing the instructions of the Lord, returned home and played exactly like a pounds-and-shillings man.**
