====== CC Antya 6.213 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata rahe teṅha svarūpa-caraṇe
> govinda prasāda tāṅre dila pañca dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---under the shelter of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of the food of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:p:pañca]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---for five days.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa stayed under the care of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, and Govinda supplied him remnants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's food for five days.**
