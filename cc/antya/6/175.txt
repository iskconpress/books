====== Cc. Antya 6.175 ======

===== Text =====

> upavāsī dekhi' gopa dugdha āni' dilā
> sei dugdha pāna kari' paḍiyā rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upavāsī]]---fasting; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]---the milkman; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---milk; [[synonyms:a:ani']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---that milk; [[synonyms:p:pāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---drinking; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---lying down; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---he remained.

===== Translation =====

**When the milkman saw that Raghunātha dāsa was fasting, he gave him some milk. Raghunātha dāsa drank the milk and lay down to rest there for the night.**
