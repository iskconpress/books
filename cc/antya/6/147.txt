====== CC Antya 6.147 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅre niṣedhilā,---"prabhure ebe nā kahibā
> nija-ghare yābena yabe tabe nivedibā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhilā]]---he forbade; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahibā]]---do not speak; [[synonyms:n:nila]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to His home; [[synonyms:y:yābena]]---will return; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:n:nivedibā]]---kindly inform Him.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa admonished the treasurer, "Do not speak about this to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu now, but when He returns home, kindly inform Him about this presentation."**
