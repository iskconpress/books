====== CC Antya 6.136 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-pāda-padma-gandha yei jana pāya
> brahmaloka-ādi-sukha tāṅre nāhi bhāya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]]---of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---the fragrance; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---of Brahmaloka; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and so on; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---does not appear valuable.

===== Translation =====

**"One who experiences the fragrance of the lotus feet of Lord Kṛṣṇa does not even value the standard of happiness available in Brahmaloka, the topmost planet. And what to speak of heavenly happiness?**
