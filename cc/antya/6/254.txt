====== Cc. Antya 6.254 ======

===== Text =====

> parama vairāgya tāra, nāhi bhakṣya-paridhāna
> yaiche taiche āhāra kari' rākhaye parāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]]---supreme; [[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---renunciation; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]]---eating; [[synonyms:p:paridhāna]]---dressing; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]] [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---somehow or other; [[synonyms:ā:āhāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---eating; [[synonyms:r:rākhaye]] [[synonyms:p:parāṇa]]---keeps life.

===== Translation =====

**"He is in the supreme order of renounced life. Indeed, he does not care about eating or dressing. Somehow or other he eats and maintains his life.**
