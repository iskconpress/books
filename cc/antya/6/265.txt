====== CC Antya 6.265 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda yaiche sei manuṣye kahilā
> karṇapūra sei-rūpe śloka varṇilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---unto the; [[synonyms:m:manuṣye]]---messenger; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---said; [[synonyms:k:karṇapūra]]---the great poet Kavi-karṇapūra; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---in that way; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:v:varṇilā]]---composed verses.

===== Translation =====

**In these verses, the great poet Kavi-karṇapūra gives the same information that Śivānanda Sena conveyed to the messenger from Raghunātha dāsa's father.**
