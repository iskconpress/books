====== Cc. Antya 6.100 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ nityānandera vihāra
> 'ciḍā-dadhi-mahotsava'-nāme khyāti yāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:n:nityānandera]] [[synonyms:v:vihāra]]---the pastimes of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]-[[synonyms:d:dadhi]]-[[synonyms:m:mahotsava]]---the festival of eating chipped rice and yogurt; [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---of the name; [[synonyms:k:khyāti]]---the fame; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described the pastimes of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu in relation to the celebrated festival of chipped rice and yogurt.**
