====== Cc. Antya 6.42 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe raghunātha kichu vicārilā mane
> nityānanda-gosāñira pāśa calilā āra dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghu nātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:v:vicārilā]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---considered within his mind; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]] [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---unto Nityānanda Gosāñi; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---the next day.

===== Translation =====

**Then Raghunātha dāsa considered something in his mind, and the next day he went to Nityānanda Gosāñi.**
