====== Cc. Antya 6.60 ======

===== Text =====

> cabutarā-upare yata prabhura nija-gaṇe
> baḍa baḍa loka vasilā maṇḍalī-racane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cabutarā]]-[[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the raised platform; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---very intimate associates of the Lord; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---big, big people; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍalī]]-[[synonyms:r:racane]]---in a circle.

===== Translation =====

**On that platform, all the most important associates of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu, as well as other important men, sat down in a circle around the Lord.**
