====== Cc. Antya 6.293 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata tina-vatsara śilā-mālā dharilā
> tuṣṭa hañā śilā-mālā raghunāthe dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---for three years; [[synonyms:ś:śilā]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---the stone and the garland of conchshells; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---He kept; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---when He became very happy; [[synonyms:ś:śilā]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---the stone and the garland; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---to Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---He delivered.

===== Translation =====

**For three years He kept the stone and garland. Then, greatly satisfied by the behavior of Raghunātha dāsa, the Lord delivered both of them to him.**
