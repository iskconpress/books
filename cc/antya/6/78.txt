====== CC Antya 6.78 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu āilā dekhi' nitāi uṭhilā
> tāṅre lañā sabāra ciḍā dekhite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:n:nitāi]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:u:uṭhilā]]---stood up; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with Him; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to see.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu stood up. They then saw how the others were enjoying the chipped rice with yogurt and condensed milk.**
