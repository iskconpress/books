====== CC Antya 6.20 ======

===== Text =====

> rāja-ghare kaiphiyat diyā ujīre ānila
> hiraṇya-dāsa palāila, raghunāthere bāndhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to the government treasury; [[synonyms:k:kaiphiyat]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---sending a confidential account; [[synonyms:u:ujīre]] [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought the minister in charge; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇya]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]] [[synonyms:p:palāila]]---Hiraṇya dāsa fled; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthere]] [[synonyms:b:bāndhila]]---he arrested Raghunātha dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**After sending a confidential account to the government treasury, the caudhurī brought the minister in charge. The caudhurī came, wanting to arrest Hiraṇya dāsa, but Hiraṇya dāsa had left home. Therefore the caudhurī arrested Raghunātha dāsa.**
