====== CC Antya 6.16 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mathurā haite prabhu āilā',---vārtā yabe pāilā
> prabhu-pāśa calibāre udyoga karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Mathurā; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has come back; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---message; [[synonyms:y:yabe]] [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---when he received; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calibāre]]---to go; [[synonyms:u:udyoga]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made an endeavor.

===== Translation =====

**When he received a message that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had returned from Mathurā City, Raghunātha dāsa endeavored to go to the lotus feet of the Lord.**
