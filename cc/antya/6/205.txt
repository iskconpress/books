====== CC Antya 6.205 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,---'mahāprabhura ye ājñā haila'
> eta kahi' raghunāthe punaḥ āliṅgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara said; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there is; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgila]]---he embraced.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī accepted Raghunātha dāsa, saying, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, whatever You order is accepted." He then embraced Raghunātha dāsa again.**
