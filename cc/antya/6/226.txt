====== Cc. Antya 6.226 ======

===== Text =====

> vairāgīra kṛtya---sadā nāma-saṅkīrtana
> śāka-patra-phala-mūle udara-bharaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vairāgīra]]---of a person in the renounced order; [[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---duty; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śāka]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:p:patra]]---leaves; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---fruit; [[synonyms:m:mūle]]---by roots; [[synonyms:u:udara]]-[[synonyms:b:bharaṇa]]---filling the belly.

===== Translation =====

**"The duty of a person in the renounced order is to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra always. He should satisfy his belly with whatever vegetables, leaves, fruits and roots are available.**
