====== Cc. Antya 6.89 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-prabhāva-kṛpā jānibe kon jana?
> mahāprabhu āni' karā ya pulina-bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---influence and mercy; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---can know; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---who; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāya]] [[synonyms:p:pulina]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---induces Him to eat on the river bank.

===== Translation =====

**Who can understand the influence and mercy of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu? He is so powerful that He induced Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to come eat chipped rice on the bank of the Ganges.**
