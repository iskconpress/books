====== CC Antya 6.52 ======

===== Text =====

> sei-kṣaṇe nija-loka pāṭhāilā grāme
> bhakṣya-dravya loka saba grāma haite āne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---immediately; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---his servants; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---he sent to the nearby village; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]]---eatables; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all the persons; [[synonyms:g:grāma]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the village; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---bring.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa immediately sent his own men to the village to purchase all kinds of eatables and bring them back.**
