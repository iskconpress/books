====== CC Antya 6.90 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rāmadāsādi gopa premāviṣṭa hailā
> gaṅgā-tīre 'yamunā-pulina' jñāna kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmadāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrī Rāmadāsa; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]---the cowherd boys; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became absorbed in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---the bank of the Ganges River; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]-[[synonyms:p:pulina]]---the bank of the Yamunā River; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---they thought.

===== Translation =====

**All the confidential devotees who were cowherd boys, headed by Śrī Rāmadāsa, were absorbed in ecstatic love. They thought the bank of the Ganges to be the bank of the Yamunā.**
