====== Cc. Antya 6.155 ======

===== Text =====

> sei haite abhyantare nā karena gamana
> bāhire durgā-maṇḍape yāñā karena śayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from that day; [[synonyms:a:abhyantare]]---in the interior rooms; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---did not go; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:d:durgā]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍape]]---to the place where Durgā worship was performed; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---he sleeps.

===== Translation =====

**From that day on, he did not go into the interior section of the house. Instead, he would sleep on the Durgā-maṇḍapa [the place where mother Durgā was worshiped].**
