====== Cc. Antya 6.240 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' raghunātha vandilā caraṇa
> mahāprabhu kailā tāṅre kṛpā-āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghu nātha dāsa; [[synonyms:v:vandilā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---offered prayers to the lotus feet; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing out of mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Having heard this, Raghunātha dāsa offered prayers at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord, with great mercy, embraced him.**
