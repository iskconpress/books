====== CC Antya 6.59 ======

===== Text =====

> dhuti pari' prabhu yadi piṇḍāte vasilā
> sāta-kuṇḍī vipra tāṅra āgete dharilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhuti]] [[synonyms:p:pari']]---putting on a new cloth; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍāte]] [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat on a high platform; [[synonyms:s:sāta]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍī]]---the seven big, big earthen pots; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---the brāhmaṇa,; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ā:āgete]]---in front of Him; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---placed.

===== Translation =====

**After Nityānanda Prabhu had changed His cloth for a new one and sat on a raised platform, the brāhmaṇa brought before Him the seven huge pots.**
