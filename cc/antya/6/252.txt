====== CC Antya 6.252 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpera sthāne tāre kariyāchena samarpaṇa
> prabhura bhakta-gaṇera teṅho haya prāṇa-sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpera]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---Lord Caitanya has given charge of; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---the life; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has placed him under the charge of Svarūpa Dāmodara. Raghunātha dāsa has become just like the life of all the Lord's devotees.**
