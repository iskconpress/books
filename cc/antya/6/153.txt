====== CC Antya 6.153 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-śata mudrā āra soṇā tolā-dvaya
> paṇḍitera āge dila kariyā vinaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---one hundred coins; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:s:soṇā]]---gold; [[synonyms:t:tolā]]-[[synonyms:d:dvaya]]---two //tolās//; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---presented; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---with great humility.

===== Translation =====

**With great humility, Raghunātha dāsa placed one hundred gold coins and about two tolās of gold before Rāghava Paṇḍita for all the other devotees.**
