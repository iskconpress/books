====== CC Antya 6.124 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-citte bhakta-gṛhe sadā avasthāna
> kabhu gupta, kabhu vyakta, svatantra bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:c:citte]]---in the heart of a devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the house of a devotee; [[synonyms:s:sadā]] [[synonyms:a:avasthāna]]---always resides; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:g:gupta]]---sometimes hidden; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:v:vyakta]]---sometimes manifest; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---fully independent; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead always resides either in the heart or in the home of a devotee. This fact is sometimes hidden and sometimes manifest, for the Supreme Personality of Godhead is fully independent.**
