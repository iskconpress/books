====== CC Antya 6.302 ======

===== Text =====

> jala-tulasīra sevāya tāṅra yata sukhodaya
> ṣoḍaśopacāra-pūjāya tata sukha naya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:t:tulasīra]] [[synonyms:s:sevāya]]---by worshiping with water and //tulasī//; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as much as; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---rise of transcendental happiness; [[synonyms:ṣ:ṣoḍaśa]]-[[synonyms:u:upacāra]]-[[synonyms:p:pūjāya]]---by worshiping with sixteen kinds of paraphernalia; [[synonyms:t:tata]]---so much; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:n:naya]]---is not.

===== Translation =====

**The amount of transcendental bliss that Raghunātha dāsa enjoyed simply by offering water and tulasī is impossible to achieve even if one worships the Deity with sixteen kinds of paraphernalia.**
