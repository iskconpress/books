====== Cc. Antya 6.305 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe aṣṭa-kauḍira khājā kare samarpaṇa
> svarūpa-ājñāya govinda tāhā kare samādhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:k:kauḍira]]---costing eight kauḍis; [[synonyms:k:khājā]]---the sweetmeat named //khājā//; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---offers; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---by the order of Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---arranges.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa then began offering the costly sweetmeats known as khājā, which Govinda, following the order of Svarūpa Dāmodara, would supply.**
