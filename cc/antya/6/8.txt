====== CC Antya 6.8 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra sukha-hetu saṅge rahe dui janā
> kṛṣṇa-rasa-śloka-gīte karena sāntvanā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for His happiness; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in His association; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remain; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:janā]]---two personalities; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of the transcendental mellows of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:g:gīte]]---by songs; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:s:sāntvanā]]---they pacified.

===== Translation =====

**Two people-Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī-stayed with the Lord to pacify Him by reciting various verses about Kṛṣṇa's pastimes and by singing appropriate songs for His satisfaction.**
