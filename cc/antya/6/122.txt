====== CC Antya 6.122 ======

===== Text =====

> rāghavera kṛpā raghunāthera upare
> dui bhāiera avaśiṣṭa pātra dilā tāṅre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāghavera]]---of Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---unto Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāiera]]---of the two brothers; [[synonyms:a:avaśiṣṭa]]---of remnants of food; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the dishes; [[synonyms:d:dilā]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---offered to him.

===== Translation =====

**Rāghava Paṇḍita, being very merciful toward Raghunātha dāsa, offered him the dishes with the remnants of food left by the two brothers.**
