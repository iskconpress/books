====== Cc. Antya 6.150 ======

===== Text =====

> "prabhura saṅge yata mahānta, bhṛtya āśrita jana
> pūjite cāhiye āmi sabāra caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mahānta]]---great devotees; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---servants; [[synonyms:ā:āśrita]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---subservient people; [[synonyms:p:pūjite]]---to worship; [[synonyms:c:cāhiye]]---want; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of all of them.

===== Translation =====

**"I want to give money," he said, "just to worship the lotus feet of all the great devotees, servants and subservants of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu.**
