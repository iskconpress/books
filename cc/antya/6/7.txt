====== CC Antya 6.7 ======

===== Text =====

> dine prabhu nānā-saṅge haya anya mana
> rātri-kāle bāḍe prabhura viraha-vedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dine]]---during the daytime; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:na]]-[[synonyms:n:na]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---by different association; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---diverted; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---His mind; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at night; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increase; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:viraha]]-[[synonyms:v:vedana]]---pangs of separation.

===== Translation =====

**Because the Lord associated with various devotees during the day, His mind was somewhat diverted, but at night the pangs of separation from Kṛṣṇa increased very rapidly.**
