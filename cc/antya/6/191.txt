====== Cc. Antya 6.191 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena,---'āisa;, teṅho dharilā caraṇa
> uṭhi' prabhu kṛpāya tāṅre kailā āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---the Lord said; [[synonyms:ā:āisa]]---come here; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---caught His lotus feet; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---standing up; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---out of mercy; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard these words, He immediately welcomed Raghunātha dāsa. "Come here," He said. Raghunātha dāsa then clasped the lotus feet of the Lord, but the Lord stood up and embraced him out of His causeless mercy.**
