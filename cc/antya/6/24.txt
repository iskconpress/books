====== CC Antya 6.24 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe raghunātha kichu cintilā upāya
> vinati kariyā kahe sei mleccha-pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:c:cintilā]]---thought of; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---means; [[synonyms:v:vinati]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---in great humility; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he says; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]---of that Mohammedan caudhurī; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the feet.

===== Translation =====

**While this was going on, Raghunātha dāsa thought of a tricky method of escape. Thus he humbly submitted this plea at the feet of the Mohammedan caudhurī.**
