====== Cc. Antya 6.117 ======

===== Text =====

> sugandhi sundara prasāda---mādhuryera sāra
> dui bhāi tāhā khāñā santoṣa apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:g:gandhi]]---fragrant; [[synonyms:s:sundara]]---beautiful; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---food; [[synonyms:m:mādhuryera]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of all sweetness; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:khāñā]]---eating; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---very, very happy.

===== Translation =====

**Aromatic and pleasing to see, the food was the essence of all sweetness. Thus the two brothers, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, ate it with great satisfaction.**
