====== CC Antya 6.114 ======

===== Text =====

> dui bhāire rāghava āni' pariveśe
> yatna kari' khāoyāya, nā rahe avaśeṣe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāire]]---to the two brothers; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:p:pariveśe]]---distributed; [[synonyms:y:yatna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great attention; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāya]]---fed Them; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:r:rahe]] [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣe]]---there were no remnants.

===== Translation =====

**Rāghava Paṇḍita would bring and distribute prasāda to the two brothers, feeding Them with great attention. They ate everything, and therefore there were no remnants left.**
