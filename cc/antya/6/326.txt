====== Cc. Antya 6.326 ======

===== Text =====

> āpana-uddhāra ei raghunātha-dāsa
> 'gaurāṅga-stava-kalpa-vṛkṣe' kariyāchena prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:u:uddhāra]]---his personal deliverance; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---this Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:stava]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]]---in his poem known as //Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa//; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---has manifested.

===== Translation =====

**In his own poem known as the Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa, Raghunātha dāsa has described his personal deliverance.**
