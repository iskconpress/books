====== Cc. Antya 6.72 ======

===== Text =====

> ni-sakḍi nānā-mata prasāda ānila
> prabhure āge diyā bhakta-gaṇe bāṅṭi dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:ni]]-[[synonyms:s:sakḍi]]---food cooked in ghee; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---various types; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of the Lord's food; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---he brought; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---placing; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭi]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---distributed.

===== Translation =====

**He brought many other kinds of food cooked in ghee and offered to the Lord. This prasāda he first placed before Lord Nityānanda and then distributed among the devotees.**
