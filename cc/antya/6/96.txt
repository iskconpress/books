====== CC Antya 6.96 ======

===== Text =====

> puṣpa-mālā vipra āni' prabhu-gale dila
> candana āniyā prabhura sarvāṅge lepila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---a flower garland; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---one brāhmaṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:p:prabhugale]]---on the neck of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---placed; [[synonyms:c:candana]] [[synonyms:ā:āniyā]]---bringing sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:s:sarvāṅge]] [[synonyms:l:lepila]]---smeared all over the body.

===== Translation =====

**Then a brāhmaṇa brought a flower garland, placed the garland on Nityānanda Prabhu's neck and smeared sandalwood pulp all over His body.**
