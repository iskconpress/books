====== Cc. Antya 6.119 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa ākaṇṭha bhariyā karila bhojana
> 'hari' dhvani kari' uṭhi' kailā ācamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ākaṇṭha]]---up to the neck; [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---filling; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---took prasāda; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---chanting of the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---washed their mouths and hands.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees took prasāda, filling themselves to the brim. Thereafter, chanting the holy name of Hari, they stood up and washed their hands and mouths.**
