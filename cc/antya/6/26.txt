====== CC Antya 6.26 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu kalaha, kabhu prīti---ihāra niścaya nāi
> kāli punaḥ tina bhāi ha-ibā eka-ṭhāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:kalaha]]---fight; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---very intimate friendly behavior; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of these things; [[synonyms:n:niścayanāi]]---there is no certainty; [[synonyms:k:kāli]]---the next day; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---three brothers; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibā]]---will be; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---at one place.

===== Translation =====

**"Sometimes brothers fight among themselves, and sometimes they have very friendly dealings. There is no certainty when such changes will take place. Thus I am sure that although today you are fighting, tomorrow you three brothers will be sitting together in peace.**
