====== Cc. Antya 6.255 ======

===== Text =====

> daśa-daṇḍa rātri gele 'puṣpāñjali' dekhiyā
> siṁha-dvāre khāḍā haya āhāra lāgiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---ten //daṇḍas// (240 minutes); [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---night; [[synonyms:g:gele]]---having gone; [[synonyms:p:puṣpāñjali]]---the puṣpāñjali performance; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the Siṁha-dvāra gate; [[synonyms:k:khāḍā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---stands; [[synonyms:ā:āhāra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---to get some alms for eating.

===== Translation =====

**"After ten daṇḍas [four hours] of the night have passed and Raghunātha dāsa has seen the performance of puṣpāñjali, he stands at the Siṁha-dvāra gate to beg some alms to eat.**
