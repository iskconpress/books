====== Cc. Antya 6.230 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura āge kathā-mātra nā kahe raghunātha
> svarūpa-govinda-dvārā kahā ya nija-vāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---any speaking; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---does not say; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---through Govinda and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kahāya]]---he informs; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:v:vāt]]---his intention.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa never even spoke a word before the Lord. Instead, he informed the Lord of his desires through Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Govinda.**
