====== Cc. Antya 6.233 ======

===== Text =====

> hāsi' mahāprabhu raghunāthere kahila
> "tomāra upadeṣṭā kari' svarūpere dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthere]]---to Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:u:upadeṣṭā]]---instructor; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---as; [[synonyms:s:svarūpere]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---I have appointed Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**Smiling, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told Raghunātha dāsa, "I have already appointed Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī as your instructor.**
