====== CC Antya 6.98 ======

===== Text =====

> mālā-candana-tāmbūla śeṣa ye āchila
> śrī-haste prabhu tāhā sabākāre bāṅṭi' dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]-[[synonyms:t:tāmbūla]]---the flower garlands, sandalwood pulp and betel; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]] [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:ā:āchila]]---whatever remained; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---in His own hand; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---to all; [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭi']] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---distributed.

===== Translation =====

**With His own hands Lord Nityānanda Prabhu distributed to all the devotees whatever flower garlands, sandalwood pulp and betel nuts remained.**
