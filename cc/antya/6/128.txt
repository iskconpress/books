====== CC Antya 6.128 ======

===== Text =====

> "adhama, pāmara mui hīna jīvādhama!
> mora icchā haya---pāṅa caitanya-caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adhama]]---the most fallen; [[synonyms:p:pāmara]]---the most sinful; [[synonyms:m:mui]]---I; [[synonyms:h:hīna]]---condemned; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:a:adhama]]---the lowest of all living beings; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:pāṅa]]---I can get; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the shelter of the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**"I am the lowest of men, the most sinful, fallen and condemned. Nevertheless, I desire to attain the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
