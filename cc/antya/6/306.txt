====== CC Antya 6.306 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha sei śilā-mālā yabe pāilā
> gosāñira abhiprāya ei bhāvanā karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śilā]]---that stone; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---garland; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---he got; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:abhiprāya]]---intention; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhāvanā]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---he thought.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghunātha dāsa received from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu the stone and the garland of conchshells, he could understand the Lord's intention. Thus he thought as follows.**
