====== CC Antya 6.273 ======

===== Text =====

> 'raghu kene āmāya nimantraṇa chāḍi' dila?'
> svarūpa kahe,---"mane kichu vicāra karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghu]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:ā:āmāya]]---to Me; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---has stopped; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara replied; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within his mind; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---he has thought.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord asked, "Why has Raghunātha dāsa stopped inviting Me?" Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "He must have reconsidered something in his mind.**
