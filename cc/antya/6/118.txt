====== CC Antya 6.118 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojane vasite raghunāthe kahe sarva-jana
> paṇḍita kahe,---iṅha pāche karibe bhojana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---to eat; [[synonyms:v:vasite]]---to sit down; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---unto Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---requested; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---everyone; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita said; [[synonyms:i:iṅha]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:k:karibe]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---will take food.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees present requested Raghunātha dāsa to sit down and take prasāda, but Rāghava Paṇḍita told them, "He will take prasāda later."**
