====== CC Antya 6.253 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-dina kare teṅho nāma-saṅkīrtana
> kṣaṇa-mātra nāhi chāḍe prabhura caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---all day and night; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---even for a moment; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍe]]---does not give up; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**"He chants the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra all day and night. He never gives up the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, not even for a moment.**
