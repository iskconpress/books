====== Cc. Antya 6.82 ======

===== Text =====

> ki kariyā beḍāya,---ihā keha nāhi jāne
> mahāprabhura darśana pā ya kona bhāgyavāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing what; [[synonyms:b:beḍāya]]---walks through; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---no one could understand; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavāne]]---some fortunate men.

===== Translation =====

**No one could understand what Nityānanda Prabhu was doing as He walked about. Some, however, who were very fortunate, could see that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was also present.**
