====== CC Antya 6.167 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryera ghara ihāra pūrva-diśāte
> kahite śunite duṅhe cale sei pathe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---the house of Yadunandana Ācārya; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:d:diśāte]]---to the east; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---talking; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---listening; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---go; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on that path.

===== Translation =====

**East of the house of Raghunātha dāsa was the house of Yadunandana Ācārya. Yadunandana Ācārya and Raghunātha dāsa talked together as they went toward that house.**
