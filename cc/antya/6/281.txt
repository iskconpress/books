====== CC Antya 6.281 ======

===== Text =====

> kata dine raghunātha siṁha-dvāra chāḍilā
> chatre yāi' māgiyā khāite ārambha karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---after some days; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍilā]]---gave up standing at the gate known as Siṁha-dvāra; [[synonyms:c:chatre]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going to an alms booth; [[synonyms:m:māgiyā]]---begging; [[synonyms:k:khāite]]---to eat; [[synonyms:ā:ārambha]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---he began.

===== Translation =====

**After some days, Raghunātha dāsa gave up standing near the Siṁha-dvāra gate and instead began eating by begging alms from a booth for free distribution of food.**
