====== CC Antya 6.131 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra kṛpā vinā keha 'caitanya' nā pāya
> tumi kṛpā kaile tāṅre adhameha pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---Your mercy; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---cannot get; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---if You are merciful; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:a:adhameha]]---even the fallen soul; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---can get.

===== Translation =====

**"No one can attain the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu without Your mercy, but if You are merciful, even the lowest of men ca attain shelter at His lotus feet.**
