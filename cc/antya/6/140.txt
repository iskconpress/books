====== CC Antya 6.140 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛpā kari' kailā ciḍā-dugdha bhojana
> nṛtya dekhi' rātrye kailā prasāda bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by His causeless mercy; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]-[[synonyms:d:dugdha]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating of the chipped rice and milk; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing the dancing; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---ate the prasāda.

===== Translation =====

**"By His causeless mercy He ate the chipped rice and milk. Then, after seeing the dancing of the devotees at night, He took His supper.**
