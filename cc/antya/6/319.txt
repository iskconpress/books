====== CC Antya 6.319 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina svarūpa tāhā karite dekhilā
> hāsiyā tāhāra kichu māgiyā khāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---doing; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---saw; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:m:māgiyā]] [[synonyms:k:khāilā]]---he begged and ate.

===== Translation =====

**One day Svarūpa Dāmodara saw the activities of Raghunātha dāsa. Thus he smiled and asked for a small portion of that food and ate it.**
