====== CC Antya 6.121 ======

===== Text =====

> biḍā khāoyāilā, kailā caraṇa vandana
> bhakta-gaṇe dilā biḍā, mālya-candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:biḍā]] [[synonyms:k:khāoyāilā]]---he offered betel nuts; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---prayed to the lotus feet; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto the devotees; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:b:biḍā]]---betel nuts; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---garlands and sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**Rāghava Paṇḍita offered Them betel nuts and worshiped Their lotus feet. He also distributed betel nuts, flower garlands and sandalwood pulp to the devotees.**
