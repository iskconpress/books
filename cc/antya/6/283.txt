====== CC Antya 6.283 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,---"siṁha-dvāre duḥkha anubhaviyā
> chatre māgi' khāya madhyāhna-kāle giyā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara replied; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the Siṁha-dvāra gate; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:a:anubhaviyā]]---feeling unhappy; [[synonyms:c:chatre]]---at the charity booth; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---begging; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---he eats; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at midday; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "Raghunātha dāsa felt unhappy standing at the Siṁha-dvāra. Therefore he is now going at midday to beg alms from the charity booth."**
