====== CC Antya 6.68 ======

===== Text =====

> kona kona vipra upare sthāna nā pāñā
> dui holnāya ciḍā bhijāya gaṅgā-tīre giyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---brāhmaṇas; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the platform; [[synonyms:s:sthāna]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---not having gotten a place; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:h:holnāya]]---in two earthen pots; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]] [[synonyms:b:bhijāya]]---soak chipped rice; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**Some of the brāhmaṇas, not having gotten a place on the platform, went to the bank of the Ganges with their two earthen pots and soaked their chipped rice there.**
