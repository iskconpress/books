====== CC Antya 6.43 ======

===== Text =====

> pānihāṭi-grāme pāilā prabhura daraśana
> kīrtanīyā sevaka saṅge āra bahu-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pānihāṭi]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the village known as Pānihāṭi; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---got; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---the audience of Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyā]] [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---performers of saṅkīrtana and servants; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---many other persons.

===== Translation =====

**In the village of Pānihāṭi, Raghunātha dāsa obtained an interview with Nityānanda Prabhu, who was accompanied by many kīrtana performers, servants and others.**
