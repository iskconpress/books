====== CC Antya 6.125 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvatra 'vyāpaka' prabhura sadā sarvatra vāsa
> ihāte saṁśaya yāra, sei yāya nāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:v:vyāpaka]]---pervading; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence; [[synonyms:i:ihāte]]---about this; [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]]---doubt; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---becomes annihilated.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead is all-pervasive, and therefore He resides everywhere. Anyone who doubts this will be annihilated.**
