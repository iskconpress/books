====== Cc. Antya 6.148 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe rāghava-paṇḍita tāṅre ghare lañā gelā
> ṭhākura darśana karāñā mālā-candana dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---took to his home; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---after inducing him to see the Deity; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---a garland and sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**Thereupon, Rāghava Paṇḍita took Raghunātha dāsa to his home. After inducing him to see the Deity, he gave Raghunātha dāsa a garland and sandalwood pulp.**
