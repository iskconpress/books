====== CC Antya 6.69 ======

===== Text =====

> tīre sthāna nā pāñā āra kata jana
> jale nāmi' dadhi-ciḍā karaye bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank; [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---place; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---not having gotten; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:k:kata]]---some; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---persons; [[synonyms:j:jale]] [[synonyms:n:nāmi']]---getting down into the water; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]-[[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---yogurt and chipped rice; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---began to eat.

===== Translation =====

**Others, who could not get a place even on the bank of the Ganges, got down into the water and began eating their two kinds of chipped rice.**
