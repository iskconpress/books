====== Cc. Antya 6.123 ======

===== Text =====

> kahilā,---"caitanya gosāñi kariyāchena bhojana
> tāṅra śeṣa pāile, tomāra khaṇḍila bandhana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---he said; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---has eaten; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---His remnants; [[synonyms:p:pāile]]---if you take; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍila]]---will cease; [[synonyms:b:bandhana]]---bondage.

===== Translation =====

**He said, "Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has eaten this food. If you take His remnants, you will be released from the bondage of your family."**
