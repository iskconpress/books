====== CC Antya 6.303 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata kata dina karena pūjana
> tabe svarūpa-gosāñi tāṅre kahilā vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---for some days; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:p:pūjana]]---he worshiped; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---said some words.

===== Translation =====

**After Raghunātha dāsa had thus worshiped the govardhana-śilā for some time, Svarūpa Dāmodara one day spoke to him as follows.**
