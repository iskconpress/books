====== CC Antya 6.58 ======

===== Text =====

> āra ardheka ghanāvṛta-dugdhete chānila
> cāṅpā-kalā, cini, ghṛta, karpūra tāte dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:a:ardheka]]---the other half; [[synonyms:g:ghana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āvṛta]]---condensed; [[synonyms:d:dugdhete]]---in milk; [[synonyms:c:chānila]]---mixed; [[synonyms:c:cāṅpā]]-[[synonyms:k:kalā]]---a special type of banana; [[synonyms:c:cini]]---sugar; [[synonyms:g:ghṛta]]---clarified butter, //ghee//; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---camphor; [[synonyms:t:tāte]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---put into that.

===== Translation =====

**The other half was mixed with condensed milk and a special type of banana known as cāṅpā-kalā. Then sugar, clarified butter and camphor were added.**
