====== Cc. Antya 6.84 ======

===== Text =====

> āsana diyā mahāprabhure tāhāṅ vasāilā
> dui bhāi tabe ciḍā khāite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsana]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering a sitting place; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made sit; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice; [[synonyms:k:khāite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to eat.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu a place and had Him sit down. Then together the two brothers began eating chipped rice.**
