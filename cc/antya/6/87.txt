====== Cc. Antya 6.87 ======

===== Text =====

> 'hari' 'hari' bali' vaiṣṇava karaye bhojana
> pulina-bhojana sabāra ha-ila smaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---chanting Hari, Hari; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eat; [[synonyms:p:pulina]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating on the bank of the Yamunā; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]---everyone could remember.

===== Translation =====

**When all the Vaiṣṇavas were chanting the holy names"Hari, Hari" and eating, they remembered how Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma ate with Their companions the cowherd boys on the bank of the Yamunā.**
