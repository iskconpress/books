====== CC Antya 6.247 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāre pāṭhāite patrī pāṭhāila more
> jhāṅkarā ha-ite tomā nā pāñā gela ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---you; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāite]]---to send back; [[synonyms:p:patrī]]---letter; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]] [[synonyms:m:more]]---sent to me; [[synonyms:j:jhāṅkarā]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from Jhāṅkarā; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---you; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---not getting; [[synonyms:g:gela]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---returned home.

===== Translation =====

**"He wrote me a letter asking me to send you back, but when those ten men received no information about you, they returned home from Jhāṅkarā."**
