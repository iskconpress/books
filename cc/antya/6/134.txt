====== Cc. Antya 6.134 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' hāsi' kahe prabhu saba bhakta-gaṇe
> "ihāra viṣaya-sukha---indra-sukha-sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto all the devotees; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---material happiness; [[synonyms:i:indra]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---the material happiness of the King of heaven, Indra; [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal to.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this appeal by Raghunātha dāsa, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu smiled and told all the devotees, "Raghunātha dāsa's standard of material happiness is equal to that of Indra, the King of heaven.**
