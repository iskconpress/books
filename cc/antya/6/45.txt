====== CC Antya 6.45 ======

===== Text =====

> tale upare bahu-bhakta hañāche veṣṭita
> dekhi' prabhura prabhāva raghunātha---vismita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tale]]---the surface; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---many devotees; [[synonyms:h:hañāche]] [[synonyms:v:veṣṭita]]---He was surrounded; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---the influence of Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---astonished.

===== Translation =====

**Many devotees sat on the ground surrounding Him. Seeing the influence of Nityānanda Prabhu, Raghunātha dāsa was astonished.**
