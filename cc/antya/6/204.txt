====== CC Antya 6.204 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' raghunāthera hasta dharilā
> svarūpera haste tāṅre samarpaṇa kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]]---of Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:h:hasta]] [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---caught the hand; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]] [[synonyms:h:haste]]---in the hands of Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---entrusted.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu grasped the hand of Raghunātha dāsa and entrusted him to the hands of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.**
