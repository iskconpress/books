====== CC Antya 6.132 ======

===== Text =====

> ayogya mui nivedana karite kari bhaya
> more 'caitanya' deha' gosāñi hañā sadaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ayogya]]---unfit; [[synonyms:m:mui]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to submit my desires; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---I am afraid; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---kindly give the shelter of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---O my Lord; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:daya]]---being merciful.

===== Translation =====

**"Although I am unfit and greatly afraid to submit this plea, I nevertheless request You, sir, to be especially merciful toward me by granting me shelter at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
