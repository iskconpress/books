====== CC Antya 6.221 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure govinda kahe,---"raghunātha 'prasāda' nā laya
> rātrye siṁha-dvāre khāḍā hañā māgi' khāya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:govinda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Govinda said; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---does not take prasāda; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the Siṁha-dvāra gate; [[synonyms:k:khāḍā]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---standing; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---begging; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---he eats.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda said to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Raghunātha dāsa no longer takes prasāda here. Now he stands at the Siṁha-dvāra, where he begs some alms to eat."**
