====== CC Antya 6.215 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera sevaka yata---'viṣayīra gaṇa'
> sevā sāri' rātrye kare gṛhete gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servants; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:v:viṣayīra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---generally known as viṣayī; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:s:sāri']]---after finishing their service; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:g:gṛhete]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---returning home.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing their prescribed duties, the many servants of Lord Jagannātha, who are known as viṣayīs, return home at night.**
