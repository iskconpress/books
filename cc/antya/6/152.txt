====== CC Antya 6.152 ======

===== Text =====

> saba lekhā kariyā rāghava-pāśa dilā
> yāṅra nāme yata rāghava ciṭhi lekhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:l:lekhā]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---writing; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered to Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in whose name; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as much; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:ciṭhi]]---a list; [[synonyms:l:lekhāilā]]---had written.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa drew up an account of the amount to be given and submitted it to Rāghava Paṇḍita, who then made up a list showing how much money was to be paid to each and every devotee.**
