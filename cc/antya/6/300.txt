====== CC Antya 6.300 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata raghunātha karena pūjana
> pūjā-kāle dekhe śilāya 'vrajendra-nandana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:p:pūjana]]---worships; [[synonyms:p:pūjā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---while worshiping; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---he sees; [[synonyms:ś:śilāya]]---in the stone from Govardhana; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Raghunātha dāsa began worshiping the stone from Govardhana, and as he worshiped he saw the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja, directly in the stone.**
