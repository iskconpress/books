====== CC Antya 6.217 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata sarva-kāla āche vyavahāra
> niṣkiñcana bhakta khāḍā haya siṁha-dvāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]]---for all time; [[synonyms:a:ache]]---is; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---the etiquette; [[synonyms:n:niṣkiñcana]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---a devotee who has no other support; [[synonyms:k:khāḍā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---stands; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---at the gate known as Siṁha-dvāra.

===== Translation =====

**Thus it is a custom for all time that a devotee who has no other means of support stands at the Siṁha-dvāra gate to receive alms from the servants.**
