====== CC Antya 6.81 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata nitāi bule sakala maṇḍale
> dāṇḍāñā raṅga dekhe vaiṣṇava sakale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nitāi]] [[synonyms:b:bule]]---Lord Nityānanda was walking; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:m:maṇḍale]]---through all the groups; [[synonyms:d:dāṇḍāñā]]---standing; [[synonyms:r:raṅga]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---see the fun; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:s:sakale]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Lord Nityānanda was walking through all the groups of eaters, and all the Vaiṣṇavas standing there were seeing the fun.**
