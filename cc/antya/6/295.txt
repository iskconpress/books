====== CC Antya 6.295 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śilāre kara tumi sāttvika pūjana
> acirāt pābe tumi kṛṣṇa-prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śilāra]]---of this stone; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:s:sāttvika]] [[synonyms:p:pūjana]]---worshiping like a perfect //brāhmaṇa,// or in the mode of goodness; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:p:pābe]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you will get; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---wealth.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "Worship this stone in the mode of goodness like a perfect brāhmaṇa, for by such worship you will surely attain ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa without delay.**
