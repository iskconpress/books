====== Cc. Antya 6.103 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu tāṅra nṛtya karena daraśana
> sabe nityānanda dekhe, nā dekhe anya-jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---sees; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---Nityānanda Prabhu sees; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---do not see; [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---others.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was observing the dancing of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu. Nityānanda Prabhu could see this, but the others could not.**
