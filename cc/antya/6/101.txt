====== Cc. Antya 6.101 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu viśrāma kailā, yadi dina-śeṣa haila
> rāghava-mandire tabe kīrtana ārambhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---took rest; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:d:dina]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---the day was ended; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---at the temple of Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:ā:ārambhila]]---began congregational chanting of the holy name.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu rested for the day, and when the day ended He went to the temple of Rāghava Paṇḍita and began congregational chanting of the holy name of the Lord.**
