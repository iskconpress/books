====== Cc. Antya 6.9 ======

===== Text =====

> subala yaiche pūrve kṛṣṇa-sukhera sahāya
> gaura-sukha-dāna-hetu taiche rāma-rāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:subala]]---Subala, one of the cowherd boy friends of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sukhera]]---to give happiness to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]---helper; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for giving happiness to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Previously, when Lord Kṛṣṇa was personally present, Subala, one of His cowherd boy friends, gave Him happiness when He felt separation from Rādhārāṇī. Similarly, Rāmānanda Rāya helped give happiness to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
