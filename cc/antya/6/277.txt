====== CC Antya 6.277 ======

===== Text =====

> eta vicāriyā nimantraṇa chāḍi' dila"
> śuni' mahāprabhu hāsi' balite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:v:vicāriyā]]---considering this; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---he has stopped; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:b:balite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**"Considering all these points," Svarūpa Dāmodara concluded, "he has stopped inviting You." Hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled and spoke as follows.**
