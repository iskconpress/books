====== CC Antya 6.21 ======

===== Text =====

> prati-dina raghunāthe karaye bhartsanā
> 'bāpa-jyeṭhāre āna;, nahe pāibā yātanā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---daily; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:b:bhartsanā]]---he chastised; [[synonyms:b:bāpa]]-[[synonyms:j:jyeṭhāre]] [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---bring your father and his elder brother; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---otherwise; [[synonyms:p:pāibā]] [[synonyms:y:yātanā]]---you will be punished.

===== Translation =====

**Every day, the Mohammedan would chastise Raghunātha dāsa and tell him, "Bring your father and his elder brother. Otherwise you will be punished."**
