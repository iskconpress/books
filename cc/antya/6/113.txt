====== CC Antya 6.113 ======

===== Text =====

> prati-dina mahāprabhu karena bhojana
> madhye madhye prabhu tāṅre dena daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---daily; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eats; [[synonyms:m:madhye]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:dena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---gives His audience.

===== Translation =====

**Every day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would eat at the house of Rāghava Paṇḍita. Sometimes He would give Rāghava Paṇḍita the opportunity to see Him.**
