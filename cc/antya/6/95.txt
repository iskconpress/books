====== CC Antya 6.95 ======

===== Text =====

> āra tina kuṇḍikāya avaśeṣa chila
> grāse-grāse kari' vipra saba bhakte dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍikāya]]---in three pots; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]] [[synonyms:c:chila]]---there was food remaining; [[synonyms:g:grāse]]-[[synonyms:g:grāse]]---by morsel; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---delivering; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**There was food remaining in the three other big pots of Lord Nityānanda, and a brāhmaṇa distributed it to all the devotees, giving a morsel to each.**
