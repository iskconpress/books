====== CC Antya 6.172 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-caitanya-nityānanda-caraṇa cintiyā
> patha chāḍi' upapathe yāyena dhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:c:cintiyā]]---thinking of; [[synonyms:p:patha]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up the general pathway; [[synonyms:u:upapathe]]---by the path not generally used; [[synonyms:y:yāyena]] [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---he went very swiftly.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking of the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, he left the general path and proceeded with great haste on the one not generally used.**
