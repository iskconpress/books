====== CC Antya 6.49 ======

===== Text =====

> kautukī nityānanda sahaje dayāmaya
> raghunāthe kahe kichu hañā sadaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kautukī]]---very funny; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:s:sahaje]]---by nature; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---very merciful; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---unto Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:d:daya]]---being merciful.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda was by nature very merciful and funny. Being merciful, He spoke to Raghunātha dāsa as follows.**
