====== Cc. Antya 6.258 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' tāṅra mātā pitā duḥkhita ha-ila
> putra-ṭhāñi dravya-manuṣya pāṭhāite mana kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mātā]] [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---father and mother; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhita]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became very unhappy; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to their son; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]-[[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---articles and men; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāite]]---to send; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---decided.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing the description of Raghunātha dāsa's behavior in the renounced order, his father and mother were very unhappy. Therefore they decided to send him some men with goods for his comfort.**
