====== CC Antya 6.212 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura avaśiṣṭa pātra govinda tāṅre dilā
> ānandita hañā raghunātha prasāda pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avaśiṣṭa]] [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---a plate of remnants of food; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the personal servant of the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---offered; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming very happy; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---accepted the prasāda.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda offered him a plate with the remnants of food left by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and Raghunātha dāsa accepted the prasāda with great happiness.**
