====== CC Antya 6.13 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve śāntipure raghunātha yabe āilā
> mahāprabhu kṛpā kari' tāṅre śikhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:ś:śāntipure]]---to Śāntipura; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:y:yabe]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---when he came; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing causeless mercy; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---gave him lessons.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghunātha dāsa, during his family life, went to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Śāntipura, the Lord gave him worthy instructions by His causeless mercy.**
