====== CC Antya 6.177 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅha kahe, 'ājñā māgi' gelā nija-ghara'
> 'palāila raghunātha'---uṭhila kolāhala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅha]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he said; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---asking my permission; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---to his home; [[synonyms:p:palāila]] [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa has gone away; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---arose; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---a tumultuous sound.

===== Translation =====

**Yadunandana Ācārya said, "He has already asked my permission and returned home." Thus there arose a tumultuous sound, as everyone cried, "Now Raghunātha has gone away!"**
