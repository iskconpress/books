====== Cc. Antya 6.88 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda mahāprabhu---kṛpālu, udāra
> raghunāthera bhāgye eta kailā aṅgīkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpālu]]---merciful; [[synonyms:u:udāra]]---liberal; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---by the great fortune of Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---all this; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---They accepted.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu are extremely merciful and liberal. It was Raghunātha dāsa's good fortune that They accepted all these dealings.**
