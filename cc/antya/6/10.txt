====== CC Antya 6.10 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yaiche rādhāra lalitā sahāya-pradhāna
> taiche svarūpa-gosāñi rākhe mahāprabhura prāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:l:lalitā]]---Her companion named Lalitā; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]-[[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the best helper; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rākhe]]---keeps; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---the life of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Previously, when Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī felt the pangs of separation from Kṛṣṇa, Her constant companion Lalitā kept Her alive by helping Her in many ways. Similarly, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt Rādhārāṇī's emotions, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī helped Him maintain His life.**
