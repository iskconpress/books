====== CC Antya 6.80 ======

===== Text =====

> hāsi' mahāprabhu āra eka grāsa lañā
> tāṅra mukhe diyā khāoyāya hāsiyā hāsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:g:grāsa]]---one morsel; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---putting; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāya]]---makes eat; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]] [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---laughing.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, also smiling, took a morsel of food, pushed it into the mouth of Nityānanda and laughed as He made Lord Nityānanda eat it.**
