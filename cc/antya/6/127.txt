====== CC Antya 6.127 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha āsi' kailā caraṇa vandana
> rāghava-paṇḍita-dvārā kailā nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiped His lotus feet; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---through Rāghava paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted his desire.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa went there and worshiped Lord Nityānanda's lotus feet. Through Rāghava Paṇḍita, he submitted his desire.**
