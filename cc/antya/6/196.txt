====== CC Antya 6.196 ======

===== Text =====

> cakravartīra duhe haya bhrātṛ-rūpa dāsa
> ataeva tāre āmi kari parihāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cakravartīra]]---of Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; [[synonyms:d:duhe]]---both; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:b:bhrātṛ]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---servants as younger brothers; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto them; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:p:parihāsa]]---say something jokingly.

===== Translation =====

**"Since your father and his elder brother are younger brothers of Nīlāmbara Cakravartī, I may joke about them in this way.**
