====== CC Antya 6.171 ======

===== Text =====

> eta cinti' pūrva-mukhe karilā gamana
> ulaṭiyā cāhe pāche,---nāhi kona jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:c:cinti']]---thinking this; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---toward the east; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---began to proceed; [[synonyms:u:ulaṭiyā]]---turning around; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---looks; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---there was no one.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking in this way, he quickly proceeded toward the east. Sometimes he turned around and looked back, but no one was following him.**
