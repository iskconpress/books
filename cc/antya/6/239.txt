====== CC Antya 6.239 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛṇād api sunīcena
> taror iva sahiṣṇunā
> amāninā mānadena
> kīrtanīyaḥ sadā hariḥ"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛṇāt]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---than downtrodden grass; [[synonyms:s:sunīcena]]---being lower; [[synonyms:t:taroḥ]]---than a tree; [[synonyms:i:iva]]---indeed; [[synonyms:s:sahiṣṇunā]]---with more tolerance; [[synonyms:a:amāninā]]---without being puffed up by false pride; [[synonyms:m:mānadena]]---giving respect to all; [[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyaḥ]]---to be chanted; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the holy name of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**"One who thinks himself lower than grass, who is more tolerant tha a tree, and who does not expect personal honor but is always prepared to give respect to others, can very easily always chant the holy name of the Lord."**
