====== Cc. Antya 6.104 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānandera nṛtya,---yena tāṅhāra nartane
> upamā dibāra nāhi e-tina bhuvane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānandera]] [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---the dancing of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:n:nartane]]---with the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:upamā]] [[synonyms:d:dibāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there cannot be any comparison; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:b:bhuvane]]---within these three worlds.

===== Translation =====

**The dancing of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, like the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, cannot be compared to anything within these three worlds.**
