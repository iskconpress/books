====== CC Antya 6.85 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' nityānanda-prabhu ānandita hailā
> kata kata bhāvāveśa prakāśa karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very happy; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:k:kata]]---so much; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---He manifested.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu eating with Him, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu became very happy and exhibited varieties of ecstatic love.**
