====== CC Antya 6.154 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra pada-dhūli lañā svagṛhe āilā
> nityānanda-kṛpā pāñā kṛtārtha mānilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:d:dhūli]]---the dust of the feet; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned to his home; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:k:kṛtārtha]] [[synonyms:m:mānilā]]---he felt greatly obligated.

===== Translation =====

**After taking dust from the feet of Rāghava Paṇḍita, Raghunātha dāsa returned to his home, feeling greatly obligated to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu because of having received His merciful benediction.**
