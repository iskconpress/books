====== CC Antya 6.139 ======

===== Text =====

> "tumi ye karāilā ei pulina-bhojana
> tomāya kṛpā kari' gaura kailā āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---caused to do; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pulina]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---picnic on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---unto you; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Raghunātha dāsa," He said, "since you arranged the feast on the bank of the Ganges, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came here just to show you His mercy.**
