====== Cc. Antya 6.110 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā-prakāra piṭhā, pāyasa, divya śālyanna
> amṛta nindaye aiche vividha vyañjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]] [[synonyms:p:piṭhā]]---various types of cake; [[synonyms:p:pāyasa]]---sweet rice; [[synonyms:d:divya]] [[synonyms:ś:śālyanna]]---fine cooked rice; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---nectar; [[synonyms:n:nindaye]]---surpassed; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:v:vividha]] [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---varieties of vegetables.

===== Translation =====

**There were varieties of cakes, sweet rice and fine cooked rice that surpassed the taste of nectar. There were also varieties of vegetables.**
