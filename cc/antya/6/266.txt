====== CC Antya 6.266 ======

===== Text =====

> varṣāntare śivānanda cale nīlācale
> raghunāthera sevaka, vipra tāṅra saṅge cale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---the next year; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:c:cale]] [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---was going to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]]---of Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servants; [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---and the brāhmaṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---go.

===== Translation =====

**The next year, when Śivānanda Sena was going to Jagannātha Purī as usual, the servants and the brāhmaṇa, who was a cook, went with him.**
