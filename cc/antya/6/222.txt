====== Cc. Antya 6.222 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' tuṣṭa hañā prabhu kahite lāgila
> "bhāla kaila, vairāgīra dharma ācarila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very satisfied; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to say; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---he has done well; [[synonyms:v:vairāgīra]]---of a person in the renounced order; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the principles; [[synonyms:ā:ācarila]]---he has performed.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this, He was greatly satisfied. "Raghunātha dāsa has done well," He said."He has acted suitably for a person in the renounced order.**
