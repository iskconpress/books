====== CC Antya 6.192 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpādi saba bhaktera caraṇa vandilā
> prabhu-kṛpā dekhi' sabe āliṅgana kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandilā]]---offered prayers to the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa offered prayers at the lotus feet of all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī. Seeing the special mercy Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had bestowed upon Raghunātha dāsa, they embraced him also.**
