====== Cc. Antya 6.288 ======

===== Text =====

> śaṅkarānanda-sarasvatī vṛndāvana haite āilā
> teṅha sei śilā-guñjā-mālā lañā gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkarānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---one of the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanahaite]]---from Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śilā]]-[[synonyms:g:guñjā]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---stone and the garland of small conchshells; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**Previously, when Śaṅkarānanda Sarasvatī had returned from Vṛndāvana, he had brought the piece of stone from Govardhana Hill and also the garland of conchshells.**
