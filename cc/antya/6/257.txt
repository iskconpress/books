====== CC Antya 6.257 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' sei manuṣya govardhana-sthāne
> kahila giyā saba raghunātha-vivaraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---that messenger; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to Govardhana Majumadāra; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---spoke; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everything; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇe]]---the description of Raghunātha dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this, the messenger returned to Govardhana Majumadāra and informed him all about Raghunātha dāsa.**
