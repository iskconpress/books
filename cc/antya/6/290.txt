====== CC Antya 6.290 ======

===== Text =====

> dui apūrva-vastu pāñā prabhu tuṣṭa hailā
> smaraṇera kāle gale pare guñjā-mālā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vastu]]---uncommon things; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very happy; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇera]] [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time of remembering (when He was chanting Hare Kṛṣṇa); [[synonyms:g:gale]]---on the neck; [[synonyms:p:pare]]---wears; [[synonyms:g:guñjā]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---the garland of small conchshells.

===== Translation =====

**Upon receiving these two uncommon items, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was extremely happy. While chanting, He would put the garland around His neck.**
