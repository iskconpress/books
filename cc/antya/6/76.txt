====== CC Antya 6.76 ======

===== Text =====

> rāghave vasāñā dui kuṇḍī deoyāilā
> rāghava dvividha ciḍā tāte bhijāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāghave]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:v:vasāñā]]---making sit down; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍī]]---earthen pots; [[synonyms:d:deoyāilā]]---arranged to be delivered to him; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:v:vidha]]---two kinds; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in them; [[synonyms:b:bhijāilā]]---soaked.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda made Rāghava Paṇḍita sit down and had two pots delivered to him also. There were two kinds of chipped rice soaked in them.**
