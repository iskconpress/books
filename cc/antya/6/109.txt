====== CC Antya 6.109 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-bhāi-āge prasāda āniyā dharilā
> sakala vaiṣṇave piche pariveśana kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāi]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of the two brothers; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of food offered to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āniyā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---put; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇave]]---to all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:p:piche]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---distributed.

===== Translation =====

**Rāghava Paṇḍita brought the prasāda before the two brothers and thereafter distributed prasāda to all the other Vaiṣṇavas.**
