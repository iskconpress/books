====== CC Antya 6.120 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana kari' dui bhāi kailā ācamana
> rāghava āni' parāilā mālya-candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after eating; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---washed Their hands and mouths; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:p:parāilā]]---decorated Them with; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---flower garlands and sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**After eating, the two brothers washed Their hands and mouths. Then Rāghava Paṇḍita brought flower garlands and sandalwood pulp and decorated Them.**
