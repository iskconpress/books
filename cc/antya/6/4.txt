====== CC Antya 6.4 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi antare kṛṣṇa-viyoga bādha ye
> bāhire nā prakāśaya bhakta-duḥkha-bhaye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viyoga]]---separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bādhye]]---obstructs; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśaya]]---does not exhibit; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---fearing the unhappiness of the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Although Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt pangs of separation from Kṛṣṇa, He did not manifest His feelings externally, for He feared the unhappiness of His devotees.**
