====== CC Antya 6.186 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakṣaṇa apekṣā nāhi, samasta divasa gamana
> kṣudhā nāhi bādhe, caitanya-caraṇa-prāptye mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]] [[synonyms:a:apekṣā]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---did not care for eating; [[synonyms:s:samasta]] [[synonyms:d:divasa]]---all day; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---traveling; [[synonyms:k:kṣudhā]]---hunger; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:b:bādhe]]---did not become an impediment; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prāptye]]---on obtaining; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**Not caring about eating, he traveled all day. Hunger was not an impediment, for his mind was concentrated upon obtaining shelter at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
