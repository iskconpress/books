====== CC Antya 6.242 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle āilā saba gauḍera bhakta-gaṇa
> pūrvavat prabhu sabāya karilā milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees from Bengal; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as formerly; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---every one of them; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**At this time, all the devotees from Bengal arrived, and, as previously, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu met them with great feeling.**
