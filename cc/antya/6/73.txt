====== Cc. Antya 6.73 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure kahe,---"tomā lāgi' bhoga lāgāila
> tumi ihāṅ utsava kara, ghare prasāda rahila"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he said to Lord Nityānanda prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for You; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:l:lāgāila]]---I have offered food to the Deity; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:u:utsava]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---are engaged in a festival; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the prasāda; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained.

===== Translation =====

**Rāghava Paṇḍita said to Lord Nityānanda, "For You, sir, I have already offered food to the Deity, but You are engaged in a festival here, and so the food is lying there untouched."**
