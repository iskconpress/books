====== Cc. Antya 6.99 ======

===== Text =====

> ānandita raghunātha prabhura 'śeṣa, pāñā
> āpanāra gaṇa-saha khāilā bāṅtiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---being very happy; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---after getting the remnants left by Lord Nityānanda prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---his own associates; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:k:khāilā]]---ate; [[synonyms:b:bāṅtiyā]]---distributing.

===== Translation =====

**After receiving the remnants of food left by Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, Raghunātha dāsa, who was greatly happy, ate some and distributed the rest among his own associates.**
