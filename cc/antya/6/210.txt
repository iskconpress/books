====== CC Antya 6.210 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunāthe prabhura kṛpā dekhi, bhakta-gaṇa
> vismita hañā kare tāṅra bhāgya-praśaṁsana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---unto Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---struck with wonder; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---fortune; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsana]]---praise.

===== Translation =====

**Having seen the causeless mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu upon Raghunātha dāsa, all the devotees, struck with wonder, praised his good fortune.**
