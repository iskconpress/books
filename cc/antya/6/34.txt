====== CC Antya 6.34 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha āsi' tabe jyeṭhāre milāila
> mleccha-sahita vaśa kaila---saba śānta haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:j:jyeṭhāre]] [[synonyms:m:milāila]]---arranged a meeting between the caudhurī and the elder brother of his father; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with the Mohammedan; [[synonyms:v:vaśa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---he settled; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---everything; [[synonyms:ś:śānta]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became peaceful.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa arranged a meeting between his uncle and the caudhurī. He settled the matter, and everything was peaceful.**
