====== CC Antya 6.94 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana kari' nityānanda ācamana kailā
> cāri kuṇḍīra avaśeṣa raghunāthe dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after finishing eating; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---washing His hands and mouth; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍīra]]---of the four pots; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]]---what was remaining; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered to Raghunātha dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**After Lord Nityānanda Prabhu finished eating, He washed His hands and mouth and gave Raghunātha dāsa the food remaining in the four pots.**
