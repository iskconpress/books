====== Cc. Antya 6.31 ======

===== Text =====

> ujire kahiyā raghunāthe chāḍāila
> prīti kari' raghunāthe kahite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ujire]]---unto the minister; [[synonyms:k:kahiyā]]---speaking; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍāila]]---released Raghu nātha dāsa; [[synonyms:p:prīti]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great affection; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]]---unto Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**After informing the minister, the caudhurī released Raghunātha dāsa and then began to speak to him with great affection.**
