====== Cc. Antya 6.149 ======

===== Text =====

> aneka 'prasāda' dilā pathe khāibāre
> tabe punaḥ raghunātha kahe paṇḍitere

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---much //prasāda//; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered; [[synonyms:p:pathe]] [[synonyms:k:khāibāre]]---to eat on his way; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Raghunātha dāsa said; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitere]]---to Rāghava Paṇḍita.

===== Translation =====

**He gave Raghunātha dāsa a large quantity of prasāda to eat on his way home. Then Raghunātha dāsa again spoke to Rāghava Paṇḍita.**
