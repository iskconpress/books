====== CC Antya 6.83 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe hāsi' nityānanda vasilā āsane
> cāri kuṇḍī āroyā ciḍā rākhilā ḍāhine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]] [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---thereupon smiling; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsane]]---sat down on His seat; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:k:kuṇḍī]]---four earthen pots; [[synonyms:ā:āroyā]] [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice not made from boiled paddy; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]] [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāhine]]---He kept on His right side.

===== Translation =====

**Then Nityānanda Prabhu smiled and sat down. On His right side He kept four pots of chipped rice that had not been made from boiled paddy.**
