====== CC Antya 6.208 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunāthe kahe---"yāñā, kara sindhu-snāna
> jagannātha dekhi' āsi' karaha bhojana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunāthe]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---He said to Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathe in the sea; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---after coming; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---take your meal.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told Raghunātha dāsa, "Go bathe in the sea. Then see Lord Jagannātha in the temple and return here to take your meal."**
