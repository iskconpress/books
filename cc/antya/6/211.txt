====== CC Antya 6.211 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha samudre yāñā snāna karilā
> jagannātha dekhi' punaḥ govinda-pāśa āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:s:samudre]]---to the sea; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---took a bath; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Govinda.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa took his bath in the sea and saw Lord Jagannātha. Then he returned to Govinda, the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
