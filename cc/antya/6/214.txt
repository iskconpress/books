====== CC Antya 6.214 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina haite 'puṣpa-añjali' dekhiyā
> siṁha-dvāre khāḍā rahe bhikṣāra lāgiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:a:añjali]]---the ceremony of offering flowers to the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---after seeing; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the main gate; [[synonyms:k:khāḍā]] [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains standing; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣāra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---for begging some alms.

===== Translation =====

**Beginning from the sixth day, Raghunātha dāsa would stand at the gate known as Siṁha-dvāra to beg alms after the puṣpa-añjali ceremony, in which flowers were offered to the Lord.**
