====== CC Antya 6.248 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri māsa rahi' bhakta-gaṇa gauḍe gelā
> śuni' raghunāthera pitā manuṣya pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---for four months; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned to Bengal; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]] [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---the father of Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---a man; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**When all the devotees from Bengal returned home after staying at Jagannātha Purī for four months, Raghunātha dāsa's father heard about their arrival and therefore sent a man to Śivānanda Sena.**
