====== CC Antya 6.142 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpera sthāne tomā karibe samarpaṇe
> 'antaraṅga' bhṛtya bali' rākhibe caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpera]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---unto Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---you; [[synonyms:k:karibe]] [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇe]]---He will give; [[synonyms:a:antaraṅga]]---very confidential; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---servant; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---as; [[synonyms:r:rākhibe]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---will keep under His lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu will accept you and place you under the charge of His secretary, Svarūpa Dāmodara. You will thus become one of the most confidential internal servants and will attain the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
