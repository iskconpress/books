====== Cc. Antya 6.311 ======

===== Text =====

> vairāgyera kathā tāṅra adbhuta-kathana
> ājanma nā dila jihvāya rasera sparśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vairāgyera]]---of the renunciation; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---talks; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]-[[synonyms:k:kathana]]---wonderful topics; [[synonyms:ā:ā]]-[[synonyms:j:janma]]---from birth; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---did not allow; [[synonyms:j:jihvāya]]---to the tongue; [[synonyms:r:rasera]] [[synonyms:s:sparśana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**Topics concerning his renunciation are wonderful. Throughout his life he never allowed his tongue sense gratification.**
