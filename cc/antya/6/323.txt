====== CC Antya 6.323 ======

===== Text =====

> āra grāsa laite svarūpa hātete dharilā
> 'tava yogya nahe' bali' bale kāḍi' nilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:g:grāsa]]---morsel; [[synonyms:l:laite]]---taking; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:h:hātete]]---the hand; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---caught; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---for You; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---fit; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---by force; [[synonyms:k:kāḍi']]---snatching; [[synonyms:n:nilā]]---he took.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was taking another morsel of food, Svarūpa Dāmodara caught Him by the hand and said, "It is not fit for You." Thus he forcibly took the food away.**
