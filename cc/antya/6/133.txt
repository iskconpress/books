====== Cc. Antya 6.133 ======

===== Text =====

> mora māthe pada dhari' karaha prasāda
> nirvighne caitanya pāṅa---kara āśīrvāda"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:m:māthe]]---upon my head; [[synonyms:p:pada]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---keeping Your feet; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---bless me; [[synonyms:n:nirvighne]]---without difficulty; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:p:pāṅa]]---I may get the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:ā:āśīrvāda]]---give this benediction.

===== Translation =====

**"Placing Your feet on my head, give me the benediction that I may achieve the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu without difficulty. I pray for this benediction."**
