====== Cc. Antya 6.321 ======

===== Text =====

> govindera mukhe prabhu se vārtā śunilā
> āra dina āsi' prabhu kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govindera]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth of Govinda; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---that news; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---heard; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard news of this from the mouth of Govinda, He went there the next day and spoke as follows.**
