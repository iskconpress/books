====== Cc. Antya 6.173 ======

===== Text =====

> grāme-grāmera patha chāḍi' yāya vane vane
> kāya-mano-vākye cinte caitanya-caraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:grāme]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmera]]---from village to village; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---the general path; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:v:vane]] [[synonyms:v:vane]]---through the jungles; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]]---with body, mind and words; [[synonyms:c:cinte]]---thinks; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---of the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Giving up the general path from village to village, he passed through the jungles, thinking with heart and soul about the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
