====== CC Antya 6.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata vihare gaura lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> raghunātha-milana ebe śuna, bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vihare]]---enjoys; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---keeping company with His devotees; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting with Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord thus enjoyed His life with His devotees. O devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, now hear how Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī met the Lord.**
