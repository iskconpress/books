====== CC Antya 6.5 ======

===== Text =====

> utkaṭa viraha-duḥkha yabe bāhirāya
> tabe ye vaikalya prabhura varṇana nā yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:utkaṭa]]---severe; [[synonyms:v:viraha]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness of separation; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:b:bāhirāya]]---is manifested; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---what; [[synonyms:v:vaikalya]]---transformations; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---cannot be described.

===== Translation =====

**The transformations undergone by the Lord when He manifested severe unhappiness due to separation from Kṛṣṇa cannot be described.**
