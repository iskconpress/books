====== CC Antya 6.189 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpādi-saha gosāñi āchena vasiyā
> hena-kāle raghunātha milila āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---in the company of devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]] [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---was sitting; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:m:milila]]---met; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghunātha dāsa met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord was sitting with His companions, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
