====== Cc. Antya 6.48 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu bolāya, teṅho nikaṭe nā kare gamana
> ākarṣiyā tāṅra māthe prabhu dharilā caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:b:bolāya]]---the Lord calls; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---nearby; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---does not come; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣiyā]]---bringing him near; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:māthe]]---on his head; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---placed His feet.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord called him, but Raghunātha dāsa did not go near the Lord. Then the Lord forcibly caught him and placed His lotus feet upon Raghunātha dāsa's head.**
