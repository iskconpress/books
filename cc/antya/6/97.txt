====== CC Antya 6.97 ======

===== Text =====

> sevaka tāmbūla lañā kare samarpaṇa
> hāsiyā hāsiyā prabhu karaye carvaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servant; [[synonyms:t:tāmbūla]]---betel nuts; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---bringing; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---offers; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]] [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:c:carvaṇa]]---chews.

===== Translation =====

**When a servant brought betel nuts and offered them to Lord Nityānanda, the Lord smiled and chewed them.**
