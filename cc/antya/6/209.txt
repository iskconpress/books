====== CC Antya 6.209 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu madhyāhna karite uṭhilā
> raghunātha-dāsa saba bhaktere mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---after saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---for performing His midday duties; [[synonyms:u:uṭhilā]]---got up; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhaktere]]---devotees; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up and went to perform His midday duties, and Raghunātha met all the devotees present.**
