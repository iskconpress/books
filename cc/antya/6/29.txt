====== CC Antya 6.29 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' sei mlecchera mana ārdra haila
> dāḍi vāhi' aśru paḍe, kāṅdite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:mlecchera]]---of that Mohammedan; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:ā:ārdra]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became softened; [[synonyms:d:dāḍi]] [[synonyms:v:vāhi']]---flowing over his beard; [[synonyms:a:aśru]] [[synonyms:p:pade]]---tears fell; [[synonyms:k:kāṅdite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to cry.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing Raghunātha dāsa's appealing voice, the Mohammedan's heart softened. He began to cry, and tears glided down his beard.**
