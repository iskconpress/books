====== CC Antya 6.310 ======

===== Text =====

> sāḍe sāta prahara yāya kīrtana-smaraṇe
> āhāra-nidrā cāri daṇḍa seha nahe kona dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāḍe]] [[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:p:prahara]]---7.5 //praharas// (one //prahara// equals three hours); [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is spent; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:s:smaraṇe]]---in chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra// and remembering the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āhāra]]-[[synonyms:n:nidrā]]---eating and sleeping; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---four //daṇḍas// (one //daṇḍa// equals twenty-four minutes); [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---some days.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa spent more than twenty-two hours out of every twenty-four chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra and remembering the lotus feet of the Lord. He ate and slept for less than an hour and a half, and on some days that also was impossible.**
