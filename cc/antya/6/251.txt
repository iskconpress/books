====== CC Antya 6.251 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda kahe,---"teṅho haya prabhura sthāne
> parama vikhyāta teṅho, kebā nāhi jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śivānanda Sena replied; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:v:vikhyāta]]---very famous; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kebā]]---who; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena replied, "Yes, sir. Raghunātha dāsa is with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and is a very famous man. Who does not know him?**
