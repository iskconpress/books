====== CC Antya 6.301 ======

===== Text =====

> 'prabhura svahasta-datta govardhana-śilā
> ei cinti' raghunātha preme bhāsi' gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:h:hasta]]---own hand; [[synonyms:d:datta]]---handed over by; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:ś:śilā]]---the stone from Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:c:cinti']]---thinking this; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bhāsi']] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---became overflooded.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking of how he had received the govardhana-śilā directly from the hands of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Raghunātha dāsa was always overflooded with ecstatic love.**
