====== Cc. Antya 6.243 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā lañā kailā prabhu guṇḍicā-mārjana
> sabā lañā kailā prabhu vanya-bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]-[[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---washing of the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya; [[synonyms:v:vanya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating in the garden.

===== Translation =====

**As He had previously done, He cleansed the Guṇḍicā temple and held a picnic feast in the garden with the devotees.**
