====== CC Antya 6.74 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"e-dravya dine kariye bhojana
> rātrye tomāra ghare prasāda karimu bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu said; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]]---this food; [[synonyms:d:dine]]---during the daytime; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---let Me eat; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in your house; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the prasāda; [[synonyms:k:karimu]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---I shall eat.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Nityānanda replied, "Let Me eat all this food here during the day, and I shall eat at your home at night.**
