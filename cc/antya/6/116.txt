====== CC Antya 6.116 ======

===== Text =====

> durvāsāra ṭhāñi teṅho pāñāchena vara
> amṛta ha-ite pāka tāṅra adhika madhura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:durvāsāra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from Durvāsā Muni; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---She; [[synonyms:p:pāñāchena]] [[synonyms:v:vara]]---got the benediction; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---than nectar; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]---cooking; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---Her; [[synonyms:a:adhika]] [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---more sweet.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī received from Durvāsā Muni the benediction that whatever She cooked would be sweeter than nectar. That is the special feature of Her cooking.**
