====== Cc. Antya 6.65 ======

===== Text =====

> dui dui mṛt-kuṇḍikā sabāra āge dila
> eke dugdha-ciḍā, āre dadhi-ciḍā kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two and two; [[synonyms:m:mṛt]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṇḍikā]]---earthen pots; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before everyone; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---offered; [[synonyms:e:eke]]---in one; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice with condensed milk; [[synonyms:ā:āre]]---in the other; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]-[[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice with yogurt; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---put.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was offered two earthen pots. In one was put chipped rice with condensed milk and in the other chipped rice with yogurt.**
