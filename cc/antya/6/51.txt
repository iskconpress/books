====== CC Antya 6.51 ======

===== Text =====

> dadhi, ciḍā bhakṣaṇa karāha mora gaṇe"
> śuni' ānandita haila raghunātha mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---chipped rice; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:karāha]]---feed; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---My associates; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became very happy; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**"Make a festival and feed all My associates yogurt and chipped rice." Hearing this, Raghunātha dāsa was greatly pleased.**
