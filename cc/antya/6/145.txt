====== CC Antya 6.145 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-ājñā lañā vaiṣṇavera ājñā la-ilā
> rāghava-sahite nibhṛte yukti karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the permission of all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---he took; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:s:sahite]]---with Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:y:yukti]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---he consulted.

===== Translation =====

**After taking leave of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu and then all the other Vaiṣṇavas, Srī Raghunātha dāsa consulted secretly with Rāghava Paṇḍita.**
