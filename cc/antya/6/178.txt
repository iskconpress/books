====== CC Antya 6.178 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra pitā kahe,---"gauḍera saba bhakta-gaṇa
> prabhu-sthāne nīlācale karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---father; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]]---of Bengal; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha purī; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---have gone.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa's father said, "Now all the devotees from Bengal have gone to Jagannātha Purī to see Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
