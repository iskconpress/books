====== CC Antya 6.115 ======

===== Text =====

> kata upahāra āne, hena nāhi jāni
> rāghavera ghare rāndhe rādhā-ṭhākurāṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:u:upahāra]]---many presentations; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---brings; [[synonyms:h:hena]]---such; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I cannot understand; [[synonyms:r:rāghavera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the house of Rāghava paṇḍita; [[synonyms:r:rāndhe]]---cooks; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---the supreme mother, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.

===== Translation =====

**He brought so many presentations that no one could know them perfectly. Indeed, it was a fact that the supreme mother, Rādhārāṇī, personally cooked in the house of Rāghava Paṇḍita.**
