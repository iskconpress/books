====== CC Antya 6.259 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-śata mudrā, dui bhṛtya, eka brāhmaṇa
> śivānandera ṭhāñi pāṭhāila tata-kṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---four hundred coins; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---two servants; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---one //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent; [[synonyms:t:tata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---immediately.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa's father immediately sent four hundred coins, two servants and one brāhmaṇa to Śivānanda Sena.**
