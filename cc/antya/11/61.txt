====== Cc. Antya 11.61 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate nṛtya prabhu kailā kata-kṣaṇa
> svarūpa-gosāñi prabhure karāila sāvadhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---caused to do; [[synonyms:s:sāvadhāna]]---care of other rituals.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced for some time, and then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī informed Him of other rituals for the body of Ṭhākura Haridāsa.**
