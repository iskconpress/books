====== CC Antya 11.8 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya gaura-bhakta-gaṇa,---gaura yāṅra prāṇa
> saba bhakta mili' more bhakti deha' dāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---the life and soul; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---together; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to me; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:d:deha']] [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---kindly give the charity.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, for the Lord is their life and soul. All of you, kindly bestow devotional service upon me.**
