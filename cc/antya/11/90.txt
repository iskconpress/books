====== CC Antya 11.90 ======

===== Text =====

> premāviṣṭa hañā prabhu karena vara-dāna
> śuni' bhakta-gaṇera juḍāya manaskāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being overwhelmed by ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:v:vara]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]---offered a benediction; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:j:juḍāya]]---became fulfilled; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāma]]---the desires of the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Overwhelmed with ecstatic love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered a benediction to all the devotees, which all the devotees heard with great satisfaction.**
