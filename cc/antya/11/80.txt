====== CC Antya 11.80 ======

===== Text =====

> vāṇīnātha paṭṭanāyaka prasāda ānilā
> kāśī-miśra aneka prasāda pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]] [[synonyms:p:paṭṭanāyaka]]---Vāṇīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---brought in; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---varieties of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**Not only did Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī bring prasāda, but Vāṇīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka, as well as Kāśī Miśra, sent large quantities.**
