====== CC Antya 11.86 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane kāśī-miśra āilā prasāda lañā
> prabhure bhikṣā karāilā āgraha kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking //prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---delivered //prasāda// to eat; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great attention.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore Kāśī Miśra personally went there and delivered prasāda to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with great attention and made Him eat.**
