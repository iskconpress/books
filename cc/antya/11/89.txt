====== CC Antya 11.89 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana kariyā sabe kailā ācamana
> sabāre parāilā prabhu mālya-candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---after eating; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---washing of the mouth and hands; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:parāilā]]---put on; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]---flower garland; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**After all the devotees finished accepting prasāda and had washed their hands and mouths, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu decorated each of them with a flower garland and sandalwood pulp.**
