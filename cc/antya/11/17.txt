====== CC Antya 11.17 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhe,---haridāsa ṭhākura kariyāche śayana
> manda manda kariteche saṅkhyā-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---he saw; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---was lying down; [[synonyms:m:manda]] [[synonyms:m:manda]]---very slowly; [[synonyms:k:kariteche]]---he was doing; [[synonyms:s:saṅkhyā]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting the fixed number of rounds.

===== Translation =====

**When Govinda came to Haridāsa, he saw that Haridāsa Ṭhākura was lying on his back and chanting his rounds very slowly.**
