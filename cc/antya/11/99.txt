====== CC Antya 11.99 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe gāya,---"jaya jaya jaya haridāsa
> nāmera mahimā yeṅha karilā prakāśa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---everyone chanted; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---to Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:y:yeṅha]]---who; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---revealed.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone began to chant, "All glories to Haridāsa Ṭhākura, who revealed the importance of chanting the holy name of the Lord!"**
