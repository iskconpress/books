====== CC Antya 11.40 ======

===== Text =====

> mora śiromaṇi kata kata mahāśaya
> tomāra līlāra sahāya koṭi-bhakta haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---crown jewels; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:k:kata]]---many, many; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---great persons; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---in Your pastimes; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]---helpers; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---millions of devotees; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord, there are many respectable personalities, millions of devotees, who are fit to sit on my head. They are all helpful in Your pastimes.**
