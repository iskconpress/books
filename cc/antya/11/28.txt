====== CC Antya 11.28 ======

===== Text =====

> adṛśya, aspṛśya more aṅgīkāra kailā
> raurava ha-ite kāḍi' more vaikuṇṭhe caḍāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adṛśya]]---unseeable; [[synonyms:a:aspṛśya]]---untouchable; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---You have accepted; [[synonyms:r:raurava]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from a hellish condition; [[synonyms:k:kāḍi']]---taking away; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhe]] [[synonyms:c:caḍāilā]]---have raised to the Vaikuṇṭha platform.

===== Translation =====

**"I am unseeable and untouchable, but You have accepted me as Your servant. This means that You have delivered me from a hellish condition and raised me to the Vaikuṇṭha platform.**
