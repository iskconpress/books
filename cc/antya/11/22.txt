====== CC Antya 11.22 ======

===== Text =====

> namaskāra kari' teṅho kailā nivedana
> śarīra sustha haya mora, asustha buddhi-mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after offering obeisances; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he, Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted; [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]---body; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:stha]]---all right; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:a:asustha]]---not in a healthy condition; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---my mind and intelligence.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa offered his obeisances to the Lord and replied, "My body is all right, but my mind and intelligence are not well."**
