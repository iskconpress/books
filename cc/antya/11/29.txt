====== CC Antya 11.29 ======

===== Text =====

> svatantra īśvara tumi hao icchāmaya
> jagat nācāo, yāre yaiche icchā haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---fully independent; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:h:hao]]---are; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---free to act according to Your desire; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the world; [[synonyms:n:nācāo]]---You are causing to dance; [[synonyms:y:yāre]]---which; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:i:icchā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---You like.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You are the fully independent Personality of Godhead. You act by Your own free will. You cause the whole world to dance and act as You like.**
