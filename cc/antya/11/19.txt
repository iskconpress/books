====== Cc. Antya 11.19 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkhyā-kīrtana pūre nāhi, ke-mate khāiba?
> mahā-prasāda āniyācha, ke-mate upekṣiba?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkhyā]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---the fixed amount of chanting; [[synonyms:p:pūre]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---is not complete; [[synonyms:k:ke]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]] [[synonyms:k:khāiba]]---how shall I eat; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:ā:āniyācha]]---you have brought the //mahā-prasāda//; [[synonyms:k:ke]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]] [[synonyms:u:upekṣiba]]---how shall I neglect.

===== Translation =====

**"I have not finished chanting my regular number of rounds. How, then, can I eat? But you have brought mahā-prasāda, and how can I neglect it?"**
