====== Cc. Antya 11.91-93 ======

===== Text =====

> "haridāsera vijayotsava ye kaila darśana
> ye ihāṅ nṛtya kaila, ye kaila kīrtana
>
> ye tāṅre vālukā dite karila gamana
> tāra madhye mahotsave ye kaila bhojana
>
> acire ha-ibe tā-sabāra 'kṛṣṇa-prāpti'
> haridāsa-daraśane haya aiche 'śakti'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:v:vijaya]]-[[synonyms:u:utsava]]---the festival of the passing away; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---has seen; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---danced; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanted; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---upon him; [[synonyms:v:vālukā]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to offer sand; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---came forward; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in that connection; [[synonyms:m:mahotsave]]---in the festival; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---took //prasāda//; [[synonyms:a:acire]]---very soon; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---attainment of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---by seeing Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave this benediction: "Anyone who has seen the festival of Śrī Haridāsa Ṭhākura's passing away, anyone who has chanted and danced here, anyone who has offered sand on the body of Haridāsa Ṭhākura and anyone who has joined this festival to partake of the prasāda will achieve the favor of Kṛṣṇa very soon. There is such wonderful power in seeing Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
