====== CC Antya 11.70 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhā beḍi' prabhu kailā kīrtana, nartana
> hari-dhvani-kolāhale bharila bhuvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:b:beḍi']]---surrounding; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]-[[synonyms:k:kolāhale]]---the tumultuous sound of the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:b:bharila]]---filled; [[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---the entire universe.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced and chanted all around the platform, and as the holy name of Hari roared tumultuously, the whole universe became filled with the vibration.**
