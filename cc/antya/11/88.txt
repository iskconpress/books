====== Cc. Antya 11.88 ======

===== Text =====

> ākaṇṭha pūrāñā sabāya karāilā bhojana
> deha' deha' bali' prabhu balena vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākaṇṭha]] [[synonyms:p:pūrāñā]]---filling to the neck; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---everyone; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---He made to eat; [[synonyms:d:deha']] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---give them more, give them more; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:balena]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---talked.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was filled up to the neck because Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu kept telling the distributors, "Give them more! Give them more!"**
