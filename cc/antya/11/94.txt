====== Cc. Antya 11.94 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛpā kari' kṛṣṇa more diyāchilā saṅga
> svatantra kṛṣṇera icchā,---kailā saṅga-bhaṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:d:diyāchilā]] [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---gave the association; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅga]]---He has broken My association.

===== Translation =====

**"Being merciful upon Me, Kṛṣṇa gave Me the association of Haridāsa Ṭhākura. Being independent in His desires, He has now broken that association.**
