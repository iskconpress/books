====== CC Antya 11.32 ======

===== Text =====

> sei līlā prabhu more kabhu nā dekhāibā
> āpanāra āge mora śarīra pāḍibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---that pastime; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---ever; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhāibā]]---do not show; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before You; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]---my body; [[synonyms:p:pāḍibā]]---let fall down.

===== Translation =====

**"I wish that You not show me this closing chapter of Your pastimes. Before that time comes, kindly let my body fall down in Your presence.**
