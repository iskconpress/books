====== CC Antya 11.59 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsera tanu prabhu kole laila uṭhāñā
> aṅgane nācena prabhu premāviṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tanu]]---the body; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kole]]---on the lap; [[synonyms:l:laila]]---took; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---raising; [[synonyms:a:aṅgane]]---in the yard; [[synonyms:n:nācena]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:premāviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming overwhelmed by ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord raised the body of Haridāsa Ṭhākura and placed it on His lap. Then He began to dance in the courtyard in great ecstatic love.**
