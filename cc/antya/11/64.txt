====== Cc. Antya 11.64 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāse samudra-jale snāna karāilā
> prabhu kahe,---"samudra ei 'mahā-tīrtha' ha-ilā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---the body of Haridāsa; [[synonyms:s:samudra]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---in the water of the sea; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---bathed; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:s:samudra]]---sea; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---has become a great place of pilgrimage.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bathed the body of Haridāsa Ṭhākura in the sea and then declared, "From this day on, this sea has become a great pilgrimage site."**
