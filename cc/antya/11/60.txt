====== CC Antya 11.60 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura āveśe avaśa sarva-bhakta-gaṇa
> premāveśe sabe nāce, karena kīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---because of the ecstatic emotions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avaśa]]---helpless; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---and perform congregational chanting.

===== Translation =====

**Because of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic love, all the devotees were helpless, and in ecstatic love they also began to dance and chant congregationally.**
