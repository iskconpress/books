====== Cc. Antya 11.48 ======

===== Text =====

> aṅgane ārambhilā prabhu mahā-saṅkīrtana
> vakreśvara-paṇḍita tāhāṅ karena nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṅgane]]---in the courtyard; [[synonyms:ā:ārambhilā]]---began; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---great congregational chanting; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Vakreśvara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---danced.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately began great congregational chanting in the courtyard. Vakreśvara Paṇḍita was the chief dancer.**
