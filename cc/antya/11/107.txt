====== CC Antya 11.107 ======

===== Text =====

> bhava-sindhu taribāre āche yāra citta
> śraddhā kari' śuna sei caitanya-caritra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhava]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of material existence; [[synonyms:t:taribāre]]---to cross over; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---is; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---desire; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with faith and love; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---life and characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Anyone who desires to cross over the ocean of nescience, please hear with great faith the life and characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
