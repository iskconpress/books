====== CC Antya 11.71 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu saba bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> samudre karilā snāna-jala-keli raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:s:samudre]]---in the sea; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---took a bath; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---playing in the water; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**After saṅkīrtana, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bathed in the sea with His devotees, swimming and playing in the water in great jubilation.**
