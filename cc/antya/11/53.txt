====== CC Antya 11.53 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa nijāgrete prabhure vasāilā
> nija-netra---dui bhṛṅga---mukha-padme dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:a:agrete]]---in front of himself; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made the Lord sit down; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:n:netra]]---his eyes; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhṛṅga]]---as if two bumblebees; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:p:padme]]---on the lotus face; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---he fixed.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura made Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sit down in front of him, and then he fixed his eyes, like two bumblebees, on the lotus face of the Lord.**
