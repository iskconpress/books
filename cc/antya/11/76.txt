====== CC Antya 11.76 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi pasārike niṣedhila
> cāṅgaḍā lañā pasāri pasāre vasila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pasārike]]---the shopkeepers; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhila]]---forbade; [[synonyms:c:cāṅgaḍā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the baskets; [[synonyms:p:pasāri]]---shopkeepers; [[synonyms:p:pasāre]] [[synonyms:v:vasila]]---sat down in their shops.

===== Translation =====

**However, Svarūpa Dāmodara stopped them, and the shopkeepers returned to their shops and sat down with their baskets.**
