====== CC Antya 11.58 ======

===== Text =====

> 'hari' 'kṛṣṇa'-śabde sabe kare kolāhala
> premānande mahāprabhu ha-ilā vihvala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---with the sound vibration; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---great noise; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---became overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**There was a tumultuous noise as they all chanted the holy names "Hari" and "Kṛṣṇa." Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became overwhelmed with ecstatic love.**
