====== CC Antya 11.45 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāle īśvara dekhi' saba bhakta lañā
> haridāse dekhite āilā śīghra kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after visiting Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by; [[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---Haridāsa; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---hastily.

===== Translation =====

**The next morning, after visiting the Jagannātha temple, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, accompanied by all His other devotees, came hastily to see Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
