====== CC Antya 11.9 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya rūpa, sanātana, jīva, raghunātha
> raghunātha, gopāla,---chaya mora nātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---to Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---Jīva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---Gopāla Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:c:chaya]]---six; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:n:nātha]]---lords.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Rūpa Gosvāmī, Sanātana Gosvāmī, Jīva Gosvāmī, Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī, and Gopāla Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī, the six Gosvāmīs of Vṛndāvana. They are all my masters.**
