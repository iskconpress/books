====== CC Antya 11.13 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhura sukhe kāla yāya
> kṛṣṇera viraha-vikāra aṅge nānā haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---time passes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viraha]]---from separation; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformations; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu very happily passed His days in this way at Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī. Feeling separation from Kṛṣṇa, He exhibited many transcendental symptoms all over His body.**
