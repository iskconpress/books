====== CC Antya 11.97 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa āchila pṛthivīra 'śiromaṇi'
> tāhā vinā ratna-śūnyā ha-ila medinī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:ā:āchila]]---was; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīra]]---of this world; [[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---the crown jewel; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without him; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:ś:śūnyā]]---without the valuable jewel; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:medinī]]---this world.

===== Translation =====

**"Haridāsa Ṭhākura was the crown jewel on the head of this world; without him, this world is now bereft of its valuable jewel."**
