====== CC Antya 11.73 ======

===== Text =====

> siṁha-dvāre āsi' prabhu pasārira ṭhāṅi
> āṅcala pātiyā prasāda māgilā tathāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming in front of the Siṁha-dvāra; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pasārira]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāṅi]]---from all the shopkeepers; [[synonyms:ā:āṅcala]] [[synonyms:p:pātiyā]]---spreading His cloth; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---Jagannātha's //prasāda//; [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---begged; [[synonyms:t:tathāi]]---there.

===== Translation =====

**Approaching the Siṁha-dvāra gate, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spread His cloth and began to beg prasāda from all the shopkeepers there.**
