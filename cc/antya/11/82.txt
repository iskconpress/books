====== CC Antya 11.82 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura śrī-haste alpa nā āise
> eka eka pāte pañca-janāra bhakṣya pariveśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---in the transcendental hands; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]---a small quantity; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---did not come; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:p:pāte]]---on each and every plate; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:j:janāra]]---of five men; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]]---eatables; [[synonyms:p:pariveśe]]---He administered.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was not accustomed to taking prasāda in small quantities. He therefore put on each plate what at least five men could eat.**
