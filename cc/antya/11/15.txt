====== CC Antya 11.15 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa gosāñi, āra rāmānanda-rāya
> rātri-dine kare doṅhe prabhura sahāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:d:doṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sahāya]]---help.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya, the chief assistants in Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes, remained with Him both day and night.**
