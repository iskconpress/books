====== Cc. Antya 11.67 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-dike bhakta-gaṇa karena kīrtana
> vakreśvara-paṇḍita karena ānande nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---all around; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---congregational chanting; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Vakreśvara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performed; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in jubilation; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**All around the body, the devotees performed congregational chanting, and Vakreśvara Paṇḍita danced in jubilation.**
