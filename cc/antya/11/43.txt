====== CC Antya 11.43 ======

===== Text =====

> madhyāhna karite prabhu calilā āpane
> īśvara dekhiyā kāli dibena daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to perform His noon duties; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calilā]] [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---aroused Himself; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---after visiting Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:kāli]]---tomorrow; [[synonyms:d:dibena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---He would see Haridāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**Because He had to perform His noon duties, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up to leave, but it was settled that the following day, after He saw Lord Jagannātha, He would return to visit Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
