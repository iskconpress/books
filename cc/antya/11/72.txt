====== CC Antya 11.72 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāse pradakṣiṇa kari' āila siṁha-dvāre
> hari-kīrtana-kolāhala sakala nagare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---Haridāsa; [[synonyms:p:pradakṣiṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---circumambulating; [[synonyms:ā:āila]] [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---came to the gate of Jagannātha temple known as Siṁha-dvāra; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---the tumultuous sound of congregational chanting; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:n:nagare]]---all over the city.

===== Translation =====

**After circumambulating the tomb of Haridāsa Ṭhākura, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the Siṁha-dvāra gate of the Jagannātha temple. The whole city chanted in congregation, and the tumultuous sound vibrated all over the city.**
