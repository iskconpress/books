====== Cc. Antya 11.87 ======

===== Text =====

> purī-bhāratīra saṅge prabhu bhikṣā kailā
> sakala vaiṣṇava tabe bhojana karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāratīra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Paramānanda Purī and Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---honored the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---began to eat.

===== Translation =====

**With Paramānanda Purī and Brahmānanda Bhāratī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down and accepted the prasāda. When He began to eat, so did all the Vaiṣṇavas.**
