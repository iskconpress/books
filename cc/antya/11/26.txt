====== Cc. Antya 11.26 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe alpa saṅkhyā kari' kara saṅkīrtana"
> haridāsa kahe,---"śuna mora satya nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:a:alpa]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkhyā]]---a reduced number of chanting; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chant the //Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura replied; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---kindly hear; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---real; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord concluded, "Now, therefore, please reduce the fixed number of times you chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra." Haridāsa Ṭhākura replied, "Kindly hear my real plea.**
