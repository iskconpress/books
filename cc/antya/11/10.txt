====== CC Antya 11.10 ======

===== Text =====

> e-saba prasāde likhi caitanya-līlā-guṇa
> yaiche taiche likhi, kari āpana pāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---of all these; [[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I am writing; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the attributes and pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]] [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---somehow or other; [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I am writing; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]] [[synonyms:p:pāvana]]---purifying myself.

===== Translation =====

**I am writing this narration of the pastimes and attributes of the Lord by the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His associates. I do not know how to write properly, but I am purifying myself by writing this description.**
