====== CC Antya 11.27 ======

===== Text =====

> hīna-jāti janma mora nindya-kalevara
> hīna-karme rata muñi adhama pāmara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hīna]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---in a low family; [[synonyms:j:janma]] [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my birth; [[synonyms:n:nindya]]---abominable; [[synonyms:k:kalevara]]---body; [[synonyms:h:hīna]]-[[synonyms:k:karme]]---in low activities; [[synonyms:r:rata]] [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I am fully engaged; [[synonyms:a:adhama]]---the lowest of men; [[synonyms:p:pāmara]]---most condemned.

===== Translation =====

**"I was born in an inferior family, and my body is most abominable. I always engage in low work. Therefore, I am the lowest, most condemned of men.**
