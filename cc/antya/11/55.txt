====== CC Antya 11.55 ======

===== Text =====

> 'śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya' śabda balena bāra bāra
> prabhu-mukha-mādhurī piye, netre jala-dhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya; [[synonyms:ś:śabda]]---vibration; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---speaks; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhurī]]---the sweetness of the face of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:piye]]---he drinks; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---through the eyes; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---a continuous flow of water.

===== Translation =====

**He began to chant the holy name of Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya again and again. As he drank the sweetness of the face of the Lord, tears constantly glided down from his eyes.**
