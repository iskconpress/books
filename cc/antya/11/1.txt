====== Cc. Antya 11.1 ======

===== Text =====

> namāmi haridāsaṁ taṁ
> caitanyaṁ taṁ ca tat-prabhum
> saṁsthitām api yan-mūrtiṁ
> svāṅke kṛtvā nanarta yaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namāmi]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:h:haridāsam]]---unto Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---him; [[synonyms:c:caitanyam]]---unto Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---Him; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhum]]---his master; [[synonyms:s:saṁsthitām]]---dead; [[synonyms:a:api]]---certainly; [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whose; [[synonyms:m:mūrtim]]---bodily form; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅke]]---on His lap; [[synonyms:k:kṛtvā]]---keeping; [[synonyms:n:nanarta]]---danced; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---He who.

===== Translation =====

**Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Haridāsa Ṭhākura and his master, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who danced with the body of Haridāsa Ṭhākura on His lap.**
