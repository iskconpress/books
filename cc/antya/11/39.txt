====== Cc. Antya 11.39 ======

===== Text =====

> caraṇe dhari' kahe haridāsa,---"nā kariha 'māyā'
> avaśya mo-adhame, prabhu, kara ei 'dayā'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:m:māyā]]---do not create an illusion; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:m:mo]]-[[synonyms:a:adhame]]---unto me, who am so fallen; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dayā]]---show this mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Catching the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "My Lord, do not create an illusion! Although I am so fallen, You must certainly show me this mercy!**
