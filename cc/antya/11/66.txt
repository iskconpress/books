====== CC Antya 11.66 ======

===== Text =====

> ḍora, kaḍāra, prasāda, vastra aṅge dilā
> vālukāra garta kari' tāhe śoyāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ḍ:ḍora]]---silken ropes; [[synonyms:k:kaḍāra]]---remnants of Lord Jagannātha's sandalwood pulp; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of Jagannātha's food; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---cloth; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:v:vālukāra]]---of sand; [[synonyms:g:garta]]---a ditch; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---within that; [[synonyms:ś:śoyāilā]]---put down.

===== Translation =====

**After a hole was dug in the sand, the body of Haridāsa Ṭhākura was placed in it. Remnants from Lord Jagannātha, such as His silken ropes, sandalwood pulp, food and cloth, were placed on the body.**
