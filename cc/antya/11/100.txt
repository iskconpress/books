====== CC Antya 11.100 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu saba bhakte vidāya dilā
> harṣa-viṣāde prabhu viśrāma karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣāde]]---in mixed happiness and distress; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:viśrāma]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---took His rest.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bid farewell to all the devotees, and He Himself, with mixed feelings of happiness and distress, took rest.**
