====== CC Antya 11.79 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate nānā prasāda bojhā bāndhāñā
> lañā āilā cāri janera mastake caḍāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:b:bojhā]]---load; [[synonyms:b:bāndhāñā]]---packing; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:j:janera]]---of the four persons; [[synonyms:m:mastake]]---on the heads; [[synonyms:c:caḍāñā]]---mounting.

===== Translation =====

**In this way varieties of prasāda were collected, then packed up in different loads and carried on the heads of the four servants.**
