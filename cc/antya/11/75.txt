====== CC Antya 11.75 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā pasāri saba cāṅgaḍā uṭhāñā
> prasāda dite āse tārā ānandita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:pasāri]]---the shopkeepers; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:c:cāṅgaḍā]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---taking a big basket; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to deliver the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:āse]]---come forward; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---they; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, all the shopkeepers immediately came forward with big baskets of prasāda, which they jubilantly delivered to Lord Caitanya.**
