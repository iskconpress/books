====== Cc. Antya 11.50 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda, sārvabhauma, sabāra agrete
> haridāsera guṇa prabhu lāgilā kahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:a:agrete]]---in front; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---began to describe.

===== Translation =====

**In front of all the great devotees like Rāmānanda Rāya and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to describe the holy attributes of Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
