====== Cc. Antya 11.84 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa, jagadānanda, kāśīśvara, śaṅkara
> cāri-jana pariveśana kare nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---four men; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---administer; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---continuously.

===== Translation =====

**The four men-Svarūpa, Jagadānanda, Kāśīśvara and Śaṅkara-distributed the prasāda continuously.**
