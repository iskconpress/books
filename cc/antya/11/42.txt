====== CC Antya 11.42 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bhakata-vatsala' prabhu, tumi, mui 'bhaktābhāsa'
> avaśya pūrābe, prabhu, mora ei āśa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakata]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsala]]---always affectionate to devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:m:mui]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---an imitation devotee; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:pūrābe]]---You will fulfill; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ā:āśa]]---expectation.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord, You are always affectionate to Your devotees. I am just an imitation devotee, but nevertheless I wish that You fulfill my desire. That is my expectation."**
