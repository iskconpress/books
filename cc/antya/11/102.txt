====== CC Antya 11.102 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyera bhakta-vātsalya ihātei jāni
> bhakta-vāñchā pūrṇa kailā nyāsi-śiromaṇi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]---affection for His devotees; [[synonyms:i:ihātei]]---from this; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---one can understand; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---the desire of the devotee; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---fully satisfied; [[synonyms:n:nyāsi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---the crown jewel of the //sannyāsīs,// Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**From the incident of Haridāsa Ṭhākura's passing away and the great care Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took in commemorating it, one can understand just how affectionate He is toward His devotees. Although He is the topmost of all sannyāsīs, He fully satisfied the desire of Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
