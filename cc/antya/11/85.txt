====== CC Antya 11.85 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu nā khāile keha nā kare bhojana
> prabhure se dine kāśī-miśrera nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:khāile]]---as long as the Lord does not eat; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---no one would eat; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on that day; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśrera]]---of Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---the invitation.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees who sat down would not accept the prasāda as long as the Lord had not eaten. On that day, however, Kāśī Miśra had extended an invitation to the Lord.**
