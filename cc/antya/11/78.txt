====== Cc. Antya 11.78 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi kahilena saba pasārire
> eka eka dravyera eka eka puñjā deha' more

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kahilena]]---said; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:p:pasārire]]---to all the shopkeepers; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:d:dravyera]]---of each particular type of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:p:puñjā]]---four palmfuls; [[synonyms:d:deha']] [[synonyms:m:more]]---deliver to me.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara said to all the shopkeepers, "Deliver to me four palmfuls of prasāda from each and every item."**
