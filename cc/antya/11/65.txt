====== Cc. Antya 11.65 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsera pādodaka piye bhakta-gaṇa
> haridāsera aṅge dilā prasāda-candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:u:udaka]]---the water that touched the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:piye]]---drink; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---smeared; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:c:candana]]---remnants of sandalwood pulp offered to Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone drank the water that had touched the lotus feet of Haridāsa Ṭhākura, and then they smeared remnants of Lord Jagannātha's sandalwood pulp over Haridāsa Ṭhākura's body.**
