====== Cc. Antya 11.68 ======

===== Text =====

> 'hari-bola' 'hari-bola' bale gaurarāya
> āpani sri-haste vālu dilā tāṅra gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:b:bola]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:b:bola]]---chant Hari, chant Hari; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---chanted; [[synonyms:g:gaurarāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with His transcendental hands; [[synonyms:v:vālu]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---placed sand; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---on his body.

===== Translation =====

**With His transcendental hands, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally covered the body of Haridāsa Ṭhākura with sand, chanting "Hari bol! Hari bol!"**
