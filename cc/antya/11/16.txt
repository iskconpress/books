====== Cc. Antya 11.16 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina govinda mahā-prasāda lañā
> haridāse dite gelā ānandita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the personal servant of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking //mahā-prasāda//; [[synonyms:h:haridāse]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to deliver to Haridāsa; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**One day Govinda, the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, went in great jubilation to deliver the remnants of Lord Jagannātha's food to Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
