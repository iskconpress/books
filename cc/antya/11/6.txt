====== CC Antya 11.6 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya nityānanda-candra jaya caitanyera prāṇa
> tomāra caraṇāravinde bhakti deha' dāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---to the life and soul of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:aravinde]]---at Your lotus feet; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---please give; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---the gift.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Nityānanda, who is the life and soul of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. My dear Lord, kindly give me engagement in devotional service at Your lotus feet.**
