====== Cc. Antya 11.81 ======

===== Text =====

> saba vaiṣṇave prabhu vasāilā sāri sāri
> āpane pariveśe prabhu lañā janā cāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇave]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made to sit down; [[synonyms:s:sāri]] [[synonyms:s:sāri]]---in lines; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:pariveśe]]---distributes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:j:janā]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four men.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made all the devotees sit in rows and personally began to distribute the prasāda, assisted by four other men.**
