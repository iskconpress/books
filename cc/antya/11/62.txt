====== Cc. Antya 11.62 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa-ṭhākure tabe vimāne caḍāñā
> samudre lañā gelā tabe kīrtana kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākure]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:v:vimāne]]---on a carrier like an airship; [[synonyms:c:caḍāñā]]---raising; [[synonyms:s:samudre]]---to the seashore; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---took; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---performing congregational chanting.

===== Translation =====

**The body of Haridāsa Ṭhākura was then raised onto a carrier that resembled an airship and taken to the sea, accompanied by congregational chanting.**
