====== Cc. Antya 11.5 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya gaura-deha kṛṣṇa svayaṁ bhagavān
> kṛpā kari' deha' prabhu, nija-pada-dāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:d:deha]]---to the transcendental body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---please give; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]---shelter at Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to the transcendental form of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is Kṛṣṇa Himself, the Supreme Personality of Godhead. My dear Lord, kindly give me shelter at Your lotus feet by Your causeless mercy.**
