====== CC Antya 11.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya dayāmaya
> jayādvaita-priya nityānanda-priya jaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---to the most merciful; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:p:priya]]---to the dear master of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:priya]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is very dear to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is very merciful and who is very dear to Advaita Ācārya and Lord Nityānanda.**
