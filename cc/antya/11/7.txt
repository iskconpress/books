====== CC Antya 11.7 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jayādvaita-candra caitanyera ārya
> sva-caraṇe bhakti deha' jayādvaitācārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---respected by the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at Your lotus feet; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---please give devotional service; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---to Advaita Ācārya.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Advaita Ācārya, who is treated by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as superior due to His age and respectability. Please give me engagement in devotional service at Your lotus feet.**
