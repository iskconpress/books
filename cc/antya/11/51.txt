====== CC Antya 11.51 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsera guṇa kahite prabhu ha-ilā pañca-mukha
> kahite kahite prabhura bāḍe mahā-sukha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---speaking; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---as if possessing five mouths; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---while He was speaking; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increased; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---great happiness.

===== Translation =====

**As He described the transcendental attributes of Haridāsa Ṭhākura, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu seemed to possess five mouths. The more He described, the more His great happiness increased.**
