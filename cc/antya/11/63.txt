====== CC Antya 11.63 ======

===== Text =====

> āge mahāprabhu calena nṛtya karite karite
> pāche nṛtya kare vakreśvara bhakta-gaṇa-sāthe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calena]]---goes; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---performing; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---dances; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sāthe]]---with other devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced in front of the procession, and Vakreśvara Paṇḍita, along with the other devotees, chanted and danced behind Him.**
