====== CC Antya 3.212 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi haridāsa viprera doṣa nā la-ilā
> tathāpi īśvara tāre phala bhuñjāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---offense; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---did not; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---take seriously; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---the result of insulting a Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:b:bhuñjāilā]]---made to suffer.

===== Translation =====

**Although Haridāsa Ṭhākura, as a Vaiṣṇava, did not take seriously the brāhmaṇa's offense, the Supreme Personality of Godhead could not tolerate it, and thus he made the brāhmaṇa suffer the consequences.**
