====== CC Antya 3.7 ======

===== Text =====

> nitya āise, prabhu tāre kare mahā-prīta
> yāṅhā prīti tāṅhā āise,---bālakera rīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nitya]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---he comes daily; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---to him; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prīta]]---very affectionate behavior; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhā]] [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---wherever there is love; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---one comes there; [[synonyms:b:bālakera]] [[synonyms:r:rīta]]---the nature of a small boy.

===== Translation =====

**The boy came every day to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who treated him with great affection. It is the nature of ay boy to go see a ma who loves him.**
