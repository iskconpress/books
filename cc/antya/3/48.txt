====== CC Antya 3.48 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva gūḍha artha kichui nā jāni
> bāhya artha karibāre kari ṭānāṭāni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---deep meaning; [[synonyms:k:kichui]]---any; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I do not know; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]] [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to explain the external meanings; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I make; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭānāṭāni]]---hard endeavor.

===== Translation =====

**I do not know the deep meaning of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's activities. As far as possible I shall try to explain them externally.**
