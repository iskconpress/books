====== CC Antya 3.70 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā jaṅgamera haya saṁsāra-kṣaya
> sthāvare se śabda lāge, pratidhvani haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:j:jaṅgamera]]---of the living entities who can move; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---annihilation of bondage to the material world; [[synonyms:s:sthāvare]]---unto the nonmoving living entities; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:ś:śabda]]---that transcendental vibration; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---touches; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---echo; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord, the moving entities who have heard Your loud saṅkīrtana have already been delivered from bondage to the material world, and after the nonmoving living entities like trees hear it, there is an echo.**
