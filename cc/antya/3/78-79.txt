====== CC Antya 3.78-79 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa bale,---"tomāra yāvat martye sthiti
> tāvat sthāvara-jaṅgama, sarva jīva-jāti
>
> saba mukta kari' tumi vaikuṇṭhe pāṭhāibā
> sūkṣma-jīve punaḥ karme udbuddha karibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as long as; [[synonyms:m:martye]]---in this material world; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---situation; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]]---for that duration of time; [[synonyms:s:sthāvara]]-[[synonyms:j:jaṅgama]]---nonmoving and moving; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---species of living entities; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mukta]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---liberating; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhe]]---to the spiritual world; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāibā]]---will send; [[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]-[[synonyms:j:jīve]]---the undeveloped living entities; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:k:karme]]---in their activities; [[synonyms:u:udbuddha]] [[synonyms:k:karibā]]---You will awaken;

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa said, "My Lord, as long as You are situated within the material world, You will send to the spiritual sky all the developed moving and nonmoving living entities in different species. Then again You will awaken the living entities who are not yet developed and engage them in activities.**
