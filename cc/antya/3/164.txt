====== CC Antya 3.164 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāntera apamāna ye deśa-grāme haya
> eka janāra doṣe saba deśa ujāḍaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāntera]]---of persons who are highly advanced in spiritual life; [[synonyms:a:apamāna]]---disrespect; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:d:deśa]]-[[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in which country or village; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:j:janāra]]---of one man; [[synonyms:d:doṣe]]---for the fault; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:d:deśa]]---the whole country; [[synonyms:u:ujāḍaya]]---becomes afflicted.

===== Translation =====

**Wherever an advanced devotee is insulted, for one man's fault the entire town or place is afflicted.**
