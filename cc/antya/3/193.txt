====== Cc. Antya 3.193 ======

===== Text =====

> kruddha hañā bale sei saroṣa vacana
> "bhāvukera siddhānta śuna, paṇḍitera gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kruddha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming very angry; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---said; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:r:roṣa]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---angry words; [[synonyms:b:bhāvukera]]---of an emotional person; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---conclusion; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---just hear; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O assembly of learned scholars.

===== Translation =====

**This young man, Gopāla Cakravartī, became very angry upon hearing the statements of Haridāsa Ṭhākura. He immediately criticized him. "O assembly of learned scholars," he said, "just hear the conclusion of the emotional devotee.**
