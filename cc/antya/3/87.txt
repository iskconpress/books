====== CC Antya 3.87 ======

===== Text =====

> ye kahe,---caitanya-mahimā mora gocara haya'
> se jānuka, mora punaḥ ei ta' niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---whoever says; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:gocara]]---known to me; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:j:jānuka]]---he may know; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---of me; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---this is the decision;

===== Translation =====

**"One may say that he understands the glories of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He may know whatever he may know, but as far as I am concerned, this is my conclusion.**
