====== Cc. Antya 3.156 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' krodhe gosāñi uṭhiyā calilā
> tāre daṇḍa dite se grāme nā rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:k:krodhe]]---in anger; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---got up and left; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to chastise; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the village; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---did not stay.

===== Translation =====

**Having said this, Lord Nityānanda stood up and left in an angry mood. To chastise Rāmacandra Khān, He did not even stay in that village.**
