====== Cc. Antya 3.161 ======

===== Text =====

> strī-putra-sahita rāmacandrere bāndhiyā
> tāra ghara-grāma luṭe tina-dina rahiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---his wife and children; [[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandrere]] [[synonyms:b:bāndhiyā]]---arresting Rāmacandra Khān; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]-[[synonyms:g:grāma]]---house and village; [[synonyms:l:luṭe]]---plundered; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:r:rahiyā]]---staying three days.

===== Translation =====

**He arrested Rāmacandra Khān, along with his wife and sons, and then he continuously plundered the house and village for three days.**
