====== Cc. Antya 3.121 ======

===== Text =====

> tāvat ihāṅ vasi' śuna nāma-saṅkīrtana
> nāma pūrṇa haile, pūrṇa habe tomāra mana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāvat]]---until that time; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haile]]---as soon as the regular chanting is fulfilled; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---will be; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---your mind.

===== Translation =====

**"Please sit down and hear the chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra until my regular chanting is finished. Then your desire will surely be fulfilled."**
