{{description>"My dear Lord, all the incidents that took place while You were going to Vṛndāvana through the forest known as Jhārikhaṇḍa have been related to me by Your servant Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya.}}

====== Cc. Antya 3.73 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche kailā jhārikhaṇḍe vṛndāvana yāite
> balabhadra-bhaṭṭācārya kahiyāchena āmāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---You have performed; [[synonyms:j:jhārikhaṇḍe]]---in the forest known as Jhārikhaṇḍa; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---while going to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:b:balabhadra]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Your servant Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahiyāchena]] [[synonyms:ā:āmāte]]---has said to me.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, all the incidents that took place while You were going to Vṛndāvana through the forest known as Jhārikhaṇḍa have been related to me by Your servant Balabhadra Bhaṭṭācārya.**
