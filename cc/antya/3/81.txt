====== Cc. Antya 3.81 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yena raghunātha saba āyodhyā lañā
> vaikuṇṭhake gelā, anya-jīve āyodhyā bharāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āyodhyā]]---the population of Āyodhyā; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with Him; [[synonyms:v:vaikuṇṭhake]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went back to Vaikuṇṭhaloka; [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:j:jīve]]---other living entities; [[synonyms:ā:āyodhyā]]---Āyodhyā; [[synonyms:b:bharāñā]]---filling.

===== Translation =====

**"Previously, when Lord Rāmacandra left this world, He took with Him all the living entities of Āyodhyā. Then He filled Āyodhyā again with other living entities.**
