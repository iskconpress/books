====== Cc. Antya 3.173 ======

===== Text =====

> ṭhākura dekhi' dui bhāi kailā abhyutthāna
> pāya paḍi' āsana dilā kariyā sammāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:a:abhyutthāna]]---stood up; [[synonyms:p:pāya]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---falling at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---offered a sitting place; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---with great respect.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Haridāsa Ṭhākura, the two brothers immediately stood up and fell at his lotus feet. Then with great respect they offered him a place to sit.**
