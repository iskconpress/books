====== Cc. Antya 3.90 ======

===== Text =====

> manera santoṣe tāṅre kailā āliṅgana
> bāhye prakāśite e-saba karilā varjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manera]] [[synonyms:s:santoṣe]]---with complete satisfaction of the mind; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---He embraced; [[synonyms:b:bāhye]]---externally; [[synonyms:p:prakāśite]]---to disclose; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all this; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:varjana]]---He avoided;

===== Translation =====

**Greatly satisfied by the statements of Haridāsa Ṭhākura, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced him. Outwardly, however, He avoided further discussions of these matters.**
