====== CC Antya 3.138 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' tāre 'nāma' upadeśa kari'
> uṭhiyā calilā ṭhākura bali' 'hari' 'hari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---her; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---instructing about the process of chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]]---standing up; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---left; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---chanting; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra.//

===== Translation =====

**After thus instructing the prostitute about the process of chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra, Haridāsa Ṭhākura stood up and left, continuously chanting "Hari, Hari."**
