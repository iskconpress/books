====== CC Antya 3.254 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyāvatāre vahe premāmṛta-vanyā
> saba jīva preme bhāse, pṛthivī haila dhanyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---by the incarnation of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---flows; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---of the eternal nectar of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vanyā]]---the flood; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---all living entities; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---float; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivī]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:d:dhanyā]]---thankful.

===== Translation =====

**"There is now a flood of the eternal nectar of love of Godhead due to the incarnation of Lord Caitanya, All living entities are floating in that flood. The entire world is now thankful to the Lord.**
