====== CC Antya 3.33 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇe bhoga lāgāñā yabe kailā dhyāna
> āmāra sphūrti haila, aśru bharila nayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---offering; [[synonyms:l:lāgāñā]]---giving; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]]---you meditated; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---sudden appearance; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:b:bharila]]---filled; [[synonyms:n:nayana]]---your eyes.

===== Translation =====

**"You offered the food to Lord Kṛṣṇa, and while you were in meditation I suddenly appeared, and your eyes filled with tears.**
