====== CC Antya 3.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe gosāñira guṇa-yaśa saba loke gāibe
> tabe gosāñira pratiṣṭhā puruṣottame ha-ibe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:y:yaśa]]---attributes and reputation; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:l:loke]]---everyone; [[synonyms:g:gāibe]]---will talk about; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:pratiṣṭhā]]---the position; [[synonyms:p:puruṣottame]]---in Puruṣottama (Jagannātha Purī); [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---will be.

===== Translation =====

**"You are known as Gosāñi [teacher or ācārya], but now talk about Your attributes and reputation will spread throughout the city of Puruṣottama. How Your position will be impaired!"**
