====== Cc. Antya 3.162 ======

===== Text =====

> sei ghare tina dina kare amedhya randhana
> āra dina sabā lañā karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in that room; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---for three days; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:amedhya]] [[synonyms:r:randhana]]---cooking the flesh of a cow; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by his followers; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---left.

===== Translation =====

**In that very room he cooked the flesh of a cow for three consecutive days. Then the next day he left, accompanied by his followers.**
