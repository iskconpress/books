====== CC Antya 3.107 ======

===== Text =====

> khāṅna kahe,---"mora pāika yāuka tomāra sane
> tomāra sahita ekatra tāre dhari' yena āne"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:khāṅna]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmacandra Khān said; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:p:pāika]]---my constable; [[synonyms:y:yāuka]]---let him go; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with you; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with you; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---together; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---arresting; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---so that; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---can bring.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmacandra Khān said to the prostitute, "My constable will go with you so that as soon as he sees you with Haridāsa Ṭhākura, immediately he will arrest him and bring both of you to me."**
