====== CC Antya 3.166 ======

===== Text =====

> hiraṇya, govardhana---dui mulukera majumadāra
> tāra purohita---'balarāma' nāma tāṅra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hiraṇya]]---Hiraṇya; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]---Govardhana; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:m:mulukera]]---of that country; [[synonyms:m:majumadāra]]---treasurers of the government; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---their; [[synonyms:p:purohita]]---priest; [[synonyms:b:balarāma]]---Balarāma; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his.

===== Translation =====

**Hiraṇya and Govardhana were the two governmental treasurers in that division of the country. Their priest was named Balarāma Ācārya.**

===== Purport =====

The word //majumadāra// refers to a treasurer who keeps accounts of revenue.
