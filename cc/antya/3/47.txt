====== Cc. Antya 3.47 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyera līlā---gambhīra, koṭi-samudra haite
> ki lāgi' ki kare, keha nā pāre bujhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---very deep; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:s:samudra]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---more than millions of seas; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for what reason; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---what He does; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---anyone; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāre]] [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---can understand.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are deeper than millions of seas and oceans. Therefore no one can understand what He does nor why He does it.**
