====== Cc. Antya 3.66 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā prabhura sukha bāḍaye antare
> punarapi bhaṅgī kari' puchaye tāṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:b:bāḍaye]]---increased; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---still; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgī]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---as a matter of course; [[synonyms:p:puchaye]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---inquires from Haridāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**As Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this from Haridāsa Ṭhākura, the happiness within His heart increased, but as a matter of course, He still inquired further.**
