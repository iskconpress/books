====== CC Antya 3.269 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi kaḍacāya ye-līlā likhila
> raghunātha-dāsa-mukhe ye saba śunila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kaḍacāya]]---in his notes; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:l:likhila]]---has noted; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from the mouth of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śunila]]---I have heard.

===== Translation =====

**I have heard from the mouth of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī all that Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī recorded in his notes about the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
