====== CC Antya 3.243 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' karena teṅho nāma-saṅkīrtana
> sei nārī vasi' kare śrī-nāma-śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---that woman; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hearing the holy name.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Haridāsa Ṭhākura continued to chant the holy name of the Lord. Thus the woman sitting before him began to hear the chanting of the holy name.**
