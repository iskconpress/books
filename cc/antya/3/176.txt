====== Cc. Antya 3.176 ======

===== Text =====

> tina-lakṣa nāma ṭhākura karena kīrtana
> nāmera mahimā uṭhāila paṇḍita-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]---300,000; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---holy names of the Lord; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---used to chant; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāila]]---raised; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the learned scholars.

===== Translation =====

**It was mentioned in the assembly that Haridāsa Ṭhākura chanted the holy names of Kṛṣṇa 300,000 times a day. Thus all the learned scholars began to discuss the glories of the holy name.**
