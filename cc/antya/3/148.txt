====== CC Antya 3.148 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-gosāñi gauḍe yabe āilā
> prema pracārite tabe bhramite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back; [[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:p:pracārite]]---to preach the cult of //bhakti,// love of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:b:bhramite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to tour.

===== Translation =====

**When Lord Nityānanda returned to Bengal to preach the cult of bhakti, love of Godhead, He began touring all over the country.**
