====== Cc. Antya 3.116 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' sei veśyā vasiyā rahilā
> kīrtana kare haridāsa prātaḥ-kāla hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:veśyā]]---that prostitute; v[[synonyms:a:asiyā]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---stayed there sitting; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---there was the light of morning.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, the prostitute remained sitting there while Haridāsa Ṭhākura chanted on his beads until the light of morning appeared.**
