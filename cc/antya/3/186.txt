====== CC Antya 3.186 ======

===== Text =====

> mukti' tuccha-phala haya nāmābhāsa haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:'mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:t:tuccha]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---insignificant result; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from a glimpse of awakening of offenseless chanting of the holy name.

===== Translation =====

**"Liberation is the insignificant result derived from a glimpse of awakening of offenseless chanting of the holy name.**
