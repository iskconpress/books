====== Cc. Antya 3.6 ======

===== Text =====

> bāra bāra niṣedha kare brāhmaṇa-kumāre
> prabhure nā dekhile sei rahite nā pāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:n:niṣedha]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---forbids; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kumāre]]---the son of the brāhmaṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhile]]---without seeing; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that boy; [[synonyms:r:rahite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---could not stay.

===== Translation =====

**Dāmodara Paṇḍita again and again forbade the son of the brāhmaṇa to visit the Lord, but the boy could not bear staying home and not seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
