====== CC Antya 3.160 ======

===== Text =====

> āsi' sei durgā-maṇḍape vāsā kaila
> avadhya vadha kari' māṁsa se-ghare rāndhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:durgā]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍape]]---at that very place of the Durgā-maṇḍapa; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made his residence; [[synonyms:a:avadhya]]---a cow or calf, which is not to be killed; [[synonyms:v:vadha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---killing; [[synonyms:m:māṁsa]]---meat; [[synonyms:s:se]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in that place; [[synonyms:r:rāndhāila]]---cooked.

===== Translation =====

**The Mohammedan minister made his residence in the Durgā-maṇḍapa of Rāmacandra Khān. He killed a cow and cooked the meat at that very place.**
