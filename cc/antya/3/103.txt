====== CC Antya 3.103 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāse loke pūje, sahite nā pāre
> tāṅra apamāna karite nānā upāya kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---unto Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people; [[synonyms:p:pūje]]---offer respect; [[synonyms:s:sahite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---he could not tolerate; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:a:apamāna]]---dishonor; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---means; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---plans.

===== Translation =====

**Unable to tolerate that such respect was being offered to Haridāsa Ṭhākura, Rāmacandra Khān planned in various ways to dishonor him.**
