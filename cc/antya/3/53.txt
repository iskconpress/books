====== Cc. Antya 3.53 ======

===== Text =====

> yavana-sakalera 'mukti' habe anāyāse
> 'hā rāma, hā rāma' bali' kahe nāmābhāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yavana]]-[[synonyms:s:sakalera]]---of all the //yavanas//; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:a:anāyāse]]---very easily; [[synonyms:h:hā]] [[synonyms:r:rāma]] [[synonyms:h:hā]] [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---O Lord Rāma, O Lord Rāma; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---they say; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāse]]---almost chanting the holy name of the Lord without offenses.

===== Translation =====

**"Because the yavanas are accustomed to saying, 'hā rāma, hā rāma' [O Lord Rāmacandra], they will very easily be delivered by this nāmābhāsa.**
