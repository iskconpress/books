====== CC Antya 3.46 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-ta kahila dāmodarera vākya-daṇḍa
> yāhāra śravaṇe bhāge 'ajñāna pāṣaṇḍa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:t:ta]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---I have described; [[synonyms:d:dāmodarera]]---of Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---chastisement by words; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing which; [[synonyms:b:bhāge]]---goes away; [[synonyms:a:ajñāna]] [[synonyms:p:pāṣaṇḍa]]---the atheist of ignorance.

===== Translation =====

**In this way I have described Dāmodara Paṇḍita's verbal chastisements. As one hears about this, atheistic principles and ignorance depart.**
