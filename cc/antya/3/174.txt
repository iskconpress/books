====== CC Antya 3.174 ======

===== Text =====

> aneka paṇḍita sabhāya, brāhmaṇa, sajjana
> dui bhāi mahā-paṇḍita---hiraṇya, govardhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---many learned scholars; [[synonyms:s:sabhāya]]---in that assembly; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]---//brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---respectable gentlemen; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---very learned scholars; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇya]]---Hiraṇya; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]---Govardhana.

===== Translation =====

**In that assembly were many learned scholars, brāhmaṇas and respectable gentlemen. The two brothers Hiraṇya and Govardhana were also greatly learned.**
