====== CC Antya 3.245 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata tina-dina kare āgamana
> nānā bhāva dekhāya, yāte brahmāra hare mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---three days; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---she does; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---approaching; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---all kinds of feminine postures; [[synonyms:d:dekhāya]]---exhibits; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:b:brahmāra]]---even of Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---attracts; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**For three days she approached Haridāsa Ṭhākura in this way, exhibiting various feminine postures that would bewilder the mind of even Lord Brahmā.**
