====== Cc. Antya 3.97 ======

===== Text =====

> saba kahā nā yāya haridāsera caritra
> keha kichu kahe karite āpanā pavitra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kahā]]---to speak; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is not possible; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]] [[synonyms:c:caritra]]---the characteristics of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---someone says something; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---just to make; [[synonyms:ā:āpanā]]---himself; [[synonyms:p:pavitra]]---purified.

===== Translation =====

**No one can describe all the qualities of Haridāsa Ṭhākura. One may say something about them just to purify himself.**
