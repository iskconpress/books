====== Cc. Antya 3.74 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsudeva jīva lāgi' kaila nivedana
> tabe aṅgīkāra kailā jīvera mocana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---the Lord's devotee named Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:j:jīva]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for all living entities; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted his appeal; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---You accepted; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]] [[synonyms:m:mocana]]---the deliverance of all living entities.

===== Translation =====

**"When Your devotee Vāsudeva Datta submitted his plea at Your lotus feet for the deliverance of all living entities, You accepted that request.**
