====== CC Antya 3.234 ======

===== Text =====

> āsiyā tulasīre sei kailā namaskāra
> tulasī parikramā kari' gelā goṅphā-dvāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tulasīre]]---unto the //tulasī// plant; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that woman; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]---the //tulasī// plant; [[synonyms:p:parikramā]]---circumambulating; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:g:goṅphā]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---to the door of the cave.

===== Translation =====

**After coming there, the woman offered obeisances to the tulasī plant, and after circumambulating the tulasī plant she came to the door of the cave where Haridāsa Ṭhākura was sitting.**
