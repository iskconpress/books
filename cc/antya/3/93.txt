====== CC Antya 3.93 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu nija-bhakta-pāśe yāñā
> haridāsera guṇa kahe śata-mukha hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---to His personal devotees; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the transcendental qualities of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---explains; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:m:mukha]]---as if with hundreds of mouths; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to His personal devotees and began speaking about Haridāsa Ṭhākura's transcendental qualities as if He had hundreds of mouths. hundreds of mouths.**
