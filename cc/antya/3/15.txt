====== CC Antya 3.15 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita hañā mane kene vicāra nā kara?
> rāṇḍī brāhmaṇīra bālake prīti kene kara?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a learned teacher; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do You not consider; [[synonyms:r:rāṇḍī]] [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇīra]]---of a widowed wife of a //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:b:bālake]]---unto the son; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---affection; [[synonyms:k:kene]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---why do You show.

===== Translation =====

**"Dear Lord, You are a learned teacher. Why then don't You consider that this boy is the son of a widowed brāhmaṇī? Why are You so affectionate to him?**
