====== CC Antya 3.75 ======

===== Text =====

> jagat nistārite ei tomāra avatāra
> bhakta-bhāva āge tāte kailā aṅgīkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagat]] [[synonyms:n:nistārite]]---to deliver the whole world; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---Your incarnation; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the mood of a devotee; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---previously; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---You accepted;

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You have accepted the form of a devotee just to deliver all the fallen souls of this world.**
