====== CC Antya 3.262 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyāvatāre kṛṣṇa-preme lubdha hañā
> brahma-śiva-sanakādi pṛthivīte janmiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāre]]---in the incarnation of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---for ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:lubdha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very greedy; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]]---Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:s:sanaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the Kumāras and others; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīte]]---on this earth; [[synonyms:j:janmiyā]]---taking birth.

===== Translation =====

**During the incarnation of Lord Caitanya to inaugurate the Kṛṣṇa consciousness movement, even such personalities as Lord Brahmā, Lord Śiva and the four Kumāras took birth upon this earth, being allured by ecstatic love of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
