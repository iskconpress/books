====== Cc. Antya 3.140 ======

===== Text =====

> māthā muḍi' eka-vastre rahila sei ghare
> rātri-dine tina-lakṣa nāma grahaṇa kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māthā]] [[synonyms:m:muḍi']]---shaving her head; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:v:vastre]]---wearing one cloth; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in that room; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---throughout the entire day and night; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣa]]---300,000; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---holy names; [[synonyms:g:grahaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---chants.

===== Translation =====

**The prostitute shaved her head clean in accordance with Vaiṣṇava principles and stayed in that room wearing only one cloth. Following in the footsteps of her spiritual master, she began chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra 300,000 times a day. She chanted throughout the entire day and night.**
