====== CC Antya 3.132 ======

===== Text =====

> "veśyā hañā muñi pāpa kariyāchoṅ apāra
> kṛpā kari' kara mo-adhame nistāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:veśyā]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a prostitute; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]---sinful activities; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchoṅ]]---have done; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---please do; [[synonyms:m:mo]]-[[synonyms:a:adhame]]---unto me, the most fallen; [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---deliverance.

===== Translation =====

**"Because I have taken the profession of a prostitute," she said, "I have performed unlimited sinful acts. My lord, be merciful to me. Deliver my fallen soul."**
