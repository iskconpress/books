====== CC Antya 3.98 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-dāsa yāhā nā kaila varṇana
> haridāsera guṇa kichu śuna, bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---whatever; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---described; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**O devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, please hear something about the qualities of Haridāsa Ṭhākura that Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has not described in detail.**
