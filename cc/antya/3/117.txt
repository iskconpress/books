====== CC Antya 3.117 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāla dekhi' veśyā uṭhiyā calilā
> saba samācāra yāi khāṅnere kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the morning; [[synonyms:v:veśyā]]---the prostitute; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---stood up and left; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---all information; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---going; [[synonyms:k:khāṅnere]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---she spoke to Rāmacandra Khān.

===== Translation =====

**When she saw that it was morning, the prostitute stood up and left. Coming before Rāmacandra Khān, she informed him of all the news.**
