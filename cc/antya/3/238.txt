====== CC Antya 3.238 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' nānā-bhāva karaye prakāśa
> yāhāra darśane munira haya dhairya-nāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---various postures; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---began to manifest; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---seeing which; [[synonyms:m:munira]]---of even the great philosophers; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:d:dhairya]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---loss of patience.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, she began to manifest various postures, which even the greatest philosopher would lose his patience upon seeing.**
