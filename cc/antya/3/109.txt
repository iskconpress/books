====== CC Antya 3.109 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-kāle sei veśyā suveśa dhariyā
> haridāsera vāsāya gela ullasita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at night; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:veśyā]]---prostitute; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:veśa]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---dressing herself very nicely; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:v:vāsāya]]---to the place; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:u:ullasita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---with great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**At night the prostitute, after dressing herself most attractively, went to the cottage of Haridāsa Ṭhākura with great jubilation.**
