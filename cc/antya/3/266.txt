====== Cc. Antya 3.266 ======

===== Text =====

> māyā-dāsī 'prema' māge,---ithe ki vismaya?
> 'sādhu-kṛpā-'nāma' vinā 'prema' nā janmaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māyā]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsī]]---the external energy is a maidservant; [[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:m:māge]]---she wants love of Godhead; [[synonyms:i:ithe]]---in this; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---what is the wonder; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy of the devotee; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:janmaya]]---is not possible.

===== Translation =====

**What is the wonder if the maidservant of Kṛṣṇa, His external energy, begs for love of Godhead? Without the mercy of a devotee and without the chanting of the holy name of the Lord, love of Godhead cannot be possible.**
