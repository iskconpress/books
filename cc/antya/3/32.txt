====== CC Antya 3.32 ======

===== Text =====

> ei māgha-saṅkrāntye tumi randhana karilā
> nānā vyañjana, kṣīra, piṭhā, pāyasa rāndhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:māgha]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkrāntye]]---on the occasion of the Māgha-saṅkrānti festival; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:r:randhana]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---cooked; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---varieties of vegetables; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---condensed milk; [[synonyms:p:piṭhā]]---cakes; [[synonyms:p:pāyasa]]---sweet rice; [[synonyms:r:rāndhilā]]---cooked.

===== Translation =====

**"During the last Māgha-saṅkrānti festival, you cooked varieties of vegetables, condensed milk, cakes and sweet rice for Me.**
