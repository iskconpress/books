====== Cc. Antya 3.91 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara-svabhāva,---aiśvarya cāhe ācchādite
> bhakta-ṭhāñi lukāite nāre, haya ta' vidite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---the characteristic of the Supreme personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]---opulence; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:ā:ācchādite]]---to cover; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---before His devotee; [[synonyms:l:lukāite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---He cannot cover; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:v:vidite]]---is well-known.

===== Translation =====

**This is a characteristic of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Although He wants to cover His opulence, He cannot do so before His devotees. This is well-known everywhere.**
