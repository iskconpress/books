====== CC Antya 3.125 ======

===== Text =====

> āji samāpta ha-ibe,---hena jñāna chila
> samasta rātri niluṅ nāma samāpta nā haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:s:samāpta]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---will be finished; [[synonyms:h:hena]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]] [[synonyms:c:chila]]---I thought that; [[synonyms:s:samasta]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---all night; [[synonyms:n:niluṅ]]---I took; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:samāpta]]---finished; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was not.

===== Translation =====

**"I thought that today I would be able to finish my performance of yajña, my chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra. I tried my best to chant the holy name all night, but I still did not finish.**
