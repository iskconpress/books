====== CC Antya 3.180 ======

===== Text =====

> ānuṣaṅgika phala nāmera---'mukti', 'pāpa-nāśa'
> tāhāra dṛṣṭānta yaiche sūryera prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānuṣaṅgika]]---concomitant; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---result; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---extinction of the resultant actions of sinful life; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭānta]]---example; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:s:sūryera]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---light of the sun.

===== Translation =====

**"Liberation and extinction of the reactions of sinful life are two concomitant by-products of chanting the holy name of the Lord. An example is found in the gleams of morning sunlight.**
