====== CC Antya 3.199 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahena,---"yadi nāmābhāse 'mukti' naya
> tabe āmāra nāka kāṭimu,---ei suniścaya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura said; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāse]]---simply by the awakening of the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:m:mukti]] [[synonyms:n:naya]]---mukti is not available; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:n:nāka]]---nose; [[synonyms:k:kāṭimu]]---I shall cut off; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:suniścaya]]---certain.

===== Translation =====

**Then Haridāsa Ṭhākura accepted the challenge offered by Gopāla Cakravartī. "If by nāmābhāsa liberation is not available," he said, "certainly I shall cut off my nose."**
