====== CC Antya 3.204 ======

===== Text =====

> sabhā-sahite haridāsera paḍilā caraṇe
> haridāsa hāsi' kahe madhura-vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sahite]]---with all the members of the assembly; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---fell down at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]-[[synonyms:v:vacane]]---in a sweet voice.

===== Translation =====

**With all the members of the assembly, the two Majumadāras fell at the lotus feet of Haridāsa Ṭhākura. Haridāsa Ṭhākura was smiling, however, and he spoke in a sweet voice.**
