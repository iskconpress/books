====== CC Antya 3.96 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-maṅgale śrī-vṛndāvana-dāsa
> haridāsera guṇa kichu kariyāchena prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgale]]---in the book known as //Caitanya-maṅgala (Caitanya-bhāgavata)//; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Śrī Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifested.

===== Translation =====

**In Caitanya-maṅgala, Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has described the attributes of Haridāsa Ṭhākura to some extent.**
