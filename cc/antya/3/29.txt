====== CC Antya 3.29 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' mātāra mane santoṣa janmāiha
> āra guhya-kathā tāṅre smaraṇa karāiha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mātāra]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind of My mother; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]] [[synonyms:j:janmāiha]]---give satisfaction; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:g:guhya]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---very confidential message; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---her; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:karāiha]]---make to remember.

===== Translation =====

**"Speaking in this way, satisfy the mind of mother Śacī. Also, remind her of one most confidential incident with this message from Me.**
