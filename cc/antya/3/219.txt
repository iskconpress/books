====== CC Antya 3.219 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-mahā-vipra ethā kulīna-samāja
> nīce ādara kara, nā vāsaha bhaya lāja!!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---great, great //brāhmaṇas//; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:s:samāja]]---aristocratic society; [[synonyms:n:nīce]]---to a low-class person; [[synonyms:ā:ādara]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You show honor; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:v:vāsaha]]---You do not care for; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]] [[synonyms:l:lāja]]---fear or shame.

===== Translation =====

**"Sir, You are living within a society of great, great brāhmaṇas and aristocrats, but without fear or shame You adore a lower-class man like me.**
