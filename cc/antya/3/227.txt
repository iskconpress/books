====== CC Antya 3.227 ======

===== Text =====

> āra alaukika eka caritra tāṅhāra
> yāhāra śravaṇe loke haya camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:c:caritra]]---characteristic; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:y:yāhāraśravaṇe]]---in hearing which; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---in human society; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**There is another incident concerning Haridāsa Ṭhākura's uncommon behavior. One will be astonished to hear about it.**
