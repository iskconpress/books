====== CC Antya 3.86 ======

===== Text =====

> taiche tumi navadvīpe kari' avatāra
> sakala-brahmāṇḍa-jīvera karilā nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:taiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpe]]---at Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---descending as an incarnation; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---of the universe; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---the living entities; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---have delivered;

===== Translation =====

**"By descending as an incarnation at Navadvīpa, You, just like Kṛṣṇa, have already delivered all the living entities of the universe.**
