====== CC Antya 3.110 ======

===== Text =====

> tulasī namaskari' haridāsera dvāre yāñā
> gosāñire namaskari' rahilā dāṇḍāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tulasī]] [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---after offering obeisances to the //tulasī// plant; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---unto the //ācārya//; [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]] [[synonyms:d:dāṇḍāñā]]---remained standing.

===== Translation =====

**After offering obeisances to the tulasī plant, she went to the door of Haridāsa Ṭhākura, offered him obeisances and stood there.**
