====== Cc. Antya 3.167 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsera kṛpā-pātra, tāte bhakti-māne
> yatna kari' ṭhākurere rākhilā sei grāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---favored by Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:m:māne]]---a great devotee of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:y:yatna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great care and attention; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurere]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the village.

===== Translation =====

**Balarāma Ācārya, being favored by Haridāsa Ṭhākura, was very attached to him. Therefore he kept Haridāsa Ṭhākura in the village with great care and attention.**
