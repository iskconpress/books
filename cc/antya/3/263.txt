====== CC Antya 3.263 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nāma lañā nāce, prema-vanyāya bhāse
> nārada-prahlādādi āse manuṣya-prakāśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---chanting; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vanyāya]]---in the inundation of the flood of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---float; [[synonyms:n:nārada]]---the sage Nārada; [[synonyms:p:prahlāda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and devotees like Prahlāda; [[synonyms:ā:āse]]---come; [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśe]]---as if human beings.

===== Translation =====

**All of them, including the great sage Nārada and devotees like Prahlāda, came here as if human beings, chanting the holy names of Lord Kṛṣṇa together and dancing and floating in the inundation of love of Godhead.**
