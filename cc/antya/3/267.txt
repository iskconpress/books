====== Cc. Antya 3.267 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-gosāñira līlāra ei ta' svabhāva
> tribhuvana nāce, gāya, pāñā prema-bhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the characteristic; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvana]] [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---the three worlds dance; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**In the activities of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the three worlds dance and chant, having come in touch with love of Godhead. This is the characteristic of His pastimes.**
