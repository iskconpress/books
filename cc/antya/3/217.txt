====== CC Antya 3.217 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryera ghare nitya bhikṣā-nirvāhaṇa
> dui janā mili' kṛṣṇa-kathā-āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the house of Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]-[[synonyms:n:nirvāhaṇa]]---accepting food as alms; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:janā]]---the two of them; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting together; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---discourses on the subject matter of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura accepted food daily at the house of Advaita Ācārya. Meeting together, the two of them would taste the nectar of discourses on the subject matter of Kṛṣṇa.**
