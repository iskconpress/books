====== Cc. Antya 3.268 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-ādi, āra yata sthāvara-jaṅgame
> kṛṣṇa-preme matta kare kṛṣṇa-saṅkīrtane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---ail; [[synonyms:s:sthāvara]]-[[synonyms:j:jaṅgame]]---moving and nonmoving creatures; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**The holy name of Kṛṣṇa is so attractive that anyone who chants it-including all living entities, moving and nonmoving, and even Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself-becomes imbued with love of Kṛṣṇa. This is the effect of chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra.**
