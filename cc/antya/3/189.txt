====== CC Antya 3.189 ======

===== Text =====

> sālokya-sārṣṭi-sārūpya-
> sāmīpyaikatvam apy uta
> dīyamānaṁ na gṛhṇanti
> vinā mat-sevanaṁ janāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sālokya]]---to live on the same planet; [[synonyms:s:sārṣṭi]]---to acquire the same opulence; [[synonyms:s:sārūpya]]---to achieve the same bodily features; [[synonyms:s:sāmīpya]]---to live always near the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:e:ekatvam]]---to merge into the existence of the Lord; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:u:uta]]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:dīyamānam]]---being offered; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:g:gṛhṇanti]]---do not take; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:s:sevanam]]---My service; [[synonyms:j:janāḥ]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**"My devotees do not accept sālokya, sārṣṭi, sārūpya, sāmīpya, or oneness with Me-even if I offer these liberations-in preference to serving Me.' "**

===== Purport =====

This verse is spoken by Lord Kapila, an //avatāra// of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// [[books:sb:3:29:13|(3.29.13)]].
