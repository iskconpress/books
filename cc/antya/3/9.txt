====== CC Antya 3.9 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina sei bālaka prabhu-sthāne āilā
> gosāñi tāre prīti kari' vārtā puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bālaka]]---that boy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:p:prīti]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great affection; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired.

===== Translation =====

**One day when the boy came to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord very affectionately inquired from him about all kinds of news.**
