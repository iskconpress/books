====== Cc. Antya 3.41 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' jagannāthera prasāda ānāila
> mātāke vaiṣṇave dite pṛthak pṛthak dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:ā:ānāila]]---ordered to be brought; [[synonyms:m:mātāke]]---to His mother; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇave]]---and all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separately; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---he gave.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered that varieties of prasāda offered to Lord Jagannātha be brought. The Lord then gave him the prasāda, separately packed, to offer to various Vaiṣṇavas and His mother.**
