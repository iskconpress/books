====== Cc. Antya 3.144 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmacandra khāṅna aparādha-bīja kaila
> sei bīja vṛkṣa hañā āgete phalila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]] [[synonyms:k:khāṅna]]---Rāmacandra Khān; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---of the offense; [[synonyms:b:bīja]]---seed; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---caused to germinate; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bīja]]---that seed; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming a tree; [[synonyms:ā:āgete]]---later; [[synonyms:p:phalila]]---fructified.

===== Translation =====

**By inducing a prostitute to disturb Haridāsa Ṭhākura, Rāmacandra Khān caused a seed of offense at his lotus feet to germinate. This seed later became a tree, and when it fructified, Rāmacandra Khān ate its fruits.**
