====== Cc. Antya 3.183 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahena,---"yaiche sūryera udaya
> udaya nā haite ārambhe tamera haya kṣaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura began to explain; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---like; [[synonyms:s:sūryera]] [[synonyms:u:udaya]]---sunrise; [[synonyms:u:udaya]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---although not visible; [[synonyms:ā:ārambhe]]---from the beginning; [[synonyms:t:tamera]]---of darkness; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---there is dissipation.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "As the sun begins to rise, even before visible it dissipates the darkness of night.**
