====== CC Antya 3.145 ======

===== Text =====

> mahad-aparādhera phala adbhuta kathana
> prastāva pāñā kahi, śuna, bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahat]]-[[synonyms:a:aparādhera]]---of a great offense at the feet of the exalted devotee; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---the result; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---narration; [[synonyms:p:prastāva]]---opportunity; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---taking advantage of; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I say; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees.

===== Translation =====

**This offense at the lotus feet of an exalted devotee has resulted in a wonderful narration. Taking advantage of the opportunity afforded by these incidents, I shall explain what happened. O devotees, please listen.**
