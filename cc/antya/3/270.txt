====== CC Antya 3.270 ======

===== Text =====

> sei saba līlā kahi saṅkṣepa kariyā
> caitanya-kṛpāte likhi kṣudra-jīva hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all those; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I say; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---in brief; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by the mercy of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I write; [[synonyms:k:kṣudra]]-[[synonyms:j:jīva]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a very insignificant living entity.

===== Translation =====

**I have briefly described those pastimes. Whatever I have written is by the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, since I am an insignificant living being.**
