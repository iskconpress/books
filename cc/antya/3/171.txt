====== Cc. Antya 3.171 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅ yaiche haila haridāsera mahimā kathana
> vyākhyāna,---adbhuta kathā śuna, bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---at that place; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---glories; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---discussion; [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---discourse; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---incident; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees.

===== Translation =====

**At the residence of Hiraṇya and Govardhana, discourses took place by which Haridāsa Ṭhākura was glorified. O devotees, please listen to that wonderful story.**
