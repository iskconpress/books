====== CC Antya 3.76 ======

===== Text =====

> ucca saṅkīrtana tāte karilā pracāra
> sthira-cara jīvera saba khaṇḍāilā saṁsāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ucca]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---loud chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---You have spread; [[synonyms:s:sthira]]-[[synonyms:c:cara]]---nonmoving and moving; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of the living entities; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāilā]]---You finished; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---the bondage to material existence;

===== Translation =====

**"You have preached the loud chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra and in this way freed all moving and nonmoving living entities from material bondage."**
