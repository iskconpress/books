====== CC Antya 3.229 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina haridāsa goṅphāte vasiyā
> nāma-saṅkīrtana karena ucca kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:goṅphāte]] [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting in his cave; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---was chanting the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:u:ucca]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---resounding very loudly.

===== Translation =====

**One day Haridāsa Ṭhākura was sitting in his cave, reciting very loudly the holy name of the Lord.**
