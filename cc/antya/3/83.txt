====== Cc. Antya 3.83 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yena vraje kṛṣṇa kari' avatāra
> sakala brahmāṇḍa-jīvera khaṇḍāilā saṁsāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:v:vraje]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---descending as an incarnation; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of living entities within this universe; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāilā]]---destroyed; [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---the material existence.

===== Translation =====

**"Formerly, when Lord Kṛṣṇa descended in Vṛndāvana, He freed all living entities in the universe from material existence in the same way.**
