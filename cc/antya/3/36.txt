====== CC Antya 3.36 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhya-viraha-daśāya punaḥ bhrānti haila
> 'bhoga nā lāgāiluṅ',---ei jñāna haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:v:viraha]]---of external separation; [[synonyms:d:daśāya]]---by the condition; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:b:bhrānti]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was illusion; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---offering to the Deity; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgāiluṅ]]---I have not given; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---you thought.

===== Translation =====

**"In the condition of external separation, you were again under illusion, thinking that you had not offered the food to Lord Viṣṇu.**
