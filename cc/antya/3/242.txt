====== CC Antya 3.242 ======

===== Text =====

> dvāre vasi' śuna tumi nāma-saṅkīrtana
> nāma samāpta haile karimu tava prīti-ācaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvāre]] [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting at the door; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy names; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name; [[synonyms:s:samāpta]] [[synonyms:h:haile]]---when finished; [[synonyms:k:karimu]]---I shall do; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---your; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇa]]---activities.

===== Translation =====

**"Sit down at the door and hear the chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra. As soon as the chanting is finished, I shall satisfy you as you desire."**
