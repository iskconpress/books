====== Cc. Antya 3.14 ======

===== Text =====

> svacchande ācāra kara, ke pāre balite?
> mukhara jagatera mukha pāra ācchādite?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svacchande]]---without restriction; [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You behave; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]] [[synonyms:b:balite]]---who can talk; [[synonyms:m:mukhara]]---talkative; [[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the whole world; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---mouth; [[synonyms:p:pāra]] [[synonyms:ā:ācchādite]]---can You cover.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You can act as You please. No one can say anything to restrict You. Nevertheless, the entire world is impudent. People can say anything. How can You stop them?**
