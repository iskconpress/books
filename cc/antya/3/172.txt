====== CC Antya 3.172 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina balarāma minati kariyā
> majumadārera sabhāya āilā ṭhākure lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:b:balarāma]]---Balarāma Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:minati]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---in great humility; [[synonyms:m:majumadārera]]---of the Majumadāras, Hiraṇya and Govardhana; [[synonyms:s:sabhāya]]---at the assembly; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākure]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking with him.

===== Translation =====

**One day Balarāma Ācārya requested Haridāsa Ṭhākura with great humility to come to the assembly of the Majumadāras, Hiraṇya and Govardhana. Thus Balarāma Ācārya went there with Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
