====== Cc. Antya 3.182 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ślokera artha kara paṇḍitera gaṇa"
> sabe kahe,---'tumi kaha artha-vivaraṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of this verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---explain; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O groups of learned scholars; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---everyone said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---you speak; [[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---the meaning and explanation.

===== Translation =====

**After reciting this verse, Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "O learned scholars, please explain the meaning of this verse." But the audience requested Haridāsa Ṭhākura, "It is better for you to explain the meaning of this important verse."**
