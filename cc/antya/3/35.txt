====== Cc. Antya 3.35 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣaṇeke aśru muchiyā śūnya dekhi' pāta
> svapana dekhiluṅ,'yena nimāñi khāila bhāta'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeke]]---in a moment; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:m:muchiyā]]---wiping; [[synonyms:ś:śūnya]]---vacant; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:pāta]]---the plate; [[synonyms:s:svapana]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiluṅ]]---I saw a dream; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:n:nimāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:khāila]] [[synonyms:b:bhāta]]---ate the food.

===== Translation =====

**"In a moment, after you had wiped your eyes, you saw that the plate you had offered Me was empty. Then you thought, "I dreamt as if Nimāi were eating everything."**
