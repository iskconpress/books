====== CC Antya 3.163 ======

===== Text =====

> jāti-dhana-jana khānera sakala la-ila
> bahu-dina paryanta grāma ujāḍa rahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---birthright; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---riches; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---followers; [[synonyms:k:khānera]]---of Rāmacandra Khān; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---everything; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---he took away; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---a long time; [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---for; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]---the village; [[synonyms:u:ujāḍa]] [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained deserted.

===== Translation =====

**The Mohammedan minister took away Rāmacandra Khān's position, wealth and followers. For many days the village remained deserted.**
