====== Cc. Antya 3.159 ======

===== Text =====

> dasyu-vṛtti kare rāmacandra rājāre nā deya kara
> kruddha hañā mleccha ujira āila tāra ghara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dasyu]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛtti]]---the business of a thief; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]---Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:r:rājāre]]---to the government; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---does not; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---pay; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---tax; [[synonyms:k:kruddha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being angry; [[synonyms:m:mleccha]]---the Mohammedan; [[synonyms:u:ujira]]---minister; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---to his house.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmacandra Khān's business was questionable, for he tried to avoid paying income tax to the government. Therefore the government's minister of finance was angry and came to his residence.**
