====== CC Antya 3.224 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇe avatārite advaita pratijñā karilā
> jala-tulasī diyā pūjā karite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatārite]]---to cause to descend; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:pratijñā]]---promise; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:t:tulasī]]---Ganges water and //tulasī// leaves; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering; [[synonyms:p:pūjā]]---worship; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**Determined to deliver all the fallen souls, Advaita Ācārya decided to cause Kṛṣṇa to descend. With this vow, he began to offer Ganges water and tulasī leaves to worship the Lord.**
