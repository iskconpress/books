====== Cc. Antya 3.218 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahe,---"gosāñi, kari nivedane
> more pratyaha anna deha' kon prayojane?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura said; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---my dear Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---let me submit one prayer; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:a:aha]]---daily; [[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---You give food; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:p:prayojane]]---what is the necessity.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "My dear Advaita Ācārya, let me submit something before Your Honor. Every day You give me alms of food to eat. What is the necessity of this?**
