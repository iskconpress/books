====== Cc. Antya 3.61 ======

===== Text =====

> nāmābhāsa haite haya sarva-pāpa-kṣaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the vibration of //nāmābhāsa//; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:p:pāpa]]---of all reactions to sins; [[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---destruction;

===== Translation =====

**Nāmācārya Haridāsa Ṭhākura continued, "If one offenselessly utters the holy name even imperfectly, one can be freed from all the results of sinful life.**
