====== CC Antya 3.168 ======

===== Text =====

> nirjana parṇa-śālāya karena kīrtana
> balarāma-ācārya-gṛhe bhikṣā-nirvāhaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirjana]]---solitary; [[synonyms:p:parṇa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śālāya]]---in a thatched cottage; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:b:balarāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the house of Balarāma Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]-[[synonyms:n:nirvāhaṇa]]---accepting alms.

===== Translation =====

**In the village, Haridāsa Ṭhākura was given a solitary thatched cottage, where he performed the chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra He accepted prasāda at the house of Balarāma Ācārya.**
