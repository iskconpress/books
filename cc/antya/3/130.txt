====== CC Antya 3.130 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtana karite aiche rātri-śeṣa haila
> ṭhākurera sane veśyāra mana phiri' gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---chanting and chanting; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---the night ended; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurera]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---by the association of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:v:veśyāra]]---of the prostitute; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:p:phiri']] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---was converted.

===== Translation =====

**The night ended while Haridāsa Ṭhākura was chanting, but by his association the mind of the prostitute had changed.**
