====== CC Antya 3.239 ======

===== Text =====

> nirvikāra haridāsa gambhīra-āśaya
> balite lāgilā tāṅre hañā sadaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirvikāra]]---unmoved; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---very deep; [[synonyms:ā:āśaya]]---determination; [[synonyms:b:balite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto her; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:s:sadaya]]---being merciful.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura was immovable, for he was deeply determined. He began to speak to her, being very merciful toward her.**
