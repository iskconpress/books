====== CC Antya 3.146 ======

===== Text =====

> sahajei avaiṣṇava rāmacandra-khāṅna
> haridāsera aparādhe haila asura-samāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahajei]]---naturally; [[synonyms:a:avaiṣṇava]]---nondevotee; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:k:khāṅna]]---Rāmacandra Khān; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---at the lotus feet of Haridāsa; [[synonyms:a:aparādhe]]---by offenses; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:a:asura]]-[[synonyms:s:samāna]]---exactly like a demon.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmacandra Khān was naturally a nondevotee. Now, having offended the lotus feet of Haridāsa Ṭhākura, he became just like a demoniac atheist.**
