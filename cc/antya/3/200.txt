====== CC Antya 3.200 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' sabhā-sad uṭhe kari' hāhākāra
> majumadāra sei vipre karila dhikkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sabhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sat]]---all the members of the assembly; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---got up; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:h:hāhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---making a tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:m:majumadāra]]---Hiraṇya and Govardhana Majumadāra; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---unto that //brāhmaṇa// who was their servant; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:d:dhik]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---chastisement.

===== Translation =====

**All the members of the assembly who had heard the challenge were greatly agitated, and they got up, making a tumultuous sound. Hiraṇya and Govardhana Majumadāra both immediately chastised the brāhmaṇa tax collector.**
