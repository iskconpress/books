====== CC Antya 3.203 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' haridāsa tabe uṭhiyā calilā
> majumadāra sei vipre tyāga karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---got up and began to go away; [[synonyms:m:majumadāra]]---Hiraṇya and Govardhana Majumadāra; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vipre]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tyāga]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---kicked out.

===== Translation =====

**Then Haridāsa Ṭhākura got up to leave, and the Majumadāras, the masters of Gopāla Cakravartī, immediately dismissed Gopāla Cakravartī from their service.**
