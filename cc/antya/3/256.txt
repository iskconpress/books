====== Cc. Antya 3.256 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve āmi rāma-nāma pāñāchi 'śiva' haite
> tomāra saṅge lobha haila kṛṣṇa-nāma laite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Rāma; [[synonyms:p:pāñāchi]]---got; [[synonyms:ś:śiva]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---by your association; [[synonyms:l:lobha]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---I became greedy; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:l:laite]]---to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra.//

===== Translation =====

**"Formerly I received the holy name of Lord Rāma from Lord Śiva, but now, due to your association, I am greatly eager to chant the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
