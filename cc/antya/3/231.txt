====== CC Antya 3.231 ======

===== Text =====

> dvāre tulasī lepā-piṇḍira upara
> goṅphāra śobhā dekhi' lokera juḍāya antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]---the //tulasī// plant; [[synonyms:l:lepā]]---very clean; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍira]] [[synonyms:u:upara]]---on the altar; [[synonyms:g:goṅphāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śobhā]]---the beauty of the cave; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:j:juḍāya]]---was satisfied; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---the heart.

===== Translation =====

**Thus everyone who saw the beauty of the cave, with the tulasī plant on a clean altar, was astonished and satisfied at heart.**
