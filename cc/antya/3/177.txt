====== CC Antya 3.177 ======

===== Text =====

> keha bale,---'nāma haite haya pāpa-kṣaya'
> keha bale,---'nāma haite jīvera mokṣa haya'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---some of them said; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---by chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---disappearance of all reactions to sinful activities; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---some of them said; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---by chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]]---of the living entities; [[synonyms:m:mokṣa]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is liberation.

===== Translation =====

**Some of them said, "By chanting the holy name of the Lord, one is freed from the reactions of all sinful life." Others said, "Simply by chanting the holy name of the Lord, a living being is liberated from material bondage."**
