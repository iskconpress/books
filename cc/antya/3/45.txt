====== CC Antya 3.45 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-gaṇe yāṅra dekhe alpa-maryādā-laṅghana
> vākya-daṇḍa kari' kare maryādā sthāpana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---in the associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]-[[synonyms:m:maryādā]]-[[synonyms:l:laṅghana]]---a slight deviation from the standard etiquette and behavior; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---chastising with words; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:maryādā]]---etiquette; [[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---establishing.

===== Translation =====

**Dāmodara Paṇḍita would verbally chastise every devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu whom he found deviating even slightly from proper behavior. Thus he established the standard etiquette.**
