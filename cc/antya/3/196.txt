====== CC Antya 3.196 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakti-sukha-āge 'mukti' ati-tuccha haya
> ataeva bhakta-gaṇa 'mukti' nāhi laya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---transcendental bliss derived from devotional service; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:t:tuccha]]---extremely insignificant; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---pure devotees; [[synonyms:m:mukti]]---liberation; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---do not accept.

===== Translation =====

**"For a devotee who enjoys the transcendental bliss of devotional service, liberation is most insignificant. Therefore pure devotees never desire to achieve liberation.**
