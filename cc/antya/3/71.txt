====== Cc. Antya 3.71 ======

===== Text =====

> 'pratidhvani' nahe, sei karaye 'kīrtana'
> tomāra kṛpāra ei akathya kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---that sound vibration is not an echo; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---they; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---are chanting; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāra]]---of Your mercy; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:akathya]] [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---inconceivable incident;

===== Translation =====

**"Actually, however, it is not an echo; it is the kīrtana of the nonmoving living entities. All this, although inconceivable, is possible by Your mercy.**
