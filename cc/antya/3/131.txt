====== CC Antya 3.131 ======

===== Text =====

> daṇḍavat hañā paḍe ṭhākura-caraṇe
> rāmacandra-khāṅnera kathā kaila nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---she fell down; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:k:khāṅnera]]---of Rāmacandra Khān; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---policy; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---submission.

===== Translation =====

**The prostitute, now purified, fell at the lotus feet of Haridāsa Ṭhākura and confessed that Rāmacandra Khān had appointed her to pollute him.**
