~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya 3.195~~

====== CC Antya 3.195 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahena,---kene karaha saṁśaya?
> śāstre kahe,---nāmābhāsa-mātre 'mukti' haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura said; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]]---are you doubtful; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---it is stated in the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsamātre]]---simply by a glimpse of the chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:m:mukti]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is liberation.

===== Translation =====

Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "Why are you doubtful? The revealed scriptures say that one can attain liberation simply by a glimpse of offenseless chanting of the holy name.
