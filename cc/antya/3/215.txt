====== CC Antya 3.215 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārye miliyā kailā daṇḍavat praṇāma
> advaita āliṅgana kari' karilā sammāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārye]] [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---meeting Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---offered; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:p:praṇāma]]---obeisances and respects; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---showed respect.

===== Translation =====

**Upon meeting Advaita Ācārya, Haridāsa Ṭhākura offered Him respect and obeisances. Advaita Ācārya embraced him and showed respect to him in return.**
