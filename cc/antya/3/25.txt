====== CC Antya 3.25 ======

===== Text =====

> mātāra gṛhe raha yāi mātāra caraṇe
> tomāra āge nahibe kāro svacchandācaraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---of My mother; [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the home; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---stay; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---going; [[synonyms:m:mātāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the shelter of My mother's lotus feet; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of you; [[synonyms:n:nahibe]]---there will not be; [[synonyms:k:kāro]]---of anyone; [[synonyms:s:svacchanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācaraṇe]]---independent activities.

===== Translation =====

**"It is best for you to go to the shelter of My mother's lotus feet, for no one will be able to behave independently in front of you.**
