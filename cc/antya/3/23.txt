====== CC Antya 3.23 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā sama 'nirapekṣa' nāhi mora gaṇe
> 'nirapekṣa' nahile 'dharma' nā yāya rakṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like you; [[synonyms:n:nirapekṣa]]---neutral; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---among My associates; [[synonyms:n:nirapekṣa]]---neutral; [[synonyms:n:nahile]]---without being; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:r:rakṣaṇe]]---cannot be protected.

===== Translation =====

**"You are the most neutral among My associates. This is very good, for without being neutral one cannot protect religious principles.**
