====== CC Antya 3.265 ======

===== Text =====

> anyera kā kathā, āpane vrajendra-nandana
> avatari' karena prema-rasa āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anyera]] [[synonyms:k:kā]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---what to speak of others; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatari']]---descending; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]] [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting of the nectar of love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**What to speak of others, even Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja, personally descends to taste the nectar of love of Godhead in the form of the chanting of Hare Kṛṣṇa.**
