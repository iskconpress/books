====== CC Antya 3.178 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahena,---"nāmera ei dui phala naya
> nāmera phale kṛṣṇa-pade prema upajaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura replied; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of chanting the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---results; [[synonyms:n:naya]]---are not; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]] [[synonyms:p:phale]]---by the result of chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:u:upajaya]]---awakening of ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura protested, "These two benedictions are not the true result of chanting the holy name. By actually chanting the holy name without offenses, one awakens his ecstatic love for the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa.**
