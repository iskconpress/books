====== CC Antya 3.43 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryādi vaiṣṇavere mahā-prasāda dilā
> prabhura yaiche ājñā, paṇḍita tāhā ācarilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavere]]---to all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered all the //prasāda// of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:ā:ācarilā]]---performed.

===== Translation =====

**He delivered all the prasāda to such great Vaiṣṇavas as Advaita Ācārya. Thus he stayed there and behaved according to the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
