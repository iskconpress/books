====== CC Antya 3.68 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahe,---"prabhu, se kṛpā tomāra
> sthāvara-jaṅgama āge kariyācha nistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Haridāsa replied; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my dear Lord; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:s:sthāvara]]-[[synonyms:j:jaṅgama]]---nonmoving and moving living entities; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---previously; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]] [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---You have delivered.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura replied, "My dear Lord, the deliverance of all moving and nonmoving living entities takes place only by Your mercy. You have already grated this mercy and delivered them.**
