====== CC Antya 3.95 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsera guṇa-gaṇa---asaṅkhya, apāra
> keha kona aṁśe varṇe, nāhi pāya pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the stock of transcendental qualities of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:a:asaṅkhya]]---innumerable; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unfathomed; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:a:aṁśe]]---some part; [[synonyms:v:varṇe]]---describes; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---cannot reach the limit.

===== Translation =====

**The transcendental qualities of Haridāsa Ṭhākura are innumerable and unfathomable. One may describe a portion of them, but to count them all is impossible.**
