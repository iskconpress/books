====== CC Antya 3.258 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-nāma deha' tumi more kara dhanyā
> āmāre bhāsāya yaiche ei prema-vanyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---please give; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:d:dhanyā]]---make fortunate; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]]---me; [[synonyms:b:bhāsāya]]---may cause to float; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---so that; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vanyā]]---inundation of ecstatic love of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Please give me the holy name of Kṛṣṇa and thus make me fortunate, so that I also may float in the flood of love of Godhead inaugurated by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu."**
