====== CC Antya 3.127 ======

===== Text =====

> veśyā giyā samācāra khāṅnere kahila
> āra dina sandhyā ha-ite ṭhākura-ṭhāñi āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:veśyā]]---the prostitute; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---returning; [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---information; [[synonyms:k:khāṅnere]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---spoke to Rāmacandra Khān; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---beginning from the evening; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---she came and remained at the residence of Haridāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**The prostitute returned to Rāmacandra Khān and informed him of what had happened. The next day she came earlier, at the beginning of the evening, and stayed with Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
