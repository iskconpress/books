====== Cc. Antya 3.216 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-tīre goṅphā kari' nirjane tāṅre dilā
> bhāgavata-gītāra bhakti-artha śunāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank of the Ganges; [[synonyms:g:goṅphā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---constructing a small cavelike residence; [[synonyms:n:nirjane]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---offered; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:g:gītāra]]---of //Bhagavad-gītā//; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the real meaning of devotional service; [[synonyms:ś:śunāilā]]---spoke to him.

===== Translation =====

**On the bank of the Ganges, in a solitary place, Advaita Ācārya made a cavelike home for Haridāsa Ṭhākura and spoke to him about the real meaning of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam and Bhagavad-gītā in terms of devotional service.**
