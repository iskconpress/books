====== CC Antya 3.37 ======

===== Text =====

> pāka-pātre dekhilā saba anna āche bhari'
> punaḥ bhoga lāgāilā sthāna-saṁskāra kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāka]]-[[synonyms:p:pātre]]---the cooking pots; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---she saw; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:ā:āche]] [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---were filled with; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:l:lāgāilā]]---offered the food; [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---the place for offering; [[synonyms:s:saṁskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---cleansing.

===== Translation =====

**"Then you went to see the cooking pots and found that every pot was filled with food. Therefore you again offered the food, after cleansing the place for the offering.**
