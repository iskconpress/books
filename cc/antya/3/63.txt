====== Cc. Antya 3.63 ======

===== Text =====

> nāmābhāsa haite haya saṁsārera kṣaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---even on account of //nāmābhāsa//; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:saṁsārera]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---deliverance from material bondage;

===== Translation =====

**"Even a faint light from the holy name of the Lord can eradicate all the reactions of sinful life."**
