====== CC Antya 3.82 ======

===== Text =====

> avatari' tumi aiche pātiyācha hāṭa
> keha nā bujhite pāre tomāra gūḍha nāṭa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avatari']]---descending; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---like that; [[synonyms:p:pātiyācha]] [[synonyms:h:hāṭa]]---have set up a market; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:bujhite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---no one can understand; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭa]]---deep acting.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You have set a plan in motion by descending on the material world, but no one can understand how You are acting.**
