====== CC Antya 3.19 ======

===== Text =====

> "ihāre kahiye śuddha-premera taraṅga
> dāmodara-sama mora nāhi 'antaraṅga' "

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāre]]---such behavior; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I can say; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:p:premerataraṅga]]---waves of pure devotional service; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like Dāmodara; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:a:antaraṅga]]---intimate friend.

===== Translation =====

**"This impudence is also a sign of pure love for Me. I have no other intimate friend like Dāmodara Paṇḍita."**
