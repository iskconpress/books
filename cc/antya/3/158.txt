====== Cc. Antya 3.158 ======

===== Text =====

> gomaya-jale lepilā saba mandira-prāṅgaṇa
> tabu rāmacandrera mana nā haila parasanna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---with water mixed with cow dung; [[synonyms:l:lepilā]]---smeared; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mandira]]---the Durgā-maṇḍapa temple; [[synonyms:p:prāṅgaṇa]]---the courtyard; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandrera]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Rāmacandra Khān; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:p:parasanna]]---was not happy.

===== Translation =====

**To purify the Durgā-maṇḍapa temple and the courtyard, Rāmacandra Khān sprinkled and smeared it with water mixed with cow dung, but still his mind was unsatisfied.**
