====== CC Antya 3.187 ======

===== Text =====

> mriyamāṇo harer nāma
> gṛṇan putropacāritam
> ajāmilo 'py agād dhāma
> kim uta śraddhayā gṛṇan

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mriyamāṇaḥ]]---dying; [[synonyms:h:hareḥ]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of the Supreme Lord; [[synonyms:g:gṛṇan]]---chanting; [[synonyms:p:putra]]-[[synonyms:u:upacāritam]]---though spoken for his son; [[synonyms:a:ajāmilaḥ]]---Ajāmila; [[synonyms:a:api]]---also; [[synonyms:a:agāt]]---attained; [[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---the spiritual world; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:u:uta]]---what to speak of; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---with faith and reverence; [[synonyms:g:gṛṇan]]---chanting.

===== Translation =====

**"While dying, Ajāmila chanted the holy name of the Lord, intending to call his son Nārāyaṇa. Nevertheless, he attained the spiritual world. What then to speak of those who chant the holy name with faith and reverence?'**
