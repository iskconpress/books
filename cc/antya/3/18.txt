====== CC Antya 3.18 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' dāmodara mauna ha-ilā
> antare santoṣa prabhu hāsi' vicārilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became silent; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within Himself; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---pleased; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hāsi']]---smiling; [[synonyms:v:vicārilā]]---considered.

===== Translation =====

**Having said this, Dāmodara Paṇḍita became silent. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled, pleased within Himself, and considered the impudence of Dāmodara Paṇḍita.**
