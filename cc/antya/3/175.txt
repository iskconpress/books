====== Cc. Antya 3.175 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsera guṇa sabe kahe pañca-mukhe
> śuniyā ta' dui bhāi pāilā baḍa sukhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the qualities; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---began to speak; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---as if speaking with five mouths; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---the two brothers; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---got; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---very great happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone there began to speak of Haridāsa Ṭhākura's great qualities as if they had five mouths. Hearing this, both brothers were extremely happy.**
