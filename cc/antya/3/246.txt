====== CC Antya 3.246 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇe nāmāviṣṭa-manā sadā haridāsa
> araṇye rodita haila strī-bhāva-prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---absorbed in chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:m:manā]]---mind; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:a:araṇye]]---in the wilderness; [[synonyms:r:rodita]]---crying; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:s:strī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---exhibition of feminine postures.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura was always absorbed in thoughts of Kṛṣṇa and the holy name of Kṛṣṇa. Therefore the feminine poses the woman exhibited were just like crying in the forest.**
