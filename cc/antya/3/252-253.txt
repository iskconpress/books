====== CC Antya 3.252-253 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-bhāgavata tumi,---tomāra darśane
> tomāra kṛṣṇa-nāma-kīrtana-śravaṇe

> citta śuddha haila, cāhe kṛṣṇa-nāma laite
> kṛṣṇa-nāma upadeśi' kṛpā kara mote

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---the foremost devotee; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by seeing you; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became purified; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:l:laite]]---to chant the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:u:upadeśi']]---instructing about chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---show mercy; [[synonyms:m:mote]]---unto me.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear sir, you are the foremost devotee. Simply seeing you and hearing you chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa has purified my consciousness. Now I want to chant the holy name of the Lord. Please be kind to me by instructing me about the ecstasy of chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra.**
