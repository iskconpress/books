====== CC Antya 3.153 ======

===== Text =====

> goyālāra gośālā haya atyanta vistāra
> ihāṅ saṅkīrṇa-sthala, tomāra manuṣya---apāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:goyālāra]]---of a milkman; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:ś:śālā]]---cow shed; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---very; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---spacious; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthala]]---very narrow place; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---adherents; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited.

===== Translation =====

**"You might go to the house of a milkman, for the cow shed is spacious, whereas the space here in the Durgā-maṇḍapa is insufficient because You have many followers with You."**
