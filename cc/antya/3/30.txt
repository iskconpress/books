====== Cc. Antya 3.30 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bāre bāre āsi' āmi tomāra bhavane
> miṣṭānna vyañjana saba kariye bhojane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāre]] [[synonyms:b:bāre]]---again and again; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhavane]]---at your place; [[synonyms:m:miṣṭānna]]---sweetmeats; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kariye]]---do; [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---eating.

===== Translation =====

**"I come to your home again and again to eat all the sweetmeats and vegetables you offer.**
