====== CC Antya 3.225 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kare goṅphāya nāma-saṅkīrtana
> kṛṣṇa avatīrṇa ha-ibena,---ei tāṅra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed; [[synonyms:g:goṅphāya]]---in the cave; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:avatīrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibena]]---will descend; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---his mind.

===== Translation =====

**Similarly, Haridāsa Ṭhākura chanted in his cave on the bank of the Ganges with the intention to cause Kṛṣṇa's descent.**
