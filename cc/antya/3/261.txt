====== CC Antya 3.261 ======

===== Text =====

> pratīta karite kahi kāraṇa ihāra
> yāhāra śravaṇe haya viśvāsa sabāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pratīta]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---just to make one faithful; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I say; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]] [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---the reason for this; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---hearing which; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---faith; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore I shall explain the reasons why people should have faith. Everyone who hears this will be faithful.**
