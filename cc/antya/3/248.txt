====== Cc. Antya 3.248 ======

===== Text =====

> "tina dina vañcilā āmā kari' āśvāsana
> rātri-dine nahe tomāra nāma-samāpana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---for three days; [[synonyms:v:vañcilā]]---you have cheated; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---me; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsana]]---giving assurance; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---throughout the entire day and night; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:samāpana]]---finishing of the chanting of the holy name.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear sir, for three days you have cheated me by giving me false assurances, for I see that throughout the entire day and night your chanting of the holy name is never finished."**
