====== Cc. Antya 3.112 ======

===== Text =====

> "ṭhākura, tumi---parama-sundara, prathama yauvana
> tomā dekhi' kon nārī dharite pāre mana?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---O great devotee //ācārya//; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:s:sundara]]---very beautifully constructed; [[synonyms:p:prathama]] [[synonyms:y:yauvana]]---the beginning of youth; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing you; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---what woman; [[synonyms:d:dharite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can control; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---her mind.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Ṭhākura, O great preacher, great devotee, you are so beautifully built, and your youth is just beginning. Who is the woman who could control her mind after seeing you?**
