====== Cc. Antya 3.264 ======

===== Text =====

> lakṣmī-ādi kari' kṛṣṇa-preme lubdha hañā
> nāma-prema āsvādilā manuṣye janmiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lakṣmī]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the goddess of fortune and others; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---for love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:lubdha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being greedy; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa in love; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādilā]]---tasted; [[synonyms:m:manuṣye]] [[synonyms:j:janmiyā]]---taking birth in human society.

===== Translation =====

**The goddess of fortune and others, allured by love of Kṛṣṇa, also came down in the form of human beings and tasted the holy name of the Lord in love.**
