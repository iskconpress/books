====== CC Antya 3.3 ======

===== Text =====

> puruṣottame eka uḍiyā-brāhmaṇa-kumāra
> pitṛ-śūnya, mahā-sundara, mṛdu-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puruṣottame]]---in Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]-[[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kumāra]]---young son of a //brāhmaṇa// from Orissa; [[synonyms:p:pitṛ]]-[[synonyms:ś:śūnya]]---without his father; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sundara]]---possessing very beautiful bodily features; [[synonyms:m:mṛdu]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---having very gentle behavior.

===== Translation =====

**In Jagannātha Purī there was a young boy who had been born of an Orissa brāhmaṇa but had later lost his father. The boy's features were very beautiful, and his behavior was extremely gentle.**
