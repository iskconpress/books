====== CC Antya 3.214 ======

===== Text =====

> viprera kuṣṭha śuni' haridāsa mane duḥkhī hailā
> balāi-purohite kahi' śāntipura āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viprera]]---of the //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:k:kuṣṭha]]---leprosy; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became unhappy; [[synonyms:b:balāi]]-[[synonyms:p:purohite]]---unto Balarāma Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---speaking; [[synonyms:ś:śāntipura]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Śāntipura.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura was unhappy when he heard that the brāhmaṇa Gopāla Cakravartī had been attacked by leprosy. Thus after informing Balarāma Ācārya, the priest of Hiraṇya Majumadāra, he went to Śāntipura, the home of Advaita Ācārya.**
