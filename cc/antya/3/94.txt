====== CC Antya 3.94 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaktera guṇa kahite prabhura bāḍaye ullāsa
> bhakta-gaṇa-śreṣṭha tāte śrī-haridāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---qualities; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---speaking; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāḍaye]]---increases; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---of all devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---topmost; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu derives great pleasure from glorifying His devotees, and among the devotees, Haridāsa Ṭhākura is the foremost.**
