====== CC Antya 3.211 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhiyā sakala loka haila camatkāra
> haridāse praśaṁsi' tāṅre kare namaskāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---all people; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonished; [[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsi']]---praising; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offer; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the condition of Gopāla Cakravartī, everyone was astonished. Everyone praised the influence of Haridāsa Ṭhākura and offered him obeisances.**
