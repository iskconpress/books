====== Cc. Antya 5.99 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpera ṭhāñi ācārya kailā nivedana
> eka vipra prabhura nāṭaka kariyāche uttama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpera]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---before Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submission; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---one //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---drama; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]]---has composed; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---very nice.

===== Translation =====

**Bhagavān Ācārya submitted to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, "A good brāhmaṇa has prepared a drama about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that appears exceptionally well composed.**
