====== Cc. Antya 5.129 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā kavira haila lajjā, bhaya, vismaya
> haṁsa-madhye baka yaiche kichu nāhi kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kavira]]---of the poet; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:l:lajjā]]---shame; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:h:haṁsa]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in a society of white swans; [[synonyms:b:baka]]---a duck; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---utters.

===== Translation =====

**When the Bengali poet heard this chastisement from Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, he was ashamed, fearfuI and astonished. Indeed, being like a duck in a society of white swans, he could not say anything.**
