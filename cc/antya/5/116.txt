====== CC Antya 5.116 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā sabāra haila ānandita-mana
> duḥkha pāñā svarūpa kahe sakrodha vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---great happiness in the mind; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---becoming unhappy; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī began to speak; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:k:krodha]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---angry words.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, everyone present was greatly happy. But Svarūpa Dāmodara, who alone was very unhappy, began to speak in great anger.**
