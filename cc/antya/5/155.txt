====== CC Antya 5.155 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇe gāli dite kare nāma uccāraṇa
> sei nāma haya tāra 'muktira' kāraṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gāli]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to blaspheme or chastise; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:u:uccāraṇa]]---chants the name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---that holy name; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:muktira]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of liberation.

===== Translation =====

**"Sometimes it so happens that one who wants to chastise Kṛṣṇa utters the holy name, and thus the holy name becomes the cause of his liberation."**
