====== CC Antya 5.117 ======

===== Text =====

> "āre mūrkha, āpanāra kaili sarva-nāśa!
> dui ta' īśvare tora nāhika viśvāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āre]] [[synonyms:m:mūrkha]]---O fool; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]]---of yourself; [[synonyms:k:kaili]]---you have done; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---loss of all auspiciousness; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:ī:īśvare]]---in the two controllers; [[synonyms:t:tora]]---your; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---there is no faith.

===== Translation =====

**"You are a fool," he said."You have brought ill fortune upon yourself, for you have no knowledge of the existence of the two Lords, Jagannāthadeva and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, nor have you faith in Them.**
