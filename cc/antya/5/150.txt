====== CC Antya 5.150 ======

===== Text =====

> saṁsāra-tāraṇa-hetu yei icchā-śakti
> tāhāra milana kari' ekatā yaiche prāpti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]-[[synonyms:t:tāraṇa]]-[[synonyms:h:hetu]]---for delivering the entire world; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---that; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---the potency of will; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that will; [[synonyms:m:milana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by the meeting; [[synonyms:e:ekatā]]---oneness; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---so that; [[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---obtainment.

===== Translation =====

**"The supreme desire to deliver the entire world meets in both of Them, and for that reason also They are one and the same.**
