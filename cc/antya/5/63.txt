====== CC Antya 5.63 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe rāmānanda krame kahite lāgilā
> kṛṣṇa-kathā-rasāmṛta-sindhu uthalilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:krame]]---gradually; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---of the topics of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rasāmṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of transcendental mellow; [[synonyms:u:uthalilā]]---became agitated.

===== Translation =====

**Thereupon Rāmānanda Rāya gradually began speaking on topics of Kṛṣṇa. Thus the ocean of the transcendental mellow of those topics became agitated.**
