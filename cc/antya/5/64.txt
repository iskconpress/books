====== Cc. Antya 5.64 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane praśna kari' pāche karena siddhānta
> tṛtīya prahara haila, nahe kathā-anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:praśna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making the question; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---after that; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---gives the conclusion; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:p:prahara]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it became afternoon; [[synonyms:n:nahe]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:a:anta]]---there was no end to such topics.

===== Translation =====

**He began personally posing questions and then answering them with conclusive statements. When afternoon came, the topics still did not end.**
