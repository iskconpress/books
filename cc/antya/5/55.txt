====== Cc. Antya 5.55 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya-pāśa gela, rāya praṇati karila
> 'ājñā kara, ye lāgi' āgamana haila'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---to Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---he went; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:praṇati]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---offered his respects; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---please order me; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for what purpose; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---have you come.

===== Translation =====

**Pradyumna Miśra went to Rāmānanda Rāya, who offered him respectful obeisances and said, "Please order me. For what purpose have you come?"**
