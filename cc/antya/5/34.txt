====== CC Antya 5.34 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe miśra rāmānandera vṛttānta kahilā
> śuni' mahāprabhu tabe kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]]---of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---described the activities; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Pradyumna Miśra thereupon described the activities of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya. After hearing about these activities, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to speak.**
