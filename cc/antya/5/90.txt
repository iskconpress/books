====== CC Antya 5.90 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu bhakta-gaṇa lañā
> nīlācale viharaye bhakti pracāriyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by His pure devotees; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:viharaye]]---enjoys transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:p:pracāriyā]]---preaching the cult of devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, accompanied by His associates, His pure devotees, enjoyed transcendental bliss in Jagannātha Purī [Nīlācala] preaching the bhakti cult in many ways.**
