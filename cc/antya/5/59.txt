====== CC Antya 5.59 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi tāre lañā nibhṛte vasilā
> 'ki kathā śunite cāha?' miśrere puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]] [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat in a secluded place; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---what kind of topics; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]] [[synonyms:c:cāha]]---do you want to hear; [[synonyms:m:miśrere]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---he inquired from Pradyumna Miśra.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya took Pradyumna Miśra to a secluded place and inquired from him, "What kind of kṛṣṇa-kathā do you want to hear from me?"**
