====== Cc. Antya 5.33 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina miśra āila prabhu-vidyamāne
> prabhu kahe,---'kṛṣṇa-kathā śunilā rāya-sthāne'?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:v:vidyamāne]]---in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---talks about Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---have you heard; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---from Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, when Pradyumna Miśra arrived in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord inquired, "Have you heard talks about Kṛṣṇa from Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya?"**
