====== Cc. Antya 5.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śacī-suta śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya
> jaya jaya kṛpā-maya nityānanda dhanya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:s:suta]]---to the son of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---to the most merciful; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]] [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---the glorious Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the son of mother Śacī! All glories to Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu! Indeed, He is the most glorious and merciful.**
