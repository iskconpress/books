====== CC Antya 5.40 ======

===== Text =====

> tabu nirvikāra rāya-rāmānandera mana
> nānā-bhāvodgāra tāre karāya śikṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:n:nirvikāra]]---unchanged; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:u:udgāra]]---all the symptoms and transformations of ecstasy; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto them; [[synonyms:k:karāya]] [[synonyms:ś:śikṣaṇa]]---he teaches.

===== Translation =====

**"Nevertheless, the mind of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya never changes, although he teaches the girls how to physically express all the transformations of ecstasy.**
