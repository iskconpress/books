====== CC Antya 5.93 ======

===== Text =====

> prathame nāṭaka teṅho tāṅre śunāila
> tāṅra saṅge aneka vaiṣṇava nāṭaka śunila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathame]]---at first; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---the drama; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:ś:śunāila]]---made to hear; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---many; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]] [[synonyms:ś:śunila]]---listened to the drama.

===== Translation =====

**First the brāhmaṇa induced Bhagavān Ācārya to hear the drama, and then many other devotees joined Bhagavān Ācārya in listening to it.**
