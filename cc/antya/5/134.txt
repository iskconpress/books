====== CC Antya 5.134 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloka kariyācha pāñā santoṣa
> tomāra hṛdayera arthe duṅhāya lāge 'doṣa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]]---you have composed; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]] [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---getting satisfaction; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:h:hṛdayera]]---of your heart; [[synonyms:a:arthe]]---by the meaning; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāya]]---to both; [[synonyms:l:lāge]] [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---there is an offense.

===== Translation =====

**"You have composed this introductory verse to your great satisfaction, but the meaning you have expressed is contaminated by offenses to both Lord Jagannātha and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
