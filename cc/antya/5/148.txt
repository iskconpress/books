====== Cc. Antya 5.148 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha hana kṛṣṇera 'ātma-svarupa'
> kintu ihāṅ dāru-brahma---sthāvara-svarūpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:hana]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---identical with Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here, at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:d:dāru]]-[[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the Absolute appearing as wood; [[synonyms:s:sthāvara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---nonmoving identity.

===== Translation =====

**"There is no difference between Lord Jagannātha and Kṛṣṇa, but here Lord Jagannātha is fixed as the Absolute Person appearing in wood. Therefore He does not move.**
