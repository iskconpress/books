====== CC Antya 5.38 ======

===== Text =====

> eke deva-dāsī, āra sundarī taruṇī
> tāra saba aṅga-sevā karena āpani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eke]]---on one side; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsī]]---the professional dancing girls; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:s:sundarī]] [[synonyms:t:taruṇī]]---very beautiful and youthful; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---their; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---of the body,; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]---service; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---performs personally.

===== Translation =====

**"The two professional dancing girls are beautiful and youthful, yet Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya personally massages oil upon their bodies.**
