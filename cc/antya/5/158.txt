====== CC Antya 5.158 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kavi sarva tyaji' rahilā nīlācale
> gaura-bhakta-gaṇera kṛpā ke kahite pāre?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kavi]]---that poet; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:t:tyaji']]---giving up all nonsensical activities; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can explain.

===== Translation =====

**By the mercy of the devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, that poet from Bengal gave up all other activities and stayed with them at Jagannātha Purī. Who can explain the mercy of the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu?**
