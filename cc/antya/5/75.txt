====== Cc. Antya 5.75 ======

===== Text =====

> ye-saba śuniluṅ, kṛṣṇa-rasera sāgara
> brahmādi-devera e saba nā haya gocara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all that; [[synonyms:ś:śuniluṅ]]---I heard; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of the nectar of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sāgara]]---the ocean; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:d:devera]]---of the demigods, beginning with Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all this; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:g:gocara]]---is not possible to be understood.

===== Translation =====

**"What I have heard from Rāmānanda Rāya is like a nectarean ocean of discourses about Kṛṣṇa. Even the demigods, beginning with Lord Brahmā, cannot understand all these topics.**
