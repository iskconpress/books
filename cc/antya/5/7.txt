====== Cc. Antya 5.7 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena,---"kṛṣṇa-kathā āmi nāhi jāni
> sabe rāmānanda jāne, tāṅra mukhe śuni

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---talks about Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---do not know; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---only; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---Rāmānanda Rāya knows; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from his mouth; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---I hear.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "I do not know about topics concerning Lord Kṛṣṇa. I think that only Rāmānanda Rāya knows, for I hear these topics from him.**
