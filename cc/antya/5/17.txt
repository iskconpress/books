====== Cc. Antya 5.17 ======

===== Text =====

> sva-haste karena tāra abhyaṅga-mardana
> sva-haste karāna snāna, gātra sammārjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with his own hand; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of those two girls; [[synonyms:a:abhyaṅga]]-[[synonyms:m:mardana]]---massaging the body with oil; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with his own hand; [[synonyms:k:karāna]] [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathes them; [[synonyms:g:gātra]] [[synonyms:s:sammārjana]]---cleansing the whole body.

===== Translation =====

**With his own hand, Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya massaged their bodies with oil and bathed them with water. Indeed, Rāmānanda Rāya cleansed their entire bodies with his own hand.**
