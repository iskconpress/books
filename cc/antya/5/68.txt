====== CC Antya 5.68 ======

===== Text =====

> ghare giyā miśra kaila snāna, bhojana
> sandhyā-kāle dekhite āila prabhura caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---returning home; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bathing; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the evening; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After returning home, Pradyumna Miśra bathed and ate his meal. In the evening he came to see the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
