====== CC Antya 5.113 ======

===== Text =====

> śloka śuni' sarva-loka tāhāre vākhāne
> svarūpa kahe,---'ei śloka karaha vyākhyāne'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing the verse; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---everyone; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]]---him; [[synonyms:v:vākhāne]]---praised; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---kindly explain.

===== Translation =====

**When everyone present heard the verse, they all commended the poet, but Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī requested him, "Kindly explain this verse."**
