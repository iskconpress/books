====== CC Antya 5.110 ======

===== Text =====

> dui tina dina ācārya āgraha karila
> tāṅra āgrahe svarūpera śunite icchā ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---for two or three days; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---expressed his ardent desire; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ā:āgrahe]]---by his eagerness; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]]---of Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**For two or three days Bhagavān Ācārya continually asked Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī to hear the poetry. Because of his repeated requests, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī wanted to hear the poetry written by the brāhmaṇa from Bengal.**
