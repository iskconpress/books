====== Cc. Antya 5.76 ======

===== Text =====

> hena 'rasa' pāna more karāilā tumi
> janme janme tomāra pāya vikāilāṅa āmi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]] [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---such transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---to drink; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You have caused; [[synonyms:j:janme]] [[synonyms:j:janme]]---life after life; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at Your feet; [[synonyms:v:vikāilāṅa]] [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I have become sold.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You have made me drink this transcendental nectar of kṛṣṇa-kathā. Therefore I am sold to Your lotus feet, life after life."**
