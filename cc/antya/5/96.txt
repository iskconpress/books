====== CC Antya 5.96 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-ṭhāñi uttare yadi, lañā, tāṅra mana
> tabe mahāprabhu-ṭhāñi karāya śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---before Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:u:uttare]]---passes; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if one; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---his mind; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāya]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---causes to be heard.

===== Translation =====

**If passed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, it could be presented for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to hear.**
