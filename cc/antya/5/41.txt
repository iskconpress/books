====== CC Antya 5.41 ======

===== Text =====

> nirvikāra deha-mana---kāṣṭha-pāṣāṇa-sama!
> āścarya,---taruṇī-sparśe nirvikāra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirvikāra]]---unchanged; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---body and mind; [[synonyms:k:kāṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:p:pāṣāṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like wood or stone; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:t:taruṇī]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśe]]---in touching young girls; [[synonyms:n:nirvikāra]]---unchanged; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"His mind is as steady as wood or stone. Indeed, it is wonderful that even when he touches such young girls, his mind never changes.**
