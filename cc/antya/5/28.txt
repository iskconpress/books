====== Cc. Antya 5.28 ======

===== Text =====

> miśrere namaskāra kare sammāna kariyā
> nivedana kare kichu vinīta hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśrere]]---unto Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:n:namaskārakare]]---offers respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:s:sammānakariyā]]---with all respect; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---submitted; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:v:vinīta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---with great humility.

===== Translation =====

**He offered his obeisances to Pradyumna Miśra with all respect and then, with great humility, spoke as follows.**
