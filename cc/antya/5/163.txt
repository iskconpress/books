====== Cc. Antya 5.163 ======

===== Text =====

> śraddhā kari' ei līlā yei paḍe, śune
> gaura-līlā, bhakti-bhakta-rasa-tattva jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with faith and love; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---these pastimes; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:p:paḍe,]] [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---reads and hears; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth about devotional service, devotees and their transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---understands.

===== Translation =====

**Anyone who reads and hears these pastimes with faith and love can understand the truth about devotional service, devotees and the transcendental mellows of the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
