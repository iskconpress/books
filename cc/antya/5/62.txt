====== CC Antya 5.62 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāla, manda---kichu āmi puchite nā jāni
> 'dīna' dekhi' kṛpā kari' kahibā āpani"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---good; [[synonyms:m:manda]]---bad; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:puchite]]---to inquire; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---do not know; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]---very poor in knowledge; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing (me); [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---very mercifully; [[synonyms:k:kahibā]]---please; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---by your own good will.

===== Translation =====

**"I do not know how to inquire, for I do not know what is good and what is bad. Seeing me to be poor in knowledge, kindly speak whatever is good for me by your own good will."**
