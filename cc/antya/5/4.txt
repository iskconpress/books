====== CC Antya 5.4 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina pradyumna-miśra prabhura caraṇe
> daṇḍavat kari' kichu kare nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---the devotee named Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering his respects; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---submits as a petition.

===== Translation =====

**One day Pradyumna Miśra came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, offering his respects and inquiring from Him with great submission.**
