====== CC Antya 5.39 ======

===== Text =====

> snānādi karāya, parāya vāsa-vibhūṣaṇa
> guhya aṅgera haya tāhā darśana-sparśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:snāna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---he performs their bathing and so on; [[synonyms:p:parāya]] [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vibhūṣaṇa]]---dresses and decorates the body with various types of ornaments; [[synonyms:g:guhya]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgera]]---of the private parts of the body; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśana]]---seeing and touching.

===== Translation =====

**"He personally bathes and dresses them and decorates them with ornaments. In this way, he naturally sees and touches the private parts of their bodies.**
