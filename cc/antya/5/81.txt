====== CC Antya 5.81 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-saba guṇa tāṅra prakāśa karite
> miśrere pāṭhāilā tāhāṅ śravaṇa karite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to demonstrate; [[synonyms:m:miśrere]]---Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---He sent; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to hear.

===== Translation =====

**To demonstrate the transcendental attributes of Rāmānanda Rāya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sent Pradyumna Miśra to hear discourses about Kṛṣṇa from him.**
