====== Cc. Antya 5.94 ======

===== Text =====

> sabei praśaṁse nāṭaka 'parama uttama'
> mahāprabhure śunāite sabāra haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabei]]---all; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁse]]---praised; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---the drama; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:u:uttama]]---"very good, very good"; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śunāite]]---to cause to hear; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**All the Vaiṣṇavas praised the drama, saying, "Very good, very good." They also desired for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to hear the drama.**
