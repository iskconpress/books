====== CC Antya 5.95 ======

===== Text =====

> gīta, śloka, grantha, kavitva---yei kari' āne
> prathame śunāya sei svarūpera sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---song; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---literature; [[synonyms:k:kavitva]]---poetry; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---brings; [[synonyms:p:prathame]]---first; [[synonyms:ś:śunāya]]---recites; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---before Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**Customarily, anyone who composed a song, verse, literary composition or poem about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu first had to bring it to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī to be heard.**
