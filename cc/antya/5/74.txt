====== CC Antya 5.74 ======

===== Text =====

> mora mukhe kahāya kathā, kare paracāra
> pṛthivīte ke jānibe e-līlā tāṅhāra?'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in my mouth; [[synonyms:k:kahāya]]---causes to speak; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---words; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:paracāra]]---preaches; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīte]]---in this world; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:j:jānibe]]---who will understand; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---this pastime; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way the Lord speaks through my mouth to preach the cult of Kṛṣṇa consciousness. Within the world, who will understand this pastime of the Lord?'**
