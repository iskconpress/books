====== CC Antya 5.12 ======

===== Text =====

> darśana nā pāñā miśra sevake puchila
> rāyera vṛttānta sevaka kahite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:darśana]]---audience; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:s:sevake]]---unto the servant; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:r:rāyera]]---of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]]---description; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servant; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Unable to see Rāmānanda Rāya immediately, Pradyumna Miśra inquired from the servant, who then gave a description of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya.**
