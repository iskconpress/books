====== Cc. Antya 5.157 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe saba bhakta tāre aṅgīkāra kailā
> tāra guṇa kahi' mahāprabhure milāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---accepted as one of the associates; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---explaining his humble behavior; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]] [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced him to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thereupon all the devotees accepted his association. Explaining his humble behavior, they introduced him to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
