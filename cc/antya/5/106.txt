====== CC Antya 5.106 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-līlā, gaura-līlā se kare varṇana
> gaura-pāda-padma yāṅra haya prāṇa-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:se]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---describes; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]]---the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the life and soul.

===== Translation =====

**"One who has accepted the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as his life and soul can describe the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa or the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
