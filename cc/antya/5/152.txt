====== Cc. Antya 5.152 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera darśane khaṇḍāya saṁsāra
> saba-deśera saba-loka nāre āsibāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by visiting; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāya]] [[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]---one is freed from material existence; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:d:deśera]]---of all countries; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---all men; [[synonyms:n:nāre]] [[synonyms:ā:āsibāra]]---cannot come.

===== Translation =====

**"By visiting Lord Jagannātha one is freed from material existence, but not all men of all countries can come or be admitted here in Jagannātha Purī.**
