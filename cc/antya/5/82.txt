====== Cc. Antya 5.82 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-guṇa prakāśite prabhu bhāla jāne
> nānā-bhaṅgīte guṇa prakāśi' nija-lābha māne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the attributes of a devotee; [[synonyms:p:prakāśite]]---to manifest; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows very well how; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅgīte]]---by various ways; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes; [[synonyms:p:prakāśi']]---manifesting; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:l:lābha]]---His profit; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---He considers.

===== Translation =====

**The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, knows very well how to demonstrate the qualities of His devotees. Therefore, acting like an artistic painter, He does so in various ways and considers this His personal profit.**
