====== Cc. Antya 5.58 ======

===== Text =====

> "prabhura ājñāya kṛṣṇa-kathā śunite āilā ethā
> ihā va-i mahā-bhāgya āmi pāba kothā?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---under the instruction of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]] [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---you have come here; [[synonyms:i:ihā]] [[synonyms:v:va]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---without this; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---great fortune; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:pāba]]---will get; [[synonyms:k:kothā]]---where.

===== Translation =====

**"Following the instruction of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, you have come to hear about Kṛṣṇa. This is my great fortune. How else would I get such an opportunity?"**
