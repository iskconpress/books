====== CC Antya 5.37 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda rāyera kathā śuna, sarva-jana
> kahibāra kathā nahe, yāhā āścarya-kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāyera]]---of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---all people; [[synonyms:k:kahibāra]]---to be spoken; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---talks; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---they are not; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---which; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]]-[[synonyms:k:kathana]]---wonderful and uncommon talks.

===== Translation =====

**"Everyone please hear these topics about Rāmānanda Rāya, although they are so wonderful and uncommon that they should not be spoken.**
