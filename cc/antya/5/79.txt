====== CC Antya 5.79 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-rāyera ei kahilu guṇa-leśa
> pradyumna miśrere yaiche kailā upadeśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāyera]]---of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kahilu]]---I have spoken; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:leśa]]---a fraction of the transcendental attributes; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]] [[synonyms:m:miśrere]]---unto Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---in which way; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]]---he gave instruction.

===== Translation =====

**I have described but a fraction of the transcendental attributes of Rāmānanda Rāya, as revealed when he instructed Pradyumna Miśra.**
