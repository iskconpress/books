====== CC Antya 5.70 ======

===== Text =====

> miśra kahe,---"prabhu, more kṛtārtha karilā
> kṛṣṇa-kathāmṛtārṇave more ḍubāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Pradyumna Miśra said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my dear Lord; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:k:kṛtārtha]]---satisfied; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---You have made; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---of talks about Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:arṇave]]---in the ocean of the nectar; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubāilā]]---You have drowned.

===== Translation =====

**Pradyumna Miśra said, "My dear Lord, You have made me extremely obliged to You because You have drowned me in a nectarean ocean of talks about Kṛṣṇa.**
