====== CC Antya 5.51 ======

===== Text =====

> rāgānuga-mārge jāni rāyera bhajana
> siddha-deha-tulya, tāte 'prākṛta' nahe mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāgānuga]]-[[synonyms:m:mārge]]---on the path of spontaneous love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---we can understand; [[synonyms:r:rāyera]] [[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---the devotional service of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]-[[synonyms:d:deha]]---spiritual body; [[synonyms:t:tulya]]---equal to; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:p:prākṛta]]---material; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya is situated on the path of spontaneous love of Godhead. Therefore he is in his spiritual body, and his mind is not materially affected.**
