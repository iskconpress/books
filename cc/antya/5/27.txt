====== CC Antya 5.27 ======

===== Text =====

> miśrera āgamana rāye sevaka kahilā
> śīghra rāmānanda tabe sabhāte āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśrera]]---of Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---arrival; [[synonyms:r:rāye]]---to Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---the servant informed; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very soon; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:s:sabhāte]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to the assembly room.

===== Translation =====

**When the servant informed Rāmānanda Rāya of Pradyumna Miśra's arrival, Rāmānanda Rāya immediately went to the assembly room.**
