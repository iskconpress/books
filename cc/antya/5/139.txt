====== CC Antya 5.139 ======

===== Text =====

> indra bale,---"muñi kṛṣṇera kariyāchi nindana"
> tāra-i mukhe sarasvatī karena stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:indra]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---Indra says; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchi]]---have done; [[synonyms:n:nindana]]---chastisement and defamation; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]-[[synonyms:i:i]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from his mouth; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---mother Sarasvatī, the goddess of learning; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---offers prayers.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus Indra thought, 'I have properly chastised Kṛṣṇa and defamed Him.' But Sarasvatī, the goddess of learning, took this opportunity to offer prayers to Kṛṣṇa.**
