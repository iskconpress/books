====== CC Antya 5.69 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura caraṇa vande ullasita-mane
> prabhu kake,---'kṛṣṇa-kathā ha-ila śravaṇe'?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:v:vande]]---he worships; [[synonyms:u:ullasita]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---talks of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---did you hear.

===== Translation =====

**In great happiness he worshiped the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The Lord inquired, "Have you heard topics about Kṛṣṇa?"**
