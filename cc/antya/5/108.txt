====== CC Antya 5.108 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpa yaiche dui nāṭaka kariyāche ārambhe
> śunite ānanda bāḍe yāra mukha-bandhe"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---dramas; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]] [[synonyms:ā:ārambhe]]---has compiled; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---transcendental happiness increases; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhe]]---even the introductory portion.

===== Translation =====

**"The standard for writing dramas has been set by Rūpa Gosvāmī. If a devotee hears the introductory portions of his two dramas, they enhance his transcendental pleasure."**
