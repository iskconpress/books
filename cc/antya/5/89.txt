====== CC Antya 5.89 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-caritāmṛta nitya kara pāna
> yāhā haite 'premānanda', 'bhakti-tattva-jñāna'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritāmṛta]]---this transcendental literature known as //Caitanya-caritāmṛta//; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---do relish; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---by which; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---transcendental knowledge in devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**O devotees, relish daily the nectar of Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta and the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, for by doing so one can merge in the transcendental bliss and full knowledge of devotional service.**
