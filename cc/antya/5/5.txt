====== Cc. Antya 5.5 ======

===== Text =====

> "śuna, prabhu, muñi dīna gṛhastha adhama!
> kona bhāgye pāñāchoṅ tomāra durlabha caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]---very fallen; [[synonyms:g:gṛhastha]]---householder; [[synonyms:a:adhama]]---the lowest of men; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---by some good fortune; [[synonyms:p:pāñāchoṅ]]---i have gotten; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:d:durlabha]]---rarely achieved; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord," he said, "kindly hear me. I am a cripple-minded householder, the most fallen of men, but somehow, by my good fortune, I have received the shelter of Your lotus feet, which are rarely to be seen.**
