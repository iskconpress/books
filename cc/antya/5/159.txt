====== CC Antya 5.159 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ pradyumna-miśra-vivaraṇa
> prabhura ājñāya kaila kṛṣṇa-kathāra śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---thus I have described; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---the descriptive narration of Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---on the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---listening to discourses on topics concerning Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**I have thus described the narration concerning Pradyumna Miśra and how, following the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he listened to discourses about Kṛṣṇa spoken by Rāmānanda Rāya.**
