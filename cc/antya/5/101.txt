====== CC Antya 5.101 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,---"tumi 'gopa' parama-udāra
> ye-se śāstra śunite icchā upaje tomāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]---cowherd boy; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:u:udāra]]---very liberal; [[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---anything written as scripture; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:u:upaje]]---awakens; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of you.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī replied, "Dear Bhagavān Ācārya, you are a very liberal cowherd boy. Sometimes the desire awakens within you to hear any kind of poetry.**
