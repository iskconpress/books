====== Cc. Antya 5.11 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe pradyumna-miśra gelā rāmānandera sthāne
> rāyera sevaka tāṅre vasāila āsane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---of the name Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:r:rāyera]] [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servant of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:v:vasāila]] [[synonyms:ā:āsane]]---gave a sitting place.

===== Translation =====

**Pradyumna Miśra, being thus advised by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, went to the home of Rāmānanda Rāya. There the servant of Rāmānanda Rāya gave him a proper place to sit down.**
