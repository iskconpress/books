====== CC Antya 5.103 ======

===== Text =====

> 'rasa', 'rasābhāsa' yāra nāhika vicāra
> bhakti-siddhānta-sindhu nāhi pāya pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---overlapping of transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---there is no consideration; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of the conclusions of devotional service; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---attains; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**"A so-called poet who has no knowledge of transcendentaI mellows and the overlapping of transcendental mellows cannot cross the ocean of the conclusions of devotional service.**
