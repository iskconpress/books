====== CC Antya 5.115 ======

===== Text =====

> sahaje jaḍa-jagatera cetana karāite
> nīlācale mahāprabhu hailā āvirbhute

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahaje]]---naturally; [[synonyms:j:jaḍa]]-[[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---the dull material world; [[synonyms:c:cetana]] [[synonyms:k:karāite]]---to inspire to spiritual consciousness; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āvirbhute]]---has appeared.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has appeared here in Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī] to spiritualize the entire dull material world."**
