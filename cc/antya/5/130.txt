====== Cc. Antya 5.130 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra duḥkha dekhi, svarūpa sadaya-hṛdaya
> upadeśa kailā tāre yaiche 'hita' haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---observing the unhappiness; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sadaya]]-[[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---very kindhearted; [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---gave instruction; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---so that; [[synonyms:h:hita]]---benefit; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there can be.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the poet's unhappiness, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, who was naturally very kindhearted, advised him so that he could derive some benefit.**
