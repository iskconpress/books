====== CC Antya 5.78 ======

===== Text =====

> mahānubhavera ei sahaja 'svabhāva' haya
> āpanāra guṇa nāhi āpane kahaya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahānubhavera]]---of those who are advanced in realization; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sahaja]]---natural; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---characteristic; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---their own personal qualities; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---do not; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:k:kahaya]]---speak.

===== Translation =====

**"This is a natural characteristic of those advanced in devotional service. They do not personally speak of their own good qualities."**
