====== CC Antya 5.160 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye kahiluṅ rāmānandera mahimā
> āpane śrī-mukhe prabhu varṇe yāṅra sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within these statements; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have explained; [[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from his mouth; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:v:varṇe]]---explains; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limit of ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Within the narration I have explained the glorious characteristics of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya, through whom Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally described the limits of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
