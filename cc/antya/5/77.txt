====== Cc. Antya 5.77 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"rāmānanda vinayera khani
> āpanāra kathā para-muṇḍe dena āni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the Lord replied; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vinayera]] [[synonyms:k:khani]]---the mine of humility; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---his own words; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:m:muṇḍe]]---on another's head; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---confers; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Rāmānanda Rāya is a source of all humility. Therefore he has attributed his own words to another's intelligence.**
