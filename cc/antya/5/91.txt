====== CC Antya 5.91 ======

===== Text =====

> baṅga-deśī eka vipra prabhura carite
> nāṭaka kari' lañā āila prabhuke śunāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:baṅga]]-[[synonyms:d:deśī]]---from Bengal; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---one //brāhmaṇa//; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:carite]]---about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's characteristics; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making a drama; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]] [[synonyms:ś:śunāite]]---to induce Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to hear.

===== Translation =====

**A brāhmaṇa from Bengal wrote a drama about the characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and came with his manuscript to induce the Lord to hear it.**
