====== CC Antya 5.162 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-līlā---amṛtera sāra
> eka-līlā-pravāhe vahe śata-śata dhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of nectar; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of one pastime; [[synonyms:p:pravāhe]]---by the stream; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---flow; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:ś:śata]] [[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---hundreds and hundreds of branches.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu are the essence of nectar. From the stream of one of His pastimes flow hundreds and thousands of branches.**
