====== CC Antya 5.146 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata śiśupāla karila nindana
> sei-vākye sarasvatī karena stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ś:śiśu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāla]]---of the name Śiśupāla; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:n:nindana]]---blasphemed; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:v:vākye]]---by those words; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---the goddess of learning; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---offers prayers.

===== Translation =====

**"Śiśupāla also blasphemed Kṛṣṇa in this way, but the goddess of learning, Sarasvatī, offered her prayers to Kṛṣṇa even by his words.**
