====== Cc. Antya 5.57 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' rāmānanda rāya hailā premāveśe
> kahite lāgilā kichu manera hariṣe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---absorbed in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:m:manera]] [[synonyms:h:hariṣe]]---in transcendental pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, Rāmānanda Rāya became absorbed in ecstatic love and began to speak with great transcendental pleasure.**
