====== CC Antya 5.32 ======

===== Text =====

> atikāla dekhi' miśra kichu nā kahila
> vidāya ha-iyā miśra nija-ghara gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:atikāla]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing that it was too late; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---did not say; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:iyā]]---taking leave; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghara]]---to his own place; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---returned.

===== Translation =====

**Because Pradyumna Miśra saw that it was late, he did not say anything else to Rāmānanda Rāya. Instead, he took leave of him and returned to his own home.**
