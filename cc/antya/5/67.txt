====== CC Antya 5.67 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu-sammāna kari' miśre vidāya dilā
> 'kṛtārtha ha-ilāṅa' bali' miśra nācite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---much respectful behavior; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:m:miśre]]---unto Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:k:kṛtārtha]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilāṅa]]---i have become very satisfied; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:n:nācite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to dance.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya paid great respect to Pradyumna Miśra and bade him farewell. Pradyumna Miśra said, "I have become very satisfied." He then began to dance.**
