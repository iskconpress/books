====== CC Antya 5.156 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe sei kavi sabāra caraṇe paḍiyā
> sabāra śaraṇa laila dante tṛṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kavi]]---poet; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the feet; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---falling down; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]] [[synonyms:l:laila]]---took shelter; [[synonyms:d:dante]]---in the mouth; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking a straw.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing this proper explanation by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, the Bengali poet fell down at the feet of all the devotees and took shelter of them with a straw in his mouth.**
