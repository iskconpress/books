====== Cc. Antya 5.136 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche indra, daityādi kare kṛṣṇera bhartsana
> sei-śabde sarasvatī karena stavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just like; [[synonyms:i:indra]]---Lord Indra, the King of heaven; [[synonyms:d:daitya]]---demons; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:b:bhartsana]]---chastisement to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---by those words; [[synonyms:s:sarasvatī]]---the goddess of learning; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:s:stavana]]---offers prayers.

===== Translation =====

**"Sometimes demons, and even Lord Indra, the King of heaven, chastised Kṛṣṇa, but mother Sarasvatī, taking advantage of their words, offered prayers to the Lord.**
