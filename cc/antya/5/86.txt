====== Cc. Antya 5.86 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa-dvārā nāma-māhātmya-prakāśa
> sanātana-dvārā bhakti-siddhānta-vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---by Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:m:māhātmya]]---of the glories of chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---by Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---spreading the essence of devotional life.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu exhibited the glories of the holy name of the Lord through Haridāsa Ṭhākura, who was born in a Mohammedan family. Similarly, He exhibited the essence of devotional service through Sanātana Gosvāmī, who had almost been converted into a Mohammedan.**
