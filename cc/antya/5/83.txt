====== CC Antya 5.83 ======

===== Text =====

> āra eka 'svabhāva' gaurera śuna, bhakta-gaṇa
> aiśvarya-svabhāva gūḍha kare prakaṭana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---characteristic; [[synonyms:g:gaurera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---opulences and characteristics; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]---very deep; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭana]]---manifestation.

===== Translation =====

**There is yet another characteristic of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. O devotees, listen carefully to how He manifests His opulence and characteristics, although they are exceptionally deep.**
