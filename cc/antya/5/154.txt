====== Cc. Antya 5.154 ======

===== Text =====

> sarasvatīra artha ei kahiluṅ vivaraṇa
> eho bhāgya tomāra aiche karile varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarasvatīra]]---of Sarasvatī; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]] [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---I have explained; [[synonyms:e:eho]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]---great fortune; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in such a way; [[synonyms:k:karile]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---you have described.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus I have explained the meaning intended by mother Sarasvatī, the goddess of learning. It is your great fortune that you have described Lord Jagannātha and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in that way.**
