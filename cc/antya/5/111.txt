====== CC Antya 5.111 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā lañā svarūpa gosāñi śunite vasilā
> tabe sei kavi nāndī-śloka paḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---in the company of other devotees; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]] [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down to hear; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kavi]]---that poet; [[synonyms:n:nāndī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the introductory verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---read.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī sat down with other devotees to hear the poetry, and then the poet began to read the introductory verse.**
