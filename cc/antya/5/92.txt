====== CC Antya 5.92 ======

===== Text =====

> bhagavān-ācārya-sane tāra paricaya
> tāṅre mili' tāṅra ghare karila ālaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---the devotee of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu named Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:p:paricaya]]---his acquaintance; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:m:mili']]---meeting him; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at his place; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:ā:ālaya]]---made residence.

===== Translation =====

**The brāhmaṇa was acquainted with Bhagavān Ācārya, one of the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Therefore after meeting him at Jagannātha Purī, the brāhmaṇa made his residence at Bhagavān Ācārya' s home.**
