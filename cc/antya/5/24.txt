====== CC Antya 5.24 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāva-prakaṭana-lāsya rāya ye śikhāya
> jagannāthera āge duṅhe prakaṭa dekhāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭana]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:l:lāsya]]---feminine poses and dancing; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāya]]---was teaching; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]] [[synonyms:d:dekhāya]]---demonstrated.

===== Translation =====

**Through the feminine poses and dances they were taught by Rāmānanda Rāya, the two girls precisely exhibited all these expressions of ecstasy before Lord Jagannātha.**
