====== CC Antya 5.109 ======

===== Text =====

> bhagavān-ācārya kahe,---'śuna eka-bāra
> tumi śunile bhāla-manda jānibe vicāra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---once; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:ś:śunile]]---if you hear; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]-[[synonyms:m:manda]]---good or bad; [[synonyms:j:jānibe]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---will be able to understand.

===== Translation =====

**Despite the explanation of Svarūpa Dāmodara, Bhagavān Ācārya requested, "Please hear the drama once. If you hear it, you can consider whether it is good or bad."**
