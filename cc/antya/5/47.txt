====== CC Antya 5.47 ======

===== Text =====

> ujjvala madhura prema-bhakti sei pāya
> ānande kṛṣṇa-mādhurye vihare sadāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---illuminated; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhurye]]---the sweetness of Kṛṣṇa's pastimes; [[synonyms:v:vihare]]---enjoys; [[synonyms:s:sadāya]]---always.

===== Translation =====

**"Tasting the transcendental, effulgent, sweetly ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa, such a person can enjoy life twenty-four hours a day in the transcendental bIiss of the sweetness of Kṛṣṇa's pastimes.**
