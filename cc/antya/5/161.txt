====== CC Antya 5.161 ======

===== Text =====

> prastāve kahiluṅ kavira nāṭaka-vivaraṇa
> ajña hañā śraddhāya pāila prabhura caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prastāve]]---by the way; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have explained; [[synonyms:k:kavira]]---of the poet; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description of the drama; [[synonyms:a:ajña]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---although being ignorant; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhāya]]---with faith and love; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the shelter of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**In the course of the narration, I have also told about the drama by the poet from Bengal. Although he was ignorant, because of his faith and humility he nevertheless obtained the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
