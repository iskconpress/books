====== CC Antya 5.138 ======

===== Text =====

> aiśvarya-made matta indra,---yena mātoyāla
> buddhi-nāśa haila, kevala nāhika sāmbhāla

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]]-[[synonyms:m:made]]---being proud of his opulence; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:i:indra]]---the King of heaven; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:m:mātoyāla]]---a mad person; [[synonyms:b:buddhi]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---bereft of intelligence; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is not; [[synonyms:s:sāmbhāla]]---caution.

===== Translation =====

**"Indra, the King of heaven, being too proud of his heavenly opulences, became like a madman. Thus bereft of his intelligence, he could not restrain himself from speaking nonsensically about Kṛṣṇa.**
