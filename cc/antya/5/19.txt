====== CC Antya 5.19 ======

===== Text =====

> kāṣṭha-pāṣāṇa-sparśe haya yaiche bhāva
> taruṇī-sparśe rāmānandera taiche 'svabhāva'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāṣṭha]]---wood; [[synonyms:p:pāṣāṇa]]---stone; [[synonyms:s:sparśe]]---by touching; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---mental position; [[synonyms:t:taruṇī]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśe]]---by touching the young girls; [[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]]---of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---like that; [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---nature.

===== Translation =====

**While touching the young girls, he was like a person touching wood or stone, for his body and mind were unaffected.**
