====== CC Antya 5.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jayādvaita kṛpā-sindhu jaya bhakta-gaṇa
> jaya svarūpa, gadādhara, rūpa, sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:a:advaita]]---all glories to Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of mercy,; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all glories to the devotees; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---all glories to Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**I offer my respectful obeisances unto Advaita Prabhu, the ocean of mercy, and to all the devotees, such as Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, Gadādhara Paṇḍita, Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī.**
