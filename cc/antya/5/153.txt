====== CC Antya 5.153 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-prabhu deśe deśe yāñā
> saba-loke nistārilā jaṅgama-brahma hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:deśe]] [[synonyms:d:deśe]] [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---moving from one country to another; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:l:loke]] [[synonyms:n:nistārilā]]---delivered all conditioned souls; [[synonyms:j:jaṅgama]]-[[synonyms:b:brahma]]---moving Brahman; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, however, moves from one country to another, personally or by His representative. Thus He, as the moving Brahman, delivers all the people of the world.**
