====== CC Antya 5.8 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāgye tomāra kṛṣṇa-kathā śunite haya mana
> rāmānanda-pāśa yāi' karaha śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgye]]---by fortune; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics about Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---there is an inclination; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---to Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hear.

===== Translation =====

**"It is your good fortune that you are inclined to hear topics regarding Kṛṣṇa. The best course for you would be to go to Rāmānanda Rāya and hear these topics from him.**
