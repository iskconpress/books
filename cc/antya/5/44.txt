====== Cc. Antya 5.44 ======

===== Text =====

> kintu śāstra-dṛṣṭye eka kari anumāna
> śrī-bhāgavata-śāstra---tāhāte pramāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kintu]]---but; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭye]]---according to the direction of the //śāstra//; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:a:anumāna]]---I make a guess; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---//Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam,// the Vedic scripture; [[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---in that connection; [[synonyms:p:pramāṇa]]---evidence.

===== Translation =====

**"But I can make a guess in terms of directions from the śāstra. Śrīmad Bhāgavatam, the Vedic scripture, gives the direct evidence in this matter.**
