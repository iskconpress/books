====== CC Antya 5.16 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe pradyumna-miśra tāhāṅ rahila vasiyā
> rāmānanda nibhṛte sei dui-jana lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---of the name Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rahila]] [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---remained seated; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two girls; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**While Pradyumna Miśra remained seated there, Rāmānanda Rāya took the two girls to a solitary place.**
