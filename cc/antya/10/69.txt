====== Cc. Antya 10.69 ======

===== Text =====

> ei pade nṛtya karena parama-āveśe
> saba-loka caudike prabhura prema-jale bhāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:p:pade]]---by this line; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---dances; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]---all people; [[synonyms:c:cau]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---in all four directions; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---in tears of love; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---float.

===== Translation =====

**Simply because of this line, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was dancing in greatly ecstatic love. People all around Him floated in the water of His tears.**
