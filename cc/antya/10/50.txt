~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya 10.50~~

====== CC Antya 10.50 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ei jala-krīḍā dāsa-vṛndāvana
> 'caitanya-maṅgale' vistāri' kariyāchena varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---activities in the water; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:m:maṅgale]]---in his book //Caitanya-maṅgala,// now known as //Caitanya-bhāgavata//; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---describing in detail; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---has narrated.

===== Translation =====

**In his Caitanya-maṅgala [now known as Caitanya-bhāgavata], Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has given a detailed description of the activities the Lord performed in the water.**
