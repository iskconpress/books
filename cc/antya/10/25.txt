====== CC Antya 10.25 ======

===== Text =====

> nārikela-khaṇḍa nāḍu, āra nāḍu gaṅgā-jala
> cira-sthāyī khaṇḍa-vikāra karilā sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nārikela]]-[[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:n:nāḍu]]---sweetmeat balls made with coconut powder; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:n:nāḍu]] [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---a sweetmeat ball as white as Ganges water; [[synonyms:c:cira]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāyi]]---long-lasting; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---modified forms of sugar-candy sweets; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**She made many sweetmeats shaped like balls. Some were made with powdered coconut, and others looked as white as the water of the Ganges. In this way she made many varieties of long-lasting sugar confections.**
