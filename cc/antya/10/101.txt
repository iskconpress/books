====== CC Antya 10.101 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-guṇa prakāśite prabhu baḍa raṅgī
> ei saba prakāśite kailā eta bhaṅgī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the attributes of the devotee; [[synonyms:p:prakāśite]]---to manifest; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:r:raṅgī]]---very interested; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:p:prakāśite]]---to manifest; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---He performed; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---such; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgī]]---incident.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord is very interested in manifesting the exalted qualities of His devotees, and that is why He engineered this incident.**
