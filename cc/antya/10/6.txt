====== CC Antya 10.6 ======

===== Text =====

> anurāgera lakṣaṇa ei,---'vidhi' nāhi māne
> tāṅra ājñā bhāṅge tāṅra saṅgera kāraṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anurāgera]]---of real affection; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---symptom; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---the regulation; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---does not care for; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅge]]---neglects; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:s:saṅgera]]---association; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇe]]---for the purpose of.

===== Translation =====

**Indeed, it is a symptom of real affection that one breaks the order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, not caring for the regulative principles, to associate with Him.**
