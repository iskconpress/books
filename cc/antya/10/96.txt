====== CC Antya 10.96 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sevā' lāgi' koṭi 'aparādha' nāhi gaṇi
> sva-nimitta 'aparādhābhāse' bhaya māni"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the matter of service; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]] [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---ten million offenses; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇi]]---I do not care for; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:n:nimitta]]---for my personal self; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāse]]---by a glimpse of an offense; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]] [[synonyms:m:māni]]---I am afraid.

===== Translation =====

**"I would not mind committing hundreds and thousands of offenses for the service of the Lord, but I greatly fear committing even a glimpse of an offense for my own self."**
