====== CC Antya 10.42 ======

===== Text =====

> narendrera jale 'govinda' naukāte caḍiyā
> jala-krīḍā kare saba bhakta-gaṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:narendrera]] [[synonyms:j:jale]]---upon the water of the lake known as Narendra-sarovara; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Lord Govinda; [[synonyms:n:naukāte]] [[synonyms:c:caḍiyā]]---getting aboard a boat; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---displays His water pastimes; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Boarding a boat in the waters of Narendra-sarovara, Lord Govinda performed His water pastimes with all the devotees.**
