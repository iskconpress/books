====== CC Antya 10.37 ======

===== Text =====

> sāmānya jhāli haite dviguṇa jhāli kailā
> pāripāṭi kari' saba jhāli bharāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāmānya]]---small; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]---bags; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---twice as big; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]---bags; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:p:pāripāṭi]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great attention; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]---all the bags; [[synonyms:b:bharāilā]]---she filled.

===== Translation =====

**From small bags Damayantī made bags that were twice as large. Then with great attention she filled all the large ones with the small ones.**
