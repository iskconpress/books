====== Cc. Antya 10.48 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍīyā-saṅkīrtane āra rodana miliyā
> mahā-kolāhala haila brahmāṇḍa bhariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍīyā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtane]]---congregational chanting by the Gauḍīya Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---crying; [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---mixing; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---a great, tumultuous sound vibration; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]---the universe; [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---filling.

===== Translation =====

**The chanting and crying of the Gauḍīya Vaiṣṇavas mixed and created a tumultuous sound vibration that filled the entire universe.**
