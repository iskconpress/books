====== CC Antya 10.64 ======

===== Text =====

> kīrtana-āṭope pṛthivī kare ṭalamala
> 'hari-dhvani' kare loka, haila kolāhala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āṭope]]---by the force of congregational chanting; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivī]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭalamala]]---trembles; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---chanted the transcendental sound Hari; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people in general; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---a tumultuous sound.

===== Translation =====

**Due to the forceful vibration of kīrtana, the entire world began trembling. When everyone chanted the holy name, they made a tumultuous sound.**
