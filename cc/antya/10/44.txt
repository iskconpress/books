====== CC Antya 10.44 ======

===== Text =====

> sei-kāle āilā saba gauḍera bhakta-gaṇa
> narendrete prabhu-saṅge ha-ila milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the same time; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees from Bengal; [[synonyms:n:narendrete]]---at the lake known as Narendra-sarovara; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---there was a meeting.

===== Translation =====

**At the same time, all the devotees from Bengal arrived at the lake and had a great meeting with the Lord.**
