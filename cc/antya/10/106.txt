====== CC Antya 10.106 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-māsa varṣāya rahilā saba bhakta-gaṇa
> janmāṣṭamī ādi yātrā kailā daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---for four months; [[synonyms:v:varṣāya]]---of the rainy season; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---stayed; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:j:janmāṣṭamī]] [[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---festivals like Lord Kṛṣṇa's birth ceremony; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---observed.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees from Bengal stayed in Jagannātha Purī for the four months of the rainy season and observed many other ceremonies, such as the anniversary of Lord Kṛṣṇa's birth.**
