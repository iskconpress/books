====== CC Antya 10.143 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure milāite tāṅre saṅgei ānilā
> milāile, prabhu tāṅra nāma ta' puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:m:milāite]]---to introduce to the Lord; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him, //Caitanya dāsa//; [[synonyms:s:saṅgei]]---along; [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:m:milāile]]---when he introduced him; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thereupon; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired.

===== Translation =====

**When Śivānanda brought his son, Caitanya dāsa, to be introduced to the Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired about his name.**
