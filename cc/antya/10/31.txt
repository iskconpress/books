====== Cc. Antya 10.31 ======

===== Text =====

> śāli-dhānyera kha-i punaḥ ghṛtete bhājiyā
> cini-pāka ukhḍā kailā karpūrādi diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāli]]-[[synonyms:d:dhānyera]] [[synonyms:k:kha]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---parched rice from fine paddy; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:g:ghṛtete]] [[synonyms:b:bhājiyā]]---frying with ghee; [[synonyms:c:cini]]-[[synonyms:p:pāka]]---boiling with sugar juice; [[synonyms:u:ukhḍā]]---of the name //ukhḍā//; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---mixing with camphor.

===== Translation =====

**She took parched rice from fine paddy, fried it in ghee, cooked it in a sugar solution, mixed in some camphor and thus made a preparation called ukhḍā or muḍki.**
