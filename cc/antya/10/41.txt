====== CC Antya 10.41 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate vaiṣṇava saba nīlācale āilā
> daive jagannāthera se dina jala-līlā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Nīlācala; [[synonyms:d:daive]]---by chance; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---that day; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes in the water.

===== Translation =====

**Thus all the Vaiṣṇavas from Bengal went to Jagannātha Purī. By chance, they arrived on the day when Lord Jagannātha performs pastimes in the water.**
