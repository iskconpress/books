====== CC Antya 10.156 ======

===== Text =====

> prathame āchila 'nirbandha' kauḍi cāri-paṇa
> rāmacandra-purī-bha ye ghāṭāilā nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathame]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:ā:āchila]]---it was; [[synonyms:n:nirbandha]]---fixed; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇa]]---four //paṇas// of conchshells; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---because of the restriction of Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭāilā]]---decreased; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---the price of an invitation.

===== Translation =====

**At first the cost of Jagannātha prasāda for an invitation was four paṇas of conchshells, but when Rāmacandra Purī was there, the price was cut in half.**
