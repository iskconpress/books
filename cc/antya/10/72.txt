====== CC Antya 10.72 ======

===== Text =====

> saghana pulaka,---yena śimulera taru
> kabhu praphullita aṅga, kabhu haya saru

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:ghana]]---constant; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]---standing of the hairs of the body; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---like; [[synonyms:ś:śimulera]] [[synonyms:t:taru]]---the //śimula// tree; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:praphullita]]---swollen; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---body; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:s:saru]]---lean and thin.

===== Translation =====

**The hairs on His body constantly stood up like the thorns on a śimula tree. Sometimes His body was swollen and sometimes lean and thin.**
