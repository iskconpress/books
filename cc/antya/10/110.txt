====== CC Antya 10.110 ======

===== Text =====

> 'amuk ei diyāche' govinda kare nivedana
> 'dhari' rākha' bali' prabhu nā karena bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:amuk]]---such and such devotee; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:d:diyāche]]---has given; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---informs; [[synonyms:d:dhari']] [[synonyms:r:rākha]]---please keep them; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---does not eat.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda would present the prasāda and say to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "This has been given by such and such devotee." The Lord, however, would not actually eat it. He would simply say, "Keep it in storage."**
