====== Cc. Antya 10.35 ======

===== Text =====

> gaṅgā-mṛttikā āni' vastrete chāniyā
> pāṅpaḍi kariyā dilā gandha-dravya diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:m:mṛttikā]]---dirt from the River Ganges; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:v:vastrete]]---through a cloth; [[synonyms:c:chāniyā]]---pressing; [[synonyms:p:pāṅpaḍi]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---made into small balls; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---mixing with aromatic agents.

===== Translation =====

**Damayantī took earth from the Ganges, dried it, powdered it, strained it through a fine cloth, mixed in aromatic ingredients and rolled it into small balls.**
