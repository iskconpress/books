====== CC Antya 10.36 ======

===== Text =====

> pātala mṛt-pātre sandhānādi bhari'
> āra saba vastu bhare vastrera kuthalī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pātala]]---thin; [[synonyms:m:mṛt]]-[[synonyms:p:pātre]]---in pots of earth; [[synonyms:s:sandhāna]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---condiments and other items; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---other; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---things; [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---filled; [[synonyms:v:vastrera]] [[synonyms:k:kuthalī]]---small bags of cloth.

===== Translation =====

**The condiments and similar items were put into thin earthen pots, and everything else was put into small cloth bags.**
