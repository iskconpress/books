====== CC Antya 10.79 ======

===== Text =====

> kolāhala nāhi, prabhura kichu bāhya haila
> tabe nityānanda sabāra śrama jānāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was not; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---fatigue; [[synonyms:j:jānāila]]---informed.

===== Translation =====

**When there was no longer a tumultuous sound, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to consciousness. Then Nityānanda Prabhu informed Him of the fatigue of the chanters and dancers.**
