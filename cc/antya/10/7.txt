====== CC Antya 10.7 ======

===== Text =====

> rāse yaiche ghara yāite gopīre ājñā dilā
> tāṅra ājñā bhāṅgi' tāṅra saṅge se rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāse]]---at the time of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:g:ghara]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to return home; [[synonyms:g:gopīre]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa ordered; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgi']]---breaking; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in His association; [[synonyms:s:se]]---they; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---kept themselves.

===== Translation =====

**During the rāsa dance, Kṛṣṇa asked all the gopīs to return home, but the gopīs neglected His order and stayed there for His association.**
