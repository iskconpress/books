====== Cc. Antya 10.46 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍīyā-sampradāya saba karena kīrtana
> prabhura milane uṭhe premera krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍīyā]]-[[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---of the group of Vaiṣṇavas from Bengal; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---perform congregational chanting; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:milane]]---upon meeting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---rise; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---crying in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**The Gauḍīya-sampradāya, consisting of all the devotees from Bengal, began congregational chanting. When they met the Lord, they began to cry loudly in ecstatic love.**
