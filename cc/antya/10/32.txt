====== CC Antya 10.32 ======

===== Text =====

> phuṭkalāi cūrṇa kari' ghṛte bhājāila
> cini-pāke karpūrādi diyā nāḍu kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:phuṭkalāi]]---fused peas fried in ghee and soaked in sugar juice; [[synonyms:c:cūrṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making into powder; [[synonyms:g:ghṛte]] [[synonyms:b:bhājāila]]---fried with ghee; [[synonyms:c:cini]]-[[synonyms:p:pāke]]---cooking with sugar; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---camphor and other ingredients; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---adding; [[synonyms:n:nāḍu]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made round sweetmeat balls.

===== Translation =====

**Another variety of sweet was made with fused peas that were powdered, fried in ghee and then cooked in sugar juice. Camphor was mixed in, and then the sweet was rolled into a ball.**
