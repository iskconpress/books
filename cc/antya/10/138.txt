====== Cc. Antya 10.138 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera prasāda āne karite miśrita
> kāhāṅ ekā yāyena, kāhāṅ gaṇera sahita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---bring; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:m:miśrita]]---mixing; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---somewhere; [[synonyms:e:ekā]] [[synonyms:y:yāyena]]---goes alone; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---somewhere; [[synonyms:g:gaṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with associates.

===== Translation =====

**They would mix these preparations with the remnants of food from Lord Jagannātha. When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the invitations, He went sometimes alone and sometimes with His associates.**
