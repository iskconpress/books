====== CC Antya 10.127 ======

===== Text =====

> śata-janera bhakṣya prabhu daṇḍeke khāilā!
> 'āra kichu āche?' bali' govinde puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:j:janera]]---of one hundred persons; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]]---eatables; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍeke]] [[synonyms:k:khāilā]]---ate within twenty-four minutes; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---is there anything more; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:g:govinde]]---unto Govinda; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired.

===== Translation =====

**Within a very short time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ate enough for a hundred people. Then He asked Govinda, "Is there anything more left?"**
