====== Cc. Antya 10.160 ======

===== Text =====

> śraddhā kari' śune yei caitanyera kathā
> caitanya-caraṇe prema pāibe sarvathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great faith and love; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the narration of the activities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love; [[synonyms:p:pāibe]]---must achieve; [[synonyms:s:sarvathā]]---without fail.

===== Translation =====

**One who hears about the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with faith and love will certainly attain ecstatic love for the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu without fail.**
