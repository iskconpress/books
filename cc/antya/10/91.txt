====== CC Antya 10.91 ======

===== Text =====

> sukhe nidrā haila prabhura, govinda cāpe aṅga
> daṇḍa-dui ba-i prabhura hailā nidrā-bhaṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---very nicely; [[synonyms:n:nidrā]] [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu slept; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:c:cāpe]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---pressed the body; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bāi]]---after about forty-five minutes; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---there was; [[synonyms:n:nidrā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅga]]---breaking of sleep.

===== Translation =====

**As Govinda stroked His body, the Lord slept very nicely for about forty-five minutes, and then His sleep broke.**
