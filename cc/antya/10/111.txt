====== CC Antya 10.111 ======

===== Text =====

> dharite dharite gharera bharila eka koṇa
> śata-janera bhakṣya yata haila sañcayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dharite]] [[synonyms:d:dharite]]---keeping and keeping; [[synonyms:g:gharera]]---of the room; [[synonyms:b:bharila]]---filled up; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:koṇa]]---one corner; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:j:janera]]---of one hundred people; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]]---sufficient for feeding; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:sañcayana]]---accumulation.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda kept accumulating the food, and soon it filled a corner of the room. There was quite enough to feed at least a hundred people.**
