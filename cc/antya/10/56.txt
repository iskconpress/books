====== CC Antya 10.56 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva-vatsarera jhāli ājāḍa kariyā
> dravya bharibāre rākhe anya gṛhe lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsarera]]---of the previous year; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]---bags; [[synonyms:ā:ājāḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---emptying; [[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:b:bharibāre]]---to fill with goods; [[synonyms:r:rākhe]]---keeps; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to another room; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda thoroughly emptied the bags from the previous year and kept them in another room to fill them with other goods.**
