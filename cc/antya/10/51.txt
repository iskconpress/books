====== CC Antya 10.51 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ ihāṅ varṇile punarukti haya
> vyartha likhana haya, āra grantha bāḍaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:v:varṇile]]---if I describe; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]-[[synonyms:u:ukti]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it will be repetition; [[synonyms:v:vyartha]]---useless; [[synonyms:l:likhana]]---writing; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:g:grantha]] [[synonyms:b:bāḍaya]]---increases the volume of the book.

===== Translation =====

**There is no use describing here the activities of the Lord again. It would simply be repetitious and would increase the size of this book.**
