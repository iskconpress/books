====== CC Antya 10.38 ======

===== Text =====

> jhāli bāndhi' mohara dila āgraha kariyā
> tina bojhāri jhāli vahe krama kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jhāli]] [[synonyms:b:bāndhi']]---binding the bags; [[synonyms:m:mohara]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---she sealed; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great attention; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:b:bojhāri]]---three carriers; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]] [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---carried the bags; [[synonyms:k:krama]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---one after another.

===== Translation =====

**She then wrapped and sealed each and every bag with great attention. The bags were carried by three bearers, one after another.**
