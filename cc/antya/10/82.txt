====== CC Antya 10.82 ======

===== Text =====

> gambhīrāra dvāre karena āpane śayana
> govinda āsiyā kare pāda-samvāhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gambhīrāra]] [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door of the Gambhīrā, the small room within the room; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---His personal servant Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:samvāhana]]---massaging the legs.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lay down at the door of the Gambhīrā, and Govinda came there to massage His legs.**
