====== CC Antya 10.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vande śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanyaṁ
> bhaktānugraha-kātaram
> yena kenāpi santuṣṭaṁ
> bhakta-dattena śraddhayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanyam]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---to His devotees; [[synonyms:a:anugraha]]-[[synonyms:k:kātaram]]---eager to show mercy; [[synonyms:y:yena]] [[synonyms:k:kenāpi]]---by anything; [[synonyms:s:santuṣṭam]]---pleased; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---by His devotees; [[synonyms:d:dattena]]---offered; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---with faith and love.

===== Translation =====

**Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is always pleased to accept anything given with faith and love by His devotees and is always ready to bestow mercy upon them.**
