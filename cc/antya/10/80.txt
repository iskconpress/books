====== CC Antya 10.80 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-śrama jāni' kailā kīrtana samāpana
> sabā lañā āsi' kailā samudre snapana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---the fatigue of the devotees; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---understanding; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:s:samāpana]]---ending the chanting; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---accompanied by all of them; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:s:samudre]]---in the sea; [[synonyms:s:snapana]]---bathing.

===== Translation =====

**Understanding the fatigue of the devotees, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stopped the congregational chanting. Then He bathed in the sea, accompanied by them all.**
