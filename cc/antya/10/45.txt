====== CC Antya 10.45 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa paḍe āsi' prabhura caraṇe
> uṭhāñā prabhu sabāre kailā āliṅgane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---getting them up; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgane]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees immediately fell at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord lifted and embraced every one of them.**
