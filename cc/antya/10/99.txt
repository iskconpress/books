====== CC Antya 10.99 ======

===== Text =====

> yāiteha patha nāhi, yāibe kemane?
> mahā-aparādha haya prabhura laṅghane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāiteha]]---to go; [[synonyms:p:patha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was no passage; [[synonyms:y:yāibe]] [[synonyms:k:kemane]]---how would he go away; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---a great offense; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there would be; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:l:laṅghane]]---to cross over the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**There was no way to go. How could he leave? When he thought of crossing over the Lord's body, he considered it a great offense.**
