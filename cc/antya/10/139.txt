====== CC Antya 10.139 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryaratna, ācāryanidhi, nandana, rāghava
> śrīvāsa-ādi yata bhakta, vipra saba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryaratna]]---Ācāryaratna; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryanidhi]]---Ācāryanidhi; [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Nandana Ācārya; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all devotees; [[synonyms:v:vipra]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all //brāhmaṇas.//

===== Translation =====

**Devotees like Ācāryaratna, Ācāryanidhi, Nandana Ācārya, Rāghava Paṇḍita and Śrīvāsa were all of the brāhmaṇa caste.**
