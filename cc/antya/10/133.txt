====== CC Antya 10.133 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> cāturmāsya goṅāilā kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with His personal devotees; [[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]] [[synonyms:g:goṅāilā]]---passed the four months of the rainy season; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the happiness of discussing topics about Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spent the entire period of Cāturmāsya [the four months of the rainy season] in the happiness of discussing topics of Kṛṣṇa with His devotees.**
