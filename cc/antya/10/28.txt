====== Cc. Antya 10.28 ======

===== Text =====

> kateka ciḍā huḍum kari' ghṛtete bhājiyā
> cini-pāke nāḍu kailā karpūrādi diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kateka]] [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]---some of the flat rice; [[synonyms:h:huḍum]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making into puffed rice; [[synonyms:g:ghṛtete]] [[synonyms:b:bhājiyā]]---frying in ghee; [[synonyms:c:cini]]-[[synonyms:p:pāke]]---by cooking in sugar juice; [[synonyms:n:nāḍu]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made into round balls; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---mixing with camphor and other ingredients.

===== Translation =====

**She made some of the flat rice into puffed rice, fried it in ghee, cooked it in sugar juice, mixed in some camphor and rolled it into balls.**
