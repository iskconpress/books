====== CC Antya 10.66 ======

===== Text =====

> sāta-dike sāta-sampradāya gāya, bājāya
> madhye mahā-premāveśe nāce gaura-rāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāta]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---in seven directions; [[synonyms:s:sāta]]-[[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---the seven groups; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chant; [[synonyms:b:bājāya]]---play on the //mṛdaṅga//; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the center; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:premāveśe]]---in great ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**The seven groups began chanting and beating their drums in seven directions, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began dancing in the center in great ecstatic love.**
