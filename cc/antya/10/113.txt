====== Cc. Antya 10.113 ======

===== Text =====

> kāhāṅ kichu kahi' govinda kare vañcana
> āra dina prabhure kahe nirveda-vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---to someone; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:v:vañcana]]---told lies; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:n:nirveda]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---a statement of disappointment.

===== Translation =====

**When the devotees questioned Govinda, he had to tell them lies. Therefore one day he spoke to the Lord in disappointment.**
