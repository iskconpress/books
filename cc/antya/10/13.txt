====== CC Antya 10.13 ======

===== Text =====

> rāghava-paṇḍita cale jhāli sājāiyā
> damayantī yata dravya diyāche kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]] [[synonyms:s:sājāiyā]]---after preparing his bag of food; [[synonyms:d:damayantī]]---his sister; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---all the goods; [[synonyms:d:diyāche]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---cooked and prepared.

===== Translation =====

**Rāghava Paṇḍita came with bags full of food prepared very nicely by his sister, Damayantī.**
