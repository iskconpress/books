====== Cc. Antya 10.54 ======

===== Text =====

> iṣṭa-goṣṭhī sabā lañā kata-kṣaṇa kailā
> nija nija pūrva-vāsāya sabāya pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---discussion of spiritual matters; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:n:nija]]---respective; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsāya]]---to the former residences; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---all; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---He sent.

===== Translation =====

**After talking with all the devotees for some time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked them to occupy the individual residences in which they had lived the previous year.**
