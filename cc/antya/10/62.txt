====== CC Antya 10.62 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkīrtana-kolāhale ākāśa bhedila
> saba jagannātha-vāsī dekhite āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]-[[synonyms:k:kolāhale]]---tumultuous roaring of congregational chanting; [[synonyms:ā:ākāśa]] [[synonyms:b:bhedila]]---filled the sky; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---the inhabitants of Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came to see.

===== Translation =====

**The congregational chanting made a tumultuous roar that filled the sky. All the inhabitants of Jagannātha Purī came to see the kīrtana.**
