====== CC Antya 10.22 ======

===== Text =====

> dhaniyā-mauharīra taṇḍula guṇḍā kariyā
> nāḍu bāndhiyāche cini-pāka kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhaniyā]]---of coriander seeds; [[synonyms:m:mauharīra]]---of anise seeds; [[synonyms:t:taṇḍula]]---grains; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍā]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---grinding to a powder; [[synonyms:n:nāḍu]] [[synonyms:b:bāndhiyāche]]---rendered into //laḍḍus//; [[synonyms:c:cini]]-[[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---cooking with sugar.

===== Translation =====

**Damayantī powdered coriander and anise seeds, cooked them with sugar and made them into small sweetmeats that were shaped like small balls.**
