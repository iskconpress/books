====== Cc. Antya 10.60 ======

===== Text =====

> vakreśvara, acyutānanda, paṇḍita-śrīvāsa
> satyarāja-khāṅna, āra narahari-dāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara; [[synonyms:a:acyutānanda]]---Acyutānanda; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Paṇḍita Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:s:satyarāja]]-[[synonyms:k:khāṅna]]---Satyarāja Khān; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:n:narahari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Narahari dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**The dancers in the other groups were Vakreśvara Paṇḍita, Acyutānanda, Paṇḍita Śrīvāsa, Satyarāja Khān and Narahari dāsa.**
