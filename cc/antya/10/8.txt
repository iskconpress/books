====== Cc. Antya 10.8 ======

===== Text =====

> ājñā-pālane kṛṣṇera yaiche paritoṣa
> preme ājñā bhāṅgile haya koṭi-sukha-poṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]-[[synonyms:p:pālane]]---by carrying out the order; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:p:paritoṣa]]---happiness; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgile]]---when one breaks the order; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:p:poṣa]]---millions of times more happiness.

===== Translation =====

**If one carries out Kṛṣṇa's order, Kṛṣṇa is certainly pleased, but if one sometimes breaks His order due to ecstatic love, that gives Him millions of times greater happiness.**
