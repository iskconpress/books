====== CC Antya 10.61 ======

===== Text =====

> sāta-sampradāye prabhu karena bhramaṇa
> 'mora sampradāye prabhu'---aiche sabāra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāta]]-[[synonyms:s:sampradāye]]---in the seven groups; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:b:bhramaṇa]]---wanders; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:sampradāye]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is in our group; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---everyone was thinking.

===== Translation =====

**As Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu walked from one group to another inspecting them, the men in each group thought, "The Lord is within our group."**
