====== Cc. Antya 10.26 ======

===== Text =====

> cira-sthāyī kṣīra-sāra, maṇḍādi-vikāra
> amṛta-karpūra ādi aneka prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cira]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāyī]]---long-lasting; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---cheese; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍādi]]-[[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---varieties of sweetmeats made from //maṇḍa,// or milk and cream; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---a preparation made with milk and camphor; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---many varieties.

===== Translation =====

**She made long-lasting cheese, many varieties of sweetmeats with milk and cream, and many other varied preparations, such as amṛta-karpūra.**
