====== CC Antya 10.71 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu paḍi' mūrchā yāya, śvāsa nāhi āra
> ācambite uṭhe prabhu kariyā huṅkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---falling down; [[synonyms:m:mūrchā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---became unconscious; [[synonyms:ś:śvāsa]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there was no breathing; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---stands up; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---making a loud sound.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord fell to the ground unconscious, not even breathing. Then suddenly He stood up, making a loud sound.**
