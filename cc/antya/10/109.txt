====== CC Antya 10.109 ======

===== Text =====

> keha paiḍa, keha nāḍu, keha piṭhā-pānā
> bahu-mūlya uttama-prasāda-prakāra yāra nānā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:p:paiḍa]]---a coconut preparation; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:n:nāḍu]]---sweetballs; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:p:piṭhā]]---cakes; [[synonyms:p:pānā]]---sweet rice; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---costly; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---very palatable food; [[synonyms:p:prakāra]] [[synonyms:y:yāra]] [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---of different varieties.

===== Translation =====

**Some brought paiḍa [a coconut preparation], some brought sweetballs, and some brought cakes and sweet rice. The prasāda was of different varieties, all very costly.**
