====== CC Antya 10.75 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣaṇe kṣaṇe bāḍe prabhura ānanda-āveśa
> tṛtīya prahara ha-ila, nṛtya nahe śeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---at every moment; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:p:prahara]]---midafternoon; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---the dancing; [[synonyms:n:nahe]] [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---did not end.

===== Translation =====

**His transcendental bliss increased at every moment. Therefore even by midafternoon the dancing had not ended.**
