====== CC Antya 10.90 ======

===== Text =====

> pāda-samvāhana kaila, kaṭi-pṛṣṭha cāpila
> madhūra-mardane prabhura pariśrama gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:samvāhana]]---massaging of the legs; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---he performed; [[synonyms:k:kaṭi]]---waist; [[synonyms:p:pṛṣṭha]]---back; [[synonyms:c:cāpila]]---pressed; [[synonyms:m:madhūra]]-[[synonyms:m:mardane]]---by mild pressing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pariśrama]]---fatigue; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went away.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda massaged the Lord's legs as usual. He pressed the Lord's waist and back very softly, and thus all the Lord's fatigue went away.**
