====== Cc. Antya 10.24 ======

===== Text =====

> koli-śuṇṭhi, koli-cūrṇa, koli-khaṇḍa āra
> kata nāma la-iba, śata-prakāra 'ācāra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:koli]]-[[synonyms:ś:śuṇṭhi]]---dried ginger and berries; [[synonyms:k:koli]]-[[synonyms:c:cūrṇa]]---powder of berries; [[synonyms:k:koli]]-[[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---another preparation of berries; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---how many names; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:iba]]---I shall call; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---a hundred varieties; [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---condiments and pickles.

===== Translation =====

**She made a hundred varieties of condiments and pickles. She also made koli-śuṇṭhi, koli-cūrṇa, koli-khaṇḍa and many other preparations. How many should I name?**
