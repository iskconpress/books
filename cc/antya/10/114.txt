====== Cc. Antya 10.114 ======

===== Text =====

> "ācāryādi mahāśaya kariyā yatane
> tomāre khāoyāite vastu dena mora sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---respectable gentlemen; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:y:yatane]]---with great endeavor; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]] [[synonyms:k:khāoyāite]]---to feed You; [[synonyms:v:vastu]] [[synonyms:d:dena]]---deliver varieties of food; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to me.

===== Translation =====

**"Many respectable devotees, headed by Advaita Ācārya, make a great endeavor to entrust me with varieties of food for You.**
