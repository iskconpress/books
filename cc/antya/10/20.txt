====== Cc. Antya 10.20 ======

===== Text =====

> sukutā khāile sei āma ha-ibeka nāśa
> ei sneha mane bhāvi' prabhura ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukutā]] [[synonyms:k:khāile]]---by eating the //sukutā//; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ā:āma]]---that mucus; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibeka]] [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---will be vanquished; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]---affection; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:b:bhāvi']]---thinking of; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---delight.

===== Translation =====

**Because of sincere affection, she thought that eating this sukutā would cure the Lord's disease. Considering these affectionate thoughts of Damayantī, the Lord was very pleased.**
