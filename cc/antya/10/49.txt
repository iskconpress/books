====== CC Antya 10.49 ======

===== Text =====

> saba bhakta lañā prabhu nāmilena jale
> sabā lañā jala-krīḍā karena kutūhale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāmilena]] [[synonyms:j:jale]]---got down in the water; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---activities in the water; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kutūhale]]---in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu entered the water with His devotees and began His pastimes with them in great jubilation.**
