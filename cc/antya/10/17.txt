====== CC Antya 10.17 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sukutā' bali' avajñā nā kariha citte
> sukutāya ye sukha prabhura, tāhā nahe pañcāmṛte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukutā]]---sukutā; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---because; [[synonyms:a:avajñā]]---neglect; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do not make; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:s:sukutāya]]---from //sukutā//; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:p:pañcāmṛte]]---in //pañcāmṛta.//

===== Translation =====

**Do not neglect sukutā because it is a bitter preparation. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu derived more happiness from eating this sukutā than from drinking pañcāmṛta [a preparation of milk, sugar, ghee, honey and curd].**
