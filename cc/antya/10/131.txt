====== Cc. Antya 10.131 ======

===== Text =====

> vatsareka tare āra rākhilā dhariyā
> bhojana-kāle svarūpa pariveśe khasāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vatsareka]]---one year; [[synonyms:t:tare]]---for; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---balance; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]] [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---kept in stock; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time of lunch; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pariveśe]]---administered; [[synonyms:k:khasāñā]]---taking out little by little.

===== Translation =====

**All the varieties of the remaining prasāda were kept to eat throughout the year. When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ate His lunch, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī would serve it little by little.**
