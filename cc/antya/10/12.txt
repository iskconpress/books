====== CC Antya 10.12 ======

===== Text =====

> kulīna-grāmī, khaṇḍa-vāsī mililā āsiyā
> śivānanda-sena calilā sabāre lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmī]]---the residents of Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---the residents of Khaṇḍa; [[synonyms:m:mililā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---came and joined; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them.

===== Translation =====

**The inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma and Khaṇḍa also came and joined. Śivānanda Sena took the leadership and thus started taking care of them all.**
