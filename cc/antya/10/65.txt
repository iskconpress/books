====== CC Antya 10.65 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata kata-kṣaṇa karāilā kīrtana
> āpane nācite tabe prabhura haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]] [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---caused //kīrtana// to be performed; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:n:nācite]]---to dance; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu desired.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the Lord had congregational chanting performed for some time, and then He Himself desired to dance.**
