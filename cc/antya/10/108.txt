====== CC Antya 10.108 ======

===== Text =====

> keha kona prasāda āni' deya govinda-ṭhāñi
> 'ihā yena avaśya bhakṣaṇa karena gosāñi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---some variety of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---delivers; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to Govinda; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---that; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---eats; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Each devotee would bring a certain type of prasāda. He would entrust it to Govinda and request him, "Please arrange that the Lord will surely eat this prasāda."**
