====== Cc. Antya 10.70 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bol 'bol' balena prabhu śrī-bāhu tuliyā
> hari-dhvani kare loka ānande bhāsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bol]]---chant; [[synonyms:b:bol]]---chant; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhu]]---His transcendental arms; [[synonyms:t:tuliyā]]---raising; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---chanted the holy name Hari; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]] [[synonyms:b:bhāsiyā]]---floating in transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**Raising His two arms, the Lord said, "Chant! Chant!" Floating in transcendental bliss, the people responded by chanting the holy name of Hari.**
