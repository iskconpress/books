====== CC Antya 10.55 ======

===== Text =====

> govinda-ṭhāñi rāghava jhāli samarpilā
> bhojana-gṛhera koṇe jhāli govinda rākhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in charge of Govinda; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]---the //jhāli,// the bags of eatables; [[synonyms:s:samarpilā]]---delivered; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhera]]---of the dining room; [[synonyms:k:koṇe]]---in the corner; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]---the bags; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept.

===== Translation =====

**Rāghava Paṇḍita delivered the bags of eatables to Govinda, who kept them in a corner of the dining room.**
