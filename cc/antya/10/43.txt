====== CC Antya 10.43 ======

===== Text =====

> sei-kāle mahāprabhu bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> narendre āilā dekhite jala-keli-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with His devotees; [[synonyms:n:narendre]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived at the Narendra Lake; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---the pastimes on the water; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in great jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived with His personal associates to see the jubilant pastimes of Lord Jagannātha in the Narendra-sarovara.**
