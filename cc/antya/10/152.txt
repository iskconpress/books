====== CC Antya 10.152 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-māsa ei-mata nimantraṇe yāya
> kona kona vaiṣṇava 'divasa' nāhi pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---for four months; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇe]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu keeps His invitations; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---some of the Vaiṣṇava devotees; [[synonyms:d:divasa]]---day; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---could not get.

===== Translation =====

**The four months of Cāturmāsya passed in this manner, with the Lord accepting invitations from His devotees. Because of a heavy schedule of invitations, however, some of the Vaiṣṇavas could not get an open day on which to invite the Lord.**
