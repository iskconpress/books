====== Cc. Antya 10.159 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye rāghavera jhāli-vivaraṇa
> tāra madhye pari-muṇḍā-nṛtya-kathana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the midst of that; [[synonyms:r:rāghavera]]---of Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---description of the bags of food; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---along with that; [[synonyms:p:pari]]-[[synonyms:m:muṇḍā]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:k:kathana]]---the description of dancing in the temple of Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**In the midst of that narration are descriptions of Rāghava Paṇḍita's bags of food and the dancing in the temple of Jagannātha.**
