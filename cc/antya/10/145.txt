====== Cc. Antya 10.145 ======

===== Text =====

> sena kahe,---'ye jāniluṅ, sei nāma dharila'
> eta bali' mahāprabhure nimantraṇa kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sena]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śivānanda Sena replied; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:j:jāniluṅ]]---whatever I know; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---that name; [[synonyms:d:dharila]]---he has kept; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---gave an invitation.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena replied, "He has kept the name that appeared to me from within." Then he invited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for lunch.**
