====== Cc. Antya 10.89 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe govinda bahirvāsa tāṅra upare diyā
> bhitara-ghare gelā mahāprabhure laṅghiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---outward wrapper; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---over Him; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---spreading; [[synonyms:b:bhitara]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---within the room; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]] [[synonyms:l:laṅghiyā]]---crossing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Then Govinda spread the Lord's wrapper over the Lord's body and in this way entered the room by crossing over Him.**
