====== CC Antya 10.142 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda-senera śuna nimantraṇākhyāna
> śivānandera baḍa-putrera 'caitanya-dāsa' nāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:senera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākhyāna]]---the story of the invitation; [[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]-[[synonyms:p:putrera]]---of the eldest son; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name is Caitanya dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Now hear about the invitation Śivānanda Sena extended to the Lord. His eldest son was named Caitanya dāsa.**
