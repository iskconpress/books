====== CC Antya 10.40 ======

===== Text =====

> jhālira upara 'munsiba' makaradhvaja-kara
> prāṇa-rūpe jhāli rākhe hañā tatpara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jhālira]] [[synonyms:u:upara]]---upon the bags; [[synonyms:m:munsiba]]---the superintendent; [[synonyms:m:makaradhvaja]]-[[synonyms:k:kara]]---Makaradhvaja Kara; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---like his life; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]] [[synonyms:r:rākhe]]---he keeps the bags; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:t:tatpara]]---with great attention.

===== Translation =====

**The superintendent for all those bags was Makaradhvaja Kara, who kept them with great attention like his very life.**
