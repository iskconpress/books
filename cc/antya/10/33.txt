====== CC Antya 10.33 ======

===== Text =====

> kahite nā jāni nāma e-janme yāhāra
> aiche nānā bhakṣya-dravya sahasra-prakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I cannot speak; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the names; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:j:janme]]---in this life; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---similar; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---many; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣya]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]]---eatables; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---hundreds and thousands of varieties.

===== Translation =====

**I could not mention the names of all these wonderful eatables, even in a lifetime. Damayantī made hundreds and thousands of varieties.**
