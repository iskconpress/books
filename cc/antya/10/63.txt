====== CC Antya 10.63 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā āsi' dūre dekhe nija-gaṇa lañā
> rāja-patnī saba dekhe aṭṭālī caḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---from a distant place; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by his personal staff; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:patnī]]---the queens; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---see; [[synonyms:a:aṭṭālī]] [[synonyms:c:caḍiyā]]---going up high in the palace.

===== Translation =====

**Accompanied by his personal staff, the King also came there and watched from a distance, and all the queens watched from the elevated parts of the palace.**
