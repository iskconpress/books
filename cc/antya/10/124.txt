====== CC Antya 10.124 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche sabāra nāma lañā prabhura āge dhare
> santuṣṭa hañā prabhu saba bhojana kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---everyone's name; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before the Lord; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---he places; [[synonyms:s:santuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very satisfied; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---began to eat.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Govinda gave everyone's name as he put the food before the Lord. Being very satisfied, the Lord began to eat it all.**
