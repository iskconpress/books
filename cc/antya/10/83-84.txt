====== Cc. Antya 10.83-84 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-kāla āche ei sudṛḍha 'niyama'
> 'prabhu yadi prasāda pāñā karena śayana

> govinda āsiyā kare pāda-samvāhana
> tabe yāi' prabhura 'śeṣa' karena bhojana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]]---all the time; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---hard-and-fast; [[synonyms:n:niyama]]---regulation; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---after taking His meals; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lies down; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:samvāhana]]---massaging the legs; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---the remnants of food; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eats.

===== Translation =====

**It was a steady, long-standing rule that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would lie down to rest after lunch and Govinda would come to massage His legs. Then Govinda would honor the remnants of food left by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
