====== Cc. Antya 10.57 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina mahāprabhu nija-gaṇa lañā
> jagannātha dekhilena śayyotthāne yāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by His personal devotees; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhilena]]---saw Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ś:śayyā]]-[[synonyms:u:utthāne]]---at the time of rising early from bed; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went with His personal devotees to see Lord Jagannātha when Lord Jagannātha arose early in the morning.**
