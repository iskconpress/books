====== CC Antya 10.58 ======

===== Text =====

> beḍā-saṅkīrtana tāhāṅ ārambha karilā
> sāta-sampradāya tabe gāite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:beḍā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---surrounding congregational chanting; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:ārambha]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---began; [[synonyms:s:sāta]]-[[synonyms:s:sampradāya]]---seven groups; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:g:gāite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to chant.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began His all-encompassing saṅkīrtana. He formed seven groups, which then began to chant.**

===== Purport =====

For an explanation of the //beḍā-saṅkīrtana,// one may refer to //Madhya-līlā,// Chapter Eleven, verses 215-238.
