====== CC Antya 10.107 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yadi gauḍa ha-ite bhakta-gaṇa āila
> prabhure kichu khāoyāite sabāra icchā haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---the devotees arrived; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāite]]---to feed; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---everyone desired.

===== Translation =====

**Formerly, when all the devotees had arrived from Bengal, they all desired to give Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu something to eat.**
