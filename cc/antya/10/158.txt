====== CC Antya 10.158 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ prabhura bhikṣā-nimantraṇa
> bhakta-datta vastu yaiche kailā āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]-[[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---the invitation to dine; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:d:datta]]---offered by the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---things; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---He tasted.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted invitations and how He accepted and tasted the prasāda offered by His devotees.**
