====== Cc. Antya 10.92 ======

===== Text =====

> govinde dekhiyā prabhu bale kruddha hañā
> 'āji kene eta-kṣaṇa āchis vasiyā?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinde]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing Govinda; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---said; [[synonyms:k:kruddha]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---in an angry mood; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:e:eta]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for so long; [[synonyms:ā:āchis]]---have you been; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw Govinda sitting by His side, He was somewhat angry."Why have you been sitting here for so long today?" the Lord asked.**
