====== CC Antya 10.67 ======

===== Text =====

> uḍiyā-pada mahāprabhura mane smṛti haila
> svarūpere sei pada gāite ājñā dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]---a line of a song in the language of Orissa; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:smṛti]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was remembered; [[synonyms:s:svarūpere]]---unto Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:pada]]---that special line; [[synonyms:g:gāite]]---to sing; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---ordered.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remembered a line in the Orissan language and ordered Svarūpa Dāmodara to sing it.**
