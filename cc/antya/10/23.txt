====== CC Antya 10.23 ======

===== Text =====

> śuṇṭhi-khaṇḍa nāḍu, āra āma-pitta-hara
> pṛthak pṛthak bāndhi' vastrera kuthalī bhitara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuṇṭhi]]-[[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]] [[synonyms:n:nāḍu]]---sweetmeat balls made with dried ginger; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ā:āma]]-[[synonyms:p:pitta]]-[[synonyms:h:hara]]---which removes mucus caused by too much bile; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separately; [[synonyms:b:bāndhi']]---packaging; [[synonyms:v:vastrera]]---of cloth; [[synonyms:k:kuthalī]]---small bags; [[synonyms:b:bhitara]]---within.

===== Translation =====

**She made balls of sweetmeats with dried ginger to remove mucus caused by too much bile. She put all these preparations separately into small cloth bags.**
