====== Cc. Antya 10.9-11 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsudeva-datta, murāri-gupta, gaṅgādāsa
> śrīmān-sena, śrīmān-paṇḍita, akiñcana kṛṣṇadāsa
>
> murāri, garuḍa-paṇḍita, buddhimanta-khāṅna
> sañjaya-puruṣottama, paṇḍita-bhagavān
>
> śuklāmbara, nṛsiṁhānanda āra yata jana
> sabāi calilā, nāma nā yāya likhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]-[[synonyms:d:datta]]---Vāsudeva Datta; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]-[[synonyms:g:gupta]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgādāsa]]---Gaṅgādāsa; [[synonyms:ś:śrīmān]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]]---Śrīmān Sena; [[synonyms:ś:śrīmān]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Śrīmān Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:a:akiñcana]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadāsa]]---Akiñcana Kṛṣṇadāsa; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:g:garuḍa]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Garuḍa Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:buddhimanta]]-[[synonyms:k:khāṅna]]---Buddhimanta Khān; [[synonyms:s:sañjaya]] [[synonyms:p:puruṣottama]]---Sañjaya Puruṣottama; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Bhagavān Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śuklāmbara]]---Śuklāmbara; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhānanda]]---Nṛsiṁhānanda; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as many; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---persons; [[synonyms:s:sabāi]]---all; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---names; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:l:likhana]]---it is not possible to mention.

===== Translation =====

**Vāsudeva Datta, Murāri Gupta, Gaṅgādāsa, Śrīmān Sena, Śrīmān Paṇḍita, Akiñcana Kṛṣṇadāsa, Murāri Gupta, Garuḍa Paṇḍita, Buddhimanta Khān, Sañjaya Puruṣottama, Bhagavān Paṇḍita, Śuklāmbara Brahmacārī, Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī and many others joined together to go to Jagannātha Purī. It would be impossible to mention the names of them all.**
