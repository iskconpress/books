====== CC Antya 10.53 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha dekhi' punaḥ nija-ghare āilā
> prasāda ānāñā bhakta-gaṇe khāoyāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to His residence; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:ānāñā]]---causing to be brought; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:khāoyāilā]]---fed the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His residence after visiting the temple of Jagannātha, He asked for a large quantity of Lord Jagannātha's prasāda, which He then distributed among His devotees so that they could eat sumptuously.**
