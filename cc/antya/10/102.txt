====== Cc. Antya 10.102 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahiluṅ ei pari-muṇḍā-nṛtya
> adyāpiha gāya yāhā caitanyera bhṛtya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pari]]-[[synonyms:m:muṇḍā]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing in the dancing hall of Jagannātha's temple; [[synonyms:a:adyāpiha]]---even up-to-dately; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sing about; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---which; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]---the servants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have briefly described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dancing in the hall of the Jagannātha temple. The servants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sing about this dancing even now.**
