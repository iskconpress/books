====== CC Antya 10.47 ======

===== Text =====

> jala-krīḍā, vādya, gīta, nartana, kīrtana
> mahā-kolāhala tīre, salile khelana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---the pastimes in the water; [[synonyms:v:vādya]]---musical vibration; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---singing; [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kolāhala]]---tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the bank; [[synonyms:s:salile]]---in the water; [[synonyms:k:khelana]]---sporting.

===== Translation =====

**Because of the pastimes in the water, there was great jubilation on the shore, with music, singing, chanting, dancing and tumultuous crying.**
