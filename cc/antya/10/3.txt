====== CC Antya 10.3 ======

===== Text =====

> varṣāntare saba bhakta prabhure dekhite
> parama-ānande sabe nīlācala yāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---at the next year; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Jagannātha Purī, Nīlācala.

===== Translation =====

**The next year, all the devotees were very pleased to go to Jagannātha Purī [Nīlācala] to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
