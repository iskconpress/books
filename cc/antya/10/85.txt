====== CC Antya 10.85 ======

===== Text =====

> saba dvāra yuḍi' prabhu kariyāchena śayana
> bhitare yāite nāre, govinda kare nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---the whole door; [[synonyms:y:yuḍi']]---occupying the space of; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---was lying down; [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within; [[synonyms:y:yāite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---could not go; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---he requested.

===== Translation =====

**This time when the Lord lay down, He occupied the entire doorway. Govinda could not enter the room, and therefore he made the following request.**
