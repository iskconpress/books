====== CC Antya 18.81 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhikādi gopī-gaṇa-saṅge ekatra meli'
> yamunāra jale mahā-raṅge karena keli

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]] [[synonyms:m:meli']]---meeting together; [[synonyms:y:yamunāra]]---of the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:j:jale]]---in the water; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in a great sporting attitude; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:keli]]---performs pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Kṛṣṇa was in the water of the Yamunā in the company of the gopīs, headed by Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī. They were performing pastimes in a great sporting manner.**
