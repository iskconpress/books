====== CC Antya 18.80 ======

===== Text =====

> "kālindī dekhiyā āmi gelāṅa vṛndāvana
> dekhi,---jala-krīḍā karena vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kālindī]]---River Yamunā; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:g:gelāṅa]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---pastimes in the water; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**"Seeing the River Yamunā," He said, "I went to Vṛndāvana. There I saw the son of Nanda Mahārāja performing His sporting pastimes in the water.**
