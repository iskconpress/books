====== CC Antya 18.28 ======

===== Text =====

> yamunāra bhrame prabhu dhāñā calilā
> alakṣite yāi' sindhu-jale jhāṅpa dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yamunāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhrame]]---by mistaking for the Yamunā; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---began to run very swiftly; [[synonyms:a:alakṣite]]---without being seen; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:j:jale]]---into the water of the sea; [[synonyms:j:jhāṅpa]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---He jumped.

===== Translation =====

**Mistaking the sea for the Yamunā, the Lord ran swiftly and jumped into the water, unseen by the others.**
