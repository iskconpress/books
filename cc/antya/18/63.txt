====== CC Antya 18.63 ======

===== Text =====

> eke prema, āre bhaya,---dviguṇa asthira
> bhaya-aṁśa gela,---se haila kichu dhīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eke]]---on one hand; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:ā:āre]]---on the other hand; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---doubly; [[synonyms:a:asthira]]---agitated; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---the fear part; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---disappeared; [[synonyms:s:se]]---he; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---somewhat; [[synonyms:d:dhīra]]---sober.

===== Translation =====

**The fisherman was affected by ecstatic love, but he was also fearful. He had thus become doubly agitated. Now that his fear had subsided, however, he had become somewhat normal.**
