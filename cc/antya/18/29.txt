====== Cc. Antya 18.29 ======

===== Text =====

> paḍitei haila mūrcchā, kichui nā jāne
> kabhu ḍubāya, kabhu bhāsāya taraṅgera gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paḍitei]]---falling down; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:m:mūrcchā]]---He became unconscious; [[synonyms:k:kichui]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---did not understand; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍubāya]]---cause to sink; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bhāsāya]]---float; [[synonyms:t:taraṅgera]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the waves.

===== Translation =====

**Falling into the sea, He lost consciousness and could not understand where He was. Sometimes He sank beneath the waves, and sometimes He floated above them.**
