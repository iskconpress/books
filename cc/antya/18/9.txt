====== Cc. Antya 18.9 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata rāsa-līlāya haya yata śloka
> sabāra artha kare, pāya kabhu harṣa-śoka

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---in the pastimes of the //rāsa-līlā//; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---as many verses; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---He explains the meaning; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---happiness and lamentation.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, He explained the meaning of all the verses concerning the rāsa-līlā. Sometimes He would be very sad and sometimes very happy.**
