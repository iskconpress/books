====== CC Antya 18.21 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣaṇe kṣaṇe uṭhe premāra taraṅga ananta
> jīva chāra kāhāṅ tāra pāibeka anta?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]] [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---moment after moment; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---rise; [[synonyms:p:premāra]]---of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---waves; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]---a living entity; [[synonyms:c:chāra]]---insignificant; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:p:pāibeka]]---will get; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**Endless waves arise moment after moment in that ocean of love. How could an insignificant living entity estimate their limits?**
