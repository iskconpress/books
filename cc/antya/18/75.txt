====== CC Antya 18.75 ======

===== Text =====

> kata-kṣaṇe prabhura kāṇe śabda paraśila
> huṅkāra kariyā prabhu tabahi uṭhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---after some time; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kāṇe]]---within the ear; [[synonyms:ś:śabda]]---the sound; [[synonyms:p:paraśila]]---entered; [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making a loud sound; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tabahi]]---immediately; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---got up.

===== Translation =====

**After some time, the sound of the holy name entered the ear of the Lord, who immediately got up, making a great noise.**
