====== CC Antya 18.109 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle more dhari, mahā-kolāhala kari;,
> tumi-saba ihāṅ lañā āilā
> kāṅhā yamunā, vṛndāvana, kāṅhā kṛṣṇa, gopī-gaṇa,
> sei sukha bhaṅga karāilā!"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:more]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---picking Me up; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kolāhala]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---and making a great tumult; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of you; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:k:kāṅhā]]---where; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]---the Yamunā River; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kāṅhā]]---where; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---that happiness; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅga]] [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---you have broken.

===== Translation =====

**"Suddenly, all of you created a great tumult and picked Me up and brought Me back here. Where now is the River Yamunā? Where is Vṛndāvana? Where are Kṛṣṇa and the gopīs? You have broken My happy dream!"**
