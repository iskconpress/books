====== Cc. Antya 18.5 ======

===== Text =====

> udyāne udyāne bhramena kautuka dekhite
> rāsa-līlāra gīta-śloka paḍite śunite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:udyāne]] [[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---from garden to garden; [[synonyms:b:bhramena]]---He walks; [[synonyms:k:kautuka]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---seeing the fun; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---songs and verses; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]] [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---reciting and hearing.

===== Translation =====

**He walked from garden to garden, seeing the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa and hearing and reciting songs and verses concerning the rāsa-līlā.**
