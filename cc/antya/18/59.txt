====== CC Antya 18.59 ======

===== Text =====

> othā nā yāiha, āmi niṣedhi tomāre
> tāhāṅ gele sei bhūta lāgibe sabāre"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:o:othā]]---there; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāiha]]---do not go; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhi]]---forbid; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---you; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:g:gele]]---if you go; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]---that ghost; [[synonyms:l:lāgibe]]---will catch; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---all of you.

===== Translation =====

**"Do not go near there. I forbid you. If you go, that ghost will catch you all."**
