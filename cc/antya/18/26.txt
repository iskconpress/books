====== Cc. Antya 18.26 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu bhramite bhramite
> āiṭoṭā haite samudra dekhena ācambite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhramite]] [[synonyms:b:bhramite]]---while wandering; [[synonyms:ā:āiṭoṭā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the temple of Āiṭoṭā; [[synonyms:s:samudra]]---the sea; [[synonyms:d:dekhena]]---sees; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly.

===== Translation =====

**While thus wandering near the temple of Āiṭoṭā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu suddenly saw the sea.**
