====== CC Antya 18.79 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ardha-bāhye' kahena prabhu pralāpa-vacane
> ākāśe kahena prabhu, śunena bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhye]]---in half-external consciousness; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vacane]]---crazy words; [[synonyms:ā:ākāśe]]---to the sky; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---speaks; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śunena]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**In this half-external consciousness, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu talked like a madman. The devotees could distinctly hear Him speaking to the sky.**
