====== CC Antya 18.84 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhi he, dekha kṛṣṇera jala-keli-raṅge
> kṛṣṇa matta kari-vara, cañcala kara-puṣkara,
> gopī-gaṇa kariṇīra saṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhi]] [[synonyms:h:he]]---O my dear friends; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---just see; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---of the pastimes in the water; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---the sporting mood; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:k:kari]]-[[synonyms:v:vara]]---chief elephant; [[synonyms:c:cañcala]]---restless; [[synonyms:k:kara]]-[[synonyms:p:puṣkara]]---lotus palms; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:k:kariṇīra]]---of the she-elephants; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the company.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear friends, just see Lord Kṛṣṇa's sporting pastimes in the water. Kṛṣṇa's restless palms resemble lotus flowers. He is just like a chief of mad elephants, and the gopīs who accompany Him are like she-elephants.**
