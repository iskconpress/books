====== CC Antya 18.8 ======

===== Text =====

> rāsa-līlāra eka śloka yabe paḍe, śune
> pūrvavat tabe artha karena āpane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the //rāsa-līlā//; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---explains; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**When He heard Svarūpa Dāmodara recite a verse concerning the rāsa-līlā or He Himself recited one, He would personally explain it, as He had previously done.**
