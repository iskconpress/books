====== CC Antya 18.119 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe svarūpa-gosāñi taṅre snāna karāñā
> prabhure lañā ghara āilā ānandita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---causing to bathe; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:ghara]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back to His house; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very happy.

===== Translation =====

**Thereafter, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī had Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bathe in the sea, and then he very happily brought Him back home.**
