====== CC Antya 18.64 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,---"yāṅre tumi kara 'bhūta'-jñāna
> bhūta nahe---teṅho kṛṣṇa-caitanya bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---the person whom; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---consider a ghost; [[synonyms:b:bhūta]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not a ghost; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara said to the fisherman, "My dear sir, the person whom you are thinking a ghost is not actually a ghost but the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
