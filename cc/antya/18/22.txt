====== CC Antya 18.22 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya yāhā karena āsvādana
> sabe eka jāne tāhā svarūpādi 'gaṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---fully; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees like Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**Only a person on the level of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī can fully know what Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tastes in His love for Kṛṣṇa.**
