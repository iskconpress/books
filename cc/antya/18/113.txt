====== CC Antya 18.113 ======

===== Text =====

> ei jāliyā jāle kari' tomā uṭhāila
> tomāra paraśe ei preme matta ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:j:jāliyā]]---this fisherman; [[synonyms:j:jāle]]---in the net; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---catching; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---You; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāila]]---rescued from the water; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:paraśe]]---by Your touch; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this man; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:matta]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became maddened.

===== Translation =====

**"This fisherman caught You in his net and rescued You from the water. Because of Your touch, he is now mad with ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
