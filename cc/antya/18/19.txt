====== CC Antya 18.19 ======

===== Text =====

> premāra vikāra varṇite cāhe yei jana
> cānda dharite cāhe, yena hañā 'vāmana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:premāra]]---of ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformations; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---to describe; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---which person; [[synonyms:c:cānda]] [[synonyms:d:dharite]]---to catch the moon; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---he wants; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:v:vāmana]]---a dwarf.

===== Translation =====

**One who wants to describe the transformations of ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa is like a dwarf trying to catch the moon in the sky.**
