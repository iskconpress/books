====== CC Antya 18.108 ======

===== Text =====

> keha kare vījana, keha pāda-samvāhana,
> keha karāya tāmbūla bhakṣaṇa
> rādhā-kṛṣṇa nidrā gelā, sakhī-gaṇa śayana kailā,
> dekhi' āmāra sukhī haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:v:vījana]]---fanning; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:samvāhana]]---massaging of the feet; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---made them do; [[synonyms:t:tāmbūla]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eating a preparation of betel leaves; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nidrā]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went to sleep; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---lay down; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:s:sukhī]]---happy; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"Some of the gopīs fanned Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa, others massaged Their feet, and some fed Them betel leaves to chew. When Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa fell asleep, all the other gopīs also lay down. When I saw this, My mind was very happy.**
