====== CC Antya 18.13 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra-vadane yabe kahaye 'ananta'
> eka-dinera līlāra tabu nāhi pāya anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadane]]---in thousands of mouths; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:k:kahaye]]---says; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---Lord Ananta; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dinera]]---of one day; [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of pastimes; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---does not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---reach; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**If Ananta, with His one thousand hoods, tried to describe even one day's pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, He would find them impossible to describe fully.**
