====== Cc. Antya 18.3 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate mahāprabhu nīlācale vaise
> rātri-dine kṛṣṇa-vicchedārṇave bhāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---resides; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---night and day; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viccheda]]---of separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:arṇave]]---in the ocean; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---floats.

===== Translation =====

**While thus living at Jagannātha Purī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu floated all day and night in an ocean of separation from Kṛṣṇa.**
