====== CC Antya 18.23 ======

===== Text =====

> jīva hañā kare yei tāhāra varṇana
> āpanā śodhite tāra choṅye eka 'kaṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīva]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being an ordinary living entity; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whoever; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:ā:āpanā]]---himself; [[synonyms:ś:śodhite]]---to purify; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:c:choṅye]]---touches; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:kaṇa]]---one particle.

===== Translation =====

**When an ordinary living entity describes the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he purifies himself by touching one drop of that great ocean.**
