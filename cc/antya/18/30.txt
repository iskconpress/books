====== CC Antya 18.30 ======

===== Text =====

> taraṅge vahiyā phire,---yena śuṣka kāṣṭha
> ke bujhite pāre ei caitanyera nāṭa?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:taraṅge]]---the waves; [[synonyms:v:vahiyā]] [[synonyms:p:phire]]---carry here and there; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---like; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]] [[synonyms:k:kāṣṭha]]---a piece of dry wood; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can understand; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭa]]---dramatic performance of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**The waves carried Him here and there like a piece of dry wood. Who can understand this dramatic performance by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu?**
