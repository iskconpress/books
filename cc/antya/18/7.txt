====== CC Antya 18.7 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu bhāvonmāde prabhu iti-uti dhāya
> bhūme paḍi' kabhu mūrcchā, kabhu gaḍi' yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāde]]---in the madness of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:u:uti]]---here and there; [[synonyms:d:dhāya]]---runs; [[synonyms:b:bhūme]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---falling on the ground; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:m:mūrcchā]]---sometimes unconscious; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:g:gaḍi']] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---rolls on the ground.

===== Translation =====

**He sometimes ran here and there in the madness of ecstasy and sometimes fell and rolled on the ground. Sometimes He became completely unconscious.**
