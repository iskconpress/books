====== Cc. Antya 18.107 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakṣyera paripāṭī dekhi', kṛṣṇa hailā mahā-sukhī,
> vasi' kaila vanya bhojana
> saṅge lañā sakhī-gaṇa, rādhā kailā bhojana,
> duṅhe kailā mandire śayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakṣyera]]---of eatables; [[synonyms:p:paripāṭī]]---the arrangements; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sukhī]]---very happy; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting down; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---performed; [[synonyms:v:vanya]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---a picnic in the forest; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in association; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---took the remnants; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:m:mandire]]---in the jeweled house; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down.

===== Translation =====

**"When Kṛṣṇa saw the very nice arrangement of food, He happily sat down and had a forest picnic. Then, after Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī and Her gopī friends partook of the remnants, Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa lay down together in the jeweled house.**
