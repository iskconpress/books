====== Cc. Antya 18.71 ======

===== Text =====

> bhūmite paḍi' āche prabhu dīrgha saba kāya
> jale śveta-tanu, vālu lāgiyāche gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhūmite]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---lying; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---was; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dīrgha]]---elongated; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:k:kāya]]---the whole body; [[synonyms:j:jale]]---by the water; [[synonyms:ś:śveta]]-[[synonyms:t:tanu]]---white body; [[synonyms:v:vālu]]---sand; [[synonyms:l:lāgiyāche]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---was smeared over the body.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord was lying on the ground, His body elongated and bleached white by the water. He was covered from head to foot with sand.**
