====== CC Antya 18.43 ======

===== Text =====

> viṣāde vihvala sabe, nāhika 'cetana'
> tabu preme bule kari' prabhura anveṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṣāde]]---in great moroseness; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---in everyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there was not; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---wander; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:anveṣaṇa]]---searching for.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was overwhelmed with moroseness and almost unconscious, but out of ecstatic love they continued to wander here and there, searching for the Lord.**
