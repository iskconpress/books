====== Cc. Antya 18.37 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' sabe phire prabhure cāhiyā
> samudrera tīre āilā kata jana lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:phire]]---wander; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:c:cāhiyā]]---looking for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:samudrera]] [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the seashore; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:k:kata]]---many; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---people; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by.

===== Translation =====

**Talking like this, the devotees wandered here and there looking for the Lord. Finally they came to the shore, accompanied by many others.**
