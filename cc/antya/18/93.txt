====== CC Antya 18.93 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā kṛṣṇa rādhā-sane, kailā ye āchila mane,
> gopī-gaṇa anveṣite gelā
> tabe rādhā sūkṣma-mati, jāniyā sakhīra sthiti,
> sakhī-madhye āsiyā mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---what; [[synonyms:ā:āchila]]---was; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:a:anveṣite]]---to search out; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]-[[synonyms:m:mati]]---very finely intelligent; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:s:sakhīra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---situation; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among the friends; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---mixed.

===== Translation =====

**"In the absence of the other gopīs, Lord Kṛṣṇa behaved with Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī as freely as He desired. When the gopīs began searching for Kṛṣṇa, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, being of very fine intelligence and thus knowing the situation of Her friends, immediately mingled in their midst.**
