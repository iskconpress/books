====== CC Antya 18.103 ======

===== Text =====

> uttama saṁskāra kari', baḍa baḍa thālī bhari',
> ratna-mandire piṇḍāra upare
> bhakṣaṇera krama kari', dhariyāche sāri sāri,
> āge āsana vasibāra tare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---topmost; [[synonyms:s:saṁskāra]]---cleaning; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---big; [[synonyms:t:thālī]]---plates; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling up; [[synonyms:r:ratna]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---in the house of jewels; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍāra]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the platform; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇera]] [[synonyms:k:krama]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making arrangements for eating; [[synonyms:d:dhariyāche]]---have kept; [[synonyms:s:sāri]] [[synonyms:s:sāri]]---one after another; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---sitting place; [[synonyms:v:vasibāra]] [[synonyms:t:tare]]---to sit down.

===== Translation =====

**"The gopīs peeled all the fruits and placed them together on large plates on a platform in the jeweled cottage. They arranged the fruit in orderly rows for eating, and in front of it they made a place to sit.**
