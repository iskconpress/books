====== CC Antya 18.33 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāṅ svarūpādi-gaṇa prabhu nā dekhiyā
> 'kāhāṅ gelā prabhu?' kahe camakita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---not seeing; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---has gone; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---say; [[synonyms:c:camakita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being astonished.

===== Translation =====

**Meanwhile, all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara, lost sight of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Astonished, they began searching for Him, asking, "Where has the Lord gone?"**
