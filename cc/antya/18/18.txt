====== CC Antya 18.18 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇere nācāya premā, bhaktere nācāya
> āpane nācaye,---tine nāce eka-ṭhāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇere]]---Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nācāya]]---causes to dance; [[synonyms:p:premā]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhaktere]]---the devotee; [[synonyms:n:nācāya]]---causes to dance; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:n:nācaye]]---dances; [[synonyms:t:tine]]---all three; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in one place.

===== Translation =====

**Ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa makes Kṛṣṇa and His devotees dance, and it also dances personally. In this way, all three dance together in one place.**
