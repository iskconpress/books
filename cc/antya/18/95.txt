====== CC Antya 18.95 ======

===== Text =====

> cakravāka-maṇḍala, pṛthak pṛthak yugala,
> jala haite karila udgama
> uṭhila padma-maṇḍala, pṛthak pṛthak yugala,
> cakravāke kaila ācchādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cakravāka]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---the globes of //cakravāka// birds; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separate; [[synonyms:y:yugala]]---couples; [[synonyms:j:jala]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the water; [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made; [[synonyms:u:udgama]]---appearance; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---arose; [[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇḍala]]---the circle of lotus flowers; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]]---separate; [[synonyms:y:yugala]]---couples; [[synonyms:c:cakravāke]]---the //cakravāka// birds; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:ācchādana]]---covering.

===== Translation =====

**"When the raised breasts of the gopīs, which resembled the globelike bodies of cakravāka birds, emerged from the water in separate couples, the bluish lotuses of Kṛṣṇa's hands rose to cover them.**
