====== Cc. Antya 18.73 ======

===== Text =====

> ārdra kaupīna dūra kari' śuṣka parāñā
> bahirvāse śoyāilā vālukā chāḍāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ārdra]]---wet; [[synonyms:k:kaupīna]]---underwear; [[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---removing; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]---dry; [[synonyms:p:parāñā]]---putting on; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāse]]---on a covering cloth; [[synonyms:ś:śoyāilā]]---put down; [[synonyms:v:vālukā]]---sand; [[synonyms:c:chāḍāñā]]---removing.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees removed His wet undergarment and replaced it with a dry one. Then, laying the Lord on an outer cloth, they cleaned the sand from His body.**
