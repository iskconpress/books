====== CC Antya 18.88 ======

===== Text =====

> sahasra-kare jala seke, sahasra netre gopī dekhe,
> sahasra-pade nikaṭa gamane
> sahasra-mukha-cumbane, sahasra-vapu-saṅgame,
> gopī-narma śune sahasra-kāṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousands; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---with hands; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---water; [[synonyms:s:seke]]---throw; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousands; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---with eyes; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---see; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousands; [[synonyms:p:pade]]---with legs; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near; [[synonyms:g:gamane]]---in going; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousands; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---faces; [[synonyms:c:cumbane]]---kissing; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousands; [[synonyms:v:vapu]]---bodies; [[synonyms:s:saṅgame]]---in embracing; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:n:narma]]---joking; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hear; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]---thousands; [[synonyms:k:kāṇe]]---in ears.

===== Translation =====

**"Thousands of hands splashed water, and the gopīs saw Kṛṣṇa with thousands of eyes. With thousands of legs they came near Him and kissed Him with thousands of faces. Thousands of bodies embraced Him. The gopīs heard His joking words with thousands of ears.**
