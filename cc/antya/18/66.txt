====== Cc. Antya 18.66 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra sparśe ha-ila tomāra kṛṣṇa-premodaya
> bhūta-preta-jñāne tomāra haila mahā-bhaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sparśe]]---by His touch; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---awakening of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]-[[synonyms:p:preta]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---by thinking to be a ghost; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---great fear.

===== Translation =====

**"Simply touching Him has awakened your dormant love of Kṛṣṇa, but because you thought Him a ghost, you were very much afraid of Him.**
