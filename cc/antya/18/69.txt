====== Cc. Antya 18.69 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,---"tāṅra haya premera vikāra
> asthi-sandhi chāḍe, haya ati dīrghākāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara said; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of love of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformations of the body; [[synonyms:a:asthi]]-[[synonyms:s:sandhi]]---the joints of the bones; [[synonyms:c:chāḍe]]---become separated; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very; [[synonyms:d:dīrgha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---elongated body.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara said, "The Lord's body becomes transformed in His love for God. Sometimes the joints of His bones separate, and His body becomes very elongated."**
