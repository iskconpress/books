====== CC Antya 18.34 ======

===== Text =====

> mano-vege gelā prabhu, dekhite nārilā
> prabhure nā dekhiyā saṁśaya karite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:v:vege]]---at the speed of mind; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:n:nārilā]]---no one could see; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---not seeing; [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]]---doubts; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to feel.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had run off at the speed of mind. No one could see Him. Thus everyone was puzzled as to His whereabouts.**
