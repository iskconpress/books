====== CC Antya 18.27 ======

===== Text =====

> candra-kāntye uchalita taraṅga ujjvala
> jhalamala kare,---yena 'yamunāra jala'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:k:kāntye]]---by the shining of the moon; [[synonyms:u:uchalita]]---swollen high; [[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---waves; [[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---very bright; [[synonyms:j:jhalamala]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---glitter; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:y:yamunāra]] [[synonyms:j:jala]]---the water of the River Yamunā.

===== Translation =====

**Brightened by the shining light of the moon, the high waves of the sea glittered like the waters of the River Yamunā.**
