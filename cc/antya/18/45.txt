====== CC Antya 18.45 ======

===== Text =====

> jāliyāra ceṣṭā dekhi' sabāra camatkāra
> svarūpa-gosāñi tāre puchena samācāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jāliyāra]]---of the fisherman; [[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---activity; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:p:puchena]]---inquires; [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---news.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the activities of the fisherman, everyone was astonished. Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, therefore, asked him for information.**
