====== Cc. Antya 18.38 ======

===== Text =====

> cāhiye beḍāite aiche rātri-śeṣa haila
> 'antardhāna ha-ilā prabhu',---niścaya karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāhiye]]---looking; [[synonyms:b:beḍāite]]---wandering; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in this way; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---the night ended; [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---has disappeared; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---they decided.

===== Translation =====

**While they were searching for the Lord, the night ended, and thus they all decided, "Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has now disappeared."**
