====== CC Antya 18.12 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yei dekhāñāchi dig-daraśana
> taiche jāniha 'vikāra' 'pralāpa' varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---as; [[synonyms:d:dekhāñāchi]]---I have shown; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---only an indication; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:j:jāniha]]---you may know; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformations; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---crazy talks; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description.

===== Translation =====

**As I have previously indicated, I am describing the mad speeches and bodily transformations of the Lord only in brief.**
