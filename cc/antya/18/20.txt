====== CC Antya 18.20 ======

===== Text =====

> vāyu yaiche sindhu-jalera hare eka 'kaṇa'
> kṛṣṇa-prema-kaṇa taiche jīvera sparśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāyu]]---the wind; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:j:jalera]]---of the water of the ocean; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---takes away; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:kaṇa]]---one particle; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:k:kaṇa]]---one particle of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:j:jīvera]] [[synonyms:s:sparśana]]---a living entity can touch.

===== Translation =====

**As the wind can carry away but a drop of the water in the ocean, a living entity can touch only a particle of the ocean of love of Kṛṣṇa.**
