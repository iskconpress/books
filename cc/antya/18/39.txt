====== Cc. Antya 18.39 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura vicchede kāra dehe nāhi prāṇa
> aniṣṭā-śaṅkā vinā kāra mane nāhi āna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---from the Lord; [[synonyms:v:vicchede]]---due to separation; [[synonyms:k:kāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the body; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---there was practically no life; [[synonyms:a:aniṣṭā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkā]]---doubts of some mishap; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---besides; [[synonyms:k:kāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---there is nothing else.

===== Translation =====

**In separation from the Lord, everyone felt as though he had lost his very life. They concluded that there must have been some mishap. They could not think of anything else.**
