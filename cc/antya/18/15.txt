====== CC Antya 18.15 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaktera prema-vikāra dekhi' kṛṣṇera camatkāra!
> kṛṣṇa yāra nā pāya anta, kebā chāra āra?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of a devotee; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformations of ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---wonder; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---cannot get; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---the limit; [[synonyms:k:kebā]]---who; [[synonyms:c:chāra]]---insignificant; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---others.

===== Translation =====

**Even Lord Kṛṣṇa is struck with wonder at seeing the transformations of ecstasy in His devotees. If Kṛṣṇa Himself cannot estimate the limits of such emotions, how could others?**
