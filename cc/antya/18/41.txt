====== CC Antya 18.41 ======

===== Text =====

> samudrera tīre āsi' yukati karilā
> cirāyu-parvata-dike kata-jana gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:samudrera]] [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the seashore; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:y:yukati]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---they consulted among themselves; [[synonyms:c:cirāyu]]-[[synonyms:p:parvata]]---of Caṭaka-parvata; [[synonyms:d:dike]]---in the direction; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---some of them; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**When they arrived at the seashore, they conferred among themselves. Then some of them sought out Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Caṭaka-parvata.**
