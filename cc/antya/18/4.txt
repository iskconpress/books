====== Cc. Antya 18.4 ======

===== Text =====

> śarat-kālera rātri, saba candrikā-ujjvala
> prabhu nija-gaṇa lañā beḍāna rātri-sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śarat]]-[[synonyms:k:kālera]]---of autumn; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]---night; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:c:candrikā]]-[[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]---brightened by the moonlight; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---His own associates; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:beḍāna]]---walks; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---the whole night.

===== Translation =====

**During a night of the autumn season when a full moon brightened everything, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wandered all night long with His devotees.**
