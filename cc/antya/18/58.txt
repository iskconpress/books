====== Cc. Antya 18.58 ======

===== Text =====

> ei bhūta nṛsiṁha-nāme cāpaye dviguṇe
> tāhāra ākāra dekhite bhaya lāge mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:b:bhūta]]---this ghost; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]-[[synonyms:n:nāme]]---by the holy name of Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:c:cāpaye]]---comes over me; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---with doubled strength; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---form; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear; [[synonyms:l:lāge]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---arises in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**"This ghost, however, overcomes me with redoubled strength when I chant the Nṛsiṁha mantra. When I even see the form of this ghost, great fear arises in my mind.**
