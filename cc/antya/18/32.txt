====== Cc. Antya 18.32 ======

===== Text =====

> yamunāte jala-keli gopī-gaṇa-saṅge
> kṛṣṇa karena---mahāprabhu magna sei raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yamunāte]]---in the River Yamunā; [[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---pastimes in the water; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---Kṛṣṇa performs; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:magna]]---fully merged; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in those pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Kṛṣṇa performed pastimes with the gopīs in the waters of the Yamunā, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fully merged in those pastimes.**
