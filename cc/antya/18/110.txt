====== CC Antya 18.110 ======

===== Text =====

> eteka kahite prabhura kevala 'bāhya' haila
> svarūpa-gosāñire dekhi' tāṅhāre puchila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eteka]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---while speaking; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kevala]]---only; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---Svarūpa Gosāñi; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]] [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---He asked him.

===== Translation =====

**Speaking in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fully returned to external consciousness. Seeing Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, the Lord questioned him.**
