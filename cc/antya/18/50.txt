====== Cc. Antya 18.50 ======

===== Text =====

> bhaye kampa haila, mora netre vahe jala
> gadgada vāṇī, roma uṭhila sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---out of fear; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---shivering; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---in the eyes; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---flow; [[synonyms:j:jala]]---tears; [[synonyms:g:gadgada]]---faltering; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---voice; [[synonyms:r:roma]]---body hair; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---stood up; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**"I shivered in fear and shed tears. My voice faltered, and all the hairs on my body stood up.**
