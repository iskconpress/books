====== CC Antya 18.115 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi mūrcchā-chale vṛndāvane dekha krīḍā
> tomāra mūrcchā dekhi' sabe mane pāi pīḍā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchā]]-[[synonyms:c:chale]]---pretending to be unconscious; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---see; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---the pastimes; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:m:mūrcchā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Your unconsciousness; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of us; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---get; [[synonyms:p:pīḍā]]---agony.

===== Translation =====

**"While apparently unconscious, You witnessed the pastimes in Vṛndāvana, but when we saw You unconscious, we suffered great agony in our minds.**
