====== CC Antya 12.105 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu-ṭhāñi govinda kaila nivedana
> 'jagadānanda candanādi-taila āniyāchena

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---before Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:candana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:t:taila]]---scented sandalwood oil; [[synonyms:ā:āniyāchena]]---has brought.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda therefore told Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Jagadānanda Paṇḍita has brought some scented sandalwood oil.**
