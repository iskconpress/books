====== CC Antya 12.149 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane prabhura 'śeṣa' karilā bhojana
> tabe govindere prabhu pāṭhāilā punaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---ate; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:g:govindere]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again.

===== Translation =====

**He also personally ate the remnants of food left by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Then the Lord again sent Govinda.**
