====== CC Antya 12.6 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-dina ei daśā svasti nāhi mane
> kaṣṭe rātri goṅāya svarūpa-rāmānanda-sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---day and night; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---this situation; [[synonyms:s:svasti]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---no peace of mind; [[synonyms:k:kaṣṭe]]---with great difficulty; [[synonyms:r:rātri]] [[synonyms:g:goṅāya]]---passes the night; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---in the company of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**This was His situation day and night. Unable to find peace of mind, He passed His nights with great difficulty in the company of Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya.**
