====== CC Antya 12.41 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānande lāthi mārilā,---ihā nā kahilā
> ethā saba vaiṣṇava-gaṇa āsiyā mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānande]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:l:lāthi]] [[synonyms:m:mārilā]]---(Lord Nityānanda) has kicked; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---he did not say; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**As he described the Vaiṣṇavas, therefore, he did not mention Lord Nitya-nanda's kicking Śivānanda Sena. Meanwhile, all the devotees arrived and went to meet the Lord.**
