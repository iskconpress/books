====== CC Antya 12.95 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata jagadānanda śacīmātā-sane
> caitanyera sukha-kathā kahe rātri-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śacīmātā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with mother Śacī; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---words of happiness; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita and mother Śacī talked day and night about the happiness of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
