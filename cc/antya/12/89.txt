====== CC Antya 12.89 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānande pāñā mātā ānandita mane
> teṅho prabhura kathā kahe, śune rātri-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānande]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in great satisfaction; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---listens; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda's coming pleased mother Śacī very much. As he talked of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, she listened day and night.**
