====== Cc. Antya 12.56 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-viṣaye sneha tāra bālaka-kāla haite
> se vatsara seha āila prabhure dekhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣaye]]---in regard to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sneha]]---affection; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of Parameśvara Modaka; [[synonyms:b:bālaka]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---since He was a boy; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---that year; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---he also; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Parameśvara Modaka had been affectionate toward the Lord since His childhood, and he was one of those who came that year to see the Lord at Jagannātha Purī.**
