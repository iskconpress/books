====== CC Antya 12.35 ======

===== Text =====

> "caitanyera pāriṣada mora mātulera khyāti
> 'ṭhākurālī' karena gosāñi, tāṅre māre lāthi"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]] [[synonyms:p:pāriṣada]]---associate of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:m:mātulera]]---of the maternal uncle; [[synonyms:k:khyāti]]---reputation; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurālī]]---superiority; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---exhibits; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:m:māre]] [[synonyms:l:lāthi]]---kicks.

===== Translation =====

**"My uncle is well known as one of the associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, but Lord Nityānanda Prabhu asserts His superiority by kicking him."**
