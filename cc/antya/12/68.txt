====== Cc. Antya 12.68 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā-sabāra duḥkha jāni' cāhi niṣedhite
> tomā-sabāra saṅga-sukhe lobha bāḍe citte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---trouble; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---understanding; [[synonyms:c:cāhi]] [[synonyms:n:niṣedhite]]---I want to stop this; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---for the happiness of association; [[synonyms:l:lobha]]---desire; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in My mind.

===== Translation =====

**"I would like to forbid you to do this, but I enjoy your company so much that My desire for your association only increases.**
