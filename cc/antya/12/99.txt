====== CC Antya 12.99 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyera marma-kathā śune tāṅra mukhe
> āpanā pāsare sabe caitanya-kathā-sukhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:marma]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---confidential talks; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---they hear; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---through his mouth; [[synonyms:ā:āpanā]] [[synonyms:p:pāsare]]---forget themselves; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in the happiness of talks of Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**They heard confidential narrations about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu from the mouth of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita and forgot themselves in the great happiness of hearing about the Lord.**
