====== CC Antya 12.144 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena,---"govinda, tumi ihāṅi rahibā
> paṇḍita bhojana kaile, āmāre kahibā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅi]] [[synonyms:r:rahibā]]---will stay here; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---after he has taken his meal; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]] [[synonyms:k:kahibā]]---you should inform Me.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then told Govinda, "You remain here. When the Paṇḍita has taken his food, come inform Me.**
