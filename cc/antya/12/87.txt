====== Cc. Antya 12.87 ======

===== Text =====

> āira caraṇa yāi' karilā vandana
> jagannāthera vastra-prasāda kailā nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āira]]---of Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---to the lotus feet; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---offered prayers; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---cloth and //prasāda//; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---offered.

===== Translation =====

**When he arrived, he offered prayers at her lotus feet and then offered her the cloth and prasāda of Lord Jagannātha.**
