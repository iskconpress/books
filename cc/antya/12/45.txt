====== CC Antya 12.45 ======

===== Text =====

> choṭa-putre dekhi' prabhu nāma puchilā
> 'paramānanda-dāsa'-nāma sena jānāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:putre]]---the youngest son; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired about his name; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Paramānanda dāsa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:s:sena]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:j:jānāilā]]---informed.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya asked the youngest son's name, and Śivānanda Sena informed the Lord that his name was Paramānanda dāsa.**
