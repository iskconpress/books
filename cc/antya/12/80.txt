====== CC Antya 12.80 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu sabākāre prabodha kariyā
> sabāre vidāya dilā susthira hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:prabodha]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---pacifying; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---to every one of them; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:sthira]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being in a peaceful condition.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu peacefully calmed them all and bade each of them farewell.**
