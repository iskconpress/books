====== CC Antya 12.43 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsā-ghara pūrvavat sabāre deoyāilā
> mahāprasāda-bhojane sabāre bolāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:g:ghara]]---residential quarters; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:d:deoyāilā]]---caused to be given; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of food from Jagannātha; [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---to eat; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:b:bolāilā]]---He called.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord again arranged for the residential quarters of all the devotees and thereafter called them to partake of the remnants of food offered to Lord Jagannātha.**
