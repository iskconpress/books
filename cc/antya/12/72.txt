====== CC Antya 12.72 ======

===== Text =====

> āmi ei nīlācale rahi ye vasiyā
> pariśrama nāhi mora tomā sabāra lāgiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:rahi]]---remain; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting; [[synonyms:p:pariśrama]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:mora]]---I have no fatigue; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---due to all of you.

===== Translation =====

**"There is no fatigue or trouble for Me, for I stay here at Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī, and do not move at all. This is the favor of all of you.**
