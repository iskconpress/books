====== Cc. Antya 12.58 ======

===== Text =====

> 'parameśvara kuśala hao, bhāla haila, āilā'
> 'mukundāra mātā āsiyāche, seha prabhure kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parameśvara]]---O Parameśvara; [[synonyms:k:kuśala]] [[synonyms:h:hao]]---be blessed; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it is very good; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---you have come; [[synonyms:m:mukundāra]] [[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the mother of Mukunda; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyāche]]---has come; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---he; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---informed the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Parameśvara, may you be blessed. It is very good that you have come here." Parameśvara then informed the Lord, "Mukundāra Mātā has also come."**
