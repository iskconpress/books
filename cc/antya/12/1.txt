====== CC Antya 12.1 ======

===== Text =====

> śrūyatāṁ śrūyatāṁ nityaṁ
> gīyatāṁ gīyatāṁ mudā
> cintyatāṁ cintyatāṁ bhaktāś
> caitanya-caritāmṛtam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrūyatām]]---let it be heard; [[synonyms:ś:śrūyatām]]---let it be heard; [[synonyms:n:nityam]]---always; [[synonyms:g:gīyatām]]---let it be chanted; [[synonyms:g:gīyatām]]---let it be chanted; [[synonyms:m:mudā]]---with great happiness; [[synonyms:c:cintyatām]]---let it be meditated upon; [[synonyms:c:cintyatām]]---let it be meditated upon; [[synonyms:b:bhaktāḥ]]---O devotees; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritāmṛtam]]---the transcendental life and characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**O devotees, may the transcendental life and characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu be always heard, chanted and meditated upon with great happiness.**
