====== CC Antya 12.49 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-ājñāya dharilā nāma---'paramānanda-dāsa'
> 'purī-dāsa' kari' prabhu karena upahāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---under the order of Śrī-Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---held the name; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Paramānanda dāsa; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Purī dāsa; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---as; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:u:upahāsa]]---began to joke.

===== Translation =====

**The child was named Paramānanda dāsa in accordance with the Lord's order, and the Lord jokingly called him Purī dāsa.**
