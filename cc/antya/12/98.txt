====== Cc. Antya 12.98 ======

===== Text =====

> vāsudeva, murāri-gupta jagadānande pāñā
> ānande rākhilā ghare, nā dena chāḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]-[[synonyms:g:gupta]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:j:jagadānande]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dena]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---did not allow to go out.

===== Translation =====

**Vāsudeva Datta and Murāri Gupta were so pleased to see Jagadānanda Paṇḍita that they kept him at their homes and would not allow him to leave.**
