====== CC Antya 12.50 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda yabe sei bālake milāilā
> mahāprabhu pādāṅguṣṭha tāra mukhe dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bālake]]---child; [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅguṣṭha]]---His toe; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---within the mouth; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---pushed.

===== Translation =====

**When Śivānanda Sena introduced the child to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord put His toe in the child's mouth.**

===== Purport =====

In this connection one may refer to //Antya-līlā,// Chapter Sixteen, verses 65-75, for information about the later manifestations of the Lord's mercy.
