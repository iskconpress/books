====== CC Antya 12.16 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāra saba kārya karena, dena vāsa-sthāna
> śivānanda jāne uḍiyā-pathera sandhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---business; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---place of residence; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]-[[synonyms:p:pathera]]---of the path going to Orissa; [[synonyms:s:sandhāna]]---junctions.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena took care of everyone and gave each devotee places to stay. He knew all the paths leading to Orissa.**
