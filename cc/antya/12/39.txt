====== Cc. Antya 12.39 ======

===== Text =====

> vaiṣṇavera samācāra gosāñi puchilā
> eke eke sabāra nāma śrīkānta jānāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of all the //Vaiṣṇavas//; [[synonyms:s:samācāra]]---news; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired; [[synonyms:e:eke]] [[synonyms:e:eke]]---one after another; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---names; [[synonyms:ś:śrīkānta]]---the nephew of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:j:jānāilā]]---informed.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired from Śrīkānta about all the Vaiṣṇavas, and the boy informed the Lord about them, naming them one after another.**
