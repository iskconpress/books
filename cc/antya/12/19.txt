====== CC Antya 12.19 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-prabhu bhokhe vyākula hañā
> śivānanda gāli pāḍe vāsā nā pāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhokhe]]---became very hungry; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being disturbed; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:g:gāli]] [[synonyms:p:pāḍe]]---was calling Śivānanda ill names; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---not getting residential quarters.

===== Translation =====

**Nityānanda Prabhu meanwhile became very hungry and upset. Because He had not yet obtained a suitable residence, He began calling Śivānanda Sena ill names.**
