====== Cc. Antya 12.61 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat sabā lañā guṇḍicā-mārjana
> ratha-āge pūrvavat karilā nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]]-[[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---the cleaning of the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of the chariot; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---danced.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees engaged in the cleansing ceremony of the Guṇḍicā temple and danced in front of the Ratha-yātrā chariot, just as they had done in the past.**
