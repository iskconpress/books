====== CC Antya 12.34 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānandera bhāginā,---śrīkānta-sena nāma
> māmāra agocare kahe kari' abhimāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:b:bhāginā]]---the sister's son; [[synonyms:ś:śrīkānta]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named Śrīkānta Sena; [[synonyms:m:māmāra]]---of his maternal uncle; [[synonyms:a:agocare]]---in the absence of; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---with an offended state of mind.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena's nephew, Śrīkānta, the son of his sister, felt offended, and he commented on the matter when his uncle was absent.**
