====== CC Antya 12.26 ======

===== Text =====

> caraṇe dhariyā prabhure vāsāya lañā gelā
> vāsā diyā hṛṣṭa hañā kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---the feet; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---catching; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāsāya]]---to His residence; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---after giving His residential quarters; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very pleased; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena touched the lotus feet of Nityānanda Prabhu and led Him to His residence. After giving the Lord His quarters, Śivānanda Sena, being very pleased, spoke as follows.**
