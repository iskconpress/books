====== Cc. Antya 12.84 ======

===== Text =====

> yāre yaiche nācāya prabhu svatantra īśvara
> tāte tāṅre chāḍi' loka yāya deśāntara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāre]]---whomever; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:n:nācāya]]---causes to dance; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the fully independent Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---go; [[synonyms:d:deśa]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]]---to different parts of the country.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the fully independent Personality of Godhead and makes everyone dance as He likes. Leaving His company, therefore, all the devotees returned to their homes in different parts of the country.**
