====== CC Antya 12.140 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu uṭhi' kailā ācamana
> paṇḍita ānila, mukhavāsa, mālya, candana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---performed washing of the hands and mouth; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---spices; [[synonyms:m:mālya]]---flower garland; [[synonyms:c:candana]]---sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood up and washed His hands and mouth, while Jagadānanda Paṇḍita brought spices, a garland, and sandalwood pulp.**
