====== Cc. Antya 12.44 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda tina-putre gosāñire milāilā
> śivānanda-sambandhe sabāya bahu-kṛpā kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:p:putre]]---three sons; [[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sambandhe]]---because they were sons of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---showed much mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena introduced his three sons to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Because they were his sons, the Lord showed the boys great mercy.**
