====== CC Antya 12.100 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda milite yāya yei bhakta-ghare
> sei sei bhakta sukhe āpanā pāsare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---which; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to a devotee's house; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:ā:āpanā]] [[synonyms:p:pāsare]]---forgets himself.

===== Translation =====

**Whenever Jagadānanda Paṇḍita went to visit a devotee's house, that devotee immediately forgot himself in great happiness.**
