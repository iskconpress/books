====== CC Antya 12.20 ======

===== Text =====

> 'tina putra maruka śivāra, ekhana nā āila
> bhokhe mari' genu, more vāsā nā deoyāila'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---three sons; [[synonyms:m:maruka]]---let them die; [[synonyms:ś:śivāra]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:e:ekhana]]---here; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---he does not come; [[synonyms:b:bhokhe]] [[synonyms:m:mari']] [[synonyms:g:genu]]---I am dying from hunger; [[synonyms:m:more]]---for Me; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---residential place; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:deoyāila]]---he did not arrange.

===== Translation =====

**"Śivānanda Sena has not arranged for My residence," He complained, "and I am so hungry I could die. Because he has not come, I curse his three sons to die."**
