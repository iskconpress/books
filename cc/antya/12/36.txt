====== CC Antya 12.36 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' śrīkānta, bālaka āge cali' yāna
> saṅga chāḍi' āge gelā mahāprabhura sthāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:ś:śrīkānta]]---the nephew of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:b:bālaka]]---a boy; [[synonyms:ā:āge]] [[synonyms:c:cali']] [[synonyms:y:yāna]]---went forward; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up their association; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---forward; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrīkānta, who was only a boy, left the group and traveled on alone to the residence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
