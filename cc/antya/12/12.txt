====== CC Antya 12.12 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda-patnī cale tina-putra lañā
> rāghava-paṇḍita cale jhāli sājāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:patnī]]---the wife of Śivānanda; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---was going; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by her three sons; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:c:cale]]---Rāghava Paṇḍita was going; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]] [[synonyms:s:sājāñā]]---carrying his bags.

===== Translation =====

**The wife of Śivānanda Sena also came, along with their three sons. Rāghava Paṇḍita joined them, carrying his famous bags of food.**
