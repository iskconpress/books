====== CC Antya 12.138 ======

===== Text =====

> kichu balite nārena prabhu, khāyena tarāse
> nā khāile jagadānanda karibe upavāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:b:balite]] [[synonyms:n:nārena]]---could not say; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:khāyena]]---eats; [[synonyms:t:tarāse]]---out of fear; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:khāile]]---if He did not eat; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:karibe]] [[synonyms:u:upavāse]]---would fast.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dared not forbid him to feed Him more. He just continued eating, fearful that Jagadānanda would fast if He stopped.**
