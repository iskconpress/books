====== CC Antya 12.51 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānandera bhāgya-sindhu ke pāibe pāra?
> yāṅra saba goṣṭhīke prabhu kahe 'āpanāra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of fortune; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:p:pāibe]] [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---can cross over; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:g:goṣṭhīke]]---whole family; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]]---His own.

===== Translation =====

**No one can cross over the ocean of Śivānanda Sena's good fortune, for the Lord considered Śivānanda's whole family His own.**
