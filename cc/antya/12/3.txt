====== CC Antya 12.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jayādvaita-candra jaya karuṇā-sāgara
> jaya gaura-bhakta-gaṇa kṛpā-pūrṇāntara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:k:karuṇā]]-[[synonyms:s:sāgara]]---the ocean of mercy; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]]---whose hearts are always filled with mercy.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Advaita Ācārya, who is also an ocean of mercy! All glories to all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, whose hearts are always filled with mercy!**
