====== Cc. Antya 12.148 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmāi, nandāi āra govinda, raghunātha
> sabāre bāṅṭiyā dilā prabhura vyañjana-bhāta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmāi]]---Rāmāi Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nandāi]]---Nandāi; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---for all of them; [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭiyā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---distributed; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāta]]---vegetables and rice.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita thus distributed remnants of the Lord's food to Rāmāi, Nandāi, Govinda and Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa.**
