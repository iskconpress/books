====== CC Antya 12.64 ======

===== Text =====

> dine nānā krīḍā kare lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> rātrye kṛṣṇa-vicchede prabhu karena rodana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dine]]---during the day; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performed pastimes; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---with His devotees; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vicchede]]---because of separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---cries.

===== Translation =====

**During the day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu engaged in various activities with His devotees, but at night He felt great separation from Kṛṣṇa and used to cry.**
