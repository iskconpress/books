====== CC Antya 12.65 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata nānā-līlāya cāturmāsya gela
> gauḍa-deśe yāite tabe bhakte ājñā dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---in various pastimes; [[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---the four months of the rainy season passed; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to return to Bengal; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:b:bhakte]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the Lord spent the four months of the rainy season in various pastimes, and then He ordered the Bengali devotees to return to their homes.**
