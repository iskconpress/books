{{description>There was a resident of Nadia named Parameśvara, who was a confectioner living near the home of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.}}

====== Cc. Antya 12.54 ======

===== Text =====

> nadīyā-vāsī modaka, tāra nāma---'parameśvara'
> modaka vece, prabhura vāṭīra nikaṭa tāra ghara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nadīyā]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---inhabitant of the district of Nadia; [[synonyms:m:modaka]]---one confectioner; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---his name; [[synonyms:p:parameśvara]]---Parameśvara; [[synonyms:m:modaka]] [[synonyms:v:vece]]---does the business of a confectioner; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāṭīra]] [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near the house; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---his house.

===== Translation =====

**There was a resident of Nadia named Parameśvara, who was a confectioner living near the home of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
