====== Cc. Antya 12.69 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānande ājñā diluṅ gauḍete rahite
> ājñā laṅghi' āilā, ki pāri balite?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānande]]---unto Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:diluṅ]]---I ordered; [[synonyms:g:gauḍete]] [[synonyms:r:rahite]]---to stay in Bengal; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:laṅghi']]---transgressing My order; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---He has come; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:p:pāri]] [[synonyms:b:balite]]---can I say.

===== Translation =====

**"I ordered Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu not to leave Bengal, but He has transgressed My order and come to see Me. What can I say?**
