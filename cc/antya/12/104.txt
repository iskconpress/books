====== CC Antya 12.104 ======

===== Text =====

> govindera ṭhāñi taila dhariyā rākhilā
> "prabhu-aṅge diha' taila" govinde kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govindera]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in the care of Govinda; [[synonyms:t:taila]]---the oil; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]] [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---was kept; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---over the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:diha']]---put; [[synonyms:t:taila]]---oil; [[synonyms:g:govinde]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---he advised Govinda.

===== Translation =====

**This oil was placed in the care of Govinda, and Jagadānanda requested him, "Please rub this oil on the body of the Lord."**
