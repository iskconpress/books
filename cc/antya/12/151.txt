====== Cc. Antya 12.151 ======

===== Text =====

> govinda āsi' dekhi' kahila paṇḍitera bhojana
> tabe mahāprabhu svastye karila śayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---informed; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---the eating of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svastye]]---in peace; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---went to sleep.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing that Jagadānanda Paṇḍita was indeed eating, Govinda informed the Lord, who then became peaceful and went to sleep.**
