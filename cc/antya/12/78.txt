====== CC Antya 12.78 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita avadhūta kichu kahe prabhu-pāya
> "sahaje tomāra guṇe jagat vikāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:a:avadhūta]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sahaje]]---naturally; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---because of transcendental attributes; [[synonyms:j:jagat]] [[synonyms:v:vikāya]]---the whole world is obligated to You.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Prabhu and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu submitted these words at the lotus feet of the Lord: "The entire world is naturally obligated to You for Your transcendental attributes.**
