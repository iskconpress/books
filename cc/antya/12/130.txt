====== CC Antya 12.130 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu sukhe bhojane vasilā
> vyañjanera svāda pāñā kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:b:bhojane]] [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat to take His food; [[synonyms:v:vyañjanera]] [[synonyms:s:svāda]]---the taste of the vegetables; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to say.

===== Translation =====

**In great happiness, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then accepted the lunch. When He had tasted the vegetables, He again began to speak.**
