====== Cc. Antya 12.97 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya milite tabe gelā jagadānanda
> jagadānande pāñā haila ācārya ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:j:jagadānande]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---very happy.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita thereafter went to meet Advaita Ācārya, who also was very happy to have him.**
