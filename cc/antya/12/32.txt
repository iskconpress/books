====== Cc. Antya 12.32 ======

===== Text =====

> ānandita śivānanda kare samādhāna
> ācāryādi-vaiṣṇavere dilā vāsā-sthāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---pleased; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---began to arrange things; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavere]]---unto all the Vaiṣṇavas, headed by Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---residential places.

===== Translation =====

**Being very pleased by Nityānanda Prabhu's behavior, Śivānanda Sena began to arrange residential quarters for all the Vaiṣṇavas, headed by Advaita Ācārya.**
