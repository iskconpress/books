====== Cc. Antya 12.13 ======

===== Text =====

> datta, gupta, vidyānidhi, āra yata jana
> dui-tina śata bhakta karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:datta]]---Vāsudeva Datta; [[synonyms:g:gupta]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:v:vidyānidhi]]---Vidyānidhi; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---all persons; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---two hundred to three hundred; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**Vāsudeva Datta, Murāri Gupta, Vidyānidhi and many other devotees went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. All together, they numbered two or three hundred.**
