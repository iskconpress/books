====== CC Antya 12.111 ======

===== Text =====

> dina daśa gele govinda jānāila āra-bāra
> paṇḍitera icchā,---'taila prabhu kare aṅgīkāra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:d:daśa]] [[synonyms:g:gele]]---when ten days passed; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:j:jānāila]]---informed; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---the desire of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:taila]]---oil; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---accepts.

===== Translation =====

**When ten days had passed, Govinda again told Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "It is the desire of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita that Your Lordship accept the oil."**
