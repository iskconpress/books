====== CC Antya 12.88 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura nāme mātāre daṇḍavat kailā
> prabhura vinati-stuti mātāre kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in the name of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mātāre]]---to His mother; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---he offered obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vinati]]-[[synonyms:s:stuti]]---very submissive prayers; [[synonyms:m:mātāre]]---to His mother; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---he informed.

===== Translation =====

**He offered obeisances to Śacīmātā in the name of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and informed her of all the Lord's submissive prayers to her.**
