====== Cc. Antya 12.14 ======

===== Text =====

> śacīmātā dekhi' sabe tāṅra ājñā lañā
> ānande calilā kṛṣṇa-kīrtana kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:m:mātā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking her permission; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---with great jubilation; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---they proceeded; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---performing congregational chanting.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees first saw Śacīmātā and took her permission. Then, in great happiness, they started for Jagannātha Purī, congregationally chanting the holy name of the Lord.**
