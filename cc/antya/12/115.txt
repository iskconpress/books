====== Cc. Antya 12.115 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni prabhura vākya govinda mauna karilā
> prātaḥ-kāle jagadānanda prabhu-sthāne āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---the statement of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---remained silent; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing these words of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Govinda remained silent. The next morning, Jagadānanda went to see the Lord.**
