====== CC Antya 12.38 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"śrīkānta āsiyāche pāñā mano-duḥkha
> kichu nā baliha, karuka, yāte ihāra sukha"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:ś:śrīkānta]]---//Śrīkānta//; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyāche]]---has come; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---distress in the mind; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:baliha]]---do not say; [[synonyms:k:karuka]]---let him do; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness.

===== Translation =====

**As Govinda was warning Śrīkānta, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Don't bother him. Let Śrīkānta do whatever he likes, for he has come here in a distressed state of mind."**
