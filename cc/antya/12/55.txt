====== CC Antya 12.55 ======

===== Text =====

> bālaka-kāle prabhu tāra ghare bāra bāra yā'na
> dugdha, khaṇḍa modaka deya, prabhu tāhā khā'na

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bālaka]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---when He was a boy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at his house; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:y:yā'na]]---used to go; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---milk; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]---sweetmeats; [[synonyms:m:modaka]] [[synonyms:d:deya]]---the confectioner used to give; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:khā'na]]---used to eat.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord was a boy, He would visit the house of Parameśvara Modaka again and again. The confectioner would supply the Lord milk and sweetmeats, and the Lord would eat them.**
