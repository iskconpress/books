====== CC Antya 12.124 ======

===== Text =====

> madhyāhna kariyā prabhu āilā bhojane
> pāda prakṣālana kari' dilena āsane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---after finishing his noon ritualistic ceremonies; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:b:bhojane]]---to take lunch; [[synonyms:p:pāda]] [[synonyms:p:prakṣālana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after washing His feet; [[synonyms:d:dilena]] [[synonyms:ā:āsane]]---offered a sitting place.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing His noontime ritualistic duties, the Lord arrived for lunch. Jagadānanda Paṇḍita washed the Lord's feet and gave the Lord a sitting place.**
