====== Cc. Antya 12.152 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānande-prabhute prema cale ei-mate
> satyabhāmā-kṛṣṇe yaiche śuni bhāgavate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānande]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhute]]---between Jagadānanda Paṇḍita and the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---affection; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes on; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:satyabhāmā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---between Satyabhāmā and Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---we learn; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in the //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.//

===== Translation =====

**The affectionate loving exchanges between Jagadānanda Paṇḍita and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued in this manner, exactly like the exchanges between Satyabhāmā and Lord Kṛṣṇa related in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.**
