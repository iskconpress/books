====== Cc. Antya 12.71 ======

===== Text =====

> mora lāgi' strī-putra-gṛhādi chāḍiyā
> nānā durgama patha laṅghi' āisena dhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for Me; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---wife; [[synonyms:p:putra]]---sons; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---home and so on; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---leaving aside; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:d:durgama]]---difficult; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---paths; [[synonyms:l:laṅghi']]---crossing; [[synonyms:ā:āisena]] [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---come here with great haste.

===== Translation =====

**"All My devotees come here just for Me. Leaving aside their homes and families, they travel by very difficult paths to come here in great haste.**
