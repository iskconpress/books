====== CC Antya 12.119 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' ghara haite taila-kalasa lañā
> prabhura āge āṅgināte phelilā bhāṅgiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:g:ghara]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the room; [[synonyms:t:taila]]-[[synonyms:k:kalasa]]---the jugful of oil; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āṅgināte]]---in the courtyard; [[synonyms:p:phelilā]]---threw; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgiyā]]---breaking.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita took the jug of oil from the room and threw it down before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in the courtyard and broke it.**
