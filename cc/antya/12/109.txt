====== Cc. Antya 12.109 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthe deha' taila,---dīpa yena jvale
> tāra pariśrama haiba parama-saphale"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]]---unto Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---deliver; [[synonyms:t:taila]]---oil; [[synonyms:d:dīpa]]---lamps; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---so; [[synonyms:j:jvale]]---burn; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:p:pariśrama]]---his labor; [[synonyms:h:haiba]]---will become; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:s:saphale]]---completely successful.

===== Translation =====

**"Deliver this oil to the temple of Jagannātha, where it may be burned in the lamps. In this way, Jagadānanda's labor to manufacture the oil will be perfectly successful."**
