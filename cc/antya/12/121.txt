====== Cc. Antya 12.121 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīya divase prabhu tāṅra dvāre yāñā
> 'uṭhaha' paṇḍita'---kari' kahena ḍākiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīya]] [[synonyms:d:divase]]---on the third day; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---to the door; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:u:uṭhaha']]---please get up; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---My dear Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---saying; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---said; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍākiyā]]---calling.

===== Translation =====

**Three days later, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the door of his room and said, "My dear Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, please get up.**
