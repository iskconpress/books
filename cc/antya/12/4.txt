====== Cc. Antya 12.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ataḥpara mahāprabhura viṣaṇṇa-antara
> kṛṣṇera viyoga-daśā sphure nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataḥpara]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:viṣaṇṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]]---morose mind; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viyoga]]-[[synonyms:d:daśā]]---feeling of separation; [[synonyms:s:sphure]]---manifests; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---continuously.

===== Translation =====

**The mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was always morose because of a continuously manifested feeling of separation from Kṛṣṇa.**
