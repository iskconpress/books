====== Cc. Antya 12.127 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"dvitīya-pāte bāḍa' anna-vyañjana
> tomāya āmāya āji ekatra kariba bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]-[[synonyms:p:pāte]]---on a second leaf; [[synonyms:b:bāḍa']]---deliver; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---cooked rice and vegetables; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]] [[synonyms:ā:āmāya]]---both you and I; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---together; [[synonyms:k:kariba]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---will take lunch.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord said, "Spread another leaf with a helping of rice and vegetables so that today you and I may take lunch together."**
