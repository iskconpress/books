====== CC Antya 12.110 ======

===== Text =====

> ei kathā govinda jagadānandere kahila
> mauna kari' rahila paṇḍita, kichu nā kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---this message; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:j:jagadānandere]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---informed Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---keeping silent; [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---did not reply.

===== Translation =====

**When Govinda informed Jagadānanda Paṇḍita of this message, Jagadānanda remained silent, not saying even a word.**
