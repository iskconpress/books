====== Cc. Antya 12.18 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe giyā rahilā grāma-bhitara vṛkṣa-tale
> śivānanda vinā vāsa-sthāna nāhi mile

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:g:grāma]]-[[synonyms:b:bhitara]]---inside a village; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---under a tree; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---residential quarters; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:mile]]---no one could get.

===== Translation =====

**The party went into a village and waited beneath a tree because no one but Śivānanda Sena could arrange for their residential quarters.**
