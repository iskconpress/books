====== CC Antya 12.139 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu kahena kari' vinaya-sammāna
> 'daśa-guṇa khāoyāilā ebe kara samādhāna'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]-[[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---submissive respect; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---ten times more; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāilā]]---you have made to eat; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---please stop.

===== Translation =====

**At last the Lord respectfully submitted, "My dear Jagadānanda, you have already made Me eat ten times more than I am used to. Now, please stop."**
