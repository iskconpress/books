====== CC Antya 12.57 ======

===== Text =====

> 'parameśvarā muñi' bali' daṇḍavat kaila
> tāre dekhi' prabhu prīte tāhāre puchila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parameśvarā]]---Parameśvara; [[synonyms:m:muñi']]---I am; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---he said; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prīte]]---with affection; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]]---him; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---asked.

===== Translation =====

**When he offered his obeisances to the Lord, he said, "I am the same Parameśvara." Upon seeing him, the Lord asked him questions with great affection.**
