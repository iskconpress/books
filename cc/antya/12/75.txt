====== CC Antya 12.75 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura vacane sabāra dravī-bhūta mana
> ajhora-nayane sabe karena krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---by the words; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:d:dravī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhūta]]---melted; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---hearts; [[synonyms:a:ajhora]]---incessantly pouring tears; [[synonyms:n:nayane]]---eyes; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---were crying.

===== Translation =====

**When all the devotees heard these sweet words of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, their hearts melted, and they began to shed incessant tears.**
