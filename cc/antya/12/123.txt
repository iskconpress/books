====== CC Antya 12.123 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu gelā, paṇḍita uṭhilā
> snāna kari' nānā vyañjana randhana karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhilā]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita got up; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking his bath; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:r:randhana]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---cooked.

===== Translation =====

**After Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said this and left, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita got up from his bed, bathed, and began to cook varieties of vegetables.**
