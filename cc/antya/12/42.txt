====== Cc. Antya 12.42 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat prabhu kailā sabāra milana
> strī-saba dūra ha-ite kailā prabhura daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting everyone; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---women; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from a distance; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu received them all, just as He had in previous years. The women, however, saw the Lord from a distance.**
