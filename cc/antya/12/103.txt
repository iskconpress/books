====== CC Antya 12.103 ======

===== Text =====

> sugandhi kariyā taila gāgarī bhariyā
> nīlācale lañā āilā yatana kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sū]]-[[synonyms:g:gandhi]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making aromatic; [[synonyms:t:taila]]---oil; [[synonyms:g:gāgarī]]---a big pot; [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---filling; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:y:yatana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great care.

===== Translation =====

**They filled a large earthen pot with the aromatic oil, and with great care Jagadānanda Paṇḍita brought it to Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī.**
