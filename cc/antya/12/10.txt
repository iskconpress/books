====== CC Antya 12.10 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānanda-prabhure yadyapi ājñā nāi
> tathāpi dekhite calena caitanya-gosāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---there was no order; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:c:calena]]---He went; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Caitanya.

===== Translation =====

**Because Nityānanda Prabhu was preaching in Bengal, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had ordered Him not to come to Jagannātha Purī. That year, however, He went with the rest of the party to see the Lord.**
