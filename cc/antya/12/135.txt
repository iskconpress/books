====== CC Antya 12.135 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ punaḥ paṇḍita nānā vyañjana pariveśe
> bhaye kichu nā balena prabhu, khāyena hariṣe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---various vegetables; [[synonyms:p:pariveśe]]---administered; [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---out of fear; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:balena]]---does not speak; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:khāyena]]---eats; [[synonyms:h:hariṣe]]---very happily.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita continued to offer the Lord varieties of vegetables. Out of fear, the Lord said nothing, but continued eating happily.**
