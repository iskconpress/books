====== CC Antya 12.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya jaya dayāmaya
> jaya jaya nityānanda kṛpā-sindhu jaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---all-merciful; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of mercy; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is all-merciful! All glories to Nityānanda Prabhu, who is an ocean of mercy!**
