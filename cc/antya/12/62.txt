====== Cc. Antya 12.62 ======

===== Text =====

> cāturmāsya saba yātrā kailā daraśana
> mālinī-prabhṛti prabhure kailā nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāturmāsya]]---for four months; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---all the festivals; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---saw; [[synonyms:m:mālinī]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhṛti]]---ladies like Mālinī, the wife of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---made invitations.

===== Translation =====

**For four consecutive months, the devotees observed all the festivals. The wives, such as Mālinī, extended invitations for lunch to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
