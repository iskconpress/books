====== Cc. Antya 12.82 ======

===== Text =====

> cale saba bhakta-gaṇa rodana kariyā
> mahāprabhu rahilā ghare viṣaṇṇa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cale]]---proceed; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:r:rodana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---crying; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at His place; [[synonyms:v:viṣaṇṇa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being very morose.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began their journey crying, while the Lord remained morosely at His residence.**
