====== CC Antya 12.125 ======

===== Text =====

> saghṛta śālyanna kalā-pāte stūpa kailā
> kalāra ḍoṅgā bhari' vyañjana caudike dharilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:ghṛta]]---mixed with ghee; [[synonyms:ś:śāli]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]]---very fine rice; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāte]]---on a banana leaf; [[synonyms:s:stūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---stacked; [[synonyms:k:kalāra]] [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍoṅgā]]---pots made of the bark of a banana tree; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:c:cau]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---all around; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---placed.

===== Translation =====

**He had cooked fine rice, mixed it with ghee and piled it high on a banana leaf. There were also varieties of vegetables, placed all around in pots made of banana tree bark.**
