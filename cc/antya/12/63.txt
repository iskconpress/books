====== CC Antya 12.63 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura priya nānā dravya āniyāche deśa haite
> sei vyañjana kari' bhikṣā dena ghara-bhāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---dear; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---varieties of things; [[synonyms:ā:āniyāche]]---brought; [[synonyms:d:deśa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from their country; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---preparing those vegetables; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dena]]---offer food; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāte]]---cooking at home.

===== Translation =====

**From Bengal the devotees had brought varieties of Bengali food that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu liked. They also cooked various grains and vegetables in their homes and offered them to the Lord.**
