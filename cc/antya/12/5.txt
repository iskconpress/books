====== Cc. Antya 12.5 ======

===== Text =====

> 'hāhā kṛṣṇa prāṇa-nātha vrajendra-nandana!
> kāhāṅ yāṅa kāhāṅ pāṅa, muralī-vadana!'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hāhā]]---O; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---My dear Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]---My life and soul; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of Mahārāja Nanda; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:y:yāṅa]]---where shall I go; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:p:pāṅa]]---where shall I get; [[synonyms:m:muralī]]---flute; [[synonyms:v:vadana]]---mouth.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord would cry, "O My Lord Kṛṣṇa, My life and soul! O son of Mahārāja Nanda, where shall I go? Where shall I attain You? O Supreme Personality who plays with Your flute to Your mouth!"**
