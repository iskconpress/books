====== CC Antya 12.86 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva-varṣe jagadānanda 'ai' dekhibāre
> prabhu-ājñā lañā āilā nadīyā-nagare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣe]]---in the previous year; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āi]]---Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the permission of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nadīyā]]-[[synonyms:n:nagare]]---to the city of Nadia.

===== Translation =====

**The previous year, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, following the Lord's order, had returned to the city of Nadia to see Śacīmātā.**
