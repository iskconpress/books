====== Cc. Antya 12.128 ======

===== Text =====

> hasta tuli' rahena prabhu, nā karena bhojana
> tabe paṇḍita kahena kichu saprema vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hasta]] [[synonyms:t:tuli']]---raising His hands; [[synonyms:r:rahena]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---did not eat; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Jagadānanda said; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words with great affection and love.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu kept His hands raised and would not accept the prasāda until Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, with great affection and love, spoke the following words.**
