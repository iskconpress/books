====== CC Antya 12.102 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda-sena-gṛhe yāñā rahilā
> 'candanādi' taila tāhāṅ eka-mātrā kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to the house of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:c:candana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:t:taila]]---oil distilled from sandalwood and other substances; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:m:mātrā]]---one //mātrā// (sixteen seers, or //seras//); [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---prepared.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita stayed at Śivānanda Sena's house for some time, and they prepared about sixteen seers of scented sandalwood oil.**
