====== CC Antya 12.52 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe saba bhakta lañā karilā bhojana
> govindere ājñā dilā kari' ācamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with all the devotees; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---took lunch; [[synonyms:g:govindere]]---unto Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave the order; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]]---after washing His hands and mouth.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord ate lunch in the company of all the other devotees, and after washing His hands and mouth He gave an order to Govinda.**
