====== Cc. Antya 12.11 ======

===== Text =====

> śrīvāsādi cāri bhāi, saṅgete mālinī
> ācāryaratnera saṅge tāṅhāra gṛhiṇī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---four brothers; [[synonyms:s:saṅgete]] [[synonyms:m:mālinī]]---accompanied by his wife, Mālinī; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryaratnera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---and with Ācāryaratna; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:g:gṛhiṇī]]---his wife.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura was also there with his three brothers and his wife, Mālinī. Ācāryaratna was similarly accompanied by his wife.**
