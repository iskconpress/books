====== Cc. Antya 16.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tāre namaskari' kālidāsa vidāya māgilā
> jhaḍu-ṭhākura tabe tāṅra anuvraji, āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him (Jhaḍu Ṭhākura); [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kālidāsa]]---Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---asked permission to go; [[synonyms:j:jhaḍu]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---Jhaḍu Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---him; [[synonyms:a:anuvraji']]---following; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**Kālidāsa again offered his obeisances to Jhaḍu Ṭhākura and asked his permission to go. The saint Jhaḍu Ṭhākura followed him as he left.**
