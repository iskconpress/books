====== CC Antya 16.116 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe mahāprabhu yabe ājñā dilā
> rāmānanda-rāya śloka paḍite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---ordered; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to recite.

===== Translation =====

**In ecstatic love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered Rāmānanda Rāya to recite some verses. Thus Rāmānanda Rāya spoke as follows.**
