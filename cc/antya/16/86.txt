====== CC Antya 16.86 ======

===== Text =====

> ei līlā nija-granthe raghunātha-dāsa
> 'gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣe' kariyāchena prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---this pastime; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:granthe]]---in his own book; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:stava]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]]---//Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa//; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---has described.

===== Translation =====

**In his book known as Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa, Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī has described this incident very nicely.**
