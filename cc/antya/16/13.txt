====== CC Antya 16.13 ======

===== Text =====

> śūdra-vaiṣṇavera ghare yāya bheṭa lañā
> ei-mata tāṅra ucchiṣṭa khāya lukāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śūdra]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of Vaiṣṇavas born in //śūdra// families; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the homes; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:b:bheṭa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking gifts; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---their; [[synonyms:u:ucchiṣṭa]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---eats; [[synonyms:l:lukāñā]]---hiding.

===== Translation =====

**He would also take gifts to the homes of Vaiṣṇavas born in śūdra families. Then he would hide and eat the remnants of food they threw away in this manner.**
