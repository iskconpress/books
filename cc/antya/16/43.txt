====== CC Antya 16.43 ======

===== Text =====

> govindere mahāprabhu kairāche niyama
> 'mora pāda-jala yena nā laya kona jana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govindere]]---unto Govinda; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kairāche]]---has given; [[synonyms:n:niyama]]---a regulative principle; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---water from washing the feet; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:l:laya]]---does not take; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---anyone.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered His personal servant Govinda that no one should take the water that had washed His feet.**
