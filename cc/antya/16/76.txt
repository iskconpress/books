====== CC Antya 16.76 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-prabhura ei kṛpāra mahimā
> brahmādi deva yāra nāhi pāya sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāra]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glory of the mercy; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:d:deva]]---the demigods; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---do not reach; [[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**This is the glory of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's causeless mercy, which even the demigods, headed by Lord Brahmā, cannot estimate.**
