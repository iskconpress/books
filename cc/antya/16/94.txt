====== Cc. Antya 16.94 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ei dravye eta svāda kāhāṅ haite āila?
> kṛṣṇera adharāmṛta ithe sañcārila'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dravye]]---in these ingredients; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:s:svāda]]---taste; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---has come; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:adhara]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---nectar from the lips; [[synonyms:i:ithe]]---in this; [[synonyms:s:sañcārila]]---has spread.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu considered, "Where has such a taste in this prasāda come from? Certainly it is due to its having been touched by the nectar of Kṛṣṇa's lips."**
