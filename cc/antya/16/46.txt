====== CC Antya 16.46 ======

===== Text =====

> eka añjali, dui añjali, tina añjali pilā
> tabe mahāprabhu tāṅre niṣedha karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:a:añjali]]---one palmful; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:a:añjali]]---two palmfuls; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:a:añjali]]---three palmfuls; [[synonyms:p:pilā]]---he drank; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:n:niṣedha]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---forbade.

===== Translation =====

**Kālidāsa drank one palmful and then a second and a third. Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu forbade him to drink more.**
