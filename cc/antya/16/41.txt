====== Cc. Antya 16.41 ======

===== Text =====

> siṁha-dvārera uttara-dike kapāṭera āḍe
> bāiśa 'pāhāca'-tale āche eka nimna gāḍe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārera]]---of the Siṁha-dvāra; [[synonyms:u:uttara]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---on the northern side; [[synonyms:k:kapāṭera]] [[synonyms:ā:āḍe]]---behind the door; [[synonyms:b:bāiśa]] [[synonyms:p:'pāhāca']]---of the twenty-two steps; [[synonyms:t:tale]]---at the bottom; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:n:nimna]]---low; [[synonyms:g:gāḍe]]---ditch.

===== Translation =====

**On the northern side of the Siṁha-dvāra, behind the door, there are twenty-two steps leading to the temple, and at the bottom of those steps is a ditch.**
