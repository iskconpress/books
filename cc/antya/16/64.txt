====== CC Antya 16.64 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlācale mahāprabhu rahe ei-mate
> kālidāse mahā-kṛpā kailā alakṣite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:k:kālidāse]]---unto Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---great favor; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---bestowed; [[synonyms:a:alakṣite]]---invisibly.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at Jagannātha Purī, Nīlācala, and He invisibly bestowed great mercy upon Kālidāsa.**
