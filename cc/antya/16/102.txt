====== CC Antya 16.102 ======

===== Text =====

> madhyāhna kariyā kailā bhikṣā nirvāhaṇa
> kṛṣṇādharāmṛta sadā antare smaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---after finishing His noon duties; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:n:nirvāhaṇa]]---completed His lunch; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:adhara]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the nectar from the lips of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within Himself; [[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]---remembering.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing His noon duties, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ate His lunch, but He constantly remembered the remnants of Kṛṣṇa's food.**
