====== Cc. Antya 16.39 ======

===== Text =====

> sei kālidāsa yabe nīlācale āilā
> mahāprabhu tāṅra upara mahā-kṛpā kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kālidāsa]]---that Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:u:upara]]---upon him; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---great mercy; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---bestowed.

===== Translation =====

**When Kālidāsa visited Jagannātha Purī, Nīlācala, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed great mercy upon him.**
