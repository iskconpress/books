====== CC Antya 16.78 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅ-sabāra saṅge prabhura chila bāhya-jñāna
> tāṅrā gele punaḥ hailā unmāda pradhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---all of them; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:t:tāṅrā]] [[synonyms:g:gele]]---when they departed; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---there was; [[synonyms:u:unmāda]]---madness; [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the chief business.

===== Translation =====

**As long as the devotees were in Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu maintained His external consciousness, but after their departure His chief engagement was again the madness of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
