====== CC Antya 16.120 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' gaura-prabhu bhāvāviṣṭa hañā
> dui ślokera artha kare pralāpa kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---overwhelmed by ecstatic loving emotions; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the two verses; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---talking like a madman.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was overwhelmed by ecstatic loving emotions. Talking like a madman, He began to explain the meaning of the two verses.**
