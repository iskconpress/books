====== CC Antya 16.23 ======

===== Text =====

> ṭhākura kahe,---"aiche vāt kahite nā yuyāya
> āmi---nīca-jāti, tumi---susajjana rāya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Jhaḍu Ṭhākura said; [[synonyms:a:aiche]] [[synonyms:v:vāt]]---such a request; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---should not be spoken; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---coming from a family of a very low caste; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---very respectable and rich gentleman.

===== Translation =====

**Jhaḍu Ṭhākura replied, "It does not befit you to ask this of me. I belong to a very low-caste family, whereas you are a respectable rich gentleman."**
