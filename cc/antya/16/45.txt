====== Cc. Antya 16.45 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina prabhu tāṅhā pāda prakṣālite
> kālidāsa āsi' tāhāṅ pātilena hāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:p:pāda]] [[synonyms:p:prakṣālite]]---washing His feet; [[synonyms:k:kālidāsa]]---Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:pātilena]]---spread; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---his palm.

===== Translation =====

**One day as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was washing His feet in that place, Kālidāsa came and extended his palm to take the water.**
