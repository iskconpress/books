====== CC Antya 16.57 ======

===== Text =====

> vaiṣṇavera śeṣa-bhakṣaṇera eteka mahimā
> kālidāse pāoyāila prabhura kṛpā-sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇera]]---of eating the remnants of food; [[synonyms:e:eteka]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---so much value; [[synonyms:k:kālidāse]]---Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:p:pāoyāila]]---caused to get; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the supreme mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Taking the remnants of the food of Vaiṣṇavas is so valuable that it induced Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to offer Kālidāsa His supreme mercy.**
