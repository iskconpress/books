====== Cc. Antya 16.95 ======

===== Text =====

> ei buddhye mahāprabhura premāveśa haila
> jagannāthera sevaka dekhi' samvaraṇa kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:b:buddhye]]---by this understanding; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servants; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:samvaraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---restrained Himself.

===== Translation =====

**Understanding this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt an emotion of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa, but upon seeing the servants of Lord Jagannātha, He restrained Himself.**
