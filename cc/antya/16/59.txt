====== CC Antya 16.59 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera ucchiṣṭa haya 'mahā-prasāda' nāma
> 'bhakta-śeṣa' haile 'mahā-mahā-prasādākhyāna'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:u:ucchiṣṭa]]---remnants of the food of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---called //mahā-prasāda//; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]---the remnants of a devotee; [[synonyms:h:haile]]---when it becomes; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---great //mahā-prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāna]]---named.

===== Translation =====

**The remnants of food offered to Lord Kṛṣṇa are called mahā-prasāda. After this same mahā-prasāda has been taken by a devotee, the remnants are elevated to mahā-mahā-prasāda.**
