====== CC Antya 16.66 ======

===== Text =====

> putra saṅge lañā teṅho āilā prabhu-sthāne
> putrere karāilā prabhura caraṇa vandane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:putra]]---the son; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:putrere]]---his son; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---made to do; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandane]]---worshiping the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Taking his son, Śivānanda Sena went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at His residence. He made his son offer respectful obeisances at the lotus feet of the Lord.**
