====== CC Antya 16.92 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra alpa lañā prabhu jihvāte yadi dilā
> āra saba govindera āṅcale bāndhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]---very little; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jihvāte]]---on the tongue; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---put; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all the balance; [[synonyms:g:govindera]]---of Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:āṅcale]]---at the end of the wrapper; [[synonyms:b:bāndhilā]]---bound.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tasted a portion of the prasāda. Govinda took the rest and bound it in the end of his wrapper.**
