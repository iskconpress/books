====== CC Antya 16.42 ======

===== Text =====

> sei gāḍe karena prabhu pāda-prakṣālane
> tabe karibāre yāya īśvara-daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:g:gāḍe]]---in that ditch; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:prakṣālane]]---washing the feet; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to do; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---He goes; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to visit Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would wash His feet in this ditch, and then He would enter the temple to see Lord Jagannātha.**
