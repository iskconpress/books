====== CC Antya 16.52 ======

===== Text =====

> namas te nara-siṁhāya
> prahlādāhlāda-dāyine
> hiraṇyakaśipor vakṣaḥ-
> śilā-ṭaṅka-nakhālaye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:namaḥ]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:t:te]]---unto You; [[synonyms:n:nara]]-[[synonyms:s:siṁhāya]]---Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:p:prahlāda]]---to Mahārāja Prahlāda; [[synonyms:ā:āhlāda]]---of pleasure; [[synonyms:d:dāyine]]---giver; [[synonyms:h:hiraṇya]]-[[synonyms:k:kaśipoḥ]]---of Hiraṇyakaśipu; [[synonyms:v:vakṣaḥ]]---chest; [[synonyms:ś:śilā]]---like stone; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭaṅka]]---like the chisel; [[synonyms:n:nakha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaye]]---whose fingernails.

===== Translation =====

**"I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva. You are the giver of pleasure to Mahārāja Prahlāda, and Your nails cut the chest of Hiraṇyakaśipu like a chisel cutting stone.**

===== Purport =====

This and the following verse are quoted from the //Nṛsiṁha Purāṇa.//
