====== CC Antya 16.60 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-pada-dhūli āra bhakta-pada-jala
> bhakta-bhukta-avaśeṣa,---tina mahā-bala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:d:dhūli]]---the dust of the lotus feet of a devotee; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]---the water that washed the feet of a devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhukta]]-[[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]]---and the remnants of food eaten by a devotee; [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bala]]---very powerful.

===== Translation =====

**The dust of the feet of a devotee, the water that has washed the feet of a devotee, and the remnants of food left by a devotee are three very powerful substances.**
