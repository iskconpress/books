====== Cc. Antya 16.84 ======

===== Text =====

> seha bale,---'ei dekha śrī-puruṣottama
> netra bhariyā tumi karaha daraśana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:seha]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---he also said; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---just see; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa, the best of all Personalities of Godhead; [[synonyms:n:netra]] [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---to the full satisfaction of Your eyes; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---see.

===== Translation =====

**"Just see!" the doorkeeper said. "Here is the best of the Personalities of Godhead. From here You may see the Lord to the full satisfaction of Your eyes."**
