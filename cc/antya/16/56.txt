====== CC Antya 16.56 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura iṅgita govinda saba jāne
> kālidāsere dila prabhura śeṣa-pātra-dāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:i:iṅgita]]---indications; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---His personal servant; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:k:kālidāsere]]---unto Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the remnants of food; [[synonyms:d:dāne]]---presentation.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda understood all the indications of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Therefore he immediately delivered the remnants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's food to Kālidāsa.**
