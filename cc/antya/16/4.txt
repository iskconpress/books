====== CC Antya 16.4 ======

===== Text =====

> varṣāntare āilā saba gauḍera bhakta-gaṇa
> pūrvavat āsi' kaila prabhura milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---the next year; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]]---of Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**The next year, as usual, all the devotees from Bengal went to Jagannātha Purī, and, as in previous years, there was a meeting between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and the devotees.**
