====== Cc. Antya 16.61 ======

===== Text =====

> ei tina-sevā haite kṛṣṇa-premā haya
> punaḥ punaḥ sarva-śāstre phukāriyā kaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---rendering service to these three; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---all the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:p:phu]]-[[synonyms:k:kāriyā]] [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---declare loudly.

===== Translation =====

**By rendering service to these three, one attains the supreme goal of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa. In all the revealed scriptures this is loudly declared again and again.**
