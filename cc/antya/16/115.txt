====== CC Antya 16.115 ======

===== Text =====

> hari-dhvani kari' sabe kailā āsvādana
> āsvādite preme matta ha-ila sabāra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---loudly resounding the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasted; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādite]]---as soon as they tasted; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:matta]]---maddened; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds of all.

===== Translation =====

**Loudly chanting the holy name of Hari, all of them tasted the prasāda. As they tasted it, their minds became mad in the ecstasy of love.**
