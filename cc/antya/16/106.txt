====== CC Antya 16.106 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-sārvabhauma-svarūpādi-gaṇe
> sabāre prasāda dila kariyā baṇṭane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by; [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto them; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of the food of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:b:baṇṭane]]---making shares.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then gave shares of the prasāda to Rāmānanda Rāya, Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and all the other devotees.**
