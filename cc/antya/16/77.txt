====== Cc. Antya 16.77 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa prabhu-saṅge rahe cāri-māse
> prabhu ājñā dilā sabe gelā gauḍa-deśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:m:māse]]---for four months; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave the order; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---unto everyone; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to Bengal.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continuously for four months. Then the Lord ordered them back to Bengal, and therefore they returned.**
