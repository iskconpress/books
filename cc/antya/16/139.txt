====== CC Antya 16.139 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kaha rāma-rāya, kichu śunite haya mana'
> bhāva jāni' paḍe rāya gopīra vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaha]]---speak; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---to hear; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---I wish; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the situation; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---understanding; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya cites; [[synonyms:g:gopīra]] [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the words of the //gopīs.//

===== Translation =====

**Again Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to Rāmānanda Rāya, "Please say something. I want to hear." Understanding the situation, Rāmānanda Rāya recited the following words of the gopīs.**
