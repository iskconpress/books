====== Cc. Antya 16.101 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu tā-sabāre vidāya dilā
> upala-bhoga dekhiyā prabhu nija-vāsā āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:u:upala]]-[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---the next offering of food; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---to His place; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade farewell to all the servants. After seeing the next offering of food to Lord Jagannātha, a function known as upala-bhoga, He returned to His own quarters.**
