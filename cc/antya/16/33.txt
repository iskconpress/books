====== CC Antya 16.33 ======

===== Text =====

> jhaḍu-ṭhākura ghara yāi' dekhi' āmra-phala
> mānasei kṛṣṇa-candre arpilā sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jhaḍu]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---Jhaḍu Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:g:ghara]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---returning home; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']] [[synonyms:ā:āmra]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---seeing the mangoes; [[synonyms:m:mānasei]]---within his mind; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:candre]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:arpilā]]---offered; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all.

===== Translation =====

**Upon returning home, Jhaḍu Ṭhākura saw the mangoes Kālidāsa had presented. Within his mind he offered them to Kṛṣṇa-candra.**
