====== Cc. Antya 16.110 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dravye eta āsvāda, gandha lokātīta
> āsvāda kariyā dekha,---sabāra pratīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dravye]]---in such material things; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]]---pleasing taste; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---fragrance; [[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:a:atīta]]---never experienced by any common man; [[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---tasting; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---see; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:p:pratīta]]---experience.

===== Translation =====

**"However," the Lord continued, "in these ingredients there are extraordinary tastes and uncommon fragrances. Just taste them and see the difference in the experience.**
