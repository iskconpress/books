====== Cc. Antya 16.100 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sukṛti'-śabde kahe 'kṛṣṇa-kṛpā-hetu puṇya'
> sei yāṅra haya, 'phelā' pāya sei dhanya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sukṛti]]---//sukṛti// (pious activities); [[synonyms:ś:śabde]]---the word; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---is to be understood; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hetu]]---because of; [[synonyms:p:puṇya]]---pious activities; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---of whom; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:phelā]]---the remnants of food; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---very glorious.

===== Translation =====

**"The word 'sukṛti' refers to pious activities performed by the mercy of Kṛṣṇa. One who is fortunate enough to obtain such mercy receives the remnants of the Lord's food and thus becomes glorious."**
