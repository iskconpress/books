====== Cc. Antya 16.79 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-dine sphure kṛṣṇera rūpa-gandha-rasa
> sākṣād-anubhave,---yena kṛṣṇa-upasparśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---night and day; [[synonyms:s:sphure]]---appears; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---fragrance; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---mellow; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]-[[synonyms:a:anubhave]]---directly experienced; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:u:upasparśa]]---touching Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Throughout the entire day and night, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu directly relished Kṛṣṇa's beauty, fragrance and mellow as if He were touching Kṛṣṇa hand to hand.**
