====== CC Antya 16.28 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' ṭhākura kahe,---"śāstra ei satya kaya
> sei śreṣṭha, aiche yāṅte kṛṣṇa-bhakti haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Jhaḍu Ṭhākura said; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---revealed scripture; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---truth; [[synonyms:k:kaya]]---says; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:ś:śreṣṭha]]---best; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in such a way; [[synonyms:y:yāṅte]]---in whom; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing these quotations from the revealed scripture Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, Jhaḍu Ṭhākura replied, "Yes, this is true, for it is the version of śāstra. It is true, however, for one who is genuinely advanced in devotion to Kṛṣṇa.**
