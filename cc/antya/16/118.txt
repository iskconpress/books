====== CC Antya 16.118 ======

===== Text =====

> śloka śuni' mahāprabhu mahā-tuṣṭa hailā
> rādhāra utkaṇṭhā-śloka paḍite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing the verse; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]]---very satisfied; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---a verse pertaining to the anxiety; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to recite.

===== Translation =====

**Upon hearing Rāmānanda Rāya quote this verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very satisfied. Then He recited the following verse, which had been spoken by Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī in great anxiety.**
