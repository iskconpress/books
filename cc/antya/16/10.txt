====== CC Antya 16.10 ======

===== Text =====

> brāhmaṇa-vaiṣṇava yata---choṭa, baḍa haya
> uttama-vastu bheṭa lañā tāṅra ṭhāñi yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---Vaiṣṇavas coming from //brāhmaṇa// families; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]]---neophyte; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---highly advanced; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:u:uttama]]-[[synonyms:v:vastu]]---first-class eatables; [[synonyms:b:bheṭa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking as gifts; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to them; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes.

===== Translation =====

**He would go to all the Vaiṣṇavas born in brāhmaṇa families, be they neophyte or advanced devotees, and present them gifts of first-class eatables.**
