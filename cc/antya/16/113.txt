====== CC Antya 16.113 ======

===== Text =====

> alaukika-gandha-svāda, anya-vismāraṇa
> mahā-mādaka haya ei kṛṣṇādharera guṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---fragrance; [[synonyms:s:svāda]]---taste; [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:v:vismāraṇa]]---forgetting all others; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:m:mādaka]]---highly enchanting; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:adharera]]---of the lips of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes.

===== Translation =====

**"An uncommon, greatly enchanting fragrance and taste that makes one forget all other experiences are attributes of Kṛṣṇa's lips.**
