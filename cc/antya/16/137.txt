====== CC Antya 16.137 ======

===== Text =====

> ayogya hañā tāhā keha sadā pāna kare
> yogya jana nāhi pāya, lobhe mātra mare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ayogya]]---unfit; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:p:pāna]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---drinks; [[synonyms:y:yogya]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---the competent person; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---does not get; [[synonyms:l:lobhe]]---out of greed; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---simply; [[synonyms:m:mare]]---dies.

===== Translation =====

**"There are persons who are unfit to drink that nectar but who nevertheless drink it continuously, whereas some who are suitable never get it and thus die of greed.**
