====== CC Antya 16.91 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu-mūlya prasāda sei vastu sarvottama
> tāra alpa khāoyāite sevaka karila yatana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---very valuable; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vastu]]---ingredients; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---first class; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]---very little; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāite]]---to feed; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servant; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:y:yatana]]---made some endeavor.

===== Translation =====

**The prasāda was made of very valuable ingredients. Therefore the servant wanted to feed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu a portion of it.**
