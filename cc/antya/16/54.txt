====== CC Antya 16.54 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu karilā jagannātha daraśana
> ghare āsi' madhyāhna kari' karila bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---after this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---after returning home; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after performing His noon activities; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---took lunch.

===== Translation =====

**Having offered obeisances to Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited the temple of Lord Jagannātha. Then He returned to His residence, finished His noon duties and took His lunch.**
