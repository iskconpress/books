====== CC Antya 16.80 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina prabhu gelā jagannātha-daraśane
> siṁha-dvāre dala-i āsi' karila vandane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the gate known as //Siṁha-dvāra//; [[synonyms:d:dala]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---the gatekeeper; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:v:vandane]]---offered respectful obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**One day, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to visit the temple of Lord Jagannātha, the gatekeeper at Siṁha-dvāra approached Him and offered respectful obeisances.**
