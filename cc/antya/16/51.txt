====== CC Antya 16.51 ======

===== Text =====

> prati-dina tāṅre prabhu karena namaskāra
> namaskari' ei śloka paḍe bāra-bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---every day; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto the Deity of Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---these verses; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:b:bāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, His left side toward the Deity, offered obeisances to Lord Nṛsiṁha as He proceeded toward the temple. He recited the following verses again and again while offering obeisances.**
