====== CC Antya 16.6 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-bhāgavata teṅho sarala udāra
> kṛṣṇa-nāma-'saṅkete' cālāya vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]---a highly advanced devotee; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:s:sarala]] [[synonyms:u:udāra]]---very simple and liberal; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkete]]---with chanting of the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:cālāya]]---performs; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---ordinary dealings.

===== Translation =====

**Kālidāsa was a very advanced devotee, yet he was simple and liberal. He would chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa while performing all his ordinary dealings.**
