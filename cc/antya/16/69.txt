====== Cc. Antya 16.69 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"āmi nāma jagate laoyāiluṅ
> sthāvare paryanta kṛṣṇa-nāma kahāiluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name; [[synonyms:j:jagate]]---throughout the whole world; [[synonyms:l:laoyāiluṅ]]---induced to take; [[synonyms:s:sthāvare]]---the unmovable; [[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---up to; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahāiluṅ]]---I induced to chant.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I have induced the whole world to take to the holy name of Kṛṣṇa. I have induced even the trees and immovable plants to chant the holy name.**
