====== Cc. Antya 16.31 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅre vidāya diyā ṭhākura yadi ghare āila
> tāṅra caraṇa-cihna yei ṭhāñi paḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him (Kālidāsa); [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---bidding farewell; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---Jhaḍu Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---returned to his home; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:cihna]]---the mark of his feet; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---wherever; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell.

===== Translation =====

**After bidding farewell to Kālidāsa, Jhaḍu Ṭhākura returned to his home, leaving the marks of his feet plainly visible in many places.**
