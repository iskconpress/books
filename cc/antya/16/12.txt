====== Cc. Antya 16.12 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana karile pātra phelāñā yāya
> lukāñā sei pātra āni' cāṭi' khāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karile]]---after eating; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the plate; [[synonyms:p:phelāñā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---is thrown away; [[synonyms:l:lukāñā]]---hiding; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---that plate; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:c:cāṭi']] [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---he licks up.

===== Translation =====

**After the Vaiṣṇavas finished eating, they would throw away their dishes or leaves, and Kālidāsa would come out of hiding, take the leaves and lick up the remnants.**
