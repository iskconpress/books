====== CC Antya 16.48 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvajña-śiromaṇi caitanya īśvara
> vaiṣṇave tāṅhāra viśvāsa, jānena antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---omniscient; [[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---topmost; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇave]]---unto the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---his faith; [[synonyms:j:jānena]]---He knows; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---the heart.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the most exalted, omniscient Supreme Personality of Godhead, and therefore He knew that Kālidāsa, in the core of his heart, had full faith in Vaiṣṇavas.**
