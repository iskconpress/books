====== CC Antya 16.15 ======

===== Text =====

> āmra bheṭa diyā tāṅra caraṇa vandilā
> tāṅra patnīre tabe namaskāra kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmra]]---mangoes; [[synonyms:b:bheṭa]]---gift; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---presenting; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---feet; [[synonyms:v:vandilā]]---offered respects to; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:patnīre]]---to his wife; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---offered respectful obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**Kālidāsa presented the mangoes to Jhaḍu Ṭhākura and offered him respectful obeisances. Then he also offered respectful obeisances to the Ṭhākura's wife.**
