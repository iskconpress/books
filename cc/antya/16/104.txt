====== Cc. Antya 16.104 ======

===== Text =====

> sandhyā-kṛtya kari' punaḥ nija-gaṇa-saṅge
> nibhṛte vasilā nānā-kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---the evening duties; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after performing; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with His personal associates; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---of topics of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing His evening duties, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down with His personal associates in a secluded place and discussed the pastimes of Kṛṣṇa in great jubilation.**
