====== CC Antya 16.16 ======

===== Text =====

> patnī-sahita teṅho āchena vasiyā
> bahu sammāna kailā kālidāsere dekhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:patnī]]-[[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with his wife; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he (Jhaḍu Ṭhākura); [[synonyms:ā:āchena]] [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---was sitting; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---much; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---respect; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---offered; [[synonyms:k:kālidāsere]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing Kālidāsa.

===== Translation =====

**When Kālidāsa went to Jhaḍu Ṭhākura, he saw that saintly person sitting with his wife. As soon as Jhaḍu Ṭhākura saw Kālidāsa, he likewise offered his respectful obeisances unto him.**
