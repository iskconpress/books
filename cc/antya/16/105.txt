====== CC Antya 16.105 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura iṅgite govinda prasāda ānilā
> purī-bhāratīre prabhu kichu pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:i:iṅgite]]---by the indication of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---brought the remnants of the food of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---to Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhāratīre]]---to Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**Following the indications of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Govinda brought the prasāda of Lord Jagannātha. The Lord sent some to Paramānanda Purī and Brahmānanda Bhāratī.**
