====== CC Antya 16.32 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dhūli lañā kālidāsa sarvāṅge lepilā
> tāṅra nikaṭa eka-sthāne lukāñā rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dhūli]]---that dust; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kālidāsa]]---Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---all over his body; [[synonyms:l:lepilā]]---smeared; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nikaṭa]]---near his place; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in one place; [[synonyms:l:lukāñā]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained hidden.

===== Translation =====

**Kālidāsa smeared the dust from those footprints all over his body. Then he hid in a place near Jhaḍu Ṭhākura's home.**
