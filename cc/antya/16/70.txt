====== CC Antya 16.70 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāre nāriluṅ kṛṣṇa-nāma kahāite!"
> śuniyā svarūpa-gosāñi lāgilā kahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāre]]---this boy; [[synonyms:n:nāriluṅ]]---I could not; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahāite]]---cause to speak; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to say.

===== Translation =====

**"But I could not induce this boy to chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa." Hearing this, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī began to speak.**
