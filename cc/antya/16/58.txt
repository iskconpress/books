====== CC Antya 16.58 ======

===== Text =====

> tāte 'vaiṣṇavera jhuṭā' khāo chāḍi' ghṛṇā-lāja
> yāhā haite pāibā nija vāñchita saba kāja

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]] [[synonyms:j:jhuṭā]]---remnants of the food of Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:khāo]]---eat; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:g:ghṛṇā]]-[[synonyms:l:lāja]]---hate and hesitation; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---by which; [[synonyms:p:pāibā]]---you will get; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---your own; [[synonyms:v:vāñchita]]---desired; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kāja]]---success.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore, giving up hatred and hesitation, try to eat the remnants of the food of Vaiṣṇavas, for you will thus be able to achieve your desired goal of life.**
