====== Cc. Antya 16.111 ======

===== Text =====

> āsvāda dūre rahu, yāra gandhe māte mana
> āpanā vinā anya mādhurya karāya vismaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]]---the taste; [[synonyms:d:dūre]] [[synonyms:r:rahu]]---leave aside; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:g:gandhe]]---by the fragrance; [[synonyms:m:māte]]---becomes pleased; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:ā:āpanā]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---besides itself; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---different; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:k:karāya]] [[synonyms:v:vismaraṇa]]---causes to forget.

===== Translation =====

**"Apart from the taste, even the fragrance pleases the mind and makes one forget any other sweetness besides its own.**
