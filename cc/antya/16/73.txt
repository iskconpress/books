====== CC Antya 16.73 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina kahena prabhu,---'paḍa, purīdāsa'
> ei śloka kari' teṅho karilā prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---another day; [[synonyms:k:kahena]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:p:paḍa]]---recite; [[synonyms:p:purīdāsa]]---Purīdāsa; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifested.

===== Translation =====

**Another day, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to the boy, "Recite, My dear Purīdāsa," the boy composed the following verse and expressed it before everyone.**
