====== CC Antya 16.63 ======

===== Text =====

> tina haite kṛṣṇa-nāma-premera ullāsa
> kṛṣṇera prasāda, tāte 'sākṣī' kālidāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from these three; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---of the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---awakening of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the mercy of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:s:sākṣī]]---evidence; [[synonyms:k:kālidāsa]]---Kālidāsa.

===== Translation =====

**From these three one achieves the highest goal of life-ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa. This is the greatest mercy of Lord Kṛṣṇa. The evidence is Kālidāsa himself.**
