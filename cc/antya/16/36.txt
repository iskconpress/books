====== CC Antya 16.36 ======

===== Text =====

> āṅṭhi-coṣā sei pāṭuyā-kholāte bhariyā
> bāhire ucchiṣṭa-garte phelāilā lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āṅṭhi]]---the seeds; [[synonyms:c:coṣā]]---that had been sucked; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:p:pāṭuyā]]-[[synonyms:k:kholāte]]---banana leaf and bark; [[synonyms:b:bhariyā]]---filling; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:u:ucchiṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:garte]]---in the ditch where refuse was thrown; [[synonyms:p:phelāilā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---picked up and threw.

===== Translation =====

**After she finished eating, she filled the banana leaves and bark with the seeds, picked them up and threw them in the ditch where all the refuse was thrown.**
