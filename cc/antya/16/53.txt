====== CC Antya 16.53 ======

===== Text =====

> ito nṛsiṁhaḥ parato nṛsiṁho
> yato yato yāmi tato nṛsiṁhaḥ
> bahir nṛsiṁho hṛdaye nṛsiṁho
> nṛsiṁham ādiṁ śaraṇaṁ prapadye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:itaḥ]]---here; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhaḥ]]---Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:p:parataḥ]]---on the opposite side; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhaḥ]]---Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]] [[synonyms:y:yataḥ]]---wherever; [[synonyms:y:yāmi]]---I go; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---there; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhaḥ]]---Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]---outside; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhaḥ]]---Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in my heart; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhaḥ]]---Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁham]]---Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:ā:ādim]]---the original Supreme Personality; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇam]] [[synonyms:p:prapadye]]---I take shelter of.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva is here, and He is also there on the opposite side. Wherever I go, there I see Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva. He is outside and within My heart. Therefore I take shelter of Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva, the original Supreme Personality of Godhead."**
