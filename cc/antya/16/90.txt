====== CC Antya 16.90 ======

===== Text =====

> mālā parāñā prasāda dila prabhura hāte
> āsvāda dūre rahu, yāra gandhe mana māte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālā]] [[synonyms:p:parāñā]]---after garlanding; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remnants of Lord Jagannātha's food; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in the hand of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsvāda]]---tasting; [[synonyms:d:dūre]] [[synonyms:r:rahu]]---what to speak of; [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:g:gandhe]]---by the aroma; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:m:māte]]---becomes maddened.

===== Translation =====

**The servants of Lord Jagannātha first garlanded Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and then offered Him Lord Jagannātha's prasāda. The prasāda was so nice that its aroma alone, to say nothing of its taste, would drive the mind mad.**
