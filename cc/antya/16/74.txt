====== CC Antya 16.74 ======

===== Text =====

> śravasoḥ kuvalayam akṣṇor añjanam
> uraso mahendra-maṇi-dāma
> vṛndāvana-ramaṇīnāṁ maṇḍanam
> akhilaṁ harir jayati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śravasoḥ]]---of the two ears; [[synonyms:k:kuvalayam]]---blue lotus flowers; [[synonyms:a:akṣṇoḥ]]---of the two eyes; [[synonyms:a:añjanam]]---ointment; [[synonyms:u:urasaḥ]]---of the chest; [[synonyms:m:mahendra]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇi]]-[[synonyms:d:dāma]]---a necklace of //indranīla// gems; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:r:ramaṇīnām]]---of the damsels of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:m:maṇḍanam]]---ornaments; [[synonyms:a:akhilam]]---all; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]] [[synonyms:j:jayati]]---all glories to Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa is just like a bluish lotus flower for the ears; He is ointment for the eyes, a necklace of indranīla gems for the chest, and universal ornaments for the gopī damsels of Vṛndāvana. Let that Lord Śrī Hari, Kṛṣṇa, be glorified."**
