====== CC Antya 16.50 ======

===== Text =====

> bāiśa 'pāhāca'-pāche upara dakṣiṇa-dike
> eka nṛsiṁha-mūrti āchena uṭhite vāma-bhāge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāiśa]] [[synonyms:p:pāhāca]]---of the twenty-two steps; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---toward the back; [[synonyms:u:upara]]---above; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---on the southern side; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]-[[synonyms:m:mūrti]]---Deity of Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---there is; [[synonyms:u:uṭhite]]---while stepping upward; [[synonyms:v:vāma]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāge]]---on the left side.

===== Translation =====

**On the southern side, behind and above the twenty-two steps, is a Deity of Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva. It is on the left as one goes up the steps toward the temple.**
