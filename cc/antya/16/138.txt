====== CC Antya 16.138 ======

===== Text =====

> tāte jāni,---kona tapasyāra āche bala
> ayogyere deoyāya kṛṣṇādharāmṛta-phala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I can understand; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:t:tapasyāra]]---of austerity; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:b:bala]]---strength; [[synonyms:a:ayogyere]]---unto the unfit; [[synonyms:d:deoyāya]]---delivers; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:adhara]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the nectar of Kṛṣṇa's lips; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---the result.

===== Translation =====

**"It is therefore to be understood that such an unfit person must have obtained the nectar of Kṛṣṇa's lips on the strength of some austerity."**
