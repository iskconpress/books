====== CC Antya 16.62 ======

===== Text =====

> tāte bāra bāra kahi,---śuna bhakta-gaṇa
> viśvāsa kariyā kara e-tina sevana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I say; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---keeping faith; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:e:e]]-[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---rendering service to these three.

===== Translation =====

**Therefore, my dear devotees, please hear from me, for I insist again and again: please keep faith in these three and render service to them without hesitation.**
