====== CC Antya 16.3 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu rahena nīlācale
> bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge sadā prema-vihvale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahena]]---stays; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---in Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of devotees; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vihvale]]---merged in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus stayed at Jagannātha Purī in the association of His devotees, always merged in ecstatic devotional love.**
