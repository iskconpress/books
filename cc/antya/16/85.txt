====== Cc. Antya 16.85 ======

===== Text =====

> garuḍera pāche rahi' karena daraśana
> dekhena,---jagannātha haya muralī-vadana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:garuḍera]] [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind the Garuḍa column; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---He was seeing; [[synonyms:d:dekhena]]---He saw; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---was; [[synonyms:m:muralī]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa with His flute to the mouth.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed behind the huge column called the Garuḍa-stambha and looked upon Lord Jagannātha, but as He looked He saw that Lord Jagannātha had become Lord Kṛṣṇa, with His flute to His mouth.**
