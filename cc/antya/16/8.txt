====== CC Antya 16.8 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha-dāsera teṅho haya jñāti-khuḍā
> vaiṣṇavera ucchiṣṭa khāite teṅho haila buḍā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsera]]---of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he (Kālidāsa); [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:j:jñāti]]---relative; [[synonyms:k:khuḍā]]---uncle; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:u:ucchiṣṭa]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:k:khāite]]---eating; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:b:buḍā]]---aged.

===== Translation =====

**Kālidāsa was an uncle of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī. Throughout his entire life, even in his old age, he tried to eat the remnants of food left by Vaiṣṇavas.**
