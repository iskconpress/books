====== CC Antya 16.75 ======

===== Text =====

> sāta vatsarera śiśu, nāhi adhyayana
> aiche śloka kare,---lokera camatkāra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāta]] [[synonyms:v:vatsarera]]---seven years old; [[synonyms:ś:śiśu]]---the boy; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:a:adhyayana]]---without education; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---composes; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all the people; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---struck with wonder; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**Although the boy was only seven years old and still had no education, he composed such a nice verse. Everyone was struck with wonder.**
