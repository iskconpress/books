====== Cc. Antya 16.55 ======

===== Text =====

> bahir-dvāre āche kālidāsa pratyāśā kariyā
> govindere ṭhāre prabhu kahena jāniyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahiḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---outside the door; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:kālidāsa]]---Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:p:pratyāśā]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---expecting; [[synonyms:g:govindere]]---unto Govinda; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāre]]---by indications; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---speaks; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---knowing.

===== Translation =====

**Kālidāsa was standing outside the door, expecting the remnants of food from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Knowing this, Mahāprabhu gave an indication to Govinda.**
