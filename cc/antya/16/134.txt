====== CC Antya 16.134 ======

===== Text =====

> kahite kahite prabhura mana phiri' gela
> krodha-aṁśa śānta haila, utkaṇṭhā bāḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---talking and talking; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:p:phiri']] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---became changed; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]-[[synonyms:a:aṁśa]]---the part of anger; [[synonyms:ś:śānta]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became pacified; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---agitation of the mind; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was talking like this, His mind changed. His anger subsided, but His mental agitation increased.**
