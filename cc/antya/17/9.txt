====== Cc. Antya 17.9 ======

===== Text =====

> gambhīrāra dvāre govinda karilā śayana
> saba-rātri prabhu karena ucca-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gambhīrāra]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's room; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---His personal servant; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lay down; [[synonyms:s:saba]]-[[synonyms:r:rātri]]---all night; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:u:ucca]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---loud chanting.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's personal servant, Govinda, lay down at the door of His room, and the Lord very loudly chanted the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra all night.**
