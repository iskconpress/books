====== Cc. Antya 17.69 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-bhāve bhaja, loka, caitanya-caraṇa
> yāhā haite pāibā kṛṣṇa-premāmṛta-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in all respects; [[synonyms:b:bhaja]]---worship; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---O entire world; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---by which; [[synonyms:p:pāibā]]---you will get; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---of the nectar; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the treasure.

===== Translation =====

**Worship the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in all respects. Only in this way will you achieve the nectarean treasure of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
