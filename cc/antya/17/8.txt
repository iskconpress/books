====== CC Antya 17.8 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate nānā-bhāve ardha-rātri haila
> gosāñire śayana karāi' duṅhe ghare gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in varieties of emotions; [[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:r:rātri]]---half the night; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---passed; [[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]] [[synonyms:k:karāi']]---making Him lie down; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went home.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed half the night experiencing varieties of emotions. Finally, after making the Lord lie down on His bed, both Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya returned to their homes.**
