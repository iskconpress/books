~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya 17.61~~

====== CC Antya 17.61 ======

===== Text =====

> kāhāṅ gele tomā pāi, tumi kaha,---tāhāṅ yāi",
> eta kahi' calilā dhāñā
> svarūpa uṭhi' kole kari', prabhure ānila dhari',
> nija-sthāne vasāilā laiñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:g:gele]]---going; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---You; [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---I can get; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please tell; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:c:calilā]] [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---began to run; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:k:kole]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---taking on His lap; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought back; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in His own place; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:l:laiñā]]---taking.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Where shall I go? Where can I find You? Please tell Me. I shall go there.' " Speaking in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began running. Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, however, stood up, caught Him and took Him on his lap. Then Svarūpa Dāmodara brought Him back to His place and made Him sit down.**
