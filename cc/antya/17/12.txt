====== Cc. Antya 17.12 ======

===== Text =====

> siṁha-dvāra-dakṣiṇe āche tailaṅgī-gābhī-gaṇa
> tāhāṅ yāi' paḍilā prabhu hañā acetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---of the gate named Siṁha-dvāra; [[synonyms:d:dakṣiṇe]]---on the southern side; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:t:tailaṅgī]]-[[synonyms:g:gābhī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---cows belonging to the Tailaṅga district; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:a:acetana]]---becoming unconscious.

===== Translation =====

**He went to a cow shed on the southern side of the Siṁha-dvāra. There the Lord fell down unconscious among cows from the district of Tailaṅga.**
