====== Cc. Antya 17.67 ======

===== Text =====

> adbhuta nigūḍha premera mādhurya-mahimā
> āpani āsvādi' prabhu dekhāilā sīmā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:n:nigūḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:p:premera]]---of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]-[[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of the sweetness; [[synonyms:ā:āpani]]---personally; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādi']]---tasting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhāilā]]---showed; [[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the extreme limit.

===== Translation =====

**Ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa is wonderfully deep. By personally tasting the glorious sweetness of that love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed us its extreme limit.**
