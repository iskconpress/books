====== CC Antya 17.70 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ 'kūrmākṛti'-anubhāva
> unmāda-ceṣṭita tāte unmāda-pralāpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---thus I have described; [[synonyms:k:kūrma]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākṛti]]---of becoming like a tortoise; [[synonyms:a:anubhāva]]---the ecstatic symptom; [[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:c:ceṣṭita]]---enacted in madness; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---talking like a madman.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic transformation of becoming like a tortoise. In that ecstasy, He talked and acted like a madman.**
