====== Cc. Antya 17.64 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dine yata haya bhāvera vikāra
> sahasra-mukhe varṇe yadi, nāhi pāya pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---in one day; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---as many as there are; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of ecstasy; [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---transformations; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---possessing thousands of mouths; [[synonyms:v:varṇe]] [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if describes; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---cannot reach; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**Even Anantadeva, who possesses thousands of mouths, cannot fully describe the ecstatic transformations that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu experienced in a single day.**
