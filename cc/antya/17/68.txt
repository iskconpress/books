====== CC Antya 17.68 ======

===== Text =====

> adbhuta-dayālu caitanya---adbhuta-vadānya!
> aiche dayālu dātā loke nāhi śuni anya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderfully; [[synonyms:d:dayālu]]---merciful; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]-[[synonyms:v:vadānya]]---wonderfully magnanimous; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:d:dayālu]]---merciful; [[synonyms:d:dātā]]---charitable person; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---within this world; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---we have heard of; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is wonderfully merciful and wonderfully magnanimous. We have heard of no one else within this world so merciful and charitable.**
