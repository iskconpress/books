====== Cc. Antya 17.65 ======

===== Text =====

> jīva dīna ki karibe tāhāra varṇana?
> śākhā-candra-nyāya kari' dig-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīva]]---a living entity; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]---very poor; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---will do; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:n:nyāya]]---the logic of showing the moon through the branches of a tree; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---I make; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing the direction.

===== Translation =====

**What can a poor creature like me describe of those transformations? I can give only a hint of them, as if showing the moon through the branches of a tree.**
