====== CC Antya 17.58 ======

===== Text =====

> autsukyera prāvīṇye, jiti' anya bhāva-sainye,
> udaya haila nija-rājya-mane
> mane ha-ila lālasa, nā haya āpana-vaśa,
> duḥkhe mane karena bhartsane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:autsukyera]]---of eagerness; [[synonyms:p:prāvīṇye]]---because of high development; [[synonyms:j:jiti']]---conquering; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:s:sainye]]---soldiers of ecstasy; [[synonyms:u:udaya]]---arising; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:r:rājya]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the kingdom of Her own mind; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:l:lālasa]]---greed; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---within Her own control; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in unhappiness; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---to the mind; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhartsane]]---chastisement.

===== Translation =====

**"Then great eagerness conquered all the other soldiers of ecstasy, and an uncontrollable desire arose in the kingdom of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's mind. Greatly unhappy, She then chastised Her own mind.**
