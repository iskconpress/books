====== Cc. Antya 17.66 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā yei śune, tāra juḍāya mana-kāṇa
> alaukika gūḍha-prema-ceṣṭā haya jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---anyone who hears; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:j:juḍāya]]---become satisfied; [[synonyms:m:mana]]-[[synonyms:k:kāṇa]]---mind and ears; [[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---of deep ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---activities; [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---he can understand.

===== Translation =====

**This description, however, will satisfy the mind and ears of anyone who hears it, and he will be able to understand these uncommon activities of deep ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
