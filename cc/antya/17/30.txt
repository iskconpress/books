====== CC Antya 17.30 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi prabhura bhāva jāniyā
> bhāgavatera śloka paḍe madhura kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the emotion; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---understanding; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavatera]]---of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---a verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:m:madhura]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---in a sweet voice.

===== Translation =====

**Understanding the ecstatic emotions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Svarūpa Dāmodara, in a sweet voice, recited the following verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.**
