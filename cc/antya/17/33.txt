====== CC Antya 17.33 ======

===== Text =====

> haila gopī-bhāvāveśa, kaila rāse paraveśa,
> kṛṣṇera śuni' upekṣā-vacana
> kṛṣṇera mukha-hāsya-vāṇī, tyāge tāhā satya māni',
> roṣe kṛṣṇe dena olāhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:r:rāse]]---in the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:p:paraveśa]]---entrance; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:u:upekṣā]]-[[synonyms:v:vacana]]---the words of negligence; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---face; [[synonyms:h:hāsya]]---smiling; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---talking; [[synonyms:t:tyāge]]---renounce; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:m:māni']]---taking as a fact; [[synonyms:r:roṣe]]---in anger; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---give; [[synonyms:o:olāhana]]---chastisement.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "The gopīs entered the arena of the rāsa dance in ecstasy, but after hearing Kṛṣṇa's words of negligence and detachment, they understood that He was going to renounce them. Thus they began to chastise Him in anger.**
