====== CC Antya 17.55 ======

===== Text =====

> "dekhi ei upāye, kṛṣṇa-āśā chāḍi' diye,
> āśā chāḍile sukhī haya mana
> chāḍa' kṛṣṇa-kathā adhanya, kaha anya-kathā dhanya,
> yāte haya kṛṣṇa-vismaraṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:u:upāye]]---this means; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āśā]]---hope for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']] [[synonyms:d:diye]]---I give up; [[synonyms:ā:āśā]]---hope; [[synonyms:c:chāḍile]]---if I give up; [[synonyms:s:sukhī]]---happy; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:c:chāḍa']]---give up; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---talks of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:adhanya]]---most inglorious; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---speak; [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---other topics; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---glorious; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vismaraṇa]]---forgetfulness of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, " 'If I give up hope of meeting Kṛṣṇa, I shall then be happy. Therefore, let us stop this most inglorious discussion of Kṛṣṇa. It would be better for us to talk of glorious topics and forget Him.'**
