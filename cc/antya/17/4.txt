====== CC Antya 17.4 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina prabhu svarūpa-rāmānanda-saṅge
> ardha-rātri goṅāilā kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:r:rātri]]---half the night; [[synonyms:g:goṅāilā]]---passed; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---of discussing Kṛṣṇa's pastimes; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the matter.

===== Translation =====

**In the company of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu once passed half the night talking about the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
