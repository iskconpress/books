====== CC Antya 17.62 ======

===== Text =====

> kṣaṇeke prabhura bāhya haila, svarūpere ājñā dila,
> "svarūpa, kichu kara madhura gāna"
> svarūpa gāya vidyāpati, gīta-govinda-gīti,
> śuni' prabhura juḍāila kāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeke]]---within a moment; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---external consciousness; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:svarūpere]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---He ordered Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---My dear Svarūpa; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---make; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---songs; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sings; [[synonyms:v:vidyāpati]]---songs by Vidyāpati; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:g:gīti]]---songs from //Gīta-govinda//; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:juḍāila]]---became satisfied; [[synonyms:k:kāṇa]]---ears.

===== Translation =====

**Suddenly Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to external consciousness and said to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, "My dear Svarūpa, please sing some sweet songs." The Lord's ears were satisfied when He heard Svarūpa Dāmodara sing songs from Gīta-govinda and those by the poet Vidyāpati.**
