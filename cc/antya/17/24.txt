====== CC Antya 17.24 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅketa-veṇu-nāde rādhā āni' kuñja-ghare
> kuñjere calilā kṛṣṇa krīḍā karibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅketa]]-[[synonyms:v:veṇu]]-[[synonyms:n:nāde]]---by the signal of the vibration of the flute; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:k:kuñja]]-[[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to a bower; [[synonyms:k:kuñjere]]---within the bower; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]] [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to perform pastimes.

===== Translation =====

**"He brought Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī to a bower by signaling with His flute. Then He entered within that bower to perform pastimes with Her.**
