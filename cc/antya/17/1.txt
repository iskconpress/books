====== CC Antya 17.1 ======

===== Text =====

> likhyate śrīla-gaurendor
> atyadbhutam alaukikam
> yair dṛṣṭaṁ tan-mukhāc chrutvā
> divyonmāda-viceṣṭitam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:likhyate]]---they are being written; [[synonyms:ś:śrīla]]---most opulent; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:i:indoḥ]]---moonlike; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very; [[synonyms:a:adbhutam]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:a:alaukikam]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:y:yaiḥ]]---by whom; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭam]]---personally seen; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhāt]]---from their mouths; [[synonyms:ś:śrutvā]]---after hearing; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāda]]---in transcendental madness; [[synonyms:v:viceṣṭitam]]---activities.

===== Translation =====

**I am simply trying to write about Lord Gauracandra's transcendental activities and spiritual madness, which are very wonderful and uncommon. I dare to write of them only because I have heard from the mouths of those who have personally seen the Lord's activities.**
