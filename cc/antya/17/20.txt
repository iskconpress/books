====== Cc. Antya 17.20 ======

===== Text =====

> ucca kari' śravaṇe kare nāma-saṅkīrtana
> aneka-kṣaṇe mahāprabhu pāilā cetana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ucca]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---very loudly; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---in the ears; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---perform; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy name; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---after a considerable time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]] [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---returned to consciousness.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees began to chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra very loudly, and after a considerable time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu regained consciousness.**
