====== CC Antya 17.56 ======

===== Text =====

> kahitei ha-ila smṛti, citte haila kṛṣṇa-sphūrti,
> sakhīre kahe hañā vismite
> "yāre cāhi chāḍite, sei śuñā āche citte,
> kona rīte nā pāri chāḍite"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kahitei]]---while speaking; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:smṛti]]---remembrance; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---the appearance of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sakhīre]]---to the friends; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:h:hañā]] [[synonyms:v:vismite]]---being very astonished; [[synonyms:y:yāre]]---He whom; [[synonyms:c:cāhi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍite]]---I want to give up; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:ś:śuñā]] [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---is lying; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:r:rīte]]---by any process; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am not able; [[synonyms:c:chāḍite]]---to give up.

===== Translation =====

**"While speaking in this way, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī suddenly remembered Kṛṣṇa. Indeed, He appeared within Her heart. Greatly astonished, She told Her friends, 'The person I want to forget is lying in My heart.'**
