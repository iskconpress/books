====== CC Antya 17.71 ======

===== Text =====

> ei līlā sva-granthe raghunātha-dāsa
> gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣe kairāchena prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---this pastime; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:g:granthe]]---in his book; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:stava]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]]---named //Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa//; [[synonyms:k:kairāchena]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---has fully described.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī has fully described this pastime in his book Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa.**
