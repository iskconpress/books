====== CC Antya 17.13 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā govinda mahāprabhura śabda nā pāñā
> svarūpere bolāila kapāṭa khuliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śabda]]---sound; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---not getting; [[synonyms:s:svarūpere]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bolāila]]---called for; [[synonyms:k:kapāṭa]]---the doors; [[synonyms:k:khuliyā]]---opening.

===== Translation =====

**Meanwhile, not hearing any sounds from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Govinda immediately sent for Svarūpa Dāmodara and opened the doors.**
