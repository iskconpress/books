====== Cc. Antya 17.28 ======

===== Text =====

> śunite nā pāinu sei amṛta-sama vāṇī
> śunite nā pāinu bhūṣaṇa-muralīra dhvani"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śunite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I could not hear; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---exactly like nectar; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---voices; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I could not hear; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣaṇa]]---of ornaments; [[synonyms:m:muralīra]]---of the flute; [[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---vibration.

===== Translation =====

**"Because you brought Me back here, I could no longer hear the nectarean voices of Kṛṣṇa and the gopīs, nor could I hear the sounds of their ornaments or the flute."**
