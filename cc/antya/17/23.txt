====== Cc. Antya 17.23 ======

===== Text =====

> veṇu-śabda śuni' āmi gelāṅa vṛndāvana
> dekhi,---goṣṭhe veṇu bājāya vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:veṇu]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabda]]---the vibration of the flute; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---after hearing; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:g:gelāṅa]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I saw; [[synonyms:g:goṣṭhe]]---in the pasturing field; [[synonyms:v:veṇu]]---the flute; [[synonyms:b:bājāya]]---played; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Kṛṣṇa, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

===== Translation =====

**"After hearing the vibration of a flute, I went to Vṛndāvana, and there I saw that Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda, was playing on His flute in the pasturing grounds.**
