====== CC Antya 17.11 ======

===== Text =====

> tina-dvāre kapāṭa aiche āche ta' lāgiyā
> bhāvāveśe prabhu gelā bāhira hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---in three doorways; [[synonyms:k:kapāṭa]]---the doors; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---as previously; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---are; [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---being closed; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:b:bāhira]]---out; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being.

===== Translation =====

**All three doors were fastened as usual, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, in great ecstasy, nevertheless got out of the room and left the house.**
