====== CC Antya 17.14 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe svarūpa-gosāñi saṅge lañā bhakta-gaṇa
> deuṭi jvāliyā karena prabhura anveṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with him; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:d:deuṭi]]---lamp; [[synonyms:j:jvāliyā]]---burning; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:anveṣaṇa]]---searching.

===== Translation =====

**Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī lit a torch, and went out with all the devotees to search for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
