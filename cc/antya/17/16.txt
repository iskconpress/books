====== CC Antya 17.16 ======

===== Text =====

> peṭera bhitara hasta-pada---kūrmera ākāra
> mukhe phena, pulakāṅga, netre aśru-dhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:peṭera]]---the abdomen; [[synonyms:b:bhitara]]---within; [[synonyms:h:hasta]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]---the arms and legs; [[synonyms:k:kūrmera]] [[synonyms:ā:ākāra]]---just like a tortoise; [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---in the mouth; [[synonyms:p:phena]]---foam; [[synonyms:p:pulaka]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---eruptions on the body; [[synonyms:n:netre]]---in the eyes; [[synonyms:a:aśru]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---a flow of tears.

===== Translation =====

**His arms and legs had entered the trunk of His body, exactly like those of a tortoise. His mouth was foaming, there were eruptions on His body, and tears flowed from His eyes.**
