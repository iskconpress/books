====== Cc. Antya 17.29 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāvāveśe svarūpe kahena gadgada-vāṇī
> 'karṇa tṛṣṇāya mare, paḍa rasāyana, śuni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in great ecstasy; [[synonyms:s:svarūpe]]---to Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:g:gadgada]]-[[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---in a faltering voice; [[synonyms:k:karṇa]]---the ears; [[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇāya]]---because of thirst; [[synonyms:m:mare]]---dies; [[synonyms:p:paḍa]]---recite; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āyana]]---something relishable; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---let Me hear.

===== Translation =====

**In great ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to Svarūpa Dāmodara in a faltering voice, "My ears are dying of thirst. Please recite something to quench this thirst. Let me hear it."**
