====== CC Antya 17.10 ======

===== Text =====

> ācambite śunena prabhu kṛṣṇa-veṇu-gāna
> bhāvāveśe prabhu tāhāṅ karilā prayāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly; [[synonyms:ś:śunena]]---hears; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:veṇu]]---of Kṛṣṇa's flute; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---the vibration; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:p:prayāṇa]]---departed.

===== Translation =====

**Suddenly, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard the vibration of Kṛṣṇa's flute. Then, in ecstasy, He began to depart to see Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
