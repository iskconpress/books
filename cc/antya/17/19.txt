====== CC Antya 17.19 ======

===== Text =====

> aneka karilā yatna, nā haya cetana
> prabhure uṭhāñā ghare ānilā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aneka]]---many; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---made; [[synonyms:y:yatna]]---endeavors; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was not; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---consciousness; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---lifting; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---home; [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees tried to rouse the Lord by various means, but His consciousness did not return. Therefore they all lifted Him and brought Him back home.**
