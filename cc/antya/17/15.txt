====== Cc. Antya 17.15 ======

===== Text =====

> iti-uti anveṣiyā siṁha-dvāre gelā
> gābhī-gaṇa-madhye yāi' prabhure pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:u:uti]]---here and there; [[synonyms:a:anveṣiyā]]---searching; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---to the gate named Siṁha-dvāra; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:g:gābhī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---among the cows; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---found Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After searching here and there, they finally came to the cow shed near the Siṁha-dvāra. There they saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lying unconscious among the cows.**
