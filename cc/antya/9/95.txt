====== CC Antya 9.95 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-kṣaṇa prabhura yadi pāiye daraśana
> koṭi-cintāmaṇi-lābha nahe tāra sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for a moment; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---I get; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---an interview; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:c:cintāmaṇi]]-[[synonyms:l:lābha]]---obtaining millions of //cintāmaṇi// stones; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal to that.

===== Translation =====

**"If even for a moment I could get an interview with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, I would not care for the profit of millions of cintāmaṇi stones.**
