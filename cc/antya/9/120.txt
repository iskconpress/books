====== CC Antya 9.120 ======

===== Text =====

> bhavānandera putra saba---mora priyatama
> iṅhā-sabākāre āmi dekhi ātma-sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhavānandera]]---of Bhavānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:putra]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all the sons; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---to me; [[synonyms:p:priyatama]]---very dear; [[synonyms:i:iṅhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabākāre]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---see; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---as my relatives.

===== Translation =====

**"Inform Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that all the sons of Bhavānanda Rāya are especially dear to me. I consider them like members of my family.**
