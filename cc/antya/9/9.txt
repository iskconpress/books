====== Cc. Antya 9.9 ======

===== Text =====

> sapta-dvīpe nava-khaṇḍe vaise yata jana
> nānā-veśe āsi' kare prabhura daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:d:dvīpe]]---in seven islands; [[synonyms:n:nava]]-[[synonyms:k:khaṇḍe]]---in nine //khaṇḍas//; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---reside; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---all persons; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:v:veśe]]---in different dresses; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Dressed in different ways, people from the seven islands and nine khaṇḍas visited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
