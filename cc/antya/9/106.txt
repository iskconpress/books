====== Cc. Antya 9.106 ======

===== Text =====

> āra bāra aiche nā khāiha rāja-dhana
> āji haite diluṅ tomāya dviguṇa vartana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---another time; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---like this; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:khāiha]]---do not misappropriate; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---government revenue; [[synonyms:ā:āji]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from today; [[synonyms:d:diluṅ]]---I award; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---unto you; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vartana]]---twice the salary.

===== Translation =====

**"Do not again misappropriate the revenue of the government. In case you think your salary insufficient, henceforward it will be doubled."**
