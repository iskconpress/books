====== Cc. Antya 9.34 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle āra loka āila dhāñā
> 'vāṇīnāthādi savaṁśe lañā gela bāndhiyā'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---person; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---in great haste; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with his family; [[synonyms:g:gela]] [[synonyms:b:bāndhiyā]]---was arrested.

===== Translation =====

**At that time another person came there in great haste, bringing the news that Vāṇīnātha Rāya and his entire family had been arrested.**
