====== Cc. Antya 9.47 ======

===== Text =====

> viśeṣa tāhāra ṭhāñi kauḍi bākī haya
> prāṇa nile kibā lābha? nija dhana-kṣaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---particular; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from him; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]]---of //kauḍis//; [[synonyms:b:bākī]]---debt; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]] [[synonyms:n:nile]]---if he is killed; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---what; [[synonyms:l:lābha]]---profit; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---own; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---wealth; [[synonyms:k:kṣaya]]---loss.

===== Translation =====

**"His only fault is that he owes some money to the government. If he is killed, however, what profit will there be? The government will be the loser, for it will not get the money.**
