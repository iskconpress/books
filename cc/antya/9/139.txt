{{description>"Kindly bestow upon me Your pure mercy so that I may also become renounced. I am no longer interested in material enjoyment."}}

====== Cc. Antya 9.139 ======

===== Text =====

> śuddha kṛpā kara, gosāñi, ghucāha 'viṣaya'
> nirviṇṇa ha-inu, mote 'viṣaya' nā haya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuddha]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---pure mercy; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---kindly bestow; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:g:ghucāha]] [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---let me be free from all these material opulences; [[synonyms:n:nirviṇṇa]]---detached; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:inu]]---I have become; [[synonyms:m:mote]] [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---I am no longer interested in material opulences.

===== Translation =====

**"Kindly bestow upon me Your pure mercy so that I may also become renounced. I am no longer interested in material enjoyment."**
