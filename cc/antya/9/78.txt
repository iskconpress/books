====== Cc. Antya 9.78 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā tumi vasi' raha, kene yābe ālālanātha?
> keha tomā nā śunābe viṣayīra vāt

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---residing; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---kindly stay; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:y:yābe]]---shall You go; [[synonyms:ā:ālālanātha]]---to Ālālanātha; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ś:śunābe]]---no one will inform You; [[synonyms:v:viṣayīra]] [[synonyms:v:vāt]]---about the affairs of materialistic persons.

===== Translation =====

**"Kindly stay here at Jagannātha Purī. Why should You go to Ālālanātha? Henceforward, no one will approach You about material affairs."**
