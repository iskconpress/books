====== CC Antya 9.74 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra duḥkha dekhi' tāra sevakādi-gaṇa
> tomāre jānāila,---yāte 'ananya-śaraṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---distressed condition; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---servants and friends; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]] [[synonyms:j:jānāila]]---informed You; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---because; [[synonyms:a:ananya]]---no other; [[synonyms:ś:śaraṇa]]---shelter.

===== Translation =====

**"It is not Gopīnātha who sent all those men so that You would release him from his plight. Rather, his friends and servants, seeing his distressed condition, informed You because they all knew that Gopīnātha is a soul surrendered unto You.**
