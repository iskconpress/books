====== Cc. Antya 9.152 ======

===== Text =====

> yei ihāṅ śune prabhura vātsalya-prakāśa
> prema-bhakti pāya, tāṅra vipada yāya nāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]]---one who; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---manifestation of special affection; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---loving devotional service; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---attains; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:v:vipada]]---dangerous condition of life; [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---is destroyed.

===== Translation =====

**Whether or not one understands it, if one hears of this incident concerning Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka's activities and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's causeless mercy upon him, certainly he will be promoted to the platform of ecstatic love for the Lord, and for him all dangers will be nullified.**
