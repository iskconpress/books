====== CC Antya 9.59 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle kāśī-miśra āilā prabhu-sthāne
> prabhu tāṅre kahe kichu sodvega-vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:u:udvega]]---with anxiety; [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**At that time Kāśī Miśra came to the residence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord talked with him with some agitation.**
