====== CC Antya 9.102 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe, "kauḍi chāḍimu,---ihā nā kahibā
> sahaje mora priya tā'rā,---ihā jānāibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King said; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍimu]]---I shall excuse all the dues; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahibā]]---do not speak; [[synonyms:s:sahaje]]---naturally; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:p:priya]]---my dear friends; [[synonyms:t:tā'rā]]---they; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jānāibā]]---let Him know.

===== Translation =====

**The King said, "I shall absolve Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka of all his debts, but don't speak of this to the Lord. Simply let Him know that all the family members of Bhavānanda Rāya and Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka are naturally my dear friends.**
