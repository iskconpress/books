====== CC Antya 9.30 ======

===== Text =====

> rāja-putra āsi' tāre cāṅge caḍāila
> khaḍaga-upare phelāite tale khaḍaga pātila"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---the prince; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:c:cāṅge]]---on the //cāṅga//; [[synonyms:c:caḍāila]]---raised; [[synonyms:k:khaḍaga]]-[[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon the swords; [[synonyms:p:phelāite]]---to throw; [[synonyms:t:tale]]---below; [[synonyms:k:khaḍaga]] [[synonyms:p:pātila]]---he spread swords.

===== Translation =====

**"Thus the prince went back, raised Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka onto the platform of the cāṅga and spread swords below upon which to throw him."**
