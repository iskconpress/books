====== CC Antya 9.66 ======

===== Text =====

> viṣayīra vārtā śuni' kṣubdha haya mana
> tāte ihāṅ rahi' mora nāhi prayojana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṣayīra]]---of materialistic persons; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kṣubdha]]---agitated; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---becomes; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---to stay here; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:prayojana]]---there is no necessity.

===== Translation =====

**"If we hear about the activities of materialistic persons, our minds become agitated. There is no need for Me to stay here and be disturbed in that way."**
