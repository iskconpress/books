====== Cc. Antya 9.101 ======

===== Text =====

> miśra kahe, "kauḍi chāḍibā,---nahe prabhura mane
> kauḍi chāḍile prabhu kadācit duḥkha māne"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Kāśī Miśra said; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍibā]]---you will excuse all dues; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---the thought of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍile]]---if you excuse all the dues; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kadācit]]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---will be sorry.

===== Translation =====

**Kāśī Miśra said, "Excusing Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka of all his debts will not make the Lord happy, for that is not His intention."**
