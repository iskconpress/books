====== CC Antya 9.84 ======

===== Text =====

> "deva, śuna āra eka aparūpa vāt!
> mahāprabhu kṣetra chāḍi' yābena ālālanātha!"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deva]]---my dear King; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:a:aparūpa]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:v:vāt]]---news; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṣetra]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---leaving Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:y:yābena]]---will go; [[synonyms:ā:ālālanātha]]---to Ālālanātha.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear King," he said, "please hear one uncommon item of news. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wants to leave Jagannātha Purī and go to Ālālanātha."**
