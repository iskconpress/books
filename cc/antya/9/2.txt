====== CC Antya 9.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya dayāmaya
> jaya jaya nityānanda karuṇa-hṛdaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:k:karuṇa]]-[[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---whose heart is very compassionate.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the most merciful incarnation! All glories to Lord Nityānanda, whose heart is always compassionate!**
