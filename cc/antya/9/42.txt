====== Cc. Antya 9.42 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' prabhura gaṇa prabhure kare anunaya
> prabhu kahe,---"āmi bhikṣuka, āmā haite kichu naya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees of the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:a:anunaya]]---entreated the Lord; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:b:bhikṣuka]]---I am a beggar; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:n:naya]]---it is not possible for Me to do anything.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this news, all the devotees again appealed to the Lord, but the Lord replied, "I am a beggar. It is impossible for Me to do anything about this.**
