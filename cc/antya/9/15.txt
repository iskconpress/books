====== Cc. Antya 9.15 ======

===== Text =====

> savaṁśe tomāra sevaka---bhavānanda-rāya
> tāṅra putra---tomāra sevake rākhite yuyāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---with his family; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---servant; [[synonyms:b:bhavānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Bhavānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---his son; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sevake]]---Your servant; [[synonyms:r:rākhite]]---to protect; [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---is quite fit.

===== Translation =====

**"Bhavānanda Rāya and his entire family are your servants. Therefore it is quite fitting for You to save the son of Bhavānanda Rāya."**
