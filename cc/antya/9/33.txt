====== CC Antya 9.33 ======

===== Text =====

> yei catura, sei kuruka rāja-viṣaya
> rāja-dravya śodhi' pāya, tāra karuka vyaya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]]---one who; [[synonyms:c:catura]]---intelligent; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---such a person; [[synonyms:k:kuruka]]---let him do; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---government service; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:ś:śodhi']]---after paying the dues of the government; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---whatever he gets; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:k:karuka]] [[synonyms:v:vyaya]]---let him spend that.

===== Translation =====

**"If one is intelligent, let him perform service to the government, and after paying the government, he can spend whatever money is left."**
