====== CC Antya 9.11 ======

===== Text =====

> bāhire phukāre loka, darśana nā pāñā
> 'kṛṣṇa kaha' balena prabhu bāhire āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---outside; [[synonyms:p:phu]]-[[synonyms:k:kāre]]---with a tumultuous sound; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---not being able to see; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---chant Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming outside.

===== Translation =====

**Being unable to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the populace outside His room would make a tumultuous sound. Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go outside and tell them, "Chant Hare Kṛṣṇa."**
