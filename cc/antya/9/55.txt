====== CC Antya 9.55 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā prabhu sei manuṣyere praśna kaila
> "vāṇīnātha ki kare, yabe bāndhiyā ānila?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:manuṣyere]]---unto that person who brought the message; [[synonyms:p:praśna]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]] [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---what was Vāṇīnātha doing; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:b:bāndhiyā]] [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---he was arrested and brought there.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired from the messenger, "What was Vāṇīnātha doing when he was arrested and brought there?"**
