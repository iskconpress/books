====== Cc. Antya 9.143 ======

===== Text =====

> rājāra mūla-dhana diyā ye kichu labhya haya
> sei dhana kariha nānā dharme-karme vyaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājāra]]---of the King; [[synonyms:m:mūla]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---revenue; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---after paying; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:l:labhya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---whatever is obtained; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dhana]]---money; [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:d:dharme]]-[[synonyms:k:karme]] [[synonyms:v:vyaya]]---spend in various types of religious and fruitive activities.

===== Translation =====

**"First you should pay the revenue due the King, and then you may spend the balance for religious and fruitive activities.**
