====== Cc. Antya 9.115 ======

===== Text =====

> ke kahite pāre gaurera āścarya svabhāva?
> brahmā-śiva ādi yāṅra nā pāya antarbhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can estimate; [[synonyms:g:gaurera]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]] [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---wonderful characteristics; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiva]]---Lord Brahmā, Lord Śiva; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---cannot understand; [[synonyms:a:antarbhāva]]---the intention.

===== Translation =====

**No one can estimate the wonderful characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Even Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva cannot understand the intentions of the Lord.**
