====== CC Antya 9.83 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā miśrera caraṇa yabe cāpite lāgilā
> tabe miśra tāṅre kichu bhaṅgīte kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāja]]---the King; [[synonyms:m:miśrera]]---of Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:c:cāpite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to press; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that opportunity; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgīte]]---by a hint; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---informed.

===== Translation =====

**When the King began pressing his lotus feet, Kāśī Miśra informed him about something through hints.**
