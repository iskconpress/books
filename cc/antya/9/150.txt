====== CC Antya 9.150 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśī-miśre nā sādhila, rājāre nā sādhila
> udyoga vinā mahāprabhu eta phala dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśre]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:s:sādhila]]---He did not request; [[synonyms:r:rājāre]]---the King; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:s:sādhila]]---He did not request; [[synonyms:u:udyoga]] [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without endeavor; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---such; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---result; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave so much to Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka without directly making requests of either Kāśī Miśra or the King.**
