====== CC Antya 9.61 ======

===== Text =====

> bhavānanda-rāyera goṣṭhī kare rāja-viṣaya
> nānā-prakāre kare tārā rāja-dravya vyaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhavānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāyera]]---of Bhavānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---the family; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---engages; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---in government service; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāre]]---in various ways; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:t:tārā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:v:vyaya]]---spending the government's revenues.

===== Translation =====

**"All the family members of Bhavānanda Rāya are engaged in government service, but they spend the government's revenue in various ways.**
