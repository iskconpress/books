====== CC Antya 9.88 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ajitendriya hañā kare rāja-viṣaya
> nānā asat-pathe kare rāja-dravya vyaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ajitendriya]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being mad after sense gratification; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---serves the government; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:a:asat]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---in various sinful activities; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:v:vyaya]]---spends the revenue of government.

===== Translation =====

**"Because he is mad after sense gratification,' the Lord said, 'he acts as a government servant but spends the government's revenue for various sinful activities.**
