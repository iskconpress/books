====== CC Antya 9.105 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,---"saba kauḍi tomāre chāḍiluṅ
> sei mālajāṭhyā daṇḍa pāṭa tomāre ta' diluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King said; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]]---money; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---unto you; [[synonyms:c:chāḍiluṅ]]---I excuse; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:mālajāṭhyā]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]] [[synonyms:p:pāṭa]]---the place called Mālajāṭhyā Daṇḍapāṭa; [[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---to you; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:diluṅ]]---I give.

===== Translation =====

**The King told Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka, "You are excused for all the money you owe the treasury, and the place known as Mālajāṭhyā Daṇḍapāṭa is again given to you for collections.**
