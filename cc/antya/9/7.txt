====== CC Antya 9.7 ======

===== Text =====

> trijagatera loka āsi' karena daraśana
> yei dekhe, sei pāya kṛṣṇa-prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the three worlds; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visited; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---anyone who saw; [[synonyms:s:seipāya]]---he got; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the transcendental treasure of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**People from the three worlds used to come visit Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Anyone who saw Him received the transcendental treasure of love for Kṛṣṇa.**
