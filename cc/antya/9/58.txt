====== CC Antya 9.58 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' mahāprabhu ha-ilā parama ānanda
> ke bujhite pāre gaurera kṛpā-chanda-bandha?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:b:bujhite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---who can understand; [[synonyms:g:gaurera]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:c:chanda]]-[[synonyms:b:bandha]]---mercy upon His devotee.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this news, the Lord was very pleased. Who can understand the mercy of the Lord upon His devotee?**
