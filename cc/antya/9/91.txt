====== CC Antya 9.91 ======

===== Text =====

> nija-kauḍi māge, rājā nāhi kare daṇḍa
> rājā---mahā-dhārmika, ei haya pāpī bhaṇḍa!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:k:kauḍi]]---his own money; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---demands; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---does not punish; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:dhārmika]]---very religious; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this man; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:p:pāpī]]---sinful; [[synonyms:b:bhaṇḍa]]---cheat.

===== Translation =====

**"The King wanted his revenue paid and did not want to enforce punishment. Therefore the King is certainly very religious. But Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka is a great cheat.**
