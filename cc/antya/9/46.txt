====== CC Antya 9.46 ======

===== Text =====

> "gopīnātha-paṭṭanāyaka---sevaka tomāra
> sevakera prāṇa-daṇḍa nahe vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:p:paṭṭanāyaka]]---Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]] [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your servant; [[synonyms:s:sevakera]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---to condemn a servant to death; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---good behavior.

===== Translation =====

**"After all," he said, "Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka is your faithful servant. To condemn a servant to death is not good behavior.**
