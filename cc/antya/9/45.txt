====== Cc. Antya 9.45 ======

===== Text =====

> ihāṅ yadi mahāprabhu eteka kahilā
> haricandana-pātra yāi' rājāre kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:eteka]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---spoke like this; [[synonyms:h:haricandana]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the officer named Haricandana Pātra; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:r:rājāre]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---informed the King.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied in this way, an officer named Haricandana Pātra went to the King and spoke with him.**
