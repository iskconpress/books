====== Cc. Antya 9.108 ======

===== Text =====

> paramārthe prabhura kṛpā, seha rahu dūre
> ananta tāhāra phala, ke balite pāre?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paramārthe]]---for spiritual advancement; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that; [[synonyms:r:rahu]] [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---let alone; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---unlimited; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---result; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:b:balite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can estimate.

===== Translation =====

**By the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, one can certainly become spiritually advanced. Indeed, no one can estimate the results of His mercy.**
