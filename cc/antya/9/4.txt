====== Cc. Antya 9.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu bhakta-gaṇa-saṅge
> nīlācale vāsa karena kṛṣṇa-prema-raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with His devotees; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---resides; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---overwhelmed by the ecstatic love of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lived at Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī] with His personal devotees, always merged in ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
