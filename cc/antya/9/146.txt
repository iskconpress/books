====== Cc. Antya 9.146 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāya āliṅgiyā prabhu vidāya yabe dilā
> hari-dhvani kari' saba bhakta uṭhi' gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---all of them; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]]---farewell; [[synonyms:y:yabe]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---when He gave; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---chanting the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all devotees; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---left.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced them all and bade them farewell. Then all the devotees got up and left, loudly chanting the holy name of Hari.**
