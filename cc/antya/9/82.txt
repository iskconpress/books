====== Cc. Antya 9.82 ======

===== Text =====

> nitya āsi' kare miśrera pāda saṁvāhana
> jagannātha-sevāra kare bhiyāna śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nitya]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming daily; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:m:miśrera]]---of Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]---the feet; [[synonyms:s:saṁvāhana]]---massaging; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:s:sevāra]]---for the service of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhiyāna]]---arrangements; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hearing.

===== Translation =====

**He would come daily to the house of Kāśī Miśra to massage his lotus feet. The King would also hear from him about how opulently Lord Jagannātha was served.**
