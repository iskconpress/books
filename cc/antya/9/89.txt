====== Cc. Antya 9.89 ======

===== Text =====

> brahmasva-adhika ei haya rāja-dhana
> tāhā hari' bhoga kare mahā-pāpī jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:brahmasva]]---a //brāhmaṇa's// property; [[synonyms:a:adhika]]---more than; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the revenue of the government; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:h:hari']]---stealing that; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---enjoys sense gratification; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāpī]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---a most sinful person.

===== Translation =====

**"The revenue of the government is more sacred than the property of a brāhmaṇa. One who misappropriates the government's money and uses it to enjoy sense gratification is most sinful.**
