====== Cc. Antya 9.6 ======

===== Text =====

> dine nṛtya-kīrtana, jagannātha-daraśana
> rātrye rāya-svarūpa-sane rasa-āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dine]]---during the day; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---dancing and chanting; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---at night; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**During the day He chanted, danced and saw Lord Jagannātha in the temple. At night He tasted transcendental bliss in the company of Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
