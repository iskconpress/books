====== CC Antya 9.111 ======

===== Text =====

> kāhāṅ sarvasva veci' laya, deyā nā yāya kauḍi!
> kāhāṅ dviguṇa vartana, parāya neta-dhaḍi!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---on one hand; [[synonyms:s:sarvasva]]---all possessions; [[synonyms:v:veci']]---selling; [[synonyms:l:laya]]---takes; [[synonyms:d:deyā]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---cannot be paid; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]]---the debt; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---on the other hand; [[synonyms:d:dviguṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vartana]]---twice the salary; [[synonyms:p:parāya]]---puts on; [[synonyms:n:neta]]-[[synonyms:d:dhaḍi]]---the silken cover.

===== Translation =====

**On one hand Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka was unable to clear his debt even by selling all his possessions, but on the other his salary was doubled, and he was honored with the silken wrapper.**
