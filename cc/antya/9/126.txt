====== CC Antya 9.126 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhā lāgi' dravya chāḍi'---ihā māt jāne
> 'sahajei mora prīti haya tāhā-sane' "

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for them; [[synonyms:d:dravya]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---I remit the debt; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:m:māt]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---He does not know; [[synonyms:s:sahajei]]---naturally; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:p:prīti]]---my affection; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with them all.

===== Translation =====

**"Because of my intimate relationship with them, I have absolved Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka of all his debts. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu does not know this fact. Whatever I have done is because of my intimate relationship with the family of Bhavānanda Rāya.' "**
