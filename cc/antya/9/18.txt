====== Cc. Antya 9.18 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mālajāṭhyā-daṇḍapāṭe' tāra adhikāra
> sādhi' pāḍi' āni' dravya dila rāja-dvāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālajāṭhyā]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍapāṭe]]---in the place known as Mālajāṭhyā Daṇḍapāta; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:a:adhikāra]]---authority; [[synonyms:s:sādhi']]---soliciting; [[synonyms:p:pāḍi']]---collecting; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---the money; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---gave; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---to the door of the King.

===== Translation =====

**"He served in the place known as Mālajāṭhyā Daṇḍapāta, soliciting and collecting money there and depositing it in the government treasury.**
