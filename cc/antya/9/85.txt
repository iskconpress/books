====== Cc. Antya 9.85 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni rājā duḥkhī hailā, puchilena kāraṇa
> tabe miśra kahe tāṅre saba vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very unhappy; [[synonyms:p:puchilena]]---inquired; [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the reason; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:miśra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Miśra said; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---details.

===== Translation =====

**When the King heard that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was going to Ālālanātha, he was very unhappy and inquired about the reason. Then Kāśī Miśra informed him of all the details.**
