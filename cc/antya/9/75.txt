====== Cc. Antya 9.75 ======

===== Text =====

> sei 'śuddha-bhakta', ye tomā bhaje tomā lāgi'
> āpanāra sukha-duḥkhe haya bhoga-bhogī'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---a pure devotee; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---who; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---worships You; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for Your satisfaction; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāra]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---for personal happiness and distress; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]-[[synonyms:b:bhogī]]---one who wants to enjoy this material world.

===== Translation =====

**"Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka is a pure devotee who worships You only for Your satisfaction. He does not care about his personal happiness or distress, for that is the business of a materialist.**
