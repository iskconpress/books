====== Cc. Antya 9.87 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā kṣobhita haila mahāprabhura mana
> krodhe gopīnāthe kailā bahuta bhartsana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kṣobhita]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became agitated; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind; [[synonyms:k:krodhe]]---in anger; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthe]]---unto Gopīnātha //Paṭṭanāyaka//; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:b:bhartsana]]---much chastisement.

===== Translation =====

**"Hearing about this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was extremely sorry at heart, and in anger He chastised Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka.**
