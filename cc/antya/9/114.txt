====== CC Antya 9.114 ======

===== Text =====

> viṣaya-sukha dite prabhura nāhi manobala
> nivedana-prabhāveha tabu phale eta phala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---of material opulence; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---award; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---is not; [[synonyms:m:manobala]]---desire; [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhāveha]]---simply because He was informed about it; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:p:phale]] [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:p:phala]]---so much of a result was obtained.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord had no intention to award His devotee the happiness of material opulence, yet simply because of His being informed, such a great result was obtained.**
