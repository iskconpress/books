====== CC Antya 9.70 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā lāgi' rāmānanda rājya tyāga kailā
> tomā lāgi' sanātana 'viṣaya' chāḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for Your sake; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:r:rājya]]---the kingdom; [[synonyms:t:tyāga]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---gave up; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for Your sake; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---material life; [[synonyms:c:chāḍilā]]---gave up.

===== Translation =====

**"It is only for Your sake that Rāmānanda Rāya resigned from the governorship of South India and Sanātana Gosvāmī gave up his post as minister.**
