====== CC Antya 9.51 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe haricandana āsi' jānāre kahila
> cāṅge haite gopīnāthe śīghra nāmāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:h:haricandana]]---Haricandana Pātra; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:j:jānāre]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---informed the prince; [[synonyms:c:cāṅge]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the //cāṅga// platform; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthe]]---Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very soon; [[synonyms:n:nāmāila]]---took down.

===== Translation =====

**Haricandana then returned and informed the prince of the King's desire, and immediately Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka was taken down from the cāṅga.**
