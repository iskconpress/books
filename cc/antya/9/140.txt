====== Cc. Antya 9.140 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---sannyāsī yabe ha-ibā pañca-jana
> kuṭumba-bāhulya tomāra ke kare bharaṇa?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---//sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibā]]---will be; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---five persons; [[synonyms:k:kuṭumba]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhulya]]---the many members of the family; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:b:bharaṇa]]---will maintain.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "If you all adopt the renounced order and lose interest in dealing with pounds, shillings and pence, who will take charge of maintaining your large family?**
