====== CC Antya 9.109 ======

===== Text =====

> 'rājya-viṣaya'-phala ei---kṛpāra 'ābhāse'!
> tāhāra gaṇanā kāro mane nāhi āise!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājya]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]---kingly opulence; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---result; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāra]] [[synonyms:ā:ābhāse]]---by only a glimpse of such mercy; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:g:gaṇanā]]---calculation; [[synonyms:k:kāro]]---of anyone; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---does not come.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka achieved the result of kingly opulence due to but a glimpse of the Lord's mercy. Therefore no one can calculate the full value of His mercy.**
