====== CC Antya 9.32 ======

===== Text =====

> rāja-bilāt sādhi' khāya, nāhi rāja-bhaya
> dārī-nāṭuyāre diyā kare nānā vyaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:b:bilāt]]---money due the King; [[synonyms:s:sādhi']]---collecting; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---he uses; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---without fear of the King; [[synonyms:d:dārī]]-[[synonyms:n:nāṭuyāre]]---to dancing girls; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---giving; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:v:vyaya]]---expenditures.

===== Translation =====

**"Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka is in charge of collecting money on behalf of the government, but he misappropriates it. Not fearing the King, he squanders it to see dancing girls.**
