~~Title:Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya-līlā 9.93~~

====== Cc. Antya 9.93 ======

===== Text =====

> ālālanātha yāi' tāhāṅ niścinte rahimu
> viṣayīra bhāla manda vārtā nā śunimu' "

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ālālanātha]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going to Ālālanātha; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:n:niścinte]] [[synonyms:r:rahimu]]---I shall live peacefully; [[synonyms:v:viṣayīra]]---of materialistic persons; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:m:manda]]---good and bad; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ś:śunimu]]---I shall not hear.

===== Translation =====

"Therefore I shall leave Jagannātha Purī and go to Ālālanātha, where I shall live peacefully and not hear about all these affairs of materialistic people.' "
