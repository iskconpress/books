====== CC Antya 9.97 ======

===== Text =====

> miśra kahe, "kauḍi chāḍibā,---nahe prabhura mana
> tārā duḥkha pāya,---ei nā yāya sahana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśra]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Kāśī Miśra said; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍibā]]---you shall abandon the money; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the desire of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tārā]]---they; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get unhappiness; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:s:sahana]]---is intolerable.

===== Translation =====

**Kāśī Miśra hinted to the King, "It is not the Lord's desire that you forfeit the payment. He is unhappy only because the whole family is troubled."**
