====== CC Antya 9.112 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura icchā nāhi, tāre kauḍi chāḍāibe
> dviguṇa vartana kari' punaḥ 'viṣaya' dibe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---the wish of the Lord; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---was not; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍāibe]]---he be excused from the dues; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---twice as much; [[synonyms:v:vartana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---increasing the salary; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]] [[synonyms:d:dibe]]---he should be appointed to the post.

===== Translation =====

**It was not the desire of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu that Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka be excused of his debt to the government, nor was it His desire that his salary be doubled or that he be reappointed collector at the same place.**
