====== CC Antya 9.64 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣuka sannyāsī āmi nirjana-vāsī
> āmāya duḥkha deya, nija-duḥkha kahi' āsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣuka]]---beggar; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]]---mendicant; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nirjana]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---living in a solitary place; [[synonyms:ā:āmāya]]---to Me; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---they give; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---their own unhappiness; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---speaking; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**"As a beggar sannyāsī, a mendicant, I wish to live alone in a solitary place, but these people come to tell Me about their unhappiness and disturb Me.**
