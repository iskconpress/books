====== CC Antya 9.138 ======

===== Text =====

> rāma-rāye, vāṇīnāthe kailā 'nirviṣaya'
> sei kṛpā mote nāhi, yāte aiche haya!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāye]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnāthe]]---Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---You have made; [[synonyms:n:nirviṣaya]]---free from all material attachment; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---that mercy; [[synonyms:m:mote]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---I have not received; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is.

===== Translation =====

**"Your real mercy has been granted to Rāmānanda Rāya and Vāṇīnātha Rāya, for You have detached them from all material opulence. I think that I have not been favored by such mercy.**
