====== Cc. Antya 9.20 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅha kahe,---"sthūla-dravya nāhi ye gaṇi' diba
> krame-krame veci' kini' dravya bhariba

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅha]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he replied; [[synonyms:s:sthūla]]-[[synonyms:d:dravya]]---cash money; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:g:gaṇi']]---counting; [[synonyms:d:diba]]---I can give; [[synonyms:k:krame]]-[[synonyms:k:krame]]---gradually; [[synonyms:v:veci']] [[synonyms:k:kini']]---purchasing and selling; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---goods; [[synonyms:b:bhariba]]---I shall fill.

===== Translation =====

**"Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka replied,'There is no money I can immediately give you in cash. Please give me time. Gradually I shall purchase and sell my gross goods and in this way fill your treasury.**
