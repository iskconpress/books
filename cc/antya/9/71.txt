====== CC Antya 9.71 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā lāgi' raghunātha sakala chāḍila
> hethāya tāhāra pitā viṣaya pāṭhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for Your sake; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍila]]---gave up everything; [[synonyms:h:hethāya]]---here; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]] [[synonyms:p:pitā]]---his father; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]] [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent money.

===== Translation =====

**"It is for Your sake that Raghunātha dāsa gave up all his family relationships. His father sent money and men here to serve him.**
