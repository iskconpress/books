====== Cc. Antya 9.57 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkhyā lāgi' dui-hāte aṅgulīte lekhā
> sahasrādi pūrṇa haile, aṅge kāṭe rekhā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkhyā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for counting; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in two hands; [[synonyms:a:aṅgulīte]]---on the fingers; [[synonyms:l:lekhā]]---marking; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---one thousand times; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]] [[synonyms:h:haile]]---when finished; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:k:kāṭe]] [[synonyms:r:rekhā]]---makes a mark.

===== Translation =====

**"He counted the chants on the fingers of both hands, and after he had finished chanting one thousand times, he would make a mark on his body."**
