====== CC Antya 9.98 ======

===== Text =====

> rājā kahe,---"tāre āmi duḥkha nāhi diye
> cāṅge caḍā, khaḍge ḍārā,---āmi nā jāniye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājā]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---the King replied; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---to him; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:d:diye]]---have no desire to give; [[synonyms:c:cāṅge]] [[synonyms:c:caḍā]]---the raising on the //cāṅga//; [[synonyms:k:khaḍge]]---on the swords; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍārā]]---the throwing; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāniye]]---did not know.

===== Translation =====

**The King replied, "I have no desire to give pain to Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka and his family, nor did I know about his being lifted on the cāṅga to be thrown on the swords and killed.**
