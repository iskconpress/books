====== CC Antya 9.125 ======

===== Text =====

> 'jānā' eta kailā,---ihā mui nāhi jānoṅ
> bhavānandera putra-sabe ātma-sama mānoṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jānā]]---the prince; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---such; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---has done; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mui]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jānoṅ]]---did not know; [[synonyms:b:bhavānandera]] [[synonyms:p:putra]]---the sons of Bhavānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]] [[synonyms:m:mānoṅ]]---I considered like my relatives.

===== Translation =====

**"The prince created this situation without my knowledge, but actually I consider all the sons of Bhavānanda Rāya to be like my relatives.**
