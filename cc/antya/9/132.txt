====== Cc. Antya 9.132 ======

===== Text =====

> 'netadhaṭī'-māthe gopīnātha caraṇe paḍilā
> rājāra kṛpā-vṛttānta sakala kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:netadhaṭī]]-[[synonyms:m:māthe]]---with the silken cover on the head; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]]---of the King; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]]---story of the mercy; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---narrated.

===== Translation =====

**Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka, his head covered with the silken wrapper, fell at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and described in detail the King's mercy toward him.**
