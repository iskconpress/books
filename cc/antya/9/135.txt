====== CC Antya 9.135 ======

===== Text =====

> cāṅgera upare tomāra caraṇa dhyāna kailuṅ
> caraṇa-smaraṇa-prabhāve ei phala pāiluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāṅgera]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---on the //cāṅga//; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---on Your lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]] [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I meditated; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:smaraṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhāve]]---by the power of remembering Your lotus feet; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:p:phala]]---these results; [[synonyms:p:pāiluṅ]]---I have got.

===== Translation =====

**"On the cāṅga I began meditating upon Your lotus feet, and the power of that remembrance has yielded all these results.**
