====== Cc. Antya 9.107 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' 'neta-dhaṭī' tāre parāila
> "prabhu-ājñā lañā yāha, vidāya tomā dila"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:n:neta]]-[[synonyms:d:dhaṭī]]---silken wrapper; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:p:parāila]]---put on him; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---after taking permission from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---go; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]]---farewell; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---to you; [[synonyms:d:dila]]---I give.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, the King appointed him by offering him a silken wrapper for his body. "Go to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu," he said."After taking permission from Him, go to your home. I bid you farewell. Now you may go."**
