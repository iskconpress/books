====== CC Antya 9.37 ======

===== Text =====

> "rāmānanda-rāyera goṣṭhī, saba---tomāra 'dāsa'
> tomāra ucita nahe aichana udāsa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāyera]]---of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---family; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Your servants; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---for You; [[synonyms:u:ucita]]---befitting; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:a:aichana]]---such; [[synonyms:u:udāsa]]---indifference.

===== Translation =====

**"All the members of Rāmānanda Rāya's family are Your eternal servants. Now they are in danger. It is not befitting for You to be indifferent to them in this way."**
