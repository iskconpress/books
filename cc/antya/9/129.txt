====== CC Antya 9.129 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-rāya ādi sabāi mililā
> bhavānanda-rāya tabe balite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Rāmānanda Rāya and other brothers; [[synonyms:s:sabāi]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:b:bhavānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Bhavānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:b:balite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Rāmānanda Rāya, all his brothers, and their father met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Then Bhavānanda Rāya began speaking.**
