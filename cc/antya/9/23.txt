====== Cc. Antya 9.23 ======

===== Text =====

> sei rāja-putra mūlya kare ghāṭāñā
> gopīnāthera krodha haila mūlya śuniyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---prince; [[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---price; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---estimates; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭāñā]]---reducing; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthera]]---of Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:k:krodha]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was anger; [[synonyms:m:mūlya]] [[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing the price.

===== Translation =====

**"The prince, however, purposely gave a reduced estimate for the value of the horses. When Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka heard the price quoted, he was very angry.**
