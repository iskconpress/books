====== CC Antya 9.36 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe svarūpādi yata prabhura bhakta-gaṇa
> prabhura caraṇe sabe kailā nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted.

===== Translation =====

**Then all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, fell at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and submitted the following plea.**
