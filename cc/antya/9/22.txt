====== CC Antya 9.22 ======

===== Text =====

> eka rāja-putra ghoḍāra mūlya bhāla jāne
> tāre pāṭhāila rājā pātra-mitra sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---prince; [[synonyms:g:ghoḍāra]] [[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---the price of horses; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]]---well; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent for him; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]-[[synonyms:m:mitra]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---accompanied by ministers and friends.

===== Translation =====

**"One of the princes knew how to estimate the price of horses very well. Thus the King sent for him to come with his ministers and friends.**
