====== CC Antya 9.27 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' rājaputra-mane krodha upajila
> rājāra ṭhāñi yāi' bahu lāgāni karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---of the prince; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]---anger; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---arose; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---before the King; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:l:lāgāni]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---made many false allegations.

===== Translation =====

**"Hearing this criticism, the prince became very angry. Going before the King, he made some false allegations against Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka.**
