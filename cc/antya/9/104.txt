====== CC Antya 9.104 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' miśre namaskari' rājā ghare gelā
> gopīnāthe 'baḍa jānāya' ḍākiyā ānilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:miśre]] [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---after offering obeisances to Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:r:rājā]]---the King; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned to his palace; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthe]]---Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:j:jānāya]]---the eldest prince; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍākiyā]] [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---called forth.

===== Translation =====

**After offering obeisances to Kāśī Miśra, the King returned to his palace and called for both Gopīnātha and the eldest prince.**
