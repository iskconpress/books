====== Cc. Antya 9.8 ======

===== Text =====

> manuṣyera veśe deva-gandharva-kinnara
> sapta-pātālera yata daitya viṣadhara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manuṣyera]] [[synonyms:v:veśe]]---in the dress of human beings; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:g:gandharva]]-[[synonyms:k:kinnara]]---the demigods, the Gandharvas and the Kinnaras; [[synonyms:s:sapta]]-[[synonyms:p:pātālera]]---of the seven lower planetary systems; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all kinds of; [[synonyms:d:daitya]]---demons; [[synonyms:v:viṣa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhara]]---serpentine living entities.

===== Translation =====

**The inhabitants of the seven higher planetary systems-including the demigods, the Gandharvas and the Kinnaras-and the inhabitants of the seven lower planetary systems [Pātālaloka], including the demons and serpentine living entities, all visited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in the dress of human beings.**
