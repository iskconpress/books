====== Cc. Antya 9.1 ======

===== Text =====

> agaṇya-dhanya-caitanya-
> gaṇānāṁ prema-vanyayā
> ninye 'dhanya-jana-svānta-
> maruḥ śaśvad anūpatām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:agaṇya]]---innumerable; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---glorious; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇānām]]---of the associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vanyayā]]---by the inundation of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:ninye]]---was brought; [[synonyms:a:adhanya]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---of unfortunate persons; [[synonyms:s:svānta]]-[[synonyms:m:maruḥ]]---the desert of the heart; [[synonyms:ś:śaśvat]]---always; [[synonyms:a:anūpatām]]---to the state of being filled with water.

===== Translation =====

**The innumerable, glorious followers of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu brought a constant flood to the desertlike hearts of the unfortunate with an inundation of ecstatic love.**
