====== CC Antya 9.41 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle āra loka āila dhāñā
> khaḍegara upare gopīnāthe diteche ḍāriyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---person; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running; [[synonyms:k:khaḍegara]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon the swords; [[synonyms:g:gopīnāthe]]---Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:d:diteche]] [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍāriyā]]---they are throwing.

===== Translation =====

**Then another person came with the news that Gopīnātha had already been set up to be thrown upon the points of the swords.**
