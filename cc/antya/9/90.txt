====== CC Antya 9.90 ======

===== Text =====

> rājāra vartana khāya, āra curi kare
> rāja-daṇḍya haya sei śāstrera vicāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rājāra]] [[synonyms:v:vartana]]---the salary of the King; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---he takes; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:c:curi]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---steals; [[synonyms:r:rāja]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍya]]---liable to be punished by the King; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]] [[synonyms:v:vicāre]]---the verdict of the revealed scripture.

===== Translation =====

**"One who serves the government but misappropriates the government's revenue is liable to be punished by the king. That is the verdict of all revealed scriptures.**
