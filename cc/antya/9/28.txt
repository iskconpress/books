====== Cc. Antya 9.28 ======

===== Text =====

> "kauḍi nāhi dibe ei, beḍāya chadma kari'
> ājñā deha yadi,---'cāṅge caḍāñā la-i kauḍi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kauḍi]]---the money; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:d:dibe]]---will not pay; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this man; [[synonyms:b:beḍāya]]---wanders; [[synonyms:c:chadma]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---on some pretext; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha]] [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if you order; [[synonyms:c:cāṅge]] [[synonyms:c:caḍāñā]]---raising on the //cāṅga//; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:i]] [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]]---I shall get the money.

===== Translation =====

**"This Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka,' he said,'is unwilling to pay the money due. Instead, he is squandering it under some pretext. If you issue an order, I can put him on the cāṅga and thus realize the money.'**
