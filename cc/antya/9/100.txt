====== CC Antya 9.100 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi yāha, prabhure rākhaha yatna kari'
> ei mui tāhāre chāḍinu saba kauḍi"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---go; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rākhaha]]---keep; [[synonyms:y:yatna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great attention; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mui]]---as far as I am concerned; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:c:chāḍinu]]---I abandon; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]]---all dues.

===== Translation =====

**"Go personally to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and keep Him at Jagannātha Purī with great attention. I shall excuse Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka from all his debts."**
