====== Cc. Antya 9.26 ======

===== Text =====

> 'āmāra ghoḍā grīvā nā phirāya ūrdhve nāhi cāya
> tāte ghoḍāra mūlya ghāṭi karite nā yuyāya'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghoḍā]]---my horses; [[synonyms:g:grīvā]]---the neck; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:phirāya]]---do not turn; [[synonyms:ū:ūrdhve]]---upward; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:c:cāya]]---do not look; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---because of this; [[synonyms:g:ghoḍāra]] [[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---the price of the horse; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭi]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to reduce; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---is not proper.

===== Translation =====

**"Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka said, 'My horses never turn their necks or look upward. Therefore the price for them should not be reduced.'**
