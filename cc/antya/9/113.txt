====== CC Antya 9.113 ======

===== Text =====

> tathāpi tāra sevaka āsi' kaila nivedana
> tāte kṣubdha haila yabe mahāprabhura mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---his servant; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---submitted; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---by that; [[synonyms:k:kṣubdha]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was agitated; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind.

===== Translation =====

**When Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka's servant went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and informed the Lord of his plight, the Lord was somewhat agitated and dissatisfied.**
