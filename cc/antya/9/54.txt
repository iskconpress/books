====== Cc. Antya 9.54 ======

===== Text =====

> yathārtha mūlya kari' tabe saba ghoḍā la-ila
> āra dravyera muddatī kari' ghare pāṭhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:m:mūlya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---estimating the proper price; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:g:ghoḍā]]---horses; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---took; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dravyera]]---of the balance; [[synonyms:m:muddatī]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---setting a time for payment; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāila]]---sent home.

===== Translation =====

**Then the government took all the horses for a proper price, a time was set for payment of the balance, and Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka was released.**
