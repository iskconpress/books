====== CC Antya 9.56 ======

===== Text =====

> se kahe---"vāṇīnātha nirbhaye laya kṛṣṇa-nāma
> 'hare kṛṣṇa, hare kṛṣṇa' kahe aviśrāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he replied; [[synonyms:v:vāṇīnātha]]---Vāṇīnātha; [[synonyms:n:nirbhaye]]---without fear; [[synonyms:l:laya]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---was chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mahā-mantra//; [[synonyms:h:hare]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa,]] [[synonyms:h:hare]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Hare Kṛṣṇa, Hare Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahe]] [[synonyms:a:aviśrāma]]---was chanting incessantly.

===== Translation =====

**The messenger replied, "He was fearlessly, incessantly chanting the mahā-mantra-Hare Kṛṣṇa, Hare Kṛṣṇa, Kṛṣṇa Kṛṣṇa, Hare Hare Hare Rāma, Hare Rāma, Rāma Rāma, Hare Hare.**
