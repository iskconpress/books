====== CC Antya 9.76 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra anukampā cāhe, bhaje anukṣaṇa
> acirāt mile tāṅre tomāra caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:a:anukampā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---desires; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]] [[synonyms:a:anukṣaṇa]]---engages in devotional service twenty-four hours a day; [[synonyms:a:acirāt]]---very soon; [[synonyms:m:mile]]---meet; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**"One who engages in Your devotional service twenty-four hours a day, desiring only Your mercy, will very soon attain shelter at Your lotus feet.**
