====== CC Antya 9.123 ======

===== Text =====

> gopīnātha ei-mata 'viṣaya' kariyā
> dui-cāri-lakṣa kāhana rahe ta' khāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing business; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣa]] [[synonyms:k:kāhana]]---two to four hundred thousand //kāhanas//; [[synonyms:r:rahe]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:khāñā]]---spends as he likes.

===== Translation =====

**"Having been appointed collector, Gopīnātha, in the same way, also generally spends two to four hundred thousand kāhanas as he likes.**
