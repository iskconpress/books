====== CC Antya 9.151 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-caritra ei parama gambhīra
> sei bujhe' tāṅra pade yāṅra mana 'dhīra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---behavior of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---very grave; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bujhe]]---he understands; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:p:pade]]---upon His lotus feet; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:d:dhīra]]---sober.

===== Translation =====

**The intentions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are so deep that one can understand them only if he has complete faith in service to the lotus feet of the Lord.**
