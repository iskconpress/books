====== CC Antya 9.5 ======

===== Text =====

> antare-bāhire kṛṣṇa-viraha-taraṅga
> nānā-bhāve vyākula prabhura mana āra aṅga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:antare]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---internally and externally; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viraha]]-[[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---the waves of separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---by various ecstasies; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]]---agitated; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---mind and body.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu always felt waves of separation from Kṛṣṇa, externally and internally. His mind and body were agitated by various spiritual transformations.**
