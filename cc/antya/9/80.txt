====== CC Antya 9.80 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' kāśī-miśra gelā sva-mandire
> madhyāhne pratāparudra āilā tāṅra ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Kāśī Miśra; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---to his own temple; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhne]]---at noon; [[synonyms:p:pratāparudra]]---King Pratāparudra; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his home.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Kāśī Miśra left the abode of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and returned to his own temple. At noon King Pratāparudra came to Kāśī Miśra's home.**
