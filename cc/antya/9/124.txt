====== Cc. Antya 9.124 ======

===== Text =====

> kichu deya, kichu nā deya, nā kari vicāra
> jānā'-sahita aprītye duḥkha pāila ei-bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---he pays; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:deya]]---he does not pay; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---I do not consider; [[synonyms:j:jānā]] [[synonyms:s:sahita]]---with the prince; [[synonyms:a:aprītye]]---due to some unfriendliness; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---has gotten so much trouble; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---this time.

===== Translation =====

**"Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka would collect some and pay some, spending it at will, but I would not consider this very seriously. This time, however, he was put in trouble because of a misunderstanding with the prince.**
