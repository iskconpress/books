====== Cc. Antya 9.127 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā rājāra vinaya prabhura ānanda
> hena-kāle āilā tathā rāya bhavānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rājāra]]---of the King; [[synonyms:v:vinaya]]---submission; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very happy; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---there; [[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:b:bhavānanda]]---Bhavānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Having heard from Kāśī Miśra all these statements concerning the King's mentality, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very happy. At that time, Bhavānanda Rāya also arrived there.**
