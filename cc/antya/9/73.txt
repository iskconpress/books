====== CC Antya 9.73 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānandera bhāi gopīnātha-mahāśaya
> tomā haite viṣaya-vāñchā, tāra icchā naya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]]---of Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---brother; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]---Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---a great gentleman; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from You; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]-[[synonyms:v:vāñchā]]---desire for material advantage; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---his desire; [[synonyms:n:naya]]---is not.

===== Translation =====

**"Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka is a good gentleman. He does not desire material benefits from You.**
