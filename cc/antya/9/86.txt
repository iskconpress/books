====== CC Antya 9.86 ======

===== Text =====

> "gopīnātha-paṭṭanāyake yabe cāṅge caḍāilā
> tāra sevaka saba āsi' prabhure kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:p:paṭṭanāyake]]---Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:c:cāṅge]]---on the //cāṅga//; [[synonyms:c:caḍāilā]]---they lifted; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---his servants; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**"When Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka was lifted onto the cāṅga," he said, "all his servants went to inform Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
