====== CC Antya 1.214 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri māsa rahi' saba prabhura bhakta-gaṇa
> gosāñi vidāya dilā, gauḍe karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---four months; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vidāyadilā]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---they returned.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus spent four months with Him. Then the Lord bade them farewell, and they returned to Bengal.**
