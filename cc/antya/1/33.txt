====== CC Antya 1.33 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche divya-līlā kare śacīra nandana
> kukkurake kṛṣṇa kahāñā karilā mocana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---transcendental activities; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:k:kukkurake]]---even a dog; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kahāñā]]---inducing to chant the holy name "Kṛṣṇa"; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:m:mocana]]---delivered.

===== Translation =====

**Such are the transcendental pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the son of mother Śacī. He even delivered a dog simply by inducing it to chant the mahā-mantra, Hare Kṛṣṇa.**
