====== CC Antya 1.29 ======

===== Text =====

> prasāda nārikela-śasya dena phelāñā
> 'rāma' 'kṛṣṇa' 'hari' kaha'---balena hāsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---food; [[synonyms:n:nārikela]]-[[synonyms:ś:śasya]]---pulp of green coconut; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:p:phelāñā]]---throwing; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]---Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---say; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling.

===== Translation =====

**Furthermore, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was throwing remnants of green coconut pulp to the dog. Smiling in His own way, He was saying to the dog, "Chant the holy names Rāma, Kṛṣṇa, and Hari."**
