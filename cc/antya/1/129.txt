====== CC Antya 1.129 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---'kaha iṣṭa-devera varṇana'
> prabhura saṅkoce rūpa nā kare paṭhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---now speak; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:d:devera]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description of your worshipable Deity; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkoce]]---embarrassment in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does not do; [[synonyms:p:paṭhana]]---recitation.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya said, "Now please recite the description of the glories of your worshipable Deity." Rūpa Gosvāmī, however, hesitated due to embarrassment because Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was present.**
