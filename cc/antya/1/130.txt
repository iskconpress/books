====== CC Antya 1.130 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"kaha, kene kara saṅkoca-lāje?
> granthera phala śunāibā vaiṣṇava samāje?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---speak up; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you do; [[synonyms:s:saṅkoca]]-[[synonyms:l:lāje]]---in shame and embarrassment; [[synonyms:g:granthera]]---of the book; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---the fruit; [[synonyms:ś:śunāibā]]---you should make heard; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:s:samāje]]---in the society of pure devotees.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord, however, encouraged Rūpa Gosvāmī, saying, "Why are you embarrassed? You should recite it so the devotees can hear the good fruit of your writing."**
