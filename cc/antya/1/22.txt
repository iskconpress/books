====== CC Antya 1.22 ======

===== Text =====

> kukkura nāhi pāya bhāta śuni' duḥkhī hailā
> kukkura cāhite daśa-manuṣya pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kukkura]]---the dog; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---did not; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:b:bhāta]]---rice; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---Śivānanda Sena became very unhappy; [[synonyms:k:kukkura]] [[synonyms:c:cāhite]]---to look for the dog; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---ten men; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**When he learned that the dog had not been supplied food in his absence, he was very unhappy. He then immediately sent ten men to find the dog.**
