====== Cc. Antya 1.218 ======

===== Text =====

> vraje yāi rasa-śāstra kariha nirūpaṇa
> lupta-tīrtha saba tāhāṅ kariha pracāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vraje]] [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---going to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---all transcendental literature concerning the pastimes of Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:n:nirūpaṇa]]---write carefully; [[synonyms:l:lupta]]-[[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]---the lost holy places; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:p:pracāraṇa]]---make known.

===== Translation =====

**"When you go to Vṛndāvana, stay there, preach transcendental literature and excavate the lost holy places.**
