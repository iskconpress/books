====== CC Antya 1.64 ======

===== Text =====

> govinda-dvārā prabhura śeṣa-prasāda pāilā
> preme matta dui-jana nācite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---through Govinda; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---they got; [[synonyms:p:preme]] [[synonyms:m:matta]]---overwhelmed by ecstasy; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---both of them; [[synonyms:n:nācite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to dance.

===== Translation =====

**When they received the remnants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's prasāda through Govinda, they respected it, and then they both began to dance in ecstasy.**
