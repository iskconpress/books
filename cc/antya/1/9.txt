====== CC Antya 1.9 ======

===== Text =====

> madhya-līlā saṅkṣepete kariluṅ varṇana
> antya-līlā-varṇana kichu śuna, bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes known as //madhya-līlā//; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepete]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---I have described; [[synonyms:a:antya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the last pastimes; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---O devotees.

===== Translation =====

**I have briefly described the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu known as the madhya-līlā. Now I shall attempt to describe something about His last pastimes, which are known as the antya-līlā.**
