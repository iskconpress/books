====== CC Antya 1.76 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe ekā svarūpa gosāñi ślokera artha jāne
> ślokānurūpa pada prabhuke karāna āsvādane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]]---only; [[synonyms:e:ekā]]---one; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning of that verse; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:a:anurūpa]] [[synonyms:p:pada]]---other verses following that particular verse; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāna]]---causes; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādane]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**Only Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī knew the purpose for which the Lord recited that verse. According to the Lord's attitude, he used to quote other verses to enable the Lord to relish mellows.**
