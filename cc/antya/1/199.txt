====== CC Antya 1.199 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe kṛpā kari' iṅhāre deha' ei vara
> vraja-līlā-prema-rasa yena varṇe nirantara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing your mercy; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāre]]---unto Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---give; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:v:vara]]---this benediction; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---transcendental mellows of the pastimes of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---so that; [[synonyms:v:varṇe]]---he can describe; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---without cessation.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested all His personal associates to bless Rūpa Gosvāmī so that he might continuously describe the pastimes of Vṛndāvana, which are full of emotional love of Godhead.**
