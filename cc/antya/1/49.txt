====== CC Antya 1.49 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa-rūpe lañā prabhu vasilā eka-sthāne
> kuśala-praśna, iṣṭa-goṣṭhī kailā kata-kṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---both Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in one place; [[synonyms:k:kuśala]]-[[synonyms:p:praśna]]---questions about auspicious news; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---talking together; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---continued for some time.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then sat down with Haridāsa and Rūpa Gosvāmī. They inquired from one another about auspicious news, and then continued to talk together for some time.**
