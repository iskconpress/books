====== CC Antya 1.198 ======

===== Text =====

> madhura prasanna ihāra kāvya sālaṅkāra
> aiche kavitva vinu nahe rasera pracāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhura]]---sweet; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]]---pleasing; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---his; [[synonyms:k:kāvya]]---poetry; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:a:alaṅkāra]]---with metaphors and other ornaments; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such as that; [[synonyms:k:kavitva]]---poetic qualifications; [[synonyms:v:vinu]]---without; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---there is not; [[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of mellows; [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---preaching.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu praised the metaphors and other literary ornaments of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's transcendental poetry. Without such poetic attributes, He said, there is no possibility of preaching transcendental mellows.**
