====== Cc. Antya 1.220 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu tāṅre kailā āliṅgana
> rūpa gosāñi śire dhare prabhura caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embraced; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śire]]---on the head; [[synonyms:d:dhare]]---takes; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Having thus spoken, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Rūpa Gosvāmī, who then placed the lotus feet of the Lord upon his head.**
