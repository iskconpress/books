====== Cc. Antya 1.135 ======

===== Text =====

> ākṣiptaḥ kāla-sāmyena
> praveśaḥ syāt pravartakaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ākṣiptaḥ]]---set in motion; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]-[[synonyms:s:sāmyena]]---by a suitable time; [[synonyms:p:praveśaḥ]]---the entrance; [[synonyms:s:syāt]]---should be; [[synonyms:p:pravartakaḥ]]---named //pravartaka.//

===== Translation =====

**'When the entrance of the actors is set in motion by the arrival of a suitable time, the entrance is called pravartaka.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is from //Nāṭaka-candrikā// (12) by Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.
