====== Cc. Antya 1.171 ======

===== Text =====

> pramada-rasa-taraṅga-smera-gaṇḍa-sthalāyāḥ
> smara-dhanur anubandhi-bhrū-latā-lāsya-bhājaḥ
> mada-kala-cala-bhṛṅgī-bhrānti-bhaṅgīṁ dadhāno
> hṛdayam idam adāṅkṣīt pakṣmalākṣyāḥ kaṭākṣaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pramada]]---of joy; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:t:taraṅga]]---by the continuous waves of the mellow; [[synonyms:s:smera]]---mildly smiling; [[synonyms:g:gaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthalāyāḥ]]---whose cheeks; [[synonyms:s:smara]]-[[synonyms:d:dhanuḥ]]---the bow of Cupid; [[synonyms:a:anubandhi]]---related with; [[synonyms:b:bhrū]]-[[synonyms:l:latā]]---of the arched eyebrows; [[synonyms:l:lāsya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:b:bhājaḥ]]---of one who has; [[synonyms:m:mada]]-[[synonyms:k:kala]]---intoxicated; [[synonyms:c:cala]]---unsteady; [[synonyms:b:bhṛṅgī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrānti]]---the moving to and fro of bees; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgīm]]---the semblance of; [[synonyms:d:dadhānaḥ]]---giving; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayam]] [[synonyms:i:idam]]---this heart; [[synonyms:a:adāṅkṣīt]]---has bitten; [[synonyms:p:pakṣmala]]---possessing exquisite eyelashes; [[synonyms:a:akṣyāḥ]]---of whose two eyes; [[synonyms:k:kaṭa]]-[[synonyms:a:akṣaḥ]]---the glance.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī smiles, waves of joy overtake Her cheeks, and Her arched eyebrows dance like the bow of Cupid. Her glance is so enchanting that it is like a dancing bumblebee, moving unsteadily due to intoxication. That bee has bitten the whorl of My heart.' "**

===== Purport =====

This verse from //Vidagdha-mādhava// (2.51) is also spoken by Lord Kṛṣṇa.
