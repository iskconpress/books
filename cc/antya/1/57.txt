====== CC Antya 1.57 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā-duṅhāra kṛpāte iṅhāra ha-u taiche śakti
> yāte vivarite pārena kṛṣṇa-rasa-bhakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by the mercy of both of You; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---of Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:u:u]]---let there be; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---such; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---by which; [[synonyms:v:vivarite]]---to describe; [[synonyms:p:pārena]]---is able; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---the transcendental mellows of devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**May Rūpa Gosvāmī, by Your mercy, become so powerful that he will be able to describe the transcendental mellows of devotional service."**
