====== Cc. Antya 1.180 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"rūpera kāvya amṛtera pūra
> tāra madhye eka bindu diyāche karpūra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:r:rūpera]] [[synonyms:k:kāvya]]---the poetic expression of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:p:pūra]]---filled with all nectar; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:b:bindu]]---one drop; [[synonyms:d:diyāche]]---he has given; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---camphor.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya objected, "It is not alkali at all. It is a particle of camphor he has put into the nectar of his exalted poetic expression."**
