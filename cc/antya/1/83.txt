====== Cc. Antya 1.83 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu tāṅre cāpaḍa māri' kahite lāgilā
> prabhu dekhi' daṇḍavat prāṅgaṇe padilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing the Lord there; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prāṅgaṇe]]---in the courtyard; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:c:cāpaḍa]] [[synonyms:m:māri']]---giving a mild slap; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the Lord, Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī fell flat in the courtyard to offer obeisances. The Lord slapped him mildly in love and spoke as follows.**
