====== CC Antya 1.112 ======

===== Text =====

> 'pūrva-śloka paḍa, rūpa' prabhu ājñā kailā
> lajjāte nā paḍe rūpa mauna dharilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---he previous verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍa]]---just read; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---My dear Rūpa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---ordered; [[synonyms:l:lajjāte]]---in great shyness; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---did not read; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---remained silent.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered Rūpa Gosvāmī to read the verse they had previously heard, Rūpa Gosvāmī, because of great shyness, did not read it but instead remained silent.**
