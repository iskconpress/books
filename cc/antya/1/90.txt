====== Cc. Antya 1.90 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe---"yāte ei śloka dekhiluṅ
> tumi kariyācha kṛpā, tavaṅhi jānilu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara says; [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---since; [[synonyms:e:eiśloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:d:dekhiluṅ]]---I have seen; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---have bestowed Your mercy; [[synonyms:t:tavaṅhi]]---immediately; [[synonyms:j:jānilu]]---I could understand.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara said, "As soon as I saw the unique composition of this verse, I could immediately understand that You had bestowed upon him Your special mercy.**
