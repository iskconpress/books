====== CC Antya 1.31 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda kukkura dekhi' daṇḍavat kailā
> dainya kari' nija aparādha kṣamāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:k:kukkura]]---the dog; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing there; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:d:dainya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---exhibiting humbleness; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---personal; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offenses; [[synonyms:k:kṣamāilā]]---was forgiven.

===== Translation =====

**When he saw the dog sitting in that way and chanting the name of Kṛṣṇa, Śivānanda, because of his natural humility, immediately offered his obeisances to the dog just to counteract his offenses to it.**
