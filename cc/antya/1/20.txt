====== Cc. Antya 1.20 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina śivānande ghāṭiyāle rākhila
> kukkurake bhāta dite sevaka pāsarilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:ś:śivānande]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭiyāle]]---tollman; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---detained; [[synonyms:k:kukkurake]]---unto the dog; [[synonyms:b:bhāta]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to supply rice; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servant; [[synonyms:p:pāsarilā]]---forgot.

===== Translation =====

**One day while Śivānanda was detained by a tollman, his servant forgot to give the dog its cooked rice.**
