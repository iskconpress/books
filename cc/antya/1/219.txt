====== CC Antya 1.219 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-sevā, rasa-bhakti kariha pracāra
> āmiha dekhite tāhāṅ yāimu ekabāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sevā]]---the service of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---preach; [[synonyms:ā:āmiha]]---I also; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yāimu]]---I shall go; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---once more.

===== Translation =====

**"Establish the service of Lord Kṛṣṇa and preach the mellows of Lord Kṛṣṇa's devotional service. I shall also go to Vṛndāvana once more."**
