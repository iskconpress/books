====== Cc. Antya 1.81 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle prabhu āilā tāṅhāre milite
> cāle śloka dekhi prabhu lāgilā paḍite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came there; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]] [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet him; [[synonyms:c:cāle]]---in the thatched roof; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]]---to read.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went there to meet him, and when He saw the leaf pushed into the roof and saw the verse, He began to read it.**
