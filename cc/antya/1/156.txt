====== Cc. Antya 1.156 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"vṛndāvana, muralī-niḥsvana
> kṛṣṇa, rādhikāra kaiche kariyācha varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---the place named Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:m:muralī]]-[[synonyms:n:niḥsvana]]---the vibration of Kṛṣṇa's flute; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:rādhikāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---you have described.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya further inquired: "How have you described Vṛndāvana, the vibration of the transcendental flute, and the relationship between Kṛṣṇa and Rādhikā?**
