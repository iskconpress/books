====== CC Antya 1.63 ======

===== Text =====

> prasāda khāya, 'hari' bale sarva-bhakta-jana
> dekhi' haridāsa-rūpera haraṣita mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---eat the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---chant the holy name of Hari; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing this; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---and of Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]---jubilant; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the minds.

===== Translation =====

**When Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Rūpa Gosvāmī saw that all the devotees were accepting prasāda and chanting the holy name of Hari, they both were greatly pleased.**
