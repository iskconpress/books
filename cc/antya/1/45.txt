====== Cc. Antya 1.45 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāvite bhāvite śīghra āilā nīlācale
> āsi' uttarilā haridāsa-vāsā-sthale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāvite]] [[synonyms:b:bhāvite]]---thinking and thinking; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very soon; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]] [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---reached Nīlācala (Jagannātha Purī); [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:u:uttarilā]]---approached; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthale]]---the place where Haridāsa Ṭhākura was residing.

===== Translation =====

**Thus absorbed in thought, he quickly reached Jagannātha Purī. When he arrived, he approached the hut of Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
