====== CC Antya 1.215 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rūpa prabhu-pade nīlācale rahilā
> dola-yātrā prabhu-saṅge ānande dekhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]]---at the feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:d:dola]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the festival of Dola-yātrā; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---saw.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, however, stayed at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and when the Dola-yātrā festival took place, he saw it in great happiness with the Lord.**
