====== Cc. Antya 1.27 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat sabāre prabhu pāṭhāilā vāsā-sthāne
> prabhu-ṭhāñi prātaḥ-kāle āilā āra dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrvavat]]---as it was previously; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---everyone; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to their respective residential quarters; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---they came; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---on the next day.

===== Translation =====

**As previously, the Lord provided them all with residential quarters. And the next morning all the devotees came to see the Lord.**
