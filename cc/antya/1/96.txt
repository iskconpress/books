====== CC Antya 1.96 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kyā puṅthi likha?' bali' eka-patra nilā
> akṣara dekhiyā prabhu mane sukhī hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kyā]]---what; [[synonyms:p:puṅthi]]---book; [[synonyms:l:likha]]---you are writing; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:p:patra]] [[synonyms:n:nilā]]---took one page written on a palm leaf; [[synonyms:a:akṣara]]---the good handwriting; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:s:sukhī]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very happy.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord inquired, "What kind of book are you writing?" He held up a palm leaf that was a page of the manuscript, and when He saw the fine handwriting, His mind was very pleased.**
