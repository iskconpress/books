====== CC Antya 1.43 ======

===== Text =====

> svapna dekhi' rūpa-gosāñi karilā vicāra
> satya-bhāmāra ājñā---pṛthak nāṭaka karibāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svapna]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after dreaming; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---considered; [[synonyms:s:satya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāmāra]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order of Śrīmatī Satyabhāmā; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]] [[synonyms:k:karibāra]]---to write a separate drama.

===== Translation =====

**After having that dream, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī considered, "It is the order of Satyabhāmā that I write a separate drama for her.**
