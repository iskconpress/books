====== Cc. Antya 1.172 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"tomāra kavitva amṛtera dhāra
> dvitīya nāṭakera kaha nāndī-vyavahāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---you; [[synonyms:k:kavitva]]---superexcellence in poetic presentation; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---a continuous shower of nectar; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭakera]]---of the second drama; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please tell; [[synonyms:n:nāndī]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---treatment of the introduction.

===== Translation =====

**Having heard these verses recited by Rūpa Gosvāmī, Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya said, "Your poetic expressions are like continuous showers of nectar. Kindly let me hear the introductory portion of the second drama."**
