====== CC Antya 1.82 ======

===== Text =====

> śloka paḍi' prabhu sukhe premāviṣṭa hailā
> hena-kāle rūpa-gosāñi snāna kari' āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reading this verse; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became overwhelmed by ecstatic love; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after taking his bath; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came back.

===== Translation =====

**After reading the verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was overwhelmed by ecstatic love. At that very time, Rūpa Gosvāmī returned, having finished bathing in the sea.**
