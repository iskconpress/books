====== Cc. Antya 1.125 ======

===== Text =====

> ārambhiyāchilā, ebe prabhu-ājñā pāñā
> dui nāṭaka kariteche vibhāga kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ārambhiyāchilā]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī began; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---two different dramas; [[synonyms:k:kariteche]]---he is compiling; [[synonyms:v:vibhāga]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---dividing the original idea.

===== Translation =====

**"He began it in that way, but now, following the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he has divided it in two and is writing two plays, one concerning the pastimes of Mathurā and Dvārakā and the other concerning the pastimes of Vṛndāvana.**
