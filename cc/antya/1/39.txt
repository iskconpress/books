====== Cc. Antya 1.39 ======

===== Text =====

> anupamera lāgi' tāṅra kichu vilamba ha-ila
> bhakta-gaṇa-pāśa āilā, lāg nā pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anupamera]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---on account of the passing away of Anupama; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vilamba]]---delay; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---to the devotees in Bengal; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:l:lāg]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---he could not contact them.

===== Translation =====

**There was some delay because of the death of Anupama, and therefore when Rūpa Gosvāmī went to Bengal to see the devotees there, he could not get in touch with them because they had already left.**
