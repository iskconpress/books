====== CC Antya 1.61 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata pratidina prabhura vyavahāra
> prabhu-kṛpā pāñā rūpera ānanda apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:pratidina]]---daily; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---the dealings of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---unlimited happiness.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dealings with them continued every day. Thus receiving the transcendental favor of the Lord, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī felt unlimited pleasure.**
