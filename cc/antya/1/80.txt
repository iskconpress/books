====== Cc. Antya 1.80 ======

===== Text =====

> tāla-patre śloka likhi' cālete rākhilā
> samudra-snāna karibāre rūpa-gosāñi gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāla]]-[[synonyms:p:patre]]---on a palm leaf; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:l:likhi']]---writing; [[synonyms:c:cālete]]---in the thatched roof; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept it; [[synonyms:s:samudra]]-[[synonyms:s:snāna]]---bath in the sea; [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---for taking; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---of the name Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---departed.

===== Translation =====

**After writing this verse on a palm leaf, Rūpa Gosvāmī put it somewhere in his thatched roof and went to bathe in the sea.**
