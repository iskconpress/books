====== CC Antya 1.34 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā prabhu-ājñāya rūpa āilā vṛndāvana
> kṛṣṇa-līlā-nāṭaka karite haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---on the other side; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---upon the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---a drama on Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to compose; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it was; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Meanwhile, following the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī returned to Vṛndāvana. He desired to write dramas concerning the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
