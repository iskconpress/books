====== CC Antya 1.68 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' mahāprabhu madhyāhne calilā
> rūpa-gosāñi mane kichu vismaya ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:a:ahne]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---left to execute noon duties; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in mind; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---there was surprise.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to perform His noontime duties, leaving Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī somewhat surprised.**
