====== CC Antya 1.89 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe śakti sañcāri' āmi kailuṅ upadeśa
> tumiha kahio ihāṅya rasera viśeṣa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]] [[synonyms:s:sañcāri']]---empowering him with My transcendental potency; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]] [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]]---gave instruction; [[synonyms:t:tumiha]]---you also; [[synonyms:k:kahio]]---inform; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅya]]---unto him; [[synonyms:r:rasera]] [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---particular information about transcendental mellows.

===== Translation =====

**I thereupon also bestowed upon him My transcendental potency. Now you also should give him instructions. In particular, instruct him in transcendental mellows."**
