====== CC Antya 1.41 ======

===== Text =====

> rātre svapne dekhe,---eka divya-rūpā nārī
> sammukhe āsiyā ājñā dilā bahu kṛpā kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātre]]---at night; [[synonyms:s:svapne]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---he dreamed; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpā]] [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---celestially beautiful woman; [[synonyms:s:sammukhe]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming before him; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---ordered; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing him much mercy.

===== Translation =====

**While resting in Satyabhāmā-pura, he dreamed that a celestiaIly beautiful woman had come before him and very mercifully gave him the folIowing order.**
