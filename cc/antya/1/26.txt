====== CC Antya 1.26 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā lañā kailā jagannātha daraśana
> sabā lañā mahāprabhu karena bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting the Jagannātha temple; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---took //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went with them to see the Lord in the temple, and on that day He also took lunch in the company of all those devotees.**
