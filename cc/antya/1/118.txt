====== Cc. Antya 1.118 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"kaha rūpa, nāṭakera śloka
> ye śloka śunile lokera yāya duḥkha-śoka

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please recite; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---My dear Rūpa; [[synonyms:n:nāṭakera]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse of your drama; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:ś:śunile]]---hearing; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of all people; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---go away; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---the unhappiness and lamentation.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Rūpa, please recite that verse from your drama which, upon being heard, makes all people's unhappiness and lamentation go away."**
