====== CC Antya 1.53 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpe tāhāṅ vāsā diyā gosāñi calilā
> gosāñira saṅgī bhakta rūpere mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---to Rūpa; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offered a residence; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left the place; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]] [[synonyms:s:saṅgī]]---the associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:r:rūpere]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met Rūpa Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**After allotting residential quarters there to Rūpa Gosvāmī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left. Then all of the Lord's personal associates met Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.**
