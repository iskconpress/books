====== CC Antya 1.131 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe rūpa-gosāñi yadi śloka paḍila
> śuni' prabhu kahe,---'ei ati stuti haila'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---of the name Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---recited the verse; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:ati]] [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---exaggerated offering of prayers; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was.

===== Translation =====

**When Rūpa Gosvāmī thus recited his verse, Caitanya Mahāprabhu disapproved of it because it described His personal glories. He expressed the opinion that it was an exaggerated explanation.**
