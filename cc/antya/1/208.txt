====== Cc. Antya 1.208 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-kṛpā rūpe, āra rūpera sad-guṇa
> dekhi' camatkāra haila sabākāra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---Lord Caitanya's mercy; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---upon Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rūpera]] [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the transcendental qualities of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was astonishment; [[synonyms:s:sabākāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---in the minds.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's special mercy toward Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and seeing his personal qualities, all the devotees were struck with wonder.**
