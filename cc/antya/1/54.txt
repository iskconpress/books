====== Cc. Antya 1.54 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina mahāprabhu saba bhakta lañā
> rūpe milāilā sabāya kṛpā ta' kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the devotees; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]] [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sabāya]]---to all of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:t:ta']] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---showing His mercy.

===== Translation =====

**On the next day, Caitanya Mahāprabhu again met Rūpa Gosvāmī, and with great mercy the Lord introduced him to all the devotees.**
