====== CC Antya 1.102 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu duṅhe kari' āliṅgana
> madhyāhna karite samudre karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---unto both Rūpa Gosvāmī and Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:a:ahna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to perform His noontime duties; [[synonyms:s:samudre]]---o the seaside; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced both Haridāsa and Rūpa Gosvāmī and left for the seaside to perform His noontime duties.**
