====== Cc. Antya 1.15 ======

===== Text =====

> kulīna-grāmī bhakta āra yata khaṇḍa-vāsī
> ācārya śivānanda sane mililā sabe āsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kulīna]]-[[synonyms:g:grāmī]]---residents of the village known as Kulīna-grāma; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---the residents of Śrī Khaṇḍa; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---all coming together.

===== Translation =====

**Thus all the devotees of Kulīna-grāma and Śrī Khaṇḍa, as well as Advaita Ācārya, came together to meet Śivānanda Sena.**
