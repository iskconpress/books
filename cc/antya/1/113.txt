====== CC Antya 1.113 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi tabe sei śloka paḍila
> śuni' sabākāra citte camatkāra haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---recited the verse; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sabākāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in the minds; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was great wonder.

===== Translation =====

**Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī recited the verse, and when all the devotees heard it, their minds were struck with wonder.**
