====== Cc. Antya 1.109 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-saṅge prabhu āilā, dekhi' dui jana
> daṇḍavat hañā kailā caraṇa vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---accompanied by other devotee associates; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing this; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī and Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---falling flat like logs; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---prayers to their lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**When Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Rūpa Gosvāmī saw that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had come with His intimate devotees, they both immediately fell down like logs and offered prayers to their lotus feet.**
