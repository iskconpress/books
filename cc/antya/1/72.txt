====== CC Antya 1.72 ======

===== Text =====

> ratha-yātrāya jagannātha darśana karilā
> ratha-agre prabhura nṛtya-kīrtana dekhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrāya]]---during the function of Ratha-yātrā; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---he saw; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:a:agre]]---the front of the //ratha,// or chariot; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---he saw.

===== Translation =====

**During the Ratha-yātrā ceremony Rūpa Gosvāmī saw Lord Jagannātha. He also saw Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu dancing and chanting in front of the ratha.**
