====== CC Antya 1.65 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina prabhu rūpe miliyā vasilā
> sarvajña-śiromaṇi prabhu kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---with Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---meeting; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the best of the omniscient; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**On the next day, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to see Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, the omniscient Lord spoke as follows.**
