====== CC Antya 1.201 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra yaiche viṣaya-tyāga, taiche tāṅra rīti
> dainya-vairāgya-pāṇḍityera tāṅhātei sthiti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---just as; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāga]]---renunciation of material connections; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:r:rīti]]---his manner of activity; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]---humbleness; [[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---renunciation; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍityera]]---of learned scholarship; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhātei]]---in him; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---existing.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told Rāmānanda Rāya, "Sanātana Gosvāmī's renunciation of material connections is just like yours. Humility, renunciation and excellent learning exist in him simultaneously.**
