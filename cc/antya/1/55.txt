====== CC Antya 1.55 ======

===== Text =====

> sabāra caraṇa rūpa karilā vandana
> kṛpā kari' rūpe sabe kailā āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all the devotees; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---to the lotus feet; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---offered prayers; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing great mercy; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī offered his respectful obeisances unto the lotus feet of them all, and all the devotees, by their mercy, embraced him.**
