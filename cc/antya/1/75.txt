====== CC Antya 1.75 ======

===== Text =====

> sāmānya eka śloka prabhu paḍena kīrtane
> kene śloka paḍe---ihā keha nāhi jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sāmānya]]---generally; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:paḍena]]---recites; [[synonyms:k:kīrtane]]---while chanting; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---that verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---He recites; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---no one knows.

===== Translation =====

**Generally Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited a verse while dancing and chanting before the ratha, but no one knew why He was reciting that particular verse.**
