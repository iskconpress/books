====== CC Antya 1.176 ======

===== Text =====

> 'dvitīya nāndī kaha dekhi?'---rāya puchilā
> saṅkoca pāñā rūpa paḍite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvitīya]] [[synonyms:n:nāndī]]---the second introductory verse; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---recite; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---so that we can see; [[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya again inquired; [[synonyms:s:saṅkoca]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---becoming a little hesitant; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to read.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya further inquired about the second introductory verse, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī was somewhat hesitant, but nevertheless he began to read.**
