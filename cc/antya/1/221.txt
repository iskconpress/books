====== Cc. Antya 1.221 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura bhakta-gaṇa-pāśe vidāya la-ilā
> punarapi gauḍa-pathe vṛndāvane āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---from the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---took leave; [[synonyms:p:punarapi]]---again; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way to Bengal; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī took leave of all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and returned to Vṛndāvana by the path to Bengal.**
