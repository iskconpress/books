====== Cc. Antya 1.47 ======

===== Text =====

> 'upala-bhoga' dekhi' haridāsere dekhite
> pratidina āisena, prabhu āilā ācambite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upala]]-[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---the offering of food to Lord Jagannātha at noon; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsere]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:pratidina]]---daily; [[synonyms:ā:āisena]]---comes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---He reached there; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---all of a sudden.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the upala-bhoga ceremony at the Jagannātha temple, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would regularly come to see Haridāsa every day. Thus He suddenly arrived there.**
