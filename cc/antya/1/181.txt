====== CC Antya 1.181 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"rāya, tomāra ihāte ullāsa
> śunitei lajjā, loke kare upahāsa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:i:ihāte]]---in this; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:ś:śunitei]]---to hear; [[synonyms:l:lajjā]]---ashamed; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:u:upahāsa]]---joking.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Rāmānanda Rāya, you are jubilant at hearing these poetic expressions, but I am ashamed to hear them, for people in general will joke about the subject of this verse."**
