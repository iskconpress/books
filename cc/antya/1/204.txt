====== CC Antya 1.204 ======

===== Text =====

> mora mukhe ye saba rasa karilā pracāraṇe
> sei rasa dekhi ei ihāra likhane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---through my mouth; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---all such transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---You did; [[synonyms:p:pracāraṇe]]---preaching; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---those same transcendental mellows; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]] [[synonyms:l:likhane]]---in the writing of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**"I see that the truths regarding transcendental mellow that You have expounded through my mouth are all explained in the writings of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.**
