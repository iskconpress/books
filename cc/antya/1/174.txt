====== CC Antya 1.174 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra āge dhārṣṭya ei mukha-vyādāna"
> eta bali' nāndī-śloka karilā vyākhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before you; [[synonyms:d:dhārṣṭya]]---impudence; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:v:vyādāna]]---simply opening the mouth; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:n:nāndī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the introductory verses; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāna]]---explained.

===== Translation =====

**It is even impudent for me to open my mouth before you." Then, having said this, he recited the introductory verse of Lalita-mādhava.**
