====== Cc. Antya 1.95 ======

===== Text =====

> sambhrame duṅhe uṭhi' daṇḍavat hailā
> duṅhe āliṅgiyā prabhu āsane vasilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sambhrame]]---with great respect; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---standing up; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---fell down to offer obeisances; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---the two of them; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsane]] [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down on a seat.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Rūpa Gosvāmī saw the Lord coming, they both stood up and then fell down to offer Him their respectful obeisances. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced them both and then sat down.**
