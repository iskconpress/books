====== Cc. Antya 1.115 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya, bhaṭṭācārya bale,---"tomāra prasāda vine
> tomāra hṛdaya ei jānila kemane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---say; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:v:vine]]---without Your special mercy; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---Your mind; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---understood; [[synonyms:k:kemane]]---how.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this verse, both Rāmānanda Rāya and Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya said to Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Without Your special mercy, how could this Rūpa Gosvāmī have understood Your mind?"**
