====== Cc. Antya 1.70 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve dui nāṭaka chila ekatra racanā
> dui-bhāga kari ebe karimu ghaṭanā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---two dramas; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---there was; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---together; [[synonyms:r:racanā]]---composition; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāga]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---dividing into two; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:karimu]] [[synonyms:g:ghaṭanā]]---I shall write the incidents.

===== Translation =====

**FormerIy I wrote the two dramas as one composition. Now I shall divide it and describe the incidents in two separate works.**
