====== CC Antya 1.97 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-rūpera akṣara---yena mukutāra pāṅti
> prīta hañā karena prabhu akṣarera stuti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpera]] [[synonyms:a:akṣara]]---the handwriting of Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---like; [[synonyms:m:mukutāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāṅti]]---a row of pearls; [[synonyms:p:prīta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being pleased; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:akṣarera]] [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---praise of the handwriting of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**Thus being pleased, the Lord praised the writing by saying, "The handwriting of Rūpa Gosvāmī is just like rows of pearls."**
