====== CC Antya 1.85 ======

===== Text =====

> sei śloka lañā prabhu svarūpe dekhāilā
> svarūpera parīkṣā lāgi' tāṅhāre puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---that verse; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svarūpe]] [[synonyms:d:dekhāilā]]---showed to Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:s:svarūpera]]---of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the examination; [[synonyms:t:taṅhāre]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---He inquired from him.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took that verse and showed it to Svarūpa Dāmodara for him to examine. Then the Lord questioned him.**
