====== CC Antya 1.149 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"kaha sahaja-premera lakṣaṇa"
> rūpa-gosāñi kahe,---"sāhajika prema-dharma"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya inquires; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please tell me; [[synonyms:s:sahaja]]-[[synonyms:p:premera]]---of natural love; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the characteristics; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī replies; [[synonyms:s:sāhajika]]---spontaneous; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---character of love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya further inquired, "What are the natural characteristics of awakening love of Godhead?" Rūpa Gosvāmī replied, "These are the natural characteristics of love of God.**
