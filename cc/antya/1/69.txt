====== Cc. Antya 1.69 ======

===== Text =====

> "pṛthak nāṭaka karite satyabhāmā ājñā dila
> jānilu, pṛthak nāṭaka karite prabhu-ājñā haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---different dramas; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to write; [[synonyms:s:satyabhāmā]]---of the name Satyabhāmā; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---ordered; [[synonyms:j:jānilu]]---now I understand; [[synonyms:p:pṛthak]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---different dramas; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to write; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was.

===== Translation =====

**"Satyabhāmā ordered me to write two different dramas," Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī thought. "Now I understand that this order has been confirmed by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
