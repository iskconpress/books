====== CC Antya 1.77 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpa-gosāñi prabhura jāniyā abhiprāya
> sei arthe śloka kailā prabhure ye bhāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jāniyā]]---knowing; [[synonyms:a:abhiprāya]]---the intention; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:a:arthe]]---in that meaning; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---a verse; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---composed; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---appealed.

===== Translation =====

**Rūpa Gosvāmī, however, could understand the intention of the Lord, and thus he composed another verse that appealed to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
