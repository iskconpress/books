====== CC Antya 1.16 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda kare sabāra ghāṭi samādhāna
> sabāre pālana kare, deya vāsā-sthāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---of the name Śivānanda; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭi]]---the camp; [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---arrangement; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:p:pālana]]---maintenance; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---gives; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---residential quarters.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena arranged for the journey. He maintained everyone and provided residential quarters.**
