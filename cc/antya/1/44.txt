====== CC Antya 1.44 ======

===== Text =====

> vraja-pura-līlā ekatra kariyāchi ghaṭanā
> dui bhāga kari' ebe karimu racanā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:p:pura]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes in Vraja and Dvārakā; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---in one place; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchi]]---I have collected; [[synonyms:g:ghaṭanā]]---all the events; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāga]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---dividing into two different parts; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:k:karimu]] [[synonyms:r:racanā]]---I shall write.

===== Translation =====

**I have brought together in one work all the pastimes performed by Lord Kṛṣṇa in Vṛndāvana and in Dvārakā. Now I shall have to divide them into two dramas."**
