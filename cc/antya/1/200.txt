====== CC Antya 1.200 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāra ye jyeṣṭha-bhrātā, nāma---'sanātana'
> pṛthivīte vijña-vara nāhi tāṅra sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---who; [[synonyms:j:jyeṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrātā]]---the elder brother; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivīte]]---on the surface of the world; [[synonyms:v:vijña]]-[[synonyms:v:vara]]---most learned; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is none; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---equal to him.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's elder brother, whose name is Sanātana Gosvāmī, is such a wise and learned scholar that no one is equal to him."**
