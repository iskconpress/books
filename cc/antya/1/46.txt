====== CC Antya 1.46 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa-ṭhākura tāṅre bahu-kṛpā kailā
> 'tumi āsibe,---more prabhu ye kahilā'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---of the name Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---showed much affection because of love and mercy; [[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:ā:āsibe]]---you will come; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---informed.

===== Translation =====

**Out of affectionate love and mercy, Haridāsa Ṭhākura told Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has already informed me that you would come here."**
