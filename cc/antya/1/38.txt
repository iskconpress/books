====== CC Antya 1.38 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpa-gosāñi prabhu-pāśe karilā gamana
> prabhure dekhite tāṅra utkaṇṭhita mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---of the name Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departed for; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]]---full of anxiety; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**Rūpa Gosvāmī then departed to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, for he was very eager to see Him.**
