====== CC Antya 1.52 ======

===== Text =====

> prayāge śuniluṅ,---teṅho gelā vṛndāvane
> anupamera gaṅgā-prāpti kaila nivedane"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prayāge]]---in Prayāga; [[synonyms:ś:śuniluṅ]]---I heard; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:g:gelā]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---has gone to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:a:anupamera]]---of Anupama; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---getting the mercy of the Ganges (death); [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---he informed.

===== Translation =====

**"In Prayāga I heard that he had already gone to Vṛndāvana." Rūpa Gosvāmī next informed the Lord about the death of Anupama.**
