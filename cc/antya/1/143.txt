====== Cc. Antya 1.143 ======

===== Text =====

> iyam sakhi suduḥsādhyā
> rādhā hṛdaya-vedanā
> kṛtā yatra cikitsāpi
> kutsāyāṁ paryavasyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this; [[synonyms:s:sakhi]]---my dear friend; [[synonyms:s:suduḥsādhyā]]---incurable; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]-[[synonyms:v:vedanā]]---palpitation of the heart; [[synonyms:k:kṛtā]]---done; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---in which; [[synonyms:c:cikitsā]]---treatment; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:k:kutsāyām]]---in defamation; [[synonyms:p:paryavasyati]]---ends in.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear friend, these palpitations of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's heart are extremely difficult to cure. Even if one applied some medical treatment, it would only end in defamation."**

===== Purport =====

This verse ( //Vidagdha-mādhava// 2.8) is spoken by Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī Herself.
