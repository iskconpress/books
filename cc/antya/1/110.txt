====== CC Antya 1.110 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-saṅge kailā prabhu duṅhāre milana
> piṇḍāte vasilā prabhu lañā bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with His intimate associates; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāre]]---the two (Rūpa Gosvāmī and Haridāsa Ṭhākura); [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍāte]]---on a raised place; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---with His personal devotees.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His personal devotees met Rūpa Gosvāmī and Haridāsa Ṭhākura. The Lord then sat down in an elevated place with His devotees.**
