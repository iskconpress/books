====== CC Antya 1.48 ======

===== Text =====

> 'rūpa daṇḍavat kare',---haridāsa kahilā
> haridāse mili' prabhu rūpe āliṅgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offers You obeisances; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---Haridāsa informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāse]] [[synonyms:m:mili']]---after meeting Haridāsa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgilā]]---embraced Rūpa Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord arrived, Rūpa Gosvāmī immediately offered his obeisances. Haridāsa informed the Lord, "This is Rūpa Gosvāmī offering You obeisances" and the Lord embraced him.**
