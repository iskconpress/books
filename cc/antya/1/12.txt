====== CC Antya 1.12 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrva-likhita grantha-sūtra-anusāre
> yei nāhi likhi, tāhā likhiye vistāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:l:likhita]]---previously mentioned; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]-[[synonyms:s:sūtra]]---the synopsis of the pastimes; [[synonyms:a:anusāre]]---according to; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I have not mentioned; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---hat; [[synonyms:l:likhiye]]---I shall write; [[synonyms:v:vistāre]]---elaborately.

===== Translation =====

**In accordance with the synopsis previously written, I shall describe in detail whatever I have not mentioned.**
