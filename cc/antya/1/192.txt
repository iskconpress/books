====== CC Antya 1.192 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' rāya kahe prabhura caraṇe
> rūpera kavitva praśaṁsi' sahasra-vadane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---of Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kavitva]]---poetic art; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsi']]---glorifying; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadane]]---as if with a thousand mouths.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this, Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya submitted at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu the superexcellence of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's poetic expression and began to praise it as if he had thousands of mouths.**
