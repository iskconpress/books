====== CC Antya 1.60 ======

===== Text =====

> iṣṭa-goṣṭhī duṅhā sane kari' kata-kṣaṇa
> madhyāhna karite prabhu karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---conversation; [[synonyms:d:duṅhā]] [[synonyms:s:sane]]---with both Rūpa Gosvāmī and Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---doing; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:a:ahna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to execute daily noontime duties; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---left that place.

===== Translation =====

**He would talk for some time with them both and then leave to perform His noontime duties.**
