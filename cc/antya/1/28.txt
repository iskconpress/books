====== Cc. Antya 1.28 ======

===== Text =====

> āsiyā dekhila sabe sei ta kukkure
> prabhu-pāśe vasiyāche kichu alpa-dūre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---they saw; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:t:ta]] [[synonyms:k:kukkure]]---that very same dog; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---near Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasiyāche]]---sat; [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:a:alpa]]-[[synonyms:d:dūre]]---a little bit away from the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**When all the devotees came to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they saw the same dog was sitting a little apart the Lord.**
