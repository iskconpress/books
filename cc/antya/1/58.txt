====== Cc. Antya 1.58 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍiyā, uḍiyā, yata prabhura bhakta-gaṇa
> sabāra ha-ila rūpa snehera bhājana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍiyā]]---devotees from Bengal; [[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]---devotees belonging to Orissa; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---was; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:snehera]] [[synonyms:b:bhājana]]---an object of love and affection.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Rūpa Gosvāmī became the object of love and affection for all the devotees of the Lord, including those who came from Bengal and those who resided in Orissa.**
