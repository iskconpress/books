====== Cc. Antya 1.182 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"lokera sukha ihāra śravaṇe
> abhīṣṭa-devera smṛti maṅgalācaraṇe"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of the people in general; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---in hearing such poetic expressions; [[synonyms:a:abhīṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:d:devera]]---of the worshipable Deity; [[synonyms:s:smṛti]]---remembrance; [[synonyms:m:maṅgalācaraṇe]]---in the performance of auspiciousness in the beginning.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya said, "Instead of joking, people in general will feel great pleasure in hearing such poetry, for the initial remembrance of the worshipable Deity invokes good fortune."**
