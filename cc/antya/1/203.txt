====== Cc. Antya 1.203 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"īśvara tumi ye cāha karite
> kāṣṭhera putalī tumi pāra nācāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:c:cāha]]---You want; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:k:kāṣṭhera]]---of wood; [[synonyms:p:putalī]]---a doll; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---are able; [[synonyms:n:nācāite]]---to make dance.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya replied to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "My Lord, You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead. If You like, You can cause even a wooden doll to dance.**
