====== CC Antya 1.98 ======

===== Text =====

> sei patre prabhu eka śloka ye dekhilā
> paḍitei śloka, preme āviṣṭa ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:patre]]---on that palm leaf; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---one verse; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---He saw; [[synonyms:p:paḍitei]]---by reading; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---was overwhelmed.

===== Translation =====

**While reading the manuscript, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw a verse on that page, and as soon as He read it He was overwhelmed by ecstatic love.**
