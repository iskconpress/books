====== Cc. Antya 1.87 ======

===== Text =====

> anyathā e artha kāra nāhi haya jñāna
> tumi pūrve kṛpā kailā, kari anumāna"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anyathā]]---otherwise; [[synonyms:e:e]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---this confidential meaning; [[synonyms:k:kāra]]---of anyone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---the knowledge; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---before this; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---bestowed mercy; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:a:anumāna]]---I can conjecture.

===== Translation =====

**No one could otherwise understand this meaning. I can therefore guess that previously You bestowed upon him Your causeless mercy."**
