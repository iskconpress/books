====== CC Antya 1.2 ======

===== Text =====

> durgame pathi me 'ndhasya
> skhalat-pāda-gater muhuḥ
> sva-kṛpā-yaṣṭi-dānena
> santaḥ santv avalambanam

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:durgame]]---very difficult; [[synonyms:p:pathi]]---on the path; [[synonyms:m:me]]---of me; [[synonyms:a:andhasya]]---one who is blind; [[synonyms:s:skhalat]]---slipping; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]---on feet; [[synonyms:g:gateḥ]]---whose manner of moving; [[synonyms:m:muhuḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---of their own mercy; [[synonyms:y:yaṣṭi]]---the stick; [[synonyms:d:dānena]]---by giving; [[synonyms:s:santaḥ]]---those saintly persons; [[synonyms:s:santu]]---let that become; [[synonyms:a:avalambanam]]---my support.

===== Translation =====

**My path is very difficult. I am blind, and my feet are slipping again and again. Therefore, may the saints help me by granting me the stick of their mercy as my support.**
