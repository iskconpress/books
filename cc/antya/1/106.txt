====== CC Antya 1.106 ======

===== Text =====

> sārvabhauma-rāmānande parīkṣā karite
> śrī-rūpera guṇa duṅhāre lāgilā kahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānande]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to examine; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpera]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---the transcendental qualities of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāre]]---unto both of them; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---He began to praise.

===== Translation =====

**Just to examine Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya, the Lord began to praise the transcendental qualities of Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī before them.**
