====== CC Antya 1.116 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāte sañcāri' pūrve kahilā siddhānta
> ye saba siddhānte brahmā nāhi pāya anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāte]]---within me; [[synonyms:s:sañcāri']]---creating all logical truths; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---You express; [[synonyms:s:siddhānta]]---conclusive statements; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of; [[synonyms:s:siddhānte]]---conclusive statements; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]---even Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]] [[synonyms:a:anta]]---cannot understand the limit.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya admitted that previously Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had empowered his heart so that he could express elevated and conclusive statements to which even Lord Brahmā has no access.**
