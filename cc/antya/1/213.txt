====== Cc. Antya 1.213 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata dui-jana kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge
> sukhe kāla goṅāya rūpa haridāsa-saṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the pleasure of discussing topics about Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sukhe]]---in happiness; [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---time; [[synonyms:g:goṅāya]]---passes; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the company of Haridāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī passed his time in close association with Haridāsa Ṭhākura by discussing the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa in great happiness.**
