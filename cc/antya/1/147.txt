====== CC Antya 1.147 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"kaha dekhi bhāvera svabhāva?"
> rūpa kahe,---"aiche haya kṛṣṇa-viṣayaka 'bhāva"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---kindly recite; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---so that I may see; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]] [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---the characteristic of emotional love; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī replies; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viṣayaka]]---concerning Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---emotional love.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya inquired, "What are the characteristics of emotional love?" Rūpa Gosvāmī replied, "This is the nature of emotional love for Kṛṣṇa.**
