====== CC Antya 1.169 ======

===== Text =====

> balād akṣṇor lakṣmīḥ kavalayati navyaṁ kuvalayaṁ
> mukhollāsaḥ phullaṁ kamala-vanam ullaṅghayati ca
> daśāṁ kaṣṭām aṣṭā-padam api nayaty āṅgika-rucir
> vicitraṁ rādhāyāḥ kim api kila rūpaṁ vilasati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:balāt]]---by force; [[synonyms:a:akṣṇoḥ]]---of the two eyes; [[synonyms:l:lakṣmīḥ]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:k:kavalayati]]---devours; [[synonyms:n:navyam]]---newly awakened; [[synonyms:k:kuvalayam]]---lotus flower; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:u:ullāsaḥ]]---the beauty of the face; [[synonyms:p:phullam]]---fructified; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]-[[synonyms:v:vanam]]---a forest of lotus flowers; [[synonyms:u:ullaṅghayati]]---surpasses; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:d:daśām]]---to a situation; [[synonyms:k:kaṣṭām]]---painful; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭā]]-[[synonyms:p:padam]]---gold; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:n:nayati]]---brings; [[synonyms:ā:āṅgika]]-[[synonyms:r:ruciḥ]]---the luster of the body; [[synonyms:v:vicitram]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:r:rādhāyāḥ]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:k:kim]] [[synonyms:a:api]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kila]]---certainly; [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---the beauty; [[synonyms:v:vilasati]]---manifests.

===== Translation =====

**The beauty of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī's eyes forcibly devours the beauty of newly grown blue lotus flowers, and the beauty of Her face surpasses that of an entire forest of fully blossomed lotuses. Her bodily luster seems to place even gold in a painful situation. Thus the wonderful, unprecedented beauty of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī is awakening in Vṛndāvana.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is from //Vidagdha-mādhava// (1.32). It is spoken by Paurṇamāsī.
