====== CC Antya 1.206 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu kailā rūpe āliṅgana
> tāṅre karāilā sabāra caraṇa vandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---induced to do; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Rūpa Gosvāmī and asked him to offer prayers at the lotus feet of all the devotees present.**
