====== Cc. Antya 1.62 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇa lañā kailā guṇḍicā mārjana
> āiṭoṭā āsi' kailā vanya-bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:g:guṇḍicā]] [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---cleansing and washing of the Guṇḍicā temple; [[synonyms:ā:āiṭoṭā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming to the nearby garden named Āiṭoṭā; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---had; [[synonyms:v:vanya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---a picnic within the garden.

===== Translation =====

**After Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, taking all His devotees with Him, performed the Guṇḍicā-mārjana [washing and cleansing the temple Guṇḍicā], He went to the garden known as Āiṭoṭā and accepted prasāda at a picnic within the garden.**
