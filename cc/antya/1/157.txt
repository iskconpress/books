====== Cc. Antya 1.157 ======

===== Text =====

> kaha, tomāra kavitva śuni' haya camatkāra"
> krame rūpa-gosāñi kahe kari' namaskāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaha]]---kindly let me know; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:k:kavitva]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---by hearing your poetic ability; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---much astonishment; [[synonyms:k:krame]]---gradually; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---continues to speak; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---offering obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**"Please tell me all this, for your poetic ability is wonderful." After offering obeisances to Rāmānanda Rāya, Rūpa Gosvāmī gradually began answering his inquiries.**
