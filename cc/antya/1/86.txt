====== CC Antya 1.86 ======

===== Text =====

> 'mora antara-vārtā rūpa jānila kemane?'
> svarūpa kahe---'jāni, kṛpā kariyācha āpane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:a:antara]]-[[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---My internal intentions; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---knew; [[synonyms:k:kemane]]---how; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa replied; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I can understand; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]]---You have bestowed Your mercy; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally.

===== Translation =====

**How could Rūpa Gosvāmī have understood My heart?" the Lord asked. Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "I can understand that You have already bestowed Your causeless mercy upon him.**
