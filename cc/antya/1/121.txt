====== Cc. Antya 1.121 ======

===== Text =====

> yata bhakta-vṛnda āra rāmānanda rāya
> śloka śuni' sabāra ha-ila ānanda-vismaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---all the personal devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---of the name Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this verse; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---transcendental bliss and astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**When all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, especially Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya, heard this verse, they were all filled with transcendental bliss and were struck with wonder.**
