====== CC Antya 1.127 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"nāndī-śloka paḍa dekhi, śuni?"
> śrī-rūpa śloka paḍe prabhu-ājñā māni'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:n:nāndī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍa]]---please recite the introductory verse; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---so that I can see; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---so that I can hear; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī recites the verse; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māni']]---accepting the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya said, "PIease recite the introductory verse of Vidagdhamādhava so that I can hear and examine it." Thus Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī, being ordered by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, recited the verse (1.1).**
