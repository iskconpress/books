====== CC Antya 1.193 ======

===== Text =====

> "kavitva nā haya ei amṛtera dhāra
> nāṭaka-lakṣaṇa saba siddhāntera sāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kavitva]]---poetic art; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is not; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---constant shower of nectar; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]---a drama; [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---appearing as; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:siddhāntera]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---essences of ultimate realization.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya said, "This is not a poetic presentation; it is a continuous shower of nectar. Indeed, it is the essence of all ultimate realizations, appearing in the form of plays.**
