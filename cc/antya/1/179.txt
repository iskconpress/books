====== CC Antya 1.179 ======

===== Text =====

> kāṅhā tomāra kṛṣṇa-rasa-kāvya-sudhā-sindhu
> tāra madhye mithyā kene stuti-kṣāra-bindu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāṅhā]]---where; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:k:kāvya]]---of exalted poetry concerning the mellows of Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes; [[synonyms:s:sudhā]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---ocean of the nectar; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that; [[synonyms:m:mithyā]]---false; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:s:stuti]]---prayer; [[synonyms:k:kṣāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bindu]]---like a drop of alkali.

===== Translation =====

**Your exalted poetic descriptions of the mellows of Lord Kṛṣṇa's pastimes are like an ocean of nectar. But why have you put in a false prayer about Me? It is like a drop of detestful alkali."**
