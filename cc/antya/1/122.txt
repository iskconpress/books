====== CC Antya 1.122 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe bale,---'nāma-mahimā śuniyāchi apāra
> emana mādhurya keha nāhi varṇe āra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:b:bale]]---every one of them said; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:ś:śuniyāchi]]---we have heard; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---many times; [[synonyms:e:emana]]---this kind of; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:v:varṇe]]---describes; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---else.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone admitted that although they had heard many statements glorifying the holy name of the Lord, they had never heard such sweet descriptions as those of Rūpa Gosvāmī.**
