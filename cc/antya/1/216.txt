====== Cc. Antya 1.216 ======

===== Text =====

> dola anantare prabhu rūpe vidāya dilā
> aneka prasāda kari' śakti sañcārilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dola]] [[synonyms:a:anantare]]---after the Dola-yātrā; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bade farewell; [[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---endowing with all kinds of mercy; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]] [[synonyms:s:sañcārilā]]---empowered him.

===== Translation =====

**After the Dola-yātrā festival ended, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade farewell to Rūpa Gosvāmī also. The Lord empowered him and bestowed upon him all kinds of mercy.**
