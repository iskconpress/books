====== CC Antya 1.123 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"kon grantha kara hena jāni?
> yāhāra bhitare ei siddhāntera khani?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya inquired; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---dramatic literature; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---you are writing; [[synonyms:h:hena]]---such; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I can understand; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within which; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:s:siddhāntera]] [[synonyms:k:khani]]---a mine of conclusive statements.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya inquired, "What kind of drama are you writing? We ca understand that it is a mine of conclusive statements."**
