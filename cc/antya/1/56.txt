====== CC Antya 1.56 ======

===== Text =====

> 'advaita nityānanda, tomarā dui-jane
> prabhu kahe---rūpe kṛpā kara kāya-mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:t:tomarā]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---both of You; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---to Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---show; [[synonyms:k:kāya]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---wholeheartedly.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu, "You should both show Your mercy wholeheartedly to Rūpa Gosvāmī.**
