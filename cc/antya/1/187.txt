====== CC Antya 1.187 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"kaha āge aṅgera viśeṣa"
> śrī-rūpa kahena kichu saṅkṣepa-uddeśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:k:kaha]]---please tell me; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---further; [[synonyms:a:aṅgera]] [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---particular portions; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī says; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepa]]---in brief; [[synonyms:u:uddeśa]]---reference.

===== Translation =====

**When Rāmānanda Rāya requested Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī to speak further about various portions of the play, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī briefly quoted his Lalita-mādhava.**
