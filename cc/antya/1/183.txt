====== CC Antya 1.183 ======

===== Text =====

> rāya kahe,---"kon aṅge pātrera praveśa?"
> tabe rūpa-gosāñi kahe tāhāra viśeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Rāmānanda Rāya says; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---what; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---subdivision of style; [[synonyms:p:pātrera]] [[synonyms:p:praveśa]]---the entrance of the players; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---continues to speak; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]] [[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---specifically on this matter.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya inquired, "By which subdivision of style do the players enter?" Rūpa Gosvāmī then began to speak specifically about this subject.**
