====== CC Antya 1.100 ======

===== Text =====

> śloka śuni haridāsa ha-ila ullāsī
> nācite lāgilā ślokera artha praśaṁsi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this verse; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:u:ullāsī]]---became very jubilant; [[synonyms:n:nācite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---he began to dance; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsi']]---praising the meaning.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted this verse, Haridāsa Ṭhākura, upon hearing the vibration, became jubilant and began dancing and praising its meaning.**
