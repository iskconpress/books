====== Cc. Antya 14.70 ======

===== Text =====

> bahu-kṣaṇe kṛṣṇa-nāma hṛdaye paśilā
> 'hari-bola' bali' prabhu garjiyā uṭhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---after a long time; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]] [[synonyms:p:paśilā]]---entered the heart; [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:b:bola]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying Hari bol; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:garjiyā]]---making a loud sound; [[synonyms:u:uṭhilā]]---got up.

===== Translation =====

**After they chanted in this way for a long time, the holy name of Kṛṣṇa entered the heart of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and He suddenly arose with a loud shout of "Hari bol."**
