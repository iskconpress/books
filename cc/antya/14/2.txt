====== CC Antya 14.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya svayaṁ bhagavān
> jaya jaya gauracandra bhakta-gaṇa-prāṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:svayam]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Personality of Godhead Himself; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]---the life and soul of the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead! All glories to Lord Gauracandra, the life and soul of His devotees.**
