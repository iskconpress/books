====== CC Antya 14.117 ======

===== Text =====

> snāna kari' mahāprabhu gharete āilā
> sabā lañā mahā-prasāda bhojana karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after taking a bath; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gharete]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned home; [[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking everyone with Him; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food from Jagannātha; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---ate.

===== Translation =====

**After bathing in the sea, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His residence with all the devotees. Then they all lunched on the remnants of food offered to Lord Jagannātha.**
