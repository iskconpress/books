====== CC Antya 14.103 ======

===== Text =====

> uṭhi' mahāprabhu vismita, iti uti cāya
> ye dekhite cāya, tāhā dekhite nā pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---standing up; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vismita]]---astonished; [[synonyms:i:iti]] [[synonyms:u:uti]]---here and there; [[synonyms:c:cāya]]---looks; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---what; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:c:cāya]]---He wanted to see; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---He could not see.

===== Translation =====

**Astonished, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood up and began looking here and there, trying to see something. But He could not catch sight of it.**
