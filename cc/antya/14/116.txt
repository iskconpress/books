====== Cc. Antya 14.116 ======

===== Text =====

> lajjita ha-ilā prabhu purīra vacane
> samudra-ghāṭa āilā saba vaiṣṇava-sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lajjita]]---ashamed; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---by the words of Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:s:samudra]]---of the sea; [[synonyms:g:ghāṭa]]---to the bathing place; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with all the Vaiṣṇavas.

===== Translation =====

**When He heard this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was somewhat ashamed. Then He went to bathe in the sea with all the Vaiṣṇavas.**
