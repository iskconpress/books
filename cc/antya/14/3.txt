{{description>All glories to Lord Nityānanda, who is Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's very life. And all glories to Advaita Ācārya, who is extremely dear to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.}}

====== CC Antya 14.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya nityānanda caitanya-jīvana
> jayādvaitācārya jaya gaura-priyatama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:j:jīvana]]---the life of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---very, very dear to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Nityānanda, who is Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's very life. And all glories to Advaita Ācārya, who is extremely dear to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
