====== CC Antya 14.61 ======

===== Text =====

> cintita ha-ila sabe prabhure nā dekhiyā
> prabhu cāhi' bule sabe deuṭī jvāliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cintita]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became very anxious; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---not seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:cāhi']]---searching for; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---wander; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:d:deuṭī]]---lamp; [[synonyms:j:jvāliyā]]---lighting.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees were very anxious when they saw that the Lord was not in His room. They wandered about searching for Him with a warning lamp.**
