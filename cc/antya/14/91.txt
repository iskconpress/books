====== CC Antya 14.91 ======

===== Text =====

> prathame calilā prabhu,---yena vāyu-gati
> stambha-bhāva pathe haila, calite nāhi śakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathame]]---in the beginning; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---like; [[synonyms:v:vāyu]]-[[synonyms:g:gati]]---the speed of the wind; [[synonyms:s:stambha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the emotion of being stunned; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:c:calite]]---to move; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---no; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was running with the speed of the wind, but He suddenly became stunned in ecstasy and lost all strength to proceed further.**
