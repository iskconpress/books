====== Cc. Antya 14.10 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa---'sūtra-kartā', raghunātha---'vṛttikāra'
> tāra bāhulya varṇi---pāṅji-ṭīkā-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sūtra]]-[[synonyms:k:kartā]]---maker of short codes; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:v:vṛttikāra]]---maker of elaborate explanations; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of those; [[synonyms:b:bāhulya]]---more elaborately; [[synonyms:v:varṇi]]---I shall describe; [[synonyms:p:pāṅji]]---fluffing out (as of cotton); [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭīkā]]---explanation; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---behavior.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara wrote short codes, whereas Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī wrote elaborate descriptions. I shall now describe Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's activities more elaborately, as if fluffing out compressed cotton.**

===== Purport =====

//Pāṅji-ṭīkā// means further explanations of a subject. Writing such explanations is likened to the process of fluffing out cotton.
