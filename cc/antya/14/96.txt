====== Cc. Antya 14.96 ======

===== Text =====

> kāṅpite kāṅpite prabhu bhūmete paḍilā
> tabe ta' govinda prabhura nikaṭe āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāṅpite]] [[synonyms:k:kāṅpite]]---while shivering; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhūmete]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---fell down; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**While quivering in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell down on the ground. Then Govinda approached Him.**
