====== Cc. Antya 14.34 ======

===== Text =====

> kurukṣetre dekhi' kṛṣṇe aiche haila mana
> 'kāhāṅ kurukṣetre āilāṅa, kāhāṅ vṛndāvana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kurukṣetre]]---in Kurukṣetra; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---became His mind; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:k:kurukṣetre]] [[synonyms:ā:āilāṅa]]---I have come to Kurukṣetra; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**When He saw the Deities, Lord Caitanya thought He was seeing Kṛṣṇa in Kurukṣetra. He wondered, "Have I come to Kurukṣetra? Where is Vṛndāvana?"**
