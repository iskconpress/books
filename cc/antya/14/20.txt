====== Cc. Antya 14.20 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' prabhu sei rase āviṣṭa hailā
> 'vṛndāvane kṛṣṇa pāinu'---ei jñāna kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:r:rase]]---in the transcendental mellow; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became overwhelmed; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I have gotten Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---He thought.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was overwhelmed with the transcendental mellow of the rāsa dance, and He thought, "Now I am with Kṛṣṇa in Vṛndāvana."**
