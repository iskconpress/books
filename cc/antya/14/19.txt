====== CC Antya 14.19 ======

===== Text =====

> maṇḍalī-bandhe gopī-gaṇa karena nartana
> madhye rādhā-saha nāce vrajendra-nandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:maṇḍalī]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhe]]---in a circle; [[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---engaged in dancing; [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the middle; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:n:nandana]]---Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda.

===== Translation =====

**The gopīs were dancing in a circle, and in the middle of that circle, Kṛṣṇa, the son of Mahārāja Nanda, danced with Rādhārāṇī.**
