====== Cc. Antya 14.29 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthe āviṣṭa ihāra tanu-mana-prāṇe
> mora skandhe pada diyāche, tāho nāhi jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]]---in Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]]---fully absorbed; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of this woman; [[synonyms:t:tanu]]---body; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:p:prāṇe]]---life; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:skandhe]]---upon My shoulder; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---foot; [[synonyms:d:diyāche]]---has put; [[synonyms:t:tāho]]---she; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---did not understand.

===== Translation =====

**"She has fully absorbed her body, mind and life in Lord Jagannātha. Therefore she was unaware that she was putting her foot on My shoulder.**
