====== CC Antya 14.35 ======

===== Text =====

> prāpta-ratna hārāñā aiche vyagra ha-ilā
> viṣaṇṇa hañā prabhu nija-vāsā āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prāpta]]-[[synonyms:r:ratna]]---the achieved jewel; [[synonyms:h:hārāñā]]---having lost; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:v:vyagra]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became very agitated; [[synonyms:v:viṣaṇṇa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being morose; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]---own; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---to His residential place; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya grew very agitated, like a person who has just lost a recently acquired jewel. Then He became very morose and returned home.**
