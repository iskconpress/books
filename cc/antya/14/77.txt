====== CC Antya 14.77 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' mahāprabhu baḍa hailā camatkāra
> prabhu kahe,---'kichu smṛti nāhika āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very much; [[synonyms:h:hailā]] [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---became astonished; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---any; [[synonyms:s:smṛti]]---remembrance; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---is not; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---Mine.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing the description of His condition while lying near the Siṁha-dvāra, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very astonished. He said, "I do not remember any of these things.**
