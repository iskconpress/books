====== CC Antya 14.122 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahiyā kari dik daraśana
> yei ihā śune, pāya kṛṣṇera caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]]---in brief; [[synonyms:k:kahiyā]]---describing; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:d:dik]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---I show an indication; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the shelter of the lotus feet of Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**I have briefly described them just to give an indication of His transcendental pastimes. Nevertheless, anyone who hears this will certainly attain the shelter of Lord Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet.**
