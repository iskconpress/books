====== Cc. Antya 14.87 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloka paḍi' prabhu calena vāyu-vege
> govinda dhāila pāche, nāhi pāya lāge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:calena]]---goes; [[synonyms:v:vāyu]]-[[synonyms:v:vege]]---at the speed of the wind; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:d:dhāila]]---ran; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---behind; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]] [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---could not catch.

===== Translation =====

**Reciting this verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ran toward the sand dune as fast as the wind. Govinda ran after Him, but he could not approach Him.**
