====== CC Antya 14.6 ======

===== Text =====

> bujhite nā pāri yāhā, varṇite ke pāre?
> sei bujhe, varṇe, caitanya śakti dena yāṅre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---not being able; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---subject which; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]] [[synonyms:k:ke]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---who can describe; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bujhe]]---he can understand; [[synonyms:v:varṇe]]---can describe; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---capacity; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---to whom.

===== Translation =====

**How can one describe unfathomable subject matters? It is possible only if Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gives him the capability.**
