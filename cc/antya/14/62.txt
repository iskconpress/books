====== CC Antya 14.62 ======

===== Text =====

> siṁha-dvārera uttara-diśāya āche eka ṭhāñi
> tāra madhye paḍi' āchena caitanya-gosāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārera]]---of the gate known as Siṁha-dvāra; [[synonyms:u:uttara]]-[[synonyms:d:diśāya]]---on the northern side; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there is; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---a place; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that place; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---lying down; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---was; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After searching for some time, they came upon Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lying in a corner by the northern side of the Siṁha-dvāra gate.**
