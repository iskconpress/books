====== CC Antya 14.118 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ prabhura divyonmāda-bhāva
> brahmāo kahite nāre yāhāra prabhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---transcendental ecstatic emotions; [[synonyms:b:brahmāo]]---even Lord Brahmā; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---cannot speak; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---the influence.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described the transcendental ecstatic emotions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Even Lord Brahmā cannot describe their influence.**
