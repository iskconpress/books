====== CC Antya 14.27 ======

===== Text =====

> āste-vyaste sei nārī bhūmete nāmilā
> mahāprabhure dekhi' tāṅra caraṇa vandilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---in great haste; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---that woman; [[synonyms:b:bhūmete]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:n:nāmilā]]---got down; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandilā]]---begged pardon at the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**When the woman came to her senses, however, she quickly climbed back down to the ground and, seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, immediately begged at His lotus feet for forgiveness.**
