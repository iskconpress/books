====== Cc. Antya 14.58 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-rāya tabe gelā nija ghare
> svarūpa-govinda duṅhe śuilena dvāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to his home; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---and Govinda; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:ś:śuilena]]---lay down; [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door.

===== Translation =====

**Then Rāmānanda Rāya returned home, and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, along with Govinda, lay down in front of the door to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's room.**
