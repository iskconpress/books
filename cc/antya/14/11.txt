====== Cc. Antya 14.11 ======

===== Text =====

> tāte viśvāsa kari' śuna bhāvera varṇana
> ha-ibe bhāvera jñāna, pāibā prema-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---having faith; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description of ecstatic emotions; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:b:bhāvera]]---of ecstatic emotions; [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:p:pāibā]]---you will get; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Please hear faithfully this description of Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic emotions. Thus you will come to know of His ecstatic love, and ultimately you will achieve love of Godhead.**
