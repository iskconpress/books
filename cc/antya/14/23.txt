====== CC Antya 14.23 ======

===== Text =====

> yāvat kāla darśana karena garuḍera pāche
> prabhura āge darśana kare loka lākhe lākhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāvat]] [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---as long as; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:g:garuḍera]] [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---from the back of the Garuḍa column; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---see; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:l:lākhe]] [[synonyms:l:lākhe]]---in hundreds of thousands.

===== Translation =====

**As He viewed Lord Jagannātha from behind the Garuḍa column, hundreds and thousands of people in front of Him were seeing the Deity.**
