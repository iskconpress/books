====== CC Antya 14.80 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ prabhura adbhuta vikāra
> yāhāra śravaṇe loke lāge camatkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]] [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---uncommon transformations of the body; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---hearing which; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---feel; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---astonishment.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described the uncommon transformations of the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. When people hear about this, they are very astonished.**
