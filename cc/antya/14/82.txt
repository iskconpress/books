====== CC Antya 14.82 ======

===== Text =====

> śāstra-lokātīta yei yei bhāva haya
> itara-lokera tāte nā haya niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:a:atīta]]---beyond the conception of people and the revealed scriptures; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---emotional ecstasies; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:i:itara]]-[[synonyms:l:lokera]]---of common men; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is not; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---belief.

===== Translation =====

**These ecstasies are not described in the śāstras, and they are inconceivable to common men. Therefore people in general do not believe in them.**
