====== CC Antya 14.85 ======

===== Text =====

> govardhana-śaila-jñāne āviṣṭa ha-ilā
> parvata-diśāte prabhu dhāñā calilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaila]]---Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:j:jñāne]]---by the understanding; [[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became overwhelmed; [[synonyms:p:parvata]]-[[synonyms:d:diśāte]]---in the direction of the sand hill; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---began to run.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu mistook the sand dune for Govardhana Hill and ran toward it.**
