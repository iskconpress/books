====== CC Antya 14.24 ======

===== Text =====

> uḍiyā eka strī bhīḍe darśana nā pāñā
> garuḍe caḍi' dekhe prabhura skandhe pada diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uḍiyā]]---a native of Orissa; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---woman; [[synonyms:b:bhīḍe]]---in the crowd; [[synonyms:d:darśana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---being unable to see; [[synonyms:g:garuḍe]] [[synonyms:c:caḍi']]---climbing up the column of Garuḍa; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:skandhe]]---on the shoulder; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---her foot; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---placing.

===== Translation =====

**Suddenly, a woman from Orissa, unable to see Lord Jagannātha because of the crowd, climbed the column of Garuḍa, placing her foot on Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's shoulder.**
