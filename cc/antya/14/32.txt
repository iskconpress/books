====== Cc. Antya 14.32 ======

===== Text =====

> svapnera darśanāveśe tad-rūpa haila mana
> yāhāṅ tāhāṅ dekhe sarvatra muralī-vadana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svapnera]]---of the dream; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---by being fully absorbed in the vision; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---like that; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind became; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---anywhere and everywhere; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---all around; [[synonyms:m:muralī]]-[[synonyms:v:vadana]]---Kṛṣṇa with His flute to His mouth.

===== Translation =====

**Becoming fully absorbed in that vision, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had assumed the mood of the gopīs, so much so that everywhere He looked He saw Kṛṣṇa standing with His flute to His lips.**
