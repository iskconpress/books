====== CC Antya 14.121 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe prabhu yata kailā alaukika-līlā
> ke varṇite pāre sei mahāprabhura khelā?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all that; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:a:alaukika]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---uncommon pastimes; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can describe; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---they; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:k:khelā]]---the play of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Who can properly describe all the uncommon pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu? They are all simply His play.**
