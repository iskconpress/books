====== CC Antya 14.93 ======

===== Text =====

> prati-rome prasveda paḍe rudhirera dhāra
> kaṇṭhe gharghara, nāhi varṇera uccāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:r:rome]]---from each hair; [[synonyms:p:prasveda]]---sweat; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---drops; [[synonyms:r:rudhirera]]---of blood; [[synonyms:d:dhāra]]---flow; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭhe]]---in the throat; [[synonyms:g:gharghara]]---gargling sound; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:v:varṇera]]---of letters; [[synonyms:u:uccāra]]---pronunciation.

===== Translation =====

**Blood and perspiration flowed incessantly from every pore of His body, and He could not speak a word but simply produced a gargling sound within His throat.**
