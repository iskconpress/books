====== CC Antya 14.74 ======

===== Text =====

> siṁha-dvāre dekhi' prabhura vismaya ha-ilā
> 'kāṅhā kara ki'---ei svarūpe puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the gate known as Siṁha-dvāra; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---there was astonishment; [[synonyms:k:kāṅhā]]---where; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:k:ki']]---what am I doing; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:svarūpe]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very astonished to find Himself in front of the Siṁha-dvāra. He asked Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, "Where am I? What am I doing here?"**
