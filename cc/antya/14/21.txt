====== CC Antya 14.21 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura vilamba dekhi' govinda jāgāilā
> jāgile 'svapna'-jñāna haila, prabhu duḥkhī hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vilamba]]---delay; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:g:govinda]] [[synonyms:j:jāgāilā]]---caused to awaken; [[synonyms:j:jāgile]]---when He awoke; [[synonyms:s:svapna]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---could understand that it was a dream; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became unhappy.

===== Translation =====

**When Govinda saw that the Lord had not yet risen, he awakened Him. Understanding that He had only been dreaming, the Lord was somewhat unhappy.**
