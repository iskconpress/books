====== CC Antya 14.104 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vaiṣṇava' dekhiyā prabhura ardha-bāhya ha-ila
> svarūpa-gosāñire kichu kahite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---half-external consciousness; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---unto Svarūpa Gosāñi; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw all the Vaiṣṇavas, He returned to partial external consciousness and spoke to Svarūpa Dāmodara.**
