====== CC Antya 14.52 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera viyoge gopīra daśa daśā haya
> sei daśa daśā haya prabhura udaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:v:viyoge]]---by separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gopīra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:d:daśa]] [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---ten kinds of transcendental transformations of the body; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there are; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:d:daśa]] [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---ten kinds of transcendental transformations; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---are; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:u:udaya]]---visible in the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**When the gopīs felt separation from Kṛṣṇa, they experienced ten kinds of bodily transformations. These same symptoms appeared on the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
