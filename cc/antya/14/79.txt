====== CC Antya 14.79 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle jagannāthera pāṇi-śaṅkha bājilā
> snāna kari' mahāprabhu daraśane gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:p:pāṇi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkha]]---a conchshell that can be held in the hand; [[synonyms:b:bājilā]]---vibrated; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after bathing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daraśane]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went to see Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Just then, everyone heard the blowing of the conchshell at the Jagannātha temple. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately took His bath and went to see Lord Jagannātha.**
