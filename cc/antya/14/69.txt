====== CC Antya 14.69 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi tabe ucca kariyā
> prabhura kāṇe kṛṣṇa-nāma kahe bhakta-gaṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:u:ucca]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---very loudly; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kāṇe]]---in the ear of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---began to chant; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with all the other devotees.

===== Translation =====

**When they saw this, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and all the other devotees began to chant the holy name of Kṛṣṇa very loudly into Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ear.**
