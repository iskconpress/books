====== CC Antya 14.22 ======

===== Text =====

> dehābhyāse nitya-kṛtya kari' samāpana
> kāle yāi' kailā jagannātha daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:a:abhyāse]]---as a habit; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]]---the daily duties; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:s:samāpana]]---finishing; [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the proper time; [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His customary daily duties, and at the usual time He went to see Lord Jagannātha in the temple.**
