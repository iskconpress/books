====== CC Antya 14.112 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' mahāprabhu karena krandana
> tāṅra daśā dekhi' vaiṣṇava karena rodana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---began to cry; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---His state; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:r:rodana]]---began to cry.

===== Translation =====

**Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to weep. When all the Vaiṣṇavas saw the Lord's condition, they also wept.**
