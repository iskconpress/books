====== Cc. Antya 14.108 ======

===== Text =====

> veṇu-nāda śuni' āilā rādhā-ṭhākurāṇī
> saba sakhī-gaṇa-saṅge kariyā sājani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:veṇu]]-[[synonyms:n:nāda]]---the vibration of the flute; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurāṇī]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---accompanied by //gopīs//; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sājani]]---nicely dressed.

===== Translation =====

**"Hearing the vibration of Kṛṣṇa's flute, Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī and all Her gopī friends came there to meet Him. They were all very nicely dressed.**
