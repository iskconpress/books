====== Cc. Antya 14.97 ======

===== Text =====

> karaṅgera jale kare sarvāṅga siñcana
> bahirvāsa lañā kare aṅga saṁvījana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karaṅgera]] [[synonyms:j:jale]]---with water from a //karaṅga// waterpot; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---all parts of the body; [[synonyms:s:siñcana]]---sprinkling; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---covering cloth; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the body; [[synonyms:s:saṁvījana]]---fanning.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda sprinkled water from a karaṅga waterpot all over the Lord's body, and then, taking His own outer garment, he began to fan Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
