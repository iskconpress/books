====== CC Antya 14.54 ======

===== Text =====

> ei daśa-daśāya prabhu vyākula rātri-dine
> kabhu kona daśā uṭhe, sthira nahe mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:d:daśa]]-[[synonyms:d:daśāya]]---by ten conditions; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---night and day; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---condition; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---arises; [[synonyms:s:sthira]]---steady; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was overwhelmed night and day by these ten ecstatic conditions. Whenever such symptoms arose, His mind became unsteady.**
