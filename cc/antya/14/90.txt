====== CC Antya 14.90 ======

===== Text =====

> purī-bhāratī-gosāñi āilā sindhu-tīre
> bhagavān-ācārya khañja calilā dhīre dhīre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Brahmānanda Bhāratī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:t:tīre]]---on the shore of the sea; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:khañja]]---lame; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---ran; [[synonyms:d:dhīre]] [[synonyms:d:dhīre]]---very slowly.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī and Brahmānanda Bhāratī also went toward the beach, and Bhagavān Ācārya, who was lame, followed them very slowly.**
