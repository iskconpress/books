====== CC Antya 14.81 ======

===== Text =====

> loke nāhi dekhi aiche, śāstre nāhi śuni
> hena bhāva vyakta kare nyāsi-cūḍāmaṇi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loke]]---among people; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---we do not see; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:ś:śāstre]]---in scripture; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---we do not hear; [[synonyms:h:hena]]---such; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---emotions; [[synonyms:v:vyakta]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---exhibits; [[synonyms:n:nyāsi]]-[[synonyms:c:cūḍāmaṇi]]---the supreme //sannyāsī.//

===== Translation =====

**No one has witnessed such bodily changes elsewhere, nor has anyone read of them in the revealed scriptures. Yet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the supreme sannyāsī, exhibited these ecstatic symptoms.**
