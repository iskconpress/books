====== Cc. Antya 14.13 ======

===== Text =====

> uddhava-darśane yaiche rādhāra vilāpa
> krame krame haila prabhura se unmāda-vilāpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uddhava]]-[[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by seeing Uddhava; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:v:vilāpa]]---lamentation; [[synonyms:k:krame]] [[synonyms:k:krame]]---gradually; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāpa]]---lamentation in madness.

===== Translation =====

**The lamentation of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī when Uddhava visited Vṛndāvana gradually became a feature of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's transcendental madness.**
