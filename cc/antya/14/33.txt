====== CC Antya 14.33 ======

===== Text =====

> ebe yadi strīre dekhi' prabhura bāhya haila
> jagannātha-subhadrā-balarāmera svarūpa dekhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:s:strīre]]---the woman; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was external consciousness; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:subhadrā]]---His sister, Subhadrā; [[synonyms:b:balarāmera]]---and of His elder brother, Balarāma; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---forms; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw.

===== Translation =====

**After seeing the woman, the Lord's external consciousness returned, and He saw the original Deity forms of Lord Jagannātha, Subhadrā and Lord Balarāma.**
