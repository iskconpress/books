====== CC Antya 14.26 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ādi-vasyā' ei strīre nā kara varjana
> karuka yatheṣṭa jagannātha daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:v:vasyā]]---uncivilized; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:s:strīre]]---woman; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:v:varjana]]---do not forbid; [[synonyms:k:karuka]]---let her do; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]---as desired; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to Govinda, "O ādi-vasyā [uncivilized man], do not forbid this woman to climb the Garuḍa-stambha. Let her see Lord Jagannātha to her satisfaction."**

===== Purport =====

For an explanation of the word //ādi-vasyā,// refer to //Antya-līlā,// Chapter Ten, verse 116.
