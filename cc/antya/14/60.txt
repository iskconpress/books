====== CC Antya 14.60 ======

===== Text =====

> śabda nā pāñā svarūpa kapāṭa kailā dūre
> tina-dvāra deoyā āche, prabhu nāhi ghare!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śabda]]---sound; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kapāṭa]]---the door; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---opened; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāra]]---three doors; [[synonyms:d:deoyā]] [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---were locked; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---was not within the room.

===== Translation =====

**After some time, Svarūpa Dāmodara could no longer hear Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanting. When he entered the room, he found the three doors locked, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had gone.**
