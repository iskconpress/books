====== CC Antya 14.100 ======

===== Text =====

> ucca saṅkīrtana kare prabhura śravaṇe
> śītala jale kare prabhura aṅga sammārjane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ucca]]---loud; [[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---within the hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śītala]]---cold; [[synonyms:j:jale]]---with water; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---body; [[synonyms:s:sammārjane]]---washing.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees loudly chanted the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra near Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and washed His body with cold water.**
