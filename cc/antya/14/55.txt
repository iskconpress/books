====== CC Antya 14.55 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' mahāprabhu mauna karilā
> rāmānanda-rāya śloka paḍite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---speaking this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---became silent; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to recite.

===== Translation =====

**After speaking in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell silent. Then Rāma-nanda Rāya began to recite various verses.**
