====== CC Antya 14.76 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhure dhari' ghare lañā gelā
> tāṅhāra avasthā saba kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---taking Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---home; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---took back; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:a:avasthā]]---His condition; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**Thus all the devotees carried Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu back to His residence. Then they all described to Him what had happened.**
