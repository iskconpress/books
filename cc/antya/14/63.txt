====== CC Antya 14.63 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' svarūpa-gosāñi-ādi ānandita hailā
> prabhura daśā dekhi' punaḥ cintite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very glad; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---the condition; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:c:cintite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---they began to feel anxiety.

===== Translation =====

**At first they were overjoyed to see Him, but when they saw His condition, all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, were very anxious.**
