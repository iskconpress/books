====== Cc. Antya 14.84 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina mahāprabhu samudre yāite
> 'caṭaka'-parvata dekhilena ācambite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:samudre]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---while going to the sea; [[synonyms:c:caṭaka]]-[[synonyms:p:parvata]]---the Caṭaka-parvata sand hill; [[synonyms:d:dekhilena]]---saw; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly.

===== Translation =====

**One day, while Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was going to the sea to bathe, He suddenly saw a sand dune named Caṭaka-parvata.**
