====== CC Antya 14.102 ======

===== Text =====

> sānande sakala vaiṣṇava bale 'hari' 'hari'
> uṭhila maṅgala-dhvani catur-dik bhari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānande]]---with great pleasure; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:b:bale]]---chanted; [[synonyms:h:hari]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---the holy name of the Lord; [[synonyms:u:uṭhila]]---there arose; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---an auspicious sound; [[synonyms:c:catuḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dik]]---all directions; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood up, all the Vaiṣṇavas loudly chanted, "Hari! Hari!" in great jubilation. The auspicious sound filled the air in all directions.**
