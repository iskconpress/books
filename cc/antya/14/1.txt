====== CC Antya 14.1 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-viccheda-vibhrāntyā
> manasā vapuṣā dhiyā
> yad yad vyadhatta gaurāṅgas
> tal-leśaḥ kathyate 'dhunā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viccheda]]---of separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vibhrāntyā]]---by the bewilderment; [[synonyms:m:manasā]]---by the mind; [[synonyms:v:vapuṣā]]---by the body; [[synonyms:d:dhiyā]]---by the intelligence; [[synonyms:y:yat]] [[synonyms:y:yat]]---whatever; [[synonyms:v:vyadhatta]]---performed; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅgaḥ]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---of that; [[synonyms:l:leśaḥ]]---a very small fragment; [[synonyms:k:kathyate]]---is being described; [[synonyms:a:adhunā]]---now.

===== Translation =====

**I shall now describe a very small portion of the activities performed by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with His mind, intelligence and body when He was bewildered by strong feelings of separation from Kṛṣṇa.**
