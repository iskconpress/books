====== CC Antya 14.89 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa, jagadānanda, paṇḍita-gadādhara
> rāmāi, nandāi, āra paṇḍita śaṅkara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:r:rāmāi]]---Rāmāi; [[synonyms:n:nandāi]]---Nandāi; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara Paṇḍita.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, Gadādhara Paṇḍita, Rāmāi, Nandāi and Śaṅkara Paṇḍita are some of the devotees who ran after Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
