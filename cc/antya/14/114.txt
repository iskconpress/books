====== CC Antya 14.114 ======

===== Text =====

> nipaṭṭa-bāhya ha-ile prabhu duṅhāre vandilā
> mahāprabhure dui-jana premāliṅgana kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nipaṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:b:bāhya]]---complete external consciousness; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ile]]---when there was; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāre]]---to both of them; [[synonyms:v:vandilā]]---offered prayers; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---both persons; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---embraced with love and affection.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to complete external consciousness and immediately offered prayers to them. Then these two elderly gentlemen both embraced the Lord with loving affection.**
