====== Cc. Antya 14.40 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri haile svarūpa-rāmānande lañā
> āpana manera bhāva kahe ughāḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]] [[synonyms:h:haile]]---when there was night; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rāmānande]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking together; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]] [[synonyms:m:manera]]---of His own mind; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the ecstatic feelings; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:u:ughāḍiyā]]---revealing.

===== Translation =====

**At night, Lord Caitanya would reveal to Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya the ecstatic feelings of His mind.**
