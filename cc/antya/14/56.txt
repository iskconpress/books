====== CC Antya 14.56 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi kare kṛṣṇa-līlā gāna
> dui jane kichu kailā prabhura bāhya jñāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of the pastimes of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---singing; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jane]]---both of them; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---revived; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]] [[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---external consciousness.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmānanda Rāya recited verses from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī sang of Kṛṣṇa's pastimes. In this way, they brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to external consciousness.**
