====== CC Antya 14.12 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa mathurāya gele, gopīra ye daśā haila
> kṛṣṇa-vicchede prabhura se daśā upajila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:m:mathurāya]] [[synonyms:g:gele]]---when Lord Kṛṣṇa departed for Mathurā; [[synonyms:g:gopīra]]---of the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---which condition; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vicchede]]---by separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---that situation; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---happened.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt separation from Kṛṣṇa, His condition exactly corresponded to the condition of the gopīs in Vṛndāvana after Kṛṣṇa's departure for Mathurā.**
