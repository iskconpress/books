====== CC Antya 14.83 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha-dāsera sadā prabhu-saṅge sthiti
> tāṅra mukhe śuni' likhi kariyā pratīti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsera]]---of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---living; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---from his mouth; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:l:likhi]]---I write; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:p:pratīti]]---accepting in total.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī lived continuously with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. I am simply recording whatever I have heard from him. Although common men do not believe in these pastimes, I believe in them totally.**
