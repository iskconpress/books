====== Cc. Antya 14.98 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpādi-gaṇa tāhāṅ āsiyā mililā
> prabhura avasthā dekhi' kāndite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avasthā]]---condition; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:kāndite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to cry.

===== Translation =====

**When Svarūpa Dāmodara and the other devotees reached the spot and saw the condition of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they began to cry.**
