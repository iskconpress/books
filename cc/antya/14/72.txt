====== Cc. Antya 14.72 ======

===== Text =====

> ei līlā mahāprabhura raghunātha-dāsa
> 'gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣe' kariyāche prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---this pastime; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:stava]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]]---in the book known as //Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa//; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---has described.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī has described these pastimes elaborately in his book Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa.**
