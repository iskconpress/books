====== Cc. Antya 14.119 ======

===== Text =====

> 'caṭaka'-giri-gamana-līlā raghunātha-dāsa
> 'gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣe' kariyāchena prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caṭaka]]-[[synonyms:g:giri]]---the sand hill known as Caṭaka-parvata; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---of going to; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastime; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:stava]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]]---in the book known as //Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa//; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---has described.

===== Translation =====

**In his book Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa, Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī has very vividly described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastime of running toward the Caṭaka-parvata sand dune.**
