====== CC Antya 14.4 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya svarūpa, śrīvāsādi prabhu-bhakta-gaṇa
> śakti deha',---kari yena caitanya-varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---to Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the devotees of the Lord; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---please give strength; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I can make; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---so that; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Svarūpa Dāmodara and the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura. Please give me the strength to describe the character of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
