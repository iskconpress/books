====== CC Antya 15.13 ======

===== Text =====

> sei śloka paḍi' āpane kare manastāpa
> ślokera artha śunāya duṅhāre kariyā vilāpa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---that verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:t:tāpa]]---burning of the mind; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:ś:śunāya]]---causes to hear; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāre]]---both; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:v:vilāpa]]---lamenting.

===== Translation =====

**Reciting that verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu expressed His burning emotions. Then, with great lamentation, He explained the verse to Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya.**
