====== CC Antya 15.44 ======

===== Text =====

> apy eṇa-patny upagataḥ priyayeha gātrais
> tanvan dṛśāṁ sakhi sunirvṛtim acyuto vaḥ
> kāntāṅga-saṅga-kuca-kuṅkuma-rañjitāyāḥ
> kunda-srajaḥ kula-pater iha vāti gandhaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:api]]---whether; [[synonyms:e:eṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:patnī]]---O she-deer; [[synonyms:u:upagataḥ]]---has come; [[synonyms:p:priyayā]]---along with His dearmost companion; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---here; [[synonyms:g:gātraiḥ]]---by the bodily limbs; [[synonyms:t:tanvan]]---increasing; [[synonyms:d:dṛśām]]---of the eyes; [[synonyms:s:sakhi]]---O my dear friend; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:n:nirvṛtim]]---happiness; [[synonyms:a:acyutaḥ]]---Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vaḥ]]---of all of you; [[synonyms:k:kānta]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---with the body of the beloved; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---by association; [[synonyms:k:kuca]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṅkuma]]---with //kuṅkuma// powder from the breasts; [[synonyms:r:rañjitāyāḥ]]---colored; [[synonyms:k:kunda]]-[[synonyms:s:srajaḥ]]---of the garland of //kunda// flowers; [[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:p:pateḥ]]---of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:i:iha]]---here; [[synonyms:v:vāti]]---flows; [[synonyms:g:gandhaḥ]]---the fragrance.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'O wife of the deer, Lord Kṛṣṇa has been embracing His beloved, and thus the kuṅkuma powder on Her raised breasts has covered His garland of kunda flowers. The fragrance of this garland is flowing here. O my dear friend, have you seen Kṛṣṇa passing this way with His dearmost companion, increasing the pleasure of the eyes of all of you?'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is quoted from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.30.11).
