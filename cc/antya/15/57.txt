====== CC Antya 15.57 ======

===== Text =====

> saundarya dekhiyā bhūme paḍe mūrcchā pāñā
> hena-kāle svarūpādi mililā āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---beauty; [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:bhūme]]---on the ground; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell; [[synonyms:m:mūrcchā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---becoming unconscious; [[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:m:mililā]] [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---came there and met.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the transcendental beauty of Kṛṣṇa, He fell down on the ground unconscious. At that time, all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, joined Him in the garden.**
