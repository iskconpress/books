====== CC Antya 15.33 ======

===== Text =====

> kaccit tulasi kalyāṇi
> govinda-caraṇa-priye
> saha tvāli-kulair bibhrad
> dṛṣṭas te 'ti-priyo 'cyutaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaccit]]---whether; [[synonyms:t:tulasi]]---O //tulasī// plant; [[synonyms:k:kalyāṇi]]---all-auspicious; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---to Govinda's lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:priye]]---very dear; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:t:tvā]]---you; [[synonyms:a:ali]]-[[synonyms:k:kulaiḥ]]---bumblebees; [[synonyms:b:bibhrat]]---bearing; [[synonyms:d:dṛṣṭaḥ]]---has been seen; [[synonyms:t:te]]---your; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:p:priyaḥ]]---very dear; [[synonyms:a:acyutaḥ]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'O all-auspicious tulasī plant, you are very dear to Govinda's lotus feet, and He is very dear to you. Have you seen Kṛṣṇa walking here wearing a garland of your leaves, surrounded by a swarm of bumblebees?**
