====== CC Antya 15.95 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahiluṅ prabhura udyāna-vihāra
> vṛndāvana-bhrame yāhāṅ praveśa tāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:udyāna]]-[[synonyms:v:vihāra]]---pastimes in the garden; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrame]]---mistaking for Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:p:praveśa]]---entrance; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---His.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes in the garden, which He entered, mistaking it for Vṛndāvana.**
