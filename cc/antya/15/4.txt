====== Cc. Antya 15.4 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu rātri-divase
> ātma-sphūrti nāhi kṛṣṇa-bhāvāveśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:divase]]---night and day; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrti]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---forgot Himself; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---being merged in ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu forgot Himself throughout the entire day and night, being merged in an ocean of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
