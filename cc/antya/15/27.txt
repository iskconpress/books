====== Cc. Antya 15.27 ======

===== Text =====

> karṇāmṛta, vidyāpati, śrī-gīta-govinda
> ihāra śloka-gīte prabhura karāya ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:karṇāmṛta]]---the book //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta//; [[synonyms:v:vidyāpati]]---the author Vidyāpati; [[synonyms:ś:śri]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the book //Śrī Gīta-govinda// by Jayadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]]---of these; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:g:gīte]]---verses and songs; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāya]]---create; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---happiness.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord especially liked to hear Bilvamaṅgala Ṭhākura's Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta, the poetry of Vidyāpati and Śrī Gīta-govinda by Jayadeva Gosvāmī. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt great pleasure in His heart when His associates chanted verses from these books.**
