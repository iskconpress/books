====== CC Antya 15.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jayādvaitācārya kṛṣṇa-caitanya-priyatama
> jaya śrīvāsa-ādi prabhura bhakta-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:priya]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---very dear; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the devotees.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Advaita Ācārya, who is very dear to Lord Caitanya! And all glories to the devotees of the Lord, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura!**
