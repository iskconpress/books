====== CC Antya 15.83 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi tabe madhura kariyā
> gīta-govindera pada gāya prabhure śunāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:madhura]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---very sweetly; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:g:govindera]]---of the book //Gīta-govinda//; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---one verse; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sings; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śunāñā]]---making to hear.

===== Translation =====

**Thus for the pleasure of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī began very sweetly singing the following verse from Gīta-govinda.**
