====== CC Antya 15.12 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera viyoge rādhāra utkaṇṭhita mana
> viśākhāre kahe āpana utkaṇṭhā-kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---from Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viyoge]]---in separation; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]]---very agitated; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:v:viśākhāre]]---to Viśākhā; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---spoke; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]---own; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of great anxiety and restlessness.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī was very agitated due to feeling great separation from Kṛṣṇa, She spoke a verse to Viśākhā explaining the cause of Her great anxiety and restlessness.**
