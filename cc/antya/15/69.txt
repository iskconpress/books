~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya 15.69~~

====== CC Antya 15.69 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ kahe,---'hāya hāya, paḍa paḍa rāma-raya',
> kahe prabhu gadgada ākhyāne
> rāmānanda paḍe śloka, śuni' prabhura harṣa-śoka,
> āpane prabhu karena vyākhyāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:h:hāya]] [[synonyms:h:hāya]]---alas, alas; [[synonyms:p:paḍa]] [[synonyms:p:paḍa]]---go on reading; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gadgada]] [[synonyms:ā:ākhyāne]]---in a faltering voice; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---reads; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---a verse; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]-[[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---jubilation and lamentation; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:v:vyākhyāne]]---explains.

===== Translation =====

**In a faltering voice, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again said, "Alas, go on reading, Rāma Rāya." Thus Rāmānanda Rāya began to read a verse. While listening to this verse, the Lord was sometimes very jubilant and sometimes overcome by lamentation. Afterwards the Lord personally explained the verse.**
