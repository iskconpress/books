====== Cc. Antya 15.88 ======

===== Text =====

> sei pada punaḥ punaḥ karāya gāyana
> punaḥ punaḥ āsvādaye, karena nartana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:pada]]---that verse; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:k:karāya]] [[synonyms:g:gāyana]]---made to sing; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādaye]]---tastes; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---dances.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu had Svarūpa Dāmodara sing the same verse again and again. Each time he sang it, the Lord tasted it anew, and thus He danced again and again.**
