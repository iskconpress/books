====== CC Antya 15.11 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa, rāmānanda,---ei dui-jana lañā
> vilāpa karena duṅhāra kaṇṭhete dhariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---these two personalities; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with; [[synonyms:v:vilāpa]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---laments; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭhete]]---the necks; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]]---holding.

===== Translation =====

**That night, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was attended by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya. Keeping His hands around their necks, the Lord began to lament.**
