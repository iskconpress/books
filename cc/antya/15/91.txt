====== Cc. Antya 15.91 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bal' 'bal' prabhu balena, bhakta-gaṇa śuni'
> caudikete sabe meli' kare hari-dhvani

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bal]] [[synonyms:b:bal]]---go on singing, go on singing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:b:balena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:c:cau]]-[[synonyms:d:dikete]]---all around; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:m:meli']]---combining; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]-[[synonyms:d:dhvani]]---vibrate the holy name of Hari.

===== Translation =====

**When the devotees heard Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu say, "Go on singing!" they all gathered around Him and began to chant the holy name of Hari in unison.**
