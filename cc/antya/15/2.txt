====== CC Antya 15.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya adhīśvara
> jaya nityānanda pūrṇānanda-kalevara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:adhīśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---filled with transcendental pleasure; [[synonyms:k:kalevara]]---His body.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya, the Supreme Personality of Godhead! All glories to Lord Nityānanda, whose body is always filled with transcendental bliss!**
