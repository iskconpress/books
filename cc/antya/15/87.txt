====== Cc. Antya 15.87 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāvodaya, bhāva-sandhi, bhāva-śābalya
> bhāve-bhāve mahā-yuddhe sabāra prābalya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---awakening of all the ecstatic symptoms; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:s:sandhi]]---meeting of ecstatic symptoms; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śābalya]]---mixing of ecstatic symptoms; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---between one ecstasy and another; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:y:yuddhe]]---a great fight; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:p:prābalya]]---prominence.

===== Translation =====

**All the ecstatic symptoms, such as bhāvodaya, bhāva-sandhi and bhāva-śābalya, awakened in the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. A great fight arose between one emotion and another, and each of them became prominent.**
