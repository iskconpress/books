====== CC Antya 15.34 ======

===== Text =====

> mālaty adarśi vaḥ kaccin
> mallike jāti yūthike
> prītiṁ vo janayan yātaḥ
> kara-sparśena mādhavaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mālati]]---O plant of //mālatī// flowers; [[synonyms:a:adarśi]]---was seen; [[synonyms:v:vaḥ]]---by you; [[synonyms:k:kaccit]]---whether; [[synonyms:m:mallike]]---O plant of //mallikā// flowers; [[synonyms:j:jāti]]---O plant of //jātī// flowers; [[synonyms:y:yūthike]]---O plant of //yūthikā// flowers; [[synonyms:p:prītim]]---pleasure; [[synonyms:v:vaḥ]]---your; [[synonyms:j:janayan]]---creating; [[synonyms:y:yātaḥ]]---passed by; [[synonyms:k:kara]]-[[synonyms:s:sparśena]]---by the touch of His hand; [[synonyms:m:mādhavaḥ]]---Śrī Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'O plants of mālatī flowers, mallikā flowers, jātī and yūthikā flowers, have you seen Kṛṣṇa passing this way, touching you with His hand to give you pleasure?' "**
