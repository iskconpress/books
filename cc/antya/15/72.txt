====== Cc. Antya 15.72 ======

===== Text =====

> bāndhava kṛṣṇa kare vyādhera ācāra
> nāhi māne dharmādharma, hare nārī-mṛgī-marma,
> kare nānā upāya tāhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāndhava]]---O friend; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:v:vyādhera]] [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---the behavior of a hunter; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---cares for; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:a:adharma]]---piety and impiety; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---attracts; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]---of a woman; [[synonyms:m:mṛgī]]---doe; [[synonyms:m:marma]]---the core of the heart; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---varieties of; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---means; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]]---for that purpose.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear friend, Kṛṣṇa acts just like a hunter. This hunter does not care for piety or impiety; He simply creates many devices to conquer the cores of the hearts of the doelike gopīs.**
