====== CC Antya 15.94 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana karāñā prabhure karāilā śayana
> rāmānanda-ādi sabe gelā nija-sthāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---feeding; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---made to lie down; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---to their homes.

===== Translation =====

**After they fed Him lunch, they made Him lie down. Then all the devotees, headed by Rāmānanda Rāya, returned to their respective homes.**
