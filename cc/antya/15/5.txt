====== Cc. Antya 15.5 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu bhāve magna, kabhu ardha-bāhya-sphūrti
> kabhu bāhya-sphūrti,---tina rīte prabhu-sthiti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:m:magna]]---merged; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:a:ardha]]---half; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---in external consciousness; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---in full external consciousness; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:r:rīte]]---in three ways; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---the situation of the Lord.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord would maintain Himself in three kinds of consciousness: sometimes He merged totally in ecstatic emotion, sometimes He was in partial external consciousness and sometimes in full external consciousness.**
