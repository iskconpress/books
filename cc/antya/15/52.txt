====== Cc. Antya 15.52 ======

===== Text =====

> priyā-mukhe bhṛṅga paḍe, tāhā nivārite
> līlā-padma cālāite haila anya-citte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:priyā]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhe]]---on His beloved's face; [[synonyms:b:bhṛṅga]]---the bumblebees; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fall; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nivārite]]---to prevent; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes; [[synonyms:p:padma]]---the lotus flower; [[synonyms:c:cālāite]]---causing to move; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:a:anya]]-[[synonyms:c:citte]]---diverted in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'To stop the bumblebees from landing on the face of His beloved, He whisked them away with the lotus flower in His hand, and thus His mind was slightly diverted.**
