====== CC Antya 15.47 ======

===== Text =====

> rādhā-aṅga-saṅge kuca-kuṅkuma-bhūṣita
> kṛṣṇa-kunda-mālā-gandhe vāyu---suvāsita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the body of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---by embracing; [[synonyms:k:kuca]]-[[synonyms:k:kuṅkuma]]---with the //kuṅkuma// from the breasts; [[synonyms:b:bhūṣita]]---decorated; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kunda]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---of the garland of //kunda// flowers; [[synonyms:g:gandhe]]---by the fragrance; [[synonyms:v:vāyu]]---the air; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsita]]---aromatic.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Kṛṣṇa has been embracing Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, and the kuṅkuma powder on Her breasts has mixed with the garland of kunda flowers decorating His body. The fragrance of the garland has scented the entire atmosphere.**
