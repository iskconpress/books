====== CC Antya 15.50 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇe dekhi' ei saba karena namaskāra
> kṛṣṇa-gamana puche tāre kariyā nirdhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---offer respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---the passing of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquire; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---from them; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:n:nirdhāra]]---making certain.

===== Translation =====

**"The gopīs thought that because all the trees must have seen Kṛṣṇa pass by they were offering respectful obeisances to Him. To be certain, the gopīs inquired from the trees.**
