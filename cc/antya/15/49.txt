====== CC Antya 15.49 ======

===== Text =====

> āge vṛkṣa-gaṇa dekhe puṣpa-phala-bhare
> śākhā saba paḍiyāche pṛthivī-upare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the trees; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---see; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:b:bhare]]---because of the heavy burden of flowers and fruits; [[synonyms:ś:śākhā]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all the branches; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyāche]]---have bent down; [[synonyms:p:pṛthivī]]-[[synonyms:u:upare]]---to the ground.

===== Translation =====

**"The gopīs then came upon many trees so laden with fruits and flowers that their branches bent down to the ground.**
