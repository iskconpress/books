====== CC Antya 15.93 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure lañā gelā sabe samudrera tīre
> snāna karāñā punaḥ tāṅre lañā āilā ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:s:samudrera]] [[synonyms:t:tīre]]---to the beach by the sea; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---bathing Him; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Him; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---brought back; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to His residence.

===== Translation =====

**Then all the devotees took Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to the beach and bathed Him. Finally they brought Him back home.**
