====== Cc. Antya 15.98 ======

===== Text =====

> ananta caitanya-līlā nā yāya likhana
> diṅ-mātra dekhāñā tāhā kariye sūcana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ananta]]---endless; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:l:likhana]]---it is impossible to write; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only a direction; [[synonyms:d:dekhāñā]]---showing; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---them; [[synonyms:k:kariye]] [[synonyms:s:sūcana]]---I introduce.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are unlimited; it is not possible to write of them properly. I can only give an indication of them as I try to introduce them.**
