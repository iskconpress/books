====== CC Antya 15.86 ======

===== Text =====

> 'aṣṭa-sāttvika' bhāva aṅge prakaṭa ha-ila
> harṣādi 'vyabhicārī' saba uthalila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:asta]]-[[synonyms:s:sāttvika]]---eight spiritual; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---emotions; [[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---on the body; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became manifest; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with jubilation; [[synonyms:v:vyabhicārī]]---thirty-three changes of //vyabhicārī-bhāva//; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:u:uthalila]]---were manifest.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, all eight kinds of spiritual transformations became manifest in Lord Caitanya's body. The thirty-three symptoms of vyabhicārī-bhāva, beginning with lamentation and jubilation, became prominent as well.**
