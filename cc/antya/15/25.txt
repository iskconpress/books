====== Cc. Antya 15.25 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata gaura-prabhu prati dine-dine
> vilāpa karena svarūpa-rāmānanda-sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prati]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day after day; [[synonyms:v:vilāpa]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---laments; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---in the company of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Thus absorbed in transcendental pain, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lamented day after day in the company of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya.**
