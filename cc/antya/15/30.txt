====== CC Antya 15.30 ======

===== Text =====

> rāse rādhā lañā kṛṣṇa antardhāna kailā
> pāche sakhī-gaṇa yaiche cāhi' beḍāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāse]]---in the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---disappeared; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---afterward; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:c:cāhi']]---looking; [[synonyms:b:beḍāilā]]---wandered.

===== Translation =====

**After Kṛṣṇa disappeared with Rādhārāṇī during the rāsa dance, the gopīs wandered in the forest looking for Him. In the same way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wandered in that garden by the sea.**
