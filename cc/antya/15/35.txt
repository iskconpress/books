====== Cc. Antya 15.35 ======

===== Text =====

> āmra, panasa, piyāla, jambu, kovidāra
> tīrtha-vāsī sabe, kara para-upakāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmra]]---O mango tree; [[synonyms:p:panasa]]---O jackfruit tree; [[synonyms:p:piyāla]]---O //piyāla// tree; [[synonyms:j:jambu]]---O //jambu// tree; [[synonyms:k:kovidāra]]---O //kovidāra// tree; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsī]]---inhabitants of a holy place; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---please do; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:u:upakāra]]---others' benefit.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued: " 'O mango tree, O jackfruit tree, O piyāla, jambu and kovidāra trees, you are all inhabitants of a holy place. Therefore kindly act for the welfare of others.**
