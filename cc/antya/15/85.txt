====== Cc. Antya 15.85 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi yabe ei pada gāhilā
> uṭhi' premāveśe prabhu nācite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---verse; [[synonyms:g:gāhilā]]---sang; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---standing up; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nācite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to dance.

===== Translation =====

**When Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī sang this special song, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately got up and began to dance in ecstatic love.**
