====== Cc. Antya 15.59 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat sabe mili' karāilā cetana
> uṭhiyā caudike prabhu karena darśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as before; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:m:mili']]---coming together; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]] [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---brought to consciousness; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]]---standing up; [[synonyms:c:cau]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---all around; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---was looking.

===== Translation =====

**Once again all the devotees brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu back to consciousness by a concerted effort. Then the Lord got up and began wandering here and there, looking all around.**
