====== CC Antya 15.16 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhi he, śuna mora duḥkhera kāraṇa
> mora pañcendriya-gaṇa, mahā-lampaṭa dasyu-gaṇa,
> sabe kahe,---hara' para-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhi]]---My dear friend; [[synonyms:h:he]]---O; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhera]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of unhappiness; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---five senses of perception; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]---very; [[synonyms:l:lampaṭa]]---extravagant; [[synonyms:d:dasyu]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---rogues; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---they all say; [[synonyms:h:hara']]---plunder; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---another's property.

===== Translation =====

**"O My dear friend, please hear the cause of My misery. My five senses are actually extravagant rogues. They know very well that Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, but they still want to plunder Kṛṣṇa's property.**
