====== CC Antya 15.39 ======

===== Text =====

> avaśya kahibe,---pāñāche kṛṣṇera darśane
> eta anumāni' puche tulasy-ādi-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:k:kahibe]]---they will say; [[synonyms:p:pāñāche]]---they have gotten; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---audience; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---this; [[synonyms:a:anumāni']]---guessing; [[synonyms:p:puche]]---inquire from; [[synonyms:t:tulasī]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the plants and creepers, headed by the //tulasī// plant.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'They will certainly tell us where Kṛṣṇa has gone, since they have seen Him personally.' Guessing in this way, the gopīs inquired from the plants and creepers, headed by tulasī.**
