====== Cc. Antya 15.92 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānanda-rāya tabe prabhure vasāilā
> vījanādi kari' prabhura śrama ghucāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---made to sit down; [[synonyms:v:vījana]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---fanning and so on; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---fatigue; [[synonyms:g:ghucāilā]]---dissipated.

===== Translation =====

**At that time, Rāmānanda Rāya made the Lord sit down and dissipated His fatigue by fanning Him.**
