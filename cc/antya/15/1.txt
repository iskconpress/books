~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya 15.1~~

====== CC Antya 15.1 ======

===== Text =====

> durgame kṛṣṇa-bhāvābdhau
> nimagnonmagna-cetasā
> gaureṇa hariṇā prema-
> maryādā bhūri darśitā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:durgame]]---very difficult to understand; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:a:abdhau]]---in the ocean of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nimagna]]---submerged; [[synonyms:u:unmagna]]-[[synonyms:c:cetasā]]---His heart being absorbed; [[synonyms:g:gaureṇa]]---by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hariṇā]]---by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:m:maryādā]]---the exalted position of transcendental love; [[synonyms:b:bhūri]]---in various ways; [[synonyms:d:darśitā]]---was exhibited.

===== Translation =====

**The ocean of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa is very difficult to understand, even for such demigods as Lord Brahmā. By enacting His pastimes, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu submerged Himself in that ocean, and His heart was absorbed in that love. Thus He exhibited in various ways the exalted position of transcendental love for Kṛṣṇa.**
