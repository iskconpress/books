====== Cc. Antya 15.10 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle īśvarera upala-bhoga sarila
> bhakta-gaṇa mahāprabhure ghare lañā āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:ī:īśvarera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:u:upala]]-[[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---the //upala-bhoga// ceremony; [[synonyms:s:sarila]]---was finished; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---home; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---brought.

===== Translation =====

**Just then, the upala-bhoga ceremony of Lord Jagannātha concluded, and the devotees who had accompanied Lord Caitanya to the temple brought Him back home.**
