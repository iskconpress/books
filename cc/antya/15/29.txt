====== Cc. Antya 15.29 ======

===== Text =====

> vṛndāvana-bhrame tāhāṅ paśilā dhāñā
> premāveśe bule tāhāṅ kṛṣṇa anveṣiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrame]]---taking it for Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:paśilā]]---entered; [[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---running; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---wanders; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:anveṣiyā]]---searching for.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Caitanya mistook that garden for Vṛndāvana and very quickly entered it. Absorbed in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa, He wandered throughout the garden, searching for Him.**
