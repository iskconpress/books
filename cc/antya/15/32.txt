====== CC Antya 15.32 ======

===== Text =====

> cūta-priyāla-panasāsana-kovidāra-
> jambv-arka-bilva-bakulāmra-kadamba-nīpāḥ
> ye 'nye parārtha-bhavakā yamunopakūlāḥ
> śaṁsantu kṛṣṇa-padavīṁ rahitātmanāṁ naḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cūta]]---O //cūta// tree (a kind of mango tree); [[synonyms:p:priyāla]]---O //priyāla// tree; [[synonyms:p:panasa]]---O jackfruit tree; [[synonyms:ā:āsana]]---O //āsana// tree; [[synonyms:k:kovidāra]]---O //kovidāra// tree; [[synonyms:j:jambu]]---O //jambu// tree; [[synonyms:a:arka]]---O //arka// tree; [[synonyms:b:bilva]]---O belfruit tree; [[synonyms:b:bakula]]---O //bakula// tree; [[synonyms:ā:āmra]]---O mango tree; [[synonyms:k:kadamba]]---O //kadamba// tree; [[synonyms:n:nīpāḥ]]---O //nīpa// tree; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:a:anye]]---others; [[synonyms:p:para]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavakāḥ]]---very beneficial to others; [[synonyms:y:yamunā]]-[[synonyms:u:upakūlāḥ]]---on the bank of the Yamunā; [[synonyms:ś:śaṁsantu]]---please tell; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:padavīm]]---where Kṛṣṇa has gone; [[synonyms:r:rahita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ātmanām]]---who have lost our minds; [[synonyms:n:naḥ]]---us.

===== Translation =====

**"(The gopīs said:) 'O cūta tree, priyāla tree, pasana, āsana and kovidāra! O jambu tree, O arka tree, O bel, bakula and mango! O kadamba tree, O nīpa tree and all other trees living on the bank of the Yamunā for the welfare of others, please let us know where Kṛṣṇa has gone. We have lost our minds and are almost dead.**
