====== CC Antya 15.31 ======

===== Text =====

> sei bhāvāveśe prabhu prati-taru-latā
> śloka paḍi' paḍi' cāhi' bule yathā tathā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:t:taru]]-[[synonyms:l:latā]]---each tree and creeper; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']] [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting verses; [[synonyms:c:cāhi']]---inquiring; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---wanders; [[synonyms:y:yathā]] [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---here and there.

===== Translation =====

**Absorbed in the ecstatic mood of the gopīs, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wandered here and there. He began to inquire after Kṛṣṇa by quoting verses to all the trees and creepers.**

===== Purport =====

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then quoted the following three verses from //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam// (10.30.9,7,8).
