====== Cc. Antya 15.77 ======

===== Text =====

> eteka vilāpa kari' premāveśe gaurahari,
> ei arthe paḍe eka śloka
> sei śloka paḍi' rādhā, viśākhāre kahe bādhā,
> ughāḍiyā hṛdayera śoka

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eteka]]---thus; [[synonyms:v:vilāpa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---lamenting; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:a:arthe]]---in understanding the purpose; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---one verse; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---this verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reading; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:v:viśākhāre]]---to Viśākhā; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:b:bādhā]]---obstacle; [[synonyms:u:ughāḍiyā]]---exposing; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayera]]---of the heart; [[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---lamentation.

===== Translation =====

**Lamenting in ecstatic love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then recited the following verse, which was spoken by Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī while exposing the lamentation of Her heart to Her friend Śrīmatī Viśākhā.**
