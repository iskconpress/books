====== Cc. Antya 15.26 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dui-jana prabhure kare āśvāsana
> svarūpa gāya, rāya kare śloka paṭhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsana]]---pacification; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara sings; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paṭhana]]---recitation of verses.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī would sing appropriate songs, and Rāmānanda Rāya would recite suitable verses to enhance the ecstatic mood of the Lord. In this way they were able to pacify Him.**
