====== Cc. Antya 4.150 ======

===== Text =====

> dui jana lañā prabhu vasilā piṇḍāte
> nirviṇṇa sanātana lāgilā kahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:j:jana]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the two of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍāte]]---on the altar; [[synonyms:n:nirviṇṇa]]---advanced in renunciation; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]] [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord took them both with Him and sat down in a sacred place. Then Sanātana Gosvāmī, who was advanced in renunciation, began to speak.**
