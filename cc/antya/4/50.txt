====== CC Antya 4.50 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' mahāprabhu uṭhiyā calilā
> govinda-dvārāya duṅhe prasāda pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---got up and left; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārāya]]---through Govinda; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---to both of them; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**Having said this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up and left, and through Govinda He sent prasāda for them to eat.**
