====== Cc. Antya 4.165 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' mahāprabhu kichu lajjita hailā mane
> tāṅre santoṣite kichu balena vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---somewhat; [[synonyms:l:lajjita]]---ashamed; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:s:santoṣite]]---to satisfy; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:b:balena]]---said; [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was somewhat ashamed. Just to satisfy Sanātana Gosvāmī, He spoke the following words.**
