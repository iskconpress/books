====== Cc. Antya 4.52 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu āsi' prati-dina milena dui-jane
> iṣṭa-goṣṭhī, kṛṣṇa-kathā kahe kata-kṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---every day; [[synonyms:m:milena]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---meets both of them; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---discussion; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---for some time.

===== Translation =====

**Every day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go there to meet these two stalwart devotees and discuss topics of Kṛṣṇa with them for some time.**
