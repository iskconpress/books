====== Cc. Antya 4.163 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānande piyāo ātmīyatā-sudhā-rasa
> more piyāo gaurava-stuti-nimba-niśindā-rasa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānande]]---unto Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:piyāo]]---You cause to drink; [[synonyms:ā:ātmīyatā]]-[[synonyms:s:sudhā]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the nectar of affectionate relations; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:p:piyāo]]---You cause to drink; [[synonyms:g:gaurava]]-[[synonyms:s:stuti]]---honorable prayers; [[synonyms:n:nimba]]-[[synonyms:n:niśindā]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the juice of //nimba// fruit and //niśindā.//

===== Translation =====

**"Sir, You are making Jagadānanda drink the nectar of affectionate relationships, whereas by offering me honorable prayers, You are making me drink the bitter juice of nimba and niśindā.**
