====== Cc. Antya 4.137 ======

===== Text =====

> "ihāṅ āilāṅ prabhure dekhi' duḥkha khaṇḍāite
> yebā mane, tāhā prabhu nā dilā karite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here (to Jagannātha Purī); [[synonyms:ā:āilāṅ]]---I have come; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍāite]]---to diminish my unhappiness; [[synonyms:y:yebā]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---what was in my mind; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---did not allow me to do.

===== Translation =====

**"I came here to diminish my unhappiness by seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, but the Lord did not allow me to execute what was in my mind.**
