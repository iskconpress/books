====== CC Antya 4.147 ======

===== Text =====

> dūra haite daṇḍa-paraṇāma kare sanātana
> prabhu bolāya bāra bāra karite āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dūra]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from a distant place; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:p:paraṇāma]]---offering obeisances and //daṇḍavats//; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bolāya]]---calls; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---to embrace.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī offered his obeisances and daṇḍavats from a distant place, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu called him again and again to embrace him.**
