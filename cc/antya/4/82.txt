====== CC Antya 4.82 ======

===== Text =====

> mātāra ājñāya āmi vasi nīlācale
> tāhāṅ 'dharma' śikhāite nāhi nija-bale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---of My mother; [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---by the order; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:v:vasi]]---reside; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---in Mathurā and Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:d:dharma]] [[synonyms:ś:śikhāite]]---to teach religious principles; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:b:bale]]---within My ability.

===== Translation =====

**"By the order of My mother I am sitting here in Jagannātha Purī; therefore, I cannot go to Mathurā-Vṛndāvana to teach people how to live there according to religious principles.**
