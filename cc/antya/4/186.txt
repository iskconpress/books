====== CC Antya 4.186 ======

===== Text =====

> mātāra yaiche bālakera 'amedhya' lāge gāya
> ghṛṇā nāhi janme, āra mahā-sukha pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---of the mother; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---as; [[synonyms:b:bālakera]]---of the child; [[synonyms:a:amedhya]]---stool and urine; [[synonyms:l:lāge]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---touches the body; [[synonyms:g:ghṛṇā]]---hatred; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:janme]]---does not arise; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---more; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---great pleasure; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets.

===== Translation =====

**"When a child passes stool and urine that touch the body of the mother, the mother never hates the child. On the contrary, she takes much pleasure in cleansing him.**
