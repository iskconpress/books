====== CC Antya 4.24 ======

===== Text =====

> kuśala-vārtā mahāprabhu puchena sanātane
> teṅha kahena,---'parama maṅgala dekhinu caraṇe'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kuśala]]---of well-being; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:puchena]]---inquires; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---from Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---he said; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]---everything is auspicious; [[synonyms:d:dekhinu]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---I have seen Your lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired from Sanātana about news of his well-being. Sanātana replied, "Everything is auspicious because I have seen Your lotus feet."**
