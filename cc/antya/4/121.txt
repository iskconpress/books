====== CC Antya 4.121 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā-avaśeṣa-pātra govinda tāre dilā
> prasāda pāñā sanātana prabhu-pāśe āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]-[[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]]---of remnants of the food; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---plate; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered to him; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---after taking the remnants of food; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**Govinda gave Sanātana Gosvāmī the plate with the remnants of Lord Caitanya's food. After taking the prasāda, Sanātana Gosvāmī approached Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
