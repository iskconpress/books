====== CC Antya 4.106 ======

===== Text =====

> ratha-agre prabhu taiche karilā nartana
> dekhi camatkāra haila sanātanera mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:a:agre]]---in front of the car; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:n:nartana]]---performed dancing; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---seeing; [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was astonished; [[synonyms:s:sanātanera]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of Sanātana.

===== Translation =====

**During the Ratha-yātrā festival, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again danced before the cart of Jagannātha. When Sanātana Gosvāmī saw this, his mind was astonished.**
