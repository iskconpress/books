====== CC Antya 4.72 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' sanātanera haila camatkāra
> prabhure nā bhāya mora maraṇa-vicāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:c:camatkāra]]---there was astonishment; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:bhāya]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu does not approve; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:m:maraṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---decision to commit suicide.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this, Sanātana Gosvāmī was exceedingly astonished. He could understand, "My decision to commit suicide has not been greatly appreciated by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu."**
