====== CC Antya 4.229 ======

===== Text =====

> 'bhāgavata-sandarbha'-nāma kaila grantha-sāra
> bhāgavata-siddhāntera tāhāṅ pāiye pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:s:sandarbha]]---the //Bhāgavata-sandarbha,// which is also known as //Ṣaṭ-sandarbha//; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]-[[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of all scriptures; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:s:siddhāntera]]---of conclusive information about the Supreme Personality of Godhead and His service; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---we get; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**In particular, Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī compiled the book named Bhāgavata-sandarbha, or Ṣaṭ-sandarbha, which is the essence of all scriptures. From this book one can obtain a conclusive understanding of devotional service and the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
