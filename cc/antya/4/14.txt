====== Cc. Antya 4.14 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsera kailā teṅha caraṇa vandana
> jāni' haridāsa tāṅre kailā āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**He offered his respects to the lotus feet of Haridāsa Ṭhākura, who knew him and thus embraced him.**
