====== Cc. Antya 4.168 ======

===== Text =====

> āmākeha bujhāite tumi dhara śakti
> kata ṭhāñi bujhāñācha vyavahāra-bhakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmākeha]]---even Me; [[synonyms:b:bujhāite]]---to convince; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:d:dhara]]---have; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---power; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in how many places; [[synonyms:b:bujhāñācha]]---you have convinced; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---ordinary behavior as well as devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**"You have the power to convince even Me. In many places you have already convinced Me about ordinary behavior and devotional service.**
