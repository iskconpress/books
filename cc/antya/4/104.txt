====== Cc. Antya 4.104 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata dui-jana nānā-kathā-raṅge
> kṛṣṇa-kathā āsvādaya rahi' eka-saṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in the happiness of discussing various subject matters; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---the subject matter of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādaya]]---they taste; [[synonyms:r:rahi']] [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---keeping together.

===== Translation =====

**In this way the two of them passed their time discussing subjects concerning Kṛṣṇa. Thus they enjoyed life together.**
