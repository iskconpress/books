====== CC Antya 4.11 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannātha ratha-yātrāya ha-ibena bāhira
> tāṅra ratha-cākāya chāḍimu ei śarīra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannātha]] [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrāya]]---on the occasion of the car festival of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibena]] [[synonyms:b:bāhira]]---He will be out; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of Him; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:c:cākāya]]---under the wheel of the cart; [[synonyms:c:chāḍimu]]---I shall give up; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]---this body

===== Translation =====

**"During the Ratha-yātrā festival, when Lord Jagannātha comes out of the temple, I shall give up this body under the wheel of His cart.**
