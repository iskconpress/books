====== Cc. Antya 4.160 ======

===== Text =====

> āmāra upadeṣṭā tumi---prāmāṇika ārya
> tomāreha upadeśe---bālakā kare aiche kārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:u:upadeṣṭā]]---adviser; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:p:prāmāṇika]] [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---authorized person; [[synonyms:t:tomāreha]]---even you; [[synonyms:u:upadeśe]]---he advises; [[synonyms:b:bālakā]]---boy; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---business.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Sanātana, you are on the level of My adviser, for you are an authorized person. But Jagā wants to advise you. This is but the impudence of a naughty boy."**
