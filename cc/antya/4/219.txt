====== CC Antya 4.219 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana grantha kailā 'bhāgavatāmṛte'
> bhakta-bhakti-kṛṣṇa-tattva jāni yāhā haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---books; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---compiled; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛte]]---in the //Bhāgavatāmṛta//; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---Kṛṣṇa, the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---we know; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from which.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Sanātana Gosvāmī compiled the Bhāgavatāmṛta. From this book one can understand who is a devotee, what is the process of devotional service, and who is Kṛṣṇa, the Absolute Truth.**
