====== CC Antya 4.158 ======

===== Text =====

> "kālikāra baṭuyā jagā aiche garvī haila
> tomā-sabāreha upadeśa karite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kālikāra]]---new; [[synonyms:b:baṭuyā]]---boy; [[synonyms:j:jagā]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---so; [[synonyms:g:garvī]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---has become proud; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāreha]]---persons like you; [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to advise; [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---has begun.

===== Translation =====

**"Jagā [Jagadānanda Paṇḍita] is only a new boy, but he has become so proud that he thinks himself competent to advise a person like you.**
