====== Cc. Antya 4.102 ======

===== Text =====

> āpane ācare keha, nā kare pracāra
> pracāra karena keha, nā karena ācāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:ā:ācare]]---behaves; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---does not do preaching work; [[synonyms:p:pracāra]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does preaching work; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ā:ācāra]]---does not behave strictly according to the principles.

===== Translation =====

**"Some behave very well but do not preach the cult of Kṛṣṇa consciousness, whereas others preach but do not behave properly.**
