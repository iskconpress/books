====== Cc. Antya 4.225 ======

===== Text =====

> 'vidagdha-mādhava', 'lalita-mādhava,---nāṭaka-yuga la
> kṛṣṇa-līlā-rasa tāhāṅ pāiye sakala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vidagdha]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]---//Vidagdha-mādhava//; [[synonyms:l:lalita]]-[[synonyms:m:mādhava]]---//Lalita-mādhava//; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]-[[synonyms:y:yugala]]---two dramas; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the mellow derived from the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]] [[synonyms:s:sakala]]---we can understand all.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī also compiled two important dramas named Vidagdha-mādhava and Lalita-mādhava, from which one can understand all the mellows derived from the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
