====== CC Antya 4.96 ======

===== Text =====

> ye karāite cāhe īśvara, sei siddha haya
> tomāra saubhāgya ei kahiluṅ niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:karāite]]---to cause to do; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:siddha]]---successful; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:saubhāgya]]---your great fortune; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have spoken; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---my considered opinion.

===== Translation =====

**"Whatever the Supreme Personality of Godhead wants us to do will successfully be accomplished. This is your great fortune. That is my mature opinion.**
