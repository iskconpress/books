====== CC Antya 4.182 ======

===== Text =====

> āmā-saba adhame ye kariyācha aṅgīkāra
> dīna-dayālu-guṇa tomāra tāhāte pracāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmā]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of us; [[synonyms:a:adhame]]---most fallen; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]]---You have done; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---acceptance; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]-[[synonyms:d:dayālu]]---merciful unto the fallen souls; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attribute; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---of You; [[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---in that; [[synonyms:p:pracāra]]---proclaiming.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord, we are all fallen, but You have accepted us due to Your attribute of being merciful to the fallen. This is well known all over the world."**
