====== Cc. Antya 4.31 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri-dine raghunāthera 'nāma' āra 'dhyāna'
> rāmāyaṇa niravadhi śune, kare gāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night; [[synonyms:r:raghu]]-[[synonyms:n:nāthera]]---of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---holy name; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]]---meditation; [[synonyms:r:rāmāyaṇa]]---the epic about the activities of Lord Rāmacandra known as the //Rāmāyaṇa//; [[synonyms:n:niravadhi]]---continuously; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:g:gāna]]---chants.

===== Translation =====

**"He always chanted the holy name of Raghunātha and meditated upon Him. He continuously heard about the activities of the Lord from the Rāmāyaṇa and chanted about them.**
