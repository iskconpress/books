====== CC Antya 4.142 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ājñā hañāche tomā' dui bhāye
> vṛndāvane vaisa, tāhāṅ sarva-sukha pāiye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---order; [[synonyms:h:hañāche]]---has been; [[synonyms:t:tomā']]---you; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:bhāye]]---to the two brothers; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]] [[synonyms:v:vaisa]]---sit down at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:s:sukha]]---all happiness; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---you will get.

===== Translation =====

**"The Lord has already ordered both of you brothers to situate yourselves in Vṛndāvana. There you will achieve all happiness.**
