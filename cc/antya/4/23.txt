====== Cc. Antya 4.23 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu lañā vasilā piṇḍāra upare bhakta-gaṇa
> piṇḍāra tale vasilā haridāsa sanātana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍāra]] [[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon the raised platform; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍāra]] [[synonyms:t:tale]]---below the platform; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord and His devotees sat on a raised platform, and below that sat Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Sanātana Gosvāmī.**
