====== CC Antya 4.41 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛpā kari' more ājñā deha' dui-jana
> janme-janme sevoṅ raghunāthera caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---give the order; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---both of you; [[synonyms:j:janme]]-[[synonyms:j:janme]]---life after life; [[synonyms:s:sevoṅ]]---let me serve; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Lord Raghunātha.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Both of you please be merciful to me and order me in such a way so that life after life I may serve the lotus feet of Lord Raghunātha.**
