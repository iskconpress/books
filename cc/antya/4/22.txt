====== CC Antya 4.22 ======

===== Text =====

> saba bhakta-gaṇe prabhu milāilā sanātane
> sanātana kailā sabāra caraṇa vandane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---to Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandane]]---worshiping the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord introduced all the devotees to Sanātana Gosvāmī, who offered his respectful obeisances unto the lotus feet of them all.**
