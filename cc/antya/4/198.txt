====== CC Antya 4.198 ======

===== Text =====

> vastutaḥ prabhu yabe kailā āliṅgana
> tāṅra sparśe gandha haila candanera sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vastutaḥ]]---in fact; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sparśe]]---by His touch; [[synonyms:g:gandha]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was a fragrance; [[synonyms:c:candanera]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---exactly like that of sandalwood pulp.

===== Translation =====

**In fact, however, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced the body of Sanātana Gosvāmī, by the Lord's touch alone there was manifest a fragrance exactly like that of sandalwood pulp.**
