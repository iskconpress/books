====== CC Antya 4.151 ======

===== Text =====

> "hita lāgi' āinu muñi, haila viparīta
> sevā-yogya nahi, aparādha karoṅ niti niti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hita]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for benefit; [[synonyms:ā:āinu]] [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I came; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:v:viparīta]]---it has become just the opposite; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]] [[synonyms:n:nahi]]---I am not fit to render service; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]] [[synonyms:k:karoṅ]]---I commit offenses; [[synonyms:n:niti]] [[synonyms:n:niti]]---day after day.

===== Translation =====

**"I came here for my benefit," he said, "but I see that I am getting just the opposite. I am unfit to render service. I simply commit offenses day after day.**
