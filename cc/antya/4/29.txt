====== CC Antya 4.29 ======

===== Text =====

> hena vaṁśa ghṛṇā chāḍi' kailā aṅgīkāra
> tomāra kṛpāya vaṁśe maṅgala āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]---such; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśa]]---family; [[synonyms:g:ghṛṇā]]---hatred; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---You have done; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---acceptance; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by mercy; [[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---in the family; [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]---auspiciousness; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord, without hatred for my family You have accepted me as Your servant. Only by Your mercy is there good fortune in my family.**
