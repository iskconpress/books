====== Cc. Antya 4.55 ======

===== Text =====

> "sanātana, deha-tyāge kṛṣṇa yadi pāiye
> koṭi-deha kṣaṇeke tabe chāḍite pāriye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---My dear Sanātana; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāge]]---by committing suicide; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---I can get; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]]-[[synonyms:d:deha]]---millions of bodies; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeke]]---in a moment; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:c:chāḍite]] [[synonyms:p:pāriye]]---I can give up.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Sanātana," He said, "if I could attain Kṛṣṇa by committing suicide, I would certainly give up millions of bodies without a moment's hesitation.**
