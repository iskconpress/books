====== CC Antya 4.164 ======

===== Text =====

> ājiha nahila more ātmīyatā-jñāna!
> mora abhāgya, tumi---svatantra bhagavān!"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājiha]]---even until now; [[synonyms:n:nahila]]---there has not been; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:ā:ātmīyatā]]-[[synonyms:j:jñāna]]---feeling as one of Your relations; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:a:abhāgya]]---my misfortune; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the independent Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"It is my misfortune that You have not accepted me as one of Your intimate relations. But You are the completely independent Supreme Personality of Godhead."**
