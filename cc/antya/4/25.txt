====== Cc. Antya 4.25 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurāra vaiṣṇava-sabera kuśala puchilā
> sabāra kuśala sanātana jānāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurāra]]---of Mathurā; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:s:sabera]]---of all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:kuśala]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired about the auspiciousness; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:k:kuśala]]---the well-being of all of them; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:j:jānāilā]]---informed.

===== Translation =====

**When the Lord asked about all the Vaiṣṇavas at Mathurā, Sanātana Gosvāmī informed Him of their good health and fortune.**
