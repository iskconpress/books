====== CC Antya 4.185 ======

===== Text =====

> āpanāre haya mora amānya-samāna
> tomā-sabāre karoṅ muñi bālaka-abhimāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpanāre]]---unto Myself; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:a:amānya]]---not deserving respect; [[synonyms:s:samāna]]---like; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto all of you; [[synonyms:k:karoṅ]]---do; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bālaka]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---considering My sons.

===== Translation =====

**"I always think of Myself as deserving no respect, but because of affection I always consider you to be like My little children.**
