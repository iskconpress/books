====== CC Antya 4.43 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe āmi-duṅhe tāre āliṅgana kailuṅ
> 'sādhu, dṛḍha-bhakti tomāra '---kahi' praśaṁsiluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]-[[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of us; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---embraced; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]---very good; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---very determined; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---devotion; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying; [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsiluṅ]]---we praised.

===== Translation =====

**"Upon hearing this, both of us embraced him and encouraged him by saying, 'You are a great saintly devotee, for your determination in devotional service is fixed.' In this way we both praised him.**
