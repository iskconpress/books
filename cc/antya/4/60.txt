====== CC Antya 4.60 ======

===== Text =====

> deha-tyāgādi tamo-dharma---pātaka-kāraṇa
> sādhaka nā pāya tāte kṛṣṇera caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāga]]---giving up the material body by suicide; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---on the platform of the mode of ignorance; [[synonyms:p:pātaka]]-[[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---cause of sinful activities; [[synonyms:s:sādhaka]]---the devotee; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---does not get; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---by that; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Measures like suicide are causes for sin. A devotee never achieves shelter at Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet by such actions.**
