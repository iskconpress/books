====== CC Antya 4.47 ======

===== Text =====

> durdaive sevaka yadi yāya anya sthāne
> sei ṭhākura dhanya tāre cule dhari' āne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:durdaive]]---by chance; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---the servant; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to another place; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]]---that master; [[synonyms:d:dhanya]]---glorified; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:c:cule]]---by the hair; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---capturing; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---brings back.

===== Translation =====

**"If by chance a servant falls down and goes somewhere else, glorious is that master who captures him and brings him back by the hair.**
