====== CC Antya 4.105 ======

===== Text =====

> yātrā-kāle āilā saba gauḍera bhakta-gaṇa
> pūrvavat kailā sabe ratha-yātrā daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---during the time of the car festival; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gauḍera]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees from Bengal; [[synonyms:p:pūrvavat]]---like previously; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting the car festival of Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**During the time of Ratha-yātrā, all the devotees arrived from Bengal to visit the cart festival as they had done previously.**
