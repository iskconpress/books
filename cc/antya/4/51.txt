====== CC Antya 4.51 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata sanātana rahe prabhu-sthāne
> jagannāthera cakra dekhi' karena praṇāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---under the care of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:c:cakra]]---the wheel on the top of the temple; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:p:praṇāme]]---offers respectful obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Sanātana Gosvāmī stayed under the care of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He would see the wheel on the pinnacle of the Jagannātha temple and offer respectful obeisances.**
