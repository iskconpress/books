====== Cc. Antya 4.4 ======

===== Text =====

> jhārikhaṇḍa-vanapathe āilā ekelā caliyā
> kabhu upavāsa, kabhu carvaṇa kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jhārikhaṇḍa]]---known as Jhārikhaṇḍa; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]]---through the path of the forest of central India; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:e:ekelā]]---alone; [[synonyms:c:caliyā]]---walking; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:u:upavāsa]]---fasting; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:c:carvaṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---chewing.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī walked alone on the path through Jhārikhaṇḍa Forest in central India. Sometimes he fasted, and sometimes he would eat.**
