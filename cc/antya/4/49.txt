====== CC Antya 4.49 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-bhakti-rase duṅhe parama pradhāna
> kṛṣṇa-rasa āsvādana kara, laha kṛṣṇa-nāma"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rase]]---in the transcendental mellow of devotional service; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of you; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---highly expert; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the transcendental taste of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---relishing; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---do; [[synonyms:l:laha]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Both of you are expert in understanding the mellows of Lord Kṛṣṇa's devotional service. Therefore you should both continue relishing the taste for such activities and chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra."**
