====== CC Antya 4.35 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-bhajana kara tumi āmā-duṅhāra saṅge
> tina bhāi ekatra rahimu kṛṣṇa-kathā-raṅge"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---engage in; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]-[[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---the two of us; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---three brothers; [[synonyms:e:ekatra]]---in one place; [[synonyms:r:rahimu]]---we shall stay; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---of the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---in enjoyment.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Engage yourself in devotional service to Kṛṣṇa with both of us. We three brothers shall stay together and enjoy discussing the pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa.'**
