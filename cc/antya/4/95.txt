====== CC Antya 4.95 ======

===== Text =====

> nija-dehe ye kārya nā pārena karite
> se kārya karāibe tomā, seha mathurāte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:d:dehe]]---with His personal body; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---whatever business; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pārena]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---He cannot do; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---those things; [[synonyms:k:karāibe]]---He will cause to do; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]---you; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---that; [[synonyms:m:mathurāte]]---in Mathurā.

===== Translation =====

**"What Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu cannot do with His personal body He wants to do through you, and He wants to do it in Mathurā.**
