====== CC Antya 4.107 ======

===== Text =====

> varṣāra cāri-māsa rahilā saba nija bhakta-gaṇe
> sabā-saṅge prabhu milāilā sanātane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varṣāra]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---the four months of the rainy season; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---Sanātana.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord's devotees from Bengal stayed at Jagannātha Purī during the four months of the rainy season, and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu introduced Sanātana Gosvāmī to them all.**
