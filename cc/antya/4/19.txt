====== CC Antya 4.19 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātane āliṅgite prabhu āgu hailā
> pāche bhāge sanātana kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgite]]---to embrace; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āgu]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---came forward; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---back; [[synonyms:b:bhāge]]---runs; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came forward to embrace him, Sanātana backed away and spoke as folIows.**
