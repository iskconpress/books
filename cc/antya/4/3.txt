====== CC Antya 4.3 ======

===== Text =====

> nīlācala haite rūpa gauḍe yabe gelā
> mathurā haite sanātana nīlācala āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīlācala]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Nīlācala (Jagannātha Purī); [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Mathurā; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:n:nīlācala]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī returned from Jagannātha Purī to Bengal, Sanātana Gosvāmī went from Mathurā to Jagannātha Purī to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
