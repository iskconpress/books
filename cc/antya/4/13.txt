====== CC Antya 4.13 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' niścaya kari' nīlācale āilā
> loke puchi' haridāsa-sthāne uttarilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---ascertaining; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:l:loke]] [[synonyms:p:puchi']]---inquiring from people; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---the place of Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:u:uttarilā]]---approached.

===== Translation =====

**Having made this resolution, Sanātana Gosvāmī went to Nīlācala, where he inquired directions from people and approached the residence of Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
