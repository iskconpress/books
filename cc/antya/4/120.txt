====== CC Antya 4.120 ======

===== Text =====

> dui pāye phoskā haila, tabu gelā prabhu-sthāne
> bhikṣā kari' mahāprabhu kariyāchena viśrāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:p:pāye]]---on the two soles; [[synonyms:p:phoskā]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there were blisters; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after finishing lunch; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchena]] [[synonyms:v:viśrāme]]---was taking rest.

===== Translation =====

**Although the soles of both his feet were blistered because of the heat, he nevertheless went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. There he found that the Lord, having taken His lunch, was resting.**
