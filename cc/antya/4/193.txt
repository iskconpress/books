====== CC Antya 4.193 ======

===== Text =====

> sei deha kare tāra cid-ānanda-maya
> aprākṛta-dehe tāṅra caraṇa bhajaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:deha]]---that body; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---makes; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:c:cit]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---full of transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:a:aprākṛta]]-[[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in that transcendental body; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---feet; [[synonyms:b:bhajaya]]---worships.

===== Translation =====

**"When the devotee's body is thus transformed into spiritual existence, the devotee, in that transcendental body, renders service to the lotus feet of the Lord.**
