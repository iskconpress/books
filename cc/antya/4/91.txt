====== CC Antya 4.91 ======

===== Text =====

> etādṛśa tumi iṅhāre kariyācha aṅgīkāra
> eta saubhāgya ihāṅ nā haya kāhāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:etādṛśa]]---such; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---have accepted; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:s:saubhāgya]]---so much fortune; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---upon him; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is not possible; [[synonyms:k:kāhāra]]---by anyone else.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear sir, since You, a great personality, have accepted Sanātana Gosvāmī, he is greatly fortunate; no one can be as fortunate as he."**
