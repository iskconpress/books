====== CC Antya 4.228 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva tyāji' teṅho pāche āilā vṛndāvana
> teṅha bhakti-śāstra bahu kailā pracāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:t:tyāji']]---renouncing everything; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he (Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī); [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---came to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstra]]---books on devotional service; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]---many; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:pracāraṇa]]---spreading.

===== Translation =====

**After renouncing everything, Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī went to Vṛndāvana. Later he also wrote many books on devotional service and expanded the work of preaching.**
