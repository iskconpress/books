====== CC Antya 4.92 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu kari' duṅhāre āliṅgana
> 'madhyāhna' karite uṭhi' karilā gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:d:duṅhāre]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing both of them; [[synonyms:m:madhya]]-[[synonyms:a:ahna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to perform His noon duties; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---left.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced both Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Sanātana Gosvāmī and then got up and left to perform His noon duties.**
