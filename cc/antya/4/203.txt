====== Cc. Antya 4.203 ======

===== Text =====

> sei jhārikhaṇḍera pānī tumi khāoyāilā
> sei pānī-lakṣye iṅhāra kaṇḍu upajāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:j:jhārikhaṇḍera]]---of Jhārikhaṇḍa; [[synonyms:p:pānī]]---water; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāilā]]---made to drink; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:pānī]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣye]]---on account of this water; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kaṇḍu]] [[synonyms:u:upajāilā]]---You generated the itching.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, You made Sanātana Gosvāmī drink the water of Jhārikhaṇḍa, and You actually generated the consequent itching sores on his body.**
