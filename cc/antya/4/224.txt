====== CC Antya 4.224 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ujjvala-nīlamaṇi'-nāma grantha kaila āra
> rādhā-kṛṣṇa-līlā-rasa tāhāṅ pāiye pāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ujjvala]]-[[synonyms:n:nīla]]-[[synonyms:m:maṇi]]---//Ujjvala-nīlamaṇi//; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---scripture; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---compiled; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---the transcendental mellow of the pastimes of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---we get; [[synonyms:p:pāra]]---the extreme limit.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī also compiled the book named Ujjvala-nīlamaṇi, from which one can understand, to the fullest limits, the loving affairs of Śrī Śrī Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa.**
