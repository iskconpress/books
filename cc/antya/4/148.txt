====== CC Antya 4.148 ======

===== Text =====

> aparādha-bhaye teṅha milite nā āila
> mahāprabhu milibāre sei ṭhāñi gela

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparādha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---out of fear of offenses; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:m:milite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---did not come forward; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:milibāre]]---to meet; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went.

===== Translation =====

**Out of fear of committing offenses, Sanātana Gosvāmī did not come forward to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The Lord, however, went forward to meet him.**
