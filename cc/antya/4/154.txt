====== CC Antya 4.154 ======

===== Text =====

> bībhatsa sparśite nā kara ghṛṇā-leśe
> ei aparādhe mora habe sarva-nāśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bībhatsa]]---horrible; [[synonyms:s:sparśite]]---to touch; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You do not do; [[synonyms:g:ghṛṇā]]-[[synonyms:l:leśe]]---even a small bit of aversion; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:a:aparādhe]]---because of this offense; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśe]]---loss of everything auspicious.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear sir, You do not have even a pinch of aversion to touching my body, which is in a horrible condition. Because of this offense, everything auspicious will be vanquished for me.**
