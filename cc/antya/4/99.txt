====== CC Antya 4.99 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana kahe,---"tomā-sama kebā āche āna
> mahāprabhura gaṇe tumi---mahā-bhāgyavān!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like you; [[synonyms:k:kebā]]---who; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---is there; [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---another; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---among the personal associates; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---the most fortunate.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī replied, "O Haridāsa Ṭhākura, who is equal to you? You are one of the associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Therefore you are the most fortunate.**
