====== CC Antya 4.36 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata bāra-bāra kahi dui-jana
> āmā-duṅhāra gaurave kichu phiri' gela mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bāra]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again; [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---we speak; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]-[[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of us both; [[synonyms:g:gaurave]]---out of respect; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---somewhat; [[synonyms:p:phiri']] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---turned; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way we both spoke to him again and again, and because of this persuasion and his respect for us, his mind turned somewhat toward our instructions.**
