====== CC Antya 4.85 ======

===== Text =====

> kāṣṭhera putalī yena kuhake nācāya
> āpane nā jāne, putalī kibā nāce gāya!

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāṣṭhera]] [[synonyms:p:putalī]]---a doll made of wood; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:k:kuhake]] [[synonyms:n:nācāya]]---magician causes to dance; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know; [[synonyms:p:putalī]]---the doll; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---how; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sings.

===== Translation =====

**"A wooden doll chants and dances according to the direction of a magician but does not know how he is dancing and singing.**
