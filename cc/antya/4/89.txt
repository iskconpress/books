====== CC Antya 4.89 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahe,---"mithyā abhimāna kari
> tomāra gambhīra hṛdaya bujhite nā pāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura replied; [[synonyms:m:mithyā]]---falsely; [[synonyms:a:abhimāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---are proud; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---deep; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---intention; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---we cannot understand.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura replied, "We are falsely proud of our capabilities. Actually we cannot understand Your deep intentions.**
