====== CC Antya 4.232 ======

===== Text =====

> jīva-gosāñi gauḍa haite mathurā calilā
> nityānanda-prabhu-ṭhāñi ājñā māgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīpāda Jīva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Bengal; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---started for Mathurā; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---he asked permission.

===== Translation =====

**When Jīva Gosvāmī wanted to go to Mathurā from Bengal, he requested permission from Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu.**
