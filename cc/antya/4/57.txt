====== CC Antya 4.57 ======

===== Text =====

> deha-tyāgādi yata, saba---tamo-dharma
> tamo-rajo-dharme kṛṣṇera nā pāiye marma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāga]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with giving up the material body; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as many; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---performed under the mode of ignorance; [[synonyms:t:tamaḥ]]-[[synonyms:r:rajaḥ]]-[[synonyms:d:dharme]]---by remaining in the modes of ignorance and passion; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---I cannot attain; [[synonyms:m:marma]]---the truth.

===== Translation =====

**"Acts such as suicide are influenced by the mode of ignorance, and in ignorance and passion one cannot understand who Kṛṣṇa is.**
