====== CC Antya 4.230 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gopāla-campū' nāma grantha sāra kaila
> vraja-prema-līlā-rasa-sāra dekhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]-[[synonyms:c:campū]]---//Gopāla-campū//; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:g:grantha]] [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of all Vedic literature; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]---of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---of love; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of pastimes; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]---of mellows; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---essence; [[synonyms:d:dekhāila]]---exhibited.

===== Translation =====

**He also compiled the book named Gopāla-campū, which is the essence of all Vedic literature. In this book he has exhibited the ecstatic loving transactions and pastimes of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa in Vṛndāvana.**
