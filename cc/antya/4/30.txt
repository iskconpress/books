====== Cc. Antya 4.30 ======

===== Text =====

> sei anupama-bhāi śiśu-kāla haite
> raghunātha-upāsanā kare dṛḍha-citte

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:a:anupama]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---brother named Anupama; [[synonyms:ś:śiśu]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the beginning of childhood; [[synonyms:r:raghu]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]---of Lord Rāmacandra; [[synonyms:u:upāsanā]]---worship; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]-[[synonyms:c:citte]]---with great determination.

===== Translation =====

**"From the very beginning of his childhood, my younger brother Anupama was a great devotee of Raghunātha [Lord Rāmacandra], and he worshiped Him with great determination.**
