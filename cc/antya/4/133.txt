====== Cc. Antya 4.133 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu tāṅre āliṅgana kaila
> tāṅra kaṇḍu-rasā prabhura śrī-aṅge lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---embraced; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:k:kaṇḍu]]-[[synonyms:r:rasā]]---moisture oozing from the itches; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---smeared the body.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Sanātana Gosvāmī, and the moisture oozing from the itching sores on Sanātana's body smeared the body of the Lord.**
