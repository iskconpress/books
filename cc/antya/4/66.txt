====== CC Antya 4.66 ======

===== Text =====

> nīca-jāti nahe kṛṣṇa-bhajane ayogya
> sat-kula-vipra nahe bhajanera yogya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---a lowborn person; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhajane]]---in discharging devotional service; [[synonyms:a:ayogya]]---unfit; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:v:vipra]]---a //brāhmaṇa// born in a very respectable aristocratic family; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:b:bhajanera]] [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---fit for discharging devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**"A person born in a low family is not unfit for discharging devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa, nor is one fit for devotional service simply because he is born in an aristocratic family of brāhmaṇas.**
