====== CC Antya 4.146 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kailā prabhura caraṇa vandana
> haridāse kailā prabhu prema-āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---unto Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura offered obeisances to the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord embraced him in ecstatic love.**
