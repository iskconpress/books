====== CC Antya 4.9 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthera sevaka phere kārya-anurodhe
> tāṅra sparśa haile mora habe aparādhe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---different servants; [[synonyms:p:phere]]---move about; [[synonyms:k:kārya]]-[[synonyms:a:anurodhe]]---because of different duties; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of them; [[synonyms:s:sparśa]]---touch; [[synonyms:h:haile]]---if there is; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:a:aparādhe]]---offense.

===== Translation =====

**"The servants of Lord Jagannātha generally move about tending to their duties, but if they touch me I shall be an offender.**
