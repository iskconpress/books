====== CC Antya 4.94 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra deha kahena prabhu 'mora nija-dhana'
> tomā-sama bhāgyavān nāhi kona jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:d:deha]]---your body; [[synonyms:k:kahena]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---personal property; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like you; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---fortunate person; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---anyone.

===== Translation =====

**"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has accepted your body as His own property. Therefore no one can equal you in good fortune.**
