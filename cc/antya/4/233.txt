====== CC Antya 4.233 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu prītye tāṅra māthe dharilā caraṇa
> rūpa-sanātana-sambandhe kailā āliṅgana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:p:prītye]]---because of the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:m:māthe]]---on the head; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---rested His lotus feet; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:s:sam]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhe]]---because of his relationship with Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**Because of Jīva Gosvāmī's relationship with Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī, who were greatly favored by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu placed His feet on the head of Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī and embraced him.**
