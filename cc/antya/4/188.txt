====== Cc. Antya 4.188 ======

===== Text =====

> haridāsa kahe,---"tumi īśvara dayā-maya
> tomāra gambhīra hṛdaya bujhana nā yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura said; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---deep; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---heart; [[synonyms:b:bujhana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---cannot be understood.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "My dear sir, You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead and are most merciful toward us. No one can understand what is within Your deeply affectionate heart.**
