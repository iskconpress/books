====== CC Antya 4.108-110 ======

===== Text =====

> advaita, nityānanda, śrīvāsa, vakreśvara
> vāsudeva, murāri, rāghava, dāmodara
>
> purī, bhāratī, svarūpa, paṇḍitā gadādhara
> sārvabhauma, rāmānanda, jagadānanda, śaṅkara
>
> kāśīśvara, govindādi yata bhakta-gaṇa
> sabā-sane sanātanera karāilā milana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:advaita]]---Advaita; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Nityānanda; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa; [[synonyms:v:vakreśvara]]---Vakreśvara; [[synonyms:v:vāsudeva]]---Vāsudeva; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]---Murāri; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]---Rāghava; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]---Dāmodara; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Purī; [[synonyms:b:bhāratī]]---Bhāratī; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gadādhara]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:s:sabā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with all of them; [[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---made introduction.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu introduced Sanātana Gosvāmī to these and other selected devotees: Advaita, Nityānanda, Śrīvāsa, Vakreśvara, Vāsudeva, Murāri, Rāghava, Dāmodara, Paramānanda Purī, Brahmānanda Bhāratī, Svarūpa Dāmodara, Gadādhara Paṇḍita, Sārvabhauma, Rāmānanda, Jagadānanda, Śaṅkara, Kāśīśvara and Govinda.**
