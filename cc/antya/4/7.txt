====== CC Antya 4.7 ======

===== Text =====

> jagannāthe gele tāṅra darśana nā pāimu
> prabhura darśana sadā karite nārimu

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagannāthe]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:g:gele]]---when I go; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---visit; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāimu]]---I shall not get; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to do; [[synonyms:n:nārimu]]---I shall not be able.

===== Translation =====

**"When I go to Jagannātha Purī, I shall not be able to see Lord Jagannātha, nor shall I always be able to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
