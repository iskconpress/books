====== Cc. Antya 4.201 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' punaḥ tāṅre kailā āliṅgana
> kaṇḍu gela, aṅga haila suvarṇera sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:kaṇḍu]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---the itching sores disappeared; [[synonyms:a:aṅga]]---the body; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:s:suvarṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sama]]---like gold.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again embraced Sanātana Gosvāmī. Thus immediately Sanātana's itches disappeared, and his entire body resembled the color of gold.**
