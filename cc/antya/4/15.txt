====== CC Antya 4.15 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu dekhite tāṅra utkaṇṭhita mana
> haridāsa kahe,---'prabhu āsibena ekhana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to see; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhita]]---anxious; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Haridāsa said; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsibena]] [[synonyms:e:ekhana]]---will come here.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī was very eager to see the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Therefore Haridāsa Ṭhākura said, "The Lord is coming here very soon."**
