====== Cc. Antya 4.116 ======

===== Text =====

> jyaiṣṭha-māse prabhu yameśvara-ṭoṭā āilā
> bhakta-anurodhe tāhāṅ bhikṣā ye karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jyaiṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:m:māse]]---during the month of May-June; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yameśvara]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭoṭā]]---to the garden of Lord Śiva, Yameśvara; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:a:anurodhe]]---on the request of the devotees; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---accepted //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**In that month of May-June, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came to the garden of Yameśvara [Lord Śiva] and accepted prasāda there at the request of the devotees.**
