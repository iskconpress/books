====== CC Antya 4.204 ======

===== Text =====

> kaṇḍu kari' parīkṣā karile sanātane
> ei līlā-bhaṅgī tomāra keha nāhi jāne"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kaṇḍu]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---generating the itching sores; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣā]]---examination; [[synonyms:k:karile]]---You did; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---of pastimes; [[synonyms:b:bhaṅgī]]---trick; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---no one knows.

===== Translation =====

**"After thus causing these itching sores, You examined Sanātana Gosvāmī. No one can understand Your transcendental pastimes."**
