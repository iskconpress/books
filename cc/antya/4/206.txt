====== CC Antya 4.206 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata sanātana rahe prabhu-sthāne
> kṛṣṇa-caitanya-guṇa-kathā haridāsa-sane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---of the attributes; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---discussion; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Haridāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Sanātana Gosvāmī stayed under the care of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and discussed the transcendental qualities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with Haridāsa Ṭhākura.**
