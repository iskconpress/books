====== CC Antya 4.76 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"tomāra deha mora nija-dhana
> tumi more kariyācha ātma-samarpaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:d:deha]]---your body; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---personal property; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to Me; [[synonyms:k:kariyācha]]---have done; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:s:samarpaṇa]]---complete surrender.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Your body is My property. You have already surrendered unto Me. Therefore you no longer have any claim to your body.**
