====== CC Antya 4.34 ======

===== Text =====

> śunaha vallabha, kṛṣṇa---parama-madhura
> saundarya, mādhurya, prema-vilāsa---pracura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śunaha]]---please hear; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]]---dear Vallabha; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:m:madhura]]---supremely attractive; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---beauty; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastimes of love; [[synonyms:p:pracura]]---without limitation.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Dear Vallabha,' we said, 'please hear from us. Lord Kṛṣṇa is supremely attractive. His beauty, sweetness and pastimes of love are without limit.**
