====== CC Antya 4.73 ======

===== Text =====

> sarvajña mahāprabhu niṣedhilā more
> prabhura caraṇa dhari' kahena tāṅhāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---who knows everything; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhilā]]---has forbidden; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the feet; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---touching; [[synonyms:k:kahena]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]]---began to speak to Him.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī concIuded, "Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who knows everything-past, present and future-has forbidden me to commit suicide." He then fell down, touching the lotus feet of the Lord, and spoke to Him as follows.**
