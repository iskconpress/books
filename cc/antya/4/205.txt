====== CC Antya 4.205 ======

===== Text =====

> duṅhe āliṅgiyā prabhu gelā nijālaya
> prabhura guṇa kahe duṅhe hañā prema-maya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgiyā]]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---departed; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ā:ālaya]]---for His place; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---attributes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---discussed; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---overwhelmed by ecstasy.

===== Translation =====

**After embracing both Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His residence. Then both Haridāsa Ṭhākura and Sanātana Gosvāmī, in great ecstatic love, began to describe the Lord's transcendental attributes.**
