====== CC Antya 4.88 ======

===== Text =====

> parera sthāpya dravya keha nā khāya, vilāya
> niṣedhiha iṅhāre,---yena nā kare anyāya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parera]]---by another; [[synonyms:s:sthāpya]]---to be kept; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---property; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---no one uses; [[synonyms:v:vilāya]]---distributes; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhiha]]---forbid; [[synonyms:i:iṅhāre]]---him; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---so; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---he does not do; [[synonyms:a:anyāya]]---something unlawful.

===== Translation =====

**"One who is entrusted with another's property does not distribute it or use it for his own purposes. Therefore, tell him not to do such an unlawful thing."**
