====== CC Antya 4.171 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi kāhāra 'mamatā' bahu-jane haya
> prīti-svabhāve kāhāte kona bhāvodaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:k:kāhāra]]---of someone; [[synonyms:m:mamatā]]---affection; [[synonyms:b:bahu]]-[[synonyms:l:lane]]---unto many persons; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:p:prīti]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāve]]---according to one's affection; [[synonyms:k:kāhāte]]---in someone; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---some; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---awakening of ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**"Although one has affection for many persons, different types of ecstatic love awaken according to the nature of one's personal relationships.**
