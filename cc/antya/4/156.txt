====== CC Antya 4.156 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda-paṇḍite āmi yukti puchila
> vṛndāvana yāite teṅha upadeśa dila"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍite]]---from Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:y:yukti]]---advice; [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---has given instruction.

===== Translation =====

**"I have consulted Jagadānanda Paṇḍita for his opinion, and he has also advised me to return to Vṛndāvana."**
