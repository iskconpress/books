====== Cc. Antya 4.196 ======

===== Text =====

> ghṛṇā kari' āliṅgana nā karitāma yabe
> kṛṣṇa-ṭhāñi aparādha-daṇḍa pāitāma tabe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghṛṇā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---hating; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karitāma]]---I would not do; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---punishment for offenses; [[synonyms:p:pāitāma]]---I would have gotten; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then.

===== Translation =====

**"If I had hated Sanātana Gosvāmī and had not embraced him, I would certainly have been chastised for offenses to Kṛṣṇa.**
