~~Title:Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya 4.161~~

====== CC Antya 4.161 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' sanātana pāye dhari' prabhure kahila
> 'jagadānandera saubhāgya āji se jānila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pāye]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---capturing the feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---began to say to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagadānandera]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:s:saubhāgya]]---fortune; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---now; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:j:jānila]]---I understand.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was thus chastising Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, Sanātana Gosvāmī fell at the Lord's feet and said, "I can now understand the fortunate position of Jagadānanda.**
