====== CC Antya 4.17 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu dekhi' duṅhe paḍe daṇḍavat hañā
> prabhu āliṅgilā haridāsere uṭhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of them; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---fell down; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---flat like rods; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgilā]]---embraced; [[synonyms:h:haridāsere]]---Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---after lifting.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they both immediately fell flat like rods to offer obeisances. The Lord then lifted Haridāsa and embraced him.**
