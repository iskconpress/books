====== Cc. Antya 4.68 ======

===== Text =====

> dīnere adhika dayā kare bhagavān
> kulīna, paṇḍita, dhanīra baḍa abhimāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dīnere]]---to the humble; [[synonyms:a:adhika]]---more; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---shows; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kulīna]]---aristocratic; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned scholar; [[synonyms:d:dhanīra]]---of rich man; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---great pride.

===== Translation =====

**"The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa, is always favorable to the humble and meek, but aristocrats, learned scholars and the wealthy are always proud of their positions.**
