====== CC Antya 4.20 ======

===== Text =====

> "more nā chuṅiha, prabhu, paḍoṅ tomāra pāya
> eke nīca-jāti adhama, āra kaṇḍu-rasā gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:chuṅiha]]---please do not touch; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---my Lord; [[synonyms:p:paḍoṅ]]---I fall down; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---at Your feet; [[synonyms:e:eke]]---on one side; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---of a low caste; [[synonyms:a:adhama]]---the lowest of mankind; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kaṇḍu]]-[[synonyms:r:rasā]]---a disease of wet, itching infections; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---on the body.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord, please do not touch me. I fall at Your lotus feet. I am the lowest of men, having been born of a low caste. Besides that, I have infections on my body."**
