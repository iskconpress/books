====== Cc. Antya 4.6 ======

===== Text =====

> nirveda ha-ila pathe, karena vicāra
> 'nīca-jāti, deha mora---atyanta asāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirveda]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was disappointment; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the path; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:v:vicāra]]---he considered; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:j:jāti]]---of a lower caste; [[synonyms:d:deha]] [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my body; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---completely; [[synonyms:a:asāra]]---useless for devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**In disappointment, Sanātana Gosvāmī considered, "I am of a low caste, and my body is useless for devotional service.**
