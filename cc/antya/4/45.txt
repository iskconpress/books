====== CC Antya 4.45 ======

===== Text =====

> gosāñi kahena,---"ei-mata murāri-gupta
> pūrve āmi parīkṣiluṅ tāra ei rīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:murāri]]-[[synonyms:g:gupta]]---Murāri Gupta; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣiluṅ]]---examined; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of him; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:r:rīta]]---manner.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "There was a similar incident concerning Murāri Gupta. Formerly I examined him, and his determination was similar.**
