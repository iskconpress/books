====== CC Antya 4.113 ======

===== Text =====

> sakala vaiṣṇava yabe gauḍa-deśe gelā
> sanātana mahāprabhura caraṇe rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotees; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---returned; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---stayed at the lotus feet.

===== Translation =====

**When all the other devotees returned to Bengal after the Ratha-yātrā festival, Sanātana Gosvāmī stayed under the care of the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
