====== CC Antya 4.53 ======

===== Text =====

> divya prasāda pāya nitya jagannātha-mandire
> tāhā āni' nitya avaśya dena doṅhākāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:divya]]---first-class; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:m:mandire]]---at the temple of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing that; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]---daily; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---delivers; [[synonyms:d:doṅhākāre]]---to both of them.

===== Translation =====

**The offerings of prasāda in the temple of Lord Jagannātha were of the highest quality. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would bring this prasāda and deliver it to both devotees.**
