====== CC Antya 4.139 ======

===== Text =====

> aparādha haya mora, nāhika nistāra
> jagannātheha nā dekhiye,---e duḥkha apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---mine; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:n:nistāra]]---there is no deliverance; [[synonyms:j:jagannātheha]]---also Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiye]]---I cannot see; [[synonyms:e:e]]---this; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---great unhappiness.

===== Translation =====

**"In this way I am committing offenses at His lotus feet, for which I shall certainly not be delivered. At the same time, I cannot see Lord Jagannātha. This is my great unhappiness.**
