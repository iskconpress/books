====== CC Antya 4.235 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra ājñāya āilā, ājñā-phala pāilā
> śāstra kari' kata-kāla 'bhakti' pracārilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---by His order; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---the result of His order; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---got; [[synonyms:ś:śāstra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---compiling various scriptures; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:p:pracārilā]]---preached devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**By the order of Nityānanda Prabhu, he went and actually achieved the result of His order, for he compiled many books for a long time and preached the cult of bhakti from Vṛndāvana.**
