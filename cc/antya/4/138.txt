====== CC Antya 4.138 ======

===== Text =====

> niṣedhite prabhu āliṅgana karena more
> mora kaṇḍu-rasā lāge prabhura śarīre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:niṣedhite]]---although I forbid; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto me; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:k:kaṇḍu]]-[[synonyms:r:rasā]]---my wet sores; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---touches; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śarīre]]---on the body.

===== Translation =====

**"Although I forbid Him to do so, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu nevertheless embraces me, and therefore His body becomes smeared with the discharges from my itching sores.**
