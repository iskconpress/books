====== Cc. Antya 4.140 ======

===== Text =====

> hita-nimitta āilāṅa āmi, haila viparīte
> ki karile hita haya nāri nirdhārite"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hita]]-[[synonyms:n:nimitta]]---for benefit; [[synonyms:ā:āilāṅa]]---came; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:v:viparīte]]---it has become just the opposite; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:k:karile]]---how; [[synonyms:h:hita]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there will be benefit; [[synonyms:n:nāri]] [[synonyms:n:nirdhārite]]---I can not ascertain.

===== Translation =====

**"I came here for my benefit, but now I see that I am getting just the opposite. I do not know, nor can I ascertain, how there will be benefit for me."**
