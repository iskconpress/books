====== CC Antya 4.141 ======

===== Text =====

> paṇḍita kahe,---"tomāra vāsa-yogya 'vṛndāvana'
> ratha-yātrā dekhi' tāhāṅ karaha gamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---a suitable place for residence; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:r:ratha]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing the Ratha-yātrā festival; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---go.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita said, "The most suitable place for you to reside is Vṛndāvana. After seeing the Ratha-yātrā festival, you can return there.**
