====== CC Antya 4.223 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpa-gosāñi kailā 'rasāmṛta-sindhu' sāra
> kṛṣṇa-bhakti-rasera yāhāṅ pāiye vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---compiled; [[synonyms:r:rasāmṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the book known as //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu//; [[synonyms:s:sāra]]---the essence of knowledge in devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:r:rasera]]---of the transcendental mellow in devotional service; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherein; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---we can get; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---elaboration.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī also wrote many books, the most famous of which is Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu. From that book one can understand the essence of devotional service to Kṛṣṇa and the transcendental mellow one can derive from such service.**
