====== Cc. Antya 4.44 ======

===== Text =====

> ye vaṁśera upare tomāra haya kṛpā-leśa
> sakala maṅgala tāhe khaṇḍe saba kleśa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:v:vaṁśera]]---which family; [[synonyms:u:upare]]---upon; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:l:leśa]]---little mercy; [[synonyms:s:sakala]] [[synonyms:m:maṅgala]]---all auspiciousness; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---on that; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍe]]---are destroyed; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:k:kleśa]]---miserable conditions.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, the family upon which You bestow even a little mercy is always fortunate, for such mercy makes all miseries disappear."**
