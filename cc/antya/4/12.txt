====== Cc. Antya 4.12 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura āge, āra dekhi' jagannātha
> rathe deha chāḍimu,---ei parama-puruṣārtha'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']] [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---after seeing Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:r:rathe]]---under the cart; [[synonyms:d:deha]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍimu]]---I shall give up this body; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:parama]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---the highest benediction of life.

===== Translation =====

**"After seeing Lord Jagannātha, I shall give up my body under the wheel of the cart in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. This will be the highest benediction of my life."**
