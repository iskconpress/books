====== CC Antya 4.195 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātanera dehe kṛṣṇa kaṇḍu upajāñā
> āmā parīkṣite ihāṅ dilā pāṭhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the body; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kaṇḍu]]---itches; [[synonyms:u:upajāñā]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]---Me; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣite]]---to test; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:d:dilā]] [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāñā]]---has sent.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa somehow or other manifested these itching sores on the body of Sanātana Gosvāmī and sent him here to test Me.**
