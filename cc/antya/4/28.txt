====== Cc. Antya 4.28 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana kahe,---"nīca-vaṁśe mora janma
> adharma anyāya yata,---āmāra kula-dharma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:n:nīca]]-[[synonyms:v:vaṁśe]]---in a low family; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:j:janma]]---my birth; [[synonyms:a:adharma]]---irreligion; [[synonyms:a:anyāya]]---sinful activities; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---all; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---my; [[synonyms:k:kula]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]---family business.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "I was born in a low family, for my family commits all kinds of irreligious acts that violate the scriptural injunctions.**
