====== Cc. Antya 4.114 ======

===== Text =====

> dola-yātrā-ādi prabhura saṅgete dekhila
> dine-dine prabhu-saṅge ānanda bāḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dola]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the festival of Dola-yātrā; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and others; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:saṅgete]]---with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---he saw; [[synonyms:d:dine]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day after day; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]] [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---his pleasure increased.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī observed the Dola-yātrā ceremony with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. In this way, his pleasure increased in the company of the Lord.**
