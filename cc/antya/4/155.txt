====== Cc. Antya 4.155 ======

===== Text =====

> tāte ihāṅ rahile mora nā haya 'kalyāṇa'
> ājñā deha'---ratha dekhi' yāṅa vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāte]]---because of this; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:r:rahile]]---if I remain; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kalyāṇa]]---auspiciousness; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---kindly give the order; [[synonyms:r:ratha]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after witnessing the Ratha-yātrā festival; [[synonyms:y:yāṅa]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---I may return to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**"Therefore I see that I will get nothing auspicious by staying here. Kindly give me orders allowing me to return to Vṛndāvana after the Ratha-yātrā festival.**
