====== Cc. Antya 4.216 ======

===== Text =====

> saba manaḥ-kathā gosāñi kari' nirvāhaṇa
> niścinta hañā śīghra āilā vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---decisions; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:n:nirvāhaṇa]]---executing properly; [[synonyms:n:niścinta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being freed from all anxiety; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---very soon returned; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**Thus after finishing all the tasks he had on his mind, he returned to Vṛndāvana fully satisfied.**
