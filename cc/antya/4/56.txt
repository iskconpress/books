====== CC Antya 4.56 ======

===== Text =====

> deha-tyāge kṛṣṇa nā paī, pāiye bhajane
> kṛṣṇa-prāptyera upāya kona nāhi 'bhakti' vine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāge]]---by giving up the body; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---I do not get; [[synonyms:p:pāiye]]---I get; [[synonyms:b:bhajane]]---by devotional service; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāptyera]]---to get the shelter of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---means; [[synonyms:k:kona]]---any; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:v:vine]]---without devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**"You should know that one cannot attain Kṛṣṇa simply by giving up the body. Kṛṣṇa is attainable by devotional service. There is no other meas to attain Him.**
