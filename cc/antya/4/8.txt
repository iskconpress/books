====== CC Antya 4.8 ======

===== Text =====

> mandira-nikaṭe śuni tāṅra vāsā-sthiti
> mandira-nikaṭe yāite mora nāhi śakti

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mandira]]-[[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near the temple; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---I hear; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---residential place; [[synonyms:m:mandira]]-[[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near the temple; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---there is no power

===== Translation =====

**"I have heard that the residential quarters of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are near the temple of Jagannātha. But I shall not have the power to go near the temple.**
