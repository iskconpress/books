====== CC Antya 4.207 ======

===== Text =====

> dola-yātrā dekhi' prabhu tāṅre vidāya dilā
> vṛndāvane ye karibena, saba śikhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dola]]-[[synonyms:y:yātrā]]---the festival of Dola-yātrā; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bid farewell; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yekaribena]]---whatever he would do; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---instructed.

===== Translation =====

**After they saw the Dola-yātrā festival, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu instructed Sanātana Gosvāmī fully about what to do in Vṛndāvana and bade him farewell.**
