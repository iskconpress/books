====== CC Antya 4.37 ======

===== Text =====

> "tomā-duṅhāra ājñā āmi kemane laṅghimu?
> dīkṣā-mantra deha' kṛṣṇa-bhajana karimu"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]]---of you; [[synonyms:d:duṅhāra]]---of both; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---the order; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kemane]]---how; [[synonyms:l:laṅghimu]]---shall disobey; [[synonyms:d:dīkṣā]]---initiation; [[synonyms:m:mantra]]---mantra; [[synonyms:d:deha']]---just give; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:b:bhajana]]---devotional service to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karimu]]---I shall perform.

===== Translation =====

**"Vallabha replied, 'My dear brothers, how can I disobey your orders? Initiate me into the Kṛṣṇa mantra so that I may perform devotional service to Lord Kṛṣṇa.'**
