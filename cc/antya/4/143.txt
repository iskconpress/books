====== CC Antya 4.143 ======

===== Text =====

> ye-kārye āilā, prabhura dekhilā caraṇa
> rathe jagannātha dekhi' karaha gamana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:k:kārye]]---for which business; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---you have come; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---you have seen; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the feet; [[synonyms:r:rathe]]---on the car; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---go.

===== Translation =====

**'Your purpose in coming has been fulfilled, for you have seen the lotus feet of the Lord. Therefore, after seeing Lord Jagannātha on the Ratha-yātrā car, you can leave."**
