====== CC Antya 4.27 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra bhāi anupamera haila gaṅgā-prāpti
> bhāla chila, raghunāthe dṛḍha tāra bhakti"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhāi]]---your brother; [[synonyms:a:anupamera]]---of Anupama; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:g:gaṅgā]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---death; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:c:chila]]---he was a very good man; [[synonyms:r:raghu]]-[[synonyms:n:nāthe]]---unto Lord Raghunātha (Lord Rāmacandra); [[synonyms:d:dṛḍha]]---firm; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---his devotion.

===== Translation =====

**"Your brother Anupama is now dead. He was a very good devotee who had firm conviction in Raghunātha [Lord Rāmacandra]."**
