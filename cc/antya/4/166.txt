====== CC Antya 4.166 ======

===== Text =====

> 'jagadānanda priya āmāra nahe tomā haite
> maryādā-laṅghana āmi nā pāroṅ sahite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---more dear; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---to Me; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---than you; [[synonyms:m:maryādā]]-[[synonyms:l:laṅghana]]---transgressing the etiquette; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pāroṅ]]---can; [[synonyms:s:sahite]]---tolerate.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Sanātana, please do not think that Jagadānanda is more dear to Me than you. However, I cannot tolerate transgressions of the standard etiquette.**
