====== Cc. Antya 4.111 ======

===== Text =====

> yathā-yogya karāila sabāra caraṇa vandana
> tāṅre karāilā sabāra kṛpāra bhājana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---as it is fit; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---caused to perform; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---made; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāra]] [[synonyms:b:bhājana]]---object of mercy.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord asked Sanātana Gosvāmī to offer obeisances to all the devotees in a way that befitted each one. Thus He introduced Sanātana Gosvāmī to them all, just to make him an object of their mercy.**
