====== CC Antya 4.62 ======

===== Text =====

> gāḍhānurāgera viyoga nā yāya sahana
> tāte anurāgī vāñche āpana maraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]-[[synonyms:a:anurāgera]]---of one who has deep attachment; [[synonyms:v:viyoga]]---separation; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:s:sahana]]---tolerated; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---therefore; [[synonyms:a:anurāgī]]---a deeply attached devotee; [[synonyms:v:vāñche]]---desires; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]] [[synonyms:m:maraṇa]]---death of himself.

===== Translation =====

**"One who is deeply in love with Kṛṣṇa cannot tolerate separation from the Lord. Therefore such a devotee always desires his own death.**
