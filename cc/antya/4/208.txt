====== CC Antya 4.208 ======

===== Text =====

> ye-kāle vidāya hailā prabhura caraṇe
> dui-janāra viccheda-daśā nā yāya varṇane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---when; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]]---farewell; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---there was; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:janāra]]---both of them; [[synonyms:v:viccheda]]-[[synonyms:d:daśā]]---condition of separation; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:v:varṇane]]---cannot be described.

===== Translation =====

**The scene of separation that took place when Sanātana Gosvāmī and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took leave of one another is so piteous that it cannot be described herein.**
