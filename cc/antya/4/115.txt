====== Cc. Antya 4.115 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve vaiśākha-māse sanātana yabe āilā
> jyaiṣṭha-māse prabhu tāṅre parīkṣā karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:v:vaiśākha]]-[[synonyms:m:māse]]---during the month of April-May; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:j:jyaiṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:m:māse]]---in the month of May-June; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---tested.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī had come to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Jagannātha Purī during the month of April-May, and during the month of May-June Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tested him.**
