====== Cc. Antya 4.231 ======

===== Text =====

> 'ṣaṭ sandarbhe' kṛṣṇa-prema-tattva prakāśila
> cāri-lakṣa grantha teṅho vistāra karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṭ]] [[synonyms:s:sandarbhe]]---in the //Ṣaṭ-sandarbha//; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:t:tattva]]---the truth about transcendental love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prakāśila]]---he exhibited; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣa]] [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---400,000 verses; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---expanded.

===== Translation =====

**In the Ṣaṭ-sandarbha Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī set forth the truths about the transcendental love of Kṛṣṇa. In this way he expanded 400,000 verses in all his books.**
