====== CC Antya 4.212 ======

===== Text =====

> ye-ye-līlā prabhu pathe kailā ye-ye-sthāne
> tāhā dekhi' premāveśa haya sanātane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pathe]]---on the way; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in whatever places; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---those places; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---by seeing; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---in Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**As soon as Sanātana Gosvāmī visited a place where Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had performed His pastimes on the way, he was immediately filled with ecstatic love.**
