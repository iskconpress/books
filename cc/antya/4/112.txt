====== CC Antya 4.112 ======

===== Text =====

> sad-guṇe, pāṇḍitye, sabāra priya---sanātana
> yathā-yogya kṛpā-maitrī-gaurava-bhājana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---in good qualities; [[synonyms:p:pāṇḍitye]]---in learning; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:p:priya]]---dear to everyone; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogya]]---as it is suitable; [[synonyms:k:kṛpa]]---mercy; [[synonyms:m:maitrī]]---friendship; [[synonyms:g:gaurava]]---honor; [[synonyms:b:bhājana]]---worthy of being offered.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī was dear to everyone because of his exalted qualities in learning. Suitably, therefore, they bestowed upon him mercy, friendship and honor.**
