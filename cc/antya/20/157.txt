====== CC Antya 20.157 ======

===== Text =====

> śāke sindhv-agni-vāṇendau
> jyaiṣṭhe vṛndāvanāntare
> sūryāhe 'sita-pañcamyāṁ
> grantho 'yaṁ pūrṇatāṁ gataḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śāke]]---in the Śakābda Era; [[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:a:agni]]-[[synonyms:v:vāṇendau]]---in 1537; [[synonyms:j:jyaiṣṭhe]]---in the month of Jyaiṣṭha (May-June); [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---in the forest of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:s:sūrya]]-[[synonyms:a:ahe]]---on the day of the sun (Sunday); [[synonyms:a:asita]]-[[synonyms:p:pañcamyām]]---on the fifth day of the dark fortnight; [[synonyms:g:granthaḥ]]---book; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this (//Caitanya-caritāmṛta)//; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇatām]]---completion; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---achieved.

===== Translation =====

**In Vṛndāvana in the year 1537, Śakābda Era, in the month of Jyaiṣṭha [May-June], on Sunday, the fifth day of the waning moon, this Caitanya-caritāmṛta has been completed.**


//Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports to Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta, Antya-līlā,  Twentieth Chapter, describing the meaning of Śikṣāṣṭaka and how the Lord tasted it Himself.//

END OF THE ANTYA-LĪLĀ
