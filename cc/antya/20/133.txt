====== CC Antya 20.133 ======

===== Text =====

> bhāva-śābalye punaḥ kailā pralapana
> karṇāmṛta-ślokera artha kailā vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śābalye]]---from the aggregate of all ecstatic emotions; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:p:pralapana]]---He talked like a madman; [[synonyms:k:karṇāmṛta]]-[[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of a verse from //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta//; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---described in detail.

===== Translation =====

**Due to the conjunction of various ecstatic emotions, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again began speaking like a madman and described in detail the meaning of a verse from Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta.**
