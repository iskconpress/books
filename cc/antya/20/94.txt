====== CC Antya 20.94 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā-roga-grasta,---calite vasite nā pāri
> pañca-roga-pīḍā-vyākula, rātri-dine mari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:r:roga]]-[[synonyms:g:grasta]]---affected by so many diseases; [[synonyms:c:calite]]---to walk; [[synonyms:v:vasite]]---to sit down; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am not properly able; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:r:roga]]-[[synonyms:p:pīḍā]]-[[synonyms:v:vyākula]]---always disturbed by five kinds of disease; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day or night; [[synonyms:m:mari]]---I can die any time.

===== Translation =====

**I am infected by so many diseases that I can neither properly walk nor properly sit. Indeed, I am always exhausted by five kinds of disease. I may die at any time of the day or night.**
