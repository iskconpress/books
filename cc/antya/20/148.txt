====== CC Antya 20.148 ======

===== Text =====

> śiṣyāra śrama dekhi' guru nācāna rākhilā
> 'kṛpā' nā nācāya, 'vāṇī' vasiyā rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śiṣyāra]]---of the disciples; [[synonyms:ś:śrama]]---the fatigue; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:g:guru]]---the spiritual master; [[synonyms:n:nācāna]] [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---stopped causing the dancing; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---mercy; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:n:nācāya]]---does not make dance; [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---the words; [[synonyms:v:vasiyā]]---sitting down; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remain silent.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing the fatigue of the disciples, the spiritual master has stopped making them dance, and because that mercy no longer makes them dance, my words now sit silently.**
