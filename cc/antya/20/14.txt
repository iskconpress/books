====== Cc. Antya 20.14 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-premodgama, premāmṛta-āsvādana
> kṛṣṇa-prāpti, sevāmṛta-samudre majjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:udgama]]---awakening of love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting of the transcendental bliss of love for Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāpti]]---attainment of the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sevā]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---of the nectar of service; [[synonyms:s:samudre]]---in the ocean; [[synonyms:m:majjana]]---immersing.

===== Translation =====

**"The result of chanting is that one awakens his love for Kṛṣṇa and tastes transcendental bliss. Ultimately, one attains the association of Kṛṣṇa and engages in His devotional service, as if immersing himself in a great ocean of love."**
