====== Cc. Antya 20.116 ======

===== Text =====

> navame---gopīnātha-paṭṭanāyaka-mocana
> trijagatera loka prabhura pāila daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:navame]]---in the Ninth Chapter; [[synonyms:g:gopīnātha]]-[[synonyms:p:paṭṭanāyaka]]-[[synonyms:m:mocana]]---the deliverance of Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka, the brother of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:j:jagatera]]---of the three worlds; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāila]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---got the audience.

===== Translation =====

**In the Ninth Chapter is a description of how Gopīnātha Paṭṭanāyaka was delivered and how the people of the three worlds were able to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
