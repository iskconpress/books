====== CC Antya 20.20 ======

===== Text =====

> ye-rūpe la-ile nāma prema upajaya
> tāhāra lakṣaṇa śuna, svarūpa-rāma-rāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpe]]---by which process; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ile]]---if chanting; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name; [[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:u:upajaya]]---dormant love of Kṛṣṇa awakens; [[synonyms:t:tāhāra]] [[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]] [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---just hear the symptom of that; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---O Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "O Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya, hear from Me the symptoms of how one should chant the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra to awaken very easily one's dormant love for Kṛṣṇa.**
