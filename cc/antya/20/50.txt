====== Cc. Antya 20.50 ======

===== Text =====

> chāḍi' anya nārī-gaṇa, mora vaśa tanu-mana,
> mora saubhāgya prakaṭa kariyā
> tā-sabāre deya pīḍā, āmā-sane kare krīḍā,
> sei nārī-gaṇe dekhāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---women; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---control; [[synonyms:t:tanu]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind and body; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:s:saubhāgya]]---fortune; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---manifesting; [[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāre]]---unto all of them; [[synonyms:d:deya]] [[synonyms:p:pīḍā]]---gives distress; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with Me; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---performs loving activities; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:n:nārī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---unto these women; [[synonyms:d:dekhāñā]]---showing.

===== Translation =====

**"Sometimes Kṛṣṇa gives up the company of other gopīs and becomes controlled, mind and body, by Me. Thus He manifests My good fortune and gives others distress by performing His loving affairs with Me.**
