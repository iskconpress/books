====== CC Antya 20.114 ======

===== Text =====

> saptama-paricchede---vallabha bhaṭṭera milana
> nānā-mate kailā tāṅra garva khaṇḍana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saptama]]-[[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Seventh Chapter; [[synonyms:v:vallabha]] [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]] [[synonyms:m:milana]]---the meeting of Vallabha Bhaṭṭa with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in various ways; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:g:garva]]---pride; [[synonyms:k:khaṇḍana]]---dismantling.

===== Translation =====

**The Seventh Chapter tells how Śrī Caitanya met Vallabha Bhaṭṭa and dismantled his false pride in various ways.**
