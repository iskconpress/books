====== CC Antya 20.111 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye 'bāṅgāla'-kavira nāṭaka-upekṣaṇa
> svarūpa-gosāñi kailā vigrahera mahimā-sthāpana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that chapter; [[synonyms:b:bāṅgāla]]-[[synonyms:k:kavira]]---of a poet from Bengal; [[synonyms:n:nāṭaka]]-[[synonyms:u:upekṣaṇa]]---the rejection of the drama; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:v:vigrahera]]---of the Deity; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---the establishment of the glories.

===== Translation =====

**Also in that chapter, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī rejected the drama of a poet from Bengal and established the glories of the Deity.**
