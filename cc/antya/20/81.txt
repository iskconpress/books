====== Cc. Antya 20.81 ======

===== Text =====

> yāvat buddhira gati, tateka varṇiluṅ
> samudrera madhye yena eka kaṇa chuṅiluṅ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāvat]]---as far; [[synonyms:b:buddhira]] [[synonyms:g:gati]]---the limit of my intelligence; [[synonyms:t:tateka]]---that far; [[synonyms:v:varṇiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:s:samudrera]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in the midst of the great ocean; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---just like; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:kaṇa]]---one particle; [[synonyms:c:chuṅiluṅ]]---I have touched.

===== Translation =====

**I have tried to describe them as far as my intelligence allows, as if trying to touch a drop in the midst of a great ocean.**
