====== CC Antya 20.130 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-prasādera tāhāṅ mahimā varṇilā
> kṛṣṇādharāmṛtera phala-śloka āsvādilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:p:prasādera]]---of the remnants of the food of the Lord, //mahā-prasāda//; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories; [[synonyms:v:varṇilā]]---described; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:adhara]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛtera]]---of the nectar from the lips of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse mentioning the result; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādilā]]---tasted.

===== Translation =====

**Also in that chapter, the glories of mahā-prasāda are explained, and a verse is tasted describing the effect of nectar from the lips of Kṛṣṇa.**
