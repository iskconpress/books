====== Cc. Antya 20.6 ======

===== Text =====

> sei sei bhāve nija-śloka paḍiyā
> ślokera artha āsvādaye dui-bandhu lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in that particular emotion; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---reciting His own verses; [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verses; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---meaning; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādaye]]---tastes; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:b:bandhu]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---with two friends.

===== Translation =====

**He would recite His own verses, expressing their meanings and emotions, and thus enjoy tasting them with these two friends.**
