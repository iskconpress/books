====== Cc. Antya 20.89 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra jhārī-śeṣāmṛta kichu more dilā
> tateke bharila peṭa, tṛṣṇā mora gelā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:j:jhārī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śeṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the remnants of the milk of Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura's pitcher; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:m:more]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---has given to me; [[synonyms:t:tateke]]---by those remnants; [[synonyms:b:bharila]] [[synonyms:p:peṭa]]---my abdomen is filled; [[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇā]] [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---now my thirst has gone.

===== Translation =====

**Whatever remnants of milk Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has given me are sufficient to fill my belly. Now my thirst is completely satiated.**
