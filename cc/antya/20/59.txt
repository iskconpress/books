====== CC Antya 20.59 ======

===== Text =====

> mora sukha---sevane, kṛṣṇera sukha---saṅgame,
> ataeva deha deṅa dāna
> kṛṣṇa more 'kāntā' kari', kahe more 'prāṇeśvari',
> mora haya 'dāsī'-abhimāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---My happiness; [[synonyms:s:sevane]]---in service; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---Kṛṣṇa's happiness; [[synonyms:s:saṅgame]]---by union with Me; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---My body; [[synonyms:d:deṅa]]---I offer; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---as charity; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:more]]---Me; [[synonyms:k:kāntā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting as beloved; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:m:more]]---to Me; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśvari]]---the most beloved; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:d:dāsī]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---considering His maidservant.

===== Translation =====

**"My happiness is in the service of Kṛṣṇa, and Kṛṣṇa's happiness is in union with Me. For this reason, I give My body in charity to the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa, who accepts Me as His loved one and calls Me His most beloved. It is then that I consider Myself His maidservant.**
