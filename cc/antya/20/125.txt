====== CC Antya 20.125 ======

===== Text =====

> caṭaka-parvata dekhi' prabhura dhāvana
> tāra madhye prabhura kichu pralāpa-varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caṭaka]]-[[synonyms:p:parvata]]---the hill known as Caṭaka-parvata; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:d:dhāvana]]---the running of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in that chapter; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---talking like a madman.

===== Translation =====

**Also in that chapter there is a description of how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ran toward Caṭaka-parvata and spoke like a madman.**
