====== CC Antya 20.38 ======

===== Text =====

> rasāntarāveśe ha-ila viyoga-sphuraṇa
> udvega, viṣāda, dainye kare pralapana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstatic love of different mellows; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:v:viyoga]]-[[synonyms:s:sphuraṇa]]---awakening of separation; [[synonyms:u:udvega]]---distress; [[synonyms:v:viṣāda]]---moroseness; [[synonyms:d:dainye]]---humility; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:pralapana]]---speaks like a crazy person.

===== Translation =====

**Separation from Kṛṣṇa awoke various mellows of distress, lamentation and humility. Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke like a crazy man.**
