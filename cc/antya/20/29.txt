====== CC Antya 20.29 ======

===== Text =====

> na dhanaṁ na janaṁ na sundarīṁ
> kavitāṁ vā jagadīśa kāmaye
> mama janmani jamanīśvare
> bhavatād bhaktir ahaitukī tvayi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:d:dhanam]]---riches; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:j:janam]]---followers; [[synonyms:n:na]]---not; [[synonyms:s:sundarīm]]---a very beautiful woman; [[synonyms:k:kavitām]]---fruitive activities described in flowery language; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:ī:īśa]]---O Lord of the universe; [[synonyms:k:kāmaye]]---I desire; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---My; [[synonyms:j:janmani]]---in birth; [[synonyms:j:janmani]]---after birth; [[synonyms:ī:īśvare]]---unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:b:bhavatāt]]---let there be; [[synonyms:b:bhaktiḥ]]---devotional service; [[synonyms:a:ahaitukī]]---with no motives; [[synonyms:t:tvayi]]---unto You.

===== Translation =====

**"O Lord of the universe, I do not desire material wealth, materialistic followers, a beautiful wife or fruitive activities described in flowery language. All I want, life after life, is unmotivated devotional service to You.**
