====== Cc. Antya 20.106 ======

===== Text =====

> tṛtīye---haridāsera mahimā pracaṇḍa
> dāmodara-paṇḍita kailā prabhure vākya-danda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tṛtīye]]---in the Third Chapter; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Ṭhākura Haridāsa; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]] [[synonyms:p:pracaṇḍa]]---very forceful glories; [[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vākya]]-[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]---impudence of chastising by words.

===== Translation =====

**In the Third Chapter is a description of the forceful glories of Haridāsa Ṭhākura. That chapter also mentions how Dāmodara Paṇḍita spoke words of criticism to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
