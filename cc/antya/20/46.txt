====== CC Antya 20.46 ======

===== Text =====

> sei bhāve prabhu sei śloka uccārilā
> śloka uccārite tad-rūpa āpane ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in that ecstatic mood; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:u:uccārilā]]---recited; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verse; [[synonyms:u:uccārite]]---by reciting; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---like Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---Himself; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**In the same spirit of ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited that verse, and as soon as He did so, He felt like Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī.**
