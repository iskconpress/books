====== Cc. Antya 20.35 ======

===== Text =====

> punaḥ ati-utkaṇṭhā, dainya ha-ila udgama
> kṛṣṇa-ṭhāñi māge prema-nāma-saṅkīrtana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---great eagerness; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]---humility; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:u:udgama]]---was awakened; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:t:thāñi]]---from Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---prays for; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting the //mahā-mantra.//

===== Translation =====

**Natural humility and eagerness then awoke in Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He prayed to Kṛṣṇa to be able to chant the mahā-mantra in ecstatic love.**
