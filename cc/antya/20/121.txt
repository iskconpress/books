====== Cc. Antya 20.121 ======

===== Text =====

> trayodaśe---jagadānanda mathurā yāi' āilā
> mahāprabhu deva-dāsīra gīta śunilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:trayodaśe]]---in the Thirteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---going to Mathurā; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsīra]]---of the deva-dāsī dancing girl; [[synonyms:g:gīta]] [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---heard the song.

===== Translation =====

**In the Thirteenth Chapter, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita went to Mathurā and returned, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu by chance heard a song sung by a deva-dāsī dancing girl.**
