====== CC Antya 20.103 ======

===== Text =====

> prathama paricchede---rūpera dvitīya-milana
> tāra madhye dui-nāṭakera vidhāna-śravaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prathama]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the First Chapter; [[synonyms:r:rūpera]]---of Rūpa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]-[[synonyms:m:milana]]---the second meeting with Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that chapter; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:n:nāṭakera]]---of the two dramas; [[synonyms:v:vidhāna]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇa]]---hearing of the process of writing.

===== Translation =====

**The First Chapter describes how Rūpa Gosvāmī met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for the second time and how the Lord heard his two dramas [Vidagdha-mādhava and Lalita-mādhava].**
