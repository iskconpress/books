====== CC Antya 20.88 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-līlāmṛta-sindhu---dugdhābdhi-samāna
> tṛṣṇānurūpa jhārī bhari' teṅho kailā pāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---the ocean of nectarean pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:a:abdhi]]-[[synonyms:s:samāna]]---exactly like the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇā]]-[[synonyms:a:anurūpa]]---according to one's thirst; [[synonyms:j:jhārī]]---the pitcher; [[synonyms:b:bhari']]---filling; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drank.

===== Translation =====

**The ocean of nectarean pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is like the ocean of milk. According to his thirst, Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura filled his pitcher and drank from that ocean.**
