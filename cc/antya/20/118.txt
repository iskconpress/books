====== CC Antya 20.118 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye govindera kailā parīkṣaṇa
> tāra madhye parimuṇḍā-nṛtyera varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that chapter; [[synonyms:g:govindera]]---of Govinda, His personal assistant; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣaṇa]]---testing; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in that chapter; [[synonyms:p:parimuṇḍā]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛtyera]] [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dancing in the temple.

===== Translation =====

**Also in that chapter is a description of how the Lord examined Govinda and how He danced in the temple.**
