====== CC Antya 20.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya gauracandra jaya nityānanda
> jayādvaita-candra jaya gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to Advaita Prabhu; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛnda]]---to the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Lord Nityānanda! All glories to Advaitacandra! And all glories to all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu!**
