====== Cc. Antya 20.39 ======

===== Text =====

> yugāyitaṁ nimeṣeṇa
> cakṣuṣā prāvṛṣāyitam
> śūnyāyitaṁ jagat sarvaṁ
> govinda-viraheṇa me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yugāyitam]]---appearing like a great millennium; [[synonyms:n:nimeṣeṇa]]---by a moment; [[synonyms:c:cakṣuṣā]]---from the eyes; [[synonyms:p:prāvṛṣāyitam]]---tears falling like torrents of rain; [[synonyms:ś:śūnyāyitam]]---appearing void; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the world; [[synonyms:s:sarvam]]---all; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---from Lord Govinda, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viraheṇa]] [[synonyms:m:me]]---by My separation.

===== Translation =====

**"My Lord Govinda, because of separation from You, I consider even a moment a great millennium. Tears flow from My eyes like torrents of rain, and I see the entire world as void.**
