====== Cc. Antya 20.1 ======

===== Text =====

> premodbhāvita-harṣerṣyod-
> vega-dainyārti-miśritam
> lapitaṁ gauracandrasya
> bhāgyavadbhir niṣevyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:udbhāvita]]---produced from ecstatic love and emotion; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:ī:īrṣyā]]---envy; [[synonyms:u:udvega]]---agitation; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]---submissiveness; [[synonyms:ā:ārti]]---grief; [[synonyms:m:miśritam]]---mixed with; [[synonyms:l:lapitam]]---talks like those of a crazy man; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:c:candrasya]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavadbhiḥ]]---by the most fortunate; [[synonyms:n:niṣevyate]]---is enjoyable.

===== Translation =====

**Only the most fortunate will relish the mad words of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, which mixed with jubilation, envy, agitation, submissiveness and grief, all produced by ecstatic loving emotions.**
