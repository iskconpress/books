====== CC Antya 20.109 ======

===== Text =====

> jyaiṣṭha-māsera dhūpe tāṅre kailā parīkṣaṇa
> śakti sañcāriyā punaḥ pāṭhāilā vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jyaiṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:m:māsera]]---of the month of May-June; [[synonyms:d:dhūpe]]---in the sunshine; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:parīkṣaṇa]]---examining; [[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---potency; [[synonyms:s:sañcāriyā]]---giving him; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---sent back to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**The Fourth Chapter also tells how Sanātana Gosvāmī was tested in the sunshine of Jyaiṣṭha [May and June] and was then empowered and sent back to Vṛndāvana.**
