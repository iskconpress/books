====== CC Antya 20.5 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā-bhāva uṭhe prabhura harṣa, śoka, roṣa
> dainyodvega-ārti utkaṇṭhā, santoṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---all kinds of emotion; [[synonyms:u:uṭhe]]---awaken; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:harṣa]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:ś:śoka]]---lamentation; [[synonyms:r:roṣa]]---anger; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]---humility; [[synonyms:u:udvega]]---anxiety; [[synonyms:ā:ārti]]---grief; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]---more eagerness; [[synonyms:s:santoṣa]]---satisfaction.

===== Translation =====

**He relished the symptoms of various transcendental emotions such as jubilation, lamentation, anger, humility, anxiety, grief, eagerness and satisfaction.**
