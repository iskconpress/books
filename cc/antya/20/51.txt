====== Cc. Antya 20.51 ======

===== Text =====

> kibā teṅho lampaṭa, śaṭha, dhṛṣṭa, sakapaṭa,
> anya nārī-gaṇa kari' sātha
> more dite manaḥ-pīḍā, mora āge kare krīḍā,
> tabu teṅho---mora prāṇa-nātha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kibā]]---or; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:l:lampaṭa]]---debauchee; [[synonyms:ś:śaṭha]]---deceitful; [[synonyms:d:dhṛṣṭa]]---obstinate; [[synonyms:s:sakapaṭa]]---with a cheating propensity; [[synonyms:a:anya]]---other; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---women; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting; [[synonyms:s:sātha]]---as companions; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]-[[synonyms:p:pīḍā]]---distress in the mind; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Me; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]]---performs loving affairs; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]---the Lord of My life.

===== Translation =====

**"Or, since after all He is a very cunning, obstinate debauchee with a propensity to cheat, He takes to the company of other women. He then indulges in loving affairs with them in front of Me to give distress to My mind. Nevertheless, He is still the Lord of My life.**
