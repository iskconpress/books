====== CC Antya 20.76 ======

===== Text =====

> ye kichu kahiluṅ ei dig-daraśana
> ei anusāre habe tāra āsvādana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have said; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:d:dik]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---just to make an indication; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:a:anusāre]]---in this way; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---there will be; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---tasting.

===== Translation =====

**What I have described gives merely an indication, but by following this indication one may obtain a taste of all the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
