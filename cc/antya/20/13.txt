====== CC Antya 20.13 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkīrtana haite pāpa-saṁsāra-nāśana
> citta-śuddhi, sarva-bhakti-sādhana-udgama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from the process of chanting the holy name; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṁsāra]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśana]]---annihilation of materialistic life resulting from sins; [[synonyms:c:citta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śuddhi]]---cleansing of the heart; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---all kinds of devotional service; [[synonyms:s:sādhana]]---of the performances; [[synonyms:u:udgama]]---awakening.

===== Translation =====

**"By performing congregational chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra, one can destroy the sinful condition of material existence, purify the unclean heart and awaken all varieties of devotional service.**
