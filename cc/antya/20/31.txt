====== CC Antya 20.31 ======

===== Text =====

> ati-dainye punaḥ māge dāsya-bhakti-dāna
> āpanāre kare saṁsārī jīva-abhimāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ati]]-[[synonyms:d:dainye]]---in great humility; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---begs; [[synonyms:d:dāsya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]-[[synonyms:d:dāna]]---the awarding of devotion in servitude; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāre]]---to Himself; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:s:saṁsārī]]---materialistic; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:a:abhimāna]]---conception as a conditioned soul.

===== Translation =====

**In great humility, considering Himself a conditioned soul of the material world, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again expressed His desire to be endowed with service to the Lord.**
