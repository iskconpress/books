====== CC Antya 20.107 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu 'nāma' diyā kailā brahmāṇḍa-mocana
> haridāsa karilā nāmera mahimā-sthāpana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāma]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering the holy name; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:b:brahmāṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:m:mocana]]---the liberation of the universe; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:n:nāmera]]---of the holy name; [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāpana]]---the establishment of the glories.

===== Translation =====

**The Third Chapter also tells how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu delivered everyone by bestowing upon the universe the holy name of the Lord, and it describes how Haridāsa Ṭhākura established the glories of the holy name by his practical example.**
