====== CC Antya 20.72 ======

===== Text =====

> yata ceṣṭā, yata pralāpa,---nāhi pārāvāra
> sei saba varṇite grantha haya suvistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---all activities; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---all crazy talking; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pārāvāra]]---there was no limit; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of them; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---to describe; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]---the book; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---would be; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---very voluminous.

===== Translation =====

**There is no limit to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's activities and His words of madness. Therefore describing them all would greatly increase the size of this book.**
