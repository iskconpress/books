====== CC Antya 20.47 ======

===== Text =====

> āśliṣya vā pāda-ratāṁ pinaṣṭu mām
> adarśanān marma-hatāṁ karotu vā
> yathā tathā vā vidadhātu lampaṭo
> mat-prāṇa-nāthas tu sa eva nāparaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āśliṣya]]---embracing with great pleasure; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:r:ratām]]---who is fallen at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:pinaṣṭu]]---let Him trample; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---Me; [[synonyms:a:adarśanāt]]---by not being visible; [[synonyms:m:marma]]-[[synonyms:h:hatām]]---brokenhearted; [[synonyms:k:karotu]]---let Him make; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as (He likes); [[synonyms:t:tathā]]---so; [[synonyms:v:vā]]---or; [[synonyms:v:vidadhātu]]---let Him do; [[synonyms:l:lampaṭaḥ]]---a debauchee, who mixes with other women; [[synonyms:m:mat]]-[[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāthaḥ]]---the Lord of My life; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---He; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---only; [[synonyms:n:na]] [[synonyms:a:aparaḥ]]---not anyone else.

===== Translation =====

**"Let Kṛṣṇa tightly embrace this maidservant, who has fallen at His lotus feet. Let Him trample Me or break My heart by never being visible to Me. He is a debauchee, after all, and can do whatever He likes, but He is still no one other than the worshipable Lord of My heart.**
