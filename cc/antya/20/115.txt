====== Cc. Antya 20.115 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭame---rāmacandra-purīra āgamana
> tāṅra bhaye kailā prabhu bhikṣā saṅkocana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭame]]---in the Eighth Chapter; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---the arrival of Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:b:bhaye]]---because of fear of him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkocana]]---minimizing His eating.

===== Translation =====

**The Eighth Chapter describes the arrival of Rāmacandra Purī and how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu minimized His eating due to fear of him.**
