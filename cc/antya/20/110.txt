====== CC Antya 20.110 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcame---pradyumna-miśre prabhu kṛpā karilā
> rāya-dvārā kṛṣṇa-kathā tāṅre śunāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañcame]]---in the Fifth Chapter; [[synonyms:p:pradyumna]]-[[synonyms:m:miśre]]---unto Pradyumna Miśra; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---showed mercy; [[synonyms:r:rāya]]-[[synonyms:d:dvārā]]---with the help of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:ś:śunāilā]]---made him hear.

===== Translation =====

**In the Fifth Chapter, the Lord showed His favor to Pradyumna Miśra and made him hear topics of Kṛṣṇa from Rāmānanda Rāya.**
