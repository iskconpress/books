====== CC Antya 20.63 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu bhāvāviṣṭa hañā
> pralāpa karilā tat-tat śloka paḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being overwhelmed by ecstatic love; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---said crazy words; [[synonyms:t:tat]]-[[synonyms:t:tat]]---appropriate; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---by reading the verse.

===== Translation =====

**Thus overwhelmed by ecstatic love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke like a madman and recited suitable verses.**
