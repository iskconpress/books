====== CC Antya 20.71 ======

===== Text =====

> jīva kṣudra-buddhi kon tāhā pāre varṇite?
> tāra eka kaṇā sparśi āpanā śodhite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jīva]]---living being; [[synonyms:k:kṣudra]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---limited intelligence; [[synonyms:k:kon]]---who; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is able; [[synonyms:v:varṇite]]---to write; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:k:kaṇā]]---one particle; [[synonyms:s:sparśi]]---I touch; [[synonyms:ā:āpanā]] [[synonyms:ś:śodhite]]---to correct myself.

===== Translation =====

**How, then, could an ordinary living being with very little intelligence describe such pastimes? Nevertheless, I am trying to touch but a particle of them just to rectify my own self.**
