====== Cc. Antya 20.132 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera śabda-guṇe prabhura mana ākarṣilā
> "kā stry aṅga te" ślokera artha āveśe karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:ś:śabda]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇe]]---by the qualities of the sound; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:ā:ākarṣilā]]---was attracted; [[synonyms:k:kā]] [[synonyms:s:stry]] [[synonyms:a:aṅga]] [[synonyms:t:te]] [[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of the verse beginning with the words //kā stry aṅga te//; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstasy; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---described.

===== Translation =====

**Also in the Seventeenth Chapter, the attributes of Kṛṣṇa's sound attracted the mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who described in ecstasy the meaning of the "kā stry aṅga te" verse.**
