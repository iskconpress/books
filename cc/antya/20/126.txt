====== Cc. Antya 20.126 ======

===== Text =====

> pañcadaśa-paricchede---udyāna-vilāse
> vṛndāvana-bhrame yāhāṅ karilā praveśe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pañcadaśa]]-[[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Fifteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:u:udyāna]]-[[synonyms:v:vilāse]]---in His pastimes within the garden; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:b:bhrame]]---His taking the garden to be Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:p:praveśe]]---He entered.

===== Translation =====

**In the Fifteenth Chapter is a description of how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu entered a garden on the shore of the sea, mistaking the garden for Vṛndāvana.**
