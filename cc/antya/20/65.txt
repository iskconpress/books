====== CC Antya 20.65 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura 'śikṣāṣṭaka'-śloka yei paḍe, śune
> kṛṣṇe prema-bhakti tāra bāḍe dine-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]]-[[synonyms:a:aṣṭaka]]---of the eight instructions; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---the verses; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---or hears; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---ecstatic love and devotion; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:d:dine]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day after day.

===== Translation =====

**If anyone recites or hears these eight verses of instruction by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, his ecstatic love and devotion for Kṛṣṇa increase day by day.**
