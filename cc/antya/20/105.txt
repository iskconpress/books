====== CC Antya 20.105 ======

===== Text =====

> dvitīye---choṭa-haridāse karāilā śikṣana
> tāra madhye śivānandera āścarya darśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvitīye]]---in the Second Chapter; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:h:haridāse]]---Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]] [[synonyms:ś:śikṣaṇa]]---He taught very strictly; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that chapter; [[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---the wonderful vision.

===== Translation =====

**In the Second Chapter the Lord instructively punished Junior Haridāsa. Also in that chapter is the wonderful vision of Śivānanda Sena.**
