====== Cc. Antya 20.137 ======

===== Text =====

> vasanta-rajanīte puṣpodyāne viharaṇa
> kṛṣṇera saurabhya-ślokera artha-vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vasanta]]-[[synonyms:r:rajanīte]]---on a spring night; [[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---in a flower garden; [[synonyms:v:viharaṇa]]---the wandering; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:saurabhya]]-[[synonyms:ś:ślokera]]---of a verse about the bodily fragrance; [[synonyms:a:artha]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---describing the meaning.

===== Translation =====

**That chapter also describes Kṛṣṇa's wandering in a garden on a spring night, and it fully describes the meaning of a verse about the scent of Kṛṣṇa's body.**
