====== CC Antya 20.128 ======

===== Text =====

> ṣoḍaśe---kālidāse prabhu kṛpā karilā
> vaiṣṇavocchiṣṭa khāibāra phala dekhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣoḍaśe]]---in the Sixteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:k:kāli]]-[[synonyms:d:dāse]]---unto Kālidāsa; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---showed favor; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:u:ucchiṣṭa]] [[synonyms:k:khāibāra]]---of eating the remnants of food left by Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:p:phala]] [[synonyms:d:dekhāilā]]---showed the result.

===== Translation =====

**The Sixteenth Chapter tells how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed His mercy to Kālidāsa and thus demonstrated the result of eating the remnants of the food of Vaiṣṇavas.**
