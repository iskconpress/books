====== CC Antya 20.19 ======

===== Text =====

> "sarva-śakti nāme dilā kariyā vibhāga
> āmāra durdaiva,---nāme nāhi anurāga!!"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---all potencies; [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in the holy name; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---You have bestowed; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:v:vibhāga]]---making separation; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:d:durdaiva]]---My misfortune; [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---for chanting the holy names; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---there is not; [[synonyms:a:anurāga]]---attachment.

===== Translation =====

**"You have invested Your full potencies in each individual holy name, but I am so unfortunate that I have no attachment for chanting Your holy names."**
