====== CC Antya 20.84 ======

===== Text =====

> ye kichu varṇiluṅ, seha saṅkṣepa kariyā
> likhite nā pārena, tabu rākhiyāchena likhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:v:varṇiluṅ]]---whatever I have described; [[synonyms:s:seha]]---them; [[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepa]]---briefly; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---doing; [[synonyms:l:likhite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pārena]]---Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura was not able to describe; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:r:rākhiyāchena]]---has kept; [[synonyms:l:likhiyā]]---recording in writing.

===== Translation =====

**What I have described was left aside by Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura, but although he could not describe these pastimes, he gave us a synopsis.**
