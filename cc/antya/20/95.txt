====== Cc. Antya 20.95 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve granthe ihā kariyāchi nivedana
> tathāpi likhiye, śuna ihāra kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---previously; [[synonyms:g:granthe]]---in the book; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kariyāchi]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---I have submitted to the readers; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:l:likhiye]]---I write; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---please hear; [[synonyms:i:ihārakāraṇa]]---the reason for this.

===== Translation =====

**I have previously given an account of my inabilities. Please hear the reason why I nevertheless still write.**
