====== CC Antya 20.45 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bhāve rādhāra mana asthira ha-ilā
> sakhī-gaṇa-āge prauḍhi-śloka ye paḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in that mood; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:a:asthira]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became agitated; [[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before the friends, the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:p:prauḍhi]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---advanced devotional verse; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---She recited.

===== Translation =====

**In that mood, the mind of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī was agitated, and therefore She spoke a verse of advanced devotion to Her gopī friends.**
