====== CC Antya 20.156 ======

===== Text =====

> parimala-vāsita-bhuvanaṁ
> svarasonmādita-rasajña-rolambam
> giridhara-caraṇāmbhojaṁ
> kaḥ khalu rasikaḥ samīhate hātum

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parimala]]---with the scent; [[synonyms:v:vāsita]]---perfumed; [[synonyms:b:bhuvanam]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:u:unmādita]]---inspired by their own mellows; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:j:jña]]---devotees; [[synonyms:r:rolambam]]---like bumblebees; [[synonyms:g:giridhara]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:ambhojam]]---the lotus feet of Lord Giridhari; [[synonyms:k:kaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:k:khalu]]---certainly; [[synonyms:r:rasikaḥ]]---a realized soul; [[synonyms:s:samīhate]] [[synonyms:h:hātum]]---endeavors to give up.

===== Translation =====

**Realized devotees are like bumblebees maddened by their own mellows at Kṛṣṇa's lotus feet. The scent of those lotus feet perfumes the entire world. Who is the realized soul that could give them up?**
