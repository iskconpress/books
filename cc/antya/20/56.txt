====== CC Antya 20.56 ======

===== Text =====

> ye gopī mora kare dveṣe, kṛṣṇera kare santoṣe,
> kṛṣṇa yāre kare abhilāṣa
> mui tāra ghare yāñā, tāre sevoṅ dāsī hañā,
> tabe mora sukhera ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:g:gopī]]---any //gopī// who; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:d:dveṣe]]---shows envy; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:s:santoṣe]]---but satisfies Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---unto Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:y:yāre]]---unto whom; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:a:abhilāṣa]]---desiring; [[synonyms:m:mui]]---I; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---her; [[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going to the house; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:s:sevoṅ]]---shall render service unto her; [[synonyms:d:dāsī]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming a maidservant; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:s:sukhera]] [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---awakening of happiness.

===== Translation =====

**"If a gopī envious of Me satisfies Kṛṣṇa and Kṛṣṇa desires her, I shall not hesitate to go to her house and become her maidservant, for then My happiness will be awakened.**
