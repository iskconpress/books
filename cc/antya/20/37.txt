====== CC Antya 20.37 ======

===== Text =====

> "prema-dhana vinā vyartha daridra jīvana
> 'dāsa' kari' vetana more deha prema-dhana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the wealth of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]---without; [[synonyms:v:vyartha]]---useless; [[synonyms:d:daridra]] [[synonyms:j:jīvana]]---poor life; [[synonyms:d:dāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting as Your eternal servant; [[synonyms:v:vetana]]---salary; [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:d:deha]]---give; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the treasure of love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**"Without love of Godhead, My life is useless. Therefore I pray that You accept Me as Your servant and give Me the salary of ecstatic love of God."**
