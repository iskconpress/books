====== CC Antya 20.134 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭādaśa paricchede---samudre patana
> kṛṣṇa-gopī-jala-keli tāhāṅ daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭādaśa]] [[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Eighteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:s:samudre]] [[synonyms:p:patana]]---the Lord's falling into the ocean; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gopī]]-[[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:k:keli]]---the water pastimes of Kṛṣṇa and the //gopīs//; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---seeing there.

===== Translation =====

**In the Eighteenth Chapter, the Lord fell into the ocean, and in ecstasy He saw the pastimes of the water fight between Kṛṣṇa and the gopīs.**
