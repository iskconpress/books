====== CC Antya 20.127 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye prabhura pañcendriya-ākarṣaṇa
> tāra madhye karilā rāse kṛṣṇa-anveṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pañca]]-[[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākarṣaṇa]]---the attraction of the five senses; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---within that chapter; [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---did; [[synonyms:r:rāse]]---in the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:anveṣaṇa]]---searching for Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Also in that chapter is a description of the attraction of Lord Caitanya's five senses to Kṛṣṇa and how He searched for Kṛṣṇa in the rāsa dance.**
