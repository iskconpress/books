====== Cc. Antya 20.58 ======

===== Text =====

> "kṛṣṇa---mora jīvana, kṛṣṇa---mora prāṇa-dhana,
> kṛṣṇa---mora prāṇera parāṇa
> hṛdaya-upare dharoṅ, sevā kari' sukhī karoṅ,
> ei mora sadā rahe dhyāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:j:jīvana]]---My life and soul; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the wealth of My life; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:p:prāṇera]] [[synonyms:p:parāna]]---the life of My life; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]-[[synonyms:u:upare]]---on My heart; [[synonyms:d:dharoṅ]]---I hold; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---serving; [[synonyms:s:sukhī]] [[synonyms:k:karoṅ]]---I make happy; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains; [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]]---meditation.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa is My life and soul. Kṛṣṇa is the treasure of My life. Indeed, Kṛṣṇa is the very life of My life. I therefore keep Him always in My heart and try to please Him by rendering service. That is My constant meditation.**
