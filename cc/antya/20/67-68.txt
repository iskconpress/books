====== CC Antya 20.67-68 ======

===== Text =====

> yei yei śloka jayadeva, bhāgavate
> rāyera nāṭake, yei āra karṇāmṛte
>
> sei sei bhāve śloka kariyā paṭhane
> sei sei bhāvāveśe karena āsvādane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:j:jayadeva]]---Jayadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavate]]---in //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:r:rāyera]] [[synonyms:n:nāṭake]]---in the drama made by Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---also; [[synonyms:k:karṇāmṛte]]---in the book named //Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta,// written by Bilvamaṅgala Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in those ecstatic emotions; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:p:paṭhane]]---reading regularly; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sei]]---in that particular; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ā:āsvādane]]---He tastes.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu read the verses of Jayadeva's Gīta-govinda, of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, of Rāmānanda Rāya's drama Jagannātha-vallabha-nāṭaka and of Bilvamaṅgala Ṭhākura's Kṛṣṇa-karṇāmṛta, He was overwhelmed by the various ecstatic emotions of those verses. Thus He tasted their purports.**
