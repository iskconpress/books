====== Cc. Antya 20.32 ======

===== Text =====

> ayi nanda-tanuja kiṅkaraṁ
> patitaṁ māṁ viṣame bhavāmbudhau
> kṛpayā tava pāda-paṅkaja-
> sthita-dhūlī-sadṛśaṁ vicintaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ayi]]---oh, My Lord; [[synonyms:n:nanda]]-[[synonyms:t:tanuja]]---the son of Nanda Mahārāja, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kiṅkaram]]---the servant; [[synonyms:p:patitam]]---fallen; [[synonyms:m:mām]]---Me; [[synonyms:v:viṣame]]---horrible; [[synonyms:b:bhava]]-[[synonyms:a:ambudhau]]---in the ocean of nescience; [[synonyms:k:kṛpayā]]---by causeless mercy; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---Your; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:paṅkaja]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:s:sthita]]---situated at; [[synonyms:d:dhūlī]]-[[synonyms:s:sadṛśam]]---like a particle of dust; [[synonyms:v:vicintaya]]---kindly consider.

===== Translation =====

**"Oh, My Lord, O Kṛṣṇa, son of Mahārāja Nanda, I am Your eternal servant, but because of My own fruitive acts, I have fallen in this horrible ocean of nescience. Now please be causelessly merciful to Me. Consider Me a particle of dust at Your lotus feet.**
