====== CC Antya 20.34 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛpā kari' kara more pada-dhūli-sama
> tomāra sevaka karoṅ tomāra sevana"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---being merciful; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---make; [[synonyms:m:more]]---Me; [[synonyms:p:pada]]-[[synonyms:d:dhūli]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like a particle of dust at Your lotus feet; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---as I am Your eternal servant; [[synonyms:k:karoṅ]]---let Me be engaged; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---in Your service.

===== Translation =====

**"Be causelessly merciful to Me by giving Me a place with the particles of dust at Your lotus feet so that I may engage in the service of Your Lordship as Your eternal servant."**
