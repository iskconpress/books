====== CC Antya 20.24 ======

===== Text =====

> yei ye māgaye, tāre deya āpana-dhana
> gharma-vṛṣṭi sahe, ānera karaye rakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:m:māgaye]]---if anyone asks anything from the tree; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---gives; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---its own wealth; [[synonyms:g:gharma]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛṣṭi]]---the scorching heat of the sun and torrents of rain; [[synonyms:s:sahe]]---tolerates; [[synonyms:ā:ānera]]---to others; [[synonyms:k:karaye]] [[synonyms:r:rakṣaṇa]]---gives protection.

===== Translation =====

**"The tree delivers its fruits, flowers and whatever it possesses to anyone and everyone. It tolerates scorching heat and torrents of rain, yet it still gives shelter to others.**
