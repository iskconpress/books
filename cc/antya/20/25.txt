====== Cc. Antya 20.25 ======

===== Text =====

> uttama hañā vaiṣṇava habe nirabhimāna
> jīve sammāna dibe jāni' 'kṛṣṇa'-adhiṣṭhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttama]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---although being very exalted; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---a devotee; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---should become; [[synonyms:n:nirabhimāna]]---without pride; [[synonyms:j:jīve]]---to all living entities; [[synonyms:s:sammāna]] [[synonyms:d:dibe]]---should give respect; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:adhiṣṭhāna]]---the residing place of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Although a Vaiṣṇava is the most exalted person, he is prideless and gives all respect to everyone, knowing everyone to be the resting place of Kṛṣṇa.**
