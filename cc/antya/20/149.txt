====== Cc. Antya 20.149 ======

===== Text =====

> anipuṇā vāṇī āpane nācite nā jāne
> yata nācāilā, nāci' karilā viśrāme

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anipuṇā]] [[synonyms:v:vāṇī]]---inexperienced words; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---by themselves; [[synonyms:n:nācite]]---to dance; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---know how; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---whatever; [[synonyms:n:nācāilā]]---caused to dance; [[synonyms:n:nāci']]---after dancing; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:v:viśrāme]]---took rest.

===== Translation =====

**My inexperienced words do not know how to dance by themselves. The mercy of the guru made them dance as much as possible, and now, after dancing, they have taken rest.**
