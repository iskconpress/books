====== Cc. Antya 20.70 ======

===== Text =====

> sei saba līlā-rasa āpane ananta
> sahasra-vadane varṇi' nāhi pā'na anta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all these; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]-[[synonyms:r:rasa]]---transcendental mellows of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:a:ananta]]---the Personality of Godhead Ananta; [[synonyms:s:sahasra]]-[[synonyms:v:vadane]]---with His thousands of faces; [[synonyms:v:varṇi']]---describing; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]]---not; [[synonyms:p:pā'na]]---gets; [[synonyms:a:anta]]---the limit.

===== Translation =====

**Even Anantadeva, who has thousands of faces, could not reach the end of describing the transcendental bliss of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes.**
