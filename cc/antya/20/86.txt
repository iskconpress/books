====== Cc. Antya 20.86 ======

===== Text =====

> saṅkṣepe kahiluṅ, vistāra nā yāya kathane
> vistāriyā veda-vyāsa kariba varṇane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saṅkṣepe]] [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described very briefly; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:k:kathane]]---it is not possible to describe them in full; [[synonyms:v:vistāriyā]]---elaborating; [[synonyms:v:veda]]-[[synonyms:v:vyāsa]]---a representative of Vyāsadeva; [[synonyms:k:kariba]]---will do; [[synonyms:v:varṇane]]---describing.

===== Translation =====

**I have described the pastimes very briefly, for it is impossible for me to describe them in full. In the future, however, Vedavyāsa will describe them elaborately.**
