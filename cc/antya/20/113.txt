====== CC Antya 20.113 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara-svarūpa-ṭhāñi tāṅre samarpila
> 'govardhana-śilā', 'guñjā-mālā' tāṅre dila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]]-[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to the care of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:s:samarpila]]---the Lord entrusted him; [[synonyms:g:govardhana]]-[[synonyms:ś:śilā]]---the stone from Govardhana Hill; [[synonyms:g:guñjā]]-[[synonyms:m:mālā]]---the garland of small conchshells; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:d:dila]]---delivered to him.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord entrusted Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī to the care of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and gave Raghunātha dāsa the gift of a stone from Govardhana Hill and a garland of small conchshells.**
