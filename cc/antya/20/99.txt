====== CC Antya 20.99 ======

===== Text =====

> śrī-madana-gopāla more lekhāya ājñā kari'
> kahite nā yuyāya, tabu rahite nā pāri

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:m:madana]]-[[synonyms:g:gopāla]]---the Madana-mohana Deity of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:m:more]]---me; [[synonyms:l:lekhāya]]---causes to write; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---by giving the order; [[synonyms:k:kahite]]---to say; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---is not befitting; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:r:rahite]]---to remain silent; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am not able.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Madana-mohana Deity of Vṛndāvana has given the order that is making me write. Although this should not be disclosed, I disclose it because I am unable to remain silent.**
