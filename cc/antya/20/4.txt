====== CC Antya 20.4 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa, rāmānanda,---ei duijana-sane
> rātri-dine rasa-gīta-śloka āsvādane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with two persons; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---night and day; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verses and songs containing the mellows of transcendental bliss; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādane]]---in tasting.

===== Translation =====

**Day and night He tasted transcendental blissful songs and verses with two associates, namely Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya.**
