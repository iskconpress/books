====== CC Antya 20.122 ======

===== Text =====

> raghunātha-bhaṭṭācāryera tāhāṅi milana
> prabhu tāṅre kṛpā kari' pāṭhāilā vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācāryera]]---of Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]]---there; [[synonyms:m:milana]]---meeting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---showing causeless mercy; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---sent to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**Also in the Thirteenth Chapter, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who by His causeless mercy sent him to Vṛndāvana.**
