====== CC Antya 20.7 ======

===== Text =====

> kona dine kona bhāve śloka-paṭhana
> sei śloka āsvādite rātri-jāgaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:k:kona]] [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in some emotion; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]-[[synonyms:p:paṭhana]]---reciting the verses; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---those verses; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādite]]---to taste; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:j:jāgaraṇa]]---keeping awake at night.

===== Translation =====

**Sometimes the Lord would be absorbed in a particular emotion and would stay awake all night reciting related verses and relishing their taste.**
