====== Cc. Antya 20.138 ======

===== Text =====

> viṁśa-paricchede---nija-'śikṣāṣṭaka' paḍiyā
> tāra artha āsvādilā premāviṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṁśa]]-[[synonyms:p:paricchede]]---in the Twentieth Chapter; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:ś:śikṣāṣṭaka]] [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---reciting His own //Śikṣāṣṭaka// verses; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---their meaning; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādilā]]---tasted; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being absorbed in ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**In the Twentieth Chapter, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited His own eight stanzas of instruction and tasted their meaning in ecstatic love.**
