====== Cc. Antya 20.22 ======

===== Text =====

> uttama hañā āpanāke māne tṛṇādhama
> dui-prakāre sahiṣṇutā kare vṛkṣa-sama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:uttama]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---although being very exalted; [[synonyms:ā:āpanāke]]---himself; [[synonyms:m:māne]]---thinks; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:adhama]]---lower than a blade of the grass on the ground; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāre]]---in two ways; [[synonyms:s:sahiṣṇutā]]---tolerance; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like the tree.

===== Translation =====

**"These are the symptoms of one who chants the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra. Although he is very exalted, he thinks himself lower than the grass on the ground, and like a tree, he tolerates everything in two ways.**
