====== Cc. Antya 20.108 ======

===== Text =====

> caturthe---śrī-sanātanera dvitīya-milana
> deha-tyāga haite tāṅra karilā rakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturthe]]---in the Fourth Chapter; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dvitīya]]-[[synonyms:m:milana]]---visiting for the second time; [[synonyms:d:deha]]-[[synonyms:t:tyāga]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from committing suicide; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:r:rakṣaṇa]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu protected him.

===== Translation =====

**The Fourth Chapter describes Sanātana Gosvāmī's second visit with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and how the Lord saved him from committing suicide.**
