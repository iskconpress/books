====== CC Antya 20.80 ======

===== Text =====

> aiche mahāprabhura līlā---nāhi ora-pāra
> 'jīva' hañā kebā samyak pāre varṇibāra?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:o:ora]]-[[synonyms:p:pāra]]---there is no limit above or below; [[synonyms:j:jīva]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being an ordinary living entity; [[synonyms:k:kebā]]---who; [[synonyms:s:samyak]]---fully; [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---is able; [[synonyms:v:varṇibāra]]---to describe.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are like the unlimited sky. How, then, can an ordinary living being describe them all?**
