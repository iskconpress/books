====== CC Antya 20.151 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-caritāmṛta yei jana śune
> tāṅra caraṇa dhuñā karoṅ muñi pāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritāmṛta]]---the description of Lord Śrī Caitanya's pastimes; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:j:jana]] [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---anyone who hears; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---his lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhuñā]]---washing; [[synonyms:k:karoṅ]]---do; [[synonyms:m:muñi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:pāne]]---drinking.

===== Translation =====

**If one hears the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as described in Śrī Caitanya-caritāmṛta, I wash his lotus feet and drink the water.**
