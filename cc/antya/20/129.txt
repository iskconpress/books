====== CC Antya 20.129 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānandera bālake śloka karāilā
> siṁha-dvāre dvārī prabhure kṛṣṇa dekhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:b:bālake]]---the son; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]] [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---made to compose a verse; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the Siṁha-dvāra gate of the Jagannātha temple; [[synonyms:d:dvārī]]---the doorkeeper; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇadekhāilā]]---showed Lord Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**It also describes how Śivānanda's son composed a verse and how the doorkeeper of the Siṁha-dvāra showed Kṛṣṇa to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
