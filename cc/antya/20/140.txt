====== Cc. Antya 20.140 ======

===== Text =====

> mukhya-mukhya-līlāra artha kariluṅ kathana
> 'anuvāda' haite smare grantha-vivaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the chief pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]]---have done; [[synonyms:k:kathana]]---describing; [[synonyms:a:anuvāda]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---by repeating them again; [[synonyms:s:smare]]---one remembers; [[synonyms:g:grantha]]-[[synonyms:v:vivaraṇa]]---the description in the book.

===== Translation =====

**I have thus described the principal pastimes and their meaning, for by such repetition one can remember the descriptions in the book.**
