====== CC Antya 20.117 ======

===== Text =====

> daśame---kahiluṅ bhakta-datta-āsvādana
> rāghava-paṇḍitera tāhāṅ jhālira sājana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daśame]]---in the Tenth Chapter; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:d:datta]]-[[synonyms:ā:āsvādana]]---the tasting of the food given by the devotees; [[synonyms:r:rāghava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Rāghava Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---therein; [[synonyms:j:jhālira]] [[synonyms:s:sājana]]---the assortment in the bags.

===== Translation =====

**In the Tenth Chapter I have described how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tasted the food given by His devotees, and I have also described the assortments in the bags of Rāghava Paṇḍita.**
