====== CC Antya 20.141 ======

===== Text =====

> eka eka paricchedera kathā---aneka-prakāra
> mukhya-mukhya kahiluṅ, kathā nā yāya vistāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:p:paricchedera]]---of every chapter; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---description; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---different varieties; [[synonyms:m:mukhya]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhya]] [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have repeated only the principal ones; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---discussions; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---not possible; [[synonyms:v:vistāra]]---expansion.

===== Translation =====

**In every chapter there are various topics, but I have selected only those that are principal, for not all of them could be described again.**
