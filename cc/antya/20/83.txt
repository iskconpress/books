====== CC Antya 20.83 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra āge yadyapi saba līlāra bhāṇḍāra
> tathāpi alpa varṇiyā chāḍilena āra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before him; [[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:l:līlāra]]---of the pastimes; [[synonyms:b:bhāṇḍāra]]---full store; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:a:alpa]]---very little; [[synonyms:v:varṇiyā]]---describing; [[synonyms:c:chāḍilena]]---he left; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---the others.

===== Translation =====

**Although Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura has within his jurisdiction the full store of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes, he has left aside most of them and described but a small portion.**
