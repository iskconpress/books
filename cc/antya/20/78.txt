====== CC Antya 20.78 ======

===== Text =====

> saba śrotā vaiṣṇavera vandiyā caraṇa
> caitanya-caritra-varṇana kailuṅ samāpana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:ś:śrotā]]---all readers; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:v:vandiyā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---offering respectful obeisances unto the lotus feet; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---of the characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---I have done; [[synonyms:s:samāpana]]---finishing.

===== Translation =====

**After offering my respectful obeisances to the lotus feet of all my Vaiṣṇava readers, I shall therefore end this description of the characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
