====== CC Antya 20.150 ======

===== Text =====

> saba śrotā-gaṇera kari caraṇa vandana
> yāṅ-sabāra caraṇa-kṛpā---śubhera kāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śrotā]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the readers; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I do; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---worshiping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:y:yāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of whom; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy of the lotus feet; [[synonyms:ś:śubhera]] [[synonyms:k:kāraṇa]]---the cause of all good fortune.

===== Translation =====

**I now worship the lotus feet of all my readers, for by the mercy of their lotus feet there is all good fortune.**
