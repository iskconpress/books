====== CC Antya 20.119 ======

===== Text =====

> ekādaśe---haridāsa-ṭhākurera niryāṇa
> bhakta-vātsalya yāhāṅ dekhāilā gaura bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekādaśe]]---in the Eleventh Chapter; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākurera]] [[synonyms:n:niryāṇa]]---the disappearance of Śrīla Haridāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:v:vātsalya]]---affection for the devotee; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---wherein; [[synonyms:d:dekhāilā]]---exhibited; [[synonyms:g:gaura]] [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**The Eleventh Chapter describes the disappearance of Haridāsa Ṭhākura and how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, showed His affection for His devotees.**
