====== CC Antya 20.123 ======

===== Text =====

> caturdaśe---divyonmāda-ārambha varṇana
> 'śarīra' ethā prabhura, 'mana' gelā vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caturdaśe]]---in the Fourteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārambha]]---the beginning of the spiritual trance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---describing; [[synonyms:ś:śarīra]]---the body; [[synonyms:e:ethā]]---here; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**The Fourteenth Chapter describes the beginning of the Lord's spiritual trance, in which His body was at Jagannātha Purī but His mind was in Vṛndāvana.**
