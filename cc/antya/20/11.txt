====== CC Antya 20.11 ======

===== Text =====

> nāma-saṅkīrtana haite sarvānartha-nāśa
> sarva-śubhodaya, kṛṣṇa-premera ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---chanting of the holy names of the Lord; [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:anartha]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destruction of all undesirable things; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:ś:śubha]]-[[synonyms:u:udaya]]---awakening of all good fortune; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---the beginning of the flow of love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**"Simply by chanting the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa, one can be freed from all undesirable habits. This is the means of awakening all good fortune and initiating the flow of waves of love for Kṛṣṇa.**
