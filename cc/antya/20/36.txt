====== CC Antya 20.36 ======

===== Text =====

> nayanaṁ galad-aśru-dhārayā
> vadanaṁ gadgada-ruddhayā girā
> pulakair nicitaṁ vapuḥ kadā,
> tava nāma-grahaṇe bhaviṣyati

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nayanam]]---the eyes; [[synonyms:g:galat]]-[[synonyms:a:aśru]]-[[synonyms:d:dhārayā]]---by streams of tears running down; [[synonyms:v:vadanam]]---mouth; [[synonyms:g:gadgada]]---faltering; [[synonyms:r:ruddhayā]]---choked up; [[synonyms:g:girā]]---with words; [[synonyms:p:pulakaiḥ]]---with erection of the hairs due to transcendental happiness; [[synonyms:n:nicitam]]---covered; [[synonyms:v:vapuḥ]]---the body; [[synonyms:k:kadā]]---when; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---Your; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:g:grahaṇe]]---in chanting the name; [[synonyms:b:bhaviṣyati]]---will be.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Lord, when will My eyes be beautified by filling with tears that constantly glide down as I chant Your holy name? When will My voice falter and all the hairs on My body stand erect in transcendental happiness as I chant Your holy name?**
