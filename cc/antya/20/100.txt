====== Cc. Antya 20.100 ======

===== Text =====

> nā kahile haya mora kṛta-ghnatā-doṣa
> dambha kari bali' śrotā, nā kariha roṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kahile]]---if I do not say; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:g:ghnatā]]-[[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault of ingratitude; [[synonyms:d:dambha]] [[synonyms:k:kari]]---I am proud; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---taking as; [[synonyms:ś:śrotā]]---O readers; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:r:roṣa]]---do not be angry.

===== Translation =====

**If I did not disclose this fact, I would be guilty of ingratitude to the Lord. Therefore, my dear readers, please do not consider me too proud and be angry at me.**
