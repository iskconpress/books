====== CC Antya 20.136 ======

===== Text =====

> ūnaviṁśe---bhittye prabhura mukha-saṅgharṣaṇa
> kṛṣṇera viraha-sphūrti-pralāpa-varṇana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ū:ūnaviṁśe]]---in the Nineteenth Chapter; [[synonyms:b:bhittye]]---on the walls; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅgharṣaṇa]]---the rubbing of the face of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:v:viraha]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrti]]---the awakening of separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]-[[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---and talking like a crazy person.

===== Translation =====

**In the Nineteenth Chapter is a description of how Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu rubbed His face against the walls and spoke like a madman because of separation from Kṛṣṇa.**
