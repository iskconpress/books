====== CC Antya 20.75 ======

===== Text =====

> ataeva sei-saba līlā nā pāri varṇibāre
> samāpti kariluṅ līlāke kari' namaskāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all those; [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I am not able; [[synonyms:v:varṇibāre]]---to narrate; [[synonyms:s:samāpti]] [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]]---now I have finished; [[synonyms:l:līlāke]]---to the pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:n:namaskāre]]---offering my respectful obeisances.

===== Translation =====

**It is impossible to describe all the pastimes elaborately. I shall therefore end this description and offer them my respectful obeisances.**
