====== CC Antya 20.135 ======

===== Text =====

> tāhāṅi dekhilā kṛṣṇera vanya-bhojana
> jāliyā uṭhāila, prabhu āilā sva-bhavana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]]---there; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---He saw; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vanya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---a picnic in the forest; [[synonyms:j:jāliyā]]---a fisherman; [[synonyms:u:uṭhāila]]---caught Him; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---to His own residence.

===== Translation =====

**In that dream, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw Kṛṣṇa's picnic in the forest. As Lord Caitanya floated in the sea, a fisherman caught Him, and then the Lord returned to His own residence.**
