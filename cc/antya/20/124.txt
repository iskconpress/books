====== CC Antya 20.124 ======

===== Text =====

> tāra madhye prabhura siṁha-dvāre patana
> asthi-sandhi-tyāga, anubhāvera udgama

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in that chapter; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:siṁha]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]] [[synonyms:p:patana]]---falling down by the Siṁha-dvāra gate; [[synonyms:a:asthi]]-[[synonyms:s:sandhi]]---of the joints of the bones; [[synonyms:t:tyāga]]---release; [[synonyms:a:anubhāvera]] [[synonyms:u:udgama]]---the awakening of a trance and emotion.

===== Translation =====

**Also in that chapter is a description of how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell down in front of the Siṁha-dvāra gate of Jagannātha temple, His bones separated at the joints, and how various transcendental symptoms awakened.**
