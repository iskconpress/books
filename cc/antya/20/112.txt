====== CC Antya 20.112 ======

===== Text =====

> ṣaṣṭhe---raghunātha-dāsa prabhure mililā
> nityānanda-ājñāya ciḍā-mahotsava kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ṣ:ṣaṣṭhe]]---in the Sixth Chapter; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---by the order of Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:c:ciḍā]]-[[synonyms:m:mahotsava]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed the festival of chipped rice.

===== Translation =====

**The Sixth Chapter describes how Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and performed the chipped rice festival in accordance with Nityānanda Prabhu's order.**
