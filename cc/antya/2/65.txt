====== Cc. Antya 2.65 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛsiṁhera haila jāni āji upavāsa
> ṭhākura upavāsī rahe, jiye kaiche dāsa?'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhera]]---of Lord Nṛsiṁha; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---I understand; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:u:upavāsa]]---fasting; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhākura]] [[synonyms:u:upavāsī]] [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---the master remains fasting; [[synonyms:j:jiye]] [[synonyms:k:kaiche]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---how can the servant sustain his life.

===== Translation =====

**"I think that Nṛsiṁhadeva could not eat anything today, and therefore He is fasting. If the master fasts, how can the servant live?"**
