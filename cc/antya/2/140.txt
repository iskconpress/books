====== CC Antya 2.140 ======

===== Text =====

> tumi haṭha kaile tāṅra haṭha se bāḍibe
> snāna bhojana kara, āpane krodha yābe"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tumi]] [[synonyms:h:haṭha]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---if you go on persisting; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:h:haṭha]]---persistence; [[synonyms:s:se]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bāḍibe]]---will increase; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---take your bath and take //prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]] [[synonyms:k:krodha]] [[synonyms:y:yābe]]---automatically His anger will subside.

===== Translation =====

**"The Lord is persisting, and if you also persist, His persistence will increase. It is better for you to bathe and take prasāda. In due course, His anger will automatically subside."**
