====== CC Antya 2.52 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅhāra prabhāva-prema jāne dui-jane
> ānibe prabhure ebe niścaya kailā mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---his; [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---influence; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---know; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---both of them; [[synonyms:ā:ānibe]] [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---he will bring Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---they were greatly assured within their minds.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda and Jagadānanda knew of Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī's influence and love of Godhead. Therefore they now felt assured that he would certainly bring Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
