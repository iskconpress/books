====== CC Antya 2.128 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina sabe paramānanda-purī-sthāne
> 'prabhuke prasanna kara'---kailā nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---at the place of Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---please try to pacify; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---submitted.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, all the devotees went to Śrī Paramānanda Purī and requested him to pacify the Lord.**
