====== CC Antya 2.93 ======

===== Text =====

> sabe meli' āisa, śuni 'bhāṣya' ihāra sthāne'
> prema-krodha kari' svarūpa balaya vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:m:meli']]---all together; [[synonyms:ā:āisa]]---come; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---let us hear; [[synonyms:b:bhāṣya]]---the commentary; [[synonyms:i:ihāra]] [[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---from him; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:k:krodha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in an angry mood of love; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:b:balaya]] [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---said these words.

===== Translation =====

**Bhagavān Ācārya requested Svarūpa Dāmodara to hear from Gopāla the commentary upon Vedānta. Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, however, somewhat angry because of love, spoke as follows.**
