====== CC Antya 2.83 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' kahilu gaurera 'āvirbhāva'
> ihā yei śune, jāne caitanya-prabhāva

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahilu]]---I have described; [[synonyms:g:gaurera]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āvirbhāva]]---appearance; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this incident; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---whoever hears; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---the opulence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described the appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Anyone who hears about these incidents can understand the transcendental opulence of the Lord.**
