====== Cc. Antya 2.160 ======

===== Text =====

> prayāga ha-ite eka vaiṣṇava navadvīpa āila
> haridāsera vārtā teṅho sabāre kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prayāga]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---from Prayāga; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---devotee of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:navadvīpa]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came to Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]] [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---the news of Haridāsa; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:s:sabāre]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---informed everyone.

===== Translation =====

**A devotee returned to Navadvīpa from Prayāga and told everyone the details of Junior Haridāsa's suicide.**
