====== CC Antya 2.76 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina sabhāte prabhu vāta cālāilā
> nṛsiṁhānandera guṇa kahite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:s:sabhāte]]---in the presence of all the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vāta]] [[synonyms:c:cālāilā]]---raised the topic (of eating at Nṛsiṁhānanda's house); [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhānandera]]---of Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---transcendental qualities; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to speak.

===== Translation =====

**One day, in the presence of all the devotees, the Lord raised these topics concerning Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī and praised his transcendental qualities.**
