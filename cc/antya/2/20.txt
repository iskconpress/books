====== CC Antya 2.20 ======

===== Text =====

> taiche gaura-kānti, taiche sadā premāveśa
> tāhā dekhibāre āise sarva gauḍa-deśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:taiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:k:kānti]]---a bodily luster like that of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---similarly; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---absorbed in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see that; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---come; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśa]]---people from all provinces of Bengal.

===== Translation =====

**His body shone with the same luster as that of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and he showed the same absorption in ecstatic love of Godhead. People came from all provinces of Bengal to see these symptoms.**
