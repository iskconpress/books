====== CC Antya 2.139 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu haṭhe paḍiyāche svatantra īśvara
> kabhu kṛpā karibena yāte dayālu antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haṭhe]] [[synonyms:p:paḍiyāche]]---has taken up a persistently angry mood; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the independent Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---some time; [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:karibena]]---He will be merciful (to you); [[synonyms:y:yāte]]---because; [[synonyms:d:dayālu]]---merciful; [[synonyms:a:antara]]---at heart.

===== Translation =====

**"At present Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is persisting in His mood of anger because He is the independent Supreme Personality of Godhead. At some time, however, He will surely be merciful, for at heart He is very kind.**
