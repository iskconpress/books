====== Cc. Antya 2.54 ======

===== Text =====

> dui dina dhyāna kari' śivānandere kahila
> "pāṇihāṭi grāme āmi prabhure ānila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---for two days; [[synonyms:d:dhyāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after meditating; [[synonyms:ś:śivānandere]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---he said to Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:p:pāṇihāṭi]] [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---to the village called Pāṇihāṭi; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---have brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**After meditating for two days, Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī told Śivānanda Sena, "I have already brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to the village known as Pāṇihāṭi.**
