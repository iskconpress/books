====== CC Antya 2.101 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina ācārya prabhure kailā nimantraṇa
> ghare bhāta kari' kare vividha vyañjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---made an invitation for dinner; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:b:bhāta]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---cooking rice; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---prepares; [[synonyms:v:vividha]] [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---varieties of vegetable preparations.

===== Translation =====

**One day Bhagavān Ācārya invited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to dine at his home. Thus he was preparing rice and various types of vegetables.**
