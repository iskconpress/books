====== CC Antya 2.7 ======

===== Text =====

> sākṣāt-darśane saba jagat tārilā
> eka-bāra ye dekhilā, se kṛtārtha ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]-[[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by direct meetings; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the universe; [[synonyms:t:tārilā]]---He delivered; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:b:bāra]]---once; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---anyone who; [[synonyms:d:dekhilā]]---saw; [[synonyms:s:se]]---he; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---fully satisfied; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was personally present, anyone in the world who met Him even once was fully satisfied and became spiritually advanced.**
