====== Cc. Antya 2.96 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-bhāgavata yei, kṛṣṇa prāṇa-dhana yāra
> māyāvāda-śravaṇe citta avaśya phire tāṅra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]] [[synonyms:y:yei]]---one who is a highly elevated devotee; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]] [[synonyms:y:yāra]]---whose life and soul; [[synonyms:m:māyāvāda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing the Māyāvāda philosophy; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---the heart; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:p:phire]]---changes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his.

===== Translation =====

**"The Māyāvāda philosophy presents such a jugglery of words that even a highly elevated devotee who has accepted Kṛṣṇa as his life and soul changes his decision when he reads the Māyāvāda commentary on Vedānta-sūtra."**
