====== Cc. Antya 2.29 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni, śivānanda sena tāṅhā śīghra āila
> namaskāra kari' tāṅra nikaṭe vasila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:s:sena]]---of the name Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]]---there; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---quickly; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near him; [[synonyms:v:vasila]]---sat down.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing these calls, Śivānanda Sena quickly went there, offered obeisances to Nakula Brahmacārī, and sat down near him.**
