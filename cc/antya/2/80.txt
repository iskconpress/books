====== CC Antya 2.80 ======

===== Text =====

> nityānandera nṛtya dekhena āsi' bāre bāre
> 'nirantara āvirbhāva' rāghavera ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nityānandera]] [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---the dancing of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhena]]---He sees; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:b:bāre]] [[synonyms:b:bāre]]---again and again; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]] [[synonyms:ā:āvirbhāva]]---constant appearance; [[synonyms:r:rāghavera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in the house of Rāghava.

===== Translation =====

**Similarly, He was always present when Nityānanda Prabhu danced, and He regularly appeared at the house of Rāghava.**
