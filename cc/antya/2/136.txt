====== CC Antya 2.136 ======

===== Text =====

> loka-hita lāgi' tomāra saba vyavahāra
> āmi saba nā jāni gambhīra hṛdaya tomāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:h:hita]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for the benefit of people in general; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---activities; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of us; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---cannot understand; [[synonyms:g:gambhīra]]---very deep and grave; [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---heart; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---Your.

===== Translation =====

**"All Your activities are for the benefit of people in general. We cannot understand them, for Your intentions are deep and grave."**
