====== CC Antya 2.146 ======

===== Text =====

> rātri avaśeṣe prabhure daṇḍavat hañā
> prayāgete gela kāreha kichu nā baliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātri]] [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣe]]---at the end of one night; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prayāgete]]---to the holy place known as Prayāga (Allahabad); [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:k:kāreha]]---to anyone; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:b:baliyā]]---not saying.

===== Translation =====

**Thus at the end of one night, Junior Haridāsa, after offering Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu his respectful obeisances, departed for Prayāga without saying anything to anyone.**
