====== CC Antya 2.33 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhura acintya prabhāva
> ebe śuna prabhura yaiche haya 'āvirbhāva'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:acintya]] [[synonyms:p:prabhāva]]---inconceivable influence; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---in which way; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:ā:āvirbhāva]]---appearance.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, one should understand the inconceivable potencies of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Now please hear how His appearance [āvirbhāva] takes place.**
