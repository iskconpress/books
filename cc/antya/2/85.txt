====== CC Antya 2.85 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhya-bhāvākrānta-citta, gopa-avatāra
> svarūpa-gosāñi-saha sakhya-vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---by fraternal love; [[synonyms:ā:ākrānta]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:c:citta]]---heart; [[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---an incarnation of one of the cowherd boys; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:s:sakhya]]-[[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---dealings just like those of a friend.

===== Translation =====

**He was fully absorbed in thoughts of fraternal relationships with God. He was an incarnation of a cowherd boy, and thus his dealings with Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī were very friendly.**
