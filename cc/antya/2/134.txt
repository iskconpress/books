====== CC Antya 2.134 ======

===== Text =====

> āste-vyaste purī-gosāñi prabhu āge gelā
> anunaya kari' prabhure ghare vasāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āste]]-[[synonyms:v:vyaste]]---with great haste; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:a:anunaya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great humility; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---in His room; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---got to sit down.

===== Translation =====

**In great haste Paramānanda Purī Gosāñi went before Him and with great humility persuaded Him to sit down in His own room.**
