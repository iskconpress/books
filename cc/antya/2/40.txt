====== CC Antya 2.40 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe prabhu tāṅre ājñā kailā gauḍe yāite
> "bhakta-gaṇe niṣedhiha ethāke āsite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---ordered; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---returning to Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:n:niṣedhiha]]---forbid; [[synonyms:e:ethāke]] [[synonyms:ā:āsite]]---to come to this place.

===== Translation =====

**When he was about to return to Bengal, the Lord told him, "Forbid the devotees of Bengal to come to Jagannātha Purī this year.**
