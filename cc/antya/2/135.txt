====== CC Antya 2.135 ======

===== Text =====

> "tomāra ye icchā, kara, svatantra īśvara
> kebā ki balite pāre tomāra upara?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---whatever is Your desire; [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You can do; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]] [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the independent Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kebā]]---who; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:b:balite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can speak; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:u:upara]]---above You.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī said, "My dear Lord Caitanya, You are the independent Personality of Godhead. You can do whatever You like. Who can say anything above You?**
