====== CC Antya 2.145 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate haridāsera eka vatsara gela
> tabu mahāprabhura mane prasāda nahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---one year; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---passed; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:n:nahila]]---there were no symptoms of mercy.

===== Translation =====

**In this way a complete year passed for Junior Haridāsa, but still there was not a sign of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mercy toward him.**
