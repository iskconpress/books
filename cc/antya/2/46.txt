====== CC Antya 2.46 ======

===== Text =====

> pauṣa-māse āila duṅhe sāmagrī kariyā
> sandhyā-paryanta rahe apekṣā kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pauṣa]]-[[synonyms:m:māse]]---the month of Pauṣa (December-January); [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---Śivānanda Sena and Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:s:sāmagrī]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---making all arrangements; [[synonyms:s:sandhyā]]-[[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---until the evening; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remain; [[synonyms:a:apekṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---waiting.

===== Translation =====

**When the month of Pauṣa arrived, both Jagadānanda and Śivānanda collected all kinds of paraphernalia for the Lord's reception. Every day, they would wait until evening for the Lord to come.**
