====== CC Antya 2.155 ======

===== Text =====

> manuṣya nā dekhe---madhura gīta-mātra śune
> govindādi sabe meli' kaila anumāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:manuṣya]]---a man; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---could not see; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---very sweet; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]---singing; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---one could hear; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees, headed by Govinda; [[synonyms:m:meli']]---coming together; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:a:anumāne]]---guessed.

===== Translation =====

**No one could see him, but they could hear him singing in a sweet voice. Therefore all the devotees, headed by Govinda, made this guess.**
