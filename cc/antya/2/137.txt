====== Cc. Antya 2.137 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' purī-gosāñi gelā nija-sthāne
> haridāsa-sthāne gelā saba bhakta-gaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Paramānanda Gosāñi; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---left; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---for his own place; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---unto the place of Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the other devotees.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Paramānanda Purī Gosāñi left for his own home. Then all the devotees went to see Junior Haridāsa.**
