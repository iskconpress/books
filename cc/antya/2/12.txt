====== CC Antya 2.12 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata darśane trijagat nistāri
> ye keha āsite nāre aneka saṁsārī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by direct visits; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the three worlds; [[synonyms:n:nistāri]]---delivering; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:keha]]---some who; [[synonyms:ā:āsite]] [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---could not come; [[synonyms:a:aneka]]---many; [[synonyms:s:saṁsārī]]---persons entangled in this material world.

===== Translation =====

**Thus by direct meetings, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu delivered the three worlds. Some people, however, could not go and were entangled in material activities.**
