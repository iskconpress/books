====== CC Antya 2.116 ======

===== Text =====

> "kon aparādha, prabhu, kaila haridāsa?
> ki lāgiyā dvāra-mānā, kare upavāsa?"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---what great offense; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---O Lord; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---has Haridāsa committed; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---for what reason; [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]-[[synonyms:m:mānā]]---the closed door; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:u:upavāsa]]---he is now fasting.

===== Translation =====

**"What great offense has Junior Haridāsa committed? Why has he been forbidden to come to Your door? He has now been fasting for three days."**
