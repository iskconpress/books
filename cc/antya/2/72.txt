====== CC Antya 2.72 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni śivānandera citte ha-ila saṁśaya
> kibā premāveśe kahe, kibā satya haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]] [[synonyms:s:saṁśaya]]---there was some doubt; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---whether; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---was speaking something in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---or; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---it was a fact.

===== Translation =====

**When Śivānanda Sena heard this statement, he was unsure whether Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī was speaking that way because of ecstatic love or because it was actually a fact.**
