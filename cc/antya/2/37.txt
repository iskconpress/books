====== CC Antya 2.37 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānandera bhāginā śrī-kānta-sena nāma
> prabhura kṛpāte teṅho baḍa bhāgyavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:b:bhāginā]]---nephew; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kānta]]-[[synonyms:s:sena]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named Śrīkānta Sena; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by the causeless mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]---very; [[synonyms:b:bhāgyavān]]---fortunate.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena had a nephew named Śrīkānta Sena, who by the grace of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was extremely fortunate.**
