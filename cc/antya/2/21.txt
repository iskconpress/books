====== Cc. Antya 2.21 ======

===== Text =====

> yāre dekhe tāre kahe,---'kaha kṛṣṇa-nāma'
> tāṅhāra darśane loka haya premoddāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāre]] [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---to whomever he saw; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he addresses him; [[synonyms:k:kaha]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---my dear friend, chant Kṛṣṇa's holy name; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]] [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---by seeing him; [[synonyms:l:loka]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---people became; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:uddāma]]---highly elevated in love of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**He advised whomever he met to chant the holy names Hare Kṛṣṇa. Thus upon seeing him, people were overwhelmed with love of Godhead.**
