====== Cc. Antya 2.79 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata śacī-gṛhe satata bhojana
> śrīvāsera gṛhe karena kīrtana-darśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ś:śacī]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---at the house of Śacīmātā; [[synonyms:s:satata]]---always; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eating; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsera]] [[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in the house of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:d:darśana]]---visiting the //kīrtana// performances.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu used to eat at the temple of Śacīmātā every day and also visit the house of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura when kīrtana was performed.**
