====== Cc. Antya 2.147 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-pada-prāpti lāgi' saṅkalpa karila
> triveṇī praveśa kari' prāṇa chāḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pada]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prāpti]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---with a view to getting; [[synonyms:s:saṅkalpa]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---decided definitely; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:v:veṇī]] [[synonyms:p:praveśa]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---entering into the water at the confluence of the Ganges and Yamunā at Prayāga; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍila]]---give up his life.

===== Translation =====

**Junior Haridāsa had conclusively decided to attain shelter at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Thus he entered deep into the water at Triveṇī, the confluence of the Ganges and Yamunā at Prayāga, and in this way gave up his life.**
