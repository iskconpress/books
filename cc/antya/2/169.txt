====== Cc. Antya 2.169 ======

===== Text =====

> tīrthera mahimā, nija bhakte ātmasāt
> eka līlāya karena prabhu kārya pāṅca-sāta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tīrthera]] [[synonyms:m:mahimā]]---the glories of a holy place; [[synonyms:n:nija]] [[synonyms:b:bhakte]] [[synonyms:ā:ātmasāt]]---accepting His devotee again; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---by one pastime; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---executes; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kārya]] [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:s:sāta]]---five to seven different purposes.

===== Translation =====

**It also demonstrates the glories of holy places and shows how the Lord accepts His faithful devotee. Thus the Lord fulfilled five or seven purposes by performing one pastime.**
