====== Cc. Antya 2.77 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gata-varṣa pauṣe more karāila bhojana
> kabhu nāhi khāi aiche miṣṭānna-vyañjana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gata]]-[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]---last year; [[synonyms:p:pauṣe]]---in the month of Pauṣa (December-January); [[synonyms:m:more]]---unto Me; [[synonyms:k:karāila]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---offered many foodstuffs; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:k:khāi]]---I never tasted; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:m:miṣṭānna]]---sweetmeats; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord said, "Last year in the month of Pauṣa, when Nṛsiṁhānanda gave me varieties of sweetmeats and vegetables to eat, they were so good that I had never before eaten such preparations."**
