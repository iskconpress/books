====== CC Antya 2.90 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya tāhāre prabhu-pade milāilā
> antaryāmī prabhu citte sukha nā pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]]---him (his brother); [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pade]] [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---got to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:antaryāmī]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who could study anyone's heart; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---within Himself; [[synonyms:s:sukha]]---happiness; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---could not get.

===== Translation =====

**Bhagavān Ācārya took his brother to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, but the Lord, knowing that Gopāla Bhaṭṭācārya was a Māyāvādī philosopher, could not get much happiness from meeting him.**
