====== Cc. Antya 2.107 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra ṭhāñi taṇḍula māgi' ānila haridāsa
> taṇḍula dekhi' ācāryera adhika ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from her; [[synonyms:t:taṇḍula]] [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---begging rice; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]] [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Haridāsa brought; [[synonyms:t:taṇḍula]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the rice; [[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]]---of Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:a:adhika]] [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---very great satisfaction.

===== Translation =====

**After begging the rice from her, Junior Haridāsa brought it to Bhagavān Ācārya, who was very pleased to see its quality.**
