====== CC Antya 2.66 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana dekhi' yadyapi tāṅra hṛdaye ullāsa
> nṛsiṁha lakṣya kari' bāhye kichu kare duḥkhābhāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing the eating; [[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---within his heart; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---jubilation; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]---Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:l:lakṣya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---for the sake of; [[synonyms:b:bāhye]]---externally; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ābhāsa]]---expression of disappointment.

===== Translation =====

**Although Nṛsiṁha Brahmacārī felt jubilation within his heart to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu eating everything, for the sake of Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva he externally expressed disappointment.**
