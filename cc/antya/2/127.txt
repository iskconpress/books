====== Cc. Antya 2.127 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu madhyāhna karite cali, gelā
> bujhana nā yāya ei mahāprabhura līlā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhna]] [[synonyms:k:karite]]---to execute His noon activities; [[synonyms:c:cali]]---walking; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---left; [[synonyms:b:bujhana]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---no one could understand; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastime of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu also left that place to perform His noon duties. No one could understand His pastimes.**
