====== CC Antya 2.11 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure dekhiyā yāya 'vaiṣṇava' hañā
> kṛṣṇa bali' nāce saba premāviṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:d:dekhiyā]]---by seeing the Lord; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---they return; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---having become devotees of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---chanting Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dance; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all of them; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---overwhelmed by ecstatic love.

===== Translation =====

**Having seen the Lord, they all became Vaiṣṇavas. Thus they danced and chanted the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra in ecstatic love of Godhead.**
