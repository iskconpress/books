====== Cc. Antya 2.58 ======

===== Text =====

> pāka-sāmagrī ānaha, āmi yāhā cāi'
> ye māgila, śivānanda āni' dilā tāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāka]]-[[synonyms:s:sāmagrī]] [[synonyms:ā:ānaha]]---bring all cooking ingredients; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:c:cāi]]---whatever I want; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:m:māgila]]---whatever he wanted; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:d:dilā]] [[synonyms:t:tāi]]---delivered everything.

===== Translation =====

**Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī said to Śivānanda, "Please bring whatever cooking ingredients I want." Thus Śivānanda Sena immediately brought whatever he asked for.**
