====== CC Antya 2.130 ======

===== Text =====

> puchilā,---ki ājñā, kene haila āgamana?
> 'haridāse prasāda lāgi' kailā nivedana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---the Lord inquired; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---what is your order; [[synonyms:k:kenehaila]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---what is the reason you have come; [[synonyms:h:haridāse]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for a favor to Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---he made a request.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord inquired, "What is your order? For what purpose have you come here?" Paramānanda Purī then submitted his prayer that the Lord show favor to Junior Haridāsa.**
