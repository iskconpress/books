====== CC Antya 2.9 ======

===== Text =====

> āra nānā-deśera loka āsi' jagannātha
> caitanya-caraṇa dekhi' ha-ila kṛtārtha

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]]---again; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:d:deśera]]---of different provinces; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---people; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:k:kṛta]]-[[synonyms:a:artha]]---fully satisfied.

===== Translation =====

**Similarly, people who went to Jagannātha Purī from various provinces of India were fully satisfied after seeing the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
