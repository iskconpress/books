====== CC Antya 2.36 ======

===== Text =====

> nṛsiṁhānandera āge āvirbhūta hañā
> bhojana karilā, tāhā śuna mana diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhānandera]]---the //brahmacārī// known as Nṛsiṁhānanda; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---before; [[synonyms:ā:āvirbhūta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---appearing; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---He accepted offerings of food; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---hear; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---with attention.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu appeared before Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī and ate his offerings. Please hear about this with attention.**
