====== Cc. Antya 2.70 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānanda kahe,---'kene karaha phutkāra?'
> teṅha kahe,---"dekha tomāra prabhura vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śivānanda Sena said; [[synonyms:k:kene]] [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:p:phut]]-[[synonyms:k:kāra]]---why are you expressing dismay; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---he replied; [[synonyms:d:dekha]]---see; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of your Lord; [[synonyms:v:vyayahāra]]---the behavior.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda said to Nṛsiṁhānanda, "Why are you expressing dismay?" Nṛsiṁhānanda replied, "Just see the behavior of your Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
