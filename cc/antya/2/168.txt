====== CC Antya 2.168 ======

===== Text =====

> āpana kāruṇya, loke vairāgya-śikṣaṇa
> sva-bhaktera gāḍha-anurāga-prakaṭī-karaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āpana]]---personal; [[synonyms:k:kāruṇya]]---favor; [[synonyms:l:loke]]---to the people in general; [[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śikṣaṇa]]---teaching about the order of renunciation; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of His devotees; [[synonyms:g:gāḍha]]---deep; [[synonyms:a:anurāga]]---of attachment; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭī]]---manifestation; [[synonyms:k:karaṇa]]---causing.

===== Translation =====

**This incident manifests the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, His teaching that a sannyāsī should remain in the renounced order, and the deep attachment to Him felt by His faithful devotees.**
