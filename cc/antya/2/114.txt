====== Cc. Antya 2.114 ======

===== Text =====

> dvāra mānā haila, haridāsa duḥkhī haila mane
> ki lāgiyā dvāra-mānā keha nāhi jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dvāra]] [[synonyms:m:mānā]]---closed door; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Choṭa Haridāsa; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---very unhappy; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---became within his mind; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:l:lāgiyā]]---for what reason; [[synonyms:d:dvāra]]-[[synonyms:m:mānā]]---the door was closed; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---no one could understand.

===== Translation =====

**When Junior Haridāsa heard that he had been ordered not to approach Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he was very unhappy. No one could understand why he had been ordered not to come.**
