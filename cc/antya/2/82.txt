====== Cc. Antya 2.82 ======

===== Text =====

> śivānandera prema-sīmā ke kahite pāre?
> yāṅra preme vaśa prabhu āise bāre bāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sīmā]]---the limit of love; [[synonyms:k:ke]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---can estimate; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---by loving affairs; [[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---influenced; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---comes; [[synonyms:b:bāre]] [[synonyms:b:bāre]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**Influenced by the loving affairs of Śivānanda Sena, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came again and again. Therefore who can estimate the limits of his love?**
