====== CC Antya 2.94 ======

===== Text =====

> "buddhi bhraṣṭa haila tomāra gopālera saṅge
> māyāvāda śunibāre upajila raṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:buddhi]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:b:bhraṣṭa]]---lost; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---has been; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:g:gopālera]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the association of Gopāla; [[synonyms:m:māyāvāda]] [[synonyms:ś:śunibāre]]---to hear the commentary of Māyāvāda philosophy; [[synonyms:u:upajila]] [[synonyms:r:raṅge]]---has awakened the propensity.

===== Translation =====

**"You have lost your intelligence in the association of Gopāla, and therefore you are eager to hear the Māyāvāda philosophy.**
