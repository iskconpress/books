====== CC Antya 2.32 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe śivānandera mane pratīti ha-ila
> aneka sammāna kari' bahu bhakti kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:p:pratīti]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was confidence; [[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering him much respect; [[synonyms:b:bahu]] [[synonyms:b:bhakti]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---offered him devotional service.

===== Translation =====

**Śivānanda Sena thereupon developed full confidence in his mind that Nakula Brahmacārī was filled with the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Śivānanda Sena then offered him respect and devotional service.**
