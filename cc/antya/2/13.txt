====== CC Antya 2.13 ======

===== Text =====

> tā-sabā tārite prabhu sei saba deśe
> yogya-bhakta jīva-dehe karena 'āveśe'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tā]]-[[synonyms:s:sabā]]---all of them; [[synonyms:t:tārite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---those; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---in countries; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---a suitable devotee; [[synonyms:j:jīva]]-[[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the body of such a living entity; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---entrance.

===== Translation =====

**To deliver people in regions throughout the universe who could not meet Him, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally entered the bodies of pure devotees.**
