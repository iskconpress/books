====== CC Antya 2.129 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe purī-gosāñi ekā prabhu-sthāne āilā
> namaskari' prabhu tāṅre sambhrame vasāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:e:ekā]]---alone; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:n:namaskari']]---after offering obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:s:sambhrame]]---with great respect; [[synonyms:v:vasāilā]]---got to sit down.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī thereupon went alone to the residence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The Lord, after offering him obeisances, seated him by His side with great respect.**
