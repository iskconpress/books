====== Cc. Antya 2.100 ======

===== Text =====

> lajjā-bhaya pāñā ācārya mauna ha-ilā
> āra dina gopālere deśe pāṭhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lajjā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear and shame; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became silent; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:g:gopālere]]---Gopāla Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to his own country; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāilā]]---sent.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Bhagavān Ācārya, greatly ashamed and fearful, remained silent. The next day, he asked Gopāla Bhaṭṭācārya to return to his own district.**
