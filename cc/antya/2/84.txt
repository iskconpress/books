====== CC Antya 2.84 ======

===== Text =====

> puruṣottame prabhu-pāśe bhagavān ācārya
> parama vaiṣṇava teṅho supaṇḍita ārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puruṣottame]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---in the association of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]] [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Bhagavān Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---pure devotee; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:s:su]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---very learned scholar; [[synonyms:ā:ārya]]---gentleman.

===== Translation =====

**At Jagannātha Purī, in the association of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, lived Bhagavān Ācārya, who was certainly a gentleman, a learned scholar and a great devotee.**

===== Purport =====

For a description of Bhagavān Ācārya, one may refer to the //Ādi-līlā,// Tenth Chapter, verse 136.
