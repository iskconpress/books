====== CC Antya 2.158 ======

===== Text =====

> ājanma kṛṣṇa-kīrtana, prabhura sevana
> prabhu-kṛpā-pātra, āra kṣetrera maraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ājanma]]---throughout the whole life; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]---chanting of the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---very dear to the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kṣetrera]] [[synonyms:m:maraṇa]]---his death in a holy place.

===== Translation =====

**"Junior Haridāsa chanted the Hare Kṛṣṇa mantra throughout his entire life and served the Supreme Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Moreover, he is dear to the Lord and has died in a holy place.**
