====== CC Antya 2.28 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-dike dhāya loke 'śivānanda' bali
> śivānanda kon, tomāya bolāya brahmacārī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:d:dike]]---in four directions; [[synonyms:d:dhāya]] [[synonyms:l:loke]]---people began to run; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:b:bali]]---calling loudly the name of Śivānanda; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:k:kon]]---whoever is Śivānanda; [[synonyms:t:tomāya]]---unto you; [[synonyms:b:bolāya]]---calls; [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]]---Nakula Brahmacārī.

===== Translation =====

**Thus people began running here and there, calling in all directions, "Śivānanda! Whoever is Śivānanda, please come. Nakula Brahmacārī is calling you."**
