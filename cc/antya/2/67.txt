====== Cc. Antya 2.67 ======

===== Text =====

> svayaṁ bhagavān kṛṣṇa-caitanya-gosāñi
> jagannātha-nṛsiṁha-saha kichu bheda nāi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svayam]]---personally; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with Lord Jagannātha and Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:b:bheda]]---any difference; [[synonyms:n:nāi]]---there is not.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself. Therefore there is no difference between Him, Lord Jagannātha and Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva.**
