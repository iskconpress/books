====== Cc. Antya 2.163 ======

===== Text =====

> 'haridāsa kāṅhā?' yadi śrīvāsa puchilā
> "sva-karma-phala-bhuk pumān"---prabhu uttara dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kāṅhā]]---where is junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura inquired; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:k:karma]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuk]]---sure to accept the resultant action of his fruitive activities; [[synonyms:p:pumān]]---a person; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uttara]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---replied.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura inquired from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Where is Junior Haridāsa?" The Lord replied, "A person is sure to achieve the results of his fruitive activities."**
