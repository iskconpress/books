====== CC Antya 2.141 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali tāre snāna bhojana karāñā
> āpana bhavana āilā tāre āśvāsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali]]---saying this; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---inducing to bathe and take //prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]] [[synonyms:b:bhavana]]---to his own place; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsiyā]]---assuring him.

===== Translation =====

**Having said this, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī induced Haridāsa to bathe and take prasāda. After thus reassuring him, he returned home.**
