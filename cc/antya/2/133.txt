====== CC Antya 2.133 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu yadi govinde bolāilā
> purīre namaskāra kari' uṭhiyā calilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:g:govinde]] [[synonyms:b:bolāilā]]---called for Govinda; [[synonyms:p:purīre]]---to Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering respect; [[synonyms:u:uṭhiyā]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---got up and began to go away.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, the Lord called for Govinda. Offering obeisances to Paramānanda Purī, He got up and began to leave.**
