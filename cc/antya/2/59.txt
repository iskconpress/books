====== Cc. Antya 2.59 ======

===== Text =====

> prātaḥ-kāla haite pāka karilā apāra
> nānā vyañjana, piṭhā, kṣīra nānā upahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---beginning from the morning; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---cooked many varieties of food; [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---varieties of vegetables; [[synonyms:p:piṭhā]]---cakes; [[synonyms:k:kṣīra]]---sweet rice; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:u:upahāra]]---offerings of food.

===== Translation =====

**Beginning early in the morning, Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī cooked many varieties of food, including vegetables, cakes, sweet rice and other preparations.**
