====== Cc. Antya 2.38 ======

===== Text =====

> eka vatsara teṅho prathama ekeśvara
> prabhu dekhibāre āilā utkaṇṭhā-antara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---one year; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---Śrīkānta Sena; [[synonyms:p:prathama]]---first; [[synonyms:e:ekeśvara]]---alone; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see the Lord; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:u:utkaṇṭhā]]-[[synonyms:a:antara]]---with great anxiety in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**One year, Śrīkānta Sena came alone to Jagannātha Purī in great eagerness to see the Lord.**
