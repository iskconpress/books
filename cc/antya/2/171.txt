====== Cc. Antya 2.171 ======

===== Text =====

> viśvāsa kariyā śuna caitanya-carita
> tarka nā kariha, tarke habe viparīta

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with faith and confidence; [[synonyms:ś:śuna]]---just hear; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:carita]]---the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tarka]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do not uselessly argue; [[synonyms:t:tarke]]---by argument; [[synonyms:h:habe]] [[synonyms:v:viparīta]]---will get the opposite result.

===== Translation =====

**Please hear the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with faith and confidence. Do not argue, for arguments will produce a contrary result.**
