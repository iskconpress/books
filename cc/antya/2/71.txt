====== CC Antya 2.71 ======

===== Text =====

> tina janāra bhoga teṅho ekelā khāilā
> jagannātha-nṛsiṁha upavāsī ha-ilā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:j:janāra]]---of the three Deities; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]---offerings; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:e:ekelā]]---alone; [[synonyms:k:khāilā]]---ate; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]]---Lord Jagannātha and Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:u:upavāsī]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---remained fasting.

===== Translation =====

**"He alone has eaten the offerings for all three Deities. Because of this, both Jagannātha and Nṛsiṁhadeva remain fasting."**
