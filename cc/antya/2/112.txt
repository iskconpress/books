====== CC Antya 2.112 ======

===== Text =====

> anna praśaṁsiyā prabhu bhojana karilā
> nija-gṛhe āsi' govindere ājñā dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:p:praśaṁsiyā]]---praising the rice; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhojana]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---partook of the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---to His own residence; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming back; [[synonyms:g:govindere]]---to Govinda; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---He gave order.

===== Translation =====

**Praising the quality of the rice, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu partook of the prasāda. Then, after returning to His residence, He gave the following order to Govinda, His personal assistant.**
