====== CC Antya 2.161 ======

===== Text =====

> yaiche saṅkalpa, yaiche triveṇī praveśila
> śuni, śrīvāsādira mane vismaya ha-ila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yaiche]] [[synonyms:s:saṅkalpa]]---how he was determined; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:t:triveṇī]] [[synonyms:p:praveśila]]---he entered into Triveṇī; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādira]]---of Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura and others; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the minds; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was surprise.

===== Translation =====

**He explained how Junior Haridāsa had made his resolution and had thus entered the waters at the confluence of the Yamunā and Ganges. Hearing these details, Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura and the other devotees were very surprised.**
