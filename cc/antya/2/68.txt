====== CC Antya 2.68 ======

===== Text =====

> ihā jānibāre pradyumnera gūḍha haita mana
> tāhā dekhāilā prabhu kariyā bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this fact; [[synonyms:j:jānibāre]]---to know; [[synonyms:p:pradyumnera]]---of Pradyumna Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:g:gūḍha]]---deeply; [[synonyms:h:haita]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---was eager; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:d:dekhāilā]]---exhibited; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---by eating.

===== Translation =====

**Pradyumna Brahmacārī was deeply eager to understand this fact. Therefore Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu revealed it to him by a practical demonstration.**
