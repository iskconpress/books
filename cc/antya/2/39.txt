====== Cc. Antya 2.39 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu tāre dekhi' baḍa kṛpā kailā
> māsa-dui teṅho prabhura nikaṭe rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---bestowed great mercy; [[synonyms:m:māsa]]-[[synonyms:d:dui]]---for two months; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---Śrīkānta Sena; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nikaṭe]]---near Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---stayed.

===== Translation =====

**Seeing Śrīkānta Sena, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed causeless mercy upon him. Śrīkānta Sena stayed near Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for about two months at Jagannātha Purī.**
