====== CC Antya 2.27 ======

===== Text =====

> āveśe brahmacārī kahe,---'śivānanda āche dūre
> jana dui cāri yāha, bolāha tāhāre'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in that state of possession; [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Nakula Brahmacārī said; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:ā:āche]] [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---is staying some distance off; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---persons; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]---four; [[synonyms:y:yāha]]---go; [[synonyms:b:bolāha]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]]---call him.

===== Translation =====

**In his inspired state, Nakula Brahmacārī said, "Śivānanda Sena is staying some distance away. Two or four of you go call him."**
