====== CC Antya 2.4 ======

===== Text =====

> sākṣāt-darśana, āra yogya-bhakta-jīve
> 'āveśa' karaye kāhāṅ, kāhāṅ 'āvirbhāve'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]-[[synonyms:d:darśana]]---direct meeting; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:y:yogya]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---perfect devotee; [[synonyms:j:jīve]]---living beings; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]] [[synonyms:k:karaye]]---empowers with specific spiritual potencies; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---somewhere; [[synonyms:k:kāhāṅ]]---in other places; [[synonyms:ā:āvirbhāve]]---by appearing Himself.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord delivered the fallen souls in some places by meeting them directly, in other places by empowering a pure devotee, and in still other places by appearing before someone Himself.**
