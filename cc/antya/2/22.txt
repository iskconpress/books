====== Cc. Antya 2.22 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanyera āveśa haya nakulera dehe
> śuni' śivānanda āilā kariyā sandehe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---taking possession; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:n:nakulera]] [[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in the body of Nakula Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---Śivānanda Sena came; [[synonyms:k:kariyā]] [[synonyms:s:sandehe]]---doubting.

===== Translation =====

**When Śivānanda Sena heard that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had entered the body of Nakula Brahmacārī, he went there with doubts in his mind.**
