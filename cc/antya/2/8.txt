====== CC Antya 2.8 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍa-deśera bhakta-gaṇa pratyabda āsiyā
> punaḥ gauḍa-deśe yāya prabhure miliyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśera]]---of Bengal; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---devotees; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:a:abda]]---every year; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśe]]---to Bengal; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---return; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:miliyā]]---after meeting.

===== Translation =====

**Every year, devotees from Bengal would go to Jagannātha Purī to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and after the meeting they would return to Bengal.**
