====== CC Antya 2.73 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe śivānande kichu kahe brahmacārī
> sāmagrī āna nṛsiṁha lāgi punaḥ pāka kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---upon this; [[synonyms:ś:śivānande]]---unto Śivānanda; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]]---Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:s:sāmagrī]] [[synonyms:ā:āna]]---bring more ingredients; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁha]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---let me cook.

===== Translation =====

**When Śivānanda Sena was thus perplexed, Nṛsiṁhānanda Brahmacārī said to him, "Bring more food. Let me cook again for Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva."**
