====== Cc. Antya 2.121 ======

===== Text =====

> eta kahi' mahāprabhu abhyantare gelā
> gosāñira āveśa dekhi' sabe mauna hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---saying this; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:abhyantare]] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---entered His room; [[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---absorption in anger; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became silent.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu entered His room. Seeing Him in such an angry mood, all the devotees fell silent.**
