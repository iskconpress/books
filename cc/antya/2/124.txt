====== Cc. Antya 2.124 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"mora vaśa nahe mora mana
> prakṛti-sambhāṣī vairāgī nā kare darśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---under My control; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind; [[synonyms:p:prakṛti]]-[[synonyms:s:sambhāṣī]]---one who talks with women; [[synonyms:v:vairāgī]]---a person in the renounced order; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:d:darśana]]---does not see.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My mind is not under My control. It does not like to see anyone in the renounced order who talks intimately with women.**
