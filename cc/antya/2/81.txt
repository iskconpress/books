====== CC Antya 2.81 ======

===== Text =====

> prema-vaśa gaura-prabhu, yāhāṅ premottama
> prema-vasa hañā tāhā dena daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---subdued by loving service; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Gaurasundara; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:u:uttama]]---wherever there is pure love; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being subdued by such love; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---there; [[synonyms:d:dena]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---appears personally.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Gaurasundara is greatly influenced by the love of His devotees. Therefore wherever there is pure devotion to the Lord, the Lord Himself, subdued by such love, appears, and His devotees see Him.**
