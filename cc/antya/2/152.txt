====== CC Antya 2.152 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' mahāprabhu īṣat hāsiyā rahilā
> saba bhakta-gaṇa mane vismaya ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ī:īṣat]]---slightly; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained smiling; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the minds; [[synonyms:v:vismaya]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became surprised.

===== Translation =====

**While hearing the devotees lament, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was mildly smiling. Thus all the devotees were very astonished.**
