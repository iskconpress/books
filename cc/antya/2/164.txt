====== CC Antya 2.164 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe śrīvāsa tāra vṛttānta kahila
> yaiche saṅkalpa, yaiche triveṇī praveśila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]---Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:v:vṛttānta]]---story; [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---spoke; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:s:saṅkalpa]]---he decided; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:t:triveṇī]] [[synonyms:p:praveśila]]---he entered the waters at the confluence of the Ganges and Yamunā.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura related the details of Haridāsa's decision and his entering the waters at the confluence of the Ganges and Yamunā.**
