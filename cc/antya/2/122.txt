====== CC Antya 2.122 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dine sabe meli' prabhura caraṇe
> haridāsa lāgi, kichu kailā nivedane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---the next day; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:m:meli']]---all the devotees, coming together; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---unto the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi]]---on behalf of Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedane]]---submitted petition.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, all the devotees together approached the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to submit an appeal on behalf of Junior Haridāsa.**
