====== CC Antya 2.18 ======

===== Text =====

> graha-grasta-prāya nakula premāviṣṭa hañā
> hāse, kānde, nāce, gāya unmatta hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:graha]]-[[synonyms:g:grasta]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---exactly like one haunted by a ghost; [[synonyms:n:nakula]]---Nakula Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being overwhelmed by ecstatic love of God; [[synonyms:h:hāse]]---laughs; [[synonyms:k:kānde]]---cries; [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---dances; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---chants; [[synonyms:u:unmatta]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---just like a madman.

===== Translation =====

**Nakula Brahmacārī became exactly like a man haunted by a ghost. Thus he sometimes laughed, sometimes cried, sometimes danced and sometimes chanted like a madman.**
