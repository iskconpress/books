====== Cc. Antya 2.17 ======

===== Text =====

> gauḍa-deśera loka nistārite mana haila
> nakula-hṛdaye prabhu 'āveśa' karila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:d:deśera]] [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people of Bengal; [[synonyms:n:nistārite]]---to deliver; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---wanted; [[synonyms:n:nakula]]-[[synonyms:h:hṛdaye]]---in the heart of Nakula Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]] [[synonyms:k:karila]]---entered.

===== Translation =====

**Desiring to deliver all the people of Bengal, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu entered the heart of Nakula Brahmacārī.**
