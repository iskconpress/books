====== CC Antya 2.104 ======

===== Text =====

> māhitira bhaginī sei, nāma---mādhavī-devī
> vṛddhā tapasvinī āra paramā vaiṣṇavī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:māhitira]] [[synonyms:b:bhaginī]]---sister of Śikhi Māhiti; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:m:mādhavī]]-[[synonyms:d:devī]]---of the name Mādhavīdevī; [[synonyms:v:vṛddhā]]---an elderly lady; [[synonyms:t:tapasvinī]]---very strict in executing devotional service; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paramā]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavī]]---a first-class devotee.

===== Translation =====

**Śikhi Māhiti's sister was named Mādhavīdevī. She was an elderly lady who always performed austerities. She was very advanced in devotional service.**
