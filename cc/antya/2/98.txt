====== CC Antya 2.98 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe, "tathāpi māyāvāda-śravaṇe
> 'cit, brahma, māyā, mithyā'---ei-mātra śune

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara replied; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:m:māyāvāda]]-[[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing the Māyāvāda commentary; [[synonyms:c:cit]]---knowledge; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---the Absolute Truth; [[synonyms:m:māyā]]---external energy; [[synonyms:m:mithyā]]---false; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only these; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "Nevertheless, when we hear the Māyāvāda philosophy, we hear that Brahman is knowledge and that the universe of māyā is false, but we gain no spiritual understanding.**
