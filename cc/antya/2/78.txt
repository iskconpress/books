====== CC Antya 2.78 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' bhakta-gaṇa mane āścarya mānila
> śivānandera mane tabe pratyaya janmila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:ā:āścarya]] [[synonyms:m:mānila]]---felt wonder; [[synonyms:ś:śivānandera]]---of Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:p:pratyaya]] [[synonyms:j:janmila]]---there was confidence.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, all the devotees were struck with wonder, and Śivānanda became confident that the incident was true.**
