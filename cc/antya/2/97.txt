====== Cc. Antya 2.97 ======

===== Text =====

> ācārya kahe,---'āmā sabāra kṛṣṇa-niṣṭha-citte
> āmā sabāra mana bhāṣya nāre phirāite'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Bhagavān Ācārya replied; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of us; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:niṣṭha]]---devoted to Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:c:citte]]---hearts; [[synonyms:ā:āmā]] [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of us; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---minds; [[synonyms:b:bhāṣya]]---//Śārīraka-bhāṣya//; [[synonyms:n:nāre]] [[synonyms:p:phirāite]]---cannot change.

===== Translation =====

**In spite of Svarūpa Dāmodara's protest, Bhagavān Ācārya continued, "We are all fixed at the lotus feet of Kṛṣṇa with our hearts and souls. Therefore the Śārīraka-bhāṣya cannot change our minds."**
