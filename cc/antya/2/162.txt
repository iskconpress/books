====== CC Antya 2.162 ======

===== Text =====

> varṣāntare śivānanda saba bhakta lañā
> prabhure mililā āsi' ānandita hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varṣa]]-[[synonyms:a:antare]]---at the end of the year; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the devotees; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming greatly happy.

===== Translation =====

**At the end of the year, Śivānanda Sena came to Jagannātha Purī as usual, accompanied by the other devotees, and thus in great happiness met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
