====== CC Antya 2.159 ======

===== Text =====

> durgati nā haya tāra, sad-gati se haya
> prabhu-bhaṅgī ei, pāche jānibā niścaya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:durgati]]---a bad result; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---is not his; [[synonyms:s:sat]]-[[synonyms:g:gati]] [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---he must have achieved liberation; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṅgī]]---a sport of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pāche]]---later; [[synonyms:j:jānibā]]---you will understand; [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---the real fact.

===== Translation =====

**"Haridāsa cannot have been degraded; he must have attained liberation. This is a pastime of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. You will all understand it later."**
