====== CC Antya 2.74 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe śivānanda bhoga-sāmagrī ānilā
> pāka kari' nṛsiṁhera bhoga lāgāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:ś:śivānanda]]---Śivānanda Sena; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]]-[[synonyms:s:sāmagrī]]---ingredients for preparing food; [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after cooking; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhera]]---of Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva; [[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:l:lāgāilā]]---offered the food.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śivānanda Sena again brought the ingredients with which to cook, and Pradyumna Brahmacārī again cooked and offered the food to Nṛsiṁhadeva.**
