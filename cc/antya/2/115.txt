====== CC Antya 2.115 ======

===== Text =====

> tina-dina haila haridāsa kare upavāsa
> svarūpādi āsi, puchilā mahāprabhura pāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---for three days; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:u:upavāsa]]---was fasting; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara and other confidential devotees; [[synonyms:ā:āsi]]---coming; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Haridāsa fasted continuously for three days. Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and other confidential devotees approached Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to inquire from Him.**
