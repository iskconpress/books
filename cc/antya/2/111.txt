====== CC Antya 2.111 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---'kon yāi' māgiyā ānila?'
> choṭa-haridāsera nāma ācārya kahila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:y:yāi']]---who went; [[synonyms:m:māgiyā]]---begging; [[synonyms:ā:ānila]]---brought; [[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:h:haridāsera]]---of Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the name; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:k:kahila]]---Bhagavān Ācārya informed.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked who had begged the rice and brought it back, Bhagavān Ācārya mentioned the name of Junior Haridāsa.**
