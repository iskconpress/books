====== CC Antya 2.102 ======

===== Text =====

> 'choṭa-haridāsa' nāma prabhura kīrtanīyā
> tāhāre kahena ācārya ḍākiyā āniyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:choṭa]]-[[synonyms:h:haridāsa]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---a devotee named Choṭa Haridāsa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kīrtanīyā]]---a chanter of songs for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---says; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---the //ācārya//; [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍākiyā]] [[synonyms:ā:āniyā]]---calling him to his place.

===== Translation =====

**A devotee named Choṭa Haridāsa used to sing for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Bhagavān Ācārya called him to his home and spoke as follows.**
