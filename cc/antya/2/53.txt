====== CC Antya 2.53 ======

===== Text =====

> 'pradyumna brahmacārī'---tāṅra nija-nāma
> 'nṛsiṁhānanda' nāma tāṅra kailā gaura-dhāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pradyumna]] [[synonyms:b:brahmacārī]]---Pradyumna Brahmacārī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---real name; [[synonyms:n:nṛsiṁhānanda]]---Nṛsiṁhānanda; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:d:dhāma]]---was given by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**His real name was Pradyumna Brahmacārī. The name Nṛsiṁhānanda had been given to him by Lord Gaurasundara Himself.**
