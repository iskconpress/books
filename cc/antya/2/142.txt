====== CC Antya 2.142 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu yadi yāna jagannātha-daraśane
> dūre rahi' haridāsa karena darśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---when; [[synonyms:y:yāna]]---goes; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:d:dūre]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---remaining in a distant place; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:d:darśane]]---sees.

===== Translation =====

**When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to see Lord Jagannātha in the temple, Haridāsa would stay a long distance away and see Him.**
