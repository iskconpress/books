====== CC Antya 2.15 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata āveśe tārila tribhuvana
> gauḍe yaiche āveśa, kari dig daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---by empowering; [[synonyms:t:tārila]] [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---delivered the entire three worlds; [[synonyms:g:gauḍe]]---in Bengal; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:ā:āveśa]]---empowering; [[synonyms:k:kari]] [[synonyms:d:dik]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---I shall describe in brief.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu delivered the entire three worlds, not only by His personal presence but also by empowering others. I shall briefly describe how He empowered a living being in Bengal.**
