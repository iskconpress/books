====== CC Antya 2.131 ======

===== Text =====

> śuniyā kahena prabhu,---"śunaha, gosāñi
> saba vaiṣṇava lañā tumi raha ei ṭhāñi

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuniyā]]---hearing; [[synonyms:k:kahena]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:ś:śunaha]]---please hear; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---my lord; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]---all the Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---your lordship; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---stay; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in this place.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this request, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "My dear lord, please hear me. It is better for you to stay here with all the Vaiṣṇavas.**
