====== CC Antya 2.19 ======

===== Text =====

> aśru, kampa, stambha, sveda, sāttvika vikāra
> nirantara preme nṛtya, saghana huṅkāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:s:stambha]]---becoming stunned; [[synonyms:s:sveda]]---perspiration; [[synonyms:s:sāttvika]] [[synonyms:v:vikāra]]---all such transcendental transformations; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---continuously; [[synonyms:p:preme]] [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:g:ghana]] [[synonyms:h:huṅkāra]]---a sound like that of a cloud.

===== Translation =====

**He continuously exhibited bodily transformations of transcendental love. Thus he cried, trembled, became stunned, perspired, danced in love of Godhead and made sounds like those of a cloud.**
