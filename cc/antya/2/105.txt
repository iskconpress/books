====== Cc. Antya 2.105 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu lekhā kare yāre---rādhikāra 'gaṇa'
> jagatera madhye 'pātra'---sāḍe tina jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:l:lekhā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---accepts; [[synonyms:y:yāre]]---whom; [[synonyms:r:rādhikāra]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---as one of the associates of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:j:jagatera]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---throughout the whole world; [[synonyms:p:pātra]]---most confidential devotees; [[synonyms:s:sāḍe]] [[synonyms:t:tina]]---three and a half; [[synonyms:j:jana]]---persons.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted her as having formerly been an associate of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī. In the entire world, three and a half people were His intimate devotees.**
