====== CC Antya 2.148 ======

===== Text =====

> sei-kṣaṇe divya-dehe prabhu-sthāne āilā
> prabhu-kṛpā pāñā antardhānei rahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇe]]---immediately thereupon; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:d:dehe]]---in a spiritual body; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]---the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting; [[synonyms:a:antardhānei]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained invisible.

===== Translation =====

**Immediately after committing suicide in this way, he went in his spiritual body to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and received the mercy of the Lord. However, he still remained invisible.**
