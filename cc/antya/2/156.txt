====== CC Antya 2.156 ======

===== Text =====

> 'viṣādi khāñā haridāsa ātma-ghāta kaila
> sei pāpe jāni 'brahma-rākṣasa' haila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:viṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:k:khāñā]]---by drinking poison; [[synonyms:h:haridāsa]]---Junior Haridāsa; [[synonyms:ā:ātma]]-[[synonyms:g:ghāta]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---has committed suicide; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:p:pāpe]]---on account of that sinful activity; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---we understand; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:r:rākṣasa]]---a //brāhmaṇa// ghost; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---he has become.

===== Translation =====

**"Haridāsa must have committed suicide by drinking poison, and because of this sinful act, he has now become a brāhmaṇa ghost.**
