====== CC Antya 2.123 ======

===== Text =====

> "alpa aparādha, prabhu karaha prasāda
> ebe śikṣā ha-ila nā karibe aparādha"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alpa]] [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---the offense is not very great; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---O Lord; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---be merciful; [[synonyms:e:ebe]]---now; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---he has gotten a sufficient lesson; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karibe]]---he will not do; [[synonyms:a:aparādha]]---offense.

===== Translation =====

**"Haridāsa has committed a small offense," they said. "Therefore, O Lord, please be merciful to him. Now he has received a sufficient lesson. In the future he will not commit such a offense."**
