====== CC Antya 2.108 ======

===== Text =====

> snehe rāndhila prabhura priya ye vyañjana
> deula prasāda, ādā-cāki, lembu-salavaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:snehe]]---in great affection; [[synonyms:r:rāndhila]]---cooked; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---dear; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---whatever; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables; [[synonyms:d:deula]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants from the temple of Jagannātha; [[synonyms:ā:ādā]]-[[synonyms:c:cāki]]---ground ginger; [[synonyms:l:lembu]]---lime; [[synonyms:s:sa]]-[[synonyms:l:lavaṇa]]---with salt.

===== Translation =====

**In great affection, Bhagavān Ācārya cooked varieties of vegetables and other preparations dear to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He also obtained remnants of food from Lord Jagannātha and digestive aids such as ground ginger and also lime with salt.**
