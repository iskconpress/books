====== Cc. Antya 2.55 ======

===== Text =====

> kāli madhyāhne teṅho āsibena tomāra ghare
> pāka-sāmagrī ānaha, āmi bhikṣā dimu tāṅre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāli]] [[synonyms:m:madhyāhne]]---tomorrow at noon; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:ā:āsibena]]---will come; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---to your place; [[synonyms:p:pāka]]-[[synonyms:s:sāmagrī]] [[synonyms:ā:ānaha]]---please bring everything necessary for cooking; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:dimu]]---shall cook and offer food; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to Him.

===== Translation =====

**"Tomorrow at noon He will come to your home. Therefore please bring all kinds of cooking ingredients. I shall personally cook and offer Him food.**
