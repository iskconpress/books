====== CC Antya 2.86 ======

===== Text =====

> ekānta-bhāve āśriyāchena caitanya-caraṇa
> madhye madhye prabhura teṅho karena nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ekānta]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---with full attention; [[synonyms:ā:āśriyāchena]]---has taken shelter of; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:m:madhye]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation.

===== Translation =====

**He sought the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with full surrender. Sometimes he would invite the Lord to dine at his home.**
