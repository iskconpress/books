====== CC Antya 2.167 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata līlā kare śacīra nandana
> yāhā śuni' bhakta-gaṇera yuḍāya karṇa-mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:l:līlā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---continues to execute pastimes; [[synonyms:ś:śacīra]] [[synonyms:n:nandana]]---the son of mother Śacī; [[synonyms:y:yāhā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing which; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the devotees; [[synonyms:y:yuḍāya]]---satisfies; [[synonyms:k:karṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---the ears and the mind.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the son of mother Śacī, performs His pastimes, which greatly satisfy the ears and minds of pure devotees who hear about them.**
