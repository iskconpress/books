====== CC Antya 19.18 ======

===== Text =====

> tarajā-prahelī ācārya kahena ṭhāre-ṭhore
> prabhu mātra bujhena, keha bujhite nā pāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tarajā]]-[[synonyms:p:prahelī]]---a sonnet in equivocal language; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---spoke; [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāre]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhore]]---making some indications; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only; [[synonyms:b:bujhena]]---could understand; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:b:bujhite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---others could not understand.

===== Translation =====

**Advaita Ācārya had written a sonnet in equivocal language with an import that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu could understand but others could not.**
