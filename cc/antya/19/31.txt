====== Cc. Antya 19.31 ======

===== Text =====

> unmāda-pralāpa-ceṣṭā kare rātri-dine
> rādhā-bhāvāveśe viraha bāḍe anukṣaṇe

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:unmāda]]---madness; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---craziness; [[synonyms:c:ceṣṭā]]---activities; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---He performed day and night; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in the ecstatic emotion of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:v:viraha]]---separation; [[synonyms:b:bāḍe]]---increases; [[synonyms:a:anukṣaṇe]]---every moment.

===== Translation =====

**As His feelings of separation in the ecstasy of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī increased at every moment, the Lord's activities, both day and night, were now wild, insane performances.**
