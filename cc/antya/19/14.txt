====== Cc. Antya 19.14 ======

===== Text =====

> mātṛ-bhakta-gaṇera prabhu hana śiromaṇi
> sannyāsa kariyā sadā sevena jananī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mātṛ]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇera]]---of the devotees of mothers; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hana]]---is; [[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇi]]---the topmost jewel; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---even after taking the //sannyāsa// order; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:s:sevena]]---renders service; [[synonyms:j:jananī]]---to His mother.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the topmost gem of all devotees of mothers. He rendered service to His mother even after He had accepted the vow of sannyāsa.**
