====== CC Antya 19.68 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu-pāda-tale śaṅkara karena śayana
> prabhu tāṅra upara karena pāda-prasāraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:t:tale]]---at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lies down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him; [[synonyms:u:upara]]---on the body; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:prasāraṇa]]---extending His legs.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śaṅkara Paṇḍita lay at the feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord placed His legs upon Śaṅkara's body.**
