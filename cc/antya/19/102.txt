====== CC Antya 19.102 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu pāñā cetana
> snāna kari' kaila jagannātha-daraśana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāñā]] [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---becoming conscious; [[synonyms:s:snāna]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after bathing; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---saw Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus returned to consciousness. He then bathed and went to see Lord Jagannātha.**
