====== CC Antya 19.99 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata gaurahari, gandhe kaila mana curi,
> bhṛṅga-prāya iti-uti dhāya
> yāya vṛkṣa-latā-pāśe, kṛṣṇa sphure---sei āśe,
> kṛṣṇa nā pāya, gandha-mātra pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gandhe]]---by the scent; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did; [[synonyms:m:mana]] [[synonyms:c:curi]]---stealing of the mind; [[synonyms:b:bhṛṅga]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---just like a bumblebee; [[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:u:uti]] [[synonyms:d:dhāya]]---wanders here and there; [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes; [[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:l:latā]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---by the side of the trees and creepers; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:s:sphure]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa will appear; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ā:āśe]]---by that hope; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---does not obtain Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]-[[synonyms:m:mātra]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---gets only the scent.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, His mind thus stolen by that scent, ran here and there like a bumblebee. He ran to the trees and plants, hoping that Lord Kṛṣṇa would appear, but instead He found only the scent of Kṛṣṇa's body.**
