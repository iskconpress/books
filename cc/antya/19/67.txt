====== CC Antya 19.67 ======

===== Text =====

> saba bhakta meli' tabe prabhure sādhila
> śaṅkara-paṇḍite prabhura saṅge śoyāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:m:meli']]---all the devotees, meeting together; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereupon; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:s:sādhila]]---entreated Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍite]]---Śaṅkara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with; [[synonyms:ś:śoyāila]]---made to lie down.

===== Translation =====

**After consulting with one another, they entreated Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to allow Śaṅkara Paṇḍita to lie down in the same room with Him.**
