====== CC Antya 19.21 ======

===== Text =====

> bāulake kahiha,---kāye nāhika āula
> bāulake kahiha,---ihā kahiyāche bāula"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāulake]] [[synonyms:k:kahiha]]---again inform the //bāula,// Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kāye]]---in business; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]]---there is not; [[synonyms:ā:āula]]---persons who have become mad in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bāulake]] [[synonyms:k:kahiha]]---again inform the //bāula,// Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:kahiyāche]]---has spoken; [[synonyms:b:bāula]]---another madman, Śrī Advaita Prabhu Himself.

===== Translation =====

**"Those now mad in ecstatic love are no longer interested in the material world. Tell Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that Advaita Prabhu, who has also become a madman in ecstatic love, has spoken these words."**
