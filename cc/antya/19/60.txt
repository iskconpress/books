====== CC Antya 19.60 ======

===== Text =====

> sarva-rātri karena bhāve mukha saṅgharṣaṇa
> goṅ-goṅ-śabda karena,---svarūpa śunilā takhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:r:rātri]]---the whole night; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in emotion; [[synonyms:m:mukha]] [[synonyms:s:saṅgharṣaṇa]]---rubbing of the face; [[synonyms:g:goṅ]]-[[synonyms:g:goṅ]]-[[synonyms:ś:śabda]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---makes a peculiar sound, //goṅ-goṅ//; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---heard; [[synonyms:t:takhana]]---then.

===== Translation =====

**In ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu rubbed His face against the walls all night long, making a peculiar sound, "goṅ-goṅ," which Svarūpa Dāmodara could hear through the door.**
