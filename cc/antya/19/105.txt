====== Cc. Antya 19.105 ======

===== Text =====

> dhanyasyāyaṁ navaḥ premā
> yasyonmīlati cetasi
> antarvāṇībhir apy asya
> mudrā suṣṭhu sudurgamā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhanyasya]]---of a most fortunate person; [[synonyms:a:ayam]]---this; [[synonyms:n:navaḥ]]---new; [[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Godhead; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom; [[synonyms:u:unmīlati]]---manifests; [[synonyms:c:cetasi]]---in the heart; [[synonyms:a:antarvāṇībhiḥ]]---by persons well versed in //śāstras//; [[synonyms:a:api]]---even; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of him; [[synonyms:m:mudrā]]---the symptoms; [[synonyms:s:suṣṭhu]]---exceedingly; [[synonyms:s:sudurgamā]]---difficult to understand.

===== Translation =====

**"The activities and symptoms of that exalted personality in whose heart love of Godhead has awakened cannot be understood even by the most learned scholar."**

===== Purport =====

This verse is quoted from //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (1.4.17).
