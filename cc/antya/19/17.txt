====== CC Antya 19.17 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryera ṭhāñi giyā ājñā māgilā
> ācārya-gosāñi prabhure sandeśa kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācāryera]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:g:giyā]]---going; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---begged for permission to leave; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sandeśa]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---sent a message.

===== Translation =====

**When he went to Advaita Ācārya and also asked His permission to return, Advaita Prabhu gave him a message to deliver to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
