====== Cc. Antya 19.100 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-rāmānanda gāya, prabhu nāce, sukha pāya,
> ei-mate prātaḥ-kāla haila
> svarūpa-rāmānanda-rāya, kari nānā upāya,
> mahāprabhura bāhya-sphūrti kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya sing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dances; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---enjoys happiness; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---morning arrived; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---both Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kari]]---devising; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]---various; [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---means; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bāhya]]-[[synonyms:s:sphūrti]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---awakened the external consciousness.

===== Translation =====

**Both Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya sang to the Lord, who danced and enjoyed happiness until the morning arrived. Then they devised a plan to revive the Lord to external consciousness.**
