====== CC Antya 19.83 ======

===== Text =====

> chaya ṛtu-gaṇa yāhāṅ vasanta pradhāna
> dekhi' ānandita hailā gaura bhagavān

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:chaya]]---six; [[synonyms:ṛ:ṛtu]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---seasons; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:v:vasanta]] [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the spring season was chief; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]]---very pleased; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

===== Translation =====

**The six seasons, especially spring, seemed present there. Seeing the garden, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, was very happy.**
