====== CC Antya 19.5 ======

===== Text =====

> prati-vatsara prabhu tāṅre pāṭhāna nadīyāte
> viccheda-duḥkhitā jāni' jananī āśvāsite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---every year; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāna]]---sends; [[synonyms:n:nadīyāte]]---to Navadvīpa; [[synonyms:v:viccheda]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkhitā]] [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing her affliction due to separation; [[synonyms:j:jananī]]---His mother; [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsite]]---to console.

===== Translation =====

**Knowing His mother to be greatly afflicted by separation, the Lord would send Jagadānanda Paṇḍita to Navadvīpa every year to console her.**
