====== Cc. Antya 19.19 ======

===== Text =====

> "prabhure kahiha āmāra koṭi namaskāra
> ei nivedana tāṅra caraṇe āmāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:k:kahiha]]---just inform Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:k:koṭi]] [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---hundreds and thousands of obeisances; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---this is the submission; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---His; [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---unto the lotus feet; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My.

===== Translation =====

**In His sonnet, Advaita Prabhu first offered His obeisances hundreds and thousands of times unto the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He then submitted the following statement at His lotus feet.**
