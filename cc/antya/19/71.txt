====== Cc. Antya 19.71 ======

===== Text =====

> śaṅkara karena prabhura pāda-samvāhana
> ghumāñā paḍena, taiche karena śayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:samvāhana]]---massaging the legs; [[synonyms:g:ghumāñā]] [[synonyms:p:paḍena]]---he falls asleep; [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---he lies down.

===== Translation =====

**Śaṅkara massaged the legs of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, but while massaging he would fall asleep and thus lie down.**
