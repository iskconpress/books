====== CC Antya 19.93 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhi he, kṛṣṇa-gandha jagat mātāya
> nārīra nāsāte paśe, sarva-kāla tāhāṅ vaise,
> kṛṣṇa-pāśa dhari' lañā yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhi]] [[synonyms:h:he]]---O My dear friend; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gandha]]---the fragrance of the body of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jagat]] [[synonyms:m:mātāya]]---enchants the whole world; [[synonyms:n:nārīra]]---of women; [[synonyms:n:nāsāte]]---in the nostrils; [[synonyms:p:paśe]]---enters; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:k:kāla]]---always; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---there; [[synonyms:v:vaise]]---remains; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]]---to the side of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---catching; [[synonyms:l:lañā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---takes one.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear friend, the scent of Kṛṣṇa's body enchants the entire world. It especially enters the nostrils of women and remains seated there. Thus it captures them and forcibly brings them to Kṛṣṇa.**
