====== CC Antya 19.66 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi tabe cintā pāilā mane
> bhakta-gaṇa lañā vicāra kailā āra dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:c:cintā]]---an anxiety or thought; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---got in his mind; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---among all the devotees; [[synonyms:v:vicāra]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---considered; [[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---the next day.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara was very anxious, but then he had an idea. The following day, he and the other devotees considered it together.**
