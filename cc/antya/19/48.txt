====== CC Antya 19.48 ======

===== Text =====

> are vidhi akaruṇa, dekhāñā kṛṣṇānana,
> netra-mana lobhāilā mora
> kṣaṇeke karite pāna, kāḍi' nilā anya sthāna,
> pāpa kaili 'datta-apahāra'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:are]]---oh; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---Providence; [[synonyms:a:akaruṇa]]---most unkind; [[synonyms:d:dekhāñā]]---showing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānana]]---the beautiful face of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:netra]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind and eyes; [[synonyms:l:lobhāilā]]---have made greedy; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeke]] [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---drinking only for a moment; [[synonyms:k:kāḍi']] [[synonyms:n:nilā]]---took it away; [[synonyms:a:anya]] [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---to another place; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]] [[synonyms:k:kaili]]---have performed a most sinful act; [[synonyms:d:datta]]-[[synonyms:a:apahāra]]---taking back things given in charity.

===== Translation =====

**"O Providence, you are so unkind! You reveal the beautiful face of Kṛṣṇa and make the mind and eyes greedy, but after they have drunk that nectar for only a moment, you whisk Kṛṣṇa away to another place. This is a great sin because you thus take away what you have given as charity.**
