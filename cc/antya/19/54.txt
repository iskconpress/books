====== CC Antya 19.54 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe svarūpa-rāma-rāya, kari' nānā upāya,
> mahāprabhura kare āśvāsana
> gāyena saṅgama-gīta, prabhura phirāilā cita,
> prabhura kichu sthira haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:r:rāya]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:k:kari']] [[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---devising many means; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:ā:āśvāsana]]---pacify; [[synonyms:g:gāyena]]---they sang; [[synonyms:s:saṅgama]]-[[synonyms:g:gīta]]---meeting songs; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:phirāilā]] [[synonyms:c:cita]]---transformed the heart; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---somewhat; [[synonyms:s:sthira]]---peaceful; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya then devised various means to pacify the Lord. They sang songs of meeting that transformed His heart and made His mind peaceful.**
