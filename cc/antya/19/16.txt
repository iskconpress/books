====== CC Antya 19.16 ======

===== Text =====

> ācāryādi bhakta-gaṇe mililā prasāda diyā
> mātā-ṭhāñi ājñā la-ilā māseka rahiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---beginning with Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---he met; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering the //prasāda// of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:m:mātā]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from mother Śacī; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---took permission to leave; [[synonyms:m:māseka]] [[synonyms:r:rahiyā]]---remaining for one month.

===== Translation =====

**He then met all the other devotees, headed by Advaita Ācārya, and gave them the prasāda of Jagannātha. After staying for one month, he took permission from mother Śacī to leave.**
