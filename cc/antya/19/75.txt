====== Cc. Antya 19.75 ======

===== Text =====

> ei līlā mahāprabhura raghunātha-dāsa
> gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣe kariyāche prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---this pastime; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:gaurāṅga]]-[[synonyms:s:stava]]-[[synonyms:k:kalpa]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣe]]---in his book known as //Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa//; [[synonyms:k:kariyāche]] [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---has described very nicely.

===== Translation =====

**This pastime of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's has been described very nicely by Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī in his book known as Gaurāṅga-stava-kalpavṛkṣa.**
