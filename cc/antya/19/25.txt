====== Cc. Antya 19.25 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena,---'ācārya haya pūjaka prabala
> āgama-śāstrera vidhi-vidhāne kuśala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]] [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:p:pūjaka]] [[synonyms:p:prabala]]---Advaita Ācārya is a great worshiper; [[synonyms:ā:āgama]]-[[synonyms:ś:śāstrera]]---of the Vedic literature; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]-[[synonyms:v:vidhāne]] [[synonyms:k:kuśala]]---very expert in the regulative principles.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Advaita Ācārya is a great worshiper of the Lord and is very expert in the regulative principles enjoined in the Vedic literatures.**
