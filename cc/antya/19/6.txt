====== CC Antya 19.6 ======

===== Text =====

> "nadīyā calaha, mātāre kahiha namaskāra
> āmāra nāme pāda-padma dhariha tāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nadīyā]] [[synonyms:c:calaha]]---start for Nadia; [[synonyms:m:mātāre]]---unto My mother; [[synonyms:k:kahiha]]---tell; [[synonyms:n:namaskāra]]---My obeisances; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in My name; [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]]---the lotus feet; [[synonyms:d:dhariha]]---catch; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāra]]---her.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, "Go to Nadia and offer My obeisances to My mother. Touch her lotus feet in My name.**
