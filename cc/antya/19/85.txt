====== CC Antya 19.85 ======

===== Text =====

> prati-vṛkṣa-vallī aiche bhramite bhramite
> aśokera tale kṛṣṇe dekhena ācambite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:v:vṛkṣa]]-[[synonyms:v:vallī]]---around each and every tree and creeper; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---in that way; [[synonyms:b:bhramite]] [[synonyms:b:bhramite]]---wandering; [[synonyms:a:aśokera]] [[synonyms:t:tale]]---underneath an //aśoka// tree; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:dekhena]]---He sees; [[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly.

===== Translation =====

**As He thus wandered around every tree and creeper, He came beneath an aśoka tree and suddenly saw Lord Kṛṣṇa.**
