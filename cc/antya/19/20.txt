====== CC Antya 19.20 ======

===== Text =====

> bāulake kahiha,---loka ha-ila bāula
> bāulake kahiha,---hāṭe nā vikāya cāula

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bāulake]] [[synonyms:k:kahiha]]---please inform Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is playing the part of a madman in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:l:loka]]---the people in general; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---have become; [[synonyms:b:bāula]]---also mad in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:b:bāulake]] [[synonyms:k:kahiha]]---again inform Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the //bāula//; [[synonyms:h:hāṭe]]---in the market; [[synonyms:n:nā]]---not; [[synonyms:v:vikāya]]---sells; [[synonyms:c:cāula]]---rice.

===== Translation =====

**"Please inform Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is acting like a madman, that everyone here has become mad like Him. Inform Him also that in the marketplace, rice is no longer in demand.**
