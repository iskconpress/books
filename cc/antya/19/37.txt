====== CC Antya 19.37 ======

===== Text =====

> sakhi he, kothā kṛṣṇa, karāha daraśana
> kṣaṇeke yāhāra mukha, nā dekhile phāṭe buka,
> śīghra dekhāha, nā rahe jīvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sakhi]] [[synonyms:h:he]]---O My dear friend; [[synonyms:k:kothā]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---where is Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karāha]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---please let Me see Him; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇeke]]---within a moment; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---whose; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---face; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhile]]---if not seeing; [[synonyms:p:phāṭe]] [[synonyms:b:buka]]---My heart is breaking; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---quickly; [[synonyms:d:dekhāha]]---show; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:r:rahe]] [[synonyms:j:jīvana]]---My life is expiring.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear friend, where is Kṛṣṇa? Kindly let Me see Him. My heart breaks at not seeing His face even for a moment. Kindly show Him to Me immediately; otherwise I cannot live.**
