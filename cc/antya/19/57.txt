====== CC Antya 19.57 ======

===== Text =====

> premāveśe mahāprabhura gara-gara mana
> nāma-saṅkīrtana kari' karena jāgaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in greatly ecstatic emotion; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gara]]-[[synonyms:g:gara]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind was fully overwhelmed; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa //mantra//; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:j:jāgaraṇa]]---wakefulness.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed awake all night, chanting the Hare Kṛṣṇa mahā-mantra, His mind overwhelmed by spiritual ecstasy.**
