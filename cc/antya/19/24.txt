====== CC Antya 19.24 ======

===== Text =====

> jāniyāo svarūpa gosāñi prabhure puchila
> 'ei tarajāra artha bujhite nārila'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jāniyāo]]---although knowing; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:p:puchila]]---inquired from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:tarajāra]] [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning of this sonnet; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:n:nārila]]---I was not able.

===== Translation =====

**Although he knew the secret, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī inquired from the Lord, "What is the meaning of this sonnet? I could not understand it."**
