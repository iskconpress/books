====== CC Antya 19.1 ======

===== Text =====

> vande taṁ kṛṣṇa-caitanyaṁ
> mātṛ-bhakta-śiromaṇim
> pralapya mukha-saṅgharṣī
> madhūdyāne lalāsa yaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanyam]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mātṛ]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]---of great devotees of mothers; [[synonyms:ś:śiromaṇim]]---the crown jewel; [[synonyms:p:pralapya]]---talking like a madman; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅgharṣī]]---who used to rub His face; [[synonyms:m:madhu]]-[[synonyms:u:udyāne]]---in the garden known as Jagannātha-vallabha; [[synonyms:l:lalāsa]]---enjoyed; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the most exalted of all devotees of mothers, spoke like a madman and rubbed His face against the walls. Overwhelmed by emotions of ecstatic love, He would sometimes enter the Jagannātha-vallabha garden to perform His pastimes. I offer my respectful obeisances unto Him.**
