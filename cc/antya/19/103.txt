====== CC Antya 19.103 ======

===== Text =====

> alaukika kṛṣṇa-līlā, divya-śakti tāra
> tarkera gocara nahe caritra yāhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:alaukika]]---uncommon; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlā]]---pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:ś:śakti]]---transcendental potency; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:t:tarkera]]---of argument; [[synonyms:g:gocara]]---within the purview; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:c:caritra]]---the characteristics; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of which.

===== Translation =====

**The pastimes of Lord Kṛṣṇa are uncommonly full of transcendental potency. It is a characteristic of such pastimes that they do not fall within the jurisdiction of experimental logic and arguments.**
