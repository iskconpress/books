====== CC Antya 19.97 ======

===== Text =====

> sei gandha-vaśa nāsā, sadā kare gandhera āśā,
> kabhu pāya, kabhu nāhi pāya
> pāile piyā peṭa bhare, piṅa piṅa tabu kare,
> nā pāile tṛṣṇāya mari' yāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]-[[synonyms:v:vaśa]]---under the control of the fragrance; [[synonyms:n:nāsā]]---the nostrils; [[synonyms:s:sadā]]---always; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---make; [[synonyms:g:gandhera]]---for the fragrance; [[synonyms:ā:āśā]]---hope; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---sometimes they obtain; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---sometimes do not obtain; [[synonyms:p:pāile]]---if obtaining; [[synonyms:p:piyā]]---drinking; [[synonyms:p:peṭa]]---the belly; [[synonyms:b:bhare]]---fills; [[synonyms:p:piṅa]]---let me drink; [[synonyms:p:piṅa]]---let me drink; [[synonyms:t:tabu]]---still; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---they hanker; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāile]]---if they do not get; [[synonyms:t:tṛṣṇāya]]---out of thirst; [[synonyms:m:mari']] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---they die.

===== Translation =====

**"Falling completely under its influence, the nostrils yearn for it continuously, although sometimes they obtain it and sometimes not. When they do they drink their fill, though they still want more and more, but if they don't, out of thirst they die.**
