====== CC Antya 19.30 ======

===== Text =====

> sei dina haite prabhura āra daśā ha-ila
> kṛṣṇera viccheda-daśā dviguṇa bāḍila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:d:dine]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from that day on; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---another; [[synonyms:d:daśā]]---condition; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---from Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:viccheda]]-[[synonyms:d:daśā]]---the condition of separation; [[synonyms:d:dvi]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---twice; [[synonyms:b:bāḍila]]---increased.

===== Translation =====

**From that day on, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's emotional state changed markedly; His feelings of separation from Kṛṣṇa doubled in intensity.**
