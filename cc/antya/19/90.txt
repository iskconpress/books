====== Cc. Antya 19.90 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-gandha-lubdhā rādhā sakhīre ye kahilā
> sei śloka paḍi' prabhu artha karilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:g:gandha]]---the scent of the body of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:l:lubdhā]]---hankering after; [[synonyms:r:rādhā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:s:sakhīre]]---to the //gopī// friends; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---whatever She said; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reciting; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:artha]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---explained its meaning.

===== Translation =====

**Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī expressed to Her gopī friends how She hankers for the transcendental scent of Kṛṣṇa's body. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited that same verse and made its meaning clear.**
