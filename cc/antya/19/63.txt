====== CC Antya 19.63 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena,---"udvege ghare nā pāri rahite
> dvāra cāhi' buli' śīghra bāhira ha-ite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:u:udvege]]---out of great agitation; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---within the room; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---I was not able; [[synonyms:r:rahite]]---to stay; [[synonyms:d:dvāra]] [[synonyms:c:cāhi']]---looking for the door; [[synonyms:b:buli']]---wandering; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---quickly; [[synonyms:b:bāhira]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---to get out.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "I was in such anxiety that I could not stay in the room. I wanted to go out, and therefore I wandered about the room, looking for the door.**
