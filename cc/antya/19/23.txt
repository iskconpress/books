====== Cc. Antya 19.23 ======

===== Text =====

> tarajā śuni' mahāprabhu īṣat hāsilā
> 'tāṅra yei ājñā'---bali' mauna dharilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tarajā]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing the sonnet; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ī:īṣat]] [[synonyms:h:hāsilā]]---quietly smiled; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---that is His order; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:m:mauna]] [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---became silent.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing the equivocal sonnet by Advaita Ācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu quietly smiled. "That is His order," He said. Then He fell silent.**
