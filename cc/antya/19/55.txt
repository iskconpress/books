====== CC Antya 19.55 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata vilapite ardha-rātri gela
> gambhīrāte svarūpa-gosāñi prabhure śoyāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:v:vilapite]]---speaking in lamentation; [[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:r:rātri]] [[synonyms:g:gela]]---half the night passed; [[synonyms:g:gambhīrāte]]---in the room known as the Gambhīrā; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:ś:śoyāila]]---made the Lord lie down.

===== Translation =====

**As Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lamented in this way, half the night passed. Then Svarūpa Dāmodara made the Lord lie down in the room known as the Gambhīrā.**
