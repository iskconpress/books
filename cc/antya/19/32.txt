====== Cc. Antya 19.32 ======

===== Text =====

> ācambite sphure kṛṣṇera mathurā-gamana
> udghūrṇā-daśā haila unmāda-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:ācambite]]---suddenly; [[synonyms:s:sphure]]---there awoke; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:g:gamana]]---the departure for Mathurā; [[synonyms:u:udghūrṇā]]-[[synonyms:d:daśā]]---the ecstatic condition known as //udghūrṇā//; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was; [[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---the symptom of madness.

===== Translation =====

**Suddenly there awoke within Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu the scene of Lord Kṛṣṇa's departure to Mathurā, and He began exhibiting the symptom of ecstatic madness known as udghūrṇā.**
