====== Cc. Antya 19.26 ======

===== Text =====

> upāsanā lāgi' devera karena āvāhana
> pūjā lāgi' kata kāla karena nirodhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:upāsanā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for worshiping the Deity; [[synonyms:d:devera]]---of the Lord; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:ā:āvāhana]]---invites to come; [[synonyms:p:pūjā]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---to perform the worship; [[synonyms:k:kata]] [[synonyms:k:kāla]]---for some time; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nirodhana]]---He keeps the Deity.

===== Translation =====

**"Advaita Ācārya invites the Lord to come and be worshiped, and to perform the worship He keeps the Deity for some time.**
