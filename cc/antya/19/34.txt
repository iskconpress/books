====== CC Antya 19.34 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yena viśākhāre rādhikā puchilā
> sei śloka paḍi' pralāpa karite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:v:viśākhāre]]---unto Viśākhā; [[synonyms:r:rādhikā]]---Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---that verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍi']]---reading; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---talking like a crazy man; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to do.

===== Translation =====

**Just as Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī inquired from Her personal friend Viśākhā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, reading that very verse, began speaking like a madman.**
