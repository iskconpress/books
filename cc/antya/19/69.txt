====== Cc. Antya 19.69 ======

===== Text =====

> 'prabhu-pādopādhāna' bali' tāṅra nāma ha-ila
> pūrve vidure yena śrī-śuka varṇila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:u:upādhāna]]---the pillow of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's legs; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---calling as such; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---his name; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---became; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:v:vidure]]---Vidura; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:ś:śuka]] [[synonyms:v:varṇila]]---Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī described.

===== Translation =====

**Śaṅkara became celebrated by the name "the pillow of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu." He was like Vidura, as Śukadeva Gosvāmī previously described him.**
