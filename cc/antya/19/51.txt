====== CC Antya 19.51 ======

===== Text =====

> saba tyaji' bhaji yāṅre, sei āpana-hāte māre,
> nārī-vadhe kṛṣṇera nāhi bhaya
> tāṅra lāgi' āmi mari, ulaṭi' nā cāhe hari,
> kṣaṇa-mātre bhāṅgila praṇaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:t:tyaji']]---leaving everything aside; [[synonyms:b:bhaji]] [[synonyms:y:yāṅre]]---the person whom I worship; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that person; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---by His own hand; [[synonyms:m:māre]]---kills; [[synonyms:n:nārī]]-[[synonyms:v:vadhe]]---in killing one woman; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---there is no fear; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:l:lāgi']]---for Him; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]] [[synonyms:m:mari]]---I die; [[synonyms:u:ulaṭi']]---turning back; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:cāhe]] [[synonyms:h:hari]]---Kṛṣṇa does not look; [[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:mātre]]---within a moment; [[synonyms:b:bhāṅgila]]---has broken; [[synonyms:p:praṇaya]]---all loving affairs.

===== Translation =====

**"He for whom I have left everything is personally killing Me with His own hands. Kṛṣṇa has no fear of killing women. Indeed, I am dying for Him, but He doesn't even turn back to look at Me. Within a moment, He has broken off our loving affairs.**
