====== CC Antya 19.3 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate mahāprabhu kṛṣṇa-premāveśe
> unmāda-pralāpa kare rātri-divase

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āveśe]]---in ecstatic emotional love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:u:unmāda]]---madness; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---and crazy talk; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:divase]]---throughout the entire day and night.

===== Translation =====

**In the ecstasy of love of Kṛṣṇa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus behaved like a madman, talking insanely all day and night.**
