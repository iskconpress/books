====== CC Antya 19.98 ======

===== Text =====

> madana-mohana-nāṭa, pasāri gandhera hāṭa,
> jagan-nārī-grāhake lobhāya
> vinā-mūlye deya gandha, gandha diyā kare andha,
> ghara yāite patha nāhi pāya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madana]]-[[synonyms:m:mohana]]-[[synonyms:n:nāṭa]]---the dramatic player Madana-mohana; [[synonyms:p:pasāri]]---a dealer; [[synonyms:g:gandhera]] [[synonyms:h:hāṭa]]---in a market of scents; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]-[[synonyms:n:nārī]]---of the women of the whole world; [[synonyms:g:grāhake]]---to the customers; [[synonyms:l:lobhāya]]---attracts; [[synonyms:v:vinā]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlye]]---without a price; [[synonyms:d:deya]]---distributes; [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---the scent; [[synonyms:g:gandha]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---delivering the scent; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:a:andha]]---makes the customers blind; [[synonyms:g:ghara]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to return home; [[synonyms:p:patha]]---the path; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---do not find.

===== Translation =====

**"The dramatic actor Madana-mohana has opened a shop of scents that attract the women of the world to be His customers. He delivers the scents freely, but they make the women all so blind they cannot find the path returning home."**
