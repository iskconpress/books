====== CC Antya 19.28 ======

===== Text =====

> mahā-yogeśvara ācārya---tarajāte samartha
> āmiha bujhite nāri tarajāra artha'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogeśvara]]---the greatest mystic; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:t:tarajāte]] [[synonyms:s:samartha]]---very expert in writing sonnets; [[synonyms:ā:āmiha]]---and yet I; [[synonyms:b:bujhite]]---to understand; [[synonyms:n:nāri]]---am not able; [[synonyms:t:tarajāra]]---of the sonnet; [[synonyms:a:artha]]---the meaning.

===== Translation =====

**"Advaita Ācārya is a great mystic. No one can understand Him. He is expert in writing sonnets that even I Myself cannot understand."**
