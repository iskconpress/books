====== CC Antya 19.33 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmānandera galā dhari' karena pralāpana
> svarūpe puchena māni' nija-sakhī-gaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmānandera]]---of Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:g:galā]] [[synonyms:d:dhari']]---holding the neck; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:p:pralāpana]]---begins talking like a crazy man; [[synonyms:s:svarūpe]] [[synonyms:p:puchena]]---inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara; [[synonyms:m:māni']]---accepting; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sakhī]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---as a //gopī// friend.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke like a madman, holding Rāmānanda Rāya by the neck, and He questioned Svarūpa Dāmodara, thinking him to be His gopī friend.**
