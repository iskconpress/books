====== CC Antya 19.56 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhure śoyāñā rāmānanda gelā ghare
> svarūpa, govinda śuilā gambhīrāra dvāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śoyāñā]]---making lie down; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāmānanda Rāya; [[synonyms:g:gelā]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---returned to his home; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:ś:śuilā]]---lay down; [[synonyms:g:gambhīrāra]] [[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door of the Gambhīrā.

===== Translation =====

**After the Lord was made to lie down, Rāmānanda Rāya returned home, and Svarūpa Dāmodara and Govinda lay down at the door of the Gambhīrā.**
