====== Cc. Antya 19.43 ======

===== Text =====

> mora sei kalā-nidhi, prāṇa-rakṣā-mahauṣadhi,
> sakhi, mora teṅho suhṛttama
> deha jīye tāṅhā vine, dhik ei jīvane,
> vidhi kare eta viḍambana!"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mora]]---of Me; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:k:kalā]]-[[synonyms:n:nidhi]]---reservoir of art and culture; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:r:rakṣa]]-[[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:a:auṣaudhi]]---the panacea for saving My life; [[synonyms:s:sakhi]]---O My dear friend; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---He; [[synonyms:s:suhṛt]]-[[synonyms:t:tama]]---the best of friends; [[synonyms:d:deha]] [[synonyms:j:jīye]]---My body lives; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhā]] [[synonyms:v:vine]]---without Him; [[synonyms:d:dhik]]---condemnation; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:j:jīvane]]---to this life; [[synonyms:v:vidhi]]---Providence; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:v:viḍambana]]---so much cheating.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa is the reservoir of art and culture, and He is the panacea that saves My life. O My dear friend, since I live without Him, who is the best among My friends, I condemn the duration of My life. I think that Providence has cheated Me in many ways.**
