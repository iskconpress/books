====== CC Antya 19.94 ======

===== Text =====

> netra-nābhi, vadana, kara-yuga caraṇa,
> ei aṣṭa-padma kṛṣṇa-aṅge
> karpūra-lipta kamala, tāra yaiche parimala,
> sei gandha aṣṭa-padma-saṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:netra]]---the eyes; [[synonyms:n:nābhi]]---the navel; [[synonyms:v:vadana]]---the face; [[synonyms:k:kara]]-[[synonyms:y:yuga]]---the palms; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---the feet; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---these; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]---eight; [[synonyms:p:padma]]---lotus flowers; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---in the body of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:karpūra]]---with camphor; [[synonyms:l:lipta]]---smeared; [[synonyms:k:kamala]]---the lotus flower; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---of that; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]]---like; [[synonyms:p:parimala]]---the fragrance; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:g:gandha]]---that fragrance; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:padma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---is associated with the eight lotus flowers.

===== Translation =====

**"Kṛṣṇa's eyes, navel and face, hands and feet are like eight lotus flowers on His body. From those eight lotuses emanates a fragrance like a mixture of camphor and lotus. That is the scent associated with His body.**
