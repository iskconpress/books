====== CC Antya 19.65 ======

===== Text =====

> unmāda-daśāya prabhura sthira nahe mana
> yei kare, yei bole saba,---unmāda-lakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:d:daśāya]]---in such a state of madness; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sthira]] [[synonyms:n:nahe]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind is not steady; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---whatever He does; [[synonyms:y:yei]] [[synonyms:b:bole]]---whatever He says; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:u:unmāda]]-[[synonyms:l:lakṣaṇa]]---simply symptoms of madness.

===== Translation =====

**In this state of madness, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind was unsteady. Whatever He said or did was all symptomatic of madness.**
