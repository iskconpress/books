====== CC Antya 19.73 ======

===== Text =====

> nirantara ghumāya śaṅkara śīghra-cetana
> vasi' pāda cāpi' kare rātri-jāgaraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---incessantly; [[synonyms:g:ghumāya]]---sleeps; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very quickly; [[synonyms:c:cetana]]---waking up; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:p:pāda]] [[synonyms:c:cāpi']]---massaging the legs; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:j:jāgaraṇa]]---keeps awake at night.

===== Translation =====

**Śaṅkara Paṇḍita would always fall asleep, but he would quickly awaken, sit up and again begin massaging the legs of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. In this way he would stay awake the entire night.**
