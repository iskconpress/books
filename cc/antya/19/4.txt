====== CC Antya 19.4 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura atyanta priya paṇḍita-jagadānanda
> yāhāra caritre prabhu pāyena ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]]---very; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---affectionate; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]] [[synonyms:c:caritre]]---in whose activities; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pāyena]]---gets; [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---great pleasure.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita was a very dear devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The Lord derived great pleasure from his activities.**
