====== CC Antya 19.36 ======

===== Text =====

> "vrajendra-kula---dugdha-sindhu, kṛṣṇa tāhe pūrṇa indu,
> janmi' kailā jagat ujora
> kānty-amṛta yebā piye, nirantara piyā jiye,
> vraja-janera nayana-cakora

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:vrajendra]]-[[synonyms:k:kula]]---the dynasty of Mahārāja Nanda in Vrajabhūmi; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]---like the ocean of milk; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tāhe]]---in that; [[synonyms:p:pūrṇa]]---full; [[synonyms:i:indu]]---moon; [[synonyms:j:janmi]]---arising; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---has made; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the whole world; [[synonyms:u:ujora]]---illuminated; [[synonyms:k:kānti]]-[[synonyms:a:amṛta]]---the nectar of His bodily luster; [[synonyms:y:yebā]] [[synonyms:p:piye]]---anyone who drinks; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---always; [[synonyms:p:piyā]]---drinking; [[synonyms:j:jiye]]---sustains life; [[synonyms:v:vraja]]-[[synonyms:j:janera]]---of the inhabitants of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:n:nayana]]-[[synonyms:c:cakora]]---eyes that are like //cakora// birds.

===== Translation =====

**"The family of Mahārāja Nanda is just like an ocean of milk, wherein Lord Kṛṣṇa has arisen like the full moon to illuminate the entire universe. The eyes of the residents of Vraja are like cakora birds that continuously drink the nectar of His bodily luster and thus live peacefully.**
