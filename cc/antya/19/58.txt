====== Cc. Antya 19.58 ======

===== Text =====

> virahe vyākula prabhu udvege uṭhilā
> gambhīrāra bhittye mukha ghaṣite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in the lamentation of separation; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]]---very agitated; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:udvege]]---in great anxiety; [[synonyms:u:uṭhilā]]---stood up; [[synonyms:g:gambhīrāra]]---of the Gambhīrā; [[synonyms:b:bhittye]]---on the walls; [[synonyms:m:mukha]]---face; [[synonyms:g:ghaṣite]]---to rub; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began.

===== Translation =====

**Feeling separation from Kṛṣṇa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was so distraught that in great anxiety He stood up and began rubbing His face against the walls of the Gambhīrā.**
