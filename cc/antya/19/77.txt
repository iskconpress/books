====== Cc. Antya 19.77 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata mahāprabhu rātri-divase
> prema-sindhu-magna rahe, kabhu ḍube, bhāse

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:divase]]---day and night; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:m:magna]] [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remains merged in the ocean of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:ḍ:ḍube]]---sometimes drowns; [[synonyms:b:bhāse]]---floats.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in this way stayed immersed day and night in an ocean of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa. Sometimes He was submerged, and sometimes He floated.**
