====== Cc. Antya 19.89 ======

===== Text =====

> nirantara nāsāya paśe kṛṣṇa-parimala
> gandha āsvādite prabhu ha-ilā pāgala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---incessantly; [[synonyms:n:nāsāya]]---in the nostrils; [[synonyms:p:paśe]]---enters; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:parimala]]---the scent of Kṛṣṇa's body; [[synonyms:g:gandha]] [[synonyms:ā:āsvādite]]---to relish the fragrance; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]] [[synonyms:p:pāgala]]---became mad.

===== Translation =====

**The scent of Kṛṣṇa's body, however, incessantly entered His nostrils, and the Lord became mad to relish it.**
