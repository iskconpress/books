====== CC Antya 19.109 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhu-nityānanda, doṅhāra dāsera dāsa
> yāre kṛpā karena, tāra haya ithe viśvāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāra]] [[synonyms:d:dāsera]] [[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---I am a servant of the servant of the servants of these two personalities; [[synonyms:y:yāre]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpā]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---if anyone is favored by Them; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:h:haya]]---he certainly maintains; [[synonyms:i:ithe]] [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---faith in all these affairs.

===== Translation =====

**If one becomes a servant of the servants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu and is favored by Them, he can believe in all these discourses.**
