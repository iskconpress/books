====== CC Antya 19.81 ======

===== Text =====

> puṣpa-gandha lañā vahe malaya-pavana
> 'guru' hañā taru-latāya śikhāya nācana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:puṣpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gandha]]---the fragrance of the flowers; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:v:vahe]]---blows; [[synonyms:m:malaya]]-[[synonyms:p:pavana]]---the mild breeze; [[synonyms:g:guru]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming the spiritual master; [[synonyms:t:taru]]-[[synonyms:l:latāya]]---to the trees and creepers; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāya]]---instructs; [[synonyms:n:nācana]]---dancing.

===== Translation =====

**A mild breeze was blowing, carrying the fragrance of aromatic flowers. The breeze had become a guru and was teaching all the trees and creepers how to dance.**
