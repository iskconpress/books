====== CC Antya 19.12 ======

===== Text =====

> gopa-līlāya pāilā yei prasāda-vasane
> mātāre pāṭhāna tāhā purīra vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopa]]-[[synonyms:l:līlāya]]---in His pastimes as a cowherd boy; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---got; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnant; [[synonyms:v:vasane]]---clothing; [[synonyms:m:mātāre]]---unto His mother; [[synonyms:p:pāṭhāna]]---sent; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---on the order of Paramānanda Purī.

===== Translation =====

**Following the order of Paramānanda Purī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sent His mother the prasāda clothing left by Lord Jagannātha after His pastimes as a cowherd boy.**
