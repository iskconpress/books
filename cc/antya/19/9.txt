====== Cc. Antya 19.9 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāra sevā chāḍi' āmi kariluṅ sannyāsa
> 'bāula' hañā āmi kailuṅ dharma-nāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up your service; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kariluṅ]]---accepted; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsa]]---the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:b:bāula]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---becoming mad; [[synonyms:ā:āmi]]---I; [[synonyms:k:kailuṅ]]---did; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destruction of religion.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'I have given up service to you and have accepted the vow of sannyāsa. I have thus become mad and have destroyed the principles of religion.**
