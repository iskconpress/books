====== CC Antya 19.72 ======

===== Text =====

> ughāḍa-aṅge paḍiyā śaṅkara nidrā yāya
> prabhu uṭhi' āpana-kāṅthā tāhāre jaḍāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:u:ughāḍa]]-[[synonyms:a:aṅge]]---without a covering over the body; [[synonyms:p:paḍiyā]]---lying down; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkara]]---Śaṅkara; [[synonyms:n:nidrā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]]---goes to sleep; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']]---getting up; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:k:kāṅthā]]---His own quilt; [[synonyms:t:tāhāre]] [[synonyms:j:jaḍāya]]---wraps him with.

===== Translation =====

**He would lie asleep without a covering on his body, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would get up and wrap him with His own quilt.**
