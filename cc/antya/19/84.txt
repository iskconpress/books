====== CC Antya 19.84 ======

===== Text =====

> "lalita-lavaṅga-latā" pada gāoyāñā
> nṛtya kari' bulena prabhu nija-gaṇa lañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lalita]]-[[synonyms:l:lavaṅga]]-[[synonyms:l:latā]]---beginning with the words //lalita-lavaṅga-latā//; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---the verse; [[synonyms:g:gāoyāñā]]---making sing; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---dancing; [[synonyms:b:bulena]]---wanders; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by His personal associates.

===== Translation =====

**In this atmosphere, the Lord had His associates sing a verse from the Gīta-govinda, beginning with the words "lalita-lavaṅga-latā," as He danced and wandered about with them.**
