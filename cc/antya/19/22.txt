====== CC Antya 19.22 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' jagadānanda hāsite lāgilā
> nīlācale āsi' tabe prabhure kahilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:h:hāsite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to laugh; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---returning; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---he told all this to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**When he heard Advaita Ācārya's statement, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita began to laugh, and when he returned to Jagannātha Purī, Nīlācala, he informed Caitanya Mahāprabhu of everything.**
