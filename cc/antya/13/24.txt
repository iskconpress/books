====== CC Antya 13.24 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda kahe prabhura dhariyā caraṇa
> "pūrva haite icchā mora yāite vṛndāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---grasping the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---for a very long time; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---my; [[synonyms:y:yāite]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---to go to Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**Grasping the Lord's feet, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita then said, "For a long time I have desired to go to Vṛndāvana.**
