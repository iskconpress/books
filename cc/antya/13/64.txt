====== Cc. Antya 13.64 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata māsa dui rahilā vṛndāvane
> caitanya-viraha-duḥkha nā yāya sahane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:māsa]]---months; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---in Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:v:viraha]]---of separation from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yāya]] [[synonyms:s:sahane]]---could not tolerate.

===== Translation =====

**They passed two months in Vṛndāvana in this way. Finally they could no longer tolerate the unhappiness of separation from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
