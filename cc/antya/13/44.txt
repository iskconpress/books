====== CC Antya 13.44 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurāte āsi' mililā sanātane
> dui-janera saṅge duṅhe ānandita mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurāte]] [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---when he reached Mathurā; [[synonyms:m:mililā]] [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---he met Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:janera]]---of both; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in association; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:m:mane]]---very pleased within their minds.

===== Translation =====

**Finally Jagadānanda Paṇḍita reached Mathurā, where he met Sanātana Gosvāmī. They were very pleased to see each other.**
