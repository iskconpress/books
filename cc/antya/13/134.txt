====== CC Antya 13.134 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura datta mālā mananera kāle
> prasāda-kaḍāra saha bāndhi lena gale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:datta]]---given; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---//tulasī// garland; [[synonyms:m:mananera]]---of remembering; [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at the time; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:k:kaḍāra]]---the remnants of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with; [[synonyms:b:bāndhi]]---binding together; [[synonyms:l:lena]]---takes; [[synonyms:g:gale]]---on his neck.

===== Translation =====

**When Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī was absorbed in remembrance of Lord Kṛṣṇa, he would take the tulasī garland and the prasāda of Lord Jagannātha given to him by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, bind them together and wear them on his neck.**
