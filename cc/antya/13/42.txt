====== CC Antya 13.42 ======

===== Text =====

> saba bhakta-gaṇa-ṭhāñi ājñā māgilā
> vana-pathe cali' cali' vārāṇasī āilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from all the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---asked permission; [[synonyms:v:vana]]-[[synonyms:p:pathe]] [[synonyms:c:cali']] [[synonyms:c:cali']]---traversing the forest path; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---he reached Vārāṇasī.

===== Translation =====

**He took permission from all the devotees and then departed. Traveling on the forest path, he soon reached Vārāṇasī.**
