====== Cc. Antya 13.118 ======

===== Text =====

> pitā-mātā kāśī pāile udāsīna hañā
> punaḥ prabhura ṭhāñi āilā gṛhādi chāḍiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pitā]]-[[synonyms:m:mātā]]---the father and mother; [[synonyms:k:kāśī]] [[synonyms:p:pāile]]---when they passed away at Kāśī (Vārāṇasī); [[synonyms:u:udāsīna]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being indifferent; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned; [[synonyms:g:gṛha]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍiyā]]---leaving all relationships with home.

===== Translation =====

**Then his parents died at Kāśī [Vārāṇasī], and he became detached. He therefore returned to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, giving up all relationships with his home.**
