====== Cc. Antya 13.54 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' paṇḍitera mane krodha upajila
> bhātera hāṇḍi hāte lañā mārite āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]---anger; [[synonyms:u:upajila]]---arose; [[synonyms:b:bhātera]] [[synonyms:h:hāṇḍi]]---the cooking pot; [[synonyms:h:hāte]]---in his hand; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:m:mārite]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---was ready to beat.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita was immediately very angry and took a cooking pot in his hand, intending to beat Sanātana Gosvāmī.**
