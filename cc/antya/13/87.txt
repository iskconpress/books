====== CC Antya 13.87 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"govinda, mora saṅge rahibā
> yāhāṅ tāhāṅ mora rakṣāya sāvadhāna ha-ibā"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---My dear Govinda; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]] [[synonyms:r:rahibā]]---you should always remain with Me; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]]---anywhere and everywhere; [[synonyms:m:mora]]---My; [[synonyms:r:rakṣāya]]---for protection; [[synonyms:s:sāvadhāna]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ibā]]---you should be very careful.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "My dear Govinda, you should stay with Me always. There is danger anywhere and everywhere; therefore you should protect Me very carefully."**
