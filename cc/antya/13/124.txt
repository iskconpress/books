====== CC Antya 13.124 ======

===== Text =====

> sei mālā, chuṭā pāna prabhu tāṅre dilā
> 'iṣṭa-deva' kari' mālā dhariyā rākhilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---that garland; [[synonyms:c:chuṭā]] [[synonyms:p:pāna]]---the betel; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---delivered to him; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:d:deva]]---his worshipable Deity; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---accepting as; [[synonyms:m:mālā]]---that garland; [[synonyms:d:dhariyā]] [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave the garland and betel to Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa, who accepted them as a worshipable Deity, preserving them very carefully.**
