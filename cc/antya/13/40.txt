====== CC Antya 13.40 ======

===== Text =====

> āmiha āsitechi,---kahiha sanātane
> āmāra tare eka-sthāna yena kare vṛndāvane"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmiha]]---I also; [[synonyms:ā:āsitechi]]---am coming; [[synonyms:k:kahiha]] [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---inform Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:t:tare]]---for Me; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---one place; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---so; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---he may make; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana.

===== Translation =====

**"Inform Sanātana Gosvāmī that I am coming to Vṛndāvana for a second time and that he should therefore arrange a place for Me to stay."**
