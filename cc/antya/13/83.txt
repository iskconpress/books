====== CC Antya 13.83 ======

===== Text =====

> dhāñā yāyena prabhu, strī āche alpa dūre
> strī gāya' bali' govinda prabhure kailā kole

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dhāñā]]---very hastily; [[synonyms:y:yāyena]]---was going; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:strī]]---the woman; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---was; [[synonyms:a:alpa]] [[synonyms:d:dūre]]---within a short distance; [[synonyms:s:strī]] [[synonyms:g:gāya']]---a woman is singing; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---His personal servant; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:k:kole]]---held in his arms.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was running very rapidly, and the girl was only a short distance away. Just then Govinda caught the Lord in his arms and cried, "It is a woman singing!"**
