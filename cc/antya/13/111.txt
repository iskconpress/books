====== CC Antya 13.111 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmadāsa kailā tabe nīlācale vāsa
> paṭṭanāyaka-goṣṭhīke paḍāya 'kāvya-prakāśa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmadāsa]]---Rāmadāsa Viśvāsa; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]] [[synonyms:v:vāsa]]---residence at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:paṭṭanāyaka]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhīke]]---to the Paṭṭanāyaka family (the descendants of Bhavānanda Rāya); [[synonyms:p:paḍāya]]---teaches; [[synonyms:k:kāvya]]-[[synonyms:p:prakāśa]]---on the book //Kāvya-prakāśa.//

===== Translation =====

**Rāmadāsa Viśvāsa then took up residence in Jagannātha Purī and taught the Kāvya-prakāśa to the Paṭṭanāyaka family [the descendants of Bhavānanda Rāya].**
