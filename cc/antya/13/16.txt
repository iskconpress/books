====== Cc. Antya 13.16 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi āsi' paṇḍite kahilā
> śuni' jagadānanda mane mahā-duḥkha pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---returning; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍite]] [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---said to Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]] [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---felt great unhappiness.

===== Translation =====

**When Svarūpa Dāmodara returned and related all these incidents, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita felt very unhappy.**
