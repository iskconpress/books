====== CC Antya 13.70 ======

===== Text =====

> sei sthāna rākhilā gosāñi saṁskāra kariyā
> maṭhera āge rākhilā eka chāuni bāndhiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---that place; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept reserved; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:saṁskāra]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---cleansing and repairing; [[synonyms:m:maṭhera]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of the temple; [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---kept; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:c:chāuni]]---small hut; [[synonyms:b:bāndhiyā]]---erecting.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī kept the temple very clean and in good repair. In front of it he erected a small hut.**
