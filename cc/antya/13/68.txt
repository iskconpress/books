====== CC Antya 13.68 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda-paṇḍita calilā saba lañā
> vyākula hailā sanātana tāṅre vidāya diyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---proceeded; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking; [[synonyms:v:vyākula]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became very agitated; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---bidding farewell.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, bearing all these gifts, started on his journey. Sanātana Gosvāmī, however, was very agitated after bidding him farewell.**
