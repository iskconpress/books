====== Cc. Antya 13.11 ======

===== Text =====

> govindere puchena,---'ihā karāila kon jana?'
> jagadānandera nāma śuni' saṅkoca haila mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govindere]] [[synonyms:p:puchena]]---He inquired from Govinda; [[synonyms:i:ihā]]---this; [[synonyms:k:karāila]]---has made; [[synonyms:k:kon]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---what person; [[synonyms:j:jagadānandera]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---name; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:s:saṅkoca]]---afraid; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---was; [[synonyms:m:mana]]---mind.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord inquired from Govinda, "Who has made this?" When Govinda named Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was somewhat fearful.**
