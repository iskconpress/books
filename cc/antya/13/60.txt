====== Cc. Antya 13.60 ======

===== Text =====

> yāhā dekhibāre vastra mastake bāndhila
> sei apūrva prema ei pratyakṣa dekhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāhā]]---which; [[synonyms:d:dekhibāre]]---to see; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---the cloth; [[synonyms:m:mastake]] [[synonyms:b:bāndhila]]---I bound on my head; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:a:apūrva]] [[synonyms:p:prema]]---uncommon love; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:p:pratyakṣa]]---by direct experience; [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---I have seen.

===== Translation =====

**"My purpose in binding my head with the cloth has now been fulfilled because I have personally seen your uncommon love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
