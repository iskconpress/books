====== CC Antya 13.27 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñire paṇḍita kailā nivedana
> "pūrva haite vṛndāvana yāite mora mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñire]]---to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nivedana]]---made his petition; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---for a long time; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:m:mora]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---my mind.

===== Translation =====

**He then submitted a plea to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī. "For a very long time," he said, "I have wanted to go to Vṛndāvana.**
