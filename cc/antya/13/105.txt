====== CC Antya 13.105 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata prabhu-saṅge rahilā aṣṭa-māsa
> dine dine prabhura kṛpāya bāḍaye ullāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---eight months; [[synonyms:d:dine]] [[synonyms:d:dine]]---day after day; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:b:bāḍaye]] [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---felt increased jubilation.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa lived with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continuously for eight months, and by the Lord's mercy he felt increased transcendental happiness every day.**
