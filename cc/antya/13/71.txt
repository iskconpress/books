====== CC Antya 13.71 ======

===== Text =====

> śīghra cali' nīlācale gelā jagadānanda
> bhakta saha gosāñi hailā parama ānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śīghra]]---very quickly; [[synonyms:c:cali']]---going; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---arrived; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]] [[synonyms:s:saha]]---with His devotees; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became; [[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---very happy.

===== Translation =====

**Meanwhile, traveling very quickly, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita soon arrived in Jagannātha Purī, much to the joy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees.**
