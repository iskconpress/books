====== Cc. Antya 13.74 ======

===== Text =====

> saba dravya rākhilena, pīlu dilena bāṅṭiyā
> 'vṛndāvanera phala' bali' khāilā hṛṣṭa hañā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:d:dravya]]---gifts; [[synonyms:r:rākhilena]]---kept; [[synonyms:p:pīlu]]---the //pīlu// fruit; [[synonyms:d:dilena]]---gave; [[synonyms:b:bāṅṭiyā]]---distributing; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanera]] [[synonyms:p:phala]]---fruit from Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---because of; [[synonyms:k:khāilā]]---ate; [[synonyms:h:hṛṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---with great happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu kept all the gifts except the pīlu fruits, which He distributed to the devotees. Because the fruits were from Vṛndāvana, everyone ate them with great happiness.**
