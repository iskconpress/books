====== CC Antya 13.76 ======

===== Text =====

> mukhe tāra jhāla gela, jihvā kare jvālā
> vṛndāvanera 'pīlu' khāite ei eka līlā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mukhe]] [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---in their mouths; [[synonyms:j:jhāla]]---the taste of chili; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---went; [[synonyms:j:jihvā]]---the tongue; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:j:jvālā]]---was burning; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvanera]]---of Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:pīlu]]---the //pīlu// fruit; [[synonyms:k:khāite]]---eating; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:l:līlā]]---a pastime.

===== Translation =====

**The hot chili-like taste burned the tongues of those who chewed the seeds. The eating of pīlu fruits from Vṛndāvana was a pastime for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
