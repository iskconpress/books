====== CC Antya 13.89 ======

===== Text =====

> ethā tapana-miśra-putra raghunātha-bhaṭṭācārya
> prabhure dekhite calilā chāḍi' sarva kārya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ethā]]---on the other hand; [[synonyms:t:tapana]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:p:putra]]---the son of Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]]---to meet; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---proceeded; [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up; [[synonyms:s:sarva]] [[synonyms:k:kārya]]---all duties.

===== Translation =====

**During this time, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭācārya, the son of Tapana Miśra, gave up all his duties and left home, intending to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
