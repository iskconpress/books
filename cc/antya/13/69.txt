====== CC Antya 13.69 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura nimitta eka-sthāna mane vicārila
> dvādaśāditya-ṭilāya eka 'maṭha' pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nimitta]]---for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---one place; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---within the mind; [[synonyms:v:vicārila]]---considered; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśāditya]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭilāya]]---on the highland named Dvādaśāditya; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:m:maṭha]]---temple; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got.

===== Translation =====

**Soon afterward, Sanātana Gosvāmī selected a place where Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu could stay while in Vṛndāvana. It was a temple in the highlands named Dvādaśāditya-ṭilā.**
