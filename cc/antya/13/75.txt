====== CC Antya 13.75 ======

===== Text =====

> ye keha jāne, āṅṭi cuṣite lāgila
> ye nā jāne gauḍiyā pīlu cāvāñā khāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]---those who; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---some; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knew; [[synonyms:ā:āṅṭi]]---seeds; [[synonyms:c:cuṣite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to lick; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---those who; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---did not know; [[synonyms:g:gauḍiyā]]---Bengali devotees; [[synonyms:p:pīlu]]---the //pīlu// fruit; [[synonyms:c:cāvāñā]]---chewing; [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate.

===== Translation =====

**Those devotees who were familiar with pīlu fruits sucked on the seeds, but the Bengali devotees who did not know what they were chewed the seeds and swallowed them.**
