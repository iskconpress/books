====== CC Antya 13.117 ======

===== Text =====

> cāri-vatsara ghare pitā-mātāra sevā kailā
> vaiṣṇava-paṇḍita-ṭhāñi bhāgavata paḍilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:v:vatsara]]---for four years; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home; [[synonyms:p:pitā]]-[[synonyms:m:mātāra]]---of the father and mother; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---rendered service; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇava]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from a self-realized, advanced Vaiṣṇava; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]] [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]]---he studied //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.//

===== Translation =====

**In accordance with the instructions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he continuously rendered service to his mother and father for four years. He also regularly studied the Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam from a self-realized Vaiṣṇava.**
