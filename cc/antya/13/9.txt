====== CC Antya 13.9 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñike kahe jagadānanda
> 'āji āpane yāñā prabhure karāiha śayana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñike]]---to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---says; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āji]]---today; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---Your Honor; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāiha]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---cause to lie down.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda said to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, "Today please personally persuade Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to lie down on the bed."**
