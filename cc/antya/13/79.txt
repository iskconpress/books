====== CC Antya 13.79 ======

===== Text =====

> gujjarī-rāgiṇī lañā sumadhura-svare
> 'gīta-govinda'-pada gāya jaga-mana hare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gujjarī]]-[[synonyms:r:rāgiṇī]]---the //gujjarī// mode of singing; [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---accompanied by; [[synonyms:s:sumadhura]]-[[synonyms:s:svare]]---in a very sweet voice; [[synonyms:g:gīta]]-[[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Gīta-govinda, by Jayadeva Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:pada]]---verses; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---sings; [[synonyms:j:jaga]]-[[synonyms:m:mana]]---the mind of the entire world; [[synonyms:h:hare]]---attracts.

===== Translation =====

**She sang a gujjarī tune in a very sweet voice, and because the subject was Jayadeva Gosvāmī's Gīta-govinda, the song attracted the attention of the entire world.**
