====== Cc. Antya 13.95 ======

===== Text =====

> nānā sevā kari' kare pāda-samvāhana
> tāte raghunāthera haya saṅkucita mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nānā]] [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---serving in various ways; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:samvāhana]]---massaged his legs; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---because of this; [[synonyms:r:raghunāthera]]---of Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:saṅkucita]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---hesitation in the mind.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmadāsa served Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa in various ways, even massaging his legs. Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa felt some hesitation in accepting all this service.**
