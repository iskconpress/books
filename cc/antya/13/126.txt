====== Cc. Antya 13.126 ======

===== Text =====

> rūpa-gosāñira sabhāya karena bhāgavata-paṭhana
> bhāgavata paḍite preme āulāya tāṅra mana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]] [[synonyms:s:sabhāya]]---in the assembly of Rūpa, Sanātana and other Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]]-[[synonyms:p:paṭhana]]---recitation of //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:b:bhāgavata]] [[synonyms:p:paḍite]]---while reciting //Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam//; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:ā:āulāya]]---becomes overwhelmed; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:m:mana]]---his mind.

===== Translation =====

**When reciting Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam in the company of Rūpa and Sanātana, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa would be overwhelmed with ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
