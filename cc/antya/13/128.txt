====== CC Antya 13.128 ======

===== Text =====

> pika-svara-kaṇṭha, tāte rāgera vibhāga
> eka-śloka paḍite phirāya tina-cāri rāga

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pika]]-[[synonyms:s:svara]]-[[synonyms:k:kaṇṭha]]---a very sweet voice like a cuckoo's; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---above that; [[synonyms:r:rāgera]]---of tunes; [[synonyms:v:vibhāga]]---division; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ś:śloka]]---one verse; [[synonyms:p:paḍite]]---reciting; [[synonyms:p:phirāya]]---changes; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:r:rāga]]---three or four different tunes.

===== Translation =====

**His voice was as sweet as a cuckoo's, and he would recite each verse of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam in three or four tunes. Thus his recitations were very sweet to hear.**
