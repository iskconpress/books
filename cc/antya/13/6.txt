====== CC Antya 13.6 ======

===== Text =====

> dekhi' saba bhakta-gaṇa mahā-duḥkha pāya
> sahite nāre jagadānanda, sṛjilā upāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---great unhappiness; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get; [[synonyms:s:sahite]]---to tolerate; [[synonyms:n:nāre]]---was unable; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:s:sṛjilā]] [[synonyms:u:upāya]]---devised a means.

===== Translation =====

**All the devotees felt very unhappy to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in pain. Indeed, they could not tolerate it. Then Jagadānanda Paṇḍita devised a remedy.**
