====== CC Antya 13.20 ======

===== Text =====

> tāte śayana karena prabhu,---dekhi' sabe sukhī
> jagadānanda---bhitare krodha bāhire mahā-duḥkhī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāte]]---on that; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---lies down; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:s:sabe]] [[synonyms:s:sukhī]]---everyone became happy; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:bhitare]]---within his mind; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]---angry; [[synonyms:b:bāhire]]---externally; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkhī]]---very unhappy.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone was happy to see the Lord lie down on that bed, but Jagadānanda was inwardly angry, and externally he appeared very unhappy.**
