====== Cc. Antya 13.120 ======

===== Text =====

> "āmāra ājñāya, raghunātha, yāha vṛndāvane
> tāhāṅ yāñā raha rūpa-sanātana-sthāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āmāra]] [[synonyms:ā:ājñāya]]---upon My order; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---My dear Raghunātha; [[synonyms:y:yāha]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going there; [[synonyms:r:raha]]---remain; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---in the care of Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**"My dear Raghunātha, go to Vṛndāvana, following My instructions, and place yourself under the care of Rūpa and Sanātana Gosvāmīs.**
