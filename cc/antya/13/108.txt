====== CC Antya 13.108 ======

===== Text =====

> parama santoṣe prabhu karena bhojana
> prabhura avaśiṣṭa-pātra bhaṭṭera bhakṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:parama]] [[synonyms:s:santoṣe]]---in great satisfaction; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---eats; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avaśiṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:pātra]]---the plate of remnants; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭera]]---of Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---the eatables.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would accept with great satisfaction all the food he prepared. After the Lord was satisfied, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa would eat His remnants.**
