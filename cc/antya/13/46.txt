====== CC Antya 13.46 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātanera gophāte duṅhe rahe eka-ṭhāñi
> paṇḍita pāka karena devālaye yāi'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātanera]] [[synonyms:g:gophāte]]---in the cave where Sanātana Gosvāmī stayed; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---stay; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---in one place; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---cooks; [[synonyms:d:devālaye]] [[synonyms:y:yāi]]---going to a temple.

===== Translation =====

**They stayed in Sanātana Gosvāmī's cave, but Jagadānanda Paṇḍita would go cook for himself at a nearby temple.**
