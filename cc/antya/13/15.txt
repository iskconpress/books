====== CC Antya 13.15 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsī mānuṣa āmāra bhūmite śayana
> āmāre khāṭa-tūli-bālisa mastaka-muṇḍana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]] [[synonyms:m:mānuṣa]]---a person in the renounced order of life; [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My; [[synonyms:b:bhūmite]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying on the floor; [[synonyms:ā:āmāre]]---for Me; [[synonyms:k:khāṭa]]---bedstead; [[synonyms:t:tūli]]---quilt; [[synonyms:b:bālisa]]---pillow; [[synonyms:m:mastaka]]-[[synonyms:m:muṇḍana]]---a great shame.

===== Translation =====

**"I am in the renounced order, and therefore I must lie on the floor. For Me to use a bedstead, quilt or pillow would be very shameful."**
