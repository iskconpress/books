====== CC Antya 13.17 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñi tabe sṛjilā prakāra
> kadalīra śuṣka-patra ānilā apāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:s:sṛjilā]] [[synonyms:p:prakāra]]---devised a means; [[synonyms:k:kadalīra]]---of banana; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]-[[synonyms:p:patra]]---dry leaves; [[synonyms:ā:ānilā]]---brought; [[synonyms:a:apāra]]---in great quantity.

===== Translation =====

**Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī devised another method. First he secured a large quantity of dry banana leaves.**
