====== Cc. Antya 13.52 ======

===== Text =====

> rātula vastra dekhi' paṇḍita premāviṣṭa ha-ilā
> 'mahāprabhura prasāda' jāni' tāṅhāre puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātula]]---red; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---cloth; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became overwhelmed in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the blessed gift of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---thinking; [[synonyms:t:tāṅhāre]] [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired from him.

===== Translation =====

**Assuming the reddish cloth to be a gift from Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita was overwhelmed with ecstatic love. Thus he questioned Sanātana Gosvāmī.**
