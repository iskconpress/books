====== Cc. Antya 13.5 ======

===== Text =====

> kalāra śaralāte, śayana, ati kṣīṇa kāya
> śaralāte hāḍa lāge, vyathā haya gāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kalāra]] [[synonyms:ś:śaralāte]]---on the dry bark of a plantain tree; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---lying down; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very; [[synonyms:k:kṣīṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kāya]]---skinny body; [[synonyms:ś:śaralāte]]---on the dry bark of a plantain tree; [[synonyms:h:hāḍa]] [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---bones contact; [[synonyms:v:vyathā]]---pain; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---in the body.

===== Translation =====

**Because He was very thin, when He lay down to rest on the dry bark of plantain trees, it caused Him pain in His bones.**
