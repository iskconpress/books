====== CC Antya 13.38 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana-saṅge kariha vana daraśana
> sanātanera saṅga nā chāḍibā eka-kṣaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kariha]]---do; [[synonyms:v:vana]] [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting the twelve forests; [[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:saṅga]]---association; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍibā]]---do not leave; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---even for a moment.

===== Translation =====

**"Visit all twelve forests of Vṛndāvana in the company of Sanātana Gosvāmī. Do not leave his association for even a moment.**
