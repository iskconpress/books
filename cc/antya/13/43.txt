====== Cc. Antya 13.43 ======

===== Text =====

> tapana-miśra, candraśekhara,---doṅhāre mililā
> tāṅra ṭhāñi prabhura kathā sakala-i śunilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tapana]]-[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:c:candra]]-[[synonyms:ś:śekhara]]---Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:d:doṅhāre]] [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---he met both; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from him; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics; [[synonyms:s:sakala]]-[[synonyms:i:i]]---all; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---they heard.

===== Translation =====

**When he met Tapana Miśra and Candraśekhara in Vārāṇasī, they both heard from him about topics concerning Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
