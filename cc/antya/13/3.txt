====== Cc. Antya 13.3 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-mate mahāprabhu jagadānanda-saṅge
> nānā-mate āsvādaya premera taraṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---in the company of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nānā]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in various ways; [[synonyms:ā:āsvādaya]]---tastes; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:t:taraṅge]]---the waves of spiritual loving affairs.

===== Translation =====

**In the company of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would taste various transcendental relationships of pure love.**
