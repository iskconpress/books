====== Cc. Antya 13.48 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana paṇḍitera kare samādhāna
> mahāvane dena āni' māgi' anna-pāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitera]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:s:samādhāna]]---gave all kinds of service; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:vane]]---at Mahāvana; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---gives; [[synonyms:ā:āni']]---bringing; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---by begging; [[synonyms:a:anna]]-[[synonyms:p:pāna]]---food and drink.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī attended to all of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita's needs. He begged in the area of Mahāvana and brought Jagadānanda all kinds of things to eat and drink.**
