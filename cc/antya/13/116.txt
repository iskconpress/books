====== Cc. Antya 13.116 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-ādi bhakta-ṭhāñi ājñā māgiyā
> vārāṇasī āilā bhaṭṭa prabhura ājñā pāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from the devotees; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgiyā]]---asking permission; [[synonyms:v:vārāṇasī]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---returned to Vārāṇasī; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:p:pāñā]]---getting permission.

===== Translation =====

**After taking permission from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa returned to Vārāṇasī.**
