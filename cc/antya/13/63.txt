====== CC Antya 13.63 ======

===== Text =====

> prasāda pāi anyonye kailā āliṅgana
> caitanya-virahe duṅhe karilā krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:p:pāi]]---after eating the remnants of food; [[synonyms:a:anyonye]]---one another; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---they embraced; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:v:virahe]]---in separation from Lord Caitanya; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:k:karilā]] [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---cried.

===== Translation =====

**After eating the prasāda, they embraced each other and cried due to separation from Lord Caitanya.**
