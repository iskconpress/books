====== CC Antya 13.73 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātanera nāme paṇḍita daṇḍavat kailā
> rāsa-sthalīra dhūli ādi saba bheṭa dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātanera]]---of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:n:nāme]]---in the name; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---offered obeisances; [[synonyms:r:rāsa]]-[[synonyms:s:sthalīra]]---the arena of the //rāsa// dance; [[synonyms:d:dhūli]]---dust; [[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---and other things; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bheṭa]]---gifts; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---presented.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita also offered obeisances to the Lord on behalf of Sanātana Gosvāmī. Then he gave the Lord the dust from the site of the rāsa dance and the other gifts.**
