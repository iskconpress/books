====== CC Antya 13.138 ======

===== Text =====

> ye ei-sakala kathā śune śraddhā kari'
> tāṅre kṛṣṇa-prema-dhana dena gaurahari

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:ye]]---one who; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:s:sakala]]---all these; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---topics; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:ś:śraddhā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with faith and love; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---unto him; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the wealth of ecstatic love of Lord //Kṛṣṇa//; [[synonyms:d:dena]]---delivers; [[synonyms:g:gaurahari]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu [Gaurahari] bestows ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa upon anyone who hears all these topics with faith and love.**
