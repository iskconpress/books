====== CC Antya 13.58 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana kahe---"sādhu paṇḍita-mahāśaya!
> tomā-sama caitanyera priya keha naya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:s:sādhu]]---saint; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---learned scholar; [[synonyms:m:mahāśaya]]---a great soul; [[synonyms:t:tomā]]-[[synonyms:s:sama]]---like you; [[synonyms:c:caitanyera]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:priya]]---dear; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:naya]]---no one is.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "My dear Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, you are a greatly learned saint. No one is dearer to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu than you.**
