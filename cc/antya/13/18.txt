====== CC Antya 13.18 ======

===== Text =====

> nakhe ciri' ciri' tāhā ati sūkṣma kailā
> prabhura bahirvāsa duite se saba bharilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nakhe]]---with the nails; [[synonyms:c:ciri']] [[synonyms:c:ciri']]---tearing and tearing; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---them; [[synonyms:a:ati]]---very; [[synonyms:s:sūkṣma]]---fine; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bahirvāsa]]---covering cloths; [[synonyms:d:duite]]---in two; [[synonyms:s:se]] [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all those; [[synonyms:b:bharilā]]---filled.

===== Translation =====

**He then tore the leaves into very fine fibers with his nails and filled two of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's outer garments with the fibers.**
