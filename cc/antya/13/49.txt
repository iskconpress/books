====== CC Antya 13.49 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina sanātane paṇḍita nimantrilā
> nitya-kṛtya kari' teṅha pāka caḍāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍitanimantrilā]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita invited; [[synonyms:n:nitya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after finishing his routine duties; [[synonyms:t:teṅha]]---he; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:c:caḍāilā]]---began to cook.

===== Translation =====

**One day Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, having invited Sanātana to the nearby temple for lunch, finished his routine duties and began to cook.**
