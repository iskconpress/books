====== Cc. Antya 13.88 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' leuṭi' prabhu gelā nija-sthāne
> śuni' mahā-bhaya ha-ila svarūpādi-mane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:l:leuṭi']]---returning; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---went; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to His own place; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---great fear; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ila]]---there was; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]-[[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the minds of Svarūpa Dāmodara and other attendants.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned home. When Svarūpa Gosvāmī and His other attendants heard about the incident, they were very afraid.**
