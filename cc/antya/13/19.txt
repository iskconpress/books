====== CC Antya 13.19 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata dui kailā oḍana-pāḍane
> aṅgīkāra kailā prabhu aneka yatane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:d:dui]]---two pieces; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:o:oḍana]]-[[synonyms:p:pāḍane]]---one for bedding, one for the pillow; [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did accept; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:aneka]] [[synonyms:y:yatane]]---after much endeavor.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, Svarūpa Dāmodara made some bedding and a pillow, and after much endeavor by the devotees, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted them.**
