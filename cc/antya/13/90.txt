====== Cc. Antya 13.90 ======

===== Text =====

> kāśī haite calilā teṅho gauḍa-patha diyā
> saṅge sevaka cale tāṅra jhāli vahiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kāśī]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from Kāśī; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---proceeded; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:g:gauḍa]]-[[synonyms:p:patha]] [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---by the path through Bengal; [[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---along with him; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]---one servant; [[synonyms:c:cale]]---goes; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:j:jhāli]]---baggage; [[synonyms:v:vahiyā]]---carrying.

===== Translation =====

**Accompanied by one servant carrying his baggage, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa started from Vārāṇasī and traveled along the path leading through Bengal.**
