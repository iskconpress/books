====== CC Antya 13.119 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrvavat aṣṭa-māsa prabhu-pāśa chilā
> aṣṭa-māsa rahi' punaḥ prabhu ājñā dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]]---for eight months; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]] [[synonyms:c:chilā]]---remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:m:māsa]] [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---after staying for eight months; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---ordered him.

===== Translation =====

**As previously, Raghunātha remained continuously with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for eight months. Then the Lord gave him the following order.**
