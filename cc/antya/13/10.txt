====== CC Antya 13.10 ======

===== Text =====

> śayanera kāle svarūpa tāhāṅi rahilā
> tulī-bālisa dekhi' prabhu krodhāviṣṭa ha-ilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śayanera]] [[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at bedtime; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅi]] [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained there; [[synonyms:t:tulī]]---quilt; [[synonyms:b:bālisa]]---pillow; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]-[[synonyms:ā:āviṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became very angry.

===== Translation =====

**When it was time for the Lord to go to bed, Svarūpa Dāmodara stayed nearby, but when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the quilt and pillow, He was immediately very angry.**
