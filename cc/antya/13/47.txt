====== CC Antya 13.47 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana bhikṣā karena yāi' mahāvane
> kabhu devālaye, kabhu brāhmaṇa-sadane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---begs alms; [[synonyms:y:yāi']] [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:vane]]---going to the vicinity of Mahāvana; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:d:devālaye]]---in a temple; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:b:brāhmaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sadane]]---in the house of a //brāhmaṇa.//

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī would beg alms from door to door in the vicinity of Mahāvana. Sometimes he would go to a temple and sometimes to a brāhmaṇa's house.**
