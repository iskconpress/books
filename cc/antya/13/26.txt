====== CC Antya 13.26 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu prīte tāṅra gamana nā karena aṅgīkāra
> teṅho prabhura ṭhāñi ājñā māge bāra bāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:prīte]]---out of affection; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]---departure; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:a:aṅgīkāra]]---does not accept; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---permission; [[synonyms:m:māge]]---begs; [[synonyms:b:bāra]] [[synonyms:b:bāra]]---again and again.

===== Translation =====

**Because of affection for Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would not permit him to depart, but Jagadānanda Paṇḍita repeatedly insisted that the Lord give him permission to go.**
