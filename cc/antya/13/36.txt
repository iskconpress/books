====== Cc. Antya 13.36 ======

===== Text =====

> mathurā gele sanātana-saṅgei rahibā
> mathurāra svāmī sabera caraṇa vandibā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:g:gele]]---when you go to Mathurā; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅgei]]---in the association of Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:r:rahibā]]---remain; [[synonyms:m:mathurāra]] [[synonyms:s:svāmī]]---the leading men of Mathurā; [[synonyms:s:sabera]]---of all; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandibā]]---worship the feet.

===== Translation =====

**"When you reach Mathurā, you should remain with Sanātana Gosvāmī and offer respectful obeisances to the feet of all the leading men there.**
