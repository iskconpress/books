====== CC Antya 13.66 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānanda-paṇḍita tabe ājñā māgilā
> sanātana prabhure kichu bheṭa-vastu dilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgilā]]---asked permission; [[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:b:bheṭa]]-[[synonyms:v:vastu]]---gifts; [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---presented.

===== Translation =====

**When Sanātana Gosvāmī granted permission for Jagadānanda to return to Jagannātha Purī, he gave Jagadānanda some gifts for Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
