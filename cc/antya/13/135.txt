====== CC Antya 13.135 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura kṛpāya kṛṣṇa-prema anargala
> ei ta' kahiluṅ tāte caitanya-kṛpā-phala

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]] [[synonyms:k:kṛpāya]]---by the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]] [[synonyms:a:anargala]]---incessantly overwhelmed with ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:t:ta']]---thus; [[synonyms:k:kahiluṅ]]---I have described; [[synonyms:t:tāte]]---thereby; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛpā]]-[[synonyms:p:phala]]---the result of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mercy.

===== Translation =====

**Thus I have described the powerful mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, by which Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa Gosvāmī remained constantly overwhelmed with ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
