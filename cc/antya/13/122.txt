====== CC Antya 13.122 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' prabhu tāṅre āliṅgana kailā
> prabhura kṛpāte kṛṣṇa-preme matta hailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---embraced; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:preme]]---in love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:matta]] [[synonyms:h:hailā]]---became enlivened.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa, and by the Lord's mercy Raghunātha was enlivened with ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa.**
