====== CC Antya 13.100 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mate raghunātha āilā nīlācale
> prabhura caraṇe yāñā mililā kutūhale

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---to Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---to the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāñā]]---going; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met; [[synonyms:k:kutūhale]]---in great delight.

===== Translation =====

**Traveling in this way, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa soon arrived at Jagannātha Purī. There, with great delight, he met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and fell at His lotus feet.**
