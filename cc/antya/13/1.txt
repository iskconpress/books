====== CC Antya 13.1 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇa-viccheda-jātārtyā
> kṣīṇe cāpi manas-tanū
> dadhāte phullatāṁ bhāvair
> yasya taṁ gauram āśraye

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:v:viccheda]]---by separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:j:jāta]]---produced; [[synonyms:ā:ārtyā]]---by the pain; [[synonyms:k:kṣīṇe]]---thin, exhausted; [[synonyms:c:cā]]---and; [[synonyms:a:api]]---although; [[synonyms:m:manaḥ]]---mind; [[synonyms:t:tanū]]---and body; [[synonyms:d:dadhāte]]---assumes; [[synonyms:p:phullatām]]---developed state; [[synonyms:b:bhāvaiḥ]]---by ecstatic emotions; [[synonyms:y:yasya]]---of whom; [[synonyms:t:tam]]---unto Him; [[synonyms:g:gauram]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:āśraye]]---I take shelter.

===== Translation =====

**Let me take shelter at the lotus feet of Lord Gauracandra. His mind became exhausted and His body very thin from the pain of separation from Kṛṣṇa, but when He felt ecstatic love for the Lord, He again became fully developed.**
