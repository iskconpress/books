====== CC Antya 13.45 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana karāilā tāṅre dvādaśa vana daraśana
> gokule rahilā duṅhe dekhi' mahāvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]]---made; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:d:dvādaśa]]---twelve; [[synonyms:v:vana]]---forests; [[synonyms:d:daraśana]]---visiting; [[synonyms:g:gokule]]---at Gokula; [[synonyms:r:rahilā]]---remained; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both; [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---after seeing; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:v:vana]]---Mahāvana.

===== Translation =====

**After Sanātana Gosvāmī had taken Jagadānanda to see all twelve forests of Vṛndāvana, concluding with Mahāvana, they both remained in Gokula.**
