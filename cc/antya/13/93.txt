====== CC Antya 13.93 ======

===== Text =====

> aṣṭa-prahara rāma-nāma japena rātri-dine
> sarva tyaji' calilā jagannātha-daraśane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aṣṭa]]-[[synonyms:p:prahara]]---twenty-four hours a day; [[synonyms:r:rāma]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]---the holy name of Lord Rāma; [[synonyms:j:japena]]---chants; [[synonyms:r:rātri]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---day and night; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tyaji']]---giving up; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went; [[synonyms:j:jagannātha]]-[[synonyms:d:daraśane]]---to see Lord Jagannātha.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmadāsa had renounced everything and was going to see Lord Jagannātha. While traveling, he chanted the holy name of Lord Rāma twenty-four hours a day.**
