====== Cc. Antya 13.55 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana tāṅre jāni' lajjita ha-ilā
> balite lāgilā paṇḍita hāṇḍi culāte dharilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:j:jāni']]---knowing; [[synonyms:l:lajjita]] [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:i:ilā]]---became ashamed; [[synonyms:b:balite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to s peak; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:h:hāṇḍi]]---the cooking pot; [[synonyms:c:culāte]]---on the stove; [[synonyms:d:dharilā]]---kept.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī, however, knew Jagadānanda Paṇḍita very well and was consequently somewhat ashamed. Jagadānanda therefore left the cooking pot on the stove and spoke as follows.**
