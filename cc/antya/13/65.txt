====== CC Antya 13.65 ======

===== Text =====

> mahāprabhura sandeśa kahilā sanātane
> 'āmiha āsitechi, rahite kariha eka-sthāne'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sandeśa]]---message; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]]---said; [[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---to Sanātana; [[synonyms:ā:āmiha]] [[synonyms:ā:āsitechi]]---I am also coming; [[synonyms:r:rahite]]---for My stay; [[synonyms:k:kariha]] [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---arrange for one place.

===== Translation =====

**Jagadānanda Paṇḍita therefore gave Sanātana Gosvāmī the message from the Lord: "I am also coming to Vṛndāvana; please arrange a place for Me to stay."**
