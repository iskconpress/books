====== CC Antya 13.104 ======

===== Text =====

> govindere kahi' eka vāsā deoyāilā
> svarūpādi bhakta-gaṇa-sane milāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govindere]]---to Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kahi']]---speaking; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]---residential place; [[synonyms:d:deoyāilā]]---caused to be given; [[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:sane]]---with the devotees; [[synonyms:m:milāilā]]---introduced.

===== Translation =====

**The Lord asked Govinda to arrange for Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa's accommodations and then introduced him to all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.**
