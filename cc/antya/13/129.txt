====== CC Antya 13.129 ======

===== Text =====

> kṛṣṇera saundarya-mādhurya yabe paḍe, śune
> premete vihvala tabe, kichui nā jāne

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]]---of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:saundarya]]---beauty; [[synonyms:m:mādhurya]]---sweetness; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:p:paḍe]]---recites; [[synonyms:ś:śune]]---hears; [[synonyms:p:premete]]---in ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:v:vihvala]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:k:kichui]]---anything; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---does not know.

===== Translation =====

**When he recited or heard about the beauty and sweetness of Kṛṣṇa, he would be overwhelmed with ecstatic love and become oblivious to everything.**
