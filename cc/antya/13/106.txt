====== CC Antya 13.106 ======

===== Text =====

> madhye madhye mahāprabhura karena nimantraṇa
> ghara-bhāta karena, āra vividha vyañjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:madhye]] [[synonyms:m:madhye]]---at intervals; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhura]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---he makes invitations; [[synonyms:g:ghara]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāta]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---he cooks rice at home; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vividha]] [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---various kinds of vegetables.

===== Translation =====

**He would periodically cook rice with various vegetables and invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home.**
