====== CC Antya 13.127 ======

===== Text =====

> aśru, kampa, gadgada prabhura kṛpāte
> netra kaṇṭha rodhe bāṣpa, nā pāre paḍite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:aśru]]---tears; [[synonyms:k:kampa]]---trembling; [[synonyms:g:gadgada]]---faltering of the voice; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kṛpāte]]---by the mercy; [[synonyms:n:netra]]---eyes; [[synonyms:k:kaṇṭha]]---neck; [[synonyms:r:rodhe]]---choked up; [[synonyms:b:bāṣpa]]---tears; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]] [[synonyms:p:paḍite]]---could not recite.

===== Translation =====

**By the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he experienced the symptoms of ecstatic love-tears, trembling, and faltering of the voice. His eyes filled with tears, his throat became choked, and thus he could not recite Śrīmad Bhāgavatam.**
