====== CC Antya 13.14 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahena,---"khāṭa eka ānaha pāḍite
> jagadānanda cāhe āmāya viṣaya bhuñjāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:k:khāṭa]]---bedstead; [[synonyms:e:eka]]---one; [[synonyms:ā:ānaha]]---bring; [[synonyms:p:pāḍite]]---to lie down; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---wants; [[synonyms:ā:āmāya]]---Me; [[synonyms:v:viṣaya]] [[synonyms:b:bhuñjāite]]---to cause to enjoy material happiness.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "You should bring a bedstead here for Me to lie on. Jagadānanda wants Me to enjoy material happiness.**
