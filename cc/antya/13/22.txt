====== CC Antya 13.22 ======

===== Text =====

> bhitarera krodha-duḥkha prakāśa nā kaila
> mathurā yāite prabhu-sthāne ājñā māgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhitarera]]---internal; [[synonyms:k:krodha]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---anger and unhappiness; [[synonyms:p:prakāśa]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---did not disclose; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Mathurā; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:m:māgila]]---asked for permission.

===== Translation =====

**Now, concealing his anger and unhappiness, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita again asked Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for permission to go to Mathurā.**
