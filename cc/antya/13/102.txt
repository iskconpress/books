====== CC Antya 13.102 ======

===== Text =====

> miśra āra śekharera daṇḍavat jānāilā
> mahāprabhu tāṅ-sabāra vārtā puchilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:m:miśra]]---of Tapana Miśra; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:ś:śekharera]]---of Candraśekhara; [[synonyms:d:daṇḍavat]]---obeisances; [[synonyms:j:jānāilā]]---he informed; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:t:tāṅ]]-[[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:v:vārtā]]---news; [[synonyms:p:puchilā]]---inquired.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha offered respectful obeisances to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu on behalf of Tapana Miśra and Candraśekhara, and the Lord also inquired about them.**
