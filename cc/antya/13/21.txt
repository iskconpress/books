====== Cc. Antya 13.21 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve jagadānandera icchā vṛndāvana yāite
> prabhu ājñā nā dena tāṅre, nā pāre calite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:j:jagadānandera]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---desire; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]] [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---to go to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]]---permission; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:d:dena]]---did not give; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]] [[synonyms:c:calite]]---he could not go.

===== Translation =====

**Formerly, when Jagadānanda Paṇḍita had desired to go to Vṛndāvana, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had not given His permission, and therefore he could not go.**
