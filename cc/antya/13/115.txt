====== CC Antya 13.115 ======

===== Text =====

> āliṅgana kari' prabhu vidāya tāṅre dilā
> preme gara gara bhaṭṭa kāndite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---embracing; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vidāya]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---bade him farewell; [[synonyms:p:preme]]---in ecstatic love; [[synonyms:g:gara]] [[synonyms:g:gara]]---overwhelmed; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:kāndite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to cry.

===== Translation =====

**Then the Lord embraced him and bade him farewell. Overwhelmed with ecstatic love, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa began to cry due to imminent separation from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
