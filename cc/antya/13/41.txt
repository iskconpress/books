====== CC Antya 13.41 ======

===== Text =====

> eta bali' jagadānande kailā āliṅgana
> jagadānanda calilā prabhura vandiyā caraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying this; [[synonyms:j:jagadānande]]---to Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---did; [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embracing; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---proceeded; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vandiyā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---after worshiping the feet.

===== Translation =====

**After saying this, the Lord embraced Jagadānanda Paṇḍita, who then worshiped the Lord's lotus feet and started for Vṛndāvana.**
