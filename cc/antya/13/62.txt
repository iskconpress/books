====== CC Antya 13.62 ======

===== Text =====

> pāka kari' jagadānanda caitanya samarpilā
> dui-jana vasi' tabe prasāda pāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after cooking; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:s:samarpilā]]---offered to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two persons; [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---sitting; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---remnants of food; [[synonyms:p:pāilā]]---took.

===== Translation =====

**When Jagadānanda Paṇḍita finished cooking, he offered the food to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Then he and Sanātana Gosvāmī sat down and ate the prasāda.**
