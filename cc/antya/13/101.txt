====== Cc. Antya 13.101 ======

===== Text =====

> daṇḍa-paraṇāma kari' bhaṭṭa paḍilā caraṇe
> prabhu 'raghunātha' jāni kailā āliṅgane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:daṇḍa]]-[[synonyms:p:paraṇāma]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering obeisances by falling down on the ground; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:p:paḍilā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇe]]---fell at the lotus feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:raghunātha]]---Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:j:jāni]]---knowing; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgane]]---embraced.

===== Translation =====

**Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa fell straight as a rod at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Then the Lord embraced him, knowing well who he was.**
