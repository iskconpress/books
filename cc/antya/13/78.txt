====== Cc. Antya 13.78 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina prabhu yameśvara-ṭoṭā yāite
> sei-kāle deva-dāsī lāgilā gāite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yameśvara]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭoṭā]]---to the temple of Yameśvara-ṭoṭā; [[synonyms:y:yāite]]---when He was going; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at that time; [[synonyms:d:deva]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsī]]---a female singer of the Jagannātha temple; [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began; [[synonyms:g:gāite]]---to sing.

===== Translation =====

**One day when the Lord was going to the temple of Yameśvara, a female singer began to sing in the Jagannātha temple.**
