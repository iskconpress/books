====== CC Antya 13.13 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa kahe,---'tomāra icchā, ki kahite pāri?
> śayyā upekṣile paṇḍita duḥkha pābe bhārī'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī said; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:i:icchā]]---Your will; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---who; [[synonyms:k:kahite]] [[synonyms:p:pāri]]---can say; [[synonyms:ś:śayyā]] [[synonyms:u:upekṣile]]---if You do not accept the bedding; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness; [[synonyms:p:pābe]]---will feel; [[synonyms:b:bhari]]---great.

===== Translation =====

**Svarūpa Dāmodara said to the Lord, "I cannot contradict Your supreme will, my Lord, but if You do not accept the bedding, Jagadānanda Paṇḍita will feel great unhappiness."**
