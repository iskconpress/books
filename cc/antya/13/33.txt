====== Cc. Antya 13.33 ======

===== Text =====

> svarūpa-gosāñira bole prabhu ājñā dilā
> jagadānande bolāñā tāṅre śikhāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svarūpa]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñira]]---of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:b:bole]]---on the request; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave permission; [[synonyms:j:jagadānande]]---to Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:b:bolāñā]]---calling; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---to him; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāilā]]---gave instructions.

===== Translation =====

**At the request of Svarūpa Dāmodara, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu granted Jagadānanda Paṇḍita permission to go. The Lord sent for him and instructed him as follows.**
