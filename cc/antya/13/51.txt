====== Cc. Antya 13.51 ======

===== Text =====

> sanātana sei vastra mastake bāndhiyā
> jagadānandera vāsā-dvāre vasilā āsiyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sanātana]]---Sanātana Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:v:vastra]]---cloth; [[synonyms:m:mastake]]---on the head; [[synonyms:b:bāndhiyā]]---binding; [[synonyms:j:jagadānandera]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:v:vāsā]]-[[synonyms:d:dvāre]]---at the door of the residence; [[synonyms:v:vasilā]]---sat down; [[synonyms:ā:āsiyā]]---coming.

===== Translation =====

**Sanātana Gosvāmī was wearing this cloth bound about his head when he came to Jagadānanda Paṇḍita's door and sat down.**
