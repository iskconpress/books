====== Cc. Antya 8.34 ======

===== Text =====

> ayi dīna-dayārdra nātha he
> mathurā-nātha kadāvalokyase
> hṛdayaṁ tvad-aloka-kātaraṁ
> dayita bhrāmyati kiṁ karomy aham

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ayi]]---O my Lord; [[synonyms:d:dīna]]---on the poor; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ārdra]]---compassionate; [[synonyms:n:nātha]]---O master; [[synonyms:h:he]]---O; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]-[[synonyms:n:nātha]]---the master of Mathurā; [[synonyms:k:kadā]]---when; [[synonyms:a:avalokyase]]---I shall see you; [[synonyms:h:hṛdayam]]---my heart; [[synonyms:t:tvat]]---of You; [[synonyms:a:aloka]]---without seeing; [[synonyms:k:kātaram]]---very much aggrieved; [[synonyms:d:dayita]]---O most beloved; [[synonyms:b:bhrāmyati]]---becomes overwhelmed; [[synonyms:k:kim]]---what; [[synonyms:k:karomi]]---shall do; [[synonyms:a:aham]]---I.

===== Translation =====

**"O my Lord! O most merciful master! O master of Mathurā! When shall I see You again? Because of my not seeing You, my agitated heart has become unsteady. O most beloved one, what shall I do now?"**
