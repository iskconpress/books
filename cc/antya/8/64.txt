====== CC Antya 8.64 ======

===== Text =====

> "sannyāsīra dharma nahe 'indriya-tarpaṇa'
> yaiche taiche kare mātra udara bharaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]]---of a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principle; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:t:tarpaṇa]]---to gratify the senses; [[synonyms:y:yaiche]] [[synonyms:t:taiche]]---some way or other; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only; [[synonyms:u:udara]] [[synonyms:b:bharaṇa]]---filling the belly.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmacandra Purī advised, "It is not the business of a sannyāsī to gratify his senses. He should fill his belly some way or other.**
