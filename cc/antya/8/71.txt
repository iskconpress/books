====== Cc. Antya 8.71 ======

===== Text =====

> āra dina bhakta-gaṇa-saha paramānanda-purī
> prabhu-pāśe nivedilā dainya-vinaya kari'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āra]] [[synonyms:d:dina]]---the next day; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]-[[synonyms:s:saha]]---with the other devotees; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśe]]---before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nivedilā]]---submitted; [[synonyms:d:dainya]]-[[synonyms:v:vinaya]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---in great humility and submission.

===== Translation =====

**The next day, Paramānanda Purī and other devotees approached Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with great humility and submission.**
