====== CC Antya 8.49 ======

===== Text =====

>    "rātrāv atra aikṣavam āsīt, tena
> pipīlikāḥ sañcaranti aho! viraktānāṁ
> sannyāsinām iyam indriya-lālaseti
> bruvann utthāya gataḥ."

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rātrau]]---at night; [[synonyms:a:atra]]---here; [[synonyms:a:aikṣavam]]---sugar candy; [[synonyms:ā:āsīt]]---was; [[synonyms:t:tena]]---by that; [[synonyms:p:pipīlikāḥ]]---ants; [[synonyms:s:sañcaranti]]---wander about; [[synonyms:a:aho]]---alas; [[synonyms:v:viraktānām]]---renounced; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsinām]]---of //sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:i:iyam]]---this; [[synonyms:i:indriya]]---to the senses; [[synonyms:l:lālasa]]---attachment; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:b:bruvan]]---speaking; [[synonyms:u:utthāya]]---getting up; [[synonyms:g:gataḥ]]---left.

===== Translation =====

**"Last night there was sugar candy here," he said. "Therefore ants are wandering about. Alas, this renounced sannyāsī is attached to such sense gratification!" After speaking in this way, he got up and left.**
