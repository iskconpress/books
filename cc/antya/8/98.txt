====== CC Antya 8.98 ======

===== Text =====

> svacchande nimantraṇa, prabhura kīrtana-nartana
> svacchande karena sabe prasāda bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:svacchande]]---freely; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invitation; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kīrtana]]-[[synonyms:n:nartana]]---chanting and dancing; [[synonyms:s:svacchande]]---in full independence; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---everyone did; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---accepting //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**After his departure, everything was happy once again. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted invitations as usual and led congregational chanting and dancing. Everyone else also accepted prasāda without hindrances.**
