====== CC Antya 8.18 ======

===== Text =====

> pūrve yabe mādhavendra karena antardhāna
> rāmacandra-purī tabe āilā tāṅra sthāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:m:mādhavendra]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:a:antardhāna]]---was about to die; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]] [[synonyms:s:sthāna]]---to his place.

===== Translation =====

**Formerly, when Mādhavendra Purī was at the last stage of his life, Rāmacandra Purī came to where he was staying.**
