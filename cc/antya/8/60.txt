====== CC Antya 8.60 ======

===== Text =====

> ardhāśana karena prabhu, govinda ardhāśana
> saba bhakta-gaṇa tabe chāḍila bhojana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:a:aśana]] [[synonyms:k:karena]]---eats half; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:a:aśana]]---eats half; [[synonyms:s:saba]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---all the devotees; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---at that time; [[synonyms:c:chāḍila]] [[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---gave up eating.

===== Translation =====

**Thus both Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Govinda ate only half the food they needed. Because of this, all the other devotees gave up eating.**
