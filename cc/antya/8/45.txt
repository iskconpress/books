====== CC Antya 8.45 ======

===== Text =====

> ei nindā kari' kahe sarva-loka-sthāne
> prabhure dekhiteha avaśya āise prati-dine

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:n:nindā]]---this criticism; [[synonyms:k:kari']]---making; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:l:loka]]-[[synonyms:s:sthāne]]---to everyone; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:d:dekhiteha]]---to see, still; [[synonyms:a:avaśya]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:āise]]---comes; [[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:d:dine]]---daily.

===== Translation =====

**In this way Rāmacandra Purī blasphemed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu before everyone, but nevertheless he would regularly come to see the Lord every day.**
