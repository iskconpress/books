====== CC Antya 8.101 ======

===== Text =====

> caitanya-caritra---yena amṛtera pūra
> śunite śravaṇe mane lāgaye madhura

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---the character of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:a:amṛtera]] [[synonyms:p:pūra]]---filled with nectar; [[synonyms:ś:śunite]]---hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---to the ear; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---to the mind; [[synonyms:l:lāgaye]]---feels; [[synonyms:m:madhura]]---pleasing.

===== Translation =====

**The character of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is full of nectar. Hearing about it is pleasing to the ear and mind.**
