====== CC Antya 8.20 ======

===== Text =====

> rāmacandra-purī tabe upadeśe tāṅre
> śiṣya hañā guruke kahe, bhaya nāhi kare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:u:upadeśe]] [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---instructed him; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a disciple; [[synonyms:g:guruke]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---said to his spiritual master; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---without fear.

===== Translation =====

**Then Rāmacandra Purī was so foolish that he fearlessly dared to instruct his spiritual master.**
