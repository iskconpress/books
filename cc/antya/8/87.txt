====== CC Antya 8.87 ======

===== Text =====

> dui-paṇa kauḍi lāge prabhura nimantraṇe
> kabhu dui-jana bhoktā, kabhu tina-jane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]]---two //paṇas// of //kauḍis// (160 small conchshells); [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---costs; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇe]]---to invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---two men; [[synonyms:b:bhoktā]]---eating; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---three men.

===== Translation =====

**The cost for the food needed to invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was fixed at two paṇas of kauḍis [160 conchshells], and that food would be taken by two men and sometimes three.**
