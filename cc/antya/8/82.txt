====== CC Antya 8.82 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāra svabhāva ihāṅ karite nā yuyāya
> tathāpi kahiye kichu marma-duḥkha pāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---his characteristics; [[synonyms:i:ihāṅ]]---here; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:y:yuyāya]]---one should not follow; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:k:kahiye]]---I say; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---something; [[synonyms:m:marma]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkha]]---unhappiness within the heart; [[synonyms:p:pāya]]---get.

===== Translation =====

**"One should not, therefore, follow the principles of Rāmacandra Purī. Nevertheless, I have to say something against him because he is making our hearts unhappy.**
