====== CC Antya 8.43 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura yateka guṇa sparśite nārila
> chidra cāhi' bule, kāṅhā chidra nā pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yateka]] [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---all the transcendental attributes; [[synonyms:s:sparśite]] [[synonyms:n:nārila]]---could not touch or understand; [[synonyms:c:chidra]] [[synonyms:c:cāhi']]---looking for faults; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---he goes about; [[synonyms:k:kāṅhā]]---anywhere; [[synonyms:c:chidra]]---fault; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---he could not find.

===== Translation =====

**Because Rāmacandra Purī was interested only in finding faults, he could not understand the transcendental qualities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. His only concern was finding faults, but still he could not find any.**
