====== CC Antya 8.39 ======

===== Text =====

> animantraṇa bhikṣā kare, nāhika nirṇaya
> anyera bhikṣāra sthitira layena niścaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:animantraṇa]]---without being invited; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---accepts food; [[synonyms:n:nāhika]] [[synonyms:n:nirṇaya]]---there is no certainty; [[synonyms:a:anyera]]---of others; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣāra]]---of acceptance of //prasāda//; [[synonyms:s:sthitira]]---of the situation; [[synonyms:l:layena]] [[synonyms:n:niścaya]]---takes account.

===== Translation =====

**There was no certainty of where Rāmacandra Purī would take his meal, for he would do so even uninvited. Nevertheless, he was very particular to keep account of how others were taking their meals.**
