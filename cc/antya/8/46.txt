====== Cc. Antya 8.46 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu guru-buddhye karena sambhrama, sammāna
> teṅho chidra cāhi' bule,---ei tāra kāma

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:guru]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhye]]---accepting him as a Godbrother of His spiritual master; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:s:sambhrama]] [[synonyms:s:sammāna]]---offers full respect and obeisances; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:c:chidra]] [[synonyms:c:cāhi']]---trying to find fault; [[synonyms:b:bule]]---goes about; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:tāra]]---his; [[synonyms:k:kāma]]---business.

===== Translation =====

**When they met, the Lord would offer him respectful obeisances, considering him a Godbrother of His spiritual master. Rāmacandra Purī's business, however, was to search for faults in the Lord.**
