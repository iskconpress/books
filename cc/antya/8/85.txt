====== CC Antya 8.85 ======

===== Text =====

> yati hañā jihvā-lāmpaṭya---atyanta anyāya
> yatira dharma,---prāṇa rākhite āhāra-mātra khāya"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yati]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:j:jihvā]]-[[synonyms:l:lāmpaṭya]]---indulging in satisfying the tongue; [[synonyms:a:atyanta]] [[synonyms:a:anyāya]]---grievous offense; [[synonyms:y:yatira]] [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religious principle of a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]] [[synonyms:r:rākhite]]---to maintain life; [[synonyms:ā:āhāra]]---food; [[synonyms:m:mātra]]---only; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---eats.

===== Translation =====

**"For a sannyāsī to indulge in satisfying the tongue is a great offense. The duty of a sannyāsī is to eat only as much as needed to keep body and soul together."**
