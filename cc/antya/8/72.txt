====== CC Antya 8.72 ======

===== Text =====

> "rāmacandra-purī haya ninduka-svabhāva
> tāra bole anna chāḍi' kibā habe lābha?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:n:ninduka]]-[[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---a critic by nature; [[synonyms:t:tāra]] [[synonyms:b:bole]]---by his words; [[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:c:chāḍi']]---giving up eating properly; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---what; [[synonyms:h:habe]]---will be; [[synonyms:l:lābha]]---profit.

===== Translation =====

**Paramānanda Purī said, "My Godbrother Rāmacandra Purī is by nature a bad critic. If You give up eating because of his words, what will be the profit?**
