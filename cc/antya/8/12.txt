====== CC Antya 8.12 ======

===== Text =====

> bhikṣā kari' kahe purī,---"śuna, jagadānanda
> avaśeṣa prasāda tumi karaha bhakṣaṇa"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---after finishing the lunch; [[synonyms:k:kahe]] [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī began to speak; [[synonyms:ś:śuna,]] [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]---my dear Jagadānanda, just hear; [[synonyms:a:avaśeṣa]] [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---the remaining //prasāda//; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you; [[synonyms:k:karaha]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eat.

===== Translation =====

**After finishing the meal, Rāmacandra Purī requested, "My dear Jagadānanda, please listen. You eat the food that is left."**
