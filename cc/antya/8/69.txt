====== CC Antya 8.69 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"ajña bālaka mui 'śiṣya' tomāra
> more śikṣā deha',---ei bhāgya āmāra"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:a:ajña]]---ignorant; [[synonyms:b:bālaka]]---boy; [[synonyms:m:mui]]---I; [[synonyms:ś:śiṣya]] [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your disciple; [[synonyms:m:more]]---Me; [[synonyms:ś:śikṣā]] [[synonyms:d:deha']]---you are instructing; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:b:bhāgya]] [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My great fortune.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then humbly submitted, "I am just like an ignorant boy and am like your disciple. It is My great fortune that you are instructing Me."**
