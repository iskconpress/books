====== Cc. Antya 8.44 ======

===== Text =====

> 'sannyāsī hañā kare miṣṭānna bhakṣaṇa
> ei bhoge haya kaiche indriya-vāraṇa'?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsī]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:m:miṣṭānna]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eats sweetmeats; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:b:bhoge]]---by this eating; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:kaiche]]---how; [[synonyms:i:indriya]]-[[synonyms:v:vāraṇa]]---controlling the senses.

===== Translation =====

**At last he found a fault."How can a person in the renounced order eat so many sweetmeats?" he said. "If one eats sweets, controlling the senses is very difficult."**
