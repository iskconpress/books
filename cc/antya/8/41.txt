====== CC Antya 8.41 ======

===== Text =====

> pratyaha prabhura bhikṣā iti-uti haya
> keha yadi mūlya āne, cāri-paṇa-nirṇaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prati]]-[[synonyms:a:aha]]---daily; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---alms; [[synonyms:i:iti]]-[[synonyms:u:uti]]---here and there; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:keha]]---someone; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:m:mūlya]] [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---pays; [[synonyms:c:cāri]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇa]]---four times eighty small conchshells; [[synonyms:n:nirṇaya]]---as a fixed amount.

===== Translation =====

**Every day the Lord would take His meal at a different place, and if someone was prepared to pay for a meal, the price was fixed at only four paṇas.**
