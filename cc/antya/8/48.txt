====== CC Antya 8.48 ======

===== Text =====

> eka-dina prātaḥ-kāle āilā prabhura ghara
> pipīlikā dekhi' kichu kahena uttara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:d:dina]]---one day; [[synonyms:p:prātaḥ]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---in the morning; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:g:ghara]]---to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:p:pipīlikā]] [[synonyms:d:dekhi']]---seeing many ants; [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:k:kahena]] [[synonyms:u:uttara]]---began to say something critical.

===== Translation =====

**One day Rāmacandra Purī came in the morning to the abode of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Seeing many ants, he said something to criticize the Lord.**
