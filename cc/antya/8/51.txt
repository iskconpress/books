====== CC Antya 8.51 ======

===== Text =====

> sahajei pipīlikā sarvatra beḍāya
> tāhāte tarka uṭhāñā doṣa lāgāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sahajei]]---generally; [[synonyms:p:pipīlikā]]---ants; [[synonyms:s:sarvatra]]---everywhere; [[synonyms:b:beḍāya]]---loiter; [[synonyms:t:tāhāte]]---by that; [[synonyms:t:tarka]] [[synonyms:u:uṭhāñā]]---raising a controversy; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]] [[synonyms:l:lāgāya]]---find faults.

===== Translation =====

**Ants generally crawl about here, there and everywhere, but Rāmacandra Purī, looking for imaginary faults, criticized Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu by alleging that there had been sweetmeats in His room.**
