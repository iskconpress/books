====== CC Antya 8.40 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura nimantraṇe lāge kauḍi cāri paṇa
> kabhu kāśīśvara, govinda khāna tina jana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇe]]---for invitation; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---is required; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]] [[synonyms:c:cāri]] [[synonyms:p:paṇa]]---four times eighty small conchshells; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kāśīśvara]]---sometimes Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---the personal servant of Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:khāna]]---eat; [[synonyms:t:tina]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---three persons.

===== Translation =====

**To invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would cost 320 kauḍis [small conchshells]. This would provide lunch for three people, including Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and sometimes Kāśīśvara and Govinda.**
