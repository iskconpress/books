====== CC Antya 8.88 ======

===== Text =====

> abhojyānna vipra yadi karena nimantraṇa
> prasāda-mūlya la-ite lāge kauḍi dui-paṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:abhojya]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---a //brāhmaṇa// at whose place an invitation cannot be accepted; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---he invites; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]-[[synonyms:m:mūlya]]---the price of the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:ite]]---to bear; [[synonyms:l:lāge]]---costs; [[synonyms:k:kauḍi]] [[synonyms:d:dui]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇa]]---two //paṇas// of //kauḍis.//

===== Translation =====

**When a brāhmaṇa at whose home an invitation could not be accepted invited the Lord, he would pay two paṇas of conchshells to purchase the prasāda.**
