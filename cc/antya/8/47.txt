====== CC Antya 8.47 ======

===== Text =====

> yata nindā kare tāhā prabhu saba jāne
> tathāpi ādara kaṛe baḍa-i sambhrame

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yata]]---whatever; [[synonyms:n:nindā]]---blasphemy; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:j:jāne]]---knows; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:ā:ādara]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---offers respect; [[synonyms:b:baḍa]]-[[synonyms:i:i]] [[synonyms:s:sambhrame]]---with great attention.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu knew that Rāmacandra Purī was criticizing Him before everyone, but whenever Rāmacandra Purī came to see Him, the Lord offered him respects with great attention.**
