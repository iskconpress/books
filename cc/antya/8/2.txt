====== CC Antya 8.2 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrī-caitanya karuṇā-sindhu-avatāra
> brahmā-śivādika bhaje caraṇa yāṅhāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]]---to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karuṇā]]-[[synonyms:s:sindhu]]-[[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---the incarnation of the ocean of mercy; [[synonyms:b:brahmā]]-[[synonyms:ś:śiva]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādika]]---demigods, beginning from Lord Brahmā and Śiva; [[synonyms:b:bhaje]]---worship; [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]]---lotus feet; [[synonyms:y:yāṅhāra]]---whose.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the incarnation of the ocean of mercy. His lotus feet are worshiped by demigods like Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva.**
