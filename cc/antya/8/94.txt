====== CC Antya 8.94 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu rāmacandra-purīra haya bhṛtya-prāya
> kabhu tāre nāhi māne, dekhe tṛṇa-prāya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purīra]]---of Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---was; [[synonyms:b:bhṛtya]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---exactly like a servant; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:t:tāre]]---for him; [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:m:māne]]---He did not care; [[synonyms:d:dekhe]]---sees; [[synonyms:t:tṛṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prāya]]---just like a straw.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sometimes accepted Rāmacandra Purī as His master and considered Himself a servant, and sometimes the Lord, not caring for him, would see him as being just like a straw.**
