====== CC Antya 8.96 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata rāmacandra-purī nīlācale
> dina kata rahi' gelā 'tīrtha' karibāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:k:kata]]---for some days; [[synonyms:r:rahi']]---staying; [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---left; [[synonyms:t:tīrtha]] [[synonyms:k:karibāre]]---to visit holy places.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Rāmacandra Purī stayed for some days at Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī]. Then he left to visit various holy places of pilgrimage.**
