====== CC Antya 8.92 ======

===== Text =====

> bhakta-gaṇe sukha dite prabhura 'avatāra'
> yāhāṅ yaiche yogya, tāhāṅ karena vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇe]]---to His devotees; [[synonyms:s:sukha]] [[synonyms:d:dite]]---to give happiness; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:avatāra]]---incarnation; [[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:y:yaiche]] [[synonyms:y:yogya]]---whatever was fitting according to the time and circumstances; [[synonyms:t:tāhāṅ]] [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---He behaved in that way.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu actually descended to give happiness to the devotees. Thus He behaved in whatever way fit the time and circumstances.**
