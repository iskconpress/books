====== CC Antya 8.73 ======

===== Text =====

> purīra svabhāva,---yatheṣṭa āhāra karāñā
> ye nā khāya, tāre khāoyāya yatana kariyā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purīra]] [[synonyms:s:svabhāva]]---Rāmacandra Purī's character; [[synonyms:y:yathā]]-[[synonyms:i:iṣṭa]]---as much as desired; [[synonyms:ā:āhāra]] [[synonyms:k:karāñā]]---getting someone to eat; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---what; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---one does not eat; [[synonyms:t:tāre]] [[synonyms:k:khāoyāya]]---causes him to eat; [[synonyms:y:yatana]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great attention.

===== Translation =====

**"It is the nature of Rāmacandra Purī that first he lets one eat as much as desired, and if one does not eat more than necessary, with great attention he makes him eat more.**
