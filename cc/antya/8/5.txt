====== Cc. Antya 8.5 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya śrīvāsādi yata bhakta-gaṇa
> śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya prabhu---yāṅra prāṇa-dhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:ś:śrīvāsa]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura; [[synonyms:y:yata]] [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to all the devotees; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanya]] [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:y:yāṅra]]---whose; [[synonyms:p:prāṇa]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---life and soul.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Ṭhākura. Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu is their life and soul.**
