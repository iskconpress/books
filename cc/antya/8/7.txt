====== CC Antya 8.7 ======

===== Text =====

> hena-kāle rāmacandra-purī-gosāñi āilā
> paramānanda-purīre āra prabhure mililā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:h:hena]]-[[synonyms:k:kāle]]---at this time; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---a //sannyāsī// named Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:paramānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:purīre]]---Paramānanda Purī; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:mililā]]---met.

===== Translation =====

**Then a sannyāsī named Rāmacandra Purī Gosāñi came to see Paramānanda Purī and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
