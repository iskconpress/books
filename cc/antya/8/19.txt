====== CC Antya 8.19 ======

===== Text =====

> purī-gosāñi kare kṛṣṇa-nāma-saṅkīrtana
> 'mathurā nā pāinu' bali' karena krandana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---was performing; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:n:nāma]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅkīrtana]]---the chanting of the holy name of Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I did not get shelter at Mathurā; [[synonyms:b:bali']]---saying; [[synonyms:k:karena]] [[synonyms:k:krandana]]---was crying.

===== Translation =====

**Mādhavendra Purī was chanting the holy name of Kṛṣṇa, and sometimes he would cry, "O my Lord, I did not get shelter at Mathurā."**
