====== Cc. Antya 8.81 ======

===== Text =====

> yāhāṅ guṇa śata āche, tāhā nā kare grahaṇa
> guṇa-madhye chale kare doṣa-āropaṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yāhāṅ]]---where; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]---good attributes; [[synonyms:ś:śata]]---hundreds; [[synonyms:ā:āche]]---there are; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---them; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:g:grahaṇa]]---he does not accept; [[synonyms:g:guṇa]]-[[synonyms:m:madhye]]---in such good attributes; [[synonyms:c:chale]]---by tricks; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]-[[synonyms:ā:āropaṇa]]---attributing faults.

===== Translation =====

**"Even where there are hundreds of good qualities, a critic does not consider them. Rather, he attempts by some trick to point out a fault in those attributes.**
