====== CC Antya 8.13 ======

===== Text =====

> āgraha kariyā tāṅre vasi' khāoyāila
> āpane āgraha kari' pariveśana kaila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great eagerness; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]] [[synonyms:v:vasi']]---seating him; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāila]]---he fed; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---with great eagerness; [[synonyms:p:pariveśana]] [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---administered the //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**With great eagerness Rāmacandra Purī seated Jagadānanda Paṇḍita and personally served him prasāda.**
