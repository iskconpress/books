====== Cc. Antya 8.17 ======

===== Text =====

> ei ta' svabhāva tāṅra āgraha kariyā
> piche nindā kare, āge bahuta khāoyāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---characteristic; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---his; [[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with great eagerness; [[synonyms:p:piche]]---afterward; [[synonyms:n:nindā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---criticizes; [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---at first; [[synonyms:b:bahuta]]---much; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāñā]]---feeding.

===== Translation =====

**The characteristic of Rāmacandra Purī was that first he would induce someone to eat more than necessary and then he would criticize him.**
