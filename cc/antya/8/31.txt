====== Cc. Antya 8.31 ======

===== Text =====

> sei haite īśvara-purī---'premera sāgara'
> rāmacandra-purī haila sarva-nindākara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from that; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Īśvara Purī; [[synonyms:p:premera]] [[synonyms:s:sāgara]]---the ocean of ecstatic love; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:h:haila]]---became; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:n:nindākara]]---critic of all others.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Īśvara Purī became like an ocean of ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa, whereas Rāmacandra Purī became a dry speculator and a critic of everyone else.**
