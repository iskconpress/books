====== CC Antya 8.61 ======

===== Text =====

> govinda-kāśīśvare prabhu kailā ājñāpana
> 'duṅhe anyatra māgi' kara udara bharaṇa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:govinda]]-[[synonyms:k:kāśīśvare]]---unto Govinda and Kāśīśvara; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---made; [[synonyms:ā:ājñāpana]]---order; [[synonyms:d:duṅhe]]---both of you; [[synonyms:a:anyatra]]---elsewhere; [[synonyms:m:māgi']]---begging; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:u:udara]] [[synonyms:b:bharaṇa]]---fill your bellies.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered Govinda and Kāśīśvara, "You may both take alms elsewhere to fill your bellies."**
