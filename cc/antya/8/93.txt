====== Cc. Antya 8.93 ======

===== Text =====

> kabhu laukika rīti,---yena 'itara' jana
> kabhu svatantra, karena 'aiśvarya' prakaṭana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:l:laukika]] [[synonyms:r:rīti]]---common behavior; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as; [[synonyms:i:itara]] [[synonyms:j:jana]]---a common man; [[synonyms:k:kabhu]]---sometimes; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---fully independent; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---does; [[synonyms:a:aiśvarya]] [[synonyms:p:prakaṭana]]---manifestation of godly opulence.

===== Translation =====

**Because of His full independence, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sometimes behaved like a common man and sometimes manifested His godly opulence.**
