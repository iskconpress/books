====== CC Antya 8.97 ======

===== Text =====

> teṅho gele prabhura gaṇa haila haraṣita
> śirera pāthara yena paḍila ācambita

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:teṅho]] [[synonyms:g:gele]]---when he left; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:h:haila]] [[synonyms:h:haraṣita]]---became very, very happy; [[synonyms:ś:śirera]]---on the head; [[synonyms:p:pāthara]]---a stone; [[synonyms:y:yena]]---as if; [[synonyms:p:paḍila]]---fell down; [[synonyms:ā:ācambita]]---suddenly.

===== Translation =====

**The devotees considered Rāmacandra Purī to be like a great burden on their heads. When he left Jagannātha Purī, everyone felt extremely happy, as if a great stone burden had suddenly fallen from their heads to the ground.**
