====== Cc. Antya 8.63 ======

===== Text =====

> praṇāma kari' prabhu kailā caraṇa vandana
> prabhure kahaye kichu hāsiyā vacana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:praṇāma]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---offering obeisances; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:c:caraṇa]] [[synonyms:v:vandana]]---offered respect at his feet; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---to Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kahaye]]---he says; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:h:hāsiyā]]---smiling; [[synonyms:v:vacana]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His obeisances to Rāmacandra Purī, worshiping his feet. Then Rāmacandra Purī smiled and spoke to the Lord.**
