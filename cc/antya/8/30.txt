====== Cc. Antya 8.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tuṣṭa hañā purī tāṅre kailā āliṅgana
> vara dilā---'kṛṣṇe tomāra ha-uka prema-dhana'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tuṣṭa]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being pleased; [[synonyms:p:purī]]---Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---him; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:ā:āliṅgana]]---embraced; [[synonyms:v:vara]] [[synonyms:d:dilā]]---gave the benediction; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇe]]---unto Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:h:ha]]-[[synonyms:u:uka]]---let there be; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:d:dhana]]---the wealth of love.

===== Translation =====

**Pleased with Īśvara Purī, Mādhavendra Purī embraced him and gave him the benediction that he would be a great devotee and lover of Kṛṣṇa.**
