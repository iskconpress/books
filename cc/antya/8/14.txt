====== CC Antya 8.14 ======

===== Text =====

> āgraha kariyā punaḥ punaḥ khāoyāila
> ācamana kaile nindā karite lāgila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āgraha]] [[synonyms:k:kariyā]]---with eagerness; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]] [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again and again; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāila]]---fed; [[synonyms:ā:ācamana]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---when he had washed his hands and mouth; [[synonyms:n:nindā]] [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgila]]---began to criticize.

===== Translation =====

**Encouraging him again and again, Rāmacandra Purī fed him sumptuously, but when Jagadānanda had washed his hands and mouth, Rāmacandra Purī began criticizing him.**
