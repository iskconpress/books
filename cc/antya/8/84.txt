====== Cc. Antya 8.84 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,---"sabe kene purīre kara roṣa?
> 'sahaja' dharma kahe teṅho, tāṅra kibā doṣa?

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhu]] [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of you; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:p:purīre]]---at Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:r:roṣa]]---are angry; [[synonyms:s:sahaja]]---natural; [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---religious principles; [[synonyms:k:kahe]]---speaks; [[synonyms:t:teṅho]]---he; [[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---of him; [[synonyms:k:kibā]]---what; [[synonyms:d:doṣa]]---fault.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Why are all of you angry at Rāmacandra Purī? He is expounding the natural principles of sannyāsa life. Why are you accusing him?**
