====== CC Antya 8.10 ======

===== Text =====

> tina-jane iṣṭha-goṣṭhī kailā kata-kṣaṇa
> jagadānanda-paṇḍita tāṅre kailā nimantraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tina]]-[[synonyms:j:jane]]---three persons; [[synonyms:i:iṣṭha]]-[[synonyms:g:goṣṭhī]]---discussion on Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---performed; [[synonyms:k:kata]]-[[synonyms:k:kṣaṇa]]---for some time; [[synonyms:j:jagadānanda]]-[[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]---Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:t:tāṅre]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]]---invited.

===== Translation =====

**The three of them talked about Kṛṣṇa for some time, and then Jagadānanda came and extended an invitation to Rāmacandra Purī.**
