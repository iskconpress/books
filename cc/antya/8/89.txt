====== CC Antya 8.89 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojyānna vipra yadi nimantraṇa kare
> kichu 'prasāda' āne, kichu pāka kare ghare

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojya]]-[[synonyms:a:anna]] [[synonyms:v:vipra]]---a //brāhmaṇa// at whose place an invitation could be accepted; [[synonyms:y:yadi]]---if; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---invites; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]]---//prasāda//; [[synonyms:ā:āne]]---brings; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:p:pāka]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---cooks; [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at home.

===== Translation =====

**When a brāhmaṇa at whose home an invitation could be accepted invited Him, the brāhmaṇa would purchase part of the prasāda and cook the rest at home.**
