====== CC Antya 8.28 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara-purī gosāñi kare śrī-pāda-sevana
> svahaste karena mala-mūtrādi mārjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Īśvara Purī; [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Gosvāmī; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---performs; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:pāda]]-[[synonyms:s:sevana]]---service to Mādhavendra Purī; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:h:haste]]---with his own hand; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---performs; [[synonyms:m:mala]]-[[synonyms:m:mūtra]]-[[synonyms:ā:ādi]]---stool, urine, and so on; [[synonyms:m:mārjana]]---cleaning.

===== Translation =====

**Īśvara Purī, the spiritual master of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, performed service to Mādhavendra Purī, cleaning up his stool and urine with his own hand.**
