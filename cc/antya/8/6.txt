====== Cc. Antya 8.6 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata gauracandra nija-bhakta-saṅge
> nīlācale krīḍā kare kṛṣṇa-prema-taraṅge

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nija]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with His own devotees; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:k:krīḍā]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---executes different pastimes; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:t:taraṅge]]---in the waves of love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, at Jagannātha Purī, performed His various pastimes with His own devotees in the waves of love for Kṛṣṇa.**
