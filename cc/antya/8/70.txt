====== CC Antya 8.70 ======

===== Text =====

> eta śuni' rāmacandra-purī uṭhi' gelā
> bhakta-gaṇa ardhāśana kare,---purī gosāñi śunilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eta]] [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing this; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:u:uṭhi']] [[synonyms:g:gelā]]---got up and left; [[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the devotees; [[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:a:aśana]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---were taking half the quantity of food; [[synonyms:p:purī]] [[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:ś:śunilā]]---heard.

===== Translation =====

**Hearing this, Rāmacandra Purī got up and left. He also heard from various sources that all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were eating half as much as usual.**
