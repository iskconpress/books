====== CC Antya 8.23 ======

===== Text =====

> 'kṛṣṇa nā pāinu, nā pāinu 'mathurā'
> āpana-duḥkhe maroṅ---ei dite āila jvālā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---I did not get; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāinu]]---did not get; [[synonyms:m:mathurā]]---Mathurā; [[synonyms:ā:āpana]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in my own unhappiness; [[synonyms:m:maroṅ]]---i am dying; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this person; [[synonyms:d:dite]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]] [[synonyms:j:jvālā]]---has come to give more pain.

===== Translation =====

**"O my Lord Kṛṣṇa, I could not reach You, nor could I reach Your abode, Mathurā. I am dying in my unhappiness, and now this rascal has come to give me more pain.**
