====== CC Antya 8.99 ======

===== Text =====

> guru upekṣā kaile, aiche phala haya
> krame īśvara-paryanta aparādhe ṭhekaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:guru]] [[synonyms:u:upekṣā]] [[synonyms:k:kaile]]---if one's spiritual master rejects him; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:p:phala]]---result; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is; [[synonyms:k:krame]]---gradually; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:p:paryanta]]---up to the point of the Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:a:aparādhe]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhekaya]]---commits offenses.

===== Translation =====

**If one's spiritual master rejects him, one becomes so fallen that he, like Rāmacandra Purī, commits offenses even to the Supreme Personality of Godhead.**
