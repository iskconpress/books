====== CC Antya 8.42 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura sthiti, rīti, bhikṣā, śayana, prayāṇa
> rāmacandra-purī kare sarvānusandhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sthiti]]---situation; [[synonyms:r:rīti]]---regulative principles; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]---accepting lunch; [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---sleeping; [[synonyms:p:prayāṇa]]---movement; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:anusandhāna]]---takes all information of.

===== Translation =====

**Rāmacandra Purī concerned himself with gathering all sorts of information about how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was situated, including His regulative principles, His lunch, His sleep and His movements.**
