====== CC Antya 8.75 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsīke eta khāoyāñā kara dharma nāśa!
> ataeva jāninu,---tomāra kichu nāhi bhāsa'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsīke]]---//sannyāsīs//; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāñā]]---inducing to eat; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:d:dharma]] [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---you spoil their religious principles; [[synonyms:a:ataeva]]---therefore; [[synonyms:j:jāninu]]---I can understand; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]]---your; [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:b:bhāsa]]---there is no advancement.

===== Translation =====

**"Also, by inducing sannyāsīs to eat so much, you spoil their religious principles. Therefore I can understand that you have no advancement.'**
