====== CC Antya 8.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jaya jaya avadhūta-candra nityānanda
> jagat bāṅdhila yeṅha diyā prema-phāṅda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:avadhūta]]-[[synonyms:c:candra]]---to the moon of mendicants; [[synonyms:n:nityānanda]]---Lord Nityānanda; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the world; [[synonyms:b:bāṅdhila]]---bound; [[synonyms:y:yeṅha]]---who; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---by; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:phāṅda]]---the noose of ecstatic love of Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Nityānanda Prabhu, the greatest of mendicants, who bound the entire world with a knot of ecstatic love for God.**
