====== Cc. Antya 8.59 ======

===== Text =====

> sei bhāta-vyañjana prabhu ardheka khāila
> ye kichu rahila, tāhā govinda pāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:b:bhāta]]---rice; [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetable; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:a:ardheka]] [[synonyms:k:khāila]]---ate half; [[synonyms:y:ye]] [[synonyms:k:kichu]] [[synonyms:r:rahila]]---whatever remained; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:g:govinda]]---Govinda; [[synonyms:p:pāila]]---got.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ate only half of the rice and vegetables, and whatever remained was taken by Govinda.**
