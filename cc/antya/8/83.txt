====== CC Antya 8.83 ======

===== Text =====

> iṅhāra vacane kene anna tyāga kara?
> pūrvavat nimantraṇa māna',---sabāra bola dhara"

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:i:iṅhāra]] [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---by his words; [[synonyms:k:kene]]---why; [[synonyms:a:anna]]---food; [[synonyms:t:tyāga]] [[synonyms:k:kara]]---You give up; [[synonyms:p:pūrva]]-[[synonyms:v:vat]]---as before; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:m:māna']]---please accept the invitation; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of everyone; [[synonyms:b:bola]]---the words; [[synonyms:d:dhara]]---accept.

===== Translation =====

**"Why have you given up proper eating due to the criticism of Rāmacandra Purī? Please accept invitations as before. This is the request of us all."**
