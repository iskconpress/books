====== CC Antya 8.62 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-rūpa mahā-duḥkhe dina kata gela
> śuni, rāmacandra-purī prabhu-pāśa āila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:r:rūpa]]---in this way; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:d:duḥkhe]]---in great unhappiness; [[synonyms:d:dina]] [[synonyms:k:kata]]---some days; [[synonyms:g:gela]]---passed; [[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]---Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:p:pāśa]] [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---came to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

===== Translation =====

**In this way, some days passed in great unhappiness. Hearing of all this, Rāmacandra Purī went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
