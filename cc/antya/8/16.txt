====== CC Antya 8.16 ======

===== Text =====

> sannyāsīre eta khāoyāñā kare dharma nāśa
> vairāgī hañā eta khāya, vairāgyera nāhi 'bhāsa' "

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sannyāsīre]]---unto a //sannyāsī//; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:k:khāoyāñā]]---feeding; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:d:dharma]] [[synonyms:n:nāśa]]---destroys the regulative principles; [[synonyms:v:vairāgī]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---being in the renounced order; [[synonyms:e:eta]]---so much; [[synonyms:k:khāya]]---eats; [[synonyms:v:vairāgyera]] [[synonyms:n:nāhi]] [[synonyms:b:bhāsa]]---there is no trace of renunciation.

===== Translation =====

**"Feeding a sannyāsī too much breaks his regulative principles, for when a sannyāsī eats too much, his renunciation is destroyed."**
