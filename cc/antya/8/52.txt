====== Cc. Antya 8.52 ======

===== Text =====

> śuni' tāhā prabhura saṅkoca-bhaya mane
> govinde bolāñā kichu kahena vacane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni']]---hearing; [[synonyms:t:tāhā]]---that; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya; [[synonyms:s:saṅkoca]]---doubt; [[synonyms:b:bhaya]]---fear; [[synonyms:m:mane]]---in the mind; [[synonyms:g:govinde]] [[synonyms:b:bolāñā]]---calling for Govinda; [[synonyms:k:kichu]]---some; [[synonyms:k:kahena]]---spoke; [[synonyms:v:vacane]]---words.

===== Translation =====

**After hearing this criticism, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was doubtful and apprehensive. Therefore He called Govinda and instructed him as follows.**
