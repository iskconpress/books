====== CC Antya 8.35 ======

===== Text =====

> ei śloke kṛṣṇa-prema kare upadeśa
> kṛṣṇera virahe bhaktera bhāva-viśeṣa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:ś:śloke]]---in this verse; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:p:prema]]---love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:u:upadeśa]]---instructs; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇera]] [[synonyms:v:virahe]]---by feeling separation from Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:b:bhaktera]]---of the devotee; [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]-[[synonyms:v:viśeṣa]]---transcendental situation.

===== Translation =====

**In this verse Mādhavendra Purī instructs how to achieve ecstatic love for Kṛṣṇa. By feeling separation from Kṛṣṇa, one becomes spiritually situated.**
