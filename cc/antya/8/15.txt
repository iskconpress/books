====== CC Antya 8.15 ======

===== Text =====

> "śuni, caitanya-gaṇa kare bahuta bhakṣaṇa
> 'satya' sei vākya,---sākṣāt dekhiluṅ ekhana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---I have heard; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the followers of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kare]] [[synonyms:b:bahuta]] [[synonyms:b:bhakṣaṇa]]---eat more than necessary; [[synonyms:s:satya]]---true; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vākya]]---that statement; [[synonyms:s:sākṣāt]]---directly; [[synonyms:d:dekhiluṅ]]---I have seen; [[synonyms:e:ekhana]]---now.

===== Translation =====

**"I have heard," he said, "that the followers of Caitanya Mahāprabhu eat more than necessary. Now I have directly seen that this is true.**
