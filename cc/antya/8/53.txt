====== CC Antya 8.53 ======

===== Text =====

> "āji haite bhikṣā āmāra ei ta' niyama
> piṇḍā-bhogera eka cauṭhi, pāṅca-gaṇḍāra vyañjana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ā:āji]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from today; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]] [[synonyms:ā:āmāra]]---My accepting //prasāda//; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:t:ta']]---certainly; [[synonyms:n:niyama]]---the rule; [[synonyms:p:piṇḍā]]-[[synonyms:b:bhogera]]---of Lord Jagannātha's //prasāda//; [[synonyms:e:eka]] [[synonyms:c:cauṭhi]]---one-fourth of a pot; [[synonyms:p:pāṅca]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇḍāra]] [[synonyms:v:vyañjana]]---vegetables costing five //gaṇḍās// (one //gaṇḍā// equals four //kauḍis//).

===== Translation =====

**"From today on it will be a rule that I shall accept only one-fourth of a pot of Lord Jagannātha's prasāda and five gaṇḍās worth of vegetables.**
