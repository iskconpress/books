====== CC Antya 8.65 ======

===== Text =====

> tomāre kṣīṇa dekhi, śuni,---kara ardhāśana
> ei 'śuṣka-vairāgya' nahe sannyāsīra 'dharma'

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomāre]]---You; [[synonyms:k:kṣīṇa]]---skinny; [[synonyms:d:dekhi]]---I see; [[synonyms:ś:śuni]]---I have heard; [[synonyms:k:kara]] [[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:a:aśana]]---You are eating half; [[synonyms:e:ei]]---this; [[synonyms:ś:śuṣka]]-[[synonyms:v:vairāgya]]---dry renunciation; [[synonyms:n:nahe]]---is not; [[synonyms:s:sannyāsīra]] [[synonyms:d:dharma]]---the religion of a //sannyāsī.//

===== Translation =====

**"I have heard that You have cut Your eating in half. Indeed, I see that You are skinny. Such dry renunciation is also not the religion of a sannyāsī.**
