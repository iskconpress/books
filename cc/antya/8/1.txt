====== CC Antya 8.1 ======

===== Text =====

> taṁ vande kṛṣṇa-caitanyaṁ
> rāmacandra-purī-bhayāt
> laukikāhārataḥ svaṁ yo
> bhikṣānnaṁ samakocayat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tam]]---to Him; [[synonyms:v:vande]]---I offer my respectful obeisances; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:c:caitanyam]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:r:rāmacandra]]-[[synonyms:p:purī]]-[[synonyms:b:bhayāt]]---due to fear of Rāmacandra Purī; [[synonyms:l:laukika]]---ordinary; [[synonyms:ā:āhārataḥ]]---from eating; [[synonyms:s:svam]]---His own; [[synonyms:y:yaḥ]]---who; [[synonyms:b:bhikṣā]]-[[synonyms:a:annam]]---quantity of food; [[synonyms:s:samakocayat]]---reduced.

===== Translation =====

**Let me offer my respectful obeisances to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who reduced His eating due to fear of the criticism of Rāmacandra Purī.**
