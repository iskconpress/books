====== Bg. 17.24 ======

> तस्मादोमित्युदाहृत्य यज्ञदानतपःक्रियाः ।
> प्रवर्तन्ते विधानोक्ताः सततं ब्रह्मवादिनाम् ॥२४॥

===== Text =====

> tasmād oṁ ity udāhṛtya
> yajña-dāna-tapaḥ-kriyāḥ
> pravartante vidhānoktāḥ
> satataṁ brahma-vādinām

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tasmāt]]---therefore; [[synonyms:o:om]]---beginning with //om//; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:u:udāhṛtya]]---indicating; [[synonyms:y:yajña]]---sacrifice; [[synonyms:d:dāna]]---charity; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---penance; [[synonyms:k:kriyāḥ]]---performances; [[synonyms:p:pravartante]]---begins; [[synonyms:v:vidhāna]]-[[synonyms:u:uktāḥ]]---according to scriptural regulation; [[synonyms:s:satatam]]---always; [[synonyms:b:brahma]]-[[synonyms:v:vādinām]]---of the transcendentalists.

===== Translation =====

**Thus the transcendentalists undertake sacrifices, charities, and penances, beginning always with om, to attain the Supreme.**

===== Purport =====

//Om tad viṣṇoḥ paramaṁ padam.// The lotus feet of Viṣṇu are the supreme devotional platform. The performance of everything on behalf of the Supreme Personality of Godhead assures the perfection of all activity.
