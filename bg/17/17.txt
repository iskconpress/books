====== Bg. 17.17 ======

> श्रद्धया परया तप्तं तपस्तत्त्रिविधं नरैः ।
> अफलाकाङ्क्षिभिर्युक्तैः सात्त्विकं परिचक्षते ॥१७॥

===== Text =====

> śraddhayā parayā taptaṁ
> tapas tat tri-vidhaṁ naraiḥ
> aphalākāṅkṣibhir yuktaiḥ
> sāttvikaṁ paricakṣate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ś:śraddhayā]]---with faith; [[synonyms:p:parayā]]---transcendental; [[synonyms:t:taptam]]---executed; [[synonyms:t:tapaḥ]]---austerity; [[synonyms:t:tat]]---that; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:v:vidham]]---three kinds; [[synonyms:n:naraiḥ]]---by men; [[synonyms:a:aphala]]-[[synonyms:ā:ākāṅkṣibhiḥ]]---without desires for fruits; [[synonyms:y:yuktaiḥ]]---engaged; [[synonyms:s:sāttvikam]]---in the mode of goodness; [[synonyms:p:pari]]-[[synonyms:c:cakṣate]]---is called.

===== Translation =====

**This threefold austerity, practiced by men whose aim is not to benefit themselves materially but to please the Supreme, is of the nature of goodness.**
