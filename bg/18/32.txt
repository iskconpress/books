====== Bg. 18.32 ======

> अधर्मं धर्ममिति या मन्यते तमसावृता ।
> सर्वार्थान्विपरीतांश्च बुद्धिः सा पार्थ तामसी ॥३२॥

===== Text =====

> adharmaṁ dharmam iti yā
> manyate tamasāvṛtā
> sarvārthān viparītāṁś ca
> buddhiḥ sā pārtha tāmasī

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:a:adharmam]]---irreligion; [[synonyms:d:dharmam]]---religion; [[synonyms:i:iti]]---thus; [[synonyms:y:yā]]---which; [[synonyms:m:manyate]]---thinks; [[synonyms:t:tamasā]]---by illusion; [[synonyms:ā:āvṛtā]]---covered; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]-[[synonyms:a:arthān]]---all things; [[synonyms:v:viparītān]]---the wrong direction; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:b:buddhiḥ]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:s:sa]]---that; [[synonyms:p:pārtha]]---O son of Pṛthā; [[synonyms:t:tāmasī]]---the mode of ignorance.

===== Translation =====

**That understanding which considers irreligion to be religion and religion to be irreligion, under the spell of illusion and darkness, and strives always in the wrong direction, O Pārtha, is in the mode of ignorance.**
