====== Bg. 6.9 ======

> सुहृन्मित्रार्युदासीनमध्यस्थद्वेष्यबन्धुषु ।
> साधुष्वपि च पापेषु समबुद्धिर्विशिष्यते ॥९॥

===== Text =====

> suhṛn-mitrāry-udāsīna-
> madhyastha-dveṣya-bandhuṣu
> sādhuṣv api ca pāpeṣu
> sama-buddhir viśiṣyate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:suhṛt]]---by nature a well-wisher; [[synonyms:m:mitra]]---benefactor with affection; [[synonyms:a:ari]]---enemy; [[synonyms:u:udāsīna]]---neutral between the belligerents; [[synonyms:m:madhyastha]]---mediator between the belligerents; [[synonyms:d:dveṣya]]---envious; [[synonyms:b:bandhuṣu]]---among the relatives or well-wishers; [[synonyms:s:sādhuṣu]]---unto the pious; [[synonyms:a:api]]---as well as; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:pāpeṣu]]---unto the sinners; [[synonyms:s:sama]]-[[synonyms:b:buddhiḥ]]---having equal intelligence; [[synonyms:v:viśiṣyate]]---is far advanced.

===== Translation =====

**A person is said to be still further advanced when he regards all---the honest well-wisher, friends and enemies, the envious, the pious, the sinner and those who are indifferent and impartial---with an equal mind.**
