====== Bg. 6.19 ======

> यथा दीपो निवातस्थो नेङ्गते सोपमा स्मृता ।
> योगिनो यतचित्तस्य युञ्जतो योगमात्मनः ॥१९॥

===== Text =====

> yathā dīpo nivāta-stho
> neṅgate sopamā smṛtā
> yogino yata-cittasya
> yuñjato yogam ātmanaḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yathā]]---as; [[synonyms:d:dīpaḥ]]---a lamp; [[synonyms:n:nivātasthaḥ]]---in a place without wind; [[synonyms:n:na]]---does not; [[synonyms:i:iṅgate]]---waver; [[synonyms:s:sā]] [[synonyms:u:upamā]]---compared to that; [[synonyms:s:smṛtā]]---likened; [[synonyms:y:yoginaḥ]]---of the //yogī//; [[synonyms:y:yata]]-[[synonyms:c:cittasya]]---whose mind is controlled; [[synonyms:y:yuñjataḥ]]---constantly engaged in; [[synonyms:y:yogam]]---meditation; [[synonyms:ā:ātmanaḥ]]---on Transcendence.

===== Translation =====

**As a lamp in a windless place does not waver, so the transcendentalist, whose mind is controlled, remains always steady in his meditation on the transcendent Self.**

===== Purport =====

A truly Kṛṣṇa conscious person, always absorbed in Transcendence, in constant undisturbed meditation on his worshipable Lord, is as steady as a lamp in a windless place.
