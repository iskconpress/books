====== Bg 10.40 ======

> नान्तोऽस्ति मम दिव्यानां विभूतीनां परन्तप ।
> एष तूद्देशतः प्रोक्तो विभूतेर्विस्तरो मया ॥४०॥

===== Text =====

> nānto 'sti mama divyānāṁ
> vibhūtīnāṁ parantapa
> eṣa tūddeśataḥ prokto
> vibhūter vistaro mayā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:na]]---nor; [[synonyms:a:antaḥ]]---a limit; [[synonyms:a:asti]]---is there; [[synonyms:m:mama]]---of My; [[synonyms:d:divyānām]]---divine; [[synonyms:v:vibhūtīnam]]---opulences; [[synonyms:p:parantapa]]---O conquerer of the enemies; [[synonyms:e:eṣaḥ]]---all this; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---that; [[synonyms:u:uddeśataḥ]]---examples; [[synonyms:p:proktaḥ]]---spoken; [[synonyms:v:vibhūteḥ]]---opulences; [[synonyms:v:vistaraḥ]]---expanded; [[synonyms:m:mayā]]---by Me.

===== Translation =====

**O mighty conqueror of enemies, there is no end to My divine manifestations. What I have spoken to you is but a mere indication of My infinite opulences.**

===== Purport =====

As stated in the Vedic literature, although the opulences and energies of the Supreme are understood in various ways, there is no limit to such opulences; therefore not all the opulences and energies can be explained. Simply a few examples are being described to Arjuna to pacify his inquisitiveness.
