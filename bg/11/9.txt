====== Bg 11.9 ======

> सञ्जय उवाच ।
> एवमुक्त्वा ततो राजन्महायोगेश्वरो हरिः ।
> दर्शयामास पार्थाय परमं रूपमैश्वरम् ॥९॥

===== Text =====

> sañjaya uvāca
> evam uktvā tato rājan
> mahā-yogeśvaro hariḥ
> darśayām āsa pārthāya
> paramaṁ rūpam aiśvaram

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sañjayaḥ]] [[synonyms:u:uvāca]]---Sanjaya said; [[synonyms:e:evam]]---thus; [[synonyms:u:uktvā]]---saying; [[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:r:rājan]]---O King; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:y:yogeśvaraḥ]]---the most powerful mystic; [[synonyms:h:hariḥ]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:darśayāmāsa]]---showed; [[synonyms:p:pārthāya]]---unto Arjuna; [[synonyms:p:paramam]]---divine; [[synonyms:r:rūpam]]---universal form; [[synonyms:a:aiśvaram]]---opulences.

===== Translation =====

**Sañjaya said: O King, speaking thus, the Supreme, the Lord of all mystic power, the Personality of Godhead, displayed His universal form to Arjuna.**
