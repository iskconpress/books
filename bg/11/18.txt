====== Bg. 11.18 ======

> त्वमक्षरं परमं वेदितव्यं
> त्वमस्य विश्वस्य परं निधानम् ।
> त्वमव्ययः शाश्वतधर्मगोप्ता
> सनातनस्त्वं पुरुषो मतो मे ॥१८॥

===== Text =====

> tvam akṣaraṁ paramaṁ veditavyaṁ
> tvam asya viśvasya paraṁ nidhānam
> tvam avyayaḥ śāśvata-dharma-goptā
> sanātanas tvaṁ puruṣo mato me

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tvam]]---You; [[synonyms:a:akṣaram]]---inexhaustible; [[synonyms:p:paramam]]---supreme; [[synonyms:v:veditavyam]]---to be understood; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---You; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---of this; [[synonyms:v:viśvasya]]---of the universe; [[synonyms:p:param]]---supreme; [[synonyms:n:nidhānam]]---basis; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---You are; [[synonyms:a:avyayaḥ]]---inexhaustible; [[synonyms:ś:śāśvata]]-[[synonyms:d:dharma]]-[[synonyms:g:goptā]]---maintainer of the eternal religion; [[synonyms:s:sanātanaḥ]]---eternal; [[synonyms:t:tvam]]---You; [[synonyms:p:puruṣaḥ]]---Supreme Personality; [[synonyms:m:mataḥ]] [[synonyms:m:me]]---is my opinion.

===== Translation =====

**You are the supreme primal objective; You are the best in all the universes; You are inexhaustible, and You are the oldest; You are the maintainer of religion, the eternal Personality of Godhead.**
