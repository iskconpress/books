====== Bg 11.30 ======

> लेलिह्यसे ग्रसमानः समन्ताल्-
> लोकान्समग्रान्वदनैर्ज्वलद्भिः ।
> तेजोभिरापूर्य जगत्समग्रं
> भासस्तवोग्राः प्रतपन्ति विष्णो ॥३०॥

===== Text =====

> lelihyase grasamānaḥ samantāl
> lokān samagrān vadanair jvaladbhiḥ
> tejobhir āpūrya jagat samagraṁ
> bhāsas tavogrāḥ pratapanti viṣṇo

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:l:lelihyase]]---licking; [[synonyms:g:grasamānaḥ]]---devouring; [[synonyms:s:samantāt]]---from all directions; [[synonyms:l:lokān]]---people; [[synonyms:s:samagrān]]---completely; [[synonyms:v:vadanaiḥ]]---by the mouth; [[synonyms:j:jvaladbhiḥ]]---with blazing; [[synonyms:t:tejobhiḥ]]---by effulgence; [[synonyms:ā:āpūrya]]---covering; [[synonyms:j:jagat]]---the universe; [[synonyms:s:samagram]]---all; [[synonyms:b:bhāsaḥ]]---illuminating; [[synonyms:t:tava]]---Your; [[synonyms:u:ugrāḥ]]---terrible; [[synonyms:p:pratapanti]]---scorching; [[synonyms:v:viṣṇo]]---O all-pervading Lord.

===== Translation =====

**O Viṣṇu, I see You devouring all people in Your flaming mouths and covering the universe with Your immeasurable rays. Scorching the worlds, You are manifest.**
