====== Bg. 2.65 ======

> प्रसादे सर्वदुःखानां हानिरस्योपजायते ।
> प्रसन्नचेतसो ह्याशु बुद्धिः पर्यवतिष्ठते ॥६५॥

===== Text =====

> prasāde sarva-duḥkhānāṁ
> hānir asyopajāyate
> prasanna-cetaso hy āśu
> buddhiḥ paryavatiṣṭhate

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prasāde]]---on achievement of the causeless mercy of the Lord; [[synonyms:s:sarva]]---all; [[synonyms:d:duḥkhānām]]---material miseries; [[synonyms:h:hāniḥ]]---destruction; [[synonyms:a:asya]]---his; [[synonyms:u:upajāyate]]---takes place; [[synonyms:p:prasanna]]-[[synonyms:c:cetasaḥ]]---of the happy-minded; [[synonyms:h:hi]]---certainly; [[synonyms:ā:āśu]]---very soon; [[synonyms:b:buddhiḥ]]---intelligence; [[synonyms:p:pari]]---sufficiently; [[synonyms:a:avatiṣṭhate]]---established.

===== Translation =====

**For one who is so situated in the Divine consciousness, the threefold miseries of material existence exist no longer; in such a happy state, one's intelligence soon becomes steady.**
