====== Bg. 1.6 ======

> युधामन्युश्च विक्रान्त उत्तमौजाश्च वीर्यवान् ।
> सौभद्रो द्रौपदेयाश्च सर्व एव महारथाः ॥६॥

===== Text =====

> yudhāmanyuś ca vikrānta
> uttamaujāś ca vīryavān
> saubhadro draupadeyāś ca
> sarva eva mahā-rathāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yudhāmanyuḥ]]---Yudhāmanyu; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vikrāntaḥ]]---mighty; [[synonyms:u:uttamaujāḥ]]---Uttamaujā; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vīryavān]]---very powerful; [[synonyms:s:saubhadraḥ]]---the son of Subhadrā; [[synonyms:d:draupadeyāḥ]]---the sons of Draupadī; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:r:rathāḥ]]---great chariot fighters.

===== Translation =====

**There are the mighty Yudhāmanyu, the very powerful Uttamaujā, the son of Subhadrā and the sons of Draupadī. All these warriors are great chariot fighters.**
