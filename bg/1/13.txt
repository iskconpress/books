====== Bg 1.13 ======

> ततः शङ्खाश्च भेर्यश्च पणवानकगोमुखाः ।
> सहसैवाभ्यहन्यन्त स शब्दस्तुमुलोऽभवत् ॥१३॥

===== Text =====

> tataḥ śaṅkhāś ca bheryaś ca
> paṇavānaka-gomukhāḥ
> sahasaivābhyahanyanta
> sa śabdas tumulo ’bhavat

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tataḥ]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:ś:śaṅkhāḥ]]---conchshells; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---also; [[synonyms:b:bheryaḥ]]---bugles; [[synonyms:c:ca]]---and; [[synonyms:p:paṇava]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānaka]]---trumpets and drums; [[synonyms:g:go]]-[[synonyms:m:mukhāḥ]]---horns; [[synonyms:s:sahasā]]---all of a sudden; [[synonyms:e:eva]]---certainly; [[synonyms:a:abhyahanyanta]]---being simultaneously sounded; [[synonyms:s:saḥ]]---that; [[synonyms:ś:śabdaḥ]]---combined sound; [[synonyms:t:tumulaḥ]]---tumultuous; [[synonyms:a:abhavat]]---became.

===== Translation =====

**After that, the conchshells, bugles, trumpets, drums and horns were all suddenly sounded, and the combined sound was tumultuous.**
